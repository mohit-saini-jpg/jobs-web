FAQs – Admit Cards

How to download SSC admit card?

Visit the official SSC site, go to the admit card section, select your region, and log in with your roll number and date of birth.

What should I carry along with my admit card?

You must carry a valid photo ID (Aadhaar, voter ID, driving license) along with your printed admit card.

What if my admit card has incorrect details?

Contact the respective board/commission immediately via helpline or email to correct discrepancies before the exam.
