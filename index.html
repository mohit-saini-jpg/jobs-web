The Indian Government Recruitment Landscape

India’s government recruitment system is decentralized. Multiple authorities conduct exams simultaneously:

  • Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) – upsc.gov.in
  • National Testing Agency (NTA) – nta.ac.in
  • Staff Selection Commission (SSC)
  • Railway Recruitment Boards (RRB)
  • State Public Service Commissions
  • Banking Recruitment Bodies (IBPS / SBI)
  • Police & Defence Boards
  • Public Sector Undertakings

A delay of even 24 hours in tracking updates can lead to missed deadlines. That is why daily structured monitoring is essential.
