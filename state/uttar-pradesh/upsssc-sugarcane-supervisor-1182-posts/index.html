  15. 15Printout of the duly filled online application form
  16. 16Only candidates who appeared in PET-2025 and hold a valid UPSSSC score card are eligible to apply - others need not apply.
  17. 17Only online applications through the official UPSSSC portal will be accepted; hard copy applications will be rejected.
  18. 18All reserved category candidates of UP must mention their category in the application form; only the most beneficial single relaxation will be granted if multiple categories are claimed.
  19. 19Candidates not domiciled in UP will not be entitled to reservation benefits and will be treated as Unreserved (General).
  20. 20The date of birth as recorded in the High School (Madhyamik) certificate is the only accepted proof; no other document will be accepted.
  21. 21EWS candidates must hold a certificate based on financial year 2025-26 income, valid for FY 2026-27, issued between 01-04-2026 and 28-07-2026; the certificate must be in the prescribed format as per UP Govt. Office Letter No. 0-3/2019/4/1/2002/ka-2/19 T.C.-II dated 14-03-2019.

  23. 23For female candidates of UP, only caste certificate issued from the father's side will be accepted.
  24. 24Ex-Servicemen claiming reservation must verify that they possess a certificate as per the prescribed format of the UP Government (GR dated 21 April 2015).
  25. 25Government/Semi-Government employees must obtain an NOC from their employer before participating in the selection process; they must produce it when demanded by the Commission.
  26. 26Married male candidates with more than one living wife, or female candidates who have married a man with an existing living wife, are not eligible - unless exempted by the Governor.
  27. 27If a candidate submits more than one application, only the application with the latest Application Number will be considered; fee paid against other applications will not be adjusted.
  28. 28Mobile phones and electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited inside examination premises.
  29. 29Negative marking of 1/4 mark applies for each wrong answer in the Written Examination.
  30. 30The Commission does not communicate individually with candidates; all information is published on the official website. Candidates must check https://upsssc.gov.in regularly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the total number of vacancies in UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak Recruitment 2026?
A
UPSSSC has announced a total of 1182 vacancies for Ganna Paryavekshak (Sugarcane Supervisor), including 1000 posts under General Selection and 182 posts under Special Selection.
Q2 What are the important dates for UPSSSC Sugarcane Supervisor Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application starts on 7 July 2026 and the last date to apply is 28 July 2026. Candidates can make corrections in the form until 4 August 2026.
Q3 Who is eligible to apply for UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak 2026?
A
Only candidates who have appeared in PET-2025 and possess a valid scorecard are eligible. Additionally, candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in Agriculture and CCC certificate.
Q4 What is the age limit for UPSSSC Sugarcane Supervisor Vacancy 2026?
A
The minimum age is 21 years and the maximum age is 40 years as of 1 July 2026. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per UP Government rules.
Q5 What is the salary of a Ganna Paryavekshak in UPSSSC?
A
The salary is under Pay Level-4, with a pay scale of ₹25,500 to ₹81,100 per month, along with additional allowances.
Q6 What is the application fee for UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak Recruitment 2026?
A
All candidates need to pay only a processing fee of ₹25, while the examination fee will be charged later for shortlisted candidates.
Q7 What is the selection process for UPSSSC Sugarcane Supervisor 2026?
A
The selection process includes a single written (main) examination of 100 marks. Candidates are shortlisted based on PET-2025 scores, and there is no interview.
Q8 What is the exam pattern for UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak Main Exam 2026?
A
The exam consists of 100 MCQ questions (100 marks) with a duration of 2 hours. There is a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer.
Q9 How can I apply online for UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online through the official website upsssc.gov.in by logging in using their PET-2025 registration number and completing the application form.
Q10 Is CCC certificate mandatory for UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak 2026?
A
Yes, a CCC certificate issued by NIELIT (DOEACC) is mandatory along with an Agriculture degree to be eligible for the post.
