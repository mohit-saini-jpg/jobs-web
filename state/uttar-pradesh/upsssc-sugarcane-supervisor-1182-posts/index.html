How To Apply
  • 1Applications must be submitted online only through the UPSSSC Online Application System at the official website https://upsssc.gov.in. Hard copy/offline applications will not be accepted under any circumstances. Candidates must login using their PET-2025 Registration Number via OTP sent to their registered mobile/email. All personal information filled during PET-2025 (name, father's name, category, EWS, reservation details, DOB, gender, marital status, contact number, email etc.) will be pre-filled and auto-displayed - these cannot be modified. Candidates must fill in educational qualification details in Section-II of the application. After filling all sections and entering the Verification Code, clicking "Submit" will display the Application Form with an 11-digit registration number - candidates must take a printout and keep it safely. The application form correction window will be open up to 04-08-2026.
