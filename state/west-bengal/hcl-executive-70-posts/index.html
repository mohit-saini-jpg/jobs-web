Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is Hindustan Copper Limited Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
Hindustan Copper Limited (HCL), a Schedule-A Miniratna Category-I CPSE under the Ministry of Mines, has released Advertisement No. Estt./1/2033/2025-26 dated 16/05/2026 for recruitment of 70 Executives in Grades E2, E3, and E4 across multiple disciplines.
Q2 How many vacancies are announced under HCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 70 Executive vacancies have been announced, including posts of Deputy Manager (E2), Manager (E3), and Senior Manager (E4) in 15 different disciplines, out of which 5 posts are reserved for PwBD candidates.
Q3 What are the important dates for HCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application portal will open on 05-06-2026 from 11:00 AM and will remain active till 25-06-2026 (midnight), while the advertisement was released on 16-05-2026 and the date of reckoning for age, qualification, and experience is 01-05-2026.
Q4 Which posts and grades are available in HCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
The recruitment is for Executive posts of Deputy Manager in Grade E2, Manager in Grade E3, and Senior Manager in Grade E4 in disciplines such as Mining, Geology, Environment Management, Safety & Fire, Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, HR, Finance, Materials & Contracts, Medical & Health Services, and others.
Q5 What is the pay scale for HCL Executive posts?
A
The pay scales are as per IDA pattern: E2 – Rs. 50000–160000/-, E3 – Rs. 60000–180000/-, and E4 – Rs. 70000–200000/-, with corresponding CTC ranging approximately from about 16–18 lakhs at entry level to above 50–60 lakhs at the maximum level depending on mining/non-mining category and grade.
Q6 What is the application fee for HCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
General, OBC, and EWS candidates must pay a non-refundable application fee of Rs. 500/- plus applicable bank charges, whereas SC, ST, PwBD, and internal HCL candidates are exempted from payment of the application fee.
Q7 What is the selection process for HCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection is based on a Personal Interview (PI) carrying 100% weightage, with candidates shortlisted for interview based on relevant post-qualification experience and additional higher qualifications if applications exceed the prescribed zone of consideration.
Q8 What is the age and nationality eligibility for HCL Executive posts?
A
Only Indian Nationals who are at least 18 years of age as on 01-05-2026 are eligible to apply, and upper age limits are relaxable as per Government norms for SC, ST, OBC (NCL), PwBD, and Ex-Servicemen candidates.
Q9 How can I apply online for HCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
Eligible candidates must apply online only through the Career section of the official HCL website https://www.hindustancopper.com between 05-06-2026 and 25-06-2026, and no offline or any other mode of application will be accepted.
Q10 Is there any service bond for selected HCL Executive candidates?
A
Yes, selected candidates are required to execute a Service Agreement Bond of Rs. 3 lakhs, committing to serve Hindustan Copper Limited for a minimum period of three years from the date of joining.
