Selection Process

1Candidates expecting permanent government employment — all posts are purely on contract basis and will not be regularized at any stage.
2Candidates who do not possess the specific qualification prescribed for the post (e.g., non-BDS candidates for Dental Surgeon, non-MBBS candidates for Medical Officer, non-BPT candidates for Physiotherapist).
3Candidates who exceed the prescribed age limit as on the date of notification — no relaxation is explicitly mentioned for general category candidates beyond what Tamil Nadu Govt. rules allow.
4Dental Surgeon and Medical Officer aspirants who are not registered with Tamil Nadu Dental Council or Tamil Nadu Medical Council respectively at the time of application.
5Counsellor applicants without minimum 3 years of HIV/AIDS-related experience (unless PLHIV concession applies).
6Candidates for Audiologist/Speech Language Pathologist/Audiometrician posts who are not from RCI-recognized institutes.
7Multi Purpose Health Worker applicants who do not possess the 2-year MPHW/Health Inspector training certificate granted by the Director of Public Health and Preventive Medicine.
8Occupational Therapist candidates who are unwilling to serve for a minimum of 2 years or whose continuance will extend beyond the TN-Rights project end date.
9Not registering the application online at https://tiruppur.nic.in/notice_category/recruitment/ before submitting the physical/postal application — online registration is step one per notification instructions.
10Failing to attach self-attested copies of all certificates along with the application — incomplete applications will not be considered for scrutiny.
11Not attaching the Undertaking (Self-declaration) and 11-month contract bond document required along with the application.
12Submitting applications after 05:00 PM on 29-06-2026 — late applications will be summarily rejected.
13Not attaching 10th, 12th, Degree marksheets, experience certificates, and relevant documents as per the post applied for — missing documents lead to rejection at scrutiny.
14Candidates for posts requiring RCI recognition (Audiologist, Speech Language Pathologist, Audiometrician) not verifying that their institute/degree is RCI-approved before applying.
15Account Assistant applicants not mentioning Tally Package proficiency and relevant experience in accounts maintenance in the application.

How to Apply

  1. 1Self-attested copies of 10th, 12th, Degree/Diploma Certificates with Mark Sheets
  2. 2Self-attested copies of all relevant qualifications and experience certificates prescribed for the post
  3. 3Self-attested copies of registration certificates (Tamil Nadu Medical Council / Tamil Nadu Dental Council / Indian Nursing Council / RCI, as applicable)
  4. 4Community certificate (if applicable) in prescribed format
  5. 5Undertaking (Self-declaration) and 11-month contract bond
  6. 6Any other supporting documents relevant to the post applied for
  7. 7This contract appointment is not permanent in nature and will not be regularized at any stage under any circumstances.
  8. 8Candidates must provide a Self-declaration (Undertaking) and must be willing to execute an 11-month contract bond at the time of joining.
  9. 9Applications must be submitted online first via the URL https://tiruppur.nic.in/notice_category/recruitment/ and then physically/by post to the DHS office with all required self-attested certificates before 29-06-2026 at 05:00 PM.
  10. 10Applications without complete enclosures will not be considered for scrutiny.
  11. 11Vacancies where currently serving contractual staff are in position will be given priority to eligible persons in those posts. The number of such vacancies may be revised.
  12. 12For interview-based posts, eligible candidates from the district will be called first; candidates from other districts will be considered only if no eligible district candidates are available.
  13. 13For direct/non-interview posts, merit will be determined based on marks scored in the prescribed academic qualifications.
  14. 14Candidates already serving in these positions in other districts may also apply and compete for posts in Tiruppur District.
  15. 15All original certificates must be produced at the time of certificate verification / interview.
  16. 16The contract will be renewed after every 11 months subject to performance appraisal and availability of NHM funds/programme requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the total number of posts in DHS Tiruppur NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 97 posts across 15 categories including Hospital Worker/MPHW/Sanitary Worker/Security (44), Multi Purpose Health Worker/Health Inspector Grade II (21), Staff Nurse/MLHP (11), Medical Officer UHWC (6), Lab Technician Grade III (4), Counsellor (2), and 1 post each for Dental Surgeon, Physiotherapist, Audiologist GMCH, Audiometrician JDHS, Audiologist & SLP JDHS, Early Interventionist, Occupational Therapist, Account Assistant, and OT Technician.
Q2 What is the last date to submit applications for DHS Tiruppur NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for submission of completed applications is 29-06-2026 by 05:00 PM.
Q3 Are the posts in DHS Tiruppur NHM Recruitment 2026 permanent government jobs?
A
No. All posts are purely on a contract basis under the National Health Mission (NHM). The appointment will not be made permanent or regularized at any stage, as stated explicitly in the notification.
Q4 What is the mode of selection for DHS Tiruppur NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
Medical Officer (UHWC), Staff Nurse/MLHP, and Multi Purpose Health Worker posts are filled through a non-interview (merit-based) process. All other posts are filled through a personal interview process.
Q5 What is the consolidated pay for Medical Officer (UHWC) post in DHS Tiruppur 2026?
A
The consolidated pay for Medical Officer (UHWC) is Rs. 63,000/- per month.
Q6 What qualification is required for the Lab Technician (Grade III) post?
A
Candidates must hold a 2-year Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology from King Institute of Preventive Medicine or any TN Govt. recognized institution; OR pass +2, possess a 1-year MLT Certificate from an institution recognized by the Director of Medical Education, and have good physique and vision for outdoor work.
Q7 Where should the application be submitted for DHS Tiruppur NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications must first be registered online at https://tiruppur.nic.in/notice_category/recruitment/ and then submitted with self-attested certificate copies in person or by post to: Executive Director/District Health Officer, District Health Society, 147-Pooluvapatti Road, Neeruperichsal Salai, Tiruppur – 641 602.
Q8 Is Tamil language mandatory for any post in DHS Tiruppur NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. For the Multi Purpose Health Worker (Health Inspector Grade II) post, candidates must have passed Tamil as a subject at SSLC level. For Hospital Worker/MPHW/Sanitary Worker/Security posts, candidates must be able to read and write in Tamil.
Q9 What documents must be attached with the application for DHS Tiruppur NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
Self-attested copies of 10th, 12th, Degree/Diploma certificates with marksheets, experience certificates, registration certificates (Medical/Dental/Nursing Council, RCI as applicable), community certificates (if applicable), the Undertaking/Self-declaration, and 11-month contract bond document.
Q10 Will currently serving NHM contractual staff get priority in DHS Tiruppur NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. As per the notification, vacancies where contractual staff are already serving will give priority to currently eligible persons in those positions. For interview posts, eligible candidates from Tiruppur District are given first preference over candidates from other districts.
