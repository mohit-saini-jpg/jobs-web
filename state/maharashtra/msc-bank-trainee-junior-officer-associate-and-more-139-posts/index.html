How to Apply

  1. 1Photograph (4.5 cm × 3.5 cm, passport style, colour, 200×230 pixels, 20–50 KB)
  2. 2Signature (black ink on white paper, 140×60 pixels, 10–20 KB)
  3. 3Left thumb impression (white paper, black/blue ink, 240×240 pixels, 20–50 KB)
  4. 4Handwritten declaration in English on white paper with black ink (800×400 pixels, 50–100 KB)
  5. 5Visit https://msc.bank.in/career and click "APPLY ONLINE".
  6. 6Click "Click here for New Registration" and enter Name, Contact details, and Email ID. Note the Provisional Registration Number and Password sent via SMS and email.
  7. 7Log in and fill the application form. Use "SAVE AND NEXT" to save progress at each step.
  8. 8Upload Photograph, Signature, Left Thumb Impression, and Handwritten Declaration as per specifications.
  9. 9Fill remaining application details and click "Validate Your Details" followed by "Save & Next".
  10. 10Preview the complete application using the "Preview Tab" and verify all entries.
  11. 11Click "COMPLETE REGISTRATION" after final verification.
  12. 12Proceed to the Payment Tab and pay the application fee online.
  13. 13Click "Submit" and save/print the e-Receipt and application form for future reference.
  14. 14Applying for multiple posts: MSC Bank allows one application per candidate. Submit only one application for the post you are most qualified for.
  15. 15Wrong photograph format: Passport-style colour photo against a light background is required. Selfies, group photos, or blurred images will lead to rejection.
  16. 16Signature in CAPITAL LETTERS: Signatures in block/capital letters are strictly rejected. Sign in your normal cursive style with black ink.
  17. 17Missing Domicile Certificate: Only Maharashtra residents can apply. Ensure your Domicile Certificate is valid and ready for document verification.
  18. 18Not checking Marathi in SSC: All posts require Marathi as a subject in Matriculation. Check your mark sheet before applying.
  19. 19Pressing Back/Refresh during payment: After submitting payment information, do not press Back or Refresh. Wait for the server confirmation to avoid double charges.
  20. 20Incomplete scanning of thumb impression: The left thumb impression must be properly scanned and not smudged. A blurred or partial impression can invalidate your application.
  21. 21Not saving the e-Receipt: If an e-Receipt is not generated, the transaction may have failed. Log in again and repeat the payment process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the last date to apply for MSC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application and pay the application fee is 01 July 2026. Applications received after this date will not be accepted.
Q2 How many total vacancies are there in MSC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
MSC Bank has notified 139 vacancies in total — 49 for Trainee Junior Officers, 81 for Trainee Associates, 04 for Trainee Typists, and 05 for Trainee Drivers.
Q3 Who is eligible to apply for MSC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
Only residents of Maharashtra State are eligible. All posts require passing the Matriculation Examination with Marathi as a subject. For Trainee Junior Officer and Associate posts, graduation with minimum 50% marks is required. Trainee Drivers only need SSC pass with a valid LMV licence.
Q4 What is the application fee for MSC Bank 2026?
A
The application fee for Trainee Junior Officer is Rs. 1,770/- (including GST). For Trainee Associate, Trainee Typist, and Trainee Driver, the fee is Rs. 1,180/- each (including GST). Payment is online only; fees are non-refundable.
Q5 What is the age limit for the Trainee Junior Officer post?
A
Candidates must be between 23 and 35 years of age as on 01 June 2026, i.e., born on or after 01 June 1991.
Q6 What is the selection process for MSC Bank Trainee Junior Officer?
A
Selection involves an online written examination conducted by IBPS (200 marks, 120 minutes) followed by a Personal Interview by MSC Bank. Final merit is based on combined performance in both stages.
Q7 Is there negative marking in MSC Bank exam 2026?
A
Yes, all online tests carry negative marking. For questions with four options, the penalty is 1/3 mark per wrong answer. For questions with five options, the penalty is 1/4 mark per wrong answer.
Q8 What is the salary for MSC Bank Trainee Junior Officer after training?
A
After successful completion of the 12-month training period, Trainee Junior Officers are placed in the Bank's regular grade with total emoluments of approximately Rs. 52,100/- per month. During training, a stipend of Rs. 30,000/- per month is paid.
Q9 Where will the MSC Bank exam be held?
A
The online written test will be held at centres in Mumbai/Navi Mumbai/Thane/MMR Region, Pune, Nagpur, Nashik, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Nanded, and Kolhapur. Personal Interviews and Skill Tests will be held at Mumbai.
Q10 What is the official website for MSC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
The official website is https://msc.bank.in. The career/apply page is at https://msc.bank.in/career. For queries, candidates may email [email protected].
