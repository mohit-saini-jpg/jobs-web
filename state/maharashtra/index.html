About Maharashtra Government Jobs 2026

Maharashtra Sarkari Naukri 2026 ke liye iss page par sabhi latest updates milenge. MPSC (Maharashtra Public Service Commission) Maharashtra ka sabse prestigious exam conduct karta hai jo Maharashtra Civil Services, Police Service aur Engineering Services ke liye selection karta hai. Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur, Nashik, Aurangabad, Thane jaise cities ke candidates badi sankhya mein Maharashtra Government Jobs 2026 ki taiyari karte hain.

MSRTC, MAHADISCOM aur Maharashtra Metro Rail important recruitment bodies hain. Maharashtra Police aur MHB ke through constable, PSI aur other posts nikale jaate hain. Maharashtra NHM aur KEM Mumbai ke under nursing officer, technician aur doctor posts aate hain. Maharashtra SSC Board aur MSEB ke through teacher bharti hoti hai. Mumbai University, Pune University aur Nagpur University mein bhi teaching aur non-teaching posts available hain 2026 mein.

Maharashtra Recruitment 2026 mein 10th, 12th, ITI, Diploma, Graduate aur PG — sabhi ke liye opportunities hain. Kolhapur, Solapur, Amravati ke candidates bhi online form se apply kar sakte hain.

Latest Government Jobs in Maharashtra

Latest Maharashtra Government Jobs mein MPSC, Maharashtra Police, MSRTC aur NHM ki vacancies currently open hain. Online form, admit card aur result Top Sarkari Jobs par ek jagah milta hai. Maharashtra Sarkari Naukri 2026 ki notification miss mat karo.

Qualification Wise Jobs in Maharashtra

Maharashtra mein 10th pass ke liye peon, constable aur Group D posts hain. 12th pass ke liye clerk aur assistant posts milte hain. ITI holders MAHADISCOM mein, diploma candidates PWD mein apply kar sakte hain. Graduate aur PG waale MPSC exams aur teacher posts ke liye eligible hain.

Department Wise Recruitment in Maharashtra

Maharashtra Police aur MHB, NHM health bharti, MSEB teacher recruitment aur MSRTC driver posts — yeh Maharashtra ke top hiring departments hain 2026 mein. MPSC schedule follow karo aur Top Sarkari Jobs par Maharashtra Recruitment 2026 ka full update paao.
