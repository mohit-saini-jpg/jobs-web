Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the last date to apply for GSSSB X-Ray Technician 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 29 June 2026 by 23:59 hours. The deadline to pay the exam fee is 1 July 2026 by 23:59 hours.
Q2 How many vacancies are available in GSSSB X-Ray Technician Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 246 vacancies are available for the post of X-Ray Technician, Class 3, distributed across General, EWS, SEBC, SC, and ST categories.
Q3 What is the educational qualification required?
A
Candidates must hold a B.Sc. degree from a recognised university and must have completed an X-Ray Technician course from a Government Institute or NMC-recognised Medical College. Basic computer knowledge is also mandatory.
Q4 What is the age limit for GSSSB X-Ray Technician 2026?
A
The general age limit is 18 to 36 years as on 29 June 2026. Age relaxation is available - up to 41 years for General women and reserved category men, up to 45 years for reserved category women and Divyang candidates.
Q5 What is the application fee?
A
The fee is ₹500 for General/Unreserved candidates and ₹400 for reserved category candidates (including all women, SEBC, SC, ST, EWS, Divyang, and Ex-Servicemen). Payment is accepted online only.
Q6 What is the selection process for this recruitment?
A
Selection is through a single written competitive exam (MCQ-based, CBRT/OMR format) consisting of 210 questions (90 in Part A + 120 in Part B) over 3 hours, followed by document verification. There is no interview.
Q7 Is there negative marking in the exam?
A
Yes. ¼ mark is deducted for every wrong answer. Questions left unanswered do not attract any penalty.
Q8 What is the salary for GSSSB X-Ray Technician?
A
For the first five years, the fixed pay is ₹40,800 per month. After satisfactory completion of five years, candidates are regularised at the 7th CPC pay scale of ₹35,400 to ₹1,12,400 (Level 6).
Q9 Where can I apply for GSSSB X-Ray Technician 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted online at https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in only. No offline or postal applications are accepted.
Q10 Can I make changes to my application after confirmation?
A
No. Once an application is confirmed, no changes - including to category/caste - can be made by either the candidate or GSSSB. If correction is needed before the deadline, submit a fresh application and pay the fee again.
