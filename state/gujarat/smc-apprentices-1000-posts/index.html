SMC – Apprentices – 1000 Posts

Gujarat Last Date: 10-06-2026
About
SMC Apprentices Recruitment 2026 notification is out to fill 1000 vacancies across Surat. Candidates with Any Graduate, B.Sc, ITI, diploma Can Apply Online. The online application opens on 01-06-2026, and closes on 10-06-2026. The candidate shall apply online through SMC website, suratmunicipal.gov.
