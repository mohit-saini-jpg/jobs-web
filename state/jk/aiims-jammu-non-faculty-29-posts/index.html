How to Apply

  1. 1Visit the official AIIMS Jammu website at www.aiimsjammu.edu.in and click on the "APPLY" link for the application form.
  2. 2Scan and keep ready: proof of date of birth, qualifying degree, registration certificate, passport-size photograph, signature, thumb impression, and valid identity proof.
  3. 3Fill in the online application form carefully. Ensure no column is left blank.
  4. 4Pay the application fee of Rs. 1,000/- (non-refundable) through the online payment gateway.
  5. 5Submit the online application form before 25 June 2026.
  6. 6Take a printout of the submitted application form, self-attest every page including all attachments, and speed post it to the following address so it is received by 30 June 2026:
  7. 7Not sending the hard copy in time: Submitting the online form alone is not sufficient. The hard copy must be speed-posted and reach AIIMS Jammu by 30 June 2026. Late receipt of hard copy will lead to cancellation with no exceptions stated.
  8. 8Editing restricted fields: Once submitted, no changes are permitted in Name, Date of Birth, Post Applied, or Category. Double-check these fields before hitting submit.
  9. 9Claiming experience from before the qualifying degree: Only experience acquired after obtaining the qualifying degree/diploma is counted. Listing pre-qualification experience will not strengthen your candidature and may raise flags during document verification.
  10. 10Forgetting self-attestation: Every page of the printed application form, including all attachments, must be self-attested before posting. Unattested copies may be rejected.
  11. 11Payment gateway issues: If the fee payment screen times out or errors occur, do not pay again immediately. Check your payment status first by logging back in to avoid double payment — the fee is non-refundable regardless.
  12. 12Applying without confirming eligibility: It is the sole responsibility of the candidate to verify their own eligibility. If ineligibility is detected at any stage — even after appointment — the appointment will be terminated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the last date to apply for AIIMS Jammu Non-Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to fill the online application form is 25 June 2026. The hard copy of the application must be submitted (by hand or post) and received by the Institute by 30 June 2026. Both deadlines are mandatory.
Q2 How many vacancies are there in AIIMS Jammu Non-Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 29 vacancies have been notified across 10 technician posts including Cath Lab Technician, Medical Lab Technician, Operation Theatre & Anaesthesia Technician, and others. The number may increase or decrease at the time of selection.
Q3 What is the age limit for AIIMS Jammu Non-Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit for all 10 posts is 35 years. The official notification does not specify a minimum age limit or age relaxation details for reserved categories beyond the upper limit of 35 years.
Q4 What is the application fee for AIIMS Jammu Non-Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 1,000/- (non-refundable) for all candidates. The fee must be paid online at the time of submitting the application form.
Q5 What is the salary for selected candidates in AIIMS Jammu Non-Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
All selected candidates will receive a fixed monthly remuneration of Rs. 51,684/-. This is uniform for all posts. Selected candidates are not entitled to PF, NPS, Gratuity, Medical Attendance Treatment, or any other regular government service benefits.
Q6 What is the selection process for AIIMS Jammu Non-Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection is based on an Applied Test and/or Interview carrying 100 marks. The date of the applied test will be notified separately on the official website. In case of a tie in marks, the older candidate (earlier date of birth) will be ranked higher.
Q7 Is the appointment permanent or contractual?
A
The appointment is purely on a contract basis, valid up to 31 October 2026 or until regular posts sanctioned by MoHFW are filled, whichever is earlier. There is no right to claim absorption, extension, or permanent employment.
Q8 Is a hard copy of the application mandatory for AIIMS Jammu Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. After submitting the online application, candidates must take a printout, self-attest every page including attachments, and speed post it to the Administrative Officer at AIIMS Vijaypur, Jammu – 181134 so it is received by 30 June 2026. Failure to submit the hard copy by this date will lead to cancellation of candidature.
Q9 Where can I find the official notification for AIIMS Jammu Non-Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The official notification is available on the AIIMS Jammu website at www.aiimsjammu.edu.in. All corrigenda, revision notices, and results will also be published exclusively on this website.
Q10 What documents are needed for AIIMS Jammu Non-Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates need proof of date of birth, qualifying degree/diploma, experience certificates, registration certificate (if applicable), valid identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN/Passport/Driving Licence), passport-size photograph, signature, and thumb impression. Original documents along with two sets of self-attested photocopies must be produced at the time of the applied test/interview and before joining.
