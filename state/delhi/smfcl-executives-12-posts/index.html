How to Apply

  1. 1Visit the official website of SMFCL at http://www.sdclindia.com and open the “Careers” section.
  2. 2Click on the link for SMFCL Rectt. Advt. No. 01/2026 and read the detailed advertisement and instructions carefully.
  3. 3Click on “Apply Online” and complete the registration / application form by entering personal, educational, category and experience details correctly.
  4. 4On submission of application details, a system‑generated Registration Number will appear at the top of the filled form; note it down for future login and correspondence.
  5. 5Upload all required documents under the “Upload Document” section in clear and legible scanned copies (PDF/JPEG) as specified.
  6. 6Proceed to “Make Payment” section and pay the applicable application fee online (Rs. 1,000/- for UR/OBC‑NCL/EWS; Nil for SC/ST/PwBD).
  7. 7After successful payment, download the final submitted application form and keep a printout for future reference and production at the time of selection, if called.
  8. 8High School (Class 10th) certificate as proof of Date of Birth.
  9. 9Certificates and marksheets of all academic/professional qualifications (10th, 12th, Diploma, Graduation, Post‑Graduation, CA/CMA/LL.B/CS/B.Tech, etc., as applicable).
  10. 10Percentage conversion document (wherever CGPA is awarded) issued by the University/Institute.
  11. 11Valid SC/ST/OBC‑NCL/EWS/PwBD certificate in prescribed format of Government of India, wherever reservation is claimed.
  12. 12Proof of Identity/Address such as Passport, Voter ID, Driving License, Aadhaar Card, etc.
  13. 13PAN Card.
  14. 14Proof of each period of experience claimed (offer letters, joining orders, relieving letters, service certificates, relevant pay slips, etc.) merged into a single PDF in chronological order.
  15. 15Any other document supporting the claims made in the online application.
  16. 16Printout of CBT Admit Card / Interview Call Letter along with original photo identity proof and one self‑attested photocopy.
  17. 17Original certificates and mark sheets of all educational qualifications and experience, along with original category certificates for SC/ST/OBC‑NCL/EWS/PwBD candidates.
  18. 18No Objection Certificate (NOC) / relieving order for candidates serving in Government/PSU/Autonomous Bodies, as required.
  19. 19The cut‑off date for determining age, qualification and post‑qualification experience is 18.06.2026; any qualification or experience acquired after this date will not be considered.
  20. 20Mere submission of application does not guarantee shortlisting; candidates must meet all eligibility criteria and clear the selection stages.
  21. 21If at any stage it is found that a candidate has furnished incorrect/false information or suppressed any material fact, his/her candidature/appointment is liable to be cancelled/terminated without notice.
  22. 22If a candidate applies for more than one post and CBT for multiple posts is held in a single session, he/she will be allowed to appear in CBT for only one post/discipline; remaining applications will not be considered and fee will not be refunded.
  23. 23Change of category, post, city for CBT, email ID, or any key information after submission of online application will not be entertained.
  24. 24All communication will be made only through email and SMFCL website; candidates must keep their registered email ID active for at least one year.
  25. 25Selected candidates can be posted anywhere in SMFCL offices or its subsidiaries in India or abroad as per organizational requirements.
  26. 26SMFCL reserves the right to cancel, restrict or modify the recruitment process or number of vacancies at any stage without assigning any reason.
  27. 27Canvassing in any form will disqualify the candidature.
  28. 28In case of any dispute, only courts at Delhi shall have jurisdiction; in case of discrepancy between versions, the English version of the advertisement will prevail.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
The SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026 is a national-level hiring process conducted by Sagarmala Finance Corporation Limited (SMFCL) for Executive (E-1 Grade) posts in various disciplines. It is a Mini Ratna Category-I CPSE under the Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways, Government of India. The recruitment aims to fill professional positions in Finance, Projects, Legal, Company Secretary, and IT departments through a transparent selection process.
Q2 How many vacancies are released under SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 12 Executive vacancies have been announced in the 2026 recruitment cycle. These posts are distributed across different disciplines including Finance, Projects, Legal, IT, and Company Secretary roles. Reservation benefits are also provided for SC, ST, OBC-NCL, EWS, and PwBD candidates as per Government of India rules.
Q3 What is the eligibility criteria for SMFCL Executive posts?
A
Candidates must possess qualifications such as CA/CMA for Finance, B.E./B.Tech for Engineering posts, LL.B for Legal, CS membership for Company Secretary, and B.Tech in Computer Science for IT posts. Only candidates with minimum required marks and valid degrees from recognized institutions are eligible. Final-year result declaration is mandatory before applying.
Q4 What is the age limit for SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit for SMFCL Executive posts is 30 years as on 18 June 2026. However, age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories like SC, ST, OBC-NCL, PwBD, and other eligible groups as per Government of India guidelines. The relaxation varies from 3 to 10 years depending on category.
Q5 What is the selection process for SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process includes a Computer Based Test (CBT) followed by a Personal Interview. The CBT carries 85% weightage in the final merit, while the interview carries 15%. Candidates must qualify both stages and also clear document verification and medical fitness requirements for final appointment.
Q6 What is the exam pattern for SMFCL Executive CBT?
A
The CBT consists of 150 multiple-choice questions carrying 150 marks, divided into domain-specific questions and general aptitude sections. The exam duration is 120 minutes, and there is a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer. The test will be conducted in major cities across India.
Q7 What is the application fee for SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee for General, OBC-NCL, and EWS candidates is Rs. 1000, while SC, ST, and PwBD candidates are exempted from paying any fee. The payment must be made online during the application process and is strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.
Q8 How can I apply for SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online through the official SMFCL website under the Careers section. They need to complete registration, fill in personal and academic details, upload documents, and pay the application fee. After successful submission, a registration number is generated for future reference.
Q9 What is the salary offered under SMFCL Executive E-1 Grade posts?
A
Selected candidates will receive a pay scale of Rs. 40,000 to Rs. 1,40,000 (IDA pattern) along with DA, allowances, and other benefits as per company rules. The salary package also includes additional perks depending on company policies and government norms. It is a high-paying PSU-level executive position.
Q10 Where can I download the SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026 notification PDF?
A
The official notification PDF is available on the SMFCL website under the recruitment or careers section. It contains complete details regarding eligibility, vacancies, exam pattern, and application instructions. Candidates should always refer to the official notification before applying to avoid misinformation.
