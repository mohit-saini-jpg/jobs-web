Selection Process

1Written Examination + Physical Efficiency Test (PET) + Trade Test (TT) + Primary Medical Examination (PME) + Document Verification (DV)
2Female candidates - this recruitment is strictly for Indian National Males only.
3Candidates who do not possess the requisite educational/technical qualification as on the closing date of application; those awaiting final exam results are not eligible.
4Candidates outside the age band 18-27 years as on 01 Jun 2026 (except those eligible for applicable category-based relaxation).
5OBC candidates from the Creamy Layer - they are not entitled to OBC reservation or age relaxation and must apply as UR/General category.
6Candidates who cannot meet the region-wise physical standards (height, chest, weight) as physical fitness is mandatory for induction.
7Candidates with permanent medical conditions listed as grounds for permanent rejection (colour blindness for Drivers/Operators, cardiac conditions, gross myopia, etc.) who cannot pass the Primary Medical Examination at GREF Centre.
8Candidates unwilling or unable to be posted anywhere in India, especially in remote, high-altitude, or difficult terrain areas in Northern and Eastern regions.
9Candidates who cannot arrange and send a physical application form with photograph, thumb impression, signature, fee e-receipt, and attested copies by post to Pune - there is no online application mode.
10Candidates with tattoos on any body part other than the inner forearm (inside elbow to wrist) or reverse palm - tattoos on other parts lead to disqualification at PME.
11Ex-servicemen who have already joined Govt civil service using ESM benefits and are claiming ESM status again - such candidates are treated as civilian employees.
12Not printing the application form on A4 size 75 GSM bond paper (one side only) and admit card on both sides of a single paper as mandated - non-standard paper leads to rejection.
13Applying without attaching the e-receipt copy of the application fee paid via SBI Collect; no other payment mode is accepted and missing receipt causes rejection.
14Sending the application from a wrong/old address instead of present postal address - applications posted from wrong address are rejected to prevent fraudulent claims of 60-day receipt benefit.
15Not superscribing the envelope with "APPLICATION FOR (NAME OF TRADE/POST) Under Category (UR/SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD/ESM/CPL)" as required - unlabelled envelopes may be misdirected.
16Mismatch between name in application and name in Matriculation certificate - even a dropped or added part of the name requires a Gazette Notification; otherwise candidature is summarily cancelled.
17Submitting multiple applications for the same post with different details or photographs - this results in lifetime debarment from all GREF examinations.
18OBC/EWS certificate dated older than one year from the closing date - OBC and EWS certificates must be fresh (not older than one year as on closing date) and in the prescribed Central Govt format.
19Candidates applying for more than one post/trade using the same envelope - each post requires a separate application with separate fee in a separate envelope.
20Not carrying the minimum 12 passport-size photographs (same as pasted on application and admit card) to all subsequent recruitment stages - shortage of photos causes disruption at PET/TT/PME stages.
21Ignoring the trade-specific syllabus and treating the written exam as a general knowledge test - each post has a distinct subject-wise syllabus and candidates must prepare trade-specific topics.

  3. 3Signature of candidate at the prescribed place
  4. 4Copy of e-receipt of application fee paid via SBI Collect

  19. 19There shall be no provision for re-evaluation/re-checking of scores in examination; no correspondence in this regard shall be entertained.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 How many vacancies are available in BRO GREF Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 899 vacancies are available across 6 trades: Store Keeper Technical (300), Operator Communication (261), Operator Excavating Machinery OEM (207), Electrician (79), Draughtsman (42), and Hindi Typist (10), with 134 ESM and 15 PwBD seats.
Q2 What is BRO GREF Recruitment 2026?
A
BRO GREF Recruitment 2026 is a recruitment drive by Border Roads Organisation, General Reserve Engineer Force under Ministry of Defence, Government of India, under Advertisement No. 01/2026 to fill 899 posts across 6 trades/posts for Indian National Males only.
Q3 What is the eligibility for BRO GREF Recruitment 2026?
A
Eligibility varies by trade: Draughtsman requires 10+2 Science plus 2-year Architecture/Draughtsmanship certificate; SKT/Hindi Typist require 10+2; Operator Communication/OEM/Electrician require Matriculation plus relevant ITI certificate or Defence Trade Certificate with experience. Only Indian National Males aged 18-27 years are eligible.
Q4 What is the last date to apply for BRO GREF Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for receipt of offline applications is 04 July 2026 for most states, and 19 July 2026 for candidates from NE States (Assam, Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh, Mizoram, Manipur, Nagaland, Tripura, Sikkim), Ladakh, remote HP areas, and Andaman & Nicobar Islands.
Q5 What is the selection process for BRO GREF Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process includes Written Examination, Physical Efficiency Test (1.6 km run in 10 minutes), Trade Test (where applicable), Primary Medical Examination (PME), and Document Verification. Final merit is based solely on written exam marks (minimum 50% for UR/EWS/OBC, 40% for SC/ST) subject to qualifying PET, TT, and PME.
Q6 What is the age limit for BRO GREF Recruitment 2026?
A
Age limit is 18 to 27 years as on 01 June 2026, with relaxation of 5 years for SC/ST, 3 years for OBC, 10 years for PwBD, up to 40 years for Central Govt employees (UR), and deduction of military service period plus 3 years for Ex-Servicemen.
Q7 Is there any application fee for BRO GREF Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, application fee is ₹50 (non-refundable) for General/EWS/OBC/Ex-Servicemen/Ex-Agniveers candidates, payable online only through SBI Collect portal. SC/ST/PwBD candidates are exempted from fee payment.
Q8 What is the salary for BRO GREF posts 2026?
A
Draughtsman receives Pay Band-1 (₹5,200-20,200) plus Grade Pay ₹2,800; all other posts receive Grade Pay ₹1,900, along with DA, HRA, Transport Allowance, Hard & Risk Allowance as per Government of India rules.
Q9 Can female candidates apply for BRO GREF Recruitment 2026?
A
No, this recruitment is strictly for Indian National Males only. Female candidates are not eligible to apply for any of the 6 trades/posts advertised under this notification.
Q10 How to apply for BRO GREF Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply offline only by downloading the application form from bro.gov.in, paying ₹50 fee via SBI Collect portal, filling the form on A4 75 GSM bond paper, attaching e-receipt and self-attested documents, and sending by post to Commandant, GREF Centre, Dighi Camp, Pune-411015 by 04 July 2026.
