Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is EdCIL AAI Recruitment 2026?
A
EdCIL AAI Recruitment 2026 refers to a set of five contractual recruitment advertisements (Nos. 02, 04, 06, 07, and 08 of 2026) issued by EdCIL (India) Limited on behalf of Airports Authority of India (AAI), inviting applications for Consultant positions across multiple directorates at AAI's Corporate Headquarters in New Delhi.
Q2 What is the last date to apply for AAI Consultant posts 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for all EdCIL AAI Consultant posts 2026 is 28 June 2026 at 23:55 HRS. Applications open from 12 June 2026.
Q3 How many total vacancies are there in EdCIL AAI Recruitment 2026?
A
Across all five advertisements, there are approximately 16 vacancies: 2 in Airport Security, 9 in Planning (4 Architects + 5 Data Analysts), 1 in HRM Pension Cell, 2 in SIU, and 2 in CSR Directorate.
Q4 What is the salary for AAI Consultant posts in 2026?
A
Monthly remuneration ranges from Rs. 70,000/- for Associate Consultant posts in the Planning Directorate to Rs. 1,80,000/- for the Principal Consultant (Security) post. All amounts are all-inclusive.
Q5 Is there an application fee for AAI Consultant Recruitment 2026?
A
No. There is no application fee for any of the consultant posts under EdCIL AAI Recruitment 2026.
Q6 What is the age limit for AAI Consultant posts 2026?
A
Age limits vary by post: 30 years for Associate Consultants (Planning), 35 years for Junior Consultants (SIU), 40 years for Consultant (CSR), 45 years for Senior Consultants (CSR, HRM, Security), and 55 years for Principal Consultant (Security). Age is calculated as of 28 June 2026.
Q7 Is the AAI Consultant engagement permanent or contractual?
A
All engagements are purely contractual, initially for one year, with provision for extension based on AAI's requirements and applicable rules.
Q8 Where will the selected consultants be posted?
A
All selected consultants will be posted exclusively at AAI Corporate Headquarters, Safdarjung Airport, New Delhi.
Q9 What is the selection process for AAI Consultant posts?
A
The selection process involves evaluation of academic qualifications and experience, followed by document verification and a personal interview. Shortlisted candidates are notified by email only.
Q10 Can I apply for more than one post in EdCIL AAI Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, candidates can apply for more than one post, but they must submit a separate online application for each post they wish to be considered for.
