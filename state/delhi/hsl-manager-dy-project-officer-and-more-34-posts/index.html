Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the total number of vacancies in HSL Visakhapatnam Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 34 vacancies have been announced under Hindustan Shipyard Limited Recruitment 2026 — 17 posts on Permanent Absorption basis (Manager Technical-05, Manager HR-02, Deputy Manager Technical-10) and 17 posts on Fixed Term Contract (FTC) basis across 8 different positions. The number of vacancies is subject to change at the discretion of HSL Management.
Q2 What is the last date to apply for HSL Visakhapatnam Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for HSL Visakhapatnam Recruitment 2026 is 24 June 2026 up to 5:00 PM (17:00 hrs) for both Permanent and FTC posts. Once submitted, editing is not allowed. The date may be extended at the discretion of HSL. Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date.
Q3 How can I apply for HSL Visakhapatnam Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online only through the official HSL website at http://www.hslvizag.in. Click on "Careers" under "Human Resources" and then "Current Openings." The registration has 3 stages: Personal Details, Educational Details, and Payment Details. Once submitted, no editing is possible. For clarification, contact [email protected].
Q4 What is the application fee for HSL Visakhapatnam Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 300/- for General/OBC/EWS candidates. There is no registration fee for SC/ST/PH (PwD) candidates and Internal Candidates (only permanent HSL employees). The fee once paid will not be refunded under any circumstances.
Q5 What is the age limit for HSL Visakhapatnam Recruitment 2026?
A
The maximum age limit varies by post — 40 years for Manager (Technical) [P1] and all Dy. Project Officer FTC posts, 45 years for Manager (HR) [P2], 35 years for Deputy Manager (Technical) [P3], and 57 years for Project Superintendent [F1] and Dy. Project Superintendent [F2] FTC posts, all calculated as on 03 June 2026. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST/OBC/PwD/Ex-Servicemen and Internal Candidates as per rules.
Q6 What is the selection process for HSL Visakhapatnam Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of shortlisting (up to 6 times the vacancies based on % marks in essential qualifying examination) followed by an Interview. The interview may be conducted online in VC Mode or physically at HSL Visakhapatnam, and may include Group Discussion, Preliminary Screening Interview, and Final Selection Interview. Permanent post candidates (P1/P2/P3) will be reimbursed to-and-from 2nd AC train fare for physical interview. No travel fare is paid for FTC post candidates.
Q7 What is the pay offered under HSL Visakhapatnam Recruitment 2026?
A
For Permanent posts, the pay scale is IDA E3 (Rs. 60,000-1,80,000; approx. CTC Rs. 18.42 LPA) and E2 (Rs. 50,000-1,60,000; approx. CTC Rs. 15.51 LPA) plus DA, HRA, allowances, CPF, gratuity, medical reimbursement, and other benefits. For FTC posts, consolidated pay ranges from Rs. 90,000/- per month (Dy. Project Officer level) to Rs. 1,85,000/- per month (Project Superintendent), all inclusive, with a Retention Incentive of Rs. 2,500/- per year for Dy. Project Officer posts.
Q8 Is there a service bond for HSL Permanent post candidates?
A
Yes. Candidates selected for Permanent Absorption posts must execute a Service Agreement Bond of Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakhs) committing to serve HSL for a minimum period of 5 years from the date of joining. No such service bond is applicable for Fixed Term Contract (FTC) posts.
Q9 Can candidates from outside Visakhapatnam apply for HSL Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. All Indian Nationals can apply. However, all vacancies are based in Visakhapatnam except the Dy. Project Superintendent (Technical) – Delhi Region (F2) post. Officers selected may also be transferred to other stations for projects up to 2 years at a stretch and may need to undertake outstation duties to Delhi/Yards/Vendor Premises as required by HSL Management.
Q10 What experience is required for the Fixed Term Contract (FTC) posts in HSL Recruitment 2026?
A
The experience requirements vary by FTC post: Project Superintendent (F1) requires minimum 20 years in Shipbuilding/Naval Ships/Submarines; Dy. Project Superintendent (F2) requires minimum 18 years; all Dy. Project Officer posts (F3 to F8) require minimum 02 years of relevant post-qualification experience in their respective disciplines (Technical/Civil/Design/HR/Safety/Fire Safety). Detailed experience criteria for each post are available in the official notification at www.hslvizag.in.
