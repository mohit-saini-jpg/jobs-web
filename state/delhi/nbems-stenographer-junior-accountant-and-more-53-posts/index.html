Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is NBEMS Recruitment 2026?
A
NBEMS Recruitment 2026 refers to the official Vacancy Notice (No. A-12011/3/2025-ESTB, dated 02 June 2026) released by the National Board of Examinations in Medical Sciences for regular appointment to 53 posts across Group A, B, and C categories, including Deputy Director (Medical), Junior Programmer, Junior Accountant, Stenographer, and Junior Assistant.
Q2 How many total vacancies are there in NBEMS Recruitment 2026?
A
There are a total of 53 vacancies in NBEMS Recruitment 2026 - 2 for Deputy Director (Medical), 1 for Junior Programmer, 4 for Junior Accountant, 7 for Stenographer, and 39 for Junior Assistant. The vacancy count is liable to change as per the official notification.
Q3 When will NBEMS online registration open in 2026?
A
The online registration for NBEMS Recruitment 2026 will be activated with effect from 29th June 2026 on the official NBEMS website at www.natboard.edu.in.
Q4 What is the exam date for NBEMS Recruitment 2026?
A
The tentative date of examination for NBEMS Recruitment 2026 is scheduled to be held on 5th and 6th September 2026, as mentioned in the official vacancy notice.
Q5 What is the eligibility for NBEMS Deputy Director (Medical) post?
A
For the Deputy Director (Medical) post, the candidate must be below 35 years of age and must hold a recognized Postgraduate Medical qualification approved under the Indian Medical Council Act-1956. Desirable qualifications include training in medical education technology and knowledge of modern evaluation techniques such as MCQ generation and MEQ analysis.
Q6 What is the qualification required for NBEMS Junior Assistant post?
A
For the Junior Assistant post, candidates must have passed the Senior Secondary Examination (12th of 10+2) from a Board/University recognized by Central/State Govt./UT Administration, must have proficiency in computers and basic software (Windows, LAN architecture), and must qualify the examination as may be prescribed by NBEMS.
Q7 What is the pay scale for NBEMS Junior Assistant 2026?
A
The Junior Assistant post is placed at Pay Matrix Level 2 under the 7th CPC, with a basic pay range of approximately ₹19,900 to ₹63,200. In addition to basic pay, employees receive Dearness Allowance, HRA, Transport Allowance, and other Central Government benefits.
Q8 What is the age limit for NBEMS Stenographer post?
A
The age limit for the NBEMS Stenographer post is 18 to 27 years. Age relaxation for SC/ST/OBC/PwBD and other reserved categories is applicable as per Government of India norms.
