Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is Mission Vatsalya Scheme and what posts are available under this Delhi WCD Recruitment 2026?
A
Mission Vatsalya is a flagship scheme of the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, aimed at child protection and welfare. Under this scheme, the Department of Women and Child Development, Government of NCT of Delhi has announced contractual vacancies for 10 post categories: Programme Officer (SCPS), District Child Protection Officer (DCPO), Protection Officer (Institutional Care), Protection Officer (Non-Institutional Care), Legal cum Probation Officer, Counselor, Social Worker, Accountant, Data Analyst, Assistant cum Data Entry Operator (ADEO), and Out Reach Worker — for placement in SCPS, DCPUs, JJBs, CWCs, and SJPUs across Delhi.
Q2 What is the last date to apply for Mission Vatsalya Delhi WCD Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for submission of online applications is 26-06-2026 (by 11:59 PM). The application process commenced on 05-06-2026 (12:01 AM). Applications must be submitted online only through http://cams.wcddel.in. No offline or physical applications will be accepted. Candidates are advised to apply well before the deadline to avoid last-minute technical issues.
Q3 What is the salary for various posts under Mission Vatsalya Delhi WCD Recruitment 2026?
A
The consolidated monthly remuneration varies by post: DCPO — Rs. 44,023/-; Programme Officer — Rs. 34,755/-; Protection Officers (IC & NIC) and Legal cum Probation Officer — Rs. 27,804/-; Counselor, Social Worker, Accountant, and Data Analyst — Rs. 24,356/-; ADEO and Out Reach Worker — Rs. 22,411/-. Posts marked * are as per Mission Vatsalya Scheme; posts marked ** are as per Minimum Wages applicable in Delhi.
Q4 Is Mission Vatsalya Delhi WCD 2026 a permanent government job?
A
No. This recruitment is purely on a contractual basis, initially for a period of one year. It is explicitly stated in the notification that this is NOT a permanent job with the Government of NCT of Delhi or the Government of India. The Department also reserves the right to terminate the contract at any stage/point of time. There is no assurance of regularization or extension after the initial one-year period.
Q5 Can I apply for more than one post in Mission Vatsalya Delhi WCD Recruitment 2026?
A
No. A candidate is eligible to apply for only one post. This is explicitly stated in the notification: "In case a candidate applies for more than one post, all applications submitted by the candidate shall be summarily rejected." Carefully read the TORs for all posts, identify the one post for which you are most eligible and experienced, and apply for that post only.
Q6 What is the age limit for District Child Protection Officer (DCPO) in Mission Vatsalya Delhi 2026?
A
The upper age limit for DCPO is 40 years as on the application closing date (26-06-2026). Similarly, Programme Officer, Protection Officer (IC & NIC), and Legal cum Probation Officer also have an upper age limit of 40 years. Posts like Counselor, Social Worker, Accountant, Data Analyst, ADEO, and Out Reach Worker have an upper age limit of 35 years. Age relaxation as per Delhi State Government rules is applicable for reserved category candidates; additional relaxation is available for candidates already working in WCD, GNCTD.
Q7 What is the qualification required for Protection Officer posts in Mission Vatsalya Delhi WCD 2026?
A
For Protection Officer (Institutional Care) and Protection Officer (Non-Institutional Care), the eligibility is either: (a) Post Graduate degree in Social Work/Sociology/Child Development/Human Rights/Public Administration/Psychology/Psychiatry/Law/Public Health/Community Resource Management with minimum 50% marks, plus 12th with min. 60%; OR (b) Graduate in the above disciplines with minimum 50% marks plus 2 years of post-graduation experience in Women & Child Development/Social Welfare, plus 12th with min. 60%. Computer skills and fluency in English & Hindi are also required for both routes.
Q8 What documents are required for Mission Vatsalya Delhi WCD Recruitment 2026?
A
Key documents include: educational qualification certificates (12th, graduation, PG degree, LLB, PG Diploma, or Computer Diploma as applicable per post); experience certificates from employers (specifying period, nature of work, and regular paid capacity); age proof (10th certificate); category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS as per Delhi State Govt. format); typing speed certificate (min. 35 WPM for ADEO post); recent passport-size photograph; photo identity proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID); and NOC from present employer (for departmental/government employees). Keep all documents ready before the interview call.
