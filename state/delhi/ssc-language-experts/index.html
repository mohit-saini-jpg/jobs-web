Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the last date to apply for SSC Language Experts Empanelment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the application (resume/CV with supporting documents) via email is 17 June 2026. Applications received after this date will not be considered. Candidates are advised to send their applications well before the deadline to avoid last-minute issues.
Q2 What is the mode of application for SSC Language Experts Empanelment 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted through email only at [email protected], addressed to the Joint Director, SSC. No other mode of application — including post, Speed Post, or in-person submission — will be accepted.
Q3 Which languages are included in SSC Language Experts Empanelment 2026?
A
The Commission invites applications for empanelment in 13 Regional Languages: Assamese, Bengali, Gujarati, Kannada, Konkani, Malayalam, Marathi, Odiya, Punjabi, Tamil, Telugu, Urdu, and Manipuri (Meitei/Meithei).
Q4 Who is eligible to apply for SSC Language Experts Empanelment 2026?
A
Applicants who are currently working or have retired as a Faculty Member, Lecturer, or High School Teacher in the concerned regional language from any recognised School, College, or University are eligible. Additionally, applicants must possess sound knowledge of English and Hindi in addition to expertise in the relevant regional language.
Q5 Is there any application fee for SSC Language Experts Empanelment 2026?
A
No application fee has been mentioned in the official notification. Candidates can submit their resume/CV with supporting documents via email at [email protected] free of cost.
Q6 What is the nature of engagement for SSC Language Experts?
A
The empanelment is purely temporary and on a requirement basis. Empanelled experts will be called to the premises of SSC Headquarters, CGO Complex, New Delhi, for assigned work as and when required by the Commission. Mere inclusion in the panel does not confer any right for regular assignment of work or permanent engagement by the Commission.
Q7 What is the remuneration for SSC Language Experts?
A
Remuneration will be paid as per rates approved by the Commission. The specific rates have not been mentioned in the official notification. Candidates may contact the Commission at [email protected] for further details on remuneration.
Q8 Where will the empanelled Language Experts be required to work?
A
Empanelled Language Experts will be required to come to the premises of SSC Headquarters, CGO Complex, New Delhi for the assigned work. The work will be carried out as per the instructions and timelines prescribed by the Commission from time to time.
Q9 Can a retired teacher apply for SSC Language Experts Empanelment 2026?
A
Yes. The notification explicitly states that both working and retired Faculty Members, Lecturers, and High School Teachers in the concerned regional language from any recognised School, College, or University are eligible to apply. There is no age bar mentioned in the notification.
Q10 What documents should be submitted along with the application for SSC Language Experts Empanelment 2026?
A
Candidates must send a detailed resume/CV clearly indicating teaching specialization, experience in the concerned regional language, and knowledge of English and Hindi, along with all relevant supporting documents such as educational qualification certificates, proof of current or past employment as Faculty Member/Lecturer/High School Teacher, experience certificates from recognised institutions, and any other documents establishing expertise in the concerned language. All documents must be sent as email attachments to [email protected].
