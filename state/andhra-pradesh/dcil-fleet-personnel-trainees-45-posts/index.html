How to Apply

  1. 1Visit the official DCI website at https://www.dredge-india.com/career.
  2. 2Download the prescribed Bio-Data format available on the career page.
  3. 3Fill in the Bio-Data form completely - include post applied for, date of birth, qualifications, CDC/Passport/INDOS details, and full experience history.
  4. 4Attach self-attested photocopies of all required certificates (COC, CDC, Passport, INDOS, STCW 2010, Mark Sheets, Caste Certificate as applicable).
  5. 5Send the soft copy of your Bio-Data and all documents to [email protected] on or before 17th June 2026 by 15:00 hours.
  6. 6Senior fleet ranks unable to attend in person (Dredge Master, Chief Officer, Chief Engineer Officer, 2nd Engineer Officer) - mention "Request for attending Interview through VC Mode" in the subject line of your email.
  7. 7Appear at the walk-in interview venue with original documents and one set of self-attested photocopies on the scheduled date.
  8. 8Candidates currently on board (internal floating staff) who cannot sign off must attend via VC mode with the recommendation of the Master concerned.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the last date to apply for DCI Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to send soft copy of Bio-Data and documents to [email protected] is 17th June 2026 by 15:00 hours. Applications received after this deadline will not be considered.
Q2 When is the DCI Recruitment 2026 Walk-In Interview?
A
The walk-in interviews are scheduled on 23rd June 2026 (Fleet Personnel & Trainees ERR/SHM/Cook), 24th June 2026 (TME & TELO), and 25th June 2026 (NCV(T)) — all from 10:00 AM at Dredge House, Visakhapatnam.
Q3 How many vacancies are there in DCI Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 45 confirmed trainee vacancies — 15 each for TME, TELO, and NCV(T). Fleet Personnel vacancies are based on operational requirements and are not fixed.
Q4 What is the stipend for Trainee Marine Engineer in DCI 2026?
A
Trainee Marine Engineers will receive a stipend of Rs. 25,000/- per month plus Rs. 600/- victualing allowance per day during the training period.
Q5 Can I attend the DCI interview via video call (VC Mode)?
A
Yes. Senior fleet ranks — Dredge Master, Inland Master, Chief Officer, 2nd Officer, Chief Engineer Officer, and 2nd Engineer Officer — who are unable to attend in person can request a VC Mode interview by mentioning it in the subject line of their email to [email protected] before 17th June 2026.
Q6 What is the age limit for NCV Trainee in DCI Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates applying for NCV Trainee (NCV(T)) must be less than 25 years of age as on the date of joining onboard.
Q7 Is DCI Recruitment 2026 a permanent job?
A
No. All vacancies under DCI Recruitment 2026 both fleet personnel and trainees are of a purely temporary contractual nature and do not confer any right to permanent or regular employment.
Q8 What is the minimum qualifying score for Fleet Personnel selection?
A
The minimum qualifying score for Fleet Personnel selection through interview is 50 marks out of 100.
Q9 Do I need to apply online for DCI Walk-In Interview 2026?
A
No. Candidates attending the walk-in interview under Advertisement No. 02/2026 need not apply online. Simply carry the prescribed Bio-Data form along with original and self-attested copies of all documents.
Q10 Where is the DCI Walk-In Interview 2026 venue?
A
The interview will be held at DCIL, Dredge House, H.B. Colony, Seethammadhara, Visakhapatnam – 530022, Andhra Pradesh.
