About Tripura Government Jobs 2026

Tripura Sarkari Naukri 2026 ke liye TPSC (Tripura Public Service Commission) aur TRPB pramukh recruitment bodies hain. Agartala, Dharmanagar, Udaipur, Kailashahar, Belonia aur Sabroom ke candidates Tripura Government Jobs 2026 ki taiyari mein hain.

TRTC, TSECL aur Tripura Tribal Welfare Department important recruitment bodies hain. Tripura Police aur TRPB ke through constable posts nikalte hain. AGMC Agartala aur Tripura NHM ke under health posts aate hain. TBSE aur Tripura School Education ke through teacher recruitment hoti hai. Tripura University, MBB University aur ICFAI University Tripura mein bhi academic posts available hain 2026 mein.

Tripura Recruitment 2026 mein 10th, 12th, ITI, Diploma aur Graduate sabhi ke liye avsar hain. Sepahijala, Gomati, Unakoti, Khowai ke candidates bhi online form se apply kar sakte hain.
