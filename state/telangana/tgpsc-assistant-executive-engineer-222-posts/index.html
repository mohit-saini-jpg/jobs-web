Selection Process

1Written Examination – Objective Type (OMR / CBRT mode) + Medical Examination
2Candidates who do not hold a Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering (B.E./B.Tech Civil) from a UGC/AICTE recognized University/Institution as on 02/06/2026. Other engineering branches are not eligible.
3Candidates whose Civil Engineering degree result had not been declared by the concerned University as on 02/06/2026, even if the examination was conducted or result processing was in progress.
4Candidates whose Civil Engineering degree was obtained through Open University / Distance Education mode without UGC / AICTE / Distance Education Bureau recognition.
5Candidates born after 01/07/2008 (below 18 years as on 01/07/2026).
6Candidates born before 02/07/1982 (above 44 years as on 01/07/2026 for OC/General category) unless eligible for specific age relaxations, and even then, no one who reaches superannuation age after relaxation is eligible.
7Candidates belonging to other States who are seeking reservation benefits; inter-state candidates are not entitled to any reservation under Telangana rules.
8OBC candidates from the Creamy Layer applying under BC reservation; only Non-Creamy Layer BC candidates with a valid NCL certificate from a Telangana Tahsildar are entitled to BC reservation.
9Candidates who are Government employees of Corporations, Municipalities etc. seeking age relaxation under the "Government Employee" category; only regular Telangana State Government employees are eligible for that relaxation.
10Candidates who do not have a valid TGPSC OTR (One Time Registration); OTR is mandatory before the application can be submitted.
11Candidates who submit applications through any mode other than the prescribed online mode on the TGPSC website.
12Not completing OTR (One Time Registration) before applying; the application cannot be submitted without a valid TGPSC ID generated through OTR.
13Failing to correctly update PH/Disability status in OTR; if PH status is not updated in OTR, the applicant will not be considered for PH vacancies even if eligible.
14Not verifying that the result of the qualifying examination was declared by the University on or before 02/06/2026; "appeared" or "awaiting result" does not satisfy the educational qualification requirement.
15Applying for the wrong Multi Zone (Zone-I or Zone-II) without verifying local candidate eligibility based on study/residence history; local reservation (95%) is applied zone-wise.
16Uploading a photograph older than 3 months before the last date of application, or uploading in incorrect file size (Photo: 100kb–200kb; Signature: 20kb–30kb) or format (JPG/PNG only).
17BC candidates failing to obtain a fresh Non-Creamy Layer (NCL) certificate in the prescribed Form-VIIB from a Telangana Tahsildar/RDO in the name of the applicant with Father's/Mother's name; old or improperly issued NCL certificates will result in candidature being treated as OC/General.
18Claiming exemption from Examination Fee (Rs.120/-) as "unemployed" without having a valid declaration; employed candidates in any Government/PSU/Corporations must pay the examination fee.
19Not clicking the "CONFIRM & SUBMIT" button after filling and previewing the application; unless this button is clicked, application particulars will not be saved in the database.
20Not noting down the Reference ID Number from the PDF application form for future correspondence, and not downloading/saving a copy of the submitted PDF application.
21Bringing electronic gadgets (mobile phones, smartwatches, calculators, Bluetooth devices, pen drives, tablets, etc.) to the examination centre; these are strictly banned and will attract penal action including imprisonment of 3 to 7 years and fine of Rs.5,000 to Rs.1,00,000 under Act No.25/1997.

How to Apply

  1. 1Step I: Register for OTR at https://www.tgpsc.gov.in (if not already registered) to obtain TGPSC ID. Verify all bio-data details carefully.
  2. 2Step II: Login using TGPSC ID and Date of Birth, enter OTP received on registered mobile, verify pre-filled details from OTR (Name, DOB, Qualifications, Community, Gender, Disability etc.), and select examination centre.
  3. 3Step III: Preview the application for correctness, then click "SAVE & PAYMENT" to proceed to online fee payment via Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card or UPI.
  4. 4Step IV: After fee payment, a PDF application will be generated. Download and save the PDF. Note the Reference ID Number for future correspondence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026?
A
TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026 is the Telangana Public Service Commission notification for 222 Assistant Executive Engineer (Civil) posts in the Transport, Roads and Buildings Department.
Q2 How many vacancies are available in TGPSC AEE Civil 2026?
A
A total of 222 vacancies are available, distributed across Multi Zone-I (112) and Multi Zone-II (110) with reservations for various categories including SC, ST, BC, EWS, and PwBD.
Q3 What is the eligibility for TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must have a Bachelor’s Degree in Civil Engineering (B.E./B.Tech) from a recognized Indian University or institution recognized by UGC/AICTE or AMIE.
Q4 What is the age limit for TGPSC AEE Civil 2026?
A
The age limit is 18 to 44 years as of 01/07/2026 for OC candidates, with relaxations for SC/ST/BC, PwD, Ex-Servicemen, NCC instructors, and Telangana State Government employees as per rules.
Q5 What is the TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026 selection process?
A
Selection includes a Written Examination (Objective Type – Paper I: General Studies & Aptitude, Paper II: Civil Engineering), followed by Document Verification and Medical Examination.
Q6 How can I apply for TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must register for One Time Registration (OTR) on the TGPSC website, then apply online with the TGPSC ID and upload required documents before the last date.
Q7 What is the application fee for TGPSC AEE Civil 2026?
A
The application processing fee is ₹1,000 for OC & BC candidates and ₹500 for SC/ST/PwD candidates. The examination fee is ₹120 for employed candidates; unemployed applicants are exempted.
Q8 When will the TGPSC AEE Civil 2026 online application start and end?
A
The online application opens on 06-06-2026 and closes on 13-07-2026 at 5:00 PM. Candidates must complete OTR registration beforehand.
Q9 Where will the TGPSC AEE Civil exam be conducted?
A
Examination centres include Hyderabad, Karimnagar, Khammam, Warangal, and Nizamabad, with the exact venue details mentioned in the hall ticket.
Q10 Who should not apply for TGPSC AEE Civil 2026?
A
Candidates without a Civil Engineering degree, those whose result is pending, candidates from other engineering branches, underage/overage candidates, non-Telangana locals seeking reservation, and those without OTR registration should not apply.
