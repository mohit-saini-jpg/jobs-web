Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the last date to apply for RailTel KSITM Project Engineer Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for submission of the offline application for RailTel KSITM Project Engineer Recruitment 2026 is 22 June 2026. All applications must physically reach the office of the Territory Manager, RailTel Corporation of India Limited, located at First Floor, Eastern Entry Tower, Ernakulam Railway Station, Kochi – 682016 on or before 22.06.2026 during office working hours. Any applications received after this deadline will not be considered under any circumstances, so candidates are strongly advised to send their documents well in advance through reliable post or courier services to avoid any transit delays.
Q2 How many vacancies are available in RailTel KSITM Project Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 13 posts have been announced for the RailTel KSITM Project Recruitment 2026, which includes 09 posts for L-I Engineer, 03 posts for L-II Engineer, and 01 post for L-III Engineer. It is important to note that the number of vacancies is tentative and subject to change depending upon the actual project requirements and operational needs of RailTel. Candidates should regularly check the official website for any updates regarding revisions in the number of posts.
Q3 What is the salary/CTC for RailTel KSITM Project Engineer posts in 2026?
A
The annual Cost to Company (CTC) offered for the RailTel KSITM Project Engineer posts in 2026 is ₹4,20,000 for L-I Engineer, ₹5,58,000 for L-II Engineer, and ₹18,00,000 for L-III Engineer. All positions come with an annual increment of 3% and the CTC is inclusive of Gratuity. This compensation package reflects the contractual nature of the engagement and is designed to attract experienced professionals for the specialized roles in the project.
Q4 What is the contract period for RailTel KSITM Project Engineer posts?
A
The contract period for RailTel KSITM Project Engineer posts is initially for a period of 3 years from the date of joining. This contract may be extended further based on the requirements of the project and the satisfactory performance of the selected candidate. However, the contract can also be terminated earlier if there is non-availability of work, unsatisfactory performance, or due to any administrative reasons as decided by RailTel management.
Q5 What is the age limit for RailTel KSITM Project Recruitment 2026?
A
The prescribed age limit for RailTel KSITM Project Recruitment 2026 is a minimum of 24 years and a maximum of 50 years as on the cut-off date of 22.06.2026. The maximum age limit can go up to 55 years after considering all applicable relaxations. Relaxation in the upper age limit is provided as follows: 3 years for OBC candidates, 5 years for SC/ST candidates, and 10 years for PwBD candidates. Candidates must ensure they meet the age criteria as on the specified cut-off date.
Q6 What qualifications and certifications are required for RailTel L-III Engineer post?
A
For the RailTel L-III Engineer post, candidates must possess Graduation or Post Graduation in Science or Engineering along with a minimum of 8 years of experience in Security Operations Centre (SOC), which should include at least 3 years as SOC Manager. Additionally, two mandatory certifications are required — one in Cyber Security (CISSP or CISM or CISA — any one) and another in Incident and Program Management (ITIL or PMP — any one). These stringent requirements ensure that only highly experienced and certified professionals are selected for this senior-level role.
Q7 Is the RailTel KSITM Project recruitment a permanent government job?
A
No, the RailTel KSITM Project recruitment is not a permanent government job. The engagement is purely on a contractual basis and selected candidates will not be treated as regular employees of RailTel Corporation of India Limited. This is a fixed-term contractual position initially for 3 years which may be extended based on project needs and performance, but it does not carry the benefits or status associated with permanent government employment.
Q8 What documents are required to apply for RailTel KSITM Project Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates applying for RailTel KSITM Project Recruitment 2026 must attach self-attested copies of several important documents including Matriculation Certificate for age proof, all educational and professional qualification certificates, experience certificates or appointment letters supported by pay slips, caste/EWS/disability certificate (if applicable), a detailed CV with recent passport-size photograph, photo ID proof and address proof, and NOC from the present employer if currently working in Government or PSU. All original documents must be produced at the time of the interview for verification.
Q9 How to apply for RailTel KSITM Project Engineer Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply offline only for the RailTel KSITM Project Engineer Recruitment 2026. They need to download Annexure-I from the official website www.railtelindia.com, fill it carefully, sign it, affix a recent passport-size photograph, attach all required self-attested documents, and superscribe the envelope with the necessary details. The completed application should then be sent by post or courier so that it reaches the Territory Manager, RailTel Corporation of India Limited, First Floor, Eastern Entry Tower, Ernakulam Railway Station, Kochi – 682016 on or before the last date of 22.06.2026.
Q10 Will RailTel reimburse travel expenses for the interview?
A
RailTel will not pay or reimburse any travelling expenses to candidates for appearing in the screening process, interview, or medical examination. Candidates are required to attend all stages of the selection process at their own cost and arrangement. This is standard for this contractual recruitment drive, and no requests for reimbursement will be entertained by the organization.
