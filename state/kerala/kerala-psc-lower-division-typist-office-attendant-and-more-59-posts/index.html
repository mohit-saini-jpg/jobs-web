3Candidates will be advised from the Ranked List prepared in the order of merit.
4The Ranked List will remain in force for a minimum period of one year and a maximum period of three years.

6Final selection is subject to verification of original documents and medical fitness.

How to Apply

  1. 1Age proof - SSLC Book or equivalent certificate
  2. 2SSLC Certificate/Marksheet and Pass Certificate
  3. 3Government approved 2-year National/State Trade Certificate in Electrician/Wireman
  4. 4Community/Caste Certificate (SC/ST/OBC) if applicable
  5. 5Medical Certificate in prescribed format (from a Medical Officer not below the rank of Assistant Surgeon) to prove physical fitness and vision
  6. 6Gazette notification in case of difference in caste/community between application and SSLC book (along with Non-Creamy Layer/Community Certificate)
  7. 7AADHAAR Card (for candidates who possess one)
  8. 8Printout of online application
  9. 9Applications must be submitted online only through the KPSC One Time Registration portal - applications in any other manner will be summarily rejected.
  10. 10Candidates must apply for only one district; applying for more than one district will lead to rejection of all applications and disciplinary action.
  11. 11Candidates appointed in Idukki, Wayanad, or Kasargod districts will not be eligible for working arrangement, deputation, transfer, or mutual transfer for at least ten years, and promotion will also be limited to those districts.
  12. 12No application fee is required for this post.
  13. 13Candidates already in Government service holding the same post in any district are prohibited from applying again for this post.
  14. 144% of vacancies are reserved for Differently Abled candidates as per the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016.
  15. 15Candidates must submit confirmation for the examination through their OTR profile within the stipulated period - failure to do so will result in absolute rejection of the application.
  16. 16Admission Tickets will be available for download only to candidates who have submitted confirmation, 15 days prior to the examination date.
  17. 17Candidates with AADHAAR card must add it as Identity Proof in their profile.
  18. 18Profile corrections made after the last date of application will not be reflected in the application - such corrections take effect only from the date on which they are made.
  19. 19Candidates must ensure that the photograph uploaded in the profile has the candidate's name and date of photograph printed legibly at the bottom portion; photograph is valid for 10 years from the date of taking.
  20. 20Disciplinary action as per Rule 22 of Kerala Public Service Commission Rules of Procedure 1976 shall be initiated against candidates who submit applications with bogus claims of qualifications or other details.
  21. 21Candidates are advised to visit www.keralapsc.gov.in regularly for updates on all stages of the recruitment process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the last date to apply for Kerala PSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application process closes on 01 July 2026. Candidates must submit their applications before the deadline.
Q2 How many posts are available under Kerala PSC Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 59 posts are available, including positions like Lower Division Typist, Office Attendant, Assistant Manager, and others.
Q3 What is the age limit for applying to Kerala PSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The age limit varies by post, generally ranging from 18 to 45 years, with category-wise relaxations as per government rules.
Q4 What educational qualifications are required?
A
Qualifications depend on the post. Candidates must have Bachelors Degree, B.Sc, B.Tech/B.E, Diploma, 10th, M.A, M.Com, M.Sc, or MSW, depending on the position.
Q5 Is there an application fee for Kerala PSC Recruitment 2026?
A
No. All candidates can apply free of cost through the official KPSC One Time Registration portal.
Q6 How will the selection be conducted for Kerala PSC Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection may involve written/OMR/online examinations, followed by a merit-based ranked list. Final selection is subject to document verification and medical fitness.
Q7 Can candidates apply for more than one district?
A
No. Candidates must apply for only one district. Applications for multiple districts will be rejected, and disciplinary action may be taken.
Q8 What documents are required at the time of verification?
A
Candidates must produce age proof, educational certificates, caste/community certificate (if applicable), medical certificate, Aadhaar card, and printout of online application.
Q9 Are there any reservations under Kerala PSC Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. Reservation applies as per SC/ST/OBC/Differently Abled candidates norms. For example, 4% of vacancies are reserved for Differently Abled candidates.
Q10 How can candidates apply for Kerala PSC Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted online only via the official website: www.keralapsc.gov.in through the One Time Registration (OTR) system.
