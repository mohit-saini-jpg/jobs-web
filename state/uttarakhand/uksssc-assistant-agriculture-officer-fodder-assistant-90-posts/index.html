  22. 22Each Aadhaar number can be used only once for registration. Candidates must use their own mobile number and email ID in the online application.
  23. 23Note down your Registration Number after successful registration - it is required for all future login and processes.
  24. 24Information filled in the online application will be treated as final. No changes/modifications to name, mother's/father's name, age, or examination centre will be permitted after submission.

  32. 32All instructions given during security check at the examination centre must be followed mandatorily.
  33. 33OBC candidates from the Creamy Layer must apply under UR (Unreserved) category; they are not entitled to OBC reservation or relaxation.
  34. 34If a candidate claims reservation in more than one sub-category, they will be eligible for the benefit of only one sub-category - the most beneficial one - provided they submit valid proof for that category.
  35. 35All revisions/corrigenda (if any) will be hosted on the Commission's website only - candidates should visit www.sssc.uk.gov.in regularly.

  37. 37No request for change of examination centre shall be entertained. Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification.
  38. 38The Commission does not advise candidates regarding their eligibility. Candidates must read the advertisement carefully and apply only when fully satisfied that they meet all conditions.
