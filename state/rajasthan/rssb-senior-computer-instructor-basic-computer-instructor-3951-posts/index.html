How to Apply

  1. 1Visit https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in and log in with your SSO ID.
  2. 2Under Citizen Apps (G2C), select the Recruitment Portal.
  3. 3If you have not yet completed OTR (One Time Registration), go to the OTR tab, select your category (Unreserved or Reserved), enter your disability and home state details, and pay the applicable OTR fee.
  4. 4After OTR is complete, click Apply Now for Advertisement No. 07/2026.
  5. 5In the OTR Dashboard, carefully select your educational qualification in the Document Details section.
  6. 6Upload required documents including educational certificates, category certificate, and sports certificate (if applicable under Outstanding Player quota) - all as per the formats and specifications published on the Board's website.
  7. 7Fill in your department preference order (Secondary Education / Sanskrit Education) in the designated column. This cannot be changed later.
  8. 8Enter your visible body mark (mandatory field).
  9. 9Provide your own mobile number and email ID - all important communications will be sent to these contacts. Do not change them until the recruitment process is complete.
  10. 10Review the complete application using the Checklist option under "My Recruitment" section before final submission.
  11. 11Click Final Submit. An Application ID will be generated - save it for future reference.
  12. 12Take a printout of the submitted application form and keep it safely.
  13. 13The live photo must be taken against a white or light-coloured background with the face covering at least 50% of the frame.
  14. 14Head, eyes, nose, and chin must be clearly visible. No dark or tinted glasses; no cap or cloth covering the face.
  15. 15The photo must not be blurred or dark.
  16. 16The signature must be signed in a rectangular box of 7 cm × 2 cm on white A4 paper using a black or dark blue pen, then scanned and uploaded in JPEG format (size: 20 KB to 50 KB; minimum 280×80 pixels, maximum 560×160 pixels).
  17. 17The left thumb impression must also be uploaded separately after the signature (right thumb if left is missing).
  18. 18Mobile phone photographs of signatures are not accepted.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the total number of vacancies in RSSB Computer Instructor Recruitment 20
A
There are 3,951 total vacancies - 322 for Senior Computer Instructor and 3,629 for Basic Computer Instructor — spread across the Secondary Education Department and the Sanskrit Education Department of Rajasthan.
Q2 What is the last date to apply online for RSSB Computer Instructor 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 23 June 2026 at 11:59 PM through the SSO Portal at sso.rajasthan.gov.in. Applications submitted after this deadline will not be accepted under any circumstances.
Q3 What is the educational qualification required for Senior Computer Instructor?
A
Candidates must hold an ME/M.Tech or M.Sc. in Computer Science/IT or a Master in Computer Application (MCA) or 'B'/'C' Level certificate, or any equivalent qualification recognised by the Government, along with working knowledge of Hindi in Devanagari script and Rajasthani culture.
Q4 What is the qualification needed for Basic Computer Instructor?
A
A graduate degree with 'A' Level/PGDCA, or a BE/B.Tech in CS/IT/ECE/EE, or B.Sc. in CS/IT, or BCA from a recognised Indian university is required, along with knowledge of Hindi written in Devanagari script and Rajasthani culture.
Q5 What is the salary for Senior and Basic Computer Instructor posts?
A
The Senior Computer Instructor post carries Pay Matrix Level 10 and the Basic Computer Instructor post carries Pay Matrix Level 8 as per the 7th Pay Commission. During the two-year probation period, only a fixed monthly remuneration is payable - no additional allowances.
Q6 When is the written examination scheduled for RSSB Computer Instructor 2026?
A
The exam for Senior Computer Instructor is proposed on 22 August 2026 and for Basic Computer Instructor on 23 August 2026. The Board reserves the right to change these dates; the final schedule will be notified on rssb.rajasthan.gov.in.
Q7 How is the RSSB Computer Instructor exam structured?
A
The exam has two papers, each of 100 marks and 2 hours duration, totalling 200 marks. Paper I covers Rajasthan GK and General Ability. Paper II covers subject-specific computer topics. Negative marking of 1/3 mark applies for each wrong answer. Each question has 5 options (A to E), and unattempted questions must have option E darkened.
Q8 What is the application fee for General and Reserved category candidates?
A
General category and creamy layer OBC/MBC candidates must pay an OTR fee of ₹600. Rajasthan-domiciled non-creamy layer OBC/MBC, EWS, SC, ST candidates, and all Divyang applicants pay ₹400. Candidates who have previously paid the OTR fee need not pay again.
Q9 Is there any age relaxation for OBC and SC/ST candidates in this recruitment?
A
Yes. SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS male candidates who are Rajasthan domicile get 5 years of upper age relaxation. Women candidates from these categories (Rajasthan domicile) get 10 years. For the Secondary Education Department posts only, all candidates eligible during the 2022 recruitment cycle receive an additional 3 years of relaxation.
Q10 Can candidates from other states apply under reserved category benefits?
A
No. Candidates originally from states other than Rajasthan who belong to SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS categories are treated as General category for this recruitment. They must pay the General category OTR fee and cannot claim reserved category benefits.
