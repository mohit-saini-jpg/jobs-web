How To Apply
1Candidates must apply online only at https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in. Before applying, candidates must first complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the SSO Portal at https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in using a Citizen Apps (G2C) login and access the Recruitment Portal. During OTR, candidates must upload a live/recent photo, provide Aadhaar/Janaadhar details, and fill all personal details (Name, Father's Name, DOB, Gender) accurately as per Aadhaar — these cannot be changed post-OTR without Aadhaar correction. Candidates who have already completed OTR can directly log in with their SSO ID and apply using their OTR Number/Registration Number. Candidates are strongly advised to read the detailed advertisement and Instructions for Applicants available on the RPSC website before filling the online application form.
