How to Apply

  1. 1Visit the official RPSC website: https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in and read the complete notification and Instructions for Applicants before applying.
  2. 2Click on the Apply Online link or log in to the SSO portal at https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in using your SSO ID to access the Recruitment Portal (G2C portal).
  3. 3If you are a new user, complete One Time Registration (OTR) by providing your name, father's name (if available from Aadhaar), date of birth, gender, and Aadhaar card / Janaadhar card details. Upload a live self-photo and scan of OTR documents.
  4. 4After completing OTR, log in from SSO portal → Citizen Apps (G2C) → Recruitment Portal and fill the online application form using your OTR number.
  5. 5Important: No amendment to name, father's name, date of birth, or gender can be made in OTR Profile once created without Aadhaar/Janaadhar correction. Ensure all Aadhaar/Janaadhar details are corrected before completing OTR.
  6. 6During online application, a Live Photo will be captured via OTP camera. Submit the form only after verifying the live photo is clear.
  7. 7Candidates must scan and upload thumb impression (left hand) and signature along with the online application.
  8. 8Candidates who wish to avail scribe facility must mandatorily select the scribe option in their online application form.
  9. 9All educational qualification and experience details as at the application closing date must be clearly entered in the OTR/application form.
  10. 10After submission, an Application Number will be generated. If no Application Number is received, it means the form has not been submitted successfully.
  11. 11Candidates must take a printout of the finally submitted application form for future reference.
  12. 12For any technical issues during online application, contact the Helpdesk number on the Recruitment Portal or email as indicated on the portal.
  13. 13Call Letter (Admit Card) downloaded from RPSC website along with valid Photo ID proof (Aadhaar / PAN Card / Passport / Driving License / Voter ID / Bank Passbook with photo) in original and self-attested photocopy.
  14. 14Caste Certificate in prescribed Government of India / Government of Rajasthan format (for SC/ST/OBC/MBC candidates).
  15. 15OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in prescribed format issued for the current year (for OBC NCL candidates).
  16. 16Income and Asset Certificate issued by Competent Authority in prescribed format (for EWS candidates).
  17. 17Disability Certificate in prescribed format issued by Competent Authority (for PwBD candidates).
  18. 18Educational qualification certificates and marksheets (graduation / Law degree onwards).
  19. 19Domicile certificate / Residence proof (for reserved category benefits applicable to Rajasthan domiciles).
  20. 20Character Certificate from the last attended educational institution (at least "Good" mention required).
  21. 21Police verification / antecedent certificate at the time of appointment.
  22. 22Medical fitness certificate from a competent authority at the time of appointment.
  23. 23NOC from current employer if already in Government / PSU service.
  24. 24EWS / widowhood / divorce proof documents as applicable (court decree / death certificate).
  25. 25Ex-Serviceman discharge certificate / NOC as applicable.
  26. 26UIDAI / Unique Disability ID (U-D.I.D.) card, if available, must be produced before the Preliminary Examination.
  27. 27Submitting false/incorrect information in the application form or failing to meet eligibility conditions is a punishable offence under Section 217 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023 (BNS). Such candidates may be debarred from appearing in the examination or disqualified during counselling/selection/appointment.
  28. 28All revisions, corrigenda, and updates will be hosted on the RPSC official website only - candidates should visit regularly.
  29. 29Candidates must not change their registered mobile number or email ID. Changes, if necessary, should be informed to RPSC at the earliest.
  30. 30Candidates must apply under the correct category. Applying under a wrong category will not entitle them to any category benefit; the application may be treated as General category.
  31. 31Candidates from other states belonging to SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS will be treated as General (UR) for this recruitment as reservation benefits are applicable only to Rajasthan domiciles.
  32. 32OBC candidates from Rajasthan belonging to the Creamy Layer must apply as General (UR). Only Non-Creamy Layer OBC candidates with a valid and recent certificate are eligible for OBC reservation.
  33. 33Category once filled in the online application form cannot be changed after the application submission deadline.
  34. 34Not more than one application should be submitted. In case of multiple applications, only the last valid completed application will be retained.
  35. 35PwBD candidates with temporary/likely-to-improve disabilities are not eligible for PwBD reservation benefits.
  36. 36Candidates already in Central / State / PSU Government service must produce a NOC from their employer at the time of appointment, or an NOC obtained before joining.
  37. 37Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification.
  38. 38No request for change of examination centre shall be entertained. Travel expenses will not be reimbursed by RPSC.
  39. 39The Commission reserves the right to cancel the selection process partly or entirely at any stage.
  40. 40Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be as per Hon'ble Rajasthan High Court, Jaipur / Jodhpur as applicable.
  41. 41Age relaxation provisions in Hindi language shall prevail over English translation in case of any discrepancy.
  42. 42Candidates who are provisionally selected will be given actual appointment only after document verification and medical fitness confirmation. Provisional admission to examination does not guarantee final appointment.
  43. 43Mobile phones, smartwatches, pagers, earphones, or any other electronic/communication devices are not allowed inside the examination premises.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the last date to apply for RPSC APO Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to apply online for RPSC Assistant Prosecution Officer Recruitment 2026 is 07 July 2026. Candidates must complete the application process before midnight to avoid last-minute technical issues. It is advised to apply early and verify all details before final submission.
Q2 What is the educational qualification required for RPSC APO 2026?
A
Applicants must possess a Bachelor’s Degree in Law (LLB Professional) from a recognized university in India. The qualification must be completed before the last date of application submission. Candidates who are in the final year of their law degree are not eligible to apply.
Q3 What is the age limit for RPSC Assistant Prosecution Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age required is 21 years and the maximum age is 40 years as on 01 January 2027. Age relaxation is provided to reserved categories such as SC, ST, OBC, EWS, and others as per Rajasthan government norms. Candidates must check category-wise relaxation carefully.
Q4 How can I apply online for RPSC APO Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online through the official RPSC website or Rajasthan SSO portal. Before filling the application form, One Time Registration (OTR) is mandatory. Ensure all required documents are uploaded correctly and details match official records.
Q5 What is the application fee for RPSC APO 2026?
A
The application fee for General, BC, and MBC (Creamy Layer) candidates is Rs. 600. For SC, ST, OBC (Non-Creamy Layer), EWS, and PwBD candidates, the fee is Rs. 400. The fee must be paid online and is non-refundable under any circumstances.
Q6 What is the selection process for RPSC Assistant Prosecution Officer 2026?
A
The selection process consists of two stages: Preliminary Examination and Main Written Examination. The prelim exam is qualifying in nature and does not count toward final merit. Final selection is based on the marks obtained in the main examination.
Q7 What subjects are included in the RPSC APO syllabus 2026?
A
The syllabus mainly focuses on criminal law subjects such as IPC, Evidence Act, CrPC, BNS, BNSS, and BSA. It also includes important acts like POCSO and SC/ST Act. Additionally, candidates are tested on General Hindi and English language skills.
Q8 Is there negative marking in RPSC APO Preliminary Examination 2026?
A
Yes, there is negative marking in the preliminary examination as per RPSC rules. Candidates should answer questions carefully to avoid losing marks for incorrect answers. Accuracy along with speed is important to qualify this stage.
Q9 What is the salary of RPSC Assistant Prosecution Officer?
A
Selected candidates will be placed under Pay Matrix Level L-11 with Grade Pay of Rs. 4200. During the probation period, fixed pay is provided as per Rajasthan government rules. After confirmation, additional allowances and benefits are applicable.
Q10 Is One Time Registration (OTR) mandatory for RPSC APO Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, One Time Registration (OTR) is compulsory for all candidates applying through the SSO portal. Without OTR, candidates cannot proceed with the application form. It is important to ensure that OTR details are accurate as they cannot be easily modified later.
