Selection Process

1Written Examination (Objective Type) + Merit-based selection; Screening / Moderation if required
2Candidates who do not hold the specified M.Sc. / M.Tech. / M.E. degree with at least Second Division (IInd Division) in the relevant subject for the post they are applying for.
3Candidates who do not have at least 2 years of experience of working in a FSL (State/Central Government) or equivalent public undertaking/corporation after attainment of educational qualification.
4Candidates without a Certificate/Diploma/Degree in Computer Science or Computer Application (or 'O' level / higher NIELIT certification) - this is a mandatory part of the educational qualification.
5Candidates above 40 years of age as on 01-01-2027 (for UR/General candidates) who do not qualify for any applicable age relaxation.
6Candidates whose M.Sc. degree is in a subject other than what is specified for each respective Division (e.g., a Chemistry M.Sc. holder should NOT apply for the Ballistics Division post).
7Candidates who obtained their degree from an institution not established by law in India or not recognized by the Government.
8Candidates from other states applying under reserved categories (SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS) - such candidates are treated as General/UR for fee and reservation purposes.
9Candidates with pending vigilance/disciplinary cases or adverse RBI/Statutory Authority observations against them.
10Candidates who have not completed One Time Registration (OTR) on the SSO Portal - this is a mandatory prerequisite for applying.
11Applying for the wrong Division post (e.g., applying for Physics Division with a Chemistry background) - ensure your M.Sc. subject matches the specific post requirements exactly.
12Not completing OTR (One Time Registration) on the SSO Portal before applying - without OTR, the online application cannot be submitted.
13Entering incorrect details in OTR (name, father's name, date of birth, gender) - these cannot be changed in the OTR and a mismatch with Aadhaar/documents can lead to disqualification.
14Uploading blurred or incorrectly sized photograph or signature - causing rejection at the scrutiny stage.
15Not noting the Application Form Number (Application No.) after submission - if this is not received, it means the application was not successfully submitted.
16Applying under the wrong category (e.g., OBC Creamy Layer applying as OBC Non-Creamy Layer) - category change is not permitted after submission and mismatch leads to cancellation.
17Candidates from outside Rajasthan claiming SC/ST/OBC/EWS reservation - out-of-state candidates are treated as General/UR for all purposes.
18Submitting the online application after the deadline of 10-07-2026 midnight - the portal will close exactly at 12:00 midnight and no extension will be granted.
19Not keeping the registered mobile number and email ID active - call letters, interview notifications, and all further communication will be sent only to these registered contacts.
20Ignoring negative marking: one-third marks are deducted per wrong answer and one-third are deducted if no circle is darkened - over-attempting without knowing the answer can lower your score.

How to Apply

  1. 1Recent photograph (as per RPSC guidelines - to be captured live via webcam/mobile at the time of online application)
  2. 2Left thumb impression scanned and uploaded (mandatory; also required at examination centre)
  3. 3Aadhaar Card / Janadhaar Card for identity verification (mandatory before applying)
  4. 4Admit Card / Call Letter (downloaded from RPSC website) along with valid photo identity proof in original
  5. 5Caste Certificate in prescribed Government format (for SC/ST candidates) - issued by competent authority
  6. 6OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in prescribed format issued before the last date of application (for OBC candidates)
  7. 7Income and Assets Certificate (for EWS candidates) - issued in prescribed format by competent authority
  8. 8Disability Certificate in prescribed format (for PwBD candidates) as per RPwD Act, 2016
  9. 9Educational qualification certificates and marksheets (10th onwards, including M.Sc./M.Tech./M.E. and Computer Science qualification)
  10. 10Experience Certificate in prescribed proforma (as per Annexure available in notification) - certifying at least 2 years of FSL/relevant experience after attainment of educational qualification
  11. 11Last month's salary slip / proof of current employment (if applicable)
  12. 12Character / Antecedent verification certificate from competent authority
  13. 13Medical fitness certificate from competent authority at the time of appointment
  14. 14Candidates must complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the SSO Portal before applying; no application can be submitted without a valid OTR.
  15. 15The detailed advertisement and Instructions for Applicants on the RPSC website (https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in) are part of this notification - candidates must read them before applying.
  16. 16Candidates must apply separately for each post they wish to apply for - a separate online application must be submitted for each post.
  17. 17Candidates belonging to OBC Creamy Layer from Rajasthan and candidates from other states' SC/ST/OBC categories must apply as General/UR candidates.
  18. 18The online application must be submitted on or before 10-07-2026 (up to 12:00 midnight). No offline or physical application will be accepted under any circumstances.
  19. 19After submitting the online application, an Application Form Number (Application No.) will be generated - if this is not received, the application has not been successfully submitted.
  20. 20Candidates must ensure that all educational qualification and experience required as per this advertisement is obtained on or before the last date of online application.
  21. 21Online application Amendment (correction) is possible on payment of Rs. 500/- within 10 days after the last date of application, through the RPSC website; after this, no amendment will be accepted.
  22. 22One Time Registration (OTR) details - name, father's name, date of birth, gender - cannot be amended in the online application; any discrepancy with Aadhaar/Janadhaar must be corrected in the OTR before applying.
  23. 23Candidates with pending disciplinary/vigilance cases or adverse RBI/Statutory Authority observations are not eligible for appointment.
  24. 24PwBD candidates eligible for scribe facility: candidates with 40% or more physical disability having limitation in writing are eligible; scribe facility and compensatory time will be provided as per RPwD Act, 2016 and RPSC guidelines. Candidates must apply for scribe facility at least two days before the examination date.
  25. 25Mobile phones, smartwatches, or any electronic/communication devices are not allowed inside the examination hall.
  26. 26All revisions/corrigendum (if any) will be hosted on the RPSC website (https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in) only - candidates should visit regularly.
  27. 27Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification.
  28. 28Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be Rajasthan only.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the RPSC Senior Scientific Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has announced 03 vacancies for Senior Scientific Officer posts in Toxicology, Physics, and Ballistics Divisions of the State Forensic Science Laboratory, Jaipur. Candidates can apply online from 11 June to 10 July 2026
Q2 How many vacancies are available in RPSC SSO 2026?
A
A total of 03 vacancies are available: 1 for Toxicology Division (ST), 1 for Physics Division (UR), and 1 for Ballistics Division (OBC)
Q3 What is the eligibility criteria for RPSC Senior Scientific Officer 2026?
A
Candidates must have an M.Sc./M.Tech./M.E. in relevant disciplines with at least Second Division, along with 2 years’ experience in a State or Central Government FSL. Computer Science qualification and working knowledge of Hindi & Rajasthan culture are mandatory
Q4 What is the age limit for applying to RPSC SSO 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 21 and 40 years as of 01 January 2027. Age relaxations apply for reserved categories, PwBD, ex-servicemen, and women under specific conditions
Q5 How can I apply for RPSC Senior Scientific Officer 2026?
A
Applications are accepted online only through the RPSC website or SSO Portal after completing One Time Registration (OTR). Offline applications are not accepted
Q6 What is the application fee for RPSC SSO Recruitment 2026?
A
General, OBC (Creamy Layer), and candidates from other states: ₹600. Reserved category candidates from Rajasthan & PwBD: ₹400. Fees are non-refundable and must be paid online
Q7 What is the selection process for RPSC SSO 2026?
A
Selection will be based on an Online Written Examination (objective type) followed by merit-based selection and document verification. Negative marking applies for wrong or unattempted answers
Q8 What documents are required for RPSC SSO 2026 application?
A
Candidates must upload a recent photograph, left thumb impression, Aadhaar/Janadhaar, educational qualification certificates, computer science certification, and experience certificates during online application. Originals must be produced during verification
Q9 Can candidates from other states apply under reserved categories for RPSC SSO 2026?
A
No, candidates from other states applying under SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS categories are treated as General/UR for fee, eligibility, and reservation purposes
Q10 Where can I download the RPSC Senior Scientific Officer 2026 official notification?
A
The official notification PDF is available on the RPSC website: https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in and candidates should read it carefully before applying
