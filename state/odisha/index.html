About Odisha Government Jobs 2026

Odisha Sarkari Naukri 2026 ke liye OPSC (Odisha Public Service Commission) sabse important recruitment body hai jo OAS, OPS aur other state civil services ke liye exam conduct karta hai. Bhubaneswar, Cuttack, Rourkela, Berhampur, Sambalpur aur Puri jaise cities mein candidates badi sankhya mein Odisha Government Jobs 2026 ki taiyari mein hain.

OSRTC, GRIDCO, Odisha Mining Corporation aur NALCO pramukh recruitment bodies hain. Odisha Police aur OSSC ke through constable aur other posts nikalte hain. OHRC aur AIIMS Bhubaneswar ke under health department mein medical posts aate hain. BSE Odisha aur CHSE Odisha ke through teacher recruitment hoti hai. Utkal University, Berhampur University aur Sambalpur University mein bhi academic posts available hain 2026 mein.

Odisha Recruitment 2026 mein 10th, 12th, ITI, Diploma aur Graduate sabhi ke liye avsar hain. Koraput, Sundargarh, Keonjhar, Ganjam ke candidates bhi online form se apply kar sakte hain.

Latest Government Jobs in Odisha

Latest Odisha Government Jobs mein OPSC, OSSC, Odisha Police aur NHM ki vacancies open hain. Online form, admit card aur result Top Sarkari Jobs par milenge. Odisha Sarkari Naukri 2026 ka koi update miss mat karo.

Qualification Wise Jobs in Odisha

Odisha mein 10th pass ke liye Group D aur constable, 12th pass ke liye clerk aur assistant posts hain. ITI holders GRIDCO mein, Diploma candidates PWD mein apply kar sakte hain. Graduate aur PG candidates OPSC OAS aur teacher posts ke liye eligible hain.

Department Wise Recruitment in Odisha

Odisha Police OSSC, OHRC health bharti, BSE Odisha teacher recruitment aur GRIDCO technical posts — yeh Odisha ke top hiring departments hain 2026 mein. OPSC calendar follow karo aur Top Sarkari Jobs par Odisha Recruitment 2026 ka full update paao.
