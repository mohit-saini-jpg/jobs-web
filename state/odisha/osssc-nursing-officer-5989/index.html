How to Apply

  1. 1Visit the OSSSC official website at www.osssc.gov.in.
  2. 2On the Home page, click "Apply Online" if you are a New User (not registered earlier for any OSSSC recruitment). If you are a Registered User (already registered for any earlier OSSSC post and have a User ID), click the "Re-registration" option under the Applicant Menu to re-register for this post.
  3. 3Read the instructions carefully by clicking "How do I register/re-register/apply" on the Home page before filling the Registration Form.
  4. 4Complete the Registration Form first. Keep the following ready before starting: scanned signature in jpg/jpeg/png format between 20 KB and 50 KB; scanned colour passport-size photograph in jpg/jpeg/png format between 20 KB and 100 KB; valid personal email ID and mobile/cell phone number (to be kept active throughout the recruitment process).
  5. 5After successful registration, log in and fill the Online Application Form with all required details and upload documents as per the guidance of the online system.
  6. 6Indicate district preferences (up to the districts available) in order of priority.
  7. 7Pay the examination fee of ₹500 (if applicable) using Net Banking/Credit Card/Debit Card/UPI through the Payment Gateway. After successful payment, print the Confirmation Page.
  8. 8Submit the online application. Incomplete applications will be summarily rejected. No Admission Letter will be issued to candidates whose application is found incomplete.
  9. 9Keep the User ID, Password, and registration number safely - these will be required for downloading the Admission Letter and future communications from OSSSC.
  10. 10Certificate of Registration in the Nursing Council of the State of Odisha (valid as on the date of online application submission).
  11. 11HSC examination certificate or equivalent, 10+2 pass certificate or equivalent, Diploma in GNM or BSc Nursing certificate and mark sheets.
  12. 12Certificate of passing Odia as a language subject (Class-VII or above from a recognised school/institution) or certificate of passing a test in Odia in M.E. School Standard.
  13. 13Caste certificate (SC/ST/SEBC), or UDID card/Disability Certificate showing permanent disability issued by the Competent Authority (for PwDs).
  14. 14Discharge Certificate/Identity Card and documents in support of defence service rendered, along with an undertaking of non-availing of re-employment under State Government (for Ex-Servicemen).
  15. 15Certificate/documents of contractual work experience under OSH&FW Society/Scheme or as ASHA (for contractual Nursing Officers claiming age relaxation).
  16. 16Sports ID card issued by the Sports & Youth Services Department, Government of Odisha (for Sportsperson category claimants).

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the last date to apply for OSSSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for OSSSC Nursing Officer 2026 is 13 July 2026. The online system will be automatically disabled after this date and no application will be accepted thereafter.
Q2 How many vacancies are there in OSSSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
The OSSSC has notified 5989 vacancies for the post of Nursing Officer across all 30 districts of Odisha under the Health & Family Welfare Department.
Q3 What is the educational qualification required for OSSSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
Candidates must have passed 10+2, hold a Diploma in GNM or BSc Nursing from a recognised institution approved by the Indian Nursing Council, and possess a valid Registration or Renewal Certificate from the Nursing Council of Odisha as on the date of submission of the online application form.
Q4 What is the age limit for OSSSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 21 and 42 years of age as on the date of advertisement (12 June 2026). SC/ST/SEBC/Women candidates get a 5-year relaxation (upper limit becomes 47 years), PwD candidates get a 10-year relaxation (upper limit becomes 52 years), and contractual Nursing Officers under OSH&FW Society/Scheme and ASHAs get an upper limit of 45 years.
Q5 What is the application fee for OSSSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
The examination fee is ₹500 for all candidates except those belonging to SC, ST, and Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) categories, who are exempted from paying any fee. The fee can be paid via Net Banking, Credit Card, Debit Card, or UPI.
Q6 What is the pay scale for OSSSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
The Nursing Officer post carries a pay scale of ₹29,200–92,300 at Pay Matrix Level-8, Cell-01 as per Odisha Revised Scale of Pay (ORSP) Rules 2017. The starting basic pay is ₹29,200 per month.
Q7 What is the selection process for OSSSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
Selection is based on a Written Test of 100 marks (MCQ type, OMR/CBRE mode, 2 hours duration) followed by Document Verification and preparation of a Provisional Select List. There is no interview. Minimum qualifying marks are 30% for ST/SC/PwDs/Ex-Servicemen/Sportsperson and 35% for all others.
Q8 What is the exam pattern for OSSSC Nursing Officer Written Test?
A
The Written Test has 100 questions for 100 marks in 2 hours. The paper includes 60 questions from nursing syllabus subjects, 25 questions on practical skills, 10 questions on Arithmetic (High School level), and 5 questions on English (High School level). There is negative marking of 0.25 marks per wrong answer.
