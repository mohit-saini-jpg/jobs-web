How To Apply
  • 1Visit the HIMS recruitment portal at https://himsrecruitment.odisha.gov.in/ from 12.05.2026 onwards and access the online application link for Vikram Dev University faculty posts.
  • 2Register on the portal, then fill the online application form in all respects with correct personal, academic and experience details as per UGC and University requirements.
  • 3Upload all required supporting documents (certificates, mark sheets, experience, reservation documents, etc.) in the prescribed format along with the online form.
  • 4Pay the applicable non-refundable application processing fee online to the specified SBI bank account of Vikram Dev University and ensure proper entry of payment details.
  • 5Submit the online application form on or before 10.06.2026 (11:59 PM), ensuring it is complete in all respects.
  • 6Download and take a printout of the submitted application form from the portal.
  • 7Send the signed printout of the downloaded application along with all certificates, mark sheets and supporting documents by Registered/Speed Post only to: The Registrar, Vikram Dev University, Jeypore, Dist- Koraput, Odisha, PIN-764001 so as to reach on or before 20.06.2026 by 5.00 PM.
  • 8Keep proof of dispatch and payment details for future reference. For details and updates, regularly check the University website.
