Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the walk-in date for HAL NATS Apprenticeship 2026 at Bangalore?
A
There are three separate walk-in drives. Diploma holders must attend on 18 June 2026. Non-Engineering Graduates (BA/BSc/BCom/BBA/BCA) must attend on 19 June 2026. BE/BTech Engineering Graduates have stream-wise dates from 22 to 26 June 2026. Reporting time for all drives is 08:00 to 11:00 Hrs.
Q2 Where is the HAL NATS walk-in venue?
A
The walk-in is held at the Technical Training Institute, Suranjan Das Road, Bangalore – 560017. The contact numbers are 080-22322516 and 080-22323358.
Q3 What is the stipend for HAL NATS Apprenticeship 2026?
A
Diploma holders receive a stipend of Rs. 10,900/- per month. BE/BTech Engineering Graduates and Non-Engineering Graduates (BA/BSc/BCom/BBA/BCA) both receive Rs. 12,300/- per month. The training period is 12 months. Out of the total stipend, 50% is borne by the Government.
Q4 What is the eligibility for HAL NATS Apprenticeship 2026?
A
Candidates must hold a Diploma or Degree (BE/BTech or BA/BSc/BCom/BBA/BCA) in the relevant stream from a recognised university in India, obtained on or after 01 August 2021. Candidates with work experience of one or more years are not eligible. Candidates already registered or having completed a NATS contract elsewhere are also not eligible.
Q5 Is there any application fee for HAL NATS Apprenticeship 2026?
A
No application fee is mentioned in any of the three official notifications for this walk-in selection.
Q6 How is selection done for HAL NATS Apprenticeship 2026?
A
Selection is based on merit on a first-come, first-served basis and is subject to vacancy availability. There is no written examination. Candidates report to the venue within the reporting window and undergo document verification.
Q7 Is there any guarantee of employment after HAL NATS training?
A
No. After completion of the apprenticeship training, the contract is terminated and HAL has no obligation to offer any employment to the trained apprentice. The engagement is purely for the one-year training period under NATS.
Q8 What documents are needed for the HAL NATS walk-in?
A
Candidates must bring original documents including 10th marks sheet, Degree/Diploma Certificate or Provisional Certificate, all semester mark sheets (as applicable), NATS portal registration printout, two passport-size colour photographs, and one set of self-attested photocopies of all documents. Reserved category candidates must also bring a Caste/PWD Certificate.
Q9 What is the NATS portal where I need to register before the walk-in?
A
Candidates must register as a "STUDENT" at www.mhrdnats.gov.in before attending the walk-in and must bring a printed copy of the registration form to the venue.
Q10 What streams are covered under HAL NATS Diploma walk-in on 18 June 2026?
A
The Diploma walk-in on 18 June 2026 covers Aeronautical/Aerospace/Aviation/Avionics, Aircraft Maintenance Engineering (AME), Mechanical/Industrial/Production/Mechatronics/Metallurgy, Electrical/Electrical & Electronics/Instrumentation, Computer/IT streams, Electronics & Communication, Civil/Chemical Engineering, and Commercial Practice/Library & Information Science.
