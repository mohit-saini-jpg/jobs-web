Selection Process

1Merit-based on ITI total percentage marks (Category-wise, Trade-wise) + Medical Examination
2Candidates looking for a permanent job; this is a 12-month apprenticeship only, with no guarantee of absorption or regular employment in MPEZ after training completion.
3Candidates who have already completed apprenticeship training in the same trade at MPEZ or any other organisation under the Apprentices Act, 1961.
4Candidates who are not M.P. domicile (Mool Niwasi); vacancies are exclusively for Madhya Pradesh residents.
5Candidates who have not passed Class 10th (Dasvin) along with ITI (NCVT/SCVT) in the relevant trade.
6Candidates who are Post Graduates or hold degrees higher than ITI/Graduation where they exceed the prescribed educational ceiling — the role requires ITI; overqualified applicants must check if they remain eligible per NAPS portal norms.
7Candidates outside the age band of 18–25 years as on 01-01-2026, except those eligible for notified relaxation under SC/ST/OBC/PwBD categories.
8Candidates who do not have a 100% completed Apprentice profile on the NAPS portal (https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in/) with a valid Apprentice Registration Number — this is a mandatory prerequisite.
9Candidates applying for more than one trade — only one trade application is permitted per candidate.
10Candidates who are unable to appear for posting at offices under Jabalpur, Sagar, Rewa, or Shahdol zones.
11Not completing 100% Apprentice profile on the NAPS portal before applying; incomplete profiles will render the candidate ineligible and no Apprenticeship Contract can be offered.
12Not having a valid Apprentice Registration Number from NAPS portal — this is mandatory and must be available before submitting the application.
13Applying for more than one trade; only one trade application is allowed. Multiple applications will lead to disqualification.
14Entering the wrong category (SC/ST/OBC/UR); category cannot be changed after submission and any mismatch at document verification will lead to cancellation.
15OBC candidates from the creamy layer applying under OBC reservation instead of UR; only Non-Creamy Layer OBC candidates with a valid, current certificate are entitled to reservation.
16Not checking domicile certificate validity — candidates must be M.P. Mool Niwasi; applying without valid domicile proof will lead to rejection at document verification.
17Entering the wrong ITI trade or wrong ITI passing year in the application, causing mismatch at document verification stage.
18Using an incorrect or inactive mobile number/email ID, resulting in missing OTPs or shortlisting intimations from MPEZ.
19Submitting applications before or after the window (25-05-2026 12:00 AM to 25-06-2026 11:59 PM); applications received outside this window will not be accepted.
20Not reading that this is NOT regular employment — disputes about permanent absorption after the 12-month training period are not entertained.
