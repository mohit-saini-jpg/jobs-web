Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 How many vacancies are there in Central Bank of India Apprentice 2026?
A
Central Bank of India has announced 4,500 apprentice training seats for FY 2026–27 under the Apprentices Act, 1961.
Q2 What is the last date to apply for CBI Apprentice 2026?
A
The last date to apply online and pay the application fee is June 22, 2026.
Q3 What is the monthly stipend for CBI Apprentice 2026?
A
Selected apprentices receive a monthly stipend of ₹15,000 for the 12-month engagement period. No other allowances or benefits are provided.
Q4 What is the educational qualification required for CBI Apprentice 2026?
A
Candidates must have a graduation degree in any discipline from a recognized university. The graduation must have been completed on or after January 1, 2022.
Q5 What is the age limit for CBI Apprentice 2026?
A
The age limit is 20 to 28 years as on May 31, 2026, for General/EWS candidates. SC/ST candidates get 5 years of relaxation; OBC candidates get 3 years.
Q6 What is the selection process for CBI Apprentice 2026?
A
Selection involves four stages: (1) Online Written Examination (100 marks, 60 minutes), (2) Test of Local Language of the applied state, (3) Document Verification, and (4) Medical Fitness Verification.
Q7 Is there negative marking in the CBI Apprentice 2026 online exam?
A
No. There is no negative marking for wrong answers in the CBI Apprentice 2026 online examination.
Q8 How do I apply for CBI Apprentice 2026?
A
First, register on the NATS portal (https://nats.education.gov.in). Then complete your final application, including category and district details, and pay the fee on the BEEP portal (https://beep.bfsissc.com) after receiving an email from BFSI SSC.
Q9 Can I get a permanent job at Central Bank of India after completing the apprenticeship?
A
No. Apprentices have no right to claim employment in the bank after training. Central Bank of India has no obligation to offer regular employment after the 12-month apprenticeship period.
Q10 What is the exam date for CBI Apprentice 2026?
A
The online written examination is tentatively scheduled for the first week of July 2026. The exact date will be updated on the BEEP portal and the bank's official website.
