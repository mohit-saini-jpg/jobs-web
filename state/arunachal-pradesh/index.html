About Arunachal Pradesh Government Jobs 2026

APST, APECL aur Arunachal Pradesh Housing Board important recruitment bodies hain. Arunachal Pradesh Police aur APSSB ke through constable posts nikalte hain. TRIHMS Naharlagun aur AP NHM Arunachal ke under health posts aate hain. CBSE Arunachal aur APDHTE ke through teacher recruitment hoti hai. Rajiv Gandhi University aur Arunachal University of Studies mein bhi academic posts available hain 2026 mein.

Arunachal Pradesh Recruitment 2026 mein 10th, 12th, ITI, Diploma aur Graduate sabhi ke liye avsar hain. Papum Pare, East Siang, West Kameng, Dibang Valley ke candidates bhi online form se apply kar sakte hain.
