Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the total number of vacancies in NHM Mungeli District Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 106 vacancies have been announced under NHM Mungeli District Recruitment 2026 across 23 posts including Staff Nurse (27), Community Health Officer (21), Nursing Officer (12), RMA (5), Jr. Secretarial Assistant NHM (5), Physiotherapist (3), and other posts. The number of vacancies is subject to change as per organizational requirements.
Q2 What is the last date to apply for NHM Mungeli District Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for NHM Mungeli District Recruitment 2026 is 20 June 2026 up to 5:00 PM. Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date to avoid last-minute technical issues on the official portal.
Q3 How can I apply for NHM Mungeli District Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online only through the official portal at https://mungelinhms.indicompute.com. No offline/postal applications will be accepted. A candidate can apply for a maximum of three posts using a single application link. The application fee must be paid via UPI by scanning the QR Code given in the official notification.
Q4 What is the application fee for NHM Mungeli District Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 100/- for PwD/SC/ST/Female candidates, Rs. 200/- for OBC candidates, and Rs. 300/- for Unreserved (General) candidates. The fee must be paid via UPI/BHIM/SBI Pay/Google Pay/Paytm by scanning the QR Code in the notification. The UTR number and payment screenshot must be submitted in the online application.
Q5 What is the age limit for NHM Mungeli District Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years. The maximum age is 70 years for medical/clinical posts and 64 years for administrative/managerial posts, calculated as on 01 January 2026. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST/OBC/PwD/Ex-Servicemen/Women candidates as per Chhattisgarh Government rules.
Q6 What is the selection process for NHM Mungeli District Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of document scrutiny to determine eligible/ineligible candidates, followed by a Skill/Written Test of 20 marks for most posts. Candidates must score a minimum of 30% (6 marks) in the Skill Test to qualify. The final merit list is prepared based on educational qualification marks (65% weightage), experience marks (maximum 10–15 marks), and Skill Test marks. For Class-IV posts (Aaya Bai, Cleaner, Class-IV UHWC), selection is based on educational qualification and experience only, without a Skill Test. No interview will be conducted.
Q7 Is this a permanent government job?
A
No. All posts under NHM Mungeli District Recruitment 2026 are purely contractual (Sanvida) in nature under NHM Chhattisgarh HR Policy 2018. The contract period is up to 31 March 2027 and may be extended based on satisfactory performance and central government approval. In case of regular recruitment, the contractual posts will automatically stand cancelled.
Q8 Can candidates from outside Chhattisgarh apply for NHM Mungeli Recruitment 2026?
A
No. Candidates must be permanent domicile residents of Chhattisgarh (Chhattisgarh Mool Niwasi). A Domicile Certificate issued by a competent authority is mandatory and must be uploaded at the time of online application. Candidates without a valid domicile certificate will be treated as ineligible.
