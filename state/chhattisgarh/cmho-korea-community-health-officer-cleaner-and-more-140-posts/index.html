Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the last date to apply for Korea District NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the application for Korea District NHM Recruitment 2026 is 3rd July 2026 by 5:00 PM. Applications must be sent only via Registered Post / Speed Post / Registry to the Chief Medical and Health Officer, District Korea, Baikunthpur, Chhattisgarh. Applications received late or by other modes will not be accepted.
Q2 How many vacancies are released in Korea NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
Vacancies have been released for two districts - Korea and MCB (Manendragarh). District Korea has 21 post categories and District MCB has 30 post categories covering 140 contractual posts under NHM, RBSK, SNCU, NTEP, UHWC, NUHM, NPPCD, NIDDCP, NRC, NBSU, and BPHU programmes. The exact total is provisional and subject to change.
Q3 What is the application fee for Korea NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
Application fee varies by category and salary of the applied post. For General/UR candidates it is Rs. 300 (salary ≤25,000) or Rs. 400 (salary >25,000). For OBC/Female it is Rs. 200 or Rs. 300. For SC/ST/PwD it is Rs. 100 or Rs. 200. Fee must be paid via DD in favour of District Health Society-Non-NRHM Fund.
Q4 What is the selection process for Korea NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection is based on a merit list of 100 marks comprising: educational qualification marks (65%), experience marks (10/15/20), bonus marks (10 for COVID frontline workers), and skill/written test marks (20). Candidates must score minimum 30% in the skill/written test to qualify.
Q5 Can candidates from outside Korea or MCB district apply for this recruitment?
A
No. Only candidates applying for Korea district or MCB district may apply. The district name must be clearly mentioned in the application form and on the envelope. Applications without the district name mentioned will be rejected outright.
