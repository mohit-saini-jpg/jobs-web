Qualification / Eligibility

No person who has entered into or contracted a marriage with a person having a spouse living, or who (having a spouse living) has entered into a marriage with any person shall be eligible. Exception: only if such marriage is permissible under the personal law applicable to such person as certified by the Hon'ble High Court.
