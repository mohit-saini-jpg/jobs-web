Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is the last date to apply for SPSC Accounts Clerk / Junior Storekeeper Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 31 July 2026. Applications submitted after this date will not be accepted, and SPSC will not be responsible for late submissions.
Q2 How many vacancies are available in SPSC Accounts Clerk Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 50 posts of Accounts Clerk / Junior Storekeeper are available under Advertisement No. 21/SPSC/EXAM/2026, distributed across multiple reservation categories including UR, BL, OBC, ST, SC, Primitive Tribe, Most Backward Classes, and WSS.
Q3 What is the educational qualification required for SPSC Accounts Clerk 2026?
A
Candidates must have passed Class XII (or equivalent) from a recognized Board with Mathematics as one of the subjects in Class X. Additionally, a certificate or diploma of at least 3 months basic computer application course (or higher) from a recognized board/university is mandatory.
Q4 What is the age limit for SPSC Accounts Clerk Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 18 and 40 years of age as on 31 July 2026, as per Notification No. M(3)/(55)/GEN/DOP/Pt.III dated 03/07/2019.
Q5 What is the application fee for SPSC Accounts Clerk 2026?
A
The application fee is ₹200/- for all candidates, payable online through Net Banking, Credit Card, or Debit Card (Visa/MasterCard). The fee is non-refundable once paid.
Q6 What is the pay scale for Accounts Clerk / Junior Storekeeper in Sikkim?
A
Selected candidates will be placed at Level-7 of the Pay Matrix of the Sikkim Sub Ordinate Accounts Service under the Finance Department, Government of Sikkim. During probation/training, pay will be as per Notification No. 489/GEN/DOP dated 31.10.2011.
Q7 What is the selection process for SPSC Accounts Clerk Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of a Written Examination followed by Document Scrutiny. Candidates who qualify in the written exam will be called for verification of original documents on dates specified by the Commission.
Q8 Is this recruitment open to candidates from outside Sikkim?
A
No. This recruitment is exclusively for local candidates of Sikkim. Candidates must possess either a Sikkim Subject Certificate or a Certificate of Identification issued by the competent authority of the State Government.
Q9 Where can I apply for SPSC Accounts Clerk Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted online at the official SPSC website: http://spsc.sikkim.gov.in/. No other mode of application is accepted. Applications received through any other means will be summarily rejected.
Q10 Can employed candidates apply for SPSC Accounts Clerk 2026?
A
Yes, but employed candidates must submit a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their Head of Department at the time of document verification. Currently employed candidates in any government department must obtain this NOC before appearing for document scrutiny.
