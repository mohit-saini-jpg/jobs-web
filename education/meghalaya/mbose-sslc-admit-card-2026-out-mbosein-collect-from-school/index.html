For Schools:

School authorities should follow these steps to download admit cards:
  • Visit the official website: mbose.in
  • On the homepage, click on the hall ticket download link
  • Log in using User ID and Password
  • Download student-wise admit cards
  • Verify details, add school stamp and Principal's signature
  • Distribute to students

For Students:

Students cannot download the admit card directly. Contact your school authorities to collect the original signed and stamped admit card as soon as possible.

Step: Download and Verify

After collecting, carefully verify all details including:
  • Your name and photograph
  • Roll number and exam centre
  • Subject-wise exam dates and timings
  • Reporting time and instructions
Important: The admit card must bear the Principal's signature and school stamp to be valid. Report any discrepancies to your school immediately.

Details Mentioned on MBOSE SSLC Admit Card 2026

  • Name of the candidate
  • Roll number
  • Date of birth
  • Subjects appearing for
  • Candidate's photograph
  • Exam centre details
  • Subject-wise exam schedule
  • Reporting time and important instructions

Before Leaving for Exam:

  • Check exam centre location on Google Maps
  • Plan to reach 30-45 minutes before reporting time
  • Keep signed admit card and valid ID proof ready
  • Carry necessary stationery (pen, pencil, etc.)
  • Get a good night's sleep
  • Eat a light, healthy breakfast

At the Examination Centre:

  • Follow all instructions given by the invigilators
  • Maintain silence and discipline
  • No use of mobile phones or electronic devices
  • Do not communicate with other candidates
  • Raise your hand if you need any assistance

Prohibited Items in Exam Hall:

  • Mobile phones and smartphones
  • Smartwatches and electronic gadgets
  • Calculators (unless allowed for specific subjects)
  • Bluetooth devices and earphones
  • Textbooks, study materials, and notes
  • Bags and pouches
