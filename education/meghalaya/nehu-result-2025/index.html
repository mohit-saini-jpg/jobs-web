How to Check the NEHU Results 2025?

NEHU (North Eastern Hill University) Results 2025, students should visit the official NEHU examination portal, select their course and semester, and enter their roll number to view and download their individual marksheet online.​

  • Go to the NEHU examination results portal: http://exams.nehu.ac.in.​
  • Click on the “Results” section to view the list of available UG, PG, and professional course results.​
  • Select your specific course and semester (e.g., BA, BSc, BCom, MA, LLB, MTech, BTech, etc.) from the list.​
  • Enter your Roll Number or Registration Number as prompted to access your results.​
  • Click “Submit” or “View Result” to display your individual marksheet on the screen.​
  • Download or print your marksheet for academic and admission purposes.
