North-Eastern Hill University has released NEHU Result 2026 notifications for several PhD candidates, publishing individual result PDFs for scholars from the Departments of Philosophy, Hindi, Chemistry, Commerce, Biochemistry, Biotechnology and Bioinformatics, and a corrigendum for the PhD result of a Botany scholar. NEHU Result 2026 has been made available online through the official exam result portal exams.nehu.ac.in/PGresults, which is linked from the main university website nehu.ac.in.

PhD candidates can open their respective result PDFs using the direct links provided by the examination department and verify their final PhD result status, title (where mentioned) and other remarks. After checking the result, scholars should cross-verify their name, department and registration details and contact the NEHU examination/PhD section if any discrepancy or clarification is required, particularly in light of the corrigendum issued for the Botany department candidate.

How to Download NEHU Result 2026

Use the steps below to access and download the NEHU PhD Result 2026 notifications:

  • Go to the NEHU PG and professional results portal: http://exams.nehu.ac.in/PGresults.
  • On the results index page, look for the latest entries around 02-04-2026 or directly use the individual PhD result links provided above for your department.
  • Click on the relevant PhD Result PDF link (for example, PhD Result of Vikedunuo Livi, Deptt. of Philosophy).
  • Let the PDF open in your browser, then read the notification carefully to confirm your name, department, registration number and final result status.
  • Download or print the PDF for your personal academic record, submission to employers or for further administrative procedures such as degree award and convocation.
  • If you are associated with the Botany department candidate mentioned in the corrigendum, verify both the original result and the corrigendum PDF to understand the corrected information.
