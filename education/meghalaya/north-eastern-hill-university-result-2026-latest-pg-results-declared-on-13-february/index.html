How to Check and Download NEHU Result 2026 Online

Follow these simple steps to access your result:

  • Visit the official NEHU examination portal: Click Here
  • Scroll through the list of recently declared results.
  • Click on the relevant result link (displayed with upload date).
  • The PDF will open or download automatically.
  • Search for your roll number using Ctrl+F (or Command+F on Mac) to find your marks/grade.

Always use the official NEHU website to avoid fraudulent sources. No login or registration is required for these PDF results.

Next Steps After Checking Your NEHU Result

If you have cleared the semester, collect your marksheet from your department when notified. In case of any discrepancy or if you need to improve your grades:

  • Contact your department head or the examination branch promptly.
  • Watch the official NEHU website for notifications on revaluation, scrutiny, or supplementary examinations (typically announced within 2-4 weeks of result declaration).
  • Keep checking Click Here for updates on remaining 2026 semester results.
