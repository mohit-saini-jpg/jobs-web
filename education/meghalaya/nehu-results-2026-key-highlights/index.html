Steps to Check NEHU Results 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your NEHU Results 2026 online:

  1. Visit the official North-Eastern Hill University examination portal at exams.nehu.ac.in/PGresults for PG and Professional courses or exams.nehu.ac.in/UGresults for UG courses

  10. Verify all personal details, subject codes, and semester information mentioned in the result
  11. Download and save the result PDF to your computer or mobile device for future reference
  12. Take a printout of the result page containing your marks for record-keeping and admission purposes
  13. If you cannot find your roll number or notice any discrepancies, contact the NEHU examination department immediately
