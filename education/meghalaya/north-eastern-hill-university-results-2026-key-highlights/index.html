How to Check NEHU Result 2026

Students can check NEHU Result 2026 directly from the PDFs linked on the NEHU examination results portal. The procedure is simple and does not usually require any login; candidates just need to locate their roll numbers within the published files.

Follow these steps to check NEHU Result 2026:

  1. Open the NEHU result portal at http://exams.nehu.ac.in/PGresults or visit the examination home page at http://exams.nehu.ac.in.
  2. On the results page, locate the latest result entries dated 07-04-2026 for: BTech BME 1st Semester (Regular) 2025 Batch BTech BME 1st Semester (Reappear) 2024 Batch History 3rd Semester Repeater 2023 Batch Adult and Continuing Education (LLE) 1st Semester Repeater 2024 Batch History 1st Semester Repeater 2023 Batch History 1st Semester Repeater 2022 Batch

  7. History 1st Semester Repeater 2023 Batch
  8. History 1st Semester Repeater 2022 Batch
  9. Click on the relevant “Result of …” link for your course and batch to open the PDF result document.
