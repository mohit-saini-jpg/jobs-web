Detailed Information

North-Eastern Hill University has officially released NEHU Result 2026 for several UG and professional programmes for the 2025-26 odd semester examinations. The latest release dated 07-04-2026 includes Result of BTech BME 1st Semester (Regular) 2025 Batch, BTech BME 1st Semester (Reappear) 2024 Batch, History 3rd Semester Repeater 2023 Batch, Adult and Continuing Education (LLE) 1st Semester Repeater 2024 Batch, History 1st Semester Repeater 2023 Batch and History 1st Semester Repeater 2022 Batch, all held in December 2025.

NEHU Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - nehu.ac.in - through the examination department portals at exams.nehu.ac.in and PGresults pages. Students can open the respective PDF result links, search for their roll numbers and check their subject-wise marks, GPA (where applicable) and pass/fail status. After verifying their details, students are advised to contact the university examination section or their colleges in case of any discrepancy in name, roll number or marks.

How to Check NEHU Result 2026

Students can check NEHU Result 2026 directly from the PDFs linked on the NEHU examination results portal. The procedure is simple and does not usually require any login; candidates just need to locate their roll numbers within the published files.

Follow these steps to check NEHU Result 2026:

  • Open the NEHU result portal at http://exams.nehu.ac.in/PGresults or visit the examination home page at http://exams.nehu.ac.in.
  • On the results page, locate the latest result entries dated 07-04-2026 for: BTech BME 1st Semester (Regular) 2025 Batch BTech BME 1st Semester (Reappear) 2024 Batch History 3rd Semester Repeater 2023 Batch Adult and Continuing Education (LLE) 1st Semester Repeater 2024 Batch History 1st Semester Repeater 2023 Batch History 1st Semester Repeater 2022 Batch
  • BTech BME 1st Semester (Regular) 2025 Batch
  • BTech BME 1st Semester (Reappear) 2024 Batch
  • History 3rd Semester Repeater 2023 Batch
  • Adult and Continuing Education (LLE) 1st Semester Repeater 2024 Batch
  • History 1st Semester Repeater 2023 Batch
  • History 1st Semester Repeater 2022 Batch
  • Click on the relevant “Result of …” link for your course and batch to open the PDF result document.
  • Once the PDF opens, use the search function (Ctrl+F on desktop or the search icon in mobile PDF viewer) and type your roll number or enrolment number.
  • Check your name, roll number, GPA/marks, result remark (Pass/Fail/Backlog etc.) and any other information printed against your entry.
  • Download or print the PDF and retain a copy for academic records and future use such as admission to the next semester or documentation.
  • If there is any mismatch or you are unable to find your roll number, contact the NEHU Examination Department or your college with the exam details and a copy of the notification.
