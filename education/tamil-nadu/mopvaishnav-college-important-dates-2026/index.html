Detailed Information

M.O.P. Vaishnav College for Women Application Form 2026 is now open for UG, PG, and MBA admissions at M.O.P. Vaishnav College for Women (Autonomous), Chennai. Eligible women candidates can apply online through the official admission portal for the academic year 2026-27.

M.O.P. Vaishnav College for Women (Autonomous), Chennai (commonly known as M.O.P.Vaishnav College), is a reputed autonomous institution affiliated to the University of Madras. The college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in Commerce, Management, Computer Science, Media, Psychology, and other streams. Admissions for 2026-27 are conducted entirely through an online process on a merit basis for most programmes.

M.O.P.Vaishnav College Application Fee 2026

Application fee is approximately ₹500 (varies by programme). Payment is made online through the admission portal.

How to Apply for M.O.P.Vaishnav College Application Form 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official admission portal: mopvaishnav.co.in/admission
  • Complete registration using a valid email ID and mobile number (OTP-based verification).
  • Login using registered email and Date of Birth as password.
  • Fill in personal, academic, and qualification details accurately.
  • Select desired programme(s).
  • Upload scanned copies of required documents.
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Submit the form and download/print the confirmation page.

M.O.P.Vaishnav College Selection Process

Admissions are primarily based on merit (marks in qualifying examination). Some programmes (especially in Media, Visual Communication, and MBA) may include additional rounds such as interviews or portfolio evaluation. Sports quota admissions are also available.

Documents Required for M.O.P.Vaishnav College Admission 2026

  • 10th and 12th Mark Sheets & Certificates
  • Graduation Mark Sheets (for PG applicants)
  • Transfer Certificate & Conduct Certificate
  • Community Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Aadhaar Card or other ID proof
  • Any other documents specified during online application

List of Programmes Offered at M.O.P.Vaishnav College

UG Programmes: BBA, B.Com (General, Accounting & Finance, Corporate Secretaryship, Honours, etc.), B.Sc. (Computer Science, AI, Data Science, Mathematics, Visual Communication, Electronic Media, Psychology, Food Science & Management), B.A. (Journalism, Sociology, Economics) and more.

PG Programmes: MBA, M.A. (HRM, Communication, Public Policy), M.Sc. (IT, Food Technology & Management, Applied Psychology), M.Com. etc.
