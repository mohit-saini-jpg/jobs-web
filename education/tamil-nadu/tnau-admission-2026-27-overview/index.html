TNAU Admission 2026 (Tamil Nadu Agricultural University) for Undergraduate and Diploma programmes is now open. Online applications have started and the last date to apply is 8 June 2026.

Tamil Nadu Agricultural University (TNAU) Admission 2026-27 has officially begun for B.Sc (Hons.) Agriculture, Horticulture, Agricultural Engineering and Diploma programmes in Agriculture, Horticulture and Agricultural Engineering. Students can apply online through the single window portal till 8 June 2026.

Admissions are purely merit-based on Class 12th marks. TNAU, along with its affiliated colleges and Annamalai University Faculty of Agriculture, offers excellent opportunities in agricultural education with strong focus on practical training, research and entrepreneurship. This is the best time for 10+2 Science stream students to secure admission in one of India’s premier agricultural universities. Apply early and keep all documents ready. (96 words)

Eligibility Criteria for TNAU Admission 2026
Diploma in Agriculture / HorticulturePass in HSC (10+2) with Physics, Chemistry, Biology (Academic or Vocational Stream with Agriculture subjects)
Diploma in Agricultural EngineeringHSC with Mathematics, Physics & Chemistry (Vocational not eligible)
B.Sc (Hons.) Agriculture / HorticulturePass in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics as per ICAR norms

Age Limit: Should not have completed 21 years as on 01.07.2026 (No upper age limit for SC/ST)

How to Apply for TNAU Admission 2026 (Step-by-Step)
  1. Visit the official admission portal: https://tnau.ucanapply.com
  2. Complete New Registration with valid email and mobile number.
  3. Fill the online application form with personal, academic and reservation details.
  4. Upload scanned copies of required documents.
  5. Pay the application fee online.
  6. Submit the form and take a printout for future reference.
Documents Required
  • HSC (+2) Mark Sheet & Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate & Conduct Certificate
  • Community & Nativity Certificate (for Tamil Nadu candidates)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport size photograph & signature
  • Physical Fitness Certificate (at the time of admission)
Courses Offered
  • Undergraduate: B.Sc. (Hons.) Agriculture, B.Sc. (Hons.) Horticulture, B.Tech Agricultural Engineering etc.
  • Diploma: 2-year Diploma in Agriculture, Horticulture and Agricultural Engineering (offered in Constituent, Government and Private affiliated institutes)
