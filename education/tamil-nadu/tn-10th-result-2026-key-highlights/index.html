How to Check TN 10th Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the steps given below to check and download the Tamil Nadu SSLC Class 10 result 2026 in online mode.

  • Visit the official Tamil Nadu result website - tnresults.nic.in in any browser.
  • On the homepage, click on the link that says "SSLC Examination Results 2026" or "TN 10th Result 2026".
  • A new login page will open where you need to enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth in the specified format.
  • After entering the details correctly, click on the "Submit" or "Get Marks" button to proceed.
  • Your TN SSLC 10th Result 2026 marksheet will be displayed on the screen with subject-wise marks and result status.
  • Carefully check all the details and download the provisional marksheet in PDF format for future reference.
  • Take a printout of the online result copy until you receive the original marksheet and certificate from your school.
