TANCET 2026 Answer Key Out

The TANCET 2026 Answer Key has been released by Anna University on 12 May 2026 for the Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test held on 09 May 2026, and candidates can now download the provisional answer key and response sheet from tancet.annauniv.edu. The answer key has been uploaded in online mode only, and candidates need to log in with their registered email ID and password to view and download it.

The board has also opened an online objection window for a limited period, during which candidates can challenge any question or answer by sending an email to [email protected] with proper supporting evidence. After evaluating all objections, Anna University will release the final TANCET 2026 answer key and result on or before 23 May 2026 on the official portal.

How to Download TANCET 2026 Answer Key

Candidates can download the TANCET 2026 answer key in PDF format from the official website by following these steps:

  • Visit the official TANCET website at tancet.annauniv.edu
  • Click on the "TANCET 2026 Answer Key" download link on the homepage
  • Enter your registered email ID and password in the login section
  • Submit the details to access the answer key
  • The answer key PDF will appear on the screen for both MBA and MCA courses
  • Download the answer key PDF corresponding to your programme (MBA or MCA)
  • Save and take a printout for future reference

How to Raise Objections Against TANCET 2026 Answer Key

If candidates find any discrepancies or errors in the provisional answer key, they can raise objections through the challenge window:

  • Visit the official website at tancet.annauniv.edu
  • Click on the objection or challenge link available on the homepage
  • Login using your registered email ID and password
  • Select the questions you want to challenge with proper justification and supporting documents
  • Pay the required objection fee per question (if applicable)
  • Submit your objections within the stipulated deadline
  • Download the confirmation receipt for future reference

Candidates must raise objections by sending an email to [email protected] with proper evidence. Anna University will review all objections and release the final answer key after expert evaluation.

How to Calculate Marks Using TANCET 2026 Answer Key

Candidates can estimate their tentative scores before the official result declaration using the answer key and marking scheme:

  • Download the TANCET 2026 Answer Key from tancet.annauniv.edu
  • Retrieve your question paper or download it from the official website
  • Open the answer key using your roll number and password
  • Start with question 1 and mark whether your answer is correct or incorrect
  • Continue the same procedure for all 100 questions
  • Make a list of all correct answers and all incorrect answers
  • Multiply all correct answers by 1 and all incorrect answers by 0.25
  • Subtract the total negative marks from total positive marks

Formula: Final Score = (Correct Answers × 1) - (Incorrect Answers × 0.25)

TANCET 2026 Exam Details

The TANCET 2026 examination was conducted on 9 May 2026 in two separate sessions. The MCA session was held from 10:00 AM to 12:00 noon in the forenoon shift, while the MBA session was conducted from 02:30 PM to 04:30 PM in the afternoon shift. The exam was held in computer-based mode with a duration of 2 hours for each programme. TANCET is conducted for admission to postgraduate programmes including MBA, MCA, M.E., M.Tech, M.Arch, and M.Plan in colleges across Tamil Nadu.
