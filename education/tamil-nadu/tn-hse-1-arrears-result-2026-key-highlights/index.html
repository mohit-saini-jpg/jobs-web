Detailed Information

The Directorate of Government Examinations (DGE), Tamil Nadu, has officially released the TN HSE +1 Arrears Result 2026 for Class 11 students who appeared in the arrear examinations for the 2025-26 academic session. The results have been made available online for various streams of HSE +1, and candidates can now access their provisional marksheets through the state result portals. TN HSE +1 Arrears Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - dge.tn.gov.in.

Students can check and download their TN 11th arrears result by visiting tnresults.nic.in, entering their registration number and date of birth, and then saving the provisional marksheet for reference.

After downloading the result, students are advised to carefully verify personal details, subject-wise marks, and result status, and contact the concerned school or DGE Tamil Nadu in case of any discrepancy.

How to Check TN HSE +1 Arrears Result 2026 Online

Follow the steps below to check TN HSE +1 Arrears Result 2026 online in a simple and error-free manner.

  • Visit the official Tamil Nadu result portal tnresults.nic.in or go to dge.tn.gov.in and navigate to the results section.
  • On the homepage, click on the link that reads "HSE(+1) Arrears Examination Results 2026" or similar.
  • On the result login page, enter your registration number in the given field exactly as per your hall ticket.
  • Enter your date of birth in the prescribed format (DD/MM/YYYY).
  • Click on the "Submit" or "Get Marks" button to proceed.
  • Your TN HSE +1 arrears result 2026 provisional marksheet will be displayed on the screen with subject-wise marks and result status.
  • Download the marksheet and take a printout for future reference and admission or re-appearing requirements.
