VITEEE Counselling 2026

VITEEE Counselling 2026 is currently ongoing. Phase 1 choice filling for ranks 1-20,000 is open from May 12 to May 13, 2026 (5:00 PM). Seat allotment for Phase 1 will be announced on May 14, 2026. Qualified candidates can register on the official portal for B.Tech admissions at VIT Vellore, Chennai, VIT-AP, and VIT-Bhopal campuses.

VIT (Vellore Institute of Technology) conducts a fully online centralized counselling process for admission to its B.Tech programmes. The process is rank-based and includes registration, payment of counselling fee, choice filling (campus + programme + fee category), seat allotment, document verification, and fee payment for confirmation.

VITEEE Counselling Fee 2026

Counselling Fee: ₹5,900 (non-refundable). This amount will be adjusted against the tuition fee upon admission. Pay online via net banking, credit/debit card during registration.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Participate in VITEEE Counselling 2026

  • Visit the official counselling portal and login using your VITEEE Application Number, Password, and registered email.
  • Pay the ₹5,900 counselling fee.
  • Fill your choices: Select campus (Vellore, Chennai, VIT-AP, Bhopal), programme (20+ B.Tech branches), and fee category (Category 1 to 5).
  • Review and lock your choices before the deadline (no changes allowed after locking).
  • Check the seat allotment result on the announced date.
  • Pay the advance tuition fee within the deadline and download the provisional admission letter.
  • Pay the balance fee by the final deadline and report to the allotted campus with original documents for verification.

Direct Link to Counselling Portal: https://ugresults.vit.ac.in/viteee2026/

Eligibility for VITEEE Counselling 2026

  • Must have qualified VITEEE 2026 and obtained a valid rank.
  • Lower ranks get preference in early phases and better seat options.
  • Candidates with Biology in VITEEE are eligible only for Bio-related programmes.
  • Ranks above 1 lakh are generally limited to VIT-AP and VIT-Bhopal campuses.

Documents Required for VITEEE Counselling 2026

Candidates must carry originals for verification at the time of reporting:

  • VITEEE 2026 Admit Card and Scorecard
  • Provisional Allotment Letter
  • Class 10 and Class 12 Mark Sheets / Passing Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / Migration Certificate
  • Community Certificate (if applicable for SC/ST)
  • Recent passport-size photographs
  • Photo ID proof (Aadhaar, etc.)

Seat Allotment and Fee Categories

Seat allotment depends on your rank, filled choices, seat availability, and selected fee category. VIT offers five fee categories per branch/campus (Category 1 has the lowest fee; Category 5 the highest). Allotment is final and cannot be changed.

After Seat Allotment: What to Do Next

Accept the allotted seat, pay the advance tuition fee within the given window, followed by the balance fee by the final deadline. Report to the campus for document verification. Admission is confirmed only after successful verification. Missing deadlines may result in seat cancellation.
