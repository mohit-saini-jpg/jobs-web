Educational Qualification

Tamil Nadu Education 2026 IIT Madras BS Degree eligibility criteria 2026 Post Date: 09/05/2026
IIT Madras BS Degree eligibility criteria 2026 require only a Class 12 pass (any stream). No age limit. Apply via the Qualifier Process or direct JEE Advanced entry for the January/May 2026 sessions.
Detailed Information

IIT Madras BS Degree eligibility criteria 2026 allow anyone who has passed Class 12 (or equivalent) to apply, irrespective of age, stream, or academic background. Candidates who have appeared for Class 11 finals can also apply and join after passing Class 12.

The program (primarily BS in Data Science and Applications) requires Mathematics and English in Class 10. Admission occurs via a Qualifier Process (Regular Entry) or direct JEE Advanced-based entry, with no upper age limit. This flexible online degree from IIT Madras provides an excellent opportunity for students to earn a prestigious IIT degree without the traditional JEE Main/Advanced for most applicants.

This comprehensive guide covers complete eligibility details, qualification requirements, qualifier process, important dates, application fees, and admission steps for the 2026 sessions based on official IIT Madras information.

Educational Qualification

  • Anyone who has passed Class 12 or equivalent can apply, irrespective of age or academic background. School students who have appeared for Class 11 final exams can also apply and join after passing Class 12. It is expected that applicants have studied Mathematics and English in Class 10.
  • No specific minimum percentage in Class 12 is required to apply. Admission depends on clearing the Qualifier Process (for regular entry).
  • Open to students from any stream (Science, Commerce, Arts, etc.), provided they meet the basic Class 10 Mathematics and English requirement.

Age Limit

There is no upper age limit for the IIT Madras BS Degree 2026. Candidates of any age can apply for the program. The youngest learners are around 17 years old, while the oldest recorded learners are above 81 years. This makes the IIT Madras BS Degree one of the most flexible IIT programs in terms of age criteria, allowing working professionals, homemakers, and students of all age groups to pursue the degree.

Nationality

IIT Madras BS Degree 2026 is open to candidates of all nationalities. Both Indian and foreign nationals (including NRIs, PIOs, and OCIs) can apply for the program. There is no restriction based on nationality. International students can join the fully online BS in Data Science and Applications program and will receive the same IIT Madras degree upon successful completion.

Regular Entry (Qualifier Process)

Fill out the application and pay the fee. Complete 4 weeks of foundational coursework + assignments. Appear for Qualifier Exam. Clear minimum assignment and exam scores (category-wise relaxations available).

JEE Advanced-Based Entry

Candidates who qualified/are eligible to appear for the most recent JEE Advanced can get direct admission to the Foundation Level.
