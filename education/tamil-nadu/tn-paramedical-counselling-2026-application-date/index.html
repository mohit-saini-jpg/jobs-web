What is TN Paramedical Counselling?

TN Paramedical Counselling is the centralized single-window admission process conducted by the Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Tamil Nadu for admission to various Paramedical Degree courses in government and private colleges across Tamil Nadu.

TN Paramedical Counselling 2026 Highlights

  • Conducting Body: Directorate of Medical Education and Research (DMER), Tamil Nadu
  • Counselling Mode: Online
  • Courses Offered: B.Sc. Nursing, B.Pharm, B.P.T, B.O.T, B.ASLP, B.Sc. Radiology & Imaging Technology, and many other paramedical courses
  • Admission Basis: Merit based on Class 12 marks
  • Official Website: tnmedicalselection.net
