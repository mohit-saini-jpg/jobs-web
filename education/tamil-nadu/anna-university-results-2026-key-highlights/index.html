Detailed Information

Anna University has officially released the TANCET Result 2026 for the Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test conducted for the 2026 academic session for various postgraduate courses. The result includes scorecards for PG programs such as MBA, MCA, M.E, M.Tech, M.Arch, and M.Plan, enabling candidates to check their entrance test performance for admission to affiliated colleges in Tamil Nadu.

Anna University Result 2026 has been announced online on the official website - tancet.annauniv.edu. Candidates can log in using their registered email ID or login ID and password to download their TANCET 2026 scorecard and verify their marks and qualifying status. After downloading the result, students are advised to carefully check their name, registration number, marks, and other details, and contact Anna University or the TANCET helpdesk immediately in case of any discrepancy.

How to Download TANCET Result 2026

Candidates can follow the step-by-step process given below to check and download their TANCET Result 2026 online from the official Anna University TANCET portal.

  • Visit the official TANCET website at https://tancet.annauniv.edu/.
  • On the homepage, click on the link for "TANCET Result 2026" or "TANCET 2026 Marksheet / Scorecard".
  • You will be redirected to the result login page.
  • Enter your registered email ID or login ID and password in the respective fields.
  • Click on the "Login" or "Submit" button to access your TANCET 2026 result dashboard.
  • View your TANCET scorecard showing your marks and qualifying status.
  • Click on the download or print option to save your TANCET Result 2026 for future reference.
