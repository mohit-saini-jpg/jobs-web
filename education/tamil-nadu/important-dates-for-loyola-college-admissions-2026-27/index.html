Loyola College Application Form 2026-27 is now open for admissions. The online application portal for Undergraduate (UG) programmes opened on 06 May 2026 and will close on 16 May 2026. PG applications will open on 10 May 2026 and close on 25 May 2026. Loyola College (Autonomous), Chennai, has started the admission process for the academic year 2026-27. Interested candidates can apply online only through the official admission portal for various Aided and Self-Supporting Undergraduate and Postgraduate programmes. This is one of the most prestigious colleges in Chennai, offering excellent academic programmes in Arts, Science, Commerce and Media streams. How to Apply for Loyola College Application Form 2026-27
  1. Visit the official admission portal:admission.loyolacollege.edu/lccadmission
  2. Select UG or PG application.
  3. Register with valid email and mobile number.
  4. Fill the online application form carefully.
  5. Upload required documents and photograph.
  6. Pay the application fee online.
  7. Submit and download the confirmation page.
