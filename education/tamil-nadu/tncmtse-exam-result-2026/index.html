How to Check TNCMTSE Exam Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your result:

  1. Visit the official website of Tamil Nadu DGE - tndge.org
  2. Click on the “TNCMTSE Exam Result 2026” link on the homepage

  4. Click on the submit button
  5. Your result will be displayed on the screen
  6. Download and take a printout for future reference
