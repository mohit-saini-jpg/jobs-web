How to Download IMU CET Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official IMU website: https://www.imu.edu.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the “Admissions 2026-27” section and click on the “IMU CET 2026 Admit Card” link, which redirects to the admit card portal (imu.cbexams.com / apply.registernow.in/IMUCET/Admitcard).
  • On the admit card login page, enter your User ID / Application Number and Password and then the captcha/security code if shown.
  • Click on “Login / Submit” to access your dashboard.
  • Click on “Download Admit Card / Hall Ticket”; your IMU CET Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen showing exam date, shift timing and exam centre address.
  • Verify all details carefully, then download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies of the hall ticket for exam day.

If you do not remember your login credentials, use the “I don’t remember my login details / Forgot password” option on the admit card portal and follow the steps sent to your registered email/mobile.

Details Mentioned on IMU CET Admit Card 2026

The IMU CET 2026 hall ticket contains the following key information, which candidates must verify:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph and signature
  • User ID / application number and IMU CET roll number
  • Exam name: IMU CET 2026
  • Course/category applied for (UG/PG, DNS, etc.) as per application
  • Exam date: 24 May 2026 and exam shift timing (for example, 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM)
  • Exam centre name, centre code and full address
  • Reporting time and gate‑closing time
  • Important exam‑day instructions, including items permitted and prohibited in the exam hall

If there is any discrepancy in personal details, photograph, course, or exam centre, candidates should immediately contact IMU via the helpline given in the admission notification.

Exam‑Day Instructions for IMU CET Admit Card 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the IMU CET 2026 examination, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the IMU CET Admit Card 2026 so that verification, biometric/ID checks and seating can be completed; late entry after the gate‑closing time may not be allowed.
  • Carry only essential items: your IMU CET admit card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout), a valid photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Driving Licence, Passport or other government‑approved ID) and permitted stationery as specified in the IMU CET instructions (such as blue/black ball‑point pen if required; follow the CBT instructions carefully).
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not permitted), Bluetooth devices, earphones, notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or written material inside the examination hall; such items are strictly prohibited and may lead to cancellation of your paper and further disciplinary action.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the IMU CET admit card and those announced by the invigilators during the exam; indulging in any unfair means, talking to other candidates, or disobeying exam rules can result in cancellation of your examination and possible debarment from future tests.
  • Keep your IMU CET admit card safely even after the exam, as it may be required during counselling, seat allotment, document verification and admission formalities at Indian Maritime University and its campuses/affiliated institutes.
