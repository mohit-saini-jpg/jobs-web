How to Download TANCET 2026 Final Answer Key (MBA & MCA)

Downloading the TANCET final answer key is a simple online process that can be completed in a few steps.

  • Visit the official TANCET website: tancet.annauniv.edu.
  • Click on the TANCET 2026 link or the “Answer Key / Response Sheet” section on the homepage.
  • Select your programme - MBA or MCA.
  • Enter your registered email ID and password in the login window.
  • Complete the captcha verification and click on “Login”.
  • Click on the TANCET 2026 final answer key link visible on your dashboard.
  • Download the answer key PDF and save it for future reference.

The same login credentials are generally used later for checking results and downloading scorecards, so candidates should keep these details safe.

TANCET 2026 MBA Final Answer Key - What It Contains

The TANCET 2026 MBA final answer key carries the officially verified correct answers for all questions asked in the MBA paper. Candidates can match the question numbers in the key with their question booklet and OMR or online response sheet to verify how many questions they have answered correctly. By applying the official marking scheme, MBA aspirants can arrive at a fairly accurate estimate of their raw TANCET 2026 score before the result is announced.

TANCET 2026 MCA Final Answer Key - What It Contains

The final answer key for TANCET 2026 MCA provides question‑wise correct responses for all sections of the MCA question paper. MCA candidates can use this key to cross‑check each of their responses, identify mistakes, and calculate their expected marks. This helps them understand where they performed well and where they lost marks, which is useful not only for the current admission cycle but also for those planning to re‑attempt in future years.

How to Calculate Marks Using TANCET 2026 Final Answer Key

To calculate your expected marks, you need the final answer key, your question paper or response sheet, and the official marking scheme.

  • First, go through each question and mark the ones where your answer matches the final answer key as correct and the rest as incorrect.
  • Using the TANCET exam pattern, multiply the number of correct responses by the marks allotted per correct answer and then subtract negative marks, if any, for each wrong response as per official rules.
  • The total after applying positive and negative marks will give you your approximate raw TANCET 2026 score, while the official percentile and rank will be shared later on the result portal.

Always rely on the official brochure or latest notification for the exact marking scheme instead of guessing.

TANCET 2026 Eligibility (Quick Recap for MBA & MCA)

Although the final answer key is mainly relevant for those who have already taken the exam, many candidates simultaneously research eligibility for upcoming sessions.
