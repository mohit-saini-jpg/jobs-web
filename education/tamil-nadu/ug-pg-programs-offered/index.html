Eligibility Criteria for UG Courses

Eligibility depends on subjects studied in +2/Higher Secondary:
Aided Stream (Key Requirements):
  • B.Sc. Mathematics / Physics / Chemistry: Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics.
  • B.Sc. Plant Biology & Biotechnology: Botany, Zoology / Biology.
  • B.Com Programmes: Commerce & Accountancy with specific subjects.
Unaided Stream (Key Requirements):
  • B.Com (Honours) EU15: Commerce, Accountancy, Economics with Maths/Business Maths (Minimum 75% aggregate).
  • B.Sc. Computer Science / Data Science / AI: Science Stream with Mathematics.
  • B.C.A.: Mathematics / Business Mathematics / Computer Science.
  • Most Arts & other Commerce courses: Any stream or Commerce background.
Full detailed eligibility is available in the official prospectus/PDF.

How to Apply Online for DG Vaishnav College Application Form 2026-27

  1. Visit the respective UG or PG application portal.
  2. Enter 10th Std Registration Number, Date of Birth & Verification Code → Click “I Agree”.
  3. Fill Personal Details, Basic Information, Address, Parent/Guardian Details & Academic Records.
  4. Enter +2 subject-wise marks.
  5. Upload passport-size photograph (3.5 cm × 4.5 cm, white background, 10-50 KB, no selfie).
  6. Pay application fee online (Credit Card, Net Banking, UPI, QR Code).
  7. Submit and download the filled application form & acknowledgement.
Apply Online links are provided above.

Selection Process

Admissions are merit-based through counselling as per Tamil Nadu Government reservation policy. Candidates must attend counselling in formal dress. Latecomers are not allowed.
