IIM JAP Final Result 2026

IIM JAP Final Result 2026 has been officially released for MBA (PGP) admissions by IIM Kashipur, IIM Trichy, IIM Raipur and IIM Ranchi for the academic session 2026-28. Candidates who appeared for the admission process can now check their final selection status online.

IIM JAP Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - https://mba.iimkashipurportal.org/. The results are available for PG management courses, and candidates must log in using their registered credentials such as user ID and password to access their result.

Students can check and download their results through the official portals of respective IIMs. It is advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the result and contact the respective institute in case of any discrepancy.

How to Download IIM JAP Final Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your IIM JAP MBA Result 2026:
