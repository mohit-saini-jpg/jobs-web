Refund Guidelines for UG and PG

  • 10% deduction if withdrawn within 15 days from admission date or up to 10 June 2026 .
  • 20% deduction up to 25 June 2026.
  • No refund from 26 June 2026 onwards.

Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility varies by programme (details in respective prospectuses):

  • UG Programmes: Pass in +2 (HSC) or equivalent from recognized board with required subject combinations.
  • PG/MCA/MBA: Relevant bachelor's degree + TANCET (for professional courses like MCA/MBA).

How to Apply for Ethiraj College Admission Form 2026-27

  • Visit the official admissions portal: ethirajcollege.edu.in.
  • Register with a valid email ID and mobile number (Aadhaar-linked preferred).
  • Fill the online application form carefully.
  • Upload required documents and recent passport-size photo (125 × 165 pixels, JPG/GIF, max 100 KB). School uniform/selfies not accepted.
  • Pay ₹50 application fee online.
  • Submit and keep a printout for records.

Note: Separate forms required for multiple programmes.

Application Fee

  • General: ₹50 per application (online).
  • SC/ST: Exempted for Aided stream.

Documents Required

  • 10th, 11th & 12th mark sheets
  • UG marksheets (for PG)
  • Community Certificate
  • TANCET 2026 Hall Ticket & Marksheet (for MCA/MBA)
  • Passport-size photo
  • Medical Fitness & Blood Group Certificate
  • Aadhaar details
