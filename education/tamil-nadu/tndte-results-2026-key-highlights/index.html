Details

The Tamil Nadu Directorate of Technical Education has officially released the TNDTE Result 2026 for Government Technical Examinations in Typewriting and Shorthand for various grades and categories. The latest result covers multiple sessions of Typewriting and Shorthand (Tamil and English) examinations conducted under the GTE scheme, enabling candidates from different centres to check their updated marks online.

TNDTE Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - tndtegteonline.in. Candidates who appeared for the Typewriting and Shorthand exams can download their provisional mark sheet by entering their registration number and date of birth on the result portal. After downloading the result PDF or online marksheet, students are advised to carefully verify name, registration number, subject, grade and marks, and contact the Directorate or concerned institute immediately in case of any discrepancy.

Steps to Check TNDTE Typewriting, Shorthand Result 2026

Candidates can follow the steps given below to check and download the TNDTE GTE Result 2026 for Typewriting and Shorthand examinations online:

  1. Visit the official TNDTE GTE result website at tndtegteonline.in.
  2. On the homepage, find and click on the latest link for "GTE Typewriting & Shorthand Result 2026" or similar result notification.
  3. If a login page opens, enter your registration number and date of birth in the specified format.
  4. Submit the details to view your TNDTE GTE Typewriting / Shorthand marks online.
  5. If a PDF result link is provided, click on it to open the result PDF and search your registration number to check your marks and grade.
  6. Download and save the online mark sheet or PDF for future reference and take a printout if required for documentation or certificate verification purposes.
