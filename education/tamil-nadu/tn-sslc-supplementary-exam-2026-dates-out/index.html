How to Download TN SSLC Supplementary Exam Dates 2026

Students can download the TN SSLC Supplementary Exam 2026 Dates easily by following the steps below:

  • Visit the official website of Directorate of Government Examinations, Tamil Nadu.
  • Go to the SSLC or Time Table section on the homepage.
  • Locate the link for SSLC Supplementary Examination July 2026 Time Table.
  • Click on the PDF link to open the timetable.
  • Download and save the file on your device.
  • Take a printout and keep it safe for exam preparation.

Note: Always refer to the official timetable and check all dates and timings carefully before the examinations.
