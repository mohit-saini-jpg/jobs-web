TNEA Counselling Process

TNEA counselling is conducted by the Directorate of Technical Education (DoTE), Tamil Nadu. It is fully online and based on +2 marks (Maths, Physics, Chemistry). No separate entrance exam is conducted. Key Steps:

  • Registration → Apply online on the official TNEA website.
  • Document Verification → Upload certificates.
  • Rank List Publication → Based on merit (cutoff marks out of 200).
  • Choice Filling & Locking → Select colleges and branches.
  • Seat Allotment → Multiple rounds + supplementary.
  • Reporting & Admission → Pay fees and join the college.
Factors Affecting Cutoff
  • Branch Demand: CSE, IT, ECE, AI & DS usually have higher cutoffs.
  • Category: OC gets the highest cutoff; reserved categories get lower.
  • College Type: Government & University colleges have higher cutoffs than private ones.
  • Previous Year Trends: Cutoffs fluctuate slightly every year.
