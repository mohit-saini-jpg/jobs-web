VITEEE 2026 Phase 3 Counselling

VITEEE 2026 Phase 3 Counselling is currently ongoing for candidates with ranks between 45,001 and 70,000. Choice filling and locking for Phase 3 is scheduled from 28 May to 29 May 2026, with seat allotment expected on 31 May 2026. VITEEE Phase 3 Counselling is the third round of centralized counselling conducted by Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) for admission to B.Tech programs across its campuses — Vellore, Chennai, Amaravati, and Bhopal.

Official Website: viteee.vit.ac.in

How to Participate in VITEEE 2026 Phase 3 Counselling (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official website: viteee.vit.ac.in
  • Login using VITEEE Application Number and Password
  • Pay the counselling fee (if not paid earlier)
  • Fill and lock your campus + branch preferences
  • Download the filled choices for reference

VITEEE Counselling Process 2026

  • Choice Filling & Locking
  • Seat Allotment
  • Document Verification
