How to Check Hemchand Yadav Vishwavidyalaya Result 2026

Students can follow the steps below to check and download Hemchand Yadav Vishwavidyalaya Result 2026 online from the official result portal:
  1. Visit the official website of Hemchand Yadav Vishwavidyalaya at durguniversity.ac.in.
  2. Click on the result / examination link, or directly visit the smart examination system at https://durg.ucanapply.com/result-details.
  3. On the result page, select your course (UG/PG), semester and category such as Regular/Private/EX/Supply-ATKT/New Syllabus from the available result list.
  4. Click on the relevant result link for your programme (for example, LLB 3rd Sem, LLB 5th Sem, MA Economics, MA Sociology, MCom or MSc Mathematics).
  5. Enter your roll number or other required login details in the given fields.
  6. Submit the details to view your individual result on the screen.
  7. Check your name, roll number, course, semester, subject-wise marks and result status carefully.
  8. Download and print the result copy or marksheet for future reference and official use.
