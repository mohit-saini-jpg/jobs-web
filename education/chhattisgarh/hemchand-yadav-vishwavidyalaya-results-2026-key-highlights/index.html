Detailed Information

Hemchand Yadav Vishwavidyalaya, Durg has officially released Hemchand Yadav Vishwavidyalaya Result 2026 for various UG and PG courses for the December-January 2025-26 exam session. The latest result notification covers Bachelor of Laws (LLB) 3rd and 5th semester (Regular/Private/EX/Supply/ATKT, including New Syllabus), along with MA Economics, MA Sociology, MCom and MSc Mathematics 1st and 3rd semester results for different categories such as Regular/Private, Supply/ATKT and Ex-students.

Hemchand Yadav Vishwavidyalaya Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - durguniversity.ac.in, and students can access their marks through the smart examination system at durg.ucanapply.com. To check and download their result, candidates must visit the university result portal, select their course and semester, and log in using their roll number or other required credentials.

After viewing the provisional result, students should carefully verify their personal details, course name, semester, subject-wise marks and result status. In case of any discrepancy in the result, candidates are advised to immediately contact Hemchand Yadav Vishwavidyalaya examination authorities or their respective colleges with the necessary documents for correction.

How to Check Hemchand Yadav Vishwavidyalaya Result 2026

Students can follow the steps below to check and download Hemchand Yadav Vishwavidyalaya Result 2026 online from the official result portal:

  • Visit the official website of Hemchand Yadav Vishwavidyalaya at durguniversity.ac.in.
  • Click on the result / examination link, or directly visit the smart examination system at https://durg.ucanapply.com/result-details.
  • On the result page, select your course (UG/PG), semester and category such as Regular/Private/EX/Supply-ATKT/New Syllabus from the available result list.
  • Click on the relevant result link for your programme (for example, LLB 3rd Sem, LLB 5th Sem, MA Economics, MA Sociology, MCom or MSc Mathematics).
  • Enter your roll number or other required login details in the given fields.
  • Submit the details to view your individual result on the screen.
  • Check your name, roll number, course, semester, subject-wise marks and result status carefully.
  • Download and print the result copy or marksheet for future reference and official use.
