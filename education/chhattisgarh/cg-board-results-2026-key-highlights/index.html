The Chhattisgarh Board of Secondary Education (CGBSE) and the School Education Department have officially released the CG Board Class 5th and Class 8th Result 2026 for the 2025-26 academic session. Lakhs of students who appeared for the centralized Class 5 and Class 8 examinations can now check their scores and qualifying status online. The board has activated the result link for CG 5th and 8th results, allowing students to access their mark sheets using basic login details.

CG Board Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - cgbse.nic.in. Students can check and download the CG Board Class 5th and 8th result by visiting the official portal, selecting their class result link and entering the required credentials such as roll number or other details as specified.

After downloading the online scorecard, students are advised to carefully verify their name, roll number, school name, subject-wise marks and overall result status. In case of any discrepancy in their CG Board Class 5th or 8th result, they should immediately contact their respective school authorities or the concerned board office for correction and further guidance.

CG Board Results 2026 Key Highlights

The CG Board Class 5th and Class 8th Results 2026 have been declared for all government and affiliated schools across Chhattisgarh, marking a major milestone for primary and upper primary students. The results are available online through the official CG Board website, enabling students and parents to quickly check marks and download provisional mark sheets for future reference.

Students can access the CG Board 5th and 8th results using their roll number or details provided on the admit card, and schools will also provide official mark sheets as per the schedule notified by the School Education Department.

How to Check CG Board Class 5th & 8th Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check CG Board Class 5th and Class 8th Result 2026 online:

  1. Visit the official CG Board website - https://cgbse.nic.in/
  2. On the homepage, look for the link related to CG Board Class 5th Result 2026 or CG Board Class 8th Result 2026
  3. Click on the respective class result link to open the result login page
  4. Enter your roll number or other required credentials as mentioned on the screen
  5. Click on the submit button to proceed
  6. The CG Board Class 5th or 8th result 2026 will be displayed on the screen with subject-wise marks and overall status
  7. Download the provisional result and take a printout for future reference
