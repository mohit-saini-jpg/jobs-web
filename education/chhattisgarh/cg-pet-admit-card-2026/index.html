CG PET Admit Card 2026

Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board (CGPEB), also known as CG Vyapam, has released the CG PET Admit Card 2026 for the Chhattisgarh Pre‑Engineering Test on its official website. Candidates who successfully registered for the exam can now log in and download their hall ticket in online mode only.

As per the official schedule, the CG PET 2026 exam will be held on 14 May 2026 as a pen‑and‑paper‑based (offline) test. The CG PET 2026 admit card download link is activated on 7 May 2026 on the CG Vyapam portal, and candidates must carry a printed copy of the hall ticket to the exam centre along with a valid ID.

How to Download CG PET Admit Card 2026

Candidates can download the CG PET hall ticket by following these steps:

  1. Visit the CG Vyapam official site: https://vyapam.cgstate.gov.in and go to the CG PET 2026 page, or directly open the candidate login via vyapamprofile.cgstate.gov.in.
  2. Click on the “Admit Card – CG PET 2026” link on the homepage or notices section.
  3. On the admit card login page, enter your CG PET Registration ID / registered mobile number and password/date of birth, then fill the captcha/security code.
  4. Click on “Login / Submit” to access your candidate dashboard.
  5. Click on the “Download CG PET 2026 Admit Card” button; your hall ticket will appear on the screen with exam date, time, and centre details.
  6. Verify all details, then download and print at least 2–3 copies of the admit card on A4‑size paper to carry on the exam day.

If you forget your login details, use the “Forgot Registration ID / password” option on the login page to retrieve or reset them via mobile/email.

Details Mentioned on CG PET Admit Card 2026

After downloading the hall ticket, candidates should carefully check these details:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph and signature
  • CG PET 2026 registration ID and roll number
  • Exam name: CG PET 2026
  • Exam date and time: 14 May 2026, 10:00 AM – 1:15 PM
  • Reporting time and gate‑closing time
  • Exam centre name, centre code and full address
  • Category and other profile details (if printed)
  • Important exam‑day instructions and guidelines from CG Vyapam

If there is any discrepancy in the admit card (name, photo, exam centre, etc.), candidates must contact CG Vyapam immediately through the helpline before the exam.

Documents Required with CG PET Admit Card 2026

On the day of the CG PET 2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the CG PET Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Driving Licence Voter ID Other government‑issued ID as allowed in the information brochure.
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Voter ID
  • Other government‑issued ID as allowed in the information brochure.
  • Additional documents, if specified in the admit card/brochure (for example, PwD certificate and scribe approval letter for candidates using scribe facility).

Entry to the exam hall will not be permitted without the printed CG PET hall ticket and a valid photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for CG PET 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the CG PET exam, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card so that frisking and verification can be completed; candidates arriving after the gate‑closing time will not be allowed entry.
  • Carry only essential items: CG PET admit card, valid photo ID and permitted stationery (blue/black ball‑point pens, pencils, eraser, etc.) as specified; follow the instructions regarding dress code and allowed items in the information brochure.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not allowed), Bluetooth devices, earphones, electronic gadgets or written material inside the exam hall; such items are strictly prohibited and may lead to disqualification.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the admit card and announced by invigilators; any unfair means or misconduct can result in cancellation of the exam and debarment from future examinations.
  • Keep your CG PET admit card safely even after the exam, as it may be required during counselling and admission.
