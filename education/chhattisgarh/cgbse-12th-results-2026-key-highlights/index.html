The Chhattisgarh Board of Secondary Education (CGBSE) has officially released the CGBSE 12th Result 2026 for the academic session 2025-26 today. Students who appeared for the Class 12 board examinations can now check their results online. CGBSE 12th Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - https://cgbse.nic.in/ at 2.30 PM.

The board has published results for Class 12 students across all streams including Science, Commerce and Arts today. Students will be able to check and download their marksheet by entering their roll number and other required details on the official result portal once the result link is activated after the official declaration.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the result, such as their name, roll number, subject-wise marks, total marks and qualifying status. In case of any discrepancies, they should immediately contact the board authorities or their respective schools for correction.

How to Check CGBSE 12th Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your CGBSE 12th Result 2026 online:

  1. Visit the official website - https://cgbse.nic.in/.
  2. Click on the link “CGBSE 12th Result 2026” or “Higher Secondary Exam Results 2026” available on the homepage.
  3. Enter your Roll Number in the login field
  4. Click on the Submit button
  5. Your CGBSE 12th Result 2026 will appear on the screen with subject-wise marks
  6. Download and take a printout of the online marksheet for future reference
