Detailed Information

Shaheed Mahendra Karma Vishwavidyalaya, Bastar has released SMKV Exam Result 2026 for a fresh set of UG and PG semester examinations held in the July–December 2025 session for the 2025-26 academic year. The latest declaration dated 06-04-2026 covers Bachelor of Business Administration (Old) Third Semester Regular/Ex, Master of Social Work First Semester Regular, MSc in Botany Third Semester Regular, MSc in Rural Technology First Semester Regular, MSc in Mathematics First Semester Regular, MA in Sanskrit First Semester Regular/Non Collegiate and Backlog, MA/MSc in Anthropology and Tribal Studies First Semester Regular, MA in Economics First Semester Regular/Non Collegiate and Backlog, MSc in Chemistry First Semester Backlog, MSc in Mathematics First Semester Backlog, MSc in Rural Technology First Semester Backlog, MCA Third Semester Regular and First Semester Backlog, MA in Political Science First Semester Backlog and MSc in Mathematics (Old) Fourth Semester Backlog (ATKT).

SMKV Result 2026 has been published online on the official websites smkvbastar.in and the central result portal smkv.ucanapply.com, with result access also provided via smkvbastar.in/result.php. Students can open the result portal, select session 2025-2026, choose programme type and specific course/semester, enter their roll number, and then download their subject-wise marksheet. After viewing the result, candidates should verify their name, roll number, course, semester, subject-wise marks and result status, and contact the university or college examination cell in case of any discrepancy.

Steps to Check SMKV Exam Result 2026

Students can check SMKV Exam Result 2026 online using the official result portal, which lists all declared results with course and semester details. The process is straightforward and requires only a few basic exam credentials.

Follow these steps to check SMKV Result 2026:

  • Go to the official SMKV result portal at https://smkvbastar.in/result.php.
  • Alternatively, open the central result page at https://smkv.ucanapply.com/declared-results for a full list of declared results up to the current date.
  • On the result page, ensure that the Session is set to 2025-2026, which covers the July–December 2025 semester examinations.
  • Select Programme Type (UG, PG, etc.) and then choose your specific Programme from the dropdown list, such as: Bachelor of Business Administration (Old) Third Semester Regular/Ex Master of Social Work First Semester Regular MSc in Botany Third Semester Regular MSc in Rural Technology/Mathematics/Chemistry First Semester (Regular/Backlog) MA in Sanskrit/Economics/Political Science First Semester (Regular/NC/Backlog) MCA First/Third Semester Regular/Backlog MSc Mathematics (Old) Fourth Semester Backlog (ATKT)
