Steps to Check SMKV Exam Result 2026

Students can check SMKV Exam Result 2026 online using the official result portal, which lists all declared results with course and semester details. The process is straightforward and requires only a few basic exam credentials.

Follow these steps to check SMKV Result 2026:

  1. Go to the official SMKV result portal at https://smkvbastar.in/result.php.
  2. Alternatively, open the central result page at https://smkv.ucanapply.com/declared-results for a full list of declared results up to the current date.
  3. On the result page, ensure that the Session is set to 2025-2026, which covers the July–December 2025 semester examinations.
  4. Select Programme Type (UG, PG, etc.) and then choose your specific Programme from the dropdown list, such as: Bachelor of Business Administration (Old) Third Semester Regular/Ex Master of Social Work First Semester Regular MSc in Botany Third Semester Regular MSc in Rural Technology/Mathematics/Chemistry First Semester (Regular/Backlog) MA in Sanskrit/Economics/Political Science First Semester (Regular/NC/Backlog) MCA First/Third Semester Regular/Backlog MSc Mathematics (Old) Fourth Semester Backlog (ATKT)
  5. Bachelor of Business Administration (Old) Third Semester Regular/Ex
  6. Master of Social Work First Semester Regular
  7. MSc in Botany Third Semester Regular
  8. MSc in Rural Technology/Mathematics/Chemistry First Semester (Regular/Backlog)
  9. MA in Sanskrit/Economics/Political Science First Semester (Regular/NC/Backlog)
  10. MCA First/Third Semester Regular/Backlog
  11. MSc Mathematics (Old) Fourth Semester Backlog (ATKT)
  12. Select the relevant Semester (1st, 3rd or 4th) and exam type (Regular/Back/ATKT) as applicable.
  13. Enter your Roll Number (and any other requested detail) exactly as printed on your admit card.
  14. Click on the Search/View Result button to display your SMKV Result 2026 marksheet on the screen.
  15. Check your subject-wise marks, total marks, division and result status, and then download or print the marksheet for future reference.
  16. Remember that online results are for immediate information only and cannot be treated as original mark sheets; collect your official mark sheet from the university/college later.
  17. If you notice any error or mismatch in your details or marks, contact the university exam cell or your college examination branch immediately with the necessary documents.
