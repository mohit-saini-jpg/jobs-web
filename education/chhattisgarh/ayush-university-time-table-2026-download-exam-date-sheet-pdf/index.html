What is Ayush University Time Table 2026?

The Ayush University Time Table 2026 is the official examination schedule released by Ayush University for the academic year 2025-26. This document contains crucial information including:

  • Exact exam dates for each subject/paper
  • Exam timing and duration
  • Course-wise and semester-wise schedule
  • Regular and supplementary exam dates
  • Important instructions for students

Students appearing for B.Sc (1st to 7th Semester) examinations must download the time table to prepare their study schedule effectively.

How to Download Ayush University Time Table 2026? (Step-by-Step)

Follow these simple steps to download your exam date sheet 2026:

Method 1: Direct Download from Official Website

  • Visit Official Website: Go to ddumhsaucg.ac.in or results.ddumhsaucg.ac.in
  • Navigate to Examination Section: Look for "Examinations" or "Time Table" section
  • Click on Time Table/Date Sheet: Find "Examination Schedule" or "Time Table" link
  • Select Academic Year: Choose 2025-26 or current session
  • Choose Your Course: Select B.Sc or your specific semester
  • Select Exam Type: Regular/Supplementary
  • Download PDF: Click on the download button to save the time table
  • Save and Print: Save the PDF and take a printout for reference
