CG Vyapam Pre DElEd Admit Card 2026 Out

Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board (CG Vyapam) has released the CG Vyapam Pre DElEd Admit Card 2026 for the Pre Diploma in Elementary Education entrance examination. The hall ticket has been issued online at the official Vyapam portal, and candidates who applied for the Pre D.El.Ed exam can now download it using their login credentials.

As per the official schedule, the CG Pre D.El.Ed 2026 exam will be held on 4 June 2026 from 10:00 AM to 12:15 PM at examination centres across all 33 district headquarters in Chhattisgarh. The admit card release date is 25 May 2026, and the Pre DElEd admit card status is shown as “Released” on official and third‑party portals.

How to Download CG Vyapam Pre DElEd Admit Card 2026
  1. Visit the official CG Vyapam website vyapam.cgstate.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the “Admit Card” link or open the Pre D.El.Ed 26 section and then click on the Pre D.El.Ed admit card link.
  3. Log in using your registered mobile number/username and password (or other credentials specified by CG Vyapam).
  4. After login, click on the CG Pre D.El.Ed Admit Card 2026 download link.
  5. Your CG Vyapam Pre DElEd hall ticket 2026 will appear on the screen with your roll number, exam date, centre and other details.
  6. Check all the details carefully and download and print at least 2-3 clear A4‑size copies for exam day and future reference.

The CG Pre D.El.Ed admit card 2026 contains key information that must be verified:

Documents Required with CG Vyapam Pre DElEd Admit Card 2026

On the day of the CG Pre D.El.Ed 2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the CG Vyapam Pre DElEd Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence, Passport or any other government‑issued ID accepted as per CG Vyapam instructions.
  • Any other document/information mentioned in the instructions (if applicable).

Entry to the examination hall will not be permitted without the admit card and valid photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for CG Vyapam Pre DElEd 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the CG Pre D.El.Ed Entrance Exam 2026, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time so that verification and seating can be completed; latecomers after gate‑closing time may not be allowed to enter.
  • Carry only essential items: Pre DElEd admit card, valid photo ID and permitted stationery (blue/black ball‑point pens etc.); follow the instructions printed on your hall ticket.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not allowed), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material into the exam hall; possession of such items may lead to cancellation of your exam.
  • Follow all instructions announced by invigilators and printed on the OMR sheet/question paper; indulging in any unfair means or disobeying rules can result in disqualification from the exam.
  • Keep your CG Vyapam Pre D.El.Ed admit card safely even after the exam, as it may be required during result checking and counselling/admission procedures.
