IIT Bhilai M.Tech Admission 2026

IIT Bhilai M.Tech Admission 2026 applications are open for the Monsoon Semester (2026-27). Indian Institute of Technology Bhilai (IIT Bhilai) invites online applications for its full-time M.Tech programmes. The application portal opened on 20 March 2026 (10:00 Hrs IST) and the last date to submit the online application is 20 April 2026 (17:00 Hrs IST). Admissions are primarily based on a valid GATE score (or GPAT for specific disciplines). Registration on COAP 2026 is mandatory -candidates must register on the COAP portal before filling the IIT Bhilai application form. COAP registration number is required during the application process. Offers will be made available only through the COAP 2026 portal.

Programmes Offered at IIT Bhilai for M.Tech 2026-27

IIT Bhilai offers the following M.Tech programmes:

  • Bioscience and Biomedical Engineering -Bioengineering, Biomedical Devices
  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Data Science and Artificial Intelligence
  • Electrical Engineering -Power Systems and Power Electronics (PSPE), Control and Instrumentation (CI), Electric Vehicle Technology (EVT)
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering -Communication and Signal Processing (CSP), Microelectronics and VLSI (ME & VLSI)
  • Materials Science and Metallurgical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering -Design and Manufacturing, Thermal and Fluids Engineering
  • Mechatronics Engineering

IIT Bhilai M.Tech Eligibility Criteria 2026

Candidates must have one of the following qualifications with first class or minimum 60% marks (55% for SC/ST/PwD):

  • Bachelor’s degree in Engineering/Technology
  • Master’s degree in Science or equivalent
  • 4-year Bachelor’s degree in Science
  • B.Pharma / M.B.B.S / B.D.S or related degree (for Bioengineering & Biomedical Devices)

Additionally, candidates must have a valid GATE score in the relevant paper (or GPAT where applicable) or CPI/CGPA of 8.0 in B.Tech from an IIT (exemption from GATE).

Discipline-specific accepted GATE papers are listed on the official admission page.

How to Apply for IIT Bhilai M.Tech 2026 – Step-by-Step Guide

  • Register on COAP 2026 and note your COAP registration number (mandatory).
  • Visit the IIT Bhilai application portal and complete fresh registration for each programme/discipline.
  • Fill personal details, educational qualifications, and upload required documents.
  • Generate SBI Collect reference number and pay the application fee.
  • Add payment details after 48 hours and finalize the application.

Note: Separate applications and fees are required for each programme. Editing is not possible after finalization.

Selection Procedure

Shortlisting is based on valid GATE/GPAT scores (or IIT B.Tech CGPA exemption). Final offers will be made through the COAP 2026 portal. The selection process is scheduled for May 2026. Institute fellowship may be provided as per Ministry of Education norms.

Application Fee

  • General / OBC-NCL / EWS: ₹200 per programme
  • SC / ST / PwD / Female: ₹100 per programme

Payment through State Bank Collect (SBI Collect) only. Fee is non-refundable.

Documents Required

  • Photograph and signature
  • GATE scorecard (if applicable)
  • Academic mark sheets and degree certificates
  • Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC-NCL/EWS/PwD)
  • COAP registration number
  • Experience certificate (if any)
