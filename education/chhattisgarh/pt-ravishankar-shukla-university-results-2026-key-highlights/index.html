How to Download PRSU Result 2026 Online

Students can first read the consolidated result notification PDF and then check detailed course-wise marks via the PRSU online result portal. The portal provides semester-wise mark sheets accessible using roll number and course details.

Follow these steps to check PRSU Result 2026:

  1. Open the official website of Pt. Ravishankar Shukla University at prsu.ac.in.
  2. Go to the Examination or Results/Notification section and download the result notification PDF using the link: https://www.prsu.ac.in/admin/theme/examination_uploads/06042026013054.pdf.
  3. In the PDF, confirm that your course and semester (such as M.Ed Sem 3, M.Sc Computer Science Sem 3, M.Sc Mathematics Sem 1, PGDCA Sem 1, BA LL.B (5 Yr Integrated) etc.) are listed.
  4. Then visit the PRSU online result portal (commonly prsuuniv.in or result.prsuuniv.in as guided in the notification) for individual marksheets.
  5. On the result login page, select your course, semester and exam type from the dropdown menu.
  6. Enter your roll number or other required credentials and click on the search/view result button.
  7. Your PRSU Result 2026 marksheet will appear on the screen, showing subject-wise marks, total marks and result status.
  8. Download or print the online marksheet for your records, and verify all details such as name, roll number, course, semester and marks.
  9. If any discrepancy or technical issue is found, contact your college examination cell or the PRSU examination department with relevant documents.
