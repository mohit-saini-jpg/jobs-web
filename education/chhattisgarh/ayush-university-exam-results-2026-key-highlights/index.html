How to Download Ayush University Exam Result 2026 Online

  1. Visit the official Ayush University result page at ddumhsaucg.ac.in/result_university.php.
  2. On the result page, check the list of latest result notifications for different courses and exam sessions.
  3. Locate the relevant result entry such as: "Result of B.A.M.S. Final Professional Examination Jan/Feb-2026" "Revised Result of M.B.B.S. First Year Examination Dec-2025" "Result of Revaluation B.D.S. 3rd Year Examination Oct-2025" "Revised Result of M.B.B.S. Second Year Examination Nov-2025"
  4. "Result of B.A.M.S. Final Professional Examination Jan/Feb-2026"
  5. "Revised Result of M.B.B.S. First Year Examination Dec-2025"
  6. "Result of Revaluation B.D.S. 3rd Year Examination Oct-2025"
  7. "Revised Result of M.B.B.S. Second Year Examination Nov-2025"
  8. Click on the associated "Click Here" or PDF result link for your course.
  9. The official result PDF will open in a new tab showing roll numbers, marks and result details or specific revised information.
  10. Use the search function (Ctrl+F) in the PDF viewer and enter your roll number to find your result quickly.
  11. Verify your roll number, course, examination session and result status carefully.
  12. Download or print the PDF for future reference and contact the university examination section if you find any discrepancy.
