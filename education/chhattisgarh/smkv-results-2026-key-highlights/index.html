Detailed Information

Shaheed Mahendra Karma Vishwavidyalaya, Bastar has released the SMKV Result 2026 for a large number of UG and PG semester examinations, including DCA 1st sem, BBA (Old/New) 1st and 5th sem, BEd 1st and 3rd sem, BSc Home Science 1st and 3rd sem, BLISc 1st sem, MCA 1st sem, MBA 3rd sem, and various MSc and MA programmes across multiple semesters (regular and back). The latest result update, dated 31-03-2026, covers 1st, 3rd, 4th and 5th semester exams for both regular and old schemes, giving students from different faculties access to their current and backlog performance in one consolidated release.

Shaheed Mahendra Karma Vishwavidyalaya Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - smkvbastar.in - through the central result portal at smkvbastar.in/result.php. Students can check and download their marksheets by visiting the portal, selecting course, year and semester, and entering their roll number or other required details. After viewing the result, candidates should verify their name, roll number, course, semester, subject wise marks and result status and immediately contact the university or college examination cell if any discrepancy is noticed.

Steps to Check SMKV Result 2026

Follow the step by step process below to check and download your SMKV Result 2026 for DCA, UG or PG courses:

  • Visit the official result portal: https://smkvbastar.in/result.php.
  • On the result page, select your Course/Programme from the drop down list (for example, DCA, BBA, BEd, BSc Home Science, BLISc, MCA, MBA, MA, MSc, MSW, Anthropology & Tribal Studies etc.).
  • Choose the relevant Semester and Exam Type such as 1st/3rd/4th/5th Sem and Regular/Back/Old as applicable.
  • Enter your Roll Number/Enrollment Number and any additional details requested on the page.
  • Click on the Submit / Get Result / View Result button to display your online marksheet.
  • Verify your name, roll number, course, semester, subject wise marks, total marks and result status and then download or print the result for future reference, revaluation or next semester admission formalities.
