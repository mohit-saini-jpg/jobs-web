Steps to Check SMKV Result 2026

Follow the step by step process below to check and download your SMKV Result 2026 for DCA, UG or PG courses:

  1. Visit the official result portal: https://smkvbastar.in/result.php.
  2. On the result page, select your Course/Programme from the drop down list (for example, DCA, BBA, BEd, BSc Home Science, BLISc, MCA, MBA, MA, MSc, MSW, Anthropology & Tribal Studies etc.).
  3. Choose the relevant Semester and Exam Type such as 1st/3rd/4th/5th Sem and Regular/Back/Old as applicable.
  4. Enter your Roll Number/Enrollment Number and any additional details requested on the page.
  5. Click on the Submit / Get Result / View Result button to display your online marksheet.
  6. Verify your name, roll number, course, semester, subject wise marks, total marks and result status and then download or print the result for future reference, revaluation or next semester admission formalities.
