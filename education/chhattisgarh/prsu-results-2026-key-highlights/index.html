How to Download PRSU Result 2026

Follow the step-by-step process given below to check and download PRSU 1st and 3rd Sem Result 2026 online:

  1. Visit the official website of Pt. Ravishankar Shukla University - prsu.ac.in.
  2. Go to the examination or results section available on the homepage.
  3. Look for the latest notification or link related to "B.Ed/M.A/M.C.A/M.Sc Semester Exam Dec-Jan 2025-2026 Result".

How to Check PRSU Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your PRSU Result 2026 for the Dec-Jan 2025-26 semester examinations:

  1. Visit the official website of Pt. Ravishankar Shukla University at https://www.prsu.ac.in (you can also use https://www.prsuuniv.in).
  2. Go to the Examination/Results section available on the homepage.
  3. Open the latest Exam Result Notification PDF or click on the relevant online result link for your course and semester.
  4. If checking through the PDF notification, note your exam name from the list and then proceed to the online result page for detailed marks.
  5. On the online result page, select your exam type, course, semester and year as applicable.
  6. Enter your Roll Number or other required details and click on Submit/View Result.

How to Download PRSU Result 2026
