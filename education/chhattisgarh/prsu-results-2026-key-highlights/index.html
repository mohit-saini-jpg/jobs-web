How to Download PRSU Result 2026

Follow the step-by-step process given below to check and download PRSU 1st and 3rd Sem Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official website of Pt. Ravishankar Shukla University - prsu.ac.in.
  • Go to the examination or results section available on the homepage.
  • Look for the latest notification or link related to "B.Ed/M.A/M.C.A/M.Sc Semester Exam Dec-Jan 2025-2026 Result".
  • Click on the provided result PDF link, or directly open the link https://www.prsu.ac.in/admin/theme/examination_uploads/07042026055506.pdf.
  • The result PDF will open on your screen.
  • Use the search option in the PDF (Ctrl+F) and enter your roll number to find your result.
  • Check your name, roll number, subject-wise marks and result status.
  • Download the PDF and save or print it for future reference.
