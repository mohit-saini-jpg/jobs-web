CG Vyapam M.Sc Nursing 2026 Application Form

Candidates must apply online exclusively through the official CG Vyapam website (vyapam.cgstate.gov.in). Before starting the application, carefully read the official notification to understand the eligibility criteria, required documents, and other guidelines.
During the process, upload scanned copies of necessary documents (such as photographs, signatures, and academic certificates) and pay the exam fee via online payment modes like net banking, credit/debit card, or UPI.

How to Apply for CG Vyapam MSCN26 Entrance Exam 2026

Follow these step-by-step instructions to complete your application successfully:
  • Visit the official website: vyapam.cgstate.gov.in.
  • Locate and click on “MSCN26 – M.Sc Nursing Entrance Examination 2026” under the online application section.
  • Register by providing basic details like name, mobile number, email, and date of birth to generate login credentials.
  • Log in and fill in personal, academic, and category-related information accurately.
  • Upload the required scanned documents in the specified format and size.
  • Pay the application/exam fee through the available online payment options.
  • Preview the entire form for any errors, submit it, and download the confirmation page/printout for future reference.

CG Vyapam MSCN26 Eligibility Criteria 2026

To be eligible for the CG Vyapam M.Sc Nursing (MSCN26) Entrance Examination 2026 and subsequent admission to M.Sc Nursing programs in Chhattisgarh, candidates must fulfil the following criteria (as per Indian Nursing Council norms and Chhattisgarh state rules):

Educational Qualification:

  • Must have passed B.Sc Nursing (Basic) / B.Sc (Hons) Nursing / Post Basic B.Sc Nursing from an institution recognized by the Indian Nursing Council (INC).

Minimum Marks:

  • At least 55% aggregate marks in the qualifying degree (B.Sc Nursing / Post Basic B.Sc Nursing).
  • Relaxation to 50% for candidates belonging to SC/ST/OBC categories (as per state reservation norms).

Registration:

  • Must be a Registered Nurse (RN) and Registered Midwife (RM) with any State Nursing Registration Council / Indian Nursing Council.

Work Experience:

  • Minimum 1 year of work experience (after registration as RN & RM) for candidates with Basic B.Sc Nursing.
  • Post Basic B.Sc Nursing candidates may have different experience requirements—check the official Vibhagiya Pravesh Niyam PDF for exact details.

Other Requirements:

  • Candidates must be Indian citizens.
  • Preference/reservation may apply for Chhattisgarh domicile holders (as per state policy for seats and fee refunds).
  • No specific upper age limit is typically mentioned for M.Sc Nursing entrances in CG, but confirm in the official notification.

CG Vyapam MSCN26 Application Fee Refund Policy

The examination fee is non-refundable in general and must be paid during the online submission process. However, Chhattisgarh resident candidates who appear for the exam are eligible for a refund of the fee.
The refund amount will be transferred directly to the same bank account used for payment. Non-resident candidates (from outside Chhattisgarh) are not eligible for any refund. Refer to the official notification for exact refund procedures and timelines.

CG Vyapam MSCN26 Admit Card 2026

The admit card (hall ticket) for the CG Vyapam MSCN26 exam will be released online around 04 May 2026 (tentative). Candidates can download it from the official website using their registration number/login credentials.
The admit card contains important details such as the candidate's name, photograph, roll number, exam centre address, date, and timing. It is mandatory to carry the printed admit card along with a valid photo ID to the exam hall.

CG Vyapam MSCN26 Exam Pattern 2026

The exam is designed to assess candidates' knowledge in nursing subjects at the undergraduate level.
  • Duration: 2 hours 15 minutes
  • Mode: Offline (OMR-based pen-and-paper test)
  • Question Type: Multiple-choice questions (MCQs)
  • Subjects: Nursing-related undergraduate topics (detailed syllabus available in the official notification)
  • Marking Scheme and Total Questions: As per the official norms in the notification (typically includes no negative marking; check the latest PDF for exact details).

CG Vyapam MSCN26 Exam Centres

The examination will be held in two major cities in Chhattisgarh:
  • Raipur
  • Bilaspur
CG Vyapam allocates the exam centre based on the candidate's preference (if provided during application) and availability. No requests for centre changes are usually entertained after submission.

CG Vyapam MSCN26 Result 2026

Results for the CG Vyapam MSCN26 Entrance Examination will be declared online on the official website after the exam. Candidates can access their results and scorecards by logging in with their registration number and other credentials.
A merit list will be prepared based on performance, which determines eligibility for counselling and admission to M.Sc Nursing programs in Chhattisgarh.
