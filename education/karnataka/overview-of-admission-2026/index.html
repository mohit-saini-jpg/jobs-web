Christ University Entrance Test Pattern 2026

The BBA Entrance Test (ET) is online, 90 minutes duration with 120 MCQs. Typical sections for BBA:

  • English Language, Comprehension & Verbal Reasoning
  • General Knowledge & Current Affairs
  • Logical & Analytical Reasoning
  • Mathematics / Data Analysis
  • Fundamentals of Accounting & Management

Negative marking of 0.25 applies. Admit card released 2–3 days before the test.
