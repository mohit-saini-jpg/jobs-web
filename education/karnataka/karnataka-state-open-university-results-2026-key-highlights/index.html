Steps to Check Result

Follow the step-by-step instructions below to check and download KSOU M.Sc Result 2026 for III Semester M.Sc Mathematics revaluation:

  • Visit the official KSOU result portal at ksouportal.com or directly open https://ksouportal.com/results/ksouresult.aspx.
  • On the results page, select the relevant result option for "III Semester M.Sc Mathematics (2023-24 January Cycle & Repeaters) Revaluation Result" from the available list or dropdown.
  • Enter your registration number, roll number or enrollment number as required on the screen.
  • Provide any additional details such as programme or semester if asked.
  • Click on the submit or view result button to proceed.
  • Your KSOU M.Sc 3rd Semester provisional revaluation result will be displayed on the screen.
  • Check your personal details, internal and external marks, and overall result status carefully.
  • Download or print the result copy for future reference and academic record.
