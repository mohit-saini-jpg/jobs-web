Details

Karnataka State Law University has officially released the KSLU Results 2026 for various UG and PG law programmes, including LLB, BA LLB, BBA LLB, BCom LLB and postgraduate courses for the latest semester examinations. The results have been published online for different semesters such as 1st, 3rd, 5th and other applicable odd or even semester exams conducted in the recent academic session.

KSLU Result 2026 has been announced online on the official website - https://kslu.ed-it.in/. Students can check and download their Karnataka State Law University results by visiting the result portal and entering their exam details such as register number and semester. After accessing the marksheet, candidates should carefully verify their personal details, subject-wise marks, total, result status and in case of any discrepancy or error, they must immediately contact the university or college examination cell for necessary correction.

Steps to Check KSLU Result 2026

Follow the step-by-step process below to check KSLU Results 2026 online in a simple and user-friendly manner:

  1. Visit the official KSLU result portal - https://kslu.ed-it.in/result1.aspx
  2. On the result page, select your course and semester from the available options (LLB, BA LLB, BBA LLB, BCom LLB or PG)
  3. Enter your Register Number or Roll Number correctly in the given field
  4. Verify the entered details and click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button
  5. Your KSLU Result 2026 will appear on the screen, showing subject-wise marks and overall result status
  6. Download the result page as a PDF or take a printout for future reference and admission or revaluation purposes
