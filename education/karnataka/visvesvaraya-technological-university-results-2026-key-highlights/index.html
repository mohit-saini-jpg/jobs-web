Steps to Check VTU Result 2026

Students can check VTU Result 2026 online through the official result server by selecting the correct course category and entering their USN. The process is simple and works for B.E and B.Arch results across all regions.

Follow these steps to check VTU Result 2026:

  1. Visit the official VTU result portal at https://results.vtu.ac.in/indexD5J6.php.
  2. On the page, find the relevant result link for B.E or B.Arch for all regions.
  3. If multiple categories are shown, select the correct tab such as CBCS, CBCS RV, NON-CBCS or NON-CBCS RV based on your course and exam type.
  4. Click on the appropriate B.E or B.Arch result link.
  5. Enter your USN (University Seat Number) in the required field.
  6. Fill in the captcha code displayed on the screen and click the submit/view result button.
  7. Your VTU Result 2026 will appear on the screen with subject-wise marks, total marks and result status.
  8. Download, print or save a copy of the result for future academic use.
  9. If any discrepancy appears in your marks or personal details, contact your college or VTU examination authorities for clarification.
