How to Download VTU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your VTU Result 2026 online:

  1. Visit the official VTU result portal - https://results.vtu.ac.in/.
  2. On the homepage, click the relevant result link for your course, such as B.E, B.Arch, MBA, MCA, M.Tech, M.Plan, M.Sc, BBA, BCA, B.Sc (Hons), B.Plan or B.Design.
  3. Select the correct semester or scheme, for example B.E 5th and 6th semester [2021 Scheme] or B.E 1st, 2nd and 4th semester [2022 Scheme].
  4. Enter your USN (University Seat Number) in the result login field.
  5. Fill in the captcha as shown on the screen and click on Submit / View Result.
  6. Your VTU online result will appear on the screen with subject wise marks, SGPA/CGPA and result status.
  7. Download or print the digital marks card for future academic use.
