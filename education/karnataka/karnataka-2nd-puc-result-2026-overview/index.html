Detailed Information

Karnataka 2nd PUC Result 2026 is expected to be released soon in the first or second week of April 2026 by the Karnataka School Examination and Assessment Board (KSEAB). Students who appeared for the 2nd PUC (Pre-University Course) Exam 1 held from February 28 to March 17, 2026, are eagerly waiting for their results.

The board usually announces the Karnataka 2nd PUC results through a press conference followed by activation of the official result portal. Last year, the result was declared on April 8, 2025. Students can check their marks on the official websites karresults.nic.in and kseab.karnataka.gov.in using their registration number. This article provides complete details on expected date, time, steps to download the marksheet, and previous year trends.

How to Check Karnataka 2nd PUC Result 2026?

  • Visit the official result website: karresults.nic.in or kseab.karnataka.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the link for “Karnataka 2nd PUC Result 2026” or “II PUC Result 2026”.
  • Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth (or select stream if required).
  • Click on Submit or View Result.
  • Your marksheet will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of the provisional marks memo for future reference.
