4What Is the DTE Karnataka C25 Diploma Result 2026?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphThe Department of Technical Education, Karnataka (DTE Karnataka) has activated the DTE Karnataka Diploma Result 2026 link for candidates who appeared in the C25 scheme supplementary diploma examinations held in JuneJuly 2025. The result has been published in online mode only, allowing students from polytechnics across the state to quickly access their subject-wise marks and overall result status from any device
2paragraphDTE Karnataka conducts diploma exams under different schemes and sessions, and the current release primarily covers C25 scheme supplementary exams, while results for other schemes and main exams are announced separately on the same portals.
3paragraphSteps to Check and Download DTE Karnataka Diploma Result 2026
4paragraphFollow these simple steps to access your DTE Karnataka C25 Diploma Result 2026 and download your marksheet PDF.
5paragraphStep 1: Visit the official websites of the Department of Technical Education, Karnataka, at dtek.karnataka.gov.in or the BTELinx result portal at bteresults.in.
6paragraphStep 2: On the homepage, look for the flashing link or notification that says 'Diploma C25 Result 2026' and click on it.
7paragraphStep 3: You will be redirected to a login page. Enter your registration number and any other required details, such as your date of birth or roll number.
8paragraphStep 4: After entering the details, click on the Submit or View Result button.
9paragraphStep 5: Your DTE Karnataka C25 Diploma result will be displayed on the screen, showing your subject-wise marks, total score, and pass/fail status.
10paragraphStep 6: Download the online marksheet as a PDF and take a printout for future reference, revaluation applications, and admission processes.
