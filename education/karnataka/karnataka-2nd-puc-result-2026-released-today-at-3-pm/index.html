How to Check KSEEB 2nd PUC Result 2026 Online

Students can follow these easy steps to access their Karnataka PUC Results 2026:

  1. Visit the official website: karresults.nic.in and kseab.karnataka.gov.in.
  2. Click on “Karnataka 2nd PUC Result 2026” link.
  3. Enter your registration number and other required details.
  4. Click the Submit button.
  5. Your PUC 2nd Year Result 2026 will appear on the screen.
  6. Download and print the marksheet for future reference.

Tip: Always verify your marks and personal details in the result to avoid discrepancies during admission or document verification.
