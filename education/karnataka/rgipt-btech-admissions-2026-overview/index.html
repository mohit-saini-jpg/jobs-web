3About RGIPT
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphRajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT) is an Institution of National Importance established by the Government of India. It focuses on the energy sector, including petroleum, petrochemicals, green energy, AI, and digitalization. The main campus is at Jais, Amethi, Uttar Pradesh, with additional centres in Sivasagar (Assam) and Bengaluru (Karnataka). Final-year students shift to the Bengaluru campus for better industry exposure.
