Detailed Information

Karnataka State Open University has announced the KSOU Result 2026 for I Semester MA Sociology (2024-25 January Cycle & Repeaters) and III Semester MA Journalism and Mass Communication, III Semester MCom (2023-24 January Cycle & Repeaters) examinations conducted in October/November 2025. KSOU Result 2026 has been announced online on the official results website - ksouportal.com - through the dedicated result page at ksouportal.com/results/ksouresult.aspx.

Students can check and download their provisional results by visiting the KSOU result portal, selecting the appropriate programme and semester, and entering their enrollment or registration number along with other required details. After viewing the provisional marksheet, candidates should carefully verify their personal details, internal assessment marks, subject wise scores and overall result status and contact the Registrar Evaluation/KSOU Academic Platform in case of any queries related to internal assessment or discrepancies.

Steps to Check KSOU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your KSOU Result 2026 for MA Sociology, MA JMC or MCom:

  • Visit the KSOU result portal: https://ksouportal.com/results/ksouresult.aspx.
  • On the results page, select your Programme (for example, MA Sociology, MA Journalism and Mass Communication or MCom) and the appropriate Semester (I or III) from the dropdown list, if provided.
  • Choose your Academic Cycle/Year if the portal asks for it (for example, 2024-25 January Cycle or 2023-24 January Cycle).
  • Enter your Enrollment/Registration Number and any additional details such as date of birth or security code, if required.
  • Click on the Submit / View Result button to display your provisional result on the screen.
  • Check your name, enrollment number, programme, semester, internal assessment marks, theory marks, total marks and pass/fail status carefully, then download or print the result for your records and future academic use.
