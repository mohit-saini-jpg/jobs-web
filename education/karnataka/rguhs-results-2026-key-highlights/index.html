How to Check RGUHS Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your RGUHS Result 2026 for MBBS, BHMS, BSc Nursing, BPharm or any other course:

  1. Visit the RGUHS result portal: http://gnanasangama.karnataka.gov.in/rguresult/.
  2. On the homepage, select the result type/exam category, such as MBBS, BSc Nursing, BPharm, BHMS or other relevant programme.
  3. Choose the exam session/semester or year from the dropdown, if prompted.
  4. Enter your Registration Number / Roll Number / Student ID exactly as printed on your hall ticket.
  5. Click on the View Result / Submit button to display your marksheet.
  6. Check your name, college, course, semester/year, subject wise marks (theory/practical), internal and external marks, totals and result status.
  7. Download or print the online result for your records, counselling, internship, licensing or higher education applications.
