KCET Answer Key 2026 Out

The Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) has released the KCET 2026 provisional answer key on April 27, 2026, at cetonline.karnataka.gov.in. Candidates who appeared for the Karnataka Common Entrance Test can now download the subject-wise answer keys for Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology to estimate their scores before the official result declaration.

KCET Answer Key 2026 - Overview

The KCET 2026 answer key provides correct answers to all questions asked in the examination and serves as an essential resource for candidates to evaluate their performance. KEA has published the provisional answer key for all 16 sets of papers across all subjects. The exam was conducted on April 23 and 24, 2026, with the Kannada Language Test held on April 22, 2026.

How to Download KCET Answer Key 2026

Candidates can download the KCET 2026 provisional answer key by following these simple steps:

  • Visit the official KEA website at cetonline.karnataka.gov.in
  • Click on the link titled 'Objection link for provisional key answers' on the homepage
  • A new page named KEA Provisional Answer Key Objection Portal will open
  • Enter your KCET 2026 application number or registration number
  • Provide the first four characters of your name and date of birth
  • Select the exam type as 'Undergraduate Common Entrance Test (UGCET) 2026'
  • Enter the captcha code and click on the login button
  • The subject-wise answer key will appear on the screen
  • Download the PDF for your respective subjects and save for future reference

How to Raise Objections to KCET Answer Key

KEA allows candidates to challenge the provisional answer key if they find any discrepancies. The objection facility is available until April 30, 2026, till 5:00 PM. Candidates must submit objections through the official KEA portal with valid supporting documentation in PDF format.

Important requirements for raising objections:

  • Subject paper details must be clearly mentioned
  • Exam date should be specified
  • Exact version code of the question paper
  • Question number for which objection is being raised
  • Supporting documents or proof in PDF format
  • Proper justification for the objection

KEA has clearly stated that objections without the correct question number, version code, or proper proof will not be accepted. There is no fee required to raise objections against the KCET 2026 provisional answer key.

KCET Answer Key Marking Scheme

Candidates should use the official marking scheme while calculating their estimated scores using the answer key. The KCET exam follows a specific marking pattern where each correct answer carries marks, and incorrect answers may lead to negative marking depending on the subject. Candidates are advised to carefully match their responses with the official answer key for accurate score calculation.

What Happens After Objections

After the objection window closes on April 30, 2026, a subject expert committee will review all the objections submitted by candidates. Based on the review, KEA will prepare and release the final answer key. The final answer key is expected to be released in May 2026 and will be used for the preparation of KCET 2026 results. The result declaration is expected in the first week of May 2026.
