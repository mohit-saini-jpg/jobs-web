KCET Admit Card 2026 Out

Karnataka Examinations Authority has issued the KCET Admit Card 2026 for all eligible candidates registered for the Karnataka Common Entrance Test. The hall ticket is now available in online mode only, and no physical admit card will be sent by post. KCET Admit Card 2026 has been released online on the official website - kea.kar.nic.in - and through the dedicated login link shared by KEA.

Candidates must download and print their KCET hall ticket well before the exam date, as it is mandatory for entry into the examination centre. After downloading the admit card, students should cross-check their personal details, exam centre address and exam schedule, and immediately contact KEA in case of any discrepancy.

How to Download KCET Admit Card 2026

A clear and simple step-by-step guide helps candidates complete the download process without errors and also aligns with common search queries like “how to download KCET hall ticket”.

Step-by-step Process to Download KCET 2026 Hall Ticket

  1. Open a web browser on your mobile or computer and visit the KCET 2026 admit card login page at https://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/UGCET_Admit_Card_2026/Forms/Login.aspx.
  2. On the login screen, enter your KCET 2026 application number in the specified field.
  3. Enter your date of birth or password, exactly as used during the KCET registration process.
  4. Double-check the details and click on the "Login" or "Submit" button.
  5. After successful login, look for the option "Download KCET Admit Card 2026" or "KCET Hall Ticket".
  6. Click on the download button; your KCET admit card will open in PDF format on the screen.
  7. Save the PDF on your device and take at least one clear A4-size printout, preferably in colour, to carry to the exam centre.

If you forget your application number, you should first check your registered email, SMS or printout of the KCET application form. In case of technical errors on the site, wait a while and try again or use another browser and device.

Details Mentioned on KCET Admit Card 2026

Candidates should thoroughly verify all the details printed on the KCET hall ticket to avoid issues on the exam day.

Key details printed on KCET Admit Card 2026 typically include:

  • Candidate name and photograph
  • KCET 2026 application number and roll number
  • Date of birth, category and gender
  • Exam date and reporting time
  • Exam centre name, code and full address
  • Subjects or papers to be attempted (for example, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology as applicable)
  • Space for invigilator signature and candidate signature
  • Important exam day rules and instructions

If any major detail on the admit card does not match the details provided in your application form, you should contact KEA through the official helpdesk channels as early as possible.

KCET 2026 Exam Day Guidelines (As per Admit Card)

This section answers frequent user doubts related to what to carry, when to report and exam hall rules, which often appear in “People also ask” on Google.

Some important exam day guidelines for KCET 2026 are:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card to complete frisking and verification procedures.
  • Carry a printed copy of KCET Admit Card 2026 along with a valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar card, PAN card, passport, voter ID, driving licence or school/college ID (as permitted by KEA).
  • Do not carry electronic items like mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, earphones or any other gadgets inside the examination hall.
  • Stationery instructions (such as whether you need to bring a black/blue ball pen or any other item) should be followed exactly as mentioned on the admit card and in the KEA instructions.
  • Follow the instructions given by the invigilators and avoid any kind of malpractice or unfair means, as that may lead to cancellation of your candidature.

Reading and understanding all instructions printed on the admit card before the exam day will help avoid last-minute confusion and stress.

KCET 2026 Admit Card - Eligibility and Issuance Note

KCET Admit Card 2026 is issued only to those candidates who:

  • Successfully completed the KCET 2026 application form,
  • Paid the required application fee within the deadline, and
  • Fulfilled the basic eligibility conditions prescribed by KEA for appearing in the exam.

The availability of the admit card does not guarantee admission; it only permits the candidate to appear for the entrance examination. Final admission will be based on KCET scores, counselling, document verification and other criteria laid down by the Karnataka Examinations Authority and participating institutions.

Important Instructions About KCET Admit Card 2026

To help candidates avoid common mistakes, you can highlight the most crucial points in one place.

  • KCET 2026 admit card will not be sent via post or courier; it must be downloaded online.
  • Keep multiple copies of the printed admit card in case one is lost or damaged.
  • Ensure that the photo and signature on the admit card are clearly visible; if not, re-download and reprint the document.
  • Do not write or make unnecessary markings on the admit card, except where signatures are required.
  • Preserve the KCET admit card until the completion of counselling and admission, as it may be needed during verification.
