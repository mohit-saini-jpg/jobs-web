COMEDK UGET 2026 Application Form Correction Window

The COMEDK UGET 2026 Application Form Correction Window has started from 10 April 2026 and will remain open until 13 April 2026. Registered candidates can now edit select fields in their submitted application form online at the official website comedk.org.

The Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMEDK) has activated this one-time correction facility to rectify inadvertent mistakes in the COMEDK UGET 2026 application form. Candidates can make changes in limited fields such as photograph, signature, category details, and academic information. Critical fields like applicant name, email ID, mobile number, and test city preference cannot be changed. Candidates are strongly advised to log in immediately, review their details, make necessary corrections, and download the updated confirmation page before the window closes.

How to Correct COMEDK UGET 2026 Application Form (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official website comedk.org.
  • Click on the COMEDK UGET 2026 Form Correction or Application Edit link.
  • Log in using your Application Number / User ID and Password.
  • Review the submitted form and make changes in the allowed fields.
  • Upload correct photograph and signature (if needed) as per specifications.
  • Carefully preview all details before final submission.
  • Submit the corrected form and download the updated confirmation page for future reference.

Important Note: This is a one-time correction facility. No further changes will be allowed after 13 April 2026 (2:00 PM). The COMEDK authority will not entertain any correction requests via email, phone, or offline mode.

Non-Editable Fields (Cannot be changed):

  • Applicant’s Name
  • Email ID
  • Mobile Number
  • Test City Preference
  • Date of Birth
  • ID Proof details

Editable Fields (Can be corrected):

  • Photograph and Signature upload
  • Category details
  • Academic qualification details

Important Instructions for COMEDK UGET 2026 Form Correction

  • Corrections can be made only during the specified window.
  • Ensure uploaded photo and signature meet the prescribed format and size.
  • Review all information thoroughly before final submission as no further editing will be possible.
  • Any mismatch in details may cause issues during admit card generation or counselling.
  • Keep a printed copy of the final corrected application form safely.
