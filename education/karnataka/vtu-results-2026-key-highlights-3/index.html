How to Download VTU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your VTU Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official VTU result portal - https://results.vtu.ac.in/.
  • On the homepage, click the relevant result link for your course, such as B.E, B.Arch, MBA, MCA, M.Tech, M.Plan, M.Sc, BBA, BCA, B.Sc (Hons), B.Plan or B.Design.
  • Select the correct semester or scheme, for example B.E 5th and 6th semester [2021 Scheme] or B.E 1st, 2nd and 4th semester [2022 Scheme].
  • Enter your USN (University Seat Number) in the result login field.
  • Fill in the captcha as shown on the screen and click on Submit / View Result.
  • Your VTU online result will appear on the screen with subject wise marks, SGPA/CGPA and result status.
  • Download or print the digital marks card for future academic use.
