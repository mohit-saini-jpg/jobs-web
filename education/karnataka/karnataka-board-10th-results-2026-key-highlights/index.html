How to Check Karnataka Board 10th Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the step-by-step process given below to check Karnataka Board 10th Result 2026 on the official portal.

  • Visit the official Karnataka results website - karresults.nic.in using any browser.
  • On the homepage, click on the link that reads "Karnataka SSLC Main Examination Results 2026" or "SSLC Exam-1 Result 2026".
  • A login window for Karnataka SSLC Result 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Enter your SSLC registration number and date of birth in the respective fields exactly as printed on the admit card.
  • Click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button to proceed.
  • Your Karnataka Board 10th Result 2026 marks card will be displayed on the screen with subject-wise marks and total score.
  • Download the online marksheet and take a printout for future reference until the original mark sheet is issued by the school.
