KKHSOU Degree and Master Degree 2nd Semester Exam Timing & Shift Details 2026

All theory examinations under the KKHSOU Time Table Degree and Master Degree 2nd Semester 2026 will be held in a single morning shift. Full papers are scheduled from 9:30 am to 12:30 pm and half papers from 9:30 am to 11:30 am. Practical examinations will start from 10 am onwards. Exact timings for each paper are clearly mentioned in the official PDF.
