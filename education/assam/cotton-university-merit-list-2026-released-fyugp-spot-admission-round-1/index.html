4How to Confirm Admission After Checking the Cotton University Merit List 2026?
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThis section explains the step-by-step process for selected candidates to confirm their admission seat after their name appears in the Cotton University Spot Admission merit list.
2listordered
  • Visit the official SAMARTH-based admissions portal of Cotton University.
  • Log in using your registered credentials.
  • Check whether your name appears in the Round 1 merit list for your chosen major.
  • Keep your registered phone number active the university will call to confirm your admission offer.
  • Confirm your willingness for admission before 6:00 PM on June 15, 2026.
  • After confirmation, the university will process the payment link for you.
  • Complete the fee payment within the stipulated time to secure your seat.

8Cotton University Spot Admission 2026 - Waiting List Explained
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphCandidates whose names do not appear in the main merit list but are on the waiting list may still receive an admission offer. The university has published a waiting list for all major subjects. Vacancies arising from seat cancellations in the main merit list will be filled from the waiting list, subject to seat availability in each major subject.
2paragraphWaiting list candidates are identified by the category suffix "_WL" (e.g., UR_WL, OBC_WL, SC_WL, EWS_WL, STP_WL, STH_WL) in the official document.
3listunordered
  • Waiting list candidates should keep their registered phone numbers active.
  • Confirmation calls from the university will be the primary mode of communication.
  • Seat offers from the waiting list depend on vacancies created by main list candidates who fail to confirm by the deadline.
