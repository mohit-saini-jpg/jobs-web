How to Check Bodoland University Result 2026

Students can check Bodoland University Result 2026 and college-wise performance reports for FYUGP examinations directly from the official result-notice page. Since the results are published as PDF notices, candidates need to identify the correct notification and then search for their details inside the file.

Follow these steps to check Bodoland University FYUGP Result 2026:

  1. Go to the official website of Bodoland University at buniv.edu.in.
  2. Navigate to the "Result Notice" or "Notice" section or directly open the result notice page at https://buniv.edu.in/tender/result-notice.
  3. On the result notice page, look for the notifications dated 06-Apr-2026 related to: Re-checking Results of (BA/B.Sc/B.Com/BCA/BPT) FYUGP 2nd Semester Examination 2025 (Batch 2024) Results of (B.Sc.) FYUGP 1st, 3rd and 5th Semester Examinations 2025 (Batches 2025, 2024 and 2023) College wise performance reports for B.Sc FYUGP 1st, 3rd and 5th semesters

  5. Results of (B.Sc.) FYUGP 1st, 3rd and 5th Semester Examinations 2025 (Batches 2025, 2024 and 2023)
  6. College wise performance reports for B.Sc FYUGP 1st, 3rd and 5th semesters
