Detailed Information

Bodoland University has officially released Bodoland University Result 2026 for various FYUGP undergraduate examinations for the 2025 and 2025-26 academic sessions. The latest notifications include re-checking results of BA/B.Sc/B.Com/BCA/BPT FYUGP 2nd Semester Examination 2025 (Batch 2024), results of B.Sc FYUGP 1st Semester Examination 2025 (Batch 2025), results of B.Sc FYUGP 3rd Semester Examination 2025 (Batch 2024) and results of B.Sc FYUGP 5th Semester Examination 2025 (Batch 2023), along with separate college-wise performance reports for B.Sc FYUGP 1st, 3rd and 5th semesters. Students can open the result notice page, click on the relevant PDF notification for their course, batch and semester, and then check their marks and result status as given in the notice. After downloading the result or performance report, candidates should verify their name, roll number, course, batch, semester and subject-wise marks and contact the university or their college in case of any discrepancy.

How to Check Bodoland University Result 2026

Students can check Bodoland University Result 2026 and college-wise performance reports for FYUGP examinations directly from the official result-notice page. Since the results are published as PDF notices, candidates need to identify the correct notification and then search for their details inside the file.

Follow these steps to check Bodoland University FYUGP Result 2026:

  • Go to the official website of Bodoland University at buniv.edu.in.
  • Navigate to the "Result Notice" or "Notice" section or directly open the result notice page at https://buniv.edu.in/tender/result-notice.
  • On the result notice page, look for the notifications dated 06-Apr-2026 related to: Re-checking Results of (BA/B.Sc/B.Com/BCA/BPT) FYUGP 2nd Semester Examination 2025 (Batch 2024) Results of (B.Sc.) FYUGP 1st, 3rd and 5th Semester Examinations 2025 (Batches 2025, 2024 and 2023) College wise performance reports for B.Sc FYUGP 1st, 3rd and 5th semesters
  • Re-checking Results of (BA/B.Sc/B.Com/BCA/BPT) FYUGP 2nd Semester Examination 2025 (Batch 2024)
  • Results of (B.Sc.) FYUGP 1st, 3rd and 5th Semester Examinations 2025 (Batches 2025, 2024 and 2023)
  • College wise performance reports for B.Sc FYUGP 1st, 3rd and 5th semesters
  • Click on the appropriate notice link to open the result or performance report PDF in your browser.
  • Use the search option (Ctrl+F on computer) and type your roll number, name or college code (as applicable) to find your result entry in the PDF.
  • Check your subject-wise marks, total marks, pass/fail status or any college-wise performance statistics displayed.
  • Download and save the PDF on your device and take a printout if needed for future reference.
  • After verifying all personal and academic details, contact your college or the Bodoland University examination branch if you notice any errors or have queries regarding the re-checking or main result.
