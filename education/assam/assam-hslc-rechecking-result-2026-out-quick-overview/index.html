6About the Assam HSLC Rechecking Process 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThis section explains what the rechecking process involves and what students can expect from the outcome of their revaluation application.
2paragraphThe SEBA HSLC rechecking process is an official mechanism that gives students the opportunity to have their answer scripts re-examined by the board. The process typically involves two key components:
3listunordered
  • Re-totalling of marks: The board verifies that all answers have been evaluated and that marks have been added up correctly. This catches any arithmetic or clerical errors.
  • Re-evaluation/Revaluation: A qualified examiner re-examines the student's answer script to determine whether marks were awarded fairly and correctly.
4paragraphStudents who applied for the photocopy of their evaluated answer scripts along with rechecking were required to pay a higher fee (approximately Rs. 550 per subject, based on the previous year's fee structure), compared to those who applied only for rechecking (approximately Rs. 350 per subject).
5paragraphThe rechecking result carries the same official validity as the original HSLC result. If marks are revised upward, students may become eligible for scholarships, stream changes, or college admissions that were previously out of reach.
