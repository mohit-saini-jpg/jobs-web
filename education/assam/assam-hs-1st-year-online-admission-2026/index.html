Assam HS 1st Year Online Admission 2026

Assam HS 1st Year Online Admission 2026 application registration is currently open from 12 April 2026 to 22 April 2026 on the official DARPAN portal. Conducted through the Digital Admission and Registration Portal for Assam New (DARPAN), this is a completely online, merit-based process (no entrance exam) for admission to Class 11 (HS 1st Year) in Arts, Science, and Commerce streams across Assam. Eligible students who have passed HSLC (Class 10) in 2026 can apply online at darpan.ahseconline.in.

This complete guide provides exact dates, eligibility criteria, a step-by-step application process, required documents, and direct official links so you can complete your Assam HS 1st Year Online Admission 2026 without missing the fast-approaching deadline.

How to Apply for Assam HS 1st Year Online Admission 2026

  • Visit the official portal → Apply / Register Here.
  • Click on “Admission” / “Student Registration” and create your profile using a valid mobile number (same number cannot be used for multiple students).
  • Fill personal details, academic details (HSLC marks), and choose up to 5 institutions & streams.
  • Upload recent passport-size photograph and HSLC marksheet (as per specified format).
  • Preview and submit the application before 22 April 2026.
  • After submission, track your application status through the Student Corner login.

Documents Required for Assam HS 1st Year Online Admission 2026

  • HSLC (Class 10) marksheet and certificate
  • Recent passport-size photograph (as per portal specifications)
  • Valid mobile number (for OTP and future communication)
  • Caste / Category certificate (if applying under reserved quota)
  • Other documents as demanded by the chosen institution at the time of admission confirmation
