Steps to Check Dibrugarh University Result 2026 Online

Dibrugarh University publishes its results mainly as PDFs attached to course-wise notifications under the Results category. Students can follow the steps below to check their BA LLB, BBA LLB or B.Ed. result.

Step-by-step Process to Check Dibrugarh University BA LLB, BBA LLB, B.Ed Result 2026

  1. Visit the official Dibrugarh University results page at https://www.dibru.ac.in/categories/results.
  2. On the results page, scroll through the list of notifications and locate: “Result of the 7th Semester Five Year Integrated B.A. LLB (Hons.) (Regular/Backlog) Examination held in November-December 2025”, “Result of the 1st Semester Five Year Integrated B.B.A LLB (Hons.) (Regular/Backlog) / BBA LLB (Non Hons) Examination held in December 2025”, or “Result of 1st and 3rd Semester B.Ed. Examination 2025, held in December 2025”.
  3. “Result of the 7th Semester Five Year Integrated B.A. LLB (Hons.) (Regular/Backlog) Examination held in November-December 2025”,
  4. “Result of the 1st Semester Five Year Integrated B.B.A LLB (Hons.) (Regular/Backlog) / BBA LLB (Non Hons) Examination held in December 2025”, or
  5. “Result of 1st and 3rd Semester B.Ed. Examination 2025, held in December 2025”.
  6. Click on the relevant result notification link for your course and semester.
  7. On the result notice page, click on the attached PDF link to open the result document.
  8. Once the PDF opens, use the search option (Ctrl+F) and type your roll number to quickly locate your entry in the list.
  9. Check your result status (pass/fail) and any remarks or division indicated in the PDF.
  10. Download the PDF or print a copy of the result for your personal record and for future academic purposes.
