Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree with minimum 60% marks (55% for SC/ST/PwD) from a recognized university.​

  • Valid score in CAT, MAT, or CMAT (no fixed cutoff; used for shortlisting).​
  • Final-year students eligible provisionally with proof of completion.​
  • Reservations follow Government of India norms for OBC-NCL, SC/ST, EWS, and PwD.​

Step-by-Step Application Guide

The process combines a downloadable PDF form with online submission via Google Form.​

  • Download brochure and form from the official PDF link above.​
  • Complete the form with personal details, academics, entrance scores, and attach required annexures.​
  • Pay the application fee (typically INR 1,000-1,200) via bank transfer or online (details in brochure).​
  • Scan the filled form, certificates, photo, fee receipt, and proofs.​
  • Submit everything through the Google Form: Click Here

Selection Process

Shortlisting relies on entrance exam scores, followed by Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI).​

  • Composite score: ~70% entrance test, 20% GD/PI, 10% academics (standard for NIT Silchar).​
  • Merit list published online; selected candidates verify documents on campus.​
