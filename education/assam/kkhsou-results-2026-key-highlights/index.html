How to Check KKHSOU Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your KKHSOU MA 2nd Semester Result 2026 for Assamese or English:

  1. Visit the official KKHSOU website: https://kkhsou.ac.in.

  7. If a PDF result list is attached, open it and use Ctrl+F to search by your Enrollment/Registration Number or Name; if an e-marksheet link is provided, proceed to the Online E-Marksheet portal at https://eservices.kkhsou.ac.in/e-marksheet/.
  8. On the e-marksheet page, select MA – II Semester (MA_2) and enter your Enrollment Number, then submit to view and download your marksheet.
