Detailed Information

Krishna Kanta Handiqui State Open University has announced the KKHSOU Result 2026 for the MBA 2nd Semester (NEP 2024) examination, providing a major update for postgraduate management students enrolled under the new education framework. KKHSOU Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - kkhsou.ac.in - through a dedicated news/result notification for “Result of the MBA - II Semester (NEP 2024)”.

MBA candidates can check and download their result by opening the result notification, accessing the attached result PDF or result server link and then entering their enrollment or roll number as required to view the marks. After checking their scorecard, students should verify their personal details, course name, semester, subject wise marks and overall result status, and contact the university examination section in case of any discrepancy.

How to Download KKHSOU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your KKHSOU MBA 2nd Semester (NEP 2024) Result 2026:

  • Visit the KKHSOU official website: https://kkhsou.ac.in.
  • Navigate to the News/Results section or directly open the result listing page: https://kkhsou.ac.in/web/index_news_all.php?c=dE9hTkRwd05wc3RrV1JjaTBDQ2VBZz09.
  • In the list, locate the item titled “RESULT OF THE MBA - II SEMESTER (NEP 2024)” dated April 1, 2026.
  • Click on this item or directly use the detailed link https://kkhsou.ac.in/web/index_news_details.php?p=5491 to open the result notice page.
  • On the notice page, click on the result PDF link or marks portal link provided for MBA II Semester NEP 2024.
  • If a PDF opens, use Ctrl+F and search with your enrolment/roll number to find your result; if redirected to an online result page, enter your enrolment/roll number and other requested details and submit to view your marksheet.
  • Check your name, enrolment number, course, semester, subject wise marks, total marks and result status, then download or print the result for future reference.
