How to Check ASTU Result 2026

Students can check ASTU Result 2026 for 6th Semester re-evaluation in a few simple steps using the official ASTU website. The result is typically published as a notification with an attached PDF or list, so students need to download the file and search for their roll number.

Follow these steps to check ASTU Result 2026:

  1. Visit the official website of Assam Science and Technology University at astu.ac.in.
  2. On the homepage, go to the Examination or Results/Notifications section, or directly open the re-evaluation result page at https://astu.ac.in/?page_id=39880.
  3. On the result page, look for the link titled "Re-evaluation Results of 6th (Regular/Repeater) End-Semester-Examinations, May-June, 2025".
  4. Click on the result link to open or download the official re-evaluation result file (usually in PDF format).
  5. After opening the PDF, use the search function (Ctrl+F on a computer or search icon in the PDF viewer) and enter your examination roll number.
  6. Check your updated subject-wise marks, total marks and result status to see whether your marks have increased, decreased or remained unchanged after re-evaluation.
  7. Save or print the re-evaluation result PDF for future reference and comparison with your original marksheet.
  8. If you find any mismatch or have doubts about your updated result, contact your college examination department or ASTU examination branch with your roll number, course details and relevant documents.
