Assam Science and Technology University has officially declared ASTU Result 2026 for the re-evaluation of 6th Semester (Regular/Repeater) End-Semester Examinations conducted in May-June 2025. How to Check ASTU Result 2026

Students can check ASTU Result 2026 for 6th Semester re-evaluation in a few simple steps using the official ASTU website. The result is typically published as a notification with an attached PDF or list, so students need to download the file and search for their roll number.

Follow these steps to check ASTU Result 2026:

  • Visit the official website of Assam Science and Technology University at astu.ac.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the Examination or Results/Notifications section, or directly open the re-evaluation result page at https://astu.ac.in/?page_id=39880.
  • On the result page, look for the link titled "Re-evaluation Results of 6th (Regular/Repeater) End-Semester-Examinations, May-June, 2025".
  • Click on the result link to open or download the official re-evaluation result file (usually in PDF format).
  • After opening the PDF, use the search function (Ctrl+F on a computer or search icon in the PDF viewer) and enter your examination roll number.
  • Check your updated subject-wise marks, total marks and result status to see whether your marks have increased, decreased or remained unchanged after re-evaluation.
  • Save or print the re-evaluation result PDF for future reference and comparison with your original marksheet.
  • If you find any mismatch or have doubts about your updated result, contact your college examination department or ASTU examination branch with your roll number, course details and relevant documents.
