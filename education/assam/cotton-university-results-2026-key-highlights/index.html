How to Check Cotton University Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your Cotton University BA 3rd Semester Result 2026:

  1. Visit the Cotton University Samarth portal: https://cottonuniversity.samarth.edu.in/index.php/site/login.
  2. Enter your User ID / Enrollment Number and Password, then complete any captcha/security check if shown.
  3. After logging in, go to the Examination / Result section of the dashboard.
  4. Select your programme (BA), batch (2024) and semester (3rd Semester) from the available options.
  5. Click on the View Result / Download Marksheet link to open your third semester marksheet.
  6. Check your personal details, subject wise marks/grades, total marks and overall result status (Pass/Fail/Backlog).
  7. Download or print the marksheet for your academic records, course registration and any scholarship or documentation needs.
