How to Check Assam HSLC Result 2026 Online

  1. Visit the official website of SEBA, sebaonline.org.
  2. Click on the link for Assam HSLC Result 2026.
  3. Enter your roll number and captcha code.
  4. Submit the details and view your result on screen.
  5. Download and print the provisional marksheet for future use.

Details on Marksheet

  1. The online result will display provisional marksheet details.
  2. It will include the student’s name.
  3. It will show the roll number.
  4. Subject-wise marks will be available.
  5. The total marks will be shown.
  6. The result status will also be mentioned.
  7. The original marksheet is usually issued later by the respective schools.
  8. Students should save the downloaded copy safely for future use.

Revaluation and Compartment
