How to Check ASTU B.Tech 7th Sem & B.Pharm 2nd, 4th Sem Result 2026?

Use the steps below to check your ASTU Result 2026 for B.Tech 7th Semester (Regular) and B.Pharm 2nd & 4th Semester (Repeater) 2025 online. These steps ensure that you reach the correct official ASTU pages and download your semester marksheet successfully.

  • Visit the official ASTU website https://astu.ac.in/ in your browser.
  • On the homepage, click on the “Results” option or directly go to https://astu.ac.in/?page_id=116.
  • In the results list, find the entry titled “Result notice for B.Tech 7th semester (Regular) and B.Pharm 2nd & 4th semester (Repeater), 2025” with the latest date 20.03.2026.
  • Click on this result notice (or on the “View Result” link from the table above) to open the detailed ASTU result notice page.
  • Follow the link given in the notice to the online marksheet portal, usually https://astuonline.in/studentresult.aspx.
  • On the ASTU online result page, select the Exam Year, Semester (7th for B.Tech or 2nd/4th for B.Pharm) and Regular/Backlog as applicable, then enter your Roll Number and Registration Number / Date of Birth.
  • Click on “View Result” / “Submit” to display your ASTU B.Tech 7th Sem result or ASTU B.Pharm 2nd/4th Sem result on the screen.
  • Verify your personal details, subject-wise marks, SGPA and overall result status, then download or print the online marksheet for future academic and placement use.
