How to Check ASTU B.Tech 7th Sem & B.Pharm 2nd, 4th Sem Result 2026?

Use the steps below to check your ASTU Result 2026 for B.Tech 7th Semester (Regular) and B.Pharm 2nd & 4th Semester (Repeater) 2025 online. These steps ensure that you reach the correct official ASTU pages and download your semester marksheet successfully.

  1. Visit the official ASTU website https://astu.ac.in/ in your browser.
  2. On the homepage, click on the “Results” option or directly go to https://astu.ac.in/?page_id=116.
  3. In the results list, find the entry titled “Result notice for B.Tech 7th semester (Regular) and B.Pharm 2nd & 4th semester (Repeater), 2025” with the latest date 20.03.2026.
  4. Click on this result notice (or on the “View Result” link from the table above) to open the detailed ASTU result notice page.
  5. Follow the link given in the notice to the online marksheet portal, usually https://astuonline.in/studentresult.aspx.
  6. On the ASTU online result page, select the Exam Year, Semester (7th for B.Tech or 2nd/4th for B.Pharm) and Regular/Backlog as applicable, then enter your Roll Number and Registration Number / Date of Birth.
  7. Click on “View Result” / “Submit” to display your ASTU B.Tech 7th Sem result or ASTU B.Pharm 2nd/4th Sem result on the screen.
  8. Verify your personal details, subject-wise marks, SGPA and overall result status, then download or print the online marksheet for future academic and placement use.

  1. Open the official ASTU website https://astu.ac.in in your browser.
  2. Go to the “Examination / Result / Notifications” section, or directly visit the results page at https://astu.ac.in/?page_id=116.
  3. In the list of result notices, locate: “Results Notice of B.Sc (Hons.) Horticulture (ASTU ERP) End Semester Examination, 2025” “Results Notice of B.Sc (Hons.) Agriculture (ASTU ERP) End Semester Examination, 2025”.
  4. “Results Notice of B.Sc (Hons.) Horticulture (ASTU ERP) End Semester Examination, 2025”
  5. “Results Notice of B.Sc (Hons.) Agriculture (ASTU ERP) End Semester Examination, 2025”.
  6. Click on the relevant result notice; a detailed page will open (for example, ?page_id=39718 for Horticulture and ?page_id=39715 for Agriculture), usually containing an attached PDF or an ERP link for viewing marks.
  7. If a PDF gazette opens, use Ctrl+F and enter your roll number/enrolment number to find your record; if an ERP/login link is provided, follow it and log in with your student ID/roll number, password or other credentials as instructed by your college.
