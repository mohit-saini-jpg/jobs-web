2What is CCMT 2026 and Who is It For?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphCCMT Centralised Counselling for MTech/M.Arch/M.Plan admissions is an online counselling portal through which institutes such as NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs offer admission to GATE-qualified candidates for MTech, M.Arch, M.Plan, and M.Des programmes. The counselling is conducted based on GATE scores, which remain valid for three years. Seats are offered across 5 regular rounds, 2 special rounds, and a national spot counselling round.
2paragraphNIT Agartala is conducting CCMT counselling 2026. Online registration, fee payment, and choice-filling for the regular round closed on June 8, 2026.

5How to Check CCMT Round 1 Seat Allotment Result 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphFollow these simple steps to access your CCMT Round 1 Provisional Seat Allotment Letter (PSAL) on the official portal.
2listordered
  • Visit the official CCMT website : ccmt.admissions.nic.in
  • Click on the Candidate Login section on the homepage
  • Enter your GATE Registration ID and Password
  • Click Submit / Login
  • Your Provisional Seat Allotment Letter (PSAL) will be displayed on screen
  • Verify all allotment details institute name, branch, category
  • Download and save the allotment letter for future reference
6What to Do After CCMT Round 1 Seat Allotment 2026?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphOnce the result is out, candidates must take action promptly. Here is everything you need to do after checking your allotment.
2paragraphOnce the seat is allocated, candidates must complete their online reporting process within the deadline. They will have to pay their seat acceptance fee and choose one of the available willingness options.
3paragraphAfter reviewing the allotment, candidates must select one of the following three options:
7Option 1 : Freeze
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphIf you are fully satisfied with the allotted seat, choose Freeze. This confirms your admission to the allotted institute and branch. Once candidates freeze the option, they do not participate in subsequent rounds of counselling for seat upgradation.
8Option 2 - Float
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphChoose Float if you want to retain your current allotment but also wish to be considered for a higher-preference choice in upcoming rounds. If a better seat is available in Round 2 or 3, it will be automatically upgraded; otherwise, the current seat is retained.
9Option 3 - Slide
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphChoose Slide if you want to be upgraded to a better branch within the same institute in subsequent rounds. Your current seat is retained if no upgrade is available.
10Option 4 - Surrender
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphCandidates can also surrender the currently allotted seat and participate only in the next round for better-preferred choices. However, a warning applies: if no seat is allotted in the next round, the surrendered seat is permanently lost.
