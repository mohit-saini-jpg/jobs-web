How to Download Tripura University Result 2026 Online

Follow the step‑by‑step process given below to check Tripura University Result 2026 for MA in Tourism Administration 1st Semester:

  • Open the official website of Tripura University in your browser: tripurauniv.ac.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the Examination or Results section where the latest result notifications are published.
  • Search for the link titled “Provisional Result of MA in Tourism Administration 1st Semester Examination‑2025”.
  • Click on the result link; the provisional result PDF will open in a new tab or will download to your device.
  • In the PDF, use the search option (Ctrl + F on keyboard) and type your roll number or enrolment number.
  • Check your subject‑wise marks, total marks and overall result status displayed against your roll number.
  • Download the PDF file and, if required, take a printout for future reference.
