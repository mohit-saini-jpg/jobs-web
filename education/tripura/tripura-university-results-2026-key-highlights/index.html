How to Download Tripura University Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your Tripura University IMD 9th Semester (English) Provisional Result 2025:

  • Open the direct provisional result link: https://tripurauniv.ac.in/result/uploads/pdf/1775465224_IMD_Eng_9_25.pdf.
  • Wait for the PDF to load in your browser; if it prompts for download, save the file and then open it using a PDF viewer.
  • Inside the PDF, look for your Enrolment Number/Name as per the tabular list.
  • Check your SGPA, CGPA, remarks and any course codes where less than required grade is obtained, if provided in the format.
  • Review the summary or statistics section (if present) for an overview of pass percentage and performance distribution.
  • Download or print the PDF for your records, higher education or scholarship applications.
