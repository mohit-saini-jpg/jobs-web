How to Check the Tripura University Results 2025?

Tripura University Results 2025, students should use the official university portal, locate their exam link, and enter their roll or enrollment number to view and download their individual marksheet.​

  • Visit the official Tripura University website: tripurauniv.ac.in.​
  • On the homepage, select the “Results” or “University Results” tab—often visible in the top menu or dedicated results section.​
  • Browse the list of latest results or use the search function for your program, course, and semester.​
  • Click the specific result link, which may offer three options:
  • “View” for direct PDF download (list of marks/pass/fail)
  • “Search Result” to enter your roll number for individual scorecard access
  • “External Link” for the Samarth portal, which requires your enrollment number and password.​
  • Enter your credentials as prompted, and submit to get your marksheet or result PDF.​
  • Download and print your result for future reference and official use.
