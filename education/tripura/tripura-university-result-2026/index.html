Tripura University Result 2026

Tripura University has been releasing results for various UG, PG, and diploma semester examinations in 2026 (corresponding to 2025 exam sessions). These include courses like M.Com, M.Sc, MA, B.Sc, B.Com (TDP/TDPH), FYUG-NEP batches, and others. Results are published as provisional PDFs or through search-based portals on the official website tripurauniv.ac.in.

On or around 10 February 2026, the university released the provisional result for M.Com 1st & 3rd Semester Examination-2025 in PDF format. This is accessible directly from the University Results page.

Tripura University M.Com Result 2026 – 10th February Release The latest update is the Provisional Result of MCOM 1st & 3rd Semester Examination-2025, declared around 10 February 2026 (result date noted in related documents). The PDF lists roll-number wise details, including subject-wise marks/status and overall result (Pass/Fail/Re-appear). Students can search for their roll number in the PDF to check their performance. Direct PDF link (from official portal): Download M.Com Provisional Result PDF

Revaluation, Supplementary and Further Process Students dissatisfied with marks can apply for revaluation/re-totaling or appear in supplementary/back exams per notifications from the Examination Branch. Check tripurauniv.ac.in for application dates, fees, and supplementary schedules post-result release. Applications are usually submitted through colleges or online portals within specified windows.
