Details

The Tripura Board of Secondary Education (TBSE) 12th Result 2026 announced on May 8, 2026 at 12:00 PM for students of the science, commerce, and arts streams. More than 50,000 students are waiting for the Tripura Board Higher Secondary result, which available online on the official website, tbse.tripura.gov.in, using the roll number and registration number mentioned on the admit card. Once released, students can check their scorecard and download the result from the official TBSE portal.
