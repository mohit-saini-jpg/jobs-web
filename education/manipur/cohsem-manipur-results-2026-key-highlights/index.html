How to Check COHSEM Manipur 12th Result 2026 Online

Follow the steps given below to check and download your COHSEM Manipur 12th Result 2026 online in a simple and error-free manner.

  1. Visit the official COHSEM result website using any browser: https://cohsemresult.nic.in/.
  2. On the homepage, click on the link that reads 'Higher Secondary Examination (HSE) Result 2026' or similar.
  3. A result login page will open where you need to enter your COHSEM Class 12 roll number as printed on your admit card.
  4. After entering the roll number correctly, click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button to proceed.
  5. Your COHSEM Manipur 12th Result 2026 marksheet will be displayed on the screen, showing subject-wise marks, total marks, and qualifying status.
  6. Download the online result and take a printout of the provisional marksheet for future reference until the original certificate is issued by the council.
