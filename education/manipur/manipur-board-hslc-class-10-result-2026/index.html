Manipur Board HSLC Class 10 Result 2026

The Board of Secondary Education Manipur (BSEM) has officially released the HSLC Class 10 Result 2026 on May 8, 2026. All students who appeared for the Manipur HSLC Class 10 examinations can now access their results online from the official result portals. Manipur Board HSLC Result 2026 has been made available on the official websites - manresults.nic.in and bsem.nic.in where students can download their provisional marksheets by entering their roll number and other required login details.

Students can check and download their Manipur HSLC 10th Result 2026 by visiting the official result portal and logging in with their roll number and other credentials. Once the results are declared, the result links are activated on the websites for students to view and download their scorecards.

After downloading the online marksheet, students are advised to carefully verify all details such as their name, roll number, subject-wise marks, total marks and qualifying status, and contact the Board of Secondary Education Manipur immediately in case of any discrepancy or error in the result.
