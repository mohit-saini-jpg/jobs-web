How to Download Dhanamanjuri University Result 2026

Follow the step by step process given below to check and download your Dhanamanjuri University LLB or BBA Result 2026:
  1. Visit the official notifications page: https://dmu.ac.in/notifications.
  2. Scroll through the latest notices and locate the result link titled: “RESULT: L.L.B 3rd Semester Examination, 2025 (Dec)” “RESULT: L.L.B 1st Semester Examination, 2025 (Dec)” “RESULT: B.B.A 5th Semester Examination, 2025 (Dec)” “RESULT: B.B.A 3rd Semester Examination, 2025 (Dec)” “RESULT: B.B.A 1st Semester Examination, 2025 (Dec)”
  3. “RESULT: L.L.B 3rd Semester Examination, 2025 (Dec)”
  4. “RESULT: L.L.B 1st Semester Examination, 2025 (Dec)”
  5. “RESULT: B.B.A 5th Semester Examination, 2025 (Dec)”
  6. “RESULT: B.B.A 3rd Semester Examination, 2025 (Dec)”
  7. “RESULT: B.B.A 1st Semester Examination, 2025 (Dec)”
  8. Click on the relevant result link to open the PDF document in your browser.
  9. Use the search function (Ctrl+F) to type your Roll Number / Name and quickly locate your entry in the list.
  10. Check your course, semester, subject wise marks or grades and result status as printed in the PDF.
  11. Download the PDF or take a printout for your records, revaluation purposes and higher semester admission.
