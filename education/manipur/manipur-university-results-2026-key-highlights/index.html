Manipur University has released new Manipur University Result 2026 notifications for various 3rd semester undergraduate courses conducted in November 2025 under NEP and old schemes. Students can open the relevant PDF result links for their course and check roll number-wise marks and pass status for the 3rd semester November 2025 examinations. After downloading the provisional result, candidates should verify their name, roll number, subjects and marks, and contact the examination section if any discrepancy is found.

Steps to Check Manipur University Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download Manipur University 3rd Semester Result 2026 online:

  1. Visit the official Manipur University Exam Result page: https://www.manipuruniv.ac.in/exam_result.
  2. Scroll through the list of latest results and locate the entries for your course, such as "Result of BA Philosophy 3rd Semester Examination, 2025 (Nov) (NEP)" or "Result of BSc 3rd Semester Exam 2025 Nov old back".
  3. Click on the desired course result link to open the PDF file in a new tab.
  4. Inside the PDF, use the search function (Ctrl+F) and type your roll number to quickly find your result row.
  5. Check your subject-wise marks, total marks and pass/failed/backlog status as mentioned in the PDF.
  6. Download or print the PDF result for your records and use it as a provisional marksheet for academic purposes.
  7. In case of any discrepancy, withheld result or query, contact the Manipur University examination section or your college with your roll number and a copy of the result notice.
