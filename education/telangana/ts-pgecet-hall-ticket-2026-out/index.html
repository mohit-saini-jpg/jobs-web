TS PGECET Hall Ticket 2026 Out

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) Hyderabad, on behalf of TSCHE, has released the TS PGECET Hall Ticket 2026 in online mode. The hall ticket download link is active on the official website pgecet.tgche.ac.in, and all registered candidates can now access their admit cards.

The Telangana State Post Graduate Engineering Common Entrance Test (TS PGECET) 2026 will be conducted from 29 May to 1 June 2026 in computer‑based test (CBT) mode in two shifts - 10:00 AM to 12:00 Noon and 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM. Candidates must download and print their TG PGECET 2026 hall ticket and carry it with a valid photo ID to their allotted exam centre.

How to Download TS PGECET Hall Ticket 2026
  1. Visit the official TS PGECET website pgecet.tgche.ac.in.
  2. Click on the “TS PGECET 2026 Hall Ticket” / “Download Admit Card” link on the homepage.
  3. On the login page, enter your Registration Number, select your Exam Paper for Entrance Test, and enter your Date of Birth in the prescribed format.
  4. Click on the “Download Hall Ticket” / Submit button.
  5. Your TS PGECET Hall Ticket 2026 will be displayed on the screen with your exam date, time and centre details.
  6. Check all details carefully and download and print at least 2-3 clear A4‑size copies of the hall ticket.

The hall ticket is available only in online mode; it will not be sent by post.

Details Mentioned on TS PGECET Hall Ticket 2026

The TS PGECET 2026 hall ticket includes crucial information about the candidate and the exam, which must be verified:

  • Candidate’s name
  • Father’s name (in some formats)
  • Registration number / Hall ticket number
  • Category and gender
  • Date of birth
  • Exam paper name (subject selected)
  • Exam date, day and shift timing (10 AM-12 PM / 2 PM-4 PM)
  • Reporting time and exam duration
  • Test centre address (centre name, code, city)
  • Candidate’s photograph and signature
  • Important exam‑day instructions

If any discrepancy is found in your name, category, photo or exam centre details, you should contact the TS PGECET helpline or TSCHE immediately before the exam.

Documents Required with TS PGECET Hall Ticket 2026

On the exam day, candidates must bring the following to the exam centre:

  • A printed copy of the TS PGECET Hall Ticket 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence, Passport, or any other government‑issued ID accepted by the authorities.
  • A recent passport‑size photograph, if specifically required in the exam instructions.

Candidates will not be allowed to enter the exam hall without the hall ticket and valid photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for TS PGECET 2026

To ensure a smooth TS PGECET 2026 exam, candidates must follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time printed on the hall ticket; latecomers after gate‑closing time may not be allowed entry.
  • Carry only essential items: TS PGECET hall ticket, valid photo ID and allowed stationery (as per instructions); follow all guidelines displayed at the centre.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless allowed), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the examination hall.
  • Follow instructions given by invigilators and those printed on the admit card; any unfair practice or violation of rules can lead to cancellation of your candidature.
  • Keep your hall ticket safe even after the exam, as it may be required for counselling and admission processes.
