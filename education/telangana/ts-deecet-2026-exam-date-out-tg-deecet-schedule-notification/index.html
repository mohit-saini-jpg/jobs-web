What is TS DEECET 2026?

TS DEECET 2026, also known as TG DEECET 2026, is the Telangana Diploma in Elementary Education Common Entrance Test conducted by the Department of School Education for admission into teacher education programmes in the state. Through this exam, eligible candidates can secure seats in two-year D.El.Ed. and D.P.S.E. courses offered by Government DIETs (District Institutes of Education and Training) and recognised private institutions.

The entrance test acts as a common gateway for admission into Telugu, Urdu and English medium D.El.Ed. seats and English medium D.P.S.E. seats in Telangana. Admission is granted based on the candidate’s rank, category, medium, and preferences exercised during the web counselling process.

TS DEECET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must ensure they meet the prescribed eligibility conditions before applying for TS DEECET 2026, as applications from ineligible candidates can be rejected at any stage.

Educational Qualification

  • Candidates should have passed or appeared for the Intermediate examination (10+2) or its equivalent as decided by the TG DEECET Committee.
  • They must pass the qualifying exam by the time of admission to the D.El.Ed. and D.P.S.E. programmes.
  • A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in the qualifying examination is required for general category candidates; for SC, ST and PwD candidates, the minimum aggregate required is 45%.
  • Candidates should have studied Telugu or Urdu as a medium at SSC/Intermediate level or as a language subject, as specified in the bulletin, depending on the medium they opt for in the entrance test.
  • Certain categories such as Polytechnic candidates and holders of specific non-equivalent certificates (like some Urdu Fazil courses) are not considered eligible, as clarified in the information bulletin.

Age Limit

  • Candidates must have completed 17 years of age as on 1 September of the year of admission.
  • There is no upper age limit for admission into D.El.Ed. and D.P.S.E. programmes through TS DEECET 2026.

Local / Non-Local Criteria

  • Candidates must satisfy the local/non-local status as per the Telangana Educational Institutions (Regulation of Admission) Order, 1974.
  • In general, 85% of seats are reserved for local candidates in the state, while the remaining 15% seats are unreserved and open to both local and non-local candidates as per the rules.

TS DEECET 2026 Application Process

The TS DEECET 2026 application process is fully online and must be completed within the notified dates using the official website of the Department of School Education.

Steps to Fill TS DEECET 2026 Online Form

Follow the step-by-step process given below to submit the TG DEECET 2026 online application:

  • Visit the official TS/TG DEECET website at deecet.cdse.telangana.gov.in.
  • Download and carefully read the TS DEECET 2026 Information Bulletin to understand eligibility, instructions and important dates.
  • Click on the “Fee Payment” link and enter basic details such as candidate name, date of birth, gender and mobile number.
  • Pay the prescribed application fee online through the available payment gateway between 15 March 2026 and 15 April 2026.
  • After successful payment, note down the Payment Reference ID / Reference ID generated by the system.
  • Click on “Fill Application Form” and log in using the Reference ID and other required details to open the online application.
  • Enter personal details, educational qualifications, local area status, category, medium, and exam centre preferences accurately.
  • Upload a recent passport-size photograph and signature in the specified size and format as mentioned in the bulletin.
  • Verify all the details carefully before submitting, as only a limited edit option is available later.
  • Submit the form and download/print the filled-in application for future reference and verification.

Application Fee Details

The application fee for TS DEECET 2026 is as follows:

  • SC, ST and PwD candidates: 450 rupees.
  • All other candidates: 500 rupees.

The fee must be paid only through the online payment gateway provided on the official website, and fees once paid are generally non-refundable.

How to Download TS DEECET 2026 Hall Ticket

TS DEECET 2026 hall tickets will be available for download on the official website from 15 May 2026 to 21 May 2026. Candidates must carry a printed hall ticket along with a valid photo identity proof to the allotted test centre on the day of the exam.

Steps to download TS DEECET 2026 hall ticket:

  • Visit deecet.cdse.telangana.gov.in.
  • Click on the “Download Hall Ticket” link for TG DEECET 2026.
  • Enter your Payment Reference ID / application number, date of birth and any other required details.
  • Click on “Submit” to view your hall ticket.
  • Download the hall ticket and take a clear printout, checking details such as name, photograph, exam date, time, medium and test centre.

Candidates are advised to report any discrepancies in their hall ticket to the authorities within the specified time, as per instructions in the bulletin.

TS DEECET 2026 Exam Pattern & Syllabus (Brief)

TS DEECET 2026 will be conducted as an objective-type computer-based examination with multiple-choice questions. Understanding the pattern helps candidates plan their preparation more effectively.

Key features of the exam pattern:

  • Mode of exam: Online Computer Based Test (CBT).
  • Question type: Multiple-choice questions (MCQs) with four options each.
  • Total questions: 100.
  • Total marks: 100.
  • Medium: The question paper will be in the medium chosen by the candidate (Telugu, Urdu or English), subject to the specific rules for language and common sections in the bulletin.

Broadly, the test is divided into parts such as:

  • Part I - General Knowledge and Teaching Aptitude (around 10 questions, 10 marks).
  • Part II - General English and concerned language (Telugu/Urdu) as applicable (around 30 questions, 30 marks).
  • Part III - Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Biological Sciences and Social Studies based on the syllabus up to Class X (around 60 questions, 60 marks).

The detailed section-wise distribution, medium-wise pattern and syllabus topics are provided in the official information bulletin, and candidates should refer to it for exact coverage.

TS DEECET 2026 Result, Rank Card & Counselling

As per the published schedule, TS DEECET 2026 results and rank cards are expected to be released on 30 May 2026 on the official website. Each candidate’s marks and rank will be made available online, and they must download their rank card for use during counselling and admission.

  • Candidates belonging to OC and BC categories must secure at least 35% marks in the entrance test to be assigned a rank.
  • There is no minimum qualifying mark requirement for SC and ST candidates for consideration against their reserved category seats.
  • Admissions into Government DIETs and Category A seats of private unaided institutions will be carried out through a single window system, based on TS DEECET 2026 ranks, reservations and options exercised.

Certificate verification is scheduled from 1 June 2026 to 5 June 2026, followed by web options entry from 6 June 2026 to 8 June 2026. Seat allotment is planned for 14 June 2026, and selected candidates must pay the prescribed tuition fee between 14 June 2026 and 17 June 2026 and report to their allotted institutions before the commencement of classes on 1 July 2026.
