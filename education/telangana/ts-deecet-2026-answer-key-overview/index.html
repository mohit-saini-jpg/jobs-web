3paragraphTG DEECET 2026 exam overview
4paragraphTS DEECET 2026 is conducted as a computer-based test for candidates seeking admission into two-year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed) and other teacher training certificate (TTC) programmes in Telangana. The exam comprises multiple sections with questions in different mediums (Telugu, Urdu, English) and follows a fixed pattern and marking scheme notified in the official information bulletin.
5paragraphTS DEECET 2026 preliminary key and response sheet details
6paragraphThe preliminary answer key provides the officially considered correct answers for each question, while the response sheet provides the candidate’s recorded responses as captured in the CBT. Together, these documents help candidates understand how their score will be computed and where they might have made mistakes.
7paragraphTS DEECET 2026 important dates (exam, key, result)
8paragraphAccording to the official schedule, TS DEECET 2026 CBT was held around 21 May 2026, the initial key and response sheets were released on 25 May 2026, and the result is scheduled around 30 May 2026. Candidates should keep checking the TS DEECET website for any updates or changes to these dates.

6How to Calculate Your TS DEECET 2026 Score Using Answer Key
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphTS DEECET 2026 marking scheme
2paragraphTS DEECET follows a marking scheme where each question carries a fixed number of marks, usually one mark per correct answer, and there is generally no negative marking for wrong attempts as per past patterns. The exact marking scheme for TS DEECET 2026 is mentioned in the official notification and information bulletin and should be checked for confirmation.
3paragraphExample of calculating expected score
4paragraphIf a candidate attempted 100 questions in TS DEECET 2026 and finds that 80 answers match the correct options in the preliminary key while 20 are incorrect, and if there is 1 mark per correct answer with no negative marking, the estimated score would be 80 marks. This tentative score can help candidates predict their chances of qualifying and getting a seat in D.El.Ed or TTC institutions in Telangana.
7TS DEECET 2026 Answer Key Objections: Step-by-Step Process
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphTS DEECET key objection window and rules
2paragraphThe Department of School Education provides a limited-time objection window for candidates who wish to challenge any answer in the TS DEECET preliminary key. Objections must be submitted within the dates notified on the official website, and any requests received after the deadline are typically not considered.
3paragraphDocuments and proofs required for objections
4paragraphTo raise an objection, candidates generally need to:
5listunordered
  • Visit the TS DEECET website and click on “View/Challenge Answer Keys”.
  • Select the question they want to challenge and choose the option they believe is correct.
  • Provide a valid explanation and upload supporting documents such as textbook references, official guides or other authoritative sources.
  • Review the objection details and submit the form, and in some cases, pay any applicable objection fee if specified.
6paragraphThe exam authority evaluates the objections, and if found valid, corrections are made in the final TS DEECET 2026 answer key.
8TS DEECET 2026 Eligibility and Next Steps After Answer Key
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphBasic eligibility for TS DEECET 2026
2paragraphTS DEECET is meant for candidates who aspire to pursue a Diploma in Elementary Education and related teacher training courses in Telangana. Applicants must meet eligibility conditions such as passing the qualifying examination (usually intermediate or its equivalent) with required minimum marks, age criteria and other norms stated in the official notification.
3paragraphTS DEECET 2026 result date and rank
4paragraphAs per the schedule and recent updates, the TS DEECET 2026 result is expected around 30 May 2026, subject to confirmation by the Department of School Education. The result will be prepared based on the final answer key, and candidates will be able to check their rank and score by logging in with their credentials on the official portal.
5paragraphWhat to do after TS DEECET result (counselling outline)
6paragraphAfter the result, shortlisted candidates will be called for TS DEECET counselling, during which they can exercise web options for D.El.Ed and related TTC colleges. The final allotment of seats will depend on the candidate’s rank, category, preferences and availability of seats in participating institutions.
