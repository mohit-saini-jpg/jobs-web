How to Check TG EDCET Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your TG EDCET Result 2026.

  1. Visit the official website: https://edcet.tgche.ac.in/
  2. Click the relevant result link for your course/semester (B.Ed/M.Ed 1st/2nd/3rd Semester).
  3. Enter your Hall Ticket Number and Date of Birth (or other required credentials).
  4. Click 'Submit' or 'View Result'.
  5. Your TG EDCET Result 2026 will appear on screen; verify details and click 'Download' or 'Print' to save a copy.
