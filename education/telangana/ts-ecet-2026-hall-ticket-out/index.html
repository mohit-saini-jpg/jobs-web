TS ECET 2026 Hall Ticket Out

Osmania University, on behalf of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE), has released the TS ECET 2026 hall ticket (TG ECET) on the official exam portal. Candidates who successfully submitted the TS ECET 2026 application form can now download their admit card in online mode only; no hall tickets will be sent by post.

As per the official schedule, the TS ECET 2026 exam will be conducted on 15 May 2026 in a single session from 9:00 AM to 12:00 Noon, and the hall ticket download has started from 9 May 2026. The hall ticket contains details like exam centre, reporting time, exam time, and candidate particulars, and is mandatory for entry into the examination hall.

How to Download TS ECET 2026 Hall Ticket

Candidates can download their TS ECET admit card 2026 by following these steps:

  1. Visit the official TS ECET website: https://ecet.tgche.ac.in (or ecet.tsche.ac.in as notified).
  2. Click on the “Download Hall Ticket” or “TS ECET 2026 Hall Ticket” link on the homepage.
  3. On the login page, enter your Registration Number, Qualifying Exam Hall Ticket Number and Date of Birth (in DD/MM/YYYY format).
  4. Click on the “Download Hall Ticket” / “Submit” button.
  5. Your TS ECET 2026 hall ticket will appear on the screen showing exam date, time, centre and candidate details.
  6. Verify all details carefully, then download and take at least 2-3 printouts of the hall ticket on A4‑size paper.

If you face login issues or forget your registration details, check your registered email/SMS or use the support links given on the TS ECET portal.

Details Mentioned on TS ECET 2026 Hall Ticket

After downloading, candidates should cross‑check the following details on their TS ECET admit card:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Registration Number and TS ECET Hall Ticket Number
  • Name of the exam: TS ECET 2026 / TG ECET 2026
  • Exam date: 15 May 2026 and exam time: 9:00 AM - 12:00 Noon
  • Exam centre name, code and full address
  • Stream/branch (Engineering/Pharmacy and relevant subject group)
  • Candidate’s signature and other personal details
  • Important exam‑day instructions and reporting time

In case of any discrepancy in the hall ticket (name, photograph, centre, stream, etc.), candidates must immediately contact the TS ECET helpline or exam authorities before the exam day.

Documents Required with TS ECET 2026 Hall Ticket

On the day of the TS ECET 2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the TS ECET 2026 hall ticket (clear A4‑size printout - colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Driving Licence Voter ID Any other government‑issued ID accepted as per information bulletin.
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Voter ID
  • Any other government‑issued ID accepted as per information bulletin.
  • Additional documents if specified in the instructions (for example, category/PwD certificate where applicable).

Entry to the examination hall will not be permitted without the printed hall ticket and valid photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for TS ECET 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of TS ECET 2026, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the hall ticket, so that verification and biometric attendance can be completed; latecomers after the gate‑closing time will not be allowed entry.
  • Carry only essential items: TS ECET hall ticket, valid photo ID and permitted items as per instructions; follow the dress code and allowed‑items list in the information bulletin.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not specifically allowed), Bluetooth devices, notes, books or any electronic gadgets inside the exam hall; such items are strictly prohibited.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the hall ticket and those given by invigilators; any unfair means or misconduct can lead to cancellation of the exam and disqualification from admission.
