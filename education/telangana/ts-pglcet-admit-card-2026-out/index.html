TS PGLCET Admit Card 2026 Out

Osmania University, Hyderabad on behalf of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE) has released the TS PGLCET 2026 admit card on the common LAWCET–PGLCET portal. The hall ticket is available online for all registered candidates who have applied for the Telangana PG Law Common Entrance Test for LLM admissions.

As per the official schedule and news reports, TS PGLCET 2026 will be conducted on 18 May 2026 in online computer‑based mode, in a separate shift from TS LAWCET. Candidates must download and carry a printed copy of the TG PGLCET hall ticket along with a valid photo ID to the allotted exam centre to be allowed entry.

How to Download TS PGLCET Admit Card 2026

Candidates can download the TS PGLCET 2026 hall ticket by following these steps:

  1. Visit the official TG LAWCET & TG PGLCET website: https://lawcet.tgche.ac.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the “Download Hall Ticket” link under TS PGLCET‑2026 (or combined “TS LAWCET & TS PGLCET 2026 Hall Ticket” link).
  3. You will be redirected to the hall ticket download page (like LAWCET_Hallticket_2026HT.aspx).
  4. Enter your Registration Number, Mobile Number and Date of Birth in the respective fields.
  5. Click on the “Get Hall Ticket / Submit” button.
  6. Your TS PGLCET 2026 hall ticket will be displayed on the screen, showing details of exam date, time, centre and candidate information.
  7. Verify all details carefully, then download and print 2–3 copies of the hall ticket on A4‑size paper.

In case you face login problems or your admit card is not visible, recheck your registration number and mobile number, try again after some time, or contact the TS PGLCET helpdesk given on the official site.

Details Mentioned on TS PGLCET 2026 Hall Ticket

After downloading the hall ticket, candidates must verify the following details:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Registration number and TS PGLCET hall ticket number
  • Exam name: TS PGLCET‑2026 / TG PGLCET‑2026
  • Exam date: 18 May 2026 and exam timing (shift)
  • Exam centre name, centre code and full address
  • Programme (LLM) and subject details
  • Candidate’s category and other basic information
  • Important exam‑day instructions and reporting time

If there is any discrepancy in the hall ticket (name, photo, category, exam centre, etc.), contact the TS PGLCET authorities immediately through the contact details mentioned on the official website before the exam day.

Documents Required with TS PGLCET 2026 Admit Card

On the day of the TS PGLCET 2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the TS PGLCET 2026 Hall Ticket (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Driving Licence Voter ID Any other government‑issued photo ID specified in the information brochure.
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Voter ID
  • Any other government‑issued photo ID specified in the information brochure.
  • Additional documents if applicable (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter for candidates availing such facilities).

Without the hall ticket and valid photo ID, entry to the exam centre will not be permitted.
