4Why Are TS Inter Supplementary Exams Important?
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThe supplementary exam system serves as a vital academic safety net for students who could not clear all papers in the main board examination. Understanding its purpose helps students make informed decisions about their academic future.
2paragraphStudents appear for the IPASE for one of two main reasons: to clear a failed subject (backlog clearance) or to improve their marks in a passed subject. Clearing the supplementary exam enables a student to:
3listunordered
  • Get promoted to TS Inter 2nd Year without repeating the entire 1st year
  • Improve their subject-wise marks and overall percentage
  • Qualify for entrance exam eligibility where minimum percentage requirements apply
  • Avoid losing an academic year
4paragraphThe marks secured in the supplementary examination replace the earlier marks in the respective subjects and are treated as the student's final score.
5How to Check TS Inter 1st Year Supplementary Results 2026
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphThis section walks students step by step through the process of accessing their IPASE marks memo online from official portals.
2paragraphStep 1: Visit the official TGBIE result portal tsbie.cgg.gov.in or results.cgg.gov.in using any browser on mobile or desktop.
3paragraphStep 2: On the homepage, look for and click the link labelled "TS Inter 1st Year Supply Result 2026" or "IPASE 1st Year Marks Memo 2026."
4paragraphStep 3: Select the result year as 2026 and year as "First Year."
5paragraphStep 4: Select the exam type as IPASE (Supplementary) and choose your category General or Vocational from the dropdown.
6paragraphStep 5: Enter your Hall Ticket Number exactly as printed on your admit card, along with your Date of Birth (DOB).
7paragraphStep 6: Click "Submit" or "Get Memo." Your marks memo will appear on screen.
8paragraphStep 7: Download the result and save multiple copies (on your phone, laptop, and cloud storage). Also take at least 5-6 printouts for use in future admission processes.
9paragraphImportant: If official websites are slow due to heavy traffic, try alternative portals like tgbienew.cgg.gov.in or wait a few minutes and refresh.

8TS Inter 1st Year Supplementary Passing Criteria 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphStudents must meet both the per-subject and overall aggregate requirements to be declared passed in the IPASE examination.
2paragraphTo clear the TS Inter 1st Year supply exam, students must score:
3listunordered
  • Minimum 35% marks in each individual subject (e.g., at least 35 out of 100 in every paper)
  • Minimum 33% overall aggregate across all subjects combined
  • Both theory and practical components must be cleared independently wherever applicable
4paragraphThere is no negative marking in the Telangana Intermediate board exams.

10What to Do After Checking TS Inter 1st Year Supply Result 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphKnowing what steps to take after accessing the result helps students transition smoothly to the next phase of their academic journey.
2paragraphIf you have passed:
3listordered
  • Download and save the digital provisional marks memo immediately.
  • Collect the original updated marks memo from your junior college within the stipulated time.
  • Submit the updated marksheet for 2nd year admission or entrance exam eligibility verification.

4paragraphIf you have not passed:
5listordered

  • Seek guidance from your college counsellor for the most suitable next step
