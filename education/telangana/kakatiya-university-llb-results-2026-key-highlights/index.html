How to Check Kakatiya University LLB Result 2026 Online

Students can easily check their Kakatiya University LL.B. results by following these simple steps:

  1. Visit the official Kakatiya University examination website at https://kuexams.org/ or directly access the UG results portal at https://kuexams.org/ugresults
  2. Navigate to the relevant result section and click on the LL.B. (3YDC) V Semester and LL.B. (5YDC) IX Semester March 2026 examination result link
  3. You will be redirected to the login page where you need to enter your hall ticket number or roll number as mentioned on your admit card
  4. Enter the security code or captcha displayed on the screen correctly in the designated field
  5. Click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button to proceed with result retrieval
  6. Your Kakatiya University LLB result 2026 will be displayed on the screen with your marks, grades, and pass/fail status
  7. Carefully verify all the details mentioned in the result including your name, hall ticket number, subject-wise marks, and total marks obtained
  8. Download the provisional mark sheet in PDF format by clicking on the download button for future reference
  9. Take a printout of your result and keep it safely until the original mark sheet is issued by the university
  10. In case of any discrepancies or errors in your result, immediately contact the Kakatiya University examination branch at 0870-2461491 for clarification
