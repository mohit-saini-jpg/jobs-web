6TG ICET 2026 Answer Key - Objection Window & Fee
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphCandidates who find any discrepancy in the TG ICET Answer Key 2026 can raise objections during the official objection window. As per the schedule, objections against the provisional answer key can be submitted from 30 May 2026 to 5 June 2026.
2paragraphA processing fee of ₹500 per question is applicable while raising each objection, payable through the prescribed payment modes on the portal. After scrutinising the objections and supporting documents, the authorities will release the final answer key, which will be used for result preparation.
3paragraphYou can describe the generic steps for raising objections as:
4listunordered
  • Log in at icet.tgche.ac.in using your TG ICET credentials.
  • Click on the “Objections on Preliminary Key” link.
  • Select the question number and exam session/set where you find an error.
  • Enter your proposed correct answer and justification with reference.
  • Upload documentary proof (standard textbooks, official sources) if asked.
  • Pay the objection fee and submit the form within the deadline.

7TG ICET 2026 Exam - Eligibility & Key Details (Brief)
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThis section satisfies broader informational intent for users who are also searching about the exam.
2paragraphTG ICET 2026 Eligibility Snapshot
3paragraphTG ICET is conducted for admission to MBA and MCA programmes offered by universities and colleges in Telangana. In general terms:
4listunordered
  • For MBA: Candidates should hold a bachelor’s degree of at least three years duration from a recognised university with minimum marks as specified in the notification.
  • For MCA: Candidates usually require a bachelor’s degree with Mathematics at 10+2 level or at the degree level, with minimum marks as prescribed.
5paragraphAlways advise readers to refer to the official TG ICET 2026 notification/brochure for detailed, category‑wise eligibility, reservations and age criteria.
6paragraphExam Pattern & Courses Through TG ICET
7paragraphTG ICET 2026 is held in computer‑based test mode and typically assesses Analytical Ability, Mathematical Ability and Communication Ability through objective questions. The exam acts as a common entrance for:
8listunordered
  • MBA courses in universities and affiliated colleges in Telangana
  • MCA courses in participating institutions across the state
9paragraphMention that exact pattern (number of questions, duration, section‑wise distribution) should be confirmed from the latest official brochure to avoid outdated details.
