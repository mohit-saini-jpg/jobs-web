University of Hyderabad PG Admission 2026

University of Hyderabad PG Admission 2026 application process is open. The University of Hyderabad (UoH) has invited online applications for various PG programs for the academic year 2026-27 based on CUET (PG) 2026 scores.

University of Hyderabad, an Institution of Eminence with NAAC A+ grade, has announced PG Admission 2026-27. Candidates who have appeared for CUET (PG) 2026 can apply for admission to 42 PG programs including M.A., M.Sc., M.P.A., M.V.A., M.B.A., and others. All applications must be submitted online through the official university portal. The last date to apply is 08 June 2026 for programs with interview component.

Eligibility Criteria for University of Hyderabad PG Admission 2026

Eligibility criteria for each PG program are given in the Prospectus 2026-27 available on the university website. Candidates must have passed the qualifying examination with minimum required marks as prescribed for each course. Admission is primarily based on CUET (PG) 2026 score followed by interview wherever applicable. Candidates are advised to check the detailed Minimum Qualifications and Criteria for Admissions on the official portal before applying.

Documents Required for University of Hyderabad PG Admission 2026

Candidates must keep ready scanned copies of all academic certificates, CUET (PG) 2026 scorecard, category certificates (if applicable), photograph, signature, and other relevant documents as mentioned in the prospectus.

Selection Process

Selection is based on CUET (PG) 2026 scores. For certain programs like MPA, MVA, MBA, etc., there will be an interview component. Final admission is confirmed only after physical document verification and reporting.
