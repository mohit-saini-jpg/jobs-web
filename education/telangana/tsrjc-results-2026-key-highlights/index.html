Details

Telangana Residential Educational Institutions Society (TREIS) has officially released the TSRJC Results 2026 for the Common Entrance Test (CET) conducted for admissions into Residential Junior Colleges across Telangana for the academic year 2026-27. The entrance examination was held for students seeking admission into Class 11 in various streams including MPC (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry), BiPC (Biology, Physics, Chemistry), MEC (Mathematics, Economics, Commerce), and CEC groups. TSRJC Results 2026 has been released online on the official website - https://treirb.cgg.gov.in/.

Students who appeared for the TSRJC CET 2026 exam can now check and download their scorecards by entering their hall ticket number and date of birth or other login credentials on the official portal. The result includes details such as candidate name, marks secured, rank obtained, and qualifying status.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the result scorecard and contact TREIS authorities in case of any discrepancies or errors.

How to Check TSRJC Results 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your TSRJC CET Results 2026 online:

  • Visit the official website https://treirb.cgg.gov.in/ or https://admissionhalltickets.cgg.gov.in/
  • Click on the "TSRJC CET Results 2026" link available on the homepage
  • Enter your hall ticket number and date of birth or other required login credentials
  • Click on the submit button to view your result
  • Check your marks, rank, and qualifying status displayed on the screen
  • Download the scorecard and take a printout for counselling and future reference
