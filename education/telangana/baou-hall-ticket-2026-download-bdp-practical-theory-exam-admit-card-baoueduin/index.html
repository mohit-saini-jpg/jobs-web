4How to Download BAOU Hall Ticket 2026 - Step-by-Step Guide
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphDownloading the BAOU BDP Hall Ticket 2026 is a simple online process. Follow these steps carefully to download your admit card without any errors.
2paragraphStep 1: Open your web browser and go to the official BAOU website at baou.edu.in.
3paragraphStep 2: On the homepage, navigate to the "Student Life" section or look for the "Examination Hall Ticket" link in the announcements/notifications area.
4paragraphStep 3: Click on the relevant hall ticket link select either the Practical/Project Hall Ticket or Theory Hall Ticket based on your examination type.
5paragraphStep 4: You will be redirected to the hall ticket login page. Enter your 10-digit Enrollment Number correctly in the required field.
6paragraphStep 5: Click the Submit button. Your BAOU Hall Ticket 2026 will appear on the screen.
7paragraphStep 6: Carefully verify all the details printed on the admit card your name, enrollment number, exam centre address, exam date, and exam timing.
8paragraphStep 7: Download the hall ticket as a PDF file and take a printout. Keep the printed copy safe until the completion of the examination.

6Important Instructions for BAOU June 2026 Exam
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphAll students must carefully follow the official guidelines before and on the day of the examination. These instructions are issued by the University Examination Cell to ensure a smooth examination process.
2listunordered
  • Download and print the hall ticket well in advance do not wait until the last day.
  • Always carry a valid photo ID proof (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence) along with the hall ticket to the exam centre.
  • Reach the examination centre at least 30 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • Students who have not paid their examination fee will not be able to download the hall ticket. Verify fee payment status in advance.
  • In case of any error in the hall ticket, immediately contact the Exam Controller or call the toll-free number 1800 233 1020.
  • A photocopy of the hall ticket is recommended as a backup.
  • Hall tickets must be kept safe until the final results are declared, as they may be required for future reference.
  • Mobile phones and electronic devices are strictly prohibited inside the examination hall.
