How to Check TS EAMCET Answer Key 2026

Candidates can follow these steps to check and download the TS EAMCET 2026 Engineering answer key once it is released today.
  1. Visit the official TS EAPCET website eapcet.tgche.ac.in in your browser.
  2. On the home page, click on the link that says "TS EAMCET 2026 Engineering Preliminary Answer Key / Response Sheet".
  3. You will be redirected to the TG EAPCET answer key login page.
  4. Enter your registration number, TS EAMCET hall ticket number and date of birth in the required fields.
  5. Click on the submit button to view the preliminary answer key, response sheet and master question paper on the screen.
  6. Download the TS EAMCET Engineering answer key PDF and save your response sheet for future reference.
  7. Compare your responses with the official key and calculate your probable score as per the TS EAMCET marking scheme.
  8. If you find any mistakes in the key, use the objection facility on the official website to submit challenges within the given timeline.
