  • Candidates must have passed a Diploma in Engineering, Technology, or Pharmacy, or hold a B.Sc degree with Mathematics as one of the subjects.
  • A minimum of 45% marks in the qualifying examination is required for general category candidates.
  • Candidates belonging to reserved categories (SC/ST/OBC, etc.) require a minimum of 40% marks in the qualifying examination.
  • Candidates must have a valid TG ECET 2026 rank/score to be eligible for counselling.

5How to Register for TG ECET Counselling 2026 - Step-by-Step Process
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThe TG ECET counselling registration process is entirely online. Candidates must follow the steps below carefully to complete their registration without errors. It is advisable to keep all required documents and login credentials ready before starting the process.
2listunordered
  • Step 1: Visit the official TG ECET counselling website at tgecet.nic.in.
  • Step 2: Click on the counselling registration link available on the homepage.
  • Step 3: Log in using your TG ECET Registration Number (as mentioned on your hall ticket), Hall Ticket Number, and Date of Birth (as recorded on your SSC marks memo).
  • Step 4: Pay the counselling registration fee (₹1,200 for general / ₹600 for reserved category) through the online payment gateway using a credit card, debit card, or net banking.
  • Step 5: Book your slot select your preferred Help Line Centre (HLC), date, and time for attending certificate verification.
  • Step 6: Attend certificate verification at the selected HLC on your chosen date (June 17-19, 2026).
  • Step 7: After certificate verification, exercise your college and branch preferences (choice filling) between June 17 and 21, 2026.
  • Step 8: Freeze your options by June 21, 2026.
  • Step 9: Check the provisional seat allotment result on or before June 24, 2026.
  • Step 10: Pay the tuition fee and complete self-reporting through the website between June 24 and 27, 2026.

Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
