How to Check TGSWRJC CET 2026 Result at rjcet.telangana.gov.in

Once the result link is activated, students can follow this simple step‑by‑step process to check their TGSWRJC CET 2026 result online.

Step-by-step process

  • Visit the official TGSWRJC CET website: https://rjcet.telangana.gov.in/.
  • On the homepage, find and click on the link that says “TGSWRJC CET 2026 Results / Rank Card Download”.
  • A login page will open; enter your Hall Ticket Number and registered Mobile Number or Date of Birth in the given fields.
  • Click on the Submit button to view your result.
  • Your scorecard with subject-wise marks and overall rank will appear on the screen.
  • Download the PDF and take 2-3 printouts of the rank card for counselling and future reference.
Details Mentioned on TGSWRJC CET 2026 Rank Card

After downloading the TGSWRJC CET 2026 rank card, students should carefully verify all the information printed on it. Any mismatch in name or category should be reported quickly during the counselling or helpdesk window.

Typically, the rank card will include the following details:

  • Candidate’s name
  • Hall ticket number
  • Date of birth
  • Category (SC, ST, BC, etc.)
  • Gender and region / local status
  • Subject‑wise marks obtained
  • Total marks and qualifying status
  • TGSWRJC CET 2026 rank
  • Allotted college details after seat allotment (updated during counselling rounds)
TGSWRJC CET 2026 Result - Important Instructions for Students
  • Keep your hall ticket and registered mobile number handy before visiting the result website.
  • Use a stable internet connection during result time to avoid page time‑outs or incomplete downloads.
  • If the site is slow or not opening, wait for a few minutes and try again instead of refreshing too fast, as heavy traffic is normal when results are published.
  • After checking the result, immediately download and save the PDF to your device and cloud storage so that you don’t lose access later.
  • Regularly check the official website and your SMS for updates on counselling dates, venue and instructions.
