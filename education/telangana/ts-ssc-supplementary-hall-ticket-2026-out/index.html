TS SSC Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026 Out

The Directorate of Government Examinations, Telangana has released the TS SSC Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026 for the Secondary School Certificate Advanced Supplementary Examinations (TS SSC ASE June 2026). Students who registered for the supplementary exams can now download their hall tickets from the official Telangana board website.

According to the official schedule, the TS SSC supplementary examinations 2026 will be conducted from 5 June to 12 June 2026 in a single shift each day. The hall ticket download link is active on bse.telangana.gov.in and on the TS SSC ASE 2026 page TGHTT26 (bse.telangana.gov.in/TGHTT26).

How to Download TS SSC Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026

Students (or school authorities) can follow these steps to download the TS SSC ASE June 2026 hall ticket:

  • Visit the official Telangana board website bse.telangana.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the link “SSC ASE Examinations - 2026 Hall Tickets” or go to the direct module TGHTT26.
  • Select the appropriate category (Regular / Private / OSSC / Vocational), if the option is given.
  • Choose your District and School, then enter Student Name and Date of Birth in the fields provided.
  • Click on Download Hall Ticket / Submit; the TS SSC supplementary hall ticket 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download the PDF and take a clear printout; students should collect a signed copy from their school if prescribed by the board.

In many cases, students are required to collect the printed hall ticket from their respective schools, which download and verify it via school login before distribution.

Details Mentioned on TS SSC Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026

The TS SSC ASE June 2026 hall ticket carries key information that students must verify before the exam:

  • Student’s name.
  • District, school name and school code.
  • Hall ticket number / roll number.
  • Exam name: SSC Advanced Supplementary Examinations June 2026.
  • Exam dates and subject‑wise schedule (5–12 June 2026) with timings.
  • Examination centre name, code and full address.
  • Candidate’s photograph (where printed).
  • Important general instructions and exam‑day guidelines for candidates.

If there is any discrepancy in name, date of birth, hall ticket number, subjects or exam centre, students must immediately inform their school authorities so they can contact the board before the exam.

Documents Required for TS SSC ASE June 2026

On each day of the TS SSC supplementary examinations 2026, students must carry:

  • A printed copy of the TS SSC Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026 (original school‑stamped copy where applicable).
  • A valid school ID card or another authorised identity proof if instructed by the school/board.

The Telangana board clearly states that no student will be allowed to enter the examination hall without the official hall ticket, so students should keep it safely until the end of all papers.

Exam‑Day Instructions for TS SSC Supplementary Exams 2026

Students appearing for the TS SSC ASE June 2026 must follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before 9:00 AM to complete verification, as the exam runs from 9:30 AM to 12:30 PM (or till 11:00 AM for some Science papers).
  • Carry only necessary items: hall ticket, ID, pens and permitted stationery; follow any additional instructions printed on the hall ticket.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless expressly permitted), Bluetooth devices, books, notes or any electronic gadgets into the examination hall; violation can lead to cancellation of the exam.
  • Sit on the seat allotted as per your hall ticket number and follow invigilators’ instructions throughout the exam.
  • Preserve your hall ticket carefully for all exam days and for checking results later on the official website.
