TS DEECET Hall Ticket 2026 Out

The Department of School Education, Government of Telangana has released the TS DEECET (TG DEECET) Hall Ticket 2026 for the Telangana Diploma in Elementary Education Common Entrance Test. The hall ticket download link is active on the official DEECET portal for all registered candidates.

As per the official schedule, TS DEECET 2026 will be conducted on 21 and 22 May 2026 in online computer‑based mode. Candidates can download their admit card from 15 May 2026 to 21 May 2026, after which the link will be disabled. Carrying the hall ticket and valid ID to the exam centre is mandatory.

How to Download TS DEECET Hall Ticket 2026

Candidates can download their TS DEECET 2026 hall ticket by following these steps:

  1. Visit the official DEECET website: https://deecet.cdse.telangana.gov.in.
  2. Click on the “Hall Ticket” / “Download Hall Ticket” link for TG DEECET‑2026 on the homepage.
  3. You will be redirected to the TS DEECET Hall Ticket 2026 download page.
  4. Enter your Registration Number, Date of Birth and Qualifying Examination Hall Ticket Number (as asked on the page).
  5. Click on “Get Hall Ticket / Submit”.
  6. Your TS DEECET Hall Ticket 2026 will appear on the screen with exam date, time and centre details.
  7. Check all details carefully, then download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies of the hall ticket.

If your details are not accepted or the page shows an error, cross‑check your registration number and hall ticket number, then contact the TS DEECET help desk if needed.

Details Mentioned on TS DEECET Hall Ticket 2026

After downloading, verify that the following details on your hall ticket are correct:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph.
  • Registration number and TS DEECET hall ticket number.
  • Exam name: TS DEECET‑2026 / TG DEECET‑2026.
  • Exam date and session: 21 May 2026 or 22 May 2026, with specific timing.
  • Exam centre name, code and full address.
  • Medium of exam (Telugu/English/Urdu) and category/course details, if printed.
  • Reporting time and gate‑closing time.
  • Important exam‑day instructions and rules.
Documents Required with TS DEECET Hall Ticket 2026

On the day of the TS DEECET 2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the TS DEECET Hall Ticket 2026 (clear A4‑size printout, colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card Voter ID PAN Card Passport Driving Licence Any other government‑issued ID accepted by TS DEECET authorities.
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Any other government‑issued ID accepted by TS DEECET authorities.
  • Additional documents, if mentioned (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter for candidates availing scribe facility).

Without the hall ticket and valid ID, entry to the examination centre will not be permitted.
