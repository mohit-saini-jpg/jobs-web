How to Download TS LAWCET Hall Ticket 2026

Candidates can follow the simple, step-by-step process given below to download their TS LAWCET 2026 hall ticket from the official website.

  1. Visit the official TS LAWCET website at lawcet.tgche.ac.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the "Download Hall Ticket" or "TS LAWCET Hall Ticket 2026" link.
  3. You will be redirected to the TS LAWCET 2026 hall ticket login page.
  4. Enter your registration number, date of birth and mobile number in the required fields.
  5. Click on the "Submit" or "Download Hall Ticket" button to proceed.
  6. Your TS LAWCET Hall Ticket 2026 will appear on the screen with all your exam details.
  7. Carefully check your name, photograph, signature, exam date, shift, and exam centre details mentioned on the admit card.
  8. Download the TS LAWCET 2026 hall ticket in PDF format and take 1-2 clear printouts on A4 size paper to carry on exam day.
