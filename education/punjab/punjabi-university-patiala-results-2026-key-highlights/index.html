How to Check Punjabi University Patiala Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your Punjabi University result online:
  1. Visit the official website - pupexamination.ac.in
  2. Click on the “Results” section available on the homepage
  3. Select your course and semester from the list
  4. Enter your roll number or required login credentials
  5. Click on the submit button
  6. Your result will appear on the screen
  7. Download and take a printout for future reference
