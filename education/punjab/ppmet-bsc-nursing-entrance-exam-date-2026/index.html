PPMET BSc Nursing Entrance Exam Date 2026

PPMET BSc Nursing Entrance Exam 2026 will be held on 31 May 2026. Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (BFUHS), Faridkot, has released the PPMET 2026 application form. Online registration is open from 27 April 2026 to 12 May 2026 for admission to B.Sc. Nursing courses in BFUHS-affiliated colleges.

PPMET BSc Nursing Entrance Exam 2026 will be conducted on 31 May 2026 by Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (BFUHS), Faridkot. The online application form is available from 27 April 2026 to 12 May 2026. This entrance test is conducted for admission to 4-year B.Sc. Nursing programme in government and private nursing colleges affiliated with BFUHS.

Candidates who have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English can apply. This complete guide covers PPMET 2026 important dates, eligibility criteria, application fee, how to apply online, exam pattern, syllabus and counselling process to help aspirants prepare better and successfully apply for Punjab B.Sc Nursing admission.

How to Apply for PPMET BSc Nursing 2026?
  1. Visit the official website bfuhs.ac.in or the nursing26online main portal
  2. Click on PPMET 2026 B.Sc Nursing Application Form link
  3. Register with basic details and generate login credentials
  4. Fill personal, academic and contact information
  5. Upload scanned photograph, signature and thumb impression
  6. Pay the application fee online
  7. Preview the form and submit
  8. Take printout of the confirmation page
