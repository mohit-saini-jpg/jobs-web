Notification DatePost Date: 07/05/2026

Punjabi University has officially released the latest UG, PG and Diploma semester results for the Dec 2025 and Apr 2026 examination sessions across multiple faculties. The newly declared results include BA Honours School courses, BA Honours in Gurmat Sangeet, MA Dance, MA Religious Studies, M.Tech (CSE/ECE/ME/SE/TE), LL.B, B.Sc (CSM), B.Voc, BJMC, and several Post Graduate Diploma programmes such as Health Fitness Trainer, Human Rights & Duties, Counselling Psychology, Women and Child Development and Punjabi Sahit Rachnatmik Lekhan. Punjabi University Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - results.pupexamination.ac.in.

Students can check and download their results by visiting the official examination result portal, selecting their course and semester, and entering the required details to view the marksheet, which can then be saved or printed for reference. After downloading the result, candidates should carefully verify their name, roll number, course, semester and subject-wise marks, and immediately contact Punjabi University in case of any discrepancy in the result or personal details.

How to Check Punjabi University Result 2026

  • Visit the official Punjabi University examination result portal at results.pupexamination.ac.in.
  • On the result page, look for the list of available results or directly open the respective course and semester result link provided above.
  • Click on the desired result link such as BA Honours, MA, M.Tech, LL.B, B.Sc (CSM), BJMC, B.Voc or PG Diploma result.
  • On the result page, enter your roll number or other required details (if asked) and submit to view your Punjabi University Result 2026.
  • Check your name, roll number, course, semester and subject-wise marks carefully on the marksheet.
  • Download or print the provisional result for your records and for use in further admission, reappear or revaluation processes if needed.
  • In case of any issue while accessing the result or any discrepancy in marks, contact the Punjabi University examination branch through the official channels.
