How to Check PSEB 8th Class Result 2026 Online

  1. Visit the official PSEB website: pseb.ac.in
  2. Click on the “Results” section or “Middle Examination Result 2026”.
  3. Enter your Roll Number and captcha code.
  4. Click Submit to view your scorecard.
  5. Download and print the provisional marksheet.
