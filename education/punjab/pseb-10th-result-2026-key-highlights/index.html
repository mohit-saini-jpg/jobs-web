The Punjab School Education Board (PSEB) has officially declared the PSEB 10th Result 2026 today, May 11, 2026, at 12:30 PM. Students who appeared for the Punjab Board Class 10 (Matric) examinations for the academic session 2025–26 can now check their results online.

The PSEB Class 10 result 2026 is now available on the official website at pseb.ac.in. Students can access their marksheets by entering their roll number and required login details on the result portal. The board announced the results through a press conference, following which the direct result link has been activated for students to check and download their scores online.

Students are advised to carefully verify all details mentioned on their marksheet, including personal information and subject-wise marks, and contact the Punjab School Education Board or their respective schools immediately in case of any discrepancies or errors in their result.

How to Check PSEB 10th Result 2026 Online

Follow the step-by-step process given below to check and download the PSEB 10th Result 2026 online from the official website.

  1. Visit the Punjab School Education Board official website pseb.ac.in in your browser.
  2. On the homepage, click on the Examination Results or PSEB 10th Result 2026 link when activated.
  3. Select the Class 10 / Matric result option from the list of available results.
  4. Enter your PSEB 10th roll number and name as printed on the admit card in the login window.
  5. Click on the Submit or View Result button to proceed.
  6. Your PSEB 10th Result 2026 marksheet will be displayed on the screen with subject-wise marks and overall status.
  7. Download the provisional marksheet in PDF format and take a printout for future reference until the original marksheet is issued by the school.
