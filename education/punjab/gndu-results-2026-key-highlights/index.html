Detailed Information

Guru Nanak Dev University has activated the GNDU Result 2026 portal for semester and annual system UG/PG examinations, allowing students to check their latest exam results with an 11 digit roll number. GNDU Result 2026 has been published online on the official result site gnduresult.org, which can be accessed directly as well as through the main university portal online.gndu.ac.in.

Both undergraduate and postgraduate students can view their 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th, 6th semester or annual exam results by selecting the appropriate result type (Semester/Annual System New UG/PG) and entering their roll number. After downloading the online marksheet, students should verify their personal details, subject wise marks, total marks and result status and contact the university examination branch or their college if any discrepancy is noticed.

How to Download GNDU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your GNDU Result 2026 for semester/annual UG or PG exams:

  • Open the official GNDU result portal: https://gnduresult.org/examination-results.php.
  • On the results page, click on the link “Semester/Annual System New (UG / PG) (11 Digits Roll Number)” or go directly to https://gnduresult.org/studentarea/gnduexamresult.php.
  • On the student result screen, ensure the correct result category/exam type is selected (as shown or pre-selected).
  • Enter your 11 digit Roll Number exactly as printed on your GNDU exam admit card or marksheet.
  • Click on the Submit / Search / View Result button to open your result.
  • Your result will display name, roll number, course, semester/year, subject wise marks/grades, total marks and result status.
  • Download or print the provisional marksheet for record, revaluation applications, scholarship, counselling or higher semester admission.
