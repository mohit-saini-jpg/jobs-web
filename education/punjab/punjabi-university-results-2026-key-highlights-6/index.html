How to Check Punjabi University Result 2026 Online

Punjabi University provides an online result portal where students can check their UG and PG semester results in a few easy steps. The process is straightforward and can be completed using a mobile or desktop browser with internet access.

Step by Step Process to Check Punjabi University Result 2026

  • Visit the official Punjabi University examination result portal at https://results.pupexamination.ac.in/t8/results/results.php.
  • On the result page, select or open the specific link for your course and semester, such as B.A. Hons, B.Sc. Hons or Bachelor of Tourism & Travel Management for the DEC-2025 exam.
  • If you are using the direct links, click on the appropriate result name for your programme and semester from the list.
  • Once the result page opens, enter your roll number or any other required login details in the fields provided.
  • Double-check the entered roll number to avoid errors and click on the "Submit" or "Show Result" button.
  • Your Punjabi University Result 2026 for the selected course and semester will appear on the screen, displaying subject-wise marks, total marks and result status (Pass/Fail/Re-appear).
  • Download and save the online marksheet or take a printout for reference until you receive the official mark sheet from the university or your college.
