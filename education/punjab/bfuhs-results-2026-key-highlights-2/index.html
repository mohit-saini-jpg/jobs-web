How to Download BFUHS Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their BFUHS B.Pharmacy 2nd Semester Result 2026 from the official website:

  • Visit the official website of Baba Farid University of Health Sciences at bfuhs.ac.in
  • Navigate to the 'Examination' or 'Results' section on the homepage
  • Click on the examination result portal link or directly visit examination.bfuhs.ac.in/eos/resultweb.aspx
  • Look for B.Pharmacy 2nd Semester MJ-25 result in the list of declared results
  • Click on 'View Result' button next to the B.Pharmacy 2nd Semester result entry
  • Enter your Roll Number in the first field
  • Enter the last 3 digits of your Registration Number in the second field
  • Click on the 'Submit' or 'Show Result' button to access your result
  • Your BFUHS B.Pharmacy Result 2026 will appear on the screen showing subject-wise marks and overall status
  • Check all the details carefully including name, roll number, subject-wise marks, and total marks
  • Download and save the provisional result copy for future reference
  • Take a printout of the result for your academic records
  • Verify your name, roll number, marks, and course details carefully after downloading
