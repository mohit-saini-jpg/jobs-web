How to Check Punjabi University Result 2026

Use the simple steps below to check and download your Punjabi University Result 2026 online from the official results portal.
  1. Open your browser and go to the official results website of Punjabi University: results.pupexamination.ac.in (you can also access it via links on pupexamination.ac.in).
  2. On the result page, either use the course list to find your programme and semester or directly click on the specific result link for your course such as B.Pharmacy Sem-IV DEC-2025 or BA LLB Sem-X Preponement NOV-2025.
  3. Click on the relevant Punjabi University Result 2026 link to open the result window for that examination.
  4. Enter your Roll Number in the designated field on the results page.
  5. Click on the submit or view result button to display your Punjabi University marksheet on the screen.
  6. Download the provisional scorecard or take a printout for your records until the original marksheet is issued by the university or your college.
