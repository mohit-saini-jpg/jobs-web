1paragraphPunjabi University, Patiala has officially released the latest UG and PG semester exam results for the 2026 result year for exams conducted in May, September, November and December 2025. The newly published Punjabi University Result 2026 includes B.Pharmacy Sem-IV DEC-2025, BCA (Hons with Research 3+1) Sem-II and Sem-III, M.Sc Honours Multi Disciplinary (FYIP) in Mathematical and Computing Sciences Sem-IX DEC-2025, B.Voc Computer Graphics Designing Sem-III and Sem-V, B.Voc (Beauty and Wellness) Sem-I, B.Tech Civil Engineering Sem-V and BA LLB Sem-IV, Sem-VIII and Sem-X Preponement results along with Punjabi Praveshka SEP-2025. Punjabi University Result 2026 has been released online on the official result website - results.pupexamination.ac.in, accessible through pupexamination.ac.in.
2paragraphStudents can check and download their Punjabi University result by visiting the official result portal, selecting their programme and semester from the course-wise list or using the given direct links, and then entering their roll number on the login page to view the marksheet. After downloading the online marksheet, candidates should carefully verify their personal details, course, semester, subject-wise marks/grades and overall result status, and contact the examination branch or college authorities immediately in case of any discrepancy.

4How to Check Punjabi University Result 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphUse the simple steps below to check and download your Punjabi University Result 2026 online from the official results portal.
2listordered
  • Open your browser and go to the official results website of Punjabi University: results.pupexamination.ac.in (you can also access it via links on pupexamination.ac.in).
  • On the result page, either use the course list to find your programme and semester or directly click on the specific result link for your course such as B.Pharmacy Sem-IV DEC-2025 or BA LLB Sem-X Preponement NOV-2025.
  • Click on the relevant Punjabi University Result 2026 link to open the result window for that examination.
  • Enter your Roll Number in the designated field on the results page.
  • Click on the submit or view result button to display your Punjabi University marksheet on the screen.
  • Download the provisional scorecard or take a printout for your records until the original marksheet is issued by the university or your college.
