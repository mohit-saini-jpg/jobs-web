How to Check BFUHS Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your BFUHS Exam Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official BFUHS examination result portal at examination.bfuhs.ac.in/eos/resultweb.aspx
  • On the result web page, locate the list of recently declared results
  • Find your specific course and semester entry such as BSc Nursing (NS21) 1st Semester MJ-25, BSc Nursing (NS21) 2nd Semester MJ-25, or BSc Nursing (NS21) 7th Semester MJ-25 from the result grid
  • Click on the "View Result" link corresponding to your course and semester
  • Enter your roll number or registration number in the required field
  • Click on the "Submit" or "Show Result" button to proceed
  • Your BFUHS Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen showing subject-wise marks and pass status
  • Check your marks carefully and verify all personal details mentioned on the marksheet
  • Download the result PDF or take a printout for future reference
  • Save the downloaded marksheet in a secure location for admission or verification purposes
