6MP PAT Answer Key 2026 - Objection Process
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphMPESB allows candidates to challenge the provisional MP PAT Answer Key 2026 within a specified objection window via the online portal. Objections must be submitted only through the official website; offline challenges are generally not accepted.
2paragraphStep-by-step process to raise objections
3listordered
  • Visit the official MPESB website esb.mp.gov.in and open the MP PAT 2026 answer key / objection link.
  • Log in using your application number, roll number, TAC code, Date of Birth, and other required details.
  • Select the question(s) you wish to challenge from your response sheet or the answer key view.
  • Provide the correct answer as per your understanding and upload valid supporting documents or proofs (such as textbook or official references) in the prescribed format.
  • Pay the objection fee per question (as notified by MPESB) through the available online payment modes.
  • Submit the objection form and keep the payment receipt and confirmation page for future reference.
7Objection fee and key points
Sr.TypeStyleItems
1listunordered
  • An objection fee per question is charged to discourage frivolous challenges, and this fee may be refunded if the objection is found valid and accepted by the board.
  • Only challenges submitted within the official objection window are considered, and late submissions are typically rejected.
  • The final answer key is prepared after evaluating all objections, and the decision of MPESB is considered final for result preparation.
