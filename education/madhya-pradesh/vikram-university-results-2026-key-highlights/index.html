How to Check Vikram University Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your Vikram University Revaluation or Retotaling result:

  1. Visit the official website - vikramuniv.ac.in
  2. Click on the “Notifications” section available on the homepage
  3. Find the relevant result link for your course and exam
  4. Click on the result PDF link
  5. Download the PDF and search your roll number using Ctrl + F
  6. Save or print the result for future reference
