RDVV Time Table 2026 LL.M III Semester Revised Exam Schedule Out

Rani Durgavati Vishwavidyalaya (RDVV), Jabalpur has officially released the revised RDVV Time Table 2026 for LL.M. III Semester (Regular, Ex and ATKT) Examinations 2025-26.   2. Navigate to the "Examination" or "Notice Board" section on the homepage.
  3. Look for the latest notification titled "REVISED Programme of The LL.M. III Semester Exam 2025-26".
  4. Click on the link to open the PDF file.
