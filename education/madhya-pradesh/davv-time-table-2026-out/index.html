How to Download DAVV Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the DAVV Time Table 2026 from the official university website. The process is simple and requires only a stable internet connection.

  • Visit the official DAVV website and navigate to the UG Time Table section at https://www.dauniv.ac.in/ug-time-table.
  • Locate your course group such as Art, Commerce or Science and find the relevant revised exam notification for your year and program.
  • Click on the desired PDF link corresponding to your course (e.g., B.A. NEP I Year or B.Com II Year).
  • The notification will open in a new tab. Review the exam dates, timings, and instructions carefully.
  • Download the PDF file to your device for easy access.
  • Take a printout of the timetable and save a digital copy on your phone for quick reference during preparation.

Students should check the official timetable carefully and keep a copy handy for effective exam preparation. Regularly visit the university portal for any last-minute revisions.
