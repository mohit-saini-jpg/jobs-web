MP Study Abroad Scholarship 2026

The Government of Madhya Pradesh has launched the MP Study Abroad Scholarship 2026 (Overseas Scholarship Scheme) to support meritorious Scheduled Caste (SC) students of the state in pursuing Master’s and PhD programmes in reputed foreign universities. This scheme focuses on candidates who secure admission in institutions listed in leading global rankings (such as QS or Times) and who come from families with limited financial capacity. The scholarship aims to reduce the economic barrier for talented students so they can access high‑quality international education and research opportunities. It combines tuition fee support with maintenance, contingency and travel assistance, making overseas study more affordable for eligible students from MP.
