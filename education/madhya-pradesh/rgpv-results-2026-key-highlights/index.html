Details

RGPV 3rd Sem Result 2026 has been officially released for Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) Computer Science students. Candidates who appeared for the 3rd semester examinations can now check their results for the academic session 2025-26.

RGPV 3rd Sem Result 2026 has been announced online on the official website - rgpv.ac.in. Students can access and download their results by entering their enrollment number or roll number through the official result portal.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned on their result. In case of any discrepancy, they should immediately contact Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya authorities for correction.
