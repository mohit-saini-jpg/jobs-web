How to Download Jiwaji University Exam Time Table 2026

Students can download the Jiwaji University Exam Time Table 2026 from the official university portal by following the steps given below:

  • Visit the official website of Jiwaji University.
  • Go to the Information Corner section and click on Timetable.
  • Locate the B.Ed or M.A/M.Sc/M.Com II Semester June 2026 timetable link.
  • Click on the relevant course timetable PDF link.
  • The timetable will open. Download and save the PDF file.
  • Take a printout for easy reference during exam preparation.

Note: Always refer to the official timetable and check all dates and timings carefully before the examinations.
