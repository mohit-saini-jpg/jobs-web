How to Download MCU Bhopal Date Sheet 2026

Students can easily download the MCU Bhopal Date Sheet 2026 from the official university website. Follow these simple steps to get your copy:

  • Visit the official website of MCU Bhopal at www.mcu.ac.in.
  • Navigate to the "Examinations" or "Examination Info & Results" section on the homepage.
  • Look for the link that says "Date Sheet of Theory Examination May – June 2026".
  • Click on the link to open the PDF file.
  • Download the timetable PDF and save it on your device.
  • Take a printout for future reference and exam preparation.

Make sure to go through the official timetable carefully and note down important dates for your subjects. Keep a copy handy during your exam preparation.
