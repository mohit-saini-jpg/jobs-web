UGC-DAE CSR PhD Admission 2026 Overview

UGC-DAE Consortium for Scientific Research (UGC-DAE CSR) has invited applications for its Ph.D. programme (2026-27) at its centres in Indore, Mumbai, and Kalpakkam. The programme offers doctoral research in Physics and related interdisciplinary areas with access to advanced facilities like synchrotron sources, neutron scattering, and low-temperature high-magnetic field setups.

Vacancies and Research Areas

  • Indore Centre: 20 vacancies – Properties of materials at low temperatures & high magnetic fields, thin films/multilayers, microscopy, spectroscopic studies, theoretical condensed matter physics, single crystals, non-linear optics etc.
  • Mumbai Centre: 02 vacancies – Physics of quantum magnets, multiferroics, topological materials, rare-earth intermetallics, strongly-correlated electron systems.
  • Kalpakkam Centre: 06 vacancies – Nanostructured materials, magnetism in carbon-based materials, wide band gap materials, energy materials, flexible functional thin films.

Eligibility Criteria for UGC-DAE CSR PhD 2026

For Indore, Mumbai & Kalpakkam Centres:
  • M.Sc. degree in Physics (2023 or after) with minimum 60% marks (55% for reserved & women candidates).
  • Must have qualified any one of the following: Valid CSIR-UGC NET (Category 1, 2 & 3) JEST 2026 (Rank within top 300) DST-Inspire Fellowship (Ph.D.)# GATE (Physics)
  • Valid CSIR-UGC NET (Category 1, 2 & 3)
  • JEST 2026 (Rank within top 300)
  • DST-Inspire Fellowship (Ph.D.)#
  • GATE (Physics)
#Final JRF offer for DST-Inspire candidates is subject to clearance of Level-II evaluation.

Application Fee

The official advertisement does not mention any application fee. Most candidates can apply free of cost. Confirm on the portal at the time of filling the form.

How to Apply for UGC-DAE CSR PhD Admission 2026

  1. Visit the official application portal: https://inhousejrf.csr.res.in/
  2. Register and fill the online application form.
  3. Indicate your preference of CSR Centres.
  4. Submit the form before 15 June 2026 (17:00 Hrs).
Apply Online Link: UGC-DAE CSR JRF Application Portal

Selection Process

The selection is a two-step process:
  • Screening of applications
  • Online interview of shortlisted candidates (tentative June/July 2026)
Selected students will be registered for Ph.D. with:
  • Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV), Indore (for Indore Centre)
  • Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) (for Mumbai Centre)
  • University of Madras (for Kalpakkam Centre)
