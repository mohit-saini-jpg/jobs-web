Detailed Information

Barkatullah Vishwavidyalaya (Barkatullah University), Bhopal has officially released the Barkatullah University Exam Result 2026 for multiple UG and PG courses across various semesters on April 29, 2026. The results have been declared for MED HI ID III Semester, MED III Semester, BED HI ID III Semester, BSc I-II-III-IV Semester Revaluation Result, LLB V Semester, BALLB IX Semester, BALLB Hons Part V Semester I-IX Semester, BCom I and II Year, MA 1 and 3 Semester Retotaling Result, and BLIB-MLIB 1 Semester. Barkatullah University Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - bubhopal.ac.in/1594/Online-Result.

Students can check and download their semester and annual examination results by visiting the official result portal and selecting their respective course and semester from the result list, then entering their roll number or registration number as required. The university has made provisional results available through multiple portals including bubhopal.icrp.in/erp and bubhopal.mponline.gov.in for different courses. Students are advised to carefully verify all personal details, subject-wise marks, and grades mentioned on their result marksheet and contact the university examination department in case of any discrepancies or errors.

How to Check Barkatullah University Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your Barkatullah University Exam Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official Barkatullah University result portal at bubhopal.ac.in/1594/Online-Result
  • On the result page, select your result category from the dropdown menu - UG, PG, UTD, IODE, or PHD/MPHIL
  • Scroll through the list of results and locate your specific course, semester, and result date
  • Click on the result entry corresponding to your course and semester such as MED III Sem, BED HI ID III Sem, LLB V Semester, or BALLB IX Semester
  • You will be redirected to the provisional result download page at bubhopal.icrp.in/erp or MPOnline portal
  • Enter your roll number or registration number in the required field
  • If prompted, also enter your date of birth in the specified format
  • Click on the "Submit" or "Show Result" button to proceed
  • Your Barkatullah University Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen showing subject-wise marks, grades, and overall result status
  • For PDF result sheets, click on the direct PDF link to open the result document
  • Check all marks, personal details, and semester information carefully
  • Download the result PDF or take a printout for future reference and admission purposes
  • Save the downloaded marksheet in a secure location
