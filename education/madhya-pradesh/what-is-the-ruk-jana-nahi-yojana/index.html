1paragraphThe Madhya Pradesh State Open School Education Board (MPSOS), Bhopal, has officially launched the registration process for the Ruk Jana Nahi (RJN) Exam Scheme 2026. This scheme is designed for students who were unsuccessful in the main examination, and the last date to register is June 20, 2026. Eligible students can apply through the official portals at mpsos.mponline.gov.in and mpsos.nic.in.
2paragraphIf you appeared in the MP Board Class 10 or Class 12 exams in February–March 2026 and could not clear one or more subjects, this is your second chance and you must act fast. The application window closes on June 20, 2026, after which no registrations will be accepted for the June session.
2What is the Ruk Jana Nahi Yojana?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphBefore diving into registration details, it is important to understand what this scheme offers and why it matters for lakhs of students across Madhya Pradesh.
2paragraphRuk Jana Nahi Yojana is a special educational initiative launched by the Government of Madhya Pradesh and conducted through MPSOS. The objective is straightforward: students who fail in one or more subjects in Class 10 or Class 12 board examinations get another chance to appear for those subjects and improve their results without wasting an entire academic year. The scheme has helped lakhs of students continue their education and pursue higher studies despite initial setbacks.
3paragraphThe name itself "Ruk Jana Nahi" translates to "Don't Stop" in English, reflecting the government's commitment to ensuring no student is forced to drop out due to a single academic failure.

4Who is Eligible for Ruk Jana Nahi 2026?
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphUnderstanding eligibility is the first step before beginning your application. The scheme has specific criteria and is exclusively for students under the Madhya Pradesh Board.
2paragraphThe following students are eligible to apply for the Ruk Jana Nahi Exam Scheme 2026:
3listunordered
  • Students who failed in Class 10 or Class 12 exams conducted by the Madhya Pradesh Board in February–March 2026
  • Students who remained absent in the main examination
  • Candidates who also failed in the second chance exams held in April–May 2026
  • Only students under Madhyamik Shiksha Mandal, Madhya Pradesh, are eligible.
4paragraphStudents from other state boards or CBSE/ICSE are not eligible for this scheme. This initiative is exclusively for MP Board students.
5How to Apply for MPSOS Ruk Jana Nahi 2026 Online
Sr.TypeTextStyleItemsLinks
1paragraphThe application process is entirely online and can be completed through the official MPSOS portals or MP Online kiosks. Follow these steps carefully to complete your registration without errors.
2paragraphTo register for the Ruk Jana Nahi (RJN) Exam Scheme 2026, students can follow these steps:
3listordered
  • Visit the MP Online kiosk or the official application portals at mpsos.mponline.gov.in and mpsos.nic.in
  • Complete the Ruk Jana Nahi (RJN) Exam 2026 application form with the required details
  • Select whether you appeared previously under the scheme
  • Enter your date of birth exactly as mentioned on your marksheet
  • Fill in subject-wise marks as required
  • Upload required documents
  • Verify all details carefully before submitting
  • Pay the application fee and save the receipt

7Exam Pattern and Schedule
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphThis section outlines the examination structure so students know what to expect when they appear for the Ruk Jana Nahi 2026 exams in June.
2paragraphThe RJN exam will be conducted online via MP Online kiosk in the last week of June 2026. Class 12 exams begin on June 29, 2026, and Class 10 exams begin on June 30, 2026. The exam timing is 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM as per the schedule.
3paragraphStudents will appear only in the subjects they failed or were absent in they do not need to re-appear in subjects they already passed. Students appearing for the Ruk Jana Nahi exam are required to pay the applicable fee. However, they do not have to pay fees for vocational subjects, additional subjects, or minor subjects.
8How to Download Ruk Jana Nahi 2026 Admit Card
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThe admit card is a mandatory document for the examination and must be carried on the day of the exam. Students should download their hall ticket well before the exam date.
2paragraphTo download the Ruk Jana Nahi Exam Scheme 2026 admit card, follow these steps:
3listordered
  • Visit the official websites www.mpsos.mponline.gov.in or www.mpsos.nic.in
  • Click on the "Ruk Jana Nahi Exam 2026 Admit Card" link available on the homepage
  • Enter your registration number and date of birth in the login window
  • Download the admit card and take a printout for examination purposes
4paragraphStudents who do not carry their admit card on the day of the exam may not be permitted to appear.
9What Happens After the June 2026 Exam?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphStudents who successfully clear the June 2026 session will be able to move forward in their academic journey without losing any time. Those who are unable to clear it will still have options.
2paragraphStudents who clear the first phase in June 2026 will be eligible for admission to Class 11. Those qualifying in the second phase will have alternate pathways available. If students are unable to clear the exam in June 2026, they will get another opportunity through re-registration for the December 2026 session.
3paragraphThe board is expected to announce the Ruk Jana Nahi result for Class 10 and Class 12 in July/August 2026.
4paragraphThis two-session structure (June and December) ensures that no student is permanently left behind due to a single failed attempt.
