How to Check DAVV Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their DAVV M.A and M.Sc Result 2026 from the official website:

  1. Visit the official DAVV website at dauniv.ac.in
  2. Click on the Results section or navigate to the results page
  3. Select the appropriate result link for M.A or M.Sc from the results list
  4. Enter your login credentials including roll number, enrollment number, or registration number
  5. Enter your date of birth or other required details as prompted
  6. Click on the Submit or View Result button to access your scorecard
  7. Check all the details mentioned in your result carefully including marks and grades
  8. Download the result PDF and take a printout for future reference and admission purposes
