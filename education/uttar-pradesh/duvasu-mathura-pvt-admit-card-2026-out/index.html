DUVASU Mathura PVT Admit Card 2026 Out

Pandit Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Pashu Chikitsa Vigyan Vishwavidyalaya (DUVASU), Mathura has released the PVT (UGEE) Admit Card 2026 on its official portal. The admit card has been issued for candidates appearing in the Undergraduate Entrance Examination (UGEE‑2026) for BVSc & AH and other UG veterinary courses under the DUVASU PVT admission process.

As per the admission schedule, the DUVASU UGEE‑2026 exam will be conducted on 17 May 2026, followed later by DEE‑2026 on 7 June and PGEE‑2026 on 21 June 2026 for diploma and PG courses. Candidates who applied online between 15 February and 15 April 2026 can now log in and download their PVT admit card in online mode only.

How to Download DUVASU Mathura PVT Admit Card 2026
  1. Open the DUVASU admission/exam portal: go to duvasu.com or upvetuniv.edu.in, and click on the “Admit Card UGEE‑2026 / PVT Admit Card” link, or directly visit the login page duvasu.com/Login.
  2. On the login screen, enter your Registration Number/User ID and Password or Date of Birth as used during online application.
  3. Fill the captcha/security code correctly and click on “Login / Sign In”.
  4. After login, click on the link “Download Admit Card for UGEE‑2026 / PVT 2026” in your dashboard.
  5. Your DUVASU Mathura PVT Admit Card 2026 will open on the screen with your exam details and centre information.
  6. Check all particulars carefully, then download and print 2–3 clear A4‑size copies of the hall ticket for exam‑day use.

If you have forgotten your login credentials, use the Forgot Password/User ID options (if provided) or refer to your registration email/SMS, and contact the university helpdesk if the issue persists.

Details Mentioned on DUVASU PVT Admit Card 2026

The DUVASU UGEE‑2026 admit card typically includes the following information, which candidates must verify:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Father’s/guardian’s name
  • Registration number / roll number
  • Exam name: UGEE‑2026 / PVT 2026
  • Course applied for (BVSc & AH / B.Tech Biotech / B.F.Sc / B.Tech Dairy Tech, etc.)
  • Exam date and reporting time (UGEE: 17 May 2026)
  • Exam centre name, code and full address
  • Important instructions regarding exam‑day conduct and permitted items

If any error is found in your name, photo, course or exam centre, immediately contact the university examination or admission section using the contact details on upvetuniv.edu.in.

Documents Required with DUVASU Mathura PVT Admit Card 2026

On the day of the UGEE‑2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the DUVASU Mathura PVT Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout, preferably colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Voter ID Passport Driving Licence Any other government‑issued ID accepted by the university.
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Voter ID
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Any other government‑issued ID accepted by the university.
  • Additional documents, if mentioned in the admit card/information brochure (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter, if applicable).

Entry to the examination centre will not be allowed without the printed admit card and valid photo ID.
