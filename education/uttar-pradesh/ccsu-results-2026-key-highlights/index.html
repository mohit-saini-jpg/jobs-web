How to Check CCSU 1st Sem Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the steps given below to check and download CCSU 1st Sem Result 2026 for M.Lib and BA LLB courses from the official result portal.

  • Visit the official CCSU result website at https://result.ccsuniversityweb.in/ using any browser.
  • On the homepage, choose the relevant result section for UG/PG semester results or search for the M.Lib/BA LLB 1st Semester result link for Dec-25 exams.
  • Click on the respective CCSU 1st Semester Result 2026 link for your course (M.Lib or BA LLB).
  • Enter your roll number and other required details in the login window, then submit the form to proceed.
  • The CCSU 1st Sem Result 2026 will appear on the screen; check your personal details, subject-wise marks, and overall result status carefully.
  • Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future reference until the original marksheet is issued by your college/university department.
