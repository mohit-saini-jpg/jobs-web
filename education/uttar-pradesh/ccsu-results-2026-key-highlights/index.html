How to Check CCSU 1st Sem Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the steps given below to check and download CCSU 1st Sem Result 2026 for M.Lib and BA LLB courses from the official result portal.

  1. Visit the official CCSU result website at https://result.ccsuniversityweb.in/ using any browser.

  5. The CCSU 1st Sem Result 2026 will appear on the screen; check your personal details, subject-wise marks, and overall result status carefully.
  6. Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future reference until the original marksheet is issued by your college/university department.
