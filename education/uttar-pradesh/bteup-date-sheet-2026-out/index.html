How to Download BTEUP Date Sheet 2026

Students can easily download the BTEUP Date Sheet 2026 from the official website in just a few simple steps. The complete proposed examination scheme PDF is available for all branches and contains the latest updated schedule for Even Semester May 2026 exams.

  • Open any web browser on your mobile or computer and visit the official BTEUP website.
  • On the homepage, locate the Examination or Date Sheet section and click on it.
  • Find and select the link titled Even Semester May 2026 or Proposed Examination Scheme 2026.
  • The PDF file will open - click the download icon to save it directly to your device.
  • Once downloaded, open the file and use the search option to locate your branch code.
  • View your subject-wise dates and timings, then take a printout for quick reference during exam preparation.

Always cross-check the downloaded BTEUP Date Sheet 2026 carefully and keep a copy handy for your exam preparation.
