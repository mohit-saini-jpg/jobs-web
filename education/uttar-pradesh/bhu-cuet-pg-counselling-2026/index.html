BHU CUET PG Counselling 2026

BHU CUET PG Counselling 2026 registration has started on 19 May 2026. Candidates who appeared in CUET PG 2026 can now register for Banaras Hindu University (BHU) postgraduate admissions through the Combined Allotment Programme (CAP PG 2026) on the official portal.

Banaras Hindu University conducts centralized counselling for all Postgraduate programmes through CUET PG scores. The process is known as CAP PG (Combined Allotment Programme). It includes registration, preference filling, seat allotment in multiple rounds, document verification, and fee payment. There is no separate entrance test by BHU for most PG courses -admission is based on CUET PG 2026 scores.

BHU CUET PG Counselling Process 2026 (Step-by-Step)
  1. Registration & Preference Filling -Register on the official portal and fill course, campus, and seat type preferences.
  2. Seat Allotment -BHU releases merit lists based on CUET PG scores, category, and preferences.
  3. Document Verification -Upload and verify documents online.
  4. Fee Payment -Pay admission fees to confirm the seat.
  5. Final Reporting -Physical reporting to the allotted department in later rounds.

Official Portal: https://bhucuetpg.samarth.edu.in/

Documents Required for BHU CUET PG Counselling 2026
  • CUET PG 2026 Scorecard and Admit Card
  • Class 10th and 12th Marksheet & Certificate
  • Graduation Marksheet & Degree/Provisional Certificate
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwD) if applicable
  • Transfer/Migration Certificate
  • Character Certificate
  • BHU Employee Ward Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photographs
Eligibility Criteria
  • Must have appeared and qualified in CUET PG 2026 for the respective subject.
  • Must meet the minimum qualifying marks in graduation as per course-specific requirements.
  • Candidates must have registered on the BHU CAP PG portal.
