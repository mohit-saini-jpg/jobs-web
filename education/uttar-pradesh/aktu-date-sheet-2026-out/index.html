How to Download AKTU Date Sheet 2026

Students can easily download the AKTU Date Sheet 2026 from the official university portal. Following these simple steps will help you access the complete exam schedule quickly.

  1. Visit the official AKTU website at aktu.ac.in.
  2. Navigate to the Examination or Notice section on the homepage.
  3. Look for the link titled "Even Semester Examination Schedule 2026" or similar notification.
  4. Click on the relevant PDF link for your course and semester.
  5. The date sheet will open in a new tab - review all details carefully.
  6. Download and save the PDF file for future reference.
  7. Take a printout if needed for better preparation planning.
