The Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP) has officially released the UP Board Class 10th and 12th Result 2026 for the 2025-26 academic session. The UP Board 2026 results have been declared for High School (Class 10) and Intermediate (Class 12) students who appeared in the February-March 2026 board examinations.

UPMSP Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - upmsp.edu.in. Students of both classes can check and download their UP Board marksheet by visiting the official result portals and logging in with their roll number and other required details as given on their admit card. After downloading the provisional scorecard, candidates must carefully verify their name, roll number, subjects, marks and overall result status, and contact UPMSP or their respective schools immediately in case of any discrepancy.

How to Check UP Board Class 10th 12th Result 2026

Students can follow the steps given below to check UPMSP UP Board Result 2026 online in a simple and error-free manner.

  1. Open any web browser and visit the official UP Board website - upmsp.edu.in or the official result portal upresults.nic.in.
  2. On the homepage, look for the link that says "UP Board Class 10 High School Result 2026" or "UP Board Class 12 Intermediate Result 2026".
  3. Click on the relevant result link as per your class to open the login page.
  4. Enter your UP Board roll number and district/school code in the specified fields exactly as printed on the admit card.
  5. Verify the entered details and click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button.
  6. Your UP Board Class 10th or 12th Result 2026 marksheet will appear on the screen with subject-wise marks, total marks and result status.
  7. Download the online marksheet and take a printout for future admission, scholarship or documentation purposes until the original marksheet is issued by the school.
