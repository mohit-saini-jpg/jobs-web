Detailed Information

Lucknow University Application Form 2026 has been officially released for both Undergraduate (UG) and Postgraduate (PG) admissions for the academic session 2026-27. The University of Lucknow has opened the online registration process on its official Samarth admission portal.

Eligible candidates can now apply for various UG and PG programmes including BA, BSc, BCom, MA, MSc, MCom, MBA and many other courses. The application window is open and interested students are advised to apply before the last date to avoid late fees. How to Apply for Lucknow University Application Form 2026

  • Generate Lucknow University Registration Number (LURN) by paying Rs. 100.
  • Visit the official admission portal lkounivadm.samarth.edu.in.
  • Register and login using LURN.
  • Fill the application form with required details.
  • Upload documents and pay the application fee.
  • Submit the form and take a printout.

Eligibility Criteria for Lucknow University Admission 2026

Candidates must have passed the qualifying examination (Class 12th for UG and Graduation for PG) from a recognised board/university with minimum required marks as per course. Detailed eligibility is mentioned in the respective admission brochures.

Documents Required

  • Scanned Photograph and Signature
  • 10th and 12th Marksheet
  • Graduation Marksheet (for PG)
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Valid Email ID and Mobile Number

UG & PG Programmes at Lucknow University

Lucknow University offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in Arts, Science, Commerce, Law, Education, Management and other streams. The UG courses include BA, BSc, BCom, BBA, BCA, B.El.Ed etc. PG courses include MA, MSc, MCom, MBA, LLB, LLM and many more. All admissions are conducted through online application followed by entrance test or merit as per programme. Candidates are advised to check the detailed course list and seat matrix on the official admission portal before applying.
