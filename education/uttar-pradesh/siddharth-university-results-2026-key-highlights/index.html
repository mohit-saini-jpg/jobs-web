Siddharth University, Kapilvastu, Siddharth Nagar has released an important update regarding the Siddharth University Odd Semester Result 2026 for the examination session 2025-26, along with official notices about the cancellation of results for students found guilty of using unfair means. The university has declared odd semester results for various UG and PG courses such as BA, BSc, BCom, BBA, BCA, MA, MSc and other programmes and simultaneously notified strict action against candidates involved in malpractice during the 2025-26 odd semester exams.
Siddharth University Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - suksn.edu.in. Students can check and download their odd semester results by visiting the university ERP/result portal through the official site, selecting their course and semester, and entering roll number and enrolment number to view the online marksheet. At the same time, two key notifications dated 18-04-2026 clarify that for students proven guilty of using unfair means, the current session 2025-26 result has been cancelled and in some cases they are also barred from appearing in the 2026-27 examination session. After accessing their result and any related UFM notice, students must carefully verify their details, read the university orders in full, and contact Siddharth University authorities in case of any discrepancy or if they need clarification about the action taken.

Siddharth University Result 2026 Direct Link

Siddharth University has activated online access for the Odd Semester Result 2026 through its result/ERP system and has also placed unfair means notices under the Announcements section on the official site. Students of BA, BSc, BCom, BBA, BCA, MA, MSc and other courses from the 2025-26 odd semester exam session can use the direct links below to open the announcements page, read the UFM circulars and then navigate to the semester result portal to download their marksheet. Using these links, candidates can instantly view whether their result is available, check if any unfair means action applies to them and save the online result for future reference.

Steps To Check Siddharth University Odd Semester Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your Siddharth University Odd Semester Result 2026 online for the examination session 2025-26.
  1. Open a browser and visit the official Siddharth University website suksn.edu.in.
  2. On the homepage, go to the Academic Activity & Examination or Results section, or click on the Announcements link to see the latest result and circular updates.
  3. Look for the link related to Odd Semester Examination 2025-26 result or the Semester Exam Result portal and click on it to open the ERP result page.
  4. On the semester exam result page, select the session as 2025-26, choose the category (Regular/Back/Ex-Student etc.) and pick the relevant odd semester (1st, 3rd or 5th) and your course (BA, BSc, BCom, BBA, BCA, MA, MSc etc.).
  5. Enter your roll number and enrolment number in the respective fields and double-check the details.
  6. Click on the View Result button to display your Siddharth University Odd Semester Result 2026 marksheet on the screen.
  7. Download the online marksheet and take a printout for future use, and also visit the Announcements page to see if any unfair means-related notification affects your examination result.
