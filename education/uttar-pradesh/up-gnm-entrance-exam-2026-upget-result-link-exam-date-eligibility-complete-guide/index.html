Domicile

Preparation Tips for UPGET 2026

1. Understand the Exam Pattern

  • Focus on Science (50%) as it carries maximum marks
  • Practice Class 10 NCERT for Physics, Chemistry, Biology

2. Subject-wise Strategy

  • Biology: Prioritize human physiology, health, and disease
  • GK: Follow current affairs and Indian health schemes
  • English: Practice grammar and comprehension daily

3. Practice Previous Year Papers

  • Solve UPGET previous year question papers with solutions
  • Time yourself using 140-minute mock tests

4. Revision Plan

  • Make a GK monthly diary for current affairs

  • Days 51-60: Rapid revision & formula sheets
