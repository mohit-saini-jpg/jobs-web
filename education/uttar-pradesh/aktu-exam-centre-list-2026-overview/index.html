  • Use the search option (Ctrl + F on PC / search icon in mobile viewer).
  • Type your college code (or a unique part of your college name).

  • In the same row, check the Exam Centre Code, Exam Centre Name, and address - this is your allotted centre for the given exam phase.
  • Note down the centre name, full address and centre code for future reference.

  • Always rely on official communication rather than random social media screenshots.
10Important Instructions Related to AKTU Exam Centre 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThis section helps students avoid common mistakes and sets correct expectations.
2listunordered
  • The exam centre allotted in the latest AKTU exam centre list is generally treated as final for that exam phase unless the university issues a revised list.
  • AKTU usually does not allow individual students to request a change of exam centre; any corrections are handled through official university/college communication.
  • Students must carefully check the exam centre printed on their admit card and ensure it matches the latest official list.
  • In case of a mismatch, students should immediately contact their college exam cell and follow the instructions based on the most recent official notice.
  • On the exam day, candidates should reach the centre at least 30-45 minutes before the reporting time with: Printed AKTU admit card Valid photo ID (such as Aadhaar, college ID or other accepted ID) Allowed stationery and materials as per exam guidelines
