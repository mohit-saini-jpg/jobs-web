Detailed Information

UPTAC Counselling 2026 registration has started. The online registration for B.Tech admissions opened on 25 May 2026 and will close on 15 June 2026 on the official portal https://uptac.samarth.edu.in/. UPTAC stands for Uttar Pradesh Technical Admission Counselling. It is the centralized counselling process conducted by Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) for admissions to B.Tech and other professional courses in Uttar Pradesh.

How to Apply for UPTAC Counselling 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official website: https://uptac.samarth.edu.in/
  • Click on New Registration
  • Enter required details and generate APAAR ID if not available
  • Pay the registration fee
  • Upload documents
  • Submit application before 15 June 2026

UPTAC Counselling Process 2026

  • Online Registration & Document Upload
  • Document Verification
  • Choice Filling & Locking
  • Seat Allotment
  • Fee Payment & Willingness
  • Multiple Rounds

Documents Required for UPTAC Counselling 2026

  • Class 10th and 12th marksheets
  • JEE Main 2026 scorecard
  • APAAR ID
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile certificate
  • Photograph & Signature
  • Other certificates as per brochure

Seat Allotment & Choice Filling

Choice filling for Round 1 is scheduled from 22 June to 25 June 2026. Allotment will be published on 27 June 2026.
