1paragraphUttar Pradesh Rajarshi Tandon Open University (UPRTOU), Prayagraj has activated the Admit Card June‑2026 download link on its official website for various annual and semester‑based programmes. Hall tickets are available online for BA, BSc, BCom and other UG courses, PG programmes, B.Ed/B.Ed (SE), MA Yoga, MBA, MCA, and selected integrated/open courses as per the university notice.

Students must download their admit card and carry a printed copy along with a valid photo ID to the examination centre.

3How to Download UPRTOU Admit Card June 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphStudents can download the UPRTOU June 2026 hall ticket by following these steps:
2listordered
  • Open the official UPRTOU website: https://www.uprtou.ac.in.
  • On the homepage or Latest Notices section, click on “Admit Card June‑2026 (Click Here)”.
  • You will reach the Admit Card Front Page, where different links are shown, such as: Samarth‑admitted 1st & 2nd Semester Admit Card June‑2026 1st, 2nd & 3rd Year Admit Card June‑2026 (for B.A/B.Sc/B.Com etc.) Programme‑specific admit cards (B.Ed/B.Ed SE/MA‑Yoga/MBA/MCA etc.), if listed.
  • Samarth‑admitted 1st & 2nd Semester Admit Card June‑2026
  • 1st, 2nd & 3rd Year Admit Card June‑2026 (for B.A/B.Sc/B.Com etc.)
  • Programme‑specific admit cards (B.Ed/B.Ed SE/MA‑Yoga/MBA/MCA etc.), if listed.
  • Click on the relevant admit card link as per your course/year/semester. This will open the admit card search page.
  • On the search page, enter your Enrollment Number or registration/roll number in the given box.
  • Click on the “Search” / “Submit” button. Your UPRTOU Admit Card June 2026 will be shown on the screen.
  • Check all details carefully (name, programme, year/semester, exam centre, subjects) and then download and print the hall ticket on A4‑size paper.
3paragraphIf the admit card is not showing, students are advised to re‑check their enrollment number, try again later, or contact the UPRTOU exam helpline.
