UPRTOU Admit Card 2026 Out (June Session)

Uttar Pradesh Rajarshi Tandon Open University (UPRTOU), Prayagraj has activated the Admit Card June‑2026 download link on its official website for various annual and semester‑based programmes. Hall tickets are available online for BA, BSc, BCom and other UG courses, PG programmes, B.Ed/B.Ed (SE), MA Yoga, MBA, MCA, and selected integrated/open courses as per the university notice.

According to recent updates, the UPRTOU June 2026 term‑end exams are scheduled to begin from 14 May 2026 and continue till around 3 June 2026, and admit cards have been released about one week before commencement of exams. Students must download their admit card and carry a printed copy along with a valid photo ID to the examination centre.

How to Download UPRTOU Admit Card June 2026

Students can download the UPRTOU June 2026 hall ticket by following these steps:

  1. Open the official UPRTOU website: https://www.uprtou.ac.in.
  2. On the homepage or Latest Notices section, click on “Admit Card June‑2026 (Click Here)”.
  3. You will reach the Admit Card Front Page, where different links are shown, such as: Samarth‑admitted 1st & 2nd Semester Admit Card June‑2026 1st, 2nd & 3rd Year Admit Card June‑2026 (for B.A/B.Sc/B.Com etc.) Programme‑specific admit cards (B.Ed/B.Ed SE/MA‑Yoga/MBA/MCA etc.), if listed.
  4. Samarth‑admitted 1st & 2nd Semester Admit Card June‑2026
  5. 1st, 2nd & 3rd Year Admit Card June‑2026 (for B.A/B.Sc/B.Com etc.)
  6. Programme‑specific admit cards (B.Ed/B.Ed SE/MA‑Yoga/MBA/MCA etc.), if listed.
  7. Click on the relevant admit card link as per your course/year/semester. This will open the admit card search page.
  8. On the search page, enter your Enrollment Number or registration/roll number in the given box.
  9. Click on the “Search” / “Submit” button. Your UPRTOU Admit Card June 2026 will be shown on the screen.
  10. Check all details carefully (name, programme, year/semester, exam centre, subjects) and then download and print the hall ticket on A4‑size paper.

If the admit card is not showing, students are advised to re‑check their enrollment number, try again later, or contact the UPRTOU exam helpline.

Details Mentioned on UPRTOU Admit Card 2026

The UPRTOU June‑2026 admit card contains key exam and candidate details, such as:

  • Student’s name and photograph (if printed)
  • Enrollment number / roll number
  • Programme and year/semester (e.g., BA 1st/2nd/3rd year, BSc, BCom, B.Ed, MA, MBA, MCA etc.)
  • Exam session: June 2026
  • Exam centre name, centre code and full address
  • Subject/paper codes and titles (or reference to time‑table)
  • Exam dates and timings as per the schedule
  • Important exam‑day instructions and rules of the university

Any mismatch in the candidate’s name, enrollment number, programme or exam centre should be reported to the regional centre/study centre or examination section before the exam.

Documents Required with UPRTOU Admit Card June 2026

On each exam day, students must carry:

  • A printed copy of the UPRTOU Admit Card June 2026 (clear A4‑size printout, preferably colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One valid photo ID proof, such as: UPRTOU/college ID card (if issued) Aadhaar Card Voter ID Driving Licence Other government‑issued ID accepted by the exam centre.
  • UPRTOU/college ID card (if issued)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID
  • Driving Licence
  • Other government‑issued ID accepted by the exam centre.
  • Additional documents, if specifically mentioned in the admit card/university notice (for example, PwD/medical certificate for students with special needs).

Without the printed admit card and a valid identity proof, entry to the examination hall may be denied.
