The Board of Technical Education, Uttar Pradesh (BTEUP) has released the BTEUP Admit Card 2026 for Annual and Even Semester (2nd, 4th, 6th) Polytechnic diploma examinations. As per recent updates, the BTEUP exam scheme 2026 for May‑June exams has been issued and the hall ticket download facility has been activated on the official portal for all eligible diploma and PG diploma students.

Students enrolled in government, aided and private polytechnic institutions under BTEUP must download their admit card online and carry a printed copy to the examination centre on each exam day; entry will not be permitted without a valid hall ticket.

How to Download BTEUP Admit Card 2026

Students can download their BTEUP Annual/Even Semester hall ticket 2026 by following these steps:

  1. Visit the official BTEUP website: https://bteup.ac.in.
  2. Click on the “Student Login / E‑Seva” link or directly open https://bteup.ac.in/Eseva/Outerpages/StudentLogin.aspx.
  3. On the student login page, enter your Enrolment Number and Date of Birth in the specified format, then click on “Login”.
  4. After successful login, go to the Admit Card or Download Admit Card section in your dashboard.
  5. Select your exam type/semester (Annual / Even Semester / Back Paper) and click on the relevant admit card link.
  6. Your BTEUP Admit Card 2026 will be displayed on the screen; verify your details, then download and print 2-3 clear copies on A4‑size paper.

If students are unable to log in or see their admit card, they should contact their institute or BTEUP support before the exam dates.

Details Mentioned on BTEUP Admit Card 2026

The BTEUP Polytechnic hall ticket 2026 contains important candidate and exam details, which must be checked carefully:

  • Student’s name and photograph
  • Enrolment number and roll number
  • Course name (Diploma/PG Diploma) and branch
  • Semester / exam type (Annual, 2nd/4th/6th Sem, Back/Special Back)
  • Subject codes and paper‑wise exam dates
  • Exam centre name and full address
  • Reporting time and exam timing
  • Important exam‑day instructions for candidates

In case of any discrepancy in name, course, semester or exam centre, students should immediately inform their institute or the BTEUP examination section.

Documents Required with BTEUP Admit Card 2026

On each exam day, candidates must carry the following documents to the exam centre:

  • A printed copy of BTEUP Admit Card 2026 (clean, readable A4 printout).
  • One valid photo identity proof, such as: Aadhaar Card Voter ID PAN Card Driving Licence College/Institute ID card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID
  • PAN Card
  • Driving Licence
  • College/Institute ID card

No student will be allowed to enter the examination hall without a valid BTEUP admit card and acceptable ID proof.

Exam‑Day Instructions for BTEUP Diploma Exams 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the BTEUP 2026 exams, students must comply with these instructions:

  • Reach the exam centre at least 30-45 minutes before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card.
  • Carry only essential items - admit card, ID proof and permitted stationery (blue/black pen, pencil, etc.).
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless allowed), Bluetooth devices, notes or any electronic gadgets inside the exam hall.
  • Follow all instructions given by invigilators and maintain discipline throughout the exam.
  • Keep your BTEUP admit card safe even after the exam for result verification and future reference.
