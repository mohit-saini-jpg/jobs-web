ERP Admit Card Portal

How to Download AKTU Admit Card 2026

Follow these steps to download your AKTU Even Semester admit card from the ERP portal:

  • Open the AKTU ERP admit card page: https://erp.aktu.ac.in/WebPages/public/examination/printadmitcard.aspx.
  • Select your Institute/College and Course/Semester if the page asks for these details.
  • Enter your Enrollment Number / Roll Number and Date of Birth in the specified format.
  • Complete any captcha/verification, then click on “Search” / “Show Admit Card”.
  • Your AKTU Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen with your exam schedule and centre details.
  • Verify all details carefully, then download and print 2-3 copies of the hall ticket on A4‑size paper.

If the admit card is not visible or an error appears, students should recheck their details and contact their college or AKTU exam section if the problem persists.

Details Mentioned on AKTU Admit Card 2026

The AKTU Even Semester admit card includes important exam and candidate information:

  • Student’s name and photograph
  • Enrollment number, roll number and institute name
  • Course, branch and semester (e.g., B.Tech 4th Sem)
  • List of subjects/paper codes with exam dates
  • Exam time/shift (generally 09:30 AM or 02:00 PM)
  • Exam centre name, code and full address
  • Important exam‑day instructions and rules

Any mismatch in name, course, semester or exam centre should be reported immediately to the college administration or university authority for correction.

Documents Required with AKTU Admit Card 2026

On each exam day, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of AKTU Admit Card 2026 (clear A4 printout, preferably in good quality).
  • One valid photo ID proof, such as: College/University ID card Aadhaar Card Voter ID Driving Licence PAN Card
  • College/University ID card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID
  • Driving Licence
  • PAN Card

Students who do not carry the admit card and valid ID proof may be denied entry to the examination hall.

Exam‑Day Instructions for AKTU Exams 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the AKTU Even Semester 2026 exams, students should follow these exam‑day guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre at least 30-45 minutes before the reporting time printed on your AKTU Admit Card 2026 to complete verification and seating formalities.
  • Carry only essential items: a printed AKTU admit card 2026, valid photo ID (college ID/Aadhaar/Voter ID/Driving Licence/PAN), and permitted stationery like blue/black pens, pencils and simple instruments as allowed for your paper.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets inside the exam hall; use of unfair means or prohibited items can lead to cancellation of your paper and disciplinary action.
  • Follow all instructions given by invigilators and strictly abide by the rules printed on the admit card and answer booklet; sit only on your allotted seat and fill in roll number and other details carefully where asked.
  • Keep your AKTU admit card safe even after the exams are over, as it may be required later for internal verification, result checking, revaluation or future semester formalities.
