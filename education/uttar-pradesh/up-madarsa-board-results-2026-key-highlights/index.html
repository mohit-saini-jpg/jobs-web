How to Check UP Madarsa Board Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check UP Madarsa Board Result 2026 online on the official website:

  1. Visit the official website of Uttar Pradesh Madarsa Board at madarsaboard.upsdc.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the 'Exam Result' or relevant 'Result 2026' link.
  3. You will be redirected to the UP Madarsa Board online result page.
  4. Select the exam year (2026) and choose your course/class, such as Munshi/Maulvi or Alim.
  5. Enter your roll number and the security code shown on the screen.
  6. Click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button to proceed.
  7. Your UP Madarsa Board Result 2026 will appear on the screen.
  8. Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future reference.
