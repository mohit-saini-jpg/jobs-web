How to Check JNVST 9th Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their JNVST Class 9 Result 2026 from the official website:

  1. Visit the official website of Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti at navodaya.gov.in
  2. Navigate to the 'Results' or 'Admissions' section on the homepage
  3. Look for the link 'JNVST Class 9 Result 2026' or 'LEST 2026 Result'
  4. Click on the result link to open the login page
  5. Alternatively, you can directly visit the result portal at cbseit.in/cbse/2026/nvsrst/NVS_Cls9.aspx
  6. Enter your Roll Number in the first field
  7. Enter your Date of Birth in DD/MM/YYYY format in the second field
  8. Click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button
  9. Your JNVST Class 9 Result 2026 will appear on the screen showing marks obtained and selection status
  10. Check all the details carefully including name, roll number, marks, and whether you are selected
  11. Download and save the scorecard PDF for future reference
  12. Take a printout of the result for document verification and admission process
  13. If selected, check the merit list to find your rank and prepare for document verification
