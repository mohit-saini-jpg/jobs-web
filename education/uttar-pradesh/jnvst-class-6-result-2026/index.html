JNVST Class 6 Result 2026

Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) has officially released the JNVST Class 6 Phase 2 Result 2026 for students who appeared in the entrance examination. The results are now available online for admission into Class 6 across Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas for the academic session 2026-27.

JNVST Class 6 Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - https://navodaya.gov.in. Students who appeared for the Phase 2 exam can check and download their results using their roll number and date of birth through the official result portal.

Candidates are advised to carefully verify all details mentioned in the result and contact the respective authorities in case of any discrepancies.

How to Download JNVST Class 6 Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your Navodaya Class 6 result online:

  1. Visit the official website https://navodaya.gov.in
  2. Click on the "JNVST Class 6 Result 2026" link
  3. You will be redirected to the result portal
  4. Enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth
  5. Click on the Submit button
  6. Your result will appear on the screen
  7. Download and take a printout for future reference
