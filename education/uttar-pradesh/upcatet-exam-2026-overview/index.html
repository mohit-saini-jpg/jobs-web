Details

The Uttar Pradesh Combined Agriculture and Technology Entrance Test (UPCATET) 2026 will be conducted in offline (pen-and-paper) mode on 10 and 11 June 2026 across multiple exam centres in Uttar Pradesh. The registration window is open till 30 April 2026, with fee payment allowed up to 1 May 2026 (5:00 PM) as per the latest schedule. Admit cards for UPCATET 2026 will be available for download from 1 to 9 June 2026 on the official website upcatetexam.org.

UPCATET 2026 Eligibility Criteria (UG, PG, PhD)

  • UG courses (BSc Agriculture and allied): Candidates should have passed 10+2 with Agriculture/PCB/PCM or relevant combinations as specified in the brochure, with a minimum qualifying percentage as per category.
  • PG courses: A relevant bachelor’s degree in the concerned discipline from a recognised university is required; percentage and subject combinations vary by programme.
  • PhD courses: A master’s degree in the concerned subject from a recognised institution, with minimum marks as per university norms, is required.
  • Age limit: For most UG programmes, the minimum age is 16 years, with upper age and category-wise relaxations mentioned in the official information brochure; PG and PhD generally have no upper age limit.
How to Apply Online for UPCATET 2026

This section serves informational + navigational intent by guiding users to the official portal and explaining the workflow. UPCATET 2026 application form is available in online mode only on the official portal upcatetexam.org during the notified dates. Candidates must complete registration, fill the form, upload documents and pay the exam fee before the last date.

Step‑by‑Step Process to Fill UPCATET 2026 Form

  1. Visit the official website: Go to the official UPCATET website (upcatetexam.org) and click on “New Registration” for UPCATET 2026.
  2. Complete new registration: Enter your name, date of birth, mobile number, email ID and other basic details, then verify via OTP to generate your Registration Number and password.
  3. Login and fill application form: Login with your Registration Number and password, then fill in personal details, address, educational qualifications, category, domicile and course group details carefully.
  4. Upload required documents: Upload scanned images of your recent passport-size photograph, signature and relevant certificates in the prescribed size and format mentioned in the instructions.
  5. Pay the application fee: Pay the UPCATET 2026 application fee online through debit card, credit card, net banking or UPI and ensure the transaction is successfully completed before 1 May 2026 (5:00 PM).
  6. Final submission and printout: Submit the application form, then download and print the confirmation page and filled form for future reference, counselling and document verification.
