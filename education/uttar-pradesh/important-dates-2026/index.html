Details

Allahabad University (AU) PGAT I and PGAT II Admissions 2026 application process has started. Online registration for Postgraduate Admission Test (PGAT) 2026-27 began on 5 May 2026 and will continue until 25 May 2026.

University of Allahabad (AU) conducts PGAT for admission to various postgraduate courses for the academic session 2026-27. It is divided into PGAT-I (conventional PG courses) and PGAT-II (non-conventional PG courses). PGAT-I is available in both online and offline modes, while PGAT-II is online only.

Eligibility & Key Highlights

Candidates must check detailed eligibility in the PGAT-2026 Information Bulletin available on the university website. Minor changes have been made in eligibility and admission criteria this year.

Key Points:

  • 15% SC, 7.5% ST, 27% OBC, 10% EWS reservations as per GoI norms.
  • 5% PwD and 2% Sports quota (fitness test compulsory for Sports quota).
  • NCC Weightage & Sports Quota not applicable for B.Ed., M.Ed., MBA & MBA(RD).
  • M.Sc. Agriculture Botany renamed to M.Sc. Agricultural Microbiology.
  • Foreign Nationals apply separately (no PGAT required).
