How to Check CCS University Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your CCS University semester result:

  • Visit the official result portal - https://result.ccsuniversityweb.in/
  • Click on the relevant UG course result link (BBA, BJMC, BAJMC).
  • Enter your Roll Number or Registration Number.
  • Submit the details to view your result.
  • Download and print the result for future reference.
