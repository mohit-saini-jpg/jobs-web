Kashmir University Exam Result 2026

Kashmir University has officially released the Exam Result 2026 for various UG and PG courses including LLB Honours 3rd Semester, B.Tech B.E 8th Semester (Regular, Backlog, Supplementary), and BG 3rd and 5th Semester examinations. Students who appeared for these exams can now check their results online.

Kashmir University Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - uok.edu.in. The results are declared for multiple professional and non professional courses conducted across different sessions.

Students can check and download their results by entering their registration number or login credentials through the official portal. It is advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the result. In case of any discrepancies, students should contact the university authorities immediately.

How to Check Kashmir University Result 2026

Follow these steps to check your result:

  1. Visit the official website - uok.edu.in
  2. Go to the "Results" section
  3. Click on the relevant course and semester result link
  4. Enter your registration number or required details
  5. Click on submit
  6. Your result will appear on the screen
  7. Download and print the result for future reference
