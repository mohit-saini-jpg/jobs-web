What Has JKBOSE Changed?

Jammu And Kashmir Education 2026 JKBOSE Revised Syllabus for Class 11 & 12 Post Date: 07/03/2026
JKBOSE has revised the Class 11 & 12 syllabus 2026-27 for Punjabi, Physical Education (Class 11), Education & EVS (Class 12). Classes 9 & 10 unchanged. Zone-wise exam dates (Oct-Nov 2026 / Feb-Mar 2027) & private school compliance rules.
Detailed Information

The JKBOSE Class 11 12 syllabus 2026-27 revised announcement is out, and it's targeted at specific subjects only. The Jammu and Kashmir Board of School Education (JKBOSE) has updated the syllabus for just four subjects in Classes 11 and 12, while everything else stays the same, including no changes for Classes 9 and 10 in the relevant sessions. This comes from the board's recent notification, aimed at clarifying what students and schools need to follow for the upcoming exams.

What Has JKBOSE Changed?

JKBOSE has revised the syllabus for four specific subjects in higher secondary classes, effective for the notified academic sessions across zones. The updates apply to:

  • Class 11: Punjabi and Physical Education
  • Class 12: Education and Environmental Science (EVS)

All other subjects in Classes 11 and 12 stick to the existing syllabus, no broad overhaul here. For Classes 9 and 10, the board has confirmed no revisions at all for the Academic Session 2025–26 in Kashmir Division and winter zone areas of Jammu Division, and the same unchanged setup continues for 2026–27 in summer zone areas of Jammu Division.

The notification also spells out exam timelines: annual session exams for the Kashmir Division and winter zone Jammu are set for October–November 2026, while summer zone Jammu gets February–March 2027. Question papers for the revised subjects (regular and fresh private candidates) will follow the new syllabus only.

Separately, JKBOSE has reminded private schools (especially in Jammu Division) to stick strictly to the board's prescribed curriculum and textbooks from Classes 1 to 12, no deviations allowed.

JKBOSE Class 11 Syllabus 2026–27: Subjects Revised

For the JKBOSE Class 11 syllabus, the changes are limited and straightforward.

  • Class 11 Punjabi – Revised syllabus introduced
  • Class 11 Physical Education – Revised syllabus introduced

All other Class 11 subjects (whether Arts, Science, Commerce, or others) continue as per the existing pattern; no updates announced.

JKBOSE Class 12 Syllabus 2026–27: Subjects Revised

Similarly, for the JKBOSE Class 12 syllabus, only two subjects see changes.

  • Class 12 Education – Revised syllabus notified
  • Class 12 Environmental Science (EVS) – Revised syllabus notified

No changes for other Class 12 subjects. Students in other streams or subjects will follow last year’s syllabus pattern, as nothing new has been announced for them.

Classes 9 and 10 Syllabus: No Change for 2025–26 / 2026–27

Good news for younger students: JKBOSE Class 9 syllabus 2025–26 and JKBOSE Class 10 syllabus unchanged across the board.

  • Kashmir Division and winter zone areas of Jammu Division: Classes 9 and 10 syllabi remain the same for the Academic Session 2025–26.
  • Summer zone areas of Jammu Division: The same unchanged syllabus applies for 2026–27.

No academic alterations for these classes in the mentioned sessions.

Question Papers and Assessment: How Exams Will Be Set

For the four revised subjects only, question papers in the annual exams (both regular and fresh private candidates) will be based strictly on the updated JKBOSE revised syllabus 2026–27.

Exam cycles reminder:

  • Kashmir Division and winter zone Jammu: October–November 2026
  • Summer zone Jammu: February–March 2027

Students, make sure to download and study only the latest syllabus PDF for these subjects; don't waste time on deleted or outdated topics.

Board Orders Mandatory Implementation of JKBOSE Curriculum

JKBOSE has put out a clear directive (marked "most urgent") to all affiliated private schools in Jammu Division: you must implement the JKBOSE curriculum and prescribed textbooks for Classes 1 to 12, no exceptions.

This is backed by the Jammu and Kashmir Board of School Education Act, 1975, and J&K School Education Rules, 2010, which make adherence compulsory. It repeats earlier instructions from the Director of Academics.

Inspections are coming: JKBOSE teams will visit schools to check on-ground compliance with approved textbooks and syllabi. Non-compliance (like pushing non-JKBOSE books or materials) could lead to strict penalties, disciplinary action, or other sanctions under board regulations.

Where to Download JKBOSE Class 11, 12 Revised Syllabus

The revised syllabus PDFs for Class 11 Punjabi, Physical Education, and Class 12 Education, Environmental Science are available on the official JKBOSE website (jkbose.jk.gov.in or jkbose.nic.in).

Steps to grab them:

  • Visit the official JKBOSE website.
  • Head to the “Syllabus” or “Curriculum” section.
  • Select Class 11 or 12, then the specific subject.
  • Download the latest PDF for the relevant academic session.
