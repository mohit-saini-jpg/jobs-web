Detailed Information

The University of Kashmir has announced the Kashmir University Result 2026 for the BUMS Final Year examination held in November 2025, as notified under Notification No.13. The result has been made available online for all concerned BUMS Final Year students for the 2025 examination session. Kashmir University Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - uok.edu.in - through the dedicated result portal at egov.uok.edu.in/results.

Students can check and download their BUMS Final Year result by visiting the online result portal, selecting the relevant examination link and entering their roll number or university registration details to view their marksheet. After downloading the online result, candidates are advised to carefully verify their name, roll number, course, year, subject wise marks and overall result status and immediately contact the university examination branch in case of any discrepancy.

How to Download Kashmir University Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your Kashmir University BUMS Final Year Result 2026:

  • Visit the official result portal: https://egov.uok.edu.in/results/.
  • On the results page, look for the link or notification titled similar to “Notification No.13 - BUMS Final Year November 2025”.
  • Click on the relevant BUMS Final Year result link to open the result page or login screen.
  • Enter your Roll Number / Registration Number / University Number and any other details (such as captcha) required on the page.
  • Click on the Submit / View Result button to display your BUMS Final Year marksheet.
  • Check your name, roll number, course, year, subject wise marks, total marks and pass/fail status and then download or print the result for future reference and official procedures.
