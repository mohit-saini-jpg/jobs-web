How to Download Jammu University Result 2026

Students can follow the steps below to check Jammu University Result 2026 online through the official COE Jammu website. The process is simple and requires the relevant course details and student credentials to access the marksheet or notification.

  1. Visit the official Controller of Examinations website at www.coeju.com.
  2. Open the Results section available on the homepage.
  3. Select the relevant course, semester, and result type from the available options.
  4. Enter your roll number, registration number, or other required login details.
  5. Click on the submit button to view your result.
  6. Check all details mentioned on the result or marksheet carefully.
  7. Download and print the provisional result for future reference.
