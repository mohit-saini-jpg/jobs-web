The Jammu and Kashmir Board of School Education (JKBOSE) has officially released the JKBOSE Class 11th Result 2026 for the Summer Zone annual regular examinations. Students who appeared for the Higher Secondary Part I (Class 11) exams across various streams such as Arts, Science, and Commerce can now check their scores online through the official result portal. JKBOSE Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - jkresults.nic.in.

The board has published the results in digital format, allowing students to access their subject-wise marks by entering their roll number and registration number on the result page. Candidates can download their provisional marksheet from jkresults.nic.in and keep a printed copy for future academic use, such as admission to Class 12 and other official purposes.

After downloading the result, students are advised to carefully verify personal details, subject-wise marks, and overall status, and contact JKBOSE or their respective school authorities in case of any discrepancy.

How to Check JKBOSE Class 11th Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the step-by-step process given below to check and download the JKBOSE Class 11th Result 2026 Summer Zone from the official website:

  1. Visit the official JKBOSE result portal at jkresults.nic.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the link that reads "View Result of Higher Secondary School Examination (Class 11) Session Annual Regular Summer Zone 2026".
  3. A new result page will open on the screen.
  4. Enter your roll number and registration number in the respective fields as printed on the admit card.
  5. Click on the Submit button to proceed.
  6. Your JKBOSE Class 11th result marksheet 2026 will be displayed on the screen with subject-wise marks and overall status.
  7. Download the marksheet PDF and take a printout for future use (admission, scholarship, documentation).

Students who face any technical issue while checking the result online can try again after some time or contact their respective schools for assistance.
