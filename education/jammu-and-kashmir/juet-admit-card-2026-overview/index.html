Detailed Information

The University of Jammu has released the JUET Admit Card 2026 for candidates registered for the Jammu University Entrance Test (JUET‑2026) for admission to various postgraduate and other programmes. Registered applicants can now download their JUET 2026 admit card from the official JUET registration/admission portals from 21 April 2026 onwards. JUET Admit Card 2026 Download Link

The University has enabled the JUET‑2026 admit card download facility for all registered applicants on its official online portals. Candidates must log in to juet.formapply.in or the admission page of jammuuniversity.ac.in using their registered credentials to access and download their JUET admit card 2026. After download, candidates should take a clear printout and carefully verify their personal details, exam date, reporting time and exam centre address.

How to Download JUET Admit Card 2026

Candidates can follow the steps given below to download the JUET 2026 hall ticket:

  • Visit the JUET‑2026 registration portal: juet.formapply.in or go to the Admissions/JUET section at jammuuniversity.ac.in.
  • Click on the “Download JUET‑2026 Admit Card” or similar hall ticket link.
  • Log in using your registered application number / username and password as created during JUET registration.
  • After successful login, your JUET admit card 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download the admit card PDF and take multiple printouts for exam day and future reference.

Any discrepancy in candidate details, photograph, signature or exam centre information should be immediately reported to the University of Jammu/JUET helpdesk for correction before the exam date.

Details Mentioned on JUET Admit Card 2026

The JUET admit card is an essential document, carrying key candidate and exam-related information that must be produced at the examination centre. Typically, the JUET 2026 hall ticket will include:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Roll number / application number
  • Exam name: JUET‑2026
  • Exam date and time (25 April 2026, 10:30 AM to 01:00 PM)
  • Exam centre name and full address
  • Category and course applied (as per form)
  • Important instructions and exam‑day guidelines

Candidates must ensure that all these details are correct and clearly visible on the printed admit card.

Documents Required with JUET Admit Card 2026

On the day of the JUET‑2026 exam, candidates must carry the following documents to the exam centre along with their printed admit card:

  • A hard copy (printout) of the JUET Admit Card 2026
  • A valid government‑issued photo ID such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence, or Passport
  • Recent passport‑size photographs as per university instructions (if required for verification)

Failure to produce the admit card and valid ID proof may lead to denial of entry into the examination hall.

JUET 2026 Exam Date and Timing

As per the official schedule, the Jammu University Entrance Test (JUET‑2026) will be held on 25 April 2026. The exam timing has been fixed from 10:30 AM to 01:00 PM. Candidates are advised to reach the examination centre well before the reporting time to complete entry and verification formalities without last‑minute rush.

JUET 2026 Exam Day Instructions

Candidates appearing for JUET‑2026 must follow the exam‑day guidelines mentioned on the admit card and official notifications:

  • Reach the exam centre at least 60–90 minutes before the scheduled exam time to avoid delays.
  • Carry the JUET admit card 2026 printout, valid photo ID and required photographs.
  • Do not carry prohibited items such as mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, Bluetooth devices or any electronic gadgets inside the exam hall.
  • Follow the invigilators’ instructions regarding seating, filling OMR/answer sheet, and marking responses.
  • Maintain discipline; any unfair means or malpractice may lead to cancellation of candidature and further action as per university rules.
