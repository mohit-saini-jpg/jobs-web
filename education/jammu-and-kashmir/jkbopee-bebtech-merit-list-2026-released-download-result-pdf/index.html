How to download JKBOPEE B.E./B.Tech Merit List 2026 PDF

Many candidates just want a simple step‑by‑step guide. You can add both official and alternate (direct link) methods.

Steps to download the merit list:

  • Visit the official JKBOPEE website: jkbopee.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the “Results” or “Notifications” section.
  • Look for the link titled “Provisional Merit List/Result of CET for B.E./B.Tech Courses 2026” or similar.
  • Click on the link; the merit list PDF (Annexure‑A) will open in a new tab.
  • Use Ctrl+F and type your roll number or name to search quickly in the list.
  • Check your marks, rank, category and other details carefully.
  • Download the PDF and save a copy on your phone or laptop for future counselling and admission rounds.

You can also provide the direct merit list PDF link (like the Freejobalert PDF) as an additional option, but always mention that students should cross‑check on the official JKBOPEE site for latest updates.

Important Instructions for Candidates

To avoid last‑minute confusion, keep these points in mind:

  • The current JKBOPEE B.E./B.Tech Merit List 2026 is provisional; it can change after re‑totaling or corrections.
  • Candidates with pending documents (like incomplete marks cards, expired category certificates, or pending domicile) have been allowed provisionally and must submit valid documents at the time of admission if selected.
  • Missing the 3‑day objection window or 5‑day correction window means you may have to accept whatever is printed in the merit list.
  • Always keep both digital and printed copies of the merit list and future counselling letters for safety.
