About
JKBOPEE BSc Nursing CET Exam 2026 application is open from 26 March to 7 April 2026. The exam will be held on 26 April 2026 in offline mode. Check important dates, eligibility, exam pattern, and direct apply link for B.Sc Nursing admission in J&K and Ladakh.
Details

JKBOPEE BSc Nursing CET Exam 2026 is the Common Entrance Test conducted by the Jammu and Kashmir Board of Professional Entrance Examinations (JKBOPEE) for admission to B.Sc. Nursing, B.Sc. Paramedical and B.Sc. Technology courses in government and private colleges/institutes of the Union Territory of J&K and Ladakh. The online application window opened on 26 March 2026 and will remain open until 7 April 2026 (midnight).

The exam is scheduled for 26 April 2026 in offline (OMR-based) mode. This guide provides complete details on important dates, eligibility, application process, exam pattern, syllabus, and counselling to help aspiring nursing students from J&K and Ladakh apply successfully and prepare effectively.

JKBOPEE BSc Nursing CET 2026 Exam Pattern

The exam will be conducted in offline (OMR sheet) mode.

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Total Questions: 180 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology (based on 10+2 level)
  • Marking Scheme: +1 mark for each correct answer
  • Negative Marking: -0.25 marks for each incorrect answer

Candidates must use a black ballpoint pen to mark answers on the OMR sheet.

How to Apply for JKBOPEE BSc Nursing CET 2026?
  • Visit the official JKBOPEE website.
  • Download and carefully read the e-Information Brochure.
  • Click on the Common Application Form (CAF) link.
  • Register with basic details and fill the online application form.
  • Upload scanned documents (photo, signature, and certificates).
  • Pay the application fee of ₹1,000 online (Credit Card/Debit Card/Net Banking).
  • Submit the form and take a printout of the confirmation page for future reference.
Admit Card, Result & Counselling Process
  • Admit Card: Will be released on the official website before the exam. Download using application number and date of birth.
  • Result: Merit list will be prepared based on marks scored in the entrance test.
  • Counselling: Qualified candidates will participate in online/offline counselling rounds for seat allotment as per merit, category, and preferences.
