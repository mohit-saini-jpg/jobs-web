Detailed Information

The University of Jammu has officially released the Jammu University Result 2026 for the Re-Evaluation Result of the B.P.Ed 3rd Semester Examination held in December 2025, along with the UG 6th Semester Examination results for sessions 2019, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, and 2025 (Notification No. 125, 100, 139, 36, 13, 19, 45, 72, 36, 34, 31, 33, 57, 189, 188). The results have been declared for both undergraduate and physical education courses under the university's examination system.

Jammu University Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - coeju.com. Students can check and download their results by visiting the official COE Jammu portal and using their login credentials such as roll number and date of birth. The result PDFs and mark sheets are available for download directly from the results/notices section.

Students are advised to carefully verify their personal details, marks, and subject information on the result. In case of any discrepancy or error, candidates should immediately contact the Controller of Examinations, University of Jammu, through the official portal at coeju.com.

How to Check Jammu University Result 2026

Follow the step-by-step instructions below to check and download your Jammu University Result 2026 from the official COE portal:

  • Visit the official website of the Controller of Examinations, University of Jammu - coeju.com.
  • On the homepage, click on the "Results" or "Result Notifications" section.
  • Look for the relevant result notification - Re-Eval Result of B.P.Ed 3rd Semester (Dec-2025) or UG 6th Sem Notification No. 125, 100, 139... dated 07-04-2026.
  • Click on the result link to open the PDF notification or the result page.
  • To download your individual mark sheet, navigate to the UG Semester Marks Sheet section on coeju.com and enter your Roll Number.
  • View your result, check all details carefully, and download or save the result for future reference.
