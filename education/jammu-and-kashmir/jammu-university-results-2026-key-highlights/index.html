How to Check Jammu University Result 2026

Students can check Jammu University Result 2026 through the COEJU official portal using either the result notification links or the online marksheet module for UG and other programmes. The process is simple and requires basic exam details such as roll or registration number, course, and semester.

Follow these steps to check Jammu University Result 2026:

  1. Visit the official examination website of Jammu University at <a href="https://www.coeju.com/index.aspx" target="_self">coeju.com</a>.
  2. On the homepage, go to the "Result Notifications" or "Notices" section to view the latest result notifications for various courses.
  3. Locate the relevant notification such as B.Sc Paramedical 3rd Year Re-exam, BA LL.B (5 Years) 8th Sem Re-eval, or Business Administration (HM) 1st Sem and click on the link to open the detailed result PDF or notification.
  4. If detailed marks are available through the marksheet module, navigate to the "Results" or "UG Results" section and click on the UG Semester Marksheet link (ugsemmarksheet.aspx).
  5. Select your course scheme (CBCS/NON-CBCS/NEP), semester, and result type (Main/Reval) as applicable, and enter your roll or registration number in the required field.
  6. Submit the details to view your Jammu University Result 2026 on the screen and check subject-wise marks, total marks, and result status.
  7. Download or print the marksheet/result for future reference and preserve it until the original mark sheet is issued by the university.
  8. Carefully verify personal details and marks; for any discrepancy, contact the Controller of Examinations office using the official contact details provided on the Jammu University website.
