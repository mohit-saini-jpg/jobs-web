Jammu University Result 2026 Direct Link

The University of Jammu posts all examination notifications and result links on the Controller of Examination (COE) portal. Students can use the main result/notification page to locate their specific course and semester result by notification number and session.

How to Check Jammu University Result 2026

Use the steps below to access and download your Jammu University UG or BE Result 2026:
  1. Visit the Controller of Examination portal: https://www.coeju.com/index.aspx.
  2. Click on the “Notices/Results” section or directly open https://www.coeju.com/notices.aspx to view the latest notifications.
  3. In the list, find the notification matching your exam, such as: NOTIFICATION NO. 45 - UG 4TH SEM EXAM - 2023 NOTIFICATION NO. 02, 03 - UG 5TH SEM EXAM - 2025-26 NOTIFICATION NO. 185, 92, 104, 31, 35, 36, 04, 02, 08 - UG 6TH SEM EXAM - 2021-2025 NOTIFICATION NO. 03, 16 OF 2026 - BE 8TH SEM EXAM - 2026.
  4. NOTIFICATION NO. 45 - UG 4TH SEM EXAM - 2023
  5. NOTIFICATION NO. 02, 03 - UG 5TH SEM EXAM - 2025-26
  6. NOTIFICATION NO. 185, 92, 104, 31, 35, 36, 04, 02, 08 - UG 6TH SEM EXAM - 2021-2025
  7. NOTIFICATION NO. 03, 16 OF 2026 - BE 8TH SEM EXAM - 2026.
  8. Click on the relevant notification link to open the result PDF or the online result server link.
  9. If a PDF opens, use Ctrl+F to search for your roll number and check your marks and result status; if redirected to an online result page, choose your course/semester and enter your roll number to view your scorecard.
  10. Download or print the result page/PDF for future reference, revaluation forms or admission processes.
