3How to Download IISER IAT 2026 Admit Card
Sr.TypeStyleItemsText
1listordered
  • Visit the IISER admission portal iiseradmission.in.
  • Click on the “IAT 2026 Admit Card / Hall Ticket” link on the homepage or IAT 2026 section; you will be redirected to the Digialm login page.
  • On the login page Click Here, enter your User ID (application ID) and Password, then fill the captcha code.
  • Click on Login to access your IAT 2026 dashboard.
  • Click on the “Admit Card / Hall Ticket” link; your IISER IAT 2026 admit card will appear showing your name, roll number, exam date, reporting time and exam centre.
  • Verify all details and download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies of the admit card to carry on exam day.
2paragraphIf the website is slow due to heavy traffic, candidates are advised to try again after some time.

6Exam‑Day Instructions for IISER IAT 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphTo ensure smooth conduct of the IISER IAT 2026 CBT, candidates should follow these guidelines:
2listunordered
  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card so that verification, frisking and seating can be completed; latecomers after gate‑closing time will not be allowed entry.
  • Carry only essential items: IISER IAT admit card (A4 printout), valid photo ID and any permitted items as per instructions; follow all directions printed on the hall ticket and displayed at the centre.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless explicitly permitted), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the exam hall; violation can lead to cancellation of the exam and further action.
  • Follow instructions displayed on the computer screen and those announced by invigilators; indulging in unfair means or disobeying rules may result in disqualification and debarment from IISER admissions.
  • Keep your IISER IAT 2026 admit card safely even after the exam, as it may be required during result declaration, counselling and admission processes.
