3How to Download GDCA & CHM Exam Hall Ticket 2026
Sr.TypeStyleItems
1listordered
  • Visit the official GDCA website gdca.maharashtra.gov.in (GDCA portal link as shared in official updates and student instructions).
  • Click on Login / Candidate Login and enter your username and password created at the time of registration.
  • After login, open “My Account” from the top menu.
  • In your account dashboard, scroll down to find the section where GDCA Form and Hall Ticket options are displayed.
  • Click on Hall Ticket and select the Exam Year 2026 from the dropdown list.
  • Choose the correct exam type - GDCA if you applied for GDCA, or CHM if you applied for Co‑operative Housing Management - and then click to view/download the hall ticket.
  • Your GDCA/CHM Exam Hall Ticket 2026 will open; download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies to carry on all exam days.

6Exam‑Day Instructions for GDCA & CHM Exam 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphTo ensure smooth conduct of the GDCA & CHM Exam 2026, candidates should follow these guidelines:
2listunordered
  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the hall ticket, so that document verification and seating can be completed; latecomers may not be allowed entry once the exam starts.
  • Carry only essential items: GDCA/CHM hall ticket, valid photo ID and permitted stationery; follow any additional instructions given with the hall ticket or in the official notification.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not permitted), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material into the examination hall; violation may lead to cancellation of your paper and further action.
  • Sit only on the seat allotted to you and follow instructions given by invigilators; indulging in unfair means or talking to other candidates can result in disqualification from the exam.
  • Keep your hall ticket safely even after the exam, as it may be required for result reference and further GDCA/CHM certification processes.
