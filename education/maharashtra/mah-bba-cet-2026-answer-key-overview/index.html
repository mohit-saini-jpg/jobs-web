How to Download MAH BBA CET 2026 Answer Key and Response Sheet

Candidates must log in to the MAH BBA CET portal using their registered credentials to view the answer key. The process is simple and fully online.

Step-by-step process to check answer key

  • Visit the official MAH BBA CET website - cetcell.mahacet.org.
  • Click on “Candidate Registration AY 2026-29” or the MAH BBA CET 2026 answer key link as shown on the homepage.
  • Log in using your application number / registered email ID and password.
  • After logging in, go to the Objection Tracking tab under the UG courses section.
  • The MAH BBA CET 2026 answer key and your response sheet will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download the answer key and response sheet in PDF format and save them for reference.

Advise candidates to check the data such as name, roll number, test date and shift in the login area to ensure they are viewing the correct set.
