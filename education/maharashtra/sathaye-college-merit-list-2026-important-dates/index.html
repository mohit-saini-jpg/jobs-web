How to Check Sathaye College Merit List 2026
  1. Visit the official website: sathayecollege.edu.in.
  2. Go to the Admissions or News & Notices section.
  3. Click on the relevant merit list link (e.g., First Merit List for BA/BCom/BSc).
  4. Download the PDF and search for your name using Ctrl+F.
  5. Check your category-wise cutoff and allotted room for verification.
