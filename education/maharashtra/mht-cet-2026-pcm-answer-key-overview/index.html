How to Download MHT CET PCM Answer Key 2026 & Response Sheet

Step-by-Step Process to Download Answer Key

Follow these steps to access your answer key and response sheet:

  • Go to the official website – cetcell.mahacet.org.
  • Click on the “MHT CET 2026” or “Candidate Login” link for the PCM group.
  • Log in using your registered email ID / application number and password.
  • On the dashboard, click on “MHT CET PCM Answer Key 2026 / Response Sheet”.
  • The provisional answer key, response sheet and question paper will appear on the screen.
  • Download and save all PDFs for further analysis and objection filing, if required.

How to Calculate Marks Using MHT CET PCM Answer Key 2026

Marking Scheme for PCM Group

For the PCM group, the MHT CET 2026 paper pattern and marking scheme are as follows:

  • Mathematics: 50 questions × 2 marks = 100 marks.
  • Physics: 50 questions × 1 mark = 50 marks.
  • Chemistry: 50 questions × 1 mark = 50 marks.
  • Total marks: 200 marks for PCM.
  • There is no negative marking for wrong or unattempted questions.

Steps to Estimate Your MHT CET PCM Score

  • Download your response sheet and answer key.
  • For Mathematics: count total correct answers and multiply by 2.
  • For Physics: count correct answers and multiply by 1.
  • For Chemistry: count correct answers and multiply by 1.
  • Add all three components to get your total out of 200.

Because there is no negative marking, you do not need to subtract marks for wrong answers; only correct responses add to your score.

MHT CET PCM Answer Key Objection Window 2026

Last Date and Objection Fee

The CET Cell provides a short objection window to challenge the provisional MHT CET PCM Answer Key 2026. For Session 1 PCM, objections can be filed up to May 22, 2026, as per current updates, and the fee is Rs 1000 per question.

If your challenge is accepted by the expert committee, the answer key is corrected and the objection fee is refunded; otherwise, the fee is not returned.

How to Raise Objections to MHT CET PCM Answer Key

  • Visit cetcell.mahacet.org and open the MHT CET 2026 candidate login.
  • Enter your login credentials (registered email / application number and password).
  • Under the “Objection Tracker” section, open the question paper and provisional answer key.
  • Select the specific question you want to challenge and mark the option you believe is correct.
  • Upload valid supporting proof, such as scans from standard textbooks or official references.
  • Pay Rs 1000 per question online and submit the objection form within the deadline.

Students are advised to raise objections only where they are confident and have strong academic references, as random challenges may not be accepted.

MHT CET PCM Answer Key 2026 - Important Instructions for Candidates

  • Always download the answer key and response sheet only from cetcell.mahacet.org.
  • Check your name, roll number and other details in the response sheet before using it to compute marks.
  • For any question you plan to challenge, keep textbook references ready and clearly highlight the relevant explanation.
  • Avoid waiting till the last minute to raise objections, as payment or server issues near the deadline can cause problems.
  • Keep all PDFs and receipts safe until the final result and counselling are over.
