YCMOU Hall Ticket 2026 Out for May/ June 2026 Exams

Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University has released the YCMOU Hall Ticket 2026 for the May/June 2026 examination session in online mode. Students enrolled in UG, PG, Diploma and Certificate programmes can now download their hall tickets through the Digital University portal before the commencement of the summer exams.

The hall ticket for May/June 2026 exams is available at the dedicated download page PreExamV2_DownloadHallTicket_New.aspx under ycmou.digitaluniversity.ac, and students are required to log in using their Permanent Registration Number (PRN) or username and the security code shown. Without the hall ticket printout, candidates are not allowed to enter the examination hall.

How to Download YCMOU Hall Ticket 2026

Follow these steps to download your YCMOU May/June 2026 hall ticket:

  1. Visit the official exam portal ycmou.digitaluniversity.ac and open the Hall Ticket section, or directly use the May/June 2026 hall ticket link (PreExamV2_DownloadHallTicket_New.aspx).
  2. Choose the exam event as May/June 2026, if asked.
  3. Enter your PRN (Permanent Registration Number) or username and the code/captcha displayed on the screen.
  4. Click on Search / Submit to fetch your hall ticket.
  5. The YCMOU Hall Ticket 2026 for May/June exams will appear with your personal details and subject‑wise date and time. Check all details carefully.
  6. Download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies of the hall ticket and keep one with you on each exam day.

If any issue occurs in downloading, students can contact their Study Centre or Regional Centre for help.

Details Mentioned on YCMOU Hall Ticket 2026

The YCMOU May/June 2026 hall ticket contains important candidate and exam information which must be verified:

  • Permanent Registration Number (PRN)
  • Student name and vernacular name
  • Gender, category and nationality
  • Course name and course code
  • Medium of examination
  • Exam event/session (May/June 2026)
  • Seat number and exam centre code
  • Centre name and venue name with full address
  • Exam dates and timings paper‑wise
  • Student’s address, mobile number and email ID

If any major error is noticed in your PRN, name, course or centre, you should report it to your Study Centre/Regional Director or the University using the contact details given on the YCMOU website.

Documents Required with YCMOU Hall Ticket 2026

On each exam day for the YCMOU May/June 2026 session, candidates should carry:

  • A printed copy of the YCMOU Hall Ticket 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence, Passport or College/University ID card, as accepted by the university.
  • Basic permitted stationery such as blue/black ball‑point pens and other items mentioned in the exam instructions.

Entry to the examination hall will not be allowed without the hall ticket and valid photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for YCMOU May/June 2026 Exams

To ensure smooth conduct of the YCMOU May/June 2026 examinations, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned in the timetable/hall ticket so that seating and verification can be completed on time.
  • Carry only the YCMOU hall ticket, valid photo ID and permitted stationery; do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not allowed), books, written notes or any electronic gadgets inside the exam hall.
  • Check your seat number and room/venue printed on the hall ticket and sit only on the allotted seat.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the hall ticket and those announced by invigilators; indulging in unfair means, talking to other students or disobeying rules may lead to cancellation of your paper.
  • Preserve your YCMOU Hall Ticket 2026 even after the exam, as it may be needed for result reference and any examination‑related communication.
