2paragraphSt. Xavier’s College Mumbai, an empowered autonomous institute affiliated to the University of Mumbai, is one of the most prestigious colleges in India. It releases multiple merit lists for first-year undergraduate admissions under NEP 2020. The First Merit List for self-financing programs has been published. Separate lists are released for different courses and categories such as General, Minority, and others. Candidates selected in the first list must follow strict timelines for form completion and fee payment.

5St Xavier's College Mumbai First Merit List 2026: Self-Financing Courses
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphThe First Merit List covers admissions to B.Com in Management Studies (BMS), B.A. MCJ (BMM), B.Sc. IT, and B.Sc. (Data Science & AI). Candidates must fill the pre-filled admission form on the portal https://sxc.qualcampus.com using credentials sent via email and SMS. They are required to select Minor Subject, Open Electives (OE), and Skill Enhancement Courses (SEC).
6Document Verification and Admission Process
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphSelected candidates must report for document verification as per their allotted slot on May 27, 28, or 29, 2026 at XIMR Building (Second Floor). One parent/guardian must accompany the candidate. Original documents and photocopies are mandatory. After verification, candidates receive a payment link for fee payment.
