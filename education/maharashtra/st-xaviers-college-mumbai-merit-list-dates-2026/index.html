St. Xavier’s College (Empowered Autonomous Institute), Mumbai has published the First Merit List for undergraduate self-financing courses including B.Com in Management Studies (earlier BMS), B.A. MCJ (earlier BMM), B.Sc. IT, and B.Sc. (Data Science & AI) for the academic year 2026-2027. Candidates whose names appear in the list must complete the online form and attend document verification as per the allotted slots. The merit list is prepared based on a combination of entrance scores and Class 12 marks.

St. Xavier’s College Mumbai, an empowered autonomous institute affiliated to the University of Mumbai, is one of the most prestigious colleges in India. It releases multiple merit lists for first-year undergraduate admissions under NEP 2020. The First Merit List for self-financing programs has been published. Separate lists are released for different courses and categories such as General, Minority, and others. Candidates selected in the first list must follow strict timelines for form completion and fee payment.

Steps to Check St Xavier's College Mumbai Merit List 2026
  1. Visit the official website -xaviers.edu.
  2. Navigate to the Admissions 2026 → Merit Lists section.
  3. Select the relevant course (e.g., B.A. MCJ or B.Sc. DSAI).
  4. Download the PDF merit list.
  5. Search for your name or form number using Ctrl+F.
  6. Check your allotted time slot for document verification.

The merit list contains important details such as:

  • Serial Number

  • Candidate’s Name

For B.A. MCJ, the merit is calculated as 60% Entrance Score + 40% Class 12 Marks. Candidates must note their slot timing carefully.

St Xavier's College Mumbai First Merit List 2026: Self-Financing Courses

The First Merit List covers admissions to B.Com in Management Studies (BMS), B.A. MCJ (BMM), B.Sc. IT, and B.Sc. (Data Science & AI). Candidates must fill the pre-filled admission form on the portal https://sxc.qualcampus.com using credentials sent via email and SMS. They are required to select Minor Subject, Open Electives (OE), and Skill Enhancement Courses (SEC).

Document Verification and Admission Process

Selected candidates must report for document verification as per their allotted slot on May 27, 28, or 29, 2026 at XIMR Building (Second Floor). One parent/guardian must accompany the candidate. Original documents and photocopies are mandatory. After verification, candidates receive a payment link for fee payment.
