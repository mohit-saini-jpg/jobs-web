10paragraphAfter the publication of this final key, the objection process stands closed. That means candidates can no longer raise new objections or request changes. The final key is binding and will directly determine each candidate’s marks and percentile.
5How to Calculate MAH MBA CET 2026 Score Using Final Answer Key
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphMarking scheme for MAH MBA CET 2026
2paragraphMAH MBA CET generally follows a straightforward marking pattern:
3listunordered
  • 1 mark is awarded for every correct answer.
  • No marks are deducted for incorrect answers.
  • No marks are awarded or deducted for questions not attempted.
4paragraphCandidates should always verify the exact marking scheme in the official information brochure for the current session, but this pattern is typically followed.
5paragraphExample of calculating your tentative raw score
6paragraphTo estimate your score using the final answer key:
7listunordered
  • Step 1: Count the total number of correct answers as per the final key.
  • Step 2: Count the total number of incorrect and unattempted questions (these carry 0 marks).
  • Step 3: Your tentative raw score = number of correct answers × 1.
8paragraphFor example, if you attempted most questions and, after comparison, you find 165 correct responses, your estimated score will be 165 marks. The CET Cell will later convert this raw score to a percentile based on the performance of all candidates.
6MAH MBA CET 2026 Result and Next Steps After Final Answer Key
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphOnce the final answer key is released, the next major event is the declaration of MAH MBA CET 2026 result. The CET Cell will use the final answer key to compute each candidate’s raw marks and then convert these into percentile scores. These percentile scores will be reflected on the MAH MBA CET 2026 scorecard.
2paragraphCandidates will have to log in with their application number and password on the official website to download the scorecard. The scorecard will show your overall percentile, which plays a crucial role in MBA/MMS admissions across Maharashtra.
3paragraphWhat happens after the result - CAP rounds and admissions
4paragraphAfter the MAH MBA CET 2026 result is out, the State CET Cell will launch the Centralised Admission Process (CAP) for MBA/MMS programmes. During CAP:
5listunordered
  • Candidates must register online and upload required documents.
  • They will fill and lock their preferences for participating institutes and courses.
  • Seats will be allotted based on MAH MBA CET percentile, category, and preferences.
  • Candidates must confirm their seats by reporting to the allotted institute within the given deadlines.
6paragraphYour estimated score from the final answer key can help you identify realistic target institutes before CAP registration opens.
