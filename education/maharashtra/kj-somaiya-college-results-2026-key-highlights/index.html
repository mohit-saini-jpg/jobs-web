Details

KJ Somaiya College has officially released the KJ Somaiya College Merit List 2026 along with multiple examination results for the academic session 2026-27. The first merit list for FYBA, FYBCom, BSc, and BCom courses for external students has been published on 26 May 2026. Additionally, BCom Semester III and IV ATKT results for April and March 2026 have also been declared.

KJ Somaiya College Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - https://www.somaiya.edu.in/. The results include undergraduate merit lists and semester ATKT results for courses like B.A, B.Com, and B.Sc. Students can check and download their results directly in PDF format without login credentials from the official portal.

Candidates are advised to carefully verify their details such as name, marks, and eligibility status in the merit list or result PDFs. In case of any discrepancies, they should immediately contact the college authorities.
