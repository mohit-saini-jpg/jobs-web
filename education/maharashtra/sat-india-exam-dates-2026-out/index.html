6SAT 2026 Registration - How to Apply Online from India
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphYou must register online through the official SAT portal. There is no offline registration for Indian candidates.
2paragraphOfficial SAT Registration Portal for India
3paragraphIndian students use the same global platform as all other test takers:
4listunordered
  • Visit the SAT “Dates & Deadlines” and “Registration” sections on the official SAT website.
  • There is no separate “SAT India” website; instead, you select “India” and then your preferred city/test centre while booking your slot.
  • All registration, fee payment and admission ticket generation happen through your online College Board account.
5paragraphStep-by-step SAT Registration Process 2026 (India)
6listordered
  • Visit the official SAT portal Go to the SAT section of the official College Board site and click on “Register for the SAT”.
  • Create a College Board account Sign up with a valid email ID and provide accurate personal details. Ensure they match your passport or valid ID, as discrepancies can cause issues on exam day.
  • Log in and choose SAT After logging in, select the SAT exam option and proceed to view available test dates for 2026.
  • Select your SAT test date Choose a preferred test date from the list of 2026 dates displayed. Consider your school exams, preparation level and target admission deadlines.
  • Choose a test centre in India The portal will show available test centres in India for that date. Select your city (e.g., Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Bengaluru) and preferred centre. Seats can fill quickly in popular centres, so book early.
  • Enter personal and academic details Fill in your name, date of birth, address, school details and other required information carefully. These details appear on your admission ticket and score reports.
  • Upload a recent photograph Upload a clear, recent passport-style photograph that matches the guidelines mentioned on the portal. The same photo appears on your admission ticket.
  • Pay the exam fee Pay the SAT fee online. The total fee generally includes a base registration fee plus an additional international fee for non-US test centres. Late registration or date changes may incur extra charges.
  • Confirm registration and download the admission ticket Once payment is successful, confirm your booking and download your SAT admission ticket. Print it and keep a soft copy as backup; you must carry it to the test centre.
7paragraphEarly registration is strongly recommended for major cities such as Delhi/NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune and Kolkata, as seats in popular centres can be filled weeks before the deadline.
