3What is NMIMS CET 2026?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphNMIMS CET is the university-level entrance examination conducted by Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies for admission to its undergraduate engineering and technology programmes across its various campuses. The exam tests candidates on subjects including Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, and is a gateway to one of India's reputed deemed universities.
2paragraphFor the 2026 admission cycle, NMIMS CET was conducted earlier in the year, and the results have now been declared in the form of merit ranks assigned to each qualified candidate.
4NMIMS CET Result 2026 - What Does "Merit Rank" Mean?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphRather than publishing raw scores alone, NMIMS CET 2026 results are displayed as merit ranks. Understanding this format is essential before you log in to check your result.
2paragraphA merit rank reflects your position relative to all other candidates who appeared for the exam. A lower rank number means better performance. For example, if you have been assigned Merit Rank 1, you are the top scorer across all candidates. Your merit rank will be the primary criterion used during NMIMS CET 2026 counselling to determine seat allotment across the university's campuses and programmes.

7NMIMS CET 2026 Counselling - What Comes Next?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphWith the NMIMS CET 2026 result now declared, the focus shifts to counselling. This section explains what qualified candidates need to prepare for.
2paragraphCandidates who have secured a merit rank in NMIMS CET 2026 are required to participate in the NMIMS CET 2026 counselling process to secure their seat in one of the available programmes. The counselling is a centralised seat allocation process wherein seats across NMIMS campuses are offered to candidates based on their merit rank, choice of programme, and category.
8What to Expect During NMIMS CET 2026 Counselling
Sr.TypeStyleItemsText
1listunordered
  • Registration for counselling: Qualified candidates must complete their counselling registration on the official portal within the stipulated deadline
  • Choice filling: Candidates select their preferred campus and programme in order of priority
  • Seat allotment: Seats are allotted based on merit rank, category, and availability
  • Document verification: Allotted candidates must appear for document verification with original certificates
  • Fee payment: Candidates must pay the seat acceptance fee to confirm their admission
2paragraphCandidates are advised to keep checking ncet.nmims.edu regularly for updates on counselling dates, schedules, and round-wise seat allotment notifications.
