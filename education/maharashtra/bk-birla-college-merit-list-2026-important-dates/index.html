BK Birla College Merit List 2026 is out on 26 May 2026. Download First Merit List PDF for FYBA, FYBCom, FYBSc & Unaided courses at B.K. Birla College Kalyan (bkbck.edu.in).
Details

BK Birla College Merit List 2026 (B. K. Birla College of Arts, Science & Commerce, Kalyan) has been officially released on 26 May 2026. Students can now download the First Merit List for aided and unaided undergraduate programs for the academic year 2026-27 from the official website bkbck.edu.in.

How to Check BK Birla College Merit List 2026
  1. Visit the official website: https://bkbck.edu.in/
  2. Go to the Admission or Notices section.
  3. Click on the First Merit List link for your course.
  4. Download the PDF and search your name using Ctrl + F.
