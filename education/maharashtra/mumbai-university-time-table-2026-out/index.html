How to Download Mumbai University Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the Mumbai University Time Table 2026 from the official examination portal. Follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official Mumbai University website at www.mu.ac.in.
  2. Go to the Examination section and select the Timetable option.
  3. Choose the relevant category for May 2026 examinations.
  4. Locate the practical timetable for M.A. Geography CDOE.
  5. Click on the link to open the PDF file.
  6. Download and save the timetable on your device for ready reference.

Students should carefully review the official timetable and keep a printed copy for exam preparation.
