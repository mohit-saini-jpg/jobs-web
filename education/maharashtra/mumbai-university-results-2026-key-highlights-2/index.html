Steps to Check Mumbai University Result 2026

Follow the simple steps given below to check and download Mumbai University Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official Mumbai University result portal at https://www.mumresults.in/
  • On the homepage, search for your course and semester using the result code or course name (for example, M.Sc Hotel & Hospitality Administration Sem V, B.Com Sem VI, T.Y.B.A Sem V, BFA, or B.Ed Sem III).
  • Click on the corresponding result link to open the PDF file in your browser.
  • Once the PDF opens, use the search function (Ctrl+F) and type your seat number or name.
  • Check your subject-wise marks, total marks, grade and result status as mentioned in the PDF.
  • Download the PDF file and save it on your device or take a printout for future reference and admission or document verification purposes.
