Ruparel College Merit List 2026 (D. G. Ruparel College of Arts, Science and Commerce) has been officially released on 26 May 2026. Students can now download the First Merit List for FYBA, FYBCom, FYBSc, BSc IT, BSc CS and other undergraduate programs for the academic year 2026-27 from the official website ruparel.edu.
