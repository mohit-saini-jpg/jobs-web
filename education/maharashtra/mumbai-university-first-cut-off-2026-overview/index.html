5How to Check Mumbai University First Cut Off 2026 Online
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThis section helps students who search with queries like “how to check MU first cut off”, “how to download Mumbai University merit list”, etc.
2paragraphStep-by-step process to download MU 1st Merit List
3listunordered
  • Step 1: Open your browser and visit the official Mumbai University website https://www.mu.ac.in/ or the official MU UG admission portal, if provided.
  • Step 2: On the home page, find and click on the link for “UG Admissions 2026-27” or “First Merit List / First Cut Off 2026”.
  • Step 3: Select your stream and programme (for example, BA, BCom, BSc, BMS, BAF, BAMMC, etc.).
  • Step 4: Choose the name of your college from the list of participating colleges.
  • Step 5: Click on the link for the “First Merit List 2026” or “First Cut Off 2026” PDF for that college and course.
  • Step 6: The MU first merit list / cut off PDF will open on the screen. Check your name, application number, category and percentage.
  • Step 7: Download the PDF and save or print it for future reference.
4paragraphIf the link is not visible on the university portal, students should directly check their applied college’s official website for the latest merit list.
