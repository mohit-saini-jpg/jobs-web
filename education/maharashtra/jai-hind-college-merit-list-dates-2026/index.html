Detailed Information

Jai Hind College (Empowered Autonomous) has announced that the first merit list for undergraduate admissions (FYBA, FYBCom, FYBSc and self-financing courses) is out. Candidates can check their selection status on the official website jaihindcollege.com. The merit list is prepared based on Class 12 marks for various aided and unaided programs. Selected candidates must complete document verification and fee payment within the given deadlines to secure their seats.

Jai Hind College, affiliated to the University of Mumbai, is one of the most reputed colleges in Mumbai. It releases multiple merit lists for first-year undergraduate admissions every year. The process includes separate lists for aided courses (BA, BCom, BSc), self-financing courses (BAF, BBI, BMM, BSc IT, etc.), in-house students, Sindhi minority category, and special categories.

Steps to Download Jai Hind College Merit List 2026

  • Visit the official website - jaihindcollege.com.
  • Go to the Admission Notices 2026-27 or Merit Lists section.
  • Click on the desired merit list link (e.g., FYBCom First Merit List Open Category).
  • The list will open in PDF format.
  • Download the PDF and use Ctrl+F to search for your name or application number.

Details Mentioned in the Jai Hind College Merit List 2026

The merit list PDF contains important information such as:

  • Application Number
  • Candidate’s Full Name
  • Aggregate Percentage in Class 12
  • Category (Open / Sindhi Minority / In-house / Special)
  • Course and Stream
  • Remarks (if any)

Candidates should carefully verify all details after downloading the list.

Jai Hind College Merit List 2026: Aided vs Self-Financing

Jai Hind College releases separate merit lists for aided and self-financing programs. Aided courses generally have higher competition and slightly different cutoffs compared to self-financing courses like BAF, BBI, BMM, and BSc IT. In-house students (from Jai Hind Junior College) receive priority in separate in-house merit lists.
