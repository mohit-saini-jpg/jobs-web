Jharkhand Academic Council (JAC), Ranchi has officially released the JAC 10th Result 2026 for Class 10 students for the 2025-26 academic session. The Jharkhand Board Class 10 result has been announced for all students who appeared in the annual matriculation examinations conducted by JAC.

JAC Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - jac.jharkhand.gov.in. How to Check JAC Class 11 Result 2026

Students can follow the simple steps below to check their JAC Class 11 Result 2026 online.

  1. Visit the official JAC result website at https://jacresults.com/ using a browser on your phone or computer.
  2. On the homepage, click on the link for "Class XI Annual Examination Result 2026" or "JAC Class 11 Result 2026".
  3. On the result login page, enter your Roll Code and Roll Number exactly as printed on your Class 11 admit card.
  4. Click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button to proceed.
  5. Your JAC Class 11 Result 2026 will appear on the screen showing your name, roll details, stream (Arts/Science/Commerce), subject-wise marks, total marks and result status (Pass/Promoted etc.).
  6. Download the online scorecard and take a printout for future reference and use it until the original mark sheet is issued by your school or Jharkhand Academic Council.
  7. Check all details carefully, and if any discrepancy is found in your particulars or marks, contact your school authorities or JAC board officials immediately.

How to Check JAC 10th Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check the Jharkhand Board JAC 10th Result 2026 online in a simple and error-free manner.

  1. Visit the official Jharkhand Academic Council website jac.jharkhand.gov.in or directly open the result portal jacresults.com.
  2. On the result page, click on the link that reads "JAC Annual Secondary Examination Result 2026" or "JAC 10th Result 2026".
  3. A new result login page will open on the screen.
  4. Enter your Roll Code and Roll Number exactly as printed on your JAC Class 10 admit card.
  5. Double-check the entered details and click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button.
  6. Your JAC 10th Result 2026 marksheet will be displayed with subject-wise marks, total marks, division and overall result status.
  7. Download the online marksheet and take a printout for future admission and documentation purposes until the original mark sheet is issued by your school.
