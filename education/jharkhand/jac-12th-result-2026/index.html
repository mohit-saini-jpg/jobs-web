JAC 12th Result 2026

The Jharkhand Academic Council (JAC) has officially released the JAC 12th Result 2026 for Intermediate students. Candidates who appeared for the Jharkhand Board Class 12 examination can now check their marks and qualifying status online through the official result portal.

JAC 12th Result 2026 is available online on the official websites - jacresults.com and jac.jharkhand.gov.in. Students can check and download their provisional mark sheet by entering their roll number, roll code, or other required login details mentioned on the result page.

After downloading the online scorecard, students should carefully verify their name, roll number, stream, subject-wise marks, total marks, and result status. In case of any discrepancy in the JAC Class 12 Result 2026, they should contact their school authorities or the Jharkhand Academic Council for correction and further guidance.

JAC 12th Result 2026 Key Highlights

The Jharkhand Board Class 12th Result 2026 has been declared for Intermediate students of different streams. The result is available online, allowing students to quickly access their marks and download the provisional marksheet for admission and future use.

Students can access the JAC 12th result using their login credentials as asked on the portal, while original mark sheets will be provided later through their respective schools.

How to Check JAC 12th Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check JAC 12th Result 2026 online:

  1. Visit the official result website - https://jacresults.com/ or https://jac.jharkhand.gov.in/.
  2. On the homepage, look for the link related to JAC 12th Result 2026.
  3. Click on the result link to open the login page.
  4. Enter your roll number, roll code, captcha, or other required credentials as mentioned on the screen.
  5. Click on the submit button to proceed.
  6. The JAC 12th Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen with subject-wise marks and overall result status.
  7. Download the provisional result and take a printout for future reference.
