Detailed Information

The Jharkhand B.Ed Entrance Exam 2026 is a state-level entrance test conducted by the Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) for admission to the two-year Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) programme in various government, government-aided, self-financed, and private B.Ed colleges across Jharkhand.

This exam serves as the gateway for aspiring school teachers in the state, ensuring candidates possess the necessary aptitude and knowledge for the teaching profession.

Graduates or postgraduates aiming to pursue a career in teaching should apply once the official notification is released on the JCECEB website. The exam is held annually as a common entrance test, with admissions based on merit in the entrance exam followed by counselling.

Jharkhand B.Ed Entrance Exam 2026 Application Process – How to Apply Online?

Follow these steps:

  • Visit the official JCECEB portal (jceceb.jharkhand.gov.in or the dedicated B.Ed link in the notification).
  • Click on “Jharkhand B.Ed Entrance Examination 2026 – Apply Online”.
  • Register by providing name, mobile number, email ID, and create a password.
  • Log in with credentials and fill personal details, address, category, academic qualifications.
  • Upload scanned photograph, signature, and documents in specified format/size.
  • Select exam centre preferences (if applicable) and verify details.
  • Pay the fee online via debit/credit card, net banking, or UPI.
  • Submit and download/print the confirmation page and receipt.

Jharkhand B.Ed Entrance Exam Pattern 2026

  • Mode: Offline, OMR-based pen-and-paper.
  • Questions: Objective multiple-choice.
  • Total: 100 questions for 100 marks.
  • Duration: 2 hours.
  • Negative Marking: 0.25 marks deducted per wrong answer.

Jharkhand B.Ed Syllabus 2026 (Subject-wise Overview)

  • Teaching Aptitude: Teaching profession, learner characteristics, teaching-learning process, classroom management, communication skills, educational psychology basics, evaluation/assessment.
  • General Awareness: Current affairs (national/international), Indian polity/constitution, history, geography, economy, science & technology, education system, social issues.
  • Subject Knowledge / Reasoning / Language: Basic reasoning (analogy, series, coding-decoding, classification), language comprehension/grammar, or graduation-level subject content (as per pattern).

For the detailed syllabus PDF of the Jharkhand B.Ed Entrance Exam 2026, refer to the official JCECEB brochure once released.

  • Check result: Visit the official site, enter roll number/login, and download the scorecard/merit status.

Preparation Tips for Jharkhand B.Ed Entrance Exam 2026

  • Build conceptual clarity in Teaching Aptitude and General Awareness.
  • Follow a 2–3 month plan with daily theory, MCQ practice, and revision.
  • Use standard B.Ed entrance books, previous year papers, and Jharkhand-specific practice sets.
  • Take timed OMR mocks; attempt easy questions first to avoid negative marking.
