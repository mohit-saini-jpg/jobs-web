1paragraphThe Jharkhand B.Ed Entrance Exam 2026 is a state-level entrance test conducted by the Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) for admission to the two-year Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) programme in various government, government-aided, self-financed, and private B.Ed colleges across Jharkhand.
2paragraphThis exam serves as the gateway for aspiring school teachers in the state, ensuring candidates possess the necessary aptitude and knowledge for the teaching profession.
3paragraphGraduates or postgraduates aiming to pursue a career in teaching should apply once the official notification is released on the JCECEB website. The exam is held annually as a common entrance test, with admissions based on merit in the entrance exam followed by counselling.
