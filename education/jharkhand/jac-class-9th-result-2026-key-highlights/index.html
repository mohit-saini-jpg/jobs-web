Jharkhand Academic Council (JAC) has officially released the JAC Class 9th Result 2026 for the 2025-26 academic session in online mode. The JAC 9th annual examination was conducted in March 2026 in offline mode, and the board has now activated the result link for students to view their marks online. JAC Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - jac.jharkhand.gov.in and the JAC result portal jacresults.com.

Students can check and download their JAC Class 9th result by visiting the official result page and logging in with their roll code and roll number as mentioned on their admit card. Candidates are advised to carefully verify their personal details, subject-wise marks and grade, and contact the school or JAC officials in case of any discrepancy in the result.

How to Download JAC Class 9th Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the step by step process given below to check the JAC Board Class 9th Result 2026 online in a simple and error free manner.

  1. Visit the official JAC result website jacresults.com or go through jac.jharkhand.gov.in and open the result section.
  2. On the homepage, click on the link for "JAC Class 9th Result 2026" or "Result of Class 9 Examination 2026".
  3. A login page will open where you have to enter your roll code and roll number exactly as printed on your admit card.
  4. After entering the details correctly, click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button to proceed.
  5. Your JAC 9th Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen with subject wise marks and grade.
  6. Download the provisional marksheet and take a clear printout for future reference and school records.
