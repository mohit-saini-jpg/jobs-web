Detailed Information

Ranchi University has declared Ranchi University Sem 1 Result 2026 for Undergraduate Certificate (Science) and Undergraduate Certificate (Commerce) Semester 1 Examination 2024 under the FYUGP structure for sessions 2022-2026 and 2024-2028. Ranchi University Result 2026 has been announced online on the official website - ranchiuniversity.ac.in - through press releases available in the Examination Results and Notice for Students sections.

Students enrolled in FYUGP Science and Commerce streams can download the official press release PDFs and check the summary of results, including total candidates appeared, passed and percentage, and then refer to the detailed marks via college or result sheets as notified. After checking the information, candidates should confirm their individual marks with their college/exam cell and contact university authorities if any discrepancy appears in their result or certificate.

Steps to Check Ranchi University Sem 1 Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and interpret your Ranchi University FYUGP Semester 1 Result 2026 for Undergraduate Certificate (Science/Commerce):

  • Visit the official Notice for Students page: https://ranchiuniversity.ac.in/notice-for-students.
  • In the list of notices, find the entries dated 01-04-2026 for: Press Release of Undergraduate Certificate (Science), Semester-I Examination 2024 (Session 2022-2026/2024-2028) Press Release of Undergraduate Certificate (Commerce), Semester-I Examination 2024 (Session 2022-2026/2024-2028).
  • Press Release of Undergraduate Certificate (Science), Semester-I Examination 2024 (Session 2022-2026/2024-2028)
  • Press Release of Undergraduate Certificate (Commerce), Semester-I Examination 2024 (Session 2022-2026/2024-2028).
  • Click on the relevant press release link for your stream (Science/Commerce) and session (2022-2026 or 2024-2028) to download the PDF.
  • Open the PDF and read the summary, which usually includes total candidates appeared, passed and pass percentage, along with any general instructions.
  • For your individual marks, follow the instructions given in the press release (such as checking detailed result lists from your college or university portal, if mentioned) or contact your college examination department.
  • After confirming your marks, keep a copy of the press release and your detailed marks record for future academic procedures or documentation.
