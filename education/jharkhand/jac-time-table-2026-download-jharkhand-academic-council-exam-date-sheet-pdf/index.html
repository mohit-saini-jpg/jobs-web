What is JAC Time Table 2026?

The JAC Time Table 2026 is the official examination schedule released by Jharkhand Academic Council for Class 8, 9, and 11 annual exams in the academic year 2025-26. This schedule includes:

  • Exact exam dates and shifts for each class
  • Exam timings (generally two shifts per day)
  • Subject-wise allocation across dates
  • OMR-based objective questions
  • Guidelines for students and schools

Students must follow the schedule to plan their preparation effectively.

How to Download JAC Time Table 2026? (Step-by-Step)

Follow these steps once the official PDF is released:

Method 1: From Official Website

  • Visit Official Website: Go to https://jac.jharkhand.gov.in
  • Navigate to Notices/Examinations: Look for “Notifications” or “Examination” section
  • Find Time Table Link: Search for “Class 8/9/11 Exam Schedule 2026”
  • Select Your Class: Click on relevant link
  • Download PDF: Save the date sheet
  • Print Copy: Take a printout for reference

Before the Exam - Checklist

  • Verify your exam dates and shifts
  • Download admit card from official website
  • Check exam centre details
  • Carry valid ID and admit card
  • Reach exam centre early

Documents Required for Exam

  • Admit Card (mandatory)
  • School ID Card
  • Stationery (pen, pencil for OMR)

Passing Criteria

  • General passing marks: 33% in each subject (theory + internal/practical)
  • Class 8: 50 marks objective + school internal assessment
  • Class 9 & 11: Objective questions + internal marks
