CUJ PG Admission 2026

CUJ PG Admission 2026 (Central University of Jharkhand PG Admission 2026) registration is currently open. Candidates who appeared in CUET (PG)-2026 can now register for various postgraduate programmes at the Central University of Jharkhand for the academic session 2026-27. The online registration process started on May 20, 2026, and will continue till June 10, 2026.

Interested candidates must pay the prescribed non-refundable registration fee and complete the process on the official Samarth portal to participate in the counselling and admission process. This is a golden opportunity for CUET PG 2026 qualifiers seeking admission in reputed central university programmes.

Eligibility Criteria for CUJ PG Admission 2026

Candidates must:

  • Have appeared in CUET (PG)-2026 in the relevant mapped subject.
  • Possess a valid CUET (PG) score.
  • Fulfill the programme-specific eligibility (Bachelor’s degree with required minimum percentage as per department norms – generally 50-55% for General, relaxed for reserved categories).

Note: Final admission is subject to verification of documents and meeting university eligibility.

How to Apply for CUJ PG Admission 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official registration portal: cujcuet.samarth.edu.in
  • Enter your CUET (PG)-2026 Application Number and Roll Number.
  • Fill in required details and pay the registration fee.
  • Submit and download the confirmation page.

Documents Required for CUJ PG Registration

  • CUET (PG)-2026 Score Card & Admit Card
  • Class 10th and 12th Marksheets & Certificates
  • Graduation Marksheets & Degree/Provisional Certificate
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Character Certificate, Migration/Transfer Certificate
  • Passport-size photographs
