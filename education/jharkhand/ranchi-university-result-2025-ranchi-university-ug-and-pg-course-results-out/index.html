How to Check Ranchi University Result 2025 Online?

Students can check their Ranchi University result 2025 for UG and PG Course by following these simple steps:

  1. Visit Official Website: Go to ranchiuniversity.ac.in
  2. Find Results Section: Click on "Results" or "Examination" tab on homepage
  3. Select Your Exam: Choose UG and PG Course from the list
  4. Enter Details: Input your Roll Number and Registration Number
  5. Submit & View: Click "Submit" to view your Ranchi University result 2025
  6. Download: Save/print your mark sheet PDF for future reference

To check your Ranchi University results 2025, students must have the following details ready:
