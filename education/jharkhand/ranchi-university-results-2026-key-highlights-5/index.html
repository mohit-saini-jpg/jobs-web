  • Visit the official website of Ranchi University - ranchiuniversity.ac.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the examination or results section and click on the Exam Results link or directly open https://ranchiuniversity.ac.in/exam_results.
  • Look for the latest notifications or links related to: Undergraduate Certificate (Arts), Semester-II Examination-2024 press release. Bachelor of (Arts/Com./Sci.) (Vocational) Semester-VI Examination - 2025 (Session 2022-2025) press release.

  • Download and save the provisional result or PDF for future reference and print a copy if needed.
