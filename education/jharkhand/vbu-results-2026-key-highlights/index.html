How to Check VBU Result 2026

  • Visit the official website of Vinoba Bhave University at vbu.ac.in.
  • Click on the "Notice" or "Result" section or directly open https://www.vbu.ac.in/notice/Result.
  • On the result/notice page, look for the notification titled: "Result of BHMS (2nd Professional) Examination of 2022-27 (CBDC Course) held in the Month of December, 2025" or "Result of BBA (4th Semester) Under CBCS Examination of 2023-26 held in the month of December, 2025"
  • "Result of BHMS (2nd Professional) Examination of 2022-27 (CBDC Course) held in the Month of December, 2025"
  • or "Result of BBA (4th Semester) Under CBCS Examination of 2023-26 held in the month of December, 2025"
  • Click on the relevant result notice link.
  • The result document (PDF or webpage) will open containing the result details.
  • Search for your roll number, name, or other identifiers as given in the result to check your marks and result status.
