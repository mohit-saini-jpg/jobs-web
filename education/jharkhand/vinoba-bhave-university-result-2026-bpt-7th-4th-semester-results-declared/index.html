Vinoba Bhave University Result 2026: BPT 7th & 4th Semester Results Declared

Vinoba Bhave University (VBU), Hazaribag, has officially released the Vinoba Bhave University Result 2026 for the B.P.T. (Bachelor of Physiotherapy) programs. The results for the 7th Semester (2021–25 batch) and the 4th Semester (2023–27 batch) were declared on February 11, 2026.

Students who appeared for these exams can now check their marks and download the result sheet from the official website — www.vbuuniv.in.

How to Check Vinoba Bhave University Result 2026

  • Visit the official website — www.vbuuniv.in.
  • Navigate to the Examination or Results section.
  • Click on “Result of B.P.T. (7th/4th Semester)”.
  • Enter your Roll Number or Registration Number.
  • Click on Submit to view your marks.
  • Download and print your result for reference.

Revaluation and Rechecking Process

If a student finds any discrepancy or is not satisfied with their marks, VBU allows revaluation or rechecking of answer scripts.

  • Applications can be submitted through the college’s examination cell.
  • Students must apply within the stipulated deadline after the result declaration.
  • Revised marks (if any) will be updated on the official website.

About Vinoba Bhave University (VBU)

Vinoba Bhave University (VBU), located in Hazaribag, Jharkhand, is a prominent public university offering a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. It is affiliated with several colleges across Jharkhand and conducts semester exams for regular and professional courses such as BPT, B.Sc, BA, and B.Com every year.
