BBMKU FYUGP Semester 2 Result 2026 Declared – Check Online Now

The BBMKU, has officially released the FYUGP Semester II Result 2026 for the academic sessions 2024-28 and 2023-27. Students enrolled in undergraduate courses under the Four-Year Undergraduate Programme (FYUGP) can now check their results online using their roll number through the official result portal.

The university has published the results online only. Students must check their individual results using their roll numbers, as no public merit list or gazette has been released.

How to Check BBMKU FYUGP Semester 2 Result 2026
  1. Visit the official result portal: https://bbmkuniv.in/student/ug/result
  2. Select Course Type as "FYUGP".
  3. Choose Semester as "II".
  4. Enter your Roll Number.
  5. Click on Submit / View Result.
  6. Your subject-wise marks and overall result status will appear.
  7. Download or print your result for future reference.

Tip: Keep your admit card ready to enter the correct roll number. If the website is slow, try again after some time.
