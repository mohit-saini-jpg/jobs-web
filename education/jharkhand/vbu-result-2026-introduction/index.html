VBU Result 2026 Introduction

Vinoba Bhave University has declared the VBU Result 2026 for key medical and dental professional examinations conducted in 2025 (II session). The latest notification includes the Result of MBBS 1st professional exam 2025 (II) along with Result of BDS 3rd professional exam 2025 (II), Result of BDS 2nd professional exam 2025 (II) and Result of BDS 1st professional exam 2025 (II). VBU Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - vbu.ac.in - under the Result/Notice section.

Students of affiliated medical and dental colleges can now check and download their professional exam results through the official result link by using their roll number or other exam credentials. After viewing the marksheet or result PDF, candidates should verify their name, roll number, college, professional year, subject wise marks and overall pass/fail status and contact the examination department in case of any discrepancy.

Vinoba Bhave University Results 2026 Key Highlights

VBU has issued a consolidated professional exam update for MBBS and BDS programmes, making it easier for medical and dental students to access their 2025 (II) cycle results. These results are crucial as they determine progression to the next professional year and eligibility for clinical training and internships.

How to Download VBU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your VBU MBBS or BDS Professional Result 2026:

  1. Visit the VBU result page: https://www.vbu.ac.in/notice/Result.
  2. Scroll through the list of result notices and locate your exam title, such as: Result of M.B.B.S 1st professional exam, 2025 (II) Result of B.D.S 3rd professional exam, 2025 (II) Result of B.D.S 2nd professional exam, 2025 (II) Result of B.D.S 1st professional exam, 2025 (II).
  3. Result of M.B.B.S 1st professional exam, 2025 (II)
  4. Result of B.D.S 3rd professional exam, 2025 (II)
  5. Result of B.D.S 2nd professional exam, 2025 (II)
  6. Result of B.D.S 1st professional exam, 2025 (II).
  7. Click on the relevant result link to open the PDF or result document.
  8. Once the PDF opens, press Ctrl+F and type your Roll Number to quickly find your entry.
  9. Check your name, roll number, college, professional year and subject wise marks/result status (Pass/Fail/Repeat).
  10. Download or print the PDF for use in academic records, internship applications and future verification.
