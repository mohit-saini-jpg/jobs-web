Detailed Information

The Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE), Kerala has officially released the KEAM Result 2026 for the Kerala Engineering, Architecture and Medical entrance examination for the current academic session. The scorecards have been made available online for candidates who appeared for the KEAM 2026 entrance test for Engineering, Architecture, Pharmacy and other professional courses in Kerala. KEAM Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - cee.kerala.gov.in.

Candidates can check and download their KEAM 2026 result by logging in with their application number, password and security code on the CEE Kerala candidate portal. After logging in, they can view, download and save their KEAM scorecard for use during counselling and admission procedures. Students are advised to carefully verify their personal details, scores and other information mentioned on the scorecard and immediately contact CEE Kerala in case of any discrepancy.

How to check KEAM Result 2026

Follow the step-by-step process given below to check the KEAM Result 2026 online in a simple and error-free way.

  • Visit the official CEE Kerala website at cee.kerala.gov.in
  • On the homepage, click on the 'KEAM 2026 Candidate Portal' or KEAM Result 2026 link.
  • Enter your KEAM application number, password and the security code as shown on the screen.
  • Click on the login button to open your KEAM 2026 candidate dashboard.
  • Click on the 'Result' or 'Scorecard' option to view your KEAM Result 2026.
  • Download and save the KEAM 2026 scorecard, and take a printout for counselling and future admission processes.
