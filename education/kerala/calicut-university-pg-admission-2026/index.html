Calicut University PG Admission 2026

Calicut University PG Admission 2026 online registration has started from 20th May 2026. The last date to apply is 29th May 2026. University of Calicut (UOC) has invited online applications for admission to various Post Graduate Programmes (Without Entrance) in its Affiliated Colleges and University Centres for the academic year 2026-27 through PGCAP 2026 (PG Centralised Admission Process). All candidates seeking admission must register through the Centralised Admission Process.

Eligibility Criteria for Calicut University PG Admission 2026

Candidates must satisfy the academic qualifications and indexing rules prescribed for each programme. Eligibility details are available in the official Prospectus.

  • Candidates should have a valid Bachelor’s degree in the relevant subject.
  • Final year students can also apply (subject to fulfilling eligibility at the time of admission).
  • All candidates (General / Reservation / Management / Sports / PwD etc.) must register through CAP.

How to Apply for Calicut University PG Admission 2026 (Step-by-Step Process)

  • Visit the official website: admission.uoc.ac.in
  • Click on the PGCAP 2026 link and register using a valid email and mobile number.
  • Fill in personal, academic, and reservation details.
  • Pay the registration fee online (₹520/- for General, ₹220/- for SC/ST).
  • Submit the application and take a printout for future reference.
  • Wait for the allotment schedule to be published.
  • Check allotment results and remit the mandatory fee to confirm the seat.
  • Report to the allotted college for document verification and admission.

Selection & Allotment Process

  • Admission is based on Index Marks and the priority/options submitted by the candidate.
  • Centralised Allotment Process (CAP) will be conducted.
  • Minimum two allotments will be done for Government, Aided, Unaided Colleges, IHRD Institutions & University Centres.
  • Reservation rules as per University norms apply.
