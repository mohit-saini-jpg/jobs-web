5How to check Kerala Plus One Result 2026 at keralaresults.nic.in?
Sr.TypeStyleItems
1listordered
  • Visit the official result portal at keralaresults.nic.in or results.hse.kerala.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the "DHSE First Year Result 2026" link.
  • Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth in the login fields.
  • Click the "Submit" button.
  • Your Kerala Plus One 2026 marks memo will appear on the screen.
  • Verify all details carefully, then download and save the scorecard for future reference.
6How to check Kerala Plus One Result 2026 via PRD LIVE App?
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThe PRD LIVE mobile application is an official, convenient alternative for students to access their Kerala Class 11 result 2026 directly on their smartphone without navigating to a web browser.
2listordered
  • Open the Google Play Store on your Android smartphone.
  • Search for and install the "PRD LIVE" application.
  • Open the app and tap on the "DHSE Kerala Plus One Result 2026" link.
  • Enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth in the login window.
  • Tap the "Submit" button.
  • Your Kerala Plus One 2026 result scorecard will open on the screen.

8How to check Kerala Plus One Result 2026 Name-Wise?
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphStudents who do not have their registration number can check results by name using the indiaresults.com portal. Follow these steps:
2listordered
  • Visit indiaresults.com.
  • Click on the "Kerala Plus One Results 2026" link.
  • Select the "View Results by Name" option.
  • Enter your full name, date of birth, and registered mobile number.
  • Click "Find Result", then select your name from the list.
  • Click "Get Results" to download your scorecard.
