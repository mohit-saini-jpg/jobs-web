2What Is the Kerala University FYUGP Trial Allotment 2026?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphThe Kerala University FYUGP Trial Allotment is a preliminary, non-binding seat distribution will be released before the official first allotment. It is generated based on each candidate's registered preferences, their academic merit, reservation category, and seat availability across affiliated colleges. The trial allotment does not guarantee admission it is purely a tool for candidates to gauge their probability of getting into a specific college or programme. Based on what the trial result shows, students are encouraged to strategically reorder their choices to maximize their chances before the binding allotment rounds begin.

4How to Edit and Rearrange College Choices After Kerala University Trial Allotment 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThe editing window is a critical opportunity this section walks you through exactly how to revise your preferences before the deadline.
2paragraphAfter reviewing your trial allotment result, you can modify your college and course preferences until June 16, 2026. This is your last chance before the first binding allotment is published on June 17. Follow these steps to edit your options:
3listunordered
  • Step 1 - Log In: Access your candidate profile using your registered application number and password on the admission portal.
  • Step 2 - Navigate to Option Registration: Click on the "Modify Options" link available on your dashboard.
  • Step 3 - Rearrange Priority: Use the navigation arrows to move your most preferred college-programme combinations to the top of the list.
  • Step 4 - Add or Delete Choices: You may add new college-course combinations or remove existing ones. If the trial result shows a low probability for certain options, consider deleting them and replacing them with more realistic ones.
  • Step 5 - Save Your Changes: Click "Save & Lock" or "Refresh Options" to confirm and submit your revised preferences.
