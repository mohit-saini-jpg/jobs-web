How to Download KTET Answer Key 2026 PDF

Step‑by‑Step Process to Download

Candidates who appeared in the KTET December 2025 session can download the answer key easily in PDF format. You can describe the process as:

  1. Visit the official KTET website - ktet.kerala.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, look for the link titled “RECTIFIED ANSWER KEY OF K‑TET DECEMBER 2025” or “KTET Answer Key 2026”.
  3. Click on the respective Category link - Category I, Category II, Category III or Category IV.
  4. The KTET Answer Key 2026 for that category will open in a new tab as a PDF file.
  5. Download and save the PDF on your device.
  6. Take a printout if needed and cross‑check your answers with the rectified key.

Because these PDFs are public, no user ID or password is usually required for downloading the category‑wise answer keys.

How to Calculate Marks Using KTET Answer Key 2026

The rectified KTET Answer Key 2026 allows candidates to calculate their expected scores more accurately, as it incorporates changes made after objections to the provisional key. You can explain the mark calculation in simple steps:

  • Step 1: Keep your KTET question booklet (or memory of responses) ready along with the rectified answer key PDF for your category.
  • Step 2: For each question, compare the option given in the rectified key with the option you marked in the exam.
  • Step 3: Count the total number of correct responses.
  • Step 4: Apply the official marking scheme mentioned in the KTET notification or information brochure (for example, one mark for each correct answer and no negative marking, if that is the scheme notified).
  • Step 5: The total marks obtained after this calculation will give you a good estimate of your KTET score.

Remind readers that actual marks and qualifying status will be confirmed only with the official result published by Kerala Pareeksha Bhavan.

KTET 2026 Answer Key - Objection and Rectified Key Details

Kerala Pareeksha Bhavan first released the KTET 2026 provisional answer key for the December 2025 session and allowed candidates to raise objections within a specified period. After reviewing the complaints and supporting documents, the authority published the rectified answer key for all categories.

The rectified answer key replaces the provisional key and is used for final evaluation and result preparation. Candidates can now only view the rectified key; the objection window is closed, and no new complaints are accepted for this session.

You can emphasise that the answers in the rectified key are final and that further re‑evaluation of answer key responses is generally not entertained.

KTET 2026 Exam & Categories - Quick Overview

KTET is conducted to assess the eligibility of candidates for teaching positions at different school levels in Kerala. Adding a short overview here addresses broader informational search intent (what is KTET, which category means what).

KTET Categories I, II, III, IV (Level‑wise)

The Kerala Teacher Eligibility Test is divided into four categories based on class level and subject profile:

  • Category I: For teachers of Lower Primary classes.
  • Category II: For teachers of Upper Primary classes.
  • Category III: For High School teachers (typically Classes 9-12).
  • Category IV: For Language Teachers and Specialist Teachers.

Each category has its own question paper pattern and eligibility requirements, which are detailed in the official KTET notification.

Basic KTET Eligibility (High‑Level)

At a high level, KTET eligibility usually requires:

  • Minimum prescribed educational qualifications such as Higher Secondary, Diploma in Education, Bachelor’s degree, or B.Ed/B.El.Ed depending on the category.
  • Fulfilment of age and category‑wise relaxation rules as per Kerala government norms.

Advising candidates to consult the latest KTET notification for category‑wise educational qualifications and eligibility norms will keep your content accurate and safe from policy changes.
