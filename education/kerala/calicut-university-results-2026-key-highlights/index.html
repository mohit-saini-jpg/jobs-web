Detailed Information

The University of Calicut has officially released the Calicut University Revaluation Result 2026 for the Third Semester Four Year Under Graduate Programme (FYUG) Examination held in November 2025. This revaluation result is applicable for students from the 2024 admission batch who had applied for reassessment of their answer scripts. Calicut University Result 2026 has been announced online on the official website - uoc.ac.in through the dedicated results portal at results.uoc.ac.in.

Students can check and download their revaluation results by entering their registration number and security code on the official result portal. Candidates are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in their result and immediately contact the university examination cell in case of any discrepancies or errors in the published marks.

How to Download Calicut University Revaluation Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their Calicut University Third Semester FYUG Revaluation Result 2026 from the official portal:

  • Visit the official result portal of University of Calicut at results.uoc.ac.in
  • On the homepage, locate and click on the revaluation results section or notification link
  • Select the option for "Revaluation Result of Third Semester FYUG Examination November 2025" from the list of available results
  • Enter your valid registration number in the required field
  • Type the security code or captcha displayed on the screen accurately
  • Click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button to access your revaluation result
  • Your Calicut University Revaluation Result 2026 will appear on the screen with updated marks
  • Carefully verify all the details including your name, registration number, subject-wise marks and grades
  • Download the result PDF or take a printout for future reference and academic records
  • In case of any discrepancies in marks or personal details, contact the Pareeksha Bhavan examination cell immediately
