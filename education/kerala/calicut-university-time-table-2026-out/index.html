How to Download Calicut University Time Table 2026

Students can download the Calicut University Time Table 2026 directly from the official examination portal by following these simple steps.

  • Visit the official Pareeksha Bhavan website of Calicut University.
  • Click on the "Examination" section and select "Time Table".
  • Look for the notification dated 7 May 2026 mentioning your course and semester.
  • Click on the relevant time table link to view or download the PDF.
  • Save the file on your device and take a clear printout for reference.

Always refer to the official timetable and keep a copy with you throughout the examination preparation period.
