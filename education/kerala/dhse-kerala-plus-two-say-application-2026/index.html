Eligibility Criteria for DHSE Kerala Plus Two SAY 2026

Students who appeared for the Kerala Plus Two March 2026 examination and failed to secure the minimum D+ grade in one or more subjects are eligible for the SAY exam. Students who passed but wish to improve their score in one subject can also apply for the Improvement exam. Candidates must submit applications through their respective higher secondary schools.

How to Apply for DHSE Kerala Plus Two SAY Application 2026
  1. Collect the SAY examination application form from your higher secondary school.
  2. Fill in all personal details and the registration number used in the March 2026 board examination.
  3. Enter the correct subject codes for the subjects in which you want to appear.
  4. Attach a recent passport-size photograph.
  5. Pay the prescribed examination fee to the school.
  6. Submit the completed form to the school principal before June 3, 2026.

Apply Through School: Students must submit physical forms at their respective schools.

Documents Required
  • Filled application form
  • Passport size photograph
  • Copy of March 2026 Plus Two marksheet (if required)
