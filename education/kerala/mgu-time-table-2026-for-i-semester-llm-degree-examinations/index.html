MGU Time Table 2026 for I Semester LLM Degree Examinations

Mahatma Gandhi University (MGU), Kerala has officially released the revised MGU Time Table 2026 for I Semester LLM Degree Examinations. In continuation of the earlier notification dated 18.05.2026, the examinations originally scheduled from 29.05.2026 have been postponed and rescheduled to commence from 29.06.2026. Students of 2025 Admission Regular, 2022-2024 Admissions Supplementary, and 2021 Admission First Mercy Chance can now check the updated schedule on the official website – www.mgu.ac.in.

This rescheduled MGU Time Table 2026 is crucial for all LLM candidates. Students are advised to download the official notification PDF and prepare accordingly.

How to Download MGU Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the MGU Time Table 2026 from the official university website. Follow these simple steps to get the revised timetable PDF.

  1. Visit the official website of Mahatma Gandhi University at www.mgu.ac.in.
  2. Navigate to the "Examinations" or "Notifications" section on the homepage.
  3. Look for the latest notification regarding I Semester LLM examinations 2026.
  4. Click on the link titled "I Semester LLM Degree Examinations - Rescheduled Time Table".
  5. The PDF file will open. Click on the download icon to save it to your device.
  6. Take a printout of the timetable for future reference and exam preparation.

Students are advised to regularly check the official MGU website for any further updates regarding the MGU Time Table 2026.
