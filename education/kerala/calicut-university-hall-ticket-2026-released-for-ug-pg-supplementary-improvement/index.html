Calicut University Hall Ticket 2026 Released for UG & PG Supplementary/ Improvement Exams

The University of Calicut has released the Calicut University Hall Ticket 2026 for various UG and PG Supplementary/ Improvement Examinations scheduled in June 2026. Students appearing for the notified examinations can now download their hall tickets through the official examination portal at cupbonline.uoc.ac.in.

Candidates must carry a printed copy of the hall ticket along with a valid photo ID to the examination centre. Entry to the examination hall will not be permitted without the admit card.

How to Download Calicut University Hall Ticket 2026

Students can download their hall ticket by following these steps:

  • Visit the official Calicut University hall ticket portal.
  • Open the Hall Ticket section available on the homepage.
  • Select the relevant examination from the list.
  • Enter the required registration details.
  • Click on the Submit/View Hall Ticket option.
  • The hall ticket will appear on the screen.
  • Download the PDF and take a clear printout for future use.

Details Mentioned on Calicut University Hall Ticket 2026

Candidates should carefully verify the following information printed on the hall ticket:

  • Candidate's Name
  • Registration Number
  • Roll Number
  • Course and Semester
  • Examination Name
  • Subject/Paper Details
  • Examination Date and Time
  • Examination Centre Name
  • Centre Address
  • Candidate Photograph
  • Signature (if applicable)
  • Important Examination Instructions

In case of any discrepancy in the hall ticket, students should immediately contact their college or the University examination branch.

Documents Required Along with Calicut University Hall Ticket 2026

Candidates must carry the following documents to the examination centre:

  • Printed copy of the Calicut University Hall Ticket 2026
  • Original College Identity Card
  • Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card, Driving Licence, Passport, or any valid government-issued photo ID
  • Category/PwD certificate (if applicable and required)
  • Any additional document specified by the university

Candidates without valid identification documents may not be allowed to appear for the examination.

Exam-Day Instructions for Calicut University Exams 2026

Students appearing for the examination should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the examination centre at least 30 minutes before the reporting time.
  • Carry the hall ticket and valid photo ID without fail.
  • Verify your allotted examination room before entering the hall.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless permitted), earphones, or electronic gadgets.
  • Follow all instructions given by invigilators and university authorities.
  • Use only permitted stationery items during the examination.
  • Maintain discipline and avoid any unfair practices.
  • Sign the attendance sheet wherever required.
  • Ensure that answer booklets and OMR sheets are filled correctly.
  • Candidates involved in malpractice may face cancellation of their examination.
