How to Check Kerala LSS, USS Scholarship Result 2026 Online

Follow the steps below to check your Kerala LSS, USS scholarship result 2026 online:

  1. Visit the official website bpekerala.in.
  2. On the homepage, open the “Results” section or click on the LSS/USS result notice.
  3. Click on the “CM Kids LSS USS 2026 Result” link or select LSS / USS result from the list.
  4. Enter your registration number and date of birth in the specified fields.
  5. Submit the details to view your CM Kids LSS/USS scorecard on the screen.
