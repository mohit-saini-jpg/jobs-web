How to Check Kannur University Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your Kannur University VI Semester UG Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official Kannur University exam result portal at http://www.exam.kannuruniversity.ac.in/ug_result.php.
  • On the result page, click on the link for "VI Semester UG C.B.C.S.S - OBE Regular/Imp./Sup. Results April 2026".
  • You will be redirected to the 6th Semester result page for the 2020 - 2023 admission batches.
  • Enter your Register Number (PRN) in the specified field correctly.
  • Click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button to proceed.
  • Your Kannur University VI Semester UG Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future reference and admission or document verification.
