4What Is Kerala Plus Two SAY (Save A Year) Exam?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphThe Kerala Plus Two SAY (Save A Year) exam is a special opportunity for students who could not pass one or more subjects in the regular March Plus Two examination. Instead of waiting a full year, they can appear in the SAY exam soon after results.
2paragraphThe exam also serves improvement candidates who have already passed but want to enhance their scores in one subject. This dual purpose makes SAY an important second-chance and score-improvement mechanism under DHSE Kerala.
5Kerala Plus Two SAY Exam 2026 Eligibility Criteria
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphSAY (supplementary) candidates
2paragraphStudents are generally eligible for the Kerala Plus Two SAY Exam 2026 if:
3listunordered
  • They appeared in the regular Plus Two (Class 12) board examination in March 2026, and
  • They failed to secure the minimum required grade (often D+ or equivalent pass mark) in one or more subjects.
4paragraphOnly those subjects in which the student has not qualified can be chosen for SAY. The exact number of subjects allowed and detailed conditions are clarified in the official DHSE notification.
5paragraphImprovement candidates
6paragraphImprovement candidates are students who have:
7listunordered
  • Qualified for higher studies in the March 2026 Plus Two exams, but
  • Wish to improve their marks in any one permitted subject.
8paragraphImprovement candidates usually appear again in that specific subject in the SAY session, and as per board rules, the better of the two marks is carried forward. Students should verify the improvement rules, including any restrictions, from the official circular.
6Kerala Plus Two SAY Exam 2026 Application Process
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphApplication dates and fees
2paragraphDHSE Kerala generally opens the application window shortly after declaring the regular Plus Two results and publishing the SAY notification. During this window, eligible students can apply for:
3listunordered
  • SAY (supplementary) exams
  • Improvement exams
  • In many cases, related processes such as photocopy or scrutiny of answer scripts
4paragraphThe exact application dates, fee per subject, additional charges and any late fee will be mentioned in the official notification. Students must strictly adhere to these dates to avoid rejection of their applications.
5paragraphHow to apply for Kerala +2 SAY exam through school
6paragraphKerala Plus Two SAY exam applications are normally processed through the respective schools. A typical step-by-step process that you can include is:
7listordered
  • Contact your school office or class teacher after the SAY notification is published to know the application dates and instructions.
  • Confirm the subjects you want to apply for - failed subjects for SAY or one subject for improvement.
  • Fill in the SAY/improvement application form with correct personal details, register number and subject codes as per your March 2026 hall ticket.
  • Pay the prescribed examination fee per subject and any additional fees specified by DHSE Kerala.
  • Submit the filled form and fee proof to the school within the deadline; the school will forward applications to the board.
  • Keep a photocopy or screenshot of the application and payment details for your records.

8Kerala Plus Two Result 2026 and Revaluation Options
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems

1paragraphThe Kerala Plus Two SAY exam follows the same syllabus and pattern as the regular March board examination. This means:
2listunordered
