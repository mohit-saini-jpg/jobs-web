Pondicherry University Time Table 2026 OUT

Pondicherry University has released the tentative Time Table 2026 for NEP, CBCS Arts & Science, and Vocational Courses for May/June 2026 examinations. Students appearing for Undergraduate and Postgraduate programmes can now check their exam schedule on the official examination portal.

How to Download Pondicherry University Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the Pondicherry University Time Table 2026 from the official examination portal by following the steps mentioned below.

  • Visit the official Pondicherry University exam portal.
  • Navigate to the Time Table section under UG/PG examinations.
  • Locate the link for Tentative Schedule May/June 2026 NEP & CBCS courses.
  • Click on the PDF link to open the complete timetable.
  • Download and save the file for future reference.
  • Take a printout and note important exam dates.

Students should carefully check the official timetable and keep a copy for their exam preparation.

Details Mentioned in Pondicherry University Time Table 2026

The official tentative timetable PDF released by Pondicherry University contains all necessary information for students appearing in the May/June 2026 examinations.

  • Subject-wise exam dates and days
  • Session timings (Forenoon / Afternoon)
