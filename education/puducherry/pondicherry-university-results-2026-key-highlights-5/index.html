How to Check Pondicherry University Result 2026 Online?

  • Go to the official exam portal https://exam.pondiuni.edu.in/results/ or visit https://www.pondiuni.edu.in and click the “Examination Results” link.
  • On the results page, locate your course code and name (for example, BSBT1/BSBT2 - B.Sc Biotechnology, BSCS2 - B.Sc Computer Science, BCA2 - BCA, BBA1 - BBA, BAEC1 - BA Economics, BAMA2 - BA Malayalam, BSIT2 - B.Sc IT, BSCAM - B.Sc AI & ML, BSCDS - B.Sc Data Science, etc.) and the corresponding semester (I-VI) with date 25/03/2026.
  • Click on the relevant result link; you may see an input form or a direct PDF/marksheet view depending on the course.
  • Enter your Register/Roll Number and select the semester/exam session if required, then submit the form to display your Pondicherry University UG result 2026.
  • Check your name, register number, programme, semester, subject‑wise marks/grades, credits and overall result status (Pass/Fail) carefully.
  • Download or print the provisional marksheet for your records; use it for higher‑semester admission, scholarship forms, placements and revaluation/re‑totalling requests where applicable.
  • If you notice any discrepancy in personal details or marks, contact your college examination cell or the Pondicherry University exam section promptly with supporting documents for correction.
