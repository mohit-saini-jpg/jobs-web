Steps to Check Pondicherry University Result 2026

Follow these steps to access the Pondicherry University Four Year Integrated B.A B.Ed/B.Sc B.Ed Result 2026:

  • Go to the Pondicherry University examination result portal: https://exam.pondiuni.edu.in/results/.
  • On the results page, choose the programme code and description matching your course, for example: EHIS1 - Four Year Integrated B.A B.Ed (History) EHIN1 - Four Year Integrated B.A B.Ed (Hindi) EENG1 - Four Year Integrated B.A B.Ed (English) ECSC1 - Four Year Integrated B.Sc B.Ed (Computer Science) EMAT1 - Four Year Integrated B.Sc B.Ed (Mathematics) EPHY1 - Four Year Integrated B.Sc B.Ed (Physics) ECHM1 - Four Year Integrated B.Sc B.Ed (Chemistry).
  • EHIS1 - Four Year Integrated B.A B.Ed (History)
  • EHIN1 - Four Year Integrated B.A B.Ed (Hindi)
  • EENG1 - Four Year Integrated B.A B.Ed (English)
  • ECSC1 - Four Year Integrated B.Sc B.Ed (Computer Science)
  • EMAT1 - Four Year Integrated B.Sc B.Ed (Mathematics)
  • EPHY1 - Four Year Integrated B.Sc B.Ed (Physics)
  • ECHM1 - Four Year Integrated B.Sc B.Ed (Chemistry).
  • Select the appropriate Semester (I, II, III, IV, V, VI, VII or VIII) from the list corresponding to your examination.
  • Enter your Registration Number / Roll Number / Date of Birth as required on the login screen.
  • Click on the Submit / Get Result / View Result button to display your marks.
  • Check your name, registration number, semester, subject wise marks/grades, SGPA/CGPA and result status, then download or print the provisional marksheet for future reference.
