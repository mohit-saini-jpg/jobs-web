How to Check Pondicherry University Result 2026 Online?

Students can check their Pondicherry University result 2026 for 4th Semester by following these simple steps:

  1. Visit Official Website: Go to pondiuni.edu.in
  2. Find Results Section: Click on "Results" or "Examination" tab on homepage
  3. Select Your Exam: Choose 4th Semester (Diploma) from the list
  4. Enter Details: Input your Roll Number and Registration Number
  5. Submit & View: Click "Submit" to view your Pondicherry University result 2026
  6. Download: Save/print your mark sheet PDF for future reference
