Follow the steps below to check and download your Pondicherry University UG Result 2026 for BPES, BBA or BA Sociology:

  1. Open the official result portal: https://exam.pondiuni.edu.in/results/.
  2. On the results page, look for the links corresponding to your Course Code and Name, such as BPE2 – BPES, BBA2 – BBA, BASO2 – BA Sociology or BASO1 – BA Sociology along with the required Semester (I/II/III/IV/V/VI).

  5. Click on the Submit / View Result button to display your semester marks.
