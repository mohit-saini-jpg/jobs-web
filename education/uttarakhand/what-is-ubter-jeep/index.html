2What is UBTER JEEP?
Sr.TypeTextHeadersRows
1paragraphBefore diving into the result details, here is a quick overview of the exam for first-time visitors. UBTER JEEP (Joint Entrance Examination Polytechnics) is a state-level entrance examination conducted by the Institute of Research, Development and Training (IRDT) Dehradun under the Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education (UBTER) Roorkee. It is the gateway for students seeking admission to diploma programmes in Engineering, Pharmacy, Textile Design, Garment Technology, Fashion Design, and other technical courses at polytechnic colleges across Uttarakhand.

4How to Download UBTER JEEP Rank Card 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphDownloading the UBTER JEEP 2026 rank card is a straightforward process. Follow the step-by-step instructions below to access your result quickly and save it for future use during counselling and admission.
2listordered
  • Visit the official UBTER JEEP portal: ubterjeep.co.in
  • On the homepage, look for the "Download Rank Card (Result)" link under the "Important Works" section.
  • Click on the result link - it will open the result entry page.
  • Enter your JEEP 2026 Roll Number in the field provided.
  • Click the "Show" button.
  • Your rank card will appear on the screen displaying your name, rank, and marks.
  • Download and save the rank card as a PDF for future reference.
  • Print at least two copies - one to submit at the institution during admission and one for your personal records.
3paragraphImportant Tip: The rank card is available in both Hindi and English on the portal. If the Hindi version loads by default, look for a language toggle on the download page.
5Details Mentioned on UBTER JEEP 2026 Rank Card
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThe rank card is not merely a score sheet - it is the official document you carry to counselling and ultimately to admission. Knowing what information it contains helps you verify its accuracy immediately after downloading.
2paragraphThe following details are printed on the UBTER JEEP 2026 rank card:
3listunordered
  • Candidate's Name
  • Candidate's Roll Number
  • Registration Number
  • Photograph of the Candidate
  • Candidate's Signature
  • Total Marks Obtained
  • Rank Secured in the Examination
  • Category (General / OBC / SC / ST)
  • Date of Birth
4paragraphAction Required: If any detail on your rank card is incorrect - such as wrong name, wrong marks, or wrong date of birth - you must raise a discrepancy complaint within 24 hours of the result publication. Contact UBTER / IRDT Dehradun through the contact details listed on ubter.in and submit a written complaint along with your registration number and the specific error.
6UBTER JEEP Merit List 2026
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphThe merit list is a critical outcome of the result process. This section explains how the UBTER JEEP merit list is prepared and what role it plays in the admission journey.
2paragraphThe UBTER JEEP 2026 merit list will be released shortly after the result declaration. The merit list is prepared purely on the basis of marks scored in the JEEP 2026 entrance exam - no board exam marks or internal assessment scores are factored in. All qualified candidates are ranked according to their performance, and seat allocation during counselling is done strictly on the basis of this merit list.
7Tie-Breaking Criteria for UBTER JEEP 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphIn cases where two or more candidates score identical marks, the following tie-breaking rules are applied in order:
2listordered
  • Date of Birth - The older candidate is ranked higher.
  • Subject-wise Marks - If the tie persists, marks obtained in Mathematics, Science/Chemistry, and Biology are considered.
8UBTER JEEP 2026 Cutoff Marks
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThe cutoff is the minimum score a candidate must achieve to qualify for UBTER JEEP counselling. Understanding cutoff trends helps candidates assess their chances of admission.
2paragraphThe cutoff marks for UBTER JEEP 2026 vary depending on the course and category. The following factors determine the JEEP cutoff each year:
3listunordered
  • Total number of applications received that year
  • Category of the candidate (General, OBC, SC/ST)
  • Difficulty level of the examination paper
  • Number of seats available for each course
4paragraphCandidates who do not meet the cutoff for their desired course may consider applying for UBTER JEEP in the following academic year.
9What's Next? UBTER JEEP Counselling 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphAfter the result is declared, qualified candidates must participate in the counselling process to secure admission. Here is what to expect after downloading your rank card.
2paragraphAll candidates who qualify the minimum cutoff criteria will be eligible for UBTER JEEP Counselling 2026. The counselling process involves online registration, choice filling (selecting preferred colleges and courses), seat allotment based on rank and availability, and document verification followed by fee payment at the allotted institution. The counselling dates for JEEP 2026 will be announced on the official website shortly after the result. Keep checking ubterjeep.co.in regularly for updates.
