How to Check UBSE Uttarakhand Board 10th 12th Result 2026 Online

Once the UBSE Uttarakhand Board 10TH 12TH result 2026 link is activated, students can follow these steps on the official portal.
Step‑by‑step process:
  1. Visit the official result website: ubse.uk.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on either: “Uttarakhand Board Class 10 Result 2026” or “Uttarakhand Board Class 12 Result 2026”.
  3. “Uttarakhand Board Class 10 Result 2026” or “Uttarakhand Board Class 12 Result 2026”.
  4. Enter your roll number and other required details exactly as printed on the admit card.
  5. Click on the “Get Result” or similar button.
  6. Your online marksheet for UBSE Uttarakhand Board 10TH 12TH result 2026 will appear on the screen.
  7. Download the marksheet PDF and take a printout for future use (until you receive the original mark sheet from your school).

Details Mentioned on UBSE 10th & 12th Online Marksheet

The online scorecard available after UBSE Uttarakhand Board 10TH 12TH result 2026 declaration will carry important academic details.
Key fields commonly included:
  • Student’s name
  • Roll number and registration/application number
  • Father’s and mother’s name
  • Class and stream (for Class 12 – Science/Arts/Commerce if applicable)
  • School name and code
  • Subject‑wise marks (theory and practical, where applicable)
  • Total marks and percentage
  • Result status (Pass / Fail / Compartment)
  • Division or grade, as per board rules
The online marksheet is provisional and students must collect the original mark sheet and passing certificate from their respective schools.
