About
JEE Advanced Admit Card 2026 will be released today, 11 May 2026, at jeeadv.ac.in. Download your hall ticket using registration number, date of birth and mobile number, and check exam date 17 May 2026, exam timings, centre details and instructions here.
Details

IIT Roorkee, the organising institute for JEE Advanced 2026, will release the JEE Advanced 2026 admit card today, Monday, 11 May 2026 on the official website jeeadv.ac.in. As per the official schedule, the admit card download window will remain open from 11 May 2026 (10:00 IST) to 17 May 2026 (14:30 IST).

The JEE Advanced 2026 exam is scheduled to be held on 17 May 2026 in two papers (Paper 1 and Paper 2) on the same day, and candidates must appear in both papers for their scores to be considered. Candidates who have qualified JEE Main 2026 and registered for JEE Advanced 2026 can download the hall ticket using their registration details once the link is activated.
