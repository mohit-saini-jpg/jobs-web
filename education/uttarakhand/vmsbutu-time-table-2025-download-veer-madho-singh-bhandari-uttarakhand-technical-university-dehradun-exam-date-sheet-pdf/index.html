What is VMSBUTU Time Table 2025?

The VMSBUTU Time Table 2025 is the official examination schedule released by Veer Madho Singh Bhandari Uttarakhand Technical University, Dehradun for the academic year 2025-26. This document contains crucial information including:

  • Exact exam dates for each subject/paper
  • Exam timing and duration
  • Course-wise and semester-wise schedule
  • Regular and supplementary exam dates
  • Important instructions for students

Students appearing for Odd Semester examinations must download the Veer Madho Singh Bhandari Uttarakhand Technical University, Dehradun time table to prepare their study schedule effectively.

How to Download VMSBUTU Time Table 2025? (Step-by-Step)

Follow these simple steps to download your VMSBUTU exam date sheet 2025:

Method 1: Direct Download from Official Website
  1. Visit Official Website: Go to uktech.ac.in
  2. Navigate to Examination Section: Look for "Examinations" or "Students" section on homepage
  3. Click on Time Table/Date Sheet: Find "Examination Schedule" or "Time Table" link
  4. Select Academic Year: Choose 2025-26 or current session
  5. Choose Your Course: Select M.Pharm, B.Pharm, M.Tech, MBA, MCA, LLM, LLB and B.Arch or your specific course
  6. Select Exam Type: Regular/Supplementary/Backlog
  7. Download PDF: Click on the download button to save the time table
  8. Save and Print: Save the PDF and take a printout for reference
