How to Check Result

Follow these simple steps to check and download your JEE Advanced 2026 result and final answer key:

  1. Visit the official website - https://cdata.jeeadv.ac.in/
  2. Click the 'JEE Advanced 2026 Result' link or the result banner on the homepage.
  3. Enter your registration number and date of birth (or other required credentials).
  4. Submit the details to view your scorecard and final answer key.
  5. Download the PDF of the result/scorecard and save it for future reference; print a copy if needed.
