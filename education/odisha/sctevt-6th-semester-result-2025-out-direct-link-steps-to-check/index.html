How to Check SCTEVT 6th Semester Result 2025 Online

The process to check SCTEVT 6th Semester Result 2025 is simple and can be completed in a few minutes on a mobile or desktop browser.

  • Visit the official SCTEVT 6th Semester result portal: Click Here.
  • On the page, locate the login box for “6TH SEMESTER – RESULTS”.
  • Enter your Registration Number exactly as printed on your admit card.
  • Enter your Date of Birth in the specified format.
  • Click on the “View” or similar button to submit your details.
  • Your SCTEVT 6th Semester Result 2025 marksheet will appear on the screen, showing subject-wise and total marks.
  • Download the result PDF or take a clear printout for future reference.

If the page is slow or not loading due to heavy traffic, wait for a few minutes and then refresh or try during non-peak hours.

Students should confirm that their name, registration number, branch and subjects match with the details in their college records.

SCTEVT 6th Semester Result 2025 – Revaluation and Rechecking

If you are not satisfied with your SCTEVT 6th Semester Result 2025 or suspect an error in evaluation, SCTE&VT generally allows students to apply for revaluation or rechecking as per the rules mentioned in official notifications. Such notices are usually published on the council website under the examination or notice-board section, along with last dates and fee details.

While the exact schedule and fee for the 2025 6th semester revaluation will be defined by SCTE&VT, students can expect a process similar to earlier sessions, where:

  • Applications are submitted online or through the institute within the given deadline.
  • A prescribed fee is charged per subject or paper.
  • Revaluated marks, if changed, are updated in the official records and reflected in an updated marksheet.

Always rely on the official SCTE&VT Odisha website and your institute for accurate information about revaluation timelines and procedures.

What After SCTEVT 6th Semester Result 2025?

After checking the SCTEVT 6th Semester Result 2025, students reach a crucial stage in their academic journey, as the 6th semester typically marks the completion of the diploma programme in many branches. Depending on your score, interests and career plan, you can explore multiple options.

  • Apply for lateral entry to B.Tech / B.E. programmes in engineering colleges, where diploma holders can often join directly in the second year as per state or national rules.
  • Appear for recruitment drives or competitive exams that accept diploma qualifications for technical posts.
  • Seek apprenticeship or trainee positions in industries, public sector undertakings and private companies that recruit diploma engineers.
  • Upgrade skills with specialized certifications or short-term courses in areas such as CAD tools, maintenance, quality control or emerging technologies.

Keep digital and physical copies of your marksheets and certificates safely, as they will be needed for counselling, document verification and job applications.

SCTEVT 6th Semester Result 2025 – Important Instructions for Students

To avoid problems later, students should follow some basic precautions related to SCTEVT 6th Semester Result 2025.

  • Treat the online result as a provisional marksheet; the final, official document is the original marksheet issued by your institute or SCTE&VT.
  • Cross-check your personal details, subject list and marks; if you find any mismatch, inform your college exam section immediately.
  • Keep multiple photocopies and scanned copies of the marksheet, especially before applying for higher studies or jobs.
  • Stay updated with further notices related to revaluation, supplementary exams or counselling on the official SCTE&VT website.
  • Do not share your registration number and date of birth on untrusted portals or social media platforms.
