OAV Puri Result 2026 Direct Link

The Puri district website hosts separate PDFs for each OAV and class, which act as the official selection/merit lists for OAV admissions. Students and parents can use the direct links below to open and download the relevant OAV Puri Result 2026 PDFs.

How to Check OAV Puri Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download the OAV Puri Result 2026 selection lists:
  1. Visit the official notice page: https://puri.odisha.gov.in/en/notice/recruitment.
  2. In the list of notices, locate the links for “OAV [School Name] CLASS-[VI/VII/VIII/IX].pdf” corresponding to your OAV and class (for example, OAV Astaranga Class-VI, OAV Gop Class-IX, OAV Nimapara Class-VIII).
  3. Click on the required link to open the PDF in your browser.
  4. Use the search option (Ctrl + F) and type the candidate’s name or roll number to quickly find it in the list.
  5. Confirm the candidate’s details, category and whether they are selected/admitted for the concerned OAV and class.
  6. Download or print the PDF for admission counselling, document verification and future reference.
