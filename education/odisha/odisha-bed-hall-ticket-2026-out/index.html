Odisha B.Ed Hall Ticket 2026 Out

State Selection Board, Odisha has released the Odisha B.Ed Hall Ticket 2026 for the Teacher Education entrance tests on its admit card portal. The hall ticket is available for candidates appearing in B.Ed, B.H.Ed and M.Ed Entrance Test 2026 for admission to the 2‑year programmes under SAMS Odisha.

As per the official schedule, the Odisha B.Ed Entrance Exam 2026 will be held on 31 May 2026, while B.H.Ed (Hindi) and M.Ed entrance exams will be conducted on 3 June 2026 at designated centres across the state. The admit card download link for these exams has been activated from 27 May 2026 on the SSB Odisha/SAMS portals.

How to Download Odisha B.Ed Hall Ticket 2026

Follow these steps to download your Odisha B.Ed Entrance Exam 2026 hall ticket:

  • Visit the official SSB Odisha website ssbodisha.ac.in or the admit card link shared by SSB (ssbodisha.site/bedmed26/public/login.php).
  • On the SSB homepage, go to the “Admit Card” section and click on “Download Hall Ticket (Admit Card) for B.Ed./B.H.Ed./M.Ed. Entrance Test - 2026”.
  • You will be redirected to the login page; enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth in the specified fields.
  • Click on Submit / Login; your Odisha B.Ed hall ticket 2026 will be displayed on the screen.
  • Check your name, exam date (31 May / 3 June), exam centre and other details carefully.
  • Download and print a clear A4‑size coloured copy of the hall ticket and keep additional copies for safety.

If the website is slow or shows error soon after release, candidates are advised to wait some time and try again instead of repeatedly refreshing.

Details Mentioned on Odisha B.Ed Hall Ticket 2026

The Odisha B.Ed/B.H.Ed/M.Ed admit card 2026 includes the following information which candidates must verify:

  • Candidate’s name and roll number.
  • Application number / registration details (if printed).
  • Exam name: B.Ed/B.H.Ed/M.Ed Entrance Test 2026.
  • Exam date and timing (31 May for B.Ed; 3 June for B.H.Ed & M.Ed).
  • Examination centre name, code and full address.
  • Candidate’s photograph and signature.
  • Important exam‑day instructions and guidelines.

In case of any mismatch in name, photograph, date of birth or exam centre details, candidates should immediately contact SSB Odisha/SAMS helpline before the exam date.

Documents Required with Odisha B.Ed Hall Ticket 2026

On the day of the Odisha B.Ed/B.H.Ed/M.Ed Entrance Test 2026, candidates must carry:

  • A printed coloured A4‑size copy of the Odisha B.Ed Hall Ticket 2026.
  • One original photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card, Passport, Driving Licence or any other government‑issued identity.
  • It is also advisable to carry 2 recent passport‑size photographs, similar to those used in the application, for verification if required.

Candidates without the hall ticket and valid ID will not be permitted to enter the examination hall.
