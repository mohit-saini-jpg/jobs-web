NEST Admit Card 2026

The National Entrance Screening Test (NEST) 2026 conducting body has released the NEST Admit Card 2026 on the official exam portal. Candidates who successfully completed the NEST 2026 application process can now log in and download their hall ticket in online mode only; no hard copy is sent by post or email.

The admit card carries important details such as candidate’s name, roll number, exam date, exam time, reporting time, exam centre and exam‑day instructions. Candidates must carefully verify all details and carry a printed copy of the NEST admit card along with a valid photo ID to the test centre on exam day.

How to Download NEST Admit Card 2026

Follow these steps to download your NEST 2026 hall ticket:

  1. Visit the official NEST exam website (nestexam.in) and go to the candidate login/admit card link.
  2. Click on the “NEST 2026 Admit Card / Download Hall Ticket” link on the homepage.
  3. Log in using your registered email ID (or username) and password created at the time of registration; you may also need to enter a security code/captcha.
  4. After successful login, click on the “Download Admit Card / Hall Ticket” button.
  5. Your NEST Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen; check all details like name, exam date, time and centre.
  6. Download the PDF and print 2–3 clear A4‑size copies of the admit card to carry to the exam centre.

If you forget your password, use the “Forgot Password” option on the login page to reset it via your registered email/mobile.

Details Mentioned on NEST Admit Card 2026

The NEST hall ticket generally includes the following information:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • NEST 2026 roll number and application number
  • Date of birth and category (as per application)
  • Exam name: NEST 2026
  • Exam date and exam time / shift
  • Reporting time and gate‑closing time
  • Exam centre name, centre code and full address
  • Exam‑day instructions and guidelines for candidates

Any discrepancy in personal details, exam date or exam centre should be reported immediately to the NEST helpdesk before the exam.

Documents Required with NEST Admit Card 2026

On the day of the NEST 2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the NEST Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout, colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Voter ID Driving Licence Any other government‑issued photo ID mentioned in the information brochure
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID
  • Driving Licence
  • Any other government‑issued photo ID mentioned in the information brochure
  • Additional documents, if specified (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter for candidates availing scribe facility).

Entry to the examination hall will not be permitted without the printed admit card and valid photo ID.
