OTET Exam Date 2026 - Latest Update

The Board of Secondary Education (BSE), Odisha has rescheduled the Odisha Teacher Eligibility Test, OTET 2026, to 21 June 2026 in offline mode across the state. OTET 2026 will be conducted in two shifts on the same day - Paper 1 in the morning and Paper 2 in the afternoon for primary and upper primary teaching eligibility.

As per the detailed schedule, OTET Paper 1 will be conducted from 9:00 am to 11:30 am and Paper 2 from 2:00 pm to 4:30 pm. The official exam date and timetable have been notified through the OTET 2026 notice and subsequent reschedule notification published by BSE Odisha.

OTET 2026 Overview

The Odisha Teacher Eligibility Test is a state-level eligibility test for candidates who wish to become teachers for Classes I to VIII in government, private aided, and private unaided schools of Odisha. The exam is conducted by the Board of Secondary Education, Odisha, Cuttack once a year, in two papers - Paper I for primary level and Paper II for upper primary level.

How to Download OTET 2026 Exam Date Notice PDF

Candidates can easily download the OTET 2026 exam date notice from the official BSE Odisha website using the steps below.

  1. Visit the official website of the Board of Secondary Education, Odisha: www.bseodisha.ac.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the “Examinations” section and select “OTET Examinations”.
  3. Look for the OTET 2026 Exam Date / Notification link in the list of latest updates.
  4. Click on the link titled for OTET-2026 Exam Date or Notice to open the PDF.
  5. Download the PDF and save it on your device for quick access and verification.

By following these steps, candidates ensure that they are checking the authentic, updated schedule as notified by BSE Odisha.
