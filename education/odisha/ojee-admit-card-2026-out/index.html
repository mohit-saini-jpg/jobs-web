OJEE Admit Card 2026 Out

The Odisha Joint Entrance Examination Board (OJEEB) has released the OJEE Admit Card 2026 for candidates appearing in the Odisha Joint Entrance Examination for MBA, MCA, M.Tech, B.Tech (LE), B.Pharm and other professional courses. Registered candidates can now download their OJEE hall ticket 2026 from the official website ojee.nic.in by logging in with their application number and date of birth. As per the official schedule, OJEE Admit Card 2026 was released on 25 April 2026, around 9-10 days before the entrance exam, which is scheduled from 4 to 10 May 2026 in computer-based mode across multiple centres.

OJEE Admit Card 2026 Download Link

OJEEB has activated the OJEE admit card 2026 download link on the official website ojee.nic.in under the candidate login section. Candidates must use this official link to log in and download their hall ticket, as the admit card is not sent by post or email. After downloading, students should verify all details printed on the OJEE hall ticket 2026 and take multiple printouts to carry to the exam centre on their exam date.

How To Download OJEE Admit Card 2026

Candidates can follow the step-by-step process below to download OJEE Admit Card 2026:

  • Open your browser and visit the official OJEE website: https://ojee.nic.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the link that says 'OJEE 2026 Admit Card' or 'Download Admit Card for OJEE 2026'.
  • You will be redirected to the candidate login page. Enter your Application Number and Date of Birth in DDMMYYYY or DD/MM/YYYY format (as specified), and enter the security pin/captcha.
  • Click on the 'Login' or 'Submit' button to access your OJEE 2026 hall ticket.
  • The OJEE Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen; check your name, photograph, exam date, shift timing and exam centre address.
  • Download the admit card PDF and take one or more clear printouts for exam-day use and future reference.

In case of difficulty in downloading the admit card or if there is any error in the details, candidates should immediately contact the OJEEB helpdesk through the contact numbers or email mentioned on ojee.nic.in.

OJEE 2026 Exam Dates And Pattern

OJEE 2026 will be conducted on 4, 5, 7, 8 and 9 May 2026 in computer-based test mode across multiple exam centres in Odisha and some neighbouring states. The exam is held in different shifts each day, with specific dates and shifts allocated based on the course applied for (MBA, MCA, M.Tech, B.Tech LE, etc.), and the same is indicated on the admit card. Each paper includes multiple-choice questions, and candidates must carefully follow the on-screen instructions during the CBT.

Details Mentioned On OJEE Admit Card 2026

The OJEE admit card 2026 carries important information which candidates must verify:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph and signature
  • Application number and roll number (if allotted)
  • Exam name: OJEE 2026 and course name (MBA, MCA, etc.)
  • Exam date and shift timing (morning/afternoon)
  • Test centre name, code and full address
  • Reporting time and gate closing time
  • Instructions regarding allowed items, COVID/safety guidelines (if any) and exam-day rules

Any mismatch in personal details or exam-related information must be reported to OJEEB for rectification.

Documents Required With OJEE Admit Card 2026

On the day of the OJEE 2026 exam, candidates must bring the following to the exam centre:

  • Printed copy of the OJEE Admit Card 2026 (preferably on A4 paper)
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Voter ID Driving Licence
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID
  • Driving Licence
  • Any other document(s) specified in the exam instructions (e.g., photograph or undertaking, if applicable)

Candidates who do not carry their admit card and valid photo ID will not be permitted to enter the examination hall.
