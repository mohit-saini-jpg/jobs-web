3paragraphStep 2: Locate the "View Result" tab on the portal home page and tap on it.

5paragraphStep 4: Once the login page is visible on the screen, enter your "Registration Number", "Date of Birth", and "Captcha Code" in the given boxes and then select the "Submit" option.
6paragraphStep 5: Your Odisha Diploma semester result will appear on the screen.
7paragraphStep 6: Download the result PDF and take a printout for future reference.
