Steps to Check Sambalpur University Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your Sambalpur University 3rd Semester Result 2026:

  • Visit the official Sambalpur University notice board page: https://www.suniv.ac.in/notice-board.php.
  • On the notice board, look for the result notification dated 31/03/2026 for your course, such as MSc Bioinformatics, MSc Biotechnology, MSc Nutrition and Dietetics, MSc Food Science and Technology, MSW or MA Anthropology.
  • Click on the relevant result title to open the PDF or result document.
  • If the result opens in PDF format, use the search option to find your roll number or name in the document.
  • If the result page asks for credentials, enter your roll number/registration number and submit to view your marksheet.
  • Check your personal details, course name, semester, subject wise marks, total marks and result status, then download or print the result for future reference.
