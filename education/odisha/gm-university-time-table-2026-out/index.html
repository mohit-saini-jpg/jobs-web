How to Download GM University Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the GM University Time Table 2026 from the official university website. The process is simple and requires only a few clicks. Follow the steps below to access your class schedule quickly.

  • Visit the official website of Gangadhar Meher University at https://gmuniversity.ac.in.
  • Navigate to the Examination or Student Corner section on the homepage.
  • Look for the link titled "Time Table 2025-26 (as per NEP-2020) for +3 UG 2nd and 4th Semester".
  • Click on the notification to open the timetable page.
  • Select the PDF for your specific stream - Arts, Science or Commerce.
  • Download the file directly to your device.
  • Open the downloaded PDF and take a printout for future reference during the semester.

Students are advised to check the official GM University Time Table 2026 carefully for exact timings and instructions. Keep a printed copy handy for smooth class attendance.
