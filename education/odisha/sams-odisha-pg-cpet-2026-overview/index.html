Eligibility Criteria for CPET 2026
  • Educational Qualification: Candidates must have completed a Bachelor’s degree in a relevant discipline from a recognized university.
  • Minimum Marks: Generally, 45-50% aggregate marks required (varies by subject/university).
  • Final Year Students: Candidates appearing in the final semester of graduation are eligible to apply.
  • Domicile: Must be a resident of Odisha or fulfill specific university policies for other-state applicants.
How to Apply for SAMS Odisha PG CPET 2026 Online
  1. Visit the official SAMS PG portal at pg.samsodisha.gov.in.
  2. Click on “New User Registration” and create an applicant profile.
  3. Login using credentials and fill in educational, academic, and subject preference details.
  4. Upload the required documents, photograph, and signature.
  5. Pay the application fee through online payment gateways.
  6. Submit the application and print a copy for reference.
Exam Pattern and Selection Process
  • Mode of Exam: Offline (OMR-based)
  • Type of Questions: Multiple Choice (MCQs)
  • Total Marks: 70 marks (subject paper)
  • Time Duration: 90 minutes
  • Selection Process: Based on CPET marks + career marks (graduation and +2 level)
How to Download SAMS Odisha CPET Admit Card 2026
  1. Visit pg.samsodisha.gov.in.
  2. Click on the “Admit Card” link once activated.
  3. Enter your registration number and password.
  4. Download and print the admit card for exam day verification.
Important Documents Required
  • CPET 2026 Admit Card
  • Original ID proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Recent passport-size photographs
  • Graduation marksheet and certificate
  • Category and domicile certificate (if applicable)
