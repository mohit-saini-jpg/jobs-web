Direct Link to Check SCTEVT 1st, 3rd Semester Diploma Result 2025

Students can check their SCTEVT 1st and 3rd Semester Diploma Result 2025 through the dedicated SCTEVT result portals. Use the links provided below and keep your registration number and date of birth ready for login.

  • SCTEVT 1st Semester Result 2025: Click Here
  • SCTEVT 3rd Semester Result 2025: Click Here
  • Main SCTEVT Diploma Result page: sctevt.odisha.gov.in > Exam Results (Diploma)

In case any specific semester link is not opening directly, students can first visit the SCTEVT official website and navigate to the Diploma results section to find the active result link for their semester.

Step-by-step process to download SCTEVT Diploma result

Follow the steps below to check and download your SCTEVT Odisha Diploma result:

  • Open the official SCTEVT website at sctevt.odisha.gov.in or go to the result portal sctevtexams.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the “Exam Results” or “Diploma Result” section.
  • Select the link for “SCTEVT Diploma Result 2025 – Winter 2024” and choose your semester (1st or 3rd).
  • You will be redirected to the semester-wise result page (for example, 1st semester or 3rd semester results portal).
  • Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth in the respective fields.
  • Click on the “View Result” or “Submit” button.
  • Your SCTEVT 1st or 3rd Semester Diploma Result 2025 marksheet will appear on the screen.
  • Download the marksheet as a PDF and take a printout for future reference, admission, and verification purposes.

Details Mentioned on SCTEVT 1st, 3rd Semester Marksheet 2025

After downloading the SCTEVT result, students should carefully verify all the details printed on the provisional marksheet. Typically, the following information is displayed:

  • Student’s name
  • Registration number / Enrollment number
  • Institute / College name
  • Course name, branch and semester
  • Subject-wise names (theory and practical)
  • Subject-wise marks obtained
  • Maximum marks and total marks
  • Result status (Pass / Fail)
  • Remarks or division, if applicable

If there is any spelling error, mismatch in details, or discrepancy in marks, students are advised to contact their institute or the concerned examination section of SCTEVT at the earliest for correction.

Minimum marks and passing rules

To be declared as passed in the SCTEVT 1st or 3rd semester, students must obtain the minimum qualifying marks prescribed by SCTEVT in each subject as well as in the overall aggregate. Both theory and practical components are important, and failing in any compulsory component may result in an overall “Fail” status for that subject or semester, as per the rules framed by SCTEVT and the respective institute.

Revaluation, rechecking and supplementary exams

Students who are not satisfied with their marks, or who feel there may have been an error in evaluation, can look for official notifications regarding revaluation, rechecking or scrutiny of answer scripts. These processes, if available for the concerned session, are usually announced separately on the official SCTEVT website, along with:

  • Application start and end dates
  • Prescribed fees per subject or per paper
  • Mode of application (through institute or online)
  • Timeline for declaration of revaluation results

Students who do not clear one or more subjects may be given an opportunity to appear in supplementary or subsequent semester examinations, subject to SCTEVT regulations and institutional guidelines.

What to Do After SCTEVT 1st, 3rd Semester Result 2025

Once the SCTEVT Diploma results are declared and students have downloaded their marksheets, they should plan their next academic steps accordingly. Students who have passed:

  • 1st semester: move to 2nd semester and complete registration, fee payment and class joining as per their institute schedule.
  • 3rd semester: proceed to 4th semester, focusing more on core subjects that are crucial for future career opportunities and higher studies.

Those with backlogs should consult their college examination cell or department regarding the next available chance for improvement or supplementary exams, as well as attendance and academic requirements for the upcoming semester.
