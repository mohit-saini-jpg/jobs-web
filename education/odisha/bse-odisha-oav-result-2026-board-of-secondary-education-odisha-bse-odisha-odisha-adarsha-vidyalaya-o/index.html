  • Open the browser on mobile or desktop and go to the official BSE Odisha website (bseodisha.ac.in).
  • Click on the “Results” section or “Result of OAVET–2026 and OMAVET–2026” notice.
  • On the result page, click on the OAV Entrance Exam Result 2026 link for your class (Class 6 / lateral entry).
