Details

Central University of Odisha (CUO), Koraput has officially declared the Central University of Odisha Exam Result 2026 for various UG professional programmes under the School of Integrated Agriculture and Natural Resources. The latest result update includes semester-wise results for B.Tech in Dairy Technology, B.Sc (Hons) Agriculture and B.Sc (Hons) Forestry for the academic sessions 2023-24, 2024-25 and 2025-26, covering Monsoon (1st, 3rd, 5th Semester) and Winter (2nd, 4th Semester) sessions. Central University of Odisha Exam Result 2026 has been announced online on the official website - cuo.ac.in.

Students can check and download their CUO UG semester results by visiting the university’s Academic Results page and opening the course-wise PDF result links provided for each session and semester combination. The result PDFs carry student details, enrollment numbers, grade points and semester performance, and are accessible directly without login, while the website may also host other UG/PG result links for reference. After viewing the result, students should carefully verify their name, enrollment number, semester, course and grade details, and in case of any discrepancy, they should promptly contact the Central University of Odisha examination branch or their department for necessary corrections.

How to Check Central University of Odisha Results 2026 Online?

Central University of Odisha has made the result download process simple and quick for students. Candidates only need their registration number and date of birth to view results on the official portal. Here is a step-by-step process you can follow:

  1. Visit the Official Website - Go to the official Central University of Odisha exam portal at cuo.ac.in.
  2. Find the Results Section - Click on the Examination or Results tab on the homepage.
  3. Select Your Exam - Choose MBA 3rd Semester or M.Sc 3rd Semester from the list of available results.
  4. Enter Your Details - Input your Registration Number and Date of Birth in the required fields.
  5. Submit & View - Click the Submit button to view your Central University of Odisha result 2026 on screen.
  6. Download the Mark Sheet - Click Download or Print to save your result PDF for official use.
  7. Verify Your Details - Check that your name, roll number, and marks are correctly reflected on the mark sheet.

How to Check Central University of Odisha Exam Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your CUO Exam Result 2026 for B.Tech Dairy Technology, B.Sc (Hons) Agriculture and B.Sc (Hons) Forestry.

  1. Visit the official Central University of Odisha website: cuo.ac.in.
  2. Navigate to the 'Academic' or 'Examination' section and click on 'Results', or directly open the Academic Results page at https://cuo.ac.in/Academic_Results.asp?pgid=4&subid=4.
  3. On the Academic Results page, look for the section listing results for the School of Integrated Agriculture and Natural Resources, including B.Tech in Dairy Technology, B.Sc (Hons) Agriculture and B.Sc (Hons) Forestry.
  4. Identify the correct academic session (2023-24, 2024-25 or 2025-26) and semester (1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th or 5th, Monsoon/Winter) for your programme.
  5. Click on the corresponding PDF link such as 'B.Tech in Dairy Technology 2nd Semester Result (Winter Session) 2024-25' to open the result sheet.
  6. In the PDF, check your name, enrollment number, grade points and result status, then download or print the file for future academic reference and contact the university if any discrepancy is found.
