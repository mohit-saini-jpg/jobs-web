How to Check Utkal University Result 2026 Online

Students will be able to check Utkal University Result 2026 online from the UUEMS portal by following the simple steps mentioned below.

  1. Visit the official Utkal University Examination Management System (UUEMS) result portal at uuems.in from any browser.
  2. On the homepage, look for the latest Utkal University Result 2026 UG or PG semester result link under the results section.
  3. Click on the relevant course and semester result link to open the online result login page.
  4. Enter your registration or roll number and other required details carefully in the given fields.
  5. Submit the details to view your Utkal University semester result 2026 on the screen.
  6. Check your name, roll number, subject wise marks, total marks and result status displayed in the online marksheet.
  7. Download the provisional result and take a printout for future academic reference, counselling or documentation purposes.
  8. In case of any error or discrepancy in the result, contact the university exam section or your respective department for clarification and correction.
