How to Check Utkal University Result 2026 Online

Students will be able to check Utkal University Result 2026 online from the UUEMS portal by following the simple steps mentioned below.

  • Visit the official Utkal University Examination Management System (UUEMS) result portal at uuems.in from any browser.
  • On the homepage, look for the latest Utkal University Result 2026 UG or PG semester result link under the results section.
  • Click on the relevant course and semester result link to open the online result login page.
  • Enter your registration or roll number and other required details carefully in the given fields.
  • Submit the details to view your Utkal University semester result 2026 on the screen.
  • Check your name, roll number, subject wise marks, total marks and result status displayed in the online marksheet.
  • Download the provisional result and take a printout for future academic reference, counselling or documentation purposes.
  • In case of any error or discrepancy in the result, contact the university exam section or your respective department for clarification and correction.
