How to Check Odisha B.Ed Exam Date 2026 at te.samsodisha.gov.in

Candidates can easily verify the official SAMS Odisha B.Ed Exam Date 2026 through the Teacher Education portal. A clear stepwise section helps target “how to check exam date” searches.
  1. Visit the Teacher Education portal of SAMS Odisha at te.samsodisha.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, look for the B.Ed section under Teacher Education admissions.
  3. Click on the link for the Information Brochure for 2-year B.Ed course 2026-27 or equivalent exam notice.
  4. Open the brochure PDF and scroll to the section mentioning the Entrance Examination Date / Schedule.
  5. Note the exam date, session and reporting time carefully for your reference.
