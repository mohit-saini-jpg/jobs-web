Steps to Check GM University Result 2026

Use the steps below to check and download your GM University Result 2026 from the official website:

  • Go to the official GM University website https://www.gmuniversity.ac.in.
  • Click on the Notice/Result section, or directly open https://www.gmuniversity.ac.in/notice.php.
  • On the notice page, locate the relevant result notice such as: MCA-SEM-II-AUG-2025 MBA-SEM-II-AUG-2025 / MBA-FM-SEM-II-AUG-2025 UG-BBA-SEM-IV-AUG-2025 UG-ART/SCIENCE/COMMERCE/IST-SEM-IV-AUG-2025 PG-SEM-II-AUG-2025 BA. BED-SEM-IV-AUG-2025 BED-SEM-IV-AUG-2025
