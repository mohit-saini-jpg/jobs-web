Detailed Information

Gangadhar Meher University (GM University), Sambalpur has released the GM University Exam Result 2026 for Integrated Bachelor of Arts & Bachelor of Education (Economics) Semester 4 and Bachelor of Arts Core (Economics) Semester 4 examinations. These results form part of GM University’s UG 4th semester result updates for the 2025-26 academic session, where Arts and integrated teacher education programmes have been evaluated. GM University Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - gmuniversity.ac.in through the university notice board and the online result publication system result.gmuniversity.ac.in. Students of Integrated BA B.Ed (Economics) and BA Core (Economics) can check and download their 4th semester marksheets by opening the relevant result notice and using their exam roll number or credentials on the result portal. After downloading the provisional marksheet, candidates should verify their name, roll number, subject-wise marks and SGPA/CGPA, and contact the examination section in case of any discrepancy.

How to Download GM University Exam Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download GM University Economics 4th Semester Result 2026:

  • Open the official GM University website and go to the notice/result section at https://www.gmuniversity.ac.in/notice.php.
  • In the notice board, search for result notices titled for Integrated BA B.Ed (Economics) Semester-IV and BA Core (Economics) Semester-IV examinations (August 2025 session).
  • Click on the relevant notice link to open the PDF containing roll number-wise results for the course.
  • Use the search function (Ctrl+F) inside the PDF to find your exam roll number and check your result, marks and pass status.
  • Alternatively, visit the URPS portal at https://result.gmuniversity.ac.in and select the appropriate course, semester and examination session to download your individual grade sheet by entering your roll number or registration number.
  • Verify your name, paper-wise marks, SGPA and overall result; then save or print the marksheet for future academic reference.
  • If you notice any discrepancy (such as wrong marks or personal details), contact the GM University examination cell or your department immediately with supporting documents.
