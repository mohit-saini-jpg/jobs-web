How to Download GM University Exam Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download GM University Economics 4th Semester Result 2026:

  1. Open the official GM University website and go to the notice/result section at https://www.gmuniversity.ac.in/notice.php.
  2. In the notice board, search for result notices titled for Integrated BA B.Ed (Economics) Semester-IV and BA Core (Economics) Semester-IV examinations (August 2025 session).
  3. Click on the relevant notice link to open the PDF containing roll number-wise results for the course.
  4. Use the search function (Ctrl+F) inside the PDF to find your exam roll number and check your result, marks and pass status.
  5. Alternatively, visit the URPS portal at https://result.gmuniversity.ac.in and select the appropriate course, semester and examination session to download your individual grade sheet by entering your roll number or registration number.
  6. Verify your name, paper-wise marks, SGPA and overall result; then save or print the marksheet for future academic reference.
  7. If you notice any discrepancy (such as wrong marks or personal details), contact the GM University examination cell or your department immediately with supporting documents.
