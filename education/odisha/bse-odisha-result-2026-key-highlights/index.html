How to Check Odisha Class 10th Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their Odisha Class 10th Result 2026 from the official website:

  1. Visit the official website of Board of Secondary Education, Odisha at bseodisha.ac.in or orissaresults.nic.in
  2. On the homepage, look for the Annual Examination Result 2026 link or HSC Result 2026 link
  3. Click on the Odisha 10th Result 2026 link to proceed
  4. A new login window will appear on the screen
  5. Enter your Roll Number as mentioned on the admit card
  6. Enter your Date of Birth in the DD/MM/YYYY format
  7. Click on the Submit or Find Result button
  8. Your Odisha Class 10th Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen with complete details
  9. Check all details including student name, roll number, father's name, mother's name, school name, and subject-wise marks carefully
  10. Verify the theory marks, practical marks (if applicable), total marks obtained in each subject, overall grade, division, and qualifying status
  11. Download the marksheet in PDF format and save it on your device
  12. Take a printout of the result for admission and future reference
