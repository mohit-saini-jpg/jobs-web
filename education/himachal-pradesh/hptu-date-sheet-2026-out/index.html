How to Download HPTU Date Sheet 2026

Students can download the HPTU Date Sheet 2026 from the official university website by following the steps given below:

  • Visit the official website of Himachal Pradesh Technical University.
  • Navigate to the Examination or Date Sheet section.
  • Look for the link titled "Final Date Sheet for June 2026 Examinations".
  • Click on the relevant B.Tech or programme-wise date sheet PDF.
  • The file will open. Download and save it on your device.
  • Take a printout for ready reference during exam preparation.

Note: Always refer to the official HPTU date sheet and check all dates, timings, and instructions carefully before the examinations.

Details Mentioned in HPTU Date Sheet 2026

The official HPTU Date Sheet 2026 contains complete information required by the students. The key details mentioned in the date sheet are:

  • Examination dates and day
  • Session timing (Morning / Evening)
  • Subject code and title
  • Branch-wise / Group-wise schedule
  • Applicable scheme (NEP / CBCS)
  • Important instructions for candidates
