Himachal Pradesh Technical University (HPTU) has released the HPCET 2026 admit card for the Himachal Pradesh Common Entrance Test on its official website. Candidates who registered successfully can now log in to the HPCET portal and download their hall ticket in online mode only; no hard copy will be sent by post.

According to the latest schedule, HPCET 2026 will be conducted as a pen‑and‑paper‑based test in May 2026 (for BTech and other programmes), and the admit card serves as the entry pass to the exam centre. All candidates must carry a printed copy of the hall ticket along with a valid photo ID to be allowed to appear in the exam.

How to Download HPCET 2026 Admit Card

Follow these steps to download your HPCET hall ticket 2026:

  1. Visit the official HPCET 2026 website via HPTU: himtu.ac.in and go to the HPCET‑2026 section, or directly open the SSO portal counselling‑hptu.hp.gov.in.
  2. Click on the “HPCET 2026 Admit Card / Hall Ticket” download link available on the homepage/notifications.
  3. Log in using your User ID and Password created during HPCET 2026 online application.
  4. After successful login, click on the “Download Admit Card / Hall Ticket” button.
  5. Your HPCET 2026 admit card will be displayed with details of exam date, time, exam centre and candidate information.
  6. Verify all details carefully, then download and print 2-3 clear copies of the hall ticket on A4‑size paper.

If you forget your login credentials, use the “Forgot Password” option on the portal or refer to your registration email/SMS to reset your password.

Details Mentioned on HPCET 2026 Admit Card

The HPCET hall ticket contains key details which must match your application form:

  • Candidate’s name
  • Father/guardian’s name
  • Date of birth
  • Photograph and signature
  • Roll number / application details
  • Exam name: HPCET 2026
  • Exam date and exam time
  • Exam centre/college name and full address
  • Programme applied for (e.g., BTech)
  • Important exam‑day instructions for candidates

Any discrepancy in name, photograph, exam centre or programme should be reported immediately to HPTU before the examination day.

Documents Required with HPCET 2026 Admit Card

On the day of the exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the HPCET 2026 admit card (clear A4‑size printout, colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Driving Licence Voter ID Any other government‑issued ID accepted by HPTU.
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Voter ID
  • Any other government‑issued ID accepted by HPTU.

Entry to the exam centre will not be allowed without the printed hall ticket and valid ID proof.

Exam‑Day Instructions for HPCET 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of HPCET 2026, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card to complete verification and seating; latecomers may not be permitted entry.
  • Carry only essential items: HPCET admit card, valid photo ID and permitted stationery (blue/black ball‑point pens, etc.), as specified in the instructions.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not allowed), Bluetooth devices, notes, books or any electronic gadgets in the exam hall; these are strictly prohibited.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the hall ticket and announced by invigilators; any unfair means or misconduct can lead to cancellation of your exam and disqualification from the admission process.
  • Keep your HPCET 2026 admit card safe even after the exam, as it will be required during counselling and document verification.
