How to Apply for HPCET 2026 - Step-by-Step Guide

Applications are accepted only in online mode via the HPTU official website. Follow the steps below carefully to fill the HPCET 2026 application form without errors:

  1. Visit the official HPTU website www.himtu.ac.in and navigate to the HPCET 2026 application page. Click on One Time Registration (OTR) under the "Services" section.
  2. Click "Register →" and then "As Student". New users must click "New User? Sign up for Citizen Login".
  3. You will be redirected to the Him Access Portal. Choose the Aadhaar number option, provide eKYC consent, enter OTP, then provide your mobile number and email to generate a unique Him Access ID.
  4. Login "As Student" using your Him Access ID / registered mobile number or other available option.
  5. Update your profile in four tabs: Applicant Details, Contact Details, Qualifications (10th, 12th, graduation if applicable), and Upload Photo & Signature (JPG/JPEG/PNG, 60-100 KB).
  6. Click "Complete Profile" → Logout → Login again → navigate to "Courses List".
  7. Under UG/PG list, click "Apply" against your desired course or combined course option (e.g., B.Tech & B.Pharmacy both, MBA & MCA, etc.).
  8. Fill the HPCET 2026 Application Form: select quota (AIQ / HPSQ / NRI / KM), category, minimum qualification, sub-category etc. Ensure all entries match your profile/qualification details.
  9. Tick the declaration checkbox, then click "Save Draft". Check under "My Applications", use "Edit Application" if corrections are needed, then click "Submit Application". After submission, no further edits are allowed.
  10. Note down the Application Number (format: HPCET26XXXXXX) displayed on screen. Go to "My Applications" → click "₹ Pay XXXX" → Proceed to Payment and complete the fee payment via the payment gateway.
  11. After successful payment, download and print the application form PDF. If the PDF icon does not appear, click the payment button again to refresh payment status. If not generated within 2 working days, email: [email protected] with your Form Number, transaction ID, and payment screenshot.

HPCET 2026 - Result & Counselling

The HPCET 2026 Result is tentatively expected by 21 May 2026 on the official HPTU website. After result declaration, candidates will need to apply separately for centralized counselling using a new online form (Prospectus Part-II: Admission Brochure 2026-27) which will be released in May 2026.

Admission to all courses is made on the basis of merit (rank/marks) in HPCET 2026 or applicable National Level Entrance Tests, subject to fulfillment of minimum educational qualification. Centralized counselling is tentatively scheduled for June 2026 and classes are expected to start from the last week of July or first week of August 2026.

HPCET 2026 - Exam Centres

Exam centres for HPCET 2026 vary by course. The University may add, change, or cancel centres depending on the number of applicants. Candidates should select their preferred centre carefully during form submission; centre change requests (if approved) attract a fee of ₹2000.
