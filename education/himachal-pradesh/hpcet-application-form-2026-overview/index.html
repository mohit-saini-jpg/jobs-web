Sr.TypeTextHeadersRows
1paragraphThe minimum eligibility criteria for HPCET 2026 are as per AICTE/PCI/UGC norms. Candidates appearing in their final 10+2 or graduation examination in March/April 2026 are also eligible to apply, subject to fulfilling eligibility at the time of admission. Check course-wise eligibility below:

  • Course
  • Minimum Eligibility

Min. 45% marks (40% for reserved). OR Passed D.Voc. Stream in same/allied sector.
    • B.Pharmacy Allopathy (Direct Entry)
    • 10+2 with Physics & Chemistry (compulsory) + one of Mathematics/Biology. Min. 45% marks (40% reserved). Age: 17 years on or before 31 December of admission year.

    • 10+2 in any stream from a recognized Board. Min. 45% marks (40% reserved) in qualifying exam.
    • MBA & MBA (T&HM)
    • Bachelor's degree of minimum 3 years duration. Min. 50% marks (45% reserved).
    • MCA
    • Any graduation degree (B.E./B.Tech/B.Sc/B.Com/B.A/B.Voc/BCA etc.), preferably with Mathematics at 10+2 or graduation level. Min. 50% marks (45% reserved). Bridge course for non-Maths candidates.
    • M.Sc. Physics
    • B.Sc. with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics OR Honors in Physics. Min. 50% marks in aggregate over 3 years (45% reserved).
    • M.Sc. Environmental Sciences
    • Any Bachelor/UG degree in basic/applied sciences including Engineering/Medical Sciences/Pharmacy/Architecture. Min. 50% marks (45% reserved).

6How to Apply for HPCET 2026 - Step-by-Step Guide
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphApplications are accepted only in online mode via the HPTU official website. Follow the steps below carefully to fill the HPCET 2026 application form without errors:
2listordered
  • Visit the official HPTU website www.himtu.ac.in and navigate to the HPCET 2026 application page. Click on One Time Registration (OTR) under the "Services" section.
  • Click "Register →" and then "As Student". New users must click "New User? Sign up for Citizen Login".
  • You will be redirected to the Him Access Portal. Choose the Aadhaar number option, provide eKYC consent, enter OTP, then provide your mobile number and email to generate a unique Him Access ID.
  • Login "As Student" using your Him Access ID / registered mobile number or other available option.
  • Update your profile in four tabs: Applicant Details, Contact Details, Qualifications (10th, 12th, graduation if applicable), and Upload Photo & Signature (JPG/JPEG/PNG, 60-100 KB).
  • Click "Complete Profile" → Logout → Login again → navigate to "Courses List".
  • Under UG/PG list, click "Apply" against your desired course or combined course option (e.g., B.Tech & B.Pharmacy both, MBA & MCA, etc.).
  • Fill the HPCET 2026 Application Form: select quota (AIQ / HPSQ / NRI / KM), category, minimum qualification, sub-category etc. Ensure all entries match your profile/qualification details.
  • Tick the declaration checkbox, then click "Save Draft". Check under "My Applications", use "Edit Application" if corrections are needed, then click "Submit Application". After submission, no further edits are allowed.
  • Note down the Application Number (format: HPCET26XXXXXX) displayed on screen. Go to "My Applications" → click "₹ Pay XXXX" → Proceed to Payment and complete the fee payment via the payment gateway.
  • After successful payment, download and print the application form PDF. If the PDF icon does not appear, click the payment button again to refresh payment status. If not generated within 2 working days, email: [email protected] with your Form Number, transaction ID, and payment screenshot.

9HPCET 2026 - Result & Counselling
