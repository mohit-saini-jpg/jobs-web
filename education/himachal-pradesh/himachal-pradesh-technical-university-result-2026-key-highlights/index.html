How To Check HPCET Result 2026 Online

Follow the step by step process given below to check and download your HPCET Result 2026 from the official HPTU website:

  • Visit the official website of Himachal Pradesh Technical University at https://www.himtu.ac.in/.
  • On the homepage, go to the examination or result section and click on the HPCET Result 2026 link.
  • You will be redirected to the HPCET result login page.
  • Enter your application number and date of birth in the respective fields.
  • Click on the submit or login button to view your HPCET 2026 scorecard and rank.
  • Check your name, roll number, subject wise marks, total marks, and rank displayed on the screen.
  • Download the result or take a printout for future counselling and admission reference.
