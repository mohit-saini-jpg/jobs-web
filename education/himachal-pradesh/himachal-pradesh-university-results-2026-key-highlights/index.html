Detailed Information

Himachal Pradesh University has declared the HPU Result 2026 for key PG CBCS programmes, including MSc Biotechnology 1st and 3rd Semester and MA English 2nd and 4th Semester examinations held in November-December 2025. HPU Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - hpuniv.ac.in.

Students enrolled in these CBCS postgraduate courses can now check their semester wise marks and pass status through the examination result section using their roll number or other required details. After downloading the online marksheet, candidates should verify their personal details, subject wise marks, credits and result status and contact the HPU examination branch or their department in case of any discrepancy.

How to Check HPU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your HPU MSc Biotechnology / MA English CBCS Result 2026:

  • Visit the HPU examination result/news page: https://www.hpuniv.ac.in/university-detail/view_all_news.php?examination.
  • Scroll through the list and locate the notification titles for: MSc Biotechnology (CBCS) 1st Semester Examination Nov–Dec 2025 Result MSc Biotechnology (CBCS) 3rd Semester Examination Nov–Dec 2025 Result MA English (CBCS) 2nd Semester Examination Nov–Dec 2025 Result MA English (CBCS) 4th Semester Examination Nov–Dec 2025 Result
  • MSc Biotechnology (CBCS) 1st Semester Examination Nov–Dec 2025 Result
  • MSc Biotechnology (CBCS) 3rd Semester Examination Nov–Dec 2025 Result
  • MA English (CBCS) 2nd Semester Examination Nov–Dec 2025 Result
  • MA English (CBCS) 4th Semester Examination Nov–Dec 2025 Result
  • Click on the relevant View Result / PDF link for your course and semester.
  • If a PDF opens, press Ctrl+F and type your Roll Number or Name to find your entry; if a marks entry page opens, enter your Roll Number / Registration details as required.
  • Check your subject wise marks/grades, total credits, SGPA/CGPA and overall result status.
  • Download and save the PDF or marksheet and, if needed, print a copy for your academic records, revaluation applications or higher studies documentation.
