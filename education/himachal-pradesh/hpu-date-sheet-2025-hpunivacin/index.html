How to Download the HPU Date Sheet 2025?

  • Visit the official HPU website: https://hpuniv.ac.in.​
  • On the homepage, click on the “Examination” tab and select "Date Sheet" or "Examination Schedules".​
  • Browse the list of recently released and revised date sheets by course and semester/year.
  • Click on the specific date sheet link for your program (e.g., BHM, M.A, M.Sc, MCA, M.Tech, MBA., etc.).​
  • The date sheet will open as a PDF or document; download and save/print the schedule for future reference.
