Detailed Information

Central University of Himachal Pradesh has officially released the CUHP Result 2026 for various PG programmes for the academic session 2025-26, including 1st and 3rd semester MFA, MCA, MSc and MA examinations held in December 2025. Reappear examination results have been declared for MFA (Sculpture) I Sem Batch-2024, MSc (Bioinformatics) I Sem Batch-2023, MCA I Sem Batch-2024 and MCA III Sem Batch-2023, along with reappear results for MA (New Media) I Sem Batch-2024 and MA (Hindu Studies) I Sem Batch-2024. Regular examination results are available for MSc (Mathematics) III Sem Batch-2024, MSc (Environmental Sciences) I Sem Batch-2025, MFA (Sculpture) III Sem Batch-2024, MFA (Painting) III Sem Batch-2024, MA (History) I Sem Batch-2025, MA (Hindu Studies) I Sem Batch-2025, MA (Hindu Studies) III Sem Batch-2024 and MA (Hindi) III Sem Batch-2024, all dated 30 March 2026.

CUHP Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - cuhimachal.ac.in - through the PG results section available in the student corner. Students can check and download their CUHP PG results by opening the PG result page, selecting the relevant course and semester link and entering their roll number or other login credentials if required. After viewing the online marksheet, candidates are advised to verify their personal details, course name, batch, semester, subject wise marks and overall result status and immediately contact the university examination branch in case of any discrepancy.

How to Check CUHP Result 2026

Students can follow the steps below to check and download their CUHP PG Result 2026 for December 2025 examinations:

  • Visit the official CUHP PG results page: https://www.cuhimachal.ac.in/index.php/Student_corner/pg_result.
  • On the PG results list, look for the appropriate link mentioning your course, batch, semester and exam session such as: Reappear Examination Result of MFA (Sculpture) I Sem Batch-2024. Reappear Examination Result of MSc (Bioinformatics) I Sem Batch-2023. Reappear Examination Result of MCA I Sem Batch-2024 or MCA III Sem Batch-2023. Reappear Examination Result of MA (New Media) I Sem Batch-2024 or MA (Hindu Studies) I Sem Batch-2024. Regular Examination Result of MSc (Mathematics) III Sem Batch-2024, MSc (Environmental Sciences) I Sem Batch-2025, MFA (Sculpture/Painting) III Sem Batch-2024, MA (History) I Sem Batch-2025, MA (Hindu Studies) I/III Sem Batches-2025/2024 and MA (Hindi) III Sem Batch-2024.
  • Reappear Examination Result of MFA (Sculpture) I Sem Batch-2024.
  • Reappear Examination Result of MSc (Bioinformatics) I Sem Batch-2023.
  • Reappear Examination Result of MCA I Sem Batch-2024 or MCA III Sem Batch-2023.
  • Reappear Examination Result of MA (New Media) I Sem Batch-2024 or MA (Hindu Studies) I Sem Batch-2024.

  • Click on the relevant result link to open the PDF or result page.
  • If the result is published in PDF format, search for your roll number, name and batch in the list to view your marks and result status.

  • After the result is displayed, carefully check your personal details, course title, semester, subject wise marks, total marks, SGPA/CGPA and pass/fail status and then download or print the result for future academic use and record keeping.
