HPCET 2026 Registration Open – Apply Now for B.Tech, B.Pharmacy, MBA & More

HPCET 2026 registration began on March 13 and closes on April 19, 2026 – apply online at https://counselling-hptu.hp.gov.in/ for admissions to Himachal Pradesh Technical University (HPTU) programs.

The Himachal Pradesh Common Entrance Test (HPCET) provides entry to professional UG and PG courses at HPTU-affiliated institutes. Conducted offline on May 9-10, it focuses on subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Math for tech programs.

Courses Offered

HPCET 2026 covers these programs:

  • B.Tech
  • B.Pharmacy
  • MBA
  • MCA
  • MBA (T&HM)
  • B.Sc. (HM & CT)/BHMCT
  • M.Sc. Physics
  • M.Sc. Environmental Sciences

Separate papers apply for PG courses like MBA/MCA.

Important Dates

  • Registration Start: March 13, 2026
  • Registration End: April 19, 2026
  • Exam Dates: May 9-10, 2026
  • Counselling: July 2026 (tentative)

Apply early to avoid issues; no extensions expected.

Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility varies by course – generally 10+2 with 45% for B.Tech/B.Pharmacy (40% for reserved). Check full details in the prospectus PDF, as specifics like age or subject requirements apply (e.g., PCM for B.Tech). HPTU verifies at counselling.

How to Apply for HPCET 2026 – Step-by-Step Guide

  • Visit Click Here.
  • Click "New Registration" and enter personal/contact details to get a form number.
  • Log in, fill academic/program preferences, and upload documents (photo, signature).
  • Pay the fee online (amount per prospectus).
  • Submit and print the form.

Use a stable connection; keep form number safe for future logins.

How to Download HPCET 2026 Prospectus PDF

  • Go to Click Here.
  • Navigate to "Notifications" or "Admissions" section.
  • Download "Prospectus Part-I Information Brochure 2026" (dated March 13, 2026).

This PDF has fees, syllabus, and rules – read before applying.​
