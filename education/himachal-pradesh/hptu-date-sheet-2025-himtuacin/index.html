How to Download the HPTU Date Sheet 2025?

HPTU (Himachal Pradesh Technical University) Date Sheet 2025, visit the HPTU official website, go to the "Examination" or "Notice Board" section, and select the relevant date sheet link to access and download the PDF for your course and semester.​

  • Go to the official HPTU website: https://www.himtu.ac.in.​
  • Click on “Examination” or go directly to the “Examination Schedule” section: https://www.himtu.ac.in/en/examination.​
  • Browse the latest notices for “Tentative/Final Date Sheet” links for your course and session (such as BTech, MBA, BSc HMCT, MPharmacy, etc.).​
  • Click the date sheet link (often a PDF) to open, view, or download it to your device.​
  • Save or print the PDF for exam reference.
