Details

Himachal Pradesh Technical University (HPTU) has officially released the HPCET 2026 Final Answer Key on May 27, 2026, for various undergraduate and postgraduate entrance examinations conducted under the Himachal Pradesh Common Entrance Test (HPCET). Candidates who appeared for courses such as B.Tech, B.Pharmacy, MBA, MCA, M.Sc, BHMCT, and B.Sc HMCT can now download the course-wise final answer key PDFs from the official website, himtu.ac.in. The final answer key includes revised and verified answers after reviewing objections raised against the provisional key and will be used for preparing the HPCET 2026 results and merit list.

How to Download HPCET 2026 Final Answer Key

Candidates can follow these simple steps to download the answer key:

  1. Visit the official website - himtu.ac.in
  2. Navigate to the “Latest News” or “Notifications” section
  3. Click on the link titled “HPCET 2026 Final Answer Key”
  4. Select your respective course
  5. The answer key PDF will open on the screen
  6. Download and save it for reference
Details Available in HPCET Final Answer Key

The final answer key contains important information required for score estimation:

  • Question paper set or code
  • Correct answers for all questions
  • Course name and exam details
  • Revised answers after objection review
HPCET 2026 Marking Scheme and Score Calculation

Candidates can use the final answer key to estimate their probable scores.

  • Add marks for each correct answer
  • Deduct marks for incorrect answers (if negative marking applies)
  • Calculate total score

Example: If each correct answer carries 4 marks and 1 mark is deducted for wrong answers: Correct Answers: 45 → 180 marks Wrong Answers: 8 → -8 marks Estimated Score: 172 marks
