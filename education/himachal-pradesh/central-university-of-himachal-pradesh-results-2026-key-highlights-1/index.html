How to Check CUHP Exam Result 2026

Use the steps below to check CUHP Exam Result 2026 for your PG course and semester.

  • Visit the Central University of Himachal Pradesh official PG results page at https://www.cuhimachal.ac.in/index.php/Student_corner/pg_result.
  • On the PG results page, browse the list of results and locate the entry matching your programme, batch and semester, such as: "Regular Examination Result of M.Sc (Chemistry), Batch-2024, III SEM, 2025-26, Dec 2025" "Regular Examination Result of M.Sc (Bioinformatics), Batch-2025, I SEM, 2025-26, Dec 2025" "Reappear Examination Result MSW, 2024 Batch, I SEM" "Reappear Examination Result MBA, 2024 Batch, I SEM" etc.
  • "Regular Examination Result of M.Sc (Chemistry), Batch-2024, III SEM, 2025-26, Dec 2025"
  • "Regular Examination Result of M.Sc (Bioinformatics), Batch-2025, I SEM, 2025-26, Dec 2025"
  • "Reappear Examination Result MSW, 2024 Batch, I SEM"
  • "Reappear Examination Result MBA, 2024 Batch, I SEM" etc.
  • Click on the "View" link corresponding to your result; a PDF file will open in a new tab or will be downloaded to your device.
  • Open the PDF and use the search function (Ctrl+F or Command+F) to search for your roll number.
  • Check your name, roll number, course, batch, semester, subject-wise marks/credits and overall result status (Pass/Fail/Reappear/Promoted, as mentioned).
  • Save the PDF or take a printout of the result for future reference, counselling or scholarship purposes.
  • If you notice any discrepancy in your marks or personal details, or if you wish to apply for revaluation/retotaling, contact your department or the CUHP examination section and follow the official instructions published on cuhimachal.ac.in.
