2What Is HP PAT 2026?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphHP PAT, or the Himachal Pradesh Polytechnic Admission Test, is a state-level entrance examination conducted annually by the Himachal Pradesh Technical Education Board (HPTEB). The exam serves as the gateway for admission into Diploma Engineering programmes at Government and Private Polytechnic colleges across Himachal Pradesh.
2paragraphThe HP PAT 2026 exam was held on May 17, 2026, in a single shift from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM, in offline (pen-and-paper) mode, lasting 3 hours. The marking scheme awards 4 marks for every correct answer and deducts 1 mark for every incorrect response. The total marks for the exam are 600.

5HP PAT 2026 Merit List & Tie-Breaking Criteria
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThe HP PAT 2026 merit list is published alongside the result on hptechboard.com. It ranks all qualified candidates based on their total marks obtained in the entrance examination. Understanding how ties are resolved helps candidates know exactly where they stand.
2paragraphIn cases where two or more candidates score identical marks, the board applies a tie-breaking formula in the following order of priority:
3listordered
  • Marks in Chemistry - The candidate with higher Chemistry marks ranks higher.
  • Marks in Physics - If Chemistry marks are also equal, Physics scores are compared.
  • Marks in Biology/Mathematics - The next tiebreaker if Physics scores are also tied.
  • Marks in English - Applied if the above three subjects also result in a tie.
  • Age - In case of a further tie, the older candidate is given the higher rank.

7What's Next After HP PAT 2026 Result? - Counselling Process
Sr.TypeTextStyleItemsHeadersRows
1paragraphAfter checking their result, qualified candidates must register for the HP PAT 2026 counselling process. The counselling determines final admission to polytechnic institutes in Himachal Pradesh, and seat allotment is based purely on merit rank and course/institute preferences.
2paragraphThe HP PAT 2026 counselling process includes the following stages:
3listordered
  • Online Registration for Counselling - Candidates must register on the official portal.
  • Updating Class 10 Marks - Candidates are required to update their Himachal Pradesh Board Class 10 marks.
  • Choice Filling - Select preferred institutes and diploma courses.
  • Document Verification - Verify academic certificates, category certificates, and other required documents.
  • Seat Allotment - Allotment is published round-wise based on rank, category, and choices.
  • Reporting to Institute - Candidates who accept their allotted seat must report to the respective polytechnic within the stipulated deadline.
