Steps to Check HPSOS Result 2026

  • Visit the official website https://hpbose.org/.

  • Select the relevant result link for Class 8, 10, or 12.
  • Enter your roll number or required login credentials.
  • Click on the "Search" or "Submit" button.
  • Your HPSOS Result 2026 will appear on the screen.
