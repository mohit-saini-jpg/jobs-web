Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education (HPBOSE) has officially released the HPBOSE 10th Result 2026 for the March annual matric examination. The HP Board Class 10 result has been made available online for all matric students who appeared for the exams conducted in March 2026. HPBOSE 10th Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - hpbose.org.

Students can check and download their HPBOSE Class 10 results by entering their roll number as mentioned on the admit card on the result login page. After downloading the provisional marksheet, students are advised to carefully verify their name, roll number, subjects, marks obtained, total marks, and qualifying status. In case of any discrepancy in the HP Board 10th marksheet, students should immediately contact their school authorities or HPBOSE for correction through the official channels.

How To Check HPBOSE 10th Result 2026 Online

Follow the steps given below to check HPBOSE 10th Result 2026 on the official website in an easy and error-free manner.

  1. Visit the official website of Himachal Pradesh Board Of School Education at hpbose.org.
  2. On the homepage, click on the 'Results' section to open the result portal.
  3. Select the link that reads 'Matric (Class 10) Examination Result 2026' or similar.
  4. Enter your HPBOSE 10th roll number as mentioned on your admit card in the given field.
  5. Click on the 'Search' or 'Submit' button to view your HP Board 10th result 2026 on the screen.
  6. Check your subject wise marks, total marks and overall result status carefully.
  7. Download and take a printout of the provisional HPBOSE 10th marksheet for future reference.

Students facing issues on the website can try again after some time or opt for alternative modes such as SMS result services if provided by the board.
