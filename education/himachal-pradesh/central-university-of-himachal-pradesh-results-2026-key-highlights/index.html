1paragraphCentral University of Himachal Pradesh has released the CUHP Result 2026 for various postgraduate programmes for the 2024 and 2025 batches. The latest results include MSc (Bioinformatics), MCA, MBA, MBA Travel & Tourism (MBA TT), MA Punjabi, MA Journalism & Mass Communication (JMC), MA English 3rd Semester (Batch 2024) and MA Education 1st Semester (Batch 2025) regular examinations. CUHP Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - cuhimachal.ac.in.
2paragraphStudents can check and download their PG semester results by visiting the CUHP PG result portal, selecting their course and semester and logging in with their roll number or other required details. After viewing the provisional marksheet, they should carefully verify their personal details, subject-wise marks and result status and contact the university or their department in case of any discrepancy.

4How to Check CUHP Result 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphFollow the steps below to check and download your CUHP Result 2026 for PG exams:
2listordered
  • Visit the CUHP PG result page: https://www.cuhimachal.ac.in/index.php/Student_corner/pg_result.
  • On the result listing, find your programme and semester, such as: MSc (Bioinformatics) Batch 2024 – III Sem MCA Batch 2024 – III Sem MBA / MBA TT Batch 2024 – III Sem MA Punjabi / MA JMC / MA English Batch 2024 – III Sem MA Education Batch 2025 – I Sem
  • MSc (Bioinformatics) Batch 2024 – III Sem
  • MCA Batch 2024 – III Sem
  • MBA / MBA TT Batch 2024 – III Sem
  • MA Punjabi / MA JMC / MA English Batch 2024 – III Sem
  • MA Education Batch 2025 – I Sem
  • Click on the View Result or corresponding link/button next to your course.
  • If results are provided in PDF format, open the file and use Ctrl+F to search your roll number or name.
  • If a login form appears, select your course and semester, then enter your roll number/registration number and any captcha/security code, and submit.
  • Check your subject-wise marks or grades, total marks and overall result status (pass/fail/back, if shown).
  • Download or print the provisional marksheet for your academic records and future use.
