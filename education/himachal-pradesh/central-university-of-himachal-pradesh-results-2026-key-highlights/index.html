How to Check CUHP Result 2026

Students can check CUHP Result 2026 for PG semester examinations directly from the official CUHP PG result portal. Since the results are published in PDF format, candidates can simply open the relevant link and search for their roll number or name.

Follow these steps to check CUHP Result 2026:

  • Visit the official website of the Central University of Himachal Pradesh at cuhimachal.ac.in.
  • Go to the Student Corner section and open the PG Result page, or directly visit https://www.cuhimachal.ac.in/index.php/Student_corner/pg_result.
  • On the result page, look for the latest results dated 06 April 2026 for your course and semester.
  • Identify the correct result link, such as MA Sanskrit I Sem, M.Sc Plant Sciences I Sem, MSW III Sem, M.Sc Environmental Sciences III Sem or other listed result PDFs.
  • Click on the "View" or relevant PDF link to open the result file.
  • Once the PDF opens, use the search option (Ctrl+F on desktop or search in PDF viewer on mobile) and enter your roll number or name.
  • Check your marks, course details, semester, result status and any remarks shown in the PDF.
  • Download or print the result PDF for future reference and record-keeping.
  • If there is any error in marks or personal details, contact the CUHP examination branch or your department immediately.
