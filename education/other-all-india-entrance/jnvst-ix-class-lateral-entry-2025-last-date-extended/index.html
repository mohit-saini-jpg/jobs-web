Name of the Post: JNVST IX Class (Lateral Entry) 2025 Online Form

Post Date: 03-10-2024

Latest Update: 21-11-2024

Brief Information: Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti has given an employment notification for Conducting the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test (JNVST 2025) for IX Class Lateral Entry Admission 2025. Those Candidates who are interested in the admission details & completed all eligibility criteria can read the Notification & Apply Online.

Important Dates
Last Date to Apply Online: 26-11-2024 Date of Online Correction Window: 27-11-2024 & 28-11-2024 Date of Exam: Saturday, 08th February 2025
Age Limit
Candidates Born between 1st May 2010 to 31st July 2012 (both days inclusive)
Qualification
Candidates Should Possess 8th Class.
