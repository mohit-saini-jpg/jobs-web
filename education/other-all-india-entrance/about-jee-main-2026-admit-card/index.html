About JEE Main 2026 Admit Card

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has officially released the JEE Main Admit Card 2026 for Session 1 on January 17, 2026. The Joint Entrance Examination (Main) is India's premier engineering entrance examination, serving as the gateway to prestigious institutions including NITs, IIITs, CFTIs, and other government-funded technical institutes.
The JEE Main 2026 Hall Ticket is an essential document that every candidate must carry to the examination center. Without a valid admit card, candidates will NOT be allowed entry into the exam hall, regardless of having other identification documents.
Important Notice: All candidates must download and carry a clear printed copy of their admit card along with a self-declaration form and valid photo ID to the examination center. Digital copies or mobile displays are NOT accepted.

How to Download JEE Main Admit Card 2026 - Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to download your JEE Main 2026 Session 1 Admit Card:
  1. Visit Official Website: Go to jeemain.nta.nic.in
  2. Locate Download Link: On the homepage, click on the link titled "Admit Card for JEE(Main)-2026 [Session-I]" or similar notification
  3. Access Login Portal: You will be redirected to the candidate login page
  4. Enter Credentials: Application Number: Your unique registration number, Password or Date of Birth: Choose preferred login method, Security Code: Enter the captcha displayed
  5. Submit Details: Click on "Submit" or "Sign In" button
  6. Download Admit Card: Your JEE Main 2026 Admit Card will appear on screen
  7. Verify Details: Carefully check all information including: Name spelling and photograph, Exam center address, Exam date, shift, and paper type, Reporting time
  8. Print Multiple Copies: Download PDF and take at least 2-3 clear printouts

Important Documents Required at JEE Main 2026 Exam Center

Mandatory Documents (MUST Carry):
  1. JEE Main 2026 Admit Card - Original printed copy with clear photograph and signature
  2. Self-Declaration Form - Properly filled and signed with witness signature
  3. Valid Photo ID Proof (Original + Photocopy) - Any ONE of the following: Aadhaar Card (most preferred), PAN Card, Passport, Driving License, School/College ID Card with photograph, Bank Passbook with photograph
  4. PWD Certificate (if applicable) - For candidates who claimed disability benefits
  5. Scribe Form (if applicable) - For candidates using a scribe

Prohibited Items (Strictly NOT Allowed):

  • Mobile phones & smartphones
  • Smartwatches & electronic watches
  • Calculators (scientific/normal)
  • Bluetooth devices & earphones
  • Cameras & recording devices
  • Handbags, purses, wallets
  • Geometry boxes & pencil boxes
  • Log tables & slide rules
  • Books, notes, printed material
  • Water bottles (even sealed)
  • Eatables of any kind
  • Goggles, sunglasses

DO's - Follow These Instructions

  • Report to exam center at least 2 hours early
  • Carry all mandatory documents in a transparent folder
  • Wear clothes as per prescribed dress code
  • Take your allotted seat marked with Roll Number
  • Follow invigilator's instructions carefully
  • Read all questions thoroughly before answering
  • Use rough sheets provided for calculations
  • Write Name & Application Number on rough sheets
  • Return rough sheets before leaving exam hall
  • Maintain silence and discipline throughout
  • Approach superintendent for technical issues
  • Check virtual calculator functionality before exam

DON'Ts - Strictly Avoid These

  • Do NOT carry mobile phones or electronic devices
  • Do NOT wear watches of any type
  • Do NOT bring water bottles or eatables
  • Do NOT wear jewelry or metallic items
  • Do NOT communicate with other candidates
  • Do NOT leave exam hall without permission
  • Do NOT take rough sheets out of exam hall
  • Do NOT argue with invigilators
  • Do NOT create disturbance in exam hall
  • Do NOT attempt unfair means
  • Do NOT panic during exam
  • Do NOT bring calculators or log books

Details Mentioned on NTA Admit Card 2026

  • Name of the candidate
  • Application number / Roll number
  • Date of birth
  • Category (General/OBC/SC/ST)
  • Candidate's photograph
  • Candidate's signature
  • Exam date, shift and paper
  • Exam centre name and address
  • Reporting time and gate closing time

Before Leaving for Exam:

  • Check exam center location on Google Maps
  • Plan to reach 30-45 minutes before reporting time
  • Keep admit card and ID proof in a clear folder
  • Carry extra pens and pencils
  • Get a good night's sleep
  • Eat a light, healthy breakfast

At the Examination Center:

  • Follow all instructions given by the invigilators
  • Maintain silence and discipline
  • No use of mobile phones or electronic devices
  • Do not communicate with other candidates
  • Raise your hand if you need any assistance

Prohibited Items in Exam Hall:

  • Mobile phones and smartphones
  • Smartwatches and electronic watches
  • Calculators (unless specifically allowed)
  • Bluetooth devices and earphones
  • Textbooks, study materials, and notes
  • Bags and pouches
