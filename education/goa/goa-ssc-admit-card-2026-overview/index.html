Details Mentioned on Goa SSC Admit Card 2026

After downloading the Admit Card, students should verify all details carefully. The Admit Card/hall ticket usually includes the following:

  • Candidate's name and roll number
  • Registration number and course details
  • Exam date and reporting time
  • Exam center name and address
  • Photograph and signature
  • Subject/paper details (if applicable)
  • Important instructions for exam day

If any detail appears incorrect, contact the Goa Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board examination office immediately for rectification.

How to Download Goa SSC Admit Card 2026 Online?

Goa Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board has made the Admit Card download process simple and quick for students. Candidates only need their registration number and date of birth to view Admit Cards on the official portal. Here is a step-by-step process you can follow:

  • Visit the official website gssc.goa.gov.in.
  • Click on the Admit Card / Hall Ticket link on the homepage.
  • Enter your Registration Number / Roll Number and Date of Birth.
  • Click Submit and your Admit Card will appear on the screen.
  • Download and print the Admit Card for exam use.

Exam Day Important Instructions

  • Double-check all personal details on the Admit Card immediately after downloading.
  • Carry a government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, Voter ID, Passport) along with the Admit Card.
  • Report to the examination centre at least 30 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • Mobile phones, smartwatches, and electronic devices are strictly prohibited in the exam hall.
  • Keep multiple printouts of the Admit Card to avoid last-minute issues.
