How to Check Goa Board Results 2026 Online?

Follow these steps to access your Goa Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education result easily from the official website:

  • Visit the Official Website - Go to the official Goa Board exam portal at gbshse.in.
  • Find the Results Section - Click on the Examination or Results tab on the homepage.
  • Select Your Exam - Choose HSSC from the list of available results.
  • Enter Your Details - Input your Registration Number and Date of Birth in the required fields.
  • Submit & View - Click the Submit button to view your Goa Board result 2026 on screen.
  • Download the Mark Sheet - Click Download or Print to save your result PDF for official use.
  • Verify Your Details - Check that your name, roll number, and marks are correctly reflected on the mark sheet.

Details Mentioned on Goa Board HSSC Result 2026

Once downloaded, your mark sheet will include key details such as:

  • Candidate's Name
  • Roll Number & Registration ID
  • Course & Semester Information
  • Subject-wise Marks / Grades
  • Total CGPA or Percentage
  • Result Status (Pass / Fail)
  • University Seal or Verification Code

Students should verify all details. In case of discrepancies, immediately report to the Examination Department of Goa Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education.
