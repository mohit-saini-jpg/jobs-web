What is Goa SSC Time Table 2025?

The Goa SSC Time Table 2025 is the official examination schedule released by Goa Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education for the academic year 2024-25. This document contains crucial information including:

  • Exact exam dates for each subject/paper
  • Exam timing and duration
  • Course-wise and semester-wise schedule
  • Regular and supplementary exam dates
  • Important instructions for students

Students appearing for Class 10th examinations must download the Goa Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education time table to prepare their study schedule effectively.

How to Download Goa SSC Time Table 2025? (Step-by-Step)

Follow these simple steps to download your Goa SSC exam date sheet 2025:

Method 1: Direct Download from Official Website
  1. Visit Official Website: Go to gbshse.in
  2. Navigate to Examination Section: Look for "Examinations" or "Students" section on homepage
  3. Click on Time Table/Date Sheet: Find "Examination Schedule" or "Time Table" link
  4. Select Academic Year: Choose 2024-25 or current session
  5. Choose Your Course: Select Class 10th or your specific course
  6. Select Exam Type: Regular/Supplementary/Backlog
  7. Download PDF: Click on the download button to save the time table
  8. Save and Print: Save the PDF and take a printout for reference
