4How to Download BITSAT 2026 Admit Card Online
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphBITSAT 2026 admit card will be issued only through the online admission portal; it will not be sent by post or courier.
2paragraphStep-by-Step BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Download Process
3listordered
  • Visit the official BITS admission website: bitsadmission.com.
  • On the homepage, find and click on the “BITSAT 2026 Admit Card / Hall Ticket” link.
  • You will be redirected to the candidate login page.
  • Enter your BITSAT 2026 application number and password / date of birth, then enter the security code if asked.
  • Click on the “Login / Submit” button.
  • The BITSAT 2026 admit card will be displayed on the screen with your photo and exam details.
  • Check all the details carefully for any errors.
  • Download the admit card and take 2-3 clear printouts on A4-size paper.
4paragraphLogin Details Required for BITSAT 2026 Admit Card
5listunordered
  • BITSAT 2026 application number.
  • Password or date of birth used at registration.
  • Access to the registered email ID / mobile number in case you need to recover login details.

6BITSAT 2026 Exam Date, Slot and Centre Allotment
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphBITSAT 2026 Exam Schedule and Slot
2paragraphBITSAT is usually conducted over multiple days and in several time slots. The specific date and slot allotted to each candidate will be mentioned clearly on the BITSAT 2026 admit card. Candidates must carefully note the slot printed on the admit card and appear for the test only at that date and time.
3paragraphBITSAT 2026 Test Centre Allotment
4paragraphTest centres are allotted based on the preferences filled in the BITSAT application form and the availability of seats at each centre. Once a test centre is allotted and mentioned on the admit card, change of centre is normally not allowed, so candidates must plan travel and reporting time accordingly.
7Documents to Carry with BITSAT 2026 Admit Card
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphBringing the correct documents to the test centre is essential to avoid rejection at the entry gate.
2paragraphMandatory Documents on Exam Day
3listunordered
  • Printed BITSAT 2026 admit card (preferably in colour, though black-and-white is usually accepted unless otherwise specified).

8Important Instructions on BITSAT 2026 Admit Card
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThe BITSAT admit card will contain essential instructions that candidates must follow strictly.
2paragraphKey instructions typically include:
3listunordered
  • Reach the exam centre at least 60 minutes before the reporting time to complete security checks and registration.
  • Carry the admit card and photo ID in original; photocopies of ID may not be accepted.
  • Do not carry prohibited items to the exam lab; there may not be any facility for safe-keeping of personal belongings.
  • Follow the invigilator’s guidelines for logging in to the computer, navigating between questions and submitting the test.
  • Any kind of malpractice or misconduct can lead to cancellation of candidature and further disciplinary action.
