How to Download BITSAT 2026 Admit Card Online

BITSAT 2026 admit card will be issued only through the online admission portal; it will not be sent by post or courier.
Step-by-Step BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Download Process
  1. Visit the official BITS admission website: bitsadmission.com.
  2. On the homepage, find and click on the “BITSAT 2026 Admit Card / Hall Ticket” link.
  3. You will be redirected to the candidate login page.
  4. Enter your BITSAT 2026 application number and password / date of birth, then enter the security code if asked.
  5. Click on the “Login / Submit” button.
  6. The BITSAT 2026 admit card will be displayed on the screen with your photo and exam details.
  7. Check all the details carefully for any errors.
  8. Download the admit card and take 2-3 clear printouts on A4-size paper.
Login Details Required for BITSAT 2026 Admit Card
  • BITSAT 2026 application number.
  • Password or date of birth used at registration.
  • Access to the registered email ID / mobile number in case you need to recover login details.

Details Printed on BITSAT 2026 Admit Card

Candidates must verify all the information printed on the BITSAT admit card and ensure it matches their application details.
Typical details include:
  • Candidate’s name, photograph and signature (if printed).
  • BITSAT 2026 application number / roll number.
  • Exam date and time slot allotted.
  • Test centre name, complete address and test city.
  • Reporting time and gate closing time.
  • Instructions regarding acceptable ID proofs and permitted items.
  • General exam-day rules and code of conduct.
If any detail is incorrect or unclear, candidates should contact the BITS admission office immediately.

BITSAT 2026 Exam Date, Slot and Centre Allotment

BITSAT 2026 Exam Schedule and Slot
BITSAT is usually conducted over multiple days and in several time slots. The specific date and slot allotted to each candidate will be mentioned clearly on the BITSAT 2026 admit card. Candidates must carefully note the slot printed on the admit card and appear for the test only at that date and time.
BITSAT 2026 Test Centre Allotment
Test centres are allotted based on the preferences filled in the BITSAT application form and the availability of seats at each centre. Once a test centre is allotted and mentioned on the admit card, change of centre is normally not allowed, so candidates must plan travel and reporting time accordingly.

Documents to Carry with BITSAT 2026 Admit Card

Bringing the correct documents to the test centre is essential to avoid rejection at the entry gate.
Mandatory Documents on Exam Day
  • Printed BITSAT 2026 admit card (preferably in colour, though black-and-white is usually accepted unless otherwise specified).
  • A valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence, or School/College ID as per the latest instructions.
  • Recent passport-size photographs if mentioned in the exam instructions.
Items Not Allowed Inside BITSAT Exam Centre
  • Mobile phones, smart watches, Bluetooth devices, earphones or any form of electronic gadget.
  • Calculators, digital diaries, programmable watches or any calculation device (unless specifically allowed).
  • Books, notes, written slips and any other study material.

Important Instructions on BITSAT 2026 Admit Card

The BITSAT admit card will contain essential instructions that candidates must follow strictly.
Key instructions typically include:
  • Reach the exam centre at least 60 minutes before the reporting time to complete security checks and registration.
  • Carry the admit card and photo ID in original; photocopies of ID may not be accepted.
  • Do not carry prohibited items to the exam lab; there may not be any facility for safe-keeping of personal belongings.
  • Follow the invigilator’s guidelines for logging in to the computer, navigating between questions and submitting the test.
  • Any kind of malpractice or misconduct can lead to cancellation of candidature and further disciplinary action.

What to Do If There Is an Error or Download Issue

Incorrect Details / Photo on BITSAT 2026 Admit Card
If you notice mistakes such as wrong name spelling, incorrect date, wrong centre or unclear photograph, you should:
  • Immediately contact the BITS admission office using the helpline number or email given in the information brochure.
  • Provide your application number, registered email ID and a detailed description of the error.
  • Follow the instructions given by the authorities and complete any required correction process before the exam day.
Unable to Download BITSAT Admit Card 2026
If the website is not opening or the admit card is not downloading:
  • Check your internet connection and browser; try clearing cache or using a different browser/device.
  • Ensure that you are entering the correct application number and password/date of birth.
  • If the problem persists, reach out to the admission helpline with your registration details and screenshot of the issue, if possible.
