Step by Step Process to Check Gurugram University UG/PG Result 2026

  1. Visit the official Gurugram University website at https://gurugramuniversity.ac.in.
  2. On the homepage, navigate to the “Notifications” or “Result Notice” section, or directly open the result notice page at https://gurugramuniversity.ac.in/allNotifications/resultNotice/index.php.
  3. On the result notice page, scroll through the list of result notifications and locate the notice corresponding to your course and semester (for example, “Bachelor of Arts in Geography Hons”, “Bachelor of Technology (Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning))” etc.).
  4. Click on the relevant result notice link to open the detailed notification.
  5. The notice may contain a direct PDF link listing roll numbers, names and marks, or provide instructions to log in to a separate result portal.
  6. If a PDF link is provided, click on it, allow the PDF to open, and then search your roll number or name within the document to see your result.
  7. If a login result portal is mentioned, follow the link, enter your roll number or other requested credentials and submit to view your semester result.
  8. Download the PDF or take a printout of your result for future reference and for use in academic or placement processes.
