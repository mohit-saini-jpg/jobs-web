6Eligibility Criteria for All Courses
Sr.TypeHeadersRows
1table
  • Programme Name
  • Eligibility Criteria
    • B.Sc. (Hons.) Agriculture 4-Year Programme
    • 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics from a recognized board with at least 50% marks in aggregate (40% for SC/ST/PwD categories).
    • B.Sc. (Hons.) Agribusiness Management
    • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Biology/Mathematics with minimum 50% marks in aggregate. Relaxation applicable for reserved categories as per rules.
    • B.F.Sc. (Bachelor of Fisheries Science) 4-Year Programme
    • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Biology with minimum 50% marks in aggregate for General category (40% for SC/ST/PwD).
    • B.Sc. (Hons.) Agriculture 2+4 Year Programme
    • 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics with minimum required marks as per university norms.
    • B.Sc. (Hons.) Community Science 4-Year Programme
    • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Biology/Mathematics with at least 50% marks (40% for reserved categories).
    • B.Tech. Biotechnology
    • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Biology/Mathematics with minimum 50% aggregate marks.
    • B.Sc. (Hons.) Physical Sciences (PCM) 4-Year Programme
    • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics with minimum 50% marks.
    • B.Sc. (Hons.) Life Sciences (PCB) 4-Year Programme
    • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Biology with minimum 50% marks in aggregate.
    • M.Sc. Programmes (Agriculture & Allied Sciences)
    • Bachelor’s degree in relevant subject with minimum OGPA of 6.0/10.0 or 60% marks. Relaxation for reserved categories as per rules.
    • M.Tech. Programmes
    • B.Tech./B.E. in relevant branch with minimum 60% marks or equivalent OGPA.
    • MBA Programmes
    • Bachelor’s degree in any discipline with minimum 50% marks. Admission based on CAT/CMAT score.
7How to Apply for Hisar HAU UG / PG Admission 2026 (Step-by-Step)
Sr.TypeStyleItems
1listunordered
  • Visit the official admission portal: admissions.hau.ac.in
  • Click on the link for “Online Application Form 2026-27” (UG or PG as applicable).
  • Register yourself by providing a valid Email ID and Mobile Number.
  • Fill the online application form carefully with personal details, educational qualifications, category, and other required information.
  • Upload scanned copies of all necessary documents (Photo, Signature, 10th & 12th Marksheets, Category Certificate etc.).
  • Pay the application fee online through Credit Card, Debit Card, or Net Banking.
  • Submit the application form and take a printout of the confirmation page for future reference.
  • Keep checking the admission portal regularly for admit card, entrance test date, and counselling schedule.
