How to Apply for Hisar HAU UG / PG Admission 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official admission portal: admissions.hau.ac.in
  • Click on the link for “Online Application Form 2026-27” (UG or PG as applicable).
  • Register yourself by providing a valid Email ID and Mobile Number.
  • Fill the online application form carefully with personal details, educational qualifications, category, and other required information.
  • Upload scanned copies of all necessary documents (Photo, Signature, 10th & 12th Marksheets, Category Certificate etc.).
  • Pay the application fee online through Credit Card, Debit Card, or Net Banking.
  • Submit the application form and take a printout of the confirmation page for future reference.
  • Keep checking the admission portal regularly for admit card, entrance test date, and counselling schedule.

Documents Required

  • Photo & Signature
  • 10th & 12th Marksheets
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Income Certificate (for EWS)
  • Disability Certificate (for PwD)
