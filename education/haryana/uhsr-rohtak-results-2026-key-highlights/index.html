How to Download UHSR Rohtak Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download the UHSR Rohtak Result 2026 online:

  1. Visit the official website of UHSR Rohtak - https://uhsr.ac.in/
  2. Click on the "Results" section available on the homepage.
  3. Find and click on the relevant result link for B.Sc Medical Technology O.T. 2nd and 3rd Year.
  4. The result PDF will open on the screen.
  5. Search your roll number or name in the PDF.
  6. Download and save the result for future reference.
