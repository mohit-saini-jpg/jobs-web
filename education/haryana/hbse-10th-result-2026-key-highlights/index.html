Detailed Information

The Board of School Education Haryana (HBSE) has released the Class 10 results for the 2025–26 academic session today. The results cover all subjects for students who appeared in the Secondary (Class 10) board examinations across Haryana and are available online through the official result portal.

How to Check HBSE 10th Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the simple, step-by-step process given below to check the HBSE 10th Result 2026 online on the official website bseh.org.in.

  • Visit the official website of the Haryana Board - bseh.org.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the "Results" or "All Results" section.
  • Select the link that reads "HBSE 10th Result 2026" or "Secondary Examination Result 2026".
  • Enter your HBSE Class 10 roll number and date of birth in the required fields.
  • Click on the "Submit" or "Search Result" button to proceed.
  • Your HBSE 10th Result 2026 marksheet will appear on the screen showing subject-wise marks and overall status.
  • Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future reference and admission processes.

Students should ensure that the details displayed on the online marksheet match the information in their admit card and school records, and report any errors to the board or school authorities without delay.
