Kurukshetra University Result 2026

Kurukshetra University has officially released the Result 2026 for various UG courses including BA B.Ed, B.Sc B.Ed, and BA (Hons) programs for multiple semesters. Students who appeared for the December 2025 examinations can now check their results online.

Kurukshetra University Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - kuk.ac.in. The results are available for 1st, 3rd, 5th, and 7th semester examinations conducted in Dec 2025.

Students can check and download their results by accessing the PDF links available on the official website. It is advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the result. In case of any discrepancies, students should contact the university authorities immediately.
