KUK UG and Integrated Course Admission 2026 application process has started. Kurukshetra University (KUK) has opened online applications for various Under Graduate, Integrated, and Apprenticeship Embedded Degree Programmes (AEDP) in University Teaching Departments/Institutes for the academic session 2026.

Kurukshetra University (KUK), a prestigious A++ NAAC-accredited institution in Haryana, has announced the admission notification for UG and Integrated programmes for 2026. Candidates can apply online for courses like B.A. LL.B. (Hons.), BBA (Hons.), BHM&CT, ITEP, B.Com (Professional) AEDP, BCA (Industry Linked) AEDP, and many more. This comprehensive guide covers all key details including important dates, eligibility, application fee, seat matrix, and step-by-step process to help aspirants secure admission.

How to Apply for KUK UG and Integrated Course Admission 2026
  1. Visit the official admission portal: iums.kuk.ac.in
  2. Register with valid email and mobile number.
  3. Fill the online application form with personal, academic, and reservation details.
  4. Upload required documents (scanned copies).
  5. Pay the application fee online.
  6. Submit and download the confirmation page.
Programmes Offered, Seat Matrix & Fee Details

Refer to Chapter-2 of the official handbook for complete list. Popular programmes include:

  • B.A. LL.B. (Hons.) 5-Year Integrated
  • BBA (Hons.), BHM&CT
  • B.Sc. Graphics & Animation, Multimedia
  • Multiple AEDP courses (industry-linked)
  • ITEP (Integrated Teacher Education Programme)
  • Seats include All India Category (AIC), Haryana General (HGC), EWS, SC, BC, DA, and supernumerary seats (Kashmiri Migrants, Sports, NCC, NSS, International Students, etc.).
Reservation, Merit List & Admission Process
  • Reservations as per Haryana Government norms.
  • Merit prepared on the basis of 10+2 marks (details in Chapter-6 of HBI).
  • Document verification and fee deposition required after merit list.
Documents Required for KUK UG Admission 2026
  • 10th and 12th Mark Sheets & Certificates
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Character Certificate
  • Domicile Certificate (for Haryana benefits)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Income Certificate (for EWS)
  • Any other relevant certificates (Sports, NCC, etc.)
