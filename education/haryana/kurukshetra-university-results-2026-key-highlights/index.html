Detailed Information

Kurukshetra University has officially released the Kurukshetra University Result 2026 for various UG and PG examinations of the December 2025 session. The newly declared results include B.A.LL.B (Hons.) Integrated (New) VII Semester, Bachelor of Computer Applications (CTIS) III Semester, M.Sc. Geography IV Semester and M.Sc. Chemistry I Semester detail results.

After accessing the online result, candidates should carefully check their name, roll number, course, semester, subject-wise marks and overall result status. How to Download Kurukshetra University Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download Kurukshetra University Result 2026 for the December 2025 examinations:

  • Visit the official Kurukshetra University website at kuk.ac.in.
  • Click on the "Results" link or directly open the results page at https://kuk.ac.in/results/.
  • On the results page, look for the latest result notifications dated 08-May-2026 for B.A.LL.B (Hons.) Integrated (New) VII Sem, BCA (CTIS) III Sem, M.Sc Geography IV Sem and M.Sc Chemistry I Sem.
  • Click on the respective result title to open the detailed result PDF in a new tab.
  • In the PDF, search for your roll number or name to check your marks and result status.
  • Download the PDF and take a printout for your records and future reference.
