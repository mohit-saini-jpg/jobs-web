How to Check Result

  • Visit the official Indira Gandhi University website at igu.ac.in.
  • Click on the "Examination" or "Result" section, or directly open the IGU examination portal at igu.ac.in/iguexamination.
  • Look for the result link or notification titled "LL.B. 3rd Sem. (Evening) Dec.-2017".
  • Click on the result PDF link or use the direct PDF link provided for the LLB 3rd Semester Evening result.
  • Once the PDF opens, search for your roll number or name in the list.
  • Check your result status and any other details mentioned against your roll number.
  • Download or print the PDF result for future reference.
  • If you find any discrepancy in your result, contact the IGU examination branch or your college immediately for clarification.
