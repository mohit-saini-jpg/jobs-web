Haryana ITI Admission 2026

The online application form for Haryana ITI Admission 2026 will open soon. The Skill Development & Industrial Training Department, Haryana has announced admissions for the session 2026-27 in 199 Government and 186 Private ITIs across the state.

Haryana ITI Admission 2026 offers admission to various Engineering and Non-Engineering trades in Government and Private Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs). Admission is merit-based on marks obtained in Class 8th, 10th, or 12th. The process is fully online through the official portal.

Eligibility Criteria (Tentative)

Age Limit: Minimum 14 years as on 01 September 2026 (no upper age limit for most trades). Educational Qualification: Passed 8th, 10th, or 12th class from a recognized board (trade-specific requirements apply). Domicile: Preference given to Haryana domicile candidates.

How to Apply for Haryana ITI Admission 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official website admissions.itiharyana.gov.in.
  • Create / Login using Family ID.
  • Fill the online application form.
  • Upload required documents.
  • Pay the application fee (if applicable).
  • Submit and take printout of the confirmation page.

Documents Required for Haryana ITI Admission 2026

Mandatory Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport Size Photo & Signature
  • 8th / 10th / 12th Marksheet & Certificate
  • Domicile Certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste Certificate (SC/BC/EWS)
  • Bank Account Details

Other Documents (if applicable):

  • Income Certificate, Ex-Serviceman Certificate, NCC Certificate, etc.
