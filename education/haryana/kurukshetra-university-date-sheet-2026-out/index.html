How to Download Kurukshetra University Date Sheet 2026

Students can download the Kurukshetra University Date Sheet 2026 directly from the official date sheet portal. The process is simple and quick.

  • Visit the official Kurukshetra University date sheet page using the link https://kuk.ac.in/date-sheet/.
  • Look for the latest notifications dated 05 May 2026.
  • Identify your course and semester from the list of notifications (N-23, N-24, N-25).
  • Click on the relevant notification link to open the PDF.
  • Review the complete exam schedule and important instructions.
  • Download the PDF file and take a printout for easy reference during exam preparation.

Students should check the official timetable carefully and keep a copy handy for effective exam preparation. Regularly visit the portal for any further updates or changes.
