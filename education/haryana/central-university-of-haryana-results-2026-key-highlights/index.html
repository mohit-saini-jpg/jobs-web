Detailed Information

Central University of Haryana (CUH) has officially released the Central University of Haryana Re Evaluation Result 2026 for the Term End Examination (TEE) December 2025 across multiple UG courses and semesters on May 4, 2026. The results have been declared for B.Tech Electrical Engineering 5th Semester, B.Tech Printing & Packaging Technology 1st and 3rd Semester, B.Tech Civil Engineering Re-appear 1st and 3rd Semester, B.Tech Electrical Engineering Re-appear 1st Semester, B.Voc. Biomedical Sciences Re-appear 1st Semester, and B.Voc. Retail & Logistics Management Re-appear 1st Semester. Central University of Haryana Re Evaluation Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - cuh.ac.in through the result portal at cuh.ac.in/Result.aspx and the Samarth login portal at cuh.samarth.edu.in.

Students can check and download their re-evaluation examination results by visiting the official CUH result portal, clicking on the re-evaluation result link, logging into the Samarth portal using their student ID and password, and accessing their subject-wise revised marks and updated grades after answer script re-evaluation. The university has made the re-evaluation results accessible through its online examination management system, allowing candidates to view their revised marksheets instantly after challenging their original evaluation. Students are advised to carefully verify all personal details, subject codes, revised marks, grades, and credits mentioned on their result marksheet and contact the university examination cell or Controller of Examinations in case of any discrepancies, errors, or queries regarding the re-evaluation process.

How to Download Central University of Haryana Re Evaluation Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your Central University of Haryana Re Evaluation Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official Central University of Haryana result portal at cuh.ac.in/Result.aspx
  • On the result page, look for the re-evaluation result announcement for TEE December 2025
  • Click on the re-evaluation result link for your specific course and semester
  • You will be redirected to the Samarth portal at cuh.samarth.edu.in/index.php/site/login
  • Enter your student ID or enrollment number in the username field
  • Enter your password in the password field
  • Click on the "Login" button to access your student account
  • After logging in, navigate to the Examination section or Results section
  • Select "Re-evaluation Result" from the available options
  • Choose "TEE December 2025" from the examination session dropdown
  • Select your course (B.Tech Electrical Engineering, B.Tech Printing & Packaging Technology, B.Tech Civil Engineering, B.Voc. Biomedical Sciences, or B.Voc. Retail & Logistics Management) and semester (1st, 3rd, or 5th Semester)
  • Your Central University of Haryana Re Evaluation Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen showing subject-wise revised marks after re-evaluation, updated grades, and overall result status
  • Check all revised marks, subject codes, personal details, and semester information carefully
  • Download the result PDF or take a screenshot for future reference
  • You can also take a printout of the result for admission, verification, or record-keeping purposes
  • Save the downloaded marksheet in a secure location
  • If you find any discrepancies or have queries, contact the examination cell immediately
