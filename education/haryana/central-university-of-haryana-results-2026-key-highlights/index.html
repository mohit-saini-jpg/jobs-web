How to Download Central University of Haryana Re Evaluation Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your Central University of Haryana Re Evaluation Result 2026 online:

  1. Visit the official Central University of Haryana result portal at cuh.ac.in/Result.aspx
  2. On the result page, look for the re-evaluation result announcement for TEE December 2025
  3. Click on the re-evaluation result link for your specific course and semester
  4. You will be redirected to the Samarth portal at cuh.samarth.edu.in/index.php/site/login
  5. Enter your student ID or enrollment number in the username field
  6. Enter your password in the password field
  7. Click on the "Login" button to access your student account
  8. After logging in, navigate to the Examination section or Results section
  9. Select "Re-evaluation Result" from the available options
  10. Choose "TEE December 2025" from the examination session dropdown
  11. Select your course (B.Tech Electrical Engineering, B.Tech Printing & Packaging Technology, B.Tech Civil Engineering, B.Voc. Biomedical Sciences, or B.Voc. Retail & Logistics Management) and semester (1st, 3rd, or 5th Semester)
  12. Your Central University of Haryana Re Evaluation Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen showing subject-wise revised marks after re-evaluation, updated grades, and overall result status
  13. Check all revised marks, subject codes, personal details, and semester information carefully
  14. Download the result PDF or take a screenshot for future reference
  15. You can also take a printout of the result for admission, verification, or record-keeping purposes
  16. Save the downloaded marksheet in a secure location
  17. If you find any discrepancies or have queries, contact the examination cell immediately

How to Download Central University of Haryana Result 2026

Students can follow the steps below to check Central University of Haryana Result 2026 for MA Hindi and M.Com 1st and 2nd Semester online.

  1. Visit the official CUH website cuh.ac.in and click on the "Results" link, or directly open https://www.cuh.ac.in/Result.aspx.
  2. On the Results page, locate the notification for "Regular Result Term End Examinations - Master of Arts (Hindi) 1st/2nd Semester, Master of Commerce 1st/2nd Semester".
  3. Click on the relevant result link or on the Samarth portal link (cuhcdoe.samarth.edu.in) if provided.
  4. On the result login page, select your Programme (MA Hindi or M.Com) and Semester (1st or 2nd) if asked.
  5. Enter your Enrollment/Registration Number and Date of Birth correctly in the given fields.
  6. Click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button to display your Central University of Haryana Result 2026 on the screen.
  7. Check your name, enrollment number, course, semester and subject-wise grades/marks carefully, and then download or print the provisional marksheet for future reference.
