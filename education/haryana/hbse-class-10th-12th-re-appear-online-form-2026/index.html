HBSE Class 10th, 12th Re-Appear Online Form 2026

HBSE Class 10th, 12th Re-Appear Online Form 2026 is now open for compartment, improvement (ELOP), additional subject, and re-appear candidates. The Board of School Education, Haryana, Bhiwani (HBSE/BSEH) has announced the online registration schedule for the July 2026 examination.

Overview of HBSE Re-Appear

HBSE conducts re-appear (compartment) exams for students who failed in one or more subjects in the February/March 2026 Secondary (Class 10th) or Senior Secondary (Class 12th) exams. It also offers improvement opportunities (Eligible for Improvement of Performance - ELOP), additional subjects, and fresh chance options. Students can apply online on the official portal. The exam is scheduled for July 2026.

How to Apply for HBSE Class 10th, 12th Re-Appear Online Form 2026 (Step-by-Step)
  1. Visit the official application portal: bsehexam2017.in/compimp2026.
  2. Login using your roll number and other credentials from the main exam.
  3. Select the category (Re-appear/Compartment, Improvement/ELOP, Additional Subject).
  4. Fill required details, choose subjects, and upload documents if needed.
  5. Pay the fee online (₹1000 base + late fee if applicable).
  6. Submit and download the confirmation page.

Tip: Apply early to avoid late fees and technical issues. Keep your February/March 2026 roll number and marksheet ready.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Must have appeared in HBSE Secondary/Senior Secondary Exam February/March 2026.
  • For compartment/re-appear: Failed in 1 or more subjects (as per board rules).
  • For improvement: Passed but wants to improve performance in one or more subjects.
  • Additional subject candidates are also eligible.

No fresh candidates; only those from the 2026 main exam cycle.
