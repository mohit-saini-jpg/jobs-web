How to Download CRSU Date Sheet 2026

Students can easily download the CRSU Date Sheet 2026 from the official university website. Follow these simple steps to get your exam timetable.

  1. Visit the official CRSU website and go to the Date Sheet section.
  2. Locate the May-June 2026 examination notices.
  3. Click on the relevant date sheet link as per your course and semester.
  4. The PDF will open in a new tab. Save the file to your device.
  5. Take a printout and read all instructions carefully.
  6. Cross-check your paper codes and exam dates before preparation.

Always refer to the latest official date sheet and keep a printed copy for your exam preparation.
