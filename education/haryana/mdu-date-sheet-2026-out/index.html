How to Download MDU Date Sheet 2026

Students can download the MDU Date Sheet 2026 directly from the university’s official examination portal. The process is quick and straightforward.

  • Go to the official MDU website and open the date sheet section using this link: https://mdu.ac.in/admin/EventPage.aspx?id=2.
  • Look for the Conduct Branch notifications related to May 2026 examinations.
  • Identify your course name such as Shastri or Vyakarnacharya along with the correct year and scheme.
  • Click on the Download button next to your relevant date sheet.
  • The PDF will open - review all dates and instructions carefully.
  • Save the file and take a clear printout for daily reference during exam preparation.

Students should check the official timetable carefully and keep a copy handy for effective exam preparation. Regularly visit the portal for any updates or changes.
