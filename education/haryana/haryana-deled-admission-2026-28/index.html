Haryana D.El.Ed Admission 2026-28

Haryana D.El.Ed Admission 2026-28 has been announced by SCERT Haryana for the two-year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed.) course. Online registration and application process starts from 04 May 2026. Admission is merit-based on 10+2 marks through online counselling in multiple rounds.

Haryana D.El.Ed Admission 2026-28 is conducted by the State Council of Educational Research and Training (SCERT) Haryana for admission to the two-year Diploma in Elementary Education course. The online application window opens on 04 May 2026. Candidates who have passed 10+2 with minimum required marks can register for merit-based counselling.

Admission is open to bonafide residents of Haryana as well as candidates from outside the state. This guide covers complete details including important dates, eligibility criteria, application fee, stream-wise seat distribution, reservation policy, and step-by-step application process to help aspirants successfully secure admission in government and self-financed D.El.Ed. institutions in Haryana.

How to Apply for Haryana D.El.Ed Admission 2026-28

  • Visit the official website www.dedharyana.org or www.scertharyana.gov.in
  • Register with valid email and mobile number
  • Fill the online application form and choose stream & category
  • Upload photo, signature, and Aadhaar card
  • Select at least 5 institutions as choices
  • Pay the application fee and submit the form
  • Take printout of the filled application for future reference

Selection Process

  • Merit list prepared stream-wise (Science 30%, Commerce 28%, Arts 40%, Vocational 2%) on the basis of 10+2 marks
  • Online counselling in multiple rounds
  • Allotment of college based on merit, choices filled, and seat availability
  • Reporting to allotted college with original documents for verification and fee payment.
