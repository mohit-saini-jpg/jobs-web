How to Check MDU Result 2026 Online
  1. Visit the official MDU result portal at result.mdu.ac.in.
  2. On the result page, choose your course and semester from the available result list.
  3. Enter your Registration Number and Roll Number in the respective fields.
  4. Click on the Proceed or Submit button to view your result.
  5. Your MDU Result 2026 marksheet will be displayed on the screen.
  6. Check your name, course, semester, subject-wise marks and total marks carefully.
  7. Download or print the provisional marksheet for future reference and verification.
