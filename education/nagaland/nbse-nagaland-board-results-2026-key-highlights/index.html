Details

The Nagaland Board of School Education (NBSE), Kohima has officially released the NBSE Nagaland Board Result 2026 for HSLC (Class 10) and HSSLC (Class 12) examinations for the 2025-26 academic session. The results have been declared for students who appeared in the NBSE HSLC and HSSLC board exams conducted in February and March 2026.

NBSE Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - nbsenl.edu.in. Students can check and download their HSLC and HSSLC provisional marksheets by visiting the NBSE result portal, selecting their exam (HSLC/HSSLC) and entering their roll number and date of birth or other login details as required. After downloading the online scorecard, candidates must carefully verify their name, roll number, subjects, marks and overall result status, and contact their school or NBSE in case of any discrepancy in the marksheet.

NBSE Nagaland Board Results 2026 Key Highlights

NBSE has published the Nagaland Board Result 2026 for both HSLC and HSSLC examinations on its official portal, enabling students to access their Class 10 and Class 12 scorecards online. The results can be checked through the NBSE portal at nbsenl.edu.in, where separate links are provided for HSLC and HSSLC provisional marksheets. The board has also released the overall pass percentages, with 72.07 percent students clearing HSLC and 85.46 percent passing HSSLC in 2026.

NBSE Nagaland Board Result 2026 Direct Link

NBSE has activated the direct links to check Nagaland Board HSLC and HSSLC Result 2026 on its official result sections, allowing students to access their scorecards quickly. Through the NBSE result pages, candidates can open the HSLC or HSSLC marksheet link, enter their roll number and other details, and instantly download their provisional marksheets for the 2026 examinations. The direct notification and result link for HSLC and HSSLC 2026 are also available via the NBSE notifications page.

Steps to Check NBSE Nagaland Board Result 2026

Students can follow the simple steps below to check NBSE HSLC and HSSLC Result 2026 online.

  1. Visit the official website of Nagaland Board of School Education at nbsenl.edu.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the 'HSLC Marksheet' link for Class 10 or 'HSSLC Marksheet' link for Class 12 from the board results section.
  3. Alternatively, open the official notification/result link for HSLC and HSSLC 2026 from the notifications page.
  4. You will be redirected to the NBSE result login window for HSLC or HSSLC.
  5. Enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth or Name in the specified fields as instructed on the page.
  6. Click on the 'Get Result' or 'Submit' button to proceed.
  7. Your NBSE Nagaland Board Result 2026 for HSLC or HSSLC will appear on the screen, showing subject-wise marks, total marks and result status.
  8. Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future admission, scholarship or documentation purposes until the original marksheet is issued by your school.
