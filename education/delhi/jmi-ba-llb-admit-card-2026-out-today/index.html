How to Download JMI BA LLB Admit Card 2026
  1. Visit the official JMI admission website jmicoe.in or admission.jmi.ac.in.
  2. Open the Entrance Exam / Admit Card section for the BA LLB programme.
  3. Enter your application number / username and password used during registration.
  4. Click on Login / Submit to access your dashboard.
  5. Download the JMI BA LLB Admit Card 2026 and print 2-3 copies for the exam.

If the portal is slow due to heavy traffic after release, wait a short while and try again.

Details Mentioned on the Admit Card

The JMI BA LLB admit card 2026 will usually include:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph.
  • Application number and roll number.
  • Exam name: JMI BA LLB Entrance Exam 2026.
  • Exam date and reporting time.
  • Test centre name, code and address.
  • Signature and basic candidate details.
  • Important exam-day instructions.

Check every detail carefully. If there is any mismatch, contact the JMI admission helpline immediately.

Documents Required on Exam Day
  • A printed copy of the JMI BA LLB Admit Card 2026.
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport or Driving Licence.
  • Recent passport-size photographs, if asked in the instructions.

Without the admit card and valid ID, entry to the examination hall may be denied.

Exam-Day Instructions
  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time.
  • Carry only allowed items, as mentioned on the admit card.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, electronic gadgets, notes or books.
  • Follow the instructions given by invigilators inside the hall.
  • Keep the admit card safe after the exam for future admission steps.
