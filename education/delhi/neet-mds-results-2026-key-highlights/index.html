How to Check NEET MDS Result 2026?

Follow these steps to check and download the NEET MDS Result 2026 after it is released:

  1. Visit the official website of NBEMS - https://nbe.edu.in/.
  2. Click on the "NEET MDS Result 2026" link available on the homepage.
  3. Enter your User ID, Password, and other required details.
  4. Click on the Submit/Login button.
  5. The NEET MDS Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen.
  6. Check your marks, rank, and qualifying status carefully.
  7. Download the result and save it for future reference.
  8. Take a printout of the result for counselling and admission purposes.
