CUET UG 2026 New Admit Card Out

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has released the CUET UG 2026 New Admit Card for candidates scheduled to appear in the examination on June 6 and June 7, 2026. Candidates can now download their revised admit card from the official portal using their application number and password/date of birth.

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET UG) 2026 is being conducted for admission to undergraduate programmes offered by central, state, deemed, and participating universities across India. Candidates are advised to download the latest admit card and verify all details before appearing for the examination.

How to Download CUET UG 2026 New Admit Card

Follow the steps below to download the CUET UG 2026 admit card:

  1. Visit the official admit card portal.
  2. Click on the CUET UG 2026 Admit Card link.
  3. Enter your Application Number.
  4. Enter your Password or Date of Birth.
  5. Complete the security verification (if required).
  6. Click on the Login/Submit button.
  7. The CUET UG 2026 Admit Card will appear on the screen.
  8. Verify all details carefully.
  9. Download and save the admit card PDF.
  10. Take multiple printouts for future use.
Details Mentioned on CUET UG 2026 New Admit Card

Candidates must carefully verify the following details printed on the admit card:

  • Candidate's Name
  • Roll Number
  • Application Number
  • Photograph and Signature
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender and Category
  • Subject/Paper Name
  • Examination Date
  • Examination Shift and Timing
  • Reporting Time
  • Exam Centre Name
  • Exam Centre Address
  • Important Instructions for Candidates

Any discrepancy should be reported immediately to NTA before the examination date.

Documents Required for CUET UG 2026 Exam

Candidates must carry the following documents to the examination centre:

Mandatory Documents
  • Printed copy of CUET UG 2026 Admit Card
  • Original Valid Photo ID Proof
Accepted Photo ID Proofs
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Voter ID Card
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • School/College Identity Card
  • Any Government-issued Photo Identity Card
Additional Documents (If Applicable)
  • Passport-size photograph (same as application form)
  • PwD Certificate (for eligible candidates)
  • Relevant category certificate if required by authorities

Candidates without a valid admit card and photo identity proof will not be permitted to appear for the examination.

Exam‑Day Instructions for CUET UG 2026

NTA has issued detailed CUET UG 2026 exam‑day guidelines that candidates must follow:

  • Reporting time: Reach the exam centre at least 2 hours before the commencement of the exam to complete frisking and registration; no entry will be allowed after the gate‑closing time mentioned on your admit card.
  • Seating: A specific seat with your roll number will be allotted; you must sit only on your designated seat/terminal.
  • Prohibited items: Mobile phones, smart watches, electronic gadgets, calculators (unless allowed in advisory), log tables, handbags, notes, books, and food items are strictly prohibited inside the exam hall.
  • Dress code: Follow the simple dress code suggested by NTA (avoid metallic items, heavy jewellery, caps, scarves etc.) to ensure smooth frisking at the entrance.
  • Conduct: Follow all instructions displayed on the computer screen and given by invigilators; any involvement in unfair means can lead to cancellation of candidature and debarment from future NTA exams.
