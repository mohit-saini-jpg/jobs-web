How to Download JIPMAT 2026 Exam City Intimation Slip Online

Checking the JIPMAT exam city slip is a simple online process that takes only a few minutes if you have your login credentials ready.
Step-by-step process to check JIPMAT 2026 exam city
  1. Visit the official JIPMAT page on the NTA exams portal. Look for the “Candidate Activity” section on the home page.
  2. Click on the link that reads “Advance City Intimation for JIPMAT - 2026” or a similar title.
  3. You will be redirected to a login page for the exam city intimation slip.
  4. Enter your JIPMAT 2026 application number and your date of birth or password, then type the security pin exactly as shown on the screen.
  5. Click on the “Submit” button to view your JIPMAT 2026 exam city intimation slip.
  6. Check your allotted exam city, verify your basic details, and download or print the slip for future reference.
If you face any issue in logging in or viewing the slip, double-check your application number, date format and security pin, and try using a different browser or device. If the problem continues, reach out to NTA through the official JIPMAT helpline or email provided in the exam notice.

Details Mentioned on JIPMAT 2026 Exam City Intimation Slip

The JIPMAT exam city intimation slip primarily focuses on the city where your exam centre will be located. This information is different from the full exam centre address that appears on the admit card.
Typically, the city slip includes:
  • Candidate name and application number.
  • JIPMAT 2026 exam city allotted (for example, Delhi, Chennai, Mumbai, etc.).
  • Exam date and shift details, if specified.
  • Important note clarifying that this is not the admit card and cannot be used for entry into the exam hall.
The full centre name, address, reporting time, and other exam-day details will be mentioned on the JIPMAT admit card released later.

JIPMAT 2026 Admit Card vs Exam City Intimation Slip

Understanding the difference between the exam city slip and the admit card is crucial so that you do not get confused close to the exam date.
What is the JIPMAT exam city intimation slip?
The JIPMAT 2026 exam city intimation slip is a pre-exam document that tells you in which city your exam centre will be located. It is released earlier so that you can arrange travel and accommodation in time, especially if the city is far from where you live.
This slip is for information only and cannot be used to enter the exam hall.
What is the JIPMAT admit card?
The JIPMAT admit card (hall ticket) is the key exam document that you must carry to the exam centre on the test day. It normally contains:
  • Candidate name and photograph.
  • Roll number and application number.
  • Exam date, shift and reporting time.
  • Exam centre name and full address.
  • Important exam-day instructions.
The admit card is usually released a few days before the exam, and you must download and print a clear copy from the official NTA JIPMAT portal.
