4How to Download JIPMAT 2026 Exam City Intimation Slip Online
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphChecking the JIPMAT exam city slip is a simple online process that takes only a few minutes if you have your login credentials ready.
2paragraphStep-by-step process to check JIPMAT 2026 exam city
3listordered
  • Visit the official JIPMAT page on the NTA exams portal. Look for the “Candidate Activity” section on the home page.
  • Click on the link that reads “Advance City Intimation for JIPMAT - 2026” or a similar title.
  • You will be redirected to a login page for the exam city intimation slip.
  • Enter your JIPMAT 2026 application number and your date of birth or password, then type the security pin exactly as shown on the screen.
  • Click on the “Submit” button to view your JIPMAT 2026 exam city intimation slip.
  • Check your allotted exam city, verify your basic details, and download or print the slip for future reference.
4paragraphIf you face any issue in logging in or viewing the slip, double-check your application number, date format and security pin, and try using a different browser or device. If the problem continues, reach out to NTA through the official JIPMAT helpline or email provided in the exam notice.

8JIPMAT 2026 Admit Card vs Exam City Intimation Slip
Sr.TypeTextStyleItemsHeadersRows
1paragraphUnderstanding the difference between the exam city slip and the admit card is crucial so that you do not get confused close to the exam date.
2paragraphWhat is the JIPMAT exam city intimation slip?
3paragraphThe JIPMAT 2026 exam city intimation slip is a pre-exam document that tells you in which city your exam centre will be located. It is released earlier so that you can arrange travel and accommodation in time, especially if the city is far from where you live.
4paragraphThis slip is for information only and cannot be used to enter the exam hall.
5paragraphWhat is the JIPMAT admit card?
6paragraphThe JIPMAT admit card (hall ticket) is the key exam document that you must carry to the exam centre on the test day. It normally contains:
7listunordered
  • Candidate name and photograph.
  • Roll number and application number.
  • Exam date, shift and reporting time.
  • Exam centre name and full address.
  • Important exam-day instructions.
8paragraphThe admit card is usually released a few days before the exam, and you must download and print a clear copy from the official NTA JIPMAT portal.
