IGNOU June TEE Admit Card 2026 Out

Indira Gandhi National Open University has released the IGNOU June TEE Admit Card 2026 for the June 2026 Term End Examination (TEE) for all eligible ODL and online programme learners. The hall ticket has been made available online through the IGNOU Samarth Portal (ignou.samarth.edu.in) as well as via the hall ticket link on the official website ignou.ac.in.

According to the official notice and date sheet, the IGNOU June 2026 TEE will be conducted from 1 June 2026 to 21 July 2026 in multiple sessions at exam centres across India and abroad. Students who have submitted the June 2026 TEE exam form can now log in to the Samarth portal with their username and password to download and print their hall ticket.

How to Download IGNOU June TEE Admit Card 2026

Follow these steps to download your IGNOU June 2026 TEE hall ticket:

  • Visit the IGNOU Samarth student portal ignou.samarth.edu.in.
  • Click on “Student Login” and enter your Username and Password (same as used for the June 2026 TEE exam form).
  • After login, locate and click on the “June 2026 TEE Hall Ticket / Admit Card” link available on the dashboard or in the Examination section.
  • Your IGNOU June 2026 TEE admit card will open, showing your enrolment number, programme, course codes, exam dates, session and exam centre details.
  • Verify all the details carefully, then download and print 2–3 clear A4‑size copies of the hall ticket to carry on all exam days.

You can also access the hall ticket link from ignou.ac.in under the Hall Ticket/Admit Card section, which redirects to the Samarth portal.

Details Mentioned on IGNOU June TEE Admit Card 2026

The IGNOU June 2026 TEE admit card carries important exam and candidate information that must be verified before appearing in the exam:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph and enrolment number
  • Programme code and course codes (subjects) for which you are appearing in June 2026
  • Exam name: June 2026 Term End Examination (TEE)
  • Exam dates and session (morning 10 AM–1 PM or evening 2 PM–5 PM, or as per revised timings) for each course code
  • Exam centre code, centre name, city and complete address
  • Instructions regarding permissible items, reporting time and exam day rules
  • Any special remark / provision (such as eligibility, provisional permission etc., if applicable)

If any mismatch or error is noticed in your details, course codes or exam centre information, you should contact your Regional Centre / IGNOU exam section immediately before the exam.

Documents Required with IGNOU June TEE Admit Card 2026

On the day of the IGNOU June 2026 TEE, candidates should carry:

  • A printed copy of the IGNOU June TEE Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence or any other government‑issued ID accepted as per IGNOU instructions.
  • Any additional documents mentioned in the hall ticket / IGNOU instructions (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter if you are availing a scribe facility).

Entry to the exam centre will not be allowed without the printed hall ticket and a valid photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for IGNOU June 2026 TEE

To ensure smooth conduct of the IGNOU June 2026 Term End Examination, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time printed on the IGNOU hall ticket so that verification, frisking and seating can be completed; late entry after the scheduled time or gate‑closing time may not be allowed.
  • Carry only essential items: IGNOU June 2026 hall ticket (A4 printout), valid photo ID and basic permitted stationery such as pens, pencils etc., as per the instructions mentioned by IGNOU for your exam.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not permitted for your course), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the examination hall; violation may lead to cancellation of your paper and further disciplinary action.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the IGNOU hall ticket and those announced by the invigilators; indulging in any unfair means, talking to other candidates or disobeying exam rules can result in cancellation of your examination and possible debarment from future IGNOU exams.
  • Keep your IGNOU June 2026 hall ticket safely even after the exam, as it may be required for result reference, re‑evaluation, project/viva and future admission / convocation formalities.
