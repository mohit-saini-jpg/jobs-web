IGNOU June 2026 TEE Revised Date Sheet OUT

How to Download IGNOU June 2026 TEE Revised Date Sheet

Students can easily download the IGNOU June 2026 TEE revised date sheet from the official IGNOU website. The process is simple and quick.

  • Go to the Student Zone or Examination section on the homepage.
  • Locate and click on the link for June 2026 TEE Revised Date Sheet.
  • The PDF will open in a new tab. Read all important instructions carefully.
