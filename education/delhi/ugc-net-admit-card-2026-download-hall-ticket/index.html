3What Is the UGC NET Admit Card 2026?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphThe UGC NET hall ticket 2026 is a mandatory entry document for all registered candidates. Without it, no candidate will be permitted to enter the examination hall. The hall ticket is a mandatory document that needs to be carried to the examination centre without the call letter, a candidate will not be allowed to sit for the NTA UGC NET exam. The admit card contains crucial details such as the candidate's name, roll number, photograph, signature, exam centre address, date and shift of the exam, and reporting time.
4UGC NET City Intimation Slip vs. Admit Card - What's the Difference?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphMany candidates confuse the City Intimation Slip with the official admit card. It is important to understand the distinction between the two documents before the exam.
2paragraphThe UGC NET City Intimation Slip is a document released by the NTA to inform candidates about the city where the exam centre is located. It is not the admit card but provides advance information to help candidates make travel arrangements in advance.
3paragraphThe NTA released the UGC NET 2026 June City Intimation Slip on June 10, 2026, at ugcnet.nta.nic.in. The UGC NET city intimation slip mentions the date of examination, shift, subject/test papers, and medium chosen by the candidate while filling the application form.
4paragraphIn short the City Slip tells you where your exam will be held; the Admit Card is your official entry pass with the complete centre address and reporting time.
5UGC NET Admit Card 2026 - How to Download (Step-by-Step Guide)
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphOnce the NTA UGC NET hall ticket 2026 is released on the official website, candidates can follow these simple steps to download it. Candidates can download their UGC NET Admit Card either from the direct link or from the official website by using their application number and password, which were generated at the time of registration.
2paragraphFollow the steps below:
3listordered
  • Visit the official UGC NET website: ugcnet.nta.nic.in
  • On the homepage, look for the link reading "UGC NET June 2026: Click Here to Download Admit Card"
  • Click on the link you will be redirected to the login page
  • Enter your Application Number and Password
  • Submit the details and your admit card will appear on the screen
  • Verify all the information carefully
  • Download and print multiple copies of the UGC NET hall ticket 2026
4paragraphKeep at least two printed copies one for submission at the exam centre and one for your personal records.
