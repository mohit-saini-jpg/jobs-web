CBSE has released the Class 12 Result 2026 for the current academic session for all streams, including Science, Commerce and Arts, on the official result portals. The CBSE 12th result has been announced online on 13 May 2026 on the official website, cbse.gov.in.

Students can now access their digital marksheet by logging in with their roll number, school number, date of birth and admit card ID as mentioned on the admit card. The online scorecard can be downloaded and saved as a provisional marksheet for admission and documentation purposes until schools issue the original marksheets.

After checking their scores, students should carefully verify all details on the marksheet, including personal information, subject-wise marks and overall result status. In case of any discrepancy in personal details or marks, they must promptly contact their school or the concerned CBSE regional office for correction.

How to check CBSE Class 12 Result 2026 online
  1. Open the official CBSE website cbse.gov.in or directly visit the result portals cbseresults.nic.in or results.cbse.nic.in when the result is declared.
  2. Click on the link that says "CBSE Class XII Result 2026" or "Senior School Certificate Examination (Class XII) Results 2026".
  3. On the login page, enter your roll number, school number, date of birth and admit card ID as mentioned on the CBSE admit card 2026.
  4. Verify the details and click on the "Submit" or "Proceed" button to view your CBSE 12th result 2026 on the screen.
  5. Check your subject wise marks, total marks, percentage and overall result status, then download the online marksheet as a PDF and take a printout for future use.
  6. Cross check all details carefully and contact your school or CBSE regional office if you notice any discrepancy in the marksheet.
