ICSI CS Executive & Professional Admit Card 2026 Out

The Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI) has released the e‑Admit Cards for CS Executive and CS Professional June 2026 examinations on its official website. The e‑admit cards have been issued online only and will not be sent in physical form to any candidate.

As per ICSI, the CS Executive and Professional June 2026 exams are scheduled from 1 June 2026 to 7 June 2026, in the morning shift from 9:00 AM to 12:15 PM as per the published timetable. Candidates must download and print their e‑admit cards well in advance to avoid last‑minute issues at the examination centre.

How to Download ICSI CS Executive & Professional Admit Card 2026

Candidates can download the ICSI CS Executive & Professional June 2026 e‑admit card using the following steps:

  • Visit the official ICSI website icsi.edu and go to the ‘Students’ → ‘Examination’ section.
  • Click on the link for “E‑Admit Card June 2026 CS Executive & Professional Examinations” or the admit card download link provided in the student/exam portal.
  • Enter your 17‑digit CS registration number in the specified field and submit.
  • Your CS Executive / CS Professional June 2026 e‑admit card will be displayed on the screen.
  • Verify personal details, exam centre, module, subjects and exam dates carefully.
  • Download and print multiple A4‑size copies of the admit card, as it must be carried to the examination centre on all exam days.

ICSI specifically advises students to download and print the e‑admit card well in advance to avoid last‑minute inconvenience.

Details Mentioned on ICSI CS Executive & Professional Admit Card 2026

The e‑admit card contains several important particulars that candidates must cross‑check after downloading:

  • Name of the candidate.
  • Photograph and signature of the candidate.
  • 17‑digit registration number and programme stage (Executive / Professional).
  • Examination centre name and full address.
  • Syllabus, exemptions, optional subject (Professional), medium and module/group details.
  • Dates and timings of examination as per June 2026 timetable (17 June, 9 AM12:15 PM).
  • Important instructions for candidates and directions applicable during the exams.

If any discrepancy is found in name, photograph, signature, exam centre or subject/module details, students should immediately contact the ICSI exam authorities through the helplines/email given in the admit card and on the ICSI website.

Documents Required with ICSI CS Executive & Professional Admit Card 2026

On each exam day of the June 2026 CS Executive & Professional examinations, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the ICSI e‑Admit Card June 2026 for CS Executive/Professional (clear A4‑size printout).
  • A valid photo identity proof issued by a government authority, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence or any other photo ID specified by ICSI.

Candidates may also be asked to carry their student identity card or other documents as specified in ICSI guidelines, so they should carefully read the instructions given with the e‑admit card and on the examination page.

Exam‑Day Instructions for ICSI CS Executive & Professional June 2026

ICSI has issued detailed guidelines and instructions for students appearing in the June 2026 CS Executive and Professional exams:

  • Reach the examination centre well before 9:00 AM to complete identity verification and seating; late entry after the scheduled time may not be allowed.
  • Carry your e‑admit card printout and valid photo ID on all exam days; without these, entry to the examination hall will be denied.
  • Bring only permitted items such as pens, pencils and transparent water bottle if allowed; carrying mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless specifically allowed for a paper), Bluetooth devices, books, notes or any electronic gadgets is strictly prohibited.
  • Follow seating plans, instructions printed on the question paper/answer booklet and directions given by invigilators; indulging in unfair practices can lead to cancellation of your exam and disciplinary action by the Institute.
  • Retain a copy of your e‑admit card even after the examinations as it may be required for future reference, result verification or during further communication with ICSI.
