Direct Link to Download COMEDK 2026 Final Answer Key

The COMEDK 2026 final answer key is available only in online mode on the official website, and no physical copies are issued to candidates. To access the key, candidates must log in to comedk.org using their registered User ID and password, where the answer key PDF is provided under the UGET 2026 section.

How to Download COMEDK 2026 Final Answer Key from comedk.org

Candidates can follow these steps to download the final answer key smoothly:

  • Visit the official COMEDK website, comedk.org.
  • Click on the “Candidate Login” or “Applicant Login” link for COMEDK UGET 2026.
  • Enter your User ID / application sequence number and password, then submit.
  • Click on the “COMEDK UGET 2026 Answer Key / Final Answer Key” link visible on the dashboard.
  • The final answer key PDF, along with the question paper or response sheet link, will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download and save the COMEDK UGET 2026 final answer key for future reference.

It is advisable to keep both the final answer key and response sheet, as these documents will help during result verification and counselling.

Provisional vs Final COMEDK 2026 Answer Key - What Changed?

COMEDK first publishes a provisional answer key to allow candidates to verify their responses and report any discrepancies. For COMEDK 2026, the provisional answer key was released on 16 May 2026 along with the response sheets, and candidates were allowed to raise objections until 18 May 2026, 2 PM. Each objection required a fee payment of ₹500 per question, which was refunded if the challenge was found valid.

After reviewing all the objections and supporting documents received from candidates, COMEDK finalised the corrected responses and released the final answer key on 23 May 2026. The final answer key is binding and is used to calculate scores and prepare the rank list, and there is no further provision to challenge it.

How to Calculate Your COMEDK 2026 Score Using Final Answer Key

The COMEDK UGET exam follows a straightforward marking scheme, making it easy to estimate your score once the final answer key and response sheet are available.

  • Each correct answer carries 1 mark.
  • There is no negative marking for incorrect or unattempted questions.

To calculate your approximate score:

  • Download the COMEDK 2026 final answer key and your individual response sheet from comedk.org.
  • Match each question ID in your response sheet with the corresponding answer in the final key.
  • Count the total number of questions you have answered correctly.
  • Since there is no negative marking, your raw score is simply the total number of correct responses.

This raw score will then be used by COMEDK, along with the performance of all candidates, to derive your rank and merit position for admissions.

COMEDK 2026 Result, Rank Card and Next Steps

Once the final answer key is published, the next major event is the announcement of the COMEDK 2026 result and rank card. As per the official calendar, the scorecard and rank list will be made available online in the last week of May 2026, and candidates will be able to log in at comedk.org to download their rank card.

The COMEDK rank card will typically carry details such as your test score, rank, and some basic personal information that must be verified carefully. Candidates must preserve the rank card and other documents, as they will be required during COMEDK counselling and seat allotment, details of which will be published separately on the official website.

COMEDK UGET 2026 Eligibility (Quick Reminder)

Although the final answer key stage comes after the exam, many students and parents landing on this page may want to re-check whether the candidate satisfied basic eligibility. COMEDK UGET 2026 is open to Indian nationals who have passed or are appearing in the 10+2 (or equivalent) examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology / Computer Science / Electronics as one of the optional subjects. For General category candidates, a minimum aggregate of 45% marks in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics is required, while it is 40% for SC, ST and OBC candidates of Karnataka.

Candidates are strongly advised to refer to the detailed COMEDK UGET 2026 information brochure on comedk.org for category-wise conditions, reservation rules and other academic eligibility requirements.
