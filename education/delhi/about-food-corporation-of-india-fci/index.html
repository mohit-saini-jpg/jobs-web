About Food Corporation of India (FCI)

FCI is a central public sector undertaking under the Ministry of Consumer Affairs, responsible for food grain procurement, storage, movement, and distribution across India. It recruits for multiple categories, such as Manager (Category II), Assistant Grade (Category III), Stenographer, Typist, and technical/engineering posts in various zones.

Candidates prefer FCI jobs for stable central PSU service, all-India postings, and structured pay scales under government guidelines.

FCI Educational Qualification 2026

Candidates must ensure their degrees are from recognized universities (UGC/AICTE approved, where applicable) and match the exact combinations given in the official FCI 2026 notification PDF.

FCI Nationality Criteria

  • Candidate must be a citizen of India, or
  • Subject of Nepal or Bhutan, or
  • Tibetan refugee who came to India before 1 January 1962 with the intention of permanent settlement, or
  • Person of Indian Origin who has migrated from specified countries with the intention of permanently settling in India, subject to an eligibility certificate from the Government of India.

These follow standard central government recruitment rules; always mirror the exact wording from the notification.

Experience Requirement (If Applicable)

Some technical or Hindi posts at the Manager level may require prior experience in translation, engineering, quality control, or related fields. Most Assistant Grade III posts do not require prior experience; they are suitable for fresh graduates who meet age and qualification norms. Check the ‘Experience’ column/post-wise details in the official FCI 2026 notification, especially for Manager (Technical), Manager (Hindi), and engineering posts.

Documents Required for Eligibility Verification

  • Graduation degree and mark sheets (and post-graduation, if counted for a post).
  • Professional or technical degree certificates (Engineering, CA/ICWA, etc.).
  • Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC-NCL/EWS) in the prescribed central government format.
  • PwBD certificate, if applicable.
  • Date of Birth proof (10th certificate, birth certificate, or equivalent).
  • Photo identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID, etc.).
  • Experience certificates from employers for posts where experience is required.
