CSEET June 2026 Admit Card Out

The Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI) has released the CSEET June 2026 Admit Card for the upcoming Company Secretary Executive Entrance Test June session. The admit card has been made available online through the official ICSI website for all candidates who successfully registered for the June 2026 exam.

CSEET June 2026 will be conducted in centre based mode between 1 June 2026 and 4 June 2026, as per the official time table, with one paper each day for Business Communication, Fundamentals of Accounting, Economic and Business Environment, and Business Laws and Management. CSEET June 2026 Admit Card has to be downloaded and printed by the candidates from icsi.edu, as no physical admit card is sent by post.

How to Download CSEET June 2026 Admit Card

Follow these steps to download the CSEET June 2026 hall ticket:

  1. Visit the official ICSI website icsi.edu.
  2. Go to the Student or Examination section and click on the link “Download CSEET June 2026 Admit Card” when visible, or follow the admit card link given in the latest notifications.
  3. You will be redirected to the CSEET admit card login page.
  4. Enter your CSEET registration / unique ID and Date of Birth in the specified format and submit.
  5. Your CSEET June 2026 Admit Card will appear on the screen with your exam date, paper name, reporting time and exam centre details.
  6. Check all details carefully and then download and print at least one clear A4-size copy of the hall ticket for exam day.

Candidates must carry the printed admit card to the allocated exam centre on each exam day as per the time table.

Details Mentioned on CSEET June 2026 Admit Card

The CSEET June 2026 admit card carries key exam and candidate information which must be verified before appearing in the exam:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph and signature
  • CSEET registration / unique ID and roll number (if allotted)
  • Exam name and session: Company Secretary Executive Entrance Test (CSEET) June 2026
  • Exam date and time for each subject as per the schedule (for example, 1 June 2026 to 4 June 2026 with 2:30 PM reporting and exam duration)
  • Exam centre name, centre code and complete address (for centre based exam)
  • Reporting time, gate closing time and exam duration
  • Instructions regarding permissible items, exam day rules and conduct inside the exam hall

If any mismatch or error is noticed in your name, photograph, registration ID, exam date or centre details, you should contact ICSI support / the exam helpdesk immediately before the exam for clarification and correction guidance.

Documents Required with CSEET June 2026 Admit Card

On the day of the CSEET June 2026 exam, candidates should carry the following:

  • A printed copy of the CSEET June 2026 Admit Card (clear A4-size printout; preferably colour or good-quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence or any other government-issued ID accepted as per ICSI instructions.
  • Any additional documents mentioned by ICSI in the admit card instructions (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter for candidates availing scribe facility).

Entry to the exam centre will not be allowed without the printed CSEET admit card and a valid photo ID.

Exam-Day Instructions for CSEET June 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the CSEET June 2026 centre based examinations, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time printed on the CSEET June 2026 admit card so that verification, frisking and seating can be completed; late entry after the scheduled time or gate-closing time may not be allowed.
  • Carry only essential items: CSEET admit card (A4 printout), valid photo ID and basic permitted stationery as mentioned in the ICSI guidelines; follow all centre based exam instructions carefully.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless specifically allowed), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the examination hall; violation may lead to cancellation of your exam and further disciplinary action.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the admit card and those announced by invigilators; indulging in unfair means, talking to other candidates or violating exam rules can result in cancellation of your examination and debarment from future ICSI exams.
  • Keep your CSEET June 2026 admit card safely even after the exam, as it may be required for future reference, result verification and subsequent stages of the CS course.
