6What's NTA (National Testing Agency)?
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphNational Testing Agency (NTA) is an autonomous organization under the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Government of India. Established in 2017, NTA conducts entrance examinations for various undergraduate and postgraduate programs across Indian universities.
2paragraphKey Facts About NTA:
3listunordered
  • Established: 2017
  • Purpose: Conduct fair, transparent, and computer-based entrance tests
  • Exams Conducted: CUET, JEE (Main), NEET, CBSE, and 50+ other entrance exams
  • Headquarters: New Delhi, India
  • Website: https://nta.ac.in
4paragraphWhy CUET Matters:
5listunordered
  • CUET (Common University Entrance Test) is the single gateway for admission to:
  • Delhi University (DU) - 75+ programs
  • Banaras Hindu University (BHU)
  • ** Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU)**
  • 40+ Central and State Universities
  • Over 2,500+ undergraduate programs across India
6paragraphWithout CUET, you cannot get admission to these prestigious universities for B.A., B.Sc., BBA, and other undergraduate courses.
