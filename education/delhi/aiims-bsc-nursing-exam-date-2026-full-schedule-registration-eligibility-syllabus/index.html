13AIIMS BSc Nursing 2026 Syllabus
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphAlthough the AIIMS BSc Nursing 2026 syllabus is not very different from your Class 11 and 12 NCERT topics, the level of questions can be slightly tricky. Most of the paper focuses on Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and sometimes General Knowledge or English depending on the year’s pattern.
14Subject-wise syllabus overview
Sr.TypeStyleItemsText
1listunordered
  • Physics: Mechanics, kinematics, work, energy, power, heat and thermodynamics, waves, optics, electrostatics, current electricity, modern physics.
  • Chemistry: Atomic structure, chemical bonding, states of matter, thermodynamics, equilibrium, redox, solutions, organic chemistry basics, biomolecules.
  • Biology: Cell structure, cell division, genetics, evolution, human physiology, plant physiology, reproduction, ecology, human health and diseases.
  • General Knowledge / English (if applicable): Basic GK, current affairs related to health and science, comprehension, vocabulary and basic grammar.
2paragraphAIIMS generally sticks close to the NCERT level for core topics, so finishing NCERTs properly is still one of the best strategies even in 2026.
