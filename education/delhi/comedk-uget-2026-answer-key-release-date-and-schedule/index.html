How to Download COMEDK UGET Provisional Answer Key 2026

Students can access the COMEDK UGET provisional answer key 2026 only through the official COMEDK portal using their login credentials. The answer key, question paper and response sheet are all provided in the same login area to make verification easier.

Follow these steps to download the COMEDK UGET 2026 answer key:

  1. Visit the official website: comedk.org.
  2. Click on the “Login” or “Candidate Login” link for UGET 2026.
  3. Enter your COMEDK application number/user ID and password.
  4. After successful login, click on the link for “Provisional Answer Key / Response Sheet”.
  5. Select your test session (if required) and download the answer key PDF and response sheet.
  6. Save the PDF to your device and take a printout if needed for offline comparison.

How to Calculate Scores Using COMEDK UGET Answer Key 2026

COMEDK UGET follows a simple marking scheme, which makes it easy for candidates to calculate their approximate score using the provisional answer key.

Basic marking scheme (as per the COMEDK information brochure):

  • Each correct answer carries 1 mark
  • There is no negative marking for wrong answers
  • Unattempted questions carry 0 marks

To calculate your probable score:

  1. Count the total number of correct responses using the official answer key.
  2. Multiply the number of correct answers by 1.
  3. Since there is no negative marking, you do not deduct marks for incorrect responses.

For example, if a candidate has 120 correct answers out of 180 questions, their estimated score will be 120 marks.

COMEDK UGET 2026 Answer Key Objection Process

COMEDK allows candidates to challenge the provisional answer key within a limited time window by submitting objections online. This process ensures that any genuine errors in the answer key can be corrected before the final result is prepared.

COMEDK UGET 2026 Answer Key Challenge Rules
  • Objections can be submitted only through the official candidate login on comedk.org.
  • A non-refundable fee of Rs 500 per question challenged is charged; the fee may be refunded if the objection is found valid and accepted by experts.
  • Candidates must provide proper academic justification or reference for each objection.
  • Objections submitted after the deadline or without payment/supporting proof are not considered.
Steps to Raise Objection Online
  1. Visit comedk.org and log in with your application number and password.
  2. Click on the link for “Raise Objection to Provisional Answer Key”.
  3. Select the question ID you want to challenge and choose the option you believe is correct.
  4. Upload supporting documents or references (such as textbook pages or official material) in the specified format.
  5. Pay Rs 500 per question through the online payment gateway.
  6. Submit the objection and note the reference number for future tracking.

Once the expert panel reviews all objections, COMEDK will finalize the answer key and publish the final version on the notified date.

COMEDK UGET 2026 Final Answer Key and Result

After evaluating all valid objections, COMEDK will release the final answer key on May 23, 2026, at 2 PM. No further challenges are allowed after the final key is published, and it serves as the basis for result compilation.

The COMEDK UGET 2026 result and rank cards are scheduled to be announced by May 29, 2026, through the official website. Candidates must download their rank card using their login credentials, as it will be required later during the counselling and seat allotment process.
