3How to Download JEECUP Answer Key 2026 - Step-by-Step Guide
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphDownloading the JEECUP 2026 provisional answer key is a straightforward process. Candidates must log in using their registered credentials on the official JEECUP candidate portal. Follow the steps below carefully to access and save your response sheet.
2listordered
  • Visit the official JEECUP website at jeecup.admissions.nic.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the "JEECUP Answer Key 2026" link.
  • Enter your Application Number / Enrollment Number and Password or Date of Birth.
  • Fill in the Captcha Code carefully and click the Login / Submit button.
  • Your JEECUP Answer Key 2026 and response sheet will appear on screen.
  • Verify the details exam date, shift timing, group, and exam centre.
  • Download the Answer Key PDF and take a printout for future reference.

10What Happens After the JEECUP 2026 Answer Key?
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphUnderstanding the sequence of events after the provisional answer key helps candidates plan their next steps effectively. Here is the complete post-answer-key timeline for JEECUP 2026.
2listunordered
  • Objection Window Closes (15 June 2026): No challenge will be accepted after this date.
  • Final Answer Key: JEECUP will publish the revised final answer key after reviewing all objections. Date to be announced.
  • JEECUP 2026 Result: Final scores and ranks will be calculated based on the final answer key. The result will be available at jeecup.admissions.nic.in. Date to be notified.
  • JEECUP 2026 Counselling: Qualified candidates will be eligible for centralised counselling for seat allotment at UP polytechnic and diploma colleges. Counselling registration will open after the result declaration.
3paragraphCandidates must keep their scorecard safe until the entire admission process is over, as it will be required at every stage of counselling. The JEECUP 2026 scorecard is valid for one year only.
