How to Check IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Exam Result 2026?

Candidates can follow the steps below to check and download the IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Exam Result 2026:

  1. Visit the official result portal at https://studentservices.ignou.ac.in/bed_result/.
  2. Click on the link for "IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Exam Result 2026".
  3. Enter your Enrollment Number or other required credentials.
  4. Click on the Submit button.
  5. The IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Exam Result 2026 will appear on the screen.
  6. Check all details carefully, including marks and qualifying status.
  7. Download the result PDF or scorecard.
  8. Take a printout for future admission and counselling procedures.
