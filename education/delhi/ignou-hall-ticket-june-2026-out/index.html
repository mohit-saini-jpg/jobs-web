3How to Download IGNOU Hall Ticket June 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphFollow the steps below to download your IGNOU June 2026 TEE hall ticket:
2listordered
  • Visit the official IGNOU website www.ignou.ac.in or directly open the IGNOU SAMARTH portal (ignou.samarth.edu.in).
  • On ignou.ac.in, go to the “Hall Ticket / Admit Card for June 2026 Term End Examination (TEE)” link in the Announcements / Alerts section; this will redirect you to the hall ticket download page on SAMARTH.
  • On the hall ticket page, select June 2026 Term End Examination, then enter your Enrolment Number / Username and choose your Programme if required.
  • Click on the “Submit / Login / Generate Hall Ticket” button to view your IGNOU Hall Ticket June 2026 on the screen.
  • Check all the details carefully such as your name, exam centre, course codes, dates and session timing, then download the PDF and take 2-3 clear A4‑size printouts for exam use.
3paragraphNo physical hall ticket is sent by post; all learners have to download it themselves from the official portal.

6Exam‑Day Instructions for IGNOU June 2026 TEE
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphTo ensure smooth conduct of the IGNOU June 2026 Term End Examination, candidates should follow these guidelines:
2listunordered
  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time printed on the IGNOU hall ticket so that verification, frisking and seating can be completed; late entry after the scheduled time or gate‑closing time may not be allowed.
  • Carry only essential items: IGNOU June 2026 hall ticket (A4 printout), valid photo ID and basic permitted stationery such as pens, pencils etc., as per the instructions mentioned by IGNOU for your exam.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not permitted for your course), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the examination hall; violation may lead to cancellation of your paper and further disciplinary action.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the hall ticket and those announced by the invigilators; indulging in unfair means, talking to other candidates, or disobeying exam rules can result in cancellation of your examination and possible debarment from future IGNOU exams.
  • Keep your IGNOU hall ticket safely even after the exam, as it may be required for result reference, re‑evaluation, project/viva, and future admission / convocation formalities.
