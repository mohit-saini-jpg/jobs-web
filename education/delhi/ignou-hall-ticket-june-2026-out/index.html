IGNOU Hall Ticket June 2026 Out

Indira Gandhi National Open University has released the IGNOU Hall Ticket for June 2026 Term-End Examination (TEE) on 19th May 2026 for UG, PG, Diploma, Certificate, and other ODL/online programmes. The June 2026 TEE is scheduled to be conducted from 01 June 2026 to 21 July 2026 in multiple sessions across various exam centres in India and abroad, as per the final date sheet issued by IGNOU.

The hall ticket is available online on the IGNOU SAMARTH portal (ignou.samarth.edu.in) as well as through the official website ignou.ac.in under the June 2026 TEE hall ticket section. Typically, hall tickets are released 7–10 days before the commencement of the examination, and candidates are advised to download and print them promptly.

How to Download IGNOU Hall Ticket June 2026

Follow the steps below to download your IGNOU June 2026 TEE hall ticket:

  1. Visit the official IGNOU website www.ignou.ac.in or directly open the IGNOU SAMARTH portal (ignou.samarth.edu.in).
  2. On ignou.ac.in, go to the “Hall Ticket / Admit Card for June 2026 Term End Examination (TEE)” link in the Announcements / Alerts section; this will redirect you to the hall ticket download page on SAMARTH.
  3. On the hall ticket page, select June 2026 Term End Examination, then enter your Enrolment Number / Username and choose your Programme if required.
  4. Click on the “Submit / Login / Generate Hall Ticket” button to view your IGNOU Hall Ticket June 2026 on the screen.
  5. Check all the details carefully such as your name, exam centre, course codes, dates and session timing, then download the PDF and take 2-3 clear A4‑size printouts for exam use.

No physical hall ticket is sent by post; all learners have to download it themselves from the official portal.

Details Mentioned on IGNOU Hall Ticket June 2026

The IGNOU June 2026 TEE admit card carries important exam and candidate information that must be verified before appearing:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph and enrolment number
  • Programme code and course codes (subjects) for which you are appearing
  • Exam name: June 2026 Term End Examination (TEE)
  • Exam dates and session (morning 10 AM-1 PM or evening 2 PM-5 PM) for each course code
  • Exam centre code, centre name and complete address
  • Instructions regarding permissible items, reporting time, and use of language for answers
  • Any special remark / provision (such as eligibility, provisional permission etc.)

If any mismatch or error is noticed in your details or courses, you should contact your Regional Centre / IGNOU exam section immediately before the exam.

Documents Required and Exam‑Day Instructions for IGNOU Hall Ticket June 2026

Documents Required with IGNOU Hall Ticket June 2026

On the day of the IGNOU June 2026 TEE, candidates should carry:

  • A printed copy of the IGNOU Hall Ticket June 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence or any other government‑issued ID accepted as per IGNOU instructions.
  • Any additional documents mentioned in the hall ticket / IGNOU instructions (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter if you are availing a scribe facility).

Entry to the exam centre will not be allowed without the printed hall ticket and a valid photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for IGNOU June 2026 TEE

To ensure smooth conduct of the IGNOU June 2026 Term End Examination, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time printed on the IGNOU hall ticket so that verification, frisking and seating can be completed; late entry after the scheduled time or gate‑closing time may not be allowed.
  • Carry only essential items: IGNOU June 2026 hall ticket (A4 printout), valid photo ID and basic permitted stationery such as pens, pencils etc., as per the instructions mentioned by IGNOU for your exam.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not permitted for your course), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the examination hall; violation may lead to cancellation of your paper and further disciplinary action.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the hall ticket and those announced by the invigilators; indulging in unfair means, talking to other candidates, or disobeying exam rules can result in cancellation of your examination and possible debarment from future IGNOU exams.
  • Keep your IGNOU hall ticket safely even after the exam, as it may be required for result reference, re‑evaluation, project/viva, and future admission / convocation formalities.
