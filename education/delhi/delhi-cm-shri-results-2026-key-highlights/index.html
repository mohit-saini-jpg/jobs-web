The Directorate of Education, Delhi has officially released the Delhi CM Shri Class 11 Result 2026 for the 2025-26 academic session. The results contain candidate details, marks obtained, qualifying status, and allotted school information based on the admission test performance.

Delhi CM Shri Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - https://edudel.nic.in/. After downloading the online scorecard or merit list PDF, students are advised to verify all details carefully and contact the Directorate of Education or their allotted school in case of any discrepancies.

How to Download Delhi CM Shri Class 11 Result 2026
  1. Visit the official website of the Directorate of Education: https://edudel.nic.in/.
  2. On the homepage, look for the link related to "CM Shri School Admission Test 2026" or "CM Shri Class 11 Result 2026".
  3. Click on the result or merit list link for CM Shri Class 11.
  4. Enter your registration number, roll number, date of birth and other required login credentials in the result window.
  5. Fill the verification code or captcha (if shown) and click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button.
  6. Your Delhi CM Shri Class 11 Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen with marks, qualifying status and allotted school (if applicable).
  7. Download the result or merit list PDF and take a printout for future reference and admission formalities.
