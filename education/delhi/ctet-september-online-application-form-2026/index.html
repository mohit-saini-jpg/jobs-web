CTET September 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Paper I (Classes 1 to 5): Senior Secondary (or equivalent) with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in final year of 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed/B.El.Ed) or equivalent.

Paper II (Classes 6 to 8): Graduation degree with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in final year of B.Ed or equivalent.

Age Limit: No age limit (as per CBSE rules).

Detailed eligibility will be available in the official Information Bulletin.

How to Apply for CTET September 2026

  • Visit the official website https://ctet.nic.in or https://www.cbse.gov.in.
  • Click on the “Apply Online” link for CTET September 2026.
  • Complete registration with basic details and generate login credentials.
  • Fill the online application form carefully with personal, educational, and contact details.
  • Upload scanned photograph and signature as per specifications.
  • Pay the required application fee online.
  • Submit the form and take a printout for future reference.

Correction Window: Limited corrections allowed from 15–18 June 2026 only.
