3How to Download SCERT Delhi DElEd Admit Card 2026
Sr.TypeStyleItemsText
1listordered
  • Visit the official SCERT Delhi admission portal scertdelhi.admissions.nic.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the link “SCERT Delhi DElEd Admit Card 2026” or the “Download Admit Card” option under e-services/admission section.
  • You will be redirected to the admit card login page. Enter your registration/application number and date of birth, then enter the captcha if shown.
  • Click on the Submit / Login button to access your hall ticket.
  • Your SCERT Delhi DElEd Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen with details like your name, exam date, time and exam centre address.
  • Verify all the information carefully and download and print 2–3 clear A4‑size copies of the admit card.
2paragraphThe admit card will be available only in online mode; SCERT will not send it by post.

Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphTo ensure smooth conduct of the SCERT Delhi DElEd Entrance Exam 2026, candidates should follow these guidelines:
2listunordered
  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card to complete verification, frisking and seating; latecomers after gate‑closing time may be denied entry.
