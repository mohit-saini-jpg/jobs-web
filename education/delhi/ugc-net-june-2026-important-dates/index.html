Detailed Information

The UGC-NET June 2026 examination will be conducted from 22 June 2026 to 30 June 2026. The online application process is open from 29 April 2026 to 20 May 2026. This national-level exam determines eligibility for Junior Research Fellowship (JRF), Assistant Professor, and Ph.D. admission in Indian universities and colleges.

UGC NET Syllabus 2026

Paper I: General Paper on Teaching & Research Aptitude (Common for all candidates). It covers Teaching Aptitude, Research Aptitude, Reasoning, Comprehension, Communication, Mathematical Reasoning, Data Interpretation, Information and Communication Technology (ICT), People and Environment, and Higher Education System.

Paper II: Subject-specific syllabus based on the candidate’s chosen subject (out of 85 subjects). Detailed syllabus for all subjects is available on the official UGC website.
