How to Check IIIT Delhi MTech Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download the IIIT Delhi MTech 2026 merit list and Round 1 allotment in an easy and user-friendly way.

  • Visit the official IIIT Delhi website - https://iiitd.ac.in/
  • On the homepage, click on the 'Admissions' section
  • From the dropdown, select 'MTech' to open the MTech admissions 2026 page
  • Scroll down to the announcements or important links section
  • Click on the link for 'MTech Merit List 2026' or 'Round 1 Seat Allotment'
  • The PDF file will open - download it to your device
  • Search your application number and name in the PDF to check your merit rank and allotment status
  • Follow the instructions given in the PDF regarding fee payment and last date to accept the Round 1 offer
