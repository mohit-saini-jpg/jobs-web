How to Download SGGU Result 2026 Online

SGGU provides results both via notification PDFs and through an online result portal where students can check their individual marks using login credentials. The steps below outline how to access D.S.I. Semester 1 January 2026 results efficiently.

Step-by-step Process to Check SGGU D.S.I. 1st Semester Result 2026

  1. Visit the official SGGU result page at https://sggu.ac.in/Results.php.
  2. On the results page, look for the link or entry corresponding to D.S.I. Semester 1 January 2026 (ICRP or ERP), as notified in the circulars.
  3. Click on the relevant exam link to open the result query or download page.
  4. If prompted, enter your roll number, registration number or other required credentials in the specified fields.
  5. Double-check the details entered and click on the “Submit” or “View Result” button.
  6. Your SGGU Result 2026 for D.S.I. Semester 1 will be displayed on the screen, showing subject-wise marks, total marks and result status (Pass/Fail).
  7. Download or print the online mark sheet for future academic use and record-keeping.
