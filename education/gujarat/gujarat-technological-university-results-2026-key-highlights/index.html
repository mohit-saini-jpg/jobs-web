How to Download GTU Sem 1 Result 2026

Students can check GTU Sem 1 Result 2026 for MBA, BA and MCA through the university’s official result portal. The process involves choosing the correct exam link and entering enrollment details.

Follow these steps to check GTU Sem 1 Result 2026:

  1. Visit the official website of Gujarat Technological University at gtu.ac.in.
  2. Click on the “Result” link or directly go to https://www.gtu.ac.in/result.aspx.
  3. On the result page, look for the appropriate 1st Semester result link such as: Result of MBA Sem 1 - Remedial (Dec 2025) Result of BA Sem 1 - Regular (Dec 2025) Re-check/Re-Assess Result of MCA Sem 1 - Regular/Remedial (Dec 2025) Re-check/Re-Assess
  4. Result of MBA Sem 1 - Remedial (Dec 2025)
  5. Result of BA Sem 1 - Regular (Dec 2025) Re-check/Re-Assess
  6. Result of MCA Sem 1 - Regular/Remedial (Dec 2025) Re-check/Re-Assess
  7. Click on the relevant link to open the result server page (Default or Recheck module).
  8. On the result screen, enter your Enrollment Number, Seat Number and any other required details.
  9. Click on “Search” or “View Result” to display your GTU Sem 1 Result 2026.
  10. Check subject-wise marks, total marks, result status (Pass/Fail/Remedial) and any updated changes after re-check/re-assess.
  11. Download or print the marksheet for your records and future reference.
  12. If you wish to apply for online re-check/re-assess (where applicable), use the link and instructions mentioned on the result page before the last date (for example, 10 April 2026 for certain exams).
