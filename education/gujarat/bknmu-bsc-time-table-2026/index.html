How to Download BKNMU B.Sc Time Table 2026

Students can download the BKNMU B.Sc Time Table 2026 from the official university website by following the steps given below:

  • Visit the official website of BKNMU.
  • Go to the News & Events or Time Table section.
  • Look for the B.Sc Nursing Semester 1 or Semester 3 May 2026 timetable link.
  • Click on the respective semester timetable.
  • The PDF will open in a new tab. Download and save the file.
  • Take a printout for easy reference during your exam preparation.
