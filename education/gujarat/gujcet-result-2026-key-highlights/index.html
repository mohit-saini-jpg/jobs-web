Details

The Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board (GSEB) has officially released the GUJCET Result 2026 on May 04, 2026, at 10:00 AM for the Gujarat Common Entrance Test conducted on March 29, 2026. The board has declared results for candidates appearing for undergraduate admission to B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology) and B.Pharm (Bachelor of Pharmacy) programs across various engineering and pharmacy colleges in Gujarat state. GUJCET Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - gseb.org.

Students who appeared for the entrance examination can check and download their results by logging into the official portal using their six-digit seat number mentioned on the GUJCET hall ticket as the primary credential. The result displays subject-wise marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology along with total marks out of 120, percentile rank, and overall GUJCET rank. Candidates are advised to verify all details mentioned in their rank card including name, seat number, group, subject-wise marks, total obtained marks, and percentile rank, and contact the GSEB authorities in case of any discrepancies or errors.

GUJCET Result 2026 Key Highlights

The Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board has successfully published the GUJCET Result 2026 on its official portal today after a gap of 36 days from the examination date. Students can access their rank cards by entering their six-digit seat number on gseb.org or gujcet.gseb.org, and the result is available for download in PDF format for future reference and counselling purposes. The GUJCET 2026 result will be used along with Class 12th marks in the ratio of 40:60 respectively to prepare the provisional merit list for ACPC counselling scheduled for June 2026.

How to Check GUJCET Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their GUJCET Result 2026 from the official website:

  1. Visit the official website of Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board at gseb.org or gujcet.gseb.org
  2. On the homepage, locate and click on the GUJCET Result 2026 link
  3. A new login page will appear on the screen
  4. Enter your six-digit Seat Number as mentioned on the GUJCET hall ticket 2026
  5. Click on the Go or Submit button to proceed
  6. Your GUJCET Result 2026 rank card will be displayed on the screen in PDF format
  7. Check all details carefully including candidate name, seat number, group (Engineering/Pharmacy), subject-wise marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology
  8. Verify the total obtained marks, total marks of the question paper (120), and percentile rank
  9. Download the GUJCET rank card PDF and save it on your device
  10. Take a printout of the rank card for ACPC counselling registration and future reference
