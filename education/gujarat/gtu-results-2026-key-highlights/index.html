How to Check GTU Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your GTU BB SEM 1 Re-check Re-Assess Result 2026 online:
  1. Visit the official Gujarat Technological University result portal at gtu.ac.in/result.aspx or gturesults.in
  2. On the result page, look for the BB SEM 1 result links from the Winter 2025 (December 2025) examination session
  3. Click on the appropriate result link based on your examination category - either BB SEM 1 Regular (Dec 2025) Re-check Re-Assess or BB SEM 1 Remedial (Dec 2025) Re-check Re-Assess
  4. You will be redirected to the GTU results server page at gturesults.in
  5. Enter your enrollment number in the first required field
  6. Enter your student portal password in the password field
  7. Type the captcha code displayed on the screen in the captcha field
  8. Click on the "Submit" or "Show Result" button to proceed
  9. Your GTU Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen showing subject-wise revised marks after re-check and re-assessment, grades, and overall updated result status
  10. Check all marks, subject codes, personal details, and semester information carefully
  11. Download the result page or take a screenshot for future reference
  12. You can also take a printout of the result for admission, verification, or record-keeping purposes
  13. Save the downloaded marksheet in a secure location
  14. If you find any discrepancies or have queries, contact the university within 15 days from the result declaration date
