Maharaja Krishnakumarsinhji Bhavnagar University has released MKBU Result 2026 for various UG and PG courses for the MAR-APR-MAY 2026 examination session, including PG DMLT Sem 1, B.Sc IT (New CBCS) Sem 3 and B.Ed (New CBCS) Sem 1 press notifications. MKBU Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - mkbhavuni.edu.in - through the university result portal mkbhavuni.edu.in/mkbhavuniweb/result.php, where course wise press notes and result links are provided.

Students from PG DMLT, B.Sc IT (New CBCS) and B.Ed (New CBCS) can now open the respective press notification PDFs for MAR-APR-MAY 2026, check the result details and use the indicated procedure or link to view their individual marks. After accessing the result, candidates should verify their name, seat number, course, semester and subject wise marks carefully and contact the university examination section or their college in case of any discrepancy.

How to Download MKBU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your MKBU Result 2026 for PG DMLT, B.Sc IT or B.Ed:

  • Open the MKBU result portal: https://www.mkbhavuni.edu.in/mkbhavuniweb/result.php.
  • On the result page, locate the 2026 MAR-APR-MAY session and choose the relevant course/semester, for example: 2105 - PG DMLT Sem 1 2067 - B.Sc IT (New CBCS) Sem 3 112 - B.Ed (New CBCS) Sem 1.
  • 2105 - PG DMLT Sem 1
  • 2067 - B.Sc IT (New CBCS) Sem 3
  • 112 - B.Ed (New CBCS) Sem 1.
  • If required, first open the PRESS NOTI PDF for your course to confirm that results for your programme and semester are declared.
  • Use the provided link or form on the result page to enter your Seat Number / Roll Number / Exam Code as specified.
  • Submit the details to view your subject wise marks, total marks, grade/SGPA and result status (Pass/Fail/ATKT etc.).
  • Download or print the online marksheet or result page and keep it for future academic use or verification.
