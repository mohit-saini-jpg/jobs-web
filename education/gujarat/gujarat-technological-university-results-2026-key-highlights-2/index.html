Steps to Check GTU Result 2026

Students can check GTU Result 2026 online through the official GTU result portal. The system typically requires candidates to select the exam, enter their enrolment or seat number, and submit the form to view the result. For BB Sem 1, there is also an option to apply online for re-checking or re-assessment within the specified last date.

  • Visit the official Gujarat Technological University website - https://www.gtu.ac.in.
  • Click on the "Results" section or directly open https://www.gtu.ac.in/result.aspx.
  • On the result page, locate the links for "Result of BB SEM 1 - Regular (DEC 2025) Exam", "Result of BB SEM 1 - Remedial (DEC 2025) Exam" or "Result of MBA SEM 1 - Regular (DEC 2025) Exam".
  • Click on the relevant result link (it will open the gturesults.in page for that exam).
  • On the result page, select the required details if prompted and enter your enrolment number or seat number.
  • Submit the details to view your GTU result on the screen.
  • Check your subject-wise marks, SPI/CPI where applicable, and download or print the result for future use.
  • If eligible and required, use the online portal link for re-check/re-assess application before the last date mentioned (such as 11-04-2026 for BB Sem 1).
