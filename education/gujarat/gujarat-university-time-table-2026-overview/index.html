How to Download Gujarat University Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the Gujarat University Time Table 2026 PDF from the official university website. Follow these simple steps to access your exam schedule quickly.

  • Visit the official RGPV or Gujarat University examination portal.
  • Locate the Time Table or Examination Schedule section on the homepage.
  • Select your program (BTech), semester (VII or VIII), and session (May 2026).
  • Click on the relevant link for your course, such as CSBS or Working Professional.
  • The PDF will open – review the subject-wise dates and timings.
  • Download the file and take a printout for daily reference.

Carefully check all dates and instructions in the official timetable. Keep a copy handy for effective exam preparation and stay updated through the university website for any revisions.
