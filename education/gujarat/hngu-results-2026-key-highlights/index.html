Detailed Information

Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University has released HNGU Result 2026 for M.P.Ed Semester 3 (Regular), M.Sc Semester 3 (Inorganic Chemistry, Organic Chemistry, Physical Chemistry, Physics Chemistry - Repeater) and B.Sc Semester 3 examinations conducted in October 2025. HNGU Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - ngu.ac.in - through the dedicated results portal where students can select their exam and view marks using basic login details.

To access their results, candidates must open the university results page, choose the relevant October 2025 examination (M.P.Ed Sem 3, M.Sc Sem 3 repeater branches, or B.Sc Sem 3) and enter their seat or roll number as required. After downloading the online marksheet, students should carefully verify their name, course, semester, subject wise marks and result status, and immediately contact the HNGU examination branch or their respective college in case of any discrepancy in details or marks.

How to Check HNGU Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your HNGU October 2025 Result 2026:

  • Visit the HNGU results section at https://www.ngu.ac.in/Results.aspx.
  • Click on the link that leads to the online Exam Result page or directly open http://ngu.ac.in/ExamResult.Aspx.
  • On the Exam Result page, select the Exam Year/Month (October 2025) and choose your Course and Semester, such as: M.P.Ed Semester 3 (Regular) M.Sc Semester 3 (Inorganic / Organic / Physical / Physics Chemistry - Repeater) B.Sc Semester 3.
  • M.P.Ed Semester 3 (Regular)
  • M.Sc Semester 3 (Inorganic / Organic / Physical / Physics Chemistry - Repeater)
  • B.Sc Semester 3.
  • After selecting the appropriate exam, enter your Seat Number / Roll Number / Student ID in the given field.
  • Click on View Result / Show Result to display your online marksheet.
  • Check your name, seat number, course, semester, subject wise marks or grades, total marks and result status carefully.
  • Use the print or download options on the page to save a copy of your result for future references like higher studies, scholarships or job applications.
