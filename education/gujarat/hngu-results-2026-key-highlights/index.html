How to Check HNGU Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your HNGU October 2025 Result 2026:

  1. Visit the HNGU results section at https://www.ngu.ac.in/Results.aspx.
  2. Click on the link that leads to the online Exam Result page or directly open http://ngu.ac.in/ExamResult.Aspx.
  3. On the Exam Result page, select the Exam Year/Month (October 2025) and choose your Course and Semester, such as: M.P.Ed Semester 3 (Regular) M.Sc Semester 3 (Inorganic / Organic / Physical / Physics Chemistry - Repeater) B.Sc Semester 3.
  4. M.P.Ed Semester 3 (Regular)
  5. M.Sc Semester 3 (Inorganic / Organic / Physical / Physics Chemistry - Repeater)
  6. B.Sc Semester 3.
  7. After selecting the appropriate exam, enter your Seat Number / Roll Number / Student ID in the given field.
  8. Click on View Result / Show Result to display your online marksheet.
  9. Check your name, seat number, course, semester, subject wise marks or grades, total marks and result status carefully.
  10. Use the print or download options on the page to save a copy of your result for future references like higher studies, scholarships or job applications.

How to Check HNGU Result 2026

Students can check HNGU Result 2026 online using the official results portal of Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University. The process is simple and requires only basic exam and candidate details.

Follow these steps to check HNGU Result 2026:

  1. Visit the official website of Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University at ngu.ac.in.
  2. On the homepage, find and click on the “Results” section, or directly open https://www.ngu.ac.in/Results.aspx.
  3. From the results page, click on the link that redirects you to the examination result server (such as http://ngu.ac.in/ExamResult.Aspx).
  4. On the result server page, select the appropriate exam details, such as: Course: M.D. Homeopathic Part-II or S.Y.B.D.S. Exam Month/Year: February 2026 (or relevant option shown).
  5. Course: M.D. Homeopathic Part-II or S.Y.B.D.S.
  6. Exam Month/Year: February 2026 (or relevant option shown).
  7. Enter your Seat Number/Roll Number or any other required credential exactly as printed on your admit card.
  8. Click on the “Submit” or “View Result” button to display your HNGU Result 2026 on the screen.
  9. Check your subject-wise marks, total marks, result status and other details, and then download or print the online marksheet for future use.
  10. If any error is noticed in your marks or personal details, contact your college examination cell or the HNGU examination department with supporting documents.
