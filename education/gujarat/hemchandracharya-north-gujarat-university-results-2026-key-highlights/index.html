Steps to Check HNGU B.Sc Result 2026 Online

HNGU provides an online system where students can search for their semester results using their seat number and exam selection. The process is straightforward and can be completed in a few steps.

Step-by-step Process to Check HNGU B.Sc Nursing Result 2026

  • Visit the official Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University result portal at https://www.ngu.ac.in/Results.aspx.
  • On the results page, look for the link or dropdown related to “Result December 2025” or the specific exam listing for B.Sc Nursing or Post Basic B.Sc Nursing.
  • If you are using the direct exam result link, open http://ngu.ac.in/ExamResult.Aspx where the December 2025 nursing result is hosted.
  • Select the appropriate exam name from the list, such as “B.Sc Nursing Semester-2”, “B.Sc Nursing Semester-3” or “F.Y-S.Y Post Basic B.Sc Nursing”.
  • Enter your seat number or roll number as printed on your hall ticket in the input box provided.
  • Double-check the entered details and click on the “Submit” or “View Result” button.
  • Your HNGU B.Sc Result 2026 for the selected nursing exam will be displayed on the screen, showing subject-wise marks, total marks and result status (Pass/Fail).
  • Download the result or print a copy of the marksheet for future reference and documentation purposes.
