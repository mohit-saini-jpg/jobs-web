How to Download MKBU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download MKBU Result 2026 for B.Com Honours Sem 3 and BA Sem 1 NEP reassessment:

  • Go to the MKBU result portal: https://www.mkbhavuni.edu.in/mkbhavuniweb/result.php.
  • On the result page, look for the 2026 result section and locate entries dated 04-04-2026 for: 2040-B.Com.(Honours) Sem-3 MAR-APR-MAY-2026 PRESS NOTI 2127-B.A. SEM-1 [NEP] SEPT-OCT-NOV-DEC-2025 REASSESSMENT
  • 2040-B.Com.(Honours) Sem-3 MAR-APR-MAY-2026 PRESS NOTI
  • 2127-B.A. SEM-1 [NEP] SEPT-OCT-NOV-DEC-2025 REASSESSMENT
  • Click on the respective result link to open the PDF notification/result file.
  • In the PDF, use the search function (Ctrl+F) and enter your seat number/roll number to find your record.
  • Check your name, seat number, course, semester, subject wise marks/grade and overall result status.
  • Download or print the PDF for your records, future admissions or any verification purpose.
