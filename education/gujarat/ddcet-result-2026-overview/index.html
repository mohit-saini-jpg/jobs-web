Detailed Information

DDCET Result 2026 has been declared by ACPC Gujarat, and candidates can now download their rank card and merit list from the official website. The Diploma to Degree Common Entrance Test (DDCET) 2026 score will be used for lateral entry admissions into second year Engineering and Pharmacy degree programmes in Gujarat.

Candidates who appeared for the exam can check their DDCET 2026 result online using their application number or roll number and verify their marks, rank, and qualification status. Along with the individual rank card, ACPC has also released the DDCET 2026 common merit list, which will be considered for the upcoming online counselling and seat allotment rounds.

How to Check DDCET Result 2026 Online

This is a core informational section; use a step-by-step list.

Steps to Download DDCET 2026 Rank Card from ACPC Website

  • Visit the official ACPC website acpc.gujarat.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the “List of Available Courses” and click “DDCET 2026”.
  • Click on the “Download DDCET Rank Card 2026” link.
  • Enter your application number or roll number and any required details.
  • Click on “Search” or “Submit” to view your DDCET score and rank.
  • Download and save the DDCET 2026 rank card as a PDF for future reference.

How to Check DDCET 2026 Merit List

  • Open the merit list link: https://acpc.gujarat.gov.in/merit_list_ddcet.
  • Select your course/branch if the page provides multiple options.
  • View or download the common merit list PDF.
  • Use Ctrl+F to search by your name, application number, or roll number.
