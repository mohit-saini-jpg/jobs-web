Steps to Check Panjab University Exam Result 2026 Online

Panjab University provides an online result portal where students can check their semester-wise results by entering examination credentials. MA Sanskrit 3rd Semester students can easily view their Panjab University Exam Result 2026 by following the steps given below.

Step by Step Process to Check Panjab University Result 2026

  • Visit the official Panjab University examination result portal at https://results.puexam.in/HomePage.aspx?Sess=qRwW+GGKc3U=.
  • On the homepage of the result portal, look for the relevant result link for your course, for example "Master of Arts (Sanskrit) 3rd Semester Examination December 2025".
  • Alternatively, directly click on the MA Sanskrit 3rd Semester result link provided above to open the result page.
  • On the result page, you will find fields to enter your roll number and any other required details.
  • Enter your correct roll number as mentioned on your admit card and double-check the input.
  • Click on the "Show Result" or "Submit" button to proceed.
  • Your Panjab University Exam Result 2026 for MA Sanskrit 3rd Semester will be displayed on the screen, showing subject-wise marks, total marks and result status (Pass/Fail/Re-appear).
  • Download the result or take a printout for your records and for use in future academic processes like admission to the next semester or document verification.
