How to Check the Panjab University Results 2025?

Panjab University announces its results online through the official website. To access their results, students must visit the website and locate the designated result link. Since the results are not publicly available, students are required to enter their roll number to view their individual scores.

  • Visit the official Panjab University results portal: https://results.puexam.in or the university website https://puchd.ac.in.​
  • On the homepage, look for the relevant “Results” or “Examination” section.
  • Select your course/semester link (for example M.Sc., etc.).​
  • Enter your roll number and any other credentials as requested.​
  • Click “Submit” or “Show Result” to view your marksheet and overall result.​
  • Download or print your official PDF marksheet for reference.
