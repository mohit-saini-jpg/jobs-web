Panjab University results for 2026 are declared online through the official results portal at results.puchd.ac.in, where students can access UG, PG, and other course results by entering their roll number.

How to Check Panjab University Result 2026 Online

  • Visit the official results portal: Click Here.
  • On the homepage, select “Semester Results” (or “Entrance Results” if applicable).
  • Choose your course (BA, B.Sc, M.Sc, B.Com, etc.).
  • Select the relevant semester/examination session.
  • Enter your roll number in the provided field.
  • Click “Show Result” or “Submit.”
  • Your result will appear on the screen – download or print it for future reference.

Tip: If the website is slow during peak hours, try accessing it early morning or late evening.

Revaluation and Supplementary Examination Details

  • Revaluation: Apply online within 15–20 days of result declaration. Fee usually ranges between ₹500–₹1000 per subject.
  • Supplementary Exams: Students who fail in one or more subjects can appear in the next semester cycle.
  • Golden Chance Exams: Additional attempt opportunity for backlog clearance. Notifications for 2026 are expected in April–May.

Detailed application forms and guidelines are available in the examination section of the university website.
