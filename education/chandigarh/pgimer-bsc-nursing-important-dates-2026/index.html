PGIMER BSc Nursing Eligibility Criteria 2026
  • Educational Qualification: Candidates must have passed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English.
  • Minimum Marks: Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST candidates).
  • Age Limit: As per PGIMER guidelines (check official prospectus).
  • Other: Only female candidates are eligible for B.Sc. Nursing (4 years) course.

How to Apply for PGIMER BSc Nursing 2026
  1. Visit the official website pgimer.edu.in.
  2. Click on the link for B.Sc. Nursing Application Form 2026.
  3. Register by providing basic details such as email ID and mobile number.
  4. Fill the online application form with personal, academic and contact details.
  5. Upload scanned documents, photograph and signature.
  6. Pay the application fee online.
  7. Submit the form and take a printout for future reference.

Apply Online Link: Will be available on the official PGIMER website (pgimer.edu.in) once released.

Documents Required for PGIMER Nursing Registration 2026

  • Scanned signature of the candidate
  • Candidate’s category certificate (if applicable)
  • Mark sheet and certificate of Class 10 and 12
  • Any other document as mentioned in the prospectus
