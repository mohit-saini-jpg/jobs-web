How to Check Panjab University Result 2025?

Students can download their Panjab University Result 2025 mark sheet by following this simple process:

  • Visit the official results portal: puchd.ac.in
  • Select your course: "Bachelor of Arts (FYUP) 2nd Sem" or "B.Sc. (Hons.) Fashion & Lifestyle Tech (FYUP) 4th Sem".
  • Enter your Roll Number and Captcha Code.
  • Click "Submit" to view your mark sheet/grade card.
  • Download the PDF and take a printout for records.

Note: Provisional results; original mark sheets from college. Merit lists available in PDF.
