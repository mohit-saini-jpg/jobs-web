How to Check Panjab University Result 2025?

Students can download their Panjab University Result 2025 mark sheet by following this simple process:

  1. Visit the official results portal: puchd.ac.in
  2. Select your course: "Bachelor of Arts (FYUP) 2nd Sem" or "B.Sc. (Hons.) Fashion & Lifestyle Tech (FYUP) 4th Sem".
  3. Enter your Roll Number and Captcha Code.
  4. Click "Submit" to view your mark sheet/grade card.
  5. Download the PDF and take a printout for records.

Note: Provisional results; original mark sheets from college. Merit lists available in PDF.
