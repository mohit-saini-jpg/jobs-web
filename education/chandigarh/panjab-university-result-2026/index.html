  • Open your browser and visit the official Panjab University result portal: https://results.puexam.in/.
  • On the homepage, look for the link related to “B.Sc. (Honours) Geology (FYP) 1st Semester Special Chance Examination July 2025” under the current or relevant session/year.
  • Click on the specific result link for this exam.
