How to Download Punjab University Chandigarh Date Sheet 2026

Students can download the Punjab University Chandigarh Date Sheet 2026 directly from the official examination portal. Follow these simple steps to get your timetable quickly.

  1. Go to the official Panjab University Examination website at https://exams.puchd.ac.in/datesheet.php?dsheet=1.
  2. Select the appropriate category such as Undergraduate Courses, Postgraduate Courses, or NEP.
  3. Locate your specific course and semester in the list.
  4. Click on the date sheet title or PDF link for your programme.
  5. The PDF will open. Save it to your device and take a printout if needed.
  6. Read all instructions mentioned in the date sheet carefully.

Always refer to the latest official date sheet and keep a copy for your exam preparation and reference.
