WBJEE 2026 Answer Key Objection - Dates, Fee, and Process

This section targets “how to challenge WBJEE answer key” intent.

WBJEE 2026 answer key objection window and fee

Explain in 2-3 sentences:

  • WBJEEB opens an online objection window for a limited time after releasing the provisional answer key.
  • During this period, candidates can challenge any answer they believe is incorrect by paying a fixed objection fee per question (for example, ₹500; confirm from official notice before publishing).
  • The fee is usually non‑refundable, even if the challenge is accepted, so students should file only well‑supported objections.

Step‑by‑step process to challenge WBJEE 2026 provisional answer key

  1. Visit the WBJEE 2026 candidate login portal from the official wbjeeb.nic.in website.
  2. Log in using your application number and password/date of birth.
  3. Click on the link “Challenge Provisional Answer Key” or similar.
  4. Select the question ID(s) for which you want to raise an objection.
  5. Choose the correct option as per your understanding and upload supporting documents (book references, official solutions, etc.) in the required format.
  6. Pay the objection fee for each challenged question via the available online payment modes.
  7. Submit the form and download or print the acknowledgement for your records.

Add a note that objections submitted without proof, beyond the deadline or without successful payment are likely to be rejected.

WBJEE 2026 exam at a glance

  • WBJEE (West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination) is a state‑level entrance test for admission to undergraduate Engineering, Technology, Pharmacy and Architecture programmes in participating institutes of West Bengal.
  • The exam is conducted by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) in offline (OMR‑based) mode.
  • Typically, there are separate papers for Mathematics and Physics & Chemistry, with multiple‑choice questions and negative marking.
