3paragraphExplain in 2-3 sentences:
4listunordered
  • WBJEEB opens an online objection window for a limited time after releasing the provisional answer key.
  • During this period, candidates can challenge any answer they believe is incorrect by paying a fixed objection fee per question (for example, ₹500; confirm from official notice before publishing).
  • The fee is usually non‑refundable, even if the challenge is accepted, so students should file only well‑supported objections.
5paragraphStep‑by‑step process to challenge WBJEE 2026 provisional answer key
6listordered
  • Visit the WBJEE 2026 candidate login portal from the official wbjeeb.nic.in website.
  • Log in using your application number and password/date of birth.
  • Click on the link “Challenge Provisional Answer Key” or similar.
  • Select the question ID(s) for which you want to raise an objection.
  • Choose the correct option as per your understanding and upload supporting documents (book references, official solutions, etc.) in the required format.
  • Pay the objection fee for each challenged question via the available online payment modes.
  • Submit the form and download or print the acknowledgement for your records.
