Sidho-Kanho-Birsha University (SKBU) has officially released the UG 1st Semester (NEP) Result 2026. The results for the 1st Semester Under NEP pattern have been declared for undergraduate courses.

SKBU Result 2026 has been released online on the official website – students.skbu.ac.in. Students can check and download their results using their login credentials on the university result portal. It is advised to verify all details mentioned in the marksheet carefully and contact the university examination department in case of any discrepancies.
