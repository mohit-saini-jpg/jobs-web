How to Download Ranaghat College Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your Ranaghat College UG 5th Semester Result 2026 online:

  1. Visit the official Ranaghat College website ranaghatcollege.org.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the Examination Result link or directly go to the result page at frmexamresult.aspx.
  3. Look for the latest links for UG 5th Semester BA, BSc, BCom 2025 (CBCS) and UG 5th Semester Result 2025 (NEP).
  4. Click on the relevant result link for your course and stream (Honours or General).
  5. The result file or marksheet (usually in Excel/PDF format) will open or start downloading on your device.
  6. Open the file to view your subject wise marks, total marks and result status.
  7. Download and save the Ranaghat College Result 2026 and take a printout for future academic reference and verification.
