How to Download Ranaghat College Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your Ranaghat College UG 5th Semester Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official Ranaghat College website ranaghatcollege.org.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the Examination Result link or directly go to the result page at frmexamresult.aspx.
  • Look for the latest links for UG 5th Semester BA, BSc, BCom 2025 (CBCS) and UG 5th Semester Result 2025 (NEP).
  • Click on the relevant result link for your course and stream (Honours or General).
  • The result file or marksheet (usually in Excel/PDF format) will open or start downloading on your device.
  • Open the file to view your subject wise marks, total marks and result status.
  • Download and save the Ranaghat College Result 2026 and take a printout for future academic reference and verification.
