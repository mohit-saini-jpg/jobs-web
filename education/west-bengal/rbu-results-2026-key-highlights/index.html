Details

The Rabindra Bharati University (RBU) Result 2026 for MA 3rd Semester Exam 2025 has been published for three important programmes: Instrumental Music (Percussion) (CBCS) and Rabindra Sangeet (CBCS) under the Faculty of Fine Arts, and Hindi (CBCS) under the Faculty of Arts. These latest RBU MA 3rd Sem results 2025 have been made available as official PDF result sheets on the university’s Examination portal, allowing students to check their roll numbers, grades and overall result status in one click.

Candidates can now visit the Rabindra Bharati University website rbu.ac.in and open the Examinations page https://www.rbu.ac.in/home/examinations/ to download the MA 3rd semester result PDFs for Instrumental Music (Percussion), Rabindra Sangeet and Hindi.

How to Check Rabindra Bharati University MA 3rd Sem Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your RBU MA 3rd Sem Result 2025 (CBCS) for Instrumental Music (Percussion), Rabindra Sangeet or Hindi from the official Rabindra Bharati University website.

  1. Open the Rabindra Bharati University website https://www.rbu.ac.in in your browser.
  2. From the homepage, go to the “Examination”/ “Examinations” section or directly visit https://www.rbu.ac.in/home/examinations/.
  3. In the list of recent examination notices and results, look for the entries: “Result of MA 3rd Sem Exam 2025 in Instrumental Music (Percussion) (CBCS) under Faculty of Fine Arts” “Result of MA 3rd Sem Exam 2025 in Rabindra Sangeet (CBCS) under Faculty of Fine Arts” “Result of MA 3rd Sem Exam 2025 in Hindi (CBCS) under Faculty of Arts”
  4. “Result of MA 3rd Sem Exam 2025 in Instrumental Music (Percussion) (CBCS) under Faculty of Fine Arts”
  5. “Result of MA 3rd Sem Exam 2025 in Rabindra Sangeet (CBCS) under Faculty of Fine Arts”
  6. “Result of MA 3rd Sem Exam 2025 in Hindi (CBCS) under Faculty of Arts”
  7. Click on the relevant result link; the official RBU result PDF for that course will open in a new tab.
  8. Inside the PDF, use the search option (Ctrl+F) and enter your roll number / examination number to quickly find your result entry.
  9. Check your roll number, course, semester and result status (Pass/Fail, SGPA/grade where applicable) carefully.
  10. Download or print the PDF for your records; you can also keep a soft copy for future reference while applying for higher studies, scholarships or review/revaluation if RBU allows it.
