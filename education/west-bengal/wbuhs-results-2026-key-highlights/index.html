Detailed Information

The West Bengal University of Health Sciences has officially released the WBUHS Result 2026 for several undergraduate and postgraduate health sciences examinations for the latest academic sessions. The newly published updates include Addendum Result of 2nd BHMS (CBDC) November-December 2025 Examination, Addendum Result of 4th Professional BDS January-February 2026 Examination, Review/Scrutiny Result of Bachelor of Pharmacy 5th Semester December 2025 - January 2026 Examination, Addendum Result of MBBS 2nd Professional January 2026 Examination, Review/Scrutiny Result of M.Sc Nursing Part I November 2025 Examination, Result of Final BOT December 2025 - January 2026 Examination, Result of B.Sc in Medical Laboratory Technology 3rd Year December 2025 - January 2026 Examination, and Result of Master of Hospital Administration 4th Semester November-December 2025 Examination.

West Bengal University of Health Sciences Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - wbuhs.ac.in. Students can check and download their results through the WBUHS examination results page and the Charaka student result portal by entering the required credentials like roll number or registration number, depending on the course and portal.Candidates are advised to download their provisional scorecards or result PDFs and verify their name, course, year, semester/professional part and marks carefully. In case of any discrepancy in the result, they should contact their college or the WBUHS examination section for necessary correction and clarification.
