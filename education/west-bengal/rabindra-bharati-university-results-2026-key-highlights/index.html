1paragraphUse the following steps to check and download your Rabindra Bharati University MA 1st and 3rd semester result:
2listordered
  • Open the official RBU website in your browser: https://www.rbu.ac.in/
  • On the homepage, navigate to the "Examinations" section or directly go to https://www.rbu.ac.in/home/examinations/
  • In the examinations/results area, locate the latest result notifications dated 13 April 2026 for: MA 1st Sem Exam 2025 in Museology and Conservation (CBCS) MA 3rd Sem Exam 2025 in Journalism and Mass Communication (CBCS) MA 3rd Sem Exam 2025 in Musicology (CBCS) MA 1st Sem Exam 2025 in Rabindra Sangeet (CBCS) MA 1st Sem Exam 2025 in Dance (CBCS)
  • MA 1st Sem Exam 2025 in Museology and Conservation (CBCS)
  • MA 3rd Sem Exam 2025 in Journalism and Mass Communication (CBCS)
  • MA 3rd Sem Exam 2025 in Musicology (CBCS)
  • MA 1st Sem Exam 2025 in Rabindra Sangeet (CBCS)
  • MA 1st Sem Exam 2025 in Dance (CBCS)
  • Click on the respective result link for your course, which will open a PDF file in a new tab or window.
  • In the result PDF, use the search function (Ctrl+F) and type your roll number or name to quickly find your result entry.
  • Check your course name, semester, roll number, and marks or grade as mentioned in the PDF.
  • Download or print the PDF for future reference, and if any error or discrepancy is noticed, contact the RBU examination department or your department office as soon as possible.
