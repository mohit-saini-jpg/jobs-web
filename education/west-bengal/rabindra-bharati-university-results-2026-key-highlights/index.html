Steps to Check RBU 1st and 3rd Sem Result 2026

Students can check RBU 1st and 3rd Sem Result 2026 online by accessing the official examination page and opening the relevant course-wise PDF results. Since the university publishes results as PDFs, candidates only need to locate their roll number in the list to confirm their marks and status.

  1. Visit the official Rabindra Bharati University examinations page - https://www.rbu.ac.in/home/examinations/.
  2. Under the latest result notifications, find the links for your course, such as MSW 1st Sem, MA 1st Sem Environmental Studies, MA 1st Sem Geography, MA 3rd Sem Political Science or MA 3rd Sem Philosophy.
  3. Click on the relevant result link to open the PDF file in your browser.
  4. Use the search option (Ctrl+F) in the PDF viewer and enter your examination roll number.
  5. Check your name, course, semester, subject-wise marks and result status as shown against your roll number.
  6. Download or print the result PDF for your records and future academic use.
  7. If you find any mistakes in your marks or personal details, contact the university examination branch or your department without delay.
