Steps to Check WBUHS Exam Result 2026

Students can check WBUHS Exam Result 2026 for BDS and M.Sc MLT courses through the university’s online result portals. The process requires correct selection of course/exam and entry of valid exam credentials.

Follow these steps to check WBUHS Exam Result 2026:

  1. Visit the official website of WBUHS at wbuhs.ac.in.
  2. Go to the “Examination Results” section or directly open https://wbuhs.ac.in/examination-results/.
  3. On the results page, locate the links/notifications for: Result of 2nd Professional B.D.S. January - February 2026 Examination Result of M.Sc in Medical Laboratory Technology in Biochemistry Part-II November - December 2025 Examination Result of M.Sc in Medical Laboratory Technology in Microbiology Part-II November - December 2025 Examination
  4. Result of 2nd Professional B.D.S. January - February 2026 Examination
  5. Result of M.Sc in Medical Laboratory Technology in Biochemistry Part-II November - December 2025 Examination
  6. Result of M.Sc in Medical Laboratory Technology in Microbiology Part-II November - December 2025 Examination
  7. Click on the relevant result link to be redirected to the student result status portal at https://charaka.wbuhs.ac.in/StudentResultStatusPublish/index.
  8. On the result portal, choose the appropriate course and examination from the dropdown or list, if required.
  9. Enter your examination credentials such as University Roll Number/Registration Number and any other details asked.
  10. Click on the “View” or “Submit” button to display your WBUHS Exam Result 2026 on the screen.
  11. Check subject-wise marks, total marks, result status (Pass/Fail/Compartment etc.) and download or print the online marks statement for future reference.
  12. Carefully verify your name, roll number, course, year/part and marks; if any discrepancy is found, contact your college or the WBUHS examination branch immediately with supporting documents.
