Presidency University UG Admission 2026

Presidency University UG Admission 2026 online application has started from 19 May 2026 (5 PM onwards). Interested candidates can apply for 4-Year BA and BSc (Honours with Research) programmes till 1 June 2026. Presidency University, Kolkata has invited online applications for admission to Four-Year (Eight Semesters) Bachelor of Arts (BA) and Bachelor of Science (BSc) (Honours with Research) courses for the academic session 2026-2027. Applications are open for candidates who have passed 10+2 in 2025 or later.

The admission process includes two categories – Category A (based on academic score) and Category B (based on admission test). The university follows NEP 2020 guidelines.

Eligibility Criteria for Presidency University UG Admission 2026

Candidates must have passed Higher Secondary (10+2) examination in 2025 or later from a recognized board. There is no age limit. Candidates need to secure minimum aggregate marks as per the course:
-Most subjects: 60% (General), 45% (SC/ST), 54% (OBC), 57% (PwD) -Science subjects (Biotechnology, Chemistry, etc.): 70% (General), 52.50% (SC/ST), 63% (OBC), 66.50% (PwD)
Aggregate is calculated on best four subjects. Candidates must have English as a subject. Detailed eligibility is available in the Information Brochure.

Presidency University UG Application Fee 2026

-Application Fee: Rs. 500/-per application + applicable bank charges -Fee is non-refundable -Payment must be made online during form submission

How to Apply for Presidency University UG Admission 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  1. Visit the official admission portal: presiuniv.ac.in or www.presiuniv.ac.in/web/admission.php
  2. Register with valid email ID and mobile number
  3. Fill the online application form carefully
  4. Upload required documents, photo, and signature
  5. Pay Rs. 500/-application fee
  6. Submit and take a printout of the completed form

  • Recent passport-size photograph

  • Caste/Category certificate (if applicable)

Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Category A Subjects: Admission based on Academic Score (calculated using 10th & 12th marks with specific weightage).
Category B Subjects (History, Political Science, Chemistry, Economics, Life Sciences, Statistics): Admission through Admission Test (50 MCQs, 2 marks for correct answer, 0.5 negative marking).
Exam Pattern: 2-hour test, English medium, OMR sheet.
