JENPAS UG Syllabus 2026

JENPAS UG Syllabus 2026, conducted by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB), serves as the official guide for aspirants seeking admission into BSc Nursing, Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT), Bachelor of Medical Laboratory Technology (BMLT), and various other undergraduate paramedical and allied health courses across West Bengal.

The JENPAS UG 2026 syllabus is divided into two papers: Paper 1 (based on Class 11 and 12 standards) for most courses and Paper 2 (a mix of Class 10–12 levels) exclusively for the Bachelor of Hospital Administration (BHA) course. Knowing the exact topics, exam pattern, and marking scheme helps candidates prepare strategically and maximize their scores in this offline OMR-based entrance test.

Paper 1 Syllabus (Class 11 & 12 Level)

Physics: Physical World and Measurement, Kinematics, Laws of Motion, Work Energy and Power, Gravitation, Properties of Bulk Matter, Thermodynamics, Oscillations and Waves, Electrostatics, Current Electricity, Magnetic Effects of Current and Magnetism, Electromagnetic Induction, Optics, Dual Nature of Matter, Atoms and Nuclei, Electronic Devices.

Chemistry: Some Basic Concepts of Chemistry, Structure of Atom, Classification of Elements, Chemical Bonding, States of Matter, Thermodynamics, Equilibrium, Redox Reactions, s-Block and p-Block Elements, Organic Chemistry – Basic Principles, Hydrocarbons, Solid State, Solutions, Electrochemistry, Chemical Kinetics, Coordination Compounds, Haloalkanes and Haloarenes, Alcohols, Phenols and Ethers, Biomolecules, Polymers.

Biology: Diversity in Living World, Structural Organisation in Animals and Plants, Cell Structure and Function, Plant Physiology, Human Physiology, Reproduction, Genetics and Evolution, Biology and Human Welfare, Biotechnology and its Applications, Ecology and Environment.

Basic English: Grammar (tenses, voice, articles, prepositions, subject-verb agreement), Vocabulary (synonyms, antonyms, idioms), Reading Comprehension, Sentence correction and rearrangement.

Health Aptitude: Basic human anatomy and physiology, Balanced diet and nutrition, Personal and environmental hygiene, Common communicable and non-communicable diseases (causes, prevention), First aid and emergency care, National health programs and healthcare systems.

Paper 2 Syllabus (For BHA Only)

Physical Science (Class 10 Level): Motion, Force and Work, Sound, Light, Electricity and Magnetism, Matter and its properties, Atomic Structure, Chemical Reactions, Acids Bases and Salts, Metals and Non-metals, Carbon Compounds.

Mathematics (Class 10 Level): Number System, Algebra (polynomials, equations), Geometry and Coordinate Geometry, Trigonometry, Mensuration, Statistics and Probability.

General Knowledge (Class 12 Level): Current national and international affairs, Indian History and Freedom Struggle, Geography, Indian Polity and Constitution, Economy, Science and Technology developments.

Basic English (Class 12 Level): Advanced grammar and error detection, Vocabulary building, Reading comprehension and inference, Writing skills.

Logical Reasoning (Class 12 Level): Verbal reasoning (analogies, coding-decoding, blood relations, series), Non-verbal reasoning (patterns, figures), Analytical reasoning (puzzles, syllogisms, data interpretation).

Preparation Tips Using JENPAS UG Syllabus 2026
  • Study Physics, Chemistry, and Biology thoroughly from NCERT Class 11 and 12 textbooks.
  • Practice English and Health Aptitude daily as they are highly scoring with no negative marking in those sections.
  • Solve previous year question papers and take mock tests to improve speed and accuracy.
  • Focus more on Category-2 questions (2 marks each) since they carry no negative marking.
  • For BHA aspirants, strengthen Class 10 level Physical Science and Mathematics along with current affairs.
