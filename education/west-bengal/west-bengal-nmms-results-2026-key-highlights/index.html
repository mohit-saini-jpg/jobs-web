How to Download West Bengal NMMS Result 2026

Follow these steps to check your NMMS Result:

  • Visit the official website - banglarshiksha.wb.gov.in
  • Go to the "Scholarships" section
  • Click on the NMMS Result 2026 link
  • Enter your roll number or required details
  • Click on submit
  • Your result will appear on the screen
  • Download and save the result for future reference
