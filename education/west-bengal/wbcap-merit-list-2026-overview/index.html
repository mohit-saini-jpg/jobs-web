3What Is WBCAP Merit List 2026?
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThe WBCAP 2026 merit list contains the names of shortlisted candidates for undergraduate admissions across 459 government and government-aided colleges in West Bengal.
2listunordered
  • College-wise merit list - Prepared by individual Higher Education Institutions (HEIs)
  • Based on Class 12 marks - Merit rank determined by board examination scores
  • Includes seat allotment - Shows allotted college & course details
  • Provisional status - Candidates can accept seat or choose upgradation
  • Fee payment required - Confirm admission by paying semester fee online
