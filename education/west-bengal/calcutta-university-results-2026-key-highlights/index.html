How to Check Calcutta University Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your Calcutta University BA/BSc Semester 5 Result 2026:

  1. Open the direct result link: https://west-bengal.indiaresults.com/caluniv/ or visit https://caluniv.ac.in/ and navigate to the BA/BSc Semester V result link.
  2. On the result page, choose the search option (for example, Search by Roll Number or Search by Name, if available).
  3. Enter your Roll Number exactly as printed on your admit card, or type your Name as required.
  4. Click on the Get Result / Find Result button to display your marksheet on the screen.
  5. Check your name, roll number, college, course (BA/BSc), stream (Honours/General/Major), semester, subject wise marks and overall result status carefully.
  6. Download or print the online result for your records, revaluation forms, higher studies applications and other academic purposes.
