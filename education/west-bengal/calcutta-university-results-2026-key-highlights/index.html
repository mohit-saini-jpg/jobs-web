Detailed Information

The University of Calcutta has announced Calcutta University Result 2026 for the Four/Three Year B.A./B.Sc. Semester 5 Examination 2025 (Under CCF 2022) and B.A./B.Sc. Semester 5 (Honours/ General/ Major) Examination 2025 (Under CBCS). Calcutta University Result 2026 has been released online on the official examination portal – cuexamwindow.in – with the marks hosted through the partnered result page at IndiaResults.

Students from BA and BSc streams (Honours, General and Major) can now check and download their CU 5th semester results by using the online result link and entering their roll number or other required details. After downloading the marksheet, candidates should carefully verify their name, roll number, college, course, semester, subject wise marks and overall result status and contact their college or university authorities immediately in case of any discrepancy.

How to Check Calcutta University Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your Calcutta University BA/BSc Semester 5 Result 2026:

  • Open the direct result link: https://west-bengal.indiaresults.com/caluniv/ or visit https://caluniv.ac.in/ and navigate to the BA/BSc Semester V result link.
  • On the result page, choose the search option (for example, Search by Roll Number or Search by Name, if available).
  • Enter your Roll Number exactly as printed on your admit card, or type your Name as required.
  • Click on the Get Result / Find Result button to display your marksheet on the screen.
  • Check your name, roll number, college, course (BA/BSc), stream (Honours/General/Major), semester, subject wise marks and overall result status carefully.
  • Download or print the online result for your records, revaluation forms, higher studies applications and other academic purposes.
