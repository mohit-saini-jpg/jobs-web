WBJEE JELET 2026 Exam Date (June 13) - Full Schedule, Registration, Syllabus

WBJEE JELET 2026 exam date is June 13, 2026. This pen-and-paper test by West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) allows lateral entry to the second year of B.Tech/B.Pharm courses in West Bengal. Registration opens online on March 19, 2026.

How to Download WBJEE JELET 2026 Admit Card

Admit cards become available in June 2026 on the official portal. Download using your application number and date of birth or password.

Step-by-Step Guide
  1. Visit wbjeeb.nic.in/jelet.​
  2. Select the "Download Admit Card" option.
  3. Enter your application number, DOB, and password.
  4. Review details on screen and download the PDF.
  5. Take a printout on A4 paper; bring it with photo ID to the exam center.​
Admit Card Details Required

It lists your photo, signature, exam venue, reporting time, and rules like no electronics allowed. Verify all info immediately—no edits after download.​

WBJEE JELET 2026 Registration Process

Applications are online-only via the WBJEEB portal starting March 19.​

Step-by-Step Application Steps
  1. Head to wbjeeb.nic.in/jelet and click "Apply Online."
  2. Complete new registration with email and mobile for credentials.
  3. Log in and enter personal, academic details (e.g., diploma percentage).
  4. Upload passport photo and signature (JPG, 20-50KB).
  5. Pay the fee online (card/net banking).
  6. Submit, download confirmation, and keep it safe.
Application Fee and Correction Window

General fee is ₹500; reserved categories pay ₹400. Corrections open briefly in late March for select fields.​

Eligibility Criteria for WBJEE JELET 2026

Candidates must meet these basics to apply.

  • 3-year diploma in Engineering/Technology with 60% aggregate (45% for reserved).​
  • B.Sc. degree holders eligible for specific B.Tech branches.
  • Minimum age 17 years; no upper limit specified.
  • Indian nationals; West Bengal domicile not mandatory.

Check full norms in the official notification PDF.​
