Steps to Check WBUHS Exam Result 2026

Students can follow the steps below to check WBUHS Exam Result 2026 for the latest allied health and nursing examinations online.

  • Visit the official website of West Bengal University of Health Sciences at wbuhs.ac.in and open the "Examination Results" section, or directly go to https://wbuhs.ac.in/examination-results/.
  • On the results page, look for the published notification for B.Sc OTT Part II, B.Sc CCT Part II, or Post Basic B.Sc Nursing Part I result dated 13-04-2026.
  • Click on the relevant result link, which will redirect you to the Charaka student result portal.
  • Select the correct examination or course from the available options on the result page.
  • Enter your registration number, roll number, date of birth, or any other required credentials correctly.
  • Click on the submit or view result button to display your WBUHS scorecard on the screen.
  • Download and print the provisional marksheet for future reference, and verify all personal and academic details carefully.
