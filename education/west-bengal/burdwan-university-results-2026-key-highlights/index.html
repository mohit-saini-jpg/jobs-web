Detailed Information

Burdwan University has officially released the Burdwan University Result 2026 for various Semester II undergraduate examinations 2025, including both 4 Year Honours Programmes under CCFUP and 3 Year Degree Programmes under CBCS. The newly declared results cover Bachelor of Science (BSc) Honours and General, Bachelor of Commerce (BCom) Honours and General, and Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) along with other CCFUP programmes in Semester II.Burdwan University Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - buruniv.ac.in.

Students can check their Semester II provisional results through the dedicated examination result portal using the common result link provided by the university.To access the result, candidates must visit the Burdwan University result page, select the respective examination option, and enter the required credentials such as roll number or other details as asked on the result interface. After downloading the provisional result, students are advised to cross-check their name, roll number, course, semester, and marks carefully. In case of any discrepancy, they should contact their college or the university examination department for rectification.
