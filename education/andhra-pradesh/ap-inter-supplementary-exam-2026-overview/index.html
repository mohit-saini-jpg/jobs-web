1paragraphWho can appear in AP Inter IPASE 2026?

3listunordered

7AP Inter Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphHall ticket‑related queries are very common and can bring good traffic, so treat this as a separate, clearly marked section.
2paragraphAP Inter supply hall ticket 2026 release
3paragraphThe AP Inter supplementary hall tickets are generally released shortly before the start of the theory exams. Students can download them online or collect them through their colleges, depending on the process followed by BIEAP in that year.
4paragraphSteps to download AP Inter IPASE hall ticket
5listordered
  • Visit the official website bieap.apcfss.in.
  • On the home page, search for a link that reads “IPASE May 2026 Hall Ticket” or “Intermediate Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026”.
  • Select your year (1st year or 2nd year).
  • Enter the required details like your previous hall ticket number, date of birth and captcha/security code.
  • Click on “Download” or “Get hall ticket”.
  • Check the hall ticket for your name, photograph, exam centre, subject list and timings.
  • Take a clear printout and keep multiple copies as backup.
6paragraphRemind students that they must carry the hall ticket to every exam day, otherwise they may not be allowed inside the exam hall.
