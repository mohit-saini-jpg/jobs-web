AP Inter Supplementary Exam 2026 - Overview

The Board of Intermediate Education, Andhra Pradesh (BIEAP), has released the AP Inter supplementary exam dates 2026 for students who could not clear one or more subjects in the regular Inter exams or want to improve their marks. These exams are conducted as Intermediate Public Advanced Supplementary Examinations (IPASE) for both 1st and 2nd year students across the state.
To help students plan better, BIEAP has announced a clear schedule with separate morning and afternoon sessions for each year. Along with theory exams, practical exams and result timelines have also been shared so that candidates can track the complete process from application to result.

Once the regular AP Inter results are declared, BIEAP opens the application window for IPASE 2026 for a short period. Students who want to appear for supplementary exams must apply within this window through their respective junior colleges.

Eligibility for AP Inter Supplementary Exams 2026

Who can appear in AP Inter IPASE 2026?
AP Inter IPASE 2026 exams are mainly for students who:
  • Failed in one or more subjects in AP Inter 1st year or 2nd year regular exams.
  • Want to improve their performance in certain subjects if BIEAP allows improvement / betterment in IPASE for that year.
  • Have appeared for the regular 2026 Intermediate examinations conducted by BIEAP.
The board generally requires students to secure a minimum of 35 marks in each subject and at least 35% aggregate to qualify in Intermediate. Students who fall short of this benchmark in any paper get another chance through the supplementary exams.
Why IPASE 2026 is important for students
For many students, IPASE is a crucial opportunity to avoid losing an entire academic year. Clearing backlogs in the supplementary exams allows them to move on to higher education admissions, professional courses, or competitive exams without delay. A well‑prepared article can reassure students that it is a second chance and provide practical preparation tips.

AP Inter Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026

Hall ticket‑related queries are very common and can bring good traffic, so treat this as a separate, clearly marked section.
AP Inter supply hall ticket 2026 release
The AP Inter supplementary hall tickets are generally released shortly before the start of the theory exams. Students can download them online or collect them through their colleges, depending on the process followed by BIEAP in that year.
Steps to download AP Inter IPASE hall ticket
  1. Visit the official website bieap.apcfss.in.
  2. On the home page, search for a link that reads “IPASE May 2026 Hall Ticket” or “Intermediate Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026”.
  3. Select your year (1st year or 2nd year).
  4. Enter the required details like your previous hall ticket number, date of birth and captcha/security code.
  5. Click on “Download” or “Get hall ticket”.
  6. Check the hall ticket for your name, photograph, exam centre, subject list and timings.
  7. Take a clear printout and keep multiple copies as backup.
Remind students that they must carry the hall ticket to every exam day, otherwise they may not be allowed inside the exam hall.

AP Inter Supplementary Result 2026

Expected result date
IPASE 2026 results are generally declared a few weeks after the supplementary exams finish, often within June. This quick turnaround helps students participate in degree admissions and other counselling processes without much delay.
How to check AP Inter supplementary result online
  1. Visit the official Intermediate results link provided through bieap.apcfss.in.
  2. On the results page, select “Inter 1st Year Advanced Supplementary Results 2026” or “Inter 2nd Year Advanced Supplementary Results 2026”.
  3. Enter your hall ticket number and any other required details.
  4. Click on “Submit” to view your result.
  5. Download or print the online marks memo for immediate use.
  6. Collect the original marksheet and pass certificate from your college when they are issued.
Make it clear that the online result is provisional and that students must preserve both the online printout and the final original document.

Important Instructions for AP Inter IPASE 2026 Candidates

To avoid last‑minute problems, students should follow a few essential instructions:
  • Reach the exam centre at least 30-45 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • Carry your hall ticket, a valid ID and necessary stationery (pens, pencils, geometry tools etc.).
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (where not allowed) or any electronic gadgets into the exam hall.
  • Check your subject code and date properly before leaving home for the exam.
  • Read all instructions printed on the hall ticket and question paper carefully.
  • Use the supplementary exams as a focused opportunity to improve your understanding and marks, rather than treating them casually.
