Direct Link to AP ICET Results 2026 & Rank Card

AP ICET 2026 result and rank card have been released in online mode through the APSCHE CET portal. A dedicated “Result / Rank Card” link is now active on the AP ICET home page at cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/ICET, allowing candidates to directly access their scorecard.

The AP ICET rank card page prompts candidates to enter their registration number and ICET hall ticket number before displaying the scorecard on the screen. After verifying their personal details and marks, candidates should download the AP ICET rank card PDF and save multiple digital copies, as it will be required during counselling registration, document verification and college reporting.

How to Check AP ICET Results 2026 Online

Candidates who appeared for the exam can follow these steps to check their AP ICET Results 2026 and download the rank card:

  • Go to the official AP ICET website: cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/ICET.
  • On the home page, click on the “AP ICET 2026 Result / Rank Card” link.
  • Enter your AP ICET registration number and ICET hall ticket number in the login window.
  • Click on “Submit” or “Get Rank Card” to view your AP ICET 2026 scorecard on the screen.
  • Check your name, hall ticket number, category, section-wise scores, total marks and AP ICET rank carefully.
  • Download the AP ICET rank card as a PDF file and take one or two printouts for future use.

If the website takes time to load due to heavy traffic, candidates are advised to wait for a few minutes, refresh the page, avoid repeated back-to-back logins and access the portal using a stable internet connection.

Details Mentioned on AP ICET 2026 Rank Card

The AP ICET rank card 2026 carries all the key details that will be referred to during counselling and admission. After downloading the document, candidates must verify each entry.

Typically, the AP ICET 2026 rank card mentions:

  • Candidate name
  • Father’s name
  • AP ICET hall ticket number
  • Registration/application number
  • Category (OC/BC/SC/ST)
  • Gender and local/non-local status (if applicable)
  • Section-wise scores: Analytical Ability, Mathematical Ability, Communication Ability
  • Total score out of 200 marks
  • AP ICET 2026 rank
  • Qualifying status as per category-wise minimum marks

If any discrepancy is noticed in the personal details, category or marks, candidates should immediately contact the AP ICET helpdesk through the contact details provided on the official website and follow the instructions given by the authorities.
