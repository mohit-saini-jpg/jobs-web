How to Check AP Class 10 Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the simple step-by-step process given below to check the AP Class 10 Result 2026 online at the official BSEAP result portal.

  1. Visit the official AP SSC result website - results.bse.ap.gov.in or go to bse.ap.gov.in and click on the SSC result link.
  2. On the homepage, click on the link that reads 'AP SSC Results 2026' or 'SSC Public Examinations 2026 - Individual Results'.
  3. You will be redirected to the AP Class 10 result login page.
  4. Enter your SSC hall ticket number / roll number in the specified field carefully.
  5. Submit the details and wait for the system to fetch your AP Class 10 Result 2026.
  6. Your AP SSC 10th result with subject-wise marks, total marks and pass/fail status will appear on the screen.
  7. Download the online marks memo and take a printout of the provisional result for future reference.
