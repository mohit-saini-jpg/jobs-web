1paragraphAndhra Pradesh Education Common Entrance Test (AP EDCET) 2026 is conducted for admission into two‑year Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) courses in colleges of education across Andhra Pradesh. The AP EDCET hall ticket is a mandatory document that confirms a candidate’s registration and provides key details such as exam date, time, and exam centre.
2paragraphWith AP EDCET Hall Ticket 2026 now available online, candidates should download it as soon as possible, verify all the information printed on it, and keep multiple copies safe. This article explains the key highlights of the hall ticket, the exam schedule, how to download it, eligibility snapshot, exam‑day instructions, official links, and important FAQs to fully satisfy user intent.

4AP EDCET 2026 - Course and Eligibility Snapshot
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphEven in an admit‑card article, adding a concise eligibility and course section helps new users understand the context.
2paragraphCourse offered through AP EDCET
3paragraphAP EDCET 2026 is conducted for admission to:
4listunordered
  • Two‑year Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) regular courses offered by universities and affiliated colleges of education in Andhra Pradesh.
5paragraphDifferent subject methodologies are available (such as Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Biological Sciences, Social Studies, English and other relevant subjects), which are allotted based on the candidate’s qualifying degree.
6paragraphBasic eligibility overview
7paragraphIn general, candidates should:
8listunordered
  • Have passed or be appearing in the final year of a Bachelor’s degree or Postgraduate degree (BA, BSc, BCom, BSc(Ag), BBA, BCA, BBM or equivalent) from a recognised university.
  • Have studied relevant subjects for the methodology they are applying for (for example, graduates with Mathematics as a subject may apply for Mathematics methodology).
  • Meet the minimum qualifying marks and age criteria as prescribed in the official AP EDCET notification, with relaxation for reserved categories as per government rules.
9paragraphFor precise and updated eligibility details, candidates must refer to the official AP EDCET information brochure.
5How to Download AP EDCET Hall Ticket 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphDownloading the hall ticket correctly is crucial, as any mistake or delay can create problems on exam day.
2paragraphOfficial websites and hall ticket link
3listunordered
  • APSCHE CETs portal: cets.apsche.ap.gov.in
  • AP EDCET hall ticket page: cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EDCET/Edcet/EDCET_HomePage
4paragraphStep‑by‑step hall ticket download process
5listordered
  • Visit the official website Open your browser and go to cets.apsche.ap.gov.in.
  • Open the AP EDCET section On the homepage, click on the “EDCET” option from the list of entrance tests to open the AP EDCET page.
  • Click on the hall ticket link On the AP EDCET page, click on the link labelled “Download Hall Ticket”, “Hall Ticket” or “Get Hall Ticket”. You can also directly visit the hall ticket URL ending with EDCET_HomePage.
  • Enter your login credentials On the hall ticket login screen, enter the required details, which usually include: Registration / application number Qualifying exam hall ticket number / mobile number (as asked in the form) Date of birth (DD/MM/YYYY)
  • Registration / application number
  • Qualifying exam hall ticket number / mobile number (as asked in the form)
  • Date of birth (DD/MM/YYYY)
  • Submit and view the hall ticket Click on “Submit” / “Get Hall Ticket” to display your AP EDCET Hall Ticket 2026 on the screen.
  • Verify all the details Carefully check your name, photograph, subject methodology, exam date, time, and exam centre address.
  • Download and print Download the hall ticket as a PDF and take one or more printouts on A4 paper. Keep at least one additional copy as backup.
6paragraphIf your details are correct but the hall ticket is not generated, you should check your application status (if such an option is provided) or contact the support details mentioned in the official notification.
6Details Mentioned on AP EDCET Hall Ticket 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphYour AP EDCET 2026 hall ticket contains several important pieces of information. Candidates should verify each of the following:
2listunordered
  • Full name of the candidate.
  • Father’s / mother’s name (if printed).
  • Candidate’s photograph and signature (where provided).
  • Registration / application number and roll number.
  • Category (General / OBC / SC / ST / others, if mentioned).
  • Exam date, session (morning/afternoon), and exam time.
  • Subject methodology applied (e.g., Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Biological Sciences, Social Studies, English, etc.).
  • Exam centre code, centre name and full postal address.
  • Reporting time and instructions on gate closing time.
  • General exam‑day instructions and list of allowed/prohibited items.
3paragraphMinor spelling differences may not cause issues, but any major discrepancy such as wrong subject, wrong category or a completely different name should be reported to the AP EDCET authorities immediately.

8Important Exam‑Day Instructions for AP EDCET 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphA clear set of instructions helps reduce last‑minute confusion and avoids common mistakes.
2listunordered
  • Carry mandatory documents Bring a printed AP EDCET Hall Ticket 2026 and a valid government‑issued photo ID such as Aadhaar card, PAN card, voter ID, driving licence or passport. Some centres may also ask for a recent passport‑size photograph.
  • Arrive early at the exam centre Reach the test centre at least 60 minutes before the reporting time mentioned on the hall ticket. Latecomers may not be allowed to enter once the gates are closed.
  • Avoid prohibited items Do not carry mobile phones, calculators (unless permitted), smart watches, Bluetooth devices, earphones, notes, books or any other electronic gadgets into the exam hall. These items may lead to disqualification.
  • Follow computer‑based test (CBT) instructions AP EDCET is usually conducted in CBT mode. Follow all on‑screen instructions carefully, read the directions before starting the test, and inform the invigilator if there is any technical issue.
  • Maintain exam discipline Any unfair means, impersonation, or disturbance may result in cancellation of your candidature and a possible ban from future exams.
3paragraphAdvise candidates to read and understand all instructions printed on their hall ticket at least a day before the exam.
