How to Download AP Inter Supplementary Exam Time Table 2026

Students can download the AP Inter Supplementary Exam Time Table 2026 from the official board website by following the steps given below:

  • Visit the official website of Board of Intermediate Education, Andhra Pradesh.
  • Navigate to the Examinations or Time Table section.
  • Look for the AP Inter Advanced Supplementary Exam Time Table 2026 link.
  • Click on the relevant 1st Year or 2nd Year timetable PDF.
  • The file will open. Download and save it on your device.
  • Take a printout for ready reference during exam preparation.

Note: Always refer to the official AP Inter Supplementary Exam Time Table 2026 and check all dates and timings carefully before the examinations.
