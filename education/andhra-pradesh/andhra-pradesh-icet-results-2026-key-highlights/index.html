Details

Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) will be releasing the AP ICET 2026 Results for candidates who appeared in the MBA and MCA entrance examination. The results will be declared for the 2026 academic session, allowing students to check their rank cards and qualifying status online.

AP ICET Result 2026 will be released online on the official website - https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/APSCHE/APSCHEHome.aspx. Candidates will be able to check and download their results by entering their hall ticket number and other required login credentials.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned on the result and contact the authorities immediately in case of any discrepancies.

How to Download AP ICET 2026 Result
  1. Visit the official website - https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/APSCHE/APSCHEHome.aspx
  2. Click on the "AP ICET 2026 Result" link on the homepage
  3. Enter your Hall Ticket Number and required details
  4. Click on the submit button
  5. Your AP ICET result will be displayed on the screen
  6. Download and save the rank card for future reference
