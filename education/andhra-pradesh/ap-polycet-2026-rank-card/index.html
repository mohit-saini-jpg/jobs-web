How to Download AP POLYCET 2026 Rank Card Online

Step-by-step process at polycetap.ap.gov.in

  • Go to the official AP POLYCET website in your browser: Click Here
  • On the home page, look for the link titled “AP POLYCET 2026 Result / Rank Card” or “AP POLYCET 2026 Rank Card Download”.
  • Click on this link; you will be redirected to the rank card page (often labelled APSSPRC).
  • Enter your Hall Ticket Number in the required field (and Date of Birth or password, if the page asks for it).
  • Click on the Submit or View Result button to proceed.
  • Your AP POLYCET 2026 Rank Card will appear on the screen, displaying your marks, total score, rank and qualifying status.
  • Download the rank card in PDF format and take printouts to use during counselling and admissions.

Details Mentioned on AP POLYCET 2026 Rank Card

The AP POLYCET 2026 rank card is more than just a marks statement; it serves as the primary document for counselling and seat allotment. Typically, the rank card includes:

  • Candidate’s name, hall ticket number, registration number and photograph.
  • Marks obtained in AP POLYCET 2026, section‑wise or subject‑wise (where applicable) and the total score.
  • Qualifying status, indicating whether the candidate has cleared the minimum required marks.
  • AP POLYCET 2026 rank (state‑wide rank and sometimes category rank), which will be used for counselling and seat allocation.
  • Basic information about the exam and important instructions for counselling, certificate verification and how to preserve the rank card.

Candidates should carefully go through each entry and confirm that there are no major spelling mistakes or mismatches in personal details. In case of serious errors, they should reach out to the exam authorities using the contact details provided in the official notification.
