How to Check Result

Candidates can follow these steps to check and download the AP POLYCET Result 2026 from the official website. The process is simple and requires the hall ticket number and other required login details to access the rank card.

  1. Visit the official AP POLYCET website - polycetap.ap.gov.in.

  3. Enter your hall ticket number in the required field.

  5. Check all the details mentioned on the rank card carefully.
  6. Download and save the result for future admission and counselling reference.
