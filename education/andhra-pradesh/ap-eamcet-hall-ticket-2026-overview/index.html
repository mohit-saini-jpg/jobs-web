Detailed Information

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) Kakinada, on behalf of APSCHE, has activated the AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2026 (AP EAPCET admit card) download link on the official portal cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET. The hall ticket is being released for candidates appearing in the Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test and can be downloaded online only.

As per the official schedule, the AP EAMCET 2026 admit card is available from 28 April 2026 for all registered candidates. AP EAMCET (EAPCET) 2026 will be held in CBT mode for Engineering between 12 and 18 May 2026 and for Agriculture & Pharmacy on 19 and 20 May 2026, as notified by APSCHE.

How To Download AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2026

Candidates can follow these steps to download AP EAPCET Hall Ticket 2026 from the official portal:

  • Visit the official AP EAMCET website: https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET.
  • On the home page, click on the “Download Engineering & Pharmacy Hall Tickets” or “Download Hall Ticket” link.
  • The AP EAMCET hall ticket login window will open. Enter your Registration Number, Qualifying Examination (10+2) Hall Ticket Number and Date of Birth exactly as in your application form.
  • Click on “Submit” / “Download Hall Ticket” to generate your AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2026.
  • Your hall ticket will be displayed on the screen. Verify your name, photograph, exam date, shift and exam centre address carefully.
  • Download the PDF and take at least two A4‑size printouts to carry on exam day and keep a digital copy as backup.

In some updates, APSCHE has also indicated that hall tickets may be accessible through WhatsApp or alternate links, but the official and primary method remains the cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET portal.

Details Mentioned On AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2026

The AP EAMCET 2026 admit card contains crucial information that candidates must verify before the exam:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • AP EAMCET registration number and hall ticket number
  • Course/stream (Engineering or Agriculture & Pharmacy)
  • Exam date, shift (forenoon/afternoon) and exam time
  • Exam centre name, code and full address
  • Reporting time and gate closing time
  • Gender, category and other candidate details
  • Important exam‑day instructions and guidelines

Any discrepancy in the candidate’s name, photograph, stream, exam date or centre should be reported to APSCHE/JNTU Kakinada immediately through the official helpdesk.

Documents Required With AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2026

On the AP EAPCET 2026 exam day, candidates must carry the following to the exam centre:

  • A printed copy of AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2026 (clear A4 printout).
  • One valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Voter ID Driving Licence
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID
  • Driving Licence
  • Any additional documents mentioned in the information bulletin, such as a copy of the filled‑in application or self‑declaration form, if applicable.

Candidates will not be permitted to enter the examination hall without the admit card and valid ID proof.

Exam‑Day Instructions for AP EAMCET 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the AP EAMCET 2026 exams, candidates are advised to follow these exam‑day instructions:

  • Reach the exam centre 1 hour before the reporting time mentioned on the hall ticket to complete verification and seating formalities.
  • Carry only permitted items: admit card, ID proof, and allowed stationery (if any).
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, Bluetooth devices, cameras, or any electronic gadgets inside the examination hall; possession may lead to disqualification.
  • Follow the instructions of invigilators, maintain discipline, and avoid any malpractice.
  • Do not carry bags, wallets, or bulky items; follow the exam‑centre specific baggage rules.
  • Keep the AP EAMCET hall ticket safe even after the exam, as it may be required during counselling and admission.
