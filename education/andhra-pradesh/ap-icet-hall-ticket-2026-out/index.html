How to Download AP ICET Hall Ticket 2026 Online

Downloading the AP ICET Hall Ticket 2026 is a simple online process. Keep your registration details ready and follow the step-by-step instructions given below.

Step-by-step process to download AP ICET 2026 hall ticket

  • Visit the official APSCHE portal Go to the official ICET page at cets.apsche.ap.gov.in in your browser.
  • Open the hall ticket link On the AP ICET home page, look for the “Download Hall Ticket” or “AP ICET 2026 Hall Ticket” link and click on it.
  • Enter login details On the login screen, enter your AP ICET 2026 registration number and date of birth in the specified fields.
  • Submit the details Click on the “Submit” or “Get Hall Ticket” button to proceed.
  • View your AP ICET hall ticket Your AP ICET Hall Ticket 2026 will appear on the screen with your personal details, exam centre, date, and timings.
  • Download and print Download the hall ticket as a PDF file and take at least one or two clear printouts to carry on the exam day.

What to do if you cannot download your hall ticket

If you face any issues while downloading your hall ticket, you can try the following:

  • Re-check your registration number and date of birth for typing errors.
  • Refresh the page or try again after some time if the server is busy.
  • Use a different browser or device if the page is not loading properly.
  • If the problem persists, contact the AP ICET helpdesk or APSCHE support using the details given in the official notification or on the website.

Details Mentioned on AP ICET Hall Ticket 2026

After downloading the AP ICET Hall Ticket 2026, candidates must carefully verify all the details printed on it. Any discrepancy should be reported to the authorities well before the exam date.

Common details printed on the hall ticket include:

  • Candidate’s name
  • Father’s / mother’s name
  • Photograph and signature
  • AP ICET registration / hall ticket number
  • Date of birth and gender
  • Category (General / OBC / SC / ST, etc.)
  • Exam date and session (forenoon/afternoon, if applicable)
  • Reporting time and exam timings
  • Test centre name and full address
  • Important exam day instructions and guidelines

If your name, photograph, or any other key detail is incorrect or unclear, contact the official helpline immediately for guidance on the correction process.

Important exam day guidelines

On the day of the exam, candidates should follow these important instructions:

  • Reach the exam centre at least 60-90 minutes before the reporting time to complete verification formalities.
  • Carry a printed copy of the AP ICET Hall Ticket 2026 along with a valid photo ID proof (such as Aadhaar card, PAN card, voter ID, driving licence, or passport).
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smartwatches, calculators, Bluetooth devices, or any other electronic gadgets inside the exam hall.
  • Avoid carrying any written notes, books, or papers unless specifically allowed in the instructions.
  • Follow the instructions given by the invigilators and maintain discipline inside the exam premises.
  • Sit only at the allotted seat as per your hall ticket and attendance sheet.

AP ICET 2026 Eligibility (Quick Reminder)

Although the hall ticket is issued after successful registration, candidates must still satisfy the AP ICET 2026 eligibility criteria for their result and admission to be valid. A short reminder section keeps the article complete and helpful for late applicants.

Key points of AP ICET eligibility (generic, to be aligned with official notification):

  • Educational qualification: Candidates should have a bachelor’s degree or equivalent from a recognised university with the minimum specified percentage (usually different for categories). Final-year students may also be eligible, subject to passing the degree exam.
  • Course-wise eligibility: - For MBA: Bachelor’s degree with required percentage and qualifying subjects. - For MCA: Bachelor’s degree with Mathematics at 10+2 level or at the degree level, as per the notification.
  • Category-wise relaxation: Certain relaxations in minimum marks may be applicable for reserved categories as per Andhra Pradesh state government rules.
  • Domicile criteria: Candidates should satisfy local/non-local status requirements of Andhra Pradesh as per APSCHE norms.

Note: Even if a candidate has downloaded the hall ticket, failure to meet eligibility conditions at counselling or admission stage may result in cancellation of candidature.

AP ICET 2026 Selection and Counselling (Brief)

AP ICET is not just an entrance test but also the first step in the MBA and MCA admission process in Andhra Pradesh. A short section on selection and counselling helps users understand the complete journey.

  • AP ICET 2026 scores will be used for admission to MBA and MCA courses offered by universities and affiliated colleges in Andhra Pradesh.
  • After the exam, authorities will declare the AP ICET 2026 result and release the rank card.
  • APSCHE will then conduct a centralised counselling process, where qualified candidates have to register, get their documents verified, and exercise web options for colleges and courses.
  • Allotment of seats will be based on AP ICET rank, category, local status, seat availability, and options exercised by the candidate.
