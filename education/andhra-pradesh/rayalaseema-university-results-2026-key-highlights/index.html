How To Download Rayalaseema University Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the steps below to check their Rayalaseema University LLB Result 2026 online for the February 2026 examination session.

  • Visit the official Rayalaseema University results portal at https://www.ruk.ac.in/results/.
  • On the Examination Results Portal page, locate the latest LLB February 2026 result links for your course and semester (3 Years / 5 Years, III / V / VII / IX Semester).
  • Click on the appropriate result link matching your LLB course and semester.
  • On the result page, enter your Student Registration Number in the given field.
  • Click on the Get Results button to view your Rayalaseema University Result 2026 on the screen.
  • Check your subject-wise marks, total marks and result status, then download or print the provisional marksheet for future reference.
