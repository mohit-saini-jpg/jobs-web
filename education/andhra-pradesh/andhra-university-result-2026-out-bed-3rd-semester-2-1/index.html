How to Download Andhra University Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download Andhra University B.Ed 3rd Semester Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official Andhra University results portal: https://results.andhrauniversity.edu.in/.
  • On the homepage, look for the link related to B.Ed (Regular) Third Semester (2-1) Regular and Supplementary examinations November 2025.
  • Click on the appropriate result link to open the B.Ed 3rd semester result page.
  • Enter your hall ticket number or registration number in the required field.
  • Submit the details to view your Andhra University Result 2026 on the screen.
  • Check your name, subject-wise marks, total marks and result status, then download or print the provisional marksheet for your records.
  • If you notice any errors or have queries regarding the result, contact your college examination cell or the Andhra University examination section as per the contact details given on the official website.
