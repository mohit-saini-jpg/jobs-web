How to Download AKNU Exam Result 2026

Students can follow the step by step process given below to check Adikavi Nannaya University Result 2026 for Law, Pre-PhD and M.Tech semester examinations online.

  1. Visit the official website of Adikavi Nannaya University at aknu.edu.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the Examination or Results section and then select the AKNU Results option.
  3. You will be redirected to the official AKNU results portal page at aknu.edu.in/results.
  4. On the result page, choose the relevant result link for Law Courses 5th Sem, Pre-PhD, M.Tech 1st Sem or M.Tech 3rd Sem as per your course and exam session.
  5. Enter your registration number or hall ticket number in the given field and correctly fill the captcha code displayed on the screen.
  6. Click on the submit or search button to view your AKNU Exam Result 2026 on the screen.
  7. Check your subject wise marks, total marks and pass/fail status carefully, then download and save the provisional marksheet for future reference.
  8. Cross check all details printed on the result, and if any discrepancy is found, contact the Adikavi Nannaya University examination branch or your college immediately for correction.
