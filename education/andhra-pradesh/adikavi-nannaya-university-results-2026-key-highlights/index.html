How to Check AKNU 1st Sem Result 2026

  • Visit the official Adikavi Nannaya University website - aknu.edu.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the 'Examinations' or 'Results' section and click on the AKNU results link or directly open aknu.edu.in/results.
  • Select your course and semester such as B.Sc 1st Sem, B.Com 1st Sem, B.Voc/BA/BBA/BCA 1st Sem, or MBA/MBA T & H/MCA 1st Sem from the list.
  • Click on the relevant result link for Feb 2026 UG exams or Jan 2026 PG exams.
  • Enter your registration number or hall ticket number and the captcha code as shown on the screen.
  • Submit the details to view your AKNU 1st Sem Result 2026 on the screen.
  • Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future reference and for use until the original marksheet is issued by the college.
