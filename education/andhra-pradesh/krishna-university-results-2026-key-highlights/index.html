How to Download Krishna University Result 2026

Students can follow the steps below to check Krishna University Result 2026 for B.Tech III, V and VII Semesters RV Net Results in online mode.

  1. Visit the official Krishna University website at kru.ac.in.
  2. On the homepage, go to the Examinations or Results section, or directly open the results category page at kru.ac.in/category/results/.
  3. Look for the notification or link titled "B.Tech-III V and VII Semesters RV Net Results".
  4. Click on the B.Tech III, V and VII Semesters RV Net Results link to open the result page.
  5. If prompted, enter your hall ticket or registration number and other required details on the result/login page.
  6. Submit the details to view your B.Tech RV result, showing revised subject wise marks, total marks and updated pass/fail status.
  7. Download or take a printout of the Krishna University Result 2026 RV marksheet and keep it safe for academic reference and future documentation.
  8. In case of any mismatch or error in the displayed result, contact the Krishna University examination branch or your college immediately with relevant proof.
