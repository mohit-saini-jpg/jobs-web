Detailed Information

Andhra University has officially released the Andhra University 2nd 3rd and 6th Semester Result 2026 for various UG and PG programmes including MA Ancient History and Archaeology 3rd semester (2-1), MA History 3rd semester (2-1), MBA (SIB) 2nd semester (1-2) revaluation phase-II and B.Arch 6th semester (3-2) revaluation phase-II. These results correspond to regular and supplementary examinations held in November 2025 for the MA programmes, September 2025 for MBA (SIB) and May 2025 for B.Arch.

Andhra University Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - andhrauniversity.edu.in. Students can check and download their results by visiting results.andhrauniversity.edu.in, selecting the relevant exam and semester, and entering their registration number or hall ticket details. After downloading the provisional marks memo, candidates must carefully verify their personal details, subjects and marks, and contact the university or college examination section immediately in case of any discrepancy.
