AP LAWCET Results 2026 Out Today - Direct Link To Check Rank Card

AP LAWCET Results 2026 will be released today in online mode by the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) for candidates who appeared in the AP LAWCET and AP PGLCET 2026 exams. AP LAWCET Result 2026 will be made available on the official APSCHE CET website - cets.apsche.ap.gov.in - where students can check their marks, qualifying status and rank once the result link is activated.

The results will cover admissions to 3 year LLB, 5 year integrated LLB and LLM (through AP PGLCET) for the 2026-27 academic session, and candidates must log in using their registration number, hall ticket number and date of birth to download their scorecard.

Students should verify details such as name, roll number, marks, rank and category on the AP LAWCET 2026 rank card, and in case of any discrepancy they must immediately contact the AP LAWCET/AP PGLCET helpdesk before the counselling schedule begins.

AP LAWCET Results 2026 Key Highlights

APSCHE will publish the AP LAWCET Results 2026 on its official CET portal for all candidates who appeared in the state level law entrance test this year. The scorecards will be issued for both AP LAWCET (3 year and 5 year LLB) and AP PGLCET (LLM), and candidates can log in to cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/LAWCET to download their provisional rank cards for reference during counselling and seat allotment. These results will be used to prepare the merit list, cut off and counselling schedule for admission into participating law colleges in Andhra Pradesh.

AP LAWCET Result 2026 Direct Link

AP LAWCET Result 2026 direct link will be activated today on the official APSCHE CET portal so that candidates can quickly access their scorecard without navigating multiple pages. Through this link, students who appeared for the AP LAWCET 3 year LLB, 5 year LLB and AP PGLCET exams can view their marks, overall rank and category rank for the upcoming counselling and seat allotment rounds. How To Check AP LAWCET Results 2026 Online

Candidates are advised to keep their AP LAWCET registration number and hall ticket number ready so that they can log in as soon as the result link is activated. Following the official step by step process helps avoid login errors and ensures that the correct scorecard is downloaded for use during counselling.

Step By Step Process To Check AP LAWCET Result 2026

  • Visit the official APSCHE CET portal by typing cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/LAWCET in your browser.
  • On the homepage, find and click on the link that says AP LAWCET Results 2026 or Download Rank Card.
  • You will be redirected to the AP LAWCET result login page where you need to enter your registration number in the first field.
  • Enter your AP LAWCET hall ticket number in the second field and then provide your date of birth in the given format.
  • Click on the View Result or Submit button to proceed and wait for the AP LAWCET 2026 scorecard to open on the screen.
  • Check your name, roll number, marks and rank carefully and then click on the download or print option to save the rank card PDF for future reference and counselling.

Details Mentioned On AP LAWCET 2026 Rank Card

The AP LAWCET rank card will have important information required for counselling and admission. Candidates should check all details and ensure that the information matches their application and hall ticket.

Key details include:

  • Candidate name, photograph and signature
  • Hall ticket number and registration number
  • Category and gender
  • Total marks obtained and maximum marks
  • Section wise marks (if applicable)
  • Overall rank and category rank
  • Qualifying status in AP LAWCET or AP PGLCET 2026

AP LAWCET Results 2026 - Qualifying Marks And Merit

APSCHE prescribes minimum qualifying marks for AP LAWCET and AP PGLCET which candidates must secure to be considered for ranking. Based on the marks scored and the qualifying cut off, a merit list is prepared and used for counselling and seat allotment in participating colleges.

AP LAWCET 2026 Counselling After Result

After the declaration of AP LAWCET Results 2026, APSCHE will announce the counselling schedule on the same CET portal. Qualified candidates will have to register online, pay the counselling fee, upload documents and take part in web option entry for seat allotment in 3 year and 5 year LLB programmes.
