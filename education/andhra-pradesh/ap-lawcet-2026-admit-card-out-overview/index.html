1paragraphAP LAWCET 2026 exam schedule
2paragraphAP LAWCET 2026 will be conducted on 4 May 2026 in online mode. The exam is held in separate sessions for different courses, typically with one session for 3‑year LL.B and another for 5‑year integrated LL.B on the same day.
3paragraphCandidates should carefully check on their hall ticket:
4listunordered
  • Exact exam date (it will show 4 May 2026).
  • Session timing (morning or afternoon).
  • Reporting time, which is usually at least 60 minutes before the exam start.
5paragraphExam centres
6paragraphAP LAWCET is conducted at multiple test centres across Andhra Pradesh. The hall ticket displays:
7listunordered
  • Centre name.
  • Centre code.
  • Full address (including city and pin code).
8paragraphYou can advise students to locate their centre on a map a day before the exam and plan travel time, especially if the centre is in another town or on the outskirts of the city.

3paragraphStep‑by‑step hall ticket download guide
4listordered
  • Visit the official website Go to cets.apsche.ap.gov.in in your browser and click on the LAWCET section from the list of CETs.
  • Open the hall ticket page On the AP LAWCET homepage, click on the link labelled “Download Hall Ticket” or directly open the LAWCET_GetPrintHallTicket URL.
  • Enter login details On the hall ticket page, you will typically be asked to enter: Registration number / application number Qualifying examination hall ticket number Date of birth (as per application form)
  • Registration number / application number
  • Qualifying examination hall ticket number
  • Date of birth (as per application form)
  • Submit the details Click on “Submit” / “Get Hall Ticket” after entering all details correctly.
  • View and verify your admit card Your AP LAWCET 2026 admit card will appear on the screen. Check your: Name and personal details Course (3‑year or 5‑year LL.B) Exam date and time Exam centre address Photograph and signature (if printed)
  • Name and personal details
  • Course (3‑year or 5‑year LL.B)
  • Exam date and time
  • Exam centre address
  • Photograph and signature (if printed)
  • Download and print Download the hall ticket as a PDF and take a clear printout on A4 paper. Keep at least two copies so you have a backup.
5paragraphIf there is any technical error (page not loading or details not accepted), candidates should retry after some time or use a different browser. Persistent issues should be reported through the official help contacts.

7Important Exam‑Day Instructions for AP LAWCET 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphA focused instruction section helps you capture “People also ask” intent around what to carry, timings, etc.
2listunordered
  • Carry mandatory documents Bring a printed AP LAWCET 2026 hall ticket and a valid government photo ID (Aadhaar card, PAN card, voter ID, passport, driving licence, etc.). Without these, entry will likely be denied.
  • Reach the centre on time Reach at least 60 minutes before the exam start time to complete frisking, ID verification and seating. Latecomers may not be allowed into the exam hall.
  • Follow the dress and gadget rules Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, earphones, Bluetooth devices, notes or any electronic gadgets inside the examination centre.
  • Adhere to CBT guidelines AP LAWCET is conducted in computer‑based mode, so candidates should follow the instructions displayed on their screen and those given by invigilators.
  • Check seat and system details Sit only on the allotted system and verify that the question paper displayed corresponds to your course (3‑year or 5‑year).
3paragraphEncourage candidates to read the complete set of instructions printed on the admit card before the exam day.
