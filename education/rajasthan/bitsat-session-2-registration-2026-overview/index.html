About
BITSAT Session 2 Registration 2026 has started earlier than scheduled at admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in. Candidates who missed Session 1 or wish to improve their score can apply from April 20 to May 2, 2026. The Session 2 exam will be held from May 24 to 26, 2026, and the best score from both sessions will be considered for admission to BITS Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad campuses.
Details

BITSAT Session 2 Registration 2026 has officially started at admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in. BITS Pilani has opened the Session 2 application window from April 20, 2026 to May 2, 2026 (5:00 PM IST). Candidates who missed Session 1 or want to improve their BITSAT score can apply online for the Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test. The BITSAT 2026 Session 2 exam is scheduled from May 24 to 26, 2026, and the admit card will be released on May 20, 2026.

The best score from both sessions will be considered for merit list preparation. Eligible candidates must have passed Class 12 with a minimum 75% aggregate in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics or Biology. Note that students who reappeared for all Class 12 subjects are no longer eligible as per the revised BITSAT 2026 eligibility criteria.

BITSAT Session 2 2026 Eligibility Criteria
  • Passed or appearing in Class 12 (10+2) in 2025 or 2026 from a recognised board
  • Minimum 75% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics or Biology
  • Minimum 60% marks individually in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics or Biology
  • Students who reappeared for all Class 12 subjects are not eligible
  • No age limit for BITSAT 2026
