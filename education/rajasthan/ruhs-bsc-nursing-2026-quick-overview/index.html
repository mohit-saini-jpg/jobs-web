RUHS CUET 2026 B.Sc Nursing Syllabus covers the Class 11 and 12 NCERT-level topics from Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Rajasthan University of Health Sciences (RUHS) designs this syllabus for the RUHS Common Undergraduate Entrance Test (CUET) 2026, which serves as the entrance exam for B.Sc Nursing admissions in Rajasthan colleges. The syllabus focuses on conceptual understanding rather than rote learning, helping aspirants prepare effectively for the 100-mark MCQ-based test.
Here is the full subject-wise syllabus, detailed exam pattern, preparation tips, and important resources to help you score well and secure admission. Whether you are looking for topic lists, marking scheme, or download links, this article covers everything you need in one place.

Preparation Tips for RUHS CUET B.Sc Nursing 2026

  • Study directly from NCERT Class 11 & 12 textbooks.
  • Solve previous year RUHS question papers and mock tests.
  • Focus on high-weightage chapters in Biology, Physics, and Chemistry.
  • Since there is no negative marking, attempt all 100 questions.
