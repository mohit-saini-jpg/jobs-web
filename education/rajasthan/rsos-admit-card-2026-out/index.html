RSOS Admit Card 2026 Out

Rajasthan State Open School (RSOS), Jaipur has activated the admit card download facility for secondary (Class 10) and senior secondary (Class 12) students appearing in the 2026 examinations. The admit card is available only in online mode, and candidates must take a printout and carry it to the exam centre on all exam days. Without the RSOS hall ticket, students will not be allowed to enter the examination hall or write the paper.

How to Download RSOS Admit Card 2026 Online

This section directly serves navigational intent by guiding students step by step.

Follow these steps to download RSOS Admit Card 2026 for Class 10th and 12th:

  • Go to the official website of Rajasthan State Open School: Click Here
  • On the homepage, find and click on the link that mentions “RSOS Admit Card 2026” or “Admit Card - Class 10/12”.
  • Select your exam type (Secondary / Senior Secondary) and the relevant exam session if asked.
  • Enter the required details such as roll number, date of birth, name or any other field shown on the screen, along with the captcha/security code.
  • Click on the “Submit” or “Download Admit Card” button.
  • Your RSOS admit card 2026 will appear on the screen; check all the details carefully.
  • Click on the print/download option and take a clear A4-size printout of the hall ticket.

If the page does not open or shows an error, students can refresh the page, try after a few minutes, or change the browser/device and try again.

Details Mentioned on RSOS 10th, 12th Admit Card 2026

This part fulfils informational intent by showing what students should verify on their hall ticket.

Typically, the RSOS admit card 2026 contains the following details:

  • Student’s name and photograph
  • Father’s / Mother’s name
  • Roll number / enrollment number
  • Class (Class 10 / Class 12) and course name
  • Subject names and subject codes
  • Exam dates and timing for each paper
  • Examination centre name, code and full address
  • Reporting time and exam duration
  • Important instructions for candidates

Students must check their name spelling, subjects, exam centre details and photo carefully. In case of any discrepancy, they should immediately contact their RSOS study centre or the concerned authority for correction guidance.

RSOS 10th, 12th Exam 2026: Important Instructions for Students

This section targets both informational and transactional intent by telling students how to use the admit card correctly.

  • Reach the exam centre at least 30-45 minutes before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card.
  • Carry a printed copy of the RSOS Admit Card 2026 on every exam day without fail.
  • If instructed by the centre, also carry a valid photo ID proof (like Aadhaar card, school ID, etc.) for verification.
  • Follow the official RSOS time table and appear only on the correct date and shift for each subject.
  • Do not write anything on the admit card except where authorised (like candidate’s signature).

What to Carry with RSOS Admit Card 2026

  • Printed RSOS hall ticket for 2026
  • Transparent pen(s) and other allowed stationery items
  • Simple watch (if permitted by the centre)
  • Any other document specifically mentioned by RSOS or centre authorities

Things Not Allowed in RSOS Exam Centre

  • Mobile phones, smartwatches, Bluetooth devices and other electronic gadgets
  • Written notes, books or any type of paper slips (chits)
  • Calculator or programmable devices, unless specifically allowed for a particular paper
  • Any item that can be treated as unfair means inside the exam hall

Candidates found using or carrying banned items may face cancellation of their exam and further action as per rules.

RSOS Admit Card 2026 - Eligibility and Who Can Download

Here you clarify who actually gets the hall ticket, which is important for both new and repeat candidates.

The RSOS Admit Card 2026 is issued only to students whose examination forms have been successfully submitted and accepted by Rajasthan State Open School. It is generally available for:

  • Students enrolled in RSOS Secondary (Class 10) course for the 2026 exam session
  • Students enrolled in RSOS Senior Secondary (Class 12) course for the 2026 exam session
  • Eligible repeaters/improvement candidates whose applications have been approved

If a student cannot find the admit card on the portal despite being eligible, they should contact their study centre or RSOS support with their enrollment details.
