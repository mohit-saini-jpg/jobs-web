BITSAT Eligibility Criteria 2026

BITSAT Eligibility Criteria 2026 requires candidates to have passed or be appearing for Class 12 in 2025 or 2026 with a minimum 75% aggregate in PCM/PCB and at least 60% in each subject. Full Class 12 reattempts are no longer allowed under the revised rules.

Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test (BITSAT) serves as the primary entrance exam for admission to undergraduate engineering (B.E.), B.Pharm, and integrated M.Sc. programmes at BITS Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad campuses. With registration ongoing and Session 2 exam approaching, understanding the exact eligibility criteria is crucial for aspirants to avoid application rejection or admission issues.

BITS Pilani has revised the eligibility criteria for BITSAT 2026. The major change removes the clause allowing candidates who retook the full set of Class 12 subjects. Now, only students appearing for Class 12 for the first time in 2025 or 2026, or those who passed in 2025 and are taking improvement in specific subjects, are eligible.

BITSAT 2026 Nationality Criteria

Indian nationals are fully eligible to apply. The official guidelines do not impose specific restrictions on nationality for most candidates. Foreign nationals, OCI, and PIO candidates may apply under separate provisions; they should refer to the official brochure for details on additional requirements or quotas.

BITSAT 2026 Age Limit

There is no specific age limit for BITSAT 2026. Candidates of any age can apply provided they meet the educational qualification and passing year requirements. No minimum or maximum age bar is prescribed, and no age relaxations apply.

This flexible rule allows genuine candidates from the eligible passing years to apply without age-related concerns.

BITSAT 2026 Educational Qualification

Candidates must have passed or be appearing in the Class 12 (10+2) examination from a recognized Central or State board or its equivalent. A minimum of five subjects is mandatory.

For B.E. (except Environmental & Sustainability) and M.Sc. Programmes:

  • Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics must be studied.
  • One language and any one additional subject.

For B.Pharm. and B.E. (Environmental & Sustainability) Programmes:

  • Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (or Mathematics).
  • One language and any one additional subject.

Minimum Marks Requirements:

  • Minimum 75% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology.
  • Minimum 60% marks in each of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology individually.

Appearing Candidates: Students appearing for Class 12 in 2026 are eligible. They must submit final results meeting the criteria by the admission deadline.

Other Essential Requirements:

  • Candidates must have appeared for the Class 12 examination for the first time in 2025 or 2026.
  • Full reattempt of all Class 12 subjects is not permitted.
  • Students who passed in 2025 can appear for improvement exams in specific subjects only; the best/latest performance will be considered.
  • PCB candidates are eligible primarily for B.Pharm; they may need Mathematics proficiency at Class 10 level for certain other programmes.
  • Results must be available by the specified verification deadline.

How to Apply for BITSAT 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official admissions portal — admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in.
  • Register using a valid email ID and mobile number.
  • Fill the online application form with personal, academic, and contact details.
  • Upload scanned photograph and signature in the prescribed format.
  • Pay the required application fee online.
  • Submit the form and take a printout of the confirmation page.
  • Choose preferred sessions and proceed with slot booking when available.

Documents Required for BITSAT 2026

  • Class 10 and Class 12 marksheets (or appearing certificate).
  • Recent passport-size photograph and signature.
  • Valid government ID proof (Aadhaar, Passport, etc.).
  • Category certificate (if claiming any reservation or concession).
  • Improvement exam details or previous marksheet (if applicable).
