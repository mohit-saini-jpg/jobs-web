Detailed Information

The University of Rajasthan has released Rajasthan University Result 2026 for key law programmes, including B.A. LL.B. (Hons) VII Semester (Five Year Integrated Course) Examination December 2025 and LL.B. Semester III Examination December 2025. Rajasthan University Result 2026 has been released online on the official result portal result.uniraj.ac.in, accessible through the main university website uniraj.ac.in.

Law students enrolled in the five year integrated BA LLB (Hons) course and three year LLB programme can now check their semester results by opening the respective result links and entering their roll number in the login field. After downloading the online marksheet, candidates should verify their name, roll number, course, semester, subject wise marks and pass/fail status and contact the university or college examination cell if any discrepancy is found.
