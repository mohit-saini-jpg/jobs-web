How To Download BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Online

Downloading the BITSAT 2026 admit card is an online process that candidates should complete well before the exam day to avoid last-minute issues.
Step-by-Step Process To Download BITSAT Hall Ticket 2026
  1. Go to the official BITSAT admission website: admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in.
  2. On the homepage, find and click on the “BITSAT 2026 Admit Card / Hall Ticket” link.
  3. You will be redirected to the BITSAT 2026 candidate login page.
  4. Enter your BITSAT 2026 application number and password or date of birth in the respective fields.
  5. Fill in the captcha or security code if asked, then click on “Login” or “Submit”.
  6. Your BITSAT 2026 admit card will appear on the screen with your photo, basic details, and exam schedule.
  7. Check all details carefully, then click on the download/print option and save the PDF.
  8. Take 2-3 clear printouts of the admit card on A4 paper and keep them safe for exam day.
Login Credentials Required For BITSAT 2026 Admit Card
To log in and download the hall ticket, candidates must have:
  • BITSAT 2026 application number.
  • Password or date of birth (as set at the time of registration).
  • Access to the registered email or mobile, in case password recovery is needed.
If you forget your password, use the “Forgot Password” link on the login screen, and follow the instructions to reset it using your registered email ID or mobile number.
Common Download Issues And Quick Fixes
  • Website not loading / server busy: Try again after some time, preferably during non-peak hours, and ensure you are using a stable internet connection.
  • Invalid credentials: Double-check the application number and password; ensure there are no extra spaces and the correct case is used.
  • Admit card not visible / blank page: Clear your browser cache, use a different browser, or try using another device.
  • Details mismatch or missing photo: Take a screenshot or printout and contact the BITS admission support immediately for guidance.

BITSAT 2026 Eligibility And Who Will Get The Admit Card

While detailed eligibility is covered in the official information brochure, the admit card is issued only to candidates who meet the minimum criteria and complete the registration process correctly.
Basic BITSAT 2026 Eligibility Criteria (Short Recap)
In general, BITSAT eligibility includes:
  • Completion or appearance in Class 12 or equivalent with the required subjects (usually Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics for engineering programmes).
  • A minimum aggregate percentage in qualifying exams as specified by BITS Pilani.
  • Meeting age and year-of-passing conditions mentioned in the BITSAT brochure.
Candidates who did not meet these conditions, or whose applications were incomplete or rejected, may not receive an admit card.
Conditions When BITSAT Admit Card May Not Be Issued
The BITSAT 2026 admit card typically will not be generated if:
  • The candidate’s application form was incomplete or rejected.
  • Payment of the application fee was not successfully confirmed.
  • Slot booking for the test date and centre was not completed within the allowed time.
Such candidates should immediately contact the admission office with proof of payment and application details.
