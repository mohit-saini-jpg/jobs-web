DCE Rajasthan UG Admission 2026-27

The Department of College Education (DCE), Rajasthan has started the online application process for Undergraduate (UG) admissions in Government Colleges for the academic session 2026-27. Online applications are invited from May 1, 2026 to May 15, 2026 for BA, BSc, BCom and other UG programmes. Admission will be purely merit-based on Class 12 marks. This complete guide covers important dates, eligibility, application fee, how to apply, documents required, and direct links.

How to Apply for DCE Rajasthan UG Admission 2026-27
  1. Visit the official website: dceapp.rajasthan.gov.in
  2. Click on the Online Admission 2026-27 link.
  3. Register as a new user using mobile number and email.
  4. Fill the Common Application Form (CAF) with personal and academic details.
  5. Upload required documents (Photo, Signature, Marksheets, etc.).
  6. Pay the application fee through online or e-Mitra.
  7. Submit the form and take a printout for future reference.
Documents Required for DCE Rajasthan UG Admission
  • 10th and 12th Marksheet & Certificate
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph
  • Scanned Signature
  • Domicile Certificate (for Rajasthan residents)
  • Caste/Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate / EWS Certificate (if applicable)
DCE Rajasthan UG Admission 2026-27: Courses Offered

Bachelor of Arts (BA), Bachelor of Science (BSc), Bachelor of Commerce (BCom) and other UG programmes under NEP 2020 (including 4-Year programmes).

Selection Process & Merit List

Admission is purely merit-based on marks obtained in Class 12th examination. Multiple merit lists will be released. Candidates must complete document verification and fee payment at the allotted college within the given timeline.
