How to Download VMOU RSCIT Admit Card 2026 Online

Downloading the VMOU RSCIT admit card is a simple online process and takes only a few minutes. Make sure you keep your exam details ready and follow the steps given below carefully.

Step-by-Step Process to Download RSCIT Admit Card

  • Open the official RKCL-VMOU portal in your browser: rkcl.vmou.ac.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the link labelled “RSCIT Admit Card 2026” or similar under the examination/notification section.
  • Select your exam district from the list displayed on the screen.
  • Enter your roll number or choose the option to search by name and date of birth.
  • Click on “View / Submit” to generate your admit card.
  • Check all details on the VMOU RSCIT Admit Card 2026, download the PDF and take a clear A4-size printout for exam day.

Details Printed on VMOU RSCIT Admit Card 2026

After downloading your hall ticket, verify the information mentioned on it to avoid any problem on exam day. The RSCIT admit card generally includes the following details:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph, and signature.
  • Roll number and registration / learner code.
  • Father’s or guardian’s name.
  • Exam date (29 March 2026 or 12 April 2026), exam shift and duration.
  • Reporting time, gate closing time, and exam timing (10:00 AM – 11:00 AM, as per schedule).
  • Exam centre name, code and full address.
  • Important exam day instructions issued by RKCL and VMOU.

VMOU RSCIT Exam Date 2026 and Schedule

The RSCIT exam is being organised in two different phases across Rajasthan to accommodate all registered candidates. As per the latest update, the exam dates are 29 March 2026 and 12 April 2026, both scheduled on Sunday.

The written exam will be conducted in offline mode using an OMR sheet, and the question paper will be available in Hindi and English languages. Candidates must appear at the exam centre mentioned on their VMOU RSCIT admit card; request for change of centre is usually not allowed.

Eligibility to Get VMOU RSCIT Admit Card 2026

Only those learners who have successfully enrolled in the RSCIT course through an authorised RKCL/VMOU centre and whose exam forms are accepted are issued the admit card. If a candidate has completed the course but does not find their admit card on the portal, they should first contact their RSCIT training centre for clarification.

The hall ticket acts as official permission to enter the exam hall, so candidates must keep it safe from the time of download till the exam date.

Important Instructions for VMOU RSCIT Exam Day

To avoid last-minute complications, follow these key instructions on the day of the RSCIT exam.

  • Carry a printed copy of your RSCIT admit card and one original photo ID proof such as Aadhaar card, voter ID or driving licence.
  • Reach the exam centre by the reporting time (around 8:30 AM) to complete verification formalities before gate closure.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators or any electronic gadgets into the exam hall.
  • Read all instructions given on the admit card and question paper carefully before starting the exam.
  • In case of any discrepancy in the admit card, inform your centre/official contact before the exam date, not on the exam day itself.
