Students will be able to check and download their Class 12 marksheet by visiting the RBSE result portal, entering their roll number and other required details printed on the admit card. How to Download RBSE Rajasthan Board 12th Result 2026

Use the following steps to check and download your RBSE 12th Result 2026 once it is released:

  • Visit the official RBSE website: Click Here.
  • On the homepage, look for the link titled “Rajasthan Board 12th Result 2026” or “Senior Secondary Examination 2026 Result” for Science/Commerce/Arts.
  • Click on the relevant Class 12 result link.
  • On the result page, enter your Roll Number as given on your admit card (and any additional details if asked).
  • Click on Submit / View Result to display your online marksheet.
  • Check your personal details, subject wise marks, total marks, percentage and result status, then download or print the marksheet for use in college admissions and documentation.
