How to Check Rajasthan University Exam Result 2026

Students can follow the steps below to check and download the Rajasthan University Result 2026 from the official portal:

  • Visit the official website of Rajasthan University at uniraj.ac.in and click on the "Results" or "Examination" section on the homepage.
  • You will be redirected to the Rajasthan University result portal at result.uniraj.ac.in.
  • On the result page, locate and click on the relevant examination name such as "B.B.A. SEMESTER-I DEC.-2025" or "B.Com. ABST SEMESTER-I DEC.-2025" from the list.
  • A result login page will open where you need to enter your roll number (and other details, if asked) exactly as mentioned on your admit card.
  • Click on the "Find" or "Submit" button to view your Rajasthan University Exam Result 2026 on the screen.
  • Check your personal details, subject-wise marks, total marks and result status carefully, then download and save the online marksheet for future use.
  • In case of any error or discrepancy in name, subject, marks or result status, contact the university/college examination cell with supporting documents for correction.
