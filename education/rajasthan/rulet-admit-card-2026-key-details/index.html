Detailed Information

The University of Rajasthan has released the RULET Admit Card 2026 for the Rajasthan University Law Entrance Test for admission to the 5 year BA LLB (Hons) course. The admit card has been issued in online mode for all candidates who successfully completed the RULET 2026 application process.

As per the official schedule, RULET 2026 will be conducted in offline mode (OMR based test) in June 2026 at designated exam centres in Jaipur. Candidates can download the RULET 2026 hall ticket from the official admission portal admissions.univraj.org/rulet-admit-card or via the RULET link on uniraj.ac.in, using their form number and date of birth.

How to Download RULET Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the University of Rajasthan admission portal admissions.univraj.org or open the direct page admissions.univraj.org/rulet-admit-card.
  • On the RULET 2026 page, click on the RULET Admit Card 2026 or Download Hall Ticket link.
  • You will be redirected to the admit card login window. Enter your Form Number and Date of Birth in the required fields.
  • Click on the Submit / Get Admit Card button.
  • Your RULET Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen showing your name, roll number, exam date, time and exam centre details.
  • Check all details carefully and download and print 2–3 clear A4-size copies of the hall ticket to carry to the exam centre.

The university will not send admit cards via post or email; candidates must download them online only.

Details Mentioned on RULET Admit Card 2026

The RULET 2026 admit card contains important candidate and exam information that must be verified before appearing for the test:

  • Candidate’s name
  • Roll number and form number
  • Category and date of birth
  • Parent’s / guardian’s name
  • Exam name: Rajasthan University Law Entrance Test (RULET) 2026
  • Exam date and exam timing
  • Exam centre name and complete address
  • Candidate’s photograph and signature
  • Important exam day guidelines and instructions

If any mismatch or error is found in your name, category, date of birth or exam centre details, you should immediately contact the University of Rajasthan exam/admissions helpline before the exam day for necessary guidance.

Documents Required with RULET Admit Card 2026

On the day of the RULET 2026 examination, candidates should carry:

  • A printed copy of the RULET Admit Card 2026 (clear A4-size printout; colour or good-quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence or any other government-issued ID accepted as per RULET instructions.
  • Any other document mentioned in the admit card or university instructions (for example, latest passport size photograph if required).

Candidates without the RULET hall ticket 2026 and a valid photo ID will not be allowed to enter the exam hall.

Exam-Day Instructions for RULET 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the RULET 2026 entrance examination, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time printed on the RULET admit card to complete verification, frisking and seating; late entry after the specified time may not be allowed.
  • Carry only essential items: RULET 2026 admit card (A4 printout), valid photo ID and permitted stationery such as blue/black ball-point pens and other items allowed by the university.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not permitted), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the examination hall; possession of such items can lead to cancellation of your paper and further disciplinary action.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the admit card and those announced by the invigilators; indulging in unfair means, talking to other candidates or disobeying exam rules can result in cancellation of your examination and debarment from future tests.
  • Keep your RULET Admit Card 2026 safely even after the exam, as it may be required for checking results, counselling, and admission formalities.
