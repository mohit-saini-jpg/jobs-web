Details

BRABU UG Admission 2026-30 application process has started. Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar Bihar University (BRABU), Muzaffarpur, has invited online applications for 4-year Undergraduate (UG) courses — B.A., B.Sc., and B.Com. under CBCS for the academic session 2026-30 in its affiliated colleges. Applications are open from 4 May 2026 to 25 May 2026 via the official UMIS portal.

BRABU UG Admission 2026-30 Eligibility Criteria

For B.A. / B.Com.: Candidates who have passed Intermediate (I.A./I.Com./+2) from Bihar School Examination Board or any equivalent recognized board.

For B.Sc.: Candidates who have passed I.Sc. or +2 Science stream only from Bihar School Examination Board or equivalent recognized board.

Application Fee
General / OBC₹600/-
SC / ST₹300/-

Payment Mode: Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking.

How to Apply for BRABU UG Admission 2026-30 (Step-by-Step)
  1. Visit the official BRABU website brabu.ac.in or directly go to umis.brabu.ac.in.
  2. Click on the link for “Applications are Invited for UG (2026-2030) New Apply”.
  3. Complete the registration by providing name, mobile number, email, and other details.
  4. Fill the detailed application form and upload scanned documents (photo, signature, mark sheets, etc.).
  5. Pay the application fee online.
  6. Preview, submit, and download/print the confirmation page for future reference.
Documents Required for BRABU UG Admission 2026
  • Valid Email ID and Active Mobile Number
  • Recent Passport-size Photograph (JPEG)
  • Scanned Signature
  • Class 10th and Class 12th Mark Sheets
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Photo ID Proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID, etc.)
