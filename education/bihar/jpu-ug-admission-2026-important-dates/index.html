JPU UG Admission 2026 is open. Apply online for 4-Year UG Courses (Arts, Science, Commerce) at Jai Prakash University, Chapra before 10 June 2026. Check eligibility, important dates, fee and merit list schedule.
Details

Jai Prakash University (JPU), Chapra has started the online admission process for CBCS-based 4-Year Undergraduate Programmes (Session 2026-30) for the academic year 2026-27. The application process began on 16 May 2026 through the Samarth Admission Portal. The last date to fill the online application form is 10 June 2026. Eligible candidates from Arts, Science, and Commerce streams can apply for UG admission 2026 at JPU and its affiliated colleges.

How to Apply for JPU UG Admission 2026
  1. Visit the official website www.jpv.ac.in.
  2. Register using a valid mobile number and email ID.
  3. Fill the online application form carefully.
  4. Select up to three programmes, one district, and up to five colleges in order of preference.
  5. Upload required documents.
  6. Pay the application fee of ₹500/- online.
  7. Submit the form and download the confirmation page.
Merit List and Selection Process

Merit list will be prepared on the basis of marks obtained in Intermediate (Class 12). Candidates can select up to 3 programmes. Allotment will be done as per preference order, merit, and seat availability. Reservation policy as per Bihar Government rules will be followed.

Documents Required for JPU UG Admission 2026
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Signature
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Class 10th and 12th Marksheet & Certificate
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • EWS Certificate (if applicable)
  • Disability Certificate (if applicable)
  • Migration Certificate / CLC / TC
