2What Is the PPU UG First Merit List 2026?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphThe PPU UG first merit list is the first official selection list released by Patliputra University for undergraduate admissions to Semester 1 of the four-year programme under the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) and the Four Year Undergraduate Programme (FYUGP) as mandated by NEP 2020. The merit list has been prepared on the basis of marks obtained in Class 12 (Intermediate) and the college and subject preferences submitted by applicants.
2paragraphThis year, PPU has made the entire admission process fully online and transparent through the Samarth Portal, eliminating college-level irregularities and ensuring merit-based seat allotment across all affiliated colleges.

4How to Download PPU UG 1st Merit List 2026 - Step-by-Step Guide
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphCandidates can access the merit list and their seat allotment letter directly from the Samarth Portal. Here is a simple step-by-step process to download your PPU UG first merit list offer letter:
2listordered
  • Visit the official Patliputra University Samarth Portal at ppupadm.samarth.edu.in or the university website at ppup.ac.in.
  • On the homepage, navigate to the Notice section or the Admissions tab.
  • Click on the link labelled "PPU UG 1st Merit List - Click Here".
  • A PDF file of the first merit list will open in a new tab. Check your name and details.
  • To download your personal seat allotment letter (offer letter), click on "Applicant Login (UG)".
  • Enter your registered User ID and Password in the login fields.
  • Click Login and navigate to your dashboard to find and download your offer letter.
  • Take a printout of the offer letter and keep a digital copy for future reference.

Students admitted under the 2026-30 batch will study for four years under the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS), with the following exit options:
