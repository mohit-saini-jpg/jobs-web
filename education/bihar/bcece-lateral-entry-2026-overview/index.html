BCECE Full Form and BCECE Lateral Entry 2026

The BCECE full form is Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination, conducted by the Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) for various professional courses in the state.

BCECE Lateral Entry (BCECE LE) is a separate entrance exam that allows eligible diploma and B.Sc. candidates to take lateral entry into the second year of undergraduate Engineering, Pharmacy and Para-medical programmes in Bihar colleges.

This route helps candidates save one academic year and continue higher studies directly from the 2nd year.

BCECE Lateral Entry 2026 Application Form – How to Apply Online

All applications must be submitted online via the official BCECEB portal; offline forms are not accepted.

Step-by-step process:

  • Visit the official website bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
  • On the home page, click the “Online Portal of BCECE(LE)–2026” link.
  • Click on “New Registration” and read the instructions carefully before proceeding.
  • Complete Step-One (Registration) by entering basic details, valid email ID and mobile number; verify using the verification code sent to your mobile/email to generate a Registration Number and Password.
  • Log in with your Registration Number and Password, then complete Step-Two (Multi-Step Application Form): Fill Personal Information and click Save & Continue. Enter Contact Details and click Save & Continue. Provide Educational Information and click Save & Continue.
  • Fill Personal Information and click Save & Continue.
  • Enter Contact Details and click Save & Continue.
  • Provide Educational Information and click Save & Continue.
  • Upload a scanned photograph, signature and placard as per size and format specifications, then Save & Continue.
  • In Step-Three, check the Application Preview carefully; if needed, use “Back to Edit” to correct any entry, then agree to the declaration and click Final Submit & Proceed to Payment.
  • In Step-Four, pay the Examination / Counselling Fee of ₹2,200 via online payment modes such as net banking, debit/credit card or UPI (processing charges extra as applicable).
  • After successful payment, go to Step-Five and download the Confirmation Page and Fee Receipt; take printouts for future reference.
  • If necessary, use the Online Editing of Application Form facility during 10–11 May 2026 to correct permissible fields, and re-download the updated Confirmation Page.

BCECE Lateral Entry 2026 Admit Card

The BCECEB will release the BCECE LE 2026 admit card on 23 May 2026 on the official portal. Candidates must log in with their Registration Number and Password to download the admit card, verify details such as name, photograph, exam date, time and exam centre, and print multiple copies for safety. It is mandatory to carry the printed admit card along with valid ID proof to the exam centre on 31 May 2026.

BCECE Lateral Entry 2026 Result and Counselling

After the exam, BCECEB will publish the BCECE LE 2026 result on bceceboard.bihar.gov.in in the form of a merit list / rank card. Counselling for lateral entry admission into second-year Engineering, Pharmacy and Para-medical courses will be held based on ranks, category and availability of seats, with instructions issued separately on the website. Candidates must attend counselling with original certificates, mark sheets, domicile and category documents, and the BCECE LE 2026 admit card and confirmation page.
