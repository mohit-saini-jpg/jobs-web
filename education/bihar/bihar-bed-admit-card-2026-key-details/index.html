How to Download Bihar BEd Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official website: biharcetbed-brabu.in
  • Click on the link for Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 Admit Card / Hall Ticket.
  • Enter your Login ID / Application Number and Password (or Date of Birth as required).
  • Enter the security code / captcha and click on Submit / Login.
  • Your Bihar BEd 2026 admit card will be displayed. Verify all details, download, and take multiple A4-size printouts for exam day and future use.

If the website is slow due to heavy traffic, wait a few minutes and try again.

Details Mentioned on Bihar BEd Admit Card 2026

  • Candidate’s full name and father’s/mother’s name
  • Roll number / Application number
  • Exam name: Bihar B.Ed CET 2026
  • Exam date and reporting time (7 June 2026)
  • Examination centre name, code, and full address
  • Candidate’s photograph and signature
  • Important instructions for the exam day

In case of any discrepancy (name, photo, date of birth, or centre), contact the university helpline immediately.

Documents Required with Bihar BEd Admit Card 2026 Candidates must carry the following on exam day:

  • Printed copy of the Bihar BEd Admit Card 2026 (clear printout on A4 size paper)
  • One original photo ID proof (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence, or any Government-issued ID)
  • Extra photocopy of admit card and ID (recommended for safety)

Candidates without a valid admit card and original photo ID will not be permitted to enter the examination hall.

Exam-Day Instructions for Bihar BEd 2026

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card.
  • Carry only permitted items: admit card, original photo ID, and basic stationery as allowed.
  • Strictly avoid bringing mobile phones, smartwatches, calculators, electronic devices, notes, or any prohibited items.
  • Follow all instructions given by invigilators and maintain discipline. Any malpractice will lead to cancellation of candidature.
  • Preserve the admit card safely after the exam as it will be required for counselling and admission.
