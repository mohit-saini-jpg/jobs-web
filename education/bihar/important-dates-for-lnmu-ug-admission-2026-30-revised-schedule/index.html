Detailed Information

LNMU UG Admission 2026-30 is open. Online applications for BA, B.Sc, and B.Com 1st Semester (4-Year Honours Programme under CBCS) started on 25 May 2026. The last date to apply without late fee is 09 June 2026.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have passed Class 12 (Intermediate) from a recognized board. Subject-wise eligibility for Honours programmes will apply as per university rules. Reservation benefits as per Bihar Government norms.

Required Documents

  • Matric (Class 10) Marksheet
  • Intermediate (Class 12) Marksheet
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • EWS Certificate (if applicable)
  • Disability Certificate (if applicable)
  • Other relevant certificates

Step-by-Step Application Process

  • Visit the official application portal lnmuadm.samarth.ac.in
  • Click on the Admission link for 2026-30 session
  • Register using valid mobile number and email
  • Fill personal, academic, and college/subject preference details
  • Upload scanned documents
  • Pay application fee (₹500)
  • Submit and download the confirmation page

Selection & Counseling Process

LNMU will release the 1st Selection List on 17 June 2026. Shortlisted candidates must report to their allotted college for Counseling-cum-Admission between 19 June to 02 July 2026 with all original documents.
