How to Download Bihar DECE LE Admit Card 2026

To download the DECE LE 2026 hall ticket, follow these steps:

  • Visit the official BCECEB website: https://bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, look for the “Online Portal” section and click on “Download Admit Card of DECE[LE]‑2026”.
  • You will be redirected to the DECE LE 2026 online exam portal on admissions.nic.in/bihar.
  • Under Registered Candidate Sign In, enter your Registration Number/Application Number, Password or Date of Birth, and the Security Pin/Captcha shown on the screen.
  • Click on “Sign In / Submit” to log in. Your Bihar DECE LE Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Check all details (name, exam date, time, exam centre) carefully and then download and print 2–3 clear A4‑size copies of the admit card.

If you have forgotten your registration number or password, use the “Forgot Registration No./Password” option on the portal (if available) or refer to your email/SMS, and contact BCECEB helpdesk if needed.

Details Mentioned on Bihar DECE LE Admit Card 2026

The DECE LE hall ticket 2026 contains vital information, which candidates must verify:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Father’s name and basic personal details
  • Registration number / roll number
  • Exam name: Diploma Entrance Competitive Examination (Lateral Entry) – DECE LE 2026
  • Exam date: 24 May 2026, exam time and reporting time (as specified)
  • Exam centre name, centre code and full address
  • Category and other eligibility details (if printed)
  • Important instructions and exam‑day guidelines

In case of any discrepancy (name, photograph, exam date, centre details), candidates should immediately contact BCECEB through the helpdesk before the exam day.

Documents Required with Bihar DECE LE Admit Card 2026

On the DECE LE 2026 exam day, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the Bihar DECE LE Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout, colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Driving Licence Voter ID Any other government‑issued ID accepted as per BCECEB instructions.
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Voter ID
  • Any other government‑issued ID accepted as per BCECEB instructions.
  • Additional documents, if specified (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter for candidates availing such facilities).

Entry to the exam centre will not be permitted without the printed DECE LE hall ticket and valid photo ID.
