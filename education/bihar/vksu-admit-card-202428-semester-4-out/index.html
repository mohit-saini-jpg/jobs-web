How to Download VKSU Admit Card 2024‑28 Semester 4

Follow these steps to download your VKSU Semester‑4 admit card:
  1. Go to the VKSU exam portal: https://vksuexams.com/admitcard.aspx
  2. Select the relevant option: “2024‑28” session and “Semester‑4” from the dropdown.
  3. Enter your Registration Number / Roll Number and DOB (or other required credentials) as asked.
  4. Click “Submit” or “Download Admit Card”.
  5. The VKSU Admit Card 2024‑28 (Semester‑4) will be displayed on the screen.
  6. Verify all details carefully.
  7. Download the admit card PDF and take at least two clear printouts to carry on exam day.
Students are advised to download the admit card as early as possible to avoid last‑minute technical issues.

Details Mentioned on VKSU Semester 4 Admit Card 2024‑28

The VKSU Semester‑4 admit card includes the following information:
  • Student’s full name and parents’ name
  • Roll number / Registration number and session (2024‑28)
  • Course and semester (e.g., BA Semester‑4, BSc Semester‑4, etc.)
  • Exam date and session (morning/afternoon)
  • Exam centre name and full address
  • Subject list for Semester‑4
  • Instructions for candidates and hall‑ticket‑related guidelines
If any detail is incorrect (name spelling, course, exam centre, or date), students must immediately contact the VKSU Examination / Examination Cell through the official helpline or email provided on the website.

Documents Required with VKSU Admit Card 2024‑28

On the exam day, students must carry:
  • A printed copy of the VKSU Admit Card 2024‑28 (Semester‑4)
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Voter ID Driving Licence
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID
  • Driving Licence
  • Any other document if specifically mentioned in the admit card or official notification
Students without a valid admit card and acceptable ID proof will not be allowed to appear for the examinations.

Exam‑Day Instructions for VKSU Semester 4 2024‑28

To ensure smooth conduct of the VKSU Semester‑4 exams, students are advised to follow these exam‑day instructions:
  • Reach the exam centre at least 30-40 minutes before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card.
  • Carry only essential items: admit card, ID proof, and permitted stationery (blue/black ball‑point pen, etc.).
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, Bluetooth devices, or any electronic gadgets inside the exam hall.
  • Follow the instructions of the invigilators and maintain discipline throughout the examination.
  • Do not carry bulky bags or unnecessary items; follow the centre‑specific rules for baggage.
  • Keep the VKSU admit card safe even after the exam, as it may be required for result verification and future semesters.
