Detailed Information

Bihar State Madrasa Education Board has declared the BSMEB Result 2026 for Wastania (Class 8), Fauquania (Class 10) and Maulvi (Class 12) examinations for the current academic session. BSMEB Result 2026 has been released online on the official website – bsmebpatna.com. Students of all three madrasa levels can now check their marks and overall result status using their examination credentials.

To download the marksheet, candidates need to visit the BSMEB result page, select the appropriate exam (Wastania, Fauquania or Maulvi), enter their roll code/roll number and submit the details. After viewing the online marksheet, they should carefully verify their name, madrasa name, subjects, marks and pass/fail status and contact the board or their madrasa immediately in case of any discrepancy in the recorded details.

How to Check BSMEB Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your BSMEB Wastania, Fauquania or Maulvi Result 2026:

  • Visit the official Bihar State Madrasa Education Board website: https://bsmebpatna.com/.
  • On the homepage, look for the Result section or the specific link for Wastania/Fauquania/Maulvi Result 2026.
  • Click on the appropriate exam link according to your class (Wastania – Class 8, Fauquania – Class 10, Maulvi – Class 12).
  • You will be redirected to the result page.
  • Enter your Roll Number and/or Roll Code as mentioned on your admit card.
  • Click on Submit / View Result to display your marksheet.
  • Check your personal details, madrasa name, subject wise marks, total marks, grade/division and result status, then download or print the marksheet for future use in admissions and documentation.
