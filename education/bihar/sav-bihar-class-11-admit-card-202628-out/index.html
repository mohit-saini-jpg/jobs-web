SAV Bihar Class 11 Admit Card 2026‑28 Out

Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has released the SAV Bihar Class 11 Admit Card 2026‑28 for admission to Simultala Awasiya Vidyalaya, Jamui. The hall ticket for the Class 11 entrance examination is now available online on the dedicated CBT portal for the 2026‑28 session.

Candidates who applied for SAV Bihar Class 11 Admission 2026‑28 can download their admit cards by logging in with their registration details on the BSEB Simultala portal. The admit card must be printed and carried to the examination centre on the day of the test.

How to Download SAV Bihar Class 11 Admit Card 2026‑28

  • Visit the official CBT portal bsebsimultala.cbrt.co.in/bseb-2026/bseb-landing (or go via the links provided on savsecondary.biharboardonline.com / savbihar.ac.in type portals).
  • Click on the option for “SAV Class 11 Admit Card 2026‑28 / Print Admit Card / Print Roll No”.
  • On the login page, enter your Application/Registration Number or Login ID and Date of Birth / Password in the required fields.
  • Enter the captcha/security code shown on the screen and click on Login / Submit.
  • Your SAV Bihar Class XI Admit Card 2026‑28 will appear showing your name, roll number, exam date, centre and other details.
  • Download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies of the admit card for the entrance test and for future reference.

If the portal is busy or slow, try again after some time; ensure that your registration details are correctly entered.

Details Mentioned on SAV Bihar Class 11 Admit Card 2026‑28

The SAV Bihar Class 11 entrance admit card 2026‑28 includes key personal and exam information such as:

  • Candidate’s name and father’s name.
  • Registration number / application number and roll number.
  • Exam name: Simultala Awasiya Vidyalaya Class 11 Admission Test 2026‑28.
  • Exam date and reporting time.
  • Examination centre name, centre code and full address.
  • Candidate’s photograph and signature as uploaded in the application.
  • Category and other relevant details (if printed).
  • Important exam‑day instructions to be followed at the test centre.

If any discrepancy is noticed in your name, photograph, date of birth, category or exam centre details, contact the SAV/BSEB helpdesk immediately using the numbers and email mentioned in the notice.

Documents Required with SAV Bihar Class 11 Admit Card 2026‑28

On the day of the SAV Class 11 entrance exam 2026‑28, candidates should carry:

  • A printed copy of the SAV Bihar Class 11 Admit Card 2026‑28 (clear A4‑size printout).
  • One original photo ID proof, such as school ID card, Aadhaar Card or other government/institution‑issued ID, as accepted by BSEB/SAV.

Carrying 1-2 extra photocopies of the admit card and ID is advisable in case one is misplaced. Students without a valid admit card will not be allowed to appear for the entrance exam.

Exam‑Day Instructions for SAV Bihar Class 11 Entrance 2026‑28

  • Reach the examination centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card to complete verification and seating; late candidates may be denied entry.
  • Carry only necessary items like admit card, ID, and basic stationery as allowed; follow the detailed instructions provided on the admit card and by the invigilators.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, electronic gadgets, notes, books or any other prohibited items inside the exam hall.
  • Sit on the seat allotted as per your roll number and carefully follow the on‑screen/OMR instructions depending on the exam mode notified.
  • Preserve the admit card even after the test, as it may be required at later stages of admission and counselling.
