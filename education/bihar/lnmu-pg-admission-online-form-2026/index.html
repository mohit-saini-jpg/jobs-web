Courses Offered in LNMU PG Admission 2026

Lalit Narayan Mithila University offers a wide range of Postgraduate programmes including M.A. in Hindi, English, Sanskrit, Urdu, Maithili, Philosophy, Economics, History, Political Science, Sociology, Psychology, Geography, and other subjects. It also offers M.Sc. in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Botany, Zoology, and other science disciplines along with M.Com. in Commerce.

These courses are available at University Departments and affiliated PG teaching colleges under LNMU. The programmes follow the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) and aim to provide quality higher education with updated syllabus and experienced faculty. Students can choose subjects according to their graduation background and career goals.
