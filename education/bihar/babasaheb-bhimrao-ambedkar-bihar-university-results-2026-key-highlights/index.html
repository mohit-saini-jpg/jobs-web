Details

Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar Bihar University (BRABU) has officially released the BRABU UG Semester 1 Result 2025-29 for CBCS courses. The provisional results for Undergraduate Semester 1 examinations under the academic session 2025-29 are now available online.

BRABU Result 2026 has been announced online on the official website - brabu.ac.in. Students who appeared for the UG Semester 1 CBCS examinations can now check and download their results using their roll number and login credentials through the official result portal.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the provisional result. In case of any discrepancies, they should immediately contact the university authorities for correction.
