Bihar ITICAT Admission Form 2026 Overview

The ITICAT-2026 is conducted by BCECEB for admission into government and private ITIs in Bihar. The entrance test is followed by counselling for seat allotment. Always refer to the official BCECEB portal for the latest updates, as dates and rules are subject to change.

Bihar ITICAT 2026 Important Dates
  • Online Registration Start: 16 March 2026
  • Last Date to Submit Application: 14 April 2026
  • Last Date for Fee Payment: 15 April 2026
  • Application Editing: 16–17 April 2026
  • Admit Card Download: 08 May 2026
  • Proposed Exam Date: 17 May 2026
Bihar ITICAT Eligibility Criteria 2026

Candidates must have passed or be appearing in the Class 10 (High School) examination from a recognized board. Specific trade-wise eligibility may apply check the official prospectus for details.

Bihar ITICAT Age Limit 2026

  • Minimum age: 14 years as on 01 August 2026
  • For Motor Mechanic Tractor (MMT/MT) trades: Minimum 17 years

There is no upper age limit.

Bihar ITICAT Required Documents
  • Class 10 mark sheet and certificate
  • Photo and signature (scanned as per specifications)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile certificate for Bihar residents
  • Disability certificate (if claiming PH reservation)
  • Valid email ID and mobile number
How to Apply Online for Bihar ITICAT Admission Form 2026
  • Visit the official online portal Bihar ITICAT.
  • Click on New Registration and enter basic details (email, mobile).
  • Verify with OTP and create password.
  • Login and fill the multi-step application form: Personal Information → Contact Details → Educational Information → Upload Photo & Signature.
  • Preview, submit, and proceed to payment.
  • Pay the fee and download the confirmation page.
Common Mistakes to Avoid While Filling the Form
  • Entering incorrect personal or educational details
  • Uploading blurred or wrong-sized photo/signature
  • Paying the wrong fee category
  • Forgetting to take a printout of the confirmation page
  • Not checking eligibility before applying
