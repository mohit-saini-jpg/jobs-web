Bihar Board 10th, 12th Compartment Result 2026

Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has officially released the Bihar Board 10th, 12th Compartment Result 2026 for students who appeared in the supplementary examinations. The results are now available for both Class 10 (Matric) and Class 12 (Intermediate) students across various streams including Arts, Science, and Commerce.

Bihar Board Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - https://www.biharboardonline.org/. Students can check and download their results by entering their roll number and roll code through the login portal.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the scorecard. In case of any discrepancy, they should immediately contact the Bihar Board authorities for correction.

How to Check Bihar Board Compartment Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your Bihar Board Class 10 or 12 compartment result online:

  • Visit the official website - https://www.biharboardonline.org/
  • Click on the link for “Bihar Board 10th or 12th Compartment Result 2026”
  • Enter your Roll Code and Roll Number in the login fields
  • Click on the ‘Submit’ button
  • Your result will appear on the screen
  • Download and take a printout for future reference
