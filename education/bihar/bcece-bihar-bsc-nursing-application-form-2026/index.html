BCECE Bihar B.Sc Nursing 2026 Eligibility Criteria

  • Nationality & Domicile: Indian Citizen and Permanent Resident of Bihar
  • Gender: Male & Female
  • Age Limit: Minimum 17 years as on 31 December 2026 (No upper age limit)
  • Educational Qualification: Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology & English
  • Minimum Marks: General – 45% aggregate | SC/ST/OBC – 40% aggregate
  • Appearing Candidates: Eligible if appearing in 10+2 in 2026

How to Fill BCECE Bihar B.Sc Nursing Application Form 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official website: bceceboard.bihar.gov.in
  • Click on the BCECE-2026 Online Application link
  • Register using a valid Email ID and Mobile Number
  • Fill in personal, educational, and contact details
  • Upload scanned documents (Photo, Signature, etc.)
  • Pay the application fee online
  • Preview the form and submit
  • Download and print the confirmation page

Documents Required

  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Signature (in black/blue ink)
  • Class 10th and 12th mark sheets & certificates
  • Bihar Domicile Certificate
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Valid Email ID and Mobile Number

BCECE Bihar B.Sc Nursing Syllabus 2026

The syllabus is based on Class 11 & 12 Bihar Board / NCERT Science stream syllabus.

Physics: Kinematics, Laws of Motion, Work Energy and Power, Gravitation, Thermodynamics, Oscillations and Waves, Current Electricity, Electromagnetic Induction, Optics, Dual Nature of Matter, Atoms and Nuclei, etc.

Chemistry: Structure of Atom, Chemical Bonding, States of Matter, Thermodynamics, Equilibrium, Redox Reactions, Hydrocarbons, Biomolecules, Polymers, Environmental Chemistry, etc.

Biology: Diversity of Living Organisms, Cell Structure and Function, Plant Physiology, Human Physiology, Reproduction, Genetics and Evolution, Biotechnology, Ecology and Environment, Biology in Human Welfare, etc.
