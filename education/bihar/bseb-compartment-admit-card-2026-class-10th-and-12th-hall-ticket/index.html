4How to Download BSEB Compartment Admit Card 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphAs per the board’s instructions, only schools can access the BSEB Compartment Admit Card 2026. The steps are:
2listordered
  • Visit the official BSEB portal for admit cards: intermediate.biharboardonline.com for Intermediate (Class 12) exam.biharboardonline.org / biharboardonline.com for Secondary (Class 10) and other exams
  • intermediate.biharboardonline.com for Intermediate (Class 12)
  • exam.biharboardonline.org / biharboardonline.com for Secondary (Class 10) and other exams
  • Click on the link for “Secondary Special & Compartmental Exam 2026 Admit Card” or “Intermediate Compartmental Exam Admit Card 2026”.
  • Log in using school user ID and password provided by BSEB.
  • Select the class and exam type, then download admit cards for all registered students.
  • Take printouts, verify details, affix headmaster’s signature and school seal, and distribute to students.
3paragraphThe board has allowed schools to download and distribute admit cards up to 6 May 2026, so institutions must complete the process within this window.

7Important Instructions on BSEB Compartment Admit Card 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphThe BSEB Compartment Admit Card 2026 carries important exam‑day instructions that students must follow strictly:
2listunordered
  • Students must carry the original BSEB compartment admit card 2026 to the exam centre; without the hall ticket, entry will not be permitted.
  • Students should also carry a valid photo ID and required stationery as per board guidelines.
  • Electronic items such as mobile phones, calculators, smart watches, Bluetooth devices or any communication gadgets are strictly prohibited in the exam hall.
  • Students must reach the examination centre well before reporting time to complete verification and seating.
  • All personal details, roll number, subjects, exam dates and centre address printed on the admit card must be checked carefully; any discrepancy should be reported to the school before the exam.
