BSEB Compartment Admit Card 2026 - Class 10th and 12th Hall Ticket

The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has released the BSEB Compartment Admit Card 2026 for Class 10 and Class 12 compartment and special examinations. The Bihar Board admit card 2026 for Intermediate and Matric Special Examination and Intermediate Compartmental Examination is now available on the official portals, and schools must download and distribute hall tickets to all eligible students.

BSEB Compartment Admit Card 2026 Download Link

The BSEB 10th and 12th compartment admit cards 2026 are available on the official Bihar Board admit card portals. School heads must log in using their user ID and password to download the BSEB compartment and special exam admit cards for all eligible students. After downloading, schools must print, sign, stamp and distribute the hall tickets to students well before the examination dates.

How to Download BSEB Compartment Admit Card 2026

As per the board’s instructions, only schools can access the BSEB Compartment Admit Card 2026. The steps are:

  1. Visit the official BSEB portal for admit cards: intermediate.biharboardonline.com for Intermediate (Class 12) exam.biharboardonline.org / biharboardonline.com for Secondary (Class 10) and other exams
  2. intermediate.biharboardonline.com for Intermediate (Class 12)
  3. exam.biharboardonline.org / biharboardonline.com for Secondary (Class 10) and other exams
  4. Click on the link for “Secondary Special & Compartmental Exam 2026 Admit Card” or “Intermediate Compartmental Exam Admit Card 2026”.
  5. Log in using school user ID and password provided by BSEB.
  6. Select the class and exam type, then download admit cards for all registered students.
  7. Take printouts, verify details, affix headmaster’s signature and school seal, and distribute to students.

The board has allowed schools to download and distribute admit cards up to 6 May 2026, so institutions must complete the process within this window.

Important Instructions on BSEB Compartment Admit Card 2026

The BSEB Compartment Admit Card 2026 carries important exam‑day instructions that students must follow strictly:

  • Students must carry the original BSEB compartment admit card 2026 to the exam centre; without the hall ticket, entry will not be permitted.
  • Students should also carry a valid photo ID and required stationery as per board guidelines.
  • Electronic items such as mobile phones, calculators, smart watches, Bluetooth devices or any communication gadgets are strictly prohibited in the exam hall.
  • Students must reach the examination centre well before reporting time to complete verification and seating.
  • All personal details, roll number, subjects, exam dates and centre address printed on the admit card must be checked carefully; any discrepancy should be reported to the school before the exam.
