Purnea University UG Admission 2026-30

Purnea University UG Admission 2026-30 online application has started. Purnea University, Purnia has invited online applications for admission to the Four-Year Undergraduate Programme under the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) for the session 2026-2030 in various regular courses offered by its Constituent and Affiliated Colleges.

Purnea University is conducting centralized online admission for various undergraduate courses for the academic session 2026-2030. The entire process is being done through the Samarth Portal. Students can apply for multiple programmes through a single application form.

Eligibility Criteria for Purnea University UG Admission 2026-30

Candidates must have passed the Intermediate (Class 12th) Examination. Candidates who have passed Class 12th in 2026 and have not received the original marksheet can upload the WEB COPY downloaded from the official Board website or DigiLocker copy, duly attested by the Principal of their school. Applications will be provisionally accepted and confirmed only after verification of original marksheet.

Documents Required
  • Class 12th Marksheet (Original / Web Copy / DigiLocker)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • PwD Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport size photograph
  • Signature
Courses Offered

Four-Year Undergraduate Programmes under CBCS

Purnea University offers a wide range of regular undergraduate courses under the Four-Year Undergraduate Programme in the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) for the session 2026-2030. These courses are available in various Constituent and Affiliated Colleges of Purnea University. Students can apply for two different programmes/subjects simultaneously through one application form. The programmes include traditional Arts, Science, Commerce and other subjects as per university guidelines. Detailed course list and subject combinations are available on the official admission portal.
