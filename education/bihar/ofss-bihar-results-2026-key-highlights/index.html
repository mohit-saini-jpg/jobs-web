Details

The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB), through the Online Facilitation System for Students (OFSS), has officially released the OFSS Bihar 2nd Merit List 2026 for admission to class 11 (Intermediate) for the 2026-27 academic session. The second merit list has been published for students seeking admission to Arts, Science and Commerce streams in various government, non-government and affiliated schools and colleges across Bihar. OFSS Bihar Result 2026 (2nd Merit List) has been released online on the official website - ofssbihar.net.

Students can check and download their OFSS Bihar 2nd Merit List 2026 status by logging in with their application number and password through the student login portal. After login, candidates can view their allotment status, allotted school or college and can download the intimation letter for admission and document verification. Students are advised to carefully verify their personal details, allotted institution and stream, and contact OFSS or the respective institution in case of any discrepancy.

How to Check OFSS Bihar 2nd Merit List 2026

Follow the step by step process given below to check and download OFSS Bihar 2nd Merit List 2026 for class 11 admission in an easy way.

  1. Visit the official OFSS Bihar website at ofssbihar.net.
  2. On the homepage, click on the link for 2nd Merit List or the know status / student login link for Intermediate admission.
  3. If using the know status link, enter your application reference number and other required details, then submit to view your selection status.
  4. If using the student login link, enter your registered mobile number or application number and password, then click on login.
  5. After login, check your OFSS Bihar 2nd Merit List 2026 allotment details such as allotted school or college, stream and reporting instructions.
  6. Download and print the intimation letter or allotment letter for further admission and document verification at the allotted institution.
  7. Follow the date and schedule mentioned in the intimation letter to complete admission formalities within the given time.

Students can also directly open the result login pages using the links provided above and then enter their credentials to check their OFSS Bihar 2nd Merit List 2026 status.
