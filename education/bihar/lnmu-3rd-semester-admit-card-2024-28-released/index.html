LNMU 3rd Semester Admit Card 2024-28 Released

Lalit Narayan Mithila University has released the LNMU 3rd Semester Admit Card 2026 for UG session 2024-28 on its official portal. Students of BA, B.Sc and B.Com can now download their LNMU 3rd Semester Admit Card 2024-28 online using their University Roll Number and registered mobile number.

The UG Semester 3 examinations for session 2024–28 will be conducted from 22 April 2026 to 11 May 2026 at various exam centres under LNMU. All candidates must carry a printed copy of their admit card to the exam hall, otherwise they will not be allowed to appear in the examination.

How to Download LNMU UG 3rd Semester Admit Card 2026

Students can download their LNMU UG 3rd Semester admit card in a few simple steps from the official portal.

Step-by-step process

  1. Open any browser on your mobile or computer and visit the official LNMU website: https://www.lnmu.ac.in.
  2. On the left side of the home page, click on the “Online Portal (UG)” or “Student Login / UG Portal” section.
  3. In the UG portal, find and click on the link such as “Get Admit Card Semester-III (2024-28)” or “UG Semester 3 Admit Card 2026” under the admit card / examination section.
  4. You will be redirected to the admit card download page for UG Semester 3 examination 2026.
  5. Enter your University Roll Number and registered Mobile Number (or Registration Number, if asked).
  6. Click on the “Submit / Download Admit Card” button.
  7. Your LNMU 3rd Semester Admit Card 2024-28 will appear on the screen.
  8. Check all the details carefully and then download and print the admit card on A4 size paper.
Details Mentioned on LNMU UG 3rd Semester Admit Card

After downloading the admit card, students should verify all the printed information. Typically, the LNMU UG Semester 3 admit card contains the following details.

  • Student’s Name
  • Father’s / Mother’s Name
  • University Roll Number
  • Registration Number
  • Course and Semester (BA/B.Sc/B.Com – 3rd Semester)
  • Session (2024–28)
  • Exam Centre Name and Address
  • Exam Date and Shift / Time
  • Subject Name and Code
  • Photograph and Signature
  • Important examination instructions

If any detail on the LNMU 3rd Semester Admit Card 2024-28 is incorrect or unclear, students should immediately contact their college or university helpline before the examination.

LNMU UG 3rd Semester Exam Date 2026 (Session 2024–28)

The university has scheduled the UG Semester 3 examination for session 2024–28 between 22 April 2026 and 11 May 2026. The detailed subject-wise exam programme is generally released separately by LNMU on its examination portal.

Key points:

  • Exams will be conducted in offline mode at designated examination centres.
  • Students should reach the exam centre at least 30–45 minutes before reporting time mentioned in the admit card.
  • No entry will be allowed without the original printed admit card and a valid college/university ID card, as per standard university practice.
Documents to Carry with LNMU 3rd Semester Admit Card

On the day of the exam, candidates must carry the following items as per usual university guidelines.

  • Printed LNMU 3rd Semester Admit Card 2024-28 (clear print on A4 paper)
  • Valid college ID card / university ID
  • Aadhar card or any other valid government ID (if required at the centre)
  • Necessary stationery items (pens, pencils, etc.) as allowed

Electronic gadgets, calculators, mobile phones and any kind of textual material are generally not allowed inside the exam hall, so students should read the instructions printed on the admit card carefully.
