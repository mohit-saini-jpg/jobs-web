5How to Download LNMU UG 3rd Semester Admit Card 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphStudents can download their LNMU UG 3rd Semester admit card in a few simple steps from the official portal.
2paragraphStep-by-step process
3listordered
  • Open any browser on your mobile or computer and visit the official LNMU website: https://www.lnmu.ac.in.
  • On the left side of the home page, click on the “Online Portal (UG)” or “Student Login / UG Portal” section.
  • In the UG portal, find and click on the link such as “Get Admit Card Semester-III (2024-28)” or “UG Semester 3 Admit Card 2026” under the admit card / examination section.
  • You will be redirected to the admit card download page for UG Semester 3 examination 2026.
  • Enter your University Roll Number and registered Mobile Number (or Registration Number, if asked).
  • Click on the “Submit / Download Admit Card” button.
  • Your LNMU 3rd Semester Admit Card 2024-28 will appear on the screen.
  • Check all the details carefully and then download and print the admit card on A4 size paper.
