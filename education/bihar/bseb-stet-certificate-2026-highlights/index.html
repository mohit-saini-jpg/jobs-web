BSEB STET Certificate 2026 is the official eligibility certificate issued by the Bihar School Examination Board to candidates who qualify the Bihar STET exam for the respective paper and subject. It acts as proof that you have cleared the teacher eligibility test and are now eligible to apply for teaching posts for classes and subjects covered under STET in Bihar.
Remember, this is an eligibility document, not an appointment letter, so it doesn’t guarantee a job, but it is compulsory for almost every government and many private school teaching recruitments in the state.

BSEB STET Certificate Distribution 2026 (Offline)

For many STET cycles, BSEB has started distributing the certificates through designated district-wise centres, usually at the District Education Office or other notified venues. Candidates have to visit the centre on the given date with their original documents and ID proof to collect the certificate.
If the board follows the same pattern, BSEB will publish a detailed schedule PDF mentioning roll numbers, district names, venue addresses, and dates for certificate distribution.

District-wise distribution schedule

You can guide students on what to look for:
  • BSEB will release a notice PDF for BSEB STET Certificate 2026 distribution on the official website.
  • The PDF generally contains: District name Venue (office/college/school) Date and reporting time Roll number ranges for each day.
  • District name
  • Venue (office/college/school)
  • Date and reporting time
  • Roll number ranges for each day.
Encourage users to carefully check their roll number and reach only on the mentioned date and time to avoid rush and confusion.

Documents required at distribution centres

BSEB usually asks candidates to bring both original and photocopies of their documents for verification before handing over the STET certificate. Commonly required documents include:
  • STET Admit Card
  • STET Result / Scorecard (if available)
  • Aadhar Card or any valid photo ID
  • Recent passport-size photographs
  • Educational qualification certificates and mark sheets (as per eligibility)
  • Caste/Category certificate (if applicable)
Students should carry at least one set of self-attested photocopies, because sometimes staff ask to submit copies.

BSEB STET Certificate 2026 Download Process (Online)

In some cycles, BSEB or the partner portal has allowed candidates to download the Bihar STET certificate online using their login credentials. If that option is enabled for the 2026 certificate, the board will clearly mention the link and instructions in the official notice.
Step-by-step process to download
Once online download starts for BSEB STET Certificate 2026, the typical process will be something like this (you can refine once exact link is live):
  1. Visit the official STET portal – usually bsebstet.org or the link shared in the notice.
  2. Click on the “Bihar STET Certificate” or similar link shown on the homepage.
  3. Log in using your registration number, date of birth, and password or OTP as asked.
  4. After login, look for the “Download Certificate” or “BSEB STET Certificate 2026” link in your dashboard.
  5. Click the link; your certificate will open in PDF format.
  6. Download the PDF and save it on your device, then take multiple printouts for future use.
Remind users to check all details printed on the certificate immediately after download and contact the board if any serious error appears.

Details printed on Bihar STET Certificate 2026

Candidates often want to know exactly what is printed on the certificate before they see it. Typically, the Bihar STET certificate includes:
  • Candidate’s full name
  • Father’s / Mother’s name
  • Roll number and registration number
  • Category (General, OBC, SC, ST, etc.)
  • Paper name (Paper 1 or Paper 2)
  • Subject / discipline for which STET is qualified
  • Marks obtained or only “Qualified” status (as per board format)
  • Year and session of the exam
  • Date of issue of certificate
  • Signature and seal of BSEB officials.
Tell students to check spelling of name, subject code and category carefully, because these details are later used in recruitment documents.

BSEB STET Certificate 2026 - Important Instructions

To avoid any last‑minute problems, ask students to keep these points in mind:
  • Always download or collect your STET certificate as soon as possible after release; don’t wait until a recruitment notification comes.
  • Keep both soft copies (PDF) and multiple hard copies in a safe place, and avoid folding the original certificate too much.
  • Use only official links from bsebstet.org or biharboardonline.bihar.gov.in; ignore random Telegram or WhatsApp links for certificate downloads.
  • If you lose your certificate in future, contact BSEB immediately for duplicate issue procedure; each board has its own rules and fees.
