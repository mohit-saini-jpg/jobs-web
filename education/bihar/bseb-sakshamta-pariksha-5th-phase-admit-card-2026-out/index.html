BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha 5th Phase Admit Card 2026 Out

Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has released the BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha 5th Phase Admit Card 2026 for the Competency Test for Local Bodies Teachers (CTT) on its official Sakshamta portal. As per the latest notice, the Bihar Sakshamta Pariksha 5th phase admit card was issued on 25 May 2026, ahead of the examination scheduled in June.

The BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha Phase‑5 exam will be conducted from 5 June 2026 to 8 June 2026 at various exam centres across Bihar in computer‑based test mode. Only in‑service teachers and librarians appointed by local bodies who have registered for Phase‑5 can download their admit card and appear in this competency test.

How to Download BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha 5th Phase Admit Card 2026

Candidates can download their Bihar Sakshamta Pariksha 5th phase admit card 2026 through the following steps:

  1. Visit the official Sakshamta portal bsebsakshamta.com and click on Login or go directly to bsebsakshamta.com/login.
  2. On the homepage, find and click the link “Sakshamta Pariksha 5th Phase Admit Card 2026” or the relevant admit card link under latest notifications.
  3. Enter your Application Number/Registration Number/Enrollment Number and Password or Date of Birth, then enter the captcha code.
  4. Click the Login / Submit button to access your dashboard.
  5. Click on the “Download Admit Card” option; your BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha 5th phase hall ticket 2026 will appear on the screen.
  6. Check all details carefully and download and print a clear A4‑size copy of the admit card; keep extra copies for safety and future use.

If candidates face issues such as “server busy” or “page not loading”, they should wait a while and try again during off‑peak hours.

Details Mentioned on BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha 5th Phase Admit Card 2026

The Bihar Sakshamta Pariksha Phase‑5 admit card 2026 contains important information that candidates must verify before the exam:

  • Candidate’s name
  • Father’s / mother’s name
  • Application / registration / enrollment number
  • Exam name: BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha (CTT) 5th Phase 2026
  • Exam date and shift (between 5-8 June 2026)
  • Exam centre name, centre code and full address
  • Candidate’s photograph and signature
  • Category and post details (teacher/librarian, level) where applicable
  • Reporting time, gate‑closing time and exam duration
  • Important exam‑day instructions and guidelines

These details are referenced in official notifications and admit‑card‑download guides, which advise candidates to check their personal information, exam date and centre details thoroughly. If any error is found, candidates must contact BSEB via the support contacts given on the portal.

Documents Required with BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha 5th Phase Admit Card 2026

On the day of the BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha Phase‑5 exam, candidates need to carry:

  • A printed copy of the BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha 5th Phase Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout, colour or high‑quality black and white).
  • One original photo identity proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence or any other government‑approved photo ID.

Guides and notices emphasise that candidates must bring both the admit card and a valid photo ID; otherwise they will not be allowed to sit for the exam.

Exam‑Day Instructions for BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha 5th Phase 2026

For the Bihar Sakshamta Pariksha 5th phase CBT, candidates should follow these key exam‑day guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time printed on the admit card; late entry after gate‑closing time will not be allowed.
  • Carry only permitted items: admit card, valid photo ID and allowed stationery; follow the list of allowed and banned items given in the BSEB instructions.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not allowed), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any electronic gadgets inside the exam hall; possession of such items can lead to cancellation of candidature.
  • Follow all instructions displayed on the computer screen and those given by invigilators; indulging in any unfair means or misbehaviour may result in disqualification and further disciplinary action by BSEB.
  • Preserve the admit card even after the exam, as details may be required during result, document verification and further processes.
