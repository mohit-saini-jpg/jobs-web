PPU Semester 2 Admit Card 2026

Patliputra University, Patna has released the PPU Semester 2 Admit Card 2026 for UG (Regular) courses for the academic session 2024–28 on its online examination portal. Eligible students of BA, BSc and BCom 2nd semester can now download their hall tickets in online mode only; admit cards are not issued offline.

According to recent updates, the university has already notified the conduct of UG Semester II theory and practical examinations under the 2024–28 session, and admit cards have been activated on the ppuponline.in portal before the start of exams. Students must carry a printed admit card and a valid ID to be allowed into the examination hall.

How to Download PPU Semester 2 Admit Card 2026

Follow these steps to download your Patliputra University UG Sem 2 hall ticket:

  • Go to the official PPU online portal: https://ppuponline.in.
  • On the homepage, under the Examination section, click on the link like “Download Admit Card [UG Semester 2]” or similar UG Sem‑II admit card link.
  • You will be redirected to the “Admit Card Search by form number” page for UG Semester 2.
  • In the Form Number / Exam Form No. field, enter your exam form number as given by the college, excluding the prefix (e.g., 20A/210) and leading zeros, as instructed on the page.
  • Enter your Date of Birth in the required format and then fill the captcha code shown on the screen.
  • Click on “Search”; your PPU UG Semester 2 Admit Card 2026 will be displayed on the screen as a PDF.
  • Download and print 2–3 clear A4‑size copies of the admit card to carry to the exam centre.

If you don’t know your form number, contact your college/department to obtain it; without the correct form number, the admit card cannot be downloaded.

Details Mentioned on PPU Semester 2 Admit Card 2026

Check the following details printed on your PPU UG 2nd semester admit card:

  • Student’s name and photograph (if printed)
  • Registration number and Form/Exam form number
  • Programme and session: UG Semester‑II, Session 2024–28 (BA/BSc/BCom Regular)
  • College name and code
  • Exam centre name, code and full address
  • Subject‑wise paper names and paper codes (as per 2nd semester)
  • Exam dates and timings (as per the PPU exam routine)
  • Important instructions and rules for the examination hall

In case of any discrepancy in your name, college, subject or exam centre details, contact your college administration or the PPU examination branch before the exam starts.

Documents Required with PPU Semester 2 Admit Card 2026

On each exam day, students must carry:

  • A printed copy of the PPU UG Semester 2 Admit Card 2026 downloaded from ppuponline.in.
  • A valid photo identity proof, such as: college ID card, Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence, or any other ID accepted by the university.
  • Any additional document specified by the university/college, if mentioned in the admit card or exam notice.

Without the printed admit card and valid ID, students may be denied entry to the examination hall.
