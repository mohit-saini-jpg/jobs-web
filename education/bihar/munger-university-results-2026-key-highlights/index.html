How To Check Munger University UG CBCS Sem 1 Result 2026

Follow the step-by-step process below to check and download Munger University UG CBCS Semester 1 Result 2026 for Session 2025-29.

  1. Visit the official Munger University result page at https://mungeruniversity.ac.in/page/Result or directly go to the result portal https://munivexam.in/.
  2. On the result page, look for the link mentioning "UG CBCS Sem-I Session 2025-2029 Result Exam - 2025" and click on it.
  3. You will be redirected to the UG CBCS Semester 1 result portal where you need to select your college from the list provided.
  4. Enter your roll number in the specified field correctly as printed on your admit card.
  5. After entering the details, click on the View Result or Submit button to fetch your UG CBCS Sem 1 result.
  6. Your Munger University UG CBCS Semester 1 (2025-29) result will appear on the screen displaying your name, roll number, college, course, subject-wise marks and result status.
  7. Download the marksheet in PDF format or take a printout and preserve it for future academic and admission purposes, and refer to the official notice PDF if you need more clarification about the result publication.

How to Download Munger University Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your Munger University PG 3rd Semester Result 2026:

  1. Visit the official Munger University result page https://mungeruniversity.ac.in/page/Result.
  2. Look for the link titled similar to “PG Sem-III 2024-2026 Exam-2025 Result Notice”.
  3. Click on the View Result link which opens the PDF: https://mungeruniversity.ac.in/images/document/PG_Sem-III_2024-2026_Exam-2025,_Result_Notice.pdf.
  4. Once the PDF opens, use Ctrl+F and type your roll number or name to find your result line.
  5. Check your subject-wise marks/grades, total marks and result status (pass/fail/back, if mentioned).
  6. Download the PDF and, if needed, take a printout for your academic records.
