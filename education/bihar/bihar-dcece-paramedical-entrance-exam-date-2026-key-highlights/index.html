Bihar DCECE Paramedical Eligibility Criteria 2026 (PM & PMM)

The eligibility criteria decide who can apply for Bihar DCECE Paramedical Entrance Exam 2026. Candidates must fulfil the conditions related to nationality, domicile, educational qualification and age as mentioned in the official prospectus.
  • Nationality & domicile: The candidate should be an Indian citizen and must satisfy the domicile/residency conditions of Bihar as per BCECEB rules. Reservation benefits are also applicable only as per the rules given in the brochure.
  • Educational qualification for PM: For Para Medical (Intermediate level) courses, candidates must have passed or be appearing in Class 12 (Intermediate) from a recognised board in the relevant stream. The required subjects for each course (like GNM, Lab Technician etc.) are given in the prospectus.
  • Educational qualification for PMM: For Para Medical Matric level courses, candidates must have passed or be appearing in Class 10 (Matric) from a recognised board. Course-wise additional conditions, if any, are clearly mentioned in the brochure.
  • Age limit: Minimum and maximum age limits differ for various trades like ANM, GNM and other paramedical courses. Candidates should refer to the official PDF for exact age criteria and relaxation rules.
For accuracy and to avoid any mistake in application, always cross-check the eligibility section of the official prospectus instead of relying only on summarized information.

Step-by-Step Process to Apply Online
  1. Visit the official BCECEB website and click on the “DCECE-2026” link under the examinations section.
  2. Click on “Apply Online” for DCECE 2026 and complete the new registration by entering basic details like name, mobile number, email ID and creating a password.
  3. Login using the registration credentials and select the appropriate course group (PM/PMM).
  4. Fill the online application form with personal information, category details, communication address and educational qualification details carefully.
  5. Upload scanned copies of recent passport-size photograph and signature in the prescribed size and file format mentioned in the prospectus.
  6. Pay the examination fee through online modes such as Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card or UPI.
  7. After successful payment, submit the form and generate the final confirmation page.
  8. Download and print the filled application form/acknowledgement and keep it safe for future use, especially during admit card download and counselling.
Make sure the mobile number and email ID used during registration remain active till the completion of the entire admission process.

Bihar DCECE Paramedical Admit Card 2026

The Bihar DCECE Paramedical Admit Card 2026 will be released online by BCECEB a few days before the exam date. Candidates must download their admit card themselves; it will not be sent by post. Admit card is a mandatory document for entry into the exam centre.
To download the admit card, candidates should:
  • Visit the official website and open the “Download Admit Card of DCECE-2026” link.
  • Login using registration number and password or date of birth.
  • Download the admit card and verify details like name, photograph, exam date, reporting time, and exam centre address.
  • Take a clear printout on A4-size paper and keep multiple copies as backup.
On the exam day, candidates must carry the printed admit card along with a valid photo ID proof and any other documents mentioned in the instructions.

Bihar DCECE Paramedical Exam Pattern 2026 (PM & PMM)

Understanding the exam pattern is very important for effective preparation. The Bihar DCECE Paramedical Entrance Exam 2026 is conducted in offline mode using OMR sheets where candidates have to darken the correct option with black or blue ballpoint pen.
Key features of the exam pattern include:
  • Mode of exam: Offline (pen-and-paper, OMR based)
  • Type of questions: Objective type Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
  • Total questions: 90
  • Total marks: 450
  • Marks per question: 5 marks for each correct answer
  • Negative marking: Generally, there is no negative marking (subject to the latest official rules)
  • Exam duration: Around 2 hours 15 minutes
Candidates should always confirm the latest pattern and marking scheme given in the prospectus to avoid any confusion.

Bihar DCECE Paramedical Syllabus 2026

The syllabus for Bihar DCECE Paramedical Entrance Exam 2026 is based on the level of the qualifying examination (Matric or Intermediate) and is clearly defined in the official brochure. Knowing the detailed syllabus helps students prepare in a focused and efficient way.
Broadly, the syllabus includes:
  • General Science: Questions from Physics, Chemistry and Biology of Class 10/12 level, depending on the course group. Topics include basic laws, definitions, formulas and everyday applications.
  • Mathematics / Quantitative Aptitude: Fundamental arithmetic, algebra, geometry, mensuration and simple data interpretation as per school level.
  • Language (English and Hindi): Grammar, vocabulary, synonyms-antonyms, sentence correction and comprehension passages.
  • General Knowledge: Current affairs, Indian history, geography, polity, economy and important events related to Bihar and India.
For serious preparation, candidates should refer to the official syllabus in the prospectus and use standard school-level textbooks, previous year question papers and practice sets.

Bihar DCECE Paramedical Result and Counselling 2026

After the exam, BCECEB will evaluate OMR sheets and declare the Bihar DCECE Paramedical Result 2026 on the official website. The result is usually published in the form of rank cards or merit lists for different course groups. Candidates can download their rank card by logging in with their credentials.
Based on the ranks, BCECEB conducts online counselling for admission to various paramedical diploma institutes in Bihar. During counselling, candidates have to register, fill in their choices of courses and colleges, and participate in seat allotment rounds. It is important to follow the counselling schedule, document verification rules and fee payment instructions carefully to secure the allotted seat.
