How to Download BCECE Admit Card 2026

Once the link is activated, candidates can download the BCECE admit card 2026 by following these steps:
  1. Visit the official BCECEB website bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
  2. On the notice board or BCECE-2026 section, click on the link “Download Admit Card of BCECE-2026”.
  3. You will be redirected to the BCECE-2026 Online Examination / Application portal (admissions.nic.in).
  4. Enter your Registration Number and Password (or registration number and date of birth, as instructed) and click on Login / Sign In.
  5. After login, click on the “Download Admit Card” link visible on your dashboard for BCECE-2026.
  6. Your BCECE admit card 2026 will appear on the screen with your exam date, group (PCMB / Agriculture), and exam centre details.
  7. Check all details carefully and download and print 2–3 clear A4-size copies of the hall ticket for use on exam day and future reference.

Details Mentioned on BCECE Admit Card 2026

The BCECE Admit Card 2026 carries important exam and candidate information that must be checked carefully before the exam day. Typically, the following details are printed on the hall ticket:
  • Candidate’s name, photograph and signature
  • BCECE 2026 registration number / roll number
  • Group applied for (PCMB, Agriculture etc.) and category (if applicable)
  • Exam name: Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination (BCECE) 2026
  • Exam date, shift and reporting time for the candidate’s group
  • Exam centre code, centre name, city and complete address
  • Basic instructions on permissible items, reporting rules and exam-day conduct
If any mismatch or error is noticed in your personal details, photo, group, or exam centre information, you should contact the BCECE Board immediately before the exam for clarification and correction as per the official instructions.

Documents Required with BCECE Admit Card 2026

On the day of the BCECE 2026 examination, candidates should carry:
  • A printed copy of the BCECE Admit Card 2026 (clear A4-size printout; colour or good-quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence or any other government-issued ID accepted as per BCECEB instructions.
  • Any additional documents mentioned in the admit card or information brochure (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter if you are using a scribe facility, or other documents specifically asked by BCECEB).
Entry to the exam centre will not be allowed without the printed BCECE admit card and a valid photo ID.

Exam-Day Instructions for BCECE Admit Card 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the BCECE 2026 examination, candidates should follow these guidelines:
  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time printed on the BCECE admit card so that verification, frisking and seating can be completed; late entry after the scheduled reporting or gate-closing time may not be allowed.
  • Carry only essential items: BCECE Admit Card 2026 (A4 printout), valid photo ID and basic permitted stationery such as blue/black ball-point pens, pencils and other items mentioned in the instructions.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not specifically permitted), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the examination hall; violation of these rules may lead to cancellation of your paper and further disciplinary action.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the BCECE admit card and those announced by the invigilators during the exam; indulging in unfair means, talking to other candidates or disobeying exam rules can result in cancellation of your examination and possible debarment from future exams conducted by BCECEB.
  • Keep your BCECE Admit Card 2026 safely even after the exam, as it may be required later during result checking, counselling, choice filling, seat allotment and document verification.
