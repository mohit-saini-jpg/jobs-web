OFSS Bihar 11th Admissions Online Form 2026

The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has extended the admission deadline for Class 11 under the Online Facilitation System for Students (OFSS) for the 2026-28 academic session. Students who were allotted seats through the first merit list can now complete their admission process till May 18, 2026.

Earlier, the admission window was open from May 5 to May 12. The board stated that the extension has been provided for students who were unable to complete the admission formalities within the initial timeline. Officials have also clarified that this will be the final opportunity for candidates to secure their allotted seats.

Eligible students who have passed Class 10 (Matriculation) from BSEB, CBSE, ICSE, or any recognized board can submit their Common Application Form through the official OFSS portal: ofssbihar.net.

How to Apply for OFSS Bihar 11th Admission 2026
  1. Visit the official website: ofssbihar.net
  2. Click on the Common Application Form for Intermediate Admission 2026-28.
  3. Register using a valid mobile number and email ID (OTP verification required).
  4. Fill personal details, educational qualifications, and choose up to 20 college & stream preferences.
  5. Upload scanned copies of required documents.
  6. Pay the application fee of ₹350/- online.
  7. Submit the form and take a printout of the acknowledgment slip.
Documents Required for OFSS Bihar 11th Admission 2026
  • Class 10th Marksheet and Admit Card
  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • Recent Passport-size Photograph
  • Scanned Signature
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Mobile Number and Email ID for OTP verification
  • JIS Number / Roll Code (for Bihar Board students)
OFSS Bihar 11th Admission 2026 Selection Process

Admission is purely merit-based. BSEB will release multiple merit lists based on Class 10 marks and student preferences. Students whose names appear in the merit list must download the intimation letter and report to the allotted college with original documents for admission confirmation.
