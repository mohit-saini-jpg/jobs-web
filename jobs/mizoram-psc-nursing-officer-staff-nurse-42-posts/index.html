FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in MPSC Nursing Recruitment 2026?
A
Mizoram PSC has announced 42 contractual vacancies including 40 Nursing Officer and 2 Staff Nurse positions under Health & Family Welfare and Home (Prisons) Departments.
Q2What is the last date to apply for MPSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
The last date to submit online applications is 12 June 2026 (up to 4:00 PM) through the MPSC online portal.
Q3What is the eligibility for MPSC Nursing Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must have RNRM registration for Nursing Officer or GNM certificate for Staff Nurse, computer proficiency certificate, and Mizo language proficiency (exemption if studied Mizo up to Class-X).
Q4What is the age limit for MPSC Nursing Vacancy 2026?
A
The age limit is 21 to 35 years as on 01 January 2026, with relaxation for SC/ST and other categories as per Government rules.
Q5What is the salary for MPSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
Selected candidates will receive Level 7 Pay Matrix (7th CPC) salary as per Government of Mizoram contractual employee rules.
Q6What is the selection process for MPSC Nursing Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection includes Written Examination, Document Verification, and Mizo Language Proficiency Test (where applicable).
Q7Is there application fee for MPSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
PwBD candidates are exempted from application fee. Other candidates must pay as per MPSC norms (check official portal).
Q8What is the qualification required for MPSC Staff Nurse 2026?
A
Candidates must have GNM (General Nursing & Midwifery) certificate and be registered with Nursing Council.
