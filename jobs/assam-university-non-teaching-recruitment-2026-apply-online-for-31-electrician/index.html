Assam University Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026 Salary Structure

Selected candidates will be placed as per the 7th CPC Pay Matrix Level applicable to each post, ranging from Level-1 (₹18,000-56,900) for attendant/MTS-level posts up to Level-14 (₹1,44,200-2,18,200) for Group A posts such as Finance Officer, Director (Computer Centre), and Controller of Examinations, along with applicable allowances. New Pension Scheme rules will apply upon appointment.

Assam University Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026 Application Fee

₹1,000/- (non-refundable, online payment) for UR, EWS, and OBC candidates applying for Sl No. 01-07 ₹500/- (non-refundable, online payment) for UR, EWS, and OBC candidates applying for Sl No. 08-16 No application fee for SC, ST, PwBD, Ex-servicemen, and Women candidates; however, they must pay ₹200/- towards postal expenses Candidates applying for more than one post must apply and pay fees separately for each post

Assam University Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

The University may conduct a Written Test/Skill Test for all Group B and Group C posts, covering Reasoning Ability, Simple Arithmetic, General Knowledge, Domain Knowledge, Finance & Accounts, Examinations, and Language Proficiency in English/Hindi, or a Noting/Drafting/Skill Test as per job requirements. As per Government of India/UGC directives, no interview will be held for Group B and Group C posts; merit will be determined based on qualifications and test/skill evaluation. The University may adopt its own procedure for recruitment to Group A Non-Teaching posts. Applications will be screened and shortlisted by Screening Committees; mere eligibility does not guarantee a call for test/interview. SC/ST outstation candidates called for tests/interviews will be reimbursed to-and-fro rail fare (Sleeper Class) or ordinary bus fare by the shortest route, except those already in Central/State
