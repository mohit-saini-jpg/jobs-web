WII Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

Shortlisting for the online interview is based on essential qualification, age limit, percentage of marks, specialization relevance, research experience, and publications. The scoring pattern differs by post category: % Marks in Bachelor's Degree: 25 Marks Specialization relevance to the project (via internship, volunteering, or work experience): 25 Marks % Marks in Master's Degree: 25 Marks Specialization relevance to the project: 25 Marks UGC/CSIR/ICAR NET with JRF or WII-NET Passed: 5 Marks % Marks in Post-Graduation: 25 Marks Research/Work Experience: 10 Marks Publications (journals, conferences, book chapters): 10 Marks The top 10 shortlisted candidates for each advertised project/position will be called for an online interview.
