Important Instructions

The applicant must be a citizen of India. All posts are regular and permanent (except Lien vacancies). Selected candidates will be covered under the National Pension System (NPS). Candidates must satisfy themselves of the suitability for the position before applying - minimum essential qualifications are compulsory regardless of higher qualifications held. CGPA to percentage conversion will be done as per formula of the degree-awarding institution. OBC-NCL certificate must have been issued within 2 years of the last date of application and a self-declaration (Annexure-I) must also be submitted. EWS certificate must be valid for Financial Year 2025-26 and clearly state the same. If caste/category certificate is in a language other than Hindi or English, a certified translation must be produced at the time of interview/document verification. Candidates must disclose details of any close relatives employed at IISER Mohali, and any penalties/inquiry against them in the application. Canvassing in any form or bringing political or any other influence will be treated as disqualification. Candidates applying for more than one post must pay the application fee separately for each post. No hard copy/postal applications will be entertained - online mode only. Once submitted, the application cannot be edited or withdrawn under any circumstances. All correspondence will be made via registered email only - no separate hard copy letters will be sent. Candidates selected for Group A post interviews are eligible for travel reimbursement (AC II Tier by rail; air fare as per slab with authorized travel agent tickets only). Any corrigendum/addendum shall be published only on the Institute website. Candidates are advised to visit www.iisermohali.ac.in regularly. Any legal dispute arising out of this advertisement shall be subject to jurisdiction of the Hon'ble Punjab and Haryana High Court, Chandigarh only. In case of ambiguity between Hindi and English versions of the advertisement, the English version shall prevail. All decisions of the Competent Authority, IISER Mohali regarding eligibility, acceptance/rejection, selection, and related matters are final and binding.

FAQs

Q1What is IISER Mohali Non-Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
IISER Mohali Non‑Faculty Recruitment 2026 is a direct recruitment drive for 40 regular Non‑Teaching posts in Group A, Group B, and Group C at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Mohali, an Institute of National Importance under the Ministry of Education, Government of India.
Q2How many vacancies are released under IISER Mohali Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026?
A
IISER Mohali has released a total of 40 vacancies for various Non‑Teaching posts such as Superintending Engineer, Deputy Registrar, Senior Technical Officer (IT), Junior Technical Assistant, Junior Office Assistant, Attendant and others across Group A, B and C categories.
Q3What is the last date to apply online for IISER Mohali Non-Faculty posts?
A
The last date to submit the IISER Mohali Non‑Faculty online application form is 15 June 2026 up to 05:00 PM, and candidates are advised to apply well before the closing time to avoid last‑minute technical issues.
Q4What are the eligibility qualifications for IISER Mohali Non-Teaching posts?
A
The educational qualification for IISER Mohali Non‑Teaching posts ranges from Matriculation (10th pass) and 3‑year Diploma to Bachelor’s degree, Master’s degree, B.E./B.Tech, M.Sc. or BS‑MS, along with post‑wise required experience, and all degrees must be from recognized Universities/Institutions as specified in the official notification.
Q5What is the age limit for IISER Mohali Non-Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit varies post‑wise from 32 years to 56 years as on 15 June 2026, with applicable relaxations for SC, ST, OBC‑NCL, EWS, PwBD, Ex‑Servicemen and IISER Mohali employees as per Government of India norms mentioned in the advertisement.
Q6What is the salary or pay scale for IISER Mohali Non-Teaching posts?
A
The IISER Mohali Non‑Teaching posts carry pay levels from Level 1 to Level 13 in the 7th CPC Pay Matrix depending on the post, offering attractive salary along with admissible allowances and coverage under the National Pension System (NPS) as per Government of India rules.
Q7What is the application fee for IISER Mohali Non-Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 1,298 (Rs. 1,100 + 18% GST) for UR and OBC‑NCL candidates, Rs. 708 (Rs. 600 + 18% GST) for EWS candidates, while SC, ST, PwBD, Ex‑Servicemen and female candidates are fully exempted from paying the application fee, and the fee must be paid separately for each post applied.
Q8What is the selection process for IISER Mohali Non-Teaching posts?
A
The selection process for IISER Mohali Non‑Teaching posts includes shortlisting by the Scrutiny Committee followed by Written Test, Skill Test and/or Interview, as applicable for each post, and the scheme of examination and syllabus will be notified later on the Institute’s official website.
