KCET UGCET Result 2026 - Download Scorecard Online at cetonline.karnataka.gov.in

KCET UGCET Result 2026 has been officially released on June 6, 2026, at 12 noon. Over 3.3 lakh candidates can now download their scorecards from the official KEA portal at cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea using their application number and the first four letters of their name.

The Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) declared the result following a formal morning press conference. The UGCET scorecard includes subject-wise marks, total score, and merit rank for counselling purposes,.

How to Check KCET UGCET 2026 Result Online

You can check your result on two official portals:

  • KEA Official Website: cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea
  • Karnataka Results Portal: karresults.nic.in

Both portals display the same information. Once KEA activates the result link, follow these steps :

KCET UGCET 2026 Scorecard Download Steps

  • Open your web browser and navigate to cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea
  • Click on the "KCET 2026 Result / UGCET Scorecard" link under "Latest Announcements"
  • A secure login portal window will open on your screen
  • Enter your KCET 2026 Application Number and the first four characters of your name exactly as printed on your hall ticket
  • Double-check the filled credentials and click "Submit"
  • Your comprehensive rank card will appear on screen showing subject-wise marks, total score, and merit rank
  • Download the PDF file and take printouts for document verification

KCET UGCET 2026 Exam Dates & Candidate Info

The KCET 2026 examination was conducted over two days:

  • Physics & Chemistry: April 23, 2026
  • Mathematics & Biology: April 24, 2026

Registration for KCET 2026 began from January 17, 2026 . This year, over 3.11 lakh candidates appeared for the pen-and-paper entrance exam held across the state .

Important Instructions for Students

Before downloading, keep the following in mind:

  • Log in to cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea at 12 noon on June 6, 2026
  • Download the scorecard and save two backup copies
  • Verify name, roll number, marks, and category details on the scorecard
  • Keep your registration details ready and regularly check the official website for updates

KCET UGCET 2026 Counselling Process

After downloading your scorecard:

  • Use the scorecard for counselling and further admission process
  • Take printouts for document verification
  • Monitor the official website for counselling timeline announcements
