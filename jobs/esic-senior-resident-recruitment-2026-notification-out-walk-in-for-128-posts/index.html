How To Apply

  • This is a Walk-in-Interview recruitment. There is no online application process. Candidates must appear in person at the Walk-in-Interview on the scheduled date for their respective department/specialty. Candidates must report between 09:30 AM and 10:30 AM at the Academic Block, 2nd Floor, ESI-PGIMSR & ESIC Medical College, Joka, Kolkata – 700104 for document verification. Candidates reporting beyond 10:30 AM will NOT be considered. At the time of the interview, candidates must submit the completed Application Form (Annexure-I) and Verification Slip (Annexure-II) along with all original documents and one set of self-attested copies.
  • Documents to be Submitted at the Time of Walk-in-Interview:
  • Completed Application Form in prescribed format (Annexure-I) - neatly typed in A4 size with double spacing
  • Verification Slip (Annexure-II)
  • Demand Draft for application fee of Rs. 500/- in favour of "ESI Fund Account No.1", payable at Kolkata (if applicable)
  • NMC / State Medical Council Registration Certificate (original and self-attested copy)
  • Educational qualification certificates and mark sheets from Matriculation or equivalent onwards (originals and self-attested copies)
  • Experience Certificate (if any)
  • Proof of Age (Matriculation Certificate / Admit Card)
  • Caste / Category Certificate for SC, ST, OBC, and EWS candidates - in updated Government of India format (original and self-attested copy)
  • Bond Clearance Certificate from Appointing Authority (if candidate has completed compulsory post-PG Bond Service)
  • NOC from present employer (for candidates already working in any Government Institution)
  • 02 recent passport-size photographs
  • Walk-in-Interview Venue: Academic Block, 2nd Floor, ESI-PGIMSR & ESIC Medical College, Joka, Diamond Harbour Road, Joka, Kolkata – 700104 Email: [email protected] | Tel: (033) 2990 0731

Important Instructions

  • Candidates who have completed 3 years tenure as Senior Resident in any Government Institution need NOT apply. Senior Residents already working in any Government Institution must obtain NOC from the respective institute before applying. The total period shall not exceed 3 years including any previous service. Candidates on bond are NOT eligible to apply; candidates who have completed compulsory post-PG Bond Service must produce a Bond Clearance Certificate from Appointing Authority. Candidates must report between 09:30 AM and 10:30 AM - those reporting beyond 10:30 AM will not be considered under any circumstances. The Demand Draft for application fee must be issued after the date of this advertisement (11.05.2026); DDs issued before this date will not be accepted. A security deposit of Rs. 32,000/- by Demand Draft must be paid at the time of joining; it will not be refunded if the Senior Resident terminates the agreement in between the engagement period. Private practice of any kind is strictly not allowed during the engagement period. No TA/DA will be admissible for joining or for any other purpose. For Non-Medical candidates, Non-Practicing Allowances (NPA) will be deducted from the consolidated remuneration of Rs. 1,48,669/-. The offer of engagement for Super Specialty Senior Residents will be subject to successful engagement of teaching faculty in the same Super Specialties. This engagement is purely contractual and terminable by either side with one month's prior notice. Selection does not confer any right of confirmation or continuation. The Dean, ESI-PGIMSR, ESIC Medical College & Hospital, Joka reserves the right to vary vacancies including increase, decrease, cancellation, or withdrawal of offer of engagement without assigning any reason.
