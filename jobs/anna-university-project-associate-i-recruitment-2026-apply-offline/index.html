Overview

Anna University ke liye ek Project Associate I ke liye apply karne ki aavashyakta hai. Ye naukri offline apply karne ke liye hai. Apna application 25 June 2026 tak pahunchayein. Yeh naukri Tamil Nadu me available hai. Iske liye aapko project management, research, aur data analysis ke kaam karna hoga.

Expert Analysis

Yeh recruitment students aur research scholars ke liye bahut acchi opportunity hai. Yeh naukri unhe research par kaam karne aur project management ke kaushal seekhne ka mauka degi.

Who Should Apply

Ideal candidate students, research scholars, aur professionals hai jo project management, research, aur data analysis ke kaam karte hain.

Preparation Tips

Aapko project management, research methods, aur data analysis ke bare mein padhna hoga. Aapko CV aur cover letter ki bhi taiyari karni hogi.

Job Profile

Project Associate I ka job role hai project management, research, aur data analysis. Aapko project ke liye data collect karna hoga, data analyze karna hoga, aur result presentation karna hoga. Iske liye aapko communication skills, problem-solving skills, aur time management skills honi chahiye.

Selection Strategy

Selection process mein online exam, interview, aur document verification hoga. Aapko online exam mein project management, research, aur data analysis ke concept par questions aayeinge. Interview mein aapko project management, research, aur data analysis ke kaushal ke bare mein sawal puchhe jayenge. Document verification mein aapko CV aur qualifications ke documents verify karni hogi.
