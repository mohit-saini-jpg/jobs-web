Age Limit

Details

Candidates must be at least 18 years and not more than 40 years of age as on 05-06-2026. The upper age limit is relaxed by 5 years for ST, SC, Physically Handicapped, and Government employee candidates. However, Government Employees belonging to ST/SC/Physically Handicapped category shall not get this additional relaxation over and above the general relaxation of 5 years already available to them. | There is no fixed number of attempts for the examination - candidates can apply and appear multiple times as long as they are otherwise eligible and within the prescribed upper age limit for their category.

Syllabus

Details

The Written Examination consists of two papers - Paper I (General Ability Test, 50 marks) and Paper II (Job Oriented Technical Paper, 120 marks). All questions are OMR-based MCQ type, answered in English only. Negative marking will apply as per TPSC norms amended from time to time. The detailed syllabus is reproduced below from Annexure-B and Annexure-C of the official notification (as published in the Tripura Gazette Extraordinary Issue, February 25, 2026). | Paper I - General Ability Test (50 Marks, 50 Questions of 1 Mark each) | Paper II - Job Oriented Technical Paper (Full Marks: 120, OMR MCQ - 120 Questions of 1 Mark each)

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the TPSC Online Application Portal, accessible on the official website https://tpsc.tripura.gov.in. Hard copy applications will not be accepted under any circumstances. The online portal will be active from 12-05-2026 to 05-06-2026 (5:30 PM). New users (first-time applicants) must complete One Time Profile Registration (OTPR) on the TPSC website before applying - a User ID will be generated and sent to the registered mobile number/email ID upon successful registration.

  • Existing OTPR users can directly apply using their User ID. Candidates should avoid submitting multiple applications; if done unavoidably, only the application with the latest Application Number will be considered valid, and fee paid against other application numbers will not be adjusted. After the closing date, a 7-day correction window will be available for making corrections in the application form - no changes are allowed after this window closes. Candidates are not required to upload supporting documents at the time of online application; documents will be called after the written examination result is published.

  • Documents to be Submitted After Written Examination (on being called):

  • Age proof - Birth Certificate or Admit Card of Madhyamik examination

Important Instructions

  • Only bonafide citizens of India with valid PRTC are eligible to apply. Candidates must possess the prescribed minimum qualification on the closing date of application. No hard copy applications will be accepted - online submission only. Recruitment fee is non-refundable; incomplete applications without fee will be rejected. Government employees must produce NOC at the time of interview if application not routed through Proper Channel. Mobile phones and electronic gadgets are banned inside examination/interview premises. Examination venue closed 10 minutes before scheduled commencement - latecomers not allowed. Candidates are advised to visit https://tpsc.tripura.gov.in regularly for updates on recruitment steps. Questions in all papers to be answered in English only - no other language permitted. Negative marking on MCQ questions will apply as per TPSC norms. For any eligibility-related enquiry, candidates should approach the concerned Government Department - the Commission will not entertain such representations. All decisions of the Commission regarding eligibility, selection, and other matters are final and binding.
