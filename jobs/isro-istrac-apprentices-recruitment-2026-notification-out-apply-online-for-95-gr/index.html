ISRO ISTRAC Apprentices Recruitment 2026 : Application Fee Details

ISRO ISTRAC has not prescribed any application fee for this apprenticeship recruitment. The application process is free of cost for all candidates across all categories.

ISRO ISTRAC Apprentices Recruitment 2026 : Eligibility & Qualification

Details
  1. The ISRO ISTRAC Apprentice 2026 eligibility rules are strict and candidates should verify each condition carefully before applying. Only those who passed their qualifying examination as regular candidates are eligible - correspondence, private, or sandwich course degrees are not accepted. Candidates currently pursuing higher studies (e.g., B.E./B.Tech for diploma holders) are also not eligible
  2. Graduate Apprentice (GA01-GA06): B.E./B.Tech in the relevant engineering discipline from any recognised University. For GA07 (Library Science): MLISc. (Library and Information Science). Diploma Apprentice (DA01-DA06): Diploma in Engineering awarded by a recognised State Board in the respective discipline. DA06 requires Diploma in Commercial Practice. ITI Trade Apprentice (TA01-TA05): ITI certificate in the concerned trade from any recognised University
  3. Candidates must have passed their qualifying course in 2024, 2025, or 2026 only. Candidates are also required to join within 3 years of acquiring the degree/diploma/ITI (calculated from the month and year of passing as stated in the certificate). If this three-year window lapses before the date of joining, the candidature will be cancelled
  4. Every candidate must register on the National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS) portal at nats.education.gov.in before applying. The NATS Enrolment Number must be included in the email application. Applications without a valid NATS Enrolment Number are liable to be rejected. Candidates must upload their Degree/Diploma/ITI certificate and a valid government-issued ID on the NATS portal during registration
  5. Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply. Ex-apprentices, candidates currently undergoing apprenticeship elsewhere, or candidates with one year or more of work experience in Government/Public/Private Sector as on the date of joining are not eligible. Combining this apprenticeship with any other institution's apprenticeship is not permitted. Courses must be recognised by any University in India or State Boards

ISRO ISTRAC Apprentices Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1How many vacancies are there in ISRO ISTRAC Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
ISRO ISTRAC has announced approximately 95 apprentice vacancies across Graduate, Diploma, and ITI Trade categories under Advt. No. ISTRAC:01:2026 dated 22 June 2026. Positions are distributed across training locations at Bengaluru, Lucknow, and Sri Vijaya Puram in disciplines including Computer Science, Electronics & Communication, Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, and Commercial Practice.
Q2What is the last date to apply for ISRO ISTRAC Apprentice 2026?
A
The last date to submit the email application for ISRO ISTRAC Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is 31 July 2026. Applications must be sent to the dedicated email ID of the respective training location before this date.
Q3What is the eligibility for ISRO ISTRAC Apprentice 2026?
A
Candidates must hold a B.E./B.Tech (Graduate Apprentice), Diploma in Engineering from a recognised State Board (Diploma Apprentice), or ITI certificate (Trade Apprentice) in the relevant discipline. Crucially, candidates must have passed in 2024, 2025, or 2026 only. Only regular candidates are eligible — private, correspondence, and sandwich course qualifications are not accepted.
Q4Is there any application fee for ISRO ISTRAC Apprentice 2026?
A
No. ISRO ISTRAC has not prescribed any application fee for this apprenticeship recruitment. All candidates can apply free of cost.
Q5What is the monthly stipend for ISRO ISTRAC Apprentices?
A
Graduate Apprentice Trainees will receive ₹12,300/month, Diploma Apprentice Trainees will receive ₹10,900/month, and ITI Trade Apprentice Trainees will receive ₹9,600/month during the one-year training period.
Q6How to apply for ISRO ISTRAC Apprentice 2026?
A
Applications are accepted via email only. Candidates must first register on the NATS portal (nats.education.gov.in), then send an email with the Post Code in the subject line and all required details and documents in the body/attachments to the location-specific email ID — [email protected] (Bengaluru), [email protected] (Lucknow), or [email protected] (Sri Vijaya Puram).
Q7Is there a written test for ISRO ISTRAC Apprentice 2026?
A
No written exam is conducted. Selection is based on shortlisting of email applications followed by document verification and an interview. The final merit panel is drawn based on academic percentage in the qualifying degree/diploma/ITI and performance in the interview.
Q8When and where will the interviews be held?
A
Interviews will be held at ISTRAC Lucknow on 27–28 August 2026, at ISTRAC Sri Vijaya Puram on 03–04 September 2026, and at ISTRAC Bengaluru on 09–11 September 2026. Reporting time at all three centres is 8:30 AM. No request to change the interview venue will be entertained.
Q9What is the training duration for ISRO ISTRAC Apprentices?
A
The training period is 12 months for all categories — Graduate, Diploma, and ITI Trade Apprentices — commencing from the execution of the Apprenticeship contract as per the Apprentices Act 1961.
Q10Will ISRO offer a job after the apprenticeship training?
A
No. Apprentices will have no claim whatsoever for regular employment in ISRO or any other Centre/Unit of ISRO or the Department of Space after completion of the one-year training period. The apprenticeship is purely a training engagement under the Apprentices Act.
