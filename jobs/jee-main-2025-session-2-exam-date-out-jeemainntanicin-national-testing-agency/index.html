Candidates follow the steps to download National Testing Agency Exam Date, candidates check the details and download it easily.

  • The examination Schedules page will pop up
  • Choose the course
  • Exam Date for different courses will show up for Regular and Regular / External.
  • Click on the course
  • Exam Date will show up.
  • Check and download.
