How to Download Osmania University Time Table 2026

Students can download the Osmania University Time Table 2026 from the official examination portal by following the steps given below:

  • Visit the official Osmania University examination website.
  • Navigate to the Exam Notifications or Time Table section.
  • Look for the B.E VIII Semester June 2026 timetable link.
  • Click on the relevant examination timetable PDF.
  • The file will open. Download and save it on your device.
  • Take a printout for ready reference during exam preparation.

Note: Always refer to the official Osmania University timetable and check all dates and instructions carefully before the examinations.
