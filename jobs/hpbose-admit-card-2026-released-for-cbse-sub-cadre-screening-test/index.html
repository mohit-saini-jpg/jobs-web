HPBOSE Admit Card 2026 Released for CBSE Sub Cadre Screening Test

The HPBOSE Admit Card 2026 for the Screening Test for Deputation to CBSE Sub Cadre has been made available on the official website. This exam targets in-service teachers from Himachal Pradesh seeking deputation opportunities to CBSE-affiliated government schools. Candidates can now access their hall tickets online by logging in with their credentials.

Download Admit Card: HPBOSE Admit Card 2026

Overview of the Screening Test

The Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education (HPBOSE) organizes this screening test for eligible in-service teachers, including Principals, Lecturers, Trained Graduate Teachers (TGTs), Junior Basic Teachers (JBTs), and other categories.

The test serves as a qualifying step for deputation to CBSE sub-cadre positions. The exam is scheduled for March 22, 2026 (Sunday), from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM. It consists of 100 multiple-choice questions (MCQs), each carrying one mark, with a total duration of 2 hours. There is no negative marking, and the qualifying threshold is 40 marks out of 100.

Admit cards are available exclusively online through the HPBOSE portal no physical copies are sent by post or email.

HPBOSE Admit Card 2026 Release Details

As per the official prospectus, admit cards were scheduled for release 4 days before the exam date. Aligning with this timeline and recent notifications, the HPBOSE Admit Card 2026 became downloadable around mid-March 2026, following the completion of applications (which closed with corrections by March 1, 2026). A specific notification regarding the admit card for the CBSE Sub-Cadre Exams 2026 was issued on March 19, 2026, confirming availability.

Direct Download Link and Steps to Download HPBOSE Admit Card 2026

To download your admit card:

  • Visit the official HPBOSE website: Click Here
  • Navigate to the "CET/Vacancies" or "Online Services" section and select the link for Screening Test for Deputation to CBSE Sub-Cadre 2026.
  • Proceed to the login portal (such as Click Here).
  • Enter your Application Number (serving as login ID), password (received via email during application), or use Application Number/Roll Number along with Date of Birth and CAPTCHA as prompted.
  • Submit the details to view your admit card.
  • Verify all information, download, and print a clear copy for the exam day.

Details Printed on the Admit Card

The hall ticket includes essential information such as:

  • Candidate's name and photograph
  • Roll number
  • Exam date, time, and shift
  • Exam center name and full address
  • Important instructions for candidates

Carefully check your photo, personal details, and center information for accuracy. Any mismatch in the photograph or other discrepancies should be addressed immediately.

Exam Day Instructions

Candidates must carry:

  • Printed HPBOSE Admit Card 2026
  • Original photo ID (preferably Aadhaar card)
  • In-service Teacher Certificate along with the required undertaking

Arrive at the exam center by 10:30 AM. Electronic devices, mobile phones, calculators, or any study materials are strictly prohibited in the examination hall.
