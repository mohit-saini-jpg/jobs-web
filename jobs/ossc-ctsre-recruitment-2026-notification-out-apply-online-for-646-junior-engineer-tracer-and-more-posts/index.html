Age Limit

DetailsCandidates must be a minimum of 21 years and maximum of 42 years as on 01-01-2025, i.e., candidates must not have been born earlier than 2nd January 1983 and not later than 1st January 2004 (both days inclusive). Maximum age indicated is for Unreserved candidates. Relaxation in upper age limit is applicable as per Government of Odisha guidelines.

Physical Eligibility

DetailsThe competition level for OSSC CTSRE 2025 is expected to be high given the 646 Group-B/C technical vacancies open to diploma holders in civil engineering, architecture, and media/AV disciplines across Odisha. The Preliminary Examination, being a general aptitude paper at 10th standard level, will attract a wide pool of aspirants including those already preparing for other state-level exams. The largest chunk of vacancies — 606 seats — is under Junior Engineer (Civil) across three departments, making it the most sought-after post. | The effective competition narrows significantly at Stage-II as the Technical Paper is post-specific and tests core subject knowledge, naturally filtering out those with weak technical preparation. Candidates with strong AutoCAD, computer literacy, or AV production skills will have a clear edge in the specialty posts. Since allotment is based on merit-cum-preference, accuracy in both stages and a well-thought-out preference list are critical to securing the desired post and department.

How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply online only through the official website www.ossc.gov.in; no other mode of application will be accepted. No physical copy/hard copy of the online application form needs to be submitted. Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date. Important instructions on filling the Online Application Form and "How to Apply" are enclosed as Annexure-A to the official notification.
  • Documents Required at the time of Examination / Certificate Verification:
  • Admission Letter (downloaded from www.ossc.gov.in) along with valid photo identity proof in original and photocopy
  • Original academic certificates and marksheets (relevant Diploma/Degree/Certificate onwards)
  • Caste Certificate in the prescribed Govt. of Odisha format (for SEBC/SC/ST candidates)
  • Disability Certificate / UDID Card issued by competent Medical Authority (for PwD candidates) — scanned copy of original to be uploaded at application stage
  • Sports ID Card issued by Director of Sports & Youth Service Department, Govt. of Odisha (for Sports Person candidates)
  • Discharge Certificate (for Ex-Servicemen candidates)
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) from competent controlling authority (for Government servants / PSU employees)
  • Any other documents as intimated in the Admission Letter for Certificate Verification (details in Annexure-B of notification)
  • Apply Online at: https://www.ossc.gov.in
  • For queries: Email [email protected] (complaints on examination conduct to be sent within 05 days of examination completion).

Important Instructions

  • The recruitment shall be guided by "Combined Technical Services Recruitment Examination Rules-2022" as amended from time to time. Candidates must possess a valid email ID and mobile number while applying and keep them active till completion of the recruitment process to receive important messages from the Commission. The appointment will be only against one of the posts carrying the corresponding pay as indicated in the vacancy table; candidates applying for more than one post/service must give option/preference for the post/service in case they qualify. The Commission will adopt a "Normalisation Formula" published vide Notice No.2444/OSSC dated 02.09.2021 for processing results if any examination is conducted through CBRE/OMR mode in multiple batches using different sets of question papers. No Admission Letter for recruitment at any stage shall be sent by post; candidates must download from www.ossc.gov.in regularly for updates. Candidates are advised to submit the Online Application Form well in advance without waiting for the closing date to avoid last-hour rush. All posts are State Cadre posts; the candidate on selection may be asked to serve anywhere in Odisha. OBC (Creamy Layer) candidates are not entitled to SEBC reservation; they must apply under UR category. PwD candidates must ensure they possess a permanent disability certificate issued online and upload the scanned copy of the original (not photocopy) of the Disability Certificate/UDID Card. There shall be no provision for re-evaluation/re-checking of scores; no correspondence in this regard shall be entertained. Mobile phones, Bluetooth devices, spy cameras, smartwatches and any other electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited inside the examination hall. In case of tie in scores, merit will be decided by: (i) Marks in Preliminary Examination, (ii) Date of Birth with older candidate placed higher, and (iii) Alphabetical order of names. The Commission's decision in all matters relating to eligibility, acceptance/rejection of applications, mode of selection, conduct of examination, allotment of examination centres and preparation of merit list shall be final and binding. Any complaint on the conduct of the examination must be sent to [email protected] within 05 (five) days of completion of examination. Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification.
