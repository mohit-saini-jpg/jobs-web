How to Check Mumbai University Result 2026 Online

Follow these steps to check and download your MU result from the official portal:

  • Visit the official result website: mumresults.in.
  • Locate the result link matching your course code and course name from the list above (e.g., B.Pharm Semester VIII or BE NEP 2020 Semester I Supplementary).
  • Click on the relevant link. This opens a PDF web list or a result search page, depending on the course.
  • If a PDF web list opens, use Ctrl+F (or the search function) to find your seat number or PRN.
  • If a search page opens, enter your seat number and other requested details (such as date of birth or PRN) to view your individual result.
  • Download and save the result as a PDF for future reference.

Details Shown on the Mumbai University Marksheet

A typical Mumbai University result/marksheet includes the following details:

  • Student's name and Permanent Registration Number (PRN)
  • Course name, semester, and scheme (CBCGS, CBCS, or NEP 2020)
  • Subject-wise marks (internal and external, where applicable)
  • Total marks, percentage, SGPA/CGPA (for grade-based courses)
  • Overall result status - Pass, ATKT (Allowed to Keep Term), or RL (Result withheld)

Exact formats can vary by course and scheme, so students should refer to their own downloaded result for confirmed figures. Specific passing marks, grading scale, and grace mark policy for each of the courses listed above are not available in the source notification provided and should be confirmed from the official Mumbai University circulars for that programme.

What to Do If You Have ATKT or Your Result Is Withheld

If your result shows ATKT, you will need to clear the pending subject(s) in the next available examination as per Mumbai University's academic rules. If your result is withheld (RL), this is usually due to pending eligibility verification or an internal administrative hold; students should contact their college examination department for the exact reason and next steps, since this information is not specified in the source notification.

Revaluation and Photocopy of Answer Sheets

Students who wish to challenge their marks can generally apply for revaluation or a photocopy of their answer sheet through Mumbai University's online revaluation portal after a result is declared. The exact revaluation window, fee, and application link for the specific courses listed on this page are not available in the source notification - students should check the "Revaluation/Photocopy" section on mumresults.in or mu.ac.in once available for their course.

Important Instructions for Students

  • Always cross-check your result using the correct course code listed against your programme, as similar-sounding branches (e.g., different NEP 2020 engineering specializations) have separate codes and separate result links.
  • Keep your seat number, PRN, and mother's name handy, as these are commonly required to search individual results.
  • Download and save a PDF copy of your result immediately after it is declared, since online web lists may be replaced or moved over time.
  • For discrepancies in marks or personal details, contact your affiliated college's examination department rather than the university directly, in the first instance.
