High Court of Karnataka Group D Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The High Court of Karnataka, Bengaluru, has released a Revised Notification dated 2nd July 2026 inviting online applications for direct recruitment to 106 Group D posts — Peons, Watchman, Sweepers, Peons (House Keeping), Lift Attender, Assistant Binder, Tailor and Dhobi. This recruitment covers two separate cadres: 98 posts (including 1 backlog post) under the Residual Parent Cadre (RPC) vide Notification No. HCRB 8/2025, and 8 posts under the Kalyana Karnataka Region (KKR) vide Notification No. HCRB 9/2025. Educational Qualification

Candidates must possess the following qualification as on the last date fixed for online application: Peons, Watchman, Sweepers, Peons (House Keeping): Tenth Standard (SSLC) pass or equivalent examination. Lift Attender: Tenth Standard pass or equivalent examination, along with experience in lift mechanism. Assistant Binder: Tenth Standard pass or equivalent examination, along with not less than two years of experience in binding work. Tailor: Tenth Standard pass or equivalent examination, along with not less than three years of experience in tailoring work. Dhobi: Tenth Standard pass or equivalent examination, along with work experience as a Dhobi. Note: "Tenth Standard or equivalent" is as prescribed under Government of Karnataka Circular No. DPAR 81 SCR 2017 dated 27.02.2018.

Age Limit

Minimum age: 18 years (as on the last date fixed for online application). General Merit candidates: Maximum 40 years. Other Backward Classes – Category II(A) / II(B) / III(A) / III(B): Maximum 43 years. Scheduled Caste / Scheduled Tribe / Category-I of Other Backward Classes: Maximum 45 years. A one-time age relaxation of 5 years has been provided as per Government Order No. DPAR 262 SRR 2025 dated 29.01.2026.

Selected candidates will draw the following pay scale (Level as per Karnataka Civil Services Pay Rules) along with admissible allowances: Peon, Watchman, Sweeper, Peons (House Keeping), Assistant Binder, Dhobi: Level-2, ₹19,900 – ₹63,200 Lift Attender, Tailor: Level-3, ₹21,700 – ₹69,100 Selected candidates will initially be placed on probation for a period of two years, or an extended period if necessary.

High Court of Karnataka Group D Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

For Peons, Watchman, Sweepers, Peons (House Keeping), Lift Attender, Tailor and Dhobi: The merit list for interview will be prepared based on the percentage of total marks secured in the qualifying examination, applying reservation rules for SC, ST, OBC, women, rural candidates, ex-servicemen and other categories as per law. Candidates called for interview will be 10 times the number of vacancies notified, in order of merit, for each category. For Assistant Binder: Candidates called for interview will be 5 times the number of vacancies notified, in order of merit. Merely attending the interview does not confer any right to appointment. Final selection depends on the High Court of Karnataka being satisfied that the candidate is suitable in all respects.
