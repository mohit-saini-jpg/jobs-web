DCECE 2026 Round 2 Seat Allotment - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 20/07/2026

The Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) is scheduled to publish the DCECE (PE) 2026 Round 2 Seat Allotment Result on 20 July 2026, as per the official counselling schedule. This is the second round of seat allotment for admission to Polytechnic Engineering (PE) diploma courses at government polytechnic colleges across Bihar. Candidates who participated in Round 2 choice filling can check and download their allotment order from the official website, bceceboard.bihar.gov.in, using their registration number and password.

How to Check DCECE 2026 Round 2 Seat Allotment Result

  • Visit the official BCECEB website: bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
  • Go to the "Online Portal of DCECE[PE]-2026" link on the homepage, or check the Download Section for "Second Round Seat Allotment Result of DCECE[PE]-2026" once it is live.
  • Log in using your DCECE(PE) 2026 registration number and password.
  • Your Round 2 seat allotment status allotted institute, branch, and category will be displayed on screen.
  • Download and save the provisional allotment order.
  • Take a printout, since a hard copy is required at the time of document verification.

Documents Required for Round 2 Document Verification

Candidates allotted a seat in Round 2 must carry the following documents to their reporting/nodal centre between 23 and 24 July 2026:

  • DCECE (PE) 2026 Admit Card
  • DCECE (PE) 2026 Rank Card
  • Provisional Seat Allotment Order (downloaded and printed)
  • Verification Slip 2 printed copies
  • Biometric Form 1 copy
  • DCECE (PE) 2026 Online Application Form / Confirmation Page (hard copy)
  • Aadhaar Card (copy)
  • Category certificate, where applicable SC/ST/BC/EBC, Economically Weaker Section (EWS), or Differently Abled (DQ) certificate as relevant to the candidate
  • Class 10 (Matric) marksheet and other original certificates for verification

The exact reporting/nodal centre depends on the allotted institute and is indicated on the candidate's allotment order and verification slip.

What Happens After Document Verification

Once document verification is complete at the reporting centre, the seat is confirmed against the required admission formalities. Candidates who wish to improve their allotment can use the upgradation option during the counselling process rather than surrendering the current seat outright; specific upgradation terms for Round 2 are detailed in the official BCECEB counselling notice. Candidates who do not report for verification within the specified window risk losing the allotted seat, so it's important to complete the process within 23-24 July 2026.

If You Are Not Allotted a Seat in Round 2

Candidates who remain unallotted after Round 2 remain in contention for the subsequent mop-up/spot round, which BCECEB conducts after the regular rounds to fill any remaining vacant seats. The exact mop-up round schedule for DCECE (PE) 2026 is Not Available Officially yet; candidates should watch the official website for the next counselling notice.

Important Instructions for DCECE 2026 Round 2 Candidates

  • Keep your registration number and password ready before the result window opens, since forgotten credentials can be recovered only through the "Forgot Password" option on the counselling portal.
  • Cross-check your allotted institute, branch, and category carefully on the allotment order before proceeding to document verification.
  • Carry both original documents and photocopies to the reporting centre, as verification is done against originals.
  • Complete document verification strictly within the 23-24 July 2026 window to avoid forfeiting the allotted seat.
  • Rely only on the official BCECEB website, bceceboard.bihar.gov.in, for the final and authoritative allotment result.
