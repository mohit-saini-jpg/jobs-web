The University of Kashmir has officially released the Kashmir University BPED Result 2026 for the BPED 3rd semester (Batch 2023) examination conducted in June-July 2025. This latest update under Kashmir University Results 2026 covers BPED students from the 2023 batch, whose odd semester exams were held in the June-July 2025 session and are now available online in marksheet format.

Kashmir University Result 2026 has been announced online on the official website - kashmiruniversity.net, through the dedicated results portal at egov.uok.edu.in/results/. Students can open the BPED 3rd semester (Batch 2023) result link, select the relevant exam, and enter their roll number or registration details to view and download their individual result. After accessing the marks, candidates must carefully verify their name, roll number, course, semester and subject-wise marks, and immediately contact the University of Kashmir or their college examination cell in case of any discrepancy in the result.

Kashmir University Result 2026 Direct Link

Kashmir University has activated the online link for BPED 3rd semester (Batch 2023) June-July 2025 result on its official results portal, allowing students to access their marks directly. From the egov.uok.edu.in/results/ page, students can select the BPED 3rd semester exam from the list of declared results and instantly view or download their marksheet by entering their roll number.

Steps to Check Kashmir University BPED Result 2026

Students can use the steps below to check Kashmir University BPED Result 2026 for the 3rd semester (Batch 2023) June-July 2025 examination.

  • Go to the University of Kashmir official website kashmiruniversity.net and click on the "Results" or "Examination" section, or directly visit https://egov.uok.edu.in/results/.
  • On the results page, locate the entry that reads "BPED 3rd semester (Batch 2023) examination held in June-July, 2025".
  • Click on the corresponding "View" or "View Result" link for the BPED 3rd semester result.
  • You will be redirected to the result view page, where you need to enter your roll number or other required credentials as per your admit card.
  • Submit the details to display your subject-wise marks, grade and overall result status for BPED 3rd semester.
  • Verify your name, roll number, course, semester and all marks carefully, then download or print the online marksheet for future reference.
  • In case of any discrepancy in the marks or personal details, contact your college examination cell or the University of Kashmir exam section for clarification and official rectification.
