Qualification / Eligibility

Junior Consultants: Retired ONGC executives who retired at E1 to E3 level from Production/ Drilling/ Mechanical/ Electrical/ Instrumentation discipline with at least 5 years of in-line experience in Work Over/Drilling field operations. Associate Consultants: Retired ONGC executives who retired at E4 to E5 and E6 level from Production/ Drilling/ Mechanical/ Electrical/ Instrumentation discipline with at least 5 years of in-line experience in Work Over/Drilling field operations.
