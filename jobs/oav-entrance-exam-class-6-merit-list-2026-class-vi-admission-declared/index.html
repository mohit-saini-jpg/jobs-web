How to Check OAV Entrance Exam Class 6 Merit List 2026 Online?

Odisha Adarsha Vidyalaya Sangathan has made the merit list check process simple and quick for students. Candidates only need their registration number and date of birth to view their merit list status on the official portal. Here is a step-by-step process you can follow:

  • Visit the Official Website - Go to the official Odisha Adarsha Vidyalaya Sangathan portal at sundargarh.odisha.gov.in.
  • Navigate to Results - Click on the Results or Examination tab on the homepage.
  • Select OAV Entrance Exam - Choose OAV Entrance Exam from the list of available results.
  • Enter Your Details - Input your Registration Number and Date of Birth in the required fields.
  • Submit and View - Click the Submit button to view your OAV Entrance Exam Class 6 Merit List 2026 on screen.
  • Download the Merit List - Click Download or Print to save your merit list PDF for official use.
  • Verify Your Information - Check that your name, registration number, and admission status are correctly displayed.
