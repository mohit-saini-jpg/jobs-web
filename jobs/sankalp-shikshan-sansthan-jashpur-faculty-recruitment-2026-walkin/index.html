Q7. What is the selection process for the Faculty posts?

The selection process will involve an Interview and a Written Test. Candidates need to perform well in both stages.

Q8. What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?

Candidates must carry all original documents, self-attested photocopies of these documents, and recent passport-size photographs.
