CG Vyapam PAT/PVPT 2026 is the state-level entrance exam conducted by the Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board (CG Vyapam) for admission to undergraduate B.Sc agriculture, horticulture, forestry programmes and diploma courses in animal husbandry and fisheries. The online application process started on 2 April 2026 and the last date to apply is 27 April 2026 (5:00 PM).

The entrance exam will be held on 21 June 2026. This single exam opens doors to BSc (Hons) Agriculture, Horticulture, Forestry, B.Tech (Dairy Technology), Diploma in Animal Husbandry, and Diploma in Fisheries Science in Chhattisgarh institutions. This article provides complete verified details to help students apply successfully.

How to Apply for CG Vyapam PAT/PVPT 2026 – Step-by-Step Guide

  • Visit the official website: vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in
  • Click on “Online Applications” → Select PAT/PVPT 2026
  • Register using a valid mobile number and email ID
  • Fill the online application form with personal and academic details
  • Upload scanned photograph, signature, and required documents
  • Pay the application fee online
  • Submit the form and take a printout for future reference

Application Fee for CG PAT/PVPT 2026

• General Category: ₹200 (approx.) • OBC Category: ₹150 (approx.) • SC/ST/PwD Category: ₹100 (approx.)

Note: Chhattisgarh domicile candidates usually receive fee refund after the exam.
