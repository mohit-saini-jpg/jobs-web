Nagindas Khandwala Merit List 2026

Nagindas Khandwala Merit List 2026 (First Merit List) has been released on 26 May 2026. Candidates can now check and download the merit list for various undergraduate courses on the official website nkc.ac.in. Nagindas Khandwala College (NKC College), Malad West, Mumbai, has published the First Merit List for First Year UG admissions for the academic year 2026-27. The college is affiliated with the University of Mumbai and offers multiple aided and self-financed courses.
