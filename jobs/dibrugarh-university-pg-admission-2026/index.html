Dibrugarh University PG Admission 2026 is open through the Dibrugarh University Admission Test (DUAT) 2026. How to Apply for Dibrugarh University PG Admission 2026

  • Visit the official website — www.dibru.ac.in
  • Click on the DUAT 2026 / PG Admission link.
  • Register with valid email and mobile number.
  • Fill the online application form carefully.
  • Upload required documents.
  • Pay the application fee of ₹1050/-.
  • Submit and take a printout of the confirmation page.

Dibrugarh University PG Admission 2026 Exam Pattern (DUAT)

  • Mode: OMR Based Offline Entrance Test
  • Duration: To be announced (usually 2 hours per paper)
  • Type of Questions: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
  • Subjects: Subject-specific + General Knowledge/Current Affairs/Reasoning (as per programme)

Documents Required

  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Signature
  • Class 10 & 12 marksheets
  • Graduation marksheets & certificate
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Valid ID proof
