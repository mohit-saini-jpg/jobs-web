How to Apply

  • 1Date of Birth proof – Xth Standard Passing Certificate or School Leaving Certificate / Birth Certificate
  • 2XIIth Standard Passing Certificate
  • 3Mark Sheets and Degree/Diploma Certificates of Graduate and/or Post-Graduate and/or Professional Qualification as applicable
  • 4Experience Certificates for each organization (with designation, department, date of joining/leaving, job description)
  • 5If working in PSU/Govt.: documentary evidence of working in next below grade and scale of pay
  • 6No Objection Certificate (NOC) from present employer if working in PSU / Government organization
  • 7Salary Slips (last 3 months), Bank Statement, and latest Form-16 (for private/bank employees)
  • 8Caste/Category Certificate – SC/ST/OBC (NCL)/EWS/PwBD as applicable, in prescribed Government of India format
  • 9Original Photo ID proof (Aadhar / Driving License / Voter-ID)
  • 10CGPA to Percentage conversion certificate (if grades awarded) duly issued by University/Institute
  • 11Only Indian Nationals of 18 years and above are eligible to apply.
  • 12Date of reckoning for age, experience, and qualification is 01-05-2026.
  • 13Experience acquired in Training / Consultancy / Teaching / Fellowship / Internship / Apprenticeship / Academic Project shall not be counted.
  • 14Experience in relevant field only shall be considered; HCL's decision on relevance is final.
  • 15Candidates from private organizations must be from companies with turnover of Rs. 250 Crores or more.
  • 16Selected candidates must execute a Service Agreement Bond of Rs. 3 Lakhs and serve a minimum of 3 years.
  • 17Government / CPSE employees must route their application through proper channel or submit NOC at the time of interview.
  • 18OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) candidates must submit OBC-NCL certificate issued on or after 01-04-2026.
  • 19EWS candidates must submit EWS certificate valid for the year 2026-27.
  • 20Category once filled/declared in the online application will not be changed.
  • 21All amendments/corrigendum will be communicated only through the HCL website - candidates should visit regularly.
  • 22Exact percentage of marks should be mentioned; rounding off of marks should not be done.
  • 23Email ID and mobile number provided in the application must remain active till recruitment is complete.
  • 24Canvassing directly or indirectly shall disqualify the candidature.
  • 25Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be Kolkata only.
  • 26HCL is an Equal Opportunity Employer and encourages workplace diversity.
