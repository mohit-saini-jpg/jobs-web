PPMET Admit Card 2026 Out

Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (BFUHS), Faridkot has released the PPMET Admit Card 2026 for the Punjab Para Medical Entrance Test (BSc Nursing). As per the latest schedule, the PPMET 2026 BSc Nursing entrance exam will be held on 7 June 2026, and the hall ticket download link is now active on the official BFUHS portal.

Registered candidates can download their PPMET 2026 roll number/admit card from the dedicated link bfuhs.ac.in/nursing26online/printrollno.aspx by logging in with their registration credentials. The admit card is available only in online mode and will not be sent by post or email.

How to Download PPMET Admit Card 2026

  • Open the official admit card link: bfuhs.ac.in/nursing26online/printrollno.aspx or visit bfuhs.ac.in and go to the PPMET/BSc Nursing 2026 section.
  • On the “Print Roll No / Admit Card” page, enter your Registration ID / User ID and Password or Date of Birth, as required.
  • Enter the security code/captcha shown on the screen and click on Login / Submit.
  • Your PPMET 2026 admit card with roll number, exam date and centre details will be displayed.
  • Check all details carefully and download and print multiple A4‑size copies of the hall ticket for use on exam day and during counselling.

If the page is slow or shows an error right after release, candidates are advised to wait a few minutes and try again rather than refreshing repeatedly.

Details Mentioned on PPMET Admit Card 2026

The PPMET 2026 BSc Nursing admit card contains important information, including:

  • Candidate’s name and father’s name.
  • Registration ID / roll number.
  • Exam name: Punjab Para Medical Entrance Test (PPMET) 2026 - BSc Nursing.
  • Exam date and time (7 June 2026, with reporting time mentioned).
  • Examination centre name, centre code and complete address.
  • Candidate’s photograph and signature (as per application).
  • Important exam‑day instructions, reporting and gate‑closing time.

If you find any error in your name, photograph, date of birth or exam centre, contact BFUHS immediately via the helpline/contact details provided on the website and in the prospectus.

Documents Required with PPMET Admit Card 2026

On the day of the PPMET 2026 exam, you must carry:

  • A printed copy of the PPMET Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or high‑quality black and white).
  • One original photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence or any other government‑issued identity card.

Guides also suggest carrying an extra photocopy of both admit card and ID for safety, although only the original ID and printed hall ticket are required for entry. Candidates without the admit card and valid ID will not be allowed to sit for the exam.

Exam‑Day Instructions for PPMET 2026

Key exam‑day guidelines for the PPMET 2026 BSc Nursing entrance test include:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on your admit card to complete verification and frisking; late entrants may be refused entry.
  • Carry only essential items: PPMET admit card, original photo ID and any permitted items as specified; follow all instructions printed on the hall ticket and question booklet.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, electronic gadgets, notes, books or other prohibited items inside the examination hall.
  • Follow the seating plan and instructions given by invigilators; any unfair practice or misconduct can lead to cancellation of your exam and further disciplinary action.
  • Keep your admit card safe even after the exam, as it may be required for result checking, counselling and admission procedures.
