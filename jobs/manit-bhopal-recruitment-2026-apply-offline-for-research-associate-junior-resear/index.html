FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the MANIT Bhopal Research Associate & Junior Research Fellow posts?
A
The last date to submit the application for the Research Associate & Junior Research Fellow positions at MANIT Bhopal is 17th June 2026. Candidates are advised to ensure their applications reach the designated address before this deadline.
Q2What are the minimum qualifications required for the Junior Research Fellow (JRF) position?
A
For the Junior Research Fellow (JRF) position, candidates must possess an M.Tech. degree in Surveying/Geodesy/Geoinformatics/Geomatics Engg./Remote Sensing & GIS or an equivalent discipline, along with a valid GATE qualification.
Q3What is the age limit for the Research Associate (RA) position?
A
The maximum age limit for the Research Associate (RA) position is 40 years. There are no specific minimum age requirements mentioned for this role.
Q4How will candidates be selected for these positions?
A
The selection process for these positions involves shortlisting candidates based on their merit and project requirements, followed by an interview. Only shortlisted candidates will be called for the interview.
Q5What is the application mode for this recruitment?
A
The application mode for the Research Associate & Junior Research Fellow positions at MANIT Bhopal is offline or via email. Candidates need to send their completed applications to the specified address or email.
Q6What is the salary offered for the Junior Research Fellow (JRF) post?
A
The Junior Research Fellow (JRF) will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 37,000/- per month, plus House Rent Allowance (HRA) as per Government of India norms.
Q7What is the salary offered for the Research Associate (RA) post?
A
The Research Associate (RA) will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 58,000/- per month, plus House Rent Allowance (HRA) as per Government of India norms.
Q8Is there any experience required for the Research Associate (RA) position?
A
Yes, for the Research Associate (RA) position, candidates with an M.Tech. degree need to have a minimum of three years of relevant experience in Research and Development, along with at least one SCI journal publication. Ph.D. holders do not require this experience.
Q9What is the advertisement number for this recruitment?

The advertisement number for this recruitment drive by Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT Bhopal) is CE/260.
