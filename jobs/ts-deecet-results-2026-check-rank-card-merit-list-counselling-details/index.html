TS DEECET Results 2026 - Check Rank Card, Merit List & Counselling Details

Telangana State DEECET (TS DEECET) Result 2026 has been released online for the 1st/2nd/3rd semester academic sessions. The exam authority has officially published results for D.El.Ed (DIET) UG-level admissions and related certificate courses. TS DEECET Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - https://deecet.cdse.telangana.gov.in.

Students can check and download their results by logging in with their hall ticket number and date of birth or other required credentials. After downloading, verify your name, hall ticket number, course and marks; contact the TS DEECET helpdesk or the examination authority immediately if any discrepancies are found.

TS DEECET Results 2026 Overview

TS DEECET (Telangana State Diploma in Elementary Education Common Entrance Test) 2026 is conducted by the Department of School Education, Government of Telangana for admission into two‑year D.El.Ed and D.P.S.E courses. The exam is held in computer‑based mode and the results are announced online only; there is no offline scorecard dispatch.

How to Check TS DEECET Results 2026 Online

Follow these simple steps to check and download your TS DEECET Result 2026.

  • Visit the official website: https://deecet.cdse.telangana.gov.in/TSDEECET/TSDEECET_GetResult.aspx
  • Click the relevant result link for your semester (1st/2nd/3rd Semester).
  • Enter your Hall Ticket Number and Date of Birth (or other required credentials).
  • Click 'Submit' or 'View Result'.
  • Your TS DEECET Result 2026 will appear on screen; verify details and click 'Download' or 'Print' to save a copy.
