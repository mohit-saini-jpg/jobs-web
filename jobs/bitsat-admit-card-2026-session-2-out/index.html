BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Session 2 Out

Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) Pilani has released the BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Session 2 for candidates registered for the second session of the BITS Admission Test. BITSAT 2026 Session 2 is being conducted for admission to Integrated BE, B.Pharm and MSc programmes at BITS Pilani, Goa and Hyderabad campuses for the 2026-27 academic year.

As per the official schedule, the BITSAT 2026 Session 2 exam will be held from 24 May to 26 May 2026 in computer based mode at various test centres. BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Session 2 has been made available online on admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in, and candidates must download and print their hall ticket before the exam date; no physical admit cards are sent by post.

How to Download BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Session 2

  • Visit the official BITS admission portal admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in.
  • Click on the BITSAT 2026 Admit Card / Hall Ticket - Session 2 link on the homepage or BITSAT page.
  • On the login page, enter your BITSAT 2026 application number, password and the captcha code displayed.
  • Click on the Submit / Login button to access your admit card.
  • Your BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Session 2 will open on the screen, showing your name, test date, slot, and exam centre details.
  • Verify all the details carefully, then download and print 2-3 clear A4 size copies of the hall ticket to carry on the exam day.

Candidates are advised to keep a digital copy of the admit card as backup, but only the printed version will be accepted at the exam centre.

Details Mentioned on BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Session 2

The BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Session 2 carries important exam and candidate information that must be verified before appearing in the online test. Candidates should carefully check the following details on their hall ticket:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph and signature
  • BITSAT 2026 application number and roll number (if allotted)
  • Exam name: BITS Admission Test (BITSAT) 2026 Session 2
  • Exam date and slot details (for example, test date, forenoon or afternoon session, reporting time and exam time)
  • Test city, exam centre name, centre code and complete address
  • Course / programme options applied for (such as BE, BPharm, MSc) as per the application form, if printed
  • Important exam day instructions regarding reporting time, permitted items, computer based test process and code of conduct
  • Any special remark or provision (such as PwD category instructions or special arrangements, if applicable)

If any mismatch or error is noticed in your name, photograph, application number, exam date or slot, or test centre details, you should contact the BITSAT helpdesk or BITS Pilani admissions office immediately before the exam for clarification and necessary correction instructions.

Documents Required with BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Session 2

On the day of the BITSAT 2026 Session 2 exam, candidates should carry:

  • A printed copy of the BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Session 2 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white)
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence or any other government‑issued photo ID accepted as per BITSAT instructions
  • Any additional documents mentioned in the admit card / information bulletin (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter if you are availing a scribe facility)

Entry to the exam centre will not be allowed without the printed BITSAT admit card and a valid photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for BITSAT 2026 Session 2

To ensure smooth conduct of the BITSAT 2026 Session 2 computer based examination, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time printed on the BITSAT admit card so that verification, frisking and seating can be completed; late entry after the scheduled reporting or gate‑closing time may not be allowed
  • Carry only essential items: BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Session 2 (A4 printout), valid photo ID and any basic permitted items as specified in the BITSAT exam instructions (for example, simple transparent water bottle or pens if mentioned; follow all CBT guidelines strictly)
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless specifically allowed), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the examination hall; violation of these rules may lead to cancellation of your test and further disciplinary action
  • Follow all instructions printed on the BITSAT admit card and those announced by the invigilators during the exam; indulging in unfair means, talking to other candidates or disobeying exam rules can result in cancellation of your examination and possible debarment from future BITSAT attempts or admissions
  • Keep your BITSAT Admit Card 2026 Session 2 safely even after the exam, as details from the hall ticket may be required later for result reference, counselling, seat allotment and document verification at BITS campuses
