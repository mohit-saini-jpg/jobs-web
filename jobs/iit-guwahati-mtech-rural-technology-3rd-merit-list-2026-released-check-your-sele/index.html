What Is the IIT Guwahati MTech Rural Technology 3rd Merit List 2026?

IIT Guwahati has released the 3rd merit list for MTech in Rural Technology, and selected candidates must confirm their admission by paying the fee. Eligibility Criteria for IIT Guwahati MTech Rural Technology

This section explains who is eligible to apply for the MTech Rural Technology programme at IIT Guwahati.

For admission to the MTech Programme, a candidate must have a four-year Bachelor's degree (B.Tech./B.Sc. (Engineering)/M.Sc. or equivalent) in the relevant discipline with a minimum 6.0 CPI (Cumulative Performance Index) on a 10-point scale (or 60% aggregate marks) with a valid GATE score. The CPI/Marks criteria will be relaxed by 0.5 CPI (or 5% marks) for SC/ST/PwD candidates.

Additionally, candidates who are still in their final semester of the qualifying degree may also apply, provided they appear in all their qualifying degree examinations and complete all requirements for their degrees before June 15, 2026. If selected, such candidates shall be admitted provisionally, and they will have to furnish the results of their qualifying degree examinations latest by August 31, 2026.

How the Merit List / Rank List Is Prepared

Understanding how IIT Guwahati prepares its merit list can help candidates know where they stand.

While preparing the rank list for regular category admission into MTech programmes, if two or more candidates scored the same GATE Score or the same final Composite Score, the following tie-breaking rules apply: first, based on the aggregate percentage of marks obtained in Class 12 (higher percentage ranked higher); if still tied, based on the aggregate percentage of marks obtained in Class 10; and finally, based on Date of Birth, with the older candidate ranked higher.
