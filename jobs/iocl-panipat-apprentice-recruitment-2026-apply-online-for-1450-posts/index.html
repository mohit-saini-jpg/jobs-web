IOCL Panipat Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) has invited applications for engagement of Apprentices at its Panipat Refinery & Petrochemical Complex under the Apprentices Act, 1961/1973. IOCL Panipat Apprentice Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

There is no written test for this recruitment. Selection is based on percentage of marks (aggregate of all subjects across all years/semesters) obtained in the prescribed qualification, arranged in descending order, subject to meeting the notified eligibility criteria. Preference is given to candidates applying for apprenticeship training for the first time. In case of a tie in marks, the candidate senior in age (date of birth) is ranked higher; if the date of birth is also the same, higher marks in Matriculation are considered. PwBD candidates who avail relaxation in eligibility qualification marks are considered against reserved seats in order of merit within the category, irrespective of their position in the overall select list. Filling of seats is solely at the discretion of management based on candidate suitability; no claim arises if seats remain unfilled due to insufficient suita

Leave admissible: 32 days General Leave (8 days/quarter, including medical grounds) and 12 days Casual Leave per calendar year (pro-rata); unavailed leave lapses at the end of the apprenticeship period. Apprentices are covered under Accident Insurance and the Certified Standing Orders applicable to workmen at the training establishment. Canvassing in any form will render a candidate ineligible.
