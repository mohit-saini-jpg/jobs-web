TN SSLC Supplementary Exam 2026 Dates Out

Directorate of Government Examinations, Tamil Nadu has officially released the TN SSLC Supplementary Exam 2026 Dates for July 2026. The Supplementary examinations will be conducted for failed candidates of SSLC March 2026. Students can now check the complete subject-wise schedule and download the official TN SSLC Supplementary Exam 2026 Time Table from the Directorate website.

How to Download TN SSLC Supplementary Exam Dates 2026

Students can download the TN SSLC Supplementary Exam 2026 Dates easily by following the steps below:

  • Visit the official website of Directorate of Government Examinations, Tamil Nadu.
  • Go to the SSLC or Time Table section on the homepage.
  • Locate the link for SSLC Supplementary Examination July 2026 Time Table.
  • Click on the PDF link to open the timetable.
  • Download and save the file on your device.
  • Take a printout and keep it safe for exam preparation.

Note: Always refer to the official timetable and check all dates and timings carefully before the examinations.

Details Mentioned in TN SSLC Supplementary Exam Dates 2026

The official TN SSLC Supplementary Exam 2026 Dates contain all necessary information for students. The key details mentioned in the timetable are:

  • Examination dates and day
  • Part-wise subjects (Part I, II, III & IV)
  • Exact timing of each paper
  • Reading time and verification time
  • Important instructions for candidates.
