The Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TGCHE) has officially released the TG EAPCET Result 2026 for Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy streams, ending the wait for lakhs of candidates who appeared in this state level entrance exam. The results have been published online for all UG level professional courses covered under the Telangana Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test conducted by Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH).

TG EAPCET Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - eapcet.tgche.ac.in. Candidates can check and download their TG EAPCET 2026 result, scorecard and rank card by entering their registration number, hall ticket number and date of birth on the official result portal. After downloading the rank card, students are advised to carefully verify their personal details, marks, qualifying status and rank, and contact the exam authorities in case of any discrepancy before the counselling process begins.

How to Check TG EAPCET Result 2026

  • Visit the official TG EAPCET website at eapcet.tgche.ac.in.
  • On the homepage, find and click on the link that says "TG EAPCET Result 2026" or "TG EAPCET 2026 Rank Card".
  • On the login page, enter your TG EAPCET 2026 registration number, hall ticket number and date of birth in the specified fields.
  • Recheck the entered details and click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button.
  • Your TG EAPCET Result 2026 scorecard and rank card will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download the TG EAPCET 2026 rank card and take a printout for counselling and future admission processes.
