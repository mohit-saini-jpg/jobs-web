PAU Teaching Assistant Eligibility Criteria 2026

Bachelor's degree in Agriculture Engineering/Mechanical Engineering. Master's degree in Farm Machinery & Power Engineering/Farm Power Machinery. At least 60% marks at Bachelor's level and 65% marks or OCPA of 6.50 out of 10.00 at Master's level. Matric level certificate of Punjabi Language from Punjab School Education Board or any other recognized Board (as first or Second language). Note: A candidate who does not possess Master's degree in the relevant subject but possesses Ph.D. degree in the discipline required at Master's level shall be eligible for the post.
