Qualification / Eligibility

Head (Incubation Centre): Ph.D. in Life Sciences /MBBS/First class in BTech/ M.Sc degree in Life Sciences/ Electrical/ Electronics/ Mechanical/ Computer science/ Biomedical/IT or equivalent. Experience: Four years’ experience with PhD, or 6 years with MBBS/MTech, or 12 years’ experience with MSc/BTech degree, having demonstrated hands-on expertise in different technology platforms. The experience in a scientific organization of repute in the area of R&D, bio-incubator or research management only shall be counted. Desirable: At least 5-7 years of experience in business management, or management of the intellectual property, or project management. 1st class in MBA or Equivalent (MBBS/MS/graduation in engineering or equivalent); minimum 10 years of work experience. Coordinator: Ph.D./First class in Postgraduate in Life Sciences /MBBS/BDS/Business Administration/Technology or equivalent. Desirable: Three years’ experience with PhD, or 5 years with MBBS/MTech, 8 years’ experience with MSc/BTech degree, having demonstrated hands-on expertise in different technology platforms. The experience in a scientific organization of repute in the area of R&D, bio-incubator or research management only shall be counted. Priority will be given to prior experience in Incubation Centres for at least 2 – 3 years.
