Bhartiya Shiksha Board (BSB), Haridwar has officially released the BSB Result 2026 for Class 10 and Class 12 board examinations conducted for the 2025-26 academic session. To check and download the BSB 10th and 12th Result 2026, students need to visit the result portal, open the respective Class 10 or Class 12 result link, and log in using details such as roll number, date of birth, application number, or registration number, as required. How to Download Bhartiya Shiksha Board Result 2026 Online

Follow the below step-by-step process to check and download BSB 10th and 12th Result 2026 online in a simple and user-friendly way.

  • Visit the official BSB result portal, preferably the DigiLocker result site: https://results.digilocker.gov.in/ or go through the official board website https://bsb.org.in/.
  • On the homepage, look for the link that says 'Bhartiya Shiksha Board Class 10 Result 2026' or 'Bhartiya Shiksha Board Class 12 Result 2026' and click on the appropriate link.
  • You will be redirected to the login page where you need to enter your roll number, date of birth, application number, or registration number as asked on the screen.
  • After entering the details correctly, click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button to proceed.
  • Your BSB Result 2026 for Class 10 or Class 12 will appear on the screen displaying subject-wise marks, total marks, percentage, and qualifying status.
  • Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future reference until the original marksheet and certificates are issued by your school or Bhartiya Shiksha Board.
