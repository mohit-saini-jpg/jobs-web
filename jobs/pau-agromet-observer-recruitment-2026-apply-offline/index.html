PAU Agromet Observer Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Punjab Agricultural University (PAU) has published the Agromet Observer Notification 2026 on 30 June 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 01 Agromet Observer position. Candidates within the age range of 18 to 37 years and holding 10+2 with Science stream, basic knowledge of computer operation, and Punjabi up to Matric standard are eligible to apply at the official website (https://www.pau.edu). The application process will be active from 30 June 2026 to 15 July 2026 through offline mode (plain paper application), and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and application details
