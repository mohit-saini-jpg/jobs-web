PMC Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Pune Municipal Corporation (PMC) Solid Waste Management Department has invited applications for the PMC Recruitment 2026 drive to fill 60 contract posts — 30 Junior Engineer and 30 Health Inspector vacancies. These are 11-month temporary, contract-based positions with consolidated monthly pay. Eligible candidates can submit applications offline from 29 June 2026 to 13 July 2026, up to 3:00 PM, at the PMC Main Building in Shivajinagar, Pune.

PMC Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for PMC Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the application for the PMC Junior Engineer and Health Inspector Recruitment 2026 is 13 July 2026, up to 3:00 PM.
Q2How many vacancies are there in PMC Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 60 total vacancies - 30 for Junior Engineer and 30 for Health Inspector — under the PMC Solid Waste Management Department.
Q3What is the educational qualification for PMC Junior Engineer post?
A
Candidates need a B.Tech or B.E. degree in Environmental Engineering and Technology from a recognized university.
Q4What is the educational qualification for PMC Health Inspector post?
A
Candidates must have passed HSC along with the Sanitary Inspector course; Science graduates get preference.
Q5Is there an application fee for PMC Recruitment 2026?
A
No application fee has been mentioned in the official PMC notice for this recruitment.
Q6Can candidates apply online for PMC Recruitment 2026?
A
No, this recruitment does not have an online application process. Applications must be submitted physically at the PMC Main Building, Shivajinagar, Pune.
Q7What is the salary for PMC Junior Engineer and Health Inspector posts?
A
Junior Engineer gets a consolidated monthly pay of ₹50,000, and Health Inspector gets ₹40,000, both on a contract basis.
Q8What is the tenure of these PMC contract posts?
A
Both posts are temporary and contract-based for a period of 11 months.
Q9Is there an age limit for PMC Recruitment 2026?
A
The official notice does not specify any age limit or age relaxation for either post.
Q10Will selected candidates get permanent employment in PMC?
A
No, selected candidates cannot claim any right to a permanent or regular post in PMC and are not eligible for benefits given to regular PMC employees.
