Qualification / Eligibility

Medical Officer 'E' (Nuclear Medicine): M.D. / D.N.B. (Nuclear Medicine) or equivalent degree in Nuclear Medicine recognized by National Medical Commission with 3 Years' post M.D. / D.N.B. experience in Nuclear Medicine in a Teaching Hospital. Medical Officer E (Radio-Diagnosis): M.D. / D.N.B. (Radiology / Radio-diagnosis) or equivalent postgraduate degree recognized by National Medical Commission with 3 years' post M.D./ D.N.B. experience in Radiodiagnosis in a teaching hospital. Medical Physicist ‘C’ : M.Sc. (Physics) and Diploma in Radiological Physics or Equivalent AERB approved qualifications with Certification of Radiological Safety Officer from AERB and 1 year clinical experience as Medical Physicist with state of art technology. Scientific Officer ‘SB’ (Transfusion Medicine) : M.Sc. (Biochemistry / Chemistry / Botany / Zoology / Microbiology) with minimum 50% marks and Degree / Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT) / Blood Bank Technology from recognized institute with 1 year experience in relevant field in a FDA licensed Blood Bank. Scientific Assistant 'C' (Nuclear Medicine) : B.Sc. (Physics / Chemistry / Biology / Nuclear Medicine or equivalent) with PGDFIT/DMRIT, and RSO Level-II (Nuclear Medicine) examination conducted by RPAD/ AERB with minimum 01-year experience post qualification OR B.Sc. Nuclear Medicine Technology + PGDNMT (4 years integrated Program) and RSO Level-II (Nuclear Medicine) examination conducted by RPAD / AERB with minimum 01-year experience post qualification OR B.Sc. Nuclear Medicine Technology (3 years Program) and RSO Level-II (Nuclear Medicine) examination conducted by RPAD / AERB with minimum 03-year experience post qualification OR M.Sc. Nuclear Medicine Technology or Equivalent course in nuclear medicine technology and RSO Level-II (Nuclear Medicine) examination conducted by RPAD/AERB. Scientific Assistant 'B' (Biomedical Engineering) : Diploma (Biomedical / Medical Electronics) with 3 years' experience out of which 1 year in hospital setup. Scientific Assistant B (Physiotherapy) : B.Sc. (Physiotherapy) / Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) with 50% marks from recognised university with Minimum One year experience in relevant field. Scientific Assistant 'B' (Radiation Oncology) : B.Sc. (Physics) with minimum 50% marks and Post Graduate Diploma in Radiotherapy Technology from recognized institute / university approved by AERB OR B.Sc. (Radiotherapy Technology) - 3/4 years course from a recognized institute / university approved by AERB with relevant experience. Technician C (Operation Theatre (OT)) (Networking) : Diploma in Electronics / Computer Engineering or Graduate in Computer Science OR any other graduate with atleast 1 year certificate course in Computer Technology from a reputed institute and having professional CCNA certification with minimum 3 years of hands on work experience in configuration / trouble shooting / maintaining large IT i-series (AS/400) System operations. Technician C (Operation Theatre (OT) : 12th Std. in Science and Diploma of one year / 6 months in ICU / OT / Electronics / Dialysis Technician from a recognized institution with Three years' experience in relevant field.
