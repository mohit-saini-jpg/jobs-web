CUET UG 2026 Result Link - Direct Download

This section explains where candidates need to go to access their CUET UG 2026 result and what they should do immediately after downloading it.

The CUET 2026 result link is active on the official NTA website, and candidates are advised to download and take a printout of their result for future use. Since the CUET UG merit list released by participating universities will be based on this result, and the hard copy of the scorecard will be required during the CUET UG counselling process, it's important not to skip the printout step.

  • Official website: cuet.nta.nic.in
  • What you need: Application number and password (or date of birth)
  • What you get: A PDF scorecard showing your marks, percentile, and category status

How to Download CUET UG 2026 Scorecard (Step-by-Step Guide)

This section walks you through the exact process to retrieve your CUET UG 2026 scorecard from the official portal, in case you haven't downloaded it yet.

Candidates need to visit the official CUET UG website at cuet.nta.nic.in and click on the CUET UG 2026 result link. Follow these steps:

  • Go to the official website cuet.nta.nic.in
  • Click on the "CUET UG 2026 Result" link on the homepage
  • Enter your application number and date of birth, followed by the captcha code
  • Click the "Submit" button
  • Your CUET UG 2026 result will be displayed on the screen
  • Download the scorecard and take a printout for future reference, including counselling

Details Mentioned on the CUET UG 2026 Scorecard

Knowing what information appears on your scorecard helps you verify that all your personal and academic details are correct. Here's what to check.

The CUET UG 2026 scorecard includes the following details:

  • Candidate's name
  • Application number
  • Course name
  • Marks obtained
  • Percentile or rank
  • Category
  • Qualifying status

If you notice any discrepancy in your name, category, or marks, it's advisable to contact the NTA helpdesk before proceeding with university counselling registrations.

CUET UG 2026 Marking Scheme and Score Calculation

This section explains how your raw marks were calculated, which is essential for understanding the score displayed on your scorecard.

The CUET UG result is prepared using raw scores based on a fixed marking scheme applied uniformly to objective-type questions across all subjects.

Type of ResponseMarks Awarded
Correct Answer+5 Marks
Incorrect Answer-1 Mark
Unattempted Question0 Marks

Formula to calculate CUET UG score:

Total Score = 5 × (Total Correct Answers) − 1 × (Total Incorrect Answers)

Since CUET UG is conducted in multiple shifts and sessions, NTA applies a normalization process known as the Equipercentile method to ensure candidates across different sessions are neither advantaged nor disadvantaged by variations in exam difficulty. Under this method, marks from an easier session may be adjusted slightly downward, while marks from a tougher session may be adjusted slightly upward, based on overall performance trends in each session.

What Happens After CUET UG Result 2026?

Once your result is out, the real work of securing admission begins. This section outlines the steps candidates need to take after checking their CUET UG scorecard.

After the CUET UG 2026 result declaration, participating universities will begin their respective admission and counselling procedures. Candidates should:

  • Check the eligibility criteria of their preferred universities and courses
  • Register for individual university counselling processes (each participating university conducts its own admission cycle)
  • Upload required documents, including the CUET scorecard, on the respective university admission portal
  • Participate in seat allocation or merit-list-based admission rounds
  • Confirm admission by paying the required fees within the stipulated deadline

It's worth noting that CUET UG scores are accepted by central universities, state universities, deemed universities, and several private institutions for undergraduate admissions but each institution sets its own cutoff and counselling schedule, so candidates should track individual university websites closely.

CUET UG 2026 Merit List and Counselling

This section clarifies how the merit list connects to your CUET UG score and what role it plays in final admission.

A separate CUET UG merit list is not released centrally by NTA. Instead, each participating university uses the CUET UG result to prepare its own merit list, and candidates whose names appear on a university's merit list become eligible for that university's counselling process. This means your CUET UG percentile or score is the foundation, but admission ultimately depends on the specific cutoff set by each university and course combination. c
