How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the AIIMS Exams portal at www.aiimsexams.ac.in. The online portal will be active from 20-05-2026 to 18-06-2026 (5:00 PM). No physical/hard copy applications will be accepted. No documents, including the registration slip, are required to be sent in physical form. Candidates must upload supporting documents at the time of online registration itself - failure to upload mandatory documents will result in a score of zero for the respective criterion. Candidates may apply for more than one post code if they meet the eligibility criteria for each. No corrections will be permitted after online submission of the form; candidates are advised to fill the registration form carefully. Candidates currently employed in Government/Semi-Government/Quasi-Government organizations must upload their NOC at the time of online registration.
  • Documents to be Uploaded at the Time of Online Application:
  • Date of Birth proof - Matriculation/10th Standard Certificate or School Leaving Certificate
  • Educational qualification certificates (Degree certificates as per Recruitment Rules)
  • SC / ST / OBC / EWS / PwBD certificate (if applicable) in prescribed format
  • Experience Certificate issued by Head(s) of the organization with duration and nature of experience
  • Professional Registration Certificate (NMC/MCI/DCI/INC/State Nursing Council as applicable)
  • NOC from present employer (for candidates employed in Govt./Semi-Govt. organizations)
  • Research/publication supporting documents (PMID numbers, PDF of papers, h-index printout, etc.)
  • Clear photograph and signature as per prescribed specifications
  • Shortlisted candidates called for interview must bring all original documents and one set of self-attested photocopies to the interview venue. Arrive at the interview venue by 8:30 AM on the scheduled date. For queries, visit “My Page” after logging in and click “RAISE A QUERY” on www.aiimsexams.ac.in.

Important Instructions

  • Only Indian citizens and OCI cardholders under Section 7A of the Citizenship Act, 1955 are eligible to apply. Candidates must possess the prescribed minimum qualification and experience on the closing date of online application. No hard copy applications will be accepted - online submission only; no documents to be sent by post. Recruitment fee is non-refundable (except for SC/ST candidates who appear in interview); incomplete applications without fee will be rejected. Government employees must upload NOC from their present employer at the time of online registration. No corrections will be allowed after online submission of the application form. All faculty positions carry a two-year probation period; private practice is strictly prohibited.
