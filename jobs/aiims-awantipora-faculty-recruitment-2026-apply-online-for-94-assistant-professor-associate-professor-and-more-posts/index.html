Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026?

AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026 is a direct recruitment drive for 94 Group ‘A’ faculty posts including Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, Additional Professor and Professor in various medical and allied departments. The recruitment is being conducted under Advertisement No. 67/2026 dated 15 May 2026 for AIIMS Awantipora, J&K.

Q2. How many vacancies are notified in AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026?

A total of 94 faculty vacancies have been announced, comprising 85 Assistant Professors, 5 Associate Professors, 2 Additional Professors and 2 Professors across about 40 departments. These posts include reservations for SC, ST, OBC, EWS and 4 percent of posts reserved for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities.

Q3. What are the important dates for AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026?

The online application portal opens on 20 May 2026 and the last date for submission of application is 18 June 2026 up to 5:00 PM. Interview dates will be notified later on the AIIMS exams website at least two weeks in advance of the schedule.

Q4. Which posts are available under AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment?

The available posts are Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, Additional Professor and Professor in departments such as Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, General Medicine, General Surgery, Community Medicine, Pathology, Pharmacology and various clinical super specialties. Some higher-level posts (Associate Professor, Additional Professor and Professor) are also open to eligible non-medical candidates as per prescribed qualifications.

Q5. What is the eligibility criteria for Assistant Professor at AIIMS Awantipora?

Assistant Professor applicants must have a recognized medical qualification under the Indian Medical Council Act and a relevant postgraduate degree such as MD, MS, DM or MCh in the concerned specialty. They also need the prescribed post-PG teaching or research experience as per AIIMS faculty recruitment rules, generally about three years in the specialty.

Q6. What is the age limit for AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026?

The upper age limit for Assistant Professor and Associate Professor posts is 50 years as on 18 June 2026, subject to relaxations for reserved categories as per Government of India rules. For Additional Professor and Professor posts the upper age limit is 58 years, and no age relaxation is admissible beyond this limit.

Q7. What is the application fee for AIIMS Awantipora Faculty posts?

The application fee is 3,000 rupees for UR and OBC candidates and 2,400 rupees for SC, ST and EWS candidates, while PwBD applicants are exempted from paying any fee. The fee is non-refundable and applications submitted without the prescribed fee will be summarily rejected.

Q8. What is the selection process for AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026?

The selection process consists of an objective screening or shortlisting stage followed by an interview or personality test conducted by the Standing Selection Committee. Screening scores are used only for shortlisting candidates for interview, and final selection is based solely on performance in the interview.
