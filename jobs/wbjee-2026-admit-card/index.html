How to Download WBJEE 2026 Admit Card

Once the link goes live today, candidates can download their WBJEE hall ticket by following these steps:

  • Visit the official WBJEEB website: https://wbjeeb.nic.in and click on the WBJEE 2026 tab.
  • Click on the “WBJEE 2026 Admit Card” / “Download Admit Card” link on the homepage or candidate login page.
  • Enter your Application Number and Password (or Date of Birth, if that option is given), then enter the security pin/captcha.
  • Click on “Sign In / Submit” to access your candidate dashboard.
  • Click on “Download Admit Card / View Hall Ticket”; your WBJEE 2026 admit card will appear on the screen with exam date, time and centre details.
  • Check all details carefully and download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies of the hall ticket.

If you forget your application number or password, use the “Forgot Application Number/Password” links available on the login page.

Details Mentioned on WBJEE 2026 Admit Card

After downloading, candidates should verify the following details:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph and signature.
  • Application number and WBJEE roll number.
  • Exam name: WBJEE 2026.
  • Exam date: 24 May 2026 and paper details (Paper I - Mathematics, Paper II - Physics & Chemistry).
  • Exam time and reporting time; gate‑closing time (mentioned in admit card).
  • Exam centre name, code and full address.
  • Important exam‑day instructions and items allowed/prohibited.

If any discrepancy is found in the admit card (name, photo, category, exam centre etc.), candidates should immediately contact WBJEEB helpdesk using the contact information on the official website.

Exam‑Day Instructions for WBJEE 2026 Admit Card

To ensure smooth conduct of the WBJEE 2026 exam, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the WBJEE 2026 admit card so that verification, biometric/ID checks and seating can be completed; late entry after the gate‑closing time may not be allowed.
  • Carry only essential items: your WBJEE 2026 Admit Card (clear A4‑size printout), a valid photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence, etc., as permitted) and allowed stationery such as blue/black ball‑point pens and other items specified in the exam instructions.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not permitted), Bluetooth devices, earphones, notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or written material inside the examination hall; such items are strictly prohibited and may lead to cancellation of your paper and other disciplinary action.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the WBJEE admit card and those announced by the invigilators during the exam; indulging in any unfair means, talking in the exam hall or disobeying exam rules can result in cancellation of your candidature.
  • Keep your WBJEE 2026 admit card safely even after the exam, as it may be required during counselling, document verification and admission procedures.
