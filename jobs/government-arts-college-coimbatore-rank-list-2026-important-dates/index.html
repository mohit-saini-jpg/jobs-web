How to Download Government Arts College Coimbatore Rank List 2026

Follow these simple steps to access the rank list:

  • Visit the official website: gacbe.ac.in
  • Navigate to the “Rank List” or “UG Admissions 2026-2027” section.
  • Click on the relevant link (General or Special Category).
  • The rank list will open as a PDF.
  • Download and save it for future reference. You can also search by application number or name in the PDF.

Pro Tip: Always download from the official portal to avoid fake lists. Keep a printout handy for counselling.

Details Mentioned in the Rank List

The Government Arts College Coimbatore 2026 rank list typically includes:

  • Candidate’s Name
  • Application Number
  • Category (General/SC/ST/BC/MBC, etc.)
  • Marks/Percentage
  • Rank
  • Programme/Course Applied For
  • Seat Availability Information (in some lists)

Government Arts College Coimbatore Counselling 2026

After the rank list, shortlisted candidates attend counselling for document verification and seat allotment. Bring original documents such as:

  • 10th and 12th mark sheets
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Community Certificate (if applicable)
  • Special category certificates
  • Aadhaar Card

Counselling is conducted in multiple rounds. Candidates must report on the assigned date and time.

Eligibility Criteria for Admissions (2026)

  • UG Courses: Pass in Higher Secondary (10+2) or equivalent from a recognized board.
  • Minimum marks requirements vary by course and category (as per Tamil Nadu government norms).
  • Admissions are purely merit-based on qualifying exam scores, with reservations applied.
