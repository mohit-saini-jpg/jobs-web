Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026 Download Link

The Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026 download link is provided only on the official KEA website. Candidates must avoid third-party or unofficial links and always use the official portal for downloading the admit card. Once KEA activates the link for a particular exam, it is generally displayed under the latest notifications or relevant exam section.

Official KEA Hall Ticket Portal

Candidates can access the Hall Ticket section by visiting cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea and then navigating to the specific exam (such as KCET, recruitment, or other KEA tests). The login page asks for the registered credentials that were generated at the time of application. Keeping these details ready helps in a smooth and quick download experience.

KEA Bell Timings PDF 2026

Along with the hall ticket, KEA usually publishes a bell timings PDF that clearly mentions reporting time, question paper distribution time, commencement of the exam, and end time. Candidates must go through this PDF carefully to understand exactly when to enter the exam hall and when the gates will be closed. The bell timings PDF is particularly important for those appearing in multiple sessions or on different dates.

How to Download Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026 Online

KEA issues the hall ticket only in online mode through its official portal, and no hall ticket is sent via post or email. Therefore, every candidate has to log in and download the admit card personally before the last date mentioned in the schedule.

Step-by-Step KEA Hall Ticket 2026 Download Process

  • Visit the official website: Click Here
  • On the homepage, click on the relevant exam section such as “KCET 2026”, “Recruitment”, or “Admission”.
  • Look for the link labelled “Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026” or the specific exam hall ticket link.
  • Click on the link to open the login page.
  • Enter your Application Number / User ID, Password / Date of Birth, and captcha code (if shown).
  • Click on the “Login” or “Submit” button.
  • Your KEA Hall Ticket 2026 will appear on the screen; check all details carefully.
  • Download the hall ticket PDF and take 2-3 printouts on A4 size paper for future use.

Credentials Required to Download KEA Hall Ticket 2026

To download Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026 without any issues, the candidate must have their application-related credentials ready. The most important details are the application number or user ID generated during registration and the password or date of birth used at the time of applying. In case of captcha verification, candidates should enter the characters exactly as shown and refresh the page only if the captcha is not readable.

Details Printed on Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026

The hall ticket contains all key information related to the candidate’s identity and exam schedule. Candidates must verify these details because any mismatch between the hall ticket and ID proof can create problems at the exam centre.

The typical details mentioned on Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026 are:

  • Candidate’s full name and photograph
  • Roll number / hall ticket number
  • Name of the exam (KCET, recruitment exam, etc.)
  • Exam date and shift (morning/afternoon/evening)
  • Reporting time and gate closing time
  • Name and full address of the exam centre
  • Candidate’s category and gender (if applicable)
  • Important exam-day instructions and guidelines

If there is any discrepancy, the candidate should immediately reach out to KEA through the helpline or email mentioned in the official notification.

Karnataka KEA Exam Date & Bell Timings 2026

KEA announces the exam schedule and bell timings through separate notifications and PDFs. Candidates should keep checking the official website so they do not miss updates about changes or rescheduling.

KEA Exam Date 2026

The exact exam dates for each KEA examination (such as KCET 2026, various recruitment exams, and other tests) are listed in the official time table. Once published, candidates must note their exam date and shift from the schedule and then cross-check with what is printed on their hall ticket. Appearing on the wrong date or at the wrong time can lead to disqualification, so careful reading of the schedule is essential.

Reporting Time & Bell Timings on Exam Day

KEA publishes a bell timings PDF that clearly lists the sequence of bells throughout the exam session. This typically includes reporting time, time for entry into the exam hall, question paper distribution, starting time, and the final bell signalling the end of the exam. Candidates must reach the centre at least 60 to 90 minutes before the reporting time mentioned in the hall ticket to complete verification and avoid last-minute rush.

Eligibility to Download Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026

While the hall ticket is available online to all accepted applicants, not everyone who registers is automatically eligible to download it. Basic eligibility conditions must be satisfied as per the respective exam notification.

Who Can Download Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026?

  • Candidates who have successfully submitted the online application form within the prescribed dates.
  • Candidates whose applications have been accepted by KEA after verification of details and fee payment.
  • For recruitment or special exams, candidates who fulfil the age, qualification, and other eligibility criteria laid down by KEA in the official advertisement.

If an application is rejected for any reason (such as incorrect details or non-payment of fee), KEA generally mentions it in a separate list and such candidates may not be able to download the hall ticket.

Documents to Carry with Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026

Carrying the hall ticket alone may not be sufficient; KEA usually mandates at least one valid photo ID along with the admit card. Candidates should prepare these documents in advance to avoid panic on exam day.

Mandatory Documents on Exam Day

  • Printed copy of Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026 (preferably more than one copy).
  • A valid government-issued photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card, Driving Licence, or Passport.
  • Recent passport-size photographs, if the instructions specifically mention it or if the photograph on the hall ticket is unclear.

Items Not Allowed in the KEA Exam Hall

  • Mobile phones, smart watches, Bluetooth devices, earphones, and any other electronic gadgets.
  • Calculators (unless explicitly allowed for a particular exam), digital diaries, or programmable devices.
  • Books, written notes, bits of paper, or any other unauthorized material.

Candidates should carefully read the list of allowed and prohibited items printed on the hall ticket and in the exam instructions to ensure full compliance.

Important Instructions Printed on Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026

The hall ticket usually contains a set of exam-day instructions meant to ensure smooth conduct of the exam. Candidates must read these instructions line by line and follow them strictly inside the exam centre.

Key instructions commonly mentioned include:

  • Report to the exam centre at the time specified on the KEA Hall Ticket 2026; late entry beyond gate closing time is generally not allowed.
  • Carry only permitted items such as hall ticket, ID proof, pen/pencil, and other items specifically mentioned in the instructions.
  • Follow the invigilator’s directions, maintain discipline, and avoid any kind of unfair practice or disturbance in the exam hall.
  • Do not write anything on the hall ticket other than the candidate’s signature where required, and do not tamper with the barcode or QR code printed on it.

Karnataka KEA Hall Ticket 2026 - Common Issues & Solutions

Even after following the process, candidates may sometimes face technical or data-related issues while downloading the hall ticket. Knowing the common problems and their possible solutions can save valuable time.

Hall Ticket Not Downloading or Page Not Opening

  • Check your internet connection and try again after a few minutes in case of server load.
  • Use a different browser or device, and clear cookies/cache if the page is not loading properly.
  • Ensure that you are entering the correct application number, password, and captcha code.

If the problem persists, candidates should contact KEA support using the helpline numbers or email address given in the official notification.

Incorrect Details on KEA Hall Ticket 2026

  • If your name, photograph, date of birth, category, or exam centre details appear incorrect on the hall ticket, you should immediately inform KEA.
  • Provide proof of the correct details such as application form printout, ID proof, or other supporting documents as instructed by KEA.

KEA may issue instructions on whether corrections will be made online or handled at the exam centre, so candidates must act quickly after noticing any error.
