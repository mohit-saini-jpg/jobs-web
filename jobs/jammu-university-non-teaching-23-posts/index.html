How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official portal at www.jammuuniversity.ac.in and navigate to the online application section.
  • 2Register and fill in all required personal, educational, and experience details carefully.
  • 3Upload the scanned copies of required documents including Domicile Certificate, Reserved Category Certificate (if applicable), and educational certificates.
  • 4Candidates without the original Degree Certificate may upload a Provisional Degree or Final Semester Mark Sheet in place of the Degree at this stage.
  • 5Pay the application fee of Rs. 1,300/- through the portal payment gateway.
  • 6Submit the application and note down the Application Form ID / Registration Number.
  • 7After the last date, verify that your name appears in the list published on the University website. If there is any discrepancy, contact [email protected] within one week from the date of display of the list.
  • 8Take a printout of the completed online application form (hard copy), self-attest it, and keep it ready along with supporting documents for presentation at the time of skill test/trade test/computer test/interview.
  • 9Keep your documents ready before starting: Once submitted, the application cannot be changed. Prepare scanned copies of your photograph, signature, certificates, and domicile/category documents to the correct file size and format before opening the form.
  • 10Save your registration credentials immediately: Note your Application Form ID and password as soon as they are generated. You will need these to log back in, check payment status, and verify your name in the published list.
  • 11Payment gateway failures: If the payment page times out or the browser closes, do not attempt payment again immediately. Wait a few minutes, log back in, and check whether the payment was deducted before retrying, to avoid a double charge.
  • 12Avoid last-day rush: University portals often experience high traffic close to the deadline. Submit well before 14 July 2026 to avoid server overload errors that could prevent submission.
  • 13Verify your name in the published list: After the last date, the University will publish a list of successfully submitted applications. If your name is missing, contact [email protected] within one week - this deadline is strict.
  • 14Routing requirement for employed candidates: Government servants and University employees who fail to route their hard copy through the proper channel risk rejection. Arrange your NOC or routing in advance.
  • 15File format and size: Upload documents in the format specified on the portal. Incorrect file formats or oversized files will trigger an error and the upload will not be accepted.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for University of Jammu UIET Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the complete online application form is 14 July 2026. The application portal opens on 15 June 2026.
Q2How many vacancies are there in University of Jammu UIET Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 23 vacancies are notified across 13 posts at UIET, Janglote, Kathua. The number is tentative and may change at the time of selection.
Q3Who is eligible to apply for these posts?
A
Only domiciles of the Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir are eligible. Candidates must upload a valid Domicile Certificate issued by the Competent Authority. Educational qualifications vary by post — check the eligibility table above.
Q4What is the application fee for University of Jammu UIET Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 1,300/- for all the notified posts. Payment is made online through the portal payment gateway and is non-refundable.
Q5What is the age limit for these posts?
A
Age is reckoned as on 01 January 2026. The minimum age for all candidates is 18 years. The maximum age for General candidates is 40 years; SC/ST/OBC/EWS/RBA/ALC/IB candidates can apply up to 43 years; Ex-Servicemen up to 48 years; and in-service permanent employees of the University of Jammu up to 50 years.
Q6What is the selection process?
A
Selection may involve a screening test, written test, trade test/skill test/computer test, and personal interview, depending on the post. Detailed selection criteria and syllabi will be separately notified on www.jammuuniversity.ac.in.
Q7What is the salary for these posts?
A
Pay is as per the 7th Pay Commission. The Foreman post carries Level-6E (Rs. 35,900–1,13,500) and Multi-Tasking Staff carries SL1 (Rs. 14,800–47,100). All other posts fall between Level-2 and Level-5 — refer to the salary table above.
Q8Can I edit my application after submission?
A
No. Once the application is submitted, it cannot be altered or resubmitted under any circumstances. Keep all data and documents ready before you start filling the form.
Q9Where will the interview and skill tests be held?
A
Personal interviews and skill/trade tests will be held at Jammu. Candidates must appear at their own expense.
Q10What should I do if my name does not appear in the published list after the last date?
A
Contact the University at [email protected] within one week from the date of display of the list. Only candidates who have paid the fee and successfully submitted the form will appear in the list.
