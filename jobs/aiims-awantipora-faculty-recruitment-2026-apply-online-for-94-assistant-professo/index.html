Important Instructions

Only Indian citizens and OCI cardholders under Section 7A of the Citizenship Act, 1955 are eligible to apply. Candidates must possess the prescribed minimum qualification and experience on the closing date of online application. No hard copy applications will be accepted - online submission only; no documents to be sent by post. Recruitment fee is non-refundable (except for SC/ST candidates who appear in interview); incomplete applications without fee will be rejected. Government employees must upload NOC from their present employer at the time of online registration. No corrections will be allowed after online submission of the application form. Shortlisted candidates will receive a virtual meeting invitation for clarification during screening if required; failure to attend will be treated as “non-responsive.” Screening scores are used only for short-listing - they will not be used for final selection. SC/ST candidates may be granted relaxation of up to one year in required experience at the discretion of the competent authority. All faculty positions carry a two-year probation period; private practice is strictly prohibited. AIIMS Awantipora reserves the right to increase/decrease the number of vacancies; the Director, AIIMS New Delhi reserves the right to amend, cancel, or modify this advertisement. All disputes arising from this advertisement shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the Court of Law of J&K, India.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 Q1. What is AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026 is a direct recruitment drive for 94 Group ‘A’ faculty posts including Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, Additional Professor and Professor in various medical and allied departments. The recruitment is being conducted under Advertisement No. 67/2026 dated 15 May 2026 for AIIMS Awantipora, J&K.
Q2 Q2. How many vacancies are notified in AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 94 faculty vacancies have been announced, comprising 85 Assistant Professors, 5 Associate Professors, 2 Additional Professors and 2 Professors across about 40 departments. These posts include reservations for SC, ST, OBC, EWS and 4 percent of posts reserved for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities.
Q3 Q3. What are the important dates for AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application portal opens on 20 May 2026 and the last date for submission of application is 18 June 2026 up to 5:00 PM. Interview dates will be notified later on the AIIMS exams website at least two weeks in advance of the schedule.
Q4 Q4. Which posts are available under AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment?
A
The available posts are Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, Additional Professor and Professor in departments such as Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, General Medicine, General Surgery, Community Medicine, Pathology, Pharmacology and various clinical super specialties. Some higher-level posts (Associate Professor, Additional Professor and Professor) are also open to eligible non-medical candidates as per prescribed qualifications.
Q5 Q5. What is the eligibility criteria for Assistant Professor at AIIMS Awantipora?
A
Assistant Professor applicants must have a recognized medical qualification under the Indian Medical Council Act and a relevant postgraduate degree such as MD, MS, DM or MCh in the concerned specialty. They also need the prescribed post-PG teaching or research experience as per AIIMS faculty recruitment rules, generally about three years in the specialty.
Q6 Q6. What is the age limit for AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit for Assistant Professor and Associate Professor posts is 50 years as on 18 June 2026, subject to relaxations for reserved categories as per Government of India rules. For Additional Professor and Professor posts the upper age limit is 58 years, and no age relaxation is admissible beyond this limit.
Q7 Q7. What is the application fee for AIIMS Awantipora Faculty posts?
A
The application fee is 3,000 rupees for UR and OBC candidates and 2,400 rupees for SC, ST and EWS candidates, while PwBD applicants are exempted from paying any fee. The fee is non-refundable and applications submitted without the prescribed fee will be summarily rejected.
Q8 Q8. What is the selection process for AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of an objective screening or shortlisting stage followed by an interview or personality test conducted by the Standing Selection Committee. Screening scores are used only for shortlisting candidates for interview, and final selection is based solely on performance in the interview.
Q9 Q9. What are the pay scales for AIIMS Awantipora Faculty posts?
A
Assistant Professors are placed in Level 12 pay scale (about 1,01,500 to 1,67,400 rupees), Associate Professors in Level 13A1+, Additional Professors in Level 13A2+ and Professors in Level 14A as per 7th CPC. Eligible medical posts also carry Non-Practicing Allowance in addition to basic pay and other admissible allowances.
Q10 Q10. How can candidates apply online for AIIMS Awantipora Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online through the AIIMS Exams portal by visiting aiimsexams.ac.in, registering, selecting the relevant AIIMS Awantipora faculty advertisement and filling the application form. All required documents supporting age, qualification, experience, category and publications must be uploaded during online registration, as no corrections or additional document submissions are permitted after final submission.
