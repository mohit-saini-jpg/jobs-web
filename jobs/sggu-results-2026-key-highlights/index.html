Shri Govind Guru University (SGGU) has officially released the M.Com 4th Semester (ERP) Result for the March 2026 examination for the academic session 2025-2026. The results have been declared for postgraduate commerce courses on the official examination portal. SGGU Exam Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - sggu.ac.in.

Students can check and download their results by visiting the official result page at sggu.ac.in/Results.php and clicking on the M.Com Semester 4 result notification dated 28th April 2026. The university has made the result notification available in PDF format for download, which students can save for future reference and academic purposes. Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in their result including name, roll number, subject-wise marks, and overall status. In case of any discrepancies or errors in the result, students should immediately contact the Controller of Examinations of Shri Govind Guru University for clarification and correction within the specified timeframe.

How to Check SGGU Exam Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their SGGU M.Com 4th Semester Result 2026 from the official website:

  • Visit the official website of Shri Govind Guru University at sggu.ac.in
  • Navigate to the 'Results' or 'Examination' section on the homepage
  • Click on the results link or directly visit sggu.ac.in/Results.php
  • Look for the M.Com Semester 4 (ERP) March 2026 result notification dated 28-04-2026
  • Click on the result notification link - Notification No. R132/2026
  • The result PDF will open in a new window or download automatically
  • Look for your roll number or registration number in the result PDF
  • Check your subject-wise marks and overall status carefully
  • Verify all the details including name, roll number, subject codes, and marks obtained
  • Download and save the result PDF for future reference
  • Take a printout of the result for your academic records
  • If any discrepancy is found, contact the Controller of Examinations immediately
