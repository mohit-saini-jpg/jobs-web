FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for NIMHANS Recruitment 2026?
A
The application process is through a walk-in interview. Candidates need to appear for the walk-in interview on 11 June 2026. There is no prior online application deadline.
Q2How many vacancies are available in NIMHANS Recruitment 2026?
A
There are a total of 3 vacancies for the posts of Project Research Scientist II (Medical: Psychiatrist), Project Technical Support III (Social Worker), and Project Technical Support III (Yoga Therapist).
Q3What is the educational qualification required for Project Research Scientist II (Medical: Psychiatrist)?
A
For Project Research Scientist II (Medical: Psychiatrist), candidates need to have an MBBS with three years of post-qualification work experience in mental health, or an MBBS with a Post Graduate Degree (MD or DNB in Psychiatry), or MBBS with a PhD in Psychiatry.
Q4What is the age limit for Project Technical Support III posts?
A
The maximum age limit for Project Technical Support III (Social Worker) and Project Technical Support III (Yoga Therapist) posts is 35 years.
Q5What is the salary offered for Project Research Scientist II (Medical: Psychiatrist)?
A
The salary offered for the Project Research Scientist II (Medical: Psychiatrist) post is Rs. 80,000/- plus 30% House Rent Allowance (HRA).
Q6What is the selection process for NIMHANS Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process includes a Walk-in-cum-Written Test, followed by verification of resume and original testimonials, a set of self-attested photocopies, and potentially a skill test/interview or a direct interview.
Q7Where will the walk-in interview for NIMHANS Recruitment 2026 be held?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at NIMHANS, Bengaluru, Karnataka, 560029. Specific venue details will be in the official notification.
Q8Is there any application fee for NIMHANS Recruitment 2026?
A
No application fee is mentioned in the official notification for this recruitment drive.
Q9What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates should carry their resume, original testimonials, and a set of self-attested photocopies of all relevant documents, including ID proof and educational/experience certificates.
Q10What is the reporting time for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates are advised to register their names 30 minutes before the commencement of the written test/interview, which is scheduled to start at 10:30 AM on 11 June 2026.
