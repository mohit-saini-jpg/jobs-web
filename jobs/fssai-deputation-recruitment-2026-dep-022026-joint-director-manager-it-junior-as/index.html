FSSAI Deputation Recruitment 2026 (DEP-02/2026): Joint : Job Overview

The Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) has issued Circular DEP-02/2026 inviting applications for 15 Group A and Group C posts — Joint Director, Manager (IT), and Junior Assistant Grade-II — to be filled on deputation (including short-term contract) on Foreign Service terms. Eligible government officers can apply online on www.fssai.gov.in from 06 June 2026 to 26 June 2026.

FSSAI Deputation Recruitment 2026 (DEP-02/2026): Joint : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Eligible candidates need to follow a specific online application process, supported by a hard-copy application countersigned by their employer. Here's the step-by-step procedure:
  • 2Visit the official FSSAI website at www.fssai.gov.in.
  • 3Go to the "jobs@fssai (careers)" tab and locate the online application link for Circular DEP-02/2026.
  • 4Register and fill in the online application form, ensuring all details match your service records.
  • 5Confirm that you fulfill the eligibility criteria as on the closing date, 26 June 2026.
  • 6Submit the application online between 06 June 2026 and 26 June 2026 — applications not submitted online through FSSAI's portal will not be considered.
  • 7Take a printout of the finally submitted online application form.
  • 8Get the "Certification by the Employer/Cadre Controlling Authority" section completed and countersigned by your employer, confirming no vigilance/disciplinary case is pending, integrity is certified, and CR Dossier/APARs for the last 5 years are enclosed.
  • 9Forward the countersigned hard copy along with NOC, Vigilance Clearance, Integrity Certificate, and attested APARs through proper channel, with the tentative last date being 20 July 2026.
  • 10Candidates who had already applied for Joint Director (Post Code: DEP-01/2025-02) or Manager (IT) (Post Code: DEP-01/2025-06) under the earlier deputation notice DEP-01/2025 need not apply again. However, such applicants must ensure that all required documents — countersigned hard copy of the finally submitted online application, certified APARs, NOC, and Integrity/Vigilance clearance certificates — have been submitted through proper channel. If any document is pending, it should be submitted within the current due date for receiving hard copies.

FSSAI Deputation Recruitment 2026 (DEP-02/2026): Joint : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is FSSAI Circular DEP-02/2026?
A
FSSAI Circular DEP-02/2026 is a notification inviting applications from eligible government officers to fill 15 Group A and Group C posts — Joint Director, Manager (IT), and Junior Assistant Grade-II — on deputation/Foreign Service Terms.
Q2What is the last date to apply for FSSAI Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application portal is open from 06 June 2026 to 26 June 2026. The tentative last date for receiving the hard copy of the application through proper channel is 20 July 2026.
Q3How many vacancies are available under DEP-02/2026?
A
There are 15 vacancies in total — 3 for Joint Director (Pay Level-12), 2 for Manager (IT) (Pay Level-11), and 10 for Junior Assistant Grade-II (Pay Level-02).
Q4What is the age limit for FSSAI deputation posts?
A
The maximum age limit for appointment on deputation is 56 years as on the closing date of the online application, i.e., 26 June 2026.
Q5What is the tenure of deputation for these FSSAI posts?
A
Tenure varies by post: Joint Director – 1 year, Manager (IT) – 2 years, and Junior Assistant Grade-II – 3 years, in the first instance, extendable subject to vacancy availability.
Q6Who is eligible to apply for FSSAI deputation posts?
A
Officers holding regular posts in Central/State Government, Union Territories, Universities, Recognized Research Institutions, PSUs, Semi-Government, Statutory, or Autonomous Organizations, meeting the post-specific qualification and experience criteria, are eligible.
Q7Can candidates who applied under DEP-01/2025 apply again?
A
No. Candidates who already applied for Joint Director (DEP-01/2025-02) or Manager (IT) (DEP-01/2025-06) under the earlier notice need not apply again, but must ensure all required documents have been submitted through proper channel.
Q8How can I apply for FSSAI Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online via the "jobs@fssai (careers)" tab on www.fssai.gov.in between 06–26 June 2026, and subsequently forward a countersigned hard copy with NOC, APARs, and Vigilance Clearance through proper channel.
