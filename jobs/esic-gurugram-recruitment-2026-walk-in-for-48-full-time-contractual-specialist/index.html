ESIC Gurugram Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Qualification : PG Degree or PG Diploma in the concerned Specialty from a Recognized University. For ICU and Accident & Emergency, Postgraduates in Critical Care is required/preferred. Experience (Specialist) : Minimum 03 years after PG Degree or 05 years after PG Diploma in concerned specialty from government/recognized private hospital. Experience (Senior Resident) : PG preferred. If not available, MBBS with 2 years experience (1 year in specialty) may be considered for 1 year. Registration : Must be registered with Medical Council of India or State Medical Council.

ESIC Gurugram Recruitment 2026 - Salary / Stipend

Full Time Contractual Specialist : Consolidated Rs. 1,48,263/- per month (No Work No Pay Basis). Senior Resident : Initial pay in Level-11 with Basic Pay of Rs. 67,700/- plus other allowances as per 7th CPC. Deductions apply for candidates with only PG Diploma or without PG.

ESIC Gurugram Recruitment 2026 - Age Limit (as on 07 July 2026)

Full Time Contractual Specialist : Not exceeding 69 years. Senior Resident : Not exceeding 45 years. Age relaxation as per rules.

ESIC Gurugram Recruitment 2026 - Application Fee

SC/ST/PWD/Women Candidates & Ex-Servicemen: Nil All Other Categories: Rs. 300/- Payment Mode : Demand Draft in favour of “ESIC Fund Account No. 2” payable at Gurugram.

ESIC Gurugram Recruitment 2026 - Important Dates
