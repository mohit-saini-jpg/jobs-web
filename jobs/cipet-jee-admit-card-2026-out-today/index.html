CIPET JEE Admit Card 2026 Out Today

The Central Institute of Petrochemicals Engineering & Technology (CIPET) will be released the CIPET JEE Admit Card 2026 today, June 4, 2026. Candidates who have successfully registered for the CIPET Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) can now download their hall tickets from the official website. The admit card is a mandatory document for appearing in the entrance examination scheduled for June 7, 2026.

Candidates are advised to download their hall tickets as early as possible and carefully verify all details mentioned on the admit card. Any discrepancy should be reported to the examination authorities immediately before the examination date.

How to Download CIPET JEE Admit Card 2026

Follow the steps below to download your hall ticket:

  • Visit the official CIPET website.
  • Click on the "CIPET JEE Admit Card 2026" link.
  • Enter your registered Mobile Number and Date of Birth/Password.
  • Fill in the security code displayed on the screen.
  • Click on the Login/Submit button.
  • Your admit card will appear on the screen.
  • Download the hall ticket PDF.
  • Take a clear printout for examination purposes.

Details Mentioned on the Admit Card

Candidates should verify the following information after downloading the admit card:

  • Candidate's Name
  • Roll Number
  • Application Number
  • Photograph and Signature
  • Date of Birth
  • Examination Date
  • Examination Time
  • Reporting Time
  • Examination Centre Name
  • Examination Centre Address
  • Course Applied For
  • Important Instructions for Candidates

In case of any error in personal or exam details, contact CIPET immediately for correction.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates must carry the following documents to the examination centre:

  • Printed Copy of CIPET JEE Admit Card 2026
  • Original Valid Photo ID Proof: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Voter ID Passport Driving Licence
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Voter ID
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Passport-Size Photograph (if specified in the admit card instructions)

Candidates without a valid admit card and photo ID proof may not be allowed to enter the examination hall.

Exam-Day Instructions

Candidates should follow these important instructions on the examination day:

  • Reach the examination centre at least 30 minutes before reporting time.
  • Carry only permitted documents and stationery items.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, calculators, smart watches, Bluetooth devices, or any electronic gadgets.
  • Follow all instructions given by the invigilators.
  • Verify your seat and examination details before the test begins.
  • Maintain discipline throughout the examination.
  • Preserve the admit card safely even after the exam for admission and counselling purposes.
