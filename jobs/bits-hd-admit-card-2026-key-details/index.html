Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) Pilani has released the BITS HD Admit Card 2026 for candidates appearing in the Higher Degree (HD) admission test for M.E and M.Pharm programmes. As per the official schedule, BITS Pilani will issue the BITS HD 2026 hall tickets from 21 May 2026 till the exam date through the online admissions portal.

The BITS HD 2026 admission test will be conducted on 25 May and 26 May 2026 as a computer based online examination at multiple test centres. Candidates who completed the registration and slot booking process can now log in with their application number and password to download the admit card from the official BITS HD admissions website.

How to Download BITS HD Admit Card 2026

Follow the steps below to download your BITS HD 2026 hall ticket:

  • Visit the official BITS HD admission portal via admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in/HD/hd.html or bitsadmission.com (HD section).
  • Click on the “BITS HD 2026 Admit Card / Hall Ticket” or “Download BITS HD 2026 Admit Card” link on the homepage/admission test page.
  • On the login page, enter your BITS HD application number and password, and fill the captcha/security code if required.
  • Click on the Login / Submit button to access your candidate dashboard.
  • Click on the “Download Admit Card / Hall Ticket” link for BITS HD 2026; your admit card will be displayed on the screen, showing exam date, slot, and centre details.
  • Verify all the details carefully, then download and print 2–3 clear A4‑size copies of the BITS HD admit card 2026 to carry to the exam centre.

The admit card is available only online; it will not be sent by post.

Details Mentioned on BITS HD Admit Card 2026

The BITS HD 2026 admit card carries crucial exam and candidate information which must be checked before the exam:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph and signature
  • Application number and roll number (if allotted)
  • Programme / stream applied for (M.E / M.Pharm specialisation)
  • Exam name: BITS Higher Degree (BITS HD) Admission Test 2026
  • Exam date and slot timing (25 or 26 May 2026, with reporting time)
  • Test centre name, centre code, city and complete address
  • Instructions regarding test day rules, reporting time and allowed items

If you find any discrepancy in your name, photo, programme, exam date or centre details, contact the BITS HD admission office immediately using the email/helpline mentioned in the brochure (for example, [email protected]).

Documents Required with BITS HD Admit Card 2026

On the day of the BITS HD 2026 exam, candidates should carry:

  • A printed copy of the BITS HD Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence or any other government‑issued ID accepted as per BITS instructions.
  • A simple calculator (non‑programmable, without connectivity), if allowed as per BITS HD exam instructions.

Candidates without a printed hall ticket and valid photo ID will not be allowed to enter the exam centre.

Exam‑Day Instructions for BITS HD 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the BITS HD Admission Test 2026, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the test centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the BITS HD admit card to complete verification and seating; latecomers may not be permitted entry after gate closing time.
  • Carry only essential items: BITS HD 2026 admit card (A4 printout), valid photo ID and permitted items such as a simple calculator if allowed; follow the detailed instructions provided with the hall ticket.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, programmable calculators, Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper materials into the examination hall; violation can lead to cancellation of your test and further action.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the admit card and those announced by invigilators; indulging in unfair means, discussing answers with others or disobeying exam rules can result in cancellation of your exam and debarment from future BITS admissions.
  • Keep your BITS HD Admit Card 2026 safely even after the exam, as it may be required during result checking, counselling and admission formalities.
