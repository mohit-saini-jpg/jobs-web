Job Overview

Medical postgraduates looking for a short-term residency opportunity under the Government of India scheme now have a new opening at one of India's premier public health institutions. All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) Raipur, Chhattisgarh, has released an official advertisement inviting applications for the post of Senior Resident (Non-Academic) across various departments for a tenure period of 11 months. Applications open on 03 June 2026 and close on 12 June 2026.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the pay for the Senior Resident post at AIIMS Raipur?
A
The selected candidates will receive a monthly pay of Rs. 67,700/- (Level-11 as per 7th CPC) along with usual allowances including NPA, if applicable.
Q2Q2. What is the tenure of the Senior Resident position?
A
The appointment is for a period of 11 months under the Government of India Residency Scheme.
Q3Q3. What qualifications are required to apply?
A
Candidates must possess a Postgraduate Medical Degree such as MD, MS, DNB, or Diploma in the respective discipline. For Dentistry posts, MDS in the concerned speciality is required.
Q4Q4. Is the application fee refundable?
A
No. The application fee of Rs. 1,000/- for General/OBC/EWS candidates is non-refundable. Women, SC, ST, PwBD, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee.
Q5Q5. What is the last date to apply?
A
The last date to submit the application is 12 June 2026.
Q6Q6. Will a written test be conducted?
A
Selection is primarily based on an Interview. However, if a large number of applications are received, a Screening Test may be conducted for shortlisting. The method and schedule will be notified on the AIIMS Raipur website.
Q7Q7. Is TA/DA provided for attending the interview?
A
No. No Travelling Allowance or Daily Allowance will be paid to candidates appearing for the interview or written test.
Q8Q8. Where will the interview be held?
A
The interview will be held at AIIMS Raipur, Tatibandh, G.E. Road, Raipur (C.G.) – 492099 only.
Q9Q9. Can candidates working in Government service apply?
A
Yes. However, they must submit a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their present employer at the time of the interview.
Q10Q10. Where can I find the official notification?
A
The official notification and application proforma are available on the AIIMS Raipur website: www.aiimsraipur.edu.in.
