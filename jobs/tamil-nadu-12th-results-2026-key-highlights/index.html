How to Check Tamil Nadu 12th Result 2026 Online

Follow the steps below to check and download your Tamil Nadu 12th Result 2026 from the official website in a simple and error-free way.

  • Visit the official TN board result website tnresults.nic.in in your browser.
  • On the homepage, click on the link labeled 'HSE (+2) Examination Results 2026' or 'Tamil Nadu 12th Result 2026'.
  • You will be redirected to the result login page. Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth exactly as printed on your hall ticket.
  • After filling in the details, click on the 'Submit' or 'Get Marks' button to proceed.
  • Your TN HSC Plus Two result 2026 with subject-wise marks, total marks, grade and result status will appear on the screen.
  • Check all the details carefully and then click on the 'Print' or 'Download' option to save a copy of your online marksheet for future reference.
