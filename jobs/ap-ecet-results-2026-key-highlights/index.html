Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) has officially released the AP ECET Results 2026 for candidates who appeared in the Engineering Common Entrance Test. The result includes rank cards for diploma holders and B.Sc (Mathematics) graduates seeking lateral entry admissions into engineering and pharmacy courses.

AP ECET Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - cets.apsche.ap.gov.in. Candidates can check and download their rank card by logging in with their registration number, hall ticket number, and date of birth. Students are advised to verify all the details mentioned on the result and contact the authorities in case of any discrepancies.

How to Download AP ECET Results 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your AP ECET rank card:

  • Visit the official website - cets.apsche.ap.gov.in
  • Click on the AP ECET 2026 result or rank card link
  • Enter your registration number, hall ticket number, and date of birth
  • Click on the submit button
  • Your rank card will be displayed on the screen
  • Download and take a printout for counselling purposes
