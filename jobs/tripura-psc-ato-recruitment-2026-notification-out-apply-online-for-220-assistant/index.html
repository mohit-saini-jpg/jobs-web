Syllabus

The Written Examination consists of two papers - Paper I (General Ability Test, 50 marks) and Paper II (Job Oriented Technical Paper, 120 marks). All questions are OMR-based MCQ type, answered in English only. Negative marking will apply as per TPSC norms amended from time to time. The detailed syllabus is reproduced below from Annexure-B and Annexure-C of the official notification (as published in the Tripura Gazette Extraordinary Issue, February 25, 2026). | Paper I - General Ability Test (50 Marks, 50 Questions of 1 Mark each) | Paper II - Job Oriented Technical Paper (Full Marks: 120, OMR MCQ - 120 Questions of 1 Mark each)

How to Apply

  • 1Applications must be submitted online only through the TPSC Online Application Portal, accessible on the official website https://tpsc.tripura.gov.in. Hard copy applications will not be accepted under any circumstances. The online portal will be active from 12-05-2026 to 05-06-2026 (5:30 PM). New users (first-time applicants) must complete One Time Profile Registration (OTPR) on the TPSC website before applying - a User ID will be generated and sent to the registered mobile number/email ID upon successful registration.
  • 2Existing OTPR users can directly apply using their User ID. Candidates should avoid submitting multiple applications; if done unavoidably, only the application with the latest Application Number will be considered valid, and fee paid against other application numbers will not be adjusted. After the closing date, a 7-day correction window will be available for making corrections in the application form - no changes are allowed after this window closes. Candidates are not required to upload supporting documents at the time of online application; documents will be called after the written examination result is published.
  • 3Documents to be Submitted After Written Examination (on being called):
  • 4Age proof - Birth Certificate or Admit Card of Madhyamik examination
  • 5Educational qualifications (Madhyamik onwards) - marksheets and certificates
  • 6ST / SC / PH / Ex-SM certificate (if applicable)
  • 7Permanent Resident Certificate of Tripura (PRTC)
  • 8Online application form duly filled up
  • 9Documents to be submitted to the Reception Counter of TPSC or by post to: The Secretary, TPSC, Agartala, PIN-799001 within the time specified. Non-submission within the specified time will result in cancellation of candidature.
  • 10Apply Online at: https://tpsc.tripura.gov.in

Important Instructions

Only bonafide citizens of India with valid PRTC are eligible to apply. Candidates must possess the prescribed minimum qualification on the closing date of application. No hard copy applications will be accepted - online submission only. Recruitment fee is non-refundable; incomplete applications without fee will be rejected. Government employees must produce NOC at the time of interview if application not routed through Proper Channel. Mobile phones and electronic gadgets are banned inside examination/interview premises. Examination venue closed 10 minutes before scheduled commencement - latecomers not allowed. Candidates are advised to visit https://tpsc.tripura.gov.in regularly for updates on recruitment steps. Questions in all papers to be answered in English only - no other language permitted. Negative marking on MCQ questions will apply as per TPSC norms. For any eligibility-related enquiry, candidates should approach the concerned Government Department - the Commission will not entertain such representations. All decisions of the Commission regarding eligibility, selection, and other matters are final and binding.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date to apply for TPSC Assistant Technical Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for TPSC Assistant Technical Officer (ATO) Recruitment 2026 is 05 June 2026 (up to 5:30 PM). The online application portal will open on 12 May 2026, giving candidates approximately 24 days to complete their registration and application submission through the official TPSC website. A 7-day correction window will be available after the closing date for making necessary corrections in the application form.
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are announced for TPSC ATO Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 220 vacancies have been announced for the post of Assistant Technical Officer, Group-B (Gazetted) under Advertisement No. 02/2026 dated 27 April 2026. The vacancies are distributed across categories: 114 for UR (including 2 Ex-Servicemen), 38 for SC (including 1 Ex-Serviceman), and 68 for ST (including 1 Ex-Serviceman), with 33% horizontal reservation for women within each category. Additionally, 9 posts (4% of total) are reserved for Physically Handicapped candidates across four sub-categories.
Q3Q3. What is the educational qualification required for TPSC Assistant Technical Officer posts?
A
Candidates must possess either a Bachelor of Engineering/Technology (BE/B.Tech) in Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics, Electronics and Communication, or Electronics & Telecommunications from any Government Institute, Government-recognized institute, or AICTE/UGC-recognized institute, OR a Master of Computer Application (MCA) from any Government or Government-recognized institute. The qualification must be obtained on or before the closing date of application submission (05 June 2026), and knowledge of Bengali or Kokborok is desirable.
Q4Q4. What is the age limit for TPSC ATO Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must be at least 18 years and not more than 40 years of age as on 05 June 2026. The upper age limit is relaxed by 5 years for ST, SC, Physically Handicapped, and Government employee candidates, extending their maximum age to 45 years. However, Government Employees belonging to ST/SC/PH categories will receive only 5 years total relaxation and not additional relaxation over and above the general 5-year concession. There is no limit on the number of attempts for this examination.
Q5Q5. Is Permanent Resident Certificate of Tripura (PRTC) mandatory for application?
A
Yes, possession of a valid Permanent Resident Certificate of Tripura (PRTC) is absolutely mandatory for all applicants as per GA(P&T) Department Notification dated 07 July 2023. Candidates without a valid PRTC are not eligible to apply for this recruitment, regardless of their educational qualifications or other criteria. Only bonafide citizens of India holding valid PRTC can submit applications for the Assistant Technical Officer posts.
Q6Q6. What is the application fee for TPSC Assistant Technical Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee for General (UR) category candidates is Rs. 350/- (Rupees Three Hundred and Fifty only), while ST/SC/BPL Card Holders/Physically Handicapped candidates need to pay Rs. 250/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Fifty only). The fee must be paid online through the TPSC application portal, and it is non-refundable under any circumstances. Applications submitted without the requisite recruitment fee or with incomplete fee payment will be rejected.
Q7Q7. What is the selection process for TPSC ATO posts?
A
The selection process consists of two successive stages: a Written Examination of 170 marks (OMR-based MCQ format, 3 hours duration) and an Interview/Personality Test of 30 marks. The written exam comprises Paper I (General Ability Test - 50 marks) and Paper II (Job Oriented Technical Paper - 120 marks). Candidates who qualify the written examination will be called for interview in a specified ratio (5 candidates for 1 vacancy, 8 for 2 vacancies, and 3 times the number of vacancies for 3 or more posts). The final merit list will be prepared based on combined total marks from both stages.
Q8Q8. What is the pay scale for TPSC Assistant Technical Officer posts?
A
The Assistant Technical Officer is a Group-B Gazetted post under the Tripura Information Technology Service (ITS) Rules, 2025, with Pay Band-3: Rs. 10,230–34,800/- and Grade Pay of Rs. 4,800/- (Level-13, Tripura State Pay Matrix 2018). This recruitment is being conducted by the Tripura Public Service Commission for the Directorate of Information Technology, Department of Industries & Commerce (IT), Government of Tripura. The selection list recommended by the Commission remains valid for 6 months from the date of recommendation, extendable up to 1 year in consultation with the Government.
Q9Q9. What are the qualifying marks required in the TPSC ATO written examination?
A
Unreserved (UR) category candidates must secure a minimum of 55% marks in each subject (Paper I and Paper II separately) to qualify the written examination. Reserved category candidates (SC/ST/PH and others) need to obtain a minimum of 45% marks in each subject to qualify. Negative marking will apply as per TPSC norms amended from time to time. All questions in both papers must be answered in English only, and no other language is permitted.
Q10Q10. Do I need to upload documents during online application for TPSC ATO Recruitment?
A
No, candidates are not required to upload supporting documents at the time of online application submission. Documents will be called only after the written examination result is published. However, shortlisted candidates must submit their original documents (age proof, educational qualifications, ST/SC/PH/Ex-SM certificate if applicable, PRTC, and online application form) at the TPSC Reception Counter or by post to The Secretary, TPSC, Agartala, PIN-799001 within the specified time. Non-submission of documents within the prescribed period will result in cancellation of candidature.
