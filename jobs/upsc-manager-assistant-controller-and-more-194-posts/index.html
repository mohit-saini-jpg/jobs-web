How to Apply

  • 1Live photograph captured via webcam/camera during CAF filling (mandatory)
  • 2Scanned recent passport-size photograph (as per instructions on UPSC website)
  • 3Scanned signature (three signatures one below the other on plain white paper, black ink)
  • 4Matriculation/10th Standard certificate or marksheet as proof of Date of Birth
  • 5Degree/Diploma certificate as proof of educational qualification; if not available, provisional certificate + all year marksheets
  • 6Experience Certificate(s) in prescribed format from Head(s) of Organisation(s) - mentioning duration, basic pay, consolidated pay, and nature of duties
  • 7Caste/Community certificate (SC/ST/OBC) in prescribed proforma from Competent Authority
  • 8OBC Non-Creamy Layer declaration in prescribed format
  • 9EWS certificate in prescribed proforma
  • 10PwBD Disability Certificate from Medical Board constituted by Central/State Government
  • 11Age relaxation certificate for Ex-Servicemen/Central/UT Government Employees in prescribed proforma
  • 12Order/letter for equivalent qualification (if applicable) citing Authority with number and date
  • 13Name change documents (if applicable - marriage certificate/divorce decree/affidavit as per situation)
  • 14Documentary support for any other claim made (Professional Registration, Publications, GATE/NET, etc.)
  • 15Online applications open from 23-05-2026 and close on 12-06-2026 at 1800 Hrs - candidates must apply well in advance without waiting for the closing date.
  • 16All candidates must enter correct and active email addresses - all correspondence including interview schedules will be communicated only through the registered email ID.
  • 17Applications once submitted are final - no correction/alteration/modification in any field is allowed after submission. No withdrawal of applications is permitted.
  • 18The date for determining eligibility in every respect is the closing date for submission of online recruitment application (12-06-2026).
  • 19Filling wrong information may lead to rejection through computer-based shortlisting apart from debarment by the Commission.
  • 20Face authentication will be mandatory for all candidates at the examination venue/Interview Hall - candidates must enter well in time for face authentication and frisking.
  • 21Mobile phones (even in switched-off mode), pagers, smart watches, pen drives, cameras, Bluetooth devices, or any electronic/communication device are strictly banned at the examination/interview venue. Violation leads to disciplinary action including ban from future examinations/recruitment.
  • 22Candidates must not bring valuables or bags to the venue - no arrangement for safe-keeping will be made and UPSC will not be responsible for any loss.
  • 23The Commission will contribute towards interview travelling expenses at Second Class Mail railway fare rate by shortest route - candidates will be informed of details when called for interview.
  • 24Canvassing in any form will disqualify a candidate.
  • 25Summoning for interview conveys no assurance of selection - appointment orders will be issued by the respective Ministry.
