What Is HPCET 2026 Counselling? : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 19/06/2026

Himachal Pradesh Technical University (HPTU) has opened the counselling registration window for HPCET 2026. Candidates who qualified the Himachal Pradesh Common Entrance Test can now register online at himtu.ac.in to begin the seat allotment process for admission to engineering, pharmacy, and other technical programmes across the state's participating institutes.

What Is HPCET 2026 Counselling?

This section explains the basic purpose of HPCET counselling for readers who are new to the process.

HPCET counselling is the centralised admission process conducted by HPTU, Hamirpur, for candidates who have qualified the Himachal Pradesh Common Entrance Test. Through this process, qualified candidates register online, fill their course and college preferences, and get seats allotted based on their rank, category, and seat availability. The counselling applies to admissions across HPTU's affiliated government and private institutes in Himachal Pradesh.

How to Register for HPCET 2026 Counselling Online

This section walks candidates through the step-by-step process to complete counselling registration at himtu.ac.in.

Candidates can complete HPCET 2026 counselling registration by following these steps:

  • Visit the official HPTU website, himtu.ac.in.
  • Click on the HPCET 2026 counselling registration link on the homepage.
  • Register using basic details such as name, HPCET roll number, and contact information to generate login credentials.
  • Log in using the registration number and password/date of birth.
  • Fill in personal, academic, and category details in the counselling-cum-admission form.
  • Upload the required scanned documents and photograph as per specifications.
  • Pay the applicable counselling fee through net banking, debit card, or credit card.
  • Fill course and college preferences in order of priority from the available seat matrix.
  • Lock your choices before the deadline; unlocked choices may be auto-considered as locked once the window closes.
  • Submit the form and save/print the confirmation page for future reference.

HPCET 2026 Counselling Fee

This section details the fee candidates need to pay during registration, based on category.

The counselling fee for candidates who do not already hold an HPCET score is structured by category, as per HPTU's notified fee schedule:

CategoryCounselling Fee
General / EWS / OBCRs. 1,600
SC / ST / BPL / IRDPRs. 800

Candidates applying through HPCET alone are generally exempted from this additional fee, while those applying via both HPCET and a national-level test may be required to pay the applicable amount. Fee payment must be completed online through net banking, debit card, or credit card; no other payment mode is accepted.

Documents Required for HPCET 2026 Counselling

This section lists the documents candidates must keep ready for verification during counselling.

Candidates must upload and later present the following documents in original for verification:

  • HPCET 2026 / JEE Main 2026 / qualifying exam scorecard
  • Class 10 mark sheet and certificate (for age proof)
  • Class 12 mark sheet and passing certificate
  • School leaving certificate / transfer certificate
  • Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS), if applicable
  • BPL/IRDP certificate, if applicable
  • Passport-size photograph and signature in the specified format

HPCET 2026 Counselling Process: Step-by-Step Stages

This section breaks down the complete counselling journey from registration to admission confirmation.

The HPCET 2026 counselling procedure includes the following stages:

  • Online Registration: Candidates register at himtu.ac.in and pay the counselling fee within the notified window.
  • Choice Filling: Candidates select course and college preferences from the published seat matrix, in order of priority.
  • Locking of Choices: Preferences must be locked before the deadline; any choices left unlocked may be treated as locked automatically.
  • Seat Allotment: Seats are allotted based on HPCET 2026 rank, category, preferences submitted, and seat availability.
  • Document Verification: Allotted candidates upload or present documents for verification.
  • Admission Fee Payment: Candidates confirm their allotted seat by paying the admission fee.
  • Reporting to Institute: Candidates report to the allotted institute with original documents within the specified timeline.
  • Multiple Rounds: HPTU conducts more than one round of counselling including a mop-up or stray vacancy round until seats are filled.

Courses and Institutes Covered Under HPCET 2026 Counselling

This section explains which programmes and colleges accept admission through HPCET counselling.

HPCET 2026 scores are accepted by HPTU and over 50 affiliated government and private colleges, deemed universities, and other institutes established under the Himachal Pradesh State Act. Admissions are offered for:

  • B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology)
  • B.Pharmacy and B.Pharmacy (Ayurveda)
  • B.Sc.
  • MCA (Master of Computer Applications)
  • MBA and MBA (Tourism & Hospitality Management)
  • M.Tech and other postgraduate technical programmes

For B.Tech admissions specifically, 50% of seats at HPTU-affiliated institutes are filled through HPCET 2026 ranks, while the remaining 50% are filled based on JEE Main 2026 scores.

HPCET 2026 Seat Allotment

This section explains how seats are allotted once choice filling is complete.

Seat allotment under HPCET 2026 counselling is based on the candidate's HPCET rank, category, the preferences locked during choice filling, and seat availability in the chosen course and institute. Once a seat is allotted, candidates must confirm admission within the notified deadline through document verification and fee payment. Candidates who fail to confirm admission by the deadline risk cancellation of their allotted seat. HPTU typically conducts multiple counselling rounds including a mop-up/stray vacancy round to fill any seats that remain vacant after the initial rounds.
