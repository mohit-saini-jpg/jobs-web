DetailsLower Division Clerk (LDC): Below 50 years (55 years for ESM/JCOs) as on 01 July 2026. Dissection Hall Attendant (DHA): Below 53 years as on 01 July 2026. Age relaxation for ESM/JCOs for LDC post is applicable.

Qualification / Eligibility

Lower Division Clerk (LDC): Graduate and computer literate with three years of experience in a reputed organization, preferably in an educational institution. For Ex-Servicemen (ESM), 10 years of service as Clerk in the Army is required. Dissection Hall Attendant (DHA): Ex-Servicemen (AMC, retired House Keeper) with a minimum of 15 years of service rendered in the Army Medical Corps.
