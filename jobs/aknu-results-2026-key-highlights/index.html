Adikavi Nannaya University (AKNU) has officially released the 1st Semester LLM and 8th Semester B.Tech results for the academic session 2025-2026. AKNU Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - aknu.edu.in.

Students can check and download their results by logging into the results portal using their registration number or hall ticket number along with the captcha code for verification. The university has made the provisional result copies available for download, which students can save for future reference and academic purposes. Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in their result including name, registration number, subject-wise marks, and overall status. In case of any discrepancies or errors in the result, students should immediately contact the examination cell of Adikavi Nannaya University for clarification and correction.

How to Check AKNU Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their AKNU 1st and 8th Semester Result 2026 from the official website:

  • Visit the official website of Adikavi Nannaya University at aknu.edu.in
  • Navigate to the 'Results' section or click on the direct result link at aknu.edu.in/results
  • Select the appropriate result link - LLM 1st Semester or B.Tech 8th Semester as applicable
  • Enter your registration number or hall ticket number in the required field
  • Type the captcha code displayed on the screen for verification
  • Click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button to access your result
  • Your AKNU Result 2026 will appear on the screen showing subject-wise marks and overall status
  • Check all the details carefully including name, registration number, and marks obtained
  • Download and save the provisional result copy for future reference
  • Take a printout of the result for your academic records
