Short Information

The Nagaland Public Service Commission (NPSC) has officially announced the Common Educational Services Examinations (CESE) Recruitment 2026 with 178 openings. The detailed notification was published in April 2026 at npsc.nagaland.gov.in. Candidates who meet the age criteria of 21 to 32 years and hold qualifications such as Master’s Degree, B.Ed., or other relevant educational qualifications (as per post) can now submit their online applications from 12 May 2026 to 02 June 2026. Direct links to download the notification and apply online are shared below.

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the NPSC portal at https://npsc.nagaland.gov.in. No hard copy applications will be accepted. The OTR (One Time Registration) starts from 12-05-2026 (Noon) and closes on 02-06-2026 (Noon). Candidates who have previously completed the OTR process may directly submit the online application. Candidates are requested to complete OTR at the earliest to avoid online traffic/complications due to last-minute submission.
  • Only those candidates with requisite qualification for CESE-2026 may register. Government employees must submit a "NO OBJECTION CERTIFICATE" signed by the Head of the Department at the time of submission of the online application form. For any assistance/clarification, candidates may refer to FAQs from the Commission's website or call the Helpdesk No. +91 9402988506 on working days (10:00 AM to 03:00 PM). Any issues/complaints must be settled on or before the last date of submission - no issues/complaints will be entertained thereafter.
  • Documents to be Submitted when Called by Commission:
  • HSLC/Matriculation/Equivalent Admit Card for proof of Date of Birth (no other certificate will be accepted for age proof)
  • Educational Qualification Certificates - Mark Sheets & Pass certificates/provisional pass certificates from matriculation onwards till requisite qualification
  • Indigenous Inhabitant Certificate, Scheduled Tribe Certificate, Backward Tribe Certificate (as applicable) - signed only by District Authority not below the rank of Additional Deputy Commissioner
  • For PwBDs - certificate from competent medical authority in Form C, D & E as mandated in No.NPSC/EXAM-2/2025 dt 07.08.2025
  • SC/ST & B.T. Certificates as relevant
  • All documents/certificates must be duly signed by the concerned authority
  • Apply Online at: https://npsc.nagaland.gov.in

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is NPSC CESE Recruitment 2026?

Nagaland PSC CESE Recruitment 2026 is a Common Educational Services Examination conducted by the Nagaland Public Service Commission (NPSC), Kohima to fill teaching and academic posts like Assistant Professor, Lecturer, PGT, Graduate Teacher, Research Associate and Librarian under various education departments of the Government of Nagaland. Recruitment is being conducted through Advertisement No. NPSC‑1/CESE‑2026 (No.NPSC/EXAM‑21/2023) published in April 2026.

Q2. How many vacancies are announced under NPSC CESE 2026 and for which posts?

The CESE 2026 notification covers around 178 vacancies clubbed into 54 item numbers across Higher Education, School Education, Technical Education and SCERT. These include a large number of Assistant Professor posts in subjects like Chemistry, English, Mathematics, Physics, Botany, Zoology, Commerce and Sociology, along with Lecturer, PGT, Graduate Teacher, Librarian and Research Associate posts.

Q3. What are the application dates and exam date for NPSC CESE 2026?

The online One Time Registration (OTR) and application process for NPSC CESE 2026 opens on 12 May 2026 (noon) and closes on 02 June 2026 (noon). The written examination for CESE 2026 is tentatively scheduled for 11 July 2026, and candidates should regularly check the official website for final exam routine and admit‑card dates.

Q4. What is the age limit and age relaxation for NPSC CESE 2026?

Candidates must be between 21 and 32 years of age as on 1 January 2026 to be eligible for CESE 2026. Upper‑age relaxations are available for SC/ST candidates (typically 5 years), Government employees, Persons with Benchmark Disabilities and Ph.D. holders for specified Assistant Professor posts, as per norms indicated in recent CESE notifications.

Q5. What are the essential educational qualifications for Assistant Professor posts under CESE 2026?

For most Assistant Professor posts, candidates must have a Master’s Degree in the relevant subject with at least 55% marks (relaxable by 5% for SC/ST and some categories) from a recognized university. In addition, candidates should have cleared NET/SLET/SET or hold a Ph.D. degree in accordance with UGC Regulations, in which case NET/SLET/SET may be exempted as per rules; for disciplines where NET is not conducted, NET is not mandatory.

Q6. What is the qualification requirement for PGT and Graduate Teacher posts?

For PGT posts under the School Education Department, the minimum qualification is a Post Graduate degree in the concerned subject with at least 50% marks (relaxable for SC/ST/differently abled candidates) along with a B.Ed. For Graduate Teacher posts, a Graduate/Post Graduate degree with 50% marks in the relevant subject and B.Ed is required, again with similar relaxations for reserved categories.

Q7. What is the application fee for NPSC CESE 2026 and is OTR chargeable?

One Time Registration (OTR) is free of cost for all candidates on the NPSC portal. For CESE 2026, candidates must pay an examination fee of ₹300 plus a convenience fee of ₹25 and applicable payment‑gateway charges at the time of submitting the online application.

Q8. How can I apply online for NPSC CESE 2026?

Candidates must first complete the One Time Registration (OTR) on the NPSC portal npsc.nagaland.gov.in and then proceed to fill the CESE 2026 online application form using their OTR profile. They need to enter personal and educational details, upload required documents, pay the examination fee online, submit the form, and download/print the final application for future reference.
