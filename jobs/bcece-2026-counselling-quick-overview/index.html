BCECE 2026 Counselling - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 14/07/2026

BCECE 2026 Counselling Schedule: The Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) released the BCECE 2026 counselling schedule on July 13, 2026, at the official website, bceceboard.bihar.gov.in. Candidates who hold a valid BCECE 2026 rank card can begin online registration for counselling from July 19, 2026. This article covers the complete BCECE 2026 counselling schedule, eligibility criteria, required documents, fee details, and the step-by-step registration and choice filling process, based on the official notification and BCECEB portal.

BCECE 2026 Counselling Eligibility Criteria

To participate in BCECE 2026 Counselling, candidates must meet the following conditions:

  • Must have appeared in BCECE 2026 and hold a valid rank card.
  • Candidates who appeared under the Lateral Entry (LE) stream are eligible only for lateral entry counselling to the second year of eligible programmes, not for the main BCECE counselling.
  • Must be an Indian citizen.
  • Must satisfy the course-wise educational qualification listed below.

BCECE 2026 Reservation Category Details

Bihar follows a state-specific reservation structure for BCECE counselling. Based on the official category structure:

  • EBC (Extremely Backward Class): 18% reservation
  • SC (Scheduled Caste): 16% reservation
  • BC (Backward Class): 12% reservation
  • RCG (Reservation for Girls): An additional gender-specific layer within SC, ST, EBC, and BC categories, giving preference to girl candidates from these backgrounds.
  • PwD (Persons with Disabilities): A horizontal reservation of 5% within every category for eligible candidates with disabilities.

Exact category-wise seat numbers depend on the official seat matrix, which BCECEB is expected to release closer to the choice filling window.

Documents Required for BCECE 2026 Counselling

Keep the following documents ready in scanned format for registration and for physical verification at the time of admission:

  • BCECE 2026 Rank Card / Score Card
  • Class 10 and Class 12 mark sheets and certificates
  • Bihar Domicile Certificate (mandatory for all candidates, without exception)
  • Caste/Category Certificate, issued by a Bihar state authority only - certificates issued outside Bihar are not accepted
  • PwD certificate, if applicable
  • Passport-size photograph and signature in the specified format
  • A valid photo ID proof

Since domicile and caste certificates issued by Bihar authorities can take several weeks to process, candidates are advised to apply for these well before the counselling registration window opens.

BCECE 2026 Counselling: Step-by-Step Process

BCECE 2026 Counselling is conducted entirely online. Candidates do not need to visit the BCECEB office for registration or choice filling. Follow these steps:

  • Visit the official website, bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
  • Click on the BCECE 2026 Counselling Registration link when the window opens.
  • Register using your BCECE 2026 roll number, rank card details, and other requested information to generate a user ID and password.
  • Log in with your user ID and password.
  • Fill in personal, academic, and category details, and upload the required documents.
  • Pay the non-refundable counselling registration fee online.
  • Proceed to choice filling: select your preferred colleges and courses in order of priority. There is no limit on the number of choices you can add.
  • Review and lock your choices before the deadline. A confirmation of your locked choices will be sent to your registered email.
  • Wait for the seat allotment result on the scheduled date.
  • If allotted a seat, pay the seat acceptance fee and report to the allotted institute with original documents for verification.

BCECE 2026 Choice Filling: What You Should Know

Choice filling lets candidates rank their preferred college-and-course combinations. Candidates can edit their choices any number of times until the final locking deadline, but once locked, choices cannot be changed. It is advisable to research previous years' closing ranks for target colleges before filling choices, so that the final list includes a realistic mix of ambitious, moderate, and safe options rather than only top-preference colleges.

BCECE 2026 Seat Allotment Process

Seat allotment is based on the candidate's BCECE 2026 rank, category, and the choices filled and locked during the counselling window. Round 1 seat allotment is scheduled for July 29, 2026. Candidates allotted a seat must:

  • Download the provisional seat allotment letter from the counselling portal.
  • Pay the seat acceptance fee within the specified deadline.
  • Report to the allotted institute with original documents for verification within the reporting window.

Candidates who do not receive a seat in Round 1, or who wish to try for a better allotment, may be eligible to participate in subsequent rounds once BCECEB announces those dates.

Important Instructions for BCECE 2026 Counselling Candidates

  • Confirm every date and requirement from the official BCECE 2026 Counselling Prospectus before taking any action.
  • Bihar Domicile Certificate is compulsory for all candidates; there is no exemption.
  • Caste certificates must be issued by a Bihar state authority; certificates from other states will not be accepted.
  • Both the registration fee and the seat acceptance fee are non-refundable once paid.
  • Keep scanned copies of all documents ready in the specified format and size before starting registration to avoid last-minute errors.
  • Regularly check bceceboard.bihar.gov.in for updates, as BCECEB may revise dates through official notices.
