Important Instructions

This is an internal notification meant ONLY for in-service departmental employees of SECL – no external/direct candidates can apply. Employees originally appointed in a higher grade are NOT eligible to apply; only employees in the same grade or below (as per Cadre Scheme) can apply. The cut-off date for eligibility is 31.05.2026 – all qualifications and service conditions must be completed by this date. Applications of employees whose Annual Confidential Report (ACR) for the actual working period or financial year 2024-2025 is rated as POOR will be summarily rejected and they will not be eligible for further stages. Minimum attendance of 240 days (above ground) and 190 days (underground) in 7 months of calendar year 2025 (June-December 2025) and 5 months of 2026 (January-May 2026) including all sanctioned leave is mandatory. Candidates must not provide incomplete or false information – if detected at any stage, candidature will be cancelled immediately and disciplinary action will be taken. All documents uploaded on the portal must be clearly legible and self-attested – unclear/illegible uploads will lead to application rejection. Employees working in Government Departments, PSUs, or Govt. Funded Agencies must forward applications through proper channel. For OBC-NCL candidates, certificate must be in the prescribed format as per Government of India norms. Selected employees will be posted at various locations of SECL as per vacancy and requirement – selected employees who do not join after posting or request cancellation of selection may be DEBARRED for two (2) years from future selection processes. Candidates appearing for the written test will be eligible for Travel Allowance (TA) and Daily Allowance (DA) as per CIL/SECL rules. The management reserves the right to modify, withdraw, or cancel this notification at any time due to administrative or other reasons. All future correspondence regarding the selection process will be available on the SECL internal recruitment portal and official website – candidates should check regularly. Legal disputes, if any, shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the competent court at Bilaspur only.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 Q1. What is the total number of vacancies in SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Grade-D Internal Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 65 vacancies (subject to change as per sanctioned manpower budget 2025-26) have been announced for the post of Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Grade-D with 15 reserved for SC, 7 for ST (including backlog), and 43 for UR category.
Q2 Q2. Who is eligible to apply for SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Grade-D Recruitment 2026?
A
This is an internal departmental selection. Only permanent and regular in-service employees of SECL are eligible to apply. External/direct candidates cannot apply. Employees originally appointed in a higher grade are also NOT eligible. The eligibility cut-off date is 31.05.2026.
Q3 Q3. What is the last date to apply online for SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic Grade-D Internal Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for eligible employees to submit the online application is 01 July 2026 (11:59 PM). Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date to avoid technical difficulties.
Q4 Q4. What is the educational qualification required for SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Grade-D post?
A
There are two qualification pathways: (1) Standard X / Matriculate or equivalent for permanent employees with one year of service in Cat. IV/Tech. & Sup. Gr. E and above with aptitude for telecom discipline; OR (2) Standard X / Matriculate or equivalent along with ITI certificate in Electronics/equivalent for any permanent employee of the Company.
Q5 Q5. What is the selection process for SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic Grade-D Internal Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection will be through a Written Test (MCQ pattern) of 100 marks conducted on OMR sheet. There is NO interview and NO negative marking. The exam consists of Subject Knowledge (60 marks), Mental Ability/Reasoning (20 marks), and General Awareness about CIL/SECL and the State (20 marks).
Q6 Q6. What are the qualifying marks for the SECL written test 2026?
A
General category candidates must score a minimum of 40% of total marks in the written test. SC/ST category candidates must score a minimum of 30% of total marks to qualify the written test.
Q7 Q7. Where can I apply online for SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Grade-D Internal Recruitment 2026?
A
Eligible in-service employees of SECL can apply only through the official internal selection portal at https://portals.secl-cil.in/internal/index.php. The link is also available on SECL's official website at https://www.secl-cil.in. No offline applications will be accepted.
Q8 Q8. Is there negative marking in the SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Grade-D Written Test 2026?
A
No. There is NO negative marking for wrong answers in the written test. The MCQ-based OMR test consists of 100 questions carrying 1 mark each, with a total of 100 marks.
