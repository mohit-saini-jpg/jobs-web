Karnatak Science College Admission Result 2026 has been officially declared by Karnatak Science College Dharwad on May 28, 2026. The BCA I Year Third and Final Provisional Selection List for the academic session 2026-27 is now available for download on the official website www.kscd.ac.in. Students who applied for BCA first year admission through the Google Form can check their names in the merit list and complete the document verification process.

Selected candidates must report to the college within three days with all required documents and pay the prescribed fees to confirm their admission. This final selection list has been prepared based on merit-cum-reservation policy for 140 BCA seats (60 Non-Fee and 80 Extra Fee seats) following Karnatak University guidelines.

How to Check Selection List

Students can check their selection status by visiting the official website or through the direct result link:

  • Visit official website: www.kscd.ac.in
  • Click on "BCA First Year Third and Final Provisional Selection List 2026-27"
  • Download the PDF file
  • Search for your name or application number
  • Check your category and merit position

The result is also displayed on the college notice board.

Admission Timeline for 2026-27

The admission process was conducted in multiple rounds this year:

First Round

  • Application Submission: April 20 to April 30, 2026
  • Merit List Preparation: May 1 to May 3, 2026
  • Selection List Publication: May 4, 2026 (11:00 AM)
  • Admission Acceptance: May 5 to May 7, 2026

Second Round

  • Merit List Preparation: May 8 to May 10, 2026
  • Selection List Publication: May 11, 2026 (11:00 AM)
  • Admission Acceptance: May 11 to May 13, 2026

Final Round

  • Counselling for Remaining Seats: May 15, 2026
  • Third & Final List: May 28, 2026

Documents Required for Admission

Selected candidates must bring these documents for verification:

  • Computer-generated II PUC marks sheet (attested by PU College Principal)
  • SSLC marks card
  • Income and caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhar card
  • NCC/NSS/Sports/Cultural/Ex-serviceman/Specially-abled certificates (if applicable)
  • Other relevant documents as required
