Tilka Manjhi Bhagalpur University (TMBU) Admission 2026-27 for Undergraduate CBCS programmes (Session 2026-2030) is now open. Online applications started on 5 May 2026 and the last date to apply is 19 May 2026.

Tilka Manjhi Bhagalpur University (TMBU) Admission 2026-27 has officially begun for 4-year undergraduate BA, B.Sc, and B.Com (Honours & General) courses under the CBCS semester system. Students seeking admission in Bihar’s reputed university can now apply online through the Samarth portal till 19 May 2026.

The entire admission process is merit-based on Class 12th marks. The university will release multiple merit lists, followed by on-spot admission for vacant seats. This is a golden opportunity for fresh 10+2 pass-outs to secure seats in one of the top universities of Bihar. Candidates are advised to apply early, fill the form carefully, and keep all required documents ready to avoid last-minute issues. (98 words)

Application Fee

The application fee is payable online. The exact amount is displayed on the portal during form submission (generally ₹200–₹500 depending on category).

How to Apply for TMBU Bhagalpur University Admission 2026-27 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official portal: https://bhagalpuradm.samarth.edu.in/
  • Click on “New Registration” and register with a valid mobile number and email.
  • Fill the online application form carefully.
  • Upload required documents (Photo, Signature, Marksheets, Category Certificate).
  • Pay the application fee and submit the form.
  • Take a printout of the confirmation page.
  • Make corrections (if needed) by 21 May 2026.

Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th Marksheet and Certificate
  • Passport-size photograph & signature
  • Aadhaar Card / Photo ID
  • Caste / Category / EWS Certificate (if applicable)
  • Migration Certificate (if required)
