How to Download Panjab University Chandigarh Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download Panjab University Chandigarh Result 2026 for B.Com LLB, B.Com (FYUP) and LLM 1st Semester exams:

  • Visit the official Panjab University exam result website at https://results.puexam.in/.
  • On the home page, click on the relevant link for "B.Com. LL.B. (Hons) 5 Year Integrated Course 1st Semester", "Bachelor of Commerce (FYUP) 1st Semester" or "Master of Laws (LLM 2 Year Course at UILS) 1st Semester" December 2025 result.
  • The course specific result page will open in a new window.
  • Enter your Roll Number and any other details asked on the result page correctly.
  • Click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button.
  • Your Panjab University Chandigarh Result 2026 for the selected course and semester will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future reference, counselling or document verification.
