Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is NHIDCL Deputy Manager Technical Recruitment 2026?

NHIDCL Deputy Manager Technical Recruitment 2026 is a direct recruitment drive by National Highways and Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited to fill 85 Deputy Manager (Technical Cadre) posts in E2 Grade under the Ministry of Road Transport & Highways, Government of India.

Q2. How many vacancies are released for NHIDCL Deputy Manager (Technical) 2026?

NHIDCL has notified a total of 85 vacancies for the post of Deputy Manager (Technical Cadre), including category‑wise seats for UR, SC, ST, OBC, EWS and 2 posts horizontally reserved for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwBD).

Q3. What is the eligibility criteria for NHIDCL Deputy Manager Technical Recruitment 2026?

To be eligible, candidates must hold a Degree in Civil Engineering from a recognized University/Institute and possess a valid GATE score in Civil Engineering from any of the years 2024, 2025, or 2026, in addition to fulfilling age limit and nationality conditions mentioned in the official notification.

Q4. Is GATE mandatory for NHIDCL Deputy Manager (Technical) 2026?

Yes, a valid GATE score in Civil Engineering for any of the years 2024, 2025 or 2026 is mandatory for NHIDCL Deputy Manager (Technical) Recruitment 2026, and candidates without a valid GATE (CE) score are not eligible to apply.

Q5. What is the age limit for NHIDCL Deputy Manager Technical posts?

The maximum age limit is 34 years as on 08 June 2026 for General and EWS category candidates, with relaxations in upper age limit for OBC (NCL), SC, ST, PwBD and Ex‑Servicemen as per Government of India rules specified in the recruitment notice.

Q6. What is the salary or pay scale for NHIDCL Deputy Manager (Technical) 2026?

Selected candidates will be placed in the IDA pay scale of Rs. 50,000‑3%‑1,60,000 in E2 Grade, along with applicable allowances and benefits as per NHIDCL and CPSE rules.

Q7. What is the selection process for NHIDCL Deputy Manager Technical Recruitment 2026?

The selection process is purely based on the best valid GATE score in Civil Engineering obtained in 2024, 2025 or 2026, with no separate written examination, and a merit list will be prepared by NHIDCL based on these GATE scores following the cadre rules.

Q8. What are the important dates for NHIDCL Deputy Manager (Technical) 2026?

As per Recruitment Notice No. 05/2026, the online application portal opens on 09 May 2026 at 10:00 AM and closes on 08 June 2026 up to 5:00 PM, while the crucial date for age and eligibility calculation is also 08 June 2026.
