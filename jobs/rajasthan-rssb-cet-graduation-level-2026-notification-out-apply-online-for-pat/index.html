RSSB CET Graduate Level Eligibility Criteria 2026

Candidates often ask what qualification is needed for RSSB CET Graduate Level Recruitment 2026 - the answer is a graduation degree or its equivalent, as per the Personnel Department notification dated 23.05.2022 and 13.10.2022. Applicants who are appearing in the final year of their graduation can also apply, but they must acquire the qualification as required by the respective service rules before final selection. Specific educational qualification, age, and experience requirements for each post will apply as per the service rules governing recruitment to that particular post. The minimum age required is 18 years and the maximum age limit is 40 years , calculated as on 01 January 2027 . Age relaxation is available for reserved categories as detailed below. RSSB CET Graduate Level Selection Process 2026

Unlike a direct recruitment exam, the RSSB CET Graduate Level 2026 works as a single qualifying eligibility test valid for multiple posts. Simply appearing in and scoring in the CET does not guarantee a job - candidates must separately qualify in the main recruitment exam conducted by the respective recruiting agency for the specific post they wish to join. Step 1: Online application submission (04 July 2026 – 02 August 2026) Step 2: Admit card release via SSO Portal / Recruitment Portal Step 3: CET Graduate Level exam (01–03 December 2026) Step 4: Answer key publication and objection window (fee ₹100 per objection) Step 5: Result and score declaration on SSO ID and official website Step 6: Score valid for 3 years; candidates apply for main exams of desired services separately Step 7: Main recruitment exam, document verification, and final selection as per respective service rules Candid

RSSB CET Graduate Level Exam Pattern and Syllabus 2026

The RSSB CET Graduate Level 2026 exam consists of objective-type multiple-choice questions covering History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Science and Technology, Reasoning, Mathematics, Current Affairs, Computer Knowledge, and General Hindi and English. All questions carry equal marks, and there is negative marking for wrong answers. Note: If a candidate does not darken any of the five circles for more than 10% of the questions, they will be declared disqualified. For unmarked questions within the 10% limit, 1/3 mark is deducted as if the answer were wrong.

RSSB CET Graduate Level Document and Photo Upload Guidelines

The RSSB CET Graduate Level 2026 application requires candidates to upload a live photograph, signature, and thumb impression as per strict formatting rules. The photo must have a white or light-colored background, cover at least 50% of the frame, and must not include spectacles with glare, caps, or dark glasses. Signature and thumb impression must be scanned in JPEG format only. Signature file size should be between 20 KB and 50 KB. Photos or signatures captured via mobile phone camera are not accepted. Left thumb impression is mandatory (right thumb if left is unavailable).
