Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies announced by ICMR NIHR?

ICMR NIHR has announced a total of 12 vacancies for Project Human Resource Positions.

Q2. What is the application mode for this recruitment?

The application mode for this recruitment is Walk-in Interview.

Q3. What is the salary range for the Project Human Resource Positions?

The salary ranges from Rs. 35,000 to Rs. 80,000 Per Month, depending on the post.

Q4. Which posts are available in this recruitment drive?

The available posts include Junior Consultant (Medical), Young Professional-I (Public Health), Young Professional-II (IT), Young Professional-I (Admin), and Young Professional-I (Accounts).

Q5. What are the minimum qualifications required for the Young Professional-I (Public Health) post?

A Post Graduate degree in Life Science Subjects or Public Health is required for the Young Professional-I (Public Health) post.

Q6. Is there any application fee for ICMR NIHR recruitment?

No, there is no application fee for General/OBC and SC/ST categories.

Q7. What is the selection process for these positions?

The selection process involves shortlisting based on qualification and experience, followed by an interview and document verification.

Q8. Where is the walk-in interview scheduled to be held?

The walk-in interviews will be held at ICMR National Institute of Health Research, Bhubaneswar, Odisha.
