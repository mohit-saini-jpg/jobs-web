FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in TGPSC DEO / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 24 vacancies have been notified for Deputy Educational Officers / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I posts under TGPSC Notification No. 03/G/SE/2026. These are distributed across Multi Zone-I (10 posts) and Multi Zone-II (14 posts) of the School Education Department, Telangana.
Q2What is the last date to apply for TGPSC DEO / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for TGPSC DEO / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I Recruitment 2026 is 19/07/2026 up to 5:00 PM. Candidates are advised not to wait till the last date and submit their applications well in advance.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for TGPSC DEO / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must hold a Bachelor’s Degree from a UGC-recognised University with subjects eligible for B.Ed. admission, AND a Post Graduate Degree with minimum 55% marks from a UGC-recognised University. For SC / ST / BC / Differently Abled candidates, the minimum percentage is relaxed to 50%.
Q4What is the age limit for TGPSC DEO / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 44 years as on 01/07/2026. The upper age limit has been raised by 10 years (from 34 to 44 years) as per G.O.Ms.No.86, G.A.(Ser.A) Dept., Dt. 18/05/2026. Age relaxation of 5 years is available for SC/ST/BC/EWS, 10 years for PH, and up to 5 years for Telangana State Government Employees.
Q5What is the pay scale offered for TGPSC DEO / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I posts?
A
Selected candidates will be placed in the pay scale of Rs. 58,850 – 1,37,050/- (Cadre/Level 10 to 14 as per the 7th Pay Commission), along with applicable Dearness Allowance and other allowances as per Telangana Government rules.
Q6How to apply for TGPSC DEO / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online only through the official TGPSC website at https://www.tgpsc.gov.in. First-time applicants must register through One Time Registration (OTR) to obtain a TGPSC ID. Existing users can directly login with their TGPSC ID and Date of Birth, fill the application form, pay the fee online, and download the PDF application before 19/07/2026 up to 5:00 PM.
Q7What is the application fee for TGPSC DEO / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I Recruitment 2026?
A
The application processing fee is Rs. 1,000/- for OC & BC candidates and Rs. 500/- for SC, ST, and PwD candidates. An additional examination fee of Rs. 120/- is applicable for all employed candidates. Unemployed candidates are exempted from the examination fee. Both fees are non-refundable.
Q8What is the selection process for TGPSC DEO / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection will be based on a Written Examination (Objective Type) conducted in CBRT or OMR mode, consisting of two papers totalling 450 marks. Candidates must secure qualifying marks (40% for OC/EWS/Sports, 35% for BC, 30% for SC/ST/PH). Final selection will be based on merit rank in the merit list against vacancies available zone-wise. Appearance in all papers is compulsory.
Q9When will the TGPSC DEO / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I Recruitment 2026 examination be held?
A
The examination is tentatively scheduled for October 2026. The exact date will be notified separately by TGPSC on its official website https://www.tgpsc.gov.in. Hall Tickets will be available for download 7 days prior to the examination date. No individual communication will be sent.
Q10Where can I download the official notification for TGPSC DEO / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I Recruitment 2026?
A
The official notification PDF for TGPSC DEO / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I Recruitment 2026 (Notification No. 03/G/SE/2026, Dated: 05/06/2026) can be downloaded from the official TGPSC website at https://www.tgpsc.gov.in or directly through the Important Links section provided above in this article.
