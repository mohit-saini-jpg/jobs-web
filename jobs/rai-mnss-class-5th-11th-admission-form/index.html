Eligibility & Admission Process

  • Admission to Class V is based on PESAT, written test, merit and medical fitness. The PESAT includes 30 m flying start, standing broad jump, medicine ball throw, shuttle run, forward bend and reach, SV jump and 800 m run. Students who qualify PESAT appear for the written test in Hindi, English, Mathematics and G.K. OSP candidates applying for Classes VI, VIII, IX and XI must be medal winners at competitions such as Khelo India, National School Games or Khelo Haryana and will undergo only a sports-specific test based on the discipline offered by the school.

School Fee Structure

  • Haryana girls from Class V to XII have no tuition fee and pay ₹30,500 annually for boarding, lodging and stationery. Haryana boys have no tuition fee in Classes V to VIII, ₹6000 in Classes IX to X and ₹9000 in Classes XI to XII, along with ₹30,500 for boarding and lodging. Non-Haryana students pay tuition fees between ₹40,000 and ₹50,000 depending on the class, plus ₹30,500 boarding charges. A security deposit of ₹50,000 and clothing charges are also applicable at the time of admission.
