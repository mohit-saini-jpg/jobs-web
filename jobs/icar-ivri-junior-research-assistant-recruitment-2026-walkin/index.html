Job Overview

The Indian Veterinary Research Institute (ICAR IVRI) has released the official Junior Research Assistant Notification 2026. A total of 1 vacancy has been announced under the ICAR IVRI Junior Research Assistant Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates up to 28 years of age with qualifications such as M.Sc. (Agri), M.Sc., M.Pharm., MVSc., B.E./B.Tech., or equivalent degree can attend the walk-in interview on 05 June 2026. The official notification PDF and application details are available below.

Qualification / Eligibility

Junior Research Assistant: M.Sc. (Agri)., M.Sc., M.Pharm., MVSc., Bachelor of Engg/Technology (B.E./B.Tech.) or equivalent degree with 1st class in graduate and post-graduate examination. Experience in tissue engineering, cell culture techniques, Molecular Biology tools/ Bioinformatics, working experience with lab animals, and National Eligibility Tests- CSIR/UGC/ICAR NET including lectureship (assistant professorship) or GATE is desirable.
