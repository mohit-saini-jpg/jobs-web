Age Limit and Nature of Appointment

This section covers who is eligible by age and what kind of appointment SMVDSB is offering for these 105 posts. Candidates must have attained 18 years of age and must not be more than 40 years old to be eligible, except for ex-servicemen, for whom the maximum age limit is 45 years . The appointment will be on a "Term Employment" basis , initially for a period of one year , which may be extended further subject to satisfactory performance, and shall be governed by the service conditions prescribed by the Shrine Board.

Application Fee and Payment Mode

This section explains how much candidates must pay and the exact mode of payment accepted for SMVDSB Recruitment 2026. The application fee is ₹200/- per post for every post listed in this notice, and it must be paid through a Demand Draft in original , drawn in favour of the FA/Chief Accounts Officer, SMVDSB, Katra . There is no online payment option mentioned in the notice; the Demand Draft must be enclosed along with the physical application form.
