Notification DatePost Date: 01/04/2026

Odisha Adarsha Vidyalaya (OAV) Puri has announced the OAV Puri Result 2026 for admission/selection to Classes VI, VII, VIII and IX for various OAVs located in Astaranga, Brahmagiri, Delang, Gop, Kanasa, Krushnaprasad, Nimapara, Pipili, Purisadar, Satyabadi and Kakatpur under Puri district. OAV Puri Result 2026 has been released online on the official district website - puri.odisha.gov.in - in the form of separate PDF selection lists for each school and class.

Students and parents can check the OAV Puri selection list by opening the respective PDF for their OAV and class (for example, OAV Astaranga Class VI/VII/VIII, OAV Gop Class VI-IX etc.) and searching for the candidate’s name or roll number. After downloading the result PDF, they should carefully verify the candidate details, allotted school and class and follow the instructions given by the OAV/district office regarding document verification, admission dates and further formalities.

How to Check OAV Puri Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download the OAV Puri Result 2026 selection lists:

  • Visit the official notice page: https://puri.odisha.gov.in/en/notice/recruitment.
  • In the list of notices, locate the links for “OAV [School Name] CLASS-[VI/VII/VIII/IX].pdf” corresponding to your OAV and class (for example, OAV Astaranga Class-VI, OAV Gop Class-IX, OAV Nimapara Class-VIII).
  • Click on the required link to open the PDF in your browser.
  • Use the search option (Ctrl + F) and type the candidate’s name or roll number to quickly find it in the list.
  • Confirm the candidate’s details, category and whether they are selected/admitted for the concerned OAV and class.
  • Download or print the PDF for admission counselling, document verification and future reference.
