How to Download CG Vyapam Pre D.El.Ed Answer Key 2026?

This section provides a simple, step-by-step guide for downloading the answer key directly from the official CG Vyapam website.

Candidates can follow these steps to download the CG Pre D.El.Ed Answer Key 2026:

  • Visit the official CG Vyapam website: vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, locate the section titled "Model Answer".
  • Click on the link labelled "CG Pre D.El.Ed Answer Key 2026".
  • The answer key PDF will open in a new tab download and save it to your device.
  • Match your recorded responses (from your OMR sheet or response sheet) with the correct answers given in the PDF to estimate your score.

How to Raise Objection Against CG Pre D.El.Ed Answer Key 2026

This section explains the complete objection/challenge process, including the timeline, login requirements, and fee structure for candidates who wish to dispute any answer in the provisional key.

If a candidate believes that any answer given in the provisional CG Vyapam Pre D.El.Ed Answer Key 2026 is incorrect, they can raise an objection during the designated challenge window from 12th to 17th June 2026. The board requires candidates to log in to their Vyapam profile and submit objections along with supporting proof and the prescribed fee.

Steps to raise an objection:

  • Visit the official CG Vyapam portal: vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in (objections are submitted via the Vyapam profile login portal).
  • Log in using your registered mobile number and password.
  • Go to the "View Provisional Answer Key" or "Objection" tab.
  • Enter your application number, date of birth, and security code as required.
  • Select the question(s) you wish to challenge and provide valid supporting proof/justification for your claimed answer.
  • Pay the objection fee of Rs. 50 per question.
  • Submit the objection before the window closes on 17th June 2026.
