LNMU PG Merit List 2026 - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 17/06/2026

Lalit Narayan Mithila University (LNMU), Darbhanga has published the PG First Merit List 2026 (also called the first selection list) for admission to the Master's Degree programmes - M.A., M.Sc., and M.Com - 1st Semester, Session 2026-28. According to the university's official revised admission schedule, the list was released on June 13, 2026, and candidates can now log in to the admission portal at lnmuadm.samarth.edu.in with their registered email ID and password to check their selection status. Candidates whose names appear on the list must complete admission formalities at their allotted university department or PG teaching college between June 15 and June 25, 2026, after which classes are scheduled to begin on July 1, 2026.

What Is the LNMU PG First Merit List 2026?

The LNMU PG First Merit List 2026 is the first provisional selection list prepared by Lalit Narayan Mithila University for candidates who applied for postgraduate admission through the Samarth portal. It is not a final admission confirmation - it only shortlists candidates who are provisionally eligible to take a seat in a specific subject and college.

A few key points to understand about this list:

  • Admission to LNMU's PG programmes (M.A., M.Sc., M.Com) is merit-based, calculated from marks scored in the qualifying graduation examination - there is no separate entrance test.
  • The selection process is centralised, covering both university departments and affiliated PG teaching colleges under LNMU.
  • Being named on the merit list only secures a provisional seat; admission is finalised only after the candidate reports to the allotted college, submits documents, and pays the requisite fee within the deadline.

How to Check and Download the LNMU PG First Merit List 2026

Candidates often search for "how to check my name in LNMU merit list" - here is the exact process. The merit list is published on the official admission portal and can only be accessed by logging in with the credentials created at the time of application; it is not released as a public PDF you can browse without logging in.

Steps to Check Your LNMU PG Selection Status Online

  • Open the official LNMU admission portal: lnmuadm.samarth.edu.in.
  • Click on Login on the homepage.
  • Enter your registered email ID and password created during application.
  • Complete the captcha verification and submit.
  • Once logged in, go to your application dashboard to view your selection/allotment status, including the subject and college allotted, if selected.

If you have forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it through your registered email.

Details Mentioned in the LNMU PG Merit List 2026

Once you access your selection status, the system typically displays the core information used to confirm your identity and allotment. This generally includes:

  • Candidate's name and application number
  • Subject applied for and subject allotted
  • Marks obtained in the qualifying examination
  • Name of the allotted university department or PG teaching college
  • Admission/reporting instructions for the allotted seat

Always cross-check these details carefully against your application form before proceeding to the admission stage, since any mismatch could delay your admission.

What to Do After Your Name Appears in the Merit List

Appearing in the LNMU PG First Merit List 2026 is only the first step - final admission requires you to act within the stipulated window. Here's what selected candidates need to do.

  • Report to the allotted college or department within the admission window of June 15 to June 25, 2026.
  • Carry original and photocopies of required documents, such as your graduation mark sheets and certificates, category/caste certificate (if applicable), EWS or income certificate (if applicable), and recent photographs, along with your signed application printout. LNMU has issued a separate official notice titled "PG Admission Guidelines & Required Documents (Session 2026-28)," so candidates should verify the complete, exact document checklist from that notice on the admission portal before reporting.
  • Pay the prescribed admission/programme fee as communicated by the allotted college.
  • Candidates who fail to report or complete formalities within the deadline risk having their provisional seat cancelled and reallotted to another applicant in the next round.

Will LNMU Release a Second PG Merit List in 2026?

Many applicants who did not find their name in the first list want to know if another chance is coming. According to LNMU's official schedule, the university will publish a subsequent selection list strictly based on the availability of vacant seats after the first round of admissions concludes. No fixed date has been announced for this round yet, since it depends on how many seats remain unfilled after the first list. Candidates are advised to keep checking the Public Notice section of lnmuadm.samarth.edu.in for updates on the next round.

Eligibility and Application Snapshot for LNMU PG Admission 2026-28

For readers who are following the merit list but want a quick recap of the admission process itself, here's a summary based on LNMU's official notices.

  • The admission is for M.A., M.Sc., and M.Com 1st Semester programmes, Session 2026-28.
  • Selection is based purely on graduation marks; there is no written entrance examination.
  • Applications were accepted only online through the Samarth-based admission portal, lnmuadm.samarth.edu.in.
  • The process covers seats in LNMU University Departments as well as affiliated PG teaching colleges.
