Samagra Shiksha Gujarat (SSA Gujarat) has released the Gyan Sahayak Primary Recruitment 2026-27 Merit List for Navsari district, Gujarati medium, on its official portal pregyansahayak.ssgujarat.org. This is a provisional, district-wise and subject-wise merit list for candidates who applied for the Gyan Sahayak (Primary) contractual teaching posts. The Navsari Gujarati medium list covers four subject categories: Std 1 to 5, Gujarati Language, Maths & Science, and Social Science.   • The PDF will open or download automatically.
  • Use Ctrl+F (or the search option in your PDF viewer) to search your name or TET number directly.
