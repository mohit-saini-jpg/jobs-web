How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official Presidency University website at https://www.presiuniv.ac.in/web/webannoncement030620261.php and click on the "Apply Online" link.
  • 2Register with a valid email address and mobile number. Note your login credentials immediately.
  • 3Fill in the online application form with correct personal, educational, and experience details.
  • 4Upload all required documents as specified in the application form.
  • 5Submit the online application before 01 July 2026 by 5:30 PM. Applications submitted after this time will not be accepted.
  • 6Candidates in employment must ensure their application is routed through the proper channel and arrange for an NOC before appearing before the Selection Committee.
  • 7Take a printout of the submitted application form for your records. At the time of interview (if shortlisted), photocopies of all testimonials and certificates must be submitted, and originals must be brought for verification.
  • 8Ph.D. degree certificate from a recognized University/Institute
  • 9Master's degree and Graduate degree mark sheets and certificates
  • 10Research publications list with evidence of publications in peer-reviewed or UGC-listed journals
  • 11Research score calculation as per Appendix I, Table 1 of the notification
  • 12Evidence of successful guidance of doctoral candidates
  • 13Teaching/research experience certificates from all previous and current employers
  • 14SC/ST/OBC/PH category certificate issued by the competent authority of the Government of West Bengal (OBC certificates must be issued/revalidated not before 18 May 2026)
  • 15No Objection Certificate from current employer (for candidates in employment)
  • 16Do not rely on your previous application: The notification explicitly states that all candidates who applied against earlier Presidency University advertisements must re-apply. Assuming your earlier application will be considered is the most common mistake.
  • 17Apply before the last day: University portals typically experience high traffic near the deadline of 01 July 2026. Submitting well before 5:30 PM on the last date is strongly advisable to avoid server errors or payment failures.
  • 18Ensure OBC certificate validity: OBC certificates must have been issued or revalidated on or after 18 May 2026. Older certificates will not be accepted for reservation benefit.
  • 19Do not count M.Phil / Ph.D. time as experience: The notification explicitly states that time spent acquiring M.Phil or Ph.D. cannot be claimed or counted as teaching or research experience. Ensure your experience calculation excludes this period.
  • 20Save your registration credentials: Note your registration number, user ID, and password immediately after registering. Recovery of login details on the last day can cost valuable time.
  • 21Incomplete applications will be rejected: Ensure all fields in the online form are filled and all required documents are uploaded before final submission. The University will not entertain correspondence regarding rejected incomplete applications.
  • 22Verify your registered email address: All communications - including shortlisting and interview calls - will be sent to the registered email and postal address only. Use an active, frequently monitored email ID.
  • 23In-person attendance is mandatory: Shortlisted candidates must physically appear before the Selection Committee. There are no exceptions and no provision for online/virtual interviews.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for Presidency University Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for the post of Professor at Presidency University, Kolkata is 01 July 2026 by 5:30 PM. Applications received after this deadline will not be considered under any circumstances.
Q2How many vacancies are available in Presidency University Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 44 Professor vacancies have been notified across 15 departments and institutes, including Physics, Chemistry, Geology, Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, Life Sciences, Bengali, English, Hindi, Political Science, History, Philosophy, Sociology, and the Institute of Health Sciences.
Q3What is the eligibility for Professor posts at Presidency University Kolkata?
A
Candidates must hold a Ph.D. in the concerned/allied discipline and satisfy one of two routes: (a) an eminent scholar with at least 10 research publications in peer-reviewed/UGC-listed journals, a research score of 120, and 10 years of teaching/research experience with evidence of doctoral guidance; OR (b) an outstanding professional from industry/academia with 10 years of experience and significant documented contribution to the field.
Q4Is there any application fee for Presidency University Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
The notification does not mention any application fee for this recruitment. Candidates should verify on the application portal at the time of filling the form.
Q5What is the salary for the Professor post at Presidency University, Kolkata?
A
The pay scale for the Professor post is Rs. 1,44,200/– to Rs. 2,18,200/- under Academic Level 14, along with other admissible allowances as per West Bengal State Government orders.
Q6What is the selection process for Presidency University Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection is based on shortlisting of applications by the respective screening committee, followed by a personal interview before the Selection Committee. There is no written examination. All shortlisted candidates must physically appear before the Selection Committee on the day of interview without exception.
Q7Do candidates who applied earlier need to re-apply?
A
Yes. The notification explicitly states that all applicants who applied against earlier advertisements must re-apply under Advertisement No. PU_RECR_AP_29-26. Previous applications will not be carried forward or considered.
Q8What is the validity requirement for OBC certificates in this recruitment?
A
OBC certificates must be issued or revalidated not before 18 May 2026. Only certificates issued by the competent authority of the Government of West Bengal will be accepted for reservation benefit.
Q9Can candidates in government service apply directly for Presidency University Professor posts?
A
Candidates already in employment must apply through the proper channel and must produce a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their current employer in order to appear before the Selection Committee.
Q10What is the probation period for selected candidates at Presidency University?
A
Appointments will be made on probation for a period of one year, which may be waived or extended in exceptional cases as per university rules. The services of a probationary incumbent may be terminated during or at the end of probation without any reasons being assigned.
