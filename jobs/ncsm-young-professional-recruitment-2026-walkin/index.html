FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview?
A
The walk-in interview for the Young Professional (Gallery Explainer) posts will be held on 10 June 2026. Candidates are advised to reach the venue well in advance.
Q2What is the educational qualification required for this post?
A
Candidates must possess an MA/M.Sc degree in Museology, Linguistics, or Anthropology with good knowledge of Linguistic Anthropology from a recognized university.
Q3What is the age limit for the Young Professional (Gallery Explainer) post?
A
The maximum age limit for this position is 35 years. Age relaxation may be applicable as per government norms.
Q4What is the salary offered for this role?
A
The selected candidates will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 35,000 per month.
Q5How many vacancies are available?
A
There are a total of 2 vacancies for the Young Professional (Gallery Explainer) position.
Q6What is the application fee for this recruitment?
A
There is no application fee for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment.
Q7What is the selection process for this recruitment?
A
The selection process will be based on a Walk-in Test/Interview.
Q8Where is the walk-in interview scheduled to be held?
A
The walk-in interview will be conducted at the NCSM office in Kolkata, West Bengal, PIN 700091.
Q9What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents along with one set of self-attested photocopies, including ID proof, educational certificates, and any other relevant documents.
Q10Is this a permanent or contractual job?
A
This is a contractual job opportunity with the National Council of Science Museums (NCSM).
