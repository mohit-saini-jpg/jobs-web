How to Apply

  • 1Duly filled and signed Bio Data Proforma (typed in Double Space on single side) as per the format prescribed in the notification
  • 2Attested copies of ACR/APAR for the last five years
  • 3Certificate furnished by the Head of Office confirming: (a) service particulars verified and correct; (b) no vigilance enquiry/disciplinary proceeding pending or contemplated; (c) the officer will be relieved of duties on selection
  • 4Supporting documents in proof of educational qualifications (degree certificates, mark sheets)
  • 5Any other relevant document in support of eligibility for the specific post applied for
  • 6The period of deputation, including deputation in another ex-cadre post held immediately preceding this appointment in the same or another organization/department of the Central Government, shall ordinarily not exceed five years in total.
  • 7Employees of the Central Administrative Tribunal are eligible for and may continue to retain General Pool Residential Accommodation as per extant rules.
  • 8The number of vacancies may increase or decrease during the final recruitment process. The selected officer/official may be posted at the Bench where the vacancy would be available at the relevant point of time.
  • 9Applications must be forwarded through proper channel only. The department, while forwarding applications, must ensure that the officer/official who volunteers for the post shall not be allowed to withdraw his/her name later.
  • 10A vigilance clearance certificate to the effect that the officer is clear from the vigilance point of view and that no disciplinary case is pending or contemplated must be furnished by the forwarding authority along with the application.
  • 11Applications received after the expiry of the last date, or incomplete applications (want of ACR/APAR, vigilance clearance certificate, or without the official seal), will not be entertained under any circumstance.
  • 12CAT will not be in a position to return any documents provided by the applicant or the parent office, irrespective of whether the candidate is selected or not.
  • 13Candidates must declare in the Proforma (Point 14) that they understand they shall not be allowed to withdraw their application after selection.
