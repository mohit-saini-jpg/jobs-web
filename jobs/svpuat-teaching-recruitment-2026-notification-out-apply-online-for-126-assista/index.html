Eligibility Criteria Professor (Sr.No.A1, A2, B1 and E1): Ph.D. degree in the concerned/relevant discipline and published work of high quality, actively engaged in research with evidence of publication work with, a minimum of 10 research publications in the peer-reviewed, NAAS rated or UGC-listed journal and a total API score of 120. Minimum of ten years of teaching experience in university/college as Assistant Professor*/Associate Professor and/or research experience at equivalent level at the University/National Level Institutions with evidence of having successfully guided doctoral candidate. Professor (S.No. F1 to F5): A recognized basic veterinary qualification included in the schedules I/II of Indian Veterinary Council Act; 1984 & must be registered with a State Veterinary Council/Veterinary Council of India. An eminent scholar having a Ph.D. degree in the concerned/relevant discipline and published work of high quality, actively engaged in research with evidence of publication work with, a minimum of 10 research publications in the peer-reviewed, NAAS rated or UGC-listed journal and a total API score of 120. A minimum of ten years of teaching experience in university/college as Assistant Professor*/Associate Professor and/or research experience at equivalent level at the University/National Level Institutions with evidence of having successfully guided doctoral candidate. Associate Professor (Sr. No. A3, B2, B3 and D1): A good academic record, with a Ph.D. Degree in the concerned/allied/relevant disciplines. A Master's Degree with at least 55% marks. A minimum of eight years of experience of teaching and/or research in an academic/research position equivalent to that of Assistant Professor in a University, College or Accredited Research Institution/industry with a minimum of seven publications in the peer-reviewed, NAAS rated or UGC-listed journals and a total research score of Seventy five (75). Associate Professor (Sr. No. C1): Ph.D. degree in relevant field and First class or equivalent at either Bachelor's or Master's Level in the relevant branch. At least total 6 research publications in SCI journals/UGC/AICTE approved list of journals. Minimum of 8 years of experience in teaching/research/industry out of which at least 2 years shall be Post Ph.D. experience and a total research score of Seventy-five (75). Assistant Professor (Sr. No. F6): A recognized basic veterinary qualification included in the schedules I/II of Indian Veterinary Council Act; 1984 & must be registered with a State Veterinary Council/Veterinary Council of India.
