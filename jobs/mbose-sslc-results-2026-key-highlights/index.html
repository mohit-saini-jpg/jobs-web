How to Download MBOSE SSLC Result 2026

Follow these steps to check your MBOSE SSLC Result 2026 once it is declared tomorrow at 11:00 AM:

  • Visit the official Meghalaya Board website https://www.mbose.in/ at or after 11:00 AM on 07-04-2026.
  • Look for the SSLC Examination 2026 Result link or result booklet download link on the homepage/notice section.
  • Alternatively, open mboseresults.in or megresults.nic.in if the main site is slow due to heavy traffic.
  • On the individual result portal, enter your Roll Number and other required details in the result login page, if such interface is provided.
  • Submit the details to view your subject wise marks, total marks and result status (Pass/Fail etc.).
  • If accessing the full result booklet PDF from mbose.in, open the booklet, search for your roll number and check your marks as printed.
  • Download or print the online result page or booklet entry for future reference, admission or scholarship purposes.
