Eligibility Criteria & Job Requirements

Core Education: Must have passed the Intermediate (Class 12) examination from the Board of High School and Intermediate Education, Uttar Pradesh, or any equivalent examination recognized by the Government. Typing Speed: Minimum typing speed of 25 words per minute in Hindi and 30 words per minute in English is mandatory. Computer Certificate: A CCC (Course on Computer Concepts) certificate issued by DOEACC Society (now NIELIT) or an equivalent certificate from any government-recognized institution is required. Candidates who opted for Computer Science as a separate subject in High School or Intermediate examination (UP Board or any Central/State Government Board) are also eligible. Candidates who have obtained a Diploma or Degree in Computer Science from a recognized institution are also eligible for recruitment.

Age Limit & Relaxations

Age Limit: Minimum 18 years and Maximum 40 years as on July 1, 2026. Age Relaxation: Note: Only permanent residents (Domicile) of Uttar Pradesh are eligible for reserved posts (both horizontal and vertical reservation). Persons dismissed from Union or State Government service are not eligible for appointment.

GIMS Selection Methodology

Stage 1 - Computer Based Test (CBT): An objective-type MCQ exam of 100 marks divided into 5 sections for a total duration of 120 minutes (2 hours) . Sections cover Hindi language proficiency & writing ability, general intelligence, general knowledge, computer knowledge, and UP-specific general knowledge. Candidates cannot go back to a previous section once it is submitted. Negative Marking: ¼ mark deducted for every wrong answer. No negative marking for unanswered questions. Stage 2 - Typing Test: Candidates equal to 3 times the number of vacancies will be shortlisted from the CBT merit list and called for a typing test. A minimum speed of 25 wpm (Hindi) and 30 wpm (English) is required. This test is qualifying in nature. Final Merit List: Prepared on the basis of CBT marks for candidates who qualify the typing test. Tie-Breaking: In case of equal marks - total CBT marks → date of birth

GIMS Non-Teaching Clerical Cadre Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the total number of vacancies?
A
There are a total of 33 vacancies - 15 for Record Keeper and 18 for Store Keeper.
Q2What is the last date to apply?
A
The last date to submit the online application is July 3, 2026. Fee payment deadline is as per the exam schedule published on the website.
Q3Can I apply for both Record Keeper and Store Keeper posts?
A
Yes, but you must submit separate applications and separate fees for each post.
Q4Is typing test mandatory for selection?
A
Yes. Candidates shortlisted from CBT (3x vacancies) will be called for a typing test, which is qualifying in nature. Only those who pass the typing test are included in the final merit list.
Q5Is there negative marking in the CBT?
A
Yes. ¼ mark is deducted for every wrong answer. No penalty for unanswered questions.
Q6Are candidates from other states eligible for reserved category benefits?
A
No. Reservation benefits (both vertical and horizontal) are available only to permanent residents (Domicile) of Uttar Pradesh. Candidates from other states will be treated as Unreserved (UR) candidates.
Q7What is the application fee for Divyang candidates?
A
Divyang (PwD) candidates who are permanent residents of Uttar Pradesh are fully exempted from paying the application fee.
Q8Can I modify my application after submission?
A
Yes, but only once. However, the registered mobile number, email ID, and reservation category cannot be modified under any circumstances.
Q9Will TA/DA be provided for the exam or document verification?
A
No. No TA/DA is admissible for attending the written test or document verification.
Q10Under which jurisdiction do disputes fall?
A
All disputes, differences, or lawsuits will be under the jurisdiction of Gautam Buddha Nagar courts only.
