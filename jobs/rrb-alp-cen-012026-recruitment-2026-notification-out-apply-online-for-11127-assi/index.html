Age Limit

DetailsCandidates must be between 18 and 30 years of age as on 01-07-2026. Age relaxation is available for various categories as detailed below. If a candidate is eligible for relaxation on more than one ground, only the maximum relaxation will be granted (not cumulative). SC, ST, and OBC (NCL) candidates applying against UR vacancies will not get any age relaxation. There is no fixed limit on the number of attempts - candidates can appear as long as they are within the prescribed upper age limit.

How to Apply

  1. 1Applications must be submitted online only through the official websites of participating RRBs (listed at Para 13.7 of the CEN). Hard copy / offline applications will not be accepted under any circumstances. The online portal is active from 15-05-2026 to 14-06-2026 (23:59 Hrs.). Candidates must first create an account on the RRB portal. Those who created an account for CENs notified in 2024/2025 should use the same credentials. Details filled in the "Create an Account" form (including mobile number, email ID) and the chosen RRB cannot be modified at any stage after account creation. Candidates can apply to only one RRB and submit only one application - multiple applications to the same or different RRBs will result in rejection of all applications and debarment from future RRB/RRC exams. Aadhaar or DigiLocker authentication is strongly advised during account creation for a smoother recruitment process. A live photograph is captured during the online application; pre-existing photographs are not accepted. No documents need to be uploaded at application stage (except signature and SC/ST certificate for free travel pass, if applicable).
  2. 2Documents to be Produced at Document Verification (on being called):
  3. 3Age proof - Matriculation / SSLC / 10th class certificate
  4. 4Educational qualifications (Matriculation onwards) - marksheets and certificates
  5. 5ITI / Diploma / Degree certificate in prescribed trade / discipline
  6. 6SC / ST / OBC-NCL / EWS / Ex-Servicemen certificate (if applicable), in prescribed format and issued by competent authority
  7. 7Self Screening Medical Declaration Report (Annexure VIII A & VIII B) - to be submitted on CBAT day
  8. 8NOC from present employer (if applicable, for Government servants)
  9. 9Candidates are advised to regularly visit the official RRB website for their chosen RRB for updates on all stages. Helpline for technical queries: Email: [email protected] | Phone: 0172-565-3333 and 9592001188 (10:00 AM to 5:00 PM on working days).
  10. 10Apply Online at: Visit the official website of your chosen RRB (see list below)

Important Instructions

Only one application per candidate is accepted - multiple applications to one or more RRBs will result in rejection of all applications and permanent debarment from RRB/RRC exams. Candidates awaiting results of their final prescribed qualification examination are NOT eligible to apply. Candidates who have undergone Lasik or any other corrective eye surgery are not eligible for the post of ALP. Selection of RRB, once submitted, is final and cannot be changed at any later date. Details filled in the "Create an Account" form (including mobile number, email ID) and chosen RRB cannot be modified at any stage after account creation. Examination fee is refundable (after deducting bank charges) only to candidates who appear in CBT-1; rejected or absent candidates are not eligible for refund. Refund will be credited only to the Aadhaar-seeded bank account; candidates must ensure their Aadhaar is linked to an active bank account. Mobile phones, electronic gadgets, watches, earphones, Bluetooth devices, calculators, books, pen, food items, and all other listed banned items are strictly prohibited inside the test centre. Examination centre, date, and shift allotted in the e-call letter are final - no requests for change will be entertained under any circumstances. Candidates must maintain identical signatures throughout all stages of the recruitment process. No BLOCK/CAPITAL/DISJOINTED letter signatures are acceptable. Free Sleeper Class Railway Pass is available for SC/ST candidates for CBT/CBAT/DV/Medical Examination stages (if opted in application). Candidates are advised to visit only official RRB websites and beware of fake websites, unscrupulous agents, and touts claiming to provide Railway jobs through influence or unfair means. RRBs never appoint agents or coaching centres for recruitment.
