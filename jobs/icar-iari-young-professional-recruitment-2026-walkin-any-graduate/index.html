Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for ICAR IARI Recruitment 2026?

The walk-in interview for the Young Professional (I) posts at ICAR IARI is scheduled to be held on 22nd June 2026. Candidates are advised to reach the venue well in advance.

Q2. What is the total number of vacancies available for Young Professional (I) posts?

There are a total of 3 vacancies for the Young Professional (I) position announced by ICAR Indian Agricultural Research Institute for this recruitment drive.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Young Professional (I) post?

For one post, a Graduation in Agriculture Science is essential. For another post, a Graduation in any stream is required. Desirable qualifications are also mentioned in the notification.

Q4. What is the age limit for the Young Professional (I) posts?

The minimum age limit is 21 years and the maximum age limit is 45 years. Age relaxation will be provided to reserved categories as per government rules.

Q5. What is the salary offered for the Young Professional (I) position?

The selected candidates will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 30,000/- per month for the Young Professional (I) role.

Q6. Is there any application fee for ICAR IARI Young Professional (I) Recruitment 2026?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.

Q7. What is the selection process for the Young Professional (I) posts?

The selection process will primarily involve an Interview and Document Verification. Candidates need to perform well in both stages to be considered for the position.

Q8. Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?

The walk-in interview will be conducted at ICAR Indian Agricultural Research Institute, New Delhi, Pincode: 110012.
