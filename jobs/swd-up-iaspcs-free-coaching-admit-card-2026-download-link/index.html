How to Download SWD UP IAS/PCS Free Coaching Admit Card 2026

Candidates need to log in to the SWD candidate portal using their registration credentials to access and download the admit card. The process is simple and takes only a few minutes.

  • Visit the official SWD admit card portal at here.
  • Enter your Enrollment Number or Registration Number.
  • Enter your Date of Birth (used as password) in the required format.
  • Fill in the captcha code, if displayed on screen.
  • Click on the submit/login button to access your admit card.
  • Download the admit card and take a clear printout for the exam day.

SWD UP IAS/PCS Free Coaching 2026: Eligibility Criteria

Before applying, candidates needed to meet specific category, domicile, education, and income requirements set by the SWD. This eligibility framework also determines who is permitted to sit for the exam.

  • Candidates must belong to the OBC, SC, or ST category.
  • Candidates must have Uttar Pradesh domicile or be originally from Uttar Pradesh.
  • Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in any stream.
  • Annual family income must be less than ₹6 lakh per annum.
  • Applicants must not have been previously enrolled in any similar free coaching institute and must not currently be engaged in any other coaching program.
  • Minimum age for IAS and UPPSC Pre coaching eligibility is 21 years, with age relaxation applicable as per SWD norms.
