How to Apply for Stella Maris College Admission 2026

  • Visit the official application portal:reg.stellamariscollege.edu.in
  • Register with valid email and mobile number.
  • Fill the online application form carefully.
  • Enter 12th marks (for UG) or UG marks (for PG).
  • Upload required documents.
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Submit and download the confirmation page.
