Rabindra Bharati University (RBU) has officially released the RBU Result 2026 for MA 1st and 3rd semester examinations 2025 in multiple postgraduate programmes including Museology and Conservation, Journalism and Mass Communication, Musicology, Rabindra Sangeet and Dance under the CBCS system. These results belong to the 2025 exam session and cover courses under the Faculty of Visual Arts, Faculty of Arts and Faculty of Fine Arts. RBU Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - rbu.ac.in.

Students can visit the examinations section, open the latest MA result PDFs for their respective subjects and check their roll number, name, and marks or grade as notified by the university. After downloading or viewing the provisional result, students should cross-check all details carefully and contact the university examination branch in case of any discrepancy or doubt regarding their result.

How to Download RBU Result 2026

Use the following steps to check and download your Rabindra Bharati University MA 1st and 3rd semester result:

  • Open the official RBU website in your browser: https://www.rbu.ac.in/

  • In the examinations/results area, locate the latest result notifications dated 13 April 2026 for: MA 1st Sem Exam 2025 in Museology and Conservation (CBCS) MA 3rd Sem Exam 2025 in Journalism and Mass Communication (CBCS) MA 3rd Sem Exam 2025 in Musicology (CBCS) MA 1st Sem Exam 2025 in Rabindra Sangeet (CBCS) MA 1st Sem Exam 2025 in Dance (CBCS)
