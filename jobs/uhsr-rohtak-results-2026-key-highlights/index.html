How to Download UHSR Rohtak Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download the UHSR Rohtak Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official website of UHSR Rohtak - https://uhsr.ac.in/
  • Click on the "Results" section available on the homepage.
  • Find and click on the relevant result link for B.Sc Medical Technology O.T. 2nd and 3rd Year.
  • The result PDF will open on the screen.
  • Search your roll number or name in the PDF.
  • Download and save the result for future reference.
