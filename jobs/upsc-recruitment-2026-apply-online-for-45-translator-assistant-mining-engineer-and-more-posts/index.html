Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for UPSC Advertisement No. 04/2026 Recruitment?

The last date to submit the online application form for UPSC Advertisement No. 04/2026 Recruitment is 29 May 2026 up to 06:00 PM. Candidates interested in applying for various Group ‘A’ and Group ‘B’ Gazetted posts must complete the online registration process before the deadline through the official UPSC ORA portal.

Q2. Which posts are available under UPSC Advertisement No. 04/2026?

UPSC has released vacancies for several prestigious posts including Assistant Library & Information Officer, Associate Professor, Assistant Professors in Engineering disciplines, Translator (Chinese & Tibetan), Senior Administrative Officer, Assistant Mining Engineer, Assistant Research Officer, and Accounts Officer. These vacancies are available under different Ministries and Departments of the Government of India.

Q3. What is the application mode for UPSC Recruitment 2026?

Candidates can apply only through online mode using the official UPSC Online Recruitment Application (ORA) portal. No offline application forms or other modes of submission will be accepted by the Union Public Service Commission. Applicants are advised to carefully fill the Common Application Form and upload all required documents before final submission

Q4. What educational qualifications are required for UPSC Advertisement No. 04/2026 posts?

Educational qualifications vary depending on the post applied for. Engineering faculty posts generally require B.E./B.Tech and M.E./M.Tech degrees in relevant disciplines, while Translator posts require proficiency in foreign languages like Chinese or Tibetan. Administrative and research-related posts require graduation along with relevant experience as specified in the official notification.

Q5. What is the age limit for UPSC Advertisement No. 04/2026 Recruitment?

The upper age limit differs according to the post and category. Most vacancies have age limits ranging between 30 and 45 years. Reserved category candidates including OBC, SC, ST, and PwBD applicants are eligible for age relaxation benefits as per Government of India and UPSC recruitment rules.

Q6. What is the selection process for UPSC Advertisement No. 04/2026?

The selection process may include shortlisting of candidates based on educational qualifications and experience, Recruitment Test (if conducted), and Personal Interview. Final merit will be prepared based on candidate performance in the interview and other recruitment stages conducted by UPSC

Q7. Is there any application fee exemption in UPSC Recruitment 2026?

Yes, fee exemption benefits are available for eligible categories such as SC, ST, PwBD, and Female candidates according to UPSC recruitment rules. The exact fee details and payment instructions will be available during the online application process on the UPSC ORA portal.

Q8. How can candidates apply for UPSC Advertisement No. 04/2026?

Candidates must first create a profile or URN on the UPSC ORA portal and complete the Common Application Form. After selecting the desired post, applicants need to upload scanned documents, photograph, signature, and pay the application fee if applicable before submitting the form online.
