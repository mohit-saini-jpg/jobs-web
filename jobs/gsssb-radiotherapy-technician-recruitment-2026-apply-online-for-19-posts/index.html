Q2What is the minimum and maximum age limit for the Radiotherapy Technician post?
A
The minimum age limit is 18 years, and the maximum age limit is 36 years for the Radiotherapy Technician post. Age relaxation is applicable as per government rules.
Q3What are the educational qualifications required for this recruitment?
A
Candidates need to have completed HSC with Science (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) and a 2-year Radiotherapy Technologist course with 2 years infield training, or a B.Sc in Medical Radiotherapy Technology with 1 year infield training.
Q4How many vacancies are available for the Radiotherapy Technician position?
A
There are a total of 19 vacancies available for the Radiotherapy Technician post in this recruitment drive.

Q6What is the selection process for GSSSB Radiotherapy Technician Recruitment?
A
The selection process includes a CBRT/OMR MCQ Examination, Document Verification, and the final Merit List preparation.
