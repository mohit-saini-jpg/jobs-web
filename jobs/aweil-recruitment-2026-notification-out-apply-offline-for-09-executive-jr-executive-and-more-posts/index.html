  • No Objection Certificate from current employer (for Govt./PSU employees - mandatory at interview stage)

  • Documents to be Produced at the Time of Interview:

  • All original documents of educational qualifications and experience for verification

  • Two self-attested copies of all documents

  • For candidates from Govt./PSU/private sector who are finally selected - document evidencing acceptance of resignation from previous employer (mandatory before joining)

Important Instructions

  • The appointment is purely temporary and on contract basis - it does not confer any right or claim of extension or absorption in AWEIL. Candidates currently serving in Govt./Quasi-Govt./PSU must seek prior NOC from their employer before applying and must submit it at the time of interview - failure to do so will result in cancellation of candidature. Candidates from Govt./PSU/private entities who are finally selected must produce the acceptance of their resignation from their last employer before joining AWEIL. Applications not in prescribed format, incomplete, without required attested certificates, received after 15-06-2026, or not routed through the present employer (for Govt./PSU employees) will be summarily rejected.
