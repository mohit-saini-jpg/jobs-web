How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply in Hard Copy only using the prescribed application format for the respective post. The application form can be downloaded from the official AWEIL website (www.aweil.in) or the DoO(C&S) website (ddpdoo.gov.in). Completed applications must be sent via Speed Post or Courier only - no other mode is accepted.
  • Send Application To:
  • Director (HR), Advanced Weapons and Equipment India Limited, Ordnance Factory Kanpur (OFC) Premises, Kalpi Road, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh – 208009
  • Documents to be Submitted Along with Application:
  • Completed application form in prescribed format with passport photograph (4.4 cm x 5.8 cm) affixed at designated space and second photograph attached (not pasted)
  • Self-attested copy of age proof - Birth Certificate or 10th Class Certificate
  • Self-attested copies of all educational qualification certificates and mark sheets (Graduation level onwards)
  • Self-attested copies of all experience certificates and documentary proof of experience
  • Self-attested copy of Bar Council Registration Certificate (for Legal Expert post)
  • Self-attested copy of caste certificate in prescribed format (for OBC candidates applying for Jr. Executive HR)
  • Aadhar Card Number and PAN Number details (mandatory in application form)
  • No Objection Certificate from current employer (for Govt./PSU employees - mandatory at interview stage)
  • Documents to be Produced at the Time of Interview:
  • All original documents of educational qualifications and experience for verification
  • Two self-attested copies of all documents
  • Original NOC from employer (for Govt./PSU/Quasi-Govt. employees)
  • For candidates from Govt./PSU/private sector who are finally selected - document evidencing acceptance of resignation from previous employer (mandatory before joining)

Important Instructions

  • The appointment is purely temporary and on contract basis - it does not confer any right or claim of extension or absorption in AWEIL. The contract can be terminated by AWEIL by giving 1 month's notice or payment of 1 month's salary in lieu thereof. Candidates wishing to leave must give 3 months' notice or pay 3 months' salary in lieu. Selected candidates will be required to sign a contractual agreement in the prescribed format. Candidates currently serving in Govt./Quasi-Govt./PSU must seek prior NOC from their employer before applying and must submit it at the time of interview - failure to do so will result in cancellation of candidature. Candidates from Govt./PSU/private entities who are finally selected must produce the acceptance of their resignation from their last employer before joining AWEIL. For candidates selected from the private sector, appointment is effective only after verification of Character and Antecedents from the concerned District Authority (applicable for Executive Corporate Communication post). Any misrepresentation, suppression of facts, use of fraudulent documents or impersonation at any stage will result in permanent debarment and/or legal/criminal prosecution, and dismissal if discovered after joining. AWEIL reserves the right to reject any application or candidature at any stage without assigning any reason. Decision of AWEIL in all matters related to recruitment shall be final and binding. Applications not in prescribed format, incomplete, without required attested certificates, received after 15-06-2026, or not routed through the present employer (for Govt./PSU employees) will be summarily rejected. New Labour Codes will be applicable to all newly engaged contract employees. AWEIL will not be responsible for any delay in receipt or loss of applications in postal transit. No correspondence will be entertained regarding shortlisting, interview schedule or selection decisions.
