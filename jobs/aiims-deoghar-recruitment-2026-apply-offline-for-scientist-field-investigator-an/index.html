Qualification / Eligibility

Health Economist (Level of Scientist C): Master's degree in Health Economics, Economics, Public Health, or Community Medicine with specialization in Healthcare Costing and Economic Evaluation. Scientist D (Medical/Non-Medical) / Senior Research Officer: Medical: MD/MS/DNB/MPH after MBBS with 6 years research experience or MBBS with 8 years experience or equivalent. Non-Medical: Ph.D. in Microbiology, Biochemistry, Biotechnology or related field with 5 years R&D/Teaching experience or equivalent. Scientist C (Medical/Non-Medical) / Research Officer: Medical: MBBS with 6 years experience or Postgraduate Diploma after MBBS with 5 years experience or MD/MS/DNB/MPH after MBBS with 4 years research experience. Non-Medical: M.Sc. Life Sciences/MPH/MHA/M.Pharm/Pharm D with 4 years experience. Administrative Officer: Bachelor's Degree or equivalent with minimum 3 years experience in Government Department/Statutory Body/Autonomous Body/University/Public Sector Undertaking or Retired Officers of the rank of Section Officer/Under Secretary to the Government of India. Field Officer: Graduate in Science/relevant subject with 3 years work experience or Master's degree in the relevant subject or Pharm D from a recognized institution. Field Investigator: Graduate in Science/relevant subject with 3 years work experience or Master's degree in the relevant subject or Pharm D from a recognized institution.
