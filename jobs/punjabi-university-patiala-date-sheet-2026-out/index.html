How to Download Punjabi University Patiala Date Sheet 2026

Students can download the Punjabi University Patiala Date Sheet 2026 by following the steps given below:

  • Visit the official examination portal of Punjabi University Patiala.
  • Navigate to the Date Sheet section.
  • Look for the re-revised notifications for BBA LLB, B.Com LLB or BA LLB.
  • Click on the relevant notification link (Notification No. 150, 151, 152).
  • The PDF will open. Download and save the file on your device.
  • Take a printout for ready reference during exam preparation.

Note: Always refer to the official re-revised date sheet and check all dates and instructions carefully before the examinations.
