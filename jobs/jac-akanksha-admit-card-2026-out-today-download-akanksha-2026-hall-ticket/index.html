How to Download JAC Akanksha Admit Card 2026 Online

Students can follow the step-by-step process given below to download their JAC Akanksha Admit Card 2026 in a simple way.

  • Visit the official Akanksha portal: jac.jharkhand.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, find and click on the link that says “JAC Akanksha Admit Card 2026” or similar.
  • You will be redirected to the login/admit card page.
  • Enter the required credentials – generally Roll Code, Roll Number or Registration Number and other asked details as per the application form.
  • Click on the “Submit” or “Login” button to proceed.
  • Your JAC Akanksha Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Check all details carefully, then click on the download/print option.
  • Take a clear printout on A4 size paper and keep at least 2–3 copies for safety.

If any student faces a problem in logging in or downloading the admit card, they should first re-check their entered details and then contact their school or JAC support if the issue continues.

Details Printed on JAC Akanksha Admit Card 2026

After downloading the JAC Akanksha Admit Card 2026, students must carefully check all the information printed on it. Any error should be reported immediately.

Important details generally printed on the admit card are:

  • Candidate’s full name
  • Father’s / Mother’s name
  • Roll code and roll number
  • Registration number
  • Class / Exam stream (Engineering / Medical / CLAT)
  • Exam date and exam time
  • Reporting time and gate closing time (if mentioned)
  • Name of the examination centre
  • Centre code and full address
  • Candidate’s photograph and signature
  • Important exam-day instructions and guidelines

If there is any mismatch in name, photograph, date of birth or exam centre details, the candidate should contact the school administration or JAC officials through the official communication channels.

Important Instructions for JAC Akanksha Entrance Examination 2026

Exam-day rules are strictly followed in JAC Akanksha Entrance Examination 2026, so students must follow all instructions mentioned on the admit card and in the official guidelines.

Key instructions include:

  • Reach the examination centre at least 30 - 45 minutes before the reporting time to avoid rush and last-minute issues.
  • Carry a printed JAC Akanksha Admit Card 2026 to the exam centre; soft copy on mobile will generally not be accepted.
  • Carry valid stationery items as allowed (such as black/blue ball pen) and avoid using gel pens or pencils unless specifically mentioned.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, calculators, smart watches, earphones, study material or any electronic devices inside the examination hall.
  • Follow the seating plan and instructions of the invigilator carefully and maintain discipline throughout the exam duration.
  • Fill the OMR sheet carefully with correct roll code, roll number and other details; any mistake can affect evaluation.

Non-compliance with exam rules or non-availability of admit card may lead to cancellation of candidature or denial of entry into the examination hall.
