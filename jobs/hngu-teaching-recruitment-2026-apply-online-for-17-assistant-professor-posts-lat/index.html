Qualification / Eligibility

NationalityIndian Citizens only
Education QualificationMaster's degree with minimum 55% marks (without grace marks) in the concerned/relevant/allied subject from an Indian University, or equivalent from an accredited foreign university + NET/SLET/SET cleared OR Ph.D. as per UGC Regulations (2009/2016)
Required DegreePh.D. from a foreign university ranked in Top 500 in QS/THE/ARWU rankings also qualifies
DetailsAs per UGC Regulations on Minimum Qualifications for Appointment of Teachers and Other Academic Staff in Universities and Colleges and Measures for the Maintenance of Standards in Higher Education 2018, and its amendments from time to time: | Option A: | Master's degree with 55% marks (without grace marks or rounding off), in a concerned/relevant/allied subject from an Indian University, or an equivalent degree from an accredited foreign university. PLUS: NET conducted by UGC/CSIR, or SLET/SET, or Ph.D. awarded as per UGC Minimum Standards Regulations (2009 or 2016). Candidates registered for Ph.D. prior to July 11, 2009 are exempt from NET/SLET/SET subject to fulfillment of conditions: Ph.D. awarded in regular mode, thesis evaluated by at least two external examiners, open viva voce conducted, two research papers published (at least one in a refereed journal), and at least two papers presented at UGC/ICSSR/CSIR-funded conferences. | Option B: | Ph.D. degree from a foreign university/institution ranked among the Top 500 in World University Rankings by QS, THE, or ARWU (Shanghai Jiao Tong University) at any time. | Marks Relaxation: | 5% relaxation (55% to 50%) at Master's level for SC/ST/OBC (Non-Creamy Layer)/Differently-abled candidates. 5% relaxation (55% to 50%) for Ph.D. holders who obtained their Master's degree prior to 19th September 1991. | As per Minimum Standards of Architectural Education Regulations, 2020 and amendments: | B.Arch. with minimum 60% marks and 3 years of relevant professional experience; OR B.Arch. + Master's degree in Architecture or allied subjects with minimum 60% marks at either level and 1 year of relevant professional experience.

Selection Process

1Selection for the post of Assistant Professor will be based on:
2Stage 1 - Academic Score Shortlisting: Candidates will be shortlisted based on Academic Score as per Appendix II
3Table 3A of UGC Regulation 2018
4subject to fulfilling all essential eligibility criteria. Shortlisting ratio is 1:15 (suitable candidates with higher merit); if eligible candidates are fewer than 1:15
5all are called for interview.
6Stage 2 - Screening Test (wherever necessary): The University may hold a Screening Test for the post of Assistant Professor wherever necessary. Syllabus and schedule of such test will be published on the University website.
7Stage 3 - Personal Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be called for interview by email only. Candidates must appear at their own cost; no TA/DA will be paid. Call letters for interview will be sent only to short-listed candidates by email.
8Note: Academic score (Appendix II
9Table 3A) is used only for short-listing purposes. Final selection will be based solely on performance in the interview.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1Q1. What is the total number of vacancies in NGU Recruitment 2026 Advertisement No.05/2026?

A
candidates will be placed at Level-10 with a basic pay of Rs. 57,700/- per month, as per State Government/UGC norms.
Q4Q4. Is NET/SLET/SET mandatory for NGU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
NET/SLET/SET is the minimum eligibility for Assistant Professor posts (Arts, Science, Commerce & Management). However, candidates who hold a Ph.D. degree as per UGC Regulations 2009 or 2016 are exempted from the NET/SLET/SET requirement. For Architecture posts, NET/SLET/SET is not required; professional experience and B.Arch. marks are the criteria.
Q5Q5. What is the application fee for NGU Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 500/- for General/Unreserved candidates and Rs. 250/- for ST/SC/OBC/EWS/PwD candidates. Fee is payable through online mode only and is non-refundable.
Q6Q6. Is knowledge of Gujarati mandatory for NGU Assistant Professor 2026?
A
Yes, knowledge of Gujarati is mandatory for all applicants regardless of department, as explicitly stated in the notification.
Q7Q7. What is the selection process for NGU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection involves three stages: (1) Academic Score-based shortlisting (as per UGC Regulation 2018, Appendix II Table 3A) in a 1:15 ratio, (2) Screening Test wherever necessary (syllabus published on the University website), and (3) Personal Interview for shortlisted candidates. Final selection is based solely on interview performance.
Q8Q8. How to apply for NGU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must: (a) Apply online at https://recruitment.ngu.ac.in by 15-06-2026; (b) Pay the application fee online; and (c) Submit 3 sets of printed hardcopy application along with all self-attested documents and Academic Score Appendix II Table 3A to the Registrar, NGU, Patan – 384265 by 20-06-2026, 06:00 PM via Speed Post or in person.

School of Business Management/MBA (3), Department of English (1), Department of Life Science — Microbiology & Zoology (2), Department of Hospital Management (3), and Department of Gujarati (4).
Q10Q10. Are there reservations for PwD candidates in NGU Recruitment 2026?
