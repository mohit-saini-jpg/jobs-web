How to Check UP Madarsa Board Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check UP Madarsa Board Result 2026 online on the official website:

  • Visit the official website of Uttar Pradesh Madarsa Board at madarsaboard.upsdc.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the 'Exam Result' or relevant 'Result 2026' link.
  • You will be redirected to the UP Madarsa Board online result page.
  • Select the exam year (2026) and choose your course/class, such as Munshi/Maulvi or Alim.
  • Enter your roll number and the security code shown on the screen.
  • Click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button to proceed.
  • Your UP Madarsa Board Result 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future reference.
