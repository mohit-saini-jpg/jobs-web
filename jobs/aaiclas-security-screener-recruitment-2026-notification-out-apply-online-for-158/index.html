Important Instructions

Candidates not willing to serve on All India Basis need not apply. Only a single application per candidate will be accepted and candidates can apply for a single location only. Candidates selected under previous AAICLAS advertisements in the last 3 years and terminated due to failure in trainings/examinations need not apply. Percentage of marks at Graduation must be entered in numeric form only; CGPA or any other format will not be accepted. All communications from AAICLAS will be made only on the registered email ID; candidates must keep checking their registered email regularly. No change in email ID will be allowed once entered in the application form. After selection, candidates must submit a surety bond of Rs. 1,50,000/- on non-judicial stamp paper of Rs. 100/- (duly notarized) for non-completion of the 3-year engagement period. The contract period of 3 years starts after passing all certification examinations and not from the date of joining on stipend. During stipend period only one casual leave per month shall be applicable on pro-rata basis. Maximum two attempts will be given for qualifying certification examinations; failure in both attempts leads to termination of Fixed Term Contract without further notice. Training costs for the 2nd attempt (if applicable) must be borne by the candidate; AAICLAS will only make the nomination. Notice period for resignation is minimum 3 months; those resigning with immediate effect must deposit 3 months' salary and no experience letter/verification will be issued. Candidates working in Govt./PSU/Autonomous Bodies must produce a NOC from their present employer. Furnishing false/incorrect information or suppression of material facts at any stage will result in cancellation of candidature or termination of engagement. No TA/DA or reimbursement shall be made by AAICLAS for the selection process. The job is transferable to any station due to operational requirements; place of posting can be changed to any location within India at sole discretion of AAICLAS Management. Any canvassing or bringing political/outside influence shall be treated as disqualification. All further announcements/details pertaining to this recruitment will be published only on www.aaiclas.aero and not by post. For queries, write to [email protected] mentioning Advt. No. and position applied for, or call helpdesk at 011-24667781.
