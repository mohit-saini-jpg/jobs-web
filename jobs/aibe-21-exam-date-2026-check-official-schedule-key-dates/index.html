What is AIBE 21 Exam 2026?

The AIBE 21 (XXI) 2026 is the All India Bar Examination conducted by the Bar Council of India. It is a certification exam that assesses law graduates' eligibility to practice law in India. Key aspects include:

  • Open-book format (offline mode)
  • 100 multiple-choice questions
  • No negative marking
  • Qualifying marks: 45% for General/OBC, 40% for SC/ST/PwD
  • Certificate of Practice (CoP) issued to qualifiers

Candidates must clear AIBE to obtain the CoP and practice in courts.
