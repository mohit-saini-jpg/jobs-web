How to Download GSEB 12th HSC Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the simple steps given below to check and download the GSEB 12th HSC Result 2026 from the official website.

  • Visit the official website of Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board - gseb.org.
  • On the homepage, find and click on the link that reads "GSEB HSC Result 2026" or "GSEB 12th Result 2026".
  • A new result login page will open on the screen.
  • Enter your GSEB Class 12 seat number in the given field carefully.
  • Click on the "Submit" or "Go" button to proceed.
  • Your GSEB 12th HSC Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen with subject-wise marks and total score.
  • Download the online marksheet and take a printout for future reference until the original marksheet is issued by the school.
