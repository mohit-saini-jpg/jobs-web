Korea District NHM Recruitment 2026

The Korea District NHM Contractual Recruitment 2026 is being conducted for filling vacant contractual posts under National Health Mission (NHM), RBSK, NUHM, UHWC, NPPCD, NIDDCP, NRC, SNCU, NBSU, NTEP, and BPHU programmes. Posts are to be filled for both District Korea and MCB (Manendragarh) as per letter No. 1336/N.H.M./Kale./2026 dated 02.04.2026 under Rashtriya Swasthya Mission Human Resource for Health ROP 2025-26. All posts are purely contractual in nature governed by NHM Chhattisgarh Manav Sansadhan Niti 2018. The contract period is up to 31st March 2027, extendable based on satisfactory performance. The last date to apply is 3rd July 2026 by 5:00 PM .

Korea NHM Selection Process 2026

Application Scrutiny: Applications received are scrutinised for eligibility. Eligible/ineligible lists are uploaded on the district website www.korea.gov.in. Skill Test / Written Test / Interview (20 marks): Candidates are called for skill/written/computer-based test or interview based on post type. Candidates must score minimum 30% marks in this stage to qualify. Merit List Preparation: Final merit list is prepared on the basis of: Mandatory Educational Qualification marks (65% or 60% weightage as per post) Experience marks - 10/15/20 marks (NHM CG employees: 3 marks/year max 15; Others: 2 marks/year max 10; ANM trained Mitanin: 4 marks/year max 20) Bonus marks - 10 marks (for temporary health workers who served continuously 6 months during COVID-19 in CG Govt. health institutions as per letter dated 31.01.2024) Skill/Written Test marks - 20 marks Total = 100 marks Final Selection: Sele

Korea District NHM Recruitment 2026 Notification Out : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for Korea District NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the application for Korea District NHM Recruitment 2026 is 3rd July 2026 by 5:00 PM. Applications must be sent only via Registered Post / Speed Post / Registry to the Chief Medical and Health Officer, District Korea, Baikunthpur, Chhattisgarh. Applications received late or by other modes will not be accepted.
Q2How many vacancies are released in Korea NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
Vacancies have been released for two districts - Korea and MCB (Manendragarh). District Korea has 21 post categories and District MCB has 30 post categories covering 140 contractual posts under NHM, RBSK, SNCU, NTEP, UHWC, NUHM, NPPCD, NIDDCP, NRC, NBSU, and BPHU programmes. The exact total is provisional and subject to change.
Q3What is the application fee for Korea NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
Application fee varies by category and salary of the applied post. For General/UR candidates it is Rs. 300 (salary ≤25,000) or Rs. 400 (salary >25,000). For OBC/Female it is Rs. 200 or Rs. 300. For SC/ST/PwD it is Rs. 100 or Rs. 200. Fee must be paid via DD in favour of District Health Society-Non-NRHM Fund.
Q4What is the selection process for Korea NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection is based on a merit list of 100 marks comprising: educational qualification marks (65%), experience marks (10/15/20), bonus marks (10 for COVID frontline workers), and skill/written test marks (20). Candidates must score minimum 30% in the skill/written test to qualify.
Q5Can candidates from outside Korea or MCB district apply for this recruitment?
A
No. Only candidates applying for Korea district or MCB district may apply. The district name must be clearly mentioned in the application form and on the envelope. Applications without the district name mentioned will be rejected outright.
