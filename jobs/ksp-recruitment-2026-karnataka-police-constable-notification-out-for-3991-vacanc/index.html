KSP Constable Salary 2026

One of the most attractive aspects of the KSP Recruitment 2026 is the government pay structure. Karnataka police constables are placed at Pay Level 7 under the 7th Pay Commission, with a monthly pay scale of Rs. 37,500 - Rs. 76,100. In addition to the base salary, candidates are eligible for: Dearness Allowance (DA) House Rent Allowance (HRA) Travel Allowance (TA) National Pension Scheme (NPS) benefits Other Karnataka Government allowances Training and Probation: Selected candidates undergo 9 months of basic police training at designated Police Training Schools in Karnataka. Candidates are on probation for 2 years and 6 months after formal appointment. Candidates who fail during training may be removed from service.

KSP Recruitment 2026 – Karnataka Police Constable : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is KSP Recruitment 2026?
A
KSP Recruitment 2026 refers to the Karnataka State Police's official recruitment drive to fill 3,991 Civil Police Constable vacancies across two cadres - the Kalyana-Karnataka cadre (596 posts) and the General/Mikkilida cadre (3,395 posts). Applications are accepted online through the KEA portal from 8th June to 3rd July 2026.
Q2What is the last date to apply for KSP Constable Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to apply online for KSP Constable Recruitment 2026 is 3rd July 2026. Candidates can pay the application fee until 4th July 2026. No applications will be accepted after these deadlines.
Q3What is the PC exam date 2026 for Karnataka?
A
The KSP Police Constable written exam date is 2nd August 2026. Notification 1 (KK Cadre) exam is scheduled from 2:30 PM to 4:00 PM, and Notification 2 (General Cadre) from 10:30 AM to 12:00 PM on the same day.
Q4What is the eligibility for KSP Constable Recruitment 2026 Karnataka?
A
Candidates must have passed Class 12 / PUC or equivalent from a recognised board. The age limit is 18 to 33 years for general category candidates, with relaxations for SC/ST, OBC, forest area residents, and ex-servicemen. Knowledge of the Kannada language is also mandatory.
Q5Where can I apply online for KSP Constable Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online at the official KEA portal: https://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea/. The application window is open from 8th June 2026 to 3rd July 2026.
Q6What is the application fee for KSP Constable 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 750 for General and OBC (Category 2A, 2B, 3A, 3B) candidates. Candidates belonging to SC, ST, Category-1, Ex-Servicemen, and Transgender categories need to pay Rs. 500.
Q7How many vacancies are there in KSP Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 3,991 Civil Police Constable vacancies have been announced - 596 under the Kalyana-Karnataka cadre and 3,395 under the General (Mikkilida/Okkalida) cadre.
Q8What is the selection process for Karnataka Civil Police Constable 2026?
A
The KSP Constable selection process includes: (1) Written Exam (OMR-based, 100 marks), (2) Physical Standard Test (PST), (3) Physical Endurance Test (PET), (4) Medical Examination, and (5) Document Verification.
Q9What is the salary of a KSP Constable in 2026?
A
The Karnataka Police Constable salary is Rs. 37,500 - Rs. 76,100 per month as per the 7th Pay Commission (Pay Level 7), plus applicable government allowances.
Q10Can female candidates apply for KSP Constable Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, both male and female candidates can apply for the KSP Constable Recruitment 2026. There are separate vacancy allocations and physical standards for female and third-gender candidates.
