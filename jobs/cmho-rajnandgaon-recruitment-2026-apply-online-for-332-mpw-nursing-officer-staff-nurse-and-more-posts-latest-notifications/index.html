How To Apply

  • Eligible candidates must complete the online registration process as follows:
  • Visit the official application portal: https://govthealth.cg.gov.in/RecRajnandgaonCont2026
  • Register using your mobile number. Registration number and password will be sent to your mobile.
  • Log in using the registration number and password, then fill in the complete online application form.
  • Pay the application fee by scanning the QR Code provided and enter the Transaction ID in the application form.
  • After final submission, take a printout of the application, sign it, and upload the self-signed copy. Applications without self-signed uploads will not be accepted.
  • Retain the payment receipt printout for document verification purposes.
  • All required documents (educational qualifications, caste, domicile, disability, council registration, experience) must be entered accurately. Experience certificates must be uploaded in the application.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is CMHO Rajnandgaon Recruitment 2026 under NHM?

CMHO Rajnandgaon Recruitment 2026 is a district-level hiring drive for 332 contractual posts under the National Health Mission (NHM), Chhattisgarh, as per HR Policy 2018. All posts are purely temporary and will be filled on contract basis through an online application process.

Q2. What is the last date to apply for CMHO Rajnandgaon NHM Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit online applications for CMHO Rajnandgaon NHM Recruitment 2026 is May 29, 2026 up to 5:00 PM. Applications submitted after the deadline will not be accepted under any circumstances.

Q3. How many vacancies are released in CMHO Rajnandgaon Recruitment 2026?

A total of 332 vacancies have been notified across 22 different posts, including Nursing Officer/Staff Nurse, ANM, Community Health Officer, Pharmacist, Radiographer, MO AYUSH, RMA, Physiotherapist, Audiologist, Secretarial Assistant, MPW (Male), STS, Attendant, and Class IV/Aya Bai/Cook cum Caretaker.

Q4. What is the salary structure for CMHO Rajnandgaon NHM posts in 2026?

The monthly salary varies by post, ranging from ₹8,800 – ₹8,860 for Attendant/Class IV/Aya Bai/Cook cum Caretaker, to ₹25,000 for MO AYUSH and Audiologist. Nursing Officer/Staff Nurse receive ₹16,000 – ₹16,500, Community Health Officer gets ₹16,500, while Block Manager–Account is paid ₹21,000 per month.

Q5. What are the eligibility and educational qualifications for CMHO Rajnandgaon posts?

Nursing Officer/Staff Nurse: B.Sc Nursing/Post Basic B.Sc Nursing or GNM with valid CG Nursing Council registration. ANM: 12th pass + ANM training + CG Nursing Council registration. Pharmacist: B.Pharma or D.Pharma with CG Pharmacy Council registration. MO AYUSH: BHMS/BAMS/BUMS with valid AYUSH board registration. Other posts require respective degrees/diplomas such as B.Com+PGDCA, BASLP, BPT, Radiography, OT Technician, MPW training, etc., as specified in the notification.

Q6. What is the age limit for CMHO Rajnandgaon NHM Recruitment 2026?

The minimum age is 18 years for all applicants. For medical posts, the maximum age is 70 years, while for managerial and other posts, the maximum age is 64 years, calculated as on January 1, 2026. Age relaxations apply as per Chhattisgarh government norms.

Q7. What is the selection process for CMHO Rajnandgaon NHM posts?

Selection is merit-based and conducted at the district level. First, applications are screened and eligible/ineligible lists are uploaded on the official website. Shortlisted candidates then appear for a Written Test / Computer-Based Test / Clinical Skill Test (depending on the post), and must score at least 30% (6 out of 20 marks) to qualify. The final merit list is prepared by combining academic marks, experience marks, and test scores.

Q8. How can I apply online for CMHO Rajnandgaon Recruitment 2026?

To apply, visit https://govthealth.cg.gov.in/RecRajnandgaonCont2026, register with your mobile number, and log in using the registration number and password. Fill the online form, upload all required documents, pay the fee via QR code/UPI, and enter the transaction ID. After final submission, take a printout, sign it, and upload the self-signed copy, as applications without this step will be rejected.
