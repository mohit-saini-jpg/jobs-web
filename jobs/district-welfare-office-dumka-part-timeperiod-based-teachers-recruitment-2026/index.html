What is Dumka Ghanti Adharit Shikshak Recruitment 2026?

Dumka Ghanti Adharit Shikshak Recruitment 2026 is a temporary, hourly-based teacher hiring drive by the District Welfare Office, Dumka, for 83 posts across primary and subject-teacher categories in 15 ST/Backward Class residential schools. Selection is purely temporary and alternative in nature, based on merit, for a maximum tenure of one year. Applications must be submitted offline in sealed envelopes by 25-07-2026.

Salary / Honorarium

Selected candidates will be paid Rs. 200 per teaching period (per ghanti), with a maximum of 4 periods per working day and 5 working days per week. No other allowance is admissible, and payment is based on daily teaching attendance.

Not Announced Yet. The official notification does not mention any application fee details.

District Welfare Office Dumka Part-time/Period-based : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection will be made purely on a temporary basis by a district-level committee based on merit (मेधाक्रम) among PG/Graduate/B.Ed or teacher-training degree holders. Preference will be given to candidates of the respective block, local language speakers, and local candidates of the area where the residential school is located. If eligible candidates are not available at the block level, eligible candidates available at the district level will be selected. All selections will follow the Jharkhand government's district reservation roster provisions applicable to direct recruitment for district-level posts. No written examination details are mentioned in the notification.

District Welfare Office Dumka Part-time/Period-based : Important Instructions

Applications will be accepted only in the prescribed format; incomplete applications or those in any other format will not be accepted. Applications received after the last date will not be accepted. One candidate can submit only one application. This engagement is purely temporary and alternative; no claim for regularization or permanency will be entertained in future. Selected teachers will initially be engaged for a maximum of 6 months, extendable by another 6 months on satisfactory service, subject to a maximum total tenure of 1 year, or until permanent teachers are posted by the department, whichever is earlier. No leave or transfer benefits will be admissible; no residential facility will be provided at the school. Service can be terminated immediately without prior notice for violation of agreement terms or unsatisfactory work.
