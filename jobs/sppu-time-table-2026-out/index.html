SPPU Time Table 2026 Out

Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) has officially released the Time Table 2026 for various PG Diploma programmes under the 2020 Pattern. The schedule includes PG Diploma in Banking, Finance and Insurance, PG Diploma in International Business, and PG Diploma in Taxation. Students can now download the official SPPU Time Table 2026 PDFs from the university portal.

How to Download SPPU Time Table 2026

Students can download the SPPU Time Table 2026 from the official university website by following the steps given below:

  • Visit the official website of Savitribai Phule Pune University.
  • Navigate to the Examination or Circulars section.
  • Look for the PG Diploma 2020 Pattern Time Table links.
  • Click on your respective programme timetable PDF.
  • The file will open. Download and save it on your device.
  • Take a printout for ready reference during exam preparation.

Note: Always refer to the official SPPU Time Table 2026 and check all dates and instructions carefully before the examinations.

Details Mentioned in SPPU Time Table 2026

The official SPPU Time Table 2026 contains all essential information for students. The key details mentioned in the timetable are:

  • Programme name and pattern
  • Examination dates and day
  • Subject name with paper code
  • Timing of each paper
  • Important instructions for candidates.
