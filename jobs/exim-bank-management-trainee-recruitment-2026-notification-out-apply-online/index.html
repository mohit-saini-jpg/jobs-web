Important Instructions

The application link will be live from 15 May 2026; no application in any other format or mode will be considered. The registration process is complete only when fee is deposited through online mode on or before the last date; incomplete applications or applications without fee (except exempt categories) are liable to be rejected. Candidates must ensure they fulfil all eligibility criteria before paying fee/submitting application; fees once paid will not be refunded even if the applicant is found ineligible. Management Trainees will be absorbed as Deputy Managers (JM-I) after successful completion of one year of training, subject to satisfactory performance. At the time of joining, selected candidates must execute a personal bond of Rs. 3 lakh to serve as Management Trainee for 1 year and minimum 5 years as an employee of the Bank after absorption. Selected candidates will be posted anywhere in India; candidates willing to serve anywhere in India only should apply. They may also be transferred to offices outside India with their consent. The Bank will analyse responses of candidates to detect similarity patterns; if scores are found not genuine/valid, the Bank reserves the right to cancel the candidature. Final merit list will be based on Written Examination (70% weightage) and Personal Interview (30% weightage). There will be no sectional cut-off in the Written Test; shortlisting for interview will be based on overall performance in the Written Test. A pre-recruitment training will be organised for candidates from reserved categories; details will be intimated to candidates who opt for it in the online application form. PwBD candidates eligible for scribe facility must indicate the same in the online application form at the time of applying; subsequent requests may not be entertained. Scribe must be arranged by the candidate at their own cost. Compensatory time of 20 minutes per hour of examination will be allowed for eligible PwBD candidates using a scribe. Mobile phones or similar electronic communication devices are strictly prohibited inside the examination/interview hall. No request for change of examination centre/venue/date/session will be entertained. Candidates must carry the call letter and original photo identity proof to both the Written Test and Personal Interview without fail; absence of either will result in denial of entry. Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification. The Bank reserves the right to cancel the recruitment process entirely at any stage without assigning any reason. All legal proceedings in respect of any claim/dispute arising out of this advertisement shall be instituted only in Mumbai; courts/tribunals/forums at Mumbai shall have sole and exclusive jurisdiction.
