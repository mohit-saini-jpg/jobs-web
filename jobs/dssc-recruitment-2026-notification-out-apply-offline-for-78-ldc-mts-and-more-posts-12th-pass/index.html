Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is DSSC Recruitment 2026?

Defence Services Staff College (DSSC), Wellington (Nilgiris), Tamil Nadu has invited offline applications from eligible Indian citizens for various Group ‘C’ civilian posts such as LDC, MTS, Driver, Stenographer and technical/support roles on a permanent basis.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in DSSC Recruitment 2026?

A total of 78 Group ‘C’ posts are proposed to be filled at DSSC Wellington, including LDC, Civilian Motor Driver, MTS (Office & Training), Stenographer Grade II, Supervisor (Networking), Network Administrator, Hardware Administrator, Lineman, Boat Builder and Technical Attendant (Printing Machine Operator), with reservations including ESM/PwBD.

Q3. What is the mode of application for DSSC Group ‘C’ posts?

The application process is completely offline; candidates must submit the prescribed application form by post to the Commandant, DSSC, Wellington (Nilgiris) – 643 231, Tamil Nadu within the stipulated time.

Q4. What is the last date to apply for DSSC Recruitment 2026?

The last date for receipt of applications is 21 days from the date of publication of the advertisement in Employment News (issue dated 23–29 May 2026), and applications received after this period are likely to be rejected.

Q5. What are the educational qualifications required for LDC and Stenographer at DSSC?

For LDC, candidates must have passed 12th and possess a typing speed of 35 wpm in English or 30 wpm in Hindi on computer, while Stenographer Grade II requires 12th pass with shorthand dictation at 80 wpm for 10 minutes and transcription in 50 minutes (English) or 65 minutes (Hindi) on computer.

Q6. What are the qualifications for MTS and Civilian Motor Driver (OG)?

MTS (Office & Training) generally requires 10th pass, whereas Civilian Motor Driver (Ordinary Grade) requires 10th/12th pass along with a valid heavy vehicle driving licence and relevant driving experience as prescribed in the detailed notification.

Q7. What is the age limit for DSSC Group ‘C’ posts?

For LDC, Stenographer, Driver and technical posts, the age limit is 18–27 years, while for MTS and some other support posts the age limit is 18–25 years, with relaxations for SC, ST, OBC, Ex-Servicemen, PwBD and other categories as per Government of India rules.

Q8. What is the pay scale for DSSC Group ‘C’ posts?

Under the 7th CPC Pay Matrix, Stenographer Grade II carries Level 4 (Rs. 25,500–81,100), LDC, Driver, Network/Hardware/Supervisor, Lineman and Boat Builder are in Level 2 (Rs. 19,900–63,200), and MTS and Technical Attendant – Printing Machine Operator are in Level 1 (Rs. 18,000–56,900).
