ESIC Pune Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

ESIC Medical College & Hospital, Bibvewadi, Pune has announced Advertisement No. 07 of 2026, inviting walk-in interviews for 77 contractual posts of Professor, Associate Professor, Assistant Professor, Senior Resident (1-Year and 3-Year), and Tutor/Demonstrator across 24 departments. The walk-in interviews will be held from 8 July 2026 to 15 July 2026, with candidates reporting between 9:00 AM and 10:30 AM at the Dean/Medical Superintendent's office on their department's scheduled date.

Eligibility for this recruitment is determined by two factors - educational qualification and age - both of which vary slightly for Senior Resident posts compared to the teaching faculty cadre.
