How to Apply

  • 1Step 1: Visit SSO Portal - Go to sso.rajasthan.gov.in and log in with your SSO ID
  • 2Step 2: Complete OTR (if not registered) - Complete One Time Registration (OTR) - fill category, disability status, and domicile details correctly
  • 3Step 3: Pay OTR Fee - Pay the OTR registration fee (Rs. 600 for General/Creamy OBC/MBC, Rs. 400 for Non-Creamy OBC/MBC/EWS/SC/ST/PwBD) through e-Mitra, CSC, Net Banking, ATM Debit Card, or Credit Card
  • 4Step 4: Access Recruitment Portal - After OTR, log in to SSO and go to Citizen Apps (G2C) → Recruitment Portal
  • 5Step 5: Click Apply Now - Click Apply Now for Advertisement No. 07/2026
  • 6Step 6: Fill Application Form - Fill in personal details, educational qualifications, and department preference (Secondary/Sanskrit Education) with priority order
  • 7Step 7: Upload Documents - Upload required documents: qualification certificates, category certificate, domicile, disability certificate (if applicable), photo, signature, thumb impression
  • 8Step 8: Verify & Submit - Verify all entries carefully before clicking Final Submit
  • 9Step 9: Save Application - Download and save the application with the generated Application ID for future reference
  • 10Applications are online only - no offline applications accepted under any circumstances
  • 11Application period: 25-05-2026 to 23-06-2026 (11:59 PM)
  • 12Age calculated as on 01-01-2027: Minimum 18 years, Maximum 40 years (relaxation as per category)
  • 13Complete OTR (One Time Registration) on SSO Portal well before last date to avoid transaction pendency issues
  • 14Correction window: 3 days after application closure (Fee: Rs. 300)
  • 15Second correction window: 7 days after examination
  • 16Name, father's name, date of birth, photo, signature, and thumb impression cannot be changed even in correction window
  • 17CBT Exam: Senior - 22-08-2026, Basic - 23-08-2026
  • 18Exam Pattern: 200 marks (Paper I: 100 + Paper II: 100), 4 hours total duration
  • 19Negative Marking: 1/3 mark per wrong answer
  • 20Qualifying Marks: 40% for General, 35% for SC/ST
  • 21OBC certificate must be based on father's name, residence, and income - husband-based OBC certificates not valid
  • 22EWS income & asset certificate valid for 1 year from issue date (extendable up to 3 years with affidavit)
  • 23Caste certificates issued by non-competent authorities will be rejected at document verification stage
  • 24For Secondary Education posts: additional 3 years age relaxation (not for Sanskrit Education)
  • 25Candidates from outside Rajasthan treated as General category - must pay Rs. 600 OTR fee
