Qualification / Eligibility

Assistant Engineer (Electrical): 1st Class Diploma in Electrical Engineering or equivalent Degree as per the nomenclature recognized by AICTE as Diploma in Electrical Engineering, along with 08 Years of post qualification Experience. 1st Class B.E./B.Tech. in Electrical Engineering or equivalent Degree as per the nomenclature recognized by AICTE in Electrical Engineering Stream with 06 Years of post qualification Experience. Experience must be in regular scale obtained from institutions like IITs/IISc/IIMs/IIITs/NITs IISERs or similar such Centrally/State funded institutions/Government Organizations/Public Sectors Units. Work experience obtained during the qualifying academic programme/s must not be considered. Junior Engineer (Civil): 1st Class Diploma in Civil Engineering or equivalent Diploma as per the nomenclature recognized by AICTE as Diploma in Civil Engineering Stream with 05 Years of post qualification Experience. 1st Class B.E./B.Tech. in Civil Engineering or equivalent Degree as per the nomenclature recognized by AICTE in Civil Engineering Stream with 02 Years of post qualification Experience. Experience must be in regular scale obtained from institutions like IITs/IISc/IIMs/IIITs/NITs /IISERs or similar such Centrally funded institutions/Government Organizations/ Public Sectors Units. Work experience obtained during the qualifying academic programme/s must not be considered.
