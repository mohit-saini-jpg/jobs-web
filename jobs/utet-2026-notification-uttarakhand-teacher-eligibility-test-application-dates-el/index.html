UTET 2026 Notification: Uttarakhand Teacher Eligibility : Job Overview

The Uttarakhand Vidyalayi Shiksha Parishad (Board of School Education Uttarakhand), Ramnagar, Nainital has released the notification for the Uttarakhand Teacher Eligibility Test 2026, known as UTET-I & II 2026. The test is conducted in two levels - UTET-I for candidates seeking eligibility to teach primary classes (I to V) and UTET-II for candidates seeking eligibility to teach upper primary classes (VI to VIII). Online applications open on 14 July 2026 and the exam is proposed to be held on 29 September 2026 (Tuesday). Passing UTET is one of the minimum qualifications required for appointment

UTET 2026 Exam Pattern and Marking Scheme

UTET is a multiple-choice, OMR-based test. Each correct answer carries 1 mark. A blank, incorrectly marked, or multiple-marked response gets zero marks. There is no negative marking for wrong answers. Question papers (other than language papers) are printed bilingually in Hindi and English. Candidates must choose their First Language (medium of instruction) as either Hindi or English; the First Language paper will be of a higher level than the Second Language paper. The Second Language must be chosen from Hindi, English, Sanskrit, or Urdu, and must be different from the First Language. For UTET-II, candidates must choose either Mathematics & Science or Social Studies as their subject.

UTET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

A candidate must meet one of the following: Senior Secondary (or equivalent) with at least 50% marks AND a 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed/BTC), passed or currently in training/enrolled. Senior Secondary (or equivalent) with at least 45% marks AND a 2-year D.El.Ed/BTC obtained as per NCTE (Recognition Norms and Procedure) Regulations, 2002, passed or currently in training/enrolled. Senior Secondary (or equivalent) with at least 50% marks AND a 4-year Bachelor of Elementary Education (B.El.Ed), passed or currently in training/enrolled. Senior Secondary (or equivalent) with at least 50% marks AND a 2-year Diploma in Education (Special Education) recognized by the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI), passed or currently in training/enrolled. UTET 2026 Exam Centres

The written exam will be held at 29 cities across the state's 13 districts. Candidates must give two city preferences in order of priority in the online application; once a centre is allotted, no request for change is entertained. If fewer than 100 candidates opt for a particular exam city, that city will not be made an exam centre, and such candidates will be allotted their second-preference city or the nearest possible city instead. No travelling allowance is payable to candidates for appearing in the exam.

UTET 2026: Facility for Scribe (Divyang/PWD Candidates)

Candidates with Blindness, Locomotor Disability, or Cerebral Palsy, and other candidates whose reading/writing ability is affected in special circumstances, may be permitted to use a scribe. The scribe must be arranged by the candidate. The scribe's highest educational qualification must be at least 2 academic years lower and not more than 3 academic years lower than the mandatory qualification for the exam - broadly, no lower than Intermediate level and no higher than first-year graduation level. Eligible candidates get 20 minutes of compensatory time per hour of examination. This extra time also applies to blind/low-vision and other differently-abled candidates whose writing speed is permanently affected, whether or not they use a scribe. UTET 2026: Validity, Number of Attempts and Applicability

The UTET certificate is valid for a lifetime. A candidate may appear for UTET any number of times, including to improve a score after already qualifying. Candidates who have qualified the central-level CTET (Central Teacher Eligibility Test) are also eligible for teacher appointment in all types of schools in Uttarakhand; for reserved categories (SC/ST/OBC) and differently-abled candidates, CTET qualifying marks are treated as equivalent to UTET qualifying marks. Candidates who qualify the state-level UTET are eligible for appointment in schools within Uttarakhand that are run by, aided by, or recognized by the state government, or affiliated to any national education board, and which have received a No Objection Certificate from the state government, subject to meeting other prescribed eligibility conditions. TET certificates issued by other states are NOT valid for teacher appointment

UTET 2026 Notification: Uttarakhand Teacher Eligibility : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is UTET 2026?
A
UTET (Uttarakhand Teacher Eligibility Test) 2026 is a two-level eligibility test conducted by the Uttarakhand Vidyalayi Shiksha Parishad, Ramnagar, for candidates seeking to become primary (Classes I-V, UTET-I) or upper primary (Classes VI-VIII, UTET-II) teachers in Uttarakhand.
Q2What is the last date to apply for UTET 2026?
A
The online application window is 14 July 2026 to 3 August 2026 (11:59 PM). The fee must be paid by 6 August 2026 (11:59 PM), and the correction window is open from 8 to 11 August 2026.
Q3When is the UTET 2026 exam?
A
The exam is proposed for 29 September 2026 (Tuesday) - UTET-I from 10:00 AM to 12:30 PM and UTET-II from 2:00 PM to 4:30 PM, in the same exam centres on the same day.
Q4Can I apply for both UTET-I and UTET-II 2026 together?
A
Yes. A candidate wishing to appear for both levels needs to submit only one online application and indicate this clearly in the application form.
Q5What is the application fee for UTET 2026?
A
General/OBC candidates pay ₹600 for one exam (UTET-I or UTET-II) or ₹1,000 for both. SC/ST/differently-abled candidates pay ₹300 for one exam or ₹500 for both.
Q6What are the minimum qualifying marks in UTET 2026?
A
60% for the General category, 40% for SC/ST, and 50% for OBC, differently-abled, freedom-fighter dependents, and ex-servicemen (self). SC/ST candidates who are also differently-abled need 40%.
Q7Is there an age limit for UTET 2026?
A
Not specified in this notification. No age criteria are mentioned for appearing in the UTET exam.
Q8Is CTET valid for teacher jobs in Uttarakhand?
A
Yes. CTET-qualified candidates are eligible for teacher appointment in all types of schools in Uttarakhand state, and CTET qualifying marks for reserved/differently-abled categories are treated as equivalent to UTET qualifying marks.
Q9Is a TET certificate from another state valid in Uttarakhand?
A
No. Teacher Eligibility Test certificates issued by other states are not valid for teacher appointment in Uttarakhand.
Q10How long is the UTET certificate valid?
A
The UTET certificate is valid for a lifetime.
