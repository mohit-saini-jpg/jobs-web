What Is the Kerala Plus 2 SAY Exam?

Before checking the schedule, it helps to understand what this exam actually is and who it's meant for.

The Kerala Plus 2 SAY Examination, or "Save A Year" exam, is a supplementary/improvement exam conducted by DHSE Kerala for two groups of students: those who appeared for the March 2026 Plus Two exam but failed in one or more subjects, and those who passed but want to improve their marks in a single subject. The exam gives candidates a chance to clear pending papers or boost their scores without losing an entire academic year, allowing them to proceed smoothly into undergraduate admissions.

Kerala Plus 2 SAY Hall Ticket 2026: Release Details

This section covers exactly when and how the hall ticket was made available.

DHSE Kerala released the Plus 2 SAY hall ticket 2026 on June 26, 2026, through the official portal dhsekerala.gov.in. Unlike the regular Plus Two exam, the SAY hall ticket is not available for individual student login and download. Instead, school authorities download the admit cards in bulk for all their registered candidates and then distribute the printed copies to students.

Key points to note:

  • Hall tickets were issued on June 26, 2026.
  • Only school heads/authorities can download the admit cards from the official website.
  • Students must collect their hall ticket from their respective schools.
  • A printed copy must be carried to the exam centre on every exam day.
  • Entry to the examination hall will not be permitted without the admit card.

How to Download Kerala Plus 2 SAY Hall Ticket 2026 (For Schools)

Since the download process is handled at the school level, here's the exact step-by-step procedure school authorities need to follow.

  • Visit the official website: dhsekerala.gov.in
  • Click on the "Plus 2 Hall Ticket" link available on the homepage.
  • Log in using the school's official credentials.
  • Download the hall tickets for all registered SAY candidates.
  • Take printouts and distribute them to students before the exam dates.

For students: Simply collect the hall ticket from your school before June 29. Verify all personal and exam details printed on it name, register number, subject codes, and exam centre before the exam begins.

Important Instructions for Students Appearing in the SAY Exam

A short checklist helps avoid last-minute exam-day problems.

  • Collect the hall ticket from your school before the exam begins.
  • Cross-check all personal and subject details printed on the admit card.
  • Carry the original printed hall ticket to the centre for every single paper.
  • Reach the examination centre well before the reporting time.
  • Follow all DHSE examination guidelines and invigilator instructions throughout.
  • Do not indulge in any unfair practices, as this can lead to cancellation of the exam.

Kerala Plus 2 SAY Result 2026: When Will It Be Declared?

Many students checking the hall ticket also want to know what comes next.

The Kerala Plus 2 SAY Result 2026 is expected to be declared in July 2026, a few weeks after the exams conclude on July 3. Once declared, the result will be available on the official DHSE Kerala websites dhsekerala.gov.in and keralaresults.nic.in and the updated marks can be used for ongoing undergraduate admissions and counselling in the same academic year.
