PSSOU Chhattisgarh B.Ed, D.El.Ed Admission 2026 is now open for the 2026-27 academic session at Pt. Sundarlal Sharma (Open) University Chhattisgarh (PSSOU). Applications for the Pre B.Ed and Pre D.El.Ed Entrance Exam started on March 25, 2026, and the last date to apply is June 12, 2026 (midnight). The entrance exam is scheduled for June 28, 2026.

Pt. Sundarlal Sharma (Open) University Chhattisgarh (PSSOU) offers two-year distance mode B.Ed and D.El.Ed programmes. Admission is based on the Pre B.Ed / Pre D.El.Ed Entrance Examination followed by counselling. This is an excellent opportunity for working professionals and students seeking teacher training through open and distance learning.

How to Apply for PSSOU B.Ed / D.El.Ed Admission 2026

  • Visit the official website pssou.ac.in or portal https://pssou.net/
  • Click on the B.Ed / D.El.Ed Admission 2026 link.
  • Register and fill the online application form carefully.
  • Upload required documents and pay the application fee.
  • Submit the form and take a printout.
  • Submit the hard copy of the application (if required) to the university office.

PSSOU Pre B.Ed & Pre D.El.Ed Entrance Exam Pattern

The entrance exam is conducted offline for both B.Ed and D.El.Ed.

Part A (Common – 80 Marks): General Hindi Comprehension, General English Comprehension, Logical & Analytical Reasoning, Educational & General Awareness, Teaching Learning & The School.

Part B (Subject Competence – 20 Marks): Choose any one – Science / Mathematics / Social Science.

Total: 100 Marks
