How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official website https://sssb.punjab.gov.in and click on the "Apply Online" link available on the homepage.
  • 2Candidates who do not have a Login/User ID must click on "Registration" and fill in their details to generate a User ID and Password. The User ID will be the Email ID provided during registration.
  • 3After successful registration, login using the User ID (Email ID) and Password and proceed with the online application.
  • 4Fill in all personal, educational, and required information correctly and completely. Upload a passport-size photograph (or capture a live photo via camera) and upload a scanned copy of the signature. Applications without photograph and signature will be treated as incomplete and rejected.
  • 5Click on "Save Draft" or "Next", then click on "Attach Annexure" and upload scanned copies of 10th class certificate, educational qualification certificates, and other mandatory documents in PDF format as indicated on the portal.
  • 6After uploading documents, click "Save Documents" and then click "Make Payment" to pay the prescribed online fee.
  • 7After successful fee payment, click "Submit" to complete the application process. The complete Application Form will be displayed on the screen - candidates must take a printout and keep it for future reference.
  • 8The Application Form will be accepted only after successful fee payment. A draft saved form can be viewed and completed later via "Menu > View Status of Application > View Incomplete Application".
  • 9Candidates must fill the online form carefully noting the category and category code, as the category and category code cannot be changed at any stage after submission.
  • 10Reservation benefits are available only to residents of Punjab. A Domicile/Residence Certificate (not older than 5 years) is mandatory for every candidate irrespective of category.
  • 11SC/BC/Ex-Servicemen/Sports/EWS certificates must be as per the prevailing instructions of the Punjab Government.
  • 12Women candidates will be given reservation benefits as per Punjab Civil Services (Reservation of Posts for Women) Rules, 2020 and amendments thereof.
  • 13Ex-Servicemen category posts will first be filled by Ex-Serviceman Self candidates; only if Ex-Serviceman Self candidates are not available will these posts be filled by Ex-Serviceman Dependent candidates.
  • 14Candidates currently in Central/State Government service must apply through proper channel; the application must be received through proper channel failing which candidature will be rejected.
  • 15Fee once paid will not be refunded under any circumstances. No fee waiver or exemption will be given to any candidate.
  • 16Applications received after the closing date, incomplete applications, or applications submitted through any mode other than the official website will be rejected.
  • 17Appearing in the written examination, having one's name in the merit list, or being called for counselling does not confer any right to recommendation for a post. Only candidates whose eligibility is fully established will be recommended.
  • 18No TA/DA will be paid to candidates for appearing in the written examination or counselling.
  • 19For further information, candidates may contact the Board on working days during office hours at: Telephone No. 0172-2298000 (Extension No. 5106 and 5107), 0172-2298083, Helpline No. 99885-80229 and 96469-32955.
  • 20The Board reserves the right to correct any error noticed in this advertisement or recruitment process.

FAQs

Q1What is SSSB Punjab Assistant District Attorney Recruitment 2026?
A
SSSB Punjab Assistant District Attorney Recruitment 2026 is a state‑level recruitment drive by Subordinate Services Selection Board (SSSB), Punjab to fill 170 Group‑B posts of Assistant District Attorney (ADA) in the Punjab Justice / Law Department under Advertisement No. 09 of 2026.
Q2How many vacancies are released for Punjab ADA Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 170 vacancies, including backlog posts, have been announced for Assistant District Attorney (ADA), with reservation for various categories such as General, SC (Mazhabi & Balmiki, Ramdasia & Others), BC, EWS, Ex‑Servicemen, Sports and Persons with Disabilities. Out of the total posts, 56 are reserved for women candidates as per Punjab’s reservation rules.
Q3What are the important dates for SSSB Punjab Assistant District Attorney 2026?
A
The notification was released on 24 April 2026 and online applications will start from 30 April 2026 at sssb.punjab.gov.in. The last date to apply online and pay the application fee is 20 May 2026, as mentioned in detailed job alerts based on the advertisement.
Q4What is the eligibility and educational qualification for Punjab ADA Recruitment 2026?
A
Applicants must possess a Bachelor of Law (LLB – Professional Degree) from a recognized university, or be Barristers of England or Ireland, or members of the Faculty of Advocates in Scotland, eligible for enrolment as an Advocate under the Advocates Act, 1961, with at least two years of practice at the Bar. Additionally, knowledge of Punjabi is mandatory, typically proven by passing Matriculation with Punjabi as a subject or an equivalent qualification.
Q5What is the age limit for SSSB Punjab Assistant District Attorney 2026?
A
The basic age limit for Punjab ADA Recruitment 2026 is 18 to 37 years for general category candidates as on 01 January 2026. Relaxations in the upper age limit apply for SC, BC, government employees, ex‑servicemen, persons with disabilities, widows and certain other categories as per Punjab Government rules.
Q6What is the salary for Assistant District Attorney in Punjab under this recruitment?
A
The initial pay for the post of Assistant District Attorney (Group‑B) under SSSB Punjab is ₹35,400 per month as per the 7th Central Pay Commission pay matrix. Some portals also mention an approximate in‑hand salary of around ₹55,000 with allowances, though the basic pay in the notification is ₹35,400.
Q7What is the application fee for SSSB Punjab ADA Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee structure generally follows previous SSSB Punjab patterns, where SC/BC candidates of Punjab pay a lower fee, Ex‑Servicemen/EWS/PwD/LDESM pay a nominal fee and all other categories pay a higher fee bracket; the short notice confirms that category‑wise fee is applicable and non‑refundable. Candidates must pay the fee online only; applications without successful fee payment are treated as incomplete.
Q8What is the selection process for SSSB Punjab Assistant District Attorney 2026?
A
Selection will be based on an Objective Type Multiple Choice written examination followed by counselling and document verification, with no interview component mentioned in the short notice. The final merit list will be prepared on the basis of marks obtained in the written exam only, subject to fulfilment of eligibility and successful verification of documents.
Q9What is the exam pattern and negative marking for Punjab ADA written exam?
A
The ADA exam is an objective‑type test where each question carries one mark and there is negative marking of ¼ mark for each wrong answer, as per recent ADA recruitment pattern in Punjab. The paper primarily focuses on law subjects along with certain general topics, and the detailed syllabus and pattern will be shared in the full notification.
Q10How can I apply online for SSSB Punjab Assistant District Attorney Recruitment 2026?
A
Eligible candidates can apply online by visiting the official SSSB Punjab website sssb.punjab.gov.in, registering to generate a User ID and Password, filling the ADA application form, uploading required documents, and paying the prescribed fee within the application window. Applicants must carefully select their category and category code, as changes will not be allowed later, and should keep a printout of the submitted form and fee receipt for future reference.
