JKSSB Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

Selection for all advertised posts will be made on the basis of merit obtained in the Written/OMR Based Examination only. In case of a large number of applications, JKSSB reserves the right to apply shortlisting criteria as deemed appropriate. Stage 1 – Written/OMR Based Examination The examination will consist of Objective Type Multiple Choice Questions set in English Language only. There will be negative marking of one-fourth of marks for each wrong answer. The date and venue of the examination shall be notified separately on the official website jkssb.nic.in. Stage 2 – Document Verification (DV) Candidates shortlisted on the basis of written examination merit will be called for Document Verification. Original documents along with self-attested photocopies must be produced. Failure to appear or produce valid documents on the scheduled DV date shall lead to cancellation of candidature.

Who should NOT apply for JKSSB Recruitment 2026?

Candidates who are not Domiciles of the Union Territory of Jammu & Kashmir or do not possess a valid Domicile Certificate as on 21-07-2026. Candidates who do not possess the exact essential qualification prescribed for the respective post as per Annexure-A. Candidates expecting reservation benefits without a valid category certificate in the prescribed format as on the cut-off date. OBC candidates belonging to the Creamy Layer. Candidates claiming equivalent qualifications without a valid Equivalence Certificate from the competent authority. Candidates who provide incomplete, incorrect, or unverifiable information in the online application form. Candidates who cannot satisfy medical fitness or document verification requirements at the time of appointment.
