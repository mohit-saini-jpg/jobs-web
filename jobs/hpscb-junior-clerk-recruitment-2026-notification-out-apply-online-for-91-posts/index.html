HPSCB Junior Clerk Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 91 Posts

Posts: 91 (PACS Quota: 74 + OCS Quota: 17) Last Date: 29-05-2026 Online

Short Information

The Himachal Pradesh State Co-operative Bank Ltd. (HPSCB) has officially announced the Junior Clerk Recruitment 2026 with 91 openings. The detailed notification was published on 09 May 2026 at www.hpscb.bank.in. Candidates who meet the age criteria of 18–45 years (with relaxations for reserved categories) and hold a Graduate degree in any discipline along with the required cooperative society experience can now submit their online applications from 09 May 2026 to 29 May 2026. Age Limit

DetailsCandidates must be above 18 years and below 45 years of age as on 01-01-2026. Candidates born between 02-01-1981 and 01-01-2008 (both days inclusive) are eligible. Age relaxation is applicable only to Bonafide Himachali candidates of the respective reserved categories. Selected PwBD candidates will have to submit a Medical Certificate from a competent authority confirming physical fitness to perform duties.

Syllabus

DetailsThe Online Examination consists of 5 sections - Reasoning, Numerical Ability, English, General Awareness, and H.P. Cooperative Societies Act and Rules - each carrying 30 marks (total 150 marks, 150 minutes). The level of examination and syllabus is in accordance with the minimum educational qualification criteria i.e. Graduation level. The question paper is bilingual (English and Hindi) and is of objective type MCQ. There is no negative marking. Preference will be given to candidates possessing knowledge of customs, traditions, languages, and culture of Himachal Pradesh and the test shall include questions on such subjects.

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the Bank's website www.hpscb.bank.in by clicking the "APPLY ONLINE" option. Hard copy applications or applications by any other mode will not be accepted. The online application window is open from 09-05-2026 to 29-05-2026. Candidates must register on the portal, make fee payment online, and upload all required documents. No change will be possible after clicking the "COMPLETE REGISTRATION" button. Candidates are advised to keep their valid personal email ID and mobile number active till the completion of the entire recruitment process.
  • Before applying, candidates must have ready: scanned photograph (4.5 cm × 3.5 cm), signature (with black ink on white paper), left thumb impression (on white paper with black or blue ink), and a hand-written declaration in English on white paper with black ink. Signature in CAPITAL LETTERS will NOT be accepted. The hand-written declaration text is: "I, _______ (Name of the candidate), hereby declare that all the information submitted by me in the application form is correct, true and valid. I will present the supporting documents as and when required."
  • Documents Required to be Produced at Document Verification (when called):
  • Graduation degree certificate and mark sheets from recognized University
  • Matriculation and 10+2 certificates from HP school/institution (if applicable)
  • Experience Certificate countersigned by Assistant Registrar, Cooperative Societies of H.P. - with copy of managing committee resolution for initial appointment and statutory audit report pages (3 years for PACS quota; 5 years for OCS quota)
  • Age proof (10th/12th class mark sheet/certificate)
  • Bonafide Himachali Certificate from competent authority (for reserved category candidates)
  • SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD/WFF/IRDP certificate from competent authority (if applicable)
  • Non-Creamy Layer (NCL) Certificate (for OBC candidates) - in latest prescribed format
  • EWS Income and Asset Certificate issued by Deputy Commissioner/SDO (Civil)/Tehsildar (for EWS candidates) - family income below Rs. 4 Lakhs per annum
  • PwBD Certificate (minimum 40% disability) + Medical Certificate from competent authority confirming fitness for duty (for PwBD candidates)
  • Discharge Certificate (for Ex-Servicemen candidates)
  • WFF Certificate (for Ward of Freedom Fighter candidates)
  • IRDP/BPL/Antodaya Certificate countersigned by Block Development Officer (if applicable)
  • NOC and Good Character Certificate from current employer (for candidates in regular/confirmed service)
  • e-Receipt and printout of online application form
  • Apply Online at: https://ibpsreg.ibps.in/hpscbmar26/

Important Instructions

  • All reserved category benefits including age relaxation and fee concession apply only to Bonafide Himachali candidates. Candidates must possess valid experience certificate from their cooperative society - false experience certificates will result in summary cancellation of candidature irrespective of qualifying the online examination. Experience certificates must be issued by the concerned Inspector of Society and countersigned by the Assistant Registrar, Cooperative Societies of H.P. supported by managing committee resolution and statutory audit report pages. No hard copy applications will be accepted - online submission only via www.hpscb.bank.in. Application fee is non-refundable; fee once paid will not be refunded under any circumstances. Signature in CAPITAL LETTERS and hand-written declaration not in candidate's own handwriting or not in English will be treated as invalid. Candidates already in regular (confirmed) service must submit NOC and good character certificate from current employer at document verification - absence of NOC will result in cancellation of candidature. There is NO negative marking in the online examination; minimum 40% marks required to qualify. Candidates must appear in person at document verification - non-appearance will lead to cancellation of candidature without notice. OBC candidates must submit a valid Non-Creamy Layer certificate; EWS candidates must have family gross annual income below Rs. 4 Lakhs per annum. PwBD candidates must have minimum 40% degree of disability and must submit Medical Certificate from competent authority confirming fitness for duty at selected post. Wards of Ex-Servicemen are NOT eligible for posts advertised for Ex-Servicemen. Selected candidates will be on probation for a period of two years (extendable up to maximum 3 years). No TA/DA is admissible for appearing in the online examination. Selected candidates will be posted anywhere in the area of operation of H.P. State Cooperative Bank in Himachal Pradesh. For any dispute related to selection process, the decision of the Registrar, Co-operative Societies of Himachal Pradesh shall be binding and final; jurisdiction shall be Shimla (HP) only. Candidates are advised to visit www.hpscb.bank.in regularly for all updates regarding the recruitment process.
