Pay Scale

Syllabus

Details

The Online Examination consists of 5 sections - Reasoning, Numerical Ability, English, General Awareness, and H.P. Cooperative Societies Act and Rules - each carrying 30 marks (total 150 marks, 150 minutes). The level of examination and syllabus is in accordance with the minimum educational qualification criteria i.e. Graduation level. The question paper is bilingual (English and Hindi) and is of objective type MCQ. There is no negative marking. Preference will be given to candidates possessing knowledge of customs, traditions, languages, and culture of Himachal Pradesh and the test shall include questions on such subjects.

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the Bank's website www.hpscb.bank.in by clicking the "APPLY ONLINE" option. Hard copy applications or applications by any other mode will not be accepted. The online application window is open from 09-05-2026 to 29-05-2026. Candidates must register on the portal, make fee payment online, and upload all required documents. No change will be possible after clicking the "COMPLETE REGISTRATION" button. The hand-written declaration text is: "I, _______ (Name of the candidate), hereby declare that all the information submitted by me in the application form is correct, true and valid. Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is HPSCB Junior Clerk Recruitment 2026?

HPSCB Junior Clerk Recruitment 2026 is a regular recruitment drive by The Himachal Pradesh State Co‑operative Bank Ltd. (HPSCB), Shimla, to fill 91 Junior Clerk posts under PACS/OCS quota through an online examination conducted by IBPS, Mumbai.

Q2. How many vacancies are announced for HPSCB Junior Clerk 2026?

The HPSCB Junior Clerk 2026 notification announces a total of 91 vacancies, out of which 74 posts are reserved for Trained Secretaries of Primary Agricultural Cooperative Societies (PACS quota) and 17 posts are for employees of other Cooperative Societies and PACS employees not covered as Trained Secretaries (OCS quota).

Q3. What are the application dates for HPSCB Junior Clerk Recruitment 2026?

Eligible candidates can submit their online applications for HPSCB Junior Clerk Recruitment 2026 from 09 May 2026 to 29 May 2026, and the online fee payment window is also available during the same period on the official HPSCB website.

Q4. What is the age limit for HPSCB Junior Clerk 2026?

Candidates must be between 18 and 45 years of age as on 01 January 2026, with upper age relaxations applicable to SC, ST, OBC, PwBD, Ex‑Servicemen, Ward of Freedom Fighter, and other reserved categories of Himachal Pradesh as per prevailing government rules.

Q5. What are the educational qualification and experience requirements for HPSCB Junior Clerk posts?

Applicants must be Graduates in any discipline from a recognized University and should have passed Matriculation and 10+2 from a school/institution located in Himachal Pradesh (this schooling condition is relaxed for Bonafide Himachali candidates); additionally, PACS quota candidates need at least 3 years continuous service as Trained Secretaries in PACS, while OCS quota candidates require a minimum of 5 years continuous service in other cooperative societies or PACS not covered as Trained Secretaries.

Q6. What is the selection process for HPSCB Junior Clerk Recruitment 2026?

The selection process consists of a single‑stage online examination of 150 marks and 150 minutes followed by document verification/scrutiny, with no personal interview; final selection is based purely on marks obtained in the online exam, subject to successful document verification.

Q7. What is the exam pattern for the HPSCB Junior Clerk 2026 online test?

The HPSCB Junior Clerk online exam comprises 150 objective‑type questions for 150 marks, divided equally into five sections—Reasoning, Numerical Ability, English, General Awareness (including HP GK), and H.P. Cooperative Societies Act & Rules—each having 30 questions and 30 marks, with a composite duration of 150 minutes and no negative marking.

Q8. What is the application fee for HPSCB Junior Clerk Recruitment 2026?

The application fee is Rs. 1,000 for General and OBC candidates of Himachal Pradesh and Rs. 800 for EWS, SC, ST, IRDP, BPL, Antodaya and all female candidates of Himachal Pradesh, payable only through the online payment gateway on the HPSCB application portal and non‑refundable under any circumstances.
