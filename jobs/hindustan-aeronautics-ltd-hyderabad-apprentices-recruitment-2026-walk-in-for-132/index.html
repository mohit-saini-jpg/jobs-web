Job Overview

The Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Avionics Division, Hyderabad has officially announced the Technician (Diploma) Apprentice, Engineering Graduate Apprentice, and General Stream Graduate Apprentice Recruitment 2026 with 132 openings. Candidates who meet the eligibility criteria of having passed the respective Diploma, Engineering, or General Graduation within the last 5 years can now attend the walk-in interviews from 02-06-2026 to 05-06-2026. Important Instructions

Selection is purely merit-based on CGPA/Percentage of marks in Diploma/Engineering Degree/Graduation in General Stream; there will be no written test or interview. Pre-registration on the NATS portal (https://nats.education.gov.in) is mandatory before appearing for the walk-in interview. Only candidates who have passed the qualifying examination and possess Original/Provisional Certificate are eligible. The gap between the date of contract after joining apprenticeship and the date of passing the qualification should not be more than 5 years for all categories. Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing Apprenticeship Training or are registered for Apprenticeship Training under the Apprentices Act 1961 elsewhere are not eligible. Candidates who have already joined/are pursuing/are about to join any regular courses for higher education during the stipulated Training Period need not apply. OBC candidates must produce a valid Non-Creamy Layer certificate. Merit list of selected candidates will be published on www.hal-india.co.in on 30th June 2026. Date of joining/reporting for the Apprenticeship is 27th July 2026; failure to report by the scheduled date will result in forfeiture of candidature. Submission of false/incorrect information or suppression of any facts at any stage will result in immediate cancellation/termination of candidature. Canvassing in any form shall lead to disqualification of the candidature. HAL reserves the right to alter the allocation of quota for trades at any time and to cancel the advertisement/selection process; HAL Management's decision regarding selection will be final. Candidates must make their own arrangements for accommodation and travelling for the walk-in process and joining. For further queries/clarifications, email: [email protected] or contact: 040-23778283 (09:00 Hrs to 16:00 Hrs).

FAQs

Q1Q1. How many vacancies are there in HAL Hyderabad Diploma & Graduate Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Avionics Division, Hyderabad has notified 132 apprentice vacancies including 30 Diploma Technician, 34 General Stream Graduate, and 68 Engineering Graduate Apprentice posts for FY 2026-27.
Q2Q2. What are the walk-in dates for HAL Hyderabad Diploma, Engineering and General Graduate Apprentices 2026?
A
The walk-in schedule is category-wise: Engineering Graduates (ECE, Civil, Aeronautical) on 02 June 2026, Engineering Graduates (EEE, CSE, Mechanical) on 03 June 2026, General Stream Graduates on 04 June 2026, and Diploma Technicians on 05 June 2026, all reporting at 9:00 AM.
Q3Q3. What is the selection process for HAL Hyderabad Diploma & Graduate Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection is purely merit-based on CGPA/percentage of marks in the qualifying Diploma, Engineering Degree or General Stream Degree, and there is no written test or interview for these apprentice posts.
Q4Q4. Is NATS registration mandatory for HAL Hyderabad Diploma and Graduate Apprentice 2026?
A
Yes, pre-registration on the NATS portal at https://nats.education.gov.in is mandatory for all Diploma, Engineering Graduate, and General Stream Graduate candidates before attending the walk-in.
Q5Q5. What is the stipend for HAL Hyderabad Diploma & Graduate Apprentices 2026-27?
A
Apprentices will receive a monthly stipend as per the Apprentices Act 1961 and its rules and amendments, with different rates applicable for Diploma and Graduate apprentices as notified by HAL and BoAT.
Q6Q6. What is the eligibility criteria for HAL Hyderabad Engineering Graduate Apprentices 2026?
A
Engineering Graduate Apprentice candidates must have completed a Degree in Engineering/Technology in the relevant branch from a recognized university, with the gap between passing and the apprenticeship contract date not exceeding 5 years.
Q7Q7. Who is not eligible for HAL Hyderabad Diploma & Graduate Apprentice 2026?
A
Candidates are not eligible if they have already undergone or are undergoing apprenticeship training, are registered for apprenticeship elsewhere under the Apprentices Act, or are already pursuing/are about to join regular higher education during the training period.
Q8Q8. When will the HAL Hyderabad Diploma & Graduate Apprentice 2026 merit list be released?
A
The merit list of provisionally shortlisted candidates is scheduled to be published on the official HAL website www.hal-india.co.in on 30 June 2026.
