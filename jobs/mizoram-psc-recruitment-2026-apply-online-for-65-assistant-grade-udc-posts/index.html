How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the Mizoram Public Service Commission online portal (https://mpsconline.mizoram.gov.in/). No other mode of application will be accepted. The online portal is active from 12-05-2026 to 19-06-2026 (up to 4:00 PM). New users must complete one-time registration before 19-06-2026 (4:00 PM).

  • Steps to Apply Online:

  • Step 1: Visit MPSC Online Portal - Go to https://mpsconline.mizoram.gov.in/

  • Step 2: One-Time Registration (for New Users) - If you are a new user, complete one-time registration on the portal before 19-06-2026 (4:00 PM). Existing users can directly login.

  • Step 3: Login to Portal - Login using your registered credentials

  • Step 4: Fill Application Form - Fill the online application form carefully with all required details and choose post preferences (post preference option once exercised is final)

  • Step 5: Upload Documents - Upload scanned copies of all required documents as per specifications

  • Step 6: Pay Application Fee - Pay application fee online (if applicable) through available payment modes. PwBD candidates are exempted from fee payment.

  • Step 7: Submit Application - Review all details carefully and submit the application

  • Step 8: Verify Submission - Verify that your application status shows "Submitted" after payment. Take printout of submitted application for future reference.

  • Documents to be Uploaded with Online Application:

  • HSLC (Class 10th) Marksheet & Certificate

  • Higher Secondary (Class 12th) Marksheet & Certificate

  • Bachelor's Degree Certificate and mark sheets (all semesters/years)

  • Computer Proficiency Certificate (as per Mizoram Group 'A', 'B' & 'C' posts Service/Recruitment (Amendment) Rules, 2023)

  • Mizo Language Proficiency Certificate (if applicable - exemption for those who studied Mizo up to HSLC or above)

  • Age Proof Certificate (10th certificate/Birth certificate)

  • PwBD Certificate issued by competent authority (if claiming PwBD reservation)

  • Ex-Servicemen Certificate (if claiming Ex-Servicemen reservation)

  • SC/ST certificate (if applicable)

  • Any other relevant documents as mentioned in the notification

  • Apply Online at: https://mpsconline.mizoram.gov.in/
