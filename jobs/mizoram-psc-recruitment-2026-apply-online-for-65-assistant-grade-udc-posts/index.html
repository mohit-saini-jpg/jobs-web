How to Apply

  • 1Applications must be submitted online only through the Mizoram Public Service Commission online portal (https://mpsconline.mizoram.gov.in/). No other mode of application will be accepted. The online portal is active from 12-05-2026 to 19-06-2026 (up to 4:00 PM). New users must complete one-time registration before 19-06-2026 (4:00 PM).
  • 2Steps to Apply Online:
  • 3Step 1: Visit MPSC Online Portal - Go to https://mpsconline.mizoram.gov.in/
  • 4Step 2: One-Time Registration (for New Users) - If you are a new user, complete one-time registration on the portal before 19-06-2026 (4:00 PM). Existing users can directly login.
  • 5Step 3: Login to Portal - Login using your registered credentials
  • 6Step 4: Fill Application Form - Fill the online application form carefully with all required details and choose post preferences (post preference option once exercised is final)
  • 7Step 5: Upload Documents - Upload scanned copies of all required documents as per specifications
  • 8Step 6: Pay Application Fee - Pay application fee online (if applicable) through available payment modes. PwBD candidates are exempted from fee payment.
  • 9Step 7: Submit Application - Review all details carefully and submit the application
  • 10Step 8: Verify Submission - Verify that your application status shows "Submitted" after payment. Take printout of submitted application for future reference.
  • 11Documents to be Uploaded with Online Application:
  • 12HSLC (Class 10th) Marksheet & Certificate
  • 13Higher Secondary (Class 12th) Marksheet & Certificate
  • 14Bachelor's Degree Certificate and mark sheets (all semesters/years)
  • 15Computer Proficiency Certificate (as per Mizoram Group 'A', 'B' & 'C' posts Service/Recruitment (Amendment) Rules, 2023)
  • 16Mizo Language Proficiency Certificate (if applicable - exemption for those who studied Mizo up to HSLC or above)
  • 17Age Proof Certificate (10th certificate/Birth certificate)
  • 18PwBD Certificate issued by competent authority (if claiming PwBD reservation)
  • 19Ex-Servicemen Certificate (if claiming Ex-Servicemen reservation)
  • 20SC/ST certificate (if applicable)
  • 21Any other relevant documents as mentioned in the notification
  • 22Apply Online at: https://mpsconline.mizoram.gov.in/
