BVFCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Brahmaputra Valley Fertilizer Corporation Limited has officially released the Apprentice Recruitment 2026 notification (Advt. No. BVFCL/Pers/Apprentice/316) for 38 vacancies across its operational units. Eligible candidates with qualifications such as B.E./B.Tech, Diploma, HSLC + ITI, B.Com, B.Sc, or B.A., aged between 18 to 24 years, can apply online on or before 31 July 2026 via the official BVFCL recruitment portal. Selected candidates will be engaged on an apprenticeship basis with a stipend as per applicable apprenticeship rules and government norms.

BVFCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in BVFCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
Brahmaputra Valley Fertilizer Corporation Limited (BVFCL) has announced a total of 38 vacancies for the Apprentice post. This recruitment drive offers various opportunities across different engineering disciplines and trade apprenticeships, as well as roles in Finance, Production/Chemical Lab, and HR.
Q2What are the important dates for BVFCL Apprentice application?
A
The online application process for BVFCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026 begins on 30 June 2026. The last date for submitting online applications is 31 July 2026. Candidates should ensure they apply within this period to be considered.
Q3What is the age limit for applying to BVFCL Apprentice posts?
A
Candidates applying for the BVFCL Apprentice posts must be between 18 and 24 years of age. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories: 5 years for SC/ST, 3 years for OBC (Non-Creamy Layer), and up to 10 years for PwBD candidates.
Q4What qualifications are required for BVFCL Apprentice positions?
A
A wide range of qualifications are accepted, including B.E./B.Tech in various engineering fields (Chemical, Mechanical, Electrical, Instrumentation, Civil), Diploma in similar disciplines, HSLC with ITI in various trades, B.Com, B.Sc, and B.A degrees.
Q5Is there any application fee for BVFCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
No, there is no application fee for the BVFCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026. Candidates from all categories, including General/OBC and SC/ST, are exempted from paying any application charges.
Q6What is the application mode for BVFCL Apprentice posts?
A
The application mode for the BVFCL Apprentice posts is entirely online. Interested and eligible candidates must submit their applications through the official website of Brahmaputra Valley Fertilizer Corporation Limited within the specified dates.
Q7What is the selection process for BVFCL Apprentice Recruitment?
A
The selection process involves registration on the NATS portal (for Graduate/Diploma) or Apprenticeship portal (for ITI), evaluation of aggregate marks in Matriculation and respective discipline, a personal interview, and a pre-employment medical fitness examination.
Q8Where will the BVFCL Apprentice posts be located?
A
The Brahmaputra Valley Fertilizer Corporation Limited (BVFCL) is located in Namrup, Assam. Therefore, selected candidates for the Apprentice positions will be placed in Namrup, Assam, for their training period.
Q9What is the official website to apply for BVFCL Apprentice jobs?
A
The official website for Brahmaputra Valley Fertilizer Corporation Limited (BVFCL) is www.bvfcl.com. Candidates can find the official notification and the online application link on this website.
Q10What types of Apprentice posts are available in BVFCL?
A
BVFCL offers various Apprentice posts including Chemical, Mechanical, Electrical, Instrumentation, Civil Engineering (B.E./B.Tech & Diploma), and Trade Apprenticeships like Machinist, Welder, Fitter, Electrician, Plumber, along with Finance, Prod/Chemical Lab, and HR roles.
