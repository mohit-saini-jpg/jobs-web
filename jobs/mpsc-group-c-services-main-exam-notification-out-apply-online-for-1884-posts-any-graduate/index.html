Selection Process

  • Selection is through a written Main Examination (400 marks
  • 2 papers) with negative marking. Prelim-qualified candidates who apply for the Main Exam will be considered. Final selection is based on merit in the Main Exam
  • category-wise
  • post-wise
  • combined with preference order (especially for Lipik-Tanklekhak). Document verification follows shortlisting.
  • Stage 1 - Main Written Examination (Mukhya Pariksha)
  • The Main Examination consists of 2 papers (total 400 marks). Paper 1: Marathi (50 Qs
  • 100 marks) and English (50 Qs
  • 100 marks) - Baaravi / 12th standard level
  • medium Marathi and English
  • objective multi-choice
  • 1 hour. Paper 2: General Studies and Intelligence Test (100 Qs
  • 200 marks) - Padvi / graduation level
  • 1 hour. Negative marking: 25% (1/4 mark) deducted per wrong answer. If more than one answer is given for a question
  • it is treated as wrong and 25% marks deducted. If final marks fall below the minimum cut-off after deductions
  • the candidate's marks are calculated as zero (not negative).
  • Stage 2 - Merit List and Preference Order (Lipik-Tanklekhak)
  • For all posts: a General Merit List (Sarvasadharan Gunvatta Yadi) is prepared based on total marks in the Combined Main Exam
  • post-wise. For Lipik-Tanklekhak: after the General Merit List is published
  • all candidates on the list must re-submit their Appointing Authority-wise preference order within the stipulated period through the MPSC online portal. If preference order is not submitted within the deadline
  • it will be treated as if no preference was given. Final Recommendation List is prepared considering the preference orders submitted.
  • Stage 3 - Document Verification (Pramanpatra Padataalni)
  • Shortlisted candidates are called for document verification before or after the written exam result
  • as decided by MPSC. Original documents along with self-attested photocopies must be produced. Only documents claimed in the application will be verified. Documents not produced at verification will not be accepted later and no extension will be given. Candidates failing to appear for verification will be treated as not qualified. Final recommendation/appointment is subject to verification of all original documents by the appointing authority.
  • Stage 4 - Physical / Medical Examination (Duyyam Nirikshak Only)
  • Recommended candidates for Duyyam Nirikshak
  • Rajya Utpadan Shulk post must meet prescribed physical standards and will be medically examined by the government-appointed competent authority before appointment.

How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply online through the MPSC portal (https://mpsconline.gov.in) only. No other mode of application will be accepted. Before applying, candidates must ensure they have qualified the Group-C Prelim 2025 and completed KYC on the MPSC portal. Detailed application instructions are available at https://mpsconline.gov.in and https://mpsc.gov.in.
  • Documents Required to be Scanned and Uploaded (PDF format, min 50KB – max 400KB each):
  • Proof of name at birth (SSC / equivalent certificate)
  • Age proof
  • Educational qualification certificates
  • Social category (Magasavargiya) certificate
  • Economically Weaker Section (EWS) certificate (Financial Year 2025-26)
  • Valid Non-Creamy Layer (Non-Kimiyer) certificate (Financial Year 2025-26)
  • PwBD (Divyang) certificate from competent authority
  • Ex-Servicemen (Maaji Sainik) certificate
  • Sports reservation eligibility proof - Sports Certificate required at document verification
  • Orphan (Anath) reservation certificate
  • Project Affected (Prakalpagrast) certificate
  • Earthquake Affected (Bhookampgrast) certificate
  • Part-time employee (Anshkalin Padvi) certificate
  • Category change / inter-category claim certificate (Araakhiv Mahila, Magasavargiya, etc.)
  • Proof of SC/ST name change if applicable
  • Marathi language knowledge proof
  • Small Family Declaration (Laghan Kutumbache Pratidnya)
  • Typewriting certificate (Marathi and/or English - for Kar Sahayak and Lipik-Tanklekhak as applicable)
  • Work experience certificate (if applicable for relevant post)
  • Documents to be Produced at Document Verification:
  • All original documents as listed above (plus self-attested photocopies)
  • Printout of submitted application form
  • Valid photo identity proof (Aadhaar / PAN Card / Passport / Smart Card issued by DGA - original Aadhaar OTP or e-Aadhaar with colour printed photo mandatory)
  • If name changed (marriage etc.) - notarized affidavit and relevant certificate from Marriage Registrar or Gazette Officer
  • Sports certificate originals for sports quota candidates (verified by Sports Authority)
  • Any other document claimed in the application
  • Documents not submitted at the time of application cannot be added later. Candidates who do not produce originals at document verification will be disqualified and no extension will be granted under any circumstances.

Important Instructions

  • Only candidates who have qualified the Maharashtra Gat-Ka Seva Sanyukt Purv Pariksha – 2025 (result declared 04 May 2026) are eligible to apply for this Main Exam. Non-qualified prelim candidates' applications will not be accepted. All reserved category (SC/ST/OBC/NT/DT/SBC) and other horizontal reservation claims (PwBD, Ex-Servicemen, Sports, Orphan, Project-Affected etc.) must be properly supported by valid documents; category claims cannot be changed after application submission under any circumstances. For Lipik-Tanklekhak post, Appointing Authority-wise preference order must be submitted at the time of application. After merit list is published, candidates will again need to re-confirm preference order within the stipulated window - failure to do so will forfeit their allocation right. Candidates must mandatorily select options (Vikalp) for all posts they wish to be considered for. Posts not selected during application will not be considered, even if the candidate is otherwise eligible. Negative marking applies: 25% (1/4) marks deducted for each wrong answer. Multiple answers to a single question also count as wrong. KYC completion on MPSC portal is mandatory before applying (per order ADR-2425/SIR.06/2025/MA.T., dated 20 June 2025). Application fee is non-refundable. After payment, fee cannot be adjusted for any other exam or selection process. Admit card (Hall Ticket) will be available on MPSC online portal 7 days before the exam. Candidates must download and bring it to the exam. Candidates must bring their own identity proof (Aadhaar / PAN / Passport / Smart Card) to the exam centre. Only original Aadhaar with OTP or colour-printed e-Aadhaar (with photo) is accepted - Aadhaar-based identity proof alone is valid without other documents. Once exam centre (district) is selected, it cannot be changed. Requests for centre change will not be entertained under any circumstances. Documents not uploaded at the time of application cannot be submitted later and no extension will be given. Candidates failing to produce originals at document verification will be disqualified. The entire recruitment process will be governed by applicable Maharashtra service entry rules for each respective post/department (as amended from time to time and as per MPSC / State Government circulars). If at any stage it is found that a candidate has given false information, hidden relevant facts, or submitted forged documents, their candidature will be cancelled and legal action will be taken. All corrigenda and updates will be published on the official MPSC website only - candidates must check regularly.
