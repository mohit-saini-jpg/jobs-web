FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Burari Hospital Senior Resident posts?
A
The recruitment is through a walk-in interview. Candidates need to attend the interview on the specified date. There is no separate application deadline mentioned for submission before the walk-in.
Q2Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Senior Resident posts?
A
Candidates must possess an MBBS degree with a Post Graduate degree/DNB/Diploma or equivalent in the concerned specialty from a recognized University/Institute as per the Residency Scheme.
Q3Q3. What is the age limit for the Senior Resident positions?
A
The maximum age limit for all Senior Resident posts is 45 years. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST and OBC candidates as per government rules.
Q4Q4. How many vacancies are available for the Senior Resident posts?
A
There are a total of 4 vacancies for the Senior Resident posts across various departments.
Q5Q5. What is the application fee for the Burari Hospital Senior Resident recruitment?
A
There is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q6Q6. What is the salary structure for the Senior Resident posts?
A
The salary for the Senior Resident posts will be as per the 7th CPC guidelines as adopted by the GNCT of Delhi.
Q7Q7. Which departments have vacancies for Senior Residents?
A
Vacancies are available in Microbiology, ENT, Ophthalmology, and Blood Bank departments.
Q8Q8. What is the mode of application for this recruitment?
A
The mode of application is Walk-in. Candidates need to attend the interview directly with their documents.
Q9Q9. Where is the walk-in interview scheduled to be held?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at Burari Hospital, Delhi. The exact address is 110084.
Q10Q10. What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates should carry all original documents, self-attested photocopies of these documents, and recent passport-size photographs.
