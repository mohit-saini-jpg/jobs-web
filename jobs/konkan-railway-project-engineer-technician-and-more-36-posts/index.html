FAQs

Q1What is the mode of selection for KRCL Recruitment 2026?
A
The mode of selection is Walk-In Interview. After preliminary screening of applications, provisionally eligible candidates will be interviewed by a nominated committee. Group Discussion or written test may be introduced depending on the number of candidates.
Q2Is there any application fee for KRCL Recruitment 2026?
A
The notification does not mention any application fee for KRCL Recruitment 2026. Candidates should check the official website at konkanrailway.com for the latest updates in this regard.
Q3What is the period of contract for KRCL posts?
A
The initial contract period is ONE year. It can be extended further up to 5 years based on satisfactory performance, mutual consent and requirement of KRCL.
Q4Will selected candidates be absorbed as regular KRCL employees?
A
No. The selected candidates will not be absorbed in KRCL regular service. They shall not have any right to claim for permanent absorption in KRCL and will be required to give an undertaking in writing to that effect.
Q5Is there any TA/DA for attending the walk-in interview?
A
No train/bus fare/TA/DA shall be payable by the Corporation for attending the walk-in interview. Candidates should come prepared to stay for a minimum of 2 days at their own cost, if required.
Q6What is the date of walk-in interview for KRCL Recruitment 2026?
A
The walk-in interview for Project Engineer, Yard Operations Supervisor and Shunting Operator posts is on 22 June 2026, and for Technician (Mechanical & Electrical) posts it is on 23 June 2026 at Visakhapatnam.
