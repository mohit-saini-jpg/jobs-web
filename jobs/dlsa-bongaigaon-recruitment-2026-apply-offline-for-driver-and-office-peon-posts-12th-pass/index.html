FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the DLSA Bongaigaon recruitment?
The last date for submission of applications is 11th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the authority before this date.
Q2. How many vacancies are available for Driver and Office Peon posts?
There are a total of 2 vacancies, with one post for Driver (Contractual) and one post for Office Peon (Contractual).
Q3. What is the minimum educational qualification required for the Office Peon post?
The minimum educational qualification for the Office Peon post is Class VIII passed. Candidates with Higher Secondary or above are not eligible.

The age limit for all posts is between 18 years and 40 years, with relaxation applicable as per government rules.
Q5. What is the salary offered for the Driver and Office Peon positions?
The Driver post offers a fixed pay of ₹ 15,000/- per month, and the Office Peon post offers a fixed pay of ₹ 10,000/- per month.
Q6. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) in the official notification.
Q7. What is the selection process for these contractual posts?
The selection process involves a Skill Test followed by a Viva-Voce for the Driver post, and a Viva-Voce for the Office Peon post.
Q8. How can I apply for the DLSA Bongaigaon recruitment?
Applications must be submitted offline by post. Download the application form from the official website, fill it, and send it along with required documents before the last date.
Q9. Where can I find the official notification and application form?
The official notification and application form can be downloaded from the official website of District Legal Services Authority, Bongaigaon: https://bongaigaon.dcourts.gov.in.
