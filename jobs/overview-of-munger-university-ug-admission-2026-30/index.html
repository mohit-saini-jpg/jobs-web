Munger University has started the online application process for admission to 4-Year Undergraduate programmes (BA, BSc, BCom under CBCS) for the academic session 2026-30. Eligible candidates can apply online through the Samarth admission portal from 29 April 2026 to 25 May 2026, with admission based on Class 12 merit marks.

Candidates can check complete details including eligibility criteria, application fee, important dates, required documents, and the step-by-step online application process to complete the admission form successfully.

How to Apply for Munger University UG Admission 2026

  • Visit the official admission portal: mungeruniversityadm.samarth.edu.in
  • Click on “Apply For Admissions” and register with valid details.
  • Fill the online application form with personal, academic and contact information.
  • Upload scanned photograph, signature and educational documents.
  • Pay the application fee of Rs. 1000/- online.
  • Submit the form and take a printout for future reference.

Documents Required for Munger University UG Admission

  • Scanned Photograph (less than 500 KB)
  • Scanned Signature (less than 500 KB)
  • Class 10th Marksheet
  • Class 12th Marksheet
  • Caste/Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile Certificate (if required)
