Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for ANGRAU Young Professional-I recruitment?

The walk-in interview for the Young Professional-I post at ANGRAU is scheduled for 11th June 2026. Candidates are advised to reach the venue on time.

A
The required educational qualification is a 4 years Bachelor's Degree in Agriculture or a Diploma in Agriculture from an ICAR accredited University/College.

A
The age limit for the Young Professional-I post is between 21 years and 35 years. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST/women candidates.

There is one vacancy available for the Young Professional-I post at Acharya N.G. Ranga Agricultural University.

There is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.

The selection process will involve a Walk-in Interview followed by Document Verification.
