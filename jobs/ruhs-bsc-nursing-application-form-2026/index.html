How to Apply for RUHS BSc Nursing 2026

  • Visit the official website ruhsraj.org or the dedicated RUHS CUET application portal.
  • Click on “RUHS CUET 2026 Apply Online” or “B.Sc Nursing Application Form” link.
  • Register with your basic details such as name, date of birth, mobile number, and email ID.
  • Login using the generated credentials and fill the application form carefully.
  • Upload scanned copies of your recent photograph and signature in the prescribed format.
  • Pay the required application fee online through debit/credit card or net banking.
  • Review all the entered details, submit the form, and download/print the confirmation page.

Documents Required for RUHS BSc Nursing 2026

  • Recent passport-size photograph (maximum 100 KB)
  • Candidate’s signature (maximum 50 KB)
  • Class 10 and Class 12 mark sheets and certificates
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile certificate (if required)
  • Valid ID proof

RUHS BSc Nursing Syllabus 2026

The syllabus is based on Class 12 NCERT curriculum. It includes:

  • Physics, Chemistry & Biology: Complete syllabus of Class 12
  • English: Grammar, Vocabulary, Comprehension and Basic Communication Skills

Major topics cover Human Physiology, Cell Structure, Laws of Motion, Organic Chemistry, Genetics, etc.
