TS EdCET 2026 Counselling - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 17/06/2026

TS EdCET Counselling 2026 is conducted by Kakatiya University, Warangal, on behalf of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TGCHE), and the process begins in June 2026 at the official portal, edcetadm.tgche.ac.in. The counselling involves online registration, certificate verification, web option exercise, seat allotment, and final admission through fee payment and document submission at the allotted college.

This article breaks down the TS EdCET 2026 counselling schedule, the step-by-step registration and admission process, the list of documents candidates must carry, and how the seat allotment rounds work, so qualified candidates can plan their B.Ed admission without missing a deadline.

TS EdCET 2026 Counselling Dates

This section covers the expected counselling timeline for TS EdCET 2026, broken down into Phase 1, Phase 2, and spot round events, so candidates know which stage comes next and when to act.

TS EdCET counselling 2026 is conducted in two regular phases followed by a spot round for any seats that remain vacant. While the authority has indicated that all major events for Phase 1 fall in June 2026, Phase 2 events are scheduled for July 2026, and spot admissions take place in August 2026, exact day-wise dates are released by Kakatiya University closer to each event through official notifications on edcetadm.tgche.ac.in. Candidates should keep checking the portal regularly, since slot-booking and verification windows can be short.

TS EdCET Counselling Process 2026: Step-by-Step

This section explains how the counselling actually works once a candidate qualifies the entrance test, covering registration, verification, web options, and final admission in sequence.

Only candidates who feature in the TS EdCET 2026 merit list, released by Kakatiya University, are eligible to take part in the counselling round. The process moves through the following stages:

  • Online registration: Candidates log in to edcetadm.tgche.ac.in and fill in personal, academic, and category-related details in the counselling registration form.
  • Slot booking and certificate verification: Candidates under reserved or special categories (NCC, CAP, PH, Sports) book a verification slot and get their certificates checked, either online or at a designated centre.
  • Verified candidates list: The university publishes a list of candidates whose registration and documents have been successfully verified.
  • Web option entry: Verified candidates log back in and arrange their preferred colleges and B.Ed specialisations in order of priority. This ranked list, called the web option, decides which seat a candidate can get based on rank, category, and seat availability.
  • Editing web options: A short window is usually opened to let candidates revise their college and course preferences before the options are locked.
  • Seat allotment: Based on the candidate's TS EdCET rank, category, and web options, the university algorithmically allots a seat and publishes the allotment list online.
  • Reporting and fee payment: Allotted candidates must report to the respective college within the deadline, submit original documents for verification, and pay the prescribed tuition fee to confirm the seat.
  • Admission confirmation: Once fees are paid and documents verified, the candidate's admission is finalised and classes begin as per the academic calendar.

Candidates who skip the reporting deadline or fail to pay the fee on time risk losing the allotted seat, which is then released for the next round.

Documents Needed for TS EdCET Counselling 2026

This section lists every certificate and mark sheet a candidate must carry to the verification and college-reporting stages, since incomplete documentation is one of the most common reasons admissions get delayed.

Candidates called for TS EdCET 2026 counselling must carry both original documents and a set of photocopies for verification. The required documents include:

  • Class 10th mark sheet and certificate
  • Class 12th mark sheet and certificate
  • TS EdCET 2026 scorecard/rank card
  • Birth certificate
  • Government-issued photo identity proof (Aadhaar card, voter ID, passport, etc.)
  • Transfer certificate from the previously attended college
  • Bachelor's degree certificate
  • Bachelor's degree mark sheet (final semester mark sheet for candidates still completing their final year)
  • Certificate of nationality (for General category candidates)
  • PwD certificate, where applicable, for candidates claiming disability-based reservation

It is advisable to keep at least two sets of self-attested photocopies of each document along with the originals, since both the certificate verification stage and the college reporting stage may require document submission.

TS EdCET Seat Allotment 2026

Kakatiya University releases the TS EdCET 2026 seat allotment in multiple rounds, starting with the Phase 1 allotment list expected in June 2026, followed by a Phase 2 list in July 2026. Seat allotment is based on the candidate's rank in the TS EdCET merit list, category, reservation status, and the web options submitted during counselling. Candidates whose names appear in the allotment list must report to the allotted college within the specified deadline, complete document verification, and pay the tuition fee to confirm admission. Seats that remain vacant after both phases are filled through spot admissions conducted directly by the colleges in August 2026.
