DCECE Bihar Polytechnic Admit Card 2026 Out Today

Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) is issuing the DCECE Bihar Polytechnic Admit Card 2026 for Diploma Certificate Entrance Competitive Examination (DCECE) today as per the latest notice and portal schedule. The admit card is being released online for candidates who applied for Polytechnic Engineering (PE), Para Medical (PM) and Para Medical Matric (PMM) courses.

According to DCECE 2026 schedule, the Bihar Polytechnic entrance exam will be conducted on 23 and 24 May 2026 at various centres across the state. The BCECEB notice mentions that the admit card download link for DCECE [PE/PMM/PM]‑2026 is being activated in mid‑May, and candidates must download and carry the hall ticket to the exam centre.

How to Download DCECE Bihar Polytechnic Admit Card 2026

Once the link is active today, candidates can download their DCECE 2026 hall ticket by following these steps:

  • Visit the official BCECEB website: https://bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the “Download” / “Online Portal” or DCECE‑2026 section and click on the link “Download Admit Card of DCECE [PE/PMM/PM]‑2026”.
  • You will be redirected to the DCECE‑2026 online application portal on admissions.nic.in/bihar.
  • Under Registered Candidate Sign‑In, enter your Application Number/Registration Number, Date of Birth (dd‑mm‑yyyy) or password, and the Security Pin/Captcha.
  • Click on “Sign In / Submit” to log in to your DCECE 2026 account.
  • Click on the “Download Admit Card / Hall Ticket” option; your DCECE Bihar Polytechnic Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen with exam date, time and centre details.
  • Verify all details carefully, then download and print 2–3 clear A4‑size copies of the hall ticket.

If you have forgotten your application number or password, use the “Forgot Application Number / Password” option on the portal or check your registered email/SMS for login details.

Details Mentioned on DCECE Bihar Polytechnic Admit Card 2026

After downloading the admit card, candidates should check the following details:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Father’s/Mother’s name
  • Application number / roll number
  • Exam name: DCECE (PE/PM/PMM) 2026
  • Course group (PE, PM, PMM) you have applied for
  • Exam date and time (23 or 24 May 2026, shift details)
  • Exam centre name, centre code and full address
  • Category and other candidate details (if printed)
  • Important exam‑day instructions and reporting/gate‑closing time

If any discrepancy is found in the admit card (name, photo, course, exam date or centre), the candidate should immediately contact BCECEB through the helpdesk before the exam.

Documents Required with DCECE Bihar Polytechnic Admit Card 2026

On the day of the DCECE 2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the DCECE Bihar Polytechnic Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Driving Licence Voter ID Any other government‑issued ID accepted by BCECEB.
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Voter ID
  • Any other government‑issued ID accepted by BCECEB.
  • Additional documents if specified in the information brochure/admit card (e.g., PwD certificate and scribe permission letter for candidates availing such facilities).

Candidates will not be allowed to enter the exam hall without the printed admit card and valid photo ID.
