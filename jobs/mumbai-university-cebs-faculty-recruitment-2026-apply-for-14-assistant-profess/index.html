Mumbai University CEBS Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

Shortlisting and selection at UM-DAE CEBS is merit-based, driven by the quality of publications, research proposal, and letters of recommendation - not just degree qualifications. The process runs in two rounds before final appointment. Step 1 - Application Screening: A Screening Committee reviews applications based on peer-reviewed publications, research proposal quality, relevant experience, and recommendation letters. Fulfilling minimum qualifications alone does not guarantee a call for interview. Step 2 - Job Talk (Round 1): Shortlisted candidates are invited for an in-person job talk before the Selection Committee. Video-conference is available only for candidates based outside India who cannot attend in person. A separate merit list is created for each post code. Q2How many vacancies are there in UM-DAE CEBS Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
There are a total of 14 vacancies — 7 for Assistant Professor, 4 for Reader, and 3 for Associate Professor — across the Schools of Biological Sciences, Chemical Sciences, Mathematical Sciences, and Physical Sciences.
Q3What is the minimum qualification required to apply?
A
All posts require a Ph.D. in the relevant subject area (Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, or Physics) with at least 60% marks or equivalent at the Master's level. The required post-Ph.D. experience varies: 2 years for Assistant Professor, 5 years for Reader, and 10 years for Associate Professor.

Q7What is the selection process for UM-DAE CEBS Faculty Recruitment 2026?
