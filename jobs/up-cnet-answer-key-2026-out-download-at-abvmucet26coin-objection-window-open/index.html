How to Download UP CNET Answer Key 2026

Candidates can access the provisional answer key directly through the ABVMU candidate portal. The process is straightforward and requires login credentials used during registration.

  • Visit the official website abvmucet26.co.in
  • Click on the "Answer Key" link on the homepage or candidate dashboard.
  • Log in using your Registration Number/Application Number and Date of Birth or Password.
  • The provisional answer key will appear on the screen.
  • Download and save the PDF for reference.
  • Compare your responses with the official answers to estimate your score.

UP CNET Answer Key Objection 2026 - How to Raise a Challenge

Along with releasing the answer key, ABVMU has opened the objection portal. Candidates who believe any answer in the provisional key is incorrect can challenge it online but only within the one-day window. Supporting documentary evidence from standard textbooks is mandatory; objections without proof will be rejected.

Steps to Submit an Objection

  • Visit abvmucet26.co.in and log in to the candidate portal.
  • Access the provisional answer key and your response sheet.
  • Click on "Raise Objection" or "Challenge Answer Key."
  • Select the question number, your booklet series, and describe the objection.
  • Upload scanned supporting documents (e.g., relevant pages from recognised textbooks).
  • Pay the objection fee through the online payment gateway, if applicable.
  • Review your submission and click Submit before the deadline.

UP CNET Final Answer Key 2026

Once the objection window closes, ABVMU will review all valid challenges and release the final answer key. No further objections will be accepted after this stage. The UP CNET 2026 Result will be prepared exclusively on the basis of this final answer key. Candidates should note that objections backed by credible references have a higher likelihood of being considered.
