Education Qualification

Details

Yoga Teacher: Any Graduate with PG Diploma in Yoga/M.P.Ed. with Yoga Specialisation from a recognized university. Sports coach: B.P.Ed. OR M.P.Ed. with specialization in any game from a recognized university. Trainer for Girls for Self Defense: A female Graduate with 3 years of experience in coaching self-defense. Dance Expert: Certificate/Diploma in any of the Indian Classical/folk Dance from a recognized university. Instrument Expert: Certificate/Diploma in a relevant field (any instrument like Flute, Tabla, Sitar, Violin, Guitar, Congo) from a recognized university. Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA): SSC (Class XII) from a recognized Board and possessing a minimum speed of 30 words per minute in English or 25 words per minute in Hindi computer-based typing. OR Passed +2 level of Senior Secondary from CBSE/State Board with secretarial practices and office management as a vocational subject.
