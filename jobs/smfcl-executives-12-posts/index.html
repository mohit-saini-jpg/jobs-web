How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official website of SMFCL at http://www.sdclindia.com and open the “Careers” section.
  • 2Click on the link for SMFCL Rectt. Advt. No. 01/2026 and read the detailed advertisement and instructions carefully.
  • 3Click on “Apply Online” and complete the registration / application form by entering personal, educational, category and experience details correctly.
  • 4On submission of application details, a system‑generated Registration Number will appear at the top of the filled form; note it down for future login and correspondence.
  • 5Upload all required documents under the “Upload Document” section in clear and legible scanned copies (PDF/JPEG) as specified.
  • 6Proceed to “Make Payment” section and pay the applicable application fee online (Rs. 1,000/- for UR/OBC‑NCL/EWS; Nil for SC/ST/PwBD).
  • 7After successful payment, download the final submitted application form and keep a printout for future reference and production at the time of selection, if called.
  • 8High School (Class 10th) certificate as proof of Date of Birth.
  • 9Certificates and marksheets of all academic/professional qualifications (10th, 12th, Diploma, Graduation, Post‑Graduation, CA/CMA/LL.B/CS/B.Tech, etc., as applicable).
  • 10Percentage conversion document (wherever CGPA is awarded) issued by the University/Institute.
  • 11Valid SC/ST/OBC‑NCL/EWS/PwBD certificate in prescribed format of Government of India, wherever reservation is claimed.
  • 12Proof of Identity/Address such as Passport, Voter ID, Driving License, Aadhaar Card, etc.
  • 13PAN Card.
  • 14Proof of each period of experience claimed (offer letters, joining orders, relieving letters, service certificates, relevant pay slips, etc.) merged into a single PDF in chronological order.
  • 15Any other document supporting the claims made in the online application.
  • 16Printout of CBT Admit Card / Interview Call Letter along with original photo identity proof and one self‑attested photocopy.
  • 17Original certificates and mark sheets of all educational qualifications and experience, along with original category certificates for SC/ST/OBC‑NCL/EWS/PwBD candidates.
  • 18No Objection Certificate (NOC) / relieving order for candidates serving in Government/PSU/Autonomous Bodies, as required.
  • 19The cut‑off date for determining age, qualification and post‑qualification experience is 18.06.2026; any qualification or experience acquired after this date will not be considered.
  • 20Mere submission of application does not guarantee shortlisting; candidates must meet all eligibility criteria and clear the selection stages.
  • 21If at any stage it is found that a candidate has furnished incorrect/false information or suppressed any material fact, his/her candidature/appointment is liable to be cancelled/terminated without notice.
  • 22If a candidate applies for more than one post and CBT for multiple posts is held in a single session, he/she will be allowed to appear in CBT for only one post/discipline; remaining applications will not be considered and fee will not be refunded.
  • 23Change of category, post, city for CBT, email ID, or any key information after submission of online application will not be entertained.
  • 24All communication will be made only through email and SMFCL website; candidates must keep their registered email ID active for at least one year.
  • 25Selected candidates can be posted anywhere in SMFCL offices or its subsidiaries in India or abroad as per organizational requirements.
