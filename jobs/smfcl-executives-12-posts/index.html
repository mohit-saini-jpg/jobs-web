FAQs

Q1What is SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
The SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026 is a national-level hiring process conducted by Sagarmala Finance Corporation Limited (SMFCL) for Executive (E-1 Grade) posts in various disciplines. It is a Mini Ratna Category-I CPSE under the Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways, Government of India. The recruitment aims to fill professional positions in Finance, Projects, Legal, Company Secretary, and IT departments through a transparent selection process.
Q2How many vacancies are released under SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 12 Executive vacancies have been announced in the 2026 recruitment cycle. These posts are distributed across different disciplines including Finance, Projects, Legal, IT, and Company Secretary roles. Reservation benefits are also provided for SC, ST, OBC-NCL, EWS, and PwBD candidates as per Government of India rules.
Q3What is the eligibility criteria for SMFCL Executive posts?
A
Candidates must possess qualifications such as CA/CMA for Finance, B.E./B.Tech for Engineering posts, LL.B for Legal, CS membership for Company Secretary, and B.Tech in Computer Science for IT posts. Only candidates with minimum required marks and valid degrees from recognized institutions are eligible. Final-year result declaration is mandatory before applying.
Q4What is the age limit for SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit for SMFCL Executive posts is 30 years as on 18 June 2026. However, age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories like SC, ST, OBC-NCL, PwBD, and other eligible groups as per Government of India guidelines. The relaxation varies from 3 to 10 years depending on category.
Q5What is the selection process for SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process includes a Computer Based Test (CBT) followed by a Personal Interview. The CBT carries 85% weightage in the final merit, while the interview carries 15%. Candidates must qualify both stages and also clear document verification and medical fitness requirements for final appointment.
Q6What is the exam pattern for SMFCL Executive CBT?
A
The CBT consists of 150 multiple-choice questions carrying 150 marks, divided into domain-specific questions and general aptitude sections. The exam duration is 120 minutes, and there is a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer. The test will be conducted in major cities across India.
Q7What is the application fee for SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee for General, OBC-NCL, and EWS candidates is Rs. 1000, while SC, ST, and PwBD candidates are exempted from paying any fee. The payment must be made online during the application process and is strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.
Q8How can I apply for SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online through the official SMFCL website under the Careers section. They need to complete registration, fill in personal and academic details, upload documents, and pay the application fee. After successful submission, a registration number is generated for future reference.
Q9What is the salary offered under SMFCL Executive E-1 Grade posts?
A
Selected candidates will receive a pay scale of Rs. 40,000 to Rs. 1,40,000 (IDA pattern) along with DA, allowances, and other benefits as per company rules. The salary package also includes additional perks depending on company policies and government norms. It is a high-paying PSU-level executive position.
Q10Where can I download the SMFCL Executive Recruitment 2026 notification PDF?
A
The official notification PDF is available on the SMFCL website under the recruitment or careers section. It contains complete details regarding eligibility, vacancies, exam pattern, and application instructions. Candidates should always refer to the official notification before applying to avoid misinformation.
