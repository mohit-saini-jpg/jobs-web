RSOS Time Table 2026 OUT

Rajasthan State Open School has officially released the RSOS Time Table 2026 for Sr. Secondary (Class XII) and Secondary (Class X) Examinations Mar-May 2026. The theory examinations will be held from 23 April 2026 to 20 May 2026.

The complete RSOS Time Table 2026 is available on the official notification link at Click Here Students can also download their admit card from the official RSOS portal https://rsosadmission.rajasthan.gov.in using registration number or login credentials.

Students are advised to verify all dates, timings, and instructions carefully on the downloaded timetable. In case of any discrepancy, contact your study centre or the Rajasthan State Open School immediately.

How to Download RSOS Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the RSOS Time Table 2026 PDF directly from the official notification link. The process is quick and does not require any login.

  • Open your web browser and visit the official RSOS Time Table 2026 notification link.
  • The PDF document will load automatically in your browser window.
  • Locate the download icon or option in the PDF viewer toolbar.
  • Click to save the file on your device for easy access.
  • Open the saved PDF and print a clear copy for reference.

Check the official timetable thoroughly for your subject codes, timings, and exam instructions. Keep a printed or digital copy handy for effective exam preparation.
