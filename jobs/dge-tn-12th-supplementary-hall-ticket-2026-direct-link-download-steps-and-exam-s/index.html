DGE TN 12th Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026 - Direct Link, Download Steps, and Exam Schedule

The Directorate of Government Examinations (DGE), Tamil Nadu, has released the hall ticket for the Class 12 (Higher Secondary Examination) Supplementary/Arrear Examinations 2026. The admit card went live on June 23, 2026, on the official website, dge.tn.gov.in. Students who registered for the June/July 2026 supplementary exams can download their hall tickets online using their application number, permanent registration number, and date of birth. The supplementary exams will be held in offline, pen-and-paper mode from June 29 to July 7, 2026.

This article explains exactly how to download the DGE TN 12th supplementary hall ticket 2026, what details you need, the complete subject-wise exam timetable, and answers to common questions students are searching for right now.

Check Notice: DGE TN 12th Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026

What Is the DGE TN 12th Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026?

This section explains what the hall ticket is and why it has been released now.

The Tamil Nadu 12th Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026 is the official admit card issued by the Directorate of Government Examinations for students appearing in the HSE (Higher Secondary Examination) Supplementary, also called arrear, examinations. This exam is meant for students who could not clear one or more subjects in the regular Class 12 board exam earlier in the year and are now attempting those papers again. The hall ticket confirms a candidate's eligibility, exam centre, and subject details, and it is mandatory to carry it to the examination hall.

Where and How to Download the TN 12th Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026

This section covers the official website and the exact step-by-step process to download the admit card.

The hall ticket is available only on the official DGE Tamil Nadu website. Candidates do not need any third-party app or portal to access it.

Official website: dge.tn.gov.in

Steps to download the hall ticket:

  • Visit the official website, dge.tn.gov.in.
  • Click on the link titled "HSE Supplementary Exam June/July 2026 Hall Ticket."
  • Enter your application number, permanent register number, and date of birth.
  • Submit the entered details.
  • Your hall ticket will be displayed on the screen.
  • Check all the details on the hall ticket carefully, such as name, subject codes, and exam centre.
  • Download the hall ticket and take a printout to carry for the examination.

Documents/details required to download:

Candidates are advised to download the hall ticket well in advance of the exam date rather than waiting until the last moment, since last-minute server load on government portals is common during peak download periods.

Important Points for Candidates Before the Exam

This section highlights precautionary steps students should take after downloading their hall ticket to avoid last-minute issues on exam day.

  • Download and print the hall ticket immediately rather than waiting close to the exam date.
  • Cross-check personal details such as name, date of birth, subject codes, and exam centre name/address on the hall ticket.
  • Report any discrepancy in the hall ticket to the concerned DGE authorities or your school immediately, well before the exam date.
  • Carry the printed hall ticket along with a valid photo ID to the examination centre, as it is mandatory for entry.
  • The supplementary exams will be conducted in offline, pen-and-paper mode at designated exam centres.
