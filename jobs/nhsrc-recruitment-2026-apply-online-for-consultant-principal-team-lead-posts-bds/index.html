Important Instructions

All positions are contractual in nature; candidates will not be treated as employees of NHSRC and will not be entitled to benefits available to regular employees. NHSRC shall have no obligation to offer regular employment to selected candidates during and/or after the completion of the contract period. Fulfilling the eligibility criteria does not result in shortlisting for interview for Consultant posts. Only shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview; a written test / screening test may also be conducted. For the Principal Team Lead (IT) post, Government Officials can also apply on Deputation subject to meeting TOR criteria and submission of NOC from their controlling department. All revisions / corrigendum (if any) will be hosted on the NHSRC website only; candidates should visit regularly. Candidates selected may be considered for other vacant positions at NHSRC requiring similar skill sets and at an appropriate level. All positions involve travel to States and Districts; candidates must be willing to undertake outstation travel. Contract for all positions is initially till 31st March 2028 with further extension subject to satisfactory performance. Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be as per applicable law.
