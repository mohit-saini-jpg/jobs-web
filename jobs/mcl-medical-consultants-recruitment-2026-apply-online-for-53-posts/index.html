Qualification / Eligibility

Consultant (Medical Specialist): MBBS from a recognized Institute/College approved by the Medical Council of India/NMC along with a recognized Post Graduate Degree/DNB/Post Graduate Diploma in the concerned specialty. Retired Medical Executives need minimum "Good" ratings in the last 05 years of service, no punishment under CDA Rules in the last 05 years, and not involved in corruption/illegal gratification/criminal cases. Outside candidates require a minimum of 07 years of experience in the concerned specialization. Consultant (GDMO): MBBS from a recognized Institute/College approved by the Medical Council of India/NMC. Outside candidates should possess a minimum of 03 years of experience as GDMO.
