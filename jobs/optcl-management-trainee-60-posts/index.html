How to Apply

  • 1Recent colour passport-size photograph (.jpg/.jpeg, 3.5 cm × 4.5 cm, 25KB–50KB)
  • 2Signature (.jpg/.jpeg, 3.5 cm × 1.5 cm, 25KB–35KB)
  • 3Date of Birth proof / 10th Pass Certificate (.pdf/.jpg/.jpeg)
  • 4Essential Qualification Pass Certificate and Mark Sheet – Degree Certificate and Mark Sheet (.pdf/.jpg/.jpeg)
  • 5GATE 2026 Score Card – colour scanned copy (Normalized Marks out of 100) (.pdf/.jpg/.jpeg)
  • 6Odia Pass Certificate (.pdf/.jpg/.jpeg)
  • 7Caste/Category certificate as applicable (SC/ST/SEBC/PwBD/Ex-SM/Sports Person)
  • 8Printout of the finally submitted Online Application form
  • 9Original GATE 2026 Admit Card and GATE 2026 Score Card
  • 10Original documents verified through Digi Locker application as per procedure intimated in the interview call letter
  • 11Valid original Photo ID (Aadhaar / PAN / Passport / Voter ID / Driving License); photocopies of ID are not acceptable
  • 12All original educational certificates and mark sheets from 10th onwards
  • 13Original caste/category certificate in prescribed format (SC/ST/SEBC/PwBD/Ex-SM/Sports Person) as applicable
  • 14No Objection Certificate (NOC) if currently working in State/Central Government/PSU/Autonomous Body
  • 15Identity Card issued by Director, Sports Odisha (for Sports Person candidates)
  • 16Selected candidates will be required to execute a service bond of Rs. 3,00,000/- (applicable for all categories) to successfully complete the training period and thereafter serve OPTCL for at least three years. The bond amount is payable if the candidate resigns or leaves service during the stipulated bond period for any reason whatsoever.
  • 17During the 1-year training period, a stipend of Rs. 50,000/- per month will be paid. On successful completion, candidates will be absorbed as Assistant Manager (Electrical) in Pay Matrix Level EE-2 (Rs. 56,100 – Rs. 1,77,500) with CTC of approximately Rs. 15 Lakhs per annum.
  • 18If performance during training is found unsatisfactory, the training period will be extended for a further six months. If performance is still not satisfactory after the extension, the service will be terminated.
  • 19After successful completion of training, candidates will be on probation for a further period of twelve months.
  • 20Selected candidates are liable to be posted at any existing or future unit of OPTCL throughout Odisha and outside, as per organizational requirements.
  • 21SC, ST, and SEBC concessions are admissible only to candidates belonging to Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, and SEBC of Odisha State by birth.
  • 22SEBC certificate must be the latest certificate (dated on or after 01-05-2025). SEBC certificates issued after 25-06-2026 will not be accepted.
  • 23Candidates must correctly declare their name and date of birth as per High School Certificate / Birth Certificate. Date of birth as entered in the High School Certificate / Birth Certificate issued by the concerned authority will only be accepted.
  • 24Candidature is provisional throughout the process and liable to be rejected or appointment terminated at any stage if any information is found false, misleading, or amounts to suppression of material facts.
  • 25OPTCL will not undertake detailed scrutiny of applications for eligibility during shortlisting; candidates must verify their own eligibility before applying. If any claim is not substantiated at the time of scrutiny, candidature will be cancelled and OPTCL's decision shall be final.
  • 26All information regarding this recruitment process will be made available only on the career section of www.optcl.co.in. Candidates must periodically check the website for updates.
  • 27Legal jurisdiction in case of any cause or dispute shall be the Hon'ble High Court of Orissa.
  • 28Management reserves the right to cancel, restrict, enlarge, modify, or alter the recruitment/selection process at any stage without issuing any notice or assigning any reason.
