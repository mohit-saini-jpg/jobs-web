Application Fee – IOCL Guwahati Refinery Apprentice Recruitment 2026

No application fee is mentioned in the official IOCL Guwahati Refinery Apprentice 2026 notification. Candidates can register and apply through the NAPS/NATS portals and submit the Microsoft Form without any fee payment.

Selection Process – IndianOil Guwahati Apprentice Vacancy 2026

There is no written test for the IOCL Guwahati Refinery Apprentice Recruitment 2026. Selection is entirely based on merit — the percentage of marks in the prescribed qualifying examination. Preference is given to candidates applying for apprenticeship for the first time over those applying for a second time. Merit List Preparation: Candidates are ranked in descending order of aggregate percentage of marks in the prescribed qualifying examination across all years/semesters. Tie-breaking: In case of a tie in percentage, the candidate senior by age (earlier date of birth) is ranked higher. If date of birth is also identical, the candidate with higher Matriculation marks is ranked higher. Shortlisting for Document Verification: Candidates are shortlisted based on merit and available seats in each trade/discipline. Tentative publication of shortlist: 05 August 2026 . Stipend / Benefits – IOCL Guwahati Apprentice 2026

The monthly stipend for IOCL Guwahati Refinery Apprentices 2026 will be paid as per the Apprentices Act, 1961/1973 and Apprentices Rules 1992 (as amended) along with IOCL Corporation guidelines.
