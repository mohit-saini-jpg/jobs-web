WBBSE Madhyamik Result 2026

The West Bengal Board of Secondary Education (WBBSE) has officially released the Madhyamik Pariksha Result 2026 for Class 10th students on May 8, 2026. Over 9.71 lakh students who appeared for the Madhyamik examination conducted from February 2 to February 12, 2026, can now access their results. WBBSE Madhyamik Pariksha Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - wbresults.nic.in and wbbse.wb.gov.in.

Students can check and download their results by logging in with their roll number and date of birth on the official result portal. The results were announced through a press conference at 9:30 AM, and the result links were activated at 10:15 AM for students to check their scores online. Students are advised to verify all details on their marksheet carefully and contact the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education immediately in case of any discrepancies or errors in their result.

WBBSE Madhyamik Results 2026 Key Highlights

The West Bengal Board of Secondary Education has successfully published the Madhyamik Pariksha Result 2026 for Class 10th students on its official portals. The board conducted examinations at 2,682 centers across West Bengal with participation from 5,44,606 female candidates, 4,26,733 male candidates, and one transgender candidate.

How to Check WBBSE Madhyamik Pariksha Result 2026

Students can check their WBBSE Madhyamik Pariksha Result 2026 by following these simple steps on the official result portal:

  • Visit the official result website wbresults.nic.in or wbbse.wb.gov.in
  • Click on the "WBBSE Madhyamik Result 2026" link on the homepage
  • Enter your Roll Number in the required field (For example, if your roll is 123456N and number is 0001, enter 123456N0001)
  • Enter your Date of Birth in the specified format (For example, if your date of birth is November 12, 2001, enter DOB as 121101)
  • Click on the "Submit" or "Get Result" button to view your result
  • Your WBBSE Madhyamik Pariksha Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen with subject-wise marks
  • Verify all the details including name, roll number, subject marks, and total marks
  • Download the result by clicking on the download button
  • Take a printout of your result for future reference and admission purposes
