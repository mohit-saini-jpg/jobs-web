How to Check Tripura University Result 2026 Online?

  • Open the official Tripura University website https://tripurauniv.ac.in in your browser.
  • Go to the “Results” / “University Results” section, or directly visit https://tripurauniv.ac.in/result/university-results.
  • On the university results page, locate the entries for: Provisional Result of Master of Science (Physics) 1st Semester Examination – 2025 Provisional Result of Master of Science (Statistics) 1st & 3rd Semester Examination – 2025 Provisional Result of M.A Linguistics & Tribal Languages 1st & 3rd Semester Examination – 2025
  • Provisional Result of Master of Science (Physics) 1st Semester Examination – 2025
  • Provisional Result of Master of Science (Statistics) 1st & 3rd Semester Examination – 2025
  • Provisional Result of M.A Linguistics & Tribal Languages 1st & 3rd Semester Examination – 2025
  • Click on the relevant result link; this will either open a PDF result gazette or a result‑view page.
  • If a PDF opens, use Ctrl+F and enter your Roll Number / Registration Number to quickly find your result entry. If it is an online result form, select your programme and semester, enter your roll/registration number, and submit.
  • Check your name, course, semester, subject‑wise marks, total marks and result status (Pass/Fail/Backlog) carefully.
  • Download or print the provisional Tripura University result/marksheet for future reference, higher‑study applications or revaluation/rechecking processes as per university rules.
