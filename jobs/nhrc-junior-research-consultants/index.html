FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for NHRC Junior Research Consultants Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application form for NHRC Junior Research Consultants Recruitment 2026 is 09 June 2026. Interested and eligible candidates must complete the registration process through the official NHRC online application portal before the closing date. Applications submitted through offline mode or after the deadline will not be accepted.
Q2What is the salary offered for NHRC Junior Research Consultant posts?
A
Selected candidates for the Junior Research Consultant (JRC) position will receive a consolidated remuneration of Rs. 70,000/- per month inclusive of taxes. In addition, selected consultants will also be eligible for annual enhancement in remuneration and official travel reimbursements as per NHRC rules and guidelines.
Q3What qualifications are required for NHRC Junior Research Consultants Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates applying for NHRC JRC Recruitment 2026 should possess either an LLB degree or a Post Graduate degree in Social Science subjects such as Political Science, Sociology, International Relations, Social Work, Human Rights, Developmental Communications, or related disciplines. Applicants must secure at least 60% marks from a recognized university to become eligible for the recruitment process.
Q4What is the age limit for NHRC Junior Research Consultant Recruitment 2026?
A
Applicants should be below 32 years of age as on the date of advertisement publication. Candidates exceeding the prescribed upper age limit will not be considered eligible for empanelment under the NHRC Junior Research Consultant recruitment process.
Q5Is prior work experience required for NHRC JRC Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, candidates must possess at least one year of research experience in Government organizations, reputed semi-government bodies, international development agencies, NGOs, or research institutions. Alternatively, candidates having one year of legal experience are also eligible. Internship or training experience will not be counted toward the required experience criteria.
Q6What is the selection process for NHRC Junior Research Consultants Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process includes screening of applications by a Screening Committee followed by a Personal Interview for shortlisted candidates. Final empanelment will be prepared based on the recommendations made by the Selection Committee after evaluating candidates’ qualifications, experience, and interview performance.
Q7Is NHRC Junior Research Consultant Recruitment 2026 a permanent government job?
A
No, the engagement of Junior Research Consultants is purely contractual in nature. Initially, candidates will be engaged for a period of two years, which may be extended up to five years based on satisfactory performance and organizational requirements. The recruitment does not provide any claim for permanent appointment in NHRC.
Q8How can candidates apply for NHRC Junior Research Consultants Recruitment 2026?
A
Eligible candidates must apply online only through the official NHRC application portal before 09 June 2026. Candidates should carefully fill in all personal, educational, and experience details while submitting the application form. Applications sent through any other mode will not be entertained by the Commission.
Q9What additional benefits are provided to NHRC Junior Research Consultants?
A
Apart from monthly remuneration, Junior Research Consultants are entitled to official travel facilities including AC 2 Tier rail or economy air travel along with hotel, taxi, and food reimbursements as per NHRC rules. Selected candidates are also eligible for leave benefits at the rate of 1.5 days per completed month.
Q10What are the important conditions for NHRC Junior Research Consultant engagement?
A
Selected consultants must maintain confidentiality, integrity, and high professional conduct throughout the engagement period. Candidates cannot take up any other employment during the contract period and will need to undergo police verification. Any violation of terms, misconduct, or adverse verification report may lead to immediate termination of the consultancy contract.
