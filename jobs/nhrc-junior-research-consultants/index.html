How to Apply

  • 1Eligible candidates may apply online only through the link: https://hrcnet.nic.in/job/candidate/Register.aspx on or before 9th June 2026.
  • 2Applications received after the due date or through any other mode will not be entertained.
  • 3The engagement is purely contractual and does not confer any right for regular appointment in NHRC.
  • 4Selected candidates will have to sign a Consultancy Contract.
  • 5During the engagement period, JRCs cannot take up any other job/assignment.
  • 6High standards of conduct, secrecy, and integrity are required. Violation may lead to immediate termination.
  • 7Police verification is mandatory. Negative report will result in termination.
  • 8NHRC reserves the right to restrict the number of candidates or cancel the empanelment process at any stage.
  • 9Candidates must maintain confidentiality of all official information.
