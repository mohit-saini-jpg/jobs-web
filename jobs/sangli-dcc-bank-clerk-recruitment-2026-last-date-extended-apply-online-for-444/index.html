Sangli DCC Bank Clerk Selection Process 2026

The selection process for the Junior Assistant post involves two stages. Understanding this process helps candidates prepare strategically. Stage 1: Online Examination All shortlisted applicants will appear for an online computer-based test. Stage 2: Personal Interview Candidates who meet the cut-off in the online exam and whose educational documents are verified will be called for a personal interview conducted at Sangli. The final merit list will be prepared in descending order based on scores obtained across both stages combined.
