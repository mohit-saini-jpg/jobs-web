Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationGraduate degrees, Diplomas in Education, Senior Secondary Certificates.
DetailsGuest Adult Education Teacher: Graduate in any school with 50% marks. Diploma in ETE/B.Ed./Moallim with 50% of marks. Ability to teach in Urdu and Hindi medium. Two years' experience of working with children and mothers in any recognized Institution or NGO of repute. Only women candidates are eligible. Guest Primary Teacher: Senior Secondary School Certificate with 50% marks or Intermediate with 50% marks or its equivalent. Pass in the Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET) conducted by the CBSE. Competence to teach through Hindi & English medium. Desirable: Knowledge of Computer Application, Ability to read and write Urdu. Only Women candidates are eligible. Guest Nursery Teacher: Senior Secondary School Certificate or its equivalent with at least 50% marks in aggregate from a Recognized Board/ University. Diploma/Certificate in Nursery Education/Training from recognized institute OR B.Ed. (Nursery). Ability to read and write Urdu. Only Women candidates are eligible. Guest Craft Teacher (Cutting & Tailoring): At least Second class secondary school certificate with Urdu or its equivalent from a recognized Board/University. Certificate in the Cutting Tailoring and Embroidery with Teacher Training Certificate from ITI or equivalent Diploma Certificate from recognized institute/ Polytechnic in the tread concerned.
Matched QualificationsIntermediate, 12TH_Pass, ITI, Diploma, B_Tech_BE, MA, B_Ed, M_A, CA

FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the JMI Guest Teacher recruitment?
The last date to submit the offline application for the JMI Guest Teacher recruitment is 03 June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well in advance of the deadline.
Q2. How many vacancies are available for Guest Teacher posts at JMI?
There are a total of 6 vacancies for Guest Teacher posts at Jamia Millia Islamia. These vacancies are distributed across various teaching roles including Adult Education, Primary, Nursery, and Craft.
Q3. What is the minimum qualification required for the Guest Primary Teacher post?
The minimum qualification for the Guest Primary Teacher post is a Senior Secondary School Certificate with 50% marks or Intermediate with 50% marks or its equivalent, along with a pass in the Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET).
Q4. Are there any specific eligibility criteria for women candidates?
Yes, for the Guest Adult Education Teacher, Guest Primary Teacher, and Guest Nursery Teacher posts, only women candidates are eligible to apply.
Q5. What is the application mode for this recruitment?
The application mode for this recruitment is offline. Candidates need to download the application form and submit it via post or by hand to the specified address.
Q6. Where should the application be sent?
The completed application form should be sent to the office of the offg. Head Mistress, Balak Mata Centre, Matia Mahal, Delhi-110006.
Q7. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
No application fee is mentioned in the official notification for this recruitment. Candidates can apply without paying any fee.
Q8. What is the experience required for the Guest Adult Education Teacher post?
For the Guest Adult Education Teacher post, two years of experience of working with children and mothers in any recognized Institution or NGO of repute is required.
Q9. Can applications be submitted via email?
No, applications submitted through email will not be entertained. Candidates must submit their applications offline as per the instructions.
Q10. What is the advertisement number for this recruitment?
The advertisement number for this recruitment is J.11/BMC/JMI/2026.
