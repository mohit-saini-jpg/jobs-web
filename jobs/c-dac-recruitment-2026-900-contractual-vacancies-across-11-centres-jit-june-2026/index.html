C-DAC Recruitment 2026 – 900+ Contractual Vacancies : Physical Eligibility

Details
  1. C-DAC JIT June 2026 is a large-scale national recruitment drive and competition is expected to be moderate to high, particularly in popular domains. The absence of any application fee significantly increases applicant volume across all centres
  2. Roles in AI/ML, Full Stack Development, Cyber Security, and Software Development at the Project Engineer and Senior Project Engineer levels will attract the largest applicant pools, especially from tier-2 and tier-3 engineering colleges. In contrast, niche domain posts in Quantum Computing, VLSI/Chip Design, Additive Manufacturing, Robotics, and HPC System Software will see comparatively lower competition but demand highly specialised credentials
  3. Initial screening is where effective filtering happens — CGPA, subject percentage, and domain match are the primary screening parameters. Candidates applying to Noida (e-Governance, Health Informatics) and Pune (200+ positions, wide domain breadth) should expect steeper competition due to location preference and higher vacancy counts. Documenting relevant project work, publications, or domain-specific internships may help distinguish profiles at the screening stage, even where such work is not formally counted as experience

C-DAC Recruitment 2026 – 900+ Contractual Vacancies : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Candidates must apply online only through the official C-DAC careers portal. A valid mobile number and active email ID are mandatory, as OTP-based login is used and all subsequent communications are sent by email.
  • 2Visit the official C-DAC careers portal at https://careers.cdac.in/.
  • 3Select the relevant centre from the list of active advertisements.
  • 4Click 'Apply' against the specific position you wish to apply for.
  • 5Log in using your registered mobile number and enter the OTP received.
  • 6Fill in all required fields in the online application form accurately.
  • 7Upload your passport-size photograph in .jpg format (max 400 KB) and your resume/CV in PDF format (max 500 KB).
  • 8Submit the form and note down the unique application number generated upon successful submission.
  • 9If applying for multiple positions or multiple centres, repeat the process with a separate application for each.
  • 10Documents to be produced at the time of document verification or joining include: educational mark sheets and degree certificates from Class 10 onwards, CGPA-to-percentage conversion certificate (if applicable), experience certificates from all previous and current employers, caste/category certificate (if applicable), disability certificate issued by the District Medical Board (for PwBD candidates), date of birth proof, and an NOC from the current employer where applicable.
