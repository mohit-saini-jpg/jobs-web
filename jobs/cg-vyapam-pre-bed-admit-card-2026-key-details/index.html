How to Download CG Vyapam Pre B.Ed Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official website vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in.
  • Click on the “Admit Card” or “Pre B.Ed 2026 Admit Card” link.
  • Login using your Application Number / Registration ID and Password / Date of Birth.
  • Enter the captcha code and submit.
  • Your admit card will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take 2-3 clear printouts on A4 size paper.

Details Mentioned on CG Vyapam Pre B.Ed 2026 Admit Card

  • Candidate’s Full Name
  • Application Number / Roll Number
  • Photograph and Signature
  • Exam Name: Pre B.Ed Entrance Exam 2026
  • Exam Date, Time & Shift
  • Exam Centre Name, Code and Address
  • Reporting Time and Gate Closing Time
  • Category, Gender, etc.
  • Important Exam Day Instructions

Note: Immediately report any discrepancy in name, photo, or centre details to the CG Vyapam helpline.

Documents Required on Exam Day

  • Printed copy of CG Vyapam Pre B.Ed Admit Card 2026
  • One original valid Photo ID Proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence, etc.)

Exam Day Instructions

  • Reach the centre before the reporting time.
  • Carry only allowed items (Admit card, Photo ID, transparent water bottle).
  • Prohibited items: Mobile phone, smartwatch, calculator, notes, etc.
  • Follow all rules strictly to avoid disqualification.
