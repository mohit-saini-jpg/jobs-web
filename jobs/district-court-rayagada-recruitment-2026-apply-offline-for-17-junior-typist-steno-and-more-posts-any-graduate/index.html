District Court Rayagada has officially announced the Junior Clerk-cum-Copyist, Junior Typist & Stenographer Grade-III Recruitment 2026 with 17 openings. The detailed notification was published under Advertisement No. 01/2026 at the official website of District Court Rayagada. Candidates who meet the age criteria of 18 to 42 years and hold qualifications such as Graduate degree, Diploma in Computer Application, and required Typing/Shorthand speed can submit their offline applications up to 17 June 2026. FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the District Court Rayagada Recruitment 2026?
The last date to submit the application for the District Court Rayagada Recruitment 2026 is 17th June 2026.
Q2. How many vacancies are available in this recruitment?
There are a total of 17 vacancies for the posts of Junior Clerk-cum-Copyist, Junior Typist, and Stenographer Grade-III.
Q3. What is the minimum educational qualification required?
The minimum educational qualification is a +3 examination or equivalent, along with a Diploma in Computer Application. Specific typing and shorthand speeds are also required for certain posts.
Q4. What is the age limit for the applicants?
The age limit for applicants is between 18 to 42 years, with relaxations applicable for reserved categories.
Q5. What is the application mode for this recruitment?
The application mode for this recruitment is offline. Candidates need to send their applications by post.
Q6. What is the salary offered for these posts?
The salary ranges from Rs. 19,900/- to Rs. 63,200/- (Level 4) for Junior Clerk-cum-Copyist and Junior Typist, and Rs. 25,500/- to Rs. 81,100/- (Level 7) for Stenographer Grade-III.
Q7. What is the selection process for the District Court Rayagada Recruitment?
The selection process includes a written competitive examination, a computer test, and a viva-voce test.
Q8. Where should the application form be sent?
The application form should be sent to the OFFICE OF THE REGISTRAR, CIVIL COURTS, RAYAGADA, AT/PO-RAYAGADA, PIN-765001, DISTRICT-RAYAGADA, ODISHA.
Q9. Is there any application fee mentioned?
No application fee is mentioned in the official notification.
Q10. What are the required typing and shorthand speeds for the posts?
For Junior Typist, a minimum speed of 40 words per minute is required. For Stenographer Grade III, a minimum speed of 80 words in shorthand and 40 words in type writing per minute is required.
