Angul District Court Recruitment 2026 - Apply Offline for 18 Salaried Amin, Steno and More Posts

Angul District Court

Posts: 18 Last Date: 18-06-2026 Offline

Short Information

Angul District Court has officially released the Junior Clerk-cum-Copyist, Stenographer Grade-III, Salaried Amin Recruitment 2026 notification (Advt. No. 03 dated 18.05.2026) for 18 vacancies across Odisha. Eligible graduates and 10th pass candidates aged 18 to 42 years can apply offline from 18 May 2026 to 18 June 2026 through the official Angul District Court application process. Selected candidates will be appointed on a full-time basis with a monthly salary ranging from ₹19,900/- to ₹81,100/-.

Qualification / Eligibility

Education Qualification

+3 examination or equivalent, Diploma in Computer Application, Ability to speak, read and write Odia equivalent to M.E. standard. Matriculation for Salaried Amin.

Details

Junior Clerk-cum-Copyist & Stenographer Grade-III: Must have passed at least +3 examination or equivalent from a recognized University and Diploma in Computer Application from a recognized institute. Ability to speak, read and write Odia equivalent to M.E. standard. For Stenographer Grade-III, minimum speed of 80 words in shorthand and 40 words in typewriting per minute is also required. Salaried Amin: Must have passed matriculation examination or equivalent, Revenue Inspector Training and knowledge in Computer Operation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Angul District Court Recruitment 2026?

The last date for the receipt of applications for the Angul District Court Recruitment 2026 is 18th June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in this recruitment?

A total of 18 vacancies are available for the posts of Junior Clerk-cum-Copyist, Stenographer Grade-III, and Salaried Amin in the Angul District Court Recruitment 2026.

Q3. What is the minimum and maximum age limit for the posts?

The minimum age limit is 18 years, and the maximum age limit is 42 years for all the posts. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per government rules.

Q4. What are the educational qualifications required for the posts?

For Junior Clerk-cum-Copyist and Stenographer Grade-III, a +3 examination or equivalent and a Diploma in Computer Application are required. For Salaried Amin, matriculation or equivalent and Revenue Inspector Training are needed.

Q5. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) in the official notification for this recruitment.

Q6. What is the selection process for the Angul District Court Recruitment 2026?

The selection process includes a written competitive examination, a Computer Science Test (Practical), and a Viva-voce Test.

Q7. What is the salary offered for the different posts?

The salary ranges from Rs. 19,900/- to Rs. 63,200/- for Junior Clerk-cum-Copyist, Rs. 25,500/- to Rs. 81,100/- for Stenographer Grade-III, and Rs. 21,700/- to Rs. 69,100/- for Salaried Amin.

Q8. How can I apply for these posts?

The application process is offline. Candidates need to download the application form from the official website, fill it correctly, attach necessary documents, and send it to the address mentioned in the notification before the last date.
