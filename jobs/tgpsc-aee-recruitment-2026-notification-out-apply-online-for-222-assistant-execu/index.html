Age Limit

DetailsCandidates must be a minimum of 18 years and maximum of 44 years as on 01/07/2026. (Note: As per G.O.Ms.No.86, G.A.(Ser.A) Dept., Dt.18/05/2026, the upper age limit has been raised by 10 years from 34 years to 44 years.) Maximum age indicated is for Unreserved (OC) candidates. How to Apply

  1. 1Candidates shall apply online through the Commission's website (https://www.tgpsc.gov.in) only. No other mode of application will be accepted. Before applying, candidates must complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the TGPSC website to obtain their TGPSC ID. OTR is not considered an application for the post.
  2. 2Step-by-Step Application Process:
  3. 3Step I: Register for OTR at https://www.tgpsc.gov.in (if not already registered) to obtain TGPSC ID. Verify all bio-data details carefully.
  4. 4Step II: Login using TGPSC ID and Date of Birth, enter OTP received on registered mobile, verify pre-filled details from OTR (Name, DOB, Qualifications, Community, Gender, Disability etc.), and select examination centre.
  5. 5Step III: Preview the application for correctness, then click "SAVE & PAYMENT" to proceed to online fee payment via Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card or UPI.
  6. 6Step IV: After fee payment, a PDF application will be generated. Download and save the PDF. Note the Reference ID Number for future correspondence.
  7. 7Documents/Files Required to be Uploaded:
  8. 8Recent colour passport-size photograph (not older than 3 months before last date) – JPG/PNG, 100kb–200kb
  9. 9Scanned specimen signature – JPG/PNG, 20kb–30kb
  10. 10PDF copies of certificates as required (educational qualifications, community, OTR documents); certificates already uploaded in OTR need not be uploaded again
  11. 11Documents to be Produced at the time of Certificate Verification / Joining:
  12. 12SSC / CBSE / ICSE certificate in support of Date of Birth
  13. 13Printout of online application form (PDF)
  14. 14School Study Certificate (1st to 7th Class) or Certificate of Residence from competent authority for the same period
  15. 15All certificates and mark sheets of educational qualification (10th, Intermediate/12th, B.E./B.Tech Civil Engineering)
  16. 16Community Certificate for SC/ST issued by Revenue Authority not below the rank of Tahsildar, Telangana Government, in the name of the applicant with Father's/Mother's name
  17. 17Community Certificate for BCs (BC-A/B/C/D/E) issued by Revenue Authority not below Tahsildar, Telangana Government, obtained through Mee-seva
  18. 18Non-Creamy Layer Certificate for BCs in Form-VIIB from Revenue Authority not below Tahsildar/RDO, Telangana Government, in the name of the applicant with Father's/Mother's name
  19. 19Income and Asset Certificate for EWS reservation, issued by Tahsildar, Telangana Government, for the financial year prior to the year of application, in the name of the applicant with Parent's/Spouse's name
  20. 20Disability Certificate in SADAREM format from competent medical authority for PH/PwD candidates
  21. 21No Objection Certificate from Head of Office/Department for Government employees
  22. 22Service Certificate (if claiming age relaxation as Government employee)
  23. 23Certificate claiming sports reservation (Form 1/2/3/4 as applicable)
  24. 24Certificate claiming Ex-servicemen age relaxation
  25. 25Declaration by unemployed (for claiming examination fee exemption)
  26. 26Photo Identity Proof: Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport / Driving License / Service ID / Bank Passbook with photograph / PAN Card
  27. 27Community, Nativity and Date of Birth Certificate
  28. 28DO NOT send any document directly to the Commission. All correspondence is through the Commission's website only. For technical problems, contact 040-23120301 / 040-23120302 / 040-22445566 / 040-67445566 (10:30 AM to 5:00 PM on working days) or email [email protected].
  29. 29Apply Online at: https://www.tgpsc.gov.in → Online Application Link → Login with TGPSC ID & DOB

Important Instructions

The applicant must visit the Commission's website regularly for all updates; no individual communication will be sent under any circumstances. OTR (One Time Registration) is NOT an application for any post. Candidates must mandatorily submit a separate online application for this notification. The details available in the OTR database at the time of submitting the application will only be considered. Changes made in OTR after application submission will not be considered for this notification. Retain the mobile number and email ID registered in OTR throughout the recruitment process; they are required for OTP/SMS communications till finalization of the process. The Commission reserves the right to conduct the examination in OMR-based Offline mode or CBRT mode. The mode will be communicated through web note/press note and Hall Ticket. For multi-session CBRT, scores will be normalized as per the Commission's formula. Normalized marks may vary from original/absolute marks obtained. Only normalized marks will be used for General Ranking List (GRL) preparation. After publication of the answer key, objections can be raised online within the stipulated period with a fee of Rs.500/- per question through the TGPSC website. Objections through representations or emails will not be entertained. Fee for genuine, sustained objections will be reimbursed. Memorandum of Marks can be obtained after results through online payment of Rs.200/- for up to 30 days. Revaluation will not be undertaken under any circumstances. Candidates are not allowed to carry any electronic gadgets, mobile/smart phones, tablets, calculators, smartwatches, Bluetooth devices, pen drives, pagers or any other electronic equipment to the examination centre. Violation is punishable under Act No.25/1997 with imprisonment of 3 to 7 years and fine of Rs.5,000 to Rs.1,00,000. The applicant should be willing to serve anywhere in Telangana State. The appointed candidate is liable to be posted in any district of the concerned Multi Zone. Any candidate found impersonating or resorting to irregular means will be liable for criminal prosecution and permanent debarment from all Public Service Commission examinations in India. Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification. Any resultant dispute arising out of this advertisement shall be subject to the sole jurisdiction of Courts at Hyderabad/Telangana. The Commission reserves the right to alter or modify conditions laid down in the notification or withdraw/cancel the notification at any time as warranted by unforeseen circumstances. All revisions/corrigendum (if any) will be hosted on the Commission's website only – candidates should visit https://www.tgpsc.gov.in regularly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1Q1. What is TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026?
A
TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026 is the Telangana Public Service Commission notification for 222 Assistant Executive Engineer (Civil) posts in the Transport, Roads and Buildings Department.
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are available in TGPSC AEE Civil 2026?
A
A total of 222 vacancies are available, distributed across Multi Zone-I (112) and Multi Zone-II (110) with reservations for various categories including SC, ST, BC, EWS, and PwBD.
Q3Q3. What is the eligibility for TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must have a Bachelor’s Degree in Civil Engineering (B.E./B.Tech) from a recognized Indian University or institution recognized by UGC/AICTE or AMIE.
Q4Q4. What is the age limit for TGPSC AEE Civil 2026?
A
The age limit is 18 to 44 years as of 01/07/2026 for OC candidates, with relaxations for SC/ST/BC, PwD, Ex-Servicemen, NCC instructors, and Telangana State Government employees as per rules.
Q5Q5. What is the TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026 selection process?
A
Selection includes a Written Examination (Objective Type – Paper I: General Studies & Aptitude, Paper II: Civil Engineering), followed by Document Verification and Medical Examination.
Q6Q6. How can I apply for TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must register for One Time Registration (OTR) on the TGPSC website, then apply online with the TGPSC ID and upload required documents before the last date.
Q7Q7. What is the application fee for TGPSC AEE Civil 2026?
A
The application processing fee is ₹1,000 for OC & BC candidates and ₹500 for SC/ST/PwD candidates. The examination fee is ₹120 for employed candidates; unemployed applicants are exempted.
Q8Q8. When will the TGPSC AEE Civil 2026 online application start and end?
A
The online application opens on 06-06-2026 and closes on 13-07-2026 at 5:00 PM. Candidates must complete OTR registration beforehand.
Q9Q9. Where will the TGPSC AEE Civil exam be conducted?
A
Examination centres include Hyderabad, Karimnagar, Khammam, Warangal, and Nizamabad, with the exact venue details mentioned in the hall ticket.
Q10Q10. Who should not apply for TGPSC AEE Civil 2026?
A
Candidates without a Civil Engineering degree, those whose result is pending, candidates from other engineering branches, underage/overage candidates, non-Telangana locals seeking reservation, and those without OTR registration should not apply.
