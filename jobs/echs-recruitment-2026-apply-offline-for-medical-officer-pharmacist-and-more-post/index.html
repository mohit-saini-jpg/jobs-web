Qualification / Eligibility

Medical Officer: MBBS with a minimum of 03 years of work experience after internship. Pharmacist: B Pharmacy from a recognized institute OR 10+2 with Science stream (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) from a recognized board, along with an approved Diploma in Pharmacy from an institute recognized by the Pharmacy Council of India and registered as a Pharmacist. Minimum 03 years' experience in pharmacy is required. Lab Technician: B.Sc (Medical Lab Technology) OR Matriculation/Higher Secondary/Senior Secondary (10+2) with Science from a Recognized institution/Board, along with a Diploma in Medical Lab Technology from a Recognized institution. Minimum 05 years' Experience in Lab is required. Dental Hygienist: Diploma holder in Dental Hygienist/Class-1 DH/DORA course (Armed Forces). Minimum 05 years' Experience in Dental Laboratory is required. Chowkidar: 8th class pass, or GD trade for Armed forces personnel. Female Attendant: Literate with a minimum of 05 years' experience in Civil/Army health institution. Safaiwala: Literate with a minimum of 05 years' experience.
