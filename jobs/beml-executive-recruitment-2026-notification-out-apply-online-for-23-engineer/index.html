BEML Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

Eligible candidates will be shortlisted for assessment. Merely meeting the advertised conditions does not automatically entitle a candidate to be called for assessment. Management reserves the right to restrict the number of candidates and increase the qualifying percentage based on the number of applications received. Shortlisted candidates must undergo a pre-employment medical examination and original document verification. This does not necessarily mean selection. Any shortcoming at document verification - such as falsification of documents, wrong declaration of age/category/qualifying marks, or non-production of original records (including category certificate) - will lead to rejection of candidature. BEML Recruitment 2026: Category Certificate Requirements

SC/ST candidates must submit an SC/ST Caste Certificate in the Government of India format. OBC candidates must submit an Other Backward Class (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in the Government of India format, along with a self-undertaking of Non-Creamy Layer status. BEML Recruitment 2026: Important Instructions for Private Sector Candidates
