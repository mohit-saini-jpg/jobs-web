SSUHS B.Sc Nursing Exam Date 2026 OUT

Srimanta Sankaradeva University of Health Sciences (SSUHS) has officially announced the SSUHS B.Sc Nursing Exam Date 2026 for admission into B.Sc Nursing courses in Assam. The Common Entrance Examination (CEE) will be conducted on 07 June 2026 (Sunday). Candidates can check the complete schedule on the official website - ssuhs.in.

The entrance test will be held in offline mode from 10:00 AM to 12:00 Noon. Students who have applied can download the official notification and important details using their login credentials. Admit cards will be available from 25 May 2026.

Students are advised to verify all details carefully from the official source and contact the university in case of any discrepancy.
