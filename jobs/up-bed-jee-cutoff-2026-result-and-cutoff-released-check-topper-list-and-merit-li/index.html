When Was the UP BEd JEE 2026 Cutoff Released?

Candidates often want to know the exact date the cutoff was announced so they can compare it against their own scorecard. The UP BEd JEE cutoff for 2026 was released on June 16, 2026, on the same day as the result declaration, by Bundelkhand University, Jhansi.

Once the result was announced, the university simultaneously published the cutoff marks needed to qualify for the next stage of admission, which is the counselling and seat allotment process. Candidates do not need to wait separately for a cutoff announcement after their result, since both were released together this year.

How to Check Your UP BEd JEE 2026 Scorecard and Cutoff Status

This section walks candidates through the process of accessing their individual result and checking whether their score meets the cutoff.

To check your UP BEd JEE 2026 scorecard and cutoff status, follow these steps:

  • Visit the official website, bujhansi.ac.in.
  • Locate and click on the UP BEd JEE Result 2026 or scorecard download link.
  • Log in using your registered credentials (application number and password or date of birth, as specified on the portal).
  • Your scorecard will display on screen with your marks and rank.
  • Compare your score against the cutoff for your category and preferred course to assess your qualifying status.
  • Download and save the scorecard as a PDF for future reference, since it will be required again during counselling.

UP BEd JEE 2026 Merit List and Counselling

Candidates frequently want to understand what happens after the result and cutoff are out, particularly how the merit list connects to admission.

After the UP BEd JEE 2026 result and cutoff declaration, the university releases a merit list containing the names of all candidates who scored at or above the cutoff marks. Only candidates who appear in this merit list, and therefore meet the minimum cutoff, are eligible to register for the counselling process. During counselling, candidates choose their preferred B.Ed colleges and courses based on their rank, and seats are allotted accordingly. After seat allotment, candidates must report to their allotted college to complete admission formalities.
