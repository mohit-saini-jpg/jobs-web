The National Testing Agency (NTA) has officially released the NCHMCT JEE Result 2026 for admission to undergraduate hospitality courses. Candidates who appeared for the National Council for Hotel Management Joint Entrance Examination (NCHM JEE) can now check their scorecards online.

NCHMCT JEE Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - https://exams.nta.nic.in/nchm-jee/. The result is available for UG courses such as B.Sc. in Hospitality and Hotel Administration. Students can check and download their results by logging in with their application number and date of birth.

Candidates are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the scorecard. In case of any discrepancies, they should immediately contact the examination authority for correction.

How to Check NCHMCT JEE Result 2026

Follow these steps to download your NCHM JEE 2026 scorecard online:

  • Visit the official website https://exams.nta.nic.in/nchm-jee/
  • Click on the "NCHMCT JEE Result 2026" link
  • Enter your Application Number and Date of Birth
  • Submit the login details
  • Your result will appear on the screen
  • Download and take a printout for future reference
