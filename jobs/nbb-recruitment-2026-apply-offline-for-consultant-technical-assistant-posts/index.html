Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationMaster’s Degree in Agriculture / Horticulture / Entomology / Apiculture and allied subjects
DetailsConsultant: Master Degree in Agriculture/ Horticulture/ Entomology/ Apiculture and allied subjects with First Division or 60% marks or equivalent overall grade points average with 02 years experience in Project formulation, execution, implementation, extension/ management of Government schemes/ programmes/ missions and consultancy in the requisite field. Technical Assistant: Masters Degree in Agriculture/ Horticulture/ Entomology/ Apiculture and allied subjects with minimum 1 year experience in the relevant field preferred.
