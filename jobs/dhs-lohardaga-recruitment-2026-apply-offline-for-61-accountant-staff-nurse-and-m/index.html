DHS Lohardaga Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Office of the Civil Surgeon-cum-Chief Medical Officer, Lohardaga, Jharkhand has released the Lohardaga NHM Recruitment 2026 (Advertisement No. 02/2026) for 23 contractual posts across multiple health programmes under the National Health Mission. Candidates with qualifications ranging from 10+2 to Post Graduate can apply. Applications must reach the office by 24 July 2026 at 5:00 PM via registered post or speed post. The minimum age for all posts is 21 years, calculated as of 31 May 2026.

DHS Lohardaga Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for Lohardaga NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the application is 24 July 2026 by 5:00 PM. Applications received after this date or hand-delivered to the office will not be accepted.
Q2How many total vacancies are available in Lohardaga NHM Bharti 2026?
A
A total of 23 posts are vacant across 23 post codes under programmes including NTEP, NHM, NMHP, NCVBDC, SNCU, MTC, ARSH, RBSK, and NUHM.
Q3What is the application fee for Lohardaga NHM Vacancy 2026?
A
The fee is ₹400 for UR/EWS/OBC/EBC candidates and ₹200 for SC/ST candidates. Persons with 40% or more disability are fully exempted from the fee.
Q4What is the minimum age to apply for Lohardaga NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 21 years, calculated as of 31 May 2026.
Q5Can I apply online for Lohardaga District Health Society Recruitment 2026?
A
No. This recruitment is offline only. Applications must be sent via registered post or speed post. Online applications are not accepted for this notification.
Q6What is the selection process for Lohardaga NHM posts?
A
Candidates are selected through a written examination (60 marks) and academic/training score (40 marks), followed by document verification. A skill test may be conducted for select posts but is only qualifying in nature.
Q7What is the salary for Staff Nurse under NHM Lohardaga?
A
Staff Nurses under NHM Lohardaga will receive a monthly contractual stipend of ₹16,564/-.
Q8Is the appointment permanent?
A
No. All appointments are strictly on a contractual basis for one year, extendable based on performance. There is no right to regularization or permanency.
Q9Which caste certificate format is valid for reservation claims in Lohardaga NHM 2026?
A
Only certificates in the formats prescribed in Appendix III, IV, or V of the notification, issued by competent authorities after the specified cut-off dates, are valid. Older formats or certificates from unauthorized levels are not accepted.
Q10Where can I check the merit list for Lohardaga NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
The final merit list will be published on the official district website at https://lohardaga.nic.in/ and on the notice boards of the CMO office, Civil Surgeon's office, and District Employment Office, Lohardaga.
