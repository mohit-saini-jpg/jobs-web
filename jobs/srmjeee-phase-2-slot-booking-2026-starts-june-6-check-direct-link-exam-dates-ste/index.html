SRMJEEE Phase 2 Slot Booking 2026 Starts June 6: Check Direct Link, Exam Dates & Step-by-Step Guide

SRMJEEE Phase 2 slot booking 2026 kicks off on June 6, and if you're planning to take the exam between June 10–15, you'll need to book your slot ASAP. The SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) has confirmed the dates, and the process is entirely online through the official portal.

Got a bit of a mix-up with the dates? Don't worry slot booking happens just a few days before the exam, so you've got time, but don't wait till the last minute. Slots fill up fast, especially for preferred timings.

How to Book Your SRMJEEE Phase 2 Slot

The process is pretty straightforward, but you'll need your credentials ready. Here's what to do:

  • Head to the official website: applications.srmist.edu.in/btech or srmist.edu.in
  • Click on the slot booking link
  • Login with your registered email ID (that's your Login ID) and password
  • Select your preferred exam centre, date, and timing from available slots
  • You can pick up to 3 slots in order of priority
  • Confirm your selection
  • Download your admit card once booking is complete

Once you've booked your slot, the admit card unlocks. You can't download it before that, so don't panic if it's not showing up immediately after login.

What You Need Before Starting

Don't start the process without these handy:

  • Registered email ID (Login ID)
  • Password
  • Phone with SMS access (for OTP if needed)
  • Stable internet connection
  • Printer or PDF saver for admit card
