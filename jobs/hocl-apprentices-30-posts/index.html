  • 16Upon completion of the Apprenticeship period, HOCL shall have no obligation to offer employment to any apprentice, nor can an apprentice claim the right for employment on the grounds of completion of Apprenticeship.
  • 17Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing Apprenticeship Training under the Apprentices Act in any Government, Public Sector, or Private Organisation are NOT ELIGIBLE to apply. Such candidature, if discovered at any stage, will be cancelled.
  • 18Applications must be submitted exclusively through the respective portal - NAPS (for ITI holders) or NATS (for Diploma/B.Tech holders). Applications through any other method or platform will not be considered.
  • 19The category/reservation claimed during the initial online submission of the application only will be considered. Candidates must produce the relevant certificate in prescribed GOI format at document verification; failing which, their reservation claim will not be considered and they will be treated as Unreserved.
  • 20Candidates must ensure marks are entered correctly in the portal. Difference between entered marks and mark sheet uploaded will result in summary rejection of application.
  • 21For NATS portal applicants: percentage of marks in qualifying examination must be compulsorily entered in the "% of Marks/CGPA" column. If not furnished, candidature will not be considered.
  • 22Call letters for certificate verification/written test/skill test/interview will NOT be sent by post. Candidates will be informed by email or through publication on the HOCL website (www.hoclindia.com). Candidates must frequently check their email and the HOCL website for updates.
  • 23No travel expenses are admissible during the apprenticeship training period or the selection process.
  • 24Accommodation will be provided if available as per company policy; subsidized canteen facility is available during office hours only.
  • 25If at any stage any information furnished is found to be false/incorrect or there has been suppression of facts, the candidate shall not be considered for selection and candidature/appointment will be cancelled without notice.
