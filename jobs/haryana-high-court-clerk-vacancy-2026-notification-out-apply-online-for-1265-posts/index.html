Selection Process

  • Sr. No. Subject Type No. of Questions Maximum Marks Qualifying Marks
  • 1 General Knowledge Objective (MCQ) 50 questions × 1 mark each 50 33% in each subject individually; AND minimum 40% aggregate of both subjects. English Composition (Subjective) checked only for candidates who score 33%+ in General Knowledge.
  • 2 English Composition Objective (MCQ) 50 questions × 1 mark each 20
  • Subjective (typed on computer) i. Essay (150 words) 10
  • ii. Letter 05
  • iii. Precis 05
  • iv. Translation from Hindi to English 10
  • Total 100 Minimum 40% aggregate
  • Part Test Duration Marks Qualifying Marks
  • Part I Spread Sheet Test 10 minutes 10 marks 40% or more (4 or more marks)
  • Part II Computer Typing Test in English (Word Processing) Stipulated time Qualifying 30 WPM (Words Per Minute). Formula: (Words Typed − Mistakes) ÷ 10. Part II checked only for those who qualify Part I.
  • Selection is through a 3-stage process: Online Written Examination (Computer Based Test) → Computer Proficiency Test (qualifying) → Checking of Original Testimonials / Interaction. The final Select List is prepared strictly on the basis of merit in the Written Examination (CBT) only. Computer Proficiency Test marks are NOT counted in the final merit. Success in examination confers no right of appointment unless the Competent Authority is satisfied after enquiry.
  • Stage 1 - Online Written Examination / Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • The CBT is conducted online (Computer Based Test format) in the following subjects. The exam may be held on different dates for different sets of candidates (multiple shifts). Normalization of objective scores using Standard Deviation Method will be applied if exam is held in multiple shifts.
  • Negative Marking: 1/4th mark deducted for every wrong answer in MCQ portion. Questions not attempted receive no credit or penalty. Subjective English Composition will be typed on computer within the stipulated time.
  • Tie-break Rule: If two or more candidates obtain same marks
  • the elder in age is ranked higher. If age is also same
  • candidates are placed alphabetically (A to Z) in the merit list.
  • Answer Key & Objections: Provisional Answer Key will be uploaded on www.sssc.gov.in after CBT. Candidates can raise objections at Rs. 25/- per objection (non-refundable). Cross-objections against proposed changes in provisional answer key are also allowed. Objections/cross-objections must include proposed correct answer and supporting reasons with documentary proof.
  • Stage 2 - Computer Proficiency Test (CPT) - Qualifying Nature Only
  • After CBT results
  • a category-wise determined number of candidates will be called for CPT. CPT marks are NOT counted in final merit - it is purely qualifying in nature.
  • PwBD Exemption from CPT: PwBD with Both Arms Affected (BA) can apply for full exemption from CPT with Disability Certificate. PwBD as defined under Section 2(r) of RPwD Act
  • 2016 can apply for CPT exemption with Disability Certificate + Medical Certificate from CMO/Civil Surgeon/Medical Superintendent of a Government healthcare institution certifying physical limitation to type at 30 WPM. PwBD under Section 2(s) of RPwD Act can apply for exemption with Medical Certificate only. All such applications must be submitted within 15 days of declaration of CBT result.
  • Stage 3 - Checking of Original Testimonials / Interaction
  • Candidates who qualify both CBT and CPT (category-wise as decided by the High Court) will be called for checking of original testimonials/interaction. No TA/DA shall be paid for appearing at any stage of the recruitment process including this stage. The Select List will be prepared strictly on the basis of CBT merit.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply in online mode only at www.sssc.gov.in under the head "Clerk Subordinate Courts of Haryana". No other means/mode of submission of application will be accepted. The online application form is submitted only after payment of fees. All applicants (including reserved category) can submit only one application per category - if more than one application is submitted, only the latest application will be considered.
  • Before starting the Online Application Form, keep the following ready:
  • Personal details (Name, Date of Birth, Category, Sub-category etc.)
  • Valid and active Email ID (keep unchanged throughout the process - do not use email of unknown persons)
  • Valid and active mobile number (keep unchanged throughout the process)
  • Online payment facility - Internet Banking / Debit Card / Credit Card / UPI
  • Scanned recent passport-size photograph (not more than one month old, against white background)
  • Scanned signature
  • Computer/device with inbuilt or attached webcam (for real-time photograph capture during application)
  • Documents to be Uploaded in Online Application Form (before 23-06-2026):
  • Real-time photograph captured via webcam/camera at time of filling form
  • Scanned recent passport-size photograph (not more than one month old)
  • Scanned signature
  • All other requisite documents as specified in the Online Application Form
  • Documents to be Produced at Checking of Original Testimonials / Interaction:
  • Printout of Online Application Form (retain 5 copies of the photograph uploaded in the application)
  • E-Admit Card (downloaded and printed from www.sssc.gov.in, duly self-attested / attested by a Gazetted Officer)
  • Date of Birth certificate / Matriculation certificate
  • Bachelor's degree certificate and mark sheets
  • Matriculation mark sheet showing Hindi/Sanskrit as a subject (or +2/BA/MA mark sheet showing Hindi)
  • Computer proficiency proof
  • Caste certificate (in prescribed format, issued by competent authority of Haryana Government, valid as on 23-06-2026) - for SC/DSC/OSC/BC-A/BC-B/EWS candidates
  • BC category undertaking that no change has occurred in their status and they do not fall in Creamy Layer up to 23-06-2026
  • EWS Income & Assets Certificate (valid for FY 2026-2027, based on FY 2025-2026 income, issued on or before 23-06-2026, on prescribed Haryana Government proforma)
  • Disability Certificate (for PwBD candidates - in prescribed format as per RPwD Act, 2016)
  • ESM Identity Card from Zila Sainik Board / Discharge Certificate or Book
  • ESM Family Member Eligibility Certificate from Zila Sainik Board (valid as on 23-06-2026)
  • Sports Certificate (for ESP candidates - as per applicable Haryana Government orders)
  • Freedom Fighter Certificate from competent authority (for Freedom Fighter / children / grandchildren)
  • No Objection Certificate (for in-service Government employees - from Head of Office/Department)
  • Experience Certificate of equivalent post from Appointing Authority (for adhoc/contract/daily wages employees availing age relaxation)
  • Any other documents in support of eligibility/category claimed in application
  • For Re-checking of Answer Sheets (if required after CBT result): Written request to Member Secretary, S.S.S.C., High Court of Punjab and Haryana, Chandigarh, along with fee of Rs. 500/- per answer sheet (Indian Postal Order payable to S.S.S.C.), within 30 days of declaration of final result. Re-evaluation is not allowed - only re-checking for totalling errors.
  • Apply Online at: www.sssc.gov.in → Clerk Subordinate Courts of Haryana → Registration Form → Application Form → Fee Payment
  • For Technical queries: Helpline No. +91 99866-38762 (Working days, 09:30 AM – 05:00 PM). For terms and conditions queries: Phone 0172-2722012 / 9115898394 (Working days, 09:30 AM – 05:00 PM). All corrigenda/notices will be notified on www.sssc.gov.in only - newspaper advertisements, if any, are secondary; the SSSC website content prevails.

Important Instructions

  • All applicants irrespective of their category (General or reserved) can submit only one application per category. If more than one application is submitted, only the latest application will be considered. All particulars including category and sub-category once filled in the Online Application Form are sacrosanct - no change shall be permitted at any stage of the recruitment process. Fill all details after taking due care and precaution. Reservation benefits (age, fee, category) are only for bonafide residents of Haryana with valid Haryana Government-issued certificates valid as on 23-06-2026. Candidates from other states' reserved categories must apply under General category. The photograph pasted on the E-Admit Card must be the same as uploaded in the Online Application Form. Mismatch will result in denial of entry at the examination centre. Facial appearance at the time of examination must match the photographs in the application and E-Admit Card - failing which candidature can be rejected at any stage. No TA/DA shall be paid for appearing in any stage of the selection process. No mobile phone, electronic device, watch, jewellery, or baggage material is allowed inside the examination centre. The decision of the High Court in all matters relating to acceptance/rejection of application, eligibility/suitability, mode/criteria for selection shall be final and binding. The High Court may shortlist candidates at any stage based on any criteria. Candidates are advised to apply online well before the closing date to avoid last-minute website congestion. The cut-off date is sacrosanct - no extension will be granted under any circumstance, and requests by post/fax/email/by hand will not be entertained. Mere satisfying eligibility criteria does not entitle an applicant to be called for examination. The High Court reserves the right to alter/modify or change any of the terms and conditions including selection criteria. Concealing any material fact or furnishing false information can result in rejection by the High Court at any stage. Canvassing in any form will entail cancellation of candidature. The candidature at all stages is purely provisional - it can be cancelled at any point if the applicant is found not meeting eligibility conditions, even after completion of the selection process. Success in the examination confers no right of appointment unless the Competent Authority is satisfied that the candidate is suitable in all respects for appointment to the service. All communications to SSSC must invariably contain: Advertisement No. & Post Name, Registration ID, Roll No. (if received), Full Name in Block Letters, Registered Email ID, and Correspondence Address as given in the application form. In case of any discrepancy between the advertisement in newspapers and the SSSC website content, www.sssc.gov.in shall prevail. All notices, results, exam dates, and corrigendum will be notified on www.sssc.gov.in only - candidates must check the website and their email (inbox as well as spam box) regularly.
