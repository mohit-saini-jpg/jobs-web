Job Overview

The Sikkim Public Service Commission (SPSC) has invited applications for 37 Warder posts on a temporary regular basis in Level 6 of the Pay Matrix, for the Sikkim State Jail under the Home Department, through Direct Recruitment. The notice was issued as Advertisement No. 22/SPSC/EXAM/2026, dated 17 June 2026, and the last date to apply online is 31 July 2026. Applications must be submitted online only; any application received through another mode will be summarily rejected.

Qualification / Eligibility

Details
  1. This section covers the educational qualification, age limit, and other criteria a candidate must meet to apply for the Warder post
  2. The minimum educational qualification required is Class XII passed from a recognized board. Candidates must possess all prescribed eligibility qualifications, including educational qualifications and age criteria, as on the closing date of application. Candidates awaiting their final results are not eligible to apply, in terms of Notification No. 177/GEN/DOP dated 29.01.2026
  3. Candidates should have attained the age of 18 years, but should not have exceeded 22 years, as on 31 July 2026, as per Notification No. 22/GEN/DOP dated 09.05.2018
  4. Candidates should have knowledge of any of the State Languages. Candidates should be conversant with the customs and usages of Sikkim

Salary & Pay Scale

Details
  1. This section explains the pay level attached to the Warder post and how pay is treated during the initial training period
  2. The Warder post carries a pay of Level 6 of the Pay Matrix, and the engagement is on a temporary regular basis. During the period of Probation, Apprenticeship, or Training, pay will instead be governed by Notification No. 489/GEN/DOP dated 31.10.2011

Important Instructions

This section lists the administrative conditions attached to this recruitment that every applicant should be aware of.
Applications received through any mode other than online will not be accepted and shall be summarily rejected. Candidates must carry a Photo Identity proof (Aadhaar Card, Voters Card, or Driving License) along with a valid e-Admit Card to the examination centre. No TA/DA is admissible for attending the examination. The Commission will entertain applications for review or RTI/correspondence only after the entire recruitment process is complete. Any further instructions, Corrigendum, or Addendum will be uploaded only on the SPSC website.
