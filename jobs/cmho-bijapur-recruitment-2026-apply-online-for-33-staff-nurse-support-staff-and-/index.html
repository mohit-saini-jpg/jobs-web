Job Overview

The latest Chief Medical and Health Office Bijapur (CMHO Bijapur) Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Various Contractual Posts! Published under Advertisement No. 175/NHM/संविदा भर्ती/विज्ञापन/2026/बीजापुर on the official CMHO Bijapur website, this recruitment drive offers 33 openings for eligible candidates. Qualification / Eligibility

Dental Surgeon/Dentist: BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery) with Registration Dental Council of CG Registration. Physiotherapist: Bachelor's Degree in Physiotherapy (B.P.T.) with Registration in CG Council. Medical Officers - RBSK (Male): BHMS/BAMS/BUMS Degree Registration from any GOI Recognised (BAMS/BHMS/BUMS) Ayush, Homeo, Unani Registration Board of CG. Laboratory Technicians (BPHU & NHM): DMLT/BMLT/Pathology in Paramedical Course passed and mandatory registration in Chhattisgarh Paramedical Council. Community Health Officer (CHO): B.Sc. Nursing with Certificate in Community Health Integrated Course. Registration in Chhattisgarh Nursing Council. OR Post Basic Nursing with Certificate in Community Health Integrated Course. Registration in Chhattisgarh Nursing Council. Staff Nurse - NRC: BSC Nursing, or GNM Course Passed. Secretarial Assistant: 12th Passed with at least 1 year Diploma in Computer Application. Jr Secretarial Assistant (PADA): Graduation with at least 1 year Diploma in Computer Application. Counselor: PG Degree/Diploma in Psychology/ Counseling/ Health Education/ Mass Communication/ Social Science. Counselor - Blood Bank: Post-Graduate in Social Work/Sociology/Psychology/ Anthropology/ Human Development. Desirable: Knowledge of Computer. Experience: Minimum Two Years after essential qualification. 2nd ANM: 12th Pass, ANM Passed & INC affiliated Training Center & Live Registration in Chhattisgarh Nursing Registration Counselling. Technical Assistant - Audiometric: Diploma in Hearing Language and speech (DHLS) from RCI Recognized institute. Technical Assistant - hearing Impaired Children: Diploma in Training young deaf and hearing handicapped (DTYDHH) from RCI Recognized institute. Feeding Demonstrator: BSc Home Science. Attendant (NRC), Cook Cum Care Taker, Support staff (housekeeping staff): 8th Pass.
