ESIC Okhla Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Private practice of any kind is not allowed for full-time contractual engagements; violation may attract action under relevant rules. Recruitment is purely contractual; selected candidates have no claim to regularization of service. Canvassing in any form leads to disqualification. Candidates already in service must submit a "No Objection Certificate" from their employer at the time of interview. The contractual engagement may be terminated by either side with one month's prior notice, without assigning any reason. No service benefits such as PF, Pension, Gratuity, Medical Allowance, Seniority, or Promotion are admissible. Absence from work for 10 days without proper permission amounts to abandonment of engagement; no separate disengagement order is necessary in such cases. If a selected candidate resigns within 6 months of joining, an experience certificate will not be issued; in case of termination, the certificate may be withheld. No TA/DA is payable for appearing in the interview or for joining the post. No hostel accommodation, quarters, or uniform will be provided. On joining, the selected candidate must deposit Rs. 10,000/- via demand draft/banker's cheque in favour of "ESIC Fund Account No. 1," refundable after completion of a minimum of 3 months' service (including the 30-day notice period) and submission of a "No Dues Certificate." Selected contractual candidates must sign an Agreement as per ESIC norms on a non-judicial stamp paper of Rs. 100/-, with the stamp paper cost borne by the candidate. The Medical Superintendent, ESIC Hospital Okhla, reserves the right to fill any or no posts, or cancel the interview at any stage, and the number of posts may increase or decrease as per requirement.

ESIC Okhla Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1When is the ESIC Okhla walk-in interview 2026?
A
The walk-in interview for Senior Resident posts is on 27 July 2026, and for Specialist/Super Specialist posts on 28 July 2026, with reporting time from 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM on both days.
Q2How many vacancies are there in ESIC Okhla Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 26 Senior Resident (Under Residency Scheme) posts, 12 Full Time Contractual Specialist posts, and 9 Full Time/Part Time Contractual Super Specialist posts. Vacancy numbers for Contractual Senior Resident (against GDMO) are not specified separately.
Q3What is the age limit for ESIC Okhla Senior Resident post?
A
The maximum age is 45 years as on 27.07.2026, with relaxation for SC/ST/OBC candidates as per rule.
Q4What is the age limit for Specialist and Super Specialist posts?
A
The maximum age is 69 years as on 28.07.2026 for both Full Time Contractual Specialist and Super Specialist posts
Q5What is the salary for ESIC Okhla Senior Resident?
A
Senior Residents under the Residency Scheme receive Rs. 67,700/- (Level-11, 7th CPC) plus allowances; Non-PG Senior Residents on 1-year contract receive a consolidated Rs. 1,50,294/-.
Q6Is there an application fee for ESIC Okhla walk-in interview?
A
Yes. Rs. 500/- for candidates in categories other than SC/ST/PH/Female; the fee is Nil for SC/ST/PH and Female candidates. Payment is via Demand Draft in favour of "ESI Fund A/c No. 01," payable at New Delhi.
Q7What documents are required for the ESIC Okhla walk-in interview?
A
Identity proof, address proof, date of birth certificate, educational mark sheets and certificates (10th, 12th, MBBS, PG), MCI/DMC registration certificate, experience certificates, category certificates (if applicable), and two passport-size photographs.
Q8What is the tenure of Senior Resident, Specialist, and Super Specialist posts?
A
Senior Resident (Under Residency Scheme) is for 3 years with annual evaluation. Full Time Contractual Specialist is for 3 years. Full Time Contractual Super Specialist is for 3 years, and Part Time Contractual Super Specialist is for 1 year, or till a regular incumbent joins, whichever is earlier.
