This section gives you a quick overview of the newly released AKU B.Pharm semester results and who they apply to.

Aryabhatta Knowledge University has declared the B.Pharm (Bachelor of Pharmacy) result 2026 for three semesters 1st, 3rd, and 4th. The results are accessible online through the official AKU examination result portal.

How to Check AKU B.Pharm Result 2026 at results.akuexam.net

Follow these simple steps to access your AKU B.Pharm result 2026 quickly and without any confusion.

Checking your AKU result online is a straightforward process. Students need to visit the official result portal at results.akuexam.net, select the relevant course and semester, enter their registration number, and click the "Show" button to view their result.

Here is the step-by-step process:

  • Visit the official result portal - Go to results.akuexam.net
  • Select your course - Choose "B.Pharm" from the available course list
  • Choose your semester - Select 1st, 3rd, or 4th semester as applicable
  • Enter your Registration Number - Type your AKU registration number in the given field
  • Click "Show" - Press the show/submit button to load your result
  • View and download - Check your marks, grade, and result status; save or print a copy for your records

AKU B.Pharm Result 2026 - Semester-Wise Details

This section covers what each semester result means for students currently in different years of the B.Pharm programme.

The B.Pharm course at AKU is a 4-year, 8-semester undergraduate degree programme. The results released in 2026 cover:

B.Pharm 1st Semester Result 2026

The 1st semester result is for first-year B.Pharm students who recently completed their initial semester exams. This is the starting point of the pharmacy degree and covers foundational subjects in pharmaceutical sciences.

B.Pharm 3rd Semester Result 2026

The 3rd semester result applies to second-year B.Pharm students (first half). This semester introduces core pharmacy subjects including pharmacology and pharmaceutical chemistry.

B.Pharm 4th Semester Result 2026

The 4th semester result is for students completing the second year of their B.Pharm programme. Clearing this semester is essential for progression into the third year of the course.

AKU Result 2026 - Important Information for Students

Here are key facts every B.Pharm student should know about their AKU 2026 semester result and what comes next.

  • AKU results for mid-semester and annual semester examinations are typically published 30 to 35 days after the completion of exams.
  • Students must enter their registration number to download the result scorecard.
  • The university accepts revaluation applications within 15 days of the declaration of the result.
  • Students who are not satisfied with their marks should apply for revaluation through the official AKU website within the specified deadline.

How to Apply for AKU B.Pharm Result 2026 Revaluation

This section explains the revaluation process for students who wish to challenge their result after reviewing their marks.

If you believe your marks do not reflect your actual performance, AKU offers a revaluation (re-checking) facility. Here is what you need to know:

  • Deadline: Apply within 15 days of the result declaration
  • Where to Apply: Visit the official AKU website - akubihar.ac.in or the examination cell
  • Fee: A revaluation fee is applicable per subject (check the official website for the current fee structure)
  • Process: Submit the revaluation form along with the required fee and a copy of your result
