Application Fee - JSSC JILCCE 2026

The application fee for JSSC JILCCE 2026 is affordable and category-specific. Candidates of Jharkhand state's Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes pay a reduced fee, while PwBD candidates of Jharkhand with 40% or above disability are fully exempt from the fee. The fee is non-refundable once paid. Candidates from categories other than SC/ST of Jharkhand who attempt to claim the concessional rate will have their applications cancelled.

Selection Process - JSSC JILCCE 2026

The JSSC JILCCE 2026 selection process is a two-stage examination - Preliminary Exam followed by Main Exam - conducted via Computer Based Test (CBT) or OMR method. If applications for a particular subject exceed 50,000, a Preliminary Exam will generally be held before the Main Exam; if applications are fewer than 50,000, the commission may directly conduct the Main Exam. Scores across multiple shifts are normalised using a published formula available on the JSSC website. The Preliminary Exam is a single paper of 120 multiple-choice questions (3 marks each) with a duration of 2 hours . There is negative marking of 1 mark for each wrong answer. The document verification stage happens after the Main Exam result. Jharkhand JSSC JILCCE Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1The JSSC JILCCE Apply Online 2026 process is entirely web-based at the official JSSC portal. No offline applications are accepted under any circumstances. Candidates must complete registration, fill the form, pay the fee, and upload photo and signature in separate stages - each with its own deadline.
  • 2Step-by-step application process:
  • 3Visit the official JSSC website at https://jssc.jharkhand.gov.in
  • 4Click on the "Online Application for JILCCE-2026" link
  • 5Complete the Registration process - you will receive a Registration Number and Password on your registered mobile number and email
  • 6Note down the Registration Number and Password carefully - they are required for all future logins
  • 7Log in again using these credentials and fill in your detailed personal, educational, and reservation information page by page, clicking "Save and Continue" after each page
  • 8Select the posts you wish to apply for in order of preference (candidates with one or more qualifying degrees can give preference order for multiple posts)
  • 9After fee payment, upload your scanned photograph and full signature
  • 10Once satisfied with the uploaded photo and signature, Submit the application form and take a printout for future reference
  • 11Corrections (except name, DOB, email, and mobile) can be made between 21 August 2026 and 23 August 2026 (midnight)
  • 12If a candidate submits more than one online application, the most recently submitted application will be treated as valid and all previous applications will be cancelled - with no refund of fee for cancelled applications.

Jharkhand JSSC JILCCE Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is JSSC JILCCE 2026?
A
JSSC JILCCE 2026 stands for Jharkhand Intermediate Level Combined Competitive Examination 2026. It is conducted by the Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission to fill 326 vacancies across 6 posts in various Jharkhand government departments.
Q2What is the last date to apply for JSSC JILCCE 2026?
A
The last date for registration and application submission is 19 August 2026 (midnight). The deadline for fee payment and uploading photo and signature is 20 August 2026 (midnight). A correction window is open from 21 to 23 August 2026.
Q3How many vacancies are notified in JSSC JILCCE 2026?
A
A total of 326 regular vacancies are notified across 6 posts: Health Supervisor (137), Multipurpose Health Worker (62), Fishery Technical Assistant (41), Insect Collector (32), Junior Regional Investigator (27), and Veterinary Assistant/Medical Assistant (27).
Q4What is the educational qualification for JSSC JILCCE 2026?
A
All 6 posts require a minimum of Intermediate (10+2) level qualification from a recognised institution. Health Supervisor, Multipurpose Health Worker, and Insect Collector require 10+2 in Science; Junior Regional Investigator requires 10+2 with Mathematics; and Veterinary Assistant and Fishery Technical Assistant require 10+2 in Life Sciences.
Q5What is the application fee for JSSC JILCCE 2026?
A
The fee is ₹100 for General/EWS candidates and ₹50 for SC/ST candidates of Jharkhand. PwBD candidates of Jharkhand with 40% or above disability are fully exempt from the fee.
Q6What is the age limit for JSSC JILCCE 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years. The maximum age for General/EWS candidates is 35 years. SC/ST candidates get a relaxation up to 40 years, female candidates (unreserved) up to 38 years, OBC/EBC candidates (male) up to 37 years, and PwBD candidates get an additional 10 years over their category limit.
Q7What is the selection process for JSSC JILCCE 2026?
A
The selection process has two stages: a Preliminary Examination (120 MCQs, 2 hours) and a Main Examination (3 papers — Language Knowledge, Regional/Tribal Language, and General Knowledge). There is negative marking of 1 mark per wrong answer in both stages. Final merit is based on Paper 2 and Paper 3 of the Main Exam.
Q8Is there negative marking in JSSC JILCCE 2026?
A
Yes. In both the Preliminary and Main Examination, 1 mark is deducted for every wrong answer. Each correct answer carries 3 marks. There is no negative marking for unattempted questions.
Q9What is the official website for JSSC JILCCE 2026?
A
The official website is https://jssc.jharkhand.gov.in. All notifications, the detailed brochure, the application form link, admit cards, and results will be published on this website.
Q10Can candidates apply for more than one post in JSSC JILCCE 2026?
A
Yes. Candidates who hold the required educational qualification for more than one post can apply for multiple posts by selecting them in order of preference within a single online application. Only one application per candidate is allowed; submitting multiple applications will result in cancellation of all earlier ones.
