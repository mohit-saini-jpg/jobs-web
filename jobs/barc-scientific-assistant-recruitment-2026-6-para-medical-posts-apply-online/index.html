BARC Scientific Assistant Recruitment 2026: 6 : Job Overview

Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (BARC), under the Department of Atomic Energy, Government of India, has invited online applications for Group 'B' Para-medical posts through Direct Recruitment. Advertisement No. 06/2026 (R-I) covers 6 vacancies of Scientific Assistant in Psychology, Pathology, Radiography, and Nuclear Medicine Technology, to be posted at BARC Mumbai and Radiation Medicine Research Centre (RMRC), Kolkata. Eligible candidates can apply online at recruit.barc.gov.in from 17 July 2026 to 18 August 2026. This article covers eligibility, age limit, pay scale, application fee, selection

BARC Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

Selection for all four posts (DR/01 to DR/04) will be based on performance in a personal interview. If the number of applications received is high, BARC reserves the right to restrict the number of candidates called for interview by conducting a screening test in the respective discipline. The decision of the Centre in this regard will be final and binding.

Nature of Duties

Scientific Assistant/C (Psychologist): Hospital-based, Occupational Health Care-based, School and Community-based Counselling Services. Scientific Assistant/B (Pathology): Shift duties in the Pathology Laboratory, covering Emergency Laboratory, Phlebotomy, Clinical Pathology, Biochemistry, Hematology, Microbiology (including virology facility), Blood Bank, and Immunology. Scientific Assistant/B (Radiography): Handling various X-ray machines, general radiography including portable X-rays for inwards and operation theatre, special radiological investigations, and handling advanced imaging modalities like computerised radiography, digital radiography, USG, CT, and MRI. Scientific Assistant/B (Nuclear Medicine Technologist): Performing nuclear medicine studies using radioisotopes on uptake probe, gamma camera SPECT systems, and PET/CT scanner; patient preparation and positioning; dispensing
