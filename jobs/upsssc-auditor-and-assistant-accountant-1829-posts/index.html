How to Apply

  • 1PET-2025 Registration Number for login.
  • 2Photograph and signature (as uploaded during PET-2025; cannot be changed — click “Continue” after viewing).
  • 3Details of educational qualification (Board/Institution name, year of passing, roll number, certificate date, percentage/marks).
  • 4Preference order of departments/posts being applied for.
  • 5Category/reservation certificate details (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD/DFF/ESM) as applicable.
  • 6Domicile/permanent address details (auto-populated from PET-2025 data).
  • 7EWS certificate (issued between 01-04-2026 and 03-08-2026 in prescribed format), if applicable.
  • 8Online applications are open from 14-07-2026 to 03-08-2026. Fee reconciliation and application amendment window closes on 10-08-2026.
  • 9Only one application is to be submitted; if a candidate submits more than one application for the same post, only the last submitted application will be considered.
  • 10The application form can be amended only for permitted fields (correspondence address, personal details like EWS/PwBD/DFF category, gender, outstanding sportsperson, marital status, mandatory/desirable qualification) up to 10-08-2026.
  • 11After fee payment and reconciliation, the application is automatically and finally submitted — candidates should download and print it immediately.
  • 12Candidates must indicate category/subcategory of PwBD disability in the application and update it as per the latest UP Government orders on Divyangjan empowerment.
  • 13All information in the application is final — no corrections to date of birth, educational qualification percentage/marks, or caste will be entertained after submission.
  • 14For candidates appearing in examinations at an allotted centre, change of centre will not be entertained under any circumstances.
  • 15Canvassing in any form will lead to cancellation of candidature and debarment from all UPSSSC examinations.
  • 16The Commission does not correspond personally with any candidate. All updates are published on upsssc.gov.in — candidates must regularly check the website.
  • 17The UP Public Examinations (Prevention of Unfair Means) Act, 2024 provisions will be strictly applicable during the examination.
