Jiwaji University B.A. Result 2026 (2nd Year Diploma Course)

The B.A. Second Year Diploma Course supplementary result (II Chance) has been declared for both regular and private students under the NEP (National Education Policy) pattern. Results are published separately for regular (college-enrolled) and private candidates. The result categories include Pass, Fail, Withheld, and Withheld (Unfair Means).

What is the result status for B.A. 2nd Year (NEP) Supp. II Chance?

The B.A. 2nd Year Diploma Course (Supp. II Chance) NEP result includes students in four categories:

  • Pass - Students who have successfully cleared the supplementary examination
  • Fail - Students who could not pass and will need to appear in subsequent attempts
  • Withheld - Results held back pending submission of required documents
  • Withheld (Unfair Means) - Results withheld due to alleged use of unfair means during the exam

Jiwaji University B.Sc. Result 2026 (3rd Year Degree Course)

The B.Sc. Third Year (Degree Course) result under NEP has been declared for both regular and private candidates from the March 2026 examination. This is one of the most anticipated results among the nine declared, covering a large number of science stream students across affiliated colleges.

What are the result divisions for Jiwaji University B.Sc. 3rd Year?

The B.Sc. Third Year result is categorized into the following performance divisions:

  • First Division with Distinction - Awarded to students achieving the highest marks, reflecting exceptional academic performance
  • First Division - Awarded to students scoring in the first division range
  • Pass - Students who have cleared the exam without securing first division marks
  • Fail - Students who did not meet the passing criteria
  • Withheld / ATKT - Results held back due to pending previous semester requirements or other reasons

Jiwaji University M.Sc. Result 2026 (Home Science - Food & Nutrition)

The M.Sc. (Home Science) Food & Nutrition Fourth Semester Exam June 2026 result has also been declared as part of this batch. Students who had pending ATKT (Allowed to Keep Terms) papers from the previous semester should note that their results in this specific exam are listed under a withheld category.

Why is the M.Sc. Home Science result withheld for some students?

For the M.Sc. (Home Science) Food & Nutrition Fourth Semester, certain students' results have been listed as "Withheld Due to Previous Semester ATKT." This means those students had an outstanding backlog from a prior semester, making them ineligible to receive the final semester result until the ATKT is cleared. Affected students should contact the Jiwaji University examination office directly to understand the next steps.

Jiwaji University M.Com. Result 2026 (4th Semester)

The M.Com. Fourth Semester results have been declared across four different categories in this notification — regular, regular ATKT, private, and private ATKT. This ensures comprehensive coverage for all M.Com. postgraduate students who appeared for June 2026 examinations.

Important Instructions for Students with Withheld Results

Students whose results appear as "Withheld" in the official notification must act promptly to avoid missing the deadline. The university has a strict six-month window for withheld result cases.

  • Deadline: Six months from the result declaration date (10 June 2026), i.e., by 10 December 2026
  • Action Required: Visit the Jiwaji University office and submit all necessary documents
  • After Deadline: The university has clearly stated that cases received after the six-month period will not be considered
  • Withheld (Unfair Means) Cases: These are handled separately and may involve a committee review process
