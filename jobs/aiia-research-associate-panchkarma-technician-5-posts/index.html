FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for AIIA Recruitment 2026?
A
The walk-in interview for the AIIA Recruitment 2026 is scheduled to be held on 11th June 2026. Candidates are advised to reach the venue on time with all necessary documents.
Q2How many vacancies are available for the Research Associate and Panchkarma Technician posts?
A
There are a total of 5 vacancies announced by AIIA. Out of these, 1 vacancy is for the Research Associate post and 4 vacancies are for the Panchkarma Technician post.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for the Research Associate position?
A
For the Research Associate position, candidates must possess a Postgraduate degree in Ayurveda with specialization in Panchakarma/Kayachikitsa from a recognized University. Experience and PhD are desirable.
Q4What is the educational qualification required for the Panchkarma Technician position?
A
Candidates applying for the Panchkarma Technician post should have passed 12th standard from a government-recognized board and possess a One Year Certificate Course in Panchkarma Technician with one year of working experience.
Q5What is the maximum age limit for these AIIA posts?
A
The maximum age limit for both Research Associate and Panchkarma Technician posts is 40 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per government norms.
Q6Is there any application fee for AIIA Recruitment 2026?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q7What is the salary offered for the Research Associate post?
A
The selected Research Associate will receive a salary of ₹ 58,000/- per month, plus HRA as admissible for Delhi-NCR.
Q8What is the salary offered for the Panchkarma Technician post?
A
The selected Panchkarma Technician will receive a consolidated salary of ₹ 24,000/- per month.
Q9What is the selection process for AIIA Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process for these posts will include an Interview and Document Verification. Candidates need to perform well in both stages.
Q10Where is the walk-in interview venue for AIIA Recruitment 2026?
A
The walk-in interview will be conducted at All India Institute of Ayurveda (AIIA), New Delhi - 110076. Candidates should report at the specified venue on the interview date.
