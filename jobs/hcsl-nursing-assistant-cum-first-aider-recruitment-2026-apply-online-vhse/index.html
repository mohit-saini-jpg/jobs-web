FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the HCSL Nursing Assistant-Cum-First Aider recruitment?
A
The last date to submit the online application form for the HCSL Nursing Assistant-Cum-First Aider posts is 09 June 2026. Ensure your application is submitted well before this deadline.
Q2What is the educational qualification required for this post?
A
Candidates must have passed VII Standard or possess a Valid First Aid Certificate from a recognized institution, or have VHSE Domestic Nursing, or General Duty Assistant QP No. HSS/Q5101 NSQF Level 4.
Q3What is the age limit for the Nursing Assistant-Cum-First Aider position?
A
The maximum age limit for this post is 30 years. Age relaxation is applicable for Ex-Servicemen and Persons with Benchmark Disabilities as per government guidelines.
Q4How many vacancies are available for the Nursing Assistant-Cum-First Aider post?
A
There is one vacancy available for the Nursing Assistant-Cum-First Aider post on a contract basis.
Q5What is the application fee for General and OBC candidates?
A
The application fee for General and OBC candidates is Rs. 300/-. SC/ST candidates are exempted from paying any application fee.
Q6What is the salary structure for the selected candidate?
A
The selected candidate will receive a salary ranging from ₹ 22,100/- in the first year to ₹ 23,400/- in the third year per month.
Q7What is the selection process for this recruitment?
A
The selection process will involve an objective type written test, followed by a practical test, and finally, certificate verification.
Q8Is there any relaxation in the age limit?
A
Yes, age relaxation is provided for Ex-Servicemen and Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwBD), not exceeding 40 years, in accordance with Government of India guidelines.
Q9What is the mode of application for this job?
A
The application process is entirely online. Candidates need to visit the official HCSL website to fill out and submit their application forms.
Q10Where can I find the official notification and apply online?
A
You can find the direct links to the official notification PDF and the online application portal in the "Important Links" section of this article.
