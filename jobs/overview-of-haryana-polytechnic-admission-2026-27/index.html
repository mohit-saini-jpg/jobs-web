Notification DatePost Date: 21/04/2026

Haryana Polytechnic Admission 2026-27 application registration has officially started from 21 April 2026 for Diploma Engineering, Lateral Entry, and Pharmacy courses. Conducted by the Haryana State Technical Education Society (HSTES), admissions are merit-based (no entrance exam) through online off-campus counselling on the basis of 10th or 12th marks. Eligible candidates can register online at the official portal techadmissionshry.gov.in. This complete guide covers important dates, eligibility, fees, application process, and direct links so you can apply without missing any deadline.

How to Apply for Haryana Polytechnic Admission 2026-27

  • Visit the official portal → Apply Online.
  • Register using valid email and mobile number.
  • Fill the online application form with personal, academic, and category details (PPP ID mandatory for Haryana candidates).
  • Upload scanned documents (PDF, max 100 KB each).
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Submit and take printout of the confirmation page.
  • Visit designated verification centre (if required) for document check.

Documents Required for Haryana Polytechnic Admission 2026-27

  • PPP ID (mandatory for Haryana residents)
  • 10th / 12th mark sheet & certificate
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Income certificate (for EWS)
  • Domicile certificate of Haryana
  • ITI certificate (for Lateral Entry)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Scanned signature
