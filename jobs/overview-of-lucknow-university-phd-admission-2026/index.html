Notification DatePost Date: 30/05/2026

Lucknow University PhD Admission 2026 online application has started. Lucknow University has invited applications for Ph.D. admission for the academic session 2026-27 from 27 May 2026. The last date to submit the online application form is 30 June 2026.

Eligible candidates can apply for both Full-Time (Regular) and Part-Time Ph.D. programmes through the official Samarth Portal. The application fee is ₹2000 for General/OBC/EWS and ₹1000 for SC/ST/PwD candidates. Aspirants are advised to read the Ph.D. Ordinance 2026 and Guidelines carefully before applying. This is a golden opportunity for research aspirants seeking admission in one of the prestigious universities of Uttar Pradesh.

How to Apply for Lucknow University PhD Admission 2026

  • Visit the official website: www.lkouniv.ac.in
  • Go to the Samarth Portal and click on Ph.D. Admission link.
  • Register with a valid email ID and mobile number.
  • Fill the online application form.
  • Upload required documents.
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Submit the form and take a printout.

Eligibility Criteria for Lucknow University PhD Admission 2026

Candidates must have a Master’s degree or a 4-year Bachelor’s degree with a minimum 55% marks (50% for SC/ST/OBC (non-creamy layer)/EWS/PwD). For candidates with a 4-year UG degree, a minimum 75% marks is required (with relaxation as per UGC rules). Candidates who have completed M.Phil. are also eligible as per UGC norms.

Courses Offered / PhD Programmes at Lucknow University

Lucknow University offers Ph.D. programmes in various subjects across Arts, Science, Commerce, Law, Education, Management and other faculties. Admissions are available in both Full-Time (Regular) and Part-Time categories. Full-time scholars must complete a minimum of three years of residence. Part-time admission is available for employed candidates with NOC and outstanding working professionals. Detailed subject-wise seats and research areas are available on the official website and Ph.D. Ordinance 2026.
