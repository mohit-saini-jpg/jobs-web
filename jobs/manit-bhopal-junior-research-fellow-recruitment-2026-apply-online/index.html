Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the MANIT Bhopal JRF recruitment?

The last date to submit the application form for the Junior Research Fellow (JRF) position at MANIT Bhopal is June 2, 2026. Ensure your application is submitted before this deadline.

Q2. What are the educational qualifications required for the JRF post?

Candidates must possess an M.E./M. Tech in relevant fields of Electrical, Power Systems, Control Systems, Electronics, Power Electronics, Communication, or Computer Engineering. Alternatively, a B. Tech/B.E. with a minimum CGPA of 7.5/10 and a valid GATE qualification is also accepted.

Q3. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive. Candidates can apply without paying any fee.

Q4. What is the salary offered for the Junior Research Fellow position?

The salary for the Junior Research Fellow (JRF) position is Rs. 37,000/- per month, plus HRA. For a Senior Research Fellow (SRF) position, the salary is Rs. 42,000/- per month, plus HRA.

Q5. How will candidates be selected for the JRF post?

The selection process involves shortlisting candidates based on their academic credentials, relevant background, and suitability for the project. Final selection will be based on performance in the interview.

Q6. What is the mode of application for this recruitment?

The application process for the MANIT Bhopal Junior Research Fellow (JRF) recruitment is entirely online. Candidates need to apply through the official MANIT Bhopal website.

Q7. What is the advertisement number for this recruitment?

The advertisement number for this recruitment is ANRF/ARG/2025/007606/ENS. This number should be used for reference when applying or inquiring about the position.

Q8. What is the total number of vacancies available?
