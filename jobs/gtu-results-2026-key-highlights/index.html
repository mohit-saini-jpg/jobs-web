How to Download GTU Sem 8 Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download GTU Semester 8 Result 2026:

  • Visit the official website - gtu.ac.in
  • Click on the Results section available on the homepage
  • Select the Semester 8 Result 2026 link
  • Enter your enrollment number or seat number
  • Click on submit
  • Your result will be displayed on the screen
  • Download and print the result for future reference
