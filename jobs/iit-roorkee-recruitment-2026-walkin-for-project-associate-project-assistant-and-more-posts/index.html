Details

Research Scientist: Ph.D / M.Tech. in Disaster Mitigation & Management / Earthquake Engineering / Earth Sciences or equivalent. Desired: Ph.D. with minimum 2 years or M.Tech with 4 years experience in earthquake early warning systems. Working experience on Earthworm platform. PhD candidates preferred. Project Associate: B.Tech in any stream / M.Sc. in Computer Sciences or equivalent. Desired: Working knowledge in network routers, switches and cloud computing. Working knowledge of website development, mobile application development and remote network accessing. Sr. Project Assistant (Technical): Diploma (3 years duration) or graduate. Desired: Minimum of 7 year experience in EEW siren installation and maintenance. Knowledge in report preparation using MS Office suite. Project Assistant (Technical): Diploma (3 years duration) or B.Tech. in Electronics and Communications. Desired: 3 year hands-on experience fabricating electronic devices such as in EEW sensor/siren installation and maintenance. Knowledge in report preparation using MS Office suite.
