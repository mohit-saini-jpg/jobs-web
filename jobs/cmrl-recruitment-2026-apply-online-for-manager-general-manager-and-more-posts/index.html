CMRL Recruitment 2026 Eligibility Criteria

CMRL has laid out separate eligibility criteria for candidates coming from Government organizations, Metro/PSU organizations, and the Private Sector. A summary is given below. Govt Organization: Officer with 5 years' service in Senior Administrative Grade (SAG) in Railways/equivalent in GoI, or 5 years as Chief Engineer in State Govt organizations. Metro/PSU: Minimum 25 years post-qualification executive experience with at least 3 years as GM in IDA pay scale Rs. 1,20,000-2,80,000 or equivalent. Private Sector: Minimum 25 years post-qualification executive experience with at least 3 years in top management reporting directly to a Director or Board, in a company with project size of at least Rs. 500 crore or turnover of at least Rs. 100 crore in the last 3 years. CMRL Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

The CMRL selection process for these posts consists of two stages: Interview: Candidates are assessed on knowledge, skills, comprehension, attitude, and aptitude. Medical Examination: The first medical examination is free of cost, borne by CMRL. If a candidate requests an extension for joining, the cost of the second medical examination is borne by the candidate. Travel expenses for the medical test are borne by the candidate. Candidates who fail the medical test are not offered alternative employment. Only shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview; merely meeting the eligibility criteria does not guarantee an interview call. CMRL reserves the right to shortlist candidates and to relax or tighten eligibility standards based on the response received.
