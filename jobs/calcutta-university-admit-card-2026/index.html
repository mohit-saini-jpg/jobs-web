How to Download Calcutta University Admit Card 2026

Students can download their admit card by following these steps:

  • Visit the official Calcutta University website.
  • Navigate to the Examination or Admit Card section.
  • Open the link related to the B.A./B.Sc./B.Com Semester VI Examination 2026 Admit Card.
  • Enter the required credentials such as Registration Number or Roll Number.
  • Submit the details.
  • The admit card will appear on the screen.
  • Download the PDF and take multiple printouts for examination purposes.

Students are advised to keep a copy of the admit card until the completion of the examination and result process.

Details Mentioned on Calcutta University Admit Card 2026

Candidates should carefully check the following information printed on the admit card:

  • Candidate’s Name
  • University Registration Number
  • Roll Number
  • Examination Name
  • Course Name (B.A./B.Sc./B.Com)
  • Semester Details
  • Subject/Paper Information
  • Examination Date
  • Examination Timing
  • Examination Centre Name
  • Examination Centre Address
  • Candidate Photograph
  • Candidate Signature (if applicable)
  • Important Instructions for Examination

In case of any error or discrepancy, candidates should immediately contact their college authorities or the university examination department.

Documents Required Along with Calcutta University Admit Card 2026

Candidates must carry the following documents on the examination day:

  • Printed Copy of Calcutta University Admit Card 2026
  • Original College Identity Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Any Other Government-Issued Photo Identity Proof
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • PwD Certificate (if applicable)

Candidates failing to produce the required documents may not be permitted to appear for the examination.

Exam-Day Instructions for Calcutta University Semester VI Examination 2026

Students should follow these important guidelines on the day of the examination:

  • Reach the examination centre at least 30-45 minutes before the reporting time.
  • Carry a printed admit card and valid photo identity proof.
  • Verify your examination room and seating arrangement before entering the hall.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, Bluetooth devices, calculators, or any electronic gadgets unless specifically permitted.
  • Follow all instructions issued by invigilators and examination authorities.
  • Use only permitted stationery items.
  • Fill in all required details correctly on answer scripts and attendance sheets.
  • Maintain discipline throughout the examination.
  • Avoid any form of unfair means or malpractice.
  • Candidates violating examination rules may face disciplinary action.
