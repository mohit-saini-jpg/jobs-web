Eligibility and Educational Qualification

As per Schedule-I of the "Rules 2019," candidates must fulfil the following criteria for the post of Senior Computer Programmer Assistant: B.E./B.Tech /B.Sc in Computer Science/IT/Electronics and Telecommunications, or B.C.A./M.C.A., or M.Sc. (Electronics/CS/IT), with at least 1 year of experience working on Linux/Open Source Software/Windows/DBMS/Software Development. Work experience of not less than 2 years in a District Court Establishment or the High Court of Madhya Pradesh. The candidate must not have been punished in any departmental enquiry, or ever punished for misconduct or any other reason. Only persons having work experience of not less than 2 years in a District Court Establishment or High Court in the State of Madhya Pradesh are eligible to appear in the suitability test, subject to meeting the above essential qualifications. MP High Court Senior Computer Programmer Assistant : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

The selection process consists of the following stages: Preliminary Screening Examination (MCQ) Practical Examination Interview Past Performance Appraisal The final merit list for selection is prepared on the basis of aggregate marks obtained in the Practical Examination, Interview, and Past Performance Appraisal, subject to securing the prescribed minimum marks. Marks obtained in the Preliminary Screening Examination are used only to shortlist candidates and are not counted in the final merit. The question paper contains 100 multiple choice objective-type questions of 1 mark each, to be solved within 2 hours (120 minutes) on an OMR sheet, in English. MP High Court Senior Computer Programmer Assistant : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Applications must be submitted offline. The application form is not filled online.
  • 2Download the Application Form from the official website of the High Court of Madhya Pradesh (www.mphc.gov.in), along with the advertisement.
  • 3Take a printout and fill it in Block Letters, in English only. All fields are mandatory; write N/A where not applicable.
  • 4Sign the form and attach self-attested photocopies of all required documents/testimonials along with recent coloured photographs, serial-wise as per the checklist in the form.
  • 5Place the completed application and documents in an envelope marked "Direct Recruitment Through Suitability Test For The Post of Senior Computer Programmer Assistant in District Courts of the State of Madhya Pradesh - 2026" on top.
  • 6Send the envelope to: Registrar (Exam), Examination Section, Administrative Block, High Court of Madhya Pradesh, Jabalpur (M.P.)
  • 7Ensure the application reaches the Receipt Section on or before 10.08.2026. It is the candidate's sole responsibility to ensure timely receipt.
  • 8There is no provision for correction after submission of the application form. Applications received after the last date will not be entertained for any reason, including postal delay. Candidates are advised to keep a copy of their finally submitted application form.
