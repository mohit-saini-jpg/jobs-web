WB ANM GNM Response Sheet 2026 - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 17/07/2026

The West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) has released the WB ANM & GNM Response Sheet 2026 along with the provisional model answer key for the entrance examination held on 14 June 2026. Candidates can now log in with their application number and password to view their OMR recorded response and download the model answer key. Those who wish to challenge any answer can do so online until 11 July 2026 (11:59 PM) by paying an objection fee of ₹500 per question.

WB ANM GNM Response Sheet 2026 - Quick Overview

WBJEEB conducted the OMR-based ANM(R) & GNM Common Entrance Test 2026 on 14 June 2026 for admission to the two-year Auxiliary Nursing & Midwifery (Revised) course and the three-year General Nursing & Midwifery course for the 2026-27 academic session. The response sheet and model answer key were made available on the official portal on 16 July 2026.

How to Download WB ANM GNM Response Sheet 2026

Candidates can follow these steps to download their OMR recorded response and model answer key from the official WBJEEB admissions portal.

  • Visit the official ANM & GNM Candidate Activity portal linked on wbjeeb.nic.in.
  • Click on the "OMR Recorded Response – View & Challenge for ANM(R) & GNM 2026" link.
  • Log in using your Application Number and Password.
  • Your response sheet and the model answer key will be displayed on screen.
  • Check your question booklet series (printed at the bottom right corner of page 7 of the booklet) to match the correct answer key.
  • Download or take a printout for your records.

How to Raise an Objection Against the Answer Key

Candidates who find a discrepancy in the model answer key can submit a challenge before the deadline of 11 July 2026, 11:59 PM. Each challenge requires a non-refundable fee of ₹500 per question.

  • Log in to the official portal using your Application Number and Password.
  • Open the answer key challenge section and select the question(s) you want to contest.
  • Upload supporting documents or a valid reference explaining your objection.
  • Pay the objection fee of ₹500 per question via Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, or UPI.
  • Review the submitted details carefully and confirm before the deadline.

The Board's decision on each objection will be treated as final and will be used to prepare the final answer key.

Why the Response Sheet Matters

The response sheet lets candidates verify exactly what was recorded against each question in the OMR sheet, independent of whether the answer given was correct. Comparing it with the model answer key helps candidates:

  • Confirm that their marked responses were captured correctly
  • Estimate their likely score before the official result
  • Identify any answers worth challenging within the objection window
  • Spot and report data-entry mismatches, if any, before the deadline

Important Instructions

  • The model answer key is provisional; only the final answer key issued after resolving objections will be used for result preparation.
  • Objections without valid supporting explanation or documentation may not be considered.
  • The objection fee is non-refundable regardless of the outcome of the challenge.
  • Candidates should retain their application number and password securely, as these are required for all portal logins.
