Q1What is the last date to apply for AIIMS Bhopal Senior Resident posts?

A
Candidates must possess a Postgraduate Medical Degree viz. MD/MS/DNB in the respective disciplines recognized by NMC/Institute of National Importance, along with valid registration with NMC/State Medical Council.

A
The maximum age limit for applying is 45 years. Age relaxations are applicable for OBC, SC/ST, and PwBD candidates as per government rules.

Q6What is the salary offered for the Senior Resident posts?

The selected candidates will receive a salary of Rs. 67,700/- (Level-11, Cell No. 01 as per 7th CPC) plus usual allowances including NPA.
