Calicut University Time Table 2026 Out

Calicut University has officially released the Revised Time Table 2026 for First Semester B.Voc. (CUFYVP) Regular Examinations November 2025 for 2025 admission candidates of affiliated colleges. The revised timetables are available for Major Courses, Minor Courses, and General Foundation Courses. Students can download the official Calicut University Time Table 2026 PDF from the Pareeksha Bhavan portal.

How to Download Calicut University Time Table 2026

Students can download the Calicut University Time Table 2026 from the official examination portal by following the steps given below:

  • Visit the official Pareeksha Bhavan website of Calicut University.
  • Go to the Time Table section on the homepage.
  • Look for the B.Voc CUFYVP First Semester November 2025 revised timetable links.
  • Click on the relevant Major / Minor / General Foundation Course timetable.
  • The PDF will open. Download and save the file.
  • Take a printout for easy reference during exam preparation.

Note: Always refer to the official revised timetable and check all dates and instructions carefully before the examinations.
