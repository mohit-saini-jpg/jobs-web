Important Instructions for Candidates

  • Candidates must strictly adhere to all instructions issued by the CBSE from time to time.
  • Communication devices are strictly prohibited inside the Examination Centre. Possession or use of such devices will invite disciplinary action under the UFM rules.
  • Candidates are required to maintain strict discipline within the Examination Centre.
  • The duration of each examination is specified in the date sheet as well as on the Admit Card.
  • An additional 15 minutes of reading time will be provided to students for perusal of the question paper.
  • For the latest updates, students are advised to visit the official website: www.cbse.gov.in

How to Download the CBSE Supplementary Date Sheet 2026

The date sheet is issued directly by CBSE as an official PDF notification. The exam schedule above reflects the full contents of that notification. For any future updates or corrigenda, candidates should check the official CBSE website.

  • Visit the Official Website : Go to CBSE Official Website.
  • Find the Notification : On the homepage, locate the "Latest @ CBSE" or "Examinations" section.
  • Select the Link : Click on the link titled "Date Sheet for Class X and XII".
  • View and save : The datesheet will open in a PDF format. Download and save the PDF for your future reference or print it out for your study desk
