How to Check KTET Result 2026 Online

Checking your KTET 2026 result is pretty straightforward if you follow these steps carefully:

  • Visit the official portal at ktet.kerala.gov.in
  • Look for the link titled "KTET FEBRUARY 2026 RESULT" on homepage
  • Select your exam Category (1, 2, 3, or 4) from dropdown
  • Enter your Register Number and Date of Birth in required fields
  • Click on "Check Results" button to view score
  • Download the result sheet as PDF and take printout for future reference

The scorecard will display on your screen immediately after clicking the check results button. Make sure you save it properly because you'll need it for teaching job applications later.

Documents Required for Certificate Download

While downloading your KTET scorecard, keep these details handy:

  • KTET registration number (received during application)
  • Date of birth as per application form
  • Category selected during registration
  • Valid email ID for saving PDF copy

What to Do After Checking Result

If you've qualified the KTET exam, here are your next steps:

  • Download and save multiple copies of your scorecard in PDF format
  • Take printouts and keep them safely for future job applications
  • Register on Kerala PSC website for teacher recruitment notifications
  • Start preparing for Kerala PSC exams specific to teaching positions
  • Keep checking official websites for latest recruitment announcements
  • Update your resume with KTET qualification details
