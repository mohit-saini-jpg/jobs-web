Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for AIIMS Mangalagiri Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit online applications for the AIIMS Mangalagiri Senior Residents/Senior Demonstrators posts is 30th May 2026. Candidates are advised to apply well before the deadline to avoid any last-minute issues.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for Senior Residents/Senior Demonstrators?

There are a total of 21 vacancies for the posts of Senior Residents/Senior Demonstrators at AIIMS Mangalagiri. These vacancies are distributed across various medical departments.

Q3. What is the minimum qualification required for these posts?

The minimum qualification required is a Postgraduate medical degree from a recognized university/institute or an equivalent qualification. Specific departmental qualifications are also detailed in the notification.

Q4. What is the age limit for applicants?

The maximum age limit for applicants is 45 years. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST, OBC, and PwBD candidates as per government rules.

Q5. What is the application fee for General category candidates?

The application fee for General and OBC category candidates is ₹1,500/-. SC/ST candidates need to pay ₹1,000/-, and PwBD candidates are exempted from the fee.

Q6. What is the salary offered for these positions?

The selected candidates will receive a salary in the range of Rs. ₹56,100/- to ₹67,700/- per month, as per the pay matrix of the government.

Q7. What is the selection process for AIIMS Mangalagiri Recruitment 2026?

The selection process primarily involves an interview. However, if a large number of applications are received, a screening test (MCQs) may be conducted, followed by an interview.

Q8. Is this a permanent or contract job?

The job type mentioned in the notification is 'Contract'. Candidates should refer to the official notification for details regarding the duration of the contract.
