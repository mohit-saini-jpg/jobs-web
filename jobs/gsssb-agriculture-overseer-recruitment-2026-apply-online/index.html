GSSSB Agriculture Overseer Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Diploma in Horticulture obtained from a Polytechnic of any of the Agriculture/Horticulture Universities incorporated or established by or under the Central Agriculture/Horticulture Universities Act or State Agriculture/Horticulture Universities Act in India. The basic knowledge of computer application as prescribed in the Gujarat Civil Services Classification and Recruitment (General) Rules, 1967. Adequate knowledge of Gujarati or Hindi or both.
