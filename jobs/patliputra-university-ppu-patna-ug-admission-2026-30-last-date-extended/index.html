Patliputra University (PPU) Patna UG Admission 2026-30: Last Date Extended

Patliputra University, Patna has extended the last date for online applications for Four-Year Undergraduate (UG) Degree Programmes under NEP-2020 for the academic session 2026-30. Applications are invited for B.A., B.Sc., and B.Com. programmes in the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) for the First Semester.
