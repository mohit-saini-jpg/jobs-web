Shahjahanpur Anganwadi Karyakartri Recruitment 2026 - 333 : Job Overview

The Office of the District Programme Officer (Child Development), Shahjahanpur has invited online applications for 333 Anganwadi Karyakartri (Anganwadi Worker) posts across 16 Child Development Projects in the district's rural and urban areas. This is a direct recruitment for honorarium-based vacant posts. Applications opened on 13 July 2026 and will close on 3 August 2026 at 11:59 PM. Only local women candidates are eligible, and applications must be submitted online through the vacancy management portal.

Shahjahanpur Anganwadi Karyakartri Eligibility Criteria

Only local women candidates are eligible to apply. Minimum educational qualification: Intermediate (12th) or equivalent. The candidate must be a resident of the ward (in case of an urban local body) or the gram panchayat (in case of a rural area) where the vacant post is located. The entire selection process will be governed by Government Order No. 1/1092245/2025/3313/58-1-2025 (1917687) dated 17 September 2025.

Shahjahanpur Anganwadi Karyakartri Age Limit

Minimum age: 18 years Maximum age: 35 years Age will be calculated as on the cut-off date: 1 July 2026. Date of birth recorded in the High School marksheet/certificate will be considered valid for age calculation.

Shahjahanpur Anganwadi Karyakartri Salary/Honorarium

The notification confirms these are honorarium-based (मानदेय आधारित) posts, but the exact honorarium amount is not specified in the notification.

Shahjahanpur Anganwadi Karyakartri Selection Process

Selection is merit-based. Candidates must hold a minimum qualification of Intermediate or equivalent. Merit is determined by adding the marks of Graduate or Postgraduate qualifications (where applicable) to the Intermediate or equivalent marks, calculated through the departmental portal as per the method laid out in Clause 7, points (I) to (IV), of the governing Government Order. A post reserved for a specific category will accept applications only from candidates of that category. Unreserved category posts are open to candidates of any caste. Widow candidates: A death certificate issued by the Gram Panchayat Officer/concerned Urban Local Body, or a certified copy of the family register, will be accepted. Divorced/deserted candidates: A legal order issued by a competent court will be accepted. Income certificate: Only income certificates issued per UP Government Order No. 3959/1-9-11-Raj
