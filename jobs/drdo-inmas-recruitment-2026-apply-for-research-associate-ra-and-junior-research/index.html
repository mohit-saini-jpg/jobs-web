DRDO INMAS Recruitment 2026: Apply for Research Associate : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the total number of vacancies available in INMAS DRDO Recruitment 2026?
A
INMAS, DRDO has announced a total of 15 vacancies, including 4 Research Associate (RA) posts and 11 Junior Research Fellow (JRF) posts.
Q2What is the last date to apply for INMAS DRDO Recruitment 2026?
A
Eligible candidates must submit their applications via email on or before 30 June 2026.
Q3What is the salary for Research Associate (RA) posts in INMAS DRDO?
A
Selected Research Associates will receive ₹67,000 per month plus HRA as per DRDO rules and a contingency grant of up to ₹20,000 per annum.
Q4What is the stipend for Junior Research Fellow (JRF) posts?
A
JRFs will receive ₹37,000 per month plus HRA during the first two years. After promotion to SRF, the fellowship increases to ₹42,000 per month plus HRA.
Q5What is the age limit for INMAS DRDO Recruitment 2026?
A
The maximum age limit is 35 years for Research Associate posts and 28 years for Junior Research Fellow posts as on the application closing date. Age relaxation is applicable as per government rules.
Q6What is the selection process for INMAS DRDO RA and JRF Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process includes application screening, a written test (if required), and a personal interview. Only shortlisted candidates will be considered for further stages.
Q7How can candidates apply for INMAS DRDO Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must fill out the prescribed application form, combine all supporting documents into a single PDF, and email it to [email protected] before the closing date
Q8Is NET, GATE, or GPAT mandatory for JRF posts?
A
Yes, for most JRF disciplines candidates must possess a valid NET, GATE, or GPAT qualification as specified in the eligibility criteria. Certain M.Tech./M.E. candidates may be exempted from this requirement.
