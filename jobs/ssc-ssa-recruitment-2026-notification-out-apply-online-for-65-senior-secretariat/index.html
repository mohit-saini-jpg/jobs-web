SSC SSA Recruitment 2026 Notification Out : Eligibility & Qualification

Details
  1. The SSC SSA / UDC Limited Departmental Competitive Examination 2025 is open only to regularly appointed Lower Division Clerks (JSA/LDC) serving in the Central Secretariat Clerical Service (CSCS) cadre of DoPT. The crucial date for counting approved service is 1 August 2025. Candidates must satisfy all the following conditions before applying
  2. A candidate must have rendered not less than five years' approved and continuous service in the Lower Division Clerk Grade as on 01 August 2025. However, if the candidate was appointed to the LDC grade through a competitive examination (including a Limited Departmental Competitive Examination), the results of such examination must have been declared at least five years before the crucial date and the candidate must have rendered at least four years of approved and continuous service in that grade
  3. Unless exempted, the candidate must have passed the typewriting test held by UPSC or SSC for confirmation in the Lower Division Clerk grade on or before the date of this notification. Candidates who have been granted exemption from the typewriting test must quote the order number and date of such exemption in the application form
  4. LDCs on deputation to ex-cadre posts with the approval of the competent authority are eligible to apply if they otherwise fulfill all eligibility criteria. However, a candidate who has been appointed to an ex-cadre post or another service on transfer without retaining a lien in the LDC grade is not eligible. A candidate who resigns, quits service, or whose services are terminated after applying will not be eligible for appointment on the result of this examination

SSC SSA Recruitment 2026 Notification Out : Exam Pattern

The SSC Senior Secretariat Assistant exam 2026 is conducted in two parts. Part-I is a Computer Based Examination (CBE) of 200 marks with four subjects, each carrying 50 marks and 50 questions. Part-II is an evaluation of service records (APARs) worth 100 marks. The final rank of the candidate is determined by adding both Part-I and Part-II scores. | All questions are of Objective Type Multiple Choice format. There is a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer. Candidates must attempt the examination in the language (English or Hindi) opted in the application form, except the "General English" section of the first subject, which must be attempted in English only. No Braille question paper will be provided. | Also read: SSC JSA LDC DoPT LDCE Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 49 Posts | One hundred marks are earmarked for evaluation of Annual Performance Appraisal Reports (APARs). Only candidates who qualify Part-I (CBE) will be called for APAR evaluation. Marks from Part-II are added to the Part-I score to determine final merit. Once APARs are evaluated, no request for change in grading will be entertained.

SSC SSA Recruitment 2026 Notification Out : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the SSC SSA Recruitment 2026 and who is it for?
A
The SSC SSA Recruitment 2026 refers to the Senior Secretariat Assistant / Upper Division Clerk Grade Limited Departmental Competitive Examination, 2025, conducted by the Staff Selection Commission. It is open exclusively to serving Lower Division Clerks (JSA/LDC) of the Central Secretariat Clerical Service (CSCS) cadre under DoPT — not for general public candidates.
Q2How many vacancies are available in SSC UDC Vacancy 2026?
A
A total of 65 vacancies are available under the Central Secretariat Clerical Service (CSCS) cadre of DoPT. Category-wise breakup (UR/SC/ST/PwBD) has not been specified in the current notification and will be communicated in due course.
Q3What is the last date to apply for SSC SSA Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 16 July 2026 (up to 11:00 PM / 2300 hours). The printed application hard copy must reach SSC (Northern Region), New Delhi through the Head of Department by 23 July 2026 (6:00 PM).
Q4What is the eligibility condition for SSC SSA UDC Limited Departmental Exam 2025?
A
Candidates must be regularly appointed Lower Division Clerks in the CSCS cadre of DoPT with not less than five years' approved and continuous service in the LDC grade as on 01 August 2025. If appointed through a competitive exam, the results must have been declared at least five years before the crucial date and the candidate must have at least four years of approved service.
Q5Is there an application fee for SSC SSA Apply Online 2026?
A
The notification does not specify any application fee for this Limited Departmental Competitive Examination. As it is an internal departmental exam, no fee has been mentioned. Candidates should confirm on the official SSC website at ssc.gov.in before applying.
Q6Where will the SSC SSA exam 2026 be held?
A
The Computer Based Examination will be held tentatively in August 2026 at centres in Delhi-NCR only. No request for change of centre will be entertained under any circumstances. Candidates serving at Indian missions abroad must appear in India at their own expense.
Q7What is the exam pattern for SSC SSA / UDC Limited Departmental Exam 2026?
A
Part-I is a Computer Based Examination of 200 marks covering four subjects — Comprehension and General English (50 marks), Parliamentary Procedure (50 marks), General Knowledge (50 marks), and Noting and Drafting (50 marks) — with a duration of 3 hours. Part-II covers APAR evaluation for 100 marks. Total marks for final selection: 300.
Q8What is the salary for SSC SSA / UDC post?
A
The SSC SSA / UDC post is at Pay Level-4 in the 7th Pay Commission Pay Matrix, with a pay range of Rs. 25,500 to Rs. 81,100 per month. It is a Group C Non-Gazetted post under DoPT, and all applicable central government allowances are additionally admissible.
Q9Is the typewriting test mandatory for SSC SSA eligibility?
A
Yes. A candidate must have passed the typewriting test held by UPSC or SSC for confirmation in the LDC grade on or before the date of notification, unless they have been formally exempted. Candidates who are exempted must quote the exemption order number and date in the application form.
Q10Where can I download the SSC SSA UDC Official Notification 2026?
A
The official SSC SSA / UDC Recruitment 2026 notification PDF can be downloaded from the SSC headquarters website at https://ssc.gov.in. Updates on the examination schedule and admit cards will be published on the SSC Northern Region website at https://sscnr.nic.in.
