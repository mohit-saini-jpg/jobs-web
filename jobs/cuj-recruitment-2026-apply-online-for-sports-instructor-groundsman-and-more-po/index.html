CUJ Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Central University of Jharkhand (CUJ) has officially announced the Consultant Sports Instructor/Coach and Consultant Groundsman Recruitment 2026 with 7 vacancies on a contractual basis. The detailed notification was released under Advertisement No. CUJ/Advt./2026-27/02. Candidates aged up to 40 years for Instructor/Coach posts and up to 35 years for Groundsman posts, possessing 10th, Diploma, BPED/MPED, or NSNIS Certificate qualifications, are eligible to apply. Selected candidates will receive a monthly salary of up to Rs. 30,000. Interested candidates can submit their online applications
