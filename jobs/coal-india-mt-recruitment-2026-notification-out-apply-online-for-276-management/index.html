Important Instructions

Only Indian Nationals with a valid GATE-2025 score in Mining Engineering (Paper Code: MN) are eligible to apply. Qualifying degree must be regular full-time from a University/Institute recognized by appropriate statutory authority in India. No hard copy applications will be accepted - online submission only via CIL website. Application fee is non-refundable; applications submitted by unqualified candidates will be rejected. No modifications are allowed after online application is submitted - discrepancies between online data and original documents may lead to rejection. GATE scores of any year other than GATE-2025 are not valid and will not be considered. OBC (Creamy Layer) candidates must apply under General (UR) category and are not entitled to OBC (NCL) concession/reservation. OBC (NCL) caste certificate must be issued on or after 01.04.2026 in the latest prescribed format for Central Government/PSU appointment purpose. Government/PSU employees must submit NOC/relieving letter from current employer at DV & IME stage or at the time of joining - no pay protection or bond transfer benefits on joining. Selected candidates must execute a service agreement bond of Rs. 3 Lakhs + GST and serve for a minimum of 60 months from date of joining. Candidates must keep their registered email ID and mobile number active for at least one year - no changes will be allowed. Canvassing in any form will disqualify the candidature. Any modifications/amendments/corrigendum in the advertisement will be published only on CIL's website www.coalindia.in. All disputes related to this recruitment will be settled within the jurisdiction of Kolkata only. CIL reserves the right to cancel/restrict/modify/alter the recruitment process at any stage without assigning any reason. The decision of the Chairman, CIL on all matters relating to eligibility, acceptance/rejection of applications, selection, and posting is final and binding.

Q1Q1. What is Coal India Management Trainee Mining Recruitment 2026?
A
Coal India Limited (CIL) has released Advertisement No. 01/2026 dated 05 May 2026 for recruitment of 276 Management Trainees (Mining) in E‑1 Grade based on GATE‑2025 scores.
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are there for CIL Management Trainee (Mining) 2026?
A
There are 276 vacancies for Management Trainee (Mining), distributed as UR 114, EWS 27, SC 41, ST 20, and OBC (NCL) 74, and the number may increase or decrease as per CIL’s requirements.
Q3Q3. What are the important dates for Coal India MT Mining Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application portal opens on 08 May 2026 (10:00 AM) and the last date to apply online is 07 June 2026 (up to 6:00 PM), while the advertisement is dated 05 May 2026.
Q4Q4. What is the eligibility criteria for CIL Management Trainee (Mining) Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must have a full‑time Degree in Mining Engineering with at least 60% marks (55% for SC/ST) from a recognized University/Institute and must possess a valid GATE‑2025 score in Mining Engineering (Paper Code: MN).
Q5Q5. Is GATE compulsory for Coal India MT Mining 2026?
A
Yes, GATE‑2025 in Mining Engineering (Paper Code MN) is mandatory, and GATE scores of any other year will not be considered for this Management Trainee (Mining) recruitment.
Q6Q6. What is the age limit for Coal India Management Trainee Mining Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit is 30 years as on 30 April 2026 for UR/EWS candidates, with relaxations of 3 years for OBC (NCL), 5 years for SC/ST, additional relaxations for J&K domicile and Ex‑Servicemen as per Government rules, and no upper age limit for departmental CIL employees (overall absolute cap 56 years).
Q7Q7. What is the salary or pay scale for CIL Management Trainee (Mining)?
A
