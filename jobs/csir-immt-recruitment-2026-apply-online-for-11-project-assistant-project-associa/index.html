CSIR IMMT Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

CSIR-IMMT Recruitment 2026 has been announced by the CSIR - Institute of Minerals and Materials Technology (CSIR-IMMT), Bhubaneswar, inviting online applications from bonafide Indian citizens for 11 temporary project positions, including Project Assistant, Project Associate, and Senior Project Associate posts. These posts are purely temporary and co-terminus with the duration of the sponsoring project. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official portal at rects.immt.res.in on or before 24.07.2026 (up to 5:00 pm).

CSIR IMMT Recruitment 2026 Eligibility Criteria

The educational qualification varies by post code, ranging from a Diploma or B.Sc. degree for Project Assistant II posts, to M.Sc., B.E./ B.Tech., M.E./ M.Tech., or PhD degrees for Project Associate and Senior Project Associate posts. Candidates should refer to the vacancy details table above for the exact qualification required for each post code. The applicant must be a citizen of India. Result awaiting candidates are not eligible to apply. Candidates pursuing a Ph.D. (including AcSIR Ph.D.) can apply only if their research domain aligns with the project's research area, and only after obtaining prior permission/NOC from their Director/Competent Authority. Currently employed candidates must furnish a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their current employer at the time of application and produce the original at the time of interview.

CSIR IMMT Recruitment 2026 Age Limit

The maximum age limit as on the last date of receipt of application is 35 years for post codes 8501 to 8510, and 40 years for post code 8511 (Senior Project Associate). Age relaxation of 5 years is applicable for SC/ST/PwBD/Women candidates, and 3 years for OBC candidates, or as per Government of India instructions issued from time to time.

CSIR IMMT Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

The selection process for CSIR-IMMT Recruitment 2026 involves the following steps: Screening of applications received, based on criteria fixed by the Competent Authority. Merely fulfilling the essential qualification does not guarantee an interview call. Walk-in interview at CSIR-IMMT, Bhubaneswar for shortlisted candidates. Final selection recommendation by the selection committee based on candidate performance. Panels may also be prepared for future project requirements. No TA/DA is admissible for appearing in the interview, and selected candidates must join duty immediately upon receiving the offer of engagement.

Documents Required for CSIR IMMT Recruitment 2026 Interview

Shortlisted candidates must carry the following original documents at the time of interview: Printout of the online application form Any ID proof (Aadhaar Card / Voter ID Card / PAN Card / Passport / Driving License) Mark sheets and certificates of all years and semesters, from 10th/Matriculation onwards, including CGPA to percentage conversion certificate, if applicable Category/EWS Certificate, if applicable No Objection Certificate (NOC), if applicable Experience Certificate, if applicable

Important Instructions for CSIR IMMT Recruitment 2026

The engagement is purely temporary and co-terminus with the tenure of the project, or 6 years (total period of engagement under one project and/or combination of projects), whichever is earlier. This engagement does not confer any right or claim for regularization to a permanent post at CSIR. Engagement may be terminated at any time on one month's notice from either side, or one month's emoluments in lieu thereof. The date for determining age, essential qualification, and experience is the last date of submission of application. Women project personnel are eligible for maternity leave of 180 days, and male project personnel for paternity leave of 15 days, in case of two surviving children, as per CSIR norms. Project personnel are entitled to 12 days of leave per year (at the rate of one day per month); any leave beyond this is without emoluments, calculated on a pro-rata basis. CSIR-IMMT
