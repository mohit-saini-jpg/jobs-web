Sound understanding of railway safety rules, procedures, and best practices, along with proven administrative capability. General Manager (Law): Full time regular bachelor's degree in law with minimum 50% marks from recognized University/Institute in India. Experience in handling Arbitrations (including commercial arbitration) & Conciliation matters, Industrial/Labour/Commercial Law matters and other dispute resolution mechanisms. Drafting & Vetting various Legal or contractual documents, providing legal opinions, handling legal matters pertaining to Land Acquisition, Contractual disputes, Service and Regulatory matters etc. Handling litigation before various judicial and quasi-judicial forums. Deputy General Manager (IT): B.E. / B. Tech in Computer Science / Information Technology / Electronics Engineering OR M.C.A. (Master of Computer Applications) OR equivalent from a recognized University/Institution. Proven in-depth experience in one or more core IT functions: Networking (LAN/WAN) design/management, Application Development (Software Development Lifecycle), or Cloud/Hybrid Hosting management. Experience in handling public procurement procedures.

  • Candidates who wish to apply for DFCCIL Various Posts Recruitment 2026 through offline mode should follow the steps below.
  • Read the official notification carefully before applying.
  • Download the application form from the official website: www.dfccil.com.
  • Fill the application form carefully with correct information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for DFCCIL Recruitment 2026?

The last date for the receipt of applications for the DFCCIL Recruitment 2026 is June 1, 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well in advance to avoid any last-minute issues.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in DFCCIL Recruitment 2026?

There are a total of 5 vacancies announced for the posts of General Manager and Deputy General Manager in the Dedicated Freight Corridor Corporation of India Limited (DFCCIL) for the year 2026.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the DFCCIL posts?

The educational qualifications vary by post. Generally, it includes B.E./B.Tech. or equivalent in relevant disciplines, a Bachelor's degree in law, or MCA. Specific details are available in the official notification.

Q4. What is the age limit for the General Manager posts?

The maximum age limit for the General Manager posts is 57 years. For Deputy General Manager posts, the maximum age limit is 55 years.

Q5. What is the application mode for DFCCIL Recruitment 2026?

The application mode for this recruitment is offline. Candidates need to download the application form from the official website and submit it via post or as instructed in the notification.

Q6. What is the salary offered for the DFCCIL posts?

The salary for the General Manager posts is E-8 Level (Rs. 1,20,000-2,80,000), and for Deputy General Manager posts, it is E-5 Level (Rs. 80,000-2,20,000).

Q7. What is the selection process for DFCCIL Recruitment 2026?

The selection process for these posts will include an interview and an assessment of the candidate's Annual Performance Appraisal Reports (APARs) for the preceding three years.

Q8. Can candidates from State Government departments apply for these posts?

Yes, DFCCIL invites applications for immediate absorption from regular officers working in Central/State Government departments, PSUs, etc.
