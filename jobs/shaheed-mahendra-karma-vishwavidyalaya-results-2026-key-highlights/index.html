Shaheed Mahendra Karma Vishwavidyalaya (SMKV), Bastar has officially released the SMKV Exam Result 2026 for various UG and PG semester examinations conducted for the 2025-2026 academic session. The latest result list on the SMKV result portal includes Bachelor of Commerce 1st Semester (Regular/Non-Collegiate and Backlog), Master of Science in Geology 3rd Semester, Master of Science in Chemistry 3rd Semester, Master of Laws 3rd Semester, Master of Commerce 1st Semester (Regular/Non-Collegiate and Backlog), Master of Business Administration 1st Semester (Regular and Backlog), Master of Arts in Economics 3rd Semester (Regular/Non-Collegiate), various LL.B 1st and 5th Semester Backlog/ATKT results, as well as old scheme M.Sc Physics and M.Com backlog results.

Shaheed Mahendra Karma Vishwavidyalaya Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - smkvbastar.in. Students can check and download their UG and PG marksheets through the central result portal at smkvbastar.in/result.php or via the declared results interface at smkv.ucanapply.com by selecting session 2025-2026, programme type, course and semester, and then entering their roll number.

After viewing their SMKV result online, candidates must carefully verify their name, roll number, course, semester, subject-wise marks and overall result status. In case of any discrepancy or if they wish to apply for re-evaluation or re-totaling, they should contact the university or college examination cell and follow the instructions given on the result/revaluation portal.

Steps to Check SMKV Exam Result 2026 Online

  • Go to the official SMKV result portal at https://smkvbastar.in/result.php.
  • Alternatively, you can open the central declared results page at https://smkv.ucanapply.com/declared-results.
  • On the result page: Select the Session as 2025-2026. Choose Programme Type (UG / PG) according to your course. Select your specific Programme (for example, B.Com, LL.B, M.Sc Geology, M.Sc Chemistry, M.Com, MBA, MA Economics, etc.). Select the relevant Semester (1st, 3rd, 5th, or old scheme semester as applicable).
  • Select the Session as 2025-2026.
