UP CNET 2026 Round 2 Seat Allotment Result - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 15/07/2026

The Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical University (ABVMU), Uttar Pradesh, conducts UP CNET (Common Nursing Entrance Test) counselling in multiple rounds to allot BSc Nursing, Post Basic BSc Nursing, and MSc Nursing/NPCC seats across its affiliated colleges. After Round 2 registration and choice filling, ABVMU releases a Round 2 seat allotment result showing which candidates have been allotted a seat, and in which institute.

Seat & Course Details

UP CNET 2026 counselling covers admission to the following programmes across ABVMU-affiliated government and private nursing colleges in Uttar Pradesh:

  • BSc Nursing (4 Years)
  • Post Basic BSc Nursing (2 Years)
  • MSc Nursing / Nurse Practitioner in Critical Care (NPCC) (2 Years)

How to Check UP CNET Round 2 Seat Allotment Result

  • Visit the official ABVMU counselling portal at abvmucet26.co.in (or the university website abvmuup.edu.in).
  • Look for the "CNET 2026" or "Counselling" section on the homepage.
  • Click the Round 2 Seat Allotment Result / Login link.
  • Enter your Registration ID (Application Number) and Password, or Date of Birth as required.
  • Submit to view your Round 2 allotment status.
  • If a seat is allotted, download and save the allotment letter for reporting.

Options After Seat Allotment

Candidates allotted a seat in Round 2 counselling typically have to choose one of the following options within the counselling window:

OptionWhat It Means
Accept / FreezeAccept the allotted seat and exit further counselling rounds.
FloatAccept the current seat but remain eligible for upgradation in a later round.
DeclineReject the allotted seat and participate in the next round.

Candidates accepting a seat are generally required to pay a seat confirmation fee of ₹40,000 (adjusted later against tuition fees). Candidates who already paid this fee in Round 1 are typically not required to pay it again — confirm this on your login dashboard, as individual cases can vary.

Required Documents for Reporting

Candidates accepting a Round 2 allotted seat are generally asked to carry original documents plus self-attested photocopies, including:

  • Verification sheet uploaded at the time of the CNET application
  • Class 12 passing certificate / marksheet / statement of marks
  • Class 10 certificate (for date of birth verification)
  • Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS), if applicable
  • Freedom Fighter / Ex-Serviceman / NCC / Christian Minority certificate, if applicable
  • Recent passport-size photographs

Always confirm the exact document checklist against the official reporting instructions issued with your allotment letter, since requirements can be updated by ABVMU.

Important Instructions

  • Report physically to the allotted institute within the prescribed reporting window; missing the deadline can lead to forfeiture of the seat.
  • Candidates not allotted a seat in Round 2, or who decline their seat, may be eligible for the Mop-Up Round.
  • ABVMU does not publish a common cut-off list; cut-offs are visible only to individual candidates after login, category-wise and round-wise.
  • Keep your Registration ID and password secure, as they are required at every counselling stage.
  • Regularly check the official portal, since counselling dates can be revised.
