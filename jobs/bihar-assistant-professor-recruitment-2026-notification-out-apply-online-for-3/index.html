BSUSC Contractual Faculty Recruitment 2026: Eligibility Criteria

Candidates should have a good academic record throughout, with a minimum of 55% marks (or equivalent grade) at the Master's degree level. A 5% relaxation in marks is available for SC/ST/BC (Non-Creamy Layer)/EBC (Non-Creamy Layer) candidates of the state, and for physically challenged persons of Bihar; however, both relaxations cannot be combined for the same candidate. In addition to the Master's degree, candidates must satisfy one of the following conditions: NET/SLET/SET is not required for Master's programmes in disciplines for which NET/SLET/SET is not conducted by UGC, CSIR, or a similar accredited body. BSUSC Contractual Faculty Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

Selection is based on a total of 100 marks, comprising Academic Qualification (88 marks) and Interview-cum-Demonstration (12 marks). Candidates must convert Grade/CGPA/CPI into percentage using only the official conversion formula/table approved and published by the concerned board or university. Shortlisting for interview is done in the ratio of 1:3, i.e., up to three candidates per notified vacancy will be called for interview, subject to availability of eligible candidates. The 12 marks for the interview stage are divided as follows: Teaching Skills (tested by the Interview Board on the spot, mandatorily video-recorded): 6 marks Interaction with the Interview Board: 6 marks Marks awarded by the Selection Committee in the interview are final and binding, and are not subject to review or re-evaluation. BSUSC Contractual Faculty Recruitment 2026: Reservation Details

Reservation benefits under this recruitment are available only to native domiciles of Bihar. Candidates from other states will be treated as Unreserved/General category, irrespective of their caste or reservation status in their home state. Candidates claiming reservation must submit a valid Permanent Residential (Domicile) Certificate issued by a Circle Office. SC/ST candidates must submit a Caste Certificate and a Permanent Residence/Domicile Certificate. BC/EBC candidates must submit a Non-Creamy Layer Certificate along with a Domicile Certificate, both issued by the notified Circle Officer. For married women, the Caste Certificate/Non-Creamy Layer Certificate must be in the name and address of their father, not their husband.
