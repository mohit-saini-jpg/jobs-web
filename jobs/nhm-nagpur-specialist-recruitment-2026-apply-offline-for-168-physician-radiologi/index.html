NHM Nagpur Specialist Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Nagpur Zila Parishad has released the Nagpur ZP Specialist Doctor Recruitment 2026 notification under the National Health Mission (NHM) for 164 specialist doctor vacancies across 7 medical specialties. Eligible MBBS/MD/MS doctors can submit their applications in person at the District Surgeon's Office, District Hospital, Manakapur, Nagpur. The last date to apply is 8 July 2026 by 5:00 PM. This recruitment is on a contractual (on-call) basis and applies to Sub-District Hospitals, Rural Hospitals, and Grameen Rugnalays across Nagpur district.

NHM Nagpur Specialist Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for Nagpur ZP Specialist Doctor Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the application is 8 July 2026 by 5:00 PM. Applications submitted after this deadline will not be accepted.
Q2How many vacancies are available in Nagpur NHM Doctor Bharti 2026?
A
There are a total of 164 vacancies across 7 specialties — 26 posts each for Gynecologist, Pediatrician, Anesthesiologist, Surgeon, Physician, and Orthopedic Specialist, and 12 posts for Radiologist.
Q3What is the application mode for Nagpur ZP Doctor Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted offline, in person at the District Surgeon's Office, District Hospital, Manakapur, Nagpur. There is no online application facility.
Q4Is there an application fee for this recruitment?
A
The official notification does not mention any application fee. Candidates should verify on www.nagpurzp.com before applying.
Q5What qualifications are needed to apply?
A
Depending on the post, candidates need an MBBS (for Physician) or a PG Degree/Diploma in the relevant specialty. Registration with the relevant specialist council is also mandatory.
Q6Is there a written exam for this recruitment?
A
No. Selection is through a direct interview only. Candidates are scored on subject knowledge, research, leadership, administrative ability, and experience, for a total of 50 marks.
Q7Where will selected doctors be posted?
A
Selected specialists will be posted at Sub-District Hospitals in Kamthi and Ramtek, and at various Grameen (Rural) Rugnalayas across Nagpur district, including Katol, Kalmeshwar, Umred, Kuhi, Parshivni, Bhivapur, Hingna, Narkhed, Mouda, Patansavangi, and Devlapar.
Q8Are retired government doctors eligible to apply?
A
Yes. Retired government officers are eligible provided their age does not exceed 70 years and they possess the relevant experience.
Q9What is the nature of employment for these posts?
A
All posts are contractual and on-call in nature under the National Health Mission. They do not confer any right to permanent government employment or service protection.
Q10Where can I get the official notification and application form?
A
