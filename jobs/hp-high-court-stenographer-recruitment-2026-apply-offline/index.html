Qualification / Eligibility

Education Qualification

Graduate, Junior Scale Stenographer with required experience and stenography/typing speed

Details

Graduate Junior Scale Stenographers working in the High Court and Graduate Junior Scale Stenographers working in the District Judiciary in H.P., with experience of one year in case of Junior Scale Stenographers of the High Court of H.P. and three years in case of Junior Scale Stenographers from the District Judiciary. Candidates must be having speed of 100 W.P.M., in English Stenography and accurate transcription of the matter dictated within a period of 5 times to the time allotted for dictation and typing speed, in English, at 50 W.P.M., on computers.
