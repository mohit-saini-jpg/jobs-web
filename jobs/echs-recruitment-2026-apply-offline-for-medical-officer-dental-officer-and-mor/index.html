ECHS Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

The selection process for ECHS Pulgaon Recruitment 2026 involves the following steps: Screening of applications based on eligibility criteria. Shortlisting of candidates, who will be intimated through mobile or email. Personal interview at Station Headquarters Pulgaon on the date and time communicated to shortlisted candidates. Final selection and result declaration on www.echs.gov.in. No TA/DA (Travelling Allowance/Dearness Allowance) will be paid to candidates attending the interview.

ECHS Recruitment 2026 Required Documents

Shortlisted candidates must carry the following original documents at the time of the interview: 10th/Matric certificate and mark sheet 10+2 certificate and mark sheet Graduation/Post-graduation/Diploma/Course certificates as applicable Work experience certificates Aadhaar card PAN card Discharge book (for Ex-Servicemen) PPO (Pension Payment Order), if applicable Service records Two passport-size colour photographs Self-attested photocopies of educational qualifications, work experience, Aadhaar, and PAN must also be attached along with the application form.
