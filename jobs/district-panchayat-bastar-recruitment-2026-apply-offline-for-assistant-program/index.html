Job Overview

Jila Panchayat Bastar (District Panchayat Bastar), Jagdalpur, Chhattisgarh has released an official recruitment notification (Advertisement No. 1765/Jipen/Bharti/Stha/2026–27, dated 23 June 2026) inviting applications for 3 contractual (Sanvida) posts under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G) scheme. Eligible residents of Chhattisgarh can apply by sending applications through Registered Post / Speed Post only on or before 10 July 2026 by 5:30 PM. Posts are based at the District and Janpad Panchayat level in Bastar district.
