Key Terms & Conditions – Jila Panchayat Bastar Contractual Appointment

The contractual appointment is initially for 1 year . It may be extended by 1 more year based on mutual consent and work performance. This appointment is governed by CG Civilian Service (Contractual Appointment) Rules 2012 and CG Civil Service (Conduct) Rules 1965. Either party may terminate the contract by giving 1 month's prior notice or 1 month's salary in lieu. No pension, gratuity, or other retirement benefits are admissible. An applicant with more than 2 living children (where at least one child was born on or after 26 January 2001) is not eligible for appointment. Candidates who have married a person with a living spouse are not eligible. Candidates must be a native of Chhattisgarh state (domicile required). A candidate can apply for the same post in only one district . Applying in more than one district for the same post will result in automatic cancellation of all applications.
