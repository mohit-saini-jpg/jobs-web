Steps to Check KSLU Result 2026

Follow the step-by-step process below to check KSLU Results 2026 online in a simple and user-friendly manner:

  • Visit the official KSLU result portal - https://kslu.ed-it.in/result1.aspx
  • On the result page, select your course and semester from the available options (LLB, BA LLB, BBA LLB, BCom LLB or PG)
  • Enter your Register Number or Roll Number correctly in the given field
  • Verify the entered details and click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button
  • Your KSLU Result 2026 will appear on the screen, showing subject-wise marks and overall result status
  • Download the result page as a PDF or take a printout for future reference and admission or revaluation purposes
