Important Instructions

  • Candidates must ensure they fulfill the minimum eligibility criteria before applying. The company reserves the right to reject applications at any stage if found ineligible. Applications once submitted cannot be withdrawn. No request for cancellation/change of mailing address/email/category/post will be entertained. All minimum eligibility qualifications should be recognized by UGC/AICTE/Govt. of India/State Govt. and from recognized/affiliated institutes/universities as on the date of passing. Post Graduate Diploma in Management must be certified as equivalent to Post Graduate by AICTE valid for the year when the PG Diploma was completed. ODL (Open and Distance Learning) degrees are accepted only if recognized by UGC; for Management/IT, AICTE recognition is mandatory. Engineering courses through ODL will not be considered (except IGNOU B.Tech for students enrolled up to 2011-12). OBC candidates belonging to "Creamy Layer" are not entitled to relaxation of age and/or reserved positions. OBC-NCL candidates must submit a valid caste certificate issued in the year of advertisement and give a self-undertaking at the time of interview confirming non-creamy layer status. Candidates working in PSU/Govt. Departments must apply through proper channel and produce NOC at the time of interview. Absence of NOC means no pay protection/continuity of service benefit for Executive positions. Selected candidates before joining will be required to undergo Medical examination. If found unfit, they will be debarred from engagement. Any canvassing directly or indirectly by the applicant will disqualify their candidature. Any information or communication regarding changes in the advertisement will be published on the Company's website only. Candidates must regularly check https://www.balmerlawrie.com for updates. All decisions of the Company regarding eligibility, selection, and other matters are final and binding. No correspondence shall be entertained. The court of jurisdiction for any dispute will be at Kolkata.
