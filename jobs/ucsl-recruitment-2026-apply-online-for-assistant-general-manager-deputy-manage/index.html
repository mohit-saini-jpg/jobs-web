UCSL Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Assistant General Manager (Contract & Procurement): Degree in Mechanical or Production or Electrical or Electronics or Civil or Marine Engineering with a minimum of 60% marks. Minimum of 15 years managerial experience in sub-contracting or vendor development/Purchasing/ Operations/ Managing government tenders in Shipbuilding, Ship repair, Offshore Fabrication Company or Marine related Engineering company or Marine related Government / Semi-Government Company / Establishment. Assistant General Manager (Design): Degree in Mechanical or Naval Architecture or Production Engineering with a minimum of 60% marks. Minimum of 15 years managerial experience in Hull structure Design or, Machinery outfit Design or Hull outfit design of ships in Shipbuilding, Ship repair, Offshore Fabrication Company or Marine related Engineering company or Marine related Government / Semi-Government Company / Establ
