Age Limit

Age DetailsResearch Associate (RA): Max 45 years; Senior Research Fellow (SRF): Max 40 years. Relaxation as per GoI/ICAR rules.
DetailsResearch Associate (RA): Maximum age limit is 45 years. Age relaxation for SC/ST is 5 years, for OBC is 3 years, and for PwD is 10 years. Senior Research Fellow (SRF): Maximum age limit is 40 years. Age relaxation for SC/ST is 5 years, for OBC is 3 years, and for PwD is 10 years.

Qualification / Eligibility

Research Associate (RA): Ph.D. degree in Agricultural Physics/Agricultural Engineering/Agricultural Meteorology/Geoinformatics, Remote Sensing & Environmental Sciences. OR Master degree in Agricultural Physics/Agricultural Engineering/Agricultural Meteorology/Geoinformatics, Remote Sensing & Environmental Sciences with 4/5 years of Bachelor's degree having 1st division or 60% marks or equivalent overall grade point average, with at least three years of research experience as evidenced from Fellowship/Associateship/Training/ other engagements as circulated vide F. No. 2-9/2012-HRD dated 25.04.2014 and one research paper in Science Citation Index (SCI)/ NAAS rated (≥4.0) Journal as per OM SR/S9/Z-09/2018 of DST dated 30.01.2019. Senior Research Fellow (SRF): Master's degree in Agronomy with 4 years / 5 years of Bachelor's degree. Candidates having Post-graduate degree in Basic Sciences with 3 years Bachelor's degree and 2 years Master's degree should have NET qualifications and 2 years of research experience as mentioned in OM No. Edn.6/27/2014/HRD dated 13th July, 2015, OM No. Agril. Edn. 6/27/2014-HRD 9th October, 2015 of the Council and OM SR/S9/Z-09/2018 of DST dated 30th January, 2019.
