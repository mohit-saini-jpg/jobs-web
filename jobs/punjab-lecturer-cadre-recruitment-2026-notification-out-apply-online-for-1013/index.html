Job Overview

The Directorate of School Education, Recruitment Directorate, Punjab has officially released the Punjab Lecturer Recruitment 2026 notification for 1,013 vacancies (829 fresh + 184 backlog) across 12 subjects including Punjabi, English, History, Mathematics, and more. Candidates can apply online from 1 July 2026 to 31 July 2026 at erd.punjab.gov.in. Eligibility requires an M.A. with at least 55% marks and B.Ed in the concerned subject. The application fee is ₹2,000 for General and ₹1,000 for SC/BC candidates. Physically Handicapped applicants are exempt from fee.

FAQs

Q1Has Punjab officially released the Lecturer Cadre Recruitment Notification 2026?
A
Yes, the Directorate of School Education, Recruitment Directorate, Punjab officially released the Punjab Lecturer Cadre Recruitment 2026 notification on 25 June 2026 for 1,013 vacancies across 12 subjects.
Q2What is the last date to apply for Punjab Lecturer Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to apply online for Punjab Lecturer Vacancy 2026 is 31 July 2026. The application window opens on 1 July 2026 at erd.punjab.gov.in.
Q3How many total vacancies are available under Punjab Lecturer Bharti 2026?
A
A total of 1,013 vacancies are available — 829 are fresh posts and 184 are backlog posts — spread across 12 subjects including Punjabi, English, Hindi, History, Mathematics, and Sciences.
Q4What is the educational qualification required for Punjab Lecturer Cadre 2026?
A
Candidates must have passed M.A. in the concerned subject with at least 55% marks and B.Ed with the teaching subject from a recognised university. They must also have studied the subject as an elective in Graduation for three years. Reserved category candidates are eligible with 50% marks in M.A.
Q5What is the application fee for Punjab Lecturer Recruitment 2026?
A
The fee is ₹2,000 for General category, ₹1,000 for SC/BC candidates, and nil (no fee) for Physically Handicapped candidates.
Q6What is the age limit for Punjab Lecturer Cadre Vacancy 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 37 years as on 1 January 2026. Reserved category candidates are entitled to age relaxation as per applicable Punjab government rules.
Q7What is the selection process for Punjab Lecturer Bharti 2026?
A
The selection process consists of two stages: an Objective Multiple Choice written examination (150 questions, 150 marks) followed by a personal interview. Final merit is prepared based on combined performance in both stages.
Q8Which subject has the most vacancies in Punjab Lecturer Recruitment 2026?
A
History has the highest number of vacancies with 210 posts (200 fresh + 10 backlog), followed by English with 141 posts and Political Science with 131 posts.
Q9Where can I apply online for Punjab Lecturer Cadre Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online at the official portal erd.punjab.gov.in from 1 July 2026 onwards. The apply online link will be activated on the start date.
Q10Is there any EWS reservation in Punjab Lecturer Vacancy 2026?
A
Yes, Economically Weaker Section (EWS) candidates are covered under the reservation policy. EWS candidates must possess a valid EWS certificate for the financial year 2025-26 as per the instructions in the official notification.
