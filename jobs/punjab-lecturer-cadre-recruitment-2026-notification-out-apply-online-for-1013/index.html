Selection Process - Punjab Lecturer Bharti 2026

The selection process for Punjab Lecturer Cadre Recruitment 2026 consists of two stages: a written examination followed by a personal interview. Candidates who clear the written test will be shortlisted for the interview round. Final merit is determined based on the combined performance in both stages as per rules under the Punjab Educational Service (School and Inspection) Group-B Rules, 2018. Stage 1 - Written Examination: The written exam is Objective Multiple Choice type. The paper carries 150 questions for 150 marks . Questions will cover the subject-specific content along with general awareness and teaching aptitude components as specified in the syllabus.
