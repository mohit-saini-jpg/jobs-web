Kerala University UG Admission 2026

Kerala University UG Admission 2026 for Four-Year Undergraduate Programmes (FYUGP) 2026-27 is open. Online registration starts on 29 May 2026 and closes on 14 June 2026. University of Kerala has released the prospectus for admission to Four-Year Undergraduate Programmes (FYUGP) in affiliated Government, Aided, and Self-Financing Colleges and University Institutes of Technology (UITs) for the academic year 2026-27.

Admissions are conducted through a centralized online process based on marks obtained in the qualifying examination (Plus Two / Higher Secondary). The FYUGP offers flexible learning pathways with options for exit after 3 years or completion of 4 years with Honours or Honours with Research degrees.

Kerala University UG Eligibility Criteria 2026

Candidates who have passed Higher Secondary / Plus Two or equivalent examination recognized by the University of Kerala are eligible for admission. There is no upper age limit for admission. Specific academic eligibility varies for different programmes. Candidates must satisfy the minimum marks requirement prescribed for each programme. APAAR ID is mandatory for college-level admission.

Kerala University UG Application Fee 2026

Application fee details are available on the official admission portal during registration. Candidates belonging to SC/ST categories usually get concessions as per university norms. The fee must be paid online before the last date.

How to Apply for Kerala University UG Admission 2026

  • Visit the official website admissions.keralauniversity.ac.in
  • Register online with valid details before 14 June 2026.
  • Fill the application form and upload required documents.
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Submit the application and take a printout.
  • Wait for trial and final allotments.

Candidates must register online compulsorily for centralized allotment.

Documents Required for Kerala University UG Admission 2026

  • Higher Secondary Marksheet and Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate and Conduct Certificate
  • Caste/Community Certificate (for reservation)
  • Income Certificate (for EWS)
  • Disability Certificate (if applicable)
  • Sports achievement certificates (for Sports Quota)
  • APAAR ID
  • Passport-size photographs

List of Courses Offered under Kerala University UG Admission 2026

University of Kerala offers a wide range of four-year undergraduate programmes (FYUGP) across various disciplines in affiliated colleges. Major programmes include BA, B.Sc, B.Com, BBA, BCA and many more in Arts, Science, Commerce, Humanities, and Social Sciences streams. The complete list of colleges and programmes with seat matrix is available in Annexure I of the official prospectus on the university website.

Selection Process

Admission is based on merit prepared from marks obtained in the qualifying Higher Secondary examination. Centralized allotment process is followed for merit seats. Separate provisions exist for Community Quota, Management Quota, and various reservation categories including SC/ST, SEBC, EWS, Differently Abled, Sports, and Transgender candidates.
