RPSC Senior Scientific Officer Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must fulfill all eligibility conditions as on the date of eligibility (01-01-2027). Only candidates with the specified educational qualification and experience for the respective Division are eligible. Working knowledge of Hindi written in Devnagari Script and knowledge of Rajasthan Culture is mandatory for all posts. Candidates must mandatorily complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the SSO Portal (https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in) before applying online.
PostEducational Qualification Experience
Senior Scientific Officer (Toxicology Division)(a) M.Sc. with at least II nd Division in Chemistry from a University established by law in India, or M.Tech./M.E. with at least II nd Division in field of Chemical Engineering from a University established by law in India, or M.Sc. with at least II nd Division in Forensic Science with specialisation in Forensic Chemistry/Toxicology/Narcotics (in III or IV Semester/specialisation course of minimum six months duration) and Chemistry/Forensic Chemistry as one of the subject in Bachelor of Science level from a University established by law in India; and (b) Certificate/Diploma in Computer Science/Computer Application or Degree with at least one subject as Computer Science/Computer Application from a University established by law in India or an institution recognized by the Government, or 'O' level or higher level Certificate Course by NIELIT (erstwhile DOEACC Society), New Delhi; and (c) Two years' experience of working in a F.S.L. of a State or Central Government or similar experience in public undertaking or corporation.
Senior Scientific Officer (Physics Division)(a) M.Sc. with at least II nd Division in Physics from University established by law in India, or M.Tech./M.E. with at least II nd Division in fields of Civil/Mechanical/Electrical/Electronics Engineering from a University established by law in India, or M.Sc. with at least II nd Division in Forensic Science with specialisation in Forensic Physics/Physical Science (in III or IV Semester/specialisation course of minimum six months duration) and Physics/Physical science as one of the subject in Bachelor of Science level from a University established by law in India; and (b) Certificate/Diploma in Computer Science/Computer Application or Degree with at least one subject as Computer Science/Computer Application from a University established by law in India or an institution recognized by the Government, or 'O' level or higher level Certificate Course by NIELIT (erstwhile DOEACC Society), New Delhi; and (c) Two years' experience of working in a F.S.L. of a State or Central Government or similar experience in public undertaking or corporation.
Senior Scientific Officer (Ballistics Division)(a) M.Sc. with at least II nd Division in Physics/Mathematics from a University established by law in India, or M.Sc. with at least II nd Division in Forensic Science with specialisation in Forensic Ballistics (in III or IV Semester/specialisation course of minimum six months duration) and Physical Science/Physics or Mathematics as one of the major subject in Bachelor of Science level from a University established by law in India; and (b) Certificate/Diploma in Computer Science/Computer Application or Degree with at least one subject as Computer Science/Computer Application from a University established by law in India or an institution recognized by the Government, or 'O' level or higher level Certificate Course by NIELIT (erstwhile DOEACC Society), New Delhi; and (c) Two years' experience of working in a F.S.L. of a State or Central Government or similar experience in public undertaking or corporation.

RPSC Senior Scientific Officer Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

Selection will be based on an Online Written Examination (Objective Type). If required, Screening / Moderation process may be adopted by the Commission. Merit list will be drawn category-wise in descending order of marks. Final selection is subject to document verification, eligibility confirmation, and other formalities as per rules. Stage 1 - Written Examination (Objective Type) The competitive examination will carry 150 marks with 150 Multiple Choice Type (objective) questions. There will be one paper only. Negative Marking: For every wrong answer, one-third (1/3rd) of the marks prescribed for that question will be deducted. OMR Answer Sheet: Candidates must darken only one circle (bubble) using BLUE BALL POINT PEN. If a candidate does not attempt a question, they must darken circle '5'. Who should NOT apply for RPSC Senior Scientific Officer Recruitment 2026?

Candidates who do not hold the specified M.Sc. / M.Tech. / M.E. degree with at least Second Division (II nd Division) in the relevant subject for the post they are applying for. Candidates who do not have at least 2 years of experience of working in a FSL (State/Central Government) or equivalent public undertaking/corporation after attainment of educational qualification. Candidates without a Certificate/Diploma/Degree in Computer Science or Computer Application (or 'O' level / higher NIELIT certification) - this is a mandatory part of the educational qualification. Candidates above 40 years of age as on 01-01-2027 (for UR/General candidates) who do not qualify for any applicable age relaxation. Candidates whose M.Sc. degree is in a subject other than what is specified for each respective Division (e.g., a Chemistry M.Sc. holder should NOT apply for the Ballistics Division post). Candida
