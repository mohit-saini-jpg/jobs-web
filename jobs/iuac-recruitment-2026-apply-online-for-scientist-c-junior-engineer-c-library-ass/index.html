IUAC Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

IUAC Recruitment 2026 notification has been released by the Inter-University Accelerator Centre (IUAC), New Delhi, an Autonomous Body of the University Grants Commission (UGC) under the Ministry of Education, Government of India, inviting applications from qualified Indian Nationals for Direct Recruitment to 8 posts of Scientist-C, Junior Engineer-C, and Library Assistant-C. Eligible candidates can apply online at www.iuac.res.in/vacancies before the last date of 4 August 2026, up to 23:59 Hrs.

IUAC Recruitment 2026: Eligibility Criteria

Scientist-C: M.Sc. (Physics/Applied Physics) with a consistently good academic record (Class-X onwards with at least 60%) from a recognized University/Institute. Candidates completing their M.Sc. in academic year 2025-26 can also apply, provided the final degree is available at the time of interview. Desirable: proficiency in the use of a variety of computer applications. Junior Engineer-C: Diploma (Electronics) from a recognized university/institute with a consistently good academic record (Class-X onwards with minimum 60% marks). Desirable: 3 years' experience in the relevant field with an R&D institution, and proficiency in computer applications. The diploma/degree must be cleared on or before the last date of application submission. Library Assistant-C: B.Lib. with B.Sc. and 4 years' experience in the operation of a computerized library. Desirable: knowledge of computer applications.

IUAC Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

Selection is based on performance in the Written Test and Interview/Skill Test/Trade Test, as applicable to each post. Online applications will be scrutinized by a shortlisting/screening committee, which may restrict the number of candidates called for the written test/interview/skill test based on qualifications, level, and relevance of experience higher than the minimum prescribed. All stages of recruitment will be held in Delhi/New Delhi only. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the written test.
