GAT B Result 2026 (Out) - Download Scorecard, Check AIR & Qualifying Status

The GAT B Result 2026 has been officially declared. The National Testing Agency (NTA) released the result on June 10, 2026, at the official portal exams.nta.nic.in/gat-bet. Candidates who appeared for the Graduate Aptitude Test - Biotechnology (GAT B) exam held on May 17, 2026, can now log in and download their scorecard using their application number and password. The scorecard contains section-wise scores, total marks obtained, percentage, All-India Rank (AIR), and qualifying status.

How to Check GAT B Result 2026

The GAT B 2026 result is available exclusively in online mode. Candidates must visit the official NTA portal and log in using their credentials. Follow these steps to access your scorecard:

  • Go to the official website - exams.nta.nic.in/gat-bet
  • Click on the "GAT B Result 2026" or "Scorecard" link on the homepage
  • You will be redirected to the login window
  • Enter your Application Number and Password (or Date of Birth)
  • Enter the security code as displayed
  • Click Submit - your scorecard will appear on screen
  • Download the PDF and save a printout for future reference

How to Check GAT B 2026 Result on Mobile

If you are accessing the result from a smartphone, the steps remain largely the same. Here is a quick guide:

  • Open any browser on your phone
  • Navigate to exams.nta.ac.in/DBT/
  • Tap on the Result / Scorecard link
  • Enter your Application Number and Password
  • Download and save the scorecard PDF to your device

Details Mentioned on the GAT B 2026 Scorecard

After downloading your scorecard, verify all printed details carefully. Any errors must be reported to NTA immediately, as discrepancies can affect your admission process. The GAT B 2026 scorecard includes the following information:

  • Candidate's name
  • Application Number and Roll Number
  • Date of Birth and Category
  • Section-wise marks obtained
  • Total score and percentage
  • All-India Rank (AIR)
  • Qualifying status (Qualified / Not Qualified)

Courses Offered Through GAT B 2026

GAT B is the gateway to postgraduate biotechnology programmes at DBT-supported universities across India. Candidates qualifying the exam can seek admission to the following courses:

  • M.Sc. Biotechnology
  • M.Sc. Agricultural Biotechnology
  • M.V.Sc. Animal Biotechnology
  • M.Tech. Biotechnology and allied areas

There are approximately 1,300 seats available across 79 participating DBT institutions.

What Happens After GAT B Result 2026 - Next Steps

Qualifying in GAT B 2026 means your name will appear on the merit list. However, it does not automatically guarantee admission. The next steps after the result are:

  • Merit List Publication: All participating universities will publish category-wise merit lists on their respective official websites.
  • GAT B Counselling 2026: The counselling process is expected to begin in June 2026. Universities will individually conduct their selection and seat allotment processes.
  • Final Admission: Admission depends on your AIR, seat availability at the chosen institution, and each university's individual selection process.
