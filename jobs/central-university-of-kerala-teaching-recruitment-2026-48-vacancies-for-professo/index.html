How to Apply

  1. 1This recruitment requires two mandatory steps - submitting the online application AND sending a hard copy by post. Completing only the online form is insufficient; applications where the hard copy is not received in time will be summarily rejected.
  2. 2Visit the application portal: Go to https://curec.samarth.ac.in (CU Chayan Portal).
  3. 3Register and fill the online application form for the specific post you are applying for. Note your Online Registration Number.
  4. 4Upload supporting documents - SC/ST/OBC (Non-creamy layer)/PwBD certificates as applicable, educational qualification documents, experience certificates, and disability certificates (if applicable).
  5. 5Pay the application fee online (Credit/Debit Card, NEFT, Net Banking) if applicable. Save the transaction receipt.
  6. 6Candidates already in service must upload an Endorsement/NOC from their employer. If unable to upload at the time of application, upload an undertaking that the NOC/Endorsement will be submitted with the hard copy or at the time of interview. Failure to produce NOC at interview will disqualify the candidate.
  7. 7Submit the online application before 10 July 2026 at 11:59 PM.
  8. 8Prepare the hard copy: Print one copy of the completed application form. Attach self-attested copies of all documents claimed in the online application. Number all supporting documents serially; the serial numbers must correspond to the relevant entries in the hard copy.
  9. 9Superscribe the envelope with: Name of Candidate | Online Registration No | Notification No | Post Applied for.
  10. 10Dispatch the hard copy by post/courier to: The Recruitment Cell, Central University of Kerala, Tejaswini Hills, Periye, Kasaragod, Kerala – 671325. The hard copy must be received before 5:00 PM on 25 July 2026.
  11. 11Candidates possessing foreign degrees must submit an Equivalence Certificate from the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) at the time of interview.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 Q1. What is the last date to apply online for Central University of Kerala Teaching Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 10 July 2026 at 11:59 PM. However, candidates must also ensure the hard copy of the application reaches the Recruitment Cell, Central University of Kerala, Kasaragod, by 25 July 2026 at 5:00 PM.
Q2 Q2. How many vacancies are there in total in CUK Teaching Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 48 teaching vacancies in total — 30 posts under the regular department notification (Non-ITEP) and 18 posts under the ITEP (School of Education) notification. Posts are at Professor, Associate Professor, and Assistant Professor levels.
Q3 Q3. What is the application fee for CUK Teaching Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application fee is Rs. 2,000/-. Women candidates and candidates belonging to SC, ST, and PwBD categories are fully exempted from the fee. Payment is accepted online through Credit/Debit Card, NEFT, or Net Banking. The fee is non-refundable.
Q4 Q4. Is the hard copy submission mandatory?
A
Yes, submission of a hard copy is mandatory for both notifications. Applications will be summarily rejected if the hard copy with all supporting documents is not received at the Recruitment Cell before 5:00 PM on 25 July 2026.
Q5 Q5. What is the salary for Professor at Central University of Kerala?
A
The Professor post carries Academic Level 14 with a pay scale of Rs. 1,44,200 to Rs. 2,18,200. Associate Professor is at Academic Level 13A (Rs. 1,31,400 – 2,17,100) and Assistant Professor at Academic Level 10 (Rs. 57,700 – 1,82,400).
Q6 Q6. What is the minimum marks requirement for eligibility?
A
The essential qualification for most posts requires a minimum of 55% marks (or equivalent grade in a point-scale) at the Master's level. A relaxation of 5% (to 50%) is available for SC/ST/PwBD/OBC (Non-creamy layer) candidates. Ph.D. holders who completed their Master's degree before 19 September 1991 also get this 5% relaxation.
Q7 Q7. What qualifications are needed for ITEP Assistant Professor posts?
A
ITEP Assistant Professor candidates need: (i) a PG degree in the relevant subject with at least 55%; (ii) a B.Ed degree with at least 55%; and (iii) either NET qualification or a Ph.D in Education or the concerned subject. For Educational Studies posts, an M.Ed with at least 55% plus NET or Ph.D is required.
Q8 Q8. Where do I apply for CUK Teaching Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted online at https://curec.samarth.ac.in (the CU Chayan Portal). No other mode of application is accepted. After completing the online form, the hard copy must be posted to the Recruitment Cell, Central University of Kerala, Tejaswini Hills, Periye, Kasaragod, Kerala – 671325.
Q9 Q9. Is there an age limit for CUK Teaching Recruitment 2026?
A
The notification does not specify any age limit for these teaching posts.
Q10 Q10. Are candidates from previous cancelled advertisements required to reapply?
A
Yes. A previous advertisement (CUK/EST/RECRT/TEACH/2023 dated 05 June 2023) for Professor (04), Associate Professor (02) and Assistant Professor (04) posts has been cancelled on administrative grounds. Candidates who had applied against that advertisement and wish to be considered for the fresh posts must apply afresh. However, they need not pay the application fee again if they had already paid for the cancelled advertisement.
