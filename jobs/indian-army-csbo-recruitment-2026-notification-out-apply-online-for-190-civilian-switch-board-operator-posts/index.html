Syllabus

DetailsThe Written Test consists of 4 parts - General Intelligence & Numerical Aptitude, General Awareness, General English, and Logic & Reasoning - each carrying 25 marks (total 100 marks), all Objective Multiple Choice Type. The question paper is bilingual (English and Hindi) except the General English section which is in English only. After the Written Test, shortlisted candidates undergo a Practical Test and Interview (both qualifying in nature) to assess proficiency in handling exchange/PBX Board operations.

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the official email ID [email protected]. Hard copy applications sent by post will not be accepted. The application window is open from 02-05-2026 (0001 Hrs) to 31-05-2026 (2359 Hrs). Candidates must open the PDF application form in Adobe Acrobat Reader ONLY - do NOT open in browser (Chrome/Edge) as fields may not work. All fields marked with * are mandatory and must not be left blank. All text must be typed in BLOCK LETTERS (CAPITALS).
  • Candidates should not send their application more than once. Incomplete or wrongly named applications will be rejected without notice. Applications with missing attachments, unsigned forms, or mismatched information will be summarily rejected. Candidates must carry originals of all documents for verification while appearing for examination along with two additional self-attested photographs. For difficulties in filling the application form, candidates may send a screenshot of the issue to [email protected].
  • Documents to be Submitted Along with Application (as self-attested attachments to email):
  • Birth Certificate (Matriculation Certificate/Mark Sheet can be produced in lieu)
  • Educational Certificate - Matriculation Passing Certificate and Mark Sheet
  • Experience Certificate in handling PBX/EPABX Board (minimum 6 months duration - mandatory)
  • Residential Certificate
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory); PAN Card (optional)
  • Caste/Category Certificate in prescribed format (for all reserved categories - SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD)
  • Discharge Certificate (mandatory for Ex-Servicemen candidates, in addition to category certificate)
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) from present employer (if already a Government servant)
  • Passport size photograph (JPG file - latest, not older than 3 months; saved as PHOTO_YourFullName.jpg)
  • Signed application form saved as CSBO_YourFullName.pdf
  • Applications must be emailed to: [email protected] with the subject line: CSBO Application – YOUR FULL NAME
  • Applications must be addressed to: The Commandant, Headquarters, 2 Signal Training Centre, Panaji (Goa) – 403001
