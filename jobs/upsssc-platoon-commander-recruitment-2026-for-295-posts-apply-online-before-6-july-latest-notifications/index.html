Short Information

Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC), Lucknow has released Advertisement No. 10-Exam/2026 and invited online applications for the Platoon Commander / Block Organizer Main Examination (PET-2025)/11. Selection Process

  • The selection process for UPSSSC Block Organizer and Platoon Commander Recruitment 2026 consists of the following stages:
  • Shortlisting: Candidates will be shortlisted for the Main Written Examination based on their normalised score in the Preliminary Eligibility Test (PET-2025). Only candidates who appeared in PET-2025 and have been issued a score card by the commission are eligible to apply for the Main Examination.
  • Main Written Examination (Likhit Pariksha): Selection will be based on the written examination consisting of 100 objective-type questions for 100 marks in 2 hours (120 minutes) with negative marking of ¼ mark per wrong answer.
  • Physical Measurement Test: Qualifying in nature; candidates must meet the prescribed physical standards.
  • Physical Efficiency Test (PET): Qualifying in nature; candidates must complete the prescribed run within the specified time.
  • Final Merit List: Will be prepared based on marks obtained in the Main Written Examination by candidates who qualify the physical tests.

How To Apply

  • Eligible candidates for UPSSSC Block Organizer, Platoon Commander Recruitment 2026 must submit their application online through the official website upsssc.gov.in within the specified last date.
  • Step 1 - Login: Visit upsssc.gov.in and login using your PET-2025 Registration Number via OTP sent to your registered mobile number/email.
  • Step 2 - Select Post: In the application section, select the post(s) you wish to apply for and confirm your educational qualification details.
  • Step 3 - Fill Application Form: Enter all required personal details, category, reservation details, educational qualifications, board/university name, passing year, certificate/roll number, and other required information carefully.
  • Step 4 - Upload Photo & Signature: Photo and signature uploaded during PET-2025 will be displayed automatically. No changes can be made to these.
  • Step 5 - Pay Application Fee: Complete fee payment via Credit Card / Debit Card / Internet Banking / UPI or SBI e-Challan as per the prescribed fee for your category.
  • Step 6 - Submit & Download: After successful fee payment/reconciliation, the application form will be submitted automatically. Download and print the application form for future reference.

Important Instructions

  • Only candidates who appeared in PET-2025 and have been issued a score card are eligible to apply. Candidates who scored zero or negative marks in PET-2025 will NOT be shortlisted for the Main Examination. Candidates must possess all required educational and other qualifications by the application last date of 06-07-2026. SC/ST/OBC candidates must possess a valid caste certificate issued by a competent authority. EWS candidates must possess an EWS certificate for financial year 2025-26, valid for FY 2026-27, issued between 01-04-2026 and 06-07-2026. Only birth date mentioned in High School / equivalent certificate will be considered valid. All communications from the Commission will be uploaded on the official website only; no personal correspondence will be done. The Commission's decision regarding eligibility of candidates will be final.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How many vacancies are there in UPSSSC Block Organizer & Platoon Commander Recruitment 2026?

UPSSSC has released 295 vacancies under Home Guards Headquarters, UP – 243 Block Organizer posts (Level‑5) and 52 Platoon Commander posts (Level‑6).

Q2. What are the application dates for UPSSSC Block Organizer, Platoon Commander 2026?

Online applications will be accepted from 16 June 2026 to 06 July 2026, and fee reconciliation/application correction can be done till 13 July 2026.

Q3. Who can apply for UPSSSC Block Organizer & Platoon Commander Main Exam 2026?

Only candidates who appeared in UPSSSC PET‑2025 and hold a valid normalised score card are eligible to apply for this Main Examination.

Q4. What is the educational qualification required for Block Organizer and Platoon Commander posts?

Applicants must have a Graduation (Bachelor’s Degree) from a recognised university as per UP Home Guards Service Rules, 1982 for non‑departmental/civilian candidates.

Q5. What is the age limit for UPSSSC Block Organizer & Platoon Commander Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must be between 21 and 40 years as on 01 January 2026, with age relaxation for SC, ST, OBC, EWS, women, ex‑servicemen and other reserved categories as per UP government norms.

Q6. What is the selection process for UPSSSC Block Organizer & Platoon Commander 2026?

Selection includes shortlisting on PET‑2025 scores, a Main Written Examination (100 marks, 2 hours, negative marking ¼), followed by Physical Measurement Test (PMT) and Physical Efficiency Test (PET); the final merit list is based on main exam marks for candidates who qualify the physical tests.

Q7. What are the physical standards and PET requirements for UPSSSC Platoon Commander & Block Organizer?

Male candidates must meet minimum height/chest standards (varied for General/OBC/SC, hill area and SC/ST), while female candidates must meet minimum height standards; PET consists of a 4.8 km run in 25 minutes for males and 2.4 km run in 14 minutes for females, both qualifying in nature.

Q8. What is the exam pattern for UPSSSC Block Organizer, Platoon Commander Main Exam 2026?

The main exam has 100 objective questions for 100 marks in 2 hours: sections include History, Polity, Geography, Economy, Current Affairs, Sports, Human Anatomy, Environment & Disaster Management, Data Interpretation, Computer & IT, and General Knowledge of Uttar Pradesh, with ¼ negative marking per wrong answer.
