How to Download the CUET PG Date Sheet 2025?

Candidates follow the steps to download National Testing Agency Date Sheet 2025, candidates check the details and download it easily.

  • Visit the official website exams.nta.ac.in
  • Click over the examination section
  • Click over the examination schedules
  • The examination Schedules page will pop up
  • Choose the course
  • Date Sheet for different courses will show up for Regular and Regular / External.
  • Click on the course
  • Date Sheet will show up.
  • Check and download.
