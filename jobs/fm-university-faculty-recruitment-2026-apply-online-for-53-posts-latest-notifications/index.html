How To Apply

  • Candidates must follow the two-step application process (Online + Hard Copy Submission) carefully. Incomplete applications or applications received after the due date will be summarily rejected.
  • Register and Fill Online Application Form Visit the official recruitment portal at https://himsrecruitment.odisha.gov.in and register using a valid email ID and mobile number. Fill all the required personal, educational, experience, and research details accurately in the online application form. Candidates must ensure that all information provided matches their original documents.
  • Upload Scanned Documents Upload clear scanned copies of all essential documents including: Matriculation/SSC certificate (proof of date of birth) Master’s Degree marksheets & certificate Ph.D. Degree & certificates NET/SET/SLET certificate (if applicable) Experience certificates Caste / SEBC / PwD certificate (if claiming reservation) Research publications, API / Research Score details (for Professor & Associate Professor) Recent passport-size photograph and signature. All documents must be in the prescribed format and size as mentioned on the portal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for Fakir Mohan University Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the online application is 7 June 2026. Candidates must also ensure that the hard copy of the application reaches the university by 17 June 2026 before 05:00 PM.

Q2. How many vacancies are announced in FM University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

A total of 53 faculty vacancies have been announced for Professor, Associate Professor, and Assistant Professor posts. These include both regular and backlog vacancies across 21 departments.

Q3. What is the application mode for Fakir Mohan University Recruitment 2026?

The application process is conducted in two steps: online submission followed by sending a hard copy. Candidates must complete both steps to ensure their application is considered valid.

Q4. What are the eligibility criteria for Assistant Professor posts?

Candidates must have a Master’s degree with at least 55% marks along with NET, SET, or a Ph.D. qualification as per UGC norms. Relaxation in marks is provided for reserved category candidates.

Q5. What qualifications are required for Professor and Associate Professor posts?

Professor applicants need a Ph.D., 10 publications, and 10 years of experience with a research score of 120. Associate Professors require a Ph.D., 8 years of experience, 7 publications, and a research score of 75.

Q6. What is the application fee for FM University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

General and SEBC candidates must pay Rs. 1000, while SC/ST candidates need to pay Rs. 600. PwD candidates are exempted from paying any application fee.

Q7. What is the selection process for Fakir Mohan University Faculty posts?

The selection process involves shortlisting of candidates followed by interview and/or presentation. The process will be conducted as per UGC regulations and university guidelines.

Q8. How can I apply for FM University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

Candidates need to apply online through the official recruitment portal, upload required documents, pay the fee, and submit the form. After that, a printed copy with documents must be sent to the university address.
