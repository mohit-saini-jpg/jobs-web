Short Information

The Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) have officially announced the Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP) Recruitment 2026 with 11,127 openings. The detailed notification was published on 14 May 2026. Candidates who meet the age criteria of 18 to 30 years and hold Matriculation + ITI or Diploma/Degree in Engineering can now submit their online applications from 15 May 2026 to 14 June 2026. Application Fee

Sc Fee

Rs. 250/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Fifty only)

Details

Candidates are required to pay the non-refundable recruitment fee online. Fee can be paid only through online mode - internet banking, debit/credit cards, or UPI. Applications received without the prescribed fee will be summarily rejected. Candidates who appear in CBT-1 will receive a refund of their examination fee (after deducting applicable bank charges) credited to their Aadhaar-seeded bank account. Candidates whose applications are rejected, or who do not appear in CBT-1, will not receive any refund.

Age Limit

Details

Candidates must be between 18 and 30 years of age as on 01-07-2026. Age relaxation is available for various categories as detailed below. If a candidate is eligible for relaxation on more than one ground, only the maximum relaxation will be granted (not cumulative). SC, ST, and OBC (NCL) candidates applying against UR vacancies will not get any age relaxation. There is no fixed limit on the number of attempts - candidates can appear as long as they are within the prescribed upper age limit.

Qualification / Eligibility

Required Degree

Matriculation / SSLC plus 3-year Diploma in Mechanical / Electrical / Electronics / Automobile Engineering (or combination of these streams) from a recognised institution in lieu of ITI. Note: Degree in the above Engineering disciplines will also be acceptable in lieu of Diploma.

Details

Candidates must fulfill all eligibility conditions as on the closing date of online application (14-06-2026). Educational/Technical qualification certificates must be from a recognized Board/University/Institute. Candidates awaiting results of their final examination for the prescribed qualification are NOT eligible to apply. The post of ALP is not suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability (PwBD). Candidates who have undergone Lasik Surgery or any other corrective eye surgery are not eligible for this post. | Educational Qualification

Salary / Pay Scale

Pay Scale

Level-2 in 7th CPC Pay Matrix; Initial Pay Rs. 19,900/-

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the official websites of participating RRBs (listed at Para 13.7 of the CEN). Hard copy / offline applications will not be accepted under any circumstances. The online portal is active from 15-05-2026 to 14-06-2026 (23:59 Hrs.). Candidates must first create an account on the RRB portal. Those who created an account for CENs notified in 2024/2025 should use the same credentials. Details filled in the "Create an Account" form (including mobile number, email ID) and the chosen RRB cannot be modified at any stage after account creation. Candidates can apply to only one RRB and submit only one application - multiple applications to the same or different RRBs will result in rejection of all applications and debarment from future RRB/RRC exams. Aadhaar or DigiLocker authentication is strongly advised during account creation for a smoother recruitment process. A live photograph is captured during the online application; pre-existing photographs are not accepted. No documents need to be uploaded at application stage (except signature and SC/ST certificate for free travel pass, if applicable).

  • Documents to be Produced at Document Verification (on being called):

  • Age proof - Matriculation / SSLC / 10th class certificate

  • Educational qualifications (Matriculation onwards) - marksheets and certificates

  • ITI / Diploma / Degree certificate in prescribed trade / discipline

  • SC / ST / OBC-NCL / EWS / Ex-Servicemen certificate (if applicable), in prescribed format and issued by competent authority

  • Self Screening Medical Declaration Report (Annexure VIII A & VIII B) - to be submitted on CBAT day

  • NOC from present employer (if applicable, for Government servants)

  • Candidates are advised to regularly visit the official RRB website for their chosen RRB for updates on all stages. Helpline for technical queries: Email: [email protected] | Phone: 0172-565-3333 and 9592001188 (10:00 AM to 5:00 PM on working days).

  • Apply Online at: Visit the official website of your chosen RRB (see list below)

Important Instructions

  • Only one application per candidate is accepted - multiple applications to one or more RRBs will result in rejection of all applications and permanent debarment from RRB/RRC exams. Candidates awaiting results of their final prescribed qualification examination are NOT eligible to apply. Candidates who have undergone Lasik or any other corrective eye surgery are not eligible for the post of ALP. Selection of RRB, once submitted, is final and cannot be changed at any later date. Details filled in the "Create an Account" form (including mobile number, email ID) and chosen RRB cannot be modified at any stage after account creation. Examination fee is refundable (after deducting bank charges) only to candidates who appear in CBT-1; rejected or absent candidates are not eligible for refund. Refund will be credited only to the Aadhaar-seeded bank account; candidates must ensure their Aadhaar is linked to an active bank account. Mobile phones, electronic gadgets, watches, earphones, Bluetooth devices, calculators, books, pen, food items, and all other listed banned items are strictly prohibited inside the test centre. Examination centre, date, and shift allotted in the e-call letter are final - no requests for change will be entertained under any circumstances. Candidates must maintain identical signatures throughout all stages of the recruitment process. No BLOCK/CAPITAL/DISJOINTED letter signatures are acceptable. Free Sleeper Class Railway Pass is available for SC/ST candidates for CBT/CBAT/DV/Medical Examination stages (if opted in application). Candidates are advised to visit only official RRB websites and beware of fake websites, unscrupulous agents, and touts claiming to provide Railway jobs through influence or unfair means. RRBs never appoint agents or coaching centres for recruitment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies in RRB ALP Recruitment 2026?

A total of 11,127 vacancies have been notified for the post of Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP) across all Railways under CEN No. 01/2026.

Q2. What is the last date to apply for RRB ALP Recruitment 2026?

The closing date for submission of the online application is 14-06-2026 (23:59 Hrs). The application window opens on 15-05-2026.

Q3. What is the salary for RRB Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP) 2026?

The Initial Pay for ALP is ₹19,900/- per month at Pay Level-2 as per the 7th CPC Pay Matrix, along with other applicable Railway allowances.

Q4. What is the age limit for RRB ALP Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must be between 18 and 30 years of age as on 01.07.2026. Age relaxation for reserved categories will be as per Railway rules in the detailed CEN.

Q5. What is the educational qualification for RRB ALP 2026?

The post-wise educational qualification and medical standards will be published in the Detailed CEN No. 01/2026 on the official websites of the participating RRBs. Candidates are advised to refer to the detailed notification for complete eligibility details.

Q6. How to apply for RRB ALP Recruitment 2026?

Applications must be submitted through Online Mode Only at the centralized portal https://www.rrbapply.gov.in. No other mode of application will be accepted.

Q7. Is this a final or indicative notification for RRB ALP 2026?

This is an Indicative (Short) Notification issued to keep prospective candidates informed. The Detailed CEN No. 01/2026 with all terms, eligibility conditions, fee details and procedures will be published separately on the official websites of the RRBs.

Q8. How many RRBs are participating in RRB ALP CEN No. 01/2026?

A total of 21 Railway Recruitment Boards are participating including Ahmedabad, Ajmer, Bengaluru, Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Bilaspur, Chandigarh, Chennai, Gorakhpur, Guwahati, Jammu-Srinagar, Kolkata, Malda, Mumbai, Muzaffarpur, Patna, Prayagraj, Ranchi, Secunderabad, Siliguri, and Thiruvananthapuram.
