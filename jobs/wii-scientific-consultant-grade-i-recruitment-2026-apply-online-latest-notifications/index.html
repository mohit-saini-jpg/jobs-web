Frequently Asked Questions

The last date to apply for the WII Scientific Consultant Grade – I post is 03 June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this date.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Scientific Consultant Grade – I position?

The required qualification is a Master's Degree in Natural Sciences (Wildlife Science/ Zoology/ Ecology/ Environmental Sciences/ Geoinformatics/ Life Sciences) or equivalent, or a BE/B Tech with five to ten years of relevant experience.

The maximum age limit for applicants is 40 years. Age relaxation is provided for SC/ST and OBC categories as per government norms.

The selection process will involve an online interview followed by the verification of original documents.
