FAQs

Q1How many vacancies are there in UPSSSC Havaldar Instructor Recruitment 2026?
A
Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission has released 209 vacancies for the post of Havaldar Instructor under Home Guard Headquarters, Uttar Pradesh, as per Advertisement No. 11‑Exam/2026.
Q2What are the application dates for UPSSSC Havaldar Instructor 2026?
A
The online application form will be available from 19 June 2026 and the last date for online application, fee payment and final submission is 09 July 2026, while the last date for fee reconciliation and application modification is 16 July 2026.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for UPSSSC Havaldar Instructor posts?
A
Candidates must have passed Intermediate (10+2) from a recognised board, while for ex‑departmental Home Guard employees, High School qualification is acceptable as per the notification.
Q4Is UPSSSC PET‑2025 mandatory for UPSSSC Havaldar Instructor Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, only those candidates are eligible who have appeared in UPSSSC Preliminary Eligibility Test (PET‑2025) and possess a valid normalised PET‑2025 scorecard issued by UPSSSC; applications without PET‑2025 score will not be accepted.
Q5How are the 209 Havaldar Instructor vacancies distributed category‑wise?
A
Out of 209 posts, 88 are Unreserved, 39 for Scheduled Caste, 5 for Scheduled Tribe, 57 for Other Backward Class, and 20 for Economically Weaker Section (EWS), with horizontal reservation for Dependants of Freedom Fighters, Ex‑Servicemen, Women and Outstanding Sportspersons as per UP government rules.
Q6What is the application fee for UPSSSC Havaldar Instructor Recruitment 2026?
A
There is no examination fee (₹0) for all categories, but candidates must pay an online processing fee of ₹25, making the total payable amount ₹25 for every applicant, through online payment modes.
Q7How can I apply online for UPSSSC Havaldar Instructor Recruitment 2026?
A
Eligible candidates must visit upsssc.gov.in, log in using their PET‑2025 Registration Number and OTP/password, fill in personal and educational details, review the auto‑fetched PET data, pay the ₹25 processing fee online, submit the form and download/print the final application for future reference.
Q8Is there any age relaxation for Home Guard (ex‑departmental) candidates in this recruitment?
A
Yes, in addition to category‑wise age relaxations as per UP Government rules for SC/ST/OBC/EWS/Women/Ex‑Servicemen/Dependants of Freedom Fighters, ex‑departmental Home Guard candidates who have completed at least 3 years of service get an additional 2‑year age relaxation over the normal upper age limit.
