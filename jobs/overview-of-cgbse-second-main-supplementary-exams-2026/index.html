How to Download CGBSE Class 10 & 12 Exam Schedule 2026

It's pretty straightforward. Just follow these steps:

  • Visit the official website: cgbse.nic.in
  • Look for the “Time Table” or “Examination” section on the homepage.
  • Click on the link for “High School / Higher Secondary Second Main / Supplementary Exam 2026”.
  • The PDF will open download and save it.
  • Take a printout for easy reference.

Tip: Always rely on the official site to avoid any fake schedules floating around.

Important Points to Remember

  • All exams will be held in the morning shift only (9 AM - 12:15 PM).
  • Students must reach the exam centre at least 30 minutes early.
  • Carry your admit card and valid ID on every exam day.
  • Follow the syllabus of the current academic session strictly.
  • Vocational and special subjects (for differently-abled students) have dedicated slots.

If you're appearing for these exams, now is the time to focus and revise properly. Many students use this second chance to improve their overall percentage.
