FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the IIMC Graduate Apprentice posts?
A
The last date to submit applications for the IIMC Apprentice posts is 22 June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline to avoid any last-minute issues.
Q2What are the educational qualifications required for the Graduate Apprentice (Library) post?
A
For the Graduate Apprentice (Library) post, candidates must possess a Bachelor's degree in Library & Information Science or an equivalent qualification from a recognized university. Knowledge of computer applications is also required.
Q3What is the age limit for applying to the IIMC Graduate Apprentice positions?
A
The age limit for applying to the IIMC Apprentice positions is a minimum of 18 years and a maximum of 28 years. Age relaxation is provided for reserved categories as per government norms.
Q4How many vacancies are available for the Graduate Apprentice posts at IIMC?
A
There are a total of 10 vacancies for the Apprentice posts at IIMC. These are distributed between the Graduate Apprentice (Library) and Graduate Apprentice (Office Assistant) roles.
Q5What is the salary offered for the Graduate Apprentice posts?
A
The selected candidates for both Graduate Apprentice (Library) and Graduate Apprentice (Office Assistant) posts will receive a stipend of Rs. 12,300/- per month.
Q6Is there any application fee for the IIMC Graduate Apprentice recruitment?
A
The official notification does not mention any application fee for the IIMC Apprentice recruitment. Candidates are advised to check the official notification for any updates regarding fees.
Q7What is the mode of application for the IIMC Graduate Apprentice posts?
A
The mode of application for the IIMC Apprentice posts is offline. Candidates need to download the application form from the official website and submit it via post.
Q8What is the selection process for the IIMC Graduate Apprentice recruitment?
A
The selection process involves shortlisting candidates based on their merit, followed by a written test, personal interview, or skill test. The institute may modify the selection mode as per its requirements.
Q9Do candidates need to enroll on any portal before applying?
A
Yes, it is mandatory for candidates to enroll on the National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS) portal (https://nats.education.gov.in) before submitting their application for the IIMC Apprentice posts.
Q10Where can I find the official notification and application form?
A
The official notification and application form can be downloaded from the official website of the Indian Institute of Mass Communication (IIMC), which is www.iimc.gov.in. Links are also provided in the Important Links section.
