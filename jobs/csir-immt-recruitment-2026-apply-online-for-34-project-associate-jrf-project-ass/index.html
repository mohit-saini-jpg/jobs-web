CSIR-IMMT Recruitment 2026: Emoluments

Monthly emoluments vary by post, ranging from Rs. 20,000 + HRA@20% (Project Assistant-II) to Rs. 37,000 + HRA@20% (Junior Research Fellow, post 8425). Several posts (8414 to 8417, 8428, 8429) carry a two-tier emolument structure: a higher rate for candidates who have qualified CSIR-UGC/ICAR/ICMR/NET (including lectureship/assistant professorship), GATE, or an equivalent national-level examination conducted by a Central Government department such as DBT/DST, and a lower rate for candidates who do not meet this qualification. Post 8424 (Project Fellow) carries a consolidated emolument of Rs. 30,000/- with no separate HRA. See the Vacancy Details table above for exact figures per post.

CSIR-IMMT Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

Applications will be screened for shortlisting as per criteria fixed by the Competent Authority. Merely fulfilling the essential qualification does not by itself entitle a candidate to be called for interview. No written examination is mentioned in the notification - shortlisted candidates will be called for a walk-in interview at CSIR-IMMT, Bhubaneswar, based on their subject/branch (see the Interview Schedule table below). No separate interview call letter will be issued, and no TA/DA is admissible for appearing at the interview.
