CG ITI 2026 Admission - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 16/06/2026

The CG ITI Merit List 2026 for Round 1 has been officially released today, June 16, 2026, on the Chhattisgarh Directorate of Technical Education's e-counselling portal at cgiti.admissions.nic.in. Candidates who registered for CG ITI admissions between May 29 and June 15, 2026 can now check their name, rank, and allotted institute on the portal. Admissions from the Round 1 selection list will take place from June 23 to 25, 2026.

The CG ITI admission process for Session 2026-27 is conducted by the Directorate of Technical Education, Chhattisgarh, and is entirely merit-based no entrance examination is required. Seats across more than 100 government ITIs in Chhattisgarh are allocated based on marks obtained in Class 10 or Class 12 examinations.

How to Check CG ITI Merit List 2026 - Step-by-Step Guide

The CG ITI Round 1 Merit List 2026 is available in PDF format on the official portal. Follow these simple steps to access and verify your selection status:

  • Go to the official website: cgiti.admissions.nic.in
  • On the homepage, look for the "Latest News" or "Public Notices" section.
  • Click on the relevant link "ITI 10th Passed Merit List Round 1 Session 2026-27" or "ITI 12th Passed Merit List Round 1 Session 2026-27".
  • A PDF will open on your screen.
  • Download the PDF and use Ctrl+F to search for your name or application number.
  • Note down your rank, category, and the allotted ITI/trade.
  • Take a printout for further reference.

Alternatively, candidates can log in to the portal using their Application Number and Date of Birth to view their individual allotment letter and selection status.

CG ITI Merit List 2026 - What Does It Contain?

The CG ITI merit list is a detailed document prepared for both Class 10 and Class 12 passed candidates. Understanding what information is listed in it helps candidates quickly verify their eligibility and allotment.

Each merit list entry includes:

  • Candidate's full name (as per application form)
  • Application / Roll number
  • Date of birth
  • Qualifying examination (10th / 12th)
  • Percentage of marks obtained
  • State rank (overall and category-wise)
  • Allotted ITI institute and trade (in selection lists)

CG ITI 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Candidates who have appeared in or passed Class 10 and Class 12 or an equivalent examination from any recognized board are eligible. The minimum age requirement is 14 years, and there is no upper age limit.

For engineering trades, candidates must have studied Science and Mathematics as compulsory subjects in their qualifying examination. For the Business Driver cum Mechanic trade, the minimum age is 18 years.

Candidates found ineligible at any stage of the admission process will have their candidature cancelled.

CG ITI 2026 Application Fee

The application form fee for the General and OBC categories is ₹60, while SC, ST, and PWD candidates are required to pay ₹50.

CG ITI 2026 Trades Offered

CG ITI admissions cover a wide range of vocational trades across two categories engineering and non-engineering with course durations of one or two years. Knowing the available trades helps candidates make informed choices during choice filling.

Engineering Trades (Two-Year Curriculum): Architectural Draughtsman, Draughtsman (Civil), Draughtsman (Mechanical), Electrician, Electronic Mechanic, Fitter, Information Technology, and others.

Non-Engineering Trades (One-Year Curriculum): Computer Hardware & Network Maintenance, Computer Operator and Programming Assistant (COPA), Dress Making, Fashion Design and Technology, Hospital Housekeeping, IoT Technician (Smart Agriculture), and more.

Government ITIs in Chhattisgarh offer 1 to 2-year certificate courses in both engineering and non-engineering trades such as Electrician, Fitter, Welder, COPA, Mechanic, and Stenography.

Documents Required for CG ITI Counselling 2026

When reporting to the allotted institute for admission, candidates must carry originals as well as photocopies of the following documents for verification:

  • CG ITI 2026 application form printout
  • Class 10 marksheet and certificate
  • Class 12 marksheet and certificate (if applicable)
  • Fee payment receipt
  • Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS, if applicable)
  • Domicile certificate (Chhattisgarh)
  • Character certificate
  • Passport-size photographs (recent)
  • Valid identity proof (Aadhaar card, etc.)
  • Medical certificate

Failure to produce any required document at the time of verification may result in cancellation of the allotment.
