FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the DRDO Paid Internship?
A
The last date to submit the offline application for the DRDO Paid Internship is 30th June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline to avoid any last-minute issues.
Q2What is the educational qualification required for this internship?
A
Candidates must be final year B.Tech./B.E. or M.Tech./M.Sc.(Electronics) students. They should have completed their 3rd year/6th semester or 1st year/2nd semester respectively, with a CGPA of 7.0 or above and no backlogs.
Q3How will the selection be made for the DRDO Paid Internship?
A
The selection process will be based entirely on merit. Candidates will be shortlisted based on their academic performance and other eligibility criteria mentioned in the notification.
Q4What is the stipend offered for this internship?
A
Selected interns will receive a stipend of Rs. 30,000/- in two installments. Each installment will be Rs. 15,000/-, disbursed after the completion of 3 months and 6 months, provided the attendance requirement is met.
Q5How many vacancies are available for the DRDO Paid Internship?
A
There are a total of 45 vacancies for the DRDO Paid Internship across various engineering disciplines like Electronics & Communication, Computer Science, and Mechanical Engineering.
Q6What is the application mode for this recruitment?
A
The application mode for this DRDO Paid Internship is offline. Candidates need to download the application form and send it via Speed Post to the specified address.
Q7Where is the Defence Electronics Application Laboratory (DEAL) located?
A
Defence Electronics Application Laboratory (DEAL) is located in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, with the pincode 248001.
Q8Is there any age limit for applying to this internship?
A
The official notification does not specify any minimum or maximum age limit for candidates applying for this DRDO Paid Internship program.
Q9What documents need to be attached with the application form?
A
Candidates need to attach self-attested copies of required documents, which typically include a photograph, signature, ID proof, and relevant academic certificates. Please refer to the official notification for a complete list.
Q10Can I apply via email or online for this internship?
A
No, applications submitted via email or online will not be considered. Only offline applications sent through Speed Post to the designated address will be accepted.
