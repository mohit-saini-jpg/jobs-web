FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for EPFO Audit Posts?
A
The last date to submit the application for the EPFO Audit Posts is 14th August 2026. Ensure your application reaches the designated address before this date.
Q2How many vacancies are available for EPFO Audit Posts?
A
There are a total of 74 vacancies announced for various Audit Posts including Deputy Director (Audit), Assistant Director (Audit), Assistant Audit Officer, and Auditor.
Q3What is the eligibility criteria for Deputy Director (Audit) post?
A
For the Deputy Director (Audit) post, candidates must be employees of EPFO/Central/State Government, hold a Graduation degree (preferably B.Com), have passed the SAS examination, and possess relevant experience in Audit and Accounts.
Q4What is the maximum age limit for applying to these EPFO posts?
A
The maximum age limit for all the advertised posts is 56 years. There is no mention of age relaxation in the provided data.
Q5What is the application mode for EPFO Audit Posts recruitment?
A
The application mode for this recruitment is Offline. Candidates need to send their filled application forms to the official address mentioned in the notification.
Q6What is the salary structure for the recruited candidates?
A
The salary will be based on the 7th CPC Pay Matrix, ranging from Level-11 for Deputy Director (Audit) down to Level-6 for Auditor.
Q7Is there any application fee for EPFO Audit Posts?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q8What is the selection process for these deputation posts?
A
The selection process is based on deputation, which will be determined by the candidate's eligibility, service records, and Annual Performance Appraisal Reports (APARs).
Q9Which government organizations are eligible for deputation?
A
Employees from EPFO, Central Government, and State Government organizations are eligible to apply for these deputation posts.
Q10Where can I find the official notification and application form?
A
The official notification and application form can be downloaded from the official website of EPFO, which is https://www.epfindia.gov.in.
