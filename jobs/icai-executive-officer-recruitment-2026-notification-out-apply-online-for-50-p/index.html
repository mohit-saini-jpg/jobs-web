Job Profile and Key Responsibilities

The Executive Officer role is designed to support ICAI's committees, boards, and departments across accounting, auditing, taxation, corporate laws, and financial reporting functions. The specific duties assigned depend on the department the candidate is placed in after joining, and may include any combination of the following broad responsibility areas. Technical Research, Standards & Guidance: Drafting and reviewing Accounting Standards, Standards on Auditing, Valuation Standards, and preparing technical notes, exposure drafts, and impact assessments of regulatory changes. Taxation & Policy Advisory: Working on Direct Taxation, GST, International Taxation, and corporate restructuring matters, including representations before CBDT, CBIC, and the Ministry of Finance. Review & Oversight: Reviewing financial statements, audit reports, and disclosures for compliance with AS/Ind AS, SAs, CARO
