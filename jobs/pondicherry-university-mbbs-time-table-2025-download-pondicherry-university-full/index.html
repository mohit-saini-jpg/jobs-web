What is Pondicherry University MBBS Time Table 2025?

The Pondicherry University MBBS Time Table 2025 is the official examination schedule released by Pondicherry University for the academic year 2024-25. This document contains crucial information including:

How to Download Pondicherry University MBBS Time Table 2025? (Step-by-Step)

Follow these simple steps to download your Pondicherry University MBBS exam date sheet 2025:

Method 1: Direct Download from Official Website
