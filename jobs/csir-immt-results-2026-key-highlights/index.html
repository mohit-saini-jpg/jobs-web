How to Download CSIR IMMT Ph.D Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download the CSIR IMMT Result 2026:

  • Visit the official website - https://www.immt.res.in/
  • Go to the "Announcements" or "Results" section on the homepage.
  • Click on the link titled "Ph.D Result August 2026 Session".
  • The result PDF will open on the screen.
  • Search your name or application number using the find option.
  • Download and save the PDF for future reference.
