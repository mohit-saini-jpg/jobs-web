APCPDCL AEE Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The APCPDCL Assistant Executive Engineer Recruitment 2026 is a direct recruitment drive for engineering posts in APCPDCL, covering the districts of NTR, Krishna, Guntur, Palnadu, Bapatla, Prakasam, and Markapuram. APCPDCL AEE Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1The APCPDCL AEE Apply Online 2026 process is conducted entirely online at apcpdcl.in or the Common Recruitment Portal at apvidyutrecruitment.apcfss.in. Registration requires an Aadhaar-linked mobile number for OTP verification - this same number will be used throughout the recruitment process. The application portal is live from 30 June 2026 at 10:00 Hrs. Candidates must complete all three steps - fee payment, form fill-up, and certificate upload - before the deadline of 20 July 2026 at 23:59 Hrs.
  • 2Visit the official portal: apcpdcl.in or apvidyutrecruitment.apcfss.in. The User Guide will be available on the website from 25 June 2026.
  • 3Click on New Registration and register using your Aadhaar-linked mobile number. An OTP will be sent to this number for verification to complete registration.
  • 4Log in using the Registration ID and password. Note these credentials carefully - a retrieval provision exists within the portal if credentials are lost.
  • 5Select the notified post (AEE-Electrical / AEE-Telecom / AEE-Civil). Only one online application can be submitted through the Common Recruitment Portal per candidate.
  • 6Fill in all required details - personal information, educational qualifications, academic details, category, and preferences for Power Utility (APCPDCL, APTRANSCO, APEPDCL, APSPDCL, APGENCO) and Zone/Unit. These preferences are final and cannot be changed after submission.
  • 7Upload scanned copies of all required certificates. All uploaded documents must be clear and legible originals - illegible or tampered documents will result in rejection. No column in the application form should be left blank.
  • 8Upload a recent passport-size photograph and scanned signature compulsorily.
  • 9Pay the application fee of ₹1,000 through the Payment Gateway. Note the Journal Number issued upon payment - this is only fee confirmation, not application completion.
  • 10Complete the full online application form submission. Take a printout of the submitted application for future reference.
  • 11Candidates in government service must attach a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their employer at the time of application submission.
  • 12Important: Applications submitted through any mode other than online will not be entertained. Candidates submitting false information or fake certificates are liable for criminal prosecution. The Public Examinations (Prevention of Unfair Means) Act, 2024 applies to this recruitment - violations can attract up to 5 years' imprisonment and ₹10 lakh fine.
