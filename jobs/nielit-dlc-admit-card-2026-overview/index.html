When Was the NIELIT DLC Admit Card 2026 Released?

This section answers the most common question candidates search for: the exact admit card release date and what it means for their exam preparation timeline.

NIELIT released the admit card for the DLC (CCC/BCC/CCCP/ECC/ACC) April-June 2026 examination cycle in mid-June 2026 on the Student Web Portal. The institute had earlier issued a public notice regarding the conduct of these DLC examinations for the April-June 2026 EMEC, after which hall tickets were made available for eligible registered candidates to download. Candidates should download their admit card well in advance and avoid waiting until the last day, as the portal can experience heavy traffic close to the exam date.

How to Download NIELIT DLC Admit Card 2026

This section provides a clear, step-by-step process so candidates can quickly access and print their hall ticket without facing technical errors.

Candidates can follow these steps to download the NIELIT DLC admit card 2026:

  • Visit the official NIELIT Student Web Portal at student.nielit.gov.in.
  • Look for the "Download Admit Card" link, usually displayed on the homepage or right-hand panel.
  • Select the relevant course (CCC, BCC, CCCP, ECC, or ACC) and the April-June 2026 exam cycle from the drop-down menu.
  • Enter your application number or registration number along with your date of birth.
  • Enter the captcha code shown on screen and click submit.
  • Your NIELIT DLC admit card 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download the admit card and take a clear printout for use on exam day.

If the admit card does not load, candidates can try clearing the browser cache, switching to a different browser, or checking the portal again after some time, since heavy traffic near the exam date can sometimes cause temporary access issues.

Details Mentioned on the NIELIT DLC Admit Card

This section explains what information appears on the hall ticket so candidates can verify their details are correct before the exam.

The NIELIT DLC admit card typically contains the following details:

  • Candidate's name and photograph
  • Roll number or registration number
  • Course name (CCC/BCC/CCCP/ECC/ACC)
  • Exam date and reporting time
  • Exam centre name and address
  • Exam-day instructions and guidelines
  • Signature or authorization stamp

Candidates are strongly advised to verify every detail on the admit card immediately after downloading it. Any discrepancy in the name, photograph, course, or exam centre should be reported to NIELIT authorities or the respective Regional Centre without delay, since corrections cannot be made once printed or close to the exam date.

NIELIT DLC Exam Date 2026 - April-June Cycle

This section covers when the corresponding DLC examination is scheduled to take place for candidates who have received their admit card.

The DLC (CCC/BCC/CCCP/ECC/ACC) examination for the April-June 2026 cycle is being conducted in a phase-wise manner at designated exam centres across India, starting from the dates specified in the official notice. Candidates must report at their allotted exam centre on the date and time mentioned on their individual admit card, as exam slots vary by regional centre and candidate batch.

Documents Required Along With the Admit Card

This section lists what candidates must carry to the exam centre besides the printed hall ticket, since entry is denied without these.

Along with the printed NIELIT DLC admit card, candidates must carry one original, valid photo ID proof. Commonly accepted IDs include:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID Card
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • PAN Card
  • College/School ID Card (for students)

Candidates without a valid admit card and original photo ID will not be permitted to enter the examination centre or appear for the test.
