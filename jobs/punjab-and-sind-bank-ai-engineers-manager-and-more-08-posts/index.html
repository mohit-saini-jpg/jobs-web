FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for Punjab & Sind Bank Cloud & AI Engineer Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application, make any edits, and pay the application fee is 28 June 2026.
Q2How many vacancies are available in this Punjab & Sind Bank recruitment?
A
A total of 8 posts are available — 4 Manager (Cloud CoE), 1 Senior Manager (Cloud CoE), 1 Chief Manager (Cloud CoE), 1 Manager (AI Engineers), and 1 Senior Manager (AI Engineers).
Q3Is this a permanent or contractual job?
A
This is a contractual engagement for an initial period of three years, subject to annual performance review. It may be extended for up to two additional years at the bank's discretion. It is not a permanent or regular appointment.
Q4What is the application fee for Punjab & Sind Bank Cloud & AI Engineer 2026?
A
General, EWS, and OBC candidates pay ₹850 plus applicable taxes and payment gateway charges. SC, ST, and PwBD candidates pay ₹100 plus applicable taxes and payment gateway charges. The fee is non-refundable.
Q5What is the selection process for these posts?
A
All posts follow a three-stage process: Written Test → Short-listing → Personal Interview. The written test has 100 questions worth 100 marks in 105 minutes, covering English Language, General Awareness, and Professional Knowledge. Only Professional Knowledge marks are used for shortlisting candidates for interview.
Q6What is the minimum CIBIL score required?
A
Candidates must have a minimum CIBIL score of 650 at the time of joining.
Q7What educational qualification is needed for the AI Engineer posts?
A
A full-time B.E./B.Tech in Computer Science, IT, Electronics, Data Science, AI, or Machine Learning — or a full-time MCA/M.Tech in a related field — from an institution recognized by the Government of India, AICTE, or UGC. Additionally, candidates must hold at least one valid professional certification in AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Science, or Generative AI.
Q8Where is the personal interview conducted?
A
The personal interview will be conducted at New Delhi unless otherwise specified. Call letters are sent by email only. Candidates must attend at their own expense.
Q9How do I apply for Punjab & Sind Bank Cloud CoE / AI Engineer Recruitment 2026?
A
Visit https://punjabandsind.bank.in/, go to the Recruitment section, and click the relevant notification. Complete the online registration, upload all required documents, pay the fee online, and submit. A candidate must apply separately for each post.
Q10What is the age limit for the MMGS II posts?
A
For MMGS II (Manager-level posts), the minimum age is 29 years and the maximum is 41 years as on 01 June 2026. Candidates must have been born between 02 June 1985 and 01 June 1997 (both dates inclusive). Relaxation applies as per government guidelines for reserved categories.
