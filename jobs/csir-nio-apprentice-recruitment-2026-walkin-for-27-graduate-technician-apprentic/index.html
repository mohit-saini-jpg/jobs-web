CSIR NIO Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

CSIR-National Institute of Oceanography (NIO), Goa has invited candidates for 27 Apprentice Trainee posts under the Apprenticeship Act, 1961, through Notification No. NIO/HRM/Apprenticeship/01/2026 dated 18 June 2026. There is no written application process - eligible candidates must register on the NATS portal and directly attend a walk-in interview on 6 July 2026 at CSIR-NIO Headquarters, Dona Paula, Goa, with reporting time between 8:30 AM and 10:00 AM.

CSIR NIO Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Eligibility & Qualification

Details
  1. Candidates wondering whether they qualify for this apprenticeship can note that eligibility depends on the specific trade applied for, with separate qualification standards for Technician and Graduate apprentices. Both categories also share common conditions on passing year, prior apprenticeship history, and job experience that apply across all 27 posts
  2. Civil Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Engineering, Computer Engineering: A three-year full-time Diploma in the relevant engineering discipline from a State Council/Board of Technical Education or recognized University/Institution. HR Management, Office Administration, Finance & Accounts, Stores & Purchase: Bachelor's degree in any discipline (B.A/B.B.A/B.Com/B.Sc or equivalent). R&D in Oceanography: Bachelor's degree in any science discipline such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Botany, Zoology, Biotechnology, Microbiology, Biochemistry, Environment Science, Marine Science, Geology, Computer Science, or IT. Library Sciences: Bachelor's Degree in Library and Information Science or equivalent from a UGC-recognized university. Dispensary/Nursing: B.Sc. in Nursing or equivalent. Guest House & Canteen Management: Bachelor's degree in Hotel Management or equivalent. Desktop Publishing: Bachelor's degree in Visual Arts, Photography, Videography, Visual Communication, or equivalent
  3. Beyond the academic qualification, CSIR-NIO has set additional conditions that apply uniformly to all applicants regardless of trade. Candidates who do not meet these conditions will not be considered, even if their educational qualification matches the post
  4. Only candidates who passed their qualifying exam in 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025, or 2026 are eligible. Candidates must have registered on the Apprenticeship Portal (NATS) and fulfilled the eligibility criteria to appear for the walk-in interview. Candidates should not have undergone or be currently undergoing apprenticeship training in CSIR or elsewhere. Candidates should not have one year or more of job experience in any role. Sandwich diplomas, private diplomas, and part-time/distance/short-term/certificate courses are not acceptable
  5. Candidates asking about the age criteria for this apprenticeship can note that the limit differs for Technician and Graduate apprentices, calculated as on the date of interview. Technician/Diploma apprentices must be between 18 and 24 years, while Graduate/Degree apprentices must be between 21 and 26 years, both as on 6 July 2026
  6. Age relaxation is applicable only on production of a relevant certificate in the prescribed Government of India format, signed by the competent authority

CSIR NIO Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Salary Details & Pay Scale

Apprentice CategoryStipend
Technician (Diploma)Rs. 10,900/- p.m

Details
  1. Candidates asking how much CSIR-NIO apprentices are paid can note that the stipend is fixed by category and does not vary by individual trade within that category. Technician (Diploma) apprentices receive Rs. 10,900/- per month, while Graduate (Non-Engineering Degree) apprentices receive Rs. 12,300/- per month, both for a training period of 1 year
  2. On successful completion of training, apprentices will be awarded a National Apprenticeship Certificate by the National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT)/BOAT. CSIR-NIO has no obligation to offer regular employment after training, and apprentices cannot claim employment rights on the basis of completing the apprenticeship
