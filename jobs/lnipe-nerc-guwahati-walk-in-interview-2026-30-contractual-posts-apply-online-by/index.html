LNIPE NERC Guwahati Walk-in Interview 2026 - 30 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for LNIPE NERC Guwahati Walk-in Interview 2026?
A
The online application portal closes on 20 June 2026. Candidates must submit their application through https://recruitment.lnipeassam.edu.in/ on or before this date.
Q2How many posts are available in LNIPE NERC Guwahati Walk-in Interview 2026?
A
A total of 30 different post are open, including Doctor, Internal Audit Officer, Physiotherapist, Nurse, Computer Instructor, Mess Supervisor, Supervisor (B&R), Assistant Accountant, Junior Hindi Translator, Fitness Trainer, IT Assistant, Lower Division Clerk, Junior Clerk, Driver, Electrician, Cook, Plumber, and Hostel In-charge.
Q3What is the nature of employment offered by LNIPE NERC Guwahati?
A
All posts are purely contractual for a period of one year or till regular appointments are made (whichever is earlier). There is no right or likelihood of permanent employment.
Q4What is the age limit for LNIPE NERC Guwahati recruitment 2026?
A
Age is reckoned as on 01 January 2026 and must be below 69 years for all the posts mentioned in the notification.
Q5What is the salary for the Doctor post at LNIPE NERC Guwahati?
A
The Doctor/Physician post carries a monthly salary of ₹70,000/-. This is the highest salary offered in this recruitment.
Q6Is the Walk-in Interview conducted online or offline?
A
Both modes are in use. Posts such as Doctor, Internal Audit Officer, Physiotherapist, Nurse, Computer Instructor, Mess Supervisor, Supervisor B&R, Assistant Accountant, Junior Hindi Translator, Fitness Trainer, and IT Assistant will be interviewed in virtual mode. Posts like Lower Division Clerk, Junior Clerk, Driver, Electrician, Cook, Plumber, and Hostel In-charge require candidates to appear in offline mode.
Q7Where can I find the Walk-in Interview date and venue for LNIPE NERC 2026?
A
The schedule and venue for the Walk-in Interview will be published on the institute's official recruitment portal at https://recruitment.lnipeassam.edu.in/ in due course.
Q8What documents should candidates carry for the LNIPE NERC Walk-in Interview?
A
Candidates must bring their bio-data/resume along with originals and copies of all certificates, mark sheets, and experience documents related to educational qualifications and work experience for verification at the time of joining.
