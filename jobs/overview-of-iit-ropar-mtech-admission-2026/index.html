Programmes Offered and Seats

IIT Ropar offers M.Tech in the following departments (seats are indicative):

  • Chemical Engineering -Chemical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering -Water Resources and Environment (15 seats), Structural Engineering and Geomechanics (12 seats)
  • Computer Science & Engineering -Computer Science & Engineering, Artificial Intelligence (15–20 seats each)
  • Electrical Engineering -Communication & Signal Processing, Microelectronics & VLSI Design, Power Engineering (15–21 seats)
  • Mechanical Engineering -Thermal & Fluids Engineering, Mechanics & Design, Manufacturing Engineering, Computational Mechanics (15 seats each)
  • Biomedical Engineering -Biomedical Engineering (15 seats)
  • Materials Engineering -Materials Engineering (8 seats), E-Waste Resources Engineering and Management (20 seats, joint)
  • Autonomous Systems & Drone Technology (Centre) -Autonomous Systems & Drone Technology (20 seats)

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have:

  • A valid GATE 2024/2025/2026 score + qualifying degree (B.Tech/B.E./M.Sc./MCA etc.) in relevant discipline with minimum 60% marks or 6.0 CGPA (55% or 5.5 CGPA for SC/ST/PwD).
  • B.Tech graduates from IITs with CGPA 8.0 or above (7.5 for SC/ST/PwD) are eligible without GATE in many programmes.

Eligibility and accepted GATE papers vary by department/specialization. Detailed criteria are available in the official brochure.

Application Process (Step-by-Step)

  • Register on COAP 2026 and obtain COAP registration number (free & mandatory).
  • Visit the IIT Ropar Application Portal
  • Register / Login and fill the online application form (separate applications may be required for multiple departments).
  • Upload required documents (photo, GATE scorecard, mark sheets, category certificates, etc.).
  • Pay application fee through SB Collect (Punjab → IIT Ropar).
  • Submit the form and save the transaction details.

Apply Online Now Register on COAP 2026

Selection Procedure

Shortlisting is based on GATE performance. Final admission offers are made through COAP 2026. Candidates can choose Accept & Freeze, Retain & Wait, or Reject & Wait options on COAP. No separate written test/interview is conducted for the regular GATE-based process.

Application Fee

  • General / OBC / EWS: ₹500
  • SC / ST / PwD: ₹250

Payment mode: State Bank Collect (SB Collect).
