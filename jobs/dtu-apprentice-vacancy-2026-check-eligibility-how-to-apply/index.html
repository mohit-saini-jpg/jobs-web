DTU Apprentice Vacancy 2026, Check Eligibility & How to : Job Overview

DTU Apprentice Vacancy 2026: Delhi Technological University has invited applications from eligible candidates for a one-year Graduate/Diploma Apprenticeship Trainee program. Details regarding eligibility, stipends, and the application form are available on the university’s website, www.dtu.ac.in. The fully filled-out application form must reach the address ‘The Assistant Registrar (GA), Shahbad Daulatpur, Bawana Road, Delhi – 42’ by July 31, 2026. Additionally, prior registration on the National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS) portal (www.nats.education.gov.in) is mandatory.
