How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official TGPSC website at https://www.tgpsc.gov.in.
  • 2If applying for the first time, complete One Time Registration (OTR) to obtain a TGPSC ID. Note: OTR alone does not constitute an application.
  • 3Login using your TGPSC ID and Date of Birth, and enter the OTP received on your registered mobile number.
  • 4Verify details fetched from OTR - Name, Date of Birth, Educational Qualifications, Community, Gender, Disability, Ex-Servicemen status, Sports, etc.
  • 5Select your preferred examination centre from the available options.
  • 6Upload a recent passport-size colour photograph (not older than 3 months; File size: 100KB–200KB) and scanned specimen signature (File size: 20KB–30KB) in JPG/PNG format.
  • 7Preview the application carefully, then click SAVE & PAYMENT to proceed to fee payment.
  • 8Pay the prescribed fee online through Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, or UPI.
  • 9After payment, a PDF application will be generated. Download and retain the PDF. Note your Reference ID Number for future correspondence.
  • 10Click CONFIRM & SUBMIT to complete the submission. No changes will be made by the Commission to any submitted application.
  • 11For technical issues: Call 040-23120301 / 040-23120302 / 040-22445566 / 040-67445566 (10:30 AM to 5:00 PM on working days) or email [email protected].
