Important Instructions

Only Indian Nationals are eligible; students studying outside India cannot apply. Minimum 6.5 CGPA is mandatory; 6th Sem (UG) / 2nd Sem (PG) marksheet must be submitted at time of joining. Candidates must commit to the full 6-month continuous internship period; early exit means no certificate will be issued. Separate applications must be submitted to each lab if applying to multiple labs. Police Verification Certificate from local police authorities must be submitted before joining. Laptops, mobile phones, Smart Watches, USB dongles or any storage media are not permitted inside lab premises. Physical presence for minimum 15 days per month is mandatory. Progress report to be submitted after 3 months and final project report after 6 months. Certificate from HoD/Principal that the student will not be called to college (except for examinations) during internship period is mandatory. Lab will not provide marks, grades or evaluation for the student. Interns must not mention or misrepresent any DRDO Lab on social media at any time. Selection as intern does not guarantee employment in DRDO at any later date. Misrepresentation of facts at any stage will result in immediate cancellation of candidature without notice. All decisions of the respective labs and DRDO regarding selection are final and binding.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is DRDO DG(Aero) Paid Internship Scheme 2026-27?
A
DRDO DG(Aero) Paid Internship Scheme 2026-27 is a 6-month paid internship program by the Defence Research and Development Organisation under Director General (Aeronautical Systems) for final year UG/PG Engineering and Science students across five labs in Bangalore, Agra, and outstation RCMAs.
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are available in DRDO DG(Aero) Paid Internship 2026?
A
A total of 200 paid internship vacancies are available across five labs: ADE Bangalore (69), ADRDE Agra (20), CABS Bangalore (60), CEMILAC including RCMAs (20), and GTRE Bangalore (31).
Q3Q3. What is the last date to apply for DRDO DG(Aero) Paid Internship 2026?
A
The last date for online application submission is 05 June 2026, with applications opening on 26 May 2026.
Q4Q4. What is the eligibility for DRDO DG(Aero) Paid Internship 2026?
A
Candidates must be final year UG or PG students in Engineering/Science streams from Indian institutions with minimum 6.5 CGPA and must submit 6th semester (UG) or 2nd semester (PG) marksheet at the time of joining.
Q5Q5. What is the stipend for DRDO DG(Aero) Paid Internship 2026?
A
Interns receive a total stipend of ₹30,000 paid in two installments: ₹15,000 after completing 3 months and ₹15,000 after completing the next 3 months.
Q6Q6. What is the duration of DRDO DG(Aero) Paid Internship 2026?
A
The internship duration is 6 months (continuous and mandatory). Candidates who leave before completing the full duration will not receive an internship certificate.
Q7Q7. Which disciplines are eligible for DRDO DG(Aero) Paid Internship 2026?
A
Eligible disciplines include CSE/IT, Aeronautical, Mechanical, Electrical/EEE, Electronics/ECE, Instrumentation, Metallurgy, Material Science, Chemical Engineering, Data Science, Textile/Polymer Science, and Defence Technologies.
Q8Q8. Can students studying outside India apply for DRDO DG(Aero) Paid Internship 2026?
A
No, only Indian nationals studying in Indian institutions are eligible. Students who have studied or are studying in countries outside India are not eligible to apply.
