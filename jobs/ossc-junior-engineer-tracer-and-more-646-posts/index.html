How to Apply

  • 1Admission Letter (downloaded from www.ossc.gov.in) along with valid photo identity proof in original and photocopy
  • 2Original academic certificates and marksheets (relevant Diploma/Degree/Certificate onwards)
  • 3Caste Certificate in the prescribed Govt. of Odisha format (for SEBC/SC/ST candidates)
  • 4Disability Certificate / UDID Card issued by competent Medical Authority (for PwD candidates) — scanned copy of original to be uploaded at application stage
  • 5Sports ID Card issued by Director of Sports & Youth Service Department, Govt. of Odisha (for Sports Person candidates)
  • 6Discharge Certificate (for Ex-Servicemen candidates)
  • 7No Objection Certificate (NOC) from competent controlling authority (for Government servants / PSU employees)
  • 8Any other documents as intimated in the Admission Letter for Certificate Verification (details in Annexure-B of notification)
  • 9The recruitment shall be guided by "Combined Technical Services Recruitment Examination Rules-2022" as amended from time to time.
  • 10Candidates must possess a valid email ID and mobile number while applying and keep them active till completion of the recruitment process to receive important messages from the Commission.
  • 11The appointment will be only against one of the posts carrying the corresponding pay as indicated in the vacancy table; candidates applying for more than one post/service must give option/preference for the post/service in case they qualify.
  • 12The Commission will adopt a "Normalisation Formula" published vide Notice No.2444/OSSC dated 02.09.2021 for processing results if any examination is conducted through CBRE/OMR mode in multiple batches using different sets of question papers.
  • 13No Admission Letter for recruitment at any stage shall be sent by post; candidates must download from www.ossc.gov.in regularly for updates.
  • 14Candidates are advised to submit the Online Application Form well in advance without waiting for the closing date to avoid last-hour rush.
  • 15All posts are State Cadre posts; the candidate on selection may be asked to serve anywhere in Odisha.
  • 16OBC (Creamy Layer) candidates are not entitled to SEBC reservation; they must apply under UR category.
  • 17PwD candidates must ensure they possess a permanent disability certificate issued online and upload the scanned copy of the original (not photocopy) of the Disability Certificate/UDID Card.
  • 18There shall be no provision for re-evaluation/re-checking of scores; no correspondence in this regard shall be entertained.
  • 19Mobile phones, Bluetooth devices, spy cameras, smartwatches and any other electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited inside the examination hall.
  • 20In case of tie in scores, merit will be decided by: (i) Marks in Preliminary Examination, (ii) Date of Birth with older candidate placed higher, and (iii) Alphabetical order of names.
  • 21The Commission's decision in all matters relating to eligibility, acceptance/rejection of applications, mode of selection, conduct of examination, allotment of examination centres and preparation of merit list shall be final and binding.
  • 22Any complaint on the conduct of the examination must be sent to [email protected] within 05 (five) days of completion of examination.
  • 23Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification.
