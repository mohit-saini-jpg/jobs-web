FAQs

Q1What is OSSC CTSRE 2025 Recruitment?
A
OSSC CTSRE 2025 is a Combined Technical Services Recruitment Examination conducted by the Odisha Staff Selection Commission to fill 646 Group-B and Group-C technical posts across various departments.
Q2How many vacancies are available in OSSC CTSRE 2025?
A
A total of 646 vacancies have been announced, mainly for Junior Engineer (Civil) along with posts like Architectural Assistant, Cameraman, and others.
Q3What is the last date to apply for OSSC CTSRE 2025?
A
The last date for online registration and fee payment is 29 June 2026, while the final submission deadline is 2 July 2026.
Q4What is the eligibility for OSSC CTSRE 2025?
A
Candidates must have a Diploma/Degree in relevant fields (like Civil Engineering, Architecture, or Media-related disciplines) and must be able to read, write, and speak Odia.
Q5What is the age limit for OSSC CTSRE 2025?
A
Applicants must be between 21 to 42 years as on 01 January 2025, with age relaxation applicable for reserved categories as per Odisha government rules.
Q6What is the selection process for OSSC CTSRE 2025?
A
The selection process includes: Preliminary Examination (Objective) Main Written Examination (Technical) Certificate Verification
Q7Is there any application fee for OSSC CTSRE 2025?
A
Yes, ₹500 for General/SEBC candidates (refundable after appearing in prelims), while SC/ST/PwD candidates are exempted.
Q8What is the exam pattern of OSSC CTSRE 2025?
A
The prelim exam consists of 150 MCQs (150 marks), and the mains includes a 200-mark technical paper. Negative marking applies in both stages.
Q9Can candidates from other states apply for OSSC CTSRE 2025?
A
Yes, but only Indian citizens are eligible. However, candidates must fulfill the Odia language requirement to qualify.
Q10How to apply for OSSC CTSRE 2025 online?
A
Candidates can apply online through the official website ossc.gov.in by completing registration, filling out the application form, uploading documents, and paying the fee.
