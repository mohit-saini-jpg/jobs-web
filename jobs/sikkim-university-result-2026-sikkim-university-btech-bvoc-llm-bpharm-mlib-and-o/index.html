How to Check Sikkim University Result 2026 Online?

Students can check their Sikkim University result 2026 for B.Tech, B.Voc, LLM, B.Pharm, M.Lib and Other exams by following these simple steps:

  • Visit Official Website: Go to results.logisys.org
  • Find Results Section: Click on "Results" or "Examination" tab on homepage
  • Select Your Exam: Choose B.Tech, B.Voc, LLM, B.Pharm, M.Lib and Other exams from the list
  • Enter Details: Input your Roll Number and Registration Number
  • Submit & View: Click "Submit" to view your Sikkim University result 2026
  • Download: Save/print your mark sheet PDF for future reference

To check your Sikkim University results 2026, students must have the following details ready:
