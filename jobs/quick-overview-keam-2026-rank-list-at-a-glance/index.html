Quick Overview: KEAM 2026 Rank List at a Glance : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 27/06/2026

The Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE), Kerala, has officially released the KEAM 2026 Rank List for Engineering and Pharmacy courses. The merit list - long awaited by over 1.48 lakh registered candidates - is now accessible through the official candidate portal at cee.kerala.gov.in. Candidates can log in using their application number and password to view and download their rank cards.

The rank list was formally announced on June 27, 2026, following a series of delays linked to the CBSE Class 12 revaluation process. With the merit list now live, the next critical phase - KEAM 2026 Centralised Allotment Process (CAP) counselling - is expected to begin shortly.

Why Was the KEAM 2026 Rank List Delayed?

The KEAM 2026 rank list was originally scheduled to be published on or before June 20, 2026. However, the release was postponed twice - first to June 22, and then to June 26/27 - before finally going live.

The delay was primarily linked to the CBSE Class 12 revaluation results, which were announced on June 21. Since the KEAM rank formula incorporates Class 12 board marks, Higher Education Minister Roji M. John directed CEE to extend the Plus Two marks submission window, ensuring CBSE students were not unfairly disadvantaged in the rank preparation process. This decision gave all eligible candidates - including those whose CBSE scores had been revised - a fair opportunity to have their updated marks reflected in the KEAM rank list.

How to Check and Download KEAM 2026 Rank Card

Downloading your KEAM 2026 rank card is a straightforward process done entirely online. Here's a step-by-step guide to accessing your rank through the CEE Kerala candidate portal:

  • Visit the official website - Go to Click Here
  • Open the Candidate Login portal - The portal is currently open for Engineering and Pharmacy candidates.
  • Enter your Application Number - This is the number you received at the time of KEAM registration.
  • Enter your Password - Use the password created during registration.
  • Enter the CAPTCHA code - Type in the access code shown on the screen.
  • Click LOGIN - You will be taken to your candidate dashboard.
  • View your rank card - Your engineering or pharmacy rank will be displayed on the dashboard.
  • Download and print the rank card - Save a digital copy and take a printout, as it may be required during counselling and document verification.

Tip: If you have forgotten your application number or password, use the "Forgot Application Number" or "Forgot Password" links available on the login page.

How Is the KEAM 2026 Rank List Calculated?

Understanding how your rank is determined helps you assess your standing and make informed college choices. The KEAM 2026 rank list uses a 50:50 weightage formula:

Engineering Rank List Formula

For Engineering (B.Tech/BE) candidates, the rank is determined by giving equal weightage to:

  • 50% - Normalised KEAM 2026 Entrance Exam Score (out of 600, for Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry combined)
  • 50% - Normalised Class 12 Board Marks (marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics from the qualifying examination)

Pharmacy Rank List Formula

For the B.Pharm merit list, the rank is based primarily on the KEAM Pharmacy Entrance Examination score, with the maximum score being 300.

Key Eligibility Condition

Candidates who did not submit their Plus Two marks within the prescribed deadline have not been assigned a rank and are, therefore, ineligible for KEAM 2026 counselling and admissions. Only candidates who fulfilled this requirement and scored a minimum of 10 normalised marks in the entrance exam appear in the rank list.

What Information Is on the KEAM 2026 Rank Card?

The rank card is an important document that you will need throughout the KEAM admission process. It typically contains the following details:

  • Candidate's full name
  • Roll number
  • Application number
  • Normalised entrance examination score
  • Engineering rank (if applicable)
  • Pharmacy rank (if applicable)
  • Category and reservation details
  • Qualifying examination status

Candidates should carefully verify all information on the rank card. Any discrepancy must be reported to CEE Kerala immediately.

KEAM 2026 Toppers List

CEE Kerala released the toppers list alongside the rank list, with separate merit toppers announced for both Engineering and Pharmacy streams. The toppers list includes:

  • Names of top-ranking candidates
  • Their scores out of 600 (Engineering) / 300 (Pharmacy)
  • Their final KEAM ranks

Candidates securing the highest ranks in KEAM 2026 will have priority access to premier institutions like College of Engineering Trivandrum (CET), Government Engineering College (GEC) Thrissur, and other top government engineering colleges in Kerala.

What Happens After the KEAM 2026 Rank List?

The rank list is the gateway to the KEAM Centralised Allotment Process (CAP), which is Kerala's fully online, Single Window System for seat allotment across Engineering, Pharmacy, Architecture, and Medical courses.

KEAM 2026 CAP Counselling Process - Step by Step

CEE Kerala is expected to announce the detailed CAP schedule shortly after the rank list publication. The counselling process involves the following stages:

  • Option Registration - Eligible candidates register their preferred colleges and courses in order of priority through the online portal.
  • Rearrangement of Options - Candidates can revise or rearrange their submitted choices within the allowed window.
  • Final Option List Submission - Candidates confirm and lock their final list of options.
  • Provisional Allotment - Seats are provisionally allotted based on rank, reservation category, and availability.
  • Final Seat Allotment Result - The confirmed allotment list is published.
  • Payment of Option Confirmation Fee - Selected candidates pay the prescribed fee to confirm their seat.
  • Reporting to Allotted College - Candidates complete the admission process by reporting to their allotted institution with all required documents.

All updates related to KEAM CAP 2026 - including the schedule, round-wise allotments, and notifications - will be posted on cee.kerala.gov.in.

Courses Covered Under KEAM Admissions

It is worth noting that while the current rank list covers Engineering and Pharmacy, CEE Kerala's Single Window System also facilitates admissions to a wider range of professional courses. The candidate portal for Medical and Architecture courses will be opened at a later stage. The full range of courses under KEAM admissions includes:

  • Engineering (B.Tech / BE)
  • Architecture
  • MBBS / BDS
  • Ayurveda / Homoeopathy / Siddha / Unani
  • Agriculture / Forestry / Veterinary / Fisheries
  • Pharmacy (B.Pharm)
  • Co-operation & Banking
  • Climate Change & Environmental Science
  • B.Tech Biotechnology.
