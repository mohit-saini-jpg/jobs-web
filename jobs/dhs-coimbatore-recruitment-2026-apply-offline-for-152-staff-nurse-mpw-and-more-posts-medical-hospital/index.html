Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for District Health Society Coimbatore NHM Recruitment 2026?

The last date for submission of applications is 15 June 2026 by 05:00 PM. Applications must be submitted either by hand delivery or through Speed Post to the District Health Society Office, Coimbatore. Applications received after the deadline will not be entertained.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in District Health Society Coimbatore NHM Recruitment 2026?

A total of 152 contractual posts have been announced across 15 categories including Medical Officer (11 posts), Health Inspector Gr.II (20 posts), Staff Nurse (8 posts), ULB-UHN (85 posts), MLHP (3 posts), Data Manager (1 post), and other supporting staff positions. The number of vacancies is subject to change as per actual requirement.

Q3. What is the salary for District Health Society Coimbatore NHM posts?

Salary varies by post: Medical Officer (UHWC) - Rs. 63,000/- per month, Data Manager (IDSP) - Rs. 25,000/-, Occupational Therapist - Rs. 23,000/-, Staff Nurse/MLHP - Rs. 21,000/-, Programme cum Administrative Assistant - Rs. 20,000/-, Health Inspector Gr.II/ULB-UHN/Optometrist - Rs. 17,500/-, Dental Technician - Rs. 17,000/-, and Cleaner/Multipurpose Hospital Worker/Sanitary Worker/Security Guard - Rs. 11,000/- per month.

Q4. Are these permanent government posts?

No, these are purely temporary and contractual posts for a period of 11 months. Appointees will NOT be given permanent status at any time and will not be transferred during the contract period. Selected candidates must submit a self-undertaking letter and an 11-month contract agreement at the time of joining.

Q5. What is the age limit for District Health Society Coimbatore NHM Recruitment 2026?

Age limit varies by post: Medical Officer and Occupational Therapist - less than 40 years; Data Manager - less than 40 years; Health Inspector Gr.II (UHWC), ULB-UHN, Cleaner, and other supporting staff - less than 35 years; Staff Nurse, Health Inspector Gr.II (MTM), and MLHP - less than 50 years; Programme cum Administrative Assistant - less than 45 years. Age relaxation for reserved categories applies as per Tamil Nadu Government rules.

Q6. What educational qualification is required for Medical Officer (UHWC) post?

Candidates must possess an MBBS Degree awarded by a UGC-recognised University or Institution, approved by the Medical Council of India/National Medical Commission (NMC), and must be registered with the Tamil Nadu Medical Council.

Q7. What is the qualification required for ULB-UHN posts (85 vacancies)?

Candidates should have passed Higher Secondary School Examination and completed a Two-year ANM (Auxiliary Nurse Midwife) course from DPH (Directorate of Public Health) approved institutions. Age limit is less than 35 years.

Q8. How to apply for District Health Society Coimbatore NHM Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must download the prescribed application form from https://coimbatore.nic.in, fill it completely with all required details, attach self-attested copies of all certificates, affix a passport-size photograph, and submit the application either by hand or through Speed Post to: Member Secretary/District Health Officer, Maavatta Nalavalvu Sangam (District Health Society), District Health Office, 219, Race Course Road, Coimbatore - 18.
