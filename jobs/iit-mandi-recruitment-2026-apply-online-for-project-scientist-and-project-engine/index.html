Qualification / Eligibility

Project Scientist: M.Tech/M.E or equivalent in Remote Sensing, Hydrology, Water Resources Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Earthquake Engineering and allied areas with B.Tech in Civil Engineering or equivalent OR Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering or Technology or equivalent from any CFTI with minimum CGPA of 7.5 for General (UR)/7.0 for OBC (NCL)/6.5 for SC/ST/PwD on a 10-point scale. Project Engineer: Bachelor's degree in Engineering/Technology (CSE/ECE/EEE/Civil/Instrumentation or allied areas) with at least 70% marks or equivalent CGPA and minimum 2 years of relevant work experience in handling laboratory equipment, instrumentation, or technical support.
