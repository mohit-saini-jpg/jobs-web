The last date to submit the offline application for the NEIGRIHMS Faculty Posts is 22nd June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well in advance to avoid any last-minute issues.
Q2How many vacancies are available for Faculty Posts at NEIGRIHMS?
A
There are a total of 52 vacancies for Faculty Posts, including Professor, Associate Professor, and Assistant Professor positions, as per the NEIGRIHMS recruitment notification.
Q3What is the application mode for NEIGRIHMS Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The application mode for the NEIGRIHMS Faculty Recruitment 2026 is offline. Candidates need to download the application form from the official website and submit it via post.
Q4What is the minimum qualification required for the Assistant Professor posts?
A
The minimum qualification for Assistant Professor posts generally includes MBBS with MD/MS/DM/MCh (depending on the specialty) and a specified number of years of teaching/research experience, along with Medical Council Registration.
Q5What is the salary offered for the Faculty Posts at NEIGRIHMS?
A
The salary for the Faculty Posts ranges from Rs. 101500/- to Rs. 159100/- per month, depending on the post level, along with applicable allowances like NPA.
Q6What is the application fee for NEIGRIHMS Faculty Recruitment?
A
The application fee is Rs. 500/- for General/OBC candidates and Rs. 250/- for SC/ST candidates. Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwBD) are exempted from paying any fee.
Q7What is the selection process for NEIGRIHMS Faculty Posts?
A
The selection process typically involves screening of applications, followed by an interview and document verification. Candidates must meet all eligibility criteria to be considered.
Q8Where can I download the official notification for NEIGRIHMS Recruitment 2026?
A
The official notification can be downloaded from the official website of NEIGRIHMS, which is http://neigrihms.gov.in. It is also available in the 'Important Links' section of this article.
Q9What is the age limit for applying to these posts?

The age limit for these posts is as per the Government of India rules for SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD/Ex-SM categories. Specific age limits are not detailed in the summary but are subject to government norms.
Q10Does NEIGRIHMS offer any age relaxation for reserved categories?

Yes, age relaxation is provided for candidates belonging to SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD/Ex-SM categories as per the prevailing Government of India rules and regulations.
