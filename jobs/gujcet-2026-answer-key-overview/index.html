2paragraphWho Can Challenge the GUJCET 2026 Answer Key?
3listunordered
  • Only candidates who appeared for GUJCET 2026 are eligible to raise objections.
  • Objections can be submitted for specific questions where the candidate believes the official answer is wrong or ambiguous.
4paragraphSteps to Raise Objections Against GUJCET Answer Key 2026
5listordered
  • Read the official notification for the GUJCET 2026 provisional answer key and objection guidelines.
  • Note the last date, time and mode for submitting objections.

  • Fill the objection form or online objection module with all details: question number, current key answer and your proposed answer.

  • Submit the objections within the given deadline; late submissions are typically not accepted.
