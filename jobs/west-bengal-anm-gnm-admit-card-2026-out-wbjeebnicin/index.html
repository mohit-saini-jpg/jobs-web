The West Bengal ANM GNM (Auxiliary Nurse Midwifery / General Nursing & Midwifery) entrance exam is a state-level test conducted annually by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) for admission to ANM and GNM nursing courses offered by government and private nursing institutes across West Bengal.

As the exam date of 14 June 2026 draws near, WBJEEB has officially published the WB ANM GNM Hall Ticket 2026 on 04 June 2026 at wbjeeb.in. The examination will be conducted in offline OMR-based mode from 12:00 Noon to 1:30 PM at designated centres in West Bengal. Candidates must note that digital copies will not be accepted at the exam venue only printed hard copies are valid.

How to Download WB ANM GNM Admit Card 2026 at wbjeeb.in?

Follow the detailed step-by-step process below to download the West Bengal ANM GNM Admit Card 2026:

  • Step 1: Go to the official website of WBJEEB : wbjeeb.in.
  • Step 2: Click on the "WB ANM GNM Admit Card 2026" PDF download link on the homepage.
  • Step 3: You will be redirected to the candidate login window.
  • Step 4: Enter your Application Number, Date of Birth, and Security Pin (Captcha) in the required fields.
  • Step 5: Click the "Submit" or "Download" button.
  • Step 6: Your WB ANM GNM Hall Ticket 2026 will appear on screen. Verify all the details carefully.
  • Step 7: Download the admit card PDF and take at least 2–3 clear printouts for use on the examination day.

What Details Are Mentioned on the WB ANM GNM Admit Card 2026?

After downloading the hall ticket, candidates must carefully check each detail. If any discrepancy is found, it must be reported to WBJEEB immediately. The following details are printed on the WB ANM GNM Hall Ticket 2026:

  • Candidate's full name
  • Roll Number / Application Number
  • Candidate's Photograph
  • Candidate's Signature
  • Father's / Mother's Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Category
  • Course Name (ANM / GNM)
  • Exam Date and Reporting Time
  • Exam Shift Timing
  • Examination Centre Name and Full Address
  • Examination Centre Code
  • Important Exam-Day Instructions
  • Candidate's Signature Space / Invigilator Signature Space

Documents to Carry Along with WB ANM GNM Hall Ticket 2026

On the day of the WB ANM GNM 2026 entrance examination, candidates must carry the following documents to the exam centre. Keep them ready 2–3 days before the exam to avoid any last-minute rush:

  • Printout of the WB ANM GNM Admit Card 2026 (colour printout preferred)
  • Valid Photo ID issued by the Government (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card, Passport, etc.)
  • Passport-sized Photograph (same as uploaded in the application form)

Documents NOT to Carry on WB ANM GNM Exam Day 2026

To ensure a fair examination environment, the following items are strictly prohibited inside the WB ANM GNM 2026 exam centre:

  • Textbooks, notebooks, or any study material
  • Mobile phones, smartwatches, or fitness bands
  • Bluetooth devices or wireless earphones
  • Calculators or digital watches
  • Electronic gadgets of any kind
  • Bags, wallets, or purses
  • Geometry boxes, pencil boxes, or pouches
