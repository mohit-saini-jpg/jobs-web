BASU Recruitment 2026 Eligibility Criteria

The University follows the Bihar Government reservation policy. Reservation benefits (including EWS, 35% female reservation, and fee relaxation) are applicable only to candidates who are original residents of Bihar, subject to submission of a residential certificate. Doctoral degree in the relevant subject, including relevant basic sciences. 08 years' experience in the relevant subject as Assistant Professor-cum-Junior Scientist/Scientist/Lecturer/Extension Specialist or an equivalent position in Pay Band-3 of Rs. 15,600–39,100 with GP/AGP/RGP of Rs. 5,400/Rs. 6,000/Rs. 7,000/Rs. 8,000, with evidence of contribution to research/teaching/extension education through published work or innovations and impact, OR Doctoral degree in the relevant subject including relevant basic sciences, with a minimum of 8 years of high-quality post-doctoral research experience in an institution/organization,

BASU Recruitment 2026 Age Limit

There is no upper age limit for the post of Associate Professor. Maximum age is fixed as per Resolution No. 294 dated 07.01.2016 issued by the General Administration Department, Govt. of Bihar: 5 years' extra age relaxation for Govt. of Bihar employees applying for a higher pay scale (Resolution No. 3/M-63/2005-2374 dated 16.07.2007). No age restriction for existing BASU employees seeking higher posts under direct recruitment procedures. Divyang (differently-abled) candidates of all categories get an additional 10 years' relaxation in maximum age (Resolution No. 13062 dated 12.10.2017). Retirement age at BASU is 65 years.

BASU Recruitment 2026 Reservation Policy

Reservation is applicable to candidates who are original residents of Bihar. As per the Bihar Reservation in Vacancies in Posts and Services and in Admission in Educational Institutes (For Economically Weaker Sections) Act, 2019, Clause-11, if EWS candidates are not available to the extent of the prescribed reservation percentage, those vacancies are filled from the Open Merit category in the same transaction. 02 posts of Associate Professor and 02 posts of Assistant Professor are reserved for Deaf and Hearing Impaired and Locomotor Disability candidates under the Divyang category (Circular No. 962 dated 22.01.2021 and Memo No. 8962 dated 21.05.2025), applicable only to original residents of Bihar. 01 post of Associate Professor and 01 post of Assistant Professor are reserved for grandchildren of freedom fighters (Circular No. 2526 dated 18.02.2016); selected candidates are counted again

BASU Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

Selection is based on the candidate's previous academic and professional record (as per the prescribed scorecard) and performance in the interview. As per Chapter-VII Note-6(iii) of the University Statute, a candidate must obtain at least 50% marks in the interview to be considered in the merit list. As per the directive of the Dairy, Fisheries and Animal Resource Department, Govt. of Bihar (L.No. 06/BASU(II)07/2023-4229 dated 13.11.2023), candidates belonging to SC, ST, Divyang category, and female candidates (irrespective of category) need to obtain only 32% marks in the interview to be considered in the merit list. Candidates must produce all original certificates and documents at the time of interview for verification; failure to do so can lead to rejection of candidature.
