DU SOL Admit Card 2026

The School of Open Learning, University of Delhi, has made the DU SOL Admit Card 2026 available online for May-June 2026 semester examinations. As per the latest update, hall tickets can now be downloaded for 2nd, 4th, 6th and 8th semester students from the official SOL admit card portal.

The DU SOL hall ticket 2026 contains essential exam details like subject codes, exam dates, examination centre and reporting time. Students must carry a printed copy of the admit card along with valid photo ID to be allowed entry into the examination hall.

How to Download DU SOL Admit Card 2026

Students can download their DU SOL May-June 2026 hall ticket by following these steps:

  • Visit the official SOL website: https://sol.du.ac.in and click on the “Student Admit Card”/hall ticket link for the relevant semester, or directly open the admit card portal https://admission.sol.du.ac.in/admissionticket/HallTicket.aspx.
  • On the hall ticket page, enter your SOL Roll Number exactly as printed on your ID card/fee receipt (e.g., 01‑1‑01‑001000; no spaces).
  • Enter your Barcode number, if required, in the given field.
  • Click on “Submit / Show” to generate your hall ticket.
  • Your DU SOL Admit Card 2026 PDF will open; check all details carefully and then download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies.

If you do not remember your barcode, use the “Know Your Barcode” option on the portal or check your previous admit card/student dashboard.

Details Mentioned on DU SOL Admit Card 2026

After downloading, verify the following details printed on your admit card:

  • Student’s name and photograph (if printed)
  • SOL roll number and exam roll number
  • Programme and semester (e.g., BA Prog 2nd/4th/6th/8th sem, B.Com, MA, M.Com etc.)
  • Academic session and exam type (Semester May-June 2026)
  • Paper titles and paper codes for each subject
  • Exam date, time and duration (as per DU date sheet)
  • Exam centre name and full address
  • Important instructions, including items allowed/prohibited in the exam hall

In case of any discrepancy in the date, time or venue printed on the admit card, the date sheet/notification issued by the Examination Branch, University of Delhi will be treated as final.

Documents Required with DU SOL Admit Card 2026

On each exam day, SOL students must carry:

  • A printed copy of the DU SOL Admit Card 2026 downloaded from the official website (this web‑generated admit card is valid subject to verification at the examination centre).
  • One original photo identity proof, such as: SOL ID card, Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport, or Driving Licence.
  • Any other document mentioned in the admit card/instructions, if applicable.

Students are not allowed to carry electronic gadgets such as mobile phones, Bluetooth devices, or any other communication devices inside the examination centre; violation may attract action under Unfair Means (UFM) rules.
