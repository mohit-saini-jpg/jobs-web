The last date to submit the offline application for the Ganjam District Motor Launch Driver and Khalasi posts is 28th May 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this date.

For Motor Launch Driver, a Minimum 2nd Class Launch Driving License is required. For Boat Khalasi, a Navik Training Certificate is necessary. A general minimum qualification of 12th pass is also indicated.

Q7. What is the mode of application for Ganjam District jobs?
