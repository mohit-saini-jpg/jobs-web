How to Apply

  • 1This is a contractual appointment-appointments to contractual positions are purely temporary. They will not be eligible to claim similar positions in government service.
  • 2Contractual appointment can be terminated at any time by the management based on conduct or work behavior, with 3 months' prior notice or 3 months' pay in lieu thereof.
  • 3Candidate must be a मूल निवासी of Chhattisgarh state. Certificate issued by competent authority must be attached.
  • 4Mandatory English Medium Requirement: Applicants must have completed the qualifying educational qualifications (from 6th grade to a postgraduate degree) in English medium. English medium will not be mandatory for Hindi and Sanskrit lecturers in 10th grade and all higher classes.
  • 5All 38 SAGES schools in Durg district operate under the High Court of New Delhi's order (SLP(C) No. 19668/2022) regarding contractual appointments.
  • 6Currently serving contractual teachers at SAGES cannot apply for the same-level posts; only for higher-level posts.
  • 7Only online applications submitted on the given website are valid. Documents submitted at time of online form submission are the only valid documents for verification.
  • 8Applicants will be called for document verification on the basis of their marks obtained in the qualifying examination.
  • 9The number of posts advertised is subject to change - the total vacancy count may change.
  • 10The selection committee's decision on appointment will be final and binding.
  • 11Incomplete or erroneous applications will be summarily rejected without separate intimation.
  • 12If only one application is received, the first application will be considered valid; subsequent applications will be treated as cancelled.

  • 14Contractual employees are entitled only to monthly lump sum remuneration; no special pay, DA, HRA, or annual increment is applicable under the contract appointment.
  • 15Candidates with SC/ST/OBC category must attach permanent caste certificate issued by a competent authority at the time of document verification.
  • 16Any candidate found to have provided incorrect information or whose documents are found to have discrepancies will be disqualified without prior notice.
  • 17After the final merit list, candidates on the waitlist will remain valid for 1 year from the date of result or until the next engagement exercise, whichever is earlier.
