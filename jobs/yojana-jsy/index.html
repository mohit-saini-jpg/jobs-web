Overview

Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY) ek mahaatvapurn Sarkari Yojana hai jo Maa ki Suraksha aur Swasthya ke liye aayojit kiya gaya hai. Is Yojana ke madhyam se aam aadmiyon tak swasthya sevaon ki prapti ki ja sakti hai.

Expert Analysis

JSY ka chayan process ek srot hai jahan aapke samajik karyakramon ki vyavastha ki jaati hai. Iske liye, aapko karyakramon ki vyavastha aur unka sahi tareeke se poora karnaa hoga.

Who Should Apply

JSY ke liye adhikarit aam aadmi aur karyakartaa hain jinkon main mahilaon ki suraksha aur swasthya ke liye karya karna hoga.

Preparation Tips

JSY ke liye taiyaar rahen: 1) Apne karyakramon ki vyavastha karke rakhen. 2) Samajik karyakramon ka sahi tareeke se samna karen. 3) Apne gatividhiyon ko sahi tareeke se samjhen.

Salary Insights

JSY ke adhikaarion ki avaranvati salary lagbhag ₹15,000 se ₹25,000 tak hai, jiska vartmaan uplabdhan ₹20,000 hai. Salary aapke karyakramon aur samay ke anusar badh sakti hai.

Job Profile

JSY ke adhikaarion ka kaam mahilaon ki suraksha aur swasthya ke liye karya karna hai. Unke din chalne wale kaam main karyakramon ki vyavastha, samajik karyakramon ka samna karna aur gatividhiyon ka samjhauna shamil hai.

Selection Strategy

JSY ke chayan process mein karyakramon ki vyavastha aur samajik karyakramon ka sahi tareeke se samna karna shamil hai. Aapko apne karyakramon ki vyavastha aur unka sahi tareeke se samjhaana hoga.
