Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies for Teaching Faculty posts at ESIC Kolkata?

There are a total of 27 vacancies announced for the Teaching Faculty positions, including Professor, Associate Professor, and Assistant Professor roles in various super specialty departments.

Q2. What is the application mode for this ESIC Kolkata recruitment?

The application mode for this recruitment is a Walk-in Interview. Candidates need to appear directly at the venue on the specified date and time with all necessary documents.

Q3. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview?

The walk-in interview for the Teaching Faculty positions at ESIC Kolkata is scheduled to be held on 28th May 2026.

Q4. What is the maximum age limit for the candidates applying for these posts?

The maximum age limit for all the posts is not exceeding 69 years as of the date of Document Verification and Interview.

Q5. What are the salary details for the Professor, Associate Professor, and Assistant Professor posts?

The salary for Professor is Rs. 2,60,226/-, for Associate Professor is Rs. 1,73,045/-, and for Assistant Professor is Rs. 1,48,669/-.

Q6. Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?

The walk-in interview will be conducted at ESIC Kolkata, located at Joka, Kolkata, West Bengal, PIN 700104.

Q7. What are the eligibility criteria for the Teaching Faculty posts?

The eligibility criteria are as per the latest guidelines of the National Medical Commission (NMC) vide Gazette notification no. CG-DL-E-05072025-264399 dated 30.06.2025 (Table C & Table D).

Q8. Is there any application fee for SC/ST candidates?

No, there is no application fee for SC/ST candidates, as well as for Regular ESIC Candidates, Female Candidates, and Ex-Servicemen.
