There are a total of 4 vacancies. One vacancy is for the Project Nurse-II post, and three vacancies are for the Project Technical Support - I post.
Q3. What is the educational qualification required for Project Nurse-II?
For the Project Nurse-II position, candidates must have completed a three-year General Nursing & Midwife (GNM) Course.
Q4. What are the eligibility criteria for Project Technical Support - I?
Candidates need to have completed 10th grade along with a Diploma (MLT/DMLT / ITI or equivalent) and two years of relevant experience, or a three-year Graduate degree in a relevant field with one year of experience.

The maximum age limit is 35 years for Project Nurse-II and 30 years for Project Technical Support - I.

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q7. What is the salary offered for these positions?
The salary ranges from Rs. 19,800 to Rs. 22,000 per month, depending on the post and including House Rent Allowance (HRA).
Q8. How will candidates be selected for these posts?
The selection process will involve a Written Examination followed by an Interview.
