IGCAR Apprentice Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

Applications will be shortlisted on the following basis: The candidate must possess an ITI certificate in the trade applied for. Candidates are shortlisted for training based on the percentage of marks obtained in their ITI course. In case of a tie in ITI marks percentage, candidates with a higher percentage in 10th standard (8th standard only for Welder, Carpenter/Wood Work Technician, Mason/Civil Mistry, and Plumber) are placed higher in the merit list. If there is still a tie in 10th/8th class marks percentage, the candidate born earlier (older) is given preference. The selected list for each trade is published on the IGCAR website. All shortlisted candidates must appear for Document Verification; failure to do so results in cancellation of candidature. IGCAR Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Register on the Apprenticeship India portal at https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in for enrollment as an Apprentice. Applications will not be accepted without the unique registration number (e.g. AXXXXXXXXXX) obtained from this portal.
  • 2Complete mandatory Aadhaar (e-KYC) verification of your enrollment on the apprenticeshipindia.gov.in portal.
  • 3Provide the same registered email ID and mobile number (used during e-KYC registration) in your IGCAR application.
  • 4Prepare scanned documents in PDF format before filling the form: (i) Caste/Disability/EWS certificate, and (ii) 8th or 10th class mark sheet as applicable, ITI trade certificate (NCVT/SCVT), semester-wise mark sheet, and consolidated mark sheet scanned as a single PDF.
  • 5Submit the application online via www.igcar.gov.in, under the "Opportunities → Recruitment" tab, between 20/07/2026 and 19/08/2026. Only one trade may be applied for.
  • 6Check all entered details carefully before final submission — no changes are allowed after submission under any circumstances.
  • 7Selected candidates will be provided hostel accommodation on a chargeable basis, subject to availability.

IGCAR Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Candidates should submit an online application for one trade only. Applicants who have already undergone or are currently undergoing apprenticeship training under the Apprentices Act, 1961 elsewhere are not eligible. Selected candidates must submit a Police Verification Certificate from their local police station at the time of Document Verification. IGCAR reserves the right to cancel or alter the number of seats in a trade, or the entire engagement process, without assigning any reason. Disrespectful, aggressive, dishonest, or evasive behavior by an apprentice can lead to termination of the apprenticeship. There is no provision for employment after completion of the apprenticeship training, and candidates cannot claim employment on this basis. IGCAR is governed by special provisions under the Atomic Energy Act, 1962; apprentices are governed by IGCAR's prevailing rules and the CCS (Conduct) Rules, 1964, in matters of conduct and discipline. If any declaration by a candidate is found false, they will be disqualified/terminated at any stage without notice. Canvassing or personal follow-up to influence selection leads to immediate cancellation of candidature. All correspondence relating to the recruitment process will be published only on the IGCAR website — no individual correspondence will be entertained. In case of any dispute, the decision of IGCAR is final.
