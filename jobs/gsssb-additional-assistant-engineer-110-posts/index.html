How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official website https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in.
  • 2Click on "Apply" under the Online Application section and select GSSSB.
  • 3Locate Advertisement No. 419/202627 (Assistant Engineer Mechanical, Class-3), click on the cadre name, then click Apply. Review the "More Details" option to read the complete advertisement before proceeding.
  • 4Click on Apply Now, then "Skip" to open the Application Format and fill in the Personal Details (fields marked with a red asterisk are mandatory).
  • 5Fill in the Educational Details section after completing Personal Details.
  • 6Note down the generated Application Number for future reference.
  • 7Upload your photograph and signature: photo should be 5 cm height x 3.6 cm width, and signature should be 2.5 cm height x 7.5 cm width, both in JPG format not exceeding 15 KB. The photo must have a white background and must not be older than one year from the last date of application.
  • 8Click on "Confirm Application" under the Online Application tab, enter your Application Number and Date of Birth, review the details, and confirm only after thorough verification, since no changes are allowed after confirmation. A Confirmation Number will be generated, which must be saved carefully.
  • 9Use "Print Application" to download and save a copy of the confirmed application form using your Confirmation Number and Date of Birth before the last date.
  • 10The exam consists of a total of 210 questions (90 in Part-A and 120 in Part-B) to be completed in a combined duration of 3 hours (180 minutes).
  • 11Negative marking applies: for every wrong answer, 1/4th of the marks allotted to that question will be deducted.
  • 12A provisional answer key will be published after the exam, followed by a final answer key after considering objections raised by candidates.
  • 13Separate minimum qualifying standards of 40% apply to both Part-A and Part-B for all categories of candidates.

FAQs

Q1How many vacancies are available in GSSSB Additional Assistant Engineer Mechanical Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 110 posts of Additional Assistant Engineer (Mechanical), Class-3 are available under the Gujarat Water Supply and Sewerage Board.
Q2What is the last date to apply for GSSSB Additional Assistant Engineer Mechanical Recruitment 2026?
A
Online applications can be submitted from 15/06/2026 (18:00 hrs) to 29/06/2026 (up to 23:59 hrs).
Q3What is the educational qualification required for GSSSB Assistant Engineer Mechanical Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must hold a Diploma in Engineering (Mechanical) or Diploma in Automobile from a recognized university or institution, along with basic computer knowledge and knowledge of Gujarati or Hindi.
Q4What is the age limit for GSSSB Additional Assistant Engineer Mechanical Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 18 and 33 years of age as on 29/06/2026, with age relaxation available for reserved categories, women, ex-servicemen, and persons with disabilities as per applicable rules.
Q5What is the application fee for GSSSB Additional Assistant Engineer Mechanical Recruitment 2026?
A
The fee is Rs. 500/- for unreserved candidates and Rs. 400/- for reserved category candidates (women of all categories, SEBC, SC, ST, EWS, PWD, and Ex-Servicemen), payable online along with transaction charges.
Q6How can candidates apply online for GSSSB Additional Assistant Engineer Mechanical 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online by visiting https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in, selecting GSSSB, and clicking Apply Online against Advertisement No. 419/202627 before the application window closes on 29/06/2026.
Q7What is the exam pattern for GSSSB Additional Assistant Engineer Mechanical Recruitment 2026?
A
The exam is a single-stage CBRT/OMR-based MCQ test with 210 questions across Part-A (90 marks: Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, Constitution, Current Affairs, Comprehension) and Part-B (120 marks: Technical subject), with a total duration of 3 hours and negative marking of 1/4 mark per wrong answer.
Q8What is the pay scale for GSSSB Additional Assistant Engineer Mechanical?
A
Selected candidates receive a fixed pay of Rs. 49,600/- per month for the first five years, after which they are placed in the regular Level-7 pay scale (Rs. 39,900 to Rs. 1,26,600), subject to satisfactory performance.
Q9What is the minimum qualifying mark for GSSSB Additional Assistant Engineer Mechanical exam?
A
Candidates must secure a minimum of 40% marks in both Part-A and Part-B separately to qualify for the selection process.
Q10Where can candidates download the GSSSB Additional Assistant Engineer Mechanical notification PDF?
A
The official notification along with the detailed syllabus is available through the PDF Download Link on the GSSSB website: https://gsssb.gujarat.gov.in.
