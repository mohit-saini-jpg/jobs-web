How to Apply

  • 1Download the prescribed application form from the CSIR-NPL official website www.nplindia.in.
  • 2Fill in the application form carefully - enter your Name, Date of Birth, and Father's/Mother's name exactly as given in your SSC/Matriculation certificate.
  • 3Paste a recent passport-size colour photograph on Part I of the application form and sign across it. Paste another recent photograph on Part II (do not sign across this one).
  • 4Prepare a Demand Draft of Rs. 500/- drawn on any nationalised bank valid for at least 3 months, in favour of "Director, National Physical Laboratory", payable at New Delhi. Write your name and post code on the back of the DD. (SC/ST/PwBD/Women/CSIR Employees/Transgender candidates are exempted from fee.)
  • 5Attach self-attested copies of all required documents (see Documents Required section below).
  • 6If you are a Government/CSIR/PSU employee, obtain an NOC/forwarding letter from your employer (Proforma-VII) and send a hard copy through proper channel. An advance copy may be submitted before the closing date.
  • 7Superscribe the envelope as: "APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF TECHNICIAN(1) – (Post Code No. ____)"
  • 8Send the complete application by post to: Senior Controller of Administration, CSIR-National Physical Laboratory, Dr. K.S. Krishnan Marg, New Delhi – 110012 to reach on or before 30.06.2026.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for CSIR NPL Technician Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for receipt of the application form at CSIR-National Physical Laboratory, New Delhi is 30th June 2026. This date is the same for candidates from all areas including far-flung regions.
Q2How many vacancies are there in CSIR NPL Technician 2026?
A
A total of 20 vacancies have been released for the post of Technician (1) across 6 trades — Electronics (10), Electrical (5), COPA (2), Multimedia (1), Digital Photography (1), and Mechanic Motor Vehicle (1).
Q3What is the salary for CSIR NPL Technician 2026?
A
The basic pay is Rs. 19,900/- per month (Pay Level 2). The total emoluments including HRA in a Class X city are approximately Rs. 44,428/- per month.
Q4What is the age limit for CSIR NPL Technician Recruitment 2026?
A
The age limit is minimum 18 years and maximum 28 years as on 30.06.2026. Relaxation is available for SC/ST (5 years), OBC-NCL (3 years), PwBD (10 years for UR), Ex-Servicemen (3 years), and regular Govt./CSIR employees (up to 5 years).
Q5What is the qualification for CSIR NPL Technician 2026?
A
Candidates must have passed SSC/10th Standard with Science subjects with minimum 55% marks AND hold an ITI certificate in the relevant trade, OR have 2 years apprentice training, OR 3 years work experience in the relevant trade in a Govt./PSU organisation.
Q6What is the application fee for CSIR NPL Technician 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 500/- via Demand Draft for General/UR/OBC/EWS candidates. SC, ST, PwBD, Women, CSIR Employees, and Transgender candidates are exempted from the fee.
Q7How is the selection done for CSIR NPL Technician 2026?
A
Selection involves three stages: Screening by the Screening Committee → Trade Test (qualifying) → Competitive Written Examination (Paper-I: Mental Ability, Paper-II: General Awareness + English, Paper-III: Trade Subject). Final merit is based on marks in Paper-II + Paper-III.
Q8Is there negative marking in CSIR NPL Technician Written Exam 2026?
A
Paper-I (Mental Ability) has no negative marking. Paper-II (General Awareness & English) and Paper-III (Trade Subject) carry 1 mark negative for every wrong answer.
Q9Where should I send the CSIR NPL Technician 2026 application?
A
Send your application by post to: Senior Controller of Administration, CSIR-National Physical Laboratory, Dr. K.S. Krishnan Marg, New Delhi – 110012. Superscribe the envelope with the post code.
Q10Can I apply online for CSIR NPL Technician Recruitment 2026?
A
No. CSIR-NPL Technician 2026 applications must be submitted by post only using the prescribed application form downloaded from nplindia.in. There is no online application mode for this recruitment.
