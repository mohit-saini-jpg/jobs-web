How to Apply

  • 1Download the prescribed application form from the CSIR-NPL official website www.nplindia.in.
  • 2Fill in the application form carefully - enter your Name, Date of Birth, and Father's/Mother's name exactly as given in your SSC/Matriculation certificate.
  • 3Paste a recent passport-size colour photograph on Part I of the application form and sign across it. Paste another recent photograph on Part II (do not sign across this one).
  • 4Prepare a Demand Draft of Rs. 500/- drawn on any nationalised bank valid for at least 3 months, in favour of "Director, National Physical Laboratory", payable at New Delhi. Write your name and post code on the back of the DD. (SC/ST/PwBD/Women/CSIR Employees/Transgender candidates are exempted from fee.)
  • 5Attach self-attested copies of all required documents (see Documents Required section below).
  • 6If you are a Government/CSIR/PSU employee, obtain an NOC/forwarding letter from your employer (Proforma-VII) and send a hard copy through proper channel. An advance copy may be submitted before the closing date.
  • 7Superscribe the envelope as: "APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF TECHNICIAN(1) – (Post Code No. ____)"
  • 8Send the complete application by post to: Senior Controller of Administration, CSIR-National Physical Laboratory, Dr. K.S. Krishnan Marg, New Delhi – 110012 to reach on or before 30.06.2026.
