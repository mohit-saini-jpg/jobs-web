How to Apply

  • 1Staff Selection Commission SSC issued a notice on 22 February 2024 saying that they have launched a new website SSC Gov in, in which all the candidates will have to do fresh one time registration (OTR)
  • 2Step 1 : Open the Website ssc.gov.in or Direct Link Will be in this Page Some Useful Important Links Section
  • 3Step 2 : Click on New Registration Tab
  • 4Step 3 : Students will have to give their basic information such as: Name, Father’s Name, Mother’s Name, Mobile Number, Email Id and High School Class 10 Roll Number.
  • 5Step 4 : Verify Your OTR Profile Details & Submit Registration.
  • 6Step 5 : An OTP will be received on the registered email and registered mobile number of the candidate, he will have to enter it.
  • 7Step 6 : The registration Part I process will be completed, the candidate will see the OTR Registration No. on the screen.
  • 8Step 7 : In the next step, you will have to change the default password which has been received on your email, after that you will have to login by writing the registration number along with the password which has been received on the email.
  • 9Step 9 : Candidates should change the default password of OTR registration number with their custom password. Use capital letters, small letters, numbers and special characters while creating the new password which should be at least 08 digits. Additionally, two security questions will also have to be selected, so that if you forget your password in future, you can recover your password with the help of the security question.
  • 10Step 10 : After changing the password, the candidate will have to login again, after that the information about his nationality, category, Visible Identification Mark and information about permanent and local address will be entered in the form.
  • 11Step 11 : The last step is that here you have to first double check all the information by clicking on the preview button of OTR, after checking, if there is any error then correct it, otherwise click on the declare button and submit the form finally.
  • 12Step 12 : The registration process of SSC OTR has now been completed, the candidate will be able to see the dashboard whenever any new recruitment comes, or the candidate can also get the information about admit card and result by logging into the SSC dashboard.
  • 13Our team has also shown a video on how to do SSC one time registration, which you can watch and do your new registration by following the step by step process: SSC OTR Registration Kaise Kare Video Dekhe (Watch Video)
  • 14For more information, you have to complete the notice issued to SSC OTR.
