Selection for DTU Apprenticeship Trainees 2026-27 is based on screening of applications followed by a document verification interview, rather than a written examination. DTU Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Although the final application form for DTU Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is submitted offline by post, the process begins online through mandatory NATS portal registration. Here is the complete step-by-step process to apply.
  • 2Visit the National Apprenticeship Training Scheme portal at nats.education.gov.in and complete candidate registration.
  • 3Download the application form from the DTU official website www.dtu.ac.in or use the format provided in the advertisement.
  • 4Fill the application form in block letters, mentioning Advertisement No., NATS Enrolment No., trade applied for, and complete educational qualification details.
  • 5Affix a passport-size photograph in the space provided on the form.
  • 6Attach self-attested copies of the 10th certificate, 12th certificate, Diploma certificate (if applicable), B.E./B.Tech/B.Sc. certificate, and identity proof.
  • 7Superscribe the envelope as "Application for engagement of Apprentice Trainee (2026-27) applied for………" mentioning the trade name.
  • 8Send the complete application by post to: The Assistant Registrar (Gen. Admin), Delhi Technological University, Shahbad Daulatpur, Bawana Road, Delhi-110042.
  • 9Ensure the application reaches DTU on or before the last date, 31 July 2026.

DTU Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for DTU Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for the application to reach DTU is 31 July 2026, and candidates must complete NATS registration before applying.
Q2How many vacancies are there in DTU Apprenticeship 2026-27?
A
There are 108 total vacancies for Graduate and Diploma Apprentice Trainees across 22 trades including Electrical, Mechanical, IT, ECE, CSE, and Library.
Q3What is the mode of application for DTU Apprentice Bharti 2026?
A
Candidates must first register on the NATS portal online, then submit the typed application form by post to the Assistant Registrar (Gen. Admin), DTU, on or before 31 July 2026.
Q4What is the stipend for DTU Apprentice Trainees?
A
Diploma Apprentices receive a monthly stipend of ₹10,900, while Graduate Apprentices receive ₹12,300 per month, depending on the trade.
Q5Is NATS registration mandatory for DTU Apprenticeship Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, registration on the National Apprenticeship Training Scheme portal (nats.education.gov.in) is mandatory, and applications without it will not be accepted.
Q6What documents are required for DTU Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates need self-attested copies of 10th, 12th, Diploma, and B.E./B.Tech/B.Sc. certificates and mark sheets, along with identity proof and a passport-size photograph.
Q7What is the duration of the DTU apprenticeship?
A
The apprenticeship engagement is for a period of one year, as stated in the official DTU notification.
Q8Does DTU offer a job after completion of the apprenticeship?
A
No, DTU has clarified that upon completion of the apprenticeship, the university has no obligation to offer employment to any apprentice under any circumstances.
Q9Which trade has the highest vacancy in DTU Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
Mechanical (Lab requirement) has the highest vacancy with 25 posts, followed by Electrical with 17 posts.
Q10Where can candidates check the official DTU Apprenticeship notification and apply online?
A
The official notification, application form, and NATS registration link are available on the Delhi Technological University website at www.dtu.ac.in.
