Qualification / Eligibility

Details
  1. Only female candidates are eligible to apply for the Nalgonda Anganwadi Recruitment 2026. Candidates must be permanent residents of the gram panchayat or ward (in urban areas) where the Anganwadi Centre vacancy exists. Both the Anganwadi Worker and Anganwadi Helper posts have specific educational qualification and age requirements as set out in the official notification
  2. For the Anganwadi Worker post, the merit list will be prepared on the basis of the highest educational qualification. Candidates with higher qualifications will be ranked above those with only Intermediate. For both posts, the candidate must belong to the same gram panchayat or ward (in urban/Samsthana areas) as the concerned AWC
  3. Candidates wondering about the age limit for the Nalgonda Anganwadi Bharti 2026 should note that the minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 35 years. The age will be calculated as on the date of notification. The official notification specifies that candidates between 18 and 35 years of age are eligible; no candidate outside this range will be considered
  4. For the Anganwadi Worker post in rural areas, the candidate must be a local woman from that specific gram panchayat area. For urban/Samsthana areas, she must be from the same ward. For the Anganwadi Helper post, the candidate must specifically be a woman from the same gram panchayat or ward area (gram panchayat/ward samstha perimitlo) as the AWC. The residential requirement is strictly enforced for both posts
  5. The notification specifies a defined priority order for selection under the Nalgonda Anganwadi Worker Vacancy 2026:
  6. Local women from the same gram sabha/ward (gram panchayat/ward area) who belong to BPL families - widows, legally divorced, or abandoned women - given first priority. If no such candidates are available from the above category, other women from BPL families of the same gram panchayat/ward area are given priority. If the above categories are exhausted, women from APL families (above poverty line) of the same gram panchayat/ward area - widows, divorced, or abandoned - are considered. If no candidates from the above categories are available, other women from APL families of the same gram panchayat/ward area are considered

How to Apply

  • 1Eligible female candidates can apply for the Nalgonda Anganwadi Worker Helper Recruitment 2026 exclusively through the online portal at wdcw.tg.nic.in. The application window is open from 27 June 2026 to 10 July 2026 by 5:00 PM. No offline or paper applications will be accepted under any circumstances for the Nalgonda Anganwadi Bharti 2026.
  • 2Visit the official Telangana WDC portal at wdcw.tg.nic.in.
  • 3Navigate to the Nalgonda Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 application link.
  • 4Identify the AWC (Anganwadi Centre) in your gram panchayat/ward from the published vacancy list.
  • 5Fill in the online application form with your personal, educational, and residential details.
  • 6Upload required documents including proof of residence, educational certificates, age proof, and caste certificate (if applicable).
  • 7Submit the application online before 10 July 2026 by 5:00 PM.
  • 8Take a printout of the submitted application for future reference.
  • 9Candidates must note that income certificates, caste certificates, and residence certificates must be issued by the competent authority (tehsil-level authority with digital signature verifiable online). For widow status, a death certificate of the husband issued by the gram panchayat/urban body and an authenticated family register copy must be submitted. For divorced/abandoned status, a court-issued legal order must be furnished.

FAQs

Q1Has the Nalgonda Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 official notification been released?
A
Yes, the District Welfare Officer, Women, Child Disabled & Senior Citizens Department, Nalgonda has officially released the Nalgonda Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 notification vide G.O. No. 725/A/2026 dated 19.06.2026. The public press notification was published on 24 June 2026.
Q2What is the last date to apply for Nalgonda Anganwadi Bharti 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for the Nalgonda Anganwadi Worker Helper Recruitment 2026 is 10 July 2026 by 5:00 PM. Applications must be submitted through the official portal wdcw.tg.nic.in only.
Q3How many vacancies are available in Nalgonda Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
The official AWC-wise vacancy list for Nalgonda district covers 343 entries for both Anganwadi Worker (AWT) and Anganwadi Helper (AWH) posts across 9 ICDS projects — Anumula, Chinthapally, Damaracherla, Deverakonda, Kondamallepally, Miryalaguda (U), Munugodu, Nakrekal, and Nalgonda. Each vacancy is specific to a particular AWC.
Q4Who is eligible to apply for Nalgonda Anganwadi Worker Vacancy 2026?
A
Only female candidates aged 18 to 35 years who are permanent residents of the concerned gram panchayat or ward are eligible. For the AWT post, the minimum qualification is Intermediate (12th pass). For the AWH post, candidates must be literate. Males are not eligible.
Q5What is the age limit for Nalgonda Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 35 years as on the notification date. No age relaxation details beyond this range are mentioned in the official notification.
Q6Is there an application fee for Nalgonda Anganwadi Bharti 2026?
A
No application fee is mentioned in the official notification for the Nalgonda Anganwadi Recruitment 2026. The application at wdcw.tg.nic.in is free of cost.
Q7Is there a written test for Nalgonda Anganwadi Worker Helper Recruitment 2026?
A
No written examination is mentioned in the official notification. Selection is based on a merit-cum-priority system that considers educational qualification marks, a local test, interview, and Zilla Emarpu Committee evaluation. Priority is given to BPL widows, divorced, and abandoned women from the same gram panchayat/ward.
Q8Where can I apply online for Nalgonda Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications can be submitted only through the official Telangana WDC portal at wdcw.tg.nic.in. No offline, postal, or email applications will be accepted. The portal is open from 27 June 2026 to 10 July 2026 (5:00 PM).
Q9Can I apply for any AWC in Nalgonda district or only for my gram panchayat?
A
Candidates can apply only for the AWC located in their own gram panchayat or ward where they are a permanent resident. Applying for an AWC outside the candidate's residential area makes her ineligible for that post.
Q10Where will the selected candidates' list be displayed for Nalgonda Anganwadi Vacancy 2026?
A
The final selected candidates' list will be displayed at the Collectorate/Vikas Bhavan, Nalgonda and at the respective CDPO/DWO office. Candidates are advised to also monitor the official website wdcw.tg.nic.in for updates.
