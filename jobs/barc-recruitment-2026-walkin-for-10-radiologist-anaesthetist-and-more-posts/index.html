Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for BARC Medical Specialists?

The walk-in interview for most of the Medical Specialist posts is scheduled for 03 June 2026. For Dermatologist, it is on 04 June 2026. Candidates should report on the specified dates.

Q2. What is the total number of vacancies for Medical Specialists at BARC?

Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (BARC) is recruiting for a total of 10 Medical Specialist posts. The detailed breakdown for each specialty is available in the vacancy details section.

Q3. What is the salary offered for the Medical Specialists posts at BARC?

The selected candidates for the Medical Specialists posts will receive an approximate salary ranging from Rs. 88,400 to Rs. 154,000 per month.

Q4. What are the minimum and maximum age limits for the Medical Specialists posts?

The minimum age limit for the Medical Specialists posts is 25 years, and the maximum age limit is 70 years. Age relaxation is applicable as per government norms.

Q5. What is the application fee for BARC Medical Specialists recruitment?

There is no application fee for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive. Candidates can attend the walk-in interview without paying any fee.

Q6. What is the selection process for the Medical Specialists positions at BARC?

The selection process for the Medical Specialists positions at BARC involves Preliminary Screening followed by a Walk-in Interview. Candidates must clear both stages to be considered for the role.

Q7. What qualifications are required for the Medical Specialists posts?

Candidates must possess an MBBS degree along with an MD/DNB/DM/MS/DA in the relevant specialty. Specific experience requirements vary for each post as detailed in the notification.

Q8. Where is the walk-in interview for BARC Medical Specialists going to be held?

The walk-in interviews will be conducted at Bhabha Atomic Research Centre, Mumbai, Maharashtra, with the pincode 400094. Candidates should report to the specified venue.
