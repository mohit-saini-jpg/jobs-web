CSIR NIO Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

CSIR-National Institute of Oceanography (NIO), Goa has invited candidates for 27 Apprentice Trainee posts under the Apprenticeship Act, 1961, through Notification No. NIO/HRM/Apprenticeship/01/2026 dated 18 June 2026. There is no written application process - eligible candidates must register on the NATS portal and directly attend a walk-in interview on 6 July 2026 at CSIR-NIO Headquarters, Dona Paula, Goa, with reporting time between 8:30 AM and 10:00 AM.

What Is the CSIR-NIO Apprenticeship Recruitment 2026?

Candidates wondering what this recruitment involves can note that it is a one-year apprenticeship training opportunity, not a regular job, offered by CSIR-NIO under the Apprenticeship Act, 1961. The institute is seeking Technician (Diploma) and Graduate (Non-Engineering Degree) holders to undergo structured on-the-job training across engineering, administrative, and oceanography-related functions at its Goa headquarters. Important Dates for CSIR-NIO Apprenticeship 2026

Candidates tracking the recruitment timeline should note that this notification has a single, fixed interview date rather than an application window. Stipend and Training Period

Candidates asking how much CSIR-NIO apprentices are paid can note that the stipend is fixed by category and does not vary by individual trade within that category. Technician (Diploma) apprentices receive Rs. 10,900/- per month , while Graduate (Non-Engineering Degree) apprentices receive Rs. 12,300/- per month , both for a training period of 1 year . On successful completion of training, apprentices will be awarded a National Apprenticeship Certificate by the National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT)/BOAT . CSIR-NIO has no obligation to offer regular employment after training, and apprentices cannot claim employment rights on the basis of completing the apprenticeship.

NATS Registration: A Mandatory First Step

Candidates wondering how to begin the application process should know there is no conventional application form; instead, registration on the National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS) portal is compulsory before attending the interview. Candidates appearing for the walk-in interview must register on the NATS portal and quote their enrollment number in the application form submitted at the interview. Visit https://nats.education.gov.in/ . Click on "Student - Student Register." Complete the registration form with accurate personal and educational details. A Unique Student Enrolment ID will be generated upon successful registration. Note down this enrollment number, as it is mandatory to mention in the application form at the walk-in interview. Candidates who have already registered on NATS in the past do not need to register again but must carry their existing enrollment number.

Documents Required at the Walk-In Interview

Candidates preparing for the interview should carry a complete document set, since missing documents will lead to the candidature not being considered at all. CSIR-NIO requires both originals (for verification) and self-attested photocopies of all relevant testimonials. Candidates curious about how CSIR-NIO will select apprentices can note that the primary mode is a direct walk-in interview, with a written test only triggered under specific conditions.
