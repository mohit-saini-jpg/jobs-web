NISST Recruitment 2026 - Apply Offline for Technical Manager and Faculty Posts

Short Information

National Institute of Secondary Steel Technology (NISST) has released an official notification for the recruitment of 6 Technical Manager and Faculty for Electrician Courses Posts. Interested and eligible candidates can apply Offline by sending the application form to the official address. The last date to submit the application is 28-05-2026.

Age Limit

Age DetailsMax 35 years for Technical Manager, no specific limit for Faculty.
DetailsTechnical Manager (Electrical & Electronics): Maximum age limit is 35 years. Age relaxation for SC/ST/OBC/PwD is as per Govt. of India rules. Faculty for Electrician Courses: No specific age limit mentioned.

Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationMasters in Electrical/Electronic Engineering for Technical Manager and B.E. in Electrical Engineering for Faculty.
DetailsTechnical Manager (Electrical / Electronics): Master's Degree in Electrical / Electronic Engineering from an AICTE-approved Institution with a minimum of seven (07) years of experience in a steel plant in the areas of Energy Management, Power Systems, Maintenance and Training. Experience in Government PSU shall be preferred. Faculty for Electrician Courses: B.E. in Electrical Engineering from an AICTE-approved Institution with a minimum of five (05) years of experience in the Steel Industry.
