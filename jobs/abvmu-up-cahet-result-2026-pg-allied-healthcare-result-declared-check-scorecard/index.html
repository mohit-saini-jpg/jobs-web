What Is UP CAHET 2026?

This section explains what the UP CAHET exam is and why it matters for candidates seeking admission to allied healthcare courses in Uttar Pradesh.

UP CAHET (Common Allied Healthcare Entrance Test) is a state-level entrance examination conducted by Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical University, Lucknow, for admission to undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) allied healthcare courses offered by affiliated institutions across Uttar Pradesh. The exam covers fields such as medical laboratory science, optometry, physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and radiology technology, among others.

How to Check and Download ABVMU UP CAHET Result 2026

This section explains the exact steps to log in and download your CAHET scorecard from the official portal.

Follow these steps to check your UP CAHET Result 2026:

  • Visit the official ABVMU candidate portal at abvmucet26.co.in.
  • Click on the relevant CAHET 2026 result/login link on the homepage.
  • Enter your Application Number/Registration Number and Password or Date of Birth.
  • Click the Login/Submit button.
  • Your result will be displayed on the screen, showing marks, rank, and qualifying status.
  • Download and save the scorecard.
  • Take a printout, as it will be required during counselling and document verification.

What's Next After UP CAHET Result 2026?

This section explains the steps candidates should take immediately after checking their result.

  • Watch for the official counselling schedule on the ABVMU portal.
