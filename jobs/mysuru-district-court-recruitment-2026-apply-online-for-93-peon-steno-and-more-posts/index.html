Selection Process

  • Selection will be made as per Karnataka Subordinate Courts (Recruitment) Rules.
  • Stenographer Grade-III & Copyist: Skill Test (Shorthand/Typing) + Computer Test + Interview (5% weightage). Process Server & Peon: Merit based on SSLC marks + Interview (10 marks).
  • Selected candidates will be appointed under New Pension Scheme (NPS) and will be on 2 years probation.

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted **online only**. The step-by-step process is given below:
  • Visit the official website: https://mysuru.dcourts.gov.in/ or go to Online Recruitment section.
  • Click on the respective recruitment notification link and online application link.
  • Register and fill the online application form with correct details.
  • Upload required scanned documents and photograph.
  • Pay the prescribed application fee through State Bank Collect (SBI).
  • Submit the application and take a printout for future reference.

Important Instructions

  • Only online applications will be accepted. Candidates must possess valid documents/certificates as on the last date of application. Application fee is non-refundable. Age and qualifications will be calculated as on the last date of application. Selected candidates will be subject to verification of documents and medical fitness.
