SVIMS Tirupati Recruitment 2026: Age Limit

Candidates must have completed 18 years and must not have completed 42 years of age as on 01-07-2026 (as per G.O.Ms.No.109, GAD (Service-A) Dept., dated 08-10-2025). Notification 03: Age relaxation of 5 years applies to SC, ST, BC, and EWS candidates. Notification 02: Age relaxation of 5 years applies to SC, ST, and BC candidates (EWS is not separately mentioned in this notification's age-relaxation clause). Persons presently working in SVIMS on Adhoc/Outsourcing/Contract basis get a 5-year relaxation, subject to completing a minimum of 5 years of service in SVIMS as on the date of notification. The maximum age limit with all relaxations put together is 52 years.

SVIMS Tirupati Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

Total marks: 100, split as follows for both notifications: If fewer than 10 applications are received for a post, selection is done via weightage of qualifying-examination marks, experience, and interview instead of a CBT. If more than 10 applications are received, a Computer Based Test is conducted through AP Online Service at various test centres. Minimum qualifying marks in the CBT: Notification 02 - OC 40%, BC 35%, SC/ST 30%. Notification 03 - OC/EWS 40%, BC 35%, SC/ST 30%. Steno Cum Junior Assistant (Notification 03 only): Candidates who clear the CBT qualifying marks (35% BC, 30% SC/ST as stated for this stage) are eligible for a separate Skill Test worth 50 marks, on payment of Rs. 590/- fee. Q1How many vacancies are there in SVIMS Tirupati Recruitment 2026?

There are 67 vacancies in total - 34 under Notification No.02/Para & Non Para/2026 (Paramedical and Non-Paramedical, open to AP State natives) and 33 under Notification No.03/Non Para/2026 (Non-Paramedical, open to Tirupati District natives only).
Q2What is the last date to apply for SVIMS Tirupati Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application closes on 10 August 2026, up to 11:59 p.m. Applications open on 15 July 2026.
Q3Who is eligible to apply for SVIMS Recruitment Notification No.02?
A
Candidates native to the State of Andhra Pradesh (post bifurcation) who profess the Hindu religion and meet the post-specific educational qualification and age criteria are eligible.
Q4Who is eligible to apply for SVIMS Recruitment Notification No.03?
A
Only candidates native to Tirupati District (post bifurcation of the districts) who profess the Hindu religion and meet the post-specific criteria are eligible.
Q5What is the age limit for SVIMS Tirupati Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 18 and 42 years of age as on 1 July 2026, with age relaxations of up to 5 years for SC/ST/BC (and EWS, under Notification 03) candidates, and 5 years for eligible SVIMS Adhoc/Outsourcing/Contract staff. The maximum age with all relaxations combined is 52 years.
Q6What is the selection process for SVIMS Tirupati Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection is based on 100 marks: a 75-mark Computer Based Test, 15 marks for experience, and 10 marks for interview. Posts with fewer than 10 applicants are selected via a weightage method instead of a CBT. The Steno Cum Junior Assistant post additionally requires a separate 50-mark Skill Test.

Rs. 2,360/- for OC (and EWS, under Notification 03) and Rs. 1,770/- for BC/SC/ST candidates, inclusive of 18% GST. The Steno Cum Junior Assistant Skill Test carries an additional fee of Rs. 590/-.
