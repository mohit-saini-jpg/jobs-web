Overview

A&N Sarkari Naukri seekers, a new notification is out for 1535 Group 'C' Non-Ministerial posts in various departments of Andaman & Nicobar Administration. Online application is invited via erecruitment.andamannicobar.gov.in; last date to apply is 28 June 2026.

Expert Analysis

This is a great opportunity for 10th pass candidates to join A&N Administration in various capacities. The selection process includes a Tablet Based Test (TBT) and a Physical/Skill/Trade Test.

Who Should Apply

Ideal candidates should be 10th pass with relevant skills and experience in handling Govt. jobs. A&N Administration is looking for dynamic and motivated individuals to join their team.

Preparation Tips

Start preparing for the Tablet Based Test (TBT) by practicing online mock tests and revising your basics. Focus on developing your communication skills and learning about the A&N Administration's policies and procedures.

Salary Insights

The selected candidates will get a salary of ₹18,000 - ₹45,000, which is a decent package for a Group 'C' Non-Ministerial post. However, career growth prospects and benefits may vary depending on individual performance and departmental requirements.

Job Profile

As a Group 'C' Non-Ministerial employee in A&N Administration, you will be responsible for handling day-to-day administrative tasks, providing support to senior officials, and maintaining records. With experience and growth, you can move to higher posts and take on more challenging responsibilities.

Selection Strategy

To crack the selection process, focus on acing the Tablet Based Test (TBT) and preparing well for the Physical/Skill/Trade Test. Develop a strategy to answer questions and practice mock tests to boost your confidence.

Andaman and Nicobar Combined Matric Level Exam 2026 : Important Instructions

Candidature will be accepted only provisionally; the A&N Administration will not undertake detailed scrutiny at the time of TBT examination. Verification of eligibility, documents, and certificates will be done at the time of Online Document Verification stage. Candidates must carry printed e-Admit Card and one valid government-issued photo ID (in original) to the TBT examination - entry without Admit Card will not be allowed. The A&N Administration reserves the right to restrict entry to the examination venue till 09:30 AM. Banned items in examination hall: Mobile Phone, Micro Phone, Calculators, Pager, Digital Diary, Electronic/Smart Watch/Wrist Watches, Bluetooth devices, any other electronic/electrical devices, Book, and Notes are strictly prohibited. Possession of these items even in switched-off mode will be treated as use of unfair means. There is no provision for re-evaluation/re-checking of Answer Sheets/Scripts for TBT examinations. No correspondence in this regard will be entertained. All information/corrigendum/addendum regarding schedule of examinations, admit cards, results, etc. shall be posted only on the official website https://erecruitment.andamannicobar.gov.in - no separate information will be issued in print media. Candidates fraudulently claiming ST/OBC/EWS/ESM/PwD status will be permanently debarred from examinations conducted by the A&N Administration. Success in the examination confers no right of appointment unless the A&N Administration is satisfied that the candidate is suitable in all respects for appointment to the Government service/post; the decision of the A&N Administration is final and binding. The decision of the A&N Administration in all matters relating to eligibility, acceptance/rejection of applications, mode of selection, conduct of examination, allotment of centres, selection, and allotment of posts/organizations to selected candidates shall be final and binding; no enquiry/correspondence shall be entertained. Any dispute in regard to this recruitment shall be subject to Courts/Tribunals having jurisdiction over the A&N Administration only. Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification.
