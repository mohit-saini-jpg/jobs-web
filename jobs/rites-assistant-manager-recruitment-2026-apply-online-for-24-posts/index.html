RITES Assistant Manager (Mechanical) Eligibility Criteria 2026

Candidates wondering whether they qualify for RITES Assistant Manager (Mechanical) Recruitment 2026 can check the educational qualification, experience, and age limit requirements below. Candidates must hold a full-time Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering/Technology in Mechanical, Production, Production & Industrial, Manufacturing, Mechanical/Railways, Mechatronics & Automobile, or a combination of the above disciplines in part or whole. UR/EWS candidates (and SC/ST/OBC(NCL)/PWD candidates applying against unreserved posts) need a first-class degree or a minimum of 60% marks . RITES Assistant Manager (Mechanical) Selection Process 2026

Selection for RITES Recruitment 2026 involves three stages - a written test, document scrutiny, and an interview - with a fixed weightage split between the written test and interview. The written test consists of 125 objective-type questions carrying one mark each, for a duration of 2.5 hours . There is no negative marking ; no marks are deducted for incorrect answers. PwBD candidates are eligible for a compensatory time of 50 minutes . A minimum of 50% marks is required for UR/EWS candidates ( 45% for SC/ST/OBC(NCL)/PwBD against reserved posts) to be considered further. Based on the written test result and the number of vacancies, RITES will scrutinize the documents submitted by candidates, and shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview preceded by verification of original documents.
