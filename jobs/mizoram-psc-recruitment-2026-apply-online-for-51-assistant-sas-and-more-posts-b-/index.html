FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for Mizoram PSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for Mizoram PSC Recruitment 2026 is 12 June 2026 up to 4:00 PM. Candidates must complete the application process through the official MPSC portal before the deadline. Late submissions will not be accepted under any circumstances.
Q2How many vacancies are available in MPSC Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 51 vacancies have been announced under this recruitment drive. It includes 41 posts for Assistant Grade through LDE and 10 posts under direct recruitment for Senior Horticulture Demonstrator and SAS-I.
Q3What are the posts offered in Mizoram PSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The recruitment includes posts such as Assistant Grade, Senior Horticulture Demonstrator, and Subordinate Agriculture Service-I (SAS-I). These positions fall under Group ‘B’ (Non-Gazetted) category in various government departments.
Q4What is the educational qualification required for MPSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The qualification depends on the post applied for. Candidates must have B.Sc. (Horticulture) for Horticulture Demonstrator, a Bachelor’s Degree in Agriculture for SAS-I, and relevant service experience for Assistant Grade (LDE).
Q5What is the age limit for Mizoram PSC Recruitment 2026?
A
For direct recruitment posts, candidates must be between 18 to 35 years as on 01 January 2026. Age relaxation of 5 years is applicable for SC/ST candidates as per government norms.
Q6Is computer knowledge required for MPSC Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, computer proficiency is mandatory as per Mizoram Government service rules. Candidates must possess the required certification or knowledge as prescribed for Group A, B, and C posts.
Q7What is the selection process for Mizoram PSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process varies depending on the post. It may include written examination, limited departmental examination, and/or interview as per the recruitment rules.
Q8How can I apply online for Mizoram PSC Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must first complete One-Time Registration on the MPSC portal. After registration, they can fill out the application form, upload documents, pay the fee, and submit the application online.
Q9Is there any application fee exemption for MPSC Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, persons with disabilities (PwD) are exempted from paying the application fee. Other candidates must pay the prescribed fee through online mode using UPI.
