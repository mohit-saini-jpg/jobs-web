FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the BITS Pilani Junior Research Fellow position?
A
The last date to apply for the Junior Research Fellow position at BITS Pilani is 07 June 2026. Candidates are strongly advised to submit their applications well before this deadline to avoid any last-minute issues.
Q2What are the educational qualifications required for the Junior Research Fellow post?
A
Candidates must have an MSc in Chemistry with at least 60% marks or an ME/MTech in interdisciplinary branches relevant to the project topic, also with at least 60% marks.
Q3Is there any experience required for this position?
A
While not strictly mandatory, lab experience in Inorganic chemistry, Synthesis, and Electrochemistry is preferred for the Junior Research Fellow position.
Q4What is the salary offered for the Junior Research Fellow role?
A
The selected candidate for the Junior Research Fellow position will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 37,000/- per month.
Q5How can I apply for the BITS Pilani Junior Research Fellow recruitment?
A
Interested candidates need to apply via email. You must send your application and curriculum vitae with research experience details to the specified email addresses.
Q6What is the selection process for this recruitment?
A
The selection process for the Junior Research Fellow position at BITS Pilani will involve an online interview.
Q7Are there any specific requirements for the email subject line?
A
Yes, the email subject line must clearly state “Application for Junior Research Fellow Position under BITS Pilani CDRF Funded Project”.
Q8Can candidates from across India apply for this position?
A
Yes, the job is open to candidates from across India, with positions available in Goa and Pilani campuses.
Q9What documents should I attach with my application?
A
You should attach self-attested copies of all required documents, including educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
Q10Where can I find the official notification for this recruitment?
A
The official notification can be accessed through the "Important Links" section of this article, under the "Official Notification PDF" heading.
