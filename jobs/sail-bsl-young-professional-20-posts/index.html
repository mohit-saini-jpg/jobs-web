FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for SAIL BSL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
A
The closing date for submitting online applications is 01-07-2026. The application window opens on 10-06-2026.
Q2What are the three levels of Young Professional engagement at SAIL Bokaro Steel Plant?
A
The three levels are Young Professional-1 (upper age 35 years, minimum 3 years post-qualification experience, retainership Rs. 70,000/month), Young Professional-2 (upper age 38 years, minimum 6 years experience, Rs. 1,00,000/month), and Young Professional-3 (upper age 42 years, minimum 10 years experience, Rs. 1,40,000/month).
Q3Is SAIL BSL Young Professional engagement a permanent government job?
A
No. This is a strictly fixed-term "Contract for Service" engagement for an initial period of up to 3 years, extendable by 2 more years at SAIL-BSL's sole discretion. It creates no right whatsoever to regularization against any permanent position in SAIL.
Q4What is the minimum educational qualification required for SAIL BSL Young Professional posts?
A
B.E./B.Tech (Regular/Full-time) in the relevant discipline from a Govt. recognised University/Institution with minimum 65% marks. For Finance Consultant (YP-1), CA/CS/CMA qualification is required. Distance/Correspondence/Off-campus qualifications are NOT eligible.
Q5Can a candidate apply for multiple roles or levels in SAIL BSL YP Recruitment 2026?
A
No. A candidate is permitted to apply for only one level and one domain/role. Applications for multiple levels or domains will not be accepted.
Q6What is the application fee for SAIL BSL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
A
The registration fee (including processing fee) is Rs. 500/- for all candidates. This fee is non-refundable under any circumstances. Payment is through Bill Desk Payment Gateway (online mode only).
Q7What is the selection process for SAIL BSL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection involves: (1) Scrutiny and shortlisting based on eligibility; (2) CBT/Aptitude Test (only if eligible applications exceed 5 times the requirement, with minimum 50 percentile qualifying score); (3) Personal Interview (physical or virtual mode); Final merit list prepared with CBT:Interview weightage of 80:20.
Q8What benefits and perks are provided to SAIL BSL Young Professionals?
A
Benefits include monthly consolidated retainership fee (Rs. 70,000/Rs. 1,00,000/Rs. 1,40,000 by level), annual increment (4%/7%/10%), residential accommodation subject to availability, OPD & IPD medical facility at Company hospital for self and family, insurance premium reimbursement (up to Rs. 20,000–30,000/year by level), communication expense reimbursement, and official tour reimbursement. PF, pension, gratuity, PRP, and loans are not applicable.
Q9What documents must be uploaded while applying for SAIL BSL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must upload: Matriculation Certificate (proof of DOB); Marks Sheet and Degree Certificate (B.E./B.Tech/M.Tech/CA/CS/CMA/MBA as applicable); Experience Certificates clearly mentioning job profile and area; Appointment Offer Letters/Promotion Orders/Release Orders and last Pay Slip from the employing company. All documents in PDF format of less than 300 KB each.
Q10Where can I check updates and corrigendum for SAIL BSL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
A
All updates, corrigendum, admit cards, CBT/interview schedules, and results will be published only on the SAIL Careers website (www.sailcareers.com). No individual communications will be sent. Candidates must visit the website regularly and ensure their email/mobile number remains active throughout the selection process.
