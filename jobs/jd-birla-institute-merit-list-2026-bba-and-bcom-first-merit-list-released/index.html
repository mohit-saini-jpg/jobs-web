JD Birla Institute Merit List 2026 - BBA and B.Com First Merit List Released

J.D. Birla Institute, Kolkata, has officially released the First Merit List 2026-27 for both the BBA (Hons.) and B.Com (Hons.) programmes on 12th June 2026. Candidates whose application numbers appear on these lists have been selected for admission and must complete fee payment within the specified deadline to confirm their seat. This article summarizes the official merit list notices, fee structure, scholarship details, and next steps for selected candidates.

How to Check Your Name in the JD Birla Institute Merit List 2026

This section explains how candidates can verify their selection status using the published PDF lists.

To check selection status:

  • Open the official merit list PDF for your course (BBA or B.Com) from the J.D. Birla Institute notices page.
  • Locate your Application Number, which was generated at the time of submitting your admission form.
  • If your application number appears on the list, you have been selected for admission to that programme.
  • Candidates marked with an *asterisk () next to their application number have additionally been selected for a merit-based scholarship.

Each list spans multiple pages and is arranged by application number rather than rank, so candidates should search the full PDF carefully for their specific number.

BBA (Hons.) First Merit List 2026 - Key Details

This section covers the specific details for candidates selected under the BBA (Hons.) Admission 2026-27 first merit list.

The BBA First Merit List, released by the Department of Management, contains the application numbers of all candidates selected for BBA (Hons.) - Year 1, Semester I for the 2026-27 session. Key points include:

  • A second merit list, if required, will be published on 16th June 2026 at 6:00 PM, subject to seat availability.
  • Candidates marked with an asterisk (*) have been awarded scholarships ranging from 20% to 80% of their 2nd Semester tuition fees. For details, candidates can call 033-24755070 or 033-24767340 between 9:30 AM and 3:30 PM on working days.
  • Selected candidates must pay the total admission fee by Tuesday, 16th June 2026.

B.Com (Hons.) First Merit List 2026 - Key Details

This section covers the specific details for candidates selected under the B.Com (Hons.) Admission 2026-27 first merit list.

The B.Com First Merit List, released by the Department of Commerce, includes selected candidates for B.Com (Hons.) - Year 1, Semester I. Important points include:

  • A second merit list, if required, will be published on 15th June 2026 at 6:00 PM, subject to seat availability.
  • Selected candidates must pay the total fee by Monday, 15th June 2026, 6:00 PM.
  • The list also specifies whether a candidate must take a Bridge Course in Mathematics, Accountancy, or both, based on their academic background. An additional Rs. 1,500 per Bridge Course applies on top of the base fee.
  • Asterisk-marked (*) candidates have been selected for 20% to 80% scholarships on their 2nd Semester tuition fees.

Document Verification and Admission Confirmation

This section explains what happens after fee payment, including document checks and required undertakings.

Admission remains provisional until all required documents are physically verified at the college. Key requirements include:

  • Candidates must personally visit the college for document verification on a date and time communicated via email.
  • An Alumni membership contribution of Rs. 5,000 is payable along with the second semester fee.
  • Candidates must submit a notarized undertaking (on Rs. 10 stamp paper, notarized in Kolkata) covering points such as accuracy of submitted information, parental consent, adherence to institute rules, a minimum 65% aggregate attendance requirement per semester, and a declaration against participation in ragging or bullying.
  • Within one week of the commencement of classes, students must also submit a separate Anti-Ragging & Anti-Bullying Undertaking to the Student Welfare Officer.
