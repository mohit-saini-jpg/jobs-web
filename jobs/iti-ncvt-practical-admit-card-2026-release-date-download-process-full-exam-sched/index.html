ITI NCVT Practical Admit Card 2026 - Release Date, Download Process & Full Exam Schedule

The ITI NCVT Practical Admit Card 2026 is expected to be released on 23 June 2026 by the Directorate General of Training (DGT) for trainees appearing in the All India Trade Test (AITT) Practical Examination. Candidates will be able to download their hall ticket using their PRN number and login credentials on the official portal, dgt.skillindiadigital.gov.in, once it goes live.

This article explains the expected release date, the practical exam schedule, how to download the admit card, and other important details trainees need to know before the AITT 2026 Practical Examinations begin.

When Will the ITI Practical Admit Card 2026 Be Released?

This section covers the expected admit card release window and why the date matters for trainees preparing for their practical exams.

As per the DGT's Revised Tentative Schedule (Version 2.0, dated 15 May 2026, signed by Swati Sethi, Controller of Examination), the Practical Hall Ticket download window is scheduled from 23 June 2026 to 10 July 2026. This means trainees should be able to access and download their admit cards anytime within this period, ahead of their respective exam dates.

ActivityStart DateEnd Date

ITI AITT 2026 Practical Exam Date

This section explains when the actual practical examinations will be conducted, which is the most important date linked to the admit card.

According to the official DGT schedule, the ITI NCVT Practical Examinations 2026 will be conducted from 6 July to 10 July 2026. These exams are conducted by respective State authorities. A detailed, day-wise practical exam timetable for each trade is provided in Annexure A of the official notice, so candidates should check their state's specific schedule once available.

Who Is Eligible for This Admit Card?

This section clarifies which batches of trainees this schedule applies to, helping candidates confirm if it's relevant to them.

The Revised Schedule 2.0 applies to the following trainee sessions, covering both Regular and Private candidates:

  • 2024-26 session - 2nd Year trainees (2-year course)
  • 2025-27 session - 1st Year trainees (2-year course)
  • 2025-26 session - Batch II trainees (1 year & 6-month course)

How to Download the ITI NCVT Practical Admit Card 2026

This section outlines the step-by-step process trainees should follow once the admit card is released on the official portal.

  • Visit the official DGT portal: dgt.skillindiadigital.gov.in
  • Log in using your PRN Number and registered login credentials.
  • Navigate to the Practical Admit Card / Hall Ticket section.
  • Download and take a printout of the admit card.
  • Verify all details printed on the hall ticket before the exam day.

Details Mentioned on the Practical Hall Ticket

This section lists the key information that will appear on the admit card so trainees know what to verify after downloading.

The ITI Practical Admit Card will typically include:

  • Candidate's name
  • Trade name
  • Exam date
  • Exam centre details
  • Other exam-related instructions

Candidates must carry both the admit card and a valid photo ID proof to the exam centre; entry without these may not be permitted.

Important Notes for Trainees

This section highlights additional points trainees should be aware of regarding eligibility and fee deadlines.

  • Trainees who completed 1200 hours of mandatory learning plus 150 hours of OJT/Project before 16 June 2026 could complete eligibility and fee payment ahead of the last date.
  • OJT (On-the-Job Training) industry registration and candidate mapping will be handled by the ITI through the SIDH portal; specific dates will be communicated separately.
  • This is a Revised Schedule 2.0, which supersedes the earlier tentative schedule.
  • The CBT exam fee submission deadline was 18 June 2026; trainees who missed it should contact their ITI immediately.
