How to Download MAH MBA CET 2025 Admit Card?

Only registered candidates will be able to download their admit cards. Steps to Download MAH MBA CET Admit Card 2025 give below.

  • Visit the official website: cetcell.mahacet.org
  • Click on the link titled ‘MBA CET 2025 Admit Card’
  • Enter your registered email ID and password
  • Click on the ‘Sign In’ button
  • Your MAH CET MBA 2025 admit card will be displayed on the screen
  • Click on ‘Download’ and save it to your device
  • It is recommended to print multiple copies for future reference.

Details Mentioned on the MAH MBA CET 2025 Hall Ticket

The admit card will contain the following details:

  • Candidate’s Name
  • Roll Number
  • Exam Date and Time Slot
  • Test Centre Address
  • Candidate’s Photograph and Signature

Documents Required Along with the Admit Card

On the exam day, candidates must carry the following documents:

  • Valid Photo ID Proof (Any One)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID
  • PAN Card
  • Driver’s License
  • College ID (if applicable)
  • Bank Passbook with Photograph
  • Additional Documents
  • Printed Copy of Admit Card
  • Recent Passport-sized Photograph (same as uploaded during registration)
  • For PWD Candidates: Medical certificate or relevant documents, if applicable

Important Exam Day Instructions

  • Reach the exam centre at least 30 minutes before the reporting time.
  • Ensure all required documents are in place to avoid last-minute hassle.
  • Electronic devices, study materials, and calculators are not allowed inside the examination hall.
  • Follow the instructions provided by the invigilators strictly.
