Telangana Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 Expert Analysis

This recruitment is a great opportunity for women candidates to join the government sector in Telangana. The Anganwadi Teacher role is a key position in the ICDS program, providing essential services to children and mothers.

Who Should Apply Telangana Anganwadi Recruitment 2026

Ideal candidates should have a degree in Arts/Science/Commerce, with a focus on education or social work. Prior experience in teaching or community work is a plus.

Telangana Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 Preparation Tips

Start by reviewing the eligibility criteria and required documents. Practice your interview skills and be prepared to answer questions on your teaching experience and community work.

Telangana Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 Salary Insights

The salary for Anganwadi Teachers in Telangana ranges from ₹21,700 to ₹69,100. This is a competitive salary, considering the job's importance and the government's efforts to improve the ICDS program.

Telangana Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 Job Profile

As an Anganwadi Teacher, you'll work with children and mothers in rural areas, providing essential services like nutrition, health, and education. This role offers opportunities for career growth and professional development.

Telangana Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 Selection Strategy

Focus on highlighting your teaching experience, community work, and communication skills during the selection process. Be prepared to answer questions on your ability to work with diverse groups and adapt to rural settings.

Bhadradri Kothagudem Anganwadi Teacher Recruitment 2026: Eligibility Criteria

Candidates should have passed Intermediate (10+2) as the minimum educational qualification for the post of Anganwadi Teacher. As on 1 July 2026, the age of candidates should be between 18 and 35 years.

Bhadradri Kothagudem Anganwadi Teacher Recruitment 2026: Selection Preference Criteria

As per the notification, the following preference criteria apply during selection: Preference is given to local candidates (residents of the local area under the concerned Anganwadi Center/project). Women with disabilities who use assistive aids and appliances are given due consideration. Unmarried, widowed, or divorced women without adequate support are given weightage in selection. Candidates should have the ability to teach basic education related to child welfare and Anganwadi activities, and to protect and nurture children, as part of the role's responsibilities. The official notification does not specify a separate written examination or interview stage; candidates are advised to refer to the official website and the local ICDS Project Office for complete details on the selection methodology and reservation-related weightage.
