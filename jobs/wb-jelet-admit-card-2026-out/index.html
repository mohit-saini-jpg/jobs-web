WB JELET Admit Card 2026 Out

The West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) has released the WB JELET Admit Card 2026 for the Joint Entrance for Lateral Entry exam. Registered candidates can now download their hall ticket from the official WBJEEB website using their login details. The admit card is mandatory for entry into the examination hall, and candidates should check all details carefully after downloading it.

How to Download WB JELET Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official website wbjeeb.nic.in and open the JELET 2026 page.
  • Click on the Admit Card for JELET-2026 link.
  • Enter your application number and date of birth.
  • Fill in the security pin/captcha.
  • Submit the details to view your hall ticket.
  • Download the PDF and print a copy for exam day.

If the portal is slow due to heavy traffic, try again after some time.

Details Mentioned on the Admit Card

The WB JELET hall ticket generally includes:

  • Candidate’s name.
  • Roll number and application number.
  • Photograph and signature.
  • Exam date and time.
  • Exam centre name and full address.
  • Reporting time and gate closing time.
  • Important exam-day instructions.

Check every detail carefully. If there is any mismatch, contact WBJEEB immediately.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates should carry the following items to the exam centre:

  • A printed copy of the WB JELET Admit Card 2026.
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence or Passport.
  • Recent passport-size photographs, if mentioned in the instructions.

Without the admit card and valid ID, entry may not be allowed.

Exam-Day Instructions

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time.
  • Carry only permitted items and follow the instructions on the hall ticket.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators or other electronic devices.
  • Follow all directions given by the invigilators inside the hall.
  • Keep the admit card safe after the exam for counselling and further admission steps.
