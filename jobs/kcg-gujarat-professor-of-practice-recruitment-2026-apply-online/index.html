Qualification / Eligibility

Professor of Practice: At least 15 years of senior-level experience in any field such as science, entrepreneurship, business, social sciences, media, literature, fine arts, civil services, armed forces, legal profession, public administration, or any other relevant field. Formal academic qualifications (like degrees) are NOT required for this post if the candidate has outstanding professional experience in the concerned field.
