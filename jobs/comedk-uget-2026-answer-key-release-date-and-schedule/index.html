5How to Calculate Scores Using COMEDK UGET Answer Key 2026
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphCOMEDK UGET follows a simple marking scheme, which makes it easy for candidates to calculate their approximate score using the provisional answer key.
2paragraphBasic marking scheme (as per the COMEDK information brochure):
3listunordered
  • Each correct answer carries 1 mark
  • There is no negative marking for wrong answers
  • Unattempted questions carry 0 marks
4paragraphTo calculate your probable score:
5listordered
  • Count the total number of correct responses using the official answer key.
  • Multiply the number of correct answers by 1.
  • Since there is no negative marking, you do not deduct marks for incorrect responses.
6paragraphFor example, if a candidate has 120 correct answers out of 180 questions, their estimated score will be 120 marks.
6COMEDK UGET 2026 Answer Key Objection Process
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphCOMEDK allows candidates to challenge the provisional answer key within a limited time window by submitting objections online. This process ensures that any genuine errors in the answer key can be corrected before the final result is prepared.
7COMEDK UGET 2026 Answer Key Challenge Rules
Sr.TypeStyleItems
1listunordered
  • Objections can be submitted only through the official candidate login on comedk.org.
  • A non-refundable fee of Rs 500 per question challenged is charged; the fee may be refunded if the objection is found valid and accepted by experts.
  • Candidates must provide proper academic justification or reference for each objection.
  • Objections submitted after the deadline or without payment/supporting proof are not considered.
8Steps to Raise Objection Online
Sr.TypeStyleItemsText
1listordered
  • Visit comedk.org and log in with your application number and password.
  • Click on the link for “Raise Objection to Provisional Answer Key”.
  • Select the question ID you want to challenge and choose the option you believe is correct.
  • Upload supporting documents or references (such as textbook pages or official material) in the specified format.
  • Pay Rs 500 per question through the online payment gateway.
  • Submit the objection and note the reference number for future tracking.
2paragraphOnce the expert panel reviews all objections, COMEDK will finalize the answer key and publish the final version on the notified date.
