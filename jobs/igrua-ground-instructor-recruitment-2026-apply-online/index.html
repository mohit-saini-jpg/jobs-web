IGRUA Ground Instructor Recruitment 2026 - Key Responsibilities

Deliver theoretical training on Air Regulations, Rules of the Air, Licensing Requirements, Flight Rules, and ICAO Annexes. Prepare lesson plans, training materials, and question banks as per DGCA CARs. Conduct internal assessments and assist in DGCA exam preparation. Maintain training records, student performance reports, and compliance documentation. Update course content with latest DGCA/ICAO regulatory changes. Coordinate with Chief Ground Instructor and Flight Instructors for integrated training. Ensure adherence to IGRUA standards of training, discipline, and safety.

Peaceful work life in one of India’s most serene aviation campuses. Healthy, safe, and family-friendly environment with low cost of living in Amethi. Quarters/Accommodation provided subject to availability. Opportunity to contribute to India’s leading pilot training institute. Ideal role for Ex-Servicemen seeking disciplined yet balanced lifestyle.
