Osmania University PG Diploma Time Table 2026 - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 20/07/2026

The Controller of Examinations, Osmania University, Hyderabad, has released the official time table for Advanced P.G. Diploma Courses for the July/August 2026 examination session. The notification (No. 265/PGCDE/Exams/2026, dated 17-07-2026) covers both Semester I and Semester II examinations under the Regular & Backlog category. Exams are conducted for five diploma programs: Mathematics, English Language Teaching (ELT), Business Management, Bioinformatics, and Vedic Astrology (including the Advanced PG Diploma in Vedic Astrology). All theory exams are scheduled from 2:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m., with separate practical exam dates for Bioinformatics and Vedic Astrology.
