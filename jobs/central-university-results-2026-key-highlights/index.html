Steps to Check CUHP Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download the CUHP 1st, 3rd and 5th Semester Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official result page of the university - https://www.cuh.ac.in/Result.aspx.
  • Look for the latest result notification dated 07-04-2026 for your course and semester.
  • Click on the relevant result entry such as MA Sociology NEP 1st Sem, MSc Chemistry 3rd Sem, or Integrated B.Sc.-M.Sc. Chemistry 5th Sem.
  • If the result is published as a downloadable PDF, open it and search your roll number or name.
  • If the university redirects result access through the Samarth portal, open https://cuh.samarth.edu.in/ and log in using your student credentials.
  • Download or save the marksheet/result page for future reference.
  • Verify all details carefully and contact the examination branch in case of any discrepancy.
