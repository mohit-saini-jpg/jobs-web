How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online through the HIMS Portal (https://hims.odisha.gov.in/) which will be available from 07.05.2026 onwards. Candidates must complete the application in all respects and upload all necessary supporting documents on or before 10.06.2026. The online application can be filled in multiple sessions. No other format of application or CV should be sent by email or any other means. In addition, a hard copy of the downloaded submitted application along with all relevant documents must be sent only via Speed Post to reach the University on or before 20.06.2026. Courier/ordinary post/hand delivery will not be accepted.
  • Persons employed in Government/Public Sector Enterprise/Autonomous bodies should forward a copy of their application through proper channel or furnish a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the competent authority or employer. In-service candidates should submit the NOC at the time of interview.
  • Hard copy to be sent via Speed Post to: The Registrar, Rama Devi Women’s University, Vidya Vihar, P.O.-Bhoi Nagar, Bhubaneswar – 751022, District-Khorda, (Odisha)

  • Apply Online at: https://hims.odisha.gov.in/

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How many vacancies are there in RDWU Faculty Recruitment 2026?

Rama Devi Women’s University has notified 80 regular faculty vacancies including 11 Professor, 23 Associate Professor and 46 Assistant Professor posts across various departments, along with backlog vacancies.

Q2. Which departments have vacancies in RDWU Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor posts?

RDWU faculty vacancies are available in departments such as Botany, Business Administration, Chemistry, Commerce, Computer Science, Economics, Education, English, Gender Studies, Hindi, History, Home Science, Journalism & Mass Communication, Life Science, Mathematics, Music, Odia, Philosophy, Physics, Political Science, Psychology, Sanskrit, Sociology, Statistics and Zoology.

Q3. What are the application dates for RDWU Faculty Recruitment 2026?

The online application portal on HIMS opens on 07 May 2026 and the last date to apply online is 10 June 2026 (midnight), while the hard copy of the application must reach the University by 20 June 2026 via Speed Post.

Q4. What are the pay scales for RDWU Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor?

As per the 7th CPC, the pay scales are Pay Level‑14 for Professor, Pay Level‑13A for Associate Professor, and Pay Level‑10 for Assistant Professor, along with other admissible benefits as per Odisha government and university rules.

Q5. What is the age limit for RDWU Faculty Recruitment 2026?

Candidates above 60 years of age are not eligible to apply for any of the Professor, Associate Professor or Assistant Professor posts at Rama Devi Women’s University.

Q6. What is the eligibility criteria for RDWU Professor posts as per UGC norms?

Professor applicants must be eminent scholars with a Ph.D. in the concerned/allied/relevant discipline, at least 10 years of teaching/research experience, minimum 10 research publications in peer‑reviewed or UGC‑listed journals, and a research score of 120 as per UGC Regulations 2018, or be outstanding professionals with equivalent research contributions.

Q7. What is the eligibility criteria for RDWU Associate Professor posts?

Associate Professor candidates need a Ph.D. in the concerned/allied/relevant discipline, a Master’s degree with at least 55% marks (50% for reserved categories), and minimum 8 years of teaching/research experience equivalent to Assistant Professor, with at least 7 publications and a research score of 75 as per UGC Regulations 2018.

Q8. What is the eligibility criteria for RDWU Assistant Professor posts?

Assistant Professor applicants must have a Master’s degree with 55% marks (50% for SC/ST/OBC‑NCL/PwD) in the relevant subject and must have cleared UGC/CSIR NET or SET/SLET, or possess a Ph.D. in accordance with UGC Ph.D. Regulations which can exempt NET as per the latest UGC guidelines.
