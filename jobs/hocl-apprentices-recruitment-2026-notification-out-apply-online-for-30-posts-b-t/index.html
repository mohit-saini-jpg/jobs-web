How to Apply

  1. 1Candidates must apply exclusively through the respective portal based on their qualification category. No applications through any other method or platform will be considered.
  2. 2For Trade Apprentices (ITI Holders) - Apply via NAPS Portal (apprenticeshipindia.gov.in):
  3. 3For candidates already enrolled on NAPS portal: Login with User ID & Password → Click Apprenticeship Opportunities → Choose Course, Location → Type Establishment Name "Hindustan Organic Chemicals Limited" → Search (Establishment Code: E07203200009) → List of trades will be displayed → Click Apply against the respective trade.
  4. 4For candidates not yet enrolled on NAPS portal: Go to https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in/ → Click Enroll under "Candidate" → Complete Registration (Qualification Certificate, Govt ID Proof, Bank Passbook, Photo, Resume required) → Complete Aadhaar verification → A unique Enrollment Number will be generated after MSDE verification (wait at least 1 day) → Then follow the steps above to apply.
  5. 5For Graduate & Technician Apprentices (B.Tech/Diploma) - Apply via NATS Portal (nats.education.gov.in):
  6. 6For candidates already registered on NATS portal: Login with User ID & Password → Search "Hindustan Organic Chemicals Limited" under "Apply against advertised vacancies" or "Apply against yearly requirements" → Click Apply (status will show as "applied").
  7. 7For candidates not yet registered on NATS portal: Go to https://nats.education.gov.in → Click Student → Click Student Register → Complete application form (Qualification Certificate, Govt ID Proof, Bank Passbook, Photo, Resume required) → A unique Enrollment Number will be generated → Login and apply as above.
  8. 8Documents Required for Portal Registration (Both Portals):
  9. 9Qualification Certificate (ITI/Diploma/B.Tech mark sheet and certificate)
  10. 10Government ID Proof
  11. 11Aadhaar card (mandatory for NAPS portal - Aadhaar verification required)
  12. 12Bank Passbook (for stipend disbursement)
  13. 13Recent passport-size photograph
  14. 14Resume / CV
  15. 15Documents to be Produced at Certificate Verification / Written Test / Skill Test / Interview:
  16. 16Original certificates and mark sheets of qualifying examination (ITI/Diploma/B.Tech) - plus self-attested copies
  17. 17Date of Birth proof (original + self-attested copy)
  18. 18Caste certificate in prescribed Government of India format for SC/ST/OBC candidates (only GOI prescribed formats accepted)
  19. 19OBC Non-Creamy Layer declaration (Performa-III) in addition to OBC certificate
  20. 20EWS Income & Asset Certificate (Annexure-I format) for EWS candidates
  21. 21Disability Certificate (Form-V for single disability / Form-VI for multiple disabilities, UDID/Certificate No.) for PwBD candidates
  22. 22Equivalency Certificate from competent authority (if qualification is equivalent to prescribed qualification)
  23. 23CGPA to percentage conversion proof from University/Institute (if applicable)
  24. 24Any other document relevant to the category/reservation claimed
  25. 25For NAPS portal queries: Contact RI Centres / RDSDE Trivandrum or check Candidate User Manual from the "Get Started Menu" on the portal homepage.

Important Instructions

Upon completion of the Apprenticeship period, HOCL shall have no obligation to offer employment to any apprentice, nor can an apprentice claim the right for employment on the grounds of completion of Apprenticeship. Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing Apprenticeship Training under the Apprentices Act in any Government, Public Sector, or Private Organisation are NOT ELIGIBLE to apply. Such candidature, if discovered at any stage, will be cancelled. Applications must be submitted exclusively through the respective portal - NAPS (for ITI holders) or NATS (for Diploma/B.Tech holders). Applications through any other method or platform will not be considered. The category/reservation claimed during the initial online submission of the application only will be considered. Candidates must produce the relevant certificate in prescribed GOI format at document verification; failing which, their reservation claim will not be considered and they will be treated as Unreserved. Candidates must ensure marks are entered correctly in the portal. Difference between entered marks and mark sheet uploaded will result in summary rejection of application. For NATS portal applicants: percentage of marks in qualifying examination must be compulsorily entered in the "% of Marks/CGPA" column. If not furnished, candidature will not be considered. Call letters for certificate verification/written test/skill test/interview will NOT be sent by post. Candidates will be informed by email or through publication on the HOCL website (www.hoclindia.com). Candidates must frequently check their email and the HOCL website for updates. No travel expenses are admissible during the apprenticeship training period or the selection process. Accommodation will be provided if available as per company policy; subsidized canteen facility is available during office hours only. If at any stage any information furnished is found to be false/incorrect or there has been suppression of facts, the candidate shall not be considered for selection and candidature/appointment will be cancelled without notice. Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification. Only Indian Nationals need to apply. The number of training seats indicated may vary depending on future requirements. HOCL reserves the right to restrict/alter/cancel/modify the recruitment process if need so arises. Any legal proceedings arising out of this advertisement can be instituted only in the Courts/Tribunals/Forums at Ernakulam, which shall have sole and exclusive jurisdiction. Any amendment, modification, or addition to this advertisement will be given on HOCL's website only - candidates should check www.hoclindia.com regularly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1Q1. What is HOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026 and how many seats are available?
A
HOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is a one-year apprenticeship engagement drive under the Apprentices Act, 1961 for Trade, Technician, and Graduate Apprentices at Hindustan Organic Chemicals Limited, Kochi. A total of 30 apprenticeship seats are notified, including 14 Trade Apprentices, 11 Technician Apprentices, and 5 Graduate Apprentices across various technical disciplines.
Q2Q2. What are the important dates for HOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
The apprenticeship notifications are dated 22 May 2026, and the online application window is open from 26 May 2026 to 08 June 2026. Candidates must complete portal registration (NAPS/NATS) and submit their application within this period; applications after the last date will not be accepted.
Q3Q3. Which categories of apprentices are being engaged under HOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
HOCL is engaging three categories of apprentices – Trade Apprentices (ITI holders), Technician Apprentices (Diploma holders), and Graduate Apprentices (B.Tech/B.E. degree holders) in relevant disciplines. Each category has specific trades/branches such as Fitter, Electrician, Mechanical, Electrical, Instrumentation, Fire & Safety, and Computer Science as advertised in the notification.
Q4Q4. What is the stipend for HOCL Trade, Technician, and Graduate Apprentices in 2026?
A
As per the 2026 apprenticeship notifications, Trade Apprentices (ITI) receive a monthly stipend of around Rs. 10,560, Technician Apprentices (Diploma) receive about Rs. 10,900, and Graduate Apprentices (B.Tech/B.E.) receive about Rs. 12,300 (including Government share). The stipend is paid for the entire one-year training period subject to satisfactory attendance and performance.
Q5Q5. What is the eligibility criteria for HOCL Trade, Technician, and Graduate Apprentices?
A
For Trade Apprentices, candidates must have a full-time ITI (NCVT/SCVT) in the relevant trade with the required minimum percentage and must have passed in the specified years. For Technician Apprentices and Graduate Apprentices, candidates must hold a Diploma or B.Tech/B.E. degree in relevant engineering disciplines with prescribed minimum marks and must have passed in the eligible passing years (recent batches only).
Q6Q6. What is the age limit for HOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must have completed 18 years of age as on the cut-off date mentioned in the notification. There is no specific upper age limit stated for these apprenticeships, but age relaxation for reserved categories is applicable as per Government of India rules wherever mentioned.
Q7Q7. How will candidates be selected for HOCL Apprentice posts in 2026?
A
Selection is based primarily on shortlisting by percentage of marks in the qualifying exam, followed by certificate verification and Written Test / Skill Test / Interview depending on the category. Final provisional selection is done in order of merit and reservation, subject to successful document verification and medical fitness as per Apprenticeship Rules.
Q8Q8. Through which portals should I apply for HOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply only through the designated apprenticeship portals – Trade Apprentices (ITI) must apply via NAPS portal (apprenticeshipindia.gov.in), while Technician and Graduate Apprentices (Diploma/B.Tech) must apply via NATS portal (nats.education.gov.in). Applications sent directly to HOCL or through any other means will not be accepted.
Q9Q9. Is there any application fee for HOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
No, there is no application fee for any category of candidates applying for HOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026. Registration and application through NAPS/NATS and participation in the selection process are completely free of cost.
Q10Q10. Will HOCL provide permanent jobs after completion of apprenticeship training?
A
No, HOCL has no obligation to offer permanent employment to apprentices after successful completion of the one-year training. Apprentices cannot legally claim any right to employment in HOCL solely on the basis of completing apprenticeship training.
