ISRO Recruitment 2026 Salary and Pay Scale

Selected candidates for Assistant, Junior Personal Assistant, Upper Division Clerk and Stenographer posts will be placed in Level 4 of the Pay Matrix , with a minimum basic pay of Rs. 25,500 per month . In addition to basic pay, the following allowances/benefits are admissible as per extant Central Government rules: Dearness Allowance (DA) House Rent Allowance (HRA) Transport Allowance New Pension Scheme Medical facilities for self and dependents, subsidised canteen, limited quarters facility (in lieu of HRA) Leave Travel Concession (LTC) Group Insurance House Building Advance (HBA)
