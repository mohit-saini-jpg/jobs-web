FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for ICAR-IARI YP-I recruitment?
A
The walk-in interview is scheduled for 10th June 2026. Candidates should ensure they reach the venue on this date for the interview and registration process.
Q2Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Young Professional – I post?
A
Candidates should be Graduates in relevant subjects or Diploma holders in relevant Agricultural Sciences/Engineering/Technology. Working knowledge of computers and MS Office, along with hands-on experience in seed pathology, is also desirable.
Q3Q3. What is the age limit for the Young Professional – I position?
A
The minimum age limit is 21 years, and the maximum age limit is 45 years. Age relaxation is applicable as per government guidelines for eligible categories.
Q4Q4. What is the salary offered for the Young Professional – I post?
A
The selected candidate will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 30,000/- per month.
Q5Q5. How many vacancies are available for the Young Professional – I post?
A

Q6Q6. What is the mode of application for this recruitment?
A

Q7Q7. Are there any application fees for this recruitment?

Q8Q8. What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?
