Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for Contractual Field Assistant (Land) posts?

The walk-in interview for the Contractual Field Assistant (Land) posts is scheduled for 28th May 2026. Candidates should report for registration between 07:00 A.M. to 09:00 A.M. on this date.

Q2. What is the minimum qualification required for the Contractual Field Assistant (Land) position?

Candidates must have passed the HSLC examination or equivalent from a government-recognized board, or possess a Qualified Recorder's Certificate Class Course (RCCC) from a government department.

Q3. How many years of experience are required for this role?

A minimum of 10 years of work experience in government, PSU, public, or private sector organizations engaged in land acquisition, land survey, or revenue matters is required.

Q4. What is the age limit for the Contractual Field Assistant (Land) posts?

The minimum age is 25 years, and the maximum age for General candidates is 40 years. Age relaxation is applicable for OBC-NCL (43 years) and ST (45 years).

Q5. What is the salary structure for the Contractual Field Assistant (Land) role?

The salary includes a fixed emolument of ₹ 21,450.00 per month and a variable emolument of ₹ 825.00 per day for each working day.

Q6. How many vacancies are available for the Contractual Field Assistant (Land) post?

There are a total of 5 vacancies for the Contractual Field Assistant (Land) position.

Q7. What is the selection process for these positions?

The selection process involves a walk-in for personal assessment, which includes evaluation of professional knowledge, skills, personal attributes, and soft skills. Final selection will be based on merit.

Q8. Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?

The walk-in interview will be conducted at the Oil India office in Duliajan, Assam. Candidates should report to the venue for registration.
