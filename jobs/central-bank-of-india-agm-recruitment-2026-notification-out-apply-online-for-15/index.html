Important Instructions

Only one application per candidate - multiple applications will result in forfeiture of fee paid for other registrations. Applications once submitted cannot be withdrawn and fee once paid is neither refunded nor held in reserve. Candidates must ensure eligibility before applying - the Bank reserves the right to reject applications at any stage. Minimum CIC score of 650 is mandatory with no credit default in the credit report. Indemnity bond of INR 3,00,000/- must be executed if selected (valid for 3 years of service). Probation period of 2 years from the date of joining. Initial posting will be at Mumbai and thereafter transferable anywhere in India as per bank's transfer policy. Government/PSU/Nationalized Bank employees must produce NOC from employer at the time of interview. OBC candidates in 'Creamy Layer' will be treated as 'General Category'. No Travelling Allowance is payable to candidates called for interview. However, unemployed SC/ST candidates will be paid 2nd Class Rail/Ordinary Bus fare on production of travel evidence. Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification. Any resulting dispute shall be subject to the sole jurisdiction of Courts situated at Mumbai. Candidates are advised to regularly visit the Bank's website www.centralbank.bank.in for updates under Career section. All decisions of the Bank regarding eligibility, selection, and other matters are final and binding on all candidates.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for Central Bank of India AGM Specialist Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for Central Bank of India AGM Specialist Recruitment 2026 is 07 June 2026, and fee payment can also be done up to this date.
Q2How many vacancies are released for CBI AGM Specialist 2026?
A
The bank has notified a total of 15 vacancies for Assistant General Manager (Scale V) in Specialist Category across Risk Management, Finance & Accounts and Credit streams.
Q3What is the age limit for Central Bank of India AGM Specialist posts?
A
The minimum age is 33 years and the maximum age is 45 years as on 31 March 2026, with relaxations for reserved categories as per Government of India rules.
Q4What is the selection process for CBI AGM Specialist Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of an online examination for 100 marks followed by an interview for 100 marks, and the final merit is prepared with 70 percent weightage to the online test and 30 percent to the interview.
Q5Is there any negative marking in the Central Bank of India AGM Specialist online exam?
A
No, there is no penalty for wrong answers in the online examination for AGM Specialist posts, so candidates can attempt all questions without fear of negative marking.
Q6What is the application fee for Central Bank of India AGM Specialist 2026?
A
SC, ST, PwBD and women candidates need to pay Rs. 175 as intimation charges, while all other candidates including General, OBC and EWS must pay Rs. 850 as application fee plus intimation charges.
Q7What qualifications are required for AGM – Finance & Accounts in Central Bank of India?
A
For AGM – Finance & Accounts, candidates must be full‑time graduates who have passed the final ICAI examination and are members of ICAI, with at least 10 years’ experience in a scheduled commercial bank and 7 years at head or administrative office in core finance functions.
Q8Is work experience mandatory for Central Bank of India AGM Specialist posts?
A
Yes, all AGM Specialist posts require a minimum of 10 years’ officer‑level experience in banking or specified domains, along with stipulated years in Risk, Finance or Credit in large scheduled commercial banks with more than 2000 branches.
