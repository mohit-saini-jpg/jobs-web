NTET 2026 Scorecards Released on DigiLocker: Download Direct Link & Steps

NTET 2026 scorecards are now available on DigiLocker; candidates can download their certificates securely by logging into their DigiLocker account using registered mobile number. The National Testing Agency (NTA) has made NTET 2026 score cards available on DigiLocker, offering candidates a secure way to access and store their examination records online.

Why NTET 2026 Scorecards on DigiLocker Matter

With scorecards now available on DigiLocker, candidates can access their documents digitally without depending solely on downloaded copies stored on personal devices.

The facility offers secure online access to examination records and can prove useful when candidates need to present their credentials during:

  • Recruitment processes
  • Admissions
  • Document verification
  • Other academic processes

This initiative ensures that NTET qualified candidates have a trustworthy digital copy of their scorecard.

How to Download NTET 2026 Scorecard from DigiLocker

Step-by-Step Guide (Website & App)

Candidates can follow these steps to access their NTET 2026 scorecards online:

  • Visit DigiLocker – Go to digilocker.gov.in or open the DigiLocker mobile application
  • Log In – Use your registered mobile number and credentials
  • New Users – Create an account by completing the registration process
  • Navigate to Issued Documents – After logging in, go to the 'Issued Documents' section
  • Search for NTET 2026 – Look for "NTET 2026 scorecard" in the issued documents
  • Download PDF – Click to download and save the scorecard for future reference

Download NTET 2026 Scorecard from NTA Portal

If you prefer the official NTA website, follow these steps:

  • Visit the official website: exams.nta.nic.in/NTET
  • Click on the "NTET 2026 Result" or "Scorecard" link on the homepage
  • Enter your application number and login credentials
  • Submit the details to view your result
  • Download and print the scorecard for future reference

Important Notices for NTET 2026 Candidates

  • The result of NTET 2026 has already been declared on May 15, 2026, and score cards were initially hosted on NTA's website
  • NTA is now also hosting NTET-2026 Score Cards on DigiLocker to facilitate secure digital access
  • Candidates are advised to visit the NTET website (exams.nta.nic.in/ntet) for further details
