How to Download Jammu University Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the simple step-by-step process given below to check Jammu University Result 2026 for B.Ed, M.Com, MA Political Science and UG 3rd and 6th Semester examinations.

  • Visit the official Controller of Examinations portal of Jammu University at https://www.coeju.com.
  • On the homepage, click on the 'Notices/Results' section or open the direct notices page at https://www.coeju.com/notices.aspx.
  • Scroll through the latest notifications and locate your relevant result, such as B.Ed 3rd Sem (2024-26) Feb 2026, M.Com 3rd Sem Feb 2026, UG 3rd Sem 2025, UG 6th Sem (various sessions) or MA Political Science 3rd Sem Feb 2026.
  • Click on the notification link to open the result PDF or scorecard page for the selected course and semester.
  • If the result is in PDF format, use the search option to find your roll number and check your marks and result status.
  • If redirected to an online result dashboard, enter your roll number/registration number and other required details, then submit to view your Jammu University Result 2026.
  • Download and save the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future reference until the university issues the original marksheet through your college.
