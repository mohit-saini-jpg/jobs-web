How to Check Jammu University Results 2026 Online?

Jammu University has made the result download process simple and quick for students. Candidates only need their registration number and date of birth to view results on the official portal. Here is a step-by-step process you can follow:

  • Visit the Official Website - Go to the official Jammu University exam portal at coeju.com.
  • Find the Results Section - Click on the Examination or Results tab on the homepage.
  • Select Your Exam - Choose Undergraduate Results from the list of available results.
  • Enter Your Details - Input your Registration Number and Date of Birth in the required fields.
  • Submit & View - Click the Submit button to view your Jammu University result 2026 on screen.
  • Download the Mark Sheet - Click Download or Print to save your result PDF for official use.
  • Verify Your Details - Check that your name, roll number, and marks are correctly reflected on the mark sheet.
