NIELIT Teaching Faculty Recruitment 2026 - Apply Offline for 68 Posts

National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (NIELIT)

Posts: Multiple (across various NIELIT Centers) Last Date: 01 June 2026 Offline

Short Information

The National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (NIELIT), an autonomous scientific society under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India, has released Detailed Advertisement No. NHQ-12/22/2025-NC [3160304] for the recruitment of Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, and Professor purely on contract basis. Applications are invited from eligible candidates for various centers across India. The selected candidates will be engaged initially for a period of one year, which may be extended based on performance and requirement.
