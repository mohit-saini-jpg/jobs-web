Telangana Public Service Commission (TGPSC) will recruit 49 Assistant Executive Engineer (Electrical) posts in Transport Roads and Buildings Department, which will be filled through TGPSC AEE Electrical Recruitment 2026. Application Fee

To apply online for TGPSC AEE Electrical 2026, candidates have to pay both the Application Processing Fee and Examination Fee through online payment gateways. The fee once remitted shall not be refunded or adjusted under any circumstances.

Qualification / Eligibility

Candidates applying for the post of Assistant Executive Engineer (Electrical) must fulfil the prescribed educational qualification and age criteria. All qualifications must be possessed as on the date of notification i.e. 02/06/2026. | Candidates applying for Assistant Executive Engineer (Electrical) in Roads & Buildings Department must possess a Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering OR Electrical and Electronics Engineering from a University in India established or incorporated under a Central Act or Telangana State Act or an Institution recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC)/All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) or Associate Member of the Institution of Engineers (A.M.I.E.). | The age is reckoned as on 01/07/2026. As per G.O.Ms.No.86, G.A.(Ser.A) Department, Dt. 18/05/2026, the upper age limit has been raised up to 10 years i.e., from 34 years to 44 years.

How to Apply

  • 1Here, we have provided the direct, simple and easy steps to apply online for TGPSC AEE Electrical Recruitment 2026. Every candidate can follow these steps one by one and submit the application form.
  • 2Visit the official TGPSC website at https://www.tgpsc.gov.in and complete the One Time Registration (OTR) to obtain your TGPSC ID (if not already registered).
  • 3Login with your TGPSC ID and Date of Birth and enter the OTP received on the registered mobile number.
  • 4Verify the details fetched from the OTR database (Name, Date of Birth, Educational Qualifications, Community, Gender, Disability, etc.).
  • 5Select the examination centre and fill in all required details in the online application form.
  • 6Upload a recent colour passport size photograph (not older than 3 months, 100-200kb) and scanned signature (20-30kb) in JPG/PNG format.
  • 7Preview the application carefully and click on SAVE & PAYMENT button to proceed with online fee payment through Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card or UPI.
  • 8After payment, click CONFIRM & SUBMIT. Download the PDF application form and note the Reference ID Number for future reference.

FAQs

Q1What is the pay scale for TGPSC AEE Electrical post?
A
The pay scale for Assistant Executive Engineer (Electrical) in Transport Roads and Buildings Department is Rs. 54,220 – 1,33,630/- as per the applicable pay matrix.
Q2Is there any negative marking in TGPSC AEE Electrical Exam 2026?
A
The notification does not specifically mention negative marking. Candidates are advised to check the official notification and Hall Ticket instructions for final confirmation on this aspect.
Q3What is One Time Registration (OTR) for TGPSC?
A
One Time Registration (OTR) is a process where candidates register their bio-data on the TGPSC website to obtain a TGPSC ID. It is mandatory for applying online but OTR itself is not considered as an application for any post. Candidates must separately submit the online application for this notification using their TGPSC ID.
Q4Are candidates from other states eligible to apply for TGPSC AEE Electrical Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, candidates from other states can apply. However, applicants belonging to other States are not entitled for any kind of reservation benefits, which are applicable only to Telangana State candidates.
Q5What are the exam centres for TGPSC AEE Electrical Examination 2026?
A
The examination will be held at Hyderabad (including HMDA Jurisdiction), Karimnagar, Khammam, Warangal and Nizamabad. The Commission reserves the right to alter examination centres for administrative reasons.
Q6What is the last date to apply online for TGPSC AEE Electrical Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for submission of online application for TGPSC AEE Electrical Recruitment 2026 is 15 July 2026 (upto 5:00 PM). Candidates are advised to submit well before the closing date and not wait till the last date.
