Bhupendra Narayan Mandal University (BNMU) has officially released the BNMU Result 2026 for various UG and PG semester examinations conducted in December 2025, including BA, BSc, BCom 1st Semester and multiple MA, MSc, MCom semesters. The latest updates reflect the publication of B.Sc and B.A 1st Semester results on 02 May 2026 along with several PG 1st and 3rd Semester results in different subjects like Botany, Chemistry, Economics, History, and more. BNMU Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - bnmu.ac.in.

Students can check and download their BNMU UG and PG results by visiting the university portal, selecting the relevant semester result link, and entering their examination roll number or other login details as required.

After downloading the online marksheet, students are advised to carefully verify their name, roll number, subject-wise marks, and overall status, and contact the university or their college immediately in case of any discrepancy.

How to Check BNMU Result 2026 Online

  • Visit the official website of Bhupendra Narayan Mandal University at bnmu.ac.in or directly open the UMIS result portal at bnmuumis.in.
  • On the homepage, look for the 'Result & Notification' or 'General Instruction and Notification' section and click on it to view the latest BNMU Result 2026 updates.
  • Under the list of notifications, find the relevant result link such as 'B.A Semester - I Examination December 2025', 'B.Sc Semester - I Examination December 2025', 'B.Com Semester - I', or respective PG semester results.
  • Click on the corresponding 'Download' or 'View Result' option to open the result PDF in a new window/tab.
  • Check your name, roll number, subject-wise marks, and overall result status in the PDF marksheet.
  • Download the BNMU marksheet PDF to your device and take a printout for future reference and admission or document verification.
