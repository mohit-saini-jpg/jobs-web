UP Anganwadi Eligibility Criteria 2026 – Who Can Apply

Only women candidates are eligible to apply for UP Anganwadi Karyakartri Bharti 2026. The minimum educational qualification differs between the Worker and Helper posts. Candidates must be a permanent resident of the same Gram Sabha / Ward in which the vacancy exists - applications from candidates of other Gram Sabhas or Wards will not be accepted. For the Anganwadi Karyakartri post, merit is prepared on the basis of marks obtained in Intermediate or equivalent qualification. For higher qualifications (graduation/post-graduation), marks are also factored into merit. The merit list is prepared using a grading and CGPA system - decimal points after three places are not rounded up. If two candidates secure equal marks, the one with higher age is given priority. UP Anganwadi Selection Process 2026 – Merit and Priority Order

There is no written examination for UP Anganwadi Sahayika and Karyakartri recruitment. Selection is entirely merit-based, using academic marks. However, within the same merit, a government-mandated priority order is applied. Understanding this priority order is essential because it determines who gets selected even among candidates with equal marks. Priority 1 (BPL): Widow / legally divorced / deserted woman from the same Gram Sabha / Ward who lives below the poverty line (BPL). If no such woman applies, then BPL woman from the same Gram Sabha who sustains the family. Priority 2 (APL): Widow / legally divorced / deserted woman from the same Gram Sabha / Ward who lives above the poverty line (APL). UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 and which districts are covered?
A
UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 refers to the notifications released by the District Programme Officers of Bijnour and Kanpur Nagar under the state government order S-2025/3313/58-1-2025(1917687). A combined total of 787 vacancies are available - 268 Karyakartri and 260 Sahayika in Bijnour, and 259 Karyakartri in Kanpur Nagar.
Q2What is the last date to apply for UP Anganwadi Bharti 2026?
A
The last date to apply online is 15 July 2026 at 12:00 midnight for Bijnour and 15 July 2026 for Kanpur Nagar. Applications must be submitted only through the official website http://upanganwadibharti.in — no offline applications are accepted.
Q3Who is eligible to apply for UP Anganwadi Karyakartri Vacancy 2026?
A
Only women candidates are eligible. For Anganwadi Karyakartri (Worker), the minimum qualification is Intermediate (10+2). For Anganwadi Sahayika (Helper), Class 5 pass is the minimum. The candidate must be a permanent resident of the specific Gram Sabha or Ward where the vacancy exists.
Q4What is the age limit for UP Anganwadi Sahayika Bharti 2026?
A
The age limit is a minimum of 18 years and a maximum of 35 years, calculated as on 01 January 2026. Age is verified from the High School certificate. Reserved category candidates receive age relaxation as per UP government norms.
Q5Is there a written examination for UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
No. There is no written examination. Selection is entirely merit-based, using marks obtained in the educational qualifications. Within equal merit, a government-mandated priority order (BPL widow → APL widow → general woman) is applied.
Q6What is the priority order for UP Anganwadi Karyakartri selection?
A
Priority is given first to BPL widows/legally divorced/deserted women of the same Gram Sabha, then to APL widows/divorced/deserted women, and finally to any other ordinary woman of the Gram Sabha based on overall merit. This priority is applicable within each Gram Sabha independently.
Q7Can women from other villages or wards apply for UP Anganwadi Vacancy 2026?
A
No. The applicant must be a permanent resident of the exact Gram Sabha or Ward where the vacancy exists. Applications from candidates belonging to other Gram Sabhas or Wards will not be considered — this is a strict eligibility condition.
Q8What income certificate is valid for BPL status in UP Anganwadi Bharti 2026?
A
For rural areas, the annual income must be up to Rs. 46,080 and for urban areas up to Rs. 56,460 to qualify as BPL. The income certificate must be issued by the Revenue Department of Uttar Pradesh. Income certificates issued by individuals are not accepted.
Q9What documents does a widow need to submit for priority status in UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
A widow must submit a death certificate of the husband issued by the Gram Panchayat Officer or the relevant urban local body (Nagar Palika/Nagar Nigam), along with a certified copy of the family register. Court orders are required for divorcees or deserted women.
Q10Where can I apply online for UP Anganwadi Karyakartri Sahayika Bharti 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted online only at http://upanganwadibharti.in. This is the only official platform for UP Anganwadi recruitment. No other website, paper form, or postal submission is valid.
