How to Check JNVU BA 3rd Semester Result 2026 Online

This section explains exactly how a BA 3rd semester student can view and download their NEP 2020 result from the official JNVU result portal. Follow the steps below in the same order to avoid any errors while fetching your scorecard.

  • Open the official JNVU result portal at results.jnvuiums.in in your browser.
  • On the homepage, look for the "Examination Results" section listing results by academic session.
  • Select Odd Semester Examination Results (since the 3rd semester is an odd semester) for the relevant session.
  • From the list of declared results, click on "B.A. (Sem.)(NEP2020) IIIrd Sem Examination Result."
  • Enter your Roll Number in the box provided.
  • Click on "Get Result" or the equivalent submit button.
  • Your JNVU BA 3rd Semester scorecard will appear on the screen, showing subject-wise marks, SGPA, and grade.
  • Download or take a print of the result/scorecard and keep it safe for future reference, such as admission to the next semester.

Alternative Route via the Official University Website

If the direct result portal is slow due to heavy traffic, students can also reach the same result through the main university website. This route is useful as a backup when the result server is overloaded right after declaration.

  • Visit the official website, jnvu.co.in.
  • Click on the "Student's Corner" option on the homepage menu.
  • Select the "Result" link from the dropdown.
  • You will be redirected to the result portal (results.jnvuiums.in) in a new tab.
  • Locate the B.A. (Sem.)(NEP2020) IIIrd Sem Examination Result link and proceed as described above.

Details Required to Check the JNVU BA Result

Knowing what information you need beforehand makes the result-checking process faster and avoids repeated login attempts. Below are the essential details required.

  • Roll Number - the unique number allotted at the time of exam form submission, also printed on the admit card.
  • A stable internet connection, since the result portal can experience heavy traffic right after declaration.
  • A device (computer or smartphone) with a PDF viewer or printer access if you wish to save or print the scorecard.

What If You Have Forgotten Your Roll Number?

Many students misplace their roll number before result day. If this happens to you, there are still simple ways to retrieve it before checking your result.

  • Check your JNVU Admit Card, where the roll number is clearly printed.
  • Contact your respective college/department examination cell, which maintains roll number records for all enrolled students.
  • Refer to your previous semester marksheet, since the roll number generally remains associated with your enrollment record.

JNVU NEP 2020 Grading System and Minimum Passing Criteria

Understanding the grading pattern is essential to correctly interpret your BA 3rd semester scorecard. JNVU follows the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 framework for grading, which is based on SGPA and CGPA rather than only percentage marks.

  • Students must secure a minimum grade of "P" (Pass) in each subject to clear the semester.
  • The minimum SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) required in every semester is 4.0.
  • At the end of the final semester, the CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) must also be 4.0 or above to successfully complete the degree.
  • The result/scorecard displays subject-wise marks along with the overall SGPA for the semester.

What Information Appears on the JNVU BA 3rd Semester Marksheet

Once your result is generated online, it is useful to know what details are mentioned on it so you can verify everything is correct. The marksheet typically includes the following information.

  • Student's name, roll number, and enrollment details
  • College/institution name affiliated with JNVU
  • Subject-wise marks/credits for the 3rd semester papers
  • SGPA for the semester and grade obtained
  • Overall result status (Pass/Promoted to next semester, or reappear, if applicable)

Revaluation and Rechecking of JNVU BA Result

Students who are not satisfied with their marks in a particular subject can apply for revaluation or rechecking through the university's prescribed process. JNVU declares revaluation results separately, after the regular result, on the same official portal.

  • Revaluation applications must be submitted within the time frame announced by the university after the regular result declaration.
  • Revaluation results, once processed, are also published on results.jnvuiums.in under a separate link.
  • Students should keep their original scorecard and fee receipt safe while applying for rechecking.

Other JNVU Semester Results Declared Around the Same Period

JNVU releases results for multiple undergraduate and postgraduate courses around the same time each session. Knowing this helps students of related courses track their own results too.

  • B.A., B.Sc., and B.Com semester results under NEP 2020
  • BBA and BCA semester results
  • M.A., M.Sc., and M.Com semester results
  • MBA and other professional course results

All of these are published on the same official result portal, results.jnvuiums.in, following a similar step-by-step checking process.
