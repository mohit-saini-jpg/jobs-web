Job Overview

The Maharashtra State Electricity Transmission Company Limited (MSETCL) has officially announced the Chief Engineer (Transmission) & Superintending Engineer (Transmission) Recruitment 2026 with 4 openings. The detailed notification was published at the official website, mahatransco.in. Candidates who meet the age criteria of 50 years for Open Category, 55 years for Reserved Category, and 57 years for Departmental candidates and hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Electrical Engineering / Technology can now submit their offline applications up to 12 June 2026. Qualification / Eligibility

Chief Engineer (Transmission): Bachelors Degree in Electrical Engineering / Technology with total 15 years experience in Power Sector. Out of which at least 5 years in the area of Power Transmission in Selection Posts like Executive Engineer & above out of which 1 year in the position of Superintending Engineer. OR 3 years in the position of Superintending Engineer. Superintending Engineer (Transmission): Bachelors Degree in Electrical Engineering / Technology with total 12 years experience in Power Sector. Out of which at least 5 years in the area of Power Transmission as Executive Engineer & Additional Executive Engineer OR 2 years as Executive Engineer.
