BEL Educational Institutions BEEI Teacher Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

BEL Educational Institutions (BEEI), Jalahalli Post, Bengaluru, has invited applications for 34 Teacher posts across 13 subject categories for the Academic Year 2026-2027, under Advertisement No. BEEI/TS/2026-2027/01 dated 17 June 2026. BEL Educational Institutions BEEI Teacher Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

This section explains the stages of selection - from shortlisting through the written test to the final interview. Candidates are first shortlisted for the Written Test based on the information declared in their application form. Written Test (70% weight): The test carries 50 marks for the respective subject, 10 marks for general knowledge, and 10 marks for aptitude, to be completed in 90 minutes . Interview (30% weight): Conducted only for candidates who qualify in the Written Test. Candidates who pass the Written Test are called for interview based on a 1:5 ratio . The venue for the Written Test and Interview will be intimated only through email . Names of candidates shortlisted for the Written Test, Interview, and the final selection list will be notified on the official BEEI website .
