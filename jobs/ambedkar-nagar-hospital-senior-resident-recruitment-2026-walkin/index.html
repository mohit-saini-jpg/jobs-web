Ambedkar Nagar Hospital Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Essential Qualifications: MBBS with PG Degree/Diploma or equivalent qualification in the concerned specialty recognized by NMC/MCI. For non-PG candidates: At least two years’ experience in the concerned specialty (if PG candidates not available). Valid registration with Delhi Medical Council (DMC). Should not have completed three years of Senior Residency. Age Limit: Maximum 45 years as on the date of interview.

Ambedkar Nagar Hospital Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

Walk-in Interview Registration: 09:30 AM to 11:00 AM (sharp) on the day of interview Venue: O/o Head of Office, 4th Floor, Ambedkar Nagar Hospital, Block-B, Sector-5, New Delhi-110062 Candidates must bring all original documents and self-attested copies.

Important Instructions for Ambedkar Nagar Hospital Recruitment 2026

Appointment is purely on ad-hoc basis for 89 days (extendable). No claim for regular appointment. Private practice is strictly prohibited. Candidates already working elsewhere must submit relieving order. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the interview.
