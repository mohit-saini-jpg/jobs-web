Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is RRB Technician Recruitment 2026 (CEN 02/2026)?

RRB Technician Recruitment 2026 is an upcoming centralised recruitment drive by Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) to fill about 6,565 Technician Grade I Signal and Technician Grade III posts for the 2026–27 cycle through a common online application.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in RRB Technician 2026?

As per the approved intent, a total of 6,565 Technician vacancies are proposed, including 323 posts for Technician Grade I Signal and 6,242 posts for various Technician Grade III categories across all RRBs.

Q3. What are the important dates for RRB Technician Recruitment 2026?

The indicative schedule mentions that online applications will be accepted from 30-06-2026 to 29-07-2026 (up to 23:59 hrs), while the indicative notice date is 23-05-2026 and CBT dates are aligned with the revised schedule for technician examinations in March 2026.

Q4. Which posts are covered under RRB Technician CEN 02/2026?

The notification covers Technician Grade I Signal (Level 5) and various Technician Grade III posts (Level 2) across departments such as Electrical, Mechanical, Engineering, and Signal & Telecommunication in Indian Railways.

Q5. What is the age limit for RRB Technician 2026?

For Technician Grade I Signal, the age limit is expected to be 18 to 36 years, and for Technician Grade III it is 18 to 33 years, with relaxations for SC, ST, OBC-NCL, EWS, PwBD, and Ex-Servicemen as per Government of India rules in the detailed CEN.

Q6. What are the educational qualifications for Technician Grade I Signal?

Candidates for Technician Grade I Signal should have either a B.Sc. degree in Physics, Electronics, Computer Science, IT, or Instrumentation, or a Degree/Diploma in Engineering in the relevant discipline from a recognized institution, as per CEN 02/2026.

Q7. What are the educational qualifications for Technician Grade III?

Technician Grade III requires 10th/Matriculation plus ITI in a relevant trade from NCVT/SCVT or completion of an Act Apprenticeship in the concerned trade, from a recognized institute.

Q8. What is the selection process for RRB Technician Recruitment 2026?

The selection process is expected to include a single-stage Computer Based Test (CBT) followed by Document Verification and Medical Examination, and there is no separate Computer Based Aptitude Test for these Technician posts.
