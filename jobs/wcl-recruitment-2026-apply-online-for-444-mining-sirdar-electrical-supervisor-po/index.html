WCL Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

Selection is through a Computer Based Test (CBT), followed by document scrutiny/verification and a medical examination. The CBT consists of 100 questions: Part 1 – General Aptitude (20 questions); Part 2 – Technical (80 questions). Each question carries 1 mark. Duration: 90 minutes. Questions are multiple-choice with four options. There is no negative marking; no marks are awarded for unattempted questions. The question paper is bilingual (English and Hindi); in case of any discrepancy, the English version is valid and final. Candidates are shortlisted for document verification based on CBT marks; the zone of consideration is 3 times the number of vacancies in each category. In case of a tie in CBT marks, it is resolved first by Date of Birth (elder candidate ranked higher), then by alphabetic order of first name as per the Matriculation certificate. WCL Recruitment 2026: Reservation Policy

Reservation for SC/ST/OBC (NCL)/EWS/PwBD/ESM follows applicable Government of India Acts, rules, and guidelines in force at the time of finalization of results. SC/ST/OBC(NCL) candidates selected purely on merit (without relaxed standards) are adjusted against Unreserved vacancies, not reserved ones; those selected using relaxed standards are counted against reserved vacancies. ESM/PwBD reservation is horizontal: General category ESM/PwBD are adjusted against UR, OBC-NCL category ESM/PwBD against OBC-NCL vacancies, and SC/ST category ESM/PwBD against SC/ST vacancies.
