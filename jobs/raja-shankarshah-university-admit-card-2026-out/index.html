How to Download Raja Shankarshah University Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official admit card portal.
  • Open the Admit Card section.
  • Enter your enrollment number / user ID and password or date of birth.
  • Submit the details.
  • Your admit card will appear on the screen.
  • Download it and take a clear printout.

If the page is slow, try again after some time.

Details Mentioned on the Admit Card

The admit card usually includes:

  • Student’s name.
  • Enrollment number.
  • Course and semester/year.
  • Exam date and time.
  • Exam centre name and address.
  • Photograph and signature.
  • Important exam instructions.

Check all details carefully. If there is any error, contact the university immediately.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the Raja Shankarshah University Admit Card 2026.
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card, Driving Licence or Passport.
  • Recent passport-size photographs, if required.

Without the admit card and valid ID, entry may not be allowed.

Exam-Day Instructions

  • Reach the exam centre before reporting time.
  • Carry only allowed items.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators or electronic gadgets.
  • Follow all instructions given by invigilators.
  • Keep the admit card safe after the exam.
