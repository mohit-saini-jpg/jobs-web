Notification DatePost Date: 15/04/2026

Central University of Haryana (CUH), Mahendergarh has officially released the Central University of Haryana Result 2026 for regular term end examinations of Master of Arts (Hindi) 1st and 2nd Semester and Master of Commerce 1st and 2nd Semester. These PG semester results for the latest academic session have been published online, allowing MA Hindi and M.Com students to check their course-wise performance for the recent TEE.

Central University of Haryana Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - cuh.ac.in through the CUH result portal at cuh.ac.in/Result.aspx and the Samarth portal cuhcdoe.samarth.edu.in. Students can log in using their enrollment or registration number and date of birth, select the relevant programme (MA Hindi or M.Com) and semester, and download their individual marksheet. After checking their marks, candidates should carefully verify their name, enrollment number, course, semester and subject-wise grades, and contact the examination branch in case of any discrepancy in the result.

How to Download Central University of Haryana Result 2026

Students can follow the steps below to check Central University of Haryana Result 2026 for MA Hindi and M.Com 1st and 2nd Semester online.

  • Visit the official CUH website cuh.ac.in and click on the "Results" link, or directly open https://www.cuh.ac.in/Result.aspx.
  • On the Results page, locate the notification for "Regular Result Term End Examinations - Master of Arts (Hindi) 1st/2nd Semester, Master of Commerce 1st/2nd Semester".
  • Click on the relevant result link or on the Samarth portal link (cuhcdoe.samarth.edu.in) if provided.
  • On the result login page, select your Programme (MA Hindi or M.Com) and Semester (1st or 2nd) if asked.
  • Enter your Enrollment/Registration Number and Date of Birth correctly in the given fields.
  • Click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button to display your Central University of Haryana Result 2026 on the screen.
  • Check your name, enrollment number, course, semester and subject-wise grades/marks carefully, and then download or print the provisional marksheet for future reference.
