Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for THSTI recruitment?

The walk-in interviews are scheduled for 10th June 2026 for Clinical Project Manager and Quality Manager, and 11th June 2026 for Clinical Research Associate and Sr. Project Associate.

Q2. What is the total number of vacancies announced by THSTI?

THSTI has announced a total of 4 vacancies for Project Positions.

Q3. What are the minimum qualifications required for the Sr. Project Associate post?

For Sr. Project Associate, a postgraduate degree in Life Sciences with minimum 2 years of experience or a professional degree with 1 year of experience or PhD in Life Sciences is required.

Q4. Is there any application fee for THSTI recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.

Q5. What is the age limit for the Project Positions at THSTI?

The age limit for the Project Positions is up to 45 years, with relaxations applicable for reserved categories.

Q6. What is the salary offered for the Clinical Project Manager position?

The Clinical Project Manager position offers a salary of Rs. 78,000/- per month plus HRA.

Q7. What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?

Candidates should carry all original documents, self-attested photocopies, ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates, and category certificates (if applicable).

The walk-in interview venue is Translational Health Science and Technology Institute (THSTI), Faridabad, Haryana, 121001.
