How to Check the NEHU Results 2025?

NEHU (North Eastern Hill University) Results 2025, visit the official NEHU examination results portal, select your course/semester, and enter your roll number to view and download your marksheet online.​

  • Go to the official NEHU results portal: exams.nehu.ac.in.​
  • Find the “Results” section; click on it to view the complete list of UG, PG, and professional course results.​
  • Select your specific program and semester (such as BA, BSc, BCom, MA, LLB, MTech, etc.) from the list of available exam results.​
  • Enter your Roll Number, Registration Number, or other exam credentials as requested.​
  • Click “Submit” or “View Result”; your individual marksheet will be displayed on the screen.​
  • Download or print your marksheet for future reference.​
