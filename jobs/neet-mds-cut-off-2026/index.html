NEET MDS Cut Off 2026

NEET MDS Cut Off 2026 has been officially released by NBEMS on 2nd June 2026 along with the result. The qualifying cutoff for the General/EWS category is 308 marks, for SC/ST/OBC is 271 marks, and for General PwD is 289 marks. Candidates scoring equal to or above these marks are eligible for NEET MDS 2026 counselling. Check complete category-wise cutoff, percentile, and previous year trends here.
