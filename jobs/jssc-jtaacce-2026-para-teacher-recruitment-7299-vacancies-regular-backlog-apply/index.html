JSSC JTAACCE-2026 Selection Process

Selection for both the regular and backlog vacancies will be based on a single-stage written examination (Main Exam) , conducted in OMR/Computer Based Test (CBT) mode. If any subject's exam is conducted in multiple groups/shifts, scores will be normalised as per the formula published on the JSSC website, and the merit list will be prepared based on normalised marks. All questions will be objective, multiple-choice type, and there is no negative marking for wrong answers. Total: 300 marks counted for merit + 100 qualifying marks. Paper 1 marks are qualifying only and not added to the merit list. Total: 380 marks counted for merit + 100 qualifying marks.

JSSC JTAACCE-2026 Para Teacher Recruitment: 7299 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1How many total vacancies are there in JSSC JTAACCE-2026?
A
There are 7299 total vacancies combining both notifications - 6101 regular vacancies under Advt No. 04/2026 and 1198 backlog vacancies under Advt No. 05/2026.
Q2What is the difference between Advt No. 04/2026 and Advt No. 05/2026?
A
Advt No. 04/2026 covers 6101 regular vacancies open to all eligible candidates. Advt No. 05/2026 covers 1198 backlog vacancies reserved for candidates already serving as Para Teachers under the Jharkhand Shiksha Pariyojna Parishad, Ranchi, with a minimum of two continuous years of service. Candidates eligible for both can apply using one form and pay only one fee, and a common exam will be held for both.
Q3What is the last date to apply for JSSC Para Teacher Recruitment 2026?
A
The online registration and application form filling window closes on 30 August 2026 (midnight) for both notifications.
Q4What is the application fee for JSSC JTAACCE-2026?
A
₹100 for general/other candidates and ₹50 for SC/ST candidates of Jharkhand state. Only one fee is payable even if applying for both the regular and backlog vacancy notifications together.
Q5Is JTET mandatory for JSSC Para Teacher recruitment?
A
Yes, candidates must have passed JTET for the relevant class level (1–5 or 6–8). Candidates who cleared CTET or a neighbouring state's TET can also apply but must clear JTET within 3 years of appointment.
Q6What is the salary for JSSC Para Teacher posts?
A
Level-4 pay scale of ₹25,500–₹81,100 for Class 1–5 posts, and Level-5 pay scale of ₹29,200–₹92,300 for Class 6–8 posts, applicable to both regular and backlog vacancies.
Q7Is there negative marking in the JSSC Para Teacher exam?
A
No, the notification specifies there is no deduction of marks for wrong answers.
Q8Who can apply for the JSSC Para Teacher backlog vacancy (Advt No. 05/2026)?
A
Only candidates currently serving as Para Teacher (Sahayak Adhyapak) under the Jharkhand Shiksha Pariyojna Parishad, Ranchi, with a minimum of two continuous years of service as on the date of advertisement publication, are eligible for the backlog vacancy posts.
Q9What is the selection process for JSSC JTAACCE-2026?
A
Selection for both regular and backlog vacancies is based on a single-stage Main Examination conducted in OMR/CBT mode, followed by document verification of shortlisted candidates.
