How To Apply

  • BDL Project Engineer 2026 is a Walk-in Interview - there is no online application form. Candidates must appear directly at the venue with all required documents. Follow these steps:

  • Download the official BDL Recruitment 2026 notification PDF from bdl-india.in → HR section.

  • Download and print the Bio-data Form (Annexure-I) attached to the notification.

  • Fill the Bio-data Form in capital letters, affix a recent passport-size colour photograph, and self-attest all required documents.

  • Calculate your age as on 07.05.2026 and verify that you meet the upper age limit for your category.

  • Arrange one set of self-attested photocopies of all required documents (originals must also be carried for verification).

  • Reach The Aeronautical Society of India (AeSI), Suranjan Das Road, New Tippasandra, Bengaluru - 560075 (near Vivekananda Metro Station) by 09:00 AM on the interview date.

  • Submit your Bio-data Form and documents at the registration desk. Documents will be scrutinised to verify eligibility.

  • Eligible candidates will be called for the interview on the same day or a later day (candidates may need to stay more than one day at their own expense).

  • No TA or DA will be paid. Mobile phones and electronic devices are not permitted inside the venue.

  • Queries: Email [email protected] with subject line: Query-BDL/Walk-in/2026-1

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for BDL Project Engineer 2026?

BDL Project Engineer 2026 is a walk-in interview — there is no online application form or last date. Candidates must appear directly at The Aeronautical Society of India (AeSI), Bengaluru on 6th or 7th June 2026 by 09:00 AM. Entry is strictly not allowed after 02:00 PM.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in BDL Recruitment 2026?

BDL has announced a total of 20 vacancies for Project Engineer — 10 for Electronics, 5 for Mechanical, and 5 for Computer Science discipline.

Q3. What is the age limit for BDL Project Engineer 2026?

The upper age limit for BDL Project Engineer 2026 is 28 years for UR/EWS, 31 years for OBC (NCL), and 33 years for SC/ST candidates, all reckoned as on 07.05.2026. PwBD candidates get an additional 10-year relaxation over their category limit.

Q4. What is the salary for BDL Project Engineer 2026?

BDL Project Engineer salary is ₹30,000/- per month in the 1st year, increasing to ₹33,000/- (2nd year), ₹36,000/- (3rd year), and ₹39,000/- (4th year). An additional ₹10,000/- per year is paid towards medical, attire, and footwear expenses.

Q5. What is the qualification required for BDL Project Engineer 2026?

Candidates must hold a First Class (60% aggregate) BE / B.Tech / B.Sc Engg (4 years) / Integrated M.E./M.Tech in the relevant discipline (Electronics / Mechanical / Computer Science) from an AICTE-approved institution. SC/ST candidates need 55% for reserved posts.

Q6. Is there any application fee for BDL Recruitment 2026?

No, there is no application fee for BDL Project Engineer Walk-in Interview 2026. However, no TA or DA is paid to candidates for attending the interview.

Q7. Where is the BDL Walk-in Interview 2026 venue?

The BDL Walk-in Interview 2026 will be held at The Aeronautical Society of India (AeSI), Suranjan Das Road, Opposite Engine Division, Binna Mangala, New Tippasandra, Bengaluru – 560075. The nearest metro station is Vivekananda Metro Station.

Q8. Is BDL Project Engineer 2026 a permanent government job?

No. BDL Project Engineer 2026 is a temporary/contract appointment initially for 1 year, extendable up to 4 years based on organisational need and individual performance. Candidates cannot claim regular employment at BDL through this appointment.
