BCKV UG Admission 2026

Bidhan Chandra Krishi Viswavidyalaya (BCKV) has released the official notification for admission to B.Sc. (Hons.) Agriculture and B.Sc. (Hons.) Horticulture courses for the academic session 2026-27. Applications are invited from eligible candidates who have passed Higher Secondary (10+2) in 2025 or 2026. The entire application process is online through the university portal www.bckv.edu.in. This is a golden opportunity for students seeking admission in top agricultural and horticultural programs in West Bengal.

The notification (U.G. Notification No. 1/2026) provides complete details on eligibility, seat matrix, reservation, and important dates. Candidates can apply for both Agriculture (at Mohanpur, Burdwan, and Bankura campuses) and Horticulture (Mohanpur only) through a single application form.

Courses Offered under BCKV UG Admission 2026

BCKV offers two major undergraduate programmes:

  • B.Sc. (Hons.) Agriculture (4 years / 8 semesters) – Available at Mohanpur Campus (Nadia), Burdwan Sadar (Burdwan), and Susunia (Bankura).
  • B.Sc. (Hons.) Horticulture (4 years / 8 semesters) – Offered only at Mohanpur Campus, Nadia.

These professional degree programmes follow ICAR guidelines and prepare students for careers in agriculture, horticulture, research, and agribusiness.

BCKV UG Admission 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Age Limit: Candidates must be not less than 16 years as on 31.08.2026 (as per ICAR guideline).

Educational Qualification:

Name of BoardEssential Qualification
WBCHSE & Other Boards (CBSE, ICSE, etc.)Passed Higher Secondary (10+2) with English, Physics, Chemistry and Biology. Minimum 65% marks in aggregate (General/OBC/EWS/Sports) and 55% for SC/ST/PwD.
WBSCVE&T (Vocational stream)Passed 10+2 with English, Biology, Physics/Chemistry and two vocational subjects (FVPR/FRGR/VGGR/PNMP/SMAG/CRNM/SPPR/JPE/FMOP/FCPR). Minimum 65% aggregate (General) and 55% (SC/ST/PwD).

Note: ICAR quota candidates are exempted from the above percentage criteria.
