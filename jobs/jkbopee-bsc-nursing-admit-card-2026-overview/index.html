JKBOPEE BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026 - Overview

The J&K Board of Professional Entrance Examinations (JKBOPEE) has released the JKBOPEE BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026 for candidates appearing in the Common Entrance Test (CET) for B.Sc. Nursing, B.Sc. Paramedical and B.Sc. Technology courses in various Government and Private Institutions of the UT of J&K. The online admit cards are available for download from 21 April 2026, 5:30 PM onwards on the official website jkbopee.gov.in.

JKBOPEE BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026 - Download Link

JKBOPEE has activated the online admit card download facility for all eligible candidates on its official website jkbopee.gov.in. Candidates must log in using their application details and download the electronic admit card, take a clear printout and carefully check all particulars.

How to Download JKBOPEE BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026

  • Follow the steps below to download the JKBOPEE BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026:
  • Visit the official website of JKBOPEE: jkbopee.gov.in.
  • Click on the link for CET B.Sc. Nursing / B.Sc. Paramedical / B.Sc. Technology Admit Card 2026.
  • Log in using your application number and other required credentials.
  • Download the electronic admit card displayed on the screen.
  • Take a printout and affix a recent passport-size photograph if it is not already printed on the admit card.
  • Candidates must carry the printed admit card to the examination centre; without it, entry will not be permitted.

JKBOPEE BSc Nursing CET 2026 Exam Date and Timing

The Common Entrance Test for B.Sc. Nursing, B.Sc. Paramedical and B.Sc. Technology Courses-2026 will be conducted on 26 April 2026 (Sunday). The examination will be held from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM at various designated centres in the UT of J&K.

JKBOPEE CET 2026 Exam Day Time Schedule

As per the official notification, candidates must strictly follow the tentative time schedule for the examination day:

TimeActivity
08:45 AMReporting time at the Examination Centre/College
09:25 AMReporting time in the Examination Hall
09:30 AMInvigilator verifies identity with photo on admit card and attendance sheet; Centre Supervisor also checks identity to prevent impersonation
09:45 AMDistribution of OMR Answer Sheets and filling of particulars by candidates
09:55 AMDistribution of Question Booklets; booklet number to be entered on the OMR Answer Sheet
10:00 AMCandidates break open seal of Question Booklet and start attempting questions
01:00 PMCandidates hand over original OMR Answer Sheet to Invigilator, retain Question Booklet and candidate’s copy of OMR, and leave only when permitted
No candidate will be allowed to leave the examination hall after distribution of OMR sheets until the exam concludes.

Documents Required with JKBOPEE BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026

Along with the JKBOPEE BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026, candidates must carry valid photo identification for verification at the exam centre. The following original documents are acceptable:

  • Aadhar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Driving License
  • Passport
  • Photo ID issued by the concerned College / Employer ID

Candidates must also ensure a recent passport-size photograph is affixed on the electronic admit card if it is not already printed.

Important Instructions Printed on JKBOPEE BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026

JKBOPEE has laid down several important instructions that all candidates must follow:

  • The admit card and status of the candidate are purely provisional and subject to verification of eligibility by JKBOPEE and other competent bodies.
  • Candidates who have submitted wrong or false information and managed to appear in the CET will be disqualified, and if admitted, their admission will be cancelled and they may face departmental/criminal action.
  • Mere appearance in the CET does not guarantee selection or admission to the course.
  • Candidates must read the Electronic Information Brochure and the instructions for filling the OMR answer sheet very carefully.
  • Before attempting questions, candidates should check that the Question Booklet contains 180 questions; in case of missing/damaged/blank pages, it is the candidate’s responsibility to get it replaced by the same series immediately.

JKBOPEE BSc Nursing CET 2026 Answer Key

JKBOPEE will upload the Answer Key of the question paper on the official website on 26 April 2026 around 6:00 PM or by 2:00 PM on the next day. The answer key will remain available for three days, i.e. 27, 28 and 29 April 2026 up to 5:00 PM.Candidates having any objection to any answer in the Answer Key can submit a written representation in the prescribed format along with documentary proof from standard textbooks.

The representation must reach the JKBOPEE office at Srinagar/Jammu within the specified time and must be accompanied by a fee of Rs. 1000, payable via Debit/Credit/ATM card through POS machines at the BOPEE offices. Representations will be examined by subject experts, and based on their decision, a revised Answer Key, if required, will be uploaded. No challenge will be entertained against the revised Answer Key.
