Scholarship Details

NSP National Scholarship Portal 2026: The National Scholarship Portal (NSP) has released a notification for students who have demonstrated exceptional academic performance, specifically those who have secured 80% or more marks in their Senior Secondary (10+2) exam or 95% or more marks in their Secondary (10th) exam, and whose parents’ annual income is less than ₹4.5 lakh. These eligible students have the opportunity to apply through the Central Schemes available on the National Scholarship Portal (CSSS). To avail this opportunity, eligible students can submit their applications online using the provided link. For more information on how to apply and who is eligible, please see the details below.
