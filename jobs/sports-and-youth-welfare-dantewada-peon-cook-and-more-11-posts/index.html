FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview?

The walk-in interview for the Sports and Youth Welfare Dantewada recruitment is scheduled for 18th June 2026. Candidates are advised to reach the venue on time with all necessary documents.
Q2How many vacancies are available for the Cook post?
A
There are 4 vacancies available for the Cook post in the Sports and Youth Welfare Dantewada recruitment drive. This position is on a contractual basis.
Q3What is the minimum age requirement for these posts?
A
The minimum age requirement for all the posts released by Sports and Youth Welfare Dantewada is 21 years. Candidates must meet this age criterion to be eligible.
Q4Is there any application fee for the Sports and Youth Welfare Dantewada recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive. Candidates can apply without paying any fee.
Q5What is the educational qualification for the Sports Coordinator post?
A
For the Sports Coordinator post, candidates must be a Graduate in Journalism (Sports) or Mass Communication from a recognized university. Experience in sports journalism is preferred.
Q6What is the maximum age limit for candidates applying for these positions?
A
The maximum age limit for candidates applying for these positions is 35 years. However, age relaxation is applicable as per Chhattisgarh government rules.
Q7What is the selection process for the Peon and Cook posts?
A
The selection process for the Peon and Cook posts includes a Walk-in Interview, Document Verification, and a Skill Test. Candidates need to perform well in all stages.
Q8What is the salary for the Superintendent (Girls) post?
A
The salary for the Superintendent (Girls) post is Rs. 20,675/- per month. This is a contractual position offered by Sports and Youth Welfare Dantewada.
Q9Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?
A
The walk-in interview will be conducted at the Sports and Youth Welfare Dantewada office. Candidates should refer to the official notification for the exact address and reporting time.
Q10What documents are required for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates need to carry all original documents along with one set of self-attested photocopies, including ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates (if applicable), and category certificates (if applicable).
