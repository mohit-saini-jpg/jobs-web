Sainik School Kalikiri Recruitment 2026 : Eligibility & Qualification

Education Qualification
  1. Bachelor's degree in Drawing and Painting/Sculpture / Graphic Art / Fine Arts with 50% marks
  2. B.Ed Degree with 50% marks
  3. Four year integrated B.P.Ed. course with 50% marks
  4. Bachelor's Degree in Physical Education with 50% marks along with B.P.Ed
Details
Art Master: Bachelor's degree in Drawing and Painting/Sculpture / Graphic Art / Fine Arts from a recognised institution with at least 50% marks provided that the candidates should have studied Drawing and Painting as Main subject in all the years of graduation. B.Ed Degree from NCTE recognized institution with at least 50% marks. PTI-Cum Matron (Female): Four year integrated B.P.Ed. course from NCTE recognized institution with at least 50% marks OR Bachelor's Degree in Physical Science/ Physical Education / Physical Education and Sports offered by NCTE recognized institution with at least 50% marks provided that, Physical Education is studied in all the years of graduation along with B.P.Ed. of at least one year duration (or its equivalent) from any NCTE recognized institution with at least 50% marks OR Bachelor's degree with Physical Education as an elective subject with 50% marks and participation in National Or State or Inter-University competitions in sports or games or athletics recognized by Association of Indian University or Indian Olympic Association along with B.P.Ed. of at least one year duration (or its equivalent) from any NCTE recognized institution with at least 50% marks.
