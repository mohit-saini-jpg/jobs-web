Qualification Details (Tentative)

  • Junior Scientific Assistant (Biology) : Master’s Degree in Zoology, Botany, Microbiology, Anthropology, Biology, Life Science, Biochemistry, Genetics, Biotechnology, Molecular Biology or Forensic Science (with Zoology or Botany in B.Sc) or B.E/B.Tech in Biotechnology.
  • Junior Scientific Assistant (Ballistics) : Master’s Degree in Physics, Mathematics, Applied Mathematics or Forensic Science (with Physics or Mathematics in B.Sc).
  • Junior Scientific Assistant (Chemistry) : Master’s Degree in Chemistry, Toxicology, Biochemistry or Forensic Science (with Chemistry in B.Sc).
  • Junior Scientific Assistant (Lie-Detection) : Master’s Degree in Psychology or Criminology.
  • Junior Scientific Assistant (Documents) : Master’s Degree in Physics, Chemistry or Forensic Science (with Physics or Chemistry studied for at least 2 years in B.Sc).
  • Junior Scientific Assistant (HRD/QC) : Master’s Degree in Physics, Maths, Computer Science, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology, Forensic Science, Psychology, Toxicology, Biotechnology or Genetics or MCA or B.Tech in Computer Science, Electronics, IT, ECE or Instrumentation or PG Diploma in Management (HR).
  • Junior Scientific Assistant (Photo) : Master’s Degree in Physics, Computer Science, Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, Information Technology or Forensic Science or B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence.
  • Junior Scientific Assistant (Cyber Forensic) : Master’s Degree in Computer Science, Computer Application, Information Technology or Physics (with Cyber Security) or B.E/B.Tech in Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication or Electrical & Electronics.
  • Junior Scientific Assistant (Crime Scene/District Mobile Forensic Unit) : Master’s Degree in Zoology, Botany, Anthropology, Microbiology, Chemistry, Physics, Toxicology, Biochemistry or Forensic Science.
  • Junior Scientific Assistant (Finger Print) : Master’s Degree in Physics, Chemistry or Forensic Science (with Physics or Chemistry studied for at least 2 years in B.Sc).
  • Junior Scientific Assistant (Physics) : Master’s Degree in Physics or Forensic Science (with Physics studied for at least 2 years in B.Sc).
  • Technical Assistant (Hindi) : Master’s Degree in Hindi/English or any subject with Hindi & English (as per specified combination) and Diploma/Certificate in Hindi-English Translation or 2 years Translation experience.
  • Assistant Archivist Grade-I : Master’s Degree in History (with Modern Indian History) and Diploma in Archives & Record Management.
  • I.T. Assistant Grade-A : 12th Pass and Typing speed 8000 key depressions per hour (26.67 wpm in English or Hindi) and Qualify Aptitude Test.
  • Fitter Grade-II : Plumber Trade Certificate and 1 Year practical experience in water distribution network (C.I. & G.I. pipes).
  • Lift Operator : Matric/10th and ITI Certificate in Electrician or Wireman.
  • Asstt. Operator cum Mechanic (A/C) : 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Maths and ITI in Refrigeration & Air Conditioning and 2 Years experience in AC Mechanic or Operator.
  • Sub Station Apprentice : Matric and National Trade Certificate (2 Years) in Electrician Trade.
  • Data Processing Assistant Grade ‘A’ : Graduation or 3 Years Diploma in Computer Engg. with 1 Year experience or Degree in Computer Engg. and Typing speed 8000 key depressions per hour.
  • Data Entry Operator Grade ‘B’ : Graduation or 3 Years Diploma in Computer Engg./Electronics & Communication and Typing speed 8000 key depressions per hour and Qualify Aptitude Test.
  • Trained Graduate Teacher (Computer Science) : BCA or B.Sc (Computer Science as main subject) or B.E/B.Tech (CS/IT) or Graduation and ‘A’ Level (DOEACC).
  • Domestic Science Teacher : Bachelor’s Degree in Domestic Science or Home Science and B.Ed with Domestic Science or Home Science as teaching subject.
  • Trained Graduate Teacher (Special Education Teacher) : Graduation and B.Ed (Special Education) or equivalent and CTET (Elementary) and RCI Registration.
  • Special Educator (Primary) : 12th Pass and D.Ed Special Education or Diploma in Special Education or equivalent RCI recognized qualification and CTET (Primary) and RCI Registration.
  • Electrical Overseer / Sub Inspector : Degree in Electrical Engineering OR Diploma in Electrical Engineering with 01 Year Experience.
