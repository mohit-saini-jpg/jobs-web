Qualification / Eligibility

ANM/UHN: Should have passed Secondary School Examination and two-year ANM conducted from DPH institution / nursing council. Should produce ANM certificate issued by Director of Public Health & Preventive Medicine. Multi-Purpose Hospital Worker: The candidate must have passed 8th Standard. Lab Technician: Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT)/above in Recognized Institution. Occupational Therapist: Bachelors / Master's degree in Occupational Therapy from a recognized University. Working Knowledge of Tamil is mandatory. Special Educator for Behavioural Therapy: Diploma/Bachelor's / Master's degree in Special education D.Ed/B.Ed/M.Ed from a UGC - recognized university. Live RCI (Rehabilitation Council of India) registration with a valid number. Working Knowledge of Tamil is mandatory. Psychologist: Master's degree in Child/Adolescent Psychologist or Child Psychologist from any recognized University in India. Account Assistant: B. Com Graduate / B.A., (Corporate) / BCS. With Minimum 1 year of Experience in Maintenance of Accounts with Tally Package and Computer Skills. Dispenser Siddha: D.Pharm/ Integrated Pharmacy Course (for Certificates issued government of Tamil Nadu only). Multi-Purpose Health Worker (Male) / Health Inspector Gr-II: Must have passed plus two with Biology (or) Botany and Zoology. Must have passed Tamil Language as a subject in SSLC Level. Must have passed two years Multi-Purpose Health Worker (male) / Health Inspector/Sanitary Inspector course Training from Government Regional Training Institutes / recognized Private Institution /Trust / Universities /Deemed Universities including Gandhi Gram Rural Institute. Mid-Level Health Provider: Diploma in GNM/B.Sc., (Nursing) from Government or Government approved Private Nursing Colleges which are Recognised by the Indian Nursing Council. Psychiatric Nurse: Nursing qualification obtained in Government Medical Institutions, with specialized Psychiatry training of six months in identified institutions preferred. Clinical Psychologist: M.Sc Psychology/ M.Phil Clinical Psychology/ M.A. or M.Sc Psychology with 6 months special training at identified institutions like the National Institute of Mental Health and Neuro Sciences. Staff Nurse: Diploma in GNM/B.Sc., (Nursing) from Government or Government approved Private Nursing Colleges which are Recognised by the Indian Nursing Council. ANM: Should have passed Higher Secondary School Examination and two-year ANM course conducted from DPH approved institution / nursing council. Should produce ANM certificate issued by Director of Public Health.
