The Rajasthan National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS) exam is conducted every year for meritorious students from economically weaker sections studying in Class 8, with the aim of supporting their education from Class 9 to 12 through a fixed annual scholarship amount.

After the completion of the 2025–26 NMMS exam, the education department has published the Rajasthan NMMS Result 2026 in online mode, where students can download their individual scorecard as well as verify their selection status in the state-level merit list. This result is crucial because only shortlisted candidates who meet the cut off and eligibility conditions will receive the NMMS scholarship in the upcoming academic years.

How to download Rajasthan NMMS 2026 merit list PDF

  • Go to the same ShalaDarpan NMMS section or RSCERT/Udaipur notification page.
  • Find the link “NMMS Rajasthan 2026 State Level Merit List” or similar.
  • Click the merit list PDF link and open the file in a new tab.
  • Use the search option in the PDF (Ctrl+F) to look up your roll number, name or district.
  • Save or print the PDF for records.

Details Mentioned in Rajasthan NMMS Result 2026 Scorecard

  • Student name
  • Roll number and registration number
  • School name and district
  • Category (General/OBC/SC/ST etc.)
  • Marks obtained in Mental Ability Test (MAT)
  • Marks obtained in Scholastic Aptitude Test (SAT)
  • Total marks in NMMS exam
  • Qualifying status and remarks
  • Any state rank or category rank, if shown in the portal/merit list

How to Check Rajasthan NMMS Result 2026 Online

Step-by-step process to check NMMS Rajasthan result 2026

  • Visit the official ShalaDarpan website – rajshaladarpan.nic.in or rajshaladarpan.rajasthan.gov.in.
  • On the left dashboard, click on the Result or NMMS Exam – Result link.
  • Select the Rajasthan NMMS Result 2025–26 / NMMS Exam Result – 2026 option.
  • Enter the student’s roll number and date of birth in the login fields.
  • Fill the captcha code and click on the Search or Submit button.
  • The Rajasthan NMMS Result 2026 marksheet will appear on the screen; check total marks, MAT and SAT scores and qualifying status.
  • Download the result PDF and take a printout for future use (scholarship verification, school documentation, etc.).
