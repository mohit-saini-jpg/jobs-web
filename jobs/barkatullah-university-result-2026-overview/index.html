How to Check Barkatullah University Result 2026 Online

  • Visit the official result portal at bubhopal.ac.in/1594/Online-Result.
  • Click on the "Online Result" link on the homepage.
  • Select the relevant course and semester from the list of declared results - for example, MSc Chemistry Semester III or BTech Mechanical Semester V.
  • Enter your login details, such as roll number or enrollment number, when prompted.
  • Click the "Search Result" or "Submit" button.
  • Your result will be displayed on the screen. Download and save it, or take a printout for reference.
