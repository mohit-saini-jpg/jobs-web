What Is UP B.Ed Counselling 2026?

UP B.Ed Counselling 2026 is the seat allotment process conducted by Bundelkhand University, Jhansi for candidates who have qualified the UP B.Ed Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) 2026 and secured a state rank in the merit list. The process is completely online and allows rank holders to register, fill college preferences, and get seats allotted in B.Ed colleges across Uttar Pradesh for the 2026-2028 session.

Only candidates who hold a valid state rank in the UP B.Ed JEE 2026 merit list are eligible to participate. Candidates who appeared in the qualifying examination in 2026 must also upload their mark sheet at the time of registration.

UP B.Ed Counselling 2026 Fee Structure

Understanding the fee structure before starting your registration helps you plan your finances and avoid confusion during the process. The counselling fee is non-refundable, while the advance institute fee may be adjusted or refunded depending on seat allotment outcome.

  • Counselling Registration Fee: Rs. 750/- per round (non-refundable, payable each round you participate in)
  • Advance Institute Fee (Rounds 1 & 2): Rs. 5,000/-
  • Full Institute Fee (Round 3): Rs. 51,250/-

Important fee adjustment rules:

  • If your allotted college fee is higher than the advance fee you paid, you must pay the remaining balance online within the specified date.
  • If the advance fee paid is more than the college's fee, the excess amount will be refunded directly to your registered bank account.
  • SC, ST, and EWS category candidates must also pay any remaining balance if the institute fee exceeds the advance fee deposited.
  • If you do not report to your allotted college, the institute fee will not be refunded under any circumstances.

How to Apply Online for UP B.Ed Counselling 2026 - Step-by-Step Process

The UP B.Ed 2026 counselling registration is entirely online. Follow these steps carefully to ensure a smooth and error-free registration. All payments must be made through online mode only Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, or UPI.

Step 1 - Visit the Official Portal Open bujhansi.ac.in on any browser. Always use the official government portal and avoid third-party or unofficial websites.

Step 2 - Find the Counselling Registration Link Look for the "UP B.Ed Counselling 2026 Online Registration" link on the homepage or notifications panel and click it.

Step 3 - Log In With Your Credentials Enter your UP B.Ed JEE 2026 roll number, registration number, date of birth, name, mobile number, and email ID exactly as mentioned on your admit card.

Step 4 - OTP Verification An OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number. Enter it correctly to verify your identity. Ensure your mobile is active and has network connectivity during this step.

Step 5 - Fill the Registration Form Enter all required personal details including category, domicile status, educational qualification, and trade/subject preference. Do not leave any mandatory field blank.

Step 6 - Enter Bank Account Details Provide your bank account number, account holder name, IFSC code, bank name, and branch details these are needed for any fee refunds.

Step 7 - Pay the Counselling Fee and Advance Institute Fee Pay the counselling fee (Rs. 750/-) and the applicable advance institute fee through the online payment gateway. Save the payment confirmation receipt immediately.

Step 8 - Upload Required Documents Upload scanned copies of all required documents in the prescribed format and file size. Candidates who appeared in the 2026 qualifying exam must upload their mark sheet here.

Step 9 - Fill College Choices This is the most critical step. Select and arrange your preferred B.Ed colleges in order of priority. Fill as many choices as possible to increase your chances of allotment. Locked choices cannot be changed later.

Step 10 - Lock Your Choices Lock your choices before the deadline. Unlocked choices are auto-locked before seat allotment, so it is best to manually lock them once you are satisfied.

Step 11 - Check Allotment Result The allotment result is accessible only through your login. It is not published anywhere else. Log in after the allotment date to check your result.

Step 12 - Accept Seat and Report to College If a seat is allotted, click ACCEPT on the portal. Pay any remaining fee balance if applicable, then download the Provisional Allotment cum Confirmation Letter and report to the allotted college on or before the notified date with all original documents.

Important Instructions for UP B.Ed Counselling 2026

Candidates must pay close attention to these official guidelines to avoid disqualification or loss of fees. These instructions have been issued by Bundelkhand University, Jhansi and are binding for all participants.

  • Only candidates with a valid state rank in the UP B.Ed JEE 2026 merit list can participate in counselling.
  • The counselling fee of Rs. 750/- is non-refundable regardless of whether a seat is allotted.
  • After receiving an allotment, candidates must click the ACCEPT button on the portal before downloading the Provisional Allotment cum Confirmation Letter.
  • Physical document verification will be carried out by the principal of the allotted institute. Candidature will be cancelled if documents are found invalid or incomplete.
  • If a candidate does not report to the allotted institute within the deadline, the institute fee will not be refunded.
  • Candidates should fill the maximum number of college choices to minimise the risk of non-allotment.
  • All payments must be made online only. Cash payments are not accepted at any stage.
  • The seat allotment result is available exclusively through the candidate's login on the official portal it is not published on any other platform.
