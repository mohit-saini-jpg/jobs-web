RRB Section Controller Selection Process 2026

Selection for the post of Section Controller involves the following stages: Computer Based Test (CBT): Single stage, 120 minutes, 100 questions, with negative marking of 1/3 mark per wrong answer Computer Based Aptitude Test (CBAT): Shortlisting to the extent of 8 times the vacancies; candidates must secure a minimum T-Score of 42 marks in each test battery to qualify; no negative marking Document Verification (DV): Shortlisting at 1:1 ratio of vacancies, based on 70% weightage to CBT and 30% weightage to CBAT Medical Examination: As per Medical Standard A-2 UR: 40% EWS: 40% OBC (Non-Creamy Layer): 30% SC: 30% ST: 25% These percentages also apply to Ex-Servicemen candidates as per their community. Qualifying marks may be relaxed by 2 marks for PwBD candidates in case of shortage of PwBD candidates against reserved vacancies.
