RSP Apprentice Eligibility Criteria 2026

Eligibility for RSP Apprentice Recruitment 2026 requires candidates to have passed their qualifying exam as a regular candidate within the last five years, and to have no prior apprenticeship or one-year work experience. These conditions apply uniformly across Trade, Diploma, and Degree categories. Only candidates who completed the qualifying examination as regular candidates are eligible. Candidates must have passed the qualifying examination in the last five years - 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025, or 2026 . Candidates who have appeared for the qualifying exam but whose results are not declared as on the last date of application are not eligible . Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing apprenticeship training at any establishment, or who have one year or more of job experience , are not eligible . The RSP Apprentice age limit is minimum 18 years and maximum 28 years a

RSP Apprentice Application Fee

The notification does not mention any application fee for RSP Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27. Candidates only need to complete online registration on the applicable government portal before the last date.

Registration Requirements Before Applying

Before filling the online application, RSP Apprentice candidates must first create an account on the correct government portal based on their category, and ensure their contact and banking details meet DBT requirements. Trade Apprentices (ITI): Register at apprenticeshipindia.org . Graduate/Technician Apprentices (Diploma & Degree): Register on the National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS) portal at nats.education.gov.in . Candidates must have an active email ID and mobile number valid for at least the next one year, since all future communication happens only through email. The bank account must be DBT-enabled with KYC verified , and candidates must verify the correctness of all information before final submission. After successful online registration, candidates must take a printout of the filled application for future reference.

RSP Apprentice Walk-In Venue

All shortlisted RSP Apprentice candidates must report in person at the same venue in Rourkela for document verification, regardless of trade or discipline, on their scheduled date. Venue: Central Recreation Room, Trainees' Hostel, Sector-5, Rourkela-769002, District Sundargarh, Odisha.

Documents Required for RSP Apprentice Walk-In

Candidates attending the RSP Apprentice walk-in must carry both original and self-attested documents for verification, along with a mandatory medical fitness certificate. Original Institution Leaving Certificate (retained by RSP until completion of training) Self-attested copy of Matriculation certificate for date-of-birth verification Self-attested copy of Provisional Pass Certificate Self-attested copy of SC/ST/OBC/PH Certificate (if applicable) Self-attested copy of Aadhaar Card/Bank Passbook (e-KYC validation mandatory) Medical Fitness Certificate from a Government hospital Apprentice Registration copy from the NAPS/NATS Portal

RSP Apprentice Selection Process 2026

Selection for RSP Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is merit-based rather than exam-based, calculated through a weighted formula applied during the walk-in shortlisting process. Selection depends on vacancies available in each category and discipline. Candidates are shortlisted on academic merit through the walk-in process using a weighted formula: 30% weightage to Class X marks and 70% weightage to Degree/Diploma/ITI examination marks , as applicable. Marks must be mentioned in percentage ; candidates with a CGPA must convert it to percentage as per their board/university norms, which will be verified during document verification. Engagement is subject to the candidate being declared medically fit as per the Apprentices Act, 1961, with the certificate issued by a Government doctor bearing a medical registration number.

RSP Apprentice Stipend/Salary 2026

The RSP Apprentice stipend varies by category and, for engineering Diploma/Degree apprentices, is shared between RSP and the Basic Off-Roll Training Provider (BOPT) under DBT mode. Stipend for Trade Apprentices is as per rates prescribed by MSDE, GoI. For Graduate/Technician (Diploma & Degree) Apprentices, 50% of the stipend is paid by RSP and 50% by BOPT under DBT mode , or as per prescribed MSDE rates. The training duration is one year . RSP will not provide accommodation or travel allowance to apprentices during training.
