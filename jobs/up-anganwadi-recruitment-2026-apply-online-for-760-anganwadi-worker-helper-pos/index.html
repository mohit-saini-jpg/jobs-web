Application Fee – UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026

No application fee is mentioned in the official notification for either the Bagpat or Kannauj UP Anganwadi Bharti 2026. Candidates should apply through the official portal at upanganwadibharti.in without any fee payment.

Selection Process – UP Anganwadi Worker Helper Vacancy 2026

The selection for the UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 is merit-based with priority given to specific categories of women. There is no written examination or interview mentioned in the official notification. The merit list for the Anganwadi Worker post will be prepared on the basis of the maximum educational qualification of the applicant. Online applications are received through upanganwadibharti.in. Eligibility and residential status of applicants is verified at the project/district level. Merit list is prepared based on highest educational qualification for the Anganwadi Worker post. Priority is given to widows/divorced/abandoned women from BPL families of the concerned gram sabha/ward, in the order specified in the official notification. Final selection and appointment orders are issued by the District Programme Officer.

UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1Has the UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 official notification been released for Bagpat and Kannauj?
A
Yes, the District Programme Officers of both Bagpat and Kannauj have officially released the UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 notification on 25 June 2026 for a total of 635 vacancies in Bagpat and 125 vacancies in Kannauj.
Q2What is the last date to apply for UP Anganwadi Bharti 2026?
A
The last date to apply online for Bagpat is 16 July 2026 up to midnight. For Kannauj, the last date is 16 July 2026. Both applications must be submitted through https://upanganwadibharti.in.
Q3How many total vacancies are available in UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
Bagpat district has a total of 635 vacancies - 261 for Anganwadi Worker (Karyakatri) and 374 for Anganwadi Helper (Sahayika). Kannauj district has 125 vacancies for Anganwadi Worker. The vacancy count may increase or decrease as per the official note.
Q4Who is eligible to apply for UP Anganwadi Worker Vacancy 2026?
A
Only female candidates who are permanent residents of the concerned gram sabha/ward are eligible. The applicant must be aged between 18 and 35 years as on 01 January 2026 and must have passed Intermediate (12th) or equivalent for the Anganwadi Worker post.
Q5What is the age limit for UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 35 years as on 01 January 2026. Age will be verified only from the date of birth recorded in the High School (10th class) marksheet and certificate.
Q6Is there any application fee for UP Anganwadi Bharti 2026?
A
No application fee is mentioned in the official notification for either Bagpat or Kannauj district UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026.
Q7What is the selection process for UP Anganwadi Worker Recruitment 2026?
A
There is no written examination. Selection is based on merit prepared from the maximum educational qualification of the applicant. Priority is also given to widows, divorced, and abandoned women from BPL families of the concerned gram sabha/ward in a defined order.
Q8Can male candidates apply for UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
No. The official notification clearly states that only female candidates are eligible to apply for the Anganwadi Worker and Anganwadi Helper posts in both Bagpat and Kannauj under this recruitment drive.
Q9What documents are required for UP Anganwadi Apply Online 2026?
A
Candidates must have income, caste, and residence certificates with online-verifiable digital signatures from the competent tehsil authority. Widows must submit the husband's death certificate and family register copy. Divorce/abandonment requires a civil court-issued legal order. For Kannauj, the income certificate must be issued within the last 3 years.
Q10Where can I find the official website for UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
The official website for UP Anganwadi Bharti 2026 is https://upanganwadibharti.in. This portal hosts the online application form, project-wise vacancy details, eligibility criteria, and the complete notification for both Bagpat and Kannauj districts.
