SVPUAT Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Non-Teaching & KVK Non-Technical Posts: Selection is based on Academic Score (maximum 60 marks) plus a Written Examination (maximum 180–250 marks depending on the post) plus an Interview (maximum 60 marks). For Stenographer Grade-III and Driver posts, a dictation/typing test or driving skill test is also conducted. Subject Matter Specialist / T6: Selection is based on a Score Card (Academic Qualifications + Research Performance/Publications + Professional Experience + Extra-Curricular Activities), a PowerPoint presentation, and an Interview. A minimum score of 50% (General/OBC) or 45% (SC/ST/PWD) in Parts A+B+C is required to qualify for interview. Director / Joint Director: Selection is based on a Score Card (Academic Qualifications, Research Performance and Publication, Teaching/Research/Extension experience), a PowerPoint presentation on work done and vision, and an Interview.   • 11Submit the form. No changes are possible after fee payment/submission.
  • 12Download and print the final submitted application form.
