What Is MPSOS and Who Can Appear in the July 2026 Exams?

The Madhya Pradesh State Open School (MPSOS) is a state government body that provides secondary and higher secondary education opportunities to students who are unable to attend regular schools. MPSOS conducts examinations for multiple streams and grade levels, offering flexible learning pathways.

The July 2026 examinations are open to students enrolled in the following categories:

  • Open School - Class 5th, 8th, 10th, and 12th
  • RJN/ALC (Ruk Jana Nahi / Attempt to Learn Continuously) - Class 10th and 12th
  • CBSE On Demand - Class 12th

How to Download MPSOS Time Table 2026

Downloading your MPSOS July 2026 exam schedule is straightforward. Follow these simple steps to access your timetable directly from the official website.

  • Visit the official MPSOS website at mpsos.nic.in
  • Navigate to the Time Table section on the homepage
  • Click on the link corresponding to your exam category and class
  • The timetable will open as a JPG image in your browser
  • Right-click and save the image, or take a screenshot for offline reference

MPSOS RJN/ALC Time Table July 2026 (Class 10th & 12th)

The Ruk Jana Nahi (RJN) scheme is a second-chance examination initiative by the Government of Madhya Pradesh, designed for students who could not pass their Class 10th or 12th board exams. The ALC (Attempt to Learn Continuously) programme runs alongside it.

MPSOS has released two separate RJN/ALC timetables for the July 2026 session:

  • RJN/ALC July 2026 - Class 10th Time Table
  • RJN/ALC July 2026 - Class 12th Time Table

Students registered under this scheme should download the timetable relevant to their class level and plan their preparation accordingly.

MPSOS Open School Time Table July 2026 (Class 5th, 8th, 10th & 12th)

The MPSOS Open School stream is designed for students who wish to complete their formal education through a flexible, open learning format. Timetables have been released for three levels under the Open School category for July 2026:

  • Open School July 2026 - Class 10th Time Table
  • Open School July 2026 - Class 12th Time Table
  • Open School July 2026 - Class 5th & 8th Time Table (combined schedule)

Students enrolled in Class 5th or 8th under MPSOS Open School can refer to the combined timetable released for both grades.

CBSE On Demand Time Table July 2026 (Class 12th)

MPSOS also facilitates the CBSE On Demand examination for Class 12th students. This exam allows eligible candidates to appear for board examinations on a demand basis, outside the regular annual examination cycle.

  • CBSE On Demand July 2026 - Class 12th Time Table has been officially released and is available on the MPSOS portal.

Students registered under the CBSE On Demand scheme should download this specific schedule and note their examination dates carefully.

Important Tips for MPSOS July 2026 Exam Preparation

Once you have downloaded your MPSOS July 2026 time table, here are some key points to keep in mind:

  • Download and save the official timetable from mpsos.nic.in to avoid relying on unofficial sources.
  • Cross-check your exam dates against your admit card once it is released.
  • Plan your revision schedule subject-wise based on the gap between exam dates.
  • Check the MPSOS official website regularly for any updates, corrections, or postponements to the time table.
  • Keep your roll number and registration details handy as you will need them to access your admit card.
