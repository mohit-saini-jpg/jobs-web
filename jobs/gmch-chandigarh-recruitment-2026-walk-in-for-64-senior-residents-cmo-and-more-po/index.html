GMCH Chandigarh Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Government Medical College & Hospital (GMCH), Chandigarh has invited applications from Indian Nationals for filling 64 tenure posts of Senior Residents, Casualty Medical Officers (CMO) in General Medicine and General Surgery, Resident Anaesthetist, and Resident Pathologist. Selection will be through a walk-in interview scheduled on 20 July 2026 (Monday). These are tenure posts, and appointments will be made for a period of 44 days. The notice is issued by the Establishment Branch-II, GMCH, Chandigarh, dated 15 July 2026.

GMCH Eligibility Criteria

Only Indian Nationals are allowed to apply. Candidates of foreign nationality are not eligible for these posts. For all four post categories - Senior Resident, CMO (General Medicine & General Surgery), Resident Anaesthetist, and Resident Pathologist - the essential qualification is: A Postgraduate Medical Degree, i.e., M.D./M.S./DNB/M.Ch./D.M. or equivalent in the concerned specialty, as per the Central Residency Scheme The degree must be recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI) and the candidate registered with either the Medical Council of India or a State Medical Council Candidates who have already completed a three-year tenure of Senior Residency are not eligible for the post of Senior Resident

GMCH Age Limit

Upper age limit as on 1 July 2026: 45 years , applicable for Senior Residents, CMO (General Medicine & General Surgery), Resident Anaesthetist, and Resident Pathologist.

GMCH Pay Scale

Senior Residents, CMO (General Medicine & General Surgery), Resident Anaesthetist, and Resident Pathologist holding a PG degree will be paid in the pay scale of ₹67,700/- (minimum pay, 1st Cell of Level-11, as per Schedule Part A of the Pay Matrix), plus Non-Practising Allowance (NPA) at 20%, as per the Central Government Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2016.

GMCH Application Fee

Non-refundable fee of ₹500/-, deposited on the spot at GMCH Applicants applying for multiple posts/departments need to deposit this fee only once The application fee, once deposited, will not be refunded under any circumstances

GMCH Selection Process

Selection is through walk-in interview only. There is no mention of a written examination in this notice.

Tenure of the Posts

The posts are tenure posts; appointments will be made for a period of 44 days If work and conduct are not found satisfactory at any stage, services may be terminated without assigning any reason If Senior Residency is done at any Central Institution/Hospital under the Central Residency Scheme, that period is counted toward the maximum tenure of three years (including previous experience); the maximum period of Senior Residency cannot exceed three years

Required Documents

Application form in the prescribed format, duly filled Self-attested copies of educational qualifications and experience certificates Date of birth certificate Registration certificate with MCI/State Medical Council Internship completion certificate Mark sheets of MBBS 1st Prof, 2nd Prof, and Final Prof-Part I & II No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the present employer, for candidates already in Government service (see Important Instructions) Recent passport-size photograph Photocopies of relevant certificates/testimonials, plus original certificates/testimonials for verification on the day

GMCH Chandigarh Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Download the prescribed application form from the official website of GMCH
  • 2Fill the form completely in the prescribed format
  • 3Attach self-attested copies of all required certificates, mark sheets, registration proof, NOC (if applicable), and a recent passport-size photograph
  • 4Report to the Establishment Branch-II, Block-D, GMCH-32, Chandigarh, on 20 July 2026, between 9:00 A.M. and 11:00 A.M.
