Maharashtra Polytechnic Merit List 2026 Overview Table : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 08/07/2026

The Maharashtra Polytechnic merit list 2026 has been released by the Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra (DTE Maharashtra) on its official website, poly26.dtemaharashtra.gov.in. The provisional merit list is for candidates seeking admission to First Year Post SSC Diploma courses in Engineering, Technology, and Architecture for the academic year 2026-27. Candidates can check their merit number by logging in with their application ID and date of birth, and raise objections through e-scrutiny or physical scrutiny mode from July 8 to 9, 2026.

Maharashtra Polytechnic Merit List 2026 Release Date

DTE Maharashtra released the provisional merit list on July 7, 2026, on the official admission portal. Candidates who submitted their application on or before July 6 were considered for this provisional list and assigned a merit number.

Organization Name

The Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra (DTE Maharashtra) is the state authority responsible for conducting the Post SSC Diploma admission process, including the release of the provisional and final merit lists.

Maharashtra Polytechnic Merit List 2026 Status

The provisional merit list for Post SSC Diploma admissions is officially out. The final merit list, which will be used for Centralised Admission Process (CAP) counselling, is scheduled to be released on July 10, 2026, after the grievance redressal window closes.

Selection Stage Covered

  • Online registration and application submission (concluded)
  • Provisional merit list (released July 7, 2026)
  • Grievance redressal via e-scrutiny/physical scrutiny (July 8–9, 2026)
  • Final merit list (scheduled for July 10, 2026)
  • CAP counselling and seat allotment rounds (to follow the final merit list)

Score Card Availability

A separate score card is not applicable for this admission process. The merit list itself displays the candidate's merit number, category, and marks obtained in the qualifying examination (SSC), which form the basis of ranking.

Cut Off Marks

Cut-off marks have not yet been officially released. Cut-offs are typically declared after the final merit list and during the CAP counselling rounds, once seat allotment begins. Candidates are advised to check the official website for updates once the cut-off list is announced.

How to Check the Maharashtra Polytechnic Merit List 2026

  • Visit the official website: poly26.dtemaharashtra.gov.in
  • Click on the link for "Display of Provisional Merit List for First Year Post SSC Diploma Technical Courses in Engineering/Technology 2026-27"
  • Click on the "Candidate Login" option
  • Enter your Application ID and date of birth (or other login credentials as prescribed)
  • Submit the details to view your provisional merit status
  • Check your merit number, marks, category, and reservation details
  • Download or take a printout for future reference

Details Mentioned on the Merit List

  • Candidate's name
  • Application ID / merit number
  • Marks obtained in the qualifying examination (SSC)
  • Category and gender
  • Reservation and special reservation status

Candidates must carefully verify all these details, as any discrepancy needs to be reported during the grievance window.

What to Do After the Merit List

Candidates should check the provisional merit list carefully and verify that all listed details are accurate. If any error is found in name, marks, category, gender, or reservation, it must be reported through e-scrutiny (for candidates who selected e-scrutiny mode) or physical scrutiny (for candidates who selected physical mode) between July 8 and July 9, 2026. Candidates should also keep watching the official website for the final merit list on July 10, 2026, and the subsequent CAP counselling schedule.

Documents Required for the Next Stage

The exact list of documents required for scrutiny and CAP counselling has not been detailed in the reviewed notification. Candidates should refer to the official website or their scrutiny mode instructions for the complete, authoritative document checklist before the next stage begins.

Selection Process

Admission to First Year Post SSC Diploma courses in Engineering, Technology, and Architecture is based on merit prepared according to marks obtained by the candidate in the qualifying examination, with ties resolved as specified under sub-section (3) of rule 8. Once the final merit list is published, eligible candidates proceed to the Centralised Admission Process (CAP) for counselling and seat allotment as per the official schedule released by DTE Maharashtra.

Important Instructions

  • The merit list released on July 7, 2026, is strictly provisional.
  • Candidates must verify their name, qualifying marks, category, gender, and reservation details carefully.
  • Grievances must be raised only through the scrutiny mode (e-scrutiny or physical) selected at the time of registration.
  • If the seat allotted to a candidate is found to be based on false claims made in the application, the allotment or admission will be cancelled automatically.
  • If marks in the qualifying examination are modified after verification, this must be reported to the competent authority before 5 pm on the day the final merit list is displayed.
  • Only candidates whose names appear in the final merit list, expected on July 10, 2026, will be eligible for CAP counselling rounds.
