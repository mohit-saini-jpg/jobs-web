How to Download UHSR BDS Date Sheet 2026

Students can download the UHSR BDS Date Sheet 2026 directly from the official university website in a few easy steps. The process is simple and does not require any login.

  • Open your browser and visit the official website of Pt. B.D. Sharma University of Health Sciences, Rohtak at www.uhsr.ac.in.
  • Navigate to the Examinations or Latest News / Notifications section on the homepage.
  • Look for the link titled Tentative theory date-sheet of BDS 1st & 2nd Year Supple Examinations and click on it.
  • The PDF file will open automatically - click the download button to save it on your device.
  • Open the downloaded file, verify the dates and subjects, then print a copy for easy reference during preparation.

Always cross-check the official timetable carefully and keep a printed copy handy for smooth exam preparation.
