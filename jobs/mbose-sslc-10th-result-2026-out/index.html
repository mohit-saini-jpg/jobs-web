MBOSE SSLC 10th Result 2026 Out

The Meghalaya Board of School Education (MBOSE) has officially declared the MBOSE SSLC 10th Result 2026 today on 7th April 2026 at 11:00 AM. Students who appeared for the Class 10 Secondary School Leaving Certificate examination can now check their results on the official websites.

MBOSE SSLC 10th Result 2026 has been released online on the official websites - megresults.nic.in and mbose.in. Students can check and download their provisional scorecard by entering their roll number.

Students are advised to verify all details mentioned in the MBOSE Class 10 result carefully and contact their respective school or the board in case of any discrepancies.

MBOSE SSLC Results 2026 Key Highlights

The Meghalaya Board has announced the MBOSE SSLC 10th Result 2026 today with an overall pass percentage of 86.84%. A total of 42,228 students passed out of 48,623 who appeared. The result includes subject-wise marks, grades, and qualifying status.

Students can download the provisional marksheet online. The digital scorecard is valid for immediate reference and admission purposes until the original marksheet is issued by the schools.

How to Check MBOSE SSLC 10th Result 2026 Online

Students can check their MBOSE SSLC 10th Result 2026 by following the simple steps given below:
