Official Notice: What GGSIPU Announced

This section summarizes the official communication issued by the university's Results Division regarding the declaration of the MA Economics result.

GGSIPU's Results Division issued a formal notice confirming that the result of the MA Economics (2nd & 4th Semester) programme for the End Term Examinations conducted in May 2026 has been declared. The notice instructs all concerned students and stakeholders to view their individual results by logging into the university's Student Portal. The circular was signed by Amit Thakur, Deputy Registrar & Incharge-Results, and was also marked to Deans, Directors, Principals of affiliated institutes, the Controller of Examinations, and other university departments for record and further action.

How to Check GGSIPU MA Economics Result 2026 Online

This section explains the exact steps students need to follow to view and download their MA Economics semester result from the official portal.

Follow these steps to check your result:

  • Visit the official GGSIPU Student Portal at https://examweb.ggsipu.ac.in/web/login.jsp
  • Enter your Login ID, which is your Student Enrollment Number.
  • Enter your Password (see the password format below).
  • Click on the login/submit button.
  • Your MA Economics 2nd or 4th Semester result will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download or take a screenshot/printout of the result for future reference.

Where to Find the Detailed User Manual

This section points students to the official reference document for additional guidance on using the result portal.

For a complete step-by-step user manual and further details on accessing the result portal, GGSIPU has referred students to an earlier circular: University Notice No. GGSIPU/COE/2026/3918, dated 05.01.2026. Students facing login issues or seeking more detailed guidance should refer to this notice or contact their respective institute/university school for assistance.

Who Issued the GGSIPU MA Economics Result Notice?

This section identifies the university officials responsible for declaring and distributing the result notice.

The result declaration notice was signed by:

  • Amit Thakur - Deputy Registrar & Incharge-Results
  • Monica Pathak - Incharge, Result-III

The notice was also circulated to key university offices, including the Director of Academic Affairs, the Controller of Examinations, the Examination Division (Conduct and EDP sections), the office of the Vice Chancellor, the office of the Registrar, and the University Information Technology Services (UITS) department, with instructions to upload the notice on the official university website.
