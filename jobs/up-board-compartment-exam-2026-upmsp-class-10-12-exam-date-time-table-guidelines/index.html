UP Board Compartment Exam 2026 - UPMSP Class 10, 12 Exam Date, Time Table & Guidelines

The UP Board Compartment Exam 2026 for Class 10 and Class 12 will be conducted on July 28, 2026. The Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP) released this schedule on July 3, 2026, for students who were placed in the compartment category in the main exam and those appearing to improve their marks. The Class 10 (High School) exam will run in the morning shift, while the Class 12 (Intermediate) exam is scheduled for the afternoon.

If you are a UP Board student waiting for the compartment or improvement exam date sheet, this article covers everything you need exam timings, the practical exam schedule, admit card details, exam centre rules, and the expected result date.

UP Board Improvement Practical Exam Date 2026

Along with the theory exams, UPMSP has also released the schedule for the compartment/improvement practical examinations, which are held a few days before the written test. This section explains when and where the practical exams will take place.

The UP Board practical exam 2026 for compartment and improvement candidates will be conducted from July 16 to July 17, 2026. These practicals are held at designated government and government-aided schools, under the direct supervision of the District School Inspector (DIOS). Students should get in touch with their respective school authorities to confirm the exact date, time, and venue assigned to them, as this can vary by district and subject.

Who Can Appear for the UP Board Compartment/Improvement Exam 2026?

This section clarifies exactly which students are eligible to sit for this exam, a common point of confusion among UP Board candidates.

Two categories of students can appear in the UP Board compartment exam 2026:

  • Compartment candidates: Students who did not clear one or more subjects in the main Class 10 or Class 12 exam held earlier in 2026.
  • Improvement candidates: Students who passed the main exam but wish to reappear in one or more subjects to improve their overall marks or percentage.

UPMSP Compartment Exam 2026: Exam Centre Rules and Instructions

UPMSP has laid out strict exam-day guidelines to ensure the compartment exam is conducted smoothly and without malpractice. Here are the main rules candidates and centre staff must follow.

  • Exams will be held only at centres designated by the District School Inspector at the district headquarters.
  • No outsiders, apart from students and authorised exam staff, will be allowed inside the exam premises.
  • Mobile phones, smartwatches, Bluetooth devices, calculators, and other electronic gadgets are strictly banned inside the exam hall.
  • Candidates will undergo frisking before entry into the centre.
  • Compartment admit cards are downloaded by school Principals from upmsp.edu.in, signed by them, and then distributed to students candidates cannot download the admit card directly.
  • Students must reach the exam centre at least 45 minutes before the exam starts.

Security Arrangements at UP Board Exam Centres

To maintain transparency during the compartment exam, UPMSP has put in place several monitoring measures at every centre. This section outlines the surveillance and paper-handling protocols in place.

  • CCTV cameras with voice-recording capability will be active throughout the examination at all centres.
  • Strong rooms storing question papers are kept under round-the-clock CCTV surveillance.
  • Question paper packets will be opened only in the presence of the Centre Superintendent and the Static Magistrate, and this process is also recorded on CCTV.
  • District authorities have been directed to ensure the smooth, disruption-free conduct of exams at every designated centre.

UP Board Compartment Result 2026 - Expected Date

Once the compartment and improvement exams are held on July 28, 2026, UPMSP will need time to evaluate answer sheets before declaring results. Based on the board's usual evaluation timeline, the UP Board compartment result 2026 is expected to be announced in August 2026 on the official website, upmsp.edu.in. An exact date has not yet been notified by the board.
