How to Check MDU Result 2026 Online

  • Visit the official MDU result portal at result.mdu.ac.in.
  • On the result page, choose your course and semester from the available result list.
  • Enter your Registration Number and Roll Number in the respective fields.
  • Click on the Proceed or Submit button to view your result.
  • Your MDU Result 2026 marksheet will be displayed on the screen.
  • Check your name, course, semester, subject-wise marks and total marks carefully.
  • Download or print the provisional marksheet for future reference and verification.
