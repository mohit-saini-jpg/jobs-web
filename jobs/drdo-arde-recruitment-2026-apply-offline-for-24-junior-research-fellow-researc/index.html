DRDO ARDE Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Armament Research & Development Establishment (ARDE), Pune, a premier laboratory under the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), has invited applications from Indian Nationals for Junior Research Fellow (JRF) and Research Associate (RA) positions through Advertisement No. ARDE/JRF-RA/2026. A total of 24 vacancies are available across Electronics, Computer Science, Mechanical/Metallurgy, Aerospace, and RF/Microwave disciplines. Applications must be sent offline by post.

Candidates must be Indian Nationals meeting the educational qualification and age limit specified for the post they are applying for. ARDE Pune Stipend/ Salary 2026

Junior Research Fellow (JRF): Rs. 37,000 per month Research Associate (RA): Rs. 67,000 per month HRA, medical facilities, and contingency grant are also admissible as per applicable rules. The fellowship is initially for two years, extendable as per rules for JRF.

DRDO ARDE Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

As per the notification: Only shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview and/or written test. Interview/written test dates will be communicated only via email to shortlisted candidates. Selected candidates must bring a copy of their Bio-data and original certificates for verification at the time of the interview. The notification does not provide further details of the written test pattern, syllabus, or interview weightage.

Required Documents

Candidates must send their CV/Bio-data (as per the enclosed proforma, filled in the candidate's own handwriting) along with self-attested copies of: Educational qualification certificates NET/GATE scorecard Caste certificate (if applicable) Other relevant documents supporting claims made in the application A recent passport-size photograph affixed on the right top corner of the Bio-data Candidates currently employed in Government/Public Sector Undertakings/Autonomous Bodies must bring a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their current employer.

Important Instructions

Fellowship offer does not confer any right to absorption in DRDO. Shortlisted candidates called for interview/written test are eligible for reimbursement of actual train/bus fare by the shortest route, limited to sleeper class railway fare, from the place mentioned in the interview letter or the place the journey started from, whichever is less, as per rules. Candidates must be ready to take up duties assigned anywhere in India. Furnishing false or concealed information can lead to summary termination of the fellowship without prior notice.

DRDO ARDE Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Download/obtain the Bio-data proforma as given in the official advertisement.
  • 2Fill the Bio-data in your own handwriting, including personal details, educational qualifications (from Matric/10th onwards), experience (if any), and NET/GATE/CSIR/UGC qualification details.
