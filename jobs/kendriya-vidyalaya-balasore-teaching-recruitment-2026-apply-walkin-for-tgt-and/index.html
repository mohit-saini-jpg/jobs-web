Kendriya Vidyalaya Balasore Teaching Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

All these engagements are purely contractual, part-time, and on a day-to-day basis. The walk-in interview will be held on 25 July 2026 (Saturday) from 8:00 AM onwards at the Vidyalaya premises. PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya Balasore Recruitment 2026Selection Process

Selection is through a walk-in interview only. The notification does not mention any written test, skill test, or demo class as part of the process. Key points on selection: Candidates must register between 8:00 AM and 9:00 AM on the interview date; no registration is entertained after 9:00 AM. Merely attending the interview does not confer appointment. A panel of selected/waitlisted candidates will be displayed on the Vidyalaya website within 10 days of the interview. The Chairman, VMC (Vidyalaya Management Committee), reserves the right to cancel the interview or the panel at any stage. Any candidate found canvassing will have their candidature cancelled.

PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya Balasore Recruitment 2026 Required Documents

Based on the application form and notification instructions, candidates should carry: Duly filled application form (in capital letters, one form per post applied for) Self-attested photocopies of all educational and professional testimonials/certificates Recent passport-size photograph (to be pasted on the form) Original certificates for verification (as is standard practice for walk-in interviews; carry originals along with self-attested copies) Proof of teaching experience, if applicable CTET or equivalent certificate, if qualified (mandatory to attach if applicable, as per the form)
