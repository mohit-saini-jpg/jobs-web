Qualification / Eligibility

Chief Research Officer: Essential: PhD in Demography/Mathematics/Statistics/Bio-Statistics with 10 years experience or M.Tech/Masters in Computer applications with 10 years experience in Large Scale Survey. Good working knowledge of CsPro. Desirable: STATA, SPSS, SAS, CsPro, Python. Senior Project Officer (Establishment & Procurement): Essential: (i) Post graduate Degree/Diploma in any discipline (ii) Retired from Central Government level 12 and above (iii) 10 years senior management experience (iv) Age not more than 65 years. Desirable: Knowledge of GoI Rules, proficient communication. Junior Research Officer (Report Writing): M.Phil in Demography/Mathematics/Statistics/Social Sciences/Public Health/Bio-Statistics and Demography/Survey Research and Data Analytics.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for IIPS Recruitment 2026?

Q2How many vacancies are available in the IIPS Recruitment 2026?
A
There are a total of 3 vacancies announced by the International Institute for Population Sciences (IIPS) for the recruitment drive. These positions are for Chief Research Officer, Senior Project Officer, and Junior Research Officer.
Q3What is the salary offered for the IIPS recruitment?
A
The remuneration for the selected candidates in the IIPS recruitment will be Rs. 1,26,000/- per month. This amount will be fixed as per the candidate's experience.

The required qualifications vary by post, including PhD in Demography/Statistics, M.Tech/Masters in Computer Applications, Post Graduate Degree/Diploma, and M.Phil in relevant fields. Please refer to the detailed notification for specific post requirements.
Q5What is the age limit for applying to IIPS jobs?
A
The maximum age limit for all the posts announced by IIPS is 65 years. The institute reserves the right to relax these criteria for deserving candidates.
Q6Is there any application fee for IIPS Recruitment 2026?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for the IIPS Recruitment 2026. Candidates can attend the walk-in interview without paying any application fee.
Q7What is the selection process for IIPS Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process for the IIPS Recruitment 2026 will be based on a Walk-in Interview. Candidates need to attend the interview with all their original documents and photocopies.
Q8Where will the walk-in interview for IIPS Recruitment 2026 be conducted?
A
The walk-in interview will be conducted at the International Institute for Population Sciences (IIPS) located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, with the pincode 400088. The exact venue details will be in the official notification.
Q9What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates should carry all original documents, self-attested photocopies of these documents, and recent passport-size photographs. This includes ID proof, educational certificates, and experience certificates.
Q10Can candidates apply offline for this recruitment?
A
No, this recruitment is being conducted through a Walk-in Interview. Candidates are required to attend the interview in person on the specified date and time with all the necessary documents.
