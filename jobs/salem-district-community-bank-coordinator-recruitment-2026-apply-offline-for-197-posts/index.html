  • Candidates shall submit applications offline only. Applications should be sent either in person or by post to the respective Village Panchayat Level Federation (Kootamaippu) office where the vacancy exists.

  • SHG Membership Certificate

  • Experience Certificate as Animator (minimum 2 years)

  • Community / Target Group Certificate (if applicable)

  • Two-wheeler Driving License (if available)

  • Any other relevant certificates

  • Step-by-Step Application Process:

  • Fill the application form neatly.

  • Attach all required self-attested documents.

  • Submit the application in person or by post to the respective Panchayat Level Federation Office on or before 29-05-2026.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for Salem District Community Bank Coordinator Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the application for Salem District Community Bank Coordinator Recruitment 2026 is 29 May 2026. Candidates must ensure that the filled application form along with all required documents reaches the concerned Village Panchayat Level Federation office before the deadline. Late submissions may not be considered during the recruitment process.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in Salem District Community Bank Coordinator Recruitment 2026?

A total of 197 vacancies have been announced for the post of Community Bank Coordinator across different blocks in Salem District. These positions are distributed among multiple Village Panchayat areas, including Athoor, Ayothiyapattinam, Omalur, Talavasal, and other blocks. Candidates should check the block-wise vacancy details before applying.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for Salem District Community Bank Coordinator Recruitment 2026?

Applicants must have passed 12th Standard (Higher Secondary) from a recognized board to be eligible for the post. Apart from educational qualification, candidates should also be able to read, write, and speak Tamil and English fluently. Preference may be given to candidates with computer knowledge and experience in banking-related activities.

Q4. What is the age limit for Salem District Community Bank Coordinator Recruitment 2026?

Candidates applying for the recruitment should be below 35 years of age as per the notification guidelines. The age limit will be considered as on the notification date, and eligible category candidates may receive age relaxation according to government rules. Applicants should verify their eligibility before submitting the form.

Q5. Is prior work experience required for Salem District Community Bank Coordinator Recruitment 2026?

Yes, candidates must have a minimum of 2 years of experience as an Animator in a Self Help Group (SHG). Additionally, applicants should be a member of the Village Panchayat Level Federation SHG, which is an important eligibility requirement. Experience in loan processing and banking transactions will be an added advantage.

Q6. What is the selection process for Salem District Community Bank Coordinator Recruitment 2026?

The selection process will mainly be based on eligibility verification, experience evaluation, and interview/document verification conducted by the concerned authorities. Candidates who meet all requirements and perform well during verification will be shortlisted for appointment. Final selection will depend on eligibility and official approval.

Q7. How can candidates apply for Salem District Community Bank Coordinator Recruitment 2026?

Interested candidates must apply through offline mode only, as online applications are not accepted for this recruitment. Applicants need to obtain the application format, fill it carefully, attach all required self-attested documents, and submit it either directly or by post to the respective Village Panchayat Level Federation office before the closing date.

Q8. What documents are required for Salem District Community Bank Coordinator Recruitment 2026?

Candidates need to submit important documents such as 12th standard certificate, age proof, SHG membership certificate, Animator experience certificate, and community certificate (if applicable). Additional documents like a two-wheeler driving license and computer-related certificates may also improve preference during selection.
