  • 1Certified true copy of the first page of the Service Book duly attested by Head of Department/Head of Office (showing Name, Father's Name, Husband's Name, Nationality, SC/ST Name if applicable, Date of Birth)

  • 5Certificate of disability in case of PwBD candidates
  • 6Live photograph captured through the application module (plain background, no cap/mask/glasses, full frontal view)
  • 7Scanned signature in JPEG format (10 to 20 KB; image dimension about 6.0 cm width x 2.0 cm height)
  • 8This is a Limited Departmental Competitive Examination - only eligible serving JSA/LDC employees of the specified 10 participating cadres can apply.
  • 9Only one application form should be submitted; submission of multiple applications may lead to rejection of candidature.
  • 10After completion of online application, the printed copy must be forwarded by the Head of Department/Office through proper channel to reach SSC (NR) by 03.07.2026 (1800 hrs); applications not forwarded through proper channel will not be considered.
  • 11A candidate who resigns, quits service, or whose services are terminated after applying will not be eligible for appointment on the result of this examination.
  • 12No change in entries made in the online application form shall be allowed at any stage under any circumstances.
  • 13The examination shall be conducted in Delhi NCR only; no centre will be at any Indian mission abroad. Candidates serving abroad will have to appear in India at their own expense.
  • 14Admission Certificate for CBE will be issued online by uploading on the website of the Northern Regional Office; it will not be issued by post. Candidates are advised to regularly visit https://sscnr.nic.in.
  • 15Candidates must carry their Departmental Identity Card (Original) along with hard copy of Admission Certificate for appearing in the examination.
  • 16Candidates who have not undergone Aadhaar Based Authentication must carry at least two passport size recent colour photographs and Original valid Photo-ID proof with Date of Birth at the examination centre.
  • 17There is no provision for re-evaluation/re-checking of scores of any stage/paper(s) of the Examination; no correspondence in this regard shall be entertained.
  • 18Tentative Answer Keys of CBE will be placed on the SSC website after the examination; candidates can submit online representations within the time limit on payment of Rs. 50/- per question (non-refundable).
  • 19Candidates are advised to opt for Aadhaar-Based Authentication during the OTR process.
  • 20The appearance of a candidate at the examination must match the photograph in the application form - no change in appearance is permitted.
  • 21All communications should be addressed to "The Regional Director, Staff Selection Commission (Northern Region), Block No. 12, C.G.O.
