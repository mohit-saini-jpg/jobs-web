Selection Process

  • Stage Details
  • Stage 1 Mandatory pre-registration on www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in with all documents uploaded
  • Stage 2 Walk-in on the respective trade date with all original documents and photocopies
  • Stage 3 Merit-based shortlisting: SSC/10th marks (70% weightage) + ITI marks (30% weightage)
  • Stage 4 Merit list published on www.hal-india.co.in on 26-06-2026; confirmation emails sent to shortlisted candidates
  • Stage 5 Joining / Reporting on 17-07-2026
  • Selection is based only on GPA/CGPA marks secured in SSC/10th (70% weightage) and ITI (30% weightage). There will be no written test or interview. The merit list of selected candidates shall be hosted on www.hal-india.co.in on 26th June 2026 and confirmation emails shall be forwarded to shortlisted candidates subsequently.
  • The date of joining/reporting for the Apprenticeship is 17th July 2026. All provisionally shortlisted candidates must be in a position to join by the prescribed date. If a candidate does not report by the scheduled date
  • it will be assumed that he/she is not interested in the apprenticeship and the candidature will be forfeited. This Apprenticeship Training does not guarantee employment at HAL.

How To Apply

  • Interested and eligible candidates must walk-in on the respective date and reporting time as per the trade-wise schedule at Utsav Sadan Auditorium, Behind Department of Training & Development, Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, Avionics Division, Balanagar, Hyderabad – 500042. There is no separate online application on the HAL website.
  • Before appearing for the walk-in, candidates must register on www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in and upload all required documents. Candidates must enter their name as it appears in the SSC/10th Certificate on the portal. Candidates must also enter a valid email ID and contact mobile number, along with ITI Certificate number, Aadhaar card number, Date of Birth, Category, Council, and Trade details on the web portal. HAL shall not be responsible for any application being rejected or candidate not shortlisted due to incorrect data entered on the portal.
  • Candidates must make their own arrangements for accommodation and travelling for the walk-in process and joining the apprenticeship. No accommodation or travel reimbursement will be provided by HAL.
  • Documents Required at the Time of Walk-in:
  • (a) For Verification of Original Certificates:
  • Aadhaar Card
  • SSC / 10th Marks Certificate
  • ITI Marks Sheet (all semesters) / Certificate
  • Birth Certificate (in case Date of Birth is not mentioned in SSC Certificate)
  • Reservation / Community / Caste Certificate (SC, ST, OBC, EWS, XSM, PWD/PH) – if applicable
  • (b) Documents for Submission:
  • Photo copy / Xerox copy of all the above certificates
  • Apprentice registration copy from apprenticeship portal www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in
  • Two passport size photographs
