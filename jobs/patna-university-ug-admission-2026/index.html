Courses Offered under Patna University UG Admission 2026 (Regular)

Patna University offers a diverse range of undergraduate programmes under the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) and four-year degree structure in Regular mode across various faculties including Arts, Science, Commerce, and Vocational Studies.

Popular programmes include BA (Hons), B.Sc (Hons), B.Com (Hons) and other traditional courses. Students can choose from multiple subjects as per their interest and eligibility. The university provides quality education with experienced faculty and good infrastructure in regular mode. Applicants must check the detailed programme list on the official admission portal before filling the application form.

Courses Offered under Patna University UG Admission 2026 (Self Finance)

Patna University also offers several undergraduate programmes in Self Finance mode. These include professional and vocational courses such as BBA, BCA, BFA and other specialized programmes.

Self-finance courses are designed to meet industry demands with modern curriculum and better placement opportunities. Students seeking these programmes can apply through the same admission portal. Detailed list of self-finance courses is available on the official website pupadm.samarth.edu.in. Candidates are advised to check fee structure and seat availability before applying.
