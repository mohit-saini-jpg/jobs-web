Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the NIT Tiruchirappalli JRF / Project Associate II recruitment?

The last date to submit your application via email is 10th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the concerned authority before this deadline.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the JRF post?

For the JRF position, candidates need a B.E/B.Tech with first class in Mechanical Engineering or M.E/M.Tech with first class in Manufacturing Engineering, Design, Thermal Engineering, or Industrial Safety Engineering, selected through CSIR-UGC NET/GATE or equivalent national-level examinations.

Q3. What is the age limit for the JRF / Project Associate II positions?

The maximum age limit for these positions is 50 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per Government of India norms for JRF candidates.

Q4. What is the salary offered for the JRF / Project Associate II posts?

The selected candidates will receive a monthly salary ranging from Rs. 33,000 to Rs. 37,000, plus HRA, depending on their qualifications and GATE status.

Q5. How can I apply for this recruitment?

Applications are to be submitted via email. You need to prepare your application with all necessary documents and send it to the official email address mentioned in the notification before the last date.

Q6. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.

Q7. What is the selection process for the JRF / Project Associate II posts?

The selection process for these positions will be based on an interview. Candidates shortlisted based on their applications will be called for the interview.

Q8. What is the total number of vacancies available?

There is a total of 1 vacancy for the post of JRF / Project Associate II in this recruitment drive.
