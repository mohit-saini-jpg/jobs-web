MMU ET 2026 UG Result - Quick Overview

MMU ET (UG) 2026 is the entrance test conducted by Marwar Medical University, Jodhpur, for admission to undergraduate and diploma-level health science programmes for the 2026-27 academic session. The exam was held on 3 July 2026, and the result was declared on 20 July 2026. Qualified candidates will now move into online counselling and seat allotment.

Courses Covered Under MMU ET (UG) 2026

MMU ET (UG) 2026 is used for admission to undergraduate and diploma health-science programmes at Marwar Medical University and its affiliated/associated colleges for the 2026-27 session. Based on the official prospectus, confirmed programmes include:

  • Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.Sc. Nursing)
  • Bachelor of Medical Laboratory Science (B.MLS)

Note: The complete, official list of all undergraduate and diploma courses, along with the college-wise and course-wise seat matrix for the 2026-27 session, has not been separately confirmed in the sources available for this article. The university has stated that the seat matrix will be published closer to counselling. Candidates should refer to the official prospectus and counselling notice for the full and final course list.

How to Check and Download the MMU ET 2026 UG Result

  • Go to the official MMU ET 2026 candidate portal at cdn.digialm.com (the configured link shared by the university) or the Rajasthan medical education portal.
  • Click on "Already Registered? Login."
  • Enter your registered application number/login ID and password.
  • Open the Result/Scorecard section.
  • Your MMU ET (UG) 2026 result will be displayed on screen.
  • Download the scorecard PDF and verify all details.
  • Take a printout to keep for the counselling and admission process.

What to Check on Your Scorecard

After downloading the result, verify the following details carefully and report any discrepancy to the university helpdesk immediately:

  • Candidate's name and roll number
  • Application number
  • Category
  • Marks obtained
  • Qualifying status and rank (if shown)
  • Counselling eligibility remarks

MMU ET 2026 Counselling and Seat Allotment

Following the result, Marwar Medical University is set to begin the online counselling process, tentatively scheduled for the fourth week of July 2026, as per the official key-dates schedule. Counselling for MMU ET (UG) generally involves online choice filling, document verification, and seat allotment based on merit rank, category, and seat availability across participating colleges. The full counselling schedule, including registration dates, choice-filling window, and reporting instructions, is expected to be released separately by the university on its official portal. This article will need to be updated once that detailed schedule is officially published, since it was not available at the time of writing.

Important Instructions

  • Use only the official MMU ET 2026 candidate portal to check your result; avoid third-party links claiming to host result data.
  • Keep your login credentials (application number and password) safe, as they are required at every stage — result, counselling, and seat allotment.
  • Cross-check every detail on your scorecard immediately after download.
  • For any discrepancy or technical issue, contact the official helpdesk rather than relying on unofficial sources.
