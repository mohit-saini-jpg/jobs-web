Job Overview

Bhakra Beas Management Board (BBMB) has released the official Apprentice Notification 2026 under Advt. No. 01/2026. A total of 16 vacancies have been announced under the BBMB Apprentice Recruitment 2026. Candidates who have passed 10th Class with a minimum of 40% marks along with an ITI/NCVT/SCVT certificate for ITI trades, or 8th Class for Non-ITI trades such as Gardner and Cook, are eligible to apply online. Interested candidates can submit their applications on or before 04 July 2026. The official notification PDF and online application link are available below.
