According to the official notification issued by GGSIPU, registration for Round 2 is open until July 15, 2026. This article explains the registration process, fee, required documents, and what to expect next in the IPU BTech 2026 admission cycle.

Who Can Register for IPU BTech 2026 Round 2 Counselling?

IPU BTech Round 2 counselling generally allows two categories of candidates to participate:

  • Candidates who registered for Round 1 but did not receive a seat allotment, and vacant seats are now available for allotment.
  • Candidates who were allotted a seat in Round 1 and chose the "Float" option (as opposed to "Freeze"), making them eligible for an upgraded seat in Round 2.

Candidates who chose to freeze their Round 1 seat are generally not part of further seat-upgrade rounds, as freezing confirms final acceptance of the allotted seat. Candidates should verify their specific status by logging into their counselling account, as exact rules can vary by category and programme.

IPU BTech 2026 Round 2 Counselling Fee

Based on the fee structure notified for IPU BTech counselling, candidates participating in the counselling process are required to pay a non-refundable counselling fee. As per the official notification for the counselling process:

  • Requisite Fee: Rs. 1,500
  • Participation Fee: Rs. 1,000
  • Total: Rs. 2,500

Candidates who have already paid the counselling fee during Round 1 registration are typically not required to pay it again for Round 2, unless specifically stated otherwise in the official Round 2 notification. The fee can be paid online via credit card, debit card, or net banking, or offline through an Indian Bank challan, as per the payment modes specified on the official portal.

Eligibility Criteria for IPU BTech 2026

  • Candidates must have a valid JEE Main 2026 Paper 1 score/rank for admission to most BTech programmes offered by GGSIPU and its affiliated institutes.
  • Admission to BTech Biotechnology is based on the IPU CET 2026 merit list, since a separate entrance test is conducted for this programme.
  • Candidates must have passed Class 12 (or equivalent) with the subject combination and minimum marks prescribed in the official GGSIPU admission brochure for the relevant programme.
  • Candidates should refer to the official IPU BTech 2026 admission brochure on ipu.admissions.nic.in for the complete, programme-wise eligibility criteria, as requirements can vary slightly across specializations.

Documents Required for IPU BTech 2026 Round 2 Counselling

Candidates are advised to keep scanned copies of the following documents ready (in the file size and format specified on the portal) before starting Round 2 registration or document verification:

  • JEE Main 2026 admit card and scorecard/rank card
  • Class 10 mark sheet and passing certificate
  • Class 12 mark sheet and passing certificate
  • Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS), if applicable
  • Delhi region/domicile certificate, if applicable
  • PwD (Persons with Disability) certificate, if applicable
  • Aadhaar card or other valid government-issued photo ID
  • Recent passport-size photograph and signature (scanned, as per specified format)

Candidates should refer to the official document checklist published with the Round 2 notification, as specific requirements may vary based on category and programme.

How to Register for IPU BTech 2026 Round 2 Counselling: Step-by-Step Process

  • Visit the official IPU admission portal: ipu.admissions.nic.in.
  • Log in using your existing counselling User ID and password (the same credentials used during Round 1 registration, if applicable).
  • Check your Round 1 status (allotted/not allotted, freeze/float) before proceeding.
  • Complete or update your registration details as required for Round 2 participation.
  • Pay the counselling fee online, if applicable to your case (see fee section above).
  • Fill in or revise your college and branch preferences in order of priority during the choice-filling window.
  • Upload any additional documents requested for Round 2, if prompted.
  • Submit the form and save/download the confirmation for your records.

IPU BTech Counselling Rounds: What Comes After Round 2?

GGSIPU conducts IPU BTech counselling in multiple stages. Here is how the process typically flows after Round 2:

RoundPurpose
Round 1First seat allotment based on JEE Main rank and choice filling. Candidates may freeze, float, or withdraw.
Round 2Re-allotment of seats vacated after Round 1, plus upgrade opportunities for candidates who chose "Float."
Round 3 (Sliding Round)Internal upgrade round for candidates who already have an allotted seat and wish to move to a better college/branch, based on vacancy.
Spot RoundConducted only if seats remain vacant after the Sliding Round; open to fresh candidates and those who missed earlier rounds. Not guaranteed depends on seat availability.

Important Instructions for Candidates

  • Enter your JEE Main 2026 credentials (name, date of birth, roll number) exactly as submitted to the National Testing Agency (NTA) to avoid mismatches during verification.
  • Keep all scanned documents ready in advance in the prescribed format and file size to avoid last-minute upload issues.
  • Regularly check ipu.admissions.nic.in and your registered email/mobile number for counselling updates, as schedules are subject to revision.
  • Beware of touts or unofficial sources promising guaranteed admission; GGSIPU has issued public advisories warning candidates against such fraudulent claims.
  • Fee once paid for counselling participation is non-refundable, except as per the official refund policy.
