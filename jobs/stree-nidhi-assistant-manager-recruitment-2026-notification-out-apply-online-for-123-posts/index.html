Important Instructions

  • Only natives of Telangana state are eligible to apply. Candidates must apply only for their native district. Candidates must possess the prescribed minimum qualification (degree certificate) prior to the date of release of notification. Candidates in their final semester are not eligible. SHG member applicants must not be defaulters in repayment to Stree Nidhi or any bank; outstanding dues must be cleared before applying. Candidates are advised to check/confirm CIBIL score/status before applying. Candidates are advised to visit https://www.streenidhi.telangana.gov.in regularly for all updates regarding the recruitment process.
