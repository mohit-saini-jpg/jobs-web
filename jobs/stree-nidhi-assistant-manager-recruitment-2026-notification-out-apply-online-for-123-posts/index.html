Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is Stree Nidhi Assistant Manager Recruitment 2026?

Stree Nidhi Assistant Manager Recruitment 2026 is a state‑level notification for 123 Assistant Manager (Field Operations) posts in Stree Nidhi Credit Cooperative Federation Ltd, Telangana, under the Department of Rural Development. It offers contract‑based field jobs across 27 districts to support SHG women through microfinance and livelihood promotion.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in Stree Nidhi Assistant Manager Recruitment 2026?

There are a total of 123 vacancies for Assistant Manager (Field Operations) in this recruitment. These posts are distributed district‑wise across all 27 districts of Telangana such as Adilabad, Khammam, Warangal, Rangareddy, Nalgonda, and others as given in Annexure‑I of the notification.

Q3. What is the application date for Stree Nidhi Assistant Manager posts 2026?

The online application and fee payment for Stree Nidhi Assistant Manager Recruitment 2026 will be open from 09 May 2026 (00:00 hrs) to 29 May 2026 (24:00 hrs). Candidates must apply only through the IBPS link available on the official Stree Nidhi website; no other mode of application will be accepted.

Q4. What is the salary of Stree Nidhi Assistant Manager (Field Operations)?

The gross salary for Stree Nidhi Assistant Manager is Rs. 21,600 per month, and the total approximate emoluments are around Rs. 33,960 per month including FTA, DA, vehicle maintenance allowance, performance‑linked variable pay and other admissible allowances. There is also scope for revision of salary in future as per Stree Nidhi HR policy.

Q5. What is the eligibility criteria for Stree Nidhi Assistant Manager Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university with minimum 65% marks for OC, 60% for BC, and 55% for SC/ST/PwD candidates. They must be natives of Telangana, know MS Office, and be able to read, write, and speak both Telugu and English.

Q6. What is the age limit for Stree Nidhi Assistant Manager 2026?

The age limit for Stree Nidhi Assistant Manager is 25 to 30 years for OC/EWS candidates as on 09 May 2026. Upper age is relaxable by 3 years for BC candidates and 5 years for SC/ST/PwD (OH) candidates, subject to maximum age conditions mentioned in the notification.

Q7. How is the selection process for Stree Nidhi Assistant Manager posts?

Selection will be done through a single Online Examination followed by document verification and medical fitness. The online exam consists of 100 objective questions (Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning, English, General Awareness including Banking) for 100 marks in 90 minutes with 1/4 negative marking for each wrong answer.

Q8. Are there any special requirements like two‑wheeler or caution deposit for Stree Nidhi AM?

Yes, selected candidates must be willing to tour at least 20 days per month in their allotted area and must own a two‑wheeler or acquire it within three months of joining along with a valid driving licence within two months. They also need to deposit Rs. 35,000 as caution deposit with Stree Nidhi for two years at the time of joining.
