How to Apply

  • 1Visit upsssc.gov.in → Homepage → Live Advertisement Segment → Click on the relevant advertisement → Download/View the notification.
  • 2Login using PET-2025 Registration Number via OTP.
  • 3Personal details registered in PET-2025 (name, father's/husband's name, mother's name, UP domicile status, DOB, gender, marital status, contact mobile number, email etc.) will be auto-populated.
  • 4Fill in educational qualification details, category/reservation details, and other required information.
  • 5Verify Photo and Signature (as uploaded in PET-2025 - cannot be changed).
  • 6Select the post applied for and indicate preferential/desirable qualification if applicable.
  • 7Complete the Declaration and submit the fee online.
  • 8After successful fee payment/reconciliation, the application will be auto-submitted. Download and print the application form.
  • 9Only Indian Nationals who appeared in PET-2025 and have a valid UPSSSC Score Card are eligible to apply.
  • 10Candidates from UP reserved categories must hold a caste certificate issued by a competent authority valid up to the application date or the last date mentioned in the advertisement.
  • 11EWS candidates must hold an EWS certificate issued between 01-04-2026 and 24-06-2026, based on income of FY 2025-26, valid for FY 2026-27, in the prescribed GoI format.
  • 12Only UP domicile candidates are entitled to reservation benefits; non-UP domicile candidates will be treated as Unreserved.
  • 13Ex-Servicemen who retire on or before the last date of application (24-06-2026) are eligible for reservation.
  • 14For female candidates, caste certificate issued from the father's side only will be accepted.
  • 15If a candidate claims to be a DFF (Dependant of Freedom Fighter), they must have a certificate as per the GO dated 21 April 2015.
  • 16PwBD candidates must hold a disability certificate in the updated format issued as per the UP Government GO dated 18-04-2022.
  • 17Candidates employed in Central/State Government services must obtain a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their employer before participating in the selection process.
  • 18State Government employees in service will get a relaxation of up to 5 years in the upper age limit.
  • 19Date of birth as recorded in the High School / equivalent certificate will be accepted; no other document will be considered for date of birth.
  • 20All future communications will be published on upsssc.gov.in only. Candidates must regularly visit the website. The Commission does not send individual communications.
  • 21If the examination is held across multiple sessions/days, scores will be normalised as per the process notified on 22 May 2019.
  • 22Candidates submitting multiple applications — only the last submitted application will be considered. All previous applications will be treated as cancelled.
  • 23Malpractice, misconduct, or submission of false information at any stage will result in cancellation of candidature and debarment from future examinations as per UP Public Examination (Prevention of Unfair Means) Act-2024, dated 06-08-2024.
  • 24Candidates selected under horizontal (क्षैतिज) reservation will be adjusted in their respective vertical category.
  • 25The Commission's decision regarding eligibility/ineligibility of a candidate shall be final.
