Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for NHSRCL Recruitment 2026?

The last date for online registration and application submission for NHSRCL Recruitment 2026 is 05 June 2026. Candidates are advised to apply well before this date to avoid any last-minute issues.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in NHSRCL Recruitment 2026?

There are a total of 4 vacancies announced for various managerial posts in the NHSRCL Recruitment 2026. These include positions like JGM (Rolling Stock), Senior Manager (Rolling Stock), and Assistant Public Relations Manager.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Assistant Public Relations Manager post?

For the Assistant Public Relations Manager post, candidates need to have a Graduation in any discipline along with a full-time master’s degree in Mass Communication, Public Relations, Journalism, Corporate Communications, Visual communication, Marketing communication, Advertising, or an MBA in a relevant stream.

Q4. What is the maximum age limit for applying to these posts?

The maximum age limit for all the managerial posts announced in NHSRCL Recruitment 2026 is 45 years. Age relaxation is applicable for certain categories as per government norms.

Q5. What is the application fee for NHSRCL Recruitment 2026?

The application fee for General and OBC category candidates is ₹400. However, SC/ST and Women candidates are exempted from paying any application fee for this recruitment.

Q6. What is the salary offered for the JGM (Rolling Stock) position?

The selected candidate for the JGM (Rolling Stock) position will receive a salary in the pay scale of Rs. 90,000 to Rs. 2,40,000 per month (E6 level).

Q7. What is the selection process for NHSRCL Recruitment 2026?

The selection process for NHSRCL Recruitment 2026 includes screening of applications, a presentation on jobs handled, document verification, a personal interview, and a medical examination.

Q8. Can candidates from outside Delhi apply for this recruitment?

The notification mentions that applications are invited from eligible candidates in Delhi. However, it is advisable to check the detailed eligibility criteria in the official notification for any specific domicile requirements.
