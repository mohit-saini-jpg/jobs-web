How to Check GTU Result 2026

Follow the steps given below to check and download your GTU IB Sem 4 Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official GTU result portal: https://www.gtu.ac.in/result.aspx
  • On the result page, search for the IB Sem 4 - Regular (May 2026) result link
  • Click on the relevant Result link to open the GTU result server page
  • Enter your Enrollment Number or Seat Number in the given field
  • Click on Search / View Result to check your subject wise marks and status
  • Download or print the displayed marksheet for future reference
