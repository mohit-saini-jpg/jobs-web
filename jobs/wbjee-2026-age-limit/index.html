WBJEE 2026 Eligibility Criteria require candidates to be Indian citizens (or OCI with conditions) who have passed or are appearing in Class 12 with Physics, Mathematics, and one of Chemistry/Biology/Computer Science etc. The minimum age is 17 years as on 31.12.2026 (born on or before 31.12.2009). There is no upper age limit except for Marine Engineering (25 years). General category candidates need 45% aggregate (40% for reserved) in the three subjects and at least 30% in English. This complete guide helps aspirants understand all requirements for admission to B.Tech, B.Pharm, B.Arch and other courses through WBJEE 2026.

Nationality

Candidates must be Indian citizens. OCI (Overseas Citizen of India) candidates are also eligible but only for Unreserved (General) seats under All India Quota. Foreign nationals are generally not eligible except as per specific government norms.

WBJEE 2026 Eligibility Criteria - Domicile Criteria

  • Residing continuously in West Bengal for at least 10 years as on 31.12.2026, OR
  • Parent(s) must be permanent resident of West Bengal with permanent address.
  • Domicile certificate is mandatory for reserved category seats and Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW) scheme.
  • Candidates from other states can apply but are eligible only for All India Quota / Unreserved seats.

Other Essential Requirements

Candidates must be physically and mentally fit as per the standards set by participating institutes. For Mining/Marine Engineering courses, candidates should not have colour blindness. All admissions are subject to the rules and regulations of the respective universities and councils (AICTE, PCI, COA, etc.).

How to Apply for WBJEE 2026 (Step-by-Step Points)

  • Visit the official website wbjeeb.nic.in.
  • Register using valid email and mobile number.
  • Fill personal, academic and domicile details.
  • Upload scanned documents and photographs.
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Submit the form and download the confirmation page.
