WBJEE 2026 Application Form Correction Window

The WBJEE 2026 Application Form Correction Window opened (10 April 2026 to 12 April 2026). Registered candidates can edit their submitted application form online at the official website wbjeeb.nic.in/wbjee or through the applicant portal.

The West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) has activated the one-time correction facility so that candidates can rectify mistakes in their WBJEE 2026 application form before the final submission. This is the last opportunity to make changes. Most fields can be edited except name, father’s name, mother’s name, domicile, and date of birth. Candidates are advised to log in immediately, review their details carefully, make necessary corrections, and download the revised confirmation page.

How to Correct WBJEE 2026 Application Form (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official website wbjeeb.nic.in/wbjee.
  • Click on the link for Online Application Form Correction for WBJEE 2026.
  • Log in using your Application Form Number and Password.
  • View your submitted application form and identify the fields that need correction.
  • Make the required changes in the editable fields.
  • Review all details carefully and click on Submit.
  • Download and save the revised application confirmation page for future reference.

Important Note: Corrections are allowed only during this window. No requests for correction will be entertained after 12 April 2026 via email, post, or any other mode.

Non-Editable Fields (Cannot be changed):

  • Candidate’s Name
  • Father’s Name
  • Mother’s Name
  • Domicile
  • Date of Birth

Editable Fields (Can be corrected):

  • Reservation Category
  • Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW) status
  • Photograph and Signature
  • PWD (Persons with Disabilities) status
  • Home District
  • Income Category
  • Religion
  • Nationality
  • Aadhaar Card details
  • Contact details (Mobile number, Email ID, Address)
  • Choice of Examination Centres
  • Academic information (wherever allowed)

Important Instructions for WBJEE 2026 Form Correction

  • This is a one-time facility only. Once you submit the corrected form, no further changes will be possible.
  • There is no correction fee for most changes. However, if you change from a reserved category (with fee concession) to General category, you must pay the difference in application fee.
  • The Board will not make any corrections on behalf of candidates.
  • After correction, download the final confirmation page and keep it safely.
  • Incorrect information even after correction may lead to rejection of the application or cancellation of candidature.
