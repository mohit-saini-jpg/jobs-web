How to Check SEEE Phase 2 Results 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your Siddhartha University SEEE Result 2026:

  • Visit the official result portal - https://results.siddhartha.edu.in/
  • Click on the link for "SEEE Phase 2 Results 2026"
  • Enter your application number and date of birth
  • Click on the submit button
  • Your result will be displayed on the screen
  • Download and save the scorecard for future reference
