ESIC Gwalior Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

ESIC Hospital, Tansen Nagar, Gwalior (Madhya Pradesh) has released a walk-in interview notification for contractual engagement of Specialists (Full Time/ Part Time), Senior Residents (3 Years) and Senior Residents (1 Year) across 40 posts. The first walk-in interview will be held on 29 July 2026, and subsequent interviews will be conducted on the 3rd Wednesday of every month. This article covers vacancy details, eligibility, salary, application fee, documents required, and the complete selection process based on the official advertisement (No. ESICH/GWL/Estt/CONTRACTUALMEDICALOFFICER-07//2026)

Eligibility Criteria

For Senior Resident (1 Year and 3 Years): MBBS from an institution recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC)/Medical Council of India/State Medical Council Post-Graduate Diploma/Degree (MD/MS/DNB) in the relevant discipline recognized by NMC/MCI/National Board of Examinations Registration with NMC/Madhya Pradesh Medical Council is mandatory No prior experience needed for PG qualification holders Alternative route: MBBS candidates with 2 years of experience (including at least 1 year in the concerned specialty) from a government or private hospital (not a private clinic) may be appointed for 1 year as Senior Resident, but only if PG-qualified doctors are not available For Full Time/Part Time Specialist: MBBS recognized by NMC/MCI/State Medical Council PG Degree/Diploma (MD/MS/DNB) in the relevant specialty Registration with NMC/Madhya Pradesh Medical Council Experience: 3 years

ESIC Gwalior Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Candidates report at the venue between 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM on the scheduled date with the filled application (Annexure-A) and all documents Document verification is conducted first Only candidates whose documents are verified and found eligible are allowed to appear for the interview Selection is based on the interview and is subject to a medical fitness certificate issued by the Medical Board at ESIC Hospital, Gwalior The decision of the selection board is final

Documents Required for Walk-in Interview

Candidates must bring originals along with one set of self-attested photocopies of the following: Identity Proof (PAN Card, Passport, Driving License, Voter Card, Aadhaar Card) Address Proof (Ration Card, Passport, Driving License, Aadhaar Card) Date of Birth Certificate (Class 10th Certificate) Two recent passport-size photographs Class 10th and 12th mark sheets/certificates Proof of Educational Qualification (MBBS, MD/MS/DNB, etc. as per NMC guidelines) Internship Completion Certificate NMC/Madhya Pradesh Medical Council Registration and Renewal Madhya Pradesh Medical Council Registration (within 30 days of joining, if not already registered) Additional Medical Qualification Registration Certificate Experience Certificates (where applicable) Reservation Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PH) in the Government of India prescribed format; OBC certificate must show non-creamy layer status and be

ESIC Gwalior Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1When is the ESIC Hospital Gwalior walk-in interview 2026?
A
The first walk-in interview is scheduled for 29 July 2026, with reporting time between 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM. Subsequent interviews will be held on the 3rd Wednesday of every month.
Q2How many vacancies are there in ESIC Hospital Gwalior recruitment 2026?
A
There are 40 total vacancies: 17 for Specialist (Full Time/Part Time), 14 for Senior Resident (3 Years), and 9 for Senior Resident (1 Year).
Q3Is there an online application process for ESIC Gwalior recruitment?
A
No. This is a walk-in interview. Candidates must fill the prescribed Annexure-A application form and appear in person with original documents at the ESIC Hospital, Gwalior on the scheduled date.
Q4What is the age limit for ESIC Hospital Gwalior Senior Resident and Specialist posts?
A
The maximum age is 45 years for Senior Resident posts and 67 years for Specialist posts, as on the date of interview. Age relaxation applies for SC/ST/OBC/PWD/Ex-servicemen as per DoPT rules.
Q5What is the application fee for ESIC Gwalior recruitment 2026?
A
SC/ST/Female/Ex-Servicemen/PH candidates are exempted from the fee. For other categories, the notification mentions Rs. 500/- in the main text and Rs. 300/- in the application annexure — candidates should confirm the exact amount on the official website before preparing the Demand Draft.
Q6What is the salary for Senior Resident and Specialist at ESIC Hospital Gwalior?
A
Senior Residents and Full Time Specialists are paid at Pay Level-11 with a basic pay of Rs. 67,700/- plus allowances. Part Time Specialists receive Rs. 60,000/- per month plus Rs. 15,000/- for emergency call availability, with extra hourly pay for additional work.
Q7Is TA/DA provided for attending the ESIC Gwalior interview?
A
No. The notification clearly states that no TA/DA will be admissible for attending the walk-in interview or for joining the post.
