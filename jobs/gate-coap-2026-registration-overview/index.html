GATE COAP 2026 Registration - Overview

GATE COAP (Common Offer Acceptance Portal) 2026 is a centralized online platform where GATE‑qualified candidates can see, compare, and accept or reject M.Tech/M.Plan/M.Des offers from participating Indian Institutes of Technology. It does not itself grant admission but works as a decision platform that connects your GATE scores to offers made by the IITs.

GATE COAP 2026 Eligibility Criteria

This section answers “who should register for COAP 2026”.

GATE Score Requirements

  • You must have a valid GATE score in at least one of the following years: 2024, 2025, or 2026.
  • If you have multiple valid GATE scores (for example GATE 2025 and GATE 2026), you should use the scorecard with the higher score or better rank while registering.

Other Basic Conditions

  • COAP is meant for Indian citizens; foreign nationals usually apply directly to IITs without using COAP.
  • COAP registration is compulsory for candidates seeking M.Tech/M.Plan/M.Des admission to participating IITs through GATE, because the offers are displayed only through this portal.
  • COAP does not replace the individual IIT application: you still need to fill separate programme‑wise forms on each IIT’s admission portal.

Details You Must Have

  • GATE Registration/Enrollment ID.

  • Full name, date of birth, category, and other personal details exactly as in the GATE form.

Useful Documents to Keep Ready

  • PDF / screenshot of the latest GATE scorecard.
  • A list of IITs and programmes you are planning to apply to (for later use on IIT portals, not at COAP).

Step‑by‑Step GATE COAP 2026 Registration Process

  • Visit the COAP 2026 portal Open your browser and go to the official COAP 2026 website (coap2026.iitr.ac.in/coap2026/).
  • Click on “Register” / “New User Registration” On the home page, click the registration button for new candidates.
  • Enter GATE details Fill in your GATE Registration/Enrollment ID, GATE paper code, year of exam, and GATE score as printed on the scorecard.
  • Fill personal information Enter your name, date of birth, category, email ID, and mobile number exactly as per your GATE records to avoid mismatch issues.
  • Create login credentials Set a strong password, confirm it, and enter the captcha/security code shown on screen.
  • Submit and verify Submit the registration form. Note down the COAP 2026 Registration ID displayed on screen and sent to your email/mobile.
  • Login to COAP account Use your COAP Registration ID and password to log in, verify your details, and keep the credentials safely for use during every round.

What to Do After GATE COAP 2026 Registration

Many students are confused after registering, so this section adds real value.

Two Parallel Steps for IIT M.Tech Admission

  • Step 1: Register on COAP (this portal). This is required only once and creates your central offer‑view account.
  • Step 2: Apply to individual IITs separately. Visit each participating IIT’s admission website (IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, IIT Madras, IIT Roorkee, etc.), fill their M.Tech/M.Plan/M.Des application forms, pay the institute‑specific fee, and mention your GATE details and COAP registration where asked.

COAP will show offers only from those institutes and programmes where you have already applied directly and been considered based on GATE.

Using COAP 2026 in Every Round

  • During each active round window, log in to the COAP portal with your Registration ID and password.
  • Check all the offers displayed from different IITs and programmes (if any).
  • For each round, choose one of the decision options provided on the portal, commonly: Accept & Freeze - Accept one offer and exit further COAP rounds for that programme. Retain & Wait - Keep your current best offer but remain eligible for upgrade in later rounds. Reject & Wait - Reject the current offer(s) and wait for potential new/ better offers in the next rounds.
  • Accept & Freeze - Accept one offer and exit further COAP rounds for that programme.
  • Retain & Wait - Keep your current best offer but remain eligible for upgrade in later rounds.
  • Reject & Wait - Reject the current offer(s) and wait for potential new/ better offers in the next rounds.
  • Save and submit your choice before that round’s deadline.
  • After you Accept & Freeze an offer, complete admission formalities (fee payment, document upload, reporting) directly on that IIT’s portal.
