Selection Process

  • Selection for Young Professional engagements will be based on: (i) Application scrutiny for eligibility; (ii) Computer Based Test (CBT)/Aptitude/General Management/Psychometric Test if applications received are more than 5 times the requirement (shortlisting ratio 1:3 based on test performance); (iii) Personal Interview if applications are fewer than 5 times the requirement - all eligible candidates called for interview. Final merit list is prepared with CBT:Interview weightage of 80:20. A discipline-wise panel (1:1 ratio of selected to waitlisted) will be maintained for 6 months for non-joining/early exit cases.
  • Stage 1 - Application Scrutiny
  • All applications received by the closing date (06-06-2026) will be scrutinized for fulfilment of eligibility criteria. Candidature is purely provisional and will be verified against original documents at the time of Interview
  • Pre-Employment Medical Examination
  • and Joining.
  • Stage 2 - Computer Based Test (CBT) / Shortlisting Test (if applicable)
  • If applications received against any engagement exceed 5 times the requirement
  • a Computer Based Test/Aptitude/General Management/Psychometric Test or any standard test/exam will be organized. Shortlisting for interview will be in the ratio of 1:3 based on test performance. If the cut-off marks are obtained by more candidates
  • all of them will be called for interview. CBT carries 80% weightage in the final merit list.
  • Stage 3 - Personal Interview
  • Shortlisted/all eligible candidates will be called for personal interview at centres spread across the country. Interview carries 20% weightage in the final merit list. No request for change in date or centre of interview will be entertained. Candidates from PSU/Govt. Departments/Autonomous Bodies must produce NOC in original from the employer at the time of interview.
  • Stage 4 - Pre-Employment Medical Examination & Joining
  • Joining of selected candidates is subject to medical fitness. Selected candidates must join within 30 days from the offer of engagement - no extension permitted. Every selected candidate must enter into a Contract for Service agreement with the respective SAIL Plant before joining.
