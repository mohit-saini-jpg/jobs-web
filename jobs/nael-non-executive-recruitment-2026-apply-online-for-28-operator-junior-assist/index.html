Job Overview

Naini Aerospace Limited (NAeL), a wholly owned subsidiary of HAL, has released its Non-Executive Recruitment 2026 notification (Advertisement No. NAeL/Rectt./26/02) inviting online applications for 28 posts across Operator SS and Junior Assistant categories on a Fixed Term Employment (FTE) basis of 4 years. The online application window opened on 27 June 2026 and closes on 17 July 2026. All posts are based at Naini, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh.

  • 1All applications must be submitted online only through the official NAeL website. No applications by post, email, or in person are accepted.
  • 2Visit the official NAeL website: https://www.nael.co.in
  • 3Click on the Recruitment or Careers section and find Advertisement No. NAeL/Rectt./26/02.
  • 4Register with a valid email ID and mobile number. Ex-Apprentices of NAeL: use your Contract No., Apprenticeship No., Date of Birth, and training period to authenticate. Sponsored candidates (Employment Exchange / Sainik Board): enter the NAeL reference letter number and unique code while applying.
