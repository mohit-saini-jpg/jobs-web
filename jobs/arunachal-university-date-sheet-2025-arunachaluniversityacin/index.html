How to Download the Arunachal University Date Sheet 2025?

Arunachal University Date Sheet 2025, students need to access the official university website, locate the examination datesheet section, and select their course and session for the PDF download.​

  • Visit the official Arunachal University of Studies website at arunachaluniversity.ac.in.​
  • On the homepage, find and click the “Examination” or “Date Sheet” section.​
  • Click over “Examination Datesheet” or search for “Academic 2025 Examinations” in the announcements or news tab.​
  • A page will open displaying various courses and examination schedules for 2025 select your required course and semester.​
  • Click the link corresponding to the date sheet for your course/session; it will typically be a downloadable PDF.​
  • Download and save the PDF date sheet, then print if needed for exam preparation.
