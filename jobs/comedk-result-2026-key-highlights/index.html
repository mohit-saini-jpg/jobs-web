The Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMEDK) has officially released the COMEDK UGET Result 2026 for admission to undergraduate engineering courses in participating colleges across Karnataka. The UGET 2026 scorecard and rank card are now available online for candidates who appeared for the entrance test held on 9 May 2026. COMEDK Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - comedk.org.

Candidates can check and download their COMEDK UGET 2026 results by logging in with their application sequence number and password on the result portal. After logging in, they can view their subject-wise scores, total marks, and overall rank, and download the rank card for counselling and admission purposes. Students are advised to carefully verify personal details, scores and ranks mentioned on the scorecard and contact COMEDK authorities in case of any discrepancy.

COMEDK Result 2026 Key Highlights

How to Check COMEDK Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download COMEDK UGET Result 2026 in an easy and error-free way.

  • Visit the official COMEDK website at comedk.org.
  • On the homepage, click on the COMEDK UGET Result 2026 link or applicant login link.
  • You will be redirected to the result login page.
  • Enter your COMEDK application sequence number and password in the given fields.
  • Click on the login button to access your dashboard.
  • Your COMEDK UGET 2026 scorecard with subject-wise marks, total score and rank will be displayed on the screen.
  • Carefully check all details mentioned on the scorecard.
  • Download and save the COMEDK rank card as a PDF and take a printout for counselling and future reference.
