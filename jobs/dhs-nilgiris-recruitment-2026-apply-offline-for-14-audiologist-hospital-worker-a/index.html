Job Overview

District Health Society Nilgiris (DHS Nilgiris) has officially released the Audiologist, Hospital Worker and More Posts Recruitment 2026 notification for 14 vacancies in The Nilgiris district of Tamil Nadu. Qualification / Eligibility

Radiographer: B.Sc.,/ Diploma in Radiographer Course Hospital Worker: Minimum 8th standard and Must able to read and write in Tamil. Audiologists & Speech Therapists: A Bachelor degree in audiology and speech language pathology/B.SC(Speech and hearing) From RCI Recognized institute Health Inspector: +2 science Group Complete, Health Inspector Training. Lab Technician: Must have passed plus two Examination. Must possess, certificate in Medical Laboratory Technology Course (One Year duration) undergone in any institution recognized by the Director of Medical Education. Must have a good physique, good vision and capacity to do outdoor work. Audio Metrician: High School and Diploma in Audiometrician or equivalent, complete a certificate programme Physio therapist: Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) from any recognized university Audiologist: A Bachelor degree in audiology and speech language pathology/ B.SC (Speech and hearing) From RCI Recognized institute.
