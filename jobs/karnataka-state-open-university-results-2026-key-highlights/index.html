How to Download KSOU Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download KSOU Result 2026 online from the official portal:

  • Visit the official KSOU result portal at https://ksouportal.com/results/ksouresult.aspx.
  • On the results page, look for the latest links for I Year BA or I Semester MA Sociology Oct/Nov 2025 provisional revaluation results.
  • Click on the relevant result link to proceed to the result access page.
  • Enter your registration number or other required login credentials in the given fields.
  • Submit the details to view your KSOU Result 2026 on the screen.
  • Download or print the provisional result for future reference and verification.
  • For any queries related to internal assessment, contact the Registrar Evaluation or KSOU Academic Platform as notified on the result page.
