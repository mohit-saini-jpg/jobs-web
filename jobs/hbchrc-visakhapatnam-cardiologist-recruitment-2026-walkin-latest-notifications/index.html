Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for the Cardiologist post?

The walk-in interview for the Cardiologist post at HBCHRC Visakhapatnam is scheduled for 28 May 2026. Candidates should ensure they attend within the specified time.

Q2. What are the essential qualifications required for the Cardiologist position?

Candidates must possess a D.M (Cardiology) or an Equivalent Degree recognized by MCI. A minimum of 1 year of post-qualification experience is desirable.

Q3. How many vacancies are available for the Cardiologist post?

There are a total of 4 vacancies for the Cardiologist post at Homi Bhabha Cancer Hospital & Research Centre, Visakhapatnam.

Q4. What is the application mode for this recruitment?

The application mode for this recruitment is Walk-in. Interested and eligible candidates need to attend the interview in person with all required documents.

Q5. What is the salary offered for the Cardiologist position?

The salary offered for the Cardiologist position is Rs.500/- per consultation. This is a temporary position.

Q6. Where is the walk-in interview scheduled to take place?

The walk-in interview will take place at the HRD Department, first floor, Homi Bhabha Cancer Hospital & Research Centre, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, 530053.

Q7. What documents should candidates bring for the walk-in interview?

Candidates should bring their updated resume, recent passport size photograph, photocopies of Pan Card and Aadhar card, original certificates, and one set of self-attested copies of all certificates.

Q8. Is there any age limit mentioned for the Cardiologist post?

The official notification does not explicitly mention an age limit. Candidates are advised to refer to the detailed notification for any specific age criteria or relaxations.
