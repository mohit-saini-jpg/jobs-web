Job Overview

Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has officially released the Bihar Home Guard Company Commander Recruitment 2026 notification (Advertisement No. 08/2026) for 65 vacancies of Gulm Samadeshak (Company Commander) under the Bihar Home Guard Service Cadre, Home Department (Special Branch), Bihar, Patna. Male, female, and third-gender candidates who hold a graduation degree from a recognised university as of 01 August 2025 are eligible to apply. Qualification / Eligibility

Details
  1. Candidates must meet both educational and physical eligibility criteria to apply for the Bihar Home Guard Company Commander Vacancy 2026. All criteria are calculated with reference to the cut-off date of 01 August 2025
  2. The candidate must be an Indian national. Male, female, and third-gender (Kinnar/Kothi/Hijra/Transgender) candidates are all eligible to apply
  3. Candidates must have passed a Bachelor's Degree (Graduation) from a recognized university or an examination equivalent thereto as recognised by the State Government, on or before 01 August 2025
  4. Age is determined on the basis of the date of birth recorded in the Matriculation (10th) or equivalent certificate. Candidates wondering about age relaxation can note that SC/ST and Third-Gender candidates receive the maximum relaxation of up to 42 years
  5. No marks are awarded for height, chest, or weight, but candidates who fail to meet the prescribed minimums will be declared ineligible at the Physical Efficiency Test (PET) stage
  6. A minimum expansion of 5 cm is mandatory for all male candidates. Chest measurement is not applicable for female candidates
  7. All female candidates must have a minimum weight of 48 kg
  8. Physical standards and PET criteria for Kinnar/Kothi/Hijra/Transgender candidates shall be the same as applicable to Backward Class (BC) candidates. Gender shall be determined as per the certificate issued by the District Magistrate under the Transgender Persons (Protection of Rights) Rules, 2020

FAQs

Q1Has BPSSC officially released the Bihar Home Guard Company Commander Recruitment 2026 notification?
A
Yes. BPSSC has officially released the Bihar Home Guard Company Commander Recruitment 2026 notification vide Advertisement No. 08/2026 on 25 June 2026, inviting online applications for 65 vacancies of Gulm Samadeshak (Company Commander) under the Bihar Home Guard Service Cadre.
Q2What is the last date to apply for Bihar Home Guard Company Commander Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to apply online is 30 July 2026. No extension of this deadline will be granted under any circumstances. The online application window opens on 30 June 2026.
Q3How many vacancies are available in BPSSC Home Guard Company Commander Bharti 2026?
A
A total of 65 vacancies are available for the post of Gulm Samadeshak (Company Commander). These are distributed across General (26), SC (10), ST (01), EBC (12), BC (07), EWS (07), and BC Women (02) categories.
Q4What is the educational qualification required for Bihar Home Guard Gulm Samadeshak Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must hold a Bachelor's Degree (Graduation) from a recognized university or an equivalent examination recognised by the Bihar State Government, completed on or before 01 August 2025.
Q5What is the age limit for Bihar Home Guard Company Commander Vacancy 2026?
A
The minimum age is 20 years for all categories. The maximum age is 37 years for General males, 40 years for General females and BC/EBC candidates, and 42 years for SC/ST candidates and Third-Gender candidates. All ages are calculated as on 01 August 2025.
Q6What is the application fee for BPSSC Bihar Home Guard Company Commander Apply Online 2026?
A
The application fee is ₹100 for all categories including General, SC, ST, OBC, EWS, male, female, and third gender. Payment must be made online through Net Banking, Credit/Debit Card, or UPI only.
Q7What is the selection process for Bihar Home Guard Company Commander Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection consists of three stages: (1) Preliminary Written Exam (200 marks, 100 MCQs, 2 hours — General Knowledge & Current Affairs), (2) Main Written Exam (two papers — General Hindi qualifying paper, and General Studies merit paper, each 200 marks), and (3) Physical Efficiency Test (Run, High Jump, Long Jump, Shot Put). Final merit is based solely on Main Exam Paper II marks.
Q8Is there negative marking in the BPSSC Home Guard Company Commander exam?
A
Yes. A deduction of 0.2 marks applies for every incorrect answer in both the Preliminary Exam and the Main Exam. There is no negative marking for unattempted questions.
Q9What is the Pay Scale / Salary for Bihar Home Guard Company Commander post?
A
Selected candidates will be appointed at Pay Level-6 under the Bihar Home Guard Service Cadre as per the state government's pay matrix. Additional allowances as applicable to Bihar government employees will also be payable.
Q10What is the official website to apply for BPSSC Bihar Home Guard Company Commander Recruitment 2026?
A
The official website is https://bpssc.bihar.gov.in. Candidates must click on the "Home Guard" tab and then select the link for Advt. No. 08/2026 to begin the online application process.
