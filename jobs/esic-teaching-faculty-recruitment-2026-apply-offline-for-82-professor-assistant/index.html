FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for ESIC Medical College Ranchi Teaching Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for submission of applications is 12 June 2026. Applications should reach the office of the Dean, ESIC Medical College and Hospital, Namkum, Ranchi by this date. Submission by email is strongly recommended for speedy delivery. Applications received after the last date will not be entertained.
Q2How many vacancies are available in ESIC Medical College Ranchi Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 82 vacancies are available — Professor (10), Associate Professor (17), Assistant Professor (15), and Senior Resident (40) — across 25 departments including Anaesthesiology, Medicine, Surgery, Paediatrics, Radiology, OBG, Orthopaedics, Medical Oncology, and others. Vacancies are provisional and may increase or decrease.
Q3What is the salary for Teaching Faculty at ESIC Medical College Ranchi 2026?
A
The consolidated remuneration is: Professor — ₹1,23,100/- per month (Level 13 of 7th CPC); Associate Professor — ₹78,800/- per month (Level 12 of 7th CPC); Assistant Professor — ₹67,700/- per month (Level 11 of 7th CPC); and Senior Resident — ₹67,700/- per month (Level 11 of 7th CPC), plus other allowances as applicable.
Q4What is the contract duration for ESIC Medical College Ranchi Teaching Faculty posts?
A
The contractual engagement is for 1 year, extendable every year up to a maximum of 3 years subject to satisfactory performance, or till the joining of the regular incumbent, whichever is earlier. The maximum tenure shall not exceed attainment of 70 years of age. There is no claim for regularisation of service.
Q5What is the application fee for ESIC Medical College Ranchi Recruitment 2026?
A
SC/ST candidates, Female candidates, Defence Ex-Servicemen, and PH candidates are exempt from the application fee. All other categories must pay ₹500/- through a Demand Draft in favour of ESIC Saving Fund A/c No.-2, payable at Namkum, to be submitted along with the application form. Fees once paid will not be refunded.
Q6What are the tentative interview dates for ESIC Medical College Ranchi Teaching Faculty 2026?
A
The tentative interview dates are 15-06-2026 or 16-06-2026 (Third week of June 2026). The exact date along with the list of candidates and departments will be published on www.esic.gov.in/recruitments/ and communicated directly to candidates via email after scrutiny of documents submitted by the closing date
Q7Is private practice allowed during the contractual period at ESIC Medical College Ranchi?
A
No. Private practice of any kind is strictly prohibited during the contractual engagement. Selected candidates will not be allowed to work simultaneously as Teaching Faculty in any other Hospital or Medical College. Breach of this condition will result in termination of appointment and initiation of proceedings with NMC/SMC for cancellation of Registration Certificate.
Q8What documents are required for ESIC Medical College Ranchi Teaching Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Self-attested copies required: Class-X marksheet/certificate (date of birth), MBBS marksheets/degree certificate, PG (MD/MS/DNB) marksheets/degree certificate, EWS/OBC/SC/ST/PwD certificate (Central Govt. format), passport size photograph, NMC/State Medical Council Registration Certificate (updated), proof of publications, experience certificates, ICMR/NMC bio-medical research completion certificate (if any), NMC Basic Medical Education Workshop certificate (if any), Relieving/NOC from employer (if applicable), Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, and Demand Draft (if applicable).
Q9What is the security deposit requirement for ESIC Medical College Ranchi 2026?
A
Selected candidates must submit a non-interest bearing security deposit of ₹30,000/- (Rupees Thirty Thousand) in the form of a Demand Draft in favour of 'ESIC Saving Fund Account No.2' payable at Namkum at the time of joining. This amount is refundable after successful completion of the contract upon production of a 'No Dues Certificate'.
Q10Can retired employees or pensioners apply for ESIC Medical College Ranchi Teaching Faculty 2026?
A
Yes. Retired employees/pensioners may apply and if engaged, they may be allowed to draw total pay without any deduction. This is not treated as re-employment under CCS (Fixation of Pay of Re-employed Pensioner) Orders, 1986. They can continue to draw their pension and dearness relief on pension during the period of contractual engagement.
