What is VMSBUTU Time Table 2026?

The VMSBUTU Time Table 2026 is the official examination schedule released by Veer Madho Singh Bhandari Uttarakhand Technical University for the academic year 2025-26. This document contains crucial information including:

  • Exact exam dates for each subject/paper
  • Exam timing and duration
  • Course-wise and semester-wise schedule
  • Regular and back paper exam dates
  • Important instructions for students

Students appearing for B.Pharm & M.Pharm 1st Year Odd Semester examinations must download the Veer Madho Singh Bhandari Uttarakhand Technical University time table to prepare their study schedule effectively.

How to Download VMSBUTU Time Table 2026? (Step-by-Step)

Follow these simple steps to download your VMSBUTU exam date sheet 2026:

Method 1: Direct Download from Official Website

  • Visit Official Website: Go to uktech.ac.in
  • Navigate to Examination Section: Look for "Examinations" or "Circulars" section on homepage
  • Click on Time Table/Date Sheet: Find "Examination Schedule" or "Time Table" link
  • Select Academic Year: Choose 2025-26 or current session
  • Choose Your Course: Select B.Pharm & M.Pharm or your specific course
  • Select Exam Type: Regular/Back Paper
  • Download PDF: Click on the download button to save the time table
  • Save and Print: Save the PDF and take a printout for reference
