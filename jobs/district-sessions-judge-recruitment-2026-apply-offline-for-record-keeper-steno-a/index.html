A
There are a total of 8 vacancies announced for various posts in the District & Sessions Judge West Jaintia Hills. These include Stenographer Gr-III, Assistant Librarian, Lower Division Clerk-Cum-Bench Assistant, Record Keeper, and Process Server.
Q3What is the minimum educational qualification required for these posts?
A
The minimum educational qualification ranges from SSLC passed for posts like Record Keeper and Process Server, up to a Bachelor's Degree with a Diploma in Stenography for the Stenographer Gr-III post.
Q4What is the age limit for applying to these positions?
A
The age limit for candidates applying for these posts is between 18 to 32 years. Age relaxation is provided for SC/ST candidates, who can avail of a 5-year relaxation.

A
The application fee for General category candidates is Rs. 300/-, while for SC & ST category candidates, it is Rs. 150/-. This fee is payable by Demand Draft.
Q6What is the selection process for the advertised posts?
A
The selection process for the various posts in the District & Sessions Judge West Jaintia Hills will involve a Written Examination followed by a Personal Interview. Candidates must clear both stages to be considered for appointment.
Q7How can I apply for the District & Sessions Judge West Jaintia Hills recruitment?
A
Applications must be submitted in offline mode. Candidates need to fill out the standard application form, attach necessary documents, and send it to the specified address before the last date of application.
Q8What is the salary structure for the selected candidates?

The salary structure varies based on the post. It ranges from Level-1 (Rs. 17,400/-) for posts like Record Keeper and Process Server, to Level-5 (Rs. 24,700/-) for Assistant Librarian and LDC-Cum-Bench Assistant, and Level-8 (Rs. 30,300/-) for Stenographer Gr-III.
Q9Where can I find the official notification and application form?

The official notification and application form can typically be downloaded from the official website of the District & Sessions Judge West Jaintia Hills or through the direct links provided in the 'Important Links' section of this article.
