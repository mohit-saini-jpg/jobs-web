DetailsResearch Associate-III (Ecology & Sustainable Land Management): Ph.D. in Forestry / Ecology / Environmental Science / Natural Resource Management / Restoration Ecology or related disciplines OR M.Sc. in Forestry / Ecology / Environmental Science / Natural Resource Management or related disciplines with NET (CSIR/UGC/ASRB) and a minimum of 5 years of research / project experience. Junior Research Fellow (JRF): Postgraduate degree in Forestry / Environmental Science / Ecology / Botany / GIS / Information Technology or related disciplines with NET (UGC/CSIR/ASRB) / GATE.
