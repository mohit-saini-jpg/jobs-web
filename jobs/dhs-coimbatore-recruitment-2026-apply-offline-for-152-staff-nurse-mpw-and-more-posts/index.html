How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply in the prescribed application form only. Application forms can be downloaded from https://coimbatore.nic.in. The duly filled application form along with self-attested copies of all required documents and a passport-size photograph affixed on the application must be submitted either by hand or through Speed Post to the address below on or before 15-06-2026 by 05:00 PM. At the time of joining, selected candidates must submit a self-undertaking letter and an 11-month contract agreement.
  • Documents to be Attached with Application (self-attested copies):
  • Duly filled prescribed application form with passport-size photograph affixed and full address with mobile number
  • Proof of age (Date of Birth Certificate)
  • Educational qualification certificates and mark sheets
  • Community / Caste Certificate (self-attested copy)
  • Residence proof (Ration Card / Aadhaar Card - self-attested copy)
  • Experience certificates (wherever required as per post eligibility)
  • Registration Certificate with Tamil Nadu Medical Council (for Medical Officer post)
  • Any other document as specified in the notification for the respective post
  • Applications received without the above documents, or received after the deadline, or submitted in any format other than the prescribed form, will be rejected without any communication.
  • Send Application to: Member Secretary / District Health Officer, Maavatta Nalavalvu Sangam (District Health Society), District Health Office, 219, Race Course Road, Coimbatore - 18.
  • For application form download: https://coimbatore.nic.in
