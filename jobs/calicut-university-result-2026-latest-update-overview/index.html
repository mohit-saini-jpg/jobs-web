Calicut University Result 2026: Latest Update Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 02/07/2026

The University of Calicut has released the Calicut University Result 2026 for the Combined I & II Semester B.Arch examinations on July 1, 2026. Students who appeared for the B.Arch Supplementary exam (2017 Scheme) or the Regular/Supplementary/Improvement exam (2022 Scheme) can check and download their results directly from the official portal, results.uoc.ac.in.

This update covers two separate B.Arch batches, and knowing exactly which one applies to you is the fastest way to find your result without confusion. Below is a complete, easy-to-follow breakdown of what has been released, how to check your score, and what to do next.

Which B.Arch Batches Are Covered in This Result?

Two distinct groups of B.Arch students have results published in this release. Identifying your batch correctly ensures you select the right link on the results portal.

1. Combined I & II Semester B.Arch Supplementary Exam (2017 Scheme)

This result is for students admitted under the 2017 Scheme (2017-21 Admission) who appeared for the Combined I & II Semester B.Arch Supplementary Examination held in April 2026. This is typically meant for students who could not clear one or both semesters in their earlier regular attempts and appeared for the supplementary round to complete their pending papers.

2. Combined I & II Semester B.Arch Regular/Supplementary/Improvement Exam (2022 Scheme)

This result covers students under the 2022 Scheme (2022-2025 Admission) who appeared for the Combined I & II Semester examination in May 2026, under any of the three categories:

  • Regular - First-time appearance for the semester exam
  • Supplementary - Reattempting previously failed papers
  • Improvement - Reattempting to improve an already-passed score

How to Check Calicut University Result 2026 Online

Checking your B.Arch result on the Calicut University portal takes just a few steps. This section walks you through the exact process to view and download your marksheet.

  • Visit the official result portal: results.uoc.ac.in
  • From the list of courses, locate and select B.Tech / B.Arch.
  • Click on the relevant result link: Combined I&II Semester B.Arch Supplementary Examination (2017 Scheme, 2017-21 Admission) April 2026, or Combined I&II Semester B.Arch Regular/Supplementary/Improvement Examination (2022 Scheme, 2022-2025 Admission) May 2026
  • Combined I&II Semester B.Arch Supplementary Examination (2017 Scheme, 2017-21 Admission) April 2026, or
  • Combined I&II Semester B.Arch Regular/Supplementary/Improvement Examination (2022 Scheme, 2022-2025 Admission) May 2026
  • Enter your Register Number in the field provided.
  • Enter the security code/captcha displayed on the screen, if applicable.
  • Click Submit to view your result.
  • Download or print your provisional marksheet for future reference.

What to Do If You Face Issues Viewing Your Result

Occasionally, students encounter errors while checking results online. This section covers the most common issues and how to resolve them.

  • Double-check your Register Number - a single incorrect digit is the most common reason for a "no record found" error.
  • Try during off-peak hours - the portal can slow down due to heavy traffic right after a result is declared.
  • Clear your browser cache or try a different browser/device if the page fails to load.
  • Contact your college examination cell or the University's Pareeksha Bhavan (examination branch) helpdesk if the issue persists.

Revaluation, Scrutiny, and Photocopy Applications

If you're not satisfied with your marks, Calicut University allows you to apply for a review after results are published. This section explains what options are available.

After the Combined I&II Semester B.Arch results are declared, eligible students can apply online for:

  • Revaluation - Re-checking of the answer script by an examiner
  • Scrutiny - Verification of totaling and marking on the answer script
  • Photocopy - Obtaining a copy of your evaluated answer script

These applications must be submitted within the time window specified in the official notification, along with the prescribed fee, through the university's online application system.
