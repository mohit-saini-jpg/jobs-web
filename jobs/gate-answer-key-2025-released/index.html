GATE Answer Key 2025 - GATE Answer Key 2025 has been released by the IIT Roorkee soon on its official website through Online mode for gate2025.iitr.ac.in. The Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee (GATE) was carried out by the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee Authority. And officials will also announce the GATE 2025 Provisional Answer Key on 27-02-2025. How to Download the GATE Answer Key 2025?

Candidates can follow the below steps to check and download GATE Answer Key 2025.

  • Visit the official website of GATE - gate2025.iitr.ac.in.
  • Search and click on the notification “Answer keys released” on the homepage.
  • Provide Roll Number and Date of Birth.
  • Click on login.
  • GATE Answer Key 2025 will appear on the screen.
  • Download and save it for future references
