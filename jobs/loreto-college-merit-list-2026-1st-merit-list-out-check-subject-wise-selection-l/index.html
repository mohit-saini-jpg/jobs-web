How to Check Loreto College Merit List 2026

Candidates can check their name in the Loreto College 1st Merit List 2026 by following the simple steps below. The merit list is published subject-wise and category-wise on the official admission portal.

  • Visit the official admission portal: admissionug.in/LoretoCollege-Admission/merit_list_new.php
  • On the Merit List page, click on the subject-wise merit list link of your choice.
  • The category-wise student list for that subject will open on screen.
  • Search for your name or application number in the list.
  • If selected, proceed to pay the admission fee within the specified dates.
