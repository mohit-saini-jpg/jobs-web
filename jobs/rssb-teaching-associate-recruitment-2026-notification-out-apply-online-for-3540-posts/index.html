How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the SSO Portal.
  • Step-by-Step Application Process:
  • Visit the official SSO Portal: http://sso.rajasthan.gov.in
  • Login with your SSO ID (or create new if not registered).
  • Complete One Time Registration (OTR) if not done earlier and pay the OTR fee.
  • Go to Citizen Apps (G2C) → Recruitment Portal.
  • Click on Apply Now for Advertisement No. 06/2026.
  • Fill all required details carefully (Personal, Educational, Subject Preference etc.).
  • Upload scanned documents as per specifications.
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Preview, verify all details, and submit the application.
  • Take a printout of the final submitted application form for future reference.
  • Important: Candidates must ensure all information filled is correct. No correction window may be provided after final submission.
  • Apply Online at: SSO Portal → Recruitment Portal

Important Instructions

  • Read the full detailed advertisement carefully before filling the online application. Only online applications will be accepted. Upload clear scanned copies of all required documents. Keep your SSO ID, email ID, and mobile number active. No offline or manual applications will be entertained. Candidates are advised to apply well before the last date to avoid last-minute technical issues. Regularly visit the official RSSB website for updates on exam date, admit card, and result.
