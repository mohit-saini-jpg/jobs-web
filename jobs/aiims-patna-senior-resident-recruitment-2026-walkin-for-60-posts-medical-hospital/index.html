FAQs

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies for Senior Resident posts at AIIMS Patna?
AIIMS Patna is recruiting for a total of 60 vacancies for the post of Senior Resident (Non-Academic) on an ad-hoc basis.
Q2. What is the application process for this recruitment?
The application process is through a Walk-in Interview. Candidates need to report directly at the specified venue on the interview date with all required documents.
Q3. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview?
The walk-in interviews are scheduled on 01 June 2026, 02 June 2026, and 03 June 2026, depending on the department.
Q4. What is the minimum educational qualification required?
The minimum educational qualification is a Post Graduate Degree i.e. MD/MS/DNB/DM/M.Ch or equivalent in the respective Department recognized by NMC/NBE.
Q5. What is the age limit for the Senior Resident posts?
The maximum age limit for all Senior Resident posts is 45 years, with relaxations applicable for reserved categories.
Q6. What is the salary offered for these positions?
The selected candidates will receive a salary of Rs. 67,700/- per month, along with NPA and other allowances.
Q7. Are there any application fees for this recruitment?
Yes, there are application fees. General/OBC candidates need to pay Rs. 1500/-, and SC/ST candidates need to pay Rs. 1200/-. PwBD and Women candidates are exempted from the fee.
Q8. Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?
The walk-in interview will be conducted at All India Institute of Medical Sciences, Patna, Bihar, 801507.
Q9. What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?
Candidates must carry all original documents, one set of self-attested photocopies, ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates, and category certificates (if applicable).
Q10. Is this a permanent job?
No, this is an ad-hoc recruitment for Senior Resident (Non-Academic) positions.
