KGBV Hamirpur Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

Selection for both notifications will be made purely on the basis of merit calculated using weightage marks, with interviews only for specific posts: For Assistant Cook posts (existing KGBV), selection includes an interview of 50 marks: 20 for physical capability, 20 for cooking knowledge, and 10 for experience. For Chowkidar posts (existing KGBV), the interview carries 50 marks: 25 for physical fitness and 25 for general knowledge. If two or more candidates score equal marks, the older candidate is ranked higher; if age also ties, candidates are ranked alphabetically by name. A waiting list of 5 candidates per vacant post will be prepared by the selection committee. Only women candidates are eligible; male applicants should not apply.
