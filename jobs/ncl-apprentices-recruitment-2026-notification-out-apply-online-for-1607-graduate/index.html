Important Instructions

Online applications are open from 01-06-2026 to 15-06-2026 through NATS/NAPS portals only. A candidate may apply for any one notified position only - multiple applications will lead to disqualification. Information pertaining to Date of Birth, Caste Category, and Qualification once entered in the online application shall be considered final - no alteration will be permitted at later stages. Stipend for Graduate and Diploma Apprentices is inclusive of the Government of India share paid through DBT; Trade Apprentice stipend will be paid directly by NCL. Stipend will be paid directly into the AADHAAR-linked bank account of the Apprenticeship Trainee as per the signed contract. Apprenticeship Training is purely temporary and statutory in nature - it does not confer any right or claim for regular employment in NCL. Apprentices are eligible for 11 days Casual Leave and 15 days Medical Leave per year of training. Engagement hours are between 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Apprentices will be covered under the Workmen Compensation Act, 1923 for any accident during training. No accommodation, fooding/lodging, or transport will be provided during the training period. No TA/DA will be paid for appearing in document scrutiny or post-scrutiny processes. Canvassing in any manner at any stage will lead to cancellation of candidature. Any dispute will be subject to courts/tribunals of Singrauli, MP only. Dates mentioned in the notification are tentative and subject to alteration/amendment.
