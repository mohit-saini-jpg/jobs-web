Selection Process

  • Component Weightage

  • 10th (Matriculation) Marks Percentage 50%

  • ITI Marks Percentage 50%

  • Basis Average of both (equal weightage)

  • Requirement Details

  • Medical Examination Medical certificate required at document verification stage as per Apprenticeship Act 1961 and Apprenticeship Rule 1992; must be signed by Government Authorized Doctor (GAZ) not below rank of Asstt. Surgeon

  • Contract Selected candidate (or guardian if minor) must enter into a contract of apprenticeship with the employer

  • Training Period 1 year for each trade, with stipend as per Railway Board rules

  • The selection of candidates for Act Apprenticeship in SECR Raipur Division and Wagon Repair Shop

  • Raipur is based purely on merit

  • with no written examination. The merit list is prepared by taking the average of the percentage marks obtained in both the 10th (Matriculation) examination and the ITI examination

  • giving equal weightage to both (as per Railway Establishment Rule 201/2017).

  • The minimum eligibility score for 10th is 50% aggregate. Candidates who qualify in the merit list will be called for document verification

  • where all original certificates must be produced. If any discrepancy is found between the uploaded documents and originals

  • or if original documents are not produced

  • the candidature will be cancelled.

  • The Railway Administration's decision in all matters relating to eligibility

  • acceptance or rejection of applications

  • and mode of selection is final and binding.

How To Apply

  • The application process is entirely online through the National Apprenticeship India portal. No physical copy of the application is to be sent to the DRM Office, Raipur Division, or Wagon Repair Shop, Raipur. Candidates must register on the portal, fill in all required details accurately, and upload soft/scanned copies of their recent passport-size colour photograph, signature, and all necessary documents pertaining to educational and technical qualification.

  • SC/ST/OBC candidates must also upload their recent caste certificate issued by the competent authority in the prescribed format and verify their Aadhaar on the portal. OBC candidates must additionally upload a Non-Creamy Layer Certificate - the OBC certificate must not be older than one year from the closing date of this notification.

  • PwBD candidates must upload a disability certificate from a Medical Board constituted by the Central or State Government certifying not less than 40% disability. EWS candidates must produce their EWS certificate as per sample Annexure-E at the time of document verification.

  • Apply Online at: https://apprenticeshipindia.gov.in

  • Application Window: 05/05/2026 to 04/06/2026 (till 23:59 hrs)

  • Upload: Passport-size colour photograph, scanned signature, 10th marksheet, ITI certificate, Caste/EWS/PwBD certificate (as applicable), Aadhaar verification

  • SC/ST/OBC candidates must upload recent caste certificate on the portal and verify Aadhaar

  • No physical application to be sent to the office
