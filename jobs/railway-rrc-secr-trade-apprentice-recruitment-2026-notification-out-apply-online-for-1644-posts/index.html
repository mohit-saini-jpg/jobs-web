South East Central Railway (SECR) Recruitment 2026-27 has been announced for Trade Apprentice (Act Apprenticeship) posts! Published on 05 May 2026 at the official website secr.indianrailways.gov.in, this new recruitment drive offers 1644 openings for eligible candidates. Applicants within the 15 to 24 years age group and possessing a 10th Pass with minimum 50% marks along with ITI in relevant trade can apply online from 05 May 2026 to 04 June 2026. Application Fee

The official notification does not specify any application fee for this Act Apprentice recruitment. This is consistent with the standard practice for apprenticeship recruitment under the Apprentices Act-1961 conducted by Indian Railway divisions through the National Apprenticeship Training portal (https://apprenticeshipindia.gov.in). | No Travelling Allowance (TA) or Daily Allowance (DA) will be paid to candidates for any stage of this recruitment process, including document verification.

Qualification / Eligibility

DetailsCandidates must fulfil both the educational/technical qualification requirements and the age limit to be eligible for this recruitment. The educational qualifications are mandatory and must have already been obtained before the date of notification - candidates appearing in qualifying examinations or awaiting results are not eligible. | The minimum 10th class marks of 50% aggregate is mandatory and the fraction will be applied strictly (e.g., 49.9% will be treated as less than 50%). | ITI qualification must be in the relevant trade from a recognised institution. Candidates are not required to wait for their ITI marksheet if already passed - they must have the actual pass certificate in hand at the time of application. How to Apply

  • 1The application process is entirely online through the National Apprenticeship India portal. No physical copy of the application is to be sent to the DRM Office, Raipur Division, or Wagon Repair Shop, Raipur. Candidates must register on the portal, fill in all required details accurately, and upload soft/scanned copies of their recent passport-size colour photograph, signature, and all necessary documents pertaining to educational and technical qualification.
  • 2SC/ST/OBC candidates must also upload their recent caste certificate issued by the competent authority in the prescribed format and verify their Aadhaar on the portal. OBC candidates must additionally upload a Non-Creamy Layer Certificate - the OBC certificate must not be older than one year from the closing date of this notification.
  • 3PwBD candidates must upload a disability certificate from a Medical Board constituted by the Central or State Government certifying not less than 40% disability. EWS candidates must produce their EWS certificate as per sample Annexure-E at the time of document verification.
  • 4Apply Online at: https://apprenticeshipindia.gov.in
  • 5Application Window: 05/05/2026 to 04/06/2026 (till 23:59 hrs)
  • 6Upload: Passport-size colour photograph, scanned signature, 10th marksheet, ITI certificate, Caste/EWS/PwBD certificate (as applicable), Aadhaar verification
  • 7SC/ST/OBC candidates must upload recent caste certificate on the portal and verify Aadhaar
  • 8No physical application to be sent to the office

  • Candidates must read all instructions carefully before applying. Applications must be submitted only through the official portal - no other mode of application will be accepted. Canvassing in any form will immediately disqualify the candidate.

If at any stage it is found that a candidate has furnished wrong, fake, or false certificates, the Railway Administration reserves the right to discharge the candidate without notice - even after selection for training.

FAQs

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies in SECR Raipur Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27?
The South East Central Railway (SECR), Raipur Division has announced a total of 1644 Act Apprentice vacancies, including 1363 posts in Raipur Division and 281 posts in Wagon Repair Shop, Raipur.
Q2. What is the last date to apply for SECR Raipur Apprentice 2026?
The last date to submit the online application is 04 June 2026 (till 23:59 hrs) through the official portal apprenticeshipindia.gov.in.
Q3. What is the eligibility for SECR Raipur Act Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
Passed 10th (Matriculation) with minimum 50% marks Completed ITI in relevant trade Both qualifications must be obtained before 05 May 2026
Q4. What is the age limit for SECR Raipur Apprentice 2026?
As on 05 May 2026: Minimum Age: 15 years Maximum Age: 24 years (UR) Age relaxation: SC/ST (5 years), OBC (3 years), PwBD/Ex-Servicemen (10 years)
Q5. What is the selection process for SECR Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
Selection is based on a merit list, prepared by taking the average of 10th marks and ITI marks (equal weightage). There is no written exam.
Q6. Is there any application fee for SECR Raipur Apprentice Recruitment?
No, there is no application fee for any category as per the official notification.
Q7. What is the training duration for SECR Act Apprentices?
The training period is 1 year for all trades, with stipend as per Railway Board rules.
Q8. When will the SECR Raipur Apprentice merit list be released?
The merit list release date is not specified in the notification. It will be published after the application process ends on the official portal or SECR website.
