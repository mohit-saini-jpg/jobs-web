UK DElEd Admission 2025

The online application for the UK DElEd Admission 2025 is open from 7th September 2025 to 6th October 2025 on the official website ukdeled.com. Candidates aspiring to join the two-year Diploma in Elementary Education (DElEd) program in Uttarakhand must apply within this period

The UK DElEd Admission 2025 is organized by the Uttarakhand Board of School Education (UBSE) for candidates aspiring to become primary teachers through a two-year Diploma in Elementary Education program. This entrance exam is mandatory for admission to District Institutes of Education and Training (DIETs) and government-affiliated colleges across Uttarakhand. The full online application process is available at ukdeled.com, and all eligible graduates from recognized universities are encouraged to apply.

Online registrations begin on 7th September 2025 and remain open until 6th October 2025. Candidates must ensure they meet the eligibility criteria, including age and academic qualifications, before submitting their applications. Reserved categories such as SC/ST/OBC and persons with disabilities are provided concessions in age and qualifying marks, making the program accessible to diverse groups.

UK DElEd Admission 2025: Eligibility Criteria

  • Educational Qualification: Must have passed graduation (any stream: science or non-science) from a university recognized by law in India before the notification publication date.
  • Age Limit: Minimum 19 years and maximum 30 years as of 01-07-2026 (age relaxations as per government rules).
  • Domicile: Candidates should have a domicile of Uttarakhand.
  • Employment Card: Possession of an Uttarakhand employment card is mandatory
