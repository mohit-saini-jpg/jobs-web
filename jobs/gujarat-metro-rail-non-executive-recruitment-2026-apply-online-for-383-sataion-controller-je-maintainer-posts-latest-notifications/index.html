How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the official GMRCL website. No hard copy or offline applications will be accepted. Candidates are advised to complete the application process well before the last date to avoid last-minute technical issues.
  • Visit the official GMRCL website: https://www.gujaratmetrorail.com
  • Click on the “Careers” section or directly go to the recruitment link.
  • Select the GMRCL O&M Non-Executives Recruitment 2026 advertisement from the drop-down menu.
  • Click on “Apply” or “Apply Now” against the advertisement.
  • If you already have a One Time Registration (OTR) number, enter your OTR Number and Date of Birth to auto-fill your details. If you do not have an OTR number, click on “SKIP” or register fresh by filling in your personal details (Name, Father’s/Husband’s Name, Mother’s Name, Gender, Date of Birth, Category, Address, Mobile Number, Email ID, Aadhaar Number, Educational Qualification, etc.). An OTR number will be generated and sent to your registered mobile and email.
  • Fill up all the required details carefully in the online application form.
  • Upload your recent passport size colour photograph and signature as per the prescribed specifications (Photograph: max 15 KB, Signature: max 15 KB).
  • Carefully preview all the details filled in the application form. Make corrections if needed before final submission. No changes will be allowed after submission.
  • Click on the “Submit” button.
  • Proceed to the Payment section and pay the applicable application fee online using Debit Card, Credit Card, Internet Banking, UPI, or Wallet.
  • After successful payment, an e-Receipt will be generated. Download and take a printout of the e-Receipt and the filled-in online application form for future reference

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for GMRCL Non-Executives Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the online application for the Gujarat Metro Rail Corporation Limited Non-Executives Recruitment 2026 is 28 May 2026. Candidates must complete the registration process before this deadline to avoid rejection. Additionally, the last date for payment of the application fee is 31 May 2026, so applicants should plan accordingly.

Q2. How many vacancies are announced in GMRCL Recruitment 2026?

A total of 383 vacancies have been released under the GMRCL Non-Executives Recruitment 2026. These vacancies are distributed among posts like Station Controller/Train Operator, Junior Engineer, and Maintainer. The recruitment aims to strengthen the Operations & Maintenance team of Ahmedabad and Surat Metro Rail projects.

Q3. What is the eligibility criteria for GMRCL Non-Executive posts 2026?

Candidates applying for GMRCL Non-Executive posts must have either a Diploma in Engineering for technical roles or SSLC with ITI certification for Maintainer posts. The qualifications should be from recognized institutions. Applicants must also meet the prescribed medical standards as per railway guidelines.

Q4. What is the age limit for GMRCL Recruitment 2026?

The age limit for Station Controller/Train Operator and Junior Engineer posts is 18 to 28 years, while for Maintainer posts it is 18 to 25 years. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories such as SC, ST, SEBC, EWS, women, and ex-servicemen as per Gujarat government norms.

Q5. What is the selection process for GMRCL Non-Executives Recruitment 2026?

The selection process includes a written test, followed by a psycho aptitude test (for SC/TO posts), document verification, and medical examination. Candidates must qualify in each stage to move forward. Final selection will be based on merit and fulfillment of all eligibility conditions.

Q6. What is the salary for GMRCL Non-Executive posts?

During the training period, candidates will receive a stipend ranging from ₹14,000 to ₹18,000 per month depending on the post. After successful training, salaries increase to ₹16,000 to ₹20,000 per month. Later, employees are placed in IDA pay scales with higher salary benefits and allowances.

Q7. How can I apply online for GMRCL Recruitment 2026?

Candidates can apply online through the official website of Gujarat Metro Rail Corporation Limited at gujaratmetrorail.com. Applicants need to register, fill out the form, upload documents, and pay the fee online. It is important to verify all details before final submission to avoid errors.

Q8. What is the application fee for GMRCL Non-Executives 2026?

The application fee for unreserved male candidates is ₹600, while candidates from SC, ST, SEBC, EWS categories, women, and ex-servicemen need to pay ₹300. The fee must be paid online through available digital payment methods. Note that the application fee is non-refundable.
