FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for SSB Constable GD Sports Quota Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application form for SSB Constable GD Sports Quota Recruitment 2026 is 07 June 2026. Candidates must complete the registration and fee payment process before the closing date through the official SSB recruitment portal. Late or incomplete applications will not be accepted under any circumstances
Q2How many vacancies are available in SSB Constable GD Sports Quota Recruitment 2026?
A
Sashastra Seema Bal has announced a total of 404 vacancies for Constable (General Duty) posts under the Sports Quota. The vacancies are available for both male and female sportspersons across multiple disciplines including Athletics, Football, Wrestling, Hockey, Boxing, Volleyball, Archery, and more. The number of vacancies may increase or decrease depending on organizational requirements.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for SSB Constable GD Sports Quota 2026?
A
Candidates applying for SSB Constable GD Sports Quota Recruitment 2026 must have passed Matriculation or equivalent examination from a recognized board or institute. Along with the educational qualification, applicants must also possess valid sports achievements in recognized competitions as mentioned in the official notification. Only eligible sportspersons can participate in this recruitment process.
Q4What sports achievements are required for SSB Sports Quota Recruitment 2026?
A
Applicants should have represented India in international competitions or won medals in National Championships, Khelo India Games, SGFI events, Inter-University tournaments, or National School Games. Only sports achievements earned during the last two years from the cut-off date will be considered valid for recruitment. Candidates are advised to upload proper sports certificates during the application process.
Q5What is the age limit for SSB Constable GD Sports Quota Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age required is 18 years, while the maximum age limit is 23 years as on the closing date of application submission. Age relaxation benefits are available for SC, ST, OBC, Ex-Servicemen, and other eligible categories as per Government of India rules. Candidates should carefully verify their eligibility before applying online.
Q6What is the application fee for SSB Constable GD Sports Quota 2026?
A
Candidates belonging to UR, EWS, and OBC categories are required to pay an application fee of Rs. 100/-. However, SC, ST, and Female candidates are fully exempted from payment of the application fee. The fee once paid will not be refunded under any circumstances.
Q7What is the selection process for SSB Constable GD Sports Quota Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process includes documentation, sports achievement verification, Physical Standard Test (PST), Field Trial or Skill Test, Detailed Medical Examination (DME), and Review Medical Examination (RME). International and National medal winners may be considered under the priority selection stage. Final selection will be based on sports performance, field trial marks, and medical fitness.
Q8What is the salary of SSB Constable GD under Sports Quota?
A
Selected candidates will receive salary under Pay Matrix Level-3 with pay scale of Rs. 21,700 to Rs. 69,100 along with other allowances admissible to SSB personnel. The post is classified as Group ‘C’ Non-Gazetted and Combatised in nature. Additional benefits may include allowances, pension-related benefits, and medical facilities as per government rules.
Q9What are the physical standards required for SSB Constable GD Sports Quota 2026?
A
Male candidates generally require a minimum height of 170 cm and female candidates require 157 cm height, while chest measurement for males should be 80-85 cm. Relaxation in physical standards is available for candidates belonging to reserved categories as per the official notification. Candidates must also maintain BMI between 18 and 25.
Q10How can candidates apply for SSB Constable GD Sports Quota Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online through the official SSB recruitment website by completing registration, uploading required documents, and paying the application fee if applicable. Applicants should upload photograph, signature, educational certificates, sports achievement certificates, and category certificates carefully. It is recommended to keep a copy of the submitted application form for future reference.
