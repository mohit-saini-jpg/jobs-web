Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for the Clerk post at Gurugram University?

The walk-in interview for the Clerk post at Gurugram University is scheduled for 03 June 2026. Candidates should ensure they report to the venue on this date.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Clerk position?

Candidates must be a Graduate or possess an equivalent qualification. Additionally, proficiency in computer applications and typing speed of at least 30 words per minute in English is required.

Q3. What is the age limit for the Clerk recruitment?

The age limit for the Clerk position is between 18 years and 42 years. Specific age relaxations may apply as per government rules.

Q4. What is the salary offered for the Clerk post?

The selected candidates for the Clerk post will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 24600 per month.

Q5. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.

Q6. How many vacancies are available for the Clerk post?

Gurugram University is recruiting for a total of 5 vacancies for the post of Clerk.

Q7. What is the mode of application for this recruitment?

The mode of application is Walk-in. Candidates need to attend the interview directly at the specified venue and time.

Q8. What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?

Candidates must carry all original documents, one set of self-attested photocopies, ID proof, educational certificates, and any other relevant certificates as mentioned in the notification.
