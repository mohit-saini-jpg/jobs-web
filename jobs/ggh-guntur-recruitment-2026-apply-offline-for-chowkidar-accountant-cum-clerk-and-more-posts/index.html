Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the GGH Guntur recruitment?

The last date to submit the application for the GGH Guntur Various Posts recruitment is 29th May 2026. Ensure your application reaches the concerned authority before this date.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Accountant cum clerk post?

For the Accountant cum clerk position, candidates must be graduates with a good knowledge of accounts and possess working knowledge of computers. This ensures they can handle financial tasks efficiently.

Q3. Are there any age relaxations for reserved categories?

Yes, age relaxation is provided. SC, ST, BC, and EWS candidates get a 5-year relaxation. Ex-service men receive a 3-year relaxation, and differently-abled individuals get a 10-year relaxation.

Q4. What is the salary offered for these posts?

The selected candidates will receive a monthly salary ranging from Rs.9,000/- to Rs.12,000/-. This is a contractual position, and the salary is fixed within this range.

Q5. How will candidates be selected for these positions?

The selection process is based on merit. Candidates will be evaluated based on their aggregate marks in the qualifying examination (90%) and a weightage for the years since passing the examination (up to 10 marks).

Q6. What is the application fee for GGH Guntur recruitment?

The application fee varies by category. General and OBC candidates need to pay Rs.300/-, while SC/ST candidates need to pay Rs.100/-. Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwBD) are exempted from the fee.

Q7. What is the application mode for this recruitment?

The application process for the GGH Guntur Various Posts recruitment is offline. Candidates need to download the application form, fill it, and send it via post to the specified address.

Q8. What is the total number of vacancies available?

There are a total of 5 vacancies announced for Various Posts in Drug De-Addiction Centers at Government General Hospital Guntur. These vacancies are distributed across different roles.
