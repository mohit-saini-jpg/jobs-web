PPUP UG 2nd Merit List 2026-30 Selection Process

This section explains how the university prepares the merit list, so candidates understand the basis on which colleges and subjects are allotted.

Selection to Patliputra University UG courses is done on a Merit-cum-Choice basis. The merit list is prepared using the marks obtained in Class 12th (Intermediate) along with the college and subject preferences filled by the applicant during registration.

How to Download Patliputra University UG 2nd Merit List 2026-30

This section provides the step-by-step process to check and download the 2nd merit list from the official admission portal.

  • Visit the official PPUP admission portal at ppupadm.samarth.edu.in.
  • Click on the Applicant Login option on the homepage.
  • Enter your Registration Number/Roll Number, Date of Birth/Password, and Captcha Code (if required).
  • Submit the details to view your 2nd Merit List status, allotted college, and subject.
  • Download and save the merit list/allotment details for further admission steps.
  • Alternatively, candidates can also check the merit list notice PDF on the official Patliputra University website, ppup.ac.in.
