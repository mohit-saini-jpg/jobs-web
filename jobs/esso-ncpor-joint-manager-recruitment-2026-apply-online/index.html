Candidates must be Indian Nationals and fulfil the following educational qualification, experience, and age criteria to be eligible for the NCPOR Joint Manager (Administration) post. Age Limit

The upper age limit for the Joint Manager (Administration) post is 40 years, reckoned as on the cut-off date of 20 August 2026. NCPOR Joint Manager Salary / Pay Level 2026

The Joint Manager (Administration) post carries Pay Level 10 as per the 7th Central Pay Commission (CPC). In addition to the basic pay, the post carries usual allowances such as Dearness Allowance (DA), House Rent Allowance (HRA), Travelling Allowance (TA), Leave Travel Concession (LTC), Children Education Allowance, National Pension System (NPS), medical facilities, and leave, as admissible to Society employees from time to time. The post is on a regular basis, with a minimum probation period of 2 years. The place of posting is currently NCPOR, Goa, with transfer liability to any part of India.
