SBI Trade Finance Officer Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 100 Posts

State Bank of India (SBI), Central Recruitment & Promotion Department, Corporate Centre, Mumbai

Posts: 100 (Regular: 88 + Backlog: 12) Last Date: 02-06-2026 Online

Short Information

The State Bank of India (SBI) has officially announced the Trade Finance Officer (MMGS-II) - Specialist Cadre Officer Recruitment 2026 with 100 openings. The detailed notification was published on 13 May 2026 at sbi.co.in. Candidates who meet the age criteria of 23 to 32 years (relaxations applicable as per category) and hold a Graduate degree in any discipline from a recognized University along with Certification in Forex Operations from IIBF can now submit their online applications from 13 May 2026 to 02 June 2026. Direct links to download the notification and apply online are shared below.

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the link available on SBI’s official website https://sbi.co.in/web/careers/current-openings. No other mode of application will be entertained. Hard copy of application and other documents need not be sent to this office. The process of registration is completed only when fee is deposited with the Bank through online mode on or before the last date. Candidates are advised to apply online well before the closing date and not to wait till the last date to avoid any technical issues.
  • Candidates must upload all required documents at the time of online application; failure to upload documents will result in candidature not being considered for shortlisting/interview. After expiry of the window for online application, no change/correction/modification will be allowed under any circumstances.
  • Documents to be Uploaded at the Time of Online Application:
  • Recent Photograph & Signature (JPEG)
  • Detailed Resume (PDF)
  • ID Proof (PDF)
  • Proof of Date of Birth (PDF)
  • Caste Certificate & PwBD Certificate (if applicable) (PDF)
  • Mandatory Educational Certificates: Degree Certificate (PDF)
  • Mandatory Certification in Forex Operations from IIBF (PDF)
  • Experience Certificates (mandatory) (PDF)
  • Preferred Certificate, if any (PDF)
  • Biodata Form (duly filled, signed & scanned - available on Bank’s career website under the advertisement) (PDF)
  • Form-16 / Offer Letter / Latest Salary Slip from current employer (PDF)
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) from present employer (mandatory if working in Government Institute/Public Sector Unit/Public Sector Bank) (PDF)
  • Mandatory Undertaking (format available on Bank’s career page against Adv. No. CRPD/SCO/2026-27/05) (PDF)
  • Apply Online at: https://sbi.co.in/web/careers/current-openings

Important Instructions

  • Candidates must apply online only; hard copy of application and other documents need not be sent to SBI’s office. The position of Trade Finance Officer (MMGS-II) shall remain in Specialist cadre and will have no provision for conversion into General cadre. Teaching and training experience will not be counted for eligibility. Candidates with any disciplinary proceedings pending or contemplated against them as on date will not be eligible to apply; an undertaking must be uploaded at the time of online application. CIBIL: Candidates who have defaulted in repayment under any lending arrangement with Banks/NBFCs/Financial Institutions including credit card dues and have not regularized/repaid their outstanding till the date of issuance of offer of recruitment by the Bank shall not be eligible for appointment. Candidates serving in Govt./Quasi Govt. offices/Public Sector undertakings/Nationalized Banks must submit NOC from their employer at the time of interview, failing which their candidature will not be considered. In case of multiple applications for the same post, only the last valid (completed) application will be retained; fee paid for other registrations will stand forfeited. OBC certificate containing the ‘non-creamy layer’ clause, issued during the period 01.04.2026 to the date of interview, must be submitted by OBC candidates if called for interview. EWS candidates not in possession of “Income & Asset Certificate” for FY2025-26 on or before closure of online application date should apply under “UR Category” only. Outstation candidates called for interview will be reimbursed airfare (economy class, shortest route, to-and-fro) up to a maximum of Rs. 10,000/- on submission of original flight tickets/invoice and boarding pass. All changes/updates/revisions/corrigendum/results will be hosted on the Bank’s website only; no separate intimation will be issued. Any legal proceedings arising out of this advertisement can be instituted only in Mumbai; Courts/Tribunals/Forums at Mumbai only shall have sole and exclusive jurisdiction. Candidates are advised to keep their email ID/mobile number active till the declaration of result and issuance of call letters on final selection. SBI does not endorse, authorize or associate with any external coaching platform, consultancy, individual or digital channel claiming to provide guaranteed selection or insider guidance. Candidates must rely solely on information available on SBI’s official career portal.
