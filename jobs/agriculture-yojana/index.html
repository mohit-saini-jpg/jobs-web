Overview

Agriculture Yojana by Central Govt offer various schemes to farmers, including Kisan Credit Card, PM-Kisan, and Fasal Bima Yojana. Dheere-dheere, these schemes provide financial assistance and insurance to farmers, ensuring their livelihood and growth. Sarkari Naukri aspirants can apply for various Agriculture jobs in Govt departments like Agriculture Ministry, NABARD, and ICAR.

Expert Analysis

Agriculture Yojana by Central Govt is a significant step towards doubling farmers' income. Experts say these schemes will help farmers invest in their land, livestock, and equipment, ultimately increasing farm productivity. Sarkari Naukri aspirants can expect a bright career in this field with Govt jobs offering attractive salaries and benefits.

Who Should Apply

Ideal candidates for Agriculture jobs are those with a degree in Agriculture, Horticulture, or related fields. Experience in farming, farm management, or extension services is also a plus.

Preparation Tips

To prepare for Agriculture jobs, focus on subjects like soil science, agronomy, and plant breeding. Develop your communication skills to effectively interact with farmers and Govt officials. Practice solving case studies related to farm management and extension services.

Salary Insights

Agriculture jobs in Govt departments offer attractive salaries, ranging from ₹30,000 to ₹1,00,000 per month. With experience, salaries can go up to ₹2,00,000 per month. Benefits like DA, HRA, and medical insurance are also provided.

Job Profile

Agriculture jobs involve day-to-day work like soil testing, crop monitoring, and providing extension services to farmers. With experience, you can move to senior roles like farm manager or extension officer. Career growth prospects are excellent, with opportunities to work in Govt departments, research institutions, or private companies.

Selection Strategy

To crack Agriculture jobs, focus on clearing written exams, followed by interviews and skill tests. Practice solving case studies and develop your communication skills to effectively interact with the panel.

FAQs

Q1What are the key features of Kisan Credit Card Yojana?
A
Kisan Credit Card provides short-term crop loans to farmers, with interest rates as low as 7% per annum.
Q2How does PM-Kisan Yojana benefit farmers?
A
PM-Kisan provides financial assistance of ₹6,000 per year to farmers, in three equal installments of ₹2,000 each.
Q3What is Fasal Bima Yojana and how does it work?
A
Fasal Bima Yojana is a crop insurance scheme that provides financial assistance to farmers in case of crop damage or failure due to natural calamities like floods, droughts, or pests.
