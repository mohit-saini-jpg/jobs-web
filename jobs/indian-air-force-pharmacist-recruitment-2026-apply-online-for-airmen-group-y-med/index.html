Indian Air Force Pharmacist Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Indian Air Force (IAF) has released the Airmen Recruitment Notification 2026 inviting online applications from male Indian/Gorkha (a subject of Nepal) candidates for the Group 'Y' (Non-Technical) Medical Assistant trade under Airmen Intake 02/2027. Indian Air Force Pharmacist Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1Has the Indian Air Force (IAF) released the Airmen Recruitment Notification 2026?
A
Yes, the IAF has released the recruitment notification for Airmen Intake 02/2027 under the Group 'Y' Medical Assistant trade, inviting online applications from 06 July 2026 to 26 July 2026.
Q2Who can apply for the IAF Medical Assistant/Pharmacist trade?
A
Male Indian citizens and Gorkha subjects of Nepal who meet the educational qualification and age criteria for either the 10+2 stream or the Diploma/B.Pharma/B.Sc in Pharmacy stream can apply.
Q3What is the age limit for IAF Pharmacist Recruitment 2026?
A
For 10+2 candidates, the date of birth must fall between 01 July 2006 and 01 July 2010. For Diploma/B.Pharma/B.Sc Pharmacy candidates, unmarried applicants must be born between 01 July 2003 and 01 July 2008, while married applicants must be born between 01 July 2003 and 01 July 2006.
Q4What is the application fee for this recruitment?
A
The application fee is Rs. 550/- plus 18% GST, and it is non-refundable under any circumstances.
Q5When is the IAF online exam for Airmen Intake 02/2027?
A
The online examination (Phase-I) is scheduled on 22-23 September 2026.
Q6What is the syllabus for the IAF Airmen online test?
A
The Phase-I online test covers English (as per 10+2 CBSE syllabus) and Reasoning & General Awareness (RAGA), with a duration of 45 minutes.
Q7What is the salary for an IAF Medical Assistant Airman?
A
During training, candidates receive a stipend of Rs. 14,600/- per month. After successful completion of training, the starting gross emoluments are Rs. 26,900/- per month, including Military Service Pay (MSP), plus other applicable allowances.
Q8What is the minimum height required for this trade?
A
The minimum height required for Medical Assistant and Medical Assistant (Pharmacist) trades is 152 cms.
Q9What physical fitness test do candidates need to clear?
A
Candidates must complete a 1.6 Km run within 7 minutes (or 7 minutes 30 seconds for candidates above 21 years or holding Diploma/B.Pharma/B.Sc Pharmacy), followed by 10 push-ups, 10 sit-ups, and 20 squats, each within one minute.
Q10When will the Provisional Select List (PSL) be published?
A
The Provisional Select List will be displayed at all Airmen Selection Centres and on the CASB web portal in the last week of May 2027.
