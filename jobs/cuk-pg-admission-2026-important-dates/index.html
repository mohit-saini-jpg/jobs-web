Eligibility Criteria for CUK PG Admission 2026

  • Candidates must have completed a Bachelor’s degree in a relevant discipline from a recognized university.
  • Minimum aggregate marks: 50% (45% for SC/ST/OBC/PwD/EWS candidates as per norms).
  • Must have appeared in CUET-PG 2026.
  • For MBA: CMAT 2026 qualifiers get first preference.
  • For M.Tech: GATE/CCMT qualifiers get first preference.
  • Final year students can apply provisionally but must submit proof of passing at the time of admission.

How to Apply for CUK PG Admission 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official admission portal: cukashmircuet.samarth.edu.in
  • Register / Login using your CUET-PG 2026 credentials.
  • Fill the online application form carefully.
  • Pay the application fee through online payment modes.
  • Upload all required documents.
  • Submit the form and take a printout of the confirmation page.

Documents Required:

  • CUET-PG 2026 Scorecard
  • Class 10th & 12th Marksheets
  • Graduation Marksheets & Degree/Provisional Certificate
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph & Signature
  • Valid Photo ID Proof

Selection & Counselling Process

  • Merit list will be prepared based on CUET-PG 2026 scores.
  • Special preference will be given to CMAT for MBA and GATE for M.Tech programmes.
  • Shortlisted candidates will be called for counselling and document verification.
  • Final admission is confirmed only after successful document verification and fee payment.
