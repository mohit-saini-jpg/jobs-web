GTU DIPL Sem 6 & BCA Sem 3 Result 2026 - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 11/07/2026

Gujarat Technological University (GTU) has declared results for two May 2026 examinations on 10 July 2026. Students who appeared for the DIPL Semester 6 Regular exam or the BCA Semester 3 Remedial exam can now check their results on the official GTU result portal. GTU has also opened the online recheck/reassessment application window, with 13 July 2026 as the last date for students to apply.

How to Check Your GTU Result 2026 Online

Students can check their DIPL SEM 6 or BCA SEM 3 result by following these steps:

  • Visit the official GTU result website, gturesults.in, or use the direct result link for your exam.
  • Select your exam from the results list (e.g., "DIPL SEM 6 - Regular (MAY 2026)" or "BCA SEM 3 - Remedial (MAY 2026)").
  • Enter your Enrollment Number as printed on your admit card or fee receipt.
  • Enter any other required login details (such as your Student Portal Password, where applicable).
  • Enter the captcha code shown on the screen.
  • Click Search or Submit to view your result.

The exact login fields required for these two specific results (enrollment number only, or enrollment number plus portal password) are not confirmed in the source notification. Students should follow the fields shown on the live result page.

Online Recheck / Re-Assessment Application -Last Date 13 July 2026

GTU has opened an online recheck/re-assessment application window for students who wish to have their answer sheets rechecked or reassessed. Key points:

  • Last date to apply: 13 July 2026
  • Applicable to: Students of the DIPL SEM 6 Regular and BCA SEM 3 Remedial (MAY 2026) results declared on 10 July 2026
  • Mode of application: Online, through the GTU result/recheck portal

The specific recheck fee per subject, maximum number of subjects allowed, and the direct recheck application link for this particular result cycle are not specified in the source notification. Students should check the official GTU recheck login page for the exact fee and subject limit applicable to this session before the 13 July 2026 deadline.

General GTU Result Policies (Typical Practice, Not Specific to This Notification)

The following points reflect GTU's commonly followed academic policies. They are general practice and are not confirmed as part of this specific 10 July 2026 result notification:

  • Students generally need a minimum of 35% marks in each subject to pass, based on combined external and mid-semester marks.
  • Recheck/reassessment applications are typically accepted only within a short window (commonly a few working days) after result declaration.
  • Online results published on gturesults.in are usually provisional; the final marksheet is issued through the respective institute.

Students should rely on the official notification or their institute for confirmation of any rule that affects their specific result.

What to Do If You Are Not Satisfied With Your Result

If you believe your marks are incorrect, you generally have the option to:

  • Apply for online recheck/reassessment before the 13 July 2026 deadline.
  • Contact your college/institute exam cell for guidance on the process.
  • Retain your admit card and fee receipt, as these are usually required for any correction request.

Important Instructions for Students

  • Check your result only through the official GTU portal (gturesults.in) or gtu.ac.in.
  • Keep your enrollment number and portal login details ready before checking your result.
  • Do not miss the 13 July 2026 deadline if you intend to apply for recheck/reassessment.
  • Download and save a copy of your result for future reference once it is available.
