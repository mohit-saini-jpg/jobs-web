Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the ANIIDCO Executive Chef recruitment?

The last date for the receipt of applications for the ANIIDCO Executive Chef recruitment is 29th May 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well in advance of this deadline.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Executive Chef post?

Candidates must possess a minimum 2nd class three-year Degree/Diploma in Hospitality Management & Hotel Administration from a Government recognized Institute in India or an equivalent degree/diploma from abroad approved by the Government of India.

Q3. How many years of experience are required for the Executive Chef position?

A minimum of 05 years of post-qualification relevant experience in 3-5 Star Hotels/Resorts is required for the Executive Chef position.

Q4. What is the age limit for the Executive Chef post?

The maximum age limit for the Executive Chef post is 35 years. Age relaxations are applicable for certain categories.

Q5. Are there any age relaxations for reserved categories?

Yes, there is an age relaxation of 05 years for ST and Government Servants, and 03 years for OBC candidates.

Q6. What is the salary offered for the Executive Chef position?

The salary for the Executive Chef position is as per Level-6, ranging from ₹35,400 to ₹1,12,400, along with admissible allowances.

Q7. How can candidates apply for this recruitment?

Applications can be submitted via email to [email protected] or through hard copy to the General Manager (P&A), ANIIDCO Ltd., Vikas Bhawan, P.B. No. 180, Sri Vijaya Puram.

Q8. What documents need to be submitted with the application?

Attested copies of all testimonials, certificates, and 2 passport-size photographs are required to be submitted with the application.
