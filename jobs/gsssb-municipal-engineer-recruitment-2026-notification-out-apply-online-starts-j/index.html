GSSSB Municipal Engineer Salary Structure 2026

Selected candidates will initially be appointed on a fixed monthly pay of ₹49,600 for the first five years. After five years of satisfactory service, candidates will be placed in the regular 7th Pay Commission pay scale of ₹39,900 to ₹1,26,600 (Level-7) , subject to conditions laid down in the relevant government resolutions.

GSSSB Municipal Engineer Selection Process 2026

Selection will be based on a single-stage objective MCQ competitive examination (CBRT/OMR), divided into two parts: Total questions: 210 (90 in Part-A, 120 in Part-B) Total duration: 3 hours (180 minutes), combined for both parts Negative marking: 1/4th mark deducted for each wrong answer Minimum qualifying standard: 40% separately in both Part-A and Part-B Candidates who qualify will be called for document verification (roughly twice the number of vacancies per category), after which the final merit-based selection list and waiting list will be published.
