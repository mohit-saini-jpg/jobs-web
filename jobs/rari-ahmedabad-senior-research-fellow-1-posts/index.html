Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for RARI Ahmedabad Recruitment 2026?

A
Candidates must possess a B.A.M.S. Degree from a recognized university. Preference will be given to those with a Degree/Diploma/Certificate in Yoga/Swasthavritta, research experience in AYUSH/ICMR/DST funded Yoga projects, and good scientific documentation skills.

The maximum age limit for the Senior Research Fellow (Ay.) post is 35 years as of 1st June 2026. Age relaxation will be provided as per Central Government rules for SC/ST/OBC/PH candidates.

A
The selected candidate will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 42,000/- per month, along with House Rent Allowance (HRA) as per applicable rules.
