How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply online only; no other mode of application will be accepted. Before applying, candidates must complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the SSO portal and read the detailed guidelines and instructions for applicants available on the RPSC website.
  • Candidates should have a valid email ID and active mobile number which should be kept active till declaration of results.
  • Visit the official RPSC website: https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in and read the complete notification and Instructions for Applicants before applying.
  • Click on the Apply Online link or log in to the SSO portal at https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in using your SSO ID to access the Recruitment Portal (G2C portal).
  • If you are a new user, complete One Time Registration (OTR) by providing your name, father's name (if available from Aadhaar), date of birth, gender, and Aadhaar card / Janaadhar card details. Upload a live self-photo and scan of OTR documents.
  • After completing OTR, log in from SSO portal → Citizen Apps (G2C) → Recruitment Portal and fill the online application form using your OTR number.
  • Important: No amendment to name, father's name, date of birth, or gender can be made in OTR Profile once created without Aadhaar/Janaadhar correction. Ensure all Aadhaar/Janaadhar details are corrected before completing OTR.
  • During online application, a Live Photo will be captured via OTP camera. Submit the form only after verifying the live photo is clear.
  • Candidates must scan and upload thumb impression (left hand) and signature along with the online application.
  • Candidates who wish to avail scribe facility must mandatorily select the scribe option in their online application form.
  • All educational qualification and experience details as at the application closing date must be clearly entered in the OTR/application form.
  • After submission, an Application Number will be generated. If no Application Number is received, it means the form has not been submitted successfully.
  • Candidates must take a printout of the finally submitted application form for future reference.
  • For any technical issues during online application, contact the Helpdesk number on the Recruitment Portal or email as indicated on the portal.
  • Call Letter (Admit Card) downloaded from RPSC website along with valid Photo ID proof (Aadhaar / PAN Card / Passport / Driving License / Voter ID / Bank Passbook with photo) in original and self-attested photocopy.
  • Caste Certificate in prescribed Government of India / Government of Rajasthan format (for SC/ST/OBC/MBC candidates).
  • OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in prescribed format issued for the current year (for OBC NCL candidates).
  • Income and Asset Certificate issued by Competent Authority in prescribed format (for EWS candidates).
  • Disability Certificate in prescribed format issued by Competent Authority (for PwBD candidates).
  • Educational qualification certificates and marksheets (graduation / Law degree onwards).
  • Domicile certificate / Residence proof (for reserved category benefits applicable to Rajasthan domiciles).
  • Character Certificate from the last attended educational institution (at least "Good" mention required).
  • Police verification / antecedent certificate at the time of appointment.
  • Medical fitness certificate from a competent authority at the time of appointment.
  • NOC from current employer if already in Government / PSU service.
  • EWS / widowhood / divorce proof documents as applicable (court decree / death certificate).
  • Ex-Serviceman discharge certificate / NOC as applicable.
  • UIDAI / Unique Disability ID (U-D.I.D.) card, if available, must be produced before the Preliminary Examination.
  • Printout of the Online Application Form is NOT to be sent to RPSC. No Hard Copy of the Call Letter will be sent by post. Candidates must appear for the test at their own expense; RPSC will not pay or reimburse any such expenses.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for RPSC APO Recruitment 2026?

The last date to apply online for RPSC Assistant Prosecution Officer Recruitment 2026 is 07 July 2026. Candidates must complete the application process before midnight to avoid last-minute technical issues. It is advised to apply early and verify all details before final submission.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for RPSC APO 2026?

Applicants must possess a Bachelor’s Degree in Law (LLB Professional) from a recognized university in India. The qualification must be completed before the last date of application submission. Candidates who are in the final year of their law degree are not eligible to apply.

Q3. What is the age limit for RPSC Assistant Prosecution Officer Recruitment 2026?

The minimum age required is 21 years and the maximum age is 40 years as on 01 January 2027. Age relaxation is provided to reserved categories such as SC, ST, OBC, EWS, and others as per Rajasthan government norms. Candidates must check category-wise relaxation carefully.

Q4. How can I apply online for RPSC APO Recruitment 2026?

Candidates can apply online through the official RPSC website or Rajasthan SSO portal. Before filling the application form, One Time Registration (OTR) is mandatory. Ensure all required documents are uploaded correctly and details match official records.

Q5. What is the application fee for RPSC APO 2026?

The application fee for General, BC, and MBC (Creamy Layer) candidates is Rs. 600. For SC, ST, OBC (Non-Creamy Layer), EWS, and PwBD candidates, the fee is Rs. 400. The fee must be paid online and is non-refundable under any circumstances.

Q6. What is the selection process for RPSC Assistant Prosecution Officer 2026?

The selection process consists of two stages: Preliminary Examination and Main Written Examination. The prelim exam is qualifying in nature and does not count toward final merit. Final selection is based on the marks obtained in the main examination.
