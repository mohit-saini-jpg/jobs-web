How to Download UP CNET Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official ABVMU website abvmuup.edu.in.
  • Open the CNET / Common Nursing Entrance Test section.
  • Click on the Admit Card / Hall Ticket link.
  • Enter your Registration ID and Password.
  • Submit the details and your UP CNET B.Sc Nursing Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download the PDF and take 2–3 printed copies for exam day.

If the website is slow after release, wait a few minutes and try again.

Details Mentioned on the Admit Card

Your UP CNET B.Sc Nursing admit card 2026 is expected to show the following details:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph.
  • Registration ID and roll number.
  • Exam name: UP CNET 2026 – B.Sc Nursing.
  • Exam date and reporting time.
  • Exam centre name, code and address.
  • Signature and other identification details.
  • Important exam-day instructions.

Check every detail carefully. If any information is incorrect, contact the ABVMU helpdesk immediately through the official portal.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates must carry the following items to the exam centre:

  • A printed copy of the UP CNET Admit Card 2026.
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence or Passport.
  • Recent passport-size photographs, if specifically mentioned on the admit card or instructions.

Without the admit card and valid ID, entry to the exam hall may be denied.
