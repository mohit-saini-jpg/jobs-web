The competitive exam is divided into two parts in a single question paper. Part-A tests general aptitude and Part-B tests domain-specific nursing knowledge. The total duration is 3 hours (180 minutes).

GSSSB Female Health Worker Class 3 Recruitment 2026 – 155 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for GSSSB Female Health Worker Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 29 June 2026 (11:59 PM). The last date to pay the exam fee is 01 July 2026 (23:59 hrs).
Q2How many vacancies are available in GSSSB Female Health Worker Class-3 2026?
A
A total of 155 posts are available - 17 General, 15 EWS, 80 SEBC, and 43 ST. SC posts are nil in this notification. Divyang posts will be notified separately.
Q3Can male candidates apply for this recruitment?
A
No. These posts are exclusively reserved for female candidates only. Male candidates are not eligible to apply.
Q4What is the educational qualification required?
A
Candidates must have passed SSC or HSC and completed the Female Health Workers Basic Training Course, or the ANM Course and be registered with Gujarat Nursing Council. Basic computer knowledge is also required.
Q5What is the age limit for GSSSB Female Health Worker 2026?
A
The general upper age limit is 40 years as on 29 June 2026. Female candidates from General category get a 5-year relaxation (max 45 years), and reserved category females get 10-year relaxation (max 45 years).
Q6What is the exam fee for this recruitment?
A
The exam fee is ₹500 for General category and ₹400 for reserved category candidates (including all female, SEBC, SC, ST, EWS, Divyang, and Ex-Servicemen). Fee is refundable if the candidate appears for the exam.
Q7What is the selection process for GSSSB Female Health Worker Class-3?
A
Selection is through a single-stage MCQ-based written exam (CBRT or OMR), totalling 210 questions and 210 marks in 3 hours, followed by certificate verification and merit-based selection list preparation.
Q8What is the salary for Female Health Worker Class-3 in Gujarat?
A
Selected candidates receive a fixed pay of ₹26,000/- per month for the first five years. On satisfactory completion of five years, they are eligible for regularisation in Pay Level-2 (₹19,900 to ₹63,200) under the 7th Pay Commission.
Q9Is there negative marking in the exam?
A
Yes. 1/4 mark is deducted for every wrong answer. Unattempted questions carry no penalty.
Q10Where can I apply and where is the official website?
A
Applications must be submitted online at ojas.gujarat.gov.in. The official recruitment notification is available at gsssb.gujarat.gov.in.
