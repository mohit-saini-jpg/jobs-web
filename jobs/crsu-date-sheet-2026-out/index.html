How to Download CRSU Date Sheet 2026

Students can easily download the CRSU Date Sheet 2026 from the official university website. Follow these simple steps to get your exam timetable.

  • Visit the official CRSU website and go to the Date Sheet section.
  • Locate the May-June 2026 examination notices.
  • Click on the relevant date sheet link as per your course and semester.
  • The PDF will open in a new tab. Save the file to your device.
  • Take a printout and read all instructions carefully.
  • Cross-check your paper codes and exam dates before preparation.

Always refer to the latest official date sheet and keep a printed copy for your exam preparation.
