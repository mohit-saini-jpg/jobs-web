AP Senior Residents Recruitment 2026: Eligibility

Eligibility shall be as per the National Medical Commission (NMC) regulations . The candidate must hold a postgraduate degree MD/MS in the concerned speciality, or a DNB in Broad Specialties granted by a medical institution with an attached hospital, or by a hospital with a strength of 500 or more beds, granted by the National Board of Examination - such DNB holders are eligible only as per the Teachers Eligibility Qualifications in Medical Institutions Regulations, 2022. The candidate must be registered with the Andhra Pradesh Medical Council . Local candidates to Andhra Pradesh state are eligible for selection. Non-local candidates holding AP Medical Registration are eligible for Senior Residency only if local candidates are not available for a given post. As per the notification, a candidate is considered "local" if they studied at least 4 years from 4th to 10th class in Andhra Prades

AP Senior Residents Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

The merit scored in the postgraduate exam (including both theory and practical marks) will be the criteria for preparing the merit list and for further allotting postings as Senior Residents, following the Rule of Reservation (RoR). The Rule of Reservation will be followed as applicable under State and Subordinate Services. If more than one candidate has the same marks, the Date of Birth will be considered, with the elder candidate given first preference. The selection committee reserves the right to alter or modify the conditions laid down in the notification for final selection and the posts notified. There is no separate written examination or interview mentioned in the notification; selection is based on existing postgraduate exam merit.
