How to Download Bharathiar University M.Sc Rank List 2026

Candidates can access the rank list or selection list for their programme directly from the Bharathiar University official notifications page. Follow these steps:

  • Visit the official website: b-u.ac.in/notifications
  • Look for your programme name under the News section on the notifications page.
  • Click on the notification title for your respective M.Sc. programme.
  • Click on "View Rank List" or "View Selection List" as applicable.
  • The PDF will open or download — check for your name, register number, and rank.
  • Save a copy of the rank list for future reference during counselling.
