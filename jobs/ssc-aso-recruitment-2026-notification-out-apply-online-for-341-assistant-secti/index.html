SSC ASO Recruitment 2026 Notification Out : Eligibility & Qualification

Details
  1. The SSC ASO Limited Departmental Exam 2025 is not open to the general public. Only serving government officials of the specific feeder cadres are eligible. The crucial date for counting approved service is 1 July 2025 for both cadres. Candidates must ensure they meet the exact conditions as specified below before applying
  2. Any officer in the Upper Division Grade of the Central Secretariat Clerical Service who, as on 01 July 2025, has rendered not less than six years' approved service in that grade is eligible to appear
  3. Officers in the Upper Division Clerk Grade of the MEA Service who, as on 01 July 2025, have (a) successfully completed such mandatory training programme(s) as prescribed by the Ministry of External Affairs and (b) rendered not less than six years' approved service in the grade are eligible
  4. A candidate who resigns, quits service, or whose services are terminated after applying will not be eligible for appointment on the result of this examination. Similarly, candidates appointed to an ex-cadre post or another service on "transfer" without a lien in the SSA/UDC grade are not eligible. This does not apply to candidates on deputation with the approval of the competent authority

SSC ASO Recruitment 2026 Notification Out : Exam Pattern

The SSC Assistant Section Officer Exam 2026 is conducted in two parts. Part-I is a Computer Based Examination (CBE) carrying 300 marks, and Part-II involves Evaluation of Service Records (APARs) carrying 100 marks. The final merit is prepared on the combined aggregate of both parts. There is no separate qualifying exam - this is a competitive examination. | There is a negative marking of 0.25 marks (25%) for each wrong answer in both Paper-I and Paper-II. Candidates must attempt the examination only in the language (English or Hindi) opted in the application form. There will be no question paper in Braille. | One hundred marks are reserved for evaluation of Annual Performance Appraisal Reports (APARs). Candidates shortlisted based on their Part-I aggregate performance will proceed to Part-II. Marks from Part-II will be added to determine the final rank. Once APARs have been evaluated, no request for change in APAR grading will be entertained. | Also Read: SSC JSA / LDC LDCE 2025 Notification Out - Apply Online for 149 Group C Posts in 12 Departments

SSC ASO Recruitment 2026 Notification Out : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the SSC ASO Recruitment 2026 and who conducts it?
A
The SSC ASO Recruitment 2026 refers to the Assistant Section Officer / Assistant Grade Limited Departmental Competitive Examination, 2025, conducted by the Staff Selection Commission. It is a promotion-based exam open only to eligible serving government employees in the CSS (DoPT) and MEA (Cadre Cell) cadres.
Q2How many vacancies are available under SSC ASO Vacancy 2026?
A
A total of 341 vacancies are available - 318 in the Central Secretariat Service under DoPT and 23 in the Ministry of External Affairs (Cadre Cell). Of the CSS vacancies, 19 are reserved for PwBD candidates.
Q3What is the last date to apply for SSC ASO 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 16 July 2026 (up to 11:00 PM / 2300 hours). The printed hard copy must reach SSC (Northern Region), New Delhi through the Head of Department by 23 July 2026 (6:00 PM).
Q4What is the eligibility condition for SSC ASO Limited Departmental Exam 2025?
A
Candidates must be serving Upper Division Clerks / Senior Secretariat Assistants in CSS (DoPT) or MEA (Cadre Cell) with at least six years of approved service in the grade as on 01 July 2025. For MEA, completion of mandatory training programmes is also required.
Q5Is there an application fee for SSC ASO Apply Online 2026?
A
The notification does not specify any application fee for this Limited Departmental Competitive Examination, as it is an internal departmental exam. Candidates should refer to the official SSC notification at ssc.gov.in for confirmation.
Q6Where will the SSC ASO exam 2026 be held?
A
The Computer Based Examination will be held tentatively in August 2026 at centres in Delhi-NCR only. No other centre will be allotted, and no request for change of centre will be entertained under any circumstances.
Q7What is the exam pattern for the SSC Assistant Section Officer Exam 2026?
A
The exam has two parts. Part-I is a Computer Based Examination with two objective-type papers of 150 marks each (Paper-I and Paper-II), totalling 300 marks, with a duration of 3 hours each. Part-II is an evaluation of service records (APARs) for 100 marks. Total marks for final selection: 400.
Q8What is the salary for SSC ASO post?
A
The SSC ASO post is at Pay Level-7 in the 7th Pay Commission Pay Matrix, with a pay scale of Rs. 44,900 to Rs. 1,42,400 per month. It is a Group B Non-Gazetted post, and additional allowances apply as per government regulations.
Q9Can candidates from outside the two specified cadres apply for SSC ASO Recruitment 2026?
A
No. This is a Limited Departmental Competitive Examination. Only officers from the Central Secretariat Service (CSS) under DoPT and the Ministry of External Affairs (Cadre Cell) are eligible to apply. General public candidates cannot apply.
Q10What documents must be forwarded through the department for SSC ASO Apply Online 2026?
A
Along with the printed online application, candidates must forward a certified true copy of the first page of the Service Book, certified particulars of service, and disability certificate if applicable. These must be forwarded by the Head of Department or Head of Office to SSC (NR), New Delhi, by 23 July 2026.
