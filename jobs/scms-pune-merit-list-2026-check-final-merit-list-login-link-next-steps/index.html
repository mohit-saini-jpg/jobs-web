SCMS Pune Merit List 2026 - Check Final Merit List, Login Link & Next Steps

Symbiosis Centre for Management Studies (SCMS), Pune has released the SCMS Pune Merit List 2026 for BBA (Honours/Honours with Research) and Global BBA admissions on June 18, 2026. The final merit list has been prepared by combining each candidate's scaled Symbiosis Entrance Test (SET) score with their Online Personal Interaction (PI) score. Candidates who registered for SCMS Pune through SET 2026 can log in to the official result portal using their application credentials to check their merit status and rank.

This article explains how to check the SCMS Pune Merit List 2026, what the login portal requires, how the merit list was prepared, the seat matrix and reservation policy, and the steps candidates must follow after the result is declared.

What Is the SCMS Pune Merit List 2026?

This section explains what the merit list represents and why it matters for candidates seeking admission to SCMS Pune.

The SCMS Pune Merit List 2026 is the official, ranked list of candidates shortlisted for admission to the BBA (Honours/Honours with Research) and Global BBA programmes for the 2026-30 academic batch. It is the final and decisive stage of the SCMS Pune admission process, prepared only after both the entrance exam and interview rounds are completed and scored. Candidates whose names appear on this list are issued a provisional offer letter and must pay the admission fee within the specified deadline to confirm their seat.

SCMS Pune Merit List 2026 - Direct Login Link

This section provides the official link candidates can use to check their individual merit list status.

Candidates can check their SCMS Pune Merit List 2026 result by logging in to the official result portal below:

Result Portal: SCMS Pune Merit List 2026

This is a secure, candidate-specific login page. It does not display a public list of names; instead, each candidate must sign in with their own application number and password to view their personal result status, score breakup, and selection outcome. If you registered for SCMS Pune through the SET 2026 admission cycle, use the credentials created during your SET/PI registration to log in.

How to Check Your SCMS Pune Merit List 2026 Result

This section walks through the step-by-step process for accessing your individual result on the portal.

Follow these steps to check your SCMS Pune Merit List 2026 status online:

  • Visit the official result portal at set2026.ishinfosys.com/SYMFINAL-PIRE2026.
  • Enter your registered Application Number or User ID.
  • Enter your Password (created at the time of SET/PI registration).
  • Click on the Login button to proceed.
  • View your merit list status, rank, and combined score on the dashboard.
  • Download or take a screenshot of the result for future reference.

If you have forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password?" link available on the same login page to reset your credentials before retrying.

How the SCMS Pune Final Merit List Is Prepared

This section explains the formula and process used to rank candidates on the final merit list.

SCMS Pune does not rely on entrance exam marks alone. The final merit list is prepared by combining a candidate's SET score with their Online Personal Interaction (PI) score, using a fixed weightage formula. The table below shows how each component contributes to the final merit score.

ComponentWeightage
SET (Symbiosis Entrance Test) Score70%
Online Personal Interaction (PI) Score30%

Only candidates who cleared the SET cutoff and were shortlisted for the PI round were eligible to feature on the final merit list. The PI round assessed academic knowledge, communication skills, situational judgment, and general awareness of current economic and business affairs.

This section breaks down the total seat intake and category-wise reservation applicable for SCMS Pune BBA admissions.

SCMS Pune offers a total of 300 seats under its 4-year BBA (Honours/Honours with Research) programme. The seat distribution between Indian and international students is shown below.

SCMS Pune SET Cutoff Trends 2026

This section gives an indicative idea of the score range typically required to be shortlisted for the PI round at SCMS Pune.

While SCMS Pune releases its own official cutoff after SET results are validated, education portals tracking SET 2026 trends estimated the BBA cutoff for general category candidates to be in the range of approximately 36 to 48 marks out of 60, with relatively lower expected cutoffs for SC and ST category candidates. These figures are indicative estimates based on previous admission cycles and are not a substitute for the official cutoff published by SCMS Pune.

Eligibility Criteria for SCMS Pune BBA Admission 2026

This section outlines the basic academic eligibility candidates needed to meet to apply for SCMS Pune.

To be eligible for admission to the BBA (Honours/Honours with Research) programme at SCMS Pune, candidates were required to meet the following criteria:

  • Passed Class 12 (or equivalent) from any recognised Board, Council, or University, in any discipline.
  • Secured a minimum of 50% aggregate marks for General (Open) category candidates.
  • Secured a minimum of 45% aggregate marks for SC and ST category candidates.
  • Qualified the Symbiosis Entrance Test (SET) 2026.
  • Cleared the Online Personal Interaction (PI) round.

What Happens After the Merit List Is Released?

This section explains the steps candidates whose names appear on the merit list must complete next.

Once a candidate's name appears on the SCMS Pune Merit List 2026, the following steps apply:

  • A provisional offer letter is issued to the selected candidate.
  • The candidate must pay the admission fee within the deadline specified in the offer letter to confirm the seat.
  • Failure to pay within the stipulated deadline may result in the seat being offered to the next eligible candidate on the waiting list.
  • Selected candidates should keep all original academic documents ready for verification at the time of admission confirmation.
  • The new academic batch is expected to commence from July 31, 2026.
