RCFL Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1Has RCF officially released the Direct Recruitment 2026 notification?
A
Yes. Rashtriya Chemicals and Fertilizers Limited has officially released the RCF Recruitment 2026 notification under Advertisement No. 18032026, inviting applications for 32 vacancies across Officer, Engineer, Senior Engineer, Deputy Manager, and Senior Manager posts.
Q2What is the last date to apply for RCF Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 13 July 2026 at 5:00 PM. The online registration window opens on 27 June 2026 at 8:00 AM.
Q3What is the application fee for RCF Bharti 2026?
A
General, OBC (NCL), and EWS candidates must pay ₹1,000 plus bank charges and GST. SC, ST, PwBD, ExSM, and Female candidates are fully exempted from paying any application fee.
Q4How many total vacancies are there in RCF Recruitment 2026?
A
RCF Ltd has advertised a total of 32 vacancies across 11 post categories, including backlog vacancies under the Special Recruitment Drive for SC and ST candidates.
Q5What is the selection process for RCF Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
For E0 grade (Assistant Engineer – Electrical), selection is through a qualifying Skill Test. For all E1 grade and above posts — including all Officer, Engineer, Deputy Manager, and Senior Manager posts — selection is through a Personal Interview.
Q6Is M.Com degree acceptable for the Officer (Finance) post?
A
No. The RCF notification explicitly states that M.Com qualification will not be considered for the Officer (Finance) post. Candidates must hold CA/CMA or an MBA/MMS/PGDM with Finance specialization.
Q7What is the salary for RCF Engineer posts?
A
Engineers at E1 grade receive a pay scale of ₹40,000–₹1,40,000 with an approximate gross monthly salary of ₹86,960. Senior Engineers at E2 grade receive ₹50,000–₹1,60,000 with approximately ₹1,08,700 per month.
Q8Where will selected candidates be posted?
A
Selected candidates may be posted at any of RCF's units — Trombay (Mumbai) or Thal (Raigad) — or at area offices or marketing offices anywhere across India, including project sites, as decided by management. The job includes shift operations.
Q9What is the official website for RCF Recruitment 2026?
A
The official website is www.rcfltd.com. All recruitment-related updates, corrigenda, and call letters will be published under the HR→Recruitment section of this website only.
Q10Can candidates currently employed in government or PSU organizations apply?
A
Yes, but they must either forward their application through the proper channel or produce a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their present employer at the time of interview or Skill Test. Failure to produce an NOC will result in rejection of candidature.
