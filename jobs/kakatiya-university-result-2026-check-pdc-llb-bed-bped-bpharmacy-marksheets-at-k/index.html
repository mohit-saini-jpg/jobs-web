How to Check Kakatiya University Result 2026 Online

This section explains the exact step-by-step process to view your KU result on the official website using your hall ticket number.

  • Visit the official result portal at kuonline.co.in or kuexams.org.
  • On the homepage, locate the "Examinations" or "Results" section.
  • Click on the result link matching your specific course and semester (for example, "LLB III Semester Result 2026" or "PDC I Year Result 2026").
  • Enter your Hall Ticket Number in the field provided.
  • Enter the security/captcha code, if asked.
  • Click "Submit" or "Get Result".
  • Your result will be displayed on screen with subject-wise marks, total score, and result status.

How to Download Kakatiya University Marksheet PDF 2026

This section covers what your provisional marksheet contains and how to save it for future use.

Once your result appears on screen, look for a "Print" or "Download PDF" option to save a copy. The KU provisional marksheet typically includes:

  • Student name and hall ticket number
  • Course and semester/year
  • Subject-wise marks or grades
  • Total marks/CGPA
  • Overall result status (Pass/Fail/Reappear)

This online marksheet is provisional. Students should retain it for admission, scholarship, or job purposes until the university issues the official marks memo and provisional certificate.

Kakatiya University Passing Criteria 2026

This section answers how many marks are needed to clear a Kakatiya University examination.

To pass an examination at Kakatiya University, students generally need to meet the following requirements:

  • A minimum of 40% marks in each individual subject
  • A minimum of 40% aggregate across all subjects
  • For CBCS-pattern courses, the required minimum credits must also be earned
  • Separate minimum qualifying marks apply for theory and practical components as per university regulations

Students who do not meet these criteria in one or more subjects are classified under the supplementary category and can appear for the next available supplementary examination.

Kakatiya University Supplementary Exam Schedule

This section explains when students who failed a paper can reappear for supplementary exams.

Kakatiya University conducts supplementary examinations alongside the next regular examination cycle, depending on whether the failed semester was odd or even:

Semester TypeSupplementary Exam Held With
Odd Semester SupplementaryEven Semester Regular Exams (May/June)
Even Semester SupplementaryOdd Semester Regular Exams (November/December)

This means students don't need to wait a full year to clear a backlog supplementary exams are aligned with the next regular exam session of the opposite semester type.

How to Apply for Kakatiya University Revaluation 2026

This section explains the process for students who want their answer scripts re-checked, relevant especially to the B.Pharmacy VII Semester revaluation result mentioned above.

  • Visit kuexams.org.
  • Go to the Revaluation section.
  • Select your course, semester, and subject(s).
  • Pay the prescribed revaluation fee online.
  • Submit the application within the notified deadline.
  • The revised result, if any change occurs, is published separately as a revaluation result, similar to the B.Pharmacy VII Semester result released in January 2026.
