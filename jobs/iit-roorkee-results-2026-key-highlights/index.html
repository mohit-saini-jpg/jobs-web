How to Check Result

Follow these simple steps to check and download your JEE Advanced 2026 result and final answer key:

  • Visit the official website - https://cdata.jeeadv.ac.in/
  • Click the 'JEE Advanced 2026 Result' link or the result banner on the homepage.
  • Enter your registration number and date of birth (or other required credentials).
  • Submit the details to view your scorecard and final answer key.
  • Download the PDF of the result/scorecard and save it for future reference; print a copy if needed.
