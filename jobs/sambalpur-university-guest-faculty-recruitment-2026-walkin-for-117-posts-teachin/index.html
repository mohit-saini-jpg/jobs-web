Job Overview

Sambalpur University has released the official Assistant Professor (Guest Faculty) Notification 2026 at the official website. A total of 117 vacancies have been announced under the Sambalpur University Assistant Professor (Guest Faculty) Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates with qualifications as per UGC Guidelines 2018, including Post Graduate Degree with good academic record, UGC NET (preferred), and Ph.D. Degree (preferred for PG courses) can attend the walk-in interview from 15 May 2026 to 25 June 2026. FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies for Assistant Professor (Guest Faculty) at Sambalpur University?
A
Sambalpur University is recruiting for a total of 117 vacancies for the post of Assistant Professor (Guest Faculty).
Q2What is the application mode for this recruitment?
A
The application mode for this recruitment is Walk-in. Candidates need to attend the interview in person.
Q3What are the eligibility criteria for the Assistant Professor (Guest Faculty) post?
A
The eligibility criteria include having a Post Graduate Degree with a good academic record, and preferably a Ph.D. Degree (for PG courses) and UGC NET qualification, as per UGC Guidelines, 2018.
Q4What is the salary offered for the Assistant Professor (Guest Faculty) position?
A
The salary is Rs. 1,000/- per class for those with a Ph.D. (maximum 60 classes) and Rs. 750/- per class for those without a Ph.D. (maximum 60 classes).
Q5What is the age limit for retired faculty applying for this post?
A
Retired faculty members may continue to serve until they reach 70 years of age.
Q6When is the walk-in interview scheduled?
A
The walk-in interview is scheduled to start on 15th May 2026 and end on 25th June 2026.
Q7What documents should candidates bring for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates should bring all original documents, self-attested photocopies of these documents, and recent passport-size photographs.
Q8Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment.
Q9What is the selection process for the Assistant Professor (Guest Faculty) role?
A
The selection process includes General Career Assessment, Research Degree, Demo Class, Research Publications, and Viva-Voce.
Q10What is the tenure of engagement for the selected candidates?
A
The tenure of engagement for the selected candidates will start on 01st July 2026 and end on 31st May 2027.
