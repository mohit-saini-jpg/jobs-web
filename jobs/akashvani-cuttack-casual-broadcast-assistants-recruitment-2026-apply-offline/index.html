FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for Akashvani Cuttack Casual Broadcast Assistants posts?
A
The last date to submit the application for the Casual Broadcast Assistants posts at Akashvani Cuttack is 15th June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline.
Q2What is the educational qualification required for Casual Broadcast Assistants?
A
Candidates must possess a Bachelor's degree in any discipline along with a Degree/Diploma in Journalism and Mass Communication, Audio-Video Editing, Cinematography, or Sound Engineering. Proficiency in Odia/Hindi/English and computer skills are also necessary.
Q3What is the age limit for applying to this recruitment?
A
The age limit for Casual Broadcast Assistants is between 21 to 50 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per government norms.
Q4How can I apply for the Akashvani Cuttack Casual Broadcast Assistants posts?
A
The application process is offline. Candidates need to download the application form, fill it correctly, attach necessary documents, and send it via post/courier to the specified address before the last date.
Q5Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
Yes, there is an application fee. General category candidates need to pay Rs. 354/-, while SC/ST/OBC candidates need to pay Rs. 266/-.
Q6What is the selection process for Casual Broadcast Assistants?
A
The selection process includes a written test on general knowledge and current affairs, followed by a practical proficiency test in computer operations for various audio/video formats.
Q7What is the salary offered for the Casual Broadcast Assistants position?
A
The salary for the Casual Broadcast Assistants position has not been specified in the official notification.
Q8Where can I find the official notification and application form?
A
The official notification and application form can be downloaded from the official website of Akashvani, which is www.newsonair.gov.in.
Q9What are the required computer skills for this role?
A
Candidates need to have knowledge of computer applications and practical provisioning knowledge in audio editing capability. Proficiency in Odia, English, and Hindi typing is also required.
Q10Can I apply through online mode?
A
No, the application mode for this recruitment is strictly offline. Online applications will not be accepted.
