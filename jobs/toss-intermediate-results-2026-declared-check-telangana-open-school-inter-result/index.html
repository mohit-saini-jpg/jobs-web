How to Check TOSS Intermediate Result 2026 - Step by Step

Checking your result takes just a couple of minutes. Here's exactly what you need to do:

  • Go to the official website: Visit telanganaopenschool.org
  • Find the Results section: Look for "Results 2026" on the homepage
  • Click on Intermediate result: Select the "TOSS Inter Result 2026" link
  • Enter your details: Type in your Admission Number or Roll Number (and Date of Birth if asked)
  • Hit Submit: Click the "Submit" or "Get Result" button
  • View and save: Your result will show up on screen download the PDF and take a printout right away
