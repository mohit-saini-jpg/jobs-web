Steps to Check APPGCET Result 2026

  • Visit the official website cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/
  • Click on the APPGCET 2026 Result link.
  • Enter your registration number and date of birth.
  • Submit the details to view your result and rank.
  • Download and print the rank card for counselling purposes.
  • Keep a copy for future reference.
