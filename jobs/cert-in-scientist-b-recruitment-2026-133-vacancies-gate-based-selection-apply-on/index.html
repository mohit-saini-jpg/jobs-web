CERT-In Scientist-B Recruitment 2026: 133 Vacancies : Job Overview

The Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In), functioning under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), has invited online applications for 133 Scientist-'B' posts (equivalent to Group-'A') on a direct recruitment basis. Shortlisting for the Personal Interaction/Interview stage will be based on a valid GATE score of 2024, 2025, or 2026. Eligible candidates can apply online from 14 July 2026 to 17 August 2026 (5:30 PM).

CERT-In Scientist-B Salary 2026

The post of Scientist-'B' carries Level 10 of the 7th Central Pay Commission (7th CPC) , with a pay scale of Rs. 56,100 to Rs. 1,77,500 . Details of allowances (DA, HRA, TA) are not specified in the notification.

CERT-In Scientist-B Application Fee

The application fee is Nil for all categories - no fee payment is required for this recruitment.

CERT-In Scientist-B Selection Process 2026

GATE Score Shortlisting: Candidates are shortlisted for interview in the ratio of 1:5 (discipline-wise and category-wise), based on GATE score merit. All candidates with a tied score at the cut-off rank are shortlisted. Document Verification: Conducted before the interaction/interview. Candidates who fail to appear with original documents are not eligible for further stages. Personal Interaction/Interview: Maximum 50 marks, assessed on Technical (Academic) knowledge, Presentation/Communication Skill/Comprehension, and Academic performance (0.05 mark per percentage in the qualifying degree). Unreserved (UR): 50% EWS/OBC/SC/ST/PwBD: 40% Candidates who do not meet the minimum qualifying marks in the interview will not be considered for the final merit list, irrespective of GATE score.
