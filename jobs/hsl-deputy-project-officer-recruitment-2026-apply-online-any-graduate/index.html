Qualification / Eligibility

Full time Graduate / Diploma with not less than 55% marks in Mechanical / Electrical / Shipwright & Equivalent from a recognized university / deemed university / Institute recognized by AICTE. Essential Experience (as on 03-06-2026): Minimum 02 Years post qualification experience for graduate engineers OR minimum 05 years work experience for Diploma Engineers in any one or more of the following: Operation / Repairs / Refits / Maintenance / Trials / Inspection of Naval Ships / Submarine Steel works like Fabrication, Erection, Welding on naval ships/submarine Installation & Integration of equipment / systems and overseeing / quality assurance during construction of Naval Ships / Submarines Setting to Work, Functional Trials, and Harbour Trials of engineering / electrical / Hull equipment / systems for Naval Ships / Submarine. Desirable: Retired ex-servicemen of Indian Navy (ERA, EAP & SWA-3 / equivalent & above) of ERA / EAP / SWA / MECH cadre.
