How to Check MGU Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your Mahatma Gandhi University result online:

  • Visit the official MGU result portal - https://dsdc.mgu.ac.in/oldresult/result.php
  • Click on the relevant course and semester result link
  • Enter your PRN number or required login credentials
  • Click on the submit or view result button
  • Your result will be displayed on the screen
  • Download and take a printout for future reference
