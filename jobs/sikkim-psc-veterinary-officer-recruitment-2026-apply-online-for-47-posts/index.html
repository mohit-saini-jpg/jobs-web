Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Sikkim PSC Veterinary Officer recruitment?

The last date to submit the application form for the Sikkim PSC Veterinary Officer recruitment is 30th June 2026. Ensure your application is submitted well before this deadline.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for Veterinary Officers in Sikkim PSC?

There are a total of 47 vacancies announced for the post of Veterinary Officer by the Sikkim Public Service Commission in this recruitment drive.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Veterinary Officer post?

Candidates must possess a Bachelor of Veterinary Science and Animal Husbandry Science from a recognized University. Knowledge of any State Language and familiarity with Sikkim's customs are also required.

Q4. What is the age limit for applying to the Veterinary Officer position?

The age limit for the Veterinary Officer post is between 21 years and 40 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per government norms.

Q5. What is the application fee for the Sikkim PSC Veterinary Officer application?

The application fee for General/OBC and SC/ST categories is Rs 500/-. Please ensure the fee is paid correctly during the online application process.

Q6. What is the selection process for the Sikkim PSC Veterinary Officer recruitment?

The selection process for the Veterinary Officer post includes a Written Examination followed by a Scrutiny of Documents. Candidates must clear both stages to be considered.

Q7. What is the salary offered for the Veterinary Officer post?

The selected candidates for the Veterinary Officer post will be placed at Level-15 of the Pay Matrix, as per the government's pay scale regulations.

Q8. How can I apply for the Sikkim PSC Veterinary Officer vacancy?

Interested candidates can apply online through the official Sikkim PSC website. The application link and detailed instructions are available in the Important Links section.
