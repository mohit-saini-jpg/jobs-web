Selection Process

1Paper-II (CBT) qualifying: Candidates must clear the minimum marks fixed for Paper-II to have their Paper-I evaluated.
2Paper-I (Descriptive) evaluation: Only those qualifying in Paper-II have their descriptive paper assessed.
3APAR evaluation (Part-II): Based on the aggregate of Paper-I + Paper-II, shortlisted candidates move to service-record evaluation.
4Final merit: Prepared on the combined total of Paper-I + Paper-II + Part-II (APAR) = 260 marks, cadre-wise, subject to reservation. Ties are
5Paper-I (Short Essay): One essay of 400–500 words on any of the specified subjects.
6Paper-II (a) Language: Simple composition and applied grammar (in the chosen English/Hindi medium).
7Paper-II (b) General Knowledge: Current events, matters of everyday observation and experience, and general information about India.

How to Apply

  • 1Generate OTR on the new website. Visit ssc.gov.in and click "Register Now." Any OTR created on the old site (ssc.nic.in) will not work on the new portal.
  • 2Opt for Aadhaar-Based Authentication during OTR (recommended). Candidates who skip Aadhaar must upload the prescribed proof documents.
  • 3Log in with your Registration Number and password, then open the JSA/LDC LDCE 2025 link under the "Live Exam" tab and click Apply.
  • 4Fill the application - service details, cadre, crucial date, length of service, and age-relaxation code (if any). Information auto-filled from OTR is non-editable; use "Modify Registration" if a correction is needed.
  • 5Capture your live photograph and upload your signature as per the specifications (see below).
  • 6Upload the required documents (listed below) and Submit. Take a printout of the submitted application.
  • 7Forward the hard copy through proper channel. Your Head of Department / Head of Office must forward the printed application along with service documents to: The Regional Director, Staff Selection Commission (Northern Region), Block No. 12, CGO Complex, Lodhi Road, New Delhi – 110003, so as to reach by 03-07-2026 (6:00 PM) - or 10-07-2026 for candidates abroad/A&N/Lakshadweep.
  • 8Certified true copy of the first page of your Service Book (attested by HoD/HoO)
  • 9Certified particulars of service (posts held, scale of pay, nature of appointment)
  • 10Class 12th certificate / educational qualification proof (if applicable)
  • 11Caste/Category certificate (SC/ST), if claiming reservation
  • 12Disability certificate (Annexure-VII / VIII) for PwBD candidates
  • 13A recent passport-size photo for the live capture reference, and a scanned signature
  • 14Photograph: Captured live via webcam/phone, in a well-lit area with a plain background. No cap, mask, or spectacles. Face must fill the on-screen red rectangle. Pre-existing/scanned photos are rejected.
  • 15Signature: JPEG/JPG, 10–20 KB, dimensions about 6.0 cm × 2.0 cm. Sign so the signature fills at least 80% of the box. Blurred or miniature signatures are rejected.
  • 16Negative marking of 0.25 marks per wrong answer applies to Paper-II (CBT) only. There is no negative marking in the descriptive Paper-I.
  • 17Candidates must attempt the exam in the language (English/Hindi) chosen in the application form.
