How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the Mizoram Public Service Commission online portal (https://mpsconline.mizoram.gov.in/). No other mode of application will be accepted. The online portal is active from 08-05-2026 to 12-06-2026 (up to 4:00 PM). New users must complete one-time registration before 12-06-2026 (4:00 PM).
  • Steps to Apply Online:
  • Step 1: Visit MPSC Online Portal - Go to https://mpsconline.mizoram.gov.in/
  • Step 2: One-Time Registration (for New Users) - If you are a new user, complete one-time registration on the portal before 12-06-2026 (4:00 PM). Existing users can directly login.
  • Step 3: Login to Portal - Login using your registered credentials
  • Step 4: Fill Application Form - Fill the online application form carefully with all required details: Personal details Educational qualifications Nursing Council registration details Computer proficiency details Mizo language proficiency details Category details (if claiming PwBD/Ex-Servicemen reservation)
  • Personal details
  • Educational qualifications
  • Nursing Council registration details
  • Computer proficiency details
  • Mizo language proficiency details
  • Category details (if claiming PwBD/Ex-Servicemen reservation)
  • Step 5: Upload Documents - Upload scanned copies of all required documents as per specifications
  • Step 6: Pay Application Fee - Pay application fee online (if applicable) through available payment modes. PwBD candidates are exempted from fee payment.
  • Step 7: Submit Application - Review all details carefully and submit the application
  • Step 8: Verify Submission - Verify that your application status shows "Submitted" after payment. Take printout of submitted application for future reference.
  • Documents to be Uploaded with Online Application:
  • HSLC (Class 10th) Marksheet & Certificate
  • Higher Secondary (Class 12th) Marksheet & Certificate
  • Nursing qualification certificates (RNRM certificate for Nursing Officer / GNM certificate for Staff Nurse)
  • Nursing Council Registration Certificate (valid registration)
  • Computer Proficiency Certificate (as per Mizoram Group 'A', 'B' & 'C' posts Rules, 2023)
  • Mizo Language Proficiency Certificate (if applicable - exemption for those who studied Mizo up to Class-X or above)
  • Age Proof Certificate (10th certificate/Birth certificate)
  • PwBD Certificate issued by competent authority (if claiming PwBD reservation)
  • Ex-Servicemen Certificate (if claiming Ex-Servicemen reservation)
  • SC/ST certificate (if applicable)
  • Any other relevant documents as mentioned in the notification
  • Apply Online at: https://mpsconline.mizoram.gov.in/
