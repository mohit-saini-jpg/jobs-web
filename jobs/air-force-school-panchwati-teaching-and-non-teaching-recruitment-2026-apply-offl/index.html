Qualification / Eligibility

Principal: Masters Degree with minimum 50% aggregate from a recognized University in a subject taught in Secondary classes and B.Ed or equivalent, with at least two years of teaching experience as TGT or Principal of a Secondary/Primary Air Force School. TGT Science: Masters or Bachelors Degree with minimum 50% in subject and aggregate in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Botany, and Zoology (any three) and B.Ed or equivalent. TGT English: Masters or Bachelors Degree with minimum 50% in subject and aggregate with English as an elective and B.Ed or equivalent. TGT Social Science: Masters or Bachelors Degree with minimum 50% in subject and aggregate with any two of History, Geography, Economics, Political Science (one must be History or Geography) and B.Ed or equivalent. TGT Hindi: Masters or Bachelors Degree with minimum 50% in subject and aggregate with Hindi as an elective and B.Ed or equivalent. TGT Math (Contractual): Masters or Bachelors Degree with minimum 50% in subject and aggregate with Maths and any two of Physics, Chemistry, Electronics, Computer Science, Statistics and B.Ed or equivalent. HM: Graduate Degree with minimum 50% aggregate in a subject taught in Middle/Primary classes and B.Ed or equivalent, with at least two years of teaching experience as PGT/TGT/PRT or three years as NTT or two years as Principal/HM of an Air Force School. PRT: Senior Secondary with 50% marks and a two-year Diploma in Elementary Education or a four-year B.El.Ed or a two-year Diploma in Education (Special Education) or Graduation with 50% and B.Ed with a 6-month Bridge Course in Elementary Education from an NCTE recognized institution. NITT: Senior Secondary with Nursery Teachers Training diploma or Diploma in Nursery/Montessori/Pre-Primary Teachers Training or Diploma in Elementary Education from a Govt recognized institution. Office Superintendent: Graduate with at least five years of experience in office work. Accts Asst: B.Com from a Govt recognized University with knowledge of accounts and book-keeping, and a typing speed of at least 40 wpm in English. Clerk: Graduate from a Govt recognized University, with a typing speed of at least 40 wpm in English. MTS (contractual): No educational qualifications laid down, suitability for trade duties.
