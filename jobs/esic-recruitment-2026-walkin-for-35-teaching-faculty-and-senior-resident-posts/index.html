Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies announced by ESIC?

ESIC has announced a total of 35 vacancies for Teaching Faculty and Senior Resident posts through this walk-in interview drive.

Q2. What is the mode of application for ESIC recruitment?

The mode of application for this recruitment is Walk-in. Candidates need to attend the interview in person on the specified date and time.

Q3. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview?

The walk-in interview will be conducted on 30th May 2026. Candidates should ensure they reach the venue within the reporting time.

Q4. What is the educational qualification required for the posts?

The educational qualification required is as per NMC (MCI) guidelines as on 30.05.2026 for all the posts announced.

Q5. What is the age limit for Teaching Faculty and Senior Resident posts?

The maximum age limit for Teaching Faculty is 69 years, and for Senior Residents, it is 44 years. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories.

Q6. What is the salary offered for the Professor post?

The Professor post offers a fixed basic salary of ₹1,18,500/-.

Q7. Is there any application fee for SC/ST and Women candidates?

No, there is no application fee for SC/ST and Women candidates. General/OBC candidates need to pay Rs. 500/-.

Q8. What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?

Candidates should carry all original documents, self-attested photocopies, and recent passport-size photographs.
