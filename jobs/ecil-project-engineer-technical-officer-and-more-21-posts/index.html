FAQs

Q1What are the walk-in dates for ECIL Recruitment 2026?
A
The walk-in dates vary by zone: Kolkata (East Zone) on 15-06-2026; Hyderabad (HQ), Mumbai (West Zone), and New Delhi (North Zone) on 18-06-2026; and Chennai (South Zone) on 19-06-2026. Registration is between 09:00 hrs and 11:30 hrs at the respective venue.
Q2How many total posts are notified in ECIL Walk-in Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 22 posts are notified across four post categories — Project Engineer on Contract (PE-C), Technical Officer on Contract (TO-C), Assistant Project Engineer on Contract (APE-C), and Senior Artisan on Contract (SA-C) — spread across five zones (Hyderabad, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, New Delhi).
Q3What is the eligibility for ECIL PE-C (Project Engineer on Contract) post?
A
Candidates must hold a B.E./B.Tech. degree with at least 60% aggregate marks in the relevant discipline, along with a minimum of 3 years of post-qualification experience (apprenticeship up to 1 year also counted). The upper age limit for UR category is 33 years as on the walk-in date.
Q4What is the salary / emoluments for ECIL Project Engineer on Contract (PE-C)?
A
PE-C receives Rs. 40,000/month in Year 1, Rs. 45,000/month in Year 2, Rs. 50,000/month in Year 3, and Rs. 55,000/month in Year 4. Additionally, a one-time lump sum attire allowance of Rs. 8,400/- is paid after completing at least 6 months of tenure.
Q5Is ECIL Walk-in Recruitment 2026 a permanent job?
A
No. This is purely a temporary/contract engagement for an initial period of 1 year, extendable up to 4 years (including the initial term) depending on project requirements and satisfactory performance. It does not create any right to regularization or permanent employment in ECIL.
Q6Can I apply for multiple posts in ECIL Walk-in Recruitment 2026?
A
No. Candidates must apply for one category of post only. Applying to multiple posts is strictly not allowed.
Q7What documents must I carry to the ECIL walk-in?
A
You must carry the duly filled application form and resume, 10th certificate (DOB proof), qualification certificates and mark sheets, CGPA conversion certificate (if applicable), experience certificates (mentioning duration and post held), Appointment Order and recent pay slips (if currently employed), category certificate (EWS/OBC-NCL/SC/ST), and a government-issued photo identity proof — all in original along with one set of self-attested photocopies.
Q8What is the selection method for ECIL Walk-in Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection is based on merit combining three criteria: Qualification (20% of aggregate percentage), Relevant Experience (maximum 30 marks — weightage varies by post), and Personal Interview (50 marks). Only candidates who clear document verification will be allowed to participate in the interview.
Q9Is internship or project work counted as experience for ECIL posts?
A
No. Experience from Academic Institutions, Colleges, Internships, or Project work is explicitly NOT considered for post-qualification experience calculation. Only industry experience from registered organizations and relevant apprenticeship (up to a maximum of 1 year) will be counted.
Q10Where can I get updates and corrigendum for ECIL Walk-in Recruitment 2026?
A
All updates, corrigendum (if any), and official information will be published only on the official ECIL website www.ecil.co.in. Candidates must not rely on other websites, social media handles, or third-party publishers for any recruitment-related information.
