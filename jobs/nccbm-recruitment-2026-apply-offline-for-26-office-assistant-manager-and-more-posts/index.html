Selection Process

  • Selection will be based on Screening of applications followed by Trade/Skill Test
  • Written Examination and/or Personal Discussion as applicable to the post. Final selection is subject to document verification and medical fitness.

How To Apply

  • Candidates should download the application form from the NCB website and send the duly filled application along with all required self-attested documents by post to the given address before the last date.
  • Last Date for Receipt of Applications: 21 days from the date of publication of the advertisement (first day of the week) of publication in the Employment News.
  • The candidates are requested to download application form (available at NCB website) and submit the same duly filled with recent passport size photograph (2 nos.) giving all details as mentioned in point no. 12 along with self-attested documents copies as mentioned this advertisement to The Director General, National Council for Cement and Building Materials, 34 KM Stone, Delhi Mathura Road, (NH-2), Ballabgarh, Haryana, 121004. Applications through email will not be accepted.
  • Application once made will not allowed to be withdrawn and fees once paid will neither be refunded nor can be held for any other recruitment or selection process.

Important Instructions

  • Applications received after the last date or incomplete applications will be rejected. Separate application should be submitted for each post. No email applications will be accepted. Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification.
