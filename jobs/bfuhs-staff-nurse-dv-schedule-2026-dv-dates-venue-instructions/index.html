BFUHS Staff Nurse DV Schedule 2026 - DV Dates, Venue & : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the BFUHS Staff Nurse DV date 2026?
A
The BFUHS Staff Nurse Document Verification is scheduled from 27 June 2026 to 06 July 2026. Each candidate has been assigned a specific date and time based on their category and marks scored in the written test.
Q2Where will the BFUHS Staff Nurse DV 2026 be held?
A
The document verification will be held at the Auditorium, Guru Gobind Singh Medical College (GGS Medical College), Faridkot, Punjab.
Q3What is the minimum cutoff for the UR category in BFUHS Staff Nurse 2026?
A
Candidates from the Unreserved (UR) / General category must have scored 66 marks or above in the written test to be called for document verification.
Q4What documents are required for BFUHS Staff Nurse DV 2026?
A
Candidates must carry originals and one set of self-attested photocopies of their application printout, admit card, photo ID, age proof, Punjabi at Matric proof, academic certificates, category certificate (if applicable), NOC from employer (if in government service), and experience certificate.
Q5What is the counselling fee for BFUHS Staff Nurse DV 2026?
A
The counselling fee is Rs. 1,000/- for General/Other category candidates and Rs. 500/- for SC category candidates. This fee is to be paid on the spot at the DV venue.
