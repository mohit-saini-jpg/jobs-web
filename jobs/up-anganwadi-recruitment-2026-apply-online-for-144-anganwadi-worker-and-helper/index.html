UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The District Programme Officer (Jila Karyakram Adhikari), Mainpuri has released an official notification inviting online applications from eligible women candidates for the recruitment of Anganwadi Karyakartri (Worker) and Anganwadi Sahayika (Helper) posts across 10 Bal Vikas Pariyojnas (Child Development Projects) in Mainpuri district, Uttar Pradesh. This recruitment is conducted as per Government Order No. 1/1092245/2025/3313/58-1-2025(1917687) dated 17.09.2025, through the state-level departmental portal upanganwadibharti.in.

UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Only women candidates are eligible to apply for both Karyakartri and Sahayika posts. Candidates must meet the educational qualification and age limit prescribed below. In rural areas, priority is given to candidates residing in the same gram sabha/ward as per the selection priority order (see Selection Process section). The minimum educational qualification for both Anganwadi Karyakartri and Sahayika posts is Intermediate (12th) or equivalent. The maximum qualification considered for merit preparation is Postgraduate - meaning merit will be prepared taking into account qualifications up to the postgraduate level. The date of birth recorded in the High School certificate/marksheet will be considered valid for age calculation. Category-wise age relaxation, if any, is not mentioned in the official notification.

UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection is merit-based, prepared according to educational qualification, with a defined priority order applicable in rural areas for candidates residing in the same gram sabha/ward. The official notification specifies the following selection priority flow chart: Widow Legally divorced / abandoned (parityakta) woman Other BPL woman Widow Legally divorced / abandoned (parityakta) woman Other APL woman If no candidate below the poverty line is found in the above priority order, preference within the same priority order will then be given to a woman living above the poverty line. Details of any separate written examination or interview are not mentioned in the official notification.

Required Documents

For widow candidates: Death certificate issued by the Gram Panchayat Officer/concerned Nagar Nikay, along with a certified copy of the family register (parivar panjika). For legally divorced/abandoned candidates: A valid legal order issued by the competent court. Residence certificate and caste certificate: Only online, digitally signed certificates issued from the Tehsil level, verifiable online, will be accepted. Income certificate: Issued by the competent authority as per Government Order No. 3959/1-9-11-Raj0-9 dated 30.09.2011 (as amended), verifiable online. Educational qualification certificates/marksheets (High School and above, as applicable). Self-attested, clean and legible scanned copies of all documents mentioned in the online application form.
