Doon University Merit List 2026 - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 18/07/2026

Doon University, Dehradun has released its Admission Schedule 2026-27 for undergraduate and postgraduate programmes. As per the official schedule, the Merit List for Doon University's Entrance Test-based admissions was scheduled to be published on 10 July 2026. This article covers the confirmed dates, how the merit list will be published, and the counselling process that follows. Candidates should check the official website (doonuniversity.ac.in) directly to confirm whether the list has gone live, as this is not confirmed at the time of writing.

How the Merit List Is Published

According to the official admission announcement, Doon University follows this process:

  • Entrance test merit lists are displayed on the university's official website there is no separate download portal.
  • Separate lists are prepared for the General Category and for Reservation Categories.
  • Selected applicants must report for admission counselling and complete all formalities; failure to do so results in automatic forfeiture of the admission offer.
  • If the number of applications received for the entrance test is less than the total intake for a course, the entrance test may not be conducted for that course, and admission may instead be based on marks in the qualifying examination.

How to Check Doon University Merit List 2026

  • Visit the official Doon University website (doonuniversity.ac.in) or the SAMARTH admission portal.
  • Look for the Merit List / Result notice under the Notice Board or Admissions section.
  • Separate lists will be available for General Category and Reservation Category candidates.
  • Locate your application/roll number in the relevant programme-wise list.
  • If selected, note your counselling date and required documents.
  • Report for counselling between 20 and 31 July 2026 (as per the official schedule) with original documents for verification.

Important Instructions

  • No individual correspondence is made regarding admission all updates are published only on the university website.
  • Candidates are advised to keep checking the official website regularly for updates.
  • Final admission is subject to submission and verification of original documents at the time of counselling.
  • If a selected candidate does not report for counselling, the seat offer is automatically forfeited.
