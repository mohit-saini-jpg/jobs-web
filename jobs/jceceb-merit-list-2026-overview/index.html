Result Release Date

The Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) released the JCECEB Merit List 2026 on 05 July 2026 (05.07.2026). This merit list has been published in a multi-page PDF format on the JCECEB official website and covers candidates who registered for counselling using their JEE (Main) 2026 credentials.

Is the JCECEB Merit List 2026 Released?

Yes. The JCECEB Merit List 2026 for the 1st Round of Online Counselling has been officially released and is available for download as a PDF on the official JCECEB website. The list has been prepared strictly on the basis of the JEE (Main) 2026 Final Total NTA Score and All India Rank (AIR) submitted by candidates during counselling registration.

How to Check the JCECEB Merit List 2026

Candidates can check and download the JCECEB Merit List 2026 by following these steps:

  • Visit the official website of JCECEB at jceceb.jharkhand.gov.in.
  • Go to the "Counselling" or "Notice/Downloads" section on the homepage.
  • Click on the link titled "State Merit List on the basis of JEE (Main) 2026 for 1st Round of Online Counselling."
  • The merit list will open as a PDF document.
  • Use the search/find function (Ctrl+F) in the PDF reader to locate your Application Number or Roll Number.
  • Download and save the PDF for future reference.

Details Mentioned in the JCECEB Merit List

As per the official PDF, the JCECEB Merit List 2026 includes the following fields for each candidate:

  • Application Number
  • JEE (Main) 2026 Roll Number (Session 1 and Session 2, where applicable)
  • Candidate's Name, Gender, and Date of Birth
  • Category (General, EWS, BC-I, BC-II, SC, ST, etc.)
  • JEE (Main) 2026 Final Total NTA Score
  • JEE (Main) 2026 All India Rank (AIR)
  • Common Merit List (CML) Rank and category-wise rank (EWS Rank, Category Rank, PTG Rank, SMQ Rank, TFW Rank, as applicable)
  • Remarks column, where relevant (e.g., document-related notes)

For privacy reasons, individual candidate records are not reproduced on this page. Candidates must check their own rank directly in the official PDF using their application number.

What to Do After Checking the Merit List

Candidates who find their name in the JCECEB Merit List 2026 should take the following steps:

  • Download and keep a printed copy of the merit list page showing your rank.
  • Wait for the official choice-filling and seat allotment schedule to be announced by JCECEB.
  • Keep all required original documents and certificates ready for verification.
  • Regularly monitor the official JCECEB website and registered mobile number/email for further counselling updates.

Documents Required for Counselling

Based on JCECEB's standard counselling requirements, candidates are generally required to keep the following documents ready (to be reconfirmed against the official counselling notice):

  • JEE (Main) 2026 Admit Card and Scorecard
  • Class 10 and Class 12 Mark Sheets and Certificates
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/BC-I/BC-II/EWS), if applicable
  • Jharkhand Local/Permanent Resident (Domicile) Certificate, if claiming state quota
  • PWD/PTG/TFW/SMQ certificates, if applicable
  • Recent passport-size photographs
  • Valid photo ID proof

Exact document requirements and verification dates will be confirmed only through the official JCECEB counselling notice, which has not yet been separately detailed for this round at the time of writing.

Selection Process / Counselling Process

JCECEB counselling for B.E./B.Tech admission through JEE (Main) generally follows this process:

  • Online counselling registration by eligible JEE (Main) rank holders.
  • Preparation and release of the State Merit List (as released for Round 1 on 05.07.2026).
  • Online choice filling and locking of preferred institutes/branches by candidates.
  • Seat allotment based on rank, category, and choices filled.
  • Document verification at designated helpline centres.
  • Reporting to the allotted institute for final admission confirmation.

Note: Inclusion in the merit list does not guarantee a seat. Final seat allotment depends on rank, category, choices filled, and seat availability at the time of each counselling round.

Important Instructions

  • Candidates should verify their name, rank, category, and score in the merit list carefully and report any discrepancy to JCECEB through the official contact channels.
  • The merit list is provisional in nature for counselling purposes; final admission is subject to document verification and seat availability.
  • Candidates should not rely on unofficial sources for their rank confirmation; always cross-check with the official JCECEB PDF.
  • Keep checking the official website regularly, as JCECEB updates counselling schedules and subsequent round dates progressively.
