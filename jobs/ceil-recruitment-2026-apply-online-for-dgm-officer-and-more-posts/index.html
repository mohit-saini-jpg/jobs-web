Officer (F&A): Graduate (full time with minimum 60% marks) from a recognized university having professional qualification as Chartered Accountant (CA) from The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India OR Cost Accountant (CMA) from The Institute of Cost Accountants of India. Minimum One years of Post qualification working experience.
