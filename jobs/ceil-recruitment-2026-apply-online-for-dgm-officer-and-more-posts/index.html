Job Overview

The Certification Engineers International Limited (CEIL) has officially announced the Finance & Accounts Personnel Recruitment 2026 with 3 openings. The detailed notification was published under Advertisement No. CEIL/HR&A/Advt./2026-27/01 on the official CEIL website. Qualification / Eligibility

Deputy General Manager (F&A)/Asst. General Manager(AGM)(F&A): Graduate (full time with minimum 55% marks) from a recognized university having professional qualification as Chartered Accountant (CA) from The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India OR Cost Accountant (CMA) from The Institute of Cost Accountants of India. Minimum 18 years of Post qualification working Experience for AGM(F&A) and Minimum 22 years of Post qualification working Experience for DGM(F&A). Deputy Manager (F&A): Graduate (full time with minimum 60% marks) from a recognized university having professional qualification as Chartered Accountant (CA) from The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India OR Cost Accountant (CMA) from The Institute of Cost Accountants of India. Minimum six years of Post qualification working experience. Officer (F&A): Graduate (full time with minimum 60% marks) from a recognized university having professional qualification as Chartered Accountant (CA) from The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India OR Cost Accountant (CMA) from The Institute of Cost Accountants of India. Minimum One years of Post qualification working experience.
