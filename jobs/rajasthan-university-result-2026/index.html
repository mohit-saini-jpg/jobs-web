Rajasthan University Result 2026

Rajasthan University has officially released the Result 2026 for various UG and PG courses including BA LLB 7th Semester (Revaluation) and Master of Museology and Conservation 3rd Semester examinations. Students who appeared for these exams can now check their results online.

Rajasthan University Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - uniraj.ac.in. The results are declared for examinations conducted in December 2025 and released on 19-05-2026.

Students can check and download their results by entering their roll number or login credentials through the official portal. Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the result. In case of any discrepancies, they should contact the university authorities immediately.

How to Check Rajasthan University Result 2026

Follow these steps to check your result:

  • Visit the official website - uniraj.ac.in
  • Click on the "Results" section
  • Select your course and semester result link
  • Enter your roll number
  • Click on submit
  • Your result will appear on the screen
  • Download and print the result for future use
