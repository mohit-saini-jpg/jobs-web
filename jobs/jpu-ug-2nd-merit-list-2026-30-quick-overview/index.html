JPU UG Admission 2026-30 in brief: Jai Prakash University, Chapra (Bihar) is admitting students to B.A., B.Sc., and B.Com. programmes under the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) for the 2026-30 batch. Applications were submitted online via the Samarth Admission Portal, the application fee is ₹500, and admission is based on merit, not an entrance exam. How to Apply for JPU UG Admission 2026-30

  • Visit the official Samarth Admission Portal of JPU at jpvadm.samarth.edu.in.
  • Click Register and create an account using your own active mobile number and email ID (not a cybercafe's or another person's).
  • Log in and fill out the online application form with personal, academic, category, and contact details exactly as per your official documents.
  • Select up to 3 programmes; for each, choose 1 district and up to 5 colleges in order of preference.
  • Upload all required documents in the prescribed format and size, ensuring they are clear and readable.
  • Review all entries carefully - corrections after final submission may not be allowed, or only as per university rules.
  • Pay the ₹500 application fee online through the available payment mode.
  • Download and safely keep the submitted application form, payment receipt, and acknowledgement.
  • After the merit list is released, log in again, check your Seat Allocation, and click Accept Allocation if you wish to take the allotted seat.
  • Print the Admission Offer Letter generated after acceptance and visit the allotted college by the notified deadline for document verification.
  • Once the college verifies your documents and accepts your admission, it will lock your fee; log in again to pay the fee online (candidates with a fee exemption should only click Submit).
  • After successful payment/submission, an Acknowledgement Letter is generated automatically, and your admission is finally locked.

Important Instructions

  • Read the admission notification, eligibility criteria, subject combination, reservation rules, fee details, and important dates carefully before applying.
  • Reservation benefits apply as per Government of Bihar rules; candidates claiming reservation must upload valid certificates in the prescribed format.
  • Use your own personal, active mobile number and email ID for registration throughout the admission process.
  • Complete each step of the Samarth portal in sequence - do not skip ahead before completing the previous step.
  • Blurred, incorrect, incomplete, or invalid uploaded documents may lead to rejection of the application or cancellation of admission.
  • Submission of the online form alone does not guarantee admission - it remains subject to eligibility, merit, seat availability, document verification, and university rules.
  • Any false, incorrect, incomplete, or misleading information/document provided at any stage may lead to rejection or cancellation of the application/admission.
  • Use a laptop or desktop for a better experience; avoid refreshing the page, using the back button, or attempting multiple payments during the fee payment process.
  • Regularly check the JPU website and Samarth Admission Portal for merit list, counselling, document verification, and fee payment updates.
  • For technical issues, contact the official helpdesk at [email protected] or through the number mentioned in the admission notification.
