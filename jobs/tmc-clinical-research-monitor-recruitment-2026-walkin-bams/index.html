FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for the Clinical Research Monitor post at TMC?
A
The walk-in interview for the Clinical Research Monitor post at Tata Memorial Centre is scheduled for 11th June 2026. Candidates should ensure they reach the venue on time with all required documents.
Q2Q2. What are the essential qualifications required for the Clinical Research Monitor position?
A
Candidates must possess an MBBS/BDS/BAMS/MHMS/BUMS degree along with a PG Diploma in Clinical Research. A D. Pharm qualification is also required. One year of experience in a relevant field is considered desirable.
Q3Q3. What is the age limit for the Clinical Research Monitor role at TMC?
A
The maximum age limit for candidates applying for the Clinical Research Monitor position is 45 years. Age relaxation may be applicable as per government norms.
Q4Q4. What is the salary offered for the Clinical Research Monitor post?
A
The selected candidate for the Clinical Research Monitor position will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 50,000 per month. This is a contractual appointment.
Q5Q5. How many vacancies are available for the Clinical Research Monitor post?
A
There is one vacancy available for the Clinical Research Monitor post at Tata Memorial Centre. This recruitment is being conducted through a walk-in interview process.
Q6Q6. What is the application fee for this recruitment?
A
There is no application fee required for any category of candidates (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive. Candidates can attend the walk-in interview without paying any fee.
Q7Q7. What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents, including educational certificates, experience certificates (if any), ID proof, and category certificates (if applicable), along with one set of self-attested photocopies and recent passport-size photographs.
Q8Q8. What is the selection process for the Clinical Research Monitor role?
A
The selection process primarily involves a Walk-in Interview. If there are a large number of candidates, an MCQ Test may be conducted to shortlist applicants for the interview.
Q9Q9. Where is the walk-in interview venue?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at Tata Memorial Centre, Mumbai, Maharashtra, with the pincode 400012. The exact address and reporting time will be available in the official notification.
Q10Q10. Is this a permanent government job?

No, the Clinical Research Monitor position at Tata Memorial Centre is a contractual job. The appointment is for a specific period as per the terms and conditions mentioned in the official notification.
