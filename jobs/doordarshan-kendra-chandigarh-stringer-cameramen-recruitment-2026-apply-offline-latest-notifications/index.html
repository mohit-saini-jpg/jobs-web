Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for Doordarshan Kendra Chandigarh Stringer/ Cameramen Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the application for the Stringer/ Cameramen posts is 15th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the specified address before this date.

Q2. What is the application fee for this recruitment?

The application fee for all categories is Rs. 1180, which includes 18% GST. This fee is non-refundable.

Q3. How can I pay the application fee?

The application fee must be paid through a Demand Draft drawn in favour of the Drawing & Disbursing Officer, Doordarshan Kendra, Chandigarh.

Q4. What are the essential qualifications for the Stringer/ Cameramen post?

Candidates must be graduates with proven experience in visual coverage, cinematography, videography, news gathering, or broadcast journalism. Additionally, they must own a four-wheel vehicle.

Q5. What is the salary structure for Stringer/ Cameramen?

For local coverage, the remuneration is Rs. 1500 for up to 6 hours and Rs. 1000 for an additional 6 hours. Outstation coverage is compensated at Rs. 1800.

Q6. What is the selection process for these positions?

The selection process involves preliminary scrutiny, a test coverage, an interview, verification of equipment and documents, followed by the final list of selected candidates.

Q7. Is there any age limit for applying?

The official notification does not specify a particular age limit. Candidates are advised to refer to the detailed notification for any age-related criteria or relaxation details.

Q8. Where should I send the application form?

The completed application form should be sent in a sealed cover superscribed "Empanelment of Stringers of DD News" to: HEAD, REGIONAL NEWS UNIT, DOORDARSHAN KENDRA, SECTOR 37, NEAR BATRA CINEMA, CHANDIGARH-160036.
