How to Check Calicut University Revaluation Result 2026 Online

Checking your revaluation result on the official portal is straightforward. The steps below walk you through the process so you can access your updated marks quickly and without confusion.

  • Visit the official Calicut University results portal: results.uoc.ac.in
  • On the homepage, look for the Revaluation Results section or search for your specific programme notification.
  • Click on the relevant result link corresponding to your programme, semester, and examination month.
  • Enter your Register Number and any other required credentials.
  • Your revaluation result will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download and save a copy of your result for your records.

Tip: If the portal is slow due to high traffic, try accessing it during off-peak hours (early morning or late evening).

What Is Revaluation?

Revaluation is a formal process through which a student requests the university to re-examine their answer script to ensure the marking was accurate and no answers were left unevaluated. The University of Calicut allows eligible students to apply for revaluation after their regular examination results are declared.

Who Is Eligible?

Students who appeared for the relevant examinations under the CBCSS (Choice Based Credit and Semester System) - both in regular mode and through the School of Distance Education (SDE) - and submitted valid revaluation applications within the prescribed time frame are eligible to check these results.
