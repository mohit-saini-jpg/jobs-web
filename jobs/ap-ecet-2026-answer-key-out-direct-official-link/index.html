AP ECET 2026 Answer Key Out - Direct Official Link

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) Anantapur, on behalf of Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE), has published the AP ECET 2026 preliminary answer key for the exam conducted in April 2026. The answer key has been released in online mode only, along with the candidate response sheet, so that students can cross-check their answers and estimate their probable scores before the declaration of result.

How to Download AP ECET 2026 Answer Key

Step-by-step process to download AP ECET 2026 Answer Key

Follow these steps to download the official AP ECET answer key from the APSCHE portal:

  • Visit the official AP ECET 2026 website: cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/ECET.
  • On the homepage, look for the link titled “AP ECET 2026 Answer Key”, “Preliminary Key”, or “Master Question Paper with Key”.
  • Click on the relevant link; you will be redirected to a page listing branch-wise or paper-wise answer key PDFs.
  • Select your branch/subject and, if applicable, your question paper set/code.
  • The answer key PDF will open in a new tab or window.
  • Download the PDF and save it on your device for further reference and score calculation.

You may also find the master question paper along with the key, which is helpful if you want to review the entire paper.

How to Download AP ECET 2026 Response Sheet

Steps to access AP ECET 2026 response sheet and question paper

The response sheet shows the options you selected during the exam, which you can compare with the official key:

  • Go to the AP ECET official website: cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/ECET.
  • Click on the link for “Candidate Response Sheet”, “Response Sheet Download”, or a similar label.
  • Enter your hall ticket/registration number and date of birth in the login window.
  • Submit the details to view your response sheet on the screen.
  • Download or print the response sheet so that you can tally each response with the official answer key.

In many cases, the system may also provide a download link for your question paper along with the response sheet, which helps in detailed analysis.

How to Calculate Marks Using AP ECET 2026 Answer Key

Marking scheme and calculation method

Once you have both the answer key and your response sheet, you can estimate your AP ECET score using the following method:

  • Check each question in your response sheet and compare it with the official correct option given in the answer key.
  • Count the total number of correct answers you have marked.
  • Multiply the number of correct responses by the marks allotted per question as per the AP ECET exam pattern.
  • If there is negative marking (as specified in the information brochure/notification), subtract the penalty for incorrect answers; if there is no negative marking, you only add marks for correct responses.

The final total will give you an approximate score, which can help you understand your chances of qualifying and securing a good rank even before the result is announced.

AP ECET 2026 Answer Key Objection Process

How to raise objections against AP ECET 2026 answer key

The preliminary answer key is released so that candidates can verify and challenge any doubtful answers within a specific time window:

  • Visit the official AP ECET website and open the link for “Objections on Preliminary Key” or “Answer Key Challenge”.
  • Log in using your hall ticket/registration number and other required credentials.
  • Select the paper/branch and the specific question(s) you want to challenge.
  • Provide your answer/justification and upload supporting documents such as textbook references or authentic sources.
  • Pay the prescribed objection fee per question, if applicable, through the available online payment modes.
  • Submit the objections before the last date and time mentioned in the notification; late objections are usually not accepted.

After the expert committee examines all challenges, the final answer key is released. The AP ECET result and ranks are prepared strictly based on this final key.

AP ECET 2026 Eligibility (Quick Reminder for New Visitors)

If a user lands directly on this answer key page, a short eligibility recap is helpful:

  • AP ECET is conducted for diploma holders in Engineering/Technology and Pharmacy, and B.Sc. (Mathematics) graduates, seeking lateral entry into B.E./B.Tech/B.Pharmacy courses in colleges of Andhra Pradesh.
  • Candidates must meet the minimum qualifying marks in their diploma/degree and satisfy the local/non-local criteria and other conditions laid down by APSCHE.
  • For exact and latest eligibility conditions (age, category-wise relaxations, academic requirements), candidates should refer to the official AP ECET 2026 notification and information brochure.

AP ECET 2026 Answer Key - Key Points for Candidates

  • The AP ECET 2026 answer key is released online only on the official APSCHE portal.
  • The response sheet is provided individually for each candidate, accessible via login.
  • Use both the key and response sheet together to estimate your marks accurately.
  • The objection window is time-bound; make sure to submit challenges (if any) before the last date.
  • The final answer key, released after objections, is the basis for result preparation and rank calculation.
