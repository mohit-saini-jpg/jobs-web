How to Apply

  • 1Duly filled Registration Form as per Annexure I (with self-attested photograph affixed)
  • 2Undertaking as per Annexure II
  • 3NATS / NAPS profile printout
  • 410th Mark sheet and Certificate
  • 5Mark sheets of qualifying examinations for all semesters / year-wise as applicable
  • 6Degree / Diploma / ITI Certificate / Provisional Degree Certificate
  • 7Aadhaar Card
  • 8Caste Certificate – SC / ST / OBC Non-Creamy Layer (if applicable)
  • 9Two recent passport size photographs
  • 10Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply.
  • 11Candidates must be registered on NATS (Graduate/Diploma) or NAPS (ITI) portal before attending the walk-in interview.
  • 12Only candidates who have passed the qualifying examination in the year 2022 or later are eligible; those who passed before 2022 are NOT eligible.
  • 13Only candidates who have passed as regular candidates are eligible; candidates who passed through distance or correspondence mode are not eligible.
  • 14Candidates with higher qualifications than the essential qualification prescribed are NOT eligible.
  • 15Candidates with training or job experience of one year or more after attaining the essential qualification are NOT eligible.
  • 16Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing apprenticeship training under any organisation are NOT eligible.
  • 17Reservations will be applied as per provisions of The Apprentices Act, 1961; SC/ST/OBC Non-Creamy Layer candidates must produce relevant certificates issued by the Competent Authority.
  • 18PWD candidates are eligible only for designated trades as per Apprenticeship Rules.
  • 19Selected candidates must produce a valid police verification certificate at the time of joining from their place of last residence (for at least past one year) and permanent address.
  • 20Selected candidates must produce a Certificate of Physical Fitness in the prescribed format issued by an authorised Medical Officer of any Government Hospital.
  • 21Marks in the qualifying examination must be mentioned in percentage; candidates with CGPA must convert to percentage as per university/institute norms.
  • 22Selected candidates must be in a position to join on the prescribed date; failure to report by the scheduled date will be treated as disinterest and candidature may be forfeited.
  • 23Selected candidates must execute a contract for one year of training as per The Apprentices Act, 1961.
  • 24Canvassing/influencing in any form shall lead to disqualification.
  • 25This apprenticeship training does not carry any guarantee of employment at GTRE on completion.
