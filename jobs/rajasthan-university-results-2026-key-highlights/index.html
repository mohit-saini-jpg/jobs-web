How to Download Rajasthan University Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download the Rajasthan University BA LLB Hons 1st Sem Revaluation Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official Rajasthan University result website at result.uniraj.ac.in or go to uniraj.ac.in and open the Result section.
  • On the Uniraj result page, find the link for "B.A. LL.B. (Hons) I Sem (Five Year) Integrated Course Exam Dec 2025 (Reval.)".
  • Click on the relevant result link to open the BA LLB Hons revaluation result page.
  • Enter your roll number and any other details asked on the screen.
  • Click on the "Find" or "Submit" button to view your Rajasthan University BA LLB Hons 1st Sem revaluation result 2026.
  • Check your subject-wise revised marks, total marks, and result status.
  • Download or print the online marksheet for future reference and for use until the original updated marksheet is issued by the university.
