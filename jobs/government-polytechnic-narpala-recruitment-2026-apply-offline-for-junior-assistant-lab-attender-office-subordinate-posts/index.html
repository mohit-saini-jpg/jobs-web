Details

Junior Assistant: Must hold a Bachelor’s Degree of any University in India established or incorporated by or under a Central Act, State Act or Provincial Act or an Institution recognized by the University Grants Commission or any equivalent qualification. Lab Attender (Mechanical): ITI Pass Certificate in related trades such as Fitter, Turner, Machinist, Welder or other equivalent Mechanical trades with 1 year of experience after ITI or A Diploma in Mechanical Engineering (DME) with 1 year of experience. Lab Attender (Electrical): ITI Pass Certificate in Electrician or related trades with 1 year of experience after ITI or A Diploma in Electrical & Electronics Engineering (DEEE) with 1 year of experience. Office Subordinate: Must have passed VII Class examination and Must be able to ride a Bicycle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Government Polytechnic Narpala recruitment?

The last date to submit the application for the Government Polytechnic Narpala recruitment is 20th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the designated address before this date.

Q2. What are the different posts available in this recruitment?

The available posts are Junior Assistant, Lab Attender (Mechanical), Lab Attender (Electrical), and Office Subordinate. There are a total of 8 vacancies across these positions.

Q3. What is the minimum and maximum age limit for these posts?

The minimum age limit is 18 years, and the maximum age limit is 42 years for all the posts. Age relaxation is provided for SC/ST/BC candidates.

Q4. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) in the official notification for this recruitment drive.

Q5. What is the educational qualification required for the Junior Assistant post?

To be eligible for the Junior Assistant post, candidates must hold a Bachelor’s Degree from any recognized University in India or an equivalent qualification.

Q6. What are the required qualifications for Lab Attender posts?

For Lab Attender (Mechanical), ITI in Mechanical trades with 1 year experience or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering (DME) with 1 year experience is required. For Lab Attender (Electrical), ITI in Electrician trades with 1 year experience or Diploma in Electrical & Electronics Engineering (DEEE) with 1 year experience is needed.

Q7. What is the salary structure for the recruited candidates?

The salary for Junior Assistant is Rs. 18500/- per month, while for Lab Attender and Office Subordinate posts, it is Rs. 15000/- per month.

Q8. How can I apply for these Government Polytechnic Narpala jobs?

The application process is offline. Candidates need to download the application form, fill it correctly, attach necessary documents, and send it to the address specified in the notification before the last date.
