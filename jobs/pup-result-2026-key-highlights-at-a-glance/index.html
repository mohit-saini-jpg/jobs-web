How to Check Punjabi University Patiala Result 2026 Online

Checking your PUP Result 2026 is straightforward. Follow the step-by-step process below to access and download your provisional marksheet from the official results portal.

Step-by-Step Process:

  • Visit the official results portal - Go to Click Here
  • Look for "Quick Search Result Here" on the homepage.
  • Select your course and semester from the list available on the left side or in the dropdown menu.
  • Enter your Roll Number (exactly as printed on your admit card) and any other required credentials.
  • Click the "Submit" button.
  • Your result will appear on the screen - review all details carefully.
  • Download and save the provisional marksheet as a PDF for future reference.
  • Take a printout to keep a physical copy until the original marksheet is issued.

Important: The result displayed on the portal is provisional. It should not be treated as the original or final marksheet. Students must collect the official, signed mark sheet from the university examination section or their department when physical certificates are issued.

What Information Is Displayed on the PUP Result 2026 Marksheet?

When you download your provisional marksheet from the Punjabi University Patiala results portal, it will typically include the following details:

  • Student's Name
  • Father's Name
  • Roll Number / Registration Number
  • Course and Semester
  • Subject-wise Marks Obtained
  • Maximum Marks per Subject
  • Total Marks
  • Pass / Fail / Reappear Status
  • Result Declaration Date

After downloading, verify every detail carefully. If you notice any discrepancy in your name, roll number, marks, or any other information, contact the Punjabi University Examination Cell immediately with your admit card and a valid ID as proof.
