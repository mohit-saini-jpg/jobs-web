What Is the Solapur University Revaluation Result 2026 : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 30/06/2026

Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University (PAHSU) has declared the Online and Offline Revaluation Result for the March/April 2026 examination (Lot No. 3) on 29 June 2026. The result has been published as a downloadable PDF on the official university website and covers students across multiple faculties who applied for revaluation or verification of their answer scripts.

Students who applied for reassessment can check whether their marks have changed by searching for their PRN number or seat number in the published list. Below is everything you need to know - what's in this result, how to check it, and what comes next.

What Is the Solapur University Revaluation Result 2026 (Lot-3)?

This section explains exactly what the 29 June 2026 release is and why it matters for applicants.

The released document is a provisional revaluation/verification result list, not a final marksheet. It is the third batch ("Lot No. 03") of results for students who had applied for revaluation of their March/April 2026 exam answer scripts. The list shows, subject-wise, whether a student's marks were revised ("Change") or remained the same ("No Change") after re-checking.

Each entry in the PDF lists the course name, branch, seat number, PRN number, subject name and code, semester, maximum and minimum marks, the marks originally obtained, the marks after revaluation, and a remark of "Change" or "No Change."

How to Check Your Solapur University Revaluation Result 2026

This section walks you through the simplest way to find your own result in the published list.

Since the revaluation result is released as a single combined PDF rather than an individual PRN-search portal, follow these steps:

  • Open the official revaluation result PDF: Online and Offline Revaluation Result MAR/APR-2026 Examination - Lot No. 03.
  • Use the "Find" or "Ctrl+F" (Cmd+F on Mac) search function in your PDF viewer.
  • Type your PRN number or Seat Number into the search box.
  • Locate your row and note the subject name, marks obtained earlier, and "Marks After Reval."
  • Check the Remark column - it will show "Change" if your marks were revised or "No Change" if they remain the same.
  • Download or save the PDF for your records.

If your name does not appear in this Lot-3 list, your result may be included in an earlier or upcoming lot, since the university is publishing revaluation results course-wise in batches.

What Happens After the Revaluation Result Is Declared?

This section explains the next steps for students whose marks have changed, based on the official notice accompanying the result.

According to the official notice issued with the Lot-3 result, the following points apply:

  • This published list is only for the information of students. The final result, factoring in the overall effect of the revaluation as per Ordinance 106, will be declared shortly for students whose marks have changed.
  • The revaluation result for remaining students (those not yet covered in Lot-3) will be published within a short period, in subsequent lots.
  • Students should contact the principal of their concerned college if they have not received their revaluation result.
  • As per Ordinance 106(B), students are advised not to visit the university in person regarding this result.
  • The university has clarified that photocopy and revaluation facilities are a special facility, not a right in the strict sense of the term.
