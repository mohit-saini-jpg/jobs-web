Eligibility Criteria

Junior Administrative Assistant: Must be a Graduate from a recognized university. Must have a Certificate Course in Computer Word Processing or an equivalent qualification from a recognized university/institute. Must have a typing speed of 40 words per minute on a computer. Senior Computer Assistant: B.E./B.Tech in Computer Science/Information Technology, or MCA from any recognized university. As on 10.08.2026, candidates must be: Not below 18 years of age Not more than 40 years of age for candidates of other (general) categories Not more than 45 years of age for candidates belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes This age limit applies uniformly to both the Junior Administrative Assistant and Senior Computer Assistant posts.
