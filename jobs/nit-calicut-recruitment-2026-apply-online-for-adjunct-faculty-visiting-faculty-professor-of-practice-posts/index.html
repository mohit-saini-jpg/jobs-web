Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is NIT Calicut Visiting / Adjunct Faculty & Professor of Practice Recruitment 2026?

NIT Calicut Visiting / Adjunct Faculty & Professor of Practice Recruitment 2026 is a standing advertisement by NIT Calicut to engage distinguished academicians, industry experts, researchers and professionals for teaching, research and innovation. The institute is inviting applications for Visiting Faculty, Adjunct Faculty, Professor of Practice and Professor Emeritus positions for the Academic Year 2025‑26.

Q2. Which departments and centres are covered under this NIT Calicut faculty advertisement?

The recruitment covers multiple departments and centres of NIT Calicut. These include Architecture and Planning, Chemical Engineering, Education, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, Centre for Career Development, Centre for Innovation, Entrepreneurship & Incubation and Centre for Women Welfare & Social Empowerment.

Q3. What is the last date to apply for NIT Calicut Visiting / Adjunct Faculty Recruitment 2026?

Candidates can apply online up to 10 June 2026, 17:00 hours as per the standing advertisement schedule. Applications are generally scrutinized every year in the month of June, and eligible profiles may be considered as and when requirements arise.

Q4. What are the essential qualifications and experience required?

Applicants should have a Ph.D., Post Graduate or Graduate degree in the relevant field from a Centrally Funded Institution under the Ministry of Education. They must possess 10/12/15 years of experience after Ph.D./PG/Graduation respectively, with at least 3 years in senior leadership roles such as GM, Director, Technical Head, R&D Head or Project Head in top corporates, national labs or reputed organizations.

Q5. Is there an age limit for NITC Visiting / Adjunct Faculty and Professor of Practice?

Yes, the upper age limit for these positions is below 70 years. However, the institute may consider exceptionally meritorious candidates with outstanding contributions even if they do not fully satisfy all eligibility parameters.

Q7. What is the remuneration / honorarium for Visiting and Adjunct Faculty at NIT Calicut?

Visiting and Adjunct Faculty are paid on a per‑day honorarium basis with a monthly ceiling. Professors receive Rs. 7,000 per day with a ceiling of Rs. 1.20 lakh per month plus up to Rs. 20,000 reimbursable; Associate Professors get Rs. 5,000 per day with a Rs. 1.00 lakh ceiling plus Rs. 20,000 reimbursable; Assistant Professors receive Rs. 3,000 per day with a Rs. 0.80 lakh ceiling plus Rs. 20,000 reimbursable.
