Kerala Plus Two SAY Result 2026 - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 18/07/2026

Kerala Plus Two SAY Result 2026 will be released by the Directorate of Higher Secondary Education (DHSE), Kerala, on Saturday, July 18, 2026. Students who appeared for the Plus Two Save A Year (SAY) / Improvement Examination 2026, held from June 29 to July 3, 2026, will be able to check their provisional result online using their Register Number and Date of Birth once the portal goes live.

Kerala Plus Two SAY Result 2026 - Quick Overview

The Kerala Plus Two SAY (Save A Year) exam, also called the Improvement Examination, gives students who could not clear one or more subjects in the regular March 2026 Plus Two exam — or who wanted to improve their score — a second chance without losing an academic year. The DHSE Kerala Plus Two SAY Result 2026 is scheduled to go live today on the official result portal.

How to Check Kerala Plus Two SAY Result 2026 Online

Once DHSE activates the result link, follow these steps to download your Kerala Plus Two SAY Result 2026 scorecard from the official portal.

  • Visit the official website at results.kite.kerala.gov.in.
  • Click on the link for "Kerala Plus Two SAY / Improvement Result 2026" on the homepage.
  • Enter your Register Number and Date of Birth (DD/MM/YYYY format) in the login window.
  • Click the Submit button.
  • Your provisional Kerala Plus Two SAY scorecard 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download the result and take a printout for future reference.

Kerala Plus Two SAY Result 2026: Passing Criteria

Based on the standard Kerala Higher Secondary passing norms reported in recent coverage, students will generally need a minimum of 30% marks (Grade D or D+) in each subject and in aggregate to be declared pass.

  • Theory-only subjects: minimum 24 marks out of 80 to pass the theory paper.
  • Subjects with a practical component: minimum 18 marks out of 60 in the theory portion.
