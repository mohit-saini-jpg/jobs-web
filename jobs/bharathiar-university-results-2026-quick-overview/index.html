How to Check Bharathiar University Results 2026 Online

This section explains the exact steps to view and download your Affiliated Colleges UG/PG result for the April/May 2026 examination cycle using the official result portal.

Follow these steps to check your result:

  • Visit the official result portal at https://results.b-u.ac.in/ac/index.html.
  • Locate and click on "Affiliated Colleges - April/May 2026 Examinations - UG/PG Results."
  • Enter your Register Number exactly as it appears on your hall ticket.
  • Submit the form to view your provisional mark statement.
  • Check your subject-wise marks and overall result status on screen.
  • Download or take a screenshot/printout of the result for future reference.

Note: Keep your hall ticket handy before checking your result, since the register number is mandatory to view your mark statement.

What Information Appears on the Mark Statement

This section explains what details students can expect to see once their Bharathiar University result is displayed, so they know what to verify on their provisional mark sheet.

The online provisional mark statement typically displays:

  • Student name and register number
  • College name and course details
  • Subject/paper-wise marks obtained
  • Overall result status (Pass or RA)
  • Semester and examination cycle details

Since this is a provisional mark statement, it is meant only to inform students of their performance. The original, official mark sheet is issued separately through the respective affiliated college.

Minimum Passing Marks at Bharathiar University

This section covers the general qualifying criteria students need to meet to clear their papers under Bharathiar University's affiliated college examination system.

Across Bharathiar University's affiliated colleges, the broadly applicable passing requirement is as follows:

  • Undergraduate (UG) programmes: Minimum of 40% marks required, both in individual papers and in aggregate.
  • Postgraduate (PG) programmes: A higher minimum aggregate percentage is generally required, as prescribed by the respective programme regulations.

Since exact passing criteria can vary slightly by course and regulation year, students should also cross-check the specific requirement with their college's examination cell.

What Does "RA" Mean in Bharathiar University Results?

This section clarifies a term that frequently confuses students when viewing their result - the abbreviation "RA" on the mark statement.

RA stands for "Re-Appearance." If RA appears next to a subject on your result, it means you did not secure the minimum required passing marks in that particular paper. You will need to write the arrear/supplementary examination for that subject in a future examination cycle to clear it.

How to Apply for Revaluation or Retotalling

This section explains the process students should follow if they wish to have their answer scripts rechecked after viewing their Bharathiar University result.

Students who are not satisfied with their marks in a particular paper can apply for revaluation or retotalling through the following process:

  • Submit the revaluation/retotalling application through your affiliated college's examination cell.
  • Applications must be submitted within the stipulated time frame announced by the university after result declaration.
  • A prescribed fee per paper applies for revaluation/retotalling requests.
  • Original mark sheets are issued to students through their respective colleges after the revaluation window closes.
