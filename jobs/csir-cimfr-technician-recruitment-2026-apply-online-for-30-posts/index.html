Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for CSIR CIMFR Technician-1 posts?

The last date to submit the online application for the CSIR CIMFR Technician-1 recruitment is 19 June 2026. Ensure your application is submitted before this deadline.

Q2. What is the minimum educational qualification required for Technician-1?

Candidates must have passed SSC/10th Class with a minimum of 55% marks in Science subjects, along with an ITI Certificate or 2 years of full-time apprenticeship or 3 years of work experience in a relevant trade.

Q3. What is the age limit for applying to the Technician-1 posts?

The age limit for applying is between 18 years and 28 years. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per government rules.

Q4. How many vacancies are available for the Technician-1 post?

There are a total of 30 vacancies for the Technician-1 post in CSIR CIMFR.

Q5. What is the application fee for General and OBC candidates?

The application fee for General and OBC candidates is Rs. 500/-. SC/ST and Women candidates are exempted from paying the application fee.

Q6. What is the selection process for CSIR CIMFR Technician-1 recruitment?

The selection process includes a Trade Test followed by a Competitive Written Examination, which consists of three papers: Mental Ability Test, General Knowledge & English, and Subject Knowledge.

Q7. What is the salary offered for the Technician-1 position?

The selected candidates will receive a salary in Level-2, ranging from Rs. 19,900 to Rs. 63,200 per month.

Q8. In which city and state is CSIR CIMFR located?
