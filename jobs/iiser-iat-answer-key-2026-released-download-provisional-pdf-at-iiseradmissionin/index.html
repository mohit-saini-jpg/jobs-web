How to Download IISER IAT 2026 Answer Key

Follow these steps to download the provisional answer key PDF:

Step 1: Visit Official Website

Go to the official IISER admission portal: iiseradmission.in

Step 2: Click Answer Key Link

On the homepage, click the link titled “IAT 2026 Provisional Answer Key”

Step 3: Select Language & Download

Choose either: - Provisional Answer Key (English): IAT 2026 - Provisional Answer Key (Hindi): IAT 2026 The PDF will open on your screen.

Step 4: Save & Print PDF

Download and save the file. Take a printout for future reference and score comparison.

Response Sheet Release Date

The student response sheet will be available from June 9, 2026, allowing candidates to verify their recorded answers [web:1][web:2].

How to Raise Objections Against Answer Key

If you find discrepancies:

  • 1. Visit iiseradmission.in
  • 2. Click the IAT 2026 Answer Key Objection Portal link
  • 3. Log in (if required)
  • 4. Select the question(s) and upload supporting documents
  • 5. Pay any applicable objection fee

Objection Submission Deadline

Objections can be submitted from June 9 to June 13, 2026. Late submissions will not be accepted.

Marking Scheme

- +3 marks for each correct answer - –1 mark for each incorrect answer - No penalty for unattempted questions

Step-by-Step Tentative Score Calculation

1. Download the answer key and response sheet [web:6] 2. Compare your answers with the official correct answers 3. Count correct and incorrect answers 4. Apply: Tentative Score = (Correct × 3) – (Incorrect × 1)
