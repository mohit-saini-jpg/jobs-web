FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the DLSA Sitapur Para Legal Volunteer recruitment?
A
The last date to receive the application for the DLSA Sitapur Para Legal Volunteer posts is 25th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the authority before this date.
Q2What is the educational qualification required for the Para Legal Volunteer post?
A
No specific educational qualification is mandated. Candidates with a genuine interest in social service, particularly for weaker sections of society, are encouraged to apply.
Q3What is the age limit for the Para Legal Volunteer position?
A
The minimum age requirement for the Para Legal Volunteer post is 18 years. There is no specified maximum age limit, and age relaxation will be provided as per government norms.
Q4What is the salary or honorarium for a Para Legal Volunteer?
A
Para Legal Volunteers will receive an honorarium of Rs. 750/- per day, which is applicable only for the days they are assigned duties.
Q5How can I apply for the DLSA Sitapur Para Legal Volunteer posts?
A
Applications must be submitted offline. Candidates need to download the application form from the official website, fill it out, and send it along with required documents to the address mentioned in the notification.
Q6Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee required for any category of candidates (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q7What is the selection process for the Para Legal Volunteer role?
A
The selection process will involve a Document Verification stage followed by an Interview. Candidates must perform well in both stages to be considered.
Q8Where can I find the official notification and application form?
A
The official notification and application form can be downloaded from the official website of the District Legal Services Authority, Sitapur, which is www.district.ecourts.gov.in/sitapur.
Q9What documents do I need to submit with my application?
A
You will need to attach self-attested copies of required documents, which typically include a photograph, signature, ID proof, and relevant certificates. Please refer to the official notification for a complete list.
Q10Is this a permanent government job?
A
The Para Legal Volunteer position is a contractual role. It offers an opportunity to contribute to legal services but is not a permanent government job.
