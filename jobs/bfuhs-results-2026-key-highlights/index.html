How to Check BFUHS Result 2026

Students can check BFUHS Result 2026 online through the official examination portal.

  • Visit the official BFUHS examination result portal - https://examination.bfuhs.ac.in/eos/resultweb.aspx.
  • Search the latest result list released on 09-04-2026.
  • Find your course name and year or semester, such as DBPS 1st Year or BSc Nursing(NS21) 8th Semester.
  • Click on the "View Result" link available against your course.
  • Enter your roll number and registration number to access the result.
  • Submit the details and check your marksheet on the screen.
  • Download or print the provisional result for future use.
  • Verify your name, roll number, marks, and course details carefully after downloading.
