Application Fee – IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026

The IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026 notification does not mention any application fee for any category. Candidates can apply online through the NAPS/NATS portals and the Microsoft Forms link without any fee payment.

Selection Process – IndianOil Apprentice Recruitment 2026

There is no written test for the IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026. Selection for all four refinery units is entirely merit-based, with the merit list prepared on the basis of aggregate percentage of marks in the prescribed qualifying examination. Candidates applying for apprenticeship training for the first time will be given preference over those applying for a second time. Merit List Preparation: Candidates are ranked in descending order of percentage of marks in the prescribed qualification (aggregate of all years/semesters). Tie-breaking: In case of a tie in percentage, the candidate senior by age (earlier date of birth) is ranked higher. If date of birth is also the same, the candidate with a higher percentage in Matriculation is ranked higher. IOCL Apprentice Stipend / Salary 2026

The stipend for IOCL Apprentice 2026 will be paid as prescribed under the Apprentices Act, 1961/1973 and Apprentices Rules 1992 (as amended) along with Corporation's guidelines. The official notification does not mention a specific monthly stipend amount. Apprentices will not receive HRA or Company accommodation. They will, however, receive accident insurance coverage and safety equipment (safety shoes and helmet) for selected trades. Candidates should also note that upon completion of the apprenticeship period, IOCL has no obligation to offer employment , and apprentices cannot claim employment rights on the basis of completion of training.

IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1Has IOCL officially released the Apprentice Recruitment 2026 notification?
A
Yes, Indian Oil Corporation Limited has officially released the IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026 notification on 29 June 2026 for four refinery units — Bongaigaon (144 seats), Gujarat (647 seats), Mathura (151 seats), and Paradip (582 seats) — covering 11 trade and discipline codes.
Q2What is the last date to apply online for IOCL Apprentice 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application (both NAPS/NATS and Microsoft Forms) for the IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is 28 July 2026 at 17:00 hrs. The application window opens on 29 June 2026 at 10:00 hrs.
Q3Is there a written test for IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
No. There is no written test for the IOCL Apprentice 2026. Selection is based entirely on the percentage of marks obtained in the prescribed qualifying examination, with preference given to first-time apprenticeship applicants.
Q4What is the age limit for IOCL Apprentice 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 24 years as on 30 June 2026 for General/EWS candidates. SC/ST candidates get 5 years of relaxation, OBC(NCL) candidates get 3 years, and PwBD candidates get 10 years of upper age relaxation.
Q5What is the application fee for IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
No application fee has been mentioned in the official IOCL Apprentice 2026 notification for any category. The application is free of cost for all candidates.
Q6Can candidates with BE/B.Tech degrees apply for IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
No. Candidates possessing higher professional qualifications such as BE/B.Tech, MBA, CA, LLB, or MCA are not eligible to apply for the IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026. Such candidates will be disqualified at the document verification stage.
Q7What are the two steps to complete the IOCL Apprentice 2026 application?
A
Step I is registering on the NAPS portal (for codes 101, 102, 103, 108, 109, 110, 111) or NATS portal (for codes 104, 105, 106, 107) and applying for the relevant opportunity. Step II is submitting additional details through the Microsoft Forms link available at www.iocl.com. Both steps are mandatory and must match in all details.
Q8Will IOCL offer permanent employment after the apprenticeship is completed?
A
No. The official notification explicitly states that upon completion of the apprenticeship period, IOCL has no obligation to offer employment and apprentices cannot claim employment rights on the basis of apprenticeship completion.
Q9What documents are needed for IOCL Apprentice Document Verification 2026?
A
Candidates must bring Class X certificate (for age proof), Class XII/ITI/Graduation/Diploma marksheets and certificates, category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD), NAPS/NATS portal registration printout, Affidavit (Annexure-A if applicable), and one original Photo ID proof (Aadhar/PAN/Voter ID/Driving License/Passport).
Q10Where can I find the official IOCL Apprentice 2026 notification?
A
The official IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026 notification for all four refinery units is available on the IOCL official website at www.iocl.com under the 'What's New' section. All corrigenda and updates will also be posted only on this website.
