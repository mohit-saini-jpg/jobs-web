University of Calcutta has officially released the Calcutta University Result 2026 for undergraduate courses including BA, BSc, and BCom for Semester II and Semester III examinations conducted under CCF and CBCS patterns in 2025. Students who appeared for these exams can now check the result notifications online.

Calcutta University Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - cuexam.net. The results include BA, BSc, and BCom Semester III examinations and Semester II re-examination or FSI results. Students can check and download their results through official PDF notices available on the portal.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the result notifications. In case of any discrepancies, they should contact the university authorities immediately.
