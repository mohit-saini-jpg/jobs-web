DetailsMinimum Age Limit: 18 Years Maximum Age limit: 40 Years Age relaxation is admissible as per rules

Qualification / Eligibility

Matched Qualifications
  • CA

Selection Process

  • Selection will be made through Main Written Examination followed by Physical Standards Test and Physical Efficiency Test (qualifying in nature).
  • Shortlisting for Main Examination: Based on normalized score of Preliminary Eligibility Test-2025 (PET-2025). Only candidates who have qualified PET-2025 with valid Score Card can apply. Candidates securing zero or negative marks in PET-2025 will not be shortlisted. Main Written Examination: 100 Multiple Choice Questions (Objective Type)
  • 2 Hours duration. Negative Marking: 0.25 marks for each wrong answer. Minimum qualifying marks will be decided by the Commission. Syllabus: Part-1 (85 Marks): History of India & Indian National Movement
  • Geography of India & World
  • Economic & Social Development of India
  • Indian Polity & Constitution
  • General Science & Technology
  • Environmental Ecology & Disaster Management
  • Current Events of National & International Importance
  • Data Interpretation
  • Important Legal Provisions & Policies related to Excise in Uttar Pradesh. Part-2 (15 Marks): Knowledge of concepts of Computer & Information Technology and contemporary technological development & innovation in this field. Physical Standards Test (Qualifying): Male: Height 167.6 cm
  • Chest 80 cm (unexpanded) / 85 cm (expanded). Female: Height 152 cm (General)
  • 147 cm (SC/ST)
  • Weight 45 kg. Physical Efficiency Test (Qualifying): Male: 4.8 km run in 25 minutes. Female: 2.4 km run in 14 minutes. Final selection is based on marks obtained in the Main Written Examination. Physical tests are qualifying in nature. Result of Main Examination and final merit list will be declared on the official website.

How To Apply

  • Application Mode: Online only through the official website https://upsssc.gov.in
  • Steps to Apply:
  • Visit the official website of UPSSSC and go to the Live Advertisement segment.
  • Login using your PET-2025 Registration Number + OTP received on registered mobile/email.
  • Fill the online application form with all mandatory details (personal information, educational qualification, category, horizontal reservation, etc.).
  • Upload/verify photo and signature (auto-populated from PET-2025).
  • Pay the online processing fee through Credit Card/Debit Card/Net Banking/UPI or SBI e-Challan.
  • Submit the application and take a printout for future reference.
  • After successful fee payment/reconciliation, download and print the final application form.

Important Instructions

  • Only candidates who have appeared in and qualified PET-2025 with a valid Score Card are eligible to apply. The number of vacancies is tentative and may increase or decrease. Reservation benefits (vertical & horizontal) will be given as per latest UP Government orders. Candidates must possess the required educational qualification and other eligibility criteria as on the last date of submission of application. Age and experience will be counted as on the last date of application. Candidates belonging to reserved categories must produce original valid certificates in the prescribed format at the time of document verification. EWS candidates must submit the latest Income & Asset Certificate (issued between 01-04-2026 to 24-06-2026) based on financial year 2025-26. No change in category or any details will be allowed after the last date of modification. Mere submission of application does not guarantee eligibility or call for the Main Examination. All updates, admit cards, results, etc., will be available only on the official website https://upsssc.gov.in. Candidates are advised to visit the website regularly. Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification. Any dispute will be subject to the jurisdiction of Courts in Lucknow only. The decision of the Commission shall be final and binding.
