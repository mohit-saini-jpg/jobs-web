Job Overview

The R.G. Kar Medical College, Kolkata (RGKMC Kolkata) has officially announced the Senior Resident (Non-Bonded) Contractual Recruitment 2026 with 7 openings. The detailed notification was published at the official website, rgkarmch.org. Candidates who meet the age criteria of not exceeding 45 years and hold qualifications such as MBBS with MD/MS/DNB/DrNB/DM/MCH in the respective discipline can now submit their applications up to 03 June 2026. The walk-in interview will be held on 05 June 2026. Direct links to download the notification and application details are shared below.
