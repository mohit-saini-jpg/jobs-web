Important Instructions

  • Only Indian citizens are eligible to apply. Candidates must necessarily produce relevant documents pertaining to nationality, age, and educational qualifications in original at the time of joining. All eligibility criteria - age and qualification - are reckoned as on 01.06.2026. Only a single application will be accepted from individual candidates. Candidates can apply for a single location only. The place of posting can be changed to any other location within India at the sole discretion of AAICLAS Management, which shall be binding. In case of closure of any AAICLAS station, the individual has to complete his/her contract at another station as per the company's requirement. The FTC is initially for 3 years; the first year will be treated as probation. Extension or fresh contract will be at the sole discretion of AAICLAS Management, subject to satisfactory performance. The contract can be terminated earlier at the discretion of Management during its validity in case of unsatisfactory performance or due to security concerns at the duty point. Notice period for resignation is a minimum of 3 months. Candidates resigning with immediate effect will not be issued an experience letter/verification and will have to deposit 3 months' salary in lieu of the notice period not served. All communications regarding this recruitment will be made only through the registered Email ID of candidates and not by post. Candidates must regularly check their registered email. Any wrong/false information furnished by the candidate at any stage will result in cancellation of candidature without assigning any reason. If detected after engagement, the engagement will be treated as cancelled and necessary recovery will be made. Candidates working in any Government Department/PSU/Autonomous Body must produce a NOC from their present employer. Any canvassing by or on behalf of the candidate or bringing political or other outside influence with regard to selection shall be considered as Disqualification. AAICLAS will not bear any liability on account of service bond/salary/leave salary/pension contribution of previous employment of any candidate working in Central/State Government/Autonomous Body/PSU. Any modifications/amendments related to this recruitment will be published on the AAICLAS website www.aaiclas.aero only. AAICLAS reserves the right to restrict/enlarge/cancel the engagement in this selection process in part or full without issuing any further notice or assigning any reason whatsoever. The decision of AAICLAS Management regarding eligibility, acceptance or rejection of applications, and mode of selection shall be final and binding on all candidates. No representation or correspondence will be entertained in this regard.
