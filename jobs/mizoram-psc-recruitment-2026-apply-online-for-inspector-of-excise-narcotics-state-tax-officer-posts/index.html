Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Mizoram PSC Recruitment 2026?

The last date to apply online for the Mizoram PSC Inspector of Excise & Narcotics / State Tax Officer posts is 19th June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well in advance of the deadline.

Q2. What are the minimum and maximum age limits for these posts?

The minimum age limit is 18 years and the maximum age limit is 35 years as of 1st January 2026. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per government rules.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the State Tax Officer post?

For the State Tax Officer post, candidates must possess a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized University. Additionally, computer proficiency and Mizo language proficiency are required.

Q4. What is the salary structure for the recruited candidates?

The salary for the recruited candidates will be as per Level 7 and Level 8 of the Pay Matrix, depending on the specific post of Inspector of Excise & Narcotics or State Tax Officer.

Q5. How many vacancies are available in this recruitment drive?

A total of 7 vacancies are available for the posts of Inspector of Excise & Narcotics and State Tax Officer in the Mizoram Public Service Commission recruitment drive.

Q6. What is the mode of application for this recruitment?

The application process for this recruitment is entirely online. Candidates need to visit the official website of Mizoram PSC to fill out and submit their application forms.

Q7. Are there any physical requirements for the Inspector of Excise & Narcotics post?

Yes, for the Inspector of Excise & Narcotics post, there are specific physical measurement requirements including height (5’4”) and chest (Normal – 34”, Expanded – 36”), along with a physical test involving a 100m race in 15 seconds and an 800m race in 3 minutes.

Q8. What is the selection process for these posts?

The selection process for these posts will be based on a Combined Competitive Examination conducted by the Mizoram Public Service Commission.
