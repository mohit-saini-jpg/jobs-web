Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Senior Resident posts at BJRMH?

The application process is through a walk-in interview. Candidates need to attend the interview on the specified date, which is 29th May 2026. There is no separate application form submission deadline.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Senior Resident positions?

Candidates must possess a Post Graduate Degree (MD/MS/DNB) or Diploma in the concerned specialty. For those without a PG degree/diploma, a minimum of 2 years of experience after MBBS from a government or government-empanelled hospital is required, with at least 1 year in the concerned specialty.

Q3. What is the maximum age limit for the Senior Resident posts?

The maximum age limit for all Senior Resident posts is 45 years. Age relaxation will be provided to candidates belonging to reserved categories as per government rules.

Q4. How many vacancies are available for Senior Resident posts?

There are a total of 7 vacancies for the Senior Resident posts. These vacancies are distributed across Anaesthesia (3 posts), Paediatrics (2 posts), and Radiology (2 posts).

Q5. What is the salary offered for the Senior Resident positions?

The selected candidates will receive a monthly salary in the pay scale of Rs. 67,700 to Rs. 2,08,700 per month.

Q6. Is there any application fee for the BJRMH Senior Resident recruitment?

No, there is no application fee for the Senior Resident posts. This applies to all categories, including General, OBC, SC, and ST candidates.

Q7. What is the selection process for the Senior Resident posts?

The selection process will involve a Walk-in Interview followed by Document Verification. Candidates are advised to be prepared for both stages.

Q8. Where is the walk-in interview for BJRMH Senior Resident posts scheduled?

The walk-in interview will be held at the BJRMH office. The exact address and reporting time will be mentioned in the official notification. Candidates should reach the venue with all required documents.
