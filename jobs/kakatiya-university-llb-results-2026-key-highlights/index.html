How to Check Kakatiya University LLB Result 2026 Online

Students can easily check their Kakatiya University LL.B. results by following these simple steps:

  • Visit the official Kakatiya University examination website at https://kuexams.org/ or directly access the UG results portal at https://kuexams.org/ugresults
  • Navigate to the relevant result section and click on the LL.B. (3YDC) V Semester and LL.B. (5YDC) IX Semester March 2026 examination result link
  • You will be redirected to the login page where you need to enter your hall ticket number or roll number as mentioned on your admit card
  • Enter the security code or captcha displayed on the screen correctly in the designated field
  • Click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button to proceed with result retrieval
  • Your Kakatiya University LLB result 2026 will be displayed on the screen with your marks, grades, and pass/fail status
  • Carefully verify all the details mentioned in the result including your name, hall ticket number, subject-wise marks, and total marks obtained
  • Download the provisional mark sheet in PDF format by clicking on the download button for future reference
  • Take a printout of your result and keep it safely until the original mark sheet is issued by the university
  • In case of any discrepancies or errors in your result, immediately contact the Kakatiya University examination branch at 0870-2461491 for clarification
