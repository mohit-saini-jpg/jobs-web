Reservation Policy in TGPSC DIET Lecturer Notification

Reservation in this recruitment follows Rule 22 and 22(A) of the Telangana State and Subordinate Service Rules, 1996, along with multiple Government Orders covering SC, ST, BC, EWS, Persons with Disabilities, and Women candidates. A few key points to note: Local Reservation: 95% of posts under both Multi Zone-I and Multi Zone-II are reserved for local candidates as per G.O. Ms. No. 124, GA (SPF-MC) Department, dated 30/08/2018. Candidates availing reservation/age relaxation benefits under SC/ST/BC/EWS/PH categories are also automatically considered for Open Category (OC) vacancies. Sports reservation (2%) applies as per G.O. Ms. No. 74, Youth Advancement, Tourism & Culture (Sports) Department. Candidates from outside Telangana State are not entitled to any reservation benefits.

TGPSC DIET Lecturer Syllabus Highlights

The syllabus for Paper-I is common across all post codes, while Paper-II varies subject-wise. Here's a quick summary of what each paper covers: Paper-I – General Studies & General Abilities covers current affairs, Indian and Telangana history/geography/economy, the Indian Constitution, social exclusion and inclusive policies, Telangana culture and policies, logical reasoning, and basic English. Paper-II, Part A – Education: Theory and Practice (common for Post Codes 1–18) covers philosophical and sociological foundations of education, child psychology and development, pedagogy, curriculum, assessment & evaluation, ICT in education, and basic educational research methods. TGPSC DIET Lecturer Notification 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is TGPSC Notification No. 04/G/SE/2026 about?
A
It is a general recruitment notification issued by the Telangana Public Service Commission for filling 86 posts of Senior Lecturer in DIET and Lecturer in DIET under the School Education Department, Telangana.
Q2How many total vacancies are there in TGPSC DIET Lecturer Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 86 total vacancies across 19 post codes, covering subjects like Education, English, Telugu, Urdu, Maths, Science, Social Studies, Psychology, Philosophy, Art Education, Educational Technology, and Physical Education.
Q3What is the last date to apply for TGPSC DIET Lecturer posts?
A
The online application window closes on 29th July 2026 at 5:00 PM. Applications submitted after this deadline will not be accepted under any circumstances.
Q4What is the age limit for this recruitment?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 44 years as on 01/07/2026. The upper limit was raised from 34 to 44 years via G.O. Ms. No. 86 dated 18/05/2026.
Q5What educational qualification is required?
A
Generally, a Postgraduate degree (55% marks, 50% for SC/ST/BC/PwD) in the relevant subject along with an M.Ed. degree (55%/50% marks) from an NCTE-recognised institution. The Physical Education post requires only a PG in Physical Education.
Q6What is the application fee for this recruitment?
A
The application processing fee is Rs. 1,000 for OC/BC candidates and Rs. 500 for SC/ST/PwD candidates. An additional examination fee of Rs. 120 applies, except for unemployed candidates who are exempted.
Q7What is the exam pattern for TGPSC DIET Lecturer Recruitment?
A
The exam consists of Paper-I (General Studies & General Abilities, 150 marks) and Paper-II (Concerned Subject, divided into Part A and Part B, 150 marks each), making a total of 450 marks. It is an objective-type test conducted in OMR or CBRT mode.
Q8Where will the TGPSC DIET Lecturer exam be conducted?
A
The exam will be held at five centres: Hyderabad (including HMDA jurisdiction), Karimnagar, Khammam, Warangal, and Nizamabad.
Q9What is the salary for Senior Lecturer and Lecturer DIET posts?
A
Senior Lecturer (Post Code 1) carries a pay scale of Rs. 58,850–1,37,050, while Lecturer posts (Post Codes 2–19) carry a pay scale of Rs. 54,220–1,33,630.
Q10How can I apply online for this recruitment?
A
You need to first complete One Time Registration (OTR) on tgpsc.gov.in, then log in with your TGPSC ID, fill the application form, pay the fee online, and download the submitted PDF application for future reference.
