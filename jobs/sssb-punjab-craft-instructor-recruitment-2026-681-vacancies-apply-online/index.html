SSSB Punjab Craft Instructor Educational and Technical Qualification

All 52 trades require, at minimum, 10th pass with Science and Mathematics under the 10+2 system from a recognized School or Board. Beyond this common academic requirement, the technical qualification follows one of two pathways depending on which annexure the trade falls under: A relevant Bachelor's Degree/B.Voc in the corresponding engineering discipline from an AICTE/UGC-recognized institution, plus one year of relevant industry or teaching experience; OR A 3-year Diploma in the corresponding discipline from an AICTE-recognized institution or relevant Advanced Diploma (Vocational) from the Director General of Training, plus two years of relevant industry or teaching experience. SSSB Punjab Craft Instructor Reservation Policy

Reservation benefits are available only to candidates domiciled in Punjab State. A Domicile/Residence Certificate is mandatory for every candidate (regardless of category) and must not be older than 5 years at the time of upload/submission. SC/BC/Ex-Serviceman/EWS certificates must conform to prevailing Government of Punjab instructions. Reservation for women is governed by the Punjab Civil Services (Reservation of Posts for Women) Rules, 2020, and subsequent amendments. Ex-Serviceman category posts will first be filled by Ex-Serviceman (Self) candidates; only if unavailable will they be filled by Ex-Serviceman (Dependent) candidates, per Government of Punjab notification dated 08-05-2020.

SSSB Punjab Craft Instructor Selection Process

Candidates who successfully apply will be called for an Objective Type (Multiple Choice Question) written examination . The merit list is prepared based on marks obtained in the written exam. Each correct answer carries 1 mark; each wrong answer attracts a negative marking of ¼ mark. In case of a tie in merit, preference is given first to the candidate with the older date of birth. If still tied, preference goes to the candidate with a higher percentage in the required educational qualification. If still tied, preference goes to the candidate with higher marks in Matriculation. Based on the merit list, candidates are called for counseling and document/certificate verification. The number of candidates called is decided by the Board based on category-wise vacancy count.
