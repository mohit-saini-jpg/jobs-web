Maharani College Second Merit List 2026 - Key Highlights : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 07/07/2026

University Maharani College, Jaipur has released the second merit list 2026 for its undergraduate programmes under College Code 379, University of Rajasthan. The second list carries revised, category-wise cut-off marks for BA Major, BSc (Maths Group, Stat Group, Physics Major, Mathematics Major), BSc Community and Applied Science (Home Science), and BCom (Pass Course, SFS, BBA, and Major streams in ABST, BADM, and EAFM).

Candidates who missed the first list, or who are aiming for an upgrade, can check whether their aggregate marks meet the revised cut-off for their category and subject below. The list has been signed off by the College Convener, Vice-Principal, and Principal.

Is the Maharani College Second Merit List 2026 Out?

Yes. University Maharani College has published its second merit list for the 2026-27 session, listing category-wise cut-off marks for each UG programme and subject combination. This follows the first merit list released earlier by the University of Rajasthan and reflects updated cut-offs based on vacant seats after first-round verification.

Candidates should match their own aggregate percentage against the cut-off for their category in their chosen subject or programme to check their standing in this round.

How Second Merit List Cut-Offs Differ From the First List

Second merit list cut-offs at Maharani College are generally lower than the first list because they are recalculated based on seats that remain vacant after first-round document verification. Candidates who did not qualify in the first round, or who applied after the first list closed, get a fresh chance to check their eligibility here.

Where a category shows "Nil" or a dash, it usually means either:

  • All available seats in that category were already filled during the first round, or
  • No further applications are pending consideration against that category and subject combination

Candidates should keep monitoring subsequent lists, since cut-offs typically continue to ease with each round as higher-ranked candidates confirm admission elsewhere.

What to Do If You Qualify in the Second List

Candidates whose aggregate marks meet or exceed the cut-off for their category and chosen subject/programme should complete admission formalities promptly to secure their seat.

  • Confirm your name, category, and subject/programme against the cut-off tables above
  • Visit the Maharani College campus with original documents for verification
  • Complete fee payment within the verification window announced by the college
  • Retain the admission slip and receipt for future reference

If your marks fall short of the second-list cut-off, a further round (third merit list or spot round) may follow, depending on seat vacancy in your category and subject.
