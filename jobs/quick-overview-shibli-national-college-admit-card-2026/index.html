How to Download Shibli National College Admit Card 2026

Based on the official notice, the admit card is issued online through the college's admission portal. Follow these steps:

  • Go to the official Shibli National College online admission portal.
  • Log in using the registration/application number and password created at the time of form submission.
  • Look for the "Admit Card" or "Download Admit Card" link on the candidate dashboard.
  • Click to view your admit card for the 2026-27 entrance test.
  • Check all details carefully (name, course, exam date, exam centre, timing).
  • Download and save a digital copy, then take at least two printed copies for the exam day.

Not available: A direct, dedicated "admit card download" URL separate from the login-based candidate portal was not specified in the official notice. Candidates should use the same portal where they submitted their application form.

Details Usually Mentioned on the Admit Card

While the official notice does not publish a sample admit card, hall tickets of this type typically include the following details. Candidates should verify each field against their own admit card after download:

  • Candidate's name and application/registration number
  • Course applied for
  • Entrance exam date and reporting time
  • Exam centre name and address
  • Photograph and signature (as uploaded during registration)
  • Exam-day instructions and code of conduct

Documents to Carry on Exam Day

  • Printed copy of the admit card
  • A valid original photo ID proof (Aadhaar card, voter ID, school/college ID, or passport)
  • Passport-size photographs (as specified on the admit card, if required)
  • Original and photocopies of educational certificates and mark sheets used for eligibility, in case verification is required

Not available: The official notice reviewed for this article did not specify an exact list of exam-day documents. The list above reflects standard practice for such entrance tests; candidates should treat the instructions printed on their own admit card as final.

Important Instructions

  • Download and check the admit card as soon as it is available (from 17 July 2026) to allow time to report any discrepancy.
  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card.
  • Carry the admit card and ID proof together entry may be denied without both.
  • If any detail on the admit card (name, photo, course, centre) is incorrect, contact the college immediately through the official helpline before the exam date.
  • Keep checking the official website for the counselling schedule with exact time slots, which is expected to be published on 22 July 2026.

Shibli National College Admission 2026-27: Related Process

The entrance exam and admit card are part of the broader Shibli National College Admission 2026-27 process, which covers both undergraduate and postgraduate courses. As per the official notice, courses are divided into two tracks:

  • Entrance-test-based courses: Admission depends on performance in the college's own entrance test, followed by result and counselling.
  • Non-test (direct counselling) courses: Certain postgraduate courses are admitted through counselling without a written entrance test, and their online forms remain open even after 15 July 2026 as long as the university's portal is active.
