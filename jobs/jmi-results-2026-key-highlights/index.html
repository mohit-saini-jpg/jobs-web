How to Check JMI Mass Communication Entrance Result 2026

Candidates can follow the simple steps given below to check the JMI Mass Communication Entrance Result 2026 online through the official admission portal. Make sure you keep your application details handy to quickly access the result page.

  • Visit the official JMI admission portal at https://admission.jmi.ac.in/.
  • On the homepage, click on the 'Entrance Results' or relevant result section link.
  • You may also directly open the JMI Entrance Result Viewer page.
  • Select the appropriate Programme Type (for example - Masters) from the drop-down menu.
  • Choose the Course Name such as MA (Mass Communication) / MA (Convergent Journalism) as applicable.
  • Click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' option to proceed.
  • The JMI Mass Communication Entrance Result 2026 / merit list will appear on the screen.
  • Check your name, roll number, application number and result status carefully.
  • Download the result or merit list PDF and save it for future reference.
  • Take a printout of the result copy to carry during further admission or interview processes, if required.
