Reservation Policy in TGPSC Recruitment 2026

Reservation is applicable for Scheduled Tribes, Scheduled Castes, Backward Classes, and Economically Weaker Sections, along with Horizontal Reservation for Persons with Disabilities, Women, and Meritorious Sportspersons, as per Rule 22 and 22(A) of the Telangana State and Subordinate Service Rules, 1996. Applicants belonging to SC, ST, BC, EWS, or differently-abled categories who avail upper age relaxation will also be considered for Open Category (OC) vacancies. Candidates need a minimum qualification of B.Arch., B.E./B.Tech. (Civil), B.Planning/B.Tech. (Planning), M.A./M.Sc. (Geography), or M.Sc. (Geoinformatics), along with a Post Graduate Degree or Diploma in Town and Country Planning, or equivalent professional membership recognised by the UGC.

A
Selection is based purely on a Written Examination (Objective Type) comprising Paper-I (General Studies and General Abilities, 150 marks) and Paper-II (Subject Concerned at P.G. level, 300 marks), conducted in OMR or CBRT mode.
Q7What are the qualifying marks required to clear the TGPSC written examination?
A
The minimum qualifying marks are 40% for OC, Sportspersons, and EWS candidates, 35% for BC candidates, and 30% for SC, ST, and PH candidates.
