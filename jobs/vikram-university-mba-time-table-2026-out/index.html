How to Download Vikram University MBA Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the Vikram University MBA Time Table 2026 from the official university portal. Follow these simple steps to get your exam schedule.

  • Visit the official website of Vikram University at vikramuniv.ac.in.
  • Navigate to the "Time Table" or "Examination" section available on the homepage or under the student menu.
  • Look for the link mentioning "MBA II & IV Semester Exam Time Table 2026" or "PG II and IV Sem Exam 2025-26".
  • Click on the relevant PDF link to open the timetable.
  • Download the PDF file and save it on your device.
  • Take a printout for future reference and exam preparation.

Students should thoroughly check the official timetable and note down all important dates. Keep a copy handy during exam preparation.
