AP SSC Supplementary Result 2026 - Key Highlights : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 19/06/2026

The AP SSC Supplementary Result 2026 has released by the Board of Secondary Education, Andhra Pradesh (BSEAP) today, June 19, 2026, at 10:00 AM IST. Students who appeared for the Andhra Pradesh Class 10 Advanced Supplementary Examinations (ASE) 2026 can now check their results and download their marks memo directly from the official BSEAP portal at bse.ap.gov.in.

The AP 10th supplementary exam 2026 was conducted from May 25 to June 4, 2026. Students who failed in one or more subjects in the AP SSC main examination (held on April 30, 2026) were eligible to appear for this supply exam. With results now declared, students can verify their performance subject-wise and take their next academic steps accordingly.

How to Check AP SSC Supplementary Result 2026 at bse.ap.gov.in

The steps below explain exactly how to access and download the AP Class 10 supplementary marks memo from the official BSEAP website. Students should keep their hall ticket number ready before following these steps.

Step-by-Step Process:

  • Open your browser and go to the official website: bse.ap.gov.in
  • On the homepage, click on the link labelled "AP SSC Supplementary Results 2026"
  • You will be redirected to the results page
  • Enter your Roll Number / Hall Ticket Number in the required field
  • Click the "Get Results" or "Submit" button
  • Your AP SSC 10th supply result 2026 will appear on the screen
  • Verify all details including name, subject-wise marks, and overall result status
  • Download and save the marks memo as a PDF
  • Take a printout for future reference and admission purposes

AP SSC Supplementary Result 2026 - Marks Memo Details

Once students download their AP 10th supplementary marks memo from bse.ap.gov.in, they will find the following details on the document.

Details Mentioned on the AP SSC Supply Marks Memo 2026:

  • Candidate's Full Name
  • Hall Ticket Number / Roll Number
  • Date of Birth
  • School Name and School Code
  • Subject-wise Marks Obtained
  • Maximum Marks per Subject
  • Grade and Grade Points per Subject
  • Overall CGPA
  • Final Result Status (Pass / Fail)

AP SSC Supplementary 2026 - Passing Marks and Minimum Score Required

To be declared pass in the AP SSC Advanced Supplementary Examination 2026, students must secure the minimum qualifying marks as defined by BSEAP. Understanding the passing criteria helps students determine their result status.

Minimum Passing Criteria:

  • Students must score at least 35% marks in each subject to be declared qualified in the AP SSC supply exam 2026.
  • Students who appear in the wrong combination of question papers will have their performance cancelled, as per official BSEAP instructions.
