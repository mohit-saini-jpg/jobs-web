  • Applications must be submitted through online mode available on CIL website www.coalindia.in only, under Career with CIL >>>> Jobs at Coal India section. No modifications are allowed after the candidate submits the online application form. Candidates are advised not to wait till the last date and submit their application well within time.
  • The Admit Card for CBT will be available for downloading through the individual login portal on the CIL website. All correspondences/announcements will be made through CIL website www.coalindia.in only. Candidates must have an active email ID and mobile number valid for at least the next one year. Important Instructions

  • Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply. Engineering Degree/M.Tech/M.Sc./MCA must be from a recognized University/Institute and must be a regular full-time course; no online/correspondence/part-time courses (except Hindi for Rajbhasha discipline). Rounding of percentage is not acceptable; 60% means 60.00% and above only; even 59.99% is not eligible. Final year/semester students of academic year 2025-26 are eligible if they expect results by 31.08.2026; minimum marks criteria applies to completed semesters. A candidate can submit online application for one discipline (post) only; if multiple applications are submitted, only the application with the higher Application Sequence Number will be entertained. OBC-NCL certificate must be issued on or after 01-04-2026 in the latest prescribed format applicable for Central Govt. PSU appointment. EWS Income & Asset Certificate must be based on income of FY 2025-26 and valid for the year 2026-27. No TA/DA will be paid to any candidate (including SC/ST/PwD/EWS) for appearing in the Computer Based Online Test. Candidates working in Govt./PSUs/Autonomous bodies must submit NOC at the time of online application or DV/IME or submit proper relieving order at the time of joining. Selected candidates will be required to execute a service agreement bond of Rs. 3 Lakhs and must serve for a minimum period of 60 months; Rs. 5,000/- per month will be deducted from salary towards bond money, refunded without interest after 60 months. Canvassing in any form shall disqualify candidature. CIL does not demand any fee other than the application fee; do not respond to fraudulent agents. All notices, selection list, etc. will be uploaded only on CIL website under the tab "Career with CIL". Candidates are advised to visit the website regularly. Any dispute with regard to recruitment will be settled within the jurisdiction of Kolkata only.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is Coal India Management Trainee Recruitment 2026 (Advt. 03/2026)?

Coal India Limited has released Advertisement No. 03/2026 dated 05 May 2026 for recruitment of 660 Management Trainees (MT) in E‑1 Grade across 9 disciplines through a Computer Based Test (CBT).

Q2. How many vacancies are there in Coal India MT Recruitment 2026?

A total of 660 MT vacancies have been notified (647 fresh + 13 backlog) across Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, System, Electronics & Communication, Geology, Industrial Engineering, Rajbhasha (Hindi), and Company Secretary disciplines, including 64 PwD posts on a horizontal basis.

Q3. What are the important dates for Coal India Limited MT Recruitment 2026?

The online registration starts on 12 May 2026 (from 10:00 hrs) and the last date for online submission of applications is 11 June 2026 (up to 18:00 hrs), while the CBT date will be notified separately through admit cards on the CIL website.

Q4. What is the salary or pay scale for Coal India Management Trainee 2026?

Selected candidates will be appointed as Management Trainees in E‑1 Grade with a pay scale of Rs. 60,000 – 1,80,000, with an initial basic pay of Rs. 60,000 per month during the training period, along with DA, HRA, perquisites and performance related pay as per company rules.

Q5. What is the eligibility criteria for Coal India MT Recruitment 2026?

Eligibility is discipline‑wise: engineering graduates with minimum 60% marks in relevant branches for Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, System, E&T and Industrial Engineering; M.Sc./M.Tech in Geology/Geophysics for Geology; MA in Hindi with graduation in Hindi and English for Rajbhasha; and any graduate with Company Secretary qualification (ICSI) for CS, with 5% relaxation in marks for SC/ST/PwBD candidates.

Q6. What is the age limit for Coal India Management Trainee 2026?

The upper age limit is 30 years as on 30 April 2026 for UR/EWS candidates, with relaxation of 3 years for OBC (NCL), 5 years for SC/ST, higher relaxations for PwBD and Ex‑Servicemen as per Government norms, and no age bar for CIL employees subject to an overall maximum age of 56 years.
