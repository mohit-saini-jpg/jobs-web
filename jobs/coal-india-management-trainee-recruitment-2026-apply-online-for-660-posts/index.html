Coal India Management Trainee Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 660 Posts

Coal India Limited (CIL), Corporate Headquarters, Kolkata (A Maharatna PSU under Ministry of Coal, Govt. of India)

Posts: 660 Posts (including 13 Backlog Vacancies; 64 vacancies reserved for PwD on horizontal basis) Last Date: 11-06-2026 (by 18:00 Hrs) Online

Short Information

Coal India Limited (CIL) has released the official Management Trainee (MT) Notification 2026 on 05.05.2026 at CIL coalindia.in. A total of 660 vacancies have been announced under the CIL MT Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged up to 30 years for General/EWS category (with relaxations for reserved categories as per Govt. rules) and possessing a relevant full-time Engineering Degree / M.Tech / M.Sc / MCA qualification can apply online from 12-05-2026 to 11-06-2026.   • Reasoning

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted through online mode available on CIL website www.coalindia.in only, under Career with CIL >>>> Jobs at Coal India section. No hard copy of the application form or any documents should be sent to the CIL office. Candidates can submit online application for one discipline (post) only. No modifications are allowed after the candidate submits the online application form. Candidates are advised not to wait till the last date and submit their application well within time.
  • The Admit Card for CBT will be available for downloading through the individual login portal on the CIL website. All correspondences/announcements will be made through CIL website www.coalindia.in only. Candidates must have an active email ID and mobile number valid for at least the next one year. For queries, candidates may refer to the FAQs section on the CIL website or write to Email: [email protected].
  • Documents to be Uploaded with Online Application (Self-attested, in PDF format unless specified):
  • Recent passport-size colour photograph (not older than 3 weeks; in jpg/jpeg format)
  • Signature with Black ink pen (in jpg/jpeg format)
  • Copy of Matriculation/Secondary Board level certificate (as age/DOB proof)
  • Marksheets of all semesters/trimesters/years of graduation/post-graduation in a single PDF
  • Final/Provisional Degree/Certificate for Graduation and Post-Graduation (as applicable)
  • Final year students: all marksheets up to latest semester/year/trimester
  • Company Secretary certificate/Associate Member certificate/Fellow Member certificate of ICSI (for Post Code 19)
  • Experience certificate of working in listed company (for CS discipline candidates - preference)
  • CGPA/GPA to percentage conversion certificate issued by University/Institute (if applicable)
  • Valid Caste Certificate in prescribed format (for OBC-NCL/SC/ST candidates)
  • PwBD Certificate in Govt. of India prescribed format + UDID Card issued by competent authority (if applicable)
  • Valid Income & Asset Certificate for EWS (based on FY 2025-26 income; valid for 2026-27)
  • Discharge/Service Certificate (for Ex-Servicemen)
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) for candidates presently employed in Govt./PSU/Semi-Govt./Autonomous body
  • Apply Online at: https://www.coalindia.in (under Career with CIL >>>> Jobs at Coal India)

Important Instructions

  • Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply. Engineering Degree/M.Tech/M.Sc./MCA must be from a recognized University/Institute and must be a regular full-time course; no online/correspondence/part-time courses (except Hindi for Rajbhasha discipline). Rounding of percentage is not acceptable; 60% means 60.00% and above only; even 59.99% is not eligible. Final year/semester students of academic year 2025-26 are eligible if they expect results by 31.08.2026; minimum marks criteria applies to completed semesters. A candidate can submit online application for one discipline (post) only; if multiple applications are submitted, only the application with the higher Application Sequence Number will be entertained. OBC-NCL certificate must be issued on or after 01-04-2026 in the latest prescribed format applicable for Central Govt. PSU appointment. EWS Income & Asset Certificate must be based on income of FY 2025-26 and valid for the year 2026-27. No TA/DA will be paid to any candidate (including SC/ST/PwD/EWS) for appearing in the Computer Based Online Test. Candidates working in Govt./PSUs/Autonomous bodies must submit NOC at the time of online application or DV/IME or submit proper relieving order at the time of joining. Selected candidates will be required to execute a service agreement bond of Rs. 3 Lakhs and must serve for a minimum period of 60 months; Rs. 5,000/- per month will be deducted from salary towards bond money, refunded without interest after 60 months. Canvassing in any form shall disqualify candidature. CIL does not demand any fee other than the application fee; do not respond to fraudulent agents. All notices, selection list, etc. will be uploaded only on CIL website under the tab "Career with CIL". Candidates are advised to visit the website regularly. Any dispute with regard to recruitment will be settled within the jurisdiction of Kolkata only.
