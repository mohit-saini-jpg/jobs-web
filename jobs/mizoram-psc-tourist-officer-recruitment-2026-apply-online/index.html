Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Mizoram PSC Tourist Officer recruitment?

The last date to submit your online application for the Mizoram PSC Tourist Officer position is 29th May 2026. It is advisable to apply well before the deadline to avoid any last-minute issues.

Q2. What are the minimum and maximum age limits for this post?

The minimum age requirement for the Tourist Officer post is 18 years, and the maximum age limit is 35 years as of 01-01-2026. Age relaxation is applicable for certain categories.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Tourist Officer role?

Candidates need a Bachelor’s degree or above in Hospitality, Hotel, Travel & Tourism Management/Administration, or a Graduate with a Diploma or above in the same fields from a recognized institution. Computer and Mizo language proficiency are also mandatory.

Q4. How many vacancies are available for the Tourist Officer post?

There is one vacancy for the post of Tourist Officer as per the official notification released by the Mizoram Public Service Commission.

Q5. What is the salary offered for the Tourist Officer position?

The selected candidate for the Tourist Officer post will be placed at Level – 9 in the Pay Matrix, which offers a competitive salary as per government norms.

Q6. What is the application fee for the Mizoram PSC Tourist Officer recruitment?

As per the notification, there is no application fee mentioned for the PwBD category. For other categories, please refer to the official notification for detailed information.

Q7. What is the selection process for the Tourist Officer recruitment?

The selection process for the Mizoram PSC Tourist Officer recruitment will be based on a Direct Recruitment Examination. Candidates need to perform well in this examination to be considered for the post.

Q8. Is there any age relaxation for reserved categories?

Yes, the upper age limit is relaxable up to 5 years in case of ST/SC candidates. Further relaxations may be provided as per government rules and regulations.
