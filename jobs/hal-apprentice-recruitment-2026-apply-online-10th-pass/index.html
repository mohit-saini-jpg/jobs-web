Job Overview

Hindustan Aeronautics Limited has released the official Apprentice Notification 2026 on 18 May 2026 at the official website of HAL. Vacancies have been announced under the HAL Apprentice Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged between 14 to 27 years with a qualification of 10+2 with Science and Mathematics or equivalent along with ITI passed with National Trade Certificate (NCVT) can apply online from 18-05-2026 to 03-06-2026. Qualification / Eligibility

All Posts: Passed 10th class under 10+2 system with Science and Mathematics or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Electronics Mechanic: Passed 10th class under 10+2 system with Science and Mathematics or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Electrician: Passed 10th class examination under 10+2 system with Science as one of the subject or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Fitter: Passed 10th class Examination under 10+2 system of education or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Turner: Passed in 10th class under 10+2 system of education or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Instrument Mechanic: Passed 10th class examination under 10+2 system of education with Science as one of the subjects or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Refrigeration & Air Conditioning Mechanic: Passed 10th class examination with Science and Mathematics under 10+2 system of education or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Draughtsman (Mechanical): Passed 10th class examination under 10+2 system with Science and Mathematics as subjects + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Computer Operator & Programming Assistant (COPA): Passed 12th class examination under 10+2 system of education with Science or Commerce + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Machinist (Grinder): Passed 10th class examination under 10+2 system of education or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Mechanic (Motor Vehicle): Passed 10th class examination under 10+2 system of education or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Electroplater: Passed 10th class examination under 10+2 system of education or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Welder (Gas & Electric): Passed 10th class examination under 10+2 system of education or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Painter (General): Passed 10th class or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). Plumber: Passed 10th class examination under 10+2 system of education or its equivalent + ITI passed with National Trade Certificate issued by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT).
