AP RGUKT IIIT Merit List 2026 - Key Highlights at a Glance : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 13/06/2026

The Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies, Andhra Pradesh (AP RGUKT), has officially released the AP RGUKT IIIT Provisional Merit List 2026 on June 12, 2026. Thousands of SSC (Class 10) 2026 passouts who applied for the coveted six-year Integrated B.Tech programme can now check their campus-wise selection status on the official admissions portal at admissions.rgukt.in. Shortlisted candidates must next attend mandatory certificate verification scheduled from June 19 to June 23, 2026, across all four RGUKT campuses.

If your name appears in the AP RGUKT IIIT Selection List 2026, read on for a complete guide covering how to check and download the merit list, what documents to carry, important dates, and what to do next.

How to Check and Download AP RGUKT IIIT Selection List 2026

Checking your AP RGUKT IIIT Merit List 2026 is a straightforward process. Follow the steps below to view and download your campus-wise selection status from the official portal.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  • Visit the official AP RGUKT admissions portal: admissions.rgukt.in (or rgukt.ac.in)
  • Look for the link titled "AP RGUKT IIIT Provisional Merit List 2026" on the homepage
  • Click on the relevant campus option (Nuzvid / RK Valley / Ongole / Srikakulam)
  • Enter your Application Number and Date of Birth when prompted
  • View your selection status on the screen
  • Download and save the merit list PDF for future reference

Understanding how seats are distributed across campuses helps students know what to expect during counselling and seat allotment.

The total number of available seats is 4,400, with 1,100 seats allotted per campus, including 10% supernumerary seats for EWS (Economically Weaker Sections) candidates.

Certificate Verification Schedule - June 2026

Being on the provisional merit list is just the first step. Shortlisted candidates must complete physical document verification to confirm their seat. This section covers the campus-wise verification schedule and what to expect.

Candidates whose names appear on the campus-wise provisional list will be required to attend mandatory certificate verification, scheduled from June 19 to June 23, 2026, across RGUKT's four campuses: Nuzvid, RK Valley, Ongole, and Srikakulam.

Candidates whose names appear in the merit list must report to their allotted campus according to the verification schedule announced by the university, and should report only on the dates assigned to their respective campuses.

Important: Being included in the provisional merit list does not automatically confirm admission. Candidates must successfully complete document verification before their seats are confirmed.

Documents Required for AP RGUKT IIIT Certificate Verification 2026

Carrying the correct documents is critical to completing the verification process without any hurdles. Below is the complete list of documents required.

Candidates must bring five sets of photocopies along with originals of: SSC Marks Memo, Caste/Community Certificate, EWS Certificate (issued after April 1, 2026), Income Certificate (issued after April 1, 2026), and applicable special category certificates.

Required documents include the SSC marks memo, caste certificate, income certificate, nativity proof, and white ration card. Candidates must ensure all documents are ready to avoid delays.

Complete Document Checklist:

  • SSC (Class 10) Marks Memo - Original + 5 photocopies
  • Caste / Community Certificate
  • EWS Certificate (if applicable, issued after April 1, 2026)
  • Income Certificate (issued after April 1, 2026)
  • Nativity / Domicile Certificate
  • White Ration Card (or equivalent family income proof)
  • Special Category Certificates (NCC, CAP, PH, Sports, Bharat Scouts & Guides - if applicable)
  • Printout of the AP RGUKT IIIT Merit List / Selection Status
  • Application Number and registration details

AP RGUKT IIIT Admissions 2026 - Reservation Policy

Understanding the reservation structure helps candidates from different backgrounds assess their eligibility and category-wise seats more clearly.

Admission counselling for 4,400 seats including 10% EWS supernumerary seats and 5% NRI (candidates who studied outside AP and Telangana) and global seats will be held in June 2026.

NRI and Global candidates need to submit their application during the counselling dates, and seats will be filled as per the merit of the candidates in the 10th class or equivalent examination held in 2026.

The university follows a structured reservation policy for SC, ST, BC, EWS, and special category students such as PH (Physically Handicapped), NCC, CAP (Children of Armed Personnel), Sports, and Bharat Scouts & Guides, as per the Government of Andhra Pradesh norms.

What to Do After Checking the AP RGUKT IIIT Merit List 2026

If your name appears in the provisional selection list, here is a clear action plan to follow immediately.

  • Download and Print your selection status from admissions.rgukt.in
  • Verify your campus allotment and note the verification date assigned to your campus
  • Collect all required original documents and make five sets of photocopies of each
  • Ensure certificates are valid and current - EWS and Income Certificates must be issued after April 1, 2026
  • Report to your allotted campus on time for certificate verification (June 19-23, 2026)
  • Do not miss your reporting date - absentees risk losing their allotted seat

If your name does not appear in the first provisional merit list, do not panic. RGUKT typically releases a second and third selection list to fill remaining vacancies, so continue checking the official portal regularly.
