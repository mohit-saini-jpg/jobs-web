Important Instructions

Applications are accepted online only - no manual or postal applications. All required educational qualification certificates must be obtained on or before 11-06-2026. Free error correction is available once only from 12-06-2026 to 14-06-2026. Paid error correction (Rs. 500/-) is available once only from 15-06-2026 to 17-06-2026 - corrections allowed only in gender, DOB, category, domicile, disability and ex-serviceman entries. No further correction of any kind is permitted after the paid correction window. Portal fee and GST are non-refundable; examination fee is refunded to CG local residents who appear in examination. Admit cards/interview call letters will be uploaded approximately 10 days before exam/interview on the CGPSC website only - not sent individually. Candidates must carry original photo ID (Voter ID / Passport / Driving Licence / PAN Card / Aadhaar) along with admit card. Mobile phones and communication devices are strictly prohibited in examination/interview premises. Candidates found using unfair means, impersonation, or submitting fake documents will be debarred and face criminal prosecution. Candidates currently employed in Govt./PSU/Bank must apply for NOC from appointing authority before or immediately after submitting the online application. Candidates with more than one spouse living are not eligible for appointment. No re-evaluation or re-totalling of answer sheets is available. Candidates are advised to regularly visit www.psc.cg.gov.in for all updates and amendments.

FAQs

Q1What is CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?
A
CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026 is a recruitment test conducted by Chhattisgarh Public Service Commission (CGPSC), Nava Raipur, for 46 Assistant Engineer (Gazetted Second Class) posts in various engineering departments of the Chhattisgarh Government under Advertisement No. 02/2026/Exam.
Q2How many vacancies are released under CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?
A
A total of 46 vacancies have been notified for Assistant Engineer posts, including 42 for AE Civil in Public Works Department, 3 for AE Electrical/Mechanical in Public Works Department, and 1 for AE Civil in General Administration Department (Chief Technical Examiner/Vigilance).
Q3What are the application dates for CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?
A
The online application window opens on 13 May 2026 at 12:00 PM and closes on 11 June 2026 at 11:59 PM, followed by a free correction window from 12 to 14 June 2026 and a paid correction window from 15 to 17 June 2026.
Q4What is the age limit for CGPSC Assistant Engineer Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 21 years and maximum age is 30 years as on 01 January 2026 for non‑residents of Chhattisgarh, while local residents of Chhattisgarh get relaxation up to 40 years with further relaxations for SC/ST/OBC (non‑creamy), women, PwD, ex‑servicemen and specified categories as per state rules, subject to an overall cap of 45 years.
Q5What educational qualifications are required for CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?
A
Candidates must possess a BE/B.Tech degree in Civil, Mechanical or Electrical Engineering from a recognized university or institution; for the AE Civil post in GAD Vigilance, AMIE Part A and B in the relevant engineering discipline, recognized as equivalent to BE by the state government, is also acceptable.
Q6What is the exam pattern for CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?
A
The written exam consists of two papers—Paper I (General Studies) with 150 objective questions for 300 marks and Paper II (Engineering: Civil/Mechanical/Electrical) with 150 objective questions for 300 marks, each of 2 hours 30 minutes duration, followed by an interview/personality test of 75 marks for candidates shortlisted in the written test.
Q7Is there any negative marking in CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?
A
There is no negative marking in either Paper I or Paper II of the State Engineering Service written examination as per the CGPSC pattern notified in earlier engineering service recruitments.
Q8What are the minimum qualifying marks for CGPSC State Engineering Service written examination?
A
Unreserved category candidates must secure at least 40% marks in each paper, while SC, ST and OBC (non‑creamy layer) candidates of Chhattisgarh must secure a minimum of 30% marks in each paper to be considered for shortlisting to the interview stage.
Q9What is the application fee for CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 400 for general/other state candidates and Rs. 300 for SC, ST, OBC (non‑creamy layer) and PwD candidates of Chhattisgarh, plus portal charges and GST for all; local CG candidates who actually appear in the exam get the examination fee refunded, but portal and service charges are non‑refundable.
Q10How can I apply online for CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?
A
Applicants must visit the official CGPSC website, complete one‑time registration, fill the online form with accurate personal and qualification details, upload required documents and photograph/signature, pay the applicable fee online, and finally submit and download the application/fee receipt for future reference.
