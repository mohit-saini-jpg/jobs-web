FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for DHFWS Udupi recruitment?
A
The walk-in interview will be conducted from 04 June 2026 to 11 June 2026. Candidates should ensure they attend within this period.
Q2What are the posts available in the DHFWS Udupi recruitment?
A
The available posts are Medical Officer (MBBS) and ANM/PHCO.
Q3What is the total number of vacancies?
A
There are a total of 17 vacancies for the Medical Officer and ANM/PHCO posts.
Q4What is the educational qualification required for the Medical Officer post?
A
The educational qualification required for the Medical Officer post is an MBBS degree from a recognized institution.
Q5What is the educational qualification for the ANM/PHCO post?
A
For the ANM/PHCO post, candidates must have completed ANM from an institution recognized by the Karnataka Government.
Q6Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment.
Q7What is the salary for the Medical Officer post?
A
The salary for the Medical Officer post is Rs. 60,000/- per month.
Q8What is the salary for the ANM/PHCO post?
A
The salary for the ANM/PHCO post is Rs. 15,667/- per month.
Q9What is the selection process for these posts?
A
The selection process involves an Interview and Document Verification.
Q10Where is the walk-in interview scheduled to be held?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at the DHFWS Udupi office. Specific venue details will be in the official notification.
