Exam Timings : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 23/07/2026

Osmania University's Controller of Examinations has released the exam time table for the BBA/MBA (5 Years Integrated Course) Backlog and Improvement Examinations to be held in August and September 2026. Two separate notifications have been issued: one for the OLD CBCS pattern (2021 & 2022 batch candidates) and one for the Non-CBCS pattern, covering Semesters I to X. Both notifications are dated 22-07-2026.

Important Instructions for Candidates

  • Both notifications are dated 22-07-2026 and issued by the Controller of Examinations, Osmania University, Hyderabad - 500007.
  • The OLD CBCS time table applies specifically to 2021 & 2022 batch candidates; the Non-CBCS time table covers general Backlog & Improvement candidates across Semesters I to X.
  • Match your semester, batch, and paper name carefully against the correct notification (OLD CBCS vs Non-CBCS) before your exam, since paper names and elective options differ between the two patterns.
  • Elective and Discipline Specific Elective (DSE) options are grouped under a single date; candidates appear only for the specific elective/specialization paper allotted to them.
  • Second Language papers offer a choice among Telugu, Hindi, Arabic, Sanskrit, and (for some semesters) French.
  • Some papers have reduced timing (e.g., 10:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. or 2:00 p.m. to 3:30 p.m.) instead of the standard 3-hour session check the specific date carefully.
  • For any information not listed here (such as exam centre allotment or fee payment deadlines), refer directly to the official notification link below.
