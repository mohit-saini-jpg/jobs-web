TG CPGET 2026 Notification

TG CPGET 2026 Notification has been officially released. The Telangana Common Post Graduate Entrance Test (TG CPGET) 2026 online registration started on 15 May 2026. Conducted by Kakatiya University on behalf of Telangana universities, TG CPGET is the gateway for admission into various PG and PG Diploma courses offered by Osmania University, Kakatiya University, Telangana University, and other participating universities.

The TG CPGET-2026 is a state-level entrance test for admission into Postgraduate (PG) and PG Diploma programmes offered by Osmania, Kakatiya, Telangana, Mahatma Gandhi, Palamuru, Satavahana, Veeranari Chakali Ilamma Women’s, JNTUH, and Dr. Manmohan Singh Earth Sciences Universities.

How to Apply for TG CPGET 2026

  • Visit the official website: https://cpget.tgche.ac.in
  • Complete registration and pay the fee.
  • Fill the online application form.
  • Upload required documents and photograph.
  • Submit and take a printout of the application.

Apply Online Link Placement: Place prominently after Important Dates and in the Important Links section.

Eligibility Criteria for TG CPGET 2026

  • Candidates must have passed the qualifying examination (Bachelor’s Degree) with minimum 40% marks (pass marks sufficient for SC/ST candidates).
  • Eligibility criteria vary as per course and subject (detailed in the brochure).
  • Candidates appearing in the final year of qualifying examination are also eligible.

Documents Required for TG CPGET 2026

  • SSC / 10th Certificate
  • Qualifying Examination Marks Memo & Provisional Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Caste / Income / EWS Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport-size Photograph & Signature

Selection Process

Qualifying in TG CPGET 2026 is followed by web-based counselling, certificate verification, and seat allotment based on rank, category, and preferences.
