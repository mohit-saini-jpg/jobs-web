How to Download OUAT Hall Ticket 2026 - Step-by-Step Process

Downloading the OUAT 2026 admit card is a straightforward process that takes only a few minutes. Follow each step carefully to ensure you download the correct hall ticket and verify all printed details before the exam day.

  • Visit the official website at ouat.ac.in
  • Click on the "Download OUAT Admit Card 2026" link on the homepage or ticker
  • You will be redirected to the candidate login page
  • Enter your Application Number and Date of Birth
  • Enter the captcha / special characters shown on screen and click Login
  • Your OUAT UG admit card 2026 will appear on screen
  • Check all details carefully name, roll number, exam centre, date, and shift
  • Download the PDF and take at least two colour printouts on A4 paper
  • Keep one copy for the exam, one for counselling, and one as a backup

Important: Your exam centre allotment cannot be changed once the admit card is issued.

Details Printed on OUAT Admit Card 2026

The OUAT hall ticket 2026 carries all the information you need to appear for the entrance examination. Before heading to the exam centre, candidates must cross-check every field on the admit card against their application details. If any discrepancy is found, the OUAT admission helpdesk must be contacted immediately corrections cannot be made at the exam centre.

The OUAT admit card includes important information such as the candidate's name, roll number, date of birth, photo, signature, exam date and time, exam centre address, and exam guidelines. Additionally, the hall ticket will indicate the exam shift and reporting time, since the exam is conducted in multiple shifts across both days.

Details present on the OUAT 2026 hall ticket:

  • Candidate's Full Name
  • Application / Roll Number
  • Date of Birth
  • Photograph and Signature
  • Exam Centre Name and Address
  • Exam Date (July 1 or July 2, 2026)
  • Exam Shift and Reporting Time
  • Course Applied For
  • Category
  • Important Instructions for Exam Day

Documents to Carry to OUAT Exam Centre 2026

Along with the printed admit card, every candidate must carry one original photo identity document for verification at the exam centre. A photocopy or digital image of the ID will not be accepted. The following documents are considered valid:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID Card
  • Driving Licence
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Student ID Card from the last attended school or college (bearing a photograph)
  • Any identity proof issued by the State or Central Government

Candidates are advised to carry two colour prints of the OUAT hall ticket at the examination centre. Along with the admit card, they will be required to carry one photo identity card in original. shiksha

Exam Day Instructions - OUAT 2026

Following exam day rules strictly ensures candidates are not turned away at the gate. Below are the key instructions every OUAT 2026 test-taker must follow:

  • Report to the exam centre at least 60 minutes before the scheduled exam time
  • Late entry will not be permitted under any circumstances
  • Carry a colour printout of the admit card digital copies on phone will not be accepted
  • Carry one original photo ID photocopies are not valid
  • Do not carry any personal electronic gadgets, calculators, or smart devices inside the exam hall
  • Follow all instructions printed on the admit card
  • If you find any error in your name, photograph, category, or exam centre, contact the OUAT helpdesk immediately before the exam date
