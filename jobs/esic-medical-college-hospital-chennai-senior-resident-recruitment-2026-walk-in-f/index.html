Reservation Policy

ESIC Chennai Senior Resident recruitment follows Central Government of India reservation guidelines. Here is what each category needs to know. OBC (Non-Creamy Layer): Must submit community certificate in the prescribed Central Government format (DoPT OM No. 36036/2/2013-Estt(Res) dated 30.05.2014). Certificates in other formats will not be accepted. SC / ST: Must hold a valid caste/tribe certificate issued by an authorized government authority. EWS: Must possess a valid income & assets certificate showing gross annual family income below ₹8 lakh and no disqualifying property assets. PwD (Persons with Disability): 17 posts horizontally reserved. Minimum 40% disability required. Certificate must be issued by a government-constituted Medical Board only - certificates from individual hospitals or practitioners are not valid. Ex-Servicemen: Fee exemption applicable; discharge summary required

Selection Process – How Candidates Are Chosen

The ESIC Chennai Senior Resident 2026 selection is primarily interview-based, but there is an important provision for screening if footfall is high. Here is exactly how the process works. Document Verification (9:00 AM – 11:00 AM): All candidates must report during this window. Only those whose documents pass verification will proceed to the interview. Screening Test (if applicable): If the candidate-to-post ratio exceeds 1:10 , a screening test may be held to shortlist candidates at a 1:5 ratio . The test will consist of MCQs in the concerned discipline. Screening test marks do not carry weight in the final result. Walk-In Interview: Final selection is based solely on interview performance before the Selection Board. Important Terms & Conditions

Before walking in, candidates should be fully aware of the contractual obligations and institutional rules governing this ESIC Senior Resident engagement. Candidates who have already completed 3 years under the Central Residency Scheme are not eligible . Government employees applying must carry a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their employer. A Security Deposit of ₹30,000/- (Demand Draft in favour of ESI Fund A/C No. 1, payable at State Bank of India, Ashok Nagar, Chennai-78) is mandatory at the time of joining. ESIC Medical College & Hospital Chennai Senior Resident : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in ESIC Chennai Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?
A
ESIC Medical College & Hospital, K.K. Nagar, Chennai has announced 60 Senior Resident posts (3-year tenure under 7th CPC) across 23 departments - 19 posts on July 8 and 41 posts on July 9, 2026.
Q2When and where is the ESIC Chennai walk-in interview 2026?
A
The walk-in interview is scheduled on July 8, 2026 (Wednesday) and July 9, 2026 (Thursday) at ESIC Medical College & Hospital, Ashok Pillar Road, K.K. Nagar, Chennai – 600 078. Candidates must report between 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM for document verification.
Q3What is the salary for ESIC Senior Resident posts under 7th CPC?
A
The basic pay is ₹67,700/- per month at Pay Level-11 under the 7th Pay Commission. The gross monthly emoluments (as of January 2026) are approximately ₹1,60,045/-, inclusive of applicable allowances.
Q4Who is eligible to apply for ESIC KK Nagar Senior Resident posts?
A
Candidates holding MD / MS / DNB in the relevant specialty, or M.Sc. with Ph.D. in applicable pre-clinical subjects are eligible. Age limit is 45 years for clinical specialties and 50 years for pre-clinical departments. Eligibility must conform to NMC-TEQ 2025 and PGMER 2023 standards.
Q5Is there any written exam for ESIC Senior Resident recruitment in Chennai?
A
Normally, selection is based on walk-in interview performance. However, if applicants exceed 10 times the number of vacancies per post, the authorities may conduct a screening MCQ test to shortlist candidates at a 1:5 ratio. Screening test marks do not affect the final selection.
Q6What is the application fee for ESIC Senior Resident 2026?
A
The fee is ₹500/- for UR, OBC, and EWS candidates, payable by Demand Draft only in favour of "ESI Fund Account No. 1" payable at Chennai. SC, ST, PwD, women, and ex-servicemen are exempt from the fee.
Q7Can a doctor with a General Medicine degree apply for ICU or Emergency Medicine at ESIC Chennai?
A
Yes. For ICU, candidates with MD/DNB in General Medicine are eligible as Preferential Order 2. For Emergency Medicine, candidates with MD/DNB/MS in General Medicine, Anaesthesia, Respiratory Medicine, General Surgery, or Orthopaedics are eligible in addition to Emergency Medicine specialists.
Q8How many posts are reserved for PwD candidates in ESIC Senior Resident 2026?
A
17 out of 60 posts are horizontally reserved for Persons with Benchmark Disability (PwBD) in identified specialties. A minimum of 40% disability, certified by a duly constituted government Medical Board, is required.
Q9Is private practice allowed during the ESIC Senior Resident tenure?
A
No. Private practice of any kind is strictly prohibited during the 3-year contractual engagement with ESIC.
Q10Where can I download the official notification and application form?
A
The official PDF notification including the application form (Annexure C) is available on the ESIC official recruitment portal.
