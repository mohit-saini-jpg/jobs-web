Important Instructions

These are CONTRACTUAL positions - no right to regular or permanent employment in C-DAC is created by engagement under this recruitment. Candidates may be deputed or transferred from one project to another at any C-DAC project location across India based on organizational requirement. The contract is initially for up to 3 years (1 year for Kolkata centre) or project completion, whichever is earlier. Extension is possible based on performance and project requirement. All correspondence including interview call letters will be sent by email only - candidates must keep their registered email ID and mobile number active throughout the process. C-DAC reserves the right to increase/decrease the number of posts, change eligibility criteria, cancel any or all positions, or modify/cancel the selection process at any stage without notice. If selected candidates are found ineligible at any stage - even after joining - their engagement is liable to be terminated without notice. No TA/DA is payable for attending the written test or interview at any centre. C-DAC has not authorized any agent or agency to represent it for recruitment matters - beware of fraudulent offers. All updates, corrigenda, and extensions will be published only on the official website www.cdac.in. Canvassing in any form will result in disqualification.

FAQs

Q1What is C-DAC JIT June 2026 Recruitment and when can I apply?
A
C-DAC’s JIT (Just‑In‑Time) June 2026 cycle (Advt. No. CORP/JIT/02/2026) invites online applications for contractual technical and support positions across multiple centres. Online applications open on 01 June 2026 and close on 20 June 2026 (time varies by centre).
Q2Which centres and domains are covered under this recruitment?
A
Recruitment covers 11 centres: Bengaluru, Chennai, Noida, Pune, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Delhi, Mohali, Kolkata, Thiruvananthapuram, and Guwahati/CINE (North East). Domains include AI/ML, Cyber Security, VLSI, HPC, Embedded Systems, Full‑Stack, Quantum Computing, IoT, Robotics, Data Science and more.
Q3What posts are available and what are the typical salary ranges?
A
Positions include Project Associate, Project Technician, Project Engineer, Senior Project Engineer / Module Lead, Project Manager, Project Officer, Administrative Associate, and others. Consolidated salaries typically range from Rs. 20,200–Rs. 1,60,000 per month depending on role and centre (e.g., Project Associate: Rs. 28,750–35,000; Senior Project Engineer: Rs. 60,000–75,000; Project Manager up to Rs. 1,60,000).
Q4What are the educational and experience eligibility criteria?
A
Eligibility varies by position: BE/B.Tech, ME/M.Tech, MCA, Ph.D., or relevant graduation/post‑graduation in ICT&E domains are common. Experience bands differ by post (fresher to 15+ years). Qualifications must be from AICTE/UGC‑approved or recognized institutions. Final semester candidates may apply but must produce final marks to join.
Q5Is there an application fee?
A
No. C-DAC charges no application fee for any post or category under this JIT June 2026 cycle.
Q6What is the selection process?
A
Multi‑stage: initial screening (based on academic records and experience), written/skill test (if applicable), and personal interview. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted by email. No TA/DA is provided for tests or interviews.
Q7Can I apply to multiple centres or multiple posts?
A
Yes, but you must submit separate online applications for each post/centre you wish to be considered for. Each post has a separate advertisement and vacancy list.
Q8What documents must I upload and keep ready for verification?
A
Upload at application: passport‑size photo (.jpg) and CV/resume (PDF). For document verification/joining: mark sheets and degree certificates from Class 10 onwards, CGPA‑to‑percentage conversion (if applicable), experience certificates with detailed employer info, category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS), PwBD certificate (if applicable), proof of DOB, and NOC if currently employed and required.
Q9Are these permanent jobs? Will candidates be posted anywhere in India?
A
No. These are purely contractual engagements (typically up to 3 years or project duration; Kolkata may have 1‑year terms). There is no right to regular/ permanent employment. Selected candidates can be deputed or transferred to any project location across India as per requirement.
Q10Where do I apply and whom should I contact for centre‑specific queries?
A
Apply online via the C‑DAC careers portal: https://careers.cdac.in/ — select the relevant centre advertisement and apply. Centre‑specific recruitment emails are listed in the respective notifications (e.g., [email protected] for Bengaluru, chn‑[email protected] for Chennai, hr‑[email protected] for Noida). All official updates and corrigenda will be on https://www.cdac.in/ and the careers portal.
