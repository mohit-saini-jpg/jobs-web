Eligibility Criteria for IAF Agniveervayu Musician Recruitment 2026

Open to Indian candidates from all districts of all States and all Union Territories of India. Passed Matriculation/10th or equivalent with minimum pass marks from any Central, State or Union Territory Government recognised School/Board, as on the date of registration. Only unmarried male and female candidates are eligible. Candidates must undertake not to marry during the four-year engagement period; violation leads to dismissal from service. Female candidates must additionally undertake not to become pregnant during the engagement period.

Musical Proficiency Requirements

Musical ability is a mandatory qualifying requirement in addition to the educational qualification. Candidates must have proficiency in music with accuracy in tempo and pitch, and must be able to sing one complete song. Candidates must be able to perform one preparatory tune and demonstrate knowledge of a notation system (Staff notation, Tablature, Tonic Solfa, Hindustani, or Carnatic). Candidates must be able to tune individual instruments (where applicable) and match unknown notes on vocal or instruments. Candidates must have proficiency in playing at least one instrument from List A or List B below. It is desirable (though not mandatory) to have proficiency in two instruments - one from each list. Salary and Pay Package

Agniveervayu (Musician) will be paid an Agniveer package of ₹30,000 per month in the first year, with a fixed yearly increment, along with Risk and Hardship allowances, Dress and Travel allowances, and perks such as ration, clothing, accommodation, and Leave Travel Concession (LTC) as per existing rules. Total contribution to the Agniveer Corpus Fund after four years is approximately ₹5.02 lakh from the Agniveervayu's own contribution, matched by approximately ₹5.02 lakh from the Government of India. On exit after four years, this totals approximately ₹10.04 lakh as the Seva Nidhi package (excluding interest). There is no Provident Fund contribution requirement, and no gratuity or pensionary benefits are applicable. Agniveervayu will also be provided non-contributory Life Insurance cover of ₹48 lakh for the duration of the four-year engagement period.

IAF Agniveervayu Musician Recruitment 2026 (Intake : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection for Agniveervayu (Musician) Intake 01/2027 involves the following stages, conducted in sequence: Verification of Eligibility Documents - original or DigiLocker-verified educational certificates and other applicable documents are scrutinised at the rally venue before the Physical Fitness Test. Physical Fitness Test (PFT-I) - 1.6 km run: within 7 minutes for male candidates, within 8 minutes for female candidates. Physical Fitness Test (PFT-II) - conducted after 10 minutes of recuperation: Male candidates: 10 push-ups (1 minute), 10 sit-ups (1 minute), 20 squats (1 minute), with rest breaks between each exercise. Female candidates: 10 sit-ups (1 minute 30 seconds), 15 squats (1 minute), with rest breaks between exercises. English Written Test - objective type, 30 questions, 30 minutes duration, based on Matriculation/10th CBSE syllabus. Candidates must qualify this test. Answers

Q2When and where will the pre-registered recruitment rally be held?

The rally will be held from 31 August 2026 to 09 September 2026 at two venues: 2 ASC, Air Force Station New Delhi, and 7 ASC, No. 1 Cubbon Road, Bengaluru.
Q3What is the age limit for IAF Agniveervayu Musician Recruitment?
A
Candidates must be born between 01 January 2005 and 01 July 2009 (both dates inclusive). The upper age limit at the time of enrolment, after clearing all selection stages, is 22 years.
Q4What is the educational qualification required?
A
Candidates must have passed Matriculation/10th or equivalent with minimum pass marks from any Central, State, or UT Government recognised School/Board.
Q5Is proficiency in a musical instrument mandatory?
A
Yes. In addition to the 10th pass qualification, candidates must demonstrate music proficiency and be able to play at least one instrument from List A or List B specified in the notification, along with a valid musical experience certificate.
Q6Can married candidates apply for Agniveervayu Musician recruitment?
A
No. Only unmarried male and female candidates are eligible. Candidates must undertake not to marry during the four-year engagement period, failing which they will be dismissed from service.

A
The starting Agniveer package is ₹30,000 per month in the first year, rising to ₹40,000 per month by the fourth year, with a fixed yearly increment plus applicable allowances.

Selection involves document verification, Physical Fitness Test (PFT-I and PFT-II), English Written Test, Music Proficiency Test, and a Medical Examination, conducted in that sequence.
