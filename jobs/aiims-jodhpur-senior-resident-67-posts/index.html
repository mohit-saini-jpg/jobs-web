FAQs

Q1What is the application date range and where can I download the notification PDF?
A
Application dates: 1st June 2026 to 30th June 2026 (Tuesday) by 17:00 hrs.
Q2How many vacancies are available and for which post?
A
Total vacancies: 67 posts of Senior Resident (Non DM/MCh.).
Q3Can I apply for multiple departments?
A
No. A candidate can apply for only one department only. If you want to apply for another department, you must choose carefully before submitting.
Q4What is the educational qualification required?
A
Clinical departments: MD/MS/DNB/MDS in the relevant specialty. Anatomy, Biochemistry: MD/DNB or M.Sc. (Human Anatomy/Biochemistry) with PhD. School of Public Health: MD/DNB in Community Medicine/PSM or MPH with PhD. Qualifying exam result deadline: Must be declared by 31st August 2026; later results lead to cancellation of candidature.
Q5What is the age limit and relaxation?
A
Upper age limit: 45 years as on 30th June 2026.
Q6What is the pay scale and tenure of appointment?
A
Pay: ₹67,700/- per month (Level-11, 7th CPC) + NPA (for medical candidates) + other allowances.
Q7How is the selection done?
A
Selection is based on a 100-mark merit system: 50% – MCQ-based written examination 30% – Academic record (MBBS/BDS marks, extra attempts, PubMed-indexed publications) 20% – Personal interview
