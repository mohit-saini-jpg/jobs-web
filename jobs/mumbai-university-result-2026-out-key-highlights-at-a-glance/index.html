About the Examination

The supplementary examination for M.Sc. Cyber Security Semester I was conducted in May 2026 as part of the NEP 2020 (National Education Policy) framework. This exam was made available to repeater candidates - students who had not cleared one or more subjects in the regular examination - giving them an opportunity to improve their academic standing.

The programme is jointly administered by the University Department of Information Technology (MU-566) and follows the credit-based grading system as mandated under NEP 2020.

Mumbai University follows a standardized letter-grade and grade-point system for all NEP 2020 programmes. Students should refer to the table below to interpret their grade correctly.

The SGPI (Semester Grade Performance Index) is calculated using the formula: SGPI = ΣCG ÷ ΣC, where C represents credit points and G represents grade points for each subject.

How to Check Mumbai University M.Sc. Cyber Security Result 2026

Candidates can access their supplementary result through the official Mumbai University results portal. Here is the step-by-step process to check and download the result:

  • Visit the official result portal: mumresults.in
  • Look for the result link titled "M.Sc. Cyber Security - Semester I - Supplementary - NEP 2020"
  • Click on the PDF result link to open the Office Register
  • Search for your Seat Number or Name in the document
  • Note down your subject-wise marks, grade points, total marks, and SGPI
  • Download and save the PDF for future reference

Students may also visit the official Mumbai University website at mu.ac.in for any additional announcements or notifications related to marksheets, rechecking, or revaluation.

Result Status of Candidates

The Office Register published by the University of Mumbai contains individual results for all candidates who appeared for this supplementary examination. Results are marked using the following status codes:

  • P (Successful): The candidate has passed in that subject
  • F (Unsuccessful): The candidate has failed in that subject
  • ABS/AA (Absent): The candidate was absent for the external examination
  • E: Exemption can be claimed
  • RPV: Result is provisional
  • NULL: Result declared null and void
  • ADC: Admission cancelled
  • RR: Reserved result

Candidates whose result is marked ABSENT are treated as unsuccessful for that semester, regardless of their internal scores.
