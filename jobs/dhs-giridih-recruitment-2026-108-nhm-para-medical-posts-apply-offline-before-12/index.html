Eligibility & Educational Qualification

Educational qualification differs post-to-post (see the vacancy table above). Some qualification requirements common across multiple posts include: ANM posts (NHM, MTC, RBSK, NUHM): 10+2 or equivalent with an ANM course recognized by the Government and approved by the Indian Nursing Council (INC), and registration under the Jharkhand Nursing Council. Staff Nurse posts (NHM, NBSU): 10+2/Intermediate with 3 years of General Nursing and Midwifery training from a Government-recognized institution, registered under the Jharkhand Nursing Council. Several technical/supervisory posts (STS, STLS, TB-HV) additionally require a certificate course in computer operations (minimum 2 months) and, in some cases, a permanent two-wheeler driving licence. Preferential/desirable qualifications (such as relevant field experience, NTEP experience, or fluency in local languages) are listed separately for each
