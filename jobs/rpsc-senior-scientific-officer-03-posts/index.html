How to Apply

  • 1Recent photograph (as per RPSC guidelines - to be captured live via webcam/mobile at the time of online application)
  • 2Left thumb impression scanned and uploaded (mandatory; also required at examination centre)
  • 3Aadhaar Card / Janadhaar Card for identity verification (mandatory before applying)
  • 4Admit Card / Call Letter (downloaded from RPSC website) along with valid photo identity proof in original
  • 5Caste Certificate in prescribed Government format (for SC/ST candidates) - issued by competent authority
  • 6OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in prescribed format issued before the last date of application (for OBC candidates)
  • 7Income and Assets Certificate (for EWS candidates) - issued in prescribed format by competent authority
  • 8Disability Certificate in prescribed format (for PwBD candidates) as per RPwD Act, 2016
  • 9Educational qualification certificates and marksheets (10th onwards, including M.Sc./M.Tech./M.E. and Computer Science qualification)
  • 10Experience Certificate in prescribed proforma (as per Annexure available in notification) - certifying at least 2 years of FSL/relevant experience after attainment of educational qualification
  • 11Last month's salary slip / proof of current employment (if applicable)
  • 12Character / Antecedent verification certificate from competent authority
  • 13Medical fitness certificate from competent authority at the time of appointment
  • 14Candidates must complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the SSO Portal before applying; no application can be submitted without a valid OTR.
  • 15The detailed advertisement and Instructions for Applicants on the RPSC website (https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in) are part of this notification - candidates must read them before applying.
  • 16Candidates must apply separately for each post they wish to apply for - a separate online application must be submitted for each post.
  • 17Candidates belonging to OBC Creamy Layer from Rajasthan and candidates from other states' SC/ST/OBC categories must apply as General/UR candidates.
  • 18The online application must be submitted on or before 10-07-2026 (up to 12:00 midnight). No offline or physical application will be accepted under any circumstances.
  • 19After submitting the online application, an Application Form Number (Application No.) will be generated - if this is not received, the application has not been successfully submitted.
  • 20Candidates must ensure that all educational qualification and experience required as per this advertisement is obtained on or before the last date of online application.
  • 21Online application Amendment (correction) is possible on payment of Rs. 500/- within 10 days after the last date of application, through the RPSC website; after this, no amendment will be accepted.
  • 22One Time Registration (OTR) details - name, father's name, date of birth, gender - cannot be amended in the online application; any discrepancy with Aadhaar/Janadhaar must be corrected in the OTR before applying.
  • 23Candidates with pending disciplinary/vigilance cases or adverse RBI/Statutory Authority observations are not eligible for appointment.
  • 24PwBD candidates eligible for scribe facility: candidates with 40% or more physical disability having limitation in writing are eligible; scribe facility and compensatory time will be provided as per RPwD Act, 2016 and RPSC guidelines. Candidates must apply for scribe facility at least two days before the examination date.
  • 25Mobile phones, smartwatches, or any electronic/communication devices are not allowed inside the examination hall.
  • 26All revisions/corrigendum (if any) will be hosted on the RPSC website (https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in) only - candidates should visit regularly.
  • 27Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification.
  • 28Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be Rajasthan only.
  • 29RPSC reserves the right to cancel the selection process partly/entirely at any stage without assigning any reason.
