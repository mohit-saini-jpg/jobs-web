Shivamogga District Court Peon Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

Selection will be based on the Karnataka Civil Services (General Recruitment) Rules, 1977 and the Karnataka Subordinate Courts (Clerical and Other Posts) (Recruitment) Rules, 1982 (as amended in 2007 and 2021). Based on the overall percentage of marks obtained in the qualifying examination (SSLC), candidates will be shortlisted at a ratio of 1:10 (10 candidates per vacancy) with the highest marks, and called for interview. The interview will assess the candidate's ability to read and write Kannada, and general knowledge, and carries 10 marks. Final selection will be based on marks scored in the interview. Candidates selected will undergo a probation period of 2 years as per the Karnataka Civil Services (Probation) Rules, 1977, extendable if necessary.
