Q3. What is the educational qualification required for Tripura Forest Guard and Forester posts?

For Forest Guard posts, candidates must have passed Madhyamik (Class 10) or equivalent examination. For Forester posts, candidates must have passed Higher Secondary (+2 Stage) or equivalent examination. Knowledge in Bengali or Kok-borok is desirable for both positions. Candidates must possess the prescribed educational qualification on or before the closing date of application.

Q4. What is the age limit for Tripura Forest Guard and Forester Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must be between 18 to 40 years of age as on 01 May 2026. Upper age relaxation of 5 years is admissible for SC/ST candidates and Government employees. The age calculation date is 01 May 2026 for all applicants. Candidates should verify their age eligibility carefully before applying, as this is a mandatory criterion.

Q5. Is Permanent Resident Certificate of Tripura (PRTC) mandatory for application?

Yes, possession of a valid Permanent Resident Certificate of Tripura (PRTC) is absolutely mandatory for all applicants. Candidates without a valid PRTC are not eligible to apply for these posts under the Tripura Forest Department Recruitment 2026. Only bonafide residents of Tripura State with valid PRTC documentation can submit applications for Forest Guard and Forester positions.

Q7. What is the Physical Efficiency Test for Tripura Forest Department Recruitment 2026?

The Physical Efficiency Test (PET) is qualifying in nature and requires candidates to cover a specified distance on foot within 4 hours. Male candidates must cover 25 km within 4 hours, while female candidates must cover 16 km within 4 hours. Only candidates who qualify in the Physical Test will proceed to the next stage of selection. Women candidates must submit a self-declaration that they are not pregnant on the date of the Physical Test.

Q8. What is the selection process for Tripura Forest Guard and Forester posts?

Selection will be conducted through a Written Examination (carrying 85% marks weightage) followed by an Interview (carrying 15% marks weightage). Only candidates securing cut-off marks in the written test will be called for the interview. Final selection is subject to medical fitness.
