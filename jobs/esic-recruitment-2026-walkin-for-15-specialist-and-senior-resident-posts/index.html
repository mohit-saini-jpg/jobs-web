Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for ESIC Kota?

The walk-in interview for ESIC Kota Specialist and Senior Resident posts is scheduled for 02 June 2026. Candidates must report on this date for registration and the interview process.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for Specialist and Senior Resident posts at ESIC Kota?

ESIC Hospital Kota has announced a total of 15 vacancies for the positions of Specialist and Senior Resident across various disciplines.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Senior Resident posts?

For Senior Resident posts, candidates must possess a DM/PG/DNB/Diploma in the concerned specialty from a recognized university and be registered with the MCI or State Medical Council.

Q4. What is the maximum age limit for applying to these posts?

The maximum age limit for all the Specialist and Senior Resident posts at ESIC Kota is 69 years.

Q5. Is there any application fee for ESIC Kota recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.

Q6. What is the salary structure for the Specialist posts?

For Specialist posts, the salary is ₹1,21,408/- (Fixed) per month for Full Time engagement, and ₹60,000 + ₹15,000 for Part Time engagement.

Q7. What is the selection process for ESIC Kota recruitment?

The selection process involves a Walk-in Interview followed by Document Verification.

Q8. What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?

Candidates must carry all original documents along with one set of self-attested photocopies, including ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates (if applicable), and category certificates (if applicable).
