Selection Process

  • Stage Details
  • Stage 1 Pre-registration on respective online portal (mandatory before walk-in)
  • Stage 2 Walk-in Interview at Training Institute, BIOM, Bacheli Complex
  • The selection process for NMDC Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is through Walk-in Interview only. No written examination will be conducted. Candidates must report at the interview venue before 09:00 AM on the respective walk-in interview date to register themselves. The walk-in interview shall commence from 10:00 AM at the Training Institute
  • BIOM
  • Bacheli Complex
  • Bacheli. Candidates appearing for the interview shall not be paid or reimbursed any TA/DA.
  • This scheme does not guarantee any employment on completion of apprenticeship training.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must attend the walk-in interview on the date mentioned against their respective category at the Training Institute, BIOM, Bacheli Complex, Bacheli. There is no online application form to be submitted on the NMDC website. Prior registration on the respective portal is mandatory before attending the walk-in interview - Trade Apprentice candidates must register at www.apprenticeshipindia.org and Graduate / Technician Apprentice candidates must register at https://nats.education.gov.in before the walk-in interview date.
  • Candidates must reach the interview site before 09:00 AM to register themselves for appearing in the walk-in interview. No TA/DA will be paid or reimbursed for attending the interview.
  • Documents to Bring at the Time of Walk-in Interview:
  • Resume affixed with a recent passport size photograph on the right corner
  • Proof of Date of Birth (original + photocopy)
  • Aadhaar Card (original + photocopy)
  • Educational Qualification certificates (original + photocopy)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable, original + photocopy)
  • Walk-in Interview Venue: Training Institute, BIOM, Bacheli Complex, Bacheli – 494553, Dantewada, Chhattisgarh

Important Instructions

  • Candidates who have already completed Apprenticeship Training under the Apprenticeship Act-1961 are not eligible to apply. Candidates with one year or more experience in the relevant category are not eligible to apply. Candidates who completed required qualifications during 2022 and before 2022 need not respond to this advertisement. Trade Apprentice candidates must pre-register at www.apprenticeshipindia.org before the walk-in interview. Graduate and Technician Apprentice candidates must pre-register at https://nats.education.gov.in before the walk-in interview. Candidates must report at the interview venue before 09:00 AM on their respective interview date. The walk-in interview commences at 10:00 AM; candidates arriving late may not be entertained. No TA/DA will be paid or reimbursed to candidates appearing for the walk-in interview. Name, Father's name, Mother's name, and Date of Birth must be identical across all educational and identity documents. This apprenticeship scheme does not guarantee any employment on completion of training. Original documents along with photocopies must be brought for verification and submission at the time of interview.
