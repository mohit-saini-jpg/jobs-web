Steps to Check Gauhati University Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their Gauhati University Result 2026 from the official website:

  • Visit the official website of Gauhati University at gauhati.ac.in
  • Navigate to the 'Results' or 'Examination' section on the homepage
  • Click on the result notification link or directly visit web.gauhati.ac.in/result-notifications
  • Look for your respective result in the list - M.Sc Mathematics Semester III Re-evaluation, M.A Women's Studies Semester I Supplementary, or M.Sc Biotechnology Semester I Supplementary
  • Click on the result link dated 28th April 2026 for your course and semester
  • The result will open as a PDF file via Google Drive link
  • Wait for the PDF to load completely in your browser
  • Look for your roll number in the result PDF
  • Check your subject-wise marks and overall status carefully
  • Verify all the details including name, roll number, subject codes, and marks obtained
  • Download the PDF result copy by clicking on the download icon
  • Save the provisional result copy for future reference and academic purposes
  • Take a printout of the result for your academic records
  • If any discrepancy is found, contact the Controller of Examinations immediately
