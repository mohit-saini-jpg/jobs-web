How to Download TS CPGET Hall Ticket 2026

This section explains the step-by-step process candidates need to follow once the download link becomes active on the official website.

  • Visit the official website, cpget.tgche.ac.in.
  • Look for the link labelled "TS CPGET 2026 Hall Ticket Download."
  • Enter your login credentials, typically the registration number and date of birth.
  • Submit the details to view your hall ticket on screen.
  • Carefully verify all the printed details for accuracy.
  • Download the admit card and take a clear printout for the exam day.

No hall tickets will be sent by post or email; candidates must download them directly from the official portal.

Details Mentioned on the TS CPGET Hall Ticket

This section lists the key information candidates should check immediately after downloading their admit card to avoid last-minute discrepancies.

The TS CPGET hall ticket typically includes the following details:

  • Candidate's name and date of birth
  • Registration number and roll number
  • Examination date and reporting time
  • Test centre name and address
  • Candidate's photograph and signature
  • Important exam-day instructions

If any discrepancy is noticed in the printed details, candidates should contact the TGCHE or Kakatiya University helpdesk immediately to get it corrected before the exam date.

TS CPGET 2026 Exam Pattern

Understanding the exam structure helps candidates plan their final revision strategy effectively before exam day.

  • The exam is conducted in online Computer Based Test (CBT) mode.
  • The question paper consists of 100 objective-type multiple-choice questions.
  • Each question carries 1 mark, taking the total to 100 marks.
  • The total duration of the exam is 90 minutes.
  • There is no negative marking for wrong answers.
  • Questions may include analogies, matching items, classification problems, and comprehension-based questions on research studies.

For combined science streams such as the BCESFSG&M group (Biochemistry, Environmental Science, Forensic Science, Genetics, and Microbiology) and M.Sc. Biotechnology, the paper is split into Part-A (Chemistry, 40 marks) and Part-B (the chosen elective subject, 60 marks).

Documents Required at the TS CPGET 2026 Exam Centre

This section outlines the essential items candidates must carry to the test centre, since entry is not permitted without proper documentation.

  • A printed copy of the TS CPGET 2026 hall ticket
  • A valid government-issued photo identity proof
  • Special institutional proformas for candidates applying under Teacher or Laboratory Assistant quotas

Electronic devices, including mobile phones, smartwatches, calculators, and any Bluetooth-enabled gadgets, are strictly prohibited inside the examination hall, and no storage facility is provided at the centres for such items.

TS CPGET 2026 Test Zones

This section covers the regional distribution of exam centres set up to make travel easier for candidates across Telangana.

The exam authorities have established 12 official test zones, covering Hyderabad (divided into North, South, East, and West zones) along with district headquarters including Adilabad, Karimnagar, Khammam, Kodad, Nalgonda, Mahabubnagar, Warangal, and Nizamabad. The exact centre allotted will be printed on the candidate's hall ticket.

TS CPGET 2026 Results and Next Steps

This section briefly explains what happens after the exam, helping candidates understand the broader admission timeline.

After the CBT is conducted, the preliminary answer key is expected to be released, followed by a window for raising objections. Once the final answer key and results are declared, candidates can participate in the counselling and web-options process to secure seats across Telangana's state universities and affiliated colleges based on their rank, category, and seat availability.
