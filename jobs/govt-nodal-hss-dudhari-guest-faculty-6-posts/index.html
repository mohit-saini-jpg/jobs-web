FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for the Guest Faculty posts?
A
The walk-in interview for the Guest Faculty posts at Government Nodal Higher Secondary School, Dudhari is scheduled for 12th June 2026. Candidates are advised to reach the venue on time.
Q2What is the minimum educational qualification required for the Guest Faculty positions?
A
The minimum educational qualification required is a Post Graduate degree with at least 55% marks in the respective subject. Preference will be given to candidates with B.Ed and PGDCA.
Q3What is the age limit for the Guest Faculty recruitment?
A
The maximum age limit for all Guest Faculty posts is 65 years. There is no minimum age limit specified in the notification.
Q4What is the salary offered for the Guest Faculty posts?
A
The salary offered is Rs. 400 per class, with a maximum limit of 50 classes per month. This is a contractual position.
Q5Are there any application fees for this recruitment?
A
No, there are no application fees for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this Guest Faculty recruitment drive.
Q6What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?
A
You should carry all original documents, one set of self-attested photocopies, ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates (if any), and category certificates (if applicable).
Q7Which subjects are recruiting Guest Faculty?
A
The recruitment is for Guest Faculty in English, Odia, History, Economics, Political Science, and Sociology.
Q8How many vacancies are available for Guest Faculty posts?
A
There is one vacancy for each of the six subjects, totaling 6 vacancies for Guest Faculty posts.
Q9What is the selection process for these Guest Faculty positions?
A
The selection process involves a Walk-in Interview, followed by Certificate Verification and Document Verification.
Q10Where is the walk-in interview venue?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at Government Nodal Higher Secondary School, Dudhari, Odisha, 764036.
