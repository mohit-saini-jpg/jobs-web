SSC CGL Exam Syllabus / Pattern 2026 released : The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the new exam pattern and detailed syllabus for the SSC CGL Exam 2026. This recruitment is conducted for Group B and Group C posts in various ministries and departments of the central government. Candidates preparing for the exam can now check the complete syllabus, marking scheme, and subject-wise information for the Tier-1 and Tier-2 exams.
