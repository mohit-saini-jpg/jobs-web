Presidency University Kolkata Professor Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for Presidency University Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for the post of Professor at Presidency University, Kolkata is 01 July 2026 by 5:30 PM. Applications received after this deadline will not be considered under any circumstances.
Q2How many vacancies are available in Presidency University Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 44 Professor vacancies have been notified across 15 departments and institutes, including Physics, Chemistry, Geology, Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, Life Sciences, Bengali, English, Hindi, Political Science, History, Philosophy, Sociology, and the Institute of Health Sciences.
Q3What is the eligibility for Professor posts at Presidency University Kolkata?
A
Candidates must hold a Ph.D. in the concerned/allied discipline and satisfy one of two routes: (a) an eminent scholar with at least 10 research publications in peer-reviewed/UGC-listed journals, a research score of 120, and 10 years of teaching/research experience with evidence of doctoral guidance; OR (b) an outstanding professional from industry/academia with 10 years of experience and significant documented contribution to the field.
Q4Is there any application fee for Presidency University Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
The notification does not mention any application fee for this recruitment. Candidates should verify on the application portal at the time of filling the form.
Q5What is the salary for the Professor post at Presidency University, Kolkata?
A
The pay scale for the Professor post is Rs. 1,44,200/– to Rs. 2,18,200/- under Academic Level 14, along with other admissible allowances as per West Bengal State Government orders.
Q6What is the selection process for Presidency University Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection is based on shortlisting of applications by the respective screening committee, followed by a personal interview before the Selection Committee. There is no written examination. All shortlisted candidates must physically appear before the Selection Committee on the day of interview without exception.
Q7Do candidates who applied earlier need to re-apply?
A
Yes. The notification explicitly states that all applicants who applied against earlier advertisements must re-apply under Advertisement No. PU_RECR_AP_29-26. Previous applications will not be carried forward or considered.
Q8What is the validity requirement for OBC certificates in this recruitment?
A
OBC certificates must be issued or revalidated not before 18 May 2026. Only certificates issued by the competent authority of the Government of West Bengal will be accepted for reservation benefit.
Q9Can candidates in government service apply directly for Presidency University Professor posts?
A
Candidates already in employment must apply through the proper channel and must produce a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their current employer in order to appear before the Selection Committee.
Q10What is the probation period for selected candidates at Presidency University?
A
Appointments will be made on probation for a period of one year, which may be waived or extended in exceptional cases as per university rules. The services of a probationary incumbent may be terminated during or at the end of probation without any reasons being assigned.
