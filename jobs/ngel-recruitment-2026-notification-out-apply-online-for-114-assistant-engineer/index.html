NGEL Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

NTPC Green Energy Limited (NGEL), a subsidiary of NTPC Limited working in the renewable power and hydrogen generation business, has released Advertisement No. 02/26 inviting applications from experienced professionals for its Renewable Energy vertical. NGEL Recruitment 2026: Eligibility

Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply. All qualifications must be from Universities/Institutions recognized and approved in India. Age, experience and qualification will be computed with reference to the last date of receipt of online application. Category (SC/ST/OBC/PwBD) selected in the online application cannot be changed later, and no benefit of another category will be admissible afterward.

NGEL Educational Qualification & Experience

Qualification: Fulltime Regular Diploma in Engineering (Electrical/Mechanical/Electronics/Electrical & Electronics/Instrumentation) from a State Technical Board or AICTE/UGC recognized institute, with pass marks. Experience: Minimum 10 years of post-qualification site experience in the Power Sector (including training period, if any), in a Govt. Organization/Public Sector Undertaking/Private Organization - of which at least 6 years must be in a 50 MW or more grid-connected Solar and/or Wind Project, in Operation and Maintenance/Asset Management. Qualification: Fulltime Graduate in Engineering (Electrical/Electrical & Electronics/Electronics/Control & Instrumentation/Civil/Mechanical) with a minimum of 60% marks from a recognized Indian University/Institute. SC, ST & PwBD candidates with passing marks are eligible to apply. Experience: Minimum 12 years of post-qualification executive-cadr
