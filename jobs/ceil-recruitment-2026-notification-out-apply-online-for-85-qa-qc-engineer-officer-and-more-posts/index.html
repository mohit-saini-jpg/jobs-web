How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply online only through CEIL's official recruitment portal. Applications received through any other mode shall not be entertained. Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date. Applications found incomplete or received after the due date are liable to be rejected.

  • Documents Required at the time of Interview:

  • Proof of Age (10th / Matriculation certificate)

  • Educational and Professional Qualifications (all marksheets & certificates)

  • Service Certificate of past employment, proof of date of joining & continuity in present organization (if applicable) & latest salary slip

  • Medical Certificate for PwD (if applicable) with photograph from competent authority, confirming minimum 40% disability

  • SC / ST / OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) certificate from competent authority (OBC certificate must clearly state non-creamy layer status)

  • EWS certificate from competent authority

  • 5 copies of duly filled Annexure / CV (format sent to shortlisted candidates only)

  • Apply Online at: https://www6.eil.co.in/ceilrecruitment/

  • For further updates: Visit www.ceil.co.in → Careers section

Important Instructions

  • This is a Fixed Term Contract engagement - purely on a temporary basis. It does not carry any liability on CEIL for regular appointment at any stage. Total contract period will not exceed 36 months under any circumstances. Initial appointment is for one year, extendable (if required) for another 36 months or till completion of the project, whichever is earlier, subject to satisfactory performance. Selected candidates will initially be posted at CEIL Head Office, Navi Mumbai but may be posted at any CEIL office or project site anywhere in India at management's discretion. Only candidates willing to serve anywhere in India need apply. No perks or allowances other than the consolidated salary are payable. In case of extension beyond one year, increment @ 3% per year will be considered for the next 2 years. Fixed Term Personnel must take Medical Insurance of minimum ₹2,00,000/- for self; the premium will be reimbursed by CEIL. CGPA/OGPA/CPI or letter grade in Degree must be converted to equivalent percentage as per university/institute norms and documentary proof will be required at interview. No TA/DA will be given for attending interviews. Candidates must be of sound health; Medical Fitness Certificate from Government Hospital / Company Nominated Hospital is required at the time of joining (expense reimbursable on original receipt). Contractual engagement may be terminated by CEIL at any time at its sole discretion without assigning any reason if performance/conduct is found unsatisfactory. For termination during currency of contract, one month's written notice or one month's consolidated pay in lieu thereof shall be given by either party. Reservation and relaxation for PwDs shall be as per GOI guidelines. Persons with 40% or more disabilities are eligible. Experience prescribed is relaxable at the discretion of CEIL for SC/ST candidates. Canvassing in any form will result in disqualification. Number of vacancies indicated are only tentative; CEIL reserves the right to cancel/restrict/modify/alter selection/recruitment process at any stage without notice. CEIL also reserves the right to reject any candidature without assigning any reason whatsoever. Any dispute arising from this recruitment will be under the jurisdiction of Mumbai court only.
