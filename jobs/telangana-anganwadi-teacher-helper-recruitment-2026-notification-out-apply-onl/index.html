Telangana Anganwadi Teacher & Helper Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1How many Anganwadi vacancies are there in Mahabubnagar district in 2026?
A
There are 177 total vacancies in Mahabubnagar district, comprising 64 Anganwadi Teacher (AWT) and 113 Anganwadi Helper (AWH) posts.
Q2What is the last date to apply for Mahabubnagar Anganwadi recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to apply online is 30 June 2026, and the application window opened on 20 June 2026.
Q3How many Anganwadi Teacher (AWT) posts are vacant in Mahabubnagar?
A
A total of 64 AWT posts are vacant, spread across Devarkadra (7), Jadcherla (24), Mahabubnagar Rural (18), and Mahabubnagar Urban (15).
Q4How many Anganwadi Helper (AWH) posts are vacant in Mahabubnagar?
A
A total of 113 AWH posts are vacant, with Jadcherla alone accounting for 49 of these, the highest among all four projects.
Q5What is the eligibility for Anganwadi Teacher and Helper posts in Telangana?
A
Candidates must be women residents of the same village/ward/thanda as the vacant AWC, generally aged 18–40 years, with a pass in 8th, 10th, or Intermediate depending on the post.
Q6Is there an application fee for Mahabubnagar Anganwadi recruitment?
A
AWT/AWH posts are honorarium-based community appointments, and these recruitment drives are generally conducted without any application fee; candidates can confirm with the local CDPO office for certainty.
Q7What is the selection process for AWT/AWH posts in Mahabubnagar?
A
Selection is merit-based with no written exam - it involves application screening, document verification by the CDPO, merit list preparation, and final approval by the District Selection Committee.
Q8What is the salary of Anganwadi Teacher and Helper in Telangana?
A
An Anganwadi Teacher earns approximately ₹12,500–₹13,500 per month, while an Anganwadi Helper earns approximately ₹8,000 per month.
Q9Which ICDS project in Mahabubnagar has the most Anganwadi vacancies?
A
Jadcherla has the highest number of vacancies with 73 posts (24 AWT + 49 AWH), followed by Mahabubnagar Urban (45), Mahabubnagar Rural (43), and Devarkadra (16).
Q10How can I apply for Mahabubnagar Anganwadi recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online at mis.tgwdcw.in by locating their AWC ID under Mahabubnagar district, filling the application form, uploading documents, and submitting before 30 June 2026.
