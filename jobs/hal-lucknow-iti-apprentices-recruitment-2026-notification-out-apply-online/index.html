HAL Lucknow ITI Apprentices Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Candidates must obtain a Police Verification Certificate from the Commissioner/Superintendent of Police from the place where they reside and submit it at the time of joining apprentice training after successful document verification. Decision of HAL management regarding the selection will be final.

HAL Lucknow ITI Apprentices Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is HAL Lucknow ITI Apprentices Selection 2026-27?
A
HAL Lucknow ITI Apprentices Selection 2026-27 is a recruitment drive by Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Accessories Division, Lucknow – Technical Training Centre, for selecting ITI-passed candidates for one year of Apprentice Training across various trades under the Apprentices Act 1961, vide Notification No. HAL-ADL/2652/26/02/TRG dated 13.06.2026.
Q2Which trades are eligible for HAL Lucknow ITI Apprentices Selection 2026-27?
A
The eligible trades are: Fitter, Turner, Machinist, Machinist Grinder, Welder, Electronics Mechanic, Electrician, Instrument Mechanic, Electroplater, Refrigeration/AC, Draughtsman Mechanical, Sheet Metal Worker, and COPA/PASAA. ITI must have been passed through NCVT/SCVT from Government or affiliated institutions.
Q3What is the last date to apply online for HAL Lucknow ITI Apprentices 2026-27?
A
The last date to submit the online application for HAL Lucknow ITI Apprentices Selection 2026-27 is 30 June 2026. The application window opens on 15 June 2026 at 09:00 AM. Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date.
Q4Which ITI pass years are eligible for HAL Lucknow Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27?
A
Only candidates who passed ITI in the years 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, or 2025 are eligible. Candidates with "Appearing" or "Result Awaited" status are NOT eligible. Candidates with pending Back Papers or awaited Supplementary Exam results are also NOT eligible.
Q5What is the age limit for HAL Lucknow ITI Apprentices Selection 2026-27?
A
Candidates should not be above 27 years of age as on 30/06/2026. Age relaxation of 5 years is given to SC/ST candidates, 3 years to OBC candidates, and 10 years to Disabled (PwD) candidates as per the Apprentices Act.
Q6What is the selection process for HAL Lucknow ITI Apprentices 2026-27?
A
Selection is purely merit-based. The merit is calculated by adding 70% weightage to the High School percentage and 30% weightage to the ITI percentage. No written test or interview is conducted. Provisionally selected candidates are called for Document Verification at HAL Technical Training Centre, Lucknow.
Q7Where can I apply online for HAL Lucknow ITI Apprentices Selection 2026-27?
A
Candidates can apply only through the official HAL recruitment portal at http://59.89.119.210/trg. No other mode of application is accepted. Candidates should search for this URL on their browser to access the application link.
Q8Are candidates who passed ITI under Centre of Excellence (COE) eligible for HAL Lucknow Apprentice Selection 2026-27?
A
No. Candidates who passed ITI under the Centre of Excellence (COE) scheme are explicitly NOT eligible for the HAL Lucknow ITI Apprentices Selection 2026-27 process.
Q9When will the provisional selection list for HAL Lucknow ITI Apprentices 2026-27 be declared?
A
The list of provisionally selected candidates will be declared on or before 10 July 2026 on the official website http://59.89.119.210/trg. Document Verification date and time will also be announced along with the result.
Q10Is there any stipend during HAL Lucknow ITI Apprenticeship Training 2026-27?
A
Yes. Stipend will be payable during the one year of apprenticeship training as per applicable rules. Candidates are advised to check with HAL (Contact: 0522-2350955 between 08:00 to 16:00 hrs) for the current stipend amount applicable as per the Apprentices Act.
