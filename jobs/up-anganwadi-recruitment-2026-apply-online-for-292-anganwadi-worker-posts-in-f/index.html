Eligibility Criteria

Only female candidates are eligible to apply for the Anganwadi Karyakatri posts. The candidate must be a resident of the same Gram Sabha/Ward (in urban areas) for which the vacancy is notified.

Educational Qualification

The minimum educational qualification for the Anganwadi Karyakatri post is Intermediate (12th pass) or an equivalent qualification. Merit for selection will be prepared on the basis of the percentage of marks obtained in the maximum qualifying educational qualification held by the candidate.

UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Age Limit

Minimum age: 18 years Maximum age: 35 years Age will be reckoned as on 1 January 2026 . Candidates must not be below 18 years or above 35 years as on this date. Only the date of birth recorded in the High School marksheet/certificate will be considered valid for age calculation.

Selection Preference Order

As per the notification, selection preference for each Gram Sabha/Ward will be given in the following order: Widow / divorced / deserted women, who are original residents of the concerned Gram Sabha/Ward and belong to a family living below the poverty line (BPL), will get first priority. If no such widow/divorced/deserted BPL candidate is available in that Gram Sabha/Ward, then women from BPL families of the same Gram Sabha/Ward will be considered eligible. If no widow/divorced/deserted or BPL candidate is available, resident women of that Gram Sabha/Ward from families living above the poverty line will be considered eligible. Document requirement for priority categories: For widow candidates: A certificate issued by the Gram Panchayat Officer/concerned Nagar Nikay along with a certified copy of the family register (Parivar Panjika) will be considered valid. Honorarium / Pay Details

The Anganwadi Karyakatri post is a honorarium-based contractual engagement (Manadey Aadharit Sanvida), not a regular salaried government post. The official notification does not specify the exact monthly honorarium amount. Candidates are advised to refer to the departmental government order dated 17 September 2025 or the official website upanganwadibharti.in for honorarium details.

UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Application Fee Details

The official notification does not mention any application fee for the Anganwadi Karyakatri recruitment 2026. Candidates should verify fee details, if any, directly on the online application portal at upanganwadibharti.in at the time of filling the form.

Required Documents

High School marksheet/certificate (for date of birth proof) Intermediate (12th) or equivalent marksheet/certificate Residence certificate (online digitally signed certificate only, where online verification is possible) Caste certificate (online digitally signed certificate only, where online verification is possible) Income certificate issued by a competent authority as per UP Government Revenue Section-09 order no. 3959/1-9-11-Ra0-9 dated 30 September 2011 (where online verification is possible) Family register (Parivar Panjika) certified copy, for widow candidates Court order, for divorced/deserted candidates Self-attested, clear, and legible scanned copies of all documents mentioned in the online form

UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Any error in facts, figures, marks, percentage, or date of birth in the online application will lead to rejection of the application, and no opportunity for correction will be provided. The applicant will be solely responsible for such errors. Only residence and caste certificates issued online with digital signatures, where online verification is possible, will be considered valid. Only income certificates issued by a competent authority as per the specified UP Government order will be considered valid, where online verification is possible. Selection will be finalized as per the decision of the District Magistrate/Selection Committee and applicable government orders. The number of vacancies is subject to increase or decrease based on any court case, decision, or order, or as decided by the Selection Committee.
