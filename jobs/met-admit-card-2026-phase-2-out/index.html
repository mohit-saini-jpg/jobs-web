MET Admit Card 2026 Phase 2 Out

Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) has released the MET Admit Card 2026 Phase 2 for candidates who have registered and completed the slot booking process for the second phase of the Manipal Entrance Test. MET 2026 Phase 2 is being conducted for admission to B.Tech and other MET-based UG and PG programmes offered at Manipal campuses for the 2026-27 academic session.

As per the MET 2026 schedule, the Phase 2 exam will be held on 23 May and 24 May 2026 in computer based test mode, with two slots per day. The MET hall ticket 2026 Phase 2 has been made available through the Online Test Booking System (OTBS) at the applicant login page hosted on cdn.digialm.com/EForms/configuredHtml/32833/98207/login.html, and candidates can download it by logging in with their application number and OTBS password.

How to Download MET Admit Card 2026 Phase 2

Follow the official steps to download your MET 2026 Phase 2 hall ticket:

  • Go to the MET OTBS applicant login page at Click Here
  • On the login screen, enter your MAHE application number and OTBS password in the respective fields.
  • Enter the captcha/security code displayed on the screen and click on the Login button.
  • After successful login, go to the Admit Card / Hall Ticket section for MET 2026 Phase 2.
  • Click on Download Admit Card / Hall Ticket; your MET Admit Card 2026 Phase 2 will be displayed with your exam date, slot, reporting time and test centre details.
  • Verify all the details carefully and then download and print 2-3 clear A4-size copies of the admit card to carry on the exam day.

MAHE also sends an email/SMS to registered candidates with the admit card download link, but candidates must ensure they log in and download their hall ticket within the specified window.

Details Mentioned on MET Admit Card 2026 Phase 2

The MET Admit Card 2026 Phase 2 carries important exam and candidate information that must be verified carefully before appearing for the test. Candidates should check the following details on their hall ticket:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph and application number / registration ID
  • Programme or course applied for (for example, B.Tech and other MET-based programmes)
  • Exam name: MET 2026 Phase 2 (Manipal Entrance Test)
  • Exam date and slot details (for example, 23 or 24 May 2026, Slot 1 or Slot 2, with reporting time and exam time)
  • Test centre name, centre code, city and complete address
  • Instructions regarding reporting time, items permitted inside the exam lab and general computer based test (CBT) guidelines
  • Any special remark / provision (such as PwD category, scribe facility or other specific instructions, if applicable)

If any mismatch or error is noticed in your name, photograph, application number, course, exam date or slot, or test centre details, you should contact the MET / MAHE admissions office or exam helpdesk immediately before the exam for clarification and necessary corrections.

Documents Required with MET Admit Card 2026 Phase 2

On the day of the MET 2026 Phase 2 exam, candidates should carry:

  • A printed copy of the MET Admit Card 2026 Phase 2 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white)
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence or any other government‑issued photo ID accepted as per MET / MAHE instructions
  • Any additional documents mentioned in the admit card or information bulletin (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter if you are availing a scribe facility)

Entry to the exam centre will not be allowed without the printed MET admit card and a valid photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for MET 2026 Phase 2

To ensure smooth conduct of the MET 2026 Phase 2 computer based examination, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time printed on the MET Phase 2 admit card so that verification, frisking and seating can be completed; late entry after the scheduled reporting or gate‑closing time may not be allowed
  • Carry only essential items: MET 2026 Phase 2 admit card (A4 printout), valid photo ID and basic permitted items as specified in the MET exam instructions (such as pens or pencils if required; follow MAHE guidelines for CBT strictly)
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless specifically permitted), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the examination hall; violation of these rules may lead to cancellation of your paper and further disciplinary action
  • Follow all instructions printed on the MET admit card and those announced by the invigilators during the exam; indulging in unfair means, talking to other candidates or disobeying exam rules can result in cancellation of your examination and possible debarment from future MET attempts or admissions
  • Keep your MET Admit Card 2026 Phase 2 safely even after the exam, as it may be required later for result reference, counselling / seat allotment and document verification during admission at Manipal Academy of Higher Education
