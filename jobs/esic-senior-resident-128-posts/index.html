Short Information

Employees State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) Recruitment 2026 for 128 posts of Senior Resident. Candidates with DNB, Master of Dental Surgery, MS/MD, M.Ch, DM Can attend the walkin. The Walk-in starts from 28-05-2026, and ends on 30-05-2026. For detailed information please visit ESIC official website, esic.gov.in.

Important Dates

Last Date To Apply30-05-2026

Age Limit

Age DetailsContractual - Initially 1 year, extendable up to a total of 3 years subject to satisfactory performance

Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationDNB, Master of Dental Surgery, MS/MD, M.Ch, DM

Selection Process

  • Walk-in-Interview (with document verification)

How To Apply

  • This is a Walk-in-Interview recruitment. There is no online application process. Candidates must appear in person at the Walk-in-Interview on the scheduled date for their respective department/specialty. Candidates must report between 09:30 AM and 10:30 AM at the Academic Block, 2nd Floor, ESI-PGIMSR & ESIC Medical College, Joka, Kolkata – 700104 for document verification. Candidates reporting beyond 10:30 AM will NOT be considered. At the time of the interview, candidates must submit the completed Application Form (Annexure-I) and Verification Slip (Annexure-II) along with all original documents and one set of self-attested copies.
