How to Download Punjabi University Date Sheet 2026

Students can easily download the Punjabi University Date Sheet 2026 from the official examination portal. Follow these simple steps to get your date sheet.

  • Go to the Date Sheet or Notification section.
  • Locate the link for B.Sc Medical & Non-Medical Sem-2, 4, 6 Re-Revised Date Sheet.
