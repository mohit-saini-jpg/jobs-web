FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the DSWO Etawa Subject Expert posts?
A
The last date to submit the offline application for the DSWO Etawa Subject Expert (Guest Faculty) posts is 20th June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well in advance to avoid any last-minute issues.
Q2How many vacancies are available for the Subject Expert (Guest Faculty) position?
A
There are a total of 21 vacancies for the Subject Expert (Guest Faculty) position announced by the District Social Welfare Officer, Etawa.
Q3What is the application mode for this recruitment?
A
The application mode for the DSWO Etawa Subject Expert (Guest Faculty) recruitment is offline. Candidates need to download the application form and submit it via post or as specified in the notification.
Q4What is the salary offered for the Subject Expert (Guest Faculty) role?
A
The selected candidates for the Subject Expert (Guest Faculty) position will receive a salary of Rs. 2000 per lecture. Each lecture is approximately 90 minutes long.
Q5What are the eligibility criteria for the Subject Expert posts?
A
Eligibility criteria vary by subject. Generally, candidates need to be a University/College teacher, UPSC main exam qualified, PCS interview qualified, or have 3 years of teaching experience in a reputed coaching institute. For specific science subjects like Zoology, Botany, Physics, and Chemistry, an M.Sc First Class in the relevant subject with 3 years of teaching experience is required.
Q6Is there any application fee for the DSWO Etawa recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive. Candidates can apply without paying any application fee.
Q7What is the selection process for the Subject Expert (Guest Faculty) posts?
A
The selection process primarily involves Application Scrutiny and Document Verification. Candidates will be shortlisted based on their applications and then called for document verification.
Q8Where can I download the application form?
A
The application form can be downloaded from the official website of the District Social Welfare Officer, Etawa, which is etawah.nic.in.
Q9What documents are required to be attached with the application form?
A
Candidates need to attach self-attested copies of required documents such as a photograph, signature, ID proof, and relevant educational and experience certificates with the application form.
Q10What is the official website for DSWO Etawa?

The official website for the District Social Welfare Officer, Etawa, is etawah.nic.in. Candidates can visit this website for more information and to download the application form.
