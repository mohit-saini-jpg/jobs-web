Short Information

Assam Public Service Commission (APSC) has released the official Class-I (Junior Grade) Home Guards Notification 2026 on 05 May 2026 at the official website https://apsc.nic.in. Eligible candidates aged between 21 and 38 years with a Graduate degree in any discipline from a Government-recognized University can apply online from 08 May 2026 to 07 June 2026. Selection Process

  • Stage Details
  • 1. Written Examination Paper-I: General Ability & Intelligence Test – 200 marks, Objective (MCQ), questions in English; covers General Mental Ability, General Science, Current Events, Indian Polity & Economy, History of India, Indian & World Geography.
  • 2. Physical Standards / Physical Efficiency Test (PET) Candidates qualified in written exam will undergo Physical Standards Test and PET as per prescribed norms.
  • 3. Medical Standards Test Medical Standards Test as per Assam Home Guards Service Rules, 2020 for candidates qualified in PET; appeals allowed within 15 days to Appellate Authority.
  • 4. Interview / Personality Test Candidates declared fit in Medical Test will be called for Interview/Personality Test of 50 marks; provisionally for those awaiting Review Medical Board outcome.
  • 5. Final Merit List Prepared based on marks obtained in Written Examination (Paper-I & II) and Interview/Personality Test.

How To Apply

  • Applicants must apply online only through the APSC recruitment website: https://apscrecruitment.in.
  • New users have to complete One Time Registration (OTR) first by clicking on “Register Here” and providing basic details.
  • Use a valid and active email ID and mobile number for registration and future communication.
  • Log in with registered email/mobile number and password, then fill in all mandatory details in the online application form.
  • Upload required documents: proof of Date of Birth, educational qualification certificates and mark sheets, caste/category certificates, BPL/PwBD certificates (if applicable) in PDF format (max 4 MB, scanned at 200 dpi, legible).
  • Upload recent passport-size Photograph and Signature (50 KB–200 KB each, photo not older than 3 months).
  • Carefully review all details and documents using the Preview option and tick the declaration checkbox before final submission.
  • Pay the applicable application fee and processing charges online; applications without successful fee payment will be treated as incomplete.
  • In case of payment failure or pending status, use “Check Status” to verify with bank/GRAS; double debit, if any, will be auto-refunded by the bank within 5–7 working days.
  • After final submission, take a printout of the submitted Online Application Form and retain it for future reference.
  • Candidates can also submit applications through Common Service Centres (CSCs) if they do not have their own online payment facility.
  • Class-I Home Guards Application Form
  • Eligible candidates who satisfy the eligibility conditions can fill and submit the online application form for Class-I (Junior Grade) Home Guards posts through the APSC Online Recruitment Portal within the specified dates.
  • The application form must be filled carefully with correct personal, educational, and category details, and all supporting documents must be uploaded as per instructions before online submission.
  • Application Mode: Online only through APSC Recruitment website – https://apscrecruitment.in

Important Instructions

  • Candidates must ensure that they fulfil all the eligibility conditions regarding age, qualification, physical standards and other criteria before applying for the post.
  • Admission at all stages of selection will be purely provisional, subject to verification of eligibility conditions by the Commission.
  • No request for withdrawal of candidature will be entertained after submission of the online application. Category/sub-category claimed in the original application cannot be changed at any stage of the selection process. Candidates furnishing wrong or false information may be disqualified from APSC recruitments for a period as decided by the Commission. Submission of false documents or suppression of material facts may lead to rejection and legal action even after recommendation for appointment. Candidates must produce valid photo ID (PAN Card, Driving Licence, Passport, Institution ID, Voter ID, Aadhaar, etc.) at the time of written test and interview. Date of birth once declared in the online form will be treated as final; only Class X/XII Board documents will be accepted as proof of age. Original documents and self-attested copies (DOB, educational certificates, caste/BPL certificates, PRC, Small Family Norms declaration Form-A, etc.) must be produced at the time of interview. Canvassing in any form will disqualify the candidate. Candidates should visit the APSC website regularly for updates; no interim enquiries about results will be entertained.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is APSC Class-I (Junior Grade) Home Guards Recruitment 2026?

APSC Class‑I Home Guards Recruitment 2026 is a state government recruitment drive by Assam Public Service Commission to fill 22 Class‑I (Junior Grade) posts in Assam Home Guards Services under the Home and Political Department.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in APSC Home Guards Recruitment 2026?

A total of 22 vacancies have been announced, including posts for Open Category, OBC/MOBC, SC, ST(P), ST(H) and 1 post reserved for Ex‑Servicemen, with 7 seats reserved for women across categories.

Q3. What are the important dates for APSC Class-I Home Guards 2026?

The notification was released on 05 May 2026, the online application starts on 08 May 2026, and the last date to apply online is 07 June 2026, while the last date for fee payment is 09 June 2026.

Q4. What is the eligibility criteria for APSC Class-I (Junior Grade) Home Guards Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must be Indian citizens and permanent residents of Assam, holding a Graduation degree in any discipline from a Government‑recognized University, along with a Permanent Resident Certificate or Employment Exchange Registration Certificate of Assam.

Q5. What is the age limit for APSC Class-I Home Guards Recruitment 2026?

The age limit is 21 to 38 years as on 01 January 2026 for General candidates, relaxable up to 41 years for OBC/MOBC, 43 years for SC/ST, and up to 50 years for Ex‑Servicemen, with further relaxation for reserved Ex‑Servicemen as per rules.

Q6. What is the application fee for APSC Class-I Home Guards 2026?

The total fee including processing charges is Rs. 297.20 for General candidates, Rs. 197.20 for OBC/MOBC, and Rs. 47.20 for SC/ST/BPL/PwBD candidates, where the latter group pays only the processing fee and no examination fee.

Q7. What is the pay scale for APSC Class-I (Junior Grade) Home Guards?

Selected candidates will receive a Pay Band‑3 salary of Rs. 22,000 – 97,000 per month, with a Grade Pay of Rs. 11,800 plus other allowances admissible to Assam State Government employees from time to time.

Q8. What is the selection process for APSC Class-I Home Guards Recruitment 2026?

The selection process includes a Written Examination, followed by Physical Standards and Physical Efficiency Tests (PET), a Medical Standards Test as per Assam Home Guards Service Rules 2020, and finally an Interview/Personality Test of 50 marks, with the final merit list based on written and interview marks.
