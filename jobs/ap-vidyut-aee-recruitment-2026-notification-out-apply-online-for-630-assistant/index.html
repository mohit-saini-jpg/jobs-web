AP Power Sector AEE Vacancy 2026 – Utility-wise & Post-wise Breakdown

The AP Power Sector AEE Bharti 2026 covers 630 total vacancies across all five utilities. APTRANSCO leads with 200 posts, followed by APEPDCL and APSPDCL with 135 each, APGENCO with 100, and APCPDCL with 60. Candidates apply once and exercise preferences for the utility and zone at the time of application. All vacancies are provisional and may increase or decrease based on administrative requirements. APGENCO conducts a separate examination for Mechanical and Electronics branches; all other disciplines are covered under the Common Recruitment Examination. Note on Common vs. Separate Exam: For Electrical and Civil branches, a single Common Recruitment Examination is conducted jointly for all five utilities. For APGENCO's Mechanical and Electronics branches, a separate exam is held exclusively by APGENCO. Candidates for Mechanical/Electronics need only apply to APGENCO.

Selection Process – AP Power Sector AEE 2026 Exam Pattern & CBT Details

The AP Power Sector AEE 2026 selection is entirely merit-based through a Computer Based Test (CBT) of 100 marks and 3 hours duration. There is no interview stage . After the CBT, candidates shortlisted in a 1:1 ratio against vacancies are called for Certificate Verification, following which the final selection list is prepared and allotments are made. Merely securing the minimum qualifying marks does NOT guarantee a call for certificate verification. The merit list is prepared in a strict 1:1 ratio against notified vacancies. Tie-breaking is done first by age (older candidate ranked higher), then by date of acquiring the qualifying degree. Salary & Pay Scale – AP Power Sector AEE 2026 Remuneration

Candidates selected under the AP Power Sector AEE Recruitment 2026 will be placed on probation for 2 years on duty within a continuous period of 3 years . During this probation, all selected candidates across all five utilities receive a fixed consolidated monthly remuneration of ₹75,000 . After successful completion of probation, the regular RPS-2022 pay scale becomes applicable. Selected candidates in Electrical posts (across all utilities) must mandatorily work in field postings - preferably in rural and remote areas - for a minimum of 5 years from the date of initial appointment . For APEPDCL AEE (Electrical), the first 5 years include 2 years in MRT/Construction wings and 3 years in Operation/Office posts in rural/tribal areas. Service Bond & Consent Deposit – AP Power Sector AEE 2026

Every candidate appointed under the AP Power Sector AEE Bharti 2026 must execute a Service Bond on Non-Judicial Stamp Paper worth ₹110 at the time of joining. The bond obligates the candidate to serve the respective utility for a minimum of 5 years after completion of the 2-year probation period - making the total minimum commitment 7 years . Original certificates (degree, date of birth, community) must be deposited with the utility for the entire bond period.

Reservation Policy – AP Power Sector AEE 2026

The AP Power Sector AEE 2026 reservation follows vertical reservations for SC (Group-I, II & III), ST, BC (A, B, C, D & E), and EWS candidates as per applicable Andhra Pradesh Government Orders and the Presidential Order (AP Presidential Order 2025). Horizontal reservations of 33⅓% for Women and 4% for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities are implemented within respective vertical categories. Local candidature : 95% of vacancies in each zone are reserved for local candidates from the respective zone. Open/Non-local : 5% of vacancies are open to non-local candidates. Candidates from other states are considered under the General (unreserved/OC) category only. PwBD candidates must hold a valid SADAREM certificate issued by a competent authority. Carried-forward vacancies (if any) are reserved for women candidates as per applicable rules.

AP Vidyut AEE Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in AP Power Sector AEE Recruitment 2026?
A
The AP Power Sector AEE Recruitment 2026 covers a combined total of 630 vacancies across all five utilities: APTRANSCO (200), APEPDCL (135), APSPDCL (135), APGENCO (100), and APCPDCL (60).
Q2What is the last date to apply online for AP Power Sector AEE 2026?
A
The last date to apply online is 20 July 2026 at 23:59 hrs. This is also the deadline for fee payment and uploading all required certificates. No extensions have been indicated.
Q3What is the application fee for AP Power Sector AEE 2026?
A
The application fee is ₹1,000 for all candidates, regardless of category. There is no fee waiver for SC/ST/BC/EWS or PwBD candidates. The fee is non-refundable once paid.
Q4What is the salary during probation for AP Power Sector AEE 2026?
A
All selected candidates across all five utilities receive a consolidated monthly remuneration of ₹75,000 during the 2-year probation period. After probation, the regular RPS-2022 pay scale starting at ₹86,245 applies.
Q5Do I need to apply separately for each AP Power utility?
A
No. Candidates submit a single application through the Common Recruitment Portal covering all five utilities. At the time of application, candidates indicate their preference for utility and zone. Allotment is based on merit, reservation, and preferences.
Q6Is there negative marking in the AP Power Sector AEE CBT 2026?
A
No, there is no negative marking in the Computer Based Test for any of the disciplines. The CBT is 100 marks, 3 hours duration, in English medium only.
Q7What is the age limit for AP Power Sector AEE Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age for General/OC candidates is 42 years as on 01/07/2026. SC/ST/BC/EWS candidates get a 5-year relaxation (maximum 47 years), and PwBD candidates get a 10-year relaxation (maximum 52 years).
Q8What is the official website to apply for AP Power Sector AEE 2026?
A
The official Common Recruitment Portal is apvidyutrecruitment.apcfss.in. Candidates can also apply through each utility's official website: aptransco.gov.in, apeasternpower.com, apcpdcl.in, apspdcl.in, or apgenco.gov.in.
Q9When is the AP Power Sector AEE CBT 2026 exam date?
A
The Computer Based Test (CBT) is tentatively scheduled from 22 August 2026 to 29 August 2026. Hall Tickets will be available for download 7 days before the commencement of the examination.
Q10Are Mechanical and Electronics engineers eligible for the AP Power Sector AEE 2026?
A
Yes, but only for APGENCO. APGENCO offers AEE posts in Mechanical (21 vacancies) and Electronics (7 vacancies). These are covered under a separate examination conducted exclusively by APGENCO — not the Common Recruitment Examination used for Electrical, Civil, and Telecom branches.
