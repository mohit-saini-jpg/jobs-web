How to Download Karnataka 2nd PUC Exam 3 Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the Karnataka 2nd PUC Exam 3 Time Table 2026 by following the steps below:

  • Visit the official website of KSEAB Karnataka.
  • Go to the Examination or Time Table section on the homepage.
  • Locate the link for 2nd PUC Exam 3 Time Table 2026.
  • Click on the PDF link for your stream.
  • The timetable will open. Download and save the file.
  • Take a printout and keep it safe for exam preparation.

Note: Always refer to the official timetable and check all dates and timings carefully before the examinations.
