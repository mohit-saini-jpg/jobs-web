OFSS Bihar 11th 3rd Merit List 2026 Released - Download Intimation Letter Now

The OFSS Bihar 3rd merit list 2026 for Intermediate (Class 11) admission has been officially released on 11 June 2026. Students who applied for 11th class admission under the Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) for the session 2026-28 can now visit the official OFSS portal at ofssbihar.net to download their intimation letter and check their college allotment. Admission at the allotted institutions is open from 11 June to 14 June 2026.

How Is the OFSS Merit List Prepared?

Understanding the selection process can help students interpret their allotment result better. The OFSS software generates merit lists based on the following factors:

  • Total seats available stream-wise in each school or college
  • The order of institution and faculty preferences filled in the CAF
  • Percentage of marks obtained in the Class 10 (Matriculation) examination
  • Reservation provisions as per Bihar Government guidelines (SC, ST, BC, EBC, EWS, and women candidates)
  • Students who have studied Class 10 from a particular school are given priority for admission to that same school, provided they listed it as an option, as per state government policy

How to Download OFSS Bihar 11th 3rd Merit List and Intimation Letter 2026

Selected students can download their intimation letter which confirms their college allotment directly from the OFSS portal. Follow these steps:

  • Open your browser and go to the official website: https://ofssbihar.net
  • On the homepage, click on the "Download Intimation Letter 2026" link
  • You will be taken to the intimation letter download page for the 11th admission session 2026-27
  • Enter your Application Reference Number / Barcode Number and registered mobile number in the fields provided
  • Type the Captcha Code displayed on the screen accurately
  • Click the Print button to download and save your intimation letter

Students are also notified about their selection through SMS, email, and the notice board of their allotted school or college.

What Is the Slide-Up Process in OFSS Bihar?

The slide-up process allows students who are not satisfied with their current college allotment to hold on and wait for a potentially better allotment in the next phase. If a higher-preference college has a vacancy in subsequent rounds, the system automatically upgrades the student's allotment. This option is available during each merit list phase including the current 3rd round, from 11 to 14 June 2026.

Documents Required for Admission Through OFSS Bihar 2026

Students who have been selected in the 3rd merit list must carry the following documents when reporting to their allotted school or college for admission:

  • Provisional or original certificate of Class 10 or equivalent examination
  • Class 10 mark sheet (original)
  • School Leaving Certificate (SLC) from the Class 10 institution
  • Character certificate from the Class 10 institution
  • Migration certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size colour photograph
  • Prescribed institutional fee

Application Fee for OFSS Bihar Intermediate Admission 2026

The application fee for OFSS Bihar Intermediate admission is ₹350 for all categories, including General, OBC, SC, ST, PH, and female candidates. Payment can be made via debit card, credit card, or net banking.

How to Apply for OFSS Bihar 11th Admission (For Future Reference)

Although the application window for the 2026 session has closed (last date was 25 April 2026), here is how the process works for reference and for students who may apply in future sessions:

  • Visit ofssbihar.net and click on the Common Application Form (CAF) link
  • Register using your mobile number and email address
  • Fill in personal details, Class 10 marks, and stream preferences (minimum 1, maximum 20 institutions)
  • Upload a passport-size photograph and other required details
  • Pay the application fee of ₹350 online
  • Submit the form and download the Acknowledgment Copy for future reference

Free help for filling the form is available at District Registration cum Counseling Centers (DRCCs) and Sahaj Vasudha Kendras across Bihar.
