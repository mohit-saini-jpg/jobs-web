Job Overview

Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme (ECHS) has published the Dental Officer, Dental A/T/H and Driver Recruitment 2026 Notification. The recruitment drive aims to fill 3 contractual positions for eligible candidates. Applicants within the prescribed age limits of up to 65 years for Dental Officer, 58 years for Dental A/T/H, and 55 years for Driver, and possessing qualifications such as BDS with relevant experience, Diploma in Dental Hygiene/Class-I DH/DORA Course, or 8th Pass with Class-I MT Driver qualification and required experience, are eligible to apply. FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for ECHS Likabali Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the offline application form for the ECHS Likabali Recruitment 2026 is 17th June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well in advance of the deadline to avoid any last-minute issues.
Q2How many vacancies are available in this ECHS Likabali Recruitment?
A
There are a total of 3 vacancies announced for the ECHS Likabali Recruitment 2026. These vacancies are for the posts of Dental Officer, Dental A/T/H, and Driver.
Q3What is the application mode for this recruitment?
A
The application mode for the ECHS Likabali Recruitment 2026 is offline. Candidates need to download the application form, fill it, and send it to the specified address.
Q4What is the salary offered for the Dental Officer post?
A
The salary offered for the Dental Officer post in the ECHS Likabali Recruitment 2026 is Rs. 95,000/- per month.
Q5What are the minimum educational qualifications required for the Driver post?
A
For the Driver post, the minimum educational qualification is 8th Class. Additionally, candidates must possess a Class I MT Driver (Armed Forces) certification and a civil driving licence for heavy and light vehicles, along with at least 5 years of driving experience.
Q6Is there any application fee for ECHS Likabali Recruitment 2026?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) in the official notification for the ECHS Likabali Recruitment 2026.
Q7What is the maximum age limit for the Dental A/T/H post?
A
The maximum age limit for the Dental A/T/H post in the ECHS Likabali Recruitment 2026 is 58 years.
Q8What is the selection process for these posts?
A
The selection process for the ECHS Likabali Recruitment 2026 will be based on an Interview.
Q9Where can I find the official notification and application form?
A
The official notification and application form can be downloaded from the official website of ECHS, which is www.echs.gov.in. Links are also provided in the "Important Links" section of this article.
Q10What is the job type for these positions?
A
The job type for all the posts announced in the ECHS Likabali Recruitment 2026 is contractual.
