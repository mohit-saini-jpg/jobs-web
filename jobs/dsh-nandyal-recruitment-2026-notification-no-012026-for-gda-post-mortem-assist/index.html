DSH Nandyal Recruitment Salary

All three notified posts - General Duty Attendant (GDA/MNO/FNO), Post Mortem Assistant, and Office Subordinate - carry a monthly remuneration of Rs. 15,000/-, as applicable from time to time.

DSH Nandyal Recruitment Selection Process

Selection is based on a total of 100 marks, distributed as follows: 75% of marks for the aggregate marks obtained in all years of the qualifying examination or its equivalent. Up to 10 marks at the rate of 1.0 mark per completed year after acquiring the requisite qualification (as per the pass certificate), reckoned up to the date of notification. Up to 15% weightage for candidates who have worked on Contract/Outsourcing/Honorarium basis (including COVID-19 service), subject to satisfactory service certified by the competent authority, as per G.O.Ms.No.211, HM&FW (B2) Dept., Dt: 08.05.2021; G.O.Rt.No.573, HM&FW (B2) Dept., Dt: 01.11.2021; and G.O.Rt.No.07, HM&FW (B2) Dept., Dt: 06.01.2022. No weightage is given for non-COVID service rendered for less than six months. For COVID service of less than 6 months, 0.8 marks per completed month is awarded. DSH Nandyal Recruitment 2026 - Notification No. 01/2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is DSH Nandyal Recruitment 2026 Notification No. 01/2026?
A
It is an official recruitment notification issued by the Director of Secondary Health, Andhra Pradesh, for filling 19 Paramedical and Supporting Staff posts in DSH facilities of Nandyal District on a contract/outsourcing basis.
Q2How many vacancies are there in DSH Nandyal Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 19 vacancies in total: 14 for General Duty Attendant (GDA/MNO/FNO), 3 for Post Mortem Assistant, and 2 for Office Subordinate. These numbers are provisional and may change based on departmental requirements.
Q3What is the last date to apply for DSH Nandyal Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for submission of the physical application is 5:30 PM on 27.07.2026.
Q4What is the educational qualification required for these posts?
A
Candidates must have passed SSC/10th class or its equivalent from a recognized board for all three posts — GDA/MNO/FNO, Post Mortem Assistant, and Office Subordinate.
Q5What is the age limit for DSH Nandyal Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 42 years, reckoned as on 01.07.2025. Age relaxation of 5 years applies for SC/ST/BC/EWS candidates, 3 years plus service length for ex-servicemen, and 10 years for differently abled persons.
Q6What is the application fee for this recruitment?
A
The application fee is Rs. 600/- for general (OC) candidates and Rs. 400/- for SC, ST, and BC candidates. The fee is exempted for PWD candidates and must be paid via demand draft in favour of the District Coordinator of Hospital Services, Nandyal.
Q7What is the salary for GDA, Post Mortem Assistant, and Office Subordinate posts in Nandyal?
A
All three posts carry a monthly remuneration of Rs. 15,000/-, as applicable from time to time.
Q8Is DSH Nandyal Recruitment 2026 a permanent job?
A
No. This is a contract/outsourcing engagement with an initial tenure of one year from the date of joining, which may be extended as per Government instructions. The services can also be terminated with one month's notice.
Q9How is the merit list prepared for DSH Nandyal Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection is out of 100 marks: 75% based on aggregate marks in the qualifying examination, up to 10 marks for completed years of experience after qualification, and up to 15% weightage for prior Contract/Outsourcing/Honorarium service (including COVID-19 duty), subject to a satisfactory service certificate from the competent authority.
Q10How long is the DSH Nandyal Recruitment 2026 merit list valid?
A
The merit list from this notification is valid for one year and can be used to fill any vacancies that arise during this period.
