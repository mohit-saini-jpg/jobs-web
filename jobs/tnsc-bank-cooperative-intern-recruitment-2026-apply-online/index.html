Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is TNSC Bank Cooperative Intern Recruitment 2026?

TNSC Bank Cooperative Intern Recruitment 2026 is a one‑year internship programme launched by The Tamil Nadu State Apex Cooperative Bank Ltd. The aim is to strengthen the cooperative movement at the grassroots level by placing Cooperative Interns in selected District Central Cooperative Banks.

Q2. How many Cooperative Intern vacancies are available and where will interns be posted?

There are a total of 5 Cooperative Intern vacancies in this recruitment. Selected candidates will be placed in the District Central Cooperative Banks at Erode, Kancheepuram, Madurai, Nilgiris and Thanjavur.

Q3. What is the tenure and salary for TNSC Bank Cooperative Intern 2026?

The tenure of the Cooperative Internship is 1 year, which may be extended based on performance and requirement. Each intern will receive a fixed monthly remuneration of ₹25,000 during the internship period.

Q4. What is the eligibility criteria for TNSC Bank Cooperative Intern Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must possess an MBA or equivalent degree in Marketing Management, Cooperative Management, Agri Business Management or Rural Development Management. In addition, proficiency in computer applications is essential for all applicants.

Q5. What is the age limit for TNSC Bank Cooperative Intern 2026?

The minimum age for applying is 21 years and the maximum age is 30 years. The age is to be calculated as on the date of publication of the advertisement.

Q6. What is the selection process for TNSC Bank Cooperative Intern Recruitment 2026?

Selection will be based on weightage of marks obtained in academic qualifications and additional cooperative/computer qualifications. Shortlisted candidates, based on this weightage, will be called for an interview before final selection.

Q7. How is the merit/weightage calculated for TNSC Bank Cooperative Intern selection?

Weightage is given as follows: 5% for 10th Standard, 15% for Higher Secondary, 25% for Graduation and 40% for Post Graduation. Additional 5% weightage each is given to candidates having Cooperative Management qualification and Computer Education.
