How to Apply

  • 1The application process for Punjab SSSB Veterinary Inspector Recruitment 2026 is completely online through the official website of SSSB Punjab. Applications submitted through any mode other than the online portal will not be accepted under any circumstances.
  • 2Candidates must first complete a one-time registration to generate a User ID (Email ID) and Password, and then log in to fill the online application form. They must enter personal, educational and category details correctly, upload their passport-size photograph and signature, and upload scanned copies of required documents in PDF format before proceeding to fee payment.
  • 3Visit the official SSSB Punjab website: https://sssb.punjab.gov.in and click on the “Apply Online” link.
  • 4For new users, click on “Registration” and fill in the required details to generate User ID (Email ID) and Password.
  • 5After successful registration, log in using your User ID (Email ID) and Password to access the online application form.
  • 6Fill in personal, educational and category details carefully and upload a recent passport size photograph and scanned signature.
  • 7Click on “Attach Annexure” and upload scanned copies of Matric (10th) certificate, educational qualification certificates and other mandatory documents in PDF format.
  • 8Click on “Make Payment” and pay the application fee online as per your category.
  • 9After successful fee payment, click on “Submit” to complete the application process and download/print the submitted application form for future reference.

FAQs

Q1What is Punjab SSSB Veterinary Inspector Recruitment 2026?
A
Punjab SSSB Veterinary Inspector Recruitment 2026 is a state‑level recruitment drive by Subordinate Services Selection Board (SSSB/PSSSB) to fill 150 Group‑C Veterinary Inspector posts in the Animal Husbandry Department, Government of Punjab, under Advertisement No. 10 of 2026.
Q2How many vacancies are there for Punjab Veterinary Inspector 2026?
A
The official short notification has announced 150 vacancies for the post of Veterinary Inspector in the Animal Husbandry Department, Punjab. These posts are further divided category‑wise with separate reservation for women candidates as per Punjab Government rules.
Q3What are the important dates for Punjab Veterinary Inspector Recruitment 2026?
A
The public notice/short notification was issued in April 2026 and the online application window will remain open from 06 June 2026 to 19 June 2026 at the SSSB Punjab website. The detailed notification and exam date are to be published separately on the official portal.
Q4What is the eligibility and educational qualification for Punjab Veterinary Inspector 2026?
A
As per the notice and previous recruitment pattern, candidates must have 10+2 with Science (Physics, Chemistry and Biology/Maths) and a Diploma in Veterinary Science & Animal Health Technology (or equivalent) from a recognised institution to be eligible for Veterinary Inspector. The detailed educational/technical qualifications are given in the “Educational/Technical Qualifications” section of the 2026 notification and should be checked before applying.
Q5What is the age limit for Punjab SSSB Veterinary Inspector Recruitment 2026?
A
For Punjab Veterinary Inspector 2026, the age limit for general category candidates is expected to be 18 to 37 years as on 01 January 2026, in line with the standard PSSSB Veterinary Inspector eligibility norms. Upper age relaxation for SC, BC, Government employees, Ex‑Servicemen, PwD and widows/divorcees is applicable as per Punjab Government instructions and will be detailed in the full notification.
Q6What is the pay scale for Veterinary Inspector in Punjab under this recruitment?
A
Past PSSSB notifications have offered a basic pay around the Punjab Group‑C pay matrix (earlier ₹29,200 per month) for Veterinary Inspector, and the 2026 detailed advertisement will specify the exact pay scale and allowances. Candidates will also be entitled to dearness allowance, HRA and other benefits as per Punjab Government rules applicable to Group‑C posts.
Q7What is the application fee for Punjab Veterinary Inspector Recruitment 2026?
A
The short notice confirms that category‑wise online application fees will be charged, and earlier recruitments have followed a pattern of higher fee for General, lower fee for SC/BC/EWS and nominal fee for Ex‑Servicemen and PwD candidates. The exact fee structure for 2026 will be given in the “Fee Details” section of the detailed notification and must be paid online only; applications without successful fee payment will be treated as incomplete.
Q8What is the selection process for Punjab SSSB Veterinary Inspector 2026?
A
Selection will be based on an objective‑type written examination followed by document verification, and the final merit list will be prepared solely on the basis of marks obtained in the written exam, subject to reservation norms. There is no mention of an interview stage in the short notice; detailed selection procedure and disqualification conditions will be provided in the full advertisement.
