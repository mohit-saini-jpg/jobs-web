MSC Bank Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

The selection methodology varies by post but all posts involve an online written examination conducted by IBPS, followed by an additional stage specific to the post. Candidates must score at least 50% (100 marks out of 200) in the written test for officer/associate posts and 50% (60 marks out of 120) for the Driver post to be shortlisted for the next round. Online Written Examination by IBPS (160 Questions, 200 Marks, 120 Minutes) Personal Interview by MSC Bank Final merit based on combined scores of Exam + Interview Exam is in English only. Negative marking applies: 1/4 penalty for 4-option questions; 1/5 penalty for 5-option questions. Examination Centres

The online written test will be held at centres across Maharashtra. Candidates cannot request a change of centre once allotted. Personal Interview and Skill Test will be held at Mumbai. Exam Centres: Mumbai / Navi Mumbai / Thane / MMR Region, Pune, Nagpur, Nashik, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Nanded, and Kolhapur .

Service Bond / Consent Deposit

Candidates wondering about post-appointment obligations should note that selected candidates must execute an undertaking on Rs. 500/- stamp paper committing to serve MSC Bank for a minimum of 4 years from the date of joining. In case of default, voluntary resignation, or termination before the expiry of 4 years, the candidate is required to pay Rs. 10,00,000/- (Ten Lakh) as liquidated damages and execute a surety bond for Rs. 1,00,000/- from two income tax payees as sureties.

MSC Bank Recruitment 2026 – 139 Posts | Trainee Junior : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for MSC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application and pay the application fee is 01 July 2026. Applications received after this date will not be accepted.
Q2How many total vacancies are there in MSC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
MSC Bank has notified 139 vacancies in total — 49 for Trainee Junior Officers, 81 for Trainee Associates, 04 for Trainee Typists, and 05 for Trainee Drivers.
Q3Who is eligible to apply for MSC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
Only residents of Maharashtra State are eligible. All posts require passing the Matriculation Examination with Marathi as a subject. For Trainee Junior Officer and Associate posts, graduation with minimum 50% marks is required. Trainee Drivers only need SSC pass with a valid LMV licence.
Q4What is the application fee for MSC Bank 2026?
A
The application fee for Trainee Junior Officer is Rs. 1,770/- (including GST). For Trainee Associate, Trainee Typist, and Trainee Driver, the fee is Rs. 1,180/- each (including GST). Payment is online only; fees are non-refundable.
Q5What is the age limit for the Trainee Junior Officer post?
A
Candidates must be between 23 and 35 years of age as on 01 June 2026, i.e., born on or after 01 June 1991.
Q6What is the selection process for MSC Bank Trainee Junior Officer?
A
Selection involves an online written examination conducted by IBPS (200 marks, 120 minutes) followed by a Personal Interview by MSC Bank. Final merit is based on combined performance in both stages.
Q7Is there negative marking in MSC Bank exam 2026?
A
Yes, all online tests carry negative marking. For questions with four options, the penalty is 1/3 mark per wrong answer. For questions with five options, the penalty is 1/4 mark per wrong answer.
Q8What is the salary for MSC Bank Trainee Junior Officer after training?
A
After successful completion of the 12-month training period, Trainee Junior Officers are placed in the Bank's regular grade with total emoluments of approximately Rs. 52,100/- per month. During training, a stipend of Rs. 30,000/- per month is paid.
Q9Where will the MSC Bank exam be held?
A
The online written test will be held at centres in Mumbai/Navi Mumbai/Thane/MMR Region, Pune, Nagpur, Nashik, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Nanded, and Kolhapur. Personal Interviews and Skill Tests will be held at Mumbai.
Q10What is the official website for MSC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
The official website is https://msc.bank.in. The career/apply page is at https://msc.bank.in/career. For queries, candidates may email [email protected].
