Indian Navy is recruiting 60 Permanent Commissioned Officer vacancies in Technical Branches through the 10+2 B.Tech Cadet Entry Scheme for the course commencing January 2027, last date for online application 18 June 2026.

NationalityAs per conditions of nationality laid down by the Government of India.

Selection Process

  • Selection will be based on JEE (Main) 2026 CRL-based shortlisting
  • followed by SSB Interview
  • Medical Examination
  • and Police & Character Verification. Merit list will be prepared based on SSB marks and medical fitness. Candidates will be appointed subject to Police Verification
  • Character Verification
  • and availability of vacancies in the entry. Mere inclusion in the common merit list does not confer a vested right to appointment. Mere empanelment under no circumstances can be treated as final selection.
  • Stage 1 - Shortlisting Based on JEE (Main) 2026 CRL
  • Naval Headquarters reserves the right to fix the cut-off for shortlisting of applications for SSB based on JEE (Main) All India Common Rank List (CRL) 2026. All candidates must fill their CRL Rank in the application. Shortlisted candidates will be informed about their SSB interview schedule through email and SMS. Candidates must not change their email/mobile number until the selection process is over.
  • Stage 2 - Service Selection Board (SSB) Interview
  • SSB interviews for shortlisted candidates will be scheduled at Bengaluru / Bhopal / Kolkata / Visakhapatnam from September 2026 onwards. Change of SSB Centre is not permissible under any circumstances. Candidates must download the call-up letter on receiving intimation from Naval Headquarters via SMS/email. AC 3-tier rail fare is admissible for SSB interview if appearing for the first time for this particular type of commission — candidates must bring a photocopy of the first page of passbook or cheque leaf with Name
  • A/C No.
  • and IFSC details. No compensation is admissible in case of any injury sustained during SSB tests.
  • Stage 3 - Medical Examination
  • All candidates recommended by SSB must undergo medical examination as per standards applicable to 10+2 (B.Tech) entry. No relaxation in medical standards on any grounds. Candidates already declared medically fit for NDA/INA Jan 27 need not repeat medical examination. Change of medical hospital/centre is not permissible under any circumstances.
  • Stage 4 - Common Merit List & Appointment
  • Final merit list will be prepared based on SSB marks and medical fitness. Candidates will be appointed subject to Police Verification
  • and availability of vacancies. All vacancies are to be filled as per service requirement and statutory guidelines issued from time to time.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must register and submit their application online only at the official Indian Navy recruitment website www.joinindiannavy.gov.in. No other mode of application is accepted. Candidates are advised to read all instructions on the website carefully before submitting the online application. The application once submitted is final and no request for amendments or changes will be entertained.
  • Documents to be Scanned and Uploaded (in original JPG/TIFF format):
  • Date of Birth proof (as per 10th/12th Class certificate)
  • 10th Class marksheet
  • 12th Class marksheet
  • JEE (Main) 2026 Score Card indicating Common Rank List (CRL)
  • Recent passport-size colour photograph
  • Documents to be Produced / Carried at the time of SSB Interview:
  • Print-out of the submitted online application form
  • Original certificates/documents as submitted during online application
  • Photocopy of first page of bank passbook or cheque leaf (showing Name, A/C No., and IFSC details) — for rail fare reimbursement claim
  • Candidates must ensure that all details including email address and mobile number are mandatory fields and are correctly filled. Personal particulars must match exactly with Matriculation/12th Class certificates. Candidature will be cancelled if any declaration is found wrong at any stage of selection.
  • Apply Online at: https://www.joinindiannavy.gov.in/en/account/account/state
  • For complete details on SSB procedure, pay & allowances, medical standards, and other information: Visit https://www.joinindiannavy.gov.in
