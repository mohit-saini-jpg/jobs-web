Selection Process – Lohardaga NHM Bharti 2026

The selection process for the Lohardaga NHM Recruitment 2026 is merit-based and involves a written examination followed by document verification. Candidates should prepare accordingly as the written test carries the majority of the weightage in the final merit list. Written Examination (60 marks): Objective, multiple-choice questions; duration of 1 hour. Academic/Training Score (40 marks): Marks are awarded based on percentage obtained in qualifying examinations as per the prescribed scoring formula. Skill Test (if applicable): For select posts, a skill test may be conducted, which is qualifying in nature only and does not contribute to the merit score. Domicile & Caste Certificate Rules – Lohardaga NHM 2026

Candidates claiming reservation under any category must submit valid Jharkhand domicile and caste certificates in the prescribed formats. Domicile certificates issued before 2 June 2016 at Sub-Divisional level and before 19 July 2019 at Anchal level are not valid. Caste certificates issued under earlier formats or by unauthorized authorities are also not accepted. Certificates must match the name spelling exactly with the Matriculation certificate, otherwise they will be rejected.

Terms and Conditions of Contractual Appointment

All posts under the Lohardaga NHM Bharti 2026 are purely contractual. Candidates must note the following service conditions before applying. There is no permanent employment — an indemnity bond must be signed before joining confirming no claim to regularization. The contract is valid for one year and may be extended annually based on Annual Performance Appraisal. The contract can be terminated with one month's notice or one month's honorarium in lieu of notice. Monthly stipend is indicative and may be revised as per government directives. The decision of the District Selection Committee, Lohardaga, is final and binding in all matters of selection. District-level reservation roster will be followed for all posts.
