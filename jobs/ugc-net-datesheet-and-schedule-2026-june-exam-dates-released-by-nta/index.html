UGC NET Datesheet and Schedule 2026 - June Exam Dates Released by NTA

UGC NET June 2026 exams will be conducted from June 22 to June 30, 2026, as officially announced by the National Testing Agency (NTA). The subject-wise datesheet and shift schedule have now been released, allowing candidates across 80+ subjects to plan their exam preparation and travel accordingly.

The Computer-Based Test (CBT) will be held in two shifts daily Shift 1 from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM and Shift 2 from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Candidates can download their exam city intimation slip 8-10 days before the exam and admit card from the official UGC-NET website at ugcnet.nta.nic.in.

How to Download UGC NET Datesheet 2026

  • Visit the official UGC-NET website: ugcnet.nta.nic.in.
  • Click on “UGC-NET June 2026 Datesheet/Schedule” on the homepage.
  • Download the PDF version of the datesheet.
  • Save and print a copy for future reference.

UGC NET June 2026 Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidates must have master’s degree or equivalent in relevant subjects.
  • Minimum 55% marks (general/EWS) or 50% (reserved categories).
  • Final-year master’s students can also apply provisionally.

Exam City Intimation Slip

  • Release Date: 8-10 days before the exam.
  • For June 22 exam: City slip between June 12-14, 2026.

Admit Card

  • Released closer to the exam date.
  • Download from ugcnet.nta.nic.in.
  • Check exam city, shift timing, and subject code carefully.

Steps to Download Admit Card

  • Go to ugcnet.nta.nic.in.
  • Click “Login to Admit Card”.
  • Enter application number and password.
  • Download and print the admit card.
