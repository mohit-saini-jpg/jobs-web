ICSIL RGSSH Nursing Officer Recruitment Expert Analysis

This recruitment is a great chance for nursing graduates to join the Indian government. The selection process will be based on a written exam and interview. Candidates should prepare well for the exam and practice their interview skills to increase their chances of selection.

Who Should Apply ICSIL RGSSH Nursing Officer Recruitment

Ideal candidates for this job are nursing graduates with a valid registration certificate from the Indian Nursing Council. They should have a good understanding of nursing practices and be able to work in a team.

ICSIL RGSSH Nursing Officer Recruitment Preparation Tips

To prepare for the exam, candidates should focus on nursing-related topics, such as anatomy, physiology, and pharmacology. They should also practice their communication skills and be ready to answer questions on their experience and skills.

ICSIL RGSSH Nursing Officer Recruitment Salary Insights

The salary for Nursing Officer posts in the Indian government is around ₹21,700-₹69,100 per month. This is a good salary compared to other government jobs, and it also offers opportunities for career growth and promotions.

ICSIL RGSSH Nursing Officer Recruitment Job Profile

As a Nursing Officer, you will be responsible for providing nursing care to patients, administering medications, and maintaining patient records. You will also be required to work in a team and communicate effectively with doctors and other healthcare professionals.

ICSIL RGSSH Nursing Officer Recruitment Selection Strategy

To increase your chances of selection, focus on preparing well for the written exam and practicing your interview skills. Be ready to answer questions on your experience, skills, and knowledge of nursing practices.

Q2How many vacancies are there for the Nursing Officer post?
A
There are 65 vacancies for the post of Nursing Officer at Rajiv Gandhi Super Specialty Hospital, Delhi.
Q3What is the age limit for this post?
A
The age limit is up to 35 years, with no relaxation details specified in the notification.
Q4What educational qualification is required?
A
Candidates need a B.Sc. Nursing from a recognised institute, or an equivalent qualification with a Certificate in General Nursing Midwifery (GNM), along with basic computer knowledge.

Selected candidates will receive a monthly remuneration of Rs 70,942.
Q8Where will selected candidates be deployed?
