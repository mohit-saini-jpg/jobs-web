Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the TNSRLM Karur Community Bank Coordinator posts?

The last date to submit the application for the Community Bank Coordinator posts is 02 June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the designated address before this deadline.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Community Bank Coordinator role?

Candidates must have passed the 12th Standard. Additionally, they should be an SHG member with 2 years of experience as an Animator, possess knowledge of Tamil and English, have computer skills, and a two-wheeler driving license.

Q3. What is the age limit for applying to this recruitment?

The maximum age limit for applicants is 35 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per the prevailing government norms and regulations.

Q4. How many vacancies are available for the Community Bank Coordinator position?

There are a total of 28 vacancies announced for the Community Bank Coordinator position by the Tamil Nadu State Rural Livelihoods Mission Karur.

Q5. What is the application mode for this recruitment?

The application process for the TNSRLM Karur Community Bank Coordinator recruitment is strictly offline. Candidates need to send their filled application forms to the official address mentioned in the notification.

Q6. Is there any application fee for the TNSRLM Karur Community Bank Coordinator posts?

No, there is no application fee required for any category of candidates applying for the Community Bank Coordinator posts. Both General/OBC and SC/ST candidates are exempted from paying any fee.

Q7. What are the selection criteria for the Community Bank Coordinator posts?

The selection process for the Community Bank Coordinator posts will involve a Written Test followed by an Interview. Candidates need to perform well in both stages to be considered for the role.

Q8. What is the job type for the Community Bank Coordinator position?

The Community Bank Coordinator positions are offered on a contractual basis. This means the employment will be for a fixed term as per the terms and conditions of the contract.
