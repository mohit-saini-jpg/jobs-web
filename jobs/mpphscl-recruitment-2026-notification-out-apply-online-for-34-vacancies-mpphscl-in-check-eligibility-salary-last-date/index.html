Selection Process

  • The selection process for MPPHSCL Recruitment 2026 involves the following stages:

  • Stage 1 - Shortlisting by MP Online MP Online will shortlist and scrutinise applications received online. A shortlist of 5 candidates per vacancy will be prepared.

  • Stage 2 - Written Examination The exam will be conducted by MP Online. The syllabus for the exam will be published on the MP Online website.

  • Stage 3 - Interview by HR Committee / Selection Committee Candidates who qualify the exam will be called for an interview conducted by the MPPHSCL HR Committee / Selection Committee. For deputation candidates

  • direct interview by the HR Committee will be held.

  • Stage 4 - Final Selection The HR Committee / Selection Committee will recommend candidates to the Managing Director

  • MPPHSCL based on final marks. The Managing Director's decision will be final and binding.

  • Tiebreaker Rules (if two or more candidates score equally): Priority will be given in the following order: (a) more experienced candidate

  • (b) higher educational qualification

  • (c) computer knowledge.

  • Waiting List: A waiting list of candidates (excluding the selected candidate) will be prepared and will remain valid for 1 year.

  • Merit Calculation:

  • Marks will be calculated up to 2 decimal places. Selected candidates will be notified via email and SMS to their registered mobile number.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for MPPHSCL Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the online application and pay the application fee is 24th June 2026. Applications received after this date will not be considered.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in MPPHSCL Recruitment 2026?

MPPHSCL has released a total of 34 vacancies across 18 posts including General Managers, Managers, Consultants, Bio-Medical Engineers, Legal Officer, Pharmacist, and Office Assistant.

Q3. What is the age limit for MPPHSCL Recruitment 2026?

The age limit is 18 to 40 years for General Category and 18 to 45 years for SC/ST/OBC/Govt. Employees/Women as on 01.01.2026.

Q4. What is the salary for General Manager in MPPHSCL 2026?
