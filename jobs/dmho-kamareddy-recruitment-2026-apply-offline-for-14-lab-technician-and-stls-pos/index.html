DMHO Kamareddy Recruitment 2026 : Eligibility & Qualification

Details

  2. SCs, STs & BCs: 5 years relaxation Ex-servicemen: 3 years + length of service Disabled persons: 10 years relaxation
  3. Educational Qualifications:
  4. Lab Technician/Sputum Microscopist: Intermediate (10+2) + Diploma/Certified course in Medical Laboratory Technology or equivalent. Preferential: 1 year NTEP experience or sputum smear microscopy skills
  5. Senior Tuberculosis Laboratory Supervisor (STLS): Graduate or Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology + Permanent two-wheeler driving license + Certificate course in computer operations (min. 2 months). Preferential: Minimum 1 year NTEP experience
