How to Check TG ECET Result 2026

  • Visit the official website - ecet.tgche.ac.in
  • Click on the TG ECET Result 2026 or Rank Card link
  • Enter your Hall Ticket Number, Registration Number, and Date of Birth
  • Click on submit
  • Your rank card will be displayed on the screen
  • Verify all details carefully
  • Download and print the result for future use
