How to Obtain the Aadhaar Supervisor Certificate Before Applying

Candidates who do not yet hold the mandatory Aadhaar Operator/Supervisor certificate need to complete two steps before they are eligible to apply for CSC Aadhaar Seva Kendra jobs. First, they must apply for and pass the Aadhaar Supervisor exam; second, they must complete the LMS certification. Both processes are managed through official UIDAI platforms. Visit the Aadhaar Supervisor exam portal at https://uidai.dexitglobalexams.com/UIDAI/LoginAction.action and submit your exam application. An authorization request will be sent to the State Team. Once approved, you will be permitted to appear for the Aadhaar Supervisor exam. After your LMS ID and Password are created and approved, appear for the Aadhaar LMS Certification through the portal at https://e-learning.uidai.gov.in Once certified, proceed to apply for the CSC Aadhaar Operator/Supervisor district post on the CSC Job Portal.
