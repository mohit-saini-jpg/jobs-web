FAQs

Q1Has CSC e-Governance Services India Ltd. officially released the Aadhaar Supervisor Operator Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, CSC e-Governance Services India Ltd. has officially released the Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator – District recruitment notification for 2026 for states including Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, and Maharashtra through the CSC Job Portal at career.csccloud.in.
Q2What is the last date to apply for CSC Aadhaar Operator Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for the CSC Aadhaar Supervisor Operator Vacancy 2026 is 20 August 2026. Candidates should apply well before the deadline to avoid last-minute technical issues.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for CSC Aadhaar Supervisor Operator 2026?
A
Candidates must have passed 12th (Intermediate/Senior Secondary), or Matriculation with 2 Years ITI, or Matriculation with 3 Years Polytechnic Diploma. A valid Aadhaar Operator/Supervisor certificate from a UIDAI-authorized agency is also mandatory.
Q4What is the minimum age to apply for CSC Aadhaar Seva Kendra Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age required to apply is 18 years. No maximum age limit is specified in the official notification.
Q5What is the salary for selected candidates in CSC Aadhaar Operator Recruitment 2026?
A
Selected candidates will receive the minimum wages of the respective state as applicable to semi-skilled manpower. The exact amount differs for Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, and Maharashtra based on their current state minimum wage rates.
Q6Are VLEs eligible to apply for the CSC Aadhaar Supervisor Operator post?
A
No. The official notification clearly states that VLEs (Village Level Entrepreneurs) are not eligible to apply for the Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator – District post under this recruitment.
Q7How can I get the Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator certificate required for this recruitment?
A
Candidates can appear for the Aadhaar Supervisor exam through the portal at uidai.dexitglobalexams.com after getting state team approval. Additionally, LMS Certification can be completed through e-learning.uidai.gov.in once an LMS ID and Password are issued.
Q8Is this a permanent government job or a contractual post?
A
This is a purely contractual post for a period of 1 year only. It is not a permanent government job. The position is under CSC e-Governance Services India Ltd. for Aadhaar Seva Kendra (ASK) operations.
Q9How many total vacancies are available across all states in CSC Aadhaar Operator Recruitment 2026?
A
Bihar has the highest number of vacancies spread across 37 districts. Chhattisgarh has 18 vacancies across 6 districts, Madhya Pradesh has 5 vacancies across 5 districts, and Maharashtra has 1 vacancy in Yavatmal district. The vacancies are district-specific and candidates must apply for their respective district.
Q10Where can I apply online for CSC Aadhaar Supervisor Operator Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online through the official CSC Job Portal. State-specific direct links are: Bihar – career.csccloud.in/job-post/MTMzNA==, Chhattisgarh – career.csccloud.in/job-post/MTMyMg==, Madhya Pradesh – career.csccloud.in/job-post/MTMyMQ==, Maharashtra – career.csccloud.in/job-post/MTMyMA==.
