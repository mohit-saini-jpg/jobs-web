Step 1: Visit Official Website

Open your web browser and go to the official website: xatonline.in

Step 2: Find Provisional Answer Key Link

On the homepage, look for the link that says "XAT 2026 Provisional Answer Key" or "Download Answer Key" or "Answer Key Objection". This is usually in the "Latest Announcements" or "Candidate Login" section.

Step 3: Enter Login Details

You will need to provide:

  • XAT ID (Application Number/Registration ID)
  • Password
  • Security Code/Captcha (if required)

Step 4: Download and Verify

Click on the "Download Provisional Answer Key" or "Submit" button. Your provisional answer key will appear on the screen (in PDF format). Carefully verify all details including:

  • Question-wise correct options
  • Your marked responses (if linked with response sheet)
  • Status of answers

Step 5: Save and Print

Save the provisional answer key as a PDF file on your device and take 2-3 printouts. Keep them safely for reference and objection raising.

Important: Download your provisional answer key as soon as it's released to avoid last-minute technical issues or server overload. Use it to calculate tentative scores and raise objections if necessary.

Exam Day Guidelines

Before Leaving for Exam:

  • Check exam center location on Google Maps
  • Plan to reach 30-45 minutes before reporting time
  • Keep admit card and ID proof in a clear folder
  • Carry extra pens and pencils
  • Get a good night’s sleep
  • Eat a light, healthy breakfast

At the Examination Center:

  • Follow all instructions given by the invigilators
  • Maintain silence and discipline
  • No use of mobile phones or electronic devices
  • Do not communicate with other candidates
  • Raise your hand if you need any assistance

Prohibited Items in Exam Hall:

  • Mobile phones and smartphones
  • Smartwatches and electronic watches
  • Calculators (unless specifically allowed)
  • Bluetooth devices and earphones
  • Textbooks, study materials, and notes
  • Bags and pouches
