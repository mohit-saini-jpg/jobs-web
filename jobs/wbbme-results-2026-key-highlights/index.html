Steps to Check WBBME Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your WBBME Madrash, Alim, Fazli Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official WBBME result portal at https://wbresults.nic.in/ or go to the official website http://wbbme.org/.
  • On the home page, click on the link that reads "WBBME Madrash/Alim/Fazli Result 2026".
  • Enter your Roll Number and other required details in the login window.
  • Click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button.
  • Your WBBME Result 2026 for Madrash, Alim or Fazli will appear on the screen.
  • Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future use.
