About AVFCCL

AVFCCL (Assam Valley Fertilizers and Chemical Company Limited) is a joint venture company incorporated by the Government of Assam, Oil India Limited (OIL), National Fertilizers Limited (NFL), Hindustan Urvarak & Rasayan Limited (HURL), and Brahmaputra Valley Fertilizer Corporation Limited (BVFCL). The company's prime objective is to establish and operate a state-of-the-art, environment-friendly, and energy-efficient Natural Gas based Fertilizer Complex (Ammonia-Urea) with a capacity of 2200 MTPD Ammonia and 3850 MTPD Urea (1.27 MMTPA neem coated urea) at Namrup, Assam.

AVFCCL CFO Eligibility Criteria 2026

Candidates must carefully check the eligibility criteria before applying for the AVFCCL Chief Financial Officer post. The main eligibility parameters are educational qualification, age limit, and post-qualification work experience. Candidates applying for the CFO post must hold any one of the following qualifications: Chartered Accountant (CA), or Cost and Management Accountant (CMA), or Two years full-time regular MBA (Finance) with a minimum of 60% marks Note: Articleship and partnership experience will not be considered towards eligibility or work experience. The candidate must possess a minimum of 25 years of post-qualification work experience. The role requires: Extensive experience in Corporate Financial Management and Corporate Accounts At least 2 years at a senior management level such as General Manager (Finance), Finance Head, or Department Head (Finance) Preferably experience
