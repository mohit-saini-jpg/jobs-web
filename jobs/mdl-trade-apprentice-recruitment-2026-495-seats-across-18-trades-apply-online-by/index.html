MDL Trade Apprentice Recruitment 2026 – 495 Seats Across : Job Overview

Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders Limited (MDL), a Navratna Company under the Ministry of Defence, has released Advertisement No. MDL_ATS/01/2026 inviting online applications for 495 Trade Apprentice seats under the Apprentices Act 1961 for the intake year 2026. Applications open from 11 June 2026 (Thursday) and the last date to apply online is 01 July 2026 (Wednesday). The online examination is tentatively scheduled for 25 July 2026. All applications must be submitted through https://mazagondock.in.

MDL Trade Apprentice Recruitment 2026 – 495 Seats Across : Eligibility & Qualification

Details
  1. Eligibility for MDL Trade Apprentice Recruitment 2026 depends on the group applied for - Group A, B, or C - and is assessed based on educational qualification marks as on 01 October 2026. ITI-passed candidates are not eligible for Group A and Group C; if discovered at any stage, their candidature will be cancelled without notice
  2. Passed SSC (10th Class) with General Science and Mathematics. General / OBC / SEBC / EWS / Divyang / AFC: Minimum 50% aggregate marks. SC / ST: Passing class only (no minimum percentage). If qualifying criteria is best-of-5 subjects, General Science and Mathematics must be part of the curriculum and the candidate must have passed both subjects; otherwise the application will be rejected. Higher-qualified candidates may apply but eligibility will be based on SSC marks only. ITI-passed candidates are NOT eligible for Group A
  3. Passed ITI in the relevant trade from a Government or Government-recognized Industrial Training Institute. General / OBC / SEBC / EWS / Divyang / AFC: Minimum 50% aggregate marks. SC / ST: Passing class only. Applicants in the semester system must have cleared all backlog papers of previous semesters, duly certified by the institute head. For Fitter Structural trade, applicants from the ITI Fitter trade can apply. Applicants appearing in the final ITI exam may apply but must pass the final exam on or before 01 October 2026. Candidates passing ITI after 01-10-2026 will not be considered
  4. Passed 8th Standard (under 10+2 system) with Science and Mathematics. General / OBC / SEBC / EWS / Divyang / AFC: Minimum 50% aggregate marks. SC / ST: Passing class only. Higher-qualified candidates may apply but eligibility will be based on 8th Standard marks only. ITI-passed candidates are NOT eligible for Group C
  5. SC / ST: Upper age relaxed by 5 years OBC: Upper age relaxed by 3 years Divyang (PwD): Upper age relaxed by 10 years
  6. Age relaxation is applicable as per Government Rules and is subject to submission of a valid certificate issued by the Competent Authority in the prescribed format
  7. Candidates must be a citizen of India, or a subject of Nepal or Bhutan, or a Tibetan refugee who arrived in India before 1 January 1962 with the intention of permanent settlement, or a person of Indian origin who has migrated from specified countries with the intention of permanently settling in India. Candidates in the latter categories must hold an eligibility certificate issued by the Government of India

MDL Trade Apprentice Recruitment 2026 – 495 Seats Across : Salary Details & Pay Scale

Details
  1. Selected Trade Apprentices will receive a monthly stipend during their training period as per the Apprentices Act. The stipend amount is credited directly to the selected candidate's savings bank account via ECS (Electronic Clearing Service). No cheques will be issued by the company. Candidates must have a savings account in a Public Sector or Private Sector Scheduled Bank with ECS facility, and must also have a valid PAN Card - failing which, income tax will be deducted from the stipend as per prevailing rates
  2. No hostel accommodation will be provided by MDL. Candidates must make their own residential arrangements. Apprentices will be entitled to leave and holidays during training as per the Apprentices Act

MDL Trade Apprentice Recruitment 2026 – 495 Seats Across : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for MDL Trade Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application including edits, modifications, and fee payment for MDL Trade Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is 01 July 2026 (Wednesday). Applications submitted after this date will not be accepted.
Q2How many seats are available in MDL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 495 Trade Apprentice seats are available across 18 trades in three groups: Group A (100 seats, 10th passed), Group B (355 seats, ITI passed), and Group C (40 seats, 8th passed).
Q3What is the application fee for MDL Trade Apprentice 2026?
A
Candidates from UR, OBC, SEBC, EWS, and AFC categories must pay Rs. 100/- + applicable bank charges. SC, ST, and Divyang (PwD) candidates are exempted from the fee but must upload valid certificates. The fee once paid is non-refundable.
Q4What is the age limit for MDL Trade Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
The age limit as on 01 October 2026 is: 15–19 years for Group A, 16–21 years for Group B, and 14–18 years for Group C. Upper age relaxation is 5 years for SC/ST, 3 years for OBC, and 10 years for Divyang candidates.
Q5Can ITI-passed candidates apply for Group A or Group C?
A
No. ITI-passed candidates are strictly not eligible for Group A (10th Class) and Group C (8th Class). If it is found at any stage that a candidate is ITI-passed or appearing in an ITI exam, their candidature will be cancelled without any notice.
Q6What is the selection process for MDL Trade Apprentice 2026?
A
Selection is in three stages: Stage 1 – Online CBT (100 MCQs, 2 hours); Stage 2 – Document Verification and Trade Allotment; Stage 3 – Medical Examination as per Apprentices Rules 1992. There is no interview. The tentative date for the online exam is 25 July 2026.
Q7What is the stipend for MDL Trade Apprentices?
A
Apprentices will receive a monthly stipend as per the Apprentices Act, credited directly via ECS to their savings bank account. No cheques will be issued. The exact stipend amount follows the Apprentices Act norms applicable at the time of training.
Q8How to apply online for MDL Trade Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
Visit https://mazagondock.in, navigate to Careers → Online Recruitment → Apprentice, click "Create New Account," register, login, fill the form, upload documents, pay the fee (if applicable), select two exam centres, and submit before 01 July 2026.
Q9What are the examination centres for MDL Trade Apprentice 2026?
A
The tentative examination centres are: Mumbai, Thane, Pune, Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar (Aurangabad), Nagpur, Latur, Kolhapur, and Nashik. Candidates can choose two preferred centres during the application. Centre change requests will not be entertained after submission.
Q10Is hostel accommodation provided by MDL for selected apprentices?
A
No. Hostel accommodation will not be provided by Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders Limited. Selected candidates must arrange their own accommodation at their own cost in Mumbai.
