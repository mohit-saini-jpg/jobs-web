How to Apply

  • 1Eligible candidates must submit offline applications in the following manner:
  • 2Prepare a complete Bio-Data along with one recent passport-size photograph.
  • 3Attach self-attested photocopies of all mark sheets, certificates, caste certificate, and testimonials.
  • 4Download the application form from the school website: www.ssghorakhal.org and fill it completely in all respects. Incomplete applications will be rejected.

  • 6Send the application by post to:
  • 7The Principal, Sainik School Ghorakhal, PO- Ghorakhal, Distt- Nainital (Uttarakhand) PIN – 263156
  • 8Applications must reach the Principal within 21 days from the date of publication of the advertisement in Employment News. Hand-delivered and late applications will not be accepted. The school will not be responsible for postal delays.

FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for Sainik School Ghorakhal Recruitment 2026?
The last date is within 21 days from the publication of the advertisement in Employment News. Candidates should apply early to avoid postal delays.
Q2. How can I apply for Sainik School Ghorakhal vacancies?
Applications must be submitted offline by post. Candidates need to download the form from the official website, fill it, attach documents, and send it to the Principal, Sainik School Ghorakhal.
Q3. Is there an online application option available?
No, only offline applications sent via post are accepted. Hand-delivered applications are not allowed.
Q4. What is the selection process for these posts?
The selection involves application screening followed by a written/practical test and interview for shortlisted candidates.
Q5. Are TA/DA provided for attending the selection process?
No, candidates will not receive any TA/DA for appearing in the test or interview.
Q6. What are the salary benefits for teaching posts?
Teaching posts offer Pay Level 07 or 08 with allowances, along with rent-free accommodation and free meals with cadets.
Q7. Is CTET mandatory for all teaching posts?
CTET Paper II is mandatory for TGT posts. For PGT, CTET is desirable but not compulsory.
Q8. What is the age limit for teaching positions?
PGT candidates must be between 21–40 years, while TGT candidates should be between 21–35 years.
Q9. Can candidates from other states apply?
Yes, candidates from across India can apply if they meet the eligibility criteria.
Q10. Where will the selection process take place?
All tests and interviews will be conducted at Sainik School Ghorakhal, Nainital (Uttarakhand).
