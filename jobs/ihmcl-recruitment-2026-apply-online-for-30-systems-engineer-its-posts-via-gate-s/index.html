IHMCL Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

Selection for the post of Systems Engineer (ITS) is based on the following criteria: Merit is determined on the basis of the valid GATE Score of the year 2025. IHMCL reserves the right to invite candidates for interview as per a cut-off for the respective category, to be decided subsequently by IHMCL. If the number of applications received is large, IHMCL will adopt a shortlisting criterion to restrict the number of candidates called for interaction/interview to a reasonable number, by a method decided by the Selection Committee. For the final merit list, if two or more candidates in the same category have equal scores/marks, the candidate older in age will be placed higher in the merit list. Note: Candidates must enter their GATE Score as mentioned in the official GATE 2025 Scorecard (range: 0 to 1000) while applying.
