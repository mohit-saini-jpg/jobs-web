How to download IGNOU B.Ed. Entrance Exam Admit Card/Hall Ticket 2025?

Following are the steps to download the IGNOU B.Ed. Entrance Exam Admit Card 2025:

  • Visit the official site of IGNOU.
  • Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) home page will display.
  • By scrolling down the home page, find the Admission section.
  • Search for the B.Ed. Entrance Exam Admit Card 2025 link.
  • Click on the link.
  • Enter the login details.
  • Click on Submit.
  • IGNOU B.Ed. Entrance Exam 2025 Admit Card will be displayed.
  • Download and take a print out.
