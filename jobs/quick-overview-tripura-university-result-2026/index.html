How to Check Tripura University Result 2026 Online

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their provisional result:

  • Visit the official Tripura University result portal: tripurauniv.ac.in/result/university-results
  • Look for your course and semester name in the examination list (for example, "M.A/M.Sc Economics 2nd & 4th Semester Examination-2026").
  • Click on the "View" link next to your examination name.
  • The result will open as a PDF file showing roll number-wise results.
  • Use Ctrl+F (or Cmd+F on Mac) to search for your roll number within the PDF.
  • Download or save the PDF for future reference.

Alternative Method: Checking Result Through Samarth Portal

For some programmes (mainly B.Tech and Diploma Engineering courses), Tripura University publishes results through the Samarth academic portal instead of a direct PDF. In such cases:

  • Click the "External Link" option shown against that examination on the result page.
  • This will redirect to tripurauniv.samarth.edu.in.
  • Log in using your student credentials to view the result.

The five results covered in this article (Economics, IMD Economics, Human Physiology, Geography and Disaster Management, and M.Pharm) are all available as direct PDF downloads and do not require Samarth login.

What Is a Provisional Result?

A provisional result is an early release of marks or grades issued by the university before the final, verified marksheet is prepared. It allows students to know their performance quickly. Official confirmation such as revaluation policy, final marksheet issuance date, or grade card collection process is not available on the source page for these specific results, so students are advised to check with their respective department or the examination section for such details.

Important Instructions for Students

  • The declared result is provisional. It is not a substitute for the final/original marksheet issued by the university.
  • Students should cross-check their marks carefully and report any discrepancy to the respective department or the Controller of Examinations.
  • Keep a downloaded copy of the result PDF safe, as result links on university portals can change over time.
  • For queries specific to revaluation, rechecking, or grievance redressal, information is not available on the official result page and students should contact the university's examination section directly.
