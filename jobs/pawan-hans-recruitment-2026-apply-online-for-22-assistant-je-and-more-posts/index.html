Pawan Hans Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Pawan Hans Limited, a Miniratna Government of India helicopter carrier enterprise, has invited applications for 22 vacancies across 7 posts including Assistant (HR&A), Junior Engineer (Civil), Quality Assistant, Assistant (CAMO), Station-in-Charge, and Trainee Technician. All posts are based in Delhi, Northern Region, with applications open now and closing on 10 July 2026. Both regular and contractual positions are on offer, with pay ranging from Rs. 16,500 to Rs. 27,000 basic per month.

Pawan Hans Recruitment 2026 : Eligibility & Qualification

Details
  1. Eligibility for Pawan Hans Recruitment 2026 varies significantly by post, ranging from a basic graduate degree to specialised aviation diplomas. The breakdown below covers the exact qualification and experience required for each role
  2. For the Regular basis post, candidates need a Graduate Degree in any discipline with 3 years of post-qualification experience in the relevant field, OR an MBA/PG Degree/2-year Diploma in HR/HRM/HRD/PM & IR with 1 year of post-qualification experience. For the Contractual basis post, the requirement is Graduation with 3 years post-qualification experience, OR Graduation with a 2-year Diploma in IR&PM plus 1 year of relevant experience
  3. This post requires a Diploma in Civil Engineering from a recognised institute, with a minimum of 5 years of post-qualification experience in preparing estimates, Bill of Quantity (BoQ), and tenders for repair and maintenance work. Experience in civil maintenance of large buildings, malls, metro stations, and high-rise apartments is required, and knowledge of computers is preferable
  4. Both these aviation-focused roles require a Diploma in AME/Aeronautical or BSc in Aviation, along with 3 years of aviation experience - specifically in Quality CAR-145 for the Quality Assistant post, and in CAMO (Continuing Airworthiness Management Organisation) for the Assistant (CAMO) post
  5. Candidates need a Graduate degree in any discipline with 3 years of relevant post-qualification experience, OR a PG Degree/2-year Diploma in Marketing/Finance/Aviation or equivalent with 1 year of relevant post-qualification experience
  6. This is a fresher-friendly entry-level post requiring a Diploma in Mechanical/Aeronautical/Electronics/Electricals/Electronics & Communication/Instrumentation, OR an AME Course from a DGCA-recognised institute, OR an Aircraft Engineering Maintenance Course in B1.3 or B2 Category from a DGCA-recognised institute. Freshers may apply, and preference is given to candidates with a B2 Category AME qualification or those who have passed DGCA Modules
  7. The upper age limit differs between the experienced posts and the Trainee Technician post, both calculated as on the application closing date
  8. Reservation and age relaxation for SC/ST/OBC/PwD candidates will apply as per Government of India directives

Pawan Hans Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Pawan Hans accepts applications through a combined online-plus-postal process, meaning an online submission alone is not sufficient - a signed hard copy must also reach the office by the deadline. Candidates unable to apply online can also use an offline route.
  • 2Visit the Careers tab on www.pawanhans.co.in and apply online.
  • 3Take a printout of the duly filled and signed online application form.
  • 4Affix a recent passport-size photograph on the printed form.
  • 5Attach self-attested copies of testimonials supporting age, caste/class, qualification, experience, Pay/CTC, and licence/medical status.
  • 6Send the complete application through proper channel to reach the office on or before 10 July 2026.
  • 7Candidates who cannot complete the online application may instead download the Application Blank Form from www.pawanhans.co.in, fill and sign it, affix a photograph, attach self-attested testimonials, and include a Demand Draft of Rs. 354 (unless fee is paid online) drawn in favour of Pawan Hans Limited, payable at Delhi.
  • 8Candidates applying for more than one post must submit a separate application for each post - failing to do so means the application will only be considered for the first post mentioned.

Pawan Hans Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for Pawan Hans Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to apply, both online and via postal submission, is 10 July 2026.
Q2How many vacancies are there in Pawan Hans Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 22 total vacancies across 7 posts: Assistant (HR&A) Regular, Assistant (HR&A) Contract, Junior Engineer (Civil), Quality Assistant, Assistant (CAMO), Station-in-Charge, and Trainee Technician.
Q3What is the application fee for Pawan Hans Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 354 (inclusive of 18% GST), with SC/ST candidates and Persons with Disabilities exempted from payment.
Q4Can freshers apply for Pawan Hans Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, freshers may apply for the Trainee Technician post, which is the only post in this recruitment that does not require prior experience.
Q5What is the age limit for Pawan Hans Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit is 28 years (as on 10 July 2026) for Assistant, Junior Engineer, Quality Assistant, CAMO, and Station-in-Charge posts, and 25 years for the Trainee Technician post.
Q6What is the salary for Trainee Technician at Pawan Hans?
A
The Trainee Technician post offers an all-inclusive salary of Rs. 16,500 per month.
Q7Is the application process fully online?
A
No, candidates must apply online and then send a signed printout of the application along with photograph and testimonials by post to the Delhi office, or alternatively use the offline Application Blank Form with a Demand Draft.
Q8Where are the Pawan Hans Recruitment 2026 posts located?
A
All posts are based in Delhi, Northern Region, with Station-in-Charge falling under the administrative control of the Delhi office.
