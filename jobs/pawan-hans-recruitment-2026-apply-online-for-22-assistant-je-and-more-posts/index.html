About Pawan Hans Limited

Candidates wondering about the recruiting organisation can note that Pawan Hans is a Government of India helicopter transport enterprise that operates a fleet of more than 50 helicopters ranging from 5 to 26 seaters. As a Miniratna PSU under the Ministry of Civil Aviation, Pawan Hans offers a professional aviation work environment along with an attractive compensation package, including PF, Gratuity, Leave Encashment, and Pension benefits as applicable to each post. Selection Process and General Conditions

Pawan Hans follows a merit-based shortlisting process rather than calling every eligible applicant, giving the management discretion over each stage of recruitment. The management will call only candidates ranking highest on eligibility criteria - mere eligibility does not guarantee a test call. The number of candidates called for test may be restricted based on the response received for each post. The management reserves the right to reject applications, cancel posts, or relax age/experience/qualification criteria at its discretion. Only post-qualification experience is counted - teaching experience and induction/training periods are excluded. Candidates called for the test must produce all original documents at the time of test; failure to do so means they will not be permitted to appear, and travelling allowance will not be reimbursed.
