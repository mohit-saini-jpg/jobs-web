Important Instructions

  • Online applications only - no other mode of application will be accepted; applications incomplete in any respect will be summarily rejected. Candidates must fully satisfy themselves about their eligibility before applying - if ineligibility is detected at any stage before or after appointment, candidature/appointment shall be cancelled/terminated immediately. Examination fee once paid will not be refunded or re-adjusted under any circumstances - application fee will be refunded only in the event of a post becoming zero. All posts are temporary in nature but likely to continue further; candidates will be confirmed after successfully completing probationary period as per rules. Candidates who desire to apply for more than one post must apply online separately for each post. FRI will not entertain any request for change in test date/time/Centre in case of any clash - candidates need to decide the test they would like to take. Candidates working in Government Departments, PSUs, and Government Funded Research Agencies must send application through proper channel and submit NOC at the time of document verification. For OBC-NCL candidates, the certificate must be valid for FY 2025-26 and issued before the last date of submission of application. Candidates must not write personal identity (name, roll number, mobile number, address etc.) inside the Answer Book during Descriptive Test - failure will be treated as Unfair Means (UFM). Photograph must be a recent passport size colour photograph taken against a light-coloured preferably white background; if photograph and signature are not clearly visible, candidature will be liable to be rejected. Canvassing in any form and/or bringing any influence, political or otherwise, will definitely be treated as a disqualification for the post applied for. All future correspondences regarding the selection process, shortlisting of candidates and important dates shall be uploaded on the Institute's website https://fri.icfre.gov.in - candidates are responsible to check the FRI website regularly. Appointment is subject to candidates being found medically fit as per the requirements of the Institute. Legal disputes, if any, shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the competent court in the state of Uttarakhand.

FAQs

Q1. What is FRI Dehradun Group C Recruitment 2026?
FRI Dehradun Group C Recruitment 2026 is a Central Government recruitment drive by ICFRE–Forest Research Institute, Dehradun for various Group C posts like MTS, LDC, Forest Guard, Technician, Technical Assistant, Store Keeper, Stenographer, and Library Information Assistant, with a total of 91 vacancies.
Q2. How many vacancies are there in FRI Dehradun Group C Recruitment 2026?
A total of 91 Group C vacancies have been notified, including posts such as Library Information Assistant, Technical Assistant, Stenographer Grade II, Technician (Field/Lab Research and Maintenance), Store Keeper, LDC, Forest Guard, and Multi Tasking Staff (MTS).
Q3. What are the important dates for FRI Dehradun Group C Recruitment 2026?
The online application process starts from 04 May 2026, and the last date to apply online is 03 June 2026 as per the recruitment details for FRI Group C posts.
Q4. What is the eligibility criteria for FRI Dehradun Group C posts?
Eligibility is post‑wise: 10th pass candidates can apply for MTS, Technician, and Forest Guard; 12th pass candidates can apply for LDC and Stenographer; ITI holders can apply for Technician trades; and graduates can apply for Library Information Assistant and Technical Assistant posts, along with fulfilling required physical and skill standards wherever applicable.
Q5. What is the age limit for FRI Dehradun Group C Recruitment 2026?
The age limit for FRI Dehradun Group C posts is generally 18 to 30 years, varying slightly post‑wise as per the notification, with applicable relaxations for reserved categories as per Government of India rules.
Q6. What is the salary or pay level for FRI Dehradun Group C posts?
The salary is as per 7th CPC Pay Matrix from Level‑1 to Level‑6: MTS is Level‑1; LDC, Forest Guard, and Technician are Level‑2; Technician (Field/Lab Research) and Store Keeper are Level‑3; Stenographer Grade II is Level‑4; Technical Assistant is Level‑5; and Library Information Assistant is Level‑6.
Q7. How can I apply online for FRI Dehradun Group C Recruitment 2026?
Candidates must visit the official FRI website [fri.icfre.gov.in], open the Group C Recruitment 2026 section, complete registration with a valid email ID and mobile number, fill the online application form, upload required documents, photograph and signature, pay the application fee if applicable, and finally submit and download/print the application form.
Q8. What documents are required for FRI Dehradun Group C online application?
Applicants typically need to upload scanned copies of their educational certificates (10th/12th/ITI/Graduation as applicable), caste/category certificate (if claiming reservation), photo and signature, and any other documents prescribed in the detailed notification such as disability certificate for PwBD candidates.
