Punjabi University, Patiala has released two separate examination date sheets in July 2026. The second covers a one-paper B.A. Part-2 Semester-3 Regular (Multidisciplinary) NEP date sheet for NCC students, issued under Notification No. 274/A. This article summarises the official exam schedule, timings, subjects, paper codes, and exam centres exactly as stated in both notifications.

B.A. Sem-3 (NCC Students) One-Paper Date Sheet -Notification No. 274/A

This date sheet covers the B.A. Part-2 (Semester-3) Regular (Multidisciplinary) NEP examination for NCC students, to be conducted on 20 July 2026 in the morning session from 9:30 AM to 12:30 PM. It follows the NEP-2020 syllabus dated December-2025.

Who Should Check These Date Sheets

  • Students should check Notification No. 279/R if they are B.A. Part-1 (Semester-2) regular-category, differently-abled (Divyang) students under NEP-2020.
  • Students should check Notification No. 274/A if they are B.A. Part-2 (Semester-3) Regular (Multidisciplinary) NEP students who are also NCC students.

Important Instructions

  • All candidates are informed that due to unavoidable administrative reasons, changes can occur in date sheets. Candidates should keep checking the university website regularly until examinations are completed.
  • In the revised Notification 279/R, the Physical Education paper (Foundation of Physical Education, Regular/2175) was corrected from the earlier paper, and a new date has been given.
  • In the corrected Notification 274/A, Notification Number 274 has been corrected to 274/A.
  • Both date sheets are for written examinations only.
