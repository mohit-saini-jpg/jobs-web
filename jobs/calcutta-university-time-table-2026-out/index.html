How to Download Calcutta University Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the Calcutta University Time Table 2026 from the official examination website. Follow these simple steps to get your copy:

  • Visit the official Calcutta University examination portal at cuexam.net.
  • On the homepage, locate the "Exam Notice" or "Time Table" section.
  • Find the link for Semester VI Revised Theoretical Programme Schedule 2026.
  • Click on the relevant notification as per your course (CBCS or CCF).
  • The PDF will open in a new tab. Click the download icon to save it.
  • Take a printout or save it on your device for future reference.

Students are advised to check the official timetable carefully and keep a printed copy for exam preparation.
