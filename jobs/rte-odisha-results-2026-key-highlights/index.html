How to Check RTE Odisha Results 2026 Online?

RTE Odisha has made the result download process simple and quick for students. Candidates only need their registration number and date of birth to view results on the official portal. Here is a step-by-step process you can follow:

  • Visit the Official Website - Go to the official RTE Odisha portal at rteparadarshi.odisha.gov.in.
  • Find the Results Section - Click on the 'Lottery Result' or 'Admission Result' tab on the homepage.
  • Select Your Lottery Round - Choose 'Round 1 Lottery Result' from the list of available options.
  • Enter Your Details - Input your Registration Number and date of birth in the designated fields.
  • Submit & View - Click the Submit button to view your RTE Odisha Round 1 Lottery Result 2026 on screen.
  • Download the Admission List - Click Download or Print to save your admission list PDF for official use.
  • Verify Your Details - Check that your name and application details are correctly reflected on the list.
