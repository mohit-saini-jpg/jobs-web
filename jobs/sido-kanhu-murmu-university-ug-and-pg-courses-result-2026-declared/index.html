Sido Kanhu Murmu University has made the result download process simple and quick for students. Candidates only need their registration number and date of birth to view results on the official portal. Here is a step-by-step process you can follow:

  • Visit the Official Website - Go to the official Sido Kanhu Murmu University exam portal at skmu.ac.in.
  • Find the Results Section - Click on the Examination or Results tab on the homepage.
  • Select Your Exam - Choose UG and PG Courses from the list of available results.
  • Enter Your Details - Input your Registration Number and Date of Birth in the required fields.
  • Submit & View - Click the Submit button to view your Sido Kanhu Murmu University result 2026 on screen.
  • Download the Mark Sheet - Click Download or Print to save your result PDF for official use.
