FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date to apply online for TMB Agricultural Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for submitting the online application form is 06 June 2026. It is highly recommended that candidates complete the entire process including document upload at least 2-3 days before the closing date. This helps avoid technical glitches, server overload, and gives time to correct any mistakes in the application. Late applications will not be accepted under any circumstances.
Q2Q2. What educational qualification is needed for the TMB Agricultural Officer post?
A
Candidates must hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Agriculture with at least 50% marks. Higher qualifications or specializations in allied fields like Horticulture, Veterinary Science, Dairy Technology, or Agricultural Engineering will be considered as added advantages during shortlisting and interview. The degree must be from a UGC-recognized university.
Q3Q3. Is prior work experience mandatory for this recruitment?
A
Yes. A minimum of 3 years relevant experience in banking or financial institutions focusing on agricultural lending is compulsory. Experience certificates and relieving letters must be uploaded. Freshers are not eligible to apply for this position.
Q4Q4. What is the maximum age limit for TMB Agricultural Officer 2026?
A
The maximum age is 30 years as on 30 April 2026. Candidates belonging to reserved categories can claim age relaxation as per Government norms mentioned in the detailed advertisement.
Q5Q5. Is there any application fee to be paid?
A
No. Tamilnad Mercantile Bank has kept the application fee as Nil for all categories. Candidates do not have to pay any amount while submitting the online form.
Q6Q6. How is the selection process conducted for Agricultural Officer in TMB?
A
The process involves shortlisting of applications followed by a Personal Interview (Direct or Video Conferencing). Final selection depends on interview performance, document verification, and medical fitness.
Q7Q7. What are the major roles of an Agricultural Officer in TMB?
A
The role involves extensive field work including loan origination, risk assessment, farmer education, scheme implementation, and portfolio monitoring. Officers act as subject matter experts in agriculture for their assigned branches and regions.
Q8Q8. Will selected candidates be posted only in Tamil Nadu?
A
No. Selected candidates can be posted anywhere in India as per the business needs of the bank. The job is fully transferable.
Q9Q9. How can I download the call letter for the interview?
A
Shortlisted candidates will be intimated individually through email and SMS. Interview call letters, if required, can be downloaded from the login portal using registration credentials.
Q10Q10. Is this a permanent job or contractual?
A
This is a regular and permanent recruitment in the Assistant Manager cadre. After successful completion of the probation period, candidates will enjoy all benefits applicable to permanent officers of the bank.
