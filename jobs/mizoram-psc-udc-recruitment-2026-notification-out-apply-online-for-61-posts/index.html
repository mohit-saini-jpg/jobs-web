Important Instructions

This is a Limited Departmental Examination (LDE) — only in-service LDC grade officers with the prescribed regular service are eligible; others need not apply. Candidates who have not registered on the MPSC one-time registration portal must register latest by 4:00 PM on 12-06-2026 before submitting the application. After making payment, candidates must verify that their application is actually submitted; otherwise their candidature claim will not be entertained. If paying via UPI, use only the mobile number registered during one-time registration. Persons with disabilities are exempted from payment of application fees; fee once paid is non-refundable under any circumstances. Canvassing (insawipui) directly or indirectly by a candidate will result in disqualification of candidature. All particulars and details mentioned in the application must be complete and correct; applications not specifying required information are liable to be rejected. Any candidate who does not fulfill any eligibility condition on verification at any stage of the recruitment process will be disqualified. The examination syllabus is available for download at https://tinyurl.com/UDCfor-LDE on the MPSC official website. For queries/clarifications, contact MPSC Helpline No. 0389-3596493 on working days between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for Mizoram PSC UDC LDE Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to complete One‑Time Registration and submit the online application for Mizoram PSC UDC Limited Departmental Examination 2026 is 12 June 2026 up to 4:00 PM.
Q2How many vacancies are released under Mizoram PSC UDC LDE 2026?
A
The Mizoram Public Service Commission has notified a total of 61 Upper Divisional Clerk (UDC) Group B (Non‑Gazetted) vacancies across 11 departments of the Government of Mizoram under LDE No. 14 of 2026‑2027.
Q3Who is eligible to apply for Mizoram PSC UDC LDE Recruitment 2026?
A
Only in‑service officers currently holding posts in the Lower Divisional Clerk (LDC) grade on a regular basis and having the prescribed years of regular service as per Mizoram Ministerial Service Rules, 2021 are eligible to apply; direct outsiders cannot apply for this Limited Departmental Examination.
Q4What is the required length of service in the LDC grade for UDC LDE 2026?
A
In general, candidates must have completed at least 7 years of regular service in the LDC grade, while “legacy” officers who were already in LDC posts on the date the extant rules were notified require 5 years of qualifying service under the Mizoram Ministerial Service Rules, 2021.
Q5What is the application mode for Mizoram PSC UDC LDE Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted online only through the Mizoram Public Service Commission’s online portal at https://mpsconline.mizoram.gov.in ; no offline or hard‑copy applications will be accepted for this examination.
Q6Is there any application fee for Mizoram PSC UDC LDE 2026 and who is exempt?
A
Candidates must pay the prescribed application fee online as per MPSC norms, but persons with disabilities are fully exempt from paying the fee; once paid, the fee is non‑refundable in all cases.
Q7What is the selection process for Mizoram PSC UDC LDE Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of a Limited Departmental Written Examination conducted by MPSC followed by document verification of service and eligibility certificates, and final appointment is based on performance in the written exam as per Mizoram Ministerial Service Rules, 2021.
Q8Where can I download the Mizoram PSC UDC LDE 2026 syllabus?
A
The detailed syllabus for the Upper Divisional Clerk Limited Departmental Examination can be downloaded from the official link provided by MPSC, accessible via the Commission’s website at the short URL mentioned in the notification for UDC LDE.
