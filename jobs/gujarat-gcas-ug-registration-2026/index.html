Gujarat GCAS UG Registration 2026

The Gujarat Common Admission Services (GCAS) has started the centralized online registration for Undergraduate (UG) admissions in Government, Grant-in-Aid, and Self-Finance colleges affiliated with participating universities for the academic year 2026-27. Registration will begin on May 7, 2026 (11:00 AM). This is a complete merit-based centralized admission process for various UG courses including BA, B.Sc, B.Com, BBA, BCA and many more. This guide covers important dates, eligibility, application fee, step-by-step process, and direct links.

Eligibility Criteria 2026

  • Must have passed Class 12 (10+2) or equivalent examination from a recognized board.
  • Minimum marks requirement varies as per university and course (generally 45% – 50% for most courses).
  • For reserved category students, relaxation in minimum marks is applicable as per Gujarat Government norms.
  • Candidates appearing in Class 12 exams in 2026 can also apply (provisional admission).

Application Fee 2026

Application / Registration fee will be charged as per the official notification. Payment can be made online through net banking, UPI, credit/debit card, or other available modes on the GCAS portal.

How to Apply for Gujarat GCAS UG Admission 2026

  • Visit the official GCAS portal — gcas.gujgov.edu.in
  • Click on UG Admission 2026-27 and complete Quick Registration.
  • Fill the online application form with personal, academic and contact details.
  • Upload required documents (Photo, Signature, Marksheets, etc.).
  • Fill course and college preferences carefully.
  • Pay the registration fee online.
  • Submit the form and take a printout of the confirmation page.

Documents Required for GCAS UG Admission

  • Class 10th and 12th Marksheet & Certificate
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph
  • Scanned Signature
  • School Leaving Certificate
  • Caste/Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • EWS Certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile Certificate (if required)
  • Income Certificate (for scholarship)
