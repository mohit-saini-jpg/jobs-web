Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for GACL Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit online applications for GACL Recruitment 2026 is 31 May 2026. The application window opened on 20 May 2026. Candidates are advised to apply before the deadline to avoid last-minute technical issues.

Q2. How to apply online for GACL Executive Trainee 2026?

To apply for GACL Executive Trainee (Mechanical/Chemical) posts, visit the official recruitment portal at https://gaclportal.gacl.co.in/hrportal/login.jsp. Register with your email and mobile number, fill the form, upload documents including GATE scorecard, and submit before 31 May 2026.

Q3. Is GATE score mandatory for GACL Executive Trainee Recruitment 2026?

Yes, a valid GATE score (2023, 2024, 2025, or 2026) is mandatory for Executive Trainee positions in Mechanical and Chemical branches. Candidates must mention their actual GATE marks out of 100 (not percentile) during application.

Q4. What is the eligibility criteria for GACL Executive Trainee (Mechanical/Chemical)?

Candidates must have passed B.E/B.Tech in the respective branch from a recognized university with minimum 60% marks and should have graduated between 2023 to 2026. GATE qualification is compulsory.

Q5. What is the experience required for Boiler Attendant post in GACL 2026?

For the Boiler Attendant position, candidates need a Diploma in Mechanical Engineering + First Class Boiler Attendant Certificate with a minimum of 5 years operational experience in 20 MT/Hr steam generation boilers with SCADA/PLC systems.

Q6. Who can apply for Assistant Officer (Security) in GACL Recruitment?

Ex-Defence personnel (NCO/JCO) with minimum 12 years of service or 5 years in force + 3+ years industrial security experience are eligible. Paramilitary forces (BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, SSB) candidates are also eligible. The candidate must be in Shape-1 medical category.

Q7. What is the age limit for Additional General Manager (Finance) in GACL?

The maximum age limit for Additional General Manager (Finance) is 53 years. Candidates must have CA/CMA qualification with minimum 23 years of relevant finance experience.

Q8. Is Gujarat domicile required for GACL jobs 2026?

Gujarat domicile is not mandatory but candidates with Gujarat domicile will be given preference in selection for most positions.
