BITSAT Syllabus 2026 has been released by Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) Pilani. It is based on the NCERT syllabus of Class 11 and 12 and covers five major parts: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (or Biology for B.Pharm), English Proficiency, and Logical Reasoning. Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) Pilani conducts the Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test (BITSAT) for admission to integrated first-degree programmes (B.E., B.Pharm, and M.Sc.) at its campuses in Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad. The exam is a computer-based test with 130 multiple-choice questions.

BITSAT Syllabus 2026 for English Proficiency and Logical Reasoning

English Proficiency: Grammar, Vocabulary, Reading Comprehension, Composition.

Logical Reasoning: Verbal Reasoning (Analogy, Classification, Series, Logical Deduction), Nonverbal Reasoning (Pattern Perception, Figure Formation, Paper Cutting, etc.).

How to Cover BITSAT Syllabus 2026?

  • Thoroughly understand the NCERT Class 11 & 12 concepts first.
  • Focus more on high-weightage topics from previous years.
  • Practice mock tests regularly to improve speed and accuracy.
  • Revise formulas and reactions daily.
  • Solve previous year BITSAT question papers.
