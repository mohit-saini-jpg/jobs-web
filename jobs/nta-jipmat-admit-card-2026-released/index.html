NTA JIPMAT Admit Card 2026 Released

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has released the JIPMAT Admit Card 2026 for candidates appearing in the Joint Integrated Programme in Management Admission Test (JIPMAT) 2026. Candidates who have successfully registered for the entrance examination can now download their hall tickets from the official NTA examination portal.

The admit card is a mandatory document for appearing in the examination and contains important information such as the exam date, reporting time, exam centre details, and candidate particulars. Candidates are advised to download the admit card well in advance and verify all details carefully.

How to Download NTA JIPMAT Admit Card 2026

Candidates can download their hall ticket by following these steps:

  • Visit the official NTA examination portal.
  • Click on the JIPMAT 2026 Admit Card link.
  • Enter your Application Number and Date of Birth/Password.
  • Complete the security verification and login.
  • The admit card will be displayed on the screen.
  • Verify all details carefully.
  • Download the admit card PDF.
  • Take at least two printouts for future use.

Candidates should keep the admit card safe until the completion of the admission process.

Details Mentioned on NTA JIPMAT Admit Card 2026

After downloading the admit card, candidates should check the following details:

  • Candidate’s Name
  • Application Number
  • Roll Number
  • Photograph of the Candidate
  • Signature
  • Gender
  • Category
  • Date of Birth
  • Examination Name
  • Exam Date
  • Exam Shift and Timing
  • Reporting Time
  • Examination Centre Name
  • Examination Centre Address
  • Centre Code
  • Important Instructions for Candidates

If any discrepancy is found, candidates should immediately contact NTA through the official helpdesk.

Documents Required Along with JIPMAT Admit Card 2026

Candidates must carry the following documents to the examination centre:

  • Printed Copy of JIPMAT Admit Card 2026
  • Original Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID Card
  • Driving Licence
  • Government-Issued Photo Identity Proof
  • Passport-size Photograph (same as uploaded in the application form, if required)
  • PwD Certificate (for eligible candidates)
  • Any additional document specified by NTA

Candidates without valid identity proof and admit card may not be allowed to appear for the examination.

Exam-Day Instructions for NTA JIPMAT 2026

Candidates should strictly follow the examination guidelines:

  • Reach the examination centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card.
  • Carry a printed admit card and original photo ID proof.
  • Cooperate with security checks and biometric verification procedures.
  • Follow all instructions issued by NTA and examination officials.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, Bluetooth devices, calculators, earphones, or other electronic gadgets.
  • Avoid carrying study material, notes, books, or written papers into the examination hall.
  • Sit only at the allotted workstation.
  • Do not indulge in unfair practices or malpractice.
  • Follow all computer-based test instructions displayed on the screen.
  • Retain the admit card for counselling and admission-related procedures.
