Karnataka Forest Department Group D Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Karnataka Forest Department, under the Office of the Chief Conservator of Forests and Selection Authority, has released Notification, inviting online applications for direct recruitment to 675 Forest Observer (Group 'D') posts across Karnataka for the year 2026-27. Candidates who have passed SSLC (Class 10) or an equivalent examination can apply online at aranya.gov.in from 01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026. Selection is based on SSLC marks followed by Physical Standard, Endurance, and Efficiency Tests - there is no written examination for this post.

Karnataka Forest Observer Recruitment: Eligibility Criteria

The candidate must be an Indian citizen. A male candidate with more than one living wife, or a woman who has married a man who already has a wife, is not eligible for appointment without prior permission of the Government. The candidate must be mentally and physically fit, and free from any physical defect that could hinder the efficient discharge of duties. The Selection Authority reserves full discretion to reject a candidate found medically unfit, based on a Medical Board's report.

Karnataka Forest Observer Educational Qualification

Candidates must have passed the SSLC examination conducted by the Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board, or an equivalent Class 10 qualification, as on the last date fixed for submission of application. The following qualifications are recognised as equivalent to SSLC: Class 10 examination conducted by CBSE or ICSE boards Class 10 examination conducted by the education boards of other State Governments Secondary-level course conducted by the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) Secondary-level course (KOS) conducted by the Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board Candidates who do not hold the prescribed qualification, or who applied and appeared in the recruitment process while awaiting SSLC (or equivalent) results, are not eligible; no qualification other than the ones listed above will be considered.

Karnataka Forest Observer Age Limit

Minimum age: candidates must have completed 18 years as on the date of the notification. As a one-time measure, a 5-year relaxation in the above maximum age limits has been granted vide Government Order No. CiAsuI 262 SoNeNi 2025 dated 29.01.2026, applicable to all category candidates participating in the selection process for notifications issued up to 31.12.2027. Age relaxation for ex-servicemen: the maximum age limit applicable to their category, plus the number of years of service rendered in the armed forces plus 3 years. Age limit for existing Forest Observers: Forest Observers who have completed a minimum of 5 years of continuous service in the Forest Department and are currently serving on honorarium are eligible under a separate, higher age limit - General: 40 years; Category 2A/2B/3A/3B: 42 years; SC/ST/Category-I: 43 years.

Karnataka Forest Observer Selection Process

There is no written examination for the Forest Observer post. The selection process consists of the following stages: Merit List: Prepared category-wise based on the marks obtained by candidates in SSLC or an equivalent qualifying examination, in the ratio of 1:20 (posts to candidates). Physical Standard Test (PST): Candidates shortlisted in the merit list are checked against the prescribed height, chest measurement/expansion (for men), and height/weight (for women) standards. Physical Endurance Test (PET) and Physical Efficiency Test: Candidates who clear the Physical Standard Test undergo endurance and efficiency tests as notified by the Department from time to time. Final Selection List: Prepared category-wise in the ratio of 1:1 (post to candidate) based on performance in the above stages. Karnataka Forest Department Group D Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Candidates must use only their own Aadhaar (UID) number while applying online. Whichever certificates/documents are uploaded at the time of application will be considered at every stage of the selection process; these cannot be changed later. Candidates claiming Local Forest Dwelling Scheduled Tribe reservation must carefully fill the relevant reservation column online; candidates are responsible for any errors here. Candidates dismissed from any Central/State Government department or government/quasi-government body are not eligible for this recruitment. Members of organisations declared unlawful, or those in close contact with such organisations, are not eligible. Candidates involved in anti-social activities or found guilty of an offence are not eligible. Candidates debarred (temporarily or permanently) from appearing in any government examination/selection are not eligible for any Forest Department recruitment. Submission of fake documents, impersonation, or furnishing false information leads to cancellation of candidature, criminal proceedings, and a 10-year debarment from Karnataka Forest Department recruitment as per Rule 20 of the Karnataka Civil Services (General Recruitment) Rules, 1977. Selected candidates will be governed by the Contributory Pension Scheme as per Government Order No. FD SPL 04 PET 2005 dated 31.03.2006. The Selection Authority's decision on all matters related to selection is final; all legal disputes fall under the jurisdiction of the Karnataka State Administrative Tribunal. The number of notified posts and reservation categories are subject to change; any changes will be published on the official website. Candidates must keep the mobile number and email ID provided in the online application active until the recruitment process is complete.
