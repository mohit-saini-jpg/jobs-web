How to Apply

  • 1Visit gpsc-ojas.gujarat.gov.in and complete One Time Registration (OTR) if not done already. OTR is mandatory but is not itself an application - you must separately apply for each advertisement.
  • 2On the Menu Bar, click "Online Application" → "Apply" and select the relevant advertisement.
  • 3Enter your OTR Registration Number and Date of Birth, then click Apply.
  • 4Fill in Personal Details, Communication Details, Other Details, Language Details, Education Details, and Experience Details carefully. Red asterisk (*) fields are mandatory.
  • 5Upload your passport-size photograph (white background, with date, 5cm × 3.6cm, JPG, max 10 KB) and signature (white paper, black/blue ink, 2.5cm × 7.5cm, JPG, max 10 KB).
  • 6Review all details before clicking SAVE/SUBMIT. Note your Application Number and Confirmation Number that appear on screen immediately after.
  • 7Pay the application fee (if applicable) and take a printout of the confirmed application.
  • 8Applications remain editable until June 19, 2026 at 11:59 PM. Use the "Edit" option to make corrections - do not submit a fresh application.
  • 9Complete the Consent Form and Consent Deposit (separate process) before June 20, 2026 at 10:00 AM.

FAQs

Q1How many total vacancies are announced for GPSC AE 2026?
A
A total of 235 vacancies have been announced - 200 for AE (Civil) under the Road and Building Department (Advt. 24/2026-27), 11 for AE (Mechanical) under Gujarat Maritime Board (Advt. 30/2026-27), and 24 for AE (Civil) under Gujarat Maritime Board (Advt. 31/2026-27).
Q2What is the last date to apply for GPSC AE 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is June 19, 2026 at 11:59 PM. Fee payment at Post Offices is accepted until June 20, 2026.
Q3What is the Consent Deposit and why is it required?
A
The Consent Deposit is a refundable amount (₹500 for General male / ₹400 for reserved/women/PwD/Ex-servicemen) that candidates must pay to confirm their intent to appear in the exam. Candidates who appear in the Preliminary Test get this amount refunded. Those who do not appear forfeit the deposit. The window to pay the Consent Deposit is June 5 to June 20, 2026 - missing this deadline leads to cancellation of your application.
Q4Are candidates selected for Gujarat Maritime Board posts government employees?
A
No. Candidates selected for Advt. 30 and 31 (Gujarat Maritime Board posts) will be employees of the Gujarat Maritime Board, not of the Government of Gujarat. Service conditions, pay administration, and applicable rules will be those of the Maritime Board.
Q5What is the selection process for GPSC AE 2026?
A
Selection is through a Preliminary Test (300 marks, 300 questions, 180 minutes) followed by a Personal Interview. Final merit is based on a 50:50 weighted score - 50% from the Preliminary Test and 50% from the Interview. Candidates scoring less than 15% in the Preliminary Test are not eligible for the Interview.
Q6Can reserved category candidates apply for unreserved posts?
A
Yes, reserved category candidates can apply for unreserved posts, but they will be assessed on unreserved category standards (eligibility, age limit) without any relaxations when competing for those posts.
Q7What is the upper age limit and relaxation criteria for the latest GPSC AE Recruitment 2026 notification for the OBC (SEBC) category?
A
The maximum age limit is 35 years as on June 19, 2026 (last date of application). For SEBC (OBC) male candidates of Gujarat origin, there is a 5-year age relaxation (maximum up to 45 years). For SEBC women candidates of Gujarat origin, the relaxation is 10 years (maximum up to 45 years). Reserved category candidates applying for unreserved posts do not receive age relaxation.
Q8What is the in-hand salary, basic pay, and grade pay for a newly recruited Assistant Engineer (Civil/Mechanical) based on the latest GPSC AE 2026 notification?
A
Selected candidates are placed in Pay Level-8 of the 7th Pay Commission Pay Matrix with a pay scale of ₹44,900 – ₹1,42,400 per month. The starting basic pay is ₹44,900/-. In addition to basic pay, candidates receive DA, HRA, TA, and other allowances as per Gujarat government rules. Note: Candidates selected under Gujarat Maritime Board ads (30 & 31) are GMB employees, not Government of Gujarat employees, which affects service conditions.
Q9What is the upper age limit and relaxation criteria for the latest GPSC AE Recruitment 2026 notification for the General (Unreserved) Women category?
A
The upper age limit for General (Unreserved) Women candidates is 35 years as on June 19, 2026. However, there is a 5-year age relaxation for women candidates, making the maximum age 45 years.
Q10What is the upper age limit and relaxation criteria for the latest GPSC AE Recruitment 2026 notification for the SC Male (Gujarat Origin) category?
A
The upper age limit for SC Male (Gujarat Origin) candidates is 35 years as on June 19, 2026. There is a 5-year age relaxation for SC/ST/SEBC/EWS Male candidates of Gujarat Origin, making the maximum age 45 years.
