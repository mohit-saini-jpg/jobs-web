Assam HS Result 2026 Date (Expected)

The Assam Higher Secondary Education Council (AHSEC) is expected to declare the Assam HS Result 2026 between April 20 and April 25, 2026. Students who appeared for the Class 12 board exams can check their results online through official portals.

While the board has not confirmed an exact date yet, trends from previous years suggest the result is usually announced in the last week of April.

Official Websites to Check AHSEC 12th Result

Students should only rely on official and trusted websites to check their results:

  • ahsec.assam.gov.in
  • resultsassam.nic.in

Tip: Due to heavy traffic, websites may slow down. Try refreshing or checking alternative official links.

How to Check Assam HS Result 2026 Online

Follow these simple steps to download your result:

  • Visit the official website: resultsassam.nic.in
  • Click on the link “Assam HS Result 2026”
  • Enter your roll number and required details
  • Click on the submit button
  • Your result will appear on the screen
  • Download and save the marksheet for future reference

Details Mentioned on Assam HS Marksheet

After downloading your result, verify the following details:

  • Student name
  • Roll number
  • Subject-wise marks
  • Total marks
  • Division (First/Second/Third)
  • Result status (Pass/Fail)

If any discrepancy is found, contact your school authorities immediately.
