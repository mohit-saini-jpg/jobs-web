The last date for the receipt of applications for the PAU Research Fellow position is June 1, 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline to avoid any last-minute issues.

Q2. What is the salary offered for the Research Fellow post?

The selected candidate for the Research Fellow post will receive a fixed salary of Rs. 31,000 per month, plus House Rent Allowance (HRA) as per university rules.

Candidates must possess a B.Sc. or B.Sc. (Agriculture) degree with a minimum OCPA of 6.00/10.00 or 60% marks. Additionally, an M.Sc. in Soil Science or Botany with a minimum OCPA of 6.50/10.00 or 65% marks is required. NET qualification is mandatory for M.Sc. candidates.

The official notification does not specify any age limit for this recruitment. However, it is always advisable to check the detailed notification for any specific guidelines or relaxations.

Q6. What is the application fee for the PAU Research Fellow recruitment?

The application fee for both General/OBC and SC/ST categories is Rs. 200/-. This fee needs to be submitted along with the application form as per the instructions in the notification.

Q7. What is the selection process for the Research Fellow role?

The selection process will primarily involve an Interview and Document Verification. Candidates who meet the eligibility criteria will be shortlisted for the interview.
