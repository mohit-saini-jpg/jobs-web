How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply offline only using Prescribed Application Proforma (Annexure-II). Applications must be submitted through proper channel only - via the Employer/Head of Office/Forwarding Authority. Applications sent directly by the candidate without being forwarded through proper channel will not be considered. Incomplete applications or those not accompanied by required documents will not be considered.
  • Steps to Apply:
  • Step 1: Download Application Proforma (Annexure-II) and eligibility conditions (Annexure-I) from www.nclt.gov.in under "Career".
  • Step 2: Fill in Annexure-II completely. Attach self-attested passport size coloured photograph. State clearly in Section 19 how you are best suited for the post.
  • Step 3: Attach all required documents (see list below).
  • Step 4: Submit the complete application to your Employer/Head of Office/Forwarding Authority for certification and forwarding.
  • Step 5: Forwarding authority sends the complete application to the Secretary, NCLT on or before the last date.
  • Documents Required (to be submitted through proper channel):
  • Bio-data in Prescribed Proforma - Annexure-II (completely filled)
  • Supporting certificates/documents for claimed educational qualifications including essential Bachelor's Degree
  • Cadre Clearance Certificate - certifying the officer will be relieved immediately upon selection
  • Attested photocopies of APARs/ACRs for the last 5 years - attested by Under Secretary rank or above
  • Vigilance Clearance Certificate issued by the respective department
  • Integrity Certificate issued by the respective department
  • Details of major/minor penalties imposed during the last 10 years
  • Employer/Head of Office forwarding certificate (as per Annexure-II format) confirming: no vigilance/disciplinary/criminal case pending; integrity certified; APAR enclosed; no penalty in last 10 years; cadre authority has no objection
  • Send Complete Application By Post To: The Secretary, NCLT National Company Law Tribunal 6th Floor, Block No. 3, C.G.O. Complex Lodhi Road, New Delhi – 110003
  • For queries: Tel: 011-24361685 | Email: [email protected]

Important Instructions

  • Applications must be submitted through proper channel only - via Employer/Head of Office/Forwarding Authority. Direct applications will not be accepted. Candidates who apply will NOT be allowed to withdraw their application subsequently. Candidates unwilling to join upon selection must NOT apply. Absorption into NCLT is NOT a matter of right. It will be done only based on NCLT's need and as per NCLT Recruitment Rules 2020. Absorption can be considered only after 1 year of satisfactory deputation. Vacancy position is tentative and may increase or decrease. Selected candidates will serve only at the specific NCLT Bench for which they are selected. GPRA benefit: Only officers already availing GPRA in their parent body can continue this benefit after joining NCLT on deputation. Others are not eligible to apply for GPRA on joining NCLT. Cadre authorities must verify that all particulars sent by their officers are correct as per official records before forwarding applications. Pre-01.01.2016 regular service will be treated as service in the corresponding level of the 7th CPC pay matrix for eligibility determination. NCLT Recruitment Rules 2020 are available on www.nclt.gov.in under "Career" - candidates must read these before applying. Applications not accompanied by all required documents, or received after the last date, will not be considered under any circumstances. All corrigenda/updates will be published on www.nclt.gov.in - visit regularly.
