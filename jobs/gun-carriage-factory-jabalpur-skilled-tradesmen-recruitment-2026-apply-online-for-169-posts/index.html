How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through https://vacancy.parasnet.com. No offline applications will be accepted.

Email ID and mobile number should be kept active until the completion of the entire recruitment process.
  • Admit Cards/Call Letters will be issued to shortlisted candidates only through Email/online application portal. Shortlisted candidates will also be informed via SMS/Email for Trade Test/Practical Test.

Important Instructions

  • Candidates must ensure they fulfil all eligibility conditions before applying; admission to all stages is purely provisional. Applications not meeting eligibility criteria or with false/incorrect/incomplete information or bogus documents will be summarily rejected. Selected candidates must undergo a medical fitness test before joining. Candidates should regularly visit the online portal and official websites for updates; GCF will not be responsible for late/non-receipt of call letters. Mere issue of a call letter/admit card does not imply final acceptance of candidature. Verification of eligibility with reference to original documents will be done only at the time of document verification in the factory. Self-declaration on detailed experience (industry, machines worked on, jobs manufactured, programming knowledge) must be submitted; failing this, candidates will not be called for Practical Test/Interview. PVR (Attestation Form) in triplicate must be submitted on the date of joining; verified PVR must be received within 3 months (maximum 4 months); non-compliance will result in discontinuation. Beware of unscrupulous elements offering appointment through illegal gratification; the entire selection is done on merit in a transparent manner.
