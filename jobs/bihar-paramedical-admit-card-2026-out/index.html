Bihar Paramedical Admit Card 2026 Out

Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) has officially released the Bihar Paramedical Admit Card 2026 for the Diploma Certificate Entrance Competitive Examination (DCECE) paramedical streams. The admit cards are now available online for candidates registered for Paramedical (PM) and Paramedical Intermediate (PMI/PMM) courses.

As per the latest notice and media reports, the Bihar paramedical entrance exam 2026 for PM and PMI/PMM will be conducted on 23 May and 24 May 2026 at various exam centres across the state. Candidates must download their hall ticket and carry a printed copy along with a valid photo ID to the examination centre; entry will not be allowed without the admit card.

How to Download Bihar Paramedical Admit Card 2026

Follow these steps to download the DCECE Paramedical hall ticket 2026:

  • Visit the official BCECEB website: https://bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, under “Online Application Forms”, click on the link “Download Admit Card of DCECE (PM/PMI)‑2026” (paramedical).
  • A new login page on the DCECE online portal will open.
  • Log in using either: Email ID and Password, or Registration Number and Date of Birth, as per the options shown on the screen.
  • Email ID and Password, or
  • Registration Number and Date of Birth, as per the options shown on the screen.
  • After entering the security code/captcha, click on “Sign In / Submit”.
  • Your Bihar Paramedical Admit Card 2026 will be displayed on the screen; check all details carefully.
  • Download the PDF and take 2-3 clear A4‑size printouts of the admit card for exam‑day use and future reference.

If you face issues while logging in, recheck your registration details or contact BCECEB helpdesk using the contact details given on the official website.

Details Mentioned on Bihar Paramedical Admit Card 2026

Candidates should ensure that the following information on the hall ticket is correct:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph.
  • Father’s/mother’s name and basic personal details.
  • Registration number and/or roll number.
  • Exam name: DCECE (Paramedical) 2026 - PM/PMI/PMM.
  • Course group applied for (PM or PMI/PMM).
  • Exam date and shift time (23 or 24 May 2026; the exact time and shift are printed on the admit card).
  • Exam centre name, code and full address.
  • Reporting time and instructions regarding entry to the exam hall.

If any discrepancy is found (e.g., wrong name, photo, category, or exam centre), the candidate should contact BCECEB immediately before the exam date.

Documents Required with Bihar Paramedical Admit Card 2026

On the day of the Bihar DCECE Paramedical exam 2026, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the Bihar Paramedical Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Voter ID Driving Licence Any other government‑issued ID accepted as per BCECEB instructions.
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID
  • Driving Licence
  • Any other government‑issued ID accepted as per BCECEB instructions.
  • Additional documents if specified in the prospectus/admit card (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter for candidates using scribe facility).

Entry to the exam centre will not be allowed without the printed hall ticket and a valid photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for Bihar Paramedical Admit Card 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the Bihar DCECE Paramedical (PM/PMI/PMM) Entrance Exam 2026, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the Bihar Paramedical Admit Card 2026 so that verification, biometric/ID checks and seating can be completed; late entry after the scheduled time or gate‑closing time may not be allowed.
  • Carry only essential items: your Bihar Paramedical admit card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout), a valid photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Driving Licence, Passport or other government‑approved ID) and permitted stationery such as blue/black ball‑point pens and other items allowed as per BCECEB instructions.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not permitted), Bluetooth devices, earphones, notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or written material inside the examination hall; such items are strictly prohibited and may lead to cancellation of your paper and further disciplinary action.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the Bihar Paramedical admit card and those announced by the invigilators during the exam; indulging in any unfair means, talking to other candidates, or disobeying exam rules can result in cancellation of the examination and possible debarment from future exams.
  • Keep your Bihar Paramedical admit card safely even after the exam, as it may be required during counselling, document verification and admission formalities.
