FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for TMC Female Nurse ?
A
The walk-in interview for the Female Nurse position at Tata Memorial Centre is scheduled for 08 June 2026. Candidates are advised to reach the venue on time with all necessary documents.
Q2What are the educational qualifications required for the Female Nurse post?
A
Candidates should possess General Nursing Midwifery and/or B.Sc. (Nursing) to be eligible for the Female Nurse (Locum/Project/Adhoc/Contract) position at TMC.
Q3What is the age limit for this recruitment?
A
The maximum age limit for candidates applying for the Female Nurse post is 40 years. There is no minimum age limit specified in the provided data.
Q4What is the salary offered for the Female Nurse position?
A
The salary offered ranges from Rs. 33,000/- (for GNM) to Rs. 35,000/- (for B.Sc. Nursing) per month. Additionally, Rs. 1,500/- is provided per completed year of experience, up to a maximum of 4 years.
Q5What is the application fee for this recruitment?
A
There is no application fee for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive, as indicated in the provided data.
Q6What is the selection process for the Female Nurse role?
A
The selection process includes an MCQ Test (if there are more applications), followed by a Walk-in Interview, and Document Verification.
Q7What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents, one set of self-attested photocopies (ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates if applicable, category certificate if applicable), and recent passport-size photographs.
Q8Where is the walk-in interview venue?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at Tata Memorial Centre, located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, with a pincode of 400012. The specific address details would be in the official notification.
Q9Is this a permanent job?
A
The job type for this recruitment is contractual, meaning the positions are for a specific period as Locum/Project/Adhoc/Contract roles.
