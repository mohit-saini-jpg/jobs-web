Visvesvaraya Technological University has announced the VTU Result 2026 for multiple UG and PG programmes for all regions through its online result portal. The latest update includes M.Tech, M.Plan and M.Sc 3rd semester results, BBA and BCA results, updated BE results (including BE 5th semester) and results for B.Arch, B.Sc (Hons), B.Plan and B.Design. VTU Result 2026 has been announced online on the official website - vtu.ac.in - via the central result page where students can select their course and semester.

Students can check and download their VTU marksheet by visiting the result link, choosing the relevant exam (for example BE 5th semester, M.Tech 3rd semester or B.Arch), entering their USN and submitting the form to view their subject wise grades. After downloading the online result, candidates must verify their name, USN, semester, scheme and SGPA/CGPA and immediately contact their college examination section or VTU if they find any discrepancy.

VTU Results 2026 Key Highlights

Steps to Check VTU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your VTU Result 2026 for BE, BBA, BCA, B.Arch, B.Sc (Hons), B.Plan, B.Design or M.Tech/M.Plan/M.Sc 3rd semester:

  • Open the VTU result page: https://results.vtu.ac.in/indexD5J6.php.
  • On the page, select the relevant result link, such as: M.Tech/M.Plan/M.Sc 3rd semester results (all regions) BBA & BCA results (all regions) BE updated results / BE 5th semester results (all regions) B.Arch results (all regions) B.Sc (Hons), B.Plan and B.Design results (all regions).
  • M.Tech/M.Plan/M.Sc 3rd semester results (all regions)
  • BBA & BCA results (all regions)
  • BE updated results / BE 5th semester results (all regions)
  • B.Arch results (all regions)
  • B.Sc (Hons), B.Plan and B.Design results (all regions).
  • After clicking your course link, you will be taken to the result form.
  • Enter your USN (University Seat Number) carefully in the given field.
  • Click on Submit / View Result to display your subject wise grades, total credits, SGPA and CGPA (if applicable).
  • Download or print the online result for your academic record, revaluation applications, placement processes and higher education documentation.
