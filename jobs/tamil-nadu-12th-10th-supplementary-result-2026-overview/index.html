Tamil Nadu 12th Supplementary Result 2026: Key Facts

  • Conducting body: Directorate of Government Examinations (DGE), Tamil Nadu
  • Result applicable to: Private/arrear candidates who did not clear one or more subjects in the main March-April 2026 board exam, and improvement candidates
  • Official websites: tnresults.nic.in and dge.tn.gov.in
  • Overall pass percentage in the main (non-supplementary) TN 12th exam 2026: 95.20%

A separate, confirmed pass percentage specifically for the 2026 supplementary exam is not available in the source material at this time. This article will be updated once the DGE releases that figure.

How to Check TN 12th, 10th Supplementary Result 2026 Online

  • Visit the official website - tnresults.nic.in or dge.tn.gov.in.
  • Click on the link for "TN 12th Supplementary Result 2026" or "TN 10th Supplementary Result 2026," as applicable.
  • Enter your registration/hall ticket number and date of birth.
  • Submit the details to view your provisional mark statement.
  • Download and save the result page, and take a printout for reference.

If the official site is slow due to heavy traffic, candidates can try the alternate official portal (tnresults.nic.in or dge.tn.gov.in, whichever is not being used) as a backup.

Details Shown on the TN Supplementary Marksheet

  • Candidate's name, registration number, and date of birth
  • Subject-wise marks obtained in the supplementary exam
  • Overall result status (pass/fail)

The source material does not specify whether the provisional marksheet also displays a grade or division for the supplementary exam. Candidates should refer to the official document for this detail.

What to Do After the Result

  • Original marksheet: After the result is declared, candidates typically collect their original marksheet from their respective schools, as has been the practice in previous years.
  • Revaluation/re-totalling: In past years, DGE Tamil Nadu has allowed candidates to apply for re-totalling or revaluation of answer scripts for a fee, with the request processed within a stated turnaround time. The exact fee amount and schedule for the 2026 supplementary result have not been officially confirmed in the source material reviewed for this article - candidates should check dge.tn.gov.in for the current notification before applying.
  • Answer script copies: In previous years, candidates could apply for a copy of their answer script at the District Government Examinations office within a specified window after the result. Confirmed 2026 dates and fees for this were not available at the time of writing.

Important Instructions

  • The result available online immediately after declaration is provisional. The original marksheet is issued later through the respective school.
  • Keep your hall ticket/registration number safe until you have downloaded and printed your result.
  • Cross-check all details (name, registration number, subject marks) on the downloaded marksheet, and report any discrepancy to your school or the DGE office promptly.
  • Any information not covered above - including exact fee amounts, revaluation windows, or subject-wise pass marks for 2026 - should be verified directly on dge.tn.gov.in, as it was not confirmed in the source available for this article.
