Qualification / Eligibility

Administrative Officer: Regular MBA/PG degree in Management/Administration from a recognized university or Company Secretaryship (under ICSI registration). LLB desirable. Total 15 years experience in handling Administration/HR matters of a reputed organization (5 years in middle or senior supervisory position). Good writing and oral communication skills in Malayalam and English. (Internal candidates and retired personnel with same qualifications and experience also eligible with specific conditions) Accounts Executive: Regular MBA(Finance)/M.Com (Regular) from a reputed institution with minimum 3 years experience in management accounting/project accounting/reporting, project team co-ordination/project analysis/data analysis OR CA/CMA Intermediate with 1 year experience in management accounting/project accounting/reporting, project team co-ordination/project analysis/data analysis. Proficiency in computerized accounting software and IT skills.
