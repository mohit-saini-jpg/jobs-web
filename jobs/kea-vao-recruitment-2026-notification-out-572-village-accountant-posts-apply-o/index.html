KEA VAO Selection Process 2026

Selection to the post of Village Accountant is made purely through a competitive written examination; the notification does not mention any separate interview round. The process is as follows: Candidates will appear for a competitive written examination conducted in Offline-OMR mode. A minimum of 35% marks in the competitive exam is required for eligibility; candidates scoring below this will be disqualified. Candidates opting for the Kannada language exam exemption must have passed Kannada as first or second language up to SSLC level, or must clear a compulsory qualifying Kannada language test (150 marks, minimum 50 marks required to qualify). This paper is not counted toward merit ranking. District-wise merit lists / marks lists will be prepared and forwarded by KEA to the respective District Magistrates for appointment, which is made at the district level.

KEA VAO Exam Pattern 2026

The written examination consists of two papers, each carrying 100 marks and lasting 2 hours, with negative marking of 0.25 marks for every wrong answer. Each question offers 4 answer options with only one correct answer. A minimum qualifying score of 35% is required in the competitive exam.
