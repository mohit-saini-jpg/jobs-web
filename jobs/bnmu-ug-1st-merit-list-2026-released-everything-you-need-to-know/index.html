BNMU UG 1st Merit List 2026 Released - Everything You Need to Know

Bhupendra Narayan Mandal University (BNMU), Madhepura has officially released the UG 1st Merit List 2026 for admission to the first semester of undergraduate courses (BA, BSc, BCom) under the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) Four-Year Degree Programme for the academic session 2026-2030. Candidates who applied online through the SAMARTH portal can now download the merit list in PDF format from the official website at bnmu.ac.in or the admission portal at admissions.bnmuumis.in. Those whose names appear in the first merit list must complete all admission formalities - including document verification and fee payment - between 13 June 2026 and 18 June 2026.

How to Download the BNMU UG 1st Merit List 2026 - Step-by-Step Guide

Downloading the BNMU UG merit list is straightforward if you follow the correct steps. Here is a simple, step-by-step process to access and download the BNMU 2026 merit list PDF from the official portal.

Method 1: Via admissions.bnmuumis.in

  • Open your browser and visit https://admissions.bnmuumis.in
  • On the homepage, click on the "View Merit List" link.
  • You will be redirected to the merit list download page.
  • Select your details from the drop-down menus: Academic Session Degree Type Degree (BA / BSc / BCom) Subject
  • Academic Session
  • Degree Type
  • Degree (BA / BSc / BCom)
  • Subject
  • Click the "Search" button.
  • Find your subject from the results and click to download the PDF from the BNMU server.
  • Open the PDF and use Ctrl+F (on PC) or Find in Page (on mobile) to search your name or application number.

Method 2: Via SAMARTH Portal (Allotment Letter)

  • Visit https://bnmuadm.samarth.edu.in
  • Click on "Login" on the homepage.
  • Log in using your registered Email ID and Password.
  • After logging in, click on "Allotment Letter" to download your individual offer letter.

Tip: Keep your application number or registration credentials ready before visiting either portal to speed up the process.

How BNMU UG Merit List 2026 Is Prepared

Understanding the selection methodology can help applicants gauge their chances and plan accordingly. The BNMU UG merit list for 2026 is prepared entirely on the basis of academic merit - there is no entrance examination.

The merit list is generated taking into account the following factors:

  • Class 12 (Intermediate) marks from the Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB), CBSE, or any other recognized State/National Board
  • Candidate's category (General, OBC, SC, ST, PH)
  • University admission rules and seat availability per college and course

Since selection is 100% merit-based, candidates with higher Class 12 scores have a stronger chance of securing admission in their preferred college and subject combination.

Eligibility Criteria for BNMU UG Admission 2026

Before confirming your admission, it is important to verify that you meet the BNMU UG eligibility requirements for 2026-30. Here is a summary of the eligibility conditions:

  • Candidates must have passed Class 12 (Intermediate) or an equivalent examination from a recognized board.
  • For BA (Arts stream): Passed 10+2 in Arts or equivalent from a recognized board.
  • For BSc (Science stream): Passed 10+2 in Science (I.Sc.) from BSEB or equivalent from any approved national/state board.
  • For BCom (Commerce stream): Passed 10+2 in Commerce or equivalent from a recognized board.
  • A general minimum of 50% marks is expected (45% for reserved category candidates; verify exact cutoff with the official notification).
  • Candidates must meet the subject-specific prerequisites for the stream they have chosen.

Documents Required for BNMU UG Admission 2026

Candidates selected in the BNMU UG 1st Merit List 2026 must bring the following original documents along with self-attested photocopies to their allotted college during the admission window (13-18 June 2026):

  • Class 10th Marksheet and Certificate
  • Class 12th Marksheet and Provisional/Original Pass Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) / School Leaving Certificate
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable - for OBC, SC, ST candidates)
  • Income Certificate (if applicable - for fee concessions)
  • Recent Passport-Size Photographs
  • Valid Photo Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Printout of the BNMU UG Merit List / Allotment Letter
  • Fee Payment Receipt (printed after online payment)

Carry both original documents and scanned/photocopies for verification. Incomplete document submission can delay or cancel your admission.

Admission Process After Checking the BNMU 1st Merit List

Once you have confirmed that your name appears in the BNMU UG 1st Merit List 2026, you need to follow a specific sequence of steps to lock in your seat. Here is what to do next:

  • Verify your details in the merit list carefully - check your name, allotted college, course, and category.
  • Download your Allotment Letter from the SAMARTH portal at bnmuadm.samarth.edu.in.
  • Log in to the student portal to confirm your admission online.
  • Pay the admission fee online within the stipulated window (13-18 June 2026) using Credit Card, Debit Card, or Net Banking.
  • Report to your allotted college with all original documents for physical verification.
  • Collect your admission confirmation or receipt - keep it safely for future reference.

What If Your Name Is Not in the BNMU UG 1st Merit List 2026?

Not appearing in the first merit list does not mean you have lost your chance at admission. BNMU typically releases multiple rounds of merit lists based on the number of vacancies remaining after each round.

  • 2nd Merit List: Released after the first admission window closes (dates to be announced).
  • 3rd Merit List (if required): Released after the second round.
  • Candidates not selected in earlier rounds will automatically be considered for subsequent merit lists based on their original application.

Do not withdraw your application and keep monitoring the official BNMU website for the next merit list release date.
