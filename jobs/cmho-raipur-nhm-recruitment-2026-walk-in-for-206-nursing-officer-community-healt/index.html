CMHO Raipur Recruitment 2026 Salary Structure

Salary (consolidated monthly remuneration) ranges from Rs. 8,800 to Rs. 31,500 depending on the post, as detailed in the vacancy table above. No allowance or facility other than the fixed monthly remuneration as per the prescribed ROP (Record of Proceedings) will be payable during the contract period.

CMHO Raipur Recruitment 2026 Application Fee

Fee must be paid via a Demand Draft (DD) from any Nationalized Bank, issued in favour of "Jila Swasthya Samiti, Raipur" (District Health Committee, Raipur), and attached with the application. The fee is non-refundable. DDs issued in any other name will not be accepted. Candidates applying for more than one post must submit a separate application and separate DD for each post. The DD number, date, and amount must be mentioned in the application form. Applications without the fee (DD) will be rejected.

CMHO Raipur NHM Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Download the application form from the notification or the district website: www.raipur.gov.in
  • 2Fill the application form completely, attach a self-attested passport-size photograph, and sign the declaration.
  • 3Attach self-attested photocopies of all required certificates, mark sheets, and experience proof.
  • 4Obtain a Demand Draft of the prescribed fee amount, drawn in favour of "Jila Swasthya Samiti, Raipur" from any Nationalized Bank.
  • 5Mention the DD number, date, and amount in the application form.
  • 6Report to the designated walk-in venue on the scheduled date for the applied post, by 9:00 AM, carrying original documents and self-attested copies.
  • 7Complete registration, document verification, and the written/skill exam as per the daily time schedule.
  • 8Candidates applying for more than one post must submit a separate application and a separate DD for each post, on the respective scheduled dates.
