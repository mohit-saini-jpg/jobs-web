Kurukshetra University PG Diploma Datesheet 2026 - Quick : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 10/07/2026

Kurukshetra University Kurukshetra (KUK) has issued Notification N-68, rescheduling the examination dates for the P.G. Diploma in Buddhist Studies programme. The revised dates apply to all three papers of the course, and candidates are advised to check the updated schedule before their exams. This article covers the complete Kurukshetra University Buddhist Studies datesheet 2026, including old dates, revised dates, paper codes, and official notification details.

  • University: Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra (KUK)
  • Notification Number: N-68
  • Endorsement Number: CT-IV/2026/30053-063
  • Notification Date: 08-07-2026
  • Course: P.G. Diploma in Buddhist Studies
  • Reason for Revision: Recommendation of the Director, Centre for Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Studies, Kurukshetra University Kurukshetra

Kurukshetra University Buddhist Studies Revised Datesheet 2026 - Important Dates

The table below lists the original exam dates as well as the revised dates notified under N-68 for the PG Diploma in Buddhist Studies (CBCS) programme.

ClassDate Already NotifiedRevised Date of Examination
P.G. Dip in Buddhist Studies17-Jul-202627-Jul-2026

All three papers have been pushed forward by 10 to 12 days from their originally notified dates. The exam timing for each paper will remain the same as notified earlier; only the dates have changed.

Who Does This Notification Apply To?

This datesheet revision applies only to candidates enrolled in the P.G. Diploma in Buddhist Studies (CBCS) programme at Kurukshetra University who are appearing for papers BS-101, BS-102, and BS-103.

Distribution of the Notification

As per the endorsement list, copies of the notification have been forwarded for information and necessary action to:

  • Director, Centre for Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Studies, K.U. Kurukshetra - to inform concerned candidates immediately
  • Director, I.T. Cell, K.U. Kurukshetra - to upload the notification on the university website
  • DY. AI (Exams-I), Kurukshetra University - to inform concerned candidates immediately
  • DR/AR (Secrecy), Kurukshetra University
  • Superintendent (Confidential/Practical/UMC), Kurukshetra University
  • Steno to Controller of Examinations, Kurukshetra University
  • CT-I, II & III (Conduct Theory Section), Kurukshetra University

Important Instructions for Candidates

  • Note that only the exam dates have changed; the exam timing remains the same as notified earlier.
  • Candidates should check the official Kurukshetra University website regularly for the latest updates related to this datesheet.
  • Carry the admit card and required documents as per the university's existing examination guidelines. Specific document requirements are not mentioned in this notification.
  • The official notification does not mention any change in exam centres, so candidates should assume centres remain as previously allotted unless informed otherwise by the university.
