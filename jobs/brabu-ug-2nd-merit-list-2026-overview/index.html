BRABU UG Admission 2026 - Eligibility and Course Details

Understanding the eligibility criteria is essential before checking the merit list. The BRABU UG Admission 2026 is open for candidates who have cleared their Class 12 (10+2) examination from any recognised board. Below are the course-wise eligibility requirements.

B.A. (Arts): Candidates who have passed the Intermediate (10+2) examination in the IA / ISc / ICom stream from any recognised board are eligible for BA admission.

B.Sc. (Science): Candidates who have passed the 10+2 examination in the Science stream from a recognised board are eligible for BSc admission.

B.Com (Commerce): Candidates who have passed the Intermediate examination from any recognised board with ISc / Science stream or Commerce stream are eligible for BCom admission.

All courses are offered under the 4-year undergraduate programme based on the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS). Admission is strictly merit-based with no entrance examination.

BRABU UG 2nd Merit List 2026 - How to Download

Downloading the BRABU UG 2nd Merit List 2026 is a simple process through the official BRABU website. Follow the steps below to access and save the 2nd Provisional Merit List PDF for your course.

Step-by-Step Process to Download BRABU 2nd Merit List 2026:

  • Open your browser and visit the official BRABU website: brabu.ac.in
  • On the homepage, look for the "UG Admission 2026-30" link in the Latest Notifications or Admissions section.
  • Click on the "UG CBCS (2026-30) 2nd Provisional Merit List" link.
  • A PDF file will open or download automatically to your device.
  • Search for your Application ID or Name in the PDF.
  • If selected, log in to the UMIS Portal at umis.brabu.ac.in using your Application ID and Password.
  • Download your Seat Allotment Letter from the Student Login section.
  • Take a printout of the merit list and allotment letter for use at the time of document verification and fee payment at the allotted college.

Important: Always download the merit list from the official brabu.ac.in website only. Do not trust third-party websites that may display unofficial or fake merit lists.

BRABU UG 2nd Merit List 2026 - Selection Process

The BRABU UG selection process is entirely merit-based, making Class 12 percentage scores the deciding factor for seat allotment. This section explains how the university prepares and releases its merit lists for BA, BSc, and BCom admissions.

  • Selection is based strictly on Class 12 board examination marks.
  • There is no entrance examination for BRABU UG Admission 2026.
  • Candidates with higher marks are given priority in college and subject allotment.
  • The university releases merit lists in rounds - 1st, 2nd, and 3rd - until all seats are filled.
  • Students who do not complete admission after appearing in one merit list will lose their seat, which is then offered to the next eligible candidate in the subsequent merit list.
  • The merit list is available in PDF format, subject-wise and college-wise.

What to Do After Your Name Appears in BRABU 2nd Merit List 2026

If your name appears in the BRABU UG 2nd Merit List 2026, you must act immediately to secure your admission. Here is the step-by-step process to follow after checking the merit list.

  • Download and print the merit list PDF and your Seat Allotment Letter from the UMIS portal.
  • Visit the allotted college within the admission deadline.
  • Carry all required original documents along with their photocopies.
  • Complete document verification at the college.
  • Pay the college admission fee within the prescribed time.
  • Collect your Admission Receipt and keep it safe for future reference.

If you miss the deadline, your seat will be cancelled and allotted to another candidate in the next merit list round.
