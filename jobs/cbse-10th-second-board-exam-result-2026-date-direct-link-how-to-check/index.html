CBSE 10th Second Board Exam Result 2026 - Date, Direct Link & How to Check

How to Check CBSE 10th Second Board Exam Result 2026 Online

This section explains the exact step-by-step process to view and download the result once it goes live on the official portal.

Students can follow these steps to check their CBSE Class 10 Second Board Result 2026:

  • Visit the official CBSE result portal at cbseresults.nic.in
  • Click on the link titled "Secondary School Examination Results 2026"
  • Enter your Roll Number, School Number, Admit Card ID, and Date of Birth
  • Complete the captcha verification
  • Recheck all entered details for accuracy
  • Click the "Submit" button
  • Your result, including subject-wise marks and grades, will appear on screen
  • Download and save a printout for future reference

Best of Two Policy: How CBSE Calculates Final Marks

This section explains how CBSE combines Phase 1 and Phase 2 scores to arrive at a student's final result, which is the most commonly asked question about the second board exam.

Under CBSE's dual-exam system, students could attempt improvement papers in up to three subjects during the second board exam. Once both Phase 1 and Phase 2 results are out, CBSE compares the marks scored in each subject across both attempts individually and retains the higher score for the final result. This means a student's final scorecard will always reflect their best performance per subject, not an average of the two attempts.

What Happens After the Result - Compartment, Re-evaluation & Class 11 Admissions

This section covers the next steps available to students after the result is announced, including options for those who don't clear the exam and the process for marks verification.

Students who are unable to pass even after the second board attempt will be eligible to appear for the CBSE Class 10 Compartment Examination, which is expected to be conducted in July 2026.

Once the result is officially declared, students will be able to apply for:

  • Verification of marks
  • Photocopy of evaluated answer books
  • Re-evaluation of answer scripts

These requests can only be submitted after the official declaration of the second board result, not before.
