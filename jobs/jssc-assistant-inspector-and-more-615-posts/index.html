FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for JSSC JTGLCCE Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to complete online registration and application submission is 14 July 2026. Candidates must also complete fee payment and document upload by 03 July 2026 as per the schedule. It is advisable to apply early to avoid last-minute issues.
Q2How many vacancies are announced under JSSC JTGLCCE 2026?
A
A total of 615 vacancies have been announced across various posts under different departments of the Jharkhand Government. These include technical, scientific, and administrative roles. Additional backlog vacancies are also available under a separate advertisement.
Q3What is the eligibility qualification for JSSC JTGLCCE 2026?
A
The required educational qualification varies depending on the post applied for. Candidates must possess a relevant graduate or postgraduate degree in disciplines like Science, Mathematics, Economics, Agriculture, Fisheries, Dairy Technology, or Pharmacy. All qualifications must be completed before the last date of application.
Q4What is the age limit for JSSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 21 years for all posts, while the maximum age ranges from 35 to 40 years depending on category. Age relaxation is provided for reserved categories, PwD candidates, and ex-servicemen as per Jharkhand Government rules. Age will be calculated as on 01 August 2025.
Q5What is the selection process for JSSC JTGLCCE 2026?
A
The selection process includes a Computer Based Test (CBT) conducted in two stages: Preliminary Exam and Main Exam. In some cases, the preliminary exam may be skipped if the number of candidates is low. Final merit is based on marks obtained in the Main Examination.
Q6Is there negative marking in JSSC JTGLCCE Exam 2026?
A
Yes, there is a negative marking system in the examination. For every incorrect answer, 1 mark will be deducted, while each correct answer carries 3 marks. Candidates should attempt questions carefully to avoid losing marks.
Q7What is the application fee for JSSC JTGLCCE 2026?
A
General, EWS, OBC and candidates from other states must pay Rs. 100 as application fee. SC/ST candidates of Jharkhand need to pay Rs. 50, while PwD candidates are exempted from the fee. The fee must be paid online and is non-refundable.
Q8How can I apply for JSSC JTGLCCE Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates need to apply online through the official JSSC website by registering and filling the application form. They must upload required documents, pay the fee, and submit the form before the deadline. No hard copy submission is required for this recruitment.
Q9What is the exam pattern for JSSC JTGLCCE 2026?
A
The Preliminary Exam consists of 120 multiple-choice questions covering General Studies, Jharkhand GK, Mathematics, Science, and Mental Ability. The Main Exam has three papers including Hindi, regional language, and technical subject with general studies. The exam is conducted in bilingual mode.
Q10Where can I download the JSSC JTGLCCE 2026 Notification PDF?
A
Candidates can download the official notification PDF from the Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission website. The notification provides complete details regarding vacancies, eligibility, syllabus, and application procedure. It is important to read it carefully before applying.
