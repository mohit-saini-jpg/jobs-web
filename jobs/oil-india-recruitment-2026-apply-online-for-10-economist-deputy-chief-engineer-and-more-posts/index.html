Deputy Chief Chemist

Drilling/workover fluid preparation, testing and quality control; wellsite chemical management; inventory, documentation and chemical operations at drilling/workover sites; cementing, oil spotting, loss control and well kick management support

Drilling fluid rheology; HSE compliance; chemical operations management

Deputy Chief Geologist

Geological studies and interpretation; basin analysis; wellsite geology; mud logging; core and cuttings analysis; formation evaluation; well log interpretation; subsurface evaluation and field development planning; technical report and map preparation

Petrel, Kingdom Suite or equivalent geological software; sedimentology; structural geology; geo-statistics

Deputy Chief Engineer (Reservoir)

Reservoir characterization and modelling; hydrocarbon reserves estimation (SPE/PRMS); dynamic reservoir simulation; field development planning; well and reservoir performance monitoring; enhanced oil recovery studies

ECLIPSE, CMG or equivalent simulation software; decline curve analysis; material balance methods

Deputy Chief Geophysicist

2D/3D seismic data acquisition and processing; seismic interpretation (AVO, inversion, attributes); gravity/magnetic/EM surveys; integration of geophysical, geological and GIS data; prospect generation and basin modelling

Modern geophysical software; machine learning for interpretation; VSP; quantitative interpretation techniques

Deputy Chief Engineer (S&E)

Safety Management Systems (SMS); HSE inspections, audits, risk assessments; incident/accident investigation; statutory compliance (Factories Act, DGMS, OISD, PNGRB, PESO, CPCB/SPCB); EIA/EMP; environmental auditing; ESG/CSR/BRSR/GRI/SBTi/SDG framework integration

ISO 14001; GIS/environmental modelling tools; HSE data analysis; emergency response planning

Economist

Business/investment opportunity identification and evaluation; domestic/global economic monitoring; due diligence for ventures; overseas regulatory/compliance coordination; stakeholder relationship management; risk-weighted portfolio management; briefing papers and presentations for Management/Government

Econometric software; SWOT/trend analysis; financial modelling; E&P sector regulatory knowledge

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only through the OIL website. No other mode of application will be accepted. Printouts of online applications should NOT be sent to OIL by post or hand.

  • Step I – Sign Up / Registration: Visit https://www.oil-india.com/advertisement-list and click on "Advertisement for Recruitment of Officers in Grade D/E/F in OIL." Select the post, fill in personal details, valid email ID and mobile number. Complete OTP authentication. Note down the Application Number and password received on registered email/mobile.

  • Step II – Complete Application Form: Fill in Basic Details, Address, Qualification Details, Eligibility Details, Experience Details. Upload photo, signature and scanned certificates. Click "Submit" to finalize.

  • Documents to be Uploaded Online:

  • Recent passport size colour photograph (jpg/jpeg/PNG format, not older than 3 weeks)

  • Signature on white paper with black ink pen (jpg/jpeg/PNG format)

  • Birth Certificate or Matriculation/10th Admit Card/Pass Certificate/Marksheet showing date of birth - no other document accepted for age proof

  • Degree/Diploma certificates and mark sheets for all academic years (or provisional certificate if degree not yet issued)

  • Experience Letter/Service Certificate from each employer on official letterhead (with name, designation, dates of joining and relieving)

  • Separate document from each employer explicitly detailing roles and responsibilities performed

  • Any two supporting documents per employment from the prescribed list: last pay slip, annual increment letter, promotion/transfer order, PF statement, or offer/appointment letter (for past employment); or identity card, increment letter, promotion/transfer order, PF statement (for current employment)

  • Caste certificate (SC/ST/OBC-NCL) in Government of India prescribed format, if applicable

  • Valid NCL certificate for OBC candidates, if applicable

  • Valid EWS Income & Assets Certificate in Government of India format, if applicable

  • Valid Disability Certificate (min. 40% disability) for PwBD candidates, if applicable

  • Valid Discharge Book/Service and Release Certificate for Ex-Servicemen, if applicable

  • Domicile Certificate (J&K, 1980–1989) for age relaxation claim, if applicable

  • CGPA/Grade to percentage conversion certificate from university/institute, if marks are in CGPA/Grade format

  • NOC from current employer (for Government/PSU employees - mandatory at PI stage)

  • Apply Online at: https://www.oil-india.com/advertisement-list
