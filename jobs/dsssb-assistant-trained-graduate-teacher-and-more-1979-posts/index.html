How to Apply

  • 1Visit the DSSSB portal: https://dsssbonline.nic.in
  • 2Complete one-time registration (if not already registered).
  • 3Login and select the relevant post code from Advertisement No. 03/2026.
  • 4Fill in all details carefully - no changes can be made after submission.
  • 5Pay the application fee of ₹100 via SBI e-Pay (exempted for Women/SC/ST/PwBD/ESM).
  • 6Upload a recent colour passport photo (plain white/off-white background, clear face, eyes open) and signature.
  • 7Submit and download the application form printout.
  • 8Exam date will be announced separately on the DSSSB website.
  • 9Only one registration is allowed per candidate on the DSSSB portal. Multiple registrations will lead to cancellation of all applications.
  • 10Only one application per post code is allowed. Multiple applications for the same post code will result in cancellation and debarment.
  • 11The eligibility cut-off date is 15 July 2026 - age, qualification, experience, and all other eligibility conditions will be determined as on this date.
  • 12DSSSB does not verify eligibility at the application stage; candidature is accepted on a provisional basis. Verification happens at the e-dossier stage after the written examination.
  • 13No calculator, mobile phone, Bluetooth device, earbud, camera, smartwatch, or any other electronic gadget is allowed in the examination hall.
  • 14Dress code: light clothes with half sleeves, no big buttons, brooch, or badge; salwar/trouser; slippers or sandals with low heels. Shoes are not allowed.
  • 15Exam centres will be in Delhi/NCR and other states as decided by DSSSB.
  • 16DSSSB reserves the right to cancel, modify, or amend any part of the advertisement or examination at any stage.
  • 17In case of any discrepancy between the English, Hindi, Urdu, and Punjabi versions of the advertisement, the English version will be treated as final.
  • 18Candidates must regularly check the DSSSB website at https://dsssb.delhi.gov.in for updates and notifications/corrigenda.
