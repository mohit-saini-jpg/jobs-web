Qualification / Eligibility

How to Apply

  1. 1Visit the official TGPSC website at https://www.tgpsc.gov.in.
  2. 2If applying for the first time, complete One Time Registration (OTR) to obtain a TGPSC ID. Note: OTR alone does not constitute an application.
  3. 3Login using your TGPSC ID and Date of Birth, and enter the OTP received on your registered mobile number.
  4. 4Verify details fetched from OTR - Name, Date of Birth, Educational Qualifications, Community, Gender, Disability, Ex-Servicemen status, Sports, etc.
  5. 5Select your preferred examination centre from the available options.
  6. 6Upload a recent passport-size colour photograph (not older than 3 months; File size: 100KB–200KB) and scanned specimen signature (File size: 20KB–30KB) in JPG/PNG format.
  7. 7Preview the application carefully, then click SAVE & PAYMENT to proceed to fee payment.
  8. 8Pay the prescribed fee online through Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, or UPI.
  9. 9After payment, a PDF application will be generated. Download and retain the PDF. Note your Reference ID Number for future correspondence.
  10. 10Click CONFIRM & SUBMIT to complete the submission. No changes will be made by the Commission to any submitted application.
  11. 11For technical issues: Call 040-23120301 / 040-23120302 / 040-22445566 / 040-67445566 (10:30 AM to 5:00 PM on working days) or email [email protected].

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 Q1. What is the total number of vacancies for this recruitment?
A
There are a total of 19 vacancies for the post of Assistant Environmental Engineer in Telangana Pollution Control Board, including carried forward vacancies from previous recruitments.
Q2 Q2. What is the revised upper age limit for this post?
A
As per G.O.Ms.No.86, G.A.(Ser.A) Department, Dt. 18/05/2026, the upper age limit has been raised by 10 years - from 34 years to 44 years, reckoned as on 01/07/2026.
Q3 Q3. Is a B.Tech in any branch eligible for this post?
A
Yes. Candidates with B.Tech/B.E. in any branch are eligible only if they also hold a PG Degree in Environmental Engineering from a recognized university.
Q4 Q4. Is local reservation applicable for this post?
A
No. Local reservation is not applicable as this is a State Level post and selection is made on state-wide merit.
Q5 Q5. Are SC/ST candidates eligible for OC vacancies?
A
Yes. Applicants belonging to SC, ST, BC, EWS, and Differently Abled who avail upper age relaxation will also be considered for Open Category (OC) vacancies.
Q6 Q6. What is the exam pattern and total marks?
A
The exam has two papers - Paper I (General Studies, 150 marks) and Paper II (Concerned Subject, 300 marks) - totaling 450 marks, with 150 questions each and 150 minutes per paper.
Q7 Q7. Can I raise objections against the answer key?
A
Yes. After publication of the provisional key, candidates can raise objections online within the stipulated period by paying ₹500/- per question. Fee is reimbursed if the objection is found genuine.
Q8 Q8. Will a Scribe be provided for Differently Abled candidates?
A
Yes. As per G.O.Ms.No.05, Dt: 23/03/2024, Scribe and compensatory time will be provided to eligible Differently Abled candidates. However, only TGPSC shall provide scribes - candidates cannot bring their own.
