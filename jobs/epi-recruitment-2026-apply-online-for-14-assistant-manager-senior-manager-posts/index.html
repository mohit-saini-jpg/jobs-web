Important Instructions

If your degree certificate shows CGPA instead of percentage, EPI has specific conversion norms you must follow when filling your application form.
If the university provides a conversion formula: Use the university's own conversion and obtain a certificate from the university confirming the equivalent percentage. Submit this certificate at the time of interview. If the university does not provide a conversion formula: A minimum CGPA of 6 on a 10-point scale will be treated as equivalent to 60%. For scales other than 10-point, the score will be prorated accordingly.

EPI Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for EPI Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for EPI Recruitment 2026 is 3 July 2026 at 5:30 PM. Applications submitted after this time will not be considered under any circumstances.
Q2How many vacancies are available under EPI Advt. HRM/REC/04/2026?
A
EPI has announced a total of 14 vacancies - 9 for Assistant Manager (Civil), 1 for Assistant Manager (Electrical), 2 for Assistant Manager (Mechanical), and 2 for Senior Manager (Civil).
Q3What is the salary for Assistant Manager in EPI?
A
Assistant Managers at E-1 grade in EPI receive a fixed consolidated remuneration of ₹40,000 per month plus applicable House Rent Allowance. An annual increment of 3% is also provided subject to satisfactory performance.
Q4What is the salary for Senior Manager – Civil in EPI?
A
Senior Manager (Civil) at E-4 grade receives ₹70,000 per month plus applicable House Rent Allowance, with an annual increment of 3% on satisfactory performance.
Q5Is EPI hiring on a permanent or contract basis?
A
EPI is hiring on a Fixed Term Contract basis - initially for one year, which is extendable up to a maximum of three years based on performance and organisational requirements. This is not a permanent government job.
Q6What is the minimum experience required to apply for EPI Assistant Manager?
A
Candidates must have a minimum of 2 years of post-qualification executive experience with a gross annual remuneration of at least ₹3,60,000 in their last or current organisation. Teaching, training, consultancy, and freelancing do not count as valid experience.
Q7What is the minimum experience for EPI Senior Manager (Civil)?
A
The Senior Manager (Civil) post requires a minimum of 9 years of post-qualification executive experience, and the gross annual remuneration in the last or current organisation should not be less than ₹7,20,000.
Q8Where will the interview for EPI Recruitment 2026 be conducted?
A
The interview will be conducted at EPI's Western Regional Office (WRO) in Mumbai. The exact date and time will be communicated to shortlisted candidates by email. No TA/DA is provided for attending the interview.
Q9Can OBC or SC/ST candidates apply for unreserved posts in EPI?
A
Yes, candidates from reserved categories (SC/ST/OBC-NCL) can apply against unreserved (UR) posts. However, no age relaxation will be extended to them when applying against unreserved posts.
Q10Is there any written test for EPI Recruitment 2026?
A
A written test is not mandatory but may be conducted at EPI's discretion if the number of applications received is large. Candidates will then be called for interview based on their written test performance.
