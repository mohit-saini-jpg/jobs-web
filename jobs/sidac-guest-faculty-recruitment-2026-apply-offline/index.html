Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the SIDAC Guest Faculty recruitment?

The last date to submit the application for the SIDAC Guest Faculty posts is 17th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the concerned authority by this date.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Guest Faculty posts?

Candidates need to have HSC (10th standard) or equivalent. However, exemptions are provided for Awardees. Additionally, being an empanelled MCP under the Directorate of Handicrafts, Odisha, and having 36 months of experience in handicraft training are crucial.

Q3. What is the age limit for applying to SIDAC Guest Faculty positions?

The age limit for applying to these positions is between 35 years and 55 years. There is also a provision for age relaxation for retired government instructors.

Q4. What is the salary offered for the Guest Faculty roles?

The salary for the Guest Faculty positions ranges from Rs. 20,000 to Rs. 22,500 per month, depending on the specific post and qualifications.

Q5. How can I apply for the SIDAC Guest Faculty recruitment?

The application process is offline. You need to download the application form from the official website, fill it out carefully, attach necessary documents, and send it to the address mentioned in the notification via Speed Post or Registered Post.

Q6. Is there any application fee for SIDAC Guest Faculty posts?

No, there is no application fee required for any category of candidates (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment.

Q7. What is the selection process for SIDAC Guest Faculty recruitment?

The selection process involves scrutiny of applications, evaluation based on experience and awards, a personal interview, and finally, the preparation of a merit list.

Q8. Does SIDAC offer any age relaxation for retired government instructors?

Yes, SIDAC offers age relaxation for retired government instructors, allowing them to apply up to the age of 65 years.
