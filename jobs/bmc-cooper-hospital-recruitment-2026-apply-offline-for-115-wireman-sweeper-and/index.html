BMC Cooper Hospital Recruitment 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility is largely common across most posts, with additional trade-specific requirements for certain skilled posts: For Sr. No. 1 to 24, candidates must generally have passed 10th/SSC, or an equivalent examination in the Marathi subject carrying a 100-mark question paper as per the respective post's educational requirement. Candidates must be able to read, write, and speak Marathi. For several posts (Sweeper, Ward Attendant, Ambulance Attendant, Tailor, etc.), candidates must also pass a trade/skill test conducted by the department.

BMC Cooper Hospital Recruitment 2026 Application Fee

The application fee is Rs. 1,028 (Rs. 870 + 18% GST of Rs. 158), payable at the Cashier Department (Room No. 65), 1st Floor, D Wing, Dr. Rustom Narsee Cooper Hospital, between 22 July 2026 and 30 July 2026, from 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM (excluding Saturdays, Sundays, and public holidays). The fee is non-refundable under any circumstances. The photocopy of the fee receipt must be attached to the application.

BMC Cooper Hospital Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

A merit list will be prepared based on the prescribed educational qualification and the marks obtained in the interview. The selection list will be prepared from candidates in the merit list. The interview date will be displayed on the hospital notice board and communicated to candidates by phone. Candidates must reach the interview venue at least half an hour before the scheduled interview time.

Documents Required for BMC Cooper Hospital Application

For Medical, Para-medical, and Administrative category posts, the following self-attested photocopies must be attached to the application: Fully filled Application Form Educational Certificates - 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates, plus documents required as per the qualification for the applied post (Degree/Diploma/Post-Graduation/Fellowship/MSCIT/Typing Certificate, etc.) Registration Certificate from the relevant council, e.g., Maharashtra Medical Council/Pharmacy Council (as required by the post's qualification) Experience Certificates Aadhaar Card PAN Card Address Proof - Driving Licence, Electricity Bill, Voter ID, or Ration Card Photocopy of Marriage Registration Certificate, for married women candidates All original documents must also be carried for verification at the time of the interview.

BMC Cooper Hospital Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Applications submitted earlier to this or any other office will not be considered. BMC has not authorized any individual or organization to sell or accept applications on its behalf. Applications without the prescribed fee receipt attached will not be considered valid. Applications received by post or submitted online will not be considered. Character and antecedent verification will be conducted through the concerned police station before a temporary appointment is given. The temporary service period at any one time will not exceed 89 days. Service may be terminated without prior notice by the Dean if performance during the temporary period is found unsatisfactory. Since the appointment is on a contractual basis, appointees cannot approach any court regarding the appointment. Selected candidates must execute an agreement on a Rs. 500 stamp paper at the time of appointment. Candidates must complete biometric attendance daily as per their duty schedule; unauthorized absence will be treated as Leave Without Pay (LWP). If misconduct is noticed during the appointment period, the appointment will be terminated immediately without prior notice. A selected candidate wishing to resign must give one month's prior notice. Appointed candidates cannot claim any right to permanent service with the Municipal Corporation and will not be entitled to any benefits of regular service. Appointed candidates may be required to work in any department assigned by the department head, across three shifts. Candidates must attend the interview at their own expense. If false information, certificates, or documents are submitted, or information is found to be concealed at any stage of selection or after appointment, the candidature or appointment will be cancelled/terminated without prior notice. The Dean has the authority to halt the selection process at any stage for administrative or other reasons.
