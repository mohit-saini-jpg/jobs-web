Important Instructions

The post of Senior Resident is a tenure post for a period of 03 (three) years only as per the Central Residency Scheme. All terms and conditions of the Scheme will be applicable. The post is whole-time; private practice of any kind is strictly prohibited. Candidates are likely to be posted at rural health and urban centres attached with the Institute for a period decided by the Institute. All eligible applicants irrespective of their category may be considered for seats advertised under a reservation category only in the absence of applicants in that reserve category, for not exceeding 44 days (extendable by competent authority). Qualification, experience, age and other eligibility conditions will be considered as on the date of publication of this notification on the Institute website. Canvassing of any kind will be a disqualification. The candidate should not have been convicted by any Court of Law. In case any information or declaration by the candidate is found to be false or if material information has been wilfully suppressed, the candidate will be liable to be removed from service and legal action will be initiated. AIIMS Guwahati reserves the right to increase or decrease the number of vacancies. The Competent Authority reserves the right of amendment, cancellation, and changes to the advertisement without assigning any reason. Residential accommodation will be provided subject to availability. If accommodation cannot be provided, HRA as admissible to Central Government Servants of similar status will be paid. The decision of the Competent Authority of AIIMS, Guwahati in all matters relating to eligibility, acceptance or rejection of applications, mode of selection, and allotment of posts will be final and binding. No representation or correspondence will be entertained. All disputes will be subject to jurisdiction of Court of Law at Guwahati, Assam. Candidates are advised to visit https://aiimsguwahati.ac.in regularly for all updates regarding the selection process.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the notification date and where can I download the AIIMS Guwahati Senior Resident 2026 notification?
A
The notification (No. 02-14/2021-22/AIIMS/GHY/ESTT/RECT(SR)/PART–II/260D(A)) was published on 29-05-2026. Download the official PDF from the AIIMS Guwahati website: https://aiimsguwahati.ac.in.
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are available and for which posts?
A
A total of 129 vacancies are available for Senior Resident (Non-Academic) / Senior Demonstrator across 35 departments. 4% of posts are horizontally reserved for PwBD candidates.
Q3Q3. What are the important dates I must note?
A
Online application opens: 01-06-2026. Last date to apply: 15-06-2026. Interviews: 29-06-2026 & 30-06-2026 (document verification reporting at 8:00 AM).
Q4Q4. What is the eligibility qualification?
A
Medical posts: MD / MS / DNB or equivalent postgraduate medical degree in the relevant discipline. Non-medical posts (Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Microbiology, Pharmacology): M.Sc. + PhD in the relevant subject. Qualifications must be from a recognized university and valid as on 15-06-2026.
Q5Q5. What is the age limit and relaxations?
A
Upper age limit: 45 years as on 15-06-2026. Age relaxation applies as per Government of India rules (SC/ST: +5 years, OBC: +3 years, PwBD: additional relaxations as applicable).
Q6Q6. How much is the application fee and how do I pay?
A
UR/OBC: Rs. 1000 + transaction charges. SC/ST/EWS: Rs. 500 + transaction charges. PwBD: Nil. Fee must be paid online while submitting the application. Separate forms and fees required if applying to multiple departments.
Q7Q7. What is the selection process?
A
Selection is by Personal Interview only. Shortlisting from online applications will be done and eligible candidates will be called for document verification and interview. No TA/DA will be provided.
Q8Q8. What is the salary and tenure of appointment?
A
Medical candidates: Level-11, entry pay Rs. 67,700/- plus NPA and allowances. Non-medical (M.Sc. + PhD): Level-10, entry pay Rs. 56,100/- plus allowances. Appointment is on tenure basis up to 3 years under the Central Residency Scheme.
Q9Q9. Can I apply for more than one department?
A
Yes. Candidates may apply for more than one department but must fill separate online applications and pay the prescribed fee separately for each department.
Q10Q10. What documents do I need to upload and bring for the interview?
A
Upload during application: passport-size photo (JPG), scanned signature (JPG), and scanned copies of educational certificates, degree/degree completion proof, age proof, category certificate (OBC NCL in DoP&T format dated on/after 01-04-2025 if applicable), EWS certificate (if applicable), PwBD certificate (if applicable), and any other relevant documents. Government employees must bring NOC and produce it at interview; otherwise they will not be permitted to attend.
