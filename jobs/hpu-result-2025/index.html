How to Check the HPU Results 2025?

Himachal Pradesh University announces its results online through the official website. To access their results, students must visit the website and locate the designated result link. Since the results are not publicly available, students are required to enter their roll number to view their individual scores.

  • Go to the official HPU website: hpuniv.ac.in.​
  • On the homepage, look for the “Results” or “Examination” tab and click on it.​
  • Find and select the relevant course and semester from the listed links (such as B.A, B.Sc, M.A, MBBS, etc.).​
  • Enter your Roll Number, Registration Number, or other required credentials in the provided field.​
  • Click the “Submit” or “View Result” button to access your result.​
  • Download your mark sheet and print it for your records.
