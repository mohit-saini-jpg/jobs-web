Haryana Police Admit Card 2026: The Haryana Staff Selection Commission (HSSC) has released the dates for the Physical Standard Test (PST) and Physical Measurement Test (PMT) for 5,500 posts under the Constable Recruitment 2026 (Advertisement No. 01/2026). The Physical Test is scheduled to commence on April 15, 2026. Candidates who applied for the Haryana Police Constable Recruitment 2026 can download their Admit Cards/Hall Tickets from the official website, hssc.gov.in. The Admit Card contains important details such as the candidate’s name, roll number, exam date, reporting time, and test venue. It is mandatory to carry a valid Admit Card and an original photo ID proof to gain entry into the PST/PMT venue. This post provides details regarding the Haryana Police Physical Test dates, the Admit Card download process, the selection procedure, and other important information.

Q1. Haryana Police Physical Test Date 2026 kya hai?

Haryana Police Constable Recruitment 2026 ki Physical Test (PST/PMT) 15 April 2026 se shuru hogi. Candidates apna specific date admit card par check karein.

Q2. Haryana Police Admit Card 2026 kahan se download karein?

Official website hssc.gov.in par jaayein → Admit Card section mein Haryana Police Constable 2026 ka link dhundhein → Roll Number/Registration Number enter karein → Admit Card download karein.

Q3. Haryana Police Admit Card 2026 mein kya-kya details hoti hain?

Admit card mein candidate ka naam, roll number, exam date, reporting time, test venue (center address), category aur important instructions hoti hain.

Q4. Haryana Police Physical Test (PST/PMT) mein kya test hota hai?

PST (Physical Standard Test) mein height, chest (male ke liye) aur weight check hote hain. PMT (Physical Measurement Test) mein running aur physical fitness test hota hai.
