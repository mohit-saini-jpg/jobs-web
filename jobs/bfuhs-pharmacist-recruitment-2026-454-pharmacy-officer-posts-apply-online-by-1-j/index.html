Competition Level Analysis

With 454 posts across Punjab's government health network, BFUHS Pharmacist Recruitment 2026 is one of the largest pharmacy recruitment drives in Punjab in recent years. General category has the highest allocation at 136 posts, while ESM/LDESM categories together account for 88 posts. The combination of written exam (90 marks) and experience marks (10 marks) means candidates with valid COVID-19 service experience under the Punjab Health Department gain a direct scoring advantage. The mandatory 50% Punjabi Language qualifying test is a key eliminator - candidates must prepare specifically for this component alongside the technical pharmacy exam. Minimum 33% in written exam is needed to even qualify for the merit list.

Who Should NOT Apply

Candidates who are not registered with Punjab Pharmacy Council under the Pharmacy Act, 1948, as on 1 July 2026 Candidates who do not hold a Diploma in Pharmacy, B.Pharm, or Pharm.D from a recognised institution Candidates who have not passed Matriculation with Punjabi as a subject and cannot qualify the Punjabi language test at 50% Candidates above 37 years of age as on 1 January 2026 (unless eligible for age relaxation under a specific category) Candidates previously disqualified by any recruiting board/government body where the disqualification period is still active Government employees with pending disciplinary proceedings who cannot obtain an NOC from their Head of Department

BFUHS Pharmacist Recruitment 2026 – 454 Pharmacy Officer : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for BFUHS Pharmacist Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for BFUHS Pharmacist (Pharmacy Officer) Recruitment 2026 is 1 July 2026. Applications must be submitted at bfuhs.ac.in.
Q2How many posts are available in BFUHS Pharmacist Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 454 posts are available - comprising 348 fresh vacancies and 106 backlog posts — across multiple reservation categories under the Punjab Health and Family Welfare Department.
Q3What is the age limit for BFUHS Pharmacist 2026?
A
The age limit is 18 to 37 years as on 1 January 2026. SC/BC candidates get 5 years relaxation, Physically Handicapped get 10 years, and government employees can apply up to 45 years.
Q4What qualifications are required for BFUHS Pharmacist post?
A
Candidates must have passed Class 12 with Science, hold a Diploma in Pharmacy or B.Pharm or Pharm.D from a recognised institution, and be registered with the Punjab Pharmacy Council under the Pharmacy Act, 1948.
Q5What is the application fee for BFUHS Pharmacist Recruitment 2026?
A
The fee is ₹2,360/- for all categories except SC (₹2,000 + 18% GST) and ₹1,180/- for SC category candidates (₹1,000 + 18% GST). The fee is non-refundable.
Q6What is the salary for Pharmacist (Pharmacy Officer) in Punjab?
A
The initial pay is ₹29,200/- at Pay Level 5 under the 7th Central Pay Commission. Additional allowances are governed by Punjab Government and Department of Health service rules.
Q7What is the selection process for BFUHS Pharmacist 2026?
A
Selection is based on a written exam (90 marks) + experience marks (10 marks) = 100 marks total, plus a mandatory Punjabi Language qualifying test (minimum 50% required). Final selection is on inter-se merit subject to Punjab Government reservation rules.
Q8Is there a Punjabi language requirement for this post?
A
Yes. Candidates must pass a Punjabi Language qualifying test at Matriculation level with at least 50% marks. Failing this test disqualifies the candidate from the final merit list regardless of their performance in other papers.
Q9Who conducts the written exam for BFUHS Pharmacist Recruitment?
A
The written examination is conducted by Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (BFUHS), Faridkot. Candidates scoring below 33% in the written test will be declared ineligible.
Q10What is the official website to apply for BFUHS Pharmacist 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online at www.bfuhs.ac.in.
