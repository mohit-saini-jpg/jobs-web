DetailsTo be eligible for BFUHS Pharmacist Recruitment 2026, candidates must satisfy all three qualification conditions simultaneously. The eligibility is determined as on the last date of application, i.e., 1 July 2026. | Passed Senior Secondary Part-II (Class 12) with Science or its equivalent from a recognised university or institution; AND Possess a Diploma in Pharmacy or Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) or Pharm.D (Doctor of Pharmacy) from a recognised university or institution, or higher qualification in the same discipline; AND Registered as a Pharmacist with the Punjab Pharmacy Council set up under the Pharmacy Act, 1948 | Certificates and diplomas from non-recognised institutions will not be accepted and candidature will be declared ineligible. Foreign university degrees require equivalence/recognition proof from the University Grants Commission. | No person shall be appointed by direct recruitment unless they have passed the Matriculation examination with Punjabi as a compulsory or elective subject, or an equivalent Punjabi language examination. Failure to secure 50% in this test will disqualify the candidate regardless of their other scores. | Exception: War Heroes discharged from defence/paramilitary services on account of disability (or their widows/dependents appointed under Government instructions) are exempt from the Punjabi language requirement. Wards of defence personnel who are bonafide Punjab residents must qualify the Punjabi test within two years of appointment. | Candidates wondering about age eligibility can note that the age limit for BFUHS Pharmacist 2026 is between 18 and 37 years as on 1 January 2026. Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 Q1. What is the last date to apply for BFUHS Pharmacist Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for BFUHS Pharmacist (Pharmacy Officer) Recruitment 2026 is 1 July 2026. Applications must be submitted at bfuhs.ac.in.
Q2 Q2. How many posts are available in BFUHS Pharmacist Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 454 posts are available - comprising 348 fresh vacancies and 106 backlog posts — across multiple reservation categories under the Punjab Health and Family Welfare Department.
Q3 Q3. What is the age limit for BFUHS Pharmacist 2026?
A
The age limit is 18 to 37 years as on 1 January 2026. SC/BC candidates get 5 years relaxation, Physically Handicapped get 10 years, and government employees can apply up to 45 years.
Q4 Q4. What qualifications are required for BFUHS Pharmacist post?
A
Candidates must have passed Class 12 with Science, hold a Diploma in Pharmacy or B.Pharm or Pharm.D from a recognised institution, and be registered with the Punjab Pharmacy Council under the Pharmacy Act, 1948.
Q5 Q5. What is the application fee for BFUHS Pharmacist Recruitment 2026?
A
The fee is ₹2,360/- for all categories except SC (₹2,000 + 18% GST) and ₹1,180/- for SC category candidates (₹1,000 + 18% GST). The fee is non-refundable.
Q6 Q6. What is the salary for Pharmacist (Pharmacy Officer) in Punjab?
A
The initial pay is ₹29,200/- at Pay Level 5 under the 7th Central Pay Commission. Additional allowances are governed by Punjab Government and Department of Health service rules.
Q7 Q7. What is the selection process for BFUHS Pharmacist 2026?
A
Selection is based on a written exam (90 marks) + experience marks (10 marks) = 100 marks total, plus a mandatory Punjabi Language qualifying test (minimum 50% required). Final selection is on inter-se merit subject to Punjab Government reservation rules.
Q8 Q8. Is there a Punjabi language requirement for this post?
A
Yes. Candidates must pass a Punjabi Language qualifying test at Matriculation level with at least 50% marks. Failing this test disqualifies the candidate from the final merit list regardless of their performance in other papers.
Q9 Q9. Who conducts the written exam for BFUHS Pharmacist Recruitment?
A
The written examination is conducted by Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (BFUHS), Faridkot. Candidates scoring below 33% in the written test will be declared ineligible.
Q10 Q10. What is the official website to apply for BFUHS Pharmacist 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online at www.bfuhs.ac.in.
