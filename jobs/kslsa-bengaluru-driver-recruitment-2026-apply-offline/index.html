FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the KSLSA Bengaluru Driver recruitment?
A
The last date to submit the offline application for the KSLSA Bengaluru Driver recruitment is 25th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the specified address before this deadline.
Q2What are the minimum and maximum age limits for the Driver post?
A
The minimum age requirement is 18 years. The maximum age limit is 40 years for General candidates. For SC/ST/OBC/Category-I candidates, the maximum age limit is relaxed to 45 years.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for the Driver position?
A
Candidates must have passed SSLC or an equivalent examination. Additionally, they need to possess a Light Motor Vehicle driving license along with a Heavy Motor Vehicle driving license and relevant driving experience.
Q4How will candidates be selected for the Driver post?
A
The selection process will involve a driving test conducted by the Police Training and Management School, followed by an interview. Candidates must perform well in both stages to be considered for the position.
Q5What is the application fee for the KSLSA Bengaluru Driver recruitment?
A
The application fee for General category candidates is Rs. 500. For SC/ST/OBC/Category-I candidates, the application fee is Rs. 250. This fee needs to be paid as per the instructions in the notification.
Q6Where should the application form be sent?
A
The completed application form, along with all necessary documents and fee payment details, should be sent offline to the Member Secretary, Karnataka State Legal Services Authority, 1st Floor, High Court Building, H. Siddiah Road, Bengaluru - 560027.
Q7What is the salary offered for the Driver post?
A
The salary for the Driver post is based on a pay scale ranging from Rs. 34,100 to Rs. 67,600. This includes various increments and allowances as per government norms.
Q8Is there any work experience required for the Driver position?
A
Yes, candidates must have relevant driving experience in addition to possessing the required driving licenses (LMV and HMV). The specific duration of experience may be detailed in the official notification.
Q9Does candidates need to know Kannada?
A
Yes, it is a mandatory requirement for candidates applying for the Driver post to have knowledge of the Kannada language. This is essential for communication and performing duties effectively.
Q10How many vacancies are available for the Driver post?
A
There are a total of 2 vacancies for the post of ವಾಹನ ಚಾಲಕ (Driver) in the Karnataka State Legal Services Authority, Bengaluru, as per the recruitment notification.
