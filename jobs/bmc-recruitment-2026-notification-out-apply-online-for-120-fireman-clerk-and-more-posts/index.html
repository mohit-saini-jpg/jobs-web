Short Information

The Bhavnagar Municipal Corporation (BMC Bhavnagar) has officially announced the Various Posts Recruitment 2026 with 120 openings. Candidates who meet the age criteria as per Gujarat Government norms and hold qualifications ranging from HSC (12th Pass), ITI, Diploma, Graduate, B.E./B.Tech, MCA, LL.B., to MBBS/MD/MS depending on the post can now submit their online applications. How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the OJAS Gujarat portal at ojas.gujarat.gov.in. No offline, postal, or in-person applications will be accepted under any circumstances. The portal will be active from 10/05/2026 (14:00 hrs) to 31/05/2026 (23:59 hrs). Candidates are strongly advised to read the detailed notification carefully before applying. Candidates must ensure eligibility before submitting the online application - the qualification, pay scale, and all other information is available on the OJAS website and the BMC website www.bmcgujarat.com.
  • Steps to Apply Online
  • Visit the official OJAS Gujarat website: ojas.gujarat.gov.in
  • Search for the recruitment link for "Bhavnagar Mahanagarpalika – Various Posts Recruitment 2026"
  • Read the full notification carefully including eligibility, age limit, physical standards, and fee structure
  • Click on the "Apply Online" link and register with basic details (name, date of birth, mobile number, email ID)
  • Save the provisional registration number and password generated after registration
  • Log in and fill the online application form with personal, educational, category, and contact details
  • Upload scanned photograph and signature in the prescribed format
  • Deposit the application fee at the Post Office and enter the receipt/registration number in the application
  • Review all details carefully before final submission and submit online
  • Take a printout of the completed application and fee payment receipt for future reference
  • Apply Online at: https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in
