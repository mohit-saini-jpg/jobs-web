IOCL Barauni Apprentice Selection Process 2026

Candidates wondering about the IOCL Barauni selection process can note that there is no written test for this apprentice engagement. Selection is based purely on the percentage of marks scored in the prescribed qualification, in descending order, among candidates registered on the NAPS/NATS portal. Merit Listing: Candidates are ranked by aggregate percentage of marks (all years/semesters) in the prescribed qualification for their trade. Preference is given to first-time apprenticeship applicants. Tie-Breaker: In case of equal marks, the candidate senior in age (date of birth) is ranked higher; if dates of birth also match, higher Matriculation marks decide the rank. PwBD Consideration: PwBD candidates who avail relaxation in qualifying marks are considered against reserved seats based on merit within their category. IOCL Barauni Apprentice Stipend & Other Benefits 2026

The stipend payable to apprentices selected under IOCL Barauni Refinery Recruitment 2026 follows the rates prescribed under the Apprentices Act, 1961/1973, Apprentices Rules 1992, and Corporation guidelines, rather than a fixed flat figure stated in this notification. Apprentices engaged against Codes 101 to 107 receive one pair of safety shoes and a helmet, returnable upon training completion. Importantly, IOCL has clarified that the Corporation has no obligation to offer regular employment upon completion of the apprenticeship period, and apprentices cannot claim employment rights based solely on completing their training.
