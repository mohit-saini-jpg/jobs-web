MGU Result 2026 - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 20/07/2026

Mahatma Gandhi University (MGU) has released two fresh examination results on its official result portal, dsdc.mgu.ac.in. These results cover the IX Semester B.Arch (2015-2018 Admissions Supplementary) Degree Examination held in December 2025, and the IV Semester MAHRM (Master of Arts in Human Resource Management) / MHRM Degree Examination held in March 2026. Both results include a PRN-wise result link and an official result notification PDF signed by the Controller of Examinations.

Result Details: IX Semester B.Arch (December 2025)

The result of the IX Semester Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) Degree Examination for the 2015-2018 Admissions Supplementary batch, held in December 2025, was declared on 18 July 2026. The notification refers to an earlier university notification dated 17 December 2025 that had scheduled this examination.

  • PRN Wise Result: Available through the B.Arch results portal on projects.mgu.ac.in
  • Result Notification PDF: View official notification

Result Details: IV Semester MAHRM/MHRM (March 2026)

The result of the IV Semester Master of Arts in Human Resource Management (MAHRM) - covering 2024 Admission Regular and 2021-2023 Admission Supplementary candidates - along with MHRM candidates from the 2018, 2019, and 2020 Admission Mercy Chance batches, for the March 2026 Degree Examination, was declared on 17 July 2026. The notification refers to an earlier university notification dated 27 March 2026 that had scheduled this examination.

  • PRN Wise Result: Available through the Pareeksha results portal on pareeksha.mgu.ac.in
  • Result Notification PDF: View official notification

How to Check MGU Result 2026 Online

  • Visit the official MGU result portal at dsdc.mgu.ac.in/oldresult/result.php.
  • Locate your examination from the list (e.g., "IX Semester B.Arch December 2025" or "IV Semester MAHRM March 2026").
  • Click the PRN Wise Result link next to your examination.
  • You will be redirected to either the B.Arch results portal (projects.mgu.ac.in) or the Pareeksha results portal (pareeksha.mgu.ac.in), depending on the course.
  • Enter your PRN (Permanent Registration Number) as prompted to view your individual result.
  • For the official notification and any revaluation/scrutiny fee details, open the linked Result Notification PDF.

Important Instructions

  • Always check results through the official MGU domains (dsdc.mgu.ac.in, pareeksha.mgu.ac.in, projects.mgu.ac.in, studentportal.mgu.ac.in) to avoid misinformation from third-party sites.
  • Revaluation and scrutiny fees and deadlines differ between the B.Arch and MAHRM/MHRM examinations - refer to the table above and confirm against the linked PDF before applying.
  • For the B.Arch result, revaluation/scrutiny applications must be submitted physically at the Controller of Examinations office; for MAHRM/MHRM, applications are submitted online via the student portal.
