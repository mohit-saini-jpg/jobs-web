Physical Standards

Candidates must meet the region-wise height, weight and chest expansion standards prescribed by the Indian Army for both male and female applicants. Weight should be proportionate to height and age as per Army Medical Standards, and candidates should be capable of a chest expansion of 5 cms. A height relaxation of 4 cms is given to female candidates belonging to the North East Region (Sikkim, Nagaland, Arunachal Pradesh, Manipur, Tripura, Mizoram, Meghalaya, Assam), Gorkhas, Garhwalis, Ladakhis, and Tribals of recognized tribal areas. Domicile and Caste certificates will be used to determine applicability of this relaxation. Waivers in physical standards and tests may be given for exceptional talent, for both male and female candidates.

Indian Army Sports Quota Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

The selection process for Indian Army Sports Quota Recruitment 2026 involves shortlisting based on sports achievements, followed by trials and medical examination. Applications are received only through Registered or normal post. Shortlisting is done based on higher sports achievements of the candidate. Call-up letters are sent to shortlisted candidates via email, indicating date, time and place of selection trials. Candidates undergo Physical Fitness Test, Physical Standards Test, and Skill Test at the designated Army Centre. Candidates qualifying the above tests undergo medical examination at the trial site itself. Candidates declared Medically Temporary Unfit may avail specialist review at a Military Hospital within 21 days; no further appeal is permissible. Candidates declared Medically Permanent Unfit may appeal for specialist opinion within 21 days on payment of Rs. 10 by Military
