Application Fee

DetailsThere is no application fee for this recruitment. Candidates only need to send the application through email with required documents.

Age Limit

DetailsCandidates should be less than 28 years of age as on 31-05-2026. Age relaxation is applicable as per Government of India norms.

Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationOption 1: M.Sc. (Physics) with minimum 55% aggregate marks. Option 2: 5-Year Integrated M.Sc. / B.S.-M.S. (Physics) with minimum 55% aggregate. Option 3: 4-Year / 8-Semester B.S. (Physics) with fourth year research with minimum 75% aggregate. Candidates awaiting final results can also apply with minimum required percentage up to previous semester/year.

Selection Process

  • Selection will be based on screening of applications followed by Offline Interview. Shortlisting will be done based on performance in national-level exams and academic records. Final selection depends on performance in the interview. Allotment of research topic will be decided after pre-Ph.D. course work.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must send the duly filled application form through email only to [email protected] on or before 31-05-2026.
  • Documents to be Sent Along with Application:
  • Passport size photo (JPEG, <1 MB)
  • Proof of Date of Birth (SSC/Matriculation or Birth Certificate)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Mark sheets and Certificates of qualifying degree
  • Score card / Certificate of National Level Exam (GATE/JEST/CSIR-NET)
  • Step-by-Step Application Process:
  • Download the prescribed Application Form from the notification.
  • Fill all details carefully including research interest and national exam details.
  • Attach all supporting documents in proper format.
  • Send the complete application through email to [email protected] before 31-05-2026.
  • Shortlisted candidates will be informed for offline interview at VECC, Kolkata.

Important Instructions

  • This is a fellowship for pursuing Ph.D. programme. No claim for permanent absorption. Interviews will be conducted in offline mode only. Selected candidates must join on 03-08-2026. Fellowship is as per DAE guidelines (Rs.37,000/- to Rs.42,000/- + HRA/Hostel). Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification.
