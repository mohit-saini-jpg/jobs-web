Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for VECC JRF Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the application for VECC Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) Recruitment 2026 is 31 May 2026. Candidates must send their duly filled application form along with all required documents through email before the deadline. Late or incomplete applications may not be considered for further selection.

Q2. How many fellowships are available in VECC JRF Recruitment 2026?

A total of 09 Junior Research Fellowships (JRFs) have been announced by Variable Energy Cyclotron Centre (VECC), Kolkata. These fellowships are offered in advanced research fields such as Nuclear Physics, Astrophysics, Accelerator Physics, Material Science, and Applied Physics. The final allocation may vary based on institutional requirements and candidate performance.

Q3. What educational qualification is required for VECC JRF 2026?

Candidates must have an M.Sc. in Physics with at least 55% marks, or an Integrated M.Sc./B.S.-M.S. degree in Physics with the same minimum percentage. Candidates with a 4-year B.S. degree in Physics and at least 75% aggregate marks are also eligible. Students awaiting final results can apply if they meet the required academic percentage till their latest semester.

Q4. Is a national-level exam score mandatory for VECC JRF Recruitment 2026?

Yes, candidates must possess a valid score or certificate in a recognized national-level examination. Accepted exams include UGC-CSIR NET Fellowship, JEST, or GATE in Physics. However, candidates having only NET Lectureship eligibility without Fellowship are not eligible for this recruitment.

Q5. What is the age limit for VECC JRF Recruitment 2026?

Applicants should be below 28 years of age as on 31 May 2026 to be eligible for the fellowship. Age relaxation benefits are available for reserved category candidates as per Government of India rules. OBC candidates receive 3 years relaxation, while SC/ST candidates are eligible for 5 years relaxation.

Q6. How can candidates apply for VECC JRF Recruitment 2026?

Interested candidates must apply through email only by sending the completed application form and supporting documents to the official email ID mentioned in the notification. Applications through offline mode, post, or any other method will not be accepted. Candidates should verify all details carefully before submission.

Q7. Is there any application fee for VECC JRF Recruitment 2026?

No, there is no application fee for VECC Junior Research Fellowship Recruitment 2026. Candidates can apply free of cost by emailing the required documents and application form. This makes the process accessible to all eligible candidates without any payment burden.

Q8. What is the selection process for VECC JRF Recruitment 2026?

The selection process includes screening of applications followed by an offline interview at VECC, Kolkata. Candidates will be shortlisted based on their academic performance and valid national-level exam scores. Final selection will depend on performance during the interview and research suitability.
