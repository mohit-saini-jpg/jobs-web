Job Overview

Bharat Dynamics Limited (BDL) has released the BDL Recruitment 2026 Notification (Advt No. BDL/C-HR (TA & CP)/2026-1) on 27th May 2026 at the official website bdl-india.in. How to Apply

  • 1BDL Project Engineer 2026 is a Walk-in Interview - there is no online application form. Candidates must appear directly at the venue with all required documents. Follow these steps:
  • 2Download the official BDL Recruitment 2026 notification PDF from bdl-india.in → HR section.
  • 3Download and print the Bio-data Form (Annexure-I) attached to the notification.
  • 4Fill the Bio-data Form in capital letters, affix a recent passport-size colour photograph, and self-attest all required documents.
  • 5Calculate your age as on 07.05.2026 and verify that you meet the upper age limit for your category.
  • 6Arrange one set of self-attested photocopies of all required documents (originals must also be carried for verification).
  • 7Reach The Aeronautical Society of India (AeSI), Suranjan Das Road, New Tippasandra, Bengaluru - 560075 (near Vivekananda Metro Station) by 09:00 AM on the interview date.
  • 8Submit your Bio-data Form and documents at the registration desk. Documents will be scrutinised to verify eligibility.
  • 9Eligible candidates will be called for the interview on the same day or a later day (candidates may need to stay more than one day at their own expense).
  • 10No TA or DA will be paid. Mobile phones and electronic devices are not permitted inside the venue.
  • 11Queries: Email [email protected] with subject line: Query-BDL/Walk-in/2026-1

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date to apply for BDL Project Engineer 2026?
A
BDL Project Engineer 2026 is a walk-in interview — there is no online application form or last date. Candidates must appear directly at The Aeronautical Society of India (AeSI), Bengaluru on 6th or 7th June 2026 by 09:00 AM. Entry is strictly not allowed after 02:00 PM.
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are there in BDL Recruitment 2026?
A
BDL has announced a total of 20 vacancies for Project Engineer — 10 for Electronics, 5 for Mechanical, and 5 for Computer Science discipline.
Q3Q3. What is the age limit for BDL Project Engineer 2026?
A
The upper age limit for BDL Project Engineer 2026 is 28 years for UR/EWS, 31 years for OBC (NCL), and 33 years for SC/ST candidates, all reckoned as on 07.05.2026. PwBD candidates get an additional 10-year relaxation over their category limit.
Q4Q4. What is the salary for BDL Project Engineer 2026?
A
BDL Project Engineer salary is ₹30,000/- per month in the 1st year, increasing to ₹33,000/- (2nd year), ₹36,000/- (3rd year), and ₹39,000/- (4th year). An additional ₹10,000/- per year is paid towards medical, attire, and footwear expenses.
Q5Q5. What is the qualification required for BDL Project Engineer 2026?
A
Candidates must hold a First Class (60% aggregate) BE / B.Tech / B.Sc Engg (4 years) / Integrated M.E./M.Tech in the relevant discipline (Electronics / Mechanical / Computer Science) from an AICTE-approved institution. SC/ST candidates need 55% for reserved posts.
Q6Q6. Is there any application fee for BDL Recruitment 2026?
A
No, there is no application fee for BDL Project Engineer Walk-in Interview 2026. However, no TA or DA is paid to candidates for attending the interview.
Q7Q7. Where is the BDL Walk-in Interview 2026 venue?
A
The BDL Walk-in Interview 2026 will be held at The Aeronautical Society of India (AeSI), Suranjan Das Road, Opposite Engine Division, Binna Mangala, New Tippasandra, Bengaluru – 560075. The nearest metro station is Vivekananda Metro Station.
Q8Q8. Is BDL Project Engineer 2026 a permanent government job?
A
No. BDL Project Engineer 2026 is a temporary/contract appointment initially for 1 year, extendable up to 4 years based on organisational need and individual performance. Candidates cannot claim regular employment at BDL through this appointment.
Q9Q9. What experience is required for BDL Project Engineer (Electronics) 2026?
A
Project Engineer (Electronics) candidates must have a minimum of 1 year of post-qualification industrial experience in areas including embedded systems, RF, power electronics, FPGA, LabVIEW, C/C++, VC++, control systems, and weapon checkout systems. Academic, teaching, internship, and project work experience do NOT count.
Q10Q10. What is the selection process for BDL Project Engineer 2026?
A
BDL Project Engineer 2026 selection is based on 100 marks: 75 marks for academic percentage (1 mark per %, max 75), 10 marks for post-qualification experience (3 marks/1yr, 6 marks/2yr, 10 marks/3yr+), and 15 marks for walk-in interview performance. Minimum qualifying marks are 60%.
