How to Check ASTU Result 2026 Online

  • Open the official ASTU website https://astu.ac.in in your browser.
  • Go to the “Examination / Result / Notifications” section, or directly visit the results page at https://astu.ac.in/?page_id=116.
  • In the list of result notices, locate: “Results Notice of B.Sc (Hons.) Horticulture (ASTU ERP) End Semester Examination, 2025” “Results Notice of B.Sc (Hons.) Agriculture (ASTU ERP) End Semester Examination, 2025”.
  • “Results Notice of B.Sc (Hons.) Horticulture (ASTU ERP) End Semester Examination, 2025”
  • “Results Notice of B.Sc (Hons.) Agriculture (ASTU ERP) End Semester Examination, 2025”.
  • Click on the relevant result notice; a detailed page will open (for example, ?page_id=39718 for Horticulture and ?page_id=39715 for Agriculture), usually containing an attached PDF or an ERP link for viewing marks.
  • If a PDF gazette opens, use Ctrl+F and enter your roll number/enrolment number to find your record; if an ERP/login link is provided, follow it and log in with your student ID/roll number, password or other credentials as instructed by your college.
  • Once the result appears, check your name, programme (B.Sc Hons Agriculture / Horticulture), semester, paper‑wise marks, credits and overall result status (Pass/Fail) carefully.
  • Download or print the provisional result/marksheet and keep it for academic records, scholarship applications and registration for the next semester or practical components.
  • If you observe any discrepancy in marks or personal details, promptly contact your college examination cell or the ASTU examination branch with supporting documents for clarification or correction.
