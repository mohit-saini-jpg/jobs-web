Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the BHU JRF recruitment?

The last date to submit your online application for the Junior Research Fellow position at Banaras Hindu University is 06 June 2026. Ensure your application is submitted well before this deadline.

Q2. What are the educational qualifications required for the JRF post?

Candidates must possess a B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Physics, Applied Physics, Materials Science, or Nanoscience with at least 55% marks. Additionally, a CSIR-UGC NET or GATE qualification is mandatory.

Q3. What is the age limit for the Junior Research Fellow position?

The maximum age limit for applicants is 28 years. Age relaxations are applicable for female candidates, SC, ST, and Physically Handicapped individuals.

Q4. What is the salary offered for the JRF role?

The salary for the Junior Research Fellow is ₹ 37,000/- p.m. + HRA for the first and second years, and ₹ 43,000/- p.m. + HRA for the third year.

Q5. How can I apply for the BHU JRF recruitment?

Applications must be submitted online through the Google Form link provided in the Important Links section. Ensure all required documents are uploaded correctly.

Q6. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

The official notification does not mention any application fee for this recruitment drive. Candidates are advised to check the official notification for any updates.

Q7. What is the selection process for the JRF position?

The selection process involves shortlisting of candidates based on their applications, followed by an in-person interview.

Q8. Does BHU offer any age relaxation for specific categories?

Yes, BHU offers an age relaxation of 5 years for Female, SC, ST, and Physically Handicapped candidates applying for the JRF position.
