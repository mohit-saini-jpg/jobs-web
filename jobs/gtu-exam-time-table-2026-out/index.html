How to Download GTU Exam Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the GTU Exam Time Table 2026 from the official GTU timetable portal. The process is simple and requires no login for most timetables. Follow the steps below to access your schedule quickly.

  • Visit the official GTU timetable website at https://timetable.gtu.ac.in/.
  • On the homepage, select the current session Summer 2026 from the available options.
  • Choose your course, semester or year, and branch from the dropdown menus.
  • Locate the relevant theory or practical timetable link for your program.
  • Click on the PDF link to open the timetable in a new tab.
  • Download the PDF file and take a printout for future reference.

Students are advised to check the official timetable carefully for exam dates, timings, and instructions. Keep a printed or digital copy handy during exam preparation and refer to it regularly.
