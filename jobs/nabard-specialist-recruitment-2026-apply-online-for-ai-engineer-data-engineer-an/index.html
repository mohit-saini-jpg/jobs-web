NABARD Specialist Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1How many vacancies are there in NABARD DDMABI recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 8 vacancies have been notified: 1 ETL Designer, 1 Senior Data Scientist, 1 Data Scientist, 1 Document Designer, 3 AI Engineers, and 1 Data Engineer.
Q2What is the last date to apply for NABARD Specialist recruitment 2026?
A
The online application window closes on 13.07.2026.
Q3What is the salary for an AI Engineer at NABARD?
A
The AI Engineer post carries a consolidated remuneration of Rs. 24–45 lakh per annum, the highest among all six posts notified.
Q4Is there a written exam for NABARD Specialist recruitment 2026?
A
No. Selection is based on shortlisting (1:3 ratio) followed directly by an interview — there is no written test.
Q5What is the application fee for NABARD Specialist posts?
A
SC/ST/PwBD candidates pay only Rs. 150 (intimation charges), while all other candidates pay Rs. 850 (Rs. 700 application fee + Rs. 150 intimation charges), exclusive of GST.
Q6What is the place of posting for NABARD DDMABI Specialist posts?
A
All selected candidates will be posted at NABARD's Head Office in Mumbai.
Q7What is the contract period for NABARD Specialist posts?
A
The initial contract is for 2 years, extendable up to a maximum of 5 years based on annual performance review.
Q8What qualification is required for the NABARD Data Scientist post?
A
A full-time B.E./B.Tech (minimum 60% marks) or M.E./M.Tech in Computer Science/IT/Data Science/ML & AI, or an MCA, along with at least 3 years of relevant experience.
Q9Are NABARD Specialists eligible for PF, pension, or gratuity?
A
No. Since this is a contract engagement, Specialists are not entitled to any superannuation benefits.
Q10Where can I apply for NABARD Specialist recruitment 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted only online through www.nabard.org under the Career Notices section — no other mode of application is accepted.
