How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the career link on EIL's official website http://www.engineersindia.com. Online submission will be permitted between 0000 hrs on 09.05.2026 till 2359 hrs on 01.06.2026. A candidate can apply for one position only — in case of multiple applications, the latest one shall be considered as final and older applications shall be rejected without notice. Applications once submitted cannot be withdrawn.
  • Any change in advertisement (including extension of last date) shall be published on EIL's website only — no separate notification shall be issued in press/other medium. The email address specified in the application should be valid/functional for at least 24 months from the date of submission.

  • Note: OBC-NCL certificate must be valid as on the closing date of application (25.05.2026). Any candidate applying without uploading valid OBC-NCL certificate will be rejected. Only castes included in the Central list of OBC as given in the official website of National Commission for Backward Classes will be considered.
