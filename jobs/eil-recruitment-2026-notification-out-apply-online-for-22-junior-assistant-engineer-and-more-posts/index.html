Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How many vacancies are there in EIL Special Recruitment Drive 2026?

Engineers India Limited has announced 22 vacancies across 15 posts under the Special Recruitment Drive for SC, ST and OBC (NCL) candidates through Advertisement No. HRD/Rectt./Advt./2026-27/04.

Q2. What are the online application dates for EIL Special Recruitment Drive 2026?

The online application window opens on 09 May 2026 (00:00 hrs) and closes on 01 June 2026 (23:59 hrs) on the EIL recruitment portal.

Q3. Which categories are eligible to apply for EIL Special Recruitment Drive 2026?

This EIL recruitment is a Special Recruitment Drive exclusively for SC, ST and OBC (Non‑Creamy Layer) candidates, and applications from other categories will not be considered.

Q4. What posts are available in EIL Special Recruitment Drive 2026?

The notification includes posts such as Senior Manager (Projects/Electrical), Manager (HR), Engineer (various disciplines), Jr. Draftsman (Process/Civil/Piping/Civil‑Structural) and Junior Assistant Grade‑III (HR & Hospitality).

Q5. What is the selection process for EIL Special Recruitment Drive 2026?

EIL will shortlist candidates based on eligibility and call them for Interview (for Senior Manager/Manager/Engineer) and Skill Test (for Jr. Draftsman and Junior Assistant posts), and only shortlisted candidates will be informed via email.

Q6. What is the pay scale and approximate CTC in EIL Special Recruitment Drive 2026?

The pay scales range from Rs. 90,000–2,40,000 for Senior Manager, Rs. 80,000–2,20,000 for Manager, Rs. 60,000–1,80,000 for Engineer, and Rs. 25,000/26,500–1,15,000 for Jr. Draftsman/Junior Assistant, with approximate CTC going up to about Rs. 30.29 lakh per annum for Senior Manager.

Q7. What is the eligibility cut-off date for age, qualification and experience in EIL SRD 2026?

For this Special Recruitment Drive, 30 April 2026 is the cut‑off date for calculating upper age, minimum educational qualifications and required post‑qualification experience.

Q8. Can candidates apply for more than one post in EIL Special Recruitment Drive 2026?

No, each candidate can apply for only one position; in case of multiple applications, the latest submitted application will be treated as final and the rest will be rejected without notice.
