How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official SSSB Punjab website: https://sssb.punjab.gov.in and click on the “Apply Online” link.
  • 2For new users, click on “Registration” and fill in the required details to generate User ID (Email ID) and Password.
  • 3After successful registration, log in using your User ID (Email ID) and Password to access the online application form.
  • 4Fill in personal, educational and category details carefully and upload a recent passport size photograph and scanned signature.
  • 5Click on “Attach Annexure” and upload scanned copies of Matric (10th) certificate, educational qualification certificates and other mandatory documents in PDF format.
  • 6Click on “Make Payment” and pay the application fee online as per your category.
  • 7After successful fee payment, click on “Submit” to complete the application process and download/print the submitted application form for future reference.
  • 8Fill the online form strictly as per your correct category and category code; category and category code cannot be changed after final submission.
  • 9Candidates will be solely responsible for any mistake made while filling the online form; once submitted, the data cannot be edited.
  • 10Scan and upload all required certificates (Matric/10th, educational qualification and other relevant documents) in PDF format only as per the portal instructions.
  • 11No fee exemption or concession is admissible; non-payment of application fee will render the application incomplete and liable to rejection.
  • 12If the online fee transaction fails for any reason and the application is not submitted, the fee once paid will be non-refundable.
  • 13Applications submitted and fees paid through any mode other than the prescribed online method will not be accepted and such applications will be rejected.
  • 14SSSB Punjab reserves the right to increase or decrease the number of vacancies, withdraw the requisition or cancel the advertisement at any stage of the recruitment process; such decisions will be binding on all candidates.
