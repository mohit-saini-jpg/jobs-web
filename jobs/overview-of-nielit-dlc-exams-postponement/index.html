NIELIT DLC Exams Postponed for the January 2026, February 2026, and March 2026 cycles due to administrative reasons. The National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (NIELIT) has officially announced that all Digital Literacy Course (DLC) examinations -including CCC, CCCP, ECC, ACC, and BCC -scheduled in these three cycles will now be merged and conducted together during the April 2026 exam cycle.

This consolidation aims to streamline the examination process for the large number of candidates. The revised schedule and admit cards for the April 2026 cycle will be published shortly on the official NIELIT student portal. Candidates are advised not to panic and to regularly check student.nielit.gov.in for the latest updates on the new timetable and admit cards. This article provides complete details about the postponement, affected cycles, new schedule, and what candidates should do next.

Official Update: Due to administrative reasons, the DLC (CCC/CCCP/ECC/ACC/BCC) examinations scheduled for the January 2026, February 2026 & March 2026 exam cycles have been postponed. These exams will now be conducted together during the DLC April 2026 exam cycle. The tentative exam schedule will be published shortly.

NIELIT DLC Exams Postponed – Official Announcement

According to an official public notice issued by NIELIT, the examinations scheduled for the January, February, and March 2026 cycles have been deferred due to administrative reasons and will now be held together in April 2026.

Affected Exam Cycles

  • January 2026 Cycle
  • February 2026 Cycle
  • March 2026 Cycle

All registered candidates for these cycles will automatically be shifted to the consolidated April 2026 exam cycle. No fresh registration is required.

New Exam Schedule for April 2026

The postponed examinations from January, February, and March 2026 will be conducted together during the NIELIT DLC April 2026 Exam Cycle. A tentative exam schedule will be published shortly on the official portal. The final timetable will accommodate the combined volume of candidates from all three cycles.

Admit Cards to be Issued Later

In line with the revised schedule, the release of admit cards has also been deferred. NIELIT has clarified that hall tickets for the DLC April 2026 examination will be issued at a later stage, once the final timetable is formalized.

Candidates are advised to closely monitor official updates, as the release of hall tickets will be critical in confirming individual exam dates and centers.

Details Mentioned on Admit Card

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Roll number / registration number
  • Exam date and time slot
  • Examination centre address
  • Course name (CCC / CCCP / ECC / ACC / BCC)
  • Reporting time and exam-day instructions
  • Signature / authority stamp
