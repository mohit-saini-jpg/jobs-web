Service Bond & Deposit Conditions - APGENCO AEE Appointment 2026

Every candidate appointed under the APGENCO AEE 2026 notification must execute a Service Bond at the time of joining. The bond requires the candidate to serve APGENCO for a minimum of 5 years after completion of the 2-year probation period - effectively a total commitment of 7 years . During this entire 7-year bond period, original certificates (degree, date of birth, community, etc.) must be deposited with APGENCO. Service Bond must be executed on a Non-Judicial Stamp Paper worth ₹110/- at the time of joining. Original certificates (Degree, DOB, Community) deposited with APGENCO for the entire 7-year bond period. If certificates are needed during the bond period for a bonafide purpose, a security deposit of ₹1,00,000/- (refundable) must be paid before obtaining them. Candidates leaving service during the 2-year probation must refund all emoluments received plus ₹5,00,000/- as liquidated

Reservation Policy - APGENCO AEE Recruitment 2026

The APGENCO AEE 2026 reservation follows vertical reservations for SC (Group-I, II & III), ST, BC (A, B, C, D & E), and EWS candidates as per applicable Andhra Pradesh Government Orders. Horizontal reservations of 33⅓% for Women and 4% for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities are implemented within respective vertical categories. Candidates from states other than Andhra Pradesh will be considered under the General (unreserved) category only. Local candidature (95% of vacancies) is reserved for candidates from the respective zone as per the Presidential Order 2025. Open (non-local) vacancies constitute 5% of total vacancies in each branch. PwBD candidates must hold a valid SADAREM certificate issued by a competent authority.   • 1The APGENCO AEE Apply Online 2026 process is available at apgenco.gov.in or apvidyutrecruitment.apcfss.in. Applications sent through any other mode will not be entertained. Certificates not uploaded by the deadline will result in non-generation of hall tickets.
  • 2Visit the official portal: Go to https://apgenco.gov.in or https://apvidyutrecruitment.apcfss.in.
  • 3Register using Aadhaar-linked mobile number: Click the registration link, enter your details, and complete OTP verification sent to your Aadhaar-linked mobile number. Save your Registration ID and password.
  • 4Pay the application fee: Pay ₹1,000/- through the Payment Gateway. Note down the Journal Number issued on successful payment. Journal Number alone does not mean application is submitted.
  • 5Log in and fill the application form: Use your Registration ID and password to log in. Fill all required fields - educational qualifications, zone preferences, academic details, etc. Do not leave any relevant column blank.
  • 6Exercise preferences: Select your preferred Power Utility (for Electrical/Civil: APGENCO, APTRANSCO, APEPDCL, APCPDCL, APSPDCL) and preferred Zone/Unit. These preferences are final and cannot be changed after submission.
  • 7Upload scanned documents: Upload a recent passport-size photograph, scanned signature, and all required certificates (community, EWS, PwBD/SADAREM, qualification, study/residence certificates, etc.). All documents must be clear, legible scanned copies.
  • 8Review and submit: Verify all entries carefully before final submission. Incomplete or incorrect applications will be summarily rejected.
  • 9Print confirmation: Save or print the submitted application form for future reference.
  • 10Important: Candidates currently in government/autonomous/aided institution service must inform their Head of Office in writing that they are applying for this recruitment and must furnish a No Objection Certificate while submitting the online application.
