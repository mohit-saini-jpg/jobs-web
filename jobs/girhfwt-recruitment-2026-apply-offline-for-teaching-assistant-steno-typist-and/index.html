GIRHFWT Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Teaching Assistant - I: M.Sc. in Biostatistics / Epidemiology / Statistics with 3 years of experience. Teaching Assistant - II: Any Degree with MPH (Master of Public Health) and 5 years of experience in Teaching / Field Work. OR M.Sc. in Public Health / M.A. in Social Work / M.A. in Sociology / M.Sc. in Psychology / Master's in Social Science with 3 years of experience in Teaching / Field Work. Librarian: Graduated in Arts/Science with B.L.I.Sc. (or) M.L.I.Sc., along with not less than 5 years of experience in the respective field in reputed institutions or organizations. Projectionist / Project Coordinator: A Bachelor's Degree in Civil or Electrical Engineering. Certificate in Computer systems. A minimum of three years of relevant experience in building, electrical, and general office management. Desirable MBA. Steno Typist: Any Degree and Certification in any recognized Computer Course
