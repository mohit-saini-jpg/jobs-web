How to check GSEB Class 10 Result 2026 online

Follow the steps below to check and download the Gujarat Board SSC Result 2026 in online mode.

  • Visit the official website of Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board at gseb.org.
  • On the homepage, click on the link that says "GSEB SSC Result 2026" or "Gujarat Board Class 10 Result 2026".
  • You will be redirected to the SSC result login page.
  • Enter your six-digit seat number in the specified field carefully as mentioned on your admit card.
  • Click on the submit/go button to proceed.
  • Your GSEB Class 10 Result 2026 provisional marksheet will appear on the screen, showing subject-wise marks, total marks, grade, and result status.
  • Download the online marksheet and take a clear printout for future reference, admissions, and documentation.
