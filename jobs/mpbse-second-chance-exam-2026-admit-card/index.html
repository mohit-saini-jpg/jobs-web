MPBSE Second Chance Exam 2026 Admit Card

Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (MPBSE) has released the admit card for the MP Board second chance exams 2026 for Class 10 and Class 12 students. These second chance (second main) exams are for students who could not clear one or more subjects in the main board exams and have applied for the second examination.

As per the official schedule, the MP Board Class 10 and 12 second exams 2026 will be conducted from 7 May to 25 May 2026 in the morning shift from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Students must carry the MPBSE second chance exam 2026 admit card to the exam centre on each exam day, otherwise they will not be allowed to appear for the exam.

How to Download MPBSE Second Chance Exam 2026 Admit Card

Students can download their MPBSE second chance exam admit card 2026 by following these steps:

  • Visit the MPBSE MPOnline portal: https://mpbse.mponline.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the “Examinations / Examination / Enrollment Forms” section.
  • Look for and click on the link “Second Examination Admit Card 2026” or “Second Exam Admit Card 2026 (Class 10/12)”.
  • On the admit card page, enter your Application Number or Roll Number and the captcha/security code shown on the screen.
  • Click on “Submit” / “Search” to view your MPBSE second exam admit card 2026.
  • Check your name, class, subjects and exam centre details carefully, then download and print 2–3 clear copies on A4‑size paper.

Regular students may also be able to collect a signed/stamped copy from their school, but it is safer to keep a downloaded copy from MPOnline as well.

Details Mentioned on MPBSE Second Exam Admit Card 2026

The MPBSE second chance exam admit card carries important exam and candidate details, including:

  • Student’s name and photograph
  • Father’s/Mother’s name
  • Class (10th or 12th) and stream (for Class 12: Arts/Science/Commerce)
  • Roll number and application number
  • Exam name: Second Examination 2026 Exam 2026
  • Subject‑wise second exam dates and codes (7–25 May 2026)
  • Exam time (9:00 AM – 12:00 PM)
  • Exam centre name, centre code and full address
  • Important exam‑day instructions and guidelines

If any information on the admit card is incorrect (spelling of name, wrong subject, exam centre, etc.), the student should contact their school or MPBSE helpline immediately.

Documents Required with MPBSE Second Exam Admit Card 2026

On each day of the MP Board second exam 2026, students must carry:

  • A printed copy of the MPBSE Second Chance Exam 2026 Admit Card (clear A4 printout).
  • One valid photo ID proof such as: School ID card (preferred for regular students) Aadhaar Card Other government‑issued ID, if accepted by the school/centre.
  • School ID card (preferred for regular students)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Other government‑issued ID, if accepted by the school/centre.

Without a valid hall ticket and ID proof, candidates may not be allowed to enter the examination hall.

Exam‑Day Instructions for MPBSE Second Chance Exams 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the MP Board second exams, students should follow these instructions:

  • Reach the exam centre at least 30–45 minutes before 9:00 AM to complete verification and seating; late entry after the scheduled time may not be allowed.
  • Carry only essential items: admit card, ID proof and permitted stationery (blue/black pens, pencils, eraser, geometry box if needed).
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not allowed), Bluetooth devices, notes or any electronic gadgets inside the exam hall; such items are strictly prohibited.
  • Follow all instructions given by invigilators and printed on the admit card; any unfair means can result in cancellation of the exam.
  • Keep your MPBSE second exam admit card safe even after the exam, as it may be required for result verification and future reference.
