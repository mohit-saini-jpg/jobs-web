How To Apply

  • Candidates are required to fill in the application form (Proforma attached with the official notification) duly complete in all respects and submit it at the venue of the walk-in interview. No online application process is involved - candidates must appear in person at any one of the listed venues on the specified dates. The completed application form has to be submitted at the venue for verification along with original documents, one set of attested copies of requisite documents, and two recent passport size photographs.
  • Those working in Government/PSUs may apply through proper channel or submit NOC at the time of interview or should submit a proper relieving letter from their present employer in the event of selection in IRCTC. No TA/DA will be paid to candidates for attending the walk-in interview. Candidate can appear for interview at any one convenient place only.
  • Documents Required at Walk-in Interview (Originals in chronological order, along with one set of self-attested photocopies):
  • 10th class Marksheet & Certificate
  • 12th class Marksheet & Certificate
  • Graduation year-wise all Marksheets & Certificate/Degree (Provisional Certificate/Degree from concerned University may also be considered). Candidates passed out from IHMs should carry all six semesters marksheets, consolidated marksheet issued from IGNOU & degree/certificate issued from IGNOU.
  • Post-Graduation or any other higher academic/education (year-wise all Marksheets & Certificate), if applicable
  • All Experience Certificates in chronological order (if any)
  • Caste certificate supporting SC/ST/OBC category (OBC certificate should be for non-creamy layer and not more than 1 year old)
  • EWS/PwBD certificate (if applicable)
  • Proof of Identity & Proof of Address
  • Two recent passport size photographs
  • Any other relevant documents
  • In absence of any of the above documents in original, the candidate will not be allowed to appear in the walk-in interview. Candidates should carry one set of self-attested photocopy of all above documents along with all originals.
  • Walk-in Interview Venue (Delhi): IRCTC, North Zone: B-148, 11th Floor, Statesman House, Barakhamba Road, Connaught Place, New Delhi - 110001
