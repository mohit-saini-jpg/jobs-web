IRCTC Hospitality Monitor Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is IRCTC Hospitality Monitor Recruitment 2026?
A
IRCTC Hospitality Monitor Recruitment 2026 is a contractual recruitment drive by Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC), North Zone, to fill 92 Hospitality Monitor posts for on‑board and static catering services for an initial period of 2 years, extendable by 1 year based on requirement and performance.
Q2How many vacancies are announced under IRCTC Hospitality Monitor Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 92 vacancies have been notified for the post of Hospitality Monitor on a contractual basis under IRCTC North Zone, with reservation applicable for SC, ST, OBC‑NCL, EWS, PwBD and Ex‑servicemen as per Government of India rules.
Q3What is the eligibility criteria for IRCTC Hospitality Monitor 2026?
A
Candidates must be Indian nationals, not more than 27 years of age as on 01‑05‑2026, and possess a full‑time B.Sc. in Hospitality and Hotel Administration, BBA/MBA in Culinary Arts, B.Sc. Hotel Management and Catering Science, or MBA (Tourism and Hotel Management) from recognized institutes/universities as specified in the notification, while freshers with these qualifications are also eligible.
Q4What is the age limit and age relaxation for IRCTC Hospitality Monitor Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit is 27 years for UR candidates as on 01‑05‑2026, with relaxations of 5 years for SC/ST, 3 years for OBC (Non‑Creamy Layer), 10 years for PwBD, and additional relaxation for Ex‑servicemen equal to their defence service plus 3 years as per Government of India norms.
Q5What is the selection process for IRCTC Hospitality Monitor Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of a walk‑in Personal Interview where candidates are assessed based on their educational credentials, relevant skills and performance in the interview, and a merit list is prepared for engagement subject to document verification and medical fitness as per IRCTC standards.
Q6What is the salary and allowances for IRCTC Hospitality Monitor (North Zone)?
A
Selected Hospitality Monitors will receive a consolidated remuneration of Rs. 30,000 per month (inclusive of statutory deductions), along with daily allowance for on‑board duty, eligible lodging charges for outstation night stay, national holiday allowance as per policy, medical insurance reimbursement, and they must also deposit a refundable security of Rs. 25,000 in the form of a demand draft in favour of IRCTC Ltd, New Delhi.
Q7What are the walk‑in interview dates and venues for IRCTC Hospitality Monitor Recruitment 2026?
A
Walk‑in interviews will be conducted from 03 June 2026 to 24 June 2026 between 11:00 AM and 05:00 PM at multiple venues including IRCTC North Zone office in New Delhi, Institute of Hotel Management in Srinagar, IRCTC regional offices at Chandigarh, Jaipur and Lucknow, and candidates can appear at any one convenient centre only.
Q8What is the place of posting for selected IRCTC Hospitality Monitors?
A
Selected candidates may be posted anywhere under IRCTC North Zone including Delhi‑NCR, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Himachal Pradesh, Punjab, Haryana, Uttarakhand, Chandigarh, Jammu & Kashmir, and can also be deployed at other locations across India at the discretion of IRCTC.
Q9What are the main job responsibilities of an IRCTC Hospitality Monitor?
A
Hospitality Monitors are responsible for supervising and monitoring food production, quality and service in trains and units, ensuring adherence to IRCTC’s hospitality standards and statutory norms, coordinating with staff and vendors, handling passenger complaints and feedback, maintaining reports, and supporting overall customer service and hygiene compliance.
Q10How can candidates apply for IRCTC Hospitality Monitor Recruitment 2026?
A
There is no online application; candidates must download and fill the prescribed application form attached with the notification, and submit it in person at the chosen walk‑in interview venue along with original documents, one set of self‑attested photocopies, and two recent passport photos on the scheduled date and time.
