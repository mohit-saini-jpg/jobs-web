Short Information

Berhampur University Recruitment 2026 notification is out to fill 95 vacancies for Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor posts. Candidates can apply online only from 07.05.2026 to 10.06.2026 through Berhampur University website buodisha.edu.in.

Education QualificationAny Masters Degree, M.Phil/Ph.D

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the HIMS Portal (https://hims.odisha.gov.in/) which will be available from 07.05.2026 onwards. No other mode of application will be accepted. The online portal is active from 07-05-2026 to 10-06-2026 (Midnight). In addition to online submission, a hard copy of the downloaded submitted application along with all relevant documents must be sent via Speed Post to reach the Registrar on or before 20.06.2026.
