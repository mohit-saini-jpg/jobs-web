RITES Individual Consultant Recruitment 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Candidates applying for any of the four RITES Individual Consultant categories must satisfy the educational qualification, experience, and age criteria described below. Eligibility is reckoned as on the last date of application submission, i.e., 26 July 2026. All four categories require a B.E. or B.Tech degree in Civil Engineering. Where a specialisation is claimed as part of the qualifying degree, candidates must submit a certificate from their university or institution clearly specifying that specialisation.
