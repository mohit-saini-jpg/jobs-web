FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the FACT Engineer (Chemical) recruitment?
A
The last date to apply and attend the walk-in selection for the Engineer (Chemical) post is 18th June 2026. Candidates are advised to ensure they complete all formalities by this date.
Q2What is the educational qualification required for the Engineer (Chemical) post?
A
Candidates must possess a Bachelor's Degree in Chemical Engineering or Petrochemical Engineering or Chemical Technology or Petrochemical Technology or Petroleum Refining & Petrochemical Engineering or Polymer Technology.
Q3What is the age limit for the Engineer (Chemical) position?
A
The maximum age limit for the Engineer (Chemical) post is 26 years. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST, OBC (NCL), and Ex-Service personnel as per government directives.
Q4What is the salary offered for the Engineer (Chemical) role?
A
The selected candidate will receive a consolidated pay of Rs. 31,000/- per month, with a 3% annual increase. Other benefits like leave, ESI, and PF are applicable as per company rules.
Q5How can I apply for the FACT Engineer (Chemical) vacancy?
A
The application process involves online registration followed by a walk-in selection. Candidates need to register online, fill the application form, and attend the walk-in interview with all required documents.
Q6Where is the walk-in interview for FACT Engineer (Chemical) recruitment scheduled?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at the HR Department, FEDO Buildings, Eloor, Udyogamandal-683501, Kerala.
Q7Is there any preference given to candidates with specific training?
A
Yes, preference will be given to candidates who have completed Apprenticeship Training in the prescribed qualification in FACT and submitted their Certificate of Proficiency (COP).
Q8What documents are required for the walk-in selection?
A
Candidates must bring original certificates and self-attested copies of all relevant documents, including ID proof, educational certificates, and category certificates if applicable.
Q9What is the nature of the job?
A
The job is on a contract basis for the Engineer (Chemical) position at FACT.
Q10What is the advertisement number for this recruitment?
A
The advertisement number for this recruitment is 03/2026.
