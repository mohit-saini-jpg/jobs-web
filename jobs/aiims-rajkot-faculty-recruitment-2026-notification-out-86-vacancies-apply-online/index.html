AIIMS Rajkot Faculty Recruitment 2026

AIIMS Rajkot is a Central Autonomous Body (CAB) established under an Act of Parliament, functioning under the Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) division of the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India. The Faculty Recruitment 2026 aims to fill Group-A posts on Direct Recruitment basis, failing which on Deputation basis. All vacancies earmarked for SC, ST, and OBC are backlog vacancies except the post of Professor in Community and Family Medicine. The last date to apply online is 10th July 2026 .

AIIMS Rajkot Faculty Selection Process 2026

Application Scrutiny: All online applications are received and scrutinised by the Selection Committee of AIIMS Rajkot. Only eligible candidates are called for the next stage. Shortlisting: If the vacancy-to-candidate ratio exceeds 1:10, the Selection Committee may shortlist on the basis of an objective screening score (Appendix 1 criteria). Document Verification: Shortlisted candidates must produce original academic, experience, category, and registration certificates before the interview. Personal Interview: Eligible candidates appear before the Standing Selection Committee at AIIMS Rajkot in person. Candidates must make a 5-minute PowerPoint presentation on their work and achievements. Final Selection: The decision of the Competent Authority regarding selection is final and binding. Note: Interview dates will be communicated via the official AIIMS Rajkot website and email only.
