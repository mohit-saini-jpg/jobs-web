Job Overview

Nizam's Institute of Medical Sciences (NIMS), Hyderabad has invited applications for 122 contract posts (98 Technician + 24 Administrative) for deployment at Telangana Institute of Medical Sciences (TIMS), Sanath Nagar. FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for NIMS TIMS Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit applications is 06 July 2026 by 4:00 PM, to be sent to the Executive Registrar, NIMS, Punjagutta, Hyderabad.
Q2How many total vacancies are there in NIMS TIMS Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 122 total vacancies — 98 Technician posts and 24 Administrative posts — for deployment at TIMS, Sanath Nagar.
Q3What is the application fee for NIMS TIMS Recruitment 2026?
A
The fee is ₹1,000 for General candidates and ₹500 for SC/ST candidates, payable via Demand Draft in favour of "The Director, NIMS," Hyderabad.
Q4What is the age limit for NIMS TIMS posts?
A
Technical, Allied, and Support Services posts have a maximum age of 35 years, while Administrative posts have a maximum age of 45 years, both calculated as on 01 July 2026.
Q5Is there an online application process for NIMS TIMS Recruitment?
A
No, NIMS TIMS Recruitment 2026 follows an offline application process; candidates must send their application and CV by post or in person to the Executive Registrar's office.
Q6What is the selection process for NIMS TIMS Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection depends on applicant numbers per post: up to 10 candidates get a direct interview, 11–30 candidates undergo screening plus interview, and more than 30 candidates face a written exam/skill test followed by interview.
Q7What is the salary for Technician posts at TIMS?
A
All Technician posts, including Lab Technician, X-Ray Technician, and Anaesthesia Technician, carry a consolidated salary of ₹32,500 per month.
Q8How long is the NIMS TIMS contract engagement valid?
A
The engagement is valid for one year from the date of joining or till the actual requirement ceases, whichever is earlier, and does not guarantee regular appointment.
Q9Which Technician post has the highest number of vacancies?
A
The Lab Technician Gr-II post has the highest vacancies, with 34 openings, followed by Anaesthesia Technician with 20 posts.
Q10Can government employees get age relaxation in NIMS TIMS Recruitment?
A
Yes, Telangana Government Employees can get up to 5 years of age relaxation based on their length of regular service, though no relaxation applies to Open Category vacancies.
