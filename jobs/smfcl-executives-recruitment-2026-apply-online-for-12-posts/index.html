How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply only through online mode; no other form of application will be accepted. Before applying, candidates should ensure that they fulfil the eligibility conditions and have all necessary documents scanned in the required formats for upload.
  • Steps to apply online:
  • Visit the official website of SMFCL at http://www.sdclindia.com and open the “Careers” section.
  • Click on the link for SMFCL Rectt. Advt. No. 01/2026 and read the detailed advertisement and instructions carefully.
  • Click on “Apply Online” and complete the registration / application form by entering personal, educational, category and experience details correctly.
  • On submission of application details, a system‑generated Registration Number will appear at the top of the filled form; note it down for future login and correspondence.
  • Upload all required documents under the “Upload Document” section in clear and legible scanned copies (PDF/JPEG) as specified.
  • Proceed to “Make Payment” section and pay the applicable application fee online (Rs. 1,000/- for UR/OBC‑NCL/EWS; Nil for SC/ST/PwBD).
  • After successful payment, download the final submitted application form and keep a printout for future reference and production at the time of selection, if called.
  • Documents Required to be Scanned and Uploaded:
  • High School (Class 10th) certificate as proof of Date of Birth.
  • Certificates and marksheets of all academic/professional qualifications (10th, 12th, Diploma, Graduation, Post‑Graduation, CA/CMA/LL.B/CS/B.Tech, etc., as applicable).
  • Percentage conversion document (wherever CGPA is awarded) issued by the University/Institute.
  • Valid SC/ST/OBC‑NCL/EWS/PwBD certificate in prescribed format of Government of India, wherever reservation is claimed.
  • Proof of Identity/Address such as Passport, Voter ID, Driving License, Aadhaar Card, etc.
  • PAN Card.
  • Proof of each period of experience claimed (offer letters, joining orders, relieving letters, service certificates, relevant pay slips, etc.) merged into a single PDF in chronological order.
  • Any other document supporting the claims made in the online application.
  • Documents to be Produced at the time of CBT / Document Verification / Interview:
  • Printout of CBT Admit Card / Interview Call Letter along with original photo identity proof and one self‑attested photocopy.
  • Original certificates and mark sheets of all educational qualifications and experience, along with original category certificates for SC/ST/OBC‑NCL/EWS/PwBD candidates.
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) / relieving order for candidates serving in Government/PSU/Autonomous Bodies, as required.
  • There is no requirement to send the printout of the application form or any documents by post to SMFCL; all processes will be handled online and at the time of selection stages.

Important Instructions

  • The cut‑off date for determining age, qualification and post‑qualification experience is 18.06.2026; any qualification or experience acquired after this date will not be considered. Mere submission of application does not guarantee shortlisting; candidates must meet all eligibility criteria and clear the selection stages. If at any stage it is found that a candidate has furnished incorrect/false information or suppressed any material fact, his/her candidature/appointment is liable to be cancelled/terminated without notice. If a candidate applies for more than one post and CBT for multiple posts is held in a single session, he/she will be allowed to appear in CBT for only one post/discipline; remaining applications will not be considered and fee will not be refunded. Change of category, post, city for CBT, email ID, or any key information after submission of online application will not be entertained. All communication will be made only through email and SMFCL website; candidates must keep their registered email ID active for at least one year. Selected candidates can be posted anywhere in SMFCL offices or its subsidiaries in India or abroad as per organizational requirements. SMFCL reserves the right to cancel, restrict or modify the recruitment process or number of vacancies at any stage without assigning any reason. Canvassing in any form will disqualify the candidature. In case of any dispute, only courts at Delhi shall have jurisdiction; in case of discrepancy between versions, the English version of the advertisement will prevail.
