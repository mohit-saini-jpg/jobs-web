How to Check Burdwan University Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your Burdwan University Semester II result:

  • Visit the official website - https://www.buruniv.ac.in
  • Navigate to the Examination or Results section
  • Click on UG Results or the relevant result link
  • Select your course and semester
  • Open the result PDF and search your roll number
  • Download and save the result for future reference
