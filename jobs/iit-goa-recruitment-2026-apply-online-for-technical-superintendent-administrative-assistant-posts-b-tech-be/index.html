Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationBachelor's Degree (55% marks), Diploma in Electrical/Electronics & Communication/CSE/IT/ECE (60% marks), B.Tech/B.E. in relevant fields (60% marks)
DetailsTechnical Superintendent (Electrical): 3-year Diploma in Electrical / Electronics & Communication Engineering + 60% marks + 6 years post-diploma experience OR B.Tech / B.E. in Electrical / Electronics & Communication Engineering + 60% marks + 3 years post-degree experience. Full-time M.Tech/M.E. duration (normal period) counted towards experience (max 2 years). Technical Superintendent: 3-year Diploma in CSE / ECE / IT or equivalent + 60% marks + 6 years post-diploma experience OR Bachelor’s degree (non B.Tech/B.E.) in CSE / ECE / IT or equivalent + 60% marks + 5 years experience OR B.Tech / B.E. in CSE / ECE / IT or equivalent + 60% marks + 3 years experience. Full-time Master’s duration counted towards experience (max 2 years). Administrative Assistant: Bachelor’s degree with at least 55% marks or equivalent CGPA.
