How to Check NEET MDS Result 2026 PDF Online

This section addresses navigational intent (“NEET MDS result 2026 kaise dekhen”).

Follow these steps to check NEET MDS 2026 result PDF on the NBEMS website:

  • Visit the official websites natboard.edu.in or nbe.edu.in.
  • Click on the “Examinations” tab and choose “NEET MDS” from the entrance examinations list.
  • Under the “NEET MDS 2026” section, click on the “Result” notification.
  • Open the notification and click on the NEET MDS 2026 result PDF link.
  • The result PDF will open; use the search/find option (Ctrl+F) and enter your roll number.
  • Check your roll number, marks obtained out of 960 and NEET MDS 2026 rank.
  • Download and save the PDF for future reference, printing a copy if needed for counselling.

How to Download NEET MDS 2026 Scorecard

NBEMS will also release individual NEET MDS 2026 scorecards for candidates, especially for All India Quota (AIQ) seats.

Steps to download NEET MDS 2026 scorecard:

  • Click on the “Download NEET MDS 2026 scorecard” link on natboard.edu.in / nbe.edu.in when activated.
  • You will be redirected to the candidate login page.
  • Enter your User ID and Password as generated during NEET MDS 2026 registration.
  • After logging in, your NEET MDS 2026 scorecard will appear on the screen.
  • Verify details such as roll number, category, total marks, percentile, All India Rank, 50% AIQ rank and category rank.
  • Download and print multiple copies of the scorecard for use during counselling and document verification.

Details Mentioned in NEET MDS 2026 Result and Scorecard

Candidates should carefully verify all details printed in the NEET MDS 2026 result PDF and individual scorecard.

Details in NEET MDS 2026 result PDF:

  • Roll number of the candidate
  • Application ID
  • Total score out of 960
  • NEET MDS 2026 All India Rank

Additional details in NEET MDS 2026 scorecard:

  • Candidate’s name and category
  • PwD status (Yes/No)
  • NEET MDS 2026 Rank
  • All India 50% Quota Rank
  • All India 50% Quota Category Rank
  • Category-wise cutoff scores/percentile for reference

If any discrepancy is noticed in demographic details, candidates should contact NBEMS promptly with supporting documents.

NEET MDS Result 2026 - Important Instructions

  • NEET MDS 2026 result will not be sent by post; it is available only in online mode.
  • There is no provision for re‑evaluation, re‑checking or re‑totaling of NEET MDS 2026 result.
  • The result and scorecard are valid only for the current academic session for MDS admissions.
  • Candidates must preserve their scorecard and result PDF till completion of admission and registration at the allotted institute.
