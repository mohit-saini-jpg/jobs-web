How to Check Ramadevi University RDWU Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your Ramadevi University Result 2026 for UG and PG 3rd semester exams:

  • Visit the official RDWU website https://rdwu.ac.in.
  • Click on the Examination/Notice/Result section or directly open https://rdwu.ac.in/ReadMore.aspx?event=1.
  • Locate the relevant notification: Regarding Publication of Result: PG 3rd Sem (Regular & Back) Examination 2025 Regarding Publication of Result: UG 3rd Sem (Regular, Back & Special Back) Examination 2025
  • Regarding Publication of Result: PG 3rd Sem (Regular & Back) Examination 2025
  • Regarding Publication of Result: UG 3rd Sem (Regular, Back & Special Back) Examination 2025
  • Click on the View Result link (PDF or web page) corresponding to your category.
  • If the result is PDF-based, open the file and use Ctrl+F to search your roll number/name.
  • If redirected to an online result page, select your course and enter your roll number/registration number and any captcha/security code, then click Submit/View Result.
  • Check your subject-wise marks, total marks and result status, and download or print the provisional marksheet for future reference.
