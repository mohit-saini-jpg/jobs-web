How to Check the Dibrugarh University Result

  • Visit the official Dibrugarh University website at dibru.ac.in.
  • Go to the "Results" or "Posts" section on the homepage.
  • Locate the relevant notification, such as "Result of 6th Semester BCA Examination (CBCS)" or "Result of B.Sc 6th Semester Examinations (FYUGP)."
  • Click on the result link to open the PDF or result portal.
  • Search for your roll number or registration number using Ctrl+F if the result is in PDF format.
  • Download and save the result for future reference.

Details Mentioned on the Result

  • Candidate's Name
  • Roll Number and Registration Number
  • Subject-wise Marks
  • Overall Result Status (Pass/Fail/Withheld, as applicable)

What to Do After the Result

Candidates should carefully verify all details mentioned on the result, including name, roll number, and marks. In case of any discrepancy, candidates are advised to contact their respective college or the Dibrugarh University examination department for correction or clarification.

Important Instructions

  • Keep your roll number and registration number handy before checking the result.
  • Download and save a copy of the result PDF immediately after it is displayed.
  • For any discrepancy in marks or personal details, contact the university or your college promptly.
  • Regularly check the official website for updates on revaluation or rechecking, if announced separately.
