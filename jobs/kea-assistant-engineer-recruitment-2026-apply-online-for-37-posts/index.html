Qualification / Eligibility

Assistant Engineer (Civil) (Group-B): Degree in Civil Engineering or Technology or equivalent from recognized University and Computer literate (minimum six months course). Assistant Engineer (Civil) (Group-B): Bachelor degree of Engineering in Civil by a University established by law in India or equivalent. Assistant Engineer (Civil) (Group-B) (Contractual): Bachelor degree of Engineering in Civil by a University established by law in India or equivalent. Assistant Engineer (Computer Science) (Group-B): Degree in Computer Science Engineering or Technology or equivalent from recognized University and Computer literate (minimum six months course). Assistant Executive Engineer (Civil) (Group-B): Bachelor Engineering Degree in Civil awarded by a University established by law in India with three years experience in relevant field.
