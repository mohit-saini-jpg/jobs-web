BRTC Chichpalli Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Bamboo Research and Training Centre (BRTC), Chichpalli, under the Forest Department, Government of Maharashtra, has released an advertisement for 21 posts on a purely contract/fixed-term basis. The posts span academic, administrative, technical, and support roles at the Centre in Chandrapur district. Eligible candidates must submit their applications on or before 27 July 2026.

Who Can Apply - Eligibility Criteria

PostQualification Experience Essential
Head of DepartmentPh.D. in Mechanical or Civil Engineering and Technology/Design with First Class at Bachelor's or Master's level. Minimum 12 years' teaching/research/industry experience in Bamboo field, including at least 2 years post-Ph.D. experience in a leadership position, with proven research publications, funded projects and academic administration, as per AICTE norms wherever applicable. OR Bachelor's and Master's Degree in relevant discipline with minimum 15 years of experience in Teaching/Research/Industry, out of which at least 3 years shall be at the level of Lecturer (Selection Grade-II).
Visiting Faculty (Basic Science & Mathematics)Bachelor's Degree/Master's Degree with First Class in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics with minimum 5 years' teaching/research/industry experience. Eminent academicians, industry professionals, entrepreneurs or retired Government officers with expertise in Bamboo Technology or other specialized subjects offered by the Centre will be preferred.
Visiting Faculty (English)Bachelor's Degree/Master's Degree with First Class English with minimum 5 years' teaching/research/industry experience. Eminent academicians, industry professionals, entrepreneurs or retired Government officers with expertise in Bamboo Technology or other specialized subjects offered by the Centre will be preferred.
IT CoordinatorB.E./B.Tech. or equivalent with minimum 2 years' experience.
PRO / Training & Placement OfficerAny Graduate with an MBA/PG Diploma in HR, Marketing, Public Relations or Mass Communication preferred and minimum 3 years' experience in Public Relations, Training & Placement or Industry Liaison.
Junior Accountant / Office AssistantBachelor's Degree in Commerce (B.Com.) with knowledge of Tally ERP, GST, MS Office and Government accounting procedures. Minimum 2 years' experience.
Laboratory TechnicianDiploma in Bamboo Technology with minimum 2 years' laboratory/industry/teaching experience in bamboo OR any Diploma with 10 years' experience in Bamboo.
LibrarianBachelor's/Master's Degree in Library & Information Science (B.Lib./M.Lib.) with knowledge of library automation software and minimum 3 years' experience.
Assistant Course HeadBachelor's Degree (minimum four years) and preferably Master's Degree in the relevant discipline. Teaching/industrial experience in Bamboo Technology field is desirable.
Supervisor (CFC)Graduate in any discipline (MSW/MBA preferred) with minimum 5 years' experience in administration, project coordination or institutional management.
Assistant Machine SupervisorDiploma in Mechanical Engineering or Production Engineering with minimum 3 years' experience in bamboo processing machinery and workshop operations.
Designer / Handicraft SupervisorMaster's Degree in Design, Fine Arts, Handicrafts, Product Design or equivalent with minimum 3 years' experience in bamboo handicrafts or product development OR Bachelor's Degree in Design, Fine Arts, Handicrafts, Product Design or equivalent with minimum 6 years' relevant experience.
Bamboo Maintenance EngineerDiploma/Degree in Engineering/Technology OR Architect with minimum 5 years' experience in bamboo structures, construction and maintenance. Structural Engineer will be preferred.
Civil EngineerDiploma/Degree in Civil Engineering with minimum 3 years' experience in construction and project execution.
Electrical EngineerDiploma/Degree in Electrical Engineering with minimum 3 years' experience in electrical installations, maintenance and safety systems.
Girls Hostel WardenFemale Graduate with minimum 2 years' experience in hostel administration, student affairs or institutional management. Experience may be relaxed for Ex-servicemen/retired Government personnel as per rules.
Boys Hostel WardenMale Graduate with minimum 2 years' experience in hostel administration, student affairs or institutional management. Experience may be relaxed for Ex-servicemen/retired Government personnel as per rules.
Nursery SupervisorB.Sc./Diploma in Agriculture, Forestry, Horticulture or Diploma in Bamboo Technology. For Diploma, minimum 4 years' experience in nursery management, bamboo cultivation and plantation activities.

BRTC Chichpalli Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Download and fill the application in the prescribed proforma (Annexure-I).
  • 2Attach self-attested copies of all relevant documents: Educational Qualification certificates and mark-sheets, Experience certificates, Domicile certificate, Aadhaar card/PAN card, and a passport-size photograph.
  • 3Send the complete application to: The Director, Bamboo Research & Training Centre, Near Hanuman Mandir, Mul-Chandrapur Road, Chichpalli – 441224, Dist. Chandrapur, Maharashtra.
  • 4Ensure the application reaches the above address on or before the last date, 27 July 2026. Applications received after the due date shall not be entertained.
  • 5Candidates employed in Government/Semi-Government/Autonomous institutions must carry a No Objection Certificate (NOC) to submit at the time of interview.
