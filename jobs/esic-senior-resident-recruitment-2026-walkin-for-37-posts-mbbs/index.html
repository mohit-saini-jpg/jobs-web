FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for ESIC Senior Resident posts?
A
The walk-in interview for the ESIC Senior Resident posts is scheduled to be held on 09 June 2026. Candidates are advised to report at the venue as per the reporting time mentioned in the notification.
Q2What is the total number of vacancies for Senior Resident posts at ESIC?
A
ESIC has announced a total of 37 vacancies for the Senior Resident posts across various specializations. The detailed breakdown of vacancies per post is available in the official notification.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for ESIC Senior Resident posts?
A
Candidates must possess an MBBS degree with MD/MS/DNB or a Super Speciality MCh/DM/DrNB/FNB qualification as per the latest NMC/MCI guidelines to be eligible for these posts.
Q4What is the age limit for applying to ESIC Senior Resident positions?
A
The maximum age limit for applying to the Senior Resident posts is 45 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per the government rules and regulations.
Q5What is the salary offered for the ESIC Senior Resident posts?
A
The selected candidates for the Senior Resident posts will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 1,48,669/- per month.
Q6How can I apply for the ESIC Senior Resident recruitment?
A
The application process for this recruitment is through a Walk-in Interview. Eligible candidates need to attend the interview at the specified venue with all required documents on the scheduled date.
Q7What documents do I need to carry for the ESIC Walk-in Interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents, including ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates (if any), and category certificates (if applicable), along with one set of self-attested photocopies.
Q8Is there any application fee for ESIC Senior Resident posts?
A
Yes, there is an application fee. General and OBC category candidates need to pay Rs. 500/-, while SC/ST and Women candidates are exempted from paying the fee.
Q9What is the selection process for ESIC Senior Resident recruitment?
A
The selection process will involve Document Verification followed by an Interview. Candidates are advised to be prepared for both stages.
Q10Where is the walk-in interview for ESIC Senior Resident posts going to be held?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at the ESIC office in Faridabad, Haryana, with the pincode 121001. The exact address and reporting time are mentioned in the official notification.
