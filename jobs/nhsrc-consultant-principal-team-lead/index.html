How to Apply

  • 1All positions are contractual in nature; candidates will not be treated as employees of NHSRC and will not be entitled to benefits available to regular employees.
  • 2NHSRC shall have no obligation to offer regular employment to selected candidates during and/or after the completion of the contract period.
  • 3Fulfilling the eligibility criteria does not result in shortlisting for interview for Consultant posts.
  • 4Only shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview; a written test / screening test may also be conducted.
  • 5For the Principal Team Lead (IT) post, Government Officials can also apply on Deputation subject to meeting TOR criteria and submission of NOC from their controlling department.
  • 6All revisions / corrigendum (if any) will be hosted on the NHSRC website only; candidates should visit regularly.
  • 7Candidates selected may be considered for other vacant positions at NHSRC requiring similar skill sets and at an appropriate level.
  • 8All positions involve travel to States and Districts; candidates must be willing to undertake outstation travel.
  • 9Contract for all positions is initially till 31st March 2028 with further extension subject to satisfactory performance.
  • 10Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be as per applicable law.
