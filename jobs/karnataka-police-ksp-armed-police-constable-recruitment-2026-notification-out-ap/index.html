Karnataka Police KSP Armed Police Constable Recruitment : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1How many vacancies are there in KSP Armed Police Constable Recruitment 2026?
A
Karnataka State Police has announced a total of 1600 vacancies for the post of Armed Police Constable (Civil/DIAR) in 2026 - 1421 posts under the General Karnataka notification and 179 posts under the Kalyana Karnataka (Section 371J) notification.
Q2What is the last date to apply for KSP Constable 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 22 July 2026. The last date for fee payment is 24 July 2026. No applications will be accepted after this date.
Q3What is the written exam date for KSP Constable 2026?
A
The KSP Armed Police Constable written examination is scheduled for 06 September 2026. General Karnataka candidates will write from 10:30 AM to 12:00 PM and Kalyana Karnataka candidates from 2:30 PM to 4:00 PM.
Q4What is the age limit for KSP Armed Police Constable 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 33 years for general male candidates. SC/ST/OBC candidates get a maximum age of 35 years. Forest tribal (Budakattu) residents of Karnataka get a maximum age of 38 years. All ages are calculated as on the last date of application.
Q5What is the educational qualification required?
A
Candidates must have passed PUC (Class 12) or an equivalent examination recognized by the State Government. Candidates whose results are pending are NOT eligible. ITI holders and 3-year diploma holders may also be eligible under specific Government Orders.
Q6What is the selection process for KSP Constable Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of: Written Examination (OMR-based, 100 marks) → Physical Standard Test (PST) → Physical Endurance Test (PET) → Medical Examination → Final Merit List. Qualifying the written exam does not guarantee PST/PET participation - only candidates shortlisted at 1:5 ratio will be called for physical tests.
Q7Is there negative marking in the KSP Constable written exam?
A
Yes. Each wrong answer carries a deduction of 0.25 marks. There is no negative marking for unanswered questions. The exam has 100 questions carrying 100 marks, to be completed in 90 minutes.
Q8What is the physical standard for the KSP Constable PST?
A
For general male candidates, the minimum height is 168 cm and the minimum chest measurement is 86 cm (fully expanded) with a minimum expansion of 5 cm. For Budakattu forest tribal male candidates, the minimum height is 155 cm with a chest expansion of at least 5 cm to a minimum expanded measurement of 75 cm.
Q9What events are included in the KSP Constable PET 2026?
A
The PET for general male candidates includes: 1600 m run (within 6 min 30 sec), Long Jump (min 3.80 m) or High Jump (min 1.20 m), and Shot Put 7.26 kg (min 5.60 m). Ex-servicemen have easier standards: 400 m run (within 2 min), Long/High Jump, and 4 kg Shot Put (min 3.75 m).
Q10What is the application fee for KSP Armed Police Constable 2026?
A
General and OBC (Cat 2A/2B/3A/3B) candidates must pay ₹750. SC/ST/Category-1 and Ex-Servicemen candidates must pay ₹500. The fee is payable online only and is non-refundable.
