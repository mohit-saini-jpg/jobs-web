UG Courses:

  • BA (Bachelor of Arts)
  • BSc (Bachelor of Science)
  • BCom (Bachelor of Commerce)
  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
  • BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)
  • B.Ed (Bachelor of Education)

PG Courses:

How to Check Periyar University Result 2026 Online

Students can check their Periyar University UG or PG Result 2026 by following these simple steps on the official examination portal. Keep your Registration Number and Date of Birth (in DD-MM-YYYY format) ready before proceeding.

Step-by-Step Process:

  • Visit the official examination portal: pucoe.periyaruniversity.ac.in/PUCoE-Exam-App/examResult
  • On the homepage, click on the "Results" or "Examination" tab
  • Select your course name and semester from the available list
  • Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth (DD-MM-YYYY)
  • Click the "Get Marks" or "Submit" button
  • Your Periyar University Result 2026 marksheet will appear on screen
  • Download and save the PDF copy of your marksheet for future reference

Login Credentials Required to Check Result

To access the Periyar University Result 2026 on the official portal, students must keep the following information ready:

  • Registration Number - printed on your admit card or university identity card
  • Date of Birth - in DD-MM-YYYY format as registered with the university

If you are unable to recall your registration number, contact your affiliated college's examination department for assistance.

Periyar University Result 2026 - Semester System

Periyar University follows a semester system for all its UG and PG courses. The university typically conducts:

  • Odd Semester Examinations - in the months of November/December (for 1st, 3rd, and 5th semesters)
  • Even Semester Examinations - in the months of April/May (for 2nd, 4th, and 6th semesters)

Results for both odd and even semester examinations are published on the official portal and results aggregator site indiaresults.com. Students from all semesters 1st year, 2nd year, and 3rd year can check their respective results once declared.

What to Do After Checking Periyar University Result 2026

Once the Periyar University Result 2026 is out, students should take the following steps:

  • Download and Print the Marksheet - Save a digital and printed copy for future use
  • Verify Personal Details - Check that your name, registration number, and other details are correct
  • Apply for Revaluation if Needed - Students unsatisfied with their marks can apply for revaluation or re-totalling through the official university portal within the stipulated time
  • Contact the Examination Department - For any errors or discrepancies, reach out to the university's Controller of Examinations office
  • Collect Original Marks Card - The official printed marks card can be collected from your affiliated college once issued by the university
