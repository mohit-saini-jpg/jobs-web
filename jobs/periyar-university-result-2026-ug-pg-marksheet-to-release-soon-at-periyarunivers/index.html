How to Check Periyar University Result 2026 Online

Students can check their Periyar University UG or PG Result 2026 by following these simple steps on the official examination portal. Keep your Registration Number and Date of Birth (in DD-MM-YYYY format) ready before proceeding.

Step-by-Step Process:

  • Visit the official examination portal: pucoe.periyaruniversity.ac.in/PUCoE-Exam-App/examResult
  • On the homepage, click on the "Results" or "Examination" tab
  • Select your course name and semester from the available list
  • Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth (DD-MM-YYYY)
  • Click the "Get Marks" or "Submit" button
  • Your Periyar University Result 2026 marksheet will appear on screen
  • Download and save the PDF copy of your marksheet for future reference

Login Credentials Required to Check Result

Periyar University Result 2026 - Semester System

Periyar University follows a semester system for all its UG and PG courses. The university typically conducts:

  • Odd Semester Examinations - in the months of November/December (for 1st, 3rd, and 5th semesters)
  • Even Semester Examinations - in the months of April/May (for 2nd, 4th, and 6th semesters)

Results for both odd and even semester examinations are published on the official portal and results aggregator site indiaresults.com. Students from all semesters 1st year, 2nd year, and 3rd year can check their respective results once declared.
