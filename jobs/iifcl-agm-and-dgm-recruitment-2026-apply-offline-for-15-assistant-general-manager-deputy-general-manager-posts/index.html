How To Apply

  • Candidates must fill the application form, sign it, and send it along with self-attested copies of all relevant documents by ordinary post / speed post only to the registered address of IIFCL. No other mode of application will be accepted.
  • Documents to be sent along with Application:
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Signature
  • Proof of Date of Birth
  • Educational qualification certificates & marksheets
  • Experience certificates (with specific roles & responsibilities)
  • Caste / Category / PwBD / EWS certificate (if applicable)
  • Latest salary slip / CTC proof
  • Any other relevant documents
  • Send Application to: The Deputy General Manager (HR), India Infrastructure Finance Company Limited, Registered Office Address (as mentioned in notification).
  • Important: Candidates should submit only one application. Multiple applications will be rejected.

Important Instructions

  • Only Indian citizens are eligible to apply. Applications received after the last date will not be considered. Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification. All future communications will be sent through the official website only. Selected candidates will be on probation for 1 year (extendable up to 2 years). Service conditions include attractive pay, perks, and career growth opportunities. IIFCL reserves the right to cancel or modify the recruitment process at any stage.
