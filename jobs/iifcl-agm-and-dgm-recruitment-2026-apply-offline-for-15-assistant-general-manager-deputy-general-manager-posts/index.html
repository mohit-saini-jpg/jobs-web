Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is IIFCL AGM & DGM Recruitment 2026?

IIFCL AGM & DGM Recruitment 2026 is a direct recruitment drive by India Infrastructure Finance Company Limited to fill 9 Assistant General Manager (Grade C) and 6 Deputy General Manager (Grade D) posts on a regular basis across domains like Risk Management, IT, CISO, Hedging/Forex, Finance & Accounts, and Project/Sustainable Finance.

Q2. How many vacancies are announced under IIFCL AGM & DGM Notification 2026?

The notification dated 08 May 2026 announces a total of 15 vacancies, including 9 posts of Assistant General Manager (Grade C) in various specialist areas and 6 posts of Deputy General Manager (Grade D), with horizontal reservation for PwBD candidates as per Government of India norms.

Q3. What are the application dates and mode for IIFCL AGM & DGM Recruitment 2026?

Applications for IIFCL AGM & DGM Recruitment 2026 must be submitted only in offline mode by post, and the last date for receipt of duly filled application forms at the IIFCL office is 08 June 2026, with 30 April 2026 taken as the cut‑off date for age, qualification and experience.

Q4. What is the age limit for IIFCL AGM (Grade C) and DGM (Grade D) posts?

For AGM (Grade C), the upper age limit is generally 45 years for General/EWS, 48 years for OBC (NCL) and 50 years for SC/ST candidates, while for DGM (Grade D) it is 50 years for General/EWS, 53 years for OBC (NCL) and 55 years for SC/ST, with higher relaxations for PwBD categories and ex‑servicemen as per Government rules.

Q5. What educational qualification and experience are required for IIFCL AGM posts?

AGM (Grade C) applicants need a relevant professional qualification such as CA, CMA, MBA, or BE/B.Tech (discipline‑wise as specified) along with at least 12 years of post‑qualification experience, out of which a minimum of 7 years must be in the relevant functional area like risk, IT, CISO, or forex/treasury, and their last position should be equivalent to Scale III or Grade B/E4‑E5 in banks/financial institutions.

Q6. What educational qualification and experience are required for IIFCL DGM posts?

DGM (Grade D) candidates must possess appropriate professional qualifications (CA, CMA, MBA, BE/B.Tech, etc.) with a minimum of 16 years of post‑qualification experience, including at least 11 years in the relevant domain such as CFO/Finance & Accounts, CRO/Risk, or Project/Sustainable Finance/Credit Monitoring, and should have held a position equivalent to Scale IV or Grade C/E5‑E6.

Q7. Is there any application fee for IIFCL AGM & DGM Recruitment 2026?

The offline recruitment advertisements for AGM (Grade C) and DGM (Grade D) specify that there is no application fee for these posts, so candidates only need to bear their postal/courier charges when sending the application.

Q8. What is the selection process for IIFCL AGM and DGM Recruitment 2026?

The selection process comprises shortlisting of eligible candidates based on their qualifications and relevant experience, followed by a personal interview; final selection and merit list will be prepared on the basis of performance in the interview, subject to satisfactory document verification and medical fitness.
