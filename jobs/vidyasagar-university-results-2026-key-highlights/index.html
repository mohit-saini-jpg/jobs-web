How to Check Vidyasagar University 3rd Semester Result 2026?

Follow the steps below to check your Vidyasagar University PG 3rd Semester Result 2025–26 from the official examination website:

  • Open the official examination portal http://exam.vidyasagar.ac.in/ in your browser.
  • On the exam portal, look for the section or link related to “PG 3rd Semester Examination 2025 – Result” for campus students; this may be under a Results or Student Login/Result area.
  • Select your Postgraduate Programme (e.g., Applied Mathematics, Microbiology, Bio‑Medical Laboratory Science & Management, Human Physiology, Philosophy, Sanskrit or History) and the 3rd Semester 2025 examination from the available options.
  • Enter your required login details such as Roll Number / Registration Number / University ID, as asked on the result page, and submit the form.
  • Your Vidyasagar University 3rd semester marksheet will appear on the screen, showing subject‑wise marks/grades, total marks and overall result status.
  • Download or print the electronic copy of the marksheet for academic reference, scholarship or higher‑study applications; keep it until the original hard‑copy marksheet is issued through the university.
  • If you find any discrepancy in the result, inform the Controller of Examinations office within 7 days from the date of publication of the result, as instructed in the notification.
  • For students wishing to apply for review of answer scripts, revisit http://exam.vidyasagar.ac.in/ from 23‑03‑2026, 16:00 hrs onwards, fill the online review application form, and pay the requisite fee before 30‑03‑2026.
