Which SPPU MBA Results Are Declared?

SPPU has declared results for two exam sessions under the MBA HRD (REV.2020) programme, applicable to First Year and Second Year students respectively.

  • First Year MBA (HRD) (REV.2020) Winter Session 2025 (N+2+1)
  • Second Year MBA (HRD) (REV.2020) Winter Session 2026 (N+2+1)

The "N+2+1" notation refers to SPPU's exam chance pattern, covering regular (New), and backlog/ATKT (2nd and 3rd) attempt candidates appearing together in the same result declaration.

How to Check SPPU MBA Result 2026 Online

Students can check their MBA (HRD) result directly through the official SPPU online result portal by following the steps below.

  • Visit the official SPPU result portal: onlineresults.unipune.ac.in/SPPU
  • On the dashboard, select the relevant exam: First Year MBA (HRD) (REV.2020) Winter Session 2025 or Second Year MBA (HRD) (REV.2020) Winter Session 2026, depending on your year.
  • Click on the selected exam to open the result login window.
  • Enter your PRN (Permanent Registration Number) or Seat Number and the security code/captcha shown on screen.
  • Click Show Result / Submit to view your result.
  • Check your name, PRN, college name, subject-wise marks/grades, SGPA, and overall result status carefully.
  • Download and save the result page as a PDF for future reference.

Important Note for SPPU MBA Students

The result displayed online is a provisional result. Students should collect their official mark sheet from their respective college or the university examination department later. Those not satisfied with their marks can apply for photocopy/revaluation of answer books through SPPU's official channels within the prescribed deadline.
