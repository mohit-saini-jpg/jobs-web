Eligibility Criteria: Qualification and Age Limit

Candidates wondering whether they qualify for this apprenticeship can check both the educational qualification for their chosen trade and the general eligibility conditions below. Eligibility is assessed strictly per category, and any qualification not specifically notified for a trade will not be considered equivalent. ITI/Trade Apprentices: Pass in ITI examinations (NCVT) of the respective trade from a government-recognized institute. Diploma/Technician Apprentices: Full-time Diploma in Engineering or Technology from a recognized institution, state council, or board of technical education. Graduate (Engineering) Apprentices: Full-time degree in Engineering or Technology from a recognized university in the relevant discipline. Graduate (Non-Engineering) Apprentices: Full-time B.Com or BBA degree from a recognized university. General eligibility conditions applicable to all categories: C

Selection Process for NLC India Apprenticeship 2026

Selection for this apprenticeship is purely merit-based, calculated from the marks scored in the candidate's qualifying examination rather than through a written test or interview. This makes accuracy in reporting percentage figures critical for every applicant. Trade Apprentices are ranked by the percentage of marks scored across all subjects in the ITI (NCVT) examination of their respective trade. Diploma/Technician Apprentices are ranked by the percentage of marks scored in their Diploma. Graduate (Engineering) Apprentices are ranked by the percentage of marks scored in their BE/B.Tech. Graduate (Non-Engineering) Apprentices are ranked by the average percentage of marks across all semesters of their B.Com/BBA degree. If a candidate's result is in CGPA/grade form, the percentage is derived by multiplying the CGPA by a factor of 10 , unless the university has its own official conversion

Q1What is the last date to apply for NLC India Barsingsar Apprenticeship 2026?
A
Online registration closes on 3 August 2026 at 17:00 hrs, and the signed hard copy with documents must reach the Barsingsar office by 10 August 2026, 5:00 pm.
Q2How many total vacancies are there in this notification?
A
There are 163 total training slots — 86 in ITI/Trade, 42 in Diploma/Technician, 31 in Graduate (Engineering), and 4 in Graduate (Non-Engineering) categories.
Q3Who is eligible to apply for this apprenticeship?
A
Only candidates who belong to the State of Rajasthan, have passed their qualifying exam in 2022–2026, are between 18 and 35 years of age, and have no prior apprenticeship or one year of job experience are eligible.
Q4What is the age limit for NLC India Barsingsar Apprenticeship 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 35 years (candidate must not have completed 35 years).
Q5What stipend will apprentices receive?
A
Stipend is Rs. 12,000/month for ITI/Trade, Rs. 12,524/month for Diploma/Technician and Graduate Non-Engineering, and Rs. 15,028/month for Graduate Engineering apprentices.
Q6How will candidates be selected?
A
Selection is based purely on the percentage of marks scored in the qualifying examination (ITI/Diploma/BE/B.Tech/B.Com/BBA), followed by a certificate verification round for shortlisted candidates.
Q7Is this apprenticeship open to candidates from outside Rajasthan?
A
No, the notification specifies that only candidates belonging to the State of Rajasthan are eligible to apply.
Q8What documents are required with the application?
A
Key documents include the ITI/Diploma/Degree marksheets and certificates, Aadhaar Card, Date of Birth proof, community certificate (if applicable), and category-specific proofs such as PAP or Employee ward certificates.
Q9Does completing this apprenticeship guarantee a job at NLC India Limited?
A
No, the notification clearly states that completion of training does not entitle candidates to any right to claim temporary or permanent employment at NLC India Limited.

Candidates should regularly check the official NLCIL website (www.nlcindia.in), where the certificate verification call list and final selection list will be hosted; no other form of correspondence will be entertained.
