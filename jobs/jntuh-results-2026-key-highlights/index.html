How to Check JNTUH BTech III Year II Semester Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your JNTUH BTech III Year II Semester April 2026 result:

  • Visit the official JNTUH result portal: http://results.jntuh.ac.in/
  • Look for the link titled 'BTech III Year II Semester April 2026 Result' on the results page.
  • Click 'View Result' next to the relevant exam entry.
  • Enter your hall ticket number and any other required details (for example date of birth) in the login fields.
  • Submit the details to view your provisional marksheet on screen.
  • Download the PDF or take a screenshot and save it for future reference.
  • Verify your name, register number, subject-wise marks, and total percentage; contact the university examination branch if there are any errors.
