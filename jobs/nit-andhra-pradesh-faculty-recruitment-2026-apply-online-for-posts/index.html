Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationB.Tech/B.E. and M.Tech/M.E. or equivalent in respective branches, or Ph.D. for some positions.
DetailsAdhoc Faculty in Civil Engineering: B.Tech/B.E. (First Class) and M.Tech/M.E. or equivalent (First Class) in respective branch or allied branches. Adhoc Faculty in Chemical Engineering: B.Tech/B.E. (First Class) and M.Tech/M.E. or equivalent (First Class) in respective branch or allied branches. Adhoc Faculty in Computer Science and Engineering: B.Tech/B.E. (First Class) and M.Tech/M.E. or equivalent (First Class) in respective branch or allied branches. Adhoc Faculty in Electrical Engineering: B.Tech/B.E. (First Class) and M.Tech/M.E. or equivalent (First Class) in respective branch or allied branches. Adhoc Faculty in Electronics & Communications Engineering: B.Tech/B.E. (First Class) and M.Tech/M.E. or equivalent (First Class) in respective branch or allied branches. Adhoc Faculty in Mechanical Engineering: B.Tech/B.E. (First Class) and M.Tech/M.E. or equivalent (First Class) in respective branch or allied branches. Adhoc Faculty in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering: B.Tech/B.E. (First Class) and M.Tech/M.E. or equivalent (First Class) in respective branch or allied branches. Adhoc Faculty in Biotechnology: B.Tech. (First Class) and M.Tech. or equivalent (First Class) in Biotechnology, OR B.Tech. in Chemical Engineering (First Class) and M.Tech. in Biotechnology/Chemical Engineering (First Class), OR B.Sc. (First Class), M.Sc. (First Class), and M.Tech in Biotechnology (First Class). Adhoc Faculty in School of Sciences (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry): UG Degree (First Class), PG Degree (First Class) in relevant discipline, and Ph.D.** in relevant or equivalent discipline. Adhoc Faculty in School of Humanities (English): UG Degree (First Class), PG Degree (First Class) in relevant discipline, and Ph.D.** in relevant or equivalent discipline. Adhoc Faculty in Schools of Humanities (Management): B.Tech. (any branch of Engineering) (First Class) and MBA (Full time) (First Class), OR Bachelor's Degree (any discipline) (First Class) with M.Com.* (Full-time) (First Class) and MBA (Full time) (First Class).
