Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for NAM Kerala Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the offline application for the NAM Kerala Recruitment 2026 is 03 June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well in advance to avoid any last-minute issues.

Q2. What is the application fee for NAM Kerala Recruitment 2026?

There is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) in the official notification for the NAM Kerala Recruitment 2026. This makes the application process accessible to all eligible candidates.

Q3. What is the minimum and maximum age limit for the posts?

The minimum age limit for all the posts is 18 years, and the maximum age limit is 40 years. However, there are specific age relaxations mentioned for certain categories in the detailed notification.

Q4. What are the educational qualifications required for the Yoga Instructor posts?

For the Yoga Instructor posts, candidates must possess a B.Sc or equivalent degree in Yoga from a Government-recognized AYUSH institution approved by the National AYUSH Mission. Prior experience in Yoga is preferred.

Q5. What is the qualification for the ANM/GNM Staff post?

The required qualification for the ANM/GNM Staff post is a GNM/ANM certificate along with a valid registration from the Kerala Nurses and Midwives Council.

Q6. What is the salary offered for these positions?

The salary for Yoga Instructor posts is Rs. 14700/- per month, and for the ANM/GNM Staff post, it is Rs. 15000/- per month. These are contractual positions.
