Steps to Check NEHU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your NEHU PG 3rd Semester Result 2026:

  • Visit the NEHU PG result portal http://exams.nehu.ac.in/PGresults.
  • Look for the links titled: Result of Zoology 3rd Semester Regular 24 Batch, December 2025 Result of Statistics 3rd Semester (Regular 2024 Batch), December 2025
  • Result of Zoology 3rd Semester Regular 24 Batch, December 2025
  • Result of Statistics 3rd Semester (Regular 2024 Batch), December 2025
  • Click on the View Result link for your course to open the PDF.
  • Once the PDF opens, press Ctrl+F and type your roll number or name to quickly locate your entry.
  • Check your subject wise marks, total marks and overall result status (Pass/Fail/Back, if mentioned).
  • Download and save the PDF or take a printout for future academic use and documentation.
