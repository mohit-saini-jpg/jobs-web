Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham has officially released the AEEE 2026 Phase 2 Result on May 9, 2026, for candidates who appeared in the Amrita Engineering Entrance Examination. The results have been declared for admission to B.Tech programs across various engineering disciplines including Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, and other branches offered at Amrita University campuses located at Amaravati, Amritapuri, Bengaluru, Chennai, Coimbatore, Faridabad, Nagercoil, and Haridwar. AEEE Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - aeee.amrita.edu.

Students can check and download their results by logging into the Amrita Online Admission Portal using their registered email ID or mobile number along with the OTP received on their registered contact details. The AEEE 2026 scorecard displays the candidate's percentile scores, ranks, marks obtained in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, English, and Quantitative Aptitude sections, along with qualifying status for admission to engineering programs. Students are advised to verify all details on their scorecard carefully including their name, registration number, and marks, and contact Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham immediately in case of any discrepancies or errors in their result.

How to Check AEEE Result 2026

Students can check their AEEE 2026 Phase 2 Result by following these simple steps on the official admission portal:

  • Visit the official AEEE result website aeee.amrita.edu/portal or click on the direct link provided above
  • On the homepage, locate and click on the "Result" link in the Amrita Online Application Portal (AOAP)
  • Enter your AEEE registered Email ID or Mobile Number in the required field
  • Click on "Send OTP" button to receive the one-time password on your registered contact details
  • Enter the OTP received on your registered email or mobile number
  • Click on the "Submit" or "Login" button to access your result
  • Your AEEE 2026 Result will be displayed on the screen with detailed scorecard information
  • The scorecard will show your percentile score, AEEE rank, marks obtained in each section, and qualifying status
  • Verify all the details including name, registration number, marks in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, English, and Quantitative Aptitude sections
  • Download the AEEE 2026 scorecard by clicking on the download button
  • Take a printout of your result and rank card for future reference and admission counselling process
  • Keep the scorecard safe as it will be required during the counselling and seat allocation process
