SBI PO 2026 Notification PDF : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for SBI PO Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for online registration and payment of fee is 8 July 2026; registration opened on 18 June 2026.
Q2How many vacancies are there in SBI PO Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 1500 vacancies for the post of Probationary Officer, comprising 1446 regular and 54 backlog vacancies.
Q3What is the age limit for SBI PO 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 21 and 30 years old as on 01 April 2026, with age relaxation available for OBC, SC/ST, PwBD, and Ex-servicemen categories.
Q4What is the educational qualification required for SBI PO?
A
Candidates need a Graduation degree in any discipline from a recognised university, with eligibility checked as on 30 September 2026. Final-year students can also apply provisionally.
Q5What is the application fee for SBI PO Recruitment 2026?
A
The fee is Rs 750 for UR, EWS, and OBC candidates, while SC, ST, and PwBD candidates are exempt from paying any fee.
Q6What is the selection process for SBI PO 2026?
A
Selection involves three phases: Preliminary Examination, Main Examination, and Psychometric Test/Group Exercise/Interview. Only Main Exam and Interview marks count toward the final merit list.
Q7What is the salary of an SBI Probationary Officer?
A
The starting basic pay is Rs 48,480 (JMGS-I scale), with an approximate CTC of Rs 21.97 Lacs at the Mumbai Centre, inclusive of allowances and benefits.
Q8Is there a bond required after joining as SBI PO?
A
Yes, selected candidates must execute a bond of Rs 2.00 Lacs to serve the Bank for a minimum of three years from the date of joining.
Q9How can I apply online for SBI PO Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online through https://ibpsreg.ibps.in/sbipojun26/ or via SBI's official careers page at https://sbi.bank.in/web/careers/current-openings.
Q10Is SBI PO Recruitment conducted by IBPS or by SBI directly?
A
SBI PO Recruitment 2026 is conducted directly by State Bank of India's Central Recruitment & Promotion Department, though candidate queries and grievances are handled through the IBPS grievance portal at cgrs.ibps.in.
