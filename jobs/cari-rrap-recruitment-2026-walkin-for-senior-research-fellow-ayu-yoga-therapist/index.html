FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the CARI RRAP recruitment?
A
The recruitment is through a walk-in interview, and the interview is scheduled for 16 June 2026. Candidates should report to the venue on this date.
Q2What is the total number of vacancies announced by CARI RRAP?
A
CARI RRAP has announced a total of 5 vacancies for the posts of Senior Research Fellow (Ayu) and Yoga Therapist.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for the Senior Research Fellow (Ayu) post?
A
Candidates must possess a BAMS degree from a recognized University. An MD (Ayurveda) is desirable, along with research experience and computer skills.
Q4What is the age limit for the advertised posts?
A
The maximum age limit for all the advertised posts is 35 years. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST/OBC candidates as per government norms.
Q5What is the salary offered for the Yoga Therapist position?
A
The selected candidate for the Yoga Therapist post will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 38,000 per month.
Q6Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee required for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q7Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?
A
The walk-in interview will be conducted in Mumbai, Maharashtra, at the CARI RRAP facility. The exact address is provided in the official notification.
Q8What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents, self-attested photocopies of these documents, and recent passport-size photographs to the interview venue.
Q9What is the mode of selection for these posts?
A
The selection process includes a Walk-in Interview, Document Verification, and a Skill Test specifically for the Yoga Therapist position.
Q10Is this a permanent government job?
A
No, these positions are contractual in nature, offering a fixed tenure as per the terms of the appointment.
