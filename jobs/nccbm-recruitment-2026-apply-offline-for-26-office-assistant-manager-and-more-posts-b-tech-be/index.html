Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is NCB Recruitment 2026?

NCB Recruitment 2026 is a direct recruitment drive by National Council for Cement and Building Materials (NCCBM/NCB) for various Cadre Official posts such as Deputy Manager, Manager, Group Manager, Office Assistant, Hindi Assistant and Admin Assistant. The recruitment is being conducted under Advertisement No. R/01/2026 (01) for around 26 vacancies on different pay levels.

Q2. Which posts are available under NCB Recruitment 2026?

NCB has notified multiple Cadre Official posts across technical and non‑technical streams. These include Deputy Manager posts in various disciplines (Level‑10), Manager posts (Level‑11), one Group Manager post (Level‑12), Office Assistant (Level‑6), Hindi Assistant (Level‑3) and Admin Assistant (Level‑3, PwBD).

Q3. How many vacancies are there in NCB Recruitment 2026?

The NCB Recruitment 2026 notification offers approximately 26 vacancies in total. Around 14 posts are for Deputy Manager, 8 posts for Manager, 1 post for Group Manager, 1 post for Office Assistant, 1 post for Hindi Assistant and 1 post for Admin Assistant (PwBD).

Q4. What is the eligibility criteria for NCB Cadre Official posts?

Candidates must be Indian citizens and possess the prescribed engineering or science qualification for the specific post with at least 60% marks. In addition, most posts require relevant post‑qualification experience ranging from about 3 to 10 years depending on the level (Deputy Manager, Manager or Group Manager).

Q5. What is the age limit for NCB Recruitment 2026?

The upper age limit varies according to the post and level as mentioned in the detailed notification. Age relaxation will be applicable for reserved categories such as SC, ST, OBC, PwBD and Ex‑Servicemen as per Government of India rules on the crucial date specified in the advertisement.

Q6. What is the application fee for NCB Recruitment 2026?

For NCB Recruitment 2026, General and OBC candidates are required to pay an application fee of Rs. 500 per post. SC, ST, PwBD, Ex‑Servicemen and Women candidates are exempted from paying any application fee.

Q7. What is the selection process for NCB Recruitment 2026?

Selection will be carried out in stages starting with screening of applications based on eligibility, qualifications and experience. Shortlisted candidates will then be called for Trade/Skill Test, Written Examination and/or Personal Discussion, and final selection will be subject to document verification and medical fitness.

Q8. How can I apply for NCB Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must download the prescribed application form from the official NCB website and fill it carefully with all required details. The duly filled form along with two recent passport size photographs and self‑attested copies of all relevant documents must be sent by post to the Director General, National Council for Cement and Building Materials, 34 KM Stone, Delhi‑Mathura Road (NH‑2), Ballabgarh, Haryana – 121004 within 21 days from publication in Employment News.
