Details Mentioned on SCTEVT Diploma Marksheet 2026

Before downloading your marksheet, it is important to know what information it contains. The SCTEVT Odd/Even semester exam result contains several details the candidate's date of birth, name, registration number, hall ticket number, and enrollment number are listed therein.

In addition to the above, the SCTEVT Summer 2026 marksheet typically includes:

  • Subject-wise marks obtained (Theory, Practical, Internal Assessment)

How to Check SCTEVT Summer Exam Result 2026 at sctevtodisha.nic.in

Follow these step-by-step instructions to access and download your SCTEVT Diploma Result 2026 easily from the official website.

Step 1: Visit the SCTEVT official website at Click Here.

Step 2: Locate the "View Result" tab on the portal home page and tap on it.

Step 3: The system will display a Dashboard with a host of results click on the relevant semester exam link to be taken to the login window.

Step 4: Once the login page is visible on the screen, enter your "Registration Number", "Date of Birth", and "Captcha Code" in the given boxes and then select the "Submit" option.

Step 5: Your Odisha Diploma semester result will appear on the screen.

Step 6: Download the result PDF and take a printout for future reference.

Alternative Method - Check Result via sctevtexams.in

Students can also access their results directly through the SCTEVT examination portal:

Step 1: Visit sctevtexams.in.

Step 2: On the Results page, select the appropriate course and semester from the results table displayed on the portal.

Step 3: Click on the "View Result" link corresponding to your semester.

Step 4: Enter your login credentials and submit.

Step 5: Download and save your marksheet PDF.
