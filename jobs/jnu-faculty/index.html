FAQs

Q1What is JNU Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
JNU Faculty Recruitment 2026 is a teaching recruitment drive by Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi, for Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor posts under Advertisement No. RC/75/2026. The recruitment covers multiple disciplines across various Schools and Centres, with appointments governed by UGC Regulations 2018.
Q2How many vacancies are released under JNU Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
JNU has announced a large number of faculty vacancies across different categories and departments. Multiple posts are available in Sciences, Engineering, Social Sciences, International Studies, Languages, Biotechnology, Life Sciences, Environmental Sciences and several specialized Centres.
Q3What are the important dates for JNU Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application process for JNU Faculty Recruitment 2026 starts on 18 May 2026 from 04:00 PM. The last date for submission of online applications is 15 June 2026 up to 05:00 PM; the last date for hard‑copy submission, if required, will be notified separately by the University.
Q4What are the pay scales for Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor in JNU?
A
The pay scales are as per 7th CPC Academic Pay Levels. Professor posts are in Academic Pay Level‑14 (Rs. 1,44,200 – 2,18,200), Associate Professor posts are in Academic Pay Level‑13A (Rs. 1,31,400 – 2,17,100), and Assistant Professor posts are in Academic Pay Level‑10 (Rs. 57,700 – 1,82,400).
Q5What are the essential qualifications for Professor posts in JNU?
A
For Professor posts, candidates must possess a Ph.D. in the concerned/allied/relevant discipline as per UGC Regulations 2018. They should have a minimum of 10 years of teaching/research experience, at least 10 research publications in peer‑reviewed or indexed journals, and a total research score of 120, along with evidence of supervising doctoral candidates.
Q6What are the eligibility criteria for Associate Professor and Assistant Professor posts?
A
Associate Professors must have a Ph.D. in the relevant discipline plus a Master’s degree with 55% marks, at least 8 years of teaching/research experience, a minimum of 7 research publications and a research score of 75. Assistant Professors require a Master’s degree with 55% marks in the relevant subject with NET/SLET/SET, or a Ph.D. as per UGC norms; a 5% marks relaxation is available for SC/ST/OBC (NCL)/PwD candidates.
Q7What is the application fee for JNU Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
General, OBC and EWS category candidates are required to pay a non‑refundable application fee of Rs. 2000 per application. There is no application fee for SC, ST, PwD and Women candidates.
Q8How can I apply online for JNU Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online only through the CU‑CHAYAN portal accessible via the JNU official website. They need to register, fill the online application form carefully, upload scanned copies of all required documents, pay the applicable fee and submit the application; a separate application is required for each post applied for.
