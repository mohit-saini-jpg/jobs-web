NTPC Assistant Chemist Trainee Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in NTPC Assistant Chemist Trainee Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 40 projected vacancies have been announced for the post of Assistant Chemist Trainee (ACT) – 17 UR, 04 EWS, 10 OBC, 06 SC, and 03 ST. The total number of vacancies may increase or decrease as per organizational requirement at the discretion of NTPC management.
Q2What is the last date to apply online for NTPC ACT Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for NTPC Assistant Chemist Trainee Recruitment 2026 is 10 July 2026. The application window opens on 26 June 2026. Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date to avoid last-minute technical issues.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for NTPC Assistant Chemist Trainee 2026?
A
Candidates must hold a Regular/Full-time M.Sc. in Chemistry from a recognized Indian University/Institute with a minimum of 60% marks in aggregate. SC, ST, and PwBD candidates with passing marks are eligible. Final year/semester students who expect their result by 31.07.2026 may also apply.
Q4What is the age limit for NTPC Assistant Chemist Trainee Recruitment 2026?
A
The maximum age limit is 27 years as on the last date of online application (10.07.2026) for General/UR/EWS candidates. Age relaxation of 5 years for SC/ST, 3 years for OBC, and 10 years for PwBD candidates is admissible as per Government guidelines.
Q5What is the application fee for NTPC ACT Recruitment 2026?
A
General, EWS, and OBC candidates must pay Rs. 500/- (non-refundable). SC, ST, PwBD, Ex-Servicemen (XSM), and Female candidates are exempted from paying the application fee. The fee can be paid online (Net Banking/Debit/Credit Card) or offline through State Bank of India.
Q6What is the selection process for NTPC Assistant Chemist Trainee Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection consists of an All India based Online Selection Test comprising two parts – Subject Knowledge Test (Chemistry) and Executive Aptitude Test. Candidates must qualify separately in both parts. Based on category-wise merit, candidates will be shortlisted for document verification. There is no interview.
Q7What is the pay scale for NTPC Assistant Chemist Trainee post?
A
Selected candidates will be placed in the pay scale of Rs. 30,000 – 1,20,000/- at a basic pay of Rs. 30,000/- (E0 Grade). They will also be eligible for Dearness Allowance, other perquisites and allowances, terminal benefits, etc. as per company rules in force from time to time during training/after absorption.
Q8Is there a service bond for NTPC ACT selected candidates?
A
Yes. Selected candidates belonging to General, EWS, and OBC categories must execute a service bond of Rs. 2,50,000/-, and SC, ST, and PwBD candidates must execute a service bond of Rs. 1,25,000/-. They are required to serve NTPC for at least 3 years after successful completion of the one-year training period.
Q9Can final year M.Sc. Chemistry students apply for NTPC ACT 2026?
A
Yes. Final year/semester students who expect their result by 31.07.2026 may apply. However, they will be considered for selection only if they can submit the prescribed final/provisional degree dated 31.07.2026 or before. There is no relaxation on this account.
Q10Where will selected NTPC Assistant Chemist Trainees be posted?
A
The selected candidates will undergo one-year training at various places. The final place of posting will be decided after completion of training and shall be at any of the Stations/Projects/JVs/Subsidiaries of NTPC across India. All posts are transferable at the sole discretion of NTPC management.
