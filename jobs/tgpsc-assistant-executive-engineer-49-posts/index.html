How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official TGPSC website at https://www.tgpsc.gov.in and complete the One Time Registration (OTR) to obtain your TGPSC ID (if not already registered).
  • 2Login with your TGPSC ID and Date of Birth and enter the OTP received on the registered mobile number.
  • 3Verify the details fetched from the OTR database (Name, Date of Birth, Educational Qualifications, Community, Gender, Disability, etc.).
  • 4Select the examination centre and fill in all required details in the online application form.
  • 5Upload a recent colour passport size photograph (not older than 3 months, 100-200kb) and scanned signature (20-30kb) in JPG/PNG format.
  • 6Preview the application carefully and click on SAVE & PAYMENT button to proceed with online fee payment through Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card or UPI.
  • 7After payment, click CONFIRM & SUBMIT. Download the PDF application form and note the Reference ID Number for future reference.
