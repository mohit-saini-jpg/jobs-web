Exam Pattern

The APSPDCL AEE 2026 exam is 100 marks with the following distribution for all three posts: | There is no negative marking in the CBT for any of the three posts. | AEE/Electrical (70 marks): Electric Circuits, Electrical Machines, Power Systems, Utilization & Control Systems, Measurements, Analog & Digital Electronics, Power Electronics & Drives. | AEE/Telecom (70 marks): Network Analysis, Fourier Series & Transforms, Electronic Devices & Circuits, Digital Circuits, Linear Control Systems, Transmission Lines & Antennas, Maxwell's Equations, Waveguides, Communication Systems, Microprocessors. | AEE/Civil (70 marks): Strength of Materials, Reinforced Concrete, Soil Mechanics, Steel Structures, Fluid Mechanics & Machinery, Foundation Engineering.

How to Apply

  • 1All APSPDCL AEE 2026 applications must be submitted online only through the official Common Recruitment Portal. No offline or postal applications are accepted under any circumstances.
  • 2Visit https://apspdcl.in or https://apvidyutrecruitment.apcfss.in from 30 June 2026 (10:00 AM).
  • 3Register using your Aadhaar-linked mobile number. An OTP will be sent to verify your identity. The mobile number used must be linked to your Aadhaar.
  • 4Pay the application fee of ₹1,000 through the Payment Gateway (Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, UPI). Note your Journal Number upon successful payment.
  • 5Log in using your registration credentials and fill in all required fields - educational qualifications, academic details, personal information, and utility/zone preferences.
  • 6Upload documents: Recent passport-size photograph, scanned signature, and all relevant certificates (caste certificate, PwBD SADAREM certificate if applicable, EWS certificate, study/residence certificate for local candidature).
  • 7Exercise your preferences for participating power utilities (APTRANSCO, APEPDCL, APCPDCL, APSPDCL, APGENCO) and zones. These preferences are final and cannot be changed after submission.
  • 8Submit the application before 23:59 hrs on 20 July 2026. Incomplete applications will be summarily rejected.
  • 9Download your Hall Ticket 7 days before the examination from the official portal.
  • 10Important: Candidates currently serving in Government/Autonomous Body/Local Body/Aided Institution must submit a No Objection Certificate (NoC) from their employer along with the online application.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for APSPDCL AEE Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to apply online, pay the fee, and upload all certificates is 20 July 2026 by 23:59 hrs. No extension has been announced.
Q2How many vacancies are there in APSPDCL AEE Bharti 2026?
A
APSPDCL has notified 135 total vacancies - 125 for AEE/Electrical (including 1 OC-Women backlog), 6 for AEE/Civil, and 4 for AEE/Telecom.
Q3What is the application fee for APSPDCL AEE 2026?
A
The application fee is ₹1,000 for all candidates, payable online through the Payment Gateway. Fees once paid are non-refundable.
Q4What is the age limit for APSPDCL AEE Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 18 and 42 years as on 1 July 2026. SC/ST/BC candidates get a 5-year relaxation (up to 47 years), and PwBD candidates get a 10-year relaxation (up to 52 years).
Q5What is the salary for APSPDCL AEE posts?
A
During the 2-year probation period, selected candidates receive ₹75,000 per month (consolidated). After probation, the regular RPS-2022 pay scale applies, starting at ₹86,245.
Q6What is the selection process for APSPDCL AEE 2026?
A
Selection is through a Computer Based Test (CBT) of 100 marks (3 hours, English medium, no negative marking), followed by certificate verification. There is no interview.
Q7When is the APSPDCL AEE 2026 exam scheduled?
A
The CBT is scheduled from 22 August to 29 August 2026. Hall tickets will be available for download 7 days before the exam date.
Q8Is there a service bond for APSPDCL AEE 2026?
A
Yes. Selected candidates must execute a service bond committing to serve for a minimum of 5 years after probation (total 7 years). Leaving during probation attracts ₹5 lakh in liquidated damages; resigning within the 5-year post-probation period attracts ₹10 lakh.
Q9What are the exam centers for APSPDCL AEE CBT 2026?
A
The CBT will be held at 47 cities in Andhra Pradesh and select centers in Hyderabad, Khammam, Bengaluru, Chennai, and Berhampore. Candidates must appear at their allotted center.
Q10Is this a common exam for all AP power utilities?
A
Yes. The APSPDCL AEE 2026 exam is a Common Recruitment Examination jointly conducted for APTRANSCO, APEPDCL, APCPDCL, APSPDCL, and APGENCO. Candidates apply once, exercise utility and zone preferences, and are allotted based on merit, reservation, and preference order.
