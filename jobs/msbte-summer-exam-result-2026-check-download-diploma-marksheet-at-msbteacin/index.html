What Is MSBTE Summer Exam Result 2026?

This section explains what the MSBTE Summer Result 2026 is and who it applies to, helping students understand the context before checking their result.

The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE) has confirmed that the Even Semester (Summer 2026) Diploma Exams were held from April 23 to May 16, 2026, with the practical exams conducted from April 8 to 18, 2026. The Summer Result 2026 covers all regular and backlog candidates who appeared in the even-numbered semesters - 2nd, 4th, and 6th - across engineering, pharmacy, and other diploma programmes offered under the K Scheme and other applicable schemes.

Set up as an autonomous body under the Government of Maharashtra, MSBTE regulates the development and promotion of Diploma Level Technical education in the state, working towards improving the employability of diploma holders.

How to Check MSBTE Summer Result 2026 Online

Follow this step-by-step process to check and download your MSBTE Summer 2026 Diploma Result from the official portal.

MSBTE provides an online portal where students can easily check and download their results by following these steps:

  • Visit the official result portal - Go to result.msbte.ac.in
  • Select the Exam Session - Click on the "Summer 2026" link on the homepage
  • Enter your credentials - Type in your Enrollment Number or Seat Number
  • Click "Show Result" - Submit your details to load your result
  • View your result - Your detailed marksheet will appear on the screen
  • Download and save - Download the PDF marksheet and take a printout for future reference

MSBTE Diploma Grading System 2026

Understanding how MSBTE evaluates and grades student performance helps students interpret their marksheet accurately.

The board uses a structured credit and grade point system to evaluate student performance, with grades such as A+, A, B+, B, C, and F assigned based on the percentage obtained.

Below is a general overview of the grade distribution:

To pass a subject in the MSBTE diploma examination, students must score at least 40% in the combined assessment of Theory and Internal Assessment. MSBTE may also award grace marks (Condonation) if a student is failing by a small margin in one or two subjects.

What to Do After Checking MSBTE Summer Result 2026

Once students have accessed their result, there are specific next steps depending on whether they have passed or have a backlog.

A student's next steps depend on their current year of study. If they have passed, they should prepare for the next semester. If they have backlogs, they should start preparing for the next backlog examination cycle. After clearing the sixth semester, students can apply for a Provisional Passing Certificate from their college, which is required for further studies and employment.

If Your Result Shows "Result Held in Reserve (RHR)"

If the result status shows "Result Held in Reserve (RHR)", students should contact their college administration immediately. This status typically indicates a pending fee, eligibility issue, or document discrepancy.

MSBTE Summer Result 2026 - Revaluation and Photocopy Process

Students who are not satisfied with their marks can apply for revaluation after the result declaration. Here is how the process works.

If students feel their marks do not reflect their performance, MSBTE allows them to request answer sheet photocopies and revaluation. Students must first apply for a photocopy of the answer sheet before requesting revaluation.

Once MSBTE announces its Diploma Results, students can apply for re-valuation through the Photocopy, Verification, and Reassessment Form that MSBTE releases on its website msbte.ac.in.

Steps for Revaluation:

  • Visit msbte.ac.in after result declaration
  • Download the Photocopy, Verification, and Reassessment Form
  • Apply for photocopy of your answer sheet first
  • Submit the revaluation request within the prescribed deadline
  • Pay the applicable fees as mentioned in the official notification

MSBTE Summer 2026 - Exam Overview

This section gives a brief recap of the Summer 2026 examination schedule that led to the current result cycle. The MSBTE Summer 2026 exams were held in two phases: practical examinations from April 8 to 18, 2026, and theory examinations from April 23 to May 16, 2026.

Regular students appeared in the Morning Session, while KT/Backlog students appeared strictly in the Afternoon Session, scheduled from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM. Diploma programmes covered under MSBTE include Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics, Pharmacy, and several other technical disciplines.
