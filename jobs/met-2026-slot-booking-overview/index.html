4How to Book MET 2026 Exam Slots (Phase 1)
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphCandidates must complete slot booking within the specified window to choose their preferred MET 2026 exam date and time.
2paragraphStep-by-Step MET 2026 Slot Booking Process
3listordered
  • Open the OTBS portal Visit the MET 2026 OTBS login link (such as the cdn.digialm.com configured login page) through the official Manipal site or notification.
  • Log in to OTBS Enter your MET 2026 application number and OTBS password. First-time users should log in with the default DOB-based password and then create a new password.
  • Check candidate details Verify your name, application number, programme and other details shown on the dashboard. If anything is incorrect, contact the MET helpdesk before proceeding.
  • Click on “Book Slot” / “Book a Seat” Go to the slot booking section and click the button to start the booking process.
  • Select test city, date and time Choose your test city from the available options, then pick a suitable exam date and time slot from the list of available slots.
  • Confirm your slot Carefully review the selected city, date and timing. Once confirmed, the slot cannot be changed, so ensure you choose wisely.
  • Save your booking details After confirmation, take a screenshot or note your booked exam date, time and city. These details will appear again on your MET 2026 hall ticket.
4paragraphBooking early in the window helps increase your chances of getting your preferred slot and centre.

7MET 2026 Hall Ticket After Slot Booking
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphSlot booking is directly linked to hall ticket generation.
2paragraphHow and When to Download MET 2026 Admit Card
3listunordered
  • After slot booking, candidates can download the MET 2026 hall ticket from 10 April 2026 to 13 April 2026 for Phase 1.
  • Hall tickets are available through the OTBS login or MET exam page using your application number and OTBS password.
  • The admit card contains exam date, time, test centre address, reporting time, and important exam-day instructions.
4paragraphCandidates must carry a printed hall ticket and a valid photo ID to the exam centre; entry is not allowed without these.
8Important Instructions for MET 2026 Slot Booking
Sr.TypeTextStyleItems
1paragraphTo avoid mistakes and issues, candidates should follow these guidelines while booking slots.
2paragraphKey Guidelines for OTBS
3listunordered
  • No changes after confirmation: Once you confirm a slot, you cannot change the date, time or centre later.
  • First-come, first-served: Popular cities and time slots can fill up quickly; book early to increase your chances of getting your preferred slot.
  • Single login session: Use a stable internet connection and avoid logging in from multiple devices at the same time.
  • Check time zones: Ensure you understand the time mentioned on the portal and confirm the slot according to your local time and availability.
  • Keep login details safe: Do not share your OTBS password with others; always log out after completing booking or download.
4paragraphIf you miss the slot booking window, the system or authority may auto-assign a slot, or you may lose the chance to choose your preferences.
