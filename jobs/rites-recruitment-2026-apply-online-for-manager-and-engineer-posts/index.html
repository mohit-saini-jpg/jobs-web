Job Overview

Rail India Technical and Economic Service (RITES) has published the Manager-Civil and Engineer-Civil Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 2 Manager-Civil and Engineer-Civil positions. Candidates within the age range of up to 40 years and holding a Full-time Bachelor’s Degree in Civil Engineering are eligible to apply online at rites.com. The application process will be active till 21 June 2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.

Qualification / Eligibility

Manager-Civil: Full time Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering with Minimum 10 years of Experience in STP/WTP/Drainage/MPS/WSS/Building of water supply/Sewage treatment plant/infra structure/ building works. Engineer-Civil: Full time Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering with Minimum 5 years of Experience in STP/WTP/Drainage/WSS/Building of water supply/Sewage treatment plant/ infra structure/ building works.
