Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for RITES Recruitment 2026?

The last date for submitting the online application and making the fee payment is 21 June 2026. Candidates are advised to apply well in advance to avoid any last-minute technical issues.

Q2. What are the educational qualifications required for Manager-Civil and Engineer-Civil posts?

For both Manager-Civil and Engineer-Civil positions, candidates must possess a Full-time Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering. Specific experience requirements vary for each post.

Q3. What is the maximum age limit for the RITES recruitment?

The maximum age limit for all posts is 40 years. Age relaxation is provided for SC/ST, OBC-NCL, and PwBD categories as per government norms.

Q4. How many vacancies are available in this RITES recruitment drive?

There are a total of 2 vacancies announced for the posts of Manager-Civil and Engineer-Civil in this recruitment drive.

Q5. What is the application fee for RITES Recruitment 2026?

The application fee for all categories is Rs. 600/- plus applicable taxes. This fee needs to be paid online during the application process.

Q6. What is the selection process for these RITES posts?

The selection process will consist of a Written Test, which carries 60% weightage, followed by an Interview, which carries 40% weightage.

Q7. What is the salary offered for Manager-Civil and Engineer-Civil posts?

The salary for Manager-Civil is between INR 50,000 to 1,60,000, and for Engineer-Civil, it ranges from INR 30,000 to 1,20,000.

Q8. Is this a permanent or contract job?

The job type for these positions is contract-based.
