COMEDK UGET 2026 Admit Card Released

The Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMEDK) has released the COMEDK UGET 2026 admit card (Test Admission Ticket - TAT) for the undergraduate engineering entrance examination. As per the official schedule, the COMEDK UGET 2026 TAT download window is open from 30 April 2026 (2 PM) to 9 May 2026, and candidates must download their hall ticket within this period.

The COMEDK UGET 2026 exam will be conducted on 9 May 2026 in computer‑based mode across multiple test centres in India, in three different shifts as per the TAT. The admit card is available through the COMEDK website (comedk.org) and the Digialm login link, and must be carried in printed form to the exam centre.

How to Download COMEDK UGET 2026 Admit Card (TAT)

Candidates can download the COMEDK UGET 2026 Test Admission Ticket by following the steps below:

  • Go to the official COMEDK website: https://www.comedk.org.
  • Click on the “Applicant Login” / “COMEDK UGET 2026 Admit Card” link, which redirects to the Digialm login page (configuredHtml/1022/97617/login.html).
  • On the login screen, enter your Application Seq No / User ID and Password, then enter the captcha code.
  • Click on “Login” to open your candidate dashboard.
  • Click on the “Download Admit Card / Test Admission Ticket (TAT)” link to generate your COMEDK UGET 2026 admit card.
  • Check details like your name, photograph, application number, exam date, shift, and test centre address, then download and print 2-3 copies of the TAT on A4 paper.

If you forget your password, use the “Forgot Password” option on the login page to reset it via your registered email/mobile and then log in again.

Details Mentioned on COMEDK UGET 2026 Admit Card

The COMEDK UGET 2026 TAT carries crucial information that candidates must verify:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Application Seq No/User ID
  • Test Admission Ticket (TAT) number
  • COMEDK UGET 2026 exam date (9 May 2026)

  • Exam centre name, code and full address
  • Candidate’s signature and basic details (DOB, category, etc.)

If any discrepancy is found (e.g., wrong name, photograph, exam centre, or shift), candidates must contact the COMEDK helpdesk immediately before the exam date.

Documents Required with COMEDK UGET 2026 Admit Card

On 9 May 2026, candidates must bring the following items to the COMEDK UGET exam centre:

  • A printed copy of the COMEDK UGET 2026 Admit Card/Test Admission Ticket (TAT) with a clearly visible photograph.
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card Passport PAN Card Voter ID Driving Licence School/College ID (if permitted as per COMEDK instructions)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport
  • PAN Card
  • Voter ID
  • Driving Licence
  • School/College ID (if permitted as per COMEDK instructions)

Candidates not carrying the printed TAT and valid photo ID will not be allowed to enter the examination hall.

Exam‑Day Instructions For COMEDK UGET 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the COMEDK 2026 exam, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card; latecomers may not be allowed entry after gate closure.
  • Carry only essential items: COMEDK admit card/TAT, valid photo ID and permitted stationery as specified in the instructions.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, Bluetooth devices, electronic gadgets or written material into the exam hall; these are strictly prohibited.
  • Follow invigilators’ instructions and on‑screen directions carefully during the CBT.
  • Preserve your admit card even after the exam, as it may be required during counselling and document verification.
