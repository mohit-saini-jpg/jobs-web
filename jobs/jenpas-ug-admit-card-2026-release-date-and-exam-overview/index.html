JENPAS UG Admit Card 2026 - Release Date and Exam Overview

West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) has released the JENPAS UG Admit Card 2026 on 29 May 2026 on its official portal. The admit card is issued online only for registered candidates of the Joint Entrance Test for Nursing, Paramedical & Allied Sciences Under‑Graduate courses (JENPAS‑UG 2026).

As per the official exam schedule, the JENPAS‑UG 2026 exam will be conducted on 7 June 2026, with Paper‑I and Paper‑II held in offline OMR mode for different courses. Candidates must download and print the admit card from wbjeeb.nic.in before the exam, as no hard copy will be sent by post.

How to Download JENPAS UG Admit Card 2026

Once WBJEEB activates the link, candidates can download the JENPAS‑UG 2026 hall ticket as follows:

  • Visit the WBJEEB official website wbjeeb.nic.in and click on the JENPAS‑UG 2026 link.
  • Click on “Download Admit Card for JENPAS‑UG 2026” to open the candidate login page.
  • Enter your Application Number, Password and the security pin displayed on the screen.
  • Click on Sign In / Login; your JENPAS UG Admit Card 2026 appear on the screen with exam and centre details.
  • Check all particulars carefully and download and print 2-3 A4‑size copies of the admit card.

If you forget your password, use the “Forgot Password” option on the login page to reset it via OTP/email as per instructions.

Details Mentioned on JENPAS UG Admit Card 2026

The JENPAS‑UG 2026 admit card contain important information that must be verified before the exam:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph.
  • Application number and roll number.
  • Exam name: JENPAS‑UG 2026.
  • Exam date and timing (7 June 2026; paper‑wise session details).
  • Allotted examination centre name, code and full address.
  • Course/paper details (Paper‑I for most courses; Paper‑II for BHA applicants).
  • Candidate’s category and gender (if printed).
  • Important exam‑day instructions, reporting time and items allowed/prohibited.

If there is any mismatch in name, photo, category, or centre details, candidates should immediately contact WBJEEB via the helpdesk numbers/email given on the website and in the information brochure.

Documents Required with JENPAS UG Admit Card 2026

On the day of the JENPAS‑UG 2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the JENPAS UG Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence, or other government‑issued ID.

Carrying multiple photocopies of the admit card and ID is advisable for safety, but only one original ID will be accepted for verification.

Exam‑Day Instructions for JENPAS‑UG 2026

WBJEEB has outlined important exam‑day guidelines for JENPAS‑UG aspirants:

  • Reach the examination centre well before reporting time, as mentioned on the admit card, to complete frisking and seating; latecomers may not be allowed entry once gates close.
  • Carry only permitted items: admit card, photo ID and basic stationery (black/blue ball‑point pens etc.); follow specific instructions printed on the hall ticket.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, electronic gadgets, books, notes or any paper material inside the examination hall; these items are strictly prohibited.
  • Sit on the seat indicated by your roll number and fill the OMR sheet carefully as per instructions; attempts at unfair means can lead to cancellation of candidature.
  • Preserve the admit card even after the exam, as it may be required at counselling and admission stages.
