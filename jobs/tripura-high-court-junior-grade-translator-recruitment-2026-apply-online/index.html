FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the Tripura High Court Junior Grade Translator posts?
A
The last date to apply online for the Tripura High Court Junior Grade Translator recruitment is 13 June 2026. Ensure your application is submitted before the deadline.
Q2What is the educational qualification required for the Junior Grade Translator post?
A
Candidates must possess a Masters Degree in any official language of Tripura with English proficiency, or a Masters Degree in English with proficiency in any official language of Tripura. Computer proficiency is also necessary.
Q3What is the age limit for the Tripura High Court Junior Grade Translator recruitment?
A
The minimum age is 18 years. The maximum age is 40 years for general candidates and 45 years for SC/ST candidates.
Q4How many vacancies are available for the Junior Grade Translator position?
A
There are a total of 5 vacancies for the Junior Grade Translator post in the Tripura High Court.
Q5What is the application fee for UR category candidates?
A
The application fee for candidates belonging to the UR category is Rs. 600/-.
Q6What is the application fee for SC/ST category candidates?
A
The application fee for candidates belonging to the SC/ST category is Rs. 300/-.
Q7What is the selection process for this recruitment?
A
The selection process includes a Written Test, which will be of 3 hours duration, followed by a Viva Voce (Interview).
Q8What is the salary offered for the Junior Grade Translator post?
A
The selected candidates will receive a salary in Pay Band-2, Rs.5700-24000/- with Grade Pay Rs.4200/-, which is revised to Level-10 of the Tripura State Pay Matrix, 2018.
Q9Can candidates apply offline for this recruitment?
A
No, candidates must submit their applications strictly through the Online Application Portal only. Hard copy applications will not be entertained.
Q10Where can I find the official notification and apply online?
A
The official notification PDF and the online application link are available in the 'Important Links' section of this article. You can also visit the official website of Tripura High Court.
