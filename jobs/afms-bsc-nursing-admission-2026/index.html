AFMS B.Sc Nursing Admission 2026

AFMS B.Sc Nursing Admission 2026 notification has been released for female candidates. Armed Forces Medical Services (AFMS) will conduct admission to 4-year B.Sc. Nursing Course 2026 in Colleges of Nursing under AFMS. Online registration will start after the declaration of NEET UG 2026 result on the official website joinindianarmy.nic.in.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Only unmarried, divorcee, legally separated or widow female candidates without encumbrances.
  • Must be a citizen of India.
  • Age Limit: Born between 01 October 2001 and 30 September 2009 (both days inclusive).
  • Educational Qualification: Passed 10+2 in first attempt with Physics, Chemistry, Biology (Botany & Zoology) and English with minimum 50% aggregate marks.
  • Must have qualified NEET UG 2026.

How to Apply for AFMS B.Sc Nursing Admission 2026

  • Visit the official website joinindianarmy.nic.in.
  • Click on the AFMS B.Sc Nursing Admission 2026 link (activated after NEET result).
  • Complete registration using valid Email ID and Mobile Number.
  • Fill the online application form.
  • Upload required documents.
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Submit and take printout of the application.

Apply Online Link: Will be available on https://www.joinindianarmy.nic.in/ after NEET UG 2026 result.

Selection Process

  • Merit based on NEET UG 2026 Score
  • Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Psychological Assessment
  • Interview
  • Medical Examination
