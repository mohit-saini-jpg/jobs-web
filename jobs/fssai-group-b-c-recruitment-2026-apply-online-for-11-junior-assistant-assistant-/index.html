Qualification / Eligibility

The maximum age limit for appointment on deputation is 56 years as on the last date of receiving online applications, i.e., 19th June 2026. | All 4 posts require the candidate to be a serving officer in Central Government, State Government, Union Territories, Universities, Recognised Research Institutions, Public Sector Undertakings, Semi-Government, Statutory, or Autonomous Organisations. The specific experience requirements for each post are as follows: | DEP-01/2026-01 - Senior Private Secretary (Pay Level-08) Candidates must be holding an analogous post on regular basis in the parent cadre; OR must have completed 2 years' regular service in Pay Level-6 or equivalent in the parent cadre. Role: Secretarial Assistance (Group B, PL-08). | DEP-01/2026-02 - Assistant Manager (IT) (Pay Level-07) Candidates must be holding an analogous post on regular basis; OR have completed 5 years' regular service in Pay Level-6 or equivalent; AND must possess B.Tech/M.Tech in Computer Science or any Engineering discipline, or MCA, or a Bachelor's Degree in a relevant field. Role: IT Support (Group B, PL-07). | DEP-01/2026-03 - Assistant (Pay Level-06) Candidates must be holding an analogous post on regular basis; OR have completed 10 years' regular service in Pay Level-4 or equivalent; AND possess 3 years' experience in administration, human resource development, or vigilance matters depending on the post requirement; AND hold a Bachelor's Degree from a recognised University. Role: Admin and Financial related duties (Group B, PL-06). | DEP-01/2026-04 - Junior Assistant Grade-I (Pay Level-04) Candidates must be holding an analogous post on regular basis; OR have completed 8 years' regular service in Pay Level-2 or equivalent; AND possess 12th Standard or equivalent from a recognised Board or University. Role: Admin and Financial related duties (Group C, PL-04).

How to Apply

  • 1Applications for FSSAI Deputation 2026 must be submitted online first, followed by a physical hard copy through proper channel. Both steps are mandatory.
  • 2Visit the official FSSAI website at www.fssai.gov.in and click on the 'jobs@fssai(careers)' tab.
  • 3Apply online for the relevant post between 30th May 2026 and 19th June 2026. Ensure you fulfil the eligibility criteria as on the closing date (19.06.2026).
  • 4Note your FSSAI Application ID generated after online submission.
  • 5Take a printout of the completed online application form and get it duly certified by your employer/Cadre Controlling Authority in the prescribed format (Certificate by the Employer/Cadre Controlling Authority as per Annexure enclosed with the circular).
  • 6Attach the following supporting documents (duly certified photocopies): Integrity certificate from employer Vigilance clearance certificate Details of major/minor penalties imposed during the last 10 years Up-to-date certified photocopies of detailed APARs for the last 5 years (attested by an officer of Under Secretary rank or above)
  • 7Integrity certificate from employer
  • 8Vigilance clearance certificate
  • 9Details of major/minor penalties imposed during the last 10 years
  • 10Up-to-date certified photocopies of detailed APARs for the last 5 years (attested by an officer of Under Secretary rank or above)
  • 11Send the complete hard copy package through proper channel to reach by 13th July 2026 at: Administrative Officer, Recruitment Cell, FSSAI Headquarters, Room No. 312, 3rd Floor, FDA Bhawan, Kotla Road, New Delhi.
  • 12Note: Departmental officers in the feeder cadre who are in the direct line of promotion are not eligible for deputation consideration.
  • 13Important: Applications submitted only online without a hard copy, and hard copies received without an online application number, will both be liable for cancellation. Once submitted, applications cannot be withdrawn.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for FSSAI Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for online application is 19th June 2026. The hard copy of the application, duly certified by the employer and sent through proper channel, must reach FSSAI Headquarters by 13th July 2026.
Q2How many vacancies are there in FSSAI Deputation 2026?
A
FSSAI has released 11 vacancies across 4 posts — Senior Private Secretary (2), Assistant Manager IT (1), Assistant (6), and Junior Assistant Grade-I (2).
Q3What is the age limit for FSSAI Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
The maximum age limit is 56 years as on the closing date of online application, i.e., 19th June 2026.
Q4Who is eligible for FSSAI Deputation 2026?
A
Only currently serving officers of Central Government, State Governments, Union Territories, PSUs, Universities, Research Institutions, and Autonomous/Statutory Organisations are eligible. Fresh/direct candidates are not eligible for deputation posts. Officers in the direct line of promotion in the feeder cadre are also not eligible.
Q5Is there an application fee for FSSAI Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
No. There is no application fee for FSSAI Deputation Recruitment 2026 (DEP-01/2026).
Q6What is the pay for Senior Private Secretary in FSSAI Deputation 2026?
A
The pay scale for Senior Private Secretary is Pay Level-08 (₹47,600 – ₹1,51,100) under the 7th Pay Commission.
Q7How long is the deputation tenure for FSSAI 2026?
A
Deputation appointments are initially for 1, 2, or 3 years depending on the post, subject to availability of vacancy, and may be extended based on performance.
Q8Where should I send the FSSAI Deputation 2026 hard copy?
A
Send the certified hard copy through proper channel to: Administrative Officer, Recruitment Cell, FSSAI Headquarters, Room No. 312, 3rd Floor, FDA Bhawan, Kotla Road, New Delhi to reach by 13th July 2026.
