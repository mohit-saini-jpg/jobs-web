Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for APEDA Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the online application for APEDA Recruitment 2026 is 22nd June 2026. Candidates are advised to apply well in advance to avoid any last-minute technical issues.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in APEDA Recruitment 2026?

There are a total of 4 vacancies for the posts of Assistant General Manager (Finance) and Assistant Manager (Agriculture) in the APEDA Recruitment 2026.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for Assistant General Manager (Finance)?

For Assistant General Manager (Finance), candidates need a Post Graduate Degree in Finance/Financial Management/MBA(Finance) or Chartered Accountancy from ICAI with 5 years of relevant experience.

Q4. What is the age limit for the APEDA posts?

The maximum age limit for all the posts is 35 years. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per government norms.

Q5. What is the application fee for APEDA Recruitment 2026?

The application fee varies for different categories. General/OBC candidates need to pay Rs. 500/- for AGM (Finance) and Rs. 300/- for AM (Agriculture). SC/ST and Women candidates are exempted from paying the fee.

Q6. What is the selection process for APEDA Recruitment 2026?

The selection process will include a Written Test, followed by an Interview, and finally Document Verification.

Q7. What is the salary offered for these posts?

The selected candidates will receive a salary in the pay scale of Rs. 35400 - Rs. 177500 Per Month.

Q8. Can candidates from any state apply for this recruitment?

Yes, candidates from any state can apply for this recruitment, provided they meet the eligibility criteria. The job location is New Delhi.
