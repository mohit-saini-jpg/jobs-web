Bihar Board D.El.Ed Dummy Admit Card 2026

The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has released the Bihar Board D.El.Ed Dummy Admit Card 2026 for candidates who have registered for the Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed) Admission Process 2026. Candidates can access the dummy admit card through the official portal and verify the information submitted during registration.

The primary purpose of the dummy admit card is to allow candidates to check their personal and academic details and request corrections, if required, before the final admit card is issued. Candidates are advised to review all information carefully within the prescribed correction period.

How to Download Bihar Board D.El.Ed Dummy Admit Card 2026

Candidates can download the dummy admit card by following these steps:

  • Visit the official website: form.bsebdeled.com.
  • Click on the Login option.
  • Enter your registered User ID/Mobile Number and Password.
  • Complete the login process.
  • Click on the Download Dummy Admit Card 2026 link.
  • The dummy admit card will be displayed on the screen.
  • Check all details carefully.
  • Download and save a copy for reference.

Documents Required for Verification and Correction

Candidates may need the following documents while checking or correcting details:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Class 10 Mark Sheet and Certificate
  • Class 12 Mark Sheet and Certificate
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS), if applicable
  • Domicile Certificate
  • PwD Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Signature Scan
  • Valid Identity Proof
  • Application Form Copy

These documents may be required to support correction requests.

Exam-Day Instructions for Bihar D.El.Ed Entrance Exam 2026

Once the final admit card is issued, candidates should follow these examination guidelines:

  • Carry a printed copy of the final admit card.
  • Bring a valid original photo identity proof.
  • Reach the examination centre before the reporting time.
  • Follow all instructions mentioned on the admit card.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, Bluetooth devices, or electronic gadgets.
  • Cooperate during frisking and verification procedures.
  • Sit only at the allotted seat.
  • Do not engage in unfair means or malpractice.
  • Follow invigilator instructions throughout the examination.
  • Preserve the admit card for counselling and admission purposes.
