Qualification / Eligibility

Education Qualification

MBBS with registration, Graduate with 3 years experience, Graduation in Social Work/Psychology with 1-2 years experience, Literate Ex-drug user with 1-2 years sobriety, Class 8th pass with hospital experience, 3 years experience in Yoga/Dance/Music/Art, 5th class pass, Ex-serviceman or civil defense/home guard trained, able to ride bicycle, 5th class pass

Details

Doctor(Fulltime): MBBS with registration with medical council/medical commission along with should undergo training arranged by the MOSJE/NISD within three months of joining the Integrated Rehabilitation Centre for Addicts (IRCA). Project Coordinator cum Vocational Counselor: Graduate with experience of managing such centers for a minimum period of 3 years (or) demonstrable capability for running such centers and having working knowledge of computers. Counselor/Social Worker/Psychologist: Graduation in social sciences preferably in Social Work/Psychology with 1-2 years experience in the field and should have knowledge of English as well as one regional language. Preference shall be given to the person holding a Certificate of Training Course in de-addiction counseling from recognized institution. Peer Educator: Should be literate; Ex-drug user with 1-2 years of sobriety. Willing to work among drug using population and having communication skills. Agrees to refrain from using, buying or selling drugs. Ready to work for the prevention of harmful drug use and relapse. Ward Boy: Class 8th pass with experience of working in Hospitals/Health Care Centers/de-addiction centers. Yoga Therapist/Dance/Music/Art Teacher: Possessing experience of at least three years in the discipline. Chowkidar: 1. Must have passed 5th class or its equivalent. 2. Must be an Ex-Service man (or) Must have been trained in civil Defense or as a home guard. 3. Must be able to ride a bicycle (Male candidates only are eligible). Housekeeping staff: Must have passed 5th class or its equivalent.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the GGH Vizianagaram recruitment?

The last date to receive the application for the GGH Vizianagaram recruitment is 29-05-2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this date to avoid any last-minute issues.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in this GGH Vizianagaram recruitment?

There are a total of 9 vacancies announced for various contractual posts in the Government Teaching General Hospital, Vizianagaram. These positions are for different roles within the de-addiction center.

Q3. What is the application mode for GGH Vizianagaram recruitment?

The application mode for the GGH Vizianagaram recruitment is offline. Candidates need to download the application form, fill it out, and send it via post to the specified address.

Q4. What is the maximum age limit for applying to these posts?

The maximum age limit for all posts in this GGH Vizianagaram recruitment is 42 years. However, age relaxation is provided for certain categories as per government rules.

Q5. What is the starting salary for the lowest post?

The starting salary for the lowest post, Yoga Therapist/Dance/Music/Art Teacher, is Rs. 5000/- per month. The salary structure varies for different posts.

Q6. What is the highest salary offered in this recruitment?

The highest salary offered in this GGH Vizianagaram recruitment is for the Doctor (Fulltime) post, which is Rs. 60000/- per month. Other posts have different salary scales.

Q7. What are the eligibility criteria for the Peer Educator post?

To be eligible for the Peer Educator post, candidates should be literate, be an ex-drug user with 1-2 years of sobriety, possess communication skills, and agree to refrain from drug use. They should also be willing to work among drug-using populations.

Yes, there is an application fee for GGH Vizianagaram recruitment. General/OBC candidates need to pay Rs. 300/-, SC/ST candidates need to pay Rs. 200/-, and PwBD candidates need to pay Rs. 100/-.
