FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for TMC Clinical Research Coordinator posts?
A
The walk-in interview for the Clinical Research Coordinator posts at Tata Memorial Centre is scheduled for 17 June 2026. Candidates should ensure they reach the venue on this date.
Q2What is the educational qualification required for the Clinical Research Coordinator position?
A
Candidates must possess a Graduate Degree in Science/ B. Pharm and a PG Diploma in Clinical Research. One year of clinical research experience is preferable but not mandatory.
Q3What is the age limit for the Clinical Research Coordinator role?
A
The maximum age limit for candidates applying for the Clinical Research Coordinator position is 35 years.
Q4What is the salary offered for the Clinical Research Coordinator position?
A
The selected candidates for the Clinical Research Coordinator post will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 27,000 per month.
Q5How many vacancies are available for the Clinical Research Coordinator post?
A
There are a total of 3 vacancies announced for the Clinical Research Coordinator position at Tata Memorial Centre.
Q6What is the application mode for this recruitment?
A
This recruitment is being conducted through a Walk-in Interview. Candidates do not need to submit an online application form beforehand.
Q7What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents, one set of self-attested photocopies of these documents, and recent passport-size photographs to the interview venue.
Q8Is there any application fee for the TMC Clinical Research Coordinator recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q9What is the selection process for the Clinical Research Coordinator role?
A
The selection process includes an MCQ Test, followed by an Interview, and finally Document Verification for eligible candidates.
Q10Where is the walk-in interview venue located?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at Tata Memorial Centre, Mumbai, Maharashtra, Pincode: 400012.
