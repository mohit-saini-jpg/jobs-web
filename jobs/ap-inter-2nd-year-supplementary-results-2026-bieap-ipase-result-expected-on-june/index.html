What is AP Inter 2nd Year Supplementary Exam (IPASE)?

The Intermediate Public Advanced Supplementary Examination (IPASE) is a second-chance exam conducted by BIEAP every year for students who either failed in one or more subjects or were absent during the regular annual exams. The supplementary exams are conducted for both General and Vocational courses across all streams MPC, BiPC, MEC, CEC, and Humanities.

  • Conducted in two daily shifts to accommodate both 1st Year and 2nd Year students
  • Open to students who failed to score the minimum 35% marks in any subject
  • Covers both theory and practical components
  • Includes General and Vocational course students

How to Check AP Inter 2nd Year Supplementary Results 2026?

Once BIEAP activates the result link, students can follow the steps below to access and download their AP Inter 2nd Year IPASE scorecard 2026 from the official website. Keep your hall ticket handy before you begin.

  • Visit the official BIEAP result portal: resultsbie.ap.gov.in
  • Click on the link for "AP Inter 2nd Year Supplementary Results 2026" on the homepage.
  • Enter your Hall Ticket Number in the field provided.
  • Enter your Date of Birth as per your hall ticket.
  • Click the "Submit" button.
  • Your AP Inter Supply Result 2026 scorecard will appear on the screen.
  • Download and print the result for future reference.

The result available online is provisional in nature. Students must collect the official marks memo from their respective colleges/schools after formal distribution by BIEAP.

AP Inter 2nd Year Supplementary Exam 2026 - Passing Marks & Criteria

Understanding the minimum passing criteria is essential for students to assess their performance. BIEAP follows the same passing criteria for both regular and supplementary examinations.

  • Students must secure a minimum of 35% marks in each subject to be declared passed.
  • Marks are assessed across both theory and practical components wherever applicable.
  • Candidates who fail to secure 35% in any single subject are required to appear for the supplementary exam for that subject.
  • Students who clear the supplementary exam will be considered as pass in the respective academic year.

What to Do After AP Inter 2nd Year Supply Result 2026 is Declared?

Depending on their result outcome, students should take the appropriate next steps promptly. Here's a clear breakdown of what to do based on your result:

If You Pass the Supplementary Exam

  • Collect the official marks memo from your college once BIEAP distributes hard copies
  • Proceed with admissions to degree colleges, diploma courses, or other programmes
  • Apply for migration certificate from BIEAP if you plan to join an institution under a different board
  • Use your result to apply for intermediate-level scholarships and welfare schemes

If You Are Not Satisfied With Your Marks - Revaluation & Rechecking

  • BIEAP will release the schedule for revaluation (full re-assessment of answer scripts) and rechecking (marks totalling verification) after the result is declared
  • Applications must be submitted within the prescribed deadline along with the required fee
  • Details will be available on the official BIEAP website at bie.ap.gov.in

If You Fail the Supplementary Exam

  • Students who still do not clear will need to appear again in the next academic year's supplementary exams
  • Explore open school or distance education options through BIEAP-recognised channels if needed
