Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for NVS Jaipur Counsellors recruitment?

The last date to submit your online application for the NVS Jaipur Counsellors (Male/Female) posts is 28-05-2026. Ensure your application is submitted well before this deadline.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Counsellor post?

Candidates must possess a Master’s Degree (M.A./M.Sc.) in Psychology and a one-year Diploma in Guidance & Counselling from a recognized institution, along with at least one year of relevant experience.

Q3. What is the age limit for the Counsellor position?

The age limit for Counsellors (Male/Female) is between 28 years and 49 years as of 01-05-2026. Age relaxation will be provided as per government norms.

Q4. What is the salary offered for the Counsellor post?

Selected candidates for the Counsellor post will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 44,900/- per month.

Q5. How will candidates be selected for the Counsellor role?

The selection process involves shortlisting based on qualifications and experience (70 marks) followed by a Personal Talk/Interview (30 marks).

Q6. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

Yes, the application fee is Rs. 500/- for General/OBC candidates. SC/ST candidates are exempted from paying the application fee.

Q7. What is the application mode for NVS Jaipur Counsellors recruitment?

Applications for this recruitment must be submitted through the online mode only. Offline applications will not be accepted.

Q8. What is the job type for the Counsellor position?

The Counsellor position is on a contract basis.
