HARTRON Advanced Skill Centre Courses Admission for the 2026-27 session is now open. HARTRON (Haryana State Electronics Development Corporation Limited) has started admissions for various NSQF-aligned job-oriented IT and skill development courses at its Hartron Advanced Skill Centres (HASC) across Haryana. These government-certified programmes focus on emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, Digital Marketing, Web Development, and more.

HARTRON Advanced Skill Centre Courses Admission 2026 has commenced for the new academic session. Candidates can now apply for industry-relevant, NSQF-aligned certificate and diploma courses in AI, Cyber Security, Data Analytics, Digital Marketing, Web Development, Python Programming, and other IT skills at authorised Hartron Advanced Skill Centres across Haryana. These courses are designed to provide practical training and government-recognised certification to enhance employability in the private and public sectors. Admissions are open on a first-come, first-served basis through walk-in or online enquiry at nearest centres.

Application Fee & Course Fees

Course fees vary by programme (typically ₹15,000 to ₹25,000 for most 6-month to 1-year courses). Some centres offer lumpsum payment options and discounts. Contact the nearest centre for exact fee structure.

How to Apply for HARTRON Advanced Skill Centre Courses Admission 2026

  • Visit the official website: www.hartron.org.in
  • Locate the nearest Hartron Advanced Skill Centre.
  • Walk-in or fill online enquiry form.
  • Attend counselling and eligibility check.
  • Submit documents and pay admission fee.
  • Receive enrolment number and batch schedule.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) Courses

HARTRON offers Artificial Intelligence Application Developer and Artificial Intelligence Associate programmes. These NSQF-aligned courses cover machine learning, neural networks, Python for AI, and real-world AI applications. Duration: 6-12 months. Ideal for graduates seeking high-demand tech careers.

Cyber Security Courses

Junior Cyber Security Associate and advanced cyber security programmes focus on ethical hacking, network security, cryptography, and threat detection. These practical, job-oriented courses prepare students for roles in cybersecurity firms and IT departments.

Cloud Computing Courses

Junior Cloud Computing Associate course covers AWS, Azure, cloud infrastructure, and deployment. NSQF Level 4/5 aligned with strong industry focus.

Digital Marketing Courses

Digital Marketing Assistant course includes SEO, SEM, social media marketing, content marketing, and analytics. Duration 6 months. Excellent for students and freelancers.

Web Development & Software Development

Courses like Course in Software Development, Web Technology, and Full Stack Development provide hands-on training in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, PHP, and modern frameworks.
