ESIC Kalaburagi Faculty Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Qualification: As per latest NMC norms for respective posts. Age Limit: Less than 69 years (including all category relaxations) as on the date of document verification. Registration: Valid registration with Karnataka State Medical Council (KMC) / NMC. Experience: As per NMC requirements for Professor / Associate / Assistant Professor. Retirees from Central/State Govt./PSU and candidates from recognized private institutions are eligible.

ESIC Faculty Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Document Verification (29.07.2026) Personal Interview (31.07.2026) Medical Examination & Certificate Verification 50% marks in interview is the minimum qualifying criteria. Final selection is based on interview performance.

Pay & Allowances

Professor: ₹2,48,850/- Associate Professor: ₹1,65,480/- Assistant Professor: ₹1,42,170/- (Plus TA & DA as applicable. No other allowances like HRA, NPA, etc.) Caution Deposit: Equal to one month’s remuneration (refundable after completion of contract subject to conditions).

Important Instructions for Candidates

This is purely contractual engagement. No claim for regular absorption. No private practice allowed. Candidates must bring all original documents + self-attested copies. Application in prescribed format only. Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification.

Who Should Attend the Walk-in Interview?

Candidates meeting NMC norms for the respective faculty posts. Retired faculty willing to work on contract. Doctors with required teaching experience in recognized institutions.
