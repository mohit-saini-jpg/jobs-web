Eligibility Criteria for BCECEB DECE-LE 2026

Before you check your result, it's worth knowing what the qualifying benchmark was. Candidates had to meet one of the following educational requirements:

  • 12th (Intermediate) in Science with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects, along with either Mathematics or Biology
  • 12th Science with Mathematics
  • 12th Science with a Vocational/Technical subject
  • Class 10 pass with an ITI certificate in a relevant trade

No age limit was prescribed for this examination, as per BCECEB rules.

How to Download BCECEB DECE-LE Rank Card 2026

Downloading your rank card is pretty simple. Here's a step-by-step breakdown:

  • Go to the official BCECEB website: bceceboard.bihar.gov.in
  • Click on the "Rank Card of DECE[LE]-2026" link
  • You'll land on the rank card portal — enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth
  • Hit the submit/search button
  • Your rank card will appear on the screen
  • Download and save it as a PDF for future use
