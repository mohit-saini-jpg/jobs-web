Nalanda University Admission 2026 Eligibility Criteria

ProgrammeEligibility Criteria
Master’s ProgrammesMinimum 3 years Bachelor’s degree (10+2+3) from a recognised University with at least 55% marks or 2.2/4.0 GPA or equivalent. Students from any stream can apply for a maximum of two programmes.
MBA in Sustainable Development and ManagementCandidates must meet the Master’s programme eligibility criteria. Additional requirements include CAT/XAT/MAT score with 70 percentile for Indian students or relevant work experience.
Ph.D. ProgrammesMaster’s degree with minimum 65% marks. For part-time Ph.D. candidates with experience, minimum 60% marks are required. Research Proposal, Statement of Purpose (SoP), Recommendation Letters, and writing samples are also required.
