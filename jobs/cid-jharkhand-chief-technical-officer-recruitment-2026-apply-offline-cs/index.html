Qualification / Eligibility

Chief Technical Officer: Bachelor of Engineering in IT, Computer Science or equivalent or MCA from a recognized Institute. M.Tech in CS/IT/Information Security desirable. Minimum 5 years work experience in IT. Proficiency in security/forensic tools, programming/scripting, data analysis, hardware extraction, networking protocols, wireless technologies. Global certifications like CEH/CISM/CISSP preferable.
