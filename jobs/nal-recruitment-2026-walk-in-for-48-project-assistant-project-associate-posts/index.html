NAL Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

CSIR-National Aerospace Laboratories (NAL), Bengaluru has invited applications for 48 Project Staff positions (Advt. No. 12/2026/PS dated 16 June 2026) across Aerospace, Electrical/Electronics, and Chemistry/Materials disciplines. There is no online application - eligible candidates must attend a walk-in interview on one of four dates between 19 June and 29 June 2026 at CSIR-NAL, Kodihalli, Bengaluru, carrying the prescribed application form and original documents.

NAL Recruitment 2026 : Application Fee Details

Candidates asking about the application fee for this recruitment can note that CSIR-NAL has not prescribed any application fee for Project Staff walk-in interviews. The only cost candidates bear is their own travel and stay, since no TA/DA (Travelling/Dearness Allowance) is paid for attending the interview, regardless of distance travelled or selection outcome.

NAL Recruitment 2026 : Eligibility & Qualification

Details
  1. Eligibility for CSIR-NAL Project Staff depends primarily on the position level (PA-II, PAT-I, or PAT-II) rather than a single uniform standard, since each grade has its own qualification and experience benchmark. The summary below explains what is required at each level
  2. This is the entry-level diploma post, requiring only a Diploma in Engineering/Technology in the relevant discipline (Electronics or Aerospace/Aeronautical/Mechanical), with no prior work experience mandated
  3. This level requires a Master's/Integrated Master's degree from a recognized University, OR a Bachelor's degree in Engineering/Technology, OR an equivalent qualification recognized in writing by the Project Investigator and Director. Candidates holding a valid NET/GATE score are eligible for the higher PAT-I (NET/GATE) emolument of Rs. 31,000/- in the Chemistry/Materials category
  4. This is the most experience-intensive grade, requiring a Bachelor's degree in Engineering/Technology with 2 years of R&D experience in industrial, academic, or scientific organizations, OR a Master's/Integrated Master's degree in Engineering/Technology without the experience requirement. Candidates with a valid NET/GATE score qualify for the enhanced Rs. 35,000/- emoluments under the Aerospace/Aeronautical category
  5. The age limit for all 48 posts is 35 years, calculated as on the date of the respective Walk-in-Interview, not a fixed cut-off date. Age relaxation of 5 years applies to SC/ST/PwBD/Women candidates, and 3 years to OBC candidates, or as per prevailing Government of India instructions; candidates must attach documentary proof of category to claim this relaxation, failing which they will not be permitted to appear for the interview

NAL Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Since CSIR-NAL accepts applications only in person, candidates must prepare their documents in advance and report physically on the correct date. Below is the complete process to follow.
  • 2Download the prescribed application form from the CSIR-NAL website (https://www.nal.res.in).
  • 3Fill in the form completely, mentioning all qualifications and relevant experience beyond the minimum prescribed requirement.
  • 4Reach CSIR-NAL, RAB Meeting Complex, Kodihalli, Bengaluru - 560017 on the interview date applicable to your post and discipline.
  • 5Report for registration and document verification between 9:00 AM and 9:45 AM.
  • 6Carry the filled application form, a latest passport-size photograph, original certificates with one set of photocopies, Aadhaar Card or any Government photo ID, and category certificate (if applicable).
  • 7Submit a separate application for each post if applying for more than one position.
  • 8Attend the interview; be prepared to return the next day if the process is not completed on the scheduled date.
  • 9Candidates are explicitly instructed NOT to send applications by post, speed post, or email - only in-person walk-in applications are accepted.

NAL Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What are the walk-in interview dates for CSIR-NAL Project Staff Recruitment 2026?
A
The interviews are scheduled on 19 June, 22 June, 24 June, and 29 June 2026, with each date assigned to a specific discipline group.
Q2How many vacancies are there in CSIR-NAL Advt. No. 12/2026/PS?
A
There are 48 total Project Staff vacancies across Project Assistant-II, Project Associate-I, and Project Associate-II posts in Aerospace, Electrical/Electronics, and Chemistry/Materials disciplines.
Q3Is there an online application for this recruitment?
A
No, CSIR-NAL does not accept applications by post, speed post, or email; candidates must attend the walk-in interview in person with a filled application form and original documents.
Q4What is the age limit for CSIR-NAL Project Staff posts?
A
The age limit is 35 years for all posts, calculated as on the date of the respective walk-in interview, with 5 years relaxation for SC/ST/PwBD/Women and 3 years for OBC candidates.
Q5What is the salary for Project Staff at CSIR-NAL?
A
Monthly emoluments range from Rs. 20,000/- for Project Assistant-II to Rs. 35,000/- for Project Associate-II (NET/GATE), with HRA added on top in each case.
Q6Is there an application fee for this recruitment?
A
No application fee has been prescribed for CSIR-NAL Project Staff walk-in interviews.
Q7What is the duration of this engagement?
A
The engagement is temporary and contract-based, co-terminus with the project, with a maximum cumulative duration of six years across one or more CSIR projects.
Q8Where is the CSIR-NAL walk-in interview venue?
A
The interview venue is the RAB Meeting Complex, National Aerospace Laboratories, Adjacent to SBI NAL Branch, Kodihalli, Bengaluru – 560017.
Q9Will travel allowance be paid for attending the interview?
A
No, no TA/DA will be paid to candidates for attending the walk-in interview.
Q10Can a candidate apply for more than one position?
A
Yes, but a separate application must be submitted for each position applied for.
