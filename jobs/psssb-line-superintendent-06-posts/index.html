FAQs

Q1What is Punjab SSSB Line Superintendent Recruitment 2026?
A
Punjab SSSB Line Superintendent Recruitment 2026 is a Group‑C recruitment drive by the Subordinate Services Selection Board (SSSB), Punjab, for 6 Line Superintendent posts in the Electricity Department (Energy Branch) under Advertisement No. 11 of 2026.
Q2How many vacancies are announced for PSSSB Line Superintendent 2026?
A
The notification declares a total of 6 vacancies for Line Superintendent, including backlog posts, distributed as 4 for General, 1 for SC (Mazhabi & Balmiki) and 1 for SC (Ramdasia & Others), with 2 of these posts reserved for women candidates.
Q3What are the application dates for Punjab SSSB Line Superintendent Recruitment 2026?
A
As per the advertisement, the online application portal opens on 06 June 2026 and the last date for submission of online applications and fee payment is 19 June 2026; all eligibility conditions such as age are calculated as on 01 January 2026.
Q4Who needs to apply again under Advt. No. 11/2026 and are they exempt from fees?
A
Candidates who had earlier applied for Line Superintendent under Advertisement No. 08/2023 (now withdrawn) must apply afresh against Advt. No. 11/2026, but they are exempt from paying the application fee and their age will be considered as on 01 January 2023.
Q5What is the educational qualification required for PSSSB Line Superintendent 2026?
A
Applicants must possess a three‑year Diploma in Electrical Engineering from a recognized institution and should have passed Matriculation with Punjabi as a compulsory or elective subject or an equivalent Punjabi language qualification in accordance with Punjab Civil Services Rules and relevant government notifications.
Q6What is the age limit for Punjab SSSB Line Superintendent posts?
A
For General category candidates, the age must be between 18 and 37 years as on 01 January 2026, while SC category candidates of Punjab can avail relaxation up to 42 years and certain other categories like government employees and specified women categories are eligible for further relaxations as per Punjab government rules.
Q7What is the application fee for PSSSB Line Superintendent Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 1,000 for General category candidates and Rs. 250 for SC candidates, payable online through the PSSSB application portal; candidates covered under withdrawn Advt. No. 08/2023 and re‑applying are exempted from paying any fee.
Q8What is the selection process and exam pattern for Punjab SSSB Line Superintendent 2026?
A
The selection process consists of an objective type written examination divided into Part A (Punjabi language qualifying test) and Part B (technical/job‑related MCQs with 1 mark each and ¼ negative marking), followed by document verification; the final merit list is prepared on the basis of Part‑B marks only, subject to qualifying Part A with at least 50% marks.
Q9How can I apply online for Punjab SSSB Line Superintendent Recruitment 2026?
A
Eligible candidates must visit the official SSSB Punjab website, complete registration to generate a User ID and password, fill the online form with personal, educational and category details, upload required documents like diploma certificate, Punjabi matric certificate, domicile and caste certificate, pay the applicable fee online, and finally submit and print the application for future reference.
Q10What is the official website and notification link for PSSSB Line Superintendent 2026?
A
All details, including the detailed Advertisement No. 11 of 2026, syllabus, exam plan, online application link, admit card and result updates, are available on the official Punjab Subordinate Services Selection Board website under the vacancies/advertisements section.
