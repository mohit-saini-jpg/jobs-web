AUEET Hall Ticket 2026

Andhra University has released the AUEET 2026 hall ticket for the Andhra University Engineering Entrance Test on its official DOA portal. Registered candidates who have successfully submitted the AUEET 2026 online application can now download their admit card in online mode only.

As per the official schedule, AUEET 2026 hall tickets are available for download from 7 May 2026, and the entrance test is scheduled for 10 May 2026 at designated test centres. Candidates must carry a printed copy of the AUEET 2026 hall ticket along with valid ID proof to the exam centre, otherwise they will not be allowed to appear for the test.

How to Download AUEET Hall Ticket 2026

Candidates can download their AUEET 2026 admit card by following these steps:

  • Visit the Andhra University DOA portal: https://audoa.andhrauniversity.edu.in and select AUEET‑2026 from the list of CETs.
  • On the AUEET page, click on the “Download Hall‑Ticket” or “Login for Downloading Hall Ticket” link.
  • On the login page, enter your Application Number and Date of Birth in the format DD/MM/YYYY.
  • Click on “Submit” / “Login” to access your AUEET 2026 hall ticket.
  • Your AUEET Hall Ticket 2026 will appear on the screen, containing exam date, time, test centre and candidate details.
  • Verify all details carefully, then download and take 2–3 printouts of the hall ticket on A4‑size paper to carry on exam day.

If you do not remember your application number, use the “Get Application No.” link on the portal to retrieve it before logging in.

Details Mentioned on AUEET Hall Ticket 2026

Candidates should cross‑check the following details printed on their AUEET 2026 admit card:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph and signature
  • Application number and AUEET‑2026 hall ticket/roll number
  • Exam name: AUEET 2026
  • Exam date (10 May 2026), reporting time and exam duration
  • Test centre name, code and full address
  • Course/group applied for (B.Tech/Integrated programme)
  • Important instructions and guidelines for the entrance test

In case of any discrepancy in name, photo, exam date or centre details, candidates must contact the Directorate of Admissions, Andhra University, before the exam date for clarification.

Documents Required with AUEET Hall Ticket 2026

On the day of the AUEET‑2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the AUEET Hall Ticket 2026 (clear A4‑size printout, preferably colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Driving Licence Voter ID Any other government‑issued ID accepted by the exam centre.
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Voter ID
  • Any other government‑issued ID accepted by the exam centre.
  • Additional documents, if mentioned in the brochure or admit card (for example, PwD certificate for candidates claiming disability‑related benefits).

Entry to the exam centre will not be permitted without the printed AUEET hall ticket and valid photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for AUEET 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the AUEET‑2026 exam, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the hall ticket so that verification and seating can be completed; late entry after the scheduled time may not be allowed.
  • Carry only essential items: AUEET hall ticket, valid photo ID and permitted stationery (blue/black ball‑point pens, pencils, eraser, etc.) as per instructions.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not allowed), Bluetooth devices, notes or any electronic gadgets into the exam hall; such items are strictly prohibited.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the hall ticket and those announced by the invigilators; indulging in any unfair means can lead to cancellation of the exam and disqualification.
  • Keep the AUEET 2026 hall ticket safely even after the exam, as it may be required during counselling and admission.
