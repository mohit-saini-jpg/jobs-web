CG Open School Result 2026

Chhattisgarh State Open School (CGSOS) has officially released the CG Open School 10th, 12th Result 2026 for the April session. Students who appeared for the Class 10th and Class 12th examinations can now access their results online.

CG Open School Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - sos.cg.nic.in. Students can check and download their results by entering their roll number or required login credentials on the result portal.

The results are available for secondary (10th) and higher secondary (12th) courses under the open school system. Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in their mark sheet and contact the board authorities in case of any discrepancies.

How to Check CG Open School Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your CGSOS 10th and 12th results online:

  • Visit the official website - sos.cg.nic.in
  • Click on the “Open School Result 2026” link available on the homepage
  • Select either Class 10th or Class 12th result link
  • Enter your roll number in the login field
  • Click on the “Submit” button
  • Your CG Open School result 2026 will appear on the screen
  • Download and take a printout for future reference
