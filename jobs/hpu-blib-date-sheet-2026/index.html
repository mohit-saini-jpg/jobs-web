HPU B.Lib Date Sheet 2026 : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 06/07/2026

Himachal Pradesh University (HPU), Shimla has released the official date sheet for several July 2026 examinations, including Bachelor of Library and Information Science (B.Lib.), B.Ed. (Regular and CDOE/ICDEOL), and left-out papers for B.A. LL.B. (Hons.). Students can check their exact exam dates, timings, and course codes below, along with direct links to download the official notifications from the HPU Conduct Branch.

The HPU Conduct Branch, Shimla-5 issued these date sheets in the first week of July 2026, and all exams are scheduled to be held through July and early August 2026. Below is a complete, section-wise breakdown of each date sheet.

HPU B.Lib Date Sheet 2026

This section covers the exam schedule for the B.Lib. 2nd Semester examination to be held in July 2026, including the exact dates, course codes, and paper titles notified by HPU.

HPU has notified the date sheet for the Bachelor of Library and Information Science 2nd Semester Examination to be held in July 2026. Both 1st and 2nd Semester papers are included in the same schedule, since some 1st Semester students are appearing alongside 2nd Semester candidates. All theory exams are held from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM.

HPU B.Ed. Date Sheet 2026 (2nd & 4th Semester Regular + CDOE/ICDEOL)

This section explains the exam schedule for B.Ed. 2nd and 4th Semester (Regular college students) as well as 1st and 2nd year Annual System students under CDOE (ICDEOL), with exams running from 14 July to 6 August 2026.

Direct Link to Download HPU B.Ed Date Sheet 2026 PDF Here

The B.Ed. date sheet applies to two separate student groups:

  • Regular college students appearing in the 2nd and 4th Semester (Semester System)
  • CDOE (ICDEOL) students appearing in the 1st and 2nd year (Annual System)

All exams are held in the Evening session, with papers of 80 marks scheduled from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM and papers of 40 marks from 2:00 PM to 3:30 PM.

How to Download the HPU Date Sheet 2026 (Official Steps)

This section walks through the simple steps to access and download any HPU date sheet directly from the university's official website.

To download any HPU date sheet, including the ones covered above, follow these steps:

  • Visit the official HPU examination date sheet page: Click Here
  • Locate your course/exam name in the "Examination Schedules" list.
  • Click the "Download" link next to the relevant entry to open the PDF.
  • Save or print the date sheet and cross-check your course code and exam date carefully.

Students are advised to keep checking the official HPU website regularly, as universities frequently issue corrigenda or notifications for paper changes closer to the exam dates.
