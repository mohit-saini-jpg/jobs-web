How To Apply

  • Applications for FSSAI Deputation 2026 must be submitted online first, followed by a physical hard copy through proper channel. Both steps are mandatory.
  • Visit the official FSSAI website at www.fssai.gov.in and click on the 'jobs@fssai(careers)' tab.
  • Apply online for the relevant post between 30th May 2026 and 19th June 2026. Ensure you fulfil the eligibility criteria as on the closing date (19.06.2026).
  • Note your FSSAI Application ID generated after online submission.
  • Take a printout of the completed online application form and get it duly certified by your employer/Cadre Controlling Authority in the prescribed format (Certificate by the Employer/Cadre Controlling Authority as per Annexure enclosed with the circular).
  • Attach the following supporting documents (duly certified photocopies): Integrity certificate from employer Vigilance clearance certificate Details of major/minor penalties imposed during the last 10 years Up-to-date certified photocopies of detailed APARs for the last 5 years (attested by an officer of Under Secretary rank or above)
  • Integrity certificate from employer
  • Vigilance clearance certificate
  • Details of major/minor penalties imposed during the last 10 years
  • Up-to-date certified photocopies of detailed APARs for the last 5 years (attested by an officer of Under Secretary rank or above)
  • Send the complete hard copy package through proper channel to reach by 13th July 2026 at: Administrative Officer, Recruitment Cell, FSSAI Headquarters, Room No. 312, 3rd Floor, FDA Bhawan, Kotla Road, New Delhi.
  • Note: Departmental officers in the feeder cadre who are in the direct line of promotion are not eligible for deputation consideration.
  • Important: Applications submitted only online without a hard copy, and hard copies received without an online application number, will both be liable for cancellation. Once submitted, applications cannot be withdrawn.
