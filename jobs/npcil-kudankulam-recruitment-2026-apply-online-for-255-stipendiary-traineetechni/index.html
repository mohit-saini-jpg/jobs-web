NPCIL Kudankulam Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

NPCIL Stipendiary Trainee/Technician Salary and Stipend 2026

Selected candidates will undergo a 2-year training programme with the following stipend structure: First year: Rs. 20,000 per month Second year: Rs. 22,000 per month One-time book allowance: Rs. 3,000 (no other allowance is admissible during training) On successful completion of training, trainees will be absorbed for appointment as Technician-B in Level-3 of the pay matrix, with Basic Pay of Rs. 21,700 plus other applicable pay and allowances.

Q3What is the age limit for NPCIL Stipendiary Trainee/Technician posts?
A
The age limit is 18 to 24 years, with age relaxation of 3 years for OBC(NCL), 5 years for SC/ST, and 10 years for PwBD candidates (cumulative where applicable).
Q4What is the stipend for NPCIL Stipendiary Trainee/Technician during training?
A
Trainees receive Rs. 20,000 per month in the first year and Rs. 22,000 per month in the second year, along with a one-time book allowance of Rs. 3,000.
Q5What educational qualification is required for the NPCIL ST/TN Plant Operator post?
A
Candidates need HSC (10+2) or ISC with Science stream (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) and a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate.
Q6Is ITI required for NPCIL Electronics Mechanic, Electrician, or Fitter posts?
A
Yes. Candidates need SSC (10th) with minimum 50% marks in Science and Mathematics individually, plus a 2-year ITI certificate in the relevant trade. If the ITI course is shorter than 2 years, 1 year of relevant work experience is required.

On successful completion of the 2-year training, trainees are absorbed as Technician-B in Level-3 of the pay matrix with Basic Pay of Rs. 21,700 plus other applicable pay and allowances.
