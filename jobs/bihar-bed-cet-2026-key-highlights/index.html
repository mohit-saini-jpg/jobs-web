How to Download Bihar BEd CET Scorecard 2026

Follow the step-by-step process below to download your Bihar BEd CET 2026 scorecard quickly from the official website without any confusion.

Step 1: Visit the official website biharcetbed-brabu.in

Step 2: On the homepage, look for the link labelled "CET B.Ed 2026 - Result / Scorecard"

Step 3: Click on the link; you will be redirected to the candidate login page

Step 4: Enter your Application Number and Password / Date of Birth along with the Captcha code

Step 5: Your Bihar BEd CET Result 2026 scorecard will be displayed on the screen

Step 6: Download and save the scorecard PDF for future reference

What Does the Bihar BEd CET 2026 Scorecard Contain?

The Bihar BEd CET scorecard is an official document issued by BRABU that serves as proof of your performance. The scorecard contains marks obtained, rank, and qualifying status, all of which are required for the counselling and admission process.

The scorecard typically includes:

  • Candidate's Name and Application Number
  • Roll Number
  • Marks Obtained (out of 120)
  • Overall Rank in Merit List
  • Category
  • Qualifying Status (Qualified / Not Qualified)
  • Course Applied For (Regular / Distance / Shiksha Shastri)

Bihar BEd CET 2026 Qualifying Marks - Category-Wise

To be eligible for counselling and B.Ed college admission, candidates must have scored at or above the minimum qualifying marks. Below are the qualifying marks based on category, as per the established norms.

General category candidates are required to score a minimum of 35% (42 marks out of 120) to qualify, while the passing marks for SC, ST, BC, EBC, WBC, EWS, and Divyaang candidates are 30%, which equals 36 marks.

About Bihar BEd CET 2026 Exam

This section gives a brief overview of the Bihar BEd CET 2026 exam structure to help candidates understand how their result has been calculated.

The Bihar CET B.Ed 2026 was conducted for Regular, Distance, and Shiksha Shastri 02 Years Course for the session 2026-28, with the exam held on 7 June 2026.

Key exam details:

  • Total Marks: 120
  • Total Questions: 120 MCQs (1 mark each)
  • Exam Duration: 2 hours
  • Negative Marking: None
  • Mode: Offline (OMR-based)

What Happens After Bihar BEd CET Result 2026?

Once the result is declared, qualified candidates move into the counselling and admission phase. Understanding the process ahead helps candidates prepare in advance.

After result declaration, merit lists are released in multiple phases based on performance and seat availability. Counselling is the final stage of selection and is conducted online by the nodal university

The post-result process broadly follows these stages:

  • Scorecard Download - Available on biharcetbed-brabu.in
  • Merit List Publication - Category-wise merit lists released in multiple phases
  • Counselling Registration - Qualified candidates register online for counselling
  • College Preference Filling - Candidates choose preferred B.Ed colleges
  • Seat Allotment - Seats allotted based on rank, category, and preference
  • Document Verification & Admission - Reporting to the allotted college
