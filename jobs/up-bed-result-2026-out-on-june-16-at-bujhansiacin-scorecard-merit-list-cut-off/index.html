UP BEd Result 2026 Date - When Will It Be Declared?

Bundelkhand University has declared the UP BEd JEE 2026 result by June 16. Along with the result, the university will also release the UP BEd JEE answer key on its official website. Based on past trends, Bundelkhand University Jhansi had announced the UP BEd JEE result 2025 on June 16 as well, making it highly likely that the 2026 result will follow the same schedule.

How to Check UP BEd Result 2026 - Step-by-Step Guide

Once the result is live, candidates can follow these simple steps to access and download their scorecard from the official Bundelkhand University portal.

Candidates who appeared for the UP BEd entrance exam can download the result by using their login credentials. Here is the complete process:

  • Visit the official website - Go to bujhansi.ac.in
  • Find the result link - Look for the "UP BEd JEE 2026 Result/Scorecard" link on the homepage
  • Enter login credentials - Enter your User ID and Password generated during registration
  • Click Login - Your result including total marks and state/category rank will appear on the screen
  • Download and print - Save the PDF and take a printout for use during the counselling process

Details Mentioned on the UP BEd JEE 2026 Scorecard

Your scorecard is an important document you will need throughout the admission process. The UP B.Ed JEE 2026 result contains a number of important details such as the name of the candidate, guardian's name, name of the category, roll number, registration number, marks obtained, rank in the UP B.Ed JEE entrance exam, and other details related to UP BEd 2026.

Here is a checklist of what your scorecard will include:

  • Candidate's full name
  • Application number and roll number
  • Guardian's name
  • Category
  • Examination qualifying status
  • Total marks obtained
  • Paper-wise marks obtained
  • State rank
  • Category rank
  • Result declaration date

Any discrepancies found must be reported to the university immediately.

UP BEd JEE 2026 Expected Cut Off Marks

The cut off is the minimum score a candidate must achieve to qualify and be considered for the merit list. This section explains the expected category-wise cut off for 2026 based on current trends and previous year patterns.

The expected UP B.Ed JEE 2026 cutoff is around 342-353 for General category, 340-345 for OBC, 321-355 for EWS, 213-218 for SC, and 211-216 for ST/PwD.

UP BEd JEE 2026 Merit List - What to Expect

The merit list is a crucial document that determines which candidates are called for the counselling process and ultimately get admission to B.Ed colleges in Uttar Pradesh.

The university will also issue the UP BEd JEE merit lists on its official website soon after the result declaration. The merit list is prepared based on the marks secured in the entrance examination. Candidates whose names appear on the merit list are eligible to participate in the counselling rounds for seat allotment in UP's B.Ed colleges.

UP BEd JEE 2026 Counselling - What Happens After the Result?

After the result is declared, qualified candidates must complete the counselling registration to secure admission. Here is what the process looks like.

The UP BEd JEE 2026 counselling will commence from June, after the result declaration, and will be conducted in multiple rounds. To be eligible for UP BEd seat allotment, candidates must complete the registration before June 25.

Key steps in the counselling process include:

  • Online Registration for counselling on the official portal
  • Document Verification at designated centres
  • Choice Filling - selecting preferred colleges
  • Seat Allotment based on merit and preferences
  • Reporting to Allotted College for admission confirmation

Documents Required for UP BEd Counselling 2026

Candidates must keep the following documents ready before attending the counselling process to avoid any last-minute issues:

  • UP BEd JEE 2026 Scorecard (printed copy)
  • Class 10 and Class 12 mark sheets and certificates
  • Graduation mark sheets and degree certificate
  • Category certificate (if applicable - SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwD)
  • Domicile/Residence certificate of Uttar Pradesh
  • Valid photo ID proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, etc.)
  • Recent passport-size photographs
  • Income certificate (if required for fee waiver)
