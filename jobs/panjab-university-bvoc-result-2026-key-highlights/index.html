How to Check Panjab University B.Voc Result 2026 Online

Checking your Panjab University semester result is a straightforward process. Follow these simple steps to access your B.Voc. scorecard from the official examination results portal.

  • Visit the official results portal at results.puexam.in
  • On the homepage, locate the "Semester Results" section in the left-hand menu
  • Click on "December, 2025" under the Semester Results list
  • A list of all recently declared results will appear on the right side of the page
  • Find and click on "B.Voc. (Fashion Technology and Apparel Design) FYUP 1st Semester Examination (June 25, 2026) – December, 2025"
  • You will be redirected to the result page - enter your roll number or enrollment details as required
  • Your result and scorecard will be displayed on the screen
  • Download or print the result for future reference

Tip: If the direct link above is not loading due to high traffic, try accessing the result through the official homepage and navigate via the December 2025 results tab.
