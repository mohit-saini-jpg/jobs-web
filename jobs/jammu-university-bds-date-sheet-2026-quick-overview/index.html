Important Instructions for Candidates

Both official date sheets carry the same set of examination instructions, summarized below:

  • Separate answer books will be provided for Part I and Part II of the examination.
  • All candidates must appear for their practical examination at their respective (own) college.
  • Mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, or any other electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited inside the examination centre.
  • Candidates must write their answers only with a blue ink pen or ball pen.
  • Candidates must not write their name, roll number, signature, or any other identifying mark anywhere in the answer book except in the space specifically provided; any violation will be treated as use of unfair means under the University Statutes.
  • Bags, books, notes, and other personal belongings are not permitted inside the examination centre; the University/Centre is not responsible for loss of such items.
  • Candidates are advised to verify their course number, course titles, date, time, and examination centre carefully and to report to the centre well before the exam begins.

How to Download the Jammu University BDS Date Sheet 2026-27

  • Visit the official Controller of Examinations,Jammu University website: https://www.coeJammu University .com/circulars.aspx
  • Look for the circular titled "Date-Sheet of BDS 2nd Prof Examination (Regular Session) Dec, 2026" or "Date-Sheet of BDS 3rd Prof Examination (Regular Session) Dec-Jan, 2026-27".
  • Open and download the relevant PDF notification.
  • Cross-check your course number, subject names, dates, and timing against your own college's centre notice once issued.
