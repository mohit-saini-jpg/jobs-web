Rayalaseema University Result 2026 - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 17/07/2026

Rayalaseema University (RUK), Kurnool, has released a fresh set of LLB Regular and Revaluation Results on 16-07-2026. The results cover both the LLB 3 Years and LLB 5 Years programmes across multiple semesters (I, III, V, VII and IX), for Regular, Supplementary and Revaluation candidates from the February 2026 and March 2026 examination sessions. Students can check and download their results directly from the official Rayalaseema University results portal.

How to Check Rayalaseema University LLB Revaluation Result 2026

  • Visit the official Rayalaseema University results page at ruk.ac.in/results.
  • Locate and click the relevant link for your course, semester, and exam session from the list above.
  • Enter your Hall Ticket Number / Registration Number as requested on the result page.
  • Submit the details to view your revaluation result on screen.
  • Download or take a printout of the result for your records.
  • Cross-check your name, hall ticket number, and marks carefully for any discrepancy.

Note: The exact login fields (such as whether a date of birth or CAPTCHA is also required) can vary by result link. If a field is unclear on the page, follow the on-screen instructions provided by the university's result portal.

The official result links do not specify any additional documents required at the result-checking stage. If a document is required beyond the hall ticket number, it will be indicated on the respective result page itself.

Important Instructions
