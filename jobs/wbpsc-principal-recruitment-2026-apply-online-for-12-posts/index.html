Short Information

West Bengal Public Service Commission (WBPSC) has released the official Principal Notification 2026 on 30-04-2026 at the official website wbpsc.gov.in . A total of 12 vacancies have been announced under the WBPSC Principal Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged between 21 to 42 years with a qualification of Master’s Degree in Education, M.Ed., or M.A. in Education, along with 5 years of teaching experience, can apply online from 30-04-2026 to 03-07-2026. Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the WBPSC Principal recruitment?

The last date for submission of online applications is 03 July 2026. It is advisable to apply well before the deadline to avoid any last-minute technical issues.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Principal post?

Candidates must have a Master’s Degree with Honours at Graduation level (55% marks), M.Ed. or M.A. in Education with a Diploma/Degree in Primary/Elementary Education (55% marks), and five years of teaching experience in a relevant institute.

Q3. What is the maximum age limit for the Principal position?

The maximum age limit for the Principal post is 42 years as of 01 January 2026. Age relaxations are applicable for reserved categories.

Q4. How many vacancies are available for the Principal post?

There are a total of 12 vacancies for the post of Principal in the District Institute of Education and Training.

Q5. What is the application fee for the WBPSC Principal recruitment?

The application fee for General/OBC candidates is Rs. 210/- plus service charges. SC/ST candidates from West Bengal and PwBD candidates (40% and above) are exempted from the fee.

Q6. What is the salary structure for the Principal post?

The selected candidates will receive a salary in the pay scale of Rs. 67,300 to Rs. 1,73,200 per month.

Q7. What is the selection process for this recruitment?

The selection process will involve a Screening Test (MCQ type) for shortlisting, followed by a merit-based Interview.

Q8. Can candidates from outside West Bengal apply for the SC/ST fee exemption?

No, the fee exemption for SC/ST categories is applicable only for candidates belonging to West Bengal.
