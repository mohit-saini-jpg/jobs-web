Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is CNP Nashik SPMCIL Recruitment 2026 for 534 posts?

CNP Nashik SPMCIL Recruitment 2026 is a centralized recruitment drive for 534 posts of Welfare Officer, Supervisors, Secretarial/Office staff and Junior Technicians in Currency Note Press Nashik and other SPMCIL units through an online application process.

Q2. How many vacancies are there and which posts are included in CNP Nashik Recruitment 2026?

There are 534 vacancies in total, including 2 Welfare Officers, 1 Safety Officer, multiple Supervisor posts in various technical streams, 1 Secretarial Assistant, 1 Artist (Graphic Design), 22 Junior Office Assistants and a large number of Junior Technicians in trades like Printing, Control, Electrical, Electronics, Fitter, Machinist and Ink Factory.

Q3. What are the online application dates for CNP Nashik SPMCIL Recruitment 2026?

The online application window will open on 20 April 2026 and remain active up to 3 June 2026, and the fee payment period is also from 20 April 2026 to 3 June 2026

Q4. What is the age limit for CNP Nashik SPMCIL Junior Technician, Supervisor and other posts?

Candidates must be at least 18 years old, and the upper age limit ranges between 25, 28 and 30 years depending on the post as on the closing date, with relaxations for reserved categories as per government rules.

Q5. What are the educational qualifications required for CNP Nashik SPMCIL Recruitment 2026?

Graduation is required for Secretarial Assistant, Artist and Junior Office Assistant; Welfare Officer needs a degree in Social Work or related field; Supervisor posts require Diploma in relevant Engineering/Printing/IT or Master’s in Hindi (Official Language), while Junior Technicians must have ITI in the respective trade such as Printing, Electrical, Electronics, Fitter, Machinist, Air Conditioning or Ink Factory trades.

Q6. What is the salary or pay scale for CNP Nashik SPMCIL posts?

Approximate pay scales are Rs 25,740–1,03,000 for Welfare Officer, Rs 27,600–95,910 for Supervisors, Rs 23,910–85,570 for Supervisor (Official Language), Secretarial Assistant and Artist, Rs 21,340–77,160 for Junior Office Assistant and Rs 18,780–67,390 for Junior Technician posts.

The short notification states that the application fee for General, OBC and EWS candidates, and the fee structure for SC, ST, PwBD, Ex‑Servicemen and Female candidates will be mentioned in the detailed advertisement, and that all payments must be made online.

Q8. What is the selection process for CNP Nashik SPMCIL Junior Technician & Supervisor posts?

As per the short advertisement, the detailed selection process (likely including online written test and trade/skill test where applicable) will be provided in the detailed notification on the official website; candidates must refer to that for exam pattern, syllabus and qualifying criteria.
