Applications are invited from eligible Indian Nationals as per UGC Regulations 2018 (and subsequent amendments) for various teaching posts. How To Apply

  • Candidates must follow the two-step application process (Online + Hard Copy Submission) carefully. Incomplete applications or applications received after the due date will be summarily rejected.
  • Register and Fill Online Application Form Visit the official recruitment portal at https://himsrecruitment.odisha.gov.in and register using a valid email ID and mobile number. Fill all the required personal, educational, experience, and research details accurately in the online application form. Candidates must ensure that all information provided matches their original documents.
  • Upload Scanned Documents Upload clear scanned copies of all essential documents including: Matriculation/SSC certificate (proof of date of birth) Master’s Degree marksheets & certificate Ph.D. Degree & certificates NET/SET/SLET certificate (if applicable) Experience certificates Caste / SEBC / PwD certificate (if claiming reservation) Research publications, API / Research Score details (for Professor & Associate Professor) Recent passport-size photograph and signature. All documents must be in the prescribed format and size as mentioned on the portal.

  • Prepare and Send Hard Copy of Application Take the printout of the duly filled online application form and attach all self-attested supporting documents in proper serial order. Send the complete set through Speed Post / Registered Post only to: The Registrar, Fakir Mohan University, Vyasa Vihar, Balasore – 756089, Odisha The envelope must be superscribed with “APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF ________________ (Subject & Post)” along with Advertisement Number.
  • Last Dates & Important Instructions Last date for submission of Online Application: 7th June 2026 Last date for receipt of Hard Copy with documents: 17th June 2026 (up to 05:00 PM) In-service candidates must route their application through proper channel or submit NOC from their present employer. Candidates are advised to keep a copy of the application and payment receipt for their records. The University will not entertain any request for change of details after final submission.

  • Only online applications followed by hard copy submission will be accepted. Separate applications are required for each post if applying in multiple departments.
