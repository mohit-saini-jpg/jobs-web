How To Apply

  • Candidates must follow the two-step application process (Online + Hard Copy Submission) carefully. Incomplete applications or applications received after the due date will be summarily rejected.

  • Register and Fill Online Application Form Visit the official recruitment portal at https://himsrecruitment.odisha.gov.in and register using a valid email ID and mobile number. Fill all the required personal, educational, experience, and research details accurately in the online application form. Candidates must ensure that all information provided matches their original documents.

  • Upload Scanned Documents Upload clear scanned copies of all essential documents including: Matriculation/SSC certificate (proof of date of birth) Master’s Degree marksheets & certificate Ph.D. Degree & certificates NET/SET/SLET certificate (if applicable) Experience certificates Caste / SEBC / PwD certificate (if claiming reservation) Research publications, API / Research Score details (for Professor & Associate Professor) Recent passport-size photograph and signature. All documents must be in the prescribed format and size as mentioned on the portal.

  • Matriculation/SSC certificate (proof of date of birth)

Important Instructions

  • Only online applications followed by hard copy submission will be accepted. Separate applications are required for each post if applying in multiple departments. In-service candidates must apply through proper channel or submit NOC. Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification. The University reserves the right to cancel or modify the recruitment process. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the interview. Jurisdiction for any dispute shall be High Court of Odisha.
