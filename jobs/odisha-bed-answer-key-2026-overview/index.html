How to Download Odisha BEd Answer Key 2026

Step-by-step instructions:

  • Visit the official SAMS Odisha website: te.samsodisha.gov.in
  • Navigate to "Notifications" or "BEd Entrance Exam 2026" section
  • Click on "Odisha BEd Answer Key 2026" link
  • Log in using your Application Number and Password/Date of Birth
  • Download and save the PDF for future reference
  • Take a printout if needed for objection filing

How to Raise Objection Against Odisha BEd Answer Key 2026

  • Login to the official portal using your credentials
  • Go to "Challenge Answer Key" section
  • Select the question(s) you want to challenge
  • Pay ₹200 per question as challenge fee
  • Upload supporting documents (if required)
  • Submit before June 7, 2026, 11:45 PM

Important Note: Objection fee is non-refundable even if the challenge is accepted.
