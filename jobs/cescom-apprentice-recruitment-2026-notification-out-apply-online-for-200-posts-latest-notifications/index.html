Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for CESCOM Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

The last date to apply online for CESCOM Apprentice Recruitment 2026 through the NATS Portal is 29 May 2026. Candidates belonging to Karnataka State who meet the eligibility conditions should complete their registration and application process before the deadline. Applications submitted after the closing date will not be accepted.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in CESCOM Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

Chamundeshwari Electricity Supply Corporation Limited (CESC), Mysore has announced a total of 200 apprenticeship positions for the training year 2026-27. These vacancies are distributed among Engineering Graduate Apprentices, Diploma Apprentices, and Non-Engineering Graduate Apprentices. Candidates from multiple educational backgrounds can apply for the available posts.

Q3. What qualifications are required for CESCOM Apprentice Training 2026?

Candidates applying for Graduate Apprentice posts must possess BE/B.Tech degrees in relevant branches, while Diploma Apprentice applicants should hold a 3-year Diploma from a recognized polytechnic. Non-Engineering categories require qualifications such as B.A, B.Sc, B.Com, BBA, or BCA from recognized universities. Only candidates who passed in 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025, or 2026 are eligible.

Q4. What stipend is offered under CESCOM Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

Graduate and Non-Engineering Apprentices selected under CESCOM Recruitment 2026 will receive a monthly stipend of Rs. 12,300/-. Diploma Apprentices will receive Rs. 10,900/- per month during the one-year training period. The stipend will be paid according to Apprenticeship Act rules and applicable guidelines.

Q5. Who is eligible to apply for CESCOM Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

Only candidates belonging to Karnataka State are eligible to apply for CESCOM Apprentice Training 2026. Applicants must be at least 18 years old and should possess knowledge of reading and writing Kannada language. Candidates who already completed apprenticeship training or have one year of work experience are not eligible for this recruitment.

Q6. What is the selection process for CESCOM Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

The Board of Apprenticeship Training (Southern Region) will shortlist candidates based on marks obtained in the qualifying examination. Shortlisted applicants will be informed through registered email IDs and called for document verification at the CESC Corporate Office, Mysuru. Final selection will be subject to successful verification of original documents.

Q7. How can candidates apply for CESCOM Apprentice Training 2026?

Candidates must first register on the National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS) Portal and complete their profile details. After registration, applicants should search for “Chamundeshwari Electricity Supply Corporation Limited” and apply for the apprenticeship vacancies. New users must generate a valid 12-digit Enrollment Number before submitting the application.

Q8. When will CESCOM Apprentice document verification be conducted?

Document verification for shortlisted candidates will be conducted at the CESC Corporate Office, Mysuru from 15 June 2026 to 19 June 2026. Candidates must appear in person between 11:00 AM and 4:00 PM along with all original educational, identity, and category-related documents for verification purposes.
