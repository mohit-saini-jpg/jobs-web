How to Apply

  • 1Bio-data in prescribed proforma (Annexure-II) - duly filled and countersigned by the competent authority (available on NIA website)
  • 2Photocopies of APAR dossier from 2020-21 to 2024-25 - duly attested on each page with rubber stamp by an officer not below the rank of Under Secretary to the Govt. of India
  • 3Vigilance Clearance Certificate and Integrity Certificate issued by the respective department
  • 4Details of major/minor penalties imposed on the official during the last 10 years
  • 5Supporting documents for claimed education
  • 6Details of experience properly filled in bio-data forms
  • 7This recruitment is exclusively on Deputation (ISTC) basis - only serving officers of Central/State Govt. / Universities / Recognized Research Institutions / PSUs / Semi-Govt. / Statutory / Autonomous Organisations are eligible.
  • 8The maximum age limit for deputation (ISTC) appointment is 56 years as on the closing date of receipt of applications.
  • 9Deputation period shall ordinarily not exceed 3 years (including any prior ISTC deputation period).
  • 10All posts can be posted anywhere in India as per administrative requirements of NIA - choice of posting will be considered in case of vacancy.
  • 11All nominees will receive Special Security Allowance @ 20% of basic pay in addition to DA, HRA, TPT and other allowances as admissible under Central Government orders.
  • 12Applications received after the closing date or not accompanied by required documents will not be considered.
  • 13Candidates who apply will not be allowed to withdraw their candidature subsequently.
  • 14APAR dossiers must be attested on each page with rubber stamp by an officer not below the rank of Under Secretary to the Govt. of India.
  • 15The nomination circular is to be circulated by all addressee Ministries/Departments/Organizations among all Departments/Institutions/Offices under their charge and hosted on their websites.
  • 16For deputation purpose, service rendered prior to 1.1.2006 or date of 6th CPC pay structure revision shall be deemed as service in the corresponding grade pay as per pay commission recommendations.
  • 17Eligibility criteria, application form, and Recruitment Rules are available at www.nia.gov.in/recruitment-rules.htm
