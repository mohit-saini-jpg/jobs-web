FAQs

Q1What is NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026 is a nomination‑based recruitment by the National Investigation Agency to fill 30 Scientific posts on deputation (ISTC) basis across India. It includes Technical Forensic Psychologist, Finger Print Expert, Explosive Expert, Cyber Forensic Examiner and Crime Scene Assistant posts.
Q2How many vacancies are there in NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 30 vacancies notified under NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026, subject to variation depending on actual requirements. These 30 posts are distributed among Technical Forensic Psychologist, Finger Print Expert, Explosive Expert, Cyber Forensic Examiner and Crime Scene Assistant.
Q3What is the last date to apply for NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to apply for NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026 is 60 days from the date of publication in Employment News, which is 13 June 2026 as per the NIA recruitment notice. Candidates should ensure their complete nomination reaches NIA HQ before this closing date.
Q4Who is eligible to apply for NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
Only serving officers from Central/State Governments, Union Territories, Universities, Recognized Research Institutions, PSUs, Semi‑Government, Statutory or Autonomous Organisations can apply for NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026 on deputation (ISTC) basis. They must also satisfy the required pay‑scale and experience conditions mentioned in the official notification.
Q5What is the age limit for NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
The maximum age limit for NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026 is 56 years as on the closing date of receipt of applications. The deputation period, including any previous ISTC deputation, ordinarily shall not exceed three years.
Q6Is there any application fee for NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
No, there is no application fee for NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026. This recruitment is purely on deputation basis, so eligible officers are nominated through proper channel without payment of any fee.
Q7How can I apply for NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
To apply for NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026, eligible officers must submit their bio‑data in the prescribed Annexure‑II proforma through proper channel to SP (Admin), NIA HQ, Lodhi Road, New Delhi, within 60 days. An advance copy of the application can also be sent by email to the specified NIA email ID before the closing date.
Q8What documents are required with the NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026 application?
A
Candidates must send the duly filled bio‑data (Annexure‑II), attested APAR dossiers for the last five years, Vigilance Clearance and Integrity Certificates, details of penalties in the last ten years, and copies of educational and experience certificates. All documents should be verified and forwarded by the competent authority of the parent department.
Q9What is the selection process for NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection for NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026 is done through scrutiny of nominations received on deputation basis, followed by evaluation by the NIA Selection Committee. There is no direct online application or open competitive exam; only eligible, properly forwarded nominations are considered.
Q10Where can I download the NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026 notification PDF?
A
You can download the official NIA Scientific Posts Deputation Recruitment 2026 notification PDF from the Recruitment Notices section of the NIA website, under the entry titled “Inviting nomination to fill up Scientific posts in NIA on deputation (ISTC) basis” with opening date 13/04/2026. The “View” link on this listing provides the full notification and application proforma.
