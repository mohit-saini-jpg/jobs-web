SRFTI Recruitment 2026 Application Fee

Application fee is Rs. 1,200/- for each post applied for. Fee is to be paid through SBI Collect under the category "Application Fee (Recruitment)". A copy of the payment receipt must be uploaded while submitting the online application form. SC, ST, PWD, and female applicants are exempted from payment of application fees, as per extant GOI practice. Candidates applying for more than one post must pay the fee separately for each post. Open the link: https://www.onlinesbi.sbi/sbicollect Select category as "Educational Institutions". Select the institution from the list (search for "Satyajit Ray Film and TV Inst"). In the payment category, select "Application Fees (Recruitment)". Fill in the payment details and proceed with payment. Once payment is done, upload the payment confirmation receipt in the application form. Save a digital copy of the payment receipt for future reference.

SRFTI Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

Depending on the number of applications received, eligible shortlisted candidates will be called for a trade test and/or interview, as applicable to the post. Since it is not possible to call all eligible candidates, shortlisting will be done based on criteria fixed by the Institute at the time of scrutiny. The decision of the Institute regarding shortlisting and selection is final and binding. The notification does not specify separate stages (such as a written exam) beyond trade test and/or interview.

SRFTI Recruitment 2026: Required Documents

Printout of the submitted online application form Copy of the payment receipt/confirmation for the application fee (except for SC/ST/PWD/female applicants) Copies of all requisite/supporting documents (degree, diploma, certificates, experience proof) For several posts, candidates are specifically required to produce evidence of professional work done (e.g., Associate Professor and several Assistant Professor posts); for the Animation Cinema Professor post, candidates must also produce evidence of teaching, case studies, and a portfolio of independent creative samples. Supporting document for experience is required, since experience is counted only after the qualifying degree/diploma/certificate.

SRFTI Recruitment 2026: General Terms & Conditions

All engagements will initially be for one year, which may be extended further for a maximum of one additional year, subject to satisfactory performance review. The contract is liable to be terminated by either side by giving one month's notice, without assigning any reason. The contractual engagement does not confer any right to regularization or absorption against any post at FTII, Itanagar or SRFTI, Kolkata. The selected candidate will be required to join on an immediate basis. Selected candidates who wish to avail a staff quarter will be charged rent as per their post/category; allotment of staff quarter is subject to availability. The crucial date for reckoning the age limit is the last date fixed for receipt of application, i.e., 03.08.2026. Experience for each post is counted only after the candidate's qualifying degree/diploma/certificate; a supporting document is required. SRFTI
