How to Check PSEB Class 12 Result 2026

  • Visit the official website of Punjab School Education Board - pseb.ac.in.
  • Click on the Results section available on the homepage.
  • Open the PSEB Class 12 Result 2026 link.
  • Enter your roll number and other required login credentials.
  • Click on the Submit button.
  • Your PSEB Class 12 Result 2026 will appear on the screen.
