Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have passed Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, securing at least 45% aggregate (40% for reserved categories). Minimum age is 17 years by December 31, 2026—no upper limit for WBJEE.

West Bengal domicile offers reservation benefits, but all-India candidates can apply. Full details appear in the upcoming WBJEE 2026 bulletin on the official site.​

Preparation Tips

Focus on NCERT Class 11-12 for Physics (Laws of Motion, Optics), Chemistry (Organic Reactions), and Mathematics (Calculus, Vectors). Practice with past papers emphasizing speed for OMR format.

WBJEE features Paper 1 (Maths: 100 marks, 2 hours) and Paper 2 (Physics+Chemistry: 100 marks, 2 hours), with negative marking on select questions. Create a study plan matching the May exam date.
