Salary / Pay Scale

Pay ScaleLump Sum per inquiry: Rs. 30,000/- to Rs. 50,000/-; Secretarial assistance: Rs. 5,000/- to Rs. 10,000/-; Transport allowance: Rs. 1000/- to Rs. 1750/-.
DetailsLump Sum per inquiry: Rs. 30,000/- (where number of witnesses up to 5), Rs.40000/- (where number of witnesses in the range of 6 – 10), Rs. 50000/- (where number of witnesses more than 10). Secretarial assistance per inquiry: Rs. 5,000/- (where number of witnesses up to 5), Rs. 7500/- (where number of witnesses in the range of 6 – 10), Rs. 10000/- (where number of witnesses more than 10). Transport Allowance per sitting: For those residing up to 100 kms Rs.1000/-, For those residing beyond 100kms and up to 200 kms – Rs.1500, For those residing beyond 200 kms Rs.1750/-. Common/Joint Inquiries: 50% additional amount on the Lumpsum amount (ie Rs.45000 / Rs.60000 / Rs. 75000, as the case may be) shall be paid, where the number of Charged Officers/Chargesheeted employees involved is more than two.
