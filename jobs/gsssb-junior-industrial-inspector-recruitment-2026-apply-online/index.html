GSSSB Junior Industrial Inspector Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Gujarat Gauna Seva Pasandgi Mandal (GSSSB), Gandhinagar has released Advertisement No. 442/202627 to fill 3 vacancies for the post of Junior Industry Inspector, Class-3 (Junior Udyog Nirikshak, Varg-3) under the office of the Industries Commissioner, Industries and Mines Department, Government of Gujarat. This recruitment is being conducted exclusively under the Special Recruitment Drive for Divyang (Persons with Disabilities) candidates to fill the backlog of PwD reserved vacancies. Eligible candidates can apply online through ojas.gujarat.gov.in from 20 July 2026 to 3 August 2026.

GSSSB Junior Industrial Inspector Salary 2026

Selected candidates will be appointed on a fixed pay of ₹40,800 per month for the first five years, as per the Finance Department resolutions (last revised 18 October 2023). GSSSB Junior Industrial Inspector Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection will be made through a single-stage Computer Based Response Test (CBRT) / OMR-based objective (MCQ) written examination, followed by document verification. Combined duration for Part A and Part B: 3 hours (180 minutes). Negative marking: 1/4th of the marks allotted to a question is deducted for every wrong answer. Qualifying standard: Candidates must score at least 40% in Part A AND at least 40% in Part B separately (independent qualifying standards); this minimum cannot be relaxed for any category. After the provisional result, roughly 2 times the number of vacancies (category/disability-group wise) will be called for document verification. The final selection list and waiting list will be prepared disability-group wise (A, B, C, D & E) based on merit.   • 7Read the "Assurance" (declaration) section, select "Yes", and click "Save" to complete the application.

  • 10Go to "Confirm Application" under the Online Application tab, enter your Application Number and Date of Birth, review all details carefully, and edit if needed. Once confirmed, no changes can be made. Click "Confirm Application" to generate your Confirmation Number - save this for future reference.

GSSSB Junior Industrial Inspector Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is GSSSB Junior Industrial Inspector Recruitment 2026?
A
It is a Special Recruitment Drive by the Gujarat Gauna Seva Pasandgi Mandal to fill 3 backlog vacancies of Junior Industry Inspector, Class-3, reserved exclusively for Divyang (PwD) candidates, under Advertisement No. 442/202627.
Q2Who is eligible to apply for this recruitment?
A
Only Divyang candidates belonging to disability Group B (Deaf/Hard of Hearing), Group C (locomotor disabilities), or Group D & E (autism, intellectual disability, SLD, mental illness, multiple disabilities) are eligible, since these are the only groups with vacancies in this drive.
Q3What is the last date to apply online?
A
The online application window is open from 20 July 2026 (5:00 PM) to 3 August 2026 (11:59 PM). The exam fee must be paid by 6 August 2026 (11:59 PM).
Q4How many total vacancies are there?
A
There are 3 total vacancies: 1 for Group B, 1 for Group C, and 1 for Group D & E disability categories. There are no vacancies in Group A (Blindness/Low Vision) in this advertisement.
Q5What is the age limit for GSSSB Junior Industrial Inspector 2026?
A
The base age limit is 18 to 35 years as on 3 August 2026, with age relaxation of 10 to 20 years for Divyang candidates depending on gender and category, subject to a maximum age of 45 years.
