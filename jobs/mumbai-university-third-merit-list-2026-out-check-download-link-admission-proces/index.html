How to Download Mumbai University 3rd Merit List 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download the third merit list:

  • Visit the official admission portal: https://muugadmission.samarth.edu.in/

  • Navigate to the "Merit List" section

Step-by-Step Mumbai University Admission Process

The University of Mumbai follows an 11-step admission procedure:

  • Registration: Student registers and logs into the website
  • Profile Completion: Fill in personal and academic details
  • Application Submission: Upload necessary documents and submit the form
  • Eligibility Verification: University team verifies minimum 24 working days post-document submission
  • Eligibility Letter: Eligible students receive the letter with registration fee payment details ($1,150 for international students)
  • Fee Payment: Pay eligible fee and submit payment proof
  • Provisional Admission Letter: Received upon fee verification
  • College Allotment: Takes minimum 24 working days after payment
  • Final Admission Letter: Used for visa application (international students)
  • Tuition Fee Information: Full fee cost provided; first-year fee must be paid within 7 days of arrival in India
  • Reporting to College: Mandatory reporting to allotted college and course; failure results in automatic cancellation with no refund

Document Verification & Fee Payment Schedule

For the third merit list round, document verification and fee payment will be conducted online from June 5 to 9, 2026 .

Required documents typically include:

  • Class 12 mark sheet and certificate
  • Transfer certificate
  • Migration certificate
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Photo ID proof
  • Passport-sized photographs

Eligibility Criteria for Mumbai University UG Admissions

Students must check eligibility before applying:

  • For BA programmes: Complete Class 12 in any subject
  • For BCom/BSc programmes: Class 12 with relevant subject background
  • For Management courses (BMS/BAF/BBI): Class 12 with Commerce/Mathematics preferred
  • Admissions are primarily merit-based, depending on Class 12 marks and course-specific criteria

Non-English speaking country students must pass the English Proficiency Test unless exempted (students from English-speaking countries) .

What to Do After Checking the Third Merit List

If your name appears in the third merit list:

  • Immediately verify documents online between June 5-9, 2026
  • Pay the admission fee within the specified deadline
  • Download your Final Admission Letter after payment confirmation
  • Report to your allotted college before classes start on June 13, 2026
  • Complete college registration formalities and submit original documents

Important: No refund of fees paid to Mumbai University is possible under any circumstances if you fail to report .
