The State Board of Technical Education and Training (SBTET), Telangana has officially released the TG POLYCET Result 2026 for candidates who appeared in the Polytechnic Common Entrance Test. The results include rank cards for admission into diploma courses across engineering and non-engineering streams for the academic session 2026.

TG POLYCET Result 2026 has been announced online on the official website - https://tgpolycet.nic.in/default.aspx. Candidates who appeared for the entrance exam can now check and download their rank card using their hall ticket number through the official portal.

Students can access their results by entering their login credentials such as hall ticket number. It is advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the rank card and immediately contact the concerned authorities in case of any discrepancies.

Steps to Check TG POLYCET Result 2026

  • Visit the official website - https://tgpolycet.nic.in/default.aspx
  • Click on the link "TG POLYCET Result 2026"
  • Enter your Hall Ticket Number in the login field
  • Click on the submit button
  • Your TG POLYCET Rank Card 2026 will be displayed on the screen
  • Download and take a printout for future reference
