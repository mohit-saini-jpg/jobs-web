Courses Offered in IGNOU July 2026 Admission

IGNOU offers a wide range of programmes including Bachelor’s Degrees (BA, B.Com, B.Sc, BCA, BSW etc.), Master’s Degrees (MA, M.Com, M.Sc, MCA, MBA etc.), Diplomas, PG Diplomas, and Certificate courses in Arts, Science, Commerce, Management, Education, Social Work, Tourism, Journalism, and many other disciplines. Candidates can check the complete list of programmes on the official admission portal.

Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility varies as per programme. Generally, for Bachelor’s programmes, candidates must have passed 10+2 or equivalent. For Master’s programmes, a Bachelor’s degree is required. Specific eligibility criteria for each programme is available in the Common Prospectus and on the admission portal.

Application Fee

Application fee is programme-specific and payable online. Candidates are advised to check the exact fee while filling the application form on the portal.

How to Apply for IGNOU July Admissions 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official admission portal: https://ignouadmission.samarth.edu.in/
  • Click on “New Registration” and register with valid details.
  • Login and fill the online application form.
  • Upload required documents and APAAR ID.
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Submit the form and download the confirmation page.
