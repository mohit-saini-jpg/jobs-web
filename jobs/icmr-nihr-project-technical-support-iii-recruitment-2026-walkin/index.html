FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for ICMR NIHR Recruitment 2026?
A
The walk-in interview for the Project Technical Support-III posts is scheduled for 08 July 2026. Candidates should report to the venue on this date.
Q2What is the total number of vacancies announced by ICMR NIHR?
A
ICMR NIHR has announced a total of 2 vacancies for the post of Project Technical Support-III through this recruitment drive.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for the Project Technical Support-III position?
A
Candidates should possess a Graduate degree in Biotechnology/Biochemistry/Microbiology/Biomedical Sciences/Genetics with 3 years of experience, or a Post Graduate Degree in the same fields.
Q4What is the age limit for the Project Technical Support-III post?
A
The maximum age limit for candidates applying for this post is 35 years. Age relaxation is applicable as per government norms.
Q5What is the salary offered for the Project Technical Support-III role?
A
The selected candidates will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 28,000/- per month, plus HRA.
Q6Is there any application fee for ICMR NIHR Recruitment 2026?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment.
Q7What is the mode of application for this recruitment?
A
The mode of application is Walk-in. Candidates need to attend the interview directly at the specified venue and time.
Q8Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?
A
The walk-in interview will be conducted in Jodhpur, Rajasthan. The exact venue details will be available in the official notification.
Q9What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents, one set of self-attested photocopies, ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates, and category certificates (if applicable).
Q10What is the selection process for the Project Technical Support-III posts?
A
The selection process involves a Walk-in Interview/Written Test. A written test may be conducted if more than 30 candidates appear, followed by an interview for shortlisted candidates.
