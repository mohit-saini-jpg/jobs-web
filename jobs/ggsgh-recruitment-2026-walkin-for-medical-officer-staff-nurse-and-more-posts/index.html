Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for GGSGH recruitment?

The walk-in interview for the GGSGH recruitment is scheduled to be held on 02 June 2026. Candidates are advised to reach the venue on this date with all necessary documents.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in the GGSGH recruitment?

There are a total of 4 vacancies announced for the posts of Medical Officer, Counsellor, Staff Nurse, and Data Manager in the Guru Gobind Singh Government Hospital.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Medical Officer post?

For the Medical Officer post, candidates must possess an MBBS degree from a recognized institution along with medical council registration. A preference will be given to those with an MD or equivalent qualification in Psychiatry.

Q4. Is there any application fee for the GGSGH recruitment?

No, there is no application fee required for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this GGSGH recruitment. Candidates can apply without paying any fee.

Q5. What is the salary structure for the Staff Nurse position?

The selected candidate for the Staff Nurse position will receive a monthly salary of Rs. 30,240/-. This is a contractual position.

Q6. What is the selection process for these posts?

The selection process for the GGSGH recruitment involves a Walk-in Interview followed by Document Verification. Candidates need to perform well in both stages to be considered for the positions.
