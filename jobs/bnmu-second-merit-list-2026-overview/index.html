BNMU Second Merit List 2026 - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 04/07/2026

The BNMU second merit list 2026 for UG admission (session 2026-30) has not been officially released yet, and Bhupendra Narayan Mandal University (BNMU) has announced that it will be published within the next few days. Candidates who applied for BA, BSc, and BCom Semester 1 admission and did not find their name in the first merit list can wait for this update, which will decide college and subject allotment for the next round of seats.

This article covers everything currently known about the BNMU second merit list 2026, including the official status, expected timeline, how to check it once released, and what to do next. Wherever information has not been officially confirmed by the university, it is clearly marked as "Not Yet Announced."

Is the BNMU Second Merit List 2026 Released?

No. As per the latest official update, the second merit list of UG 2026-30 will be released in the next few days. Candidates are advised to keep checking the official BNMU website and admission portal regularly instead of relying on unofficial sources, since the exact release date has not been confirmed yet.

When Was the First Merit List Declared?

BNMU released the first merit list for UG admission 2026-30 on 13 June 2026. Candidates selected in that list were required to complete document verification and fee payment at their allotted college between 13 June and 18 June 2026.

BNMU Second Merit List 2026: Important Dates

  • Online Application Started: 22 May 2026
  • Last Date to Apply Online: 5 June 2026
  • First Merit List Released: 13 June 2026
  • First Merit List Admission Window: 13 June to 18 June 2026
  • Second Merit List Release Date: Not Yet Announced (expected in the next few days)
  • Second Merit List Admission Window: Not Yet Announced

How to Check the BNMU Second Merit List 2026

Once released, candidates will be able to check the BNMU second merit list 2026 by following these steps:

  • Visit the official BNMU admission portal at admissions.bnmuumis.in or the university website bnmu.ac.in.
  • Click on the "View Merit List" or "UG Merit List 2026-30" link on the home page.
  • Select the academic session, degree type, degree, and subject from the drop-down menus.
  • Click the Search button to view the second merit list for your chosen course.
  • Download the PDF and use Ctrl+F (or the "Find in Page" option on mobile) to search your name or application number.
  • Check your allotted college, category, and marks carefully against your entry.

Details Mentioned on the BNMU Second Merit List

Based on the pattern followed in the first merit list, the second merit list is expected to contain the following details for each candidate:

  • Candidate's Name
  • Application/Registration Number
  • Category (General/OBC/SC/ST/EWS)
  • Class 12 Marks/Percentage
  • Allotted College Name
  • Allotted Subject/Course

BNMU Second Merit List Cut Off Marks 2026

Cut off marks for the second merit list have not been officially released. Since BNMU admission works on a rolling merit basis, the cut off for the second list is generally expected to be lower than the first list cut off, as it fills remaining vacant seats after the first round. Candidates should refer to the official PDF once released for exact subject-wise and category-wise cut off figures.

What to Do After the BNMU Second Merit List is Released

If your name appears in the BNMU second merit list 2026, follow these steps promptly to secure your seat:

  • Download and save the merit list PDF for your course and subject.
  • Note your allotted college and report there within the admission window once announced.
  • Carry all original documents along with self-attested photocopies for verification.
  • Pay the admission fee online or as instructed by the allotted college.
  • Collect the admission confirmation receipt and keep it safe for future reference.

If your name does not appear in the second merit list, you may need to wait for a subsequent (third) merit list or a spot admission round, depending on seat availability.

Documents Required for BNMU UG Admission

  • Class 10 Marksheet and Certificate
  • Class 12 Marksheet and Certificate (Original and Photocopy)
  • School/College Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste Certificate (for SC/ST/OBC/EWS candidates, if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if applicable, for fee concession or scholarship)
  • Aadhaar Card (Original and Photocopy)
  • Recent Passport-Size Photographs
  • Fee Payment Receipt

BNMU UG Admission Selection Process 2026-30

BNMU follows a fully merit-based selection process for UG admission, with no entrance examination involved.

  • Candidates apply online during the application window.
  • The university prepares and releases the merit list based on Class 12 marks, category, and preferences.
  • College and subject are allotted according to merit rank.
  • Selected candidates undergo document verification at their allotted college.
  • Admission is confirmed after fee payment.
  • Additional merit lists (2nd, 3rd, and spot admission rounds) are released if seats remain vacant.

Important Instructions for Candidates

  • Keep checking bnmu.ac.in and admissions.bnmuumis.in regularly for the official second merit list update.
  • Do not rely on unofficial WhatsApp or social media messages for the release date.
  • Complete document verification and fee payment strictly within the deadline once announced, or the allotted seat may be forfeited.
  • Keep a printed copy of the merit list and admission receipt for future reference.
  • Contact the BNMU helpline for any discrepancy in your merit list entry.
