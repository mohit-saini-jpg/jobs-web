How to Download AKNU Exam Result 2026

Students can follow the step by step process given below to check Adikavi Nannaya University Result 2026 for Law, Pre-PhD and M.Tech semester examinations online.

  • Visit the official website of Adikavi Nannaya University at aknu.edu.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the Examination or Results section and then select the AKNU Results option.
  • You will be redirected to the official AKNU results portal page at aknu.edu.in/results.
  • On the result page, choose the relevant result link for Law Courses 5th Sem, Pre-PhD, M.Tech 1st Sem or M.Tech 3rd Sem as per your course and exam session.
  • Enter your registration number or hall ticket number in the given field and correctly fill the captcha code displayed on the screen.
  • Click on the submit or search button to view your AKNU Exam Result 2026 on the screen.
  • Check your subject wise marks, total marks and pass/fail status carefully, then download and save the provisional marksheet for future reference.
  • Cross check all details printed on the result, and if any discrepancy is found, contact the Adikavi Nannaya University examination branch or your college immediately for correction.
