Haryana ITI Admission 2026

Haryana ITI Admission 2026 for the 2026-27 session has started. Online applications are invited from 02 June 2026 to 15 June 2026 for admission in 197 Government and 180 Private Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) across Haryana.

The Department of Industrial Training, Haryana has announced the Haryana ITI Admission 2026 for various Engineering and Non-Engineering trades. This is a great opportunity for students who have passed 8th, 10th, or 12th to acquire technical skills and build a secure future. Admissions will be based on merit and counselling.

Haryana ITI Eligibility Criteria 2026

Candidates must have passed 8th, 10th, or 12th class from a recognized board. Minimum age should be 14 years as on 01 September 2025. Both male and female candidates can apply. Domicile of Haryana is preferred for Government ITI seats.

How to Apply for Haryana ITI Admission 2026

  • Visit the official website: https://www.admissions.itiharyana.gov.in/
  • Register using Family ID / Mobile Number / Email ID.
  • Fill the online application form with correct details.
  • Upload required documents and pay the application fee.
  • Submit the form and take a printout for future reference.

List of Trades Offered in Haryana ITI 2026-27

Haryana ITI offers 89+ Engineering and Non-Engineering trades. Some popular trades include:

  • Draughtsman (Civil & Mechanical)
  • Electrician
  • Fitter
  • Machinist
  • Mechanic (Motor Vehicle)
  • Refrigeration & Air Conditioning
  • Turner
  • Wireman
  • Computer Operator & Programming Assistant (COPA)
  • Many more trades

Documents Required for Haryana ITI Admission 2026

  • Family ID

  • Bank Account Details
