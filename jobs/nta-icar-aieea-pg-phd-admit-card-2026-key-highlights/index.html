NTA ICAR AIEEA PG Ph.D Admit Card 2026 Download Link

This section explains where and how candidates can access the official ICAR PG admit card 2026 link, which is the first thing most applicants look for once the release is announced.

The NTA activated the ICAR AIEEA PG admit card 2026 download link on July 1 at exams.nta.nic.in/ICAR/. As per standard NTA practice, the ICAR AIEEA PG admit card is typically released 3 to 4 days before the exam date. The hall ticket is available for download only in PDF format, and candidates must log in using their registered credentials to access it. Since the exam is scheduled for July 4, candidates are advised to download and print their admit card well in advance to avoid last-minute technical issues.

How to Download ICAR AIEEA PG Admit Card 2026: Step-by-Step Process

Candidates often ask how exactly to retrieve their hall ticket from the NTA portal. Below is the complete step-by-step process to download the ICAR AIEEA PG admit card 2026 from the official website.

  • Step 1: Visit the official NTA ICAR website exams.nta.nic.in/ICAR/
  • Step 2: Click on the “Download Admit Card: AIEEA (UG/PG) – 2026” link on the homepage
  • Step 3: Enter your login credentials (application number, date of birth, and security PIN) in the login window
  • Step 4: Click the “Login” button to proceed
  • Step 5: Your ICAR AIEEA PG admit card 2026 will appear on the screen
  • Step 6: Download the admit card and take a printout for exam day
