How to Check JNVST 9th Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their JNVST Class 9 Result 2026 from the official website:

  • Visit the official website of Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti at navodaya.gov.in
  • Navigate to the 'Results' or 'Admissions' section on the homepage
  • Look for the link 'JNVST Class 9 Result 2026' or 'LEST 2026 Result'
  • Click on the result link to open the login page
  • Alternatively, you can directly visit the result portal at cbseit.in/cbse/2026/nvsrst/NVS_Cls9.aspx
  • Enter your Roll Number in the first field
  • Enter your Date of Birth in DD/MM/YYYY format in the second field
  • Click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button
  • Your JNVST Class 9 Result 2026 will appear on the screen showing marks obtained and selection status
  • Check all the details carefully including name, roll number, marks, and whether you are selected
  • Download and save the scorecard PDF for future reference
  • Take a printout of the result for document verification and admission process
  • If selected, check the merit list to find your rank and prepare for document verification
