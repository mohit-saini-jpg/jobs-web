MGU II Semester Timetable 2026 - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 11/07/2026

Mahatma Gandhi University (MGU), Kottayam has released the official timetable for the II Semester CBCSS (2013 to 2016 Admissions Mercy Chance) examinations, along with the II Semester B.Sc. Cyber Forensic (2018 Admission Reappearance / 2014 to 2017 Admissions Mercy Chance) undergraduate examinations to be held in April 2026. The notification was issued on 10.07.2026 under reference number EA I/2/101/CBCSS.

This article gives you the complete, subject-wise exam timetable exactly as published in the official notification, including exam dates, timings, and programme-wise course details for Common Courses, Model I, Model II, and Other Programmes.

Who This Timetable Applies To

This schedule applies to two distinct candidate groups:

  • II Semester CBCSS students (2013 to 2016 Admissions) appearing under the Mercy Chance scheme.
  • II Semester B.Sc. Cyber Forensic students, specifically the 2018 Admission Reappearance batch and the 2014 to 2017 Admissions Mercy Chance batch.

Programmes covered include Model I, Model II, and other undergraduate programmes across the Faculty of Arts, Faculty of Science, and Faculty of Commerce, such as B.A. English, B.A. Economics, B.Sc. Botany, B.Sc. Chemistry, B.Sc. Physics, B.Sc. Mathematics, B.Sc. Zoology, B.A./B.Sc. Psychology, B.Com, B.C.A, B.Sc. Computer Applications, B.Sc. Electronics, B.Sc. IT, B.B.A, and B.B.M.
