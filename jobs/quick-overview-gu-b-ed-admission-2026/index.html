Eligibility Criteria for GUBEDCET 2026

Before applying, every candidate must check whether they satisfy the educational qualifications set by Gauhati University. Here is a clear breakdown of the eligibility conditions for GUBEDCET 2026.

Educational Qualification

Candidates must have passed one of the following degrees from a recognised Board or University:

  • BA / BSc / B.Com. (Undergraduate)
  • MA / MSc / M.Com. or equivalent (Postgraduate)

How to Apply Online for Gauhati University B.Ed Admission 2026

The entire GUBEDCET 2026 application process is conducted online through the official Samarth portal of Gauhati University. Follow these step-by-step instructions to submit your application without errors.

  • Visit the Official Portal: Go to https://gauhatiadmission.samarth.edu.in
  • New Registration: Click on "New User? Register Here" and complete the one-time registration with your email ID and mobile number.
  • Login: After registration, log in using your credentials at the GUBEDCET Login Page.
  • Fill Application Form: Enter all personal, academic, and category-related details carefully.
  • Upload Documents: Upload scanned copies of required documents (photograph, signature, mark sheets, category certificate if applicable).
  • Pay Application Fee: Complete the payment of ₹2,000 + transaction charges via the available online payment modes.
  • Submit & Print: Submit the form and save/print a copy of the completed application for future reference.
