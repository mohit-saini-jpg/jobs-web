How To Apply

  • Applicants must apply online only through the APSC recruitment website: https://apscrecruitment.in; no other mode of application will be accepted.
  • New users must first complete One Time Registration (OTR) by clicking on “Register Here” and providing basic details; use a valid and active email ID and mobile number.
  • After creating an account, log in with credentials and fill in all required registration and application details carefully; mandatory fields are marked with an asterisk (*).
  • Upload necessary documents: proof of Date of Birth, educational qualification mark sheets and certificates, caste/category/BPL/PwBD certificates, domicile proof (PRC/Employment Exchange), Small Family Norms declaration, etc. in PDF format (max 4 MB, 200 dpi, legible).
  • Upload recent passport-size Photograph and Signature in JPG/JPEG format (Photo: 50–200 KB, not older than 3 months; Signature: 50–200 KB).
  • Verify all details and documents using the ‘Preview’ option and tick the declaration checkbox to enable final submission.
  • Proceed to pay the applicable application fee/processing charges online; in case of payment failure or pending status, use the “Check Status” option to verify with bank/GRAS.
  • After successful submission and payment, an auto-generated confirmation email/SMS will be sent to the registered email ID/mobile number.
  • Take a print-out of the submitted online application form and keep it safely for future reference and interview/document verification.
  • Candidates may also use Common Service Centres (CSCs) for filling and submitting the form if they do not have debit card/internet banking facilities.
