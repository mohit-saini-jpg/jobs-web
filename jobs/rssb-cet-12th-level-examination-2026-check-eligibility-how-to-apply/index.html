RSSB CET 12th Level Examination 2026 How to Apply
  1. Visit the official website https://rssb.rajasthan.gov.in/
  2. Find and click the “Recruitment Link” or “Apply Online” link on the homepage.
  3. First, register – enter your name, email, mobile number, and password.
  4. After registering, log in and focus on the application form—specifically, the personal details, qualifications, and selection of the position.
  5. Upload required documents: Photograph (JPG, max 50KB), Signature (JPG, max 20KB), Educational Certificates, ID Proof, Caste Certificate (if applicable).
  6. Pay the application fee [Online – UPI / Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking].
  7. Preview all details before submitting. Correct any errors.
  8. After final submission, note down the confirmation page/application number and be sure to take a printout.
