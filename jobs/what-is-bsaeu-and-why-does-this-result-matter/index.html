What Is BSAEU and Why Does This Result Matter?

Baba Saheb Ambedkar Education University (BSAEU) is the apex regulatory body for teacher education in West Bengal. Understanding the university helps students know the significance of this result and what steps to take next.

BSAEU, formerly known as the West Bengal University of Teachers' Training, Education Planning and Administration (WBUTTEPA), was established in 2014 under an act of the West Bengal Legislature. Located on Ballygunge Circular Road, Kolkata, the university serves as an umbrella institution for all teacher education colleges across West Bengal. It regulates and affiliates all B.Ed, M.Ed, M.Phil, and Ph.D. programmes in Education across the state, ensuring alignment with standards set by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE).

The B.Ed 2nd Semester Result is a critical milestone for students in the 2-year B.Ed programme (Session 2024-2026), as it determines their progression toward the final semesters and their eligibility for teacher recruitment examinations.

How to Check BSAEU B.Ed 2nd Semester Result 2026 Online - Step-by-Step Guide

Checking your BSAEU B.Ed 2nd Semester Result online is a simple process. Follow these steps carefully to view and download your web result from the official portal.

Step 1: Visit the Official Result Portal

Open your web browser and go to the official BSAEU result portal: https://bed.bsaeu.net.in/web-result

Alternatively, you can visit the main university website at bsaeu.in, navigate to the "Notice" section on the main menu, and find the link for the B.Ed 2nd Semester web result.

Step 2: Select Your Programme and Semester

On the result page, select the relevant options:

  • Programme: B.Ed (Bachelor of Education)
  • Semester: 2nd Semester
  • Session: 2024-2026

Step 3: Enter Your Credentials

Enter your required login details as prompted on the portal. This typically includes:

  • Roll Number
  • Registration Number

Step 4: Submit and View Your Result

Click the "Submit" button after entering your credentials. Your BSAEU B.Ed 2nd Semester Result 2026 will appear on the screen, displaying your marks in all subjects along with your pass/fail status.

Step 5: Download and Print the Marksheet

Once your result is displayed, download the result page in PDF format and take a printout for future reference. The web result is provisional; students must collect their official grade card/marksheet from their respective colleges as per the university's schedule.

How to Check BSAEU Result via the Official Notice Board

BSAEU also publishes result notifications on its main notice board. Here is how to access results via the notice section, which is useful if the direct portal link is temporarily down.

  • Go to https://bsaeu.in

  • Click the link to be redirected to the result page

Details About the BSAEU B.Ed 2nd Semester Examination

Understanding the examination pattern helps students interpret their results correctly. Here are important details about the BSAEU B.Ed programme and its examination structure.

About the B.Ed Programme at BSAEU

The Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) programme under BSAEU is a 2-year full-time course spread across 4 semesters. The course is designed to train aspiring teachers for primary through higher secondary levels of education. Students enrolled in the 2024-2026 session appear in 2nd Semester examinations during the second half of their first academic year.

Minimum Passing Criteria

Students should note the following general passing criteria applicable under the BSAEU examination system:

  • Minimum Pass Marks: Students are generally required to secure 35% marks in each subject/course and 40% in aggregate to be declared pass (students should verify the exact criteria from their college or the official notification).
  • Students who do not meet the minimum marks in one or more subjects may apply for Supplementary (Back) Examinations as announced by the university.

Review/Re-evaluation Option

If a student is not satisfied with their result, BSAEU provides a review/re-evaluation facility. After the declaration of web results, the university typically issues a separate notification for review applications. Students should check the official website for the review notification and submit their applications within the stipulated deadline.

How to Collect the Official BSAEU Grade Card / Marksheet

The web result available on the portal is provisional. Students must follow the proper procedure to collect their official grade card.

After the web result is published, BSAEU separately issues a Grade Card Distribution Notice, specifying dates and procedures for colleges to distribute physical grade cards to students. To obtain your official grade card:

  • Wait for the Grade Card Distribution Notice on bsaeu.in
  • Your college will receive the grade cards from the university
  • Collect the grade card from your college as per the scheduled date
  • Keep a printout of the web result as a provisional document until the official grade card is issued

What to Do If You Failed in BSAEU B.Ed 2nd Semester?

If your result shows a fail status in one or more subjects, do not panic. BSAEU has a clear process for students who need to clear backlogs.

  • Supplementary Examination: BSAEU conducts Supplementary I and Supplementary II exams for students who fail in the regular examinations. Keep track of the official notification on bsaeu.in for the supplementary exam schedule and application process.
  • Review Application: If you believe your result is incorrect, apply for review within the deadline specified in the official review notification.
  • Internal Marks Verification: Ensure your internal/practical marks have been correctly submitted by your college. Contact your institution if you notice any discrepancy.
