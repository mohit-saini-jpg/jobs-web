Eligibility Criteria – Who Can Apply for SSC JSA LDC DoPT LDCE 2025

Selection Process – SSC JSA LDC DoPT LDCE 2025 Exam Pattern

The SSC JSA LDC LDCE 2025 selection process consists of a single-stage Computer Based Examination (CBE) of 180 marks and 3 hours duration. There is no interview or skill test at the selection stage. Final merit is determined solely on the basis of aggregate performance in the CBE, and the Commission prepares a Select List in order of merit up to the number of vacancies. SSC JSA LDC DoPT LDCE Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for SSC JSA LDC DoPT LDCE 2025?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 16 July 2026 at 11:00 PM (2300 hours). After applying online, the printed hard copy must reach SSC Northern Region through the candidate's Head of Department/Head of Office by 23 July 2026 at 6:00 PM (or 30 July 2026 for candidates abroad/Andaman & Nicobar/Lakshadweep).
Q2How many vacancies are there in SSC JSA LDC LDCE 2025 DoPT?
A
There are 49 tentative vacancies in the Central Secretariat Clerical Service (CSCS) Cadre of Department of Personnel & Training (DoPT). Category-wise vacancy breakup (SC/ST/OBC/UR/PwBD) will be informed in due course by the Cadre Controlling Authority.
Q3Is this exam open to general public / fresh candidates?
A
No. This is a Limited Departmental Competitive Examination (LDCE) open only to serving permanent or regularly appointed temporary Group 'C' central government employees with Grade Pay of Rs. 1800 who have completed at least 3 years of regular service as on 1 August 2025. General public candidates cannot apply.
Q4What is the salary for JSA/LDC in CSCS Cadre, DoPT?
A
The post carries Pay Level-2 of the 7th CPC Pay Matrix, with a pay range of Rs. 19,900 to Rs. 63,200. It is a Group 'C' Non-Gazetted post. In addition to basic pay, allowances (DA, HRA, TA, etc.) are admissible as per Central Government rules.
Q5What is the crucial date for eligibility in SSC JSA LDC LDCE 2025?
A
The crucial date for counting approved service is 1 August 2025. All eligibility conditions — including the 3-year length of service requirement and other conditions — are assessed with reference to this date.
Q6What is the exam pattern for SSC JSA LDC DoPT LDCE 2025?
A
The examination is a Computer Based Examination (CBE) of 180 marks and 3 hours duration, with 60 questions each in Comprehension/General English, General Knowledge, and CSMOP (Chapters 5, 6 & 10 on Dak Receipts, File Management, and Records Management). There is a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer. The exam is conducted in Delhi-NCR only.
Q7Where is the SSC JSA LDC DoPT LDCE 2025 exam held?
A
The Computer Based Examination is held in Delhi-NCR only. Candidates serving at Indian missions abroad must appear in India at their own expense; there is no exam centre at any Indian mission abroad.
Q8Is there a typewriting test for selection in SSC JSA LDC LDCE 2025?
A
The typewriting test is not part of the selection process per se, but it is a post-appointment condition. Candidates who have not already passed the periodical typewriting test (30 wpm English / 25 wpm Hindi on manual typewriter, or 35 wpm / 30 wpm on computer) must pass it within the probation period. Failure to pass results in no annual increments and potential reversion to substantive post.
Q9What is the official website for SSC JSA LDC DoPT LDCE 2025?
A
The official website for online application and notifications is https://ssc.gov.in. Admit cards and regional updates are available on the SSC Northern Region website at https://sscnr.nic.in. The helpdesk toll-free number is 1800 309 3063 and SSC NR can be reached at 011-24363343.
Q10What happens if I resign or quit service after applying for SSC JSA LDC LDCE 2025?
A
A candidate who resigns, quits service, or whose services are terminated after applying (or even after appearing in the exam) will not be eligible for appointment on the basis of the result of this examination. This rule does not apply to candidates on approved deputation to an ex-cadre post.
