Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for AIIMS Rajkot Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit online applications for AIIMS Rajkot Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 is June 08, 2026 (up to 4:00 PM). Candidates must complete their application through the official AIIMS Rajkot website before this deadline.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in AIIMS Rajkot Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?

AIIMS Rajkot has announced a total of 88 vacancies for Senior Resident (Non-Academic) posts across 29 departments. These include both current and anticipated vacancies up to December 31, 2026, covering specialties like Anaesthesiology (9 posts), Paediatrics (5 posts), and Trauma & Emergency (5 posts).

Q3. What is the qualification required for AIIMS Rajkot Senior Resident posts?

Candidates must possess MD/MS/DNB/MCh in the respective specialty from NMC/NBE recognized institutes for medical posts. For basic science departments, M.Sc. with Ph.D. is required, while Dentistry positions need MDS in relevant specialties. Valid registration with NMC or State Medical Council is mandatory.

Q4. What is the age limit for AIIMS Rajkot Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?

The upper age limit is 45 years with age relaxation of 3 years for OBC (NCL), 5 years for SC/ST candidates, and 10 years for PwBD candidates (maximum age not exceeding 56 years). Additional relaxation is available for Ex-Servicemen and Central Government employees as per GoI rules.

Q5. What is the application fee for AIIMS Rajkot Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?

General, OBC, and EWS category candidates must pay Rs. 1,180/- (including GST) as non-refundable application fee. SC, ST, PwBD, and women candidates are completely exempted from paying any application fee.

Q6. What is the selection process for AIIMS Rajkot Senior Resident posts?

The selection process consists of a written test and interview with weightage of 80:20 respectively. The written test contains 40 multiple-choice questions with negative marking of 0.50 marks for each wrong answer. Final selection is based on combined performance in both stages.

Q7. What is the salary for AIIMS Rajkot Senior Resident posts?

Senior Residents will receive pay at Level-11 of the 7th Central Pay Commission along with allowances including Non-Practicing Allowance (NPA) for medical candidates. The initial appointment tenure is one year, extendable up to three years based on performance.

Q8. How can I apply online for AIIMS Rajkot Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?

Eligible candidates can apply online only through the link provided on the official AIIMS Rajkot website (www.aiimsrajkot.edu.in). Candidates can apply for a maximum of two departments by submitting separate applications for each department along with the required application fee.
