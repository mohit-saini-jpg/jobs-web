BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has released the BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026 notification (Advt. No. 09/2026) for direct recruitment to 150 posts of Police Sub-Inspector (General Closed Cadre) under the Special Branch of the Home Department (Police Branch), Government of Bihar. Online applications open on 09/07/2026 and close on 09/08/2026. Selection will be through a Preliminary Exam, Main Exam and Physical Efficiency Test (PET). The application fee is Rs. 100/- for all categories, and the cut-off date for age and qualification is 01/08/2025.

BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Candidates can apply for BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026 only through the official BPSSC website within the application window.
  • 2Visit the official website of the Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission: bpssc.bihar.gov.in.
  • 3Go to the "Bihar Police" tab and click on the link related to Advt. No. 09/2026.
  • 4Complete Part 1 of the online application: registration with name, nationality, mobile number, email ID, Bihar domicile status, reservation category, gender and date of birth.
  • 5Complete the fee payment. A registration number and password will be sent via SMS and email after successful registration.
  • 6Log in using the registration number and password to complete Part 2 of the application: father's/husband's name, mother's name, address and educational qualification.
  • 7Upload the scanned photograph and signature as per the specified format and size.
  • 8Review all details using the "Edit Details" option before final submission, as no changes are allowed after the application is confirmed and submitted.
  • 9Take a printout of the acknowledgement on A4 size paper after final submission and keep it safely for future reference.
  • 10Note: A candidate can submit only one online application. A single mobile number can be used for only one application. Applications sent by post or any other offline mode will not be considered.

BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

The last date for submitting the online application is fixed and will not be extended under any circumstances. Requests for extension of time at any stage of the selection process (written exam, PET, document scrutiny, etc.) will not be entertained for any reason, including family, personal, medical, fracture or pregnancy-related circumstances. Candidates from states other than Bihar claiming a reservation category will be treated as Unreserved (General) category candidates and must apply accordingly; failure to do so will render them ineligible. The 35% horizontal reservation for women is applicable only to female candidates who are original residents (domicile) of Bihar. Candidates should not make multiple payments for a single application. Application fee will not be refunded once payment is successful. In case of failed transactions due to power or internet failure, the amount will be refunded by the payment gateway within 7 working days. Providing false or incorrect reservation category, wrong date of birth, wrong gender, or any false information in the application will lead to rejection of candidature and may invite legal action. It is not necessary to send the filled application to the Commission, but the registration number must be retained for future correspondence. The entire selection process is expected to take approximately 6 months to complete. Candidates should keep their registered mobile number and email ID active throughout the recruitment process, as all important communication will be sent through these channels.

BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026 notification about?
A
The Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has released Advt. No. 09/2026 for direct recruitment to 150 posts of Police Sub-Inspector (General Closed Cadre) under the Special Branch of the Home Department (Police Branch), Government of Bihar.
Q2When can I apply for BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application window opens on 09/07/2026 and closes on 09/08/2026. The last date will not be extended.
Q3How many vacancies are there in BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 150 vacancies are notified for the post of Police Sub-Inspector (General Closed Cadre), across Unreserved, EWS, EBC, SC, BC and ST categories.
Q4What is the educational qualification for BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must hold a graduate degree or an equivalent qualification as on 01-08-2025.
Q5What is the age limit for BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026?
A
The age limit ranges from 20 to 37 years for Unreserved male candidates, 20 to 40 years for Unreserved female and Backward/Extremely Backward Class candidates, and 20 to 42 years for SC/ST candidates, as on the cut-off date of 01-08-2025.
Q6What is the application fee for BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 100/- for all category male and female candidates, payable online.
Q7What is the selection process for BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection consists of a Preliminary Exam, a Main Exam (two papers) and a Physical Efficiency Test (PET), followed by document verification and a medical examination.
Q8What are the physical standards required for BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum height requirement is 165 cm for Unreserved/Backward Class males, 160 cm for EBC/SC/ST males, and 155 cm for all category females. Male candidates must also meet minimum chest measurement criteria, and female candidates must weigh at least 48 kg.
Q9How long will the BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026 selection process take?
A
The Commission states that the selection process is generally expected to take about 6 months to complete.
Q10How do I apply for BPSSC SI Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online through bpssc.bihar.gov.in under the "Bihar Police" tab, complete both parts of the registration form, upload photograph and signature, pay the application fee, and submit the form before the last date.
