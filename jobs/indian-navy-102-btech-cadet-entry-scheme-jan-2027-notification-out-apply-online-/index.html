Application Fee

The notification does not mention any application fee. Candidates are advised to check the official Indian Navy recruitment website www.joinindiannavy.gov.in for any fee-related information before submitting the online application.

Age Limit

DetailsCandidates must have been born between 02 July 2007 and 01 January 2010 (both dates inclusive) to be eligible for the January 2027 course. There is no separate age relaxation mentioned — the age band is fixed for all candidates.

Qualification / Eligibility

Candidates must fulfill all eligibility conditions as on the date of application. Only unmarried men and women fulfilling the conditions of nationality as laid down by the Government of India are eligible. Any candidate found to be married or who marries while under training will be discharged from service and shall be liable to refund full training cost including pay, allowances and any other expenditure incurred by the Government.

Important Instructions

Only unmarried men and women fulfilling conditions of nationality as laid down by the Government of India are eligible to apply. Multiple applications by any candidate for this course will lead to cancellation of candidature. Vacancies indicated are tentative and may change depending on service requirements. Candidates must not change their email/mobile number provided in the application till the selection process is over — all SSB call-up intimations are sent on these details. Change of SSB Centre for examination/interview is not permissible under any circumstances. Change of medical hospital/centre is not permissible under any circumstances. There is no relaxation in medical standards on any grounds. Candidates who have already been declared medically fit for NDA/INA Jan 27 need not undergo repeat medical examination. No compensation is admissible in case of any injury sustained as a result of tests during SSB interviews. AC 3-tier rail fare is admissible for SSB interview only if appearing for the first time for this particular type of commission — carry photocopy of passbook/cheque leaf with Name, A/C No., and IFSC details. Any candidate who is found to be married or marries while under training will be discharged from service and shall be liable to refund full training cost including pay & allowances and any other expenditure incurred by the Government. Use/possession of narcotics is banned — candidates may be tested at any stage of selection/medicals/training/service. Mere inclusion in the common merit list does not confer a vested right to appointment. Mere empanelment is not an enforceable claim to the post. The application is subject to subsequent scrutiny and can be rejected if found ineligible/invalid at any point of time. Candidature will be cancelled if any declaration is found wrong at any stage of selection. If any scanned document is not legible/readable, the application will be rejected. The application once submitted is final — no request for amendments/changes will be entertained.
