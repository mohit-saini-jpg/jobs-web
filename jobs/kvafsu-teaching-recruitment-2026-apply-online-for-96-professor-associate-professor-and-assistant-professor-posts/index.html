Selection Process

  • Shortlisting of candidates shall be governed based on Prescribed Qualification
  • Score Card and Guidelines for Direct Recruitment of Teachers (No:R/KVAFSU/ACM-CA/Teaching SCD/2025-26 dated 12.11.2025) and Score Card as prescribed under Annexure 1-3 as per guidelines of KVAFSU available on KVAFSU website. Mere eligibility will not entitle any candidate to be called for interview - applicants having higher qualification and merit will be given preference. Good Academic Record for all teaching positions shall be as defined in the KVAFSU Score Card.
  • Stage 1 - Application Scrutiny and Score Card-based Shortlisting
  • All online applications will be scrutinized for eligibility. Shortlisting will be based on Academic Score as per the prescribed KVAFSU Score Card (Annexure 1-3). Only information submitted online - including uploaded relevant documents and details of research publications entered in the online application - will be considered for determining eligibility
  • Academic Score allocation
  • and shortlisting. No additional claims or documents will be considered during original document verification.
  • Stage 2 - Kannada Language Test (if applicable)
  • Candidates who did not study Kannada as a first
  • second
  • or third language in SSLC or equivalent qualification must qualify in a Kannada Language Test (one paper
  • maximum 150 marks
  • minimum qualifying marks 50%
  • standard of first language Kannada at SSLC level) before being called for interview for any teaching post (Professor
  • Associate Professor
  • or Assistant Professor).
  • Stage 3 - Document Verification
  • Shortlisted candidates must submit self-attested copies of certificates and marksheets from Matriculation (10th Standard) onwards
  • reprints of publications
  • and certificates in support of their research score along with 3 sets of hard copies of the application form during physical verification of original documents.
  • Stage 4 - Interview
  • Candidates clearing document verification will be called for interview. Provisional selection list preparation shall be in accordance with the Karnataka Act No. 1 of 2019.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only through the Recruitment Portal on the KVAFSU website www.kvafsu.edu.in. No other mode of application will be accepted. Applicants must first register on the Recruitment Portal, after which they can fill up the prescribed online application form. Separate applications with separate registration fees must be submitted for each post applied for. After successful submission, the fee payment link will appear - fee payment must be completed for the application to be valid. The eligibility of the candidate will be determined as on the last date of submission of application i.e. 20-06-2026.
  • Documents to be Scanned and Uploaded (JPEG/JPG/PDF format; max 500 KB each):
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Signature
  • Caste/Tribe/Community Certificate (if applicable) as per Karnataka Government proforma issued by concerned Competent Authority
  • Educational qualification certificates and marksheets from Matriculation (10th Standard) onwards
  • Experience certificates / Employment record details clearly mentioning date, month, year of appointment, scale of pay, and pay drawn
  • Valid registration of Karnataka Veterinary Council / Veterinary Council of India (both sides if expiry date is on back; signature and expiry date must be clearly visible) - for Veterinary Science candidates
  • Certificate for 371-J (Kalyana Karnataka) category as per proforma prescribed by Govt. of Karnataka vide Order No. DPAR 43 HKC 2013 - issued by Assistant Commissioner only
  • Rural Quota certificate (Form-1 & 2) issued by Competent Authority in prescribed format (if claiming Rural Quota)
  • Non-Creamy Layer Certificate from Competent Authority (mandatory for Rural Quota under GM and 2A/2B/3A/3B categories)
  • Medical Certificate from District Medical Board/appropriate authority (for Physically Challenged candidates)
  • In-service certificate from controlling officer/Competent Authority (for in-service candidates claiming age relaxation)
  • CGPA/SGPA to percentage conversion certificate/chart/formulae (if applicable)
  • Equivalence certificate for foreign university qualifications (if applicable)
  • Documents to be Submitted at Document Verification (Hard Copies - 3 Sets):
  • Printed application fee receipt (downloaded after online fee payment)
  • Self-attested copies of all certificates and marksheets from Matriculation onwards
  • Reprints of all research publications (originals must be attached to the hard copy)
  • Certificates in support of research score claimed for the post
  • 3 sets of hard copies of the online application form
  • Original documents for verification against self-attested copies
  • For technical problems during the online application process, send an email to: [email protected]. All corrigendum/changes/updates related to the recruitment process will be available only on www.kvafsu.edu.in.
  • Apply Online at: https://www.kvafsu.edu.in (Recruitment Portal)

Important Instructions

  • Online mode of application is mandatory - no other mode will be accepted. Application portal opens on 22-05-2026 at 10:00 AM and closes on 20-06-2026 at 5:00 PM. Separate applications with separate registration fees must be submitted for each post applied for. Only information submitted online (including uploaded documents and research publication details) will be considered for eligibility, Academic Score, and shortlisting - no additional claims at document verification stage. Incomplete applications will be rejected - filling all mandatory fields is required. No changes are permitted after submitting the application form. Mere eligibility does not entitle any candidate for being called for interview - applicants with higher qualifications and merit will be given preference. Knowledge of Kannada is mandatory for all posts. Candidates who did not study Kannada at SSLC level must qualify in Kannada Language Test (max 150 marks, minimum 50%) before being called for interview. Kalyana Karnataka Cadre posts are reserved exclusively for local candidates of the Kalyana Karnataka region. Local candidates appointed to Residual Cadre posts shall not be eligible to claim Article 371(J) reservation benefits during entire service period. Candidates of Veterinary Science must upload valid KVC/VCI registration - signature and expiry date must be clearly visible; incomplete/poor visibility leads to rejection. Candidates previously selected in KVAFSU teaching recruitments who did not join - if they apply again and fail to join after selection, they will be made ineligible for all future KVAFSU recruitments. Selected candidates will be entitled for New Pension Scheme as per Govt. order No. FD(SN) 04 PET 2006 dated 31.03.2006 and as amended from time to time. The University reserves the right to withdraw any advertised post at any time without giving any reason. Canvassing in any form will be liable to disqualification. No interim correspondence shall be entertained. In case of any dispute/ambiguity in the selection process, the decision of the University shall be final.
