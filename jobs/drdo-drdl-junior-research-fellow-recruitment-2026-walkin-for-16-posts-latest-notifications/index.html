Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the DRDO DRDL Junior Research Fellow posts?

The recruitment is through a walk-in interview. The interview dates are scheduled from June 1st to June 5th, 2026, depending on the specific discipline.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Junior Research Fellow posts?

Candidates need a B.Tech./B.E. with First Class in a relevant engineering discipline with a valid GATE score, or an M.E./M.Tech. with First Class in a relevant engineering discipline and a B.Tech./B.E. with First Class.

Q3. What is the age limit for the Junior Research Fellow positions?

The maximum age limit for all posts is 28 years, with age relaxations applicable for SC/ST (5 years) and OBC (3 years) candidates.

Q4. What is the salary offered for the Junior Research Fellow posts?

Selected candidates will receive a stipend of Rs. 37,000/- per month along with HRA.

Q5. How many vacancies are available for Junior Research Fellows?

There are a total of 16 vacancies for Junior Research Fellow positions across different engineering disciplines.

Q6. What is the application fee for the DRDO DRDL recruitment?

There is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.

Q7. What is the selection process for the Junior Research Fellow posts?

The selection process involves shortlisting candidates based on their valid GATE score and marks in the qualifying degree, followed by an interview.

Q8. Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?

The walk-in interview will be conducted at Defence Research and Development Laboratory (DRDL), Hyderabad, Telangana, 500058.
