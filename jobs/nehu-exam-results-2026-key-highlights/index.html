North-Eastern Hill University (NEHU), Shillong has officially released the NEHU Exam Results 2026 for various Four Years Undergraduate Programme (FYUP) semester examinations held in November 2025. The latest update includes the Provisional Results of the 1st Semester FYUP Examination, Provisional Results of the 5th Semester FYUP Examination, and a Corrigendum related to the Results of the 3rd Semester FYUP Examination, all published on April 24, 2026.

NEHU Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - nehu.ac.in - through the dedicated UG results portal at exams.nehu.ac.in/UGresults. Students can check and download their FYUP 1st, 3rd and 5th Semester result PDFs by opening the official UG results page, clicking on the respective result link and searching their roll number in the document. After downloading the provisional result, candidates are advised to verify their personal details, subject-wise marks, total marks and result status, and contact the NEHU examination department or their college immediately if they notice any discrepancies.

How to Check NEHU Exam Results 2026

Students can follow the step-by-step instructions below to check NEHU Exam Results 2026 for FYUP 1st, 3rd and 5th Semester exams.

  • Visit the official NEHU website at nehu.ac.in and navigate to the Examination or Results section, or directly open the UG results portal at Click Here.
  • On the UG results page, look for the latest result notifications titled "Provisional Results of the 1st Semester FYUP Examination held in November 2025", "Provisional Results of the 5th Semester FYUP Examination held in November 2025" and "Corrigendum - Results of the 3rd Semester FYUP Examination held in November 2025".
  • Click on the relevant result link to open the corresponding PDF file in a new tab/window.
  • Once the PDF opens, use the search option (Ctrl+F) and enter your roll number or name to quickly locate your result details.
  • Check your subject-wise marks, total marks, and result status (pass/fail/other remarks) as listed in the PDF.
  • Download the PDF file and save it for your records; you may also take a printout for further academic processes such as counselling, reappearance, or revaluation applications.
  • If your result appears under the corrigendum for the 3rd Semester, carefully review the corrected details and contact the NEHU examination section if any issue persists.
