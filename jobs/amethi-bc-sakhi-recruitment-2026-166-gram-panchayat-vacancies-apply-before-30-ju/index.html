Selection Process

1Stage Details
2Stage 1 SHG-level screening and endorsement
3Stage 2 Gram Sangathan and Sankul Sthariya Sangh verification
4Stage 3 Written Test
5Stage 4 Interview before CDO-headed committee

How to Apply

  • 1Interested women SHG members should apply through their own Self Help Group.
  • 2Application forms can be collected from the SHG, Gram Sangathan, or Sankul Sthariya Sangh.
  • 3After SHG-level shortlisting, the Gram Sangathan forwards applications to the Sankul Sthariya Sangh.
  • 4The Sankul Sthariya Sangh prepares a consolidated list of endorsed BC Sakhi candidates.
  • 5The list is sent to the District Mission Management Unit (DMMU), Amethi through the Vikas Khand office.
  • 6Final selection is done through written test and interview by a committee headed by the Chief Development Officer.
  • 7Note: Candidates should not submit applications directly to the district office. Applications must be submitted through the SHG → Gram Sangathan → Sankul Sthariya Sangh → Vikas Khand → DMMU channel.

Important Instructions

The vacant Gram Panchayat list is not included in the notification. Candidates must collect the vacancy list from the concerned Vikas Khand office. Applications submitted directly to the district office will not be considered. Candidates can apply only for their permanent resident Gram Panchayat. The advertisement is displayed at Vikas Khand offices across Amethi.
