Important Instructions

Only candidates who appeared in PET-2025 and hold a valid PET-2025 Score Card issued by UPSSSC are eligible to apply for the Main Examination. Candidates who scored zero or negative marks (absolute or normalised) in PET-2025 will NOT be shortlisted for the Main Examination. Shortlisting for Main Examination will be done category-wise at a ratio of 15 times the number of advertised vacancies based on PET-2025 normalised scores. The Physical Measurement Test (PMT) is qualifying and mandatory; only PMT-qualified candidates will sit the Written Main Examination. All UP reserved category candidates (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) must mention their category in the application; candidates claiming benefits of multiple reserved categories will be entitled to only the one most beneficial reservation. Candidates not domiciled in Uttar Pradesh will be treated as Unreserved (UR) and will not get any reservation benefits. OBC Creamy Layer candidates are not eligible for OBC reservation; they must apply as General (UR). EWS candidates must hold a valid EWS certificate issued between 01-04-2026 and 29-06-2026, based on income for FY 2025-26 and valid for FY 2026-27, in the format prescribed by UP Government (office letter No. 0-3/2019/4/1/2002/ka-2/19TSI-II dated 14-03-2019). PwBD (Divyangjan) candidates must update their disability sub-category in the application as per the latest UP Government notifications on horizontal reservation for PwBD (GO No. 3/2021/324/2021/65-3-2021-78/99TI0SI0 dated 30-07-2021 and subsequent GOs). Ex-Servicemen / Disabled Ex-Servicemen who will be discharged from service by the last date of application (29-06-2026) are eligible for Ex-Servicemen reservation as per applicable UP Acts. Outstanding Sportspersons must produce a certificate from a competent authority in the format prescribed under UP Govt Notification dated 07-01-2022 (Niyamavali-2022 and subsequent GOs) to claim sportsperson reservation. Female UP candidates will be placed in the same category to which they belong; caste certificate from the father's side is the only accepted document for female UP candidates. Date of birth as recorded in the High School or equivalent certificate will be the only accepted date of birth; no other document will be entertained. In case of multiple applications submitted, only the last finally submitted application will be accepted; fees paid for other registrations will be forfeited. The Commission does not communicate individually with candidates; all information is published on upsssc.gov.in only. Candidates must visit the website regularly. Canvassing in any form will result in disqualification. Copying, use of unfair means, or misconduct in the examination hall will lead to cancellation and debarment from all UPSSSC examinations/selections. Provisions of UP Public Examinations (Prevention of Unfair Means) Act, 2024 dated 06-08-2024 will apply. The Commission's decision on eligibility is final. Any ambiguity, error, or omission will be resolved as per the relevant UP service rules and reservation enactments.
