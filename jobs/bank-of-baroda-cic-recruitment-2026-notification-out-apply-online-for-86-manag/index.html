CIBIL Score Requirement: A Credit History Check Unique to This Recruitment

Anyone who has defaulted on loan repayments or credit card dues, or who has adverse reports from CIBIL or other external credit assessment agencies, will be treated as ineligible for appointment. The bank has reserved the right to reject a candidate's selection at any stage, including after joining, if their credit profile turns out to be unsatisfactory.

Probation Period and Mandatory Service Bond

Selected candidates do not join in a permanent capacity immediately; they first complete a probation period, and they must also commit to a minimum service period backed by a financial bond. Probation Period: 12 months (1 year) of active service from the date of joining. Service Bond: Candidates must serve the bank for a minimum of 3 years from the date of joining, or pay ₹5 lakh in lieu of completing the bond period. The exact bond terms will be confirmed in each selected candidate's offer letter and are subject to revision as per the bank's prevailing policy.

Selection Process: Online Test, Psychometric Assessment & Interview

Selection for Bank of Baroda C&IC Recruitment 2026 can include application scrutiny, an online test, a psychometric assessment, a group discussion, and a personal interview, though the bank retains the right to skip or modify any stage. Final selection depends on combined performance across whichever stages are actually conducted. Job Profile: What Will You Actually Do in This Role?

The two job families under this recruitment involve noticeably different day-to-day work, which is worth understanding before choosing which post to apply for. Credit Analyst roles (Manager/Senior Manager/Chief Manager) are appraisal-focused: examining new and existing corporate credit proposals, conducting credit due-diligence on financial, industry, and business risk, coordinating with Relationship Managers, and ensuring compliance in corporate accounts. C&IC Relationship roles (Senior Manager/Chief Manager) are client-facing: maintaining relationships with existing corporate clients, sourcing new-to-bank corporate borrowers, cross-selling ancillary business, and creating account plans with opportunity income analysis for corporate borrowers.

Mistakes That Could Cost You This Opportunity

Several avoidable errors can disqualify an otherwise eligible candidate from Bank of Baroda C&IC Recruitment 2026, so it helps to check these before hitting submit. Assuming any postgraduate degree qualifies - only a finance-specialised PG degree/diploma or a CA/CMA/CS/CFA qualification meets the educational criteria. Not checking your CIBIL score in advance - a score below 680, or any default history, can lead to rejection even after selection. Counting clerical-cadre banking experience toward the eligibility criteria, which is explicitly excluded. Including work experience of less than 6 months from a previous organisation, which will not be counted unless it is part of your current employment. Submitting more than one application - only the latest valid one is retained, and fees paid for earlier ones are forfeited. Selecting a category casually, since no change of category is allowed
