The Student Academic Management System (SAMS), under the Higher Education Department, Government of Odisha, has declared the Odisha BEd Result 2026 for the B.Ed. (Arts/Science), B.H.Ed., and M.Ed. Entrance Test on 30th June 2026. Candidates who appeared for the entrance examination held on 31st May 2026 can now check their result and download their scorecard from the official website te.samsodisha.gov.in or www.samsodisha.gov.in, using their Barcode or Roll Number. The result has been released along with the final answer key, and qualified candidates will now be eligible for the SAMS Odisha BEd counselling process.

How to Check SAMS Odisha BEd Result 2026?

This section explains the step-by-step process to download the Odisha BEd scorecard online using login credentials.

  • Visit the official SAMS Odisha website te.samsodisha.gov.in or ssbodisha.ac.in.
  • On the homepage, locate the link for the "Results of B.Ed. Arts/Science, B.H.Ed. and M.Ed. Entrance Test- 2026."
  • Click on the link to open the result login page.
  • Enter your Barcode/Roll Number and the captcha code shown on screen.
  • Click on the "Show" button.
  • Your Odisha BEd Result 2026 and scorecard will be displayed.
  • Download and take a printout for future reference, including counselling.

Odisha BEd Cut Off Marks 2026: When Will It Be Released?

A common question among candidates after the result is what score is needed to qualify for counselling. This section clarifies the current status of the cut-off and what to expect.

The official, category-wise and institute-wise Odisha BEd cut-off marks are not part of the result declaration itself. The official cut off will be published along with each selection list round during the counseling process, with the State Selection Board releasing these marks on the SAMS Odisha portal. Based on previous years' trends, General (UR) candidates have typically needed around 62–68 marks for a government college seat, while reserved categories generally see lower qualifying marks. Candidates should treat these as indicative figures only, since the actual cut off depends on category, the number of applicants, available seats, and the difficulty level of the exam. Adda247 + 2

What's Next After the Odisha BEd Result 2026?

Once the result is out, candidates naturally want to know the roadmap ahead. This section outlines the three key stages that follow the result declaration.

Counselling Process: Candidates who qualify the entrance exam can register for SAMS Odisha BEd Counselling 2026, where they will indicate college preferences based on their rank and category. The detailed counselling schedule is expected to be released shortly by the officials.

Document Verification: During counselling, candidates must submit documents for verification, including Class 10 and 12 mark sheets, graduation/post-graduation certificates (if applicable), caste/category certificate (for reserved categories), domicile certificate, and a government-issued photo ID.

Seat Allotment & Admission: After successful counselling and document verification, candidates will be allotted seats in their chosen B.Ed colleges and granted admission.

Documents Required at the Time of Admission

  • Downloaded intimation letter
  • College Leaving Certificate (CLC) of recently studied course, in original
  • Conduct certificate, in original
  • All relevant educational certificates and mark sheets (original and photocopy)
  • Certificate confirming Odia passed at HSC level (for candidates not from the Odisha Board)
  • Residential certificate issued by a Tahsildar/Additional Tahsildar or above
  • Caste certificate (for SC, ST, and SEBC candidates)
  • UDID disability certificate / ex-serviceman certificate, where applicable
