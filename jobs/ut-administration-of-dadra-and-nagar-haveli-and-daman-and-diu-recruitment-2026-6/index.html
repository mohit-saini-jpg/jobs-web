Job Overview

The Department of Social Welfare, Women and Child Development, UT Administration of Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu is recruiting 60 candidates on a short-term contract basis through a Walk-in-Interview scheduled on 23, 24, and 25 June 2026 at 9:30 AM. Posts span three national schemes - Mission Vatsalaya, Mission Shakti, and Mission Saksham Anganwadi & POSHAN 2.0 - across DNH, Daman, and Diu districts. No application fee is charged, and candidates must bring a filled application form along with original documents on the interview day.

Important Instructions

Candidates should carefully review the following conditions before attending the interview, as non-compliance may result in rejection at the venue.
Candidates previously terminated from any Government Organization shall not be considered. All educational qualifications (Master's Degree / Degree / Diploma) must be from a recognized University/Institution approved by the Government of India. Domicile Certificate holders of DNH & DD shall be given preference in selection. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the Walk-in-Interview. The actual number of vacancies may vary as per departmental requirement; the place of posting may also be changed at the Department's discretion. The Department reserves the right to cancel or terminate the recruitment process without assigning any reason. The engagement is contractual in nature and carries no right to regularization in future.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for DNH & DD Social Welfare Recruitment 2026?
A
There is no separate last date — the Walk-in-Interview itself is the application process. Candidates must attend on 23, 24, or 25 June 2026 depending on the post, at 9:30 AM with a filled application form.
Q2How many total vacancies are there in this recruitment?
A
There are 60 total vacancies across 35 different posts under Mission Vatsalaya, Mission Shakti, and Mission Saksham Anganwadi & POSHAN 2.0 schemes.
Q3Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
No. There is no application fee for any post. Candidates can appear for the Walk-in-Interview free of charge.
Q4What is the age limit for applying?
A
The desirable age limit is 18 to 35 years. Candidates with experience in the Social Welfare and Women & Child Development sector may be considered up to 50 years of age.
Q5Where is the Walk-in-Interview held?
A
For DNH and Daman district candidates: O/o The Director cum Deputy Secretary (SW/WCD), District Court Premises, Fort Area, Moti Daman – 396220. For Diu district candidates: O/o The Child Development Project Officer, Near Sai Baba Temple, Naida, Diu.
Q6What documents should I bring to the Walk-in-Interview?
A
Candidates must bring the duly filled application form, original certificates (educational qualification, experience, domicile if applicable), and one set of self-attested photocopies of all documents along with a passport-size photograph.
