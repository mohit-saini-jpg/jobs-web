How to Apply

  • 1Step 1: Visit HIMS Portal - Go to https://hims.odisha.gov.in/ (portal available from 07.05.2026)
  • 2Step 2: Register/Login - Create account or login if already registered
  • 3Step 3: Fill Online Application - Complete application form with all required details (can be filled in multiple sessions)
  • 4Step 4: Upload Documents - Upload soft copy of documents in PDF/image format with proper readability: Photograph in JPEG format (size less than 200 kb) All supporting documents in PDF/image format Self-attested certificates
  • 5Photograph in JPEG format (size less than 200 kb)
  • 6All supporting documents in PDF/image format
  • 7Self-attested certificates
  • 8Step 5: Pay Application Fee - Pay non-refundable fee online as per category
  • 9Step 6: Submit Online Application - Complete and submit application before 10.06.2026 (Midnight)
  • 10Step 7: Download Submitted Application - Download PDF format of submitted application
  • 11Step 8: Send Hard Copy via Speed Post - Send hard copy of downloaded application along with self-attested documents via Speed Post to: The Registrar, Berhampur University, Bhanja Bihar, Berhampur, Ganjam-760007 Must reach on or before 20.06.2026
  • 12Candidates applying for more than one post must apply separately for each post and pay application fee for each post
  • 13The online application can be filled in multiple sessions
  • 14Persons employed in Government/Public Sector Enterprise/Autonomous bodies should forward copy through proper channel or furnish No Objection Certificate (NOC)
  • 15The envelope containing hard copy must be superscribed "APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF " along with advertisement No and Date
  • 16Courier, ordinary post, and hand delivery not accepted for hard copy submission
  • 17No hard copy will be considered if the applicant fails to submit online application by closing date
  • 18Hard copy of application of applicants who have applied online before closing date will be accepted if it reaches by 20.06.2026
  • 19Recent passport size photograph (JPEG format, less than 200 kb)
  • 20Signature (scanned)
  • 21Matriculation/SSC certificate (for date of birth proof)
  • 22Master's Degree certificate and mark sheets
  • 23Ph.D. degree certificate (as applicable)
  • 24NET/SLET/SET certificate (for Assistant Professor)
  • 25Category certificates (SC/ST/SEBC as per Govt. of Odisha format)
  • 26Disability certificate issued by competent authority (for PWD category)
  • 27Experience certificates
  • 28Publication certificates/proof
  • 29NOC from current employer (if in service)
  • 30Any other relevant certificates mentioned in notification
  • 31Eligibility of a candidate and satisfaction of any other short-listing criteria shall be considered as on closing date of submission of online application (10.06.2026 Midnight)
  • 32Only matriculation/SSC certificate/pass certificate issued by the concerned educational board will be considered as proof of date of birth. No other document will be accepted
  • 33Candidates above 60 years will not be eligible to apply
  • 34Exchange of reservation is not permitted
  • 35The period spent by candidates to acquire M.Phil. and/or Ph.D. degree shall not be considered as research experience
  • 36As per UGC communication dated 1 March 2016, a certificate from the employer/head of Institution/University mentioning that the period of active service spent on pursuing Research Degree (Ph.D.) simultaneously without taking any leave may be counted as teaching experience for Associate Professor and above
  • 37If more than one application is submitted by the candidate for the same post, only the application submitted later within the last date of submission shall be considered. Application in part is not acceptable
  • 38Incomplete application form in any respect and/or not in the prescribed proforma or/and without required documents will not be entertained and will be rejected without assigning any reason
  • 39Application without signature will not be considered and rejected without refund of application processing fee
  • 40No TA/DA shall be paid to the candidates for attending the interview
  • 41Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification
  • 42The prescribed qualifications and experience are minimum and mere possessing the same does not entitle the candidate to be called for interview. The University will have the right to restrict and shortlist most suitable candidates
  • 43The University reserves the right not to fill up any or some of the vacancies advertised without assigning any reason
  • 44The University reserves the right to revise/reschedule/cancel/suspend the advertisement/recruitment process without assigning any reason
  • 45In case of any inadvertent mistake in the process of selection, which might be detected at any stage even after issue of appointment letter, the University reserves the right to modify/withdraw/cancel any communication made to the candidate(s)
  • 46While applying, the applicant should ensure that he/she fulfils the eligibility and other conditions/norms. In case it is detected at any stage that the candidate has furnished incorrect/false information or suppressed facts, the appointment/service will be terminated
  • 47No request for change of post(s) and update of new information in the application form will be entertained after the last date of application
  • 48In cases of any disputes, any suits or legal proceedings against the University, the jurisdiction shall be restricted to the Courts in Berhampur, Odisha which is the headquarters of the University
  • 49Any change or amendment in this advertisement shall be displayed on the University website only. Candidates are advised to visit University website regularly and remain updated
  • 50For references, UGC NORMS (wherever applicable and as amended from time to time) may be downloaded from UGC websites
  • 51Candidates are advised to visit the website www.buodisha.edu.in regularly for updated information regarding eligible/ineligible list, interview schedule, results, and other notifications
