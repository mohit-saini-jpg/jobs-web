FAQs

Q1What is Berhampur University Recruitment 2026 for faculty posts?
A
Berhampur University Recruitment 2026 is a regular faculty recruitment drive for 95 teaching posts of Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor across various PG Departments and Lingaraj Law College at Berhampur University, Bhanja Bihar, Ganjam, Odisha, as per UGC Regulations dated 18 July 2018.
Q2How many vacancies are there in Berhampur University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The notification mentions a total of 95 vacancies, including 19 Professor posts, 38 Associate Professor posts and 38 Assistant Professor posts in departments such as Business Administration, Chemistry, Commerce, Computer Science, English, Mathematics, Marine Sciences, Political Science, Zoology and Lingaraj Law College.
Q3What are the important dates for Berhampur University Recruitment 2026?
A
Online applications through the HIMS portal start from 7 May 2026 and the last date for online application submission is 10 June 2026 (midnight), while the hard copy of the application with documents must reach the Registrar, Berhampur University, by 20 June 2026.
Q4What is the eligibility criteria for Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor at Berhampur University?
A
Eligibility for all faculty posts is as per UGC Regulations Notification F.1‑2/2017(EC/PS) dated 18 July 2018, requiring Ph.D. with specified research score and experience for Professor and Associate Professor, and for Assistant Professor a Master’s degree with prescribed marks plus NET/SLET/SET or Ph.D. as per UGC 2009/2016 Regulations, with age below 60 years on the closing date.
Q5What is the age limit for Berhampur University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates above 60 years of age are not eligible to apply, and the current superannuation age for faculty in Berhampur University is 60 years as per Higher Education Department, Government of Odisha norms, with date of birth verified only through Matriculation/SSC certificate.
Q6What is the salary or pay scale for Berhampur University faculty posts in 2026?
A
Berhampur University offers pay scales as per 7th CPC approved by Government of Odisha, where Professors are placed in Pay Level‑14, Associate Professors in Pay Level‑13A and Assistant Professors in Pay Level‑10 along with admissible allowances as sanctioned by the state government.
Q7What is the application fee for Berhampur University Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates from General and SEBC categories need to pay a non‑refundable application fee of Rs. 2000 per post, while SC, ST, PwD and Women candidates are required to pay Rs. 1000 per post, and the fee must be paid online through the prescribed mode on the application portal.
Q8What is the selection process for Berhampur University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of screening/scrutiny of applications, shortlisting based on UGC academic and research score, followed by an interview where shortlisted candidates must deliver a mandatory 9‑slide PPT presentation; final selection is made as per UGC norms and University Statute.
