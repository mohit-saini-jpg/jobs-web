Selection Process

  • Selection for all advertised posts will be made on the basis of merit obtained in the Written/OMR Based Examination only. In case of a large number of applications
  • JKSSB reserves the right to apply shortlisting criteria as deemed appropriate.
  • Stage 1 – Written/OMR Based Examination
  • The examination will consist of Objective Type Multiple Choice Questions set in English Language only. There will be negative marking of one-fourth of marks for each wrong answer. The date and venue of the examination shall be notified separately on the official website jkssb.nic.in.
  • Stage 2 – Document Verification (DV)
  • Candidates shortlisted on the basis of written examination merit will be called for Document Verification. Original documents along with self-attested photocopies must be produced. Failure to appear or produce valid documents on the scheduled DV date shall lead to cancellation of candidature.
  • Stage 3 – Medical Examination
  • Selected candidates must be medically fit as per the standards prescribed by the Government of J&K.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only through the JKSSB official portal at https://jkssb.nic.in. Detailed instructions for filling the online application will be made available on the Board’s website separately. No other mode of application will be accepted.
  • Documents to be Uploaded at the time of Online Application:
  • Live photograph as required during online form filling.
  • Scanned signature (size: 10 KB to 20 KB).
  • Valid Domicile Certificate issued by the Competent Authority.
  • Matriculation/10th certificate as proof of date of birth and name.
  • Degree/Diploma/ITI certificate and marksheets for educational qualification proof (self-attested, max size 100 KB in PDF format).
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/RBA/EWS/ALC/IB) in prescribed format valid as on cut-off date (max size 100 KB in PDF format), if applicable.
  • PwBD certificate, if applicable.
  • Ex-Servicemen certificate in Form XIV, if applicable.
  • Any other supporting document for claims made in the application.
  • Candidates must take a printout of the finally submitted online application and retain it for production at the time of Document Verification. No hard copy is required to be sent to JKSSB by post or email.
  • Apply Online at: https://jkssb.nic.in
  • For assistance, candidates may use the JKSSB official website or contact ssbjk-grievance.jk.gov.in for grievances related to fee payment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is JKSSB Recruitment 2026 Advertisement No. 04/2026?

JKSSB Recruitment 2026 under Advertisement No. 04/2026 is a selection drive for 130 UT/Divisional/District Cadre posts in various departments of the J&K Government including Agriculture Production, ARI & Trainings, Industries & Commerce, Higher Education, Home, and Law Justice & Parliamentary Affairs. The notification invites online applications from eligible J&K domicile candidates for technical, clerical, and support staff posts with pay scales from Level SL1 to Level 6F.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in JKSSB Recruitment 2026 and in which departments?

A total of 130 vacancies have been notified across six departments: Agriculture Production Department (Animal Husbandry/Sheep Husbandry/Horticulture) ARI & Trainings Department (Govt. Q3. What are the important dates for JKSSB Recruitment 2026 (Advt. 04/2026)?

Date of Advertisement: 25 May 2026 Opening of Online Application: 22 June 2026 Last Date for Online Application (cut-off date): 21 July 2026 Date for determining eligibility (qualification, age, domicile, category): 21 July 2026

Q4. Who is eligible to apply for JKSSB Recruitment 2026 – domicile and qualification?

Only domiciles of the Union Territory of Jammu & Kashmir with a valid domicile certificate issued by the competent authority on or before 21-07-2026 are eligible. Educational qualification is post-specific, ranging from 10+2, Matric + ITI, Diploma in Engineering/Technology, Graduate, B.Lib, BCA/Graduate with Computer Diploma, B.Sc. (Forensic/Medical/Non-Medical), etc., as detailed in the notification for each Item Number.

Q5. What is the age limit for JKSSB Recruitment 2026 as on 01-01-2026?

Maximum age limits (not born before the date given) are: Open Merit (OM): 40 years (01-01-1986 to 01-01-2008) SC/ST/RBA/ALC/IB/EWS/OBC: 43 years (01-01-1983 to 01-01-2008) PwBD: 42 years (01-01-1984 to 01-01-2008) Ex-Servicemen (ESM): 48 years (01-01-1978 to 01-01-2008)

Q6. What is the selection process for JKSSB Recruitment 2026?

Selection will be based on merit in a Written/OMR-based objective examination only. The exam will consist of Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) in English, and there will be negative marking of 1/4th of the marks for each wrong answer. After the written exam, shortlisted candidates will undergo Document Verification, and finally a medical fitness check as per J&K Government norms.

You do not have the exact essential qualification for the specific post as per the notification. You belong to OBC Creamy Layer but want OBC reservation benefits. You cannot produce original certificates at Document Verification, or you enter incorrect personal details (name, DOB, parent’s name) differing from Matric certificate.
