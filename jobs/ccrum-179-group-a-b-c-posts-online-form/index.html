Post Wise Qualification

  • Research Officer (Unani) : Post Graduate Degree (MD or MS) in Unani System of Medicine from any University/Institution recognized by NCISM (Formerly known as CCIM) and Enrolment on the Central Registrar of NCISM/CCIM or State Register of Unani/ISM.
  • Research Officer (Pathology) : M.D. in Pathology from an MCI recognized University/Institution and Enrolment on the Central Register of MCI or State Register of Medical Council.
  • Research Officer (Physiology) : Master’s Degree in Molecular Biology/Biotechnology/Human Genetics/Genetics Engineering from a recognized University/Institution and 03 years’ experience after obtaining Master’s degree in relevant subject in a recognized Research/Academic Institution/Laboratory/Industry, OR Ph.D. after Master’s degree in above subject/areas.
  • Assistant Research Officer (Pharmacology) : M.Sc in Pharmacology or M.V.Sc Degree from a recognized University/Institution and Aptitude for research in the line.
  • Assistant Research Officer (Pharmacognosy/Botany) : Master’s Degree in the concerned Discipline from a recognized University/Institution and Three years Research/Teaching experience in the concerned discipline after obtaining Master’s Degree, OR Ph.D in the concerned discipline.
  • Staff Nurse : B.Sc. (Hons) in Nursing or Regular Course in B.Sc Nursing or Post-Basic B.Sc Nursing from a recognized University/Institute and Registered as a Nurse or Nurse & Mid-wife with State Nursing Council, OR Diploma in General Nursing Midwifery from a recognized Board or Council and Registered as a Nurse or Nurse & Mid-wife with State Nursing Council and One year’s experience in minimum fifty bedded hospital.
  • Accounts Officer (HQ) : Degree from a recognized University/Institute and 3 years’ experience in Cash, Accounts and Budget work in a Government Office/PSU/Autonomous Body/Statutory Body.
  • Research Assistant (Chemistry) : Post Graduate degree in Chemistry from a recognized University/Institution and Aptitude for Research.
  • Research Assistant (Botany) : Post Graduate degree in Botany from a recognized University/Institution and Aptitude for Research.
  • Investigator (Statistics) : Master’s degree in Statistics/Mathematics with Statistics as one of the subjects from a recognized University/Institution and 3 years’ experience in statistical work, OR Ph.D in Statistics.
  • Medical Laboratory Technologist (MLT) : Bachelor’s Degree in Medical Laboratory Science/Technology from a recognized University/Institution with 2 years relevant experience.
  • Library and Information Assistant (LIA) : Degree from a recognized University and Degree in Library Science from a recognized University/Institution.
  • Proof Reader : Degree of a recognized University and Diploma in Books publishing/production or Printing Technology or Marketing of one year duration and 3 years’ experience in the line.
  • Hindi Assistant : Degree of a recognized University and Adequate knowledge of Hindi equal to B.A. standard and Ability to translate from English to Hindi and Vice-Versa.
  • Pharmacist : 10+2 with science from recognized Board/Institution and Two years Diploma in Pharmacy from a recognized Institution and Registration with the State/Central Pharmacy Council.
  • Social Worker : Degree from a recognized University and Diploma in Social Work from a recognized University/Institution and One year experience in Social Work, OR Master’s Degree in Sociology from a recognized University.
  • Radiographer : 10+2 with science subjects from a recognized Board/Institution and Two years Diploma in Radiography and One year experience.
  • Compounder : Matric or equivalent and Certificate of training as Compounder in Unani System of Medicine and 2 years’ experience.
  • Stenographer Grade-III : Matric or equivalent and Ability to write shorthand at 80 wpm and typewriting at 40 wpm.
  • Upper Division Clerk (UDC) : Degree from a recognized University.
  • Lower Division Clerk (LDC) : 12th Class or equivalent qualification from a recognized Board or University and A typing speed of 35 wpm in English or 30 wpm in Hindi on Computer.
  • Staff Car Driver : Matriculation from a recognized Board/Institution and Possession of valid driving license for Motor Cars/LMV and Experience of driving a motor car for at least 03 years and Knowledge of motor mechanism.
  • Multi Tasking Staff (MTS) : Matriculation or equivalent pass OR ITI pass in relevant subject.
