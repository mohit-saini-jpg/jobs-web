RRC NCR Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is RRC NCR Act Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
RRC NCR Act Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is a training opportunity released by the Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC), North Central Railway for engagement of 1,853 Act Apprentices under the Apprentices Act, 1961. Eligible candidates can apply online from 08 July 2026 to 07 August 2026. The recruitment is meant to provide apprenticeship training in various designated trades across different NCR divisions.
Q2How many vacancies are available in RRC NCR Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 1,853 Apprentice vacancies have been announced by the Railway Recruitment Cell, North Central Railway. These apprenticeship slots are available at the NCR Headquarters, Prayagraj, Agra Division, Jhansi Division, and Jhansi Workshop. Candidates should refer to the official notification for the complete trade-wise vacancy distribution.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for RRC NCR Apprentice 2026?
A
Candidates must have passed 10th/Matriculation (SSC) with at least 50% aggregate marks from a recognized board. In addition, applicants must possess a National Trade Certificate (NTC) or ITI Certificate in the relevant trade issued by NCVT or SCVT recognized by the Government of India. Both qualifications are mandatory to be eligible.
Q4What is the age limit for RRC NCR Act Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age required is 15 years, while the maximum age is 24 years as on 07 July 2026. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories, including SC/ST, OBC, PwBD, and Ex-Servicemen, as per Government of India rules. Candidates should verify their eligibility before applying.
Q5What is the application fee for RRC NCR Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee for General, OBC, and EWS candidates is ₹100, which is non-refundable. However, SC, ST, PwBD, and all Female candidates are exempted from paying the application fee. Payment must be completed online through the official application portal.
Q6What is the selection process for RRC NCR Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process is based entirely on a merit list prepared using the marks obtained in the 10th examination and the relevant ITI trade. Shortlisted candidates will be called for Document Verification followed by a Medical Examination. There is no written examination or interview.
Q7What are the important dates for RRC NCR Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application process starts on 08 July 2026 and the last date to submit the application is 07 August 2026. Candidates should complete registration and fee payment before the deadline to avoid last-minute technical issues. Late applications will not be accepted.
Q8How can I apply for RRC NCR Act Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates need to visit the official RRC North Central Railway website and complete the online registration process. After filling in the application form, applicants must upload the required documents, pay the fee (if applicable), and submit the form before the closing date. It is advisable to keep a printed copy of the submitted application.
Q9Is an ITI certificate mandatory for RRC NCR Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, an ITI Certificate or National Trade Certificate (NTC) in the relevant trade is compulsory for all applicants. The certificate must be issued by NCVT or SCVT recognized by the Government of India. Candidates without the required ITI qualification are not eligible to apply.
Q10Is RRC NCR Apprentice Recruitment 2026 a permanent railway job?
A
No, the RRC NCR Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is not a permanent government job. It is an apprenticeship training program conducted under the Apprentices Act, 1961, to provide practical training and skill development. Completion of apprenticeship training does not guarantee permanent employment in the Indian Railways.
