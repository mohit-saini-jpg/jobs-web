Qualification / Eligibility

Project Assistant: B.Tech/B.E in CS/IT/EE/ECE streams with at least 60% marks (6.5 CGPA in aggregate). Experience working with Linux-based workstations and servers. Hands-on experience in managing GPU-based servers. Ability to troubleshoot system issues and experiment setups. Proficiency in Python is preferred. Knowledge of C/C++ is desirable. Ability to read, analyze, and review research papers. Strong communication skills will be an added advantage. Ability to multitask and manage multiple responsibilities effectively. Previous Experience Working in IITs or similar reputed institutes will be an added advantage. Manage and maintain lab infrastructure, servers, and experimental setups. Maintain proper documentation of experiments, results, and progress reports. Conduct experiments, data collection, and analysis as per project requirements. Handle multiple tasks/projects simultaneously and meet deadlines. Troubleshoot technical issues and ensure smooth functioning of systems. Collaborate with faculty, researchers, and students on ongoing projects.
