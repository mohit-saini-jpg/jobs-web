OSSSC Nursing Officer Recruitment 2026 Last Date Extended : Job Overview

The Odisha Sub-ordinate Staff Selection Commission (OSSSC) is recruiting 5989 District Cadre Group-C Nursing Officers across all 30 districts of Odisha under the Health & Family Welfare Department. Online registration opens on 13 June 2026 and the last date to apply is 05 August 2026. The examination fee is ₹500 for all categories except SC, ST, and PwDs. Apply exclusively through www.osssc.gov.in.

OSSSC Nursing Officer Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

The selection is based on a single Written Test followed by Document Verification. No interview is conducted. The selection process has three stages: Written Test, Provisional Master Merit List, and Document Verification followed by a Provisional Select List. Stage 1 - Written Test (100 marks, MCQ, OMR/CBRE Mode) The Written Test consists of 100 questions for 100 marks to be completed in 2 hours . The exact mode (OMR or CBRE) will be notified separately. The test paper is divided as follows: Negative Marking: For every wrong answer, 0.25 marks will be deducted as penalty. Each question has four options. If a candidate marks more than one option, it is treated as a wrong answer. Unanswered questions carry no penalty. OSSSC Nursing Officer Recruitment 2026 - Salary / Pay Scale

The Nursing Officer post carries a clearly defined pay scale under the Odisha Revised Scale of Pay (ORSP) Rules 2017. This is a permanent Group-C post in the district cadre. The starting basic pay for a Nursing Officer is ₹29,200 per month at Pay Matrix Level-8, Cell-01 as per ORSP Rules 2017, with applicable Dearness Allowance and other allowances as per Government rules. Candidates selected may also be deployed as Community Health Officer (CHO) by the appointing authorities.

Q3What is the educational qualification required for OSSSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
Candidates must have passed 10+2, hold a Diploma in GNM or BSc Nursing from a recognised institution approved by the Indian Nursing Council, and possess a valid Registration or Renewal Certificate from the Nursing Council of Odisha as on the date of submission of the online application form.
Q4What is the age limit for OSSSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 21 and 42 years of age as on the date of advertisement (12 June 2026). SC/ST/SEBC/Women candidates get a 5-year relaxation (upper limit becomes 47 years), PwD candidates get a 10-year relaxation (upper limit becomes 52 years), and contractual Nursing Officers under OSH&FW Society/Scheme and ASHAs get an upper limit of 45 years.
Q5What is the application fee for OSSSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
The examination fee is ₹500 for all candidates except those belonging to SC, ST, and Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) categories, who are exempted from paying any fee. The fee can be paid via Net Banking, Credit Card, Debit Card, or UPI.

A
The Nursing Officer post carries a pay scale of ₹29,200–92,300 at Pay Matrix Level-8, Cell-01 as per Odisha Revised Scale of Pay (ORSP) Rules 2017. The starting basic pay is ₹29,200 per month.
Q7What is the selection process for OSSSC Nursing Officer 2026?
A
Selection is based on a Written Test of 100 marks (MCQ type, OMR/CBRE mode, 2 hours duration) followed by Document Verification and preparation of a Provisional Select List. There is no interview. Minimum qualifying marks are 30% for ST/SC/PwDs/Ex-Servicemen/Sportsperson and 35% for all others.
Q8What is the exam pattern for OSSSC Nursing Officer Written Test?
A
The Written Test has 100 questions for 100 marks in 2 hours. The paper includes 60 questions from nursing syllabus subjects, 25 questions on practical skills, 10 questions on Arithmetic (High School level), and 5 questions on English (High School level). There is negative marking of 0.25 marks per wrong answer.
