IIT Madras Project Consultant (Veterinary Consultant) Eligibility Criteria 2026

Project Consultant (Veterinary Consultant): Bachelor of Veterinary Science (BVSc) is mandatory. A Master's degree (MVSc) in Veterinary Science is preferred. Minimum of 1 year of experience working in a CCSEA-approved animal house. Comprehensive understanding of CCSEA guidelines, ethical standards, and compliance requirements. Must possess a valid veterinary license recognized in India.
