Selection Process

  • Selection will be based on shortlisting through percentage of marks in the qualifying examination
  • followed by certificate verification
  • written test / skill test / interview
  • and medical fitness. Final provisional selection is in the order of merit/reservation
  • subject to medical fitness. Merely submitting an application or receiving a call letter does not confer any right to the applicant or constitute acceptance of candidature.
  • Stage 1 - Shortlisting Based on Marks Percentage
  • Candidates will be shortlisted in order of percentage of marks obtained in the minimum prescribed qualifying examination (ITI/Diploma/B.Tech as applicable to the respective discipline). Only marks entered in the application portal will be considered - if entered marks differ from the mark sheet uploaded
  • the application will be summarily rejected. For NATS portal applicants
  • percentage must be compulsorily entered in the "% of Marks/CGPA" column; if not furnished
  • candidature will not be considered.
  • Stage 2 - Certificate Verification
  • Shortlisted candidates will be called for verification of original certificates/documents. Candidates must bring original certificates as proof of age
  • qualification
  • caste
  • disability etc.
  • along with self-attested copies. Call letters will NOT be sent by post - candidates will be informed by email or through publication on the HOCL website (www.hoclindia.com). Candidates should frequently check their email and the HOCL website for updates. Failure to produce original certificates will result in rejection of candidature.
  • Stage 3 - Written Test / Skill Test / Interview
  • Candidates who successfully complete certificate verification will be called for Written Test / Skill Test / Interview. Final provisional selection is in the order of merit/reservation from among those who successfully complete all stages
  • subject to medical fitness.
  • Stage 4 - Medical Fitness
  • Appointment of selected candidates is subject to medical fitness as prescribed in Clause 4 of the Apprenticeship Rules 1992 and amendments thereof
  • and also subject to verification of character and antecedents and verification of caste/disability certificates as applicable.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply exclusively through the respective portal based on their qualification category. No applications through any other method or platform will be considered.
  • For Trade Apprentices (ITI Holders) - Apply via NAPS Portal (apprenticeshipindia.gov.in):
  • For candidates already enrolled on NAPS portal: Login with User ID & Password → Click Apprenticeship Opportunities → Choose Course, Location → Type Establishment Name "Hindustan Organic Chemicals Limited" → Search (Establishment Code: E07203200009) → List of trades will be displayed → Click Apply against the respective trade.
  • For candidates not yet enrolled on NAPS portal: Go to https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in/ → Click Enroll under "Candidate" → Complete Registration (Qualification Certificate, Govt ID Proof, Bank Passbook, Photo, Resume required) → Complete Aadhaar verification → A unique Enrollment Number will be generated after MSDE verification (wait at least 1 day) → Then follow the steps above to apply.
  • For Graduate & Technician Apprentices (B.Tech/Diploma) - Apply via NATS Portal (nats.education.gov.in):
  • For candidates already registered on NATS portal: Login with User ID & Password → Search "Hindustan Organic Chemicals Limited" under "Apply against advertised vacancies" or "Apply against yearly requirements" → Click Apply (status will show as "applied").
  • For candidates not yet registered on NATS portal: Go to https://nats.education.gov.in → Click Student → Click Student Register → Complete application form (Qualification Certificate, Govt ID Proof, Bank Passbook, Photo, Resume required) → A unique Enrollment Number will be generated → Login and apply as above.
  • Documents Required for Portal Registration (Both Portals):
  • Qualification Certificate (ITI/Diploma/B.Tech mark sheet and certificate)
  • Government ID Proof
  • Aadhaar card (mandatory for NAPS portal - Aadhaar verification required)
  • Bank Passbook (for stipend disbursement)
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Resume / CV
  • Documents to be Produced at Certificate Verification / Written Test / Skill Test / Interview:
  • Original certificates and mark sheets of qualifying examination (ITI/Diploma/B.Tech) - plus self-attested copies
  • Date of Birth proof (original + self-attested copy)
  • Caste certificate in prescribed Government of India format for SC/ST/OBC candidates (only GOI prescribed formats accepted)
  • OBC Non-Creamy Layer declaration (Performa-III) in addition to OBC certificate
  • EWS Income & Asset Certificate (Annexure-I format) for EWS candidates
  • Disability Certificate (Form-V for single disability / Form-VI for multiple disabilities, UDID/Certificate No.) for PwBD candidates
  • Equivalency Certificate from competent authority (if qualification is equivalent to prescribed qualification)
  • CGPA to percentage conversion proof from University/Institute (if applicable)
  • Any other document relevant to the category/reservation claimed
  • For NAPS portal queries: Contact RI Centres / RDSDE Trivandrum or check Candidate User Manual from the "Get Started Menu" on the portal homepage.
  • For NATS portal queries: Contact Board of Apprenticeship Training (BOAT), Southern Region, Chennai via email: [email protected]
  • Apply Online (Trade Apprentices - ITI): https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in/ | Establishment Code: E07203200009
  • Apply Online (Graduate & Technician Apprentices): https://nats.education.gov.in

Important Instructions

  • Upon completion of the Apprenticeship period, HOCL shall have no obligation to offer employment to any apprentice, nor can an apprentice claim the right for employment on the grounds of completion of Apprenticeship. Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing Apprenticeship Training under the Apprentices Act in any Government, Public Sector, or Private Organisation are NOT ELIGIBLE to apply. Such candidature, if discovered at any stage, will be cancelled. Applications must be submitted exclusively through the respective portal - NAPS (for ITI holders) or NATS (for Diploma/B.Tech holders). Applications through any other method or platform will not be considered. The category/reservation claimed during the initial online submission of the application only will be considered. Candidates must produce the relevant certificate in prescribed GOI format at document verification; failing which, their reservation claim will not be considered and they will be treated as Unreserved. Candidates must ensure marks are entered correctly in the portal. Difference between entered marks and mark sheet uploaded will result in summary rejection of application. For NATS portal applicants: percentage of marks in qualifying examination must be compulsorily entered in the "% of Marks/CGPA" column. If not furnished, candidature will not be considered. Call letters for certificate verification/written test/skill test/interview will NOT be sent by post. Candidates will be informed by email or through publication on the HOCL website (www.hoclindia.com). Candidates must frequently check their email and the HOCL website for updates. No travel expenses are admissible during the apprenticeship training period or the selection process. Accommodation will be provided if available as per company policy; subsidized canteen facility is available during office hours only. If at any stage any information furnished is found to be false/incorrect or there has been suppression of facts, the candidate shall not be considered for selection and candidature/appointment will be cancelled without notice. Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification. Only Indian Nationals need to apply. The number of training seats indicated may vary depending on future requirements. HOCL reserves the right to restrict/alter/cancel/modify the recruitment process if need so arises. Any legal proceedings arising out of this advertisement can be instituted only in the Courts/Tribunals/Forums at Ernakulam, which shall have sole and exclusive jurisdiction.
