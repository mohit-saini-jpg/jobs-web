NIPER JEE Admit Card 2026

The NIPER JEE Admit Card 2026 has released on 8 June 2026 on the official NIPER website. Candidates who have registered for the entrance exam will be able to download their hall ticket online once the link becomes active. The exam is scheduled for 13 June 2026 in computer-based test (CBT) mode.

How to Download NIPER JEE Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official website niper.gov.in/niperjee2026.
  • Open the NIPER JEE Admit Card 2026 link on the homepage or notifications section.
  • Enter your registration details, such as registration number and date of birth.
  • Submit the form to view your hall ticket.
  • Download the admit card PDF and take at least two printouts.

If the site is busy on release day, try again after some time.

Details Mentioned on the Admit Card

The NIPER JEE hall ticket generally includes the following details:

  • Candidate’s name.
  • Registration number / application number.
  • Photograph and signature.
  • Exam date and reporting time.
  • Exam city and centre address.
  • Course or programme details.
  • Important exam-day instructions.

Check every detail carefully. If any information is incorrect, contact the exam authority immediately before the exam.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates should carry the following items to the exam centre:

  • A printed copy of the NIPER JEE Admit Card 2026.
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence or Passport.
  • Recent passport-size photographs, if specifically asked in the instructions.

Without the admit card and valid ID, entry to the exam hall may not be allowed.

Exam-Day Instructions

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time.
  • Carry only allowed items and follow the instructions on the hall ticket.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators or other electronic devices.
  • Follow all instructions given by the invigilators in the exam hall.
  • Keep the admit card safe even after the exam for further admission steps.
