Karnataka State Open University has announced KSOU Result 2026 for multiple PG first semester programmes, publishing the provisional revaluation results of the October/November 2025 examinations for the 2024-25 January cycle and repeaters. The update covers I Semester M.A Education, I Semester M.Sc Environmental Science, I Semester M.Sc Zoology and I Semester M.Sc Biochemistry revaluation results. KSOU Result 2026 has been announced online on the official website - ksouportal.com - through the dedicated result portal where students can log in and check their revised marks after revaluation.

These results are labelled as provisional revaluation results, meaning that they reflect updated marks after scrutiny of answer scripts requested by students while internal assessment related queries must be addressed only to the Registrar Evaluation / KSOU Academic Platform, as mentioned in the university note. Learners should log in using their enrollment or registration details, download the revised marksheet, verify all components including internal and external marks, and contact the concerned KSOU authorities immediately if any discrepancy is noticed.

How to Check KSOU Result 2026

Follow these steps to access and download your KSOU I Semester MA / MSc Provisional Revaluation Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the KSOU result portal: https://ksouportal.com/results/ksouresult.aspx.
  • On the result page, select your Programme from the dropdown list, such as: I Semester M.A - Education I Semester M.Sc - Environmental Science I Semester M.Sc - Zoology I Semester M.Sc - Biochemistry.
  • I Semester M.A - Education
  • I Semester M.Sc - Environmental Science
  • I Semester M.Sc - Zoology
  • I Semester M.Sc - Biochemistry.
  • Choose the Exam Session as Oct/Nov 2025 and confirm the 2024-25 January Cycle & Repeaters where applicable.
  • Enter your Enrollment Number / Registration Number / Roll Number as required by the portal.
  • Click on the Submit / View Result button to load your provisional revaluation marksheet.
  • Carefully check your name, enrollment number, course, semester, internal marks, external marks, total marks, grade/grade points and result status (Pass/Fail etc.).
  • Download or print the provisional result for your academic records, scholarship or future admission processes.
