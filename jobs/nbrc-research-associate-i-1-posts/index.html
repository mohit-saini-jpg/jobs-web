A
Candidates must hold a Ph.D. in Science or Engineering, or an M.D./M.S. degree in medicine, with relevant research interests and at least one first-author publication from their PhD work.

A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General, OBC, SC, ST) for this recruitment drive.
