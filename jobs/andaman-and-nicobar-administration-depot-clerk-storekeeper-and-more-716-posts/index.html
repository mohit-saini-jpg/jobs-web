How to Apply

  • 1Recent passport size colour photograph in jpg/jpeg format (20 KB to 50 KB; resolution 200x230; plain white or off-white background; full face, front view, eyes open; without cap, mask or glasses/spectacles)
  • 2Signature in jpeg format (10 KB to 20 KB; resolution 140x60)
  • 3Other documents such as proof of age, qualifications, category, NOC etc. should be between 100 KB to 500 KB as instructed in the online portal
  • 4Educational qualification certificates and marksheets
  • 5Age proof certificate
  • 6ST Certificate in the prescribed format (Local A&N Islands ST only for reserved quota)
  • 7OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate - valid OBC-NCL certificate not older than two years from the closing date of the vacancy notice
  • 8EWS Certificate issued by the Revenue Department, A&N Administration
  • 9PwBD Certificate in the prescribed format (minimum 40% disability) issued by a Competent Authority as per form V, VI and VII of rule 18(1) under Chapter 7 of Rights of Persons with Disabilities Rules, 2017
  • 10Ex-Serviceman discharge/service certificate
  • 11NOC from employer (for departmental candidates working in Government Departments)
  • 12Online applications only - no other mode of application will be accepted; applications incomplete in any respect will be summarily rejected.
  • 13No documents are to be uploaded at the time of submission of online application; candidature will be accepted only provisionally and eligibility will be verified at the time of Online Document Verification.
  • 14Candidates must have their own active personal E-mail ID and Mobile Number which should be kept active throughout the entire recruitment process - admit cards, registration numbers, e-Call letters will be sent only on the registered e-mail ID.
  • 15Candidates are advised to check spam/junk box for e-mails; A&N Administration will not be responsible for bouncing back of any e-mail or undelivered SMS.
  • 16Candidates should apply in order of their preferences of posts; no request for change of preference of post shall be entertained at a later stage.
  • 17Once the online application form is submitted, no request for change/correction/modification (including change of category) will be entertained under any circumstances.
  • 18There is no provision of re-evaluation/re-checking of Answer Sheet/Scripts in respect of the TBT Examinations.
  • 19For OBC-NCL candidates, the certificate must not be older than two years from the closing date of the vacancy notice.
  • 20Only Local ST candidates covered under A&N Islands Scheduled Tribe Order 1959 are eligible for ST reservation; other ST candidates will be considered only for Unreserved Vacancies.
  • 21Candidates found fraudulently claiming ST/OBC/EWS/ESM/PwD status shall be permanently debarred from examinations conducted by the A&N Administration.
  • 22Banned items in examination hall: Mobile Phone, Micro Phone, Calculators, Digital Diary, Electronic/Smart Watch, Bluetooth devices, Book and Notes etc. are strictly prohibited.
  • 23The Examination will be held at centres located at South Andaman, North & Middle Andaman and Nicobar Districts; applicants should indicate preference of zone in the online application form. No request for change of Examination Centre/zone shall be entertained later.
  • 24Any further information/corrigendum/addendum shall be posted only on the official website https://erecruitment.andamannicobar.gov.in - candidates are responsible to check the website regularly.
