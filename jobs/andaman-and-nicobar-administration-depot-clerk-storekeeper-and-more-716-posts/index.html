FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for A&N Administration CHSL Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for A&N Administration Combined Higher Secondary Level Recruitment 2026 is 05 July 2026 (12:00 Midnight). The application window opens on 15 June 2026 at 12:00 Noon. Candidates are advised to apply well before the deadline to avoid last-minute technical issues on the official portal erecruitment.andamannicobar.gov.in.
Q2How many vacancies are there in A&N Administration CHSL Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 716 vacancies have been announced under A&N Administration CHSL Recruitment 2026 across 38 Group B (NG) and Group C posts in various departments. The number is tentative and may increase or decrease at the sole discretion of the A&N Administration.
Q3What is the application fee for A&N Administration CHSL Recruitment 2026?
A
Male candidates from General, OBC, and EWS categories are required to pay Rs. 100/- (Rupees One Hundred Only) through Net Banking, VISA/Master/Rupay/Credit/Debit Card, or UPI Payment/QR Code. Female candidates, ST candidates, and Persons with Benchmark Disability (PwBD) are fully exempted from payment of the examination fee. The fee once paid is non-refundable.
Q4What is the minimum educational qualification required for A&N Administration CHSL 2026?
A
The minimum educational qualification for most posts is 12th Pass (Senior Secondary Certificate - Class XII) from a recognized Board or University. However, some specialized posts require additional qualifications such as ITI/Diploma (Forester, Mechanical Foreman), specific Certificates of Competency (Master 1st Class, Chief Engine Driver, Marine Radio Operator), or Diploma in Computer Engineering (IT Analyst, Computer Assistant Gr.-A'). Candidates should check post-specific eligibility before applying.
Q5What is the selection process for A&N Administration CHSL Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of two tiers: Tier-I is an Online TBT (Tablet Based Test) of 200 marks covering English Language, General Intelligence, Quantitative Aptitude, and General Awareness (50 marks each, 120 minutes duration); Tier-II is a Physical Standard Examination/Skill/Trade Test applicable to specific posts only and is qualifying in nature. Final selection is based on merit in Tier-I, subject to qualifying Tier-II wherever applicable. No interview is conducted.
Q6Is there negative marking in the A&N Administration CHSL TBT Exam 2026?
A
Yes, there is negative marking of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer in the TBT (Tablet Based Test) Online Examination. Each question carries one mark. There are a total of 200 questions for 200 marks. Candidates are advised to keep this in mind while answering the questions to avoid unnecessary deductions.
Q7What is the age limit for A&N Administration CHSL Recruitment 2026?
A
The general age limit is 18-30 years for Group B (NG) posts (both Male and Female) and 18-33 years for Male and 18-38 years for Female for Group C posts. Age relaxation is provided to ST (5 years), OBC (3 years), PwD (10 years for UR, 13 years for OBC, 15 years for ST), Ex-Servicemen (3 years after deducting military service), and Central Government Civilian Employees (up to 40 years) as applicable. The crucial date for age calculation is the closing date i.e. 05-07-2026.
Q8How can I apply for A&N Administration CHSL Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online only through the official A&N Administration e-recruitment portal at https://erecruitment.andamannicobar.gov.in from 15 June 2026 (12:00 Noon) to 05 July 2026 (12:00 Midnight). No offline or postal applications will be accepted. Candidates must complete all six sections of the application form including payment of fees (if applicable) for the application to be considered complete.
Q9Where will the A&N Administration CHSL TBT Examination 2026 be conducted?
A
The Tablet Based Test (TBT) examination will be held at designated centres located at South Andaman, North & Middle Andaman, and Nicobar Districts. Applicants must indicate their preference of zone while filling the online application form. The A&N Administration will not entertain any request for change in Examination Centre/zone at a later stage. The exact date and venue will be intimated through SMS/e-mail to registered candidates.
Q10What is the ST reservation rule for A&N Administration CHSL Recruitment 2026?
A
Only Local Scheduled Tribe candidates covered under the category of "Tribes" as specified under the A&N Islands Scheduled Tribe Order 1959 are eligible to apply for vacancies under the Schedule Tribe (ST) quota. Other ST candidates from outside the Union Territory of A&N Islands will be considered only for Unreserved (UR) Vacancies. Similarly, only Local OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) candidates covered under the relevant Gazette Notification are eligible for OBC reservation.
