How to Check LNMU Semester 1 Result Online

Checking your result is pretty straightforward. Here's what you need to do:

  • Visit the official LNMU website at lnmu.ac.in
  • Look for the "ONLINE PORTAL" link on the left side of the homepage (under the big vertical blue section)
  • Click on it – you'll be redirected to the university's online portal
  • Find and click "View Result Semester-I 2024-28" in the middle of the portal
  • You'll land on the result download page for Semester 1

LNMU 1st Semester Result Download Steps (Easy Guide)

  • Go to lnmu.ac.in
  • Click on "ONLINE PORTAL" on the left side of the home page
  • On the online portal, click "View Result Semester-I 2024-28"
  • Under the Degree Semester-I Result section, enter your Roll Number carefully
  • Hit the Search button
  • Your result will appear on screen – download and take a printout for future use

Make sure you type your roll number correctly – even one wrong digit won't show your result.

Important Documents Required Before Checking Result

Keep these ready before you start:

  • University Roll Number (most important – you can't check without it)
  • Registration Number (sometimes needed)
  • Date of Birth (as per university records)
  • Admit Card (if you need to cross-verify details)
