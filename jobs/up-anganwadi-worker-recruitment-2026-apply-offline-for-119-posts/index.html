UP Anganwadi Worker Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Office of the District Programme Officer, Sultanpur (Uttar Pradesh) has issued a public notification inviting online applications for 119 vacant posts of Anganwadi Karyakatri (Anganwadi worker) under the Anganwadi projects operating in the urban and rural areas of Sultanpur district. The recruitment is being carried out on a contract/honorarium basis through a direct recruitment process, as per Government Order No. 2025/3313/58-1-2025 (1917687) dated 17 September 2025 issued by the Principal Secretary, Bal Vikas Seva evam Pushtahar, Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow.

Eligibility Criteria

Only female candidates are eligible to apply for the advertised posts. The candidate must be a resident of the same Gram Sabha (village) or urban Ward for which she is applying for the Anganwadi Karyakatri post.

Educational Qualification

Minimum qualification: Intermediate (12th) or an equivalent qualification. Maximum qualification considered for merit: candidates with qualifications up to postgraduate level will have their marks considered while preparing the merit list.

Age Limit

Note: The notification states the age must not exceed 35 years as on 01 July 2026; it does not mention any age relaxation.

Selection Process and Priority Order

The notification does not describe a written examination or interview. Selection is based on a merit list prepared from educational qualification marks, combined with a fixed order of social priority for candidates residing in the same Gram Sabha/Ward. The priority order, exactly as listed in the notification, is: Widow / legally divorced / abandoned (deserted) woman belonging to a family living below the poverty line (BPL) in the same Gram Sabha/Ward gets the first priority. If no such BPL widow/divorced/abandoned woman candidate is found, women belonging to BPL families in the same Gram Sabha/Ward are given priority. If no BPL candidate (widow/divorced/abandoned or general) is found, widow/divorced/abandoned women living above the poverty line in the same Gram Sabha/Ward are selected. Important Rule: One Family, One Post

As per Government Order No. 1/1092245/2025/3313/58-1-2025 (1917687) dated 17 September 2025, two women from the same family cannot be appointed as Anganwadi Karyakatri and Anganwadi Sahayika at the same Anganwadi centre.
