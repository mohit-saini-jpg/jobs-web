UKPSC Tax and Revenue Inspector Eligibility Criteria

Mandatory qualification: A Bachelor's Degree from a university established by law in India. Preferential qualification (advantage given if all else is equal): Candidates who have either served in the Territorial Army for a minimum of two years, or obtained a National Cadet Corps (NCC) "B" or "C" certificate. Additionally, under the applicable qualification rules, only one of the following conditions needs to be satisfied by the candidate (as detailed in the notification): passing High School and Intermediate (or equivalent) from a recognized institution in Uttarakhand, OR being registered with an Employment Exchange located in Uttarakhand, OR falling under specific service/residency exceptions listed in the notification for personnel serving in the armed forces, state/central government establishments, or permanent residents of Uttarakhand living outside the state for study/livelihood. UKPSC Tax and Revenue Inspector Selection Process 2026

Selection will be made purely on the basis of a Written Examination (Objective Type) . There is no mention of an interview or physical test stage in the notification for this post. The objective-type examination follows a negative marking scheme: For each wrong answer, one-fourth (1/4) of the marks allotted to that question will be deducted. If a candidate marks more than one answer to the same question, it will be treated as a wrong answer and penalized the same way — even if one of the marked answers is correct. No penalty is applied for questions left unanswered. In case of any printing or factual error in a question, the English version of the question will be treated as standard over the Hindi version. UKPSC Tax and Revenue Inspector Recruitment 2026 – 29 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Visit the official UKPSC website: psc.uk.gov.in or pscuk.net.in.
  • 2Carefully read the full advertisement, then go to the "How to Apply" section on pscuk.net.in and review the Advertisement Details, Important Dates, and Instructions for filling the online application form.
  • 3Click the "Apply Now" button.
  • 4Fill in the Registration Form with accurate personal details and create a password for login, then click Submit.
  • 5Review the Basic Information displayed. If correct, tick "I have verified all the details entered by me..." and submit; if incorrect, select "No, I want to change some details," edit, and resubmit.
  • 6After Primary Registration is completed, you will receive a confirmation message on your registered mobile number and email. Click "Click here to login."
  • 7After logging in, fill in Educational Details (High School, Intermediate, Graduate, and other qualifications) one at a time using "Add Education Details." Entries can be edited or deleted from the grid if incorrect.
  • 8Click Continue, then upload your photo and signature as per the specified guidelines. Re-upload is possible using the "I want to upload photo and signature" checkbox.
  • 9Tick the declaration confirming the photo and signature are accurate, then click Continue.
  • 10Review all the data displayed on screen carefully. Use "Back & Edit" to correct any mistakes.
  • 11Once satisfied, tick the final declaration and click the Final Submit button to complete the online application.
  • 12Click "Print Application" to download/print a copy of the submitted application for your own records.
  • 13If an error is found after final submission, a candidate can cancel the submitted application using the "Cancel My Application" button, confirm the declaration, verify via OTP sent to the registered mobile number, and then reapply. However, the fee already paid for the cancelled application will not be refunded, and no claim will be accepted later regarding a cancelled application.
  • 14The Edit/Correction link will open 5 working days after the online application closes, and will remain active for 10 days (12 August 2026 to 21 August 2026, as per the Important Dates table).
  • 15Only candidates who completed the application process can log in using their email ID and password to make corrections.
  • 16All fields can be corrected except the mobile number and email ID.
  • 17If a candidate changes category/sub-category during correction, the fee difference (if any) as per the notification will be payable; no fee is charged for correcting other entries.
  • 18Fees already paid will not be refunded.
  • 19No request for correction will be entertained after this one-time correction window closes.
