RGPV generally follows a pattern where odd semester examinations (held in November-December) have their results declared between January and February, while even semester examinations (held in May-June) have results declared between July and August. This pattern is based on the university's historical release trends and is not an official confirmation for the current First Semester Result 2026.

An exact, officially notified release date for the RGPV First Semester Result 2026 is Not Yet Announced on the university's official channels at the time of writing. Students should rely only on the official result portal for the confirmed release date.

Cut Off Marks and Qualifying Marks

RGPV has not released any specific cut-off marks for the First Semester Result 2026. However, as per the university's standard passing criteria:

These are general RGPV passing criteria and not a first-semester-specific cut-off. Any subject-wise or semester-specific cut-off, if announced, will be updated here.

How to Check the RGPV First Semester Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your RGPV First Semester Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official RGPV result portal: Click Here.
  • Select your course (BE/BTech) and the relevant semester as "First Semester."
  • Enter your enrollment number or roll number carefully.
  • Fill in the captcha code as displayed on the screen.
  • Click on the "View Result" or "Submit" button.
  • Your RGPV First Semester Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download and save a copy of your provisional marksheet for future use.

What to Do After the Result

After checking the RGPV First Semester Result 2026, students should:

  • Report any discrepancy to the college examination cell or the RGPV examination department without delay.
