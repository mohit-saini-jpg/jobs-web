FAGMIL Recruitment 2026: Eligibility Criteria

Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply. All qualifications must be from a recognized University/Institute. Candidates against whom a criminal case is pending in a court of law need not apply. Candidates must have basic knowledge of Computers, MS Office, and relevant specialized software. For the Liaison post specifically, the candidate should be a retired employee of a central government/autonomous or statutory body, having served not less than three years at the level of Section Officer/Senior Official (up to DGM level); preference is given to candidates with work experience with various Ministries in Delhi.

The engagement is on a contract basis initially for 11 months, which may be reviewed by the management for extension subject to requirement and suitability.

FAGMIL Expert Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

Selection is made purely through a Walk-In Interview. No written examination or online application process is mentioned in the notification. Candidates must: Bring a typed application form on A-4 size paper in the prescribed format along with all relevant documents. Register between 10:00 AM and 12:00 PM on the interview date; registration will not be allowed after 12:00 PM. Produce original documents for verification before being permitted to attend the interview. Note: For Senior Expert, Middle Level Expert, and Expert posts (S.No. 1 to 3), engagement is strictly on an "as and when required" basis, depending on operational necessity and project sanction under NMET norms. The actual number of experts deployed may vary, and this engagement does not confer any right to regular appointment.
