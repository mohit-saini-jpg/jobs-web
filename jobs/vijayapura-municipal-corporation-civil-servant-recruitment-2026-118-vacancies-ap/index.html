Job Overview

The District Urban Development Cell, Vijayapura (DC Office) has issued an official notification inviting applications for 118 direct recruitment civil servant (Pourakarmika) posts in Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike. Applications open from 10 June 2026 and the last date to apply (with attested documents) is 15 July 2026. The application fee is Rs. 100 and must be paid at the Municipal Corporation office. All applications must be submitted offline at Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike's Central Office.

Application Fee

All candidates are required to pay an application fee of Rs. 100 at the time of submitting their application. Applicants should note that the fee receipt issued by Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike must be collected and retained as proof of payment. Payment is made at the Municipal Corporation office itself. | Important: The application fee once paid at the Mahanagara Palike office is non-refundable. The receipt must be attached to the application.

Qualification / Eligibility

DetailsCandidates must satisfy all of the following eligibility conditions to apply for Vijayapura Municipal Corporation Pourakarmika recruitment 2026. Applications not meeting these criteria will be rejected at the screening stage. | The applicant must be a citizen of India. | As on the date of this public notification, the candidate's minimum age must be 18 years and the maximum age must not exceed 50 years. | Candidates must have previously worked in Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike as a Pourakarmika, Loader, UGDS Health Worker, Cleaner, or Health Worker on an outsourcing/daily-wage/contract basis for at least one year prior to the date of this public notification. | Must be mentally and physically fit to perform duties. Candidates declared medically unfit by the medical board will be disqualified. Candidates who worked as Pourakarmika on outsourcing/daily-wage/contract basis and died due to COVID-19 or other reasons while on duty — one family member of such deceased worker is eligible to apply. Candidates with hands-on local field experience in waste management, public toilet operations, and zero-waste management in the municipal corporation are preferred. Must have knowledge of Kannada language. Must not have been involved in any criminal case. Must not have been dismissed from any office/department for disciplinary reasons. Must not be a student (vidyarthi). Reservation benefits under SC/ST/OBC categories and other backward classes are not mentioned in this notification as per the direct (Nera Pavathi) recruitment process applicable here, and reservation provisions under that Act do not apply to this process. | Salary bank statement, ESI and EPF bank statements, or attendance records for those who have previously worked in higher-grade posts. Service certificate from the competent authority.

How to Apply

  1. 1The application process for Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike Pourakarmika recruitment 2026 is entirely offline. No online applications are accepted. Candidates must follow the steps below carefully to avoid rejection.
  2. 2Download or collect the prescribed application format from the official website www.vijayapuracity.mrc.gov.in, the notice board of Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike, or the District Urban Development Cell website www.vijayapuradudc.mrc.gov.in. Alternatively, contact the Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike Central Office to obtain the application form.
  3. 3Fill out the application completely in the prescribed format.
  4. 4Pay the application fee of Rs. 100 at the Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike office and collect the official receipt.
  5. 5Attach self-attested photocopies of all required documents, including proof of previous service (salary slips, ESI/EPF statements, bank statements, or attendance records), service certificate from competent authority, proof of date of birth, and any other relevant documents.
  6. 6Submit the completed application form along with the fee receipt and all supporting documents at Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike, Central Office within the prescribed dates.
  7. 7Applications must be submitted only at the Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike office. Applications sent to or submitted at district offices, planning offices, or any other location will not be considered.
  8. 8Ensure your application reaches the office on or before 15 July 2026.
  9. 9Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike, Central Office, Vijayapura.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 Q1. What is the last date to apply for Vijayapura Municipal Corporation Pourakarmika Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the offline application with all attested documents is 15 July 2026. The application window opens on 10 June 2026. Applications received after the deadline will not be considered.
Q2 Q2. How many vacancies are available in Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 118 Pourakarmika (civil servant / sanitation worker) posts have been notified under Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike as per this recruitment notification dated 06 June 2026.
Q3 Q3. What is the application fee for Vijayapura Municipal Corporation recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 100 for all candidates. It must be paid in person at Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike Central Office, and the official receipt must be attached to the application form.
Q4 Q4. What is the eligibility to apply for Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike Pourakarmika posts?
A
Candidates must be Indian citizens, aged between 18 and 50 years, and must have previously worked in Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike as a Pourakarmika, Loader, UGDS Health Worker, Cleaner, or Health Worker on outsourcing/daily-wage/contract basis for at least one year before the notification date. Knowledge of Kannada is also required.
Q5 Q5. Is there a written exam for this recruitment?
A
No. There is no written examination. Selection is based on a priority order determined by prior service experience, death-in-duty compassionate grounds, field experience, and Manual Scavenger identification, along with document verification. Tiebreakers are resolved by age or lottery.
Q6 Q6. How to apply for Vijayapura Municipal Corporation Pourakarmika Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted offline only. Candidates must download or collect the prescribed form from www.vijayapuracity.mrc.gov.in, fill it out, pay Rs. 100 at the Municipal Office, attach all required documents, and submit in person at Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike Central Office before 15 July 2026.
Q7 Q7. What is the salary for Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike Pourakarmika posts?
A
Selected candidates will be paid the minimum wage as revised from time to time by the Labour Department, along with PF and ESI benefits. The exact wage is subject to the Government's prevailing minimum wage notifications.
Q8 Q8. Are these posts permanent or temporary?
A
These are temporary appointments. Selected candidates will not be entitled to permanent employee benefits and cannot claim regularization or approach courts for permanent employment. They must submit a Notary Affidavit to this effect before joining.
Q9 Q9. What documents are required at the time of application?
A
Applicants must attach salary bank statements, ESI/EPF bank statements or attendance records (for those who worked in higher-grade posts), and a service certificate from the competent authority, along with proof of date of birth and any other supporting documents.
Q10 Q10. What is the official website for Vijayapura Mahanagara Palike recruitment 2026?
A
The official websites are www.vijayapuracity.mrc.gov.in (Vijayapura City Municipal Corporation) and www.vijayapuradudc.mrc.gov.in (District Urban Development Cell). The notification and application form are available on both portals.
