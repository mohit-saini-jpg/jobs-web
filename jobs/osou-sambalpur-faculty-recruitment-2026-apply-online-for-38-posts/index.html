Frequently Asked Questions

Q1Q1. How many vacancies are there in OSOU Sambalpur Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Odisha State Open University (OSOU), Sambalpur has notified 38 regular teaching vacancies, including 8 Professor, 7 Associate Professor and 23 Assistant Professor posts across various departments such as BBA, Commerce, Cyber Security, Economics, English, History, Odia and Political Science.
Q2Q2. What are the application dates for OSOU Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application portal opens on 11 May 2026 and the last date to apply online is 15 June 2026 (midnight), while the hard copy of the application with documents must reach OSOU by 22 June 2026 (5:30 PM) via Speed Post.
Q3Q3. What are the pay scales for OSOU Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor?
A
As per the 7th CPC, OSOU offers Pay Level‑14 for Professor, Pay Level‑13A for Associate Professor, and Pay Level‑10 for Assistant Professor, along with other allowances and benefits as per Government of Odisha and university rules.
Q4Q4. Which departments have vacancies in OSOU Sambalpur Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Faculty vacancies are available in BBA, Commerce, Cyber Security, Economics, English, History, Odia and Political Science, with department‑wise and category‑wise posts clearly specified for Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor levels in the notification.
Q5Q5. What is the maximum age limit for OSOU Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
For all teaching posts in OSOU, the maximum age limit is 60 years as on the last date of application, and the current superannuation age for university teachers under Government of Odisha norms is also 60 years.
Q6Q6. What is the eligibility criteria for Assistant Professor in OSOU Sambalpur 2026?
A
Assistant Professor candidates must have a Master’s degree with at least 55% marks (50% for SC/ST/OBC‑NCL/PwD) in the relevant subject and must have cleared UGC/CSIR NET or SET/SLET, or hold a Ph.D. in accordance with UGC Ph.D. Regulations, which can exempt NET as per UGC Notification 18 July 2018.
Q7Q7. What is the eligibility criteria for Associate Professor in OSOU Recruitment 2026?
A
Associate Professors must possess a Ph.D. in the concerned/allied/relevant discipline, a Master’s degree with 55% marks, at least 8 years of teaching/research experience equivalent to Assistant Professor, a minimum of 7 publications in peer‑reviewed or UGC‑listed journals, and a research score of 75 as per UGC Regulations 2018.
Q8Q8. What is the eligibility criteria for Professor posts in OSOU Sambalpur 2026?
A
Professor applicants must be eminent scholars with a Ph.D. in the relevant discipline, have a minimum of 10 years of teaching/research experience, at least 10 publications in peer‑reviewed or UGC‑listed journals and a research score of 120, or be outstanding professionals with a Ph.D. and significant contributions to the field as defined by UGC norms.
