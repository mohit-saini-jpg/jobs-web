BPUT Even Semester Result 2026 (Released) - MBA 2nd, 4th Semester & MCA Result Out

Biju Patnaik University of Technology (BPUT) has released the Even Semester Result 2026 for select postgraduate programmes. The results are now live for MBA 2nd Semester, MBA 4th Semester, and MCA, and students can check their scores directly on the official result portal at results.bput.ac.in. To access the result, students need to enter their registration number and date of birth on the portal.

Which Courses Have Been Released Under BPUT Even Semester Result 2026?

BPUT has published the Even Semester (2025-26) results for the following postgraduate programmes in the current phase. Students enrolled in these courses should check the portal immediately.

How to Check BPUT Even Semester Result 2026

Checking your BPUT semester result is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to access and download your scorecard from the official result portal.

  • Go to the official BPUT result portal: results.bput.ac.in
  • On the homepage, select the session as Even-(2025-26) from the dropdown menu.
  • Enter your Registration Number in the given field.
  • Enter your Date of Birth (DOB) in the required format.
  • Click on the "View" button.
  • Your BPUT Even Semester Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen.
  • Verify all details name, registration number, branch, and grades.
  • Save or print a copy of the result for future reference.

BPUT Revaluation 2026 - What to Do If You Are Not Satisfied With Your Result

If you feel your answer sheet was not evaluated correctly, BPUT provides a formal mechanism to apply for revaluation or rechecking after the Even Semester Result 2026.

Key points to keep in mind:

  • Applications for re-totalling or rechecking must be submitted within 7 to 15 days of the result declaration.
  • The revaluation fee is ₹500 per theory subject, or ₹560 if applying for revaluation along with a photocopy of the answer sheet.
  • Revaluation is not available for practical exams.
  • To apply, click on the "Online Exams/Revaluation" link on the BPUT official website and complete the form along with the payment receipt before the deadline.

Students who fail a subject can appear for the back (supplementary) examination conducted by the university in the subsequent academic cycle.
