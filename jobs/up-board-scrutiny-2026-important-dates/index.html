UP Board Scrutiny 2026 Class 10 & Class 12 Registrations are now open. Students can apply online from April 27, 2026, till May 17, 2026, by paying ₹500 per subject (written and practical counted separately) on the official UPMSP website.

The Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP / UP Board) has started the scrutiny (revaluation/re-checking) process for Class 10 (High School) and Class 12 (Intermediate) results 2026. Students dissatisfied with their marks can apply for scrutiny of their answer sheets. The process involves both online registration and sending documents by registered post. Strict rules apply — incomplete or late applications will be rejected.

How to Apply for UP Board Scrutiny 2026 (Step-by-Step Guide)

  • Visit the official website upmsp.edu.in.
  • Click on the link “Online Application For Scrutiny 2026”.
  • Enter your Roll Number and Centre Code (from admit card).
  • Fill the online application form and submit.
  • Generate/print the application form.
  • Pay ₹500 per subject via Treasury Challan.
  • Attach the original Treasury Challan with the printed form.
  • Send the complete documents by Registered Post to the concerned Regional Office of UPMSP (Prayagraj, Meerut, Varanasi, Bareilly, or Gorakhpur) as per your district.

Important Note: Both online registration and physical submission by registered post are mandatory. Applications without online registration or received after May 17, 2026, will be rejected.

Important Rules for UP Board Scrutiny 2026

  • Only students who appeared in UP Board 2026 exams are eligible.
  • Scrutiny is for re-checking of marks, not for re-evaluation of answers.
  • Corrections in personal details (name, DOB, etc.) have a separate process through school.
  • The final result after scrutiny is binding — no further requests will be entertained.
  • Incomplete applications or applications sent without online registration will be summarily rejected.
