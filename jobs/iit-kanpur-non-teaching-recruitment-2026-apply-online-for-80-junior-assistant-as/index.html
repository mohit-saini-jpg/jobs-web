IIT Kanpur Non Teaching Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Only Indian Nationals are eligible. All qualifications must be from a recognized University/Institution. Experience claimed must be post-qualification, full-time work experience. Only full-time work experience will be considered; the Institute's decision on relevance of experience is final. Applicants working in Government Institutions/PSUs/Government Funded Institutions must submit an NOC from their present employer along with vigilance clearance certificate at the document verification/JOPT/interview stage. Without NOC, they will not be permitted to proceed further. Candidates must apply and pay the fee separately for each post. In case of multiple applications for the same post, only the last application is considered; fees for earlier applications are forfeited. Admission to any stage of the recruitment process is purely provisional. Issue of Admit Card/Call Letter does not imply eligibility has been confirmed. The Institute reserves the right to decide the mode of screening, place a reasonable limit on the number of candidates called for the selection process, reject any/all applications without assigning reasons, revise/reschedule/cancel/suspend the recruitment process at any stage, and increase/decrease the number of posts. The Institute may assign/transfer selected candidates to any section/department within the Institute, including Noida Outreach Centre. Any attempt to influence the recruitment process will lead to disqualification. Any discrepancy found between the information provided in the application and supporting documents at any stage will render the candidature ineligible and lead to disqualification. No TA/DA is payable for written/skill tests. TA is reimbursed for Group A candidates called for interview (AC-II Tier train fare for Level 10–11; Economy class airfare for Level 12 and above). Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this advertisement shall be Kanpur (U.P.) only. IIT Kanpur will retain data in accordance with the Institute's record retention policy.

IIT Kanpur Non Teaching Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in IIT Kanpur Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 80 vacancies have been announced across 12 post categories — Superintending Engineer, Deputy Registrar, Executive Engineer, Assistant Executive Engineer, Assistant Registrar, Assistant Counsellor, Hall Management Officer, Medical Officer, Junior Superintendent, Junior Technical Superintendent, Junior Assistant, and Junior Technician — spread across Group A (Pay Level 10 and above), Group B (Pay Level 6), and Group C (Pay Level 3). Several of these are backlog vacancies (*) reserved for specific categories.
Q2What is the last date to apply for IIT Kanpur Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for submission of online applications is 03-07-2026 (11:59 PM). Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date to avoid the risk of network congestion. The age, qualification, and experience eligibility will also be reckoned as on this closing date. Applications must be submitted only through the IIT Kanpur recruitment portal (https://www.iitk.ac.in/recruitment).
Q3What is the application fee for IIT Kanpur Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026?
A
For Group A posts (Sl. No. 1–8): Rs. 1,500/- for General/OBC/EWS male candidates; Rs. 750/- for SC/ST/PwD and all female candidates. For Group B & C posts (Sl. No. 9–12): Rs. 1,000/- for General/OBC/EWS male candidates; Rs. 500/- for SC/ST/PwD and all female candidates. Fees must be paid online and are non-refundable. Separate fees are required for each post applied for; online bank/transaction charges + GST (if any) are additional.
Q4What is the age limit for IIT Kanpur Non-Teaching Staff Recruitment 2026?
A
The age limit varies by group: Group A (Level 10 and above) — up to 45 years; Group B (Level 6) — up to 35 years; Group C (Level 3) — up to 30 years. Age relaxations: SC/ST +5 years; OBC (NCL) +3 years; PwD +10 years (subject to maximum 56 years); Ex-Servicemen +3 years + service period. Regular IIT Kanpur employees may apply up to 50 years for Group B & C and 55 years for Group A. Age is reckoned as on the closing date 03-07-2026.
Q5What is the selection process for IIT Kanpur Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process differs by post group. Group A posts (except Medical Officer): Written Test + Seminar/Presentation before an expert panel + Personal Interview. Medical Officer (Sl. No. 8): Interview only (no written test). Group B & C posts: Written Test + Skill Test / Job-Oriented Practical Test (JOPT). All assessments are conducted in the English language only. The Institute reserves the right to decide the mode of screening and may shortlist candidates based on higher parameters. No TA/DA is paid for written/skill tests; TA is reimbursable for Group A candidates called for interview.
Q6What is the pay scale for various posts in IIT Kanpur Recruitment 2026?
A
Pay scales as per 7th CPC: Superintending Engineer — Level-13 (Rs. 1,23,100–2,15,900); Deputy Registrar — Level-12 (Rs. 78,800–2,09,200); Executive Engineer — Level-11 (Rs. 67,700–2,08,700); Assistant Executive Engineer, Assistant Registrar, Assistant Counsellor, Hall Management Officer, Medical Officer — Level-10 (Rs. 56,100–1,77,500); Junior Superintendent and Junior Technical Superintendent — Level-6 (Rs. 35,400–1,12,400); Junior Assistant and Junior Technician — Level-3 (Rs. 21,700–69,100). In addition, Transport Allowance, Dearness Allowance, HRA, NPS Employer's Contribution, Medical, LTC, and Children's Education Allowance are admissible as per GOI rules.
Q7Can I apply for multiple posts in IIT Kanpur Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, candidates can apply for more than one post, but must submit a separate online application and pay a separate fee for each post. In case of multiple applications for the same single post, only the last application with the registration number will be considered; earlier applications for the same post will be rejected and the respective fees forfeited. Each post has its own eligibility criteria, so candidates must verify they meet the requirements for every post they apply for.
Q8Is experience mandatory for Junior Assistant and Junior Technician posts in IIT Kanpur 2026?
A
For Junior Assistant (Sl. No. 11): The essential qualification is a Bachelor's Degree with knowledge of computer applications. No minimum experience is mandatory in the essential criteria; however, a minimum 2 years of relevant administrative experience is desirable. For Junior Technician (Sl. No. 12): The essential qualification is a Bachelor's Degree / B.Sc. / 3-year Diploma in the relevant discipline. Post-qualification hands-on relevant experience (2 years for B.E./B.Tech. holders; 3 years for B.Sc./Diploma holders) is desirable but not mandatory. Only full-time, post-qualification experience is considered.
