Short Information

The Defence Research and Development Establishment (DRDO DRDE) has published the Junior Research Fellow (JRF) Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill unlimited Junior Research Fellow (JRF) positions. Candidates within the age range of up to 28 years (relaxable for SC/ST by 5 years and OBC by 3 years) and holding a first-class M.Sc. in Chemistry or Life Sciences/Zoology/Biotechnology/Molecular Biology/Microbiology/Pharmacology/Toxicology/Immunology with a valid CSIR/UGC/DBT/ICMR JRF Fellowship are eligible to attend the walk-in interview.
