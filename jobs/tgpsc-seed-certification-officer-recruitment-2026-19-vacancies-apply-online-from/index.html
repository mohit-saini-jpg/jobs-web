TGPSC Seed Certification Officer Recruitment 2026 – 19 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection to the post of Seed Certification Officer is made purely on the basis of marks secured in a single Objective Type written examination, conducted either in OMR-based offline mode or Computer Based Recruitment Test (CBRT) mode. Paper I is bilingual (English and Telugu), while Paper II is in English only. Paper II covers General Agriculture, Agronomy, Plant Breeding, Seed Science & Technology, and Plant Physiology at postgraduate level. OC, Sportspersons & EWS: not less than 40% BCs: not less than 35% SC, ST & PH: not less than 30% Scores from multi-session CBRT exams are normalized using TGPSC's standard normalization formula, and the final merit list is prepared based on normalized marks calculated up to 5 decimal places. The examination will be conducted at Hyderabad (including HMDA jurisdiction), Karimnagar, Khammam, Warangal, and Nizamabad.
