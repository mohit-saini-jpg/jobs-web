FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for NIOT Consultants Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the offline application for the NIOT Consultants Recruitment 2026 is 12th June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well in advance to avoid any last-minute issues.
Q2What is the salary offered for the Consultant posts at NIOT?
A
The selected candidates for the Consultant posts at NIOT will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 60,000/- per month. This is a fixed remuneration for the contract period.
Q3What is the maximum age limit for applying to NIOT Consultant positions?
A
The maximum age limit for all Consultant positions at NIOT is 63 years. There are no specific age relaxations mentioned for any category.
Q4What is the application mode for NIOT Consultants Recruitment 2026?
A
The application mode for the NIOT Consultants Recruitment 2026 is offline. Candidates need to download the application form, fill it, and send it to the specified address.
Q5Are there any application fees for NIOT Consultants Recruitment 2026?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for the NIOT Consultants Recruitment 2026. The application is free of cost.
Q6What is the selection process for NIOT Consultants Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process for NIOT Consultants Recruitment 2026 involves two stages: Shortlisting of eligible candidates based on their qualifications and experience, followed by an Interview.
Q7What is the minimum experience required for the Consultant posts?
A
For Consultant positions, a minimum of 10 years of relevant experience is required. For Sr. Consultant positions, specific retired pay levels or equivalent experience is preferred.
Q8Which educational qualification is required for the Consultant (Works Management / Estate Maintenance) post?
A
For the Consultant (Works Management / Estate Maintenance) post, candidates should possess a Bachelor’s Degree or Diploma in Civil or Electrical Engineering, along with 10 years of relevant experience.
Q9Can retired government officers apply for these posts?
A
Yes, retired Government officers are preferred for these Consultant positions. Specific eligibility criteria for retired officers are mentioned in the detailed notification for each post.
Q10Where can I find the official notification and application form for NIOT Recruitment 2026?
A
The official notification and application form can be downloaded from the official website of the National Institute of Ocean Technology (NIOT), which is niot.res.in. Links are also provided in the 'Important Links' section of this article.
