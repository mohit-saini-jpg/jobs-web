FAQs

Q1What is the full form of OSWC?
A
OSWC stands for Odisha State Warehousing Corporation, a state government undertaking providing warehousing and supply chain services.
Q2How many vacancies are available in OSWC Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 30 vacancies are available: 11 posts for Assistant Superintendent and 19 posts for Warehouse Assistant.
Q3What are the eligibility criteria for Assistant Superintendent?
A
Candidates must have an MBA in Personnel / HR / Industrial Relations / Supply Chain / Marketing / Finance OR a B.Sc. in Agriculture, Botany, Zoology, or Chemistry with computer proficiency.
Q4What are the eligibility criteria for Warehouse Assistant?
A
Candidates must be any graduate with knowledge of computer operations (MS Office, Excel, Word, Internet).
Q5What is the age limit to apply for OSWC Recruitment 2026?
A
Applicants must be between 21 and 42 years as of 1st April 2026. Age relaxation applies as per government rules.
Q6How can I apply online for OSWC Assistant Superintendent/Warehouse Assistant?
A
Apply online through the official OSWC website (www.oswc.in) between 15-05-2026 and 14-06-2026. Submit your application form carefully and upload all required documents.
Q7What is the selection process for OSWC Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection will be conducted through a Computer Based Test (CBT) followed by document verification as per official notification.
Q8Is there any application fee for OSWC posts?
A
Yes, the application fee is Rs. 850 for Unreserved / SEBC / Ex-Servicemen / Departmental candidates, and Rs. 350 for ST / SC / PwBD candidates.
