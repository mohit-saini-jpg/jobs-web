How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online on the website https://careers.ntpc.co.in in the "All Careers" section using their GATE-2025 registration number. No other mode of application will be entertained. Before applying, candidates must ensure they hold a valid GATE-2025 score card and meet all eligibility criteria. The online application system will generate a unique application number which must be noted for future reference. Candidates must upload all required documents in the prescribed format at the time of online application. The GATE-2025 score card, if filled up correctly, will be reflected as appearing in the score card — no request with respect to change in any data entered will be entertained once the application is submitted successfully.
  • Documents to be uploaded (as listed in the notification):
  • Class X Passing Certificate or Marksheet for Name/DOB proof
  • PAN Card & Aadhar Card (Name & DOB should appear in conformity on all documents)
  • Scanned copy of original GATE-2025 Score Card
  • Scanned copy of latest photograph
  • Engineering Final Year or Consolidated Marksheet/Transcript clearly indicating percentage of marks in all semesters
  • Marks conversion formula from University/Institute (for conversion of CGPA/OGPA/DGPA/DSGPA etc. into percentage); conversion formula is not required if marks are already awarded in percentage
  • Valid Caste/Category Certificate in the prescribed format (for OBC-NCL/EWS/SC/ST candidates); OBC-NCL certificate should be in the current financial year format; EWS Income & Asset Certificate for FY 2026-27 based on income of FY 2025-26
  • PwBD Certificate if applicable
  • Apply Online at: https://careers.ntpc.co.in

  • Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply. Candidates must ensure they fulfil all eligibility criteria and norms mentioned in the advertisement before applying. No change in the Email ID will be allowed once entered; all future correspondence will be sent via Email only.
