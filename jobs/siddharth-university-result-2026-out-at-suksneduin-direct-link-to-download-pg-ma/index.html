How to Check Siddharth University Result 2026 Online (Step-by-Step)

Checking your result is pretty straightforward. Follow these steps:

  • Visit the official Siddharth University website: suksn.edu.in
  • Click on the “Results” tab on the homepage (under Academic Activity & Examination Monitoring System)
  • Under Semester Examination, click “Semester Exam Result”
  • Select your session: 2025-26
  • Choose category: Regular
  • Pick your semester: 1st / 3rd / 5th
  • Select your course (BA, BSc, BCom, etc.)
  • Enter your Roll Number / Enrollment Number and Date of Birth
  • Click “View Result” or “Submit”
  • Your marksheet will appear — download or print it for future use
