Patna University 2nd Merit List 2026: Released at pup.ac.in

The Patna University 2nd Merit List 2026 is now officially available on the university's website at pup.ac.in. Students who applied for undergraduate admission at Patna University for the session 2026-2030 can now check whether their name appears in the second merit list for UG Regular and UG Vocational programmes. This list is a crucial step in the admission process for candidates who were not included in the first merit list or who missed the first round of counselling.

What Is the Patna University 2nd Merit List 2026?

The second merit list is the second phase of seat allocation released by Patna University after the first round of counselling concludes. This section explains who this list applies to and why it matters.

The second merit list is published for students seeking admission to Undergraduate (UG) Regular and UG Vocational programmes at Patna University for the academic session 2026-2030. Candidates whose names appear in this list are required to complete the admission process — including fee payment and downloading the allotment letter — within the stipulated timeframe. Failure to complete these steps may result in the forfeiture of the allocated seat.

How to Check and Download Patna University 2nd Merit List 2026

Follow these steps to access your name in the Patna University second merit list and complete the required admission procedures.

Step 1: Visit the official Patna University website at pup.ac.in or go directly to pup.ac.in/result2nd.aspx.

Step 2: On the Second Merit List page, locate the table listing the merit lists available for download.

Step 3: Click the relevant link based on your programme:

  • UG Regular Second Merit List - Click the link corresponding to Sl. No. 1
  • UG Vocational Second Merit List - Click the link corresponding to Sl. No. 2

Step 4: A PDF file will open. Search for your name or roll number in the merit list.

Step 5: If your name appears, proceed immediately to pay the admission fee and download your allotment letter and application form using the link provided on the official page.

Important: The university has also provided a dedicated step-by-step guide (PDF) on the same page explaining how to pay the admission fee and download the allotment letter. Candidates are strongly advised to read those instructions carefully before proceeding.

What to Do After Checking the Patna University 2nd Merit List

Once you have confirmed your name in the second merit list, there are specific steps you must take promptly to secure your admission. Here is a clear breakdown of the post-merit-list process.

  • Pay Admission Fees: Complete the online admission fee payment through the official admission portal at pup.ac.in. The fee structure and payment link are available on the Admission Portal 2026.
  • Download Allotment Letter: After fee payment, download your official allotment letter from the portal. This document confirms your allocated seat, programme, and college.
  • Download Application Form: Along with the allotment letter, download and fill out the application form as required.
  • Report to the Allotted College: Carry the allotment letter, application form, and all original documents to the college to which you have been allocated for document verification and final enrolment.
  • Follow Deadlines Strictly: Patna University has set specific deadlines for counselling and fee payment. Missing these may lead to cancellation of your seat.

Patna University UG Admission 2026 - Important Notices

Patna University has released several official notices related to the UG admission process for 2026. Candidates should keep track of these updates to avoid missing any critical deadline.

  • A notice regarding Counselling for the Second Merit List for Undergraduate (Regular) Session 2026-2030, along with an extension of the first round of counselling, was released on 16 June 2026 for students who were unable to attend previously scheduled sessions.
  • Important Instructions regarding the First Merit List for UG Regular and Self Finance admissions have also been published and are available on the official results page.
  • Candidates applying for UG Self Finance programmes are advised to check the separate notice regarding the Admission Entrance Test for Undergraduate (Self Finance) 2026.
