Before diving into the detailed syllabus, candidates should understand the basic exam structure. The table below provides essential information about the CFA Level I exam 2026.

CFA Level I Exam Pattern 2026

  • Session 1 & Session 2: 90 MCQs each (Total 180 questions)
  • Each question has three answer choices (A, B, C)
  • No negative marking
  • Topics are mixed across both sessions
  • Ethical and Professional Standards has highest weight

  • Master Calculations in Quant, Fixed Income & FRA
  • Revise Formulas regularly
  • Take Full-Length Mock Tests under timed conditions
  • Stay Updated with CFA Institute Announcements
  • Maintain Consistency and Mental Health
