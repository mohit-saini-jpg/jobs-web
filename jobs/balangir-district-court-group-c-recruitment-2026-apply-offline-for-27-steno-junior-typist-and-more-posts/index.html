How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted offline only; no online applications will be accepted. Candidates must fill in Form-A (prescribed format given in the notification) in their own handwriting with full signature and attach all required documents. Separate applications must be submitted for each post applied for.
  • Documents Required for Junior Clerk-cum-Copyist, Junior Typist and Stenographer Grade-III:
  • Self-attested copies of +3 / Graduation certificate and mark sheet
  • Self-attested copies of +2 certificate and mark sheet
  • Self-attested copies of HSC (10th) certificate and mark sheet
  • Self-attested copy of Diploma in Computer Application certificate and mark sheet
  • Self-attested copy of technical certificate (for Stenographer Grade-III and Junior Typist)
  • Self-attested copy of Disability Certificate (for PwD candidates only)
  • Self-attested copy of Caste Certificate (for reserved category candidates)
  • Self-attested copy of Employment Exchange Registration Certificate
  • Self-attested copy of Odia pass certificate (if Odia not studied in course)
  • Self-attested copy of ID card/document (for Sports persons/Ex-Servicemen)
  • Self-declaration regarding marital status (one spouse living)
  • Undertaking regarding no criminal antecedent
  • Three self-addressed envelopes with Rs. 60/- postage stamp affixed on each
  • Three self-attested recent passport-size photographs (one affixed on application form)
  • Two original character certificates from two separate Gazetted Officers/Medical Practitioners/Sarpanch
  • No Objection Certificate from controlling authority (for Government servants and PSU employees)
  • Documents Required for Salaried Amin:
  • Self-attested copy of HSC (10th) or equivalent certificate
  • Self-attested copy of HSC mark sheet
  • Self-attested copy of Diploma in Computer Application certificate
  • Self-attested copy of Revenue Inspector Training certificate
  • Self-attested copy of Employment Exchange Registration Certificate
  • Self-attested copy of Caste Certificate (for reserved category candidates)
  • Self-attested copy of Disability Certificate (for physically handicapped candidates only)
  • Self-attested copy of Odia pass certificate (if Odia not studied in course)
  • Self-attested copy of certificate for special reserved category (if applicable)
  • Self-attested ID card/document (for Sports persons/Ex-Servicemen)
  • Self-declaration regarding marital status (one spouse living)
  • Undertaking regarding no criminal antecedent
  • Three self-addressed envelopes with Rs. 60/- postage stamp affixed on each
  • Three self-attested recent passport-size photographs (one affixed on application form)
  • Two original character certificates from two separate Gazetted Officers/Medical Practitioners/Sarpanch
  • No Objection Certificate from controlling authority (for Government servants and PSU employees)
  • Send Application To: The District Judge, Balangir, At – Civil Courts, Balangir (Patnagarh Road), Po/Dist – Balangir, Pin – 767001
  • Applications can also be dropped in the box kept inside the Civil Court Premises, Balangir (in front of the Help Desk) during office hours on working days. For updates visit: https://balangir.dcourts.gov.in

Important Instructions

  • The post name must be written in CAPITAL LETTERS on top of the application form and on the envelope. Separate applications with separate sets of documents must be submitted for each post applied for. Written examinations for all posts will be held on the same day; candidates applying for more than one post can appear for only one post's exam of their choice. No examination fee is required from any candidate. Applications received after 5:00 PM on 24-06-2026 or through any mode other than Registered Post/Speed Post/Drop Box will be rejected outright. Non-compliance with any requirement mentioned in the advertisement, including non-submission of any required document, will result in rejection of the application without any correspondence. Fraudulent certificates will lead to prosecution of the candidate even after joining. No TA/DA will be admissible to candidates for attending the recruitment examination. The District Judge, Balangir reserves the right to modify, revoke or cancel the advertisement/recruitment process at any time without prior notice or assigning any reason. The decision of the District Judge, Balangir shall be final as regards the entire recruitment process. Government servants and PSU employees must apply through proper channel with a No Objection Certificate. The engagement of Salaried Amin post is subject to the outcome of W.P.(C) No. 1273/2014 pending before the Hon'ble High Court of Orissa. Candidates are advised to regularly visit the official website https://balangir.dcourts.gov.in for updates on examination dates and other notifications.
