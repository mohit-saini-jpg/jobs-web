What Is the SAC ISRO Apprenticeship Recruitment 2026?

SAC ISRO's apprenticeship program is a structured, government-regulated training initiative conducted under the Apprentices Act, 1961. The Space Applications Centre at Ahmedabad - responsible for remote sensing, satellite communication, and space-based applications research - offers hands-on training exposure to fresh graduates, diploma holders, and ITI-qualified candidates in technical and non-technical streams. This is not a permanent job offer. The apprenticeship is a one-year training contract that gives candidates valuable on-the-job experience within ISRO's operational environment - a significant credential for any engineering or science fresher.

SAC ISRO Apprentice Recruitment 2026 – Post Codes and Disciplines

SAC has notified 16 post codes covering three categories of apprentices. Each category has distinct educational requirements and eligible disciplines. Graduate Apprentice (Post Codes 01–05) Graduate Apprentices must hold a B.E. / B.Tech. / B.Arch. / B.Com / B.Lib or an equivalent qualification from a Government-recognized University, Board, or Institute with a minimum aggregate of 60% marks. Technician Apprentice (Post Codes 06–08) Technician Apprentices must have a Diploma in Engineering or Technology in the relevant field with First Class from a recognized State Board of Technical Education. Trade Apprentice (Post Codes 09–16) Trade Apprentices must have passed SSC / Matriculation / 10th and hold an ITI (NTC/STC) certificate in the relevant trade from a recognized institute.

SAC ISRO Apprentice Recruitment 2026 – Walk-In Schedule and Venue

SAC ISRO Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

There is no written examination or interview for this apprenticeship. Selection is done purely on a first-come-first-serve basis, subject to verification of documents and eligibility. Candidates appearing earlier in the walk-in period have a higher chance of selection, given limited seats. If any discrepancy is found in name, age, qualification results, year of passing, or caste/tribe details at any stage, the candidature will be immediately disqualified. Candidates who do not meet medical fitness criteria at the time of joining will have their candidature cancelled automatically.

Important Terms and Conditions

Understanding these conditions is critical before appearing for the walk-in, as violations at any stage lead to disqualification. Apprentices will have no claim for regular employment at SAC-ISRO, any other ISRO Centre, or under the Department of Space after completion of training. The apprenticeship contract is registered under the Apprentices Act, 1961 and Amendment Act, 1973. Candidates in existing Government service must disclose this in their application. Seats are limited; early walk-in increases your chance of selection. All decisions regarding eligibility, selection, and joining rest with SAC-ISRO administration.

SAC ISRO Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for SAC ISRO Apprentice 2026?
A
The walk-in registration closes on 3 July 2026. Candidates must appear in person at Building No. 30-D, SAC-ISRO, Ahmedabad between 20 June and 3 July 2026 (working days only) during 10:00–13:00 hrs. There is no online application; hence no "last date" in the traditional sense - first-come-first-serve applies.
Q2What is the stipend for SAC ISRO Graduate Apprentice 2026?
A
The monthly stipend for a Graduate Apprentice at SAC ISRO is ₹12,300 per month. Technician Apprentices receive ₹10,900 per month, and Trade Apprentices receive ₹10,560 or ₹11,040 per month depending on the post code.
Q3Is there a written exam for SAC ISRO Apprentice 2026?
A
No. There is no written examination or interview. Selection is done on a first-come-first-serve basis based on document verification at the walk-in venue.
Q4Who is eligible for SAC ISRO Technician Apprentice 2026?
A
Candidates holding a Diploma in Engineering or Technology in Electronics & Communication, Mechanical, or Civil Engineering with First Class from a recognized State Board of Technical Education are eligible. The Diploma must be from an institute in Maharashtra, Gujarat, Goa, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, or Dadra Nagar Haveli & Daman & Diu, and must have been obtained on or after July 2023.
Q5Can candidates from Tamil Nadu or other states apply for SAC ISRO Apprentice 2026?
A
For Graduate and Technician Apprenticeships (Post Codes 01–08), only candidates who obtained their Degree/Diploma from institutes in Maharashtra, Gujarat, Goa, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, or Dadra Nagar Haveli & Daman & Diu are eligible. Candidates from Tamil Nadu or other states are not eligible for these posts. For Trade Apprenticeships (Post Codes 09–16), ITI qualification must be from Gujarat State only.
Q6Will I get a permanent job at ISRO after completing this apprenticeship?
A
No. As per the official terms and conditions, apprentices will have no claim for employment at SAC-ISRO or any other ISRO Centre/Unit after completion of training. The program is purely for training purposes.
Q7What documents are needed for the SAC ISRO Apprentice walk-in?
A
You need: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Date of Birth proof, 10th marksheet and certificate, all qualifying exam marksheets (Degree/Diploma/ITI), caste/category certificate (if applicable), Local Police Verification & Character Certificate (original), Blood Group Report, and SBI Bank Account details. Additionally, registration on NATS or Apprenticeship India portal is mandatory.
Q8What is the age limit for SAC ISRO Trade Apprentices?
A
For Trade Apprentices (Post Codes 09–16), the age limit is 18 to 35 years as on the date of joining. OBC (NCL) candidates get 3 years of relaxation, and SC/ST candidates get 5 years of relaxation in the upper age limit.
Q9How many years after passing ITI/Diploma/Degree can I apply?
A
Candidates must have passed their qualifying exam (Degree/Diploma/ITI) on or after July 2023, and must not have completed 3 years since passing as on the expected date of joining. Even if you passed after July 2023, you are ineligible if 3 years have elapsed by the time of joining.
Q10What is the contact number for SAC ISRO Apprentice 2026 inquiry?
A
For general inquiries related to SAC ISRO Apprentice 2026, call 079-26913021 during 10:00–12:30 hrs and 14:00–17:30 hrs, Monday to Friday.
