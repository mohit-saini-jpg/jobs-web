Thane Municipal Corporation Specialist Doctor Recruitment : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for Thane TMC Panel Doctor Recruitment 2026?
A
There is no traditional last date — the only mode is walk-in interview. Candidates must appear in person on 03 July 2026 at 11:00 AM at the Medical Health Officer's office, Thane Mahangarpalika Bhavan.
Q2How many vacancies are available in Thane Mahangarpalika Panel Doctor Bharti 2026?
A
A total of 58 specialist doctor posts are available across 15 medical specialities including Gynaecology, Anaesthesia, Paediatrics, Radiology, Cardiology, and more.
Q3Is there an application fee for Thane TMC Panel Doctor 2026?
A
The notification does not mention any application fee. Candidates are required to submit a filled form with documents — there is no fee payment involved.
Q4What is the qualification required for Thane TMC Panel Doctor Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must hold MBBS with a relevant postgraduate degree or diploma (MD/MS/DNB/DM/M.Ch.) matching the applied speciality. For Physiotherapist posts, MPTH or BPTH is required. Maharashtra Medical Council registration is mandatory for all medical posts.
Q5What is the salary for Thane Mahangarpalika Panel Doctor 2026?
A
Panel doctors are not paid a fixed monthly salary. Payment is on a per-visit or per-case basis, ranging from Rs. 500/- per sonography (Radiology) to Rs. 7,500/- per OPD visit (Urologist, Oncologist, Cardiologist). OPD payments are calculated at Rs. 100 per patient with a maximum cap of Rs. 5,000/- per visit for most specialities.
Q6Is the Thane TMC Panel Doctor post permanent or contractual?
A
This is a contractual panel appointment for 3 years. Being on the panel does not grant any right to permanent employment with Thane Municipal Corporation.
Q7Where is the walk-in interview venue for Thane Mahangarpalika Panel Doctor Bharti 2026?
A
The venue is: Medical Health Officer, Health Department, 4th Floor, Mahanagar Bhavan, Chandanwadi, Pachpakhadi, Thane – 400602.
Q8Is MMC registration mandatory for Thane TMC Doctor Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, Maharashtra Medical Council (MMC) registration certificate and its renewal certificate are mandatory for all medical specialist posts under this recruitment.
Q9Will TA/DA be paid to doctors attending the walk-in interview?
A
No. The official notification clearly states that no TA/DA (Travelling Allowance or Dearness Allowance) will be paid for attending the walk-in or for services rendered on the panel.
Q10Where can I find the official notification and application form for Thane TMC Panel Doctor 2026?
A
The official notification and application form (अर्जाचा नमुना) are available at the official website: www.thanecity.gov.in. The notification number is Thane-THAMPA/PRAO/Arogya/23E/2026, dated 24 June 2026.
