Selection Process

  • Selection is through document verification followed by interview. Candidates who fulfill the conditions mentioned in the notification and are found eligible after document verification will be called for interview. The University will not correspond with ineligible candidates. Candidates called for interview must appear at their own expense.
  • Stage 1 - Online Application & Shortlisting
  • Candidates must apply online at https://admin.unipune.ac.in/recruitment from 23-05-2026 to 01-06-2026. A separate application must be submitted for each department/post. Applications must be complete in all respects - incomplete applications and applications with incorrect/false information will be disqualified at any stage.
  • Stage 2 - Document Verification
  • Candidates who are prima facie eligible will be called for document verification. Candidates must submit the application form along with all enclosures to the Head of the concerned Department at the time of interview for verification. Original documents plus attested copies of all required documents must be produced. Candidates must NOT send hard copies to the Department or Administration Teaching Section by post or in person at any time before or after the interview.
  • Stage 3 - Interview
  • Candidates found eligible after document verification will be eligible for interview. Date
  • time
  • and venue of interviews will be notified by the concerned Department. Candidates appearing for interview at Ahilyanagar Sub Center (Code A-17) and Nashik Center (Code B-03) must note that interviews will be held at those respective locations
  • not at the main SPPU campus. All candidates must appear for interview at their own expense.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is SPPU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026-27?

The SPPU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026-27 is a contractual hiring process conducted by Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) for engagement of Assistant Professors on a temporary basis. This recruitment is issued under Ref. No. AT/NF/755 dated 22/05/2026 for various departments across multiple faculties. The appointment is purely temporary and valid up to 31st May 2027, with no claim for permanent employment.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in SPPU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026-27?

A total of 133 Assistant Professor (Contractual) vacancies have been announced across 35+ departments and 5 faculties. These include posts in Science & Technology, Commerce & Management, Humanities, Interdisciplinary Studies, and other academic units. Reservation is applicable for SC, ST, OBC, EWS, SEBC, PwBD, Sports Persons, and Orphan categories as per Maharashtra government rules.

Q3. What is the eligibility criteria for SPPU Assistant Professor posts?

Candidates must have a Master’s degree with at least 55% marks in the relevant subject from a recognized university. Additionally, applicants must have cleared NET/SET or possess a Ph.D. qualification as per UGC regulations for exemption. Certain relaxations in marks and eligibility are applicable for reserved category candidates as per government norms.

Q4. What is the age limit for SPPU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026?

The notification does not specify a strict upper age limit for Assistant Professor (Contractual) posts. However, age and eligibility will be calculated as on 01 June 2026, which is the last date of application. Any relaxation or additional conditions will be applicable as per Maharashtra government and university circulars.

Q5. What is the salary or honorarium for SPPU Assistant Professors?

Selected candidates will receive a consolidated honorarium of Rs. 50,000 per month for the contractual period. This payment is fixed and does not include additional allowances such as DA, HRA, or pension benefits. The engagement is purely temporary and does not confer permanent employee status.

Q6. What is the selection process for SPPU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026?

The selection process consists of document verification followed by an interview. Only candidates who meet eligibility criteria will be shortlisted for the interview stage. Final selection is based on interview performance and verification of original documents at the time of reporting.

Q7. How can I apply for SPPU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must apply online through the official SPPU recruitment portal at https://admin.unipune.ac.in/recruitment. A separate application is required for each department or post. Applicants must fill in details, upload documents, and submit the form online within the specified dates.

Q8. What is the last date to apply for SPPU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the online application is 01 June 2026. Candidates should complete the application process well before the deadline to avoid server or technical issues. No applications will be accepted after the closing date under any circumstances.
