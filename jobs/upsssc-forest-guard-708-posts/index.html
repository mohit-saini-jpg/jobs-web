How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official website upsssc.gov.in and click on the relevant advertisement under the Live Advertisement Segment on the Homepage.
  • 2Login using the OTP sent to the registered mobile number or email linked to your PET-2025 Registration Number.
  • 3After login, your personal details as entered in PET-2025 (name, father's/husband's name, mother's name, UP domicile status, category, EWS, horizontal reservation details, date of birth, gender, marital status, contact details) will be displayed automatically.
  • 4Fill in the mandatory educational qualification details as published in the advertisement, including board/university name, year of passing, certificate number/roll number, date of issue, and marks details.
  • 5Select post preference, additional/preferential qualification (if any), and provide permanent and correspondence address details.
  • 6The photograph and signature uploaded during PET-2025 will be displayed automatically; no changes can be made to these.
  • 7Read the Declaration carefully, select all checkboxes, enter the verification code, and click the "Save and Submit" button.
  • 8Pay the prescribed application fee through Credit Card, Debit Card, Internet Banking, UPI, or SBI e-Challan as per instructions given on the website.
  • 9After successful fee payment and reconciliation, download and print your application form and keep it safe for future reference.
  • 10All candidates belonging to reserved categories of Uttar Pradesh must mandatorily mention their category in the application form.
  • 11Candidates claiming reservation/age relaxation under more than one reserved category will be given only one relaxation — whichever is more beneficial.
  • 12Candidates who are not domiciles of Uttar Pradesh are not eligible for reservation benefits; such candidates will be treated as Unreserved (UR).
  • 13EWS category candidates must possess a valid EWS certificate issued between 01-04-2026 and 20-07-2026 (based on income for FY 2025-26, valid for FY 2026-27).
  • 14Divyang (Persons with Disability) candidates must update their disability category/sub-category in the application form as per the latest UP Government orders.
  • 15Ex-Servicemen who will have been discharged from military service by 20-07-2026 are also eligible to avail reservation benefits under the applicable Act.
  • 16Outstanding sportspersons of Uttar Pradesh must produce the certificate issued by the competent authority as per the prescribed format to avail reservation benefits.
  • 17No individual correspondence will be made by the Commission with any candidate; all information is uploaded on the official website upsssc.gov.in only.
  • 18Candidates who are in Central or State Government service must obtain a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their employer before participating in the selection process.
  • 19Any act of misconduct, use of unfair means, or malpractice during the examination will result in cancellation of candidature and may lead to DEBAR from all future examinations and selections by UPSSSC.
  • 20Male candidates who have more than one living wife are not eligible to apply; similarly, a female candidate who has married a person who already has a living wife is not eligible, unless exempted by the Governor.
  • 21UPSSSC reserves the right to amend, modify, or cancel the recruitment process at any stage without prior notice.
