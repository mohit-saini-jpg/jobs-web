What Is PTU Round 3 Counselling?

Round 3 (also referred to as a sliding/upgradation round in some centralised counselling systems) is conducted for candidates who either did not receive a seat in Round 1 or Round 2, or who wish to upgrade their previously allotted seat using their locked choices. Seats become available in this round due to upgrades, withdrawals, or candidates who failed to report within the Round 2 deadline.

Eligibility for Round 3 Seat Allotment

  • Candidates who registered for PTU counselling 2026 and locked their choices.
  • Candidates who did not report or confirm their seat in Round 2 within the given deadline (subject to PTU's carry-forward rules).
  • Candidates who opted to remain in contention for an upgraded seat rather than freezing their Round 2 allotment (where such an option applies - confirm the exact freeze/float rule on the official portal, as this has not been independently verified for the 2026 cycle).

How to Check PTU Round 3 Seat Allotment Result 2026

  • Visit the official counselling website: counselling.ptu.ac.in.
  • Log in to the candidate portal using your PTU application number and password.
  • Locate and click on the Round 3 seat allotment link/section on your dashboard.
  • Your allotted institute and branch (if allotted) will be displayed on screen.
  • Download the provisional seat allotment letter and save multiple copies/printouts.

Seat Acceptance Process

Based on PTU's established counselling process, candidates who receive an allotment are generally required to:

  • Log in to the candidate portal and view the seat allotment letter.
  • Pay the applicable seat acceptance/semester fee online in favour of the allotted college through the portal.
  • Download and print the fee payment/seat acceptance confirmation.
  • Physically report to the allotted institute within the reporting window for document verification.

The exact Round 3 reporting dates and fee amount have not been confirmed from an official notification; candidates should rely on the schedule shown after logging in to their counselling dashboard.

What Happens If You Miss the Reporting Deadline?

In PTU's Round 2 process, candidates who failed to report by the specified deadline had their allotted seat cancelled and forfeited the claim to that seat. A similar rule is expected to apply for Round 3, but the exact penalty/deadline for this round has not been officially confirmed and should be checked on the counselling portal.

Important Instructions

  • Always use the official portal - counselling.ptu.ac.in - to check your result; avoid third-party or unofficial links.
  • Keep your application number and password secure and readily available.
  • Complete seat acceptance/fee payment and reporting within the given window to avoid automatic cancellation.
  • If not satisfied with the Round 3 allotment, check the portal for information on further rounds or spot counselling.
