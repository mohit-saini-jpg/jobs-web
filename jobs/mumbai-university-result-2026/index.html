How to Check Mumbai University Result 2026 Online

The University of Mumbai publishes examination results as downloadable PDF grade card files on its official results portal. Follow these steps to access your result quickly:

  • Visit the official results website: https://www.mumresults.in/
  • On the homepage, look for the "First Half 2026" results section under "Online Results."
  • Scroll through the table to find your program by name or program code.
  • Click the link next to your program - it will open or download a PDF file.
  • Search for your seat number or roll number within the PDF to view your grade card.
  • Save or print the grade card for future reference.

Tip: Results are organized under two sections - First Half 2026 (Summer Session) and Second Half 2025 (Winter Session). Make sure you are checking the correct session for your examination.

Understanding Your Mumbai University Grade Card

When you download your Mumbai University result PDF, you will find a detailed grade card. Here is what the key components mean:

  • Seat Number / Roll Number - Your unique examination identity number
  • Program Code - The code identifying your degree and semester
  • Subject Names and Grades - Individual performance in each paper
  • SGPA / CGPA - Semester Grade Point Average and Cumulative GPA (for CBCS/CBCGS programs)
  • Result Status - Pass, ATKT (Allowed to Keep Terms), or Fail
  • Remarks - Any additional notes such as Distinction, First Class, etc.

Students under the NEP 2020 framework may notice slightly different grade formats compared to older CBCS and CBCGS patterns, as the grading structure is aligned with the new national education policy guidelines.

What to Do After Checking Your Mumbai University Result 2026

Once you have downloaded and reviewed your grade card, here are the recommended next steps:

  • Save a digital copy of your result PDF in a secure location.
  • Cross-verify all details - name, seat number, program - for accuracy.
  • Apply for revaluation if you believe your marks do not reflect your performance. Check the revaluation weblist on mumresults.in.
  • Contact your college or university if there are any discrepancies in personal details.
  • Plan your next semester enrollment based on your result and ATKT status.
  • Collect your original mark sheet from your college or university office once the physical documents are dispatched.
