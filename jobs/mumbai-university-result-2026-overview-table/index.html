Mumbai University Result 2026 - Overview Table : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 08/07/2026

The Mumbai University (MU) has released a fresh batch of examination results on July 7, 2026, on its official result portal, mumresults.in. This update covers postgraduate students from three distinct programs -Master of Management Studies, M.Com (Choice Based Credit System) under CDOE, and the joint-degree M.Sc. in Data Analytics. Students who appeared for these examinations, including those clearing backlog or supplementary papers, can now check and download their scorecards online.

Below is a complete breakdown of every result declared on this date, along with program codes and guidance on how to access your mark sheet.

1. Master of Management Studies (MMS) -Semester I Supplementary (NEP 2020)

Mumbai University has declared the Semester I Supplementary result for the Master of Management Studies (MMS) program under the NEP 2020 curriculum. This result is meant for MMS students who had backlog papers or reappeared for one or more subjects in their first semester examination. The program code for this result is 2120661.

Since this is a supplementary (ATKT/backlog) result and not a regular semester declaration, only students who applied for the supplementary exam under this cycle will find their names in this scorecard. MMS is one of the flagship postgraduate management programs offered through Mumbai University's affiliated institutes, and results under NEP 2020 typically display Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA) along with individual subject-wise marks and grades.

2. M.Com. (Choice Based Credit System) -Semester II, CDOE (75:25 Pattern)

The second result declared on 07/07/2026 is for M.Com. (Choice Based Credit System), Semester II, conducted under the 75:25 evaluation pattern for the July/January & December examination cycle, through the Centre for Distance and Online Education (CDOE). The program code for this result is 2C00562.

This result caters to distance and online learning students enrolled in the M.Com program through CDOE, a scheme that allows working professionals and remote learners to pursue postgraduate commerce education. The 75:25 pattern typically refers to the weightage split between the main examination and internal/continuous assessment components.

3. Master of Science (Data Analytics) -Semester I Supplementary (NEP 2020, Joint Degree)

The third result released on this date is for the M.Sc. (Data Analytics) program -a joint degree run by the University Department of Information Technology -for Semester I, under the NEP 2020 framework, as a Supplementary result. The program code is 1115761.

M.Sc. Data Analytics is a specialized postgraduate program combining statistics, programming, and applied data science, jointly administered by the University Department of I.T. As with the MMS result above, this supplementary declaration is specifically for students who had backlog subjects in their first semester and reappeared to clear them.

How to Check Your Mumbai University Result 2026

  • Visit the official result portal: https://www.mumresults.in/
  • On the homepage, locate the "Online Results" or "First Half 2026" section.
  • Find your examination name and program code from the table above.
  • Click on the corresponding PDF link to open your result document.
  • Use Ctrl+F to search for your seat number, roll number, or name within the PDF.
  • Download or print the result for your records.

Note: Mumbai University results are typically published as consolidated PDF grade cards rather than individual seat-number search tools, so students should download the relevant PDF and search within it for their entry.

Understanding Your NEP 2020 Result

Since all three results above fall under the NEP 2020 (National Education Policy 2020) scheme (except the CDOE M.Com result, which follows the CBCS pattern), here's what students should keep in mind:

  • NEP 2020 results display SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average), credits, and grade points alongside traditional marks.
  • Supplementary results are meant only for students who had ATKT (Allowed to Keep Terms) or backlog papers from their original semester attempt.
  • Students under the CDOE (Centre for Distance and Online Education) stream should ensure they check the correct examination cycle (July/January & December) applicable to their enrollment.

What to Do If You're Not Satisfied With Your Result

If you believe there has been an error in evaluation, Mumbai University allows students to apply for:

  • Revaluation of answer scripts (a fee applies, generally around ₹250 per subject, though students should confirm the current fee on the official notification).
  • Photocopy of answer sheets before applying for revaluation, if permitted for your program.
  • Grievance redressal, in case of discrepancies in the grade card.

These applications must be submitted within the window specified by the university following the result declaration, through the official process outlined on mu.ac.in.

Key Takeaways

  • Mumbai University declared three results on 07/07/2026: MMS Sem I Supplementary (NEP 2020), M.Com Sem II CDOE (75:25 pattern), and M.Sc. Data Analytics Sem I Supplementary (NEP 2020, joint I.T. department degree).
  • All results are available for download at mumresults.in under the First Half 2026 results section.
  • Use your program code to quickly locate your result among the many programs the university updates regularly.
  • Students with backlog papers should watch for supplementary result declarations specific to their subject and semester.

For the most accurate and up-to-date status of your result, always cross-check directly on the official Mumbai University result portal, as PDF links and result batches are updated frequently.
