UKSSSC Exam Pattern and Selection Process 2026

Selection is based purely on a written objective-type competitive examination, conducted offline or online, followed by document/record verification. This section explains the marking scheme and qualifying criteria. The written exam will be of 140 marks , of objective multiple-choice type, lasting 2 hours 30 minutes , covering General Knowledge and General Studies. A separate typing/shorthand skill test worth 60 marks will also be conducted; the detailed syllabus will be published separately on the UKSSSC website. Minimum qualifying marks: 45% for General and OBC category candidates, and 35% for SC/ST category candidates. Candidates scoring below this will be considered unqualified. Each question carries 4 answer options; 1 mark is awarded for each correct answer , and 0.25 marks (1/4) are deducted for each incorrect answer . Reservation Policy in UKSSSC Recruitment 2026

Uttarakhand government rules provide both vertical (category-based) and horizontal (special category) reservation, applied as per the roster supplied by the recruiting department. Key conditions to note: EWS reservation is available only to candidates not already covered under SC/ST/OBC reservation, and the EWS certificate must be issued between 1 April 2026 and the application closing date (6 July 2026), based on financial year 2025-26 income, and must remain valid for FY 2026-27. OBC certificates remain valid for 3 years from the date of issue and must be valid up to the last date of application. Reservation benefit under any category is granted only to permanent residents of Uttarakhand and only when claimed in the relevant column of the online application along with a valid supporting certificate. UKSSSC Recruitment 2026: 398 Kanisth Sahayak & : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Interested and eligible candidates can complete the Apply Online process on the official UKSSSC website in five simple steps between 22 June 2026 and 6 July 2026.
  • 2Registration & Personal Details – Register using a unique mobile number and email ID (each can be used only once) and fill in personal details.
  • 3Educational & Other Details – Enter educational qualification, category, district preference, and other required information.
  • 4Upload Photo, Signature & Thumb Impression – Upload the scanned photograph, signature, and left thumb impression as per the specified size.
  • 5Make Payment – Pay the applicable examination fee online via credit/debit card, net banking, or UPI.
  • 6Print Application – Download and print a copy of the final submitted application for personal record; it does not need to be submitted to the Commission at this stage.
  • 7Candidates get a one-time opportunity to edit their online application between 8 July 2026 and 10 July 2026 after the closing date, but corrections to name, parents' name, date of birth, and examination centre will not be permitted. Applicants are advised to Apply Online well before the last date, since heavy website traffic near the deadline may prevent timely submission.

UKSSSC Recruitment 2026: 398 Kanisth Sahayak & : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is UKSSSC Recruitment 2026?
A
UKSSSC Recruitment 2026 is a notification released by the Uttarakhand Subordinate Services Selection Commission on 15 June 2026 to fill 398 posts of Kanisth Sahayak and Stenographer Grade-1/Personal Assistant in the Civil and Family Courts under the Nainital High Court, Uttarakhand.
Q2How many total vacancies are there in UKSSSC Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 398 total vacancies: 259 posts of Kanisth Sahayak (230 in Civil Courts and 29 in Family Courts) and 139 posts of Stenographer Grade-1/Personal Assistant (128 in Civil Courts and 11 in Family Courts).
Q3What is the last date to apply online for UKSSSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application process starts on 22 June 2026, and the last date to submit the application is 6 July 2026.
Q4What is the age limit for UKSSSC Kanisth Sahayak and Stenographer posts?
A
Candidates must be between 21 and 35 years of age as on 1 January 2026, with age relaxation available for SC/ST/OBC, persons with disabilities, freedom fighter dependents, and ex-servicemen of Uttarakhand as per government rules.
Q5What is the educational qualification for UKSSSC Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must hold a graduate degree from a recognised university, with good knowledge of Hindi and English. Kanisth Sahayak posts require a Hindi typing speed of 25 words per minute, while Stenographer posts require 80 words per minute in Hindi shorthand and 25 words per minute in Hindi typing.
Q6What is the application fee for UKSSSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The fee is Rs 300 for Unreserved/OBC candidates, Rs 150 for SC/ST/EWS and disabled candidates of Uttarakhand, and Rs 0 (no fee) for orphan candidates.

Kanisth Sahayak posts carry a pay scale of Rs 21,700–69,100 (Level-3). Stenographer Grade-1/Personal Assistant posts carry Rs 29,200–92,300 (Level-5) in Civil Courts and Rs 44,900–1,42,400 (Level-7) in Family Courts.
Q8What is the selection process for UKSSSC Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection is based on a 140-mark written objective-type competitive examination covering General Knowledge and General Studies, followed by a 60-mark qualifying typing/shorthand test and document verification.
Q9How can I apply online for UKSSSC Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can Apply Online at www.sssc.uk.gov.in by completing five steps: registration, filling educational details, uploading photo/signature/thumb impression, making the fee payment, and printing the final application.
