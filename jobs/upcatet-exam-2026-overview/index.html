How to Apply Online for UPCATET 2026

This section serves informational + navigational intent by guiding users to the official portal and explaining the workflow. UPCATET 2026 application form is available in online mode only on the official portal upcatetexam.org during the notified dates. Candidates must complete registration, fill the form, upload documents and pay the exam fee before the last date.

Step‑by‑Step Process to Fill UPCATET 2026 Form

  • Visit the official website: Go to the official UPCATET website (upcatetexam.org) and click on “New Registration” for UPCATET 2026.
  • Complete new registration: Enter your name, date of birth, mobile number, email ID and other basic details, then verify via OTP to generate your Registration Number and password.
  • Login and fill application form: Login with your Registration Number and password, then fill in personal details, address, educational qualifications, category, domicile and course group details carefully.
  • Upload required documents: Upload scanned images of your recent passport-size photograph, signature and relevant certificates in the prescribed size and format mentioned in the instructions.
  • Pay the application fee: Pay the UPCATET 2026 application fee online through debit card, credit card, net banking or UPI and ensure the transaction is successfully completed before 1 May 2026 (5:00 PM).
  • Final submission and printout: Submit the application form, then download and print the confirmation page and filled form for future reference, counselling and document verification.
