AP EAMCET Result 2026 - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 30/06/2026

The AP EAMCET Result 2026 will be released by Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) Kakinada on June 30, 2026. Candidates can check their AP EAMCET 2026 result using their hall ticket number and registration number on the official website, cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET. The result is released in the form of a rank card containing scores, ranks, and qualifying status, and candidates who clear the exam become eligible for AP EAMCET counselling.

How to Check AP EAMCET Result 2026 by Hall Ticket Number

This section explains the exact process to view and download the AP EAMCET rank card using login credentials once the result link goes live.

Candidates can download their AP EAMCET 2026 rank card by following these steps:

  • Visit the official website at cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET.
  • Click on the "AP EAPCET Rank Card" or result link on the homepage.
  • Enter your AP EAMCET registration number and hall ticket number.
  • Click on "View Rank Card."
  • Your AP EAMCET rank card will appear on the screen.
  • Download the rank card and save a printout for future reference, including counselling.

What Details Appear on the AP EAMCET 2026 Rank Card?

This section lists the information that will be printed on the rank card so candidates know what to verify once they download it.

The AP EAMCET 2026 rank card will include the following details:

  • Candidate's name
  • Father's name
  • Gender
  • Category
  • Local area status
  • Registration number
  • Hall ticket number
  • Stream (Engineering / Agriculture & Pharmacy)
  • AP EAMCET qualifying status
  • Subject-wise scores
  • AP EAMCET weightage
  • Group total and maximum scores
  • Combined score
  • Intermediate percentage
  • Overall secured marks
  • AP EAMCET rank

What Happens After the AP EAMCET Result Is Declared?

This section briefly explains the next step for candidates after checking their result.

Candidates who qualify in the AP EAMCET 2026 exam become eligible to participate in the AP EAMCET counselling process, through which they can apply for seats in engineering, agriculture, and pharmacy courses at participating colleges in Andhra Pradesh based on their rank, category, and local area status.
