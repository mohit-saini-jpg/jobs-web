Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the DDMCH Keonjhar Recruitment 2026?

The application process is through a walk-in interview. The walk-in interview is scheduled for 29 May 2026. Candidates should report at the venue with all required documents on this date.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for the Asst Surgeon / Medical Officer posts?

There are a total of 9 vacancies available for the Asst Surgeon / Medical Officer positions at Dharanidhar Medical College & Hospital Keonjhar.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Asst Surgeon post?

For the Asst Surgeon post, candidates must possess an MBBS Degree or equivalent recognized by MCI/NMC, have completed one year of rotating internship, and preferably have 6 months of experience in critical care management with ICU facilities.

Q4. What is the salary offered for these positions?

The consolidated salary offered for both Asst Surgeon and Medical Officer posts is Rs. 80,000 per month.

Q5. What is the age limit for the Medical Officer post?

The age limit for the Medical Officer post is less than 65 years.

Q6. Is there any application fee for DDMCH Keonjhar Recruitment 2026?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment.

Q7. What is the selection process for these contractual posts?

The selection process includes a Walk-in Interview, Document Verification, Provisional Merit List preparation, Grievance Redressal, Final Merit List, Option Form Submission, and finally, the Appointment Order.

Q8. Where is the walk-in interview scheduled to be held?

The walk-in interview will be held at Dharanidhar Medical College & Hospital Keonjhar, Odisha, 758001.
