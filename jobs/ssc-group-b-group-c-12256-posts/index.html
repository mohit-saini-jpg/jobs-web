How to Apply

  • 1Generate One-Time Registration (OTR) at https://ssc.gov.in (mandatory; use Aadhaar-based authentication if possible)
  • 2Complete OTR profile and lock it
  • 3Fill the Common Application Form (CAF) including live photograph capture via camera
  • 4Upload scanned signature (JPEG/JPG, 10–20 KB, 6.0 cm × 2.0 cm)
  • 5Fill the Examination Specific Module (post preferences, exam centre choices - 3 centres within same region in order of priority)
  • 6Pay application fee of Rs. 100/- online (if applicable) by 23-06-2026
  • 7Preview/Print the application to verify all details before final submission
  • 8Use the correction window (29-06 to 01-07-2026) if any correction is needed (chargeable)
  • 9Keep printout of application and all documents for at least 3 years
  • 10Matriculation/Secondary School Certificate (proof of date of birth)
  • 11Mark sheets for all years/semesters of Graduation + Provisional/Degree Certificate
  • 12SC/ST Certificate in prescribed format (Annexure of the notification)
  • 13OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in prescribed format (Annexure-X); must be valid as on closing date of application
  • 14EWS Income & Asset Certificate valid for FY 2026–27 (issued based on income for FY 2025–26) in prescribed format (Annexure-XI)
  • 15PwBD Disability Certificate from competent authority specifying type of disability (as per Annexure-XII to XIII)
  • 16Ex-Serviceman discharge certificate and other relevant ESM documents
  • 17Equivalence certificate (for equivalent qualifications other than standard degrees)
  • 18DEB/UGC approval letter (for Distance Learning degree holders)
  • 19Certificate of proficiency in State Regional/official language (for AAO/Assistant Audit Officer State Cadre)
  • 20Final allotment is based on merit-cum-preference, and no alternate post will be offered if a candidate fails post-specific standards after allotment.
  • 21OBC candidates claiming reservation must not belong to the creamy layer as on the closing date of application.
  • 22EWS certificate must be valid for FY 2026–27 and should be based on income for FY 2025–26.
  • 23PwBD candidates must choose the correct sub-category in the application form because no later change is allowed.
  • 24Scribe rules are strict: the scribe must complete OTR, be Aadhaar-authenticated, and cannot assist more than one candidate.
  • 25Compensatory time of 20 minutes per hour is available to eligible PwBD candidates.
  • 26Tentative answer keys can be challenged online by paying Rs. 100 per question within the specified period only.
  • 27No re-evaluation or re-checking of scores is available at any stage.
  • 28Candidates should preserve the application form and related documents for at least 3 years.
  • 29Candidature remains provisional until certificates and documents are verified by the Appointing Authority.
  • 30Fraudulent claims of reservation status may lead to debarment from all SSC examinations.
  • 31Any update in exam schedule or process will be communicated through the SSC website only.
  • 32Applications without the required fee, unless exempted, or incomplete applications will be summarily rejected.
