How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official RPSC website: https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in and read the complete notification and Instructions for Applicants before applying.
  • 2Click on the Apply Online link or log in to the SSO portal at https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in using your SSO ID to access the Recruitment Portal (G2C portal).
  • 3If you are a new user, complete One Time Registration (OTR) by providing your name, father's name (if available from Aadhaar), date of birth, gender, and Aadhaar card / Janaadhar card details. Upload a live self-photo and scan of OTR documents.
  • 4After completing OTR, log in from SSO portal → Citizen Apps (G2C) → Recruitment Portal and fill the online application form using your OTR number.
  • 5Important: No amendment to name, father's name, date of birth, or gender can be made in OTR Profile once created without Aadhaar/Janaadhar correction. Ensure all Aadhaar/Janaadhar details are corrected before completing OTR.
  • 6During online application, a Live Photo will be captured via OTP camera. Submit the form only after verifying the live photo is clear.
  • 7Candidates must scan and upload thumb impression (left hand) and signature along with the online application.
  • 8Candidates who wish to avail scribe facility must mandatorily select the scribe option in their online application form.
  • 9All educational qualification and experience details as at the application closing date must be clearly entered in the OTR/application form.
  • 10After submission, an Application Number will be generated. If no Application Number is received, it means the form has not been submitted successfully.
  • 11Candidates must take a printout of the finally submitted application form for future reference.
  • 12For any technical issues during online application, contact the Helpdesk number on the Recruitment Portal or email as indicated on the portal.
  • 13Call Letter (Admit Card) downloaded from RPSC website along with valid Photo ID proof (Aadhaar / PAN Card / Passport / Driving License / Voter ID / Bank Passbook with photo) in original and self-attested photocopy.
  • 14Caste Certificate in prescribed Government of India / Government of Rajasthan format (for SC/ST/OBC/MBC candidates).
  • 15OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in prescribed format issued for the current year (for OBC NCL candidates).
  • 16Income and Asset Certificate issued by Competent Authority in prescribed format (for EWS candidates).
  • 17Disability Certificate in prescribed format issued by Competent Authority (for PwBD candidates).
  • 18Educational qualification certificates and marksheets (graduation / Law degree onwards).
  • 19Domicile certificate / Residence proof (for reserved category benefits applicable to Rajasthan domiciles).
  • 20Character Certificate from the last attended educational institution (at least "Good" mention required).
  • 21Police verification / antecedent certificate at the time of appointment.
  • 22Medical fitness certificate from a competent authority at the time of appointment.
  • 23NOC from current employer if already in Government / PSU service.
  • 24EWS / widowhood / divorce proof documents as applicable (court decree / death certificate).
  • 25Ex-Serviceman discharge certificate / NOC as applicable.
  • 26UIDAI / Unique Disability ID (U-D.I.D.) card, if available, must be produced before the Preliminary Examination.
  • 27Submitting false/incorrect information in the application form or failing to meet eligibility conditions is a punishable offence under Section 217 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023 (BNS). Such candidates may be debarred from appearing in the examination or disqualified during counselling/selection/appointment.
  • 28All revisions, corrigenda, and updates will be hosted on the RPSC official website only - candidates should visit regularly.
  • 29Candidates must not change their registered mobile number or email ID. Changes, if necessary, should be informed to RPSC at the earliest.
  • 30Candidates must apply under the correct category. Applying under a wrong category will not entitle them to any category benefit; the application may be treated as General category.
  • 31Candidates from other states belonging to SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS will be treated as General (UR) for this recruitment as reservation benefits are applicable only to Rajasthan domiciles.
  • 32OBC candidates from Rajasthan belonging to the Creamy Layer must apply as General (UR). Only Non-Creamy Layer OBC candidates with a valid and recent certificate are eligible for OBC reservation.
  • 33Category once filled in the online application form cannot be changed after the application submission deadline.
  • 34Not more than one application should be submitted. In case of multiple applications, only the last valid completed application will be retained.
  • 35PwBD candidates with temporary/likely-to-improve disabilities are not eligible for PwBD reservation benefits.
  • 36Candidates already in Central / State / PSU Government service must produce a NOC from their employer at the time of appointment, or an NOC obtained before joining.
  • 37Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification.
  • 38No request for change of examination centre shall be entertained. Travel expenses will not be reimbursed by RPSC.
  • 39The Commission reserves the right to cancel the selection process partly or entirely at any stage.
  • 40Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be as per Hon'ble Rajasthan High Court, Jaipur / Jodhpur as applicable.
  • 41Age relaxation provisions in Hindi language shall prevail over English translation in case of any discrepancy.
  • 42Candidates who are provisionally selected will be given actual appointment only after document verification and medical fitness confirmation. Provisional admission to examination does not guarantee final appointment.
  • 43Mobile phones, smartwatches, pagers, earphones, or any other electronic/communication devices are not allowed inside the examination premises.
