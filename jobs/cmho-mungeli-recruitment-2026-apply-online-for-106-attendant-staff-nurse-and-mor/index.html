CMHO Mungeli Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Online applications only - no other mode of application will be accepted; applications incomplete in any respect will be summarily rejected without prior notice. Candidates must be permanent residents (domicile) of Chhattisgarh; domicile proof issued by a competent authority is mandatory. All posts are purely contractual in nature under NHM Chhattisgarh HR Policy 2018. Contract period is up to 31 March 2027, extendable based on satisfactory performance and GOI/ROP approval. Candidates can apply for a maximum of three posts; combined fee for all applied posts must be paid in a single UPI transaction. Application fee payment via QR Code scan is mandatory; the UTR transaction number and date must be entered in the online form and a payment screenshot uploaded. Applications with incorrect or false information will be summarily rejected; candidates will be personally responsible for any such errors. All educational qualifications, experience, and registrations must be complete as on the date of notification. Experience certificates will only be accepted from Government, Semi-Government, and Government-funded institutions. Voluntary/honorary experience will not be considered. Experience marks will be given for a maximum of 5 years only. At least 12 months in a single organization counts as one year. For experience marks, the last 6 months' salary slips and appointment letter must be attached with the application; otherwise experience marks will not be awarded. Candidates currently working in Government/Semi-Government organizations must submit an NOC from their employer at the time of document verification. For OBC candidates, a permanent caste certificate from a competent authority must be submitted; if not provided, the candidate will be treated as Unreserved. For PwD candidates, a disability certificate showing a minimum 40% disability from a Medical Board is mandatory. For nursing cadre posts, only OL (One Leg) disability is eligible. Candidates who successfully qualify the Skill Test must appear for document verification with all original documents; without original documents, no candidate will be allowed to participate in the verification process. Posts marked with backlog vacancies are included in the advertised positions. Canvassing in any form will be treated as disqualification. All future correspondence regarding the selection process, shortlisting of candidates, and important dates will be published only on the official website https://mungeli.gov.in; candidates are responsible for regularly checking the website. For online application guidance/support, contact WhatsApp No. 9109198889 or Email: [email protected] during office hours.
