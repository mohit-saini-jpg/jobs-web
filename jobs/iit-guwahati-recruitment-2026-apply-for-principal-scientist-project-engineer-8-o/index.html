Job Overview

The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati has released a recruitment notification for 11 vacancies across 8 different positions under a research project on AI/ML and Post Quantum Cryptography at the Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering. Eligible candidates can apply online through the IIT Guwahati R&D Recruitment Portal on or before 10 June 2026. The hybrid interview (offline/online) is scheduled for 15 June 2026 at the E & ICT Conference Room, Technology Complex, IIT Guwahati.

Salary & Pay Scale

DetailsAll positions are contractual for 11 months and offer a consolidated pay that includes basic pay, House Rent Allowance (HRA at 20%), and a medical allowance of ₹1,250 per month. The salary may be revised upward by the competent authority based on the candidate's interview performance and relevant experience. | Principal Scientist: ₹1,69,979.60/month (Basic ₹1,40,608 + HRA ₹28,121.60 + Medical ₹1,250) Senior Project Engineer: ₹1,02,487.76/month (Basic ₹84,364.80 + HRA ₹16,872.96 + Medical ₹1,250) Project Scientist: ₹76,841.36/month (Basic ₹62,992.80 + HRA ₹12,598.56 + Medical ₹1,250) Associate Project Engineer: ₹43,250/month (Basic ₹35,000 + HRA ₹7,000 + Medical ₹1,250) Technical Assistant: ₹43,095.44/month (Basic ₹34,871.20 + HRA ₹6,974.24 + Medical ₹1,250) Lab Assistant: ₹28,246.70/month (Basic ₹22,497.28 + HRA ₹4,499.50 + Medical ₹1,250) Lab Attendant: ₹21,497.50/month (Basic ₹16,872.96 + HRA ₹3,374.50 + Medical ₹1,250)

Selection Process

1The selection for all posts will be based entirely on the candidate's performance in a personal interview. There is no written examination. Shortlisted candidates will need to appear for a hybrid interview - either in-person at IIT Guwahati or online - on 15 June 2026. The selection committee will decide the suitable candidate after evaluating all shortlisted applicants.
2Stage 1: Online Application & Document Upload (before 10 June 2026) Stage 2: Shortlisting based on qualification and experience Stage 3: Hybrid Interview on 15 June 2026 (Offline / Online) Stage 4: Final selection based on interview performance
3Note: No call letter will be sent separately. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the interview. No campus accommodation will be provided to selected candidates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1Q1. What is the last date to apply for IIT Guwahati Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to apply online and upload documents is 10 June 2026 (Wednesday) by 5:00 PM. Applications submitted after this deadline will not be considered. Candidates are advised to apply well in advance to avoid last-minute technical issues.
Q2Q2. When and where will the IIT Guwahati interview be held?
A
The interview is scheduled for 15 June 2026 (Monday) at 10:00 AM onwards. It will be conducted in hybrid mode — candidates can appear either in-person at the E & ICT Conference Room, Technology Complex, IIT Guwahati, or online. No separate call letter will be sent to candidates.
Q3Q3. What is the total number of vacancies in IIT Guwahati Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 11 vacancies have been announced across 8 posts: Principal Scientist (2), Senior Project Engineer (1), Project Scientist (2), Associate Project Engineer (1), Technical Assistant (2), Lab Assistant (1), and Lab Attendant (2). All posts are under a research project at the Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering.
Q4Q4. What is the salary for the Principal Scientist post at IIT Guwahati?
A
The total monthly pay for the Principal Scientist post is ₹1,69,979.60, which includes a basic pay of ₹1,40,608, HRA of ₹28,121.60, and a medical allowance of ₹1,250. The basic pay may be revised upward by the competent authority based on the candidate's experience and interview performance.
Q5Q5. Can I apply for more than one post in IIT Guwahati Recruitment 2026?
A
No. Candidates who apply for multiple posts will not be considered for selection for any of the posts. Each candidate must choose only one position and submit a single application. This is clearly mentioned in the official notification.
Q6Q6. Is there a written test for IIT Guwahati Research Posts?
A
No written test will be conducted. The selection process is entirely interview-based. Candidates will be shortlisted from the applications received and called for a hybrid interview on 15 June 2026. The final selection will be made by the selection committee based on interview performance.
Q7Q7. What is the duration of appointment for IIT Guwahati Research Posts?
A
All posts carry a fixed contract duration of 11 months. These are project-based positions under a government-funded research project. No campus accommodation will be provided to selected candidates, and no TA/DA will be reimbursed for attending the interview.
Q8Q8. What qualification is required for the Associate Project Engineer post?
A
Candidates applying for the Associate Project Engineer post must have either a Master's degree in VLSI, Microelectronics, or Computer Science, or a Bachelor's degree in EC/EE/CS with at least 3 years of relevant experience. The total monthly salary for this post is ₹43,250.
