ISRO SDSC SHAR Teacher Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1How many teacher vacancies has SDSC SHAR announced for 2026?
A
SDSC SHAR has announced 10 vacancies across five post codes: TGT Maths, TGT Science, TGT WET, TGT Telugu, and Primary Teacher.
Q2What is the last date to apply for SDSC SHAR Teacher Recruitment 2026?
A
The online registration window closes on 07 July 2026 at 23:59 Hrs.
Q3What is the application fee for this recruitment?
A
Candidates must pay Rs. 750 (Rs. 250 application fee + Rs. 500 processing fee) at the time of submission.
Q4Is the application fee refundable?
A
Yes, but only for candidates who appear in the Written Test. Women, SC, ST, PwBD, and Ex-Servicemen get a full Rs. 750 refund; other candidates get Rs. 500 back.
Q5What is the age limit for SDSC SHAR Teacher posts?
A
TGT posts (Code 01-04) require candidates to be 18-35 years, and the Primary Teacher post (Code 05) requires 18-30 years, both as on 07 July 2026.
Q6What is the pay level for these teaching posts?
A
TGT posts carry Pay Matrix Level 7 (Rs. 44,900 - Rs. 1,42,400), while the Primary Teacher post carries Level 6 (Rs. 35,400 - Rs. 1,12,400).
Q7What is the selection process for SDSC SHAR Teacher Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection involves a Written Test (CBT) followed by a qualifying Skill Test; final merit is based purely on Written Test marks among Skill Test qualifiers.
Q8Is CTET mandatory for these posts?
A
Yes, TGT posts require a qualifying score in CTET Paper-II (or State TET Paper-II for TGT Telugu), and the Primary Teacher post requires CTET Paper-I.
Q9Can a candidate apply for more than one post in this recruitment?
A
Yes, but a separate application and separate fee payment is required for each post; a candidate cannot submit multiple applications for the same post.
Q10Where will the Written Test be conducted?
A
The Computer Based Test will be held online across 22 cities in India, including Chennai, Hyderabad, Bangalore, New Delhi, Mumbai, and Kolkata, subject to candidate availability per city.
