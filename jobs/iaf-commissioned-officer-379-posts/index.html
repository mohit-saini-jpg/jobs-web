How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official AFCAT website https://afcat.edcil.co.in and read the advertisement and instructions carefully.
  • 2Click on the sign‑up/registration link and enter full name, mobile number and email ID, then verify via OTP on email and mobile.
  • 3Agree to the declaration, submit the registration form and obtain the system‑generated User ID and Password sent to registered email and mobile.
  • 4Login with User ID and Password, click on “Go to Application”, and fill personal details, communication details, qualification details, course preferences and other required fields.
  • 5Upload scanned images of recent passport size colour photograph (100-200 KB), signature (80-150 KB), thumb impression (50-100 KB) and live image capture as per specified formats.
  • 6Carefully verify all details using “Preview” and then proceed to pay the examination fee of Rs. 550/- + GST (for AFCAT entry only) through online payment gateway (Net Banking, Debit/Credit Card, UPI etc.).
  • 7After successful payment and submission, download and print the final submitted application form for future reference.
  • 8Recent passport size colour photograph (100-200 KB; JPG/JPEG).
  • 9Signature in black ink on white paper (80-150 KB; JPG/JPEG).
  • 10Thumb impression - Left thumb (male) / Right thumb (female) (50-100 KB; JPG/JPEG/PDF).
  • 11Live image capture via device camera at the time of application.
  • 12Printout of e‑Admit Card (AFCAT 02/2026).
  • 13Original Aadhaar Card and a second photo ID (PAN/Passport/Driving Licence/Voter ID etc.).
  • 14Two passport size colour photographs (same as uploaded).
  • 15Original and photocopies of Matriculation and 10+2 certificates and mark sheets.
  • 16Original/provisional Graduation/PG degree and mark sheets (for AFSB).
  • 17Certificate from College Principal for final year candidates specifying aggregate percentage and likely result date.
  • 18Original NCC ‘C’ Certificate (for NCC Special Entry) and GATE documents (for GATE Score Entry).
  • 19NOC from employer for Govt/PSU/Armed Forces personnel, if applicable.
  • 20Candidates must ensure eligibility for the branch(es) they apply for; incorrect information at any stage leads to cancellation of candidature.
  • 21Use of unfair means or possession of prohibited items (mobile phones, smart watches, electronic devices, study material etc.) inside the exam hall will result in cancellation and debarment from future AFCATs.
  • 22No request for change of exam centre/date/slot will be entertained; centres may be changed by IAF based on administrative reasons.
  • 23Serving airmen must apply online like other candidates and also follow service procedures as per AFO 11/2015.
  • 24Use/possession of narcotics is strictly banned; candidates may be tested for drugs during medicals and training, and those found positive are liable for termination.
  • 25Permanent tattoos are permitted only on the inner forearms and back of hands (with conditions); non‑permissible tattoos or those with offensive/extremist content lead to rejection.
