Qualification / Eligibility

Teaching Assistant (M.Sc./M.Phil): 1st Class or an equivalent CGPA (Min 6.5 on the scale of 10) in M.Sc./ M. Phil in Chemistry/Applied Chemistry/Industrial Chemistry with a good academic record. Teaching Assistant (Ph.D.): Ph.D. in Chemistry/Applied Chemistry/Industrial Chemistry with a good academic record and 1st class or an equivalent CGPA (Min 6.5 on the scale of 10) in M.Sc. (Chemistry/Applied Chemistry/Industrial Chemistry).
