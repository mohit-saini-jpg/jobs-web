FAQs

Q1How many MTS vacancies are there in Indian Air Force 2026?
A
The Indian Air Force has announced 6 MTS (Multi-Tasking Staff) vacancies at HQ Maintenance Command, Bangalore under Advertisement No. 02/2026. The category-wise distribution is: UR-3, OBC-2, EWS-1.
Q2What is the last date to apply for IAF MTS Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date is 30 days from the publication date of the advertisement in Employment News, which was June 27, 2026. This puts the deadline at approximately July 27, 2026. Applications must physically reach the unit by this date.
Q3What is the educational qualification for IAF MTS post 2026?
A
The essential qualification is Matriculation (Class 10 pass) or equivalent from a recognized University or Board. Desirably, one year of experience as a Watchman, Lascar, Gestetner Operator, or Gardener is preferred but not mandatory.
Q4What is the age limit for IAF Group C MTS 2026?
A
The age limit is 18 to 25 years as on the last date of receipt of applications. Age relaxation is available for OBC (3 years), SC/ST (5 years), PwBD, Ex-Servicemen, and Departmental Employees as per government rules.
Q5Is there an interview for IAF MTS recruitment?
A
No. The selection for IAF MTS 2026 is based purely on the written test. There is no interview. Candidates must perform well in the written test covering General Intelligence, Numerical Aptitude, General English, and General Awareness.
Q6Can I apply online for IAF MTS Recruitment 2026?
A
No. This recruitment requires an offline application by post. There is no online application mode. Candidates must fill the Annexure-I proforma, attach required documents, and send it by post to Commandant, IAM, IAF, Vimanapura PO, Bangalore – 560017.
Q7What is the pay scale for IAF MTS (Group C) post?
A
The IAF MTS post carries Level-1 pay as per the 7th Central Pay Commission (CPC) Pay Matrix. In addition to the basic pay, central government allowances such as DA, HRA, and transport allowance are applicable.
Q8Where is the posting location for IAF MTS Recruitment 2026?
A
The posting location is HQ Maintenance Command, IAF, Vimanapura PO, Bangalore – 560017. However, since selected candidates are subject to All India Service Liability, they may be posted anywhere in India as per IAF requirements.
Q9What documents are needed for IAF MTS application 2026?
A
Key documents include: self-attested copies of Class 10 marksheet/certificate, age proof (Birth Certificate), caste certificate (SC/ST/OBC), EWS certificate, PwBD certificate if applicable, Ex-Serviceman discharge certificate if applicable, two passport-size photographs, and a self-addressed envelope with Rs. 10/- stamp.
Q10Will TA/DA be provided for attending the IAF MTS written test?
A
No. The official notification clearly states that no TA/DA will be paid to candidates for attending the written test. Candidates must arrange their own travel and accommodation.
