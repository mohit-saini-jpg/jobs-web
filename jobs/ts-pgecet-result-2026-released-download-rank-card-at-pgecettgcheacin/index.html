How to Check TS PGECET Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to access your result:

  • Visit the official TS PGECET website.
  • Click on the "TS PGECET 2026 Rank Card" link.
  • Enter your Hall Ticket Number.
  • Submit the details.
  • The rank card will appear on the screen.
  • Download and save the PDF.
  • Take a printout for future admission and counselling processes.

Details Mentioned on TS PGECET Rank Card 2026

After downloading the result, candidates should verify the following details:

  • Candidate's Name
  • Hall Ticket Number
  • Registration Details
  • Subject/Paper Appeared
  • Marks Obtained
  • Rank Secured
  • Qualifying Status
  • Examination Year

In case of any discrepancy, candidates should contact the examination authorities immediately.

Important Points for Candidates

  • Download the rank card as early as possible.

  • Keep multiple copies of the rank card.
  • Regularly check the official website for counselling updates.
  • Preserve login credentials until the admission process is completed.
