How to Check JEECUP 2026 Round 1 Seat Allotment Result

This section explains the exact online process candidates need to follow to view and download their Round 1 seat allotment result once the link is activated.

Seat allotment is available only in online mode. Candidates need to log in with their credentials to view their allotted institute and course. Follow these steps:

  • Visit the official website, jeecup.admissions.nic.in.
  • Click on the "Round 1 Seat Allotment Result" link on the homepage.
  • Enter your application number and password, then click submit.
  • Your allotted institute name and course will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of the seat allotment letter for further admission steps.

How Is the JEECUP Seat Allotment Decided?

This section explains the factors that determine which institute and branch a candidate is allotted in Round 1.

JEECUP seat allotment is based on a combination of factors rather than rank alone:

  • Candidate's JEECUP rank
  • Choices/preferences filled during option filling
  • Candidate's category (General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS, etc.)
  • Availability of seats in the chosen institute and branch

Details Mentioned in the JEECUP Seat Allotment Letter

This section lists what information candidates should verify on their allotment letter after downloading it.

Candidates should carefully check their allotment letter for accuracy immediately after downloading it. It typically includes:

  • Candidate's name
  • Application number
  • Category
  • JEECUP rank
  • Allotted college
  • Allotted branch
  • Counselling round
  • Date and instructions for admission

If any detail appears incorrect, candidates should raise the issue at the earliest with the counselling authority before proceeding to document verification.

What to Do After JEECUP Seat Allotment 2026?

This section outlines the steps candidates must complete after receiving their Round 1 allotment.

Once the seat allotment result is out, candidates need to decide whether to accept the allotted seat. If accepted:

  • Pay the seat acceptance fee through the counselling portal.
  • Visit the designated help center for document verification.
  • Pay the remaining admission fee via the login portal within the specified deadline, after document verification is complete.
