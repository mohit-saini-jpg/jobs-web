Steps to Check YVU Result 2026 Online

Follow the steps below to check and download your Yogi Vemana University Result 2026 for B.Tech and Diploma (Honors) Jan 2026 exams in online mode.

  • Visit the official Yogi Vemana University exam portal at yvuexams.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the 'Results' option or directly open the results page.
  • On the results screen, look for the link titled 'B.Tech., Dip (Honors) Sem-1,5,7 Jan-2026 Examinations Updated- 01-05-2026'.
  • Click on the relevant exam link to open the YVU B.Tech/Diploma result page.
  • Enter your hall ticket or registration number in the required field and submit the form to view your result.
  • Your YVU Result 2026 marks memo will be displayed on the screen, showing subject-wise marks and overall result status.
  • Download the provisional result PDF or take a printout for future reference, and verify all details carefully before preserving the copy.
