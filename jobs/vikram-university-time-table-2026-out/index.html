How to Download Vikram University Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the Vikram University Time Table 2026 from the official university website by following the steps mentioned below:

  • Visit the official website of Vikram University.
  • Navigate to the Examination or Time Table section.
  • Search for the PG II Semester 2025-26 timetable.
  • Click on the relevant MA, MCom, MSc or MHSc II Semester timetable link.
  • The PDF will open. Download and save the file on your device.
