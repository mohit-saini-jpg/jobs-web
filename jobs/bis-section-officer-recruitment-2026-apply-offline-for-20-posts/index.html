How to Apply

  • 1Eligible candidates must submit applications through proper channel (Forwarding Authority) via Speed Post only:
  • 2Fill the application form in the prescribed proforma given in Annexure-I in capital letters.
  • 3Attach attested copies of certificates relating to age, caste/category, qualifications, and experience.
  • 4Complete the Declaration (Annexure-II) and ensure the Forwarding Authority countersigns with the certificate.
  • 5The Forwarding Authority must enclose: (i) attested copies of last 5 years' APARs, (ii) Vigilance Clearance Certificate, and (iii) Integrity Certificate.
  • 6Send the application only by Speed Post, superscribing the envelope: "The application for the post of Section Officer on Deputation", within 45 days from the date of publication of the advertisement in Employment News/Rozgar Samachar, to:
  • 7Director (Establishment), Bureau of Indian Standards, Manak Bhavan, 9, Bahadur Shah Zafar Marg, New Delhi – 110002

FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies announced under BIS Section Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
The Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) has announced 20 Section Officer vacancies on a deputation basis across various Regional Offices, Branch Offices, and Laboratories in India.
Q2What is the last date to apply for BIS Section Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
Eligible candidates must submit their applications within 45 days from the date of publication of the advertisement in Employment News/Rozgar Samachar.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for BIS Section Officer posts?
A
Candidates should possess an MBA (Personnel/Finance) or a Post Graduate Degree/Diploma in Personnel Management, Human Resource Management, or Finance from a recognized university or AICTE-approved institution.
Q4What is the age limit for BIS Section Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
Applicants should not exceed 56 years of age as on the closing date of receipt of applications.
Q5What is the salary of a BIS Section Officer?
A
Selected candidates will receive pay under Level-7 of the Pay Matrix, with a salary ranging from ₹44,900 to ₹1,42,400.
Q6What is the mode of recruitment for BIS Section Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
The recruitment is being conducted on a deputation basis for eligible employees of Central Government, State Government, Union Territory Government, Statutory Bodies, and Autonomous Organizations.
Q7How will candidates be selected for the BIS Section Officer post?
A
The selection process includes screening of applications, verification of eligibility, qualifications, experience, APAR records, Vigilance Clearance, and Integrity Certificate submitted through the proper channel.
Q8What is the deputation period for BIS Section Officer posts?
A
The initial deputation period is 2 years, which may be extended by one additional year at the discretion of BIS.
