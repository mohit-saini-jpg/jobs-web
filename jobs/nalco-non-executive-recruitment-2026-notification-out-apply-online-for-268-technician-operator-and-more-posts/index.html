How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the official NALCO website https://www.nalcoindia.com. Applications received through any other mode will not be accepted and will be summarily rejected. The online application is a 2-step process -
  • Step-I: Registration and profile creation
  • Step-II: Login with credentials, fill application form, upload documents, and make online payment of fee (if applicable). Candidates must carefully furnish all correct details as no change/edit will be allowed after submission. A candidate is allowed to apply for only one post; if multiple applications are submitted for a single post, the latest application will be considered and the rest rejected. If a candidate applies for multiple posts, the candidature will be summarily rejected. Before applying online, candidates must scan and keep ready: passport-size photograph (4.5 cm × 3.5 cm, 20–50 KB), signature (blue/black ink, 10–20 KB), left thumb impression, hand-written declaration, and all relevant certificates.
  • Documents to be Uploaded at the Time of Online Application:
  • Left Thumb Impression
  • Hand Written Declaration (in English, blue/black ink)
  • Proof of Date of Birth (Matriculation Certificate / School Leaving Certificate / Matriculation mark sheet) (PDF)
  • Photo Identity Proof (PAN Card / Passport / Driving Licence / Voter's Card / Bank Passbook with photograph / Photo ID issued by Gazetted Officer) (PDF)
  • Educational Certificates - All mark sheets and certificates (Final or Provisional) of Class 10th, ITI, Graduation, Diploma (as applicable) (PDF)
  • Ex-Servicemen eligibility proof with declarations as per latest Government guidelines (if applicable) (PDF)
  • Valid Employment Exchange Registration Card
  • Valid Heavy Vehicle Driving Licence (for Sl. No. 1 - Operator HEMM post)
  • Valid Mining Foreman Certificate of Competency / Electrical Supervisor's Certificate of Competency / Mining Mate Certificate of Competency issued by DGMS (as applicable for the post applied for)
  • National Apprenticeship Certificate (NAC) with requisite endorsement
  • Latest SC / ST / Disability Certificate / OBC (NCL) Certificate / EWS - Income & Asset Certificate, issued on or after 01-04-2026, in prescribed format (PDF)
  • Experience Certificate (if applicable) in the format given at Annexure-IV, on letterhead of the Organisation, along with offer of appointment and last pay slip
  • Land Ousted candidates - all supporting documents including land documents, legal heir certificate, and affidavit in prescribed format (Annexure-III) on non-judicial stamp paper of Rs. 25/-
  • Apply Online at: https://www.nalcoindia.com → Career page → Current Openings → Recruitment of Non-Executive Personnel – M&R Complex, Damanjodi-2026 → View Advertisement → Click here to Apply Online
  • For any queries: https://ccrs.ibps.in or Toll-free Helpline No. 1800222366 or 18001034566 (09:30 AM to 6:00 PM on working days except Saturday, Sunday, and holidays).

Important Instructions

  • A candidate is allowed to apply for only one post - applications for multiple posts will result in summary rejection of all applications. Only full-time qualifications from recognized institutes will be considered; ITI/Diploma/B.Sc. must be in the specific branch/trade prescribed for the post. All qualifications and experience must have been acquired on or before the closing date of application (10-06-2026). OBC (NCL) certificate must be issued on or after 01-04-2026 (Central List OBC only - State Government OBC lists are not followed). EWS Income & Asset Certificate must be issued on or after 01-04-2026 based on FY 2025-26 income. PwBD candidates must possess a Disability Certificate certifying minimum 40% disability issued as per the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Rules, 2017. Only permanent (non-temporary) disabilities are eligible for reservation and age relaxation. Land Ousted candidates whose lands have been acquired for the NALCO project at Damanjodi must upload a valid Employment Preference Certificate - maximum age is 40 years as on 10-06-2026. Employees of PSUs/Government organizations must apply through proper channel and produce a No Objection Certificate (NOC) at the time of document verification. Candidates working in PSUs/Government organizations who fail to produce NOC will not be considered for appointment; their application will be subject to submission of original release order. Application fee once paid shall neither be refunded under any circumstances nor held in reserve for any other recruitment or selection. No documents/certificates and application forms are required to be sent by post. Signature / hand written declaration in CAPITAL LETTERS will NOT be accepted. Electronic gadgets including mobile phones, smartwatches, etc. are not permitted inside the CBT examination hall - carrying them may lead to criminal prosecution. Canvassing in any form during any stage of recruitment will render the candidate ineligible. Any dispute with regard to this recruitment will be settled within the jurisdiction of Cuttack (Odisha) only. NALCO does not seek payment of any kind other than the above-mentioned application fee - candidates are cautioned against fraud.
