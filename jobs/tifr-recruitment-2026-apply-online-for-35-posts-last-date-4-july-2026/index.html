Job Overview

Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR), Mumbai has released Advertisement No. 2026/6 inviting online applications from eligible Indian nationals for 35 posts at its Colaba, Mumbai campus. Details
  1. Candidates wondering about appointment duration can note that for posts at Sr. Nos. 1 to 7 and 12 to 17, the initial appointment will be for 3 years including a 1-year probation period. Continuation beyond 3 years is subject to comprehensive performance review; upon successful review, the appointment continues till superannuation at age 60. Post No. 30 (Project Administrative Assistant) is a temporary, non-pensionable contract appointment, renewable annually up to a total of 3 years. All other selected candidates (Posts 1-29) will be governed by the National Pension System (NPS) unless already covered under CCS (Pension) Rules 1972
  2. Candidates selected for posts at Sr. Nos. 12 to 17 and 22 to 29 will be required to work on Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays and must be prepared for shift duties as per institutional requirements

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for TIFR Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for TIFR Advertisement No. 2026/6 is 4 July 2026. Applications after this date will not be accepted under any circumstances.
Q2How many total posts are advertised under TIFR Recruitment 2026?
A
TIFR has advertised 35 posts across scientific, technical, administrative, and support categories under Advertisement No. 2026/6, all tenable at TIFR Colaba, Mumbai.
Q3What is the salary for Scientific Officer (C) at TIFR?
A
The Total Monthly Emoluments (TME) for Scientific Officer (C) at TIFR is ₹1,18,110 at Pay Level-10, Stage-1 of the 7th CPC Pay Matrix.
Q4What is the application fee for TIFR Recruitment 2026?
A
The notification does not specify a separate application fee structure for this advertisement. Applications are accepted online only at tifr.res.in.
Q5What is the mode of selection for TIFR Technical and Tradesman posts?
A
Tradesman (B) posts are filled through a Written Test followed by a Trade Test. Scientific Officer posts are selected via Written Test and Personal Interview or Personal Interview only, depending on the specific post.
Q6Can SC/ST/OBC candidates apply for Unreserved (UR) posts in TIFR Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. Candidates from SC, ST, OBC, EWS, and PwBD categories may apply for UR posts. However, age relaxation is not available to SC/ST/OBC candidates applying against unreserved vacancies.
Q7Is TIFR Recruitment 2026 a permanent appointment?
A
For most posts (Sr. Nos. 1–7 and 12–17), the initial appointment is for 3 years including 1 year probation, continuing till superannuation at 60 after a performance review. Post No. 30 (Project Administrative Assistant) is a temporary, non-pensionable contract for up to 3 years.
Q8What is the age limit for Clerk (A) posts in TIFR 2026?
A
The maximum age for Clerk (A) UR posts is 28 years. For the OBC post (Sr. No. 18), the maximum age is 31 years. Age relaxation is applicable as per Government of India rules.
