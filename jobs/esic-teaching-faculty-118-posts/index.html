ESIC Teaching Faculty Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Offline for 118 Professors Posts

Rs. 500/- (non‑refundable)

Posts: Various Last Date: 03 August 2026 (for candidates from NE States, J&K Ladakh division, specified HP districts, Andaman & Nicobar, Lakshadweep etc.) Offline – via prescribed application form sent by Speed Post/file:2

Short Information

ESIC Teaching Faculty Recruitment 2026 notification is out to fill 118 vacancies across India. Candidates with MS/MD Can Apply Offline. The Offline application opens on 19-05-2026, and closes on 07-02-2026. The candidate shall apply Offline through ESIC website, esic.gov.in.

Important Dates

Last Date To Apply03 August 2026 (for candidates from NE States, J&K Ladakh division, specified HP districts, Andaman & Nicobar, Lakshadweep etc.)

Age Limit

Age DetailsUp to 5 years for ESIC / Government servants; 5 years for SC/ST; 3 years for OBC; 10 years for PwBD (with cumulative maximum as per rules); additional relaxations for Ex‑Servicemen as per Government orders.

Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationPostgraduate degree (Master’s) in concerned subject/allied discipline and a Doctorate degree (PhD) from a recognised University in the respective subject/allied discipline, as per MCI/NMC “Minimum Qualifications for Teachers in Medical Institutions Regulations”.

Salary / Pay Scale

Pay ScalePay Level‑13 in Pay Matrix (Rs. 1,23,100 – 2,15,900) plus Non‑Practicing Allowance (NPA) and other admissible allowances

Selection Process

  • Interview by ESIC Selection Board; shortlisting based on experience if applications are large

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted offline only in the prescribed application form attached with the notification. No online application facility is available for this recruitment.
