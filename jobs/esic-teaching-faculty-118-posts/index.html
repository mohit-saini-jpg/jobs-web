How to Apply

  • 1Download and print the official notification and Application Form for “Professor in ESI Corporation‑2026” from ESIC website or your job portal.
  • 2Fill the application form in BLOCK letters with accurate details such as specialty applied for, personal information, educational qualifications, teaching experience, registration details and publication list.
  • 3Prepare a Demand Draft/Banker’s Cheque of Rs. 500/- (if applicable) in favour of “ESI Fund Account No. 2”, payable at Delhi, issued after the date of advertisement.
  • 4Attach self‑attested copies of required documents (educational certificates, experience certificates, caste/EWS/PwBD certificates, proof of age, research publications, etc.) as per the checklist.
  • 5Affix a recent self‑attested passport size photograph in the space provided on the application form.
  • 6Place the filled and signed application form along with DD/Banker’s Cheque and enclosures in an envelope superscribed “Application for the post of Professor for Medical Institutions”.
  • 7Send the application preferably by Speed Post to the address given below so as to reach on or before 27.07.2026 (03.08.2026 for specified remote areas).
  • 8Original Demand Draft/Banker’s Cheque pinned on top of the application form with candidate’s name and address written on the reverse.
  • 9Recent passport size photograph (self‑attested) firmly pasted on the application form.
  • 10Matriculation/10th certificate or mark sheet indicating Date of Birth; in some cases, school leaving certificate for TN & Kerala.
  • 11Degree certificates and mark sheets of all academic years (MBBS, PG, PhD etc.); provisional certificates accepted if final degree is awaited.
  • 12Experience certificates from Heads of Organisations clearly mentioning post, period, pay and nature of duties, and specifying recognition as teaching experience by MCI/NMC or relevant body.
  • 13Certificates/testimonials supporting research publications and academic achievements.
  • 14Category certificates: SC/ST/OBC/EWS in prescribed Central Government format; OBC non‑creamy layer declaration; EWS Income & Asset certificate.
  • 15PwBD certificate in prescribed format for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities.
  • 16Documents for age relaxation (Ex‑Servicemen, Central/UT Govt employees etc.) in the prescribed formats.
  • 17Any other supporting documents for claims made in the application (name change, etc.).
  • 18Eligibility (age, qualifications, experience etc.) is calculated with reference to the closing date of applications – 27.07.2026 for most candidates.
  • 19Mere submission of application or call for interview does not confer any right to appointment; candidature remains provisional until verification.
  • 20Incomplete/unsigned applications, or those not in prescribed proforma, without required documents, fee, or received after the last date will be rejected without communication.
  • 21Any false statement or suppression of material information can lead to cancellation of candidature/appointment at any stage.
  • 22Selected candidates will be on probation as per ESIC rules and are liable to be posted anywhere in India; state preferences are only indicative and not binding.

FAQs

Q1What is ESIC Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
ESIC Professor Recruitment 2026 is a direct recruitment drive by Employees' State Insurance Corporation under the Ministry of Labour & Employment, Government of India, to fill 118 Professor posts in various medical specialties across ESIC Post Graduate Institutes of Medical Sciences & Research (PGIMSRs) and ESIC Medical Colleges.
Q2How many vacancies are available in ESIC Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 118 Professor vacancies are available across 22 medical specialties including General Medicine (8), General Surgery (8), Orthopedics (8), Transfusion Medicine (9), Anatomy (7), Anesthesiology (7), and others, distributed as UR-35, SC-25, ST-13, OBC-37, EWS-8, with 8 PwBD posts.
Q3What is the last date to apply for ESIC Professor 2026?
A
The last date for receipt of offline applications is 27 July 2026 for all India candidates, and 03 August 2026 for candidates from remote areas (NE States, J&K Ladakh division, specified HP districts, Andaman & Nicobar, Lakshadweep).
Q4What is the eligibility for ESIC Professor 2026?
A
Candidates must have a recognized medical qualification (MBBS/equivalent) with MD/MS or equivalent in the concerned specialty, plus four years teaching experience as Reader/Associate Professor OR three years as Associate Professor with minimum four research publications (two as first author during Associate Professor tenure).
Q5What is the age limit for ESIC Professor 2026?
A
Maximum age is 50 years as on 27 July 2026 for UR/EWS candidates, with relaxation of 5 years for SC/ST, 3 years for OBC, 10 years for PwBD, and up to 5 years for ESIC/Government servants, subject to cumulative maximum limits.
Q6What is the selection process for ESIC Professor 2026?
A
Selection is based on Interview by ESIC Selection Board with possible shortlisting based on teaching experience beyond minimum requirement. Minimum qualifying marks: UR/EWS-50, OBC-45, SC/ST/PwBD-40 out of 100 marks.
Q7Is there any application fee for ESIC Professor 2026?
A
Yes, application fee is ₹500 via Demand Draft/Banker's Cheque in favour of "ESI Fund Account No. 2" payable at Delhi, for General/OBC/EWS/ESIC male candidates. SC/ST/PwBD/Women/Ex-Servicemen are exempted from fee payment.
Q8What is the salary for ESIC Professor 2026?
A
Professors receive Pay Level-13 in the Pay Matrix (₹1,23,100 – ₹2,15,900) plus Non-Practicing Allowance (NPA), Dearness Allowance (DA), House Rent Allowance (HRA), Transport Allowance and other admissible Central Government allowances.
