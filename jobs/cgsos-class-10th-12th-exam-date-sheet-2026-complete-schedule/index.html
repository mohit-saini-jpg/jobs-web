CGSOS Class 10th & 12th Exam Date Sheet 2026 - Complete Schedule

The Chhattisgarh State Open School (CGSOS), operating under the Chhattisgarh Board of Secondary Education (CGBSE), has officially released the exam date sheet for Class 10 and Class 12 for the year 2026. All registered students appearing for the CGSOS annual examination must note the subject-wise schedule carefully to plan their studies and avoid any last-minute confusion.

The CGSOS 2026 examinations for both Class 10 (Secondary) and Class 12 (Higher Secondary) are scheduled to begin from 10 August 2026 (Monday) and will conclude on 29 August 2026 (Saturday). Students should download and save the timetable immediately to begin subject-wise preparation.

What Is CGSOS? - Chhattisgarh State Open School Overview

The Chhattisgarh State Open School (CGSOS) is the official open schooling authority for the state of Chhattisgarh, functioning under the Chhattisgarh Board of Secondary Education (CGBSE). It provides secondary and higher secondary level education to students who, for various reasons, are unable to attend regular schools including working adults, dropouts, and those seeking a second chance at formal education.

CGSOS offers flexibility in terms of subject selection, study pace, and exam attempts, making it a popular choice for thousands of learners across Chhattisgarh every year. The board conducts annual examinations for Class 10 (Secondary) and Class 12 (Higher Secondary), and results are recognized for further education and employment purposes.

Key Highlights of CGSOS Exam Schedule 2026

Before diving deep into preparation, students should be aware of the most important details about the CGSOS 2026 exam calendar. Here are the key takeaways from the official timetable:

  • Exam Start Date: 10 August 2026 (Monday)
  • Exam End Date (Class 10): 25 August 2026 (Tuesday)
  • Exam End Date (Class 12): 29 August 2026 (Saturday)
  • Total Exam Days (Class 12): 13 papers spread across 20 days
  • Total Exam Days (Class 10): 11 papers spread across 16 days
  • Conducting Authority: CGSOS under CGBSE (Chhattisgarh Board of Secondary Education)
  • Mode of Examination: Offline (pen and paper-based)
  • Gap Days: There are deliberate gap days in the schedule (e.g., 12, 14, 15, 16 Aug are free), allowing students adequate time to prepare between exams.

How to Download the CGSOS Date Sheet 2026 Officially

Students who want to verify and save the official CGSOS exam schedule can do so easily by visiting the official CGBSE or CGSOS website. Here is a step-by-step process:

  • Visit the official CGBSE website: cgbse.nic.in or the CGSOS portal.
  • Navigate to the "Examination" or "Date Sheet / Timetable" section on the homepage.
  • Look for the link titled "CGSOS Class 10 / Class 12 Exam Date Sheet 2026".
  • Click to open the PDF timetable.
  • Verify your subjects and dates.
  • Download and save the PDF for future reference.

Students are strongly advised to cross-check their subject codes with the admit card to avoid appearing for the wrong paper.

Important Tips for CGSOS 2026 Exam Preparation

With the CGSOS 2026 exams kicking off on 10 August, students now have a clear timeline to organize their study plan. Here are some practical and effective preparation strategies tailored for open school students:

Plan a Subject-Wise Study Timetable

Use the date sheet above to work backwards from each exam date. Allocate more revision days to high-weightage or difficult subjects like Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Accountancy. Easier or language-based papers (Hindi, English, Sanskrit) can be prepared in shorter revision windows.

Focus on CGSOS Syllabus and Sample Papers

Students should always study from the official CGSOS syllabus for 2025-26. Practicing previous year question papers and sample papers is one of the most effective strategies for scoring well. These give a clear idea of the question pattern, marking scheme, and important topics.

Prioritize Gap Days Wisely

The CGSOS 2026 schedule has several built-in gap days between exams (e.g., no exams on 12, 14-16, 23, 26, and 28 August). These breaks should be used productively for revision, solving mock tests, and addressing weak areas not for complete rest.

Stay Updated with Official Announcements

The exam schedule can occasionally be revised due to administrative or unforeseen reasons. Students must regularly check the official CGBSE/CGSOS website and keep an eye on official announcements. Also verify the exam center details printed on your admit card.

Keep Documents Ready

Students must carry their CGSOS Admit Card 2026 and a valid photo ID to every exam center. Without the admit card, entry to the examination hall will be denied.
