Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationB.Tech/B.E in CS/IT/EE/ECE
DetailsProject Assistant: B.Tech/B.E in CS/IT/EE/ECE streams with at least 60% marks (6.5 CGPA in aggregate). Experience working with Linux-based workstations and servers. Hands-on experience in managing GPU-based servers. Ability to troubleshoot system issues and experiment setups. Proficiency in Python is preferred. Knowledge of C/C++ is desirable. Ability to read, analyze, and review research papers. Strong communication skills will be an added advantage. Ability to multitask and manage multiple responsibilities effectively. Previous Experience Working in IITs or similar reputed institutes will be an added advantage. Manage and maintain lab infrastructure, servers, and experimental setups. Maintain proper documentation of experiments, results, and progress reports. Conduct experiments, data collection, and analysis as per project requirements. Handle multiple tasks/projects simultaneously and meet deadlines. Troubleshoot technical issues and ensure smooth functioning of systems. Collaborate with faculty, researchers, and students on ongoing projects.

FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Project Assistant position at IIT Hyderabad?
The last date to submit your email application for the Project Assistant position at IIT Hyderabad is 07-06-2026.
Q2. What are the minimum educational qualifications required for the Project Assistant role?
Candidates must possess a B.Tech/B.E degree in CS/IT/EE/ECE streams with at least 60% marks or a 6.5 CGPA in aggregate.
Q3. Is there any experience required for this position?
While not strictly mandatory, experience in Linux, GPU servers, Python, and C/C++ is desirable. Previous experience in IITs or similar institutions is also an added advantage.
Q4. What is the salary offered for the Project Assistant position?
The consolidated pay ranges from Rs. 25,000/- per month to Rs. 40,000/- per month, depending on the candidate's qualifications and experience.
Q5. How will candidates be selected for the Project Assistant role?
Selection will be based on a shortlist of candidates who meet the eligibility criteria, academic record, and relevant experience. Shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview via email.
Q6. Can candidates from any state apply for this recruitment?
Yes, candidates from any state can apply, provided they meet the eligibility criteria. The institute is located in Telangana.
Q7. What is the application mode for this recruitment?
Applications are to be submitted via email. Candidates need to send their complete application along with required documents to the specified email address.
Q8. Are there any age restrictions for applying?
The official notification does not specify an age limit. Candidates are advised to refer to the detailed notification for any age-related information.
Q9. What documents should be attached with the application?
Candidates should attach self-attested copies of educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs with their application.
Q10. What if no suitable candidate is found?
If no suitable candidate is found, the position may be left vacant, and a new advertisement with an extended application deadline might be issued.
