Job Details

Bank Jobs 2026: For candidates aspiring to build a career in the banking sector but unsure where to begin, bank apprenticeship programs offer a strong starting point. Especially for fresh graduates, these programs provide hands-on exposure to banking operations and help them understand the system from the inside.

Recently, the State Bank of India (SBI) and Bank of Baroda (BOB) have announced more than 12,000 apprentice vacancies, creating a major opportunity for candidates looking to enter the banking field.

A bank apprenticeship is essentially a structured training program where selected candidates are trained in various banking operations. It is not a permanent job but a learning-based program that helps candidates gain practical knowledge of day-to-day banking activities.

During the apprenticeship period, candidates are assigned basic operational tasks under the guidance of senior bank officials. These may include:

Assisting customers with account opening and form filling Helping in document verification and KYC-related processes Learning branch operations such as cash counters and token systems Performing basic data entry and using banking software Supporting customer service activities and resolving basic queries related to accounts, ATMs, and transactions

To apply for bank apprenticeship programs, candidates must hold a bachelor’s degree from a recognized university. Prior work experience is generally not required.

The age limit is typically between 20 and 28 years, with relaxation provided to reserved category candidates as per government norms.

Selection is usually based on an online examination, document verification, and a local language test. In some cases, selection may also be merit-based depending on the bank’s notification.

Selected apprentices receive a monthly stipend during the training period, usually around ₹15,000 per month. Since this is a training program, no additional allowances or permanent job benefits are provided. The stipend amount may vary slightly depending on the bank and location.

Bank of Baroda has announced 5,000 apprentice vacancies, while SBI has announced 7,150 vacancies under its apprentice recruitment drive.

The application process for both recruitments started on 19 May 2026 and will continue until 8 June 2026. The training period is generally 12 months.
