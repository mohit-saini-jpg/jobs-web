How to Apply

  • 1Typed application form (in block letters, colour printout) with recent colour photograph (white background, taken within last 3 months) and candidate's signature - mandatory
  • 2Self-attested copies of Aadhaar Card
  • 3Self-attested copies of 10th and 12th Standard Mark Sheets
  • 4Self-attested copies of UG/PG Mark Sheets & Diploma/ITI Certificates (as applicable)
  • 5Self-attested copy of Driving License (for Civilian Motor Driver post)
  • 6Self-attested copy of Experience Certificate(s) (wherever required)
  • 7Self-attested copy of Computer Course Certificate (as applicable)
  • 8Self-attested copies of valid OBC/SC/ST/EWS Certificate (OBC/EWS certificates must be issued on or after 01-04-2025)
  • 9Self-attested copy of PwBD Certificate (for PwBD candidates - disability must be permanent and not less than 40%)
  • 10Self-attested copy of ESM Discharge Certificate (for Ex-Servicemen)
  • 11No Objection Certificate from present employer (for Central/State Govt/PSU/Autonomous Body permanent employees)
  • 12A/C Payee Demand Draft of Rs. 100/- (Non-Refundable) drawn in favour of "DSSC Direct Recruitment 2026" payable at Canara Bank, DSSC Branch (Code 16239)
  • 13Self-addressed envelope of 10x22 cm with Rs. 56/- postage stamp pasted
  • 14Application form must be typed in block letters, opened/filled in Adobe Reader, and taken as colour printout - handwritten forms will NOT be accepted.
  • 15Separate applications with separate Demand Drafts must be submitted for each post applied for.
  • 16Applications without photograph, without signature, or without the prescribed Demand Draft will not be accepted.
  • 17OBC Non-Creamy Layer Certificate and EWS Certificate must be issued on or after 01-04-2025.
  • 18PwBD candidates must have permanent disability of not less than 40% certified by competent authority.
  • 19Biometric verification with Aadhaar Card will be carried out at the time of written examination/skill test.
  • 20Candidate's appearance at the time of examination must match the photograph in the application form.
  • 21Use of mobile phones, pagers, smartwatches, Bluetooth devices, pen drives, or any electronic/communication device is strictly prohibited at the examination venue. Violation will result in disciplinary action including ban from future examinations.
  • 22No TA/DA will be paid for attending written examination or skill test.
  • 23Selected candidates will be required to serve anywhere in India.
  • 24Mere submission of application does not entitle candidates to be called for examination. Depending on applications received, candidates may be shortlisted based on marks in minimum essential qualification.
  • 25Applications received after the due date will not be considered. DSSC will not be responsible for postal delays.
  • 26The Commandant reserves the right to cancel/postpone the recruitment process and increase/decrease vacancies without assigning any reason. Decision of the Appointing Authority (Commandant, DSSC) is final and no appeal will be entertained.
  • 27DSSC will not be liable/responsible for paying any compensation in case of injury/death suffered by a candidate during the recruitment process.
  • 28No interim correspondence regarding application status will be entertained.
