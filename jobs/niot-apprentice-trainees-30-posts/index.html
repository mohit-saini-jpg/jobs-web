A
For Technician Apprentice posts, the minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 24 years. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories.

A
Graduate Apprentice trainees will receive a stipend of Rs. 13,000/- per month. A portion of this stipend is supported by the Government under the DBT Scheme.

A
Candidates need to have a full-time graduate degree in Engineering/Technology or a relevant discipline from a recognized university to be eligible for Graduate Apprentice positions.

A
There are a total of 30 vacancies announced for the Apprentice Trainees positions at the National Institute of Ocean Technology.

A
The selection process for NIOT Apprentice Trainees will be based on a walk-in interview. Candidates need to perform well in the interview to be selected.
Q7Where is the walk-in interview for NIOT Apprentice Trainees being held?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at the National Institute of Ocean Technology (NIOT) in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The exact address will be in the official notification.
Q8Do I need to register online before attending the walk-in interview?
A
Yes, candidates are advised to register online on the NATS portal (https://nats.education.gov.in/) before appearing for the walk-in interview.
Q9What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry original and self-attested photocopies of relevant certificates, mark sheets, proof of date of birth, Aadhaar Card, and passport size photograph.
Q10Is there any application fee for NIOT Apprentice Trainees recruitment?

No application fee is mentioned in the official notification for the NIOT Apprentice Trainees recruitment. Candidates can attend the walk-in interview without paying any fee.
