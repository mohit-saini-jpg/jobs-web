How to Apply

  • 1Complete Profile Registration on https://vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in (if not already registered) - scan and upload a light-background passport-size photograph in .jpg/.jpeg format (50 KB–100 KB) and specimen signature in .jpg/.jpeg format (50 KB–100 KB); filename must be alphanumeric without spaces or dots.
  • 2Verify all details in Profile Registration before proceeding.
  • 3Select the WRDT26 examination and fill in the Online Application Form.
  • 4Pay the examination fee online - note the payment details and ensure the bank account details (for refund) are entered correctly.
  • 5Click "SUBMIT" after successful fee payment to complete the application.
  • 6Download and print the submitted application form and keep it safe.
  • 7Admit Card (Pravesh Patra) will be available for download approximately 7 days before the exam (tentatively 27 July 2026) from the Vyapam website - candidates must download it from their profile. Admit card will NOT be sent by post.
  • 8Carry full printout of the admit card to the exam centre along with one original photo ID proof (Voter ID/Driving License/PAN Card/Aadhaar Card with photo) and Blue/Black Ball Point Pen.
  • 9Admit Card (downloaded from Vyapam website) with photo ID proof (Voter ID/Driving License/PAN Card/Aadhaar Card) - original must be presented; entry denied without original photo ID
  • 10High School/Higher Secondary exam pass certificate (attested photocopy - self-attested or certified by Gazetted Officer) as proof of date of birth
  • 11Higher Secondary (Class XII) certificate and mark sheet showing Drawing (Rekha Chitra) as a subject
  • 12Chhattisgarh domicile/local resident certificate (Mool Niwasi Praman Patra) - original, issued by competent authority
  • 13Permanent caste/category certificate (Sthayi Jati Praman Patra) issued by authorized government officer - required for SC/ST/OBC candidates
  • 14Permanent PwBD disability certificate (minimum 40% disability) issued by competent Medical Authority and UDID card - for PwBD candidates
  • 15No Objection Certificate (Annapati Praman) from employer - for candidates working in government/semi-government organizations
  • 16Ex-Servicemen discharge certificate/relevant documents - for Ex-SM candidates
  • 17Green Card (for Family Welfare Programme beneficiaries claiming age relaxation)
  • 18Award certificate (Shaheed Rajiv Pandey/Gundadhur/Maharaja Pravirachandra Bhanjdev Samman/Rashtriya Yuva Puraskar) - if claiming age relaxation under these categories
  • 19Inter-caste marriage certificate (for anti-untouchability scheme awardees claiming age relaxation)
  • 20Merely filling the Online Application Form or appearing in the examination does not confer any right of appointment on the candidate. Eligibility is verified at document verification stage; ineligibility found at any stage will lead to cancellation of candidature.
  • 21The responsibility of verifying eligibility before applying rests entirely with the candidate. CG Vyapam is not responsible for any information provided by candidates; the full responsibility lies with the candidate.
  • 22At the time of joining, selected candidates must produce original copies of all certificates. Police verification and medical fitness certificate from District Hospital Medical Board are mandatory before appointment.
  • 23Women reservation calculation follows CG Government circular (F 13-2/2014/Aap.Pr./1-3 dated 14.07.2017) - only whole numbers are used, not decimals.
  • 24CG Vyapam's jurisdiction is limited to conducting the examination and declaring results. Document verification, selection and appointment processes are conducted by the department, not CG Vyapam.
  • 25Exam result will be declared on the CG Vyapam website https://vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in. OMR evaluation is by machine; no re-evaluation is available.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for CG Vyapam Tracer (Civil) Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application form for CG Vyapam Tracer (Civil) Recruitment 2026 is 15 June 2026 up to 5:00 PM. Candidates are advised to complete the registration and fee payment process well before the deadline to avoid technical issues on the final day. Late submissions will not be accepted under any circumstances.
Q2How many vacancies are available in CG Jal Sansadhan Vibhag Tracer Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 35 vacancies have been announced for the post of Tracer (Civil) under the Chhattisgarh Water Resources Department. These vacancies are distributed among categories such as UR, SC, ST, and OBC, along with horizontal reservations for women, ex-servicemen, and PwBD candidates. The department may increase or decrease the number of vacancies based on administrative requirements.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for CG Vyapam Tracer (Civil) 2026?
A
Candidates must have passed Higher Secondary (Class 12th) with Drawing (Rekha Chitra) as one of the subjects from a recognized board. Applicants possessing equivalent qualifications recognized by the Chhattisgarh Government are also eligible to apply. All educational qualifications must be completed before the advertisement date.
Q4Who can apply for CG Jal Sansadhan Vibhag Recruitment 2026?
A
Only candidates who are domicile residents of Chhattisgarh State are eligible to apply for this recruitment. Applicants must possess a valid domicile certificate and satisfy all eligibility conditions related to age, qualification, and category reservation. Candidates from other states are not eligible for these posts
Q5What is the age limit for CG Vyapam Tracer Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age required is 18 years, while the upper age limit for general category local residents is 35 years due to the special relaxation provided by the Chhattisgarh Government. Reserved category candidates such as SC, ST, OBC, women, and government employees can avail additional age relaxation as per state norms. However, the maximum age cannot exceed 45 years in any case.
Q6What is the selection process for CG Vyapam Tracer (Civil) Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of a written competitive examination conducted by CG Vyapam followed by document verification. The written test will be OMR-based and include objective-type questions carrying one mark each with negative marking. Final selection will be made based on merit and eligibility verification conducted by the department.
Q7Is there any negative marking in the CG Vyapam Tracer exam 2026?
A
Yes, the examination includes a negative marking system where 0.25 marks will be deducted for every wrong answer. Candidates should carefully attempt questions to avoid losing marks unnecessarily. Unattempted questions will not attract any penalty and will be awarded zero marks.
Q8What is the application fee for CG Jal Sansadhan Vibhag Tracer Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 350 for General category, Rs. 250 for OBC candidates, and Rs. 200 for SC/ST/PwBD candidates. The fee must be paid online during form submission through the CG Vyapam portal. Eligible Chhattisgarh domicile candidates who appear in the examination may receive a fee refund to their registered bank account
Q9When will the CG Vyapam Tracer (Civil) exam be conducted?
A
The tentative examination date for CG Vyapam Tracer (Civil) Recruitment 2026 is 02 August 2026 (Sunday). The exam is scheduled from 10:00 AM to 12:15 PM, and candidates must report to the exam centre before the gate closing time. Admit cards are expected to be released approximately one week before the examination.
Q10How can I apply for CG Vyapam Tracer (Civil) Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online only through the official CG Vyapam website by completing profile registration and submitting the application form. Applicants need to upload photograph, signature, and required details before paying the examination fee online. After successful submission, candidates should download and keep a copy of the application form for future reference.
