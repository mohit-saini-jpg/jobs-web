Good news for UP Police Constable PMT aspirants. The additional shortlist of 5 times more candidates has been released. This means more candidates will get a chance to appear for the physical test. Stay tuned for latest updates and news on Sarkari Naukri.

Expert Analysis

This development shows that the selection process is becoming more competitive. Aspirants need to focus on improving their physical fitness and performance in the test to increase their chances of getting selected.

Who Should Apply

Ideal candidates are those who have already cleared the written test and are looking for a chance to appear for the physical test. They should be physically fit and ready to perform well in the test.

Preparation Tips

Aspirants should focus on improving their physical fitness by following a regular exercise routine. They should also practice their running and other physical activities to increase their performance in the test.

Salary Insights

The salary for UP Police Constable is Rs. 5,200 - 20,200 (Grade Pay Rs. 2,000). With experience, the salary can increase to Rs. 35,400. There's also a chance of promotion to higher ranks with good performance.

Job Profile

As a UP Police Constable, you'll be responsible for maintaining law and order in the state. Your day-to-day work will include patrolling, investigating crimes, and providing assistance to citizens. With experience, you can move to higher ranks and take on more responsibilities.

Selection Strategy

Aspirants should focus on improving their physical fitness and performance in the test. They should also focus on their overall personality development, including their communication skills and attitude.
