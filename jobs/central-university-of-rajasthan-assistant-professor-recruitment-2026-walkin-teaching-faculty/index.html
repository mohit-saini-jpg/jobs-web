Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies for Assistant Professor posts?

There are a total of 3 vacancies announced for the Assistant Professor positions at the Central University of Rajasthan.

Q2. What is the application mode for this recruitment?

The application mode for this recruitment is Walk-in Interview. Candidates need to attend the interview directly with their documents.

Q3. What is the salary offered for the Assistant Professor post?

The selected candidates will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 60,000/- Per Month.

Q4. What are the minimum educational qualifications required?

Candidates must possess a Master's Degree with a minimum of 55% marks, be UGC NET qualified or hold a Ph.D. as per UGC Regulations 2018. For Management, two years of professional experience is also required.

Q5. Are there any application fees for this recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment.

Q6. When are the walk-in interviews scheduled?

The walk-in interviews are scheduled on 29 May 2026 for Management, 01 June 2026 for Commerce, and 05 June 2026 for English.

Q7. What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?

Candidates must carry all original documents, self-attested photocopies, and recent passport-size photographs.

Q8. Is there any age relaxation for reserved categories?

Yes, age relaxation is applicable to SC/ST/OBC/PWD candidates as per University/GOI rules.
