Krishna Kanta Handiqui State Open University has released the KKHSOU Result 2026 for multiple undergraduate semester examinations for different batches. The latest updates dated 31-03-2026 include Result of the BBA 1st Semester (2020-23), Result of the BCA 1st Semester (2020-23), Result of the BCA 1st Semester (2019), Result of the BA 1st Semester (2021-23), Result of the BA 1st Semester (2020), Result of the BA 1st Semester (2017-19) and Result of the DY 2nd Semester (old). KKHSOU Result 2026 has been announced online on the official website - kkhsou.ac.in - through the dedicated results/news page and the e‑marksheet system.

Learners can open the respective result notices from the Results section, access the detailed PDFs or e‑marksheet links and then enter their enrollment number to download individual marksheets. After viewing subject wise marks and pass/fail status, they should verify personal and course details carefully and contact KKHSOU if there is any discrepancy or issue with the displayed result.

KKHSOU Results 2026 Key Highlights

KKHSOU has simultaneously published first semester results for BA, BBA and BCA programmes across multiple admission cycles along with DY 2nd semester (old scheme) outcomes, giving a consolidated view for open and distance learners. These results are part of the ongoing 2026 academic session updates that also include earlier releases for BA 2nd/4th semester, BBA/BCA 3rd and 5th semester and PG courses.

Steps to Check KKHSOU Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your KKHSOU BA/BBA/BCA 1st Semester or DY 2nd Semester Result 2026:

  • Go to the KKHSOU results page: https://kkhsou.ac.in/web/index_news_all.php?c=dE9hTkRwd05wc3RrV1JjaTBDQ2VBZz09.
  • In the results list, locate your course result link, such as: RESULT OF THE BBA - I SEMESTER (2020-23) RESULT OF THE BCA - I SEMESTER (2020-23) or (2019) RESULT OF THE BA - I SEMESTER (2021-23/2020/2017-19) RESULT OF THE DY - II SEMESTER (old).

  • RESULT OF THE BA - I SEMESTER (2021-23/2020/2017-19)
  • RESULT OF THE DY - II SEMESTER (old).
  • Click on the appropriate result link to open the detailed news/result page.
