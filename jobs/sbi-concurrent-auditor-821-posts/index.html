How to Apply

  • 1Brief particulars of experience of last 10 years (assignment-wise details) – PDF
  • 2ID Proof – PDF
  • 3Proof of Date of Birth – PDF
  • 4Copy of PPO (Pension Payment Order)
  • 5Recent Passport size Photograph (JPG/JPEG, 20–50 kb, 200 x 230 pixels)
  • 6Signature (JPG/JPEG, 10–20 kb, 140 x 60 pixels, Black ink on white paper)
  • 7EWS / Caste Certificate (if applicable) – PDF
  • 8Any other relevant document (if available) – PDF
  • 9Only retired officers of SBI (and its e-Associate banks) who retired on superannuation at age 60 in the scales of MMGS-III, SMGS-IV/V or TEGS-VI are eligible to apply.
  • 10This engagement is purely contractual in nature and shall not be treated as extension in service for the purpose of PF/Pension/Bonus and other superannuation benefits.
  • 11The Engaged Retired Official will not be covered under SBI Officers’ Service Rules but will be bound by the terms of the offer of engagement.
  • 12The retired officers will not exercise any administrative/financial power during the period of engagement.
  • 13The retired officers will not accept any assignment with any other organization during the period of contractual service in the Bank.
  • 14Retired Officers with good credit/audit/Forex background should preferably be a resident of the area where the branch/outfit is located.
  • 15Only one application will be accepted for applying at one Circle. Multiple applications if received, only the last valid (completed) application will be accepted.
  • 16The existing wait list of Retired Officers empanelled as Concurrent Auditors will get cancelled with this publication.
  • 17Candidates must upload all required documents; failure to do so will result in their candidature not being considered for shortlisting/interview.
  • 18No TA/DA will be paid for attending the interview process.
  • 19Contract may be terminated by giving one month’s notice from either side. Contract may be terminated without notice if performance is not satisfactory.
  • 20The selected candidate has to furnish a stamped Service Level Agreement (SLA) before taking up the assignment.
  • 21If any false or incorrect information is detected at any stage of engagement, candidature/services will be summarily terminated.
  • 22Bank reserves the right to cancel the engagement process entirely at any stage without prior notice.
  • 23All revisions/corrigendum (if any) will be hosted on the Bank’s Careers website only.
  • 24For any query, write through the ‘CONTACT US’ link available on the Bank’s website (https://sbi.bank.in/web/careers/).
