The Mizoram Board of School Education (MBSE) has officially released the MBSE HSSLC Result 2026 for the academic session 2025-26 today. Students who appeared for the Class 12 board examinations across Arts, Science and Commerce streams can now check their results online. MBSE HSSLC Result 2026 has been released online on the official websites - mbse.edu.in and the result portal mbseonline.com.

The board has published results for HSSLC students from all streams and districts of Mizoram. Students can now check and download their provisional marksheet by entering their roll number and registration number on the official result portals.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the online marksheet, including their name, roll number, subjects, marks obtained, total marks, division and qualifying status. In case of any discrepancies, they should immediately contact the board authorities or their respective schools for necessary corrections.

How to Check Mizoram MBSE HSSLC Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your Mizoram MBSE HSSLC Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official website - mbse.edu.in or the result portal mbseonline.com.
  • On the homepage, click on the link “HSSLC Examination Results 2026” / “MBSE HSSLC Exam Result 2026”.
  • Enter your Roll Number and Registration Number in the login fields
  • Click on the Submit / Search Results button
  • Your Mizoram MBSE HSSLC Result 2026 marksheet will appear on the screen
  • Download and take a printout of the online marksheet for future reference
