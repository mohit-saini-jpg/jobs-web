How to Apply

  • 1Date of Birth (DOB) proof - Self attested Matriculation / High School certificate / Birth Certificate
  • 2A recent passport size colour photograph (not exceeding 30 KB)
  • 3Scanned sample of candidate's signature (not exceeding 30 KB)
  • 4Self-attested degree certificates/testimonials regarding Essential and Higher qualification along with mark-sheets
  • 5Fellowship proofs mentioning start date and end date of fellowship (if applicable)
  • 6Duly signed CCA Declaration (if applicable)
  • 7Notarized affidavit in support (if name mismatch in documents)
  • 8Notarized/certified transcript if documents are in language other than English or Hindi
  • 9All experience certificates (with start and end date) and salary Proofs / Appointment letter (with pay scale or CTC), Promotion letters (with revised scales or CTC), Form 16A and Salary slips (beginning and end of each employment)
  • 10Requisite caste / disability certificate (for SC/ST/Divyang – for fee waiver only)
  • 11Requisite Release certificate (if retired Armed Forces personnel)
  • 12CCA Declaration (applicable) and NOC at Final Personal Interview stage
  • 13All candidates must apply online through https://rac.gov.in only. No other mode of application is accepted.
  • 14Closing Date: 19 June 2026 (1600 hrs.) - facility for online application submission will remain available on RAC website only up to this time.
  • 15Do not divulge any confidential information related to work experience of present or previous employer while filling the application; RAC shall not be held responsible for revelation of such information.
  • 16Ensure candidate's name and parents' names are filled exactly as in Date of Birth proof; name mismatch requires notarized affidavit at time of filling the online application.
  • 17For multiple posts - apply separately for each post and mention item numbers and order of preference in each application.
  • 18Do not change registered mobile number or Email after application - vital information about eligibility/shortlisting/selection status will be intimated through Email/SMS only.
  • 19Only locked/finalised applications shall be considered - no correction will be allowed after final submission. Preview application with all uploaded documents before final submission.
  • 20The maximum file size of each document/certificate should not exceed 500 KB; uploaded documents must be legible/readable and not password protected.
  • 21Final Personal Interview will be held in-person at RAC, Delhi only - no online/virtual interview mode will be considered under any circumstances.
  • 22Candidates must score minimum 75% marks (75 out of 100) in Final Personal Interview for consideration for selection.
  • 23Selected candidates will have liability to serve anywhere in India including remote/field area locations as per Central Government Rules.
  • 24Canvassing in any form will disqualify the candidate.
  • 25RAC does not request for any payment at any stage of recruitment process except the application fee. No such demand should be entertained.
  • 26Any dispute will be subject to the Hon'ble Courts/Tribunals having jurisdiction over Delhi only.
  • 27DRDO under DPDP Act 2023 declares that candidate's personal data received through online application will be processed for recruitment purpose only.
  • 28Incomplete applications will be summarily rejected.
  • 29The number of vacancies may vary; RAC reserves the right to modify the screening/shortlisting procedure depending upon availability of suitable candidates.
