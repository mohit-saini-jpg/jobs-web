NHM Jalgaon Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

NHM Jalgaon Recruitment 2026 has been announced by the District Integrated Health & Family Welfare Society, Jalgaon under the National Health Mission (NHM), inviting applications for 80 contractual posts across 17 cadres, including Cardiologist, Gynaecologist, Paediatrician, Anesthetist, Physician, Psychiatrist, Medical Officer (MBBS), Dentist, and other paramedical positions. Eligible candidates can apply offline between 6 July 2026 and 16 July 2026. Eligibility Criteria for NHM Jalgaon Recruitment 2026

Educational qualification differs post-wise, ranging from MBBS/MD/MS/DM for medical specialist posts to BDS/MDS for Dentist, and diploma/degree qualifications for paramedical and technician posts. Refer to the vacancy table above for the exact qualification required for each post. Candidates whose mark sheets do not show percentage or who studied under a grading system must attach an attested photocopy of both sides of the mark sheet.

NHM Jalgaon Recruitment 2026 : Age Limit

Open Category: Maximum 38 years as on the date of application Reserved/Backward Category: Maximum 43 years as on the date of application Posts related to Nursing services: Maximum age limit is 65 years Candidates above 60 years of age must attach a physical fitness certificate issued by the District Civil Surgeon along with the application.

Salary / Remuneration

Remuneration under NHM Jalgaon Recruitment 2026 ranges from Rs. 17,000/- per month for Technician posts up to Rs. 1,25,000/- per month for the Cardiologist post. Teleconsultation posts (Physician and Paediatrician) are paid Rs. 300/- per teleconsultation instead of a fixed monthly salary. The remuneration mentioned against each post is consolidated, and no other allowances will be payable in addition to it.

NHM Jalgaon Recruitment 2026 : Application Fee Details

The application fee must be paid through a Demand Draft (DD) only, drawn in favour of "District Integrated Health & Family Welfare Society, Jalgaon". Candidates must write their name and the post applied for on the back of the DD. Applications without a valid DD will be rejected regardless of the reason.

NHM Jalgaon Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

For Super Specialist, Specialist, and Medical Officer (MBBS) posts, selection will be made through direct interview based on the following marking scheme (total 50 marks): Subject Knowledge - 10 marks Research & Academic Knowledge - 10 marks Leadership Quality - 10 marks Administrative Abilities - 10 marks Experience - 10 marks (Government experience: 2 marks/year; Private experience: 1 mark/year; maximum 10 marks) For all remaining posts, selection will be based on a 100-mark scheme as follows: Candidates with equal marks will be given preference based on seniority by date of birth (older candidate preferred).

  • 1Download the application format from the official website www.zpjalgaon.gov.in.
