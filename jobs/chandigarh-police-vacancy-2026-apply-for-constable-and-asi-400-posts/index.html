Job Profile

Chandigarh Police mein Constable aur ASI ke roop mein, aapki zimmedariyan nagrik suraksha ko sunishchit karna hoga. Constable ka kaam sadharan suraksha, traffic niyantran, aur chote-mote aparadhon ko rokna hota hai. ASI, yaani Assistant Sub-Inspector, ek adhikaristhaleey pad hai jismein aapko nirdeshon ka palan karna aur team ka netritva karna ho sakta hai. In padon par niyukti ke baad, aapko shahar mein shanti aur vyavastha bani rahe, yeh sunishchit karna hoga. Yeh ek zimmedari ka kaam hai jismein aapko samudaay ki seva karne ka mauka milta hai. Nyayik prakriyaon mein sahayata karna bhi in padon ka ek hissa ho sakta hai.

Selection Strategy

Chandigarh Police Constable aur ASI ki bharti mein safal hone ke liye ek susangathit selection strategy apnana zaruri hai. Avedan prakriya poori karne ke baad, sabse pehla kadam written examination hoga, jismein aapke general knowledge, reasoning, maths aur english ke gyan ka parikshan kiya jayega. Iske baad, short-listed ummeedwaron ko physical efficiency test (PET) aur physical measurement test (PMT) ke liye bulaya jayega. In dono mein safal hona bahut zaruri hai. Ant mein, kuch padon ke liye interview ya document verification bhi ho sakta hai. Aapko har charan ke liye alag se taiyari karni hogi aur apni kamzoriyon par kaam karna hoga.
