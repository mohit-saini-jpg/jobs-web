FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in JIPMER Senior Resident Recruitment July 2026?
A
There are 89 vacancies across 27 departments at JIPMER Puducherry and Karaikal campuses.
Q2What is the pay scale for JIPMER Senior Resident post?
A
The basic pay is Rs. 67,700/- per month (Level 11, Cell 1 as per 7th CPC) plus NPA and other allowances. The total emoluments in the first year are approximately Rs. 1,30,000/- per month.
Q3What is the last date to register for JIPMER Senior Resident July 2026?
A
The last date for online registration is 29-06-2026 (04:30 PM). Candidates can also register on the morning of the Written Test (02-07-2026 to 04-07-2026) before 08:30 AM with fee payment.
Q4What is the educational qualification required for JIPMER Senior Resident post?
A
MD/MS/DNB in the respective discipline (for Medical/Surgical departments), MDS in the relevant specialty (for Dental), or DM/MCh as specified for certain departments. All qualifications must be from NMC/MCI/DCI-recognized Universities/Institutes, and all requirements must be completed by 31-07-2026.
Q5What is the age limit for JIPMER Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit is 45 years as on 31-07-2026. Age relaxation is available: 5 years for SC/ST, 3 years for OBC, 10 years for PwBD-UR/EWS, 13 years for PwBD-OBC, and 15 years for PwBD-SC/ST.
Q6What is the application fee for JIPMER Senior Resident July 2026?
A
Rs. 500/- for General/OBC(NCL)/EWS candidates, Rs. 250/- for SC/ST candidates, and NIL for PwBD candidates. Payment is through SBI Collect only.
Q7What is the exam pattern for JIPMER Senior Resident Written Test?
A
The Written Test consists of 40 objective type (Single Best Response) questions from the concerned specialty, carrying 80 marks (2 marks each), with a duration of 60 minutes. There is no negative marking. The test is in English medium only.
Q8What is the selection process for JIPMER Senior Resident July 2026?
A
Selection is through a Written Test (80% weightage) followed by an Interview (20% weightage). Candidates must score the minimum eligibility marks in the Written Test to qualify for the Interview: 40/80 for UR/EWS, 36/80 for UR-PwBD, and 32/80 for SC/ST/OBC.
Q9Can candidates currently working in a Government hospital apply for JIPMER Senior Resident?
A
Yes, but they must produce a valid No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their present employer in the prescribed format (Annexure-IV) at the time of Interview/Certificate Verification. Without the NOC, they will not be allowed to appear for the Interview under any circumstances.
Q10Will SRs recruited at JIPMER Puducherry also work at the Karaikal campus?
A
Yes. All Senior Residents recruited at JIPMER Puducherry will be posted at the JIPMER Karaikal Campus on a rotation basis for a period of not less than 3 months at a stretch.
