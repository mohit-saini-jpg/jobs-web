FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for Allahabad HC Research Associate 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is June 20, 2026 at 11:59 PM. The last date to update payment details is June 23, 2026.
Q2What is the salary for Research Associate at Allahabad High Court?
A
The fixed honorarium is ₹40,000 per month, subject to upward revision. No other allowances like HRA or accommodation are provided.
Q3Can final year LL.B. students apply for Allahabad HC Research Associate 2026?
A
Yes. Candidates who appeared in the LL.B. Final Year examination in 2026 and are awaiting results are also eligible to apply. However, they must produce their final year marksheet at the time of joining.
Q4Is Allahabad High Court Research Associate a permanent job?
A
No. It is a purely contractual engagement for 1 year, extendable up to 3 years based on satisfactory performance. The maximum tenure is 3 years. The engagement can be terminated at any time without prior notice.
Q5What is the application fee for Research Associate 2026?
A
The application fee is ₹500 plus applicable bank charges. Payment must be made through online mode only.
Q6Where will the Screening Test be held for Allahabad HC Research Associate?
A
The Screening Test and Interview are scheduled on August 8, 2026, and will be held at Prayagraj (Allahabad). Both stages may be conducted on the same day.
