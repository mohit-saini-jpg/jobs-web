Job Overview

The CSIR Institute of Genomics & Integrative Biology (CSIR IGIB) has officially announced the Technician (Gr.-II) Recruitment 2026 with 15 openings under Advt. No. 01/2026. Interested applicants can submit their online applications on or before 04 July 2026. Selected candidates will receive a salary in the pay scale of ₹19,900–₹63,200.
