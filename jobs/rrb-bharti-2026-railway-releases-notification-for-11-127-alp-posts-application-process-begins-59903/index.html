Job Details

RRB Bharti 2026: The Railway Recruitment Board ( Railway Recruitment Board ) has released the official notification for Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP) recruitment, bringing major relief to candidates waiting for railway jobs. A total of 11,127 vacancies will be filled across various railway zones in India under this recruitment drive.

The online application process has begun from May 15. Interested candidates can apply through the official portal RRB Application Portal . The last date to submit applications is June 14.

Candidates must register through the centralized recruitment system and select their preferred Railway Recruitment Board while filling out the application form. Selected candidates will be appointed across different railway zones in India.
