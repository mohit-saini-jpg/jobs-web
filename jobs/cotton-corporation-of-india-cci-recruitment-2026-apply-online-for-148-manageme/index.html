Cotton Corporation of India CCI Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection for CCI Recruitment 2026 will be made through a Computer Based Test followed by Document Verification. The written test will consist of 120 objective-type MCQs to be completed in 120 minutes. 1 mark will be awarded for each correct answer, with 0.25 marks deducted for each wrong answer. The test covers General English, Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, General Knowledge, Computer Proficiency (post-wise), and Subject Knowledge specific to the post. Minimum qualifying percentage for the Computer Based Test is as follows: Examinations will be conducted in Mumbai/Navi Mumbai, Hyderabad, New Delhi, Chennai, Lucknow, Chandigarh, Kolkata, Bengaluru, Ahmedabad, Patna, and Jaipur.
