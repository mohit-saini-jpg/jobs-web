GDCA CHS Exam 2026 Hall Ticket Out

Co‑operative Commissioner and Registrar, Co‑operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune has released the GDCA & CHM Exam 2026 hall ticket on the official GDCA portal. The hall ticket is available online for all candidates whose GDCA/CHM application forms have been accepted for the 2026 examination.

According to the official notification, the GDCA & CHM Exam 2026 will be conducted from 26 May 2026 to 28 May 2026 at various examination centres across Maharashtra. As per recent candidate guidance, GDCA hall tickets were to be made available in candidates’ dashboards by 15 May 2026 and can now be downloaded by logging into the GDCA website.

How to Download GDCA & CHM Exam Hall Ticket 2026

  • Visit the official GDCA website gdca.maharashtra.gov.in (GDCA portal link as shared in official updates and student instructions).
  • Click on Login / Candidate Login and enter your username and password created at the time of registration.
  • After login, open “My Account” from the top menu.
  • In your account dashboard, scroll down to find the section where GDCA Form and Hall Ticket options are displayed.
  • Click on Hall Ticket and select the Exam Year 2026 from the dropdown list.
  • Choose the correct exam type - GDCA if you applied for GDCA, or CHM if you applied for Co‑operative Housing Management - and then click to view/download the hall ticket.
  • Your GDCA/CHM Exam Hall Ticket 2026 will open; download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies to carry on all exam days.

Details Mentioned on GDCA CHS Exam Hall Ticket 2026

The GDCA & CHM Exam 2026 hall ticket contains key exam and candidate details that must be checked carefully:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Username / registration details and roll number (if allotted)
  • Exam name: GDCA Exam 2026 and/or CHM Exam 2026
  • Exam dates and individual paper schedule between 26 May 2026 and 28 May 2026
  • Exam centre name, code, city and full address
  • Instructions and important guidelines to be followed in the examination hall

If any discrepancy is noticed in your name, photo, exam type (GDCA/CHM) or exam centre details, you should immediately contact the GDCA helpline/office before the exam dates.

Documents Required with GDCA CHS Exam Hall Ticket 2026

On each day of the GDCA & CHM Exam 2026, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the GDCA/CHM hall ticket 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence, Passport or any other government‑issued ID as generally accepted for state examinations.
  • Basic permitted stationery such as blue/black ball‑point pens and other items mentioned in the exam instructions.

Candidates without the hall ticket and valid photo ID will not be permitted to enter the examination hall.

Exam‑Day Instructions for GDCA & CHM Exam 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the GDCA & CHM Exam 2026, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the hall ticket, so that document verification and seating can be completed; latecomers may not be allowed entry once the exam starts.
  • Carry only essential items: GDCA/CHM hall ticket, valid photo ID and permitted stationery; follow any additional instructions given with the hall ticket or in the official notification.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not permitted), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material into the examination hall; violation may lead to cancellation of your paper and further action.
  • Sit only on the seat allotted to you and follow instructions given by invigilators; indulging in unfair means or talking to other candidates can result in disqualification from the exam.
  • Keep your hall ticket safely even after the exam, as it may be required for result reference and further GDCA/CHM certification processes.
