The Defence Electronics Application Laboratory (DRDO DEAL) has officially announced the DRDO Paid Internship Recruitment 2026 with 45 openings.   • Candidates who wish to apply for DRDO DEAL Various Posts Recruitment 2026 through offline mode should follow the steps below.

  • Download the application form from the official website: www.drdo.gov.in/drdo/careers.
  • Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
  • Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
