BFUHS Medical Officer (General) Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (BFUHS), Faridkot has invited online applications for 630 Medical Officer (General) posts under the Department of Health and Family Welfare, Punjab. This recruitment is issued vide Advertisement No. BFU-26/26. Eligible candidates holding an MBBS degree can apply online from 16 July 2026 to 5 August 2026. Selection will be through a written examination conducted by BFUHS. Here are the complete details of eligibility, vacancy breakup, age limit, salary, fee and how to apply.

BFUHS Medical Officer Eligibility Criteria

MBBS Degree Registered with the Punjab Medical Council or with any duly constituted Medical Council in India Knowledge of Punjabi Language up to Matriculation Standard or its equivalent.
