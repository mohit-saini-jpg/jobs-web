GSRTC Conductor Recruitment 2026 : Exam Pattern

OMR-based Competitive Written Examination Pattern: | The question paper will be based on Class 12 standard (Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board). Medium of examination: Gujarati for all subjects except English Grammar (which will be in English). 1 mark per question; 100 questions in total; duration 1 hour. Negative marking: 0.25 marks deducted for each wrong answer, blank/unattempted, overwritten, or multiple-option responses. If the candidate marks option (E) on the OMR sheet to indicate they do not wish to answer, no negative marking will be applied for that question. Candidates must use Blue or Black ballpoint pen only.

GSRTC Conductor Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Only online applications via https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in will be accepted; no other mode will be entertained. If multiple applications are submitted, only the last submitted (final) application will be considered; all previous applications will be automatically cancelled. Fee paid for previous applications will not be refunded. If the last submitted application has no fee payment, that application along with all previous ones will be rejected. Candidates in GSRTC / Government / Semi-Government service must inform their department/office within 7 days of applying and must obtain and submit a NOC at the time of document verification. The date of birth as per SSC Board Certificate or School Leaving Certificate will be the final accepted date of birth; any subsequent change will not be permitted. Birth extract/certificate is NOT accepted. Class 12 qualifications, Conductor Licence, Badge, Computer certificate, caste certificates, and all other documents must have been obtained before the last date of application; documents dated after the last date will not be accepted. Only original Gujarat domicile residents of SC/ST/SEBC/EWS categories will be entitled to reservation benefits. Candidates from other states are not entitled to reservation. Reservation category once mentioned in the application form cannot be changed later. If the caste stated in the application does not match the certificate, the application will be rejected - even if the candidate is eligible under the General category. Candidates must not bring any handwritten/printed material, calculator, mobile phone, smart watch, earphone, earbuds, or any electronic device to the examination hall; violation will result in disqualification. Any identification mark, writing, or alphabet in the OMR answer sheet will result in disqualification from the examination. Inclusion in the final Selection List does not automatically entitle a candidate to appointment; the appointing authority's decision regarding suitability is final. If any information provided in the application is found to be false, the application will be cancelled at any stage, and the Corporation reserves the right to take legal action. Candidates with pending criminal cases at any court of law at the time of appointment will have their applications rejected. RTI applications for recruitment-related information will not be entertained until the entire recruitment process is completed. Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be Ahmedabad only. GSRTC reserves the right to cancel or modify the recruitment process at any stage without assigning reasons; in such cases, the application fee will not be refunded.

GSRTC Conductor Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the total number of vacancies for GSRTC Conductor Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 4318 Conductor posts have been announced by Gujarat State Road Transport Corporation (GSRTC) for direct recruitment on a fixed pay contract basis. GSRTC reserves the right to increase or decrease the number of vacancies.
Q2What is the fixed pay for the GSRTC Conductor post?
A
Selected candidates will be appointed on a contract basis with a fixed pay of Rs. 26,000/- per month for a period of 5 years. After satisfactory completion of 5 years of service, candidates become eligible for regular appointment as per GSRTC's prevailing pay scale for the Conductor post. No other allowances or benefits will be payable during the contract period except as per rules.
Q3What are the essential qualifications for GSRTC Conductor Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must have passed Class 12 (10+2) from GSEB or an equivalent recognized board, must hold a valid Conductor Licence issued by the Regional Transport Authority (RTA) along with a Badge, and must possess a Basic Computer Knowledge certificate as per the prescribed Gujarat Government resolution. All these must be valid as on the last date of application (05-07-2026).
Q4What is the age limit for GSRTC Conductor Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age for General (Male) candidates is 35 years as on the last date of application (05-07-2026). General Female candidates can apply up to 40 years; Reserved category Male candidates up to 40 years; Reserved category Female candidates up to 45 years; Ex-Servicemen and Divyang (PwD) candidates up to 45 years. Under no circumstances shall the age with all relaxations exceed 45 years.
Q5What is the selection process for GSRTC Conductor Recruitment 2026?
A
All registered candidates will be called for a 100-mark OMR-based Competitive Written Examination of 1-hour duration. Based on merit in the written exam, candidates will be shortlisted at a ratio of 1.5 per vacancy for document verification. A Provisional Selection List and Waiting List (at 20% of vacancies, category-wise) will be prepared after document verification. There are no minimum qualifying marks, but candidates scoring zero or negative will not be eligible for document verification.
Q6Is there negative marking in the GSRTC Conductor written examination?
A
Yes. For every wrong answer, blank/skipped response, overwritten answer, or multiple options marked, 0.25 marks will be deducted. However, if a candidate marks option (E) on the OMR sheet indicating they do not wish to answer that question, no negative marking will be applied for that particular question.
Q7What is the application fee for GSRTC Conductor Recruitment 2026?
A
General (Unreserved) candidates must pay Rs. 300/- + GST. All reserved category candidates (SEBC, SC, ST, EWS, Divyang, Ex-Servicemen — both Male and Female) must pay Rs. 200/- + GST. Fee must be paid online via https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in and is non-refundable under any circumstances.
Q8Can widow and sports persons get additional marks in GSRTC Conductor Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. Widow candidates who have not remarried are entitled to 5% additional marks on their OMR written examination score at all stages of selection for priority consideration. Sports persons who have represented at National/International level, Inter-University level, or All India School Sports Federation level in the specified 25 sports disciplines are also entitled to 5% additional marks on their OMR score. Relevant certificates from competent authorities must be produced at document verification, failing which the additional marks will not be awarded.
Q9What types of PwD (Divyang) disabilities are eligible for GSRTC Conductor Recruitment 2026?
A
The following disability categories are eligible: Low Vision (LV) 40–70% (Category A), Hard of Hearing (HH) 40–70% (Category B), Leprosy Cured (LC), Acid Attack Victim (AAV), OA/OL/BA/BL/OAL/BLOA/BLA type locomotor disabilities subject to Medical Board opinion (Category C), and Mental Illness (MI) 40–70% (Category D & E). Disability certificate must be from a Government Hospital Superintendent / Civil Surgeon / Medical Board and must be held as on the date of online application. Divyang candidates selected through the Divyang quota will not be entitled to future inter-class transfer policy benefits in GSRTC.
Q10How long will the GSRTC Conductor 2026 Waiting List remain valid?
A
The Waiting List (Pratiksha Yadi) will remain valid for 2 years from the date of its publication OR until the result of the next recruitment examination for the Conductor post is declared — whichever is earlier. A category-wise waiting list will be prepared at 20% of the total vacancies. After this period, the waiting list will automatically stand cancelled. GSRTC reserves the right to modify the percentage of waiting list if required.
