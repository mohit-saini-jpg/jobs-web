Where to Download the Uniraj Admit Card 2026

The Uniraj Admit Card 2026 is available only online. It is not sent by post or distributed manually at colleges. Candidates can download it from:

  • uniraj.ac.in - the official University of Rajasthan website
  • univraj.org - used for UG annual exam hall tickets
  • erp.univraj.org - the ERP student portal used for login-based downloads

How to Download Uniraj Admit Card 2026 (Step-by-Step)

Follow these steps to download your UG admit card:

  • Visit the official website - uniraj.ac.in or univraj.org.
  • Look for the link titled "Admit Card," "Theory Exam Admit Card," or "Download Admit Card" on the homepage.
  • Enter your login credentials - typically your registration ID/application ID and password.
  • Click "Sign In" or "Proceed."
  • Your admit card will appear on the screen with your exam details.
  • Check every detail carefully, then download and take a printout.

Steps for PG (URATPG) Candidates

PG entrance candidates may need to follow a slightly different login process:

  • Visit uniraj.ac.in.
  • Click on "Download Admit Card of URATPG."
  • Enter your application ID, date of birth, and mother's name as required.
  • Click "Sign In" to view and download the admit card.

Details Mentioned on the Uniraj Admit Card 2026

The hall ticket contains identifying and exam-related information used for verification at the exam centre. Based on verified reports, it typically includes:

  • Candidate's name
  • Father's name and mother's name
  • Date of birth
  • Registration/roll number
  • Exam centre name and address
  • Photograph of the candidate
  • Signature of the candidate

Exact subject-wise exam date and time on the admit card, and the reporting time, are not detailed in the available reports - these will be printed on your individual hall ticket, so refer to your own downloaded copy for these specifics.

Important Instructions for Exam Day

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on your admit card.
  • Carry a valid photo ID along with the printed admit card - entry may be denied without both.
  • Read all instructions printed on the admit card, including rules on prohibited items such as mobile phones, calculators, and notes.
  • Do not write or scribble on the admit card. Sign only in the space provided, preferably in front of the invigilator.
  • Keep the admit card safe until all your exams (theory and practical) are fully over, as it may be required again.
  • If you notice any mistake in your name, course, photograph, or exam centre, contact your college or the Uniraj exam branch immediately - do not wait until exam day.

What If You Cannot Download the Uniraj Admit Card 2026?

If the admit card is not visible on the portal, it could be due to a pending fee payment, incomplete registration, or a technical/server issue. In such cases, candidates should contact their college administration or the University of Rajasthan exam branch directly rather than waiting near the exam date. The exact helpline number or email ID for this purpose is not confirmed in the available reports - candidates should check the "Contact Us" section of uniraj.ac.in for current details.
