FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies for PSPCL Assistant Lineman Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 3000 vacancies have been announced for the post of Assistant Lineman (ALM) in Punjab State Power Corporation Limited (PSPCL) under Advertisement No. CRA 318/26.
Q2What is the last date to apply for PSPCL ALM Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for online registration and submission of application is 09-07-2026. The last date for fee deposit is 14-07-2026.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for PSPCL Assistant Lineman 2026?
A
Candidates must have passed Matriculation or equivalent examination and hold an ITI certificate in Electrician Trade or Wireman Trade issued by NCVT or SCVT.
Q4What is the age limit for PSPCL ALM Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 18 and 37 years of age as on 01-01-2026. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/BC (5 years), PwD (10 years), Ex-Servicemen (as per service period), and certain categories of women (upper limit 40 years) as per Punjab Government rules.
Q5What is the pay scale for PSPCL Assistant Lineman?
A
The minimum basic pay is Rs. 19,900/- per month as per 7th CPC/Pay Matrix. During the probation period of 3 years, only the minimum admissible pay will be paid without Grade Pay, DA, or annual increment (except TA and medical reimbursement).
Q6What is the application fee for PSPCL ALM Recruitment 2026?
A
The total application fee (including 18% GST) is Rs. 1416/- for General candidates and Rs. 885/- for SC/PwD candidates, payable online only. The fee is non-refundable.
Q7What is the exam pattern for PSPCL ALM Online Test 2026?
A
The exam consists of two parts: Part-I has 50 MCQ questions on Punjabi Language (qualifying, minimum 50% required) and Part-II has 100 MCQ questions covering Technical (Electrician/Wireman), Punjabi Language, General Knowledge, Reasoning, and Arithmetic. Total duration is 3 hours. There is no negative marking.
Q8Is Punjabi language mandatory for PSPCL ALM Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. Candidates must have passed Punjabi at least at Matriculation level. Additionally, they must score a minimum of 50% marks (25 out of 50) in Part-I (Punjabi Language qualifying test) of the online exam. Failure to qualify Part-I will disqualify the candidate regardless of Part-II marks.
Q9Is the PSPCL ALM Recruitment 2026 only for Punjab domicile candidates?
A
No, any eligible Indian citizen can apply. However, reservation benefits (including women reservation, SC, BC, EWS, Ex-Servicemen, PwD categories) are applicable only to candidates with Punjab Domicile.
Q10How will the final merit list be prepared for PSPCL ALM Recruitment 2026?
A
The final merit list will be prepared based on marks secured in Part-II of the Computer Based Test only (among candidates who qualify Part-I). In case of a tie, the candidate with higher age will be placed at higher merit. For Sports Person category candidates, final merit is prepared by the Office of Director/Sports, Punjab. Normalization will be applied if the exam is held in multiple shifts.
