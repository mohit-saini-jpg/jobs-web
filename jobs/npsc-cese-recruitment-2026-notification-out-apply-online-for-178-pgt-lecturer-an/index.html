Qualification / Eligibility

DetailsCandidates must fulfill all eligibility conditions as on the closing date of online application (02-06-2026). Only those candidates who possess the requisite qualification acquired from a recognized university/institution at the time of applying will be eligible. Merely registering or appearing in the examination does not make a candidate eligible. The post-wise minimum educational qualification requirements are as follows: | For Assistant Professors - Hr. Education Department: Master's Degree from a recognized University with at least 55% marks (relaxable by 5% for ST/SC). Candidates who have been awarded Ph.D. in accordance with UGC Regulations 2009 are exempted from NET/SLET/SET requirement. NET/SLET/SET shall not be required for such Masters Degree Programmes in disciplines for which NET/SLET/SET accredited test is not conducted. | For Assistant Professors - CTE (Hr. Education Department): Master's Degree from a recognized University with at least 55% marks (relaxable by 5% for ST/SC/differently abled candidates). Candidates applying for CTE posts must appear for separate Optional Papers as per the examination pattern. | For Lecturers - Technical Education Department: (i) Civil Engineering: B.E/B.Tech with minimum 60% aggregate or 1st Class. (ii) Electronics & Electrical Engineering: B.E/B.Tech with minimum 60% aggregate or 1st Class. (iii) M.Sc Physics/Chemistry/Mathematics/English with 60% (relaxable by 5% for SC/ST). | For Lecturers/WET/Research Associate (RA)/Training-Cum: Post Graduate with 50% Marks (relaxable by 5% for ST/SC category). | For PGT - School Education Department: Post Graduate with 50% Marks (relaxable by 5% for ST/SC/differently abled candidates). | For Graduate Teachers - School Education Department: Graduate/Post Graduate with 50% Marks (relaxable by 5% for ST/SC/differently abled candidates).

How to Apply

  1. 1Applications must be submitted online only through the NPSC portal at https://npsc.nagaland.gov.in. No hard copy applications will be accepted. The OTR (One Time Registration) starts from 12-05-2026 (Noon) and closes on 02-06-2026 (Noon). Candidates who have previously completed the OTR process may directly submit the online application. Candidates are requested to complete OTR at the earliest to avoid online traffic/complications due to last-minute submission.
  2. 2Only those candidates with requisite qualification for CESE-2026 may register. Government employees must submit a "NO OBJECTION CERTIFICATE" signed by the Head of the Department at the time of submission of the online application form. For any assistance/clarification, candidates may refer to FAQs from the Commission's website or call the Helpdesk No. +91 9402988506 on working days (10:00 AM to 03:00 PM). Any issues/complaints must be settled on or before the last date of submission - no issues/complaints will be entertained thereafter.
  3. 3Documents to be Submitted when Called by Commission:
  4. 4HSLC/Matriculation/Equivalent Admit Card for proof of Date of Birth (no other certificate will be accepted for age proof)
  5. 5Educational Qualification Certificates - Mark Sheets & Pass certificates/provisional pass certificates from matriculation onwards till requisite qualification
  6. 6Indigenous Inhabitant Certificate, Scheduled Tribe Certificate, Backward Tribe Certificate (as applicable) - signed only by District Authority not below the rank of Additional Deputy Commissioner
  7. 7For PwBDs - certificate from competent medical authority in Form C, D & E as mandated in No.NPSC/EXAM-2/2025 dt 07.08.2025
  8. 8SC/ST & B.T. Certificates as relevant
  9. 9All documents/certificates must be duly signed by the concerned authority
  10. 10Apply Online at: https://npsc.nagaland.gov.in

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1Q1. What is NPSC CESE Recruitment 2026?
A
Nagaland PSC CESE Recruitment 2026 is a Common Educational Services Examination conducted by the Nagaland Public Service Commission (NPSC), Kohima to fill teaching and academic posts like Assistant Professor, Lecturer, PGT, Graduate Teacher, Research Associate and Librarian under various education departments of the Government of Nagaland. Recruitment is being conducted through Advertisement No. NPSC‑1/CESE‑2026 (No.NPSC/EXAM‑21/2023) published in April 2026.
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are announced under NPSC CESE 2026 and for which posts?
A
The CESE 2026 notification covers around 178 vacancies clubbed into 54 item numbers across Higher Education, School Education, Technical Education and SCERT. These include a large number of Assistant Professor posts in subjects like Chemistry, English, Mathematics, Physics, Botany, Zoology, Commerce and Sociology, along with Lecturer, PGT, Graduate Teacher, Librarian and Research Associate posts.
Q3Q3. What are the application dates and exam date for NPSC CESE 2026?
A
The online One Time Registration (OTR) and application process for NPSC CESE 2026 opens on 12 May 2026 (noon) and closes on 02 June 2026 (noon). The written examination for CESE 2026 is tentatively scheduled for 11 July 2026, and candidates should regularly check the official website for final exam routine and admit‑card dates.
Q4Q4. What is the age limit and age relaxation for NPSC CESE 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 21 and 32 years of age as on 1 January 2026 to be eligible for CESE 2026. Upper‑age relaxations are available for SC/ST candidates (typically 5 years), Government employees, Persons with Benchmark Disabilities and Ph.D. holders for specified Assistant Professor posts, as per norms indicated in recent CESE notifications.
Q5Q5. What are the essential educational qualifications for Assistant Professor posts under CESE 2026?
A
For most Assistant Professor posts, candidates must have a Master’s Degree in the relevant subject with at least 55% marks (relaxable by 5% for SC/ST and some categories) from a recognized university. In addition, candidates should have cleared NET/SLET/SET or hold a Ph.D. degree in accordance with UGC Regulations, in which case NET/SLET/SET may be exempted as per rules; for disciplines where NET is not conducted, NET is not mandatory.
Q6Q6. What is the qualification requirement for PGT and Graduate Teacher posts?
A
For PGT posts under the School Education Department, the minimum qualification is a Post Graduate degree in the concerned subject with at least 50% marks (relaxable for SC/ST/differently abled candidates) along with a B.Ed. For Graduate Teacher posts, a Graduate/Post Graduate degree with 50% marks in the relevant subject and B.Ed is required, again with similar relaxations for reserved categories.
Q7Q7. What is the application fee for NPSC CESE 2026 and is OTR chargeable?
A
One Time Registration (OTR) is free of cost for all candidates on the NPSC portal. For CESE 2026, candidates must pay an examination fee of ₹300 plus a convenience fee of ₹25 and applicable payment‑gateway charges at the time of submitting the online application.
Q8Q8. How can I apply online for NPSC CESE 2026?
A
Candidates must first complete the One Time Registration (OTR) on the NPSC portal npsc.nagaland.gov.in and then proceed to fill the CESE 2026 online application form using their OTR profile. They need to enter personal and educational details, upload required documents, pay the examination fee online, submit the form, and download/print the final application for future reference.
Q9Q9. What documents will be required later during verification for NPSC CESE 2026?
A
During document verification, candidates will have to produce their HSLC/Matriculation admit card for proof of date of birth, mark sheets and pass certificates from HSLC onwards, Indigenous Inhabitant Certificate, ST or Backward Tribe certificate where applicable, and PwBD certificate in prescribed formats, all duly issued by competent authorities. Government employees must also produce a No Objection Certificate from the Head of Department as per NPSC rules.
Q10Q10. Where can I download the NPSC CESE 2026 notification PDF and get updates on admit card and results?
A
The detailed CESE 2026 notification PDF, any corrigenda, examination routine, admit‑card download link and result notices will be made available on the official Nagaland PSC website npsc.nagaland.gov.in in the notifications/examinations section. Candidates should visit the website regularly to track updates, as no separate hard‑copy communication is issued by the Commission.
