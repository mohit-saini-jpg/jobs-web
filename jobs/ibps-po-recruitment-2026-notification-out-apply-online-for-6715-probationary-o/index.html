IBPS PO Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

The IBPS CRP PO/MT-XVI selection process consists of the following stages: Online Preliminary Examination (Objective Test) Online Main Examination (Objective and Descriptive) Personality Test (self-report, non-qualifying but mandatory for interview eligibility) Common Interview Provisional Allotment There is a penalty of 1/4th (0.25) marks for each wrong answer in the objective tests. No penalty applies for unattempted questions. Total marks for Interview: 100 Minimum qualifying marks in interview: 40% (35% for SC/ST/OBC/PwBD candidates) Weightage ratio of Online Main Exam to Interview: 80:20 Final merit is based on combined score of Main Exam and Interview.
