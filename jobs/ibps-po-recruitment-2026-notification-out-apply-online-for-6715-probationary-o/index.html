IBPS PO/MT-XVI Selection Process 2026

The IBPS PO/MT-XVI Selection Process 2026 consists of four stages: Online Preliminary Examination, Online Main Examination, Personality Test, and Common Interview. Only candidates who qualify at each stage move forward to the next, and provisional allotment is done based on the final combined score and vacancies reported by the banks. There is a penalty of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer in the objective tests, while unattempted questions carry no penalty. Candidates who qualify the Main Examination are called for a self-report Personality Test, which is non-qualifying in nature but mandatory to attend for interview eligibility. The Personality Test may be conducted in proctored mode or at designated examination centres, as decided by IBPS.
