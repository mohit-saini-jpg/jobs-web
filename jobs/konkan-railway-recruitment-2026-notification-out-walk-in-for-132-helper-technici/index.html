Important Instructions

Candidates must ensure they fulfill the eligibility criteria before attending the walk-in interview for a particular post. Registration time is strictly 09:00 hrs to 12:00 hrs only on the date of the walk-in interview; late arrivals will not be registered. Candidate can appear for interview at this one venue only; no TA/DA will be paid for attending the walk-in interview. This engagement is on a fixed term contract basis and shall not result in regular/permanent absorption in KRCL service. Candidates must give an undertaking in writing to KRCL to this effect. Selected candidate can be posted and transferred at any time, anywhere to the KRCL establishment during the contract period. Contract may be terminated by giving one month’s notice from either side or payment in lieu thereof. Contract can be terminated without notice if performance is not satisfactory. No accommodation will be provided and no Food/Mess Allowance will be paid by KRCL. Even after contractual agreement, if any document/certificate/information is found incorrect or false, contractual service will be immediately terminated without prior notice, besides legal action. Candidates who are already working in KRCL on contract basis and selected against this notification do not require to follow the condition of notice period for joining the new post; however, a relieving letter from the concerned Konkan Railway authority will be required at the time of joining. The Corporation reserves the right to cancel/restrict/curtail/enlarge the contract engagement process without any prior notice and without assigning any reason. Any kind of canvassing will result in disqualification of the candidate at any stage of selection without assigning any reason. Statutory Income Tax, Professional Tax, and other applicable taxes will be deducted at source. The information on KRCL’s website www.konkanrailway.com at its link Recruitment > Current Notifications should be checked for updates.
