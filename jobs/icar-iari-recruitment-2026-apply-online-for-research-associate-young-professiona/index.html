Qualification / Eligibility

Research Associate (MOEFCC-I): PhD in Environmental Sciences/Plant Physiology from a recognized university/institution. Five years of research experience in greenhouse gas emission measurement from rice fields and emission factor development. Desirable: Experience in chromatographic GHG analysis, volatilization losses, crop modeling, and data analysis software. Young Professional II: Master’s degree (First class) in Environmental Sciences from a recognized university/institution. Two years of experience in GHG measurement and gas chromatographic analysis. Desirable: Proficiency in data analysis software and soil plant analysis. Young Professional I: Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university/institution. At least one year of experience in GHG sampling and chromatographic analysis. Desirable: Working experience of gas chromatograph and canopy analyzer.
