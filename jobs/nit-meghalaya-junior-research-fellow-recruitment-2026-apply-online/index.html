This is a great opportunity for candidates with M.Tech/M.E or GATE-qualified B.Tech/B.E/M.Sc degrees. The last date to submit the application is 24 July 2026. NIT Meghalaya JRF Eligibility Criteria 2026

Essential Qualifications: M.Tech / M.E in RF and Microwave or Wireless Communication Engineering or related disciplines, OR M.Sc with valid GATE/NET qualification, OR GATE-qualified B.Tech / B.E in Electronics and Communication Engineering or related disciplines. Age Limit: Maximum 30 years (relaxation as per Institute norms). Job Nature: Design, simulation, analysis, and prototyping of Intelligent Reflecting Surface (IRS) and Drones. Other Benefits: Selected candidates are eligible to register for Ph.D. at NIT Meghalaya as per institute rules.
