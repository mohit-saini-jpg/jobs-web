FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for CSC Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the application for CSC Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator Recruitment 2026 is 20 June 2026. Candidates should complete the application process before the deadline.
Q2What is the minimum age required for CSC Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator Vacancy 2026?
A
Candidates must be at least 18 years old as of the last date of application. Any upper age limit will be applicable as per CSC guidelines
Q3What educational qualifications are required for Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator posts?
A
Applicants must have passed 12th (Senior Secondary), or 10th with 2 years ITI, or 10th with 3 years Polytechnic Diploma from a recognized institution
Q4Is Aadhaar Supervisor Certification mandatory for this recruitment?
A
Yes. Candidates must possess a valid UIDAI-authorized Aadhaar Operator/Supervisor Certificate at the time of application and throughout the contract period
Q5What is the job location for CSC Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator Recruitment 2026?
A
Selected candidates will work at Aadhaar Seva Kendras (ASKs) located in various districts of Telangana, Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, and Odisha.
Q6What is the duration of the CSC Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator contract?
A
The engagement is purely contractual and will be for a period of one year. The contract may be governed by CSC e-Governance Services India Ltd rules and requirements.
Q7What salary will be paid to Aadhaar Supervisors and Operators?
A
The remuneration will be based on the minimum wages prescribed for semi-skilled manpower in the respective state. Additional benefits, if any, will be provided as per CSC norms.
Q8Can Village Level Entrepreneurs (VLEs) apply for CSC Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator Recruitment 2026?
A
No. Village Level Entrepreneurs (VLEs) are not eligible to apply for these Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator positions
Q9How can candidates obtain Aadhaar Supervisor Certification?
A
Candidates can apply for the Aadhaar Supervisor Exam through the UIDAI-authorized examination portal. After approval by the concerned State Team and successful completion of the exam and LMS certification, candidates can obtain the required certificate.
Q10Which states have vacancies under CSC Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator Recruitment 2026?
A
Vacancies are available in multiple districts across Telangana, Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, and Odisha. Candidates can apply for openings in their preferred district, subject to eligibility and vacancy availability.
