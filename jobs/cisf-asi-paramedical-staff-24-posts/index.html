FAQs

Q1Can candidates apply for more than one post in CISF ASI Recruitment 2026?
A
No. Candidates are advised to submit the online application for one post only. In case of receipt of multiple applications from any candidate, the first application only, as received, will be processed.
Q2Is there any negative marking in the CISF ASI Written Examination?
A
No, there is no negative marking in the Written Examination (OMR/CBT). All questions will be of Objective Multiple Choice type.
Q3Are female candidates exempted from application fee?
A
Yes, female candidates are fully exempted from payment of application fee. SC/ST/ESM candidates are also exempted from payment of fee.
Q4Are Persons with Disabilities (PwD) eligible to apply for CISF ASI Recruitment 2026?
A
No, Persons with Disabilities (PwD) are not eligible to apply for this recruitment as per the official notification.
Q5Will selected candidates be posted at a fixed location?
A
No. Candidates selected for appointment are liable to serve anywhere in Indian Territory and abroad as per CISF requirements.
Q6What is the last date to apply online for CISF ASI Para Medical Staff Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to apply online for CISF ASI Para Medical Staff Recruitment 2026 is 07 July 2026 (upto 2359 Hrs).
