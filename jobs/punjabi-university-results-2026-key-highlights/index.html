How to Check Punjabi University Exam Result 2026

Use the following simple steps to check and download your Punjabi University Exam Result 2026 online through the official PUP results portal.

  • Open your browser and go to the official results website of Punjabi University: https://results.pupexamination.ac.in.
  • On the homepage, look for the "Quick Search Result Here" or the course-wise result list and click on the relevant link, or directly open the result page at https://results.pupexamination.ac.in/t8/results/results.php.
  • In the list of available results, find your course and semester title such as BA Hons School in Economics Sem I, MA Linguistics Sem III, B.Com LLB Sem IX, B.Tech Mechanical Sem V, B.Voc, MBA Distance, PGDCA or PG Diploma in Yoga.
  • Click on the specific result name to open the result entry page.
  • Enter your examination roll number or other required credentials in the given field and click on the Submit button.
  • Your Punjabi University semester result 2026 will be displayed on the screen, showing your subject-wise marks and result status.
  • Download the provisional result or marksheet and take a printout for future reference, admissions and documentation.
  • Carefully verify your name, roll number, course, semester and marks, and contact the Punjabi University examination branch or your college if there are any errors or RL (Result Later) remarks that need clarification.
