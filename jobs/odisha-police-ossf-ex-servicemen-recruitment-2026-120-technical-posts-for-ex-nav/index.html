Odisha Police OSSF Ex-Servicemen Recruitment 2026 – 120 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for Odisha Police OSSF Ex-Servicemen Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the application (bio data and service particulars) for Odisha Police OSSF Marine technical posts is 1 July 2026. The application must reach the O/o Addl. D.G. of Police, Railways and Coastal Security, Odisha, Bhubaneswar by this date.
Q2When and where is the Odisha Marine Police OSSF recruitment 2026 scheduled?
A
The recruitment (in-person selection) is scheduled on 6th and 7th July 2026 at OSAP 7th Battalion, Bhubaneswar, starting at 09:00 AM. Candidates must report with all original documents and self-attested copies on one of these dates.
Q3How many vacancies are available in Odisha Police OSSF 2026?
A
Approximately 120 technical posts are available across five categories: Master (20), Syrang (20), Engine Driver (40), Dingy Driver (25), and Seaman Mechanic (15). The vacancy count may subsequently be varied by the organization.
Q4Who is eligible to apply for Odisha Police Marine Police OSSF recruitment 2026?
A
Only Ex-Navy and Ex-Coastguard personnel with experience in the Seaman, Communication, Engineering, or Electrical branches are eligible. Applicants must have retired after 01 July 2021 and must not have been discharged from service for more than 5 years.
Q5What is the age limit for Odisha OSSF recruitment 2026?
A
The maximum age limit depends on the rank at retirement: 43 years for Seaman II and Seaman, 50 years for NCO, and 54 years for JCO.
Q6What is the salary for Odisha Marine Police OSSF technical posts?
A
The monthly contractual remuneration is: Master – Rs. 55,000; Syrang and Engine Driver – Rs. 50,000 each; Dingy Driver and Seaman Mechanic – Rs. 45,000 each.
Q7What documents are required for Odisha Police OSSF recruitment?
A
Candidates must carry originals and self-attested copies of: PPO, Discharge Book, ESM ID Card, Aadhaar Card, 3 passport-size colour photographs, original bank passbook (where pension is credited), and course certificate relevant to the branch applied for.
Q8What is the contract period for Odisha Marine Police OSSF posts?
A
The appointment is on a purely contractual basis for three years. The contract may be extended further based on the candidate's performance, ability, sincerity, and devotion to duty.
Q9What is the medical category requirement for Odisha Police OSSF recruitment?
A
Candidates must hold medical category S1A1. Additionally, character must be graded as Exemplary in service records.
Q10Where should I send my application for Odisha Police OSSF Ex-Servicemen Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications (bio data with service particulars and self-attested document copies) must be sent to: O/o Additional Director General of Police, Railways and Coastal Security, Odisha, Bhubaneswar so as to reach by 1 July 2026. For queries, email [email protected] or call 0674-2311110.
