How to Check Solapur University Result 2026 Online

This section provides a simple step-by-step process for students to access and download their semester mark statement from the official portal.

Students can follow these steps to view their result:

  • Visit the official Solapur University website: sus.ac.in
  • Go to the Examination menu and click on Results, or directly open the Online Result March 2026 page.
  • Scroll to the relevant faculty section - for B.Tech/M.Tech results, look under "Faculty of Engineering & Technology."
  • Locate your course, branch, semester, and category (Fresher or Repeater) from the listed table.
  • Click the "Click Here" link next to your course to open the result PDF (ledger format).
  • The file will open or download as a PDF; use Ctrl+F (or Cmd+F) to search for your seat number or roll number within the document.
  • Download and save a copy of your mark statement for future reference.

Important Points to Note About the Result

This section highlights key disclaimers and notices published by the university alongside the result, which every student should be aware of before relying on the online mark statement.

  • Solapur University has clarified that the results published online are meant for immediate information only. The original mark sheet or Statement of Grades (SOG) issued through respective colleges is considered the sole authentic document for any official purpose.
  • The university has also stated that for students whose results are declared as EHB/RR (Eligible for Higher Standing/Result Reserved), a revised result will be announced once the previous semester's result process is completed.
  • Several results on the portal have been revised after the initial declaration date; students should check whether a "Revised" tag appears next to their course before treating the mark statement as final.
  • Results are declared in a ledger PDF format, listing multiple students together rather than as individual mark sheets - students need to locate their own seat number within the shared document.
