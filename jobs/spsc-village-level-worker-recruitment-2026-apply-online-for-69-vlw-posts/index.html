SPSC Village Level Worker Recruitment Expert Analysis

This recruitment is a great opportunity for those looking for Sarkari Naukri in Sikkim. The Village Level Worker role is a key position in rural development, and candidates with relevant experience will have an edge.

Who Should Apply SPSC Village Level Worker Recruitment

Ideal candidates for this role are graduates with a passion for rural development and community work. Those with experience in social work or related fields will be preferred.

SPSC Village Level Worker Recruitment Preparation Tips

Start by preparing for the written exam, focusing on subjects like general knowledge, reasoning, and village development. Practice mock tests to improve your time management and accuracy.

SPSC Village Level Worker Recruitment Salary Insights

The salary for Village Level Worker (VLW) posts is between ₹53,100 to ₹1,32,750 per month. This is a decent starting salary for a Sarkari Naukri, with opportunities for growth and promotion.

SPSC Village Level Worker Recruitment Job Profile

As a Village Level Worker, you will be responsible for implementing government schemes and programs at the village level. Your day-to-day work will involve interacting with villagers, conducting surveys, and providing support for rural development projects.

SPSC Village Level Worker Recruitment Selection Strategy

The selection process for VLW posts will involve a written exam and a personal interview. Focus on preparing for the written exam, and make sure to practice your communication skills for the interview.

SPSC Village Level Worker Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Sikkim Public Service Commission (SPSC) has released Advertisement No. 03/SPSC/EXAM/2026 dated 07 July 2026, inviting online applications from eligible local candidates for recruitment to 69 posts of Village Level Worker (VLW) under the Agriculture and Horticulture Department, Government of Sikkim. The SPSC Village Level Worker Recruitment 2026 drive will fill these posts on a temporary-regular basis in Level-9 of the Pay Matrix under the Sikkim State Subordinate Agriculture Service, through Direct Recruitment. Eligibility Criteria

Note: Candidates must fulfil all eligibility conditions, including educational qualification and age criteria, as on the closing date of application. Candidates awaiting final results are not eligible to apply, as per Notification No. 177/GEN/DOP dated 29.01.2026. Candidates must be able to read, write, and speak at least one State language of Sikkim. Candidates must be conversant with the local customs and traditions of Sikkim. Applications are invited only from eligible local candidates of Sikkim possessing a valid Sikkim Subject Certificate or Certificate of Identification (COI) issued by the competent authority.

Village Level Worker (VLW)B.Sc. in Agriculture or Horticulture from an ICAR-recognized University or Institute. Candidates holding an M.Sc. in any discipline of Agriculture or Horticulture must mandatorily also possess a B.Sc. in Agriculture or Horticulture.

SPSC VLW Recruitment 2026: Application Fee

Candidates need to pay an application fee of ₹200/- online through Net Banking, or Credit/Debit Card (Visa/MasterCard). The fee once deposited will not be refunded or adjusted against any other purpose.

SPSC Village Level Worker Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

The selection of candidates for the SPSC Village Level Worker Recruitment 2026 will be based on the following stages: Written Examination — as per the scheme and pattern given in Appendix-I of the official notification. Document Scrutiny — candidates who qualify in the written examination will be called for verification of original/attested documents on dates specified by the Commission. Candidates are advised to download the detailed syllabus and exam pattern from the official SPSC website before beginning preparation.

Pay Scale

Selected candidates will be placed in Level-9 of the Pay Matrix under the Sikkim State Subordinate Agriculture Service. During the period of Probation/Apprenticeship/Training, pay shall be governed as per Notification No. 489/GEN/DOP dated 31.10.2011.
