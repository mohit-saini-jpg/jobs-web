The CISF ASI Para Medical Staff Selection Process 2026 consists of multiple stages. Candidates must qualify at each stage to proceed to the next round. CISF ASI Para Medical Staff Exam Pattern 2026

The CISF ASI Para Medical Staff Exam 2026 is conducted in multiple stages. The Written Examination is objective in nature and conducted in OMR/CBT mode. Candidates who qualify the written exam are shortlisted for PST, Documentation, BVAT and Medical Examination at a ratio of about 5 (five) times the number of vacancies notified.
