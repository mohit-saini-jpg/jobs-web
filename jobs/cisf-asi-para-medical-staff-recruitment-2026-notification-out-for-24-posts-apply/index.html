Job Overview

Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) has invited online applications from eligible Male and Female Indian citizens for filling up temporary posts of Para Medical Staff in Pay Level-5 of 7th CPC (Rs. 29,200 – 92,300/-) through CISF ASI Para Medical Staff Recruitment 2026. All interested and eligible candidates can apply online at the official CISF recruitment website cisfrectt.cisf.gov.in from 08 June 2026. This article highlights all major details like eligibility, selection process, application dates and more.

Qualification / Eligibility

Details
  1. The recruitment notification includes vacancies for three para-medical posts. Candidates must fulfil the prescribed educational qualifications and age criteria to be eligible. The required qualifications vary according to the post applied for
  2. Candidates applying for ASI (Pharmacist) must have:
  3. Passed 10+2 from a recognized Board or University. Diploma or Degree in Pharmacy granted by an institution recognized by the Central or State Government. Should be registered as a "PHARMACIST" under the Pharmacy Act, 1948
  4. Candidates applying for ASI (X-Ray Technician) must have:
  5. Passed 10+2 from a recognized Board or University. 02 years Diploma in Radiography from an institution recognized by Central or State Government
  6. Candidates applying for ASI (Lab Technician) must have:
  7. Passed 10+2 from a recognized Board or University with Science. 02 years Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology from an institution recognized by any Central or State Government
  8. The age limit is the same for all three posts. Age will be calculated as on the closing date of online application i.e. 07 July 2026. Age relaxation of 5 years for SC/ST and 3 years for OBC candidates is applicable as per Government orders

Exam Pattern

The CISF ASI Para Medical Staff Exam 2026 is conducted in multiple stages. The Written Examination is objective in nature and conducted in OMR/CBT mode. Candidates who qualify the written exam are shortlisted for PST, Documentation, BVAT and Medical Examination at a ratio of about 5 (five) times the number of vacancies notified. | Objective Multiple Choice type written examination. Total Marks: 100 Duration: 2 Hours Questions are asked to test knowledge in the respective trade. Conducted in English and Hindi (bilingual). There is no negative marking. Candidates are shortlisted for PST based on their performance in the Written Examination.

How to Apply

  • 1Here, we have provided the direct, simple and easy steps to apply online for the CISF ASI Para Medical Staff Recruitment 2026. Every candidate can follow these steps one by one and submit the application form.
  • 2Visit the official CISF recruitment website at cisfrectt.cisf.gov.in.
  • 3Click on the "NEW REGISTRATION" button and complete the One-Time Registration process using your Mobile Number and Email ID.
  • 4Login using your Registration ID and auto-generated password and click on "Recruitment of Paramedical Staff in CISF – 2026" tab.
  • 5Fill in the essential details such as Registration ID, Password and Captcha and click "SUBMIT".
  • 6Fill the required personal and educational details in the online application form.
  • 7Upload your recent passport size photograph (not more than 3 months old), signature, Date of Birth Certificate and educational qualification certificate as per specified format.
  • 8Pay the application fee through UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit card or SBI Challan (cash at SBI branches).
  • 9Review all the details carefully, click "Final Submission" and take a printout of the submitted application form for future reference.

FAQs

Q1Can candidates apply for more than one post in CISF ASI Recruitment 2026?
A
No. Candidates are advised to submit the online application for one post only. In case of receipt of multiple applications from any candidate, the first application only, as received, will be processed.
Q2Is there any negative marking in the CISF ASI Written Examination?
A
No, there is no negative marking in the Written Examination (OMR/CBT). All questions will be of Objective Multiple Choice type.
Q3Are female candidates exempted from application fee?
A
Yes, female candidates are fully exempted from payment of application fee. SC/ST/ESM candidates are also exempted from payment of fee.
Q4Are Persons with Disabilities (PwD) eligible to apply for CISF ASI Recruitment 2026?
A
No, Persons with Disabilities (PwD) are not eligible to apply for this recruitment as per the official notification.
Q5Will selected candidates be posted at a fixed location?
A
No. Candidates selected for appointment are liable to serve anywhere in Indian Territory and abroad as per CISF requirements.
Q6What is the last date to apply online for CISF ASI Para Medical Staff Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to apply online for CISF ASI Para Medical Staff Recruitment 2026 is 07 July 2026 (upto 2359 Hrs).
