How to Apply

  • 18th standard marksheet (for Post Nos. 5 and 6)
  • 210th standard marksheet / Date of Birth proof
  • 312th / Graduation / Post-Graduation / all required educational qualification marksheets for all years
  • 4Computer Diploma/Degree marksheet from a recognized institution
  • 5CG Council Registration Certificate (wherever applicable)
  • 6Original Domicile Certificate issued by a competent authority (Chhattisgarh residents only)
  • 7Permanent Caste Certificate issued by a competent authority (for SC/ST/OBC candidates)
  • 8PwD Certificate from a Medical Board with minimum 40% disability (if applicable)
  • 9Identity proof (Aadhaar Card/Voter ID/PAN Card/Driving Licence with name, address and photo)
  • 10Experience Certificate (from Government/Semi-Government/Government-funded institutions only)
  • 11NOC/No Objection Certificate from current employer (for candidates working in Government/Semi-Government organizations)
  • 12Live employment registration certificate from Employment Office
  • 13Last 6 months' salary slips (mandatory for experience marks)
  • 14Appointment letter for claimed work experience (mandatory)
  • 15Online applications only - no other mode of application will be accepted; applications incomplete in any respect will be summarily rejected without prior notice.
  • 16Candidates must be permanent residents (domicile) of Chhattisgarh; domicile proof issued by a competent authority is mandatory.
  • 17All posts are purely contractual in nature under NHM Chhattisgarh HR Policy 2018. Contract period is up to 31 March 2027, extendable based on satisfactory performance and GOI/ROP approval.
  • 18Candidates can apply for a maximum of three posts; combined fee for all applied posts must be paid in a single UPI transaction.
  • 19Application fee payment via QR Code scan is mandatory; the UTR transaction number and date must be entered in the online form and a payment screenshot uploaded.
  • 20Applications with incorrect or false information will be summarily rejected; candidates will be personally responsible for any such errors.
  • 21All educational qualifications, experience, and registrations must be complete as on the date of notification.
  • 22Experience certificates will only be accepted from Government, Semi-Government, and Government-funded institutions. Voluntary/honorary experience will not be considered.
  • 23Experience marks will be given for a maximum of 5 years only. At least 12 months in a single organization counts as one year.
  • 24For experience marks, the last 6 months' salary slips and appointment letter must be attached with the application; otherwise experience marks will not be awarded.
  • 25Candidates currently working in Government/Semi-Government organizations must submit an NOC from their employer at the time of document verification.
  • 26For OBC candidates, a permanent caste certificate from a competent authority must be submitted; if not provided, the candidate will be treated as Unreserved.
  • 27For PwD candidates, a disability certificate showing a minimum 40% disability from a Medical Board is mandatory. For nursing cadre posts, only OL (One Leg) disability is eligible.
  • 28Candidates who successfully qualify the Skill Test must appear for document verification with all original documents; without original documents, no candidate will be allowed to participate in the verification process.
  • 29Posts marked with backlog vacancies are included in the advertised positions.
  • 30Canvassing in any form will be treated as disqualification.
  • 31All future correspondence regarding the selection process, shortlisting of candidates, and important dates will be published only on the official website https://mungeli.gov.in; candidates are responsible for regularly checking the website.
  • 32For online application guidance/support, contact WhatsApp No. 9109198889 or Email: [email protected] during office hours.
