MET Rank Card 2026 Released - Download Scorecard at manipal.edu

MET Rank Card 2026 has been released by the Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) on its official admission portal. Candidates who appeared for the Manipal Entrance Test (MET) 2026 can now download their rank cards online and participate in the counselling process for admission to BTech and other undergraduate programmes offered by the university.

The rank card contains the candidate's rank, application details, and admission-related information required during counselling. Candidates should carefully verify the details mentioned on the scorecard and complete counselling registration within the prescribed deadline.

How to Download MET Rank Card 2026

Follow these steps to download the rank card:

  • Visit the official MAHE admission portal.
  • Click on the MET Rank Card 2026 link.
  • Log in using your application number and password.
  • Open the rank card displayed on the screen.
  • Verify all details carefully.
  • Download the PDF.
  • Take a printout for counselling and admission purposes.

Details Mentioned on MET Rank Card 2026

Candidates should check the following information:

  • Candidate's Name
  • Application Number
  • MET Rank
  • Category Details (if applicable)
  • Course Applied For
  • Examination Details
  • Admission Session
  • Counselling Eligibility Information

Any discrepancy should be reported to MAHE immediately.

Documents Required for MET Counselling 2026

Keep the following documents ready:

  • MET Rank Card 2026
  • MET Admit Card
  • Class 10 Mark Sheet
  • Class 12 Mark Sheet
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Character Certificate
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size Photographs
  • Government-issued Photo ID
  • Fee Payment Receipt

MET 2026 Seat Allotment Process

Seat allotment is conducted based on:

  • MET Rank
  • Candidate Preferences
  • Seat Availability
  • Reservation Criteria (where applicable)

After seat allotment, candidates must confirm their admission by paying the prescribed fee within the given timeline.

Important Instructions for Candidates

  • Download the rank card only from the official website.
  • Complete counselling registration before the deadline.
  • Keep original documents ready for verification.
  • Monitor official announcements regularly.
  • Do not wait until the last day for choice filling and fee payment.
