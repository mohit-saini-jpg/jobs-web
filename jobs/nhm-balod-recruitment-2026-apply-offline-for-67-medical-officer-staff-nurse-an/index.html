NHM Balod Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

The selection process for NHM Balod 2026 is merit-based with marks assigned across three components. Candidates should understand how marks are distributed to prepare effectively. Step-by-Step Selection Process: Written/Academic Marks: Candidates are evaluated on the basis of qualifying marks from their academic certificates (scaled to the appropriate category above). Experience Weightage: Up to 20 marks are awarded for relevant work experience. Merit List Preparation: A combined merit list is prepared based on total marks. Document Verification: Shortlisted candidates must appear for document verification. Contract Appointment: Selected candidates are offered contractual appointments for the NHM period 2024-26. For equal marks between candidates, the older candidate is given preference. If age is also equal, the candidate with higher qualifying marks is ranked higher.
