Kerala Plus One Result 2026: What the Scorecard Will Contain

Once declared, the provisional online scorecard for the Kerala Plus One Result 2026 is expected to include the following details:

  • Student's name
  • Registration/roll number
  • School name and code
  • Subject-wise marks
  • Total marks obtained
  • Pass/fail (qualifying) status

The online result is provisional. Students will need to collect the original mark sheet from their respective school after the official declaration.

How to Check Kerala Plus One Result 2026 Online

Based on the process used for previous DHSE Kerala results, students are likely to be able to check their Kerala Plus One Result 2026 using the following methods once it is live. Exact field requirements (such as date of birth or father's name) should be confirmed on the live result page, as these can vary by portal.

Method 1: Official Website

  • Visit the official result portal at results.hse.kerala.gov.in or dhsekerala.gov.in.
  • Click on the link for "DHSE Plus One Result 2026" / "HSE First Year Result 2026".
  • Enter your roll number/registration number and the other required detail (such as date of birth), as prompted on the page.
  • Enter the captcha code, if shown.
  • Click "Submit" to view your result on screen.
  • Download and save the scorecard for future reference.

Method 2: SMS Service

  • Open the messaging app on your registered mobile number.
  • Type the message in the format: KERALA11<space>RegistrationNumber
  • Send it to 56263.
  • The result summary will be sent back to the same number.

Method 3: DigiLocker

  • Open the DigiLocker app or website.
  • Sign in using your registered mobile number and email ID.
  • Search for "DHSE Kerala First Year Result 2026".
  • View and download your digital marksheet.

Method 4: PRD Live App

  • Install the "PRD Live" app from the Play Store.
  • Open the app and navigate to the results section.
  • Select the Kerala Plus One Result 2026 option.
  • Enter your roll number to view the result.

Kerala Plus One Result 2026: School-Wise and District-Wise Result

Along with individual results, DHSE typically also publishes a school-wise result option on the official portal, where a school can enter its school code to view the consolidated pass percentage and performance of its students. This option, if enabled for 2026, would appear on the same official result websites listed above.

What After the Kerala Plus One Result 2026?

  • Students who pass: Are promoted to Plus Two (Class 12) and can proceed with subject/stream continuation as per their school's guidance.
  • Students who do not clear one or more subjects: May be eligible to apply for revaluation, scrutiny, or a photocopy of the answer sheet, and can appear for the SAY (Save A Year) or improvement examination, subject to DHSE's official schedule.
  • Original mark sheet: Should be collected from the respective school after the online result is declared.

Specific deadlines and fees for revaluation, scrutiny, or SAY exams are not available in the official notification as of now and will be updated once DHSE announces them.

Important Instructions for Students

  • Keep your roll number/registration number and admit card handy before checking the result.
  • The online result is provisional; the school-issued original mark sheet is the final document for admissions and records.
  • Avoid relying on unofficial websites or forwarded messages for your result; always cross-check on the official DHSE portals.
  • In case of heavy website traffic on the result day, try the SMS or DigiLocker options as alternatives.
  • Report any discrepancy in your marks or personal details to your school immediately after checking the result.
