What is Pondicherry University Time Table 2026?

The Pondicherry University Time Table 2026 is the official examination schedule released by Pondicherry University for the academic year 2025-26. This document contains crucial information including:

  • Exact exam dates for each subject/paper
  • Exam timing and duration
  • Course-wise and year-wise schedule
  • Regular and supplementary exam dates
  • Important instructions for students

Students appearing for 1st Year MBBS examinations must download the Pondicherry University time table to prepare their study schedule effectively.

How to Download Pondicherry University Time Table 2026? (Step-by-Step)

Follow these simple steps to download your Pondicherry University exam date sheet 2026:

Method 1: Direct Download from Exam Portal

  • Visit Exam Portal: Go to exam.pondiuni.edu.in
  • Navigate to Time Table Section: Look for "Time Table" or "Medical" category
  • Select Your Course: Choose MBBS 1st Year February 2026 schedule
  • Download PDF: Click on the PDF link to save the time table
  • Save and Print: Save the PDF and take a printout for reference

Method 2: Via Official Website

  • Visit Official Website: Go to pondiuni.edu.in
  • Navigate to Examinations: Click on "Examinations" or "Exam Wing" section
  • Find Time Table Link: Look for latest notifications or direct to exam portal
  • Select Your Course: Choose 1st Year MBBS
  • Download PDF: Save and print the file
