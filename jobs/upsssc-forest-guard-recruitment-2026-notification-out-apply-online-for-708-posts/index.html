Selection Process

  • Stage Details

  • 1. Shortlisting for Main Exam Based on PET-2025 Normalised Score; candidates up to 15 times the number of advertised posts (up to cut-off marks rounded to 2 decimal places) will be shortlisted for the Written Examination

  • 2. Written Examination 100 Questions, 100 Marks, Duration: 120 Minutes (2 Hours); Objective and Multiple Choice type questions; Negative marking of ¼ (25%) of prescribed marks for each wrong answer

  • 3. Physical Standard Test (PST) Measurement of height and chest as per prescribed standards; qualifying in nature

How To Apply

  • The application process for UPSSSC Forest Guard and Wildlife Guard Recruitment 2026 is strictly online. Applications submitted through any other medium will not be accepted. Candidates must read the complete official notification carefully before filling out the application form. All steps of the application process can be completed in one sitting. The step-by-step process to apply online is given below.

  • Visit the official website upsssc.gov.in and click on the relevant advertisement under the Live Advertisement Segment on the Homepage.

  • Login using the OTP sent to the registered mobile number or email linked to your PET-2025 Registration Number.

  • After login, your personal details as entered in PET-2025 (name, father's/husband's name, mother's name, UP domicile status, category, EWS, horizontal reservation details, date of birth, gender, marital status, contact details) will be displayed automatically.

Important Instructions

  • Candidates are advised to read all instructions issued by UPSSSC carefully before applying for Forest Guard and Wildlife Guard Recruitment 2026. Non-compliance with any of the instructions may result in outright rejection of the application or disqualification (DEBAR) from future examinations and selections conducted by UPSSSC. The key instructions are listed below.

  • All candidates belonging to reserved categories of Uttar Pradesh must mandatorily mention their category in the application form. Candidates claiming reservation/age relaxation under more than one reserved category will be given only one relaxation — whichever is more beneficial. Candidates who are not domiciles of Uttar Pradesh are not eligible for reservation benefits; such candidates will be treated as Unreserved (UR). EWS category candidates must possess a valid EWS certificate issued between 01-04-2026 and 20-07-2026 (based on income for FY 2025-26, valid for FY 2026-27). Divyang (Persons with Disability) candidates must update their disability category/sub-category in the application form as per the latest UP Government orders. Ex-Servicemen who will have been discharged from military service by 20-07-2026 are also eligible to avail reservation benefits under the applicable Act. Outstanding sportspersons of Uttar Pradesh must produce the certificate issued by the competent authority as per the prescribed format to avail reservation benefits. No individual correspondence will be made by the Commission with any candidate; all information is uploaded on the official website upsssc.gov.in only. Candidates who are in Central or State Government service must obtain a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their employer before participating in the selection process. Any act of misconduct, use of unfair means, or malpractice during the examination will result in cancellation of candidature and may lead to DEBAR from all future examinations and selections by UPSSSC. Male candidates who have more than one living wife are not eligible to apply; similarly, a female candidate who has married a person who already has a living wife is not eligible, unless exempted by the Governor. UPSSSC reserves the right to amend, modify, or cancel the recruitment process at any stage without prior notice.
