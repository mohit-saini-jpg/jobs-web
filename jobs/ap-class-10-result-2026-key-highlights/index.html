How to Check AP Class 10 Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the simple step-by-step process given below to check the AP Class 10 Result 2026 online at the official BSEAP result portal.

  • Visit the official AP SSC result website - results.bse.ap.gov.in or go to bse.ap.gov.in and click on the SSC result link.
  • On the homepage, click on the link that reads 'AP SSC Results 2026' or 'SSC Public Examinations 2026 - Individual Results'.
  • You will be redirected to the AP Class 10 result login page.
  • Enter your SSC hall ticket number / roll number in the specified field carefully.
  • Submit the details and wait for the system to fetch your AP Class 10 Result 2026.
  • Your AP SSC 10th result with subject-wise marks, total marks and pass/fail status will appear on the screen.
  • Download the online marks memo and take a printout of the provisional result for future reference.
