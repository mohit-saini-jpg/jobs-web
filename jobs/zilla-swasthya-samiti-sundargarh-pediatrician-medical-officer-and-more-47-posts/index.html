How to Apply

  • 1Duly filled application form in prescribed format (downloaded from www.sundargarh.odisha.gov.in)
  • 2All original certificates and testimonials in support of age, qualification and experience
  • 3A set of self-attested photocopies of all certificates/testimonials
  • 4Two recent passport size colour photographs (one self-attested for affixing on application form)
  • 5Self-attested photocopy of Identity Proof (Voter ID Card / PAN Card / Driving License / Aadhar Card / Passport)
  • 6Valid Registration Certificate from Odisha Council of Medical Registration (for MBBS posts) or Odisha State Council/Board of Ayurvedic/Homeopathy Medicine (for AYUSH posts)
  • 7Internship completion certificate (for AYUSH MO posts)
  • 8Conversion certificate (if marks are in CGPA/OGPA/GPA/CPI format) and AICTE/UGC recognition certificate wherever applicable
  • 9No Objection Certificate from employer (for candidates currently working in Health Department on regular or contractual basis)
  • 10Proof of PAR Rating such as PI Order or certification from employer (for OSH & FW Society employees claiming age relaxation/grace marks)
  • 11All positions are purely temporary and co-terminus with the scheme. Canvassing in any form will render the candidate disqualified.
  • 12Registration timing is strictly 8:00 AM to 11:00 AM. No application will be received after 11:00 AM on the respective Walk-in date.
  • 13After registration, candidates will be shortlisted based on eligibility and original documents will be verified on the same day. Shortlisted candidates may be asked to stay back.
  • 14Candidates not fulfilling eligibility criteria need not come for the interview/registration.
  • 15If any candidate fails to produce original documents during verification, candidature will be rejected and no conditionality will be entertained.
  • 16All communication will be made through email or the district website only. No postal/personal correspondence will be made to candidates. Candidates must visit www.sundargarh.odisha.gov.in regularly for notifications, updates and results.
  • 17Number of vacancies and remunerations may vary at the time of actual engagement.
  • 18Performance Incentive and Performance Linked Experience Incentive will be paid as per norm for the same post.
  • 19Candidates securing 50% or more in the Final Panel Merit List will be kept in the Panel with validity of 1 year; the panel is also valid for similar posts in other NHM programmes with the same qualification and remuneration.
  • 20Suppression of material information or furnishing false documents will lead to rejection of candidature/termination from service and the same will apply even after engagement.
  • 21The undersigned reserves the right to cancel any or all applications/positions at any stage of the recruitment process without assigning any reason.

FAQs

Q1What is the walk-in interview date for Sundargarh NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
The walk-in interviews will be conducted from June 9 to June 12, 2026, depending on the post.
Q2How many vacancies are available in NHM Sundargarh Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 47 vacancies have been announced for various medical and paramedical posts.
Q3What is the mode of application for this recruitment?
A
There is no online application. Candidates must attend the walk-in interview with a filled application form.
Q4What is the salary for NHM Sundargarh posts?
A
The salary ranges from ₹16,837 to ₹90,422 per month, along with performance incentives up to 25%.
Q5What is the age limit for Sundargarh NHM Recruitment 2026?
A
Minimum age: 21 years Maximum age varies by post: up to 67 years for MBBS posts, 38 years for AYUSH, and 40 years for paramedical posts
Q6What is the selection process for NHM Sundargarh Recruitment?
A
Selection is based on: Walk-in interview (Personal Interview) for most posts Career assessment (merit-based evaluation) for AYUSH Medical Officer posts
Q7What qualifications are required for NHM Sundargarh jobs?
A
Qualifications vary by post, including: MBBS / MD / Diploma for medical posts BAMS / BHMS / Unani degree for AYUSH posts Diploma in Optometry or Dental Technology for paramedical posts
Q8Is there any application fee for NHM Sundargarh Recruitment 2026?
A
No, there is no application fee for any category.
