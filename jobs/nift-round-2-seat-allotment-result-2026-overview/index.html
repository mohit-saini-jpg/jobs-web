NIFT Round 2 Seat Allotment Result 2026 - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

The NIFT Round 2 seat allotment result 2026 has been declared by the National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) on its official counselling portal, nift.admissions.nic.in. Candidates who participated in Round 2 choice filling can now log in with their application number and password to check their allotted campus and programme for BDes and BFTech courses. Shortlisted candidates must submit their willingness and pay the seat acceptance fee by July 9, 2026, failing which the allotted seat will be cancelled.

NIFT Round 2 Seat Allotment Result Release Date

NIFT declared the Round 2 seat allotment result on July 7, 2026, on the official counselling portal. This follows the Round 2 choice filling and modification window, which was open from July 3 to July 5, 2026, for candidates who had opted for the "float" or "slide" option in Round 1.

NIFT Round 2 Seat Allotment Result Status

The result is officially Out. Candidates who submitted or modified their choices in Round 2 can now view their allotted institute and programme on the official portal by logging in with their credentials.

Score Card and Merit List Availability

NIFT counselling operates through a rank-based seat allotment system rather than a separate scorecard or merit list release at this stage. Candidates' allotment is based on their NIFTEE 2026 rank, category, and choices filled. A distinct cut-off marks list or qualifying marks document has not been officially released for the Round 2 seat allotment.

How to Check the NIFT Round 2 Seat Allotment Result

Follow these steps to check and download your NIFT Round 2 seat allotment result 2026:

  • Visit the official website: nift.admissions.nic.in.
  • Click on the link that reads "Round 2 Seat Allotment Result for NIFT UG Counselling 2026."
  • Enter your application number and password on the login page.
  • Click the submit icon to proceed.
  • Your NIFT Round 2 seat allotment status will appear on the screen.
  • Download and save the provisional allotment letter for future reference.

Details Mentioned on the Allotment Letter

The NIFT Round 2 provisional allotment letter typically includes the following details:

  • Candidate's name and application number
  • NIFTEE 2026 rank and category
  • Allotted campus/institute
  • Allotted programme (BDes or BFTech)
  • Fee payment instructions and deadline
  • Reporting and document verification guidelines

Seat Acceptance Fee Payment

Candidates who receive a seat allotment in Round 2 must submit their willingness and pay the admission fee between July 7 and July 9, 2026. According to the official notification, if a candidate does not pay the required fee within this time frame, the allotted seat will be cancelled, and the candidate will not be allowed to participate in the subsequent regular rounds of counselling.

What to Do After the NIFT Round 2 Result

Candidates who are satisfied with their Round 2 allotment should submit their willingness and pay the seat acceptance fee before the July 9, 2026 deadline. Candidates who wish to try for a better campus or programme may check the eligibility rules for Round 3 choice filling. Those who do not wish to continue with their allotted seat should refer to the official seat withdrawal guidelines on the portal.

Documents Required for the Next Stage

Candidates are generally advised to keep the following documents ready for verification and admission formalities:

  • NIFTEE 2026 admit card and result/rank card
  • Class 10 and Class 12 mark sheets and certificates
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Identity proof
  • Provisional seat allotment letter

Candidates should always refer to the official notification on nift.admissions.nic.in for the exact and updated list of documents required.

Selection Process / Counselling Rounds

NIFT 2026 admissions are conducted through centralised counselling comprising three regular rounds followed by a spot round:

  • Round 1 Seat Allotment
  • Round 2 Seat Allotment (current stage)
  • Round 3 Seat Allotment
  • Spot Round (for vacant seats, if any)

Important Instructions

  • Candidates must pay the seat acceptance fee within the given deadline to retain their allotted seat.
  • Candidates who selected "freeze" in an earlier round will not be allowed to upgrade or change their allotted seat.
  • Candidates on "float" or "slide" may be considered for a higher-preference campus or programme in subsequent rounds.
  • Always cross-check details on the provisional allotment letter with the official notification.
  • For any discrepancy, contact the official NIFT or NTA helpdesk mentioned on the portal.
