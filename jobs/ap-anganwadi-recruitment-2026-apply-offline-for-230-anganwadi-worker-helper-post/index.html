Important Instructions

Only Women candidates who are permanent local residents of the village/ward where the AWC is located are eligible. This is a mandatory condition, not merely preferred. Candidates applying for Anganwadi Worker (AWW) or Mini Anganwadi Worker posts must be married and have a permanent residence and marital home (husband's village) in the same village/panchayat where the AWC exists. For Anganwadi Helper (AWH) posts under SC and ST reserved vacancies, only SC and ST candidates respectively are eligible to apply - no other category candidate can apply for these seats. AWW/AWH appointments are governed by Government of AP rules and G.O.MS No. 13 WCD&SC (PROGS) dated 26/06/19 - honorarium (not regular salary) is paid as per rules. Vacancy details notified at present are final; the Collector, Sri Satya Sai District alone has authority to modify or cancel vacancies under unforeseen circumstances. Applications without photograph, without signature over the photograph, or without required documents will be summarily rejected without any communication. Candidates must submit their AP Open School Society (APOSS) TC/study certificates along with their application - no opportunity will be given to produce them later during CDPO screening. If a candidate has a disability, the disability proof must be issued by the concerned Welfare Department branch office - self-declarations are not accepted. Caste and residence certificates for SC/ST/BC categories must be issued by the Tahsildar and not by any other authority. Candidates must confirm on the application that they agree to serve in the specific AWC location applied for - transfers are generally not applicable for Anganwadi posts. All details provided in the application and the gazzetted officer-verified documents carry full legal responsibility on the candidate. For vacancy centre-wise details (AWC name, Mandal, Project, Roster Category), candidates must refer to the official notification PDF and apply only for the AWC vacancy that matches their local residence.

FAQs

Q1Who can apply for Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 in Sri Satya Sai District?
A
Only women candidates who are permanent local residents of the specific village/ward where the Anganwadi Centre (AWC) vacancy exists can apply. Candidates must be between 21 and 35 years of age as on 01-07-2026 and must have passed 10th Class (SSC) from a recognized board. For AWW posts, candidates must also be married.
Q2What is the last date to apply and where should I submit my application?
A
The last date for submitting the application is 18-06-2026 by 5:00 PM. Applications must be submitted in person (walk-in) at the concerned ICDS Project Office (Kadiri / Nallacheruvu / Dharmavaram / Battalapalli / C.K. Palli) based on which project division the AWC vacancy belongs to. No postal or online applications are accepted.
Q3Is there any application fee for Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
No. There is absolutely no application fee for any category of candidates. The application process is completely free of cost.
Q4What is the salary/honorarium for Anganwadi Worker and Anganwadi Helper?
A
As per G.O.MS No. 13 WCD&SC (PROGS) dated 26/06/2019, the honorarium payable is Rs. 11,500/- per month for Anganwadi Workers (AWW) and Rs. 7,000/- per month for Anganwadi Helpers (AWH). Note that this is an honorarium, not a regular government salary, but it is paid consistently by the State Government.
Q5How is the merit list prepared? Is there a written exam?
A
There is no written examination for Anganwadi posts. The merit list is prepared based on marks obtained in the 10th Class (SSC) qualification. Candidates with relevant training certificates (Pre-School training, ICDS worker training, Creche & Pre-School Management Training) may receive additional weightage as per government rules. Final selection is based on roster category and merit within each category.
Q6Can a woman from a neighbouring village apply for an AWC vacancy in a different village?
A
No. The candidate must be a permanent local resident of the specific village/ward where the AWC is located. Even if you live in a nearby village or the same panchayat, you are not eligible unless your permanent residence is in the exact village/ward of that AWC. This is strictly enforced.
Q7Can unmarried women apply for Anganwadi Worker posts?
A
For Anganwadi Worker (AWW) and Mini Anganwadi Worker posts, candidates should preferably be married as per the notification requirements. The candidate's permanent marital residence (husband's place) should ideally be in the same village as the AWC. Unmarried candidates should check the specific AWC vacancy instructions before applying.
Q8I belong to BC-B category. Can I apply for an OC (Open Category) AWC vacancy?
A
Yes. BC/SC/ST candidates who qualify on their own merit (without relaxed standards) can be accommodated against OC/Open Category vacancies as per the roster. However, you must check the specific Roster Category assigned to the AWC vacancy you are applying for (available in the vacancy list in the official notification). Each AWC vacancy has a specific roster category assigned and only candidates of that category can apply for that seat.
