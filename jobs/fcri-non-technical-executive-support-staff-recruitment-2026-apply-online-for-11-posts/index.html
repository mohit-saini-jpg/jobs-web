Important Instructions

  • Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply. Only candidates willing to work for a minimum period of ONE YEAR may apply. Candidates must be willing to work anywhere in India - duties/assignments may entail tours/travel anywhere for any length of period. Only full-time qualifications from UGC/AICTE recognized institutions will be considered - part-time, correspondence, or distance mode qualifications are not eligible. Consistent scores of above 60% aggregate marks in all examinations from Class X onwards are required for Levels 1 to 3. Freelancing, part-time jobs, Guest Lectureship, etc. will not be treated as relevant experience. Experience must be verifiable through an Experience Certificate issued by the employer. The engagement is purely of a short-tenure nature - it does not create any preferential claims, rights, or guarantees for future regular employment at FCRI. Only ONE application form will be accepted per applicant - multiple applications from the same candidate will be rejected. Applications must be in MS Word format only - CVs/Resumes, postal applications, and applications to any other email will be rejected. Canvassing in any form, follow-ups, or sending applications to any other email will not be entertained. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the written test or interview. Selected candidates must submit a Medical Fitness Certificate at the time of joining. Leave entitlement: 1 day Casual Leave per completed month; Medical Leave up to 15 days per calendar year on production of Medical Certificate. Leaves are not encashable and cannot be carried forward. FCRI reserves the right to terminate the contract at any time with 1 month notice (except in disciplinary/misconduct cases where immediate termination applies). There will be no separate newspaper/media advertisements for corrigendum, addendum, or updates - candidates must visit www.fcriindia.com/careers regularly. All decisions of FCRI regarding eligibility, selection, and related matters are final and binding.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is FCRI Non‑Technical Executive Support Staff (ESS) Recruitment 2026?

FCRI Non‑Technical Executive Support Staff (ESS) Recruitment 2026 is a tenure‑based contract recruitment by Fluid Control Research Institute, Palakkad for 11 Non‑Technical ESS vacancies in areas like Purchase & Logistics, Finance & Accounts, Personnel & Administration, Customer Care, Hindi Cell & Training, and Documentation & Media Cell.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in FCRI Non‑Technical ESS Recruitment 2026?

There are 11 tentative vacancies under FCRI Non‑Technical ESS Recruitment 2026, distributed across various functional areas and contract levels, with reservation for UR, OBC, SC, ST and EWS categories as per the notification.

Q3. What is the application last date for FCRI Non‑Technical ESS Recruitment 2026?

The last date to apply for FCRI Non‑Technical Executive Support Staff Recruitment 2026 is 30 May 2026, and applications must reach FCRI by email before this closing date.

Q4. What are the eligibility criteria for FCRI Non‑Technical ESS posts in 2026?

Candidates must have a full‑time Post Graduate degree in a relevant discipline from UGC/AICTE‑recognized institutions with at least 60% marks, along with the required experience for Level 2, 3 and 4 posts; only full‑time qualifications and verifiable work experience are considered.

Q5. What is the age limit for FCRI Non‑Technical ESS Recruitment 2026?

The age limit varies by contract level: around 28 years for Level 1 freshers, 30 years for Level 2, 35 years for Level 3 and up to about 62 years for Level 4 retired officers, with applicable relaxations for SC/ST and OBC candidates as per Government norms. These exact limits are given in the ESS notification.

Q6. How do I apply for FCRI Non‑Technical Executive Support Staff Recruitment 2026?

To apply, candidates must download the prescribed MS Word application form from the FCRI careers page, fill it without modifying the format, and email it to [email protected] with the subject line “NTA‑EXEC SUPPORT”, mentioning the notification number and relevant post code (ESS1–ESS6) in the email body.

Q7. Is there any application fee for FCRI Non‑Technical ESS Recruitment 2026?

The FCRI Non‑Technical ESS notification and current careers page do not mention any application fee, and job‑portal summaries also do not specify a fee; candidates are advised to treat it as no‑fee unless a fee is clearly updated in the official notice.

Q8. What is the salary for FCRI Non‑Technical Executive Support Staff posts?

The FCRI Non‑Technical ESS posts offer consolidated monthly remuneration that varies by contract level, with higher pay for Level 3 experienced professionals and Level 4 retired officers and slightly lower, fixed pay for Level 1 and Level 2 positions as indicated in the notification.
