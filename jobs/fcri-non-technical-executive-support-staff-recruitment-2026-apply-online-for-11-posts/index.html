FCRI Non-Technical Executive Support Staff Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 11 Posts

Government of India

Posts: 11 (Tentative) - UR: 6 | OBC: 2 | SC: 1 | ST: 1 | EWS: 1 Last Date: 30/05/2026 Email

Short Information

The Fluid Control Research Institute (FCRI) has released the official Non-Technical Executive Support Staff (ESS) Notification 2026 on 30 April 2026 at https://www.fcriindia.com/. A total of 11 vacancies have been announced under the FCRI ESS Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged between 28 to 62 years (depending on contract level) with a qualification of Post Graduate degree in relevant discipline (minimum 60% marks) or relevant experience for higher levels can apply online (via email) from 05 May 2026 to 30 May 2026. Selection Process

  • Level Selection Process
  • Level 1 & Level 2 Written Test (MCQ, 90 Minutes) + Skill Test (if applicable) + Interview. Written test covers: General Aptitude (Current Affairs, Logical Reasoning, Numerical Ability) - 60 questions; Skill Test as per nature of job profile.
  • Level 3 Interview only
  • Level 4 Interview only
  • Selection will be based on contract level. Mere possession of minimum qualifications/experience will not entitle any candidate to be called for test/interview. FCRI reserves the right to cancel the recruitment process at any time or not fill up the posts notified without assigning any reasons. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the written examination or interview.

Important Instructions

  • Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply. Only candidates willing to work for a minimum period of ONE YEAR may apply. Candidates must be willing to work anywhere in India - duties/assignments may entail tours/travel anywhere for any length of period. Only full-time qualifications from UGC/AICTE recognized institutions will be considered - part-time, correspondence, or distance mode qualifications are not eligible. Consistent scores of above 60% aggregate marks in all examinations from Class X onwards are required for Levels 1 to 3. Freelancing, part-time jobs, Guest Lectureship, etc. will not be treated as relevant experience. Experience must be verifiable through an Experience Certificate issued by the employer. The engagement is purely of a short-tenure nature - it does not create any preferential claims, rights, or guarantees for future regular employment at FCRI. Only ONE application form will be accepted per applicant - multiple applications from the same candidate will be rejected. Applications must be in MS Word format only - CVs/Resumes, postal applications, and applications to any other email will be rejected. Canvassing in any form, follow-ups, or sending applications to any other email will not be entertained. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the written test or interview. Selected candidates must submit a Medical Fitness Certificate at the time of joining. Leave entitlement: 1 day Casual Leave per completed month; Medical Leave up to 15 days per calendar year on production of Medical Certificate. Leaves are not encashable and cannot be carried forward. FCRI reserves the right to terminate the contract at any time with 1 month notice (except in disciplinary/misconduct cases where immediate termination applies). There will be no separate newspaper/media advertisements for corrigendum, addendum, or updates - candidates must visit www.fcriindia.com/careers regularly. All decisions of FCRI regarding eligibility, selection, and related matters are final and binding.
