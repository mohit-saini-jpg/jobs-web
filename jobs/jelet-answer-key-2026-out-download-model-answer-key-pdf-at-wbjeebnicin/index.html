What is the JELET 2026 Model Answer Key?

The JELET 2026 model answer key is the official set of correct responses to all questions asked in the entrance examination. It is published by WBJEEB through its official portal to bring transparency to the evaluation process.

The board releases the answer key in two stages. First, the provisional or model answer key is published, which is open for candidate challenges. After the objection window closes and all valid challenges are reviewed by subject experts, the board releases the final answer key. This final version is used as the basis for computing results and preparing rank cards for the JELET 2026 counselling process.

How to Download JELET Answer Key 2026 at wbjeeb.nic.in

The JELET 2026 model answer key can be downloaded directly from the official WBJEEB portal. Candidates need their login credentials to access the answer key.

Follow these steps to download the WB JELET 2026 provisional answer key:

  • Visit the official website at wbjeeb.nic.in or go directly to wbjeeb.nic.in/jelet.
  • On the homepage, look for the notice or link that reads "Model Answer Key - View & Challenge for JELET-2026."
  • Click on the link to open the login page.
  • Enter your application number along with your password or date of birth.
  • Click the "Sign In" or "Submit" button.
  • The provisional answer key will appear on the screen.
  • Download the answer key PDF and save it for offline reference.
  • Compare your marked responses with the official answers to estimate your probable score.

How to Calculate Score Using JELET 2026 Answer Key

Once candidates have downloaded both the model answer key and their OMR response sheet, they can calculate their estimated marks before the official results are declared. The JELET 2026 question paper is divided into two categories with different marking schemes.

The scoring formula for JELET 2026 is as follows:

Total Score = (Category 1 Correct Answers × 1) − (Category 1 Wrong Answers × 0.25) + (Category 2 Fully Correct Answers × 2) + (Category 2 Partial Credit Marks)

How to Challenge JELET 2026 Provisional Answer Key

If a candidate believes that any answer marked in the provisional answer key is incorrect, WBJEEB provides an official objection mechanism. Challenges must be submitted within the objection window along with supporting evidence and the prescribed fee.

Follow these steps to raise an objection against the JELET 2026 model answer key:

  • Visit wbjeeb.nic.in/jelet and log in using your application number and date of birth.
  • Click on the "View OMR & Challenge Recorded Response" tab.
  • Select the question number(s) you wish to challenge.
  • Click on your chosen option for each question.
  • Upload supporting documents such as standard textbook references as evidence.
  • Pay the mandatory non-refundable objection fee of Rs. 500 per question via online payment.
  • Submit your challenge and download the acknowledgement page for future reference.

Note: No objection will be considered without successful payment of the prescribed fee. The board will review all valid challenges and revise the answer key if any objection is found to be correct. Once the final answer key is published, no further challenges are permitted.
