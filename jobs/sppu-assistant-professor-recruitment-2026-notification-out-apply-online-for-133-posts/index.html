Selection Process

  • Selection is through document verification followed by interview. Candidates who fulfill the conditions mentioned in the notification and are found eligible after document verification will be called for interview. The University will not correspond with ineligible candidates. Candidates called for interview must appear at their own expense.
  • Stage 1 - Online Application & Shortlisting
  • Candidates must apply online at https://admin.unipune.ac.in/recruitment from 23-05-2026 to 01-06-2026. A separate application must be submitted for each department/post. Applications must be complete in all respects - incomplete applications and applications with incorrect/false information will be disqualified at any stage.
  • Stage 2 - Document Verification
  • Candidates who are prima facie eligible will be called for document verification. Candidates must submit the application form along with all enclosures to the Head of the concerned Department at the time of interview for verification. Original documents plus attested copies of all required documents must be produced. Candidates must NOT send hard copies to the Department or Administration Teaching Section by post or in person at any time before or after the interview.
  • Stage 3 - Interview
  • Candidates found eligible after document verification will be eligible for interview. Date
  • time
  • and venue of interviews will be notified by the concerned Department. Candidates appearing for interview at Ahilyanagar Sub Center (Code A-17) and Nashik Center (Code B-03) must note that interviews will be held at those respective locations
  • not at the main SPPU campus. All candidates must appear for interview at their own expense.

How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply online only through the University website https://admin.unipune.ac.in/recruitment from 23-05-2026 to 01-06-2026. A separate application must be submitted for each post/department. Candidates should NOT send or submit hard copies of the recruitment application to the Head of the concerned Department or the Administration Teaching Section by post or in person at any time.
  • Documents Required to be Uploaded / Brought at Interview (Original + Self-attested/attested copies):
  • Printout of Online Application Form

  • Caste certificate issued by competent authority - for SC / ST / DT-A / NT-B / NT-C / NT-D / OBC / SBC candidates
  • Non-Creamy Layer Certificate (valid up to 31.03.2026) for DT-A, NT-B, NT-C, NT-D, OBC, SBC categories - as per Maharashtra Government Circular No. CBC 10/2006/Pra.Kra.15/MAVAK 5 dated 5th June 2006
  • Caste Validity Certificate, if already obtained
  • EWS Certificate - as per Maharashtra GR No. RAAADho/4019/Pra.kra.31/16-A dated 12.02.2019 & 31.05.2021

  • Disability Certificate issued by authorized Medical Expert of Government Hospital in the concerned District - for PwBD candidates
  • Sports certificate - for Sports Persons Reservation (as per Maharashtra GR RaKriDho-2002/Prakra68/KriYuSe-2 dated 01.07.2016 & Sankirna-1716/PraKra18/KriYuSe-2 dated 30.06.2022)

Important Instructions

  • THIS IS NOT A PERMANENT APPOINTMENT. The post of Assistant Professor (Contractual) is purely temporary in nature, up to 31st May, 2027. Selected candidates will not have any claim for regularization or permanent appointment on the basis of this contractual engagement. Separate application must be submitted for each post/department. A single common application for multiple departments is not valid. Candidates must NOT send or submit hard copies of the application to the Department or Administration Teaching Section by post or in person - only online applications are accepted. Application at interview: Candidates must bring the printout of the online application form along with all supporting original documents and attested/self-attested photocopies to the concerned Department Head at the time of interview for verification. Qualifications, relevant experience, and age will be counted as on the last date of receipt of application (01-06-2026). Documents acquired after the cut-off date will not be considered. Candidates furnishing incorrect or false information shall stand disqualified at any stage, including after appointment. Canvassing directly or indirectly will be a disqualification. Candidates called for interview must appear at their own expense. The University will not reimburse travel or any other expenses. Candidates from reserved categories from outside Maharashtra will be treated as Open category only. DT-A, NT-B, NT-C, NT-D category reservations are separate and internally transferable - if a candidate from one of these categories is not available, the post can be filled interchangeably within DT-A/NT-B/NT-C/NT-D. SEBC reservation is applicable as per Maharashtra GR No. BCC 2024/Pra.Kra.75/16-C dated 27.02.2024. Reserved category candidates appointed against reserved posts must submit their Caste Validity Certificate within 6 months of the appointment order - as per Maharashtra Notification No. BBC-2011/Pra.Kra./1064/2011/16-B dated 12.12.2011. The University reserves the right to fill or not to fill any post, or to modify/alter/cancel the advertisement at any time without assigning any reason. University Circular No. 90/2026 (Outward No. AT/726 dated 15.05.2026) forms an integral part of this notification - candidates must read it before applying. All information, corrigenda, and circulars will be published on the University website only - candidates should check the website regularly.
