AIIMS BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026

All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi has issued the AIIMS BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026 today for the AIIMS BSc Nursing (Post-Basic) entrance examination 2026. As per the official schedule, the admit card for the BSc Post-Basic Nursing exam is available from 25 May 2026, while the admit card for BSc (Hons.) Nursing will be released separately on 19 June 2026.

The AIIMS BSc Nursing (Post-Basic) exam 2026 will be conducted on 30 May 2026, and the AIIMS BSc Nursing (Hons.) exam 2026 will be held on 27 June 2026, in computer‑based mode at various centres across India. Candidates can download the AIIMS nursing admit card 2026 from the official exam portal aiimsexams.ac.in using their Candidate ID, Registration Unique Code (RUC) and password.

How to Download AIIMS BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026

Follow the steps below to download your AIIMS BSc Nursing 2026 admit card:

  • Visit the AIIMS exam portal aiimsexams.ac.in.
  • Click on “Academic Courses” on the homepage and select “B.Sc. Courses” followed by “B.Sc. Nursing (Post-Basic)” or “B.Sc. (Hons.) Nursing” as applicable.
  • Click on the link “Proceed” against the relevant course, then click on “Login” for “Applicant Login Zone”.
  • Enter your Candidate ID, Password and Captcha, then click Login.
  • In your dashboard, click on the link “Admit Card - AIIMS BSc Nursing 2026” for your chosen course (Post-Basic / Hons).
  • Your AIIMS BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026 will be displayed on the screen with exam date, shift timing and exam centre details; download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies.

Admit cards are available only in online mode; AIIMS will not send them by post.

Details Mentioned on AIIMS BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026

The AIIMS BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026 contains important candidate and exam information that must be checked carefully:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Candidate ID and RUC (Registration Unique Code)
  • Exam name and course: BSc Nursing (Post-Basic) / BSc Nursing (Hons.) 2026
  • Exam date, shift, and reporting time
  • Exam centre name, centre code, city and full address
  • Category and basic details (if printed)
  • Space for candidate’s signature and invigilator’s signature
  • Important instructions and exam‑day guidelines

If any discrepancy is found in your name, photograph, course, or exam centre details, you should immediately contact the AIIMS Examination Section using the contact details provided on aiimsexams.ac.in.

Documents Required with AIIMS BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026

On the day of the AIIMS BSc Nursing 2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the AIIMS BSc Nursing Admit Card 2026 (A4‑size, clear printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence or any other government‑issued ID as mentioned in the AIIMS instructions.
  • A recent passport‑size photograph (preferably the same as uploaded in the application), if specifically required to be affixed on the admit card or attendance sheet.

Candidates who fail to produce the admit card and original photo ID will not be allowed to enter the exam centre.

Exam‑Day Instructions for AIIMS BSc Nursing 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the AIIMS BSc Nursing (Post-Basic & Hons.) exams 2026, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card to complete security checks, document verification and seating; late entry after the gate‑closing time may not be permitted.
  • Carry only essential items: AIIMS BSc Nursing admit card 2026, original photo ID, a simple transparent water bottle (if allowed) and basic stationery as per instructions; follow the latest exam‑day advisory issued by AIIMS.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless expressly permitted), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the exam hall; such items are strictly prohibited.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the admit card and those announced by the invigilators inside the exam centre; indulging in unfair means or disobeying instructions can lead to cancellation of the exam and further disciplinary action.
  • Preserve your AIIMS BSc Nursing admit card until completion of counselling and admission, as it may be required for verification at later stages.
