What is CCMN Counselling 2026? : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 17/06/2026

CCMN (Centralized Counselling for M.Sc./M.Sc. Tech) 2026 is the official centralised admission process through which IIT JAM-qualified candidates can secure seats in M.Sc. and M.Sc. Tech programmes offered by 20 NITs and other Centrally Funded Technical Institutes (CFTIs) across India. Conducted by NIT Warangal, the CCMN 2026 process covers online registration, choice filling, seat allotment, document verification, and physical reporting all carried out in multiple rounds. The CCMN 2026 first seat allotment result was released on June 12, 2026, and document verification must be completed by June 17, 2026. The second seat allotment is scheduled for June 22, 2026.

What is CCMN Counselling 2026?

CCMN counselling is the centralised seat-allocation mechanism for postgraduate science programmes at NITs and CFTIs in India. This section gives you the complete picture of what CCMN is and how it works.

CCMN stands for Centralized Counselling for M.Sc./M.Sc. Tech. Administered by NIT Warangal on behalf of all participating institutes, it uses candidates' IIT JAM 2026 scores to allocate seats across M.Sc. programmes in subjects such as Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Data Science, Biotechnology, and more. The process is conducted entirely online through the official NIC portal. The CCMN counselling process 2026 consists of registration, choice filling, seat allotment, and payment confirmation, with the goal of providing admissions for M.Sc. programmes at 20 NITs and CFTIs.

CCMN 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Understanding who can participate in CCMN 2026 counselling is the first step before beginning the registration process.

To be eligible for CCMN counselling 2026, students need to have appeared for the JAM exam with a valid score. From the list of courses offered by different universities, they can select programmes based on admission requirements. Candidates must also check the individual eligibility conditions of each programme they wish to apply for before submitting the CCMN 2026 application, since subject-specific requirements may vary across NITs and CFTIs.

CCMN Counselling Procedure 2026 Step-by-Step Guide

The CCMN 2026 counselling procedure follows a clearly defined sequence of steps. Here is exactly what candidates need to do at each stage.

Step 1: CCMN Registration 2026

Candidates must complete the CCMN application form with personal details including gender, JAM score, mobile number, email address, paper code, and registration number. The registration fee can be paid through the ICICI bank Eazypay system. Providing false information on the application form will lead to disqualification from consideration for admission.

Step 2: CCMN Choice Filling 2026

Before the submission deadline, candidates must complete the CCMN 2026 application form for counselling by selecting their preferred programme and institution. Candidates are presented with a list of options during the process and must rank them in order of priority. Choices can be modified before final submission, but if candidates forget to lock their choices before the deadline, they will be locked automatically.

Step 3: CCMN Seat Allotment 2026

The CCMN 2026 seat allocation list is created using the candidates' choices and JAM scores. Candidates can view the seat allotment result using their IIT JAM registration ID and password. To accept the allotted seat, candidates should select the "Freeze" option. Upon accepting the seat allotment, candidates must pay the admission fee and report to the counselling location. A provisional admission notification will be issued to candidates who have frozen their seats and paid the CCMN counselling participation fee. Candidates can also withdraw their seats following the first and second allotment rounds.

Step 4: Document Verification

After the seat allotment, candidates must upload documents online and complete verification through the allotted institute. Physical reporting at the finally allotted institute takes place as per the schedule of each round.

CCMN National Spot Round 2026

The National Spot Round (NSR) is an additional opportunity for candidates who missed out on seats in the regular rounds. Here is what you need to know.

CCMN National Spot Round 2026 counselling will take place tentatively from August 1. Candidates must fill out and submit the CCMN choice filling form and complete registration before the submission deadline. The NSR is held only after the completion of all three regular rounds of CCMN seat allotment 2026.

Who is Eligible for the NSR?

Eligibility for the National Spot Round is limited to the following categories of candidates: those who have not yet completed the CCMN application form 2026; those who registered for CCMN 2026 but were not allocated a seat in any round; candidates who were given a seat in an earlier round but did not pay the counselling fee; and candidates who withdrew their seats in the first, second, or third rounds of allotment.
