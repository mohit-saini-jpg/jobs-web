WBJEE 2026 Round 1 Seat Allotment- Key Highlights : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 07/07/2026

The West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) has releasing the WBJEE 2026 Round 1 seat allotment result today, Tuesday, July 7, 2026, on the official counselling portal, wbjeeb.nic.in. Candidates who registered for WBJEE 2026 counselling and locked their choices can now log in with their roll number, password, and security pin to check which college and course they have been allotted, based on their WBJEE or JEE Main 2026 rank, category, and choice-filling preferences.

If you registered for counselling and are wondering what to do next, this article walks you through the allotment timeline, the exact login steps, the fee you need to pay, the documents required for verification, and what your options are if you want to try for a better seat in later rounds.

How to Check WBJEE 2026 Round 1 Seat Allotment Result

Candidates need to log in to the official portal with their counselling credentials to view their allotment status; no allotment letters are sent by post, email, or SMS. Follow the steps below in order.

  • Visit the official website, wbjeeb.nic.in.
  • Click on the "WBJEE" link under the Examination menu, then select "View Seat Allotment Result Round No. 1 and Pay Acceptance Fee."
  • Enter your WBJEE roll number, password, and security pin.
  • Click "Sign In" to proceed to your candidate dashboard.
  • Your allotted institute and course will be displayed on the screen -check it carefully.
  • If you wish to accept the seat, proceed to pay the seat acceptance fee and download your provisional allotment letter.

Options After Seat Allotment - Freeze, Float, or Withdraw

Once a seat is allotted, candidates are not locked into a single path -WBJEEB offers three distinct choices, and picking the right one depends on whether you are satisfied with your current allotment or want a shot at a better one in later rounds.

  • Accept and Freeze- Pay the seat acceptance fee and download the allotment letter. This confirms your admission at the allotted institute, subject to physical document verification, and you will not participate in further allotment rounds.
  • Accept with Upgradation ("Upgradation Yes")- Pay the acceptance fee and download the provisional letter, but remain in the running for a better college or branch in Round 2 or the mop-up round. If you get upgraded, your earlier seat is releasing.
  • Do Nothing- If you take no action, you are simply not considered for admission in the current allotment cycle, and your candidature effectively lapses for that round.

Seat Acceptance Fee and Reporting Process

Candidates who wish to confirm their allotted seat must complete an online payment before the deadline, followed by physical reporting to the allotted institute for document verification.

  • The seat acceptance fee is Rs. 5,000, payable online via net banking, debit card, or credit card.
  • After payment, download the provisional seat allotment letter -this is mandatory for reporting.
  • Report to your allotted institute's reporting centre between July 7 and July 11, 2026, with original documents and self-attested photocopies for verification.
  • Admission is confirmed only after successful physical verification at the institute; the online allotment letter is provisional until then.

Documents Required for WBJEE 2026 Counselling Reporting

Carrying the correct set of original and photocopied documents is essential -incomplete paperwork at the reporting centre can lead to cancellation of an allotted seat even after the acceptance fee has been paid.

What Happens If You're Not Allotted a Seat in Round1?

Missing out in Round 1 does not end your chances -WBJEE counselling runs across three structured rounds, and each subsequent round reopens both allotment and, in some cases, registration.

Candidates who do not receive a seat in Round 1 automatically remain eligible for Round 2 (July 21 result) without needing to register again, since a single counselling registration covers all rounds. Those who missed registering earlier, or who did not accept a seat in Rounds 1 and 2, can still join the mop-up round, for which fresh registration and choice filling open from July 24 to 26, with the allotment result declared on July 28, 2026.
