The Directorate of Primary Education, Gujarat has officially released the Gujarat RTE Result 2026 for Round 1 Lottery on May 4, 2026. The results have been declared for Class 1 admissions under the 25% reservation quota in private unaided schools across Gujarat. Gujarat RTE Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - rte.orpgujarat.com. Parents and guardians can check and download their child's seat allotment status by logging into the official portal using their registered mobile number and application ID. The lottery result determines which children have been allocated seats in their preferred private schools under the Right to Education Act 2009.

Students who have been allotted seats must visit the school within 72 hours of admit card generation to complete the admission process. In case of any discrepancies or issues with the allotment, parents are advised to contact the helpline number provided on the official website during working days between 11:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Gujarat RTE Result 2026 Key Highlights

The Gujarat RTE Result 2026 Round 1 Lottery has been successfully declared on May 4, 2026 for over 84,000 seats across 9,741 participating private schools. Parents can access their child's seat allotment status through the official portal rte.orpgujarat.com using their login credentials. The admission process will continue through multiple rounds including Round 2, Round 3, and spot admission to fill all available seats under the RTE quota.

How to Check Gujarat RTE Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your Gujarat RTE Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official website of Gujarat RTE at rte.orpgujarat.com
  • On the homepage, click on the "ADMIT CARD (પ્રવેશ પત્ર)" or "Application Status (અરજીની સ્થિતિ)" option
  • Enter your registered mobile number and application ID or registration number
  • Click on the "Submit" or "Login" button to proceed
  • The Gujarat RTE Result 2026 lottery status will be displayed on the screen
  • Check your seat allotment details including the school name and other information
  • If allotted, download and print the admit card for school admission
  • Visit the allotted school within 72 hours with required documents to complete the admission process
  • Save a copy of the result and admit card for future reference
