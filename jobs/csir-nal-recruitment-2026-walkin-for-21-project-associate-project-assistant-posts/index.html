Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies announced by CSIR NAL?

CSIR NAL has announced a total of 21 vacancies for Project Staff positions through this walk-in recruitment drive.

Q2. What is the application mode for these CSIR NAL posts?

The application mode for these positions is Walk-in. Candidates need to attend the interview in person with all required documents.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Project Staff posts?

The educational qualifications range from B.Sc./3 years Diploma in Engineering/Technology, Masters/Integrated Masters, Bachelor's degree in Engineering or Technology, to SSC/10th plus ITI.

Q4. What is the age limit for the CSIR NAL Project Staff recruitment?

The maximum age limit for all posts is 35 years, with age relaxations applicable for SC/ST/PwBD/Women and OBC categories.

Q5. What is the salary offered for the Project Staff positions?

The salary ranges from Rs. 18,000/- to Rs. 28,000/- per month, plus HRA, depending on the specific post.

Q6. Are there any application fees for this recruitment?

No, there are no application fees mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.

Q7. What are the important dates for the CSIR NAL walk-in interview?

The walk-in interviews are scheduled for 26 May 2026 and 29 May 2026. The date of publication was 2 May 2026.

Q8. Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?

The walk-in interview will be conducted at CSIR NAL, Bengaluru, Karnataka, 560017. Specific venue details will be in the official notification.
