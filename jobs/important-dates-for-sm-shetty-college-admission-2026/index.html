How to Check SM Shetty College Merit List 2026

  • Visit the official website: smshettycollege.edu.in
  • Go to the Admission or First Merit List 2026-27 section.
  • Click on the respective course merit list link.
  • Download the PDF and search for your name/application number.
  • Follow the instructions for document verification and fee payment.

Documents Required for Admission

  • 10th and 12th Marksheets
  • Leaving Certificate / Transfer Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Pre-Admission Form Printout
