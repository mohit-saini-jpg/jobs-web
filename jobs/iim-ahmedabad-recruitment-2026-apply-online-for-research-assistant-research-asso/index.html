IIM Ahmedabad Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for IIM Ahmedabad Mattu Centre Research Assistant Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit applications for the IIM Ahmedabad Mattu Centre Research Assistant/Research Associate Recruitment 2026 is 03 July 2026. Candidates must send their application documents via email before the deadline. Applications received after the closing date may not be considered for the recruitment process.
Q2How many vacancies are available in IIM Ahmedabad Mattu Centre Recruitment 2026?
A
The Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad (IIMA) has announced a total of 04 vacancies for the positions of Research Assistant and Research Associate. These positions are available under the Mattu Centre for Research in Security Pricing and Corporate Finance. Eligible candidates with strong research and analytical skills are encouraged to apply.
Q3What are the eligibility criteria for IIM Ahmedabad Research Assistant Recruitment 2026?
A
Applicants should possess strong research, analytical, and writing skills along with the ability to collect and analyze qualitative and quantitative data. Candidates must also demonstrate attention to detail and problem-solving abilities. Knowledge of research methodologies and academic writing will be beneficial for the role.
Q4Is prior experience with statistical tools required for the IIMA Research Assistant post?
A
While not mandatory, familiarity with statistical and data analysis tools such as Stata, R, Python, or Excel is considered an added advantage. Candidates with experience in business analytics, data visualization, and quantitative research may have better opportunities during the selection process. These skills are valuable for supporting research projects at the Centre.
Q5What is the age limit for IIM Ahmedabad Mattu Centre Research Assistant Recruitment 2026?
A
The maximum age limit for applicants is 35 years as of the application deadline. Candidates should ensure that they meet the age requirement before submitting their applications. Any applicable relaxations will be subject to institutional guidelines and recruitment policies.
Q6What is the tenure of the Research Assistant/Research Associate position at IIMA?
A
The appointment is initially offered for a period of one year. However, the tenure may be extended depending on the candidate's performance and the Centre's requirements. This provides an excellent opportunity for individuals seeking research experience in finance and capital market studies.
Q7What documents are required to apply for IIM Ahmedabad Research Assistant Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must submit a detailed Curriculum Vitae (CV) along with a writing sample, such as a report, article, case study, blog, or similar work. These documents help the selection committee assess the applicant's research, analytical, and communication skills. Incomplete applications may not be shortlisted.
