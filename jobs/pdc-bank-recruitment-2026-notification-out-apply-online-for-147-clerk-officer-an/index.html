PDC Bank Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is PDC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
PDC Bank Recruitment 2026 is the recruitment drive announced by the Panchmahal District Co-operative Bank Limited on 13 June 2026 for 147 vacancies across nine posts, including General Manager, Deputy General Manager, Assistant General Manager, Manager, Deputy Manager, Executive, Senior Officer, Junior Officer and Clerk.
Q2How many vacancies are there in PDC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
There are a total of 147 vacancies. The Clerk post carries the highest share with 100 vacancies, followed by 30 vacancies for Junior Officer, with the remaining seven posts together accounting for 17 vacancies.
Q3What is the last date to apply for PDC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application window opened on 14 June 2026 and closes on 28 June 2026 at midnight. Candidates should apply well before the deadline, as the bank reserves the right to extend this date.
Q4What is the application fee for PDC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is ₹1000 per post, payable online through UPI QR Code. The fee is non-refundable, and candidates applying for more than one post must pay the fee separately for each post.
Q5What is the age limit for PDC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
Age limits vary by post, ranging from 20–30 years for Clerk (no relaxation) to 35–51 years for General Manager. Age for all posts will be calculated as on 1 July 2026.
Q6What is the educational qualification required for PDC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
A graduate degree in any subject from a recognised university is the essential qualification for all nine posts. Clerk applicants additionally need a minimum of 55% marks in graduation.
Q7What is the selection process for PDC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection involves a written examination for eligible candidates, followed by an interview for those shortlisted. Candidates applying for multiple posts must appear for a separate written exam for each post.
Q8How can I apply online for PDC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online through https://pdc.bank.in/career or https://pdc.ugrowhrservices.com by filling the digital application form, uploading the required documents, and paying the ₹1000 fee via UPI QR Code before 28 June 2026.
Q9Is there any age relaxation in PDC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
Age relaxation on the upper age limit is allowed only as an exception for exceptionally capable and experienced candidates. There is no relaxation in the minimum age requirement for any post, including Clerk.
Q10Is computer knowledge mandatory for the Clerk post in PDC Bank Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates without a computer background must hold a Basic Computer Course certificate, or complete the relevant course within six months of joining the bank, to be eligible for continued appointment to the Clerk post.
