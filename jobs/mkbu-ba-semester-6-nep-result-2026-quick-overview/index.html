How to Check MKBU B.A. Sem 6 NEP Result 2026

Based on the official notification, candidates can check their result as follows:

  • Visit the official MKBU website: www.mkbhavuni.edu.in.
  • Open the official result notification PDF for 2127 - B.A. Sem-6 (NEP), June-July-Aug 2026.
  • Use "Find" (Ctrl+F) in the PDF to locate your seat/enrollment number under the Pass, Cancel, or Withheld list.
  • Note which category your number falls under.

The notification is a list of seat numbers only; it does not describe a separate online result-checking portal, login process, or roll-number search tool, so no such steps are included here.

What Does "Withheld" and "Cancel" Mean in MKBU Results?

The notification uses these categories without further explanation:

  • Pass: The candidate has cleared the examination.
  • Cancel: The candidate's result for this examination has been cancelled.
  • Withheld: The candidate's result is on hold and has not been finalized.

The specific reason for a cancelled or withheld result (such as pending fees, discrepancy, or malpractice) is not stated in this notification. Affected candidates should contact the Academic Section of M.K. Bhavnagar University directly for case-specific information, as this detail is not available in the official document.

Reassessment Process

The notification specifies only the following about reassessment:

  • Last date for reassessment application: 27/07/2026
  • Late submission accepted for 5 additional days with a late fee

Important Instructions

  • Cross-check your seat/enrollment number carefully against the official PDF before drawing conclusions about your result.
  • Candidates under the "Cancel" or "Withheld" category should contact the university's Academic Section for further clarification, as reasons are not published in the notification.
  • The last date for reassessment is 27/07/2026, extendable by 5 days with a late fee, as per the notification.
  • The notification is issued for internal circulation to colleges and university offices as well as public notification; it is copied to the Principal of all Arts Colleges, the Dean of Faculty of Arts, the Registrar of all universities in Gujarat State, the P.S. to the Vice-Chancellor, the Sr. Assistant to the Registrar, and the Academic Section of MKBU.
