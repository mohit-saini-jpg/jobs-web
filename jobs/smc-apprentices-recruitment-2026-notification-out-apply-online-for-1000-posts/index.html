Selection Process

  • Selection for engagement of apprentices will be based on merit / document verification. No written examination has been announced. Final engagement is subject to verification of eligibility and documents as per the Apprentices Act
  • 1961.
  • Stage 1 – Online Application and NAPS Registration
  • Candidates must first complete registration and eKYC update on the NAPS portal (https://apprenticeshipindia.gov.in) and then apply online on the SMC recruitment portal. Only candidates with a 100% completed NAPS profile with eKYC updated are eligible to apply on the SMC website.
  • Stage 2 – Document Verification
  • Shortlisted candidates will be intimated about the document verification date via their registered mobile number and email ID. Candidates must appear with the original application form and all original documents along with self-attested copies. Candidates absent for document verification will have their candidature cancelled. Any discrepancy found between the application and original documents at any stage will result in cancellation of candidature.
  • Stage 3 – Contract Generation and Joining
  • On successful verification
  • the Apprenticeship contract will be generated on the Government website. The apprenticeship period will be as per the contract generated. On completion of the apprenticeship period
  • candidates will be immediately discharged. If a candidate leaves the apprenticeship before completion
  • the contract will be terminated.

Salary / Pay Scale

Pay ScaleRs. 9,600/- per month (ITI trades – Health Sanitary Inspector, Computer Operator); Rs. 10,560/- per month (ITI trades – Electrician, Fitter, Draughtsman, Surveyor, Mechanic); Rs. 12,300/- per month (Graduation/Degree level trades)

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only. Before applying, every candidate must mandatorily: (1) register as an Apprentice on the NAPS portal at https://apprenticeshipindia.gov.in, and (2) update eKYC in the Apprentice profile. Only after completing both steps on the NAPS portal should candidates proceed to apply online at the SMC recruitment portal. For more details, candidates may contact the Recruitment Division on Phone No. 0261-2423751-56 (Extension-231).
  • Documents to be Produced at the time of Document Verification:
  • Printed copy of the online application form
  • All original educational qualification certificates and mark sheets (ITI Trade Certificate / Degree Certificate as applicable to the trade applied for)
  • Date of Birth certificate / School Leaving Certificate
  • NAPS portal registration and eKYC completion proof
  • Caste/category certificate as applicable
  • Valid Photo ID proof
  • Self-attested copies of all above documents
  • Apply Online at: https://www.suratmunicipal.gov.in/Information/Recruitment
  • For queries: Phone: 0261-2423751-56 (Extension-231) – Recruitment Division, Surat Municipal Corporation

Important Instructions

  • THIS IS NOT A PERMANENT EMPLOYMENT. The apprenticeship period is as per the contract generated on the Government website and candidates will be immediately discharged on completion of the apprenticeship period. Mandatory NAPS portal registration with eKYC update must be completed before applying on the SMC website. Applications submitted without prior NAPS registration will not be processed. Candidates who have already completed an apprenticeship in any trade are not eligible — online contract generation will not be possible for such candidates. Stipend payable to apprentices is subject to Government policy and guidelines as amended from time to time. Document verification date and intimations will be sent only to the registered mobile number and email ID provided in the application. In case of change in mobile number or email, SMC will not be responsible for any future communication gaps. Candidates must appear for document verification with both original documents and self-attested copies. Absence from document verification will result in automatic cancellation of candidature. If, during scrutiny of application form and documents, any candidate is found not meeting the eligibility criteria under the Apprentices Act or the notification provisions, or if any discrepancy is found between the application and original certificates, candidature will be cancelled at any stage. If a candidate leaves the apprenticeship before completion of the period, the contract will be terminated. The Appointing Authority (SMC) reserves full rights to cancel this notification, cancel any trade, or make any modifications for any reason without being obligated to provide reasons for the same.
