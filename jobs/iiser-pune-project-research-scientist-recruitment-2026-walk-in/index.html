Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for IISER Pune Recruitment 2026?

There is no last date for online application. Eligible candidates must appear for the Walk-in Interview on 09 June 2026.

Q2. What is the salary for Project Research Scientist-II at IISER Pune?

Selected candidates will receive Rs. 67,000/- per month + 30% House Rent Allowance (HRA)

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for IISER Pune Project Research Scientist-II?

Candidates must have a Ph.D. in Organic Chemistry or related areas with minimum 3 years of Post-Doctoral experience.

Q4. What is the age limit for IISER Pune Recruitment 2026?

Maximum age should not exceed 40 years.

Q5. Where will the Walk-in Interview be conducted?

The interview will be held at Seminar Room No.33, Main Building, IISER Pune.

Q6. Is prior registration or online application required?

No online application is required. Candidates have to directly appear for the walk-in interview with all documents.

Q7. What documents are required for the IISER Pune Walk-in Interview?

Duly filled application form, educational certificates, experience certificates, photo ID proof, and passport-size photograph.

Q8. What documents are required to bring for the IISER Pune Walk-in Interview?

Candidates must bring a duly filled application form, original documents + one set of self-attested copies of educational certificates, experience certificates, date of birth proof, government-issued photo ID, and one recent passport-size photograph.
