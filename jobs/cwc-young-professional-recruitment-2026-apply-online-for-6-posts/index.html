CWC Young Professional Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Central Warehousing Corporation (CWC), a Navratna Central Public Sector Undertaking under the Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution, has invited online applications for the engagement of 6 Young Professionals across four post codes. The recruitment is notified vide Advertisement No. CWC/I-Engagement/Young Professional/2026/01 dated 09.07.2026. The engagement is initially for a fixed period of two years, extendable by one year, up to a maximum of three years (2+1 years).
