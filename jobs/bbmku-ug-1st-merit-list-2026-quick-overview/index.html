Selection Process for BBMKU UG Admission

  • The merit list is prepared based on academic marks scored in Class 12 (10+2) or an equivalent qualifying examination.
  • In case two applicants have the same total merit points, preference goes first to higher aggregate marks, then to an earlier date of birth.
  • Outsider students (from outside the university's home district/region) are eligible for admission only against the top 15% of seats, based on the pattern followed in recent cycles.
  • Admission is confirmed only after physical document verification at the allotted college, within the notified verification window.

How to Check the BBMKU UG 1st Merit List 2026

  • Visit the official website: bbmku.ac.in
  • Go to the Admission menu and select "UG Selection Lists" from the dropdown.
  • Find your college name in the list released colleges are marked with a "New" tag and dated 9 July 2026 onward.
  • Open the Hindi or English version of your college's First Merit List PDF.
  • Search for your form number or name in the PDF and note your allotted subject/category.
  • Download and save the PDF for the document verification stage.

Important Instructions

  • Merit lists are being released college-wise and on a rolling basis absence of your college today does not mean you were not selected; check again as more lists go live.
  • Selection lists are provisional; admission is finalized only after successful document verification at the college.
  • Keep original mark sheets, category certificates, and ID proof ready ahead of the verification period, which colleges are expected to announce individually.
  • Always verify merit list PDFs only on bbmku.ac.in avoid unofficial WhatsApp or Telegram forwards.
