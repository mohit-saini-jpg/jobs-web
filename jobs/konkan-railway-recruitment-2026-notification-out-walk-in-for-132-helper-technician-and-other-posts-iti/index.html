How To Apply

  • Candidates fulfilling the eligibility criteria may report directly for the walk-in interview at the venue on the specified date and time for their respective post. Candidates must fill and bring one copy of the application in the prescribed format (Annexure-A attached with the notification), available on the KRCL website at www.konkanrailway.com under Recruitment > Current Notifications. No online or offline application submission by post/courier is required – candidates must appear in person.
  • Registration time is strictly 09:00 hrs to 12:00 hrs only on the date of walk-in interview. Applications not conforming to the prescribed format or having illegible/ambiguous certificates or without certificates or incomplete will be summarily rejected. Any kind of canvassing will result in disqualification of the candidate at any stage of selection without assigning any reason.
  • Documents (Self-attested copies) to be attached with the filled application form:
  • Copies of certificate in proof of qualification (as per the qualification specified in the Notification Annexure)
  • Copy of proof of Date of Birth (SSLC/SSC Certificate/Birth Certificate)
  • Copy of Caste certificate in support of claims, if any (OBC-NCL certificate in prescribed Central Government format; certificate not indicating validity will not be accepted)
  • Copy of service certificate in support of claims for Ex-servicemen, if any
  • Experience Certificate issued by previous employer
  • Two passport size recent photographs
  • Character certificate from Gazetted Officer/Executive Officer certifying good moral character
  • All original certificates must also be brought for verification at the time of the walk-in interview. Selected candidates will be required to pass a prescribed medical examination before the contract is entered into. Selected candidates must be able to join within a maximum of 30 days after issuance of offer of appointment. Selected candidates will be required to execute an agreement and indemnity bond of Rs. 50,000/- in favour of KRCL on stamp paper of Rs. 100/- or more.
  • Walk-in Interview Venue: Administrative Building, Wagon Repair Workshop, Autonagar, Vadlapudi, Gajuwaka, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is KRCL Mechanical Department Recruitment 2026?

KRCL Mechanical Department Recruitment 2026 is a fixed term contract recruitment by Konkan Railway Corporation Limited to engage 132 candidates across 9 posts in the Mechanical/Project Department for VDPD Workshop, Visakhapatnam, under Notification No. CO/P‑R/7C/2026.

Q2. How many vacancies are released under KRCL Recruitment 2026 and for which posts?

A total of 132 vacancies are announced for Junior Technical Assistant/Mechanical, Quality Inspector, Technician (Welder), Technician (Fitter), Technician (EOT Operator), Vehicle Driver, Lister/Forklift Operator, Hydra Operator, and Office Boy/Helper/Khalasi posts in the Mechanical/Project Department.

Q3. What are the walk‑in interview dates and venue for KRCL Mechanical Recruitment 2026?

Walk‑in interviews will be held from 08 June 2026 to 12 June 2026 (post‑wise scheduled dates) between 09:00 hrs and 12:00 hrs at the Administrative Building, Wagon Repair Workshop, Autonagar, Vadlapudi, Gajuwaka, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.

Q4. What is the nature and duration of engagement in KRCL Mechanical Recruitment 2026?

The engagement is purely on fixed term contract for an initial period of 1 year, which can be extended up to a maximum of 5 years based on satisfactory performance, conduct, and the requirement of KRCL, without any claim for regular absorption.

Q5. What is the eligibility and educational qualification for Junior Technical Assistant/Mechanical in KRCL?

For Junior Technical Assistant/Mechanical, candidates must have a full‑time Degree or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering or related branches (Production & Industrial, Automobile, Manufacturing, Mechatronic, Material Sciences, Metallurgical) from an AICTE‑recognized institute, along with at least 1 year post‑qualification experience, preferably in repair workshops or manufacturing units.

Q6. What is the age limit for KRCL Mechanical Department Recruitment 2026?

For all posts, the upper age limit is 50 years as on 01 May 2026, with relaxations for OBC (Non‑Creamy Layer) and SC/ST candidates as per government rules, while Ex‑servicemen age relaxation is as per government guidelines.

Q7. What is the salary structure for KRCL Mechanical contract posts?

KRCL offers consolidated monthly remuneration varying by post and city class; for example, Junior Technical Assistant/Mechanical gets around Rs. 47,220 in A‑Class cities (including Basic, DA, HRA, conveyance and mobile allowance), with slightly lower consolidated pay for B and C class city postings, and annual 4% increment on completion of each year, subject to KRCL’s discretion.

Q8. Is accommodation or food provided to KRCL Mechanical Department contract staff?

No, KRCL does not provide accommodation or food/mess allowance to contract employees; however, certain benefits like insurance, gratuity and bonus as per applicable labour codes, TA for duty travel, railway pass for official journeys, and leave benefits are provided as per KRCL rules.
