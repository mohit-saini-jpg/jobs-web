FAQs

Q1What is MPPSC Recruitment 2026?
A
MPPSC Recruitment 2026 is a government job notification released by the Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission for various administrative and accounts-related posts. The recruitment includes positions like Internal Accounts Examiner Officer, Assistant Accounts Officer, and Assistant Director. Eligible candidates can apply online and submit the required documents before the deadline.
Q2How many vacancies are available in MPPSC Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 06 vacancies have been announced under MPPSC Recruitment 2026 across multiple posts. These include Internal Accounts Examiner Officer, Assistant Accounts Officer, and Assistant Director (Statistics). The vacancies are distributed across different advertisement numbers issued by the commission.
Q3What is the eligibility for MPPSC Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must have a relevant postgraduate degree such as in Commerce, Economics, Statistics, or Mathematics depending on the post. Some posts also require additional qualifications like PGDCA in Computer Applications. Applicants must carefully check post-wise eligibility criteria before applying.
Q4What is the age limit for MPPSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The age limit for candidates is between 21 to 40 years as on 01 January 2027. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per Madhya Pradesh government rules. Candidates must ensure they meet the age criteria before applying.
Q5What is the application fee for MPPSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee details are mentioned in the official notification and vary by category. Candidates must pay the fee online or through MP Online kiosks during the application process. The fee once paid is non-refundable.
Q6What is the selection process for MPPSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process includes shortlisting, document verification, and a personal interview. If a large number of applications are received, a written examination may also be conducted. Final selection is based on interview marks or combined written and interview performance.
Q7What is the salary for MPPSC posts?
A
The salary varies by post, with Internal Accounts Examiner Officer receiving ₹42,700–₹1,35,100. Assistant Accounts Officer and Assistant Director posts offer ₹56,100–₹1,77,500 with additional grade pay. These pay scales are as per government norms and 7th Pay Commission.
Q8What are the important dates for MPPSC Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application process starts on 04 August 2026 and ends on 03 September 2026. The last date for submitting the hard copy of the application is 16 September 2026. Candidates must complete both steps within the given timeline.
