Tripura Forest Department Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 271 Forester, Forest Guard Posts

Government of India

Posts: 271 (Forest Guard - 167 + Forester - 104) Last Date: 31 May 2026 (upto 5:30 PM) Online

Short Information

Tripura Forest Department has released the official Forest Guard & Forester Notification 2026 on 28th April 2026 at forest.tripura.gov.in. A total of 271 vacancies have been announced under the Tripura Forest Guard & Forester Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged between 18 to 40 years with a qualification of Madhyamik (Class 10) for Forest Guard and Higher Secondary (+2 Stage) for Forester can apply online from 1st May 2026 to 31st May 2026. The official notification PDF and online application link are available below.

Salary / Pay Scale

Pay ScaleForest Guard: Level-6 (Rs. 5700-24000 + GP 2100) Forester: Level-7 (Rs. 5700-24000 + GP 2200)

Selection Process

  • Selection will be done through:
  • Written Examination (85% marks) Interview (15% marks)
  • Only candidates securing cut-off marks in the written test will be called for interview. Final selection is subject to medical fitness.
  • Written Test Syllabus:
  • For Forest Guard: Mathematics (20)
  • English (30)
  • General Knowledge (120)
  • Interview (30). Bengali/Kokborok is qualifying in nature (Min 40%).
  • For Forester: Mathematics (20)
  • English (20)
  • General Knowledge (100)
  • Science (30)

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted in online mode only through the official portal. The step-by-step process is as follows:
  • Visit the official recruitment portal: https://forestrecruitment.tripura.gov.in
  • Register and fill the online application form carefully.
  • Upload required documents and submit the form.
  • Take a printout of the submitted application for future reference.
  • Candidates applying for both Forest Guard and Forester posts need not appear twice for Physical Test.

Important Instructions

  • Only online applications will be accepted. No physical applications are entertained. Candidates must have Permanent Resident Certificate of Tripura (PRTC). Women candidates must submit a self-declaration that they are not pregnant on the date of Physical Test. No TA/DA will be paid for attending any stage of recruitment. The vacancies are subject to increase or decrease. Candidates who have applied for both posts are not required to appear for physical test twice. For detailed syllabus and selection rules, refer to the official notification and Annexures.
