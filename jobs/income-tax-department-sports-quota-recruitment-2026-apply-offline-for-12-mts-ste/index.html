Income Tax Department Sports Quota Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for Income Tax Department Odisha Sports Quota Recruitment 2026?
A
The completed application must reach the office by 31-07-2026, 6:00 PM, by Registered/Speed Post. Candidates from North Eastern States, Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Lakshadweep, and Jammu & Kashmir have until 10-08-2026.
Q2How many total vacancies are there in this recruitment?
A
There are 12 total vacancies - 7 Tax Assistant, 1 Stenographer Grade-II, and 4 Multi-Tasking Staff posts.
Q3Can a non-sportsperson apply for these posts?
A
No, this recruitment is exclusively for meritorious sportspersons who meet the sports eligibility conditions, such as representing a state, country, university, or school in recognized competitions.
Q4Is there an online application process?
A
No, applications must be submitted offline by Registered or Speed Post only. No other mode of application will be accepted.
Q5What is the educational qualification for Tax Assistant?
A
Candidates need a Bachelor's Degree from a recognized University, along with a data entry speed of 8,000 key depressions per hour.
Q6What is the age limit for these posts?
A
The age limit as on 01-08-2026 is 18-27 years for Tax Assistant and Stenographer Grade-II, and 18-25 years for MTS, with age relaxation of up to 5 years (10 years for SC/ST) for sportspersons.
Q7How is the merit list prepared for this recruitment?
A
The merit list is based on a sports achievement scoring system, where points are awarded according to the level of competition (International, National, University, Khelo India, or School) and the position achieved, subject to qualifying the post-specific skill test where applicable.
Q8Is a skill test required for all posts?
A
A skill test is required for Tax Assistant (data entry speed) and Stenographer Grade-II (dictation and transcription). MTS selection is based purely on the sports achievement merit list, with no skill test.
Q9Up to which date are sports achievements considered?
A
Only sports achievements and participation up to 31-03-2026 are considered for this recruitment.
Q10Where can candidates find the official notification and detailed eligibility criteria?
A
The detailed eligibility criteria, list of sports disciplines, and application format are available on www.incometaxindia.gov.in.
