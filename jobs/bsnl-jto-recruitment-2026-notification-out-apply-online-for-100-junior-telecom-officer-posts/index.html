How To Apply

  • Eligible candidates must complete the online registration process as follows:
  • Visit the BSNL official examination website: www.externalexam.bsnl.co.in and register online only. No manual/paper applications will be entertained.
  • Keep the following ready before registering: valid mobile number and email ID (for OTP), scanned passport size colour photograph (jpg/jpeg, 20–50 kb, white background), scanned signature (jpg/jpeg, 10–20 kb), Class 10th Certificate (date of birth proof), Degree Certificate and consolidated marksheet, photo identity proof (Aadhaar/Passport/Driving License/Voter ID), valid category/reservation certificate as applicable, and Medical Certificate for PwBD candidates.
  • Pay the requisite non-refundable examination fee online through Internet Banking, Credit Card, or Debit Card.
  • After completing registration, take a printout of the computer-generated registration form and retain it for future reference.
  • An editing window will be open for 7 days after the registration period closes (July 4–11, 2026). Selective fields can be edited during this period only.
  • Candidates currently employed in Government Departments/Public Sector Undertakings/Autonomous Bodies must produce a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their present employer at the time of document verification.
  • Candidates must select up to 21 Circle preferences (in order of preference) from the registration portal dropdown menu. Options once exercised shall be final.
  • Admit cards can be downloaded from the BSNL website using the registered username and password well before the examination date.
