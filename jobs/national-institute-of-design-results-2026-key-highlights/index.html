Notification DatePost Date: 26/05/2026

National Institute of Design (NID) has officially released the NID DAT 2026 Mains Result for the B.Des admission process for the academic session 2026-27. Candidates who appeared for the Design Aptitude Test (DAT) Mains can now check their final results online.

National Institute of Design Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - https://admissions.nid.edu/NIDA2026/ResultBDes2026Final.aspx. The result is available for undergraduate B.Des admissions, and shortlisted candidates are required to proceed with document upload and institute preference submission. Students can check and download their results using their login credentials such as email ID and password.

Candidates are advised to verify all the details mentioned in their result carefully. In case of any discrepancies, they should immediately contact the NID admission authorities.

How to Download NID DAT 2026 Mains Result

Follow the steps below to check and download your NID DAT Result 2026:

  • Visit the official website - https://admissions.nid.edu/
  • Click on the "NID DAT 2026 Mains Result" link
  • Enter your registered email ID and password
  • Click on the login button
  • Your result will appear on the screen
  • Download and save the result for future reference
