Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the DPS Nalconagar Recruitment 2026?

The last date for the submission of offline application forms for the DPS Nalconagar Recruitment 2026 is 05 June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for Teaching and Non-Teaching Staff?

There are a total of 31 vacancies available for Teaching and Non-Teaching Staff positions at Delhi Public School Nalconagar (DPS Nalconagar) through this recruitment drive.

Q3. What is the age limit for the DPS Nalconagar recruitment?

The maximum age limit for all posts is 45 years as of 01 May 2026. Age relaxation may be provided for experienced candidates, particularly those working in CBSE affiliated schools.

Q4. What is the application fee for this recruitment?

There is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive. Candidates can apply without paying any application fee.

Q5. What is the mode of application for DPS Nalconagar Recruitment 2026?

The application process for the DPS Nalconagar Recruitment 2026 is strictly offline. Candidates need to download the application form, fill it, and submit it via post or as specified in the notification.

Q6. What are the educational qualifications required for the posts?

Educational qualifications vary by post. Generally, candidates need a Post-Graduate or Graduate degree in the relevant subject with a minimum of 50% marks and B.Ed. or NTT, along with OSSTET/CTET (Paper II) for teaching positions.

Q7. What is the salary offered for the Teaching and Non-Teaching Staff positions?

The salary ranges from Rs. 25,500/- to Rs. 35,400/- per month, depending on the specific post. Office Assistants will receive Rs. 25,500/- per month, while most teaching positions offer Rs. 35,400/- per month.

Q8. What is the selection process for these posts?

The selection process for the DPS Nalconagar Recruitment 2026 includes a Written Test, followed by a Demo and an Interview for shortlisted candidates.
