Job Overview

Bhakra Beas Management Board (BBMB) has released the official Apprentice Notification 2026. A total of 20 vacancies have been announced under the BBMB Apprentice Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates with 10th class with minimum 40% marks and ITI in the respective trade for Lineman and Electrician, and 8th class pass for Gardner, can apply online till 11-06-2026. The official notification PDF and online application link are available below.
