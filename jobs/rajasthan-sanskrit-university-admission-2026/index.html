How to Apply for Rajasthan Sanskrit University Admission 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official admission portal: admission.jrrsu.in
  • Click on “New Registration” and fill basic details.
  • Login with credentials and complete the application form.
  • Upload required documents and pay the application fee.
  • Submit and take a printout of the confirmation page.

Documents Required for JRRSU Admission 2026

  • 10th and 12th Marksheet & Certificate
  • Graduation Marksheet (for PG courses)
  • Caste/Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card / ABC ID
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Signature
