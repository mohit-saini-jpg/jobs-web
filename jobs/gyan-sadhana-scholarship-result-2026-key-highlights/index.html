Gyan Sadhana Scholarship Result 2026 Key Highlights : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 29/04/2026

The State Examination Board (SEB), Gandhinagar has officially released the Gyan Sadhana Scholarship Result 2026 for the Chief Minister Gyan Sadhana Merit Scholarship (CGMS) examination conducted for the academic year 2026-27. Students who appeared for the Gyan Sadhana Scholarship exam can now check their CGMS scorecard and selection status online. As per the latest update, the Gyan Sadhana Scholarship Result 2026 has been declared online on the official website - https://sebexam.org/ on 28th April 2026.

The examination authority has published the Gyan Sadhana Scholarship result and the CGMS merit list in online mode. Students can access their scorecard by entering their confirmation number and date of birth or other login details as mentioned in the hall ticket on the SEB result portal. Along with the individual scorecards, SEB has also released the Gyan Sadhana Scholarship merit list 2026 containing the names of selected students eligible for the scholarship benefits.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the Gyan Sadhana Scholarship scorecard, including their name, roll/seat number, confirmation number, marks obtained and qualifying status. In case of any discrepancy in the result or personal details, candidates should immediately contact the State Examination Board, Gandhinagar through the official helpline or their respective schools for necessary corrections.

How to Check Gyan Sadhana Scholarship Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your Gyan Sadhana Scholarship Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official website of the State Examination Board - sebexam.org
  • On the homepage, look for the “Print Result” section or click on the link “Gyan Sadhana Merit Scholarship Result 2026 / CGMS Result 2026”.
  • Enter your Confirmation Number and Date of Birth (or Aadhaar UID / Seat number as specified in the instructions) in the login fields.
  • Click on the Submit button to view your result.
  • Your Gyan Sadhana Scholarship Result 2026 scorecard will appear on the screen with your marks and qualifying status.
  • Download the CGMS scorecard PDF and take a printout for future reference and scholarship documentation.
