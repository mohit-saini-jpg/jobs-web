Pondicherry University Admission 2026-27

Pondicherry University will admit students to its undergraduate and integrated programmes for the academic year 2026-27 on the basis of CUET (UG) 2026 scores as per the NTA CUET framework and the university’s own admission schedule.

CUET (UG) 2026 will be held in CBT mode from 11–31 May 2026 (tentative), and candidates must first apply for CUET via the NTA portal and later complete a separate Pondicherry University counselling/registration process after the CUET results are declared.

Pondicherry University 2026-27 Eligibility

  • There is no upper or lower age limit prescribed for appearing in CUET (UG) 2026 for any candidate.
  • Candidates who have already passed the Class 12 or equivalent examination are eligible to appear in CUET (UG) 2026.
  • Candidates who are appearing in Class 12 or equivalent in the year 2026 are also allowed to take CUET (UG) 2026.
  • Even though CUET itself has no age bar, candidates must satisfy any age conditions fixed by the specific University/Institution/Organization where they seek admission.

Pondicherry University 2026-27 Application Process

For 4-year UG (Hons.), B.Tech and 5-year integrated PG programmes in 2026-27, Pondicherry University will admit students through CUET (UG) 2026 conducted by NTA, followed by a separate university-level online counselling/application process.

Step 1: Apply for CUET (UG) 2026

  • Register and submit CUET (UG) 2026 application at the NTA portal https://cuet.nta.nic.in within the notified dates, choosing relevant subjects as per the eligibility of Pondicherry University programmes.
  • Appear for the CUET (UG) 2026 computer-based test between 11 and 31 May 2026 (tentative) and retain the NTA scorecard for university admission.

Step 2: Register on Pondicherry University admission portal

  • After CUET (UG) results, visit the university admission site (likely admissions.pondiuni.edu.in / pondiunicuet.samarth.edu.in) and complete online registration for UG admissions 2026-27 using CUET application number and score details.
  • Fill in programme preferences, personal and academic details, upload required documents, and pay the university counselling/application fee as notified in the 2026-27 admission brochure.

Step 3: Merit list, verification and fee payment

  • The university will publish programme-wise merit or rank lists based on CUET (UG) scores and category/reservation rules on the official website.
  • Shortlisted candidates must upload/produce certificates for verification and pay the prescribed admission fee within the deadline to confirm their seat for the 2026-27 session.

Pondicherry University 2026-27 UG: Key Documents Required

Basic academic documents

  • Class 10 (X Std.) certificate/mark sheet (for date of birth and eligibility proof).
  • Class 12 (H.Sc./equivalent) certificate and mark sheet for the qualifying examination.
  • CUET (UG) 2026 score card and CUET admit card printout.

Admission and category documents

  • Transfer Certificate (and Migration Certificate, if applicable) from the last institution attended.
  • Copy of the Pondicherry University UG application form submitted on the university admission portal.
  • Latest community/category certificate: SC/ST, OBC-NCL, EWS, or other reservation certificates as applicable and issued by the competent authority.

Identity and other supporting documents

  • Valid photo identity proof such as Aadhaar card, passport, or similar government ID.
  • Passport-size colour photographs as specified in the admission guidelines.
  • Any additional certificates claimed (PwD certificate, domicile/residence certificate for Puducherry/Karaikal quota, etc.).

How to Apply for Pondicherry University Admission 2026-27?

  • Register for CUET (UG) 2026 at https://cuet.nta.nic.in by filling the online form, choosing up to five subjects relevant to Pondicherry University, uploading photo/signature and paying the NTA exam fee.
  • Download the CUET admit card, appear for the CBT exam scheduled between 11–31 May 2026 (tentative), and later download your CUET (UG) 2026 scorecard.
  • After CUET results, visit the Pondicherry University admission portal (admissions.pondiuni.edu.in or pondiunicuet.samarth.edu.in) and complete the separate UG application using your CUET application number and score details.
  • Fill programme preferences, personal and academic information, upload required documents (marksheets, category certificates, ID proof, etc.), and pay the university’s application/counselling fee online.
  • Track merit lists and counselling notices on the university website; when selected, upload/produce original documents for verification and pay the admission fee within the given deadline to confirm your seat for 2026-27.
