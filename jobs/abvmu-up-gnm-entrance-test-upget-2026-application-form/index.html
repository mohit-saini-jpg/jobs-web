ABVMU UP GNM Entrance Test UPGET 2026 Application Form

ABVMU UP GNM Entrance Test UPGET 2026 Application Online Form is now available from 29 April 2026 to 26 May 2026. Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical University (ABVMU), Uttar Pradesh is conducting the Uttar Pradesh GNM Entrance Test (UPGET) 2026 for admission to the 3-year GNM (General Nursing and Midwifery) course in Government and Private nursing institutions across Uttar Pradesh.

ABVMU UP GNM Entrance Test UPGET 2026 Application Online Form has been released by Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical University, Uttar Pradesh. Interested candidates can submit the online application from 29 April 2026 to 26 May 2026 for admission into the 3-year GNM Nursing course for the academic session 2026-29.

The entrance exam will be held on 16 June 2026. This guide provides complete details including important dates, eligibility criteria, application fee, step-by-step application process, exam pattern, syllabus, and direct links to help aspirants successfully fill the ABVMU UP GNM Entrance Test UPGET 2026 Application Online Form and secure admission in reputed GNM training centres in Uttar Pradesh.

How to Apply for ABVMU UP GNM Entrance Test UPGET 2026

  • Visit the official website abvmucet26.co.in or www.abvmuup.edu.in
  • Click on UP GNM Entrance Test (UPGET) 2026 link
  • Complete Candidate Registration with personal details and verify via OTP
  • Login using Registration ID and Password
  • Fill the application form in stages (Personal, Address, Education, Centre Preference)
  • Upload scanned documents, photo, signature and verification sheet
  • Pay the application fee online
  • Preview and submit the form
  • Download and print the confirmation page
