What is Manipur Board Date Sheet 2026?

The Manipur Board Date Sheet 2026 is the official examination schedule released by Council of Higher Secondary Education, Manipur for Class 12 (Higher Secondary) students for the academic year 2024-25. This comprehensive document provides:

  • Subject-wise Exam Dates: Specific date for each paper/subject
  • Exam Timing: Start time, duration, and end time for each exam
  • Semester/Year-wise Schedule: Separate schedules for different streams
  • Exam Shift Details: Morning/afternoon/evening shift information
  • Paper Codes: Subject codes for identification
  • Exam Mode: Theory/Practical/Viva-Voce details

Students appearing for Manipur Board Class 12 examinations must download and thoroughly review the date sheet to avoid missing any examination.

Method 1: Download from Official Website (Step-by-Step)

  • Open Official Website: Visit cohsem.nic.in in your browser
  • Navigate to Examinations: Click on "Examinations" or "Students" section from the main menu
  • Find Date Sheet Link: Look for "Date Sheet", "Examination Schedule", or "Time Table" option
  • Select Academic Session: Choose "2024-25" or the current academic year
  • Choose Your Course: Select "Class 12" or "Higher Secondary"
  • Select Exam Category: Choose Regular/Supplementary/Private as applicable
  • Select Semester/Year: Pick your current year
  • Download PDF: Click the "Download" or "View" button to open the date sheet
  • Save the File: Save the PDF on your device for future reference
  • Take Printout: Print a copy to keep handy during exams

Frequently Asked Questions - Manipur Board Class 12 Date Sheet 2026

Q1. When will Manipur Board Class 12 Date Sheet 2026 be released?

Answer: The Council of Higher Secondary Education, Manipur typically releases the date sheet 3-4 weeks before examinations begin. For exact dates, regularly check cohsem.nic.in or bookmark this page for instant updates.

Q2. Where can I download Manipur Board Date Sheet 2026?

Answer: You can download the Manipur Board Class 12 Date Sheet 2026 from the official website cohsem.nic.in under the "Examinations" section. Alternatively, use the direct download links provided on this page for instant access.

Q3. Is the date sheet same for regular and private students?

Answer: Usually, the exam dates are the same for both regular and private/external students, but there might be different reporting times or exam centers. Check the specific date sheet for your category.

Q4. What if I miss downloading the date sheet?

Answer: The date sheet remains available on the official website throughout the exam period. You can download it anytime. Additionally, your college will display it on notice boards, and you can get printouts from the examination office.

Q5. Can Manipur Board change exam dates after releasing date sheet?

Answer: In exceptional circumstances (natural calamities, public holidays, unforeseen events), exam dates may be revised. If this happens, a revised date sheet will be published with clear notifications. Always check the official website for updates.

Q6. Is admit card mandatory along with date sheet?

Answer: Yes, admit card is absolutely mandatory for appearing in exams. The date sheet only tells you when and what exams are scheduled. You must download your admit card separately from cohsem.nic.in before each exam.

Q7. What is the exam timing for Manipur Board Class 12 exams?

Answer: Typically, theory exams are conducted from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM (3 hours duration). Practical exams have separate timings. Check your specific date sheet for accurate timing information.

Q8. How many days gap between exams?

Answer: Usually, there's a 2-3 day gap between consecutive exams, giving students adequate preparation time. However, this can vary based on the number of subjects and administrative requirements.

Q9. Where can I check my exam center?

Answer: Your exam center details will be mentioned on your admit card, not on the date sheet. Download your admit card from the official website to know your exact examination center and seat number.

Q10. What to do if date sheet PDF is not opening?

Answer: Ensure you have Adobe Reader or any PDF reader installed. Try downloading again, clear browser cache, or use a different browser. If the problem persists, the server might be busy—try after some time or use alternative links provided.
