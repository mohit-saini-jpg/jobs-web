Students can also check their scorecard via the PRD LIVE app and SMS.

Official Websites to Check Kerala Plus One Result 2026

DHSE Kerala has activated the Kerala Plus One Result 2026 link on multiple official portals simultaneously to handle high traffic from over 4 lakh students. Students can use any of the following websites to download their DHSE First Year marks memo:

  • keralaresults.nic.in
  • results.hse.kerala.gov.in

If one portal is slow or unresponsive due to high traffic, students should try an alternate URL from the list above. Keeping the admit card handy before logging in will avoid delays during the login process.

How to Check Kerala Plus One Result 2026 Online - Step-by-Step

Students can follow these simple steps to access and download their DHSE Kerala Class 11 result 2026 scorecard from the official result portal. The process requires only a registration number and date of birth.

How to check Kerala Plus One Result 2026 at keralaresults.nic.in?

  • Visit the official result portal at keralaresults.nic.in or results.hse.kerala.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the "DHSE First Year Result 2026" link.
  • Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth in the login fields.
  • Click the "Submit" button.
  • Your Kerala Plus One 2026 marks memo will appear on the screen.
  • Verify all details carefully, then download and save the scorecard for future reference.

How to check Kerala Plus One Result 2026 via PRD LIVE App?

The PRD LIVE mobile application is an official, convenient alternative for students to access their Kerala Class 11 result 2026 directly on their smartphone without navigating to a web browser.

  • Open the Google Play Store on your Android smartphone.
  • Search for and install the "PRD LIVE" application.
  • Open the app and tap on the "DHSE Kerala Plus One Result 2026" link.
  • Enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth in the login window.
  • Tap the "Submit" button.
  • Your Kerala Plus One 2026 result scorecard will open on the screen.

How to check Kerala Plus One Result 2026 via SMS?

Students without reliable internet access can check their DHSE Kerala Plus One result 2026 through a simple SMS service. The result delivered directly to the registered mobile number.

  • Type: KERALA11 [Your Registration Number]
  • Send to: 56263
  • The Kerala Plus One result 2026 sent as an SMS reply.

How to check Kerala Plus One Result 2026 Name-Wise?

Students who do not have their registration number can check results by name using the indiaresults.com portal. Follow these steps:

  • Visit indiaresults.com.
  • Click on the "Kerala Plus One Results 2026" link.
  • Select the "View Results by Name" option.
  • Enter your full name, date of birth, and registered mobile number.
  • Click "Find Result", then select your name from the list.
  • Click "Get Results" to download your scorecard.

Details Mentioned on Kerala Plus One 2026 Marks Memo (Scorecard)

The DHSE Kerala Plus One Result 2026 scorecard, also known as the marks memo, contains all academic and personal details of the student. Students must verify each entry carefully after downloading the document.

  • Student Name
  • Roll Number
  • Date of Birth
  • School Code
  • Subject Names and Marks Obtained
  • Total Marks
  • Qualifying Status (Pass / Fail)

The online marks memo is provisional in nature. Students must collect the original, signed marks memo from their school once it is issued by DHSE Kerala.

Kerala Plus One Result 2026 School-Wise

In addition to individual results, DHSE Kerala also makes the Plus One result 2026 available school-wise. This is useful for school administrators and parents who want to check the performance of students from a particular institution.

To access the school-wise result, students or school authorities can enter the school code in the login window on the official result portals. The school-wise result link is available on the same websites keralaresults.nic.in and results.hse.kerala.gov.in.

Kerala Plus One Result 2026 - What After the Result?

Once the DHSE Kerala Plus One Result 2026 has declared, students need to take specific next steps depending on whether they have passed, failed, or wish to improve their marks. Here is what students should do after checking their result.

For students who passed

  • Collect the original marks memo from your school once issued by DHSE Kerala.
  • Preserve the downloaded scorecard as a provisional document until then.
  • Students who clear the Plus One exam will be promoted to DHSE Kerala Plus Two (Class 12).

For students who wish to improve marks (Revaluation / Scrutiny)

DHSE Kerala provides an official revaluation and scrutiny process for students not satisfied with their Plus One result 2026. Detailed instructions, application timelines, and fee details has released shortly after the result announcement.

  • Students must submit a duly completed application form in the prescribed format to their school principal.
  • The application must be accompanied by the required fee, deposited through the treasury.
  • Application forms available at respective schools and on the DHSE official portal at dhsekerala.gov.in.
  • Options available: Revaluation, Scrutiny of Answer Sheets, and Photocopy of Answer Sheets.
  • Revaluation results published at dhsekerala.gov.in.
