Qualification / Eligibility

Graduate Apprentice (Civil): BE/B.Tech (Civil) with not less than 55% marks from a reputed Institute/University approved by AICTE. Candidates should not have completed three years after passing out of the qualifying examination as on 05 June 2026. Candidates awaiting results of the minimum qualification prescribed need not apply. Candidates who have undergone apprentice training in any other organization need not apply.
