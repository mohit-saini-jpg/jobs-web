CSIR UGC NET June 2026

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has officially announced the Joint CSIR-UGC NET Examination June 2026. This exam determines the eligibility of Indian nationals for Junior Research Fellowship (JRF), Assistant Professor, and Ph.D. admission in Indian universities and colleges.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Qualification: Master’s Degree or equivalent from a recognized university
  • Minimum Marks: 55% for General/EWS, 50% for SC/ST/OBC/PwD
  • Final Year Students: Eligible to apply

Subjects (Test Papers)

  • Chemical Sciences
  • Earth, Atmospheric, Ocean and Planetary Sciences
  • Life Sciences
  • Mathematical Sciences
  • Physical Sciences

Medium of Paper: English and Hindi

How to Apply for CSIR UGC NET June 2026

  • Visit the official website: https://csirnet.nta.nic.in/
  • Click on the "CSIR UGC NET June 2026 Application Form" link
  • Register using a valid email ID and mobile number
  • Fill the application form carefully
  • Upload scanned photograph and signature
  • Pay the application fee online
  • Submit the form and download the confirmation page.
