Telangana Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Sangareddy Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 notification has been released by the Women Development and Child Welfare (WDCW) Department, Sangareddy District, Government of Telangana, for 246 posts of Anganwadi Teacher and Anganwadi Ayah. This is an Application Open stage recruitment, with online applications accepted from 06.07.2026 to 17.07.2026 (up to 5:00 PM), as per Notification No. 125/A/2026 dated 03.07.2026. This single notification covers two posts, Anganwadi Teacher and Anganwadi Ayah, across five ICDS projects in Sangareddy district, and this article explains both in detail.

Sangareddy Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 Vacancy Details

The 246 vacancies are spread across five ICDS Projects in Sangareddy district. Anganwadi Teacher posts are open in all areas, while Anganwadi Ayah posts are reserved only for tribal (Girijana) areas. Anganwadi Ayah vacancies are available only in tribal/Girijana area Anganwadi Centres (AWCs), which is why Narayankhed project, with a high concentration of tribal thanda settlements, accounts for the largest share of Ayah posts. A detailed centre-wise (AWC-wise) list covering 243 Anganwadi Centres across all five projects, including the reserved category for each post (SC, ST, BC-A/B/C/D/E, EWS, or General/Open) and the application window of 06.07.2026 to 17.07.2026 for each centre, is available in the official annexure issued by the District Welfare Officer, Sangareddy.
