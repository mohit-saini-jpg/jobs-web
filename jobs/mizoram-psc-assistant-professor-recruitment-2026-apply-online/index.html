Mizoram PSC Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online

Posts: 9 Last Date: 29 May 2026 Online

Short Information

Mizoram Public Service Commission has released the official Assistant Professor (Stage-I) Notification 2026 on 28 April 2026 at its official website mpsc.mizoram.gov.in. A total of 9 vacancies have been announced under the MPSC Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates with qualifications such as Master’s degree with 55% marks + NET/Ph.D., B.E./B.Tech. + M.E./M.Tech. in relevant branch, or MBA/PGDM with experience can apply online from 28 April 2026 to 29 May 2026. Assistant Professor (English): Master’s degree with 55% marks (50% for SC/ST/OBC/PWD) in relevant subject + NET/SLET/SET or Ph.D. as per UGC norms. Workshop Superintendent: B.E./B.Tech./B.S. and M.E./M.Tech./M.S. or integrated M.Tech. in relevant branch with first class or equivalent.
