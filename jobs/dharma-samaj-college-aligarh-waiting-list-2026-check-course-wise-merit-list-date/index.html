Dharma Samaj College Aligarh Waiting List 2026 - Check Course-Wise Merit List, Dates & Venue

Dharma Samaj College (DSC), Aligarh has released the waiting list for admission to various undergraduate courses for the academic session 2026-27. Candidates whose names appear in the waiting list must report for document verification and admission formalities on 13 July 2026, between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM, at the venue specified for their respective course and category.

The college, affiliated with Raja Mahendra Pratap Singh State University, Aligarh, has issued category-wise waiting lists for General, SC, and OBC candidates across nine undergraduate programmes, including BA, BBA, BCA, BALLB, B.Com, B.Sc (PCM/PMG), B.Sc (ZBC/ZBG), and BFA.

What Is the DSC Aligarh Waiting List 2026?

This section explains what the waiting list means for candidates who did not initially secure a seat in the provisional merit list.

The waiting list is a supplementary admission list released by Dharma Samaj College for candidates who were not accommodated in the earlier provisional merit list but are next in line for seat allotment, subject to seat availability in each course and category. Candidates named in this list get a fresh opportunity to complete their admission process if seats remain vacant after the previous round.

How to Check the DSC Aligarh Waiting List Online

This section explains the steps candidates need to follow to view or download their course-specific waiting list from the official website.

  • Visit the official college website: dscaligarh.ac.in
  • Go to the "Merit List" section on the homepage, or open the direct admission merit list page.
  • Locate the "Admission Merit List 2026-2027" table.
  • Find your course and category row marked as a "Waiting List."
  • Click the corresponding action link to view or download the PDF.
  • Note your roll number, reporting date, time, and venue.
