Short Information

Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has published the Havaldar Instructor Notification 2026 on 17th April 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 209 Havaldar Instructor positions. Candidates within the age range of 18 to 40 years and holding a 10+2 (Intermediate) qualification along with PET-2025 scorecard are eligible to apply online at upsssc.gov.in. The application process will be active from 19th June 2026 to 9th July 2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only through https://upsssc.gov.in.
  • Login using PET-2025 Registration Number and OTP.
  • Fill all required details, upload documents if needed, and pay processing fee.
  • Download and print the submitted application form for future reference.
  • Last date for submission of online application is 09 July 2026.

Important Instructions

  • Only candidates who appeared in PET-2025 and possess the scorecard can apply. Read the detailed advertisement carefully before filling the online application. Keep registered mobile number and email active. Application fee once paid will not be refunded. UPSSSC reserves the right to cancel or modify the recruitment process without assigning any reason.
