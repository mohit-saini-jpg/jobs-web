CITS Admit Card 2026 Out

The Directorate General of Training (DGT) has released the CITS Admit Card 2026 for the All India Common Entrance Test (AICET) for admission to Craft Instructor Training Scheme (CITS) 2026-27. The hall ticket is now available online at the dedicated admit card portal for all candidates who completed the CITS 2026 application before the last date.

As per the CITS 2026-27 prospectus, the All India Common Entrance Test (AICET) for CITS 2026 will be held on 31 May 2026 in computer‑based mode at designated centres across India. Candidates must download and print their CITS admit card 2026 and carry it along with a valid photo ID on the exam day.

How to Download CITS Admit Card 2026

Follow these steps to download your CITS AICET 2026 hall ticket:

  • Open the official admit card portal https://shikharcits46op9as.onlineregistrationform.org:8780/CITSAdmitcard/.
  • On the CITS AICET 2026 login page, enter your Registration Number and Password in the respective fields.
  • Click on the Submit / Login button to access your candidate dashboard.
  • Your CITS Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen with your exam date, reporting time and examination centre details.
  • Check all details carefully and then download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies of the admit card to carry to the exam centre.

If you face any issues in login or download, visit the main CITS admission site (cits2026.nimi.co.in / nimionlineadmission.in) for updates or contact the helpline given in the prospectus.

Details Mentioned on CITS Admit Card 2026

The CITS Entrance Exam Admit Card 2026 carries essential details which candidates must verify before the exam:

  • Candidate’s name
  • Registration number / application ID
  • Exam name: All India Common Entrance Test (AICET) - CITS 2026
  • Trade / course for which you have applied
  • Exam date and time (31 May 2026, with reporting time)
  • Exam centre name and full address (city, centre code)
  • Candidate’s photograph and basic details
  • Important instructions and exam‑day guidelines

If any mismatch or major error is found (name, photograph, exam centre, trade etc.), candidates should immediately contact the CITS/NIMI helpline or DGT support before the exam day.

Documents Required with CITS Admit Card 2026

On the day of the CITS All India Common Entrance Test 2026, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the CITS Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence, Passport or any other government‑issued ID as per exam instructions.
  • Any additional document mentioned in the prospectus/instructions (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter for candidates availing scribe facility).

Entry to the exam centre will not be allowed without the printed CITS hall ticket and original photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for CITS Entrance Exam 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the CITS AICET 2026, candidates should follow these exam‑day guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the CITS admit card to complete verification and seating; late entry after gate closing may not be permitted.
  • Carry only essential items: CITS admit card (A4 printout), valid photo ID and permitted stationery as per DGT instructions; follow all CBT guidelines strictly.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not specifically allowed), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the exam hall; violation can lead to cancellation of your paper and further disciplinary action.
  • Follow all instructions displayed on the computer screen and those announced by invigilators; indulging in unfair means or disobeying rules may result in disqualification from the exam and debarment from future CITS admissions.
  • Keep your CITS Admit Card 2026 safely even after the exam, as details from it will be required during result, counselling and admission at NSTIs / IToTs.
