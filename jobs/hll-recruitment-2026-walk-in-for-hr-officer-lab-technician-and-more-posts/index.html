FAQs

Q1What is HLL Lifecare HINDLABS Chhattisgarh Recruitment 2026?
A
HLL Lifecare HINDLABS Chhattisgarh Recruitment 2026 is a walk‑in recruitment drive by HLL Lifecare Limited (HINDLABS – Healthcare Services Division) to hire Lab Technicians, Accounts Officer, District Project Coordinator, HR Officer, IT Officer, Lab Coordinator and Consultants for its Medical Laboratory Project in Chhattisgarh on Fixed‑Term Contract/consultant basis. HLL is a Mini Ratna PSU under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare and operates diagnostic laboratories and imaging services in multiple states.
Q2Which posts are available under HLL HINDLABS Chhattisgarh 2026 recruitment?
A
The notification covers multiple posts including Lab Technician, Accounts Officer, District Project Coordinator, HR Officer, IT Officer (Divisional), Lab Coordinator and consultant roles such as Consultant Pathologist, Consultant Histopathologist and Consultant Microbiologist. FTC positions are for field and lab operations across State Reference Labs, Divisional Labs, District Labs, Civil Hospital Labs and selected CHCs, while consultant posts are largely specialist doctor roles.
Q3What are the important dates and walk‑in schedule for HLL HINDLABS in Chhattisgarh?
A
Walk‑in interviews for FTC posts will be conducted between 3 May 2026 and 12 May 2026 at various locations such as Manendragarh, Raipur, Jagdalpur, Jashpur, Durg, Kondagaon, Ambikapur, Bilaspur, Sukma and Balodabazar, with reporting time from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The last date to submit CVs for Consultant Pathologist, Histopathologist and Microbiologist posts via email is 30 May 2026.
Q4What is the maximum age limit and is there any age relaxation?
A
The maximum age limit for all Fixed‑Term Contract positions under this recruitment is 37 years as on 1 May 2026. Age relaxation for SC/ST/OBC/PwD candidates will be provided as per Government of India rules mentioned in the general conditions of HINDLABS/HLL notifications.
Q5What are the essential qualifications and experience required for Lab Technician and other key posts?
A
Lab Technician posts generally require DMLT/BSc(MLT)/PG(DMLT)/MSc(MLT) with 1–5 years of relevant laboratory experience depending on qualification level. Accounts Officer, District Project Coordinator, HR Officer, IT Officer and Lab Coordinator roles require post‑graduate or graduate degrees such as MCom/CA Inter/ICWA Inter, MBA in Healthcare, MSc in relevant health sciences, HR/Management or IT degrees plus 2–5 years of experience as specified in the notification.
Q6What is the selection process for HLL HINDLABS FTC posts in Chhattisgarh?
A
For FTC posts, the selection process consists of a Skill Test followed by a Written Test conducted on the same day of the walk‑in. Only candidates who qualify in the Skill Test will be allowed to appear for the Written Test, which is an objective MCQ‑based paper of 50 marks with a 30‑minute duration and no negative marking.
Q7Is there any application fee for this HLL HINDLABS recruitment?
A
No application fee is mentioned for the walk‑in‑based HLL HINDLABS Chhattisgarh recruitment. Candidates can directly attend the walk‑in interviews at the specified venues with their documents without any prior online fee payment.
Q8How can candidates apply for HLL HINDLABS Chhattisgarh FTC and Consultant posts?
A
For FTC posts, eligible candidates must attend the walk‑in interview at the designated venue and date, carrying original certificates and self‑attested copies for verification. For Consultant Pathologist/Histopathologist/Microbiologist posts, candidates need to email their updated CVs to the official HCS recruitment email ID [email protected] on or before the specified last date.
