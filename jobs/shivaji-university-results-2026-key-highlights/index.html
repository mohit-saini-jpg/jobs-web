Steps to Check Shivaji University Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your Shivaji University semester result:

  • Visit the official website of Shivaji University - http://unishivaji.ac.in/
  • Click on the "Results" section available on the homepage
  • You will be redirected to the result portal
  • Select your course and semester
  • Enter your seat number or roll number
  • Click on the submit button
  • Your result will appear on the screen
  • Download and take a printout for future reference
