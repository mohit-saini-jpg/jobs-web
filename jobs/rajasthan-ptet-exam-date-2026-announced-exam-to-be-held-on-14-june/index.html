Important Instructions for Rajasthan PTET Exam 2026

Candidates appearing for the examination should follow these guidelines:

  • Carry a printed copy of the admit card.
  • Bring a valid photo identity proof.
  • Reach the exam centre at least 60 minutes before reporting time.
  • Follow all instructions provided by invigilators.
  • Do not carry electronic gadgets such as mobile phones, smart watches, or calculators.
  • Check the exam centre location in advance to avoid last-minute issues.

A little planning before exam day can save a lot of stress.

What Should Candidates Do Before the Exam?

With the exam date now fixed, candidates should focus on revision rather than starting new topics. A few practical steps include:

  • Revise important concepts daily.
  • Solve previous year question papers.
  • Attempt mock tests to improve speed.
  • Prepare all required documents in advance.
  • Get adequate rest before the examination.

Consistency in the final days often matters more than studying for long hours.
