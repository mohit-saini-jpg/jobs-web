Eligibility Criteria for BPSSC Forest Range Officer

Candidates must be Indian citizens. Male, Female, and Third Gender candidates are eligible to apply. A candidate must hold at least one of the following: A Bachelor's degree with at least one subject from: Animal Husbandry & Veterinary Science, Botany, Chemistry, Geology, Mathematics, Physics, Statistics, or Zoology, from a recognized university, OR A Bachelor's degree in Agriculture, Forestry, or Engineering from a recognized university or equivalent institution. Selection Process for Forest Range Officer

Selection is made through a Written Examination, Interview, and Physical Efficiency Test. Candidates equal to 6 times the number of vacancies (category-wise, based on merit in the written exam) will be shortlisted and called for interview. This ratio may be reduced if a sufficient number of qualified candidates are not available for a category. The interview carries 50 marks. Marks obtained in the interview are added to the written exam score to prepare the final merit list. Conducted as per Rule 8(4) of the Bihar Forest Range Officer Cadre (Recruitment and Service Conditions) Rules, 2013, for candidates called for interview: Candidates who fail the Physical Efficiency Test will not be recommended for appointment, irrespective of their marks in the written exam and interview. BPSSC Forest Range Officer Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Visit the official BPSSC website: https://bpssc.bihar.gov.in
  • 2Go to the "Forest Dept." tab and click the link related to Advertisement No. 10/2026.
  • 3Part 1 - Registration: Enter name, nationality, mobile number, email ID, Bihar domicile status, reservation category, gender, and date of birth.
  • 4Complete the fee payment. After successful registration, a Registration ID and Password will be sent via SMS and email.
  • 5Candidates can edit their registration details before payment. After payment, details cannot be edited directly - a "Cancel Registration" option must be used instead, but the fee already paid will not be refunded, and a fresh fee must be paid for a new registration.
  • 6Part 2 - Application Form: Log in using the Registration ID and Password, and fill in father's/husband's name, mother's name, address, and educational qualification details.
  • 7Upload the scanned photograph and signature as per the specifications above.
  • 8Review all details using the "Edit Details" option before final submission. Once submitted and confirmed, no changes can be made.
  • 9After final submission, a confirmation message (SMS/email) will be sent to the registered mobile number and email ID, which completes the application process.
  • 10Take a printout of the Acknowledgement on A4-size paper and preserve it for future reference/correspondence.
  • 11Direct Link to Apply Online
  • 12Only one online application can be submitted per candidate, and only one application can be submitted per mobile number. Multiple applications by the same candidate will lead to rejection of all such applications.
