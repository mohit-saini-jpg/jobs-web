How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted through online mode at https://jssc.jharkhand.gov.in/ only. No hard copy of the application or documents should be sent to the JSSC office. Candidates should not wait until the last date and must submit applications well in advance.
  • Step-by-Step Process:
  • Visit https://jssc.jharkhand.gov.in/ and log in to apply online.
  • Read the notification/brochure carefully before filling the form.
  • Keep scanned copies of your photograph, full signature and all required documents ready before filling the form.
  • Fill in details as per your Matriculation/10th certificate (name spelling must match exactly).
  • Pay the examination fee online; select JTGLCCE-2026 under Select Payment Category and enter your Registration Number.
  • The Admit Card will be uploaded on the JSSC website; it will not be sent by post. Candidates must download it themselves.
  • All examination-related announcements will be made on the JSSC website only.
  • Documents Required:
  • Photograph and full signature (scanned, for upload)
  • Matriculation/10th Board Certificate (for DOB and name proof)
  • Educational qualification marksheets and degree certificates
  • Valid Caste Certificate in prescribed format (for SC/ST/OBC/EBC candidates - must be issued after 02.06.2016 and from Sub-Divisional level or above)
  • Local Resident Certificate (Sthaniy Niwas Praman Patra) in prescribed format
  • PwD Certificate in Govt. of Jharkhand prescribed format (Appendix-X) with minimum 40% disability
  • Sports quota documents (as per Resolution No. 1709, dated 12.09.2007) - if applicable
  • Discharge Certificate (for Ex-Servicemen)
  • Income & Asset Certificate for EWS (if applicable)
  • Apply Online at: https://jssc.jharkhand.gov.in/application-forms-apply

Important Instructions

  • Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply. Admission to the exam is purely provisional. Issuance of Admit Card does not confirm eligibility; JSSC will verify certificates after the main exam. Mobile phones, pagers, Bluetooth devices or any electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited inside the examination centre; candidature will be cancelled if found. Candidates debarred by UPSC / JPSC / JSSC or any other selection commission within the specified period are not eligible to appear. A re-evaluation fee of Rs. 500/- can be applied within 7 days of result declaration. If mass copying is reported and proved at a centre, JSSC reserves the right to cancel the entire exam at that centre. Candidates currently employed in Govt./PSU/Autonomous bodies need not submit NOC at application stage but must produce a proper relieving order at the time of joining. The correction window (05-07-2026 to 10-07-2026) allows changes to all fields except Name, Date of Birth, Email ID and Mobile Number. If a category is changed (e.g. from reserved to unreserved), the difference in exam fee must be paid. Candidates applying for both Advertisement No. 01/2026 and 02/2026 need to pay only one exam fee. Candidates applying under Sports Quota must note the eligible standards as per Resolution No. 1709, dated 12.09.2007, available on the JSSC website. All selections, notices and merit lists will be published only on the JSSC official website. Candidates are advised to check regularly. Any dispute regarding the recruitment will be settled within the jurisdiction of Ranchi only.
