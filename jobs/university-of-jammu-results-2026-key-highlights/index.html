Notification DatePost Date: 15/04/2026

University of Jammu has released Jammu University Exam Result 2026 for multiple undergraduate and engineering examinations, including UG 5th semester exams for 2024 and 2025-26 sessions (Notification No. 15, 05, 04), UG 6th semester exams for 2022, 2023, 2024, 2024-25 and 2025 sessions (Notification No. 40, 141, 64, 73, 10, 4, 25, 32, 14, 06), as well as Bachelor of Technology 5th semester (UIET) and 1st semester (UIET) exams held in January 2026. These latest results form part of the ongoing Jammu University Result 2026 updates, which cover various UG programmes and B.Tech courses through the Controller of Examinations portal.

Jammu University Result 2026 has been announced online on the official websites - jammuuniversity.ac.in and the COE portal coeju.com - with detailed marks accessible via the result dashboard at coeju.com/index.aspx. Students can visit the COE portal, select the relevant notification (UG 5th/6th semester or B.Tech 1st/5th semester UIET), and enter their roll number or registration number to view and download their scorecard. After checking their subject-wise marks and result status, candidates should verify their details carefully and contact the Controller of Examinations in case of any discrepancy or if they wish to apply for revaluation, rechecking or photocopy of answer scripts as per university rules.

University of Jammu Results 2026 Key Highlights

The results are provisional online scorecards, and students must collect their original marksheets and degree documents from the university or affiliated colleges later.

Steps to Check Jammu University Result 2026

Students can follow the steps given below to check Jammu University Result 2026 for UG 5th/6th semester and B.Tech UIET 1st/5th semester examinations.

  • Go to the Controller of Examinations Jammu University website at https://www.coeju.com/index.aspx in your browser.
  • On the homepage, locate the latest result notifications or result links for: UG 5th Semester Exams 2024, 2025-26 (Notification No. 15, 05, 04) UG 6th Semester Exams 2022, 2023, 2024, 2024-25, 2025 (Notification No. 40, 141, 64, 73, 10, 4, 25, 32, 14, 06) B.Tech (UIET) 5th Semester Exam Jan 2026 B.Tech (UIET) 1st Semester Exam Jan 2026.
  • UG 5th Semester Exams 2024, 2025-26 (Notification No. 15, 05, 04)
  • UG 6th Semester Exams 2022, 2023, 2024, 2024-25, 2025 (Notification No. 40, 141, 64, 73, 10, 4, 25, 32, 14, 06)
  • B.Tech (UIET) 5th Semester Exam Jan 2026
  • B.Tech (UIET) 1st Semester Exam Jan 2026.
  • Click on the appropriate result link for your course and semester; you may be taken to a result input page or be prompted to choose your exam from a dropdown list.
  • Enter your Roll Number or Registration Number exactly as printed on your admit card.
  • Submit the details to view your Jammu University Result 2026, which will display your personal details, course, semester, subject-wise marks, total marks, grade and result status (Pass/Fail/Reappear, etc.).
  • Download or print the online scorecard/marksheet for future reference; keep it safely until the original marksheet and degree are issued by the university or college.
  • If you find any discrepancy in your result or wish to apply for revaluation, photocopy or rechecking, follow the instructions and deadlines published by Jammu University on the COE portal or main university website.
