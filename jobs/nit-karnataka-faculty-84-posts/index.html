NIT Surathkal Faculty Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 84 Assistant Professor, Professor and Associate Professor Posts

Short Information

NIT Surathkal Faculty Recruitment 2026 notification is out to fill 84 vacancies across Karnataka. Candidates with Masters Degree, M.Phil/Ph.D Can Apply Online. The online application opens on 04-05-2026, and closes on 02-06-2026. The candidate shall apply online through NIT Surathkal website, nitk.ac.in

How To Apply

  • Visit the official website of NITK Surathkal and click on the faculty recruitment link available on the homepage (recruitment portal).
  • Register on the recruitment portal with a valid email ID and mobile number, then log in to access the online application form.
  • Fill in all required personal, academic, and experience details carefully; ensure eligibility as per department and post applied for.
  • Upload all relevant documents: identity proof, proof of date of birth, academic mark sheets and degree certificates (UG, PG, PhD), category certificates (SC/ST/OBC-NCL/EWS/PwD), experience certificates and salary slips, publications list (SCOPUS/Web of Science), proof of projects, patents, PhD guidance, administrative roles, outreach, awards, etc.
  • Pay the non-refundable application processing fee (as per category) via NEFT/RTGS/online transfer to the specified NITK bank account and upload proof of payment in PDF format.
  • Download, hand-fill and sign the mandatory “Attestation Form” (Annexure II), then upload the scanned copy in the portal.
  • Candidates in Government/Quasi Govt/Universities/PSUs must upload No Objection Certificate or Self Declaration (Annexure I) and provide Vigilance Clearance Certificate at the time of interview.
  • Verify all details and documents in the online form and then click on “Submit” to complete the application process.
  • After successful submission, download and save the PDF of the completed application form for future reference.
  • At the time of interview, candidates must produce the printed application form, original Attestation Form, and all original supporting documents along with self-attested copies.
