Qualification / Eligibility

Visiting Professor / Engineer / Scientist: Ph.D. in Engineering, Physics, Materials Science, Electronics, Photonics, Quantum Technologies, or related discipline or Master's with exceptional industry/research experience. Minimum 10-15 years experience. Organic Synthetic Chemist – Organic Gel Scientist: Bachelor's/Master's/Ph.D. in Organic Chemistry or Synthetic Organic Chemistry with at least one peer-reviewed publication and completed synthesis project. Product Development Manager – Scientific Product Design: Bachelor's/Master's/Ph.D. in Product Design / Industrial Design / Design Engineering / Mechanical Engineering with at least one end-to-end project from concept to prototype. Optical Laser Circuit Design Specialist: Bachelor's/Master's/Ph.D. in Photonics / Optics / Applied Physics / Electrical Engineering with at least one completed project in optical circuits/laser systems. Photonics Device Maker / Quantum Optical Systems Specialist: Bachelor's/Master's/Ph.D. in Photonics / Optics / Applied Physics / Electrical Engineering with at least one completed project in optical sensing/photonic devices/quantum optics. PCB Design and Microwave/Antenna Engineer: Bachelor's/Master's/Ph.D. in Electronics / Electrical / Instrumentation / RF Engineering with at least one fabricated and tested device PCB project. FPGA Systems and Instrumentation Engineer: Bachelor's/Master's/Ph.D. in Electronics / Electrical / Computer Engineering / Embedded Systems with at least one functional FPGA-based device hardware system. Electronic Device Programming, Interfacing, and Wireless Electromagnetics Engineer: Bachelor's/Master's/Ph.D. in Electronics / Instrumentation / Electrical / Embedded Systems with at least one project in device control/sensor interfacing/embedded systems/wireless circuits. AI/LLM Hardware Integration Engineer: Bachelor's/Master's/Ph.D. in Computer Science / Electronics / IT / Computer Engineering with at least one demonstrable project in local/personal LLM deployment. Chief Executive Officer (CEO): Bachelor's in Engineering or Master's in Science/Engineering/Management with 8-10 years experience in technology/innovation-driven organisations. Manager cum Accountant (Project Manager): Bachelor's or Master's in Commerce/Finance/Accounting with professional qualifications such as CA/CMA preferred and 5-10 years experience. Assistant Project Manager: Bachelor's degree in any discipline with 3-7 years experience in administrative/secretarial/office management roles. Project Office Assistant: Graduate (B.Sc./B.A./B.Com./B.B.A./B.C.A. or equivalent) with 0-3 years experience in office support roles.
