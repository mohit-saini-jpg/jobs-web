Qualification / Eligibility

Details
  1. Candidates wondering if they qualify for this recruitment can note that eligibility depends on three things — nursing qualification, council registration, and Uttarakhand domicile (for reservation benefits). The sections below break this down post code by post code
  2. All four post codes accept candidates holding any one of the following nursing qualifications from an Indian Nursing Council (INC) recognised institution: B.Sc. (Honours) in Nursing, B.Sc. Nursing (regular course), Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing, or Diploma in General Nursing and Midwifery/Psychiatric Nursing. Whether a candidate's qualification falls under "diploma holder" or "degree holder" post codes depends on the nature of their original nursing programme, so candidates should match their qualification carefully against the official notification before selecting a post code
  3. In addition to the nursing qualification, every applicant must have working knowledge of Hindi and a valid, renewed permanent registration certificate from the Uttarakhand Nurses and Midwives Council, Dehradun, issued on or before 2 July 2026. Importantly, an NUI card or a mere receipt of registration application is not accepted — only the permanent registration certificate qualifies. A desirable (optional) qualification of either 2 years of Territorial Army service or an NCC 'B' or 'C' certificate can strengthen an application but is not mandatory
  4. The age cut-off date for this recruitment is fixed at 1 July 2025, not the application closing date. Candidates must be at least 21 years old and not older than 42 years as on that date, with relaxation available to specific reserved categories domiciled in Uttarakhand
  5. Only one age relaxation benefit applies per candidate, even if eligible for multiple categories, so candidates should claim the one most favourable to them rather than expecting cumulative relaxations
  6. Reservation benefits — both vertical (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) and horizontal (PwBD, Ex-Servicemen, Freedom Fighter Dependents, Orphans, State Agitators, Skilled Sportspersons) — are available only to permanent residents of Uttarakhand. Candidates from outside Uttarakhand belonging to a reserved category elsewhere will be treated as unreserved here unless they hold a valid Uttarakhand domicile certificate. OBC candidates must additionally ensure their certificate is dated no earlier than 2 July 2023 (within 3 years of the application deadline) and excludes them from the creamy layer, while EWS candidates need an Income and Asset Certificate valid for FY 2026-27

How to Apply

  • 1UKMSSB accepts applications exclusively through its website, with no offline submission allowed. Following these five steps in order helps candidates avoid the most common application errors.
  • 2Register: Go to www.ukmssb.org → click "Apply Now" → "Candidate Register Here." Complete registration with a valid email ID and mobile number, and select the correct post code carefully at this stage, since it cannot be changed later.
  • 3Log In and Fill the Form: Use the Registration Number and Password sent to your email/mobile to log in, then enter personal details, educational qualification, and council registration details (registration number, date, validity). Select two preferred exam centre cities from Dehradun, Srinagar, Haldwani, or Almora.
  • 4Upload Documents: Upload a passport photo (20-50 KB, JPG) and signature (5-20 KB, JPG), plus a single combined PDF (200 KB - 2 MB) containing all mandatory and applicable certificates.
  • 5Pay the Fee: Pay the category-applicable fee online via Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, or UPI, and save the receipt — applications without fee payment will be rejected.
  • 6Download and Save: After successful payment, download and print the final application form for future reference.
  • 7A one-time correction window opens from 4 July to 7 July 2026 (5:00 PM), where candidates can log in with their Registration Number and Password to fix most details — except mobile number and email ID. If a candidate changes category or sub-category during this window, any extra fee already paid for the earlier category will not be refunded.
