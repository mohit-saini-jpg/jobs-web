FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the UKMSSB Nursing Officer Recruitment 2026 notification date?
A
The Uttarakhand Medical Service Selection Board (UKMSSB) released the Nursing Officer Recruitment 2026 notification on 03-06-2026.
Q2Q2. How many Nursing Officer vacancies are available under UKMSSB 2026?
A
There are a total of 196 Nursing Officer posts, including male and female candidates with diploma and degree qualifications.
Q3Q3. What are the eligibility criteria for UKMSSB Nursing Officer 2026?
A
Candidates must possess GNM, B.Sc. (Hons.) Nursing, B.Sc. Nursing, or Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing degrees.
Q4Q4. What is the age limit for UKMSSB Nursing Officer 2026?
A
Applicants should be between 21 and 42 years. Age relaxation is applicable as per government rules.
Q5Q5. What is the application fee for UKMSSB Nursing Officer 2026?
A
General/OBC: Rs. 300 SC/ST/EWS/PWD: Rs. 150 Payment is to be made online.
Q6Q6. What is the UKMSSB Nursing Officer pay scale 2026?
A
The salary is Rs. 44,900 – Rs. 1,42,400 (Level-7) as per the 7th CPC pay matrix.
Q7Q7. How can I apply online for UKMSSB Nursing Officer 2026?
A
Candidates must apply through the official UKMSSB website: ukmssb.org. Ensure eligibility before submitting the application.
Q8Q8. Where can I download the UKMSSB Nursing Officer Recruitment 2026 notification PDF?
A
The official notification PDF can be downloaded directly from UKMSSB official website under the Recruitment section.
