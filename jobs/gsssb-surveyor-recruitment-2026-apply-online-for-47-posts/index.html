Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the GSSSB Surveyor, Class-3 recruitment?

The last date to apply for the GSSSB Surveyor, Class-3 recruitment is 29-05-2026. Ensure your application is submitted before this date to be considered.

Q2. What are the minimum and maximum age limits for the Surveyor, Class-3 post?

The minimum age limit is 18 years, and the maximum age limit is 33 years. Age relaxation is applicable for disabled candidates as per government rules.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Surveyor, Class-3 post?

Candidates must possess a Diploma in Civil Engineering or an equivalent qualification. A basic knowledge of computer applications and proficiency in Gujarati or Hindi is also required.

Q4. What is the application fee for the GSSSB Surveyor, Class-3 recruitment?

The application fee is Rs. 500/- for Unreserved category candidates and Rs. 300/- for Reserved category candidates, including women, SEBC, SC, ST, EWS, Ex-Servicemen, and Disabled individuals.

Q5. How will candidates be selected for the Surveyor, Class-3 position?

The selection process involves a Competitive Examination (MCQ - Computer Based Response Test) followed by Document Verification.

Q6. What is the starting salary for the Surveyor, Class-3 post?

Initially, candidates will receive a fixed pay of Rs. 26,000/- per month for the first five years. After regularization, the salary will be in the pay scale of Rs. 25,500/- to Rs. 81,100/- (Level-4).

Q7. Can candidates with a Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering apply for this post?

No, candidates holding a Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering are not eligible to apply for this Surveyor, Class-3 post.

Q8. Where can I find the official notification for the GSSSB Surveyor, Class-3 recruitment?

The official notification can be downloaded from the 'Important Links' section of this article or from the GSSSB official website.
