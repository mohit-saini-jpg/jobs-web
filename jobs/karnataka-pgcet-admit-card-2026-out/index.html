Karnataka PGCET Admit Card 2026 Out

The Karnataka Examinations Authority has released the Karnataka PGCET Admit Card 2026 for the postgraduate entrance test. Candidates who registered for MBA, MCA, M.E./M.Tech, and M.Arch courses can now download their hall ticket from the official portal.

The admit card is mandatory for entry into the examination hall, and candidates should verify all details carefully before appearing for the test.

How to Download Karnataka PGCET Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official KEA portal cetonline.karnataka.gov.in.
  • Open the PGCET 2026 Hall Ticket / Admit Card link.
  • Enter your application number and the first four letters of your name or the details requested on the login page.
  • Submit the information to view your hall ticket.
  • Download the admit card and take a clear printout.

If the portal is busy, candidates should try again after some time.

Details Mentioned on the Admit Card

The Karnataka PGCET hall ticket generally includes:

  • Candidate’s name.
  • Application number and roll number.
  • Photograph and signature.
  • Course name and paper details.
  • Exam date and time.
  • Exam centre name and address.
  • Reporting time and instructions.

Check every detail carefully. If any information is incorrect, contact KEA immediately.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates should carry the following items to the exam centre:

  • A printed copy of the Karnataka PGCET Admit Card 2026.
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence or Passport.
  • Recent passport-size photographs, if mentioned in the instructions.

Without the admit card and valid ID, entry may not be allowed.

Exam-Day Instructions

  • Reach the exam centre well before reporting time.
  • Carry only permitted stationery and documents.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators or electronic gadgets.
  • Follow all instructions given by the invigilators.
  • Keep the hall ticket safe until the admission process is over.
