IDBI Bank Specialist Officer Selection Process

Selection is based on a multi-stage process, and merely meeting the eligibility criteria does not guarantee a call for the next stage. Preliminary Screening: Based on age, educational qualification, and experience declared in the application form. Document Verification (DV): Verification of all declared details against original documents. Group Discussion (GD) and/or Personal Interview (PI): Shortlisted candidates are called based on qualification, experience, and overall suitability. Pre-Recruitment Medical Test (PRMT): Final offer is subject to being declared medically fit as per the Bank's Medical Fitness Standards. IDBI Bank reserves the right to change, modify, or cancel any part of the selection process, method, or criteria at its discretion.
