About Engineers India Limited (EIL)

Engineers India Limited is a Government of India undertaking and a Navratna PSU that has built a formidable reputation as India's foremost engineering consultancy company. EIL provides complete engineering and project management services across sectors including oil & gas, petrochemicals, refineries, fertilizers, infrastructure, and more. Working with EIL offers engineers the opportunity to contribute to large-scale national and international energy projects.

Terms and Conditions of EIL Fixed Term Employment

Before applying, candidates must be aware of the contractual nature of this engagement. EIL's fixed term employment comes with specific conditions that differ from permanent PSU roles. The engagement is purely on a fixed term basis , initially for 2 years , extendable up to a maximum of 3 years from the date of joining, subject to satisfactory performance The engagement will automatically terminate on the expiry of the term unless extended Candidates may be required to work anywhere in India and abroad Only medically fit candidates will be permitted to join No claim for regular/permanent appointment in EIL shall be entertained on the basis of fixed term service Selected candidates must join within 15 days of the announcement of results

Place of Posting

The primary place of posting for all selected candidates will be in the National Capital Region (NCR) . Candidates must be prepared for transfers and outstation postings as a condition of this role.

Engineers India Limited Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the Engineers India Limited Recruitment 2026 notification number?
A
The official notification number is HRD/Rectt./Advt./2026-27/05, released by Engineers India Limited for the engagement of experienced engineers on a Fixed Term Basis across multiple engineering disciplines.
Q2What is the salary for Associate Engineers in EIL 2026?
A
The consolidated monthly remuneration for Associate Engineer Grade II ranges from ₹72,000 to ₹80,000 per month (including HRA & PF), and for Associate Engineer Grade III it ranges from ₹86,400 to ₹96,000 per month, depending on the city category (X/Y/Z).
Q3What is the last date to apply for EIL Walk-in Interview 2026?
A
The online application window closes on 07 July 2026 at 23:59 hrs. Applications submitted after this deadline will not be accepted. The portal opens on 16 June 2026 at 00:00 hrs.
Q4What is the minimum experience required for EIL Fixed Term Engineer Recruitment?
A
Candidates must have a minimum of 5 years of post-qualification relevant experience to apply for Associate Engineer Grade II positions, and a minimum of 9 years for Associate Engineer Grade III positions, calculated as on 31 May 2026.
Q5Which disciplines are eligible to apply for EIL 2026?
A
EIL is recruiting engineers from the following disciplines: Chemical, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, Instrumentation & Control, Environmental/Industrial Pollution Abatement, Metallurgy/Materials/Corrosion. The exact educational qualification requirement varies by position code.
Q6Is this a permanent job at Engineers India Limited?
A
No. This recruitment is purely on a Fixed Term Basis for an initial period of 2 years, extendable up to a maximum of 3 years from the date of joining. There is no provision for conversion to a permanent/regular appointment in EIL through this engagement.
Q7Where will the selected engineers be posted?
A
The primary place of posting is the National Capital Region (NCR). However, EIL may post selected engineers anywhere in India or abroad depending on project requirements.
Q8In which cities will the EIL Walk-in Interview 2026 be conducted?
A
The walk-in interview will be conducted in Gurugram, Kolkata, Mumbai, Chennai, Bengaluru, and Vadodara. The exact interview location and date will be communicated to candidates via their registered email ID.
Q9Is there age relaxation for OBC/SC/ST candidates in EIL Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. SC/ST candidates get a relaxation of 5 years, OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) candidates get 3 years, and PwD candidates get relaxations ranging from 10 to 15 years depending on their sub-category.
Q10What is the minimum educational qualification for EIL Fixed Term Engineer posts?
A
Candidates must hold a B.E. / B.Tech. / B.Sc (Engineering) degree in the relevant discipline with a minimum of 60% marks or equivalent CGPA from a recognized university or institution.
