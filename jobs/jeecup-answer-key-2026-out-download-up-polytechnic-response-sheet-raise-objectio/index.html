JEECUP Answer Key 2026 OUT - Download UP Polytechnic Response Sheet & Raise Objections by 15 June

JEECUP Answer Key 2026 has been released on 11 June 2026 by the Joint Entrance Examination Council, Uttar Pradesh (JEECUP). Candidates who appeared for the UP Polytechnic Entrance Examination (UPJEE Polytechnic) 2026, held from 2 June to 9 June 2026, can now download their provisional answer key and candidate response sheet by logging into the official portal at jeecup.admissions.nic.in. The objection window is open until 15 June 2026. Candidates can challenge any answer they believe is incorrect by paying a fee of ₹100 per question.

How to Download JEECUP Answer Key 2026 - Step-by-Step Guide

Downloading the JEECUP 2026 provisional answer key is a straightforward process. Candidates must log in using their registered credentials on the official JEECUP candidate portal. Follow the steps below carefully to access and save your response sheet.

  • Visit the official JEECUP website at jeecup.admissions.nic.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the "JEECUP Answer Key 2026" link.
  • Enter your Application Number / Enrollment Number and Password or Date of Birth.
  • Fill in the Captcha Code carefully and click the Login / Submit button.
  • Your JEECUP Answer Key 2026 and response sheet will appear on screen.
  • Verify the details exam date, shift timing, group, and exam centre.
  • Download the Answer Key PDF and take a printout for future reference.

How to Raise Objections Against JEECUP Answer Key 2026

If a candidate believes any answer in the provisional JEECUP 2026 answer key is incorrect, they can formally challenge it through the official online objection window. The objection facility is open until 15 June 2026. Candidates are advised not to wait until the last hour submit your objection on the same day you identify the discrepancy.

JEECUP 2026 Objection Fee

  • Objection Fee: ₹100 per question challenged
  • Refund Policy: The fee is refunded only if the objection is accepted by the authorities.
  • Mode: Online only, through the official JEECUP candidate portal

Steps to Raise Objection Against JEECUP Answer Key 2026

  • Visit jeecup.admissions.nic.in and log in with your credentials.
  • Navigate to the Answer Key Challenge section.
  • Select the question(s) you want to challenge and provide your justification with supporting documents.
  • Pay the objection fee of ₹100 per question online.
  • Submit your challenge and save the acknowledgement for your records.

After the objection window closes, JEECUP authorities will review all valid challenges. If accepted, the answer key will be updated and the final JEECUP 2026 answer key will be published. Final scores and the JEECUP 2026 result will be calculated based on the revised final answer key.

What Happens After the JEECUP 2026 Answer Key?

Understanding the sequence of events after the provisional answer key helps candidates plan their next steps effectively. Here is the complete post-answer-key timeline for JEECUP 2026.

  • Objection Window Closes (15 June 2026): No challenge will be accepted after this date.
  • Final Answer Key: JEECUP will publish the revised final answer key after reviewing all objections. Date to be announced.
  • JEECUP 2026 Result: Final scores and ranks will be calculated based on the final answer key. The result will be available at jeecup.admissions.nic.in. Date to be notified.
  • JEECUP 2026 Counselling: Qualified candidates will be eligible for centralised counselling for seat allotment at UP polytechnic and diploma colleges. Counselling registration will open after the result declaration.

Candidates must keep their scorecard safe until the entire admission process is over, as it will be required at every stage of counselling. The JEECUP 2026 scorecard is valid for one year only.
