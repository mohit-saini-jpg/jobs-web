NSTI Kanpur Guest Faculty Recruitment 2026-27 Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility for NSTI Kanpur Guest Faculty posts is trade-specific. Broadly, each trade accepts candidates through one of four routes: a relevant Degree/B.Voc with CITS and post-CITS experience, a relevant Diploma with CITS and post-CITS experience, an Ex-serviceman equivalency route through DGR, or an NTC/NAC qualification with CITS and longer post-CITS experience. Minimum age for all trades is 21 years as on 01 August 2026.

NSTI Kanpur Guest Faculty Educational Qualification (Trade-Wise)

Note: For all routes above, candidates must hold CITS (Craft Instructor Training Scheme) qualification unless applying under the Ex-serviceman equivalency route, which requires 15 years of service in the related field with a minimum 2-year methods-of-instruction course from a technical training institute of the Indian Armed Forces.

NSTI Kanpur Guest Faculty Recruitment 2026-27 Selection Process

Selection will be made on the basis of a Written Test and a Skill Test. The tentative examination date will be published on the institute website and communicated via email. Call letters for the examination will be sent through email to eligible candidates only. The written and skill test for all trades/subjects will be conducted at the same time, so applicants are advised to apply for only one post.

Documents Required for NSTI Kanpur Guest Faculty Application

Duly filled application form (in capital letters) Self-attested photocopies of all certificates and mark sheets (10th, 10+2, and technical/professional qualification) Self-attested photocopies of experience certificates 2 recent passport-size color photographs (in addition to the one affixed on the form) Valid MCWG & LMV driving license (mandatory only for MMV trade applicants)

NSTI Kanpur Guest Faculty Recruitment 2026-27 Important Instructions

Engagement of Guest Faculty is purely temporary and can be cancelled at any time without providing any reason by the engaging authority (RD/HOD). Engagement is renewed periodically every three months. Applicants are advised to apply for only one post, as the written and skill test for all trades/subjects will be held at the same time. The Regional Director, NSTI Kanpur, reserves the right to cancel or alter any recruitment without assigning any reason. The number of vacancies may increase or decrease as per requirement.
