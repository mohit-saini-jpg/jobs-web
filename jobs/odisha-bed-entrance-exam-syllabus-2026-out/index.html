Odisha B.Ed Entrance Exam Pattern 2026

Understanding the Odisha B.Ed Entrance exam pattern 2026 is crucial for effective preparation.

SectionSubjectNo. of QuestionsMarksDuration
Section I (Compulsory)English Language, General Awareness, Reasoning & Teaching Aptitude40402 Hours
Section II (Choose Any One)Arts Stream OR Science Stream6060
Total100100

Important Points:

  • Each correct answer carries 1 mark
  • Negative marking: 0.25 marks deducted for each wrong answer
  • Unattempted questions: 0 marks
  • Section I is compulsory for all candidates
  • Section II: Candidates must choose either Arts or Science Stream

Section I (Compulsory - 40 Marks)

1. Language (English) - 10 Marks

  • Comprehension – Unseen text
  • Vocabulary & Usage, Word formation, Synonyms & Antonyms
  • Spelling and Punctuation
  • Grammar (Parts of speech, Tenses, Voice, Direct-Indirect Speech)

2. General Awareness - 10 Marks

  • Everyday Science, Political System & Current Happenings
  • General Information, Educational Committees & Commissions
  • Educational Policies, Schemes & Initiatives
  • Agencies working in Education

3. Reasoning - 10 Marks

  • Logical and Analytical Reasoning
  • Series, Analogy, Coding-Decoding, Blood Relations

4. Teaching Aptitude - 10 Marks

  • Solving problems of educational context
  • Managing classroom and learning
  • Learner and learning-related issues
  • Goals of education, Role of agencies in education

Section II: Arts Stream (60 Marks)

  • General English: 15 Marks
  • Odia: 10 Marks
  • History & Political Science: 20 Marks
  • Geography & Economics: 15 Marks

Section II: Science Stream (60 Marks)

  • Physical Science: 20 Marks
  • Biological Science: 20 Marks
  • Mathematics: 20 Marks

Detailed Syllabus for Science Stream and Arts Stream is available in the official brochure.
