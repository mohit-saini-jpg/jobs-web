DTU MTech Admission 2026

DTU MTech Admission 2026 registration has started. Delhi Technological University (DTU) has opened the online application portal for M.Tech admissions for the academic session 2026-27 from 26 May 2026. Candidates can now apply for various M.Tech programmes through the official admission portal till the last date of 23 June 2026. Admission will be primarily based on valid GATE score. This is a great opportunity for engineering graduates seeking admission to one of the top technical universities in Delhi.

Application Fee for DTU MTech Admission 2026

Application fee details will be shown during the online registration process on the official portal. Candidates are advised to check the exact amount as per their category while filling the form.

How to Apply for DTU MTech Admission 2026

  • Visit the official admission portal: https://saarthi.dtu.ac.in
  • Register with valid email ID and mobile number.
  • Fill the M.Tech application form with personal, academic and GATE details.
  • Upload required documents.
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Submit the form and download the confirmation page.

Eligibility Criteria for DTU MTech Admission 2026

Candidates must hold a B.E./B.Tech degree in the relevant discipline with a minimum 60% marks (55% for SC/ST/PwD). A valid GATE score in the relevant paper is mandatory for most seats. Final year students can apply provisionally.

Courses Offered at DTU MTech 2026

Delhi Technological University offers M.Tech programmes in various specializations across multiple departments, including Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Biotechnology, Information Technology, and many emerging areas like Data Science, AI, VLSI, Power Systems, Structural Engineering etc.

The programmes are designed with an industry-oriented curriculum and a strong research focus. Candidates can select their preferred specialization during the application process as per the availability of seats and their GATE score. A detailed list of all M.Tech programmes and the seat matrix is available on the official admission portal.
