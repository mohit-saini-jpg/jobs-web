ISC Class 12 Improvement Result 2026 - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 17/07/2026

The Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE) has declared the ISC (Class XII) Year 2026 Improvement Examination Result on July 16, 2026. Students who appeared for the Improvement Examination, held from June 15 to July 1, 2026, can now check their scorecards on the official CISCE result portal using their Unique ID, Index Number, and CAPTCHA. Digital marksheets will be available on DigiLocker from July 20, 2026.

How the Final Marks Are Calculated

CISCE has clarified the rule for students who appeared for the Improvement Examination in one or more subjects:

  • For every subject in which a student appeared for the Improvement Examination, the higher of the two scores Main Examination or Improvement Examination will be treated as the final mark.
  • If the Improvement Examination score is higher than the Main Examination score, the improved mark becomes the final mark for that subject.
  • If the original Main Examination mark is higher, that mark continues to remain valid and final.

This means students who opted for improvement do not risk losing marks even if their improvement attempt scores lower than their original attempt.

ISC Class 12 Improvement Result 2026: How to Check Online

  • Visit the official CISCE result website.
  • Select "ISC" from the examination options.
  • Enter your Unique ID, Index Number, and the CAPTCHA displayed on the screen.
  • Click on the result button to view your scorecard.
  • Download or print the result for future reference.

ISC Improvement Result 2026 on DigiLocker: Steps to Download

CISCE has confirmed that digital result documents will be available on DigiLocker from July 20, 2026.

  • Visit the DigiLocker Results portal.
  • Open the CISCE section.
  • Click on "Get Class XII Result."
  • Enter your Index Number, Unique ID, and Date of Birth.
  • Click Submit to view and download the result.

Documents Students Must Return After the Improvement Result

CISCE has instructed schools to inform students about their Improvement Examination results and collect the original result documents before issuing revised certificates. Candidates whose marks have been updated must submit their previously issued documents to receive amended ones. The specific document depends on the student's result status:

Result StatusDocument to Submit
Qualified candidatesPass Certificate-cum-Statement of Marks and Migration Certificate
Candidates with SupplementarySupplementary Statement of Marks
Candidates marked Not QualifiedStatement of Marks

After schools collect the original documents, CISCE will prepare and dispatch the revised result documents to the respective schools.

Important Instructions for Students

  • Results checked online are provisional; original documents will be issued through schools.
  • Students must return their previous result documents to their school to receive the revised, updated certificates.
  • Keep the Unique ID and Index Number ready, as both are required to access the result online and on DigiLocker.
