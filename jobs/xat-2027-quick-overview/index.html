XAT 2027 Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 10/07/2026

XLRI Jamshedpur, on behalf of the Xavier Association of Management Institutes (XAMI), will open registration for the Xavier Aptitude Test (XAT) 2027 on July 15, 2026, at the official website xatonline.in. XAT is one of India's most widely accepted MBA and PGDM entrance exams, used by XLRI and 150+ other business schools for admission. This article covers everything currently known about XAT 2027 registration, dates, eligibility, fee, and the application process, based on official announcements and verified reports.

XAT 2027 New Initiative for Early Registrants

XLRI has introduced an early-registration benefit for XAT 2027: candidates who complete their application on the opening day, July 15, 2026, will be allotted their first-preferred test city, with the allotted city confirmed by August 5, 2026. This is intended to help candidates plan travel and preparation in advance.

XAT 2027 Eligibility Criteria

  • A bachelor's degree (or equivalent) in any discipline from a recognised university.
  • Candidates in their final year of graduation, or those awaiting results, can also apply.
  • No minimum percentage or marks requirement has been specified.
  • No age limit applies to XAT candidates.
  • Candidates with or without prior work experience are eligible.

Exact final-year documentation deadlines (such as a graduation-completion cut-off date) will be specified in the official brochure once released; treat any specific cut-off date circulating online as unconfirmed until XLRI publishes it.

XAT 2027 Application Fee

The application fee for XAT 2027 has not yet been officially announced. Based on the fee charged for XAT 2026 (₹2,200), the fee is widely expected to remain similar, but this figure is not confirmed and should not be treated as final until XLRI publishes the official fee structure.

How to Apply for XAT 2027

  • Visit the official website: xatonline.in
  • Click on the "Register" option and enter your name, email address, and contact details.
  • Set a password and verify your email/phone via the system-generated link or OTP.
  • Log in using your credentials and fill in the XAT 2027 application form with personal, academic, and work-experience details.
  • Upload the required scanned documents - photograph, signature, and ID proof.
  • Select your preferred test city or cities.
  • Review the declaration and all entered details carefully.
  • Pay the application fee online (once announced) via debit/credit card, net banking, or mobile wallet.
  • Submit the form and save/download the confirmation page for your records.

Important Instructions for XAT 2027 Candidates

  • Register on July 15, 2026, itself if you want your first-preferred test city allotted.
  • Double-check all personal and academic details before final submission - the fee is expected to be non-refundable and edits after submission are typically not allowed.
  • Keep all documents ready in the correct format and size before starting the form to avoid upload errors.
  • Rely only on the official website, xatonline.in, for the final notification, fee structure, and exam-date confirmation.
