What are the Steps to Download NIELIT Admit Card 2025?

  • Open your browser and go to the NIELIT official website: student.nielit.gov.in
  • On the homepage, find and click on the “Admit Card for CCC Exam 2025” link under the "Examination/Enrollment Forms" section.
  • Choose whether you are downloading the admit card for CCC from the available options.
  • Input the required details, such as your roll number or other necessary identification details to locate your admit card.
  • After entering the details, click the “Submit” button to fetch your admit card.
  • Once the admit card appears on the screen, carefully review all the details for accuracy.
  • If all the information is correct, download the admit card and take a printout for exam entry.
