AIIMS Delhi Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

A merit list will be prepared based on percentage marks in the essential qualification, desirable qualifications, merit, and experience. Only 1:5 shortlisted candidates per post (based on merit and experience) will be called for a physical interview. In case of a tie in qualifying marks, preference is given to candidates with additional desirable qualifications. The selection committee may conduct a written test, computer-based exercise, or clinical examination before the interview. A typing test will be conducted for the Data Entry Operator (DEO) post as per rules. Interviews will be conducted only in person; no TA/ DA is admissible for attending the interview. The decision of the selection committee is final.
