West Bengal State University (WBSU), Barasat, has activated the WBSU Admit Card 2026 download link for UG NEP semester examinations on its exam portal. As per official and college notices, admit cards for NEP Semester‑I (session 2025‑26) are available online for BA, BSc, BCom and other UG programmes.

The theory exams are scheduled to start from 16 May 2026, and students must download their admit cards from 6 May 2026 onwards and get them attested by their respective colleges. WBSU has also issued a notification regarding admit card correction for NEP Semester‑I 2025‑26, allowing students to rectify errors within a specified correction window.

How to Download WBSU Admit Card 2026

Students can download their WBSU NEP admit card 2026 by following these steps:

  • Go to the WBSU exam portal: https://www.wbsuexams.net.
  • Click on the “Admit Cards” section or directly open admit_all.php from the portal links.
  • On the admit card page, select the relevant exam type, such as “UG NEP SEM‑I Admit Card 2025‑26 / SEM‑III & SEM‑V” as applicable.
  • Enter your Registration Number in the given box as instructed.
  • Click on the appropriate Semester button (SEM‑I / SEM‑III / SEM‑V) to generate your admit card.
  • Your WBSU Admit Card 2026 will be displayed; download it and take a printout, then get it attested by the Principal or authorised college official (signature and stamp) on the admit card and photograph.

Colleges like Barasat Government College and others explicitly instruct students to bring printed admit cards for attestation on specified dates before exams.

Details Mentioned on WBSU Admit Card 2026

The WBSU NEP/CBCS admit card contains key examination details which students must verify carefully:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Registration number and roll number
  • Course and semester (e.g., BA/BSc/BCom NEP Semester‑I 2025‑26)
  • College name and college code
  • Exam centre name and full address
  • Subject‑wise paper names and examination dates
  • Important instructions and notes, including: Validity subject to WBSU exam rules Information on schedule changes via official websites Instruction to get admit card attested by the College Principal or representative
  • Validity subject to WBSU exam rules
  • Information on schedule changes via official websites
  • Instruction to get admit card attested by the College Principal or representative

If any error is found (name, registration number, subjects, exam centre), students must follow the admit card correction notice and contact the college or Controller of Examinations office within the correction dates.

WBSU Admit Card Correction 2025‑26 (NEP Semester‑I)

WBSU has issued a “Notice for Admit Card Correction of NEP Semester‑I 2025‑26” directing students to carefully check their admit cards and report discrepancies in time. As per the notification and college circulars, students can seek corrections only within the notified correction window, typically between 6 May and 11 May 2026 up to 4 PM, through their colleges.

Students must:

  • Download their admit card from wbsuexams.net.
  • Visit their college office with a printed copy of the admit card.
  • Report any incorrect details (name, registration, subject, centre, etc.) and submit a request as per college instructions.

Corrections are handled through the college to the WBSU Controller of Examinations; late requests beyond the deadline are usually not entertained.

Documents Required with WBSU Admit Card 2026

On each exam day, students must carry:

  • A printed copy of the WBSU Admit Card 2026 (NEP Sem‑I/III/V) with college attestation (Principal’s signature and college seal on admit card and photograph).
  • One valid photo ID if instructed by college/university (such as college ID or government photo ID).

Without a properly attested admit card, students may not be allowed to sit for the examinations.

Exam‑Day Instructions for WBSU Admit Card 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the WBSU UG NEP semester exams 2026, students should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card so that verification, seating and attendance can be completed; late entry after the scheduled time may not be allowed.
  • Carry only essential items: your WBSU Admit Card 2026 (duly attested by the college with signature and seal), a valid photo ID (college ID or government ID, if instructed) and permitted stationery such as blue/black pens, pencils, eraser and other items allowed by the university.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not allowed), Bluetooth devices, notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or written material inside the examination hall; such items are strictly prohibited and may lead to cancellation of your paper.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the WBSU admit card and those announced by the invigilators during the exam; indulging in any unfair means or disobeying exam rules can result in cancellation of the examination and further disciplinary action.
  • Keep your WBSU admit card safely even after the exam, as it may be required for future reference, result verification or university/college documentation.
