How to Download SSUHS CEE Admit Card 2026 (Once Released)

When the admit card link is activated today, candidates can download the SSUHS CEE hall ticket 2026 as follows:

  • Visit the SSUHS CEE portal ssuhs.formsrec.in or open the admit card link from the SSUHS website/notice section.
  • On the homepage, look for the link “CEE 2026 Admit Card / Download Hall Ticket” and click it.
  • Enter your Application Number and Password/Date of Birth in the specified fields and enter the security code if shown.
  • Click on Login / Submit to access your candidate dashboard.
  • Click on the “Download Admit Card / Hall Ticket” button for CEE 2026; your SSUHS CEE Admit Card 2026 will be displayed with exam date, time and centre details.
  • Verify the information carefully and download and print 2–3 clear A4‑size copies of the admit card.

The admit card will not be sent by post; it has to be downloaded online only.

Details Mentioned on SSUHS CEE Admit Card 2026

The SSUHS CEE Admit Card 2026 will contain important exam and candidate details which must be checked thoroughly:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Application number / roll number
  • Exam name: SSUHS CEE 2026
  • Course applied for (BSc Nursing / D.Pharm / B.Pharm / Allied & Healthcare)
  • Exam date and time (7 June 2026, with reporting time)
  • Exam centre name, centre code, city and full address
  • Category and other basic details (if printed)
  • Instructions and exam‑day guidelines

If there is any discrepancy in your name, course, category or exam centre details, you should contact SSUHS or the helpline numbers mentioned in the CEE notification immediately before the exam.

Documents Required with SSUHS CEE Admit Card 2026

On the day of the SSUHS CEE 2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the SSUHS CEE Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence, Passport or any other government‑issued ID as per SSUHS instructions.
  • Any additional documents mentioned in the admit card/notification (for example, PwD certificate for candidates claiming reservation under PwD category).

Without the admit card printout and valid photo ID, entry to the examination hall will not be allowed.

Exam‑Day Instructions for SSUHS CEE 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the SSUHS CEE 2026 examination, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card to complete verification and seating; late entry after gate‑closing time may not be permitted.
  • Carry only essential items: SSUHS CEE admit card 2026, valid photo ID and basic permitted stationery (pens, pencils, etc.) as per exam instructions.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless allowed), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the exam hall; violation may lead to cancellation of the paper and further action.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the admit card and those announced by the invigilators; indulging in unfair means or disobeying exam rules can result in disqualification from SSUHS CEE 2026 and future examinations.
  • Preserve your SSUHS CEE Admit Card 2026 until the completion of counselling and admission, as it may be required for verification and reference.
