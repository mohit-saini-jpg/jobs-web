What is BSA Aligarh KGBV Recruitment 2026?

BSA Aligarh KGBV Recruitment 2026 is a contract-based hiring drive by the District Basic Education Officer, Aligarh, to fill 122 posts of Principal, PGT teachers, Lab Assistant, Clerk, Caretaker, and support staff in 9 upgraded Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas. Only female candidates are eligible. Applications must reach the BSA office by 20-07-2026 via Speed Post/Registered Post only.

BSA Aligarh KGBV Recruitment 2026: 122 Vacancies for : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection will be based on merit marks calculated as per the formula below, except for Cook, Assistant Cook, Peon, and Watchman posts, which will be selected through an interview. If two or more candidates have equal quality marks, the candidate with a higher age will be ranked above in the merit list. If quality marks and age are both equal, candidates will be ranked as per the English alphabetical order of their names. All submitted certificates will be verified with the concerned board/institution at the time of selection. Honorarium payment will be made only after verification of documents. The service agreement for teaching and non-teaching staff in KGBV schools will be for 11 months and 29 days per academic session. On satisfactory performance, a new contract for the next session may be made for another 11 months and 29 days after approval of the District Magistrate. Unsatisfactory
