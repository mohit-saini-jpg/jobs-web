There are a total of 2 vacancies for the Assistant Professor posts at the Central University of Haryana. These positions are distributed across different departments as specified in the notification.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Assistant Professor posts?

The educational qualification required is as per UGC Regulations on Minimum Qualifications for Appointment of Teachers 2018 (as amended) and AICTE/NCTE norms wherever applicable. Specific details are available in the official notification.

Q4. What is the application fee for General and OBC candidates?

The application fee for General and OBC category candidates is Rs. 1000/-. SC/ST and Women candidates are exempted from paying any application fee.

The selected candidates will be appointed at Level-10 as per the 7th Pay Commission. The salary will be consolidated as per Central University of Haryana norms.

Q6. What is the mode of application for this recruitment?

The application process for the Central University of Haryana Assistant Professor recruitment is entirely online. Candidates need to visit the official website to fill out and submit their applications.

Q7. Are there any age relaxations for reserved categories?

The official notification does not specify age limits or relaxations. Candidates are advised to refer to the detailed advertisement for any such information.

Q8. What are the key stages in the selection process?

The selection process includes shortlisting based on UGC regulations, followed by a seminar/lecture/discussion on teaching/research aptitude, and finally, recommendations from a duly constituted Selection Committee.
