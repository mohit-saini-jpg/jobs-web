Salary / Pay Scale

Selected candidates will be appointed on a fixed monthly pay of ₹26,000 for the first five years, along with other benefits as per applicable Finance Department resolutions. After five years, subject to satisfactory service, candidates become eligible for regular appointment in the 7th Pay Commission scale of ₹25,000 to ₹81,100 (Level-4) , subject to the outcome of Supreme Court SLP No. 14124/2012 and SLP No. 14125/2012. For candidates appointed at Gandhinagar Municipal Corporation specifically: they will not be treated as Gujarat Government employees, and after three years of satisfactory service become eligible for the same 7th Pay Commission Level-4 scale (₹25,500–₹81,100 per the corrigendum).

GSSSB Municipal Sanitary Inspector Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection is through a single-stage competitive exam conducted in CBRT/OMR (Computer Based Response Test / Optical Mark Recognition) mode, comprising objective MCQ-type questions, divided into two parts: Combined duration for Part A and Part B: 3 hours (180 minutes). Negative marking: 1/4 mark deducted per wrong answer. Qualifying standard: minimum 40% in Part A and minimum 40% in Part B, independently, for all categories. Candidates scoring the required qualifying marks will be shortlisted for document verification, called at approximately twice the number of category-wise vacancies. Final selection/waiting list will be prepared based on merit within the category-wise vacancies.

GSSSB Municipal Sanitary Inspector Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1How many total vacancies are there in GSSSB Advertisement No. 349/202526?
A
173 vacancies in total - 145 for Municipal Sanitary Inspector, Class-3, and 28 for Sanitary Sub Inspector, Class-3 at Gandhinagar Municipal Corporation.
Q2What is the educational qualification required for GSSSB Sanitary Inspector posts?
A
Candidates must have passed HSC (or equivalent) and hold either a one-year Sanitary Inspector's Diploma or a one-year Certificate of Health Sanitary Inspectors from an ITI, along with basic computer knowledge and knowledge of Gujarati or Hindi.
Q3What is the age limit for this recruitment?
A
Candidates must be between 18 and 33 years of age as on 15/09/2025, with relaxations available for women, reserved categories, persons with disabilities, and ex-servicemen.
Q4What is the salary for GSSSB Sanitary Inspector, Class-3?
A
Selected candidates receive a fixed pay of ₹26,000 per month for the first five years, after which — subject to satisfactory service — they are placed in the 7th Pay Commission scale of ₹25,000 to ₹81,100 (Level-4).
Q5What is the application fee?
A
₹500 for unreserved category candidates and ₹400 for women (all categories), SEBC, SC, ST, EWS, PwD, and ex-servicemen candidates. The fee is refunded to those who appear for the exam.
Q6What is the exam pattern?
A
A single-stage CBRT/OMR MCQ exam with two parts: Part A (Reasoning, Data Interpretation, and Mathematics — 60 marks) and Part B (Constitution, Current Affairs, Language Comprehension, and Subject knowledge — 150 marks), totaling 210 marks over 3 hours, with negative marking of 1/4 mark per wrong answer.

A
The revised last date to apply online is 26 July 2026 (11:59 PM), with the fee payable until 29 July 2026 (11:59 PM).
