What Happens After the Rajasthan BSTC Result 2026?

After the result and merit list are published, the next major milestone for qualified candidates is the counselling process. This section covers what candidates can expect after clearing the Pre DElEd entrance exam.

Rajasthan BSTC Counselling 2026

VMOU will conduct the BSTC counselling process in June 2026 for all qualified candidates. The key steps involved are:

  • Qualified candidates must register for counselling online on the official website.
  • A non-refundable counselling registration fee of ₹3,000 is applicable.
  • The fee can be paid through online modes including e-Mitra, Net Banking, Debit/Credit Cards, or UPI.
  • Shortlisted candidates allotted institutions based on their merit rank and seat availability.
  • Candidates must report to their allotted college with all verified documents, including mark sheets, certificates, identity proof, and caste or PWD certificates (where applicable).

The exact counselling schedule notified on the official website after the result declaration.

Rajasthan BSTC 2026 - About the Exam

The Rajasthan Pre DElEd examination is a state-level entrance test conducted annually by Vardhman Mahavir Open University (VMOU), Kota. It serves as the gateway for admission into the 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed.) programme offered across government and private colleges in Rajasthan for both General and Sanskrit streams. This year, 4.97 lakh candidates appeared for 26,000 available seats spread across 376 D.El.Ed. colleges in the state, making it a highly competitive exam.
