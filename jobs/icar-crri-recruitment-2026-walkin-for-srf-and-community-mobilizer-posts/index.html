FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for ICAR-CRRI Recruitment?
A
The walk-in interview for both Senior Research Fellow and Community Mobilizer (AFO) posts is scheduled for 17 June 2026. Candidates should ensure they report on this date.
Q2What are the total number of vacancies released by ICAR-CRRI?
A
ICAR-CRRI has released a total of 4 vacancies for the posts of Senior Research Fellow and Community Mobilizer (AFO).
Q3What is the educational qualification required for the Senior Research Fellow post?
A
For the Senior Research Fellow post, candidates need an M.Sc. (Ag.) degree in specified specializations or a Post Graduate degree with NET qualification and relevant Bachelor's degree.
Q4What is the salary offered for the Community Mobilizer (AFO) position?
A
The Community Mobilizer (AFO) position offers a fixed salary of ₹ 15,000/- per month.
Q5Is there any application fee for ICAR-CRRI recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q6What is the selection process for these posts?
A
The selection process will involve a Written-cum-Personal Interview followed by Certificate Verification.
Q7Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?
A
The walk-in interview will be conducted at the ICAR-CRRI office in Cuttack, Odisha.
Q8What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents and one set of self-attested photocopies, including ID proof, educational certificates, and experience certificates if applicable.
Q9Are there any age relaxations for reserved categories?
A
Yes, age is relaxable for SC, ST, and OBC candidates as per the norms of the Government of India.
Q10What is the nature of the job at ICAR-CRRI?
A
The posts of Senior Research Fellow and Community Mobilizer (AFO) are contractual in nature.
