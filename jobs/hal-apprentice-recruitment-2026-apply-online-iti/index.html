Short Information

Hindustan Aeronautics Limited has released the official Apprentice Notification 2026 on 18 May 2026 at the official website of HAL. Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for HAL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the application form for the HAL Apprentice recruitment is 03 June 2026. Ensure your application is submitted well before this deadline.

Q2. What is the minimum age requirement for HAL Apprentice posts?

The minimum age requirement for the HAL Apprentice posts is 14 years. Candidates must meet this age criterion to be eligible for the apprenticeship.

Q3. What is the maximum age limit for HAL Apprentice recruitment?

The maximum age limit for the HAL Apprentice recruitment is 27 years. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per government norms.

Q4. What are the educational qualifications required for HAL Apprentice posts?

Candidates must have passed the 10th class under the 10+2 system with Science and Mathematics or its equivalent, along with an ITI passed certificate from NCVT.

Q5. How will candidates be selected for the HAL Apprentice positions?

Selection will be based on 100% merit, with 70% weightage given to 10th class marks and 30% weightage to ITI examination marks.

Q6. Is there any application fee for HAL Apprentice recruitment?

As per the provided data, no application fee is mentioned in the official notification for the HAL Apprentice recruitment.

Q7. What is the salary structure for HAL Apprentices?

The salary for HAL Apprentices will be as per the rule per month or as revised by the Regional Directorate of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship, Kanpur.

Q8. Where can I find the official notification for HAL Recruitment 2026?

The official notification PDF can be accessed through the 'Important Links' section of this article. It is advisable to read it thoroughly before applying.
