How to Download VKSU UG Semester 6 Result 2026

Students can access their result entirely online through the official VKSU examination portal. The process is straightforward and requires only two credentials — your Registration Number and Date of Birth. Follow the steps below to download your marksheet without any hassle.

  • Open your browser and go to the official VKSU examination portal: https://vksuexams.com
  • On the homepage, locate and click the "Result" tab in the navigation menu.
  • You will be redirected to the result download page for UG Semester 6 Examination, Session 2023-27.
  • Under the Veer Kunwar Singh University Results section, enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth in the designated fields.
  • Click the "View Result" button.
  • Your result will appear on the screen. Save it as a PDF or take a printout for future reference.
