SSUHS GNM Exam Date 2026 Out

Srimanta Sankaradeva University of Health Sciences (SSUHS) has officially released the GNM Exam Date 2026 for the entrance examination. The SSUHS GNM Entrance Exam 2026 is scheduled for 28 June 2026. This exam is conducted for admission into General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) courses in government and private nursing institutes across Assam for the academic session 2026-2027.

The official notification is available on the university website ssuhs.ac.in. Students can check complete details including application schedule, exam pattern, and important instructions from the prospectus.
