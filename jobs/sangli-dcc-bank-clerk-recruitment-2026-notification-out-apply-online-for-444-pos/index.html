Sangli DCC Bank Clerk Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in Sangli DCC Bank Clerk Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 444 vacancies for the post of Clerk (Junior Assistant) in this recruitment drive.
Q2What is the last date to apply for Sangli DCC Bank Clerk 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application and pay the fee is 08 July 2026.
Q3What is the application fee for Sangli DCC Bank Junior Assistant 2026?
A
The application fee is ₹1,180/- (₹1,000 + 18% GST) for all categories. This fee is non-refundable.
Q4Is MS-CIT mandatory for Sangli DCC Bank Clerk Recruitment?
A
Yes, MS-CIT or an equivalent recognized computer certificate is required. However, graduates or post-graduates in Computer Science or IT are exempt from this requirement.
Q5What is the age limit for Sangli DCC Bank Clerk 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 18 and 35 years of age as on 20/06/2026 (born not before 21/06/1991).
Q6What is the salary of a Clerk in Sangli DCC Bank?
A
During the probation period of 12 months, clerks receive a monthly stipend of ₹22,000/-. After confirmation, they receive pay as per the bank's clerk pay scale.
Q7Is domicile certificate of Sangli mandatory?
A
Yes, a valid Sangli District domicile certificate is mandatory for all applicants.
Q8Where will the Sangli DCC Bank Clerk exam be held?
A
The online exam will be held at four centres: Sangli, Kolhapur, Satara, and Pune. Centre allocation is at the bank's discretion.
Q9Is there negative marking in the Sangli DCC Bank Clerk exam?
A
Yes, 0.25 marks will be deducted for every wrong answer. Unattempted questions carry no penalty.
Q10How can I download the Sangli DCC Bank Clerk Admit Card 2026?
A
Hall tickets will be available on sanglidccb.bank.in/career 7–10 days before the exam date. Candidates will need their registration number and date of birth to access it.
