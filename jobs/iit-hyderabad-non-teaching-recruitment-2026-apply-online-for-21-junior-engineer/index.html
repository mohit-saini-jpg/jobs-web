IIT Hyderabad Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for IIT Hyderabad Non-Teaching Positions?

Q2How many vacancies are available for Non-Teaching Positions at IIT Hyderabad?
A
There are a total of 21 vacancies announced for various Non-Teaching Positions at IIT Hyderabad in this recruitment drive.
Q3What is the minimum qualification required for the Accountant post?
A
The minimum qualification for the Accountant post is a Bachelor's Degree in Commerce or Finance, along with at least 2 years of post-qualification experience.
Q4Is there any age relaxation for reserved categories?
A
Yes, age relaxation is provided for SC/ST (5 years), OBC-NCL (3 years), and PWD candidates (up to 15 years) as per government norms.
Q5What is the application fee for IIT Hyderabad Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 500/- per post for all candidates, except for SC/ST, PwD, EWS, Ex-Servicemen, and Women candidates who are exempted.
Q6What is the selection process for these Non-Teaching Positions?
A
The selection process typically involves the submission of applications, followed by a preliminary written test/skill test/trade test, and finally a final written test and document verification.
Q7What is the salary structure for the Junior Engineer posts?
A
Junior Engineer posts are placed in Pay Level-06, offering a competitive salary as per the IIT Hyderabad pay matrix.
Q8Can candidates currently working at IIT Hyderabad apply?
A
Yes, regular internal candidates of IIT Hyderabad have no age limit, and currently serving contractual and outsourced employees are eligible with specific age relaxations.
Q9What is the mode of application for this recruitment?
A
Applications must be submitted exclusively through the online mode via the recruitment portal on the official website of IIT Hyderabad.
