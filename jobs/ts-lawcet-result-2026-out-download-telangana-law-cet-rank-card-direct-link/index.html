How to Download TS LAWCET Result 2026 - Step by Step

Following these simple steps to get your rank card:

  • Visit the official website: lawcet.tgche.ac.in
  • Click on the "Download Rank Card" or "TS LAWCET & PGLCET Result 2026" link on the homepage
  • Enter your login credentials: Registration number Hall ticket number Date of birth
  • Registration number
  • Hall ticket number
  • Date of birth
  • Click "Get Rank Card" or "Submit"
  • Your result will display on screen
  • Download the PDF and take multiple printouts for counselling

What's Inside Your TS LAWCET Rank Card?

The scorecard includes important details you'll need for admissions:

  • Candidate name and photograph
  • Roll number/registration number
  • Total marks obtained
  • Allotted rank
  • Qualifying status
  • Category-wise cutoff (if applicable)

Important: The conducting authority won't send rank cards by post, so downloading is mandatory.
