How to Download RUHS CUET BSc Nursing Result & Cut Off 2026

Follow these steps to download your RUHS CUET BSc Nursing Result 2026 and cutoff:

  • Step 1: Go to the official RUHS website at ruhsraj.org or the CUET portal ruhscuet2026.com
  • Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link titled "RUHS CUET BSc Nursing Result 2026" under latest notifications
  • Step 3: Enter your Application Number, Roll Number, and Date of Birth in the prescribed fields
  • Step 4: Verify the information and enter the security captcha, then click Submit
  • Step 5: Your RUHS BSc Nursing Result 2026 will appear on screen. Check all details including marks and merit status
  • Step 6: Download the RUHS CUET Result 2026 PDF and save it for future reference

RUHS BSc Nursing Eligibility Criteria 2026

To qualify for BSc Nursing admission through RUHS CUET, candidates must meet these eligibility requirements:

  • Qualification: Pass 10+2 (PCB stream) with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Biotechnology
  • Minimum Marks: General category: 50%, Reserved categories (OBC/SC/ST): 40%
  • Age Limit: Minimum 17 years as of December 31, 2026
  • Language: English, Hindi, or Sanskrit (as per choice)
  • Domicile: Rajasthan state domicile required for state quota seats (80% cutoff for Rajasthan students, 85% for out-of-state)

Counselling Process After Cut Off Release

After cutoff declaration, the counselling process follows these steps:

  • MERIT LIST: RUHS releases merit list based on CUET scores
  • REGISTRATION: Qualified candidates register for counselling online
  • COLLEGE CHOICE FILLING: Candidates select preferred colleges based on cutoff and rank
  • ALLOTMENT: College allotment based on merit, preference, and seat availability
  • ADMISSION: Report to allotted college with documents for final admission
