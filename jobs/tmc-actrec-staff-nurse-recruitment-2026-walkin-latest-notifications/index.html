Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the post name for this recruitment?

The post name for this recruitment is Staff Nurse. This is a contract-based position at TMC ACTREC.

Q2. What is the application mode for this recruitment?

The application mode for this recruitment is Walk-in. Candidates need to attend the interview in person with all required documents.

Q3. What is the walk-in date for the Staff Nurse position?

The walk-in interview for the Staff Nurse position is scheduled for 04th June 2026. Candidates are advised to reach the venue on time.

Q4. What are the educational qualifications required for the Staff Nurse post?

Candidates should possess a B.Sc. (Nursing) degree with at least one year of clinical experience. General Nursing and Midwifery is also accepted.

Q5. What is the age limit for the Staff Nurse position?

The age limit for the Staff Nurse position is that candidates should not exceed 35 years of age.

Q6. What is the salary offered for the Staff Nurse role?

The salary offered ranges from Rs. 33,000 to Rs. 35,000 per month, with additional pay for experience.

Q7. Where is the walk-in interview venue?

The walk-in interview will be held at KS Auditorium, Gr. Floor, Khanolkar Shodhika, TMC-ACTREC, Sec-22, Kharghar, Navi Mumbai- 410210.

Q8. What documents are required for the walk-in interview?

Required documents include Bio-data, photograph, Aadhar Card, PAN CARD, original and self-attested copies of qualification and experience certificates, MNC registration, and Hepatitis 'B' vaccination certificate.
