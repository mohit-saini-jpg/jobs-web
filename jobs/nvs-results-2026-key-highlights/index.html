How to Check NVS Class 11 Admission Exam Result 2026

Follow the steps given below to check and download the NVS Class 11 Admission Exam Result 2026 in online mode:

  • Visit the official Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti website - navodaya.gov.in or the official result portal.
  • On the homepage, look for the link titled 'NVS Class 11 Admission Exam Result 2026' or 'JNVST Class 11 Result 2026'.
  • Click on the result link and you will be redirected to the NVS Class 11 result login page.
  • Enter your roll number and date of birth exactly as mentioned on your admit card.
  • Click on the 'Submit' or 'Login' button to proceed and view your result on the screen.
  • Check your marks, selection status, and other details displayed in the result/scorecard carefully.
  • Download the result PDF or take a printout of the NVS Class 11 Admission Exam Result 2026 for future admission and document verification use.
