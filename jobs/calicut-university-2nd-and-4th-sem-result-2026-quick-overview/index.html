How to Check Calicut University Result Online

Follow these steps to check your Fourth Semester BPEd or Second Semester MCA supplementary result:

  • Visit the official result portal: results.uoc.ac.in
  • Look for the result link matching your course "Fourth Semester BPEd (Two Year) One Time Regular Supplementary Examinations 09/2024" or "Second Semester MCA One Time Regular Supplementary Examination 9/2025."
  • Click on the relevant result link.
  • Enter your registration number and other requested login details.
  • Submit the form to view your result.
  • Download or take a screenshot/printout of the result for future reference.

Details Usually Shown on the Result

Calicut University semester results typically display the student's name, registration number, course/subject-wise marks, and overall result status (Pass/Fail or grade). However, the exact format and fields for these specific results have not been detailed in the notification, so students should refer to their downloaded result copy for confirmed details.
