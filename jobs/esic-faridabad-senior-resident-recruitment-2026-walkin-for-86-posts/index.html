ESIC Faridabad Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection for ESIC Faridabad Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 will be made on the basis of the candidate's acquired academic credentials and their performance in the interview, which will be conducted by a duly constituted selection board. There is no written examination. Key points to remember: Only candidates whose documents are successfully verified by the Document Verification Committee will be allowed to appear before the Interview Board. The result will be published only in the Recruitment section of the official website, www.esic.gov.in. The Offer of Appointment will be sent through email only. Selected candidates must join as per the instructions mentioned in the Offer of Appointment.

Required Documents for Document Verification

Candidates must appear before the Document Verification Committee on the interview date before being permitted to attend the interview, carrying the offline application form along with self-attested copies of the following documents: Certificate of passing Class 10th, for proof of date of birth All mark sheets, attempt certificates, and degree certificate of MBBS All mark sheets, attempt certificates, and degree certificate of MD/MS/DNB or other postgraduate examination Valid EWS/OBC-NCL/SC/ST/PwD certificate issued in the format applicable for appointment to a Central Government job, wherever applicable State or NMC Medical Registration Certificate (updated) Experience Certificate Recent passport-size photograph of the candidate Relieving or No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the previous or current employer, if applicable Aadhaar Card All the above documents will be verified against t

ESIC Faridabad Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1There is no online application process for this recruitment. Candidates must follow the offline, walk-in procedure described below:
  • 2Download and fill the offline application form attached with the recruitment advertisement.
  • 3Prepare self-attested photocopies of all the documents listed in the required documents section above.
  • 4Report to the Examination Hall, Academic Block, ESIC Medical College & Hospital, Faridabad, between 9:00 AM and 10:00 AM on 15 July 2026.
  • 5Appear before the Document Verification Committee with the original documents and self-attested copies.
  • 6Once documents are verified, appear before the Interview Board on the same day.
  • 7Candidates applying for more than one post must submit a separate application, along with the required documents and applicable fee, for each post.
  • 8Candidates working in Government, Autonomous, or Statutory institutions should preferably carry a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their employer at the time of the interview. If the NOC is not immediately available, a copy of the "applied for NOC" along with an undertaking must be submitted at the time of document verification.
