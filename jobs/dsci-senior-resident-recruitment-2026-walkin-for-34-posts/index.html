Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for DSCI Senior Resident posts?

The walk-in interview for the Senior Resident posts at Delhi State Cancer Institutes is scheduled for 29th May 2026. Candidates should ensure they report to the venue on this date.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for the Senior Resident post?

There are a total of 34 vacancies announced for the Senior Resident position across various specializations at Delhi State Cancer Institutes.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Senior Resident posts?

Candidates must possess a recognized MBBS qualification and a recognized PG (MD/DNB) in the concerned field, along with registration with the Delhi Medical Council.

Q4. What is the age limit for applying to these positions?

The maximum age limit for candidates applying for the Senior Resident posts is 45 years. Age relaxation will be applicable as per government norms.

Q5. What is the salary offered for the Senior Resident position?

Selected candidates will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 67700 per month, along with applicable allowances.

Q6. Is there an application fee for this recruitment?

No, there is no application fee required for candidates applying under any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.

Q7. What is the selection process for the Senior Resident posts?

The selection process will primarily involve a Personal Interview. A Written Exam may be conducted if more than 5 candidates apply for a specific discipline.

Q8. How should candidates apply for the Senior Resident positions?

This is a walk-in recruitment. Eligible candidates need to report directly at the specified venue on the interview date with all required documents.
