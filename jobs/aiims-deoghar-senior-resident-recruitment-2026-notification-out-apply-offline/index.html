AIIMS Deoghar SR Recruitment 2026: Department-wise Vacancy Details

Vacancies are distributed across 39 departments as follows. Eligibility Criteria

A Postgraduate Degree (MD/MS/DNB/MDS) from a recognized University/Institute is required. Applicants holding DNB in broad specialties as their essential qualifying education must provide documentary evidence of its equivalence with MD/MS. For the Department of Transfusion Medicine & Blood Bank specifically, the qualification required is MD (Transfusion Medicine & Blood Bank) or DNB (Transfusion Medicine & Blood Bank). Eligibility with regard to age, educational qualification, and experience will be determined as on the date the candidate appears in the interview/written test examination.

AIIMS Deoghar Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection is through an Interview/Written Test Examination conducted department-wise. The date of the interview will be notified later on the AIIMS Deoghar website, and candidates are advised to check the website regularly. Eligible candidates must report to the Registrar Office, Administrative Block, 4th Floor, AIIMS Deoghar on the notified interview date with original documents, photocopies of relevant documents, and one passport-size colour photograph. No TA/DA is admissible for appearing at the interview.
