The Board of Intermediate Education Andhra Pradesh (BIEAP) has officially released the AP Inter 1st 2nd Year Results 2026 for the Intermediate Public Examinations (IPE) conducted for first and second year students across the state. Both AP Inter 1st year (Class 11) and 2nd year (Class 12) results have been declared together in online mode, with students now able to check their subject-wise scores and overall pass status.

AP Inter Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - bie.ap.gov.in. Students can check and download their AP Inter marks memo by visiting resultsbie.ap.gov.in or bie.ap.gov.in and logging in with their hall ticket number and other required details. After downloading the provisional marks memo, candidates are advised to carefully verify their personal details, subjects and marks, and contact BIEAP or their respective college authorities immediately in case of any discrepancy.

How to Check AP Inter 1st 2nd Year Results 2026

Students can follow the simple steps given below to check AP Inter 1st and 2nd year results online in a hassle-free manner.

  • Visit the official AP Inter result portals resultsbie.ap.gov.in or bie.ap.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the link that reads "AP Inter 1st Year Results 2026" or "AP Inter 2nd Year Results 2026" as per your class.
  • You will be redirected to the AP Inter result login window.
  • Enter your Hall Ticket Number and the other required details (such as date of birth, captcha etc. if asked).
  • Click on the "Submit" or "Get Result" button to proceed.
  • Your AP Inter 1st or 2nd year Result 2026 will appear on the screen, showing subject-wise marks, total marks and result status.
  • Download the online marks memo and take a printout for future reference until the original mark sheet is issued by your college.
