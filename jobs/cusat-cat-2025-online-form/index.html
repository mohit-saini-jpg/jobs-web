Name of the Post: CUSAT CAT 2025 Online Form

Post Date: 04-01-2025

Brief Information: Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT) has given a notification for conducting of Common Admission Test (CAT) 2025. Those Candidates who are interested in the Exam details & completed all eligibility criteria can read the Notification & Apply Online.

Application Fee: For State Merit / All India Quota (excluding NRI, International, PIO, and Children of Indian Gulf Workers): ee for up to two test codes: Rs.1,200/- Kerala SC / Kerala ST Candidates (for up to two test codes): Rs. 600/- International Candidates / Persons of Indian Origin (PIO): USD 110 (One Hundred and Ten US Dollars) Children of Indian Gulf Workers (CGW) Category (for up to two test codes): Rs.6,200/- CGW Candidates Belonging to Kerala SC / Kerala ST (for up to two test codes): Rs.5,600/- Non-CGW Candidates Opting for NRI Seats: Rs. 5000/- PhD Applicants: Rs. 1,200/- PhD and M.Phil Programmes (for Kerala SC / Kerala ST candidates): Rs. 600/-
Important Dates: Online Registration and fee payment for CAT - (All PG and UG Programmes): 17-01-2025 to 16-02-2025 Online registration to M.Tech programmes: 17-01-2025 to 31-05-2025 Online Registartion for seats reserved for international candidates: 17-01-2025 to 31-05-2025 Issuance and receipt of applications for Ph.D., PDF, Certificate / Diploma Programmes etc., from the Department/Schools/Centres concerned: 17-01-2025 to 31-05-2025 Online registration to MBA programmes: 17-01-2025 to 31-05-2025 Downloading of Admit Cards: 26-04-2025 to 12-05-2025
Age Limit: For appearing in the CUSAT (CAT) 2025, there is no age limit for the candidates.
Qualification: Candidates Should Posses 12th Class (or equivalent)
