Short Information

The National Human Rights Commission (NHRC) has officially announced the Junior Research Consultant (JRC) Empanelment 2026 on contractual basis. The detailed notification under Advertisement No. 06/2026 was published on 08th May 2026 at NHRC Official Website. Candidates who are below 32 years of age and possess LLB or Post Graduate qualification in Social Science subjects with minimum 60% marks can submit their online applications on or before 09th June 2026. Direct links to download the official notification and apply online are provided below.

Salary / Pay Scale

DetailsConsolidated Remuneration: Rs. 70,000/- per month (Inclusive of Taxes). No dearness or other allowances. - Annual enhancement of Rs. 5,000/- in remuneration. - Official travel entitlement (AC 2 Tier Rail / Economy Air) with hotel, taxi, and food reimbursement as per rules. - Leave: 1.5 days per completed month (no accumulation beyond calendar year).

Selection Process

  • Applications will be screened by a Screening Committee. Shortlisted candidates will be called for a Personal Interview by a Selection Committee. Final empanelment will be based on the recommendation of the Selection Committee.

How To Apply

  • Eligible candidates may apply online only through the link: https://hrcnet.nic.in/job/candidate/Register.aspx on or before 9th June 2026.
  • Applications received after the due date or through any other mode will not be entertained.

Important Instructions

  • The engagement is purely contractual and does not confer any right for regular appointment in NHRC. Selected candidates will have to sign a Consultancy Contract. During the engagement period, JRCs cannot take up any other job/assignment. High standards of conduct, secrecy, and integrity are required. Violation may lead to immediate termination. Police verification is mandatory. Negative report will result in termination. NHRC reserves the right to restrict the number of candidates or cancel the empanelment process at any stage. Candidates must maintain confidentiality of all official information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for NHRC Junior Research Consultants Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the online application form for NHRC Junior Research Consultants Recruitment 2026 is 09 June 2026. Interested and eligible candidates must complete the registration process through the official NHRC online application portal before the closing date. Applications submitted through offline mode or after the deadline will not be accepted.

Q2. What is the salary offered for NHRC Junior Research Consultant posts?

Selected candidates for the Junior Research Consultant (JRC) position will receive a consolidated remuneration of Rs. 70,000/- per month inclusive of taxes. In addition, selected consultants will also be eligible for annual enhancement in remuneration and official travel reimbursements as per NHRC rules and guidelines.

Q3. What qualifications are required for NHRC Junior Research Consultants Recruitment 2026?

Candidates applying for NHRC JRC Recruitment 2026 should possess either an LLB degree or a Post Graduate degree in Social Science subjects such as Political Science, Sociology, International Relations, Social Work, Human Rights, Developmental Communications, or related disciplines. Applicants must secure at least 60% marks from a recognized university to become eligible for the recruitment process.

Q4. What is the age limit for NHRC Junior Research Consultant Recruitment 2026?

Applicants should be below 32 years of age as on the date of advertisement publication. Candidates exceeding the prescribed upper age limit will not be considered eligible for empanelment under the NHRC Junior Research Consultant recruitment process.

Q5. Is prior work experience required for NHRC JRC Recruitment 2026?

Yes, candidates must possess at least one year of research experience in Government organizations, reputed semi-government bodies, international development agencies, NGOs, or research institutions. Alternatively, candidates having one year of legal experience are also eligible. Internship or training experience will not be counted toward the required experience criteria.

Q6. What is the selection process for NHRC Junior Research Consultants Recruitment 2026?

The selection process includes screening of applications by a Screening Committee followed by a Personal Interview for shortlisted candidates. Final empanelment will be prepared based on the recommendations made by the Selection Committee after evaluating candidates’ qualifications, experience, and interview performance.

Q7. Is NHRC Junior Research Consultant Recruitment 2026 a permanent government job?

No, the engagement of Junior Research Consultants is purely contractual in nature. Initially, candidates will be engaged for a period of two years, which may be extended up to five years based on satisfactory performance and organizational requirements. The recruitment does not provide any claim for permanent appointment in NHRC.

Q8. How can candidates apply for NHRC Junior Research Consultants Recruitment 2026?

Eligible candidates must apply online only through the official NHRC application portal before 09 June 2026. Candidates should carefully fill in all personal, educational, and experience details while submitting the application form. Applications sent through any other mode will not be entertained by the Commission.
