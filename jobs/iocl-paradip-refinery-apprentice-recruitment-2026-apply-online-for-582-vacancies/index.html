IOCL Paradip Refinery Apprentice Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

There is no written test for this recruitment. Candidates registered on the NAPS/NATS portal and applying against the notification shall be considered for selection as follows: Selection is based on the percentage of marks in descending order (aggregate of marks of all subjects across all years/semesters) obtained in the prescribed qualification applicable to the trade applied for, and meeting the notified eligibility criteria. Preference is given to candidates applying for apprenticeship training for the first time. In case of a tie in percentage of marks, the candidate senior in age (date of birth) shall be placed higher in the merit list. If the date of birth is also the same, the candidate with a higher percentage in Matriculation shall be considered. IOCL Paradip Refinery Apprentice Recruitment 2026: Reservation for PwBD Candidates

PwBD (Persons with Benchmark Disability) candidates can apply against identified seats under Codes 102, 105, 106, 107, 108, 109, 110, and 111 only. A "person with benchmark disability" is defined under Section 2(r) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, as a person certified with not less than 40% of a specified disability. Categories under which PwBD apprentices will be engaged: PV – Low vision, Blind PH – Hard of hearing, Deaf PL – Musculoskeletal (Orthopaedic Handicap/One Arm/One Leg), Dwarfism, Acid Attack Victim, Cerebral Palsy, Leprosy Cured Multiple Disabilities (a combination of the above) Candidates must submit a Disability Certificate issued by a competent authority as per the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Rules, 2017, failing which their candidature as a PwBD candidate will not be considered.
