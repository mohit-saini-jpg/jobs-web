How to Check Odisha B.Ed Result 2026?

  • Visit the official SAMS Odisha portal at te.samsodisha.gov.in or ssbodisha.ac.in.
  • Look for the link titled "Results of B.Ed. Arts/Science, B.H.Ed. and M.Ed. Entrance Test 2026" on the homepage and click on it.
  • A login page will open on the screen.
  • Enter your Barcode/Roll Number (or SAMS Application Number and Date of Birth, depending on the login page) along with the captcha code shown.
  • Click on the "Show" or "Submit" button.
  • Your Odisha B.Ed Result 2026 and scorecard will appear on the screen.
  • Download and print the result for future reference and admission purposes.

Forgot Login Credentials? How to Recover Them

Candidates unable to log in can recover their credentials using the steps below:

  • Visit the official SAMS Odisha portal and click on "Forgot Application Number" or "Forgot Password" on the login page.
  • Enter your registered mobile number or email ID to receive an OTP for verification.
  • Reset your password after verification and log in to download the result.
  • If the online recovery does not work, contact the SAMS Central Helpline (Sanjoog Helpline) at 155335 or 1800-345-6770 (toll-free within Odisha).
  • The helpdesk operates from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM on all working days.
  • Keep your Aadhaar card and Class 10/12 roll numbers ready so the helpdesk executive can locate your profile quickly.

Details Mentioned on the Odisha B.Ed Scorecard

The Odisha B.Ed scorecard and result includes the following candidate information:

  • Candidate's name, gender, and date of birth
  • Barcode number / SAMS application number
  • Course and stream applied for
  • Social category and special reserved category (if applicable)
  • Section-wise marks and total entrance test score
  • Qualifying status and state rank
