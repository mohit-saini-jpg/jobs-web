Syllabus

DetailsThere is no written examination for this recruitment. Shortlisting is based on normalised marks in the qualifying degree. The SSB Interview assesses the candidate's Officer Like Qualities (OLQ), intelligence, psychological suitability, and overall personality. Detailed SSB procedure is available on the Indian Navy website www.joinindiannavy.gov.in. How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the Indian Navy official website www.joinindiannavy.gov.in when the application window opens from 16-05-2026 to 01-06-2026. Hard copy applications will not be accepted. To save time during the application submission window, candidates can fill in their details and upload documents in advance. The application once submitted is final - no request for amendments or changes will be entertained.
  • Personal particulars must be filled exactly as they appear in the Matriculation Certificate. Aadhaar Card issued by UIDAI is mandatory. All documents must be scanned in original JPG/TIFF format. If any scanned document is not legible or readable, the application will be rejected. Candidates must take a printout of the submitted application and carry it while appearing for the SSB interview.
  • Documents / Information Required at the Time of Online Application:
  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory - issued by UIDAI)
  • Valid and active Email ID and Mobile Number (mandatory - must not be changed until selection process is over)
  • Matriculation (10th) and Class XII certificates (for date of birth and English marks proof)
  • Mark sheets up to 5th & 7th semester (regular/integrated BE/B.Tech) or all semesters (completed degree) or pre-final year marks (final year PG students)
  • CGPA conversion formula certificate (for BE/B.Tech candidates)
  • NCC 'C' Certificate (if applicable) issued by National Cadet Corps
  • Recent passport size colour photograph (JPG/TIFF format, original)
  • Post-Selection Document Required: Selected candidates must submit proof of completing qualifying degree with minimum 60% marks by emailing [email protected] by June 2026.
  • Apply Online at: https://www.joinindiannavy.gov.in

Important Instructions

  • Only unmarried Indian citizens (Men & Women) born between 01 January 2002 and 01 July 2007 are eligible to apply. Minimum 60% marks in English in Class X or XII and minimum 60% overall in qualifying degree is mandatory. Only one application can be submitted - multiple applications will result in cancellation of candidature. No hard copy applications will be accepted - online submission only via www.joinindiannavy.gov.in. Application once submitted is final - no amendments or changes will be entertained under any circumstances. Change of SSB Centre for examination or interview is not permissible under any circumstances. There is no relaxation in medical standards on any grounds; change of medical hospital/centre is not permissible. Any candidate who is found to be married or marries while under training will be discharged from service and shall be liable to refund full pay, allowances, and training expenditure. Use or possession of narcotics is banned - candidates may be tested for drugs during SSB selection, medical, training, and subsequently during service. All candidates recommended by SSB must undergo compulsory medical examination as per Indian Navy standards. All officers/cadets will be compulsory members of the Naval Group Insurance Scheme (NGIS). Police verification and character verification are part of the final appointment process. Candidature will be cancelled at any stage of selection/training/post-commissioning in case of false declaration, misinformation, or concealment of information. Candidates are advised to regularly check www.joinindiannavy.gov.in for updates on the recruitment process. All decisions of the Indian Navy regarding eligibility, selection, and other matters are final and binding.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is Indian Navy SSC IT Officer Recruitment 2026?

Indian Navy SSC IT Officer Recruitment 2026 is a Short Service Commission entry in the Executive (Information Technology) Branch for the Jan 2027 course at Indian Naval Academy, Ezhimala, where unmarried men and women IT professionals are inducted as Sub Lieutenant rank officers.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in Indian Navy SSC IT Officer Recruitment 2026?

The Indian Navy SSC Executive (Information Technology) Jan 2027 course is expected to have around 15 vacancies, similar to the previous SSC IT January 2026 course which notified 15 posts for unmarried male and female candidates. Final vacancy numbers are confirmed in the official notification PDF.

Q3. What are the important dates for Indian Navy SSC IT Officer Recruitment 2026?

For the Jan 2027 SSC IT course, the notification is expected in the first half of 2026, with online applications typically open for about three to four weeks—previously, the Jan 2026 SSC IT entry accepted applications from early to mid‑August, and the new cycle is likely to follow a similar timeline. Candidates must check joinindiannavy.gov.in for exact opening and closing dates.

Q4. What is the eligibility criteria for Indian Navy SSC Executive (IT) Jan 27 Course?

Candidates must be unmarried Indian citizens with at least 60% marks in English in Class X or XII and minimum 60% aggregate in an eligible IT‑related degree such as MSc/BE/B.Tech/M.Tech in Computer Science, IT, Software Systems, Cyber Security, Data Analytics, AI or related branches, or MCA with BCA/BSc (CS/IT). Only degrees from recognised universities or recognised foreign equivalents are accepted.

Q5. What is the age limit for Indian Navy SSC IT Officer Recruitment 2026?

For the recent SSC Executive IT entries, candidates had to be born between 2 January 2001 and 1 July 2006 (both dates inclusive) or in a very similar range, and the Jan 27 course is expected to follow an age bracket where applicants are roughly 20–25 years at the time of course commencement. The exact date‑of‑birth range will be given in the official notification and must be strictly followed.

Q6. Is there any written exam for Indian Navy SSC IT Officer Recruitment 2026?

There is no separate written exam for Indian Navy SSC IT Officer Recruitment; shortlisting is based on normalised marks in the qualifying degree, followed by SSB Interview and medical examination, similar to other SSC Officer entries for the Jan 2027 course.

Q7. How does the Indian Navy shortlist candidates for SSC IT SSB interview?

For SSC IT entries, the Indian Navy shortlists candidates using their normalised marks from the degree programme—BE/B.Tech candidates are shortlisted using marks up to a specified semester, while PG candidates like MSc/MCA/M.Tech are considered on available semester marks—after which shortlisted applicants receive SSB call‑up information on the Join Indian Navy portal and via email.

Q8. Is NCC ‘C’ certificate useful for Indian Navy SSC IT Officer Recruitment 2026?

Yes, NCC ‘C’ certificate holders in Naval/Army/Air wings with at least ‘B’ grade and required senior‑division tenure get relaxation or additional weight in many SSC Officer entries, and similar benefits are expected to apply for SSC IT Jan 27 course, subject to conditions mentioned in the official notification.
