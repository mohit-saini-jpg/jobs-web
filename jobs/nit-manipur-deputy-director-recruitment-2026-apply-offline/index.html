NIT Manipur Deputy Director Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

National Institute of Technology (NIT) Manipur has invited applications for the position of Deputy Director from among the presently working Professors of the Institute. Eligibility Criteria

Applications are invited only from presently working Professors of NIT Manipur. The detailed eligibility criteria are as under: Minimum 5 years of experience as Professor. The candidate should not be exceeding 60 years of age as on the last date of application (31.07.2026). Should have done at least 2 years as HoD / Dean. Sufficient contribution in academics, administration, and outreach activities. Must be an eminent person in his/her field of specialization with an outstanding academic record throughout. All eligibility shall be reckoned as on 31st July 2026.
