Important Instructions

The post name must be written in CAPITAL LETTERS on top of the application form and on the envelope. Separate applications with separate sets of documents must be submitted for each post applied for. Written examinations for all posts will be held on the same day; candidates applying for more than one post can appear for only one post's exam of their choice. No examination fee is required from any candidate. Applications received after 5:00 PM on 24-06-2026 or through any mode other than Registered Post/Speed Post/Drop Box will be rejected outright. Non-compliance with any requirement mentioned in the advertisement, including non-submission of any required document, will result in rejection of the application without any correspondence. Fraudulent certificates will lead to prosecution of the candidate even after joining. No TA/DA will be admissible to candidates for attending the recruitment examination. The District Judge, Balangir reserves the right to modify, revoke or cancel the advertisement/recruitment process at any time without prior notice or assigning any reason. The decision of the District Judge, Balangir shall be final as regards the entire recruitment process. Government servants and PSU employees must apply through proper channel with a No Objection Certificate. The engagement of Salaried Amin post is subject to the outcome of W.P.(C) No. 1273/2014 pending before the Hon'ble High Court of Orissa. Candidates are advised to regularly visit the official website https://balangir.dcourts.gov.in for updates on examination dates and other notifications.
