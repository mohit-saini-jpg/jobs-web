OIL Contractual Logging Assistant Recruitment 2026 Eligibility Criteria

OIL Contractual Logging Assistant Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

Candidates will be selected based on the Walk-in for Personal Assessment , carrying a total of 100 marks. The qualifying/pass marks are 50 marks for all categories. Final selection will be made purely on merit, based on the marks obtained in the Walk-in for Personal Assessment, among candidates securing the qualifying marks of 50 or above.
