IIT BHU Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Indian Institute of Technology (Banaras Hindu University), Varanasi has invited online applications for 28 Group A posts across the Administrative, Technical, Library, System Analyst, IWD, Rajbhasha and Sports cadres. Eligible candidates can apply through the SAMARTH Portal from 11 July 2026 to 10 August 2026 (till 5:00 PM). No other mode of application will be accepted.

Desirable qualifications for all three Technical Officer posts cover specialized experience areas such as instrumentation design, spectroscopy, X-ray diffraction, mechanical testing, wireless communication systems, and life sciences - the full list of 16 specialization areas is given in the official notification. Educational qualification and experience for all Library Cadre posts will be as per UGC guidelines as amended from time to time.

IIT BHU Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for IIT BHU Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application window closes on 10 August 2026 at 5:00 PM. Applications opened on 11 July 2026.
Q2How many vacancies are there in IIT BHU Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 28 vacancies across 16 posts in 7 cadres: Administrative, Technical, Library, System Analyst, IWD, Rajbhasha, and Sports.
Q3What is the application fee for IIT BHU Recruitment 2026?
A
Rs. 500/- (non-refundable) for General, EWS, and OBC candidates. There is no fee for SC, ST, PwD, and Female candidates.
Q4How can I apply for IIT BHU Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted online through the SAMARTH Portal (iitbhunt.samarth.edu.in). A printout of the submitted form, along with supporting documents, must also be sent by Speed Post to the Registrar, IIT (BHU), Varanasi, within 7 days of the last date.
Q5What is the age limit for IIT BHU Recruitment 2026 posts?
A
The upper age limit varies by post, ranging from 40 years (System Analyst) to 57 years (Librarian). Age is calculated as on 10 August 2026.
Q6Is there a post reserved for PwD candidates in IIT BHU Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. One Technical Officer post is earmarked for Persons-with-Disability under Blindness or Low Vision, Hearing Impairment, Locomotor Disability, or Cerebral Palsy.
Q7What is the selection process for IIT BHU Recruitment 2026?
A
The notification does not specify a fixed process for each post. It states that a Screening Test, Written Test, Presentation, Group Discussion, and/or Personal Interview - as applicable - will be scheduled, with details emailed to shortlisted candidates.
Q8What is the correct advertisement number for this recruitment - 01/2026-27 or 02/2026-27?
A
The notification itself is inconsistent: the document header states 02/2026-27, while every page footer states 01/2026-27. Candidates should confirm the correct number on the official IIT BHU recruitment webpage before submitting their application.
