Junior Research Fellow (JRF): Postgraduate degree in Forestry / Environmental Science / Ecology / Botany / GIS / Information Technology or related disciplines with NET (UGC/CSIR/ASRB) / GATE.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies announced by ICFRE?

ICFRE has announced a total of 5 vacancies for the posts of Research Associate-III, Database Manager, and Junior Research Fellow.

Q2. What is the application mode for this recruitment?

The application mode for this recruitment is Walk-in. Candidates need to attend the interview directly.

Q3. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview?

The walk-in interview is scheduled to be held on 03 June 2026.

Q4. What is the maximum age limit for the candidates applying for ICFRE posts?

The maximum age limit for all the posts is 35 years, with age relaxation applicable for reserved categories.

Q5. What is the salary offered for the Junior Research Fellow (JRF) position?

The Junior Research Fellow (JRF) will receive a monthly salary of ₹37,000/- plus HRA @ 20%.

Q6. What are the educational qualifications required for the Research Associate-III posts?

For Research Associate-III (Ecology & Sustainable Land Management), a Ph.D. or M.Sc. with NET and 5 years of experience is required. For Research Associate-III (Web / Knowledge Platform Support), a B.Tech./M.Tech./MCA/M.Sc. in Computer Science/IT with 3-5 years of experience is needed.

Q7. Is there any application fee for the ICFRE recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment.

Q8. What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?

Candidates should carry all original documents, self-attested photocopies, and recent passport-size photographs.
