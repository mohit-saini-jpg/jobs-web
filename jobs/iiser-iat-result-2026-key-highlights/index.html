How to Check IISER IAT Result 2026 Online

Follow these steps to access your scorecard on the official admission portal using your registered login credentials.

  • Go to the official IISER admission website, iiseradmission.in.
  • Click on the candidate login or "IAT Result 2026" link.
  • Enter your User ID and password.
  • Log in to view your scorecard on screen.
  • Download and save the scorecard as a PDF.
  • Take a printout for use during counselling and document verification.

Details Mentioned on the IISER IAT 2026 Scorecard

The scorecard contains all the information candidates need to verify before downloading and printing it for counselling.

  • Candidate's name and roll number
  • User ID
  • IAT 2026 marks (subject-wise and total)
  • All India Rank (AIR)
  • Category rank, where applicable
  • Qualifying status
  • Examination details

How IISER IAT 2026 Score Is Calculated

Understanding the scoring pattern helps candidates interpret their scorecard correctly against the final answer key.

  • Marks are calculated based on the final answer key released after the objection window.
  • Correct answers receive positive marks; incorrect answers attract negative marking as per IAT rules.
  • Unattempted questions are not awarded or deducted any marks.
  • Where applicable, the final merit score uses normalized scores.
  • All India Rank and category rank are assigned based on overall performance.

IISER IAT 2026 Counselling Process: What Happens Next

Once you've downloaded your scorecard, here's the sequence of steps leading to final admission.

  • Register online for counselling between July 2 and July 9, 2026.
  • Fill in your preferred institute and programme choices.
  • Upload the required documents on the portal.
  • Participate in document verification.
  • Receive your seat allotment based on rank, category, and seat availability.
  • Accept the allotted seat within the given window.
  • Pay the admission fee.
  • Report to the allotted institute as per the schedule.

Qualified candidates can seek admission to the following programmes across participating institutes.
