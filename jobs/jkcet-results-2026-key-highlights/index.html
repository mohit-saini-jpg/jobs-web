Jammu and Kashmir Board of Professional Entrance Examinations (JKBOPEE) has officially released the JKCET Result 2026 for the Jammu and Kashmir Common Entrance Test conducted for admission to professional undergraduate courses. The result has been published in the form of a merit list, mentioning candidate names, roll numbers, total marks obtained, and ranks secured for the current academic session 2026.

JKCET Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - jkbopee.gov.in. Students can check and download their JKCET scorecard or merit list by visiting the official portal, clicking on the JKCET result link, and accessing the PDF published by JKBOPEE. Since the result is available in online mode only, candidates are advised to download and save the PDF for admission, document verification, and future reference.

To view their result, candidates must visit jkbopee.gov.in, open the JKCET Result 2026 link, and then check their name, roll number, marks, and rank in the merit list displayed on the screen. After downloading the result, students should carefully verify all details, including their name, roll number, category, and marks, and immediately contact JKBOPEE in case of any discrepancy in the published merit list.

How to Check JKCET Result 2026

Follow the steps given below to check and download your JKCET Result 2026 from the official website in a simple and user-friendly way.

  • Visit the official JKBOPEE website at jkbopee.gov.in using any browser.
  • On the homepage, go to the 'Results' section under the notifications or examination area.
  • Click on the link named 'JKCET Result 2026' or 'Provisional Merit List for JKCET 2026'.
  • The JKCET 2026 result or merit list PDF will open on the screen.
  • Use the search option (Ctrl+F) and enter your name or roll number to find your result in the list.
  • Check your marks, category, and rank mentioned in the JKCET merit list carefully.
  • Download the PDF and save a copy of the JKCET Result 2026 for future counselling and admission formalities.
