Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the UCMS Senior Demonstrator/Senior Resident recruitment?

The last date to apply online for the UCMS Senior Demonstrator/Senior Resident positions is May 30, 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline to avoid any last-minute issues.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for Senior Demonstrator/Senior Resident posts?

There are a total of 59 vacancies for the Senior Demonstrator/Senior Resident posts announced by the University College of Medical Sciences (UCMS) in Delhi for the year 2026.

Q3. What is the minimum qualification required for these posts?

The minimum qualification required is a Postgraduate Degree or Diploma in the concerned specialty. For specific subjects like Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pharmacology, and Microbiology, an MSc (Medical) is required. For Dentistry, an MDS Degree is necessary.

Q4. What is the age limit for the Senior Demonstrator/Senior Resident positions?

The maximum age limit for candidates applying for these positions is 40 years. However, age relaxation is provided for SC, ST, OBC, and PwBD candidates as per the Government of India norms.

Q5. What is the salary structure for the Senior Demonstrator/Senior Resident posts?

The salary varies based on qualification. For PG Degree/Diploma Medical Qualification, it's Pay Matrix Level 11 (Rs.67700-208700/-). For MBBS, it's Level 10 (Rs.56100-177500/-), and for Non-Medical Qualification, it's Level 7 (Rs.44900-142400/-).

Q6. What is the application fee for UR/OBC/EWS candidates?

The application fee for candidates belonging to the UR, OBC, and EWS categories is Rs. 500/-. This fee is to be paid online during the application process.

Q7. What is the application fee for SC/ST/PwBD/Women candidates?

Candidates from SC, ST, PwBD, and Women categories are required to pay a reduced application fee of Rs. 250/-. This fee should also be submitted through the online payment gateway.

Q8. What is the selection process for the Senior Demonstrator/Senior Resident recruitment?

The selection process for these positions involves an online application followed by an interview. Candidates will be shortlisted based on their application and performance in the interview.
