FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the NIT Warangal JRF recruitment?

The last date to submit your application via email for the Junior Research Fellow position at NIT Warangal is 20th June 2026.
Q2What is the educational qualification required for the JRF post?
A
Candidates must possess a B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering/Production/Manufacturing/Aerospace Engineering with an ME/M.Tech in Manufacturing/Computer Integrated/Materials Science or a relevant discipline.
Q3What is the age limit for the Junior Research Fellow position?
A
The maximum age limit for applicants is 30 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per government norms.
Q4What is the salary offered for the JRF role?
A
The selected candidate will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 37,000 per month, plus HRA, provided they have a GATE qualification.
Q5How can I apply for the NIT Warangal JRF recruitment?
A
Applications are to be submitted via email. You need to send your resume and required documents to the official email address specified in the notification.
Q6Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) in the official notification.
Q7What is the selection process for the JRF position?
A
The selection process will involve an interview, which can be conducted either online or offline.
Q8Is this a permanent government job?
A
This is a contractual position. The recruitment is for a Junior Research Fellow role on a contractual basis.
Q9Where is NIT Warangal located?

National Institute of Technology Warangal is located in Warangal, Telangana, with the pincode 506004.
