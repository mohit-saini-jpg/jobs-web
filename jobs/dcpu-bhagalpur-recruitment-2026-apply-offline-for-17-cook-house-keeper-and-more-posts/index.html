Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the DCPU Bhagalpur Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the offline application for the DCPU Bhagalpur Various Contractual Posts is 16th June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well in advance to avoid any last-minute issues.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in the DCPU Bhagalpur Recruitment 2026?

A total of 17 vacancies are available for various contractual posts in the District Child Protection Unit, Bhagalpur. These vacancies are distributed across different positions as detailed in the official notification.

Q3. What is the minimum and maximum age limit for the DCPU Bhagalpur posts?

The minimum age requirement for all posts is 18 years, and the maximum age limit is 42 years as of 01 May 2026. Age relaxation is applicable for certain categories as per Bihar government rules.

Q4. What is the application fee for the DCPU Bhagalpur Recruitment 2026?

There is no application fee for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for the DCPU Bhagalpur Recruitment 2026. Candidates can apply without paying any application charges.

Q5. What is the selection process for these posts?

The selection process for the DCPU Bhagalpur Recruitment 2026 will be based on a Merit List prepared from qualification percentage and higher degree marks, followed by an Interview.

Q6. What is the mode of application for the DCPU Bhagalpur Recruitment 2026?

The application process for the DCPU Bhagalpur Recruitment 2026 is strictly offline. Candidates need to download the application form, fill it, and submit it via post or as specified in the notification.

Q7. What are the educational qualifications required for the posts?

Educational qualifications vary by post. They range from 8th Pass to Matriculation with vocational trade certificates, and 10+2 with diplomas or degrees in elementary education, arts, music, yoga, or sports, depending on the specific role.

Q8. What is the salary offered for the contractual posts?

The salary varies for different posts, ranging from Rs. 7944/- per month for Helper cum Night Watchman and House Keeper (Sweeper) to Rs. 10000/- per month for Educator, Art & Craft cum Music Teacher, and PT Instructor cum Yoga Trainer.
