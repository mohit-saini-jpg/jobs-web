Age Limit

Age is reckoned as on 01 July 2026 . For most posts, candidates must be born between 02 July 1984 and 01 July 2005; for the Sahayak Lekhakar/Kanishtha Sampreekshak (Jal Sansthan) post, candidates must be born between 02 July 1984 and 01 July 2008.

Pay and Allowances

Selected candidates will be placed on the following pay levels as per the 7th Pay Commission-based pay matrix: Lekhakar (Bridgekul): Level-06, Rs. 35,400 – Rs. 1,12,400 Sahayak Lekhakar (most departments): Level-05, Rs. 29,800 – Rs. 94,300 or Rs. 29,200 – Rs. 92,300 (department-wise, as detailed in Vacancy Details) Karyalaya Sahayak-Tritiya (Lekha): Level-04, Rs. 27,200 – Rs. 86,100 Cashier cum Sahayak Lekhakar: Level-04, Rs. 25,500 – Rs. 81,100 Selection will be made through an offline objective-type (multiple choice) competitive written examination carrying 100 marks (100 questions) to be completed in 2 hours.
