Prayas Vidyalaya Class 9th Entrance Exam Admit Card 2026

Under the Mukhyamantri Bal Bhavishya Suraksha Yojana, the admit card for Prayas Residential Schools Class 9th Entrance Exam 2026‑27 has been issued online. The Eklavya/Prayas portal has activated the link to download the Prayas School 9th entrance exam admit card for all registered candidates.

As per the official information, the Prayas Vidyalaya Class 9th entrance exam will be held on 10 May 2026 across various districts of Chhattisgarh. The admit card (pravesh patra) is available from 1 May 2026 onwards and must be downloaded and carried to the examination centre by every student.

How to Download Prayas Vidyalaya Class 9th Admit Card 2026

Students can download the Prayas School 9th entrance exam admit card 2026 by following these steps:

  • Visit the official Prayas/Eklavya portal: https://eklavya.cg.nic.in and open the “Student Admit Card” link (Prayas Admit Card page).
  • On the Prayas Student Admit Card page, select the relevant exam/academic session for Class 9 Entrance Exam 2026‑27 if asked.
  • Enter your Student Code / Application Number and the registered mobile number or father’s mobile number, as instructed on the page.
  • Click on “Sign In / Submit” to log in to your student dashboard.
  • Click the “Admit Card” / “प्रवेश पत्र” option visible on the dashboard to open your Prayas 9th entrance exam hall ticket.
  • Check all details (name, exam date, centre, etc.), then download and print the admit card on A4‑size paper; carry at least one clear copy to the exam centre.

If you have forgotten your student code or application number, use the “Forgot my Student Code or Aavedan No.” option on the same page to retrieve it.

Details Mentioned on Prayas Class 9th Entrance Admit Card 2026

The Prayas Vidyalaya Class 9 admit card 2026 contains important information that students must verify:

  • Student’s name and photograph
  • Father’s and mother’s name
  • Student code / application number
  • Class and exam name: Prayas Residential School Class 9 Entrance Exam 2026‑27
  • Exam date: 10 May 2026
  • Reporting time and exam timing (as per centre instructions)
  • Exam centre name, centre code and full address
  • General and exam‑day instructions for candidates

In case of any discrepancy in name, photograph or other details, students should immediately contact the concerned district/Prayas school or helpline mentioned in the notification.

Exam‑Day Instructions for Prayas Class 9th Entrance Exam 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the Prayas Vidyalaya Class 9 entrance exam, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card so that verification and seating arrangements can be completed; late entry may not be allowed.
  • Carry a printed copy of the Prayas Class 9th Entrance Exam Admit Card 2026, a valid photo ID (such as Aadhaar card, school ID, etc.) and permitted stationery (blue/black pens, pencil, eraser as per instructions).
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, Bluetooth devices, notes, books or any electronic gadgets inside the exam hall; only necessary stationery and documents are allowed.
  • Follow all instructions given by invigilators and those printed on the question paper/OMR sheet; any unfair means may lead to disqualification.
  • Keep your admit card safe even after the exam, as it may be required during result declaration and admission/counselling at Prayas Residential School.
