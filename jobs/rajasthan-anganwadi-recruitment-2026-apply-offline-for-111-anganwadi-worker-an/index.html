Rajasthan Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Women and Child Development (WCD) Department, Barmer district, Rajasthan, has issued Anganwadi Recruitment Notification (Vigyapti Sankhya 06/2026-27) for filling vacant honorary (mandey) posts of Anganwadi Worker and Anganwadi Helper in various Child Development Project (Bal Vikas Pariyojana) offices of Barmer district. Eligibility Criteria for Anganwadi Worker & Helper Recruitment

Only local resident women of the concerned Gram Panchayat (for rural areas) or the concerned ward of the urban Anganwadi centre (for urban areas) are eligible to apply. A woman applying for an Anganwadi centre should be a local resident of the same Gram Panchayat as the revenue village to which that Anganwadi centre belongs. A widowed or divorced woman may be considered a local resident with reference to either her marital (sasural) home or her parental (maayka) home, as applicable. Both married and unmarried women are eligible to apply for the posts of Anganwadi Worker and Anganwadi Helper. If a selected woman marries and, as a result, is no longer a local resident of the concerned Gram Panchayat, her honorary (mandey) service is automatically treated as terminated. Candidates are required to give a self-declaration (affidavit) regarding this at the time of application.

Educational Qualification

The minimum educational qualification for both Anganwadi Worker and Anganwadi Helper posts is Senior Secondary (Class 12th) pass. Marks/weightage for educational qualification will be determined as per the selection format ("Mulyankan Patra-B") revised on 01.10.2024 issued by the Directorate.

Rajasthan Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection will be carried out following the guidelines of the departmental circular dated 12.01.2023 and the amended guidelines dated 01.03.2024 and 01.10.2024, along with other circulars/standards issued by the department from time to time. The selection process is to be conducted through the medium of upcoming Gram Sabhas. Marks for educational qualification of Worker and Helper candidates will be determined as per the Directorate's revised evaluation format ("Mulyankan Patra-B") dated 01.10.2024.
