To be eligible for the UPPSC PCS 2026 exam , candidates must hold a Bachelor's Degree from a recognized university as of the last date for receipt of applications. Candidates wondering about age requirements should note that the general age limit is 21 to 40 years , calculated as on 1 July 2026 . A Bachelor's Degree from any recognized university is the minimum qualification for most posts. Candidates must possess the required qualifications up to the last date for submitting the application. Note: Age relaxation benefits (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/Ex-Servicemen/PH/Sports) are available only to permanent residents (domicile) of Uttar Pradesh . Candidates from other states are not eligible for age relaxation or reservation benefits.

Selection Process – UPPSC PCS Exam Pattern 2026

The UPPSC PCS 2026 selection process consists of three successive stages: Preliminary Examination, Main (Written) Examination, and Viva-Voce (Interview). The final merit is based on the combined score of the Main Examination and Interview marks. For some posts specified in the service rules, selection is based only on the written examination without an interview. Two compulsory papers, each of 200 marks and 2 hours duration. Reservation Policy – UPPSC PCS 2026

Reservation benefits for the UPPSC Upper Subordinate Services Exam 2026 are available exclusively to permanent residents (domicile) of Uttar Pradesh. SC/ST/OBC/EWS candidates from other states are not entitled to reservation or age relaxation. Candidates can claim reservation under only one concession, whichever is more beneficial to them. Reservation for SC/ST/OBC/EWS of UP is as per relevant UP government rules. Horizontal reservation applies to: Dependents of Freedom Fighters (UP), Female candidates (UP), Ex-Servicemen (UP), PH (UP), and Outstanding Sports Persons (UP). For women candidates, the caste certificate issued from the father's side only will be treated as valid. EWS candidates must submit an income and asset certificate for the financial year prior to the year of application. UPPSC PCS Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for UPPSC PCS 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application and pay the examination fee is 27 July 2026. The last date for correction or modification in the submitted application is 3 August 2026.
Q2How many vacancies are available in UPPSC Combined State Services 2026?
A
Approximately 500 vacancies have been announced for the Combined State / Upper Subordinate Services (PCS) Examination 2026. The number may increase or decrease depending on requirements and circumstances.
Q3What is the age limit for UPPSC PCS 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 21 and 40 years of age as on 1 July 2026 (i.e., born between 2 July 1986 and 1 July 2005). Age relaxation of 5 years is available for SC/ST/OBC of UP; 15 years for PH (UP); and additional relaxation for Ex-Servicemen of UP.
Q4What is the application fee for UPPSC PCS 2026?
A
General/EWS/OBC candidates pay Rs. 125/- (Rs. 100 exam fee + Rs. 25 processing fee). SC/ST (UP) and Ex-Servicemen (UP) pay Rs. 65/-. PWD candidates pay only Rs. 25/- (exam fee exempted). Fee is paid online via SBI MOPS.
Q5What is the educational qualification required for UPPSC PCS 2026?
A
A Bachelor's Degree from any recognized university is the minimum general qualification. Some posts have additional specific qualifications such as Post Graduate degrees, B.Ed., LLB, specific Science degrees, or professional qualifications. All qualifications must be held by the last date for submitting applications.
Q6What is the selection process for UPPSC Upper Subordinate Services 2026?
A
Selection is through three stages: Preliminary Examination (Objective, 2 papers of 200 marks each) → Main Examination (Conventional/descriptive, 8 papers totalling 1500 marks) → Viva-Voce / Interview (100 marks). For some posts, selection is based on written examination only without an interview.
Q7How do I apply online for UPPSC PCS 2026?
A
First, register at otr.pariksha.nic.in to get an OTR number (at least 72 hours before applying). Then visit uppsc.up.nic.in, click "Apply" against the advertisement, authenticate with your OTR number, fill in application details, pay the fee via SBI MOPS, and take a printout of the final application.
Q8Is there negative marking in UPPSC PCS Prelims 2026?
A
Yes, there is negative marking in the Preliminary Examination. 1/3 mark (0.33) is deducted for each wrong answer. If more than one answer is marked for a question, it is also treated as a wrong answer with the same penalty. No penalty for unattempted questions.
Q9What is the official website for UPPSC PCS 2026?
A
The official website of UPPSC is https://uppsc.up.nic.in. All notifications, admit cards, results, and application forms are available on this website. Candidates should visit it regularly for updates sent via SMS/email to their registered OTR contact details.
Q10Can candidates from states other than UP apply for UPPSC PCS 2026?
A
Yes, candidates from other states can apply, but they will not be eligible for reservation benefits or age relaxation. These benefits are available exclusively to candidates who are permanent residents (domicile) of Uttar Pradesh. Candidates of other states belonging to SC/ST/OBC/EWS/Ex-Servicemen categories will be treated as general category candidates.
