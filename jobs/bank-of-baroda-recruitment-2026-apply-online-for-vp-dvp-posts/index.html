Nature of Employment: Fixed Term, Not Permanent

Candidates exploring this opportunity should understand that these are not regular permanent bank jobs. Bank of Baroda has clearly defined the engagement structure for these IT positions. Selected candidates will be engaged as an Officer on Fixed Term Employment for an initial period of 3 years from the date of joining. This can be renewed or extended for an additional maximum of 2 years , subject to satisfactory performance and at the Bank's discretion. The engagement automatically ends on expiry unless renewed, and in any case, it remains valid only till the candidate reaches a maximum age of 60 years or the end of the fixed term, whichever comes first. Bank of Baroda Recruitment 2026 : Application Fee Details

Candidates often want to know the cost of applying before starting their application. For this recruitment, Bank of Baroda has kept the process completely free. The application fee is Nil for all categories of candidates applying for these 5 IT positions.

CIBIL Score and Credit History Requirement

Unlike most government job notifications, this Bank of Baroda recruitment includes a specific financial credibility check. This is an important and somewhat unique eligibility condition candidates must be aware of. Candidates must maintain a satisfactory credit history , and a minimum CIBIL score of 680 or above is required at the time of joining. Candidates who have defaulted on loan repayment or credit card dues , or against whom adverse reports exist from CIBIL or other external credit rating agencies, will be deemed ineligible for appointment . The Bank reserves the right to reject candidature at any stage, including after joining, if the credit profile is found unsatisfactory.

Remuneration and Location of Posting

Salary for these roles is not fixed on a standard pay scale, unlike typical government recruitment. This section explains how pay is determined and where selected candidates will be posted. Remuneration will be offered based on the candidate's qualifications, experience, overall suitability, last drawn salary, and market benchmark - there is no fixed pay scale mentioned in the notification. The location of posting is Mumbai or Hyderabad , though selected candidates remain liable for transfer anywhere in India as per the Bank's requirement.

Bank of Baroda Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection for these IT leadership posts goes beyond a standard written exam, with the Bank assessing multiple professional parameters. No fixed exam date or selection timeline has been announced in the notification. The selection process may include an online test, a psychometric test, or any other test the Bank deems suitable, followed by an Interview or multiple interviews . The Bank may shortlist applications based on criteria such as suitability for the role, depth of prior experience, leadership exposure, domain expertise, professional certifications, and exposure to regulatory or compliance frameworks. The minimum qualifying mark for the Personal Interview (PI) is 60% for UR category and 55% for Reserved category candidates.
