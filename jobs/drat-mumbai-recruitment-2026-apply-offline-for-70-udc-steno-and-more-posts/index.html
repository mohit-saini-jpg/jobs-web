Important Instructions

Applications must be routed through Proper Channel only; directly submitted applications will not be entertained. The Debts Recovery Appellate Tribunal, Mumbai and Debts Recovery Tribunals are quasi-judicial bodies set up under the provisions of the Recovery of Debts and Bankruptcy Act, 1993, as amended from time to time. Appointment on deputation is for an initial period of 1 year, extendable up to a maximum of 3 years. No further extension will be allowed in terms of Recruitment Rules 2018 dated 08.06.2018. Officers in the feeder grade who are in direct line of promotion shall not be eligible for consideration for appointment on deputation. A deputationist already at DRAT/DRTs under DRAT Mumbai's jurisdiction shall not be eligible for consideration for absorption or promotion. The period of deputation including any deputation in another ex-cadre post immediately preceding this appointment in the same or another Organisation/Department shall ordinarily not exceed 3 years. The applicant should clearly indicate service particulars, experience, educational qualification, and place of posting for which applied in the prescribed proforma at Annexure-I & II. No action will be taken on advance copy of mail copy. The Parent Department/Office shall issue a certificate to the effect that the official is clear from Vigilance point of view and no disciplinary case is either pending or contemplated against the official (Annexure-II). Applications without this certificate will be rejected. Officials who volunteer for the post shall not be allowed to withdraw their names after forwarding. The Department while forwarding the application must ensure that the officials who volunteer shall not be allowed to withdraw their names later on. The vacancy circular is uploaded on the E-DRT portal and can be accessed through https://drt.gov.in → Notices → Public Notices → DRAT, Mumbai.

FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies for DRAT Mumbai Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 70 posts (indicative) have been announced across 6 designations — Section Officer (3), Private Secretary (7), Assistant (30), Accounts Assistant (6), Stenographer Grade-I (17), and Upper Division Clerk/UDC (7) — at DRAT Mumbai and DRTs under its jurisdiction. The number of vacancies and place of posting may change.
Q2Who is eligible to apply for DRAT Mumbai Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
Officers and officials of Central Government / State Government / Courts / Tribunals who fulfill the eligibility conditions specified for each post are eligible. The maximum age limit is 56 years as on the closing date of receipt of applications (15-06-2026).
Q3What is the nature of appointment and duration of deputation?
A
Selected candidates will be appointed on deputation basis for an initial period of 1 year, which can be extended up to a maximum of 3 years as per Recruitment Rules 2018 dated 08.06.2018. No further extension beyond 3 years will be allowed. Terms of deputation shall be regulated in accordance with DoPT OM as amended from time to time.
Q4Can officers in the feeder grade in direct line of promotion apply?
A
No. Departmental officials in the feeder grade who are in direct line of promotion shall not be eligible for consideration for appointment on deputation. Similarly, a deputationist already in DRAT/DRTs under the jurisdiction of DRAT Mumbai shall not be eligible for consideration for absorption or promotion.
Q5What is the eligibility for the Assistant (Group B) post?
A
Officers must be either (i) holding an analogous post on regular basis in the parent cadre or department, or (ii) having 10 years' service in the grade rendered after appointment thereto on a regular basis in Level 4 in the pay matrix (Rs. 25,500–81,100) or equivalent in the parent cadre or department. The pay scale for this post is Level-6 (Rs. 35,400 – 1,12,400).
Q6What additional eligibility is required for the Accounts Assistant post?
A
In addition to the standard eligibility (analogous post or 10 years in Level 4), candidates for the Accounts Assistant post must have undergone training in cash and accounts work at the Institute of Secretariat Training and Management (ISTM) or an equivalent Institute of the Central Government.
Q7What is the last date to apply for DRAT Mumbai Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for receipt of applications (through Proper Channel) at DRAT Mumbai is 15-06-2026 by 5:00 PM. Applications sent directly (not through proper channel) or received after the closing date/time will not be considered.
Q8What documents must accompany the deputation application?
A
Applications must be in the prescribed proforma at Annexure-I and Annexure-II, with attested photocopies of APARs for the last 5 years (up to 31.03.2026). The Parent Department/Office must also issue a certificate (Annexure-II) confirming the official is clear from Vigilance point of view and no disciplinary case is pending or contemplated. Applications not accompanied by these certificates will be rejected.
Q9Can an official withdraw their application after forwarding through Proper Channel?
A
No. Once the application has been forwarded by the Parent Department through proper channel, officials who volunteer for the post shall not be allowed to withdraw their names later on.
Q10Where can the vacancy circular be accessed online?
A
The vacancy circular is uploaded on the E-DRT portal and can be accessed at https://drt.gov.in → Notices → Public Notices → DRAT, Mumbai. For queries, candidates may contact the DRAT Mumbai office at Tel.: 2082 0916 / 2282 1301 or email: [email protected].
