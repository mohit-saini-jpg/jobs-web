MSC Bank Managers Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Applications must reach the Bank on or before 22-06-2026 by 05:15 PM. Incomplete applications and those received after this deadline will not be entertained under any circumstances. Submission of Maharashtra State Domicile Certificate is compulsory. Applications without it will not be considered. Candidates in Government/Semi-Government/PSU/Financial Institutions/Banks service must inform their employer (Head of Office/Department) in writing about their application and attach a photocopy of the intimation letter with the application, prior to submission. At the time of joining, recommended candidates must produce a proper discharge certificate from their employer. Any external influence/canvassing regarding appointment or transfer shall be treated as a violation of Bank rules and the Bank reserves the right to cancel such candidature. For Specialized Branch Manager: Selected candidates must execute an undertaking to serve the Bank for a minimum of 5 years. Liquidated damages of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Ten Lakh) are payable on voluntary resignation/termination before 5 years. Surety bond of Rs. 1,00,000/- each from two income tax payees is also mandatory. For Manager (Contract): The engagement shall not be construed as employment in the Bank. No PF/Gratuity/Pension provisions are applicable. There is no legal claim to regular absorption during or after the contract period. Officers who are confirmed in the services of MSC Bank and fulfill the eligibility criteria are also eligible to apply for both posts. Appointment of selected candidates is subject to their being declared medically fit as per the Bank's requirements. Candidature is liable to be cancelled at any stage if any information provided is found to be false/incorrect/incomplete. No TA will be paid to candidates called for interview. Corrigendum, if any, will be published only on the Bank's website https://msc.bank.in/career. Any dispute arising from this advertisement shall be subject to the sole jurisdiction of Courts at Mumbai only.

MSC Bank Managers Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in MSC Bank Recruitment 2026-27?
A
There are 36 total vacancies - 30 posts of Specialized Branch Managers (Officer Grade II) under Advertisement No. 01/2026-27 and 06 posts of Managers (Contract Basis) under Advertisement No. 02/2026-27.
Q2What is the last date to apply for MSC Bank Recruitment 2026-27?
A
The application must reach the Bank on or before 22-06-2026 by 05:15 PM. Applications received after this date and time will not be entertained.
Q3What is the eligibility for MSC Bank Specialized Branch Manager post?
A
Candidates must be a graduate with minimum Second Class, must have passed the JAIIB examination, and must have a minimum of 5 years of experience as a Branch Manager at Officer level in a Commercial Bank, District Central Co-operative Bank, or Urban Bank. Maharashtra domicile is compulsory.
Q4What is the eligibility for MSC Bank Manager (Contract) post?
A
Candidates must be a graduate with minimum Second Class, must have passed the CAIIB examination, and must have 10 to 12 years of total banking experience at Senior/Middle Management level in a Commercial Bank, District Central Co-operative Bank, or Urban Bank. Retired candidates or those who have resigned with adequate experience may also apply.
Q5What is the pay for MSC Bank Specialized Branch Manager and Manager (Contract) posts?
A
The minimum pay for Specialized Branch Manager (Officer Grade II) is Rs. 60,000/- per month and for Manager (Contract) is Rs. 83,000/- per month. The Bank's Management reserves the right to decide the actual remuneration based on the candidate's qualification and experience; pay shall not be a constraint for deserving candidates.
Q6Is the MSC Bank Recruitment 2026-27 open to candidates from all states?
A
No. Both advertisements are exclusively for residents of Maharashtra State. Submission of a valid Maharashtra State Domicile Certificate is compulsory along with the application for both posts.
Q7What is the age limit for MSC Bank Recruitment 2026-27?
A
For both posts, the minimum age is 45 years as on 01-05-2026. For Manager (Contract), the maximum age is 62 years as on 01-05-2026. Retired candidates or those who have resigned and have adequate experience may also apply for the Manager (Contract) post.
Q8What is the selection process for MSC Bank Recruitment 2026-27?
A
Applications will be screened for eligibility and shortlisted candidates will be called for a series of interviews (Specialized Branch Manager) or Screening, Shortlisting, and Interview/Discussion (Manager – Contract). Preliminary offer letters will be followed by Background Verification, Police Verification, Medical Examination, and Document Validation before final appointment.
Q9Is the Manager (Contract) post a permanent position at MSC Bank?
A
No. The Manager (Contract) engagement is purely on a contractual basis for 1 year, extendable up to a maximum of 5 years. There is no legal claim to regular absorption either during or after the contract period. PF/Gratuity/Pension provisions are not applicable.
Q10What is the bond condition for the Specialized Branch Manager post?
A
Selected candidates must execute an undertaking on Rs. 500/- stamp paper committing to serve the Bank for a minimum of 5 years from the date of joining. In case of voluntary resignation or termination before completion of 5 years, liquidated damages of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Ten Lakh) are payable to the Bank on demand. Additionally, a surety bond of Rs. 1,00,000/- each from two income tax payees is also required at the time of joining.
