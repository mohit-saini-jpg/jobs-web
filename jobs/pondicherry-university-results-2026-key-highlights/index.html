Pondicherry University has declared the Pondicherry University Result 2026 for a wide range of undergraduate CR scheme semester examinations for the 2025-26 academic session. The latest result list dated 31-03-2026 includes B.Com (Foreign Trade) I-VI semester, B.Com (Co-operative Management) I-VI semester, B.Com (General) I-VI semester, B.Voc Office Administration and Secretarial Assistance I-V semester, B.Sc Aquaculture I-III & V semester, B.Sc Zoology I-V semester, B.Sc Botany I-V semester, B.Ed (Two Year) I-III semester and I-II year, and B.Sc Nursing I-VI semester. Pondicherry University Result 2026 has been released online on the official exam portal - exam.pondiuni.edu.in/results/ - linked from pondiuni.edu.in.

Students can now log in to the examination results portal, locate their course code and title from the results list and then enter their Register/Roll Number and semester details to download their provisional marksheet. After checking their subject wise marks, credits and result status, they should verify personal details and contact the university or college examination section if any discrepancy is found or if revaluation is required.

How to Check Pondicherry University Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your Pondicherry University UG Result 2026 for B.Com, B.Voc, B.Sc, B.Ed or B.Sc Nursing:

  • Go to the official exam results page: https://exam.pondiuni.edu.in/results/.
  • On the results list, locate your course code and title, for example: BFT1 - B.Com (Foreign Trade) I-VI Semester BCM1 - B.Com (Co-operative Management) I-VI Semester BCOM1 - B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce) I-VI Semester BVOS1 - B.Voc (Office Administration and Secretarial Assistance) I-V Semester BSAQ1 - B.Sc Aquaculture I-III, V Semester BSZL2 - B.Sc Zoology I-V Semester; BSBO2 - B.Sc Botany I-V Semester BED1/BED2 - B.Ed (Two Year) I-III Semester / I-II Year BSCN2 - B.Sc Nursing I-VI Semester.
  • BFT1 - B.Com (Foreign Trade) I-VI Semester
  • BCM1 - B.Com (Co-operative Management) I-VI Semester
  • BCOM1 - B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce) I-VI Semester
  • BVOS1 - B.Voc (Office Administration and Secretarial Assistance) I-V Semester
  • BSAQ1 - B.Sc Aquaculture I-III, V Semester
  • BSZL2 - B.Sc Zoology I-V Semester; BSBO2 - B.Sc Botany I-V Semester
  • BED1/BED2 - B.Ed (Two Year) I-III Semester / I-II Year
  • BSCN2 - B.Sc Nursing I-VI Semester.
  • Click on the Result link corresponding to your course entry; this opens the result input page.
  • Enter your Register/Roll Number and select the appropriate Exam Year/Semester if prompted.
  • Click on Submit / Get Result to view your provisional marksheet.
  • Check your personal details, course name, semester, subject wise marks, credits and overall result status, then download or print the marksheet for academic use, revaluation, scholarship and admission processes.
