How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the official CGPSC portal www.psc.cg.gov.in. Manual or postal applications will not be accepted. The online portal will be active from 13-05-2026 (12:00 PM) to 11-06-2026 (11:59 PM).
  • First-time users must complete Candidate Registration (name, father's name, DOB, mobile, email, identity proof, live photo capture via mobile link, signature upload) before applying. Previously registered candidates can log in directly using their existing ID and password.
  • After successful fee payment, candidates will receive an application receipt. Status must show "Submitted" and payment status "Paid" - otherwise the application will be rejected.
  • Documents to be Submitted Before Interview (on being called):
  • Age proof - High School/Matriculation certificate or equivalent
  • All semester/year mark sheets of required educational qualification
  • Degree/Diploma certificate of required qualification
  • Caste certificate (SC/ST/OBC Non-Creamy Layer) - permanent certificate from competent authority
  • OBC Non-Creamy Layer income certificate issued within last 3 years from interview date
  • Local/Original Resident Certificate of Chhattisgarh
  • PwD certificate (if applicable) from competent medical authority
  • Ex-serviceman certificate (if applicable)
  • NOC/No Objection Certificate (if currently employed in Govt./PSU/Bank/University)
  • Sports certificate (if age relaxation claimed under sports quota)
  • Contractual service certificate from competent authority (if age relaxation claimed)
  • Apply Online at: www.psc.cg.gov.in

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?

CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026 is a recruitment test conducted by Chhattisgarh Public Service Commission (CGPSC), Nava Raipur, for 46 Assistant Engineer (Gazetted Second Class) posts in various engineering departments of the Chhattisgarh Government under Advertisement No. 02/2026/Exam.

Q2. How many vacancies are released under CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?

A total of 46 vacancies have been notified for Assistant Engineer posts, including 42 for AE Civil in Public Works Department, 3 for AE Electrical/Mechanical in Public Works Department, and 1 for AE Civil in General Administration Department (Chief Technical Examiner/Vigilance).

Q3. What are the application dates for CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?

The online application window opens on 13 May 2026 at 12:00 PM and closes on 11 June 2026 at 11:59 PM, followed by a free correction window from 12 to 14 June 2026 and a paid correction window from 15 to 17 June 2026.

Q4. What is the age limit for CGPSC Assistant Engineer Recruitment 2026?

The minimum age is 21 years and maximum age is 30 years as on 01 January 2026 for non‑residents of Chhattisgarh, while local residents of Chhattisgarh get relaxation up to 40 years with further relaxations for SC/ST/OBC (non‑creamy), women, PwD, ex‑servicemen and specified categories as per state rules, subject to an overall cap of 45 years.

Q5. What educational qualifications are required for CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?

Candidates must possess a BE/B.Tech degree in Civil, Mechanical or Electrical Engineering from a recognized university or institution; for the AE Civil post in GAD Vigilance, AMIE Part A and B in the relevant engineering discipline, recognized as equivalent to BE by the state government, is also acceptable.

Q6. What is the exam pattern for CGPSC State Engineering Service Exam 2026?

The written exam consists of two papers—Paper I (General Studies) with 150 objective questions for 300 marks and Paper II (Engineering: Civil/Mechanical/Electrical) with 150 objective questions for 300 marks, each of 2 hours 30 minutes duration, followed by an interview/personality test of 75 marks for candidates shortlisted in the written test.
