Overview

Haryana Sarkar launche Saksham Yojana for 2,500 Rs unemployment allowance to youths. Check eligibility criteria, online apply process and benefits.

Expert Analysis

This yojana is a major initiative by Haryana govt to provide financial support to unemployed youths. It's a great opportunity for those struggling to find employment.

Who Should Apply

Ideal candidate for Saksham Yojana Haryana is a resident of Haryana state, aged between 21 to 35 years, and has completed 12th standard or higher education.

Preparation Tips

To apply online for Saksham Yojana Haryana, register on official portal, fill application form with required documents, and submit before deadline.

Salary Insights

Unemployment allowance under Saksham Yojana Haryana is Rs 2,500 per month for 12 months, with a total benefit of Rs 30,000. No other allowances or incentives are included.

Job Profile

As a beneficiary of Saksham Yojana, you will receive unemployment allowance without any job responsibilities. However, career growth prospects are limited as it's a temporary financial assistance.

Selection Strategy

Selection process for Saksham Yojana Haryana involves online registration, document verification, and scrutiny of application form. Make sure to fill form correctly and submit required documents to avoid rejection.
