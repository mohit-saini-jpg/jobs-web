FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for ECHS Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the application for the ECHS recruitment is 16th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the designated address before this date.
Q2How many vacancies are available in ECHS Recruitment 2026?
A
There are a total of 10 vacancies announced for various Medical, Para Medical, and Non-Medical Staff positions under the Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme.
Q3What is the application mode for ECHS Recruitment 2026?
A
The application mode for this recruitment is offline. Candidates need to download the application form and send it to the official address mentioned in the notification.
Q4What is the salary structure for the ECHS posts?
A
The salary ranges from Rs. 21,800.00 to Rs. 1,30,000.00 per month, depending on the post and qualifications. Specific details are available in the official notification.
Q5What are the eligibility criteria for the Medical Specialist post?
A
For the Medical Specialist post, candidates must possess an MD/MS in the specialty concerned or DNB, along with a minimum of 03 years of experience after post-graduation.
Q6Is there any application fee for ECHS Recruitment 2026?
A
No, there is no application fee for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment. Candidates can apply without paying any fee.
Q7What is the selection process for ECHS Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process for the advertised posts will be based on an interview. Candidates will be shortlisted and called for an interview based on their applications.
Q8What is the minimum qualification for the Safaiwala post?
A
The minimum qualification for the Safaiwala post is being literate, along with a minimum of 05 years of work experience in a relevant field.
Q9Where can I download the application form for ECHS Recruitment 2026?
A
The application form can be downloaded from the official website of ECHS, which is echs.gov.in. It is advisable to check the official notification for the exact link or instructions.
Q10What documents are required to be attached with the application form?
A
Candidates are required to attach self-attested copies of necessary documents, including a photograph, signature, ID proof, and relevant educational and experience certificates, as specified in the official notification.
