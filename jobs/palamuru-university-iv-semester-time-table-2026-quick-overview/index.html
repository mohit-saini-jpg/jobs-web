How to Download the Palamuru University Time Table PDF

  • Visit the official Palamuru University website notifications page.
  • Look for the notice titled "PG, MBA, MCA - IV Semester (Regular/Backlog) Theory Examinations, July 2026 Time Table."
  • Click on the PDF link to open or download the file.
  • Save the PDF and cross-check your exam date, batch number, and subject name carefully.

Direct PDF link: Download BA BSc and BCom TimeTable 2026 Here

Important Instructions for Students

  • Reach the examination centre well before the reporting time; the exact reporting time is not mentioned in this notification, so students should check their hall ticket or contact their college for it.
  • Carry your hall ticket and a valid college/university ID card to the exam hall.
  • Cross-verify your batch number, subject name, and exam date against your own regular/backlog paper registration before the exam day.
  • Backlog candidates must confirm their applicable subject name, since some course names differ slightly between regular and backlog batches (for example, in M.Sc. Botany, Chemistry and a few other subjects).
  • The official notification does not mention the examination centre/venue, admit card download link, or reporting time students should refer to their respective college notice board or the university examination branch for this information.

What This Notification Does Not Mention

To keep this article accurate, here is what the official time table PDF does not specify, so students should not assume these details:

  • Exact reporting time before the 2:00 PM exam start
  • Examination centre or venue addresses
  • Hall ticket download link or dates
  • Seating arrangement or invigilation details
  • Practical or viva-voce examination schedule (this time table covers theory papers only)
