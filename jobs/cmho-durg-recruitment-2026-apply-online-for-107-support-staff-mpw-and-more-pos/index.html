CMHO Durg Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Office of the Chief Medical and Health Officer, District Durg (Chhattisgarh) has invited online applications for 107 contractual (samvida) posts under the National Health Mission, including Nursing Officer & Staff Nurse, ANM, MPW, Jr. Secretarial Assistant, Programme Associate, Psychologist and more. CMHO NHM Durg Recruitment 2026: Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must meet the post-specific educational qualification given in the table below. Minimum age: 18 years; Maximum age: 70 years for medical posts and 64 years for managerial posts, as on 01.01.2026. For posts requiring nursing/paramedical registration (Nursing Officer & Staff Nurse, ANM, MPW, Ophthalmic Assistant), a live and permanent registration with the relevant Chhattisgarh Council is mandatory. Reservation and horizontal reservation for women/persons with disabilities will apply as per Chhattisgarh Government rules issued from time to time. Reservation in this recruitment will be subject to the final orders of the Hon'ble Supreme Court, New Delhi, in SLP (C) No. 19668/2022.

CMHO NHM Durg Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

Selection will be based on merit prepared from document verification, examination, computer-based test, and/or clinical skill-based test, depending on the post. Candidates will be called for screening in proportion to the number of advertised vacancies: Where advertised posts are 1 to 10: 10 times the number of vacancies will be screened. Where advertised posts are 11 to 50: 5 times the number of vacancies will be screened. Candidates must secure at least 40% of the total marks fixed for the skill test/exam; those scoring below this will be declared unsuccessful in the skill test and will not be considered in the final merit list. Weightage of marks for the final merit list varies by post: Posts 1–10: 65% weightage of educational qualification marks + experience marks (10/15) + skill/written test marks (20). Posts 11–12: 30% weightage of 12th marks + 35% weightage of technical qualificat
