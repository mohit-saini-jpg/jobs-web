Selection Process

  • Stage Nature Details
  • Stage 1 - CBT Merit-based (200 marks) Two papers of 100 marks each. Negative marking of 1/3 per wrong answer. Qualifying: 40% (General), 35% (SC/ST). Normalization applied if conducted in multiple shifts.
  • Stage 2 - Document Verification Qualifying Candidates shortlisted on merit are called to verify original certificates and documents.
  • Stage 3 - Medical Examination Qualifying Medical fitness certificate from Chief Medical & Health Officer of the district or Medical Jurist.
  • The selection process for RSSB Computer Instructor Recruitment 2026 consists of three stages: Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Document Verification
  • and Medical Examination. Final selection is based on merit in CBT.

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the Rajasthan SSO Portal (sso.rajasthan.gov.in). No offline applications are accepted under any circumstances. The online portal is active from 25-05-2026 to 23-06-2026 (11:59 PM).
  • Steps to Apply Online:
  • Step 1: Visit SSO Portal - Go to sso.rajasthan.gov.in and log in with your SSO ID
  • Step 2: Complete OTR (if not registered) - Complete One Time Registration (OTR) - fill category, disability status, and domicile details correctly
  • Step 3: Pay OTR Fee - Pay the OTR registration fee (Rs. 600 for General/Creamy OBC/MBC, Rs. 400 for Non-Creamy OBC/MBC/EWS/SC/ST/PwBD) through e-Mitra, CSC, Net Banking, ATM Debit Card, or Credit Card
  • Step 4: Access Recruitment Portal - After OTR, log in to SSO and go to Citizen Apps (G2C) → Recruitment Portal
  • Step 5: Click Apply Now - Click Apply Now for Advertisement No. 07/2026
  • Step 6: Fill Application Form - Fill in personal details, educational qualifications, and department preference (Secondary/Sanskrit Education) with priority order
  • Step 7: Upload Documents - Upload required documents: qualification certificates, category certificate, domicile, disability certificate (if applicable), photo, signature, thumb impression
  • Step 8: Verify & Submit - Verify all entries carefully before clicking Final Submit
  • Step 9: Save Application - Download and save the application with the generated Application ID for future reference
  • Documents Required for Upload:
  • Apply Online at: https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in/
