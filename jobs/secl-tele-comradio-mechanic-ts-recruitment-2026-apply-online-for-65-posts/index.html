SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Grade-D Internal Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 65 vacancies (subject to change as per sanctioned manpower budget 2025-26) have been announced for the post of Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Grade-D with 15 reserved for SC, 7 for ST (including backlog), and 43 for UR category.
Q2Who is eligible to apply for SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Grade-D Recruitment 2026?
A
This is an internal departmental selection. Only permanent and regular in-service employees of SECL are eligible to apply. External/direct candidates cannot apply. Employees originally appointed in a higher grade are also NOT eligible. The eligibility cut-off date is 31.05.2026.
Q3What is the last date to apply online for SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic Grade-D Internal Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for eligible employees to submit the online application is 01 July 2026 (11:59 PM). Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date to avoid technical difficulties.
Q4What is the educational qualification required for SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Grade-D post?
A
There are two qualification pathways: (1) Standard X / Matriculate or equivalent for permanent employees with one year of service in Cat. IV/Tech. & Sup. Gr. E and above with aptitude for telecom discipline; OR (2) Standard X / Matriculate or equivalent along with ITI certificate in Electronics/equivalent for any permanent employee of the Company.
Q5What is the selection process for SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic Grade-D Internal Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection will be through a Written Test (MCQ pattern) of 100 marks conducted on OMR sheet. There is NO interview and NO negative marking. The exam consists of Subject Knowledge (60 marks), Mental Ability/Reasoning (20 marks), and General Awareness about CIL/SECL and the State (20 marks).
Q6What are the qualifying marks for the SECL written test 2026?
A
General category candidates must score a minimum of 40% of total marks in the written test. SC/ST category candidates must score a minimum of 30% of total marks to qualify the written test.
Q7Where can I apply online for SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Grade-D Internal Recruitment 2026?
A
Eligible in-service employees of SECL can apply only through the official internal selection portal at https://portals.secl-cil.in/internal/index.php. The link is also available on SECL's official website at https://www.secl-cil.in. No offline applications will be accepted.
Q8Is there negative marking in the SECL Tele-com./Radio Mechanic T&S Grade-D Written Test 2026?
A
No. There is NO negative marking for wrong answers in the written test. The MCQ-based OMR test consists of 100 questions carrying 1 mark each, with a total of 100 marks.
