BPCL Experienced Professionals Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply. All eligibility criteria - age, qualification, and experience - are reckoned as on 01-04-2026. Candidates can apply for only one profile. Candidates are advised to submit only one application; in case of multiple applications, only the latest one shall be considered as final. Percentage of marks is calculated on the basis of total marks obtained in all semesters/years in all subjects. Rounding off of percentage is not allowed. Experience gained only after the date of declaration of result of the qualifying degree will be considered. Short-term trainee experience (summer/winter internships during degree) will not be counted. All qualifications must be from AICTE approved / UGC recognized University / Deemed University. Equivalent percentage for CGPA/OGPA must be indicated and supported by a certificate from the University. OBC candidates belonging to ‘Creamy Layer’ are not entitled for OBC-NCL reservation; they must apply as General. OBC-NCL certificate should clearly indicate non-creamy layer status. Candidates presently employed in Central / State Government, Autonomous bodies, PSUs must produce a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their employer at the time of selection process. All future communications will be through e-mail only. The email ID and mobile number must remain valid for at least one year. BPCL will not be responsible for non-delivery due to invalid email ID. No email confirmation will be sent on successful registration or fee payment. Candidates must take a printout of the payment acknowledgment for future reference. 3rd AC train fare reimbursement is admissible for CBT for outstation SC, ST & PwBD candidates. 2nd AC train fare is admissible for all outstation candidates appearing in the Interview (distance of not less than 30 km). This is not applicable for candidates already in Central / State Government Services / PSUs. Corrigendum / Addendum, if any, will be published on the official BPCL website only - https://www.bharatpetroleum.in/ - candidates must check regularly. BPCL reserves the right to cancel / restrict / enlarge / modify / alter the recruitment process at any stage without notice. Canvassing in any form at any stage will lead to cancellation of candidature. Court of jurisdiction for any dispute will be at Mumbai. All applications are accepted through the online portal only. This activity has NOT been outsourced by BPCL to any agency/individual. Applicants are advised to beware of fraudulent agencies/individuals.
