How to Check JKBOSE Results 2026 Online?

Jammu and Kashmir Board of School Education has made the result download process simple and quick for students. Candidates only need their registration number and date of birth to view results on the official portal. Here is a step-by-step process you can follow:

  • Visit the Official Website - Go to the official Jammu and Kashmir Board of School Education exam portal at jkbose.nic.in.
  • Find the Results Section - Click on the Examination or Results tab on the homepage.
  • Select Your Exam - Choose Class 12 Annual Examination from the list of available results.
  • Enter Your Details - Input your Registration Number and Date of Birth in the required fields.
  • Submit & View - Click the Submit button to view your Jammu and Kashmir Board of School Education result 2026 on screen.
  • Download the Mark Sheet - Click Download or Print to save your result PDF for official use.
  • Verify Your Details - Check that your name, roll number, and marks are correctly reflected on the mark sheet.

JKBOSE 12th Result 2026: Search by Roll Number, Name Wise Online at jkbose.jk.gov.in

How to check by roll number

Follow these steps on the official JK results site:

  • Visit the result portal: jkresults.nic.in.
  • Click on the link for “JKBOSE 12th Result 2026” or “Higher Secondary Part-II (Class 12th) Annual Regular 2026”.
  • Enter your roll number exactly as on the admit card.
  • Click “Submit” / “View Result”.
  • Your marksheet will appear on the screen; download or print it for reference.

The online marksheet is provisional; students must collect the original marksheet later from their respective schools.

How to check name-wise result

Name-wise search is available through partner sites like IndiaResults:

  • Go to IndiaResults JKBOSE page: jammu-kashmir.indiaresults.com/jkbose/mindex.html.
  • Select “JKBOSE 12th Result 2026” (choose your division/zone if asked).
  • Choose the “Name Wise” option.
  • Enter your name (or partial name) and search.
  • Match your entry using father’s name/roll number, then open the result and download it.

Shiksha and other education portals have also confirmed that the 12th result 2026 and the name-wise list/qualified candidates list have been released.

JKBOSE12th Result 2026 Check By Roll Number Online

Check by roll number online

To check JKBOSE 12th result 2026 using your roll number:

  • Visit the official result portal: https://jkresults.nic.in or go directly to the Class 12 result page at jkbose.nic.in/results/jkboseresults.aspx.
  • On the homepage, click on the link “View Result of Higher Secondary Part II (Class 12th) Session Annual Regular 2026”.
  • A login window will open. Enter your Roll Number and Registration Number in the respective fields.
  • Type the captcha code as shown on the screen and click “Submit” / “View Result”.
  • Your JKBOSE 12th marksheet (provisional) will be displayed; download or print it for future use.

You can also follow a similar flow by visiting jkbose.jk.gov.in and then navigating to “Results” → “Class 12th Results”.
