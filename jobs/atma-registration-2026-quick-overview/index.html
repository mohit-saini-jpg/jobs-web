ATMA Registration 2026: Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 16/07/2026

The Association of Indian Management Schools (AIMS) has opened ATMA Registration 2026 for the fourth exam cycle of the year. Candidates aiming for MBA, PGDM and other management programme admissions through the AIMS Test for Management Admissions (ATMA) can register online from 13th July 2026. This article covers the ATMA 2026 important dates, registration fee, eligibility criteria, step-by-step application process, exam pattern, syllabus and other details based on the official notification.

ATMA Registration 2026: Quick Overview

ATMA is a national-level management entrance exam conducted by AIMS multiple times a year for admission into MBA/PGDM and allied programmes at participating institutes. The registration for the exam scheduled on 9th August 2026 began on 13th July 2026 and will continue till 3rd August 2026. The exam will be held in online, centre-based computer test mode.

ATMA 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Candidates should check the eligibility conditions before starting the registration process.

  • There is no upper age limit to apply for the ATMA 2026 exam.
  • Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognised university.
  • Candidates who have completed Chartered Accountancy (CA) are also eligible to apply.
  • Final-year students of a bachelor's degree programme can apply as well.

The exact minimum percentage or class requirement, if any, is not available in the source notification. Candidates are advised to confirm this detail on the official ATMA website before applying.

ATMA Registration 2026 Fee and Concessions

The ATMA registration fee can be paid only through Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, or UPI. Payment through cash, demand draft, or cheque is not accepted.

CategoryFee
General/All CandidatesRs. 2,000/-
Women Candidates25% concession (approx. Rs. 1,500/-)
Candidates from North Eastern States50% concession (approx. Rs. 1,000/-)

The North Eastern States eligible for the 50% fee concession are Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim, and Tripura.

Documents Required for ATMA 2026 Registration

Based on the available notification details, candidates should keep the following ready before starting the registration:

  • A personal mobile number and email ID (these cannot be shared with another applicant or reused for a second registration)
  • Aadhaar number
  • Scanned photograph in the prescribed format
  • Scanned signature in the prescribed format
  • Educational qualification details, including exact percentage/marks of SSC, HSC and degree examinations (candidates who are still appearing can mention "NA")
  • Details of post-graduation or professional degree and post-qualification experience, if applicable

Specific document formats such as file size or dimension requirements for the photograph and signature are not available in the source notification. Candidates should check the official application portal for these technical specifications.

How to Register for ATMA 2026?

ATMA registration is completed entirely online in a few stages. Candidates should note that some sources describe the sequence slightly differently, so it is advisable to follow the on-screen instructions on the official portal at the time of applying.

  • Payment of Fees: Begin the process by paying the Rs. 2,000/- registration fee (or the applicable concessional fee) using your personal mobile number and email ID.
  • Generation of PID: After successful payment, a system-generated Personal Identification Number (PID) is sent via SMS and email. This PID acts as the login ID for reprinting the application form, downloading the admit card, and checking the result. If not received, candidates can retrieve the PID or password from the official website.
  • Filling the Registration Form: Log in using the PID and complete the form with your name, postal address, email ID, date of birth, mobile number, Aadhaar number, preferred test city, educational qualification details, and choice of institutes for reporting ATMA scores. Upload the scanned photograph and signature to complete registration. A login password is then sent via SMS and email.
  • Printing the Admit Card: Once registration is complete, download and print the admit card when it becomes available.

ATMA Admit Card 2026

The ATMA 2026 admit card for the 9th August exam cycle will be available for download from 5th August 2026 (5:00 PM onwards) using the PID and password generated at the time of registration. Candidates can also reprint the filled-in application form until 7th August 2026.

ATMA Result 2026

The result for the 9th August 2026 ATMA exam is scheduled to be declared on 12th August 2026 on the official website. Admission authorities of participating institutes are advised to independently validate ATMA scores before considering candidates for admission.

Important Instructions for ATMA Registration 2026

  • Use only your own personal mobile number, email ID and Aadhaar number for registration these details cannot be shared with another candidate or reused for a second registration.
  • The registration fee is non-refundable and must be paid through Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, or UPI only.
  • Change of test centre is generally not permitted, except in exceptional cases with a valid reason. Such requests must be made at least 10 days before the test date and are subject to seat availability.
  • Keep the PID safe, as it is required for reprinting the application form, downloading the admit card, and checking the result.
