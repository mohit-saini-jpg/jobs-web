Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for CDAC Recruitment 2026?

The walk-in interviews are scheduled for 23rd May 2026 and 30th May 2026. Candidates should ensure they reach the venue on one of these dates within the registration timings.

Q2. What are the posts available in CDAC Recruitment 2026?

CDAC is recruiting for the positions of Project Manager and Senior Project Engineer. These are contract-based roles within the organization.

Q3. What is the minimum qualification required for these posts?

The minimum qualification includes a BE/B-Tech or equivalent degree. Higher qualifications like Post Graduate degrees, M.E./M.Tech, or Ph.D. in relevant disciplines are also accepted.

Q4. What is the age limit for Project Manager and Senior Project Engineer?

The maximum age limit for Project Manager is 56 years, and for Senior Project Engineer, it is 40 years. Age relaxation is applicable as per government norms.

Q5. What is the salary offered for these positions?

For Project Manager, the minimum salary is 17.52 lakh per annum. For Senior Project Engineer, the minimum salary is ₹10.11 lakhs per annum, both based on experience and C-DAC norms.

Q6. How can candidates apply for the CDAC Recruitment 2026?

The application process is through a walk-in interview. Candidates need to download the application form, fill it, and report to the venue with all required documents on the specified dates.

Q7. What documents are required for the walk-in interview?

Candidates must carry original and self-attested photocopies of documents supporting age, educational qualifications, experience, and caste (if applicable), along with a filled application form and passport-size photographs.

Q8. Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?

The walk-in interview will be conducted in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, at the Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC) facility. The exact address is provided in the official notification.
