FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the Delhi University Project Staff recruitment?
A
The recruitment is through a walk-in interview. Candidates must attend the interview on 09 June 2026. There is no separate application deadline.
Q2What are the total number of vacancies announced by Delhi University?
A
Delhi University has announced a total of 5 vacancies for the Project Staff positions, including Research Assistant and Field Investigator roles.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for the Research Assistant post?
A
For the Research Assistant position, candidates must possess a Ph.D./M.Phil./Post graduate degree in a social science discipline with a minimum of 55% marks and NET/M.Phil/Ph.D qualification.
Q4What is the salary offered for the Field Investigator post?
A
The selected candidates for the Field Investigator post will receive a monthly salary of Rs. 20,000/-p.m.
Q5Where will the walk-in interview for Delhi University Project Staff be held?
A
The walk-in interview will be conducted at the Department of Political Science, University of Delhi, North Campus, Delhi - 110007.
Q6Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
As per the information available, there is no application fee mentioned for this recruitment drive.
Q7What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents, self-attested photocopies of relevant documents, and recent passport-size photographs to the interview venue.
Q8What is the selection process for the Project Staff positions?
A
The selection will be based on the performance of the candidates in the walk-in interview. No separate call letters will be sent.
Q9Can I send an advance copy of my CV?
A
Yes, an advance copy of your CV may be sent to the Principal Investigator before the interview.
Q10What is the job type for these positions?
A
These are project-based positions, meaning they are temporary and tied to the duration of the specific research project.
