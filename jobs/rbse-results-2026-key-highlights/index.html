Steps to Check RBSE 12th Supplementary Result 2026

  • Visit the official website rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in
  • Click on the “Results” or “Supplementary Result 2026” link.
  • Select the Class 12 Supplementary Result option.
  • Enter your Roll Number and other required details.
  • Submit the information and view your result.
  • Download and print the marksheet for future reference.
