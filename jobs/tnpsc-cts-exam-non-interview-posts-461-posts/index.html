How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official TNPSC website: www.tnpscexams.in.
  • 2Complete One Time Registration (OTR) if not already registered, then log in to fill the online application.
  • 3Select the subject paper(s) for which you are eligible and specify them clearly in the application.
  • 4Upload scanned copies of all required documents including photo, signature, educational certificates, eye fitness certificate (where applicable), and Tamil knowledge proof.
  • 5Pay the examination fee of ₹100/- per subject paper online via Net Banking / Credit Card / Debit Card / UPI.
