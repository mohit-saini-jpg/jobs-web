Maharaja Krishnakumarsinhji Bhavnagar University (MKBU) has officially released the MKBU Result 2026 for various undergraduate courses including B.A, B.Sc, and B.Com under CBCS and NEP systems. The results have been announced for Semesters 3, 4, and 6 on 15 April 2026.MKBU Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - mkbhavuni.edu.in. Students can check and download their marksheets by entering valid login credentials such as their Roll Number or Seat Number. After downloading the provisional result, candidates are advised to carefully verify their details and contact the university in case of any discrepancies.

How to Check MKBU Result 2026 Online

Follow the steps below to check and download your MKBU Exam Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official website - mkbhavuni.edu.in.
  • Click on the ‘Exam Result’ tab available on the homepage.
  • Select your course name and semester from the list of released results.
  • Click on the relevant result link to open the PDF or login page.
  • Enter your seat number or roll number (if required).
  • Download and save the MKBU Result 2026 PDF for future reference.
