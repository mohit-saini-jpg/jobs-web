Qualification / Eligibility

Technical Officer (TO-1): B.E./ B.Tech. or equivalent in Electronics/ Instrumentation/ Electronics and Communication Engineering & Electronics Engineering with minimum 55% marks. OR M. Sc. or equivalent in Physics/ Electronics/ Applied Electronics with minimum 55% marks. Technical Officer (TO-2): B.E./B.Tech./ M.Sc or equivalent in Computer Science with minimum 55% marks. OR MCA* with minimum 55% marks. Technician (1) (TECH-1): Secondary School Certificate (SSC)/ 10th Standard or equivalent with Science subjects, with minimum 55% marks and ITI certificate or National/ State trade certificate in Electronics Mechanic. OR 2 years full time experience as an apprentice trainee in Electronics Mechanic. OR 3 years of work experience in Electronics Mechanic. Technician (1) (TECH-2): Secondary School Certificate (SSC)/ 10th Standard or equivalent with Science subjects, with minimum 55% marks and ITI certificate or National/ State trade certificate in Electrician/Wiremen/Electrical Fitter. OR 2 years full time experience as an apprentice trainee in Electrician/Wiremen/Electrical Fitter. OR 3 years of work experience in Electrician/Wiremen/Electrical Fitter. Technician (1) (TECH-3): Secondary School Certificate (SSC)/ 10th Standard or equivalent with Science subjects, with minimum 55% marks and ITI certificate or National/ State trade certificate in Mason (Building Constructor). OR 2 years full time experience as an apprentice trainee in Mason (Building Constructor). OR 3 years of work experience in Mason (Building Constructor). Technician (1) (TECH-4): Secondary School Certificate (SSC)/ 10th Standard or equivalent with Science subjects, with minimum 55% marks and ITI certificate or National/ State trade certificate in Draughtsman (Civil). OR 2 years full time experience as an apprentice trainee in Draughtsman (Civil). OR 3 years of work experience in Draughtsman (Civil).
