The salary for the IT Technical Engineer position at TMC ranges from Rs. 30,000/- to Rs. 40,000/- per month, depending on qualifications and experience.

Candidates need a B.E./B.Tech in Computer/Electronics/IT with 1 year of experience, or a Diploma in Computers/Electronics/IT with 2 years of experience, or a B.Sc in Computer Science/IT with a hardware/networking certificate and 2 years of experience.

Q7. Where is the walk-in interview for the IT Technical Engineer post going to be held?

The walk-in interview will be held at the TMC office in Mumbai, Maharashtra. The specific address and reporting time will be detailed in the official notification.
