TG TET Hall Ticket 2026 – Key Details : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 09/06/2026

The Department of School Education, Government of Telangana, has officially released the Telangana TG TET Hall Ticket 2026 today, 09 June 2026, for candidates appearing in the June Session Teacher Eligibility Test. Registered candidates can now download their hall tickets from the official TG TET portal using their Journal Number and Date of Birth.

The TG TET June 2026 examination is scheduled to be conducted from 15 June to 30 June 2026. Candidates must download their admit card before the examination date and verify all details carefully to avoid any issues on exam day.

How to Download TG TET Hall Ticket 2026

  • Visit the official TG TET hall ticket portal.
  • Click on the "Download Hall Ticket" link.
  • Enter your Journal Number and Date of Birth.
  • Click on the Proceed button.
  • The TG TET Hall Ticket 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download and print the admit card.
  • Verify all details before the examination.

Details Mentioned on the Hall Ticket

The TG TET Hall Ticket 2026 generally contains:

  • Candidate's Name
  • Roll Number
  • Application Number
  • Photograph and Signature
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Category
  • Paper Applied For (Paper I / Paper II)
  • Examination Date
  • Examination Time
  • Reporting Time
  • Examination Centre Name and Address
  • Important Exam Instructions

Candidates should immediately report any discrepancy to the examination authorities.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates should carry:

  • Printed Copy of TG TET Hall Ticket 2026
  • Valid Photo Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence, etc.)
  • Passport-Size Photograph (if required)
  • Any additional document specified by the authorities

Candidates without the required documents may not be permitted to appear for the examination.

Exam-Day Instructions

  • Reach the examination centre at least 60 minutes before the reporting time.
  • Carry the printed hall ticket and valid photo ID proof.
  • Follow the reporting schedule mentioned on the admit card.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, Bluetooth devices, or any electronic gadgets.
  • Follow all instructions issued by the invigilators.
  • Cooperate during security verification and biometric checks.
  • Keep the hall ticket safely until the completion of the recruitment process.
