Qualification / Eligibility

Assistant Manager (Electrical): Degree in Electrical or Electronics or Electrical & Electronics or Instrumentation or Electrical & Electronics & Communication Engineering from a recognized University. Minimum of three-year Diploma in Electrical or Electronics or Mechatronics or Shipbuilding Engineering from a State Board of Technical Education. Experience: i) For degree holders: Minimum of Three years post qualification experience. ii) For diploma holders: Minimum of Five years post qualification experience. Experience in Electrical Outfitting/Caballing/ Commissioning/ Installation in a Shipbuilding or Ship repair or Offshore Fabrication Company or Petrochemical Company or Marine related Engineering Company or Marine related Government / Semi Government Company / Establishment. Assistant Manager (Mechanical): Degree in Mechanical or Marine or Naval Architecture Engineering from a recognized University. Minimum of three-year Diploma in Mechanical or Mechatronics or Marine or Shipbuilding Engineering from a State Board of Technical Education. Experience: i) For degree holders: Minimum of Three years post qualification experience. ii) For diploma holders: Minimum of Five years post qualification experience. Experience in Machinery/ Installation & Commissioning/ Hull Fabrication Piping/Procurement /Inventory/Supply chain Management in a Shipbuilding or Ship repair or Offshore Fabrication Company or Petrochemical Company or Marine related Engineering Company or Marine related Government / Semi Government Company / Establishment.
