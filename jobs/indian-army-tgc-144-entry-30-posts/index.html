FAQs

Q1How many vacancies in Indian Army TGC-144 Recruitment 2026?
A
Indian Army has announced 30 tentative vacancies across 6 engineering streams for TGC-144 course commencing January 2027 at IMA Dehradun.
Q2What is the last date to apply for TGC-144 2026?
A
Online applications close on 11 June 2026 (3:00 PM) through joinindianarmy.nic.in portal.
Q3What is the eligibility for Indian Army TGC-144 Recruitment?
A
BE/B.Tech or M.Sc Computer Science in notified streams from AICTE/UGC recognized universities. Final year students (result by 1 Jan 2027) also eligible.
Q4What is the age limit for TGC-144 2026?
A
20 to 27 years as on 1 January 2027 (born between 1 Jan 2000 and 1 Jan 2007). Unmarried males only.
Q5Which engineering streams eligible for TGC-144?
A
Civil (8 vacancies) Computer Science (6 vacancies) Electrical (2 vacancies) Electronics (6 vacancies) Mechanical (6 vacancies) Miscellaneous (2 vacancies)
Q6What is the selection process for Indian Army TGC-144?
A
Shortlisting (stream-wise cut-off on engineering marks) → 5-day SSB Interview → Medical Examination → Merit List (SSB marks only).
Q7Is there application fee for TGC-144 Recruitment 2026?
A
No application fee required. Free online registration using Aadhaar and Matriculation certificate.
Q8What is the salary after TGC-144 training completion?
A
Lieutenant salary: ₹56,100 - ₹1,77,500 (Level 10) with ₹17-18 LPA CTC. Training stipend: ₹56,100/month at IMA.
