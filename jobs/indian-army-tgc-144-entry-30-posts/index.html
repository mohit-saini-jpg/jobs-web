How to Apply

  • 1One copy of the printout of application duly signed and affixed with self-attested photograph
  • 2Matriculation/Secondary School Examination certificate and marksheet (proof of Date of Birth, candidate’s name and Parent’s name)

  • 4Engineering Degree/Provisional Degree issued by the recognised university (Provisional Degree not more than 6 months old at time of joining)
  • 5Marksheets of all semesters/years
  • 6Certificate from Controller of Examination/Registrar regarding CGPA/Grades to marks conversion formula
  • 7Certificate from Controller of Exam/Registrar/Dean/Principal regarding cumulative percentage of marks upto 6th semester/3rd year (for final year appearing candidates)
  • 8Certificate from Principal/Head of Institution stating candidate is in final year and result will be declared by 01 Jan 2027 (for final year appearing candidates)
  • 9Declaration by final year candidate regarding submission of proof of passing by 01 Jan 2027
  • 10Documents related to civil verification
  • 11Only unmarried male Engineering Graduates who are citizens of India, subjects of Nepal, or persons of Indian origin from specified countries are eligible to apply.
  • 12Any variation between the nomenclature of Engineering stream as given on the degree parchment/marksheet and that submitted in the online application will result in cancellation of candidature.
  • 13Any variation in personal particulars (candidate’s name & parents’ name) in educational documents requires submission of an affidavit stating the variations.
  • 14Candidate must carry ORIGINAL Degree/Provisional Degree and all semester/year marksheets for SSB.
  • 15No hard copy applications will be accepted – online submission only.
  • 16Candidates must not marry until they complete full training; violation will lead to discharge and recovery of training cost.
  • 17Candidate should not have been debarred from appearing in any examination by UPSC.
  • 18Candidate should not have been arrested/convicted by a criminal court or involved in any court case.
  • 19Candidates withdrawn from NDA, IMA, OTA, Naval Academy, Air Force Academy or any Service Training Academy on disciplinary grounds are not eligible to apply.
  • 20No compensation will be paid in respect of any injury sustained during SSB tests.
  • 21Once the SSB date has been selected, no request for change of SSB Interview Date/Centre will be entertained or replied to.
  • 22Candidates appearing for SSB interview for the first time are entitled to AC III Tier to-and-fro railway fare or bus fare including reservation-cum-sleeper charges within Indian limits.
  • 23Candidates are advised to visit https://www.joinindianarmy.nic.in regularly for updates. Queries will be replied only through the ‘Feedback/Queries’ option on the website. Queries received up to 03 days prior to closing of online application will only be entertained/replied.
