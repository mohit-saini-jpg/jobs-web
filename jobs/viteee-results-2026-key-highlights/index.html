Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) has released the VITEEE Result 2026 for the VIT Engineering Entrance Examination held for B.Tech admissions across its campuses. As per the latest update, the VITEEE 2026 result and rank card link has been activated online for candidates who appeared in the exam conducted from April 28 to May 3, 2026.

VITEEE Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - vit.ac.in - and through the dedicated result link at ugresults.vit.ac.in/viteee2026/. Candidates can log in using their application number and password (and registered email ID, if required) to download their VITEEE 2026 scorecard and rank details.

After downloading the VITEEE rank card, students should carefully verify their name, application number, rank, percentile score and other details mentioned on the result. In case of any discrepancy in the VITEEE Result 2026, candidates must immediately contact VIT authorities through the official helpline or email before the counselling process begins.

VITEEE Results 2026 Key Highlights

VITEEE 2026 Results have been declared for candidates seeking admission to B.Tech programmes at VIT Vellore, Chennai, AP and Bhopal campuses for the 2026-27 academic session. The result has been released in online mode only, in the form of a normalised rank card based on percentile scores, and will be used for shortlisting candidates for phase-wise online counselling. Students can access the VITEEE 2026 result link through the official website vit.ac.in and the direct result portal ugresults.vit.ac.in/viteee2026/ using their login credentials.

How to Check VITEEE Result 2026

Candidates can follow the step-by-step process below to check and download their VITEEE Result 2026 online.

  • Visit the official VIT website at https://vit.ac.in/ or directly open the VITEEE result portal at https://ugresults.vit.ac.in/viteee2026/.
  • Click on the link that says "VITEEE 2026 Result" or similar result/rank card link on the homepage.
  • On the result login page, enter your VITEEE 2026 application number and password (and registered email ID if asked).
  • Enter the captcha/security code as displayed on the screen, if applicable.
  • Click on the "Submit" or "Login" button to proceed.
  • Your VITEEE Result 2026, including your normalised score and rank, will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download the VITEEE rank card and take a printout for future reference, counselling registration and document verification.
