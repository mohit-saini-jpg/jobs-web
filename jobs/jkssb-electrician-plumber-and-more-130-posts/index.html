  • 1Live photograph as required during online form filling.

  • 4Matriculation/10th certificate as proof of date of birth and name.
  • 5Degree/Diploma/ITI certificate and marksheets for educational qualification proof (self-attested, max size 100 KB in PDF format).
  • 6Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/RBA/EWS/ALC/IB) in prescribed format valid as on cut-off date (max size 100 KB in PDF format), if applicable.
  • 7PwBD certificate, if applicable.
  • 8Ex-Servicemen certificate in Form XIV, if applicable.
  • 9Any other supporting document for claims made in the application.
  • 10Online applications are open from 22-06-2026 to 21-07-2026.
  • 11Applications once submitted cannot be modified or withdrawn under any circumstances.
  • 12All eligibility conditions, including domicile and category certificate validity, will be determined as on 21-07-2026.
  • 13Candidates must write their Name, Date of Birth, Father’s Name and Mother’s Name strictly as per their Matriculation Certificate.

  • 15Use of mobile phones, smart watches, AI-enabled devices or any electronic gadgets is strictly prohibited at the examination venue.
  • 16Candidates must carry two recent passport size colour photographs and original valid Photo-ID to the examination hall.
  • 17The Board’s decision on all matters relating to eligibility, selection, and results shall be final and binding.
  • 18Canvassing in any form or submission of fake/fabricated applications will lead to debarment and legal action.
  • 19In-service candidates must route their application through the concerned Head of Department before the last date of Document Verification.
