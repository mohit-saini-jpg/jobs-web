Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationB.E./B.Tech. or equivalent in relevant discipline, Bachelor's degree in law, B.E./B.Tech. in Computer Science/IT/Electronics or MCA.
DetailsGeneral Manager (Mechanical): B.E./B.Tech. or equivalent qualification in Mechanical Engineering from a recognized University or Institution. Extensive working knowledge within Railway Mechanical Engineering Department at Workshop/ Division levels, with specific expertise in Carriage & Wagon (C&W) Maintenance & operations. General Manager (Safety): B.E. / B.Tech. or equivalent qualification in relevant discipline or relevant Graduation degree for officers working in Operating department in IR / other PSUs of IR from a recognized University or Institution. Minimum 03 years of experience in Safety Department in Indian Railways (IR) or DFCCIL or Other PUs / PSUs of Indian Railways OR should be currently working in Safety Department in IR or DFCCIL or Other PUs / PSUs of Indian Railways. Sound understanding of railway safety rules, procedures, and best practices, along with proven administrative capability. General Manager (Law): Full time regular bachelor's degree in law with minimum 50% marks from recognized University/Institute in India. Experience in handling Arbitrations (including commercial arbitration) & Conciliation matters, Industrial/Labour/Commercial Law matters and other dispute resolution mechanisms. Drafting & Vetting various Legal or contractual documents, providing legal opinions, handling legal matters pertaining to Land Acquisition, Contractual disputes, Service and Regulatory matters etc. Handling litigation before various judicial and quasi-judicial forums. Deputy General Manager (IT): B.E. / B. Tech in Computer Science / Information Technology / Electronics Engineering OR M.C.A. (Master of Computer Applications) OR equivalent from a recognized University/Institution. Proven in-depth experience in one or more core IT functions: Networking (LAN/WAN) design/management, Application Development (Software Development Lifecycle), or Cloud/Hybrid Hosting management. Experience in handling public procurement procedures.

How To Apply

  • Candidates who wish to apply for DFCCIL Various Posts Recruitment 2026 through offline mode should follow the steps below.
  • Read the official notification carefully before applying.
  • Download the application form from the official website: www.dfccil.com.
  • Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
  • Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
  • Check all details carefully before submission.
  • Submit applications through proper channel requesting parent organization to forward application along with NOC, D&AR and Vigilance Clearance and APARs for the preceding 03 years.
  • Envelope containing the application should be superscribed "Application for the post of on Immediate Absorption basis”.
  • One envelope should contain only one application.
  • Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 01-06-2026.
  • Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
