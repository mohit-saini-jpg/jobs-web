Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is DPCC Trainee Recruitment 2026?

Delhi Pollution Control Committee (DPCC), Department of Environment, Govt. of NCT of Delhi, has released notification F. No. DPCC/3(9)(412)/Admn-2026/ for engagement of Technical and Scientific Trainees on a contractual basis with a monthly stipend.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in DPCC Trainee Recruitment 2026?

There are 150 Trainee vacancies in total, including both Technical and Scientific Trainees, with category-wise reservation as per GNCTD/DoPT rules.

Q3. What is the last date to apply for DPCC Trainee Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the online application is 21 days from the date of advertisement (inclusive of the date of publication) uploaded on the DPCC website; for this recruitment, the advertisement date is 17-05-2026 as per DPCC.

Q4. What is the ratio of Technical to Scientific Trainee vacancies in DPCC?

The tentative ratio of Technical Trainees (B.E./B.Tech) to Scientific Trainees (M.Sc.) is 10:01, and the number of posts may be increased or decreased as per requirement.

Q5. What are the educational qualifications for DPCC Technical Trainee?

Technical Trainees must have a full-time regular B.E./B.Tech degree with First Division in 2023 or later in disciplines such as Civil, Environmental, Chemical, Electrical, Mechanical, ECE, Biomedical, Water Resources, Remote Sensing, GIS, Hydrology, or equivalent.

Q6. What are the educational qualifications for DPCC Scientific Trainee?

Scientific Trainees must have a full-time regular M.Sc. degree with First Division in 2023 or later in Biochemistry, Microbiology, Life Science, Chemistry, Environmental Chemistry, Analytical Chemistry, Toxicology, or equivalent disciplines.

Q7. Is GATE/NET compulsory for DPCC Trainee Recruitment 2026?

GATE/NET is not compulsory but candidates having valid GATE/NET scores of 2023, 2024, 2025, or 2026 will be given preference, and the primary merit list is prepared based on these scores.

Q8. What is the stipend for DPCC Trainee posts?

Selected Trainees will receive a monthly stipend of Rs. 25,000/- in the 1st year, Rs. 26,500/- in the 2nd year, and Rs. 28,000/- in the 3rd year of engagement.
