Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationMBBS/BDS/BVSc, Master's Degree, PhD, Post Graduate Degree, Bachelors in Life Sciences
DetailsClinical Project Manager: MBBS/BDS/BVSc with minimum 5 years experience in clinical project management OR Master's Degree/PG Diploma/PhD in Life Sciences/Biomedical Sciences/Pharmacy/Public Health/Clinical Research with at least 5 years experience in clinical project management AND experience in clinical trial or public health project management in recognised organisation. Clinical Research Associate: Bachelors in Life Sciences with minimum 3 years relevant clinical trial monitoring experience OR Master's degree/PG Diploma in Life Sciences/Pharmacy/Public Health/Healthcare with minimum 2 years relevant clinical trial monitoring experience; MBBS/BDS/BHMS/BAMS/BPT preferred. Quality Manager: Post Graduate Degree including integrated PG degrees; valid GCP certificate; 2 years experience as Clinical Research Associate/Coordinator/Site Manager. Sr. Project Associate: Post graduate degree in Life Sciences with minimum 2 years experience in clinical research OR Professional degree in MBBS/BDS/BVSc/BAMS/BHMS/BUMS/BSMS/BNYS/B.Sc. Nursing/BPT/B.Pharm with minimum 1 year experience in clinical research OR PhD in Life Sciences with experience in clinical research; GCP certification mandatory.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for THSTI recruitment?
A
The walk-in interviews are scheduled for 10th June 2026 for Clinical Project Manager and Quality Manager, and 11th June 2026 for Clinical Research Associate and Sr. Project Associate.
Q2What is the total number of vacancies announced by THSTI?
A
THSTI has announced a total of 4 vacancies for Project Positions.
Q3What are the minimum qualifications required for the Sr. Project Associate post?
A
For Sr. Project Associate, a postgraduate degree in Life Sciences with minimum 2 years of experience or a professional degree with 1 year of experience or PhD in Life Sciences is required.
Q4Is there any application fee for THSTI recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q5What is the age limit for the Project Positions at THSTI?
A
The age limit for the Project Positions is up to 45 years, with relaxations applicable for reserved categories.
Q6What is the salary offered for the Clinical Project Manager position?
A
The Clinical Project Manager position offers a salary of Rs. 78,000/- per month plus HRA.
Q7What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates should carry all original documents, self-attested photocopies, ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates, and category certificates (if applicable).
Q8Where is the walk-in interview venue?
A
The walk-in interview venue is Translational Health Science and Technology Institute (THSTI), Faridabad, Haryana, 121001.
Q9What is the selection process for these project positions?
A
The selection process will include a Walk-in Interview followed by Document Verification.
Q10Is experience required for the Clinical Research Associate role?
A
Yes, a Bachelor's in Life Sciences with 3 years of clinical trial monitoring experience or a Master's degree with 2 years of experience is required.
