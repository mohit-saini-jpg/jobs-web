AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026 - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 10/07/2026

The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi is releasing the AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026 today, July 10, 2026, on the official website aiimsexams.ac.in. The result is for candidates who appeared in the AIIMS Paramedical entrance exam held on July 4, 2026, for admission to BSc Paramedical courses at AIIMS institutes across India. The result will be published as a downloadable PDF containing roll numbers, category, and overall rank of qualified candidates.

When will AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026 be declared?

AIIMS has officially confirmed that the AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026 will be declared on July 10, 2026. An exact release time has not been announced by the authority.

How To Check AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026?

Candidates can download the AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026 PDF by following these steps.

  • Visit the official website: aiimsexams.ac.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the "Results & Announcements" section.
  • Click on the "AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026" link.
  • The result PDF will open on the screen.
  • Press Ctrl+F and enter your roll number to locate your result.
  • Download and save the PDF for future reference.

Is login required to check the AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026?

No. The AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026 merit/rank PDF does not require login credentials candidates only need their roll number. However, downloading the individual scorecard does require logging in with a candidate ID or registered mobile number and password.

Details Mentioned in AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026 PDF

The result PDF lists the following details for each qualified candidate.

  • Roll Number
  • Category
  • PwBD Status
  • Percentage/Percentile
  • Overall Rank

AIIMS Paramedical Scorecard 2026

Along with the result PDF, AIIMS will release individual scorecards accessible through the candidate login portal. Candidates must enter their candidate ID or registered mobile number and password to download the scorecard, which is required during the counselling process.

How long is the AIIMS Paramedical scorecard valid?

The AIIMS Paramedical scorecard is valid for one academic year only and cannot be used for admission in a subsequent year. AIIMS does not allow re-evaluation or rechecking of answer sheets; the result, once declared, is final.

How Are Ties Resolved in AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026?

If two or more candidates in the same category secure equal marks, AIIMS resolves the tie using the following order of preference.

  • Higher marks in Biology/Mathematics
  • Higher marks in Chemistry
  • Higher marks in Physics
  • Older candidate is given preference if the tie still persists

AIIMS Paramedical Eligibility Criteria

Candidates should note the following eligibility conditions applicable for AIIMS Paramedical admissions.

  • Minimum age: 17 years as of December 31, 2026.
  • Must have passed Class 12 (or equivalent) with English, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as main subjects from a recognized board.
  • Candidates applying for Bachelor of Optometry and Bachelor of Medical Radiology and Imaging Technology (BMRIT) must have Class 12 with English, Physics, Chemistry, and either Biology or Mathematics.
  • For all other AIIMS Paramedical courses, Biology is a compulsory subject at Class 12 level.

What After AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026? Selection & Counselling Process

Candidates whose roll numbers appear in the AIIMS Paramedical 2026 result PDF are eligible to participate in the counselling and seat allotment process, which broadly follows these steps.

  • Choice Filling: Candidates select and lock their preferred course and AIIMS institute.
  • Seat Allotment: Seats are allotted based on the candidate's AIIMS Paramedical rank.
  • Response to Allotment: Candidates must indicate "Accept allotted seat and do not wish to participate further" or "Accept allotted seat and want upgradation," as applicable.
  • Document Verification and Reporting: Allotted candidates report to the respective AIIMS institute with original documents for verification and admission.

Documents Required for AIIMS Paramedical Counselling

  • AIIMS Paramedical Admit Card 2026
  • AIIMS Paramedical Result/Scorecard 2026
  • Class 10 and Class 12 mark sheets and certificates
  • Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS), if applicable
  • PwBD certificate, if applicable
  • Valid photo ID proof
  • Recent passport-size photographs

Important Instructions for AIIMS Paramedical Result 2026

  • The result will be available only in online PDF format; AIIMS will not issue hard copies.
  • Candidates should keep their roll number handy to quickly locate their result in the PDF.
  • Re-evaluation or rechecking of AIIMS Paramedical answer sheets is not permitted.
  • Candidates should download and safely retain both the result PDF and the individual scorecard, as the scorecard is required at every stage of counselling.
  • Any discrepancy in the result or scorecard should be reported directly to AIIMS authorities for clarification.
