APMSRB Recruitment 2026 - 529 Vacancies for Staff Nurse : Eligibility & Qualification

Details
  1. All qualifications must be held as of the date of notification - 10 June 2026. Candidates should verify registration renewals are current before applying
  2. Staff Nurse Candidates must hold a B.Sc. Nursing or General Nursing & Midwifery (GNM) degree and must be registered with the Andhra Pradesh Nursing & Midwifery Council (APNMC) with an up-to-date renewal
  3. Pharmacist Candidates must hold a D.Pharma, B.Pharma, or an Intermediate Vocational Course in Pharmacy recognized by the Government of Andhra Pradesh, and must be registered with the AP Pharmacy Council with an up-to-date renewal
  4. Multi Rehabilitation Worker Candidates must have completed 10+2 or equivalent, a one-and-a-half-year certificate course in Multi Rehabilitation Work from a recognized university, and must be registered as Rehabilitation Personnel under the RCI Act, 1992
  5. The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 42 years, calculated as on 01 January 2026 (as per G.O.Ms.No.36, GA (Ser-A) Dept., Dt. 04/03/2025). No candidate below 18 or above 42 years (before relaxation) is eligible
  6. Only Andhra Pradesh state candidates are eligible to apply. Candidates who studied from Class 4 to Class 10 in AP are considered local. Candidates who migrated from Telangana to AP within 3 years from 2 June 2014 are also eligible as local candidates (as per G.O.Ms.No.132 & 133, GAD, 13.06.2016)
