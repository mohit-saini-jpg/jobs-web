Short Information

Nagaland University has released the official Non-Teaching Posts Notification 2026 at nagalanduniversity.ac.in. Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationMaster's Degree, Bachelor's Degree, Diploma, 10+2, HSLC
DetailsFinance Officer: Master's Degree with at least 55% marks or equivalent + 15 years experience as Assistant Professor (Level 11+) or 8 years (Level 12+) or comparable experience or 15 years administrative experience including 8 years as Deputy Registrar. Internal Audit Officer: All India Organised Audit and Accounts Services Officer with 3 years experience OR qualified CA/ICWA or SAS equivalent with analogous post and 8 years service OR 8 years service as Assistant Finance Officer/Account Officer with audit/accounts experience. Deputy Registrar: Master's degree with at least 55% marks + Nine years experience as Assistant Professor (Level 10+) or comparable or 5 years as Assistant Registrar. Desirable: Experience in establishment/finance/exams. Assistant Librarian: Master's degree in Library Science/Information Science/Documentation with 55% marks + NET qualified or Ph.D. + experience in library computerization. Nursing Officer: B.Sc. (Nursing) or B.Sc. (Hons) Nursing + registered with Nursing Council + 2 years experience in Govt. Hospital. Junior Engineer (Civil): Three years Diploma in Civil Engineering. Desirable: B.E. Civil + AutoCAD knowledge. Senior Technical Assistant (Agricultural Extension Education): Master's Degree in relevant subject + at least two years experience in relevant field. Senior Technical Assistant (Livestock Production & Management): Master's Degree in relevant subject + at least two years experience in relevant field. Senior Technical Assistant (Botany): Master's Degree in relevant subject + at least two years experience in relevant field. Senior Technical Assistant (Horticulture): Master's Degree in relevant subject + at least two years experience in relevant field. Fieldman: 10+2 (Science) + three years experience in relevant field. Work Assistant (Civil): HSLC + relevant ITI certificate + knowledge/experience in civil construction. Desirable: 3 years experience. Bus/Truck Conductor: HSLC. Desirable: Valid driving license for LMV/HMV/Passenger/ITI in Auto Electrician/Mechanic.
