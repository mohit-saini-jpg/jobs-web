Chaudhary Charan Singh University has officially declared the CCSU Result 2026 for the December 2025 examination session covering M.Com and M.Sc Agriculture postgraduate courses. How To Check CCSU Result 2026 Online

Follow the step-by-step process given below to check CCSU Result 2026 for M.Com and M.Sc Agriculture semesters online:

  • Visit the official CCSU result portal at https://result.ccsuniversityweb.in/.
  • On the homepage, select the appropriate result category for university examination results (such as Regular or Professional/P.G., depending on the layout).
  • Choose your course and semester, for example M.Com (NONEP) Sem-I, M.Com (NONEP) Sem-III or M.Sc(Ag) Sem-III for the relevant specialization.
  • Enter your CCSU exam Roll Number and any other details asked on the result page.
  • Click on the Submit or Proceed button to generate your CCSU Result 2026 on the screen.
  • Check your name, course, semester, subject-wise marks, total marks and result status, then download or print the provisional CCSU marksheet for future reference and verification.
