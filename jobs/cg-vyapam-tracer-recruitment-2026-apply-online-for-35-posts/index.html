How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply online only through the CG Vyapam official website https://vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in. Candidates applying for the first time must complete Profile Registration (Profail Panjikaran) on the Vyapam portal - once registered, personal details need not be re-entered for future exams. Detailed instructions on how to fill the form are available on the Vyapam website under "Instructions to fill the FORM" link. Candidates are advised to read the departmental recruitment rules carefully before applying.

  • Steps to Apply Online:

  • Complete Profile Registration on https://vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in (if not already registered) - scan and upload a light-background passport-size photograph in .jpg/.jpeg format (50 KB–100 KB) and specimen signature in .jpg/.jpeg format (50 KB–100 KB); filename must be alphanumeric without spaces or dots.

  • Verify all details in Profile Registration before proceeding.

  • Select the WRDT26 examination and fill in the Online Application Form.

  • Pay the examination fee online - note the payment details and ensure the bank account details (for refund) are entered correctly.

  • Click "SUBMIT" after successful fee payment to complete the application.

  • Download and print the submitted application form and keep it safe.

  • Admit Card (Pravesh Patra) will be available for download approximately 7 days before the exam (tentatively 27 July 2026) from the Vyapam website - candidates must download it from their profile. Admit card will NOT be sent by post.

  • Carry full printout of the admit card to the exam centre along with one original photo ID proof (Voter ID/Driving License/PAN Card/Aadhaar Card with photo) and Blue/Black Ball Point Pen.

  • Documents Required at Examination/Document Verification/Joining:

  • Admit Card (downloaded from Vyapam website) with photo ID proof (Voter ID/Driving License/PAN Card/Aadhaar Card) - original must be presented; entry denied without original photo ID

  • High School/Higher Secondary exam pass certificate (attested photocopy - self-attested or certified by Gazetted Officer) as proof of date of birth

  • Higher Secondary (Class XII) certificate and mark sheet showing Drawing (Rekha Chitra) as a subject

  • Chhattisgarh domicile/local resident certificate (Mool Niwasi Praman Patra) - original, issued by competent authority

  • Permanent caste/category certificate (Sthayi Jati Praman Patra) issued by authorized government officer - required for SC/ST/OBC candidates

  • Permanent PwBD disability certificate (minimum 40% disability) issued by competent Medical Authority and UDID card - for PwBD candidates

  • No Objection Certificate (Annapati Praman) from employer - for candidates working in government/semi-government organizations

  • Ex-Servicemen discharge certificate/relevant documents - for Ex-SM candidates

  • Green Card (for Family Welfare Programme beneficiaries claiming age relaxation)

  • Award certificate (Shaheed Rajiv Pandey/Gundadhur/Maharaja Pravirachandra Bhanjdev Samman/Rashtriya Yuva Puraskar) - if claiming age relaxation under these categories

  • Inter-caste marriage certificate (for anti-untouchability scheme awardees claiming age relaxation)
