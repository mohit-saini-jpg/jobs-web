How to Apply

  • 1Candidates shall apply online only through the CG Vyapam official website https://vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in. Candidates applying for the first time must complete Profile Registration (Profail Panjikaran) on the Vyapam portal - once registered, personal details need not be re-entered for future exams. Detailed instructions on how to fill the form are available on the Vyapam website under "Instructions to fill the FORM" link. Candidates are advised to read the departmental recruitment rules carefully before applying.
  • 2Steps to Apply Online:
  • 3Complete Profile Registration on https://vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in (if not already registered) - scan and upload a light-background passport-size photograph in .jpg/.jpeg format (50 KB–100 KB) and specimen signature in .jpg/.jpeg format (50 KB–100 KB); filename must be alphanumeric without spaces or dots.
  • 4Verify all details in Profile Registration before proceeding.
  • 5Select the WRDT26 examination and fill in the Online Application Form.
  • 6Pay the examination fee online - note the payment details and ensure the bank account details (for refund) are entered correctly.
  • 7Click "SUBMIT" after successful fee payment to complete the application.
  • 8Download and print the submitted application form and keep it safe.
  • 9Admit Card (Pravesh Patra) will be available for download approximately 7 days before the exam (tentatively 27 July 2026) from the Vyapam website - candidates must download it from their profile. Admit card will NOT be sent by post.
  • 10Carry full printout of the admit card to the exam centre along with one original photo ID proof (Voter ID/Driving License/PAN Card/Aadhaar Card with photo) and Blue/Black Ball Point Pen.
  • 11Documents Required at Examination/Document Verification/Joining:
  • 12Admit Card (downloaded from Vyapam website) with photo ID proof (Voter ID/Driving License/PAN Card/Aadhaar Card) - original must be presented; entry denied without original photo ID
  • 13High School/Higher Secondary exam pass certificate (attested photocopy - self-attested or certified by Gazetted Officer) as proof of date of birth
  • 14Higher Secondary (Class XII) certificate and mark sheet showing Drawing (Rekha Chitra) as a subject
  • 15Chhattisgarh domicile/local resident certificate (Mool Niwasi Praman Patra) - original, issued by competent authority
  • 16Permanent caste/category certificate (Sthayi Jati Praman Patra) issued by authorized government officer - required for SC/ST/OBC candidates
  • 17Permanent PwBD disability certificate (minimum 40% disability) issued by competent Medical Authority and UDID card - for PwBD candidates
  • 18No Objection Certificate (Annapati Praman) from employer - for candidates working in government/semi-government organizations
  • 19Ex-Servicemen discharge certificate/relevant documents - for Ex-SM candidates
  • 20Green Card (for Family Welfare Programme beneficiaries claiming age relaxation)
  • 21Award certificate (Shaheed Rajiv Pandey/Gundadhur/Maharaja Pravirachandra Bhanjdev Samman/Rashtriya Yuva Puraskar) - if claiming age relaxation under these categories
  • 22Inter-caste marriage certificate (for anti-untouchability scheme awardees claiming age relaxation)
  • 23Apply Online at: https://vyapamprofile.cgstate.gov.in/online/

Important Instructions

This appointment is purely temporary. As per terms of appointment, services can be terminated at any time. Appointees will be posted at any district in Chhattisgarh State as per vacancies. Merely filling the Online Application Form or appearing in the examination does not confer any right of appointment on the candidate. Eligibility is verified at document verification stage; ineligibility found at any stage will lead to cancellation of candidature. The responsibility of verifying eligibility before applying rests entirely with the candidate. CG Vyapam is not responsible for any information provided by candidates; the full responsibility lies with the candidate. At the time of joining, selected candidates must produce original copies of all certificates. Police verification and medical fitness certificate from District Hospital Medical Board are mandatory before appointment. Women reservation calculation follows CG Government circular (F 13-2/2014/Aap.Pr./1-3 dated 14.07.2017) - only whole numbers are used, not decimals. CG Vyapam's jurisdiction is limited to conducting the examination and declaring results. Document verification, selection and appointment processes are conducted by the department, not CG Vyapam. Exam result will be declared on the CG Vyapam website https://vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in. OMR evaluation is by machine; no re-evaluation is available. Objection to any question/answer must be raised online on the Vyapam website by logging in with profile ID and date of birth, within the specified time after model answer key release. Fee: Rs. 50/- per question online. Objections by post/email/in-person are not accepted. Final answer key, once published with results, is non-challengeable. Candidates must carry only the Admit Card, original photo ID and Blue/Black Ball Point Pen inside the examination hall. All other items including mobile phones, smartwatches, electronic devices, earrings, belts, wallets, scarves and caps are strictly prohibited. Candidates wearing religious or cultural attire must report to the exam centre in advance and will be subjected to additional security checks before being permitted to enter. All disputes relating to eligibility/ineligibility of any candidate are subject to final decision by the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Chhattisgarh. Legal jurisdiction lies within Chhattisgarh.
