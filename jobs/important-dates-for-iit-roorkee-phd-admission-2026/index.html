Eligibility Criteria for IIT Roorkee PhD 2026

Candidates must satisfy the Common Minimum Eligibility Criteria (CMEC) and the department-specific Minimum Educational Qualification (MEQ).

Common Minimum Eligibility Criteria (CMEC):

  • Master’s degree holders: Minimum CGPA 6.0 (60%) for GEN/GEN-EWS/OBC and 5.5 (55%) for SC/ST/PwD.
  • Must have qualified a national level test (GATE, CEED, JEST, UGC-NET, CSIR-NET, or equivalent) or hold a national fellowship (in most cases).
  • Exemptions:
  • B.Tech/M.Sc with CGPA ≥8.0 from MoE-funded institutions (CFTIs).
  • Bachelor’s/Master’s from IITs with CGPA ≥8.0.
  • 4-year Bachelor’s with CGPA ≥7.5 + national test (non-CFTI).
  • MBBS/BAMS/BHMS/BDS with CGPA 6.0.
  • MS/MD degrees (exempt from CGPA and test).

Department-wise MEQ details (Engineering, Sciences, Architecture, Management, Humanities, Nanotechnology, Data Science, etc.) are given in the official Information Brochure.

Departments Offering PhD at IIT Roorkee

IIT Roorkee offers PhD programmes on both Roorkee and Saharanpur campuses across a wide range of disciplines, including:

Architecture & Planning, Biosciences and Bioengineering, Civil Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Chemistry, Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical & Industrial Engineering, Metallurgical & Materials Engineering, Physics, Mathematics, Humanities & Social Sciences, Management Studies, Hydrology, Hydro & Renewable Energy, Water Resources Development & Management, Centre for Nanotechnology, Centre for Disaster Mitigation & Management, Mehta Family School of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, and many more interdisciplinary centres.

For the complete list and specific eligibility, refer to the official brochure.

Selection Process for IIT Roorkee PhD Admission

Admission is purely merit-based. Shortlisted candidates will be called for a written test and/or interview conducted by the respective Department/Centre/School. Departments will intimate the dates directly to shortlisted applicants and upload them on their portals. Final selection depends on academic record, test/interview performance, and availability of seats/supervisors.

How to Apply for IIT Roorkee PhD Admission 2026?

  • Visit the official IIT Roorkee admission portal.
  • Read the Ph.D. Information Brochure (Autumn 2026) carefully.
  • Register and fill the online application form.
  • Upload required documents (photo, signature, marksheets, category certificate, GATE/NET scorecard if applicable, etc.).
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Submit the form and note the application number for future reference.

Apply Online Link: Available on the official IIT Roorkee website.

Application Fee:

  • ₹500 per Department/Centre/School for GEN/GEN-EWS/OBC-NCL candidates.
  • ₹250 per Department/Centre/School for SC/ST/PwD candidates.

Fee is non-refundable and must be paid online.

Categories of PhD Programmes

  • Full-Time: Institute Assistantship Research Scholars (IARS), Externally Funded (EFRS), Project-funded, Foreign Nationals.
  • Part-Time: In-Service Internal (P-ISIRS), In-Service External (P-ISERS / ISERS).
  • Special Schemes: Golden Girl Scheme and SAKUNTALA Fellowship for meritorious female candidates (direct interview, fellowship benefits).
