Bhadohi UP Anganwadi Helper Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Jila Karyakram Adhikari (District Programme Officer), Bhadohi, has released a notification dated 20 July 2026 inviting online applications for the post of Anganwadi Sahayika (Helper) on a honorarium/contract basis. Bhadohi Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Only female candidates are eligible to apply for these posts. The candidate must be a resident of the same Gram Sabha (for rural areas) or the same Ward (for urban areas) for which she is applying. The entire selection process is governed by Government Order No. 1/1092245/2025/3318/58-1-2025 (1917687) dated 17.09.2025, which can be obtained from the official UP Government website. As per the government order, if one member of a family is already working as an Anganwadi Karyakatri (Worker) at a particular Anganwadi Centre, another member of the same family is not eligible for selection as Sahayika at the same centre.

Bhadohi Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 - Educational Qualification

The minimum educational qualification for the post of Anganwadi Sahayika is Intermediate (12th) or its equivalent. The maximum qualification considered is up to Post Graduate level. Merit will be prepared based on the percentage of marks obtained in the qualifying examinations, and note that High School (10th) marks will not be included while preparing the merit list.

Bhadohi Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

Selection for the post of Anganwadi Sahayika under direct recruitment follows a defined order of priority among eligible resident candidates of the same gram sabha/ward: First priority: A widow woman residing in the same gram sabha/ward (urban areas), living below the poverty line (BPL). If a widow is not available, then a BPL abandoned woman or a legally divorced woman whose maintenance case has been accepted by a competent court will be considered. If neither is available, other women living below the poverty line will be considered. Second priority: If no widow, divorced, or abandoned BPL candidate is available under the first priority, candidates living above the poverty line will be considered for selection in the order mentioned in the government order. Merit among eligible candidates is prepared based on the percentage of marks obtained from Intermediate up to Post Graduate qualif
