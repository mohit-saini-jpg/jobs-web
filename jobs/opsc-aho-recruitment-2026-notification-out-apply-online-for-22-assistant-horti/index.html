OPSC Assistant Horticulture Officer Selection Process

Selection will be made through a competitive examination consisting of a Written Examination and a Personality Test (Interview). Both papers are Objective Type Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ) with Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) evaluation. Each correct answer carries 01 mark. Negative marking applies: 25% of the marks awarded for a correct answer will be deducted for each wrong response. Unattempted questions carry zero marks. Questions will be of Bachelor's Degree standard. The Interview carries 25 marks. Candidates who obtain the minimum qualifying marks fixed by the Commission for the Written Examination will be shortlisted for the interview, in the ratio of about 1:2 of advertised vacancies per category, subject to a minimum of 5 candidates. The Select List will be prepared based on the aggregate marks secured in the Written Examination and Interview, out of a total of 225 marks. In
