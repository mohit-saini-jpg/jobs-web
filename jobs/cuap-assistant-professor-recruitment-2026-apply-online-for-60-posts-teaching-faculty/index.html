Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the CUAP Assistant Professor recruitment?

The last date to apply online for the Assistant Professor positions at Central University of Andhra Pradesh is 07 June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for Assistant Professor posts?

There are a total of 60 vacancies for the Assistant Professor positions across various departments at the Central University of Andhra Pradesh.

Q3. What is the minimum educational qualification required?

Candidates must possess a Master's degree with at least 55% marks in the relevant subject, along with NET/SLET/SET or a Ph.D. as per UGC Regulations.

Q4. What is the application fee for General category candidates?

The application fee for General and OBC category candidates is Rs. 1,000/-. SC/ST and Women candidates are exempted from paying the application fee.

Q5. What is the salary offered for the Assistant Professor positions?

The selected candidates will receive a salary ranging from Rs. 50,000/- to Rs. 75,000/- per month, or Rs. 1,500/- per lecture.

Q6. What is the mode of application for this recruitment?

Applications for the CUAP Assistant Professor recruitment must be submitted online through the official website of the university.

Q7. What is the selection process for these posts?

The selection process for the Assistant Professor positions will primarily involve an interview.

Q8. Is this a contractual or permanent job?

The Assistant Professor positions advertised by Central University of Andhra Pradesh are on a contractual basis.
