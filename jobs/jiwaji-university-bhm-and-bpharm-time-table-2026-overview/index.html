Jiwaji University BHM and BPharm Time Table 2026 : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 13/07/2026

Jiwaji University, Gwalior has issued two separate exam notifications on 10 July 2026 for the June 2026 examination session under the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS). One covers the B.Pharma VIIIth Semester revised time table for SOS Regular, Ex, and ATKT students, and the other covers the B.H.M. & C.T. VIth Semester (Old Course) time table for ATKT-only students. Both notifications are issued by the Exam Controller, Jiwaji University, and are available on the university's official Information Corner page.

This article covers only the two notifications listed below. Jiwaji University regularly publishes time tables for many other courses and semesters, so if you are looking for a program not listed here, check the official Time Table link at the end of this article.
