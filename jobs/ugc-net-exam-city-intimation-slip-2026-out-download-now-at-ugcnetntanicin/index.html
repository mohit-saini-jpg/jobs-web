What Is the UGC NET City Intimation Slip 2026?

The UGC NET City Intimation Slip, also called the Advance City Intimation, is an official document released by NTA a few days before the admit card. It tells candidates the city not the specific venue where their exam will be conducted. This gives registered candidates enough lead time to make travel and lodging arrangements, especially those appearing at a centre outside their home city.

The city slip is not a hall ticket and cannot be used as an entry pass at the exam centre. Candidates must download the official UGC NET Admit Card 2026 (expected on June 15, 2026) separately for actual entry into the examination hall.

How to Download UGC NET Exam City Intimation Slip 2026

Downloading the city slip is a straightforward process. Follow these steps carefully to access your allotted exam city without any difficulty.

  • Visit the official NTA UGC NET portal at ugcnet.nta.nic.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the link for "Advance City Intimation of UGC NET June 2026".
  • Enter your Application Number and Date of Birth in the login fields.
  • Click on Submit to proceed.
  • Your UGC NET City Intimation Slip 2026 will be displayed on the screen.
  • Verify all details and click Download or Print to save a copy for your records.

What to Do After Checking the UGC NET City Intimation Slip?

Once you have downloaded the UGC NET 2026 City Slip and confirmed your exam city, here is what you should do next to be fully prepared.

  • Book travel and accommodation early if your allotted city is different from your home city, as demand near exam centres increases quickly.
  • Keep checking the official portal for the release of the UGC NET Admit Card 2026, expected on June 15, 2026. The hall ticket will carry your exact exam centre address, exam date, and shift timing.
  • Review the exam pattern and syllabus for your chosen subject. UGC NET is conducted as an online CBT with Paper 1 (General) and Paper 2 (Subject-specific).
  • Carry valid photo ID on the day of the exam along with the printed admit card. Documents accepted include Aadhaar card, PAN card, driving licence, or passport.
