SBTE Results 2026 for Semesters

The SBTE Bihar Result 2026 has been released for multiple semesters (I, II, III & V). Students can check their marks, percentage, and division using their roll number on the official portal.

Steps to Check SBTE 2026 Result

Bihar SBTE students can check their semester results online at the board’s official website. Follow the steps mentioned below:

  • Visit the official website of the board – sbte.bihar.gov.in
  • Select the ‘Student’ section and click on the ‘Exam Result’ option available there.
  • Fill in the required information (Roll Number) and click on ‘Get Result’.
  • Check the results and download the PDF for future reference.

SBTE Result 2026 Re-Evaluation Process

Any student who is dissatisfied with their SBTE results 2026 can apply for re-evaluation. In re-evaluation, the examiner re-checks the answer sheet and awards new marks if required. The re-evaluation window usually opens within 15–20 days after the result declaration. Students need to pay the SBTE re-evaluation fee online. Check the official notice on sbte.bihar.gov.in for exact dates and procedure.
