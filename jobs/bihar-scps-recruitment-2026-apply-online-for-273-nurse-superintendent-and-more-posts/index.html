Selection Process

  • Selection will be based on merit. For each post

  • 10 times the number of candidates per vacancy will be called for interview (post wise

  • category wise 10 times the number of candidates will be called for interview against one post).

  • Final merit list will be prepared on the basis of 100 marks.

  • For posts where minimum educational qualification is Graduation

  • marks will be counted on the basis of स्नातक प्रतिष्ठा (Hons.) total marks. For Para Medical and ANM/Nurse posts

  • marks will be calculated on the basis of Nursing/Paramedical/ANM/B.Sc. Nursing degree marks. One registration can be used to apply for multiple posts.

How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply online only. No other mode of application is accepted. Online applications can be submitted at https://scpsvacancy.bihar.gov.in/. One registration can be used to apply for multiple posts. Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date.

  • For detailed eligibility, terms and conditions, visit: https://state.bihar.gov.in/socialwelfare

  • Apply Online at: https://scpsvacancy.bihar.gov.in/

Important Instructions

  • Online applications will be received from 11.05.2026 and will be accepted till 11:59 PM on 31.05.2026. All posts in Children Home (Girls) are reserved for female candidates. All Nurse posts (Special Unit) are reserved for female candidates. Age is calculated as on 01.01.2026. Workers currently deployed in homes through the District Child Protection Unit and treated as government-deployed are not required to apply separately for the post they are currently working in. Workers currently deployed through NGOs (स्वयंसेवी संस्था) are NOT treated as government-deployed; their posts are considered vacant and included in the vacancy count. For each post, 10 times the number of vacancies (padvaar, kotivaar) will be called for interview. Final merit list will be on the basis of 100 marks. For posts with minimum Graduation qualification, merit marks will be on the basis of स्नातक प्रतिष्ठा (Hons.) marks. If no Hons. degree, pass course marks will be used. For Para Medical Staff and ANM/Nurse posts, merit marks will be calculated on Nursing/Paramedical/ANM/B.Sc. Nursing degree marks. One registration can be used to apply for multiple posts. All rights pertaining to the advertisement and terms are reserved with the State Child Protection Society. SCPS reserves the right to amend/cancel the advertisement. If any earlier advertisement has been published by the District Child Protection Unit for the above posts, it stands cancelled by this advertisement. Bihar Government's reservation policy as notified from time to time will apply to this recruitment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for SCPS Bihar Mission Vatsalya Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the online application form is 31 May 2026 up to 11:59 PM. Candidates should complete the registration process before the deadline.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in SCPS Bihar Recruitment 2026?

The State Child Protection Society (SCPS), Bihar has announced 273 contractual vacancies under the Mission Vatsalya Yojana for various posts.

Q3. Which posts are available under SCPS Bihar Mission Vatsalya Recruitment 2026?

The recruitment includes posts such as Superintendent, Accountant-cum-Storekeeper, Probation Officer, Child Welfare Officer, Counsellor, Housefather/Housemother, Paramedical Staff, Nurse, and Special Educator.

Q4. What is the application mode for SCPS Bihar Recruitment 2026?

Candidates can apply online only through the official recruitment portal. No offline or postal applications will be accepted.

Q5. What is the age limit for SCPS Bihar Mission Vatsalya Recruitment 2026?

The minimum age is 18 years for Intermediate-level posts and 21 years for Graduate-level posts. The maximum age ranges from 37 to 42 years, depending on the candidate's category.

Q6. What educational qualifications are required for SCPS Bihar Recruitment 2026?

The required qualifications vary by post and include Intermediate, Graduation, Post Graduation, B.Ed. in Special Education, ANM, B.Sc Nursing, and other relevant professional qualifications.

Q7. What is the selection process for SCPS Bihar Mission Vatsalya Recruitment 2026?

Selection will be based on a merit list and interview process. Candidates will be shortlisted category-wise and post-wise, and eligible candidates will be called for interviews.

Q8. Can a candidate apply for more than one post in SCPS Bihar Recruitment 2026?

Yes. Candidates can use a single registration to apply for multiple posts, provided they meet the eligibility criteria for each post.
