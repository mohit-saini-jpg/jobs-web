Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for ABV IIITM Non-Faculty Positions?

The last date to apply for the Non-Faculty Positions at ABV IIITM Gwalior is 01-06-2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this date through the specified offline mode.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for Non-Faculty Positions at ABV IIITM?

There are a total of 2 vacancies for Non-Faculty Positions at ABV IIITM Gwalior. These vacancies are for the posts of Assistant Engineer (Electrical) and Junior Engineer (Civil).

Q3. What is the minimum qualification required for Assistant Engineer (Electrical)?

For the Assistant Engineer (Electrical) post, candidates need a 1st Class Diploma in Electrical Engineering with 08 years of experience or a 1st Class B.E./B.Tech. in Electrical Engineering with 06 years of experience.

Q4. What is the age limit for the Junior Engineer (Civil) post?

The maximum age limit for the Junior Engineer (Civil) post is 32 years. Age relaxation is applicable for SC candidates as per the Government of India norms.

Q5. What is the application fee for General category candidates?

The application fee for General category candidates is Rs. 1000. For SC/ST/PWD candidates, the fee is Rs. 500.

Q6. What is the selection process for these Non-Faculty Positions?

The selection process for the Non-Faculty Positions at ABV IIITM includes a Written Test, Interview, Trade Test, and Game Test.

Q7. How should the application form be submitted?

The application form should be submitted offline through Speed Post/Registered Post to the address mentioned in the official notification. The envelope should be superscribed with the name of the applied post.

Q8. What is the salary for the Assistant Engineer (Electrical) post?

The Assistant Engineer (Electrical) post is offered at Pay Level 7 as per the 7th Central Pay Commission (CPC).
