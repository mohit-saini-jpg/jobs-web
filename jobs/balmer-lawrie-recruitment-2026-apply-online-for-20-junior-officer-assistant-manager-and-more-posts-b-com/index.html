How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the e-recruitment portal at https://www.balmerlawrie.com/careers/current-openings. No applications sent directly by email or telephone will be entertained. Online submission of application is permitted between 1000 hours on 13.05.2026 till 2359 hours on 05.06.2026. Candidates are advised to complete the application process within official working hours (up to 1800 hours on the last date) as technical support may not be available after 1800 hours.
  • First-time applicants must register at: Registration Link. After creating your profile, apply against the appropriate position under the "Employment Opportunities" tab. Only creation of profile does not ensure consideration of candidature - candidates must apply against specific positions. Work experience dates must be entered in DD.MM.YYYY format; incorrectly entered data will result in rejection without correspondence.
  • Documents to be Produced at the Time of Interview (on being shortlisted):
  • Proof of Age
  • Educational Qualifications - all marksheets and certificates (with equivalent % if CGPA/DGPA/letter grade awarded)
  • Service Certificate of past employment with proof of date of joining and continuity in present organization
  • Last Salary Certificate / Pay slips (last 3 months) - where applicable
  • SC/ST/OBC-NCL/PwBD/EWS/ES certificate from Competent Authority (if applicable). OBC certificate must clearly indicate non-creamy layer status.
  • NOC from present employer (where applicable)
  • Past employment proof and present employment proof
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar / PAN / Passport)
  • Latest passport size photograph
  • In the absence of any of the above documents, the candidate will not be allowed to appear for interview and no travel expenses shall be reimbursed. Outstation candidates called for interview will be reimbursed travel expenses as per the rules of the Company (mode and class of travel will be intimated in the interview call letter).
  • Apply Online at: https://www.balmerlawrie.com/careers/current-openings
  • For Queries (Executive Positions): Email to [email protected] with the Ref. No. of advertisement and position name as subject, or contact 033-22225218. Applications sent directly to the above email shall be summarily rejected.
  • For General Feedback / Queries (All Notifications): https://balmerlawrie.com/feedback

Important Instructions

  • Candidates must ensure they fulfill the minimum eligibility criteria before applying. The company reserves the right to reject applications at any stage if found ineligible. Applications once submitted cannot be withdrawn. No request for cancellation/change of mailing address/email/category/post will be entertained. All minimum eligibility qualifications should be recognized by UGC/AICTE/Govt. of India/State Govt. and from recognized/affiliated institutes/universities as on the date of passing. Post Graduate Diploma in Management must be certified as equivalent to Post Graduate by AICTE valid for the year when the PG Diploma was completed. ODL (Open and Distance Learning) degrees are accepted only if recognized by UGC; for Management/IT, AICTE recognition is mandatory. Engineering courses through ODL will not be considered (except IGNOU B.Tech for students enrolled up to 2011-12). OBC candidates belonging to "Creamy Layer" are not entitled to relaxation of age and/or reserved positions. OBC-NCL candidates must submit a valid caste certificate issued in the year of advertisement and give a self-undertaking at the time of interview confirming non-creamy layer status. Candidates working in PSU/Govt. Departments must apply through proper channel and produce NOC at the time of interview. Absence of NOC means no pay protection/continuity of service benefit for Executive positions. Selected candidates before joining will be required to undergo Medical examination. If found unfit, they will be debarred from engagement. Any canvassing directly or indirectly by the applicant will disqualify their candidature. Any information or communication regarding changes in the advertisement will be published on the Company's website only. Candidates must regularly check https://www.balmerlawrie.com for updates. All decisions of the Company regarding eligibility, selection, and other matters are final and binding. No correspondence shall be entertained. The court of jurisdiction for any dispute will be at Kolkata.
