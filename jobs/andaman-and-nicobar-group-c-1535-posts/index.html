FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in A&N Administration Combined Matric Level Examination 2026?
A
A total of 1535 vacancies have been announced across 194 post codes in various departments of the Andaman & Nicobar Administration. The category-wise breakup is: UR – 704, ST – 129, OBC – 535, EWS – 167, plus 49 PwBD and 10 ESM as horizontal reservations. The number of vacancies is tentative and may increase or decrease at the sole discretion of the A&N Administration based on inputs from indenting departments.
Q2What is the last date to apply for A&N Administration Matric Level Exam 2026?
A
The last date for submission of online applications is 28-06-2026 (up to 12:00 Midnight). The online application window opens on 08-06-2026 from 12:00 Noon. Applications must be submitted through the official portal https://erecruitment.andamannicobar.gov.in only; no physical applications will be accepted. Candidates are strongly advised to apply well before the closing date to avoid last-minute server congestion issues.
Q3What is the application fee for A&N Administration Combined Matric Level Exam 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 100/- (Rupees One Hundred Only) payable through Net Banking, VISA/Master/Rupay/Credit/Debit Card, or UPI Payment/QR Code. Female candidates, ST candidates, and Persons with Benchmark Disability (PwBD) candidates are fully exempted from the application fee. Fee once paid is non-refundable under any circumstances.
Q4What is the exam pattern for A&N Administration Combined Matric Level Exam 2026?
A
The Tier-I Tablet Based Test (TBT) consists of 150 questions (150 marks) in 120 minutes: General Intelligence & Reasoning (25Q/25M), Numerical Aptitude (25Q/25M), English Language (50Q/50M), and General Awareness (50Q/50M). Each question carries 1 mark; 0.25 marks are deducted for each wrong answer. VH/Cerebral Palsy candidates get 40 additional minutes (total 160 minutes). Questions for sections I, II, and IV are set in English and Hindi; English Language section is in English only. The Tier-II Physical/Skill/Trade Test (wherever applicable) is qualifying in nature only.
Q5What is the age limit for A&N Administration Matric Level Exam 2026?
A
The age limit is 18 to 33 years for Male and 18 to 38 years for Female candidates (Group C posts) as on the closing date 28-06-2026. Age relaxation is applicable: ST candidates get 5 years; OBC (NCL) candidates get 3 years; PwBD (UR) gets 10 years; Ex-Servicemen get 3 years after deduction of military service. Central Government civilian employees with minimum 3 years regular continuous service can apply up to 40 years (45 years for ST). Candidates claiming relaxation fraudulently will be permanently debarred.
Q6Can OBC candidates from mainland India apply for A&N Administration Matric Level Exam 2026 under OBC reservation?
A
No. OBC reservation in A&N Administration is available exclusively to Local OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) candidates covered under the specific Gazette Notifications (No.343/205/F.No.8-3/2003-TW dated 16th December 2005 and No. 752006/F.No.8-3/2003-TW dated 19th April 2006). OBC candidates from outside A&N Islands will be considered only against Unreserved (UR) vacancies. Similarly, only OBC-NCL certificates not older than 2 years from the closing date are valid for claiming OBC reservation.
Q7What documents are required at the time of A&N Administration TBT examination?
A
Candidates must carry: (1) Printed copy of the e-Admit Card downloaded from the official portal; (2) One valid government-issued photo ID in original - Islander Card, Pan Card, Voter ID Card, Driving License, or Aadhaar Card issued by Government of India; and (3) One recent passport-size photograph. Mobile phones, calculators, smart watches, Bluetooth devices, and any other electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited inside the exam hall - even in switched-off mode.
Q8Is there negative marking in A&N Administration Matric Level Exam 2026?
A
Yes, there is negative marking. For each wrong answer, 0.25 marks (one-fourth of the mark) will be deducted. Each question carries 1 mark, so attempting a question incorrectly results in a net loss of 0.25 marks. There is no deduction for unattempted questions. Candidates are strongly advised to keep this in mind while answering - avoid random guessing. There is also no provision for re-evaluation or re-checking of answer sheets/scripts.
Q9What is the Tier-II examination in A&N Administration Combined Matric Level Exam 2026?
A
Tier-II consists of Physical Standard Examination / Physical Measurement and Endurance Test / Skill / Trade Test, wherever prescribed as essential qualification for the specific post (as per Annexure-I of the notification). It is conducted by the concerned department for candidates who qualify in Tier-I TBT. Importantly, the Tier-II test is only of qualifying nature - it does not contribute to the final merit score. Final selection is based solely on Tier-I TBT marks, subject to qualifying the Tier-II test where applicable.
Q10Can candidates from outside Andaman & Nicobar Islands apply for A&N Administration Matric Level Exam 2026?
A
Yes, any eligible Indian National can apply. However, candidates from outside A&N Islands applying under ST or OBC reservation will not get the benefit of these reservations - they will be treated as Unreserved (UR) category candidates. Only Local Scheduled Tribe candidates of A&N Islands (as per Scheduled Tribe Order 1959) and Local OBC (NCL) candidates (as per specified A&N Gazette Notifications) are eligible for reserved quota benefits. All selected candidates must be willing to serve in posts/departments located in the Andaman & Nicobar Islands.
