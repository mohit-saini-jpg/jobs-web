Lado Laxmi Yojana 2026 Update, 8th Kist Status: Under the Lado Laxmi Scheme, every woman in Haryana receives ₹ 2,100 in monthly financial assistance. An amount of ₹1,100 is deposited into the woman’s bank account each month, and the government makes a fixed deposit of ₹1,000 in her name. Women in Haryana who are benefiting from the Lado Laxmi Scheme can check their pension status through the official website.
