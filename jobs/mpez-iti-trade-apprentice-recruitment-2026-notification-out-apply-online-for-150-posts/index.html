Application Fee

DetailsThe official notification does not mention any application fee. Application is through the NAPS portal (https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in/). Candidates should check the official notification and NAPS portal for any applicable portal charges or processing fees before applying.

Age Limit

DetailsCandidates must be a minimum of 18 years as on 01-01-2026. Maximum age is 25 years for General (UR) candidates. Relaxation in upper age limit is applicable as per Government of India / State Government guidelines for SC/ST/OBC/PwBD candidates.

Qualification / Eligibility

DomicileMadhya Pradesh Mool Niwasi (M.P. domicile) only. Vacancies are exclusively for M.P. residents.
Education QualificationClass 10th (Dasvin) pass AND ITI certificate (NCVT / SCVT) in the relevant trade from a recognized institution. Trade-wise qualification: COPA trade – 1-year ITI (NCVT/SCVT); Stenographer Hindi – 1-year ITI (NCVT/SCVT); Electrician – 2-year ITI (NCVT/SCVT).
DetailsCandidates must fulfill all eligibility conditions as on the respective cut-off dates. Only candidates with a 100% completed Apprentice profile on the NAPS portal are eligible to apply. Candidates can apply for only one trade and must not submit more than one application.

Selection Process

  • Selection will be merit-based on ITI total percentage of marks obtained. No written examination is conducted. Final selection is subject to eligibility verification
  • category-wise vacancy
  • and medical fitness. In case of equal marks
  • older candidates will be given priority within the same category and trade.
  • Stage 1 - Merit List (Based on ITI Percentage Marks)
  • Applications received within the deadline will be screened for eligibility as per the advertisement. After screening
  • MPEZ Jabalpur will prepare a merit list in descending order of ITI total percentage marks
  • category-wise and trade-wise
  • for all applications received before the cut-off. In case of equal marks
  • the older candidate gets priority. Selection from waiting list may be done if required.
  • Stage 2 - Document Verification
  • Shortlisted candidates will be intimated and must appear for document verification at their own expense. Original documents along with self-attested photocopies must be produced. If a candidate fails to join within the stipulated time after selection
  • their selection will be treated as automatically cancelled.
  • Stage 3 - Medical Examination
  • Engagement of selected apprentices is subject to being declared medically fit. Final selection will be based on medical fitness certificate issued by the District Medical Officer (CMO). PwBD candidates must submit disability certificate (40% or more) from competent authority or District Medical Board.

How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply online through the NAPS portal (https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in/) only. No other mode of application will be accepted. Before applying, candidates must mandatorily complete 100% Apprentice profile registration on the NAPS portal. Instructional videos for Registration and Profile Completion are available at: https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in/ → Instructional Video → Candidate. Candidates must note their valid Apprentice Registration Number from NAPS before proceeding.
  • Documents Required / Steps for NAPS Portal Registration:
  • Valid Aadhaar number or other acceptable ID for NAPS portal registration
  • ITI Pass Certificate and Mark Sheet (NCVT/SCVT) in relevant trade
  • Class 10th (Dasvin) certificate and mark sheet
  • Category/caste certificate (if applicable: SC/ST/OBC/PwBD/Divyang)
  • M.P. domicile/Mool Niwasi certificate
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Valid mobile number and email ID for OTP and future communications
  • Bank account details (required for stipend disbursement on NAPS portal)
  • Documents to be Produced at the time of Document Verification / Joining:
  • NAPS Apprentice Registration printout with Registration Number
  • Printout of submitted application
  • Class 10th (Dasvin) certificate in support of Date of Birth and educational qualification
  • ITI pass certificate and all semester/year mark sheets (NCVT/SCVT) in the relevant trade
  • Caste certificate in prescribed Government of India / M.P. Government format for SC/ST candidates
  • OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in prescribed format, valid and current (issued recently); OBC Caste Name must appear in Central/State Government list
  • M.P. Mool Niwasi (domicile) certificate issued by competent authority
  • Disability Certificate (40% or more) from competent authority / District Medical Board for Divyang/PwBD candidates
  • Medical Fitness Certificate from District Medical Officer (CMO)
  • Photo Identity Proof (Aadhaar / PAN Card / Passport / Voter ID / Bank Passbook with photograph)
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Any other relevant documents in support of eligibility
  • NO DOCUMENT SHOULD BE SENT DIRECTLY TO MPEZ BEFORE OR AFTER THE PROCESS. Candidates must appear for document verification at their own expense.
  • Apply Online at: https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in/ → Candidate Registration → Complete Profile → Apply for MPEZ Training Seats

Important Instructions

  • THIS IS NOT AN EMPLOYMENT IN MPEZ. This advertisement is for Apprenticeship Training only under Section 22(1) of the Apprentices Act, 1961. MPEZ is not liable to offer regular employment after training completion, nor shall any apprentice have any right to claim employment in MPEZ after the training period ends. After completion of apprenticeship training, candidates must pass the exam conducted by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India, as per Section 21(1) and (2) of the Apprentices Act, 1961, after which a National Apprenticeship Certificate will be issued. These vacancies are only for M.P. Mool Niwasi (domicile) candidates. Non-M.P. candidates are not eligible. Candidates can apply for only one trade. Multiple applications will lead to disqualification. If at any stage of the selection process it is found that a candidate does not meet the eligibility criteria, has hidden information, or has given incorrect information, their candidature will be cancelled and legal action may be taken. SC/ST/OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) and Divyang candidates must fill their category carefully — information once submitted cannot be changed under any circumstances. The merit/seniority list prepared through this advertisement will be valid for one year from the date of announcement of first results. Vacancies arising during this period may also be filled from this merit list. Selected apprentices are entitled to 13 days casual leave, 15 days medical leave, and weekly/national holidays during the training period. Workmen's Compensation Act, 1923 will be applicable in case of accident during training. MPEZ Jabalpur reserves the right to cancel, limit, or extend the selection process at any time without prior notice or reason. If selection does not happen, no correspondence or inquiry from candidates will be entertained. Selected apprentices will be posted at MPEZ offices under Jabalpur, Sagar, Rewa, and Shahdol zones. OBC candidates belonging to the "Creamy Layer" are not entitled to reservation/relaxation; they must apply as General/UR. Candidates wishing to make changes to their online application may do so via "Edit my profile" on the portal before the last date. Applications submitted before 25-05-2026 or after 25-06-2026 will not be accepted under any circumstances.
