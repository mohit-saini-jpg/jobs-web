Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is NALCO Non-Executive Recruitment 2026?

NALCO Non-Executive Recruitment 2026 is a recruitment drive by National Aluminium Company Limited for Non-Executive Personnel at its Mines & Refinery (M&R) Complex in Damanjodi, Odisha under Advertisement No. 10260213. The notification covers various Operator, Mining Mate, Jr. Foreman, SUPT(JOT), and Technician posts in different trades.

Q2. What are the important dates for NALCO Non-Executive Recruitment 2026?

The online application process starts on 21 May 2026 at 10:00 hours and closes on 10 June 2026 up to 23:55 hours. The Computer Based Test and Trade Test dates will be notified later on the official NALCO website and via email/SMS.

Q3. Which posts are available under NALCO Non-Executive Recruitment 2026?

Available posts include Operator (HEMM) Gr. III, Mining Mate Gr. III, Jr. Foreman (Mines), Jr. Foreman (Electrical), various SUPT(JOT) posts, Operator Gr. III, and Technician posts in Fitter, Electrical, and Instrumentation disciplines. These posts fall under T0 and S0 non-executive grades with specified pay scales.

Q4. What is the educational qualification required for NALCO Non-Executive posts?

Candidates must have full-time qualifications like 10th with ITI (NCVT/NCVET), relevant trade apprenticeship certificates, B.Sc. (Hons) in Chemistry, or Diploma in Mining/Electrical Engineering as specified for each post. Only qualifications in the prescribed branches or trades from recognized institutes will be accepted, and equivalent qualifications are not considered.

Q5. What is the age limit for NALCO Non-Executive Recruitment 2026?

For SUPT(JOT) posts (fresher), the upper age limit is 27 years for UR/EWS, 30 years for OBC (NCL), and 32 years for SC/ST candidates, with higher limits for PwBD as per category. For Operator/Technician/Mining Mate and Jr. Foreman posts, the maximum upper age including all relaxations cannot exceed 56 years.

Q6. What is the application fee for NALCO Non-Executive Recruitment 2026?

General, OBC (NCL), and EWS candidates must pay a non-refundable application fee of 100 rupees through online payment modes like net banking, cards, or UPI. SC, ST, PwBD, Ex-Servicemen, Land Ousted candidates, and internal candidates are exempted from payment of the application fee.

Q7. What is the selection process for NALCO Non-Executive posts?

The selection process comprises a Computer Based Test, Trade Test for certain posts, followed by document verification and pre-employment medical examination. For some SUPT(JOT) posts selection is based only on CBT, while for Operator, Technician, Mining Mate, and Jr. Foreman posts selection is based on combined CBT and Trade Test scores with 60:40 weightage.

Q8. What is the CBT exam pattern for NALCO Non-Executive Recruitment 2026?

The CBT is an objective test of 100 multiple choice questions of one mark each conducted for 120 minutes. The paper consists of 60 percent domain-specific technical questions and 40 percent non-technical questions from quantitative aptitude, reasoning, general knowledge, and English, with no negative marking.
