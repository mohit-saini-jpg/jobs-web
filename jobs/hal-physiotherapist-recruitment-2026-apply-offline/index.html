FAQs

Q1How many Physiotherapist posts are available in HAL Nasik Recruitment 2026?
A
Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Aircraft Division, Nasik has announced 02 Physiotherapist posts on a visiting basis at the Industrial Health Center, HAL, Ojhar Township, Nashik, under Notification No. NK/1209(6)/(Physiotherapist)/2026/02 dated 01-06-2026.
Q2What is the last date to apply for HAL Nasik Physiotherapist Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the filled application form (by Post/Courier/By Hand) is 08-06-2026. Applications must physically reach the Manager (Human Resources), HAL Aircraft Division, Ojhar Township, NASHIK – 422 207 on or before this date. HAL does not take responsibility for postal delays.
Q3What is the qualification required for HAL Nasik Physiotherapist post 2026?
A
Candidates must hold a Bachelor in Physiotherapy (BPT) or Master in Physiotherapy (MPT) from a recognized institution. The qualification must be recognized by the Occupational Therapy and Physiotherapy Council. Only regular/full-time qualifications are eligible — part-time, correspondence, distance education, or e-learning qualifications are NOT accepted.
Q4What is the age limit for HAL Nasik Physiotherapist Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit is 40 years as on 01-06-2026 (date of advertisement). No further age relaxation of any kind is admissible for this post.
Q5What is the remuneration for HAL Nasik Physiotherapist (Visiting Basis) 2026?
A
The remuneration per visit will be fixed by HAL. Applicants must indicate their expected remuneration per visit (for a minimum of 03 visits per week) in the application form. In addition, selected candidates are eligible for conveyance reimbursement per visit as per applicable HAL rules. No other allowances or benefits are provided.
Q6How many visits per week are required for the HAL Nasik Physiotherapist post?
A
The selected Physiotherapist must visit 3 times per week for a minimum of 4 hours per visit at the Industrial Health Center, HAL, Ojhar Township, Nashik, to carry out routine physiotherapy activities including SWD, Microwave, TENS, ILT, ICT, Infra Red, IFT, Wax Bath, CPM, Ultrasound, Electrical Stimulator, and exercises for indoor and outdoor patients.
Q7What is the period of engagement for HAL Nasik Physiotherapist 2026?
A
The engagement is initially for 2 years, extendable by a further 2 years based on the requirement of the Hospital and the performance of the Specialist as assessed by the Head of the Hospital. This is a temporary/visiting basis engagement only — it does not confer any right to claim regular employee status at HAL.
Q8What is the selection process for HAL Nasik Physiotherapist Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of two stages: (1) Shortlisting of suitable candidates based on application review; and (2) Personal Interview conducted at HAL Hospital, Ojhar Township, Nashik. Interview date and timing will be intimated to shortlisted candidates via email. Final selection is based solely on performance in the personal interview.
Q9How to apply for HAL Nasik Physiotherapist Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must download the prescribed application form (Annexure-I) from www.hal-india.co.in, fill it completely (including expected remuneration per visit), attach self-attested photocopies of all relevant certificates (age proof, qualification, experience, council registration), and submit by Post/Courier/By Hand to reach The Manager (Human Resources), HAL Aircraft Division, Ojhar Township PO, Tal Niphad, NASHIK – 422 207, on or before 08-06-2026. Email applications are NOT accepted.
Q10Is the HAL Nasik Physiotherapist 2026 post a permanent/regular government job?
A
No. The HAL Nasik Physiotherapist post is strictly on a temporary, visiting basis only. It does not confer any right on the selected candidate to claim the status of a regular employee of HAL. The engagement is for an initial period of 2 years, extendable by another 2 years subject to performance and hospital requirements.
