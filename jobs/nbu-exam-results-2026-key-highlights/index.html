How to Check NBU Exam Result 2026

Students can check the North Bengal University Result 2026 online from the official examination website by following the steps given below. The result checking process is simple and can be completed within a few minutes through the official portal.

  • Visit the official website of North Bengal University - https://nbu.ac.in/.
  • Go to the examination or result section available on the website homepage.
  • Open the official result page - https://nbu.ac.in/exr/results.aspx?NG=R0g=#gsc.tab=0.
  • Look for your course name and semester result in the published result list.
  • Click on the relevant result link to open your NBU Result 2026.
  • Enter the required login credentials, if prompted by the portal.
  • Check your marks and download or print the result for future reference.
