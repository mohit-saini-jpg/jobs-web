How to Apply

  • 1Printed copy of the online application form
  • 2All original educational qualification certificates and mark sheets (ITI Trade Certificate / Degree Certificate as applicable to the trade applied for)
  • 3Date of Birth certificate / School Leaving Certificate
  • 4NAPS portal registration and eKYC completion proof
  • 5Caste/category certificate as applicable
  • 6Valid Photo ID proof
  • 7Self-attested copies of all above documents
  • 8THIS IS NOT A PERMANENT EMPLOYMENT. The apprenticeship period is as per the contract generated on the Government website and candidates will be immediately discharged on completion of the apprenticeship period.
  • 9Mandatory NAPS portal registration with eKYC update must be completed before applying on the SMC website. Applications submitted without prior NAPS registration will not be processed.
  • 10Candidates who have already completed an apprenticeship in any trade are not eligible — online contract generation will not be possible for such candidates.
  • 11Stipend payable to apprentices is subject to Government policy and guidelines as amended from time to time.
  • 12Document verification date and intimations will be sent only to the registered mobile number and email ID provided in the application. In case of change in mobile number or email, SMC will not be responsible for any future communication gaps.
  • 13Candidates must appear for document verification with both original documents and self-attested copies. Absence from document verification will result in automatic cancellation of candidature.
  • 14If, during scrutiny of application form and documents, any candidate is found not meeting the eligibility criteria under the Apprentices Act or the notification provisions, or if any discrepancy is found between the application and original certificates, candidature will be cancelled at any stage.
  • 15If a candidate leaves the apprenticeship before completion of the period, the contract will be terminated.
  • 16The Appointing Authority (SMC) reserves full rights to cancel this notification, cancel any trade, or make any modifications for any reason without being obligated to provide reasons for the same.
