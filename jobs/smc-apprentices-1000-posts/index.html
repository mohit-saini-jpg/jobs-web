SMC Apprentices Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 1000 Posts

Recruitment Division, Surat Municipal Corporation

Posts: Various Last Date: 10-06-2026 (11:00 PM) Online only – via Surat Municipal Corporation website (https://www.suratmunicipal.gov.in/Information/Recruitment) after mandatory NAPS portal registration

Short Information

SMC Apprentices Recruitment 2026 notification is out to fill 1000 vacancies across Surat. Candidates with Any Graduate, B.Sc, ITI, diploma Can Apply Online. The online application opens on 01-06-2026, and closes on 10-06-2026. The candidate shall apply online through SMC website, suratmunicipal.gov.in.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only. Before applying, every candidate must mandatorily: (1) register as an Apprentice on the NAPS portal at https://apprenticeshipindia.gov.in, and (2) update eKYC in the Apprentice profile. Only after completing both steps on the NAPS portal should candidates proceed to apply online at the SMC recruitment portal. For more details, candidates may contact the Recruitment Division on Phone No. 0261-2423751-56 (Extension-231).
