How to Apply

  • 1Recent passport-size photograph (scanned, clear and legible)
  • 2Signature (scanned)
  • 3Proof of age (10th/Matriculation/Secondary School Leaving Certificate or equivalent)
  • 4Educational qualification certificates and mark sheets (all degrees from qualifying degree onwards)
  • 5Experience Certificates (on official letterhead, in chronological order, with designation, period, nature of duties, and seal/stamp) - post-qualification experience only
  • 6SC/ST Caste Certificate in Central Government prescribed format (for SC/ST applicants)
  • 7OBC (NCL) Certificate in prescribed format, clearly specifying validity period and valid as on the closing date (for OBC NCL applicants)
  • 8Income and Asset Certificate (EWS) issued by Competent Authority, valid as on the closing date (for EWS applicants)
  • 9Valid UDID/Disability Certificate issued by Competent Authority (for PwD applicants)
  • 10All original certificates and mark sheets (educational qualifications, age proof, experience)
  • 11Original category/disability certificates (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwD)
  • 12No Objection Certificate (NOC) from present employer (for candidates in Govt./PSU/Autonomous Body)
  • 13Vigilance Clearance Certificate from present employer (for candidates in Govt./PSU/Autonomous Body)
  • 14Only Indian Nationals are eligible. All qualifications must be from a recognized University/Institution.
  • 15Experience claimed must be post-qualification, full-time work experience. Only full-time work experience will be considered; the Institute's decision on relevance of experience is final.
  • 16Applicants working in Government Institutions/PSUs/Government Funded Institutions must submit an NOC from their present employer along with vigilance clearance certificate at the document verification/JOPT/interview stage. Without NOC, they will not be permitted to proceed further.
  • 17Candidates must apply and pay the fee separately for each post. In case of multiple applications for the same post, only the last application is considered; fees for earlier applications are forfeited.
  • 18Admission to any stage of the recruitment process is purely provisional. Issue of Admit Card/Call Letter does not imply eligibility has been confirmed.
  • 19The Institute reserves the right to decide the mode of screening, place a reasonable limit on the number of candidates called for the selection process, reject any/all applications without assigning reasons, revise/reschedule/cancel/suspend the recruitment process at any stage, and increase/decrease the number of posts.
  • 20The Institute may assign/transfer selected candidates to any section/department within the Institute, including Noida Outreach Centre.
  • 21Any attempt to influence the recruitment process will lead to disqualification.
  • 22Any discrepancy found between the information provided in the application and supporting documents at any stage will render the candidature ineligible and lead to disqualification.
  • 23No TA/DA is payable for written/skill tests. TA is reimbursed for Group A candidates called for interview (AC-II Tier train fare for Level 10–11; Economy class airfare for Level 12 and above).
  • 24Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this advertisement shall be Kanpur (U.P.) only.
  • 25IIT Kanpur will retain data in accordance with the Institute's record retention policy.
