Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has officially declared the Bihar Board Class 10th Result 2026 for the annual matric examinations. The Class 10th marks and pass status have been made available online for all registered students of the 2025-26 academic session. Bihar Board Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - biharboardonline.bihar.gov.in.

Students can check and download their Bihar Board 10th marksheet by visiting the official result portal, entering their roll code and roll number and verifying the displayed details. After downloading the online scorecard, they should carefully check their name, roll number, subjects, marks, division and overall result status and contact their school or the board in case of any discrepancy.

How to Check Bihar Board Class 10th Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your Bihar Board 10th Result 2026:

  • Go to the official Bihar Board website: https://biharboardonline.bihar.gov.in/ or visit https://matricbiharboard.com/.
  • On the homepage, click on the link that mentions “Annual Matric Exam Result 2026” or similar.
  • You will be redirected to the result login page.
  • Enter your Roll Code and Roll Number as printed on your admit card.
  • Click on Submit / View Result / Search to display your Class 10th marksheet.
  • Check your personal details, subject wise marks, total marks, percentage and division, then download or print the marksheet for future use in admissions and documentation.
