IISER IAT 2026 Admit Cards Out

Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research have released the IISER IAT 2026 admit card for the IISER Aptitude Test. As per the updated schedule, the hall ticket, initially planned for 24 May, has now been made available online from 26 May 2026 via the official admission portal.

The IISER Aptitude Test (IAT) 2026 will be held on 7 June 2026 from 9:00 AM to 12:00 Noon (IST) in computer‑based test mode at centres across India. Candidates who registered successfully can log in at iiseradmission.in and the Digialm login page Click Here using their user ID and password to download the IISER IAT hall ticket 2026.

How to Download IISER IAT 2026 Admit Card

  • Visit the IISER admission portal iiseradmission.in.
  • Click on the “IAT 2026 Admit Card / Hall Ticket” link on the homepage or IAT 2026 section; you will be redirected to the Digialm login page.
  • On the login page Click Here, enter your User ID (application ID) and Password, then fill the captcha code.
  • Click on Login to access your IAT 2026 dashboard.
  • Click on the “Admit Card / Hall Ticket” link; your IISER IAT 2026 admit card will appear showing your name, roll number, exam date, reporting time and exam centre.
  • Verify all details and download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies of the admit card to carry on exam day.

If the website is slow due to heavy traffic, candidates are advised to try again after some time.

Details Mentioned on IISER IAT 2026 Admit Card

The IISER IAT 2026 hall ticket carries key exam and candidate information which must be checked carefully:

  • Candidate’s name
  • Application number / User ID and roll number
  • Candidate’s photograph and signature (if printed)
  • Exam name: IISER Aptitude Test (IAT) 2026
  • Exam date and time: 7 June 2026, 9:00 AM - 12:00 Noon
  • Reporting time and gate‑closing time
  • Exam centre name, centre code, city and full address
  • Category and other basic details (if available)
  • Important exam‑day instructions and guidelines

In case of any discrepancy in your name, photo, category or exam centre details, you should contact the IISER admission helpdesk immediately via the contact details given in the information brochure and on the website.

Documents Required with IISER IAT 2026 Admit Card

On the day of the IISER Aptitude Test 2026, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the IISER IAT 2026 admit card (clear A4‑size printout; preferably colour or high‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence or School/College ID, as mentioned in the instructions.

Entry to the examination centre will not be permitted without the admit card and valid photo ID.

Exam‑Day Instructions for IISER IAT 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the IISER IAT 2026 CBT, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card so that verification, frisking and seating can be completed; latecomers after gate‑closing time will not be allowed entry.
  • Carry only essential items: IISER IAT admit card (A4 printout), valid photo ID and any permitted items as per instructions; follow all directions printed on the hall ticket and displayed at the centre.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless explicitly permitted), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, books or any other electronic gadgets or paper material inside the exam hall; violation can lead to cancellation of the exam and further action.
  • Follow instructions displayed on the computer screen and those announced by invigilators; indulging in unfair means or disobeying rules may result in disqualification and debarment from IISER admissions.
  • Keep your IISER IAT 2026 admit card safely even after the exam, as it may be required during result declaration, counselling and admission processes.
