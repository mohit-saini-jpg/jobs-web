FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the pay scale for Regular Faculty posts at AIIMS Bilaspur?
A
Professor: Entry Level Pay Matrix 14A (Basic Rs. 1,68,900 – 2,20,400) as per 7th CPC. Associate Professor: Entry Level Pay Matrix 13A1 (Basic Rs. 1,38,300 – 2,09,200) as per 7th CPC. Assistant Professor: Entry Level Pay Matrix 12 (Basic Rs. 1,01,500 – 1,67,400) as per 7th CPC. Plus NPA for candidates with medical qualifications and all usual allowances.
Q2Q2. What is the remuneration for Contractual Assistant Professor at AIIMS Bilaspur?
A
Contractual Assistant Professors will be paid consolidated monthly remuneration as Basic Pay (Rs. 1,01,500) + NPA @ 20% of Basic Pay + Applicable DA. The remuneration remains unchanged for the entire contract period with no annual increment. Retired faculty appointed as consultants will be paid remuneration arrived at by deducting basic pension from pay drawn at retirement.
Q3Q3. Is this a rolling advertisement?
A
Yes. This advertisement is a rolling advertisement and will remain active for one year or till withdrawal to fill unfilled posts, without the need to re-publish. Dates for receipt of applications will be notified on the website www.aiimsbilaspur.edu.in.
Q4Q4. Are Professor posts open for Deputation?
A
Yes, Professor posts are also open for Deputation. The initial period of deputation shall be three years, extendable as per the discretion of the competent authority. Candidates applying for deputation will be considered under Unreserved category.
