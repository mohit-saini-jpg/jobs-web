ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 has been announced through Short Notice No. 03 of Advertisement No. 01/2026, inviting eligible doctors for a walk-in interview on 23 July 2026 (Thursday) at ESIC Model Hospital, Naroda-Bapunagar, Ahmedabad, Gujarat. This recruitment drive is being conducted to fill 20 Senior Resident posts under the Residency Scheme across departments such as Anesthesiology, General Medicine, General Surgery, ICU, PICU, NICU, Radiology, and more.

ESIC Senior Resident Vacancy Details 2026

The total number of Senior Resident vacancies is 20, distributed across the following departments and categories: Category-wise total break-up: 4 UR, 2 EWS, 7 OBC, 3 SC, and 4 ST. Additionally, 1 vacancy is reserved for PwBD (Persons with Benchmark Disabilities) candidates, adjustable against the respective category vacancy. Eligibility Criteria for ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment

MBBS with a Postgraduate (PG) Degree / DNB / Diploma in the concerned specialty from a Government-recognized university or college. Candidates must be registered with the Gujarat Medical Council or the National Medical Commission (NMC). Candidates registered in another state must get registered with the Gujarat Medical Council at the time of joining. For the Senior Resident (Nephrology) post specifically, the qualification is MBBS with PG Degree / DNB / Diploma in Medicine or Pediatrics from a Government-recognized institution. Preference will be given to candidates holding a DM/DNB in Nephrology. Maximum age: 45 years as on the date of the interview. Age relaxation: 3 years for OBC candidates and 5 years for SC/ST candidates.

ESIC Senior Resident Salary / Pay Scale 2026

Selected candidates will receive a Basic Pay of Rs. 67,700/- under Level-11, Index-1 of the Pay Matrix (equivalent to the pre-revised Basic Pay of Rs. 18,750/- in PB-3 with Grade Pay Rs. 6,600/-), along with allowances applicable under ESIC rules and the Senior Residency Scheme.

Application Fee for ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment

UR and OBC category candidates must deposit a Demand Draft (non-refundable) of Rs. 500/- in favour of "ESIC Fund Account No. 1", payable at Ahmedabad, at the time of the interview. SC/ST/PwBD/Female candidates are exempted from paying the application fee.

ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Candidates must report at the Conference Hall (2nd Floor), ESIC Model Hospital, Bapunagar, Ahmedabad, at 9:30 AM on 23 July 2026 for document verification. Only candidates who clear document verification will be permitted to appear before the interview panel. Selection is made purely through a walk-in interview; there is no written examination. Applicants must report by 10:00 AM; entries after this time will not be entertained.

Required Documents for the Interview

Original and photocopies of MBBS and PG Degree/DNB/Diploma certificates Gujarat Medical Council or National Medical Commission registration certificate Original category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) in the prescribed Government of India format, wherever applicable OBC-NCL certificate (valid, as per the latest DOPT format) for candidates claiming OBC reservation PwBD certificate, if applicable No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the current employer, for candidates already working in a Government or Private hospital/institute Proof of age, identity, and address Demand Draft of Rs. 500/- (for UR/OBC candidates only)

Important Instructions

The Senior Residency tenure is for a total of 3 years, including any prior ad-hoc or regular SR service in a government institution. Appointment is initially for one year, extendable annually upon satisfactory performance review. Selected candidates must pay a Security Deposit of Rs. 30,000/- and execute an agreement on a Rs. 300/- Non-Judicial Stamp Paper at the time of joining.
