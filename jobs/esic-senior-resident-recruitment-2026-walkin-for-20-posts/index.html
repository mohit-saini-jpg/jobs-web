ESIC Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

Walk-in Interview

Walk-In Procedure

Read the official notification carefully before attending the interview. Candidates must report at the venue as per the reporting time mentioned in the notification. Carry all original documents along with one set of self-attested photocopies such as ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates (if required), and category certificate (if applicable). Bring the duly filled application form if required in the notification. Verify eligibility criteria before attending the interview. Candidates are advised to reach the venue well in advance to complete registration formalities. Keep extra passport-size photographs for submission if required.

ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for the ESIC Senior Resident posts?
A
The walk-in interview for the Senior Resident posts at ESIC Hospital, Adityapur, Jharkhand is scheduled for 06th July 2026. Subsequent interviews will be held on the first Wednesday of every month.
Q2What is the eligibility criteria for the Senior Resident post?
A
Candidates must possess a PG Degree/Diploma or MBBS degree from a recognized university with at least two years of working experience, including one year in the concerned specialty from a government or private hospital.
Q3What is the age limit for the Senior Resident position?
A
The maximum age limit is 45 years as of the interview date. Age relaxation is applicable for SC, ST, and OBC candidates as per government rules.
Q4What is the salary offered for the Senior Resident post?
A
The salary is as per Pay Level -11 (Rs. 67,700/-) plus other allowances as per ESIC Rules, with total emoluments approximately Rs. 1,49,284/-.
Q5How many vacancies are available for Senior Resident posts?
A
There are a total of 20 vacancies for Senior Resident posts across various specializations.
Q6What is the application fee for the ESIC Senior Resident recruitment?
A
The application fee is Rs. 400/- for General, EWS, and OBC candidates, Rs. 100/- for SC/ST candidates, and exempted for female candidates.
Q7What is the selection process for these posts?
A
The selection will be based on a Walk-in Interview.
Q8Where is the walk-in interview scheduled to be held?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at ESIC Hospital, Adityapur, Jharkhand, Pin Code: 831013.
Q9What documents are required for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates need to bring two sets of self-attested copies of required documents, original documents for verification, and a No Objection Certificate if currently employed.
Q10Is there any preference given during the selection process?
A
Yes, preference will be given to candidates who have completed their PG Degree or Diploma.
Q11How can I apply for ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 - Walkin for 20 Posts?
A
Ans: You can apply online through the official recruitment link provided for ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 - Walkin for 20 Posts on www.topsarkarijobs.com.
