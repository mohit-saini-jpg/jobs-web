How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply online only at http://kgbvrecruitment.apcfss.in. Offline applications and applications received after the closing date will not be entertained under any circumstances. Application fee of Rs. 300/- must be paid through the Commissioner of School Education payment gateway during online application. Candidates must upload all required documents without fail.
  • Steps to Apply Online:
  • Step 1: Visit http://kgbvrecruitment.apcfss.in and register with valid mobile number and email ID.
  • Step 2: Select the post (Principal / PGT / CRT / PET), subject and the district you are applying for.
  • Step 3: Fill in all personal, educational and professional details accurately. Enter marks exactly as per certificates - do not inflate.
  • Step 4: Upload all required documents (see checklist below).
  • Step 5: Pay application fee of Rs. 300/- through the online payment gateway.
  • Step 6: Submit application before 05.06.2026. Download and save the application confirmation.
  • Documents to be Uploaded (Checklist for Certificate Verification):
  • SSC (10th) Certificate and Marks Memo
  • Intermediate (12th) Certificate and Marks Memo
  • Bachelor's Degree Certificate and Marks Memos (wherever applicable)
  • Master's Degree Certificate and Marks Memos (wherever applicable)
  • B.Ed / M.Ed / UGDPE / B.P.Ed / M.P.Ed Certificate and Marks Memos (wherever applicable)
  • Diploma Certificate (wherever applicable)
  • Local Status Certificate (Tahsildar's Residence Certificate if studied in private/unrecognized school)
  • Caste Certificate in prescribed format (for SC/ST/BC candidates)
  • Income Certificate issued by Tahsildar in the respective district
  • Differently Abled Certificate from Competent Authority (for PwD candidates; minimum 40% disability)
  • Income Certificate for EWS (below Rs. 8.00 Lakhs per annum from Competent Authority)
  • APTET Relevant Documents / Score Card
  • Apply Online at: http://kgbvrecruitment.apcfss.in

Important Instructions

  • ALL posts are for women candidates only. All rosters for general candidates are also to be filled with women candidates of that particular community. All appointments are purely on contract/outsourcing/temporary basis for academic year 2026-27 only. Selected candidates have NO right to seek regularisation, Minimum Time Scale, or enhancement of honorarium. The appointing authority has the right to terminate candidates with poor performance at any point of time without any prior notice. If any candidate's performance is found unsatisfactory in teaching, or they submit false documents, or misbehave with students - their services may be terminated immediately without prior notice. Selected candidates must enter into a Contract Agreement on Rs. 100/- Bond Paper for the period of one academic year 2026-27. The State Project Director has all rights to complete or cancel the recruitment at any point of time without prior intimation. All candidates must be placed in schools by 20.06.2026 without fail. Any deviation will be viewed very seriously. This notification is subject to the outcome of court cases pending before the Hon'ble High Court of Andhra Pradesh. No candidate is allowed to apply in more than one district. The district is the unit for applying and appointment for all cadres. Candidates who furnished wrong information or claimed higher marks than achieved will be permanently debarred from all government jobs. EWS candidates must furnish income certificate below Rs. 8.00 Lakhs per annum issued by the competent authority. All revisions/updates will be published at http://kgbvrecruitment.apcfss.in - visit regularly.
