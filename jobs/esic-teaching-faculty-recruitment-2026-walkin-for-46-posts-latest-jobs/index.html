FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for ESIC Teaching Faculty posts?
A
The walk-in interview for the ESIC Teaching Faculty posts is scheduled to be held on 09 June 2026. Candidates should ensure they reach the venue on time.
Q2How many vacancies are available for Teaching Faculty posts in ESIC?
A
There are a total of 46 vacancies for Teaching Faculty posts in ESIC, including Professor, Associate Professor, and Assistant Professor positions.
Q3What is the maximum age limit for applying to these ESIC posts?
A
The maximum age limit for all the Teaching Faculty posts in ESIC is 69 years.
Q4What is the application fee for General/OBC category candidates?
A
The application fee for General and OBC category candidates is Rs. 500. SC/ST and Women candidates are exempted from paying the application fee.
Q5What is the salary offered for the Professor post?
A
The consolidated pay for the Professor post is ₹2,60,226/- per month.
Q6What is the qualification required for the Teaching Faculty posts?
A
The qualification required for the Teaching Faculty posts is as per the latest NMC (National Medical Commission) guidelines.
Q7Is there any online application process for this recruitment?
A
No, this recruitment is being conducted through a Walk-in Interview. Candidates do not need to apply online beforehand.
Q8What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates should carry all original documents, one set of self-attested photocopies, ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates, and category certificates (if applicable).
Q9Where is the walk-in interview for ESIC Teaching Faculty posts being held?
A
The walk-in interview is being held in Faridabad, Haryana. The exact venue details will be available in the official notification.
Q10What is the selection process for these ESIC posts?
A
The selection process will be based on academic credentials and interview performance.
