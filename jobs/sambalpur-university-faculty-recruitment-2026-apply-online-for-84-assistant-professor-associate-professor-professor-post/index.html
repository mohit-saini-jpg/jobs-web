How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the HIMS Portal available at https://himsrecruitment.odisha.gov.in/home, which will be available from 15-05-2026. The online application can be filled in multiple sessions. Candidates willing to apply for more than one post must apply separately and pay processing fee for each application. No other application or CV should be sent by email or any other means.
  • After submitting the online application, candidates must download the PDF of the submitted application generated by the HIMS portal and send the hard copy along with all supporting documents via Registered Post/Speed Post to reach the University on or before 26-06-2026. The envelope must be superscribed "APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF ……………………………." along with the Advertisement No. and Date. No hard copy will be considered if the applicant fails to submit the online application on or before the closing date.
  • Persons employed in Government/Public Sector Enterprise/Autonomous Bodies must forward a copy of their application through proper channel or furnish a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the competent authority or employer. In-service candidates must forward the hard copy through proper channel or attach NOC so as to reach the University as per the specified date.
  • Hard Copy to be Sent to:
  • The Registrar, Sambalpur University, Jyoti Vihar, Burla, Sambalpur – 768019, Odisha
  • Documents to be Submitted Along with Hard Copy of Application:
  • Downloaded printed copy of the submitted application form from HIMS portal
  • Matriculation/SSC certificate/pass certificate as proof of Date of Birth
  • Marks sheets and certificates of all Educational/Technical/Professional qualifications
  • Research publications and other supporting documents as applicable
  • SC/ST/SEBC caste certificate as per Govt. of Odisha format (if applicable)
  • PWD Disability Certificate issued by competent authority as per Govt. of Odisha/India format (if applicable)
  • NOC from employer / application forwarded through proper channel (for in-service candidates)
  • UGC communication certificate from employer/head of institution regarding Ph.D. period counted as teaching experience (if applicable, as per UGC Communication dated 1 March, 2016)
  • *Candidates are required to bring original documents of all certificates/Grade Cards etc. submitted along with the application form at the time of interview. Only the downloaded application form from the HIMS portal is required; no other application or format is acceptable for the hard copy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026 notification about?

Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026 notification is for regular Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor posts in various Departments/Subjects/Colleges under Sambalpur University, Jyoti Vihar, Burla, Odisha, as per UGC Regulations 2018 and Odisha Revised Scales of Pay for University Teachers, 2019.

Q2. What are the important dates for Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

The online application portal on the HIMS recruitment website opens on 15 May 2026 and closes on 16 June 2026 (midnight), while the hard copy of the application with documents must reach the University by 26 June 2026; the interview schedule will be notified later on the official website.

Q3. How many vacancies are released for Sambalpur University Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor posts in 2026?

Sambalpur University has notified 20 Professor posts, 30 Associate Professor posts and 34 Assistant Professor posts across multiple departments including Business Administration, Chemistry, Computer Science, Earth Science, Economics, English, History, Law, Life Science, Odia, Physics, Sociology and others with category-wise reservations such as UR, SC, ST, SEBC and Women sub‑reservation.

Q4. What is the age limit for Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

For Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026, candidates must be below 60 years of age as on the closing date of online application submission, and applicants above 60 years are not eligible to apply.

Q5. What are the eligibility criteria for Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor at Sambalpur University?

Eligibility is as per UGC Regulations Notification F.1‑2/2017(EC/PS) dated 18 July 2018, which generally requires a Ph.D. in the concerned/allied/relevant subject, specified research publications and research score for Professor and Associate Professor, and for Assistant Professor a Master’s degree with prescribed marks along with NET/SLET/SET or Ph.D. as per UGC norms, with equivalent options for Performing Arts, Visual Arts and traditional art forms.

Q6. What is the pay scale for Sambalpur University Faculty posts in 2026?

The pay scales are as per Odisha Revised Scales of Pay for University Teachers (UGC Scale of Pay) Rule, 2019, where Professor posts are in Cell‑1 of Level‑14, Associate Professor posts in Cell‑1 of Level‑13A and Assistant Professor posts in Cell‑1 of Level‑10, along with admissible dearness allowance and other allowances sanctioned by the Government of Odisha.

Q7. How can I apply online for Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

Eligible candidates must apply online only through the HIMS portal at the Odisha HIMS recruitment site during 15 May 2026 to 16 June 2026, then download the submitted application PDF and send the signed hard copy with all supporting documents by Registered/Speed Post to the Registrar, Sambalpur University, Jyoti Vihar, Burla, Sambalpur – 768019, Odisha within 10 days of closure of the online application.

Q8. What is the application fee for Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

The non‑refundable application processing fee must be paid online as per HIMS portal instructions, where for Professor posts General/SEBC candidates pay Rs. 2000 and SC/ST/Women candidates pay Rs. 1000, while for Associate Professor and Assistant Professor posts General/SEBC candidates pay Rs. 1000 and SC/ST/Women candidates pay Rs. 600, and PwD candidates are exempted from fee payment.
