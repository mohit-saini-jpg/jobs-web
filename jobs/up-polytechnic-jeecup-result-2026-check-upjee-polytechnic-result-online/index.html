How to Check UP Polytechnic JEECUP Result 2026

Candidates can check the JEECUP 2026 result online by following a simple step-by-step process on the official portal. The result is accessible using the Application Number and Password or Date of Birth. Follow the steps below to view and download your UP Polytechnic Result 2026.

Step 1: Visit the official JEECUP website at jeecup.admissions.nic.in or use the direct result link provided in this article.

Step 2: On the homepage, click on the "UP Polytechnic JEECUP Result 2026" link.

Step 3: Enter your Application Number and Password or Date of Birth in the respective fields.

Step 4: Fill in the Captcha Code carefully and click the Login / Submit button.

Step 5: Your JEECUP Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen. Verify all details including your name, roll number, and marks secured.

Step 6: Download the result PDF and take a printout for future reference, including use during the counselling process.
