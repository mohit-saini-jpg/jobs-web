How to Apply

  • 1Candidature is purely provisional and subject to fulfillment of eligibility criteria at document checking — mere issue of admit card or qualifying the CBT does not imply final selection.
  • 2All qualifications, age, category, and other eligibility conditions must be fulfilled as on the last date of online application (01-07-2026).
  • 3Category/sub-category once filled cannot be changed under any circumstances. Candidates applying under the wrong category will be rejected without any further notice.
  • 4Candidates with result awaited should write "RESULT AWAITED" in the Year of Passing column but must acquire the required qualification by the last date.
  • 5SC/BC reservation is applicable only for Punjab Domicile candidates. Candidates must produce proof of Punjab domicile at document checking.
  • 6EWS candidates must possess an Income and Asset Certificate showing gross annual family income for FY 2025-26, valid for FY 2026-27.
  • 7Ex-Serviceman (Self) certificate must be issued by the concerned District Sainik Welfare Officer, Govt. of Punjab. Lineal Descendant Certificate (for Dependent) must also be from the same authority.
  • 8Freedom Fighter certificate must be issued by the Deputy Commissioner of the concerned district as per Punjab Govt. instructions.
  • 9Sports Person category candidates must submit Sports Gradation Certificates to PSPCL Head Office, Recruitment Section, Patiala before or by the last date of application — separately for each post applied for. Merit for Sports Person category is prepared by the Office of Director/Sports, Punjab.
  • 10PwD reservation is as per The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. Minimum benchmark disability is 40%. PwD certificate must be issued by Civil Surgeon, Govt. of Punjab.
  • 11For Fireman post: CBT-qualified candidates must also pass the Physical Test (100 yards with 60 kg weight in 1 minute, hook ladder, rope/pipe climbing) for final selection.
  • 12Mobile phones, smartwatches, bags, purses, electronic/non-electronic instruments, and ornaments are strictly banned in the examination complex. Only admit card and photo identity proof are to be brought.
  • 13On the day of exam, bring print of admit card along with Aadhaar Card and one more valid photo ID (Driving Licence / Voter Card / Passport / Employer ID from Govt. institution).
  • 14Candidates currently working in Government/Semi-Government/PSU/Cooperative organizations must submit a NOC from their organization after resigning and before joining PSPCL.
  • 15Selected candidates issued appointment letters must join within 21 days. A final notice of 21 more days will be given if not joined on scheduled date. Failure to join even after final notice will result in automatic cancellation of appointment letter.
  • 16Validity of selection panels is one year from the date of approval of the first selection panel by the competent authority. Candidates on the waiting list will not be considered after expiry of validity.
  • 17No TA/DA will be paid for online test, document checking, or counselling.
  • 18In case of any dispute or ambiguity, legal jurisdiction shall be subject to Local Courts at Patiala only.

FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in PSPCL CRA 314/26 Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 116 vacancies across 10 posts including Fireman (35), Laboratory Assistant (14), Bulldozer Operator (12), Electrician Grade-1 (12), Loco Shunter Operator (12), Technician Grade-1/Mechanical (11), Technician Grade-1/Instrumentation (10), APA/Mechanical (07), Welder Grade-1 (02), and Certified Welder (01).
Q2What is the last date to apply for PSPCL CRA 314/26 Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for online registration and fee deposit is 01-07-2026. The extended last date for fee deposit only is 06-07-2026.
Q3Is Punjabi language knowledge mandatory for all posts in PSPCL CRA 314/26?
A
Yes. Knowledge/qualification of Punjabi is essential for all posts. Candidates must have passed Punjabi at least at Matriculation level or equivalent. For Group C posts (except Certified Welder and APA/Mechanical), a mandatory Punjabi qualifying test (Part-I) is included in the CBT; candidates must score a minimum of 50% (25/50) to qualify.
Q4What is the application fee for PSPCL CRA 314/26 Recruitment 2026?
A
General/OBC/EWS/XSM/FF/SP candidates must pay Rs. 1,200/- + 18% GST = Rs. 1,416/- (plus bank charges if applicable). SC and PwD candidates must pay Rs. 750/- + 18% GST = Rs. 885/- (plus bank charges if applicable). Fee is non-refundable.
Q5What is the age limit for PSPCL CRA 314/26 Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 18 and 37 years of age as on 01-01-2026. SC/BC candidates get 5 years relaxation, PwD candidates get 10 years relaxation, and certain women categories (widows, divorced, etc.) have an upper limit of 40 years.
Q6What is the exam pattern for Group C posts in PSPCL CRA 314/26?
A
Group C posts (Lab Assistant, Fireman, Technician Grade-1, Welder Grade-1, Electrician Grade-1) have a two-part CBT: Part-I is a 50-question mandatory Punjabi qualifying test (min. 50% required, no negative marking); Part-II has 100 questions covering the concerned discipline, Punjabi language, GK, Reasoning, and General English (negative marking of 0.25 per wrong answer). Total duration is 3 hours.
Q7Is there a physical test for the Fireman post in PSPCL CRA 314/26?
A
Yes. Candidates who qualify the CBT for the Fireman post must additionally pass a prescribed physical test: running 100 yards with a 60 kg weight in one minute, lifting the hook ladder from the 3rd and 6th round, and climbing a rope or vertical pipe to 8–10 feet. Physical standards include height of 5'5", chest 33.5"/35", and vision 6/6 without glasses (height and vision only for women).
Q8Can a candidate apply for more than one post in PSPCL CRA 314/26?
A
Yes, but a separate online application and separate fee payment is required for each post. A single application and fee does not cover multiple posts.
Q9How is the merit list prepared for PSPCL CRA 314/26?
A
The merit list is prepared category-wise based on marks scored in the CBT only. If the exam is held in multiple shifts, score normalization will be applied to neutralize differences in difficulty levels across shifts. In case of equal marks, the higher-age candidate will be placed higher in merit.
Q10What is the reservation policy for PSPCL CRA 314/26?
A
Reservation benefits for all categories (SC, BC, EWS, XSM, PwD, Sports Person, Freedom Fighter, Women) are applicable exclusively for Punjab Domicile candidates. OBC/BC Creamy Layer candidates are not entitled to BC reservation and must apply in the General category. EWS candidates must possess a valid Income and Asset Certificate for FY 2025-26, valid for FY 2026-27.
