Notification DatePost Date: 22/05/2026

Tripura Board of Joint Entrance Examination (TBJEE) has officially released the TBJEE Result 2026 for the Tripura Joint Entrance Examination conducted for admission to Engineering, Agriculture, Veterinary, and Paramedical degree courses in the state. The entrance examination was held across multiple centres in Tripura for the 2026 academic session, and the result has now been made available online for all registered candidates.

TBJEE Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - tbjee.tripura.gov.in. Candidates can download their TBJEE 2026 scorecard and rank card by logging into the portal using their registered username, application number, and password as issued during the online application process. After downloading the result, candidates are advised to carefully verify their name, roll number, category, marks obtained, and rank, and immediately contact TBJEE or the concerned authority in case of any discrepancy.

TBJEE Result 2026 Key Highlights

How to Check TBJEE Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your TBJEE Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official TBJEE result portal at https://jeeonline.tripura.gov.in/.
  • On the homepage, click on the link for "TBJEE Result 2026" or "TJEE 2026 Result / Rank Card".
  • Enter your username/application number and password in the login window.
  • Submit the details to access your TBJEE 2026 scorecard and rank card online.
  • Check your marks, rank, and personal details displayed on the screen carefully.
  • Download the result PDF or scorecard and take a printout for counselling and admission purposes.
