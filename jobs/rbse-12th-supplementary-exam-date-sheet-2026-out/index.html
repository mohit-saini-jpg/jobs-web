RBSE 12th Supplementary Exam Date Sheet 2026 OUT

The Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (RBSE) has officially released the RBSE 12th Supplementary Exam Date Sheet 2026 for Class 12 compartment examinations. The supplementary exams are scheduled from May 14 to May 16, 2026, in a single morning shift from 8:30 AM to 11:45 AM. Students who could not clear one or more subjects in the main exams can now check the complete schedule on the official website rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in.

Students can download the official RBSE 12th Supplementary Time Table 2026 PDF using the direct link provided below. The date sheet includes subject codes, exam dates, and important instructions for regular and CwSN candidates.

How to Download RBSE 12th Supplementary Exam Date Sheet 2026

Students can easily download the RBSE 12th Supplementary Exam Date Sheet 2026 from the official RBSE website. Follow the simple steps given below to access the timetable quickly.

  • Visit the official website of Rajasthan Board at rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, look for the "News Updates" or "Important Announcements" section.
  • Click on the link that reads "Supplementary Time Table 2026" or "12th Supplementary Exam Date Sheet 2026".
  • The PDF file will open on your screen.
  • Click on the download icon or use Ctrl + S to save the PDF on your device.
  • Print a copy of the date sheet for future reference and exam preparation.

Note: Always check the official timetable carefully and keep a printed copy handy for your exam preparation.
