How to Check the Dhanamanjuri University Results 2025?

Dhanamanjuri University Results 2025, students must use the official university portal, choose their course and semester, and enter their roll or registration number to view and download their individual marksheet.​

  • Go to the official Dhanamanjuri University website: dmu.ac.in.​
  • On the homepage, click on the “Results” or “Examination” tab, or check the notifications section for recent result announcements.​
  • Select your course and semester (UG/PG, M.A., M.Sc., M.P.E.S., M.Com., LL.M., B.Sc., etc.) from the available result links.​
  • Enter your roll number, registration number, or other requested credentials as prompted.​
  • Click the “Submit” or “View” button to display your individual result and marksheet.​
  • Download and print your marksheet from the page for future reference.
