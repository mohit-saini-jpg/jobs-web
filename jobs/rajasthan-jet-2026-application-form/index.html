What is Rajasthan JET 2026?

Rajasthan Joint Entrance Test (JET) 2026 is the state-level entrance examination conducted for admission to undergraduate agriculture and allied courses in Rajasthan’s agricultural universities and affiliated colleges. The exam is scheduled for 21 June 2026.

The notification also covers Pre-PG (M.Sc.) and Ph.D. entrance examinations on the same date.

Rajasthan JET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

For Undergraduate JET (B.Sc. Agriculture & Allied Courses): Candidates must have passed 10+2 (Senior Secondary) with required subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics/Agriculture as per stream). Specific eligibility varies by course (Agriculture, Horticulture, Forestry, Food Technology, etc.).
