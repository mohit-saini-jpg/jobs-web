Preparation Tips for GPSC Preliminary Exam 2026

With the exam dates now announced, candidates should focus on smart preparation strategies: 1. Create a Study Schedule: Allocate time for General Studies and your specific subject based on the syllabus. 2. Focus on Previous Year Papers: Solve GPSC previous year question papers to understand the pattern. 3. Take Mock Tests: Practice full-length tests in afternoon shift timing to build stamina. 4. Revise Regularly: Make short notes for current affairs, Gujarat-specific topics, and technical subjects. 5. Stay Updated: Follow official GPSC website for any updates on admit card or changes.

GPSC Admit Card 2026 - When to Download?

The GPSC will release the admit card for each preliminary exam approximately 10-15 days before the respective exam date. Candidates can download their hall tickets from the official website using their application number and date of birth. Documents Required on Exam Day: Printed copy of Admit Card Valid Photo ID Proof (Aadhar Card/PAN Card/Driving License/Voter ID/Passport) Recent passport-size photographs (if specified) Black/Blue ballpoint pen for OMR sheet

What After the GPSC Preliminary Exam?

After successfully appearing in the GPSC preliminary examination, candidates should: 1. Result Declaration: Preliminary results are usually declared within 1-2 months after the exam. 2. Answer Key: Provisional answer keys are released shortly after the exam for objections. 3. Next Stage: Qualified candidates will proceed to Mains Written Examination followed by Interview / Document Verification.
