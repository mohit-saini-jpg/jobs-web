Qualification / Eligibility

Project Co-ordinator: Post Graduate degree in Social Work / Sociology / Child Development / Human Rights, Public Administration / psychology / Psychiatry / Law / Public Health / Community Resource Management from a Recognized University OR Graduate degree in Social Work / Sociology / Child Development / Human Rights, Public Administration / psychology / Psychiatry / Law / Public Health / Community Resource Management from a Recognized University with 2 years experience in project formulation / implementation, monitoring and supervision preferably in the field of Women and Child Development / Social Welfare. Proficiency in Computers. Preference may be given to personnel of working in Emergency Helplines. Supervisor: Graduate degree in Social Work / Computer Science / Information Technology / Community Sociology / Social Sciences from a recognized University. Proficiency in computers. Preference may be given to personnel having experience of working in Emergency Helpline. Protection Officer (Institutional Care): Post Graduate Degree in Social Work / Sociology / Child Development / Human Rights, Public Administration / Psychology / Psychiatry / Public Health / Community Resource Management from a recognized University OR Graduate Degree in Social Work / Sociology / Child Development / Human Rights, Public Administration / Psychology / Psychiatry / Public Health / Community Resource Management from a recognized University with 2 years experience in Project Formulation / Implementation, Monitoring and Supervision preferably in the field of Women and Child Development / Social Welfare. Proficiency in computers.
