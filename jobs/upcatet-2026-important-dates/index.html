What is UPCATET 2026?

UPCATET stands for Uttar Pradesh Combined Agriculture and Technology Entrance Test. It is conducted by Banda University of Agriculture and Technology (BUAT) for admissions into various UG, PG, and PhD courses in Agriculture, Horticulture, Forestry, Home Science, Veterinary Science, and other allied fields offered by state agricultural universities in Uttar Pradesh.

How to Apply for UPCATET 2026 – Step-by-Step Process

  • Visit the official website: upcatetexam.org
  • Click on “New Registration” and fill basic details (name, date of birth, category, mobile number, email ID).
  • Verify your mobile/email with the OTP received.
  • Login using registration number and password.
  • Fill the detailed application form: personal information, educational qualifications, choice of programme, and exam city.
  • Upload scanned documents in the prescribed format and size.
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Review all entered details carefully and submit the form.
  • Download and take a printout of the confirmation page for future reference.

Application Correction: Limited corrections can be made on 02 May 2026.

UPCATET 2026 Exam Pattern (Overview)

The exam is conducted in offline (pen-and-paper) mode. It consists of multiple-choice questions from relevant subjects depending on the chosen group (e.g., Agriculture, Biology, Mathematics for UG). There is usually one mark per correct answer with negative marking for wrong answers. Detailed pattern and syllabus will be available in the official notification/brochure on the website.
