Central Sanskrit University Recruitment 2026: 90 Teaching : Job Overview

Central Sanskrit University Recruitment 2026: 90 Teaching : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Applicants who want to know the exact application steps can follow this simple process, which is entirely online except for the final hard-copy submission.
  • 2Visit the CU Chayan Portal at curec.samarth.ac.in or access it via www.sanskrit.nic.in under "Recruitment/Notification."
  • 3Register yourself on the portal to generate a registration number.
  • 4Select the post/subject you wish to apply for and fill in the online application form.
  • 5Upload clear, high-quality scanned copies of all required documents, certificates, and publications.
  • 6Pay the prescribed application/examination fee online based on your category and post level.
  • 7Submit the application before 10 July 2026, 11:30 PM IST (no correction window is available after submission).
  • 8Take a printout of the application and fee receipt, sign it, attach self-attested document copies, and superscribe the envelope with the Advertisement Number and post/subject applied for.
  • 9Send the hard copy by registered post to "The Registrar, Central Sanskrit University, 56-57, Institutional Area, Janakpuri, New Delhi-110058" so it reaches within 15 days from the online application closing date.
  • 10Candidates already employed in Govt./PSU/Autonomous Bodies must additionally submit a No Objection Certificate (NOC) and, where applicable, a Vigilance Clearance Certificate from their employer.

Central Sanskrit University Recruitment 2026: 90 Teaching : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for CSU Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application process started on 10 June 2026 and will close on 10 July 2026 at 11:30 PM IST, after which the application link will be disabled.
Q2How many vacancies are available in CSU Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 90 vacancies are available - 23 for Associate Professor (Level-13A) posts and 67 for Assistant Professor/Other Academic (Level-10) posts.
Q3What is the application fee for CSU Recruitment 2026?
A
For Level-10 posts, the fee is ₹1500 for UR/OBC/EWS and ₹500 for SC/ST/Women/PwBD/Transgender. For Level-13A posts, it is ₹2000 for UR/OBC/EWS and ₹1000 for SC/ST/Women/PwBD/Transgender.
Q4How can I apply for CSU Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted online through the CU Chayan Portal at curec.samarth.ac.in, followed by sending a signed hard copy with documents to the Registrar, CSU, within 15 days of the online deadline.
Q5Will there be a written exam for these posts?
A
A written exam (OMR-based MCQs plus a Descriptive Test) is conducted only for Assistant Professor (Level-10) posts for shortlisting. No written exam is conducted for Associate Professor (Level-13A) posts.
Q6Which campuses are these posts for?
A
The posts are for three campuses: Vedvyas Campus (Himachal Pradesh), Shri Raghunath Kirti Campus (Uttarakhand), and Ekalavya Campus (Tripura), and are campus-specific.
Q7Is there a minimum service period after joining?
A
Yes, selected candidates must serve at their initial campus of posting for a minimum period of 10 years (or as prescribed by the University), and personal transfer requests during this period are normally not entertained.
Q8What examination centres are available?
A
Candidates can choose from Delhi, Mumbai/Pune, Kolkata, or Bengaluru as their examination centre, irrespective of the campus applied for.
Q9What is the OBC certificate validity requirement?
A
The NCL-OBC certificate must be issued on or after 01.04.2026; certificates issued before this date will not be considered valid for this advertisement.
Q10Where can I check the official CSU Recruitment notification?
A
The complete detailed advertisement (No. 04/2026) is available on the official website www.sanskrit.nic.in under the "Recruitment/Notification" section.
