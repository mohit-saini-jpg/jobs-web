How to Check IITG Results 2026 Online?

IITG has made the result download process simple and quick for students. Candidates only need their GATE 2026 application number and password to view results on the official portal. Here is a step-by-step process you can follow:

  • Visit the Official Website - Go to the official IITG exam portal at iitg.ac.in.
  • Find the Results Section - Click on the GATE 2026 Results link on the homepage.
  • Select Your Exam - Choose GATE 2026 from the list of available results.
  • Enter Your Details - Input your GATE 2026 Application Number and Password in the required fields.
  • Submit & View - Click the Submit button to view your IITG result 2026 on screen.
  • Download the Scorecard - Click Download or Print to save your result PDF for official use.
  • Verify Your Details - Check that your name, application number, and marks are correctly reflected on the scorecard.
