Job Details

Latest Jobs: Punjab & Sind Bank has invited online applications for the recruitment of Apprentices across India. As per the official notification, the bank will fill a total of 635 apprentice vacancies through a merit-based selection process.

The online application process began on May 21, 2026, and will continue till June 6, 2026. Interested candidates are advised to carefully read the official notification before applying.

Candidates applying for the apprenticeship programme must be graduates and should be between 20 and 28 years of age as on May 1, 2026. Age relaxation will be applicable for reserved category candidates as per government norms.

The selection of candidates will be based on merit, followed by document verification. Selected candidates will undergo one year of apprenticeship training.

Candidates are advised to download and read the official notification PDF thoroughly, as it contains complete details regarding eligibility, application procedure, selection process, and other important instructions.
