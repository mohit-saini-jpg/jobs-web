FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in Punjab and Haryana High Court Clerk Recruitment 2026?
A
The High Court of Punjab and Haryana has announced a total of 1265 Clerk vacancies under the Punjab and Haryana High Court Clerk Recruitment 2026. This includes 1015 existing posts and 250 anticipated vacancies up to June 2026. The number of vacancies may change depending on administrative requirements at the time of final selection
Q2What is the eligibility criteria for Haryana SSSC Clerk 2026?
A
Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree (BA, BSc, or equivalent) from a recognized university along with basic computer proficiency in Word Processing and Spreadsheets. Additionally, applicants must have studied Hindi or Sanskrit at Matric level or higher. All qualifications must be completed before 23 June 2026.
Q3What is the age limit for Punjab and Haryana High Court Clerk Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 42 years for General category candidates as on 01 January 2026. Reserved categories like SC, BC, PwBD, and others are eligible for age relaxation as per Haryana government rules. However, the upper age limit cannot exceed 52 years after all relaxations.
Q4What is the last date to apply for Punjab and Haryana High Court Clerk Vacancy 2026?
A
The online application process will start on 26 May 2026 at 4:00 PM and will remain open until 23 June 2026 at 4:00 PM. Candidates are advised to complete their application well before the deadline to avoid last-minute technical issues. Late submissions will not be accepted under any circumstances.
Q5What is the selection process for Haryana High Court Clerk Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of three stages: Computer Based Test (CBT), Computer Proficiency Test (CPT), and document verification. The final merit list is prepared based on marks obtained in the CBT only. The CPT is qualifying in nature and must be cleared to proceed further.
Q6What is the exam pattern of Punjab and Haryana High Court Clerk CBT 2026?
A
The CBT includes General Knowledge and English Composition sections with both objective and subjective questions. The exam carries 100 marks with a duration of 2 hours, and there is negative marking of 1/4 for incorrect answers. Candidates must score at least 33% in each section and 40% overall to qualify.
Q7Is there any application fee for Haryana Clerk Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, the application fee varies based on category and gender. General category males need to pay Rs. 1200, while females pay Rs. 875. Haryana reserved categories pay Rs. 710, and PwBD candidates are exempted from paying any fee.
Q8Who can apply for Punjab and Haryana High Court Clerk posts under reserved categories?
A
Only bonafide residents of Haryana are eligible to claim reservation benefits under SC, BC, EWS, ESM, and other categories. Candidates from other states must apply under the General category even if they belong to a reserved category. Valid and updated certificates are mandatory at the time of application.
Q9What is the salary of Haryana High Court Clerk 2026?
A
The pay scale for Clerk posts will be as per the latest rules and instructions issued by the Government of Haryana. Along with basic pay, selected candidates will also receive allowances and benefits applicable to state government employees. The job is permanent and offers stable career growth.
Q10How to apply online for Punjab and Haryana High Court Clerk Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online through the official website sssc.gov.in by completing registration, filling the application form, uploading documents, and paying the fee. It is important to keep scanned documents, a valid email ID, and a working mobile number ready before applying. The application is considered complete only after successful fee payment.
