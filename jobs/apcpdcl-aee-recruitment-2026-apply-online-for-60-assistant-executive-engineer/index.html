Selection Process – APCPDCL AEE Recruitment 2026

The APCPDCL AEE 2026 selection is based entirely on a Computer Based Test (CBT) conducted in English medium only - there is no interview stage. The CBT is jointly conducted for APCPDCL, APTRANSCO, APEPDCL, APSPDCL, and APGENCO, and candidates are allotted to utilities based on merit, reservation, local candidature, and preferences submitted in the online application. There are no negative marks in the CBT. Scoring the minimum qualifying marks does not guarantee a call for certificate verification. The merit list is prepared strictly in the 1:1 ratio against notified vacancies, following the Rule of Reservation and presidential order roster points. Where two candidates have equal marks, the older candidate gets priority; if age is also equal, the candidate who acquired the qualifying degree earlier is ranked higher. Service Bond and Consent Deposit – APCPDCL AEE 2026

Every selected candidate must execute a Service Bond on Non-Judicial Stamp Paper worth ₹110 at the time of joining. The bond obligates the candidate to serve APCPDCL for a minimum of 5 years after completion of the 2-year probation period - making the total bond period 7 years . Original certificates (degree, date of birth, community) must be deposited with APCPDCL for the entire bond period and will not be returned during this time. If a candidate needs original certificates during the bond period for a bonafide purpose, they must deposit ₹1,00,000 as security before collecting them, and re-deposit the certificates immediately after use. Candidates who resign before completing 5 years post-probation must pay ₹10,00,000 (₹10 lakhs) as liquidated damages to APCPDCL. All selected candidates must also undergo mandatory Induction Training before receiving their posting orders.
