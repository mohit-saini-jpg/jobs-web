How to Apply

  • 1Submit application in duplicate, addressed to "Station Headquarters (ECHS), Mhow".
  • 2Attach two sets of self-attested photocopies of all applicable documents: Educational certificates (10th, 12th, Diploma, Graduation, Post Graduation), Attempt Certificate, Computer course certificates, PPO, Discharge Book, ESM Identity Card, Experience Certificates, Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Registration Certificate, Police Verification Certificate, and two recent passport-size photographs.
  • 3Attach a Medical Fitness Certificate duly signed by the Medical Officer of the dependent Polyclinic (countersigned by SEMO for ESM candidates, and by CMO for others).
  • 4Ensure the application is complete in all respects — incomplete applications will be rejected forthwith.
  • 5Submit the application on or before June 15, 2026.
  • 6Candidates can apply for employment at more than one polyclinic. If placed on the reserve list, they may be appointed at any polyclinic if a vacancy arises at a later stage, subject to willingness.
