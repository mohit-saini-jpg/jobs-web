Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Andaman Law College Guest Faculty/Resource Person (Law) recruitment?

The last date to receive the application for the Andaman Law College Guest Faculty/Resource Person (Law) recruitment is 10th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the college before this deadline.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Guest Faculty/Resource Person (Law) post?

Candidates must possess a Master's degree with 55% marks in Law, and have cleared NET/SLET/SET or be awarded a Ph.D. as per UGC Regulations.

Q3. What is the application mode for this recruitment?

The application mode for this recruitment is offline. Candidates need to download the application form and send it via post.

Q4. What is the salary offered for the Guest Faculty/Resource Person (Law) position?

The salary offered is Rs. 1500/- per lecture session (normally two hours), with a maximum limit of Rs. 50,000/- per month.

Q5. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) in the official notification.

Q6. What is the selection process for the Guest Faculty/Resource Person (Law) post?

The selection process involves Academic Scoring as per UGC 2018 Table 3B, followed by the preparation of a Merit List.

Q7. Where can I download the application form?

The application form can be downloaded from the official website of Andaman Law College: https://andamanlawcollege.andamannicobar.gov.in.

Q8. What documents should I attach with the application form?

You should attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and relevant certificates as per the notification.
