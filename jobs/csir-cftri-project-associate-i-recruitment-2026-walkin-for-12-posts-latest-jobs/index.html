FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for CSIR-CFTRI Project Associate - I Recruitment 2026?
A
The walk-in interviews are scheduled to be held on 16th June 2026 and 17th June 2026. Candidates should ensure they report to the venue on these dates.
Q2How many vacancies are available for the Project Associate - I post?
A
A total of 12 vacancies are available for the Project Associate - I position at CSIR-CFTRI, Mysuru, as per Advertisement No. PAT 24/2026.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for the Project Associate - I posts?
A
The educational qualifications vary by specialization and include M.Sc. degrees in Microbiology, Biotechnology, Biochemistry, Chemistry, Polymer Science, Food Science & Nutrition, or Food Technology, as well as B.E. or B.Tech degrees in Computer Science (with AI&ML) or Polymer Science/Food Science & Technology.
Q4What is the age limit for the Project Associate - I posts?
A
The maximum age limit for all Project Associate - I posts is 35 years. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST/PwBD/Women and OBC categories as per government norms.
Q5What is the salary offered for the Project Associate - I position?
A
The selected candidates will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 25,000/- per month, plus House Rent Allowance (HRA) as applicable.
Q6Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q7What is the mode of application for this recruitment?
A
The mode of application is Walk-in. Candidates need to attend the interview directly at the specified venue and time.
Q8What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents, including ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates (if any), and category certificates (if applicable), along with one set of self-attested photocopies.
Q9Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?
A
The walk-in interview will be conducted at CSIR – Central Food Technological Research Institute, Mysuru, Karnataka - 570020.
Q10What is the selection process for the Project Associate - I posts?
A
The selection process will involve a Walk-in Interview followed by Document Verification of the shortlisted candidates.
