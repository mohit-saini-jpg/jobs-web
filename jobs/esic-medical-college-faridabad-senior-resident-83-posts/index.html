FAQs

Q1What is the Walk-in Interview date for ESIC Medical College Faridabad Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?
A
The Walk-in Interview and Document Verification for Senior Resident (Broad Speciality Departments) on contract basis at ESIC Medical College & Hospital, Faridabad, NIT-3 is scheduled on 08 June 2026. Candidates must report between 9:00 AM and 10:00 AM at the Faculty Reading Hall, Academic Block, ESIC Medical College & Hospital, Faridabad. Candidates reporting after 10:00 AM will not be entertained.
Q2What is the total number of vacancies and which departments are included in ESIC Faridabad Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 83 vacancies (provisional) have been announced across 19 departments including Anesthesia, Anatomy, Biochemistry, Chest, Forensic Medicine, ICU, General Medicine, NICU, OBGY, Pediatrics, Pharmacology, Physiology, PICU, Radiology, Reproductive Medicine, Radiotherapy, General Surgery, and others. Vacancies are subject to increase or decrease as per actual patient care requirements.
Q3What is the age limit for ESIC Medical College Faridabad Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit is not exceeding 45 years as on the date of Walk-in Interview, i.e., 08.06.2026. Age relaxations as per Government of India guidelines are applicable for reserved category candidates (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwD) wherever applicable.
Q4What is the salary/remuneration for Senior Resident at ESIC Medical College Faridabad?
A
The consolidated pay for Senior Resident is ₹1,48,669/- per month and for Non-PG Resident (ICU only, in absence of PG candidates) is ₹1,23,196/- per month, as on the date of advertisement. Pay will be determined as per ESIC HQ Office Memorandum No. Z-11012/51/2022-MED-VI dated 08.12.2022 or subsequent orders. Senior Residents are not entitled to PF, Pension, Gratuity, Medical Allowance, or similar service benefits.
Q5What is the nature and duration of contract for ESIC Faridabad Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?
A
The contract is initially for 01 (one) year, extendable up to 03 years subject to satisfactory performance, willingness, conduct of the candidate, and institutional requirements. The contract will be terminated upon appointment/joining of a bonded Senior Resident on the same post. The competent authority reserves the right to terminate services at any time in public interest without prior notice.
Q6What is the application fee for ESIC Medical College Faridabad Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is ₹500/- for all categories except SC/ST/ESIC Regular Employees/Female Candidates/PH (PwD) candidates, for whom the fee is NIL. The fee must be paid via a Demand Draft in favour of "ESI Fund A/c No. 1" payable at Faridabad. A separate application with applicable fee must be submitted for each post applied for. Fee once paid is non-refundable.
Q7Is there a security deposit for ESIC Faridabad Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. Selected candidates must submit a Security Deposit of ₹20,000/- via Demand Draft payable to "ESI Fund A/c No. 1" at Faridabad upon joining. The deposit will be refunded on successful completion of the contract period and submission of a "No Dues Certificate." If the selected candidate resigns within six months of joining, the security deposit will be forfeited, except in cases of unforeseen or unavoidable circumstances.
Q8Can candidates apply for multiple departments in ESIC Faridabad Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. Candidates may apply for multiple posts listed in this advertisement if they meet the eligibility criteria for each. However, a separate application form along with required documents and applicable application fee must be submitted for each post applied for.
Q9What documents are required for the Walk-in Interview at ESIC Medical College Faridabad?
A
Candidates must bring the completed offline application form (in prescribed format) along with self-attested copies and originals of: Class 10th Certificate (for Date of Birth), MBBS Marks Sheets and Degree Certificate, MD/MS/DNB Marks Sheets and Degree Certificate, Updated NMC/State Medical Registration Certificate, Valid Category Certificate (EWS/OBC-NCL/SC/ST/PwD, if applicable), Experience Certificate, recent Passport Size Photograph, Relieving/NOC Certificate from previous/current employer (if applicable), and Aadhaar Card. If the category certificate is not available, the candidate may be treated as UR candidate.
Q10Is private practice allowed for Senior Residents at ESIC Medical College Faridabad?
A
No. Private practice is strictly not allowed in any capacity after joining as Senior Resident at ESIC Medical College & Hospital, Faridabad. Additionally, absence from work exceeding 07 days without prior approval from the competent authority will be treated as voluntary abandonment of engagement, resulting in automatic termination of the contract. Attendance must be recorded both manually and through the Aadhaar Enabled Biometric Attendance System (AEBAS) on a daily basis during scheduled working days.
