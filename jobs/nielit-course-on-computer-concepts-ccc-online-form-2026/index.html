Job Overview

National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology NIELIT is a basic IT literacy program by the National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (Government of India) to teach essential computer skills like document preparation, internet browsing, emailing, presentations, and digital services. Duration is 80 hours (30 theory + 60 practicals). The online exam is held every month, with registration typically starting on the 1st of the preceding month and the last date being the last day of every month.
