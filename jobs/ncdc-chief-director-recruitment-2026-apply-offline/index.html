Qualification / Eligibility

Chief Director (Human Resources): Master's Degree/ Executive MBA/ Post Graduate Diploma (2-years duration) in Human Resources with 15 years of experience in the relevant field, of which minimum 5 years must be in Pay Level-12 or equivalent. Chief Director (General Management): Master's Degree/ Executive MBA/ Post Graduate Diploma (2-years duration) in Cooperative Management/ Cooperative Development/ Agri-Business/ Rural Development with 15 years of experience in the relevant field, of which minimum 5 years must be in Pay Level-12 or equivalent. Chief Director (Sugar): Masters degree/Post Graduate Diploma in Sugar Engineering/ Sugar Technology with 15 years of experience in the relevant field, of which minimum 5 years must be in Pay Level-12 or equivalent. Chief Director (MIS): B.Tech in Computer Science/ Information Technology or MCA with 15 years of experience in the areas of Security/ Analytics/ Database Administration/ Software Development/ Networking/ Server Management/ Database Analysis etc., of which minimum 5 years must be in Pay Level-12 or equivalent.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the NCDC Chief Director posts?
A
The last date for the submission of applications for the NCDC Chief Director posts is 25th June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well in advance to avoid any last-minute issues.
Q2How many vacancies are available for the Chief Director post?
A
There are a total of 4 vacancies announced for the Chief Director posts in the National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) through this recruitment drive.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for the Chief Director posts?
A
The required educational qualification varies by specialization. Generally, it includes a Master's Degree, Executive MBA, or Post Graduate Diploma in relevant fields, or a B.Tech in Computer Science/Information Technology or MCA for the MIS post.
Q4What is the maximum age limit for applying to these posts?
A
The maximum age limit for all Chief Director posts is 50 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per the government rules and NCDC recruitment regulations.
Q5What is the salary offered for the Chief Director position?
A
The selected candidates will be placed in Pay Level-13, with a pay scale of ₹1,23,100 - ₹2,15,900/- per month. The approximate Cost to Company (CTC) is around ₹40.4 LPA.
Q6What is the application fee for NCDC Chief Director recruitment?
A
The application fee for General/OBC/EWS/Ex-Servicemen/Departmental candidates is Rs. 1200/-. SC/ST/Persons with Benchmark Disabilities are exempted from paying the application fee.
Q7How can I apply for the NCDC Chief Director posts?
A
The application process is offline. Candidates need to download the application form from the official website, fill it out, attach necessary documents, and send it via registered post or courier to the specified address.
Q8What is the selection process for the NCDC Chief Director recruitment?
A
The selection process typically involves preliminary selection based on academic records and experience, followed by a Written Test/Skill Test, and finally an Interview.
Q9Is there any experience required for these posts?
A
Yes, candidates are required to have 15 years of experience in the relevant field, with a minimum of 5 years in Pay Level-12 or equivalent, depending on the specific Chief Director post.
Q10Where can I find the official notification and application form?

The official notification and application form can be downloaded from the official website of NCDC, www.ncdc.in. The direct link to the notification PDF is also provided in the Important Links section of this article.
