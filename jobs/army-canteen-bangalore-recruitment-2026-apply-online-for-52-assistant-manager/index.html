Army Canteen Bangalore Recruitment 2026

Army Canteen Bangalore Recruitment 2026 has been officially announced by the Golden Palm Station Canteen (GPSC), Bangalore, under HQ K & K Sub Area, for 52 posts across the canteen and its extension counters in Karnataka. The notification, dated 01 July 2026, invites applications from retired Honorary Captains/Lieutenants, JCOs, NCOs, and dependents of Ex-Servicemen (ESM) or serving personnel, both male and female. Interested and eligible candidates can apply by 31 July 2026 through registered post or email. Eligibility Criteria for Army Canteen Bangalore Recruitment 2026

As per the official notice, the following categories of candidates are eligible to apply: Retired Honorary Captains/Lieutenants Junior Commissioned Officers (JCOs) Non-Commissioned Officers (NCOs) Dependents/Wards of Ex-Servicemen (ESM) Dependents/Wards of Serving Personnel Both Male and Female candidates can apply The notice does not specify a separate civilian-only category for open recruitment; however, it does mention documentation requirements applicable to civilian applicants (see Required Documents section), indicating that dependents/civilians connected to ESM families are eligible as per the listed post requirements.

Educational Qualification

The official notice does not specify post-wise educational qualification details within the notice itself. It states that complete details of eligibility criteria are available on the official GPC website. Army Canteen Bangalore Recruitment 2026 : Age Limit

The notice does not mention a specific age limit for any of the posts. Candidates should refer to the detailed eligibility criteria available on the official GPC website (gpcscblr.co.in) for age-related requirements, as this information has not been disclosed in the notice provided.

Salary / Honorarium Structure

Selected candidates will be paid an approximate honorarium ranging from Rs 20,000/- to Rs 32,000/- per month, depending on the post and canteen location. The highest honorarium of Rs 32,000/- is offered for the post of Joint Manager (Accounts) at GPSC Bangalore, while the Watchman post at GPEC KGF carries the lowest honorarium of Rs 20,000/-. Refer to the vacancy table above for the exact honorarium applicable to each post.
