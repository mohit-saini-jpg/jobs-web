How to Apply

  • 1Pay the application fee through NEFT to the designated SBI account and save the transaction proof.
  • 2Download the soft copy of Application Form (Appendix-I), Career Graph (Appendix-II in MS Word/PDF format) and Presentation (Appendix-III in PPT format) from the official website.
  • 3Fill in the Application Form and Career Graph in the prescribed format. Rename files as per the prescribed naming convention (e.g. "Name Post Department.pdf").
  • 4Submit the Google Form at https://forms.gle/F262hswPdmZ6j2st5 by 20 June 2026 (05:00 PM) along with scanned copies of all required documents and fee transaction proof.
  • 5After submitting the Google Form, send the duly filled and signed hard copy of the application (Appendix-I) along with self-attested copies of all certificates (age, qualification, experience, caste/EWS/PwBD certificate, NOC, fee payment proof) to: Deputy Director (Administration), Administrative Block, 3rd Floor, AIIMS, Kothipura, Bilaspur, Himachal Pradesh – 174037 so as to reach on or before 25 June 2026 (05:00 PM).
  • 6Super-scribe the envelope as "Application for the post of ………, Department of ……… for AIIMS, Bilaspur (H.P.)"
  • 7Candidates applying for more than one post must submit a separate application for each post.
