District Panchayat Narayanpur Gram Panchayat Secretary : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for the Gram Panchayat Secretary post?
A
The last date to submit your application for the Gram Panchayat Secretary position is 20th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the concerned authority before this deadline.
Q2What is the educational qualification required for this recruitment?
A
Candidates must have passed Higher Secondary education and possess a 1-year Diploma in Data Entry Operator/Programming from a recognized institution to be eligible for this post.
Q3What is the age limit for the Gram Panchayat Secretary post?
A
The age limit for applicants is between 18 years and 35 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per Chhattisgarh government rules for reserved categories.
Q4How many vacancies are available for the Gram Panchayat Secretary position?
A
There are a total of 14 vacancies announced for the Gram Panchayat Secretary post in District Panchayat Narayanpur.
Q5What is the application mode for this recruitment?
A
The application process for this recruitment is offline. Candidates need to download the application form and send it via post.
Q6What is the salary offered for the Gram Panchayat Secretary role?
A
The selected candidates will receive a salary ranging from Rs. 3500 to Rs. 10000 per month, along with a Grade Pay of Rs. 1100.
Q7What is the selection process for the Gram Panchayat Secretary recruitment?
A
The selection will be based on merit from Higher Secondary marks (50%), higher educational qualifications (max 10 marks), a Computer Skill Test (25%), and experience as Rozgar Sahayak (max 15 marks).
Q8Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q9Where can I download the application form?
A
The application form can be downloaded from the official website of District Panchayat Narayanpur, which is www.narayanpur.gov.in.
Q10What documents are required to be submitted with the application form?
A
You need to attach self-attested copies of required documents such as a photograph, signature, ID proof, and educational certificates with your application form.
