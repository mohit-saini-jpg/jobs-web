5paragraphStep‑by‑Step Guide to Download the Admit Card
6listordered
  • Open the official website of Purnea University (https://purneau.ac.in) in your browser.
  • Go to the “Examination” or “Student Corner” section and click on the latest “Admit Card” or “Download Admit Card” link for your course/semester.
  • You will be redirected to the admit card portal (examinationpup.in) or a dedicated student login page.
  • Select your course (BA/BSc/BCom), semester/part and college, if those options appear on the page.
  • Enter your registration number, roll number, or user ID along with your password/date of birth (as asked on the form).
  • Click on the “Search”, “Login” or “Submit” button to fetch your hall ticket.
  • Your Purnea University Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen with all your exam details.
  • Download the admit card in PDF format and take one or more clear printouts on A4 paper. Keep one copy for backup in case the first one gets damaged.
7paragraphIf the page does not open, shows an error, or your details are not found, wait for some time and try again or contact your college examination in‑charge.
