Courses Offered in PGI Chandigarh B.Sc Nursing Admission 2026

National Institute of Nursing Education (NINE), PGIMER, Chandigarh offers two prestigious nursing programmes – B.Sc. Nursing (4 Years) exclusively for female candidates and B.Sc. Nursing (Post Basic) for both male and female candidates. These courses are designed to prepare highly skilled nursing professionals with strong clinical knowledge, research aptitude, and leadership qualities. The 4-year programme follows the latest Indian Nursing Council (INC) guidelines with semester system. The Post Basic programme is of 2 years duration and focuses on advanced nursing practice. Both programmes provide excellent clinical exposure at Nehru Hospital, PGIMER and prepare students for rewarding careers in healthcare.
