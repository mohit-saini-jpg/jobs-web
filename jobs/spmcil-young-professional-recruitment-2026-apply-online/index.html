About SPMCIL

SPMCIL is a Miniratna Category-I Central Public Sector Enterprise wholly owned by the Government of India, functioning as a corporatized entity since 13 January 2006 under the Department of Economic Affairs, Ministry of Finance. Its core business includes designing and manufacturing security papers, printing currency notes, passports, non-judicial stamp papers, and postage stamps, along with minting coins. The Company's operational units are located across the country, with four Mints at Mumbai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, and Noida, currency/security presses at Nashik, Dewas, and Hyderabad, and a paper manufacturing mill at Narmadapuram.

SPMCIL Young Professional Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection for SPMCIL Young Professional posts is a two-stage process combining an online test and a personal interview, with a fixed weightage assigned to each stage. Candidates need to clear the online test to be eligible for the interview round. Candidates who fulfill the minimum eligibility criteria may be called for an online test . Candidates in the ratio of 1:4 (based on online test performance) will then be called for an interview . The online test carries 75% weightage , and the interview carries 25% weightage in the final selection. The online examination for Young Professional (Legal/Finance/Technical) will be conducted at centers in Delhi NCR only , while the examination for Young Professional (Cyber Security) will be conducted at centers in Delhi NCR and Hyderabad only . Terms of Engagement

Candidates should understand that this is a temporary, fixed-term engagement and not a route to regular employment with SPMCIL. The key terms are outlined below. Young Professionals are engaged for a fixed period to provide high-quality services for specific, time-bound jobs, on a full-time basis . Engaged candidates are not permitted to take up any other assignment during their period of engagement with SPMCIL. The engagement is of a temporary (non-official) nature and can be cancelled at any time without assigning any reason. The engagement does not confer any right to future regular employment in SPMCIL and is to be treated strictly as a fixed-term contract engagement. The tenure is 3 years ; however, based on performance, it can be terminated earlier or further extended.
