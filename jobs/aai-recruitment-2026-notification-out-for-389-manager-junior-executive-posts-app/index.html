AAI Recruitment 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility is discipline-specific, covering educational qualification and, for Manager posts, post-qualification work experience. Junior Executive posts require no prior work experience (Nil), while Manager posts generally require 3 years of relevant post-qualification experience.

AAI Recruitment 2026 Application Fee

Application Fee: Rs. 1,000 (inclusive of GST), payable through online mode only. SC candidates, ST candidates, PwBD candidates, Women candidates, and AAI Apprentices who have completed 1 year of Apprenticeship Training in AAI are exempt from the fee. Fee can be paid via Debit Card, Credit Card, Internet Banking, IMPS, or Cash Cards/Mobile Wallets. Fees once paid are non-refundable under any circumstances; duplicate payments will be refunded after the closing date. Candidates can apply for multiple posts with separate registration and separate fee payment for each post.

AAI Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

Computer Based Test (Objective Type) will be conducted for all posts, with no negative marking for wrong answers. Provisionally eligible candidates will be called for the Computer Based Test based on details furnished in the application form. The Computer Based Test will be followed by Application Verification/Subsequent Tests for all posts. Post Codes 1 to 11 (all Manager posts) will also include an Interview as part of the selection process. For Manager (Fire Services), Application Verification is followed by a Physical Measurement Test, Driving Test, and Physical Endurance Test (Running, Casualty Carrying, Pole Climbing, Rope Climbing, and Ladder Climbing & Descending). Only candidates who qualify these tests proceed to the interview. For Manager and Junior Executive (Airport Operations), a valid Light Motor Vehicle License is essential (temporary/learning license not accepted). Final
