The Meghalaya Board of School Education (MBOSE) has officially was released the MBOSE HSSLC Result 2026 for the academic session 2025-26. Students who appeared for the Class 12 (HSSLC) board examinations can now check their results online. MBOSE Result 2026 has been released on the official website - megresults.nic.in.

The board has published results for Class 12 students across all streams including Science, Commerce, Arts, and Vocational. Students can easily check and download their marksheet and 2-in-1 certificate by entering their roll number on the official result portal.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the result. In case of any discrepancies, they should immediately contact the MBOSE authorities at Shillong or Tura for correction.

How to Check MBOSE HSSLC Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their MBOSE HSSLC Result 2026 from the official website:

  • Visit the official website of Meghalaya Board of School Education at megresults.nic.in or mbose.in
  • On the homepage, look for the HSSLC Result 2026 link for your respective stream (Science, Commerce, Arts, or Vocational)
  • Click on the HSSLC Result 2026 link to proceed
  • A new login window will appear on the screen
  • Enter your Roll Number in the required field as mentioned on your admit card
  • Enter the Captcha code shown on the screen carefully
  • Click on the Submit button to proceed
  • Your MBOSE HSSLC Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen with complete details
  • Check all details including student name, roll number, father's name, mother's name, school name, subject-wise marks (theory and practical), total marks obtained, division, and qualifying status
  • Download the marksheet and certificate (2-in-1) in PDF format
  • Save the downloaded result on your device for future reference
  • Take a printout of the result for admission and scholarship purposes
