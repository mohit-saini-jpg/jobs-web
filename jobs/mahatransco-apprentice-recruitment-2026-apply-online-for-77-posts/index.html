About MAHATRANSCO - Mahapareshan

Maharashtra State Electricity Transmission Company Limited (MAHATRANSCO), also known as Mahapareshan , is a Maharashtra government undertaking responsible for the high-voltage electricity transmission network across the state. Incorporated in 2005 (CIN: U40109MH2005SGC153646), its registered office is at Prakashganga, Plot No. C-19 "E" Block, Bandra-Kurla Complex, Bandra (East), Mumbai - 400 051. The EHV O&M Circle, Panvel, is one of its operational divisions managing extra-high-voltage transmission infrastructure in the Raigad district area. Apprenticeship at MSЕТCL gives candidates direct exposure to EHV substation operations, switchyard maintenance, and electrical transmission systems - invaluable experience for career growth in the power sector.
