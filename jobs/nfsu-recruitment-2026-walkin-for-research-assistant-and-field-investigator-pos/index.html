NFSU Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Research Assistant for NFSU Gandhinagar Campus: M.A/M.Sc. in Clinical/ Forensic Psychology/ Criminology with a minimum 55% aggregated or equivalent grade with NET/MPhil/PhD, along with Proficiency in speaking, reading and writing in Gujarati and English. Desirable Qualification: Two years' research Experience. Field Investigator for NFSU Delhi Campus: M.A/M.Sc. in Clinical/ Forensic Psychology/ Criminology with a minimum 55% aggregated or equivalent grade, along with Proficiency in speaking, reading and writing in Hindi and English. Desirable Qualification: Research Experience. Field Investigator for NFSU Chennai Campus: M.A/M.Sc. in Clinical /Forensic Psychology/ Criminology with a minimum 55% aggregated or equivalent grade, along with Proficiency in speaking, reading and writing in Tamil and English. Desirable Qualification: Research Experience.
