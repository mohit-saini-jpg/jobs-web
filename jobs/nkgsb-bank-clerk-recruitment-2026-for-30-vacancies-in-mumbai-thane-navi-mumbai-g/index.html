Candidates can confirm the exact figure by writing to [email protected].
Q10Is NKGSB Bank a government bank?
A
No, NKGSB Bank is not a government bank. It is a multi-state scheduled cooperative bank - one of the few cooperative banks in India to hold Scheduled Bank status under the Reserve Bank of India Act. It operates under RBI regulation like commercial banks but is structured as a cooperative society, not a public sector undertaking.
