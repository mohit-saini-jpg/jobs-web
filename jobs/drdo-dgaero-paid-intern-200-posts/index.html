The Director General Aeronautical Systems (DRDO DG(Aero)) Recruitment 2026 for posts of 200 Paid Intern. Candidates with B.Tech/B.E, M.E/M.Tech Can Apply Online. How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online through the DRDO website and submit the filled application along with requisite documents to the email ID of the respective lab. Candidates should not change their registered mobile number or email after submission, as vital information regarding shortlisting/selection will be communicated through email/SMS. Candidates may apply to multiple labs by submitting separate applications to each lab's email. Applications with false or incomplete information will be rejected. Applications received after the closing date will not be entertained.
