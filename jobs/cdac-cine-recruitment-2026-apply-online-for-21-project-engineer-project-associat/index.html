Important Instructions

All posts are purely on a contract basis on consolidated emoluments; selected candidates shall NOT derive any right or claim to a regular post or appointment in C-DAC. C-DAC reserves the right to terminate the contract even during the contract period after giving 45 days' prior notice or salary in lieu thereof. Selected candidates may be deputed or transferred from one project to another at any project location across India as per organizational requirements. Academic/teaching/research work and internship/project work as part of the curriculum will NOT be considered as experience. Candidates working in Government/PSUs/Govt. Autonomous bodies must apply through proper channel and carry their application forwarded through the proper channel; alternatively, an NOC from the current employer is mandatory at the interview. Candidates applying for multiple posts must submit a separate application for each post. OBC candidates (Non-Creamy Layer) must produce a valid caste certificate in the prescribed Government of India format. C-DAC internal candidates must have completed at least 2 years' service in C-DAC as of the date of notification to apply. If finally selected, they will have to resign from current services and join the selected post as fresh employees. No agent or agency is authorized to represent C-DAC for anything related to recruitment or its processes. All information/documents submitted will be verified; any false or incorrect information will lead to rejection/cancellation of candidature at any stage. Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification for selection. C-DAC reserves the right to increase/decrease the number of posts or cancel/restrict/curtail/enlarge the recruitment process at any time without prior notice. Any dispute with regard to the selection/recruitment process will be subject to courts/tribunals having jurisdiction over Guwahati, Assam only. Candidates are advised to visit www.cdac.in regularly for any notices, corrigendum, or updates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1Q1. What is C-DAC CINE and where is it located?
A
C-DAC Centre in North East (CINE) is a centre of C-DAC (Centre for Development of Advanced Computing), a Scientific Society under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India. It is located at the 5th Floor, IIT Guwahati Research Park Building, Amingaon, Guwahati, Assam – 781039.
Q2Q2. How many posts are available under C-DAC CINE Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 21 posts (indicative) are available across 8 designations: Project Manager (02), Senior Project Engineer (06), Project Engineer (11), Project Associate (01), and Project Support Staff (01). The number of posts may vary as per actual project requirements.
Q3Q3. Is this a permanent government job?
A
No. All posts are purely on a contract basis on consolidated emoluments, initially for a period of 3 years or co-terminus with the project, whichever is earlier. Selected candidates shall not derive any right or claim to a regular or permanent post in C-DAC.
Q4Q4. What is the last date to send the resume for C-DAC CINE Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to send the updated resume along with the filled application form to [email protected] is 10th June 2026.
Q5Q5. What are the Walk-in Interview dates and reporting time?
A
The Walk-in Interviews are scheduled on 16th, 17th, and 18th June 2026. Candidates must register at the interview venue between 10:00 AM and 11:00 AM on their respective interview date. Candidates arriving after 11:00 AM will not be allowed to attend.
Q6Q6. Is there any application fee for C-DAC CINE Recruitment 2026?
A
No. C-DAC does not charge any application fee for this recruitment. The application is completely free for all candidates.
Q7Q7. What is the minimum qualification required for the Project Support Staff post?
A
Candidates must be a Graduate with at least 50% marks, or a Post Graduate with at least 50% marks at the Graduate level, along with a minimum of 3 years of relevant experience.
Q8Q8. Will internship or academic project work be counted as experience?
A
No. Academic/teaching/research work and internship or project work placements that are part of the academic curriculum will not be considered as experience unless specifically stated in the notification.
Q9Q9. Can government employees apply for C-DAC CINE Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes. Candidates working in Government/PSUs/Government Autonomous Bodies can apply. However, they must forward their application through the proper channel in advance and carry the forwarded application to the interview. Alternatively, they must produce a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their current employer at the time of the interview, failing which they will not be permitted to attend.
Q10Q10. What documents must be carried to the Walk-in Interview?
A
Candidates must carry the duly filled application form affixed with a passport-size photograph, attested copies of proof of age, educational qualification certificates (with percentage proof if CGPA system is followed), experience certificates from all previous and present employers, caste/EWS/disability certificates (if applicable), and NOC from the current employer (if applicable). No TA/DA will be reimbursed for attending the interview.
