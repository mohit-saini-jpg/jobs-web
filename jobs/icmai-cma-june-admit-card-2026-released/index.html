ICMAI CMA June Admit Card 2026 Released

The Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICMAI) has released the CMA June Admit Card 2026 for candidates appearing in the CMA Intermediate and CMA Final Examinations June 2026 Session. Registered candidates can now download their hall tickets from the official ICMAI examination portal.

The admit card is a mandatory document for entry into the examination hall. Candidates must download and print the hall ticket well before the examination date and verify all the information mentioned on it.

How to Download ICMAI CMA June Admit Card 2026

Candidates can download the admit card by following these steps:

  • Visit the official admit card portal: eicmai.in.
  • Click on the CMA June 2026 Admit Card link.
  • Enter your Registration Number/Student ID and other required credentials.
  • Submit the login details.
  • The CMA June Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Verify all details carefully.
  • Download the admit card PDF.
  • Take at least two printouts for examination purposes.

Details Mentioned on ICMAI CMA June Admit Card 2026

Candidates should verify the following details after downloading the admit card:

  • Candidate’s Name
  • Registration Number
  • Student Identification Number
  • Roll Number
  • Examination Level (Intermediate/Final)
  • Group Appearing For
  • Candidate Photograph
  • Candidate Signature
  • Examination Centre Code
  • Examination Centre Name
  • Examination Centre Address
  • Subject-wise Examination Dates
  • Examination Timing
  • Reporting Time
  • Important Examination Instructions

Candidates should immediately report any discrepancy to ICMAI before the commencement of the examination.

Documents Required Along with ICMAI CMA Admit Card 2026

Candidates must carry the following documents to the examination centre:

  • Printed Copy of ICMAI CMA June Admit Card 2026
  • Original ICMAI Student Identity Card (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID Card
  • Driving Licence
  • Any Government-Issued Photo Identity Proof
  • PwD Certificate (if applicable)
  • Additional documents as specified by ICMAI

Candidates without a valid admit card and identity proof may not be permitted to enter the examination hall.

Exam-Day Instructions for ICMAI CMA June 2026 Examination

Candidates should strictly follow these examination guidelines:

  • Reach the examination centre at least 30-45 minutes before the reporting time.
  • Carry a printed admit card and valid photo identity proof.
  • Verify your seating arrangement before the examination begins.
  • Follow all instructions issued by the invigilators.
  • Ensure that all details on the attendance sheet are filled correctly.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, Bluetooth devices, earphones, or any unauthorized electronic gadgets.
  • Use only permitted stationery items.
  • Maintain discipline inside the examination hall.
  • Any form of unfair means or malpractice may lead to cancellation of candidature.
  • Retain the admit card until the declaration of results and completion of certification processes.
