How to Check Dr MGR Medical University Result 2026 Online

Follow these simple steps to download your B.Sc. result:

  • Visit the official result portal: Click Here
  • Select your course: Choose B.Sc from the dropdown list
  • Enter exam session: Select the relevant exam year/semester if prompted
  • Input your details: Enter your Registration Number/Roll Number (as on hall ticket) and date of birth
  • Submit: Click Submit or View Result to display your marks
  • Verify details: Check subject-wise internal, external, and total marks along with pass/fail status
  • Download & print: Save the PDF marksheet and take multiple printouts for future use
