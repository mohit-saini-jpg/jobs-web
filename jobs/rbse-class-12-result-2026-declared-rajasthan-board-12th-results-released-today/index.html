RBSE Class 12 Result 2026 Declared - Rajasthan Board 12th Results Released Today

The Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (RBSE) has officially announced the RBSE Class 12 Result 2026 today on 31st March 2026. Students who appeared for the Senior Secondary (Class 12) examinations in Science, Commerce, and Arts streams can now check their results online.

RBSE Class 12 Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in. Students can check and download their results by entering their roll number and other required details on the result portal.

Students are advised to verify all details mentioned in the RBSE 12th result carefully and contact the Rajasthan Board in case of any discrepancies.

RBSE Class 12 Results 2026 Key Highlights

The Rajasthan Board has published the RBSE Class 12 Result 2026 for Science, Commerce, and Arts streams. Students can now access their marksheets through the official portal using their roll number.

Results have been declared for all regular and private candidates who appeared in the 2026 board examinations. Students are advised to download and keep a printed copy of their RBSE Class 12 Result 2026 for future reference.

How to Check RBSE Class 12 Result 2026

Students can check their RBSE Class 12 Result 2026 by following the simple steps given below:

  • Visit the official website rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in.
  • Click on the "Results" section on the homepage.
  • Select the link for Class 12 / Senior Secondary Result 2026.
  • Enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth if required.
  • Click on Submit to view your result on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of the RBSE 12th Result 2026 for future use.
