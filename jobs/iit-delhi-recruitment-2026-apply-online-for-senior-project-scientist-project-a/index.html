IIT Delhi Eligibility Criteria 2026

Design Innovation Fellow: First-class B.Tech. or B.E in Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics, or Mechanical Engineering. Strong python skills. Experience in mobile application development. Experience in development of embedded systems with Arduino and Raspberry Pi. Experience in interfacing various sensors with Arduino. Sr. Project Scientist: First class M. Des or equivalent in the appropriate discipline OR First class B. Des with 3 years of experience. Hands on experience in the design and development of Assistive Solutions for persons with visual impairment. Strong skills in CAD modelling, 3D printing and prototyping techniques. Experience in design and execution of user studies. Open to travel to multiple locations for the user trials. Project Attendant: 12th class (10+2) passed & Minimum six months of work experience in a Research Institution or Government/Autonomous
