How to Download Madras University Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the Madras University Time Table 2026 from the official university portal. The process is simple and takes only a few minutes.

  • Visit the official website of University of Madras at https://egovernance.unom.ac.in.
  • Navigate to the "Examination" or "Time Table" section on the homepage.
  • Click on the link that says "University Examination April 2026 Time Table".
  • Select your course category such as UG or PG and choose the specific programme.
  • The timetable PDF will open in a new tab. Review the exam dates carefully.
  • Click the download button to save the PDF on your device.
  • Take a printout for future reference and exam preparation.

Students should go through the official timetable carefully and note down important exam dates. Keep a copy handy for regular revision during the April 2026 examination period.
