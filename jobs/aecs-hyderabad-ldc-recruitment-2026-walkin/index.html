AECS Hyderabad LDC Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Atomic Energy Central School-2 (AECS-2), Hyderabad, an autonomous body under the Department of Atomic Energy, Government of India, has invited applications for engaging Lower Division Clerk (LDC) on an ad-hoc basis against regular and anticipated vacancies at AEC Schools-1 and 2, DAE Colony, ECIL Post, Hyderabad. Selection will be through a walk-in Typing Test and Skill Test.

Eligibility Criteria
