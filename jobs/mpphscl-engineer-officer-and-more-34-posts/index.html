FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for MPPHSCL Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application and pay the application fee is 24th June 2026. Applications received after this date will not be considered.
Q2How many vacancies are there in MPPHSCL Recruitment 2026?
A
MPPHSCL has released a total of 34 vacancies across 18 posts including General Managers, Managers, Consultants, Bio-Medical Engineers, Legal Officer, Pharmacist, and Office Assistant.
Q3What is the age limit for MPPHSCL Recruitment 2026?
A
The age limit is 18 to 40 years for General Category and 18 to 45 years for SC/ST/OBC/Govt. Employees/Women as on 01.01.2026.
Q4What is the salary for General Manager in MPPHSCL 2026?
A
The monthly contractual pay for General Manager (Inventory) and General Manager (IT) is Rs. 1,00,000/- per month.
Q5What is the salary for Office Assistant in MPPHSCL 2026?
A
The monthly contractual pay for Office Assistant is Rs. 22,100/- per month.
Q6How to apply for MPPHSCL Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted online through MP Online at mpphscl.mp.gov.in. No offline applications will be accepted.
Q7Is MPPHSCL Recruitment 2026 a permanent job?
A
No. All positions under MPPHSCL Recruitment 2026 are on a contract or deputation basis and do not confer any right to permanent or regular employment.
Q8What is the selection process for MPPHSCL Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of shortlisting by MP Online, a written examination conducted by MP Online, followed by an interview by the MPPHSCL HR Committee / Selection Committee.
Q9Can I correct my application after submission?
A
Yes. A correction window is available from 22nd June 2026 to 25th June 2026 to fix errors in the submitted application.
Q10Where is the jurisdiction for any legal dispute related to MPPHSCL Recruitment 2026?
A
All legal disputes related to this recruitment will be settled within the jurisdiction of Bhopal court only.
