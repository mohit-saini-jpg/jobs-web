Gujarat TAT HS Result 2026 - Key Highlights : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 17/06/2026

Gujarat TAT HS Result 2026 is officially out. The State Examination Board (SEB), Gandhinagar, has declared the Gujarat TAT Higher Secondary Prelims Result 2026 on 16 June 2026 at the official website sebexam.org. A total of 51,971 candidates have qualified the preliminary exam out of 1,47,584 who appeared on 12 April 2026. Candidates who scored 70 or more marks out of 200 (35% and above) are declared qualified for the Main Examination. Along with the result, SEB Gujarat has also released the subject-wise merit list for all mediums. Read on to check how to download your scorecard, view the medium-wise result analysis, know the rechecking process, and find out what happens next.

How to Download Gujarat TAT HS Prelims Result 2026 - Step-by-Step Process

Candidates can follow the simple step-by-step process below to access and download their Gujarat TAT HS Result 2026 scorecard directly from the SEB official website.

  • Visit the official website of SEB Gujarat at www.sebexam.org.
  • On the homepage, navigate to the Notice Board or latest updates section.
  • Click on the link titled "Gujarat TAT HS Result 2026" or "Print Result".
  • A new page will open. Select TAT Exam and enter your Confirmation Number and Date of Birth.
  • Click the Submit button to proceed.
  • Your Gujarat TAT HS Prelims Result 2026 scorecard will appear on the screen.
  • Download or print the scorecard and keep it safe for future reference.

Gujarat TAT HS Result 2026 - Rechecking Process

Candidates who are dissatisfied with their preliminary exam result can apply for rechecking (Punah-Chasani) of their OMR sheet. The rechecking window is open from 18 June 2026 to 30 June 2026. No application will be accepted after 30 June 2026 under any circumstances.

How to Apply for Gujarat TAT HS 2026 Prelims Rechecking:

  • Visit the SEB office, Sector-21, Gandhinagar personally between 11:00 AM and 5:00 PM on working days within the stipulated dates.
  • Carry your original hall ticket and a copy of your OMR sheet.
  • Submit a written application (the format is available in the official result notification PDF).
  • Pay a rechecking fee of Rs. 100/- in cash at the Accounts Section of the State Examination Board.
  • After the rechecking process is completed, the candidate must verify on the spot whether any change in marks has occurred.
  • The candidate's signature is mandatory on the form after in-person verification.

What's Next After Gujarat TAT HS Result 2026?

Candidates who have cleared the Gujarat TAT HS Prelims 2026 should immediately start preparing for the next stage of selection. Here is a clear roadmap of what to expect after the prelims result:

  • Check the Subject-Wise Merit List: Visit sebexam.org and confirm your name and rank in the merit list for your respective subject.
  • Prepare for the Main Exam: The Mains Examination is the next decisive stage. Candidates from the Gujarati medium should note that the Main Exam is scheduled for 19 July 2026. English and Hindi medium candidates should expect their Main Exam in August 2026.
  • Keep Checking the Official Website: The official SEB Gujarat website will be updated with further instructions, admit cards, and schedules. Check sebexam.org regularly.
  • Document Verification: After the Main Exam, shortlisted candidates will be called for document verification as part of the final selection process.
  • Final Selection: The final merit list for Gujarat TAT HS 2026 will be prepared based on Main Exam performance followed by document verification.
