How to Check AKTU Result 2026 on One View Portal

This section walks you through the exact steps to access and download your AKTU semester marksheet online, so you can complete the process in under a minute.

Follow these steps to check your AKTU One View Result 2026:

  • Open the official AKTU ERP One View portal: erp.aktu.ac.in/WebPages/OneView/OneView.aspx.
  • Alternatively, visit the main website aktu.ac.in and navigate to the Examination or Result section, then select the One View option.
  • Enter your University Roll Number exactly as printed on your AKTU admit card (not your internal college roll number).
  • Enter your Date of Birth in the format requested on the portal.
  • Click the Search/Submit/View Result button.
  • Your subject-wise marks, SGPA, and overall result status will be displayed on screen.
  • Download the provisional marksheet as a PDF and save or print it for future reference.

Documents and Details Required to Check the Result

Before checking your result, it helps to know exactly what credentials and information you'll need on hand, as well as what your marksheet will display once it loads.

To log in to the One View portal, you need:

  • University Roll Number (as printed on the admit card)
  • Date of Birth (in the format requested by the portal)

Once generated, your AKTU marksheet typically includes:

  • Student's name and roll number
  • Course name, code, and semester
  • Subject-wise marks/grades
  • SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average)
  • Result status (Pass, Fail, ATKT, etc.)

Is the Online AKTU Marksheet the Final Document?

Many students assume the PDF downloaded from One View is their final certificate. This section clarifies the actual status of the online result so you know what to expect next.

The marksheet available on the AKTU One View portal is a provisional document. The official, university-sealed physical marksheet is sent to your affiliated college separately, generally a few weeks after the online result is declared. Until you receive the physical copy, the downloaded PDF serves as a valid working document for placements, internships, and further admissions.

What to Do If Your AKTU Result Is Withheld or Shows an Error

Some students may find their result delayed, withheld, or containing a discrepancy. This section explains the common reasons behind this and the corrective steps to take.

If your result is withheld, it is usually due to one of the following reasons:

  • Pending or unverified ABC ID (APAAR ID): Linking your Academic Bank of Credits ID to your ERP profile is now mandatory; an unlinked ID can cause your result to be withheld automatically.
  • Unreconciled exam fee payment: If your fee payment shows as failed despite being deducted, it may simply not have synced yet in AKTU's batch reconciliation system.
  • Incomplete internal/practical marks: If your college hasn't uploaded internal marks, your result may show as incomplete until it is updated.

If you notice an error in your marksheet (wrong name, roll number, or marks), report it immediately to your college examination cell or the AKTU examination department, as correction windows are usually time-limited.

How to Apply for AKTU Revaluation or Scrutiny

If you believe your answer sheet was not evaluated correctly, AKTU provides an official process to request a recheck. This section covers how that process works.

Students who wish to apply for revaluation, scrutiny, or challenge evaluation can do so through the AKTU ERP portal or their college examination cell, by paying the prescribed per-subject fee within the window announced alongside the result. Once the revaluation or scrutiny process is completed, the updated result is also uploaded on the One View portal and can be checked using the same Roll Number and Date of Birth.
