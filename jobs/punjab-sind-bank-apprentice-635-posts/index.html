Punjab & Sind Bank Apprentice Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 635 Posts

Human Resource Development (HRD) Department, Punjab & Sind Bank

Posts: Various Last Date: 06-06-2026 Online only (via NAPS portal + NATS portal + PSB website)

Short Information

Punjab & Sind Bank Apprentice Recruitment 2026 notification is out to fill 635 new vacancies across India. Candidates with Any Graduate Can Apply Online. The online application opens on 21-05-2026, and closes on 06-06-2026. The candidate shall apply online through Punjab & Sind Bank website, punjabandsind.bank.in.

Important Dates

Last Date To Apply06-06-2026

Age Limit

Age DetailsThe candidate must be proficient in the local language (Reading, Writing, Speaking & Understanding) of the applied State/UT and must produce 8th/10th/12th or Graduate level certificate evidencing having studied the local language.

Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationRegular Graduation in any discipline from a recognized University/Institute or equivalent qualification recognized by the Central Government.

Selection Process

  • Merit-based on HSC / 10+2 marks (State-wise, District-wise, Category-wise) + Medical Examination

How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply online through the Bank's website (https://punjabandsind.bank.in/) only. No other mode of application will be accepted. Before applying, candidates must mandatorily complete 100% registration on both the NAPS portal (https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in/) and the NATS portal (https://nats.education.gov.in/). Candidates must also note down their Enrollment ID generated on the NATS portal as it is required while applying.
