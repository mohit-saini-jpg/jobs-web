The Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) has officially released the JCECEB Jharkhand BEd Answer Key 2026 for the B.Ed./M.Ed./B.P.Ed. Combined Entrance Competitive Examination conducted on 31 May 2026.

Candidates who appeared for the entrance test can now download the Final Answer Key PDF from the official website, jceceb.jharkhand.gov.in, and verify their responses. The answer key enables candidates to estimate their probable scores before the declaration of results.

How to Download JCECEB BEd Answer Key 2026 (Step-by-Step)

Follow these simple steps to download the JCECEB Jharkhand BEd Answer Key 2026:

  • Visit the official website: Go to jceceb.jharkhand.gov.in
  • Navigate to the Download section: Click on the "Links/download.aspx" or "Download" tab on the homepage
  • Find the Answer Key link: Look for "Answer Key of B.Ed./M.Ed./B.P.Ed. Examination-2026" (Serial No. 1)
  • Click on "View": The PDF will open in your browser
  • Download & Save: Click the download button and save the PDF on your device
  • Verify your answers: Check your responses against the official answer key
