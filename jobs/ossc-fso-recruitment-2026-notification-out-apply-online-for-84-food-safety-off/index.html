OSSC FSO Recruitment 2026 - Age Limit

Candidates must be minimum 21 years and maximum 42 years as on 01-01-2025. The upper age limit is relaxable by 5 years for SEBC, SC, ST, and all Women candidates, and by 10 years for PwD candidates. PwD candidates belonging to SC/ST category are entitled to a cumulative relaxation of 10 years over and above the normal relaxation for their category. Candidates falling under more than one category shall be eligible for only one benefit of age relaxation, whichever is most beneficial. Note: To be eligible, candidates not enjoying any relaxation must not have been born earlier than 2nd January 1983 and not later than 1st January 2004.

OSSC FSO Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

The selection process for OSSC Food Safety Officer Recruitment 2026 consists of 3 (three) successive stages – Stage I: Preliminary Examination (150 marks, CBT/OMR, 150 minutes), Stage II: Main Written Examination (200 marks, CBT/OMR, Technical Paper) + Computer Skill Test (qualifying, 50% marks required), and Stage III: Certificate Verification. Candidates failing to appear at any stage will be rejected and will not be included in the merit list. The Commission at its discretion may conduct the examination through OMR or CBRE mode. Stage I – Preliminary Examination Candidates numbering a minimum of 5 times of the vacancies (post-wise and category-wise) shall be shortlisted for the Main Written Examination based on marks in Preliminary Examination. OSSC FSO Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is OSSC Food Safety Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
OSSC Food Safety Officer Recruitment 2026 is a recruitment drive by Odisha Staff Selection Commission under Advertisement No. IIE-46/2025/5919/OSSC to fill 84 Group-B Food Safety Officer posts under the Office of Commissioner of Food Safety, Health & Family Welfare Department, Government of Odisha.
Q2How many vacancies are available in OSSC FSO 2026?
A
A total of 84 vacancies are available for Food Safety Officer posts distributed as UR-44, SEBC-08, SC-13, ST-19, with 17 seats reserved for women and 6 seats for PwD candidates across categories.
Q3What is the last date to apply for OSSC Food Safety Officer 2026?
A
The last date for online application submission is 11 July 2026, with online registration opening on 09 June 2026 and an editing window available till 14 July 2026.
Q4What is the eligibility for OSSC Food Safety Officer 2026?
A
Candidates must have a Bachelor's/Master's/Doctorate degree in Food Technology, Dairy Technology, Biotechnology, Agricultural Science, Oil Technology, Veterinary Science, Biochemistry, Microbiology, Chemistry, or medical degrees (MBBS, BDS, BHMS, BUMS, BSMS, BAMS, BSRMS) from recognized universities, plus adequate computer knowledge.
Q5What is the age limit for OSSC FSO 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 21 to 42 years as on 01 January 2025, with 5 years relaxation for SEBC/SC/ST/Women and 10 years relaxation for PwD candidates, plus additional relaxation for Ex-Servicemen.
Q6What is the selection process for OSSC Food Safety Officer 2026?
A
The selection process includes Stage-I Preliminary Examination (150 marks, 150 questions, 150 minutes), Stage-II Main Written Examination Technical Paper (200 marks, 100 questions) plus Computer Skill Test (qualifying at 50%), and Stage-III Certificate Verification, with negative marking of 0.25 and 0.5 marks respectively.
Q7Is there any application fee for OSSC FSO 2026?
A
Yes, examination fee is ₹500 for General/SEBC/Women/Ex-Servicemen/Sports Person candidates (refundable to those who appear in Preliminary Examination), while SC/ST/PwD candidates are exempted from fee payment.
Q8What is the salary for OSSC Food Safety Officer 2026?
A
Food Safety Officers will receive Pay Matrix Level-9, Cell-1 as per ORSP (Odisha Revised Pay Structure) 2017 on a regular appointment basis as Group-B State Cadre officers.
Q9Is Odia language mandatory for OSSC Food Safety Officer 2026?
A
Yes, candidates must be able to read, write, and speak Odia fluently and must have proof of passing Middle School/Matriculation/Class-VII examination with Odia as a subject, or ME standard Odia test conducted by School and Mass Education Department.
Q10How to apply for OSSC Food Safety Officer 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online only through the official OSSC website ossc.gov.in from 09 June 2026 to 11 July 2026 by completing registration, uploading educational certificates, computer knowledge proof, caste/disability certificates (if applicable), paying examination fee, and submitting the application form.
