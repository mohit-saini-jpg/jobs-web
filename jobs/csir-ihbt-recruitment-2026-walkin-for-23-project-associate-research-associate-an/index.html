Frequently Asked Questions

Q1Q1. What is the total number of vacancies announced by CSIR IHBT?
A
CSIR IHBT has announced a total of 23 vacancies for the positions of Project Associate, Senior Research Fellow, Research Associate, and Project Scientist.
Q2Q2. What is the application mode for this recruitment?
A
The application mode for this recruitment is Walk-in Interview. Candidates need to attend the interview in person with all required documents.
Q3Q3. What is the last date to apply for CSIR IHBT Recruitment 2026?
A
The walk-in interview dates are scheduled from 15th June 2026 to 19th June 2026, with specific dates for different post codes.
Q4Q4. What is the salary offered for the Project Associate posts?
A
The salary for Project Associate posts ranges from Rs. 25,000/- to Rs. 31,000/- per month, plus HRA, depending on qualification and NET/GATE status.
Q5Q5. Are there any application fees for CSIR IHBT Recruitment 2026?
A
No, there are no application fees mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) in the official notification.
Q6Q6. What is the minimum qualification required for the Project Scientist-I post?
A
The minimum qualification for Project Scientist-I is a Doctoral Degree in Science or a Master's Degree in Engineering or Technology from a recognized University or equivalent.
Q7Q7. What is the age relaxation provided for reserved categories?
A
Age relaxation is provided as 5 years for SC/ST/PwBD/Women candidates and 3 years for OBC candidates.
Q8Q8. Where will the Walk-in Interview be conducted?
A
The Walk-in Interview will be conducted at the CSIR IHBT office in Palampur, Himachal Pradesh (PIN: 176061).
Q9Q9. What documents should candidates carry for the Walk-in Interview?
A
Candidates should carry all original documents and one set of self-attested photocopies, including ID proof, educational certificates, and experience certificates.
Q10Q10. Is there any specific qualification for Senior Research Fellow?
A
Yes, for the Senior Research Fellow position, an M.Sc. in Botany, Plant Physiology with 3 years of experience in R&D is required, with a preference for experience in tissue culture of High altitude medicinal plants.
