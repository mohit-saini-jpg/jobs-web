How to Download RALVV Result 2026

Students can follow the step-by-step process below to check their RALVV semester results online:

  • Visit the official website of Raja Ali Vishwavidyalaya - https://www.ralvv.mp.gov.in/
  • Click on the "Academics" or "Exam Result" section available on the homepage
  • Select your course and semester (UG/PG 1st or 3rd Semester)
  • Enter your roll number or required login credentials
  • Click on the submit button to view your result
  • Download and print the result for future reference
