Dibrugarh University 6th Semester BCA (CBCS) Result 2026

  • Examination Held In: June 2026

How to Check Dibrugarh University Result 2026

Candidates can follow the steps below to check the Dibrugarh University result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official website of Dibrugarh University at dibru.ac.in.
  • Go to the "Results" category on the homepage or navigate directly to the results page.
  • Locate the relevant result link, such as "4th Semester B.Ed Result 2026," "MA/MSc/MCom 4th Semester (CDOE) Result 2026," or "6th Semester BCA (CBCS) Result 2026."
  • Click on the applicable link to open the result PDF.
  • Use Ctrl+F (or Cmd+F) to search for your roll number within the PDF.
  • Download and save the result for future reference.

Details Mentioned on the Dibrugarh University Result 2026

The Dibrugarh University result PDF generally includes the following details:

  • Roll Number
  • Registration Number
  • Subject-wise Marks
  • Overall Result Status (Pass/Fail/Backlog)
  • Semester and Examination Name

Note: Dibrugarh University publishes results as consolidated PDF lists. Individual scorecards or mark sheets, if issued separately, will be made available through the respective department or CDOE office.

What to Do After Checking the Result

After checking the Dibrugarh University result 2026, candidates are advised to:

  • Download and take a printout of the result for record purposes.
  • Verify all details such as roll number, name, and marks carefully.
  • Contact the respective department or the CDOE office in case of any discrepancy.
  • Keep checking the official website for updates regarding mark sheet distribution, revaluation, or supplementary examination notices, if applicable.

Information regarding revaluation, supplementary exams, or mark sheet distribution dates has not been officially announced yet.
