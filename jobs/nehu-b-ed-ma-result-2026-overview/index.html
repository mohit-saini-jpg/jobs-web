NEHU B.Ed & MA Result 2026 - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 17/07/2026

North Eastern Hill University (NEHU), Shillong, has declared the results of the Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) 1st Semester Examination held in December 2025, along with two re-appear results for other courses. All results below were declared on 16th July 2026 and are based on the official result notifications issued by the Controller of Examinations, NEHU. This article covers every result PDF released in this batch, with the exact number of students who appeared, passed, and the pass percentage for each college or department.

NEHU B.Ed & MA Result 2026 - Overview

  • Examination: 2025-26 Odd Semester Examination, held December 2025 (plus two re-appear results)
  • Course covered: Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) - 1st Semester; and one Master of Arts (Political Science) re-appear result

NEHU B.Ed 1st Semester Result: What the Numbers Show

Out of the five B.Ed 1st Semester (Regular, 2025 Batch) colleges included in this release, three colleges - Don Bosco CTE, CTE Rongkhon, and St Mary's CTE - recorded a 100% pass percentage. College of Teacher Education, PGT recorded a 98.00% pass rate, and NERIE NCERT recorded 98.11%. Both re-appear results (B.Ed CTE-PGT and M.A. Political Science) show a 100% pass rate, though the number of candidates in these re-appear batches was small.

How to Check and Download the NEHU Result 2026

  • Visit the official NEHU examination result portal.
  • Look for the relevant result notification link matching your course (B.Ed 1st Semester, 2025-26 Odd Semester Exam) or department.
  • Open or download the PDF result notification.
  • Use "Ctrl+F" (or your device's search function) within the PDF to locate your name, enrolment number, or roll number.
  • Check your GPA and remarks (Passed/Not Passed) against your roll number.
  • Save or print the result PDF for your records.
