The Assam Higher Secondary Education Council (AHSEC) / Assam State School Education Board (ASSEB) Division-II has officially released the Assam HS Result 2026 for the academic session 2025-26. Students who appeared for the HS Final (Class 12) examinations across Arts, Science, Commerce and Vocational streams can now check their results online. Assam HS Result 2026 has been released online on the official websites - ahsec.assam.gov.in, resultassam.nic.in and other authorised portals.

The board has published the HS 2nd year results for all streams, and students can easily check and download their provisional marksheet by entering their roll code and roll number on the official result portal. The online marksheet will display subject-wise scores, total marks and qualifying status for immediate reference.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the Assam HS Result 2026 marksheet, including name, roll number, subjects and marks. In case of any discrepancies, they should immediately contact their school authorities or the board officials for necessary correction as per the prescribed procedure.

How to Check Assam HS Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your Assam HS Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official Assam HS result portal - https://results.ahsecregistration.in/as/ahsec/class-12th-exam-result-2026-ahsec/query.htm or go through the AHSEC / ASSEB official website.
  • Click on the link "Assam HS Result 2026" or "AHSEC Class 12 Result 2026" on the homepage.
  • Enter your Roll Code and Roll Number in the respective fields as printed on your HS admit card.
  • Click on the Submit button.
  • Your Assam HS Result 2026 marksheet will appear on the screen with subject-wise marks and result status.
  • Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future reference and admission processes.
