How to Download GBPUAT Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official admission portal gbpuat.org.in.
  • Open the Admit Card / Student Login page.
  • Enter your Login ID / Registration Number and Password.
  • Fill in the captcha if required.
  • Submit the details.
  • Your GBPUAT Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download it and take a clear printout.

If the page loads slowly, try again after some time.

Details Mentioned on the Admit Card

The GBPUAT hall ticket generally includes:

  • Candidate’s name.
  • Date of birth.
  • Photograph and signature.
  • Gender.
  • Exam date and time.
  • Exam centre and address.
  • Roll number / registration details.
  • Important exam instructions.

Check all the information carefully. If there is any error, contact the university helpdesk immediately.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the GBPUAT Admit Card 2026.
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card, Driving Licence or Passport.
  • Recent passport-size photographs, if mentioned in the instructions.

Without the admit card and valid ID, entry may be denied.

Exam-Day Instructions

  • Reach the exam centre well before reporting time.
  • Carry only permitted items.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators or any electronic devices.
  • Follow all instructions given by the invigilators.
  • Keep the admit card safe even after the exam.
