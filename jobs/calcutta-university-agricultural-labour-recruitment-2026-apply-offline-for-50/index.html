Calcutta University Agricultural Labour Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The University of Calcutta has issued a recruitment notice dated 14-07-2026 inviting applications from eligible Indian citizens for the post of Agricultural Labour at the Baruipur Agricultural Experimental Farm. This recruitment is being carried out under the University's RUSA 2.0 (Component-10) project, and the engagement is fully temporary and contract-based. Around 50 vacancies have been notified. University of Calcutta Agricultural Labour Recruitment 2026: Eligibility

Applicants must be Indian citizens . Candidates who have passed Class 8 from a recognized educational institution will be given preference . (This is stated as a preference, not stated as a strict minimum qualification requirement in the notice.) Candidates must have a minimum of 5 (five) years of experience in an agricultural field, agricultural farm, agricultural research farm, or similar agriculture-related work. Applicants must submit an experience certificate issued by the Scientist, Principal Investigator, Farm Superintendent, or relevant authority under whom they previously worked.

University of Calcutta Agricultural Labour Recruitment 2026: Salary / Remuneration

Remuneration will be paid as daily wages , as per the prevailing order of the Government of West Bengal . The exact daily wage amount is not specified in the notice.

Nature of Work

Selected candidates will be required to carry out the following agriculture-related duties at the Baruipur Agricultural Experimental Farm: Land preparation Seed sowing Seedling transplantation Irrigation Crop care Crop harvesting Maintenance of experimental plots and nursery Maintenance of agricultural infrastructure Other agriculture-related duties assigned by the University authorities

The appointment will be fully temporary and contract-based , for a period as per the project's requirement. The notice explicitly states that this recruitment does not create any claim to permanent appointment, regularization, or future employment at the University.
