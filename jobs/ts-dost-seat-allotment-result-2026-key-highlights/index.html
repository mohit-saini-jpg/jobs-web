Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE) has officially released the TS DOST Seat Allotment Result 2026 for Phase-I counselling on 14 May 2026 for admissions into Undergraduate (UG) courses for the academic session 2026-2027. TS DOST Seat Allotment Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - dost.cgg.gov.in.

Students who have successfully completed the registration and web options entry process can now check and download their Phase-I seat allotment order by logging in with their DOST ID and PIN credentials through the student login portal. The allotted candidates are required to complete online self-reporting between 15 May 2026 and 23 May 2026 to confirm their seats. Students are advised to carefully verify all details mentioned in their seat allotment letter and contact the Telangana State Council of Higher Education immediately in case of any discrepancies or issues with the allotment order.

How to Check TS DOST Seat Allotment Result 2026

Students can follow these simple steps to check and download their TS DOST Phase-I Seat Allotment Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official TS DOST website at dost.cgg.gov.in using your computer or mobile browser
  • On the homepage, locate and click on the link titled "Phase-I Seat Allotment 2026" or "Student Login" under the navigation section
  • You will be redirected to the candidate login page for TS DOST UG Admission 2026-2027
  • Enter your registered DOST ID in the first input field
  • Enter your PIN or password in the second input field accurately
  • Click on the "Login" button to access your seat allotment result
  • Your Phase-I seat allotment letter will appear on the screen displaying your allotted college, course, and other admission details
  • Download the seat allotment order and save it on your device for future reference
  • Take a printout of the allotment letter for the admission and reporting process
  • Complete the online self-reporting between 15 May 2026 and 23 May 2026 to confirm your seat
