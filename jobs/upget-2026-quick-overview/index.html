UPGET 2026 Admit Card - How to Download (Step-by-Step Guide)

The UPGET 2026 Admit Card is available online and will not be sent by post. Candidates must download and print the hall ticket themselves before appearing at the exam center. Follow these simple steps:

  • Visit the official portal: abvmucet26.co.in/entrance2026/login?form=6
  • Click on the "UPGET 2026" or "Download Admit Card" link on the homepage.
  • Select the "Candidate Login" option.
  • Enter your Registration Number and Password / Date of Birth.
  • Your admit card will appear on screen - verify all the details carefully.
  • Download and take at least 2 coloured printouts for the exam day.

Important Note: Carry your printed admit card along with a valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar card, voter ID, etc.) to the examination centre. Entry without the admit card will not be permitted.

UPGET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Before applying for UPGET 2026, candidates must meet the minimum eligibility requirements set by ABVMU. The criteria include educational qualifications, age limits, and other conditions:

Educational Qualification

Candidates must meet any one of the following conditions:

  • Passed 10+2 (Intermediate) with English as a subject, securing a minimum of 40% marks in aggregate.
  • Completed 10+2 with a Vocational ANM (Auxiliary Nurse Midwife) Course, with a minimum of 40% marks.
  • Registered ANMs who have successfully completed the ANM course are also eligible to apply.

Always refer to the official ABVMU Information Bulletin for complete and updated eligibility requirements before applying.

How to Fill the UPGET 2026 Online Form - Complete Guide

Although the application window for UPGET 2026 has closed (deadline: May 26, 2026), here is a reference guide for future aspirants and for candidates who want to verify their submission details:

Documents and Information Required

  • Valid Email ID and Mobile Number
  • Class 10 and 10+2 mark sheets
  • Valid government-issued ID proof
  • Scanned copy of passport-size photograph (recent, with white background, full-face, no spectacles)
  • Scanned signature
  • Live Photo (as required by ABVMU)

Photo Guidelines for Upload

  • Full-face, front-facing photograph
  • White background only
  • No spectacles
  • Candidate's name and date of photo must be written/printed on the image

Step-by-Step Application Process

  • Visit the official ABVMU website and open the UPGET 2026 application portal.
  • Register with your valid email ID and mobile number.
  • Fill in all personal, academic, and contact details accurately.
  • Upload all required documents as per the specified size and format guidelines.
  • Pay the application fee online using a credit card, debit card, or net banking.
  • Review the completed form carefully before final submission.
  • Submit the form and download the confirmation page for your records.

Why Should You Choose the UPGET 2026 GNM Course?

The 3-Year GNM course offered through UPGET 2026 is a gateway to a highly rewarding and stable career in the healthcare sector. Here are the key reasons to pursue this program:

  • Admission to government and private nursing colleges across Uttar Pradesh
  • Strong demand for qualified nurses in hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities
  • Foundation for further studies such as B.Sc. Nursing or specialized post-basic nursing programs
  • Opportunities to work in Central Government Health Services (CGHS), Railways, Armed Forces, and private hospitals
  • Nursing is one of the fastest-growing healthcare professions globally
