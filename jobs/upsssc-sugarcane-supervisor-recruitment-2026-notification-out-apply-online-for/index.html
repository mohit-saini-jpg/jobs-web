UPSSSC Sugarcane Supervisor Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

The selection process for UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak Recruitment 2026 consists of a single Written (Main) Examination of 100 marks. There is no interview. Shortlisting for the Written Examination is done on the basis of PET-2025 normalized scores - 15 times the number of category-wise vacancies (up to the cutoff marks, rounded to 2 decimal places) will be shortlisted. The final merit list will be prepared on the basis of marks obtained in the Written Examination. If the exam is held over multiple days/sessions, normalization will be done as per UPSSSC norms published in Mandatory Notice No. 42/05/Vigya0 Anu0 (Gyarah)/2019 dated 22 May 2019.
