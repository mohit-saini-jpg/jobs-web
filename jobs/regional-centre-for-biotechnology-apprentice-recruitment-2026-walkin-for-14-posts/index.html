Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for the Apprentice posts?

The walk-in interviews are scheduled from 08 June 2026 to 12 June 2026, with specific dates for different trade categories. Candidates should attend on the designated date for their respective trade.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Apprentice posts?

The educational qualifications vary by post, ranging from 10th & 12th passed to B.Tech/B.Sc in Life Sciences, B.Com/B.A., BE/B.Tech in Engineering disciplines, and Graduate/BCA with a Diploma in Computer Applications.

Q3. What is the age limit for the Apprentice recruitment?

The minimum age limit is 14 years, and the maximum age limit is 28 years. Age relaxation is applicable as per Government of India rules for reserved categories.

Q4. What is the salary offered for the Apprentice positions?

Selected candidates will receive a stipend of Rs. 13500 per month for the Apprentice posts.

Q5. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.

Q6. What is the selection process for the Apprentice posts?

The selection process is based on merit, determined by the marks secured by the candidates in their relevant degree or diploma course.

Q7. What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?

Candidates must carry all original documents, including ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates (if any), and category certificates (if applicable), along with one set of self-attested photocopies.

Q8. Where is the Regional Centre for Biotechnology located?

The Regional Centre for Biotechnology is located in Faridabad, Haryana, with the pincode 121001.
