How To Apply

  • Eligible candidates must complete the online registration process as follows:
  • Visit the RPSC official website: https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in and click the Apply Online link, or log in via the SSO Portal: https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in using Citizen Apps (G2C) to access the Recruitment Portal.
  • Complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the SSO portal if not already done. During OTR, keep a live photo of yourself, your name, father's name (if available on Aadhaar), date of birth, gender, Aadhaar/Janaadhar Card details ready.
  • Fill in the online application form completely and accurately with all educational qualification, experience, and category details as applicable on the last date of the notification.
  • Pay the prescribed examination fee online through the RPSC website as per your category.
  • After final submission, take a printout of the application form for your records.
  • Candidates who have already completed OTR before April 19, 2023 can log in through the SSO portal and apply using their OTR number/registration number.
  • Application window: June 4, 2026 to July 3, 2026 till 12:00 midnight. No application will be accepted after the closing date.
