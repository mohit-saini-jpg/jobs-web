HSCAP Kerala Plus One Admission 2026

HSCAP Kerala Plus One Admission 2026 has started. The Directorate of General Education (DGE), Kerala, released the official notification on 25 May 2026 for centralized online admissions to Class 11 (Plus One) in government and aided higher secondary schools for the academic year 2026-27.

The Higher Secondary Centralized Admission Process (HSCAP) is Kerala's single-window system (Ekajalakam) for Plus One admissions. It allows students to apply online for Science, Commerce, and Humanities streams with 45 subject combinations. Admissions are merit-based on Weighted Grade Point Average (WGPA) from SSLC or equivalent Class 10 exams, including bonus points and reservations.

Application Fee

The application is generally free or has a nominal fee for general category (check prospectus on the portal for exact 2026 details). SC/ST candidates often get exemptions.

How to Apply for HSCAP Kerala Plus One 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official portal: https://www.hscap.kerala.gov.in.
  • Click on "Apply Online" or "Ekajalakam 2026".
  • Register with required details (SSLC details, school info).
  • Fill the application form, choose preferred schools and streams (up to options allowed).
  • Upload documents and submit.
  • Take printout of the application for future reference.

HSCAP Kerala Plus One Admission 2026 – Allotment Process

  • Trial Allotment: Allows option modification.
  • Main Allotment: Based on WGPA and choices.
  • Multiple rounds including supplementary allotment for remaining seats.
  • Students must report to allotted schools with documents for admission.
