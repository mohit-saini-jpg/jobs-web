NHSRCL Recruitment 2026 – 15 Posts in IT, Safety, Data : Job Overview

National High Speed Rail Corporation Limited (NHSRCL) has released Vacancy Notices 08/2026 to 20/2026 inviting applications from Regular/Contractual Officers and Managers working in CPSEs, SPVs, Metro Railways, Ministry of Railways, and Government Departments for 15 posts across IT, Safety, Data Analytics, Cloud Infrastructure, and AFC Systems domains on an Absorption Basis. Posts range from Assistant Manager (E-2) to AGM/JGM (E-6/E-7). Eligibility criteria are reckoned as on 31 May 2026. Candidates can apply for any one post only from Vacancy Notice No. 08/2026 to 20/2026.

NHSRCL Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility is reckoned as on 31 May 2026 . NHSRCL has defined separate criteria for employees working in IDA pay scales (CPSEs/SPVs/Metro Railways) and CDA pay scales (Ministry of Railways/other Government Departments). Experience from Teaching, Faculty, Apprenticeship, Internship, Proprietorship, Partnership, or Freelancing will not be counted towards the required years of experience. Candidates wondering about MACP grade pay should note that higher grade pays granted under MACP by departments will not be taken into account for eligibility. Only the substantive grade will be considered for deciding the grade of absorption. Employees in IDA pay scales who do not meet the IDA-specific eligibility criteria may still be considered if they meet the CDA criteria applicable during their employment.

NHSRCL Recruitment 2026 – 15 Posts in IT, Safety, Data : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

NHSRCL will use a multi-stage selection process for all posts under Vacancy Notices 08/2026 to 20/2026. The Computer Based Test (CBT) for all posts from 08/2026 to 20/2026 may be conducted on the same day and at the same time, which is an important reason why candidates can apply for only one post. Computer Based Test (CBT) Interview Medical Examination Management reserves the right to include or dispense with any of these selection stages as deemed fit. Submission of an application does not entitle a candidate to be called for any stage of the selection process. Depending on the volume of applications received, NHSRCL may raise or relax eligibility criteria or prescribe additional written tests. The management's decision regarding selection is final. Outstation candidates called for interview will be paid travelling allowance as per company rules.

NHSRCL Recruitment 2026 – 15 Posts in IT, Safety, Data : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1How many posts has NHSRCL notified under Vacancy Notices 08/2026 to 20/2026?
A
NHSRCL has notified a total of 15 posts across IT, AFC Systems, Data Analytics, Cloud Infrastructure, and Safety (Operations and Maintenance) domains under Vacancy Notices 08/2026 to 20/2026.
Q2Who is eligible to apply for NHSRCL Recruitment 2026?
A
Only Regular or Contractual Officers and Managers currently working in Central Public Sector Undertakings (CPSEs), SPVs, Metro Railways, Ministry of Railways, or other Government Departments are eligible. Eligibility is reckoned as on 31 May 2026.
Q3What is the application fee for NHSRCL Vacancy Notice 2026?
A
Candidates belonging to UR, EWS, and OBC categories must pay a non-refundable fee of Rs. 400/- (exclusive of transaction processing charges). SC, ST, and Women candidates are exempted from the fee.
Q4Can I apply for more than one post under NHSRCL Vacancy Notices 08/2026 to 20/2026?
A
No. Candidates can apply for only one post across all Vacancy Notices 08/2026 to 20/2026. Only one application can be submitted against these vacancy notices. The CBT for all these posts may be conducted on the same day and time.
Q5What is the salary for NHSRCL DGM (IT) and DGM (AFC Systems)?
A
Both DGM posts are in E-5 grade with a pay scale of Rs. 80,000–2,20,000 (IDA) and an approximate annual CTC of Rs. 38.00 Lakhs.
Q6What is the age limit for Safety posts in NHSRCL Recruitment 2026?
A
For AGM/JGM (Safety – Operations and Maintenance) posts, the maximum age limit is 52 years. For Assistant Manager Safety posts (Civil & Track, Electrical, Signalling & Telecom, Rolling Stock, and Operations), the maximum age limit is 45 years.
Q7What is the selection process for NHSRCL Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of three stages: Computer Based Test (CBT), followed by an Interview, and finally a Medical Examination. Management may include or dispense with any of these stages as deemed appropriate.
Q8What is the surety bond amount for NHSRCL selected candidates?
A
The surety bond on joining is Rs. 3,00,000 for E-2 grade, Rs. 4,00,000 for E-4, E-5, and E-6 grades, and Rs. 5,00,000 for E-7 grade. Candidates must serve a minimum of 3 years and give 90 days' notice before resignation.
Q9What experience is NOT counted towards eligibility in NHSRCL Recruitment 2026?
A
Experience in Teaching, Faculty roles, Apprenticeship, Internship, Proprietorship, Partnership, and Freelancing will not be considered as part of the required years of post-qualification experience.
Q10Will MACP grade pay be considered for NHSRCL absorption eligibility?
A
No. For employees in CDA pay scales, higher grade pays granted under MACP will not be taken into account. Only the substantive grade will be considered for deciding the grade of absorption into NHSRCL.
