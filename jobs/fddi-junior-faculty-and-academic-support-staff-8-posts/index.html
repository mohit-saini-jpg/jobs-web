FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the FDDI recruitment?
A
The last date to submit the application form for the FDDI recruitment is 11th June 2026. Ensure your application is submitted well before this deadline.
Q2What is the total number of vacancies announced by FDDI?
A
FDDI has announced a total of 8 vacancies for the posts of Junior Faculty and Academic Support Staff. These positions are spread across various schools within the institute.
Q3What are the minimum educational qualifications required for the Jr. Lab Assistant posts?
A
For Jr. Lab Assistant posts in the School of Footwear Design & Production and School of Fashion Design, an 8th pass is essential, with a 10th pass being desirable. For the School of Leather Goods & Accessories Design, a 12th pass or above is required.
Q4What is the maximum age limit for the Junior Faculty positions?
A
The maximum age limit for all Junior Faculty positions is 35 years. There is no mention of age relaxation for specific categories in the provided data.
Q5Is there any application fee for the FDDI recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this FDDI recruitment. Candidates can apply without paying any fee.
Q6What is the salary structure for the Junior Faculty posts?
A
The Junior Faculty positions offer a monthly salary ranging from Rs. 30,000/- to Rs. 50,000/- per month, depending on the specific role and qualifications.
Q7Which mode of application is accepted by FDDI for this recruitment?
A
Candidates are required to apply online for the FDDI recruitment. The application process is conducted through the official FDDI website.
Q8What is the selection process for these FDDI posts?
A
The selection process for the Junior Faculty and Academic Support Staff posts at FDDI will be based on an Interview. Candidates shortlisted after the initial screening will be called for the interview.
Q9Are there any specific experience requirements for the Junior Faculty, Retail Operations post?
A
Yes, for the Junior Faculty, Retail Operations post, candidates need to have a Master Degree or Master Diploma of 2 years in a relevant field with minimum 55% marks or equivalent, along with 3 years of relevant experience.
