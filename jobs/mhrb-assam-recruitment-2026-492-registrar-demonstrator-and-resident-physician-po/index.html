Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have Indian citizenship as mentioned in Articles 5 to 8 of the Constitution of India. Candidates must have the requisite qualification and registration under the Assam Council of Medical Registration, Medical Council of India, or the National Medical Commission. Doctors serving under the Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Directorate of Health Services (DHS), National Health Mission (NHM) Assam, and other Government agencies are eligible to apply, and no separate NOC from the State Government is required for these posts.

Educational Qualification

A Postgraduate qualification of MD/MS/DNB/MDS in the concerned subject from an institute under any Indian University recognised by the National Medical Commission (previously MCI)/National Board of Education (NBE)/Dental Council of India is required for the following departments: Anaesthesiology, Dentistry, Dermatology, ENT, Emergency Medicine, FSM, Medicine, Microbiology, Orthopaedics, Ophthalmology, O&G, Pharmacology, Pathology, Psychiatry, Paediatrics, PM&R, Radiology, Radiation Oncology, Surgery, SPM (Community Medicine), TB & Chest (Pulmonary Medicine), and Nuclear Medicine. Anatomy Department: Postgraduate qualification MS (Anatomy)/M.Sc (Medical Anatomy) with Ph.D in Medical Anatomy, as per the latest notification of NMC, New Delhi. MHRB Assam Recruitment 2026: 492 Registrar, Demonstrator : Application Fee Details

The application fee can be paid in either of two ways: Through Treasury Challan under the Head of Account "0210-Medical and Public Health-01-Urban Health Services-800-Other Receipt", OR Online through the Government Receipt Accounting System (GRAS) at https://assamegras.gov.in . The printed copy of the Treasury Challan generated through GRAS should be submitted at the time of scrutiny of documents, with the applied post mentioned in the Remarks column. Fees are non-refundable, and no mode of deposit other than Treasury Challan (offline or via GRAS) will be accepted.
