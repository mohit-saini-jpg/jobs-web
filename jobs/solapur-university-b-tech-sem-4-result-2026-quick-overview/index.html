Solapur University Result 2026: Where to Check

Students can check their B.Tech Electrical Engineering Sem 4 result directly through the official Solapur University examination portal. The result has been published under the "Online Result - March 2026" section of the website.

  • Direct Result Link: https://www.sus.ac.in/examination/Online-Result-March-2026
  • Official Website: https://www.sus.ac.in/

How to Download Solapur University B.Tech Electrical Engineering Sem 4 Result 2026

Follow these steps to download your result online:

  • Visit the official Solapur University website at sus.ac.in.
  • Click on the "Examination" tab on the homepage menu.
  • Select "Online Result - March 2026" from the list of options.
  • Locate and click the link for B.Tech Electrical Engineering - Sem 4 (Fresh) or Sem 4 (Repeater), depending on your candidature.
  • The result opens as a PDF ledger listing multiple students together.
  • Use Ctrl+F (or the search function in your PDF viewer) to locate your Seat Number or PRN (Permanent Registration Number).
  • Download and save the PDF, or take a screenshot of your result row for reference.

What Information Does the Result PDF Contain?

Based on how Solapur University typically publishes B.Tech ledger results, students can expect to see the following details against their entry:

  • Seat Number
  • PRN (Permanent Registration Number)
  • College Name and College Code
  • Eligibility Status (ELIG: P for Pass, ELIG: E for Eligible/other status codes as used by the university)
  • Subject-wise and semester-wise performance

Individual subject-wise marks, grade points, SGPA, and CGPA details for this specific Sem 4 result are not available in the official notification provided; students should refer to their downloaded result PDF or the original Statement of Grades issued by their college for these figures.

Important Instructions for Students

  • The result published online is provisional and meant for immediate reference only.
  • The original mark sheet or Statement of Grades (SOG) issued through your college is the sole authentic document for official purposes.
  • If your result shows a status such as EHB/RR (result withheld pending a previous semester), it means an earlier semester result is incomplete. A revised result will be published once that process is finished.
  • Cross-check your name, seat number, PRN, and marks carefully after downloading the result.
  • Keep a saved copy of your result PDF, as online results may not remain accessible indefinitely once new result cycles are uploaded.
  • For discrepancies, students should contact their respective college or the university's examination department.

Revaluation and Verification

Specific revaluation or photocopy application dates for this B.Tech Electrical Engineering Sem 4 result are not available in the official notification.
