Goa Board HSSC Result 2025 OUT gbshse.in

The Goa Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education (GBSHSE) announced the HSSC Result 2025, March 27, at 5:00 PM. The Class 12 exams were conducted from February 10 to March 1, with a total of 17,686 students appearing. An official notice regarding the result declaration has been posted on the board's website. Once released, students can check their results online at gbshse.in and gbshse.gov.in. Additionally, schools will be able to download consolidated results through the school login portal starting March 29, 2025.

How to Check Goa Board 12TH HSSC Result 2025?

  • Students can check their Goa Class 12 HSSC results online by following these simple steps:
  • Visit the official website gbshse.in or gbshse.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, look for the ‘Goa HSSC Result 2025’ link in the Recent Announcements section.
  • Click on the link to proceed to the result login page.
  • Enter your seat number or roll number and solve the captcha.
  • Click Submit, and your result will be displayed on the screen.
  • Check your marks carefully and download the marksheet.
  • Take a printout for future reference.

For more details and updates, students and parents are advised to visit the official website of the Goa Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education.
