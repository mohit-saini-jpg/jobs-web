Bihar DCECE Paramedical Eligibility Criteria 2026 requires candidates to meet specific educational qualifications, age limits, and domicile conditions set by the Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) for admission to Para Medical (Intermediate Level) (PM) and Para Medical (Secondary Level) (PMM) courses.

Overview of Bihar DCECE 2026

The Bihar DCECE 2026 (Diploma Certificate Entrance Competitive Examination) is conducted by BCECEB for admissions into diploma-level Polytechnic Engineering (PE), Para Medical Secondary Level (PMM), and Para Medical Intermediate Level (PM) courses in government and private institutes across Bihar.

Applications opened on 19 March 2026 and will close on 21 April 2026. The exam is expected in May 2026. This entrance test helps students secure seats in paramedical programs like ANM, GNM, Dresser, Pharmacy Diploma, and other allied health courses.

Polytechnic Engineering (PE) Eligibility Criteria

Candidates seeking admission through the PE group must have passed or be appearing in the Class 10th examination from a recognized board, with Mathematics and Science as compulsory subjects. There is no minimum or maximum age limit for PE courses. Educational standards follow AICTE guidelines prescribed by the Department of Science, Technology & Technical Education, Bihar. No specific domicile proof is mandated beyond standard Bihar residency preferences in seat allocation.

Para Medical (Secondary Level) (PMM) Eligibility Criteria

For PMM (Para Medical Secondary Level), candidates must have passed or be appearing in Class 10th with Mathematics and Science subjects. The age limit is minimum 15 years and maximum 30 years as on 31 December 2026. This group mainly covers courses like Dresser in government hospitals and health institutions. Qualifications align with standards set by the Health Department, Government of Bihar. Candidates must be medically fit.

Para Medical (Intermediate Level) (PM) Eligibility Criteria

PM (Para Medical Intermediate Level) requires candidates to have passed or be appearing in Class 12th (Intermediate) from a recognized board. Science subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics) are preferred. Minimum marks are generally 40% in aggregate (with 5% relaxation for SC/ST categories in many programs like GNM).

  • For GNM/ANM Nursing: Minimum age 17 years, maximum 35 years (as per Indian Nursing Council provisions).
  • For other PM courses: Minimum 17 years, maximum 32 years.

All qualifications must meet Health Department, Bihar, and relevant council norms (e.g., INC for nursing). Domicile: Candidates whose parents/husband/wife are permanent residents of Bihar, or satisfy refugee/government employee conditions in Bihar, are preferred.

Important Note: Age is calculated as on 31 December 2026. Always verify exact requirements in the official notification, as some courses may have additional fitness or subject-specific criteria.

How to Apply for Bihar DCECE 2026

  • Visit the official website: bceceboard.bihar.gov.in
  • Click on the DCECE 2026 application link.
  • Register with basic details and fill the online form, selecting your course group (PE/PMM/PM).
  • Upload scanned documents (photo, signature, etc.).
  • Pay the application fee and submit.
