How To Apply

  • Visit the official website of NITK Surathkal and click on the faculty recruitment link available on the homepage (recruitment portal).

  • Register on the recruitment portal with a valid email ID and mobile number, then log in to access the online application form.

  • Fill in all required personal, academic, and experience details carefully; ensure eligibility as per department and post applied for.

  • Upload all relevant documents: identity proof, proof of date of birth, academic mark sheets and degree certificates (UG, PG, PhD), category certificates (SC/ST/OBC-NCL/EWS/PwD), experience certificates and salary slips, publications list (SCOPUS/Web of Science), proof of projects, patents, PhD guidance, administrative roles, outreach, awards, etc.

  • Pay the non-refundable application processing fee (as per category) via NEFT/RTGS/online transfer to the specified NITK bank account and upload proof of payment in PDF format.

  • Download, hand-fill and sign the mandatory “Attestation Form” (Annexure II), then upload the scanned copy in the portal.

  • Candidates in Government/Quasi Govt/Universities/PSUs must upload No Objection Certificate or Self Declaration (Annexure I) and provide Vigilance Clearance Certificate at the time of interview.

  • Verify all details and documents in the online form and then click on “Submit” to complete the application process.

  • After successful submission, download and save the PDF of the completed application form for future reference.

  • At the time of interview, candidates must produce the printed application form, original Attestation Form, and all original supporting documents along with self-attested copies.

Important Instructions

  • Candidates must read the detailed instructions and recruitment policies carefully before filling the online application form and ensure that they satisfy all eligibility criteria.

  • Reservation for SC/ST/OBC-NCL/EWS/PwD is applicable as per Government of India norms; OBC-NCL/EWS certificates must be issued on or after 01.04.2026 in prescribed format.

  • Persons with Disabilities with at least 40% permanent disability must upload prescribed disability/medical certificate issued by competent authorities. Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is NIT Surathkal Faculty Recruitment 2026?

NIT Karnataka, Surathkal has launched Faculty Recruitment 2026 (Ref. No. 5210‑Advt./Faculty‑NITK‑Estt./2026/B1) to fill 84 regular faculty positions at the levels of Assistant Professor (Grade II & I), Associate Professor, and Professor across 14 departments.

Q2. How many faculty vacancies are announced in NITK Surathkal Recruitment 2026?

The recruitment notification specifies 82 vacancies for Assistant Professor (Grade II & I), 1 vacancy for Associate Professor (Computer Science & Engineering) and 1 vacancy for Professor (Information Technology), making a total of 84 faculty posts.

Q3. What are the important dates for NIT Surathkal Faculty Recruitment 2026?

The online application portal opened on 04 May 2026 at 4:00 p.m. IST, and the last date for submitting online applications is 02 June 2026 up to 11:59 p.m. IST through the NITK recruitment portal.

Q4. Which departments are included in NITK Faculty Recruitment 2026?

Faculty positions are available in Chemical, Chemistry, Civil, Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical & Electronics, Electronics & Communication, Information Technology, Mathematical & Computational Sciences, Mechanical, Metallurgical & Materials, Mining, Physics, Humanities/Social Sciences & Management, and Water Resources & Ocean Engineering.

Q5. What is the eligibility criteria for Assistant Professor posts at NIT Surathkal?

Candidates must have First Class at both UG and PG levels in the relevant discipline (treated as 60% or 6.5/10 CGPA if class not specified) and a PhD in the specified area of specialization, with eligibility also for candidates who completed a direct PhD after UG or an integrated programme from reputed institutes, as per NIT Statutes.

Q6. Are Indian Nationals only eligible, or can OCI holders apply for NITK faculty posts?

The notification clearly states that qualified Indian Nationals and Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) cardholders from all over the world are eligible to apply for NITK Surathkal faculty positions.

Q7. What is the application fee for NITK Surathkal Faculty Recruitment 2026?

The application processing fee is Rs. 2,500 (inclusive of GST) per application for General/OBC/EWS candidates and Rs. 1,250 (inclusive of GST) for SC/ST/PwD candidates, payable only through online/NEFT/RTGS transfer to the specified NITK bank account.

Q8. What are the pay levels for Assistant Professor, Associate Professor and Professor at NIT Surathkal ?

As per the 7th CPC: Assistant Professor Grade II is in Pay Level 10, Assistant Professor Grade I in Pay Level 12, Associate Professor in Pay Level 13A2, and Professor in Pay Level 14A, along with allowances as per Central Government norms.
