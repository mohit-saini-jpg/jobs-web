J&K UG Admission 2026

J&K UG Admission 2026 last date has been officially extended. The Directorate of Colleges, Higher Education Department, Government of Jammu and Kashmir has extended the last date for submission of online application forms for Undergraduate (UG) admissions in Government Degree Colleges of J&K for the academic session 2026-27 till 30th May 2026 (5:00 PM).

Candidates who have not yet applied can now submit their online application forms and update their college/programme preferences on the official admission portal https://jkadmissions.in. This extension has been granted in view of representations received from aspirants. All other terms and conditions remain the same as per the original notification. Interested students are advised to apply before the revised deadline to avoid missing the opportunity.

Eligibility Criteria for J&K UG Admission 2026

Candidates who have passed Class 12th (10+2) examination from a recognized board are eligible to apply for UG admissions in Government Degree Colleges of J&K. Detailed eligibility criteria as per the original admission advertisement notice will apply.

How to Apply for J&K UG Admission 2026

  • Visit the official admission portal: https://jkadmissions.in
  • Register and fill the online application form.
  • Upload required documents.
  • Add or update college and programme preferences.
  • Submit the application before 30th May 2026 (5:00 PM).
