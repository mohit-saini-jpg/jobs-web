How to Check RTE Karnataka Lottery Result 2026

Follow the steps given below to check and download the RTE Karnataka Lottery Result 2026 online in a simple and user-friendly manner.

  • Visit the official RTE Karnataka admission portal at the Department of School Education website schooleducation.karnataka.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the RTE admissions section and look for the RTE Karnataka Lottery Result or Application Status link.
  • Click on the Application Status or Lottery Result link, which redirects to the RTE application status page at sdcedn.karnataka.gov.in.
  • Enter the required details such as Application Number and registered Mobile Number in the given fields.
  • Submit the details to view the RTE Karnataka Lottery Result 2026 seat allotment status on the screen.
  • Check the allotted school, category, and other details carefully and download or print the result for future reference.
