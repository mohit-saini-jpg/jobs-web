How to Check TG EDCET Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your TG EDCET Result 2026.

  • Visit the official website: https://edcet.tgche.ac.in/
  • Click the relevant result link for your course/semester (B.Ed/M.Ed 1st/2nd/3rd Semester).
  • Enter your Hall Ticket Number and Date of Birth (or other required credentials).
  • Click 'Submit' or 'View Result'.
  • Your TG EDCET Result 2026 will appear on screen; verify details and click 'Download' or 'Print' to save a copy.
