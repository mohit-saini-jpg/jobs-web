Job Overview

The District Social Welfare Office, Majuli (DSWO Majuli) has officially announced the Case Worker, MPW and More Posts Recruitment 2026 with 7 openings. Qualification / Eligibility

Case worker: Bachelor's degree in law/social work/sociology/social science/Psychology with minimum 3 years of experience of working on women related relevant domains in a government or non-government projects/programme. Counsellor: Degree/Diploma in Psychology/Psychiatry/neurosciences with a background in the health sector/Social Work/Sociology/ with minimum 3 years of experience of working with a government or non-government health project/programme at the District level. Office Assistant: Bachelor's degree with a diploma in Computer/IT etc. with 3 years' experience in office documentation and web-based reporting formats, video conferencing at state or district level with government or non-governmental/IT based organization. Multi-purpose worker: High school passed or equivalent will be preferred, with any women who is literate with knowledge/experience of working in the relevant domain. Security Guard: HSLC passed, with minimum 2 years of experience of working as a security personnel in a government or reputed organization at the District/State level.
