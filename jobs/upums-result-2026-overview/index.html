How to Check UPUMS Result 2026 Online

Students can check and download their UPUMS Saifai Etawah result by following these steps:

  • Visit the official UPUMS examination result portal: https://www.upums.ac.in/examinationresult.
  • Look for the relevant notification link matching your course (B.Pharm or B.Sc Nursing), year, and semester.
  • Open the PDF result summary and locate your roll number or enrollment number.
  • Check your marks, SGPA (where applicable), and result status (Pass / Carry Over / Fail).
  • Download or save the result summary for future reference.

Note: These are computer-generated result summaries. If any discrepancy is found, students are advised to contact the Office of the Controller of Examination, UPUMS, Saifai, Etawah, as stated on the official notifications.
