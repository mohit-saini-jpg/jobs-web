TAT HS Final Answer Key 2026 Out: Download Subject-Wise PDF at sebexam.org

TAT HS 2026 final answer key has been officially released on June 4, 2026, at sebexam.org by the State Examination Board (SEB), Gujarat. Candidates who appeared for the Teacher Aptitude Test – Higher Secondary (TAT-HS) Prelims on April 12, 2026, can now download subject-wise final answer keys in both Hindi Medium and English Medium.

The provisional answer key was released on May 2, 2026, with objections accepted until May 10, and the final key now incorporates all valid responses. SEB Gujarat is expected to declare the TAT HS 2026 preliminary result and category-wise cut-off marks very soon, with qualifying candidates becoming eligible for the TAT-HS Mains examination.

How to Download TAT HS 2026 Final Answer Key (Step-by-Step)

  • Step 1: Visit the Official Website: Open your web browser and go to sebexam.org (SEB Exam Portal).
  • Step 2: Locate the Notice Board: On the homepage, scroll down to the "Notice Board" or recent notifications section.
  • Step 3: Select Your Medium: Click on the link titled "TAT-HS Prelims – Final Answer Key of 2026" corresponding to your medium (Hindi or English).
  • Step 4: Choose Your Subject: Click on your respective teaching subject to trigger the automated download of the master key PDF.
  • Step 5: Verify Your Answers: Open the downloaded PDF and cross-match your exam series (Series A, B, C, D, or E) against the verified solutions.

How to Download Your OMR Response Sheet

Candidates can also download their original OMR sheets for precise response comparison:

  • Visit sebexam.org
  • Navigate to the dedicated OMR login window
  • Enter your Confirmation Number/Roll Number and Date of Birth
  • Download your individual OMR sheet PDF

What Lies Next for Candidates?

With the final answer keys now public, SEB Gujarat is expected to announce the official preliminary results along with category-wise cut-off marks very shortly.

Candidates who secure marks above the qualifying threshold will receive direct eligibility to register for the upcoming TAT-HS Mains Written Examination.
