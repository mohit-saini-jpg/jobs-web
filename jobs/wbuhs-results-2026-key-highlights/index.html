West Bengal University of Health Sciences has declared the WBUHS Exam Result 2026 for key undergraduate health science programmes, including the B.Sc Critical Care Technology (C.C.T.) Part 3 December 2025 - January 2026 examination and the 3rd Professional BDS January 2026 examination. WBUHS Result 2026 has been published online on the official website – wbuhs.ac.in – through the dedicated examination results and student result portals. Students from affiliated colleges can now log in with their credentials to check their subject wise marks and result status.

To access the marksheet, candidates need to visit the WBUHS student result page, choose the appropriate course and examination session and enter their university roll number or registration number. After downloading the online result, students should carefully verify their name, college, course, part/professional year, subject wise marks and overall pass/fail status and contact the university examination branch or their college immediately if any discrepancy is found.

WBUHS Results 2026 Key Highlights

How to Download WBUHS Exam Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your WBUHS B.Sc CCT Part 3 or 3rd Professional BDS Result 2026:

  • Visit the WBUHS examination results page: https://wbuhs.ac.in/examination-results/ or directly open the student result portal: https://charaka.wbuhs.ac.in/StudentResultStatusPublish/index.
  • If starting from the results page, locate the notification for: Result of B.Sc Critical Care Technology (C.C.T.) Part 3 December 2025 – January 2026 Examination, or Result of 3rd Professional B.D.S. January 2026 Examination, then click the provided result link to reach the student result portal.
  • Result of B.Sc Critical Care Technology (C.C.T.) Part 3 December 2025 – January 2026 Examination, or
  • Result of 3rd Professional B.D.S. January 2026 Examination, then click the provided result link to reach the student result portal.
  • On the student result portal, select your course/exam from the dropdown list if required.
  • Enter your University Roll Number / Registration Number and any other details requested (such as date of birth or captcha).
  • Click on Submit / View Result to display your subject wise marks and overall result status.
  • Download or print the online marksheet for future reference in academic records, registration procedures and higher studies or job applications.
