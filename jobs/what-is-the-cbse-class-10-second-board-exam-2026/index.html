What Is the CBSE Class 10 Second Board Exam 2026?

CBSE introduced the second board exam, often called the improvement or supplementary exam, to give Class 10 students a chance to better their performance after the main board exams. Knowing the exam and result timeline helps students understand when and how their second attempt scores will be available.

The Class 10 second board examination 2026 was conducted from May 15 to May 21, 2026. Following this, CBSE compiles and processes results before releasing them on its official digital platforms.

Where to Check CBSE Class 10 Improvement Result 2026 by Roll Number

Students often search for ways to access their second exam scores quickly once they are announced. This section covers the official websites where the result will be hosted and how the roll-number-based lookup works.

CBSE does not publish a downloadable roll-number-wise result list in PDF format. Instead, students must individually look up their result by entering their roll number along with other details on the official portal. Once live, the result link will be available on:

  • cbseresults.nic.in
  • results.cbse.nic.in
  • cbse.gov.in

Credentials Required to Check CBSE Second Exam Result 2026

Before the result link is activated, students should keep their Phase 2 admit card ready, since the result lookup requires specific identification details. This section lists exactly what information is needed.

To access the CBSE Class 10 second board result roll number wise, students must enter:

  • Roll number
  • School number
  • Admit card ID
  • Date of birth

Steps to Check CBSE Class 10 Second Board Result 2026

Once the credentials are ready, the actual process of viewing and downloading the result is straightforward. Here are the steps students need to follow on the official portal.

  • Visit the official CBSE result website: cbseresults.nic.in
  • Click on the link titled "Secondary School Examination Results 2026 (Phase II)"
  • Enter the roll number, school number, admit card ID, and date of birth
  • Complete the captcha verification and click Submit
  • The result will be displayed on screen; download and save the PDF for future reference

Where to Find School Number and Admit Card ID

Many students are unsure where to locate the school number and admit card ID needed for the result login. This section explains where these details are printed.

Both the school number and admit card ID are printed on the Class 10 Phase 2 admit card issued by CBSE before the second board exams. Students should refer to their hall ticket to retrieve these details accurately.

Other Ways to Access the CBSE Class 10 Improvement Result 2026

Apart from the main result portal, CBSE typically offers alternative methods for students to retrieve their scorecards. This section outlines these options.

  • DigiLocker: Students can access their marksheet and passing certificate via digilocker.gov.in or the DigiLocker mobile app.
  • UMANG App: The scorecard can be downloaded by searching for the "CBSE" service within the app.
  • SMS/IVRS: CBSE may share specific phone numbers or SMS formats on the official result day notification.
