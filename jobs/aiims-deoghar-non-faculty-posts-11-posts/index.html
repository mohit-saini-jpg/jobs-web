  • 3Attach self-attested photocopies of date of birth certificate, degree certificates, experience certificates, caste certificate (if applicable), No Objection Certificate (if working in Govt/Autonomous body) etc. in the order mentioned in the advertisement.

FAQs

Q1For how long will the AIIMS Deoghar Non-Faculty posts be filled on contract?
A
These posts are purely on contract for 11 months or until regular appointments against the same posts are made, whichever is earlier.
Q2What is the mode of application for AIIMS Deoghar Non-Faculty contractual posts?
A
The application has to be submitted in offline mode only through the prescribed application form and sent by post to AIIMS Deoghar.
Q3Is there any application fee for SC/ST/PwD/Women candidates?
A
No, there is no application fee for SC, ST, PwD and Women candidates.
Q4What is the application fee for General and OBC candidates?
A
The application fee is ₹3000 for General category and ₹1000 for OBC category candidates, payable via Demand Draft in favour of AIIMS Deoghar.
Q5Will contractual appointees be eligible for Government benefits like PF or pension?
A
No, contractual appointees will not be entitled to benefits like Provident Fund, Pension, Gratuity, Seniority or Promotion available to regular Government servants.
