UPSC Advt 08/2026 Age Limit

For every post, the age is further relaxable by up to 10 years for PwBD candidates, subject to the post being identified as suitable for the relevant category of disability, and subject to the maximum age of the applicant on the closing date not exceeding 56 years. Additional relaxations apply for SC/ST candidates (up to 5 years), OBC candidates (up to 3 years), Ex-Servicemen and Commissioned Officers (5 years, subject to conditions), and Central/UT Government servants (up to 5 years), as detailed in the "Concessions & Relaxations" section of the notification. The date for determining age and all other eligibility is the closing date for submission of the online application, i.e., 31 July 2026.

UPSC 08/2026 Selection Process

Selection is made by the Union Public Service Commission through Recruitment by Selection, which may involve a Recruitment Test (RT) or Combined Recruitment Test (CRT) followed by an interview, or interview only, depending on the post - the notification does not specify which mode applies to each individual post beyond noting that a Recruitment Test may form part of the selection process. Where a Recruitment Test forms part of selection, candidates must clear the minimum level of suitability at both the RT stage and the interview stage for their respective category. The minimum level of suitability in interview, irrespective of whether selection is by interview alone or RT plus interview, is: UR – 50 marks, OBC – 45 marks, SC/ST/PH – 40 marks, out of a total of 100 interview marks.
