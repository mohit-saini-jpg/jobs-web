Selection Process

  • Selection for engagement of technical personnel will be based on interview and medical examination. No written examination is conducted. Final empanelment is subject to verification of eligibility and medical fitness.
  • Stage 1 - Interview
  • Candidates who fulfill minimum eligibility criteria will be called for a personal interview. Interview schedules will be notified after the closing date via the RailTel official website and through email to registered candidates. No travelling expenses will be reimbursed for attending the interview.
  • Stage 2 - Document Verification
  • Shortlisted candidates will be required to produce original documents along with self-attested copies at the time of interview. Candidates failing to produce valid originals will not be allowed to proceed further.
  • Stage 3 - Medical Examination
  • Final engagement is subject to the candidate being declared medically fit by a medical authority designated by RailTel Corporation. Candidates not meeting prescribed medical standards will not be considered for engagement.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply offline only. No online application mode is available. Duly filled and signed application form (Annexure-II as per the notification), along with self-attested copies of all relevant enclosures, must be sent by post or courier so as to reach on or before 30-05-2026 during office hours. The envelope must be clearly superscribed: "APPLICATION FOR TECHNICAL FOR KSWAN PROJECT OF RAILTEL IN SOUTHERN REGION ON CONTRACT BASIS."
  • Documents Required to be Attached with the Application Form:
  • Matriculation certificate (for age proof)
  • Certificates and mark sheets in support of required educational/professional qualifications
  • Experience certificate from current/previous employers indicating length of experience; if current employer's certificate is unavailable, appointment letter along with first and latest pay slips may be submitted
  • Caste certificate in prescribed format for OBC-NCL, EWS, SC, and ST candidates
  • Disability Certificate (for PwBD candidates)
  • Copy of CV with complete employment history, duly signed with photograph
  • Photo ID proof and address proof – Passport / Aadhaar Card / Driving License / PAN Card / Voter ID Card (issued by Central/State Govt.)
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) if currently working in any Govt./PSU or public body substantially funded by the Govt.
  • NO DOCUMENT SHOULD BE SENT DIRECTLY TO THE CORPORATE OFFICE. All applications must be sent only to the Territory Office address in Bengaluru. Each page of the attached documents must be signed by the candidate.
  • Send Application to: Territory Manager/SBC, RailTel Corporation of India Limited, Territory Office, No. 6/1, 12th Main, Vasanthnagar, Opp. Mount Carmel College Gate-1 Road, BENGALURU – 560052
  • For updates and corrigendum: Visit www.railtelindia.com regularly | Call letters for interviews will be sent via email only.
