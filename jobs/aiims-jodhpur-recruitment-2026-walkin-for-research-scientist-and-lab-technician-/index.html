Qualification / Eligibility

Research Scientist-II (Medical): MBBS recognized by MCI or Equivalent degree with 4 years R&D / teaching experience. Desirable: Experience in Microbiology/Virological techniques, Post-doctoral research/training in relevant subjects, Knowledge of Computer Applications and handling the Big Data. Research Scientist - II (Non-Medical): M. Sc. + Ph. D in relevant subject + 4 years R&D/Teaching exp. Desirable: Experience in Microbiology/Virological techniques. Post-doctoral research/training in relevant subjects. Knowledge of Computer Applications or Bioinformatics and handling Big Data. Lab Technician: B.Sc. / intermediates + Diploma in MLT + 5-year Lab Experience. Desirable: Experience in Microbiology/Virological techniques.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the AIIMS Jodhpur recruitment?
A
The last date to submit applications via email for online interviews and to report for the walk-in interview is 14 June 2026 and 15 June 2026 respectively. Ensure all documents are submitted by the specified deadlines.
Q2What are the eligibility criteria for the Research Scientist-II posts?
A
For Research Scientist-II (Medical), an MBBS degree with 4 years of R&D/teaching experience is required. For Research Scientist-II (Non-Medical), an M.Sc. + Ph.D. in a relevant subject with 4 years of R&D/teaching experience is necessary.
Q3What is the age limit for the Lab Technician position?
A
The maximum age limit for the Lab Technician position is 25 years. Candidates should ensure they meet this age requirement before applying for the role.
Q4How many vacancies are available in this recruitment drive?
A
There are a total of 3 vacancies announced by AIIMS Jodhpur for this recruitment drive. These vacancies are for the posts of Research Scientist-II (Medical), Research Scientist-II (Non-Medical), and Lab Technician.
Q5What is the salary offered for the Research Scientist-II posts?
A
The selected candidates for the Research Scientist-II (Medical) and Research Scientist-II (Non-Medical) posts will receive a salary of Rs. 67,000 per month plus HRA. The tenure for these positions is 6 months, extendable.
Q6Can candidates apply through both walk-in and email?
A
Yes, candidates can apply through both walk-in and email. For offline candidates, a walk-in interview is scheduled. For online applications (Post No. 1, 2), candidates can submit their applications and bio-data via email.
Q7What documents are required for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates attending the walk-in interview must carry all original documents along with one set of self-attested photocopies, including ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates, and category certificates if applicable.
Q8What is the selection process for these posts?
A
The selection process for these posts will be based on a Walk-In-Interview and an Online Interview. Candidates will be evaluated based on their qualifications, experience, and performance in the interview.
Q9What is the location of the AIIMS recruitment?
A
The recruitment is being conducted by the All India Institute of Medical Sciences Jodhpur (AIIMS), located in Jodhpur, Rajasthan.
Q10Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
No application fee is mentioned in the official notification for this recruitment drive. Candidates are advised to check the official notification for any further details regarding fees.
