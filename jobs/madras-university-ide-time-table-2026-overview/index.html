How to Download the Madras University IDE Time Table 2026

Important Notes for Candidates

  • Practical examinations, wherever prescribed, are conducted only at the end of Practical Classes at PCP Centres, and only for candidates who have completed the required attendance and record note books.
  • Project classes are compulsory for eligibility to submit a Project Report; those who miss Project Guidance classes cannot submit the Project Report.
  • Project classes will not be conducted during supplementary examinations.
  • For CLIS practical exams, they are held only at the Chennai Centre, with dates announced separately.
  • Candidates should always confirm their exact schedule using their hall ticket, since some subject codes and dates vary depending on the year of admission (e.g., A21, A22, A23, A24, C20, C21 batches).
