How To Apply

  • Eligible candidates must download the prescribed application form from the official DSSC website (www.dssc.gov.in). The form must be opened and filled using Adobe Reader only, typed in block letters, and taken as a colour printout - handwritten forms will not be accepted. Separate applications must be submitted for each post along with requisite documents and a separate Demand Draft. The application along with all attachments must be sent by post to the address below within 21 days from the date of publication of the advertisement in the Employment News.
  • Documents to be Attached with Application (self-attested copies):
  • Typed application form (in block letters, colour printout) with recent colour photograph (white background, taken within last 3 months) and candidate's signature - mandatory
  • Self-attested copies of Aadhaar Card
  • Self-attested copies of 10th and 12th Standard Mark Sheets
  • Self-attested copies of UG/PG Mark Sheets & Diploma/ITI Certificates (as applicable)
  • Self-attested copy of Driving License (for Civilian Motor Driver post)
  • Self-attested copy of Experience Certificate(s) (wherever required)
  • Self-attested copy of Computer Course Certificate (as applicable)
  • Self-attested copies of valid OBC/SC/ST/EWS Certificate (OBC/EWS certificates must be issued on or after 01-04-2025)
  • Self-attested copy of PwBD Certificate (for PwBD candidates - disability must be permanent and not less than 40%)
  • Self-attested copy of ESM Discharge Certificate (for Ex-Servicemen)
  • No Objection Certificate from present employer (for Central/State Govt/PSU/Autonomous Body permanent employees)
  • A/C Payee Demand Draft of Rs. 100/- (Non-Refundable) drawn in favour of "DSSC Direct Recruitment 2026" payable at Canara Bank, DSSC Branch (Code 16239)
  • Self-addressed envelope of 10x22 cm with Rs. 56/- postage stamp pasted
  • No TA/DA will be paid for attending the written examination or skill test. DSSC will not be responsible for any postal delay. No interim correspondence regarding application status will be entertained.
  • Send Application to: The Commandant, Defence Services Staff College, Wellington (Nilgiris) – 643 231, Tamil Nadu.
  • Mention on envelope: "APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF ___________"
