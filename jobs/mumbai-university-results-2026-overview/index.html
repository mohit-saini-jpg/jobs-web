1. Bachelor of Performing Arts (Music-Tabla) -Semester V

Conducted by the MU-337 Department of Music. Subjects covered in this result:

  • Viva Voce (Practical)
  • History of Music (Theory)
  • Concert Performance (Practical)
  • Method of Improvisation (Practical)
  • Studio Playing Technique (Practical)
  • Riyaz Techniques Level 3 (VSC)
  • Indian Knowledge System (IKS) (Theory)
  • Music Teaching in Society (CEP)

Total marks for the semester: 550.

2. B.E. Automation and Robotics -Semester VII (CBCS, C-Scheme)

This result covers core and elective subjects such as Machine Vision System, AI for Robots, Mobile Robotics, Advanced Computational Techniques, Natural Language Processing, Internet Communication Engineering, Machinery Diagnostics, Big Data Analytics, Augmented and Virtual Reality, Block Chain, Product Life-cycle Management, Reliability Engineering, Management Information Systems, Design of Experiments, Operations Research, Cyber Security and Laws, Disaster Management and Mitigation Measures, Energy Audit and Management, and Development Engineering, along with practical/term-work heads: Machine Vision System, AI for Robots, Industrial Skills, and Major Project -I.

3. M.Sc. Data Analytics -Semester IV (Joint degree, Dept of I.T.)

Conducted by the MU-566 Department of Information Technology. Subjects covered:

  • Deep Learning (Theory)
  • Natural Language Processing (Theory)
  • Business Intelligence (Theory)
  • Deep Learning Practical
  • Natural Language Processing Practical
  • Research Project

Total marks for the semester: 650.

4. M.Sc. Maths -Semester III (Supplementary)

Conducted by the MU-336 Department of Mathematics. Core subjects listed:

  • Algebra III
  • Differential Geometry
  • Measure Theory and Integration
  • Research Project
  • Probability Theory and Statistics

Candidates were examined in one elective from: Integral Transforms, Financial Mathematics, or R Programming, depending on their choice. Total marks for the semester: 550.

5. M.Sc. Maths -Semester I (Supplementary)

Also conducted by the MU-336 Department of Mathematics. Core subjects listed:

  • Algebra I
  • Real Analysis
  • Ordinary Differential Equations
  • Research Methodology
  • Discrete Mathematics and Number Theory

Candidates were examined in one elective from: Commutative Algebra, Graph Theory, Programming in Python, or Linear & Non-Linear Programming, depending on their choice. Total marks for the semester: 550.

6. B.E. Mechanical Engineering (Automobile) -Semester I (Supplementary)

Conducted at MU-0010, Anjuman-I-Islam's M. H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering. Subjects covered:

  • Applied Mathematics-I
  • Applied Physics (Theory + Lab)
  • Applied Chemistry (Theory + Lab)
  • Engineering Mechanics (Theory + Lab)
  • Basic Electrical & Electronics Engineering (Theory + Lab)
  • Professional and Communication Ethics (Theory + Term Work)
  • Engineering Workshop-I
  • C Programming
  • Induction cum Universal Human Values

Total marks for the semester: 800.

How to Check Your Mumbai University Result Online

  • Go to the official result portal: https://www.mumresults.in/
  • Select your course, semester, and the relevant examination (Regular or Supplementary, May 2026/Summer 2026).
  • Enter your seat number/PRN as required by the portal.
  • Submit to view your result.
  • Download or print your result for future reference.

The exact on-screen steps of the mumresults.in portal (e.g., additional login fields) are not described in the source register, so only the general process based on standard seat-number lookup is given here.
