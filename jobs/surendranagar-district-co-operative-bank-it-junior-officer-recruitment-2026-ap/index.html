Job Role / Nature of Work

Selected candidates will supervise the bank's helpdesk, system administration, and communication infrastructure teams. Their responsibilities will include ensuring uninterrupted IT operations, strong networking, cyber security, system reliability, and IT compliance.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must hold one of the following degrees from a recognized university: M.C.A. B.Tech (IT) M.Sc. (IT) B.E. (IT) Or an equivalent degree Candidates must have scored 55% or more marks (or equivalent CGPA) at the graduate/post-graduate level. Note: Candidates currently studying in the final year/semester of the required degree are not eligible to apply. Candidates must have a minimum of 2 years of experience in the IT field. Preference will be given to candidates with 2 years or more of experience in the banking/financial IT industry.
