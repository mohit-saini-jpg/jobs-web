UGC NET June Admit Card 2026

The National Testing Agency is expected to release the UGC NET June Admit Card 2026 on 15 June 2026 on the official website. Candidates appearing for the June session exam can download their hall ticket online once the link becomes active.

How to Download UGC NET June Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official website ugcnet.nta.nic.in.
  • Click on the UGC NET June 2026 Admit Card link.
  • Enter your application number and date of birth.
  • Fill in the security code if asked.
  • Submit the details.
  • Your admit card will appear on the screen.
  • Download it and take a clear printout.

If the website is busy, try again after some time.

Details Mentioned on the Admit Card

The UGC NET hall ticket generally includes:

  • Candidate’s name.
  • Application number.
  • Roll number.
  • Photograph and signature.
  • Subject code and paper details.
  • Exam date and shift timing.
  • Exam city and centre address.
  • Important exam instructions.

Candidates should verify all information carefully. If any detail is incorrect, they should contact NTA immediately.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the UGC NET June Admit Card 2026.
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence or Passport.
  • A recent passport-size photograph, if required in the instructions.

Without the admit card and valid ID, entry to the exam hall may not be permitted.

Exam-Day Instructions

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time.
  • Carry only allowed items.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators or any electronic devices.
  • Follow all instructions given by the invigilators.
  • Keep the admit card safe even after the exam.
