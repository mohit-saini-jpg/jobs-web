FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies announced under RCF Recruitment 2026?
A
Rashtriya Chemicals and Fertilizers Limited (RCF) has announced a total of 46 vacancies, including 31 Honorary Doctor posts and 15 Panel Doctor posts across Mumbai and Navi Mumbai locations.
Q2What is the last date to apply for RCF Honorary Doctors & Panel Doctors Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for receipt of applications by post is 31 July 2026. Applications received after this date will not be considered.
Q3What qualifications are required for RCF Honorary Doctor posts?
A
Candidates must possess the prescribed specialist qualifications such as MD, MS, DNB, MDS, BAMS, BHMS, DM, MCh, or equivalent degrees along with the required post-qualification experience in the relevant specialty.
Q4What is the eligibility criteria for RCF Panel Doctor posts?
A
Applicants must hold an MBBS degree, have a valid Medical Council registration, possess a minimum of 10 years of experience (excluding internship), and operate their own clinic/dispensary in the specified panel area.
Q5Is there any application fee for RCF Recruitment 2026?
A
No. The notification does not prescribe any application fee for Honorary Doctor or Panel Doctor applications.
Q6What is the selection process for RCF Honorary Doctors & Panel Doctors Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection will be based on application scrutiny followed by a personal interview. Eligible candidates will be informed about the interview schedule separately.
Q7What is the tenure of appointment for RCF Honorary Doctors and Panel Doctors?
A
The engagement period is three years, with Honorary Doctor appointments scheduled from 01 January 2027 to 31 December 2029.
Q8How can candidates apply for RCF Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must submit the prescribed application form along with required documents by post to the Deputy General Manager (HR) U, Trombay Unit, Chembur, Mumbai. Online or email applications are not accepted.
