How to Apply

  • 1Duly filled and signed application in the prescribed format (separate for Honorary Doctor / Panel Doctor).
  • 2Recent passport-size photograph affixed and duly signed on the application form.
  • 3Attested photostat copies of all educational qualification certificates.
  • 4Attested photostat copies of experience certificates from all organizations/hospitals.
  • 5Copy of valid Medical Council Registration certificate (with number, date, and state).
  • 6For Panel Doctors: details of clinic/dispensary including address, telephone, timings, facilities available, and details of any existing empanelment with other organizations.
  • 7Research papers / articles published, if any (for Honorary Doctors).
  • 8Last date for receipt of application is 31-07-2026 - applications received after this date will not be considered.
  • 9The post applied for must be written at the top of the left-hand corner of the envelope.
  • 10Separate applications are required for Honorary Doctor and Panel Doctor posts.
  • 11Engagement is purely temporary in nature and will not constitute an employer-employee relationship with RCF Ltd.
  • 12Engagement may be terminated by giving one month’s notice by either side.
  • 13The honorarium/reimbursement rates will be conveyed only at the time of personal Interview.
  • 14Management reserves the right to consider applications of doctors with lesser experience than advertised, if applications received are less than three times the number of advertised posts.
  • 15Management reserves the right to modify the number of posts and to cancel/alter this advertisement partially or completely without communicating the same to candidates.
  • 16No written or telephonic correspondence will be entertained after the closing date.
  • 17Company’s decision about engagement will be final and binding.
