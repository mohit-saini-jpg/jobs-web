DRDO DEAL Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27 - Selection Process

Depending upon the number of vacancies available in various categories and disciplines, selection of candidates will be carried out by short-listing on the basis of academic merit / written test / interview / walk-in interview or a combination of the above, subject to satisfactory document verification. DEAL reserves the right to adopt its own method of short-listing and is NOT obliged to shortlist all candidates who fulfil the minimum requirements. Mere fulfilment of eligibility does not qualify a candidate for selection. Canvassing/influencing in any form shall lead to disqualification. Selection shall be deemed final only after the online approval of the apprenticeship contract by the competent authority. If online contract approval is not granted due to any document discrepancy, the apprenticeship shall be deemed cancelled.

DRDO DEAL Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for DRDO DEAL Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27?
A
The last date is 30 days from the date of publication of this advertisement on https://drdo.gov.in/drdo/en/offerings/vacancies. Candidates must visit the DRDO official website to confirm the exact advertisement publication date and calculate the last date accordingly.
Q2How many total vacancies are notified in DRDO DEAL Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27?
A
A total of 77 apprentice training seats are notified - 19 for Graduate Apprentices, 08 for Diploma Apprentices, and 50 for ITI Apprentices across multiple disciplines and trades.
Q3What is the stipend for DRDO DEAL Apprentices 2026-27?
A
Graduate Apprentices receive Rs. 12,300/- per month, Diploma Apprentices receive Rs. 10,900/- per month, and ITI Apprentices receive Rs. 10,560/- per month. The stipend is funded 50% by DEAL and 50% from BOAT under the DBT scheme and is disbursed directly to the apprentice's Aadhaar-seeded bank account.
Q4How to apply for DRDO DEAL Graduate/Diploma Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27?
A
Graduate and Diploma candidates must register and apply online through the NATS 2.0 portal at https://nats.education.gov.in using DEAL ID: NUKDDC000015. ITI candidates must apply through the NAPS portal at https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in using Establishment Code: E01160500005.
Q5Can candidates who passed their qualifying exam before 2022 apply for DRDO DEAL Apprenticeship 2026-27?
A
No. Only candidates who passed the qualifying examination in the last five years - i.e., 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025, or 2026 - are eligible. Candidates with earlier passing years are not eligible.
Q6Is prior work experience or apprenticeship training a disqualification?
A
Yes. Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing apprenticeship training in any organization, or who have post-qualification work experience of 1 year or more, are NOT eligible.
Q7What is the age limit for DRDO DEAL Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27?
A
The minimum age is 18 years. The maximum age and applicable relaxations are governed by the Apprentices Act 1961, Apprentice Rules 1992, and NATS 2.0/NAPS guidelines.
Q8What is the selection process for DRDO DEAL Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27?
A
Selection may be based on academic merit, written test, interview, walk-in interview, or a combination of these, depending on the number of applicants, subject to satisfactory document verification. DEAL reserves the right to adopt its own shortlisting method.
Q9Will DRDO/DEAL offer employment after completion of Apprenticeship Training?
A
No. DEAL explicitly states that NO employment - permanent, temporary, or contractual - is offered to any apprentice at DEAL or DRDO after completion of apprenticeship training.
Q10What documents are required at the time of joining DRDO DEAL Apprenticeship 2026-27?
A
At the time of joining, selected candidates must submit a valid Police Verification Certificate, Certificate of Physical Fitness from a Government Hospital Medical Officer, undertakings for the Official Secrets Act 1923 and IT Rules (Ministry of Defence), Aadhaar-seeded bank account details for DBT stipend, and three recent passport-size colour photographs, along with all original qualification certificates and a self-attested photocopy set.
