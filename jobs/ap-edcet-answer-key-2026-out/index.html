How to Download AP EDCET Answer Key 2026

Candidates can download the AP EDCET 2026 answer key in PDF format from the official website by following these steps:

  • Visit the official AP EDCET website at cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EDCET
  • Click on the "AP EDCET Answer Key 2026" link or "Response Sheet" link on the homepage
  • Enter your registration number or hall ticket number and other required details
  • Submit the details to view the answer key
  • The answer key PDF will appear on the screen showing all sets (Set A, Set B, Set C, Set D)
  • Download the answer key PDF corresponding to your question paper set
  • Save and take a printout for future reference

Direct Answer Key Link: https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EDCET/Edcet/EDCET_ResponseSheet.aspx

How to Raise Objections Against AP EDCET Answer Key 2026

If candidates find any discrepancies or errors in the provisional answer key, they can raise objections till 14 May 2026 through the following process:

  • Visit the official website at cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EDCET
  • Click on the "Key Objections" option on the homepage
  • Enter your registration number, hall ticket number, and mobile number
  • Select the questions you want to challenge along with supporting proof
  • Submit your objections in the prescribed format within the stipulated time
  • Keep a printed copy of the submitted objection for future use

APSCHE will review all objections and release the final answer key based on expert analysis.

How to Calculate Marks Using AP EDCET Answer Key 2026

Candidates can estimate their tentative scores before the official result declaration using the answer key:

  • Download the AP EDCET Answer Key 2026 from cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EDCET
  • Retrieve your question paper or download it from the official website if lost
  • Match your answers with those given in the official answer key PDF
  • Add 1 mark for each correct response
  • Total all correct answers to arrive at your approximate score

There is no negative marking in AP EDCET 2026, so only count the correct answers.

AP EDCET 2026 Exam Details

The AP EDCET 2026 examination was conducted on 8 May 2026 in two sessions. Session 1 ran from 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM covering Mathematics, Biological Science, and Physical Science, while Session 2 ran from 12:30 PM to 02:30 PM covering Social Studies and English. The exam was held in online computer-based mode across various test centers in Andhra Pradesh. AP EDCET is conducted for admission to 2-year Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) and Special Education programmes in university, government, aided, and private colleges across Andhra Pradesh.
