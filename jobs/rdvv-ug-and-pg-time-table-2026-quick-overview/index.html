RDVV UG and PG Time Table 2026 - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 18/07/2026

Rani Durgavati Vishwavidyalaya (RDVV), Jabalpur has released four separate examination time tables on 18 July 2026 on its official website. These cover BBA, BBA-HM and BCA Part 1; NEP Old scheme BA, BSc and BCom Part 1; BA, BSc and BCom Part 1; and MA, MSc and MCom One Year programmes. Students enrolled in these courses can download their respective time table PDFs from the official RDVV portal.

Who Should Check This Time Table (Eligibility)

  • Regular, private, and ATKT/supplementary students enrolled in BBA, BBA-HM, or BCA Part 1 at RDVV or its affiliated colleges
  • Students enrolled under the NEP Old scheme for BA, BSc, or BCom Part 1
  • Students enrolled in BA, BSc, or BCom Part 1 under the current scheme
  • Postgraduate students enrolled in the one-year MA, MSc, or MCom programmes

Students should confirm with their college examination department which category (NEP Old vs. regular) applies to them, since RDVV has issued these as two separate time tables for Part 1.

How to Download the RDVV Time Table 2026

  • Visit the official RDVV website: rdunijbpin.org.
  • Go to the Examination menu and click on Time Table, or open the direct link: rdunijbpin.org/1366/Time-Table.
  • On the Time Table page, use the "Time Table Category" filter (UG / PG / UTD / Other) if needed to narrow down the list.
  • Find the entry matching your course for example, "Time Table BBA BBA-HM BCA Part 1" or "Time Table MA MSc MCom One Year" dated 18-07-2026.
  • Click the entry to open or download the PDF.
  • Save the PDF and check the exam dates for your specific subjects and semester/year.

Important Instructions for Students

  • Always download the time table from the official website (rdunijbpin.org) to avoid outdated or incorrect information circulated elsewhere.
  • Cross-check your course, year (Part 1), and scheme (NEP Old vs. regular) carefully before noting down exam dates, since RDVV has released separate PDFs for similar-sounding categories.
  • Keep a printed or saved copy of the time table for reference while filling exam forms or during the exam period.
  • For any discrepancy or doubt regarding dates, contact your college examination cell or the RDVV examination department directly rather than relying on unofficial sources.
  • Watch the same Time Table page for revised or updated schedules, as universities occasionally issue corrections after the initial release.
