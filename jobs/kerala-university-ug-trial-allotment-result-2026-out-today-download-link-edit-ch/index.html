Kerala University UG Trial Allotment Result 2026 (Out Today) - Download Link, Edit Choices & Complete Schedule

The University of Kerala will be released the Kerala University UG Trial Allotment Result 2026 today, June 15, 2026, on its admission portal at admissions.keralauniversity.ac.in. This tentative allotment is part of the Four-Year Undergraduate Programme (FYUGP) Centralised Allotment Process (CAP) and gives registered candidates a preview of their likely seat allocation before the final rounds. Candidates have a limited window until June 16, 2026 to review their tentative status and edit or rearrange their college and course preferences. Acting quickly during this short editing window can significantly improve your chances of securing a seat in your most preferred institution.

What Is the Kerala University FYUGP Trial Allotment 2026?

The Kerala University FYUGP Trial Allotment is a preliminary, non-binding seat distribution will be released before the official first allotment. It is generated based on each candidate's registered preferences, their academic merit, reservation category, and seat availability across affiliated colleges. The trial allotment does not guarantee admission it is purely a tool for candidates to gauge their probability of getting into a specific college or programme. Based on what the trial result shows, students are encouraged to strategically reorder their choices to maximize their chances before the binding allotment rounds begin.

How to Check Kerala University UG Trial Allotment Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to access your tentative allotment status from the official admission portal.

To view your Kerala University FYUGP trial allotment result online, follow these steps:

  • Go to the official admission portal: admissions.keralauniversity.ac.in
  • Click on the "Student Login" link on the homepage.
  • Enter your Application Number and Password to log in.
  • On your dashboard, navigate to the "Trial Allotment Status" tab.
  • Your tentative seat allotment details will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download or save the allotment information for reference.

Alternatively, you can also visit keralauniversity.ac.in, click on the "Notification" tab under the Examination section, select the Admission tab, choose your programme, and click on the "Kerala University 2026 Rank List" section to view the allotment list.

How to Edit and Rearrange College Choices After Kerala University Trial Allotment 2026

The editing window is a critical opportunity this section walks you through exactly how to revise your preferences before the deadline.

After reviewing your trial allotment result, you can modify your college and course preferences until June 16, 2026. This is your last chance before the first binding allotment is published on June 17. Follow these steps to edit your options:

  • Step 1 - Log In: Access your candidate profile using your registered application number and password on the admission portal.
  • Step 2 - Navigate to Option Registration: Click on the "Modify Options" link available on your dashboard.
  • Step 3 - Rearrange Priority: Use the navigation arrows to move your most preferred college-programme combinations to the top of the list.
  • Step 4 - Add or Delete Choices: You may add new college-course combinations or remove existing ones. If the trial result shows a low probability for certain options, consider deleting them and replacing them with more realistic ones.
  • Step 5 - Save Your Changes: Click "Save & Lock" or "Refresh Options" to confirm and submit your revised preferences.
