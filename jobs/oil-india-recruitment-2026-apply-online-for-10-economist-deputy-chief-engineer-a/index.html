Important Instructions

Only Indian nationals are eligible to apply. The Cut-off date for all matters - age, experience, qualification - is 28-06-2026. Mere possession of minimum qualifications does not entitle candidates to be called for PI; shortlisting criteria may be raised if the number of applications is large. Candidates in permanent Government/PSU employment must produce a valid NOC from their employer at the time of PI - without this they will not be permitted to appear and travel fares will not be reimbursed. Appointment of provisionally selected candidates is subject to medical fitness as per OIL's Physical Fitness criteria; candidature is cancelled if declared unfit by OIL's Medical Board. Provisionally selected candidates will serve a probationary period of 1 year and will be confirmed in writing after successful completion. Any false information or document at any stage - even after joining - will result in cancellation/termination and blacklisting from all future OIL recruitments. All future announcements for this vacancy will be published only on www.oil-india.com - not on any other website or medium. OIL has warned all candidates about fraudulent fake appointment letters being circulated by unscrupulous elements - OIL does not authorize any person/organization outside OIL to offer jobs on its behalf. Any dispute regarding recruitment under this advertisement will be under the jurisdiction of Dibrugarh District Court only. Canvassing in any form will result in cancellation of candidature. OIL strives to have a gender-balanced workforce and women candidates are encouraged to apply.
