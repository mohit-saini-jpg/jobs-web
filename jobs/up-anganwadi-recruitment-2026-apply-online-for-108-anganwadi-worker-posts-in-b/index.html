Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must satisfy the gender, residence, and qualification conditions notified for the Anganwadi Karyakatri post in Bhadohi district. Only female candidates are eligible for the advertised posts. The applicant must be a resident of the same Gram Sabha/Ward for which she is applying. Income and caste certificates must be issued by a competent authority and be digitally signed, verifiable online through the eDistrict UP portal. Income certificates must be issued as per UP Government Revenue Section-9 Order No. 3959/1-9-11-Sa0-9 dated 30-09-2021 (as amended), by a competent authority, and must be verifiable online. As per government order, a family in which one member is already selected as Anganwadi Karyakatri cannot have another member selected as Anganwadi Sahayika at the same Anganwadi Centre, and vice versa.

Educational Qualification

The minimum qualification for the Anganwadi Karyakatri post is Intermediate (12th) or equivalent, with a maximum qualification of Postgraduate. Minimum qualification: Intermediate (10+2) or equivalent. Maximum qualification: Postgraduate. Merit will be prepared by adding marks obtained from Intermediate up to the highest qualification (up to Postgraduate) held by the candidate.

Selection Priority

Selection for Anganwadi Karyakatri posts under this recruitment follows a defined order of priority based on economic and social status. First priority is given to widowed or legally divorced women from families living below the poverty line (BPL). Next priority is given to other women from families living below the poverty line. If no candidate from the below-poverty-line category is available, candidates from above-poverty-line families are selected in the same order of priority described above.

UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection to the Anganwadi Karyakatri post in Bhadohi is made purely on a merit-and-priority basis as per the government order. Merit is prepared by adding marks scored from Intermediate up to the candidate's highest educational qualification (maximum Postgraduate). Priority category candidates (widowed/divorced BPL women, then other BPL women) are considered first, followed by above-poverty-line candidates in the same order. Only candidates residing in the same Gram Sabha/Ward as the vacant post are considered. Errors or incomplete applications are summarily rejected; no offline forms are accepted at any stage.
