How to Download the HPU Date Sheet 2025?

Candidates follow the steps to download the Himachal Pradesh University Date Sheet 2025, candidates check the details and download it easily.

  • Visit the official website
  • The University home page will open
  • Click over the examination section
  • Click over the examination schedules
  • The examination Schedules page will pop up
  • Choose the course
  • Date Sheet for different courses will show up for Regular and Regular / External.
  • Click on the course
  • Date Sheet will show up.
  • Check and download.
