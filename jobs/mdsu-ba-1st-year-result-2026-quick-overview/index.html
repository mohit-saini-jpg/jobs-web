Who Can Check This Result

  • Regular students of BA 1st Year enrolled at MDSU-affiliated colleges
  • Private/Non-College (NC) candidates registered for BA 1st Year
  • Ex-students appearing in back-paper or improvement exams

How to Check MDSU BA 1st Year Result 2026 Online

  • Go to the official MDSU result portal: erp.mdsuexam.org (or mdsuexam.org).
  • Click on the "Exam and Result" or "Result Panel" section on the homepage.
  • If your college follows the NEP pattern, select "Panel for NEP Classes" and log in with your student credentials.
  • If your college follows the Regular/Traditional pattern, select the UG result option directly.
  • Choose "BA" as the course and select "1st Year" or "Semester I" as applicable.
  • Enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth (name-wise search is not available on the official MDSU portal).
  • Submit the form to view your result on screen.
  • Download and print the result for future reference.

What to Do If There Is an Error in Your Result

  • Contact your college examination cell or the MDSU Controller of Examinations office immediately.
  • Apply for revaluation or rechecking of the affected subject if permitted by the university.
  • Once your original marksheet is issued by the college, cross-check every detail against your provisional online result.
  • Note: MDSU does not refund exam fees for candidates who could not appear in the exam; a medical certificate may allow you to appear in supplementary exams instead.
