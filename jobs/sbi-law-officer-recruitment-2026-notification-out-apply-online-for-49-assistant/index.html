SBI Law Officer Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in SBI SCO Law Officer Recruitment 2026-27?
A
A total of 49 vacancies have been announced – 20 for Assistant Manager (Law) on JMGS-I grade and 29 for Deputy Manager (Law) on MMGS-II grade (including 2 ST backlog vacancies). The number of vacancies is provisional and may vary as per actual requirements of the Bank.
Q2What is the last date to apply online for SBI SCO Law Officer Recruitment 2026-27?
A
The last date to submit the online application and pay the fee for SBI SCO Law Officer Recruitment 2026-27 is 14 July 2026. The application window opened on 24 June 2026. Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date to avoid last-minute technical issues.
Q3What is the eligibility/experience required for SBI Assistant Manager (Law) post?
A
Candidates must hold a Law degree (LLB) or be a Law Graduate from a recognized university and must have 02 years of mandatory experience – either as a practicing advocate (enrolled with Bar Council), or as a Law Officer in the Legal Department of a Scheduled Commercial Bank, or a combination of both. Internship and teaching experience will NOT be counted. Age: 25–32 years as on 31.05.2026.
Q4What is the eligibility/experience required for SBI Deputy Manager (Law) post?
A
Candidates must hold a Law degree (LLB) from a recognized university and must have 04 years of mandatory experience – either as a practicing advocate (enrolled with Bar Council), or as a Law Officer in the Legal Department of a Scheduled Commercial Bank, or a combination of both. Internship and teaching experience will NOT be counted. Age: 25–35 years as on 31.05.2026.
Q5What is the application fee for SBI SCO Law Officer Recruitment 2026-27?
A
General, EWS, and OBC candidates must pay Rs. 750/- (non-refundable). SC, ST, and PwBD candidates are exempted from paying any application fee or intimation charges. Payment must be made online through Debit Card/Credit Card/Internet Banking.
Q6What is the selection process for SBI SCO Law Officer Recruitment 2026-27?
A
The selection consists of an Online Written Test (Objective Test of 50 marks + Descriptive Test of 50 marks) followed by an Interview of 100 marks. The final merit list is prepared with 70% weightage to Online Written Test and 30% weightage to Interview scores. General Aptitude (Reasoning + English) in the Objective Test is qualifying in nature only.
Q7Is there negative marking in the SBI SCO Law Officer Written Exam 2026?
A
No. There will be NO negative marking for wrong answers in the Online Written Examination for SBI SCO Law Officer Recruitment 2026-27.
Q8What is the syllabus for the SBI SCO Law Officer Professional Knowledge paper?
A
The indicative syllabus for Professional Knowledge covers fundamental concepts, issues, and current developments in Constitutional Law, Administrative Law, Financial Sector Laws, Procedural Laws, New Labour Codes, and Principles of Statutory Interpretation and Legal Drafting. The syllabus is indicative only and the exam may contain questions beyond the listed topics.
Q9What is the pay scale offered for SBI SCO Law Officer 2026-27?
A
Assistant Manager (Law) – JMGS-I: Basic Pay 48480-2000/7-62480-2340/2-67160-2680/7-85920. Deputy Manager (Law) – MMGS-II: Basic Pay 64820-2340/1-67160-2680/10-93960. Officers are also eligible for DA, HRA, CCA, Provident Fund, NPS, Leave Fare Concession, Medical Facility, and other perquisites as per rules.
Q10Can a candidate apply for both Assistant Manager (Law) and Deputy Manager (Law) posts in SBI SCO 2026-27?
A
No. Candidates are NOT allowed to apply for more than one post. If multiple applications are submitted, only the last valid (completed) application will be retained and the fee paid for other registrations will stand forfeited. Multiple attendance at the interview will result in rejection of candidature.
