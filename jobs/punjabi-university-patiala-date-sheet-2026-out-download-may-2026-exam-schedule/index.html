How to Download Punjabi University Patiala Date Sheet 2026

Students can download the Punjabi University Patiala Date Sheet 2026 easily by following these steps.

  • Visit the official examination portal of Punjabi University at ds19.pupexamination.ac.in.
  • Go to the Date Sheet or View Date Sheet section.
  • Select your course and semester from the available notifications.
  • Click on the relevant date sheet link to open the PDF.
  • Download and save the file on your device.
  • Take a printout and mark important exam dates.

Always check the official date sheet carefully according to your programme and semester before preparing for the examinations.
