Chikkamagaluru District Court Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility differs by post, and candidates should check both the educational qualification and age limit applicable to the specific position they are applying for. Meeting both criteria is mandatory to be considered for the selection process. Candidates wondering what qualification is needed can note that requirements vary by post. For Peon, candidates must have passed SSLC or its equivalent examination and should be able to read and write Kannada. For Process Server, SSLC or equivalent is required, and possession of a valid driving licence will be given preference. For Typist-Copyist, candidates need a second PUC or equivalent qualification along with a Junior Grade certificate in Kannada and English typewriting from the Department of Public Instruction. Chikkamagaluru District Court Recruitment 2026 - Application Fee

Candidates from the General category must pay ₹300, while candidates belonging to Category 2A, 2B, 3A, and 3B need to pay ₹150. Candidates from SC, ST, Category-1, and Persons with Disabilities are exempted from the application fee. The fee must be paid online through State Bank Collect using Net Banking, Credit Card, Debit Card, or UPI; demand drafts, cheques, or postal orders will not be accepted, and the fee once paid is non-refundable.

Chikkamagaluru District Court Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

Selection happens through a multi-stage process that combines written tests, skill or typing tests, and interviews depending on the post applied for. Candidates should understand that the qualifying criteria and shortlisting ratios differ slightly by post. Written/Qualifying Test: Conducted for most posts based on the prescribed syllabus. Skill Test: For Typist, Typist-Copyist, and Stenographer posts, a typing/shorthand skill test on computer is conducted (e.g., 15-minute dictation for Typist-Copyist; shorthand dictation of 80 words per minute transcribed in 45 minutes for Stenographer Grade-III). Interview: Candidates shortlisted from the qualifying exam are called for interview in a ratio of 1:5 (one vacancy to five candidates) for most posts, 1:10 for Peon, and 1:25 for Process Server (final list based on 1:5 short-listing). Chikkamagaluru District Court Recruitment 2026 - Salary / Pay Scale

Pay scales follow the Karnataka government pay matrix and vary by post. The Peon post carries a pay scale of ₹27,000-46,675. The Process Server post carries a pay scale of ₹31,775-61,300. The Typist and Typist-Copyist posts carry a pay scale of ₹34,100-67,600. The Stenographer Grade-III post carries a higher pay scale of ₹44,425-83,700. All posts also include other allowances admissible as per government rules. Selected candidates will be on probation for two years as per the Karnataka Civil Services (Probation) Rules, 1977, extendable if required. During this period, candidates must pass departmental examinations in their subject as well as the Kannada language examination, and where applicable, the Compulsory Kannada Typewriting Test as per rules.
