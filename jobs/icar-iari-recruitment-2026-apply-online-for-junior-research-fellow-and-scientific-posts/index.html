Q7. What is the selection process for these posts?
The selection process for the ICAR-IARI Recruitment 2026 will involve an Application Screening, followed by an Interview, and finally Document Verification for the shortlisted candidates.
Q8. How should candidates apply for the ICAR-IARI Recruitment 2026?
