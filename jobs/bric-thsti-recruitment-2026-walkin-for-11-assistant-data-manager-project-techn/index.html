Overview

BRIC Translational Health Science and Technology Institute (THSTI) is inviting applications for 11 Sarkari Naukri vacancies through walk-in interview. Candidates with experience in research and data management can apply. Interviews will be held on 06 July 2026.

Expert Analysis

This recruitment is a great opportunity for candidates with a background in research and data management to join a prestigious institute like BRIC THSTI. The salary package is attractive, with a maximum salary of ₹80,000. Candidates should not miss this chance to boost their career.

Who Should Apply

Ideal candidates for these Sarkari Naukri vacancies are research professionals with experience in data management, medical sciences, or life sciences. Candidates with a postgraduate degree and relevant experience will be given preference.

Preparation Tips

To crack the walk-in interview, prepare thoroughly on the research methodology and data management skills. Practice your presentation skills and be ready to answer questions on your research experience. Also, make sure to bring all required documents and certificates.

Salary Insights

The salary range for these Sarkari Naukri jobs is ₹28,000 - ₹80,000. This is a competitive salary package, considering the level of expertise required for these roles. With experience, the salary can grow, and candidates can expect a significant increase in their remuneration.

Job Profile

As an Assistant Data Manager, you will be responsible for managing and analyzing data for research projects. You will work closely with researchers to design and implement data collection systems. As a Project Research Scientist, you will be involved in designing and conducting research studies, collecting and analyzing data, and publishing research findings.

Selection Strategy

To crack the selection process, focus on your research experience, data management skills, and presentation skills. Be prepared to answer questions on your research methodology, data analysis techniques, and project management skills. Also, make sure to highlight your achievements and contributions to your previous research projects.
