FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for GHSS Ambaguda Recruitment?
A
The walk-in interview for Guest Faculties at Government Higher Secondary School Ambaguda is scheduled for 12th June 2026. Candidates are advised to report at the venue before 8:45 AM on the specified date.
Q2What is the minimum qualification required for the Guest Faculties post?
A
Candidates must have a Post Graduate (PG) degree in the respective subject with a minimum of 50% marks. A B.Ed. degree and PGDCA will be given weightage, except for Logic/Philosophy and Sociology subjects.
Q3What is the age limit for applying to the Guest Faculties position?
A
The age limit for applying is below 65 years of age. This applies to both retired teachers and fresh candidates applying for the Guest Faculties position.
Q4How much salary will be paid to the selected Guest Faculties?
A
Selected candidates will be paid ₹400 per class. However, there is a maximum limit of 50 classes per month for the salary calculation.
Q5What documents are required for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates need to bring their complete Bio-data, two passport size photographs, and all original certificates and mark sheets from HSC to PG, along with one set of self-attested photocopies.
Q6Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
No application fee is mentioned in the official notification for this recruitment drive. Candidates can attend the walk-in interview without paying any application fee.
Q7What is the selection process for the Guest Faculties post?
A
The selection process for the Guest Faculties post will be based on a Walk-in Interview. Candidates will be assessed based on their qualifications and performance during the interview.
Q8Can retired teachers apply for this position?
A
Yes, retired Government, Non-Government, and Aided teachers below 65 years of age are eligible to apply for the Guest Faculties position.
Q9What is the job type for this recruitment?
A
The job type for the Guest Faculties position at GHSS Ambaguda is on a Contract basis.
Q10Where is the walk-in interview venue?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at Government Higher Secondary School Ambaguda, AMBAGUDA, Odisha, 764055.
