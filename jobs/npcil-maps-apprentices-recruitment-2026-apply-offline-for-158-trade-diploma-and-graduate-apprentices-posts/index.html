Selection Process

  • Detail Description
  • Selection Basis Marks obtained in ITI / Diploma / Graduation qualifying examination
  • Local Preference Preference given to candidates from neighbouring villages within 16 km radius of MAPS Plant Site. If all trades are not filled by local candidates, merit list is extended to other applicants.
  • Tie-Breaking Rule 1 In case of tie in percentage of marks, candidate born earlier (older candidate) will be ranked higher
  • Tie-Breaking Rule 2 In case of tie in Date of Birth also, candidate with higher percentage in Matriculation (10th) will be ranked higher
  • Selection for engagement of apprentices will be based on merit i.e. marks obtained in the qualifying examination (ITI/Diploma/Graduation). There is no written test. The final engagement is subject to document verification and medical fitness. NPCIL management reserves the right to cancel or alter the number of seats of any particular trade/discipline or cancel the entire process without assigning any reason.
  • Stage 1 – Merit-Based Shortlisting
  • Stage 2 – Document Verification
  • Shortlisted candidates will be called for document verification. All original certificates and self-attested copies must be produced. Candidature is provisional and subject to verification of originals at all stages. False information/documents at any stage will result in rejection and may lead to prosecution.
  • Stage 3 – Medical Examination
  • Engagement is subject to being declared medically fit as per NPCIL's requirements. Minimum physical standards: Height 137 cm
  • Weight 25.4 kg
  • Chest expansion 3.8 cm. Candidate must be free from Tuberculosis
  • contagious/infectious diseases
  • or any disability likely to interfere with apprenticeship.
  • Stage 4 – Police Verification
  • Since MAPS Kalpakkam is a highly prohibited area
  • all selected apprentices must submit a Police Verification Certificate issued by the concerned District Police Authority before commencement of training. A Character/Conduct Certificate from the concerned institution is also mandatory.

How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply offline only - no online application portal is available for submission. The process involves two steps: first, mandatory registration on NAPS 2.0 / NATS 2.0 portal; second, sending the filled application form by post to NPCIL MAPS Kalpakkam. Incomplete or erroneous applications or applications without proper enclosures will be summarily rejected without any communication.
  • Step-by-Step Application Process:
  • Step 1: Register on the mandatory apprenticeship portal - NAPS 2.0 (for Trade Apprentices) or NATS 2.0 (for Diploma & Graduate Apprentices). Ensure Aadhaar is linked with your bank account before registering.
  • Step 2: Download the Application Form (Annexure-1) from www.npcil.nic.in > HR Management > Opportunities. Verify your eligibility before filling the form.
  • Step 3: Fill in the application form completely in Block Letters. Affix a recent passport-size photograph in the space provided. Sign the declaration.
  • Step 4: Attach self-attested copies of all required documents (see list below).
  • Step 5: Send the complete application by post to reach on or before 30/06/2026 at: Deputy Manager (HRM), Nuclear Power Corporation of India Limited (A Government of India Enterprise), Madras Atomic Power Station, Kalpakkam – 603 102, Chengalpattu, Tamil Nadu.
  • Documents to be Enclosed (Self-Attested Copies) with Application:
  • Birth Certificate issued by Competent Authority / SSC Mark Sheet as proof of Date of Birth
  • Educational Qualification mark sheets (10th / 12th; and 8th mark sheet additionally for Welder trade)
  • ITI / Diploma / Graduate Degree certificate and mark sheets of all semesters/years
  • Other qualification/experience certificates, if any
  • Caste Certificate issued by Competent Authority in prescribed Central Government format (for SC/ST/OBC candidates - valid as on 30/06/2026)
  • Valid Income and Assets Certificate as on 30/06/2026 in prescribed format issued by Competent Authority (for EWS category)
  • Disability Certificate from Competent Authority in prescribed format (for PwBD candidates; disability must be 40% or more)
  • Police Verification Certificate issued by concerned District Police Authority
  • Character/Conduct Certificate from the concerned institution
  • Aadhaar Card (Compulsory)
  • 4 recent passport-size photographs
  • Residential proof of permanent address (if living within 16 km radius of MAPS, Kalpakkam)
  • Copy of Apprentice Registration on NAPS 2.0 (https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in) or NATS 2.0 (https://www.nats.education.gov.in)
  • Proof from University/Institute converting CGPA/GPA/SGPA into percentage (for candidates awarded grades)
  • Send Application By Post To: Deputy Manager (HRM) Nuclear Power Corporation of India Limited (A Government of India Enterprise) Madras Atomic Power Station Kalpakkam – 603 102, Chengalpattu, Tamil Nadu

Important Instructions

  • THIS IS NOT AN EMPLOYMENT IN NPCIL. Engagement as an apprentice shall not create any obligation on NPCIL to offer regular employment, nor any obligation on the apprentice to accept employment under NPCIL after completion of training. The period of apprenticeship training will be for a maximum of One Year. The contract of apprenticeship terminates on expiry of the training period. Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing apprenticeship under the Apprentices Act-1961 in any organization are strictly NOT eligible to apply. Preference in selection will be given to candidates from neighbouring villages within 16 km radius of the MAPS plant site in Kalpakkam. Merit-based selection from other candidates will follow only if trades/disciplines are not filled by local candidates. All selected apprentices must sign a contract of apprenticeship with NPCIL as per the Apprentices Act-1961 and Apprenticeship Rules-1992. Madras Atomic Power Station is a highly prohibited area. Engagement is subject to submission of Police Verification Certificate from the concerned District Police Authority. Candidature is provisional at all stages. If false information or suppressed facts are detected at any stage of the process or during training, the application/training shall be rejected forthwith and NPCIL will not entertain any correspondence. If it is detected that the candidate has provided false documents, impersonated, or affixed a non-human/irrelevant photograph, the applicant will be liable to be disqualified, prosecuted and permanently debarred from apprenticeship in NPCIL. Only Full-Time/Regular mode qualifications (ITI/Diploma/Graduation) are accepted. Distance/Part-Time/Correspondence qualifications are not eligible. Diploma and Graduate Apprentice applicants must have passed their qualifying examination within 5 years of the last date of application i.e. 30/06/2026 (applicable for NATS 2.0 portal eligibility). All SC/ST/OBC (NCL) candidates must produce a self-attested copy of valid caste certificate in the prescribed Central Government format from a Competent Authority along with originals at the time of document verification. NPCIL strives to have a workforce that reflects gender balance. Women candidates are encouraged to apply. In case of any discrepancy between Hindi and English versions of the notification, the English version shall prevail for all purposes. Management reserves the right to cancel or alter the number of seats of any particular trade/discipline or cancel the entire engagement process without assigning any reason.
