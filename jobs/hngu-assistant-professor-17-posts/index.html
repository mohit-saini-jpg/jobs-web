FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in NGU Recruitment 2026 Advertisement No.05/2026?
A
Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University, Patan has announced a total of 17 Assistant Professor posts across 8 departments/institutes under Advertisement No. 05/2026 dated 22-05-2026.
Q2What is the last date to apply online for NGU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for online application submission is 15-06-2026 up to 05:00 PM. The last date for physical hardcopy submission is 20-06-2026 up to 06:00 PM.
Q3What is the salary / pay scale for NGU Assistant Professor 2026? A. Selected
A
candidates will be placed at Level-10 with a basic pay of Rs. 57,700/- per month, as per State Government/UGC norms.
Q4Is NET/SLET/SET mandatory for NGU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
NET/SLET/SET is the minimum eligibility for Assistant Professor posts (Arts, Science, Commerce & Management). However, candidates who hold a Ph.D. degree as per UGC Regulations 2009 or 2016 are exempted from the NET/SLET/SET requirement. For Architecture posts, NET/SLET/SET is not required; professional experience and B.Arch. marks are the criteria.
Q5What is the application fee for NGU Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is Rs. 500/- for General/Unreserved candidates and Rs. 250/- for ST/SC/OBC/EWS/PwD candidates. Fee is payable through online mode only and is non-refundable.
Q6Is knowledge of Gujarati mandatory for NGU Assistant Professor 2026?
A
Yes, knowledge of Gujarati is mandatory for all applicants regardless of department, as explicitly stated in the notification.
Q7What is the selection process for NGU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection involves three stages: (1) Academic Score-based shortlisting (as per UGC Regulation 2018, Appendix II Table 3A) in a 1:15 ratio, (2) Screening Test wherever necessary (syllabus published on the University website), and (3) Personal Interview for shortlisted candidates. Final selection is based solely on interview performance.
Q8How to apply for NGU Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must: (a) Apply online at https://recruitment.ngu.ac.in by 15-06-2026; (b) Pay the application fee online; and (c) Submit 3 sets of printed hardcopy application along with all self-attested documents and Academic Score Appendix II Table 3A to the Registrar, NGU, Patan – 384265 by 20-06-2026, 06:00 PM via Speed Post or in person.
Q9Which departments have vacancies in NGU Advertisement 05/2026?
A
The 17 posts are distributed across: Institute of Architecture (1), Department of Chemistry (2), Department of Mathematics (1), S.K. School of Business Management/MBA (3), Department of English (1), Department of Life Science — Microbiology & Zoology (2), Department of Hospital Management (3), and Department of Gujarati (4).
Q10Are there reservations for PwD candidates in NGU Recruitment 2026?
A
2 posts are reserved for PwD candidates — 1 in Department of Life Science (Zoology) for Blind/Low Vision candidates (Second Attempt) and 1 in Department of Gujarati for Deaf/Hard of Hearing candidates. Only candidates with a benchmark disability of 40% or above, certified by a competent authority (Govt. Hospital Superintendent/Civil Surgeon/Medical Board), are eligible for PwD reservation benefits.
