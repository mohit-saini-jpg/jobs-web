Selection Process

  • Paper Subject Type Marks Duration

  • Paper - I General Studies (History of Rajasthan and Indian History; Mental Ability, Statistics, Mathematics; Current Affairs; General Science, Indian Polity, Geography of Rajasthan; Educational Psychology, Educational Management, Educational Scenario in Rajasthan, Right to Free and Compulsory Education Act) Multiple Choice (Objective) 150 marks 1 Hour 30 Minutes

  • Paper - II Subject Knowledge: Senior Secondary level knowledge in English, Hindi, History, Political Science, Sociology, Music Multiple Choice (Objective) 150 marks 3 Hours

  • Special Education: Agencies, Policies, Acts and Rules regarding specially abled persons and special education; Pedagogy, Teaching Learning Material, Use of Computers and IT in Teaching Learning; Education for specially abled students with various disabilities Multiple Choice (Objective) 150 marks

  • Total 450 marks

  • Selection for Lecturer (Special Education) posts will be made through a Competitive Written Examination of 450 marks. If required

  • the Commission may adopt re-scaling/moderation/normalization procedures. Candidates scoring the required qualifying marks will be recommended in merit order as per Madhyamik Shiksha Seva Niyam Rule 28. Negative marking applies — 1/3rd marks deducted for every wrong answer. SC/ST candidates get 5% relaxation in minimum qualifying marks (40% threshold relaxed to 35%).

  • Stage 1 - Written Competitive Examination (Computer Based / OMR)

  • Minimum qualifying marks: 40% in each paper (relaxed to 35% for SC/ST of Rajasthan). Negative marking: 1/3rd marks deducted per wrong answer. OMR Answer Sheet instructions: darken only one circle per question using Blue Ball Point Pen; if no option is attempted

  • darken circle '5'; a candidate who has not darkened any circle in more than 10% of questions shall be disqualified. Extra time of 10 minutes beyond scheduled time is provided. All questions in both papers are Multiple Choice Type. Detailed syllabus available on RPSC website https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in.

  • Stage 2 - Document Verification & Police/Character Verification

  • Candidates recommended on the basis of written examination merit will be required to undergo police verification

  • character verification

  • and medical fitness examination before appointment. Final appointment is subject to all verifications being satisfactory.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only at https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in. Before applying, candidates must first complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the SSO Portal at https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in using a Citizen Apps (G2C) login and access the Recruitment Portal. During OTR, candidates must upload a live/recent photo, provide Aadhaar/Janaadhar details, and fill all personal details (Name, Father's Name, DOB, Gender) accurately as per Aadhaar — these cannot be changed post-OTR without Aadhaar correction. Candidates who have already completed OTR can directly log in with their SSO ID and apply using their OTR Number/Registration Number. Candidates are strongly advised to read the detailed advertisement and Instructions for Applicants available on the RPSC website before filling the online application form.

  • Documents/Information to be ready before applying online:

  • Valid SSO ID and password (or credentials for new OTR registration)

  • Valid Aadhaar Card / Janaadhar Card details

  • Live photograph (to be captured via webcam/camera at the time of application)

  • Recent scanned passport-size photograph

  • Scanned signature

  • Both thumb impressions (right and left hand) — for OTR KYC

  • Valid and active mobile number and email ID

  • OTR profile details (Name, Father's Name, DOB, Gender exactly as per Aadhaar)

  • RCI Registration Number (permanent or provisional)

  • Online payment facility — Internet Banking / Debit Card / Credit Card / UPI (for Ekabari Panjeeyan Shulk)

  • All educational qualification details (PG degree subject, B.Ed. Special Education / Diploma details)

  • Category / reservation certificate details (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD/Ex-Servicemen as applicable)

  • Documents to be produced at the time of Examination / Document Verification:

  • E-Admit Card (downloaded from RPSC website — NOT sent by post)

  • Photo identity proof (Aadhaar Card / Voter ID / Passport / Driving License etc.)

  • All original educational certificates (PG marksheets, B.Ed. Special Education / Diploma certificate)

  • RCI Registration Certificate (permanent/provisional)

  • Date of Birth proof (10th/12th certificate)

  • Valid Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/MBC/PwBD/ESM as applicable — Rajasthan State issued)

  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) if currently employed in Government service

  • Disability certificate (for PwBD candidates — from designated medical authority)

  • Scribe undertaking/medical certificate (for eligible PwBD candidates requesting scribe — must be submitted at least 2 days before exam date)

  • Application once submitted is final. Correction can be made online within 10 days of the closing date by paying Rs. 500/-. After the Commission's first examination is held, correction can be sought up to 07 days before the next examination (online edit link will be opened). No offline corrections or changes will be accepted after any stage of examination.

  • Apply Online at: https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in (via SSO Portal: https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in)

  • For queries: Helpdesk number on Recruitment Portal; RPSC office Swagat Kaksha (in person): 0145-2635212 and 2635200 (working days, office hours).

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for RPSC Lecturer (Special Education) Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must have a Post Graduate degree in the relevant subject recognized by UGC along with B.Ed. (Special Education) or B.Ed. plus a two-year Diploma in Special Education recognized by the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI). Additionally, candidates must have valid RCI registration before the recruitment examination date. Without the required qualification and RCI registration, candidates will not be eligible.

Q4. What is the age limit for RPSC 1st Grade Special Teacher Recruitment 2026?

The minimum age required is 21 years, while the maximum age limit is 40 years as on 01 January 2027. Age relaxation benefits are available for SC, ST, OBC, EWS, women candidates, ex-servicemen, and other reserved categories according to Rajasthan Government norms. However, the total age after relaxation cannot exceed the prescribed maximum limit.

Q5. What is the application fee for RPSC Lecturer (Special Education) Recruitment 2026?

The application fee for General and OBC Creamy Layer candidates is Rs. 600, while candidates from SC, ST, OBC Non-Creamy Layer, MBC, EWS, and PwBD categories of Rajasthan need to pay Rs. 400. The fee must be paid online through the OTR (One Time Registration) system and is non-refundable after payment.

Q6. What is the selection process for RPSC 1st Grade Special Teacher Recruitment 2026?

The selection process consists mainly of a competitive written examination of 450 marks, divided into Paper I and Paper II. The exam includes General Studies, subject knowledge, and Special Education topics. Candidates who qualify in the written exam will be called for document verification, police verification, and medical fitness verification before final appointment.

Q7. Is RCI registration mandatory for RPSC Lecturer (Special Education) Recruitment 2026?

Yes, RCI registration is compulsory for candidates applying for RPSC Lecturer (Special Education) Recruitment 2026. Applicants must have permanent or provisional registration with the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI) before the recruitment examination date. Candidates are also required to provide their valid RCI registration number in the online application form.

Q8. How can I apply online for RPSC 1st Grade Special Teacher Recruitment 2026?

Candidates can apply online through the official RPSC portal by logging in via the SSO Rajasthan Portal. After registration, applicants can fill out the recruitment form, pay the fee, and submit the application online.
