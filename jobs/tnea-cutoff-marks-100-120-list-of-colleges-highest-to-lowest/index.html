Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) is the single-window counselling process for B.E/B.Tech admissions in Tamil Nadu. Candidates scoring between 100 to 120 marks (out of 200 scaled score) in TNEA have realistic chances in many private self-financing engineering colleges. Around 100+ colleges accept students in this range. This article provides a complete list of colleges, average cutoffs, counselling process, branch-wise options, and expert tips to help you make the best choice.

TNEA Counselling Process 2025-2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Online Registration — Fill application on tneaonline.org
  • Upload Documents & Certificate Verification (Online)
  • Random Number Generation
  • Rank List Publication
  • Choice Filling (College + Branch preference)
  • Seat Allotment (Multiple Rounds)
  • Confirmation & Reporting to allotted college

Branch-wise Options for 100-120 Marks

  • Good Chances: Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, EEE, Automobile, Biotechnology
  • Moderate Chances: ECE, IT (in lower-tier colleges)
  • Limited Chances: CSE (only in few colleges)

Tips for Students with 100-120 Marks

  • Prioritize college reputation and placement record over branch
  • Fill maximum choices during counselling
  • Consider location and fees
  • Keep management quota as backup option
