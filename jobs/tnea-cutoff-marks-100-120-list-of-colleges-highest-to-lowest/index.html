1paragraphTamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) is the single-window counselling process for B.E/B.Tech admissions in Tamil Nadu. Candidates scoring between 100 to 120 marks (out of 200 scaled score) in TNEA have realistic chances in many private self-financing engineering colleges. Around 100+ colleges accept students in this range. This article provides a complete list of colleges, average cutoffs, counselling process, branch-wise options, and expert tips to help you make the best choice.
