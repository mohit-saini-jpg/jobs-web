HKRN MIG Welder Recruitment 2026 Eligibility Criteria
  1. Experience: Minimum 1–3 years as a MIG welder or in a similar welding role.
  2. Certification: Welding certification preferred (ITI, vocational training, or equivalent).
  3. Strong understanding of MIG welding techniques, equipment, and safety standards.
  4. Ability to read and interpret drawings & specifications.
  5. Good eye for detail and quality-focused welding execution.
  6. Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
