Assistant Professor - English: Master Degree in English from an Indian University, or an equivalent degree from an accredited foreign University. Candidates who have been awarded a Ph.D. Degree in accordance with the provisions contained in the Para 4.1(I)(A)(ii), UGC Regulations, 2018 are exempted from the requirement of the minimum eligibility condition of NET. Assistant Professor - Commerce: Master Degree in Commerce from an Indian University, or an equivalent degree from an accredited foreign University. Candidates who have been awarded a Ph.D. Degree in accordance with the provisions contained in the Para 4.1(I)(A)(ii), UGC Regulations, 2018 are exempted from the requirement of the minimum eligibility condition of NET.
