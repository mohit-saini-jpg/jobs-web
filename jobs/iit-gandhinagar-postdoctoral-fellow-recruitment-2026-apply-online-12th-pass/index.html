Qualification / Eligibility

Postdoctoral Fellow: BTech/BE/ PhD degree in biotechnology, biosensing technology, nanotechnology, chemical engineering, electrochemical analysis and allied topics from a reputed institute with an excellent scientific publication record in the area of biosensing. The candidates who have submitted their thesis and are available to join immediately are also eligible to apply. The percentage/grade points with respect to the academic qualifications should be a minimum of 60% or equivalent grade from Graduation onward and 55% or equivalent grade in class 10th and 12th.
