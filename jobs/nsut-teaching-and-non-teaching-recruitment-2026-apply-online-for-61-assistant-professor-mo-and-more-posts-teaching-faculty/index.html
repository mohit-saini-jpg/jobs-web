How To Apply

  • Eligible candidates must complete both online and offline (hard copy) registration as follows:
  • Visit the official NSUT website: http://www.nsut.ac.in and fill the online application form in CAPITAL LETTERS.
  • Submit separate online applications and fees for each post applied for.
  • Ensure a valid email ID is provided that will remain active throughout the recruitment process.
  • After online submission, take a printout of the filled application form and attach self-attested copies of all relevant documents including educational certificates, marksheets, experience letters, research publications, category certificates, NOC from employer (if applicable), and any other supporting documents as applicable to the post.
  • Send the hard copy in an envelope superscribed with Post Applied For, Name, Address, Category, Applicant ID, and Department (for teaching posts) to: The Registrar, Netaji Subhas University of Technology, Azad Hind Fauj Marg, Sector-3, Dwarka, New Delhi – 110078.
  • Online application deadline: June 30, 2026. Hard copy deadline: July 15, 2026 by 5:00 PM.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is NSUT Recruitment 2026 all about?

NSUT Recruitment 2026 is a direct recruitment drive for Assistant Professor, Associate Professor and Group ‘A’ Officer posts at Netaji Subhas University of Technology, Dwarka, New Delhi. The posts are being filled under Recruitment Notices NSUT/FACULTY/2026/02 and NSUT/GROUP ‘A’/2026/01 on regular or tenure basis, as per the advertised terms.

Q2. What are the important dates for NSUT Recruitment 2026?

The online application process opens on 26 May 2026 (10:00 AM) and closes on 30 June 2026 (midnight). The hard copy of the application with documents must reach NSUT by 15 July 2026 up to 5:00 PM, and written test/interview schedules will be notified later on the official website.

Q3. How many vacancies are released in NSUT Recruitment 2026?

NSUT has notified 39 Assistant Professor and 19 Associate Professor vacancies across Biological Sciences & Engineering / Bio-Technology, Chemistry, Design, Geo-Informatics, Mathematics and Physics. In addition, there are 3 Group ‘A’ officer posts – Campus Director (1), Training & Placement Officer (1) and Medical Officer (1) – making a total of 61 posts.

Q4. What are the pay scales for NSUT 2026 teaching and officer posts?

Assistant Professors are placed in Level 10 (₹57,700–1,82,400) and Associate Professors in Level 13A1 (₹1,31,400–2,17,100) under 7th CPC. Campus Director carries Level 14 (₹1,44,200–2,18,200), Training & Placement Officer Level 13A1 (₹1,31,100–2,16,600) and Medical Officer Level 10 (₹56,100–1,77,500).

Q5. What is the eligibility criteria for NSUT Assistant Professor posts?

For Biological Sciences & Engineering/Bio-Technology and Geo-Informatics, candidates need B.E./B.Tech./B.S. and M.E./M.Tech./M.S./Integrated M.Tech in relevant branch with First Class in at least one degree. For Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics, candidates require a Master’s degree with 55% marks plus NET/SLET/SET, or a Ph.D. as per UGC norms (NET exempt). For Design, a First Class Bachelor’s/4-year Diploma and Master’s/PG Diploma in Design/Engineering plus required professional experience is

Q6. What is the qualification and experience required for NSUT Associate Professor posts?

Associate Professors require a Ph.D. in the relevant discipline with First Class at Bachelor’s or Master’s level and a minimum 8 years of teaching/research/industry experience, including at least 2 years post-Ph.D.. They must also have the prescribed number of SCI/SCIE/SCOPUS/UGC-listed publications and meet the UGC 2018 research score requirement, as detailed in the notification for each department.

Q7. What is the age limit for NSUT Recruitment 2026?

For Assistant Professor, the maximum age is 35 years on the closing date of online application, and for Associate Professor, it is 50 years. Campus Director must be born on or after 30 June 1966, Training & Placement Officer on or after 30 June 1976, and Medical Officer on or after 30 June 1991, with applicable relaxations for SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD as per Government of India norms.

Q8. What is the application fee for NSUT Recruitment 2026?

General, OBC and EWS candidates must pay ₹1,000 as registration fee + ₹1,000 as processing fee (₹2,000 total) per application. SC/ST/PwBD candidates are exempt from registration fee and pay only ₹1,000 processing fee. The fee is non‑refundable and must be paid only through the online application portal.
