The Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education (HPBOSE) has officially released the HPBOSE Class 12th Result 2026 for the academic session 2025-26. Students who appeared in the HP Board Class 12 examinations can now check their marks online using their roll number on the official website, hpbose.org.

The board has published results for Class 12 students across all streams including Science, Commerce, and Arts. Students can easily check and download their marksheet by entering their roll number on the official result portal.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in the result. In case of any discrepancies, they should immediately contact the HPBOSE authorities at Dharamshala for correction.
