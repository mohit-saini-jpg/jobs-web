The Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education (HPBOSE) has officially released the HPBOSE Class 12th Result 2026 for the academic session 2025-26. How to Check HPBOSE Class 12th Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check your HPBOSE 12th Result 2026 online:

  • Visit the official website - hpbose.org
  • Click on the link “HPBOSE Class 12th Result 2026”
  • Enter your Roll Number in the login field
  • Click on the Submit button
  • Your result will appear on the screen
  • Download and take a printout for future reference
