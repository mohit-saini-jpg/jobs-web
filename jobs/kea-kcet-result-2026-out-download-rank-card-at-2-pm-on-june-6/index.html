How to Download KCET 2026 Result & Rank Card

Follow these steps to check and download your KCET 2026 scorecard:

1. Visit the official website: Go to cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea 2. Click the KCET Result link: Find and click "KCET Result 2026" on the portal homepage 3. Enter login credentials: Input your KCET application number and the first four characters of your name 4. Submit: Click the submit button to view your result 5. Download & print: Save the KCET Rank Card 2026 PDF and take a printout for future reference

What's Included in KCET 2026 Scorecard

The KCET 2026 scorecard contains:

  • - Candidate's name and registration number
  • - Subject-wise marks achieved
  • - Total/aggregate marks
  • - KCET rank assigned
  • - Qualifying status (pass/fail)

Official Websites to Check KCET 2026 Result

You can access KCET 2026 results through these official KEA websites:

  • - Primary: cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea
  • - Secondary 1: www.kea.kar.nic.in
  • - Secondary 2: www.karresults.nic.in

KCET 2026 Counselling Process (UGCET)

After the result release, KEA will conduct UGCET counselling for engineering, pharmacy, agriculture, and other professional courses.

Key Counselling Steps:

- Rank card is used for counselling registration - Candidates must register within the specified window - Category-wise seat allocation based on rank - Document verification at designated centres
