The Directorate of Elementary Education, Haryana has officially released the Haryana RTE Lottery Result 2026-27 for entry-level classes under the Right to Education 25% quota for the 2026-27 academic session. The lottery-based admission result has been declared for Nursery, LKG, UKG, and Class 1 seats in recognised private schools participating in the Haryana RTE scheme.

Haryana RTE Lottery Result 2026-27 has been published online on the official RTE admission portal accessible through harprathmik.gov.in. Parents can check and download the provisional selection list and school allotment details by visiting the RTE admission login page and entering their application number or registered mobile details as required. After downloading the allotment list, parents are advised to verify the child’s name, allotted class, school, and category details carefully and contact the concerned Block or District education office in case of any discrepancy during document verification.

How to Check Haryana RTE Lottery Result 2026-27

Parents can follow the step-by-step process given below to check Haryana RTE Lottery Result 2026-27 online and download the admission list.

  • Visit the official Haryana RTE/Ujjwal admission portal through the link available on harprathmik.gov.in or directly open the RTE portal URL.
  • On the homepage, click on the option for 'RTE Admission Result 2026-27' or 'Lottery Result/Admission List' as displayed.
  • You will be redirected to the Haryana RTE admission login or result page.
  • Enter the required details such as Application Number, registered Mobile Number, or other login credentials as asked on the screen.
  • Click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button to proceed.
  • The Haryana RTE Lottery Result 2026-27 provisional list or PDF will appear, showing the child’s name, allotted class, school, district, and priority details.
  • Download the PDF or save the allotment page and take a printout to carry during document verification and final admission at the allotted school.
