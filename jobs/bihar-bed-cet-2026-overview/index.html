What is Bihar B.Ed CET 2026?

Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 is a state-level entrance examination conducted for admission to the 2-year Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) course in Bihar. The entrance test is meant for candidates seeking admission into recognized B.Ed colleges and universities in the state. Admission is generally granted on the basis of entrance exam performance followed by counselling and seat allotment.

This exam is especially important for students who want to become teachers in schools after completing a professional teacher education programme. Because of its role in the admission process, Bihar B.Ed CET remains one of the most searched teaching entrance exams in the state every year.

Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must check the Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 eligibility criteria carefully before applying. If an applicant does not meet the required qualifications, the application may be rejected during verification or counselling.

Educational Qualification

Candidates should have a Bachelor’s degree or Master’s degree with at least 50% marks from a recognized university. Candidates belonging to Engineering or Technology streams are generally required to have at least 55% marks.

Minimum Marks Requirement

For most candidates, a minimum of 50% marks in Graduation or Postgraduation is required. However, candidates should confirm category-wise relaxation details from the official notification once it is released.

Age Limit

There is generally no upper age limit for Bihar B.Ed CET. Candidates from different age groups can apply if they meet the educational qualification requirements.

Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 Application Form

The Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 application form is expected to be released online in May on the official website. Candidates must complete the application process within the prescribed deadline. Since the form is filled online, candidates should keep all documents ready before starting the registration process.

How to Fill Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 Application Form

  • Visit the official website of Bihar B.Ed CET 2026.
  • Click on the online registration link.
  • Create an account using a valid mobile number and email ID.
  • Fill in personal, educational, and communication details carefully.
  • Upload the required documents such as photograph and signature.
  • Pay the application fee through the available online payment mode.
  • Submit the form and download the confirmation page for future use.

Documents Required for Bihar B.Ed CET Application Form

  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Scanned signature
  • Graduation or Postgraduation marksheet
  • Valid ID proof
  • Caste certificate, if applicable
  • Domicile certificate, if applicable

Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 Admit Card

The Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 admit card is expected to be release online in June, a few days before the exam. Only registered candidates will be able to download the admit card by logging in with their credentials. The admit card is a mandatory document for entry to the examination centre.

The hall ticket will usually contain details such as the candidate's name, roll number, exam date, shift, reporting time, exam centre address, and important instructions. Candidates should check every detail carefully after downloading it.

Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 Answer Key

After the exam is conducted, the conducting authority may release the Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 answer key on the official website, likely in June. The answer key helps candidates estimate their probable score before the final result is announced.

If an objection facility is provided, candidates may be allowed to challenge specific answers within the prescribed time limit. The final answer key is generally used for preparing the result.

Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 Result

The Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 result is expected to be declared online, likely in June, after the completion of the entrance examination and answer key process. Candidates will be able to check their marks, rank, and qualifying status using their login details on the official website.

The result is one of the most important stages in the admission process because it determines a candidate’s eligibility for counselling and seat allotment. Candidates should download and save their scorecard for future admission rounds.

Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 Counselling Process

Qualified candidates will have to participate in the Bihar B.Ed CET 2026 counselling process for admission into colleges. Counselling is generally conducted online and includes multiple stages.

  • Online counselling registration
  • Choice filling of preferred colleges
  • Seat allotment based on rank and preferences
  • Document verification
  • Admission fee payment and final confirmation
