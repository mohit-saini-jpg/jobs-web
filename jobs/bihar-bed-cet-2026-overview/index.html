5What is Bihar B.Ed CET 2026?
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphBihar B.Ed CET 2026 is a state-level entrance examination conducted for admission to the 2-year Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) course in Bihar. Admission is generally granted on the basis of entrance exam performance followed by counselling and seat allotment.
2paragraphThis exam is especially important for students who want to become teachers in schools after completing a professional teacher education programme. Because of its role in the admission process, Bihar B.Ed CET remains one of the most searched teaching entrance exams in the state every year.
