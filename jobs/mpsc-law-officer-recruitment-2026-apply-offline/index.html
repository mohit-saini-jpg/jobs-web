Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the MPSC Law Officer (Contractual) recruitment?

The last date to submit the offline application for the MPSC Law Officer (Contractual) recruitment is 30th May 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well in advance of the deadline.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Law Officer post?

To be eligible for the Law Officer (Contractual) position, candidates must be a Law Graduate from a recognized university and an enrolled advocate with at least 5 years of experience.

Q3. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

No, as per the provided information, there is no application fee for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this MPSC Law Officer (Contractual) recruitment.

Q4. What is the maximum age limit for the Law Officer (Contractual) post?

The maximum age limit for candidates applying for the Law Officer (Contractual) post is 45 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per government norms.

Q5. What is the salary offered for the Law Officer (Contractual) position?

The selected candidates for the Law Officer (Contractual) post will receive a consolidated salary ranging from Rs. 70,000 to Rs. 78,000 per month, along with an additional allowance of Rs. 8,000 for telephone, travel, stationery, and xerox.

Q6. How many vacancies are available for the Law Officer (Contractual) post?

There are a total of 4 vacancies for the Law Officer (Contractual) position announced by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) in this recruitment drive.

Q7. What is the mode of application for this recruitment?

The application process for the MPSC Law Officer (Contractual) recruitment is offline. Candidates need to download the application form and submit it via post or as instructed in the notification.

Q8. What is the selection process for the Law Officer (Contractual) role?

The selection process for the Law Officer (Contractual) position primarily involves Document Verification. Candidates will be shortlisted based on their eligibility and submitted documents.
