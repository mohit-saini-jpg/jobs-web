How to Download Mumbai University Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the Mumbai University Time Table 2026 from the official examination portal. Follow these steps:

  • Visit the official Mumbai University website at www.mu.ac.in.
  • Go to the Examination section and select the Timetable option.
  • Choose the relevant category for May 2026 examinations.
  • Locate the practical timetable for M.A. Geography CDOE.
  • Click on the link to open the PDF file.
  • Download and save the timetable on your device for ready reference.

Students should carefully review the official timetable and keep a printed copy for exam preparation.
