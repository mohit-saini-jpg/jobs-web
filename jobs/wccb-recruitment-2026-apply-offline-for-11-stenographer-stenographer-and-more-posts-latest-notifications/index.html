Qualification / Eligibility

DetailsPublic Prosecutor: Officers holding analogous posts or with 2-3 years in feeder grade; Bachelor's Degree in Law; 3 years experience in handling prosecution of criminal cases. Head Constable / Forest Guards: Officers holding analogous posts in Level-4 or 5 years in Level-3 as Constable; 2 years experience in intelligence/enforcement/investigation. Driver: Officers holding analogous posts; valid driving licence; 3 years driving experience; 10th pass; knowledge of motor mechanism.
