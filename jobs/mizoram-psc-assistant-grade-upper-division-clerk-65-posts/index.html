FAQs

Q1How many vacancies are announced in MPSC Assistant UDC Recruitment 2026?
A
Mizoram PSC has announced 65 vacancies including 30 Assistant Grade and 35 UDC posts across various Government of Mizoram departments.
Q2What is the last date to apply for MPSC Assistant Grade 2026?
A
The last date for online applications is 19 June 2026 (up to 4:00 PM) through the MPSC online portal.
Q3What is the eligibility for MPSC Assistant & UDC Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university, computer proficiency certificate, and Mizo language proficiency (exemption if studied Mizo up to Class-X).
Q4What is the age limit for MPSC Assistant UDC Vacancy 2026?
A
Age limit is 18 to 35 years as on 01 January 2026, with 5 years relaxation for SC/ST candidates as per Government rules.
Q5What is the salary for MPSC Assistant Grade posts?
A
Assistant Grade: Level-7 Pay Matrix | UDC: Level-6 Pay Matrix as per 7th CPC adopted by Government of Mizoram.
Q6What is the selection process for MPSC Assistant & UDC 2026?
A
Selection includes Written Examination, Document Verification, and Mizo Language Proficiency Test (wherever applicable).
Q7Is there application fee for MPSC Assistant Recruitment 2026?
A
PwBD candidates are exempted. Other candidates must pay as per MPSC norms (check official portal for exact fee structure).
Q8Which departments have MPSC UDC vacancies 2026?
A
UDC vacancies are available in departments like General Administration, FCS&CA, H&TE, Urban Development, Cooperation, PHE, Sports, Art & Culture, Social Welfare, Home, AH&V, and State Election Commission.
