How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply online only at www.sssc.uk.gov.in. No other mode of application will be accepted. No hard copy of the application or any document is to be sent to the Commission's office. Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date and not wait until the last date.
  • Steps to Apply Online:
  • Step 1: Registration & Personal Details (Use your own mobile number and email ID; one Aadhaar can be used only once)
  • Step 2: Educational & Other Details
  • Step 3: Upload Photo, Signature, Left Thumb Impression (Photo: 150w×200H px JPEG; Signature: 150w×100H px JPEG; Thumb: 150w×100H px JPEG)
  • Step 4: Make Payment (Credit Card / Debit Card / Net Banking / UPI only)
  • Step 5: Print Application (Application is considered complete only when "Completed" appears in the Status column)
  • Documents to be Uploaded (Scanned):
  • Latest passport-size colour photograph (150w×200H px; JPEG/JPG)
  • Signature with black ink on white paper (150w×100H px; JPEG/JPG)
  • Left thumb impression on white paper (150w×100H px; JPEG/JPG)
  • High School Certificate (for name and date of birth verification)
  • Aadhaar Card (both sides)
  • Category / Sub-category reservation certificate (vertical/horizontal - whichever applicable)
  • Experience certificate (where mandatory for the post)
  • Documents to be Produced at Examination / Document Verification / Engagement:
  • Admit Card (downloaded from Commission's website) along with a valid photo identity proof in original and self-attested photocopy
  • Caste Certificate for SC/ST candidates – as per Annexure-2(Ka) format
  • OBC (Other Backward Class) Certificate (Non-Creamy Layer) – as per Annexure-2(Kha) format
  • EWS Income and Asset Certificate – as per Annexure-2(Ga) format, issued between 01.04.2026 and 21.06.2026
  • Freedom Fighter's Dependent Certificate – as per Annexure-2(Gha) format
  • Disability Certificate (for PwBD candidates) – as per Annexure-2(Cha) format
  • Educational qualification certificates and mark sheets (from High School onwards)
  • Employment Exchange Registration Certificate (Uttarakhand)
  • Domicile / Permanent Resident Certificate of Uttarakhand
  • Printout of Application Form is NOT to be sent to the Commission. Admit Card for Written Examination will NOT be dispatched by post. Candidates must appear for the test at their own cost; Commission will not pay or reimburse any travel expenses.
  • Apply Online at: https://www.sssc.uk.gov.in

  • Each Aadhaar number can be used only once for registration. Candidates must use their own mobile number and email ID in the online application. No changes/modifications to name, mother's/father's name, age, or examination centre will be permitted after submission. If a candidate fills in partial name changes or multiple online applications, legal action will be initiated against them. Do not apply on the last date - excessive web traffic on closing days may prevent submission of applications. The application is considered complete only when "Completed" is printed in the Status column of the online application form, and the fee has been received in the Commission's account before the last date. Candidates must ensure they hold all mandatory educational and other qualifications as on the last date of online application. After the written examination, document verification is a mandatory stage where all reservation/educational/preferential eligibility certificates are verified. Candidature will be cancelled if found ineligible at any stage. Candidates who misrepresent facts, use unfair means, copy from others, or assist others in copying during the examination will be debarred from all future UKSSSC examinations and an FIR will be registered. Mobile phones, calculators, smartwatches, pagers, scanners, earphones, or any electronic/communication device are NOT allowed inside the examination premises. Candidates are advised not to bring such items to the exam centre as security cannot be guaranteed. All instructions given during security check at the examination centre must be followed mandatorily. OBC candidates from the Creamy Layer must apply under UR (Unreserved) category; they are not entitled to OBC reservation or relaxation. If a candidate claims reservation in more than one sub-category, they will be eligible for the benefit of only one sub-category - the most beneficial one - provided they submit valid proof for that category. All revisions/corrigenda (if any) will be hosted on the Commission's website only - candidates should visit www.sssc.uk.gov.in regularly. Action under the Uttarakhand Competitive Examinations (Measures for Prevention and Prohibition of Unfair Means) Act, 2023 will be taken against candidates using unfair means. No request for change of examination centre shall be entertained. Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification. The Commission does not advise candidates regarding their eligibility. Candidates must read the advertisement carefully and apply only when fully satisfied that they meet all conditions.
