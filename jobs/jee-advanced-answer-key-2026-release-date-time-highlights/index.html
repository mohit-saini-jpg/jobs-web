JEE Advanced Answer Key 2026 PDF Link & Official Website

The official JEE Advanced Answer Key 2026 PDF will be hosted only on the JEE Advanced website, jeeadv.ac.in. The link is usually visible on the homepage or inside the candidate login portal, often labelled as “JEE Advanced 2026 Answer Key” or “Provisional Answer Key”.

You will need the following details to access the answer key or response sheet:

  • JEE Advanced 2026 registration number
  • Date of birth (in DD/MM/YYYY format)
  • Registered mobile number used during registration

Make sure you have these ready before the answer key link goes live, because the system will not let you in without correct credentials.

How to Download JEE Advanced Answer Key 2026 PDF

Downloading the JEE Advanced Answer Key 2026 is a pretty straightforward process once you’re on the official site. Follow these simple steps:

  • Go to the official JEE Advanced website: jeeadv.ac.in.
  • Look for the link that mentions “JEE Advanced 2026 Answer Key” or “Provisional Answer Key”.
  • Click on the link; you may be asked to login with registration number, date of birth and mobile number.
  • After login, select Paper 1 or Paper 2 answer key as required.
  • The answer key will open in PDF format on the screen.
  • Download and save the PDF; you can also print it if you prefer checking on paper.

In some cases, JEE Advanced also provides direct public PDFs for each paper without login, but you should still rely on the official link mentioned on the site for authenticity.

JEE Advanced Response Sheet 2026: How to Download

Your JEE Advanced response sheet 2026 is basically a copy of all the answers you marked in the exam. It has already been released on May 21, 2026, and is available under your candidate login.

To download the response sheet:

  • Visit jeeadv.ac.in and open the candidate login portal.
  • Click on the “Response Sheet” or similar link for JEE Advanced 2026.
  • Enter your registration number, date of birth and other details as asked.
  • Once logged in, download the response sheet for Paper 1 and Paper 2.
