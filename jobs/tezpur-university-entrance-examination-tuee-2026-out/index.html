Tezpur University Entrance Examination (TUEE) 2026 OUT

Tezpur University has officially released the Tezpur University Entrance Examination (TUEE) 2026 schedule for admission to various undergraduate, postgraduate, professional, and doctoral programmes. The entrance examinations will be conducted from 05 June 2026 to 07 June 2026 in multiple sessions for different courses offered by the university.

Candidates appearing for TUEE 2026 can check the complete examination schedule through the official website, www.tezu.ernet.in. The university has also informed applicants that admit cards will be available for download from 01 June 2026 through the Samarth admission portal using their login credentials.

Students can access the examination schedule PDF, verify their allotted exam date and session, and download the admit card by logging into the admission portal with the required credentials. Candidates are advised to carefully verify all details mentioned in the schedule and admit card and contact the university immediately if any discrepancy is found.

How to Download Tezpur University Entrance Examination (TUEE) 2026

Candidates can download the Tezpur University Entrance Examination (TUEE) 2026 schedule and admit card through the official admission portal by following the steps below.

  • Visit the official Tezpur University website at www.tezu.ernet.in.
  • Open the admissions or TUEE 2026 notification section.
  • Locate the examination schedule PDF link.
  • Click on the TUEE 2026 Exam Schedule notification.
  • The PDF file will open on your screen.
  • Review your programme name, examination date, and session timing.
  • Download the PDF and save it for future reference.
  • For admit cards, visit https://tezuadm.samarth.edu.in/.
  • Log in using your registered credentials.
  • Download and print the admit card for examination purposes.

Candidates should carefully check the official examination schedule and keep a printed copy for exam preparation and reference.
