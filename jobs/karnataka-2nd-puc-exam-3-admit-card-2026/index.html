Karnataka 2nd PUC Exam 3 Admit Card 2026

The Karnataka Department of Pre-University Education is expected to release the Karnataka 2nd PUC Exam 3 Admit Card 2026 today for students appearing in the supplementary exam. Students will be able to access the hall ticket through the official portal, while in practice the admit card is usually downloaded by school authorities and distributed to candidates.

The Karnataka 2nd PUC Exam 3 2026 is scheduled to be held from 9 June to 21 June 2026, so students should collect the admit card on time and verify all printed details carefully.

How to Download Karnataka 2nd PUC Exam 3 Hall Ticket

  • Visit the official website pue.karnataka.gov.in.
  • Open the Examination / PU Examination Portal section.
  • Log in using the school or college credentials.
  • Select the Karnataka 2nd PUC Exam 3 Hall Ticket 2026 link.
  • Download the admit card and print it for distribution to students.

Students generally do not download the hall ticket directly; they receive it from their school authorities.

Details Mentioned on the Hall Ticket

The Karnataka 2nd PUC hall ticket generally includes:

  • Candidate’s name.
  • Registration number.
  • Photograph and signature.
  • School name and school code.
  • Exam centre name and address.
  • Subject list and exam schedule.
  • Important exam-day instructions.

Students should check every detail carefully and report any errors to the school immediately.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates should carry:

  • A printed copy of the Karnataka 2nd PUC Exam 3 Admit Card 2026.
  • A valid school ID card or any ID specified by the school/centre.
  • Recent passport-size photographs, if asked by the school.

Without the hall ticket, entry to the exam hall may not be allowed.

Exam-Day Instructions

  • Reach the exam centre well before reporting time.
  • Carry only allowed items.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators or electronic gadgets.
  • Follow all instructions issued by the invigilators and school authorities.
  • Keep the hall ticket safe until the exam process is completed.
