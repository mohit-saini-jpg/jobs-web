FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for Bihar State Sports Authority KISCE Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for submission of online applications is 21 June 2026 at 11:59 PM at https://coach.biharsports.org/. Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date. Applications received after the last date will not be considered.
Q2How many posts are available in Bihar State Sports Authority KISCE Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 16 posts are available across 11 designations — High Performance Director (1), Physiologist Grade 2 (1), Physiologist Grade 1 (1), Nutritionist (1), Masseuse (3), Strength & Conditioning Lead (1), Strength & Conditioning Expert (1), Psychologist (1), Physiotherapist (3), Young Professional (1), Doctor (1), Nursing Assistant (1), and Yoga Instructor (1). The number of vacancies and place of deployment may vary as per workload.
Q3What is the salary for High Performance Director at BSSA KISCE 2026?
A
Performance Director will receive a remuneration of ₹1,00,000 to ₹1,50,000 per month on contractual basis. For other posts, salaries range from ₹25,000 (Nursing Assistant/Yoga Instructor) to ₹1,00,000 (Doctor/S&C Lead/Nutritionist) per month.
Q4What is the contract tenure for BSSA KISCE Recruitment 2026?
A
The contractual engagement is for an initial period of 2 years, extendable annually for up to 3 additional years (2+1+1+1) subject to satisfactory performance and periodic review. The maximum tenure shall not exceed 5 years and shall remain coterminous with the Khelo India Scheme.
Q5What is the eligibility for the Physiotherapist post in BSSA KISCE Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must hold a Master's degree in Physiotherapy from any recognized Indian or Foreign University with a minimum of 3 years of work experience as a Physiotherapist. Specialization in Sports Science carries the maximum shortlisting marks. The upper age limit is 45 years as on the closing date.
Q6How will candidates be shortlisted for interview in BSSA KISCE Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates will be shortlisted based on a scoring system that considers higher educational qualifications, total work experience, work experience in sports establishments, certifications, sports achievements (where applicable), and percentage of marks obtained. Only shortlisted candidates will be invited for the interview via their registered email ID.
