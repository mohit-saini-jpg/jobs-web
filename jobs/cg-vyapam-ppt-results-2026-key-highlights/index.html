How to Check CG Vyapam PPT Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to check and download your CG Vyapam Pre Polytechnic Test (PPT) Result 2026:

  • Visit the official website: vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in or open the direct portal link: https://vyapamprofile.cgstate.gov.in/online/.
  • Look for the 'PPT Result 2026' or 'Pre Polytechnic Test Result' link on the homepage or results/notifications section.
  • Click 'View Result' next to the PPT Result 2026 notification.
  • Enter your login credentials (roll number and date of birth or registration number) in the required fields.
  • Submit the details to view your result on-screen.
  • Download or print the result for future admission procedures and document verification.
