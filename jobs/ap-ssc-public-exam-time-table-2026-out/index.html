How to Download AP SSC Public Exam Time Table 2026

Students can easily download the AP SSC Public Exam Time Table 2026 by following the steps below:

  • Visit the official website of Board of Secondary Education, Andhra Pradesh.
  • Go to the Examinations or Time Table section.
  • Look for the link for SSC Advanced Supplementary Examinations May 2026 Time Table.
  • Click on the PDF link to open the timetable.
  • Download and save the file on your device.
  • Take a printout and keep it for exam preparation.

Note: Always refer to the official timetable and check all dates and timings carefully before the examinations.
