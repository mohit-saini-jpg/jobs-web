How to Apply

  • 1Step 1: Register on the mandatory apprenticeship portal - NAPS 2.0 (for Trade Apprentices) or NATS 2.0 (for Diploma & Graduate Apprentices). Ensure Aadhaar is linked with your bank account before registering.
  • 2Step 2: Download the Application Form (Annexure-1) from www.npcil.nic.in > HR Management > Opportunities. Verify your eligibility before filling the form.
  • 3Step 3: Fill in the application form completely in Block Letters. Affix a recent passport-size photograph in the space provided. Sign the declaration.
  • 4Step 4: Attach self-attested copies of all required documents (see list below).
  • 5Step 5: Send the complete application by post to reach on or before 30/06/2026 at: Deputy Manager (HRM), Nuclear Power Corporation of India Limited (A Government of India Enterprise), Madras Atomic Power Station, Kalpakkam – 603 102, Chengalpattu, Tamil Nadu.
  • 6Birth Certificate issued by Competent Authority / SSC Mark Sheet as proof of Date of Birth
  • 7Educational Qualification mark sheets (10th / 12th; and 8th mark sheet additionally for Welder trade)
  • 8ITI / Diploma / Graduate Degree certificate and mark sheets of all semesters/years
  • 9Other qualification/experience certificates, if any
  • 10Caste Certificate issued by Competent Authority in prescribed Central Government format (for SC/ST/OBC candidates - valid as on 30/06/2026)
  • 11Valid Income and Assets Certificate as on 30/06/2026 in prescribed format issued by Competent Authority (for EWS category)
  • 12Disability Certificate from Competent Authority in prescribed format (for PwBD candidates; disability must be 40% or more)
  • 13Police Verification Certificate issued by concerned District Police Authority
  • 14Character/Conduct Certificate from the concerned institution
  • 15Aadhaar Card (Compulsory)
  • 164 recent passport-size photographs
  • 17Residential proof of permanent address (if living within 16 km radius of MAPS, Kalpakkam)
  • 18Copy of Apprentice Registration on NAPS 2.0 (https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in) or NATS 2.0 (https://www.nats.education.gov.in)
  • 19Proof from University/Institute converting CGPA/GPA/SGPA into percentage (for candidates awarded grades)
  • 20THIS IS NOT AN EMPLOYMENT IN NPCIL. Engagement as an apprentice shall not create any obligation on NPCIL to offer regular employment, nor any obligation on the apprentice to accept employment under NPCIL after completion of training.
  • 21The period of apprenticeship training will be for a maximum of One Year. The contract of apprenticeship terminates on expiry of the training period.
  • 22Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing apprenticeship under the Apprentices Act-1961 in any organization are strictly NOT eligible to apply.
  • 23Preference in selection will be given to candidates from neighbouring villages within 16 km radius of the MAPS plant site in Kalpakkam. Merit-based selection from other candidates will follow only if trades/disciplines are not filled by local candidates.
  • 24All selected apprentices must sign a contract of apprenticeship with NPCIL as per the Apprentices Act-1961 and Apprenticeship Rules-1992.
  • 25Madras Atomic Power Station is a highly prohibited area. Engagement is subject to submission of Police Verification Certificate from the concerned District Police Authority.
