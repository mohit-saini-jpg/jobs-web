NPCIL MAPS Apprentices Recruitment 2026 - Apply Offline for 158 Trade, Diploma and Graduate Apprentices Posts

Human Resources Group, NPCIL – Madras Atomic Power Station

Posts: Various Last Date: 30/06/2026 Offline only – Application form to be sent by Post to Deputy Manager (HRM), NPCIL MAPS, Kalpakkam

Short Information

NPCIL Recruitment 2026 notification is out to fill 158 vacancies for Apprentices posts. Candidates can apply Offline only from 25.05.2026 to 30.06.2026 through NPCIL website npcil.nic.in.

Important Dates

Last Date To Apply30/06/2026

Age Limit

Age Details30/06/2026 (closing date of application)

Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationGraduate Degree in non-engineering/general streams (BA, B.Sc., B.Com., etc.) including sandwich courses granted by a College/University recognized by AICTE/UGC/State Government/Central Government (Full-Time/Regular mode only). Degree must have been passed within 5 years of 30/06/2026.

Selection Process

  • Merit based on marks obtained in ITI / Diploma / Graduation. Preference to candidates from neighbouring villages within 16 km radius of plant site.
  • Marks obtained in ITI / Diploma / Graduation qualifying examination

How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply offline only - no online application portal is available for submission. The process involves two steps: first, mandatory registration on NAPS 2.0 / NATS 2.0 portal; second, sending the filled application form by post to NPCIL MAPS Kalpakkam. Incomplete or erroneous applications or applications without proper enclosures will be summarily rejected without any communication.
