Jadavpur University UG Admission 2026

Jadavpur University UG Admission 2026 registration has started. Applications opened on May 20, 2026, for most undergraduate courses under the Faculty of Arts and Faculty of Science. The last date to apply is June 5, 2026, for BA (Hons) and BSc (Hons) programmes.

Jadavpur University (JU), Kolkata, one of India's top public universities, offers highly competitive 4-year UG Honours programmes. Admissions are primarily based on entrance tests conducted by the university for Arts and Science streams (except BTech/BPharm, which use WBJEE). This guide covers complete details on dates, eligibility, application process, programmes, and more to help aspirants.

How to Apply for Jadavpur University UG Admission 2026

  • Visit the official website https://admission.jdvu.ac.in/
  • Register and fill the online form
  • Upload documents
  • Pay test fee
  • Submit application

Application Fee

No application fee. Only the entrance test fee is applicable (₹200–₹400 depending on category).

Documents Required

  • Class 10 & 12 marksheets
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Photo and signature
  • Sports / PwD certificate (if claiming quota)
