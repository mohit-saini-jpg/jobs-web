Selection Process

  • Selection will be made strictly as per the relevant Service Rules through a multi-stage process:
  • Screening Test (Objective Type) – Tentatively on 05.07.2026 Subject Knowledge Test (Main Written Examination) – Tentatively on 09.08.2026 Interview / Viva-Voce
  • The Commission may decide to hold Screening Test + Subject Knowledge Test + Interview or direct Subject Knowledge Test + Interview depending on the number of applications.
  • Final merit list will be prepared on the basis of marks obtained in Subject Knowledge Test and Interview (Screening Test is only for shortlisting).
  • Candidates must qualify each stage to proceed to the next.
  • Document verification and medical fitness will be conducted before final appointment.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only. No other mode of application will be accepted. The application process involves four mandatory stages:
  • (i) Registration of the candidate
  • (ii) Filling up particulars in the application form
  • (iii) Payment of fee, and
  • (iv) Final submission of the signed application form.
  • Step-by-Step Application Process:
  • Visit the official website https://hpsc.gov.in
  • Candidates can register using their Mobile Number & Aadhaar Number. If Aadhaar data is not updated, register using Mobile Number.
  • After successful registration, a Login ID will be created.
  • Login and fill the online application form carefully with all required details.
  • Upload scanned copies of all necessary documents.
  • Pay the requisite application fee online (Net Banking / Debit Card / Credit Card).
  • After payment, download the application form, check all details, sign it, and upload the signed copy.
  • Take a printout of the finally submitted application form for future reference.
  • Note: Application is complete only after successful upload of the signed application form. Applications without the signed copy will be rejected.
  • Application Period (Reopened): 25.05.2026 to 29.05.2026 (upto 05:00 PM)
  • Documents Required to be Uploaded
  • Scanned Photo and Signature of the candidate
  • Scanned copies of LLB Degree and Mark Sheets
  • Proof of Hindi/Sanskrit upto Matric
  • Bar Council Enrollment Certificate
  • Caste/Category Certificate (SC/BC/EWS etc.)
  • PwBD Certificate (if applicable)
  • ESM/DESM Certificate (if applicable)
  • Haryana Resident/Domicile Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Experience Certificate (for age relaxation, if any)
  • Any other relevant documents

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for HPSC Assistant District Attorney Recruitment 2026?

The reopened online application window for HPSC ADA Recruitment 2026 is from 25 May 2026 to 29 May 2026 up to 5:00 PM.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in HPSC ADA Recruitment 2026?

There are 255 vacancies for Assistant District Attorney (Group-B) posts in the Prosecution Department, Haryana under Advertisement No. 18 of 2025.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for HPSC Assistant District Attorney 2026?

Candidates must have a Bachelor of Laws (LLB Professional) degree from a recognized university, be enrolled as an Advocate with the Bar Council, and have Hindi/Sanskrit up to Matric or higher standard.

Q4. What is the age limit for HPSC ADA Recruitment 2026?

The minimum age is 21 years and the maximum age is 42 years as per the original closing date 02 September 2025, with relaxations for reserved categories as per Haryana Government rules.

Q5. What is the selection process for HPSC Assistant District Attorney 2026?

The selection process includes a Screening Test, Subject Knowledge Test, and Interview, with final merit prepared based on marks in the Subject Knowledge Test and Interview only.

Q6. What is the salary of an HPSC Assistant District Attorney in Haryana?

Selected candidates will receive salary in Functional Pay Level-9, Rs. 53,100 to Rs. 1,67,800 as per Haryana Government pay rules.

Q7. Do candidates who applied earlier need to apply again for HPSC ADA 2026?

Candidates who already applied under Advt. No. 18 of 2025 need not apply again unless they want to update their details; fresh candidates can apply in the reopened window.

Q8. What is the application fee for HPSC ADA Recruitment 2026?

Application fees are Rs. 1000 for General/other state candidates, Rs. 250 for certain reserved categories and women of Haryana, and NIL for eligible PwBD candidates of Haryana.
