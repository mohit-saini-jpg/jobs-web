Important Instructions

THIS IS PURELY ADHOC ENGAGEMENT FOR 89 DAYS OR TILL 30.09.2026, WHICHEVER IS EARLIER. It is NOT a regular or permanent employment in VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital / VMMC. Selected candidates will NOT be treated as regular employees and will not be entitled to any benefits available to regular employees of the Hospital. Hospital shall have no obligation to offer regular employment after completion of the Adhoc period. Candidates who have already completed one year Junior Residency (Non-PG) MBBS in any Government Hospital are NOT ELIGIBLE and need not apply. Candidates must bring all original documents along with self-attested photocopies for verification. Failure to produce original documents will result in disqualification. EWS / OBC candidates must produce valid certificates issued on or after 01.04.2026 mentioning Non-Creamy Layer for Central Government posts. PwBD candidates (One Leg / One Arm / Both Orthopaedics) must produce valid disability certificate issued by competent authority. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the Walk-in-Interview. Candidates must follow all COVID-19 / safety protocols as applicable at the time of interview. The decision of the Hospital Administration regarding selection shall be final and binding. Any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be subject to the jurisdiction of Delhi courts only. Hospital reserves the right to cancel the recruitment process partially or fully at any stage without assigning any reason.

FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in Safdarjung Hospital Junior Resident Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 151 vacancies have been announced for the post of Junior Resident (Non-PG) MBBS on Adhoc basis at VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital, New Delhi. The number of posts is provisional and may increase or decrease depending on the actual requirement at the time of walk-in interview. Candidates are advised to check the official notice carefully as the final number of seats will be determined during the selection process itself.
Q2What is the walk-in interview date and reporting time for Safdarjung Hospital Junior Resident 2026?
A
The walk-in interview for Safdarjung Hospital Junior Resident (Non-PG) MBBS is scheduled on 18 June 2026 at 09:00 AM. Candidates must report between 08:30 AM to 09:30 AM at the Old Lecture Theatre, Behind OPD Building, Safdarjung Hospital, New Delhi. It is highly recommended to reach the venue early to avoid any last-minute rush and complete the registration process smoothly.
Q3What is the salary for Junior Resident (Non-PG) MBBS at Safdarjung Hospital?
A
Selected candidates will receive salary in Level 10 of the Pay Matrix at Rs. 56,100 + NPA along with other allowances as admissible under Central Government of India rules for the Adhoc period of 89 days or till 30.09.2026. This pay package makes it an attractive short-term opportunity for fresh MBBS graduates looking to gain clinical experience in a reputed central government hospital.
Q4Who is eligible to apply for Safdarjung Hospital Junior Resident Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates who have completed MBBS from a recognized university, possess Permanent Registration Certificate from Delhi Medical Council, and have completed internship on or after 01.07.2024 are eligible to attend the walk-in interview. Aspirants must ensure they meet all the eligibility conditions mentioned in the official notification before appearing for the selection process.
Q5Is previous Junior Residency experience allowed in Safdarjung Hospital recruitment?
A
No. Candidates who have already completed one year of Junior Residency (Non-PG) MBBS in any Government Hospital are not eligible and should not apply for this recruitment. This rule has been strictly implemented by the hospital administration to provide opportunity to fresh candidates who have recently completed their internship.
Q6What documents are required for Safdarjung Hospital Junior Resident walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must bring Permanent DMC Registration Certificate, Internship Completion Certificate (on or after 01.07.2024), recent passport size photograph, valid category certificate (EWS/OBC/SC/ST/PwBD) if applicable, and other identity proofs in original along with self-attested copies. All documents will be thoroughly verified on the spot, so candidates should carry complete set of originals and photocopies to avoid disqualification.
Q7What is the duration of appointment for Safdarjung Hospital Junior Resident posts?
A
The appointment is purely on Adhoc basis for a maximum period of 89 days or till 30 September 2026, whichever is earlier. It is not a regular or permanent job and selected candidates will be relieved automatically after completion of the adhoc tenure without any further extension guarantee.
Q8Is there any application fee for Safdarjung Hospital Junior Resident Recruitment 2026?
A
There is no application fee for this recruitment. Candidates only need to appear for the walk-in interview with all required documents. No online application is required, making the entire process simple and accessible for all eligible MBBS graduates across the country.
Q9Is PwBD reservation available in Safdarjung Hospital Junior Resident 2026?
A
Yes, horizontal reservation is available for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (One Leg / One Arm / Both Orthopaedics) as per Central Government rules. Eligible PwBD candidates must produce a valid disability certificate issued by a competent State or Central Government hospital at the time of interview for claiming the benefit.
Q10How can I apply for Safdarjung Hospital Junior Resident (Non-PG) MBBS 2026?
A
There is no online application process. Eligible candidates must directly attend the walk-in interview on 18 June 2026 at Safdarjung Hospital, New Delhi with duly filled application form (Annexure-I) and all original documents for verification. Candidates are strongly advised to read the full official notice and prepare all documents in advance to ensure a smooth selection process.
