GSSSB Additional Assistant Engineer (Mechanical) : Job Overview

The Gujarat Secondary Service Selection Board (GSSSB), Gandhinagar, has invited online applications for the post of Additional Assistant Engineer (Mechanical), Class-3, under the Gujarat Water Supply and Sewerage Board (GWSSB), which functions under the Narmada, Water Resources, Water Supply and Kalpsar Department. A total of 110 vacancies will be filled through a direct recruitment process based on a competitive written examination. Eligible diploma holders in Mechanical or Automobile Engineering can apply online through the OJAS portal between 15/06/2026 and 29/06/2026.

GSSSB Additional Assistant Engineer (Mechanical) : Exam Pattern

The selection process is based on a single-stage Computer Based Response Test (CBRT)/OMR-based competitive examination consisting of objective-type MCQ questions, divided into Part-A and Part-B. | Important points about the examination: | The exam consists of a total of 210 questions (90 in Part-A and 120 in Part-B) to be completed in a combined duration of 3 hours (180 minutes). Negative marking applies: for every wrong answer, 1/4th of the marks allotted to that question will be deducted. A provisional answer key will be published after the exam, followed by a final answer key after considering objections raised by candidates. Separate minimum qualifying standards of 40% apply to both Part-A and Part-B for all categories of candidates. | Part-B covers technical subjects relevant to Mechanical Engineering diploma holders. The major topics include Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Power Engineering, I.C. Engines, Refrigeration and Air-Conditioning, Engineering Mechanics, Mechanics of Materials, Kinematics and Theory of Machines, Engineering Materials, Manufacturing Processes, Metrology and Inspection, Computer Integrated Manufacturing, Operations Research, and Current Trends in these fields. The full subject-wise mark distribution is provided in Appendix-A of the official notification.

How to Apply

  • 1The Board accepts applications only through the online mode via the OJAS portal. Below is the step-by-step process for filling out the online application form for Advertisement No. 419/202627.
  • 2Visit the official website https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in.
  • 3Click on "Apply" under the Online Application section and select GSSSB.
  • 4Locate Advertisement No. 419/202627 (Assistant Engineer Mechanical, Class-3), click on the cadre name, then click Apply. Review the "More Details" option to read the complete advertisement before proceeding.
  • 5Click on Apply Now, then "Skip" to open the Application Format and fill in the Personal Details (fields marked with a red asterisk are mandatory).
  • 6Fill in the Educational Details section after completing Personal Details.
  • 7Note down the generated Application Number for future reference.
  • 8Upload your photograph and signature: photo should be 5 cm height x 3.6 cm width, and signature should be 2.5 cm height x 7.5 cm width, both in JPG format not exceeding 15 KB. The photo must have a white background and must not be older than one year from the last date of application.
  • 9Click on "Confirm Application" under the Online Application tab, enter your Application Number and Date of Birth, review the details, and confirm only after thorough verification, since no changes are allowed after confirmation. A Confirmation Number will be generated, which must be saved carefully.
  • 10Use "Print Application" to download and save a copy of the confirmed application form using your Confirmation Number and Date of Birth before the last date.
  • 11Important: A candidate should submit only one application. If multiple applications are submitted, only the last confirmed application with the requisite fee paid will be considered valid, and fees paid against earlier applications will not be adjusted or refunded.

Important Instructions

A few additional points related to reservation are worth noting for applicants belonging to reserved categories:
Vacancies in this advertisement may increase or decrease, and the figures shown are subject to change. Reserved category posts are applicable only to candidates who are originally from Gujarat and belong to the respective reserved category. If suitable women candidates are not available against women-reserved posts, those posts will be filled by male candidates from the same reserved category. Both male and female candidates can apply for posts that are not specifically reserved for women within a category, as selection is based on merit.
Before submitting the application, candidates should keep the following points in mind:
Category details once confirmed in the online application cannot be changed later. Any correction must be made by submitting a fresh application with the requisite fee before the last date. All details filled in the application form will be treated as final for the entire recruitment process and must be supported by original documents at the time of verification. Candidates appointed under the Gujarat Water Supply and Sewerage Board will not be considered employees of the Gujarat Government. The Gujarat Public Examination (Prevention of Unfair Means) Act, 2023, applies to all competitive examinations conducted by GSSSB, and any violation will attract legal action under this Act.
