UP ITI Admission 2026 - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 13/07/2026

The State Council of Vocational Training (SCVT), Lucknow has opened online registration for UP ITI Admission 2026 on July 11, 2026. Candidates who want to take admission in Uttar Pradesh's Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) can apply through the official website, scvtup.in, until August 4, 2026. This article covers the important dates, eligibility criteria, application fee, selection process, and step-by-step application procedure based on the official notification.

Educational Qualification for UP ITI Admission

Applicants need to have passed Class 10 (Secondary/Matriculation) or an equivalent examination from a recognised board. The notification does not specify separate qualification requirements for individual trades, so candidates should refer to the official prospectus on scvtup.in for trade-wise details.

Age Limit for UP ITI Admission 2026

The minimum age to apply is 14 years. The official notification states there is no upper age limit for this admission cycle.

UP ITI Admission 2026 Application Fee

The application fee has to be paid online at the time of form submission. It varies by category, as shown below.

The mode of online fee payment (net banking, UPI, debit/credit card, etc.) is not specified in the official notification. This information is not available at the moment.

UP ITI Admission 2026 Selection Process

Admission to UP ITI institutes is entirely merit-based. There is no entrance examination. A merit list is prepared using the marks candidates scored in their qualifying examination (Class 10 or equivalent). Candidates who feature in the merit list are called for counselling, which includes:

  • Choice filling of institute and trade
  • Seat allotment
  • Document verification

The overall admission process is expected to be completed in multiple rounds, with a provision for spot counselling if seats remain vacant after the regular rounds.

Required Documents for UP ITI Admission 2026

The official notification mentions that candidates need to upload the required documents during registration, but it does not list the specific documents needed. This information is not available in the source notification at this time. Candidates are advised to check the official prospectus on scvtup.in or the detailed notification PDF for the exact document checklist before applying.

How to Apply for UP ITI Admission 2026

Candidates can follow the steps below to complete their registration.

StepProcedure
1Visit the official SCVT website, scvtup.in.
2Click on the "UP ITI Admission 2026" link on the homepage.
3Enter personal details such as name, father's name, mother's name, date of birth, category, Aadhaar number, and mobile number.
4Verify the mobile number and select the type of institute.
5Fill in the remaining educational and communication details.
6Upload the required documents and pay the application fee online.
7Submit the completed application form.
8Download and print the confirmation page for future reference.

Important Instructions for UP ITI Admission 2026

  • Apply only through the official website, scvtup.in, to avoid fraud or incorrect information.
  • Check the eligibility criteria carefully before starting the application.
  • Keep your Aadhaar number and mobile number ready, as mobile verification is part of the registration process.
  • Complete the registration before the last date, August 4, 2026, since late submissions may not be accepted.
  • Download and safely keep a printout of the confirmation page after submitting the form.
  • Admission is based purely on merit in the qualifying examination; no entrance test is conducted.
