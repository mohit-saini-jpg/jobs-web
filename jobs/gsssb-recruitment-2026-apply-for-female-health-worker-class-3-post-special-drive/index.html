Job Overview

The Gujarat Gaun Seva Pasandgi Mandal (GSSSB), Gandhinagar has released an official advertisement for Female Health Worker (Female Health Worker, Class-3 / FHW Varg-3) positions under the Urban Health (Nagar Palika) wing of the Health and Family Welfare Department. This recruitment is exclusively for Divyang (Persons with Disabilities) candidates under a Special Recruitment Drive to fill backlog reserved vacancies. The deadline to submit online applications is June 20, 2026, by 11:59 PM.

Qualification / Eligibility

Core Education: Secondary or Higher Secondary School Certificate Examination from a recognized Board, or an equivalent qualification recognized by the Government. Professional Course: Female Health Workers Basic Training Course recognized by the Government; OR Auxiliary Nurse Midwife (ANM) Course recognized by the Government with valid registration under the Gujarat Nursing Council. Computer Knowledge: Basic knowledge of computer applications as prescribed under the Gujarat Civil Services Classification and Recruitment (General) Rules, 1967. Language: Adequate knowledge of Gujarati or Hindi or both. | These posts are reserved exclusively for Female candidates. Male candidates are not eligible to apply. | Only candidates with benchmark disabilities as per The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 are eligible. Candidates must apply under the relevant disability group (A, B, C, D & E) as advertised. A valid Disability Certificate in Form V, VI, or VII (as applicable) under The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Rules, 2017 is mandatory.

How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official OJAS portal at https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in.
  • 2Click on "Online Application" → Select GSSSB → Click on the advertisement for Female Health Worker, Class-3 (Advt. No. 417/202627).
  • 3Fill in all Personal Details and Educational Details carefully, ensuring all information matches your original certificates.
  • 4Upload your Passport-size Photo (5cm x 3.6cm, JPG, max 15KB) and Signature (2.5cm x 7.5cm, JPG, max 15KB). Photo must have a white background and must not be older than 1 year from the last application date.
  • 5Click Confirm Application to generate your Confirmation Number - save this for all future correspondence. No changes are possible after confirmation.
  • 6Pay the examination fee online by June 23, 2026 (11:59 PM) using Debit Card, Internet Banking, UPI, or Wallet on the OJAS portal.
  • 7Download and print the completed application form. Keep it safe until the entire recruitment process is complete.

FAQs

Q1Who can apply for this recruitment?
A
Only female candidates with benchmark disabilities (Divyang) as recognized under The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 are eligible. Male candidates cannot apply.
Q2What is the total number of vacancies?
A
There are a total of 25 vacancies distributed across 5 disability groups (A to E).
Q3What is the salary for this post?
A
Candidates will receive a fixed pay of ₹26,000/- per month for the first 5 years. After 5 years of satisfactory service, they become eligible for regular appointment in the pay scale of ₹19,900–₹63,200/- (Level-2, 7th Pay Commission).
Q4What is the application deadline?
A
The last date to apply online is June 20, 2026 (11:59 PM) and the last date to pay the exam fee is June 23, 2026 (11:59 PM).
Q5Is there negative marking in the exam?
A
Yes. ¼ mark will be deducted for each wrong answer in the MCQ-based exam.
Q6What disability certificate is required?
A
A valid disability certificate in Form V, VI, or VII (as applicable) under The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Rules, 2017 is required.
Q7Will exam fees be refunded?
A
Yes, but only for candidates who appear for the examination. Fees are non-refundable if the candidate is absent or the application is cancelled.
Q8Can a Scribe/Compensatory Time be availed?
A
Yes. Eligible Divyang candidates with writing limitations may apply for a Scribe or Compensatory Time (20 minutes per hour) by emailing [email protected] at least 2 days before the exam, along with required certificates.
