The institute provides excellent placement opportunities in core sectors like Petroleum, Energy, and IT. Final year students study at the Bengaluru campus. Interested candidates must check eligibility, fee structure, and important dates before applying to secure their seat in this prestigious Institution of National Importance.

About RGIPT

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT) is an Institution of National Importance established by the Government of India. It focuses on the energy sector, including petroleum, petrochemicals, green energy, AI, and digitalization. The main campus is at Jais, Amethi, Uttar Pradesh, with additional centres in Sivasagar (Assam) and Bengaluru (Karnataka). Final-year students shift to the Bengaluru campus for better industry exposure.
