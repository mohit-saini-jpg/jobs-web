How To Apply

  • This is a Walk-in-Interview recruitment. There is no online application process. Candidates must appear in person at the Walk-in-Interview on the scheduled date for their respective department/specialty. Candidates must report between 09:30 AM and 10:30 AM at the Academic Block, 2nd Floor, ESI-PGIMSR & ESIC Medical College, Joka, Kolkata – 700104 for document verification. Candidates reporting beyond 10:30 AM will NOT be considered. At the time of the interview, candidates must submit the completed Application Form (Annexure-I) and Verification Slip (Annexure-II) along with all original documents and one set of self-attested copies.

  • Experience Certificate (if any)

Important Instructions

  • Candidates who have completed 3 years tenure as Senior Resident in any Government Institution need NOT apply. Senior Residents already working in any Government Institution must obtain NOC from the respective institute before applying. The total period shall not exceed 3 years including any previous service. Candidates on bond are NOT eligible to apply; candidates who have completed compulsory post-PG Bond Service must produce a Bond Clearance Certificate from Appointing Authority. Candidates must report between 09:30 AM and 10:30 AM - those reporting beyond 10:30 AM will not be considered under any circumstances. The Demand Draft for application fee must be issued after the date of this advertisement (11.05.2026); DDs issued before this date will not be accepted. A security deposit of Rs. 32,000/- by Demand Draft must be paid at the time of joining; it will not be refunded if the Senior Resident terminates the agreement in between the engagement period. Private practice of any kind is strictly not allowed during the engagement period. No TA/DA will be admissible for joining or for any other purpose. For Non-Medical candidates, Non-Practicing Allowances (NPA) will be deducted from the consolidated remuneration of Rs. 1,48,669/-. The offer of engagement for Super Specialty Senior Residents will be subject to successful engagement of teaching faculty in the same Super Specialties. This engagement is purely contractual and terminable by either side with one month's prior notice. Selection does not confer any right of confirmation or continuation. The Dean, ESI-PGIMSR, ESIC Medical College & Hospital, Joka reserves the right to vary vacancies including increase, decrease, cancellation, or withdrawal of offer of engagement without assigning any reason.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 at Joka?

ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 is a walk-in-based engagement of 128 Senior Residents for 3 years under the Residency Scheme at ESI-PGIMSR, ESIC Medical College & Hospital and ODC (EZ), Joka, Kolkata. The posts include Clinical, Non-Clinical and Super Specialty departments on contractual basis under 7th CPC.

Q2. How many vacancies are announced under ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?

A total of 128 Senior Resident vacancies are notified, including 17 Non-Clinical posts and 111 Clinical/Super Specialty posts. Vacancies are distributed across UR, EWS, OBC, SC and ST categories with 4 percent reservation earmarked for PwBD when total vacancies are 100 or more.

Q3. What are the walk-in interview dates for ESIC Senior Resident 2026?

Walk-in interviews for Super Specialty Senior Residents are scheduled on 28 May 2026, for Clinical Senior Residents on 29 May 2026 and for Non-Clinical Senior Residents on 30 May 2026. Candidates must report for document verification between 09:30 AM and 10:30 AM on the respective dates.

Q4. Where will the ESIC Senior Resident walk-in interviews be conducted?

The walk-in interviews and document verification will be held at the Academic Block, 2nd Floor, ESI-PGIMSR & ESIC Medical College, Joka, Diamond Harbour Road, Kolkata – 700104. Candidates reporting after 10:30 AM will not be considered for the selection process.

Q5. What is the educational qualification required for ESIC Senior Resident posts?

For Clinical and Non-Clinical departments, candidates must possess a Medical PG Degree such as MD, MS or DNB in the concerned specialty from an MCI or NMC recognized institution with valid registration. For the Dental department, MDS is required, and for Super Specialties, DM, MCh, DNB or MD/MS/DNB in the relevant broad specialty as specified in the notification is accepted.

Q6. What is the age limit for ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?

The general upper age limit for Senior Residents in broad specialties is 45 years as on the date of interview. For specific non-clinical subjects like Anatomy, Biochemistry and Physiology, the upper age may be higher (up to 50 years), with relaxations applicable as per Central Government rules.

Q7. What is the salary for ESIC Senior Resident posts at Joka?

Selected Senior Residents will receive consolidated monthly remuneration of 1,48,669 rupees along with Transport Allowance and Dearness Allowance on Transport Allowance as per rules. Non-medical Senior Residents will have Non-Practicing Allowance (NPA) deducted from this consolidated amount.

Q8. Is there any application fee for ESIC Senior Resident Recruitment 2026?

Candidates from SC, ST, PwD, women and Ex-Servicemen categories are exempted from paying any application fee. All other categories must pay 500 rupees via Demand Draft drawn in favour of “ESI Fund Account No.1” payable at Kolkata and submit it at the time of walk-in.
