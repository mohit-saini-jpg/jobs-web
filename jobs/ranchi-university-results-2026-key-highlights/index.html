Ranchi University has released the Ranchi University Result 2026 press notices for Bachelor of Arts/Commerce/Science (VOC) Semester 1 Examination 2024 and Bachelor of Arts/Commerce (General) Semester 2 Examination 2024. Ranchi University Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - ranchiuniversity.ac.in.

Students who appeared in the BA/BCom/BSc (VOC) Sem 1 and BA/BCom (Gen) Sem 2 exams can check their result status by referring to the press release notices and then using their roll number on the university result/college notice systems, as applicable. After checking their marks and pass/fail status, they should verify all details and contact their college or the university examination branch if any discrepancy is noticed.

How to Check Download University Result 2026

Use the steps below to check your Ranchi University UG Result 2026 based on the press releases:

  • Open the student notice/result page https://ranchiuniversity.ac.in/notice-for-students.
  • Click on the relevant press release: Press Release of Bachelor of Arts/Com./Sci. (VOC) Semester 1 Examination 2024 Press Release of Bachelor of Arts/Com. (GEN) Semester 2 Examination 2024
  • Press Release of Bachelor of Arts/Com./Sci. (VOC) Semester 1 Examination 2024
  • Press Release of Bachelor of Arts/Com. (GEN) Semester 2 Examination 2024
  • Download and open the PDF by clicking on View Result.
  • Read the notice for details on result declaration, coverage (subjects/colleges) and how to obtain individual marksheets or check detailed results.
  • If a separate result link or instruction is provided (such as checking through college notice boards or online result entry), follow that process using your roll number/enrolment number.
  • Once you obtain your detailed marks, verify subject wise scores, total marks and pass/backlog status and keep a printed copy for your records.
