Eligibility Criteria

Project Manager: Master's Degree preferably MCA with at least 55% marks from a government recognized institute/university. Minimum 12 years of experience in office management at educational institute out of which at least 5 years at the level of Senior Assistant. Proficiency in various tools for office admin including MS-Office, EOffice, ERP, Systems etc., Web site, experience of admission procedure at the institute, organizing meeting, events, office file record, etc. Sr. Project Assistant (Admin.): Post Graduate Degree with 55% marks and 12 years of work experience with at least 5 years experience in the actual conduct of meetings/examinations and/or adequate experience of supervisory work at Jr. Superintendent level in a Govt. office or in an educational institution. Should know of Office Procedures, rules & regulations generally operative in Govt. office or in Educational Institution
