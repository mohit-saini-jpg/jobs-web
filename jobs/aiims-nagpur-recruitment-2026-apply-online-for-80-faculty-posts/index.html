Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for AIIMS Nagpur Faculty posts?

The closing date for the online application process is 08 June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline to avoid any last-minute issues.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for Faculty posts at AIIMS Nagpur?

AIIMS Nagpur has announced a total of 80 vacancies for various Faculty positions, including Professor, Additional Professor, Associate Professor, and Assistant Professor.

Q3. What is the minimum qualification required for Assistant Professor?

The minimum qualification for the Assistant Professor post is MBBS along with MD/MS/DM/MCh and at least 3 years of teaching experience.

Q4. What is the age limit for applying to the Faculty posts?

The maximum age limit is 58 years for Professor and Additional Professor, and 50 years for Associate Professor and Assistant Professor. Retired faculty can apply up to 70 years.

Q5. Is there any application fee for AIIMS Nagpur Faculty recruitment?

Yes, there is an application fee. General/OBC candidates need to pay Rs. 2000, while SC/ST candidates need to pay Rs. 500. Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwBD) are exempted from the fee.

Q6. What is the salary offered for these Faculty positions?

The salary ranges from Rs. 1,01,500 to Rs. 2,20,400 per month, depending on the specific post and the corresponding pay level.

Q7. What is the selection process for AIIMS Nagpur Faculty recruitment?

The selection process typically involves shortlisting based on bio-data, followed by an interview, document verification, and a medical fitness test.

Q8. Can candidates from other states apply for this recruitment?

The notification mentions that applications are invited from eligible candidates in Maharashtra. However, it's always best to check the detailed official notification for any specific state domicile requirements.
