How to Apply

  • 1Recent passport-size colour photograph (4.5 cm × 3.5 cm; 200×230 pixels; 20 KB–50 KB; JPEG)
  • 2Signature with black ink on white paper (140×60 pixels; 10 KB–20 KB; JPEG)
  • 3Left Thumb Impression on white paper with black or blue ink (240×240 pixels; 20 KB–50 KB; JPEG/JPG)
  • 4Handwritten Declaration in English on white paper with black ink (800×400 pixels; 50 KB–100 KB; JPEG/JPG) — in candidate's own handwriting
  • 5Live photograph captured via webcam or mobile phone (to be uploaded at the time of online application)
  • 6Call Letter (downloaded from Bank's website) along with photo identity proof (Passport / Aadhaar / PAN Card / Driving License / Voter's Card / Bank Passbook with photograph / Identity Card issued by School or College / Gazetted Officer in official letterhead) in original and self-attested photocopy
  • 7Caste Certificate in prescribed Government of India format (for SC/ST candidates) — to be submitted at the time of engagement if called for
  • 8OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in prescribed Government of India format, issued during the period 01-04-2026 to the date of joining (for OBC candidates)
  • 9Income and Asset Certificate issued by Competent Authority in the format prescribed by Government of India, valid for the year 2026-27 (for EWS candidates)
  • 10Disability Certificate in prescribed format issued by a Competent Authority as per Government of India guidelines (for PwBD candidates)
  • 11Educational qualification certificates and marksheets (Graduation onwards)
  • 12THIS IS NOT AN EMPLOYMENT IN THE BANK. NOR IS THE SAME CONTRACT EMPLOYMENT. Candidates engaged as Apprentice will NOT be treated as "employees" of SBI and will not be entitled to receive any benefits available for employees of the Bank.
  • 13SBI shall have no obligation to offer regular employment to Apprentices during and/or after the completion of the Apprenticeship period. After completion, candidates will be relieved from the respective work area.
  • 14Candidates can apply for engagement in one State only and can appear for the examination only once under this engagement project.
  • 15Mandatory registration on NAPS portal (https://apprenticeshipindia.gov.in) before applying online on SBI website is required.
  • 16No Ex-Servicemen / Disabled Ex-Servicemen shall be engaged as an Apprentice and there is no reservation for Ex-Servicemen in the Apprentice Engagement.
  • 17OBC candidates belonging to the "Creamy Layer" are not entitled to reservation/relaxation; they should apply as General or General (LD/VI/HI/d&e) as applicable.
  • 18Change of category will not be permitted after registration of online application.
  • 19Candidates with CIBIL default record (unpaid loans/credit card dues) are not eligible for engagement until dues are regularized/repaid.
  • 20All candidates (except Northeastern State candidates) must produce Aadhaar Number before engagement/contract signing.
  • 21Not more than one application should be submitted; in case of multiple applications, only the last valid completed application will be retained and fee paid for other registrations will stand forfeited.
  • 22PwBD candidates with disability conditions falling under "likely to improve" (temporary disability) category are not eligible for reservation.
  • 23PwBD candidates eligible for scribe facility: candidates with 40% or more disability having physical limitation to write are eligible. Scribe must be arranged by the candidate at his/her own cost. Compensatory time of 20 minutes per hour of examination is allowed (whether availing scribe or not) for eligible PwBD candidates.
  • 24Candidates are advised to keep their registered email ID/mobile active for receiving call letters/advices etc.
  • 25Mobile phones, smartwatches, pagers, earphones or any other electronic/communication devices are not allowed inside the examination premises.
