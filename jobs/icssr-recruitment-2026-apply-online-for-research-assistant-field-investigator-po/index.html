ICSSR Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for ICSSR Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit your application via email for the Research Assistant and Field Investigator positions is 20th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the designated email address before this deadline.
Q2What are the educational qualifications required for the Research Assistant post?
A
For the Research Assistant position, candidates must have a Postgraduate degree in any Social Science discipline with a minimum of 55% marks. Preference will be given to those with NET, M.Phil., or Ph.D.
Q3What is the salary for a Field Investigator in this ICSSR recruitment?
A
The selected candidates for the Field Investigator post will receive a monthly stipend of Rs. 20,000/-. This is a project-based role under the ICSSR funded project.
Q4How many vacancies are available in this ICSSR recruitment?
A
There are a total of 6 vacancies announced by the Indian Council of Social Science Research. This includes 1 post for Research Assistant and 5 posts for Field Investigator.

A
Interested candidates need to send their complete application along with their CV and required documents via email to the specified address. The application mode is strictly email-based.

Q7What is the selection process for these ICSSR posts?

The selection process primarily involves a Walk-in Interview. Candidates may also have the option to appear for the interview via video conferencing if they are unable to attend in person.
Q8What documents are required at the time of the interview?
