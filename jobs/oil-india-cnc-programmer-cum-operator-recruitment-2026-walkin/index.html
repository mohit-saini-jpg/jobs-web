Qualification / Eligibility

CNC Programmer cum Operator: Must have passed Diploma in Mechanical/Production/Electrical/Electronics & Tele-Communication or equivalent examination of minimum 02 (two) years duration from a Government Recognized Institute/Board. Must possess Certificate Course in Assistant Operator- CNC Turning or equivalent certificate course of minimum 06 (six) months duration or 600 hours duration from a recognized institute. Must have minimum 02 (Two) years post-qualification work experience on programming, operating and working in different types of CNC Machines including hands-on operation of CNC Lathe in any Govt./PSU/Reputed Private Organization.
