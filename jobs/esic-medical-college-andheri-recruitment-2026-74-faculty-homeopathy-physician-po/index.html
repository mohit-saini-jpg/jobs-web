ESIC Medical College Andheri Recruitment 2026: 74 Faculty : Job Overview

ESIC Medical College and Hospital, Andheri East, Mumbai has released Advertisement No. 11/2026 to fill 73 contract faculty/Senior Resident posts (19 Professor, 22 Associate Professor, 11 Assistant Professor, 21 Senior Resident) across 28 departments, plus 1 Homeopathy Physician post, through a walk-in (offline) interview scheduled between 24 and 26 July 2026. Candidates must report between 9:30 AM and 2:30 PM at the Dean Office, 5th Floor, ESIC Medical College & Hospital, MIDC, Andheri East, Mumbai, with the original application form, supporting documents, and demand draft for the fee.

Age Limit

The maximum age differs by post type, with additional relaxation available only for the Homeopathy Physician post. All age limits are calculated as on 30.06.2026 and verified again on the date of interview. Professor/Associate Professor/Assistant Professor (faculty posts): Not exceeding 68 years as on the date of interview. Senior Resident: Not exceeding 45 years as on the date of interview. Homeopathy Physician: Not exceeding 35 years as on the date of interview, with age relaxation of 3 years for OBC candidates and 5 years for SC/ST candidates . The maximum tenure of engagement for teaching faculty will be till 70 years of age , or 1 year extendable up to 3 years subject to satisfactory performance, whichever is earlier.

Selection for these posts is based on a combination of academic credentials and interview performance, followed by an offer of appointment sent only through email. The selection board evaluates candidates based on their acquired academic credentials and interview performance . Results are displayed in the Recruitment section of www.esic.gov.in . The Offer of Appointment is sent only through email .
