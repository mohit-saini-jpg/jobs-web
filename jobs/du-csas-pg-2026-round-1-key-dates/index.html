What Is the DU CSAS PG First Selection List 2026?

The DU CSAS PG First Selection List 2026 is the first official seat allotment list has released by Delhi University under the Common Seat Allocation System (CSAS) for postgraduate admissions. It is prepared on the basis of marks secured by candidates in CUET PG 2026, their programme preferences filled during registration, and category-wise seat availability across DU colleges and departments.

The list includes the following key details for each allotted candidate:

  • Name of the candidate
  • Application Number and Roll Number
  • Category and Category-wise rank
  • CUET PG marks / score obtained
  • Programme preference (choice) submitted
  • Allotted college or department
  • Opted postgraduate course

Only candidates whose names appear in the merit list are eligible to participate in further rounds of the DU PG 2026 admission process.

Where Is the DU PG Allocation List Available?

The DU CSAS PG First Selection List 2026 is available exclusively on the official CSAS (PG) admission portal at pgadmission.uod.ac.in (also accessible via admission.uod.ac.in). Candidates must log in using their CUET PG application number and password.

Steps to Check DU PG Merit List 2026

Follow these simple steps to access the DU PG Round 1 allocation:

  • Visit the official CSAS (PG) portal at pgadmission.uod.ac.in
  • Log in using your CUET PG Application Number and registered password
  • Navigate to the "PG Admission" section on your dashboard
  • Click on "Admission List" or "Allocation Status"
  • Select your desired programme from the list
  • The DU PG 2026 First Selection List will open in PDF format
  • Download and save the merit list for future reference
  • Verify your name, allotted college, and programme carefully

What Is the Seat Acceptance Deadline for DU PG Round 1?

Candidates allotted a seat in the DU PG CSAS Round 1 must accept their allotment by June 17, 2026, before 4:59 PM. This is a mandatory step — no acceptance means automatic cancellation of the seat.

The complete 4-step seat acceptance process is as follows:

Step 1 - Check Allocation Status

Log in to the CSAS (PG) portal at pgadmission.uod.ac.in using your CUET PG application number and password. Your dashboard will display the allotted programme name, college or department, and allotment status.

Step 2 - Accept the Allotted Seat

Click on the "Accept" button on your dashboard. This step is mandatory and must be completed by June 17, 2026, before 4:59 PM. Candidates who do not accept within this window will lose their Round 1 allotment.

Step 3 - Wait for Document Verification

After acceptance, the concerned college or department will review the candidate's uploaded academic qualifications, category certificates, and supporting documents. Document verification by colleges will continue until June 18, 2026.

Step 4 - Pay the Admission Fee Online

Once the college/department verifies and approves the documents, the online admission fee payment link is activated on the candidate's dashboard. The fee must be paid by June 19, 2026, before 4:59 PM to confirm admission. Failure to pay within the deadline will result in seat cancellation.

DU CSAS PG 2026 - Important Information for Allotted Candidates

This section covers important instructions that allotted candidates must follow to avoid losing their seat during the DU PG admission process.

  • Do not delay acceptance - Seat acceptance must be completed within the window of June 15-17. The portal closes at 4:59 PM on June 17.
  • Keep documents ready - The college/department will verify academic certificates, identity proof, and category certificates (if applicable) between June 15 and 18.
  • Fee payment activates only after approval - The admission fee payment link becomes active only after the respective college/department approves the candidate's documents.
  • Fee deadline is June 19 - Payment must be completed online by 4:59 PM on June 19, 2026 to confirm Round 1 admission.
  • Non-payment = seat cancellation - Missing the fee deadline results in automatic cancellation and exclusion from future regular rounds.
