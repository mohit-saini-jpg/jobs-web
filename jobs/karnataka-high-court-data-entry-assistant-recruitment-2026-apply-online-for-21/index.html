Karnataka High Court Data Entry Assistant Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for Karnataka High Court Data Entry Assistant Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application and pay the fee is 28 July 2026, till 11:59 PM.
Q2How many vacancies are available in this recruitment?
A
There are 28 total vacancies — 21 under the Residual Parent Cadre and 7 under the Kalyana Karnataka Region.
Q3What is the educational qualification required for Data Entry Assistant?
A
Candidates need a Bachelor's Degree (Science/Arts/Commerce/Business Management/Computer Applications) with 55% marks (General/OBC) or 50% marks (SC/ST), plus a Senior Grade Typewriting certificate in English and Kannada and a computer basics certificate.
Q4What is the age limit for this recruitment?
A
The minimum age is 18 years. The maximum age is 40 years for General Merit, 43 years for OBC Category-II/III, and 45 years for SC/ST/Category-I, with applicable relaxations.
Q5What is the application fee for Karnataka High Court DEA posts?
A
The fee is ₹350 for General Merit and OBC Category-II/III candidates, and ₹200 for SC/ST/Category-I/PwBD candidates, payable online via the SBI payment gateway.
Q6What is the selection process for Data Entry Assistant?
A
Selection is based on a Typing Test (100 marks) and an Interview (20 marks), with a combined passing requirement of 70 out of 120 marks.
