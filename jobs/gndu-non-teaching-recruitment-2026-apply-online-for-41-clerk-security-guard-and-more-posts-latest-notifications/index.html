How To Apply

  • Step-1: Pre Registration Process (SIGNUP)
  • Click on “Pre Registration” option under the ‘Online Registration’ menu.
  • Step 1.1: Select the Advertisement No.
  • Step 1.2: Select the Name of the Post
  • Step 1.3: Fill Basic Details
  • Step 1.4: Enter valid Mobile Number and Email ID
  • Step 1.5: Create a Password
  • After successful pre-registration, a unique Registration ID will be generated and sent to your registered email. This Registration ID will be used as Login ID for further steps.
  • Step-2: Fill Registration Form
  • Login using the Registration ID and Password.
  • Click on “Fill Registration Form”.
  • For ‘A’ Class Posts:
  • Enter Personal Details
  • Enter Employment/Education Details
  • Enter Publication/Experience Details
  • Enter Other Information Details
  • Enter Score Card Details
  • For all other Posts:
  • Enter Personal Details
  • Enter Employment/Education Details
  • Enter Other Information Details
  • Preview the form, verify all details, and proceed.
  • Note: One Registration Form is valid for one designation only.
  • Step-3: Lock the Registration Form
  • After filling and previewing, Lock the Registration Form.
  • Once locked, no changes or editing will be allowed in the form.
  • Step-4: Apply for the Post
  • For Regular Posts:
  • Select Campus/Station and Department/Subject.
  • Click on “Apply for the Post” button.
  • Repeat the process for additional Campus/Department (if allowed).
  • For Contractual Posts:
  • Select Department/Subject.
  • Select multiple Campuses/Stations and give preference order.
  • Click on “Apply for the Post”.
  • You can view all applied posts under ‘View Posts Applied’.
  • Step-5: Pay Application Fee
  • Ensure you have applied for all desired posts.
  • Go to “Pay Application Fee” under ‘Application Form’ menu.
  • Pay through Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking.
  • After successful payment, no changes will be allowed in the submitted form.
  • Step-6: Print Application Form
  • Take printout of all submitted Application Forms using ‘Print Application’ option.
  • Keep a copy for your reference.
  • Documents to be Sent (Hard Copy):
  • Printout of Online Application Form (with photo)
  • Self-attested copies of Educational Qualifications, Experience Certificates, Category Certificate, etc.
  • Keep one copy with yourself.
  • Send To: The Registrar, Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar-143005, Punjab

Important Instructions

  • Only online applications followed by hard copy will be accepted. Punjabi up to Matric level is essential for most posts. Reservation benefits are available only to Punjab Domicile candidates. Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification. University reserves the right to increase/decrease or cancel any post. Any dispute will be subject to the jurisdiction of Amritsar courts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for GNDU Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026?

The last date for online application submission for GNDU Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026 is May 28, 2026. However, candidates must also submit the hard copy of their application form along with required documents by June 04, 2026 to The Registrar, Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar-143005, Punjab.

Q2. How many vacancies are announced in GNDU Amritsar Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026?

Guru Nanak Dev University has announced a total of 41 vacancies under Advertisement No. 4/2026. The posts include Consultant (Taxation) - 1 post, Estimator - 1 post, Physiotherapist - 1 post, Watch & Ward Supervisor - 2 posts, Clerk-cum-Junior Data Entry Operator - 25 posts, and Security Guard - 11 posts across various categories.

Q3. What is the qualification required for GNDU Clerk-cum-Junior Data Entry Operator post?

Candidates must possess BCA/B.Sc.(IT)/B.Sc.(Computer Science) with 50% marks OR Graduation with PGDCA OR B.Tech/BE with Computer Science OR Master's in Computer Science/IT with 50% marks for the Clerk-cum-Junior Data Entry Operator position. Additionally, knowledge of Punjabi up to Matric standard is mandatory.

Q4. What is the salary for GNDU Non-Teaching posts 2026?

The salary for GNDU Non-Teaching posts ranges from Rs. 18,000/- to Rs. 50,000/- per month depending on the position. Consultant (Taxation) receives Fixed Pay of Rs. 50,000/- PM, Estimator and Physiotherapist get Rs. 29,200/- (Level-5), Watch & Ward Supervisor receives Rs. 21,700/- (Level-3), Clerk-cum-DEO gets Rs. 19,900/- (Level-2), and Security Guard receives Rs. 18,000/- (Level-1).

Q5. What is the application fee for GNDU Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026?

For Consultant (Taxation) post, General/Others category candidates must pay Rs. 1,180/- while SC/ST/PwD (Punjab Domicile) pay Rs. 590/-. For all other posts (Sr. No. 2 to 6), General/Others candidates pay Rs. 944/- and SC/ST/PwD (Punjab Domicile) candidates pay Rs. 472/-. The fee is non-refundable and must be paid online.

Q6. How to apply online for GNDU Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must first complete pre-registration on the official GNDU recruitment portal (eservices.gndu.ac.in/recruitment) by selecting the advertisement number and post name. After receiving the Registration ID via email, login to fill the registration form, lock it, apply for the post, pay application fee through online mode (Debit/Credit Card/Net Banking), and finally submit the hard copy with documents to the Registrar, GNDU Amritsar by June 04, 2026.

Q7. What is the age limit for GNDU Security Guard posts?

The upper age limit for GNDU Security Guard posts is 52 years. Candidates must be Ex-Servicemen from Defence Forces, CAPF, or Police with Matric qualification including Punjabi. Age relaxation is available as per Punjab Government rules and category-wise provisions.

Q8. Is Punjabi language mandatory for GNDU Non-Teaching posts?

Yes, Punjabi language up to Matric standard is essential for most GNDU Non-Teaching posts including Estimator, Physiotherapist, Watch & Ward Supervisor, Clerk-cum-Junior Data Entry Operator, and Security Guard. This is a mandatory eligibility criterion that candidates must fulfill to be considered for selection.
