DSSSB Recruitment 2026 - Application Fee

The application fee is ₹100 (One Hundred only) . Women candidates and candidates belonging to SC, ST, PwBD, and Ex-Serviceman categories are fully exempted from paying the fee. Ex-Servicemen who have already secured regular civil employment under the Central Government or Government of NCT of Delhi after availing ESM reservation benefits are not eligible for fee exemption. Payment must be made only through SBI ePay . No other payment mode will be accepted, and fees paid will not be refunded under any circumstances.

DSSSB Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

Selection will be made through a Computer Based Written Examination under one of three examination schemes depending on the post applied for. One Tier General (I-T-G) — Post Codes 34/26, 39/26, 40/26: Duration: 2 hours | 200 MCQs | 200 marks. Five sections of 40 marks each: General Awareness, General Intelligence & Reasoning Ability, Arithmetical & Numerical Ability, Hindi Language & Comprehension, and English Language & Comprehension. Final merit is based on written examination marks only. One Tier Technical (I-T-T) — Post Codes 21/26 to 31/26, 32/26, 33/26, 35/26 to 38/26, 45/26: Duration: 2 hours | 200 MCQs | 200 marks. Section A: 100 marks covering General Awareness, Reasoning, Arithmetic, Hindi, and English (20 marks each). Section B: 100 marks on the domain subject for the post concerned. DSSSB Recruitment 2026 – 1979 Vacancies for TGT, JSA, IT : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1The application process for DSSSB Recruitment 2026 is entirely online. No applications sent by post, by hand, or by email will be accepted.
  • 2Visit the official DSSSB registration and application portal at https://dsssbonline.nic.in.
  • 3If you are a new user, complete the one-time registration process. Note down your User ID and password — the same credentials are to be used for all future DSSSB examinations; do not register multiple times.
  • 4Log in using your registered credentials and select the relevant post code from Advertisement No. 03/2026.
  • 5Fill in all fields of the online application form accurately. Once submitted, no change or correction — including change of category — will be permitted under any circumstances.
  • 6Upload a recent, clear colour photograph (plain white or off-white background, full face, front view, eyes open) and your signature. Blurred or illegible photographs/signatures will result in rejection.
  • 7Pay the application fee of ₹100 through SBI ePay (exempted categories need not pay).
  • 8Submit the form and take a printout for your own records. Keep the registration number and application number safely.
  • 9The application portal will be live from 16 June 2026 (12:00 Noon) until 15 July 2026 (11:59 PM), after which the link will be disabled.

DSSSB Recruitment 2026 – 1979 Vacancies for TGT, JSA, IT : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is DSSSB Advertisement No. 03/2026 and how many vacancies are there?
A
DSSSB Advertisement No. 03/2026 (dated 29/05/2026) invites online applications for 1,979 vacancies across 25 post codes in various departments of the Government of NCT of Delhi.
Q2When does the online application window open and close?
A
Online applications open on 16 June 2026 (12:00 noon) and close on 15 July 2026 (11:59 PM). The eligibility cut‑off date is 15 July 2026.
Q3Where do I apply and download the official notification PDF?
A
Apply online at https://dsssbonline.nic.in. Notification PDF and details are on https://dsssb.delhi.gov.in (official notification: https://dsssb.delhi.gov.in/sites/default/files/DSSSB/circulars-orders/final_advt-03-2026_1.pdf).
Q4Which departments and major posts are covered?
A
Departments include Forensic Science Laboratory, Directorate of Education, NDMC, Information Technology Department, Department of Delhi Archives, and Labour Department. Major posts: TGT (Computer Science) — 675, Special Educator (Primary) — 450, IT Assistant Grade-A — 125, JSA (Cyber Forensic) — 81, plus many technical and teaching roles.
Q5What is the application fee and who is exempt?
A
Fee: ₹100 (paid via SBI e-Pay). Exemptions: Women, SC, ST, PwBD, and Ex-Servicemen. Fee is non‑refundable.
Q6What are the educational and skill requirements?
A
Qualifications vary by post: from Matric/ITI/NCVT (technical posts) to Graduation/Diploma and Master’s degrees (scientific/teaching posts). Several posts require typing/key‑depression speed (e.g., 8,000 key depressions/hour) or B.Ed/CTET/RCI credentials for teaching posts. Check post‑wise requirements in the notification.
Q7What is the age limit and are there relaxations?
A
Age limits vary by post (examples: JSA posts 18–27 yrs; most teaching posts up to 30 yrs; some technical posts up to 32–37 yrs). Age relaxations apply as per Central Government rules (SC/ST +5, OBC-Delhi +3, PwBD +10, Ex‑Servicemen service period +3, and others as specified).
Q8What is the exam pattern and marking scheme?
A
Single-stage Computer Based Test (CBT) of 200 marks, 2 hours. Exam types: One Tier General, One Tier Technical, or One Tier Technical/Teaching depending on the post. Negative marking: 0.25 marks per wrong answer. Minimum qualifying marks: General/EWS 40%, OBC‑Delhi 35%, SC/ST/PwBD 30%.
Q9How will reservation and OBC-Delhi rules work?
A
Reservation for UR/OBC (Delhi)/SC/ST/EWS/PwBD/ESM/MSP as per GNCTD rules. Only OBC candidates with valid OBC (Non‑Creamy Layer) certificates issued for Delhi (on/after 01/04/2026 and not later than 15/07/2026) are eligible for OBC‑Delhi reservation. EWS / income certificates must follow notification timelines.
Q10What are the key application instructions and documents to keep ready?
A
Register once on dsssbonline.nic.in, then apply for relevant post code(s). Only one application per post code; multiple apps for same code may lead to rejection. Pay fee via SBI e‑Pay if applicable. Keep scanned recent passport photo, signature, educational certificates, caste/PwBD/EWS/ESM certificates, and experience documents ready for later e‑dossier upload. No corrections allowed after submission; save the application printout.
