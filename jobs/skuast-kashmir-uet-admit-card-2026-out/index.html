SKUAST Kashmir UET Admit Card 2026 Out

Sher-e-Kashmir University of Agricultural Sciences and Technology of Kashmir (SKUAST-K) has released the UET Admit Card 2026 for admission to undergraduate programmes. Candidates who applied for the University Entrance Test can now download the hall ticket from the official portal using their login credentials.

The admit card is required for entry into the exam centre, and candidates should verify all details carefully before the examination date.

How to Download SKUAST Kashmir UET Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official website skuastkashmir.ac.in or the applicant login portal.
  • Open the UET Admit Card 2026 link.
  • Enter your Applicant ID / User ID and Date of Birth.
  • Submit the details.
  • Your SKUAST Kashmir UET Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download it and take a clear printout.

If the portal is slow, try again after some time.

Details Mentioned on the Admit Card

The admit card generally contains:

  • Candidate’s name.
  • Applicant ID / login ID.
  • Date of birth.
  • Course or programme name.
  • Exam date and time.
  • Exam centre name and address.
  • Photograph and signature.
  • Important exam instructions.

Check every detail carefully. If there is any mistake, contact the examination authority immediately.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the SKUAST Kashmir UET Admit Card 2026.
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card, Driving Licence or Passport.
  • Recent passport-size photographs, if mentioned in the instructions.

Without the admit card and valid ID, entry may be denied.

Exam-Day Instructions

  • Reach the exam centre well before reporting time.
  • Carry only permitted items.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators or any electronic devices.
  • Follow all instructions given by invigilators.
  • Keep the admit card safe after the exam for future use.
