FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the ICAR IISWC Recruitment 2026?
The last date to submit the application for the ICAR IISWC Recruitment 2026 is 20th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the specified address before this date.
Q2. How many vacancies are available for Assistant and Lower Division Clerk posts?
There are a total of 16 vacancies announced for the Assistant and Lower Division Clerk positions. The breakdown includes 7 for Assistant and 9 for Lower Division Clerk.
Q3. What is the application mode for this recruitment?
The application mode for the ICAR IISWC Recruitment 2026 is offline. Candidates need to download the form and send it via post.
Q4. What is the salary structure for the selected candidates?
Selected candidates for the Assistant post will receive Pay Level-6, and for the Lower Division Clerk post, they will receive Pay Level-2 in the pay matrix of the 7th CPC.
Q5. What are the eligibility criteria for the Assistant post?
Candidates applying for the Assistant post must be holding analogous posts on a substantive basis, working in ICAR Headquarters/ Institutes/ Project Directorates/ ATARIs, and have a minimum of 5 years of regular service.
Q6. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q7. What is the selection process for these posts?
The selection process is based on Inter-Institutional Transfer as per ICAR guidelines dated 23.08.2023.
Q8. Where is the ICAR IISWC located?
The Indian Institute of Soil & Water Conservation (ICAR IISWC) is located in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, with the pincode 248195.
Q9. Can candidates from outside ICAR apply for these posts?
The recruitment is specifically for inter-institutional transfer, implying that candidates currently working within ICAR are eligible.
Q10. What documents are required to be attached with the application form?
Candidates need to attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and relevant certificates as specified in the official notification.
