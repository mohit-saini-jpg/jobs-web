Selection Process

  • Selection will be made through interview or through a Recruitment Test followed by interview
  • depending on UPSC's decision. In case of a large number of applications
  • UPSC may shortlist candidates based on higher qualifications or other suitable criteria.
  • Stage 1 - Shortlisting
  • Applications will be scrutinized for eligibility
  • and UPSC may apply shortlisting criteria where the number of applicants is high. Candidates should mention all higher qualifications and achievements in the online application.
  • Stage 2 - Recruitment Test / Interview
  • Wherever applicable
  • candidates will have to qualify in both the Recruitment Test and Interview. Interview marks are generally assessed out of 100
  • with minimum suitability standards decided by UPSC.
  • Stage 3 - Medical Examination
  • Selected candidates must be medically fit and satisfy the medical authority as prescribed by the Government.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only through the UPSC Online Recruitment Application portal at https://upsconline.nic.in/ora/. The application process includes registration, completion of the Common Application Form, and filling the vacancy-specific module during the application window.
  • Documents to be Uploaded at the time of Online Application:
  • Live photograph captured during CAF filling.
  • Scanned recent passport-size photograph.
  • Scanned signature as per UPSC instructions.
  • Matriculation/10th certificate as proof of date of birth.
  • Degree certificate / marksheets for educational qualification proof.
  • Caste / community / EWS / OBC NCL certificate, if applicable.
  • PwBD certificate, if applicable.
  • Any other supporting document for claims made in the application.
  • Applicants should retain the printout of the finally submitted online application for future reference. No hard copy is required to be sent to UPSC by post.
  • Apply Online at: https://upsconline.nic.in/ora/
  • For assistance, candidates may use the UPSC helpline and official website during the application period.
