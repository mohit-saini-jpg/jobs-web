What Is JEECUP 2026 Round 1 Seat Allotment?

This section explains what the Round 1 seat allotment result means and how JEECUP decides which candidates get which seats.

JEECUP Round 1 seat allotment is the first phase of seat allocation under UP Polytechnic Counselling 2026, in which JEECUP matches registered candidates to available seats in polytechnic institutes across Uttar Pradesh. Seat allotment is based on the candidate's JEECUP rank, the choices (institutes and courses) they filled during counselling, their category, and the actual availability of seats. The result is available only online, and there is no offline or physical allotment slip issued separately.

How to Check JEECUP 2026 Round 1 Seat Allotment Result

This section explains the exact steps candidates need to follow to access and download their seat allotment result online.

Candidates who completed their choice filling can follow these steps to check their JEECUP Round 1 seat allotment result:

  • Visit the official website, jeecup.admissions.nic.in.
  • Click on the "Round 1 Seat Allotment Result" link on the homepage.
  • Enter your application number and password (or date of birth, as applicable).
  • Fill in the captcha code and click "Submit."
  • Your allotted institution name and course will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of the seat allotment letter for further use.

Details Mentioned in the JEECUP Seat Allotment Letter

This section covers what information appears on the downloaded allotment letter so candidates can verify it is error-free.

Once downloaded, candidates should carefully check their allotment letter for any discrepancies. It typically includes:

  • Candidate's name
  • Application number
  • Category
  • JEECUP rank
  • Allotted college
  • Allotted branch/course
  • Counselling round
  • Date and instructions for admission

If any detail appears incorrect, candidates should raise the issue with JEECUP through the official help center before proceeding further.

What to Do After JEECUP Seat Allotment 2026

This section explains the steps candidates must complete after receiving a seat allotment to secure their admission.

After the Round 1 result is declared, a candidate who accepts the allotted seat must follow this sequence:

  • Accept the allotted seat through the online counselling portal.
  • Pay the seat acceptance fee via the portal.
  • Report to the designated help center for document verification.
  • Pay the remaining admission fee through the login portal within the specified deadline.

Candidates who are not satisfied with their Round 1 allotment can also wait for or opt into subsequent rounds, depending on the choices and options available under JEECUP counselling rules.

Documents Required for JEECUP Seat Allotment Verification

This section lists the documents candidates must carry for document verification after seat acceptance.

Candidates should carry both original documents and photocopies for verification at the help center:

  • JEECUP 2026 rank card
  • JEECUP admit card
  • Class 10 marksheet and certificate
  • Class 12 marksheet (if applicable)
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile certificate
  • Income certificate (if required)
  • Aadhaar card or any other photo ID proof
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Seat allotment letter
  • Fee payment receipt
