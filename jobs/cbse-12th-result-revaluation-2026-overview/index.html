CBSE Class 12 Result 2026 is announced, the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) opens a short, online window for rechecking services so that students can get their evaluated answer sheets reviewed in a transparent way.

Through CBSE 12th Result Revaluation 2026, candidates can first obtain a scanned copy of their answer sheet, then request verification of marks, and finally apply for revaluation of selected questions where they suspect marking errors.

All applications are accepted only in online mode, within strictly defined deadlines, and only through official CBSE websites such as cbse.gov.in and its dedicated post-result service portal.

Eligibility for CBSE 12th Result Revaluation 2026

All students who appeared in the CBSE Class 12 Board Exams 2026 and have received their results are eligible to apply for revaluation‑related services. Candidates must submit applications individually through the official portal; schools or third parties cannot file combined or offline requests. Private, compartment, and improvement candidates are generally covered, but they should carefully read the respective circular to confirm specific conditions or separate schedules.

Fees for CBSE 12th Revaluation 2026

  • CBSE charges separate fees for photocopy, verification, and revaluation, and these are normally paid online and are non‑refundable. Photocopy and verification fees are usually calculated per subject, while revaluation fees are calculated per question.
  • The exact fee amounts for 2026 are notified by CBSE in its official circular and can change from previous years, so students must confirm the latest rates before payment.

How to Apply Online for CBSE 12th Result Revaluation 2026

  • Visit official CBSE website and open Post Result Services / Revaluation link.
  • Login with roll number / school number / admit card ID.
  • Select required service (photocopy / verification / revaluation).
  • Choose subject(s) and, for revaluation, specific question numbers.
  • Pay fee online (card / net banking / UPI).
  • Submit form and download confirmation receipt.
  • Track application status on the same portal.

Important Rules and Instructions for Students

There are several rules that students must keep in mind before applying for CBSE 12th revaluation.

  • Applications are accepted only in online mode through the official CBSE systems; emails, letters, or manual requests are not entertained.
  • The fees paid for photocopy, verification, and revaluation are generally non‑refundable, regardless of whether marks change.
  • After revaluation, the new markswhether higher or lowerare final, and no further review or challenge is allowed.
  • Students are advised to apply only where there is a genuine doubt, ideally after consulting teachers, because applying for unnecessary subjects increases cost without any guarantee of change.
