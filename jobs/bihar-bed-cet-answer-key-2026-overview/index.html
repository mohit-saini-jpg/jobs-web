How to Download Bihar B.Ed Answer Key 2026

Steps to Download Bihar B.Ed CET Answer Key 2026

  • Visit biharcetbed-brabu.in
  • Click on the "Answer Key / OMR Response Sheet" link on the homepage
  • Log in using your Application Number and Password / Date of Birth
  • Select your booklet series (Set A, B, C, or D)
  • The answer key PDF will appear on screen – download and save it
  • Cross-check your answers and note your estimated score
  • Take a printout, especially if you plan to raise an objection

How to Calculate Your Expected Score Using the Answer Key?

Formula box / callout: Estimated Score = Total Correct Answers × 1

Example: If you answered 95 questions correctly and 25 incorrectly → Estimated Score = 95 Marks
