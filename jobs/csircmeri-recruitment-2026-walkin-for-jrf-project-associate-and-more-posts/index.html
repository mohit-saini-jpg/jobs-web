CSIR-CMERI Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Project Associate-II: Bachelor's degree in Engineering or Technology in Mechanical Engineering/ Agricultural Engineering/ Mechatronics with 2 years' experience in R&D, OR Masters/Integrated Masters in Engineering or Technology in Mechanical Engineering/ Agricultural Engineering/ Mechatronics. Desirable: Knowledge of CAD software, mechanical fabrication techniques, and design/development of agricultural machinery. Project Associate-I: Bachelor's degree in Engineering or Technology in Mechanical Engineering/ Agricultural Engineering/ Mechatronics. Desirable: Knowledge of CAD software, mechanical fabrication techniques, and design/development of agricultural machinery. Project JRF: Graduate/Post Graduate Degree in Engineering or Technology in Mechanical Engineering/ Agriculture Engineering/ Mechatronics, selected through National Eligibility Tests (CSIR-UGC NET, GATE) or National level exam
