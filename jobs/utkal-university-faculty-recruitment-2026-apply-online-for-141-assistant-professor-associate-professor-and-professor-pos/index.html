How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online through the HIMS Portal (https://hims.odisha.gov.in) which will be available from 19.05.2026. Candidates must complete the application in all respects and upload all supporting documents on or before 20.06.2026. Candidates applying for more than one post must apply online separately for each post, paying application fee for each application. The online application can be filled in multiple sessions. No other format of application or CV will be accepted by email or any other means.

  • In addition, a hard copy of the downloaded and submitted application along with all relevant documents must be sent only via Speed Post/Registered Post to: The Registrar, Utkal University, Vani Vihar, Bhubaneswar, District-Khordha, Odisha–751004 on or before 29.06.2026. Courier/ordinary post/hand delivery will not be accepted. The envelope must be superscribed "APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF [PROFESSOR/ASSOCIATE PROFESSOR/ASSISTANT PROFESSOR]" along with Advertisement No. and Date.

  • Documents to be Submitted (Hard Copy along with Online Application):

  • Self-attested copies of all documents as per the application form, placed with serial numbers matching the form

  • Matriculation/SSC certificate or equivalent (Date of Birth proof – only this document accepted)

  • Master's Degree certificate and marksheets; Ph.D. award letter

  • Experience certificates with evidence of research/teaching as required

  • All publications with supporting documents (self-attested); no claim without supporting evidence will be counted

  • Valid caste certificate (SC/ST/SEBC) issued by competent authority in prescribed Odisha Government format

  • Disability Certificate issued by competent authority in the format prescribed by Government of Odisha (for PwBD candidates)

  • NOC from employer / copy of application forwarded through proper channel (for Govt./PSU/Autonomous Body employees)

  • Photograph in JPEG format (less than 200kb); documents in PDF/image format (clearly readable)

  • Apply Online at: https://hims.odisha.gov.in

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026 is a recruitment drive by Utkal University, Vani Vihar, Bhubaneswar to fill 141 regular faculty positions including 39 Professor, 51 Associate Professor, and 51 Assistant Professor posts across 23-27 departments under three separate notifications dated 18 May 2026.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

A total of 141 vacancies are available: 39 Professor posts (UR-18, ST-9, SC-8, SEBC-4), 51 Associate Professor posts (UR-18, ST-17, SC-9, SEBC-7), and 51 Assistant Professor posts (UR-27, ST-10, SC-4, SEBC-10) with horizontal reservation for Women and PwBD candidates.

Q3. What is the last date to apply for Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

The last date for online application submission is 20 June 2026 (midnight), with hard copy submission deadline of 29 June 2026 via Speed Post/Registered Post to The Registrar, Utkal University, Vani Vihar, Bhubaneswar.

Q4. What is the eligibility for Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

Eligibility follows UGC Notification 18th July 2018: Assistant Professor requires Master's degree with 55% marks plus NET/SET/Ph.D.; Associate Professor requires Ph.D. with 8 years experience and 75 research score; Professor requires Ph.D. with 10 years experience, 10 publications, and 120 research score.

Q5. What is the age limit for Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

Candidates above 60 years of age are not eligible to apply. There is no lower age limit specified, but candidates must fulfill the minimum qualification and experience requirements as per UGC norms.

Q6. What is the selection process for Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

The selection process includes application screening/scrutiny as per UGC Notification 18th July 2018 followed by Interview/Selection Committee assessment with mandatory PPT presentation covering biodata, research, teaching methodology, AI in pedagogy, and vision for nation building.

Q7. Is there any application fee for Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?

Yes, application fee is ₹2,000 for General/SEBC candidates and ₹1,000 for SC/ST/PwD/Women candidates, payable online through HIMS portal (non-refundable).

Q8. What is the salary for Utkal University Faculty posts 2026?

Professor posts offer Pay Level-14, Associate Professor posts offer Pay Level-13A as per 7th CPC, and Assistant Professor posts offer salary as per UGC norms with applicable allowances and perquisites.
