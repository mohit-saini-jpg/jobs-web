Selection Process

  • Publications Assessment (prior to 14-06-2019) UGC Cancelled list
  • Publications Assessment (14-06-2019 to 26-06-2025) Scopus/Web of Science/ UGC Care
  • Publications Assessment (from 27-06-2025) Scopus/Web of Science only
  • The selection process consists of application screening/scrutiny followed by an Interview/Selection Committee as per UGC Notification 18th July 2018
  • supported by the Act and Statute of the University. The list of eligible and ineligible candidates will be displayed on the University website. Candidates will be judged holistically on cognitive
  • psychomotor
  • and affective domains. Candidates called for interview must come prepared with a PPT covering brief biodata
  • subject domain strength
  • practical skills
  • student empowerment strategy
  • use of AI in pedagogy
  • funded research projects
  • consultancy research
  • potential for national/international collaborations
  • and vision for the University and nation building.
  • Tie-Breaking Rules
  • Research Score Augmentation for Papers (as per Thomson Reuters Impact Factor): Papers in refereed journals without impact factor – 5 Points; Impact factor <1 – 10 Points; between 1 and 2 – 15 Points; between 2 and 5 – 20 Points; between 5 and 10 – 25 Points; >10 – 30 Points. Two authors: 70% of total value each; more than two authors: 70% for First/Principal/Corresponding author and 30% for each joint author.

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online through the HIMS Portal (https://hims.odisha.gov.in) which will be available from 19.05.2026. Candidates must complete the application in all respects and upload all supporting documents on or before 20.06.2026. Candidates applying for more than one post must apply online separately for each post, paying application fee for each application. The online application can be filled in multiple sessions. No other format of application or CV will be accepted by email or any other means.
  • In addition, a hard copy of the downloaded and submitted application along with all relevant documents must be sent only via Speed Post/Registered Post to: The Registrar, Utkal University, Vani Vihar, Bhubaneswar, District-Khordha, Odisha–751004 on or before 29.06.2026. Courier/ordinary post/hand delivery will not be accepted. The envelope must be superscribed "APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF [PROFESSOR/ASSOCIATE PROFESSOR/ASSISTANT PROFESSOR]" along with Advertisement No. and Date.
  • Documents to be Submitted (Hard Copy along with Online Application):
  • Self-attested copies of all documents as per the application form, placed with serial numbers matching the form
  • Matriculation/SSC certificate or equivalent (Date of Birth proof – only this document accepted)
  • Master's Degree certificate and marksheets; Ph.D. award letter
  • Experience certificates with evidence of research/teaching as required
  • All publications with supporting documents (self-attested); no claim without supporting evidence will be counted
  • Valid caste certificate (SC/ST/SEBC) issued by competent authority in prescribed Odisha Government format
  • Disability Certificate issued by competent authority in the format prescribed by Government of Odisha (for PwBD candidates)
  • NOC from employer / copy of application forwarded through proper channel (for Govt./PSU/Autonomous Body employees)
  • Photograph in JPEG format (less than 200kb); documents in PDF/image format (clearly readable)
  • Apply Online at: https://hims.odisha.gov.in

Important Instructions

  • Applications are open to eligible Indian Citizens and Overseas Citizens of India (OCIs) as per UGC eligibility criteria. Candidates above 60 years of age are not eligible to apply. Candidates applying for more than one post must apply separately for each post and pay the applicable fee for each. Application fee is non-refundable; applications without fee will be summarily rejected. No hard copy will be considered if the applicant fails to submit the online application by the closing date. The period spent acquiring M.Phil. or Ph.D. degree shall not be considered as research experience. Publications in predatory/dubious journals or presentations in predatory/dubious conferences will not be considered at any point in the selection process. Any entry/claim made in the application without supporting documents will not be counted towards the score claimed for the position applied. Only the Matriculation/SSC certificate issued by the concerned educational board will be accepted for date of birth verification. Reserved category candidates (SC/ST/SEBC) must be domiciles/permanent residents of Odisha for availing reservation benefits. Candidates should produce all self-attested documents at the time of interview; only the HIMS portal downloaded application form is acceptable for hardcopy. No TA/DA shall be paid to candidates for attending the interview. Canvassing in any form will be subject to disqualification of candidature. The University reserves the right not to fill up any or some of the advertised vacancies without assigning any reason, and to revise/reschedule/cancel/suspend the recruitment process at any time. The decision of the University shall be final and binding. Any change or amendment in this advertisement will be displayed on the University website only; candidates are advised to visit https://utkaluniversity.ac.in regularly and remain updated till the last date. In case of any inadvertent mistake detected at any stage even after the issue of an appointment letter, the University reserves the right to modify/withdraw/cancel any communication made to the candidate(s). Any other relaxation and reservation applicable but not mentioned shall be as per Govt. of Odisha, as notified from time to time.
