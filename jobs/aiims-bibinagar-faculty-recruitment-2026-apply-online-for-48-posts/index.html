AIIMS Bibinagar Eligibility Criteria 2026

Professor: Medical: MBBS + PG qualification (MD/MS/DM/MCh) + 11-14 years teaching/research experience; Non-medical: Master's + PhD + 14 years experience Additional Professor: Medical: MBBS + PG qualification (MD/MS/DM/MCh) + 7-10 years teaching/research experience; Non-medical: Master's + PhD + 10 years experience Associate Professor: Medical: MBBS + PG qualification (MD/MS/DM/MCh) + 3-6 years teaching/research experience; Non-medical: Master's + PhD + 6 years experience Assistant Professor: Medical: MBBS + PG qualification (MD/MS/DM/MCh) + 0-3 years teaching experience (depending on super-speciality course duration); Non-medical: Master's + PhD + 6 years experience
