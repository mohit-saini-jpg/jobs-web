FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the total number of vacancies for Non-Academic Junior Resident (Medical) posts?
A
There are a total of 12 vacancies announced for the Non-Academic Junior Resident (Medical) positions at AIIMS Gorakhpur.
Q2Q2. What is the eligibility criteria for this recruitment?
A
Candidates must possess an MBBS degree from an MCI recognized institute and have completed their compulsory rotatory internship. An internship completion certificate is mandatory.
Q3Q3. What is the age limit for the Non-Academic Junior Resident (Medical) post?
A
The maximum age limit for this post is 37 years. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per government norms.
Q4Q4. What is the salary offered for the Non-Academic Junior Resident (Medical) post?
A
The selected candidates will receive a salary in Pay Matrix (Level-10) with a minimum of Rs. 56,100/- per month, plus NPA for medical personnel as per the 7th CPC recommendations.
Q5Q5. How can I apply for the Non-Academic Junior Resident (Medical) position?
A
The application process is through a Walk-in Interview. Candidates need to report at the specified venue on the interview date with all required documents.
Q6Q6. What is the date of the Walk-in Interview?
A
The Walk-in Interview for the Non-Academic Junior Resident (Medical) posts is scheduled for 08 June 2026.
Q7Q7. What documents should I carry for the Walk-in Interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents, one set of self-attested photocopies, ID proof, educational certificates, internship completion certificate, and category certificate (if applicable).
Q8Q8. What is the application fee for the recruitment?
A
The application fee is Rs. 500 for General/OBC candidates and Rs. 250 for SC/ST candidates. PwBD candidates are exempted from the fee.
Q9Q9. Where is the venue for the Walk-in Interview?
A
The official notification contains the detailed venue address. Candidates are advised to check the notification for the exact location.
Q10Q10. Is this a permanent or ad-hoc position?
A
This is an Ad-hoc job type position.
