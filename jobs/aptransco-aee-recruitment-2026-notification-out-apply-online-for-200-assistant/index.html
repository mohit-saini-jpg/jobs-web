Bond Conditions and Document Deposit

Candidates selected through APTRANSCO AEE Recruitment 2026 must be aware of the bond obligations before applying. Every appointed candidate must execute a Service Bond on Non-Judicial Stamp Paper worth ₹110 at the time of joining. The total bond period is 7 years - comprising 2 years of probation plus 5 years of service. During the 7-year bond period, original certificates (degree, date of birth, community) must be deposited with APTRANSCO and will not be returned. If an employee needs their certificates for any bonafide purpose during this period, they must deposit ₹1,00,000 as security before the certificates are temporarily released. Appointed candidates are required to work in field postings (Meter & Relay Testing, Line Maintenance, Sub-station Maintenance, or Construction Divisions) - preferably in rural or remote areas - for a minimum of 5 years from the date of appointment .
