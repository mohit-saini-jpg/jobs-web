What Is the OTET Answer Key 2026?

This section explains what the OTET Answer Key actually is and why it matters to candidates before the final result is declared.

The OTET Answer Key 2026 is an official document released by BSE Odisha that lists the correct responses to every question asked in the Odisha Teacher Eligibility Test (OTET) 2026. It is published in two stages:

  • Provisional Answer Key - released first, open to candidate objections.
  • Final Answer Key - released after reviewing all valid objections; used to calculate the official OTET result.

The exam is conducted for two separate papers based on the teaching level a candidate is applying for:

How to Download the Odisha TET Answer Key 2026?

This section provides a step-by-step process candidates can follow once the answer key link goes live.

  • Visit the official website: www.bseodisha.ac.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the Latest Updates / Notifications section.
  • Click the link labelled "OTET Answer Key 2026 - Provisional."
  • Select your Paper (I or II) and the correct Set code (A/B/C/D).
  • The answer key PDF will open or download automatically.
  • Save the file and cross-check your marked responses against the official answers.

How to Raise Objections Against the OTET Answer Key 2026?

This section explains the exact process for submitting a challenge, since objections sent through unofficial channels are not accepted.

Objections can only be submitted online, within the official window; submissions via email or post after the deadline will not be entertained. Here's how to raise an objection:

  • Go to www.bseodisha.ac.in.
  • Click the link titled "OTET Answer Key 2026 Objection/Challenge" from the homepage or Notifications section.
  • Log in using your Registration/Roll Number and Mobile Number (or other required credentials).
  • Select the paper and the question number(s) you want to challenge.
  • Upload supporting evidence, such as standard textbook references, to justify your claim.
  • Pay the objection fee of ₹500 per question through the online payment gateway.
  • Submit the form and save the acknowledgement receipt for future reference.

Once the window closes, BSE Odisha's Question Analysis Committee reviews every valid objection before finalizing the answer key.

OTET Answer Key 2026 Objection Fee

This section covers how much candidates need to pay per question and whether that amount is refundable.

Candidates must pay a fee of ₹500 per question they wish to challenge. This applies equally to Paper-I and Paper-II objections, and objections submitted without payment will not be considered.
