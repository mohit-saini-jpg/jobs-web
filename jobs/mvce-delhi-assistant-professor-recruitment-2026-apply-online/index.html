Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Assistant Professor post at MVCE Delhi?

The last date to submit your online application for the Assistant Professor position at Maharshi Valmiki College of Education Delhi is 22nd June 2026. Ensure your application is submitted well before this deadline.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for the Assistant Professor post?

There are a total of 6 vacancies for the Assistant Professor post in Education at Maharshi Valmiki College of Education Delhi. These positions are open for various categories.

Q3. What is the minimum educational qualification required for the Assistant Professor role?

The minimum educational qualification includes a postgraduate degree with at least 55% marks in the relevant discipline and a postgraduate degree in Education (M.Ed./M.A. Education) with 55% marks. NET qualification is also mandatory.

Q4. What is the salary structure for the Assistant Professor position?

Selected candidates will be appointed at Academic Pay Level 10 of the 7th Central Pay Commission Pay Matrix, which offers a competitive salary and benefits as per government norms.

Q5. What is the application fee for the Assistant Professor post?

The application fee varies by category. UR candidates need to pay Rs. 1500/-, OBC/EWS and women applicants pay Rs. 1000/-, and SC/ST candidates also pay Rs. 1000/-. PwBD candidates are exempt from the fee.

Q6. What is the selection process for the Assistant Professor recruitment?

The selection process involves a screening of applications based on academic credentials, followed by an assessment through a presentation, and finally an interview.

Q7. Can candidates from outside Delhi apply for this post?

The notification is for Maharshi Valmiki College of Education Delhi. While the official notification does not explicitly restrict applications based on location, candidates should ensure they meet all eligibility criteria. Relocation might be required if selected.

Q8. Is there any age relaxation for reserved categories?

Specific details regarding age relaxation for reserved categories are not provided in the summary. Candidates are advised to refer to the official notification PDF for comprehensive information on age limits and relaxations.
