How to Apply

  1. 1Applications must be submitted online only through the Rajasthan SSO Portal (sso.rajasthan.gov.in). No offline applications are accepted under any circumstances. The online portal is active from 25-05-2026 to 23-06-2026 (11:59 PM).
  2. 2Steps to Apply Online:
  3. 3Step 1: Visit SSO Portal - Go to sso.rajasthan.gov.in and log in with your SSO ID
  4. 4Step 2: Complete OTR (if not registered) - Complete One Time Registration (OTR) - fill category, disability status, and domicile details correctly
  5. 5Step 3: Pay OTR Fee - Pay the OTR registration fee (Rs. 600 for General/Creamy OBC/MBC, Rs. 400 for Non-Creamy OBC/MBC/EWS/SC/ST/PwBD) through e-Mitra, CSC, Net Banking, ATM Debit Card, or Credit Card
  6. 6Step 4: Access Recruitment Portal - After OTR, log in to SSO and go to Citizen Apps (G2C) → Recruitment Portal
  7. 7Step 5: Click Apply Now - Click Apply Now for Advertisement No. 07/2026
  8. 8Step 6: Fill Application Form - Fill in personal details, educational qualifications, and department preference (Secondary/Sanskrit Education) with priority order
  9. 9Step 7: Upload Documents - Upload required documents: qualification certificates, category certificate, domicile, disability certificate (if applicable), photo, signature, thumb impression
  10. 10Step 8: Verify & Submit - Verify all entries carefully before clicking Final Submit
  11. 11Step 9: Save Application - Download and save the application with the generated Application ID for future reference
  12. 12Documents Required for Upload:
  13. 13Apply Online at: https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in/

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1Q1. What is the total vacancy in RSSB Computer Instructor Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 3951 posts - 322 Senior Computer Instructor and 3629 Basic Computer Instructor - across Secondary Education and Sanskrit Education Departments in both Non-Scheduled and Scheduled areas of Rajasthan.
Q2Q2. What is the last date to apply for RSSB Computer Instructor 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 23 June 2026 up to 11:59 PM. Candidates must apply through the SSO Recruitment Portal only, as no offline applications are accepted. A correction window of 3 days will also be available after the deadline with a fee of ₹300.
Q3Q3. What is the age limit for RSSB Computer Instructor Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 18 to 40 years of age as on 01 January 2027 for General male applicants. Age relaxation is provided for reserved categories including SC, ST, OBC, EWS, females, PwBD, and ex-servicemen as per Rajasthan government rules. Additional relaxation of 3 years is applicable for Secondary Education posts.
Q4Q4. What are the qualifications required for RSSB Computer Instructor 2026?
A
For Senior Computer Instructor, candidates need a postgraduate degree such as ME, M.Tech, MCA, or M.Sc. in Computer Science/IT or related fields. For Basic Computer Instructor, candidates must have a graduation degree with computer qualifications like BCA, B.Tech, B.Sc. (CS/IT), or PGDCA/'A' Level. Knowledge of Hindi in Devanagari script and Rajasthani culture is mandatory for both posts.
Q5Q5. Is there negative marking in RSSB Computer Instructor Exam 2026?
A
Yes, there is a negative marking of 1/3 mark for each incorrect answer in the exam. Each question has five options (A to E), and candidates must mark option E if they choose to skip a question. Leaving more than 10% questions unanswered without marking any option will lead to additional penalties.
Q6Q6. What is the application fee for RSSB Computer Instructor Recruitment 2026?
A
The application fee is ₹600 for General and Creamy Layer OBC/MBC candidates. For Non-Creamy Layer OBC/MBC, EWS, SC, ST, and PwBD candidates of Rajasthan, the fee is ₹400. Candidates from other states are treated as General category and must pay ₹600.
Q7Q7. What is the salary of RSSB Computer Instructor 2026?
A
Senior Computer Instructors are paid under Pay Level-10 with a salary range of ₹56,100 to ₹1,77,500. Basic Computer Instructors fall under Pay Level-8 with a salary range of ₹47,600 to ₹1,51,100. During the probation period of 2 years, a fixed remuneration is provided, after which full salary and allowances are applicable.
Q8Q8. Can candidates from other states apply for RSSB Computer Instructor 2026?
A
Yes, candidates from outside Rajasthan can apply for this recruitment. However, they will be considered under the General category regardless of their caste. They are not eligible for reservation benefits and must pay the full application fee.
Q9Q9. Can I edit my RSSB Computer Instructor application form after submission?
A
Yes, RSSB provides a correction window for 3 days after the application deadline with a fee of ₹300. Additionally, a second correction window of 7 days is available after the exam. However, critical details like name, date of birth, photo, and signature cannot be modified.
Q10Q10. When will the RSSB Computer Instructor Admit Card 2026 be released?
A
The admit card release date has not been officially announced yet by RSSB. It will be made available on the official website rssb.rajasthan.gov.in before the examination. Candidates are advised to regularly check the website for the latest updates and notifications.
