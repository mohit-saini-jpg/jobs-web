AP EAMCET 2026 Official Answer Key Release

The official AP EAMCET 2026 answer key, shift‑wise question papers and response sheets for all exam dates, including May 13, will be released on the AP EAPCET official website after the completion of the exam schedule. The authorities will first publish the preliminary answer key, along with a notification specifying the objection window.

Candidates can log in, download the question paper and answer key for their date and shift, calculate their probable marks, and raise objections against any answer they believe is incorrect. After evaluating all objections, the final answer key is released, and the result is prepared based on this final version.

How to Download AP EAMCET 2026 Question Paper, Answer Key and Response Sheet

How to Download AP EAMCET 2026 Question Paper

  • Visit the official AP EAPCET website: cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/Eapcet/.
  • Navigate to the “Question Papers” or “Master Question Papers” section for AP EAMCET 2026 Engineering.
  • Select the exam date “May 13, 2026” and choose Shift 1 or Shift 2 as applicable.
  • Download the question paper PDF for review and practice.

How to Download AP EAMCET 2026 Answer Key

  • Go to the AP EAPCET official portal.
  • Click on the link for “Exam Papers and Preliminary Key” or “Answer Key” for AP EAMCET 2026.
  • Choose the Engineering stream and select the May 13, 2026 exam date and shift.
  • Download the answer key PDF and use it to check the correct options.

How to Download AP EAMCET 2026 Response Sheet

  • On the same website, open the “Response Sheet” link for AP EAMCET 2026.
  • Enter your registration number, hall ticket number and date of birth, as mentioned in the instructions.
  • View and download your personal response sheet and compare it with the answer key to calculate your estimated score.

AP EAMCET 2026 Exam Day Guidelines for May 13

Candidates appearing for the May 13 exam must follow all exam‑day instructions specified in the information bulletin and on the hall ticket. Reaching the centre well before the reporting time is essential to ensure smooth completion of verification processes.

Key guidelines include:

  • Carry a printed copy of your AP EAMCET 2026 hall ticket, a valid photo ID proof (such as Aadhaar card, voter ID, passport or driving licence), and other documents mentioned for your category.
  • Do not bring prohibited items such as mobile phones, smartwatches, calculators, electronic gadgets, books, notes, or bags into the exam hall.
  • Biometric verification, including fingerprint and photograph, may be conducted before you enter the exam room.
  • The exam will be conducted in CBT mode, and you should familiarise yourself with the on‑screen navigation, timer and question palette.
  • Follow any dress code instructions such as avoiding heavy jewellery, caps, jackets or outfits with too many pockets, and opting for simple, comfortable clothing and footwear.

AP EAMCET 2026 Eligibility (Quick Reminder)

AP EAMCET 2026 eligibility is primarily based on educational qualification, age criteria and subject combination. For the Engineering stream, candidates are usually required to have passed or be appearing in Class 12 (Intermediate) with Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry from a recognised board.

There are also course‑specific requirements and conditions, such as minimum qualifying marks and age limits, which are detailed in the official AP EAPCET notification. Aspirants from outside Andhra Pradesh and Telangana can also apply, subject to specific rules on local/non‑local status and reservation benefits.
