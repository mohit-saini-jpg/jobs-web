The registration process for JEE (Advanced) 2026 opened on April 23, 2026 (10:00 AM IST) for candidates who qualified JEE Main 2026. The last date for registration has been extended till May 5, 2026 (11:59 PM IST). The last date for fee payment is May 4, 2026 (11:59 PM IST). The examination is scheduled to be held on May 17, 2026.

JEE Advanced 2026 is the second stage of the Joint Entrance Examination conducted for admission to undergraduate engineering programmes at the 23 Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs). Only candidates who are among the top 2,50,000 successful candidates (including all categories) in JEE Main 2026 (Paper 1) are eligible to appear. The exam consists of two compulsory papers (Paper 1 and Paper 2).

How to Apply Online for JEE Advanced 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Visit the official JEE Advanced website.
  • Register using your JEE Main 2026 roll number, password, and security pin.
  • Fill in personal details such as name, parents’ names, contact details, and address.
  • Select up to eight preferred exam cities.
  • Upload scanned copies of passport-size photograph and signature.
  • Verify all details, pay the application fee, and submit the form.

Documents Required for JEE Advanced 2026

  • Class 10 certificate (for date of birth)
  • Class 12 marksheet/certificate
  • Category certificates (GEN-EWS, OBC-NCL, SC, ST, PwD) – issued on or after April 1, 2026
  • Passport-size photograph and signature
  • JEE Main 2026 admit card/rank proof (for verification)
