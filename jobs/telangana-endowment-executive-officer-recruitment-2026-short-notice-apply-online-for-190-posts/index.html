Short Information

Government of Telangana (Endowments Department) has officially released a recruitment notification for the creation of 190 Executive Officer posts (Grade-I, Grade-II, and Grade-III) under the administrative control of the Revenue Department. This is a great opportunity for candidates looking for government jobs in Telangana. As per G.O.Ms.No.42 dated 30 April 2026, the posts have been sanctioned and will be filled through Direct Recruitment. Detailed district-wise vacancies are provided in the annexure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is Telangana Endowment Recruitment 2026?

Telangana Endowment Recruitment 2026 is a government job notification released by the Endowments Department for 190 Executive Officer posts (Grade I, II, and III). These positions will be filled through a direct recruitment process under the Revenue Department.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in Telangana Endowment Recruitment 2026?

A total of 190 vacancies have been sanctioned for Executive Officer posts. These include 41 posts for Grade-I, 62 posts for Grade-II, and 87 posts for Grade-III across various zones and districts in Telangana.

Q3. What are the post names in Telangana Endowment Recruitment 2026?

The recruitment includes three categories of posts: Executive Officer Grade-I, Executive Officer Grade-II, and Executive Officer Grade-III. These posts are distributed across different temples and endowment institutions in Telangana.
