Qualification / Eligibility

Education Qualification12th-pass, Diploma, Any Graduate
DetailsPublic Health Nurse (PHN): All India Senior School Certificate Examination (10+2) or equivalent. Diploma in General Nursing and Midwifery (3 1/2 years). One year Diploma in Public Health Nursing or Basic B.Sc. (Nursing) degree (4 year) from a recognized university or board and registered with the Nursing Council of India. TBHV-NTEP: Graduate in science OR Intermediate (10+2) in science and experience of working as MPW/LHV/ANM/Health worker/Certificate OR Higher course in Health Education/Counselling OR Tuberculosis health visitor's recognized course. Certificate course in computer operations (minimum two months). Preferential: Training course for MPW or recognized sanitary inspector course.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the DHS Nicobars recruitment?

The last date for the receipt of applications for the DHS Nicobars recruitment is 13th June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this date.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for Public Health Nurse (PHN) and TBHV-NTEP posts?

There are a total of 5 vacancies. 3 posts are for Public Health Nurse (PHN) and 2 posts are for TBHV-NTEP.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Public Health Nurse (PHN) post?

For the PHN post, candidates need to have completed 10+2 or equivalent, a Diploma in General Nursing and Midwifery (3 1/2 years), and a one-year Diploma in Public Health Nursing or a Basic B.Sc. (Nursing) degree (4 year).

Q4. What is the salary for the TBHV-NTEP post?

The salary for the TBHV-NTEP post is Rs. 18,434/- per month.

Q5. What is the application mode for this recruitment?

The application mode for this recruitment is offline. Candidates need to download the application form and submit it via post.

Q6. Where should the application form be sent?

The application form should be addressed to the Chairperson (DC), District Health Society, Nicobar District – 744301, with the name of the post clearly mentioned.

Q7. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

No application fee is mentioned in the official notification for this recruitment drive.

Q8. What is the selection process for these posts?

The selection process will be based on the merit of the candidate's qualifications and experience. A merit list will be prepared and published.
