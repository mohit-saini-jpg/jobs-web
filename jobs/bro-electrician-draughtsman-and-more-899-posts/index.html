How to Apply

  • 1Recent colour passport-size photograph (not older than 1 month from the date of Advertisement) pasted at the prescribed place on application form and admit card - minimum 12 copies of the same photograph to be carried throughout the recruitment process
  • 2Left hand thumb impression at the prescribed place on application form and admit card
  • 3Signature of candidate at the prescribed place
  • 4Copy of e-receipt of application fee paid via SBI Collect
  • 5Self-attested photostat copies of certificates for essential educational/technical qualifications (Matriculation onwards), date of birth, caste certificate (as per format in Appendix A/B), OBC NCL declaration (Appendix D), EWS certificate (Appendix C), Disability Certificate (Appendix E for PwBD), experience certificate (if applicable), Driving Licence copy (for OEM post)
  • 6NCC certificate copies (B or C grade), sports certificate, or relationship certificate for Sons of Serving/Ex-GREF personnel - for claiming weightage marks
  • 7Proof of residence/domicile (for correct physical standards region)
  • 8Any certificate in language other than Hindi or English must be enclosed with an attested Hindi/English translation by a Group A Gazetted Officer
  • 9Only Male Indian Nationals can apply; Female candidates are not eligible.
  • 10Application form and/or admit card without signature, without photograph, or without thumb impression will be summarily rejected - no communication will be done for rejected applications.
  • 11Candidates must ensure their Aadhaar Card photo is current (not older than 2-3 years from written exam date) and address/phone is updated; outdated Aadhaar may prevent entry to the exam.
  • 12Son of Serving / Ex-GREF staff should write father's GS No., Rank, and Name on top of the application in red ink with a photocopy of relationship certificate, to claim the 15% weightage benefit.
  • 13Casual Paid Labours (CPL) of GREF who have worked for 179 days in the last one year and are on the Muster Roll on the date of advertisement publication get 10% additional weightage - attach experience certificate signed by OC unit, countersigned by Commander BRTF and verified by Accounts Officer.
  • 14SC/ST/OBC/EWS candidates who qualify on their own merit (without relaxed standards) will be accommodated against UR vacancies - they must NOT apply separately against UR vacancies.
  • 15OBC candidates belonging to Creamy Layer are not entitled to reservation; they should apply under UR category.
  • 16Candidates serving in other Government Departments must apply through proper channel sufficiently in advance; applications not received by due date through proper channel will be rejected.
  • 17Mobile phones, smartwatches, and all electronic/communication devices are strictly NOT ALLOWED inside GREF Centre, Pune campus during any recruitment process.
  • 18A candidate who absents himself during any recruitment activity for which he has been called will have his candidature cancelled automatically - he will not be allowed to appear for further stages.
  • 19There shall be no provision for re-evaluation/re-checking of scores in examination; no correspondence in this regard shall be entertained.
  • 20Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this Advertisement shall fall within the jurisdiction of the Hon'ble High Court of Delhi.
