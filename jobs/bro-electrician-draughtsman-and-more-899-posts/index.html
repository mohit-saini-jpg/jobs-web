BRO GREF Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Offline for 899 Electrician, Draughtsman and More Posts

General Reserve Engineer Force (GREF), Border Roads Organisation

Posts: Various Last Date: 19 Jul 2026 (Assam, Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh, Mizoram, Manipur, Nagaland, Tripura, Sikkim, Ladakh Division of J&K, Lahaul & Spiti, Pangi Sub-Division of Chamba, Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Lakshadweep) Offline only - by postal service to Commandant, GREF Centre, Dighi Camp, Pune-411015

Short Information

BRO Recruitment 2026 notification is out to fill 899 new vacancies for Electrician, Draughtsman and More Posts. Candidates can apply Offline only from 21.05.2026 to 04.07.2026 through BRO website bro.gov.in.

Age Details01 Jun 2026

Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationAny Graduate, ITI, 12TH, 10TH

Salary / Pay Scale

Pay ScalePay Band-1, Rs 5200-20200 plus Grade Pay Rs 2800/- (Draughtsman) or Rs 1900/- (all other posts); DA, HRA, Transport Allowance, Hard & Risk Allowance as per Govt of India rules

Selection Process

  • Written Examination + Physical Efficiency Test (PET) + Trade Test (TT) + Primary Medical Examination (PME) + Document Verification (DV)

How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply offline only; no online application mode is available. Application form and admit card must be downloaded from the official BRO website (www.bro.gov.in) and filled in English or Hindi only, in capital letters. The application form must be printed on A4 size 75 GSM bond paper (one side only) and admit card printed on both sides of a single paper. Candidates must first pay the application fee online via SBI Collect portal and attach the e-receipt copy. Candidates applying for more than one post must send separate applications with separate fee in separate envelopes. Candidates are strongly advised to send applications well before the closing date and not wait for the last day - GREF Centre will not be responsible for postal delays.
