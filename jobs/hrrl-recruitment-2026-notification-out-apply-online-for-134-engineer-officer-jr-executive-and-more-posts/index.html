Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is HPCL Rajasthan Refinery Limited (HRRL) Recruitment 2026?

HPCL Rajasthan Refinery Limited (HRRL) Recruitment 2026 is a recruitment drive under Advertisement No. HRRL/RECT/01/2026 to fill 129 regular posts and 5 Fixed Term Employment (FTE) positions in Engineering, Finance, Medical, Information Systems, and Fire & Safety for its 9 MMTPA Greenfield Refinery‑cum‑Petrochemical Complex at Pachpadra, Balotra, Rajasthan.

Q2. How many vacancies are released under HRRL Recruitment 2026 and for which posts?

HRRL has notified 129 regular vacancies in salary grades E0 to E5 for posts such as Jr. Executive, Assistant Engineer/Officer, Engineer/Officer, Senior Engineer, Senior Manager, Medical Officer, Asst. Accounts Officer, Accounts Officer, Asst. Officer (IS), and Jr. Executive (Fire & Safety), along with 5 high‑level FTE positions like Plant Heads and Extruder Head in polymer and cracker units.

Q3. What are the important dates for HRRL Recruitment 2026?

The online application process for HRRL Recruitment 2026 starts on 14 May 2026 (from 10:00 hrs) and the last date to apply online, including fee payment, is 03 June 2026 up to 23:45 hrs, while the cut‑off date for age, qualification, and experience eligibility is 13 May 2026.

Q4. What is the eligibility and age limit for Jr. Executive (Chemical, Mechanical, Fire & Safety) under HRRL 2026?

For Jr. Executive (E0) posts, candidates must have a 3‑year full‑time Diploma in the relevant engineering discipline or a 3‑year full‑time B.Sc. with prescribed subjects with at least 60% marks for UR/OBC‑NCL/EWS and 50% for SC/ST/PwBD, and the maximum age limit is 25 years with applicable relaxations for reserved categories.

Q5. Are B.Tech/BE or higher professional qualification holders eligible for E0 (Jr. Executive) posts in HRRL?

No, candidates possessing higher professional qualifications such as BE/B.Tech, MBA, MCA, CA, M.Sc, Ph.D, etc. are not eligible to apply for E0 level Jr. Executive posts in Chemical, Mechanical, and Fire & Safety under HRRL Recruitment 2026, as clearly mentioned in the notification.

Q6. What is the application fee for HRRL Recruitment 2026?

For HRRL Recruitment 2026, UR, OBC‑NCL, and EWS candidates must pay a non‑refundable application fee of Rs. 1,000 plus 18% GST (total Rs. 1,180) along with applicable payment gateway charges, while SC, ST, and PwBD candidates are fully exempted from paying the application fee.

Q7. What is the selection process for HRRL E0, E1, and E2 posts?

For E0 posts, the selection process comprises a Computer Based Test (CBT), Skill Test, and Personal Interview, all elimination stages with specified minimum qualifying marks, whereas for E1/E2 posts the process includes CBT, Group Task/Group Discussion, and Personal Interview, and the final merit is prepared based on aggregate marks with category‑wise qualifying percentages.

Q8. What is the selection process for Senior Engineer/Senior Manager (E3/E5) and FTE Plant Head positions?

For E3 and E5 regular posts, candidates are shortlisted and evaluated through a Technical Competency Interview and Behavioural Competency Interview with minimum qualifying marks for UR/EWS and reserved categories, while FTE Plant Head and Extruder Head positions are filled purely on the basis of a Personal Interview assessing technical and behavioural competencies.
