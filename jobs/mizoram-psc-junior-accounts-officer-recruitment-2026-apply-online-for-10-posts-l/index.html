Job Overview

Mizoram Public Service Commission (MPSC) has published the Junior Accounts Officer (JAO) Notification 2026 on 22 April 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 10 Junior Accounts Officer (JAO) positions. Candidates within the age range of 18 to 35 years (as on 01 January 2026, with relaxation for reserved categories) and holding a Bachelor’s degree from a recognised University along with required computer and Mizo language proficiency are eligible to apply online at Mizoram PSC Official Website.
