How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only. No other mode of application will be accepted. Candidates must first mandatorily register themselves on the Government Apprenticeship portals - NATS (https://nats.education.gov.in) and/or NAPS (https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in).
  • After completing registration on NAPS/NATS, eligible candidates will receive an email from [email protected] inviting them to fill the final "Application cum Examination Form" and make payment of the applicable fee/charges.
  • The application registration is considered complete only when fee/charges are deposited with BFSI SSC. Candidates must note down their Enrolment Id (NATS portal) and Apprentice Registration Code (NAPS portal) for all future correspondence.
  • Documents Required for Registration on NATS/NAPS Portals:
  • Colour scan copy of Aadhaar Card – Front & Back Page (JPEG, <1 MB)
  • Colour scan copy of PAN Card (JPEG, <1 MB)
  • Valid Personal Email ID and Mobile Number
  • Colour scan copy of Passport-size Photograph (JPEG, <1 MB)
  • Colour scan copy of Xth Marksheet (PDF, <1 MB)
  • Colour scan copy of XIIth Marksheet (PDF, <1 MB)
  • Colour scan copy of Degree Marksheet / Provisional Certificate (PDF, <1 MB)
  • Colour scan copy of Saving Bank Passbook / Cheque Leaf (PDF, <1 MB)
  • Colour scan copy of Signature (JPEG, <1 MB)
  • Documents to be Produced at the time of Document Verification / Language Proficiency Test:
  • Printout of valid Language Proficiency Test Intimation Letter/mail from Bank
  • Valid system generated printout of the online application / Registration / Enrollment form
  • Proof of Date of Birth (Birth Certificate from Competent Municipal Authority or SSLC / Std. X Certificate with DOB)
  • Photo Identity Proof (Government-issued ID)
  • Individual Semester/Year-wise Marksheets and Certificates for educational qualifications including final degree/diploma certificate
  • CGPA to Percentage conversion certificate (if grades awarded), issued by the appropriate authority
  • Caste Certificate in prescribed Government of India format (for SC/ST/OBC/EWS candidates) - OBC-NCL certificate must be issued within one year as on date of advertisement
  • Disability Certificate in prescribed format issued by District Medical Board (for PwBD candidates)
  • Any other relevant documents in support of eligibility
  • Documents to be produced at the venue of Document Verification / Language Test. Non-submission of requisite documents will debar candidature from further participation in the selection process. Candidates must appear at their own expense.
  • Apply Online at: https://bankofbaroda.bank.in/ and https://bfsissc.com

Important Instructions

  • This is NOT regular employment or contract employment in the Bank. Apprentices will be TRAINEES only and not employees of Bank of Baroda. The apprentice shall have no right to claim employment in the Bank after completion of training. Bank of Baroda has no obligation to offer regular employment to apprentices during or after the apprenticeship period. Candidates can apply for engagement as apprentice in one State/UT only. Allotment to a different State/UT will not be considered under any circumstances. Candidates must first register on NATS and/or NAPS portals before applying; application without portal registration is not valid. The process of registration is complete only when applicable fee/charges are deposited with BFSI SSC. No Ex-servicemen / Disabled Ex-Servicemen shall be engaged as an apprentice; there is no reservation for Ex-servicemen in this engagement. OBC candidates belonging to the 'Creamy Layer' are not entitled to OBC reservation and age relaxation; they should apply as General / General (OC/HI/VI/ID) as applicable. Change of category once submitted in the online application will not be permitted at any stage. Candidates should submit only one application; in case of multiple submissions, only the last valid completed application will be considered and fee paid for earlier registrations will be forfeited. The Apprentices are prohibited from joining / forming any Union Body. All correspondence will be made only on the registered email ID; candidates should keep it active throughout the recruitment process. No hard copy intimation will be sent by post for Online Examination. Candidates must appear for the selection process (Document Verification, Language Test) at their own cost; Bank will not reimburse travelling expenses. Apprentices will not be provided with Transport / Hostel facility or any other residential accommodation during training. Stipend of Rs. 15,000/- per month will be paid on completion of each month after adjusting Loss of Pay, if any. No other allowances/benefits are payable. All amendments/corrigendum (if any) will be published only on the Bank's website (https://bankofbaroda.bank.in/) and https://bfsissc.com - candidates should visit regularly. Any canvassing directly or indirectly shall disqualify the candidature. Use of unfair means, impersonation, or misbehavior will lead to disqualification and/or debarment. Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be Mumbai only.
