MSBTE Summer 2026 Result: Background

MSBTE declared the Summer 2026 Diploma Result on 18 June 2026 for students in even semesters (2nd, 4th, and 6th) across engineering, pharmacy, and other diploma programmes. The theory examinations were held from 23 April to 16 May 2026, while the practical examinations were conducted from 8 to 18 April 2026. Students who felt their subject-wise marks did not reflect their preparation became eligible to apply for a photocopy of their assessed answer sheet, and subsequently for re-evaluation, through the official MSBTE student portal.

Why the "Verification" Option Is Disabled This Year

For Summer 2026, MSBTE checked the relevant subjects using the Digital Evaluation method. Because this system calculates the total marks automatically, the standard marks "Verification" facility - where only the total is rechecked - has been switched off for these subjects. Students who want their answer sheets reassessed must instead follow the two-step route: first apply for a photocopy of the assessed answer sheet, and then, if a genuine error is found, apply separately for re-evaluation. Subjects that were evaluated manually rather than digitally may still have the standard Verification facility available; students should confirm with their institute which method applies to their subjects.

Who Can Apply for MSBTE Re-Evaluation 2026

  • Any student who appeared for the MSBTE Summer 2026 diploma examination and is not satisfied with their subject-wise marks.
  • Students must first obtain a photocopy of the assessed answer sheet before they are allowed to apply for re-evaluation.
  • Applicable mainly to subjects checked under the Digital Evaluation method; subjects evaluated manually may follow a separate Verification route.

How to Apply for Photocopy and Re-Evaluation: Step-by-Step

  • Visit the official MSBTE website at msbte.ac.in.
  • Go to the "Examination" tab and select "Digital Evaluation."
  • Click on "Photocopy/RE – Student Login."
  • Log in using your Enrolment Number and Summer 2026 Seat Number.
  • Register your active mobile number and email ID - your OTP and the digital photocopy PDF will be sent only to this registered email.
  • Select up to a maximum of two subjects for which you want a photocopy.
  • Submit the application; a fee receipt will be generated.
  • Take a printout of the fee receipt, pay the fee at your institute, and submit the receipt to your college coordinator.
  • Once you receive the photocopy review it carefully - ideally with your subject teacher - for any marking errors.
  • If you find discrepancies, apply separately for re-evaluation through the same student login within the re-evaluation window (3–5 July 2026), and pay the reassessment fee at your institute.

Documents/Details Required

  • MSBTE Enrolment Number
  • Summer 2026 Seat Number
  • Active, personally accessible mobile number (for OTP)
  • Active email ID (photocopy PDF is sent only here)
  • Printed fee receipt (generated after online submission, paid at the institute)
  • Original Summer 2026 marksheet - some institutes may require this in a sealed envelope before confirming a reassessment application; confirm this requirement with your college

How to Check and Download MSBTE Re-Evaluation Result 2026

  • Go to the official result portal at result.msbte.ac.in.
  • Look for the link labelled "Photocopy/Re-evaluation Result" or "Reassessment Result" for Summer 2026.
  • Enter your Enrolment Number or Seat Number as prompted.
  • Submit to view your revised subject-wise marks.
  • Download and save the result for your records. The physical revised marksheet is typically issued later through your institute.

Important Instructions for Students

  • Apply for re-evaluation only if you are genuinely confident there was a marking error - once declared, re-evaluation marks are treated as final, even if they turn out lower than your original marks.
  • If your marks change after receiving the photocopy but before you apply for reassessment, contact your institute and regional office first to get the correction reflected.
  • MSBTE does not accept direct online fee payment for this process - fees must be paid at your own institute.
  • Only your registered email will receive the OTP and the digital photocopy PDF, so ensure it is active and correctly entered.
  • A maximum of two subjects can be selected per seat number for a photocopy.
