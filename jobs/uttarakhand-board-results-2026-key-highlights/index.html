How to Check UK Board Result 2026 Online

Students can easily check their UK Board Results 2026 for Class 10th and 12th through the official UBSE result portals using simple login details. Follow the steps below to access and download your online marksheet.

  • Visit any official UK Board result website: uaresults.nic.in, ubse.uk.gov.in or ubse.co.in/result/.
  • On the homepage, click on the link that reads "UK Board High School Result 2026" or "UK Board Intermediate Result 2026" as per your class.
  • You will be redirected to the UBSE result login page for Class 10th or 12th.
  • Enter your board exam roll number exactly as printed on the admit card and fill the captcha/security code shown on the screen.
  • Click on the "Get Result" or "Submit" button to proceed.
  • Your UK Board Result 2026 for Class 10th or 12th will be displayed on the screen with subject-wise marks and overall result status.
  • Download the online marksheet, save it as a PDF and take a printout for future admission and documentation purposes.
