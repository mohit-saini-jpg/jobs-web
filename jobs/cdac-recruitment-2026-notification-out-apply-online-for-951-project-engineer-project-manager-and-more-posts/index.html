Age Limit

Details

Upper age limits vary by position level and are calculated as on the last date of application, i.e., 20 June 2026. SC/ST/OBC/PwBD/Ex-Servicemen and Government employees are eligible for age relaxation as per Government of India norms.

Qualification / Eligibility

Nationality

Citizen of India (standard Government of India norms apply)

Education Qualification

BE/B.Tech or equivalent degree (min 60% or equivalent CGPA for most posts; 50% for Project Associate at some centres) OR PG in Science/Computer Application/relevant domain with requisite percentage OR ME/M.Tech OR Ph.D. in relevant discipline – from AICTE/UGC-approved institution or recognized university. For support/admin posts: Graduation with 50% + relevant experience.

Experience

Post-qualification work experience as specified against each post. Academic/teaching/research/internship/project placements as part of curriculum are NOT counted as experience unless specifically mentioned. Experience must be supported by certificates from employer clearly showing period, salary, and post held.

Details

All candidates must hold qualifications from AICTE/UGC-approved colleges or recognized universities. Candidates in their final semester may apply but can join only after clearing the examination with the required minimum marks.

Physical Eligibility

Details

The C-DAC JIT June 2026 Recruitment is a large-scale opportunity with openings across 11 centres and hundreds of positions spanning fresher to senior levels. Competition is expected to be moderate to high for roles in high-demand domains such as AI/ML, Full Stack Development, Cyber Security, and Software Development, particularly at the Project Engineer and Senior Project Engineer levels, which attract the largest pools of applicants from tier-2 and tier-3 engineering colleges. Vacancies for niche domains like Quantum Computing, VLSI, Additive Manufacturing, Robotics, and HPC System Software will see comparatively lower competition but require highly specialised credentials. The absence of any application fee significantly increases the applicant volume. | Effective competition will be filtered through the initial academic screening stage, where CGPA, percentage, and domain relevance determine shortlisting. Candidates who have internships, publications, or project work in their declared domain may gain an advantage even if such experience is not counted formally. For experienced positions, those with documented employer-issued experience certificates closely matching the domain will clear the first cut. Candidates applying to Noida (e-Governance, Health Informatics) and Pune (high volume, 200+ positions) will face steeper competition due to location preference and breadth of domain coverage.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only through the C-DAC careers portal (https://careers.cdac.in/). No offline/hard copy applications are accepted. A valid mobile number and email ID are mandatory as OTP-based login is used and all communications are sent by email only.

  • Documents Required to be Scanned and Uploaded:

  • Passport-size photograph in .jpg format (not more than 400 KB)

  • Resume / CV in PDF format (not more than 500 KB)

Important Instructions

  • These are CONTRACTUAL positions - no right to regular or permanent employment in C-DAC is created by engagement under this recruitment. Candidates may be deputed or transferred from one project to another at any C-DAC project location across India based on organizational requirement. The contract is initially for up to 3 years (1 year for Kolkata centre) or project completion, whichever is earlier. Extension is possible based on performance and project requirement. All correspondence including interview call letters will be sent by email only - candidates must keep their registered email ID and mobile number active throughout the process. C-DAC reserves the right to increase/decrease the number of posts, change eligibility criteria, cancel any or all positions, or modify/cancel the selection process at any stage without notice. If selected candidates are found ineligible at any stage - even after joining - their engagement is liable to be terminated without notice. No TA/DA is payable for attending the written test or interview at any centre. C-DAC has not authorized any agent or agency to represent it for recruitment matters - beware of fraudulent offers. All updates, corrigenda, and extensions will be published only on the official website www.cdac.in. Canvassing in any form will result in disqualification.
