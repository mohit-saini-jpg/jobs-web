How to Download Pondicherry University Time Table 2026

Students can download the Pondicherry University Time Table 2026 from the official examination portal by following the steps given below:

  • Visit the official Pondicherry University examination website.
  • Navigate to the Time Table or Exam Schedule section.
  • Look for the links related to PG Arts & Science or UG NEP V & VI Semester January 2026.
  • Click on your respective course timetable PDF link.
  • The file will open. Download and save it on your device.
  • Take a printout for ready reference during exam preparation.

Note: Always refer to the official Pondicherry University timetable and check all dates and timings carefully before the examinations.
