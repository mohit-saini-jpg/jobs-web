What is UBTER JEEP? : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 13/06/2026

The UBTER JEEP Result 2026 is now officially out. The Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education (UBTER), Roorkee, has released the Joint Entrance Examination Polytechnics (JEEP) 2026 result on the official portal - ubterjeep.co.in. Candidates who appeared in the Uttarakhand polytechnic entrance exam can now download their rank card by entering their roll number on the result page. The rank card serves as the primary document for participating in the UBTER JEEP counselling 2026 process, which determines admission to diploma programmes across polytechnic colleges in Uttarakhand.

Direct Result Link: ubterjeep.co.in/Result/Jeeprank.aspx

What is UBTER JEEP?

Before diving into the result details, here is a quick overview of the exam for first-time visitors. UBTER JEEP (Joint Entrance Examination Polytechnics) is a state-level entrance examination conducted by the Institute of Research, Development and Training (IRDT) Dehradun under the Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education (UBTER) Roorkee. It is the gateway for students seeking admission to diploma programmes in Engineering, Pharmacy, Textile Design, Garment Technology, Fashion Design, and other technical courses at polytechnic colleges across Uttarakhand.

How to Download UBTER JEEP Rank Card 2026

Downloading the UBTER JEEP 2026 rank card is a straightforward process. Follow the step-by-step instructions below to access your result quickly and save it for future use during counselling and admission.

  • Visit the official UBTER JEEP portal: ubterjeep.co.in
  • On the homepage, look for the "Download Rank Card (Result)" link under the "Important Works" section.
  • Click on the result link - it will open the result entry page.
  • Enter your JEEP 2026 Roll Number in the field provided.
  • Click the "Show" button.
  • Your rank card will appear on the screen displaying your name, rank, and marks.
  • Download and save the rank card as a PDF for future reference.
  • Print at least two copies - one to submit at the institution during admission and one for your personal records.

Important Tip: The rank card is available in both Hindi and English on the portal. If the Hindi version loads by default, look for a language toggle on the download page.

Details Mentioned on UBTER JEEP 2026 Rank Card

The rank card is not merely a score sheet - it is the official document you carry to counselling and ultimately to admission. Knowing what information it contains helps you verify its accuracy immediately after downloading.

The following details are printed on the UBTER JEEP 2026 rank card:

  • Candidate's Name
  • Candidate's Roll Number
  • Registration Number
  • Photograph of the Candidate
  • Candidate's Signature
  • Total Marks Obtained
  • Rank Secured in the Examination
  • Category (General / OBC / SC / ST)
  • Date of Birth

Action Required: If any detail on your rank card is incorrect - such as wrong name, wrong marks, or wrong date of birth - you must raise a discrepancy complaint within 24 hours of the result publication. Contact UBTER / IRDT Dehradun through the contact details listed on ubter.in and submit a written complaint along with your registration number and the specific error.

UBTER JEEP Merit List 2026

The merit list is a critical outcome of the result process. This section explains how the UBTER JEEP merit list is prepared and what role it plays in the admission journey.

The UBTER JEEP 2026 merit list will be released shortly after the result declaration. The merit list is prepared purely on the basis of marks scored in the JEEP 2026 entrance exam - no board exam marks or internal assessment scores are factored in. All qualified candidates are ranked according to their performance, and seat allocation during counselling is done strictly on the basis of this merit list.

Tie-Breaking Criteria for UBTER JEEP 2026

In cases where two or more candidates score identical marks, the following tie-breaking rules are applied in order:

  • Date of Birth - The older candidate is ranked higher.
  • Subject-wise Marks - If the tie persists, marks obtained in Mathematics, Science/Chemistry, and Biology are considered.

UBTER JEEP 2026 Cutoff Marks

The cutoff is the minimum score a candidate must achieve to qualify for UBTER JEEP counselling. Understanding cutoff trends helps candidates assess their chances of admission.

The cutoff marks for UBTER JEEP 2026 vary depending on the course and category. The following factors determine the JEEP cutoff each year:

  • Total number of applications received that year
  • Category of the candidate (General, OBC, SC/ST)
  • Difficulty level of the examination paper
  • Number of seats available for each course

Candidates who do not meet the cutoff for their desired course may consider applying for UBTER JEEP in the following academic year.

What's Next? UBTER JEEP Counselling 2026

After the result is declared, qualified candidates must participate in the counselling process to secure admission. Here is what to expect after downloading your rank card.

All candidates who qualify the minimum cutoff criteria will be eligible for UBTER JEEP Counselling 2026. The counselling process involves online registration, choice filling (selecting preferred colleges and courses), seat allotment based on rank and availability, and document verification followed by fee payment at the allotted institution. The counselling dates for JEEP 2026 will be announced on the official website shortly after the result. Keep checking ubterjeep.co.in regularly for updates.

Documents to keep ready for counselling:

  • UBTER JEEP 2026 Rank Card (printed copy)
  • Class 10 and Class 12 Marksheets and Certificates
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile Certificate (Uttarakhand)
  • Aadhar Card / Government-issued ID proof
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Application form printout

Note: The UBTER JEEP 2026 result is valid for one year from the date of declaration.
