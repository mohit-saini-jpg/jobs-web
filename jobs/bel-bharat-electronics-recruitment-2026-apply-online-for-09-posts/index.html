Salary & Pay Scale

Details
  1. Candidates asking about the pay for Trainee Engineer-I (Mechanical) can note that BEL offers a consolidated monthly remuneration of Rs. 30,000 in the 1st year, Rs. 35,000 in the 2nd year, and Rs. 40,000 in the 3rd year if the contract is extended. In addition, BEL pays a consolidated Rs. 12,000 per year toward insurance premium, attire allowance, stitching charges, and footwear allowance, released in two installments - the first with the first month's salary and the second after completing 6 months of service
  2. Engagement terms include:
  3. Initial engagement period: 2 years, extendable by up to 1 year (maximum tenure of 3 years) based on project requirement and performance. Insurance coverage of Rs. 2 lakh for medical cover and Rs. 5 lakh for life cover must be arranged by the Trainee Engineer at the time of joining, with the policy copy submitted to HR; renewal and premium payment remain the individual's responsibility throughout the tenure
  4. Advancement to Project Engineer-I: After completing 2 years as Trainee Engineer, candidates become eligible for advancement to Project Engineer-I if they score 80% or above (aggregate) in Performance Assessment for both years, maintain attendance above 80% in the preceding year with good general discipline, and have no adverse remarks in the preceding 2 years. Candidates who do not meet these criteria continue as Trainee Engineer-III for one additional year, after which the engagement ends. Once advanced, Project Engineer-I is engaged for 3 years, extendable by up to 1 year based on project requirement and performance
  5. Retention Bonus for Project Engineers: Project Engineers who have advanced from the Trainee Engineer role are entitled to a Retention Bonus of Rs. 25,000 for every completed year, provided they complete an overall tenure of 4 years in the Project Engineer role. This bonus is calculated for the Project Engineer tenure only and is paid as a lump sum of Rs. 1,00,000 at the end of 4 years
