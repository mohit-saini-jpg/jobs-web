Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) has invited online applications for 9 posts of Trainee Engineer-I (Mechanical) at its Chennai Unit through Advertisement No. 4788/002/REC/HR&A/CHN/2026 dated 17 June 2026. Selection Process for Trainee Engineer-I (Mechanical)

Candidates asking how they will be selected can note that BEL shortlists eligible applicants for a written test carrying 100 marks, and final selection follows merit order calculated category-wise and discipline-wise. The minimum qualifying marks differ by category: General, EWS, and OBC-NCL candidates need 35%, while ST and PwBD candidates need 30% to be considered suitable. The question paper carries negative marking of 1/4 mark for every wrong response, so candidates should attempt questions carefully, and the written test will be conducted in Chennai. Separately, the notification mentions that candidates currently working on a permanent or fixed-tenure basis in PSUs or Government organizations must produce a No Objection Certificate "at the time of the interview," which indicates there may be a verification or interview stage for such candidates after the written test.
