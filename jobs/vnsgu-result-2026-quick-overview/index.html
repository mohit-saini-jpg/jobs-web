VNSGU Result 2026 - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 18/07/2026

Veer Narmad South Gujarat University (VNSGU), Surat, has declared the Purak (supplementary) examination results for the June-2026 session. The results cover Third Year Bachelor of Arts (External) in multiple subjects, BBA Fifth Semester, and BCA Fifth Semester (including the NCF-NEP pattern). All results were checked and released on 17 July 2026 through official result notifications issued by the Controller of Examinations.

How to Check VNSGU Result 2026 Online

  • Go to the official VNSGU result page: https://old.vnsgu.ac.in/result_notification.php
  • Locate the relevant result notification number (2421/2026 for BA External, 2433/2026 for BCA, or 2446/2026 for BBA).
  • Open the PDF notification for your course.
  • Check the Successful/Pass Numbers, DLO Numbers, or Second Class Numbers list for your seat/roll number.
  • Cross-check your seat number against the subject-wise pass list if you appeared for the BA External exam.

Important Instructions

  • Results are based on official notifications checked and issued by the Controller of Examinations, VNSGU, Surat.
  • Students marked under "D.L.O." (Division Left Over) or "W.O. 165" should refer to the official notification or contact the university/college for the exact meaning applicable to their result.
