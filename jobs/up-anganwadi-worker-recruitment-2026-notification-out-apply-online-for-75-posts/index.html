Selection Process - Merit-Based Recruitment

The Lalitpur Anganwadi Karyakatri selection process is transparent and entirely merit-based. No written examination or interview is conducted at the initial stage. Merit List Preparation: A merit list will be prepared based on educational qualifications of the applicants. Preference Criteria: Among equally qualified candidates, preference will be given based on domicile status, category (SC/ST), and special conditions (widow, divorcee, etc.). Final Authority: The final decision on recruitment (increase or decrease in vacancies) rests with the Selection Committee . No Walk-in Interviews: Candidates will not be called for in-person selection without prior merit list publication.
