Important Instructions

The following special directions are issued as part of the official notification and must be followed carefully by all applicants for Lalitpur Anganwadi Worker posts.
Only women candidates are eligible. Men cannot apply. Minimum education: Intermediate (Class 12); maximum education considered for merit: Intermediate or equivalent. Minimum age 18 years, maximum 35 years, calculated as on January 1, 2026. Widows must submit a death certificate of the deceased husband, issued by the Gram Panchayat officer or relevant urban body. Only departmental website https://www.upanganwadibharti.in should be used for online applications. No third-party sites are valid. Applicants must fill the online form carefully and correctly before submission. The self-attested copy submitted must be uploaded to the portal. Applications with wrong/missing documents or incorrect details will be rejected without notice. Last date: July 9, 2026, midnight 12:00 AM. Detailed instructions, eligibility criteria, and the application form are available on the UP Anganwadi Bharti official website. FAQs

Q1How many total vacancies are available in Lalitpur Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
There are a total of 75 Anganwadi Worker (Karyakatri) vacancies across 7 Child Development Projects in Lalitpur district — 6 rural projects and 1 urban project.
Q2What is the last date to apply for Lalitpur Anganwadi Bharti 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is July 9, 2026, by midnight (12:00 AM). Applications after this deadline will not be considered.
Q3Who is eligible to apply for Lalitpur Anganwadi posts?
A
Only women candidates who are permanent residents of the respective ward or Gram Sabha are eligible. The minimum age is 18 years and maximum is 35 years (as of January 1, 2026). Minimum educational qualification is Intermediate (Class 12).
Q4How to apply for Lalitpur Anganwadi Worker vacancy 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted online only through the official portal https://www.upanganwadibharti.in. Offline or postal applications are not accepted.
Q5Which project has the most vacancies in Lalitpur Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
The Mandavara Child Development Project has the highest number of vacancies with 16 posts, followed by Talbehat (14 posts) and Jakhaura (12 posts).
Q6Is there any written exam for Lalitpur Anganwadi Karyakatri selection?
A
No written examination is conducted. Selection is merit-based, determined by the candidate's educational qualifications and applicable reservation criteria.
Q7Are residence and caste certificates mandatory for Lalitpur Anganwadi application?
A
Yes. Both residence and caste certificates are mandatory and must be issued by the Tehsil office with an online digital signature to be considered valid.
Q8What happens if a candidate submits incorrect or false documents?
A
Applications with false information, incorrect certificates, or incomplete documents will be summarily rejected and the applicant will be disqualified from the selection process.
