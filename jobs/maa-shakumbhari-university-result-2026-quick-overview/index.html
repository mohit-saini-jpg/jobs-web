Which Courses' Results Have Been Declared?

This section lists every program and semester for which Maa Shakumbhari University has released results as part of this announcement, so students can quickly identify their specific course.

According to the official notification dated 02 July 2026, MSU has declared results for the following postgraduate (II & IV Semester) programs:

  • M.A. - Hindi, History, Political Science, Defence & Strategic Studies, Human Rights & Duties, Community Education & Disability Studies, Sanskrit, English & Cultural Studies, Sociology, Economics, Psychology, Public Administration, Philosophy
  • M.Sc. - Mathematics, Chemistry, Zoology, Botany, Physics, Statistics & Environmental Science
  • M.B.A. (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)
  • LL.M. (Master of Laws)
  • M.Com. (Entrepreneurship & Family Business)
  • M.S.W. (Master of Social Work)

In addition to these postgraduate results, the university also declared results for B.A. VI Semester (Main & Back) Examination - 2026 on the same day.

Why Are Results Released in Batches?

Universities like MSU typically publish results course-wise and semester-wise as evaluation and moderation for each subject group gets completed, rather than all at once. This is why students often see multiple result notifications on the official website spread across different dates, each covering a specific set of programs or semesters.
