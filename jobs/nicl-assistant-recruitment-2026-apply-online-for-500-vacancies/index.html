NICL Assistant Selection Process

Selection consists of two online examinations followed by a qualifying Regional Language Test. There is a penalty for wrong answers: one-fourth of the marks assigned to a question will be deducted for each wrong answer in both Prelim and Main exams. No marks are deducted for unattempted questions. Candidates qualifying the Main Examination will be shortlisted for a Regional Language Test in the language of the State/UT applied for. This is qualifying in nature only, with no marks awarded. The final merit list is prepared State-wise and category-wise, based on Main Examination scores, subject to qualifying the Regional Language Test.
