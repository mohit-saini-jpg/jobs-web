Eligibility Criteria

Candidates applying for any of these three trades must meet the same set of eligibility conditions, which are based on ITI qualification and NAPS portal registration rather than a general degree requirement. All conditions below must be satisfied together. The applicant must possess an ITI certificate in the trade applied for . The applicant must be registered on the NAPS portal in the same trade as the application. The applicant must have secured at least 40% marks in the qualifying examination , i.e., in ITI. A candidate can apply for one trade in either West Bengal or Jharkhand only . If a candidate applies for both, the application will be rejected. Candidates can apply for ANY ONE DISCIPLINE ONLY ; applying for more than one discipline results in summary rejection of the application.

Eastern Coalfields ITI Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection for ECL ITI Apprentice posts is based on merit in the qualifying ITI examination, followed by document verification and a formal engagement process. Here is the step-by-step procedure as stated in the notification. A merit list will be prepared based on marks obtained in the qualifying examination (ITI). If two or more applicants secure the same marks, preference is given to the candidate who passed the qualifying examination earlier (by year of passing). If the year of passing is also the same, date of birth is used as the tie-breaker , with older candidates given seniority. If the date of birth of two candidates is also the same, the candidate who registered earlier on the NAPS portal is considered senior . Reservation applicable for CPSUs is followed throughout.
