Qualification / Eligibility

Chartered Accountant from recognized Institution. Basic Knowledge of computer applications including knowledge of Advance Excel. Minimum experience of 15 years, including at least 3 years in a Senior Managerial Position in the field of finance, accounting, taxation, audit, and financial management, in Banks/Financial Institutions / NBFCs /Housing Finance Companies. Experience in Housing Finance / NBFC sector shall be preferred. Strong exposure to: Financial reporting (Ind AS), Regulatory compliance (NHB/RBI), Treasury & ALM management, Budgeting & financial planning, Taxation (Direct & Indirect – GST). Experience in dealing with: Statutory Auditors / Internal Auditors, Regulatory inspections (NHB/RBI/CAG). Management Trainee/Internship period will not be considered as count of experience.
