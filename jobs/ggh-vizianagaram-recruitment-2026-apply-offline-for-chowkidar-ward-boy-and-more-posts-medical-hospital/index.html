Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the GGH Vizianagaram recruitment?

There are a total of 9 vacancies announced for various contractual posts in the Government Teaching General Hospital, Vizianagaram. These positions are for different roles within the de-addiction center.

Q3. What is the application mode for GGH Vizianagaram recruitment?

The application mode for the GGH Vizianagaram recruitment is offline. Candidates need to download the application form, fill it out, and send it via post to the specified address.

The maximum age limit for all posts in this GGH Vizianagaram recruitment is 42 years. However, age relaxation is provided for certain categories as per government rules.

The starting salary for the lowest post, Yoga Therapist/Dance/Music/Art Teacher, is Rs. 5000/- per month. The salary structure varies for different posts.
