BCECEB ITI CAT Admit Card 2026

Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) has released the ITI CAT Admit Card 2026 for the Industrial Training Institute Competitive Admission Test on its official portal. The ITICAT 2026 admit card download facility is active from 10 May 2026 at 10:30 AM for all registered and eligible candidates.

The Bihar ITI CAT 2026 exam will be conducted on 17 May 2026 for admission to around 33,108 seats in government and private ITIs across the state. Candidates must carry a printed copy of their admit card and valid photo ID to be allowed into the examination hall.

How to Download BCECEB ITI CAT Admit Card 2026

Candidates can download their Bihar ITI CAT hall ticket 2026 by following these steps:

  • Visit the official BCECE Board website: https://bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, under “Online Application Forms” or Notice Board, click on the link “Download Admit Card of ITICAT‑2026”.
  • You will be redirected to the ITICAT online portal (admissions.nic.in/bihar) admit card login page.
  • Under the Registered Candidate Sign‑In section, enter your Registration Number/User ID and Password, or use the Registration Number and Date of Birth option (as given).
  • Enter the Security Pin/Captcha displayed on the screen and click on “Sign In / Submit”.
  • Your Bihar ITI CAT Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen; check all details carefully and download the PDF.
  • Take 2–3 clear printouts of the hall ticket on A4‑size paper and keep them safely for the exam and further processes.

Details Mentioned on BCECEB ITI CAT Admit Card 2026

Candidates must verify the following details printed on their ITICAT 2026 admit card:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Father’s name and other personal details
  • Registration number / roll number
  • Exam name: Industrial Training Institute Competitive Admission Test (ITI CAT/ITICAT‑2026)
  • Exam date: 17 May 2026 and exam time (as per centre instructions)
  • Exam centre name, centre code and full address
  • Category and other eligibility details (if printed)
  • Important instructions for the exam day

If any discrepancy is found (name, date, centre, photo, etc.), candidates should immediately contact BCECEB or use the helpdesk before the exam date.

Documents Required with BCECEB ITI CAT Admit Card 2026

On the day of the Bihar ITI CAT 2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the Bihar ITI CAT Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout, preferably colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card PAN Card Passport Driving Licence Voter ID Other government‑issued ID as accepted by BCECEB.
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Voter ID
  • Other government‑issued ID as accepted by BCECEB.
  • Additional documents, if specified in the instructions (for example, PwD certificate and scribe permission letter for candidates availing such facilities).

Entry to the examination centre will not be permitted without the printed ITICAT hall ticket and a valid photo ID.
