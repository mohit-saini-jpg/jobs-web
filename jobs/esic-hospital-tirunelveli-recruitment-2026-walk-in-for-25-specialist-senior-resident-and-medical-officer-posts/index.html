How To Apply

  • This is a Walk-in Interview. No prior online application is required. Eligible candidates should directly report to the venue on the scheduled date.
  • Documents Required to be Carried:
  • Duly filled Application Form (Annexure-I)
  • Two recent passport size photographs
  • Original + Photocopies of: MBBS / PG Degree / Diploma Certificates Registration Certificate with NMC / TNMC Proof of Date of Birth (SSLC / Matriculation) Caste / Community Certificate (for reserved categories) Experience Certificates No Objection Certificate (if currently employed)
  • MBBS / PG Degree / Diploma Certificates
  • Registration Certificate with NMC / TNMC
  • Proof of Date of Birth (SSLC / Matriculation)
  • Caste / Community Certificate (for reserved categories)
  • Experience Certificates
  • No Objection Certificate (if currently employed)
  • Step-by-Step Process:
  • Download and fill the Application Form (Annexure-I) from the official notification.
  • Carry all original documents and two sets of self-attested photocopies.
  • Report at ESIC Hospital, Vannarpettai, Tirunelveli between 9:00 AM to 10:30 AM on 01-06-2026 for registration.
  • Attend the Interview as per schedule.
  • Selected candidates must join within 07 days after receiving the offer letter.

Important Instructions

  • This is purely contractual engagement. No claim for regular appointment. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the interview. Selected candidates must sign a contract agreement on Rs.100/- stamp paper. Private practice is not allowed. Engagement is initially for 1 year and extendable based on performance. All disputes subject to Tirunelveli jurisdiction.
