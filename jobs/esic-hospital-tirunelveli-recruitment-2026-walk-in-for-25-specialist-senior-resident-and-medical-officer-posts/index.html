  • Original + Photocopies of: MBBS / PG Degree / Diploma Certificates Registration Certificate with NMC / TNMC Proof of Date of Birth (SSLC / Matriculation) Caste / Community Certificate (for reserved categories) Experience Certificates No Objection Certificate (if currently employed)
