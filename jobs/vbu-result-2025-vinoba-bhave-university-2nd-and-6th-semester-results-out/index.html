How to Check VBU Result 2025 Online?

Students can check their VBU result 2025 for 2nd and 6th Semester by following these simple steps:

  • Visit Official Website: Go to vbu.ac.in
  • Find Results Section: Click on "Results" or "Examination" tab on homepage
  • Select Your Exam: Choose 2nd and 6th Semester from the list
  • Enter Details: Input your Roll Number and Registration Number
  • Submit & View: Click "Submit" to view your VBU result 2025
  • Download: Save/print your mark sheet PDF for future reference
