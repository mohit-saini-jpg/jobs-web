Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationBachelor's or Master's degree in Engineering, B.Sc. or Diploma in Engineering with relevant experience.
DetailsAssociate Project Engineer: Bachelor's or Master's degree in Engineering (preferably in Electronics & Communication Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, or related disciplines). Candidates with a Bachelor's degree must have a minimum of 3 years of relevant experience. Strong foundation in probability, linear algebra, optimization techniques, and programming (Python), along with a sound understanding of machine learning methods and their application to analytical and modeling problems. Assistant Project Engineer: Bachelor's in Engineering (preferably in Electronics & Communication Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, or equivalent disciplines). Strong foundation in mathematics, and programming (Python), along with an understanding of machine learning methods and their applications. Experience in full-stack application development (web and mobile), including backend APIs and frontend frameworks. Hands-on experience in model deployment (e.g., REST APIs, containerization) and integration with GIS platforms and smartphone-based applications/sensors. Sr. Project Technician: B.Sc. OR Diploma in Engineering with a minimum of 5 years of relevant experience in system hardware and infrastructure management. Experience in setup, configuration, and maintenance of system hardware, data and compute servers, and storage infrastructure. Familiarity with data management practices, backup systems, and basic data security protocols. Working knowledge of Linux-based systems and networking will be an advantage.
