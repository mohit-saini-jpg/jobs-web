Qualification / Eligibility

Senior Executive (Tech.)-Lead Verifier (Compliance): As per The Bureau of Energy Efficiency (Qualification for Accredited Energy Auditors and Maintenance of the List) Regulations, 2010 with 13 years of experience. Senior Executive (Tech.)- Lead Verifier (Offset): Engineering Graduate (Bachelor of Engineering/Bachelor of Technology) or equivalent (Or) Bachelor of Science or equivalent with 13 years out of which min. 5 years' experience in validation and/or verification activity as team member in CDM or VCM activities.
