FRI Dehradun Recruitment 2026 - Apply Walkin : Job Overview

Forest Research Institute (FRI), Dehradun, under the Indian Council of Forestry Research and Education (ICFRE), has released a walk-in interview notification (Advertisement No. 5-1/RCS/2026 (Advert.)/(July), dated 17.07.2026) for temporary fellowship and project positions. The walk-in interviews will be held on 28 July 2026 and 29 July 2026 at the Board Room, FRI Main Building, P.O. New Forest, Dehradun - 248006. Important Dates for FRI Walk-in Interview 2026

Notification Date: 17 July 2026 Interview Day 1: 28 July 2026 (RTO/RRO, SPF, JPF, SRF posts) Interview Day 2: 29 July 2026 (PA and FA posts) Registration Window: 9:00 AM to 10:15 AM on each respective interview date No separate call letter or interview intimation letter will be issued to candidates. Eligible and interested candidates must report directly on the notified dates and times.

Documents Required for FRI Walk-in Interview

Candidates must carry the following documents at the time of registration: Copy of updated bio-data Recent passport size photograph Self-attested copies of all educational certificates Self-attested copies of all relevant/experience documents For the RTO/ RRO post only: Last Pay Certificate (LPC) and recent Pension Slip, in addition to the above documents Candidates who are already employed must bring a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their current/last institute.

FRI Dehradun Recruitment 2026 - Apply Walkin : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection will be made through a walk-in interview. There is no written exam mentioned in the notification. Candidates need to register between 9:00 AM and 10:15 AM on their respective interview date and submit their documents to the office of the Group Coordinator (Research), P.O. New Forest, FRI, Dehradun, for verification and interview.

FRI Dehradun Recruitment 2026 - Apply Walkin : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Check the post-wise eligibility criteria and select the relevant post and date (28 or 29 July 2026).
  • 2Prepare bio-data, a recent passport-size photograph, and self-attested copies of all educational and experience certificates (plus LPC and Pension Slip for RTO/RRO applicants).

  • 4Complete registration and submit documents to the office of the Group Coordinator (Research).
  • 5Attend the walk-in interview on the same day.
