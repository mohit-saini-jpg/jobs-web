Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the NIT Calicut Professor Emeritus recruitment?

The last date to submit the online application for the Professor Emeritus post at NIT Calicut is 30th June 2026. Ensure your application is submitted before this deadline.

Q2. What is the eligibility criteria for the Professor Emeritus position?

Candidates should be distinguished professors who have retired from NITs, IITs, IISc, or comparable institutions. They should have externally sponsored projects or comparable activities and be willing to undertake teaching and project guidance.

Q3. What is the age limit for this recruitment?

The maximum age limit for candidates applying for the Professor Emeritus position is 70 years. There is no minimum age limit specified.

Q4. What is the salary offered for the Professor Emeritus role?

The Professor Emeritus will receive an honorarium of Rs. 1,20,000/- per month. Additionally, monthly allowances up to Rs. 20,000/- are provided on a reimbursable basis.

Q5. How can I apply for the NIT Calicut Professor Emeritus position?

Interested candidates must apply online through the official recruitment portal of NIT Calicut. The application process involves registration, filling the form, and uploading necessary documents.

Q6. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

The official notification does not mention any specific application fee for the Professor Emeritus recruitment. Candidates are advised to check the official website for any updates regarding fees.

Q7. What is the selection process for the Professor Emeritus role?

The selection process will involve an initial scrutiny of the applications received. Shortlisted candidates will then be called for an interview.

Q8. What is the official website of NIT Calicut?

The official website of the National Institute of Technology Calicut is www.nitc.ac.in. Candidates can visit this site for more information and updates.
