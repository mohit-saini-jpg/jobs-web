How to Check SPPU Result 2026 Online

Follow these steps to check and download your SPPU Summer Session 2026 result:

  • Visit the official SPPU result portal: onlineresults.unipune.ac.in/Result/Dashboard/Default.
  • Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser, as the dashboard requires it to load correctly.
  • On the Dashboard page, select the relevant session -Summer Session 2026 (or Winter Session 2025 for the applicable MBA First Year courses).
  • Locate and click on your specific programme and year from the list, for example "Third Year B.Pharmacy (2019 Pattern)" or "B.E. (2019 Pattern)."
  • Enter your Seat Number as printed on your hall ticket/admit card, along with any other login details requested on screen (such as PRN or Mother's Name, if asked).
  • Click "Submit" or "Go for Result" to view your result on screen.
  • Download and save the provisional marksheet as a PDF, or print it for your records.

Documents Required to Check SPPU Result

  • Hall Ticket / Admit Card (for Seat Number)
  • PRN (Permanent Registration Number), if requested on the login screen
  • Mother's Name or Date of Birth, if requested on the login screen

Note: The exact login fields can vary by programme. Whether every course in this list requires a Mother's Name or PRN in addition to the Seat Number is not confirmed in the official notification; students should enter the exact details requested on their specific result login screen.

Important Instructions for SPPU Result 2026

  • The result displayed on the online portal is a provisional marksheet. The original/final marksheet is issued later through your respective college.
  • Always access your result only through the official domain unipune.ac.in or onlineresults.unipune.ac.in. Do not enter personal or login details on any third-party website claiming to show SPPU results.
  • If your result does not appear, first confirm that you have selected the correct session (Summer Session 2026 or Winter Session 2025, as applicable) and the correct programme/year on the dashboard, and that your seat number is entered correctly.
  • If the result still does not reflect, contact your college's examination department, since results are declared course-wise and year-wise and may take additional time to appear for all affiliated colleges.
  • If you find any discrepancy in your marks or personal details, contact the SPPU examination authority immediately for correction before the applicable deadline.

SPPU Revaluation and Photocopy Process

If you are not satisfied with your marks in a particular subject, SPPU allows you to apply for revaluation or a photocopy of your answer sheet through the Examination Section on unipune.ac.in. The specific fee amount and the exact application window for this 15 July 2026 declaration are not available in the official notification shared; students should check the Revaluation/Photocopy section on unipune.ac.in or their college notice board for the current fee and deadline.
