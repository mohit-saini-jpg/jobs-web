A
For the Medical Physicist 'E' post, candidates need an M.Sc. (Physics) and a Diploma in Radiological Physics conducted by BARC or equivalent AERB approved qualifications, along with Certification of Radiological Safety Officer from AERB.

Q9What is the selection process for ACTREC Various Scientific Posts?
A
The selection process for ACTREC Various Scientific Posts includes an Interview, a Written Examination, and Document Verification. Candidates must perform well in all stages to be considered for selection.
