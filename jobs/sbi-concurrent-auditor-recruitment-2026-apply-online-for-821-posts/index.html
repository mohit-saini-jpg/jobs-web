Important Instructions

Only retired officers of SBI (and its e-Associate banks) who retired on superannuation at age 60 in the scales of MMGS-III, SMGS-IV/V or TEGS-VI are eligible to apply. This engagement is purely contractual in nature and shall not be treated as extension in service for the purpose of PF/Pension/Bonus and other superannuation benefits. The Engaged Retired Official will not be covered under SBI Officers’ Service Rules but will be bound by the terms of the offer of engagement. The retired officers will not exercise any administrative/financial power during the period of engagement. The retired officers will not accept any assignment with any other organization during the period of contractual service in the Bank. Retired Officers with good credit/audit/Forex background should preferably be a resident of the area where the branch/outfit is located. Only one application will be accepted for applying at one Circle. Multiple applications if received, only the last valid (completed) application will be accepted. The existing wait list of Retired Officers empanelled as Concurrent Auditors will get cancelled with this publication. Candidates must upload all required documents; failure to do so will result in their candidature not being considered for shortlisting/interview. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the interview process. Contract may be terminated by giving one month’s notice from either side. Contract may be terminated without notice if performance is not satisfactory. The selected candidate has to furnish a stamped Service Level Agreement (SLA) before taking up the assignment. If any false or incorrect information is detected at any stage of engagement, candidature/services will be summarily terminated. Bank reserves the right to cancel the engagement process entirely at any stage without prior notice. All revisions/corrigendum (if any) will be hosted on the Bank’s Careers website only. For any query, write through the ‘CONTACT US’ link available on the Bank’s website (https://sbi.bank.in/web/careers/).

FAQs

Q1What is SBI Concurrent Auditor Recruitment 2026?
A
SBI Concurrent Auditor Recruitment 2026 is a contractual engagement drive by State Bank of India to appoint 821 retired SBI officers as Concurrent Auditors in various Circles under the Internal Audit Department, Central Control, Hyderabad for a tenure of up to 3 years.
Q2How many vacancies are there in SBI Concurrent Auditor Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 821 vacancies have been notified for the post of Concurrent Auditor, distributed across all SBI Circles including Amaravati, Bengaluru, Bhopal, Jaipur, Mumbai Metro, New Delhi and others as per Annexure‑I of Advertisement No. CRPD/RS/2026‑27/08.
Q3What are the important dates for SBI Concurrent Auditor Recruitment 2026?
A
The online registration for SBI Concurrent Auditor Recruitment 2026 starts on 14 May 2026 and the last date for online application submission is 11 June 2026, while eligibility like maximum age is reckoned as on 14 May 2026.
Q4Who is eligible to apply for SBI Concurrent Auditor 2026?
A
Only retired officers of SBI and its erstwhile e‑Associate banks who retired on superannuation at 60 years in the grades of MMGS‑III, SMGS‑IV, SMGS‑V or TEGS‑VI, with a good service record and no major penalties in the last five years, are eligible to apply.
Q5What is the age limit for SBI Concurrent Auditor Recruitment 2026?
A
For fresh engagement, the maximum age should not exceed 63 years as on 14 May 2026, since the contractual engagement is allowed for a minimum of 1 year and up to a maximum of 3 years or till the officer attains 65 years of age, whichever is earlier.
Q6Is any specific educational qualification required for SBI Concurrent Auditor?
A
No fresh academic qualification is prescribed; however, preference is given to retired officers who have work experience and expertise in areas like credit, audit, foreign exchange and a thorough knowledge of SBI’s systems and procedures.
Q7What is the remuneration for SBI Concurrent Auditor on contract?
A
The selected retired officers will receive a fixed monthly compensation depending on the retired grade: around Rs. 45,000 for MMGS‑III, Rs. 50,000 for SMGS‑IV, Rs. 65,000 for SMGS‑V and Rs. 80,000 for TEGS‑VI, without additional perquisites or housing benefits.
Q8What is the selection process for SBI Concurrent Auditor Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection is based on shortlisting and interview, where a Bank‑constituted committee fixes shortlisting parameters and conducts a personal interview of 100 marks, and the final merit list is prepared in descending order of interview scores.
