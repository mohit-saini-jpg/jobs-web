How to Apply

  • 1Step 1: Download Application Proforma (Annexure-II) and eligibility conditions (Annexure-I) from www.nclt.gov.in under "Career".
  • 2Step 2: Fill in Annexure-II completely. Attach self-attested passport size coloured photograph. State clearly in Section 19 how you are best suited for the post.
  • 3Step 3: Attach all required documents (see list below).
  • 4Step 4: Submit the complete application to your Employer/Head of Office/Forwarding Authority for certification and forwarding.
  • 5Step 5: Forwarding authority sends the complete application to the Secretary, NCLT on or before the last date.
  • 6Bio-data in Prescribed Proforma - Annexure-II (completely filled)
  • 7Supporting certificates/documents for claimed educational qualifications including essential Bachelor's Degree
  • 8Cadre Clearance Certificate - certifying the officer will be relieved immediately upon selection
  • 9Attested photocopies of APARs/ACRs for the last 5 years - attested by Under Secretary rank or above
  • 10Vigilance Clearance Certificate issued by the respective department
  • 11Integrity Certificate issued by the respective department
  • 12Details of major/minor penalties imposed during the last 10 years
  • 13Employer/Head of Office forwarding certificate (as per Annexure-II format) confirming: no vigilance/disciplinary/criminal case pending; integrity certified; APAR enclosed; no penalty in last 10 years; cadre authority has no objection
  • 14Applications must be submitted through proper channel only - via Employer/Head of Office/Forwarding Authority. Direct applications will not be accepted.
  • 15Candidates who apply will NOT be allowed to withdraw their application subsequently. Candidates unwilling to join upon selection must NOT apply.
  • 16Absorption into NCLT is NOT a matter of right. It will be done only based on NCLT's need and as per NCLT Recruitment Rules 2020. Absorption can be considered only after 1 year of satisfactory deputation.
  • 17Vacancy position is tentative and may increase or decrease. Selected candidates will serve only at the specific NCLT Bench for which they are selected.
  • 18GPRA benefit: Only officers already availing GPRA in their parent body can continue this benefit after joining NCLT on deputation. Others are not eligible to apply for GPRA on joining NCLT.
  • 19Cadre authorities must verify that all particulars sent by their officers are correct as per official records before forwarding applications.
  • 20Pre-01.01.2016 regular service will be treated as service in the corresponding level of the 7th CPC pay matrix for eligibility determination.
  • 21NCLT Recruitment Rules 2020 are available on www.nclt.gov.in under "Career" - candidates must read these before applying.
  • 22Applications not accompanied by all required documents, or received after the last date, will not be considered under any circumstances.
  • 23All corrigenda/updates will be published on www.nclt.gov.in - visit regularly.
