HAL Barrackpore ITI Apprentice Recruitment 2026: 27 : Job Overview

Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Barrackpore Division has released a notification (Advt. No. HR/TRG/2026/06/18, dated 9th July 2026) inviting eligible Indian citizens to apply for engagement as Trade Apprentices under the Apprentices Act, 1961. The recruitment is for the One Year Apprenticeship Training Programme for the year 2026-27, with a total of 27 vacancies across five ITI trades. Selection is through a walk-in process, and there is no written test or interview.

About HAL Barrackpore

Hindustan Aeronautics Limited is a Maharatna Central Public Sector Enterprise (CPSE) under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India. According to the notification, HAL is a premier aeronautical industry of South East Asia, operating 21 Production/Overhaul/Service Divisions and 10 collocated Research & Design (R&D) Centres, along with one Facility Management Division, spread across the country.

Educational Qualification

As per the notification, candidates applying for any of the five trades above must have completed: Full-time 10th / HSC / SSC, and ITI under the Craftsman Training Scheme (CTS) in the respective trade (Welder, Turner, Machinist, Electrician, or Fitter), from a recognized institute only. The qualification requirement is the same across all five trades; there is no separate qualification listed per trade beyond this.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidate should have passed regular and full-time 10th/HSC/SSC and ITI under the Craftsman Training Scheme (CTS) in the above trades, from a recognized institute only. Only candidates who passed their respective course in the year 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, or 2025 are eligible to apply. Candidates who have undergone, are undergoing, or are already registered under a NAPS Contract of Apprentice elsewhere are not eligible. Candidates with work experience of one or more years are not eligible. Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply.

HAL Barrackpore ITI Apprentice Recruitment 2026: 27 : Age Limit

Minimum age: 18 years. Maximum age: 26 years, as on 24/07/2026, for General category candidates. Relaxation in the upper age limit is extended to SC/ST/OBC (NCL) candidates as per Government guidelines. The notification does not specify the exact number of years of relaxation for each category — candidates from reserved categories should confirm the applicable relaxation as per current Government of India norms.

Reservation Policy

Reservations for engagement of Apprentices shall be applicable as per the Apprentices Act 1961 and Rules and Subsequent Amendments, and for wards of HAL employees as per HAL Corporate Office Guidelines. OBC candidates must produce a valid Non-Creamy Layer (NCL) Certificate. The notification does not specify a category-wise reserved seat breakdown.

Stipend / Salary

The stipend shall be paid monthly as per the Apprentices Act 1961 and Rules and subsequent amendments. The exact stipend amount is not mentioned in the official notification. Candidates should refer to the applicable Apprentices Act stipend rates or contact HAL Barrackpore directly for the current figure.

HAL Barrackpore ITI Apprentice Recruitment 2026: 27 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection is entirely merit-based. As per the notification: 70% weightage is given to marks scored in the 10th grade. 30% weightage is given to ITI marks. There will be no written test or interview . The list of candidates shortlisted in the selection process will be hosted on the official HAL website, www.hal-india.co.in. Candidates are advised to keep checking the website regularly for updates.
