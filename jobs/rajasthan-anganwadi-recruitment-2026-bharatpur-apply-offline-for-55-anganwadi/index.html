Rajasthan Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 (Bharatpur) : Job Overview

The Office of the Deputy Director, Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS), Bharatpur, under the Rajasthan Government's Department of Women & Child Development, has invited applications for 55 honorary posts of Anganwadi Karyakarta (Worker) and Anganwadi Sahayika (Helper). These vacancies are spread across eight ICDS project offices in Bharatpur district: Bayana, Bharatpur City, Bhusawar, Nadbai, Roopwas, Sewar, Uchhain, and Wair. Eligible local candidates from the concerned Gram Panchayat, ward, or Anganwadi Centre area can submit their applications offline at the respective CDPO office.

Eligibility for Anganwadi Karyakarta and Sahayika Posts

The notification specifies that candidates should belong to the same Gram Panchayat, ward, or Anganwadi Centre catchment area for which the post is vacant. Detailed eligibility criteria, including educational qualification and reservation-related conditions, can be obtained from the concerned CDPO office, as referenced in departmental circular no. 4244 dated 12.01.2023 and letter no. 57871–58286 dated 01.03.2024.

Important Instructions

Complete details of reserved and unreserved vacancies for each CDPO office are available at the respective office. The detailed vacancy list will also be displayed at the Block/Gram Panchayat and Anganwadi Centre level for wider publicity. Applications must be submitted only to the CDPO office under whose jurisdiction the vacant post falls.
