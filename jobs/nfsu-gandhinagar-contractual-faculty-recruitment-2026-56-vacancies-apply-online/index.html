NFSU Gandhinagar Eligibility Criteria 2026

The minimum eligibility for Assistant Professor (Contractual) is a Ph.D. in the concerned/allied/relevant discipline with a minimum of 60% (or equivalent grade) at the preceding degree, along with a very good academic record throughout. Candidates who have completed their Ph.D. viva voce may also apply. A minimum of two publications in UGC-CARE listed, Web of Science, or Scopus indexed journals is required. If sufficiently qualified Ph.D. candidates are not available for a post, the university may consider candidates at lower remuneration levels as follows: Assistant Professor (Lower Remuneration): Master's degree with minimum 60% in the concerned/allied/relevant discipline and qualified UGC NET (or equivalent) in the relevant branch.
