St. Joseph's College Darjeeling Merit List 2026 - Provisional List, Admission Call List & Counselling Guide

St. Joseph's College Darjeeling has officially released its Merit List 2026 for Undergraduate (UG) admissions. The Provisional Merit List was published on June 10, 2026, followed by the 1st Admission Call List on the same date and the 2nd Admission Call List (Phase 2) on June 18, 2026. If you applied for BA, B.Com, B.Sc, BBA, BCA, or BJMC courses at SJC Darjeeling for the 2026–27 academic session, here is everything you need to know - from how to check your name on the list to the documents you must carry for verification.

What Is the St. Joseph's College Darjeeling Merit List 2026?

The SJC Darjeeling Merit List 2026 is a ranked selection list of candidates who have been shortlisted for undergraduate admission based on their performance in Class 12 (10+2) board examinations. The college does not conduct an entrance test for UG admissions; instead, selection is entirely merit-based, using marks obtained in WBBSE Madhyamik results, WBCHSE Higher Secondary results, or equivalent 10+2 / HS examinations from recognized boards.

The merit list is published in phases first as a Provisional Merit List, followed by one or more Admission Call Lists. Candidates whose names appear on the list are called for document verification and counselling at the college.

How to Check St. Joseph's College Darjeeling Merit List 2026

Checking your name on the SJC Darjeeling merit list is straightforward. The college publishes all lists through its official ERP admission portal. Follow the steps below to access your selection status.

Step 1: Visit the official website at sjcdarjeeling.edu.in or go directly to the admission ERP portal at erp.sjcdarjeeling.edu.in

Step 2: On the homepage, look for the Merit List / Admission Call List link under the Admission section.

Step 3: Click on the relevant list - Provisional Merit List 2026, 1st Admission Call List 2026, or 2nd Admission Call List 2026 (Phase 2).

Step 4: The list will open as a PDF or online view. Search for your name or application number.

Step 5: If your name appears, note the counselling date, time, and document verification schedule mentioned alongside the list.

Step 6: Report to the college on the specified date with all required original documents.

St. Joseph's College Darjeeling - About the Institution

St. Joseph's College, located at North Point, Darjeeling, West Bengal, is one of the most reputed colleges in the hills. Established in 1927, it is a private, government-aided Christian minority institution owned and managed by the Darjeeling Jesuits of North Bengal - a Roman Catholic order known as the Society of Jesus. The college is affiliated with the University of North Bengal (NBU) and is accredited by NAAC with Grade 'B+' (Cycle 3).

Quick College Facts:

  • Established: 1927
  • Type: Private, Government-Aided, Minority Institution
  • Affiliated To: University of North Bengal (NBU)
  • NAAC Accreditation: Grade B+ (Cycle 3)
  • Location: North Point, Darjeeling – 734104, West Bengal
  • Nearest Railway Station: Darjeeling Railway Station (3.2 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Bagdogra International Airport (72.9 km)

Eligibility Criteria for SJC Darjeeling Admission 2026

Before checking the merit list, candidates should verify that they meet the eligibility conditions for the programme they applied for. Admission to St. Joseph's College Darjeeling is entirely merit-based, and candidates must satisfy the following requirements.

For UG Programmes (BA / B.Com / B.Sc / BBA / BCA / BJMC):

  • Must have passed Class 12 (10+2 or equivalent) from a recognized board.
  • English is a compulsory subject, and candidates must have studied at least four subjects.
  • Minimum aggregate score of 55% in qualifying examinations.
  • For Single Major or Single Minor courses: minimum 60% marks in the relevant or related subject in the qualifying exam.

For PG Programmes (MA):

  • Must hold a Bachelor's degree (BA or equivalent) from a recognized university.

Subject Stream Basis: Subject combinations in the SJC Darjeeling merit list are also determined based on marks obtained in Science (PCB/PCM in I.Sc), Commerce (I.Com), and Arts (IA) streams.

Understanding the SJC Darjeeling Admission Call List 2026

The Admission Call List is different from the Provisional Merit List. Here is a clear breakdown of what each list means for applicants.

Provisional Merit List: The first ranked list of eligible candidates based on Class 12 marks. This is a preliminary list and may be subject to corrections within a scheduled date. Appearing on the provisional list does not guarantee final admission.

1st Admission Call List (Released: June 10, 2026): Candidates from the provisional merit list who are called for the first round of document verification and seat confirmation.

2nd Admission Call List - Phase 2 (Released: June 18, 2026): A second round of calls issued either for vacancies arising from the first round, or for a second phase of the overall process. Candidates who missed out in Phase 1 or are on the waiting list should watch for this list.

Documents Required for SJC Darjeeling Counselling & Document Verification 2026

When your name appears in the SJC Darjeeling Admission Call List 2026, you must appear for document verification in person at the college. Carry the following documents in original along with a set of photocopies:

  • Admit card and Marksheet of Class 10 (WBBSE Madhyamik or equivalent)
  • Marksheet and Certificate of Class 12 / HS (WBCHSE or equivalent board)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) / Migration Certificate from previous institution
  • Character Certificate from the last attended institution
  • Caste / Category Certificate (SC / ST / OBC), if applicable
  • Domicile or Residence Certificate, if required
  • Recent passport-size photographs (at least 4 copies)
  • Printout of the SJC Darjeeling Online Admission Application Form
  • Fee payment receipt (if applicable at the time of verification)

SJC Darjeeling Counselling Rules & Regulations 2026

Understanding the counselling rules before you attend will help you avoid last-minute confusion. The following are the key rules and regulations for St. Joseph's College Darjeeling admission 2026.

  • All applicants must fill the SJC Darjeeling online admission form within the scheduled dates.
  • Admission form fees once paid are non-refundable.
  • Corrections in the provisional merit list may be allowed but strictly within the scheduled correction window.
  • Candidates must produce original copies of all documents at the time of admission/counselling.
  • Admission procedure follows the NEP FYUGP (Four-Year Undergraduate Programme) pattern for the 1st semester.
  • The admission call list is published on the official portal, and candidates are expected to check it regularly.

How to Apply for SJC Darjeeling Admission 2026 (Step-by-Step)

For candidates who have not yet completed their application or are looking to understand the process, here is the complete online application procedure for St. Joseph's College Darjeeling UG Admission 2026.

  • Visit the official SJC Darjeeling admission portal at sjcdarjeeling.edu.in.
  • Click on the "Apply Now" or "Online Admission" button.
  • Register yourself by entering your name, email ID, and mobile number.
  • You will receive an SMS with your Application Number - keep it safe.
  • Log in and fill in all admission-related details (personal, academic, course preference).
  • Upload a recent passport-size photograph in the required format.
  • Make the online payment of the application fee.
  • Download and take a printout of the completed SJC Darjeeling admission application form.
  • Wait for the merit list / admission call list to be published on the portal.
  • If your name appears, attend the counselling session on the specified date with all original documents.
