Maharashtra PSC Group C Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1When did the MPSC Group C 2026 notification come out?
A
The MPSC Group C Recruitment 2026 notification (Advertisement No. 017/2026) was released on 25 June 2026 on the official website mpsc.gov.in.
Q2What is the last date to apply online for MPSC Group C 2026?
A
The last date to apply online is 17 July 2026 at 11:59 PM. For offline fee payment through SBI bank challan, the last date is 20 July 2026 during banking hours.
Q3How many vacancies are available in MPSC Group C Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 2,619 vacancies have been announced across five cadres: Clerk-Typist (1,864), Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspector (461), Tax Assistant (282), Industry Inspector (9), and Technical Assistant (3).
Q4What is the application fee for MPSC Group C 2026?
A
For the Preliminary Exam, the fee is ₹394 for General/Open category, ₹294 for reserved categories (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD/Orphan), and ₹44 for Ex-Servicemen. For the Main Exam, the fee is ₹544 (General), ₹344 (Reserved), and ₹44 (Ex-Servicemen).
Q5What is the MPSC Group C Preliminary Exam date 2026?
A
The MPSC Group C Combined Preliminary Examination 2026 is scheduled for 27 September 2026 in CBT/Online mode.
Q6What is the qualification required for MPSC Clerk-Typist Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must hold the relevant graduate qualification along with a Marathi typing speed of at least 30 words per minute OR an English typing speed of at least 40 words per minute, certified by the Government Commercial Certificate Examination or a recognised equivalent.
Q7What is the salary for MPSC Group C posts?
A
Pay scales as per the 7th Pay Commission range from S-6: ₹19,900–₹63,200 (Clerk-Typist) to S-23: ₹35,400–₹1,12,400 (Industry Inspector) and S-14: ₹36,600–₹1,15,600 (AMVI). Additional allowances such as DA and HRA are payable as per rules.
Q8What is the age limit for MPSC Group C 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years (19 years for Tax Assistant). The maximum age is 38 years for Open/General category and 43 years for reserved categories. Age is calculated as on 1 October 2026. Persons with disabilities can apply up to age 45.
Q9What is the selection process for MPSC Group C AMVI Recruitment 2026?
A
For AMVI posts, the selection involves a Combined Preliminary Exam (100 marks) followed by an Independent Main Exam (300 marks) specific to the AMVI cadre. There is no interview stage.
Q10Where can I apply online for MPSC Group C Bharti 2026?
A
Applications can be submitted at the official MPSC portal mpsconline.gov.in or mpsc.gov.in from 27 June 2026 at 2:00 PM onwards.
