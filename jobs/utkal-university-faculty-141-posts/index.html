FAQs

Q1What is Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026 is a recruitment drive by Utkal University, Vani Vihar, Bhubaneswar to fill 141 regular faculty positions including 39 Professor, 51 Associate Professor, and 51 Assistant Professor posts across 23-27 departments under three separate notifications dated 18 May 2026.
Q2How many vacancies are available in Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 141 vacancies are available: 39 Professor posts (UR-18, ST-9, SC-8, SEBC-4), 51 Associate Professor posts (UR-18, ST-17, SC-9, SEBC-7), and 51 Assistant Professor posts (UR-27, ST-10, SC-4, SEBC-10) with horizontal reservation for Women and PwBD candidates.
Q3What is the last date to apply for Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for online application submission is 20 June 2026 (midnight), with hard copy submission deadline of 29 June 2026 via Speed Post/Registered Post to The Registrar, Utkal University, Vani Vihar, Bhubaneswar.
Q4What is the eligibility for Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Eligibility follows UGC Notification 18th July 2018: Assistant Professor requires Master's degree with 55% marks plus NET/SET/Ph.D.; Associate Professor requires Ph.D. with 8 years experience and 75 research score; Professor requires Ph.D. with 10 years experience, 10 publications, and 120 research score.
Q5What is the age limit for Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates above 60 years of age are not eligible to apply. There is no lower age limit specified, but candidates must fulfill the minimum qualification and experience requirements as per UGC norms.
Q6What is the selection process for Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process includes application screening/scrutiny as per UGC Notification 18th July 2018 followed by Interview/Selection Committee assessment with mandatory PPT presentation covering biodata, research, teaching methodology, AI in pedagogy, and vision for nation building.
Q7Is there any application fee for Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, application fee is ₹2,000 for General/SEBC candidates and ₹1,000 for SC/ST/PwD/Women candidates, payable online through HIMS portal (non-refundable).
Q8What is the salary for Utkal University Faculty posts 2026?
A
Professor posts offer Pay Level-14, Associate Professor posts offer Pay Level-13A as per 7th CPC, and Assistant Professor posts offer salary as per UGC norms with applicable allowances and perquisites.
Q9Can Overseas Citizens of India apply for Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, applications are open to both eligible Indian Citizens and Overseas Citizens of India (OCIs) who meet the UGC eligibility criteria as per UGC Notification 18th July 2018 and subsequent amendments.
Q10How to apply for Utkal University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online through HIMS Portal (hims.odisha.gov.in) from 19 May 2026 to 20 June 2026, upload all documents, pay application fee, and also send hard copy with supporting documents via Speed Post/Registered Post to The Registrar, Utkal University by 29 June 2026.
