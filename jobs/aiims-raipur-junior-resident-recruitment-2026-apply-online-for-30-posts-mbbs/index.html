Job Overview

All India Institute of Medical Sciences Raipur (AIIMS Raipur) has published the Junior Resident (Non Academic) Notification 2026 under Advertisement No. Reg.Off./Rec./JR/2026/AIIMS.RPR/1537. The recruitment drive aims to fill 30 Junior Resident (Non Academic) positions. Candidates up to 30 years of age (with applicable relaxations for SC/ST, OBC, and PwBD categories) and holding an MBBS degree, including completion of Internship, or an equivalent qualification recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC), are eligible to apply online. FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date to apply for the AIIMS Raipur Junior Resident (Non Academic) recruitment?
A
The last date to apply for the AIIMS Raipur Junior Resident (Non Academic) recruitment is 12-06-2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications before this date to avoid any last-minute issues.
Q2Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Junior Resident post?
A
The educational qualification required is MBBS (including completion of Internship) or an equivalent degree recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC). Candidates who completed their internship not earlier than two years before the start date of Junior Residency will be preferred.
Q3Q3. What is the age limit for the Junior Resident (Non Academic) post?
A
The maximum age limit for the Junior Resident (Non Academic) post is 30 years. Age relaxations are applicable for SC/ST, OBC, and PwBD categories as per government rules.
Q4Q4. How many vacancies are available for the Junior Resident (Non Academic) post?
A
There are a total of 30 vacancies available for the Junior Resident (Non Academic) post at AIIMS Raipur.
Q5Q5. What is the application fee for the AIIMS Raipur Junior Resident (Non Academic) recruitment?
A
The application fee for General/OBC/EWS candidates is Rs. 1,000/-. There is no application fee for Women/SC/ST/PwBD/Ex-servicemen candidates.
Q6Q6. What is the salary offered for the Junior Resident (Non Academic) post?
A
The salary offered is Rs. 56100/- (Level-10 Cell No. 01 as per 7th CPC) per month, plus usual allowances including NPA.
Q7Q7. What is the selection process for the Junior Resident (Non Academic) post?
A
For AIIMS Raipur MBBS graduates, selection will be based on merit determined by aggregate marks in the first, second, and final Professional examinations. For other candidates, the selection process involves an interview.
Q8Q8. Is there any experience required for this post?
A
While not strictly mandatory, candidates who have completed their MBBS (including Internship) not earlier than two years before the start date of Junior Residency are preferred. The internship completion date should be on or between 13.06.2024 to 12.06.2026.
Q9Q9. How can I apply for the AIIMS Raipur Junior Resident (Non Academic) recruitment?
A
