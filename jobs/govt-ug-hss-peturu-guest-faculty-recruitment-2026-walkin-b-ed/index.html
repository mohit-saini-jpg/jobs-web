FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for Govt UG HSS Peturu Guest Faculty posts?
A
The walk-in interview for the Guest Faculty positions at Govt UG HSS Peturu is scheduled for 12th June 2026. Candidates are advised to attend on this date.
Q2Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Guest Faculty position?
A
Candidates must possess a Post Graduate (PG) degree in the respective subjects with a minimum of 50% marks. A B.Ed. degree is also required, with a weightage given to those with a PGDCA.
Q3Q3. What is the age limit for applying to the Guest Faculty role?
A
The age limit for the Guest Faculty position is below 65 years of age. Retired government or non-government aided teachers are also eligible if they meet this criterion.
Q4Q4. What is the salary offered for the Guest Faculty position?
A
The salary for Guest Faculty is Rs. 400/- per class, with a maximum limit of 50 classes per month.
Q5Q5. How can I apply for the Guest Faculty position at Govt UG HSS Peturu?
A
Interested candidates need to attend a walk-in interview with their complete bio-data, passport-size photographs, and all original and self-attested photocopies of certificates from HSC to PG.
Q6Q6. What documents do I need to carry for the walk-in interview?
A
You must carry your complete bio-data, two passport-size photographs, and all original certificates and mark sheets from HSC to PG, along with one set of self-attested photocopies.
Q7Q7. What is the selection process for the Guest Faculty recruitment?
A
The selection process for the Guest Faculty positions will be based on a walk-in interview.
Q8Q8. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
The official notification does not mention any application fee for this recruitment drive.
Q9Q9. Which subjects are covered under the Guest Faculty recruitment?
A
The recruitment covers subjects such as Odia, Economics, History, English, Sociology, and Political Science for Higher Secondary classes (+2).
Q10Q10. Where is the walk-in interview for Govt UG HSS Peturu Guest Faculty posts going to be held?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at the Govt UG HSS Peturu office, located at Peturu, Koraput, Odisha, 764039.
