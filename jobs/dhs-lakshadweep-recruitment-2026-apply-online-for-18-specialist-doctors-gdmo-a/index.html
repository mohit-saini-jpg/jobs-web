Eligibility Criteria & Terms

Candidates must possess the prescribed qualifications as mentioned in the vacancy table. Appointment is purely on contract basis for 11 months and likely to be extended based on performance and requirement. Selected candidates will be posted in various islands of Lakshadweep as per the need of the department. No TA/DA will be provided for appearing in the interview.

Important Instructions

Applications received after the due date will not be considered. The selection will be based on interview and academic records. This is contractual employment. No claim for regular appointment will be entertained. Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification. For any queries, candidates may contact the Directorate of Health Services, Kavaratti.
