Eligibility Criteria

The post is gazetted and carries a contributory pension. Qualification is as per the latest NMC-TEQ Regulations - Minimum Qualifications for Teachers in Medical Institutions Regulations, dated 30 June 2025. All else being equal, preference in direct recruitment will be given to candidates who have: Served a minimum of 2 years in the Territorial Army, OR Obtained a National Cadet Corps (NCC) 'B' Certificate Candidates must hold a valid/renewed registration certificate with the medical council of any state in the country, up to the last date of online application. Note: For non-clinical subjects, candidates holding an M.Sc (Medical)/Ph.D medical degree are not required to have Medical Council registration. Reservation Policy

Reservation is available only to holders of a Uttarakhand permanent resident certificate. Categories eligible for vertical/horizontal reservation as per prevailing Uttarakhand Government orders include: Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes, Economically Weaker Sections, Ex-Servicemen, dependents of Freedom Fighters, Divyangjan (PWD), Uttarakhand Women, orphan children of Uttarakhand, Uttarakhand agitators (or their dependents), and skilled sportspersons. PWD reservation requires a minimum of 40% disability in the relevant category. For Professor/Associate Professor posts under the Medical Education Department, only the "One Leg Affected (O.L.)" disability category is eligible for reservation, as per Government Order No. 48/XVII-A-3/2023-01(11)/Vi.K./2017 dated 05 June 2023; applications from other disability categories will not be accepted. Edit/Correction Process

The edit/correction link for the submitted application will open one working day after the online application closes on 29 July 2026, and remain open for 4 days - from 31 July 2026 to 03 August 2026, up to 5:00 PM. Only candidates who have completed the application process can log in using their registration number and password to make corrections. Candidates can amend any field except mobile number and e-mail ID. Entries as edited during this window will be treated as final; data as of the original application closing date will otherwise be considered for processing timelines. If a candidate changes their category/sub-category during correction (for example, from Unreserved to SC), the fee difference, if any, will not be refunded. No request for correction/modification of any entry or claim will be considered by the Board once this correction window closes.

UKMSSB Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for UKMSSB Professor/Associate Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application closes on 29 July 2026 (Wednesday) at 5:00 PM. The fee must also be paid by the same date and time.
Q2How many total vacancies are there in UKMSSB Professor/Associate Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
There are 54 total vacancies - 8 for Professor and 46 for Associate Professor - though the notification states the number of vacancies may increase or decrease.
Q3What is the age limit for UKMSSB Professor and Associate Professor posts?
A
As on 01 July 2025, the minimum age is 35 years for Professor and 30 years for Associate Professor, with a maximum age of 62 years for both posts. Age relaxation of 5 years is available for SC/ST/OBC, freedom fighter dependents, and Divyangjan candidates of Uttarakhand, as detailed in the Age Limit section above.
Q4What is the application fee for UKMSSB Professor/Associate Professor Recruitment 2026?
A
The fee is ₹2,000 for Unreserved and Uttarakhand OBC candidates, and ₹1,000 for EWS, Uttarakhand SC, Uttarakhand ST, and Uttarakhand PWD candidates, payable online only.
Q5What is the pay scale for UKMSSB Professor and Associate Professor posts?
A
Professor is paid ₹1,44,200 - ₹2,18,200 (Level-14), and Associate Professor is paid ₹1,31,100 - ₹3,16,600 (Level-13(A)). A 50% Medical Teachers Deficiency Compensatory Scheme allowance is additionally admissible for postings in Srinagar/Almora.
Q6How is selection done for UKMSSB Professor/Associate Professor posts?
A
Selection is based on 100 total marks: up to 20 marks for paper publications, up to 20 marks for relevant government-sector experience, 10 marks for additional educational qualification (DM/M.Ch/DNB Super Specialty), and 50 marks for the interview.
Q7Is Medical Council registration compulsory for all candidates?
A
Medical Council registration is compulsory for clinical subject candidates. It is not mandatory for candidates in non-clinical subjects holding an M.Sc (Medical)/Ph.D medical degree.
Q8Can I edit my application after submission?
A
Yes, but only during the designated correction window from 31 July 2026 to 03 August 2026. You can edit any field except your registered mobile number and e-mail ID. No corrections will be entertained after this window closes.
Q9Is reservation available to candidates from outside Uttarakhand?
A
No. Reservation benefits under this recruitment are available only to candidates holding a Uttarakhand permanent resident certificate.
Q10When will the interview for UKMSSB Professor/Associate Professor Recruitment 2026 be held?
A
The interview and document verification are proposed to be held in November-December 2026. The exact schedule with roll numbers will be published on the official UKMSSB website.
