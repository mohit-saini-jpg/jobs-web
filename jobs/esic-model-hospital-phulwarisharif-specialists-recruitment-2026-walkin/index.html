FAQs

Q1What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for ESIC Model Hospital Phulwarisharif Recruitment?
A
The initial walk-in interview is scheduled for 19th June 2026. Subsequent interviews will be held on the 3rd Friday of every month until March 2027.
Q2What is the total number of vacancies available for Specialists?
A
There are a total of 5 vacancies for Specialists posts at ESIC Model Hospital Phulwarisharif.
Q3What are the minimum qualifications required for the Specialist posts?
A
Candidates must possess a PG Degree/Diploma in the concerned Specialty and be registered with the Medical Council of India.
Q4What is the age limit for the Specialist positions?
A
The maximum age limit for all Specialist positions is 67 years.
Q5How will the candidates be selected for these posts?
A
The selection process will be based on a Walk-in-Interview.
Q6What is the application fee for the ESIC Model Hospital Phulwarisharif Recruitment?
A
The application fee is Rs. 400 for General, EWS, and OBC candidates, Rs. 100 for SC/ST candidates, and exempted for female candidates.
Q7What documents should candidates bring to the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates should bring self-attested copies of their SSC/10th Certificate, MBBS and PG Degree Certificates, MCI Registration, category certificates (if applicable), experience certificates, and two passport-size photographs.
Q8What is the reporting time for the walk-in interview?
A
The reporting time for the walk-in interview is between 09:00 AM and 10:30 AM.
Q9What is the salary offered for Part-Time Specialists?
A
Part-Time Specialists will receive a fixed remuneration of Rs. 60,000 for 16 hours/week, with additional charges for emergency calls and extra hours.
Q10Is there any experience required for the Specialist posts?
A
Yes, experience requirements vary. Junior Specialists need 3 years post-PG experience, Senior Specialists need 5 years post-PG experience, and Part-Time Specialists need 3 years post-PG Degree or 5 years post-PG Diploma experience.
