IGCAR JRF Recruitment 2026 Notification Out : Job Overview

The Indira Gandhi Centre for Atomic Research (IGCAR), Kalpakkam, has invited online applications for 50 Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) positions under Advertisement No. IGCAR/02/2026, covering Physical Sciences, Chemical Sciences, and Engineering Sciences. Indian nationals up to 28 years of age can apply online from 22 June 2026 to 13 July 2026. Selected JRFs register for a Ph.D. under the Homi Bhabha National Institute (HBNI) and receive a monthly stipend ranging from ₹25,000 to ₹42,000.

IGCAR JRF Recruitment 2026 Notification Out : Application Fee Details

Candidates preparing to apply should know that the official notification confirms an application fee is payable at the time of final submission of the online application, though the exact fee amount per category is not specified within the advertisement itself. IGCAR JRF Recruitment 2026 Notification Out : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Candidates ready to apply should follow the official online process carefully, since IGCAR does not accept hardcopy applications under any circumstances. After submitting the online form, candidates must print it, attach a photo, and sign it for use during later verification stages.
  • 2Visit the official IGCAR recruitment page or the dedicated JRF Exam Portal.
  • 3Register using your email ID and create a password (note: name, date of birth, category, gender, email ID, and mobile number cannot be changed after registration).
  • 4Fill in personal, academic, and category details accurately, including GATE/UGC-CSIR-NET/JEST scores where applicable.
  • 5Select your preferred Screening Test centre (Chennai, Delhi, Kolkata, Bhubaneswar, or Hyderabad).
  • 6Submit the application and pay the application fee online.
  • 7Take a printout of the submitted application, affix a recent passport-size photo, and sign at the marked place.
  • 8Download and print the Admit Card once published, and bring it along with a valid photo ID to the Screening Test/Interview.

IGCAR JRF Recruitment 2026 Notification Out : Important Instructions

A few additional rules apply beyond eligibility and the application process, and missing these can affect a candidate's final selection even after a successful interview:
Candidature remains provisional until detailed document verification is completed at the final selection stage. Selected candidates must be declared medically fit by the Medical Superintendent, DAE Hospital, Kalpakkam, before final induction as JRF. The selected candidates list will be published on igcar.gov.in and communicated through registered email. Subsidized bachelor hostel accommodation is provided during the fellowship tenure; candidates shortlisted for interview also get nominal-charge accommodation for 48 hours at DAE Township, Kalpakkam. Selected candidates are eligible to join the Contributory Health Service Scheme (CHSS). The fellowship does not confer any claim to regular employment at IGCAR or any DAE unit, and the JRF tenure is not counted as service for any DAE/government post. Records of non-selected candidates are not preserved beyond 6 months from the publication of the select list. Any legal disputes are subject to Chennai jurisdiction, with the English version of the advertisement treated as the reference copy.

IGCAR JRF Recruitment 2026 Notification Out : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1How many vacancies are there in IGCAR JRF Recruitment 2026?
A
IGCAR has announced 50 Junior Research Fellowships under Advertisement No. IGCAR/02/2026, though the number is approximate and may vary at the time of final selection.
Q2What is the last date to apply for IGCAR JRF 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 13 July 2026, by 23:59 hrs. Applications opened on 22 June 2026 at 10:00 hrs.
Q3What is the age limit for IGCAR JRF Recruitment 2026?
A
The maximum age limit is 28 years as on the closing date (13 July 2026), with relaxation of 3 years for OBC, 5 years for SC/ST, and 10 years for PwBD candidates.
Q4What is the minimum qualification required for IGCAR JRF 2026?
A
Candidates need a minimum of 55% aggregate (50% for SC/ST/OBC/PwBD) at both graduation and post-graduation, in an eligible subject under Category A, B, C, or D as specified in the notification.
Q5Is there a written exam for IGCAR JRF Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, a Screening Test is tentatively scheduled for 02 August 2026, though candidates with a valid UGC-CSIR-NET Fellowship, JEST score, or qualifying GATE score may be exempted and called for a Direct Interview.
Q6What is the stipend for IGCAR Junior Research Fellows?
A
The stipend ranges from ₹25,000 to ₹42,000 per month depending on the year of the programme, along with an annual contingency grant of ₹60,000.
Q7Is there an application fee for IGCAR JRF 2026?
A
Yes, an application fee is payable at the time of final online submission, though the exact amount is not specified in the advertisement; candidates should check the application portal for the fee during submission.
Q8How can I apply for IGCAR JRF Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online through the JRF Exam Portal at igcjrf.in or via igcar.gov.in/recruitment.html by registering, filling the application form, paying the fee, and submitting before 13 July 2026.
