FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies for GSSSB Junior Pharmacist Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 266 vacancies have been announced for the post of Junior Pharmacist (Class-3) under the Health and Family Welfare Department, Government of Gujarat.
Q2What is the last date to apply for GSSSB Junior Pharmacist 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 25-06-2026 (up to 23:59 hrs). The last date for fee payment is 28-06-2026 (up to 23:59 hrs).
Q3What is the educational qualification required for GSSSB Junior Pharmacist 2026?
A
Candidates must possess either a Bachelor's degree in Pharmacy (B.Pharm) or Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D.) from a recognized university, OR a Diploma in Pharmacy with approximately 2 years of relevant experience as a pharmacist/dispenser in a government hospital, undertaking, or pharmaceutical company.
Q4What is the age limit for GSSSB Junior Pharmacist Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 35 years as on 25-06-2026. Age relaxation is available for women, reserved categories (SC/ST/SEBC/EWS), PwBD, and Ex-Servicemen as per Government of Gujarat rules.
Q5What is the pay scale for GSSSB Junior Pharmacist Class-3?
A
Selected candidates will be appointed on a fixed pay of Rs. 40,800/- per month for the first 5 years. After 5 years, on satisfactory performance, they will be regularized in the 7th Pay Commission Pay Scale of Rs. 29,200/- to Rs. 92,300/- (Level-5).
Q6What is the application fee for GSSSB Junior Pharmacist 2026?
A
The examination fee is Rs. 500/- for General (UR) candidates and Rs. 400/- for Reserved category candidates (all category women, SEBC, SC, ST, EWS, PwBD, and Ex-Servicemen). Fee is refundable to candidates who appear for the examination.
Q7What is the exam pattern for GSSSB Junior Pharmacist Recruitment 2026?
A
The exam is a single-stage MCQ-based competitive written test (CBRT/OMR) consisting of two parts: Part-A (90 marks – General Aptitude) and Part-B (120 marks – Pharmacy subject). Total 210 questions, 3 hours duration, with 1/4 negative marking for wrong answers.
Q8Is there any minimum qualifying marks required in the GSSSB Junior Pharmacist exam?
A
Yes. Candidates must score a minimum of 40% marks separately in both Part-A and Part-B. No relaxation below 40% will be granted to any category of candidates.
Q9How to apply for GSSSB Junior Pharmacist Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online only through the OJAS portal at https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in between 06-06-2026 (14:00 hrs) and 25-06-2026 (23:59 hrs). No offline applications will be accepted.
Q10Is GSSSB Junior Pharmacist Recruitment 2026 only for Gujarat domicile candidates?
A
Reservation benefits (age relaxation and reserved category seats) are applicable only to candidates originally domiciled in Gujarat (Mool Gujarat). However, any Indian citizen fulfilling the eligibility criteria may apply for the unreserved posts.
