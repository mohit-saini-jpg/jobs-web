Guest PGT Urdu: Master's degree in Urdu with at least 50% marks (with Urdu at graduation level) and B.Ed degree. Guest PGT Biology: Two years Integrated Post Graduate M.Sc. in concerned subject OR Master's degree with at least 50% marks in Botany/Zoology/Life Science/Bio Science/Genetics/Micro Biology/Bio Technology/Molecular Bio/Plant Physiology (with Botany and Zoology at graduation level) and B.Ed degree. Guest PGT Economics: Master's degree in Economics/Applied Economics/Business Economics with at least 50% marks (with Economics at graduation level) and B.Ed degree. Guest TGT Mathematics: Four Years Integrated degree course OR Bachelor's Degree with at least 50% marks in Mathematics and Physics, Chemistry, Electronics, Computer Science, Statistics (any two) and B.Ed. Guest TGT Science (Bio/Chem): Bachelor's Degree with at least 50% marks in Biology or Chemistry and B.Ed. Pass CTET Paper II. Guest TGT Science: Bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks in Biology or Chemistry and B.Ed. Pass CTET Paper II. PET TGT Physical Education Teacher (PET): Bachelor's Degree in Physical Education (B.P.Ed) with at least 50% marks or equivalent.
