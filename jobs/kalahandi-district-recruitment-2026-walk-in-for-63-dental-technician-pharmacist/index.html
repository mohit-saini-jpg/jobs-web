NHM Kalahandi In-House Selection 2026 Eligibility Criteria

This selection is open only to candidates already serving under National Health Mission in the OSH & FW Society, not to fresh or external applicants. Eligibility is determined based on current employment status, valid contract, and documentary proof of uninterrupted service.

NOC cum Continuation CertificateMandatory document from the competent authority confirming last uninterrupted service in the same post under the society; candidates without this certificate will not be eligible.

Nature of Engagement and Terms & Conditions

The posts notified under this In-House Selection are temporary and tied to the duration of the scheme, not regular government positions. Candidates should review the engagement conditions carefully before applying. Positions are purely temporary and co-terminus with the scheme. All positions are contractual in nature for a period of 11 months , which may be extended based on performance assessment. Canvassing in any form will render the candidate disqualified for the position. Selection will be made as per Order No. 3418, dated 13-03-2028, of the Mission Directorate, NHM, Odisha.

NHM Kalahandi In-House Selection 2026 Selection Process

Unlike open recruitment, this In-House Selection does not involve a written examination. Selection is based on Walk-in document verification followed by an incumbency-based assessment of service length. Stage 1 – Walk-in Application & Document Verification Eligible employees must attend the Walk-in on the date assigned to their post category, at the Video Conference Hall, O/o CDM & PHO, Kalahandi, between 10:00 AM and 12:00 Noon, and submit the filled application form along with all supporting documents for verification. Stage 2 – Incumbency Assessment & Selection For calculation of incumbency, the length of last uninterrupted service of the employee in the same post under the society is taken into account, along with the previous posting station and present place of posting. Candidates with the highest incumbency are selected and posted against the available vacancies.

Who Should NOT Apply for NHM Kalahandi In-House Selection 2026?

Candidates who are not currently employed under NHM in the OSH & FW Society – this selection is not open to fresh or external candidates. Candidates who do not possess a valid NOC cum Continuation Certificate from the competent authority. Candidates who do not hold a current, valid contract period or cannot produce the last contract renewal order. Candidates who have previously been disengaged from ZSS/OSH & FW Society service on administrative grounds such as disobedience, poor performance, misbehaviour, or criminal activities. Candidates who provide incomplete, incorrect, or unverifiable information in the application form. Candidates who cannot attend the Walk-in on the scheduled date and time assigned to their post.
