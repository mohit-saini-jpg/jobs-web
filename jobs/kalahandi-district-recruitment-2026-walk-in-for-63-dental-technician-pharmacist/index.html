How to Apply

  • 1Applications are accepted only in offline mode through the Walk-in process; there is no online application facility for this In-House Selection.
  • 2Download the application form from www.kalahandi.odisha.gov.in.
  • 3Fill the form completely and paste a recent colour passport-size photograph at the designated space.
  • 4Attach self-attested photocopies of all required documents, including the NOC cum Continuation Certificate, last contract renewal order, identity proof, educational certificates, and caste certificate.
  • 5Sign the declaration provided in the application form.
  • 6Attend the Walk-in on the date assigned to the post applied for, at the Video Conference Hall, O/o CDM & PHO, Kalahandi, between 10:00 AM and 12:00 Noon.
  • 7Submit the filled application along with attachments for on-the-spot verification; late submissions will not be entertained.

Important Instructions

This selection is open only to NHM employees serving in the same post in a district other than Kalahandi. No candidates will be entertained after the scheduled Walk-in date and time. The number of vacancies under this advertisement is provisional and may increase or decrease depending on the actual vacancy position. Time-to-time notifications regarding the status of the selection process will be web-hoisted on the district website. All communication will be made through email or the district website; candidates must check www.kalahandi.odisha.gov.in at regular intervals. No personal query will be entertained. The competent authority reserves the right to cancel or reject any or all applications without assigning any reason. This office will not be responsible for any delay in candidates reporting on the scheduled date.

FAQs

Q1Who can apply for NHM Kalahandi In-House Selection 2026?
A
Only NHM employees of the OSH & FW Society who are currently working in the same post in a district other than Kalahandi and wish to be posted in Kalahandi can apply.
Q2How many vacancies are notified under NHM Kalahandi In-House Selection 2026?

Q3What is the mode of application for NHM Kalahandi In-House Selection 2026?

Applications are accepted only in offline mode through Walk-in; there is no online application portal for this selection.
Q4What is the Walk-in schedule for NHM Kalahandi In-House Selection 2026?

A
Key documents include the NOC cum Continuation Certificate, last contract renewal order, identity proof, educational certificates, HSC certificate, caste certificate, and a passport-size photograph.
