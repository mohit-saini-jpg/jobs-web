Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies released by ESIC?

ESIC has released a total of 64 vacancies for Faculty and Senior Residents posts. These positions are available on a contractual basis.

Q2. What is the application mode for these ESIC vacancies?

The application mode for these ESIC vacancies is Walk-in. Candidates need to attend the interview in person with all required documents.

Q3. What is the salary offered for the ESIC Faculty and Senior Residents posts?

The salary offered ranges from Rs. 67,700/- to Rs. 2,59,515/- per month, depending on the post and experience.

Q4. What are the age limits for the ESIC Faculty and Senior Residents posts?

The maximum age limit for Faculty positions is 69 years, and for Senior Residents, it is 45 years.

Q5. What is the educational qualification required for these ESIC posts?

The qualification required is as per the Medical Institutions (Qualifications of Faculty) Regulations 2025, as per the NMC Gazette Notification dated 30-06-2025.

Q6. Is there any application fee for SC/ST and Women candidates?

No, there is no application fee for SC/ST and Women candidates. However, General/OBC candidates need to pay Rs. 500/-.

Q7. When is the Walk-in Interview scheduled for ESIC recruitment?

The Walk-in Interviews are scheduled on multiple dates in May 2026, starting from May 20, 2026, to May 28, 2026.

Q8. What documents should candidates carry for the Walk-in Interview?

Candidates must carry original documents and self-attested photocopies, including ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates, and category certificates if applicable.
