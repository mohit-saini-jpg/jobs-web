AIIMS Rishikesh Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

Applications are screened based on the bio-data/CV submitted through proper channel. AIIMS Rishikesh will shortlist eligible candidates who may be called for an interview. Shortlisted candidates must produce all relevant original documents as proof of the details furnished in their application at the time of interview/selection. Meeting the minimum qualification/eligibility criteria does not automatically guarantee selection for interview.

Documents Required for AIIMS Rishikesh Deputation Application

Duly attested photocopies of Confidential Reports (CRs) of the applicant for the last five years. Vigilance Clearance Certificate, duly signed by an authorized officer, clearly stating that no disciplinary or criminal proceedings are pending or contemplated against the officer. Latest Salary Slip mentioning designation, grade pay, and pay level. A statement showing minor/major penalties imposed on the officer, if any. An Integrity Certificate for the officer recommended for deputation. Important: Applications received without Vigilance Clearance and attested CR Dossiers will not be considered. Applications not routed through the proper channel will also not be considered for interview.

The number of vacancies can be decreased or cancelled, before or after the interview, without assigning any reason, and no intimation will be given for the same.
