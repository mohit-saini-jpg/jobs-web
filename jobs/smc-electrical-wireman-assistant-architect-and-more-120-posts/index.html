FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for Surat Municipal Corporation Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 06 July 2026 at 11:00 PM. Applications must be submitted through the official SMC website at www.suratmunicipal.gov.in.
Q2How many posts are available in SMC Recruitment 2026?
A
Surat Municipal Corporation has announced 120 vacancies across 8 post categories: Director (IT), Departmental Enquiry Officer, Administrative Officer, Assistant Architect, Senior Swimming Pool Instructor, Junior Swimming Pool Instructor, Junior Lady Swimming Pool Instructor, and Electrical Wireman.
Q3What is the application fee for SMC Recruitment 2026?
A
General/unreserved candidates must pay ₹500/-. Candidates from reserved categories (all women, SEBC, EWS, SC, ST, and PwD/Divyangjan) must pay ₹400/-. Payment is accepted online only through debit card, credit card, or net banking. The fee is non-refundable.
Q4What is the age limit for SMC Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit ranges from 35 years (Assistant Architect, Senior Swimming Pool Instructor) to 45 years (Director IT, Departmental Enquiry Officer, Administrative Officer) depending on the post. Age is calculated as on 06 July 2026. Regular SMC employees are exempt from the age limit.
Q5What is the selection process for SMC Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection may include a written test, practical test, oral interview/viva voce, physical fitness test, and document verification. The specific stages for each post will be decided by the Appointment Authority and communicated through call letters on the SMC website and via SMS.
Q6What is the salary for the Director IT post in SMC 2026?
A
The Director (Information Technology) post carries a pay scale of ₹10,000–15,200/- (Pay Band ₹15,600–39,100, Grade Pay ₹6,600/-, Pay Matrix Level-11).
Q7Can candidates from other states apply for SMC Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, candidates from other states can apply for unreserved (General) posts. However, reserved category vacancies (SC, ST, SEBC, EWS) are applicable only to original Gujarat domicile candidates of the respective reserved categories.
Q8Is the Electrical Wireman post reserved for PwD candidates?
A
Yes. Out of 69 Electrical Wireman posts, 8 posts are reserved for PwD (Divyangjan) candidates. The eligible disability types for this post include Deaf/Hard of Hearing, One Arm, One Leg, Leprosy Cured, Dwarfism, Acid Attack Victims, Autism Spectrum Disorder (Mild), Specific Learning Disability, Mental Illness, and Multiple Disabilities (involving eligible types).
Q9What documents are required for the SMC 2026 application?
A
Key documents include age proof (School Leaving Certificate), educational mark sheets and degree certificates, caste certificate (for reserved categories), non-creamy layer certificate (for SEBC), experience certificate on institutional letterhead, PwD/UDID certificate (if applicable), and EPIC Card, PAN Card, and Aadhaar Card copies.
Q10Where can I apply for Surat Municipal Corporation Recruitment 2026?
A
The official recruitment portal is https://www.suratmunicipal.gov.in. Applications are accepted exclusively online through this website from 22 June 2026 to 06 July 2026.
