How to Check Kerala University MSc Botany Result

Candidates can check their MSc Botany 3rd semester result online through the official Kerala University examination portal. Follow these steps:

  • Visit the official result link: https://exams.keralauniversity.ac.in/Login/check8
  • Select the relevant examination -Third Semester PG Degree Examination, MSc Botany, December 2025.
  • Enter your register number/candidate code as required on the portal.
  • Submit the details to view your provisional result.
  • Download or take a screenshot of the result for reference.

Alternatively, candidates can log on to www.keralauniversity.ac.in and navigate to the examination results section.

Passed in Parts, With-Held, and Malpractice Cases

As per the official notification, some candidates have been marked under special categories:

  • Passed in Parts: Several candidates across colleges such as Sanatana Dharma College, Govt. College for Women Trivandrum, Iqbal College, Mahatma Gandhi College, and Bishop Moore College have cleared some subjects but not all, with the pending subject/paper codes listed against their candidate code in the official PDF.
  • With-Held: Results have been withheld for candidates who have not produced their Eligibility Certificates or Qualifying Certificates, as stated in the notification.
  • Suspected Malpractice: One candidate (code 64524123002) from N S S College Pandalam has been listed under "Suspected Malpractice" and the result has not been released.

Revaluation and Scrutiny of Answer Books

Candidates who wish to apply for scrutiny of their answer books can do so through the following process:

  • SLCM candidates: Apply online through www.slcm.keralauniversity.ac.in
  • Non-SLCM candidates: Apply online through www.exams.keralauniversity.ac.in
  • Last date to apply: 25 July 2026

Draft mark lists, which are required to apply for scrutiny, can be downloaded from the same respective portals - www.slcm.keralauniversity.ac.in for SLCM candidates and www.exams.keralauniversity.ac.in for Non-SLCM candidates.

Important Instructions for Candidates

  • This is a provisional result. Final mark lists will be issued only after verification.
  • Originals of qualifying certificates must be submitted to the university for verification of eligibility conditions.
  • Mark lists will not be issued to candidates whose fees have not been remitted to the university, even if the result has been provisionally declared.
  • Mark lists will also not be issued to candidates who have defects to be rectified or dues pending.
  • Candidates whose Eligibility/Qualifying Certificates have not been produced will have their results withheld until further submission.
