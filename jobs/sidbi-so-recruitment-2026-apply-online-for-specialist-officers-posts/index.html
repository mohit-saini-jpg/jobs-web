Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationMaster's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, Public Policy, Development Studies, Data Science, Information Technology, Computer Science, Law, Data Governance, Business Administration, Cybersecurity, or related quantitative/analytical discipline.
DetailsImpact Assessment Analyst (IAA): Master's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics/ Statistics/ Public Policy/ Development Studies/ Data Science or related quantitative/analytical discipline. Certifications or coursework in impact evaluation, data analytics, econometrics or development economics (Desirable). Minimum 5 years of experience in impact measurement, analytics, consulting, development sector or DFI roles. Senior Program Designer (SPD): Master's degree in public policy/ economics/ development studies/ management or related field. Exposure to development finance, program design, or public systems design; certifications in policy, strategy or development programs (Desirable). Minimum 10 years of experience in program design, strategy, consulting, development sector or DFI roles. Consent Manager (CM): Graduate degree in Information Technology/ Computer Science/ Law/ Data Governance/Business Administration. Postgraduate degree in Law, Data Privacy, IT, or Management (Desirable). Certifications such as CIPP/A, CIPP/E, CIPM, DCPP, DCPLA or equivalent privacy/data governance certifications (Desirable). Overall 10 years of experience in managing IT Applications / Information risks out of which at least 4 years of relevant experience in data privacy, IT audit, risk/compliance, or data governance roles, preferably in banks, NBFCs, DFIs, fintechs, or regulated environments and 2 years of experiences in handling Information security risk. Data Auditor (DA): Graduate degree in Information Technology/ Computer Science/Data Science/ Law/ Risk Management. Postgraduate degree in Cybersecurity, Data Privacy, Law (LLB/LLM), or Data Governance (Desirable). Certifications such as CIPP/E, CIPP/A, CIPM, DCPP/DCPLA/ ISO 27001 Lead Auditor, CISA or equivalent (Desirable). Overall 10 years of relevant experience in data privacy, IT audit, risk/compliance, or data governance roles, preferably in banks, NBFCs, DFIs, fintechs, or regulated environments including at least 5 years in IT Auditing. Chief Data and Analytics Officer (CDAO): Graduate degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, Finance, Management, or related quantitative/technical discipline AND Post-graduate degree such as M.Tech, M.Stat, M.Sc (Data Science/Analytics), or equivalent. Certifications in data management, analytics, AI/ML, information management or governance (Desirable). Overall, 18+ years of experience in financial services / consulting / technology, with at least 8+ years in senior leadership roles driving data, analytics or digital transformation.
