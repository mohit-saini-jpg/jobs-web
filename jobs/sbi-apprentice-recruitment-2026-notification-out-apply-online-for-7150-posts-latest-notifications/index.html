Physical Eligibility

DetailsThe competition level for SBI Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is expected to be very high due to 7150 seats being offered at the all-India level and the exam being open to any graduate, including those already preparing for bank and SSC exams. While the written test is of easy to moderate difficulty and slightly below SBI Clerk level, the large number of serious aspirants in high-population states like Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Rajasthan, West Bengal, Maharashtra, Gujarat, Andhra Pradesh and Telangana will push state-wise and category-wise cut-offs upward. | In practical terms, each seat is likely to attract dozens to over a hundred candidates in major states, making speed, accuracy, and sectional balance crucial to clearing the single-stage written exam followed by the local language test. However, the effective competition reduces at later stages because some candidates will fail to qualify the language test or be filtered out at the document verification, CIBIL, Aadhaar, and medical fitness stages, and SBI will finally select candidates strictly on state-wise and category-wise merit from those who clear all stages.

Important Instructions

  • THIS IS NOT AN EMPLOYMENT IN THE BANK. NOR IS THE SAME CONTRACT EMPLOYMENT. Candidates engaged as Apprentice will NOT be treated as "employees" of SBI and will not be entitled to receive any benefits available for employees of the Bank. SBI shall have no obligation to offer regular employment to Apprentices during and/or after the completion of the Apprenticeship period. After completion, candidates will be relieved from the respective work area. Candidates can apply for engagement in one State only and can appear for the examination only once under this engagement project. Mandatory registration on NAPS portal (https://apprenticeshipindia.gov.in) before applying online on SBI website is required. No Ex-Servicemen / Disabled Ex-Servicemen shall be engaged as an Apprentice and there is no reservation for Ex-Servicemen in the Apprentice Engagement. OBC candidates belonging to the "Creamy Layer" are not entitled to reservation/relaxation; they should apply as General or General (LD/VI/HI/d&e) as applicable. Change of category will not be permitted after registration of online application. Candidates with CIBIL default record (unpaid loans/credit card dues) are not eligible for engagement until dues are regularized/repaid. All candidates (except Northeastern State candidates) must produce Aadhaar Number before engagement/contract signing. Not more than one application should be submitted; in case of multiple applications, only the last valid completed application will be retained and fee paid for other registrations will stand forfeited. PwBD candidates with disability conditions falling under "likely to improve" (temporary disability) category are not eligible for reservation. PwBD candidates eligible for scribe facility: candidates with 40% or more disability having physical limitation to write are eligible. Scribe must be arranged by the candidate at his/her own cost. Compensatory time of 20 minutes per hour of examination is allowed (whether availing scribe or not) for eligible PwBD candidates. Candidates are advised to keep their registered email ID/mobile active for receiving call letters/advices etc. Mobile phones, smartwatches, pagers, earphones or any other electronic/communication devices are not allowed inside the examination premises. All revisions/corrigendum (if any) will be hosted on the Bank's website only — candidates should visit regularly. No request for change of examination centre shall be entertained. Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is SBI Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

SBI Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is a one‑year apprenticeship training programme under the Apprenticeship Act 1961 for engagement of 7,150 apprentices in State Bank of India branches and offices across various States and Union Territories. It is a training position with a fixed monthly stipend of Rs. 15,000 and does not provide any guarantee of permanent employment in SBI after completion of the apprenticeship period.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in SBI Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

State Bank of India has notified a total of 7,150 Apprentice vacancies under Advertisement No. CRPD/APPR/2026‑27/07 for the 2026‑27 apprenticeship batch. These seats are distributed State/UT‑wise and category‑wise, and each candidate can apply for engagement in only one State/UT as per the detailed notification.

Q3. What are the important dates for SBI Apprentice 2026?

As per the short notification, the SBI Apprentice 2026 online application process starts on 19 May 2026 and the last date to apply online and pay fees is 08 June 2026. The online examination date and call letter download schedule will be notified separately on the official SBI careers portal under the “Current Openings” section.

Q4. What is the eligibility criteria for SBI Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must have a Graduation degree in any discipline from a recognized University or an equivalent qualification recognized by the Central Government as on 08‑06‑2026. Applicants must also be proficient in the specified local language of the chosen State/UT (reading, writing, speaking and understanding), and those having 10th/12th mark sheet showing study of that local language may be exempted from the language test.

Q5. What is the age limit for SBI Apprentice 2026 and what are the relaxations?

The age limit for SBI Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is 20 to 28 years as on 08‑06‑2026, meaning candidates must have been born between 09‑06‑1998 and 08‑06‑2006 (both days inclusive). Upper age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per rules – 5 years for SC/ST, 3 years for OBC (Non‑Creamy Layer), 10/13/15 years for PwBD (UR/OBC/SC‑ST respectively), and additional relaxation for Ex‑Servicemen as per Government norms.

Q6. What is the selection process for SBI Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

The SBI Apprentice selection process comprises an Online Written Examination followed by a Test of Local Language, Document Verification and Medical fitness. Candidates must qualify the online exam and the local language test (unless exempted), and then clear document and medical verification to be considered for engagement as apprentices, keeping in mind that this does not assure any future SBI job.

Q7. What is the exam pattern and negative marking for SBI Apprentice 2026 online test?

The SBI Apprentice 2026 online written exam is an objective‑type test consisting of 100 questions for 100 marks with a total duration of 60 minutes, divided into four sections of 15 minutes each. There is negative marking of 1/4th of the marks assigned to a question for every wrong answer, and the sections are – Reasoning Ability & Computer Aptitude, Quantitative Aptitude, General English and General/Financial Awareness.

Q8. What is the application fee for SBI Apprentice Recruitment 2026 and how can it be paid?

The application fee for SBI Apprentice 2026 is Rs. 300 for all candidates except SC/ST/PwBD/Ex‑Servicemen, who are exempted from paying any fee. The fee can be paid online only using debit card, credit card, net banking or UPI during the application process on the official SBI careers portal.
