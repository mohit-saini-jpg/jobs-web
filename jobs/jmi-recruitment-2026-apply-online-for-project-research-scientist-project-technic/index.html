Qualification / Eligibility

Project Research Scientist-I (Non-Medical): First Class Postgraduate degree (including the integrated PG degrees) in Life Sciences/Environmental Science/Zoology/Biotechnology/Biosciences or any field of Life Sciences. Project Technical Support-III: Three-year graduate degree in a relevant subject/field plus three years of post-qualification experience OR a Postgraduate degree in a relevant subject/field. Project Technical Support-II: 12th pass in Science with a Diploma (MLT/DMLT/Engineering or equivalent) plus 5 years of experience in a relevant subject/field OR a Three-year graduate degree in a relevant subject/field plus 2 years of experience in a relevant subject/field.
