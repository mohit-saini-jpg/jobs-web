How to Download BSEB Compartment Hall Ticket 2026

  • Visit the official BSEB website: Click Here.
  • Click on "Compartment Exam 2026" or "Hall Ticket" link under 10th/12th section.
  • Enter your roll number, registration number, and date of birth.
  • Verify with captcha and submit.
  • Download and print the admit card-carry it with ID to the exam center.

Hall tickets are typically available 10-15 days before exams, so check regularly.

BSEB Compartment Exam Eligibility Criteria

  • You qualify if you failed in one or two subjects in the main BSEB 10th/12th exams (2026).
  • Must have appeared in the regular exam; no eligibility for absentees without valid reason.
  • Maximum two attempts for compartment; apply within the notified window (usually post-results).
  • Fee: Around ₹200-500 per subject (confirm on official site).
  • No eligibility if you've already passed via previous compartments.

Contact BSEB helpline for case-specific queries.

Important Instructions for BSEB Compartment Exams 2026

  • Arrive 30 minutes early with hall ticket and photo ID (Aadhaar preferred).
  • No electronic devices allowed; black/blue pen only.
  • Exams at designated centers-check your admit card for location.
  • In case of clash, prioritize first sitting; report issues to invigilator.
  • Results expected 4-6 weeks post-exams; passing marks same as main board (33%).

Prepare by focusing on failed subjects and practicing past papers.
