EPI Graduate Apprentice Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Engineering Projects India (EPI) has officially released the Graduate Apprentice Recruitment 2026 notification (Advt. No. EPI/WRO/HRM/APT/001-01/2026) for 1 vacancy. Eligible BE/B.Tech candidates in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, or Instrumentation disciplines with a minimum of 55% marks and aged between 18 to 30 years (with applicable relaxation for SC/ST/OBC) can apply from the date mentioned in the notification to 06-07-2026 via email as per the official portal. Selected candidates will be appointed on an apprenticeship basis with a monthly stipend of ₹10,000.

EPI Graduate Apprentice Eligibility Criteria 2026

Graduate Apprentice: BE/B.Tech (Civil/Mechanical/Electrical/Instrumentation) with not less than 55% marks from a reputed Institute/University approved by AICTE. Candidates should not have completed three years after passing out of the qualifying examination as on 30th June 2025. Candidates awaiting results are not eligible. Candidates who have undergone apprentice training in any organization are not eligible.

EPI Graduate Apprentice Application Fee 2026

No application fee is mentioned in the official notification.

EPI Graduate Apprentice Age Limit 2026

Minimum Age: 18 years Maximum Age: 30 years Age Relaxation: 05 years for SC/ST and 03 years for OBC (Non Creamy Layer) candidates.

EPI Graduate Apprentice Salary 2026

Rs. 10,000/- (Ten Thousand only) per month (Consolidated) shall be paid as stipend during the Apprenticeship training. They shall not be paid any output bonus or other incentives or any perks and allowances.
