Details

Public Health Nurse (PHN): All India Senior School Certificate Examination (10+2) or equivalent. Diploma in General Nursing and Midwifery (3 1/2 years). One year Diploma in Public Health Nursing or Basic B.Sc. (Nursing) degree (4 year) from a recognized university or board and registered with the Nursing Council of India. TBHV-NTEP: Graduate in science OR Intermediate (10+2) in science and experience of working as MPW/LHV/ANM/Health worker/Certificate OR Higher course in Health Education/Counselling OR Tuberculosis health visitor's recognized course. Certificate course in computer operations (minimum two months). Preferential: Training course for MPW or recognized sanitary inspector course.
