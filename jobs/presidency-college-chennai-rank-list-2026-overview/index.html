Presidency College Chennai has officially released the TNGASA UG Rank List 2026 on June 3, 2026. Students who applied for admission through Tamil Nadu Government Arts and Science Colleges Admission (TNGASA) 2026 can now check their rank and merit position for undergraduate courses. The merit list is prepared based on Class 12 marks for both General and Special quota categories.

This prestigious 180+ year old autonomous institution is one of the most sought-after government colleges in Tamil Nadu. Check your name in the Presidency College Rank List 2026, download the PDF, and prepare for counselling starting June 5 (Special Quota) and June 8 (General Category).

How to Download the Rank List:

  • Visit the official website — pcc.ac.in
  • Navigate to the Admissions section
  • Click on the Rank List / Merit List 2026 link
  • Select your category (General/Special) and course if applicable
  • Download the PDF and search using your application number or name

Tip for Users: Save the PDF immediately and check for your registration number. Report any discrepancies to the college admission office promptly.

Details Mentioned in Presidency College Rank List 2026

The merit list typically includes:

  • Registration Number
  • Application ID
  • Candidate’s Name
  • Category (Community)
  • Subject/Program Applied
  • Marks Obtained (Class 12)
  • Rank Position
  • Admitted Category (if applicable)

Pro Tip: Cross-verify all details. Errors in category or marks can affect your counseling eligibility.

Factors Determining Presidency College Rank List 2026

  • Class 12 Aggregate Marks (primary criterion)
  • Number of Applicants for each program
  • Seat Availability per course and category
  • Reservation Policies (BC, MBC, SC, ST, EWS, etc.)
  • Special Quota Provisions (sports, ex-servicemen, etc.)
  • Tie-Breaking Rules (usually higher subject marks or age)
