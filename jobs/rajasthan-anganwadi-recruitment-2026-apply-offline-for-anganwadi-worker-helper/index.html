Rajasthan Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The Office of the Deputy Director, Women and Child Development Department (ICDS), Alwar has invited offline applications for vacant Anganwadi Karyakarta (Worker) and Sahayika (Helper) posts under Notice No. 01/2026-27, dated June 27, 2026. These are honorary/voluntary remuneration positions (स्वैच्छिक मानदेय सेवा), filled locally without a written examination, across 10 Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) projects in Alwar district.

Eligibility Criteria

The applicant must be a local resident of the specific revenue village (in rural areas) or ward (in urban areas) that falls within the catchment area of the Anganwadi center for which she is applying. A widowed or divorced woman may be considered a local resident based on either her parental or marital residence, whichever applies. Both married women and women with special eligibility status (such as those belonging to Scheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribe, widowed, divorced, or other specified special categories) are eligible to apply for Anganwadi Karyakarta and Sahayika posts. The applicant must have passed Class 12 (12th standard) as the minimum educational qualification. The maximum age limit for applicants is 35 years. Important Instructions

Email or registered-post applications are not accepted - only in-person or the project's specified email submission (where indicated) is valid. No modification or correction to the application will be accepted once the last date for submission has passed. The complete detailed procedure for filling honorary Anganwadi posts follows Departmental Circular No. 4244 dated 12/01/2023 and the revised guidelines dated 01/03/2023. Selection is based on eligibility, local residency, and category norms - there is no written examination for these posts.

Rajasthan Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 Job Profile

As an Anganwadi Karyakarta (Worker) or Anganwadi Sahayika (Helper), you will be responsible for providing basic services to children and pregnant women in rural areas. Your day-to-day work will involve conducting health check-ups, distributing food and nutrition, and engaging with the community.

Rajasthan Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 Selection Strategy

To get selected, focus on showcasing your community engagement skills, understanding of child development and nutrition, and ability to work in a team.
