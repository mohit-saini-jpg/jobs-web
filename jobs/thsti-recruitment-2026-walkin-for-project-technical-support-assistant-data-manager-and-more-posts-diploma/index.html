Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationPost Graduate Degree in Life Sciences, MBBS/BDS/BVSc, Post Graduate degree in Computer Application/Computer Science/Data Science, 12th in Science with DMLT, 10th with DMLT
DetailsProject Research Scientist-II: Post Graduate Degree, including the integrated PG degree, in Life Sciences from a recognized university with at least three (3) years of post-qualification experience in the relevant field. Project Research Scientist-I: Post Graduate Degree, including the integrated PG degree, in Life Sciences from a recognized university. Project Technical Support-II: 12th in Science with DMLT or equivalent with five (5) years’ of experience in a research laboratory. OR Three-year graduate degree in a relevant subject/field with two (2) years’ experience in a research laboratory. Project Technical Support-I: Standard 10th with DMLT or equivalent with two (2) years’ of experience in a research laboratory. OR Three-year graduate degree in a relevant subject/field with one (1) year of experience in a research laboratory. Assistant Data Manager: Post Graduate degree in Computer Application/Computer Science/Data Science from a recognized university with at least two (2) years of post-qualification work experience in clinical data management. OR Graduate degree in Computer Science/Computer Application from a recognized university with at least four (4) years of post-qualification work experience in clinical data management. OR Graduate degree in any discipline with a computer diploma (duration of diploma should be at least 6 months) from a recognized Institute/University with at least five (5) years of post-qualification work experience in clinical data management. Project Research Scientist-I (Medical): MBBS/BDS/BVSc or equivalent degree from a recognised university with at least three (3) years of post-qualification experience working with Randomized Controlled Trials in cardiovascular health and diseases.

FAQs

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies announced by THSTI?
THSTI has announced a total of 7 vacancies for various posts through this recruitment drive. Interested candidates can appear for the walk-in interview.
Q2. What is the application mode for THSTI Recruitment 2026?
The application mode for this recruitment is Walk-in. Candidates need to attend the interview directly at the specified venue and time.
Q3. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview?
The walk-in interviews are scheduled on different dates for different posts, with the last one being on 05 June 2026 for Project Research Scientist-II.
Q4. What is the maximum age limit for the posts?
The maximum age limit for all the posts is 40 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per government rules.
Q5. Is there any application fee for THSTI Recruitment 2026?
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q6. What are the educational qualifications required for the posts?
Educational qualifications vary by post, ranging from 10th pass with DMLT to Post Graduate degrees in Life Sciences, Computer Applications, or MBBS/BDS/BVSc.
Q7. What is the salary structure for the selected candidates?
The salary ranges from Rs. 18,000/- plus HRA to Rs. 67,000/- plus HRA per month, depending on the post and experience.
Q8. Where is the location of the THSTI institute?
The Translational Health Science and Technology Institute (THSTI) is located in Faridabad, Haryana, with a pincode of 121001.
Q9. What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
Candidates should carry all original documents, one set of self-attested photocopies, ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates, and category certificates if applicable.
Q10. What is the selection process for these posts?
The selection process typically involves a Written Test, Skill Test, and Interview, as indicated in the recruitment details.
