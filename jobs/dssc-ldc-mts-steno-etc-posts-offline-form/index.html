Post Wise Qualification

  • Lower Division Clerk (LDC) : 12th Passed, Typing (English : 35 wpm/ Hindi : 30 wpm).
  • Civilian Motor Driver (Ordinary Grade) : 12th Passed and HMV Driving License, 02 Years Experience.
  • Multi Tasking Staff (Office and Training) : 10th Class Passed.
  • Technical Attendant (Printing Machine Operator) : ITI in Printing Machine Operator OR 10th Passed with 01 Year Experience.
  • Stenographer : 12th Passed and Stenography [(Dictation (10 Mins : 80 wpm), Transcription (English : 50 Mins, Hindi : 65 Mins)].
  • Supervisor (Networking) : B.Tech / Master Degree in Computer Application or Computer Science or Information Technology, 02 Years Experience.
  • Network Administrator : 12th Passed with Diploma in Networking OR Bachelor Degree in Computer Application, Certificate in Cisco Certified Network Associate and Cisco Certified Network professional and 02 Years Experience.
  • Hardware Administrator : 12th Passed with Diploma in Computer Hardware OR Bachelor Degree in Computer Application, Certification in Very Large Scale Integration and 02 Years Experience.
  • Lineman : 12th Passed with Diploma in Optical Fiber Cable Line Management and 02 Years Experience.
  • Boat Builder : 12th Passed and 02 Years Experience.
