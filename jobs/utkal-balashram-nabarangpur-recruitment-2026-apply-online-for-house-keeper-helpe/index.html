FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for the Utkal Balashram Nabarangpur recruitment?
A
The last date to apply online for the Utkal Balashram Nabarangpur recruitment is 15th June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline.
Q2How many vacancies are available in this recruitment?
A
There are a total of 4 vacancies announced for various posts including Store Keeper-cum-Accountant, Art & Craft-cum-Music Teacher, House Keeper, and Helper-cum-Night Watchman.
Q3What is the minimum and maximum age limit for the applicants?
A
The minimum age limit for all posts is 21 years, and the maximum age limit is 45 years as of 1st April 2026.
Q4What are the educational qualifications required for these posts?
A
Qualifications vary by post, ranging from Post Graduate degrees in Commerce/MBA for Store Keeper-cum-Accountant, Bachelor's or Diploma in Art & Craft for the teacher post, to Under Matric (8th to 10th Fail) for House Keeper and Helper-cum-Night Watchman.
Q5Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) in the official notification.
Q6What is the salary structure for the selected candidates?
A
The salary ranges from Rs. 7,944/- per month for House Keeper and Helper-cum-Night Watchman to Rs. 18,536/- per month for Store Keeper-cum-Accountant.
Q7What is the selection process for these positions?
A
The selection process will be based on Career Marking, Merit List preparation, and Certificate Verification.
Q8What is the mode of application for this recruitment?
A
Applications must be submitted online through the official website or the provided application link.
Q9Are there any preferred experiences mentioned for the posts?
A
Yes, for most posts, 3 years of experience is preferred, although not strictly mandatory for all.
Q10Where can I find the official notification and apply online?

The official notification PDF and the online application link can be found in the "Important Links" section of this article.
