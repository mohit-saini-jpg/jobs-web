Andaman & Nicobar Police Constable Recruitment 2026 Notification out - Apply Online for 495 Group B (NG) & Group C Posts

Government of India

Posts: Sub-Inspector (Executive/PMT/FS/Master/GD), Constable (Executive/Bandsmen/GD/Wireless/Driver etc.), Radio Operator (ASI), Radio Technician (ASI), Firemen, Follower posts & others Last Date: 26 May 2026 10-06-2026 Online

Short Information

Andaman & Nicobar Police has released the official Group-B (Non-Gazetted) & Group-C Recruitment Notification 2026 in April 2026 at its official website. A total of 495 (tentative) vacancies have been announced under the A&N Police Group-B (NG) & Group-C Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged between 18 to 30/33 years (varies by post) with qualifications ranging from 10th, 12th, to Graduation can apply online from 27 April 2026 to 26 May 2026. The official notification PDF and online application link are available below.

Selection Process

  • Physical Measurement and Endurance Test (PM&ET): Qualifying in nature (Height
  • Chest for males
  • Race
  • Long Jump
  • High Jump
  • Swimming for some posts). Written Examination: Objective type (Graduate / Higher Secondary / Matric Level) – Common + Additional papers as per post. Negative marking of 0.25. Trade Test / Skill Test (wherever applicable): Qualifying in nature for technical/driver/bandsmen/marine posts. Document Verification Medical Examination Final merit based on performance in Written Exam + Trade Test (subject to qualifying PM&ET and other conditions).

How To Apply

  • Apply online only via official A&N Police website.
  • Fill details, upload documents/photo/signature, submit.
  • Print application for records.
  • Departmental candidates must route through proper channel with NOC.
  • Keep email/mobile active.
  • Last date: As notified (closing date 26/05/2026 mentioned in document)

Important Instructions

  • Read notification carefully before applying. Ensure eligibility (age, education, physical standards, driving license, trade skills) as on 17/05/2026. Some posts are open only for Male candidates. PM&ET and Trade Test are qualifying in nature. Govt. strives for gender balance; women candidates are encouraged to apply. No TA/DA for tests. SSC / A&N Police reserves right to modify/cancel the recruitment. Check official website regularly for updates on exam dates, admit cards, etc.
