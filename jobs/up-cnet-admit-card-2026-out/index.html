UP CNET Admit Card 2026 Out

Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical University (ABVMU), Uttar Pradesh has released the UP CNET Admit Card 2026 for the Common Nursing Entrance Test. Registered candidates can now download their hall ticket online from the official portal abvmucet26.co.in using their login credentials.

The admit card is mandatory for entry to the examination hall, and candidates should verify all details immediately after downloading it.

How to Download UP CNET Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official website abvmucet26.co.in.
  • Open the UP CNET 2026 Admit Card link.
  • Enter your Registration ID and Password.
  • Submit the details.
  • Your UP CNET Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download the PDF and take 2–3 printed copies.

If the website is slow, try again after some time.

Details Mentioned on the Admit Card

Your UP CNET hall ticket may include:

  • Candidate’s name.
  • Roll number / registration ID.
  • Exam name.
  • Exam date and reporting time.
  • Exam centre name and address.
  • Candidate photograph and signature.
  • Exam-day instructions.

Check all details carefully. If there is any mismatch, contact the official helpdesk immediately.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the UP CNET Admit Card 2026.
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence or Passport.
  • Passport-size photographs, if mentioned in the instructions.

Without the admit card and valid ID, entry may not be allowed.

Exam-Day Instructions

  • Reach the centre well before the reporting time.
  • Carry only permitted items.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators, or any electronic devices.
  • Follow all instructions given by invigilators.
  • Keep the admit card safe even after the exam.
