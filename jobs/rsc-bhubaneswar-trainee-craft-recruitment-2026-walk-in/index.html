Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is RSC Bhubaneswar Trainee (Crafts) Recruitment 2026?

RSC Bhubaneswar Trainee (Crafts) Recruitment 2026 is a walk‑in engagement drive for ITI‑qualified candidates in Electrical, Carpenter, Painter and Turner trades. Regional Science Centre, Bhubaneswar (under NCSM, Ministry of Culture) is offering one‑year traineeship, extendable by another year based on performance.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in RSC Bhubaneswar Trainee Recruitment 2026?

There are a total of 4 Trainee (Crafts) vacancies in RSC Bhubaneswar Recruitment 2026. One post is available in each trade: Electrical, Carpenter, Painter and Turner.

Q3. What is the eligibility criteria for RSC Bhubaneswar Trainee (Crafts) 2026?

Candidates must have passed SSC/Matriculation along with ITI in the relevant trade from a Government‑recognized institution. Only those who have completed ITI in or after 2023 in the respective trade (Electrical, Carpenter, Painter or Turner) are eligible to appear.

Q4. What is the date and venue for the RSC Bhubaneswar Trainee 2026 Walk‑in Test?

The Walk‑in Aptitude Test and Trade Test will be conducted on 02‑06‑2026. The venue is Industrial Training Institute, Cuttack, At/Po, Dist, Madhu Patna Colony, Cuttack, Odisha – 753010.

Q5. What is the reporting time and schedule for the selection process?

Candidates must report for document verification from 09:00 AM onwards, and the last entry allowed is 09:30 AM. The Aptitude (Written) Test will start at 10:00 AM, followed by the Trade Test for candidates who qualify the written test.

Q6. What is the stipend and duration for RSC Bhubaneswar Trainee (Crafts) posts?

Selected Trainee (Crafts) candidates will receive a consolidated stipend of Rs. 22,000 per month. The duration of traineeship is 1 year, which may be extended for another year subject to satisfactory performance and requirement.

Q7. Is there any age limit for RSC Bhubaneswar Trainee Recruitment 2026?

The advertisement does not specify a clear age limit for Trainee (Crafts) posts. Candidates are advised to check the detailed advertisement or confirm at the time of Walk‑in for any age‑related conditions.

Q8. What is the selection process for RSC Bhubaneswar Trainee (Crafts) 2026?

Selection will be done through a Walk‑in Aptitude Test followed by a Trade Test. Only those candidates who qualify the written Aptitude Test will be called for the Trade Test in their respective trade on the same day.
