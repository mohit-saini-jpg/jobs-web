Minimum 2 years experience in managerial position dealing with tourism activities in a Tourism Organization / Hotel. For Deputation: Officers from Central/State Govt/UT/PSU/Autonomous bodies/Tourism Dev. Corporations holding analogous post on regular basis OR 5 years regular service in Level-9 (Rs.53100-167800) OR 7 years regular service in Level-7 (Rs.44900-142400).
