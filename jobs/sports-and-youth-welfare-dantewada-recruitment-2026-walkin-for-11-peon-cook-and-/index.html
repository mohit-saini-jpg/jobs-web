Qualification / Eligibility

Sports Coordinator: Graduate in Journalism (Sports) or Mass Communication from a recognized university. Preference for experience in sports journalism in media institutions, sports federations, academies or sports departments. Kabaddi Instructor: NIS or equivalent one-year full-time NIS diploma and MPED from a recognized institution. International players given preference. Cricket Instructor: NIS or equivalent one-year full-time NIS diploma and MPED from a recognized institution. International players given preference. Kayaking Instructor: NIS or equivalent one-year full-time NIS diploma and MPED from a recognized institution. International players given preference. Physiotherapist: Bachelor of Physiotherapy from a recognized university, UGC recognized Physiotherapy degree, state license, physically fit. Superintendent (Girls): Graduate from a recognized university. One-year diploma/certificate in Data Entry Operator/Programming from a recognized institution. Computer test will be conducted. Peon (Collector rate): Passed 12th class. Proficient in office work. Preference to experienced candidates. Cook (Collector rate): Passed 12th class. Proficient in office work. Skilled in cooking. Physical test will be conducted. Preference to experienced candidates.
