Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for SSB recruitment?

The walk-in interviews are scheduled on different dates for various locations: 25th May 2026 in Siliguri, 28th May 2026 in Guwahati, 1st June 2026 in Lucknow, and 4th June 2026 in Patna.

Q2. What is the total number of vacancies announced by SSB?

Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB) has announced a total of 35 vacancies for GDMOs & Specialist Doctors posts.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Specialist Doctor posts?

Candidates must possess a recognized medical qualification and a Post-Graduate Degree/Diploma in the concerned specialty, along with relevant experience.

Q4. What is the age limit for applying to these SSB posts?

The maximum age limit for all posts is 70 years.

Q5. What is the salary offered for the GDMO and Specialist Doctor positions?

The selected candidates will receive a monthly salary ranging from Rs. 75,000/- to Rs. 85,000/-.

Q6. Is there any application fee for SSB recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) in the official notification.

Q7. What is the selection process for the SSB GDMOs & Specialist Doctors posts?

The selection process will involve a Walk-in Interview followed by a Medical Examination.

Q8. What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?

You should carry all original documents and one set of self-attested photocopies, including ID proof, educational certificates, and experience certificates.
