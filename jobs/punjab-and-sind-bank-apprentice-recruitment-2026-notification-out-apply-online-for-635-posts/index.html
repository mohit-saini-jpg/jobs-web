Selection Process

  • Selection for engagement of apprentices will be merit-based on HSC/10+2 marks. No written examination is conducted. Final selection is subject to verification of eligibility

  • district-wise vacancy availability

  • and medical fitness. In case of a tie in marks

  • candidates will be ranked by age in descending order within their respective category

Salary / Pay Scale

Pay Scale

Rs. 12,300/- per month (no daily allowance / conveyance / travelling allowance)

Physical Eligibility

Details

The competition level for PSB Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is expected to be moderate to high. With only 635 seats spread across 29 States/UTs, the vacancy count is significantly lower than comparable bank apprenticeship drives such as SBI. Selection is purely merit-based on 10+2 marks without any written examination, which means candidates with high HSC scores - especially from states like Uttar Pradesh (100 seats), Punjab (90 seats), Bihar (40 seats), Maharashtra (30 seats), and Haryana (50 seats) - will face intense competition within their district and category. | Since there is no written exam, the effective competition depends entirely on the candidate's 10+2 percentage and age as a tie-breaker. Attrition at later stages will occur through local language proof verification, document verification, and medical fitness, which will reduce the final eligible pool. Candidates who do not have proof of studying the local language at 8th/10th/12th level are at a disadvantage as they must demonstrate local language proficiency separately.

How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply online through the Bank's website (https://punjabandsind.bank.in/) only. No other mode of application will be accepted. Before applying, candidates must mandatorily complete 100% registration on both the NAPS portal (https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in/) and the NATS portal (https://nats.education.gov.in/). Candidates must also note down their Enrollment ID generated on the NATS portal as it is required while applying.

  • Documents Required to be Scanned and Uploaded:

  • Recent passport-size colour photograph (to be signed across by the candidate on printing)

  • Signature with black ink on white paper (Signatures in CAPITAL LETTERS will not be accepted)

Important Instructions

  • THIS IS NOT AN EMPLOYMENT IN THE BANK. Candidates engaged as Apprentice will NOT be treated as "employees" of the Bank and will not be entitled to receive any benefits available for Bank employees. The Bank shall have no obligation to offer regular employment to any Apprentice who has completed the period of training, nor shall the Apprentice be obligated to accept employment. The Apprentice shall have no right to claim employment in the Bank after completion of training. Candidates can apply for only one State and one District, and not more than one application should be submitted. In case of multiple applications, only the last valid (completed) application will be retained and fees for other registrations will stand forfeited. Mandatory registration with 100% completed profile on both NAPS and NATS portals before applying on the Bank's website is required. Candidates who had training or job experience of one or more years after completion of educational qualifications shall not be eligible for engagement as apprentice. OBC candidates belonging to the "Creamy Layer" are not entitled to reservation/relaxation; they should apply as General/UR. Change of address / email ID / phone number / caste / category will not be entertained after submission of the application. Signatures in CAPITAL LETTERS will not be acceptable. The signature must be consistent across all documents and correspondence. No daily allowance, conveyance allowance or travelling allowance will be paid to Apprentices. No overtime is permitted. Apprentices are entitled to only 1 casual leave per month (maximum 4 at a time). The Contract of Apprenticeship shall terminate automatically on expiry of the 12-month training period. A non-performing apprentice will be given 7 days' notice. An apprentice wishing to terminate early must give 15 days' prior notice to the Bank. Apprentices must devote their entire time exclusively to the Bank and shall not engage in any other commercial business, service, or advisory activity during the engagement period. Apprentices must not disclose any financial information, data, concepts or internal communications relating to the Bank's business to any unauthorized person. Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification. Any resultant dispute arising out of this advertisement shall be subject to the sole jurisdiction of the Courts situated at Delhi. Bank reserves the right to change/add/cancel the selection process or cancel the entire process at any stage without notice or refund of application fees. All revisions/corrigendum (if any) will be hosted on the Bank's website only - candidates should visit regularly.
