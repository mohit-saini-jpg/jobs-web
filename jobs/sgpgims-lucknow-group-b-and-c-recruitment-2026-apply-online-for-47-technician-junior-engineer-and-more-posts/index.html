How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply **online only** through the official website http://www.sgpgims.org.in. No other mode of application will be accepted. Candidates can apply for multiple posts but must submit separate applications and pay separate fees for each post.
  • Before filling the Online Application, candidates must keep ready the following documents:
  • Recent Scanned Colored Photograph (JPG/JPEG, max 80KB)
  • Scanned Signature (JPG/JPEG, max 80KB)
  • Valid Email ID and Mobile Number (must remain active till recruitment process ends)
  • Scanned copies of Mark sheets & Certificates for 10th, 12th, Diploma, Graduation, Post-Graduation etc. in PDF format
  • Scanned copy of Caste/Category Certificate, Sub-Category, Domicile Certificate (if applicable) & Experience Certificate (if applicable) in PDF format
  • Visit the official website: https://www.sgpgims.org.in
  • Go through Advertisement, How to Apply, FAQ, and Helpdesk sections.
  • Click on “Registration for New Users” and register with Name, Post Applied, Date of Birth, Email ID, and Mobile Number.
  • After successful registration, you will receive User ID on Email and Password on Mobile.
  • Login using “Sign in for Existing Users”.
  • Fill the Application Form carefully in following sections: Personal Details Communication Details Qualification Details Document Upload & Payment
  • Personal Details
  • Communication Details
  • Qualification Details
  • Document Upload & Payment
  • Upload all required scanned documents in correct format and size.
  • Preview the application, edit if needed, and submit.
  • Pay the application fee through online mode (Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking).
  • After successful payment, take a printout of the application form and note down the Application Sequence Number for future reference.
  • Apply Online at: https://sgpgims.eapplynow.com/SGPGIBL26/Pages/Index.aspx

Important Instructions

  • This is a direct recruitment process. No interview for most posts. Application fee is non-refundable. Reservation as per UP Government rules. All disputes subject to Lucknow Court jurisdiction.
