RITES Recruitment 2026: Selection Process

Selection is entirely through Interview, carrying 100% weightage — Technical & Professional proficiency: 65%, Personality, Communication & Competency: 35%. Minimum qualifying marks in the interview: 60% for UR/EWS candidates and 50% for SC/ST/OBC (NCL)/PWD candidates against reserved posts. There is no minimum qualifying mark required in the aggregate. Eligible candidates who successfully submit the online application are provisionally allowed for Document Scrutiny/Verification; only shortlisted/eligible candidates then appear for the Interview. RITES prepares a category-wise merit list of empanelled candidates and shares CVs with the client in order of merit. The final offer of engagement depends on approval of the CV by the client.
