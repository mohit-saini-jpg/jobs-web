How to Download Kerala University Time Table 2026

Students can download the Kerala University Time Table 2026 from the official examination portal by following the steps given below:

  • Locate the link for Sixth Semester Unitary LLB Viva-Voce April 2026.
  • Click on the relevant PDF link.
