Selection Process

  • Merit Basis Details
  • Primary Merit (i) Merit based on qualifying GATE/NET scores from 2023 examination or later
  • Secondary Merit (ii) If sufficient candidates are not available under Merit (i): merit based on marks obtained in the qualifying examination passed in year 2023 or later
  • Trainees will be selected on the basis of merit prepared as follows. The final Merit List shall be prepared by first placing GATE/NET qualified candidates in order of merit
  • followed by candidates in the merit list based on marks obtained in the qualifying exam.
  • A panel of wait-listed candidates shall be prepared for considering their engagement depending upon vacancies arising in the respective categories. The panel shall be valid for one period of one year from the date of final result on the DPCC website by way of office order.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only through the website https://dpcc.delhi.gov.in as per the link provided therein. Application in physical form or any other form will not be accepted/entertained and will be summarily rejected.
  • No correspondence will be entertained in this regard. Candidates must attach copies of GATE/NET marks obtained and the qualifying examination marksheets (passed in year 2023 or later) with the application.
  • Valid Caste Certificate must be attached along with the application for availing reservation under the respective reserved categories.
  • Last Date Note: The last date of submission of the online application will be 21 days from the date of advertisement (inclusive of the date of publication) uploaded on the DPCC website.
  • Apply Online at: https://dpcc.delhi.gov.in
