How To Check Munger University UG CBCS Sem 1 Result 2026

Follow the step-by-step process below to check and download Munger University UG CBCS Semester 1 Result 2026 for Session 2025-29.

  • Visit the official Munger University result page at https://mungeruniversity.ac.in/page/Result or directly go to the result portal https://munivexam.in/.
  • On the result page, look for the link mentioning "UG CBCS Sem-I Session 2025-2029 Result Exam - 2025" and click on it.
  • You will be redirected to the UG CBCS Semester 1 result portal where you need to select your college from the list provided.
  • Enter your roll number in the specified field correctly as printed on your admit card.
  • After entering the details, click on the View Result or Submit button to fetch your UG CBCS Sem 1 result.
  • Your Munger University UG CBCS Semester 1 (2025-29) result will appear on the screen displaying your name, roll number, college, course, subject-wise marks and result status.
  • Download the marksheet in PDF format or take a printout and preserve it for future academic and admission purposes, and refer to the official notice PDF if you need more clarification about the result publication.
