FAQs

Q1What is BTSC Food Analyst Recruitment 2026?
A
BTSC Food Analyst Recruitment 2026 is a recruitment drive by the Bihar Technical Service Commission under Advertisement No. 24/2026 to fill 11 Food Analyst posts in the Bihar Food Protection Service, Health Department, Bihar.
Q2How many vacancies are available in BTSC Food Analyst Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 11 vacancies have been announced: UR-02, SC-01, BC-02, EBC-03, EWS-00, with 35% horizontal reservation for women (3 posts) across categories.
Q3What is the last date to apply for BTSC Food Analyst 2026?
A
The last date for online application submission and certificate upload is 19 June 2026, with applications opening on 20 May 2026.
Q4What is the eligibility for BTSC Food Analyst 2026?
A
Candidates must have a Bachelor's/Master's/Doctorate degree in Chemistry, Microbiology, Food Technology, Biotechnology, Agricultural Science, Veterinary Science, or allied subjects with minimum 3 years experience in food analysis, AND must have passed the FSSAI Food Analyst examination.
Q5What is the age limit for BTSC Food Analyst 2026?
A
Minimum age is 21 years as on 01 August 2026. Maximum age is 37 years for General (Male), 40 years for UR Women/BC/EBC, 42 years for SC/ST, with 10 years relaxation for Divyang candidates.
Q6What is the selection process for BTSC Food Analyst 2026?
A
The selection process includes a Computer Based Test (CBT) of 100 marks with 2 hours duration, followed by document verification, with minimum qualifying marks of 40% for UR, 36.5% for BC, 34% for EBC, and 32% for SC/ST/Women/Divyang.
Q7Is there any application fee for BTSC Food Analyst 2026?
A
Yes, a non-refundable application fee of ₹100 must be paid online through Debit Card/Credit Card/Internet Banking/UPI for all candidates.
Q8What is the salary for BTSC Food Analyst 2026?
A
The pay scale is Revised Pay Structure Level-09 (Pre-revised Pay Band-2: ₹9,300–34,800 with Grade Pay ₹5,400), as per Bihar Government norms.
Q9What is the negative marking pattern for BTSC Food Analyst 2026?
A
Each correct answer carries +1 mark, while each wrong answer attracts -0.25 marks negative marking in the 100-mark Computer Based Test.
Q10How to apply for BTSC Food Analyst 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online only through the official BTSC website btsc.bihar.gov.in from 20 May 2026 to 19 June 2026 by completing registration, uploading required documents including FSSAI Food Analyst certificate, and paying the application fee.
