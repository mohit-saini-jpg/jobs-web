Selection Process – APEPDCL AEE Recruitment 2026

The APEPDCL AEE 2026 selection is entirely based on a Computer Based Test (CBT) - there is no interview stage. The exam will be conducted jointly for APTRANSCO, APEPDCL, APCPDCL, APSPDCL, and APGENCO, and candidates selected will be allotted to a utility based on merit, reservation, and the preferences indicated in their application. There are no negative marks in the CBT. The CBT consists of 100 questions for 100 marks - the same pattern applies to all three posts (Electrical, Telecom, Civil). The question paper is divided into Core subject questions (70 marks) and General sections (30 marks combined). Minimum qualifying marks must be secured to be considered for certificate verification. Candidates should note that merely securing minimum qualifying marks does not guarantee a call for certificate verification. APEPDCL AEE Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1The APEPDCL AEE Apply Online 2026 process is entirely online through the official portal. Candidates must use an Aadhaar-linked mobile number for OTP-based registration - the same mobile number will be used throughout the examination process. The application portal is available at apeasternpower.com or apvidyutrecruitment.apcfss.in from 30 June 2026 at 10:00 Hrs.
  • 2Visit the official portal: apeasternpower.com or apvidyutrecruitment.apcfss.in.
  • 3Click on the New Registration link and register using your Aadhaar-linked mobile number. An OTP will be sent to this number for verification.
  • 4After successful registration, log in using the credentials (Registration ID and password). Note these down carefully - a retrieval option is available if credentials are lost.
  • 5Fill in the online application form with all required personal, educational, and category details.
  • 6Indicate your preference for Power Utility (APEPDCL, APTRANSCO, APCPDCL, APSPDCL, APGENCO) and Zone/Unit - these preferences are final and cannot be changed after submission.
  • 7Upload scanned copies of all required documents and certificates. Illegible or tampered documents will lead to rejection. Hall Ticket will not be generated if certificates are not uploaded.
  • 8Upload a recent passport-size photograph and scanned signature.
  • 9Pay the application fee of ₹1,000 through the Payment Gateway. Note the Journal Number issued upon payment.
  • 10Complete the application form submission. A Journal Number alone does not confirm submission - complete all steps including document upload and form fill-up.
  • 11Take a printout of the final submitted application for future reference. No fields in the application form should be left blank.
  • 12Important: Candidates should apply well before the last date to avoid last-day technical rush. Applications sent through any mode other than online will not be entertained.
