How to Apply

  • 1The APEPDCL AEE Apply Online 2026 process is entirely online through the official portal. Candidates must use an Aadhaar-linked mobile number for OTP-based registration - the same mobile number will be used throughout the examination process. The application portal is available at apeasternpower.com or apvidyutrecruitment.apcfss.in from 30 June 2026 at 10:00 Hrs.
  • 2Visit the official portal: apeasternpower.com or apvidyutrecruitment.apcfss.in.
  • 3Click on the New Registration link and register using your Aadhaar-linked mobile number. An OTP will be sent to this number for verification.
  • 4After successful registration, log in using the credentials (Registration ID and password). Note these down carefully - a retrieval option is available if credentials are lost.
  • 5Fill in the online application form with all required personal, educational, and category details.
  • 6Indicate your preference for Power Utility (APEPDCL, APTRANSCO, APCPDCL, APSPDCL, APGENCO) and Zone/Unit - these preferences are final and cannot be changed after submission.
  • 7Upload scanned copies of all required documents and certificates. Illegible or tampered documents will lead to rejection. Hall Ticket will not be generated if certificates are not uploaded.
  • 8Upload a recent passport-size photograph and scanned signature.
  • 9Pay the application fee of ₹1,000 through the Payment Gateway. Note the Journal Number issued upon payment.
  • 10Complete the application form submission. A Journal Number alone does not confirm submission - complete all steps including document upload and form fill-up.
  • 11Take a printout of the final submitted application for future reference. No fields in the application form should be left blank.
  • 12Important: Candidates should apply well before the last date to avoid last-day technical rush. Applications sent through any mode other than online will not be entertained. Candidates submitting false information or fake certificates are liable for criminal prosecution and cancellation of selection.

FAQs

Q1Has APEPDCL officially released the AEE Recruitment Notification 2026?
A
Yes, APEPDCL officially released the Assistant Executive Engineer Recruitment 2026 notification on 25 June 2026 for 135 vacancies (Electrical: 128, Telecom: 3, Civil: 4). The notification was signed by Chairman and Managing Director Prudhvi Tej Immadi, I.A.S., from Visakhapatnam.
Q2What is the last date to apply for APEPDCL AEE 2026?
A
The last date to apply online for APEPDCL AEE Recruitment 2026 is 20 July 2026 at 23:59 Hrs. The same deadline applies for fee payment and certificate upload. The application portal opens on 30 June 2026 at 10:00 Hrs.
Q3How many vacancies are available in APEPDCL AEE Vacancy 2026?
A
A total of 135 vacancies are available - 128 for AEE (Electrical), 3 for AEE (Telecom), and 4 for AEE (Civil). These vacancies are provisional and may change based on administrative requirements.
Q4What is the educational qualification required for APEPDCL AEE 2026?
A
Candidates must hold a Degree in the relevant engineering discipline — Electrical / Electrical & Electronics for the Electrical post; Telecom / Electronics / ECE for the Telecom post; and Civil Engineering for the Civil post — from a university recognised by AICTE and UGC. Distance Mode degrees from non-AP state universities are not valid.
Q5What is the age limit for APEPDCL AEE Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 42 years as on 01.07.2026. SC/ST/BC/EWS candidates can apply up to 47 years, and Persons with Benchmark Disabilities up to 52 years of age.
Q6What is the application fee for APEPDCL AEE 2026?
A
The application fee is ₹1,000 for all candidates, regardless of category. The fee is non-refundable and must be paid through the Payment Gateway between 30 June 2026 and 20 July 2026.
Q7What is the salary for APEPDCL AEE posts?
A
During the probation period (2 years on duty within 3 years), selected candidates receive a consolidated monthly remuneration of ₹75,000. After successful probation completion, the regular Time Scale of Pay under RPS-2022 (starting at ₹86,245) applies.
Q8What is the selection process for APEPDCL Assistant Executive Engineer 2026?
A
Selection is based solely on a Computer Based Test (CBT) of 100 marks — there is no interview. The exam covers Core subject (70 marks) plus General Intelligence, General Awareness, English, Quantitative Aptitude, and Computer Knowledge (30 marks combined). There are no negative marks.
Q9Where can I apply for APEPDCL AEE Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online at apeasternpower.com or apvidyutrecruitment.apcfss.in from 30 June 2026 at 10:00 Hrs.
Q10When is the APEPDCL AEE exam date 2026?
A
The tentative Computer Based Test (CBT) exam dates are 22 August 2026 to 29 August 2026. Hall Tickets will be available for download 7 days before the exam starts. Online Mock Tests will be available from 15 August 2026 onwards for practice.
