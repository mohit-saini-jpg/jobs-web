TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

Selection will be made through Written Examination (Objective Type) conducted via CBRT (Computer Based Recruitment Test) or OMR-based Offline mode, as decided by the Commission. The mode of examination will be notified through web note/press note and in the Hall Ticket. Final selection is subject to document verification and medical fitness. There shall be no waiting list. Stage 1 – Written Examination (Objective Type) The examination consists of two papers totalling 450 marks. Paper-I is General Studies and General Abilities (150 questions, 150 marks, 150 minutes) in bilingual (English & Telugu). Paper-II is Civil Engineering – Degree Level (150 questions, 300 marks, 150 minutes) in English only. Qualifying marks: OC/Sports/EWS – minimum 40%; BC – minimum 35%; SC/ST/PH – minimum 30%. TGPSC AEE Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Candidates shall apply online through the Commission's website (https://www.tgpsc.gov.in) only. No other mode of application will be accepted. Before applying, candidates must complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the TGPSC website to obtain their TGPSC ID. OTR is not considered an application for the post.
  • 2Step-by-Step Application Process:
  • 3Step I: Register for OTR at https://www.tgpsc.gov.in (if not already registered) to obtain TGPSC ID. Verify all bio-data details carefully.
  • 4Step II: Login using TGPSC ID and Date of Birth, enter OTP received on registered mobile, verify pre-filled details from OTR (Name, DOB, Qualifications, Community, Gender, Disability etc.), and select examination centre.
  • 5Step III: Preview the application for correctness, then click "SAVE & PAYMENT" to proceed to online fee payment via Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card or UPI.
  • 6Step IV: After fee payment, a PDF application will be generated. Download and save the PDF. Note the Reference ID Number for future correspondence.
  • 7Documents/Files Required to be Uploaded:

  • 10PDF copies of certificates as required (educational qualifications, community, OTR documents); certificates already uploaded in OTR need not be uploaded again
