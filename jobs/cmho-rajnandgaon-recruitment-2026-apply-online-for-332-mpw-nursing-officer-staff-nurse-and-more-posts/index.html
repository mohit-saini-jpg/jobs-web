Qualification / Eligibility

DetailsCandidates must fulfill the minimum qualifications specified for each post: | Nursing Officer / Staff Nurse: B.Sc. Nursing / Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing with valid CG Nursing Council registration, OR GNM with valid CG Nursing Council registration. ANM: 12th passed + ANM trained + INC affiliated training centre + live registration in CG Nursing Registration Council. District Account Assistant: B.Com (minimum 55%) + 1-year Computer Diploma (PGDCA); Tally knowledge compulsory. City Accountant: M.Com (Regular, 50%) with Tally 9.0, OR B.Com (Regular, 50%) with Tally 9.0. Block Manager – Account: B.Com (minimum 55%) + 1-year Computer Diploma (PGDCA) + Tally knowledge. Secretarial Assistant / Jr. Sr. Secretary Assistant: 12th passed from recognized institution + 1-year Diploma in Computer Applications. Counselor: PG Degree / Diploma in Psychology / Counselling / Health Education / Mass Communication / Social Science. Radiographer: 12th (Science) + Radiographer/X-Ray Technician Paramedical course from CG Govt. recognized institution + valid CG Paramedical Council registration. MO – AYUSH: BHMS / BAMS / BUMS Degree with registration from GOI recognized Ayush/Homeo/Unani Registration Board. RMA: PMHM (Practitioner in Modern and Holistic Medicine) course passed and registered with Chhattisgarh Medical Board. Pharmacist: B.Pharma OR D.Pharma with valid CG Pharmacy Council registration. OT Technician: 12th (Biology, Chemistry, Physics) + OT Technician Paramedical course + valid CG Paramedical Council registration. Dental Assistant: Matriculation from recognized institution + 2 years experience in dental college/clinic (Govt. health facility preferred); preference for 1-year lab technician / dental technician course. Feeding Demonstrator: BSc Home Science. Physiotherapist: Bachelor's degree in Physiotherapy (B.P.T). Audiologist: Bachelor of Audiology & Speech Language Pathology (BASLP) / B.Sc. (Speech and Hearing) from an RCI recognized institute. Community Health Officer: Certificate Course in Community Health (B.Sc. Nursing Community Health Integrated Course) with valid CG Nursing Council registration, OR Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing Certificate in Community Health Integrated Course with valid CG Nursing Council registration. Social Worker (NMHP): M.Phil in Psychiatric Social Work preferred; otherwise Master of Social Work (Regular) preferred. MPW (Male) – UHWC: 12th (Biology) + Multipurpose Health Worker training (1 year formal or equivalent) recognized by CG Govt. + valid CG Paramedical Council registration. Senior Treatment Supervisor (STS): Bachelor's Degree OR recognized Sanitary Inspector course + Computer Operation Certificate (minimum 2 months) + permanent two-wheeler driving licence. Attendant (HA/SA): 12th Pass. Class IV / Aya Bai / Cook cum Caretaker: 8th Pass.

How To Apply

  • Eligible candidates must complete the online registration process as follows:
  • Visit the official application portal: https://govthealth.cg.gov.in/RecRajnandgaonCont2026
  • Register using your mobile number. Registration number and password will be sent to your mobile.
  • Log in using the registration number and password, then fill in the complete online application form.
  • Pay the application fee by scanning the QR Code provided and enter the Transaction ID in the application form.
  • After final submission, take a printout of the application, sign it, and upload the self-signed copy. Applications without self-signed uploads will not be accepted.
  • Retain the payment receipt printout for document verification purposes.
  • All required documents (educational qualifications, caste, domicile, disability, council registration, experience) must be entered accurately. Experience certificates must be uploaded in the application.
