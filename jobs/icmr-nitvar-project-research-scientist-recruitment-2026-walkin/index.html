Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for ICMR NITVAR Recruitment 2026?

The walk-in interview for the Project Research Scientist posts at ICMR NITVAR is scheduled for 05 June 2026. Candidates should ensure they report to the venue on this date.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for the Project Research Scientist post?

There are a total of 3 vacancies for the Project Research Scientist positions at ICMR NITVAR. These are distributed across different specializations as mentioned in the notification.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Project Research Scientist posts?

The educational qualifications vary for each post. Generally, it includes a Post Graduate Degree in Health Science or related fields with specified years of experience, or a PhD. For the medical post, an MBBS degree with experience or further specialization is required.

Q4. What is the age limit for the candidates applying for this recruitment?

The maximum age limit for all Project Research Scientist posts is 45 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per the guidelines of ICMR.

Q5. What is the salary offered for the Project Research Scientist positions?

The selected candidates will receive a salary ranging from Rs. 67,000 to Rs. 80,000 per month, plus House Rent Allowance (HRA).

Q6. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.

Q7. What is the selection process for the ICMR NITVAR Project Research Scientist posts?

The selection process will primarily involve a Walk-In-Interview. However, a written test may be conducted if more than 30 eligible candidates apply for a particular position.

Q8. Where is the walk-in interview going to be held?

The walk-in interview will be held at the National Institute of Translational Virology and AIDS Research (ICMR NITVAR) in Pune, Maharashtra, with the pincode 411026.
