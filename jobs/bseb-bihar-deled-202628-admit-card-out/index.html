BSEB Bihar D.El.Ed 2026‑28 Admit Card Out

Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB), Patna has released the BSEB Bihar D.El.Ed 2026‑28 admit card for the Diploma in Elementary Education Joint Entrance Examination 2026. As per the official notice and recent updates, the Bihar DElEd admit card 2026 has been issued on 28 May 2026 for candidates who have successfully applied for the 2‑year DElEd course (session 2026‑28).

The Bihar DElEd entrance exam 2026 will be conducted from 8 June 2026 to 22 June 2026 in computer‑based test (CBT) mode at designated centres across divisional and district headquarters in the state. Candidates can now download their hall tickets through the BSEB DElEd CBT portal link given in the admit card notice.

How to Download BSEB Bihar D.El.Ed 2026‑28 Admit Card

Once on the official CBT portal, candidates can download the Bihar DElEd 2026‑28 hall ticket by following these steps:

  • Visit the DElEd CBT admit card link bsebdeled.cbrt.co.in/bseb-2026/bseb-landing or go via form.bsebdeled.com/login as given in the board notice.
  • On the login page, enter your Application Number in the “Login ID” field.
  • Enter your Date of Birth in DDMMYYYY format as the password, or use the password created at registration if specified.
  • Type the security code / captcha shown on the screen and click on Login / Sign In.
  • Click on the link “Download Admit Card / Hall Ticket” for Bihar DElEd Joint Entrance Examination 2026.
  • Your BSEB Bihar D.El.Ed 2026‑28 admit card will appear on the screen with exam date, shift and centre; download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies for exam day and counselling.

If the website is slow just after release, candidates should wait a few minutes and try again instead of refreshing repeatedly.

Details Mentioned on Bihar DElEd 2026‑28 Admit Card

The Bihar DElEd Joint Entrance Exam 2026 admit card contains important candidate and exam details which must be checked carefully:

  • Candidate’s name.
  • Application number / roll number.
  • Date of birth and category details (if printed).
  • Exam name: D.El.Ed Joint Entrance Examination 2026.
  • Session: 2026‑28.
  • Exam date and shift (between 8 and 22 June 2026).
  • Examination city, centre name, centre code and full address.
  • Candidate’s photograph and signature (as uploaded).
  • Important instructions for CBT, reporting time and gate‑closing time.

If any discrepancy is found in your name, photo, date of birth or exam centre details, you should contact the DElEd helpdesk (phone/email given on the portal) immediately to get it clarified.

Documents Required with Bihar DElEd Admit Card 2026

On the day of the Bihar D.El.Ed Joint Entrance Examination 2026, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the BSEB Bihar D.El.Ed 2026‑28 admit card (clear A4‑size printout; colour or high‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence, Passport or any other government‑issued ID.
  • 1-2 recent passport‑size photographs (preferably the same as used in the application form), in case they are required for verification or attendance sheet.

Instructional sources emphasise that candidates without the admit card and valid ID will not be allowed to enter the exam centre.

Exam‑Day Instructions for Bihar DElEd Entrance Exam 2026

BSEB and preparation guides have listed several important exam‑day guidelines for the Bihar DElEd Joint Entrance Examination 2026:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card to complete frisking, document verification and seating; latecomers after gate‑closing time will not be allowed.
  • Carry only permitted items: DElEd admit card, photo ID, photographs and basic stationery (if allowed); follow all specific instructions printed on the hall ticket and information bulletin.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless explicitly allowed), Bluetooth devices, earphones, books, notes or any electronic gadgets inside the exam hall; violation may lead to cancellation of candidature.
  • Follow all instructions displayed on the computer screen and those given by invigilators; any unfair means or misbehaviour can result in disqualification and further action by BSEB.
  • Keep your DElEd admit card safe even after the exam, as it may be required during result, counselling and admission processes for the 2026‑28 session.
