Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for KEA Group-C posts?

The last date to apply online for the Karnataka Examination Authority Group-C posts is 03 June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline to avoid any last-minute issues.

Q2. What are the minimum and maximum age limits for the KEA Group-C recruitment?

The minimum age limit is 18 years. The maximum age limit is 40 years for General candidates, 43 years for categories 2A, 2B, 3A, 3B, and 45 years for SC, ST, and Category-1 candidates.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for First Division Assistant?

For the First Division Assistant post, candidates must possess a Degree from a University established by law in India. Equivalency of the degree is as per the specified government circular.

Q4. What is the educational qualification for Second Division Assistant?

Candidates applying for the Second Division Assistant post must have completed PUC or an equivalent qualification.

Q5. How many vacancies are available for KEA Group-C posts?

There are a total of 6 vacancies for Group-C posts in the Karnataka Examination Authority. This includes 2 vacancies for First Division Assistant and 4 vacancies for Second Division Assistant.

Q6. What is the application fee for KEA Group-C recruitment?

The application fee varies by category. General and Other Categories pay Rs. 750, SC/ST/Cat-1/Ex-Servicemen pay Rs. 500, and Persons with Disabilities pay Rs. 250.

Q7. What is the selection process for KEA Group-C posts?

The selection process for KEA Group-C posts involves a Competitive Examination followed by Document Verification.

Q8. What is the salary for First Division Assistant?

The pay scale for the First Division Assistant post is Rs. 44425-83700 per month, with specific increments detailed in the notification.
