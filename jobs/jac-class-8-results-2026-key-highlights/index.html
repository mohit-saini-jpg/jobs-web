Jharkhand Academic Council (JAC), Ranchi has declared the JAC Class 8th Result 2026 for students who appeared in the Class 8 board examination for the 2025-26 academic session. The council has activated the online result portal so that schools and students can access the Class 8 marks and grades digitally. JAC Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - jac.jharkhand.gov.in, with the dedicated Class 8 portal available at class-8.jacexamonline.com/jac-portal. Students can check their Class 8 result using their roll number and roll code (where enabled) or through school login, depending on the mode specified by the board. After downloading or viewing the marksheet, they should verify their personal details, subjects and grades and contact school authorities or JAC in case of any discrepancy.

JAC Class 8 Results 2026 Key Highlights

JAC conducts the Class 8 board examination across Jharkhand as a state-level assessment, and the JAC Class 8th Result 2026 is released online in April for all registered students. The result includes subject-wise grades, overall grade and pass status for each student and is used by schools for promotion to Class 9. Results are made available through official websites like jac.jharkhand.gov.in, jacexamportal.in and the dedicated Class 8 portal, from where schools can download student-wise or school-wise mark reports.

How to Download JAC Class 8th Result 2026

Use the steps below to check JAC Class 8th Result 2026 online (follow the method applicable this year in your school’s instructions):

  • Visit the official JAC portal at https://jac.jharkhand.gov.in or directly open the Class 8 portal at https://class-8.jacexamonline.com/jac-portal.
  • If a student login is provided, click on the Class 8 result link and enter your roll number and roll code (or UID) in the login fields.
  • If the result is school-based, the principal/teacher must log in using the school username and password, then navigate to the result section and download the student-wise PDF/marksheet.
  • After login, select the Class 8 exam 2026 result option and choose your school or class if required.
  • View your result which will show name, roll number, school name, subject-wise grades and overall result status.
  • Download or print the marksheet for your records; students should collect official result copies or certificates from their school when issued.
  • If any error is found in the name, marks or other details, immediately inform school authorities so they can coordinate with JAC for correction.
