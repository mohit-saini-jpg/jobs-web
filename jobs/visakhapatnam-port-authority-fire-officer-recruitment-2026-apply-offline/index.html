Eligibility Criteria for VPA Fire Officer Recruitment

Candidates applying for the Fire Officer post must fulfil the educational qualification, experience, age, and physical standards specified in the notification, as on the date of notification. The essential educational qualifications for the Fire Officer post are as follows: Must be a Graduate from a recognized University or Institution. Must have undergone 6 (six) months of training in a recognized Fire Training Institute or College. Should possess a Driving License for Light and Heavy Vehicles. Experience requirements: Minimum 3 (three) years of working experience in a supervisory capacity in a large Fire Service Establishment in an Industry or Government Undertaking. Candidates recommended for interview will have to undergo a Heavy Vehicle driving test.

Visakhapatnam Port Authority Fire Officer Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

The selection process for the Fire Officer post, as per the notification, involves the following stages: Screening of applications and supporting documents by a Certificate Verification Committee. Only eligible candidates recommended by the Certificate Verification Committee will be considered for the interview. Candidates recommended for interview will also be required to undergo a Heavy Vehicle driving test. Note: Mere submission of an application does not confer any right to be called for selection or interview. VPA reserves the right to cancel the notification or increase/decrease the number of posts, and may also devise its own method of selecting candidates.
