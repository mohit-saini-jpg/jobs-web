WB Madhyamik PPR/PPS Result 2026 Declared - Check at result.wbbsedata.com

The West Bengal Board of Secondary Education (WBBSE) has officially declared the WB Madhyamik PPR/PPS Result 2026 on June 16, 2026. Students who applied for Post Publication Review (PPR) or Post Publication Scrutiny (PPS) after the Madhyamik Pariksha 2026 main result can now check their revised scores directly at result.wbbsedata.com using their roll number and date of birth. School authorities can also download consolidated results in PDF format through the School Login option on the same portal.

What Is WB Madhyamik PPR/PPS Result 2026?

This section explains the meaning of PPR and PPS and why students apply for these services after the main Madhyamik result is published.

The Madhyamik PPR/PPS process is WBBSE's official post-result review and scrutiny mechanism. It gives students who are dissatisfied with their marks in the Madhyamik Pariksha (Class 10 Board Exam) an opportunity to have their answer scripts re-examined or re-checked. The two services differ in scope:

ProcessFull FormWhat It Means
PPRPost Publication ReviewFull re-evaluation of the answer booklet by a senior examiner
PPSPost Publication ScrutinyRe-totalling and arithmetic verification of marks (not re-evaluation)
  • PPR is for students who believe their written answers were not fairly evaluated and want a senior examiner to re-read and re-mark their responses.
  • PPS is for students who suspect an error in mark totalling, missing mark entries, or calculation mistakes — the content is not re-evaluated, only the arithmetic is verified.

How to Check WB Madhyamik PPR/PPS Result 2026 - Step-by-Step

Here is a simple, clear guide to help students check their WB Madhyamik PPR/PPS Result 2026 online at the official portal in just a few steps.

Direct Result Link: WB Madhyamik PPR/PPS Result 2026

Steps to Check PPR/PPS Result 2026:

  • Visit the official result portal at result.wbbsedata.com
  • On the homepage, locate the PPR/PPS Results for Madhyamik Pariksha (SE) - Year 2026 section
  • Enter your Roll Number in the given field
  • Enter your Date of Birth in the required format
  • Click the Submit button
  • Your PPR/PPS result will be displayed on screen
  • Download or take a screenshot for future reference

How to Check PPR/PPS Result via School Login (For Schools)

School authorities have a dedicated login option on the same portal to access and download the results of all their students collectively.

School authorities can download the consolidated results of all their students in PDF format from the same portal at result.wbbsedata.com using the School Login option.

For Schools - What to Do After Result:

For students whose marks have changed, schools are required to collect the revised mark sheets and certificates from their respective Regional Offices. To do so, the original mark sheets and certificates of the concerned candidates must be submitted within seven working days from the date of result publication.

WB Madhyamik PPR/PPS 2026 Application Process - Recap

This section summarizes how eligible students applied for PPR and PPS after the main Madhyamik result was declared.

Students were required to apply through their school's headmaster. Schools then uploaded the requests to the official board portal at wbbsedata.com.

Documents Required for PPR/PPS Application:

  • Photocopy of WB Madhyamik Marksheet
  • WB 10th Admit Card (photocopy)
  • Application on plain paper with name and roll number

Application Fees:

ServiceFee
PPR (Post Publication Review)₹100

Payment was made at the school office, after which the school uploaded the application to the WBBSE portal.

WB Madhyamik 2026 - Passing Criteria

Students checking their PPR/PPS result should be aware of the minimum passing marks to understand whether their revised score helps them clear the examination.

For the WBBSE Madhyamik Result 2026, students must secure a minimum of 35% marks (35 out of 100) in each subject individually. The overall minimum total to pass is 272 out of 800 marks. First Division requires 480 or more marks out of 800. For subjects with practicals, both theory and practical components must be cleared separately.

RTI / Certified Copy of Answer Script 2026

Students who want to go a step further and physically inspect their evaluated answer booklets can apply under the RTI (Right to Information) Act for a certified copy.

If students wish to see their actual answer scripts through RTI or Certified Copy, the deadline is July 23, 2026. This process remains offline. Students must submit an application on plain paper along with a ₹10 fee and a photocopy of their marksheet to the respective board office.
