HP PAT Admit Card 2026 Out

The Himachal Pradesh Takniki Shiksha Board has released the HP PAT Admit Card 2026 for the Polytechnic Admission Test. Candidates who applied for the entrance exam can now download their hall ticket online from the official portal. The admit card is a mandatory document for entry into the exam hall, so candidates should download it as soon as possible and verify all details carefully.

How to Download HP PAT Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official HP PAT admit card portal.
  • Open the HP PAT 2026 Admit Card link.
  • Enter your login credentials such as application number, password or other required details.
  • Submit the form to view your hall ticket.
  • Download the admit card PDF and take a clear printout.

If the portal is busy, try again after some time.

Details Mentioned on the Admit Card

The HP PAT hall ticket generally contains:

  • Candidate’s name.
  • Roll number / registration details.
  • Photograph and signature.
  • Exam date and reporting time.
  • Exam centre name and address.
  • Important exam-day instructions.

Check all information carefully. If there is any mistake, contact the board immediately.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates should carry the following items to the exam centre:

  • A printed copy of the HP PAT Admit Card 2026.
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card, Driving Licence or Passport.
  • Recent passport-size photographs, if mentioned in the instructions.

Without the admit card and valid ID, entry may be denied.

Exam-Day Instructions

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time.
  • Carry only permitted items to the centre.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators or electronic devices.
  • Follow all invigilator instructions inside the exam hall.
  • Keep the admit card safe after the exam for future reference.
