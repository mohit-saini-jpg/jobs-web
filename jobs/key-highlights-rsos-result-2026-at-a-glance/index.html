How to Check RSOS Result 2026 Online - Step-by-Step Guide

Checking your RSOS 2026 result is a simple process that requires only your roll number. Follow the steps below to access your result quickly and without confusion.

To check the Rajasthan State Open School Result 2026, students need their Roll Number, which can be found on their Admit Card issued before the examination. Candidates who have misplaced their roll number can retrieve it from their school, college, or the RSOS board directly.

Steps to Check RSOS 10th & 12th Result 2026:

  • Visit the Official Website - Go to rsos.co.in
  • Click on Result Link - Find and click the Result / Result Login section on the homepage
  • Enter Your Roll Number - Type in your Roll Number carefully in the required field
  • Submit the Details - Click the "Submit" or "Check Result" button
  • View Your Result - Your RSOS Result 2026 with subject-wise marks will appear on screen
  • Download / Print Your Marksheet - Save or print a copy of your result for future reference

Note: If the official website is slow due to heavy traffic, try accessing the result during off-peak hours or use alternative result portals.

RSOS Result 2026 - Subject-Wise Result & Streams Covered

The RSOS Board Result 2026 covers all three academic streams examined in the Annual Board Examination. Here is what candidates from each stream can expect in their results.

The Rajasthan State Open School conducts examinations and releases results for the following streams:

  • Arts Stream - Humanities and social science subjects
  • Science Stream - Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and related subjects
  • Commerce Stream - Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and related subjects

Students can check their subject-wise marks for each paper they appeared in, along with their overall result status (Pass/Fail/Compartment).

RSOS Result Statistics 2026 - Toppers, Pass Percentage & District-Wise Data

Along with individual results, RSOS also releases comprehensive result statistics that give a broader picture of student performance across Rajasthan.

The Rajasthan State Open School publishes detailed result statistics alongside the main result declaration. These statistics typically include:

  • Overall Pass Percentage - State-wide performance data for Class 10th and 12th
  • Stream-Wise Toppers List - Top scorers in Arts, Science, and Commerce streams
  • District-Wise Toppers - Highest performers from each district of Rajasthan
  • Gender-Wise Performance Data - Analysis of results by gender

This data helps students, parents, and educators assess the overall performance of candidates in the open school system.

What to Do After Checking Your RSOS Result 2026

After viewing your result, there are important next steps depending on whether you have passed or need re-evaluation. Here is what you should do.

If You Have Passed:

  • Download and save a digital copy of your marksheet from the official website
  • Collect your original marksheet and certificate from RSOS after they are issued
  • Use your result for admission to colleges or vocational courses

If You Are Not Satisfied with Your Marks:

  • Apply for Re-evaluation / Re-checking of answer sheets within the prescribed time period after the result
  • Check the official RSOS website for re-evaluation application dates and fees

If You Have a Compartment / Supplementary:

  • Check the official RSOS website for compartment exam dates and application procedures
  • Prepare for the compartment examination to clear the remaining subject(s)
