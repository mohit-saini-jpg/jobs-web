Indian Army SSC (Tech)-68 Men Expert Analysis

This recruitment is a great opportunity for engineering graduates to join Indian Army as SSC Tech officers. The selection process is rigorous, but with proper preparation, aspirants can crack it.

Who Should Apply Indian Army SSC (Tech)-68 Men

Ideal candidates are engineering graduates in streams like BE/BTech in Computer Science, Electronics, and Mechanical. Aspirants should be between 20-30 years of age and have a good academic record.

Indian Army SSC (Tech)-68 Men Preparation Tips

Aspirants should focus on improving their technical skills, especially in computer science and mathematics. They should also prepare for the SSB interview by practicing group discussions and personal interviews.
