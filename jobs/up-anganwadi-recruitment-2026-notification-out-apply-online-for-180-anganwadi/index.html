Application Fee – Meerut Anganwadi Bharti 2026

No application fee is mentioned in the official notification for the Meerut UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026. The online application at upanganwadibharti.in is free of cost for all eligible female candidates.

Selection Process – Meerut Anganwadi Worker Vacancy 2026

There is no written examination for the Meerut Anganwadi Karyakatri Recruitment 2026. Selection will be conducted by the district-level Selection Committee (Chayan Samiti) constituted in Meerut district as per Government Order No. 1/1092245/2025/3313/58-1-2025 (1917687) dated 17 September 2025. The decision of the district-level Chayan Samiti will be final and binding. Online applications are submitted through upanganwadibharti.in within the prescribed dates. Eligible candidates' details are verified for residential status, age, and qualification. A merit list is prepared on the basis of maximum educational qualification of the applicant. Graduates will be ranked above Intermediate pass candidates. Priority is given in the following order: BPL widows/divorced/abandoned women from the same gram sabha/ward → BPL women from the same gram sabha/ward → APL widows/divorced/abandoned women → ot

Q2What is the last date to apply for Meerut UP Anganwadi Bharti 2026?

The last date to submit the online application for the Meerut Anganwadi Worker Recruitment 2026 is 17 July 2026 up to midnight (12:00). Applications are submitted only online at http://upanganwadibharti.in.
Q3How many total vacancies are available in Meerut Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 180 Anganwadi Worker (Karyakatri) vacancies are available across 13 Child Development Projects in Meerut district. The largest number of vacancies (56) is in the Shahar (Urban) project, followed by Daurala (18 rural + 4 urban) and Haastinapur (16 rural + 1 urban).

A
The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age is 35 years as on 01 January 2026. Age will be verified only from the High School (Class X) marksheet and certificate. No other document will be accepted as age proof.
Q5What is the educational qualification required for Meerut Anganwadi Worker Recruitment 2026?
A
The minimum educational qualification is Intermediate (12th Pass) or equivalent. The merit list will be prepared on the basis of the maximum (highest) educational qualification. Candidates holding a graduation degree will be ranked above those with only Intermediate.
Q6Is there an application fee for Meerut Anganwadi Bharti 2026?

Q7Is there a written exam for Meerut Anganwadi Recruitment 2026?
A
No. There is no written examination. Selection is done by the district-level Chayan Samiti (Selection Committee) constituted in Meerut, based on merit from educational qualifications and priority category. The Chayan Samiti's decision is final.
Q8Can male candidates apply for Meerut Anganwadi Worker Vacancy 2026?
A
No. The official notification clearly states that only female candidates are eligible to apply for the Anganwadi Worker posts in Meerut district.
Q9Can I apply for any project in Meerut or only for my own gram sabha/ward?

Candidates can apply only for the Anganwadi Worker post in their own gram sabha or ward where they are a permanent resident. Applications from candidates residing in a different gram sabha or ward will be rejected.
