Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for CSIR CEERI Recruitment 2026?

The last date to submit the online application for CSIR CEERI Recruitment 2026 is 20th June 2026. Ensure your application is submitted well before this deadline.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in CSIR CEERI Recruitment 2026?

There are a total of 22 vacancies announced for the posts of Technical Officer and Technician in CSIR CEERI Recruitment 2026.

Q3. What is the minimum educational qualification required for Technician posts?

For Technician posts, the minimum qualification is Secondary School Certificate (SSC)/ 10th Standard with Science subjects and a minimum of 55% marks, along with an ITI certificate or equivalent experience.

Q4. What is the application fee for CSIR CEERI Recruitment 2026?

The application fee for all candidates applying for CSIR CEERI Recruitment 2026 is ₹500/-. This fee is to be paid online.

Q5. Is there any age relaxation for reserved categories?

Yes, the upper age limit is relaxable by 5 years for SC/ST candidates, 3 years for OBC candidates, and 10 years for PwBDs.

Q6. What is the selection process for these posts?

The selection process involves screening based on online applications followed by a Trade Test or Written Test, which includes Paper-I, Paper-II, and Paper-III.

Q7. What is the salary for a Technical Officer at CSIR CEERI?

The Technical Officer post is placed in Pay Level 7, with an approximate salary of ₹89,268/- per month.

Q8. Can I apply for more than one post?

Yes, you can apply for more than one post, but a separate application must be submitted for each post code.
