  • 16Print-out of e-Admit Card issued for CBT; any other certificate as specified
  • 17Examinations for all positions are conducted on the same day and at the same time; one candidate must apply against one post only. In case of multiple applications, only the latest one shall be considered as final and earlier applications will be rejected without notice.
  • 18The nature of engagement and training is hands-on, on-job basis; trainees may need to report on shifts. They may be located at or transferred to any of the operations/establishments of the Company in India or abroad.
