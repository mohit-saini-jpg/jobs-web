Nirwan University Datesheet 2026 - Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 17/07/2026

Nirwan University, Jaipur has released the datesheet for the End Term Theory and Practical (Main/Back) Examination for the academic session 2025-2026 under the School of Agricultural Sciences. The notification, bearing reference number NUJ/Exam/2026/641, was issued on 15.07.2026. It covers exam schedules for M.Sc (Agriculture) II Semester (Agronomy, Horticulture, Vegetable Science, Fruit Science, and Soil Science) and B.Sc (Hons.) Agriculture II, IV, and VI Semester (both Main and Back examinations).

Important Instructions

  • Students should reach the examination centre well before the scheduled shift time.
  • Shift I examinations are held from 10:00 AM to 01:00 PM, and Shift II examinations from 02:00 PM to 05:00 PM.
  • Students must carry their admit card and a valid ID to the examination hall (specific documents required are not detailed in this datesheet notification).
  • Any changes to the datesheet, if issued later by the university, will supersede this schedule.
