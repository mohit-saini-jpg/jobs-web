How To Apply

  • Candidates fulfilling the eligibility criteria may report directly for the walk-in interview at the venue on the specified date and time for their respective post. Candidates must fill and bring one copy of the application in the prescribed format (Annexure-A attached with the notification), available on the KRCL website at www.konkanrailway.com under Recruitment > Current Notifications. No online or offline application submission by post/courier is required – candidates must appear in person.

  • Registration time is strictly 09:00 hrs to 12:00 hrs only on the date of walk-in interview. Applications not conforming to the prescribed format or having illegible/ambiguous certificates or without certificates or incomplete will be summarily rejected. Any kind of canvassing will result in disqualification of the candidate at any stage of selection without assigning any reason.

  • Documents (Self-attested copies) to be attached with the filled application form:

  • Copies of certificate in proof of qualification (as per the qualification specified in the Notification Annexure)
