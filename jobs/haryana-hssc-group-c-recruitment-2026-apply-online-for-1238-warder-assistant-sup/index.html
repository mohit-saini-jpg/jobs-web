Haryana HSSC Group C Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is HSSC Recruitment 2026 Advertisement No. 06/2026 about?
A
HSSC Advt. 06/2026 is a CET Phase-II recruitment drive for Group-C posts in the Haryana Prison Department, including Prison Warder (Male & Female) and Assistant Superintendent Jail (Male & Female). A total of 1,238 vacancies are advertised, and only candidates who qualified CET Advt. No. 01/2025 are eligible to apply.
Q2What is the last date to apply for HSSC Group-C 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is June 30, 2026 at 11:59 PM. A correction window is available from July 1 to July 2, 2026.
Q3Is there any application fee for HSSC Recruitment 2026?
A
No. There is no application fee required for any candidate, regardless of category.
Q4What is the minimum CET score needed to apply for these posts?
A
General category candidates must have scored a minimum of 50% in CET Advt. No. 01/2025. Reserved category candidates (both vertical and horizontal) require a minimum of 40%.
Q5What is the exam pattern for HSSC Phase-II 2026?
A
The exam consists of 100 MCQs for 100 marks, to be completed in 1 hour 45 minutes. It is offline (OMR-based) and bilingual. There is no negative marking for wrong answers, but -1 mark is deducted for questions left unattempted without filling the fifth circle.
Q6Who is eligible for age relaxation in HSSC 2026?
A
DSC, OSC, BCA, BCB, and EWS candidates receive a 5-year age relaxation. Persons with Disabilities receive up to 10 years of relaxation. Ex-Servicemen receive relaxation equal to their military service plus 3 years.
Q7What happens if two candidates have equal marks in the written exam?
A
The older candidate gets precedence. If age is also the same, the candidate with higher marks in CET Phase-I will be ranked higher.
Q8Can I change my post preference after submitting the application?
A
No. Post preferences confirmed at the time of submission are final and irrevocable under any circumstances.
Q9Which certificates are valid for BCA/BCB and EWS categories in this recruitment?
A
BCA, BCB, and EWS certificates must have been issued on or after April 1, 2026 and on or before the closing date. Certificates issued for Central Government jobs or Panchayati Raj elections are not valid.
Q10Where can I find the official notification and apply online?
A
The official advertisement is available at www.hssc.gov.in. Applications must be submitted at https://adv062026.hryssc.com.
