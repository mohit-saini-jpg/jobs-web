DetailsSenior Research Associate : Essential: PhD in Public Policy / Public Administration / Social Sciences / Economics / Development Studies / Social Work / Gender Studies / Statistics/ Management from a recognised university with a first class in the preceding degree; OR M.Phil. with minimum 60% marks in Public Policy / Public Administration / Social Sciences / Economics / Social Work / Gender Studies from a recognised university with minimum 3 years of relevant research experience; OR Master's degree (MA/MSc/MSW/MBA) with minimum 60% marks in a relevant discipline from a reputed institute with minimum 5 years of relevant work experience in research/policy/governance projects. Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the IIM Raipur Senior Research Associate position?

The last date to submit the application form for the Senior Research Associate – Good Governance Project (Chhattisgarh) post at IIM Raipur is 29 May 2026. Ensure your application is submitted before this deadline.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Senior Research Associate role?

Candidates need a PhD in relevant fields, or an M.Phil. with 3 years of research experience, or a Master's degree with 5 years of relevant work experience in research or policy projects.

Q3. Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

No, as per the provided data, there is no application fee for General/OBC and SC/ST categories for this recruitment drive.

Q4. What is the age limit for the Senior Research Associate position?

The maximum age limit for the Senior Research Associate position is 45 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per Government of India norms for reserved categories.

Q5. What is the salary offered for the Senior Research Associate post?

The selected candidate will receive a monthly salary ranging from Rs. 60,000 to Rs. 70,000, which may be negotiable based on qualifications and experience.

Q6. What is the selection process for this recruitment?

The selection process will involve a written test followed by a personal interview. Candidates must perform well in both stages to be considered for the role.

Q7. What is the job type for this position?

The recruitment for the Senior Research Associate position is on a contractual basis.
