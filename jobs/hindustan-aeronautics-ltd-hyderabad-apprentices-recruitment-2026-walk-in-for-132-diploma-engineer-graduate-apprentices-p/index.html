Hindustan Aeronautics Ltd Hyderabad Apprentices Recruitment 2026 – Walk in for 132 Diploma, Engineer, Graduate Apprentices Posts

Posts: 132 (Diploma: 30 + General Stream Graduate: 34 + Engineering Graduate: 68) Last Date: See Notification Walk-in

Short Information

The Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Avionics Division, Hyderabad has officially announced the Technician (Diploma) Apprentice, Engineering Graduate Apprentice, and General Stream Graduate Apprentice Recruitment 2026 with 132 openings. The detailed notification was published on 11.05.2026 at HAL Official Website. Candidates who meet the eligibility criteria of having passed the respective Diploma, Engineering, or General Graduation within the last 5 years can now attend the walk-in interviews from 02-06-2026 to 05-06-2026. Selection Process

  • Stage Details
  • Stage 1 Mandatory pre-registration on https://nats.education.gov.in portal
  • Stage 2 Walk-in on the respective category/discipline date with all original documents and photocopies
  • Stage 3 Merit-based shortlisting on CGPA/Percentage of marks in Diploma/Engineering Degree/General Stream Graduation
  • Stage 4 Merit list published on www.hal-india.co.in on 30-06-2026; confirmation emails sent to shortlisted candidates
  • Stage 5 Joining / Reporting on 27-07-2026
  • Selection is based only on CGPA/Percentage of marks secured in Diploma/Engineering Degree/Graduation in General Stream. There will be no written test or interview. Merit list of provisionally shortlisted candidates shall be hosted on www.hal-india.co.in on 30th June 2026 and confirmation emails shall be forwarded to shortlisted candidates subsequently.
  • The date of joining/reporting for the Apprenticeship is 27th July 2026. All provisionally shortlisted candidates must be in a position to join by the prescribed date. If a candidate does not report by the scheduled date
  • it will be assumed that he/she is not interested in the apprenticeship and the candidature will be forfeited. This Apprenticeship Training does not guarantee employment at HAL.

How To Apply

  • Interested and eligible candidates must walk-in on the respective date as per the category/discipline-wise schedule at Utsav Sadan Auditorium, Behind Department of Training & Development, Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, Avionics Division, Balanagar, Hyderabad – 500042. There is no separate online application on the HAL website. Candidates must pre-register on the NATS portal at https://nats.education.gov.in before attending the walk-in interview.
  • Before appearing for the walk-in, candidates must ensure they meet all the requisite eligibility criteria and that all particulars furnished are true and correct in all aspects. Submission of false/incorrect information or suppression of any facts at any stage will result in immediate cancellation/termination of candidature. Candidates must make their own arrangements for accommodation and travelling for the walk-in process and joining the apprenticeship.
  • Documents Required at the Time of Walk-in:
  • (a) For Verification of Original Certificates:
  • Aadhaar Card
  • SSC / 10th Marks Certificate
  • Birth Certificate (in case Date of Birth is not mentioned in SSC Certificate)
  • Degree / Diploma Certificate marks sheets (all semesters or consolidated mark sheet)
  • Original / Provisional Certificate of qualifying Degree / Diploma
  • Reservation / Community / Caste Certificate (SC, ST, OBC, EWS, XSM, PWD/PH) – if applicable
  • (b) Documents for Submission:
  • One set of Photo copy / Xerox copy of all the above certificates
  • Passport size photographs

Important Instructions

  • Selection is purely merit-based on CGPA/Percentage of marks in Diploma/Engineering Degree/Graduation in General Stream; there will be no written test or interview. Pre-registration on the NATS portal (https://nats.education.gov.in) is mandatory before appearing for the walk-in interview. Only candidates who have passed the qualifying examination and possess Original/Provisional Certificate are eligible. The gap between the date of contract after joining apprenticeship and the date of passing the qualification should not be more than 5 years for all categories.
