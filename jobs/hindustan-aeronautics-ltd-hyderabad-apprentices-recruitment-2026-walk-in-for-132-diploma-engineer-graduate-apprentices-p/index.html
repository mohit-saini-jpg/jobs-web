  • Stage Details

  • Stage 1 Mandatory pre-registration on https://nats.education.gov.in portal

  • Stage 2 Walk-in on the respective category/discipline date with all original documents and photocopies

  • Stage 3 Merit-based shortlisting on CGPA/Percentage of marks in Diploma/Engineering Degree/General Stream Graduation

  • Stage 4 Merit list published on www.hal-india.co.in on 30-06-2026; confirmation emails sent to shortlisted candidates

  • Stage 5 Joining / Reporting on 27-07-2026

  • Selection is based only on CGPA/Percentage of marks secured in Diploma/Engineering Degree/Graduation in General Stream. There will be no written test or interview. Merit list of provisionally shortlisted candidates shall be hosted on www.hal-india.co.in on 30th June 2026 and confirmation emails shall be forwarded to shortlisted candidates subsequently.

  • The date of joining/reporting for the Apprenticeship is 27th July 2026. All provisionally shortlisted candidates must be in a position to join by the prescribed date. If a candidate does not report by the scheduled date

How To Apply

  • Interested and eligible candidates must walk-in on the respective date as per the category/discipline-wise schedule at Utsav Sadan Auditorium, Behind Department of Training & Development, Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, Avionics Division, Balanagar, Hyderabad – 500042. There is no separate online application on the HAL website. Candidates must pre-register on the NATS portal at https://nats.education.gov.in before attending the walk-in interview.

  • Before appearing for the walk-in, candidates must ensure they meet all the requisite eligibility criteria and that all particulars furnished are true and correct in all aspects. Submission of false/incorrect information or suppression of any facts at any stage will result in immediate cancellation/termination of candidature. Candidates must make their own arrangements for accommodation and travelling for the walk-in process and joining the apprenticeship.

  • Original / Provisional Certificate of qualifying Degree / Diploma

  • Reservation / Community / Caste Certificate (SC, ST, OBC, EWS, XSM, PWD/PH) – if applicable

  • (b) Documents for Submission:

  • One set of Photo copy / Xerox copy of all the above certificates

  • Passport size photographs

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How many vacancies are there in HAL Hyderabad Diploma & Graduate Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Avionics Division, Hyderabad has notified 132 apprentice vacancies including 30 Diploma Technician, 34 General Stream Graduate, and 68 Engineering Graduate Apprentice posts for FY 2026-27.

Q2. What are the walk-in dates for HAL Hyderabad Diploma, Engineering and General Graduate Apprentices 2026?

The walk-in schedule is category-wise: Engineering Graduates (ECE, Civil, Aeronautical) on 02 June 2026, Engineering Graduates (EEE, CSE, Mechanical) on 03 June 2026, General Stream Graduates on 04 June 2026, and Diploma Technicians on 05 June 2026, all reporting at 9:00 AM.

Q3. What is the selection process for HAL Hyderabad Diploma & Graduate Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

Selection is purely merit-based on CGPA/percentage of marks in the qualifying Diploma, Engineering Degree or General Stream Degree, and there is no written test or interview for these apprentice posts.

Q4. Is NATS registration mandatory for HAL Hyderabad Diploma and Graduate Apprentice 2026?

Yes, pre-registration on the NATS portal at https://nats.education.gov.in is mandatory for all Diploma, Engineering Graduate, and General Stream Graduate candidates before attending the walk-in.

Q5. What is the stipend for HAL Hyderabad Diploma & Graduate Apprentices 2026-27?

Apprentices will receive a monthly stipend as per the Apprentices Act 1961 and its rules and amendments, with different rates applicable for Diploma and Graduate apprentices as notified by HAL and BoAT.

Q6. What is the eligibility criteria for HAL Hyderabad Engineering Graduate Apprentices 2026?

Engineering Graduate Apprentice candidates must have completed a Degree in Engineering/Technology in the relevant branch from a recognized university, with the gap between passing and the apprenticeship contract date not exceeding 5 years.

Q7. Who is not eligible for HAL Hyderabad Diploma & Graduate Apprentice 2026?

Candidates are not eligible if they have already undergone or are undergoing apprenticeship training, are registered for apprenticeship elsewhere under the Apprentices Act, or are already pursuing/are about to join regular higher education during the training period.

Q8. When will the HAL Hyderabad Diploma & Graduate Apprentice 2026 merit list be released?

The merit list of provisionally shortlisted candidates is scheduled to be published on the official HAL website www.hal-india.co.in on 30 June 2026.
