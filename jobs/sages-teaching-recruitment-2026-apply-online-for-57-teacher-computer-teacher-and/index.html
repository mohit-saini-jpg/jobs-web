FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for SEJES Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for SEJES Teaching and Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026 is 15 June 2026 till 12:00 Noon. Candidates must complete the Google Form application before the deadline. Late applications will not be accepted under any circumstances.
Q2How many vacancies are available in SEJES Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 57 teaching and non-teaching vacancies have been announced under Swami Atmanand Excellent Schools in Balodabazar-Bhatapara district. Posts include Lecturer, Teacher, Assistant Teacher, Computer Teacher, PT Teacher, and Head Teacher. Vacancies are distributed across 16 schools.
Q3What is the salary for SEJES Teaching Recruitment 2026 posts?
A
Selected candidates will receive a fixed monthly salary ranging from Rs. 25,300 to Rs. 38,100 depending on the post. The salary is provided as a lump-sum contractual payment. No DA, HRA, or additional government allowances will be provided.
Q4What is the age limit for SEJES Recruitment 2026?
A
Applicants must be between 21 and 35 years of age as on 01 January 2026. Reserved category candidates will receive age relaxation as per Chhattisgarh Government rules. Women and CG domicile candidates are also eligible for additional relaxation.
Q5What are the educational qualifications required for SEJES vacancies?
A
Candidates must possess qualifications such as Higher Secondary, Graduation, Post Graduation, B.Ed., D.El.Ed., B.P.Ed., or Computer Science/IT qualifications based on the post applied for. Relevant CG TET qualification is also mandatory for several posts. English medium education is compulsory for most teaching positions.
Q6Is there any application fee for SEJES Recruitment 2026?
A
No application fee has been mentioned in the official notification. Eligible candidates can apply online completely free of cost through the Google Form link. Applicants should carefully fill out the form with correct details.
Q7What is the selection process for SEJES Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process is completely merit-based and no written examination or interview will be conducted. Merit lists will be prepared based on academic qualification marks. Shortlisted candidates will then be called for document verification.
Q8Is CG TET mandatory for SEJES Teaching posts?
A
Yes, CG TET qualification is mandatory for several posts including Teacher, Assistant Teacher, and Head Teacher positions. Primary level TET is required for Assistant Teacher posts, while Pre-Secondary level TET is needed for Teacher posts. Lecturer posts do not require TET qualification.
Q9How can candidates apply for SEJES Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online only through the official Google Form link provided in the notification. Offline applications or postal submissions will not be accepted. Applicants should also keep scanned copies of required documents ready before applying.
Q10Where can candidates download the SEJES Recruitment 2026 notification PDF?
A
Candidates can download the official SEJES Teaching and Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026 notification PDF from the official website of Balodabazar District Administration. The notification contains complete eligibility, vacancy, salary, and application details. Applicants are advised to read the full notification carefully before applying.
