What is HSTES?

HSTES (Haryana State Technical Education Society) is the state-level counselling authority responsible for admissions to engineering, pharmacy, and diploma courses in government and private technical institutions in Haryana. It conducts centralized online counselling based on merit of JEE Main, NATA, OCET, and qualifying exam marks.

Eligibility Criteria for HSTES Counselling 2026

  • B.Tech: Passed 10+2 with Physics, Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
  • B.Arch: Passed 10+2 with Mathematics + valid NATA score.
  • B.Pharmacy: Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics.
  • Diploma Engineering: Passed 10th standard.
  • Diploma Pharmacy: Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics.

Counselling Process (How to Participate in HSTES Counselling 2026)

  • Register online on hstes.org.in
  • Pay registration fee
  • Fill choice of colleges and branches
  • Seat allotment result declaration
  • Physical reporting at allotted institute

Documents Required for HSTES Counselling 2026

  • Class 10th & 12th Marksheet
  • JEE Main / NATA / OCET Score Card (if applicable)
  • Domicile Certificate of Haryana
  • Category Certificate (if claiming reservation)
  • Character Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card / Photo ID Proof
  • Passport size photographs
