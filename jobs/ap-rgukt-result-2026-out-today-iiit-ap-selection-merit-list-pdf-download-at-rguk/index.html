What is RGUKT AP IIIT Admission 2026?

RGUKT, commonly referred to as AP IIIT, offers a fully residential 6year integrated programme combining a 2year PreUniversity Course (PUC) followed by a 4year B.Tech degree. Admission is based solely on merit in the SSC (Class 10) Board Examinations of 2026. A total of 4,400 seats are available across four campuses Nuzvid (Eluru District), RK Valley (Kadapa District), Srikakulam (Etcherla), and Ongole with exactly 1,100 seats per campus.

How Is the RGUKT AP Merit List 2026 Prepared?

The merit list is not based on any entrance exam. Admissions are strictly based on merit in the SSC (10th Class) 2026 examinations. Understanding the basis of preparation helps students gauge their selection chances.

Key Merit Criteria:

  • Marks obtained in the SSC (10th Board) Examination 2026 form the primary basis of selection.
  • A deprivation score of 4% (4 marks out of 100 per subject) is added to the scores of students who studied in Government Schools.
  • For students coming from educational boards that declare results in grades or GPA, an equivalency factor recommended by RGUKT's Governing Council is used to convert those into proportional marks.

Regional Seat Distribution:

Out of the total intake pool, 65.62% of the seats are structurally allocated to candidates hailing from the Andhra University (AU) region. The remaining 34.38% of seats are meticulously allocated to prospective students from the Sri Venkateswara University (SVU) region.

Every candidate reporting for counselling must pay the admission fee at the venue. Important Points for RGUKT AP IIIT Applicants 2026

  • Multiple Applications: In cases where a candidate submitted more than one application, the system completely discards all previous entries, and only the data filled in the latest application form will be considered.
  • Local/NonLocal Status: Candidates must satisfy the Local and NonLocal status requirements as per the AP Educational Institutions (Regulation of Admission) Order, 1974.
  • No Campus Transfer: In no case will an internal transfer of candidates between campuses be permitted after admissions. Students must complete their entire six years at the specific campus where they are initially admitted.
  • Residential Obligation: RGUKT is a fully residential university. Attendance is compulsory, and no condonation of shortage of attendance is permitted except under verified medical grounds.
  • Fraudulent Certificates: Submitting false or doctored certificates at any stage will result in immediate cancellation of admission and legal action.
