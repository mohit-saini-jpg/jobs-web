How to Check Mumbai University Result 2026 Online

The official result declaration list confirms that all five results above are available on the Mumbai University results portal. While the exact on-screen steps are not detailed in the office registers, students can generally follow this process to check their individual result:

  • Visit the official Mumbai University result portal at https://www.mumresults.in/.
  • Locate and select the relevant examination from the list of declared results, for example M.Sc. Part I Sem II (CBCGS), M.E. Chemical Engineering Sem I, BMS-MMS Sem VI, or B.E. Chemical Engineering Sem VII.
  • Enter your seat number, PRN (Permanent Registration Number), or other login details as prompted on the portal.
  • Submit the details to view your result, including subject-wise marks, grades, credits, and SGPI/GPA.
  • Download or save a copy of the result for your records. This is a provisional result; the official statement of marks or grade card will be issued separately by the college or university..
