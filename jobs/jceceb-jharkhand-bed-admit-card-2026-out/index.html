JCECEB Jharkhand BEd Admit Card 2026 Out

Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) has released the Jharkhand BEd Admit Card 2026 for the BEd Combined Entrance Competitive Examination. As per the official schedule, the Jharkhand BEd entrance exam will be held on 31 May 2026, and the admit card has been issued 4 days before the exam (27 May 2026).

Candidates who registered for BEd admission 2026‑28 can now log in and download their hall ticket online. The admit card is available through the JCECEB BEd portal bedcet.jceceb.org.in and also linked from the main board site.

How to Download JCECEB Jharkhand BEd Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official BEd entrance portal bedcet.jceceb.org.in or open the admit card link via jceceb.jharkhand.gov.in → Admit Card.
  • On the BEd login page, click on “Candidate Login / Admit Card”.
  • Enter your Registration Number / Application Number and Password or Date of Birth, then type the security code/captcha shown.
  • Click on Login; your Jharkhand BEd Admit Card 2026 will be displayed on the screen.
  • Check all details (name, exam date, centre, photograph) carefully.
  • Download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies of the hall ticket to carry on exam day and for future processes.

If the site is slow or shows an error, candidates are advised to wait and try again later instead of repeatedly refreshing.

Details Mentioned on Jharkhand BEd Admit Card 2026

The JCECEB Jharkhand BEd entrance exam admit card 2026 contains the following key details:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph.
  • Registration number / application number and roll number.
  • Exam name: B.Ed Combined Entrance Competitive Examination 2026.
  • Exam date and time: 31 May 2026, usually from 10:30 AM to 12:30 PM (as per notice).
  • Examination centre name, code, city and full address.
  • Candidate’s category and other basic details (if printed).
  • Important exam‑day instructions and reporting time.

In case of any mismatch in name, photograph, category or exam centre details, candidates should immediately contact JCECEB through the helpline/email given on the website and in the notice.

Documents Required with Jharkhand BEd Admit Card 2026

On the day of the Jharkhand BEd entrance exam 2026, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the JCECEB Jharkhand BEd Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card, Passport, Driving Licence or any other government‑issued ID accepted by JCECEB.

Without the admit card and valid ID, candidates will not be allowed to enter the examination hall.

Exam‑Day Instructions for Jharkhand BEd Entrance Exam 2026

  • Reach the examination centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card to complete verification and frisking; latecomers after gate‑closing time will not be permitted.
  • Carry only permitted items: Jharkhand BEd admit card, valid photo ID and basic stationery; follow any additional instructions printed on your hall ticket.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not allowed), Bluetooth devices, earphones, books, notes or any electronic gadgets inside the exam hall; violation may lead to cancellation of candidature.
  • Sit in the seat allotted to you and follow all instructions given by invigilators and printed on the OMR sheet/question booklet.
  • Preserve your Jharkhand BEd hall ticket even after the exam, as it may be required during result, counselling and admission processes.
