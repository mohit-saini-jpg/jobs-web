Which Results Have Been Released?

Siddharth University has been declaring semester examination results for various UG and PG courses in a phased, course-wise manner throughout 2026. The current update covers the following programmes for the 2025-26 session:

  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) - 4th Semester, Session 2025-26
  • LLB (Bachelor of Laws) - 4th Semester, Session 2025-26

Students enrolled in these two programmes at Siddharth University or its affiliated colleges in Siddharthnagar, Maharajganj, Sant Kabir Nagar, Basti, Shravasti, and Balrampur districts should check the official result portal immediately.

How to Check Siddharth University BBA & LLB Result 2026 Online

Students can access their Siddharth University Result 2026 directly through the ERP portal by following the simple steps listed below. No login account is required – only your Roll Number or Registration Number is needed.

Step-by-Step Process:

  • Visit the official result portal: erp.suksn.in/resultview.aspx
  • On the result view page, select your course – BBA or LLB – from the dropdown menu.
  • Select the Semester as 4th and the Session as 2025-26.
  • Enter your Roll Number or Registration Number in the required field.
  • Click the "View Result" or "Submit" button.
  • Your online marksheet will appear on the screen.
  • Verify all details – name, roll number, subject-wise marks, and result status (Pass / Fail / Back / Compartment).
  • Download or take a printout of the provisional marksheet for your records.

Important Note: The result displayed on the ERP portal is provisional. Students should collect the official marksheet from their respective college or university examination cell for admission, scholarship, or employment-related purposes.

Details to Keep Ready Before Checking Result

Students need to have certain credentials ready before visiting the result portal. The following information is required to access your Siddharth University marksheet online:

  • Roll Number or Registration Number (as printed on your Admit Card)
  • Date of Birth or Enrolment Number (may be required on some result pages)
  • Course name and Semester details

If you cannot locate your Roll Number, check your Admit Card, ID Card, or University registration documents. You may also contact your college's examination cell for assistance.

How to Understand Your Siddharth University Marksheet

Once you access your result on the portal, it is important to correctly interpret the result status shown on your marksheet. Here is what each term means:

  • Pass: You have cleared all subjects and will be promoted to the next semester.
  • Back: You have failed in one or two subjects. You are required to appear only in those specific back-paper examinations. You may continue to the next semester simultaneously, depending on university rules.
  • Compartment: Similar to Back, but you must clear the Compartment Examination for the specific subject(s) as per the official schedule.
  • Fail: You have not met the minimum passing criteria and must re-appear in the semester examinations.
  • Provisional: The online result is not final. The official marksheet issued by the university or college is the authoritative document.

Minimum Passing Criteria: Students must secure a minimum of 40% marks in each subject. Separate minimum marks may apply for practical examinations and internal assignments.
