CDB Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Audit Officer: Associate of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India with two years' experience in auditing; or Associate of the Institute of Cost Accountants of India with three years' experience in auditing; or Master degree in Commerce with seven years' experience in auditing in a Government office or recognised research institutes or universities or public sector undertakings or statutory or autonomous bodies. Systems Analyst: Master degree in Computer Applications or equivalent with Bachelor's degree in Science from a recognized University or Institute; or Bachelor degree in Computer Engineering or Electronics and Communication or Information Technology or equivalent from a recognized University or Institute; and two years post qualification experience as system analyst in an institute of repute. Administrative Officer: Master degree from a recognised University or institute
