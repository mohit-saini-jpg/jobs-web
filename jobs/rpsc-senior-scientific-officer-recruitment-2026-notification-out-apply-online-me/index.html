Age Limit

DetailsCandidates must be a minimum of 21 years and maximum of 40 years as on 01-01-2027. Note: For Post S.No. 3 (Ballistics Division), the post was advertised by the Commission in the year 2019 - the age cut-off date for that post is 01-01-2020 and a 3-year additional relaxation applies, making the effective maximum age limit for that post 43 years; this relaxation applies to Posts 1 and 2 only in the year of advertisement. Maximum age indicated is for Unreserved (UR) category candidates. Relaxation in upper age limit is applicable as per Government of Rajasthan / Government of India guidelines.

Qualification / Eligibility

Candidates must fulfill all eligibility conditions as on the date of eligibility (01-01-2027). Only candidates with the specified educational qualification and experience for the respective Division are eligible. Working knowledge of Hindi written in Devnagari Script and knowledge of Rajasthan Culture is mandatory for all posts. Candidates must mandatorily complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the SSO Portal (https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in) before applying online.

Important Instructions

Candidates must complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the SSO Portal before applying; no application can be submitted without a valid OTR. The detailed advertisement and Instructions for Applicants on the RPSC website (https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in) are part of this notification - candidates must read them before applying. Candidates must apply separately for each post they wish to apply for - a separate online application must be submitted for each post. Candidates belonging to OBC Creamy Layer from Rajasthan and candidates from other states' SC/ST/OBC categories must apply as General/UR candidates. The online application must be submitted on or before 10-07-2026 (up to 12:00 midnight). No offline or physical application will be accepted under any circumstances. After submitting the online application, an Application Form Number (Application No.) will be generated - if this is not received, the application has not been successfully submitted. Candidates must ensure that all educational qualification and experience required as per this advertisement is obtained on or before the last date of online application. Online application Amendment (correction) is possible on payment of Rs. 500/- within 10 days after the last date of application, through the RPSC website; after this, no amendment will be accepted. One Time Registration (OTR) details - name, father's name, date of birth, gender - cannot be amended in the online application; any discrepancy with Aadhaar/Janadhaar must be corrected in the OTR before applying. Candidates with pending disciplinary/vigilance cases or adverse RBI/Statutory Authority observations are not eligible for appointment. PwBD candidates eligible for scribe facility: candidates with 40% or more physical disability having limitation in writing are eligible; scribe facility and compensatory time will be provided as per RPwD Act, 2016 and RPSC guidelines. Candidates must apply for scribe facility at least two days before the examination date. Mobile phones, smartwatches, or any electronic/communication devices are not allowed inside the examination hall. All revisions/corrigendum (if any) will be hosted on the RPSC website (https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in) only - candidates should visit regularly. Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification. Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be Rajasthan only. RPSC reserves the right to cancel the selection process partly/entirely at any stage without assigning any reason.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the RPSC Senior Scientific Officer Recruitment 2026?
A
Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has announced 03 vacancies for Senior Scientific Officer posts in Toxicology, Physics, and Ballistics Divisions of the State Forensic Science Laboratory, Jaipur. Candidates can apply online from 11 June to 10 July 2026
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are available in RPSC SSO 2026?
A
A total of 03 vacancies are available: 1 for Toxicology Division (ST), 1 for Physics Division (UR), and 1 for Ballistics Division (OBC)
Q3Q3. What is the eligibility criteria for RPSC Senior Scientific Officer 2026?
A
Candidates must have an M.Sc./M.Tech./M.E. in relevant disciplines with at least Second Division, along with 2 years’ experience in a State or Central Government FSL. Computer Science qualification and working knowledge of Hindi & Rajasthan culture are mandatory
Q4Q4. What is the age limit for applying to RPSC SSO 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 21 and 40 years as of 01 January 2027. Age relaxations apply for reserved categories, PwBD, ex-servicemen, and women under specific conditions
Q5Q5. How can I apply for RPSC Senior Scientific Officer 2026?
A
Applications are accepted online only through the RPSC website or SSO Portal after completing One Time Registration (OTR). Offline applications are not accepted
Q6Q6. What is the application fee for RPSC SSO Recruitment 2026?
A
General, OBC (Creamy Layer), and candidates from other states: ₹600. Reserved category candidates from Rajasthan & PwBD: ₹400. Fees are non-refundable and must be paid online
Q7Q7. What is the selection process for RPSC SSO 2026?
A
