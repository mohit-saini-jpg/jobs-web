Odisha UG Admissions 2026

The online application process has started from 27 May 2026. Students can begin submitting their applications online from today, 27th May 2026 through the SAMS portal at samsodisha.gov.in. The last date to apply is 10 June 2026.

Odisha UG Admissions 2026 is conducted by the Student Academic Management System (SAMS), Department of Higher Education, Government of Odisha for admission into +3 (Undergraduate) Degree courses in various colleges across the state. The admission is based on merit and choice filling through the Common Application Form (CAF).

Courses Offered in Odisha UG Admissions 2026

Students can begin submitting their applications online from today, 27th May 2026 for various undergraduate programmes including BA, B.Sc, B.Com, BBA, BCA and other streams in Arts, Science, Commerce and self-financing courses offered by government and private colleges affiliated to different universities in Odisha. The portal allows students to select multiple course preferences during choice filling.

Eligibility Criteria

Students can begin submitting their applications online from today, 27th May 2026. Candidates who have passed Class 12 (10+2) or equivalent examination from a recognized board are eligible to apply. Specific eligibility may vary as per the course and stream chosen. Students must ensure they meet the minimum marks requirement as prescribed for different categories and subjects.

Application Fee

Students can begin submitting their applications online from today, 27th May 2026. The application fee is to be paid online through the SAMS portal. Exact fee details for different categories (General, SC, ST) are available on the official portal during the application process.

How to Apply for Odisha UG Admissions 2026

  • Visit the official website samsodisha.gov.in.
  • Click on the UG Admission 2026 link.
  • Complete registration using valid mobile number and email.
  • Fill the online application form with personal and academic details.
  • Upload required documents.
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Submit the form and take a printout.

Documents for Application
