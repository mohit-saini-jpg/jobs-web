FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak Recruitment 2026?
A
UPSSSC has announced a total of 1182 vacancies for Ganna Paryavekshak (Sugarcane Supervisor), including 1000 posts under General Selection and 182 posts under Special Selection.
Q2What are the important dates for UPSSSC Sugarcane Supervisor Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application starts on 7 July 2026 and the last date to apply is 28 July 2026. Candidates can make corrections in the form until 4 August 2026.
Q3Who is eligible to apply for UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak 2026?
A
Only candidates who have appeared in PET-2025 and possess a valid scorecard are eligible. Additionally, candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in Agriculture and CCC certificate.
Q4What is the age limit for UPSSSC Sugarcane Supervisor Vacancy 2026?
A
The minimum age is 21 years and the maximum age is 40 years as of 1 July 2026. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per UP Government rules.
Q5What is the salary of a Ganna Paryavekshak in UPSSSC?
A
The salary is under Pay Level-4, with a pay scale of ₹25,500 to ₹81,100 per month, along with additional allowances.
Q6What is the application fee for UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak Recruitment 2026?
A
All candidates need to pay only a processing fee of ₹25, while the examination fee will be charged later for shortlisted candidates.
Q7What is the selection process for UPSSSC Sugarcane Supervisor 2026?
A
The selection process includes a single written (main) examination of 100 marks. Candidates are shortlisted based on PET-2025 scores, and there is no interview.
Q8What is the exam pattern for UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak Main Exam 2026?
A
The exam consists of 100 MCQ questions (100 marks) with a duration of 2 hours. There is a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer.
Q9How can I apply online for UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates can apply online through the official website upsssc.gov.in by logging in using their PET-2025 registration number and completing the application form.
Q10Is CCC certificate mandatory for UPSSSC Ganna Paryavekshak 2026?
A
Yes, a CCC certificate issued by NIELIT (DOEACC) is mandatory along with an Agriculture degree to be eligible for the post.
