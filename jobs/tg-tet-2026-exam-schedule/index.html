TG TET 2026 Exam Schedule

Telangana TGTET June 2026 examination will be conducted from 16 June 2026 to 22 June 2026 in 10 sessions across multiple districts.

The Telangana State Council of Educational Research and Training (SCERT) has released the detailed examination schedule for TGTET-June-2026. The online examination will be held for 5 days in 10 sessions.

TG TET 2026 Exam Schedule: Exam Day Tips

Exam day tips: Carry your admit card, ID proof, and a pen. Report at the exam center 1 hour before the scheduled time. Wear comfortable clothing and shoes.

TG TET 2026 Exam Schedule Exam Pattern Insights

Exam pattern and marking scheme: The Telangana TET 2026 exam will be a multiple-choice question paper with 150 questions. Each question will carry 1 mark, and there will be no negative marking.

TG TET 2026 Exam Schedule: Exam Center Guidelines

Exam center rules: Mobile phones are not allowed inside the exam center. You will need to carry a valid ID proof and your admit card. The exam center will be open 1 hour before the scheduled time.

TG TET 2026 Exam Schedule Last-Minute Strategy

Last-minute preparation strategy: Focus on your weak areas and practice as many questions as possible. Get a good night's sleep and reach the exam center early.
