TG TET 2026 Exam Schedule

Telangana TGTET June 2026 examination will be conducted from 16 June 2026 to 22 June 2026 in 10 sessions across multiple districts.

The Telangana State Council of Educational Research and Training (SCERT) has released the detailed examination schedule for TGTET-June-2026. The online examination will be held for 5 days in 10 sessions.

Detailed TG TET Exam Schedule 2026

16 June 2026

  • Shift 1 (9:00 AM - 11:30 AM): Paper-I (English/Telugu) - Adilabad, Bhadradri Kothagudem, Hanumakonda, Jagtial, Jogulamba Gadwal, Kamareddy, Medak, Mulugu, Nalgonda, Narayanpet
  • Shift 2 (2:00 PM - 4:30 PM): Paper-I (English/Telugu) - Jangaon, Karimnagar, Khammam, Kumuram Bheem Asifabad, Mahabubabad, Mahabubnagar, Nizamabad, Sangareddy, Vikarabad

17 June 2026

  • Shift 1: Paper-I - Hyderabad, Jayashankar Bhupalapally, Mancherial, Medchal Malkajgiri, Nagarkurnool, Nirmal, Peddapalli, Rajanna Siricilla, Rangareddy, Siddipet, Suryapet, Wanaparthy, Warangal, Yadadri Bhuvanagiri & Others
  • Shift 2: Paper-II (Mathematics & Science) - Specific districts (refer full table in PDF)

18 June 2026

  • Shift 1 & Shift 2: Paper-II (Mathematics & Science) - Remaining districts

19 June 2026

  • Shift 1 & Shift 2: Paper-II (Social Studies) - District-wise schedule

22 June 2026

  • Shift 1: Paper-II (Mathematics-Science & Social Studies) - Minor Languages (Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Urdu, Marathi, Sanskrit) - All Districts
  • Shift 2: Paper-I - Minor Languages - All Districts

Direct Official Schedule PDF: Available on https://schooledu.telangana.gov.in

How to Apply / Check TG TET 2026 Details

  • Visit the official application portal: https://tgtet.aptonline.in
  • Click on the relevant notification link.
  • Register or log in using your credentials.
  • Fill the application form and pay the fee (if applicable).
  • Download the confirmation page.

Important Instructions for Candidates

  • Candidates must appear at the allotted exam centre as per the schedule.
  • Carry the printout of the hall ticket (to be released later) and valid photo ID.
  • Follow all COVID / exam day guidelines issued by SCERT.
  • The detailed schedule is available on the official website https://schooledu.telangana.gov.in.
