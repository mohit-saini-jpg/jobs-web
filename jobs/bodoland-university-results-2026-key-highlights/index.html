Bodoland University has announced the Bodoland University Result 2026 for multiple law and postgraduate examinations under the 2025 exam session. The latest notices include the Re-checking Result of LLB 2nd Semester Examination 2025 (Batch 2024), PG 3rd Semester Examination 2025 (Bodo) for Batch 2024, Batch 2023 and Batch 2022, and a series of PG 1st Semester Examination 2025 Arrear/Betterment results for Batch 2024, Batch 2023, Batch 2022 and Batch 2021. Bodoland University Result 2026 has been published online on the official website - buniv.edu.in.

Students can download the respective result notices and PDFs from the result-notice section and check their roll number, subject wise marks and updated result status. After viewing the provisional online result, candidates should verify their personal details, course, batch and marks and immediately contact the university examination branch if any discrepancy is found.

How to Download Bodoland University Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your Bodoland University Result 2026 for LLB and PG Bodo courses:

  • Visit the official result notice page: https://buniv.edu.in/tender/result-notice.
  • Scroll through the latest result notices and identify the correct one, such as: “Re-checking Result of LLB 2nd Semester Examination 2025 (Batch 2024)” “Results of PG 3rd Semester Examination 2025 (Batch 2024/2023/2022) (Bodo)” “Results of PG 1st Semester Examination 2025 (Arrear/Betterment) for your specific batch”.
  • “Re-checking Result of LLB 2nd Semester Examination 2025 (Batch 2024)”
  • “Results of PG 3rd Semester Examination 2025 (Batch 2024/2023/2022) (Bodo)”
  • “Results of PG 1st Semester Examination 2025 (Arrear/Betterment) for your specific batch”.
  • Click on the View Result / PDF link given with the relevant notice.
  • Once the PDF opens, press Ctrl+F and type your Roll Number or Name to quickly find your entry.
  • Check your subject wise marks, total marks, grade/SGPA (if shown) and overall result status (Pass/Fail/Arrear/Betterment outcome).
  • Download and save the PDF or print a copy for your records, re-registration and any academic documentation.
