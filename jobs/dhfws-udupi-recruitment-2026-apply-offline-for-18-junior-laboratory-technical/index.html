DHFWS Udupi Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The District Health & Family Welfare Office (DHFWS), Udupi District, Karnataka, has invited applications for 18 contract-based posts - 5 Junior Laboratory Technical Officer and 13 Pharmacy Officer - to be filled for a period of one year. Eligible candidates must obtain the application form from udupi.nic.in and submit it, along with the required documents, to the District Health and Family Welfare Officer's office on or before 21 July 2026, 5:00 PM.

Eligibility Criteria: Educational Qualification

Each of the two posts has its own specific educational qualification requirement, and both require the qualification to be obtained only from institutions recognized by the Government of Karnataka. Pharmacy Officer candidates additionally need mandatory council registration. Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT), obtained only from institutions recognized by the Government of Karnataka. D.Pharma qualification, obtained only from institutions recognized by the Government of Karnataka. D.Pharma candidates must be mandatorily registered with the Karnataka Pharmacy Council .

Age Limit

A single age limit applies to both posts under this notification, with no separate figure mentioned for reserved categories. Candidates should confirm they meet this limit before applying. Candidates should not exceed 45 years of age . The notification does not mention any age relaxation for SC/ST/OBC or other reserved categories.

Salary

Both posts under this recruitment carry a consolidated monthly salary tied to statutory minimum wage rates rather than a fixed rupee figure. Here is what the notification specifies. Monthly consolidated salary: as per the minimum wage fixed by the Labour Department , applicable to both Junior Laboratory Technical Officer and Pharmacy Officer posts. The notification does not mention a specific rupee amount; the minimum wage rate notified by the Karnataka Labour Department will apply.

DHFWS Udupi Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection for these contract posts follows government roster and merit rules, with an interview stage after screening of eligible candidates. Here is what the notification specifies about the process. Candidates submit the filled application form along with required documents to the DHFWO office by the deadline. The office reviews applications and publishes a list of eligible candidates on udupi.nic.in and the office notice board. The interview date will be announced later to the shortlisted/eligible candidates. Recruitment will be carried out following roster and merit rules. If a suitable candidate is not available in a reserved category, the next candidate in the roster point will be considered for that category.

DHFWS Udupi Recruitment 2026 : How to Apply Online Step-by-Step

  • 1Applying for these posts is entirely offline - candidates download the application form online but must submit the completed physical form to the DHFWO office. Here are the steps to apply.
  • 2Visit the official website udupi.nic.in and download the application form for the post of Lab Technician or Pharmacist.
  • 3Fill in the Contact Information section: full name, address for communication, contact number, and a compulsory email address.
  • 4Fill in the Personal Information section: date of birth, gender, religion, caste category, and whether you are a Kannada medium candidate, rural candidate, or physically handicapped candidate (attaching supporting documents where applicable).
  • 5Fill in the Educational Qualification section with details of your qualifications, attaching the marks card and relevant documents for each.
  • 6Attach your registration certificate (KSPC for Pharmacy Officer or the relevant Paramedical Board registration for Lab Technician).
  • 7Attach a recent passport-size photograph and sign the declaration confirming the information is correct.
  • 8Submit the complete application with all self-attested documents to the District Health and Family Welfare Officer's office, Udupi, on or before 21 July 2026, 5:00 PM.
  • 9For more information, candidates can contact the office at 0820-2525566.
