Candidates within the age range of 18 to 40 years and holding a 3-year Diploma in Electrical Engineering from an AICTE-recognized institute are eligible to apply online at https://apscrecruitment.in. How to Apply

  • 1Applicants must apply online only through the APSC recruitment website: https://apscrecruitment.in; no other mode of application will be accepted.
  • 2New users must first complete One Time Registration (OTR) by clicking on “Register Here” and providing basic details; use a valid and active email ID and mobile number.
  • 3After creating an account, log in with credentials and fill in all required registration and application details carefully; mandatory fields are marked with an asterisk (*).
  • 4Upload necessary documents: proof of Date of Birth, educational qualification mark sheets and certificates, caste/category/BPL/PwBD certificates, domicile proof (PRC/Employment Exchange), Small Family Norms declaration, etc. in PDF format (max 4 MB, 200 dpi, legible).
  • 5Upload recent passport-size Photograph and Signature in JPG/JPEG format (Photo: 50–200 KB, not older than 3 months; Signature: 50–200 KB).
  • 6Verify all details and documents using the ‘Preview’ option and tick the declaration checkbox to enable final submission.
  • 7Proceed to pay the applicable application fee/processing charges online; in case of payment failure or pending status, use the “Check Status” option to verify with bank/GRAS.
  • 8After successful submission and payment, an auto-generated confirmation email/SMS will be sent to the registered email ID/mobile number.
  • 9Take a print-out of the submitted online application form and keep it safely for future reference and interview/document verification.
  • 10Candidates may also use Common Service Centres (CSCs) for filling and submitting the form if they do not have debit card/internet banking facilities.

Important Instructions

Candidates must ensure that they fulfil all eligibility conditions before applying; admission at all stages of selection will be purely provisional and subject to verification by APSC.
No change of category/sub-category (caste, etc.) will be allowed after submission of the online application form. Suppression of facts, false declarations or submission of tampered/fabricated documents may lead to rejection of candidature and action as per law, even after recommendation for appointment. Candidates must produce valid photo identity proof (PAN Card, Driving Licence, Passport, Institution ID, Voter ID, Aadhaar, etc.) at the time of written test and interview. Date of Birth once declared in the online application will be treated as final; only Class X/XII Board documents will be accepted as age proof. Candidates must upload all required documents before final submission; no additional documents will be accepted after final submission of the online application. Canvassing in any form will disqualify a candidate. The selection procedure, detailed exam scheme and any corrigendum/addendum will be notified later on the APSC website; candidates should visit regularly for updates.

FAQs

Q1What is APSC JE (Electrical) PWD Recruitment 2026?
A
APSC JE (Electrical) PWD Recruitment 2026 is a state government recruitment drive by Assam Public Service Commission to fill 53 Junior Engineer (Electrical) posts in the Public Works (Building & National Highways) Department, Government of Assam.
Q2How many vacancies are there in APSC JE (Electrical) PWD Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 53 vacancies have been notified for Junior Engineer (Electrical), including category‑wise posts for Open, OBC/MOBC (including Tea Tribe & Adivasi), SC, ST(P), ST(H), with 18 posts reserved for women, 3 posts for PwBD and 2 posts for Ex‑Servicemen.
Q3What are the important dates for APSC JE (Electrical) Recruitment 2026?
A
The notification is dated 05 May 2026; the online application starts on 11 May 2026 and closes on 10 June 2026, while the last date for payment of the application fee is 12 June 2026 on the APSC recruitment portal.
Q4What is the eligibility criteria for APSC JE (Electrical) PWD 2026?
A
Candidates must be Indian citizens and permanent residents of Assam, possessing a 3‑year regular Diploma in Electrical Engineering from an AICTE‑recognized institute, and must upload domicile proof such as a Permanent Resident Certificate or Employment Exchange Registration Certificate issued in Assam.
Q5What is the age limit for APSC Junior Engineer (Electrical) Recruitment 2026?
A
The age limit is 18 to 40 years as on 01 January 2026 for General candidates, relaxable up to 43 years for OBC/MOBC and 45 years for SC/ST, with Ex‑Servicemen (UR) up to 42 years and an additional 10‑year relaxation for PwBD candidates over and above the category limits.
Q6What is the application fee for APSC JE (Electrical) PWD Recruitment 2026?
A
Including processing charges, the fee is Rs. 297.20 for General, Rs. 197.20 for OBC/MOBC, and Rs. 47.20 for SC/ST/BPL/PwBD candidates, where SC/ST/BPL/PwBD pay only the processing fee and no examination fee.
Q7What is the salary or pay scale for APSC JE (Electrical) under PWD?
A
APSC JE (Electrical) posts carry a Pay Band‑2 salary of Rs. 14,000 – 70,000 per month with a Grade Pay of Rs. 8,700, plus other allowances as admissible to Assam State Government employees.
Q8What is the selection process for APSC JE (Electrical) PWD Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process generally includes a Written Examination/Screening Test followed by an Interview/Viva‑Voce (if applicable) and Document Verification, with further details of the exam scheme to be notified separately by APSC through corrigendum/addendum.
Q9What is the exam pattern for APSC Junior Engineer (Electrical)?
A
For APSC JE, the commission typically conducts an OMR‑based written exam/screening test of around 4 hours, with questions from General Studies/General Knowledge and Engineering discipline subjects, and candidates must secure minimum qualifying marks to be shortlisted for further stages.
Q10How can I apply online for APSC JE (Electrical) PWD Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must apply online only via [apscrecruitment.in], first completing One Time Registration, then filling the JE (Electrical) form, uploading required documents (DOB proof, Diploma certificates, caste and domicile certificates, BPL/PwBD certificates, photo and signature), paying the applicable fee online, and finally downloading/printing the submitted application for future reference.
