NBEMS Research Associate Salary / Pay Scale 2026

Selected candidates will receive a lump-sum monthly consolidated remuneration as below, with statutory deductions made as applicable. No DA, HRA, or other government allowances apply, since these are contractual, non-regular positions. Research Associate-I posts: ₹50,000/- per month (consolidated) Research Associate-II posts (Finance & Accounts, Cyber Security): ₹95,000/- per month (consolidated) Annual increment: up to 5% of remuneration as a ceiling, on satisfactory completion of one year, subject to a case-by-case performance review Extension in each case is subject to the continued requirement of the post at NBEMS and satisfactory performance evaluation by the reporting officer and Head of the Department. The engagement does not confer any right to regularization or permanent absorption.

NBEMS Research Associate Selection Process 2026

Selection for NBEMS Research Associate posts is made purely through a Walk-in-Interaction, with no separate online application stage. The process includes: Document verification of age, qualification, category, and experience certificates on the day of the Walk-in-Interaction Screening and interaction conducted by an Interaction Panel, at the panel's discretion NBEMS reserves the right to hold a written examination or fix its own additional criteria for shortlisting candidates Candidates found ineligible at any stage will have their candidature cancelled The decision of NBEMS in all matters relating to eligibility, scrutiny, and selection is final and binding on candidates.

Documents Required for NBEMS Walk-in-Interaction

Candidates must carry the duly filled application form along with original documents and photocopies in support of: Date of birth (age proof) Educational qualification certificates and mark sheets (10th, 12th, Graduation, Post-Graduation, professional qualification) Caste/category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD), where applicable Experience certificates from current and past employers Relevant professional certifications, if claimed A recent passport-size photograph (to be pasted on the application form) Valid photo identity proof
