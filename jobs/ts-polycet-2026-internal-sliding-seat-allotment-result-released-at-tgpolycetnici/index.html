TS POLYCET 2026 Internal Sliding Seat Allotment Result Released at tgpolycet.nic.in

The Telangana State Board of Technical Education and Training (SBTET) has released the TS POLYCET 2026 internal sliding seat allotment result on the official counselling portal, tgpolycet.nic.in. Candidates who exercised branch-change options during the internal sliding round can now log in with their credentials to check whether they have been allotted a new branch within their current college.

This round gave already-admitted TS POLYCET 2026 students one final chance to move into a preferred branch inside the same polytechnic institute, using leftover vacancies that emerged after the Final Phase of counselling closed.

What Is TS POLYCET Internal Sliding?

This section explains the purpose of the internal sliding round for candidates unfamiliar with the term.

Internal sliding is a post-Final-Phase facility conducted by the Convenor, TG POLYCET 2026 Admissions, that allows candidates who have already reported to a polytechnic college to switch to a different branch within the same institute, based purely on vacancy availability and their original POLYCET rank. It does not allow a change of college — only a change of course/branch inside the college where the candidate is currently studying.

Only candidates who successfully reported after the Final Phase counselling round were eligible to participate in this internal sliding option-entry process.

EventDate

Candidates who receive a new branch through this round must report to their upgraded branch within the same college campus immediately after downloading the fresh allotment order, to avoid forfeiting the new seat.

How Many Candidates Were Allotted Seats in Internal Sliding?

This section covers the participation and allotment statistics released by SBTET for the internal sliding round.

According to officials, the internal sliding process for TG/TS POLYCET 2026 saw strong participation:

  • 1,619 students registered for the internal sliding option-entry process.
  • 1,229 students were allotted a new branch, enabling them to move into their preferred stream within their allotted college.

This means a large majority of applicants - roughly 76% - successfully secured a branch upgrade through this round.

How to Check TS POLYCET 2026 Internal Sliding Seat Allotment Result

This section provides the exact login steps required to download the internal sliding allotment order.

Follow these steps to check your status:

  • Visit the official portal: tgpolycet.nic.in
  • Click on the Internal Sliding Seat Allotment Result link on the homepage.
  • Enter your ROC form number, TS POLYCET hall ticket number, password, and date of birth.
  • Submit the details to view your allotment status.
  • If a new branch has been allotted, download the fresh provisional allotment order.
  • Take a printout for submission at your college.

Candidates who are not allotted a new branch in this round will continue in the branch they were already studying under the Final Phase allotment - no action is needed in that case.

What Happens After the Internal Sliding Result?

This section explains the mandatory steps candidates must complete once they check their result.

  • If allotted a new branch: Download the new allotment order and report to the new branch within the same college by July 1, 2026.
  • If not allotted a new branch: No further action is required; the candidate's existing Final Phase seat remains unchanged.
  • Candidates should register biometric attendance at their (new or existing) branch as instructed by the college administration.
  • All admission formalities, including document handover, should be completed as per the college's local instructions.

TS POLYCET 2026: What Comes After Internal Sliding?

This section outlines the next stage of the counselling process for candidates still without a seat.

Once internal sliding concludes, SBTET releases a spot admission schedule to fill any seats that remain vacant across government and private polytechnic colleges. Based on the released schedule:

Candidates who did not secure a seat through Phase 1, Final Phase, or Internal Sliding can apply during this spot admission window for any remaining vacancies.
