ESIC Medical College &amp; Hospital Surat Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

ESIC Medical College & Hospital, Surat (Gujarat) has invited applications through Advertisement No. 01 of 2026 (No. 376/A/ESICH/SURAT/Recruitment/2026-Admin, dated 17-06-2026) for filling 85 contractual vacancies across Professor, Associate Professor, Assistant Professor, Senior Resident, and Tutor/Demonstrator posts. ESIC Medical College & Hospital Surat Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility for this ESIC Surat recruitment is assessed on two parameters - age limit and educational qualification - both checked as on the cut-off date of 29-06-2026. Candidates already working in government, autonomous, or statutory institutions, as well as retired or currently working professors, are also permitted to apply for eligible posts. Professor, Associate Professor, Assistant Professor: Not exceeding 67 years as on 29-06-2026. Senior Resident and Tutor/Demonstrator: Not exceeding 45 years as on 29-06-2026, with age relaxation applicable as per prevailing Government of India norms. ESIC Medical College & Hospital Surat Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

Selection for all posts is conducted purely through document verification followed by a personal interview - there is no written exam component mentioned in this notification. Candidates are first screened by the Document Verification Committee, and only those cleared are allowed to face the Interview Board the same day. Document Verification: Candidates report between 9 AM-10 AM on their department's scheduled date and submit original + self-attested documents. Interview: Candidates whose documents are successfully verified appear before the duly constituted Selection Board. Result: Published only on the Recruitment section of www.esic.gov.in. Offer of Appointment: Sent exclusively through email . Joining: Selected candidates must join as per the instructions mentioned in their offer of appointment. Security Deposit and Refund Terms

Every selected candidate, irrespective of post, must submit a Security Deposit of Rs. 20,000/- via Demand Draft in favor of "ESI Fund A/c No. 1" at Surat at the time of joining. This deposit is fully refundable on successful completion of the contract period , provided the candidate submits a "No Dues Certificate." However, resignation within six months of joining will result in forfeiture of the security deposit , except in cases of unforeseen or unavoidable circumstances where relaxation may be considered by the competent authority.

Terms of Contractual Engagement

This is a purely contractual position with no entitlement to regular government service benefits, so candidates should evaluate these terms carefully before applying. Selected faculty/SR/Tutor will not be eligible for Provident Fund, Pension, Gratuity, Medical Allowance, Medical Benefits, Seniority, or Promotion. Resignation requires submission of a one-month advance notice or deposit equivalent to one month's salary ; failure to comply leads to forfeiture of both the salary and security deposit, along with possible legal proceedings. Unauthorized absence exceeding 7 days is treated as voluntary abandonment, resulting in automatic termination and forfeiture of dues. Attendance must be marked both manually and through the Aadhaar Enabled Biometric Attendance System (AEBAS) on all working days.
