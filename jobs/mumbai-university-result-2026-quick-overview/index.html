How to Check Mumbai University Result 2026 Online

This section explains the exact steps students need to follow to view and download their MU semester result from the official website.

  • Visit the official result website: mumresults.in
  • Look for the "First Half 2026" results table on the homepage.
  • Locate your course name and program code (for example, 1T00538 for B.E. Chemical Engineering Sem VIII).
  • Click on the PDF link next to your course.
  • The result PDF will open - use Ctrl+F to search for your seat number within the document.
  • Download or save the PDF for future reference.

Mumbai University typically publishes results as consolidated, program-wise PDF files rather than an individual seat-number search tool, so students should download the relevant PDF and locate their own seat number within it.

Documents and Details Required to Check Your Result

This section outlines what students should keep ready before checking their result on mumresults.in.

  • Seat number / Exam roll number
  • Course name and correct program code
  • PDF reader (mobile or desktop) to open the result file
  • Stable internet connection, since result PDFs can be large during peak traffic

Why Mumbai University Releases Results Program-Code Wise

This section explains the format MU uses to organize and publish its results.

The University of Mumbai declares results program-code wise rather than department-wise. Each course, scheme (CBCS/NEP 2020/'C' Scheme), and semester combination is assigned a unique program code, which is listed alongside the result PDF link on mumresults.in. Students should match their exact program code, semester, and scheme to ensure they open the correct result file, since multiple versions (regular, revised, or updated) of the same course code are sometimes uploaded on different dates.
