FAQs

Q1What is the educational qualification for BPSC Professor (Physics, Chemistry) recruitment 2026?
A
Ph.D degree in Physics / Chemistry and First Class or equivalent at either Bachelor's or Master's level in Physics / Chemistry from recognized institutions.
Q2What is the experience required for BPSC Professor recruitment 2026?
A
Minimum 10 years in teaching/research/industry, with at least 3 years as Associate Professor equivalent, plus research publications and Ph.D guidance as specified in notification.
Q3What is the age limit for BPSC Professor (Physics, Chemistry) 2026?
A
Minimum 32 years as on 01.08.2026, no upper limit, retirement at 65 years for candidates applying to government engineering colleges.
Q4How to apply for BPSC Professor recruitment 2026?
A
Apply online through BPSC website www.bpsc.bihar.gov.in from 18/06/2026 to 30/06/2026, following detailed guidelines, with OTR registration mandatory.
Q5What is the salary for BPSC Professor posts in 2026?
A
Pay Level 14 with entry pay Rs. 1,44,200/- per month for Professors in Physics and Chemistry under Bihar government.
