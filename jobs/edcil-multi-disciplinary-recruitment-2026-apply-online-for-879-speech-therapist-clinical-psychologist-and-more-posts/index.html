DetailsCandidates must possess the prescribed qualifications from Government/RCI recognized institutions and fulfil the eligibility criteria as on 30 April 2026. | Occupational Therapist (125 Posts) | Bachelor’s or Master’s Degree in Occupational Therapy from a recognized institution. Valid registration/license to practise as an Occupational Therapist in India, as per applicable state regulations. Strong clinical reasoning and problem-solving abilities, excellent communication and interpersonal skills. Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team, with a compassionate, patient-centred approach. Experienced candidates in the relevant field will be given added advantage. | Physiotherapist (125 Posts) | Bachelor’s / Master’s Degree in Physiotherapy from a recognized institution. Valid professional registration/license. Strong clinical reasoning and communication skills, compassionate approach, ability to work independently and in a team. Experienced candidates in the relevant field will be given added advantage. | Psychologist (125 Posts) | M.A./M.Sc in Psychology from a recognized university (Clinical/Counselling/Developmental Psychology preferred). Experience in psychological assessments and behavioural therapies. Strong communication, counselling and interpersonal skills, ability to work independently and as part of a team. Experienced candidates in the relevant field will be given added advantage. | Speech Therapist (125 Posts) | Bachelor’s / Master’s Degree in Speech-Language Pathology or Audiology & Speech-Language Pathology from a recognized institution. Mandatory valid registration with the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI). Experience working with children with Autism and developmental disorders preferred. Strong assessment, communication and interpersonal skills. Proficiency in Telugu and English (knowledge of other local languages is an added advantage). Ability to work independently and as part of a team; experienced candidates will be given added advantage. | Clinical Psychologist (125 Posts) | Post Graduation in Clinical Psychology from an institution recognized by the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI). Mandatory valid RCI registration. Strong expertise in psychological assessments and behavioural therapies. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills; proficiency in Telugu is an added advantage. Ability to work independently and as part of a multidisciplinary team; experienced candidates will be given added advantage. | Special Educator (Secondary Level) – 125 Posts | The candidate must possess any one of the following RCI-recognised qualifications with valid CRR number: Bachelor’s Degree + B.Ed. (Special Education), OR Bachelor’s Degree + B.Ed. (General) with 1-year Diploma in Autism/Intellectual Disability, OR Bachelor’s Degree + B.Ed. (General) with D.Ed. in Autism/Intellectual Disability. Qualified AP TET (Paper-2B mandatory) / CTET in Special Education. Experienced candidates in the relevant field will be given added advantage. | Special Educator (Primary Level) – 125 Posts | Must have passed Intermediate (10+2) from a recognized Board. Must possess a two-year Diploma in Special Education (Autism/Intellectual Disability) recognized by RCI with valid CRR number. Qualified AP TET (Paper-1B mandatory) / CTET in Special Education. Experienced candidates in the relevant field will be given added advantage. | PMU Consultant (4 Posts) | Ph.D./M.Phil in Clinical Psychology/Psychology/Special Education. Desirable: Ph.D in the field of rehabilitation of Persons with Disabilities. Experienced candidates in the relevant field will be given added advantage. Expertise in report writing and strong communication skills is mandatory.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only; no other mode of application will be accepted.
  • Visit the online recruitment portal at https://tsgrecruitment.edcil.co.in/login.
  • Register and fill in the online application form with accurate and complete details of personal information, qualifications and experience.
  • Upload all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, resume and other supporting documents in PDF format only.
  • Verify all details before final submission, as screening and shortlisting will be based only on the information and documents submitted.
  • Submit the online application on or before 04 June 2026.
