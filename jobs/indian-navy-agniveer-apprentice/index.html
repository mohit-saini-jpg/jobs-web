How to Apply

  • 1Visit https://joinindiannavy.gov.in → Click on "Register" → Register as AVR (Apprentice).
  • 2After successful registration, click on "Login" → AVR (Apprentice) → INET 2/26 → Proceed to apply.
  • 3Fill in all details carefully. Incorrect declaration of information identified at any stage will result in cancellation of candidature.
  • 4Upload a recent passport-size colour photograph (taken not before May 2026), size 10 KB to 50 KB; front portrait in light background without head gear (except Sikhs). Date of photograph must be clearly written at the bottom.
  • 5A live photograph will be captured through the webcam during application form filling.
  • 6Pay the examination fee of ₹550/- + 18% GST online via net banking / Credit Card / Debit Card / UPI.
  • 7Give state preference for allocation of INET exam centre. Choice of centre cannot be changed once selected or allotted by the Indian Navy.
  • 8Candidates will be provided a post-closing correction window. An additional correction window will be activated post INET exam for 2 days.
  • 9Matriculation Certificate and Marksheet (proof of date of birth and educational qualification)
  • 10Diploma Certificate and Consolidated Marksheet from AICTE-recognised Polytechnic Institute
  • 11Domicile Certificate
  • 12NCC 'C' Certificate (if applicable) - to be verified through DG NCC
  • 13Sponsorship Certificate (if applicable) - to be verified through CABS
  • 14Recent passport-size colour photograph (taken not before May 2026)
  • 15All original certificates as submitted/uploaded during online application
  • 16Self-attested copies of all certificates
  • 17Original NCC 'C' Certificate / Sponsorship Certificate (if held)
  • 18Police Verification Form duly verified by the Superintendent of Police of respective jurisdiction (from place of domicile or place of residence)
  • 19Call-up letter / Admit Card
  • 20Only unmarried Indian male candidates are eligible. Agniveers cannot marry during their entire 4-year tenure; violation will result in dismissal from service.
  • 21Agniveers are enrolled under the Navy Act 1957 for a period of 4 years. Indian Navy is not obliged to retain Agniveers beyond the engagement period.
  • 22Up to 25% of each specific batch of Agniveers may be offered permanent enrolment in the Indian Navy as Sailors (Regular Cadre) / Artificers based on performance and organisational requirements. Selection for further enrolment is entirely at the discretion of the Indian Navy; Agniveers have no right to be selected.
  • 23Agniveers will not be permitted to seek release at their own request prior to completion of the engagement period.
  • 24Agniveers are not governed by Naval Pension Regulations/Rules and are not entitled to gratuity for the engagement period.
  • 25Agniveers will not be eligible for Ex-Servicemen status.
  • 26Candidates declaring incorrect domicile/gender and other details shall not be permitted to appear in the recruitment and their candidature will be cancelled.
  • 27Candidates reporting 30 minutes after the reporting time for Stage I - INET shall not be allowed to appear in the examination.
  • 28A candidate's selection pertaining to a particular batch is valid for that batch only. Qualified candidates whose names do not appear in the merit list cannot claim admission for the next batch.
  • 29Mobile phones or any other communication devices are not allowed inside the examination premises. Infringement will entail disciplinary action including ban from future examinations.
  • 30Candidates should not submit multiple applications. If more than one application is received, candidature will be cancelled.
  • 31Minimum height standard is 157 cms. Permanent body tattoos are only permitted on the inner face of forearms (inside of elbow to wrist) and on the reversed side of palm/back (dorsal) side of hand. Tattoos on any other part of the body are not acceptable.
  • 32Candidates are advised to get their ears cleaned for wax and tartar removed from teeth prior to medical examination.
  • 33Visual Standards: Uncorrected Vision 6/12, 6/12 | Corrected Vision 6/6, 6/6 | Colour Perception: CP PASS.
  • 34No enquiry will be entertained regarding this recruitment/enrolment after a period of six months post completion of the recruitment cycle for the respective batch.
  • 35The terms and conditions given in the advertisement are guidelines only and are subject to change without notice. Further, orders issued by the Government as amended from time to time will apply for selected candidates.
