What Is the LNMU UG 1st Merit List 2026?

The LNMU UG 1st Merit List 2026 is the official selection list published by Lalit Narayan Mithila University for undergraduate admissions to the 2026-30 academic batch. Understanding what this list means is essential before you check your name.

The 1st merit list is a PDF document listing the names or application IDs of students who have been selected - in the first round - for admission to UG courses (BA, BSc, BCom, etc.) across LNMU-affiliated colleges. It is prepared on the basis of your Intermediate (12th) marks, your reservation category, and the college preferences you submitted during the application process.

Important Note: If your name does not appear in any of your preferred colleges in the 1st merit list, you may still be allotted a seat in another LNMU-affiliated college based on seat availability in your chosen subject. Do not panic - wait for subsequent merit lists if needed.

How Is the LNMU UG Merit List Prepared?

The selection process for LNMU UG admission is merit-based and transparent. This section explains the exact criteria used to rank applicants in the selection list.

The LNMU UG merit list is prepared using the following factors:

  • Intermediate (Class 12) Marks - Your academic performance in the qualifying examination is the primary criterion.
  • Reservation Policy - Seats are allocated as per Bihar government reservation norms (SC, ST, OBC, EWS, etc.).
  • College & Subject Preference - Your college choices submitted during the application are considered for seat allotment.

Because all three factors are taken together, a student with high marks may still not receive their first-choice college if reserved seats are filled. The university allots seats to maximize placement across all applicants fairly.

How to Download LNMU UG 1st Merit List 2026 - Step-by-Step Guide

Downloading the LNMU 1st merit list is simple and takes only a few minutes. Follow the steps below carefully to check your name and download your seat allotment letter.

Step 1 - Visit the Official LNMU Website

Go to the official Lalit Narayan Mithila University website: https://lnmu.ac.in

Step 2 - Find the Merit List Link

On the homepage, look for the "Latest News / Announcements / Circulars" section. Click on the link labelled "Download UG Admission 2026-30 1st Merit List".

Step 3 - Access the Admission Portal

You will be redirected to the LNMU UG Admission Portal (powered by Samarth). Here, you can:

  • Download the subject-wise merit list in PDF directly from the homepage.
  • Log in to your student account to view your personal seat allotment letter.

Step 4 - Download Subject-Wise PDF (Without Login)

To check the general merit list for any subject or college, download the subject-wise PDF available on the portal without needing to log in.

Step 5 - Download Your Seat Allotment Letter (With Login)

To download your individual seat allotment letter:

  • Enter your Application ID and Password on the Student Login portal.
  • Click the "Log In" button.
  • Your college seat allotment letter will be available for download.

LNMU UG Admission 2026 - What to Do After Checking the Merit List

Once you have checked the merit list, your next steps depend on whether or not your name appears. Here is a clear guide on what to do next.

If Your Name Appears in the 1st Merit List

  • Log in to the student portal using your Application ID and Password.
  • Download your seat allotment letter.
  • Report to your allotted college within the admission date window (to be announced by LNMU).
  • Carry all required original documents and pay the admission fee as specified.

If Your Name Does Not Appear in the 1st Merit List

  • Do not lose hope. LNMU typically releases a 2nd and 3rd merit list in subsequent rounds.
  • Keep checking the official website regularly for further announcements.
  • Ensure your application details and documents are correct so you remain eligible for the next rounds.

Documents Required for LNMU UG Admission 2026

When reporting to your allotted college, carry the following documents for verification:

  • Seat allotment letter (downloaded from the portal)
  • Class 10 (Matriculation) mark sheet and certificate
  • Class 12 (Intermediate) mark sheet and certificate
  • Caste/Category certificate (if applicable - SC/ST/OBC/EWS)
  • Income certificate (if applicable)
  • Residence/Domicile certificate
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Aadhaar card or valid government-issued ID proof
  • Migration certificate (if applicable)
