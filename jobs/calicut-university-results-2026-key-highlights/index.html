University of Calicut has officially released the Calicut University Result 2026 for various UG and PG courses including B.Com LLB, MBA, and B.Ed examinations conducted in 2025 and 2026 sessions. Students who appeared for these exams can now check their results online.

Calicut University Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - uoc.ac.in. The results include scrutiny results for B.Com LLB (Hons) Semester I and II, MBA International Finance Semester III, and consolidated grade card for B.Ed Semester IV. Students can check and download their results using their registration number or login credentials.

Students are advised to carefully verify all the details mentioned in their results. In case of any discrepancies, they should contact the university authorities immediately.
