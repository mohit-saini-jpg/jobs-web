Important Instructions

This is an Adhoc appointment - initially for 89 days, extendable for further 89 days subject to satisfactory work & conduct report from concerned HOD. Maximum tenure is 3 years including all prior SR service in any Govt. of India/State Government hospital/institution. All Senior Residents recruited, including those recruited against VMMC & SJH posts, will be posted in RHTC, Najafgarh on brief rotational basis as per requirement. Teaching experience certificate will be issued by VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital. Candidates must appear for only one specialty/department - appearing in more than one specialty will lead to cancellation of candidature. The crucial date for determination of eligibility with reference to age, educational qualification, and experience is the date of walk-in-interview for the respective specialty. Candidates who are already employed must submit NOC from present employer at the interview. Relieving from present employer is mandatory before joining. Inter-hospital/institution transfer shall not be permitted after joining. Selected candidates must submit original UG & PG degrees and a refundable security deposit of Rs. 65,000/- at the time of joining. The offer of appointment shall stand cancelled if acceptance is not received within the stipulated time period after display of final result. Other service conditions will be applicable as per conditions prescribed from time to time by the Government of India. Candidates are advised to regularly visit the official website (www.vmmc-sjh.mohfw.gov.in) for updates including final result notification. Permanent Registration Certificate from Delhi Medical Council (DMC) is mandatory for both MBBS and PG qualifications.

FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies for VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital Senior Resident Adhoc Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 179 vacancies have been announced 149 posts in VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital (main hospital) and 30 posts in RHTC, Najafgarh (satellite centre), across 38 specialties/departments. Additionally, 24 posts out of these 179 are horizontally reserved for Persons with Benchmark Disability (PwBD/Divyangjan) as per DOPT, Government of India rules.
Q2What is the walk-in-interview date for VMMC Safdarjung Senior Resident 2026?
A
The walk-in-interviews are scheduled department-wise from 15 June 2026 to 25 June 2026. Reporting time is 09:00 AM to 10:30 AM on each date at Room No. 18 (Academic Section), Ground Floor, College Building, VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital, New Delhi – 110029. Each date covers specific departments — for example, 15 June covers Dental Surgery, ENT, Dermatology, Ophthalmology, and Radiology; 16 June covers Medicine, Cardiology, Endocrinology, Neurology, Medical Oncology, and Cancer Surgery.
Q3What is the eligibility criteria for VMMC Safdarjung Senior Resident 2026?
A
Candidates must hold MBBS with a PG Degree/Diploma/DNB in the relevant specialty recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC) — or MDS in Endodontics recognized by DCI for the Dental Surgery (Endodontics) department. Additionally, candidates must be registered with the Delhi Medical Council (DMC) for both MBBS and PG, or must have applied for such registration. The age limit is 45 years for General/EWS, 48 years for OBC, and 50 years for SC/ST candidates.
Q4What is the salary / pay scale for Senior Resident (Adhoc) at Safdarjung Hospital 2026?
A
The pay scale is Rs. 67,700/- in Pay Level-11 in the Pay Matrix as per the 7th Central Pay Commission (7th CPC) (pre-revised: PB-3, Rs. 15,600–39,100 with Grade Pay Rs. 6,600), plus other allowances as admissible as per Government of India rules.
Q5Is there any application fee for VMMC Safdarjung Senior Resident Walk-in 2026?
A
No application fee is required. The walk-in-interview is free for all candidates. However, selected candidates are required to submit a refundable security deposit of Rs. 65,000/- along with original UG & PG degrees at the time of joining. This deposit is refundable without interest after completion of tenure or resignation with applicable notice period.
Q6For how long is the Senior Resident Adhoc appointment at VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital?
A
The appointment is initially for 89 days, which may be extended for a further period of 89 days subject to satisfactory work & conduct report from the concerned Head of Department (HOD). The maximum total tenure as Senior Resident is 3 years, including any service rendered earlier as Senior Resident on adhoc/regular basis in any Government of India or State Government hospital/institution — whichever is earlier.
Q7What documents must be carried to the VMMC Safdarjung Senior Resident Walk-in-Interview 2026?
A
Candidates must carry: duly filled Application Form (Annexure-I) with passport size photograph; 10th class certificate (for age proof); MBBS Degree / Provisional Certificate; MD/MS/DNB/MDS/Diploma Degree / Provisional Certificate; Permanent DMC Registration Certificate for both MBBS and PG (or acknowledgement); SC/ST/OBC/EWS Certificate (if applicable); OBC certificate dated on or after 01-04-2026 (for OBC candidates); EWS Income & Asset Certificate dated on or after 01-04-2026 with Form 16/ITR for FY 2025-26; No Objection Certificate from present employer (if employed); Experience certificate (if any); and PwBD certificate (if applicable). Originals plus one set of self-attested photocopies of all documents are required.
Q8Can a candidate apply for more than one specialty in VMMC Safdarjung Senior Resident Adhoc Recruitment 2026?
A
No. Candidates can appear for only one specialty/department under this advertisement. The undertaking in the Application Form (Annexure-I) explicitly states that the candidate is appearing in only one subject for the post of Senior Resident in response to this advertisement. Appearing in more than one specialty will result in cancellation of candidature.
Q9Is DMC (Delhi Medical Council) registration mandatory for VMMC Safdarjung Senior Resident 2026?
A
Yes, DMC registration is mandatory. Permanent Registration Certificate from Delhi Medical Council (DMC) is required for both MBBS and PG qualifications. However, candidates who have applied for registration with the DMC and are awaiting the registration certificate are also eligible they must carry the acknowledgement of registration application at the time of the walk-in-interview.
Q10Will Senior Residents recruited for VMMC & SJH posts be posted at RHTC, Najafgarh?
A
Yes. All Senior Residents recruited, including those recruited against the posts of VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital, will be posted in RHTC (Rural Health Training Centre), Najafgarh on brief rotational basis as per requirement. The teaching experience certificate for all such Senior Residents will be issued by VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital. Inter-hospital/institution transfer is not permitted.
