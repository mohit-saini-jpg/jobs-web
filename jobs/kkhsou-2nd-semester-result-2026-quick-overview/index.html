KKHSOU 2nd Semester Result 2026 - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 23/07/2026

Krishna Kanta Handiqui State Open University (KKHSOU) has declared the II Semester results for the MBA, MCom, MSc (Information Technology), and MSc (Mathematics) programmes for the examination held in April- May 2026. The result was released study-centre-wise and college-wise, showing Registration Numbers (RegnNos) marked as "Clear" or "Not Clear," along with statistics such as total registered, absent, appeared, and result-withheld candidates. This article summarizes the official result, important dates, statistics, and the steps to check your result.

How to Check Your KKHSOU II Semester Result

  • Open the official KKHSOU result PDF using the link provided in the "Important Links" section below.
  • Locate your programme (MBA / MCom / MSc IT / MSc Mathematics) in the document.
  • Find your study centre or college name and code.
  • Check the list of Registration Numbers under "Clear" and "Not Clear" to locate your own Registration Number.
  • Note down whether you are listed as Clear, Not Clear, or Absent for future reference.

What to Do in Case of a Discrepancy

If a candidate finds any discrepancy in their result, the official notice specifies the following process:

  • Submit a written application along with all supporting documents.
  • The application must reach the Office of the Controller of Examinations, Resham Nagar, Khanapara, Guwahati- 781022, or be sent by email only to [email protected].
  • The application must be submitted within 30 days from the date of declaration of the result.
  • No application will be entertained after this 30-day window.
  • In case of any discrepancy between the e-mark sheet and the hardcopy mark sheet issued by the university, the hardcopy mark sheet will be considered valid.

Important Instructions

  • The university has stated that utmost care has been taken in declaring the result; however, if any discrepancy is detected, it will be processed as per university norms.
  • Any application regarding a discrepancy must be accompanied by all relevant supporting documents.
  • Only applications sent to the Controller of Examinations office or to the official email ID ([email protected]) will be considered.
