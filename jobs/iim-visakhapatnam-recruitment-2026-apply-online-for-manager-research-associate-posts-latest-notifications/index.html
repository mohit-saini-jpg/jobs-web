Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationBachelor's degree for both positions. Post-Graduate degree is preferable for Manager and Research Associate. Specific disciplines are mentioned for preferable qualifications.
DetailsManager, CEESP: Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognised university. Post-Graduate degree / PGDM / MBA in Management, Social Work, Development Studies, Public Policy, Economics, or a related discipline (Preferable). Demonstrated full-time work experience in the field of entrepreneurship, social work, or the development sector. Prior experience in managing centres, programmes, or projects involving entrepreneurs, incubators, skill development, MSMEs, or livelihoods. Experience of working with academic institutions, government bodies, NGOs, or CSR programmes in the entrepreneurship / skilling ecosystem. Experience in grant writing, fundraising, and partnership development (Desirable). Research Associate, CEESP: Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognised university. Post-Graduate degree in Management, Economics, Social Sciences, Public Policy, Development Studies, Social Work, Commerce, or a related discipline (Preferable). Prior experience in academic or policy research, case writing, fieldwork, or publication support in an institute of higher education, think-tank, or research organisation (Preferable). Exposure to entrepreneurship, skilling, MSME, or livelihoods-related research will be an added advantage.
