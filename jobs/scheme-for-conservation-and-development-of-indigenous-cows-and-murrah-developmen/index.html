Scheme for Conservation and Development of Indigenous Cows a Overview

Government of India has launched a scheme for conservation and development of indigenous cows and Murrah buffalo. Eligible candidates can apply for this Sarkari Naukri opportunity. The scheme aims to promote the conservation of indigenous breeds and improve their productivity.

Scheme for Conservation and Development of Indigenous Cows a Expert Analysis

This scheme is a great opportunity for animal science graduates and those interested in rural development. The selection process will be based on written exam and interview. Candidates should focus on their knowledge of animal husbandry and conservation techniques.
