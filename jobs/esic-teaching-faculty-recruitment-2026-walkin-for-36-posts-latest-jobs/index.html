FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the total number of vacancies released by ESIC?
A
ESIC has released a total of 36 vacancies for Teaching Faculty (Super Specialty Department) posts. These positions are available on a contract basis.
Q2Q2. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview?
A
The walk-in interview for the ESIC Teaching Faculty posts is scheduled for 08 June 2026. Candidates must report on this date with all necessary documents.
Q3Q3. What is the eligibility criteria for the ESIC Teaching Faculty posts?
A
The eligibility criteria are as per the latest NMC (National Medical Commission) guidelines, specifically referencing the NMC Notification (30/06/2025) on "Medical Institutions (Qualification of Faculty) Regulations 2025".
Q4Q4. What is the maximum age limit for the ESIC Teaching Faculty positions?
A
The maximum age limit for all the Teaching Faculty posts is 69 years. There are no specific minimum age limits mentioned for most posts.
Q5Q5. How can candidates apply for these ESIC vacancies?
A
The application mode for these vacancies is Walk-in. Eligible candidates need to attend the interview at the specified venue on the given date.
Q6Q6. What is the application fee for the ESIC recruitment?
A
The application fee is Rs. 500 for General/OBC category candidates. SC/ST and Women candidates are exempted from paying any application fee.
Q7Q7. What is the salary offered for the ESIC Teaching Faculty posts?
A
The salary varies depending on the post. Assistant Professors can expect a consolidated pay of ₹1,48,669/- per month, Associate Professors ₹1,73,045/- per month, and Professors ₹2,60,226/- per month.
Q8Q8. Where is the ESIC Medical College & Hospital located?
A
The ESIC Medical College & Hospital is located in Faridabad, Haryana, with the pincode 121001.
Q9Q9. What documents are required for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents and one set of self-attested photocopies, including ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates (if applicable), and category certificates (if applicable).
Q10Q10. What is the selection process for these posts?
A
The selection process will be based on academic credentials and interview performance. Candidates are advised to prepare well for the interview.
