Overview

Dosto, agar aap UPPSC Staff Nurse Recruitment 2023 me participate karna chahte hain toh zaroor padhein. UPPSC ne 2240 posts ke liye online aavedan janvaaya hai. Aapko 21/08/2023 se 29/09/2023 tak online aavedan karne honge.

Expert Analysis

Seedha baat karte hain, competition kaafi tough hoga kyunki yeh 2240 posts hai. Staff Nurse Male aur Female dono posts hain. Agar aapka focus hai General Nursing aur Midwifery par toh aapko is post ke liye kaafi chances milegi.

Who Should Apply

Dosto, agar aap 21-40 varsh ka hain aur Class 10th Science aur 10+2 Intermediate Exam se pass hain, aur aapka Diploma in General Nursing aur Midwifery hai, toh aap is post ke liye apply kar sakte hain. Agar aap Uttar Pradesh ke residents hain toh aapko is post ke liye apply karne ka fayda milega.

Preparation Tips

Form bharte waqt ek galti mat karna: aapko apne Diploma in General Nursing aur Midwifery ka certificate aur other documents saath jodkar submit karna hoga. Physical test tips: agar aapko physical test ka bhagidari hogi, toh aapko apne physical fitness ko banaye rakhna hoga. Exam preparation ke liye aapko 10+2 Intermediate Exam aur Nursing ke liye padhe rakhna hoga.

Salary Insights

Salary ki baat karein toh in-hand roughly 20,700 - 50,700 rupaye per month hogi. DA/HRA aur other allowances hain, lekin exact number ki jaankari abhi available nahi hai.

Job Profile

Day-to-day work: aapko hospital ya nursing home mein staff nurse ke roop mein kaam karna hoga. Field ya desk ka kis tarah ka karyakram hai, ye depend karta hai apne posting ke upar. Department culture: aapko apne colleagues aur seniors ke saath accha rehitana hoga. Real picture: aapko apne patients ke saath accha treatment karana hoga aur apne duty ko puri tarah se poora karna hoga.

Selection Strategy

Stage-wise strategy: pehle aapko online aavedan form bharna hoga, fir physical test (agar uplabdh ho) aur fir interview. Which sections to focus on: aapko apne nursing skills aur knowledge par focus karna hoga.
