How to Apply

  • 1Recent coloured passport-size photograph with applicant's name and date of capture printed on it (JPG/JPEG, 10 KB-200 KB); photo must show 80% face including both ears against white background - polaroid and computer-generated photos not acceptable
  • 2Signature in running handwriting with Blue/Black pen on white paper (JPG/JPEG, 10 KB-100 KB); capital letters not accepted
  • 3Left Hand Thumb Impression on white paper (JPG/JPEG, 10 KB-200 KB); right thumb may be used if left is unavailable
  • 4Category Certificate (PDF, 50 KB-300 KB) - wherever applicable
  • 5Class Xth or equivalent Certificate as proof of Date of Birth (PDF, 50 KB-300 KB)
  • 6Employment Certificate - optional (PDF, 50 KB-300 KB)
  • 7Live photograph captured via webcam or mobile phone at the time of filling the application form
  • 8Candidates must appear in all three stages of the examination; it is mandatory to qualify each stage individually. No exemption from any stage shall be granted to any candidate including those belonging to the Physically Handicapped category.
  • 9Final Merit List shall be prepared exclusively on the basis of aggregate marks in Stage-II and Stage-III. Candidates selected for RO post shall not be considered for ARO or CA; candidates selected for ARO shall not be considered for CA.
  • 10Biometric information (Photograph, Thumb Impression and IRIS) of all candidates shall be captured at examination centres. Candidates must cooperate with security personnel for identity and security checks.
  • 11The Provisional Answer Key for Stage-I shall be displayed on the official websites for 2-3 days, and candidates may challenge any answer key by paying Rs. 500/- per question challenged (refundable if challenge is accepted).
  • 12No Score Card will be dispatched by post; candidates must download their Score Cards from the official websites after declaration of final result, as they will be available online for a limited period only.
  • 13Mobile phones, Bluetooth devices, pagers, calculators, electronic watches with calculator facility or any other electronic gadget are strictly prohibited inside the examination premises. Possession of barred items will be treated as Unfair Means (UFM).
  • 14Merely appearing and passing in the examination does not confer any right to the candidate for selection or appointment. Selection is subject to eligibility, rank in merit list, medical fitness and verification of original documents.
  • 15Information uploaded on the official website shall not be provided under the RTI Act during the course of the recruitment process; factual information shall be provided only after declaration of the final result.
  • 16All candidates already employed in Central/State Government or Public Undertakings must apply subject to production of NOC from their Cadre Controlling Authority as and when asked for.
  • 17All disputes pertaining to the conduct of this examination shall fall within the jurisdiction of Allahabad only. The High Court reserves the right to alter the number of vacancies, modify the examination process or modify/alter the minimum cut-off marks without assigning any reason.
  • 18Examination records will be retained for up to 90 days from the date of declaration of the final result. Canvassing in any form shall disqualify the candidature of the applicant.
  • 19Candidates are bound by a Non-Disclosure Agreement upon registering. Sharing or reproducing any question paper content in part or whole through any medium is a punishable offence leading to cancellation of candidature.
