TG POLYCET Second Phase Seat Allotment 2026 Link: How to Check

This section explains where and how candidates can access their provisional seat allotment result for the final phase of TG POLYCET 2026 counselling.

Candidates can check their TG POLYCET Second Phase Seat Allotment 2026 directly on the official counselling website, tgpolycet.nic.in. To view the allotment order, candidates need to log in using the following credentials:

  • System-generated Login ID
  • TG POLYCET 2026 Hall Ticket Number
  • Account Password
  • Precise Date of Birth

Once logged in, the allotment order will display the provisionally assigned institution, course/branch, and category under which the seat has been allocated. Candidates are advised to download and print this order immediately, as it is required at every subsequent stage fee payment, self-reporting, and college reporting.

College-Wise TG POLYCET 2026 Seat Allotment Details

This section explains the type of institutions covered under the final phase allotment and how candidates can check institution-specific results.

The TG POLYCET 2026 final phase allotment covers admissions across multiple categories of polytechnic institutions in Telangana, including:

  • Government Polytechnics
  • Government-Aided Polytechnics
  • Private Unaided Polytechnics
  • Polytechnics offering Engineering Diploma courses
  • Polytechnics offering Non-Engineering Diploma courses

The official portal displays institution-wise and branch-wise allotment status based on each candidate's rank, category, and the web options submitted during the final phase.

How to Check and Accept Your TG POLYCET Allotment: Step-by-Step Process

This section breaks down the complete seat acceptance procedure that every allotted candidate must follow before the counselling window closes on June 22, 2026.

Step 1: Log in to the Candidate Dashboard

Visit the official portal and click on "Candidate Login." Enter your system-generated Login ID, TG POLYCET 2026 Hall Ticket Number, Password, and Date of Birth.

Step 2: Download the Allotment Order

Check the provisionally assigned institution and branch details carefully. Download and print the formal seat allotment order, noting any fee-related instructions mentioned on it.

Step 3: Pay the Tuition Fee Online

Pay the balance tuition fee shown on the allotment letter via the integrated payment gateway (net banking, debit card, or credit card) between June 20 and June 22, 2026.

Step 4: Complete Online Self-Reporting

After fee payment, click "Self Report" on the portal to generate your electronic joining report. This step is mandatory between June 20 and June 22, 2026 to confirm acceptance of the allotted seat. The system will automatically generate a unique admission number on successful self-reporting print this confirmation for campus reporting.

Step 5: Report Physically to the Allotted College

Visit your allotted polytechnic college in person between June 22 and June 23, 2026. Carry your original Transfer Certificate (T.C.) along with photocopies of all required documents, and register your attendance using the institutional biometric system.

TG POLYCET 2026 Fee Reimbursement Rules

This section explains who is eligible for full or partial tuition fee reimbursement under the TG POLYCET final phase admission guidelines.

Fee reimbursement under TG POLYCET 2026 varies by category and academic merit:

  • SC, ST, and MW Categories: Eligible candidates receive full tuition fee reimbursement as per Social Welfare Department guidelines.
  • Government School Alumni: Candidates from categories other than SC, ST, or MW who studied in Government, Zilla Parishad, Navodaya, or Welfare Department Residential Schools receive full fee reimbursement, irrespective of the college fee charged.
  • High Merit Candidates: General category candidates with an integrated POLYCET rank of up to 1000 qualify for full fee waiver support.
  • Other General Candidates: Those not meeting the above criteria receive reimbursement up to a maximum cap of ₹14,900 for private college fees, or the actual fee amount if it is lower.

Slot booking and fee payment are mandatory before attending verification, and physical presence at the chosen Helpline Center on the reserved date and time is compulsory.

Important Guidelines for TG POLYCET Final Phase Candidates

This section highlights the key counselling rules every candidate must be aware of before and after the final phase allotment.

  • Options submitted during the first phase are not considered for the final phase. All candidates must exercise fresh web options.
  • Certificate verification for special categories (PHC, CAP, NCC, Sports, Anglo-Indian) was conducted only in the first phase; there is no verification window for these categories in the final phase.
  • NCC and Sports category candidates must still exercise fresh choices in the final phase, even though their certificates were verified earlier.
  • If a candidate secures a new seat in the final phase, their previous (first phase) seat is automatically vacated and reallotted to the next eligible candidate.
  • No seat dropouts or cancellations are permitted after the centralized internal sliding round concludes.
  • Candidates who fail to physically report at their allotted college will not be permitted to participate in the centralized internal sliding round.
  • Vacancies arising after internal sliding will not be filled through spot admissions; such seats are reserved for second-year lateral entry through the LPCET pathway.
  • Candidates must register biometric attendance at their allotted college during physical reporting.
  • All online payments should be made from the candidate's or parent's own bank account, since refunds (in case of cancellation) are credited back to the same account.
  • Passwords generated through the registration link must be kept confidential; sharing credentials or leaving an internet center session logged in can lead to unauthorized changes to submitted options.

TG POLYCET 2026 Internal Sliding Process

This section explains the branch-upgrade facility available to candidates after they have physically reported to their allotted college.

Candidates who have physically reported to their allotted institution can apply for a branch change within the same college through the centralized internal sliding facility:

  • Option Entry Dates: June 27 to June 28, 2026 (open only to candidates who have completed physical reporting)
  • Sliding Allotment Date: On or before June 30, 2026
  • New Allotment Order Download: July 1, 2026 (for candidates who receive a branch upgrade)

Candidates who receive an upgraded branch through internal sliding must report immediately to their new branch within the same college campus after downloading the new allotment order.
