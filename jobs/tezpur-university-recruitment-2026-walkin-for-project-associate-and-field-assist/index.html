FAQs

Q1What is the last date to send the advance copy of the CV?
A
The last date to send the advance copy of your Curriculum Vitae (CV) via email is 21st June 2026. Ensure it is sent to the provided email addresses.
Q2When is the Walk-in Interview scheduled?
A
The Walk-in Interview for the Project Associate and Field Assistant positions at Tezpur University is scheduled for 23rd June 2026. Candidates should report at the venue by 10:00 AM.
Q3What are the educational qualifications required for Project Associate?
A
For the Project Associate position, candidates need to have an MA or Ph.D. in Linguistics, Applied Linguistics, Computational Linguistics, or allied subjects. UGC NET qualification is preferred.
Q4What is the salary for the Field Assistant position?
A
The Field Assistant position offers a consolidated salary of ₹20,000/- per month, excluding 18% GST. This is a project-based role.
Q5What is the maximum age limit for these posts?
A
The maximum age limit for both Project Associate and Field Assistant positions is 35 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per the Government of India rules.
Q6How many vacancies are available in total?
A
Tezpur University has announced a total of 6 vacancies for the Project Associate and Field Assistant positions combined.
Q7What documents should I carry for the Walk-in Interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original and self-attested photocopies of mark sheets, certificates from 10th standard onwards, caste certificate (if applicable), experience certificate (if any), and a copy of their recent signed CV.
Q8Is there any application fee for this recruitment?
A
No application fee is mentioned in the official notification for this recruitment drive. Candidates can attend the walk-in interview without paying any fee.
Q9What is the selection process for these positions?
A
The selection process for these project-based positions will be through a Walk-in Interview, which will be conducted in a blended mode.
Q10Where is Tezpur University located?
A
Tezpur University is located in Tezpur, Assam, with the pincode 784028. The walk-in interview will be held at the university campus.
