Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is MIDHANI Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

MIDHANI Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is a notification released by Mishra Dhatu Nigam Limited (MIDHANI), a Mini Ratna‑I PSU under the Ministry of Defence, for engagement of Graduate and Technician (Diploma) Apprentice Trainees for one year at its Hyderabad unit. The recruitment is being conducted under the Apprentices (Amendment) Act 1973/1992 for a total of 43 apprentice seats.

Q2. How many vacancies are there in MIDHANI Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

A total of 43 apprentice vacancies have been announced under Advertisement No. MDN/HR/AT/02/26 dated 18/05/2026. Out of these, 23 posts are for Graduate Apprentice Trainees (GATs) and 20 posts are for Technician (Diploma) Apprentice Trainees (TATs) across Metallurgy, Mechanical, Electrical/EEE, Civil and ECE/IT disciplines.

Q3. What are the important dates for MIDHANI Apprentice 2026?

The advertisement for MIDHANI Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is dated 18 May 2026. The last date for NATS portal registration and receipt of physical application by registered post/courier at MIDHANI is 03 June 2026 up to 5:00 PM.

Q4. What is the eligibility criteria for MIDHANI Graduate and Technician Apprentice posts?

For Graduate Apprentice Trainee (GAT), candidates must have a degree in Engineering or Technology in the relevant discipline from a recognized university or institution, and be passouts of 2023, 2024, 2025 or 2026. For Technician (Diploma) Apprentice Trainee (TAT), candidates must hold a Diploma in Engineering or Technology in the relevant discipline from a State Technical Board or recognized university, also passing out in 2023, 2024, 2025 or 2026.

Q5. Are candidates with prior apprenticeship or work experience eligible to apply?

Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing apprenticeship training under the Apprentices (Amendment) Act 1973 are not eligible to apply. Similarly, candidates having one year or more of work experience after acquiring the required qualification are not eligible for MIDHANI Apprentice Recruitment 2026.

Q6. What is the age limit for MIDHANI Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

The notification states that age will be as per Apprenticeship Rules. No specific upper or lower age limit has been separately mentioned in the advertisement, so candidates must meet the age conditions prescribed in the Apprenticeship Rules in force.

Q7. What is the selection process for MIDHANI Apprentice Recruitment 2026?

Selection will be based on a merit list prepared against reservation categories using academic performance. Merit will be calculated by giving 30% weightage to SSC (10th) marks and 70% weightage to marks obtained in Graduation (for GAT) or Diploma (for TAT) in the relevant engineering discipline.

Q8. What is the stipend for MIDHANI Graduate and Technician Apprentices?

Selected Graduate Apprentice Trainees (GATs) will be paid a monthly stipend of Rs. 12,300. Technician (Diploma) Apprentice Trainees (TATs) will receive a monthly stipend of Rs. 10,900 during the one‑year apprenticeship training period.
