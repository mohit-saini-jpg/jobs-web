Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Junior Clerk/Copyist posts?

The last date to submit the application for the Junior Clerk/Copyist posts is 17th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the designated address before this date.

Q2. What are the educational qualifications required for this recruitment?

Candidates must have completed a +3 examination or equivalent from a recognized university, possess a Diploma in Computer Application, and have passed a test in Oriya equivalent to the M.E. standard.

Q3. How many vacancies are available for the Junior Clerk/Copyist position?

There are a total of 2 vacancies announced for the Junior Clerk/Copyist post in the District Court Rayagada recruitment.

The selected candidates will receive a salary ranging from Rs. 19,900 to Rs. 63,200 per month, as per Level-04 in the Pay Matrix under ORSP Rules, 2017.

The application mode for this recruitment is offline. Candidates need to send their filled application forms to the official address mentioned in the notification.

Q6. What is the selection process for the Junior Clerk/Copyist posts?

The selection process includes a Written Test (English, Arithmetic, General Knowledge), a Computer Science Test (Practical), and a Viva-Voce Test.

Q7. Is there any application fee for the Junior Clerk/Copyist posts?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment.

Q8. Where can I find the official notification and application form?

The official notification and application form can be downloaded from the official website of the District Court Rayagada, which is https://rayagada.dcourts.gov.in.
