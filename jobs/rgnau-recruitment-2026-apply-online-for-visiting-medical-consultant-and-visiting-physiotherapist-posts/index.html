RGNAU Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for Visiting Medical Consultant and Visiting Physiotherapist Posts

Short Information

Candidates below 60 years (Medical Consultant) and below 50 years (Physiotherapist) who have MD/DNB in relevant specialization or Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) with minimum 5 years of experience are eligible to apply for these Visiting Medical Consultant and Visiting Physiotherapist vacancies. The online application process for RGNAU Recruitment 2026 has started and will remain open until 07 June 2026.
