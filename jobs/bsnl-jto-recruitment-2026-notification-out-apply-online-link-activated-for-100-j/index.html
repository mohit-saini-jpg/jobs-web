BSNL JTO Recruitment 2026 : Physical Eligibility

Details
  1. The competition level for BSNL JTO Direct Recruitment 2026 is expected to be very high, as only 100 posts are available across 21 Circles for an All-India open market recruitment targeting Engineering graduates in Electronics/Telecom/CS/IT and related disciplines. Karnataka (26 seats), Kerala (15 seats), and Maharashtra (16 seats) will attract the highest number of serious applicants since they have the most vacancies and are also home to a large pool of engineering graduates. Circles like Rajasthan, Sikkim, Tamilnadu, and Uttarakhand with only 1 seat each will be the most competitive on a per-seat basis, where even a fraction of a mark can decide selection
  2. The 480-mark, 3-hour CBT with sectional minimum qualifying marks makes it critical to maintain balance across all three sections — candidates who neglect the General Ability section or fail to meet the per-section cut-off (40%/35%) will be eliminated regardless of their total score. The Rs. 2,000 exam fee and the mandatory 5-year bond (Rs. 6 Lakhs) will filter out casual applicants, leaving mostly serious engineering graduates. The Circle preference system rewards candidates who opt for more circles, as it gives BSNL flexibility to allocate based on merit and availability

BSNL JTO Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Both internal BSNL employees and external (open market) candidates fulfilling the prescribed eligibility conditions are eligible to apply. JTO is a Circle Cadre post; candidates will be allotted a Circle depending on vacancies, their preference, and merit. Options once exercised shall be final — change of Circle afterwards will not be allowed. Candidates are strongly advised to select all 21 Circles in order of preference to maximise the possibility of selection. Selecting a limited number of Circles restricts candidature only to those opted Circles. All selected candidates must execute a Bond of Rs. 6 Lakhs to serve BSNL for at least 5 years from the date of appointment as Junior Telecom Officer. The admit card will be available only for download from the BSNL website — it will NOT be sent by post or email. Issuance of admit card does not confer any right for appointment. After closing of registration period, an Editing Window will be opened for 7 days — candidates can edit selective fields only. No further editing will be allowed after the editing period. Candidates applying for UR vacancy can also be SC/ST/EWS/OBC/Ex-Serviceman/BSNL employees subject to meeting all general eligibility standards — category relaxation in such cases cannot be availed. Candidature is liable to be rejected at any stage if any information provided is found false or not in conformity with the eligibility criteria mentioned in the advertisement. No request for withdrawal of candidature will be entertained. Any dispute will be subject to the courts/tribunals having jurisdiction at New Delhi. Canvassing in any form or influencing officials related to the selection/recruitment process will result in immediate disqualification of the candidate. Candidates who have a living spouse and have entered into another marriage, or who have married a person with a living spouse, are not eligible for appointment. BSNL reserves the right to modify/amend any terms and conditions of this recruitment at any point of time. Dispute, if any, shall be within the jurisdiction of Delhi only. BSNL Management reserves the right to cancel the recruitment exercise fully/partially at any stage at its discretion without notice. BSNL does not seek payments of any kind other than the registration fee and post-exam grievance fee — candidates are advised to beware of fraudulent advertisements.
