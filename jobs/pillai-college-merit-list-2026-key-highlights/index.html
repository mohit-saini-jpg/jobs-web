Pillai College Merit List 2026 has been released for undergraduate admissions at Pillai College of Arts, Commerce and Science (PCACS), New Panvel for the 2026-27 academic session. Candidates who applied for various UG programmes such as B.Com, B.Sc, BMS, BBA, BCA and other courses under Mumbai University can now check the PCACS first merit list online. Pillai College Merit List 2026 has been released online on the official website - https://pcacs.ac.in/ through the dedicated admission portal admission.pcacs.ac.in.

Students can log in to the admission portal or visit the college website to view and download the course-wise first merit list PDF. After checking their name and details in the merit list, candidates must verify all information carefully and contact the college admission office immediately in case of any discrepancy or error.

How to Download Pillai College Merit List 2026

Follow these easy steps to check and download Pillai College Merit List 2026 online:

  • Visit the official Pillai College website - https://pcacs.ac.in/
  • On the homepage, click on the "Admissions" or "Merit List 2026" link, or go to the admission portal admission.pcacs.ac.in.
  • Select the relevant course such as B.Com, BMS, B.Sc, BBA, BCA or other UG programme.
  • Click on the Pillai College 2026 first merit list link for your course.
  • The merit list will open as a PDF or webpage showing candidate names, application numbers, and cut off details.
  • Search your name or application number in the list to check if you have been shortlisted.
  • Download and save the merit list PDF and note the further instructions regarding document verification, fee payment and admission confirmation.
