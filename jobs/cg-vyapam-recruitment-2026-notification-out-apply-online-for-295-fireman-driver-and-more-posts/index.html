CG Vyapam Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 295 Fireman, Driver and More Posts

Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board (CG Vyapam) on behalf of Fire & Emergency Services and SDRF

Posts: Approx. 295 Posts (including backlog) Last Date: 05 June 2026 (up to 05:00 PM) Online

Short Information

Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board (CG Vyapam) has published the Fireman (NSF26) Written Recruitment Examination Notification 2026 on 8 May 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill various posts under Nagar Sena, Fire & Emergency Services and SDRF including Fireman, Vehicle Driver, Station Officer, and other positions. Eligible local residents of Chhattisgarh who have qualified the Physical Efficiency Test (PET) can apply online at vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in. The online application window is open from 08 May 2026 to 05 June 2026. The written examination is tentatively scheduled for 19 Ju

Selection Process

  • Selection is a multi-stage process:

  • Physical Efficiency Test (PET) – Already conducted by the department (candidates who qualified PET only eligible). Written Examination – 100 marks

  • 100 objective questions

  • 2 hours 15 minutes duration. Document Verification – Final stage.

How To Apply

  • Candidates must register/create profile on CG Vyapam website, then fill and submit the online application form. Upload scanned photo and signature as per specifications. Take a printout of the submitted application for future reference.

  • Documents Required (for verification later):

  • Chhattisgarh Local Resident Certificate

  • Educational Qualification Certificates

Important Instructions

  • Only candidates who qualified the Physical Efficiency Test are eligible to apply. Only one application per candidate is allowed. Admit Card will be available for download from 13 July 2026. Carry original photo ID (Aadhaar, Voter ID, Driving Licence, PAN) along with Admit Card on exam day. No electronic devices, calculators, or study material allowed inside the examination hall. Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification. All future updates will be available only on the official Vyapam website.
