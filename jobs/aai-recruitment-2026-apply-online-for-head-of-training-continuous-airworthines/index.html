Who Can Apply for AAI FIU NSOP Recruitment 2026?

Candidates having the required aviation qualifications and experience can apply for AAI NSOP Post Holder Recruitment 2026. The eligibility details are as follows: Head of Training: Candidates should have or have held Indian ATPL/CPL/FATA with appropriate ratings for the proposed category of operation. Head of Training Experience: Candidates should meet the standards for appointment as DE/TRE/TRI/LTC/Check Pilot except on-type experience. Alternative Experience Requirement: Candidates should have minimum total flying experience of 2500 hours, command experience on multi-engine or jet engine of 1250 hours and instrument flying experience of 250 hours. CAMO Post Holder: Candidates should possess an Engineering Degree in Aeronautical, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronic, Avionic or Aircraft Maintenance related qualification. CAMO Experience: Minimum 5 years of experience in aviation industry

AAI FIU NSOP Recruitment 2026 - Age Limit

Maximum Age: 65 Years Selected consultants may be re-engaged up to 70 years subject to expertise, health conditions and work requirements.

AAI FIU NSOP Recruitment 2026 - Salary Details
