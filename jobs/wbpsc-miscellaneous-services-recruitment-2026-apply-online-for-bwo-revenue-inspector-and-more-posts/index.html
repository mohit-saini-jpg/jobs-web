Selection Process

  • The examination will be held in three successive stages:
  • Preliminary Examination (Objective Type) – Screening Test only Final Examination (Conventional Type – Written) Personality Test
  • Final merit list will be prepared on the basis of marks obtained in Final Examination and Personality Test taken together. Preliminary marks will not be counted for final selection. Negative marking for wrong answers in Preliminary Examination. Qualifying marks in aggregate (Written + Personality Test): UR/EWS – 40%
  • OBC – 38%
  • SC – 35%
  • ST/PwBD – 30%
  • MSP – 40%.
