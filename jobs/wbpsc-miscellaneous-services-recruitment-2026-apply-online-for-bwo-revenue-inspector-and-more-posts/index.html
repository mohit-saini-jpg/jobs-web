Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for WBPSC Miscellaneous Services Recruitment 2026?

The last date to apply online for WBPSC Miscellaneous Services Recruitment 2026 is 09 June 2026 up to 3:00 p.m. Candidates must complete the application and fee payment before the deadline. Late submissions will not be accepted under any circumstances.

Q2. What is the age limit for WBPSC Miscellaneous Services 2026?

Candidates must be between 20 and 39 years of age as on 01 January 2024. Age relaxation is available for SC, ST, OBC, and PwBD candidates as per rules. No relaxation is applicable for candidates of other states.

Q3. What is the qualification required for WBPSC Miscellaneous Services Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must have a Bachelor’s Degree from a recognized university or equivalent. They must also be able to read, write, and speak Bengali. This requirement is not mandatory for candidates whose mother tongue is Nepali.

Q4. What is the application fee for WBPSC Miscellaneous Services 2026?

The application fee is ₹160 for General and OBC candidates along with applicable service charges. SC, ST of West Bengal, and PwBD candidates are exempted from paying the fee. Payment must be made through online mode only.

Q5. What is the selection process for WBPSC Miscellaneous Services Recruitment 2026?

The selection process includes Preliminary Examination, Final Written Examination, and Personality Test. The preliminary exam is only for screening purposes. Final merit is based on marks obtained in the final exam and personality test.

Q6. How many vacancies are announced in WBPSC Miscellaneous Services 2026?

The total number of vacancies has not been announced yet by WBPSC. The vacancies will be updated later on the official website. Candidates are advised to check regularly for updates.

Q7. When will the WBPSC Miscellaneous Services 2026 exam be conducted?

The exam dates for Preliminary, Final, and Personality Test are yet to be announced. All updates will be published on the WBPSC official website. Candidates should stay updated for official notifications.

Q8. How to apply for WBPSC Miscellaneous Services Recruitment 2026?

Candidates must apply online through the official WBPSC website by filling out the application form. They need to upload required documents and pay the application fee. The application must be submitted before the closing date.
