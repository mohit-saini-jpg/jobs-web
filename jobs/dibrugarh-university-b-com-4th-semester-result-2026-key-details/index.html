What Does B.Com 4th Semester (CBCS) Examination, 2026 Mean?

This section explains what the "2026" labelling on this result actually refers to, and who this result is relevant for.

Dibrugarh University names its semester examinations after the academic session in which the examination is conducted, rather than the year a particular batch originally enrolled. So the "B.Com 4th Semester (CBCS) Examination, 2026" refers to the examination held in 2026 for students currently in their 4th semester under the Choice Based Credit System. This result is therefore most relevant to regular B.Com students of Dibrugarh University and its affiliated colleges who appeared for the 4th Semester (CBCS) papers in this examination cycle.

What Is a Backlog Examination?

This section explains what a backlog examination means in the context of university results, since the term often appears alongside regular semester results and can cause confusion.

A backlog examination is a special chance given to students to clear papers they could not pass in their original attempt, or papers left pending from an earlier semester. It's important to note that the B.Com 4th Semester (CBCS) Examination, 2026 result is for the regular semester examination, not a backlog cycle. Students who still have pending papers from previous semesters should watch for separate backlog or improvement examination notices from the university.

What to Do After Checking Your Result

This section outlines the immediate next steps students should take once they've viewed their B.Com 4th Semester (CBCS) result.

  • Download and save a copy of your result/marksheet for your records.
  • Cross-check your marks, subject codes, and personal details for any discrepancies.
  • If you find an error, contact your college or the Dibrugarh University examination branch promptly for correction.
  • If you haven't cleared one or more papers, watch the official website for the backlog/supplementary examination notification.
  • Keep the result printout safe, as it's often required for semester promotion, scholarship applications, or further admission processes.
