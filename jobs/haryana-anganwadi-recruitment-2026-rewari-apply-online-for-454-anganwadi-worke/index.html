Haryana Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 (Rewari) : Job Overview

The Women and Child Development Department, District Rewari (Haryana) has invited online applications for 103 Anganwadi Karyakatri (Worker) and 351 Anganwadi Sahayika (Helper) supplementary posts across the district's Anganwadi projects. These are temporary, honorarium-based posts, and only female candidates residing in the concerned Gram Panchayat or Ward are eligible. Online applications can be submitted from 20 July 2026 to 29 July 2026, till 5:00 PM.

Rewari Anganwadi Eligibility Criteria

Only women candidates who are residents of the concerned Gram Panchayat/Ward against which the post is vacant are eligible Permanent residency in the applicable Gram Panchayat/Ward (or urban area) is mandatory for all applicants Anganwadi Karyakatri: Minimum qualification of Intermediate (12th); the merit list is prepared based on actual marks obtained in Matric. Additional marks are added for higher education as per rules (Graduate: 3 marks, Postgraduate: 5 marks) Anganwadi Sahayika: Minimum qualification of Matric (10th); the merit list is prepared based on actual marks obtained in Matric. Additional marks are added for higher education as per rules (12th/10+2: 3 marks, Graduate or equivalent: 5 marks)

Haryana Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 (Rewari) : Age Limit

Minimum age: 18 years Maximum age: 42 years Cut-off date for age calculation: 1 July 2026 Special age relaxation: maximum age limit of 45 years applies to widows, divorced women, deserted women, and women whose husband has been sentenced to punishment or has 70% or more disability (based on a certificate issued by the Chief Medical Officer)

Merit and Marking Scheme

Selection of Anganwadi Karyakatri and Anganwadi Sahayika is made by a departmentally constituted selection committee, based on a village/ward-wise merit list. The merit list is built using the following marking components: Base qualification marks: Actual marks obtained in Matric (10th) for both posts Special category preference: Women falling under special categories (such as widow, divorced, or other women eligible under the prescribed rules) are given a special preference of 10 marks Experience marks (maximum 5 marks): 1 mark per year of prior work as an Anganwadi Karyakatri, Kendra Worker, or Anganwadi Sahayika - 1 year: 1 mark, 2 years: 2 marks, 3 years: 3 marks, 4 years: 4 marks, 5 years or more: 5 marks.
