Uttarakhand DElEd Answer Key 2025 - Uttarakhand DElEd Answer Key 2025 has been released by the UBSE soon on its official website through Online mode for ukdeled.com. Candidates can refer to the Uttarakhand DElEd Answer Key 2025 so that they will get an idea about their scores. How to Download the Uttarakhand DElEd Answer Key 2025?

Candidates can follow the below steps to check and download Uttarakhand DElEd Answer Key 2025.

  • Visit the official website of Uttarakhand DElEd - ukdeled.com.
  • Search and click on the notification “Answer keys released” on the homepage.
  • Provide Roll Number and Date of Birth.
  • Click on login.
  • Uttarakhand DElEd Answer Key 2025 will appear on the screen.
  • Download and save it for future references
