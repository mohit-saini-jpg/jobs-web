Candidates must possess an LLB degree and have a minimum of 10 years of experience as an Advocate handling criminal matters in Sessions Courts, along with an Enrollment Certificate from the Bar Council.

No, as per the provided data, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment.
