Eligibility Criteria

Project Assistant: 3 years Diploma / B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) or Information Technology. Preference I: B.Tech/M.Tech degrees in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Information Technology (IT), Information Security/Cyber Security, Data Science, or Master of Computer Applications (MCA) are eligible to apply. Applicants should have specialization in or demonstrated experience in Bioinformatics, Computer Networking, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning (AI/ML), or Big Data Analytics. Preference II: Preference will be given to candidates having hands-on experience in programming, computer networking, cloud computing, cyber security, data analysis, computational biology tools, AI/ML frameworks, or large-scale data handling relevant to the project.
