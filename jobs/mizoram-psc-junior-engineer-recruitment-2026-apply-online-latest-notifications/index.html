Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Mizoram PSC Junior Engineer (Civil) recruitment?

The last date to submit your online application for the Mizoram PSC Junior Engineer (Civil) posts is 19th June 2026. It is advisable to apply well in advance to avoid any last-minute technical issues.

Q2. What is the minimum age requirement for the Junior Engineer (Civil) post?

The minimum age requirement for the Junior Engineer (Civil) post is 18 years as of 1st January 2026.

Q3. What is the maximum age limit for this recruitment?

The maximum age limit for the Junior Engineer (Civil) post is 35 years as of 1st January 2026. Age relaxation is applicable for certain categories.

Q4. Is there any age relaxation for SC/ST candidates?

Yes, the upper age limit is relaxable up to 5 years for ST/SC candidates applying for the Junior Engineer (Civil) post.

Q5. What are the educational qualifications required for the Junior Engineer (Civil) post?

Candidates must possess a High School Leaving Certificate (HSLC) with a 3 years Diploma in Civil Engineering or an equivalent qualification from a recognized institution. Computer proficiency and Mizo language proficiency are also required.

Q6. How many vacancies are available for the Junior Engineer (Civil) post?

There are a total of 8 vacancies for the Junior Engineer (Civil) post announced by the Mizoram Public Service Commission.

Q7. What is the application mode for this recruitment?

The application process for the Mizoram PSC Junior Engineer (Civil) recruitment is strictly online. Candidates need to apply through the official website.

Q8. What is the salary offered for the Junior Engineer (Civil) position?

The selected candidates for the Junior Engineer (Civil) post will be placed at Level-7 in the Pay Matrix.
