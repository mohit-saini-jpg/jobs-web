How to Download TNGASA Rank List 2026 PDF

Follow these simple steps to check and download your rank:

  • Go to the official TNGASA website: www.tngasa.in
  • Look for the link titled “UG Rank List 2026” or “PG Rank List 2026” on the homepage.
  • Click on the link for your category (UG or PG) and preferred medium (English/Tamil).
  • The rank list PDF will open on the screen.
  • Press Ctrl + F and search for your registration number, name, or course code.
  • Check your rank, category, and allotted course/college (if mentioned).
  • Download and take a printout for future reference.

Some colleges also host rank lists on their own pages. For example, Government Arts and Science College, Dr. RK Nagar, Chennai has previously released UG rank lists directly on the TNGASA portal

TNGASA Counselling Schedule 2026

Once the rank list is out, counselling begins. While exact dates may be updated soon, here’s the typical flow:

  • Rank List Release: June 2, 2026 (Today)
  • Counselling Registration: Shortly after rank list publication
  • College & Course Choice Filling: Online via TNGASA portal
  • Document Verification: At designated centres or online
  • Final Admission Confirmation: After seat allocation

Keep checking the official website for the exact counselling dates and schedule, as they can change depending on administrative decisions.

What to Do After Checking Your Rank

Once you find your rank in the TNGASA Rank List 2026:

  • Confirm your rank, name, and course code are correct.
  • Note your category rank (General, BC, MBC, SC, ST, etc.).
  • Check which colleges and courses you’re likely to get based on your rank and previous year cutoffs.
  • Prepare all required documents:
  • 10th & 12th mark sheets
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Community Certificate
  • Birth Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card
  • TNGASA Application Printout
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Be ready for counselling on time. Missing the deadline can mean losing your seat.

Common Issues & Troubleshooting

Many students face similar problems when checking the rank list. Here’s how to handle them:

  • Rank not found: Double-check your registration number and category. If still missing, contact your college or the TNGASA helpdesk.
  • Website not opening: The site may be slow due to heavy traffic. Try again after some time or use a different browser.
  • PDF not downloading: Clear your browser cache or try a different device.
  • Name/Details mismatch: Reach out to the Directorate of College Education immediately with your application proof.
