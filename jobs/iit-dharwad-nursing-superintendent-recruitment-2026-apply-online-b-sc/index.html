Qualification / Eligibility

Nursing Superintendent: 03 Year Diploma in General Nursing and Midwifery with minimum 60% marks Four-Year B.Sc. (Nursing) with minimum 60% marks Experience: 06 years' experience after diploma in a recognized hospital OR 03 years' experience after degree in a registered hospital. For candidates with a full-time Master's Degree (Nursing), the duration of the Master's program shall be counted towards experience (maximum up to 02 years).
