How to Check Siddharth University Result 2026 Online

Follow these steps to check and download the Siddharth University BA LLB (Hons.) 8th Semester Result 2026 from the official ERP portal:

  • Visit the official result portal at erp.suksn.in/resultview.aspx.
  • Select the Session as 2025-26 from the dropdown menu.
  • Select the Category. For BA LLB (Hons.), choose the relevant NEP/CBCS Semester Examination Results option.
  • Select the Semester as 8th Semester from the dropdown.
  • Select BA LLB (Hons.) as your course from the course list.
  • Enter your Roll Number exactly as printed on your admit card.
  • Enter your Enrollment Number in the required field.
  • Click on the Submit or View Result button.
  • Your marksheet will appear on the screen showing your name, father's name, college name, course, semester, subject-wise marks, total marks, and result status.
  • Download and save the marksheet PDF, and take a printout for future reference.

Understanding Your Result Status

Once you access your Siddharth University BA LLB (Hons.) 8th Semester result, you may see one of the following statuses on your marksheet:

  • Pass: You have cleared all subjects for the semester.
  • Back: You have failed in one or two subjects and are required to appear for the back-paper examination in those specific subjects only.
  • Compartment: Similar to a back paper; you will need to sit for a compartment examination in the specified subject(s).

As per Siddharth University's general examination pattern, a minimum of 40% marks is required in each subject to pass, with separate minimum qualifying marks applicable for practical and internal assessment components. Students should verify the exact passing criteria applicable to the BA LLB (Hons.) programme from their college or the university examination department, as course-specific rules may vary.

Documents Required to Check the Result

Keep the following details handy, as available on your admit card, before checking your result online:

  • Roll Number
  • Enrollment Number / Registration Number
  • Date of Birth (required on some result pages)

What to Do After Checking Your Result

  • Carefully verify your name, father's name, college name, course, semester, and subject-wise marks on the downloaded marksheet.
  • In case of any discrepancy, contact your college examination cell or the Siddharth University examination department immediately.
  • Remember that the result available on the ERP portal is provisional. Students must later collect the official marksheet from their respective college or the university.
  • If you are not satisfied with your marks, you can apply for scrutiny or challenge (re-evaluation) as per the official notification, fee, and last date announced by the university. Details specific to the BA LLB (Hons.) 8th Semester scrutiny window are not available at present; check suksn.edu.in/announcements.aspx for updates.
