Eligibility Criteria for JAC Delhi Counselling 2026

To participate in JAC Delhi Counselling 2026, candidates must:

  • Have appeared and secured a valid rank in JEE Main 2026 (Paper 1 for B.Tech/B.E., Paper 2 for B.Arch.).
  • Have passed Class 12 or equivalent examination with a minimum of 60% aggregate in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM).
  • Meet the domicile requirements — 85% seats are reserved for Delhi region candidates, while 15% are for Outside Delhi candidates.
  • Fulfill institute-specific eligibility as detailed in the official brochure.

Application Process and Registration Fee

  • Visit the official portal: jacdelhi.admissions.nic.in
  • Register online as per the instructions in the Admission Brochure.
  • Pay the non-refundable registration fee of Rs. 1500/- online via Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking, UPI, or other available modes.
  • Fill in choices for programmes and institutes.

Note: Applications without the prescribed fee will be rejected.

How to Apply for JAC Delhi Counselling 2026 (Step-by-Step)

  • Step 1: Visit jacdelhi.admissions.nic.in
  • Step 2: Complete online registration with valid details.
  • Step 3: Pay Rs. 1500 registration fee.
  • Step 4: Fill and lock your choices/preferences.
  • Step 5: Download and keep a printout for future reference.

Seat Matrix and Reservation Policy

For detailed seat matrix, reservation policy (including category-wise and Delhi/Outside Delhi quota), and admission guidelines, candidates should refer to the official Admission Brochure available on the JAC Delhi portal.
