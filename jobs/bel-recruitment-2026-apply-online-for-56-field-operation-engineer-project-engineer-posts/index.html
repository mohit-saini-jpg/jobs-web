Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is BEL HLS&SCB Recruitment 2026?

BEL HLS&SCB Recruitment 2026 is a contractual hiring drive by Bharat Electronics Limited's Homeland Security and Smart City Business SBU to fill 56 posts of Field Operation Engineer and Project Engineer-I for project sites in Delhi, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, and Madhya Pradesh.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in BEL HLS&SCB 2026?

A total of 56 vacancies have been announced: 25 for Field Operation Engineer (FOE) and 31 for Project Engineer-I on a temporary/contractual basis.

Q3. What is the last date to apply for BEL Field Operation Engineer and Project Engineer-I 2026?

The last date for online registration and application submission is 08 June 2026.

Q4. What is the eligibility for BEL FOE and Project Engineer-I 2026?

Candidates must have ME/M.Tech/BE/B.Tech/B.Sc Engineering (4-year)/MCA in relevant disciplines with 5 years post-qualification experience for FOE and 2 years experience for Project Engineer-I in Networking/Data Centres/Database administration.

Q5. What is the age limit for BEL HLS&SCB Recruitment 2026?

Maximum age is 40 years for Field Operation Engineer and 32 years for Project Engineer-I as on 01 June 2026, with age relaxation of 3 years for OBC, 5 years for SC/ST, and 10 years for PwBD.

Q6. What is the selection process for BEL FOE & Project Engineer-I 2026?

The selection process includes Pre-Registered Walk-In Selection with Written Test (85 marks weightage) and Interview (15 marks weightage), followed by document verification and medical fitness check.

Q7. Is there any application fee for BEL HLS&SCB 2026?

Yes, ₹450 + 18% GST for Field Operation Engineer and ₹400 + 18% GST for Project Engineer-I through SBI Collect, while SC/ST/PwBD candidates are exempted from fee payment.

Q8. What is the salary for BEL Field Operation Engineer and Project Engineer-I 2026?

FOE receives ₹60,000 per month (Year 1) plus ₹20,000/year allowances with ₹5,000 annual increment; Project Engineer-I receives ₹40,000 per month (Year 1) plus ₹12,000/year allowances with ₹5,000 annual increment.
