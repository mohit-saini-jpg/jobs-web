Job Overview

The Uttarakhand Medical Service Selection Board (UKMSSB) has released an official notification (Advertisement No. UKMSSB/Exam/16/2025-26/448, dated 3 June 2026) for the recruitment of 196 Nursing Officers (Male/Female) - both diploma and degree holders - under the Medical Health and Family Welfare Department, Uttarakhand. Online applications are open from 12 June 2026 and the last date to apply is 2 July 2026 by 5:00 PM. The written examination is tentatively scheduled for November–December 2026.

Qualification / Eligibility

Details
  1. All four post codes require a nursing degree or diploma from a recognized institution affiliated with the Indian Nursing Council, along with valid registration. Candidates must verify they hold the correct qualification category (diploma or degree) before applying, as post codes differ for each
  2. For Diploma Holder Posts (Post Codes 801 & 803), a candidate must possess one of the following:
  3. B.Sc. (Honours) in Nursing from a university/institution recognised by the Indian Nursing Council (INC), or B.Sc. Nursing (regular course) from an INC-recognised institution, or Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing from an INC-recognised institution, or Diploma in General Nursing and Midwifery/Psychiatric Nursing from an INC-recognised university
  4. For Degree Holder Posts (Post Codes 802 & 804), the same set of qualifications above applies. The classification as "diploma holder" or "degree holder" in the post code is based on the nature of the original nursing programme - candidates should refer to the official notification for the exact distinction applicable to their qualification
  5. Additionally, candidates for all posts must have:
  6. Working knowledge of Hindi Valid/renewed permanent registration certificate from Uttarakhand Nurses and Midwives Council, Dehradun, as on the last date of online application (2 July 2026). The NUI card or receipt of application for registration is not valid - only the permanent registration certificate from Uttarakhand Nurses and Midwives Council is accepted
  7. Desirable Qualification (either one):
  8. Minimum 2 years of service in the Territorial Army, or 'B' or 'C' certificate from National Cadet Corps (NCC)
  9. The age cut-off date for this recruitment is 1 July 2025. Candidates must be at least 21 years and must not have exceeded 42 years of age as on 1 July 2025
  10. Candidates wondering about age for SC/ST/OBC should note that the relaxation is given cumulatively with only one other permissible sub-category. Ex-Servicemen from Uttarakhand may deduct their period of military service from their actual age - if the resulting age does not exceed the prescribed maximum by more than 3 years, they are deemed to have met the upper age condition
  11. The benefit of reservation - both vertical (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) and horizontal (PwBD, Ex-Servicemen, Freedom Fighter Dependents, Orphans, State Agitators/Dependents, Skilled Sportspersons) - is available only to permanent residents/domiciles of Uttarakhand State. Candidates not domiciled in Uttarakhand will not receive any reservation benefit even if they belong to a reserved category. Candidates must hold a valid Uttarakhand domicile certificate issued by the competent authority by the last date of application
  12. For OBC candidates: the OBC certificate must have been issued no earlier than 2 July 2023 (i.e., not more than 3 years before the last date of application, 2 July 2026), and must conform to the conditions of Government Order No. 310/XVII-2/16-02(OBC)/2012 dated 26 February 2016. OBC candidates falling under the creamy layer are not eligible for OBC reservation
  13. For EWS candidates: the Income and Asset Certificate must be valid for Financial Year 2026–27. No age relaxation is available to EWS candidates

How to Apply

  • 1Applications are accepted only online at www.ukmssb.org. No offline or hard-copy applications are accepted. The five-step application process is as follows:
  • 2Register: Visit www.ukmssb.org → click "Apply Now" → click "Candidate Register Here." Complete registration using a valid email ID and mobile number. Choose the correct post code and post name carefully at this stage.
  • 3Log In and Fill Application: Use the Registration Number and Password received on your email/mobile to log in. Fill in all personal details, educational qualification, council registration details (Registration Number, date, and validity date of Uttarakhand Nurses and Midwives Council registration), and select two examination centre cities from: Dehradun, Srinagar, Haldwani, Almora.
  • 4Upload Documents: Upload a recent passport-size photograph (20 KB – 50 KB, JPG format) and signature (5 KB – 20 KB, JPG format). Upload a Combined Single PDF File (200 KB – 2 MB) containing the following documents: High School Certificate (mandatory) Intermediate Certificate (mandatory) Uttarakhand Domicile Certificate or Non-transferable Job Certificate (mandatory if applicable) Category Certificate SC/ST/OBC/EWS (if applicable) Sub-category certificates: DFF/Ex-Army/PwBD/Orphan/State Agitator/Skilled Sportsperson (if applicable) All year/semester mark sheets of Degree/Diploma (mandatory) Degree/Diploma Passing Certificate (mandatory) NCC/Territorial Army Certificate (if applicable) Uttarakhand Nurses and Midwives Council Registration Certificate (mandatory)
  • 5High School Certificate (mandatory)
  • 6Intermediate Certificate (mandatory)
  • 7Uttarakhand Domicile Certificate or Non-transferable Job Certificate (mandatory if applicable)
  • 8Category Certificate SC/ST/OBC/EWS (if applicable)
  • 9Sub-category certificates: DFF/Ex-Army/PwBD/Orphan/State Agitator/Skilled Sportsperson (if applicable)
  • 10All year/semester mark sheets of Degree/Diploma (mandatory)
  • 11Degree/Diploma Passing Certificate (mandatory)
  • 12NCC/Territorial Army Certificate (if applicable)
  • 13Uttarakhand Nurses and Midwives Council Registration Certificate (mandatory)
  • 14Pay the Fee: Pay the applicable application fee online through Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, or UPI. Save the payment receipt. Applications without completed fee payment will be rejected.
  • 15Download and Save Application: After successful payment, download and keep a printed copy of the application form for future reference.
  • 16After the application closes on 2 July 2026, a one-time correction window opens from 4 July 2026 to 7 July 2026 (by 5:00 PM). Candidates may log in with their Registration Number and Password to correct most details - except mobile number and email ID. If a candidate changes their category/sub-category during correction, the extra fee (if any) paid for the previous category will not be refunded.
