Job Overview

The Uttarakhand Medical Service Selection Board (UKMSSB) has released an official notification (Advertisement No. UKMSSB/Exam/16/2025-26/448, dated 3 June 2026) for the recruitment of 196 Nursing Officers (Male/Female) - both diploma and degree holders - under the Medical Health and Family Welfare Department, Uttarakhand. How to Apply

  1. 1Applications are accepted only online at www.ukmssb.org. No offline or hard-copy applications are accepted. The five-step application process is as follows:
  2. 2Register: Visit www.ukmssb.org → click "Apply Now" → click "Candidate Register Here." Complete registration using a valid email ID and mobile number. Choose the correct post code and post name carefully at this stage.
  3. 3Log In and Fill Application: Use the Registration Number and Password received on your email/mobile to log in. Fill in all personal details, educational qualification, council registration details (Registration Number, date, and validity date of Uttarakhand Nurses and Midwives Council registration), and select two examination centre cities from: Dehradun, Srinagar, Haldwani, Almora.
  4. 4Upload Documents: Upload a recent passport-size photograph (20 KB – 50 KB, JPG format) and signature (5 KB – 20 KB, JPG format). Upload a Combined Single PDF File (200 KB – 2 MB) containing the following documents: High School Certificate (mandatory) Intermediate Certificate (mandatory) Uttarakhand Domicile Certificate or Non-transferable Job Certificate (mandatory if applicable) Category Certificate SC/ST/OBC/EWS (if applicable) Sub-category certificates: DFF/Ex-Army/PwBD/Orphan/State Agitator/Skilled Sportsperson (if applicable) All year/semester mark sheets of Degree/Diploma (mandatory) Degree/Diploma Passing Certificate (mandatory) NCC/Territorial Army Certificate (if applicable) Uttarakhand Nurses and Midwives Council Registration Certificate (mandatory)
  5. 5High School Certificate (mandatory)
  6. 6Intermediate Certificate (mandatory)
  7. 7Uttarakhand Domicile Certificate or Non-transferable Job Certificate (mandatory if applicable)
  8. 8Category Certificate SC/ST/OBC/EWS (if applicable)
  9. 9Sub-category certificates: DFF/Ex-Army/PwBD/Orphan/State Agitator/Skilled Sportsperson (if applicable)
  10. 10All year/semester mark sheets of Degree/Diploma (mandatory)
  11. 11Degree/Diploma Passing Certificate (mandatory)
  12. 12NCC/Territorial Army Certificate (if applicable)
  13. 13Uttarakhand Nurses and Midwives Council Registration Certificate (mandatory)
  14. 14Pay the Fee: Pay the applicable application fee online through Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, or UPI. Save the payment receipt. Applications without completed fee payment will be rejected.
  15. 15Download and Save Application: After successful payment, download and keep a printed copy of the application form for future reference.
  16. 16After the application closes on 2 July 2026, a one-time correction window opens from 4 July 2026 to 7 July 2026 (by 5:00 PM). Candidates may log in with their Registration Number and Password to correct most details - except mobile number and email ID. If a candidate changes their category/sub-category during correction, the extra fee (if any) paid for the previous category will not be refunded.
