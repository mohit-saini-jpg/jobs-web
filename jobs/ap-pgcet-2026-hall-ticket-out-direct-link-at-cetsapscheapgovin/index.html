Step‑by‑Step Process to Download AP PGCET 2026 Hall Ticket

To help students and also target featured snippets, explain the process in simple, numbered steps:

  • Open your browser and visit the official APSCHE CET website: cets.apsche.ap.gov.in.
  • On the home page, click on the “APPGCET 2026” link to open the AP PGCET portal.
  • On the AP PGCET 2026 page, find and click on the “Download Hall Ticket” option.
  • A login page will appear asking for your registration details.
  • Enter your Application Reference ID, Qualifying Examination Hall Ticket Number, Registered Mobile Number, and Date of Birth.
  • Double‑check all details and click on the “Download Hall Ticket / Submit” button.
  • Your AP PGCET 2026 hall ticket will appear on the screen.
  • Download the hall ticket in PDF format and take at least one or two printouts on A4 paper.

Add a short tip: if the page is not loading due to high traffic, candidates should wait a few minutes and try again, preferably using a stable internet connection.

AP PGCET 2026 Hall Ticket Login Details Required

Before visiting the portal, candidates should keep the following details ready to avoid login errors:

AP PGCET 2026 Hall Ticket - Key Instructions for Exam Day

Give practical, clear instructions that candidates can quickly follow.

  • Reach the exam centre at least 60 minutes before the reporting time mentioned on the hall ticket.
  • Carry a printed copy of the AP PGCET 2026 hall ticket in good condition (preferably colour print).
  • Bring a valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar card, Passport, PAN card, Voter ID or Driving Licence in original.
  • Follow the seating plan and instructions given by invigilators at the exam centre.
  • Do not write anything on the hall ticket except where authorised (such as signature in the space provided).
