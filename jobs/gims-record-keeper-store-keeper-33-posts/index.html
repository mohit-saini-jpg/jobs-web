  • 1Visit the official institute website at www.gims.ac.in from June 4, 2026 onwards.
  • 2Click on the Online Application link for Advertisement No. GIMS/2026/Manpower/NT-02/07 and select the desired post (Record Keeper or Store Keeper).
  • 3Read the User Instructions carefully before filling the application form.
  • 4Fill in all required details - personal information, educational qualifications, category, and other eligibility details accurately based on your original certificates.
  • 5Pay the applicable examination fee online using available payment modes. The application printout can only be generated after fee payment.
  • 6After final submission, candidates may make only one correction in the application. However, registered mobile number, email ID, and reservation category cannot be changed after submission.

FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies?
A
There are a total of 33 vacancies - 15 for Record Keeper and 18 for Store Keeper.
Q2What is the last date to apply?
A
The last date to submit the online application is July 3, 2026. Fee payment deadline is as per the exam schedule published on the website.
Q3Can I apply for both Record Keeper and Store Keeper posts?
A
Yes, but you must submit separate applications and separate fees for each post.
Q4Is typing test mandatory for selection?
A
Yes. Candidates shortlisted from CBT (3x vacancies) will be called for a typing test, which is qualifying in nature. Only those who pass the typing test are included in the final merit list.
Q5Is there negative marking in the CBT?
A
Yes. ¼ mark is deducted for every wrong answer. No penalty for unanswered questions.
Q6Are candidates from other states eligible for reserved category benefits?
A
No. Reservation benefits (both vertical and horizontal) are available only to permanent residents (Domicile) of Uttar Pradesh. Candidates from other states will be treated as Unreserved (UR) candidates.
Q7What is the application fee for Divyang candidates?
A
Divyang (PwD) candidates who are permanent residents of Uttar Pradesh are fully exempted from paying the application fee.
Q8Can I modify my application after submission?
A
Yes, but only once. However, the registered mobile number, email ID, and reservation category cannot be modified under any circumstances.
Q9Will TA/DA be provided for the exam or document verification?
A
No. No TA/DA is admissible for attending the written test or document verification.
Q10Under which jurisdiction do disputes fall?
A
All disputes, differences, or lawsuits will be under the jurisdiction of Gautam Buddha Nagar courts only.
