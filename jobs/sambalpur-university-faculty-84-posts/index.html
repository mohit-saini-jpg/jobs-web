How to Apply

  • 1Downloaded printed copy of the submitted application form from HIMS portal
  • 2Matriculation/SSC certificate/pass certificate as proof of Date of Birth
  • 3Marks sheets and certificates of all Educational/Technical/Professional qualifications
  • 4Research publications and other supporting documents as applicable
  • 5SC/ST/SEBC caste certificate as per Govt. of Odisha format (if applicable)
  • 6PWD Disability Certificate issued by competent authority as per Govt. of Odisha/India format (if applicable)
  • 7NOC from employer / application forwarded through proper channel (for in-service candidates)
  • 8UGC communication certificate from employer/head of institution regarding Ph.D. period counted as teaching experience (if applicable, as per UGC Communication dated 1 March, 2016)
  • 9Candidates above 60 years of age are not eligible to apply.
  • 10Applications must be submitted online only through the HIMS portal; no application by email or any other means will be accepted.
  • 11Candidates must apply separately for each post and pay the application fee separately for each post.
  • 12The hard copy of the application along with all supporting documents must reach the University on or before 26-06-2026.
  • 13No hard copy will be considered if the online application is not submitted on or before the closing date (16-06-2026).
  • 14Application fee is non-refundable; applications without applicable fee will be summarily rejected.
  • 15Application without signature will not be considered and will be rejected without refund of application processing fee.
  • 16The period spent to acquire M.Phil. and/or Ph.D. degree shall not be considered as research experience.
  • 17Only matriculation/SSC/pass certificate issued by the concerned educational board will be accepted as proof of Date of Birth.
  • 18SC/ST/SEBC candidates must submit proper caste certificate as per Govt. of Odisha guidelines and format.
  • 19PWD category candidates must submit Disability Certificate issued by competent authority in the prescribed format.
  • 20Exchange of reservation is not permitted.
  • 21In service candidates must forward hard copy through proper channel or attach NOC from the competent authority.
  • 22If more than one application is submitted for the same post, only the application submitted last within the closing date shall be considered; partial applications are not acceptable.
  • 23Mere possession of minimum prescribed qualifications does not entitle the candidate to be called for written examination/interview.
  • 24Documents should be uploaded in PDF/Image format with readability ensured; photograph should be uploaded in JPEG format with a size less than 200 KB.
  • 25No TA/DA shall be paid for attending the interview.
  • 26Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification.
  • 27In cases of disputes, legal proceedings against the University shall be restricted to Courts in Sambalpur, Odisha.
  • 28Any change or amendment shall be displayed only on the University website; candidates are advised to visit www.suniv.ac.in regularly and remain updated.
  • 29The University reserves the right to revise/reschedule/cancel/suspend the advertisement/recruitment process without assigning any reason. Decisions of the University shall be final and binding.
  • 30The University reserves the right not to fill up any or some of the vacancies advertised without assigning any reason.
