Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 84 Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, Professor Posts

Various Departments / Subjects / Colleges under Sambalpur University

Posts: Various Last Date: 26-06-2026 (within 10 days of online closing date) Online only (via HIMS Portal) + Hard copy to be sent by Registered/Speed Post

Short Information

Sambalpur University Recruitment 2026 offers 84 vacancies for Faculty posts. Eligible candidates with Masters Degree, M.Phil/Ph.D can apply online from 15-05-2026 to 16-06-2026 through the official Sambalpur University website, suniv.ac.in.

Important Dates

Last Date To Apply26-06-2026 (within 10 days of online closing date)

Age Limit

Age DetailsBelow 60 years (Candidates above 60 years are not eligible to apply)

Qualification / Eligibility

Education Qualification(i) Good academic record with a Ph.D. Degree in the concerned/allied/relevant subject. (ii) A Master degree with 55% marks (50% for SC/ST/Non-creamy layer OBC/Differently abled) in a concerned/relevant/allied subject from an Indian University or accredited foreign university. Relaxation of 5% in marks also applicable to Ph.D. degree holders who completed Master degree prior to 19 September, 1991.

Salary / Pay Scale

Pay ScaleProfessor: Cell-1, Level-14 | Associate Professor: Cell-1, Level-13A | Assistant Professor: Cell-1, Level-10 (Odisha Revised Scales of Pay for University Teachers / UGC Scale of Pay Rule, 2019)

Selection Process

  • Screening/Scrutiny (3 stages) + Interview

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the HIMS Portal available at https://himsrecruitment.odisha.gov.in/home, which will be available from 15-05-2026. The online application can be filled in multiple sessions. Candidates willing to apply for more than one post must apply separately and pay processing fee for each application. No other application or CV should be sent by email or any other means.
