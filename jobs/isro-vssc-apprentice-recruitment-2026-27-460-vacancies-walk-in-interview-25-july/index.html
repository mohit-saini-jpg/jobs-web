VSSC Apprentice Selection Process 2026

The VSSC Apprentice selection process is based on a single walk-in interview/selection drive rather than a written examination. Candidates who clear document verification are ranked on merit for panel formation. Document Scrutiny: Candidates report to the selection drive venue with original certificates and self-attested copies; VSSC officials scrutinize documents against educational qualifications and other claims. Application Form Submission: Eligible candidates are handed an application form on the spot, to be filled and submitted along with certificate/testimonial copies at the time of the drive. Merit-Based Selection: Selection is based on the highest marks scored by candidates in the relevant qualifying examination, with due weightage to reservation categories. ISRO VSSC Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27: 460 Vacancies : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1How many vacancies are there in VSSC Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27?
A
There are 460 total vacancies - 273 Graduate Apprentice posts and 187 Technician Apprentice posts across various engineering and non-engineering disciplines.
Q2What is the VSSC Apprentice selection drive date?
A
The selection drive is on 25 July 2026 (Saturday) from 9:30 AM to 5:00 PM at Karthika Thirunal Govt V&GHSS for Girls, Manacaud, Thiruvananthapuram.
Q3Is there an online application for VSSC Apprentice 2026?
A
No, there is no online application. Applications are accepted only in person at the walk-in selection drive; applications through any other means will not be accepted.
Q4What is the stipend for VSSC Graduate and Technician Apprentices?
A
Graduate Apprentices receive ₹12,300 per month, and Technician Apprentices receive ₹10,900 per month, for a one-year training period.
Q5What is the age limit for VSSC Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
As on 31 July 2026, the age limit for Graduate Apprentice (UR) is 28 years, and for Technician Apprentice it is 30 years (Engineering Diploma) or 26 years (Commercial Practice Diploma), with relaxation for OBC, SC, ST, and PWBD candidates.
Q6What documents are required for the VSSC Apprentice selection drive?
A
Candidates need original degree/diploma certificates, consolidated mark lists, SSLC/SSC/AISSE/ICSE certificate as date-of-birth proof, experience certificate (if any), a passport photo, and applicable category certificates (SC/ST, OBC-NCL, EWS, or PWBD).
Q7Who is eligible for VSSC Graduate Apprentice posts?
A
Candidates with an engineering degree with at least 65% marks/6.84 CGPA, a Hotel Management degree with 60% marks, or a general stream bachelor's degree with 60% marks/6.32 CGPA are eligible, provided they meet the other conditions.
Q8Can candidates who already completed an apprenticeship apply again?
A
No, candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing apprenticeship training under the Apprentices Act, or who have one year or more of institutional experience, are not eligible to apply.
Q9Does completing VSSC Apprentice training guarantee a permanent job?
A
No, the notification specifies that mere completion of one year of apprenticeship training at VSSC or any ISRO/Department of Space unit does not confer any right to permanent employment.
Q10What happens after selection in the VSSC Apprentice drive?
A
Selected candidates are registered under a contract on the NATS 2.0 portal immediately after induction, through www.nats.education.gov.in, and induction is based on merit panel position, vacancy availability, and panel validity.
