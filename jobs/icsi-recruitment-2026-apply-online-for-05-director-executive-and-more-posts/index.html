Qualification / Eligibility

Deputy Director (Academics): A Graduate degree in Commerce from a recognized University and ACS/ACA/ACMA OR PG degree in Economics/Commerce/Management/Law or equivalent with 50% marks; 9 years experience. Executive (Academics): A Graduate degree in Commerce from a recognized University and ACS/ACA/ACMA OR PG degree in Economics/Commerce/Management/Law or equivalent with 50% marks; Minimum 5 years experience.
