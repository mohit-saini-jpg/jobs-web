TS EAMCET 2026 Phase 1 Allotment - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 10/07/2026

The Telangana Council of Higher Education (TGCHE) has released the TS EAMCET 2026 Phase 1 allotment result on the official counselling website, tgeapcet.nic.in. Candidates who registered for TG EAPCET Phase 1 counselling, completed certificate verification, and exercised their web options can now log in to the candidate portal and check their allotted engineering college and branch. The Phase 1 result was announced on July 10, 2026, though the authority did not notify a fixed release time in advance, meaning the link went live during the day.

This article explains where to check the TS EAMCET 2026 Phase 1 allotment result, how to download the provisional allotment order, the documents required at the time of reporting, the fee payment and self-reporting deadlines, and the options available to candidates who wish to try for a better seat in the next round.

How to Check and Download TS EAMCET 2026 Phase 1 Allotment Result

Candidates can follow the steps below to view and download their TS EAMCET Phase 1 seat allotment order:

  • Visit the official TG EAPCET counselling website, tgeapcet.nic.in.
  • Click on the "Candidate Portal" or "Candidate Login" link on the homepage.
  • Enter your application number, hall ticket number, and password or date of birth as prompted.
  • Submit the login details to view the Phase 1 seat allotment result on screen.
  • The result will display the allotted college and branch based on your rank, category, local status, and submitted web options.
  • Download and save the provisional seat allotment order/letter, as it will be needed for fee payment and physical reporting.

The allotment result is made available only through the official counselling website. Candidates will not receive the result through email or SMS, so it is important to check the portal directly using valid login credentials.

Basis of TS EAMCET 2026 Phase 1 Seat Allotment

The Phase 1 allotment has been determined using the following factors:

  • TG EAPCET rank secured by the candidate
  • Reservation category
  • Local area status (local/non-local)
  • Preferences submitted during web option entry
  • Availability of seats across participating colleges

TS EAPCET counselling covers admissions to engineering colleges across Telangana, including both government and private institutions offering popular branches such as Computer Science Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Information Technology, and Data Science.

Documents Required While Reporting to the Allotted College

Candidates allotted a seat in TS EAMCET 2026 Phase 1 counselling should carry the following along with them:

  • Provisional seat allotment order/letter (downloaded from the portal)
  • TS EAMCET 2026 hall ticket
  • Rank card
  • Original certificates along with a set of photocopies
  • Original Transfer Certificate (T.C)
  • Domicile certificate, for non-local candidates whose parents have resided in Telangana for more than a decade

Not specified: the complete list of qualification and category-specific certificates has not been detailed in the source; candidates should refer to the official checklist issued along with their allotment order.

What to Do After the TS EAMCET 2026 Phase 1 Allotment Result

Candidates who have received a seat in Phase 1 need to complete the following steps within the notified schedule:

  • Accept the seat online - Candidates can choose to accept the allotted seat with or without the option of participating in the next round of counselling.
  • Pay the tuition fee - The tuition fee mentioned in the provisional allotment order must be paid online through credit card, debit card, or net banking within the prescribed time.
  • Complete self-reporting - Self-reporting must be completed through the counselling portal.
  • Report to the allotted institute - Candidates must physically report to the college within the specified date, carrying original documents along with photocopies for verification. An acknowledgement is obtained from the college after document submission.

Candidates allotted a seat in Phase 1 are required to complete fee payment and self-reporting between July 10 and July 14, 2026, to confirm their admission. Missing this deadline may lead to cancellation of the allotted seat, and the candidate could become ineligible for that particular allotment.

Not Satisfied With Your Phase 1 Allotment? Here's What You Can Do

Candidates who are not satisfied with their TS EAMCET 2026 Phase 1 allotment result have the option to update their college and course preferences for the next round of counselling. This applies to candidates who:

  • Were allotted a seat but wish to try for an upgraded college or branch
  • Were not allotted any seat in Phase 1
  • Did not exercise web options earlier

Such candidates can participate in TS EAMCET Phase 2 counselling. Web option entry for Phase 2 will begin on July 17, 2026, and the Phase 2 seat allotment result is expected to be announced on or before July 22, 2026.

TS EAMCET 2026 Counselling: What Comes Next

The TS EAMCET 2026 counselling process is expected to be conducted in three rounds followed by a spot round. After the Phase 2 allotment, candidates who wish to make further changes or were not allotted a seat can look out for the subsequent round announcements on the official website. Candidates should keep checking tgeapcet.nic.in regularly, as all official updates, schedules, and result links are published only on the counselling portal.
