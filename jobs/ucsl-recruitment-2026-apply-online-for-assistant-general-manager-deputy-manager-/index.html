Job Overview

Udupi Cochin Shipyard Limited (UCSL) has published the Assistant General Manager & Deputy Manager Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 5 Assistant General Manager & Deputy Manager positions. Candidates within the age range of up to 50 years for Assistant General Manager and up to 35 years for Deputy Manager, and holding a Degree in Mechanical, Naval Architecture, Production, or Marine Engineering with minimum 60% marks, or a three-year Diploma in Mechanical, Marine, or Shipbuilding Engineering, are eligible to apply online at the UCSL official website. Qualification / Eligibility

Assistant General Manager (Engineering): Degree in Mechanical or Naval Architecture or Production Engineering or Marine Engineering with a minimum of 60% marks from a recognized University. Minimum of 15 years post-qualification managerial experience in Shipbuilding or Offshore Fabrication or Ship repair or Petrochemical Industry or Marine related Engineering companies or Government/Semi-Government Companies/Establishments. Experience should be in the areas of Hull fabrication/Structural outfitting/Machinery/Piping/Painting/Accommodation outfitting. Deputy Manager (Mechanical): Degree in Mechanical or Marine or Naval Architecture Engineering from a recognized University. Minimum of three-year Diploma in Mechanical or Marine or Shipbuilding Engineering from a State Board of Technical Education. For Degree holders: Minimum of 7 years post-qualification managerial experience. For Diploma holders: Minimum of 10 years post-qualification managerial experience. Experience shall be in the areas of Hull fabrication/Structural outfitting/Machinery/Piping/Painting/Accommodation outfitting/Planning in a Shipbuilding or Ship repair or Offshore Fabrication Company or Petrochemical Company or Marine related Engineering Company or Marine related Government/Semi-Government Company/Establishment.

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for UCSL Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit your online application for the UCSL Assistant General Manager and Deputy Manager posts is 30th June 2026. Ensure your application is submitted well before this deadline.
Q2What are the educational qualifications required for the Deputy Manager post?
A
For the Deputy Manager position, candidates need a Degree in Mechanical, Marine, or Naval Architecture Engineering, or a minimum of a three-year Diploma in Mechanical, Marine, or Shipbuilding Engineering.
Q3Is there any application fee for SC/ST candidates?
A
No, there is no application fee for SC/ST candidates for the UCSL recruitment. The application fee is only applicable for General and OBC category candidates.
Q4How many vacancies are available for Assistant General Manager?
A
There are 2 vacancies available for the Assistant General Manager (Engineering) post in the UCSL Recruitment 2026.
Q5What is the age limit for the Assistant General Manager post?
A
The maximum age limit for the Assistant General Manager post is 50 years. Age relaxations are applicable as per government norms for reserved categories.
Q6What is the selection process for these UCSL posts?
A
The selection process for these posts includes a Power Point Presentation, Group Discussion, and a Personal Interview. Candidates will be shortlisted based on their performance in these stages.
Q7What is the minimum experience required for a Diploma holder applying for Deputy Manager?
A
Diploma holders applying for the Deputy Manager post need a minimum of 10 years of post-qualification managerial experience in relevant fields.
Q8Can candidates apply offline for this recruitment?
A
No, applications for UCSL Recruitment 2026 must be submitted strictly online through the official website. Offline applications will not be accepted.
Q9What is the salary for the Deputy Manager position?
A
The Deputy Manager position falls under the E2 grade, with a pay scale of ₹ 50,000 to ₹ 1,60,000, with a 3% increment annually.
Q10Where can I find the official notification PDF?
A
The official notification PDF can be accessed through the 'Important Links' section of this article. It is highly recommended to read the notification thoroughly before applying.
