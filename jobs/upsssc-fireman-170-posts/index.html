UPSSSC Fireman Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 170 Posts

UPSSSC

Posts: Various Last Date: 29-06-2026 Online

Short Information

The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released an official notification for the recruitment of 170 Fireman posts. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official UPSSSC website, upsssc.gov.in. The last date to submit the application form is 29-06-2026.

Qualification / Eligibility

Education Qualification12TH

How To Apply

  • Apply only through official website upsssc.gov.in using PET-2025 Registration Number and OTP.
  • Fill personal details, educational qualifications, select posts and preferences.
  • Upload photo & signature (auto-populated from PET-2025).
  • Pay Rs. 25 processing fee online.
  • Take printout of submitted application after successful fee reconciliation.
  • Modification of application possible only till 06/07/2026.
