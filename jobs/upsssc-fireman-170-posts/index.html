How to Apply

  • 1Visit upsssc.gov.in and go to the Live Advertisement Segment on the Homepage.
  • 2Click on the relevant advertisement and login using PET-2025 Registration Number + OTP.
  • 3Personal details registered during PET-2025 (name, father's/mother's name, UP domicile, category, EWS, horizontal reservation details, DOB, gender, marital status, contact details) will auto-populate.
  • 4Select the post(s) you wish to apply for and indicate post preference.
  • 5Provide educational qualification details (board name, year of passing, certificate/roll number, certificate issue date, marks details).
  • 6Select Yes/No for desirable (additional) qualification and provide details if applicable.
  • 7Provide correspondence address and other details; verify, enter verification code, and click Submit.
  • 8Pay prescribed fee online (Credit Card / Debit Card / Internet Banking / UPI / SBI e-Challan) and complete fee reconciliation.
  • 9Download and print the submitted application form; keep it safely for future use.
  • 10Corrections in the application (permitted fields only) can be made up to 06-07-2026 via the Modify Application option using PET-2025 Registration Number + OTP.
  • 11Only candidates who appeared in PET-2025 and hold a valid PET-2025 Score Card issued by UPSSSC are eligible to apply for the Main Examination.
  • 12Candidates who scored zero or negative marks (absolute or normalised) in PET-2025 will NOT be shortlisted for the Main Examination.
  • 13Shortlisting for Main Examination will be done category-wise at a ratio of 15 times the number of advertised vacancies based on PET-2025 normalised scores.
  • 14The Physical Measurement Test (PMT) is qualifying and mandatory; only PMT-qualified candidates will sit the Written Main Examination.
  • 15All UP reserved category candidates (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) must mention their category in the application; candidates claiming benefits of multiple reserved categories will be entitled to only the one most beneficial reservation.
  • 16Candidates not domiciled in Uttar Pradesh will be treated as Unreserved (UR) and will not get any reservation benefits.
  • 17OBC Creamy Layer candidates are not eligible for OBC reservation; they must apply as General (UR).
  • 18EWS candidates must hold a valid EWS certificate issued between 01-04-2026 and 29-06-2026, based on income for FY 2025-26 and valid for FY 2026-27, in the format prescribed by UP Government (office letter No. 0-3/2019/4/1/2002/ka-2/19TSI-II dated 14-03-2019).
  • 19PwBD (Divyangjan) candidates must update their disability sub-category in the application as per the latest UP Government notifications on horizontal reservation for PwBD (GO No. 3/2021/324/2021/65-3-2021-78/99TI0SI0 dated 30-07-2021 and subsequent GOs).
  • 20Ex-Servicemen / Disabled Ex-Servicemen who will be discharged from service by the last date of application (29-06-2026) are eligible for Ex-Servicemen reservation as per applicable UP Acts.
  • 21Outstanding Sportspersons must produce a certificate from a competent authority in the format prescribed under UP Govt Notification dated 07-01-2022 (Niyamavali-2022 and subsequent GOs) to claim sportsperson reservation.
  • 22Female UP candidates will be placed in the same category to which they belong; caste certificate from the father's side is the only accepted document for female UP candidates.
  • 23Date of birth as recorded in the High School or equivalent certificate will be the only accepted date of birth; no other document will be entertained.
  • 24In case of multiple applications submitted, only the last finally submitted application will be accepted; fees paid for other registrations will be forfeited.
  • 25The Commission does not communicate individually with candidates; all information is published on upsssc.gov.in only. Candidates must visit the website regularly.
