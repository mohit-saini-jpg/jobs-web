How To Apply

  • Eligible candidates for UPSSSC Combined Lower Subordinate Services Recruitment 2026 must apply online through the official UPSSSC portal before the last date.
  • Step 1 – Login via PET-2025 Registration Number: Visit upsssc.gov.in and login using your PET-2025 Registration Number and OTP sent to the registered mobile number/email. Only PET-2025 registered candidates can apply.
  • Step 2 – Select Posts and Fill Application: Your personal details from PET-2025 will be auto-displayed. Select the post(s) you wish to apply for, verify eligibility, and fill in all educational qualification, community, and other details carefully.
  • Step 3 – Enter Post Preference: In the "Other Details" section, enter your post and department preference priority and indicate preferential/additional qualification details (if applicable).
  • Step 4 – Verify Photo and Signature: Your photo and signature uploaded in PET-2025 will be auto-displayed. No changes can be made. Click Continue to proceed.
  • Step 5 – Pay Application Fee: Pay Rs.25/- via Credit Card, Debit Card, Internet Banking, UPI, or SBI e-Challan. After successful payment, your application form will be auto-submitted. Download and print the application form immediately.
  • Step 6 – Fee Reconciliation & Amendment: If required, reconcile fee and amend permissible details (correspondence address, EWS/horizontal reservation category, educational qualification, marital status) on the Applicant's Dashboard by 25 June 2026. Save a printout of the final application for future reference.
