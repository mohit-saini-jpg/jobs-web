ICAR IARI IT Professional and SRF Recruitment 2026 Notification PDF

The Head, Division of Agricultural Physics, ICAR-IARI, Pusa, New Delhi-110012 invites applications for IT Professional-IV and Senior Research Fellow (SRF) on purely temporary/contractual basis in the FASAL 2.0 project funded by DES, Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare. ICAR IARI Eligibility Criteria

For IT Professional-IV: Master’s (M.Sc./M.Tech.) in Computer Science / IT / Computer Engineering / Data Science or equivalent with minimum 3 years experience. Strong technical skills in Linux, databases, Python, JavaScript, Geoportal development, etc. For Senior Research Fellow (SRF): Master’s degree in Agricultural Physics / Agrometeorology / Geoinformatics / Remote Sensing / Environmental Sciences or related fields with required OGPA / marks and NET/GATE (as per ICAR guidelines). Age: Max 35 years (men), 40 years (women) with relaxations. IT Professional-IV: Create database management system Develop user-friendly interface for yield forecasting Web GeoPortal development with Geoserver / Leaflet Senior Research Fellow: Remote sensing data analysis Crop modelling and simulations Machine learning applications in agriculture

Important Instructions for ICAR IARI Candidates

Positions are purely temporary and co-terminus with the project. Initial tenure up to 1 year, extendable based on performance. No TA/DA for interview. Shortlisted candidates will be informed via email. Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification.
