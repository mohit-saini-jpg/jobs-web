BNMU UG Admission 2026-30

The online application has started from 22 May 2026 and the last date to apply is 05 June 2026. B. N. Mandal University (BNMU), Madhepura is inviting online applications for admission to Undergraduate (UG) Semester-I courses (Session 2026-30) through the SAMARTH portal.

B. N. Mandal University (BNMU), Madhepura, Bihar has released the notification for admission to various Undergraduate courses for the academic session 2026-30. The admission process is fully online through the SAMARTH portal. Candidates can apply for BA, B.Sc, B.Com and other UG programmes.

How to Apply for BNMU UG Admission 2026-30

  • Visit the official portal: https://bnmuadm.samarth.edu.in
  • Click on “New Registration” and register with valid Email ID and Mobile Number.
  • Fill the application form with personal and academic details.
  • Upload scanned documents (Photo, Signature, 10th & 12th Marksheets etc.).
  • Pay the application fee of ₹150.
  • Submit the form and take a printout.
