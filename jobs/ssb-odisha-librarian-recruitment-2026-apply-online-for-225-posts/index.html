Selection Process

  • The selection of candidates will be made solely on the basis of a Written Test.

  • Written Test: 150 marks

  • 100 Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQs) Duration: 2 hours Marking: 1.5 marks for every correct answer; 0.5 marks deducted for every incorrect answer Questions will be based on the syllabus available on the SSB website and answered on carbonless OMR sheets. Qualifying Marks: 40% for UR category and 30% for SC / ST / SEBC / PwD categories. In case of tie in marks

  • aggregate percentage in Graduation / equivalent examination will be considered; further tie broken by date of birth (earlier born candidate selected).

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How many vacancies are there in SSB Odisha Librarian Recruitment 2026?

State Selection Board, Odisha has announced 225 Librarian (Group B, Level 9) vacancies in Non‑Govt. Aided Colleges, out of which 67 posts are reserved for women.

Q2. What are the application dates for SSB Odisha Librarian 2026?

The online application starts on 27 April 2026 at 2:30 PM and the last date to apply and pay fee online is 30 May 2026 up to 11:45 PM.

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for SSB Odisha Librarian posts?

Candidates must have a Bachelor Degree in Library Science or an equivalent degree from a recognised institution. Degrees obtained from outside Odisha must be supported with equivalence/affiliation certificates wherever required.

Q4. What is the age limit for SSB Odisha Librarian Recruitment 2026?

Applicants must be 21 to 42 years as on 01.01.2026 (born not earlier than 01.01.1984 and not later than 01.01.2005). Upper‑age relaxations: 5 years for SC/ST/SEBC/Women/Ex‑Servicemen, 10 years for PwD (40% and above), and 15 years for PwD belonging to SC/ST/SEBC.

Q5. Is domicile of Odisha compulsory for SSB Odisha Librarian 2026?

Yes. The candidate must be a citizen of India and a domicile of the State of Odisha, and must be able to read, write and speak Odia, having passed Odia as a language subject in HSC / equivalent or ME‑standard Odia test.

Q6. What is the application fee for SSB Odisha Librarian Recruitment 2026?

The application fee is ₹500 for General and SEBC candidates and ₹200 for SC, ST and PwD candidates, payable only through online mode within the application window.

Q7. What is the selection process and exam pattern for SSB Odisha Librarian 2026?

Selection is purely based on a Written Test of 150 marks, consisting of 100 MCQs (1.5 marks each) of 2 hours duration. There is negative marking of 0.5 marks for each wrong answer. Minimum qualifying marks are 40% for UR and 30% for SC/ST/SEBC/PwD candidates.

Q8. How are ties in marks resolved in SSB Odisha Librarian merit list?

If two or more candidates secure the same written test marks, the tie is resolved first by higher aggregate percentage in Graduation/equivalent, and if still tied, by date of birth, giving preference to the older candidate.
