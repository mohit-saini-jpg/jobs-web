Selection Process

  • The selection of candidates will be made solely on the basis of a Written Test.
  • Written Test: 150 marks
  • 100 Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQs) Duration: 2 hours Marking: 1.5 marks for every correct answer; 0.5 marks deducted for every incorrect answer Questions will be based on the syllabus available on the SSB website and answered on carbonless OMR sheets. Qualifying Marks: 40% for UR category and 30% for SC / ST / SEBC / PwD categories. In case of tie in marks
  • aggregate percentage in Graduation / equivalent examination will be considered; further tie broken by date of birth (earlier born candidate selected).

How To Apply

  • Eligible candidates must apply online only. The step-by-step process is as follows:
  • Step 1 - Visit Official Website: Log on to www.ssbodisha.ac.in.
  • Step 2 - Read Instructions: Go through the detailed instructions and advertisement before filling the form.
  • Step 3 - Register / Apply Online: Click on the link for “Recruitment to the post of Librarian” and fill the online application form carefully.
  • Step 4 - Upload Documents: Upload latest passport size photograph, scanned signature and left-hand thumb impression.
  • Step 5 - Pay Application Fee: Pay the required fee online through the designated payment gateway.
  • Step 6 - Submit & Print: Submit the application and take a printout of the confirmation page / application form for future reference.
  • Note: No edit option is available after final submission. Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date to avoid last-minute technical issues.
