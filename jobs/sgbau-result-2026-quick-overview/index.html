SGBAU Result 2026 - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 08/07/2026

SGBAU Result 2026 has been declared by Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University (SGBAU) on 07/07/2026 for multiple undergraduate and postgraduate courses under the Summer 2026 examination session. The results cover Diploma in Business Management, Diploma in Financial Management, B.Sc. (Animation), Bachelor of Fashion Designing, B.Com, B.Tech Chemical Technology streams, MCA, M.Com, M.Ed, MSW, and several other regular and back-examination results. Students can check their SGBAU Result 2026 online at the official result portal, sgbau.ucanapply.com/result-details, by entering their roll number and selecting the correct session, course, and semester.

Result Status

The SGBAU Result 2026 for the courses listed above is Declared and available for viewing and download on the official result portal. Results for other courses and semesters not mentioned here are yet to be released and will be updated by the university in due course.

How to Check SGBAU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check your Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University result online:

  • Visit the official SGBAU result portal at sgbau.ucanapply.com/result-details.
  • In the "Search Your Result" section, select the Session as Summer 2026.
  • Select the Course Type (UG, PG, PhD, or Diploma).
  • Select your Course from the dropdown list.
  • Select the Result Type: Regular or Back.
  • Enter your Roll Number in the given field.
  • Select your Semester or Year.
  • Click "Search Result" to view your result on screen.
  • Download the result or take a printout for future reference.

Details Mentioned on the Result

The SGBAU Result 2026 mark sheet generally displays the following details:

  • Student Name and Roll Number
  • Course, Semester, and Session
  • Subject-wise Marks Obtained
  • Total Marks and Percentage/CGPA
  • Result Status (Pass/Fail/ATKT)
  • Division or Grade Awarded

Score Card and Merit List Availability

The provisional score card is available for download immediately on the result portal once the result is declared. Not Yet Announced: any separate merit list, since merit lists are not applicable to routine semester examination results of this nature.

Cut Off and Qualifying Marks

Not Available Officially: SGBAU has not released any specific cut-off marks for these semester results, as cut-off marks are not applicable to standard university semester examinations. Qualifying marks follow the university's standard passing criteria as per its evaluation scheme.

What to Do After Checking the Result

  • Download and save the provisional mark sheet PDF immediately.
  • Verify your name, roll number, subject marks, and result status carefully.
  • Students under ATKT/back category should check the process for reappearing in the failed subjects.
  • Collect the original mark sheet from your respective college or university department, as the online copy is provisional.
  • Report any discrepancy in marks or personal details to your college or the university examination department immediately.

Important Instructions

  • Use only the official SGBAU result portal to check your result and avoid third-party websites for downloading mark sheets.
  • Keep your roll number and registration number handy before checking the result.
  • The online mark sheet is provisional and not a substitute for the original mark sheet issued by the university or college.
  • Students facing errors while accessing the result should retry after some time or contact the university's examination section.
