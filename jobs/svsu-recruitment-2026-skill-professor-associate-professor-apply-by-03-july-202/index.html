SVSU Recruitment 2026: Skill Professor &amp; Associate : Job Overview

Shri Vishwakarma Skill University (SVSU), Palwal, Haryana is inviting offline applications from eligible faculty members for appointment on deputation as Skill Professor and Skill Associate Professor across multiple disciplines. A total of 9 Skill Professor and 10 Skill Associate Professor vacancies (1 post each per discipline) are on offer. Applications must reach the university on or before 03 July 2026 by 5:00 PM. Candidates currently serving in government organisations, PSUs, autonomous bodies, or state/central universities in analogous posts are eligible to apply.
Job LocationPalwal, Haryana
Last Updated13 Jun 2026

SVSU Recruitment 2026 - Quick Summary

SVSU is Haryana's first government skill university, established under the Government of Haryana Act 25, 2016, with its permanent campus at Dudhola, District Palwal. This recruitment is strictly on deputation basis , initially for one year, extendable up to three years with mutual consent.

SVSU Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility

Eligibility varies by discipline and post. Candidates must verify the full conditions in Annexure-A and B of the official notification. The core requirements by category are summarised below. Computer Science & Engineering / IT and Management: Ph.D. in the relevant field, First Class at Bachelor's or Master's level, a minimum of 10 years' experience in teaching/research/industry (at least 3 years at Associate Professor level), and at least 6 research publications in SCI/UGC/AICTE-listed journals plus 2 successful Ph.D. supervised ; OR at least 10 research publications at Associate Professor level. Nursing: M.Sc. Nursing with 12 years' total experience , including 10 years' teaching experience after M.Sc. Ph.D. (Nursing) is desirable. Pharmacy: B.Pharm., registration under the Pharmacy Act 1948, Ph.D. with First Class at Bachelor's or Master's level, 10 years' experience at Lecturer level

SVSU Recruitment 2026 - Selection Process

The selection will be based on a composite score out of 100 marks , structured as follows: All eligible applicants will be called for interview. A candidate must secure a minimum composite score of 60% to be considered suitable for selection. Candidates belonging to SC/BC/PwBD categories need to secure at least 57% . In cases of a tie, preference will be given first to the older candidate, and then to the candidate who acquired the essential degree earlier.

SVSU Recruitment 2026 - Deputation Terms

The deputation period will be initially for one year , extendable on a year-to-year basis up to a maximum of three years with mutual consent. The university, however, reserves the right to repatriate a deputed employee at any stage without assigning any reason. Terms and conditions will be governed by the rules of the parent university or Haryana Government, as applicable. No TA/DA is payable for attending the interview.

SVSU Recruitment 2026: Skill Professor & Associate : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for SVSU Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
The completed application must reach Shri Vishwakarma Skill University, Dudhola, Palwal by 03 July 2026 at 5:00 PM. Applications received after this time, including those delayed in transit, will not be entertained.
Q2Who can apply for these deputation posts?
A
Candidates working on analogous posts - Professor (APL-14) or Associate Professor (APL-13A under 7th CPC) - in Government Organisations, Government Institutes, PSUs, Autonomous Bodies, or State/Central Universities are eligible. Assistant Professors and Associate Professors meeting the full eligibility criteria may also apply for the Skill Professor and Skill Associate Professor posts respectively.
Q3How many posts are available in SVSU Deputation Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 18 posts are available - 9 Skill Professor posts and 10 Skill Associate Professor posts, with 1 post per discipline in each category.
Q4What is the application mode for this recruitment?
A
Applications must be submitted in offline mode only. Applications submitted via email will not be considered.
Q5What is the selection process for SVSU Skill Professor and Skill Associate Professor?
A
Selection is based on a composite score of 100 marks covering academic performance (20), research performance (40), domain knowledge and teaching skills assessed through presentation (20), and interview performance (20).
Q6What is the minimum score required to be selected?
A
Candidates must secure a minimum composite score of 60% (57% for SC/BC/PwBD) during the interview and assessment process to be considered suitable.
Q7For how long is the deputation initially granted?
A
The deputation is granted for an initial period of one year, which may be extended year to year up to a maximum of three years with mutual consent.
