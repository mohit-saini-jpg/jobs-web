AIIMS Bilaspur Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Bilaspur, Himachal Pradesh, has released a notification for a walk-in interview to fill 70 Senior Resident (Non-Academic) posts across Broad Specialty and Super Specialty departments. The notification, numbered AIIMS-BLS(B)(2)(04)26-766 and dated 15.07.2026, states that candidates are not required to fill any online application form. Instead, eligible candidates must report in person at the Recruitment Cell, 3rd Floor, Administrative Block, AIIMS Bilaspur, Kothipura, Himachal Pradesh-174037, on or before 09:00 AM on 21 July 2026.

AIIMS Bilaspur Senior Resident Eligibility Criteria

A medical qualification included in the first or second schedule, or Part II of the third schedule, of the Indian Medical Council Act 1956 (persons possessing qualifications included in Part II of the third schedule must also fulfill the conditions specified in Section 13(3) of the Act). Must be registered with the Central/State Medical Council. A postgraduate degree, i.e., MD/MS/DNB as per MCI rules in the specialty concerned, or its equivalent from a recognized University/Institute. The candidate applying for these posts should have a valid qualification (pass certificate) as on the last date for receipt of applications. Candidates possessing relevant MD/MS/DNB qualification may also apply for certain super specialty departments as per the specific eligibility table below.

AIIMS Bilaspur Senior Resident Age Limit

Upper Age Limit: 45 years (age as on crucial date) Upper age limit shall be determined as on the last date of submission of applications. Date of Birth as recorded in the Matriculation/Secondary Examination Certificate only will be accepted for determining age; no subsequent request for change will be considered or granted. No age relaxation is available to reserved category candidates applying for unreserved vacancies. Permissible relaxation of the upper age limit as per Government orders shall be allowed, subject to submission of documentary proof of the category under which age relaxation is sought.

Tenure of the Post

The tenure of the post is three years, provided the candidate's overall past residency period at AIIMS Bilaspur or any other institute does not exceed three years. Candidates must declare their previous senior residency details in the application form under "Professional Experience" (Point No. 18).

AIIMS Bilaspur Senior Resident Application Fee

PwBD/SC/ST candidates: Exempted from payment of fee All other categories: Rs. 1,000 + 18% GST = Rs. 1,180 The application fee must be paid through NEFT to the bank account mentioned below. The fee is non-refundable. Candidates must upload proof of payment and also produce it at the time of the interview.

Reservation Certificate Requirements

OBC Candidates: Must attach a certificate issued by the competent authority, valid for posts under the Central Government of India, clearly stating that the candidate does not belong to the Creamy Layer. Since the vacancies are advertised for financial year 2026-27, only OBC-NCL certificates issued on or after 01.04.2026 will be considered valid. EWS Candidates: Persons not covered under SC/ST/OBC reservation, whose family has a gross annual income below Rs. 8 lakh, are identified as EWS. Income includes salary, agriculture, business, profession, etc., for the financial year prior to the year of application. A valid income certificate in the prescribed format is required at the time of application and must be submitted at document verification. An EWS certificate issued on or after 01.04.2026, based on income for financial year 2026-2027, will be considered valid for the year 2026-2027.

AIIMS Bilaspur Senior Resident Selection Process

Selection is through a Walk-in Interview, held at the Administrative Block, 3rd Floor, AIIMS Bilaspur, Kothipura, Himachal Pradesh-174037. Candidates are not required to fill any online application form. Candidates must appear physically before the interview board; no request for an online interview will be entertained. Based on verification of original documents, the Screening Committee may shortlist candidates for the interview. The decision of the Competent Authority, AIIMS Bilaspur, in this regard shall be final and binding. The offer of appointment, when made, will be provisional and subject to verification of credentials by the competent authority. A wait list may be maintained. Any vacancy arising due to non-joining or resignation of selected candidates will be offered to candidates from the waiting list, according to merit.

Documents Required for AIIMS Bilaspur Senior Resident Interview

The duly filled and signed original application form must be produced at the time of interview, along with one (1) set of self-attested photocopies of the following documents. Originals must be produced for verification at the time of the interview: Degrees and Certificates Mark sheets Age proof Caste certificates (SC/ST/OBC-NCL/EWS, as applicable) PwBD certificate (if applicable) Publications (Pub-Med, Non-PubMed), Awards, etc. (if any) Annexure-II: Proforma/Checklist for the post of Senior Resident (filled and submitted during document verification) MBBS degree certificate, Internship Completion Certificate Qualifying degree certificate (MBBS/MD/MS/MDS/DNB/DM/M.Ch./M.Sc./Ph.D., as applicable) Medical registration certificate Date of Birth Certificate / 10th Standard Certificate No Objection Certificate (NOC), for candidates working in Central/State Government/Semi-Government/Autonomous
