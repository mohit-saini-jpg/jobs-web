Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationGraduate in any discipline for Zonal Security Officer. Graduate with experience in Banking/related fields and knowledge of Banking, Law, Finance, Agriculture for FL Counsellor.
DetailsZonal Security Officer: Graduate in any discipline preferably from a recognized University/ Institute. Minimum of three years of experience as Deputy Superintendent of Police or equivalent. FL Counsellor: Graduate with experience in Banking/ related fields and having sound knowledge of Banking, Law, Finance, Agriculture and allied activities. Should have requisite communication and team building skills and the knowledge of local language is essential. Working knowledge of Computers. Preference will be given to candidates with knowledge/skills of having conducted Financial Literacy Camps. Preference will be given to honourably retired J&K Bank Officer not below the rank of Senior Manager (i.e. Scale-III Cadre).
