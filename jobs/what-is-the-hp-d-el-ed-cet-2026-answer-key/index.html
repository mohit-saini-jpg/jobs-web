What Is the HP D.El.Ed CET 2026 Answer Key?

This section explains what the answer key document actually contains and why it matters for candidates before the final result is prepared.

The HP D.El.Ed CET answer key is a provisional list of correct answers, released by HPBOSE, that candidates can use to estimate their probable score before the official result is declared. Because the exam is conducted for admission to the two-year Diploma in Elementary Education programme, getting this stage right matters for candidates planning their next steps in the admission cycle. The key released now is provisional, meaning it is open to challenge the final answer key used to prepare the result will only be published after HPBOSE reviews all objections.

How to Raise an Objection Against the HP DElEd CET Answer Key 2026

This section explains the process, required documents, and deadline for candidates who want to challenge any answer in the provisional key.

Candidates who wish to dispute an answer must submit their objection, along with documentary proof, to the Section Officer at the Question Paper Setting Branch by July 11, 2026. Objections can be sent via email or submitted in person at the HPBOSE board office on any working day. Here is the step-by-step process:

  • Identify the specific question number and the answer you want to challenge in the provisional answer key.
  • Prepare authentic supporting evidence reference books, standard textbooks, or other credible documentary proof.
  • Write out the objection details in the prescribed format.
  • Make sure the email includes your name, roll number, question number, and the supporting evidence.
  • Objections received after July 11, 2026, will not be considered under any circumstances.

Alternatively, candidates who prefer not to email their objection can submit it in person at the HPBOSE board office on any working day before the deadline.

What Happens After the Objection Window Closes?

This section explains how HPBOSE processes challenges and what candidates can expect next in the admission timeline.

Once the objection window closes, subject-matter experts appointed by HPBOSE will review every objection received. Based on this review, HPBOSE will publish the final answer key, and the result for HP D.El.Ed CET 2026 will be prepared using this final key. Candidates should keep checking hpbose.org for updates on the final answer key and subsequent result declaration.
