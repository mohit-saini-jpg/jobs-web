How to Apply

  1. 1Eligible candidates for UPSSSC Combined Lower Subordinate Services Recruitment 2026 must apply online through the official UPSSSC portal before the last date.
  2. 2Step 1 - Login via PET-2025 Registration Number: Visit upsssc.gov.in and login using your PET-2025 Registration Number and OTP sent to the registered mobile number/email. Only PET-2025 registered candidates can apply.
  3. 3Step 2 - Select Posts and Fill Application: Your personal details from PET-2025 will be auto-displayed. Select the post(s) you wish to apply for, verify eligibility, and fill in all educational qualification, community, and other details carefully.
  4. 4Step 3 - Enter Post Preference: In the "Other Details" section, enter your post and department preference priority and indicate preferential/additional qualification details (if applicable).
  5. 5Step 4 - Verify Photo and Signature: Your photo and signature uploaded in PET-2025 will be auto-displayed. No changes can be made. Click Continue to proceed.
  6. 6Step 5 - Pay Application Fee: Pay Rs.25/- via Credit Card, Debit Card, Internet Banking, UPI, or SBI e-Challan. After successful payment, your application form will be auto-submitted. Download and print the application form immediately.
  7. 7Step 6 - Fee Reconciliation & Amendment: If required, reconcile fee and amend permissible details (correspondence address, EWS/horizontal reservation category, educational qualification, marital status) on the Applicant's Dashboard by 25 June 2026. Save a printout of the final application for future reference.

Q4Q4. What is the eligibility criteria for UPSSSC Lower PCS 2026 exam?
A
Candidates must have a Bachelor’s Degree (Graduate Level) from a recognised university, and only those who appeared in UPSSSC PET 2025 and hold a valid PET-2025 score card will be eligible to apply for UPSSSC Combined Lower Subordinate Services Main Examination 2026.
Q5Q5. What is the age limit for UPSSSC Combined Lower Subordinate Services Recruitment 2026?
A

Q6Q6. What is the application fee for UPSSSC Lower PCS 2026 form?
A
The application fee for UPSSSC Combined Lower Subordinate Services (Graduate Level) Recruitment 2026 is Rs. 25 (online processing fee only) for all categories, which can be paid via Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, UPI or SBI e-Challan.
Q7Q7. What is the selection process for UPSSSC Combined Lower Subordinate Services 2026?
A
The selection process for UPSSSC Lower PCS 2026 includes shortlisting of candidates based on UPSSSC PET 2025 normalised score followed by a single mains written examination of 100 objective-type questions and document verification.
Q8Q8. What is the exam pattern of UPSSSC Lower PCS 2026 mains exam?
A
UPSSSC Combined Lower Subordinate Services Mains Exam 2026 consists of one paper with 100 multiple-choice questions for 100 marks, a duration of 2 hours, and a negative marking of 1/4 mark for each wrong answer; questions are asked from General Studies, General Hindi, Computer & IT, and Uttar Pradesh General Knowledge.
