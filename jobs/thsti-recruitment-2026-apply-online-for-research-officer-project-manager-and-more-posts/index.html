Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the THSTI Project Positions?

The last date to submit the online application for most of the Project Positions at THSTI is 01 June 2026. However, the Research Officer position has a walk-in interview on 25 May 2026.

Q2. What is the total number of vacancies released by THSTI?

THSTI has announced a total of 6 vacancies for various Project Positions, including Research Officer, Project Manager, Clinical Research Associate, Senior Project Associate, and Consultant (Finance).

Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the THSTI recruitment?

The educational qualifications vary by post. Generally, candidates need a Post Graduate degree in Life Sciences, Pharmacy, or Public Health. Some positions also require specific experience in clinical trial monitoring, GCP certification, or finance and accounts.

Q4. What is the salary offered for the THSTI Project Positions?

The salary ranges from ₹56,000 to ₹78,000 per month, plus House Rent Allowance (HRA), depending on the specific post. The Consultant (Finance) position has a fixed salary of ₹60,000 per month.

Q5. How can I apply for the THSTI Project Positions?

Interested candidates can apply online through the official THSTI website. The application process involves filling out the online form, uploading necessary documents, and paying the application fee, if applicable.

Q6. Is there any application fee for THSTI recruitment?

Yes, there is an application fee. General/OBC candidates need to pay ₹590, while SC/ST and Women candidates need to pay ₹118.

Q7. What is the age limit for applying to THSTI posts?

The age limit for all posts is as per Government of India norms, with relaxations provided for SC/ST (5 years), OBC (3 years), and PwBD (10-15 years) categories.

Q8. What is the selection process for THSTI Project Positions?

The selection process typically involves application screening/shortlisting, followed by a written test or skill test, and an interview. Document verification is the final stage.
