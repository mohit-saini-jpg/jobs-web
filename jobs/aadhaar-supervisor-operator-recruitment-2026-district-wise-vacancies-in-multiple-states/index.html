Short Information

CSC e-Governance Services India Ltd (CSC) has published the Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill multiple Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator positions across various districts in Telangana, Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, and Odisha. Candidates aged 18 years and above who possess 12th Pass/Intermediate, 10th Pass with 2 years ITI, or 10th Pass with 3 years Polytechnic Diploma along with a valid UIDAI Aadhaar Operator/Supervisor Certificate are eligible to apply online through the official CSC portal.
