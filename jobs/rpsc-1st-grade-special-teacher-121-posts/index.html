  • 17Date of Birth proof (10th/12th certificate)
  • 18Valid Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/MBC/PwBD/ESM as applicable — Rajasthan State issued)
  • 19No Objection Certificate (NOC) if currently employed in Government service
  • 20Disability certificate (for PwBD candidates — from designated medical authority)
  • 21Scribe undertaking/medical certificate (for eligible PwBD candidates requesting scribe — must be submitted at least 2 days before exam date)
  • 22Candidates must be permanently/provisionally registered with RCI before the date of recruitment examination — RCI registration number is mandatory in the online application.
  • 23OTR (One Time Registration) on SSO Portal is mandatory before applying. Details in OTR (Name, Father's Name, DOB, Gender) must match Aadhaar/Janaadhar and cannot be changed post-OTR without Aadhaar correction.
  • 24The application once submitted is final. Any correction in the online application can only be made online within 10 days of the closing date by paying Rs. 500/- (correction fee is non-refundable and no further correction will be accepted after this window).
  • 25Providing false or incorrect information is a punishable offence under BNS Section 217 — candidates may be debarred and face criminal prosecution.
