Eligibility Criteria

Candidates applying for the Nagpur ZP Specialist Doctor Recruitment 2026 must hold the relevant postgraduate medical degree or diploma for the specialty they are applying for. For the Physician post, an MBBS degree is sufficient; all other posts require PG-level qualifications. Gynecologist: PG Degree or Diploma in Obstetrics & Gynecology Pediatrician: PG Degree or Diploma in Pediatrics Anesthesiologist: PG Degree or Diploma in Anesthesia Surgeon: PG Degree in Surgery Physician: MBBS (Medicine) Orthopedic Specialist: PG Degree or Diploma in Orthopedics Radiologist: MD Radiology or DMRD For retired government officers , the age limit is 70 years , provided they also have the relevant experience. Registration with the relevant specialist council (Tatam Council) is mandatory for all posts. Applications without valid registration will be rejected.

Selection Process - Nagpur ZP NHM Doctor Vacancy 2026

The selection process for this Nagpur NHM Specialist Doctor Bharti 2026 is straightforward and merit-based, conducted through a direct interview. There is no written exam. Step 1 - Application Submission: Submit your application with original documents to the District Surgeon, District Hospital, Manakapur, Nagpur by 8 July 2026 (5:00 PM). Step 2 - Screening: Applications will be screened for eligibility. A list of eligible and ineligible candidates will be published on the official website. Step 3 - Direct Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be called for a direct walk-in interview. If sufficient candidates are not available for any post, interviews will be held on the first Tuesday of every month . Terms & Conditions - Key Points

Understanding the service conditions is important for all applicants to this Nagpur ZP NHM Specialist Doctor Vacancy 2026. All posts are on-call contractual in nature; the tenure may be extended but carries no right to permanent employment. Applicants must be physically and mentally fit and must have no criminal record . Priority will be given to candidates with local/government experience and higher educational qualifications. If a specialist causes unsatisfactory performance, a show-cause notice will be issued. If the reply is found unsatisfactory, service will be terminated. The entire selection process is transparent and merit-based . The authority reserves the right to cancel, postpone, or modify the recruitment process without prior notice. Retired government officers (up to age 70) are also eligible to apply.
