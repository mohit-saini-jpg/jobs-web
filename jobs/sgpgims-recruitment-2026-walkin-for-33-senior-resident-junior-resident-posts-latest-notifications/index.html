Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies released by SGPGIMS?

SGPGIMS has released a total of 33 vacancies for the posts of Senior Resident (Hospital Services) and Junior Resident (Non-academic).

Q2. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview?

The walk-in interview for the SGPGIMS recruitment will be held on 29 May 2026.

Q3. What is the maximum age limit for the Senior Resident and Junior Resident posts?

The maximum age limit for all the posts is 45 years, with age relaxation applicable for certain categories.

Q4. What are the educational qualifications required for these posts?

The educational qualifications vary by post, including degrees like MS/DNB (General Surgery), MD/MS/DNB (Obstetrics & Gynaecology), and MBBS with internship.

Q5. What is the salary structure for the selected candidates?

The salary ranges from Rs. 56100/- to Rs. 67700/- per month, depending on the post and level.

Q6. How can I apply for the SGPGIMS recruitment?

Interested candidates need to attend the walk-in interview with all required original documents and self-attested copies.

Q7. What is the application fee for SGPGIMS recruitment?

The application fee is Rs. 200/- for General/OBC and SC/ST categories.

Q8. Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?

The walk-in interview will be conducted at the SGPGIMS office in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh.
