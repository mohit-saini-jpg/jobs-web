APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) has officially released the KTU Result 2026 for select PG and UG examinations. Results declared include MCA (Two Years) S1 (R,S) Examination December 2025 (PG-Valuation) and B.Tech S8 (R, S) Examination April 2026 and B.Tech S8 (WP) Examination April 2026 (2019 Scheme). KTU Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - ktu.edu.in.

Students can check and download their results by visiting the announcements page and logging in with their registration number and password (or other required credentials) to view and save the PDF scorecards. Candidates should verify their name, register number, semester, marks and grade details; contact the university examination cell immediately if any discrepancy is found.

KTU Results 2026 Key Highlights

KTU has published results for MCA (Two Years) S1 (R,S) December 2025 valuation and B.Tech S8 (R,S) & S8 (WP) April 2026 (2019 Scheme). Results are available for PG valuation and final year/semester UG examinations; students can download provisional mark sheets from the official portal using their credentials. Direct links to view the published results are available on the KTU announcements page for instant access.

How to Check KTU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your KTU Result 2026:

  • Visit the official announcements page - https://ktu.edu.in/Menu/announcements
  • Find the relevant result link for MCA S1 or B.Tech S8 listed under latest announcements.
  • Click the 'View Result' link for your exam.
  • Enter your registration number and password
