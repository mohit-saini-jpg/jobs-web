COMEDK Counselling 2026 Round 1 Allotment Result- Quick : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 17/07/2026

Quick Overview: The Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMEDK) will be released the COMEDK UGET 2026 Counselling Round 1 seat allotment result today, July 17, 2026, at 4 PM, on the official website, comedk.org. Candidates who filled and edited their choices can log in with their user ID and password to check their allotted college and course. This follows the mock allotment result declared on July 14 and the choice-editing window that closed on July 16, 2026.

COMEDK 2026 Seat Matrix: Round 1 Vacancy Details

COMEDK has published a Round 1 seat matrix of approximately 24,576 engineering seats across participating private institutes in Karnataka for the 2026 session. (One source reported a slightly different figure of 24,626 seats in an earlier provisional matrix; candidates should check the final, college-wise seat matrix PDF on comedk.org for the exact number relevant to their category and course.) The final allotment for each candidate depends on rank, category, choices filled, and seat availability at the time of allotment.

How to Check COMEDK Counselling Round 1 Allotment Result

  • Go to the official COMEDK website, comedk.org.
  • Find the link for "COMEDK UGET 2026 Round 1 Seat Allotment Result" on the counselling section of the homepage.
  • Log in using your COMEDK UGET application number/user ID and password.
  • Your Round 1 allotment status will display, showing the allotted college and course (if any).
  • Download and save the seat allotment letter for further admission steps.

Note: The exact deep link for the result page was not confirmed in the source material at the time of writing. Candidates should navigate to it from the official COMEDK counselling homepage rather than relying on unofficial third-party links.

What to Do After the Result: Fee Payment and Reporting

Candidates who receive a seat allotment in Round 1 need to complete the following within the given window (July 17-21, 2026):

  • Accept the allotted seat on the counselling portal.
  • Pay the seat confirmation/admission fee as specified on the portal.
  • Report to the allotted college with original documents by the college reporting deadline.

Failure to complete fee payment and reporting within the deadline will lead to cancellation of the allotted seat. Candidates who wish to try for a better option in the next round can also use the seat withdrawal window (July 17-22, 2026) as per COMEDK's rules, but should read the official withdrawal/forfeiture conditions carefully before doing so, since specific refund or forfeiture terms were not detailed in the available source.

Documents Required at College Reporting

  • COMEDK UGET 2026 seat allotment letter
  • Counselling registration/verification card
  • Admission fee payment receipt
  • Class 10 and Class 12 mark sheets and certificates
  • COMEDK UGET 2026 rank card
  • Category/income certificate, if applicable
  • Passport-size photographs

This is a general document checklist based on typical COMEDK counselling requirements. Candidates should verify the exact, complete list against the official Round 1 allotment letter and instructions issued by COMEDK, since a full official checklist was not available in the source used for this article.

COMEDK UGET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidates must have passed or be appearing in Class 12 (or equivalent) with Physics and Mathematics, along with one additional approved subject.
  • A minimum aggregate of around 45% marks is generally required (reduced for reserved categories as per applicable norms).
  • Candidates seeking Architecture courses through COMEDK must meet Council of Architecture (CoA) norms and qualify through NATA or JEE scores.

Exact category-wise percentage relaxations were not fully detailed in the source; candidates should confirm precise eligibility criteria from the official COMEDK UGET 2026 brochure.

Important Instructions for Candidates

  • Keep your login credentials (application number and password) ready before the result release time.
  • Check the allotment result only on the official website, comedk.org.
  • Do not miss the fee payment and reporting deadlines, as this can lead to loss of the allotted seat.
  • Keep scanned and original copies of all required documents ready in advance.
  • Regularly check comedk.org for updates on Round 2 and subsequent round schedules.
