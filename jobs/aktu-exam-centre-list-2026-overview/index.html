How to Download AKTU Exam Centre List 2026 from aktu.ac.in

This section targets students with informational and navigational intent, guiding them through the official website.

Step‑by‑Step Process to Download AKTU Exam Centre List 2026

  • Open any browser on your mobile or computer and visit the official website aktu.ac.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the “Examination”, “Circular”, or “Notices” section from the main menu or quick links.
  • Look for the latest notice with a title similar to “Tentative Exam Centre List 2026 - Even Semester” or “Final Exam Centre List - Session 2025-26”.
  • Click on the notice link; the exam centre list PDF will open in a new tab.
  • Click the download icon or use the save option to store the PDF on your device.
  • After downloading, open the PDF and use the search (Ctrl + F / find) option to locate your college by college code or college name.

How to Check Your AKTU Exam Centre 2026 Using College Code

Most students want a quick and practical way to know “Where will my exam centre be?”. This section should be straightforward and action‑oriented.

Method 1 - Check Exam Centre Using College Code in the PDF

  • Open the AKTU Exam Centre List 2026 PDF on your device.
  • Use the search option (Ctrl + F on PC / search icon in mobile viewer).
  • Type your college code (or a unique part of your college name).
  • The PDF will highlight rows where your college appears under a particular exam centre.
  • In the same row, check the Exam Centre Code, Exam Centre Name, and address - this is your allotted centre for the given exam phase.
  • Note down the centre name, full address and centre code for future reference.

Method 2 - Confirm Exam Centre Through College or Class Representative

  • After AKTU releases the exam centre list, most colleges forward the PDF in official WhatsApp/Telegram groups, college websites or notice boards.
  • If you are confused between multiple versions (tentative vs revised vs final), contact your college exam cell, department office or class representative (CR) to confirm the final exam centre.
  • Always rely on official communication rather than random social media screenshots.

Important Instructions Related to AKTU Exam Centre 2026

This section helps students avoid common mistakes and sets correct expectations.

  • The exam centre allotted in the latest AKTU exam centre list is generally treated as final for that exam phase unless the university issues a revised list.
  • AKTU usually does not allow individual students to request a change of exam centre; any corrections are handled through official university/college communication.
  • Students must carefully check the exam centre printed on their admit card and ensure it matches the latest official list.
  • In case of a mismatch, students should immediately contact their college exam cell and follow the instructions based on the most recent official notice.
  • On the exam day, candidates should reach the centre at least 30-45 minutes before the reporting time with: Printed AKTU admit card Valid photo ID (such as Aadhaar, college ID or other accepted ID) Allowed stationery and materials as per exam guidelines
