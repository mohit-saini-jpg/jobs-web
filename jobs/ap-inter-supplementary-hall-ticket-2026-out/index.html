AP Inter Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026 Out

The Board of Intermediate Education, Andhra Pradesh (BIEAP) has released the AP Inter Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026 for the Intermediate Public Advanced Supplementary Examinations (IPASE) May 2026. Hall tickets for both 1st‑year and 2nd‑year Intermediate students are now available on the official BIEAP website.

According to the official schedule, the AP Inter supplementary exams 2026 (IPASE May) for 1st and 2nd year will be conducted from 21 May 2026 to 4 June 2026, in two sessions each day – 9:00 AM to 12:00 Noon and 2:30 PM to 5:30 PM. Students can download their supplementary hall tickets from bie.ap.gov.in or obtain them using the official BIEAP WhatsApp service.

How to Download AP Inter Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026

Students can download their AP Inter IPASE May 2026 hall ticket in two ways: via website and via WhatsApp.

A. Through bie.ap.gov.in

  • Visit the official website: https://bie.ap.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the link “Download I.P.A.S.E Theory Hall Tickets May 2026” or similar IPASE hall ticket link.
  • On the login page, enter your IPASE May 2026 roll number or previous hall ticket number (for 2nd‑year students) and Date of Birth in the required format.
  • Click on “Submit / Download”.
  • Your AP Inter Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026 will appear on the screen. Download the PDF and take 2–3 printouts.

B. Through BIEAP WhatsApp “Mana Mitra” service

  • Save the official BIEAP WhatsApp number 9552300009 on your mobile phone.
  • Open WhatsApp and send a “Hi” message to this number.
  • From the menu, select “Education Services” → “Inter Hall Ticket Download” (IPASE option when shown).
  • Enter your name, date of birth, Aadhaar/roll number or as asked.
  • Follow the prompts to receive and download your hall ticket directly on your phone and then take a printout.

Details Mentioned on AP Inter Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026

After downloading, students must verify the following information:

  • Student’s name and photograph.
  • Hall ticket number / IPASE May 2026 roll number.
  • Class and stream: 1st year / 2nd year, General/Vocational.
  • Exam name: IPASE May 2026 (Intermediate Public Advanced Supplementary Examinations).
  • Subject‑wise exam dates and timings (between 21 May and 4 June 2026).
  • Exam centre name, code and full address.
  • Important instructions, reporting time and guidelines.

If there is any error in your name, photograph or subject details, contact your college principal or BIEAP immediately for guidance.

Documents Required with AP Inter Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026

On each exam day, students must carry:

  • A printed copy of the AP Inter Supplementary Hall Ticket 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid ID proof, such as: School/college ID card, or Aadhaar Card, or Any other ID accepted by the board, if mentioned.
  • School/college ID card, or
  • Aadhaar Card, or
  • Any other ID accepted by the board, if mentioned.

Entry to the exam hall will not be allowed without the hall ticket. Principal’s signature is not required on the IPASE hall ticket as per the latest update.
