How to Download COMEDK UGET Rank Card 2026 Online

Step‑by‑Step Process to Download Rank Card

  • Visit the official COMEDK website - comedk.org.
  • Click on the “COMEDK UGET 2026 Login” or “Candidate Login” / “UGET Results” link on the homepage.
  • Enter your application number/user ID and password in the login window.
  • After successful login, click on the COMEDK UGET 2026 Rank Card / Scorecard link.
  • Your COMEDK 2026 rank card will appear on the screen, showing your details, marks, and rank.
  • Download the PDF and save it; take multiple printouts for counselling and admission.

Details Mentioned on COMEDK Rank Card 2026

  • Candidate’s name and registration number.
  • Date of birth and category.
  • Contact details.
  • COMEDK test admission ticket (TAT) number.
  • Subject‑wise marks or percentile and overall percentile score.
  • COMEDK UGET 2026 rank.
  • Qualifying status for counselling (if specified).

COMEDK UGET 2026 Rank Card - Important Instructions

If You Cannot Download Your Rank Card

  • Check if the result login is temporarily unavailable or under maintenance; COMEDK has shown “We will be back shortly” when updating the link.
  • Clear cache, try another browser, or use a stable internet connection; if the issue persists, contact COMEDK helpdesk with your registration details.

How Long Should You Keep the Rank Card?

  • Advise candidates to retain the COMEDK rank card PDF and printed copies until completion of admission, as it may be required at multiple stages of counselling and reporting.
