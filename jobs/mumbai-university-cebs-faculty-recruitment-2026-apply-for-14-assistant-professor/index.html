Job Overview

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for UM-DAE CEBS Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit both the online application form and the signed hard copy is July 15, 2026. Candidates must ensure both reach UM-DAE CEBS on or before this date.
Q2How many vacancies are there in UM-DAE CEBS Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
There are a total of 14 vacancies — 7 for Assistant Professor, 4 for Reader, and 3 for Associate Professor — across the Schools of Biological Sciences, Chemical Sciences, Mathematical Sciences, and Physical Sciences.
Q3What is the minimum qualification required to apply?
A
All posts require a Ph.D. in the relevant subject area (Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, or Physics) with at least 60% marks or equivalent at the Master's level. The required post-Ph.D. experience varies: 2 years for Assistant Professor, 5 years for Reader, and 10 years for Associate Professor.
Q4What is the age limit for each post?
A
The maximum age limit as of July 15, 2026 is 35 years for Assistant Professor, 38 years for Reader, and 43 years for Associate Professor.
Q5Is there any application fee?
A
The official advertisement does not mention any application fee. Candidates should verify this on the official website www.cbs.ac.in before applying.
Q6What is the salary for the Associate Professor post?
A
The Associate Professor post carries Academic Pay Level-13A with an entry pay of Rs. 1,31,100 – Rs. 2,16,600/-, along with Central Government allowances including HRA, DA, LTC, and CHSS medical benefits.
Q7What is the selection process for UM-DAE CEBS Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection involves two rounds: first, an in-person job talk before a Screening Committee; then, based on performance, a final in-person interview before the Final Selection Committee. A separate merit list is prepared for each post code.
