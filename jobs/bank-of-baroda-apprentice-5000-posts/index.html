  • 19This is NOT regular employment or contract employment in the Bank. Apprentices will be TRAINEES only and not employees of Bank of Baroda.
  • 20The apprentice shall have no right to claim employment in the Bank after completion of training. Bank of Baroda has no obligation to offer regular employment to apprentices during or after the apprenticeship period.
  • 21Candidates can apply for engagement as apprentice in one State/UT only. Allotment to a different State/UT will not be considered under any circumstances.
  • 22Candidates must first register on NATS and/or NAPS portals before applying; application without portal registration is not valid.
  • 23The process of registration is complete only when applicable fee/charges are deposited with BFSI SSC.
  • 24No Ex-servicemen / Disabled Ex-Servicemen shall be engaged as an apprentice; there is no reservation for Ex-servicemen in this engagement.
  • 25OBC candidates belonging to the 'Creamy Layer' are not entitled to OBC reservation and age relaxation; they should apply as General / General (OC/HI/VI/ID) as applicable.
  • 26Change of category once submitted in the online application will not be permitted at any stage.
  • 27Candidates should submit only one application; in case of multiple submissions, only the last valid completed application will be considered and fee paid for earlier registrations will be forfeited.
  • 28The Apprentices are prohibited from joining / forming any Union Body.
  • 29All correspondence will be made only on the registered email ID; candidates should keep it active throughout the recruitment process. No hard copy intimation will be sent by post for Online Examination.
  • 30Candidates must appear for the selection process (Document Verification, Language Test) at their own cost; Bank will not reimburse travelling expenses.
  • 31Apprentices will not be provided with Transport / Hostel facility or any other residential accommodation during training.
  • 32Stipend of Rs. 15,000/- per month will be paid on completion of each month after adjusting Loss of Pay, if any. No other allowances/benefits are payable.
  • 33All amendments/corrigendum (if any) will be published only on the Bank's website (https://bankofbaroda.bank.in/) and https://bfsissc.com - candidates should visit regularly.
  • 34Any canvassing directly or indirectly shall disqualify the candidature. Use of unfair means, impersonation, or misbehavior will lead to disqualification and/or debarment.
  • 35Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be Mumbai only.
