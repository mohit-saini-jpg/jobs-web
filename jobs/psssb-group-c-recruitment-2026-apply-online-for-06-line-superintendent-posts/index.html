Short Information

The latest Subordinate Services Selection Board (SSSB), Punjab Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Line Superintendent, Group-C posts! Published on 02 May 2026 at https://sssb.punjab.gov.in, this new recruitment drive offers 6 openings for eligible candidates. Applicants within the 18–37 years age group for General and 18–42 years for SC (Punjab residents) and possessing a three-year Diploma in Electrical Engineering along with Matriculation with Punjabi as a compulsory/elective subject are eligible to apply online from 15 May 2026 to 05 June 2026. Selection Process

  • Part Subject Nature Marks Negative Marking
  • Part A Punjabi Language (Matriculation Level) Qualifying only (minimum 50% required to pass) — No
  • Part B Technical / Job-related subjects Merit basis 1 mark per question Yes – ¼ mark deducted per wrong answer
  • Minimum marks required to qualify Part A (Punjabi) 50%
  • Failure to secure 50% in Punjabi Disqualified from final merit list irrespective of Part-B score
  • 1st Tie-Break Date of birth – older candidate given preference
  • 2nd Tie-Break Higher percentage in required educational qualification (Diploma)
  • 3rd Tie-Break Higher marks in Matriculation (10th standard)
  • The selection process consists of an Objective Type MCQ Written Examination (Part A + Part B) followed by Document Verification / Counselling. Final merit list is prepared on the basis of Part-B marks only.

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the SSSB Punjab portal https://sssb.punjab.gov.in. Applications submitted by any other mode (post, in person, etc.) will not be accepted and will be rejected.
  • New users must first register on the portal to generate a User ID and password, then log in and complete the online application. Once the application form is submitted, no changes/corrections can be made under any circumstances.
  • Candidates who applied under Advt. No. 08/2023 must register afresh, generate a new User ID, and verify their previously submitted Application Number and Date of Birth during the process.
  • Documents to be uploaded at time of Online Application:
  • 10th class certificate (for age proof and Punjabi language qualification)
  • Educational/Technical qualification certificate (3-year Diploma in Electrical Engineering)
  • Domicile/Residence Certificate of Punjab (not older than 5 years)
  • Caste Certificate (SC) if applicable — as per Punjab Govt. instructions
  • Any other mandatory document as specified in the portal
  • Documents to be produced in Original + Attested Copies at Counselling:
  • Educational qualification certificates (10th, Diploma)
  • Domicile/Residence Certificate of Punjab
  • SC Certificate (if applicable)
  • NOC from department (for State/Central Govt. employees claiming age relaxation)
  • Any other category-related certificate as demanded by the Board
  • Apply Online at: https://sssb.punjab.gov.in

Important Instructions

  • Online applications only — no manual or postal applications will be accepted. Category and category code once filled cannot be changed at any stage. Reservation benefits are available only to residents of Punjab. All required educational qualifications must be completed on or before the last date of application (05-06-2026). Fee once paid is non-refundable; application without successful fee payment will be summarily rejected. Photograph (passport size / live camera capture) and scanned signature must be uploaded — applications without these will be treated as incomplete. Required certificates must be uploaded in PDF format as per portal specifications at time of online application. Once application form is submitted, no correction/modification will be permitted under any circumstances. Candidates are advised to apply themselves and not take help of any cyber cafe attendant or third party — any error in the form is the candidate's own responsibility. No TA/DA will be paid for attending written examination or counselling. All information regarding exam date, admit card, exam centre, result, merit list, and other updates will be published ONLY on the official website https://sssb.punjab.gov.in — no individual communication will be sent. Candidates must regularly check the Board's website until the recruitment process is complete. If a candidate is already debarred by SSSB Punjab or any other institution, they are not eligible to apply. Govt. employees must produce NOC from their department at the time of counselling.
