Qualification / Eligibility

Centre Assistant (Senior): Graduate in Commerce/ Science/ Business Administrative or related fields and Post Graduate in Commerce/ Science/ Business Administrative or related fields from a recognized university. Minimum five (05) years of relevant work experience after completion of Post Graduate Degree. Knowledge and awareness of current initiatives of the government project, planning and development. Experience of working with government departments/ agencies, and other public stakeholders. Good communication skills in English and Local language (written and verbal). Proficiency in Microsoft Office tools, statistical and simulation software. Ability to work well in multi-disciplinary teams, balance multiple priorities, and work under tight deadlines. Ability to understand the level of detail essential for a given task. Centre Assistant (Junior): Bachelor in Commerce / Business Administrative or related fields from a recognized university. Minimum four (04) years of relevant work experience. Knowledge and awareness of current initiatives of the government in urban planning and development. Good communication skills in English and Local language (written and verbal). Proficiency in Microsoft Office tools, statistical and simulation software. Ability to work well in multi-disciplinary teams, balance multiple priorities, and work under tight deadlines. Ability to understand the level of detail essential for a given task.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for SVNIT Centre Assistant posts?
A
The walk-in interview for the Centre Assistant posts at SVNIT Surat is scheduled for 05 June 2026. Candidates should ensure they reach the venue on time with all necessary documents.
Q2Q2. What is the total number of vacancies for Centre Assistant posts?
A
There are a total of 2 vacancies announced for the Centre Assistant positions at Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat.
Q3Q3. What are the educational qualifications required for the Centre Assistant posts?
A
For the Senior Centre Assistant post, a Post Graduate degree is required with 5 years of experience. For the Junior Centre Assistant post, a Bachelor's degree with 4 years of experience is needed. Both should be in Commerce, Science, Business Administration, or related fields.
Q4Q4. What is the age limit for applying to these posts?
A
The maximum age limit for both Centre Assistant (Senior) and Centre Assistant (Junior) posts is 35 years.
Q5Q5. What is the salary offered for the Centre Assistant positions?
A
The salary for the Centre Assistant (Senior) post is Rs. 40,000/- per month, and for the Centre Assistant (Junior) post, it is Rs. 30,000/- per month, both fixed with all allowances.
Q6Q6. Is there any application fee for SVNIT Centre Assistant recruitment?
A
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for the SVNIT Centre Assistant recruitment drive.
Q7Q7. What is the selection process for these posts?
A
The selection process involves Document Verification followed by a Walk-in Interview. Candidates need to be prepared for both stages.
Q8Q8. What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents, one set of self-attested photocopies of educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and category certificates (if applicable), along with recent passport-size photographs.
Q9Q9. Where is the walk-in interview for SVNIT Centre Assistant posts going to be held?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat, Gujarat. The exact venue details will be available in the official notification.
Q10Q10. What is the nature of the job for Centre Assistant posts?
A
The Centre Assistant posts are on a contract basis. This provides a valuable opportunity for candidates to gain experience in a government-aided institution.
