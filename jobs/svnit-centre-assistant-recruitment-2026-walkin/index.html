Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for SVNIT Centre Assistant posts?

The walk-in interview for the Centre Assistant posts at SVNIT Surat is scheduled for 05 June 2026. Candidates should ensure they reach the venue on time with all necessary documents.

Q2. What is the total number of vacancies for Centre Assistant posts?

There are a total of 2 vacancies announced for the Centre Assistant positions at Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat.

Q3. What are the educational qualifications required for the Centre Assistant posts?

For the Senior Centre Assistant post, a Post Graduate degree is required with 5 years of experience. For the Junior Centre Assistant post, a Bachelor's degree with 4 years of experience is needed. Both should be in Commerce, Science, Business Administration, or related fields.

Q4. What is the age limit for applying to these posts?

The maximum age limit for both Centre Assistant (Senior) and Centre Assistant (Junior) posts is 35 years.

Q5. What is the salary offered for the Centre Assistant positions?

The salary for the Centre Assistant (Senior) post is Rs. 40,000/- per month, and for the Centre Assistant (Junior) post, it is Rs. 30,000/- per month, both fixed with all allowances.

Q6. Is there any application fee for SVNIT Centre Assistant recruitment?

No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for the SVNIT Centre Assistant recruitment drive.

Q7. What is the selection process for these posts?

The selection process involves Document Verification followed by a Walk-in Interview. Candidates need to be prepared for both stages.

Q8. What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?

Candidates must carry all original documents, one set of self-attested photocopies of educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and category certificates (if applicable), along with recent passport-size photographs.
