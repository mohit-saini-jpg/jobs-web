Qualification / Eligibility

Bachelor's Degree or its equivalent with proficiency in operation of Computer (Word processing and Spread Sheets) and a speed of 80 words per minute in Stenography (English) and speed of 20 words per minute in transcripting the same. Must have a Certificate of ICT Skills course i.e. Course on Computer Concepts (CCC) 80 hours from a Govt. recognized institution OR a reputed institution which is an ISO 9001 certified OR Department of Electronics Accreditation of Computer Course (DOEACC) of Govt. of India OR from NIELIT and its authorized institutions.
