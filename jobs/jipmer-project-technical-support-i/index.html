FAQs

Q1What is the JIPMER Project Technical Support – I Recruitment 2026?
A
JIPMER Project Technical Support – I Recruitment 2026 is a walk-in/selection-based hiring for temporary technical staff under an ICMR-funded research project in the Department of Emergency Medicine & Trauma at JIPMER, Puducherry. The engagement is purely contractual and linked to the ICMR-INDIA EMS Project, with no claim for permanent JIPMER employment.
Q2What is the last date to apply for JIPMER Project Technical Support – I post?
A
The last date to submit the application form and supporting documents by email is June 9, 2026, up to 4:30 PM. Applications received after the cut-off time will not be considered for shortlisting.
Q3How many vacancies are available for JIPMER Project Technical Support – I Recruitment 2026?
A
The exact number of Project Technical Support – I vacancies has not been specified and is project-driven, depending on the requirement of the ICMR-INDIA EMS Project. Candidates should refer to the official advertisement for any subsequent updates.
Q4What is the salary for JIPMER Project Technical Support – I in 2026?
A
Selected candidates for the Project Technical Support – I post will receive a consolidated monthly salary of ₹21,600, which includes 20% House Rent Allowance (HRA). No additional allowances or permanent benefits are applicable.
Q5What are the educational qualifications required for JIPMER Project Technical Support – I?
A
Applicants must have passed Class 10 and possess a Diploma in Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) or Medical Laboratory Technician (MLT) from a recognized institution. In addition, candidates must have at least 2 years of relevant work experience in the concerned field or subject.
Q6What is the age limit for JIPMER Project Technical Support – I Recruitment 2026?
A
The maximum age limit is 28 years as on the last date of application receipt (June 9, 2026). Candidates exceeding the age limit will not be eligible to apply, subject to any relaxations mentioned in the official notification.
Q7What is the job role and field requirement for JIPMER Project Technical Support – I?
A
Project Technical Support – I staff will support the ICMR-INDIA EMS Project by assisting in data collection, coordination and field activities under the Department of Emergency Medicine & Trauma. Candidates must be willing to travel to urban and rural health facilities across Puducherry districts for field data collection work.
Q8What is the selection process for JIPMER Project Technical Support – I Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process consists of initial shortlisting based on the application form, CV and submitted certificates, followed by a written test and personal interview for shortlisted candidates. The written test and interview will be held at the Department of Emergency Medicine & Trauma, JIPMER, Puducherry.
