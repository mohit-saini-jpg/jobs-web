How to Apply

  • 1Applicants must apply online only through the APSC recruitment website: https://apscrecruitment.in.
  • 2New users have to complete One Time Registration (OTR) first by clicking on “Register Here” and providing basic details.
  • 3Use a valid and active email ID and mobile number for registration and future communication.
  • 4Log in with registered email/mobile number and password, then fill in all mandatory details in the online application form.
  • 5Upload required documents: proof of Date of Birth, educational qualification certificates and mark sheets, caste/category certificates, BPL/PwBD certificates (if applicable) in PDF format (max 4 MB, scanned at 200 dpi, legible).
  • 6Upload recent passport-size Photograph and Signature (50 KB–200 KB each, photo not older than 3 months).
  • 7Carefully review all details and documents using the Preview option and tick the declaration checkbox before final submission.
  • 8Pay the applicable application fee and processing charges online; applications without successful fee payment will be treated as incomplete.
  • 9In case of payment failure or pending status, use “Check Status” to verify with bank/GRAS; double debit, if any, will be auto-refunded by the bank within 5–7 working days.
  • 10After final submission, take a printout of the submitted Online Application Form and retain it for future reference.
  • 11Candidates can also submit applications through Common Service Centres (CSCs) if they do not have their own online payment facility.
