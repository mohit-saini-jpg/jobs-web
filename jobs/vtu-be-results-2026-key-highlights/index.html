Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi has officially released the updated VTU BE Result 2026 for Bachelor of Engineering students for all regions through its online result portal. The latest update covers B.E CBCS semester results scheme-wise and region-wise, allowing students from different VTU regions to check their revised and updated B.E results in one place.

VTU BE Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - vtu.ac.in, and students can access their B.E semester scorecards via the VTU result server at results.vtu.ac.in. By using their USN (University Seat Number) and the given captcha code, students can log in to the VTU results portal and download their updated B.E result for the relevant semester.

Students are advised to carefully check their personal details, college name, course, region, semester, subject-wise marks and result status after downloading the online marksheet. In case of any discrepancy in the VTU BE Result 2026, candidates should immediately contact their college examination section or the VTU examination authority for clarification and necessary corrections.

VTU BE Result 2026 Direct Link

VTU has activated the direct link for updated B.E results for all regions on its official online result server. Students can use the dedicated VTU BE CBCS result link to access their semester-wise results and download their updated scorecards instantly. Steps to Check VTU BE Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your VTU BE Result 2026 for all regions using the official VTU result portal.

  • Visit the official VTU result portal at https://results.vtu.ac.in/.
  • Click on the link that reads "Updated results of B.E for all regions" or open the direct BE CBCS result link.
  • You will be redirected to the VTU BE CBCS result page for the latest updated results.
  • Enter your USN (University Seat Number) correctly in the given field.
  • Type the captcha/security code as shown on the screen.
  • Click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button to proceed.
  • Your VTU BE Result 2026 for the respective semester and region will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download the online scorecard or take a printout for future reference, counselling and academic record.
