How to Check Bihar ITI CAT 2026 2nd Round Allotment Result

  • Visit the official BCECEB website at bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
  • Go to the "Latest Notifications" or "Download Section" on the homepage.
  • Click on the "ITICAT 2026 2nd Round Allotment Result" link.
  • Log in using your Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth as required.
  • Your 2nd round seat allotment status will be displayed on screen.
  • Download and save the result, and take a printout for future reference.

Bihar ITI CAT 2026: Important Instructions

  • Candidates allotted a seat in the 2nd round should watch for the official document verification and admission schedule, since it is not yet published.
  • Candidates who wish to upgrade their allotted seat should check the official upgradation option, if made available, on the BCECEB portal.
  • If seats remain vacant after round 2, BCECEB may conduct a mop-up round; check the official website for updates.
  • Always cross-check your allotment details against the official PDF, as third-party portals may display outdated or partial information.
