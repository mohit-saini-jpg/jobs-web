Gauhati High Court Stenographer Grade-II Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

The selection process for the Stenographer Grade-II post consists of three stages: Stenography Test, Written Test, and Viva-voce. Candidates equal to 10 times the number of vacancies (1:10 ratio), who have qualified in the stenography test by securing a minimum of 30 marks, shall be called for the written test. The written test will consist of objective type (OMR based) multiple choice questions. English Language Proficiency: 50 marks Official Language of the State of Assam (Assamese): 20 marks Total: 70 marks, duration 1½ hours Both English and Assamese language segments are of qualifying nature, and candidates must secure a minimum of 40% marks in each segment to qualify. Candidates qualifying in the written test shall be called in the ratio of 1:3, in order of merit in the Stenography Test, for the Viva-voce (10 marks).
