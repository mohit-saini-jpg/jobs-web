APSC Geophysicist Vacancy and Reservation Details

APSC has notified 1 vacancy for the post of Geophysicist under the Open category. There is no reservation under OBC/MOBC, TT & ADIVASI, SC, STP, STH, or PwBD categories for this post, and no vacancy is reserved for Ex-Servicemen. Note: Since there is no post reserved for PwBD candidates, they will need to compete with other candidates without any PwBD reservation, though a candidate with a Benchmark Disability of 40% or more may still apply subject to producing the necessary certificate.

APSC Geophysicist Eligibility Criteria 2026

Candidates must meet the following eligibility conditions to apply for the APSC Geophysicist post: The candidate must be an Indian Citizen as defined in Articles 5 to 8 of the Constitution of India. The candidate must be a permanent resident of Assam. A valid Permanent Resident Certificate issued in Assam for educational purposes, or an Employment Exchange Registration Certificate, must be uploaded as proof of residency. Candidates must hold a postgraduate degree in any one of the following disciplines from a University or Institution recognized by the Government: M.Sc. in Geophysics M.Sc. in Applied Geophysics M.Sc. Tech. in Applied Geophysics M.Sc. Tech. in Geophysics M.Tech. in Exploration Geophysics M.Tech. in Geophysics Integrated M.Sc.-Ph.D. in Geophysics Candidates must upload mark sheets of all semesters/years reflecting the subjects studied, along with the final pass certificate

APSC Geophysicist Recruitment 2026 Job Profile

As a Geophysicist, the candidate will be responsible for conducting surveys and investigations to understand the Earth's physical structure. They will also be involved in data analysis and interpretation to make recommendations for oil and gas exploration. The day-to-day work will involve working in the field, collecting data, and analyzing it to make conclusions.
