How to Check JEE Advanced AAT Result 2026

Checking your result is pretty straightforward, but make sure you visit only the official website to avoid fake portals. Follow these steps:

  • Visit the official portal at jeeadv.ac.in
  • On the homepage, click on the flashing link labeled "AAT 2026 Result"
  • Enter your JEE Advanced 2026 registration number
  • Input your date of birth
  • Enter the security pin displayed on the screen
  • Click submit – your qualifying status will appear on screen

Your result will show either "Qualified" or "Not Qualified" for B.Arch admission. Download the PDF and take at least two printouts – you'll need them for JoSAA counselling and future admission rounds.

What Does "Qualified" Status Mean for B.Arch Admission?

If your AAT result shows "Qualified," you're eligible to enter the B.Arch admission process through JoSAA counselling. Here's what happens next:

  • JoSAA counselling is already ongoing – don't wait
  • Qualified candidates can participate in seat allotment for B.Arch programs at IITs
  • You'll need your AAT scorecard, JEE Advanced rank card, and Class XII marks for counselling

Remember, only candidates who qualify JEE Advanced 2026 can appear for AAT 2026 – there's no separate AAT eligibility .

Common Login Issues & How to Fix Them

Some students are reporting trouble logging in. Here are quick fixes:

  • Wrong registration number? Double-check your JEE Advanced admit card
  • Website not loading? Try again after 10-15 minutes – high traffic can slow things down
  • Security pin error? Refresh the page and enter the new pin displayed
  • Forgot credentials? Contact the JEE Advanced helpdesk – details are on jeeadv.ac.in

Don't panic – these issues usually resolve quickly. Just avoid clicking on third-party links promising "direct result access"

Documents to Keep Ready After Checking Result

Once you download your AAT scorecard, gather these documents for JoSAA counselling:

  • AAT 2026 scorecard (printed copy)
  • JEE Advanced 2026 rank card
  • Class XII mark sheet and certificate
  • Category certificate (if applicable – GEN-EWS, OBC-NCL, SC, ST, PwD)
