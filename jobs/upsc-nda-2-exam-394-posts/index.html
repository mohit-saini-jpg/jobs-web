UPSC NDA (2) Exam 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 394 Posts

Union Public Service Commission (UPSC), New Delhi

Posts: Various Last Date: 09-06-2026 (up to 6:00 PM) Online only via https://upsconline.nic.in

Short Information

UPSC Examination 2026 offers 394 vacancies for National Defence Academy and Naval Academy. Eligible candidates with 12th Pass can apply online from 20-05-2026 to 09-06-2026 through the official UPSC website, upsc.gov.in.

Important Dates

Last Date To Apply09-06-2026 (up to 6:00 PM)

Age Limit

Age Details12-06-2026

Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationCandidates who have resigned or been withdrawn from any training academy of Armed Forces on disciplinary grounds or for lack of officer-like qualities are not eligible. Candidates debarred by the Ministry of Defence from holding any commission are also not eligible.

Selection Process

  • Written Examination (Objective Type) + SSB Intelligence & Personality Test (including CPSS for Air Force Flying) + Medical Examination

How To Apply

  • Candidates must apply online only through https://upsconline.nic.in. No other mode of application is accepted. The Online Application Portal has four modules: Account Creation, Universal Registration (URN - one-time for lifetime), Common Application Form (CAF), and Examination Specific Module. The URN generated is common for all UPSC examinations; the Application Number generated is examination-specific and must be retained. Candidates in Government Service / Armed Forces / Sainik Schools / Military Schools must inform their Head of Office / Commanding Officer / Principal in writing that they have applied.
