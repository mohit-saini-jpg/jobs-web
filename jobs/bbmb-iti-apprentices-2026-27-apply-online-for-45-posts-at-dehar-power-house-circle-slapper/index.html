Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is BBMB ITI Apprenticeship 2026-27?

BBMB ITI Apprenticeship 2026-27 is a one‑year training program under the Apprenticeship Act 1961 for ITI pass candidates at Dehar Power House Circle, BBMB Slapper, District Mandi (HP). The scheme offers trade apprenticeships in 6 different trades for the 2026‑27 session.

Q2. How many vacancies are available in BBMB Apprenticeship 2026-27?

A total of 45 ITI Apprentice vacancies have been notified across six trades – Electrician, PASAA, Draughtsman (Civil), Fitter, Welder and Plumber. The highest number of seats are in the Electrician trade (23 posts) followed by PASAA (10 posts).

Q3. What are the important dates for BBMB ITI Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27?

The online application process for BBMB Apprenticeship 2026‑27 started on 08 May 2026. The last date to submit applications through the NAPS portal is 04 June 2026, and applications will be accepted online only; no offline forms are allowed.

Q4. Which trades are available under BBMB Apprenticeship 2026-27?

BBMB is offering apprentice training in the following trades for 2026‑27: Electrician – 23 posts Programming and System Administration Assistant (PASAA) – 10 posts Draughtsman (Civil) – 2 posts Fitter – 5 posts Welder – 2 posts Plumber – 3 posts

Q5. What is the educational qualification required for BBMB ITI Apprentice posts?

Candidates must have passed 10th class with at least 40% marks from a recognized board. They must also possess a National Trade Certificate (NTC) in the relevant trade issued by NCVT/SCVT and must have completed the full ITI course in that trade. An Aadhaar card is mandatory for online registration.

Q6. What is the age limit for BBMB Apprenticeship 2026-27?

The age limit for BBMB Apprenticeship 2026‑27 is as per the Apprenticeship Act, 1961 and its amendments, which generally prescribe minimum and maximum age for apprentices. Candidates must check that they fall within the applicable age range as on the last date of application as per the Act.

Q7. What is the selection process for BBMB ITI Apprenticeship 2026-27?

There is no written exam and no interview for BBMB ITI Apprenticeship 2026‑27. Selection is purely merit‑based, generated from the NAPS/NATS portal database, using the marks of 10th Class and ITI in the concerned trade.

Q8. How is the merit list prepared for BBMB Apprenticeship?

The merit list is prepared out of 100 marks, giving 30% weightage to 10th class marks and 70% weightage to ITI marks in the concerned trade. If two candidates score the same total marks, preference is given first to the older candidate, and if the date of birth is also the same, then to the candidate who passed matriculation earlier.
