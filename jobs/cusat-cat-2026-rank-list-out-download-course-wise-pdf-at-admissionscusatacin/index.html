CUSAT CAT 2026 Minimum Qualifying Marks for Rank List Inclusion

Not all candidates who appear in CUSAT CAT are automatically included in the rank list. CUSAT has defined a minimum marks threshold that candidates must meet to earn a place in the merit rank list and become eligible for counselling.

To be included in the CUSAT CAT 2026 rank list for B.Tech admissions, candidates must have scored:

  • A minimum of 10 marks in Mathematics
  • A minimum of 10 marks combined in Physics and Chemistry

Only candidates who meet both criteria are ranked and considered for seat allotment during CUSAT CAT 2026 counselling.

How Is the CUSAT CAT 2026 Rank List Prepared?

Understanding how CUSAT calculates and assigns ranks can help candidates gauge their admission prospects more accurately. The ranking methodology is based on a standardised process to ensure fairness across all test takers.

  • CUSAT uses a percentile index to determine the final rank of each candidate.
  • In case of a tie between two or more candidates, CUSAT prepares an inter se merit list to break the tie and assign distinct ranks.
  • The rank list covers both UG programmes (B.Tech, B.Arch, BBA, LLB) and PG programmes (M.Tech, M.Sc, MBA, LLM, PhD).
  • Category-wise ranks are also published separately for SC, ST, and other reserved categories.

How to Download CUSAT CAT 2026 Rank List PDF - Step-by-Step Guide

Candidates can follow these simple steps to download their course-wise CUSAT CAT 2026 rank list PDF directly from the official CUSAT admissions portal.

  • Step 1: Visit the official CUSAT admissions portal at admissions.cusat.ac.in.
  • Step 2: Click on the CUSAT CAT Rank List 2026 link on the homepage.
  • Step 3: Select your relevant course or programme from the available list.
  • Step 4: The course-wise rank list PDF will open on screen.
  • Step 5: Download the PDF and save a copy for future reference during counselling.

Tip: To check your detailed scorecard including subject-wise marks, log in to the CUSAT admissions portal using your registered Email ID and password.
