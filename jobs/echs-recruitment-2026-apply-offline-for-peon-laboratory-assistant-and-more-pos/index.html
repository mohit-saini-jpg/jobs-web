ECHS Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme (ECHS), Station Cell, Kannur has invited applications for contractual engagement of Laboratory Assistant, Dental Hygienist/Dental Assistant, and Peon at various ECHS Polyclinics under Station Cell Kannur. This is a great opportunity for ex-servicemen and other eligible candidates. The last date to submit applications is 25 July 2026.
