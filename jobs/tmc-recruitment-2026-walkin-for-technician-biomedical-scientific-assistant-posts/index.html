Age Limit

Age DetailsMax 30 years for Technician Biomedical, Max 27 years for Scientific Assistant.
DetailsTechnician Biomedical: Maximum 30 years. Scientific Assistant (Biomedical): Maximum 27 years. Deserving and bright candidate may be given relaxation in age.

Qualification / Eligibility

Technician Biomedical: Full Time Degree/Diploma in Medical Electronics/Bio-medical from the State Board of Technical Examination (10+3), years' course. Experience: 0-2 year of Experience in Hospital in Maintenance/Services of Hospital Essential. Scientific Assistant (Biomedical): Diploma (Biomedical / Medical Electronics). 3 years' experience out of which 1 year in hospital setup. Desirable: A candidate desirous to work independently for in-house repairs/maintenance/calibration activity shall be preferred.
