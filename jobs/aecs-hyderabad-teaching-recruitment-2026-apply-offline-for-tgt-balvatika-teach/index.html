AECS Hyderabad Teaching Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Atomic Energy Central School-2 (AECS-2), Hyderabad has invited applications from eligible and interested candidates for filling teaching posts purely on contract basis for the Academic Year 2026-27 (01/04/2026 to 31/03/2027) at AEC Schools-1 and 2, DAE Colony, ECIL Post, Hyderabad. The posts include Trained Graduate Teachers (TGT), Librarian, and Balvatika Teachers (BVT), to be filled against leave and regular vacancies. Selection is through a walk-in Skill Test and Practical Classroom Observation (Demo Teaching Class).

AECS Hyderabad Teaching Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Candidates will be shortlisted and empaneled through a Skill Test and Practical Classroom Observation (Demo Teaching Class) as per the notified schedule. Candidates must come prepared to present both a topic of their own choice and another topic chosen at random from a box on the day of the test. Reporting time is 8:00 AM to 12:00 Noon on the scheduled test date; candidates will not be entertained beyond reporting hours. Cut-off marks for shortlisting will be based on marks scored in both years of Intermediate/+2 for PRT and BVT posts, and marks across all years of graduation (including languages) for TGT posts. If the number of registered candidates increases, the number of test days may be extended, with updates posted on the AECS-2 notice board.
