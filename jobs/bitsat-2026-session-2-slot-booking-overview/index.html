5paragraphOn the login page:
6listunordered

11paragraphCheck all the available options before selecting your preferred date and slot.
12paragraphStep 4 - Choose Your Preferred Date and Slot
13paragraphSelect:

6Important Instructions for BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot Booking
Sr.TypeStyleItems
1listunordered
  • Slot booking is mandatory if you wish to choose your own exam date and time for Session 2.
  • Slots are allotted on a first‑come‑first‑served basis; popular dates and morning slots may fill up early.
  • If you do not book a slot within the given window, the system may automatically allocate a date and time based on availability.
  • Once a slot is booked and confirmed, change requests are generally not accepted by the institute.
  • Use a stable internet connection and avoid multiple rapid refreshes during booking to reduce the risk of session timeout or errors.
  • Keep your application number, password, and registered mobile/email handy before starting the process.
7BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Exam Dates, Shifts and Test Cities
Sr.TypeText
1paragraphBITSAT 2026 Session 2 is conducted in Computer‑Based Test (CBT) mode at various centres across India and at a few international centres such as Dubai. 10Common Issues in BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot Booking and Solutions
Sr.TypeStyleItems
1listunordered
  • Login problems: Re‑check your application number and password, ensure caps lock is off, and try again. If issues persist, use the “Forgot Password” or helpdesk options provided on the portal.
  • Website slow or not loading: Try booking during non‑peak hours, switch to a different browser, and avoid using mobile data if your signal is unstable.
  • Preferred slot not available: This usually means the slot is already full. Choose another date or shift within your test city.
  • No confirmation visible: After booking, if you are not sure whether the slot is confirmed, log out and log back in to verify that your chosen date and slot are displayed in your dashboard.
