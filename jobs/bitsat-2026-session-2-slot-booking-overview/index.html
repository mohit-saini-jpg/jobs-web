BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot Booking Overview

BITS Pilani conducts the Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test (BITSAT) 2026 in two sessions, and candidates appearing in Session 2 must book their exam date and slot within the notified window. The complete slot booking process is online and is available only through the official BITS Pilani admissions website.

BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot Booking Date and Time

The BITSAT 2026 Session 2 slot booking window opens on May 14, 2026, soon after the release of the city intimation details for Session 2. Candidates must book their exam slot before the closing date of the window, which is generally a few days before the Session 2 exam starts.

Since slot allotment is on a first‑come‑first‑served basis, students are advised to complete the process as early as possible on the opening day. Delaying slot booking can reduce the chances of getting the desired exam date and preferred shift.

BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot Booking Link (Official Website)

The only authentic place to book your BITSAT 2026 Session 2 slot is the official BITS Pilani admission portal.

  • Official website: admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in
  • Section to visit: BITSAT 2026
  • Action item: Click on the Session 2 Slot Booking link once it is activated on the portal.

Candidates should avoid unofficial links and always double‑check the URL before entering their credentials to protect their personal data.

Who Can Book BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot?

Eligibility for BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot Booking

Only those candidates can book a Session 2 slot who:

  • Have successfully registered for BITSAT 2026 and selected Session 2 (either alone or along with Session 1).
  • Have paid the required fee for Session 2 within the prescribed deadlines.
  • Possess valid login credentials: BITSAT 2026 application number and password.

If a candidate initially opted only for Session 1, BITS Pilani typically provides a separate window to apply for Session 2 by paying the additional fee. After completing this process, they become eligible for Session 2 slot booking.

Can Session 1 Candidates Apply for Session 2?

Yes, candidates who appeared in Session 1 can apply for BITSAT 2026 Session 2 as a second attempt, provided they complete the Session 2 registration and fee payment during the official window. For admission, BITS considers the higher of the two scores (Session 1 or Session 2).

How to Book BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Exam Slot (Step‑by‑Step)

The BITSAT slot booking process is straightforward. Candidates need to log in, view available slots, choose a suitable combination of date and time, and confirm.

Step 1 - Visit BITS Pilani Admissions Portal

Go to the official admissions website of BITS Pilani: admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in. On the homepage, look for the BITSAT 2026 section and click on the link titled something like “BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot Booking” or “Click here to book test date and slot”.

Step 2 - Login with BITSAT 2026 Credentials

On the login page:

  • Enter your BITSAT 2026 application number.
  • Enter your password exactly as created during registration.
  • Enter any security code / captcha shown on the screen (if applicable).

Click on Login / Proceed to open your candidate dashboard.

Step 3 - View Available Test Dates and Time Slots

After login, you will see the slot booking interface, which typically includes:

  • The test city allotted to you as per your preferences and the city intimation.
  • A calendar or list showing available dates for BITSAT 2026 Session 2.
  • For each available date, you will see one or more time slots (e.g. morning shift, afternoon shift), along with availability.

Check all the available options before selecting your preferred date and slot.

Step 4 - Choose Your Preferred Date and Slot

Select:

  • One exam date (within the official Session 2 exam dates).
  • One time slot for that date (e.g., morning or afternoon), depending on what is available at your centre.

If your first choice is not available, try alternate combinations that still suit your travel and preparation plans. Remember that once the seats in a particular slot are full, that option will disappear from the list.

Step 5 - Confirm and Lock BITSAT Session 2 Slot

Carefully verify the selected:

  • Exam date
  • Time slot
  • Test city / centre (as shown)

Once you are sure, click on Confirm / Submit to lock your slot. After confirmation, the system usually does not allow changes, so take a moment to re‑check all details before final submission.

Step 6 - Download or Save Slot Booking Confirmation

After successful booking:

  • A confirmation message or page will appear showing your booked date and slot.
  • Download this confirmation as a PDF or take a clear screenshot.
  • Store it safely on your phone and computer, and consider printing a copy.

This confirmation is mainly for your reference until the hall ticket (admit card) is released.

Important Instructions for BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot Booking

  • Slot booking is mandatory if you wish to choose your own exam date and time for Session 2.
  • Slots are allotted on a first‑come‑first‑served basis; popular dates and morning slots may fill up early.
  • If you do not book a slot within the given window, the system may automatically allocate a date and time based on availability.
  • Once a slot is booked and confirmed, change requests are generally not accepted by the institute.
  • Use a stable internet connection and avoid multiple rapid refreshes during booking to reduce the risk of session timeout or errors.
  • Keep your application number, password, and registered mobile/email handy before starting the process.

BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Exam Dates, Shifts and Test Cities

BITSAT 2026 Session 2 is conducted in Computer‑Based Test (CBT) mode at various centres across India and at a few international centres such as Dubai. The exams are usually held in multiple shifts per day (commonly morning and afternoon), with the exact reporting time mentioned on the hall ticket.

Candidates choose their test city during the application form stage. The slot booking process is used to pick the specific date and shift within that city. The final exam centre name, address and reporting time will be printed on the admit card (hall ticket).

BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Hall Ticket Download After Slot Booking

After the completion of slot booking, BITS Pilani activates the hall ticket download facility for BITSAT 2026 Session 2 on the same portal.

To download the hall ticket:

  • Visit admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in.
  • Log in with your BITSAT 2026 application number and password.
  • Click on the “Download Hall Ticket / Admit Card” link for Session 2.
  • Check all details (name, photograph, exam date, slot, test centre).
  • Download the PDF and take 2-3 printouts for exam day.

Carry the printed hall ticket, a valid photo ID proof, and any other documents mentioned in the instructions to the exam centre.

What if I Do Not Book BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot?

If you do not actively choose a slot within the BITSAT 2026 Session 2 slot booking window:

  • The system may automatically assign you an exam date and time slot based on your applied city and remaining seats.
  • You will have to appear for the exam on this assigned date and time, as requests for change are usually not entertained.

To avoid inconvenience, it is strongly recommended that you log in as early as possible and manually choose the date and slot that best suit you.

Common Issues in BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot Booking and Solutions

  • Login problems: Re‑check your application number and password, ensure caps lock is off, and try again. If issues persist, use the “Forgot Password” or helpdesk options provided on the portal.
  • Website slow or not loading: Try booking during non‑peak hours, switch to a different browser, and avoid using mobile data if your signal is unstable.
  • Preferred slot not available: This usually means the slot is already full. Choose another date or shift within your test city.
  • No confirmation visible: After booking, if you are not sure whether the slot is confirmed, log out and log back in to verify that your chosen date and slot are displayed in your dashboard.
