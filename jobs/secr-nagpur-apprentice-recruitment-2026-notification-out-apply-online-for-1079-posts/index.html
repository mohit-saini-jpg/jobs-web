SECR Nagpur Apprentice Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 1079 Posts

South East Central Railway (SECR), Divisional Office, Kingsway, Nagpur-440001

Posts: 1079 (977 – Nagpur Division + 102 – Motibagh Workshop) Last Date: 18-06-2026 (till 23:59 hrs) Online

Short Information

South East Central Railway (SECR) has published the Act Apprentice Notification 2026 on 18-05-2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 1079 Act Apprentice positions. Candidates within the age range of 15 to 24 years and holding Matriculation (10th) with minimum 50% marks along with ITI Certificate (NCVT/SCVT) in relevant trade are eligible to apply online at the secr.indianrailways.gov.in. The application process will be active from 19-05-2026 to 18-06-2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.

Selection Process

  • 1st Tie-Break Candidate with older age shall be preferred
  • 2nd Tie-Break Candidate who passed Matriculation exam earlier shall be considered first (if date of birth is also the same)
  • Selection will be based on a merit list prepared for all candidates who apply against the notification. The merit list will be prepared on the basis of percentage of marks in Matriculation (minimum 50% aggregate) plus ITI marks in the trade in which Apprenticeship is to be done. For candidates from boards like Board of Secondary Education Andhra Pradesh and Board of Public Examination Kerala (grade-based systems)
  • SECR Nagpur Division will take the midpoint of the range of grades obtained
  • treating each subject as out of 100 marks for average calculation. Shortlisted candidates will be subject to verification of all original testimonials and appropriate medical examination. After selection
  • requests to change division/unit will not be entertained.
  • Tie-Breaking Rules

How To Apply

  • Applications must be submitted online only through the National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) portal at https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in (Establishment Code: E05202702695 for Nagpur Division; E05202702494 for Motibagh Workshop). The online portal will be active from 19-05-2026 to 18-06-2026 (23:59 hrs).
  • Candidates must carefully fill their 10th and ITI marks on the portal in the qualification section - if these marks do not appear in the downloaded Excel file, the candidature will be rejected. Do not fill any other higher qualification. Candidates have to choose either Nagpur Division or Motibagh Workshop at the time of application.
  • A unique registration number will be issued to each applicant during online submission - candidates are advised to save/note it for future correspondence. If the EWS/Ex-Servicemen option is not available on the portal, apply under your actual category and submit the EWS/Ex-Servicemen certificate physically to this office.
  • Documents Required at the Time of Document Verification (on being called):
  • Matriculation / 10th Standard Mark Sheet and Certificate (original)
  • ITI National Trade Certificate (NTC) or Provisional Certificate from NCVT/SCVT
  • SC / ST / OBC / EWS / PWBD certificate in central format (if applicable)
  • Discharge Certificate (for Ex-Servicemen) or Armed Forces serving certificate (for children of Armed Forces personnel)
  • Disability Certificate issued by a competent authority (for PWBD candidates) as per Annexure III/IV/V/VI
  • Medical Certificate at the time of joining in prescribed proforma (signed by a Government Authorized Gazetted Doctor, not below the rank of Assistant Surgeon of Central/State Hospital)
  • No daily allowance, conveyance allowance, or travelling allowance will be paid to candidates called for document verification. No accommodation will be provided by Railway for apprentices.
  • Apply Online at: https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in

Important Instructions

  • Applications must be submitted online only through https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in; no other mode of application will be accepted. Candidates must fill 10th and ITI marks carefully on the NAPS portal; failure to do so will result in automatic rejection of the application. Engineering Graduates and Diploma Holders are not eligible to apply under this notification. Candidates who have previously done apprenticeship in any organization must not apply again; such candidatures will not be entertained. Candidature will be cancelled if the candidate fails to produce required original testimonials for verification or any discrepancy is noticed. If it is found that a candidate has submitted wrong/fake/false certificates, the Railway administration reserves the right to discharge the candidate at any stage without notice; they may also be liable for penal action. After selection for engagement, requests to change division/unit will not be entertained. No accommodation will be provided by Railway for apprentices; they must make their own arrangements. No daily allowance, conveyance allowance, or travelling allowance will be paid to candidates called for document verification. Age relaxation for OBC candidates is subject to submission of the latest OBC caste certificate in central format; age relaxation for SC/ST is subject to submission of caste certificate in central format. The Railway administration does not undertake responsibility for sending replies to candidates not selected or not called; no correspondence will be entertained. The decision of the administration regarding eligibility and selection at any level is final and binding.
