How to Apply

  • 1Applications for CSIR NPL Technician Recruitment 2026 must be submitted by post only. Follow the steps below:
  • 2Download the prescribed application form from the CSIR-NPL official website www.nplindia.in.
  • 3Fill in the application form carefully - enter your Name, Date of Birth, and Father's/Mother's name exactly as given in your SSC/Matriculation certificate.
  • 4Paste a recent passport-size colour photograph on Part I of the application form and sign across it. Paste another recent photograph on Part II (do not sign across this one).
  • 5Prepare a Demand Draft of Rs. 500/- drawn on any nationalised bank valid for at least 3 months, in favour of "Director, National Physical Laboratory", payable at New Delhi. Write your name and post code on the back of the DD. (SC/ST/PwBD/Women/CSIR Employees/Transgender candidates are exempted from fee.)
  • 6Attach self-attested copies of all required documents (see Documents Required section below).
  • 7If you are a Government/CSIR/PSU employee, obtain an NOC/forwarding letter from your employer (Proforma-VII) and send a hard copy through proper channel. An advance copy may be submitted before the closing date.
  • 8Superscribe the envelope as: "APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF TECHNICIAN(1) – (Post Code No. ____)"
  • 9Send the complete application by post to: Senior Controller of Administration, CSIR-National Physical Laboratory, Dr. K.S. Krishnan Marg, New Delhi – 110012 to reach on or before 30.06.2026.
  • 10Note: Candidates may apply for more than one post but must submit separate applications with separate Demand Drafts for each post. Applications received after 30.06.2026 will not be entertained on any account.

FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for CSIR NPL Technician Recruitment 2026?
The last date for receipt of the application form at CSIR-National Physical Laboratory, New Delhi is 30th June 2026. This date is the same for candidates from all areas including far-flung regions.
Q2. How many vacancies are there in CSIR NPL Technician 2026?
A total of 20 vacancies have been released for the post of Technician (1) across 6 trades — Electronics (10), Electrical (5), COPA (2), Multimedia (1), Digital Photography (1), and Mechanic Motor Vehicle (1).
Q3. What is the salary for CSIR NPL Technician 2026?
The basic pay is Rs. 19,900/- per month (Pay Level 2). The total emoluments including HRA in a Class X city are approximately Rs. 44,428/- per month.
Q4. What is the age limit for CSIR NPL Technician Recruitment 2026?
The age limit is minimum 18 years and maximum 28 years as on 30.06.2026. Relaxation is available for SC/ST (5 years), OBC-NCL (3 years), PwBD (10 years for UR), Ex-Servicemen (3 years), and regular Govt./CSIR employees (up to 5 years).
Q5. What is the qualification for CSIR NPL Technician 2026?
Candidates must have passed SSC/10th Standard with Science subjects with minimum 55% marks AND hold an ITI certificate in the relevant trade, OR have 2 years apprentice training, OR 3 years work experience in the relevant trade in a Govt./PSU organisation.
Q6. What is the application fee for CSIR NPL Technician 2026?
The application fee is Rs. 500/- via Demand Draft for General/UR/OBC/EWS candidates. SC, ST, PwBD, Women, CSIR Employees, and Transgender candidates are exempted from the fee.
Q7. How is the selection done for CSIR NPL Technician 2026?
Selection involves three stages: Screening by the Screening Committee → Trade Test (qualifying) → Competitive Written Examination (Paper-I: Mental Ability, Paper-II: General Awareness + English, Paper-III: Trade Subject). Final merit is based on marks in Paper-II + Paper-III.
Q8. Is there negative marking in CSIR NPL Technician Written Exam 2026?
Paper-I (Mental Ability) has no negative marking. Paper-II (General Awareness & English) and Paper-III (Trade Subject) carry 1 mark negative for every wrong answer.
Q9. Where should I send the CSIR NPL Technician 2026 application?
Send your application by post to: Senior Controller of Administration, CSIR-National Physical Laboratory, Dr. K.S. Krishnan Marg, New Delhi – 110012. Superscribe the envelope with the post code.
Q10. Can I apply online for CSIR NPL Technician Recruitment 2026?
No. CSIR-NPL Technician 2026 applications must be submitted by post only using the prescribed application form downloaded from nplindia.in. There is no online application mode for this recruitment.
