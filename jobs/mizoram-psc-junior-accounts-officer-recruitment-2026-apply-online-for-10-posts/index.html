Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Mizoram PSC JAO recruitment?

The last date to submit your online application for the Mizoram PSC Junior Accounts Officer (JAO) posts is 05 June 2026. It is advisable to apply well before the deadline to avoid any last-minute technical issues.

Q2. How many vacancies are available for the Junior Accounts Officer post?

There are a total of 10 vacancies announced for the Junior Accounts Officer (JAO) position by the Mizoram Public Service Commission in this recruitment drive.

Q3. What is the minimum and maximum age limit for the JAO post?

The minimum age requirement for the Junior Accounts Officer (JAO) post is 18 years, and the maximum age limit is 35 years as of 01 January 2026.

Q4. Is there any age relaxation for reserved categories?

Yes, the upper age limit is relaxable up to 5 years for ST/SC candidates, or as per the general guidelines notified by the Government of Mizoram.

Q5. What is the educational qualification required for the Junior Accounts Officer role?

Candidates must possess a Bachelor’s degree from a recognised University. Additionally, computer proficiency and Mizo language proficiency are required as per the specified rules.

Q6. What is the salary structure for a Junior Accounts Officer?

The selected candidates for the Junior Accounts Officer (JAO) post will be placed at Level-7 in the Pay Matrix, which offers a competitive salary as per government norms.

Q7. How can I apply for the Mizoram PSC JAO recruitment?

Applications must be submitted online through the official Mizoram PSC website or the direct application link provided. Ensure all details are accurate and upload necessary documents before the last date.

Q8. What is the selection process for the Junior Accounts Officer position?

The selection process for the Junior Accounts Officer (JAO) posts will be based on a General Competitive Examination conducted by the Mizoram Public Service Commission.
