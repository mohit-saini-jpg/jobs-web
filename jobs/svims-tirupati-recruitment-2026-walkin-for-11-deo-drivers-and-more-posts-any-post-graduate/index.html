The Sri Venkateswara Institute of Medical Sciences (SVIMS Tirupati) has officially announced the Various Posts under MRC Project Recruitment 2026 with 11 openings. The detailed notification was published on 20 May 2026. Candidates who meet the maximum age limit of 42 years and possess qualifications such as MBBS/BDS, B.Sc Nursing, ANM/MPHW, Diploma in Medical Imaging Technology, Cardio-Vascular Technology, Degree with Computer Knowledge, PG Diploma in Public Relations, Social Work, ITI Electrical Trade, or valid HMV Driving License can attend the walk-in interview from 01-06-2026 to 03-06-2026

FAQs

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies released by SVIMS Tirupati?
SVIMS Tirupati has released a total of 11 vacancies for various posts under the MRC Project.
Q2. What is the application mode for these posts?
The application mode for these posts is Walk-in Interview.
Q3. What is the maximum age limit for the candidates applying for these posts?
The maximum age limit for candidates applying for these posts is 42 years, with age relaxation for SC/ST candidates.
Q4. What is the salary structure for the selected candidates?
The salary structure ranges from Rs. 27,500/- to Rs. 60,000/- per month, depending on the post.
Q5. What are the essential qualifications required for the Medical Officer post?
For the Medical Officer post, candidates must possess an MBBS Degree or BDS Degree from a recognized institution.
Q6. Are there any application fees for the SVIMS Tirupati recruitment?
No, there are no application fees mentioned for any category in the official notification.
Q7. What documents should candidates carry for the walk-in interview?
Candidates should carry all original documents and one set of self-attested photocopies, including ID proof, educational certificates, and experience certificates if applicable.
Q8. What is the selection process for these contractual posts?
The selection process involves a Walk-in Interview followed by Document Verification.
Q9. Where is the walk-in interview scheduled to be held?
The walk-in interview will be held at the SVIMS Tirupati office.
Q10. What is the official website of SVIMS Tirupati?
The official website of Sri Venkateswara Institute of Medical Sciences is https://svimstpt.ap.nic.in.
