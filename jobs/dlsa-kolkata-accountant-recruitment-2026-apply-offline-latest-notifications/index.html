Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the DLSA Kolkata Accountant post?

The last date to submit the application for the Accountant post at DLSA Kolkata is 31st May 2026. Ensure your application reaches the designated address before this date.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Accountant position?

Candidates must possess a Bachelor's Degree in Commerce (B.Com) with Advanced Accountancy or equivalent, preferably with first-class or high second-class marks. Proficiency in Tally Prime, PFMS, and MS Excel is mandatory.

Q3. What is the age limit for the Accountant recruitment?

The age limit for the Accountant post is between 24 years and 35 years as of 31st January 2026. Age relaxation rules may apply as per government norms.
