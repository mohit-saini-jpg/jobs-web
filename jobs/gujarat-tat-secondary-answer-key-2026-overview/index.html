How to Download Gujarat TAT Secondary Answer Key 2026?

To avoid errors and login issues, candidates should carefully follow the official steps while downloading the OMR sheet and answer key.

Step-by-Step Process to Download OMR Sheet / Answer Key

  • Go to the official website www.sebexam.org or www.ojas.gujarat.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, look for the link “Gujarat TAT Secondary 2026 OMR Sheet / Answer Key” or related advertisement under the Answer Key / OMR section.
  • Click on the link and select “TAT-S Exam” from the drop-down, if prompted.
  • Enter your roll number or application number and your date of birth exactly as printed on the admit card.
  • Click the “Download” or “Submit” button to view your OMR sheet with the official correct answers.
  • Save the OMR sheet/answer key PDF on your device and take a printout for detailed analysis of your performance.

Details Mentioned in Gujarat TAT Secondary Answer Key / OMR Sheet

The Gujarat TAT Secondary OMR sheet and answer key carry all essential exam and candidate-related details in one place. After downloading the PDF, candidates will find:

  • Candidate name, roll number and basic exam details such as exam name and date.
  • Question booklet or series code, if applicable.
  • Question-wise responses marked by the candidate during the TAT-S exam.
  • Official correct option for each question as provided by SEB Gujarat in the answer key.

This makes it easier to cross-check answers and understand exactly where marks are gained or lost.

How to Calculate Marks Using Gujarat TAT Secondary Answer Key 2026?

Using the provisional answer key correctly helps candidates predict their result before the official scorecard is published.

Step-by-Step Marks Calculation Method

  • Download your individual OMR sheet and the official answer key for your subject and booklet series.
  • For each question, compare the option you marked with the correct option mentioned in the answer key.
  • Count the total number of correct responses; multiply this by the marks allotted per question as mentioned in the exam notification.
  • If the exam has negative marking, subtract the prescribed marks for every wrong answer to avoid overestimating your score.
  • The final figure is your approximate score in the Gujarat TAT Secondary Prelims Exam 2026; the actual result will be prepared by SEB based on the final answer key.

Gujarat TAT Secondary Answer Key 2026 - Objection / Challenge Process

If a candidate finds any discrepancy in the provisional answer key, they may get the option to challenge it within a limited time. SEB Gujarat generally provides an online objection link on the official portal, along with detailed instructions and schedule.

How to Raise Objections Against TAT-S Answer Key?

  • Visit the SEB official website and open the Gujarat TAT Secondary provisional answer key/objection link when it is activated.
  • Log in using your credentials and select the question(s) you want to challenge, providing a valid explanation and supporting proof where required.
  • Pay the objection fee per question (if prescribed) through the available online payment modes as per official rules.
  • SEB examines all objections and then releases the final answer key, which forms the basis of the TAT Secondary result.

Candidates should rely only on the official notice for exact fees, last date and format of representation.
