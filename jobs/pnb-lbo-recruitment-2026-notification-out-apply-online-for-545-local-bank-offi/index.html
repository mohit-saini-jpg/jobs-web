PNB LBO Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Punjab National Bank (PNB) has invited online applications for recruitment to 545 posts of Local Bank Officer (LBO) in Junior Management Grade Scale-I (JMGS-I). PNB LBO Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Candidates who have earlier worked in PNB or its merged/amalgamated entities and were discharged for reasons other than VRS/Resignation are not eligible to apply. Candidates against whom there are adverse reports regarding character, antecedents, or moral turpitude are not eligible to apply. There is no reservation for Ex-Servicemen in direct recruitment to Officer Cadre; however, age relaxation applies as per Government guidelines. Selected candidates must execute an Indemnity Bond to serve the Bank for at least 3 years, or pay Rs. 2,00,000/- if they leave before completing 3 years of service. Candidates must maintain a minimum CIBIL score of 680 or above at the time of joining. This is not a pre-condition to apply, and candidates with no bank account are not required to produce CIBIL status. Selected candidates will be designated "Local Bank Officer" and will be on probation for 2 years from the date of joining. After induction training, LBOs will be deployed within the Circles of the state for which they were selected, and will remain posted in that state until completion of 9 years of service or promotion to SMG Scale IV, whichever is earlier. No Travelling Allowance is payable for the Online Test and LLPT. Unemployed SC/ST candidates called for interview will be paid actual to-and-fro 2nd Class Rail/Ordinary Bus fare. Candidates must possess a valid Aadhaar card; the Bank may conduct Aadhaar verification at various stages. Any dispute arising from this recruitment is subject to the sole jurisdiction of the Courts situated at NCT of Delhi. The Bank reserves the right to cancel or modify the recruitment process at any stage without prior notice.
