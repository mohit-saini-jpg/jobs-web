HP Jal Shakti Vibhag Bilaspur Recruitment 2026 : Selection Process & Exam Pattern

Selection for Para Pump Operator, Para Fitter and Multipurpose Worker posts will be done through the following stages: Document screening and shortlisting, applicable if the number of applications received is much higher than the number of posts Skill test or physical test, as per the requirement of the post applied for Original certificates and testimonials will be verified at the time of evaluation, before the skill or physical test.

Documents Required

Legitimate proof of age Proof of BPL family, if applicable (not older than six months) Proof of educational and technical qualification Certificate of Bonafide of Himachal Certificate and experience proof, if any, of the related field issued by the competent authority Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC) EWS category certificate issued by the competent authority (not older than six months) Candidates applying for the post of Multipurpose Worker must compulsorily attach the detailed marks sheet of Middle Standard (8th). If the marks sheet is not issued by the concerned Education Board, a detailed marks certificate from the school principal must be attached instead. Shortlisting for this post is based on marks obtained in the Middle Standard only, and not on any higher qualification.
