BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Exam Date Out - Latest Update

BITS Pilani has released the BITSAT 2026 Session 2 schedule, confirming that the online test will be held over three consecutive days from 24 May 2026 to 26 May 2026 in multiple sessions each day. The dates are published in the BITSAT 2026 information brochure and reiterated by major exam portals, ensuring candidates can plan their preparation and travel accordingly.

Session 2 is intended for candidates who either chose both sessions at the time of registration or opted specifically for the second session during the dedicated application window. The exam is conducted in computer-based test (CBT) mode at approved test centres across India and select international locations, following the same pattern and rules as Session 1.

BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Eligibility Criteria

BITSAT 2026 Session 2 follows the same eligibility criteria as Session 1 because both sessions belong to the same admission cycle for the Integrated First Degree programmes offered at BITS Pilani, BITS Goa and BITS Hyderabad. Students must have passed or be appearing in Class 12 in 2026 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics (or Biology for specific programmes) and satisfy the minimum overall aggregate and subject-wise marks specified in the official brochure.

The eligibility rules also cover conditions regarding attempt limits, qualifying boards and year of passing Class 12, and they clearly state that admission is subject to fulfilling all academic requirements. Candidates are strongly advised to read the detailed eligibility section available on the official admissions portal before applying to avoid rejection at later stages.

How to Apply for BITSAT 2026 Session 2

Candidates who did not register during the initial BITSAT 2026 application window get a separate opportunity to apply exclusively for Session 2 during the dedicated Session 2 registration period. Those who have already selected “both sessions” while filling the original form do not need to submit a fresh application for Session 2.

Step-by-step process to apply for BITSAT 2026 Session 2:

  • Visit the official BITSAT 2026 admissions website (admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in) and click on the BITSAT link on the homepage.
  • Register as a new user by providing a valid email ID, mobile number and basic details to generate login credentials.
  • Log in to the application portal and fill in personal, academic and contact information accurately, matching your Class 12 records.
  • Choose your preferred exam cities and select the appropriate option for Session 2 only (or both sessions if the option is available and applicable).
  • Upload a recent passport-size photograph and signature in the specified format and size guidelines mentioned in the brochure.
  • Pay the prescribed application fee online through net banking, debit card or credit card, and submit the form before the last date.

The form should be reviewed carefully before final submission, as incorrect details can lead to issues during slot booking, hall ticket generation or admission. If any mistakes are noticed later, candidates can use the form correction window for Session 2 to rectify permitted fields within the given dates.

BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot Booking Process

Slot booking is a mandatory step where candidates select the date, time and test centre for their BITSAT 2026 Session 2 exam from the available options. For Session 2, the slot booking window is open from 13 May 2026 to 15 May 2026, and candidates must complete this process within the specified period.

How to book BITSAT Session 2 slot:

  • Go to the official BITSAT 2026 portal and log in with your application number and password.
  • Click on the “BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Slot Booking” link once it becomes active on the dashboard.
  • View the list of available dates, time slots and test centres for Session 2 and select the combination that best suits your schedule.
  • Confirm your choices and submit the slot booking form; once submitted, changes may not be allowed, so double-check before finalising.

If a particular slot or centre is not visible, it usually means all seats for that combination have been taken and you will need to choose another option. Candidates who fail to book a slot within the given window might be automatically allotted a slot by the system, subject to availability, which may not be convenient.

How to Download BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Hall Ticket

The BITSAT 2026 Session 2 hall ticket (admit card) is issued online and can be downloaded from 20 May 2026 until the day of the exam. Only those candidates who have submitted the application form successfully and completed slot booking are eligible to download their Session 2 admit card.

Steps to download BITSAT Session 2 admit card:

  • Visit the official BITSAT 2026 admissions website and click on the “Download Hall Ticket” link for Session 2.
  • Log in using your application number and password or date of birth, as specified in the portal instructions.
  • After logging in, click on the hall ticket download link and verify details such as your name, photograph, test date, shift and test centre address.
  • Download the admit card in PDF format, print multiple copies and keep them safely for the exam day and future reference.

The hall ticket must be carried to the exam centre along with a valid photo ID proof, and these documents are verified at the entry gate before allowing candidates to proceed to the test lab. Any discrepancy in the hall ticket details should be reported to BITS Pilani authorities immediately for rectification before the exam.

BITSAT 2026 Exam Pattern and Test Cities

BITSAT 2026 follows a computer-based test pattern with multiple-choice questions from Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics or Biology, English Proficiency and Logical Reasoning, aligned with the BITS Pilani integrated first degree curriculum requirements. The test carries a fixed number of questions and includes a provision for negative marking, details of which are given in the official brochure and exam notification.

The exam is conducted across various test cities in India and selected international test centres, and candidates choose their preferred cities during the application process. Final test centre allotment is announced before slot booking, and candidates can then select date and slot based on the available centres and timings.

Important Instructions for BITSAT 2026 Session 2 Candidates

BITS Pilani issues detailed exam-day instructions for BITSAT 2026 Session 2, which candidates must follow to ensure a smooth experience at the test centre. These instructions cover reporting time, identity verification, allowed and prohibited items, and behaviour inside the test hall.

Key points generally include reaching the test centre well before the reporting time indicated on the admit card, carrying the hall ticket and a valid photo ID, and not bringing gadgets like mobile phones, calculators or smartwatches. Candidates are also advised to read the on-screen instructions carefully, use rough sheets provided by the centre for calculations, and avoid any unfair means or misconduct to prevent disqualification.

BITSAT 2026 Session 1 vs Session 2 - Which Attempt Should You Choose?

BITSAT allows candidates to appear in Session 1, Session 2 or both, and the best of the two scores is considered for admission to BITS Pilani and its other campuses. This structure gives students flexibility and an additional opportunity to improve their performance if they are not satisfied with their first attempt.

Session 1 is usually held in April, while Session 2 takes place in May, giving candidates extra time to revise and analyse their earlier performance before attempting again. Choosing both sessions can be beneficial for those aiming for higher scores, but it also requires strategic planning of preparation, practice tests and travel arrangements.
