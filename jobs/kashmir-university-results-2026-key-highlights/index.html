How to Check Kashmir University Result 2026

Students can check the Kashmir University Result 2026 for B.Ed and other courses online through the official KU results portal. The steps are simple and help candidates quickly access their B.Ed 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 4th semester results using registration or roll number details if required.

  • Visit the official website of Kashmir University: https://www.kashmiruniversity.net.
  • On the homepage, search for the "Results" or "Examination/Results" section.
  • Click on the results link to open the KU results portal - https://egov.uok.edu.in/results/.
  • In the "Latest Results" list, locate the notification for your B.Ed semester (1st, 2nd, 3rd or 4th semester, relevant batch).
  • Click on the corresponding "View Result" link to open the result page or PDF.
  • If asked, enter your Registration Number, Roll Number, or other login credentials and submit.
  • Check your subject-wise marks, result status and other details displayed on the screen.
  • Download and save the provisional result or marksheet for future reference.
