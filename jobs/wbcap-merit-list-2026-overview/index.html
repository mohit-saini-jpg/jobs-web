What Is WBCAP Merit List 2026?

The WBCAP 2026 merit list contains the names of shortlisted candidates for undergraduate admissions across 459 government and government-aided colleges in West Bengal.

  • College-wise merit list - Prepared by individual Higher Education Institutions (HEIs)
  • Based on Class 12 marks - Merit rank determined by board examination scores
  • Includes seat allotment - Shows allotted college & course details
  • Provisional status - Candidates can accept seat or choose upgradation
  • Fee payment required - Confirm admission by paying semester fee online

How to Check WBCAP Merit List 2026 (Step-by-Step)

Follow these simple steps to download your WBCAP 2026 merit list & seat allotment:

Method 1: Via Official Portal (wbcap.in)

  • Visit official website: Go to [wbcap.in](https://wbcap.in/)
  • Click on "Merit List" tab - Located on homepage
  • Select University & College - Choose your preferred institution
  • Merit list displays - View list for selected college
  • Search your name - Find your name in the list
  • Download PDF - Save merit list for future reference

Method 2: Via Login Portal

  • Go to wbcap.in - Official admission portal
  • Click "WBCAP Merit List 2026 for Phase 1" - On homepage
  • Log in with credentials:
  • User ID / Registration Number
  • Password
  • Submit - Your merit list & allotment status displays
  • Check & download - Save seat allotment details

What to Do After WBCAP Merit List Release?

If Your Name Appears in Merit List:

  • Accept allotted seat - Confirm admission immediately
  • Pay semester fee online - Through respective college portal
  • Complete admission - Within June 9-15, 2026 deadline
  • Keep documents ready - For physical verification (June 27-July 4)

If You Want Higher Preference College:

  • Choose upgradation option - Opt for upgrade round
  • Wait for upgrade merit list - Released June 20, 2026
  • Check new allotment - View upgrade round seat allocation
  • Accept or decline - Confirm upgraded seat if allotted

If Your Name NOT in Merit List:

  • Wait for vacant seats - Published July 7, 2026
  • Register for mop-up round - July 7-16, 2026
  • Check mop-up merit list - Released July 19, 2026

WBCAP 2026 Counselling Process: CAP Rounds

  • Merit-based selection - Based on Class 12 board marks
  • Preference order - Candidates fill college preferences during registration
  • Automatic allotment - System assigns best available college per merit
  • No physical counselling - Fully online centralised admission
  • Upgradation option - Available for higher preference colleges
