Selection Process

  • The engagement shall be purely on contract basis for a period of 11 months or till regular appointments against the same posts are made
  • whichever is earlier
  • and does not confer any right for regularization.
  • Selection will be based on scrutiny of applications and interview/walk-in-interview as decided by AIIMS Deoghar. No TA/DA will be paid for attending document verification or interview. The appointee will be on whole-time appointment and shall not accept any other assignment or engage in private practice during the contract period. The contract can be terminated at any time by either side by giving one month’s notice or paying one month’s salary without assigning any reason. The appointee will not be entitled to benefits like Provident Fund
  • Pension
  • Gratuity
  • Medical Attendance
  • Seniority
  • Promotion etc. available to regular Government servants.

How To Apply

  • The application process for AIIMS Deoghar Non-Faculty contractual posts is completely offline.
  • Visit the official website of AIIMS Deoghar and download the prescribed application form from the recruitment section.
  • Fill the application form carefully in all respects and affix a recent passport size colour photograph in the given space.
  • Attach self-attested photocopies of date of birth certificate, degree certificates, experience certificates, caste certificate (if applicable), No Objection Certificate (if working in Govt/Autonomous body) etc. in the order mentioned in the advertisement.
  • Draw a Demand Draft of the applicable application fee in favour of “AIIMS Deoghar” payable at Deoghar and attach the original DD with the application.
  • Send the complete application form along with all enclosures and Demand Draft by post to the following address: Recruitment Cell, All India Institute of Medical Sciences, Deoghar, At - Devipur Campus, Ramsagar, Deoghar - 814117, Jharkhand.
  • Ensure that the application reaches the above address within 45 days from the publication of the advertisement in Employment News.
  • Shortlisted candidates will be informed about the schedule of document verification and interview via email. No TA/DA will be paid for appearing in the interview/document verification.

Important Instructions

  • Candidates already working in Central/State Government/Semi-Government/Autonomous bodies must submit a No Objection Certificate from their present employer at the time of interview. Canvassing of any kind will lead to disqualification. Applications incomplete in any respect will be summarily rejected. The Competent Authority reserves the right to amend, cancel or change the advertisement as a whole or in part without assigning any reason.
