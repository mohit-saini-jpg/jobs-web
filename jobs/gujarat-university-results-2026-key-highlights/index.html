Steps to Check Gujarat University NEP BA Sem 3 Result 2026

Students can check the Gujarat University NEP BA Sem 3 Result 2026 online through the dedicated NEP result portal using their seat number. The steps below will help NEP BA students quickly access and download their semester 3 marksheet from resultnep.gujaratuniversity.ac.in.

  • Visit the Gujarat University official website: https://www.gujaratuniversity.ac.in or directly open the NEP result portal at https://resultnep.gujaratuniversity.ac.in/.
  • On the NEP result portal, look for the option related to NEP 2020 based course results.
  • Select your course as "BA" and choose the appropriate semester "Sem 3" from the available options (if provided in dropdown menus).
  • Enter your Seat Number exactly as printed on your admit card.
  • Enter the captcha code displayed on the screen for verification.
  • Click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button.
  • Your Gujarat University NEP BA Sem 3 Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download the result marksheet and take a printout for future reference and academic use.
