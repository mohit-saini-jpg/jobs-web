SASTRA University Application Form 2026 is now open for admissions. SASTRA Deemed University has started accepting online applications from March 27, 2026 for various Undergraduate and Postgraduate programmes across its Thanjavur, Kumbakonam, and Chennai campuses.

Interested candidates can apply for B.Tech, 5-Year Integrated Law, M.Tech, MBA, B.Com, BBA, BCA, M.Sc and other courses. Admissions are based on merit, JEE Main 2026 scores, CLAT 2026 scores and Class 12th marks, depending on the programme. Candidates are advised to apply before the respective last dates to secure their seats.

Eligibility Criteria for SASTRA University Admission 2026

Candidates must have passed the qualifying examination (Class 12th for UG and Graduation for PG) with minimum required marks as prescribed for each programme. For B.Tech programmes, admission is based on JEE Main 2026 scores + Class 12th marks or Class 12th marks only (Stream 2).

Courses Offered at SASTRA University 2026

Engineering Programmes (Thanjavur Campus) SASTRA University offers a wide range of B.Tech and M.Tech programmes including Aerospace, Bio Engineering, Bioinformatics, Biotechnology, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, CSE (various specializations), Electronics and Communication, Electrical and Electronics, Mechanical, Robotics & AI, Mechatronics and many more. 5-Year Integrated M.Tech programmes are also available in all streams of Engineering.

Law Programmes (5-Year Integrated) SASTRA offers B.A. LL.B. (Hons.), B.Com. LL.B. (Hons.), and B.B.A. LL.B. (Hons.) programmes at Thanjavur campus.

Commerce & Management Programmes Programmes include B.Com. (BFSI), B.Com. (Computer Application), B.B.A., M.B.A. and M.C.A.

Education, Science and Humanities Programmes Programmes include B.Sc., B.Ed., M.Sc. in various subjects like Biotechnology, Computer Science, Data Science, Physics, Chemistry etc.

Chennai Campus Programmes Offers B.Com., B.B.A., B.C.A., B.A. (Economics & Public Policy), M.B.A. and M.Sc. Data Science.

Kumbakonam Campus Programmes Offers B.B.A., B.Com., B.Sc. in multiple disciplines and M.Com., M.Sc. programmes.
