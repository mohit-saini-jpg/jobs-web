FAQs

Q1What is the total number of vacancies in HLL Lifecare Limited Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 146 vacancies have been announced under HLL Lifecare Limited Recruitment 2026 across 9 positions including Senior Dialysis Technician (25), Dialysis Technician (75), Staff Nurse (30), Central Project Coordinator (3), QA Coordinator-Dialysis (8), Lab Technician (1), Junior Lab Technician (2), Account Assistant (1), and IT Officer (1). The number of vacancies is subject to change depending on organizational requirements.
Q2What is the walk-in selection schedule for HLL Lifecare Limited Recruitment 2026?
A
The walk-in selection for HLL Lifecare Limited Recruitment 2026 will be held from 07 June 2026 to 14 June 2026 at various venues across Maharashtra. Candidates must report between 10:00 AM and 12:30 PM on the scheduled date at the respective venue for their preferred location/post.
Q3How can I apply for HLL Lifecare Limited Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates do not need to apply online. They must directly attend the walk-in selection at the designated venue on the scheduled date with the prescribed application form (downloadable from www.lifecarehll.com/careers) and all required original documents along with self-attested copies. No other mode of application will be accepted.
Q4What is the age limit for HLL Lifecare Limited Recruitment 2026?
A
The maximum age limit for all posts is 37 years as on 01.06.2026. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST/OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) and PwD candidates as per Government of India rules.
Q5What is the selection process for HLL Lifecare Limited Recruitment 2026?
A
For most posts (Senior Dialysis Technician, Dialysis Technician, Staff Nurse, Central Project Coordinator, QA Coordinator-Dialysis, Lab Technician, Junior Lab Technician), the selection process consists of a Skill Test followed by a Written Test. For IT Officer and Account Assistant, the selection is through a Written Test only. The Written Test consists of MCQs of 50 marks to be completed in 30 minutes with no negative marking.
Q6What documents are required for the HLL Lifecare Limited walk-in selection?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents along with self-attested copies, including: proof of age, educational qualification certificates and mark sheets, experience certificates, latest salary certificate with break-up, Aadhaar and PAN Card, recent passport-size photograph, and community certificate for SC/ST/OBC (NCL) candidates. Candidates who fail to produce original documents will not be permitted to participate.
Q7What is the minimum qualification required for the Dialysis Technician post in HLL Recruitment 2026?
A
For the Dialysis Technician post, candidates must have a Certificate course, Diploma, BSc, or MSc in Medical Dialysis Technology / Renal Dialysis Technology from a recognized institution. The required experience varies from a minimum of 2 years (for MSc holders) to 7 years (for Certificate course holders) of relevant post-qualification experience.
Q8What is the pay scale for HLL Lifecare Limited Recruitment 2026 posts?
A
The basic pay range varies by post: Senior Dialysis Technician (Rs. 14000-32500), Dialysis Technician (Rs. 11500-23000), Staff Nurse (Rs. 9500-19000), Central Project Coordinator (Rs. 13000-30000), QA Coordinator-Dialysis (Rs. 13000-30000), Lab Technician (Rs. 11500-23000), Junior Lab Technician (Rs. 10000-20000), Account Assistant (Rs. 10000-20000), and IT Officer (Rs. 12000-29500). Actual remuneration will be based on qualifications, experience, and domain expertise.
Q9Is the HLL Lifecare Limited Recruitment 2026 a permanent government job?
A
No. The appointment under HLL Lifecare Limited Recruitment 2026 will be on a Fixed Term Contract basis only. It is not a permanent government job. HLL Lifecare Limited is a Mini Ratna Central Public Sector Enterprise (Government of India Enterprise) under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare.
