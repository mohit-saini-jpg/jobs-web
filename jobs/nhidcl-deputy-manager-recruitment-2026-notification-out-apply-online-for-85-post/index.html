Important Instructions

Candidates must be Indian Citizens or subjects of Nepal/Bhutan with a valid GATE score in Civil Engineering (2024/2025/2026). Candidates must possess the prescribed minimum qualification on the closing date of application. No hard copy applications will be accepted - online submission only via NHIDCL Recruitment Portal. Reserved category candidates applying for unreserved posts will not be eligible for any relaxations. Government/PSU candidates must submit NOC/relieving letter at the time of document verification - no pay protection or past service benefits on joining. Canvassing or external influence will disqualify the candidate from selection. Candidates should avoid middlemen, recruitment agents, consultants, and fraudulent job promises. Corrigendum/addendum/errata/updates will be posted only on NHIDCL's website under "Recruitment" (Career - current vacancies). Candidates must keep their email ID active for one year - no change in registered email ID will be allowed. All appointments are subject to successful completion of a Background Verification Process (BGV). Selected candidates must pass a medical test and obtain a medical fitness certificate from a Medical Board of State/Central Government Hospital before joining. Selected candidates are required to undergo a two-year probation period, extendable up to a maximum of two more years. All disputes are subject to Delhi High Court jurisdiction, with the English version prevailing for interpretation. NHIDCL at any stage may cancel the advertisement or selection process without assigning any reasons. For detailed information, refer to NHIDCL Cadre (Recruitment, Promotion & Seniority) Rules, 2025 available on the NHIDCL website.

Q1Q1. What is NHIDCL Deputy Manager Technical Recruitment 2026?
A
NHIDCL Deputy Manager Technical Recruitment 2026 is a direct recruitment drive by National Highways and Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited to fill 85 Deputy Manager (Technical Cadre) posts in E2 Grade under the Ministry of Road Transport & Highways, Government of India.
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are released for NHIDCL Deputy Manager (Technical) 2026?
A
NHIDCL has notified a total of 85 vacancies for the post of Deputy Manager (Technical Cadre), including category‑wise seats for UR, SC, ST, OBC, EWS and 2 posts horizontally reserved for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwBD).
Q3Q3. What is the eligibility criteria for NHIDCL Deputy Manager Technical Recruitment 2026?
A
To be eligible, candidates must hold a Degree in Civil Engineering from a recognized University/Institute and possess a valid GATE score in Civil Engineering from any of the years 2024, 2025, or 2026, in addition to fulfilling age limit and nationality conditions mentioned in the official notification.
Q4Q4. Is GATE mandatory for NHIDCL Deputy Manager (Technical) 2026?
A
Yes, a valid GATE score in Civil Engineering for any of the years 2024, 2025 or 2026 is mandatory for NHIDCL Deputy Manager (Technical) Recruitment 2026, and candidates without a valid GATE (CE) score are not eligible to apply.
Q5Q5. What is the age limit for NHIDCL Deputy Manager Technical posts?
A
The maximum age limit is 34 years as on 08 June 2026 for General and EWS category candidates, with relaxations in upper age limit for OBC (NCL), SC, ST, PwBD and Ex‑Servicemen as per Government of India rules specified in the recruitment notice.
