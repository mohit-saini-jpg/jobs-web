HP Jal Shakti Vibhag Para Workers Recruitment 2026 - 30 : Job Overview

Himachal Pradesh Jal Shakti Vibhag, through Jal Shakti Division Fatehpur (Tehsil Fatehpur, District Kangra), has invited applications for 30 Para Worker posts - Para Pump Operator, Para Fitter, and Multipurpose Worker - on a part-time/hourly basis for the operation and maintenance of Water Supply and Irrigation Schemes. This is Recruitment Notice No. EE/JSD/FTR/EA-I/Recruitment of Para Workers/2026-3102-08, dated 13 July 2026. Applications must reach the Executive Engineer's office by 31 July 2026, 5:00 PM.

HP Jal Shakti Vibhag Eligibility Criteria

The candidate should have a minimum qualification of matriculation (10th) with a certificate in the trade of Electrician/Wireman/Diesel mechanic/Pump mechanic/Motor mechanic/Pump operator cum mechanic from a recognized ITI, or matriculation (10th) with a certificate in the above-cited trades under the Skill Development scheme of the Government. The candidate should have a minimum qualification of matriculation (10th) with an ITI certificate in the trade of Fitter from a recognized ITI institute, or matriculation (10th) with a certificate in the above-cited trade under the Skill Development scheme of the Government. The candidate should have a minimum qualification of Middle (8th) Pass or its equivalent from a school duly recognized by the State Government.

HP Jal Shakti Vibhag Age Limit

Minimum age: 18 years. Maximum age: 45 years. Age is calculated as on 31 July 2026. This age criterion applies to all three posts - Para Pump Operator, Para Fitter, and Multipurpose Worker.
