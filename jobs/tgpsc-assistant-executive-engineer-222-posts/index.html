FAQs

Q1What is TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026?
A
TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026 is the Telangana Public Service Commission notification for 222 Assistant Executive Engineer (Civil) posts in the Transport, Roads and Buildings Department.
Q2How many vacancies are available in TGPSC AEE Civil 2026?
A
A total of 222 vacancies are available, distributed across Multi Zone-I (112) and Multi Zone-II (110) with reservations for various categories including SC, ST, BC, EWS, and PwBD.
Q3What is the eligibility for TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must have a Bachelor’s Degree in Civil Engineering (B.E./B.Tech) from a recognized Indian University or institution recognized by UGC/AICTE or AMIE.
Q4What is the age limit for TGPSC AEE Civil 2026?
A
The age limit is 18 to 44 years as of 01/07/2026 for OC candidates, with relaxations for SC/ST/BC, PwD, Ex-Servicemen, NCC instructors, and Telangana State Government employees as per rules.
Q5What is the TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026 selection process?
A
Selection includes a Written Examination (Objective Type – Paper I: General Studies & Aptitude, Paper II: Civil Engineering), followed by Document Verification and Medical Examination.
Q6How can I apply for TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must register for One Time Registration (OTR) on the TGPSC website, then apply online with the TGPSC ID and upload required documents before the last date.
Q7What is the application fee for TGPSC AEE Civil 2026?
A
The application processing fee is ₹1,000 for OC & BC candidates and ₹500 for SC/ST/PwD candidates. The examination fee is ₹120 for employed candidates; unemployed applicants are exempted.
Q8When will the TGPSC AEE Civil 2026 online application start and end?
A
The online application opens on 06-06-2026 and closes on 13-07-2026 at 5:00 PM. Candidates must complete OTR registration beforehand.
Q9Where will the TGPSC AEE Civil exam be conducted?
A
Examination centres include Hyderabad, Karimnagar, Khammam, Warangal, and Nizamabad, with the exact venue details mentioned in the hall ticket.
Q10Who should not apply for TGPSC AEE Civil 2026?
A
Candidates without a Civil Engineering degree, those whose result is pending, candidates from other engineering branches, underage/overage candidates, non-Telangana locals seeking reservation, and those without OTR registration should not apply.
