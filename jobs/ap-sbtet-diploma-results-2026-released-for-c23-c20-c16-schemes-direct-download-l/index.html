How to Check AP SBTET Diploma Results 2026

Checking your scorecard is straightforward. You'll only need your PIN (Permanent Identification Number) or Hall Ticket number and date of birth.

  • Go to the official website: sbtet.ap.gov.in or apsbtet.net
  • Click on the Results or Examination tab on the homepage
  • Select Diploma Examinations from the list
  • Choose your scheme (C23/C20/C16/C14) and semester
  • Enter your PIN or Hall Ticket number + date of birth
  • Click Submit to view your result
  • Download and print the PDF marksheet for future use

Alternate portals like Manabadi and Vidyavision also host the results if the official site is down

Important Details to Verify

The online scorecard is provisional, but you should cross-check everything right away:

Your full name
PIN/Hall Ticket number
Subject codes and names
Marks obtained in each subject
Pass/fail status

The board will send original hard-copy marks memos to your polytechnic or pharmacy college within the next few weeks

Re-Verification (RV) and Recounting

Not happy with your grades? You can apply for re-verification or recounting. The board is expected to release:

  • RV application schedule
  • Fee structure
  • Online application link

These details should appear on the main portal by next week
