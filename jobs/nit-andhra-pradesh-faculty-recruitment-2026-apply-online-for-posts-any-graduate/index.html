The minimum qualifications generally include a B.Tech/B.E. and M.Tech/M.E. or equivalent in the respective branches. Some positions, particularly in Schools of Sciences and Humanities, require a Ph.D.

Q6. What is the selection process for the Adhoc Faculty roles?

The selection process typically involves an offline lecture demonstration followed by an offline interview. Candidates are evaluated based on their teaching skills and subject knowledge.

Q7. Are there any specific age limits for the Adhoc Faculty positions?

The provided data does not specify age limits. Candidates are advised to check the official notification for any age criteria or relaxation details.

Q8. Which departments are recruiting Adhoc Faculty?

NIT Andhra Pradesh is recruiting Adhoc Faculty in various departments including Civil Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communications Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Metallurgical and Materials Engineering, Biotechnology, School of Sciences (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry), and School of Humanities (English, Management).
