DCET Admit Card 2026 OUT

The Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) has released the DCET Admit Card 2026 for the Karnataka Diploma Common Entrance Test on the official KEA portal. Candidates who submitted the DCET 2026 application form can now log in and download their hall ticket in online mode only.

As per the official schedule, the Karnataka DCET 2026 exam will be conducted on 23 May 2026 across designated centres in the state, for lateral entry admission to 2nd year B.E/B.Tech and 1st year B.Arch courses for diploma holders.

How to Download DCET Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the KEA official site: https://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea and click on the “DCET‑2026” link, or open the login page https://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/dcet2026app/login directly.
  • On the DCET‑2026 page, click on “DCET‑2026 Admit Card Download Link”.
  • Enter your User ID / Application Number and Password, then type the captcha/security code shown on the screen.
  • Click on “Login / Submit” to access your DCET 2026 candidate dashboard.
  • Click on the “Download Admit Card / Hall Ticket” button; your DCET Admit Card 2026 will be displayed as a PDF.
  • Verify all details (name, paper, exam date, centre, shift) and download and print 2-3 clear A4‑size copies of the hall ticket.

If you forget your User ID or password, use the “Forgot User ID / Password” option on the login page (if available) or check your registration email/SMS for the login details.

Details Mentioned on DCET Admit Card 2026

After downloading, candidates must cross‑check the following details:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • DCET 2026 User ID/application number and hall ticket number
  • Exam name: Diploma CET (DCET) 2026
  • Course applied for (B.E/B.Tech lateral entry / B.Arch, etc.)
  • Exam date: 23 May 2026, and exam time/duration as per the paper timetable
  • Exam centre name, code and full address
  • Candidate’s category and other application details (if printed)
  • Important exam‑day instructions and reporting/gate‑closing time

If there is any discrepancy in the admit card (name, photograph, course, or exam centre), candidates should immediately contact KEA through the official helpdesk before the exam date.

Documents Required with DCET Admit Card 2026

On the day of the Karnataka DCET 2026 exam, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the DCET Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof, such as: Aadhaar Card Pan Card Passport Voter ID Driving Licence Any other government‑issued ID accepted by KEA.
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Pan Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID
  • Driving Licence
  • Any other government‑issued ID accepted by KEA.
  • Additional documents if specified (e.g., PwD certificate and scribe permission letter for candidates availing scribe facility).

Entry to the exam hall will not be permitted without the printed admit card and valid photo ID.
