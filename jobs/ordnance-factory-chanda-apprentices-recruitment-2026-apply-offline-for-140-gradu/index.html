Ordnance Factory Chanda Apprentices Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

Ordnance Factory Chanda (OFCH), a unit of Munitions India Limited under the Ministry of Defence, has invited applications for 140 Apprentice posts for one year of practical training under the Apprentices Act, 1961. The vacancies cover 45 Graduate Apprentice (Engineering Streams), 45 Graduate Apprentice (General Streams), and 50 Technician Apprentice (Diploma Holders) positions. Eligible candidates must submit the prescribed application form offline to OFCH, Chandrapur, Maharashtra, on or before 9 July 2026. There is no online application facility for this recruitment.
