FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for Bengaluru Civil Court Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 30 July 2026 by 11:59 PM. The fee payment deadline is also 30 July 2026 at 11:59 PM. No applications will be accepted after this date.
Q2How many total vacancies are there in Bengaluru Civil Court Recruitment 2026?
A
Across both notifications (429/2026 and 428/2026), there are over 106 vacancies covering Typist Grade-III, Peon (Attender), Driver, Process Server, and Peon (Javana) posts.
Q3What is the application fee for Bengaluru Civil Court Recruitment 2026?
A
General Merit candidates must pay ₹300/-. Category-II(A), II(B), III(A), III(B) candidates pay ₹150/-. SC, ST, Category-I, and Persons with Disability candidates are fully exempted from paying any fee.
Q4What is the official website to apply for Bengaluru Civil Court Recruitment 2026?
A
Applications must be submitted only at https://bengaluru.dcourts.gov.in/recruitment-status-ccc/. No other website or offline method is valid.
Q5What is the educational qualification required for the Typist Grade-III post?
A
Candidates must have passed the Second PUC or hold a 3-year Diploma in Commercial Practice, along with a Senior Grade pass in Kannada and English Typing and Shorthand from the Public Education Department (or an equivalent Diploma), or be a current student in these courses.
Q6What is the age limit for applying to Bengaluru Civil Court Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must be at least 18 years old. The upper age limit is 40 years for General Merit, 43 years for OBC (Category-II/III), and 45 years for SC/ST/Category-I candidates, as on the last date of application.
