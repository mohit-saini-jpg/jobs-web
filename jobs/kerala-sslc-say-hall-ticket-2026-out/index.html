Kerala SSLC SAY Hall Ticket 2026 Out

The Kerala Pareeksha Bhavan has released the Kerala SSLC SAY Hall Ticket 2026 for the supplementary examinations. Schools and examination officers can download the hall tickets from the official portal, and students will receive their admit cards through their respective schools rather than downloading them directly.

The Kerala SSLC SAY exams are scheduled to begin from 10 June 2026 and continue through mid-June. Students should collect the hall ticket on time and verify all details carefully before the exam.

How to Collect Kerala SSLC SAY Hall Ticket 2026

  • School authorities should visit the official portal sslcexam.kerala.gov.in.
  • Log in using the school login ID and password.
  • Open the Kerala SSLC SAY Hall Ticket 2026 download section.
  • Download the hall tickets for the registered students.
  • Distribute the printed admit cards to students after checking the details.

Students do not get a direct download option, so they must collect the hall ticket from their school.

Details Mentioned on the Hall Ticket

The Kerala SSLC SAY hall ticket generally includes:

  • Student’s name.
  • Register number / roll number.
  • School name and code.
  • Exam centre name and address.
  • Subject names and subject codes.
  • Exam date, day and timing.
  • Candidate instructions and exam-day guidelines.

Students should verify the details once they receive the hall ticket from school and report any errors immediately to school authorities.

Documents Required on Exam Day

Candidates should carry the following items to the exam centre:

  • A printed copy of the Kerala SSLC SAY Hall Ticket 2026 issued by the school.
  • A valid school identity card or any other ID specified by the school or exam centre.
  • Recent passport-size photographs, if asked by the school or centre authorities.

Without the hall ticket, candidates may not be allowed to enter the exam hall.

Exam-Day Instructions

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time.
  • Carry only the items allowed by the board and school.
  • Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, calculators or any electronic gadgets.
  • Follow the instructions given by invigilators and school authorities.
  • Keep the hall ticket safe until the completion of the SAY examination process.
