AIBE 21 Admit Card 2026 Out

The Bar Council of India (BCI) has released the AIBE 21 Admit Card 2026 for the All India Bar Examination XXI (AIBE 21) on its official website. Candidates who successfully registered for AIBE 21 can now log in to the exam portal and download their hall ticket online.

As per the official schedule, the AIBE 21 exam will be held on 7 June 2026 in offline, OMR based mode at exam centres across the country. The admit card download link is active on allindiabarexamination.com, and candidates must carry a printed copy of the AIBE XXI hall ticket 2026 along with a valid photo ID to the exam centre.

How to Download AIBE 21 Admit Card 2026

  • Visit the official AIBE website allindiabarexamination.com.
  • On the homepage, click on the link “AIBE XXI Admit Card 2026” or “AIBE 21 Admit Card 2026” under the latest updates.
  • You will be redirected to the admit card login page. Enter your registration number/login ID and password, then submit the captcha code if required.
  • Click on the Login / Submit button to access your candidate dashboard.
  • Click on “Download Admit Card / Hall Ticket”; your AIBE 21 admit card 2026 will open on the screen.
  • Check all details printed on the hall ticket carefully and download and print 2–3 clear A4‑size copies for exam day.

Candidates are advised to download the admit card well before the exam day to avoid last minute technical issues.

Details Mentioned on AIBE 21 Admit Card 2026

The AIBE 21 admit card 2026 contains important details that must be verified by the candidate before the exam:

  • Candidate’s name, photograph and signature
  • AIBE 21 registration number / roll number
  • Exam name: All India Bar Examination XXI (AIBE 21) 2026
  • Exam date: 7 June 2026 and exam timing, including reporting time
  • Exam city, centre name, centre code and full address
  • Category details (General/OBC/SC/ST/PwD), if printed
  • Instructions regarding items allowed/prohibited, reporting schedule and exam day code of conduct

In case of any discrepancy in the details, candidates must contact the AIBE helpdesk/BCI immediately using the contact information given on the official website.

Documents Required with AIBE 21 Admit Card 2026

On the exam day, candidates must carry:

  • A printed copy of the AIBE 21 Admit Card 2026 (clear A4‑size printout; colour or good‑quality black and white).
  • One original valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence or any other government‑issued ID accepted as per BCI instructions.
  • Any additional documents mentioned in the admit card/instructions (for example, disability certificate for PwD candidates, if applicable).

Without the admit card and valid photo ID, candidates will not be allowed to enter the exam centre.

Exam‑Day Instructions for AIBE 21 Exam 2026

To ensure smooth conduct of the All India Bar Examination XXI (AIBE 21) 2026, candidates should follow these guidelines:

  • Reach the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on the AIBE admit card so that verification and seating formalities can be completed; latecomers after gate‑closing time may not be allowed entry.
  • Carry only essential items: AIBE 21 admit card (A4 printout), valid photo ID and permitted stationery (pens etc.); follow the latest instructions on whether bare acts/books are allowed, as notified by BCI.
  • Do not carry mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (if not allowed), Bluetooth devices, earphones, written notes, or any electronic gadgets and loose papers into the exam hall; violation can lead to cancellation of candidature and further disciplinary action.
  • Follow all instructions printed on the admit card and those announced by the invigilators; indulging in unfair means, talking to other candidates or disobeying instructions may result in cancellation of the exam and debarment from future AIBE attempts.
  • Preserve your AIBE 21 admit card 2026 safely even after the exam, as details from it may be required for result reference and issue of Certificate of Practice.
