AIIMS Bhopal Eligibility Criteria 2026

Project Research Scientist-1 (Non-Medical): Post Graduate Degree, including the integrated pg degrees (Life Sciences). Hands-on experience in bacterial culture and Molecular Biology techniques such as nucleic acid isolation and PCR (Desirable). Project technical support-II: 10th + diploma(MLT/DMLT/ITI or equivalent) + 2years experience in relevant subject/field OR 3 year Graduate degree in relevant subject/field + 1 year experience in relevant subject. Hand on experience in media preparation, reagent and molecular reagent preparations (Desirable).

AIIMS Bhopal Age Limit 2026

Project Research Scientist-1 (Non-Medical): Maximum age limit is 35 years. Age relaxation for SC/ST is applicable as per rule. Project technical support-II: Maximum age limit is 30 years. Age relaxation for SC/ST is applicable as per rule.
