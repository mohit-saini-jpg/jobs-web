NCERT RIE Admission 2026: Registration Begins May 15, CEE Exam on June 28

NCERT RIE admission 2026 registration process will start from May 15, 2026, for various teacher education programmes offered through Regional Institutes of Education across India. The National Council of Educational Research and Training has released the official notification for admissions to undergraduate and postgraduate courses for the academic session 2026. Candidates who want to pursue a career in teaching can apply online through the Common Entrance Examination portal till June 5, 2026.

The CEE 2026 exam is gonna be conducted on June 28, 2026, at multiple centres throughout the country. This entrance test is mandatory for getting admission into B.Ed., M.Ed., integrated courses, and other teacher training programs at different RIE campuses.

Courses Offered in NCERT RIE 2026

NCERT is offering multiple teacher education programmes through RIE admissions 2026 for different qualification levels. The courses available are designed to prepare quality teachers for secondary and higher secondary schools.

  • Integrated M.Sc.Ed. (6-Year Programme) - For students who completed 12th with PCM and want to become higher secondary teachers in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Bachelor of Education - B.Ed. (2-Year Programme) - For graduates wanting to teach at secondary level in Science & Mathematics or Language & Social Science
  • Integrated B.Ed.-M.Ed. (3-Year Programme) - A combined degree program for B.Ed. holders
  • Master of Education - M.Ed. (2-Year Programme) - Primarily designed for preparing teacher educators and education professionals
  • Master of Arts in Education Technology (2-Year Programme) - Specialized course in educational technology applications

These courses are available at various RIE campuses located in Ajmer, Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Mysore, and Shillong.

Eligibility Criteria for RIE CEE 2026

The eligibility requirements vary depending on which course you're applying for in NCERT RIE admission 2026:

For Integrated M.Sc.Ed. (6-Year):

  • 12th pass in Science with PCM stream
  • Minimum 50% marks in aggregate (45% for SC/ST/PwD candidates)

For B.Ed. (2-Year):

  • Bachelor's degree in Science/Arts/Commerce/Social Science/Humanities
  • At least 50% marks in graduation or post-graduation (45% for reserved categories)

For Integrated B.Ed.-M.Ed. (3-Year):

  • B.Ed. degree with minimum 50% marks
  • Relaxation of 5% for SC/ST/PwD candidates

For M.Ed. (2-Year):

  • B.Ed./B.A.B.Ed./B.Sc.Ed./B.El.Ed. or equivalent teaching degree
  • Minimum 50% marks (45% for SC/ST/PwD)

For M.A. in Education Technology:

  • Bachelor's degree with relevant background
  • Minimum qualifying percentage as per NCERT norms

Candidates appearing in their qualifying examination can also apply, but they must upload their final marks by the specified deadline.

How to Apply for NCERT RIE Admission 2026

The application process for RIE CEE 2026 is completely online and pretty straightforward. Follow these steps to complete your registration:

  • Visit the official CEE portal at cee.ncert.gov.in
  • Click on the "NCERT RIE Admission 2026" or "Online Application" link (will be active from May 15)
  • Register yourself by providing basic details like name, email ID, and mobile number
  • Fill the application form with personal, educational, and other required information
  • Upload scanned copies of photograph, signature, and necessary documents
  • Select your preferred course and RIE centre
  • Pay the application fee through online mode (credit card/debit card/net banking/UPI)
  • Submit the form and take a printout for future reference

Make sure all the details you enter are accurate because mistakes can lead to rejection of application. Keep your valid email ID and phone number handy as all communication will be sent through these.

RIE CEE 2026 Exam Pattern

The Common Entrance Examination for RIE admission 2026 follows a specific pattern that candidates should understand before appearing:

  • Exam Mode: Offline (pen and paper based)
  • Exam Date: June 28, 2026 (Sunday)
  • Duration: Specific timing will be mentioned on admit card
  • Question Type: Multiple choice questions (MCQs)
  • Subjects Covered: General Knowledge, Teaching Aptitude, Reasoning, English Language, and subject-specific questions depending on course

The syllabus includes topics from graduation level for most courses, while integrated programmes may have questions based on 12th standard subjects. Detailed syllabus and exam pattern will be available in the information brochure on the official website.

Admit Card and Answer Key Details

NCERT will release the RIE CEE 2026 admit card on June 20, 2026, through the official portal. Candidates need to login with their registration credentials to download the hall ticket.

The admit card will contain important details like:

  • Candidate's name and photograph
  • Roll number and registration number
  • Exam centre name and address
  • Exam date and reporting time
  • Important instructions for the exam day

Answer Key Release: The provisional answer key for CEE 2026 will be published on the official website after the examination is over. Candidates can raise objections against the answer key within the specified time frame, and NCERT will consider valid objections before declaring final results. Students are advised to keep checking the official website regularly for updates regarding answer key, results, and counselling schedule.
