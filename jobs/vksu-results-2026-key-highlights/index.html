Veer Kunwar Singh University, Ara has declared VKSU Result 2026 for the Bachelor of Library and Information Science (BLIS) 1st Semester examination held in February 2026 for the 2025-2026 session. VKSU Result 2026 has been published online on the official examination portal – vksuexams.com – where students can access their BLIS 1st semester marks by using the result link.

BLIS candidates can check and download their marksheet by visiting the VKSU result page, selecting the relevant BLIS 1st semester result option and entering their registration number and date of birth in the login fields. After viewing the online marks memo, students should verify their personal details, subject wise marks, total marks and pass/fail status and contact the university examination branch in case of any discrepancy.

VKSU Results 2026 Key Highlights

The latest VKSU Results 2026 update adds professional library science to the list of programmes whose results are available online for the 2025-2026 academic session. This BLIS 1st semester publication follows VKSU’s ongoing schedule of releasing semester wise results for UG and professional courses on its dedicated exam portal.

How to Download VKSU Result 2026

Follow these steps to check and download your VKSU BLIS 1st Semester Result 2026:

  • Go to the VKSU exam portal: https://vksuexams.com.
  • On the homepage, click on the “Result” link or directly open https://vksuexams.com/results.aspx.
  • On the results page, select the appropriate Exam/Result option corresponding to BLIS 1st Semester, Session 2025-2026, Exam February 2026 (if a dropdown is provided).
  • Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth in the specified fields.
  • Click on the View Result / Submit button to display your BLIS 1st semester marksheet.
  • Check your name, registration number, course, semester, subject wise marks, total marks and result status, then download or print the result for your records and future academic processes.
