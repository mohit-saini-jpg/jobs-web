CUHP Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Medical Officer (Female): MBBS registered with MCI/State Medical Council with 4 years experience as Medical Officer. Private Secretary (On Direct Recruitment basis): Bachelor's Degree with 3 years as PA or 5 years as Stenographer, English/Hindi Stenography 120/100 wpm, Typing 35/30 wpm, computer knowledge. Private Secretary (On Deputation basis): Bachelor's Degree with 3 years as PA or 5 years as Stenographer, English/Hindi Stenography 120/100 wpm, Typing 35/30 wpm, computer knowledge. Hindi Translator (On Direct Recruitment basis): Master's Degree in Hindi/English or related with translation diploma/experience. Multi-Tasking Staff: 10th Pass or ITI Pass. Lower Division Clerk (LDC) (On Deputation basis): Bachelor's Degree with English/Hindi Typing 35/30 wpm and computer proficiency. Laboratory Attendant: 10+2 with Science or 10th with Science and Laboratory Technology certificate.
