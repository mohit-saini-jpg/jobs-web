AP ICET 2026 Answer Key - Overview

The Andhra Pradesh Integrated Common Entrance Test (AP ICET) 2026 is conducted for admission to MBA and MCA courses in universities and colleges across Andhra Pradesh. The exam was held in computer-based mode in two shifts, and Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) has released the preliminary answer key along with the master question papers and response sheets. Candidates can use these to verify their responses and raise objections, if necessary, within the stipulated deadline.

AP ICET 2026 Answer Key Link @ cets.apsche.ap.gov.in

The AP ICET 2026 answer key is available only in online mode on the APSCHE CET portal. Candidates do not need to sign up again to access the master question papers and preliminary keys. The same portal hosts the link for response sheets, which allows each candidate to see their marked responses.

Master Question Papers with Preliminary Keys (Shift 1 & 2)

APSCHE has published separate master question paper PDFs with preliminary keys for Shift 1 and Shift 2. These documents contain all questions and the officially suggested answers for that shift. Candidates can download the PDFs directly and use them to compare with their own response sheets.

AP ICET 2026 Response Sheet Download

The response sheet shows the exact options that the candidate marked during the exam. It is candidate-specific and can be accessed only after login. By matching the response sheet with the answer key, candidates can estimate their raw score very accurately.

How to Download AP ICET 2026 Answer Key Online (Step-by-Step)

This section targets “how to download AP ICET answer key” type searches. Keep the steps simple and scannable.

  • Go to the official APSCHE CET website: cets.apsche.ap.gov.in.
  • Click on the “AP ICET 2026” link on the homepage.
  • Look for the “Exam Papers / Keys” or “Master Question Papers with Preliminary Keys” section.
  • Click on the relevant link for preliminary answer key and question paper.
  • Select your exam shift (Shift 1 or Shift 2).
  • The master question paper with preliminary key PDF will open; download and save it on your device.

To download your response sheet:

  • Open the AP ICET 2026 page on the same portal.
  • Click on the “Response Sheet” link or similar option.
  • Log in using your registration number, hall ticket number and date of birth.
  • View, download and save your AP ICET 2026 response sheet.

Remind users to confirm their exam shift from their hall ticket before selecting any key.

How to Use AP ICET 2026 Answer Key to Calculate Your Score

Once candidates have both the preliminary key and their response sheet, they can easily estimate their AP ICET score. This helps them understand their performance, predict approximate rank and plan for counselling.

Follow these steps:

  • Keep the master question paper, preliminary key and your response sheet open.
  • For each question, check which option you marked in the response sheet.
  • Compare it with the correct option given in the preliminary key.
  • Count total correct answers and note them down.

AP ICET 2026 Marking Scheme

AP ICET uses a simple, candidate-friendly marking scheme:

  • Each correct answer: +1 mark
  • Wrong answer: 0 mark (no negative marking)
  • Unattempted question: 0 mark
  • Total questions: 200
  • Maximum marks: 200

Score Calculation Using Answer Key

Because there is no negative marking, the formula is straightforward:

  • AP ICET 2026 score = Number of correct answers × 1

Example: If a candidate has 142 correct answers out of 200, the estimated AP ICET score will be 142 marks.

AP ICET 2026 Answer Key Objection Process

The preliminary answer key is open to challenge so that any genuine mistakes can be corrected before finalization. Candidates who find discrepancies in the preliminary key can raise objections within the given time window.

Steps to Raise Objections Online

  • Visit cets.apsche.ap.gov.in and open the AP ICET 2026 section.
  • Click on the link related to “Objections on Preliminary Key” or “Key Objections”.
  • Download and read the official instructions and objection format (if provided).
  • For each objection, mention: exam shift, question number, answer in preliminary key, and your proposed correct answer.
  • Provide a clear explanation with reference to standard textbooks or authentic sources.
  • Submit objections through the prescribed mode (online form/email as specified).

Last Date, Fee and Important Rules

  • The objection window is open only for a limited duration after the preliminary key is published.
  • Objections submitted after the deadline or without proper justification may not be accepted.
  • The exam authority will review all valid objections, prepare the final answer key and publish it before or along with the result.
  • AP ICET result and ranks will be based only on the final answer key.

Encourage candidates to raise objections only when they are confident and have strong proof.

AP ICET 2026 Result Date and Next Steps for Counselling

After the final answer key is prepared, the AP ICET 2026 result will be declared on the same official portal. Candidates will be able to download their rank cards using their registration details and hall ticket number.

Once results are out:

  • APSCHE will issue a detailed AP ICET 2026 counselling notification.
  • Candidates will need to register for counselling, pay the counselling fee and complete document verification.
  • They can then exercise web options, choose colleges and courses, and participate in seat allotment rounds.

AP ICET 2026 Answer Key - Important Instructions for Candidates

  • Confirm your exam shift (Shift 1 or 2) as mentioned on the hall ticket before downloading the answer key.
  • Use the official master question paper and preliminary key from cets.apsche.ap.gov.in for score estimation.
  • Always compare the key with your official response sheet instead of relying on memory alone.
  • Do not blindly trust unofficial keys circulating on social media; use them only as secondary references.
  • Raise objections, if any, strictly within the official schedule and format.
  • Keep a copy of the answer key, question paper and response sheet until the admission process is complete.
