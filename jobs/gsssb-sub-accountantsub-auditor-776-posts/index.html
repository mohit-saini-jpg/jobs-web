FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply online for GSSSB Sub Accountant Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for submitting the online application under the corrected advertisement is 30 June 2026. Candidates should not wait until the last day to avoid technical issues. The fee payment window closes on 02 July 2026. Early application is highly recommended.
Q2How many total vacancies are available in GSSSB Advt. 366/202526?
A
After the correction, the total vacancies have been increased to 776 — 671 posts for Sub Accountant/Sub Auditor and 105 posts for Accountant/ Auditor/ Deputy Treasury Officer/ Inspector.
Q3What is the required educational qualification for these posts?
A
Candidates must have a Bachelor’s Degree in BBA, BCA, B.Com, B.Sc (Maths/Statistics), or BA (Statistics/Economics/Mathematics) from a recognized university. The degree must be completed on or before 30 June 2026. B.Sc (CA & IT) and M.Sc (CA & IT) are not considered eligible.
Q4What is the age limit for GSSSB Sub Accountant posts?
A
The age limit is between 20 to 35 years as on 30 November 2025. Upper age relaxation is provided for reserved categories, women, PwBD, and Ex-Servicemen as per Gujarat Government norms (maximum 45 years in any case).
Q5Is there any application fee for GSSSB Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, application/examination fee is applicable. General category candidates pay higher fees while SC/ST/PwBD candidates get concessions. Exact fee structure is mentioned in the detailed notification.
Q6What is the selection process for GSSSB Class-3 Finance posts?
A
Selection is based on Preliminary Objective Exam, Main Written Exam, and Document Verification. Only candidates qualifying both exams will be called for document verification.
Q7What is the salary structure for selected candidates?
A
For the first five years, a fixed pay is given (₹26,000 for Sub Auditor and ₹49,600 for Accountant cadre). After five years of satisfactory service, regular pay scales (Level-4 and Level-7) are applicable as per 7th Pay Commission.
Q8Do previous applicants need to apply again?
A
No. Candidates who had successfully submitted and confirmed their application in November 2025 do not need to re-apply. Only new candidates or those whose previous applications were invalid should apply afresh.
Q9Is computer knowledge mandatory?
A
Yes. Basic computer knowledge as prescribed by the Government of Gujarat is required. Candidates will have to produce proof at the time of appointment if not submitted earlier.
Q10Where will the selected candidates be posted?
A
Selected candidates will be posted in various Accounts, Treasury, and Finance offices across different districts of Gujarat as per the requirement of the Finance Department.
