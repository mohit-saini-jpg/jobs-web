WB JENPAS UG 2026 Registration: Dates, Link, Steps & Eligibility (wbjeeb.nic.in)

WB JENPAS UG 2026 registration opens on March 24, 2026, via the official link at wbjeeb.nic.in/jenpas-ug.​

WB JENPAS UG 2026 Overview

The West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) organizes JENPAS UG for admission to undergraduate programs like BSc Nursing, BPT, BMLT, and other paramedical courses in West Bengal colleges. This online-only process ensures candidates can apply from anywhere, with exams scheduled for June 7, 2026.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have passed Class 12 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics, securing at least 50% aggregate marks (40% for reserved categories). The minimum age is 17 years as of December 31, 2026, with no upper age limit mentioned. Indian citizens, including those from other states, can apply, though West Bengal domicile may offer reservation benefits.

Step-by-Step Registration Guide

Follow these steps to complete your WB JENPAS UG 2026 application without errors.

  • Go to wbjeeb.nic.in/jenpas-ug and select "JENPAS UG 2026 Registration."​
  • Register with your email, mobile number, and a secure password to create an account.
  • Log in and enter personal details like name, date of birth, category, domicile, and academic qualifications.
  • Upload a recent passport photo (20-50 KB, 4x3 cm, JPG) and signature (10-20 KB, JPG).​
  • Choose your exam papers: Paper 1 (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English) and/or Paper 2 (Physics/Math, General English/Awareness).
  • Review details, pay the fee using net banking, debit/credit card, or UPI.
  • Submit and download the confirmation page print it for records, though no physical submission is needed.

Required Documents & Fees

Prepare scanned copies of your photo and signature meeting the exact specifications to avoid rejection. The non-refundable fee is ₹500 for general category candidates and ₹400 for SC/ST/OBC (plus bank charges). Payment is online only; save your transaction ID and confirmation.
