How to Apply

  • 1First complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the GPSC-OJAS portal at https://gpsc-ojas.gujarat.gov.in if not already done. Note: OTR alone does not constitute an application.
  • 2Visit https://gpsc-ojas.gujarat.gov.in → Click "Online Application" → Click "Apply" → Select the relevant advertisement from the active list.
  • 3Log in using your OTR Registration Number and Date of Birth, then click Apply Now.
  • 4Fill in Personal Details, Communication Details, Other Details (sports, disability, widow, ex-serviceman status), Language Details, Educational Details, and Experience Details (if required).
  • 5Upload your Passport-size Photo (5 cm × 3.6 cm, JPG, max 10 KB, white background, with date of photograph - not older than 1 year from application closing date) and Signature (2.5 cm × 7.5 cm, JPG, max 10 KB).
  • 6Accept the Self-Declaration (Assurance) and click Save. Your Application Number and Confirmation Number will be generated - save both.
  • 7Pay the applicable application fee via Post Office or Online before the fee deadline.
  • 8Download and keep the printed copy of the confirmed application form. Applications can be edited using the Edit option until the closing date - do not submit multiple applications.
  • 9After the Preliminary Test provisional result, shortlisted candidates must upload required certificates/documents online at https://gpsc-iass.gujarat.gov.in/ - physical documents sent by post/in-person will not be accepted.
  • 10Candidates applying for more than one post must submit separate applications and separate fees for each post.

FAQs

Q1Can I apply for multiple posts?
A
Yes, but you must submit a separate online application and pay a separate fee for each post. Your OTR registration can be reused across all applications.
Q2Who can apply for Advt. 41/2026-27 (District Education Officer)?
A
Only Divyang (Persons with Disabilities) candidates are eligible. This is a Special Recruitment Drive for PwD backlog vacancies. The 1 vacancy is reserved for Group D & E disability types (SLD, MI, MD involving Groups A to D).
Q3What is the final merit basis for selection?
A
The final selection is based on 50% weightage each for the Preliminary Test (300 marks) and Interview (100 marks), resulting in a combined score out of 100.
Q4Are all these 15 advertisements part of the same recruitment cycle?
A
Yes. All 15 advertisements were released in June 2026 and share the same application window (June 5 to June 19, 2026). The Preliminary Test Part-I (General Studies) is also scheduled on the same date (July 26, 2026) for most posts.
Q5What happens if I submit more than one application for the same advertisement?
A
Only the last confirmed application will be considered. For unreserved (General) candidates, the last confirmed application with fee payment will be valid. Prior fees are not refunded or carried forward.
Q6Is there a physical fitness requirement for any post?
A
Yes - the Assistant Director, Boilers (Advt. 40) requires candidates to have a good physique with maximum 180 cm height and 105 cm girth, and the ability to enter the manhole of a boiler. PwD candidates with BA/BL disabilities will be subject to a Medical Board opinion before appointment.
Q7Will OTR registration count as an application?
A
No. OTR is only a helper module for faster application. Candidates must mandatorily submit a separate online application for each advertisement after completing OTR.
