CNCI Kolkata Apprenticeship Recruitment 2026: 44 : Job Overview

The Chittaranjan National Cancer Institute (CNCI), Kolkata has invited applications for 10 different apprenticeship programs at its 2nd Campus in New Town, Kolkata. The recruitment is issued vide Advertisement No. N/022/2026 dated 13.07.2026, offering a total of 44 apprenticeship vacancies across posts such as Deputy Duty Manager, General Duty Assistant, Medical Laboratory Technician, Patient Counsellor, Operation Theatre Technician, Emergency Care Assistant, Medical Records Assistant and Frontline Healthcare Associate.
