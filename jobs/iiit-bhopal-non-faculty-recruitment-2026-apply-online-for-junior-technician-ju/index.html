IIIT Bhopal Selection Process 2026

The tentative method of selection for all three posts (Junior Superintendent, Junior Technician, and Junior Assistant) consists of: A Written Test/Examination comprising a General Aptitude/Ability Test Followed by a Discipline-Specific Proficiency/Skill Test The Institute reserves the right to restrict the number of candidates called for the Written Test/Skill Test based on qualifications, salary, relevance of experience, and other academic achievements exceeding the minimum eligibility criteria. Mere fulfillment of essential qualifications does not guarantee a call for the test.

IIIT Bhopal Non Faculty Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

Applicants must be citizens of India. Candidature remains provisional until document verification by the appointing authority; fraudulent claims of reserved category status will lead to debarment. The Institute may restrict the number of candidates called for the Written Test/Skill Test through additional shortlisting criteria. Shortlisted candidates' Application Numbers will be displayed only on the Institute website; no separate postal communication will be sent. Vacancy numbers are provisional and subject to variation; the Institute may not fill a post, or may fill additional vacancies. Appointment is subject to the candidate being declared medically fit by the competent authority. Canvassing in any form will disqualify the candidature. Records of non-selected (excluding waitlisted) candidates will not be preserved beyond six months from the date of the final result. Any dispute related to the recruitment process is subject to courts/tribunals having jurisdiction over Bhopal. The Institute has clarified that selections are made purely on merit, and candidates are strongly advised not to pay money to any person assuring employment or to seek individual/political influence.
