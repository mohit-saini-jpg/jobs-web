Qualification / Eligibility

Historian /Social Science Expert: Masters in History / Archaeology / Anthropology / Sociology / Heritage Management / Cultural Studies with 5 years of experience in stakeholder consultation/ public outreach, research, documentation, and heritage interpretation. Assistant Director: Bachelor's in Architecture with 10 years of experience with the Council of Architecture (CoA), or B.Arch + Master's in Management / Public Administration / Heritage Management with 4 years of experience. Assistant Architects: B.Arch with 02 years experience. G.I.S. Expert: Master's/Bachelor's in Geography, Geoinformatics, Urban Planning, or related field with 3 years of experience in GIS mapping, spatial analysis, and data management. Administrative Coordinator/ Office Superintendent: Bachelor's in Public / Business Administration, Management, Commerce, Urban/ Heritage Management, or related field; post graduation in Public/Business Administration, Project Management, Urban Governance, or Urban/ Heritage Management with minimum 5 years' experience in administrative coordination. Or a retired AMC employee from the post of Office Superintendent or above. Conservation Architects: M.Arch (Conservation) with 2 Years of Experience in Architectural Heritage conservation field. Draft Person: ITI / Diploma / higher degree in Draftsmanship / Architecture / Civil Engineering from a recognized institution with Min 3 years' experience in drafting, technical documentation. Senior Executive – Tourism Cell: Master's in Tourism, Business Management, Urban Planning, or related fields with a tourism/heritage focus, with 5+ years experience. Manager Creative Communication & Branding: Master's in Mass Communication, Visual Communication, Design, Journalism, or related fields with a tourism/heritage focus, with 4+ years in branding, tourism promotion, or public communication. Asst. Manager - Tourism Planning & Community Programmes: Master's in Social Work, Heritage Management, Development Studies, or Economics or related fields with a tourism/heritage focus, with 2+ years in community-based tourism or development projects. Asst. Manager Visitor Experience & Operations: Bachelor's or Master's in Tourism Management, Event Management, Hospitality, or Business Administration or related fields with a tourism/heritage focus, with 2+ years in tourism or city-level operations. Asst. Manager Tourism Data & Digital Platforms: Master's in IT, Computer Science, Data Science or related fields with a tourism/heritage focus, with 2+ years in data analytics or digital platform management. Restoration Engineer: Retired Assistant Civil Engineer and above officer of Engineering department of AMC. Restoration Supervisor: Retired Technical Supervisor and above officer of Engineering department of AMC.
