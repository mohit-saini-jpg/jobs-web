Dr BR Ambedkar Open University Hall Ticket 2026 - Quick : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 13/06/2026

The Dr BR Ambedkar Open University Hall Ticket 2026 is the official admit card issued by Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Open University (Dr BR Ambedkar Open University), Hyderabad, for its UG CBCS (Choice-Based Credit System) semester examinations. Every student enrolled in BA, BCom, BSc, or any other undergraduate programme must download and carry this hall ticket to their examination centre - without it, entry to the exam hall is strictly denied.

The hall ticket serves as both a photo identity document and an official exam entry pass. It contains your exam schedule, assigned examination centre, subject details, and important instructions you must follow on exam day. Since Dr BR Ambedkar Open University does not distribute physical copies, downloading and printing the hall ticket in advance is entirely the student's responsibility.

Who Is Eligible to Download the Dr BR Ambedkar Open University Hall Ticket 2026?

Not every registered student can automatically download the hall ticket. Dr BR Ambedkar Open University issues the admit card only to students who meet specific conditions. Here is a summary of who is eligible:

  • Students admitted to UG (CBCS) programmes at Dr BR Ambedkar Open University for the relevant academic year
  • Candidates who have submitted the examination application form for the 1st, 3rd, or 5th semester within the stipulated deadline
  • Students whose examination fee has been paid successfully and confirmed by the university
  • Candidates who fulfil any additional eligibility conditions mentioned in the latest official exam notification

If your hall ticket is not available despite meeting the above conditions, contact the Dr BR Ambedkar Open University examination branch or your assigned study centre immediately.

How to Download Dr BR Ambedkar Open University Hall Ticket 2026 - Step-by-Step Process

Downloading your Dr BR Ambedkar Open University UG CBCS hall ticket is a straightforward process that takes only a few minutes. Follow these steps carefully to get your admit card without any issues.

Step 1: Visit the Official Dr BR Ambedkar Open University Portal

Open your browser and go to the official Dr BR Ambedkar Open University website at Dr BR Ambedkar Open University.ac.in, or navigate directly to the hall ticket login page at online.Dr BR Ambedkar Open University.ac.in/UGCBCSExamHallTicket/UGCBCSExamHallTicketLogin.

Step 2: Locate the Hall Ticket Download Link

On the main website, scroll down to find the "Exam Registration & Notification" or "Hall-Tickets Download" section. Click on the relevant link for your course and semester - for example, "Download hall tickets for UG (CBCS) I-Semester Exam 2026."

Step 3: Enter Your Admission Number

You will be redirected to the hall ticket login page. Enter the last 7 or 8 digits of your Admission Number exactly as registered with the university.

Step 4: Select Your Semester

From the dropdown menu, choose the correct semester - 1st, 3rd, or 5th Semester - as applicable to you.

Step 5: Click Submit

Click the "Submit" or "Download" button. Your Dr BR Ambedkar Open University hall ticket will appear on the screen.

Step 6: Verify All Details

Before printing, carefully check every detail on the hall ticket (see the next section for what to verify).

Step 7: Download and Print

Click "Print" or "Download" to save the hall ticket as a PDF. Take at least two printed copies - one to carry to the examination centre and one as a backup.

Details Printed on the Dr BR Ambedkar Open University Hall Ticket 2026

The Dr BR Ambedkar Open University hall ticket carries several important pieces of information that help exam centre staff verify your identity and exam eligibility. After downloading, cross-check the following details against your official admission records:

  • Student's Full Name (as registered)
  • Admission/Registration Number
  • Course Name (BA / BCom / BSc etc.)
  • Semester (1st / 3rd / 5th)
  • Subject Names and Subject Codes
  • Exam Dates and Time Slots
  • Assigned Examination Centre Name and Address
  • Candidate's Photograph
  • University Seal and Authorised Signature
  • Important Instructions for Exam Day

If you spot any discrepancy - such as a wrong name, incorrect subject list, or wrong exam centre - report it immediately to the Dr BR Ambedkar Open University examination branch or your study centre. Do not wait until exam day.

Documents to Carry to the Examination Centre

Carrying only the hall ticket is not sufficient. Dr BR Ambedkar Open University requires students to carry an additional valid photo identity proof alongside the hall ticket. The following documents are accepted as identity proof:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID Card
  • Driving Licence
  • Passport
  • College/University ID Card

Both documents - the printed hall ticket and one valid photo ID - are mandatory for entry into the examination hall.

What to Do If Dr BR Ambedkar Open University Hall Ticket Is Not Available?

Sometimes students are unable to find or download their hall ticket even after the official release. Here is what you should do if this happens:

  • Double-check your Admission Number - Ensure you are entering the correct last 7 or 8 digits. Typographical errors are a common cause of login failures.
  • Confirm exam registration - Verify that you completed the examination registration form and paid the exam fee before the deadline (including any late fee window, if applicable).
  • Clear browser cache - Sometimes cached data prevents the portal from loading correctly. Try using a different browser or incognito/private window.
  • Contact your Study Centre - Your assigned Dr BR Ambedkar Open University study centre (one of the 213+ centres across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh) can verify your registration status.
  • Contact the Dr BR Ambedkar Open University Examination Branch - Reach out to the official examination branch directly at Dr BR Ambedkar Open University, Hyderabad, for urgent resolution.

Important Instructions for Dr BR Ambedkar Open University Exam Day

Following the instructions printed on the hall ticket is mandatory. Here are the general guidelines that Dr BR Ambedkar Open University students must observe on the day of the exam:

  • Arrive early - Reach the examination centre at least 30 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • Carry both documents - Hall ticket and a valid photo ID are non-negotiable.
  • No electronic devices - Mobile phones, calculators (unless permitted), and other electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited inside the exam hall.
  • Follow dress code guidelines - If any specific dress code instructions are mentioned in the hall ticket, follow them.
  • Do not share your hall ticket - The hall ticket is issued exclusively for the registered candidate.
  • Report errors in advance - Any error in the hall ticket must be reported before the exam, not on the day itself.
