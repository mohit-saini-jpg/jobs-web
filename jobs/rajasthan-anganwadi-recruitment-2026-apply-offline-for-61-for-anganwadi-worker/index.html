Eligibility Criteria

In rural areas, the woman must be a resident of the same Gram Panchayat where the Anganwadi centre is located. In urban areas, the woman must be a resident of the same ward. A widow or divorced woman may be considered a local resident of either her in-laws' home or her maternal home, as applicable. As per court orders, both married and unmarried women are eligible to apply for the Anganwadi Worker/Helper post. If a selected woman ceases to be a local resident due to marriage or any other reason, her honorarium service will be automatically terminated. An undertaking (affidavit) on this condition must be given at the time of selection. If found ineligible for local residency at any point in future, the department may remove her from honorarium service even after selection. The minimum educational qualification for the post of Anganwadi Worker/Helper is Class 12th (Senior Secondary) pass .

Age Limit

If a woman already engaged on honorarium service is selected again for any post, the age limit will not apply to her, as per the notification.

Rajasthan Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 : Important Instructions

This recruitment is for voluntary honorarium-based Anganwadi Worker/Helper posts, not a salaried government job in the conventional sense. Applications must be submitted only at the concerned project office; applications submitted at the Deputy Director's office (the signing authority) will not be accepted. Complete details and the departmental circulars (No. 4244 dated 12.01.2023 and No. 57871-58286 dated 01.03.2024) governing the evaluation criteria are available at the Deputy Director's office, the concerned CDPO office, and on the department's website, wcd.rajasthan.gov.in. The notification has also been marked for wide publicity through Sarpanch/Secretary of the concerned Gram Panchayat/Nagar Parishad/Nagar Palika and the respective Panchayat Samiti Development Officers.
