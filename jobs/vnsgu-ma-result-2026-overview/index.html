How to Check Your VNSGU MA Result 2026 Online

Follow these steps to check your individual MA result on the official VNSGU website:

  • Visit the official VNSGU result page: https://old.vnsgu.ac.in/result_notification.php
  • Locate your relevant MA program and semester result notification (2369/2026 for 3rd Semester or 2381/2026 for 4th Semester) from the list.
  • Click on the corresponding result link/PDF to open the result notification.
  • Use Ctrl+F (or the search function) to locate your seat number within the successful/first/second/first-dist list, or check the detailed mark statement if published separately.
  • Download or print the result notification PDF for your records.

Note: The uploaded notification pages provide the official successful seat number lists, subject-wise highest marks, and category-wise pass counts. They do not include a full individual mark sheet for every student; students must refer to the official VNSGU portal for their personal detailed mark statement.
