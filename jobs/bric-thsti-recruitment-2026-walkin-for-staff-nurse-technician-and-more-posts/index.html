BRIC THSTI Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Staff Nurse: GNM / B.Sc. (Nursing) from a recognised University with experience in Clinical training in antenatal care with skills relevant to maternal health. Research Assistant: B.Sc. (Nursing) or Health related discipline from a recognized University OR Diploma in Nursing & Midwifery (GNM) or ANM or equivalent from a recognized university, with one (1) year of post-qualification experience in the relevant field. Technician: B.Sc./ DMLT from a recognised University OR Standard 12th with Science stream minimum three (3) years post-qualification experience in clinical trials/ health-related research projects.

BRIC THSTI Recruitment 2026 - Age Limit

Staff Nurse: Maximum age 35 years. Research Assistant: Maximum age 35 years. Technician: Maximum age 50 years.
