FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for GSSSB Junior Industry Inspector (Junior Udyog Nirikshak) Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application is 20 June 2026 at 23:59 hrs at https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in. The last date to pay the exam fee online is 22 June 2026 at 23:59 hrs. Candidates are advised to apply well before the closing date and not wait till the last moment.
Q2How many vacancies are available for Junior Udyog Nirikshak Class-3 in GSSSB Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 60 vacancies have been announced for Junior Industry Inspector (Junior Udyog Nirikshak) Class-3 under the Office of the Industries Commissioner, Gandhinagar. The number of vacancies may increase or decrease. Divyang reserved posts will be advertised separately.
Q3What is the salary for Junior Industry Inspector (Junior Udyog Nirikshak) in GSSSB 2026?
A
For the first 5 years, selected candidates will receive a fixed pay of ₹40,800/- per month. After 5 years, on satisfactory service, they will be regularized at 7th Pay Commission Level-5 (₹29,200/- to ₹92,300/-), subject to Supreme Court orders in SLP No. 14124/2012 and 14125/2012.
Q4Is Civil Engineering eligible for GSSSB Junior Udyog Nirikshak 2026?
A
No. Civil Engineering is specifically excluded from the eligible qualifications. Only B.E./B.Tech. or Diploma in Engineering/Technology in branches other than Civil Engineering are eligible. Refer to the detailed list of eligible specializations in the official notification.
Q5What is the exam pattern for GSSSB Junior Industry Inspector Recruitment 2026?
A
The exam is a single-stage MCQ-based CBRT/OMR exam with two parts - Part-A (90 marks: Logical Tests, Mathematical Tests, Constitution/Current Affairs/Comprehension) and Part-B (120 marks: Subject-related questions). Total: 210 questions, 210 marks, 180 minutes. Negative marking of 1/4 mark per wrong answer applies. Minimum 40% marks required in both parts separately.
