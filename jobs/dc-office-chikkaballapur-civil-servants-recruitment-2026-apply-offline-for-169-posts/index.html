Chikkaballapur District Urban Local Bodies are recruiting 169 Pourakarmika (Sanitation Workers) posts under Direct Payment scheme with ability to speak Kannada as minimum qualification, last date 30 May 2026.

Selection Process

  • Priority Category Proof Required
  • 1 (Highest) Candidates who have worked or are currently working as Pourakarmika, Loaders, UGD Helpers, Cleaners or Helpers in district Urban Local Bodies on outsource/daily wage/contract basis for minimum 1 year ESI/PF payment records OR Bank Passbook showing salary credit OR Attendance records for the service period
  • 2 Family member (one per family) of a Pourakarmika who died due to COVID-19 or other reasons while working on outsource/daily wage/contract basis in district Urban Local Bodies Death Certificate, Service proof of deceased Pourakarmika
  • 3 Local sanitation workers with experience working for minimum 1 year as Loaders, UGD Helpers, Cleaners, Helpers in Waste Processing Units, Public Toilets, Zero Waste Management Units on outsource basis in district Urban Local Bodies ESI/PF payment records OR Bank Passbook showing salary credit OR Attendance records
  • 4 Victims identified under the Manual Scavengers Act or their family members (husband/wife/son/daughter) - 10% of total vacancies reserved for this category Certificate from competent authority under Manual Scavengers Act; if eligible candidates not available, posts filled from other categories
  • 5 (Last) Other eligible candidates (after filling vacancies from priority categories 1–4) Relevant certification from competent authority as applicable
  • Selection is based on a priority system - candidates are considered in the order of priority categories listed below. There is no written examination. After priority-based shortlisting
  • document verification and medical fitness check will be conducted. Candidates in priority categories 1 to 4 must produce certification from the competent authority. Remaining vacancies after giving priority to categories 1–4 will be filled from other eligible candidates.

How To Apply

  • Candidates shall apply offline only using the prescribed application form. No online application mode is available. The application form is to be obtained from and submitted - along with all required documents - at the office of the respective City Municipal Council (Nagarasabhe) where the vacancy exists.
  • Steps to Apply:
  • Step 1: Visit the respective City Municipal Council office (Chikkaballapur / Chintamani / Shidlaghatta / Gowribidanur) and collect the prescribed application form.
  • Step 2: Fill in the application form completely and attach all relevant supporting documents.
  • Step 3: Submit the duly filled application form along with self-attested copies of all documents at the respective City Municipal Council office on or before 30/05/2026.
  • Documents to be Submitted with Application:
  • Proof of prior service as Pourakarmika/Loader/UGD Helper/Cleaner/Helper - ESI/PF payment records OR Bank Passbook showing salary credit for the service period OR Attendance records (minimum 1 year, for Priority 1 and 3 candidates)
  • Death Certificate of deceased Pourakarmika + his/her service records (for Priority 2 - family members of deceased Pourakarmika)
  • Certificate from competent authority under the Manual Scavengers Act (for Priority 4 candidates)
  • Age proof (Birth Certificate / School Leaving Certificate / SSLC / any Govt-issued age proof)
  • Kannada language proficiency proof (if any)
  • Residential/Domicile proof
  • Medical Fitness Certificate from a Medical Officer (to be produced at time of joining)
  • Notary Affidavit (to be submitted at time of engagement - undertaking not to demand regularisation or approach court for permanency)
  • Any other certification from the competent authority as applicable to the priority category claimed

Important Instructions

  • THIS IS NOT A PERMANENT / REGULAR GOVERNMENT JOB. Selected Pourakarmika are appointed on a temporary Direct Payment basis and are NOT entitled to any benefits available to permanent employees. Selected candidates must mandatorily submit a Notary Affidavit undertaking that they will not demand regularisation/permanency individually or through unions and will not approach any court for regularisation at any point. If a Direct Payment Pourakarmika dies during service, his/her family members are NOT entitled to compassionate appointment, pension, gratuity or any other service benefit. Direct Payment Pourakarmika must perform sanitation/cleaning duties ONLY. They cannot be assigned to any other type of work or office duties. No substitute can perform duty in place of the selected candidate - only the selected individual must personally perform all assigned duties. If a selected candidate fails to perform duties or does not follow instructions/advice given by the office head, the concerned Commissioner/Municipal Commissioner may release them from duty without prior notice. Selected employees must comply with all orders/circulars issued by the Government and the Directorate of Municipal Administration from time to time. A Notary Affidavit to this effect is mandatory. Salary will be fixed as per the Minimum Wages Act of the Labour Department and revised periodically. PF and ESI benefits will be provided. Candidates in priority categories 1 to 4 must ensure they have the required certification from the competent authority - without this, their priority claim cannot be considered. For detailed terms and conditions, candidates must visit the respective ULB website (links given in the Important Links section below).
