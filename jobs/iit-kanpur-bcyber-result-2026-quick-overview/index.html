IIT Kanpur B.Cyber Result 2026 - Quick Overview : Important Dates & Timelines

Notification DatePost Date: 09/07/2026

The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur has declared the result of the Bachelor of Cybersecurity (B.Cyber.) admission test 2026 on July 8, 2026 (Wednesday), as per the institute's official admissions timeline. The result follows the on-campus admission test and hackathon held on July 5, 2026 at IIT Kanpur. The B.Cyber programme is run by the institute's Wadhwani School of AI and Intelligent Systems (WSAIS) and is being launched for the 2026-27 academic year. Candidates who cleared the admission test must report to IIT Kanpur on July 21, 2026 to complete admission formalities.

This article covers everything officially confirmed about the B.Cyber Result 2026 the declaration date, how the result fits into the overall selection process, and the next steps for candidates. Where specific figures such as category-wise cut-offs or the exact number of finally selected candidates have not been made available on an accessible official source at the time of writing, this is clearly stated below rather than estimated.

How to Check the IIT Kanpur B.Cyber Result 2026

IIT Kanpur has published admission-related notices and result updates directly on the official B.Cyber admissions page. Based on the format IIT Kanpur has used throughout this admission cycle (releasing shortlists as downloadable PDF notices on the same page), candidates are advised to follow these general steps.   • Visit the official B.Cyber admissions page at iitk.ac.in.
  • Look for the latest "Notification" or result-related update on the page.
  • Open the linked result notice or PDF.
  • Check your Application Reference ID against the published list.
  • Download and save the result/selection notice for future reference.

Next Steps for Selected Candidates

  • Selected candidates must report to IIT Kanpur on July 21, 2026 to complete admission formalities.
  • Candidates should keep required documents ready, including proof of category certificates where applicable those without a valid EWS/OBC-NCL certificate at present may submit IIT Kanpur's prescribed undertaking, with the admission offer remaining provisional until the certificate is submitted.
  • The program fee structure is the same as that applicable to regular IIT Kanpur students; the application fee (already paid at the applications stage) was ₹1,600 for female candidates and SC/ST/PwD candidates, and ₹3,200 for all other candidates.

Important Instructions

  • The B.Cyber programme is a Senate-approved undergraduate programme run under IIT Kanpur's Wadhwani School of AI and Intelligent Systems (WSAIS).
  • Candidates must have been born on or after October 1, 2001, to be eligible (age relaxation to October 1, 1996, for SC, ST, and PwD candidates).
  • Only Indian nationals are eligible to apply for this program.
  • The program follows a 2+2 structure: two years of coursework at IIT Kanpur, followed by two years of internship, primarily with government organizations.
  • This admission process (JEE Main-based shortlisting) is specific to the 2026 cycle. IIT Kanpur has indicated that future B.Cyber admissions will use a separate entrance test instead of JEE Main scores, with details to be announced later.
