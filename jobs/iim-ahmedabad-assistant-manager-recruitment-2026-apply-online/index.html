Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the IIM Ahmedabad Assistant Manager-Contracts recruitment?

The last date to apply online for the Assistant Manager-Contracts position at IIM Ahmedabad is 16th June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline to avoid any last-minute issues.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Assistant Manager-Contracts post?

Candidates must be graduates (full-time) from a recognized university. A postgraduate management degree is preferred. Experience in tender preparation and knowledge of e-tendering platforms, GFR, contractual clauses, and SAP MM are also required.

Q3. What is the age limit for the Assistant Manager-Contracts position?

The maximum age limit for the Assistant Manager-Contracts post is 35 years. Age relaxation will be provided as per Government of India rules, with an additional 5 years for women candidates.

Q4. What is the salary offered for the Assistant Manager-Contracts role?

The selected candidate will be placed at Pay Level 6 under the 7th Central Pay Commission (CPC). This indicates a competitive salary structure as per government norms.

Q5. Is there an application fee for this recruitment?

No, as per the provided information, there is no application fee for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment.

Q6. What is the mode of application for this recruitment?

Applications for the Assistant Manager-Contracts position must be submitted online. Candidates are encouraged to use the official website or the provided application link.

Q7. What is the job type for this position?

The job type for the Assistant Manager-Contracts position is on a contract basis.

Q8. Where is the job location for this recruitment?

The job is located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat.
