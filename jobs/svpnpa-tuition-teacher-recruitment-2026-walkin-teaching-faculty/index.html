Qualification / Eligibility

Tuition Teacher (Science): Graduation in Bachelor of Science (PCM/PCB) with at least 50 percent marks in the desired subject in Graduation and Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) with at least 55 percent marks in the degree. Desirable: Master's degree i.e. M.Sc with 55 percent marks. Minimum 02 years of teaching experience in school/Tuition Centre/Some Private Organisation. Fair command on Hindi, Telugu and English.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for the SVPNPA Tuition Teacher post?
A
The walk-in interview for the Tuition Teacher (Science) post at SVP NPA is scheduled for 03 June 2026. Candidates should ensure they reach the venue on time on this date.
Q2Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Tuition Teacher (Science) position?
A
Candidates must have a Graduation in Bachelor of Science (PCM/PCB) with at least 50 percent marks and a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) with at least 55 percent marks. A Master's degree (M.Sc) with 55 percent marks is desirable.
Q3Q3. Is there any teaching experience required for this role?
A
Yes, a minimum of 02 years of teaching experience in a school, tuition center, or any private organization is required for the Tuition Teacher (Science) position.
Q4Q4. What is the salary offered for the Tuition Teacher (Science) post?
A
The selected candidate will receive a consolidated salary of Rs. 15,000 per month for the Tuition Teacher (Science) role.
Q5Q5. What is the age limit for applying to this position?
A
The minimum age limit for the Tuition Teacher (Science) post is 21 years. There is no maximum age limit specified in the notification.
Q6Q6. What is the application fee for the SVP NPA Tuition Teacher recruitment?
A
There is no application fee required for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive.
Q7Q7. What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must carry all original documents, self-attested photocopies of educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and passport-size photographs.
Q8Q8. Where is the walk-in interview for the Tuition Teacher (Science) post going to be held?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel National Police Academy (SVP NPA), Hyderabad, Telangana - 500052.
Q9Q9. What is the selection process for this recruitment?
A
The selection process for the Tuition Teacher (Science) post will be based on a Walk-in Interview. Candidates will be shortlisted based on their performance in the interview.
