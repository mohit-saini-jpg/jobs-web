How to Download CCSU Date Sheet 2026

Students can easily download the CCSU Date Sheet 2026 from the official source in just a few clicks. The complete exam programme is available in PDF format for quick reference during preparation.

  • Open your browser and visit the official CCSU website or directly use the PDF link shared above.
  • Locate the examination section or the latest notifications area on the homepage.
  • Search for the CCSU Date Sheet 2026 for CBCS PG Campus Courses June 2026.
