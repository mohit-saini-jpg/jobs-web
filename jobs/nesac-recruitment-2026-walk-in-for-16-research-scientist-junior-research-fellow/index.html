NESAC Recruitment 2026 : Job Overview

The North Eastern Space Applications Centre (NESAC) has announced NESAC Recruitment 2026 (Advertisement No. NESAC/RMT-TEMP/WI-02/2026, dated 17 July 2026) for 16 temporary project-based posts of Research Scientist, Junior Research Fellow (JRF), and Project Associate-I. Selection will be made purely through a walk-in interview scheduled tentatively on 10, 11, and 12 August 2026 at NESAC, Umiam, Ri-Bhoi District, Meghalaya. There is no online application process for this recruitment - eligible candidates must report directly to the interview venue with their biodata and original documents.

NESAC Eligibility Criteria 2026

Candidates often ask what minimum academic percentage is required for NESAC Recruitment 2026 - the notification specifies fixed minimum aggregate marks or CGPA for postgraduate and graduate degree holders across all posts. M.E./M.Tech degree holders need a minimum aggregate of 60% (average of all semesters) or a CGPA/CPI of 6.5 on a 10-point scale or equivalent grade, from a recognized university. B.E./B.Tech/M.Sc degree holders need a minimum aggregate of 65% (average of all semesters) or a CGPA/CPI of 6.84 on a 10-point scale or equivalent grade, from a recognized university. NESAC Age Limit 2026

The maximum age limit for NESAC Recruitment 2026 is calculated as on the date of interview and varies by post category, since Research Scientist, JRF, and Project Associate-I posts have separate age ceilings. Age relaxation of 5 years is available for SC/ST candidates and 3 years for OBC candidates. Ex-servicemen and Persons with Disabilities are eligible for age relaxation as per Government of India orders, and candidates claiming any relaxation must submit valid documentary proof. The date of interview will be treated as the cut-off date for age, qualification, and all other eligibility purposes.

Tenure of NESAC Temporary Positions

All positions under NESAC Recruitment 2026 are purely temporary and project-based, with no provision for regularization at any stage. Research Scientist: Initially engaged for one year, extendable up to a maximum of three years in total (or co-terminus with the project, whichever is earlier), based on performance in periodic reviews. Junior Research Fellow: Initially engaged for one year or the project period, whichever is earlier, extendable up to a maximum of five years or the project duration, whichever is earlier. Project Associate-I: Initially engaged for one year or the project period, whichever is earlier, extendable up to a maximum of five years. Appointment to these posts does not confer any claim or right to regular employment at NESAC, and services can be terminated at any time without prior notice and without assigning any reason.

NESAC Application Fee 2026

There is no application fee mentioned in the NESAC Recruitment 2026 notification, since selection is conducted entirely through a walk-in interview rather than an online application process. Candidates only need to carry their biodata and original documents to the venue on the scheduled interview date.

NESAC Selection Process 2026

Selection for all posts under NESAC Recruitment 2026 is based purely on performance in the walk-in interview - there is no written exam or shortlisting test involved. Meeting the minimum qualification and experience criteria does not automatically make a candidate eligible for the interview; the prescribed qualification and benchmark are treated only as the minimum requirement. Only candidates who possess the required qualification as on the date of interview should attend. Students awaiting their final result should not appear for the walk-in interview. The prescribed experience will be considered valid only if gained after acquiring the essential qualification. Selection is entirely at the discretion of the interview panel based on performance on the day.
