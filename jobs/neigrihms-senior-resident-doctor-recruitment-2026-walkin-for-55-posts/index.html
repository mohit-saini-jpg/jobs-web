NEIGRIHMS Senior Resident Doctor Recruitment 2026 : Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1What is the last date to apply for the NEIGRIHMS Senior Resident Doctor posts?

A
The required educational qualification is a Postgraduate degree or diploma in the respective subject recognized by MCI, after obtaining MBBS with compulsory internship and registration with the State Medical Council.

A
The maximum age limit for the Senior Resident Doctor posts is 45 years. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST and OBC candidates as per government norms.
Q4How many vacancies are available for the Senior Resident Doctor posts?
A
There are a total of 55 vacancies available for the Senior Resident Doctor posts across various specializations.
Q5What is the salary offered for the Senior Resident Doctor position?
A
The selected candidates will receive a salary of Rs. 67700 per month, which is at Level 11 of the Pay Matrix, plus Non-Practicing Allowance (NPA).
Q6What is the application fee for NEIGRIHMS Senior Resident Doctor recruitment?

The selection process will involve a Walk-in Interview followed by Document Verification.
