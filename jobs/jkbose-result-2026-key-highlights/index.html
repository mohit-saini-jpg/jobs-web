How to Check JKBOSE Class 11th Result 2026 Online

Students can follow the step-by-step process given below to check and download the JKBOSE Class 11th Result 2026 Summer Zone from the official website:

  • Visit the official JKBOSE result portal at jkresults.nic.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the link that reads "View Result of Higher Secondary School Examination (Class 11) Session Annual Regular Summer Zone 2026".
  • A new result page will open on the screen.
  • Enter your roll number and registration number in the respective fields as printed on the admit card.
  • Click on the Submit button to proceed.
  • Your JKBOSE Class 11th result marksheet 2026 will be displayed on the screen with subject-wise marks and overall status.
  • Download the marksheet PDF and take a printout for future use (admission, scholarship, documentation).

Students who face any technical issue while checking the result online can try again after some time or contact their respective schools for assistance.
