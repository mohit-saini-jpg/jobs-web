Maharashtra School Education Department has officially released the Maharashtra FYJC Admission 1st Merit List 2026 for Class 11 admissions for the academic session 2026-27. Students who applied for First Year Junior College admissions can now check their selection status online.

Maharashtra FYJC Admission Result 2026 has been released online on the official website - mahafyjcadmissions.in. The merit list includes admission status for Class 11 (FYJC) across Arts, Commerce, and Science streams. Students can check and download their allotment result using their login credentials such as application ID and password.

Students are advised to carefully verify their allotment details, college name, and stream. In case of any discrepancies, they should immediately contact the admission authorities.

How to Check Maharashtra FYJC Admission 1st Merit List 2026

  • Visit the official website - mahafyjcadmissions.in
  • Click on the FYJC Admission login or merit list section
  • Enter your Application ID and Password
  • Click on the login button
  • Your allotment result will be displayed on the screen
  • Check your allotted college and stream details
  • Download and save the result for future reference
