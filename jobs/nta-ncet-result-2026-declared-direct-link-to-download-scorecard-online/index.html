How to Check NTA NCET Result 2026

Follow these simple steps to download your NCET 2026 scorecard:

  • Visit the official NCET website: exams.nta.nic.in/ncet
  • Click on the "NCET 2026 Score Card" or "NCET 2026 Result" link on the homepage
  • Enter your Application Number, Date of Birth, and the Security Pin displayed on the screen
  • Click the Submit button
  • Your NCET 2026 scorecard will appear on the screen
  • Verify all details carefully, including your scores and personal information
  • Download the scorecard and take a printout for future admission and counseling processes

NCET 2026 Final Answer Key

The NCET final answer key 2026 was released on the same day as the result—June 4, 2026. Candidates can download it from the official website to verify their responses.

Steps to download the final answer key:

  • Go to exams.nta.nic.in
  • Click on the "NCET 2026 Final Answer Key" link
  • Log in using your application number and date of birth
  • Complete the captcha and access the answer key PDF
  • Save it for your records

What's Next After NCET Result 2026?

After checking your results, here's what you should expect:

  • Merk List Release: Based on NCET scores, NTA will publish the merit list
  • Counseling Process: Qualified candidates will be called for counseling for admission to the 4-year ITEP program
  • Cut-off Scores: Expected cut-offs vary for top institutions like IITs, NITs, and RIEs
  • Document Verification: Keep all original documents ready, including your NCET scorecard

Important Tips for Candidates

  • Keep multiple printouts of your NCET scorecard safe
  • Verify all personal details on the scorecard immediately after downloading
  • If you spot any discrepancy, contact NTA immediately through the official helpline
  • Stay updated on the official website for counseling dates and instructions
  • Do not share your login credentials with anyone
