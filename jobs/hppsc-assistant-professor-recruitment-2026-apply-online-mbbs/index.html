Job Overview

The Himachal Pradesh Public Service Commission (HPPSC) has officially announced the Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026 with 8 openings under Advt. Nos. 7/6-2026, 5/6-2026, and 11/6-2026. Candidates who are not more than 45 years of age and possess a recognized medical qualification as per the IMC Act/NMC Act 2019, along with the required postgraduate and post-doctoral degree in the concerned super-speciality and a minimum of three years of teaching experience after post-graduation, are eligible to apply. Interested candidates can submit their online applications on or before 01 July 2026. Se

FAQs

Q1What is the last date to apply for HPPSC Assistant Professor Recruitment?
A
The last date to apply online for the HPPSC Assistant Professor (Super-specialty) posts is 01 July 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this deadline.
Q2How many vacancies are available for Assistant Professor posts?
A
There are a total of 8 vacancies for Assistant Professor (Super-specialty) posts released by the Himachal Pradesh Public Service Commission (HPPSC).
Q3What is the minimum teaching experience required for these posts?
A
A minimum of 3 years of teaching experience as Lecturer/Registrar/Demonstrator/Resident after completing post-graduation in the concerned specialty is required.
Q4What is the maximum age limit for the Assistant Professor posts?
A
The maximum age limit for the Assistant Professor (Super-specialty) posts is 45 years. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories.
Q5What is the application fee for General/OBC candidates?
A
The application fee for General and OBC candidates is Rs. 600/-. SC/ST candidates also have to pay Rs. 600/-.
Q6What are the different specializations for the Assistant Professor posts?
A
The available specializations include Neurology, Thoracic Surgery (CTVS), and Cardiac Anaesthesia.
Q7What is the selection process for HPPSC Assistant Professor Recruitment?
A
The selection process includes an Objective Type Screening Test/Descriptive Subject Aptitude Test followed by a Personality Test.
Q8Where can I find the official notification for this recruitment?
A
The official notification PDF can be downloaded from the Important Links section of this article, or directly from the HPPSC official website.
Q9What is the salary offered for these Assistant Professor positions?
A
The salary varies by post, with some offering Rs.37400-67000 + (G.P. 8900) and others Rs.1,29,700/- (Cell-1, Level-30).
Q10Is there any age relaxation for reserved categories?
A
Yes, there is an age relaxation of five years for SC/ST/OBC/PWD/WFF of H.P. if the post is reserved for their category.
