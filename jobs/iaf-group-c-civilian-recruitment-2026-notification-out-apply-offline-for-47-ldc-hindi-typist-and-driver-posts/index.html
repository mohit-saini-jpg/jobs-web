Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How many vacancies have been announced for Indian Air Force Group C Civilian Recruitment 2026?

The Indian Air Force has announced vacancies for Group C Civilian posts through Advertisement No. 01/2026 across various Air Force Stations/Units. The total number of vacancies is distributed across multiple commands: HQ Maintenance Command (14 posts), HQ Training Command (5 posts), HQ Central Air Command (3 posts), HQ Western Air Command (6 posts), HQ Eastern Air Command (6 posts), HQ South Western Air Command (5 posts), HQ Southern Air Command (1 post), and Independent Units (7 posts). The posts include Lower Division Clerk (LDC), Hindi Typist, and Civilian Mechanical Transport Driver - Ordinary Grade (CMTD).

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for IAF Group C Civilian posts?

For Lower Division Clerk (LDC) and Hindi Typist positions, candidates must have passed 12th Class from a recognised Board with typing speed of 35 wpm in English or 30 wpm in Hindi on computer (corresponding to 10500 KDPH/9000 KDPH on an average of 5 key depressions for each word). For Civilian Mechanical Transport Driver - Ordinary Grade (CMTD), candidates must have Matriculation pass or equivalent qualification from a recognised board or university, hold a valid Civil Driving Licence for light and heavy vehicles, possess professional skill in driving and knowledge of motor mechanism, and have minimum two years' experience in driving motor vehicles.

Q3. What is the age limit for Indian Air Force Group C Civilian Recruitment 2026?

The age limit for all posts is 18 to 25 years, calculated as on the last date of receipt of application. Age relaxation is provided for reserved categories: OBC candidates get 3 years relaxation (up to 28 years), SC/ST candidates get 5 years relaxation (up to 30 years), Persons with Disabilities (PwBD) get 10 years relaxation with additional 5 years for SC/ST and 3 years for OBC categories. Ex-Servicemen get relaxation for full period of continuous service in Armed Forces (minimum 6 months), and Departmental Employees can apply up to 40 years (45 years for SC/ST).

Q4. Is there any application fee for IAF Group C Civilian Recruitment 2026?

No, there is no application fee mentioned in the official notification for Indian Air Force Group C Civilian Recruitment 2026. However, candidates must attach a self-addressed envelope with stamps of Rs. 10/- pasted along with the application. The application process is completely offline and candidates need to send their applications by post to the concerned Air Force Station/Unit.

Q5. What is the pay scale for IAF Group C Civilian posts?

All the Group C Civilian posts including Lower Division Clerk (LDC), Hindi Typist, and Civilian Mechanical Transport Driver - Ordinary Grade (CMTD) are placed at Level-2 as per Pay Matrix 7th CPC. This corresponds to a pay scale of Rs. 19,900 - Rs. 63,200 plus allowances as admissible. The exact salary depends on various allowances like Dearness Allowance (DA), House Rent Allowance (HRA), and other applicable benefits.

Q6. How to apply for Indian Air Force Group C Civilian Recruitment 2026?

The application process is strictly offline. Candidates must download and fill the Application Form as per the prescribed Proforma (Annexure-I) given in the advertisement, affix a recent passport-size self-attested photograph, attach self-attested copies of all required documents (educational qualification, age, caste certificate, PwBD certificate if applicable, experience certificate), attach a self-addressed envelope with Rs. 10/- stamps, and send the complete application by post to the concerned Air Force Station/Unit's postal address. The envelope must be superscribed clearly with "APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF AND CATEGORY ". Applications must reach within 30 days from the date of publication in Employment News (i.e., by approximately 01-06-2026).

Q7. What is the last date to apply for IAF Group C Civilian Recruitment 2026?

The last date for receipt of application is 30 days from the date of publication of this advertisement in Employment News/Rozgar Samachar. Since the advertisement was published in Employment News dated 2-8 May 2026, the last date is approximately 01-06-2026. Applications received prior to the date of publication or after the closing date will not be entertained and will be rejected. IAF will not be held responsible for any postal delay.

Q8. What is the selection process for Indian Air Force Group C Civilian Recruitment 2026?

The selection process involves multiple stages: Stage 1 - Scrutiny of Applications based on age limit, minimum qualification, documents and certificates; Stage 2 - Written Test with 100% weightage (question cum answer paper in English and Hindi) covering General Intelligence, English Language, Numerical Aptitude & General Awareness for LDC/Hindi Typist, and additionally Trade/Post related questions for CMTD; Stage 3 - Skill/Practical/Physical Test which is qualifying in nature for shortlisted candidates based on merit in written test. Eligible candidates will be issued call letters for the written test, and there will be simultaneous conduct of test for all categories.
