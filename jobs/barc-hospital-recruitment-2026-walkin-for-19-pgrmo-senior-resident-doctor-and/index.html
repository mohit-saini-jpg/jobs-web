BARC Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Post Graduate Resident Medical Officer/Senior Resident (PG) - All Specialties: MS/MD/DNB degree or Diploma from a recognized university in the concerned specialty. Diploma candidates must possess a minimum of 2 years of post-diploma experience. Degree/Diploma not awarded before December 2022 preferably. Non-DNB Junior/Senior Resident Doctor: Junior: MBBS with one year internship. Senior: MBBS plus PG Diploma or MBBS with one year JR experience preferably in the requisite discipline.
