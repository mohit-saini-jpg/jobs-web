HAL Executive Recruitment 2026 Service Agreement Bond

Selected candidates must execute a Service Bond to serve HAL for 3 years. The Bond amount is Rs. 5,00,000, limited to 6 months' Basic Pay plus Dearness Allowance, and the liability is non-depreciating during the bond tenure. Inducted executives will undergo two weeks of structured training at the HAL Management Academy/Division of posting, followed by one week of familiarization at the Division of posting.

HAL Executive Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

Selection will be through Personal Interview only. Applications are first scrutinized based on essential qualification, age, category, and PPQE; candidates must produce supporting documents at the document verification stage. Only Executive Cadre experience in the concerned PSU/Central/State/Private organization is considered eligible; contract experience engaged directly by PSU/Government bodies may be considered, but contract experience through third-party contractors will not be considered. Candidates will be shortlisted for interview in a ratio of 1:10 (maximum), prioritized by relevant experience, then qualifying exam marks, then age (elder/senior first) in case of ties. The Selection Committee will assess candidates on job knowledge, general knowledge related to the post, and personality, in addition to qualifications and PPQE.
