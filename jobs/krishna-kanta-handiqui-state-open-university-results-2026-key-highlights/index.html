How to Check KKHSOU Result 2026

Follow the steps below to check and download your KKHSOU MA Education 2nd Semester NEP 24 Result 2026:

  • Open the official result link https://kkhsou.ac.in/web/index_news_details.php?p=5476 in your browser.
  • On the result notice page, look for the attachment or link labelled with Result of the MA (Education) - II Semester (NEP 24).
  • Click on the attached PDF or result link to open the detailed result document.
  • If it is a PDF, press Ctrl+F and type your Enrollment Number / Roll Number / Name to quickly find your entry.
  • Check your paper wise marks, total marks, grade or SGPA (if shown) and overall result status (Pass/Fail/Back, etc.).
  • Download the PDF or take a printout of your result for future academic reference, re-registration and documentation.
