How to Download the Osmania University Time Table 2026

Follow these steps to check or download your official exam schedule:

  • Visit the official Osmania University examination branch website.
  • Look for the notification titled "Time Table" under the Examinations section for your specific course (BA/BSc/B.Com/BBA/BSW) and semester (Regular or Backlog).
  • Open the relevant PDF and verify your program, specialization, and batch (2025-26).
  • Cross-check your exam dates and timings, then download or print a copy for reference.

Important Instructions for Candidates

  • Candidates must write the exam only at their allotted examination centers.
  • Any omission or clash in the timetable should be reported immediately to the Controller of Examinations, O.U.
  • Candidates whose registration forms were rejected by the Examination Branch will not be considered eligible.
  • It is the candidate's responsibility to collect the correct question paper from the invigilator; answering the wrong paper may lead to cancellation of results.
  • Mobile phones, programmable calculators, and other electronic gadgets are strictly not allowed inside the examination hall.
