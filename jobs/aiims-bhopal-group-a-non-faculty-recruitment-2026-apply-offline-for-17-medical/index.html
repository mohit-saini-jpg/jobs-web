AIIMS Bhopal Recruitment 2026 - Eligibility Criteria

Medical Superintendent: Medical qualification as per IMC Act + PG (MD/MS/MHA or equivalent) + 10 years experience in Hospital Administration in a senior position, preferably in a 300-bed hospital. Superintending Engineer: Analogous post or Executive Engineer with 5 years in GP 7600 or 10 years in GP 6600. Chief Nursing Officer: Analogous post or 5 years in GP 6600 or 10 years in GP 5400 + M.Sc. Nursing from an INC recognized institute. Senior Analyst (System Analyst): Analogous post or 5 years in GP 6600 or above. Chief Medical Social Service Officer: Analogous post or 5 years in GP 5400 + M.A./MSW + 5 years experience in Medical social welfare. CSSD Officer: Analogous post or CSSD Supervisor with 5/7 years in GP 5400/4600. Hospital Architect: Degree in Architecture + 5 years experience (preferably from CPWD).
