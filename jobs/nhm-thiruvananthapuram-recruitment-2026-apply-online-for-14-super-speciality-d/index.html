NHM TVM Recruitment 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must fulfill all criteria as on 01.07.2026. Only Indian citizens with valid professional registrations are eligible. Preference will be given to candidates with relevant experience and additional qualifications. Detailed eligibility is as follows: Super Speciality Doctor (Professor of Neonatology): MBBS + MD in Paediatrics + DM/DNB in Neonatology + minimum 3 years teaching experience as Associate Professor in a NMC recognised Medical College. Specialist Doctor - Paediatrics: MBBS + PG Degree/Diploma in Paediatrics (or DNB) + Permanent Registration with TCMC/Kerala Medical Council. Preference for Developmental Paediatrics fellowship. Audiologist cum Speech Therapist / Audiologist (NPPCD): Bachelor of Audiology and Speech Language Pathology (BASLP) + Valid RCI Registration + 3 years post-qualification experience. Assistant Quality Assurance Officer: MHA (Master of Hospital Admin

NHM Recruitment 2026 Age Limit

Super Speciality & Specialist Doctors: Maximum 63 years Lady Health Visitor: Maximum 57 years Other posts: Maximum 40 years (as on 01.07.2026)

NHM TVM Recruitment 2026 Application Fee

Rs. 350/- per application (Demand Draft in favour of “DHFWS-OTHERS TRIVANDRUM” payable at Thiruvananthapuram). Non-refundable.

NHM Thiruvananthapuram Recruitment 2026 Selection Process

Selection is based on qualification, experience, and performance in Written Test / Interview. Shortlisted candidates will be informed via email. Candidates must produce original documents at the time of verification.

Important Instructions for NHM TVM Candidates

Posts are purely temporary/contractual. No claim for permanency. Candidates must be willing to work in any institution in Thiruvananthapuram District. Canvassing leads to disqualification. No TA/DA for interview. Check nhmtvm.com or arogyakeralam.gov.in regularly for updates. Incomplete or false information will lead to rejection.
