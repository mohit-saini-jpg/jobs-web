What Is the PDUSU Admit Card 2026 Release Update?

This section explains what the university has announced regarding the admit card release and which group of students it applies to, so candidates can quickly confirm whether the notice is relevant to them.

Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Shekhawati University, Sikar, has released the online admit card for the UG even semester (2nd, 4th, and 6th) examination 2026 on its official portal, shekhauniexam.in, ahead of the exam. Students can download the admit card from 4th July 2026 onwards by entering their Form Number or Roll Number. It is worth noting that the UG odd semester (1st, 3rd, and 5th) examination 2026 is scheduled to start from 8th July 2026 onwards as well, so students appearing for odd semesters should keep an eye on the same portal for updates specific to their semester.

How to Download PDUSU Shekhawati University Admit Card 2026

This section provides a simple step-by-step process to download the admit card so students can access their hall ticket without any confusion.

Follow the steps below to download your Shekhawati University UG semester (2nd, 4th, and 6th) examination 2026 admit card:

  • Visit the official PDUSU examination portal at shekhauniexam.in.
  • On the homepage, look under the "Online Panel" section and click on "Download Admit Card 2026."
  • You will be redirected to the admit card download page for the UG semester 2, 4, and 6 examination 2026.
  • Enter your Form Number or Roll Number correctly in the given field.
  • Click on the "Show Admit Card" button to fetch and view your admit card.
  • Download the admit card and take a clear printout for exam-day use.

The examinations will begin from 8 July 2026 and continue till 31 July 2026 (depending on the semester). Semester II exams will be held in the 07:00 AM - 10:00 AM session, Semester IV exams in the 03:00 PM - 06:00 PM session, and Semester VI exams in the 11:00 AM - 02:00 PM session.
