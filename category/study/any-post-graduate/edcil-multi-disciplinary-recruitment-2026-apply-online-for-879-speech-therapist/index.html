Qualification / Eligibility

Candidates must possess the prescribed qualifications from Government/RCI recognized institutions and fulfil the eligibility criteria as on 30 April 2026. | Occupational Therapist (125 Posts) | Bachelor’s or Master’s Degree in Occupational Therapy from a recognized institution. Valid registration/license to practise as an Occupational Therapist in India, as per applicable state regulations. Strong clinical reasoning and problem-solving abilities, excellent communication and interpersonal skills. Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team, with a compassionate, patient-centred approach. Experienced candidates in the relevant field will be given added advantage. | Physiotherapist (125 Posts) | Bachelor’s / Master’s Degree in Physiotherapy from a recognized institution. Valid professional registration/license. Strong clinical reasoning and communication skills, compassionate approach, ability to work independently and in a team. Experienced candidates in the relevant field will be given added advantage. | Psychologist (125 Posts) | M.A./M.Sc in Psychology from a recognized university (Clinical/Counselling/Developmental Psychology preferred). Experience in psychological assessments and behavioural therapies. Strong communication, counselling and interpersonal skills, ability to work independently and as part of a team. Experienced candidates in the relevant field will be given added advantage. | Speech Therapist (125 Posts) | Bachelor’s / Master’s Degree in Speech-Language Pathology or Audiology & Speech-Language Pathology from a recognized institution. Mandatory valid registration with the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI). Experience working with children with Autism and developmental disorders preferred. Strong assessment, communication and interpersonal skills. Proficiency in Telugu and English (knowledge of other local languages is an added advantage). Ability to work independently and as part of a team; experienced candidates will be given added advantage. | Clinical Psychologist (125 Posts) | Post Graduation in Clinical Psychology from an institution recognized by the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI). Mandatory valid RCI registration. Strong expertise in psychological assessments and behavioural therapies. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills; proficiency in Telugu is an added advantage. Ability to work independently and as part of a multidisciplinary team; experienced candidates will be given added advantage. | Special Educator (Secondary Level) – 125 Posts | The candidate must possess any one of the following RCI-recognised qualifications with valid CRR number: Bachelor’s Degree + B.Ed. (Special Education), OR Bachelor’s Degree + B.Ed. (General) with 1-year Diploma in Autism/Intellectual Disability, OR Bachelor’s Degree + B.Ed. (General) with D.Ed. in Autism/Intellectual Disability. Qualified AP TET (Paper-2B mandatory) / CTET in Special Education. Experienced candidates in the relevant field will be given added advantage. | Special Educator (Primary Level) – 125 Posts | Must have passed Intermediate (10+2) from a recognized Board. Must possess a two-year Diploma in Special Education (Autism/Intellectual Disability) recognized by RCI with valid CRR number. Qualified AP TET (Paper-1B mandatory) / CTET in Special Education. Experienced candidates in the relevant field will be given added advantage. | PMU Consultant (4 Posts) | Ph.D./M.Phil in Clinical Psychology/Psychology/Special Education. Desirable: Ph.D in the field of rehabilitation of Persons with Disabilities. Experienced candidates in the relevant field will be given added advantage. Expertise in report writing and strong communication skills is mandatory.

Important Instructions

Only online applications will be accepted; only Indian Nationals are eligible. The workplace will be Autism Centres across various Municipalities of Andhra Pradesh as approved by SPD (Inclusive Education–PM SHRI, Andhra Pradesh). The competent authority reserves the right to amend, cancel or change the advertisement wholly or partly without assigning any reason. The engagement will not confer any right for regular engagement, permanent absorption or continued contractual engagement. Qualifications must be from Government recognized institutions only. Candidates should have strong verbal and written communication skills and excellent working knowledge of computers (MS Word, MS Excel, MS PowerPoint etc.) and presentation skills. Selection will be based on academic/professional qualifications and may include an interview on a prescribed date and time as decided by the competent authority. A waiting list of candidates will be prepared for possible future vacancies or non-joining of selected candidates. Original documents to prove qualifications, age, experience, etc., must be produced at the time of interview. The cut-off date for age and experience is 30 April 2026; eligibility must be satisfied as on this date. Candidates must upload all required documents (educational certificates, resume, experience certificates etc.) in PDF format only; failure will result in disqualification/rejection. Screening and shortlisting will strictly be based on details and documents provided in the online application; incomplete, false or misrepresented information will lead to disqualification. Any false declaration or suppression of information detected at any stage will lead to cancellation of candidature/termination of services and may attract disciplinary/legal action. Interview letters and related communication will be sent only to shortlisted candidates via e-mail as provided in the online application; candidates must keep their email active. Merely fulfilling eligibility criteria does not entitle a candidate to be called for interview. Engagement is purely contractual; extension of contract depends on the needs of Inclusive Education–PM SHRI, Andhra Pradesh and the performance of the professional. The last date for receipt of online applications is 04 June 2026. No application fee is required for any of the posts.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is EdCIL Multidisciplinary Staff 2026 recruitment about?
A
EdCIL Multidisciplinary Staff 2026 recruitment is a contractual hiring drive for 875 professionals like Occupational Therapists, Physiotherapists, Psychologists, Speech Therapists, Clinical Psychologists, Special Educators and 4 PMU Consultants to work in Autism Centres across Andhra Pradesh under the Inclusive Education – PM SHRI scheme. The roles focus on assessment, therapy, intervention and academic support for children with Autism and related developmental needs in government-supported centres.
Q2Q2. Is the EdCIL Multidisciplinary Staff 2026 job permanent?
A
No, all posts under this notification are purely contractual. The engagement does not give any right to regular government service, permanent absorption in EdCIL, or automatic extension beyond the project period. Continuation of the contract generally depends on project funding, the requirement of the Autism Centres and the individual’s performance review.
Q3Q3. Who is eligible to apply for EdCIL Multidisciplinary Staff 2026 posts?
A
Eligibility depends on the post, but in general you must: Be an Indian citizen. Have the specific degree/diploma prescribed for the post (e.g., Occupational Therapy, Physiotherapy, Psychology, Speech-Language Pathology, RCI-recognised Special Education, etc.) from a recognised institution. Meet the upper age limit of 45 years as on 30 April 2026 and possess relevant experience where required. Candidates with valid registrations (RCI/state councils) and prior work with children with disabilities will have an advantage during shortlisting.
Q4Q4. Is there any application fee for EdCIL Multidisciplinary Staff 2026?
A
No, there is no application fee for any of the posts under this recruitment. Candidates can fill and submit the online form free of cost on the official EdCIL recruitment portal. However, you must ensure all details and documents are correct because incomplete or incorrect applications can still be rejected even without a fee.
Q5Q5. What is the age limit and is there any age relaxation?
A
For all posts – Occupational Therapist, Physiotherapist, Psychologist, Speech Therapist, Clinical Psychologist, Special Educator (Primary/Secondary), and PMU Consultant – the maximum age is 45 years as on 30 April 2026. The notification is contractual (not a standard central government cadre recruitment), so category-based relaxations like regular government jobs may not automatically apply unless expressly mentioned in the detailed advertisement.
Q6Q6. Where will the posting location be for selected candidates?
A
Selected candidates will be posted in Autism Centres located across various Municipalities in Andhra Pradesh under Inclusive Education – PM SHRI. Exact place of posting is generally decided by the project authorities based on centre-wise requirements, so you should be prepared for deployment in any listed municipal location within the state rather than expecting a specific city of your choice.
Q7Q7. How will candidates be selected for EdCIL Multidisciplinary Staff 2026?
A
The selection process is typically based on: Shortlisting using academic qualifications, relevant degrees, registrations and work experience mentioned in the application; and An interview (online or offline) scheduled by EdCIL/TSG, where communication skills, domain knowledge and suitability for working in Autism Centres are assessed. Merely fulfilling eligibility does not guarantee an interview call; only shortlisted candidates are contacted through email.
Q8Q8. Can freshers apply for EdCIL Multidisciplinary Staff 2026 posts?
A
Yes, freshers who possess the required degrees and registrations as on 30 April 2026 can apply, especially for posts like Occupational Therapist, Physiotherapist, Psychologist, Speech Therapist and Special Educator. However, candidates with hands-on experience in disability rehabilitation, school-based inclusion, or Autism-specific interventions are likely to be preferred, since the project demands practical skills with children and families.
