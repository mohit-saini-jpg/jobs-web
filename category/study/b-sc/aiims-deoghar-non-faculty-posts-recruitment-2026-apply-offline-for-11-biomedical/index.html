How to Apply

  • 1The application process for AIIMS Deoghar Non-Faculty contractual posts is completely offline.
  • 2Visit the official website of AIIMS Deoghar and download the prescribed application form from the recruitment section.
  • 3Fill the application form carefully in all respects and affix a recent passport size colour photograph in the given space.
  • 4Attach self-attested photocopies of date of birth certificate, degree certificates, experience certificates, caste certificate (if applicable), No Objection Certificate (if working in Govt/Autonomous body) etc. in the order mentioned in the advertisement.
  • 5Draw a Demand Draft of the applicable application fee in favour of “AIIMS Deoghar” payable at Deoghar and attach the original DD with the application.
  • 6Send the complete application form along with all enclosures and Demand Draft by post to the following address: Recruitment Cell, All India Institute of Medical Sciences, Deoghar, At - Devipur Campus, Ramsagar, Deoghar - 814117, Jharkhand.
  • 7Ensure that the application reaches the above address within 45 days from the publication of the advertisement in Employment News.
  • 8Shortlisted candidates will be informed about the schedule of document verification and interview via email. No TA/DA will be paid for appearing in the interview/document verification.

FAQs

Q1. For how long will the AIIMS Deoghar Non-Faculty posts be filled on contract?
These posts are purely on contract for 11 months or until regular appointments against the same posts are made, whichever is earlier.
Q2. What is the mode of application for AIIMS Deoghar Non-Faculty contractual posts?
The application has to be submitted in offline mode only through the prescribed application form and sent by post to AIIMS Deoghar.
