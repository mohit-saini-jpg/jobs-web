Qualification / Eligibility

Candidates must have passed the qualifying examination and possess Original/Provisional Certificate to be eligible. The gap between the date of contract after joining apprenticeship and the date of passing the qualification should not be more than 5 years for all categories. Candidates who have already undergone Apprenticeship Training or who are undergoing similar apprenticeship training or are registered for Apprenticeship Training in the respective qualification under the Apprentices Act 1961 elsewhere are not eligible. Candidates who have already joined/are pursuing/are about to join any regular courses for higher education during the stipulated Training Period need not apply. Important Instructions

Selection is purely merit-based on CGPA/Percentage of marks in Diploma/Engineering Degree/Graduation in General Stream; there will be no written test or interview. Pre-registration on the NATS portal (https://nats.education.gov.in) is mandatory before appearing for the walk-in interview. Only candidates who have passed the qualifying examination and possess Original/Provisional Certificate are eligible. The gap between the date of contract after joining apprenticeship and the date of passing the qualification should not be more than 5 years for all categories. Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing Apprenticeship Training or are registered for Apprenticeship Training under the Apprentices Act 1961 elsewhere are not eligible. Candidates who have already joined/are pursuing/are about to join any regular courses for higher education during the stipulated Training Period need not apply. OBC candidates must produce a valid Non-Creamy Layer certificate. Merit list of selected candidates will be published on www.hal-india.co.in on 30th June 2026. Date of joining/reporting for the Apprenticeship is 27th July 2026; failure to report by the scheduled date will result in forfeiture of candidature. Submission of false/incorrect information or suppression of any facts at any stage will result in immediate cancellation/termination of candidature. Canvassing in any form shall lead to disqualification of the candidature. HAL reserves the right to alter the allocation of quota for trades at any time and to cancel the advertisement/selection process; HAL Management's decision regarding selection will be final. Candidates must make their own arrangements for accommodation and travelling for the walk-in process and joining.
