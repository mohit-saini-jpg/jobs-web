Qualification / Eligibility

Only candidates who have completed their qualifying examination as regular candidates and acquired the requisite qualification on or after the year 2022 are eligible. Qualifying qualification must be from a University in India or a State Board recognized by the competent authority. Candidates currently undergoing apprenticeship training in Govt./Public/Private Sector for one year or more as on the date of joining are not eligible. Combining apprenticeship with other institutions is not permitted. Selected candidates must execute a contract of Apprenticeship Training as per the Apprentices Act, 1961 and Amendment Act of 1973. Apprentices will have no claim for regular employment in NIT Calicut after completion of training.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date to apply for NIT Calicut Apprentice Trainee Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date to submit the online application for NIT Calicut Apprentice Trainee Recruitment 2026 is 12 June 2026. Candidates must complete the application process before the deadline to be considered for the apprenticeship positions.
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are available in NIT Calicut Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
NIT Calicut has announced a total of 79 Apprentice Trainee vacancies, including 44 Graduate Apprentice posts and 35 Diploma Apprentice posts across various disciplines.
Q3Q3. What is the stipend offered for NIT Calicut Apprentice Trainees?
A
Selected Graduate Apprentice Trainees will receive a stipend of ₹15,000 per month, while Diploma Apprentice Trainees will receive ₹12,500 per month during the one-year apprenticeship training period.
Q4Q4. What is the duration of the NIT Calicut Apprenticeship Training?
A
The apprenticeship training at NIT Calicut will be conducted for a period of one year under the Apprentices Act, 1961. The engagement is purely temporary and does not guarantee future employment.
Q5Q5. Who is eligible to apply for NIT Calicut Apprentice Trainee Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates who completed their Degree or Diploma as regular students and obtained the qualification in 2022 or later from a recognized institution are eligible to apply, subject to the post-specific requirements.
Q6Q6. What is the age limit for NIT Calicut Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
The upper age limit is 28 years for Graduate Apprentices (UR category) and 30 years for Diploma Apprentices (UR category). Age relaxation is available for OBC, SC, and ST candidates as per the notification.
Q7Q7. What is the selection process for NIT Calicut Apprentice Trainee Posts?
A
The selection process includes application screening, document verification, written test (if required), and personal interview. Final selection will be based on academic merit, eligibility, and performance during the evaluation process.
Q8Q8. Can apprentices claim permanent employment at NIT Calicut after training?
A
No. Candidates selected for apprenticeship training will not have any claim for regular or permanent employment at NIT Calicut after the completion of the apprenticeship period.
