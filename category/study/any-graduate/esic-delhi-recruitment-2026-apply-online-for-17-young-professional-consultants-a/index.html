Consultant Grade-1 (Information & Communication Technology): Bachelors' or post graduate degree in Computer Science/IT/Statistics/Mathematics with 3-8 years of experience. Consultant Grade-1 (Actuarial): Cleared the following exams conducted by IAI: CS1, CS2, CM1, CB1 with minimum 3 years of experience. Consultant Grade-1 Public Relations (Social Media): Any Graduate with 3 to 8 years of experience. Consultant Grade-2 (ICT-Network and Infrastructure): Bachelors' or Post Graduate degree in Computer Science/Information technology with 8-15 years of experience. Consultant Grade-2 (Medical Service): Masters in Hospital Administration with 8 -15 years of experience. Senior Consultant (ICT -Management and Technology Profile): Bachelors' or Post Graduate degree in Computer Science/IT / Software Engineering with MBA (regular) with 15 years and above experience.
