Job Overview

The latest CSIR-Centre for Cellular and Molecular Biology (CSIR-CCMB) Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Technical Officer, Technician (1)/Gr.II(1), and Driver posts! Published on 28 April 2026 at www.ccmb.res.in, this new recruitment drive offers 28 openings for eligible candidates. Applicants within the age group of 27 to 30 years and possessing a qualification of M.Sc., SSC/10th with ITI, or valid driving license (as per the post) can apply online from 28 April 2026 to 05 June 2026. Technician (1) (TE-01, TE-02, TE-03): SSC/10th Standard with Science subjects + ITI Certificate or 2 years Apprentice Training or 3 years experience in relevant trade (Laboratory Assistant, Computer Operator, Food Production etc.). Driver: 10th Standard + Valid Driving Licence for LMV & HMV + 3 years driving experience + Knowledge of motor mechanism.

How to Apply

  • 1Eligible candidates are required to apply ONLINE ONLY by visiting CSIR-CCMB website followed by sending hard copy of online application duly signed along with self-attested copies of mark sheets, certificates, etc. No other mode of application will be entertained.
  • 2ONLINE APPLICATIONS WITHOUT RECEIPT OF HARD COPY OF THE SAME WILL BE SUMMARILY REJECTED.
  • 3Only one application per candidate will be accepted for each postcode. If a candidate submits multiple online applications for the same postcode using different email addresses, only the most recent completed application will be considered.
  • 4The Online application will be available on CSIR-CCMB website https://www.ccmb.res.in [opens on 28.04.2026 from 11:00 AM and closes on 05.06.2026 at 11:59 PM]
  • 5Candidates are advised to go through the instructions (available on CSIR-CCMB recruitment portal) for filling up of online applications carefully.
  • 6Interested candidates are advised to apply in time to avoid last minute rush.
  • 7For submission of online applications, the candidates are required to pay a nonrefundable fee of Rs. 500/- (Rupee FIVE Hundred only) through State Bank Collect Click here only. The transaction number/UTR number generated after successful payment of fee is required to be mentioned in the online application. Further, the candidates are required to upload the e-receipt of SB Collect (wherever applicable) and preserve the same for future communication.
  • 8Applications without the prescribed fee will not be considered and summarily rejected. No representation against such rejection would be entertained.
  • 9In case of universities/institute awarding CGPA/SGPA/OGPA/DGPA/CPI grades etc., candidates are requested to convert the same into percentage based on the formula as per their university/institute and mention the percentage so arrived at the appropriate place inthe online application form, while filling the same.
  • 10After completely filling the online application form candidate can submit and take printout of the online generated filled application and note down Application Number for future communication. The candidate must sign the computer generated application while sending the hardcopy of application.
  • 11The computer generated online application duly signed by the candidate and accompanied by SELF-ATTESTED copies of the certificates such as mark sheets, testimonials in support of age, education qualifications, experience and caste certificate, if applicable along with one recent passport size self-signed photograph affixed should be sent by post in an envelope super scribed “APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF ____________ (Post Code: ________)” so as to reach the following address on or before 15.06.2026 (06:00 PM) (By hand submission of hard copy will not be accepted).
  • 12The Section Officer (Recruitment Section), CSIR-Centre for Cellular and Molecular Biology, Uppal Road, Habsiguda, Hyderabad – 500007, Telangana
