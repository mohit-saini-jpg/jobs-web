Qualification / Eligibility

Medical Officer (Homoeopathy): BHMS from an Institution recognized under HCC Act. 1973/ Central Council of Homoeopathy including compulsory internship of 1 (one) year duration after passing the final degree examination. The candidate must have a valid Registration of the Central or a State Council. Desirable: Knowledge of Bengali/ Kokborok. Medical Officer (Ayurveda): BAMS or its equivalent Degree from a recognized Ayurvedic Medical Institution and completion of 1 (one) year compulsory internship training after passing of B.A.M.S or its equivalent. The candidate must have a valid Registration of the Central or a State Council. Desirable: Knowledge of Bengali/ Kokborok. Community Health Officer, Ayush: Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine & Surgery (BAMS) from an Ayurvedic Medical Institution recognized by the Central Council of Indian Medicine (CCIM)/NCISM OR Bachelor of Homoeopathic Medicine & Surgery (BHMS) from a Homoeopathic Medical Institution recognized by the Central Council of Homoeopathy (CCH)/ NCH.
