Mizoram Public Service Commission (Mizoram PSC) Veterinary Assistant Surgeon Notification 2026 has been released on 8 May 2026 on the official website. FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Mizoram PSC VAS Recruitment 2026?

Q2. What is the minimum and maximum age limit for the VAS post?
The minimum age requirement is 21 years, and the maximum age limit is 35 years as of 1st January 2026. Age relaxation is provided for certain categories as per government rules.
Q3. What are the educational qualifications required for the Veterinary Assistant Surgeon post?
Candidates must possess a Bachelor of Veterinary Science & Animal Husbandry Degree from a recognized university, along with valid registration and computer and Mizo language proficiency.
Q4. How many vacancies are available for the Veterinary Assistant Surgeon post?
There is a total of 1 vacancy for the Veterinary Assistant Surgeon (VAS) position announced in this recruitment drive by the Mizoram Public Service Commission.
Q5. What is the application fee for the Mizoram PSC VAS Recruitment 2026?
As per the notification, there is no application fee for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwBD) candidates. Other categories' fee details should be checked in the official notification.
Q6. What is the salary structure for the Veterinary Assistant Surgeon post?
Selected candidates will be appointed at Level-10 in the Pay Matrix, which indicates a competitive salary as per the government pay scales.
Q7. What is the selection process for this recruitment?
The selection process for the Veterinary Assistant Surgeon (VAS) post will be based on a Direct Recruitment Examination conducted by the Mizoram Public Service Commission.
Q8. Is there any age relaxation for reserved categories?
Yes, the upper age limit is relaxable up to 5 years for candidates belonging to Scheduled Tribe (ST) and Scheduled Caste (SC) categories.
