Job Overview

Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi) Jr. Research Fellow Notification 2026 has been released on the official website of Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. A total of 1 vacancy has been announced through the IIT Delhi Jr. Research Fellow Recruitment 2026. Candidates who have completed 1st class B.Tech in Civil Engineering and 1st class M.Tech in Geotechnical Engineering along with NET/GATE qualification are eligible to apply for these Jr. Research Fellow vacancies. Qualification / Eligibility

1st class B.Tech in Civil Engineering and 1st class M.Tech in Geotechnical Engineering. Experience in conducting laboratory and model-scale experiments, specifically focusing on the application of acoustic emissions in geotechnical engineering for early prediction of failures. NET/GATE qualified. The requirement of NET/GATE examination may be relaxed for candidates graduated from Centrally Funded Technical Institutes (CFTIs) with CGPA more than 8.000 (80% aggregate marks).
