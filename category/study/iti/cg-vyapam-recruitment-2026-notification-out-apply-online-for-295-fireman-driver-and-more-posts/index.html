FAQs

Q1. What is CG Vyapam Fireman & Fire Services Recruitment 2026 (NSF26)?
CG Vyapam Fireman & Fire Services Recruitment 2026 (NSF26) is the written examination stage conducted by Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board, Raipur, for filling around 295 posts such as Fireman, Vehicle Driver, Station Officer and other posts under Fire & Emergency Services and SDRF for candidates who have already qualified the Physical Efficiency Test.
Q2. How many vacancies are there in CG Vyapam Fireman & Fire Services Recruitment 2026?
The notification mentions approximately 295 vacancies, including about 21 Station Officer (Sub‑Inspector), 14 Vehicle Driver, 86 Vehicle Driver‑cum‑Operator, 117 Fireman, 32 Store Keeper, 2 Mechanic, 19 Watch Room Operator and 4 Wireless Operator (contract) posts.
Q3. What are the application dates for CG Vyapam Fireman Written Exam 2026?
The online application window for the NSF26 written examination opens on 08 May 2026 and closes on 05 June 2026 up to 05:00 PM, and candidates must submit their forms within this period on the CG Vyapam portal.
Q4. When will the CG Vyapam Fireman 2026 written exam be conducted?
The CG Vyapam Fireman & Fire Services written examination (NSF26) is tentatively scheduled on 19 July 2026 (Sunday) from 10:00 AM to 12:15 PM, and the exam will be held at Raipur examination centre; admit cards are expected to be available from 13 July 2026.
Q5. Who is eligible to apply for CG Vyapam Fireman NSF26 written exam?
Only local residents of Chhattisgarh who have already qualified the Physical Efficiency Test conducted by the Fire & Emergency Services/SDRF for these posts, and who meet the required educational qualification and age criteria, are eligible to apply online for the NSF26 written examination.
Q6. What is the age limit for CG Vyapam Fireman Recruitment 2026?
Candidates must be between 18 and 28 years of age as on 01 January 2025, with relaxations for SC, ST and OBC candidates up to 5 years and additional relaxations for women, Ex‑servicemen, government employees and Nagar Sainiks as per Chhattisgarh government rules.
Q7. What is the exam pattern for CG Vyapam Fireman NSF26 written test?
The written exam comprises 100 objective multiple‑choice questions for 100 marks, with a duration of 2 hours 15 minutes, covering subject‑specific knowledge along with General Knowledge, Reasoning and Analytical Ability, and there is no negative marking.
Q8. What is the application fee for CG Vyapam Fireman Written Exam 2026?
The application fee is Rs. 350 for General category, Rs. 250 for OBC and Rs. 200 for SC/ST/PwD candidates; for eligible local residents of Chhattisgarh, the examination fee component is refundable after the declaration of result as per state government orders.
