Important Instructions

Online applications only — no manual or postal applications will be accepted. Candidates must ensure they fulfill all eligibility conditions (educational qualification, age, domicile, employment registration) before applying. Admission at all stages is provisional and candidature can be cancelled even after final selection if found ineligible. Candidates must not fill more than one online application form for the same post. If multiple applications are submitted, all will be cancelled. Candidates can cancel and re-apply before the deadline, but fee paid for a cancelled application will not be refunded or adjusted. All information filled in the online application is treated as final and confirmed. Incorrect or false information / fake certificates will result in debarment from all UKSSSC examinations and FIR under applicable laws. Signatures on the application, attendance sheet, document verification, and other correspondence must be identical at all places — any discrepancy may result in cancellation of candidature. Each candidate must provide their own separate mobile number and email ID — not shared with other family members. Admit cards will not be sent by post — they must be downloaded from the UKSSSC website. Candidates must regularly check the website throughout the recruitment process. Mobile phones, calculators, pagers, scanners, or any electronic communication devices are strictly prohibited inside the examination hall. Violation will attract debarment from future UKSSSC exams and further action. Examination centre preferences are taken into account, but the Commission's decision on exam centre allotment is final and binding. Candidates absent during document verification will have their candidature cancelled without any notice. All Uttarakhand State Agitation Activist / dependant certificates must be claimed in the online application form to avail reservation; no benefit is available if not claimed at application stage. No TA/DA is payable to any candidate for attending written examination or document verification. Any person with more than one living spouse, or a woman who has married a person with a living spouse, is not eligible for appointment. All decisions of the Commission regarding eligibility, selection, and other matters are final and binding. The Commission does not provide individual counselling on eligibility.
