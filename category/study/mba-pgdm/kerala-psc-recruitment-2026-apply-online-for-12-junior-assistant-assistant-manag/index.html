Job Overview

Kerala Public Service Commission (Kerala PSC) has released the official Cine Assistant, Junior Assistant, Assistant Manager Notification 2026 on 30 April 2026 at the official website. A total of 12 vacancies have been announced under the Kerala PSC Various Posts Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged between 18 to 43 years with qualifications such as SSLC or equivalent, Degree, DCA/Diploma in Computer Application, MBA, PGDCA, and relevant experience can apply online from 30 April 2026 to 03 June 2026. Qualification / Eligibility

Cine Assistant: SSLC or equivalent + Two years experience in the Camera section of a reputed film studio. Junior Assistant: Degree in any discipline + DCA/Diploma in Computer Application (minimum 6 months) from Govt. recognised institute. Assistant Manager: Degree in any discipline + MBA (regular course) + PGDCA (regular course) + 2 years experience in supervisory cadre after acquiring MBA.
