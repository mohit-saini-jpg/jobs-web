The Sri Venkateswara Institute of Medical Sciences (SVIMS Tirupati) has officially announced the Various Posts under MRC Project Recruitment 2026 with 11 openings. Qualification / Eligibility

Medical Officer: MBBS Degree examination or its equivalent (MCI Act. 1956 as amended) from a College recognized by the NMC or BDS Degree from a College recognized by DCI Staff Nurse: B.Sc. Nursing from a recognized university and registered with Andhra Pradesh Nursing Council or Intermediate with General Nursing Course of three years and Midwifery training of six months with three years experience in Medical College/tertiary care hospital and registered with Andhra Pradesh Nursing Council ANM: SSC or equivalent and ANM or MPHW training course recognized by Government of Andhra Pradesh with two years experience as ANM or equivalent in Government or corporate hospital and registered with Andhra Pradesh Nursing Council/Midwifery Council Mammography Technician: B.Sc. degree with Diploma in Medical Imaging Technology or PG Diploma in Radiography & Imaging Technology or B.Sc. Radiography and Imaging Technology (RIT) or Intermediate with science group and Certificate course in Radiographic Assistant (CRA) or Diploma in Medical Imaging Technology (DMIT) with five years experience as Radiographer and registered with Andhra Pradesh paramedical board (APPMB) Cardiovascular Technician Gr. II: B.Sc. from a recognized university with P.G. Diploma of two years duration in Cardio Vascular Technology or B.Sc. in Cardio-Vascular Technology from a University recognized by UGC with one year experience in the field from any reputed corporate hospital and registered with Andhra Pradesh paramedical board (APPMB) Data Entry Operator: Bachelor’s degree of a recognized University with knowledge in computer applications and passed typewriting higher grade English Public Relations Officer: Degree from a recognized University and Post Graduate Diploma in Public Relations or equivalent from a recognized University or institution with two years experience as Public Relations Officer in a corporate hospital or a University or a Medical College or a public sector undertaking Medico social Worker: Post graduate degree in Social work with medical and psychiatric specialization with one year experience in social work in a tertiary hospital Electrician: S.S.C examination or equivalent and D.E.E. or Certificate in I.T.I. Electrical Trade awarded by the Department of Technical Education, Government of Andhra Pradesh or equivalent with three years experience in any reputed organization Drivers: SSC examination or equivalent and current valid driving license for heavy motor vehicles with normal vision without glasses and colour vision normal with 2 years experience as a Driver in a Govt. organization or public sector organization or Military service or in any reputed organization and not less than 5 ft. 4 inch in height
