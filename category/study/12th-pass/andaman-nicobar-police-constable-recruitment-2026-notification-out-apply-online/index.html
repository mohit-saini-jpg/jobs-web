Job Overview

Andaman & Nicobar Police has released the official Group-B (Non-Gazetted) & Group-C Recruitment Notification 2026 in April 2026 at its official website. A total of 495 (tentative) vacancies have been announced under the A&N Police Group-B (NG) & Group-C Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged between 18 to 30/33 years (varies by post) with qualifications ranging from 10th, 12th, to Graduation can apply online from 27 April 2026 to 26 May 2026. The official notification PDF and online application link are available below.

Application Fee

Candidates (except Female/ST candidates who are exempted from payment of fee) are required to pay a fee of Rs. 100/- (Rupees one hundred only) No Fee for ST/Women candidates of any community. No "fee exemption" is available to Gen/OBC/EWS male candidates and they are required to pay the full prescribed fee. After completing payment: candidate has to upload the transaction slip in the portal in image format.
