Age Limit

DetailsGeneral Age Limit: Minimum 20 years and maximum 30 years as on the closing date of receipt of application.

Qualification / Eligibility

Candidates must fulfill the minimum qualifications specified for the Telecom stream: | Core Qualification (Telecom Stream): Bachelor of Engineering Degree or equivalent from a Central Govt./State Govt. recognized Institution/University in any of the following disciplines: Telecommunications, Electronics, Radio, Computer, Electrical, Information Technology (IT), or Instrumentation. Alternative Qualification: M.Sc. (Electronics) or M.Sc. (Computer Science). Higher Qualifications: Candidates possessing M.Tech. in the prescribed disciplines are also eligible to appear in the Direct Recruitment examination. Vision Requirement: The applicant must be capable of distinguishing principal colours and should have binocular vision. Nationality: Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply.
