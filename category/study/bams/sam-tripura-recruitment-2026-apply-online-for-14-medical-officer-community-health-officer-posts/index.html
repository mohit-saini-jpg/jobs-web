State AYUSH Mission Tripura (SAM Tripura) has released the official Medical Officer / Community Health Officer Notification 2026 on 15 May 2026 at the official website. A total of 14 vacancies have been announced under the SAM Tripura Medical Officer / Community Health Officer Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged up to 40 years with a qualification of BHMS, BAMS or equivalent can apply via email from 22 May 2026 to 02 June 2026. The official notification PDF and application details are available below.

Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationBHMS, BAMS or equivalent
DetailsMedical Officer (Homoeopathy): BHMS from an Institution recognized under HCC Act. 1973/ Central Council of Homoeopathy including compulsory internship of 1 (one) year duration after passing the final degree examination. The candidate must have a valid Registration of the Central or a State Council. Desirable: Knowledge of Bengali/ Kokborok. Medical Officer (Ayurveda): BAMS or its equivalent Degree from a recognized Ayurvedic Medical Institution and completion of 1 (one) year compulsory internship training after passing of B.A.M.S or its equivalent. The candidate must have a valid Registration of the Central or a State Council. Desirable: Knowledge of Bengali/ Kokborok. Community Health Officer, Ayush: Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine & Surgery (BAMS) from an Ayurvedic Medical Institution recognized by the Central Council of Indian Medicine (CCIM)/NCISM OR Bachelor of Homoeopathic Medicine & Surgery (BHMS) from a Homoeopathic Medical Institution recognized by the Central Council of Homoeopathy (CCH)/ NCH.

FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the SAM Tripura Recruitment 2026?
The last date to submit your application via email for the SAM Tripura Recruitment 2026 is June 2, 2026. Ensure your application reaches the concerned authority before this deadline.
Q2. How many vacancies are available for Medical Officer / Community Health Officer posts?
There are a total of 14 vacancies announced for the Medical Officer / Community Health Officer positions under the State AYUSH Mission Tripura Recruitment 2026.
Q3. What is the educational qualification required for these posts?
Candidates need to possess a BHMS or BAMS degree from a recognized institution, along with a compulsory internship and valid registration. Specific details vary slightly per post.
Q4. What is the age limit for applicants?
The maximum age limit for all posts is 40 years as of December 31, 2025. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST/PH candidates.
Q5. What is the salary offered for the Medical Officer posts?
Medical Officers (Homoeopathy and Ayurveda) will receive a salary of Rs. 35,000 per month. Community Health Officers will get Rs. 25,000 plus performance incentives.
Q6. Is there an application fee for this recruitment?
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive by State AYUSH Mission Tripura.
Q7. How will candidates be selected for the advertised posts?
The selection process involves an Academic Performance Index (API) of 50 marks and a Viva-voce/Personality Test of 50 marks, followed by merit list preparation and document verification.
Q8. What is the application mode for SAM Tripura Recruitment 2026?
Applications for this recruitment must be submitted via email. Candidates are advised to carefully follow the instructions provided in the official notification.
Q9. Are there any desirable qualifications mentioned for the Medical Officer posts?
Yes, for Medical Officer (Homoeopathy) and Medical Officer (Ayurveda) posts, knowledge of Bengali/Kokborok is considered a desirable qualification.
Q10. Where can I find the official notification PDF?
The official notification PDF link is provided in the "Important Links" section of this article. It is highly recommended to download and read it thoroughly before applying.
