Job Overview

The Jharkhand Home Guard Vahini is a voluntary organisation functioning under the Home Department of Jharkhand Government. Home Guards are voluntary members who are deployed on duty as per necessity and paid allowances at the rate determined by the Government for the number of working days of deployment. This is not a permanent or salaried government job. The recruitment drive for Ramgarh district covers both Rural Home Guards (Gramin Grih Rakshak) across 6 blocks and Urban Home Guards (Shahri Grih Rakshak) across 2 urban areas (Technical and Non-Technical). The enrollment is done for a period of 4 years as per the Jharkhand Home Guard (Volunteer) Rules, 2014. Online applications are to be submitted at https://recruitment.jharkhand.gov.in, and the printed application along with documents must be submitted at the District Commandant Office, Ramgarh.

The application fee for Jharkhand Home Guard Ramgarh Recruitment 2026 is ₹100/- (One Hundred Rupees) for all categories. The fee must be paid compulsorily through the link provided on the official website https://recruitment.jharkhand.gov.in. No other mode of payment is accepted. Candidates must ensure they pay the application fee while submitting the online application. The application fee payment is mandatory to complete the application process.

Salary & Pay Scale

DetailsHome Guards (Grih Rakshak) are voluntary members of the Jharkhand Home Guard Vahini and are not entitled to a regular salary. They are paid allowances at the rate determined by the Government of Jharkhand for the number of working days on which they are deployed on duty as per requirement. | This is not a salaried government job or a means of regular livelihood. The number of days of deployment and the applicable allowance rate are determined by the organisational requirement and the Home Department of Jharkhand Government from time to time. Candidates must clearly understand this voluntary nature of service before applying.

Important Instructions

Home Guard service is voluntary and not a salaried government job. Enrollment is for 4 years as per Jharkhand Home Guard (Volunteer) Rules, 2014. 50% of posts are reserved for women in both Rural and Urban categories. 5% of women-reserved posts are for eligible divorced/deserted/widowed women candidates. 50% of Urban posts are for technically skilled candidates. Government servants can be enrolled up to 10% of total Urban Home Guard vacancies. One application per candidate only; multiple applications will be automatically cancelled. Any incorrect information in the online application will result in automatic cancellation. Physical/Written Test date and venue will be communicated through daily newspapers. SC/ST candidates are entitled to relaxation in physical measurements with valid caste certificate. All disputes regarding enrollment are governed by the Jharkhand Home Guard (Volunteer) Rules, 2014.

FAQs

Q1Q1. How many vacancies are there in Jharkhand Home Guard Ramgarh Recruitment 2026 and how are they divided?
A
Jharkhand Home Guard Vahini, Ramgarh has released 284 Home Guard (Grih Rakshak) vacancies, comprising 213 Rural Home Guard posts across 6 blocks and 71 Urban Home Guard posts (Technical & Non‑Technical) across 2 urban areas, with 50% reservation for women in both Rural and Urban categories.
Q2Q2. What are the online application dates and printout submission dates for Ramgarh Home Guard 2026?
A
The online application window is from 07 May 2026 to 04 June 2026 on the official recruitment portal recruitment.jharkhand.gov.in. After online submission, candidates must submit the printout with documents at the District Commandant Office, Ramgarh between 08 May 2026 and 05 June 2026; applications without printout submission will not be considered complete.
Q3Q3. What is the nature of engagement and salary/allowance for Jharkhand Home Guard Ramgarh posts?
A
Home Guard is a voluntary, honorary service, not a permanent or salaried government job; enrolled Home Guards are paid duty‑based allowances at rates fixed by the Jharkhand Government only for the days they are deployed on duty, and there is no fixed monthly salary or regular livelihood guarantee.
Q4Q4. What is the educational qualification required for Rural and Urban Home Guards in Ramgarh?
A
For Rural Home Guard (Gramin Grih Rakshak), the minimum qualification is Class 7th pass, while for Urban Home Guard (Shahri Grih Rakshak), the minimum qualification is Matriculation/Class 10th pass from a recognised board.
Q5Q5. What is the age limit for Jharkhand Home Guard Ramgarh Recruitment 2026?
A
As on 01 January 2026, the minimum age is 19 years and the maximum age is 40 years for both Rural and Urban Home Guards, similar to other district Home Guard notifications in the state.
Q6Q6. What are the domicile and residential requirements for Ramgarh Home Guard posts?
A
For Rural Home Guard, the applicant must be a permanent resident of the concerned block of Ramgarh district from which they apply. For Urban Home Guard, the applicant must be a general resident of the urban area of Ramgarh district. In both cases, an online residential certificate issued by the competent authority of Jharkhand must be uploaded and attached with the printout.
Q7Q7. What are the physical standards (height and chest) for Jharkhand Home Guard Ramgarh recruitment?
A
Indicative state Home Guard norms apply: male candidates of General/OBC/BC categories must have a minimum height of 162 cm and chest 79 cm (unexpanded), while SC/ST males require 157 cm height and 76 cm chest; female candidates of all categories require a minimum height of 148 cm, with no chest measurement.
Q8Q8. How can candidates apply online and submit applications for Jharkhand Home Guard Ramgarh 2026?
A
Candidates must visit recruitment.jharkhand.gov.in, select the Ramgarh Home Guard Recruitment 2026 link, register with a mobile number/email, fill the online form separately for Rural or Urban Home Guard, upload photograph, signature, domicile, educational, caste and other certificates, pay the ₹100 application fee online, submit the form, then take a printout and submit it with all enclosures at the District Commandant Office, Ramgarh between 08 May and 05 June 2026.
