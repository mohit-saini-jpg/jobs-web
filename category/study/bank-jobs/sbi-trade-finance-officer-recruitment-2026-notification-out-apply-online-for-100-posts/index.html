Candidates with any disciplinary proceedings pending or contemplated against them as on date will not be eligible to apply; an undertaking must be uploaded at the time of online application. CIBIL: Candidates who have defaulted in repayment under any lending arrangement with Banks/NBFCs/Financial Institutions including credit card dues and have not regularized/repaid their outstanding till the date of issuance of offer of recruitment by the Bank shall not be eligible for appointment. In case of multiple applications for the same post, only the last valid (completed) application will be retained; fee paid for other registrations will stand forfeited. OBC certificate containing the ‘non-creamy layer’ clause, issued during the period 01.04.2026 to the date of interview, must be submitted by OBC candidates if called for interview. EWS candidates not in possession of “Income & Asset Certificate” for FY2025-26 on or before closure of online application date should apply under “UR Category” only. Outstation candidates called for interview will be reimbursed airfare (economy class, shortest route, to-and-fro) up to a maximum of Rs. 10,000/- on submission of original flight tickets/invoice and boarding pass. All changes/updates/revisions/corrigendum/results will be hosted on the Bank’s website only; no separate intimation will be issued. Any legal proceedings arising out of this advertisement can be instituted only in Mumbai; Courts/Tribunals/Forums at Mumbai only shall have sole and exclusive jurisdiction.
