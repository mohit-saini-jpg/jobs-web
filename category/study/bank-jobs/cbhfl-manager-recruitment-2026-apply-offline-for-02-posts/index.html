Job Overview

The Cent Bank Home Finance Limited (CBHFL) has published the Manager Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 2 Manager positions. Candidates within the age range of up to 40 years (relaxable by 5 years for SC/ST and 3 years for OBC) and holding a qualified Chartered Accountant (CA) degree with relevant experience in finance, accounts, or credit underwriting are eligible to apply offline at www.cbhfl.com. The application process will be active until 06 June 2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.
