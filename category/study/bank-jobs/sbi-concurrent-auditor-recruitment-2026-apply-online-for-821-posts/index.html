Important Instructions

Only retired officers of SBI (and its e-Associate banks) who retired on superannuation at age 60 in the scales of MMGS-III, SMGS-IV/V or TEGS-VI are eligible to apply. This engagement is purely contractual in nature and shall not be treated as extension in service for the purpose of PF/Pension/Bonus and other superannuation benefits. The Engaged Retired Official will not be covered under SBI Officers’ Service Rules but will be bound by the terms of the offer of engagement. The retired officers will not exercise any administrative/financial power during the period of engagement. The retired officers will not accept any assignment with any other organization during the period of contractual service in the Bank. Retired Officers with good credit/audit/Forex background should preferably be a resident of the area where the branch/outfit is located. Only one application will be accepted for applying at one Circle. Multiple applications if received, only the last valid (completed) application will be accepted. The existing wait list of Retired Officers empanelled as Concurrent Auditors will get cancelled with this publication. Candidates must upload all required documents; failure to do so will result in their candidature not being considered for shortlisting/interview. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the interview process. Contract may be terminated by giving one month’s notice from either side. Contract may be terminated without notice if performance is not satisfactory. The selected candidate has to furnish a stamped Service Level Agreement (SLA) before taking up the assignment. If any false or incorrect information is detected at any stage of engagement, candidature/services will be summarily terminated. Bank reserves the right to cancel the engagement process entirely at any stage without prior notice. All revisions/corrigendum (if any) will be hosted on the Bank’s Careers website only. For any query, write through the ‘CONTACT US’ link available on the Bank’s website (https://sbi.bank.in/web/careers/).
