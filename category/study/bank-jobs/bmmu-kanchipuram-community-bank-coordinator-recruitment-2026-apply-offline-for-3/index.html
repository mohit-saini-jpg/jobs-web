Qualification / Eligibility

Must be a woman SHG member from village level federation. Minimum 2 years experience as SHG motivator. 12th pass with good Tamil and English reading/writing and arithmetic skills. Must be resident of the concerned village panchayat. No NPA in SHG. Communication skills and banking transaction experience required. Preference for computer knowledge and two-wheeler driving license.
