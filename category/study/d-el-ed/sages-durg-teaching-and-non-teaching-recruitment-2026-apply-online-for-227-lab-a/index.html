Important Instructions

  • This is a contractual appointment-appointments to contractual positions are purely temporary. They will not be eligible to claim similar positions in government service. Contractual appointment can be terminated at any time by the management based on conduct or work behavior, with 3 months' prior notice or 3 months' pay in lieu thereof. Candidate must be a मूल निवासी of Chhattisgarh state. Certificate issued by competent authority must be attached. Mandatory English Medium Requirement: Applicants must have completed the qualifying educational qualifications (from 6th grade to a postgraduate degree) in English medium. English medium will not be mandatory for Hindi and Sanskrit lecturers in 10th grade and all higher classes. All 38 SAGES schools in Durg district operate under the High Court of New Delhi's order (SLP(C) No. 19668/2022) regarding contractual appointments. Currently serving contractual teachers at SAGES cannot apply for the same-level posts; only for higher-level posts. Only online applications submitted on the given website are valid. Documents submitted at time of online form submission are the only valid documents for verification. Applicants will be called for document verification on the basis of their marks obtained in the qualifying examination. The number of posts advertised is subject to change - the total vacancy count may change. The selection committee's decision on appointment will be final and binding. Incomplete or erroneous applications will be summarily rejected without separate intimation. If only one application is received, the first application will be considered valid; subsequent applications will be treated as cancelled. After selection, appointees must submit a government medical board fitness certificate within 7 days of joining. Contractual employees are entitled only to monthly lump sum remuneration; no special pay, DA, HRA, or annual increment is applicable under the contract appointment. Candidates with SC/ST/OBC category must attach permanent caste certificate issued by a competent authority at the time of document verification. Any candidate found to have provided incorrect information or whose documents are found to have discrepancies will be disqualified without prior notice. After the final merit list, candidates on the waitlist will remain valid for 1 year from the date of result or until the next engagement exercise, whichever is earlier. All future notifications, corrigenda and updates will be published only on the official website. No separate communication will be sent via any other channel.
