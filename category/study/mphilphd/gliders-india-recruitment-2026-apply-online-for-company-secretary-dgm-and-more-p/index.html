Sr. Manager Marketing (International): Bachelor's Degree in Engineering / Technology. MBA in Marketing / International Business. Company Secretary: Registered Company Secretary (C.S.) with the Institute of Company Secretaries of India. FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for Gliders India Fixed Term Employment posts?
The last date to apply for most of the Fixed Term Employment posts at Gliders India is 20th June 2026. However, for the Company Secretary position, the last date to apply is 10th June 2026.
Q2. How many vacancies are available in Gliders India Recruitment 2026?
Gliders India is recruiting for a total of 6 Fixed Term Employment posts across various designations, including DGM and Senior Manager roles.
Q3. What is the application fee for Gliders India recruitment?
The application fee for Gliders India recruitment is Rs. 500/- (non-refundable). However, candidates belonging to SC, ST, PWBD, and Ex-servicemen categories are exempted from paying this fee.
Q4. What is the mode of application for Gliders India jobs?
The application for Gliders India Fixed Term Employment posts must be submitted via Email. Candidates need to send their complete application along with required documents to the specified email address.
Q5. What is the maximum age limit for DGM posts at Gliders India?
The maximum age limit for the Deputy General Manager (DGM) posts at Gliders India is 65 years. This applies to both DGM – Product Development and DGM – Research & Development positions.
Q6. What is the minimum qualification required for the Sr. Manager IT post?
The minimum qualification required for the Sr. Manager Information Technology (IT) post is a B.E./B.Tech in Computer Science/Information Technology or an MCA degree. A minimum of 5 years of working experience is also necessary.
Q7. Does Gliders India offer any age relaxation for reserved categories?
The provided data does not explicitly mention age relaxation for reserved categories. Candidates are advised to refer to the official notification for detailed information on age relaxation policies.
Q8. What is the salary for the DGM – Product Development post?
The annual CTC for the DGM – Product Development post at Gliders India is Rs. 14,40,000/-. This is a fixed-term employment position on a contractual basis.
Q9. What is the selection process for Gliders India recruitment?
The selection process for Gliders India recruitment may involve a Written & Interview or Interview only, depending on the discretion of the company. The selection will be based on qualifications, experience, positions held, accreditation, and achievements, with a weighted chart for qualifications/experiences and the interview.
