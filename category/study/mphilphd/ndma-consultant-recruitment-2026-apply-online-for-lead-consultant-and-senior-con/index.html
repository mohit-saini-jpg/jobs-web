FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the NDMA Consultant posts?
The last date to submit the online application form for the NDMA Consultant recruitment is 07 June 2026. Ensure your application is submitted well before this deadline.
Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the NDMA Consultant positions?
Candidates must possess a Master's Degree in Disaster Management. A Master's Degree in Disaster Management is the minimum requirement for all consultant roles advertised.
Q3. What is the maximum age limit for applying to these posts?
The maximum age limit for all Consultant posts in the NDMA recruitment is 62 years. There are no specified minimum age limits mentioned in the provided data.
Q4. Is there any application fee for the NDMA Consultant recruitment?
No, there is no application fee for any category, including General/OBC and SC/ST candidates, for this recruitment drive. Candidates can apply without any financial obligation.
Q5. How many vacancies are available for the Consultant posts at NDMA?
There are a total of 4 vacancies for Consultant posts at the National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA). These are distributed across different specialized roles.
Q6. What is the salary range for the Consultant positions?
The salary for the Consultant positions ranges from Rs. 1,25,000 to Rs. 2,50,000 per month, depending on the specific role and experience of the selected candidate.
Q7. In which mode should the application for NDMA Recruitment 2026 be submitted?
Applications for the NDMA Consultant recruitment must be submitted strictly through the online mode. Candidates are advised to visit the official NDMA website for the application portal.
Q8. What is the job type for these Consultant positions?
The Consultant positions at NDMA are contractual in nature. This means the employment will be for a fixed tenure as per the terms and conditions of the contract.
Q9. Where is the National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA) located?
The National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA) is located in New Delhi, Delhi, with a pincode of 110029. This is where the recruitment drive is being managed from.
Q10. What is the advertisement number for this recruitment?
The advertisement number for this recruitment of Consultant posts by the NDMA is F. No. 01-37/2023-Admn. This number should be used for any official correspondence.
