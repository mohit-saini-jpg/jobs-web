How to Apply

  • 1Candidates must apply **online only** through the official website http://www.sgpgims.org.in. No other mode of application will be accepted. Candidates can apply for multiple posts but must submit separate applications and pay separate fees for each post.
  • 2Before filling the Online Application, candidates must keep ready the following documents:
  • 3Recent Scanned Colored Photograph (JPG/JPEG, max 80KB)
  • 4Scanned Signature (JPG/JPEG, max 80KB)
  • 5Valid Email ID and Mobile Number (must remain active till recruitment process ends)
  • 6Scanned copies of Mark sheets & Certificates for 10th, 12th, Diploma, Graduation, Post-Graduation etc. in PDF format
  • 7Scanned copy of Caste/Category Certificate, Sub-Category, Domicile Certificate (if applicable) & Experience Certificate (if applicable) in PDF format
  • 8Visit the official website: https://www.sgpgims.org.in
  • 9Go through Advertisement, How to Apply, FAQ, and Helpdesk sections.
  • 10Click on “Registration for New Users” and register with Name, Post Applied, Date of Birth, Email ID, and Mobile Number.

  • 12Login using “Sign in for Existing Users”.
  • 13Fill the Application Form carefully in following sections: Personal Details Communication Details Qualification Details Document Upload & Payment
  • 14Personal Details
  • 15Communication Details
  • 16Qualification Details
  • 17Document Upload & Payment
  • 18Upload all required scanned documents in correct format and size.
  • 19Preview the application, edit if needed, and submit.
  • 20Pay the application fee through online mode (Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking).
  • 21After successful payment, take a printout of the application form and note down the Application Sequence Number for future reference.
  • 22Apply Online at: https://sgpgims.eapplynow.com/SGPGIBL26/Pages/Index.aspx

FAQs

Q1. What is the total number of vacancies in SGPGIMS Lucknow Recruitment 2026?
Sanjay Gandhi Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences (SGPGIMS), Lucknow has announced a total of 47 backlog vacancies for various Group B and Group C posts. These vacancies are available for candidates belonging to SC, ST, and OBC categories under a special recruitment drive. The recruitment includes technical, paramedical, and support staff positions.
