Qualification / Eligibility

Junior Superintendent: First Class Bachelor's degree with 5 years' experience as Senior Assistant/Assistant in Pay Level-5 in the relevant area. Junior Superintendent (Library): First Class Bachelor's degree in Library Science with 5 years' experience as Senior Assistant/Assistant (Library) in Pay Level-5 in the relevant area. Junior Superintendent (Translator): First Class Bachelor's degree in Hindi or English with English or Hindi as a compulsory or elective subject or as a medium of examination at the degree level; alongwith 5 years' experience as Senior Assistant/Assistant (Hindi Translator) in Pay Level-5 in the relevant area. Junior Technical Superintendent: BE/B.Tech(CSE/IT)/MCA/ M.Sc. (Computer Science) (Electrical & Electronics Engineering) / M.Sc. (Electrical/Instrumentation, Electronics/EEE/Mechatronics Engineering) with 5 years' experience in Level-5 or equivalent industrial experience. Staff Nurse: B.Sc. in Nursing with 2 years experience OR 3 years diploma in Nursing & Midwifery with 5 years experience.
