Qualification / Eligibility

Assistant Manager (Electrical): Degree in Electrical or Electronics or Electrical & Electronics or Instrumentation or Electrical & Electronics & Communication Engineering from a recognized University. Minimum of three-year Diploma in Electrical or Electronics or Mechatronics or Shipbuilding Engineering from a State Board of Technical Education. Experience: i) For degree holders: Minimum of Three years post qualification experience. ii) For diploma holders: Minimum of Five years post qualification experience.
