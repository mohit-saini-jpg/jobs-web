How to Apply

  • 1Visit the official MMRCL website: https://corporate.mmrcl.com → Careers → MMRCL Recruitment Advertisement 2026-02.
  • 2Register and fill out the online application form carefully. No other mode of application will be accepted.
  • 3Upload scanned copies of your updated Resume, passport-size photograph (.jpg/.jpeg), and recent pay slip (.pdf).
  • 4Clearly indicate whether applying for Regular, Contract, or Deputation basis.
  • 5Candidates from Government departments must also send a hard copy of their application with NOC, ACRs/APARs of the last 5 years, vigilance clearance, and penalty certificate to: General Manager (HR & Admin), Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation Limited, MMRCL–Line 3 Transit Office, E Block, Bandra Kurla Complex, Bandra (East), Mumbai – 400051.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is MMRCL Recruitment 2026 – Advertisement 2026-02?
A
MMRCL Recruitment 2026 under Advertisement 2026‑02 is a hiring drive by Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation Limited for 24 posts across 19 categories from General Manager to Assistant (IT). The appointments will be made on Regular, Contract (mostly 5 years) or Deputation basis depending on the post.
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are announced in MMRCL Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 24 vacancies have been announced across 19 different post categories, including General Manager, Deputy General Manager, Assistant General Manager, Manager (Finance), Deputy Engineer, Assistant Manager, Systems Analyst/Jr. Engg‑I, Draftsman, Junior Engineer‑II (S&T) and Assistant (IT).
Q3Q3. What are the important dates for MMRCL Recruitment 2026?
A
The online registration starts on 27th May 2026 (10:00 Hrs.) and closes on 26th June 2026 (23:59 Hrs.). The eligibility cut‑off date for age, qualification and experience is 1st May 2026, and applications submitted after the closing date will not be accepted.
Q4Q4. What are the pay scales for MMRCL 2026 posts?
A
Pay scales are as per IDA pattern: E8 (GM): ₹1,20,000 – 2,80,000 E5 (DGM): ₹80,000 – 2,20,000 E4 (AGM): ₹70,000 – 2,00,000 E3 (Manager): ₹60,000 – 1,80,000 E2 (Dy. Engineer/Assistant Manager): ₹50,000 – 1,60,000 W7 (Systems Analyst/Jr. Engg‑I): ₹40,320 – 77,540 W6 (Draftsman/Jr. Engineer‑II): ₹35,280 – 67,920 W5 (Assistant IT): ₹34,020 – 64,310.
Q5Q5. What is the age limit for MMRCL Recruitment 2026?
A
The maximum age varies by grade as on 1st May 2026: General Manager (E8): 53 years DGM/AGM (E5–E4): 40 years Manager (E3): 35 years Deputy Engineer/Assistant Manager (E2): 35 years Systems Analyst/Jr. Engg‑I (W7): 40 years Draftsman/Jr. Engineer‑II/Assistant (W5–W6): 35 years. Relaxations: SC/ST – 5 years, OBC (NCL) – 3 years, PwD – up to 10 years, Ex‑Servicemen – 5 years, with further relaxation for internal/deputation candidates as per rules.
Q6Q6. What is the selection process for MMRCL Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process is shortlisting followed by Personal Interview: Applications are shortlisted based on eligibility, educational qualifications and relevant post‑qualification experience. Shortlisted candidates are called for a Personal Interview at MMRCL’s office in Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai. Final selection is made on merit, subject to medical fitness and verification of documents.
Q7Q7. Is there any bond or service agreement for selected candidates in MMRCL?
A
Yes, candidates appointed on Regular/Contract basis must execute a Surety Bond ranging roughly from ₹1,00,000 to ₹4,00,000 depending on the grade, as per MMRCL’s Surety & Training Cost Recovery Bond Policy. The bond is not applicable to candidates joining on Deputation.
Q8Q8. What experience is required for various MMRCL posts?
A
Post‑qualification experience (as on 1st May 2026) is mandatory and differs by level: GM (E8): 18 years in Govt/PSU or 20 years in Private with minimum CTC ₹30 Lakhs. DGM (E5): 12 years in Govt/PSU or 14 years Private with CTC ₹18 Lakhs. AGM/Manager (E4/E3): 9/6 years in Govt/PSU or 11 years Private with CTC ₹15 Lakhs. Dy. Engineer/Assistant Manager (E2): 5 years Govt/PSU or 7 years Private with CTC ₹10 Lakhs. W‑grade posts: 2–10 years depending on degree/diploma and Govt/Private background as per the advertisement.
