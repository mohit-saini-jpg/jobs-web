Important Instructions

THIS IS NOT REGULAR EMPLOYMENT. This advertisement is for apprenticeship training under Apprentices Act 1961, Section 22(1). MPEZ is not obligated to offer any employment after completion of training. Regular/contractual recruitment at MPEZ will not carry any reservation benefit related to this apprenticeship. Vacancies are only for Madhya Pradesh domicile (Mool Niwasi) candidates. Applications from non-MP domicile candidates will be rejected. Candidates must apply for only ONE trade/branch. Applications with more than one trade selected will be cancelled. NATS portal registration with 100% completed profile and a valid Apprentice Registration Number is mandatory before applying. The date of passing the qualifying examination must not be more than 5 years from the NATS registration approval date; if NATS registration is not approved, the selection will be cancelled. Candidates who have previously completed apprenticeship training in the same trade at MPEZ or any other organization cannot apply again for the same trade. If any candidate is found to have concealed or provided incorrect information at any stage, their candidature will be cancelled and legal action may be taken. SC/ST/OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) and PwBD candidates must carefully fill in their category; no category change will be permitted after submission. During the training period, selected candidates will be entitled to 13 days casual leave, 15 days medical leave, and weekly and national holidays. In case of an accident, the Employee Compensation Act, 1923 (Karmachari Kshatipurti Niyam 1923) will be applicable. Candidates who do not join within the specified time will have their selection treated as automatically cancelled. MPEZ reserves the right to cancel/limit/extend the selection process without prior notice or providing any reason. All legal and judicial matters related to this advertisement and recruitment will be under the jurisdiction of Jabalpur courts only. Selected candidates will be bound by all existing and future rules and regulations framed by MPEZ Jabalpur for apprentice engagement. All revisions/corrigendum (if any) will be hosted on the company's official website www.mpez.co.in only — candidates should visit regularly.
