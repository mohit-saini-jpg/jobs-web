Qualification / Eligibility

Junior Assistant: Must hold a Bachelor’s Degree of any University in India established or incorporated by or under a Central Act, State Act or Provincial Act or an Institution recognized by the University Grants Commission or any equivalent qualification. Lab Attender (Mechanical): ITI Pass Certificate in related trades such as Fitter, Turner, Machinist, Welder or other equivalent Mechanical trades with 1 year of experience after ITI or A Diploma in Mechanical Engineering (DME) with 1 year of experience. Lab Attender (Electrical): ITI Pass Certificate in Electrician or related trades with 1 year of experience after ITI or A Diploma in Electrical & Electronics Engineering (DEEE) with 1 year of experience. Office Subordinate: Must have passed VII Class examination and Must be able to ride a Bicycle.
