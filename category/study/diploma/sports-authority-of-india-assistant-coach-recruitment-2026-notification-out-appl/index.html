Candidates must fulfill the minimum qualifications and parameters specified by SAI: | Option A: Officers working under Central Government/State Governments/Union Territory Administrations holding an analogous post in their parent cadre/department. Option D: As defined by the governing body of SAI in respect of sports quota recruits.
