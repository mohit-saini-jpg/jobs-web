How to Apply

  • 1Eligible candidates must complete the online application process only:
  • 2Visit the official website: https://virtualofficeerp.com/gumul2026 (for regular posts) or https://virtualofficeerp.com/gumulbl2026 (for Backlog posts).
  • 3Register and fill the online application form carefully. Ensure all details are correct before final submission.
  • 4Upload self-attested scanned copies of: passport-size photo (white background, max 100 KB), signature (max 50 KB), educational/qualification certificates, caste certificate, experience certificate, Kannada medium certificate, Ex-Serviceman certificate (if applicable), Graama certificate, Yojana Niraashritara certificate, and disability certificate (if applicable).
  • 5Pay the application fee online through Debit Card/Credit Card/Net Banking/Online modes. Fee once paid will not be refunded.
  • 6Fee payment must be completed on or before June 27, 2026. DD/MO/Postal Orders will not be accepted.
  • 7After successful submission, take a printout of the application and bring it when called for the written test/interview.
  • 8For technical difficulties during online application, contact Help Line: 9036072155 (between 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM).
