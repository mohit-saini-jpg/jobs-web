Job Overview

The Indian Institute of Technology Patna (IIT Patna) has officially announced the Project Engineer, Project Assistant Recruitment 2026 with 3 openings. The detailed notification was released under Advertisement No. R&D/1474/TCN/580 on the official IIT Patna website. Candidates who meet the age criteria of up to 65 years (with applicable relaxations as per Government of India norms) and hold a B.Tech degree in Civil, Construction, Electrical, or Mechanical Engineering with a minimum of 60% marks or a CPI of 6.5 are eligible to apply through email mode. Qualification / Eligibility

Project Engineer (Civil/Structural Engineer): B.Tech degree in Civil or Construction Engineering with minimum 60% marks or CPI of 6.5. Minimum 15 years' experience of civil works including structural works, RC and steel structure construction, and pavement works. Project Engineer (Electrical/Mechanical Engineer): B.Tech degree in Mechanical or Electrical Engineering with minimum 60% marks or CPI of 6.5. 15 years' experience of Electrical Mechanical works. Project Assistant (Civil Engineer): B.Tech degree in Civil Engineering with minimum 60% marks or CPI of 6.5.
