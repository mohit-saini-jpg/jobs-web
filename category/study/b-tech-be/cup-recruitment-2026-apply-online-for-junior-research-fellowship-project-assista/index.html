Project Assistant: B.Sc./B.Tech. / M.Sc. degree with minimum 55% marks in Biotechnology, Botany, Molecular Biology, Bioinformatics, or relevant allied disciplines from a recognized University or Institute.

FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the CUP Recruitment 2026?
The last date to submit your application via email for the Junior Research Fellowship / Project Assistant posts at Central University of Punjab is June 5, 2026. Ensure your application reaches before this deadline.
Q2. What are the educational qualifications required for the Junior Research Fellowship post?
For the Junior Research Fellowship position, candidates need a Master’s degree (M.Sc./M.Tech.) with at least 55% marks in relevant Life Sciences fields and must have cleared national-level eligibility tests like CSIR-UGC NET or GATE.
Q3. Is there any application fee for the CUP Recruitment 2026?
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for this recruitment drive by the Central University of Punjab.
Q4. What is the maximum age limit for the advertised posts?
The maximum age limit for both Junior Research Fellowship and Project Assistant positions is 50 years. Age relaxation may be applicable as per official DBT rules.
Q5. How will candidates be selected for these positions?
The selection process for the Junior Research Fellowship / Project Assistant posts at Central University of Punjab will be based on an Interview.
Q6. What is the salary offered for the Project Assistant role?
The Project Assistant will receive a consolidated salary of ₹20,000/- per month along with 9% House Rent Allowance (HRA), as per DBT guidelines.
Q7. Can candidates apply through any other mode apart from email?
No, the application mode for this recruitment is strictly via email. Ensure your application is sent to the correct official email address before the last date.
Q8. What documents are required to be attached with the application?
Candidates need to attach self-attested copies of educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs with their application.
Q9. Where can I find the official notification for CUP Recruitment 2026?
The official notification PDF can be downloaded from the Important Links section of this article. It contains all the detailed information regarding the recruitment.
