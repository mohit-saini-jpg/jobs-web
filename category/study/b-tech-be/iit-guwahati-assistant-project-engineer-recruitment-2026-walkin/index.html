Candidates must possess a BE/B.Tech in Engineering with relevant experience in ML Accelerator Design to be eligible for the Assistant Project Engineer position.

A
The salary for the Assistant Project Engineer post ranges from Rs. 37,000 to Rs. 44,170.00 per month, depending on qualifications and experience.

A

Q6Q6. When is the Walk-in Interview scheduled?
