Important Instructions

  • This engagement is purely of temporary nature (Contract for Service) and shall NOT create any right for regular appointment on any ground whatsoever. It does NOT create an employer-employee relationship with SAIL. Initial tenure is 3 years, extendable up to 2 additional years (one year at a time) based on satisfactory performance - maximum total period is 5 years. Performance is reviewed annually; tenure extension is at the discretion of the Company based on performance and eligibility. During the first 6 months, either party can terminate with 7 days' notice without notice pay. Beyond 6 months, 1 month's notice or 1 month's retainership fee in lieu is required by either party. Absence for 15 consecutive days without prior written sanction may lead to immediate termination without notice. Self-insurance (Medical/Life/Health/Accident) is mandatory for all Young Professionals. No other perks, allowances, loans, advances, PRP, PF, Pension, Gratuity, or terminal benefits are payable beyond those specifically mentioned in the advertisement. In the event of death, injury or illness attributable to performance of services, neither the Young Professional nor family members shall be entitled to any compensation from SAIL. Candidates must join within 30 days from the offer of engagement - no extension is permitted on any grounds. Every selected candidate must sign a Contract for Service agreement with the respective SAIL Plant before joining. Engagement without a signed agreement is null and void. Candidates must furnish an undertaking/declaration regarding good moral character and absence of criminal conviction or pending judicial proceedings at the time of joining. SAIL reserves the right to carry out pre-engagement verification of antecedents and credentials including general character. Adverse reports may lead to termination without notice. Examination centres allotted are binding - no request for change of examination centre will be entertained. Court of jurisdiction for any dispute will be at Asansol/Durgapur, West Bengal. SAIL-ISP/DSP/ASP reserves the right to cancel/restrict/modify/alter the engagement process at any stage without notice or reason, without any liability to compensate applicants. All corrigendum/addendum/notices will be published only on www.sail.co.in - candidates must check the website periodically.
