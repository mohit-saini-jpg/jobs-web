Important Instructions

  • This engagement is purely of temporary nature (Contract for Service) and shall NOT create any right for regular appointment on any ground whatsoever. It does NOT create an employer-employee relationship with SAIL. Initial tenure is 3 years, extendable up to 2 additional years (one year at a time) based on satisfactory performance - maximum total period is 5 years. Performance is reviewed annually; tenure extension is at the discretion of the Company based on performance and eligibility. During the first 6 months, either party can terminate with 7 days' notice without notice pay. Beyond 6 months, 1 month's notice or 1 month's retainership fee in lieu is required by either party. Absence for 15 consecutive days without prior written sanction may lead to immediate termination without notice. Self-insurance (Medical/Life/Health/Accident) is mandatory for all Young Professionals. No other perks, allowances, loans, advances, PRP, PF, Pension, Gratuity, or terminal benefits are payable beyond those specifically mentioned in the advertisement. In the event of death, injury or illness attributable to performance of services, neither the Young Professional nor family members shall be entitled to any compensation from SAIL. Candidates must join within 30 days from the offer of engagement - no extension is permitted on any grounds. Every selected candidate must sign a Contract for Service agreement with the respective SAIL Plant before joining. Engagement without a signed agreement is null and void. Candidates must furnish an undertaking/declaration regarding good moral character and absence of criminal conviction or pending judicial proceedings at the time of joining. SAIL reserves the right to carry out pre-engagement verification of antecedents and credentials including general character. Adverse reports may lead to termination without notice. Examination centres allotted are binding - no request for change of examination centre will be entertained. Court of jurisdiction for any dispute will be at Asansol/Durgapur, West Bengal. SAIL-ISP/DSP/ASP reserves the right to cancel/restrict/modify/alter the engagement process at any stage without notice or reason, without any liability to compensate applicants. All corrigendum/addendum/notices will be published only on www.sail.co.in - candidates must check the website periodically.

FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for SAIL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
The last date to submit the online application for SAIL Young Professional Recruitment 2026 is 06 June 2026. Candidates must complete the registration, upload required documents, and pay the application fee before the deadline to avoid last-minute technical issues. Applications submitted after the closing date will not be accepted under any circumstances.
Q2. How many vacancies are available in SAIL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
SAIL has announced a total of 48 Young Professional vacancies across Durgapur Steel Plant (DSP), IISCO Steel Plant (ISP), and Alloy Steels Plant (ASP). These positions are distributed among Projects, Works, and Materials Management Divisions for YP-1, YP-2, and YP-3 grades depending on qualification and work experience.
Q3. What educational qualification is required for SAIL Young Professional posts?
Candidates must possess a full-time regular B.E./B.Tech degree with at least 65% marks from a Government-recognized University or Institution. The required engineering stream varies according to the post, including Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, Computer Science, Chemical, Instrumentation, and related disciplines. Higher qualifications such as M.E./M.Tech or Ph.D. are desirable for several posts.
Q4. What is the age limit for SAIL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
The minimum age required is 23 years, while the upper age limit depends on the grade applied for. The maximum age is 35 years for YP-1, 38 years for YP-2, and 42 years for YP-3, calculated as on 06 June 2026. Candidates should ensure they meet the prescribed age criteria before applying.
Q5. What is the salary offered under SAIL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
Selected candidates will receive an attractive monthly retainership based on the grade of engagement. YP-1 candidates will receive ₹70,000 per month, YP-2 candidates ₹1,00,000, and YP-3 candidates ₹1,40,000, along with annual performance-based increments. Additional benefits such as accommodation, medical facilities, insurance reimbursement, and travel allowances are also available.
Q6. What is the selection process for SAIL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
The selection process includes application screening, Computer Based Test (CBT) if required, and personal interview. If applications exceed five times the available vacancies, SAIL may conduct a CBT or aptitude/psychometric test for shortlisting. Final merit will be prepared with 80% weightage for CBT and 20% for interview performance
Q7. Is SAIL Young Professional Recruitment 2026 a permanent government job?
No, the Young Professional engagement in SAIL is purely contractual and temporary in nature. Initially, the contract is for 3 years, which may be extended annually up to a maximum of 5 years depending on performance and organizational requirements. This engagement does not guarantee regular employment in SAIL.
Q8. What is the application fee for SAIL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
All candidates applying for SAIL Young Professional posts are required to pay a non-refundable application fee of ₹500 through online mode only. Applicants should carefully verify eligibility details before making payment because no refund or correction request will be entertained after fee submission.
Q9. Can candidates apply for more than one post in SAIL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
Candidates are advised to apply for only one engagement code as the Computer Based Test may not be conducted on different dates for multiple applications. Applying for multiple posts could create scheduling conflicts and may affect candidature during the recruitment process.
Q10. Where can candidates apply online for SAIL Young Professional Recruitment 2026?
