Job Overview

Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA) has published the Assistant Engineer Notification 2026 on 20 May 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 37 Assistant Engineer positions. Candidates within the age range of 18–40 years and holding a relevant Engineering degree are eligible to apply online at the official KEA website. The application process will be active from 21 May 2026 to 04 June 2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below

Qualification / Eligibility

Assistant Engineer (Civil) (Group-B): Degree in Civil Engineering or Technology or equivalent from recognized University and Computer literate (minimum six months course). Assistant Engineer (Mechanical) (Group-B): Degree in Mechanical Engineering or Technology or equivalent from recognized University and Computer literate (minimum six months course). Assistant Engineer (Civil) (Group-B): Bachelor degree of Engineering in Civil by a University established by law in India or equivalent. Assistant Engineer (Civil) (Group-B) (Contractual): Bachelor degree of Engineering in Civil by a University established by law in India or equivalent. Assistant Engineer (Computer Science) (Group-B): Degree in Computer Science Engineering or Technology or equivalent from recognized University and Computer literate (minimum six months course). Assistant Executive Engineer (Civil) (Group-B): Bachelor Engineering Degree in Civil awarded by a University established by law in India with three years experience in relevant field.
