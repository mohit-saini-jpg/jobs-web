Qualification / Eligibility

TGT (Mathematics): Bachelor's Degree with at least 50% marks in Mathematics OR Four years Integrated B.Sc.B.Ed (PCM) of Regional College of Education of NCERT with at least 50% marks AND B.Ed or equivalent with 50% marks AND Passed CTET/STET Paper II. TGT (Hindi): Graduate in Hindi with at least 50% marks AND B.Ed or equivalent AND Passed CTET/STET Paper II. TGT (Social Science): Graduate with two subjects out of History, Political Science, Economics, Sociology, Geography with 50% marks OR Hons Graduation with History/Political Science/Geography AND B.Ed OR B.A.Ed AND Passed CTET/STET Paper II. TGT (Marathi): Graduate in Marathi with 50% marks AND B.Ed or equivalent AND Qualified CTET/State TET Paper II. Art Master: Four years Diploma in Fine Arts after Class XII OR Five years after Class X OR Degree in Fine Arts/Crafts OR B.Ed in Fine Arts from Regional College of Education. Music Teacher: Five years study in Music equivalent to Graduate/Post Graduate OR Bachelor's with Music OR Higher Secondary with Sangeet Visharad etc. Laboratory Assistant (Physics): Intermediate Science or equivalent in Physics with 50% marks. Medical Officer: MBBS degree. Ward Boy / Matron (Hostel): Matriculation or equivalent; able to converse fluently in English.
