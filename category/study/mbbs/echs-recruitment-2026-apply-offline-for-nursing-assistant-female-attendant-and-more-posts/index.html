FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the ECHS recruitment?
The last date to submit the application for the ECHS Para Medical & Non-Medical Staff posts is 06-06-2026. Ensure your application reaches the designated address by this date.

There are a total of 9 vacancies for Para Medical & Non-Medical Staff posts released by the Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme (ECHS) in this recruitment drive.

The application mode for the ECHS Para Medical & Non-Medical Staff recruitment is offline. Candidates need to send their filled application forms to the official address mentioned in the notification.

The salary for the ECHS posts varies, with some positions offering up to 95,000/-, while others range from 36,500/- down to 21,800/- per month, as per the official notification.
Q5. What are the eligibility criteria for the Officer-in-Charge post?
The eligibility criteria for the Officer-in-Charge post (only for ESM) is a Graduate degree with 05 years of relevant experience.
Q6. What is the selection process for ECHS recruitment?
The selection process for ECHS recruitment includes shortlisting of candidates, followed by an interview, and a 10-day on-the-job training (OJT).
Q7. Where can I download the ECHS application form?
The application form for ECHS recruitment can be downloaded from the official website of ECHS, which is www.echs.gov.in.
Q8. What is the minimum qualification for the Ambulance Driver post?
The minimum qualification for the Ambulance Driver post is an 8th pass with 05 years of experience.
Q9. Is there any application fee for ECHS recruitment?
