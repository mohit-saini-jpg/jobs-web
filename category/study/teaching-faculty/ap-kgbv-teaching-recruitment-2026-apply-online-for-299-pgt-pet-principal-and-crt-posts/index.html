Important Instructions

  • ALL posts are for women candidates only. All rosters for general candidates are also to be filled with women candidates of that particular community. All appointments are purely on contract/outsourcing/temporary basis for academic year 2026-27 only. Selected candidates have NO right to seek regularisation, Minimum Time Scale, or enhancement of honorarium. The appointing authority has the right to terminate candidates with poor performance at any point of time without any prior notice. If any candidate's performance is found unsatisfactory in teaching, or they submit false documents, or misbehave with students - their services may be terminated immediately without prior notice. Selected candidates must enter into a Contract Agreement on Rs. 100/- Bond Paper for the period of one academic year 2026-27. The State Project Director has all rights to complete or cancel the recruitment at any point of time without prior intimation. All candidates must be placed in schools by 20.06.2026 without fail. Any deviation will be viewed very seriously. This notification is subject to the outcome of court cases pending before the Hon'ble High Court of Andhra Pradesh. No candidate is allowed to apply in more than one district. The district is the unit for applying and appointment for all cadres. Candidates who furnished wrong information or claimed higher marks than achieved will be permanently debarred from all government jobs. EWS candidates must furnish income certificate below Rs. 8.00 Lakhs per annum issued by the competent authority. All revisions/updates will be published at http://kgbvrecruitment.apcfss.in - visit regularly.

FAQs

Q1. What is AP KGBV Teaching Recruitment 2026?
AP KGBV Teaching Recruitment 2026 is a state-level drive by Samagra Shiksha, Andhra Pradesh, to fill 299 contract teaching posts (Principal, PGT, CRT, PET) in Type-III KGBVs for the academic year 2026–27. These posts are exclusively for eligible women candidates to work in residential girls’ schools across 23 districts.
Q2. How many vacancies are there in AP KGBV Teaching Recruitment 2026?
There are a total of 299 teaching vacancies notified: 16 Principal, 98 Post Graduate Teacher (PGT), 133 Contract Residential Teacher (CRT), and 52 Physical Education Teacher (PET) posts. These posts are distributed across 352 Type-III KGBVs in 23 districts, excluding East Godavari, Dr. B.R.A. Konaseema, West Godavari, Krishna and Guntur.
Q3. What are the important dates for AP KGBV Teaching Recruitment 2026?
Online applications start on 27 May 2026 and the last date to apply is 5 June 2026. Certificate verification is scheduled on 9 June 2026, final merit list on 16 June 2026, appointment orders on 18 June 2026, and reporting to duty on 20 June 2026.
Q4. What is the eligibility and educational qualification for AP KGBV teaching posts?
All posts are open to women candidates only, and applicants must be able to teach in English medium with English studied up to graduation level. Post-wise, Principals and PGTs need relevant PG plus B.Ed, CRTs need a relevant bachelor’s degree plus B.Ed, while PETs require Intermediate/degree with UGDPE or B.P.Ed/M.P.Ed as per NCTE norms, with minimum 50% marks for OC, 45% for BC/EWS, and 40% for SC/ST/DA.
Q5. Is TET mandatory for AP KGBV Principal, PGT, CRT and PET posts?
APTET qualification is mandatory for Principal, CRT (all subjects) and PGT (general subjects) posts under this recruitment. TET is not required for PET posts and PGT (Vocational) posts, which follow vocational subject-specific norms.
Q6. What is the age limit for AP KGBV Recruitment 2026?
Age is calculated as on 1 July 2025, with a minimum age of 18 years for all categories. The maximum age is 45 years for OC/EWS (and ex-service women), 50 years for SC/ST/BC/EWS, and 52 years for differently abled candidates (except PET, where PwD are not eligible).
Q7. What is the salary for AP KGBV Principal, PGT, CRT and PET posts?
Selected Principals will receive a monthly honorarium of Rs. 34,139, while PGT, CRT and PET posts carry a monthly honorarium of Rs. 26,759. These are purely contract/outsourcing positions for the 2026–27 academic year with no claim for regularisation or additional government benefits.
Q8. How is the selection done for AP KGBV Teaching Recruitment 2026?
Selection is done purely on merit basis, with no written examination conducted. Merit is calculated from SSC, Intermediate, Degree, PG and professional qualifications (B.Ed/UGDPE/B.P.Ed) as per a 100-mark weightage formula separately defined for Principal/PGT, CRT and PET posts.
Q9. How can I apply online for AP KGBV Teaching Recruitment 2026?
Candidates must apply online only through the official KGBV recruitment portal at http://kgbvrecruitment.apcfss.in, by registering with a valid mobile number and email ID, filling all details, uploading certificates, and paying the fee. Offline applications or applications submitted after 5 June 2026 will not be accepted under any circumstances.
Q10. What is the application fee and which documents are required to apply?
The application fee is Rs. 300 for all candidates, payable through the Commissioner of School Education payment gateway on the recruitment portal. Applicants must upload SSC, Intermediate, Degree, PG, professional qualification certificates, APTET score card (where applicable), local status, caste, income/EWS, disability (if any) and other required documents as per the notification.
