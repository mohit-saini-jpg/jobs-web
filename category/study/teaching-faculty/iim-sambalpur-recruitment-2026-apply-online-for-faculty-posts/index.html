Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationPh.D. degree
DetailsProfessor: Ph.D. degree or equivalent, first class or equivalent at preceding degree, exceptionally good academic record. Minimum 10 years teaching/research/industry experience (at least 4 years at Associate Professor level in IITs, IIMs, IISc, IISERs, top 50 ranked B-schools or foreign universities). Successful track record of publications (at least three papers in A/A* or six in B, with at least two in A/A* in last 4 years). Professor of Practice on Contract: Substantial relevant experience of minimum 20 years with at least 5 years at a senior level in industry/government. Demonstrated understanding of and impact on practice. Interest and potential to teach LDP courses. Ph.D. desirable but not essential, must be offset by domain knowledge. First-class (Min 60%) or equivalent in Master's/Graduation and Class XII/X. Associate Professor: Ph.D. degree or equivalent, first class or equivalent at preceding degree, exceptionally good academic record. Minimum 6 years teaching/industry/research experience (at least 3 years at Assistant Professor level). Successful track record of publications (at least two papers in A/A* or four in B or eight in C, with at least one in A/A* in last 4 years). Publication requirement may be relaxed for industry experience. Associate Professor of Practice on Contract: Substantial relevant experience of minimum 15 years in industry/government. Demonstrated understanding of and impact on practice. Interest and potential to teach LDP courses. Ph.D. desirable but not essential, must be offset by domain knowledge. First-class (Min 60%) or equivalent in Master's/Graduation and Class XII/X. Assistant Professor (Grade-I): Ph.D. degree or equivalent, first class or equivalent at preceding degree, exceptionally good academic record. At least 3 years industrial/research/teaching experience (excluding Ph.D. pursuit). Publications in class A or B required for confirmation (may be relaxed for industry experience). Assistant Professor (Grade-II): PhDs with less than 3 years' experience may apply. Offered a contract position.

FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the Faculty Positions at IIM Sambalpur?
The last date to apply online for the Faculty Positions at IIM Sambalpur is 08 June 2026.
Q2. What are the different faculty positions available?
The available positions include Professor, Professor of Practice on Contract, Associate Professor, Associate Professor of Practice on Contract, Assistant Professor (Grade-I), and Assistant Professor (Grade-II).
Q3. What is the minimum qualification required for these posts?
The minimum qualification is a Ph.D. degree or equivalent in the discipline with first class or equivalent at the preceding degree and an exceptionally good academic record.
Q4. Is there any age limit for the Professor of Practice positions?
Yes, the maximum age limit for Professor of Practice on Contract and Associate Professor of Practice on Contract is 65 years.
Q5. What is the salary structure for the faculty positions?
The salary is based on the 7th CPC Academic Pay Levels, ranging from 10 to 14A, with specific levels for each post.

Q7. Is there any application fee?

Q8. What is the selection process?
The selection process typically involves shortlisting, followed by an interview and a research seminar.
Q9. Are there any vacancies for Assistant Professor (Grade-II)?
Yes, PhDs with less than three years of experience may apply for Assistant Professor (Grade-II) on a contract basis.
