How to Apply

  • 1Candidates shall apply online only at http://kgbvrecruitment.apcfss.in. Offline applications and applications received after the closing date will not be entertained under any circumstances. Application fee of Rs. 300/- must be paid through the Commissioner of School Education payment gateway during online application. Candidates must upload all required documents without fail.
  • 2Steps to Apply Online:
  • 3Step 1: Visit http://kgbvrecruitment.apcfss.in and register with valid mobile number and email ID.
  • 4Step 2: Select the post (Principal / PGT / CRT / PET), subject and the district you are applying for.

Important Instructions

  • ALL posts are for women candidates only. All rosters for general candidates are also to be filled with women candidates of that particular community. All appointments are purely on contract/outsourcing/temporary basis for academic year 2026-27 only. Selected candidates have NO right to seek regularisation, Minimum Time Scale, or enhancement of honorarium. The appointing authority has the right to terminate candidates with poor performance at any point of time without any prior notice. If any candidate's performance is found unsatisfactory in teaching, or they submit false documents, or misbehave with students - their services may be terminated immediately without prior notice. Selected candidates must enter into a Contract Agreement on Rs. 100/- Bond Paper for the period of one academic year 2026-27. The State Project Director has all rights to complete or cancel the recruitment at any point of time without prior intimation. All candidates must be placed in schools by 20.06.2026 without fail. Any deviation will be viewed very seriously. This notification is subject to the outcome of court cases pending before the Hon'ble High Court of Andhra Pradesh. No candidate is allowed to apply in more than one district. The district is the unit for applying and appointment for all cadres. Candidates who furnished wrong information or claimed higher marks than achieved will be permanently debarred from all government jobs. EWS candidates must furnish income certificate below Rs. 8.00 Lakhs per annum issued by the competent authority. All revisions/updates will be published at http://kgbvrecruitment.apcfss.in - visit regularly.
