Important Instructions

  • Online mode of application is mandatory - no other mode will be accepted. Application portal opens on 22-05-2026 at 10:00 AM and closes on 20-06-2026 at 5:00 PM. Separate applications with separate registration fees must be submitted for each post applied for. Only information submitted online (including uploaded documents and research publication details) will be considered for eligibility, Academic Score, and shortlisting - no additional claims at document verification stage. Incomplete applications will be rejected - filling all mandatory fields is required. No changes are permitted after submitting the application form. Mere eligibility does not entitle any candidate for being called for interview - applicants with higher qualifications and merit will be given preference. Knowledge of Kannada is mandatory for all posts. Candidates who did not study Kannada at SSLC level must qualify in Kannada Language Test (max 150 marks, minimum 50%) before being called for interview. Kalyana Karnataka Cadre posts are reserved exclusively for local candidates of the Kalyana Karnataka region. Local candidates appointed to Residual Cadre posts shall not be eligible to claim Article 371(J) reservation benefits during entire service period. Candidates of Veterinary Science must upload valid KVC/VCI registration - signature and expiry date must be clearly visible; incomplete/poor visibility leads to rejection. Candidates previously selected in KVAFSU teaching recruitments who did not join - if they apply again and fail to join after selection, they will be made ineligible for all future KVAFSU recruitments. Selected candidates will be entitled for New Pension Scheme as per Govt. order No. FD(SN) 04 PET 2006 dated 31.03.2006 and as amended from time to time. The University reserves the right to withdraw any advertised post at any time without giving any reason. Canvassing in any form will be liable to disqualification. No interim correspondence shall be entertained. In case of any dispute/ambiguity in the selection process, the decision of the University shall be final.

FAQs

Q1. What is KVAFSU Teaching Recruitment 2026 and how many vacancies are there?
KVAFSU Teaching Recruitment 2026 is a direct recruitment drive for Professor, Associate Professor, and Assistant Professor posts in Veterinary, Dairy, Fisheries and allied subjects at KVAFSU, Bidar. A total of 96 teaching vacancies are notified across Kalyana Karnataka Cadre (371-J) and Residual Parent Cadre under the 2016 ICAR/UGC pay scale.
Q2. What are the important dates for KVAFSU Teaching Recruitment 2026?
The notification was issued on 21 May 2026, and the online application starts from 22 May 2026 (10:00 AM). The last date for online submission of applications is 20 June 2026 (5:00 PM), and eligibility is calculated as on this date.
Q3. What are the post-wise vacancies in KVAFSU Teaching Recruitment 2026?
KVAFSU has announced 96 posts approximately, including Professor – 20 posts, Associate Professor – 22 posts, and Assistant Professor – 54 posts, distributed between Kalyana Karnataka Cadre and Residual Parent Cadre as per roster and subject-wise tables in the notification. Candidates should refer to the official notification PDF for detailed subject-wise and cadre-wise vacancy breakup before applying.
Q4. What is the eligibility criteria for Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor in KVAFSU 2026?
For all teaching posts, candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in Veterinary Science / Veterinary Science & Animal Husbandry / Dairy Science / Fisheries Science in the relevant discipline from a recognized SAU/SVU/Deemed/recognized university. Additionally, Professor and Associate Professor require a relevant Master’s degree (55% marks, 50% for SC/ST), Ph.D., research publications and prescribed experience, while Assistant Professor needs a relevant Master’s degree with NET or Ph.D. (as per UGC 2009/2016 NET exemption rules) along with other conditions mentioned in the KVAFSU score card guidelines.
Q5. Is knowledge of Kannada compulsory for KVAFSU Teaching Posts 2026?
Yes, knowledge of Kannada is mandatory for all KVAFSU teaching posts. Candidates who have not studied Kannada at SSLC (10th) level must qualify a Kannada Language Test (one paper, 150 marks, 50% qualifying, SSLC first language standard) before being called for interview.
Q6. What is the age limit for KVAFSU Teaching Recruitment 2026?
The age limit is applicable only for Assistant Professor posts, where the upper age is 40 years for General Merit, 43 years for 2A/2B/3A/3B, and 45 years for SC/ST/Cat-I as on 20 June 2026. No specific upper age limit has been prescribed in the notification for Professor and Associate Professor posts; relaxation for in-service and other categories is as per Government of Karnataka rules.
Q7. What is the application fee for KVAFSU Teaching Recruitment 2026?
The application fee is Rs. 1,000/- for GM, 2A, 2B, 3A, 3B categories and Rs. 500/- for SC/ST/Cat-I candidates, payable online only after submission of the application form. The fee is non-refundable, and a separate application with separate fee is required for each post applied for.
Q8. How are candidates selected in KVAFSU Teaching Recruitment 2026?
Selection is based on Academic Score / Score Card based shortlisting, followed by document verification and interview as per KVAFSU Prescribed Qualifications and Score Card guidelines (No:R/KVAFSU/ACM-CA/Teaching SCD/2025-26). Only details and documents submitted in the online application will be used to compute the Academic Score, and mere eligibility does not guarantee being called for interview.
Q9. How can I apply online for KVAFSU Teaching Posts 2026?
Eligible candidates must apply online only through the KVAFSU recruitment portal available on the official website www.kvafsu.edu.in under Recruitment/Teaching Posts. After registration, candidates should fill the online form, upload required documents (photo, signature, certificates, KVC/VCI registration, reservation certificates, etc.), submit the application and then complete online fee payment; only then will the application be treated as valid.
Q10. Who can apply for Kalyana Karnataka (371-J) Cadre posts in KVAFSU Recruitment 2026?
Kalyana Karnataka Cadre (371-J) posts are reserved exclusively for local candidates of the Kalyana Karnataka region as per Article 371(J) provisions. Local candidates of Kalyana Karnataka can apply for both Local Cadre and Residual Parent Cadre posts, but those appointed in Residual Cadre will not be eligible to claim 371(J) reservation benefits during their entire service.
