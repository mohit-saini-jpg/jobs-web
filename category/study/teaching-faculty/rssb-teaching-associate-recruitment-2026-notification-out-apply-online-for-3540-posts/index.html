Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has officially announced the Teaching Associate Recruitment 2026 with 3540 openings. The detailed notification was published at the official website rssb.rajasthan.gov.in. Candidates who meet the age criteria of 21 to 40 years (age relaxation applicable as per category) and hold a Master’s Degree in the relevant subject with qualifications such as NET/SET/Ph.D. as per norms can now submit their online applications from 05 May 2026 to 03 June 2026. FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for Rajasthan RSSB Teaching Associate Recruitment 2026?
The online application window for Rajasthan RSSB Teaching Associate Recruitment 2026 remains open from 5 May 2026 to 3 June 2026, and candidates must submit their forms within this period through the SSO Recruitment portal.
Q2. How many vacancies are released under RSSB Teaching Associate Recruitment 2026?
The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board has notified a total of 3,540 Teaching Associate (contractual) posts across multiple subjects such as English, Hindi, Geography, Political Science, Science streams, Law and Agriculture disciplines under Advertisement No. 06/2026.
Q3. What is the minimum educational qualification for Rajasthan Teaching Associate 2026?
Candidates must possess a Master’s Degree in the relevant subject from a recognised university, and eligibility will be assessed as per UGC and State Government norms with additional requirements like NET, SET or Ph.D considered as per Raj‑CES rules and detailed notification.
Q4. Is NET/SET compulsory for applying to RSSB Teaching Associate posts?
As per the Raj‑CES Hiring of Man Power Rules 2023 and recruitment notification, NET, SET, SLET or an equivalent research qualification such as Ph.D will be considered according to the prescribed rules, and candidates should refer to the detailed advertisement to confirm whether their specific subject requires mandatory NET/SET.
Q5. What is the age limit for Rajasthan RSSB Teaching Associate Recruitment 2026?
For General male candidates the age limit is 21 to 40 years, while General female and Rajasthan‑domicile SC, ST, OBC, MBC and EWS male candidates get 5 years relaxation and female candidates from these reserved categories can avail up to 10 years relaxation, with additional relaxations for widows, divorced women, ex‑servicemen and PwD candidates as per state rules.
Q6. What is the application fee for RSSB Teaching Associate Recruitment 2026?
The application fee is Rs. 600 for General and Creamy Layer OBC/MBC candidates, and Rs. 400 for Non‑Creamy Layer OBC, MBC, EWS, SC and ST categories as well as PwD candidates, payable online through SSO, e‑Mitra or CSC centres.
Q7. What is the selection process for Rajasthan Teaching Associate posts?
Selection will be done as per Raj‑CES Hiring of Man Power Rules 2023 and is expected to include screening of online applications followed by a written examination or shortlisting and document verification, with the detailed exam pattern and criteria to be notified separately by RSSB.
Q8. How can I apply online for RSSB Teaching Associate Recruitment 2026?
To apply, candidates must log in to the Rajasthan SSO portal, complete or update their One Time Registration, navigate to the Recruitment portal under Citizen Apps, select Advertisement No. 06/2026 for Teaching Associate, fill in all required personal and academic details, upload documents, pay the applicable fee and submit the application before 3 June 2026.
Q9. What is the salary or remuneration for Rajasthan Teaching Associate contractual posts?
As per the latest Teaching Associate discussion and videos based on the 2026 notification, selected candidates are expected to receive a fixed contractual remuneration of around Rs. 28,850 per month, in line with Raj‑CES contractual pay norms for these posts.
Q10. When will the Rajasthan RSSB Teaching Associate exam 2026 be conducted?
The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board has tentatively scheduled the Teaching Associate written examination for the months of August, September or October 2026, and the exact date and timetable will be published later on the official RSSB website.
