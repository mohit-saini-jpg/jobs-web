FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for NIA Jaipur & Panchkula Recruitment 2026?
The last date to apply online for the NIA Jaipur & Panchkula Recruitment 2026 is 20 June 2026. Candidates are advised to submit their applications well before this date.
Q2. How many vacancies are available in the NIA Jaipur & Panchkula Recruitment 2026?
There are a total of 49 vacancies available for various posts, including Professor, Associate Professor, Assistant Professor, and others.
Q3. What is the minimum qualification required for the Professor post?
The minimum qualification for the Professor post is a Post-Graduate Degree in Ayurveda in the concerned subject, along with 15 years of teaching experience and a minimum of 10 research publications.
Q4. What is the application fee for General category candidates?
The application fee for General and OBC applicants varies depending on the post. For Professor and Associate Professor posts, it is Rs. 5,000. For Assistant Professor, Medical Officer, Panchakarma Vaidya, and Radiologist, it is Rs. 4,000. For other posts, it is Rs. 3,000.
Q5. What is the maximum age limit for the Assistant Professor post?
The maximum age limit for the Assistant Professor post is 40 years, with age relaxations applicable for SC/ST, OBC, PH, and Ex-Servicemen categories.
Q6. What is the selection process for these posts?
The selection process typically includes Preliminary Tests, a Mains Examination, and an Interview.
Q7. Can candidates from other states apply for this recruitment?
Yes, candidates from all over India are eligible to apply, provided they meet the specified eligibility criteria for the respective posts.
Q8. What is the salary offered for the Professor post?
The Professor post is offered a salary in Pay Level 13, which ranges from Rs. 1,23,100 to Rs. 2,15,900, plus NPA and other allowances.
Q9. Is there any age relaxation for reserved categories?
Yes, age relaxation is provided for SC/ST (5 years), OBC (3 years), PH (10 years), and Ex-Servicemen (3 years over and above service period) categories.
Q10. Where can I find the official notification for this recruitment?
The official notification PDF can be downloaded from the 'Important Links' section of this article, or directly from the official website of the National Institute of Ayurveda.
