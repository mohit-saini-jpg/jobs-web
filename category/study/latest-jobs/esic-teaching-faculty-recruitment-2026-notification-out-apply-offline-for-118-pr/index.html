The latest Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Professor (Teaching Faculty – Medical Specialties) posts! Published on 19 May 2026 at www.esic.gov.in, this new recruitment drive offers 118 openings for eligible candidates. Applicants within the age group of up to 50 years (relaxations as applicable for SC/ST/OBC/PwBD/ESIC employees) and possessing the required medical or postgraduate qualifications can apply offline via the prescribed application form from now until 27 July 2026 (03 August 2026 for remote area candidates). Important Instructions

  • ESIC follows Government of India rules on age relaxation, community reservation and PwBD concessions.
  • SC/ST – up to 5 years; OBC – up to 3 years; PwBD – up to 10 years (maximum age on closing date not exceeding 56 years), with cumulative relaxation for SC/ST/OBC PwBD as per norms. Ex‑Servicemen and ECOs/SSCOs – age relaxation up to 5 years subject to conditions on length of service and release certificates. Only candidates belonging to castes/communities in the Central List of SC/ST/OBC and having valid, current certificates in the prescribed format will get reservation benefits; multiple applications in different categories will lead to rejection.
  • Eligibility (age, qualifications, experience etc.) is calculated with reference to the closing date of applications – 27.07.2026 for most candidates. Mere submission of application or call for interview does not confer any right to appointment; candidature remains provisional until verification. Incomplete/unsigned applications, or those not in prescribed proforma, without required documents, fee, or received after the last date will be rejected without communication. Any false statement or suppression of material information can lead to cancellation of candidature/appointment at any stage. Selected candidates will be on probation as per ESIC rules and are liable to be posted anywhere in India; state preferences are only indicative and not binding.

FAQs

Q1. What is ESIC Professor Recruitment 2026?
ESIC Professor Recruitment 2026 is a direct recruitment drive by Employees' State Insurance Corporation under the Ministry of Labour & Employment, Government of India, to fill 118 Professor posts in various medical specialties across ESIC Post Graduate Institutes of Medical Sciences & Research (PGIMSRs) and ESIC Medical Colleges.
Q2. How many vacancies are available in ESIC Professor Recruitment 2026?
A total of 118 Professor vacancies are available across 22 medical specialties including General Medicine (8), General Surgery (8), Orthopedics (8), Transfusion Medicine (9), Anatomy (7), Anesthesiology (7), and others, distributed as UR-35, SC-25, ST-13, OBC-37, EWS-8, with 8 PwBD posts.
Q3. What is the last date to apply for ESIC Professor 2026?
The last date for receipt of offline applications is 27 July 2026 for all India candidates, and 03 August 2026 for candidates from remote areas (NE States, J&K Ladakh division, specified HP districts, Andaman & Nicobar, Lakshadweep).
Q4. What is the eligibility for ESIC Professor 2026?
Candidates must have a recognized medical qualification (MBBS/equivalent) with MD/MS or equivalent in the concerned specialty, plus four years teaching experience as Reader/Associate Professor OR three years as Associate Professor with minimum four research publications (two as first author during Associate Professor tenure).
Q5. What is the age limit for ESIC Professor 2026?
Maximum age is 50 years as on 27 July 2026 for UR/EWS candidates, with relaxation of 5 years for SC/ST, 3 years for OBC, 10 years for PwBD, and up to 5 years for ESIC/Government servants, subject to cumulative maximum limits.
Q6. What is the selection process for ESIC Professor 2026?
Selection is based on Interview by ESIC Selection Board with possible shortlisting based on teaching experience beyond minimum requirement. Minimum qualifying marks: UR/EWS-50, OBC-45, SC/ST/PwBD-40 out of 100 marks.
Q7. Is there any application fee for ESIC Professor 2026?
Yes, application fee is ₹500 via Demand Draft/Banker's Cheque in favour of "ESI Fund Account No. 2" payable at Delhi, for General/OBC/EWS/ESIC male candidates. SC/ST/PwBD/Women/Ex-Servicemen are exempted from fee payment.
Q8. What is the salary for ESIC Professor 2026?
Professors receive Pay Level-13 in the Pay Matrix (₹1,23,100 – ₹2,15,900) plus Non-Practicing Allowance (NPA), Dearness Allowance (DA), House Rent Allowance (HRA), Transport Allowance and other admissible Central Government allowances.
