How to Apply

  • 1Applications must be submitted through online mode at https://jssc.jharkhand.gov.in/ only. No hard copy of the application or documents should be sent to the JSSC office. Candidates should not wait until the last date and must submit applications well in advance.
  • 2Step-by-Step Process:
  • 3Visit https://jssc.jharkhand.gov.in/ and log in to apply online.
  • 4Read the notification/brochure carefully before filling the form.
  • 5Keep scanned copies of your photograph, full signature and all required documents ready before filling the form.
  • 6Fill in details as per your Matriculation/10th certificate (name spelling must match exactly).
  • 7Pay the examination fee online; select JTGLCCE-2026 under Select Payment Category and enter your Registration Number.
  • 8The Admit Card will be uploaded on the JSSC website; it will not be sent by post. Candidates must download it themselves.
  • 9All examination-related announcements will be made on the JSSC website only.
  • 10Documents Required:
  • 11Photograph and full signature (scanned, for upload)
  • 12Matriculation/10th Board Certificate (for DOB and name proof)
  • 13Educational qualification marksheets and degree certificates
  • 14Valid Caste Certificate in prescribed format (for SC/ST/OBC/EBC candidates - must be issued after 02.06.2016 and from Sub-Divisional level or above)
  • 15Local Resident Certificate (Sthaniy Niwas Praman Patra) in prescribed format
  • 16PwD Certificate in Govt. of Jharkhand prescribed format (Appendix-X) with minimum 40% disability
  • 17Sports quota documents (as per Resolution No. 1709, dated 12.09.2007) - if applicable
  • 18Discharge Certificate (for Ex-Servicemen)
  • 19Income & Asset Certificate for EWS (if applicable)
  • 20Apply Online at: https://jssc.jharkhand.gov.in/application-forms-apply

Important Instructions

  • Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply. Admission to the exam is purely provisional. Issuance of Admit Card does not confirm eligibility; JSSC will verify certificates after the main exam. Mobile phones, pagers, Bluetooth devices or any electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited inside the examination centre; candidature will be cancelled if found. Candidates debarred by UPSC / JPSC / JSSC or any other selection commission within the specified period are not eligible to appear. A re-evaluation fee of Rs. 500/- can be applied within 7 days of result declaration. If mass copying is reported and proved at a centre, JSSC reserves the right to cancel the entire exam at that centre. Candidates currently employed in Govt./PSU/Autonomous bodies need not submit NOC at application stage but must produce a proper relieving order at the time of joining. The correction window (05-07-2026 to 10-07-2026) allows changes to all fields except Name, Date of Birth, Email ID and Mobile Number. If a category is changed (e.g. from reserved to unreserved), the difference in exam fee must be paid. Candidates applying for both Advertisement No. 01/2026 and 02/2026 need to pay only one exam fee. Candidates applying under Sports Quota must note the eligible standards as per Resolution No. 1709, dated 12.09.2007, available on the JSSC website. All selections, notices and merit lists will be published only on the JSSC official website. Candidates are advised to check regularly. Any dispute regarding the recruitment will be settled within the jurisdiction of Ranchi only.

FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for JSSC JTGLCCE Recruitment 2026?
The last date to complete online registration and application submission is 30 June 2026. Candidates must also complete fee payment and document upload by 03 July 2026 as per the schedule. It is advisable to apply early to avoid last-minute issues.
Q2. How many vacancies are announced under JSSC JTGLCCE 2026?
A total of 611 vacancies have been announced across various posts under different departments of the Jharkhand Government. These include technical, scientific, and administrative roles. Additional backlog vacancies are also available under a separate advertisement.
Q3. What is the eligibility qualification for JSSC JTGLCCE 2026?
The required educational qualification varies depending on the post applied for. Candidates must possess a relevant graduate or postgraduate degree in disciplines like Science, Mathematics, Economics, Agriculture, Fisheries, Dairy Technology, or Pharmacy. All qualifications must be completed before the last date of application.
Q4. What is the age limit for JSSC Recruitment 2026?
The minimum age is 21 years for all posts, while the maximum age ranges from 35 to 40 years depending on category. Age relaxation is provided for reserved categories, PwD candidates, and ex-servicemen as per Jharkhand Government rules. Age will be calculated as on 01 August 2025.
Q5. What is the selection process for JSSC JTGLCCE 2026?
The selection process includes a Computer Based Test (CBT) conducted in two stages: Preliminary Exam and Main Exam. In some cases, the preliminary exam may be skipped if the number of candidates is low. Final merit is based on marks obtained in the Main Examination.
Q6. Is there negative marking in JSSC JTGLCCE Exam 2026?
Yes, there is a negative marking system in the examination. For every incorrect answer, 1 mark will be deducted, while each correct answer carries 3 marks. Candidates should attempt questions carefully to avoid losing marks.
Q7. What is the application fee for JSSC JTGLCCE 2026?
General, EWS, OBC and candidates from other states must pay Rs. 100 as application fee. SC/ST candidates of Jharkhand need to pay Rs. 50, while PwD candidates are exempted from the fee. The fee must be paid online and is non-refundable.
Q8. How can I apply for JSSC JTGLCCE Recruitment 2026?
Candidates need to apply online through the official JSSC website by registering and filling the application form. They must upload required documents, pay the fee, and submit the form before the deadline. No hard copy submission is required for this recruitment.
Q9. What is the exam pattern for JSSC JTGLCCE 2026?
The Preliminary Exam consists of 120 multiple-choice questions covering General Studies, Jharkhand GK, Mathematics, Science, and Mental Ability. The Main Exam has three papers including Hindi, regional language, and technical subject with general studies. The exam is conducted in bilingual mode.
Q10. Where can I download the JSSC JTGLCCE 2026 Notification PDF?
Candidates can download the official notification PDF from the Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission website. The notification provides complete details regarding vacancies, eligibility, syllabus, and application procedure. It is important to read it carefully before applying.
