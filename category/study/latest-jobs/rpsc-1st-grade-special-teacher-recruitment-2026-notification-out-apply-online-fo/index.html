Age Limit

DetailsMinimum age: 21 years and Maximum age: 40 years as on 01-01-2027. Since the post is being filled under Madhyamik Shiksha Vibhag (Class 1-II) as per prevailing rules effective 12.11.2025, no additional age relaxation of 03 years (under Rule 16(ix) of Rajasthan State and Subordinate Services (Direct Recruitment) Rules) is applicable. Maximum age limit for any candidate (including all relaxations) shall not exceed 52 years (per Rajasthan rules). Age relaxation is non-cumulative — only one of the applicable relaxations (whichever is highest) will apply.

Qualification / Eligibility

Candidates must fulfill all eligibility conditions as on the last date of online application i.e. 30-06-2026. Prior/provisional registration with RCI (Rehabilitation Council of India) before the date of examination is mandatory. Applicants must be permanently/provisionally registered with RCI before the date of recruitment examination and must provide their RCI registration number while applying. Candidates should possess the required educational qualification by the closing date of application.

  • 1Candidates must apply online only at https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in. Important Instructions

Candidates must be permanently/provisionally registered with RCI before the date of recruitment examination — RCI registration number is mandatory in the online application. OTR (One Time Registration) on SSO Portal is mandatory before applying. Details in OTR (Name, Father's Name, DOB, Gender) must match Aadhaar/Janaadhar and cannot be changed post-OTR without Aadhaar correction. The application once submitted is final. Any correction in the online application can only be made online within 10 days of the closing date by paying Rs. 500/- (correction fee is non-refundable and no further correction will be accepted after this window). Providing false or incorrect information is a punishable offence under BNS Section 217 — candidates may be debarred and face criminal prosecution. Candidates who are absent in 02 or more recruitment examinations conducted by any Commission/Board during 01 April to 31 March will have their online application access blocked. Re-activation requires payment of Rs. 750/- (first block) or Rs. 1500/- (subsequent blocks) via OTR. Ekabari Panjeeyan Shulk (One Time Registration Fee) once paid is non-refundable. Reservation benefits apply only to bonafide Rajasthan State residents with valid certificates issued by the Rajasthan Government Competent Authority. Candidates from other states belonging to SC/ST/OBC must apply under General category. OBC (BC) candidates of Rajasthan must submit an undertaking (as and when required) along with their BC certificate confirming they are not in the Creamy Layer up to the closing date of application. No mobile phones, electronic devices, watches, or jewellery are allowed inside the examination centre. Negative marking applies — 1/3rd mark deducted per wrong answer. If no option is filled in more than 10% of questions, the candidate will be disqualified. OMR Answer Sheet must be filled using Blue Ball Point Pen only — darken only one bubble per question. If not attempting a question, darken circle '5'. PwBD candidates requiring a scribe must submit disability certificate and scribe undertaking/vow-letter to the Centre Superintendent at least 2 days before the exam date (3 days before for those seeking scribe from RPSC). Scribe facility and compensatory time are as per Rajasthan Divyangjan Adhikar Niyam, 2018. Age limit relaxation provisions are non-cumulative — only the highest single relaxation applicable will be given. All notifications, dates, results, and corrigenda will be published only on https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in — candidates must visit the website regularly. Success in the examination does not confer right to appointment — appointment is subject to Police Verification, Character Verification, and medical fitness. The Commission's decision in all matters relating to acceptance/rejection of applications, eligibility, and selection is final.
