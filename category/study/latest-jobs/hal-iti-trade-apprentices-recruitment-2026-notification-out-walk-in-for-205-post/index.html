Important Instructions

Selection is purely merit-based on SSC/10th marks (70% weightage) and ITI marks (30% weightage); there will be no written test or interview. Registration on www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in is mandatory before appearing for the walk-in interview. Candidates must upload SSC Marks Certificate, ITI Marks Certificate (all semesters), Aadhaar card number, photograph, signature, and KYC verification on the portal. Candidates must enter their name as it appears in the SSC/10th Certificate on the portal. Candidates who have already undergone or are currently undergoing Apprenticeship Training or registered for Apprentice Training elsewhere under the Apprentices Act are not eligible. Candidates who have passed Apprenticeship under the Centre of Excellence (COE) scheme need not apply. Candidates who have already joined/are pursuing/are about to join any regular courses for higher education during the stipulated Training Period need not apply. OBC candidates must produce a valid Non-Creamy Layer certificate. Merit list of selected candidates will be published on www.hal-india.co.in on 26th June 2026. Date of joining/reporting for the Apprenticeship is 17th July 2026; failure to report by the scheduled date will result in forfeiture of candidature. Submission of false/incorrect information or suppression of any facts at any stage will result in immediate cancellation/termination of candidature. Canvassing in any form will be treated as disqualification. HAL reserves the right to alter the allocation of quota for trades at any time and to cancel the advertisement/selection process; HAL Management's decision regarding selection will be final. Candidates must make their own arrangements for accommodation and travelling for the walk-in process and joining. For further queries/clarifications, email: [email protected] or contact: 040-23778283 (09:00 Hrs to 16:00 Hrs). For issues related to the web portal www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in (operated by DGT, Govt. of India), candidates must contact the helpline numbers on the portal; HAL will not entertain correspondence regarding portal functionality.
