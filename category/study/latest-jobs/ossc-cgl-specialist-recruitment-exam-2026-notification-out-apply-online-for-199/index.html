Important Instructions

  • Appointment shall be on a regular basis carrying the level of pay as mentioned in the vacancy table; all posts are State Cadre posts and candidates may be asked to serve anywhere in Odisha. Candidates must have the requisite qualification for the post on the last date of submission of the Online Application Form (11-07-2026). Special category candidates must also have the necessary supporting documents by this date. Only one Online Application is allowed per candidate for any recruitment examination. Multiple applications will lead to cancellation of candidature and forfeiture of fee. After final submission of the corrected/final Online Application, no change/correction/modification will be allowed under any circumstances - requests through post, fax, e-mail or in person shall be summarily rejected. Candidates must write their Name, Date of Birth, Father's name and Mother's name exactly as given in the Matriculation Certificate. Marks sharing: Marks obtained in Preliminary Examination, Main Examination and Computer Skill Test are proposed to be shared with the candidate after final merit list is published. Candidates who score more than the last selection mark at any tier/stage but are not qualified for the subsequent stage must represent to the Commission within 05 days of declaration of that result through e-mail [email protected]. Government servants (State/Central) or those working under PSUs/Government Universities/Autonomous Bodies must obtain and upload a "No Objection Certificate" from their controlling authority with the Online Application Form. If a standing order of NOC exists, the same should be uploaded. Candidates who hide their Govt. servant/PSU status during certificate verification may not be considered for recommendation. Admission to the examination at all stages is provisional. Copies of supporting documents will be sought at the Certificate Verification stage; if any claim in the application is found unsubstantiated, candidature will be cancelled. No Admission Letter shall be sent by post; candidates must download it from www.ossc.gov.in. There shall be no provision for re-evaluation/re-checking of scores. No correspondence in this regard shall be entertained. Mobile phones, smartwatches, Bluetooth devices, spy cameras, and any other electronic/communication devices are not allowed inside the examination premises. The decision of the Commission in all matters relating to eligibility, acceptance or rejection of applications, mode and conduct of examinations, allotment of examination centres, manner of selection and preparation of merit list and post allocation, and debarment for malpractices will be final and binding. Canvassing in any form will result in disqualification.

FAQs

Q1. What is OSSC CGLRE Specialist-2025 Recruitment 2026?
OSSC CGLRE Specialist-2025 Recruitment 2026 is the Combined Graduate Level Recruitment Examination by Odisha Staff Selection Commission under Advertisement No. IIE-46/2025/5919/OSSC to fill 199 Group-B and Group-C specialist posts across various Government Departments of Odisha.
Q2. How many vacancies are available in OSSC CGLRE Specialist-2025 Recruitment 2026?
A total of 199 vacancies are available across 16 posts including Food Safety Officer (84), Librarian in DIETs/BIETs (30), Assistant Embankment Inspector (23), Laboratory Assistant (20), Senior Economic Investigator (16), and other specialist positions, with 52 seats reserved for women.
Q3. What is the last date to apply for OSSC CGLRE Specialist-2025 Recruitment 2026?
The last date for online application submission is 11 July 2026, with applications opening on 09 June 2026 and an application correction window from 29 June to 14 July 2026.
Q4. What is the eligibility for OSSC CGLRE Specialist-2025 Recruitment 2026?
Eligibility varies by post: candidates must have a Bachelor's or Master's degree in relevant disciplines (Food Technology, Economics, Science, Commerce, Library Science, Fine Arts, Computer Science, etc.) from AICTE/UGC-recognized institutions and must be able to read, write, and speak Odia fluently.
Q5. What is the age limit for OSSC CGLRE Specialist-2025 Recruitment 2026?
Candidates must be between 21 to 42 years as on 01 January 2025, with 5 years relaxation for SEBC/SC/ST/Women and 10 years relaxation for PwD candidates, plus additional relaxation for Ex-Servicemen.
Q6. What is the selection process for OSSC CGLRE Specialist-2025 Recruitment 2026?
The selection process includes Stage-I Preliminary Examination (150 marks, 150 questions), Stage-II Main Written Examination Technical Paper (200 marks, 100 questions) plus Computer Skill Test (qualifying), and Stage-III Certificate Verification, with negative marking at all stages.
Q7. Is there any application fee for OSSC CGLRE Specialist-2025 Recruitment 2026?
Yes, examination fee is ₹500 for General/OBC/EWS candidates (refundable to those who appear in Preliminary Examination) payable through Odisha Treasury portal, while SC/ST/PwD candidates are exempted from fee payment.
Q8. What is the salary for OSSC CGLRE Specialist-2025 posts?
Group-B posts (Food Safety Officer, Senior Economic Investigator, Librarian, etc.) offer Pay Matrix Level-9, Cell-1 of ORSP 2017, while Group-C posts offer Level-4 to Level-8, Cell-1 depending on the position.
Q9. Is Odia language knowledge mandatory for OSSC CGLRE Specialist-2025 Recruitment 2026?
Yes, candidates must be able to read, write, and speak Odia fluently and must have passed Middle School with Odia as subject, or Matriculation with Odia as medium, or Class-VII exam with Odia as subject, or ME standard Odia test conducted by School and Mass Education Department.
Q10. How to apply for OSSC CGLRE Specialist-2025 Recruitment 2026?
Candidates must apply online only through the official OSSC website ossc.gov.in from 09 June 2026 to 11 July 2026 by completing registration, uploading required documents including educational certificates, paying the examination fee, and submitting the application form.
