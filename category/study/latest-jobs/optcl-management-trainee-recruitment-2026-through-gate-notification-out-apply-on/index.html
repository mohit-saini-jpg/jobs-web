Important Instructions

Selected candidates will be required to execute a service bond of Rs. 3,00,000/- (applicable for all categories) to successfully complete the training period and thereafter serve OPTCL for at least three years. The bond amount is payable if the candidate resigns or leaves service during the stipulated bond period for any reason whatsoever. During the 1-year training period, a stipend of Rs. 50,000/- per month will be paid. On successful completion, candidates will be absorbed as Assistant Manager (Electrical) in Pay Matrix Level EE-2 (Rs. 56,100 – Rs. 1,77,500) with CTC of approximately Rs. 15 Lakhs per annum. If performance during training is found unsatisfactory, the training period will be extended for a further six months. If performance is still not satisfactory after the extension, the service will be terminated. After successful completion of training, candidates will be on probation for a further period of twelve months. Selected candidates are liable to be posted at any existing or future unit of OPTCL throughout Odisha and outside, as per organizational requirements. SC, ST, and SEBC concessions are admissible only to candidates belonging to Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, and SEBC of Odisha State by birth. SEBC certificate must be the latest certificate (dated on or after 01-05-2025). SEBC certificates issued after 25-06-2026 will not be accepted. Candidates must correctly declare their name and date of birth as per High School Certificate / Birth Certificate. Date of birth as entered in the High School Certificate / Birth Certificate issued by the concerned authority will only be accepted. Candidature is provisional throughout the process and liable to be rejected or appointment terminated at any stage if any information is found false, misleading, or amounts to suppression of material facts. OPTCL will not undertake detailed scrutiny of applications for eligibility during shortlisting; candidates must verify their own eligibility before applying. If any claim is not substantiated at the time of scrutiny, candidature will be cancelled and OPTCL's decision shall be final. All information regarding this recruitment process will be made available only on the career section of www.optcl.co.in. Candidates must periodically check the website for updates. Legal jurisdiction in case of any cause or dispute shall be the Hon'ble High Court of Orissa. Management reserves the right to cancel, restrict, enlarge, modify, or alter the recruitment/selection process at any stage without issuing any notice or assigning any reason.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date to apply for OPTCL Management Trainee (Electrical) Recruitment 2026?
A
The last date for online application submission and fee payment is 25 June 2026 (11:59 PM). Applications will open on 26 May 2026 at 4:00 PM. Only online applications through www.optcl.co.in will be accepted.
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are available in OPTCL Management Trainee Recruitment 2026?
A
A total of 60 vacancies (including 20 reserved for Women) have been announced for Management Trainee (Electrical) posts. Additionally, there are backlog posts for Ex-Servicemen (9 posts), PwBD (6 posts), and Sports Persons (over and above the 60 advertised posts).
Q3Q3. Is GATE 2026 score mandatory for OPTCL Management Trainee Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, a valid GATE 2026 score in the Electrical Engineering (EE) paper is absolutely mandatory. Only GATE 2026 normalized marks (out of 100) are accepted. GATE 2025 or any prior year's scores will not be considered for this recruitment.
Q4Q4. What is the salary/stipend for OPTCL Management Trainee posts?
A
During the 1-year training period, candidates will receive a monthly stipend of Rs. 50,000/-. After successful completion of training, they will be absorbed as Assistant Manager (Electrical) in Pay Matrix Level EE-2 (Rs. 56,100 – Rs. 1,77,500) with an approximate CTC of Rs. 15 Lakhs per annum.
Q5Q5. What is the age limit for OPTCL Management Trainee Recruitment 2026?
A
Candidates must be between 21 to 32 years as on 01-05-2026. Age relaxation is available: 5 years for SC/ST/SEBC/Women (for reserved posts), 5 years for Ex-Servicemen, and 10 years for PwBD candidates (cumulative with category relaxation). OPTCL departmental employees have no upper age bar.
Q6Q6. What educational qualification is required for OPTCL Management Trainee posts?
A
Candidates must possess a Degree in Electrical / Electrical & Electronics Engineering with minimum 60% marks in aggregate (50% for SC/ST/PwBD) from a recognized University/Institution as on 25-06-2026. Alternatively, passing Section A & B of IE (India) in relevant discipline with minimum 50% marks is acceptable.
Q7Q7. Is Odia language proficiency mandatory for this recruitment?
A
Yes, Odia language proficiency is absolutely mandatory. Candidates must be able to read, write, and speak Odia fluently and must have passed Middle School Exam with Odia as language subject, or Matriculation with Odia as medium, or Class VII final exam with Odia as subject from a recognized school, or passed a test in Odia conducted by School and Mass Education Department/BSE Odisha.
Q8Q8. What is the selection process for OPTCL Management Trainee Recruitment 2026?
A
Selection is based on GATE 2026 score (90% weightage) and Personal Interview (10% weightage). Candidates will be shortlisted category-wise based on GATE 2026 normalized marks for interview. Final appointment is subject to medical fitness and verification of antecedents. No separate written examination is conducted by OPTCL.
