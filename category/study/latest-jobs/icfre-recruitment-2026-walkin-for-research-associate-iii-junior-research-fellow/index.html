Qualification / Eligibility

Research Associate-III (Ecology & Sustainable Land Management): Ph.D. in Forestry / Ecology / Environmental Science / Natural Resource Management / Restoration Ecology or related disciplines OR M.Sc. in Forestry / Ecology / Environmental Science / Natural Resource Management or related disciplines with NET (CSIR/UGC/ASRB) and a minimum of 5 years of research / project experience. Research Associate-III (Web / Knowledge Platform Support): B.Tech. / M.Tech. / MCA / M.Sc. in Computer Science / IT / Web Technologies or related disciplines, with a minimum of 3–5 years of experience in development of web platforms / portals / LMS / dashboards / UI-UX systems. Database Manager (Digital Systems & Dashboard Support): B.Tech. / M.Tech. / MCA / Diploma in Information Technology / M.Sc. in Computer Science / Computer Applications / Information Technology / Data Science / Geoinformatics or related disciplines, with a minimum of 2–3 years of experience in database development, web-based data handling, software / dashboard / digital platform support. Junior Research Fellow (JRF): Postgraduate degree in Forestry / Environmental Science / Ecology / Botany / GIS / Information Technology or related disciplines with NET (UGC/CSIR/ASRB) / GATE.
