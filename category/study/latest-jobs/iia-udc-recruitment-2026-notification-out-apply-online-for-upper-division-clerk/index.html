  • 3Log in using your registered username and password to fill the online application form.

FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for IIA UDC Recruitment 2026?
The last date to submit the online application is 29 June 2026 up to 05:30 PM.
Q2. How many vacancies are available in IIA UDC Recruitment 2026?
A total of 5 vacancies are available for Upper Division Clerk posts (UR-02, OBC-02, SC-01).
Q3. What is the salary for IIA Upper Division Clerk 2026?
The salary is Level-04 pay scale ranging from ₹25,500 to ₹81,100 as per the 7th CPC.
Q4. What is the qualification required for IIA UDC posts?
Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree and knowledge of computer applications, including MS Office.
Q5. What is the age limit for IIA UDC Recruitment 2026?
The maximum age limit is 27 years as on the closing date, with relaxation for reserved categories as per Government rules.
Q6. What is the selection process for IIA UDC 2026?
Selection includes screening of applications, a written test (covering English, reasoning, GA, accounts, and rules), and a qualifying skill test.
Q7. Where will the IIA UDC exam be conducted?
The written test and skill test will be conducted in Bengaluru only.
Q8. Is the IIA UDC post permanent?
Yes, it is a regular post with a probation period of 2 years under the National Pension System (NPS).
Q9. How can I apply for IIA UDC Recruitment 2026?
Candidates must apply online through the official IIA website at iiap.res.in by registering and submitting the application form.
Q10. What are the job responsibilities of an IIA Upper Division Clerk?
The role includes handling administration, establishment, accounts, finance, stores, and procurement-related work.
