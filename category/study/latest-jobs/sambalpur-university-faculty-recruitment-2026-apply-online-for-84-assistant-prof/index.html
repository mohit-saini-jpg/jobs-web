How to Apply

  • 1Applications must be submitted online only through the HIMS Portal available at https://himsrecruitment.odisha.gov.in/home, which will be available from 15-05-2026. The online application can be filled in multiple sessions. Candidates willing to apply for more than one post must apply separately and pay processing fee for each application. No other application or CV should be sent by email or any other means.
  • 2After submitting the online application, candidates must download the PDF of the submitted application generated by the HIMS portal and send the hard copy along with all supporting documents via Registered Post/Speed Post to reach the University on or before 26-06-2026. The envelope must be superscribed "APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF ……………………………." along with the Advertisement No. and Date. No hard copy will be considered if the applicant fails to submit the online application on or before the closing date.
  • 3Persons employed in Government/Public Sector Enterprise/Autonomous Bodies must forward a copy of their application through proper channel or furnish a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the competent authority or employer. Important Instructions

Candidates above 60 years of age are not eligible to apply. Applications must be submitted online only through the HIMS portal; no application by email or any other means will be accepted. Candidates must apply separately for each post and pay the application fee separately for each post. The hard copy of the application along with all supporting documents must reach the University on or before 26-06-2026. No hard copy will be considered if the online application is not submitted on or before the closing date (16-06-2026). Application fee is non-refundable; applications without applicable fee will be summarily rejected. Application without signature will not be considered and will be rejected without refund of application processing fee. The period spent to acquire M.Phil. and/or Ph.D. degree shall not be considered as research experience. Only matriculation/SSC/pass certificate issued by the concerned educational board will be accepted as proof of Date of Birth. SC/ST/SEBC candidates must submit proper caste certificate as per Govt. of Odisha guidelines and format. PWD category candidates must submit Disability Certificate issued by competent authority in the prescribed format. Exchange of reservation is not permitted. In service candidates must forward hard copy through proper channel or attach NOC from the competent authority. If more than one application is submitted for the same post, only the application submitted last within the closing date shall be considered; partial applications are not acceptable. Mere possession of minimum prescribed qualifications does not entitle the candidate to be called for written examination/interview. Documents should be uploaded in PDF/Image format with readability ensured; photograph should be uploaded in JPEG format with a size less than 200 KB. No TA/DA shall be paid for attending the interview. Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification. In cases of disputes, legal proceedings against the University shall be restricted to Courts in Sambalpur, Odisha. Any change or amendment shall be displayed only on the University website; candidates are advised to visit www.suniv.ac.in regularly and remain updated. The University reserves the right to revise/reschedule/cancel/suspend the advertisement/recruitment process without assigning any reason. Decisions of the University shall be final and binding. The University reserves the right not to fill up any or some of the vacancies advertised without assigning any reason.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026 notification about?
A
Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026 notification is for regular Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor posts in various Departments/Subjects/Colleges under Sambalpur University, Jyoti Vihar, Burla, Odisha, as per UGC Regulations 2018 and Odisha Revised Scales of Pay for University Teachers, 2019.
Q2Q2. What are the important dates for Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The online application portal on the HIMS recruitment website opens on 15 May 2026 and closes on 16 June 2026 (midnight), while the hard copy of the application with documents must reach the University by 26 June 2026; the interview schedule will be notified later on the official website.
Q3Q3. How many vacancies are released for Sambalpur University Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor posts in 2026?
A
Sambalpur University has notified 20 Professor posts, 30 Associate Professor posts and 34 Assistant Professor posts across multiple departments including Business Administration, Chemistry, Computer Science, Earth Science, Economics, English, History, Law, Life Science, Odia, Physics, Sociology and others with category-wise reservations such as UR, SC, ST, SEBC and Women sub‑reservation.
Q4Q4. What is the age limit for Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
For Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026, candidates must be below 60 years of age as on the closing date of online application submission, and applicants above 60 years are not eligible to apply.
Q5Q5. What are the eligibility criteria for Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor at Sambalpur University?
A
Eligibility is as per UGC Regulations Notification F.1‑2/2017(EC/PS) dated 18 July 2018, which generally requires a Ph.D. in the concerned/allied/relevant subject, specified research publications and research score for Professor and Associate Professor, and for Assistant Professor a Master’s degree with prescribed marks along with NET/SLET/SET or Ph.D. as per UGC norms, with equivalent options for Performing Arts, Visual Arts and traditional art forms.
Q6Q6. What is the pay scale for Sambalpur University Faculty posts in 2026?
A
The pay scales are as per Odisha Revised Scales of Pay for University Teachers (UGC Scale of Pay) Rule, 2019, where Professor posts are in Cell‑1 of Level‑14, Associate Professor posts in Cell‑1 of Level‑13A and Assistant Professor posts in Cell‑1 of Level‑10, along with admissible dearness allowance and other allowances sanctioned by the Government of Odisha.
Q7Q7. How can I apply online for Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
Eligible candidates must apply online only through the HIMS portal at the Odisha HIMS recruitment site during 15 May 2026 to 16 June 2026, then download the submitted application PDF and send the signed hard copy with all supporting documents by Registered/Speed Post to the Registrar, Sambalpur University, Jyoti Vihar, Burla, Sambalpur – 768019, Odisha within 10 days of closure of the online application.
Q8Q8. What is the application fee for Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The non‑refundable application processing fee must be paid online as per HIMS portal instructions, where for Professor posts General/SEBC candidates pay Rs. 2000 and SC/ST/Women candidates pay Rs. 1000, while for Associate Professor and Assistant Professor posts General/SEBC candidates pay Rs. 1000 and SC/ST/Women candidates pay Rs. 600, and PwD candidates are exempted from fee payment.
Q9Q9. What is the selection process for Sambalpur University Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A
The selection process includes three‑stage screening/scrutiny of applications followed by interview, with shortlisting and evaluation based on UGC Regulations 2018 (Appendix II Tables 2 and 3A), and candidates are assessed on cognitive, psychomotor and affective domains along with their vision to contribute to developed Odisha and Vikashit Bharat; no TA/DA is paid for attending interviews.
Q10Q10. Is there any specific PPT requirement for the Sambalpur University Faculty interview?
A
Yes, candidates called for interview must bring a 9‑slide PPT (Arial Black font, size 24) without introduction or thank‑you slide, covering biodata, subject expertise, unique skills, plans to empower students, use of AI in pedagogy, research projects, consultancies and outreach activities linked to SDGs, possible collaborations, and strategies to enhance employability and entrepreneurship.
