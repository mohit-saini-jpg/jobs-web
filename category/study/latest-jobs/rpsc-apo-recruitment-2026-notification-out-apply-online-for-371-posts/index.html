How to Apply

  • 1Candidates shall apply online only; no other mode of application will be accepted. Before applying, candidates must complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the SSO portal and read the detailed guidelines and instructions for applicants available on the RPSC website.
  • 2Candidates should have a valid email ID and active mobile number which should be kept active till declaration of results.
  • 3Visit the official RPSC website: https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in and read the complete notification and Instructions for Applicants before applying.
  • 4Click on the Apply Online link or log in to the SSO portal at https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in using your SSO ID to access the Recruitment Portal (G2C portal).
  • 5If you are a new user, complete One Time Registration (OTR) by providing your name, father's name (if available from Aadhaar), date of birth, gender, and Aadhaar card / Janaadhar card details. Upload a live self-photo and scan of OTR documents.
  • 6After completing OTR, log in from SSO portal → Citizen Apps (G2C) → Recruitment Portal and fill the online application form using your OTR number.
  • 7Important: No amendment to name, father's name, date of birth, or gender can be made in OTR Profile once created without Aadhaar/Janaadhar correction. Ensure all Aadhaar/Janaadhar details are corrected before completing OTR.
  • 8During online application, a Live Photo will be captured via OTP camera. Submit the form only after verifying the live photo is clear.
  • 9Candidates must scan and upload thumb impression (left hand) and signature along with the online application.
  • 10Candidates who wish to avail scribe facility must mandatorily select the scribe option in their online application form.
  • 11All educational qualification and experience details as at the application closing date must be clearly entered in the OTR/application form.
  • 12After submission, an Application Number will be generated. If no Application Number is received, it means the form has not been submitted successfully.
  • 13Candidates must take a printout of the finally submitted application form for future reference.
  • 14For any technical issues during online application, contact the Helpdesk number on the Recruitment Portal or email as indicated on the portal.
  • 15Call Letter (Admit Card) downloaded from RPSC website along with valid Photo ID proof (Aadhaar / PAN Card / Passport / Driving License / Voter ID / Bank Passbook with photo) in original and self-attested photocopy.
  • 16Caste Certificate in prescribed Government of India / Government of Rajasthan format (for SC/ST/OBC/MBC candidates).
  • 17OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in prescribed format issued for the current year (for OBC NCL candidates).
  • 18Income and Asset Certificate issued by Competent Authority in prescribed format (for EWS candidates).
  • 19Disability Certificate in prescribed format issued by Competent Authority (for PwBD candidates).
  • 20Educational qualification certificates and marksheets (graduation / Law degree onwards).
  • 21Domicile certificate / Residence proof (for reserved category benefits applicable to Rajasthan domiciles).
  • 22Character Certificate from the last attended educational institution (at least "Good" mention required).
  • 23Police verification / antecedent certificate at the time of appointment.
  • 24Medical fitness certificate from a competent authority at the time of appointment.
  • 25NOC from current employer if already in Government / PSU service.
  • 26EWS / widowhood / divorce proof documents as applicable (court decree / death certificate).
  • 27Ex-Serviceman discharge certificate / NOC as applicable.
  • 28UIDAI / Unique Disability ID (U-D.I.D.) card, if available, must be produced before the Preliminary Examination.
  • 29Printout of the Online Application Form is NOT to be sent to RPSC. No Hard Copy of the Call Letter will be sent by post. Candidates must appear for the test at their own expense; RPSC will not pay or reimburse any such expenses.

Important Instructions

Submitting false/incorrect information in the application form or failing to meet eligibility conditions is a punishable offence under Section 217 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023 (BNS). Such candidates may be debarred from appearing in the examination or disqualified during counselling/selection/appointment. All revisions, corrigenda, and updates will be hosted on the RPSC official website only - candidates should visit regularly. Candidates must not change their registered mobile number or email ID. Changes, if necessary, should be informed to RPSC at the earliest. Candidates must apply under the correct category. Applying under a wrong category will not entitle them to any category benefit; the application may be treated as General category. Candidates from other states belonging to SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS will be treated as General (UR) for this recruitment as reservation benefits are applicable only to Rajasthan domiciles. OBC candidates from Rajasthan belonging to the Creamy Layer must apply as General (UR). Only Non-Creamy Layer OBC candidates with a valid and recent certificate are eligible for OBC reservation. Category once filled in the online application form cannot be changed after the application submission deadline. Not more than one application should be submitted. In case of multiple applications, only the last valid completed application will be retained. PwBD candidates with temporary/likely-to-improve disabilities are not eligible for PwBD reservation benefits. Candidates already in Central / State / PSU Government service must produce a NOC from their employer at the time of appointment, or an NOC obtained before joining. Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification. No request for change of examination centre shall be entertained. Travel expenses will not be reimbursed by RPSC. The Commission reserves the right to cancel the selection process partly or entirely at any stage. Legal jurisdiction for any dispute arising out of this recruitment shall be as per Hon'ble Rajasthan High Court, Jaipur / Jodhpur as applicable. Age relaxation provisions in Hindi language shall prevail over English translation in case of any discrepancy. Candidates who are provisionally selected will be given actual appointment only after document verification and medical fitness confirmation. Provisional admission to examination does not guarantee final appointment. Mobile phones, smartwatches, pagers, earphones, or any other electronic/communication devices are not allowed inside the examination premises.
