Important Instructions

  • Application form must be typed in block letters, opened/filled in Adobe Reader, and taken as colour printout - handwritten forms will NOT be accepted. Separate applications with separate Demand Drafts must be submitted for each post applied for. Applications without photograph, without signature, or without the prescribed Demand Draft will not be accepted. OBC Non-Creamy Layer Certificate and EWS Certificate must be issued on or after 01-04-2025. PwBD candidates must have permanent disability of not less than 40% certified by competent authority. Biometric verification with Aadhaar Card will be carried out at the time of written examination/skill test. Candidate's appearance at the time of examination must match the photograph in the application form. Use of mobile phones, pagers, smartwatches, Bluetooth devices, pen drives, or any electronic/communication device is strictly prohibited at the examination venue. Violation will result in disciplinary action including ban from future examinations. No TA/DA will be paid for attending written examination or skill test. Selected candidates will be required to serve anywhere in India. Mere submission of application does not entitle candidates to be called for examination. Depending on applications received, candidates may be shortlisted based on marks in minimum essential qualification. Applications received after the due date will not be considered. DSSC will not be responsible for postal delays. The Commandant reserves the right to cancel/postpone the recruitment process and increase/decrease vacancies without assigning any reason. Decision of the Appointing Authority (Commandant, DSSC) is final and no appeal will be entertained. DSSC will not be liable/responsible for paying any compensation in case of injury/death suffered by a candidate during the recruitment process. No interim correspondence regarding application status will be entertained.

FAQs

Q1. What is DSSC Recruitment 2026?
Defence Services Staff College (DSSC), Wellington (Nilgiris), Tamil Nadu has invited offline applications from eligible Indian citizens for various Group ‘C’ civilian posts such as LDC, MTS, Driver, Stenographer and technical/support roles on a permanent basis.
Q2. How many vacancies are available in DSSC Recruitment 2026?
A total of 78 Group ‘C’ posts are proposed to be filled at DSSC Wellington, including LDC, Civilian Motor Driver, MTS (Office & Training), Stenographer Grade II, Supervisor (Networking), Network Administrator, Hardware Administrator, Lineman, Boat Builder and Technical Attendant (Printing Machine Operator), with reservations including ESM/PwBD.
Q3. What is the mode of application for DSSC Group ‘C’ posts?
The application process is completely offline; candidates must submit the prescribed application form by post to the Commandant, DSSC, Wellington (Nilgiris) – 643 231, Tamil Nadu within the stipulated time.
Q4. What is the last date to apply for DSSC Recruitment 2026?
The last date for receipt of applications is 21 days from the date of publication of the advertisement in Employment News (issue dated 23–29 May 2026), and applications received after this period are likely to be rejected.
Q5. What are the educational qualifications required for LDC and Stenographer at DSSC?
For LDC, candidates must have passed 12th and possess a typing speed of 35 wpm in English or 30 wpm in Hindi on computer, while Stenographer Grade II requires 12th pass with shorthand dictation at 80 wpm for 10 minutes and transcription in 50 minutes (English) or 65 minutes (Hindi) on computer.
Q6. What are the qualifications for MTS and Civilian Motor Driver (OG)?
MTS (Office & Training) generally requires 10th pass, whereas Civilian Motor Driver (Ordinary Grade) requires 10th/12th pass along with a valid heavy vehicle driving licence and relevant driving experience as prescribed in the detailed notification.
Q7. What is the age limit for DSSC Group ‘C’ posts?
For LDC, Stenographer, Driver and technical posts, the age limit is 18–27 years, while for MTS and some other support posts the age limit is 18–25 years, with relaxations for SC, ST, OBC, Ex-Servicemen, PwBD and other categories as per Government of India rules.
Q8. What is the pay scale for DSSC Group ‘C’ posts?
Under the 7th CPC Pay Matrix, Stenographer Grade II carries Level 4 (Rs. 25,500–81,100), LDC, Driver, Network/Hardware/Supervisor, Lineman and Boat Builder are in Level 2 (Rs. 19,900–63,200), and MTS and Technical Attendant – Printing Machine Operator are in Level 1 (Rs. 18,000–56,900).
Q9. What is the selection process for DSSC Recruitment 2026?
The selection process generally consists of an objective-type Written Examination followed by a Skill Test or Trade Test (such as typing, stenography, driving or technical test as applicable), along with document verification and medical fitness as per DSSC norms.
Q10. How can I submit the application form for DSSC Recruitment 2026?
Candidates must fill the prescribed application form carefully, attach self-attested copies of certificates, a self-addressed envelope if required, superscribe the envelope with “APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF ”, and send it by ordinary/registered/speed post so that it reaches the Commandant, DSSC, Wellington within 21 days from the advertisement date.
