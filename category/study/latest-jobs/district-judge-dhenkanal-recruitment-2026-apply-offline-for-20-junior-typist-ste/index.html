Qualification / Eligibility

Junior Clerk-cum-Copyist: +3 examination or equivalent from a recognized University and Diploma in Computer Application from a recognized institute. Junior Typist: +3 examination or equivalent from a recognized University and Diploma in Computer Application from a recognized institute. Minimum speed of 40 wpm in typewriting on computer. Stenographer Gr-III: +3 examination or equivalent from a recognized University and Diploma in Computer Application from a recognized institute. Minimum speed of 80 wpm in shorthand and 40 wpm in typewriting on computer. Salaried Amin: H.S.C. examination or equivalent from a recognized Board and Revenue Inspector Training from Govt./Recognized institution and knowledge in Computer Application. All Posts: Ability to speak, read and write Odia (M.E. Standard).
