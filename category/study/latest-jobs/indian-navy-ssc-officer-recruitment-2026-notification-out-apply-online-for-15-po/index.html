Application Fee

The official notification does not specify any application fee for Indian Navy SSC IT Officer Recruitment 2026. Candidates are advised to check the official Indian Navy website at www.joinindiannavy.gov.in at the time of applying for the latest fee-related instructions. All communications must be made through the official Indian Navy website and portal only.

Age Limit

DetailsCandidates must have been born between 01 January 2002 and 01 July 2007 (both dates inclusive) to be eligible for this recruitment. There is no separate category-wise age relaxation for this entry. The age eligibility is determined strictly by the date of birth as recorded in the Matriculation/10th Certificate. Candidates outside this birth date range are not eligible under any circumstances.

Qualification / Eligibility

Candidates must fulfill all eligibility conditions as on the closing date of online application (01-06-2026). Only unmarried Indian citizens are eligible to apply. Candidates must have minimum 60% marks in English in Class X or Class XII. The qualifying degree must be from a University incorporated by an Act of Central or State Legislature in India, or educational institutions established by an Act of Parliament or declared deemed Universities/Autonomous Universities under UGC Act 1956, IIT Act 1961, AICTE Act 1987, NITSER Act 2007, or IIIT Act 2014. Foreign university degrees are also accepted if the institution is recognised by the Government of India or holds an Engineering Degree equivalence certificate from the Association of Indian Universities (AIU Act 1973). | Candidates in the final year of their qualifying degree (regular/integrated) are also eligible to apply. Candidates selected in the final merit list must submit proof of completing the qualifying degree with minimum 60% marks by emailing [email protected] by June 2026. Failure to do so will result in non-permission to join INA, Ezhimala. | Educational Qualification

Syllabus

There is no written examination for this recruitment. Shortlisting is based on normalised marks in the qualifying degree. The SSB Interview assesses the candidate's Officer Like Qualities (OLQ), intelligence, psychological suitability, and overall personality. Detailed SSB procedure is available on the Indian Navy website www.joinindiannavy.gov.in. The normalisation formula used for shortlisting is available at https://www.joinindiannavy.gov.in/files/normalisation.pdf.

Important Instructions

Only unmarried Indian citizens (Men & Women) born between 01 January 2002 and 01 July 2007 are eligible to apply. Minimum 60% marks in English in Class X or XII and minimum 60% overall in qualifying degree is mandatory. Only one application can be submitted - multiple applications will result in cancellation of candidature. No hard copy applications will be accepted - online submission only via www.joinindiannavy.gov.in. Application once submitted is final - no amendments or changes will be entertained under any circumstances. Change of SSB Centre for examination or interview is not permissible under any circumstances. There is no relaxation in medical standards on any grounds; change of medical hospital/centre is not permissible. Any candidate who is found to be married or marries while under training will be discharged from service and shall be liable to refund full pay, allowances, and training expenditure. Use or possession of narcotics is banned - candidates may be tested for drugs during SSB selection, medical, training, and subsequently during service. All candidates recommended by SSB must undergo compulsory medical examination as per Indian Navy standards. All officers/cadets will be compulsory members of the Naval Group Insurance Scheme (NGIS). Police verification and character verification are part of the final appointment process. Candidature will be cancelled at any stage of selection/training/post-commissioning in case of false declaration, misinformation, or concealment of information. Candidates are advised to regularly check www.joinindiannavy.gov.in for updates on the recruitment process. All decisions of the Indian Navy regarding eligibility, selection, and other matters are final and binding.
