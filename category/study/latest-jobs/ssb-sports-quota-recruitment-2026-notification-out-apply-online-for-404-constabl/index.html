Job Overview

Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB) under Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India has published the detailed advertisement for recruitment of 404 Sportspersons as Constable (General Duty) under Sports Quota for the year 2026. Online applications are invited from eligible Male & Female candidates for filling up these Group 'C' Non-Gazetted, (Non-Ministerial) Combatised posts. The recruitment is temporary but likely to be continued. Candidates with outstanding sports achievements in various disciplines can apply online. Important Instructions

Only Indian citizens (or subjects of Nepal/Bhutan with permission) are eligible. Candidates must bring all original documents at the time of documentation. Persons already in Government service must apply through proper channel and submit NOC at documentation stage. Falsification of documents or canvassing will lead to disqualification. Selected candidates will be governed under New Pension Scheme (NPS). Performance of selected sportspersons will be reviewed periodically. SSB reserves the right to cancel/postpone the recruitment without any reason. PwD candidates are not eligible for this recruitment.
