Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationPh.D. in Engineering Stream OR M.E./M. Tech. in Electrical/Electronic Engineering with 3 years experience OR B.Tech. in Electrical/Electronic Engineering with 5 years experience.
DetailsSenior Project Engineer: Ph.D. (Engineering Stream) M.E./M. Tech. in Electrical/Electronic Engineering with a minimum of 3 years of relevant experience. B.Tech. in Electrical/Electronic Engineering with a minimum of 5 years of relevant experience. Experience in power electronics, electrical motor design (e.g., 2/3-phase small BLDC motor), PCB design using Eagle/Altium or similar software, control electronics, and embedded systems. Hands-on experience in motor and electronics testing and debugging, conceptualization and design of experiments, data analysis, interpretation, and presentation. Desirable qualifications: Experience in medical device development and knowledge of the biomedical product lifecycle. Proficient in software tools such as LT Spice and STM32cubeIDE. Excellent oral and written communication skills and the ability to work effectively in a multidisciplinary and collaborative research environment.
