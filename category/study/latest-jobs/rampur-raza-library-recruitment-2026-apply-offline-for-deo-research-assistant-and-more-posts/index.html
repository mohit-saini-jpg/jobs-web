Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationBachelor's Degree in Information Technology/Computer Science/Artificial Intelligence/Data Science, Bachelor's/Master's Degree in Commerce/Accountancy or CA/CS, Bachelor's/Master's Degree in any discipline, Ph.D. in Hindi, Ph.D. in Sanskrit, Bachelor's Degree with Computer Applications Certificate and typing speed
DetailsIT Consultant: Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology/Computer Science/ Artificial Intelligence/ Data Science from a recognized University/Institution and a minimum of 5 years' work experience in the IT field in any institution of State/Central Government/ Bodies/ Research Institutions/ Universities or Public Sector Undertakings. Account Consultant: Bachelor's/ Master's Degree in Commerce/ Accountancy from a recognized University/Institution or Chartered Accountant/ Company Secretary and 7 years' experience in the field of Accounts in any institution of State/Central Government/ Bodies/ Research Institutions/ Universities or Public Sector Undertakings. Administrative Consultant: Bachelor's/Master's Degree in any discipline from a recognized University/Institution and 7 years' experience in the field of Administration in any institution of State/Central Government/ Bodies/ Research Institutions/ Universities or Public Sector Undertakings. Research Assistant-Hindi: Ph.D. Degree in Hindi from a recognized University and 3 years' experience in the field of research, translation or manuscript studies. Research Assistant-Sanskrit: Ph.D. Degree in Sanskrit from a recognized University and 3 years' experience in the field of research, translation or manuscript studies. Typist/Data Entry Operator (Hindi): Bachelor's Degree in any discipline from a recognized University/Institution, One-Year Certificate in Computer Applications, 3 years' experience in the relevant field in any institution of State/Central Government/ Bodies/ Research Institutions/ Universities or Public Sector Undertakings, and typing speed of 30 words per minute in Hindi on Computer. Typist/Data Entry Operator (English): Bachelor's Degree in any discipline from a recognized University/Institution, One-Year Certificate in Computer Applications, 3 years' experience in the relevant field in any institution of State/Central Government/ Bodies/ Research Institutions/ Universities or Public Sector Undertakings, and typing speed of 35 words per minute in English on Computer.
