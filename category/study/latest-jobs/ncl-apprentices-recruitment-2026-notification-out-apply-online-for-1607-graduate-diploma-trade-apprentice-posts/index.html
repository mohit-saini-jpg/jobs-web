How to Apply

  • 1Candidates must apply online only. Graduate and Diploma Apprentice candidates must first enrol on the NATS portal at https://nats.education.gov.in and Trade Apprentice candidates must enrol on the NAPS portal at https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in. After successful registration, a registration number (Enrolment ID) will be generated, following which the candidate must apply for the NCL positions through the respective portal.
  • 2Documents/Details required at the time of Online Application:
  • 3Valid NATS/NAPS Enrolment ID (registration reference).
  • 4Scanned colour photograph and scanned signature in black ink (as per portal specifications).
  • 5Date of Birth as per Matriculation certificate.
  • 6Academic details - total marks scored and maximum marks in qualifying degree/diploma/ITI trade.
  • 7Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) in prescribed format (Annexure-1), if applicable.
  • 8PwBD disability certificate as per Rights of Persons with Disabilities Rules 2017 (Annexure-2), if applicable.
  • 9Address for correspondence and permanent address.
  • 10Candidates must save and preserve a hard copy as well as soft copy of the finally submitted online application for future reference. No paper-based or postal applications will be accepted.
  • 11Apply Online (Graduate/Diploma) at: https://nats.education.gov.in
  • 12Apply Online (Trade/ITI) at: https://www.apprenticeshipindia.gov.in
  • 13For assistance, candidates may email the HRD Department, NCL at [email protected] or visit Menu > Career > Apprenticeship Training section on the NCL official website www.nclcil.in.

Important Instructions

  • Online applications are open from 01-06-2026 to 15-06-2026 through NATS/NAPS portals only. A candidate may apply for any one notified position only - multiple applications will lead to disqualification. Information pertaining to Date of Birth, Caste Category, and Qualification once entered in the online application shall be considered final - no alteration will be permitted at later stages. Stipend for Graduate and Diploma Apprentices is inclusive of the Government of India share paid through DBT; Trade Apprentice stipend will be paid directly by NCL. Stipend will be paid directly into the AADHAAR-linked bank account of the Apprenticeship Trainee as per the signed contract. Apprenticeship Training is purely temporary and statutory in nature - it does not confer any right or claim for regular employment in NCL. Apprentices are eligible for 11 days Casual Leave and 15 days Medical Leave per year of training. Engagement hours are between 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Apprentices will be covered under the Workmen Compensation Act, 1923 for any accident during training. No accommodation, fooding/lodging, or transport will be provided during the training period. No TA/DA will be paid for appearing in document scrutiny or post-scrutiny processes. Canvassing in any manner at any stage will lead to cancellation of candidature. Any dispute will be subject to courts/tribunals of Singrauli, MP only. Dates mentioned in the notification are tentative and subject to alteration/amendment.

FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for NCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026-27?
The last date to apply online is 15 June 2026 through the NATS and NAPS portals.
Q2. How many vacancies are announced in NCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A total of 1607 apprentice vacancies have been announced across Graduate, Diploma, ITI Trade, and Paramedical disciplines.
Q3. What is the selection process for NCL Apprentice Trainee 2026?
Selection is based on merit (marks in qualifying exam), followed by document verification and medical examination. No written test is conducted.
Q4. What is the stipend for NCL Apprentice Trainees?
Stipend ranges from ₹9,600 to ₹13,700 per month depending on the qualification and discipline.
Q5. Who is eligible to apply for NCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
Indian candidates who have completed Degree/Diploma/ITI/Paramedical courses from recognized institutes in Uttar Pradesh or Madhya Pradesh after June 2022 are eligible.
Q6. What is the age limit for NCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
Candidates must be between 18 to 26 years as on 01 June 2026, with age relaxation for reserved categories as per rules.
Q7. Is there any application fee for NCL Apprentice 2026?
No, there is no application fee. Candidates can apply free of cost through NATS/NAPS portals.
Q8. Can candidates with prior job experience apply for NCL Apprentice posts?
No, candidates with one year or more of work experience or prior apprenticeship training are not eligible.
Q9. How to apply for NCL Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
Candidates must register and apply through NATS (for Graduate/Diploma) or NAPS (for ITI Trade) portals online.
Q10. Is NCL Apprenticeship a permanent job?
No, it is a one-year training program under the Apprentice Act, 1961, and does not guarantee permanent employment.
