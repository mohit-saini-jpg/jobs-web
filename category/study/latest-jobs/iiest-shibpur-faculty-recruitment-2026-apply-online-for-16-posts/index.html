FAQs

Q1. What is IIEST Shibpur Faculty Recruitment 2026 Special Drive?
IIEST Shibpur Faculty Recruitment 2026 is a special drive on mission mode to fill 16 backlog reserved faculty vacancies for SC, ST and OBC (NCL) categories under Advertisement No. RO/MY/26/14. It covers Professor, Associate Professor, and Assistant Professor (Grade I & Grade II) positions in various Academic Units of the Institute.
Q2. How many vacancies are there in IIEST Shibpur Faculty Recruitment 2026?
A total of 16 backlog reserved faculty posts are notified: 4 Professor posts (Level 14A), 1 Associate Professor post (Level 13A2), and 11 Assistant Professor Grade I & Grade II posts (Level 10 / Level 12). All posts are reserved for SC, ST and OBC (NCL) candidates as per Government of India rules.
Q3. What are the important dates for IIEST Shibpur Faculty Special Drive 2026?
The notification is dated 20 May 2026 and the online application on the SAMARTH Portal is open from 20-05-2026 to 12-06-2026 up to 11:55 pm. Submission of the compiled application PDF through Google Form is allowed up to 19-06-2026 (11:55 pm), and eligibility is determined as on 12-06-2026.
Q4. For which posts can I apply under IIEST Shibpur Faculty Recruitment 2026?
The special drive includes four faculty levels: Professor – Level 14A Associate Professor – Level 13A2 Assistant Professor Grade I – Level 12 Assistant Professor Grade II – Level 10 The exact number of posts at each level and in each Academic Unit is given in the detailed notification.
Q5. What is the essential qualification for faculty posts at IIEST Shibpur in 2026?
The essential academic qualification for all faculty levels is a Ph.D. in the relevant/equivalent discipline from a recognized University/Institute with First Class in all preceding degrees (from Bachelor’s degree onwards). Candidates who obtained Ph.D. directly after B.E./B.Tech. are also eligible, provided they meet the First Class criteria.
Q6. What are the experience requirements for Assistant Professor, Associate Professor and Professor posts?
Assistant Professor Grade II (Level 10): Ph.D. with First Class in preceding degrees; no mandatory experience. Assistant Professor Grade I (Level 12): Ph.D. plus 3 years post-Ph.D. experience or 6 years total teaching/research experience in reputed institutes/R&D/industry with minimum 20 cumulative credit points. Associate Professor (Level 13A2): Ph.D. plus 6 years post-Ph.D. experience (at least 3 years as Assistant Professor with AGP ₹8000) or 9 years total experience with 50 cumulative credit points. Professor (Level 14A): Ph.D. plus 10 years post-Ph.D. experience or 13 years total experience (with minimum years as Associate Professor and 80 cumulative credit points).
Q7. Who is eligible to apply category-wise for IIEST Shibpur Faculty Backlog Vacancies?
These posts are exclusively for Backlog Reserved Vacancies – only candidates belonging to SC, ST and OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) categories can apply against the respective reserved posts. OBC reservation is only for Non-Creamy Layer candidates, and the OBC (NCL) certificate must be issued on or after 01 April 2026 in the prescribed Central Government format.
Q8. Is there any restriction for candidates who completed Ph.D. from IIEST Shibpur?
Yes. Candidates whose most recent degree (Ph.D.) is from IIEST Shibpur will not be considered unless there is a minimum gap of 3 years between leaving IIEST Shibpur and the last date of online application (12-06-2026). This is to promote academic mobility and avoid immediate in-house appointments.
