FAQs

Q1. What is UPSSSC Fireman Recruitment 2026?
UPSSSC Fireman Recruitment 2026 is a government job notification released by the Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission for eligible candidates. A total of 170 Fireman vacancies have been announced under this recruitment drive. Interested candidates can apply online through the official UPSSSC website before the last date.
Q2. How many vacancies are available in UPSSSC Fireman Recruitment 2026?
A total of 170 vacancies have been released for Fireman and related posts including Vidhan Bhawan Rakshak and Fire Guard. These posts are distributed across different categories as per government reservation rules. This recruitment offers a good opportunity for candidates seeking Uttar Pradesh government jobs.
Q3. What is the eligibility for UPSSSC Fireman Recruitment 2026?
Candidates must have passed Intermediate (12th) examination from a recognized board or equivalent qualification approved by the government. Preference may be given to candidates having NCC ‘B’ Certificate or Territorial Army experience. Applicants must ensure they meet all eligibility conditions before applying online.
Q4. What is the age limit for UPSSSC Fireman Recruitment 2026?
The minimum age required for applying is 18 years, while the maximum age limit is 40 years as on the specified date. Age relaxation will be applicable for reserved categories as per UPSSSC government rules. Candidates should check the official notification for complete age relaxation details.
Q5. What is the application fee for UPSSSC Fireman Recruitment 2026?
The application fee is ₹25 for all categories including General, OBC, SC, ST, and PH candidates. The fee must be paid through online mode only while submitting the application form. Applications without successful fee payment will not be considered valid.
Q6. What is the selection process for UPSSSC Fireman Recruitment 2026?
The selection process for UPSSSC Fireman Recruitment 2026 includes a written examination followed by physical efficiency test and document verification. Candidates must qualify in each stage to move forward in the selection process. Final merit will be prepared based on overall performance.
Q7. What is the salary for UPSSSC Fireman posts?
Selected candidates will receive salary under Pay Level-3 of the pay matrix as per government norms. The monthly salary ranges from ₹21,700 to ₹69,100 along with additional allowances. This makes it a stable and attractive government job opportunity.
Q8. What are the important dates for UPSSSC Fireman Recruitment 2026?
The online application process starts on 09 June 2026 and continues until 29 June 2026. The notification was released on 10 April 2026 and candidates can make corrections until 06 July 2026. Applicants must complete the process before the deadline to avoid rejection.
Q9. How can I apply for UPSSSC Fireman Recruitment 2026?
Candidates can apply online by visiting the official website upsssc.gov.in and filling out the application form. They must upload required documents, pay the fee, and submit the form before the last date. It is important to verify all details carefully before final submission.
Q10. Where can I download the UPSSSC Fireman Recruitment 2026 notification PDF?
The official notification PDF is available on the UPSSSC website under the recruitment section for candidates to download. It includes complete details such as eligibility, vacancy, application process, and important instructions. Candidates are advised to read the PDF carefully before applying.
