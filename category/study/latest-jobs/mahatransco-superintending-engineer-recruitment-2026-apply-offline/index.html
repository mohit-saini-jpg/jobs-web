FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to apply for the MSETCL Superintending Engineer (Civil) post?
The last date to submit the application for the Superintending Engineer (Civil) post is 12th June 2026. Ensure your application reaches the specified address before this date.
Q2. What is the educational qualification required for this post?
Candidates must possess a Bachelors Degree in Civil Engineering / Technology or its equivalent to be eligible for the Superintending Engineer (Civil) position.
Q3. How many years of experience are needed for the Superintending Engineer (Civil) role?
A total of 12 years of experience in civil works related to the Power Sector is required. This includes at least 5 years as Executive Engineer & Additional Executive Engineer or 2 years as Executive Engineer.
Q4. What is the age limit for applying to this position?
The age limit varies: 50 years for outsiders, 55 years for reserved category outsiders, and 57 years for departmental candidates.
Q5. What is the application fee for MSETCL Superintending Engineer (Civil) recruitment?
The application fee is Rs. 800 for Open Category candidates and Rs. 400 for Reserved Category candidates applying against reserved posts.

Applications must be submitted offline by sending the duly filled application form along with required documents and demand draft to the designated address before the last date.

Q8. What is the selection process for this recruitment?
