Important Instructions

Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply. All eligibility computations (age, experience, qualification) are as on 13.05.2026. Candidates in their final year/semester whose results are awaited by the cut-off date are not eligible. Qualifications through part-time/correspondence/distance learning mode are not eligible. For E0 positions, candidates with higher professional qualifications (BE/B.Tech, MBA, MCA, CA, M.Sc, Ph.D, etc.) are not eligible. Physical Fitness Efficiency Test (Agility, Endurance, and Working at Height) is applicable for Jr. Executive - Fire & Safety, Chemical, and Mechanical (E0) only. Jr. Executive - Fire & Safety candidates must possess a valid Heavy Vehicle Driving License as on 13.05.2026 and meet prescribed physical standards (height, weight, chest measurements, eyesight, and health criteria). OBC-NCL certificate must not be older than 6 months from the cut-off date and must clearly state the candidate does not belong to the creamy layer. EWS Income and Asset Certificate must be valid for FY 2026-27 and submitted at the time of interview. Candidates currently employed in Government/PSU/Autonomous Bodies must produce a No Objection Certificate (NOC) in original at the time of the selection process. Application fee once paid is non-refundable under any circumstances. Travel allowance (Sleeper class for E0/E1/E2 for CBT/Skill Test/GD & PI; 3rd AC for E3/E5 for interviews) is reimbursable for SC/ST/PwBD candidates (CBT stage) and all candidates for E3/E5 selection, for journeys of not less than 30 km. Canvassing in any form at any stage will lead to cancellation of candidature. Court of jurisdiction for any dispute will be at Jodhpur, Rajasthan.
