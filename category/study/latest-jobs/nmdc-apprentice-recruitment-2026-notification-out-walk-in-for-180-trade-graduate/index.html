How to Apply

  • 1Candidates must attend the walk-in interview on the date mentioned against their respective category at the Training Institute, BIOM, Bacheli Complex, Bacheli. There is no online application form to be submitted on the NMDC website. Prior registration on the respective portal is mandatory before attending the walk-in interview - Trade Apprentice candidates must register at www.apprenticeshipindia.org and Graduate / Technician Apprentice candidates must register at https://nats.education.gov.in before the walk-in interview date.
  • 2Candidates must reach the interview site before 09:00 AM to register themselves for appearing in the walk-in interview. No TA/DA will be paid or reimbursed for attending the interview.
  • 3Documents to Bring at the Time of Walk-in Interview:

  • 5Proof of Date of Birth (original + photocopy)

  • 7Educational Qualification certificates (original + photocopy)
  • 8Caste Certificate (if applicable, original + photocopy)

Important Instructions

Candidates who have already completed Apprenticeship Training under the Apprenticeship Act-1961 are not eligible to apply. Candidates with one year or more experience in the relevant category are not eligible to apply. Candidates who completed required qualifications during 2022 and before 2022 need not respond to this advertisement. Trade Apprentice candidates must pre-register at www.apprenticeshipindia.org before the walk-in interview. Graduate and Technician Apprentice candidates must pre-register at https://nats.education.gov.in before the walk-in interview. Candidates must report at the interview venue before 09:00 AM on their respective interview date. The walk-in interview commences at 10:00 AM; candidates arriving late may not be entertained. No TA/DA will be paid or reimbursed to candidates appearing for the walk-in interview. Name, Father's name, Mother's name, and Date of Birth must be identical across all educational and identity documents. This apprenticeship scheme does not guarantee any employment on completion of training. Original documents along with photocopies must be brought for verification and submission at the time of interview.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is the last date for NMDC Apprentice walk-in interview 2026?
A
The walk-in interview for NMDC Limited Apprentice Recruitment 2026 starts on June 01, 2026 and ends on June 11, 2026. Different categories have different walk-in dates - Trade Apprentices from June 1-6, while Graduate and Technician Apprentices must attend from June 8-11, 2026 based on their specialization. Candidates must report before 09:00 AM on their respective interview date.
Q2Q2. How many vacancies are announced in NMDC Bacheli Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
NMDC Limited, Bailadila Iron Ore Mine, Bacheli Complex has announced a total of 180 apprentice vacancies under Advertisement No. BC/DGM(MIN.)/T,S&E/APPR-301-A/Vol-IX/2026-27/1471 dated 06/05/2026. The positions include 150 Trade Apprentices across 7 trades, 16 Graduate Apprentices in 4 engineering disciplines, and 14 Technician Apprentices across 4 specializations under the Apprenticeship Act-1961.
Q3Q3. What is the qualification required for NMDC Trade Apprentice posts?
A
Candidates must possess ITI passed certificate in the relevant trade for Trade Apprentice positions. The trades include COPA (30 posts), Electrician (30 posts), Mechanic Diesel (25 posts), Fitter (20 posts), Welder Gas & Electric (20 posts), Mechanic Motor Vehicle (20 posts), and Machinist (5 posts). Prior registration on www.apprenticeshipindia.org is mandatory before attending the walk-in interview.
Q4Q4. Is pre-registration mandatory for NMDC Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
Yes, pre-registration on the respective online portal is mandatory before attending the walk-in interview. Trade Apprentice candidates must register at www.apprenticeshipindia.org, while Graduate and Technician Apprentice candidates must register at https://nats.education.gov.in. Without prior registration, candidates will not be allowed to participate in the walk-in interview process.
Q5Q5. What is the stipend for NMDC Apprentice posts 2026?
A
The stipend for NMDC Apprentice posts will be paid as per Apprenticeship Act-1961 norms. The exact amount varies based on the category - Trade Apprentices, Graduate Apprentices, and Technician Apprentices receive different stipend amounts as prescribed under the Central Apprenticeship Rules and guidelines issued by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship.
Q6Q6. Where is the walk-in interview venue for NMDC Apprentice Recruitment 2026?
A
The walk-in interview will be held at Training Institute, BIOM (Bailadila Iron Ore Mine), Bacheli Complex, Bacheli – 494553, Dantewada, Chhattisgarh. Candidates must report at the venue before 09:00 AM on their respective interview date for registration. The interview will commence at 10:00 AM sharp, and late arrivals may not be entertained.
Q7Q7. What documents are required for NMDC Apprentice walk-in interview?
A
Candidates must bring resume with passport size photograph, proof of Date of Birth (original + photocopy), Aadhaar Card (original + photocopy), educational qualification certificates (original + photocopy), and caste certificate if applicable (original + photocopy). All original documents must be presented for verification along with self-attested photocopies for submission at the time of walk-in interview.
Q8Q8. Can candidates who completed ITI before 2022 apply for NMDC Apprentice posts?
A
No, candidates who completed their required qualifications during 2022 and before 2022 need not respond to this NMDC Apprentice advertisement. Only candidates who completed their ITI, Diploma, or Engineering degree after 2022 are eligible to attend the walk-in interview. Additionally, candidates who have already completed apprenticeship training under Apprenticeship Act-1961 are not eligible.
Q9Q9. Does NMDC provide TA/DA for attending the apprentice walk-in interview?
A
No, NMDC will not pay or reimburse any Travel Allowance (TA) or Daily Allowance (DA) to candidates appearing for the walk-in interview. Candidates must bear their own travel and accommodation expenses for attending the interview at Training Institute, BIOM, Bacheli Complex, Bacheli, Dantewada, Chhattisgarh.
Q10Q10. Does NMDC Apprenticeship guarantee permanent employment after training?
A
No, this NMDC apprenticeship scheme does not guarantee any employment on completion of apprenticeship training. The apprenticeship is purely a training program under the Apprenticeship Act-1961 to provide practical and on-the-job training to candidates. However, apprentices may apply for regular positions when NMDC announces recruitment notifications during or after their training period.
