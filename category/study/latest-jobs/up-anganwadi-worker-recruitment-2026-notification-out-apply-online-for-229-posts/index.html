Important Instructions

THIS IS NOT A REGULAR GOVERNMENT EMPLOYMENT. Anganwadi Karyakatri is an honorary/contractual position under the Bal Vikas programme. Selected candidates will not be treated as regular government employees and will not be entitled to regular government employee benefits. Only female candidates are eligible. Male candidates cannot apply under any circumstances. Only residents of the same Ward / Gram Sabha where the Anganwadi vacancy exists are eligible. If no eligible candidate from the Gram Sabha, selection will be from the Nyaya Panchayat area. Merit is based on combined total marks of Intermediate + Graduation + Post Graduation. All exam marks must be accurately entered at time of application. Wrong entries or manipulation of marks will lead to cancellation and legal action. Tie-break is by age (higher age preferred), then by higher educational qualification - not by any other criteria. Applications will be accepted only for 21 days from the date of advertisement publication. Applications before or after this window will not be accepted. Only online applications at http://upanganwadibharti.in are valid. No offline, postal, or in-person applications will be entertained. Caste and residence certificates must be Tehsil-issued, digitally signed, and online verifiable. Manually issued or offline certificates will not be accepted. BPL income certificates must comply with GO No. 3959/1-9-11-10-9 dated 30.09.2011 and must be online verifiable to be accepted for BPL priority. Date of birth proof: Only High School (Class 10th) mark sheet / certificate is valid for age verification. No other document is accepted for date of birth. Reserved category posts will only accept applications from the respective reserved category. Unreserved posts are open to applicants from any caste/category. If any candidate is found to have provided false information, incorrect marks, or forged documents at any stage, their candidature will be cancelled and legal action will be initiated. The number of vacancies is provisional and may change as per actual administrative and judicial requirements of the department. All corrigenda and updates will be hosted on http://upanganwadibharti.in only - candidates should check the portal regularly.

FAQs

Q1Q1. What is Hathras Anganwadi Worker Recruitment 2026 and how many posts are available?
A
Hathras Anganwadi Worker Recruitment 2026 is a hiring drive by Bal Vikas Vibhag, Hathras, for 229 Anganwadi Karyakatri posts across 8 Child Development Projects in Hathras district, Uttar Pradesh.
Q2Q2. What is the application date for Hathras Anganwadi Worker Vacancy 2026?
A
Online applications will be accepted at upanganwadibharti.in for 21 days from the advertisement publication date, i.e. approximately 18-05-2026 to 04-06-2026 as per the schedule mentioned.
Q3Q3. What is the age limit for Hathras Anganwadi Karyakatri Recruitment 2026?
A
Female candidates must be between 18 to 35 years as on 01.01.2026, and the High School (10th) mark sheet/certificate is the only valid proof for date of birth.
Q4Q4. What is the educational qualification required for Hathras Anganwadi Worker posts?
A
The minimum qualification is Intermediate (10+2), while Graduation and Post Graduation marks are also counted for merit if available, since merit is based on combined marks of Intermediate + Graduation + Post Graduation.
Q5Q5. Who can apply for Hathras Anganwadi Worker Recruitment 2026 in Hathras district?
A
Only female candidates who are residents of the same Ward/Gram Sabha where the Anganwadi vacancy exists are eligible; if no suitable candidate from that Gram Sabha is available, selection will be made from the concerned Nyaya Panchayat.
Q6Q6. How are vacancies distributed across projects and categories in Hathras Anganwadi Bharti 2026?
A
There are 229 posts across 8 projects (Sadabad, Hathras Gramin, Sikandarao, Sasni, Hasayan, Sahapau, Mursan, Hathras Shahar), distributed as 128 UR, 48 OBC, and 53 SC posts, with the Kharg centre of Hathras Gramin excluded due to court proceedings.
Q7Q7. What is the selection process for Hathras Anganwadi Karyakatri posts in 2026?
A
Selection is purely merit-based on the combined total marks of Intermediate + Graduation + Post Graduation, followed by document verification, with tie-breakers based first on higher age and then on higher educational qualification.
