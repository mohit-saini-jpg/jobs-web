Qualification / Eligibility

General Physician: M.D./DNB in General Medicine, Speciality with a minimum 1 to 4 years of experience after M.D/DNB. Ophthalmologist: MS/MD/DNB (Ophthalmology) from an institute recognised by MCI. Minimum 1 to 4 years of experience. Neonatologist: DM/DNB (Neonatologist) with 4 to 10 years of experience. Radiologist: M.D. /DNB or equivalent in radio-diagnosis from a recognized University with minimum 1 to 4 years of experience. Anaesthetist: MD/DNB Anaesthesia with 1 year Experience or DA Anesthesia with 2 year Experience. Neurologist: DM/DNB (Neurology) with 10 to 16 years of experience. Dermatologist: MD/DNB in Dermatology from Institute recognized by MCI 1 to 4 years of experience.
