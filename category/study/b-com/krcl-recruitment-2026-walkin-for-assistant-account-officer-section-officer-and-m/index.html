FAQs

Q1. What is the last date to attend the walk-in interview for KRCL Accounts Posts?
The walk-in interviews are scheduled on different dates for each post: 17th June 2026 for Account Assistant, 18th June 2026 for Assistant Account Officer, and 19th June 2026 for Section Officer.
Q2. What is the total number of vacancies announced by KRCL?
Konkan Railway Corporation Limited has announced a total of 9 vacancies for various Accounts posts through this recruitment drive.
Q3. What is the educational qualification required for the Accounts Assistant post?
For the Accounts Assistant post, candidates must possess a B.Com, M.Com, or Inter CA/CMA qualification from a recognized university, along with the required experience.
Q4. Is there any application fee for KRCL Accounts Posts?
No, there is no application fee mentioned for any category (General/OBC, SC/ST) for the KRCL Accounts Posts recruitment.
Q5. What is the age limit for the Section Officer post?
The maximum age limit for the Section Officer post is 40 years. For Assistant Account Officer and Account Assistant, the maximum age limit is 35 years.
Q6. What is the salary offered for the Assistant Account Officer position?
The consolidated remuneration for the Assistant Account Officer position ranges from Rs. 67140 to Rs. 76660 per month.
Q7. How can I apply for the KRCL Accounts Posts?
The application mode is Walk-in. Candidates need to attend the interview directly at the specified venue with all required documents on the scheduled date.
Q8. Where will the walk-in interview be conducted?
The walk-in interviews will be conducted at Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra. The exact venue details are available in the official notification.
Q9. What documents should I carry for the walk-in interview?
Candidates must carry all original documents, one set of self-attested photocopies, ID proof, educational certificates, experience certificates (if required), and category certificates (if applicable).
Q10. What is the selection process for these posts?
The selection process includes Preliminary Screening, Walk-in Interview, and potentially a Group Discussion or Written Test, followed by Document Verification and Merit List preparation.
