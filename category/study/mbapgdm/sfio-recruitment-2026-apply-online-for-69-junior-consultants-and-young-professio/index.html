Serious Fraud Investigation Office (SFIO) has released the official Junior Consultants and Young Professionals Notification 2026 at sfio.nic.in. A total of 69 vacancies have been announced under the SFIO Junior Consultants and Young Professionals Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged between Max: 65 years with a qualification of Law Graduate, Advocate, CA/ICWA/MBA (Finance), PhD/Master's Degree or MBA in relevant fields can apply online from 13 May 2026 to 03 June 2026. The official notification PDF and online application link are available below.

Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationLaw Graduate, Advocate, CA/ICWA/MBA (Finance), PhD/Master's Degree or MBA in relevant field.
DetailsJunior Consultant (Law): Advocate with at least 3-8 years' experience, preferably having exposure in other investigation agencies and/or regulatory bodies in the field of Corporate Law. Young Professional (Law): Law Graduate with at least one-year experience, preferably having exposure in other investigation agencies and/or regulatory bodies in the field of Corporate Law. Junior Consultant (Financial Analysis): CA/CWA/MBA(Finance) with at least 3-8 years' experience, preferably having exposure in other investigation agencies and/or regulatory bodies in the field of Financial Analysis/Forensic Audit. Young Professional (Financial Analysis/Forensic Audit): CA/ICWA/MBA (Finance) with at least one-year experience, preferably having exposure in other investigation agencies and/or regulatory bodies. Junior Consultant (General Administration): PhD/Master's Degree or MBA in relevant field/subject with 3-8 years' experience.
