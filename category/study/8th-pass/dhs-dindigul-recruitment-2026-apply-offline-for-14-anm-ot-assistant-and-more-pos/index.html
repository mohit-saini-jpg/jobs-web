Qualification / Eligibility

Mid-Level Health Provider: Diploma in GNM/B.Sc Nursing from a recognized University/Institution. Multi Purpose Hospital Worker: Minimum 8th Standard with the ability to read and write. Audiologist & Speech Therapists: BASLP Courses from a recognized institution. Occupational Therapist: Bachelors/Master's Degree in Occupational Therapy from a recognized University. Audio metrician: 12th grade with science subjects, followed by a one-year certificate course in audiometry from a government or recognized institution, or a Diploma in Hearing Language and Speech (DHLS). Radiographer: B.Sc Radiography. OT Assistant: 3 Months OT Technician Course from recognized University/Institutions. ANM: ANM qualification from Government or Government approved private Auxiliary Nurse Midwife School recognized by the Indian Nursing Council.
