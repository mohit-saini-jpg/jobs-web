Qualification / Eligibility

Assistant Professor - Management: Bachelor's Degree in any discipline and Master's Degree in Business Administration/PGDM/C.A./ICWA/M.Com. with First Class or equivalent at master level and two years of professional experience after acquiring the degree of Master's degree. Minimum 60% marks in at least two preceding examinations before Master degree. Qualified UGC-National Eligibility Test (NET) in Management/relevant discipline. Candidates who have been awarded a Ph.D. Degree in accordance with the provisions contained in the Para 4.1(l)(A)(ii), UGC Regulations, 2018 are exempted from the requirement of the minimum eligibility condition of NET. Minimum two years of professional experience after acquiring the degree of Master's degree. Area of Specialization: Financial Management/Marketing Management/General Management. Assistant Professor - English: Master Degree in English from an Indian University, or an equivalent degree from an accredited foreign University. At least 55% marks in Master degree and at least 60% marks in at least two preceding examinations. Qualified UGC-National Eligibility Test (NET) in English. Candidates who have been awarded a Ph.D. Degree in accordance with the provisions contained in the Para 4.1(I)(A)(ii), UGC Regulations, 2018 are exempted from the requirement of the minimum eligibility condition of NET. Assistant Professor - Commerce: Master Degree in Commerce from an Indian University, or an equivalent degree from an accredited foreign University. At least 55% marks in Master degree and at least 60% marks in at least two preceding examinations. Qualified UGC-National Eligibility Test (NET) in Commerce. Candidates who have been awarded a Ph.D. Degree in accordance with the provisions contained in the Para 4.1(I)(A)(ii), UGC Regulations, 2018 are exempted from the requirement of the minimum eligibility condition of NET. Area of Specialization: Accounting, Finance, General Management, Taxation.
