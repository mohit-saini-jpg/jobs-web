Qualification / Eligibility

Only candidates who have completed their qualifying examination as regular candidates and acquired the requisite qualification on or after the year 2022 are eligible. Qualifying qualification must be from a University in India or a State Board recognized by the competent authority. Candidates currently undergoing apprenticeship training in Govt./Public/Private Sector for one year or more as on the date of joining are not eligible. Combining apprenticeship with other institutions is not permitted. Selected candidates must execute a contract of Apprenticeship Training as per the Apprentices Act, 1961 and Amendment Act of 1973. Apprentices will have no claim for regular employment in NIT Calicut after completion of training.
