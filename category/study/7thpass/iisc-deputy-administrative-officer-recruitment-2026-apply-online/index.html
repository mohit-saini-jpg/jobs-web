FAQs

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for the Deputy Administrative Officer post?
Candidates must possess a Bachelor's Degree with a minimum of 50% marks. Additionally, they need to be staff from specific government organizations or hold analogous posts with relevant experience.

The maximum age limit for the Deputy Administrative Officer post is 56 years. There is no minimum age limit specified in the provided data.

There are a total of 6 vacancies for the Deputy Administrative Officer post announced by the Indian Institute of Science (IISc).

The selected candidates will receive a salary in Level - 08 as per the 7th CPC, which ranges from Rs. 47,600 to Rs. 1,51,100 per month.

The application fee varies by category. General/OBC candidates need to pay Rs. 450/- application fee plus Rs. 50/- processing fee. SC/ST and Women candidates only need to pay Rs. 50/- processing fee.

The selection process for the Deputy Administrative Officer recruitment will primarily involve a Job-oriented Aptitude Test.
Q8. Is this recruitment on a deputation basis?
