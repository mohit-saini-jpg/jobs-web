The National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut) has officially announced the Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026 with 4 openings. The detailed notification was published on 15 May 2026 at NIT Calicut Official Website. Candidates who meet the age criteria as per the official notification and hold a Ph.D. with consistently good academic record can now submit their online applications from 15 May 2026 to 15 June 2026. FAQs

The minimum qualification required for Assistant Professor Grade II is a Ph.D. with a consistently good academic record.

Q8. Can candidates with teaching and research experience apply?
Yes, candidates with 03 years after Ph.D. or 06 years total teaching and research experience are eligible for Assistant Professor Grade I.
