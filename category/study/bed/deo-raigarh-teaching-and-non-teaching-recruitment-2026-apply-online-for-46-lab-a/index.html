Qualification / Eligibility

Education QualificationGraduate/Post Graduate with minimum 45% or second class marks and B.Ed/D.Ed/D.El.Ed as applicable, TET qualified (Primary or Upper Primary level as per post), Entire education from Class 6th to Post Graduation in English medium (except Hindi/Sanskrit Lecturer posts)
DetailsPrincipal Primary Section: Graduate with minimum 45% marks and D.Ed/D.El.Ed, TET Primary level qualified, entire education in English medium. Lab Assistant: Higher Secondary with Biology/Math group from recognized board, entire education in English medium. Teacher English: Graduate with minimum 45% marks and B.Ed, English as one subject at graduation, TET Upper Primary level qualified, entire education in English medium. Teacher Math: Graduate with minimum 45% marks and B.Ed, Math with any two subjects from Physics/Chemistry/Electronics/Computer Science/Statistics/Military Science/Geology at graduation, TET Upper Primary level qualified, entire education in English medium. Lecturer Sanskrit: Post Graduate in Sanskrit/Classics/Acharya with minimum second class and B.Ed. Assistant Teacher: Higher Secondary with minimum 45% marks and D.Ed/D.El.Ed, TET Primary level qualified, entire education in English medium. Lecturer Hindi: Post Graduate in relevant subject with minimum second class and B.Ed. Principal Secondary School: Post Graduate with minimum second class and B.Ed, TET Upper Primary level qualified, entire education in English medium. Teacher Science: Graduate with minimum 45% marks and B.Ed, Science with any two subjects from Chemistry/Botany/Zoology/Military Science/Geology at graduation, TET Upper Primary level qualified, entire education in English medium. Library Assistant: Graduate with second class and B.Lib from recognized university, preference to English medium. Physical Education Teacher: Higher Secondary and D.P.Ed or B.P.Ed with Graduate, entire education in English medium. Lecturer Math: Post Graduate in Math/Applied Math with minimum second class and B.Ed, entire education in English medium. Teacher Art: Graduate with minimum 45% marks and B.Ed, Social Science with at least one subject from History/Geography/Economics/Political Science at graduation, TET Upper Primary level qualified, entire education in English medium. Lab Assistant (Science): Higher Secondary with Biology/Math group from recognized board, entire education in English medium. Lecturer Chemistry/Biochemistry: Post Graduate in Chemistry/Biochemistry with minimum second class and B.Ed, entire education in English medium.
