How to Apply

  • 1Applications must be submitted online only through the official Indian Army Recruiting website www.joinindianarmy.nic.in by clicking the ‘online application’ button. Hard copy applications will not be accepted under any circumstances. The online portal will be active from 13-05-2026 (1200 hrs) to 12-06-2026 (1200 hrs). Candidates must enter their particulars in the online application and read the terms and conditions before submitting the form.
  • 2Candidates are permitted to make changes to incorrectly filled data in the online application until the closing of online applications; the application must be re-submitted each time it is edited. After the closing date, no changes to the online application will be permitted and no representation in this regard will be entertained. Candidates must submit only one application; receipt of multiple applications from the same candidate will result in cancellation of candidature. DIGILOCKER certificates will not be accepted in lieu of original certificates.

Important Instructions

  • Only unmarried male candidates who are citizens of India, subjects of Nepal, or persons of Indian origin from specified countries are eligible to apply. Candidates must possess the prescribed minimum qualification (10+2 with 60% PCM and JEE Mains 2026 appearance) on the closing date of application. No hard copy applications will be accepted – online submission only. Candidates must undertake not to marry until they complete full training; violation will lead to discharge and recovery of training cost. PCM percentage must be entered up to two decimals and must not be rounded off; any false entry will result in outright rejection. Candidate should not have been debarred from appearing in any examination by UPSC. Candidate should not have been arrested/convicted by a criminal court or involved in any court case. Candidate withdrawn from NDA, OTA, IMA, Naval Academy, Air Force Academy or any service training academy on disciplinary grounds is not eligible to apply. Mobile phones and electronic gadgets are banned inside SSB/interview premises. No compensation will be paid in respect of any injury sustained during SSB tests. No request for change of SSB interview date/centre will be entertained or replied to. Candidates appearing for SSB interview for the first time are entitled to AC III Tier to-and-fro railway fare or bus fare including reservation-cum-sleeper charges within Indian geographical limits. Candidates are advised to visit https://www.joinindianarmy.nic.in regularly for updates. Queries will be replied only through the ‘Feedback/Queries’ option on the website. Queries received up to 03 days prior to closing of online application will only be entertained/replied. In case of any dispute between English and Hindi versions of the notification, the English version will prevail.

FAQs

Q1. What is Indian Army TES-56 Recruitment 2026?
Indian Army TES-56 is a Technical Entry Scheme for unmarried male candidates who have completed 10+2 with PCM and appeared in JEE Mains 2026, leading to a Permanent Commission as Lieutenant.
Q2. How many vacancies are available in TES-56 2026?
There are 90 vacancies (provisional) announced for the TES-56 course commencing in January 2027.
Q3. What are the important dates for Indian Army TES-56 2026?
The online application starts on 13 May 2026 (12 PM) and the last date is 12 June 2026 (12 PM).
Q4. What is the eligibility for Indian Army TES-56 Recruitment 2026?
Candidates must have passed 10+2 with at least 60% in PCM and must have appeared in JEE Mains 2026. Only unmarried male candidates are eligible.
Q5. What is the age limit for TES-56 2026?
Candidates must be between 16½ to 19½ years as on 1 January 2027 (born between 01 July 2007 and 01 July 2010).
Q6. What is the selection process for Indian Army TES-56?
The selection includes shortlisting based on JEE Mains rank, SSB interview, medical examination, and final merit list.
Q7. Is there any application fee for TES-56 Recruitment 2026?
No, there is no application fee for applying to Indian Army TES-56.
Q8. What is the salary after selection in TES-56?
After training, candidates are commissioned as Lieutenant with salary ₹56,100 – ₹1,77,500 (Level 10) and approx. ₹17–18 LPA CTC.
Q9. What is the training duration for TES-56?
The total training duration is 4 years (3 years at CTW + 1 year at IMA Dehradun), after which candidates receive an engineering degree.
Q10. How can I apply for Indian Army TES-56 Recruitment 2026?
Candidates can apply online through the official website joinindianarmy.nic.in by filling out the application form before the deadline.
