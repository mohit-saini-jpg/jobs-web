How to Apply

  • 1Applications must be submitted online only through the BPSSC website. Hard copy of applications need not be sent to the Commission. Candidates should visit https://bpssc.bihar.gov.in and click on the "Home Guard" tab, then click the link related to Advt. No. 05/2026 to begin the application process. Only one online application can be submitted per candidate. If multiple applications are submitted by a candidate, all will be rejected and legal action may also be taken.
  • 2The online application is filled in two parts. In Part 1, candidates register with their name, nationality, mobile number, email ID, domicile, reservation category, gender, and date of birth, followed by fee payment. After successful payment, Registration ID and Password are sent via SMS and Email. In Part 2, candidates fill in father/husband name, mother's name, address, educational qualifications, upload photograph and signature, and finally submit the application.

Important Instructions

Only Indian citizens are eligible to apply. Male, Female and Third Gender candidates can apply. All reservations (including 35% women's reservation) are applicable only to original residents (Mool Niwasi) of Bihar State. Candidates from other states must apply as Unreserved/General. Married female candidates found pregnant at the time of Running/Physical Efficiency Test (as per medical report) will not be allowed to participate and will be declared disqualified. Married female candidates must submit a declaration that they are not pregnant. Candidates must ensure they fulfill all eligibility criteria before applying. False information in applications will lead to rejection and legal action. No hard copy of application to be sent to the Commission - online submission only. Examination fee is non-refundable; fee charged back from bank will result in rejection and legal action. Only one application per mobile number is allowed. Candidates must be physically present with mobile phone during registration as Registration ID and Password are sent by SMS. Date of Birth will be calculated strictly as per Matriculation or equivalent certificate. Married/divorced/separated female candidates applying under reserved category must submit caste certificate based on their father's caste (not husband's), as caste is determined by birth, not marriage. EWS certificate must be based on income and assets for financial year 2025-26 as per prescribed format from General Administration Department, Bihar. The selection process may take approximately 6 months. No extension will be granted for any reason (personal, medical, pregnancy, fracture, etc.) at any stage. Candidates are advised to regularly check the BPSSC website and keep their registered mobile/email active for all updates. All decisions of the Commission regarding eligibility, selection, and other matters are final and binding.
