About Top Sarkari Jobs

Top Sarkari Jobs (topsarkarijobs.com) is an independent government-job information portal that collects and organises recruitment notifications, results, admit cards, answer keys and online application forms issued by official departments, commissions and recruitment boards across India. The site has been running since 2020 and exists to save aspirants the effort of checking dozens of separate official websites every day.

Site Owner

Top Sarkari Jobs is owned and operated independently and is not registered as, owned by, or affiliated with any Government department, ministry, commission, or recruitment agency. The site is run as a private information service for job aspirants. For ownership, partnership, or media queries, write to Topsarkarijobs.com@gmail.com.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easier for Indian job aspirants — especially in smaller towns with limited internet access — to find accurate, timely information about government recruitment without having to track dozens of official websites individually. We aim to be a single, reliable starting point that always points back to the official source.

What This Site Does

We monitor official sources and publish the key details of each notification — eligibility, important dates, vacancy count, application links and the official notification PDF — in a clean, mobile-friendly format. Every listing links back to the official source it is based on, so you can confirm the original before acting on it.

Our Content Process

Each notification published on Top Sarkari Jobs goes through the following steps before it appears on the site:

  1. The notification is identified from the relevant official recruitment website, PIB release, or official gazette/employment news.
  2. Key details — title, eligibility, age limit, vacancy count, important dates, fee and application link — are extracted and rewritten in plain, easy-to-read language.
  3. The extracted details are checked against the original official notification PDF for accuracy before the page is published.
  4. A direct link to the official notification / application portal is added to every listing so readers can verify and apply at the source.
  5. Listings are revisited and corrected if the official source updates dates, vacancy numbers, or eligibility after publishing.

Source Verification Process

Every notification is manually checked against the official recruitment website before publishing. We rely only on official notifications, government gazettes, PIB releases, and recruitment boards' own portals as our source — not on unverified third-party posts. Because eligibility, fees and dates can change after publication, we always recommend verifying the latest details on the relevant official portal before you apply or pay any fee.

Who It's For

The portal serves Sarkari exam aspirants across India — from 8th, 10th and 12th pass candidates to ITI and diploma holders, graduates and postgraduates. Listings can be browsed qualification-wise and state-wise so you can quickly reach the openings that match your profile.

Coverage

Top Sarkari Jobs covers central and state government recruitment across categories including Railway, SSC, Banking, Police & Defence, Teaching, Medical and State PSC roles, along with state-wise job and education listings. New notifications are added daily.

Important Note

Top Sarkari Jobs is an information aggregator only. It is not a government website and is not affiliated with any government body, department, ministry, commission, or recruitment agency. All official notifications, logos and documents belong to their respective departments.
