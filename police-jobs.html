FAQs – Police Recruitment

Police constable exam pattern क्या होता है?

State police constable recruitment exams usually consist of a written test followed by a physical efficiency test (PET), physical measurement test (PMT) and document verification. Some states also conduct a medical examination.

क्या पुलिस भर्तियों में reservation होता है?

हाँ, SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwD candidates के लिए reservation and age relaxation उपलब्ध है। अधिक जानकारी official notification में दी जाती है।

Sub‑Inspector posts के लिए minimum qualification क्या है?

Generally a Graduation degree is required. Age limits vary by state (usually 20–25 years), with relaxations for reserved categories.
