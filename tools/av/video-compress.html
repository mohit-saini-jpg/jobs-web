❓ Aksar Puche Jane Wale Sawal (FAQ)

    
Nahi. Yeh tool FFmpeg (WebAssembly) use karta hai jo seedha aapke browser ke andar chalta hai — video kabhi kisi server par nahi jaati. Isliye "auto-delete" jaisi cheez ki zaroorat hi nahi, kyunki upload hi nahi hoti.

    
Yeh aapke device ki RAM par depend karta hai — mobile par ~200-300MB tak, desktop/laptop par kai GB tak comfortably chal jaata hai. Bahut badi file par browser tab open rakhein, processing time lagega.

    
Browser mein chalne wala FFmpeg engine (WebAssembly build) abhi H.264 aur VP9 tak hi support karta hai — H.265/AV1 encoder shamil nahi hote (file size/license reasons se). H.264 sabse zyada compatible hai aur achhi compression deta hai.

    
Kaafi kareeb (generally ±10%) — video content (motion, detail) ke hisaab se thoda upar-neeche ho sakta hai, ekdum exact byte-perfect guarantee nahi di ja sakti kyunki compression content-dependent hoti hai.

    
Aapke video ka current bitrate, resolution aur duration analyze karke ek smart formula (heuristic) best CRF aur resolution decide karta hai — yeh ek real cloud-AI model nahi hai (sab kuch browser mein instantly calculate hota hai), balki proven video-compression logic hai jo achhe results deta hai.

    
Video ki length, resolution aur aapke device ki speed par depend karta hai — ek 1-2 minute ka 1080p video generally 30 sec - 2 min mein process ho jaata hai.
