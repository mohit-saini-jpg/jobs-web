How It Works

Is my photo uploaded anywhere?
No. Every operation — AI upscaling, color correction, face detection, sharpening — runs entirely inside your browser using TensorFlow.js and WebGL/WebAssembly. Your photo never leaves your device.
What powers the "AI" in this tool?
Real on-device neural networks: an ESRGAN-style TensorFlow.js super-resolution model for upscaling, and Google's MediaPipe Face Landmarker for face detection — both run locally, no API keys, no cloud calls. Color/tone/sharpening tools use classical image-processing algorithms (histogram auto-levels, unsharp masking, dark-channel dehaze) rather than generative AI, so results stay natural and don't invent fake detail.
Why is 4× / 8× upscaling slow on my device?
4× and 8× run the real AI model 2 or 3 times in sequence for genuine detail recovery at each stage. Devices with a good GPU (WebGL) finish in seconds; older devices without WebGL fall back to CPU and can take longer on large photos.

Yes, completely free, unlimited use, no login and no watermark on downloads.
