Government Forms Ke Liye Size Guide

  • SSC / Railway / UPSC Photo: 200×230 px ya 3.5cm×4.5cm
  • Signature: 140×60 px ya 140×60 pixels
  • Passport Photo: 413×531 px (51mm×63mm @ 200 DPI)
  • WhatsApp DP: 500×500 ya 1000×1000 px
  • Social Media Profile: 400×400 px recommended
