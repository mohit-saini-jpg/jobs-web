State Government Jobs 2026 – Complete Guide

Find the latest government job notifications for your state on Top Sarkari Jobs. We cover all state PSC jobs, police recruitment, teacher vacancies, revenue department jobs, health department vacancies and more — updated daily.

Types of State Govt Jobs Available

  • State Public Service Commission (PSC) – Group A, B, C posts
  • State Police – Constable, Sub Inspector, Inspector
  • State Education – Primary, Secondary, College Teachers
  • Revenue & Patwari – Lekhpal, Patwari, Girdawar
  • Health Department – ANM, Staff Nurse, Medical Officer
  • Forest Department – Forest Guard, Range Officer
  • PWD & Irrigation – Junior Engineer, Overseer
  • Panchayati Raj – Gram Sachiv, Panchayat Secretary

How to Apply for State Govt Jobs?

Eligible candidates can apply online through the official state government recruitment portal. Check the official notification for eligibility criteria, application fee, selection process and important dates. We recommend joining our WhatsApp channel for instant alerts.

Important Documents Required

Candidates typically need: Aadhaar Card, Educational Certificates, Domicile/Residence Certificate, Caste Certificate (if applicable), Passport Size Photo, Signature.
