RSSB Teaching Associate Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 3540 Posts

Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB), Jaipur

3540 Posts Last Date: 3rd June 2026 Online India

Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has officially announced the Teaching Associate Recruitment 2026 with 3540 openings. The detailed notification was published at the official website rssb.rajasthan.gov.in. Candidates who meet the age criteria of 21 to 40 years (age relaxation applicable as per category) and hold a Master’s Degree in the relevant subject with qualifications such as NET/SET/Ph.D. as per norms can now submit their online applications from 05 May 2026 to 03 June 2026.
