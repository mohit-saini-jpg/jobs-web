Angul District Court

18 Posts Last Date: 2026-06-18 Offline India

Angul District Court has officially released the Junior Clerk-cum-Copyist, Stenographer Grade-III, Salaried Amin Recruitment 2026 notification (Advt. No. 03 dated 18.05.2026) for 18 vacancies across Odisha. Eligible graduates and 10th pass candidates aged 18 to 42 years can apply offline from 18 May 2026 to 18 June 2026 through the official Angul District Court application process. Selected candidates will be appointed on a full-time basis with a monthly salary ranging from ₹19,900/- to ₹81,100/-.
