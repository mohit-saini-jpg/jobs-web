Konkan Railway Corporation Limited (KRCL) has announced a new walk-in recruitment drive for 9 Accounts posts. Eligible candidates with CA, CMA, B.Com, or M.Com qualifications can attend the walk-in interviews scheduled from 17 June to 19 June 2026 for different posts.
