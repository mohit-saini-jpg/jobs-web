TNPSC CTS Exam Notification 2026 Out For 461 Non Interview Post - Apply Online

Government of India

Last Date: 25.06.2026 (11:59 PM) Online India

The Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission (TNPSC) has released Advertisement No. 734 / Notification No. 04/2026 inviting online applications for the Combined Technical Services Examination (Non-Interview Posts). A total of 461 vacancies across 45 posts in various Government Departments and Public Sector Organisations are being filled through this recruitment.
