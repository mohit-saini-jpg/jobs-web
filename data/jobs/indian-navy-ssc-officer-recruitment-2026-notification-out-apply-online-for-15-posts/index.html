Indian Navy SSC Officer Recruitment 2026 Notification Out – Apply Online for 15 Posts

SSC Officer - Executive Branch (Information Technology Cadre)

15 (Men & Women) Posts Last Date: 2026-06-01 Online Indian Naval Academy (INA), Ezhimala, Kerala

The Indian Navy has released the official SSC Officer - Executive Branch (Information Technology) Notification 2026 on 05 May 2026 at joinindiannavy.gov.in. A total of 15 vacancies have been announced under the Indian Navy SSC IT Officer Recruitment 2026. Eligible unmarried candidates born between 01 January 2002 and 01 July 2007 with MSc / BE / B.Tech / M.Tech / MCA in eligible IT and Computer Science-related disciplines can apply online from 16 May 2026 to 01 June 2026.
