Andaman & Nicobar Police Constable Recruitment 2026 Notification out - Apply Online for 495 Group B (NG) & Group C Posts

Government of India

Sub-Inspector (Executive/PMT/FS/Master/GD), Constable (Executive/Bandsmen/GD/Wireless/Driver etc.), Radio Operator (ASI), Radio Technician (ASI), Firemen, Follower posts & others Posts Last Date: 2026-06-10 Online Andaman

Andaman & Nicobar Police has released the official Group-B (Non-Gazetted) & Group-C Recruitment Notification 2026 in April 2026 at its official website. A total of 495 (tentative) vacancies have been announced under the A&N Police Group-B (NG) & Group-C Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged between 18 to 30/33 years (varies by post) with qualifications ranging from 10th, 12th, to Graduation can apply online from 27 April 2026 to 26 May 2026.
