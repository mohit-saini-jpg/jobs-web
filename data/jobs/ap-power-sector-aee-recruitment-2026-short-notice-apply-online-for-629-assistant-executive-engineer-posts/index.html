AP Power Sector AEE Recruitment 2026 (Short Notice) - Apply Online for 629 Assistant Executive Engineer Posts

Andhra Pradesh Energy Department / AP Power Utilities

629 Posts Last Date: Updated Soon Online India

The Andhra Pradesh Government has officially approved a new recruitment drive for Assistant Executive Engineer (AEE) posts in the AP Power Sector, offering a strong opportunity for engineering graduates seeking government jobs in the state. As per the approval issued by the Energy Department on May 15, 2026, a total of 629 AEE vacancies in Electrical, Civil, and Telecom wings will be filled through direct recruitment, while the detailed notification is awaited.
