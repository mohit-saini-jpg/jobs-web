SECR Nagpur Apprentice Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 1079 Posts

South East Central Railway (SECR), Divisional Office, Kingsway, Nagpur-440001

1079 (977 – Nagpur Division + 102 – Motibagh Workshop) Posts Last Date: 2026-06-18 Online India

South East Central Railway (SECR) has published the Act Apprentice Notification 2026 on 18-05-2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 1079 Act Apprentice positions. Candidates within the age range of 15 to 24 years and holding Matriculation (10th) with minimum 50% marks along with ITI Certificate (NCVT/SCVT) in relevant trade are eligible to apply online at the secr.indianrailways.gov.in. The application process will be active from 19-05-2026 to 18-06-2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.
