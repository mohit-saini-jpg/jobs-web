JNU Faculty Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for Professor, Associate and Assistant Professor Posts

Professor, Associate Professor, Assistant Professor

Multiple positions across various categories Posts Last Date: 2026-06-15 Online India

The Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU) has officially announced the Faculty Recruitment 2026 with multiple openings for Professor, Associate Professor, and Assistant Professor posts. The detailed notification was published on 18-05-2026 at JNU Official Website under Advertisement No. RC/75/2026. Candidates holding qualifications such as Master’s Degree, M.A, M.Sc, M.E/M.Tech, MS, or Ph.D. as per UGC Regulations 2018 and meeting the required eligibility criteria can now submit their online applications from 18-05-2026 to 15-06-2026.
