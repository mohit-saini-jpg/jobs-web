KSEB Electricity Worker Recruitment 2026 via Kerala PSC Notification Out - Apply Online for 985 Posts

Electricity Worker

985 (District-wise; including 4% PH reservation) Posts Last Date: 2026-06-03 Online Kerala

Kerala Public Service Commission (KPSC) has officially announced the Electricity Worker Recruitment 2026 with 985 vacancies under the Kerala State Electricity Board Ltd. The detailed notification (Category No. 21/2026, Gazette Date: 30 April 2026) was published on the official website www.keralapsc.gov.in. Candidates who meet the age criteria of 18 to 40 years (with relaxations for reserved categories) and hold a SSLC pass along with a 2-year Government-approved Electrician/Wireman Trade Certificate can now submit their online applications from 30 April 2026 to 03 June 2026.
