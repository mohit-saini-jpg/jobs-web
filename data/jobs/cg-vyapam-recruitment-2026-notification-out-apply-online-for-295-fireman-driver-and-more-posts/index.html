CG Vyapam Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 295 Fireman, Driver and More Posts

Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board (CG Vyapam) on behalf of Fire & Emergency Services and SDRF

Approx. 295 Posts (including backlog) Posts Last Date: 2026-06-05 Online India

Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board (CG Vyapam) has published the Fireman (NSF26) Written Recruitment Examination Notification 2026 on 8 May 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill various posts under Nagar Sena, Fire & Emergency Services and SDRF including Fireman, Vehicle Driver, Station Officer, and other positions. Eligible local residents of Chhattisgarh who have qualified the Physical Efficiency Test (PET) can apply online at vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in.
