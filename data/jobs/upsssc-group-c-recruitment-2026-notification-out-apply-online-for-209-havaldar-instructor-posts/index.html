UPSSSC Group C Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 209 Havaldar Instructor Posts

Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC)

209 Posts Posts Last Date: 2026-07-09 Online India

Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has published the Havaldar Instructor Notification 2026 on 17th April 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 209 Havaldar Instructor positions. Candidates within the age range of 18 to 40 years and holding a 10+2 (Intermediate) qualification along with PET-2025 scorecard are eligible to apply online at upsssc.gov.in. The application process will be active from 19th June 2026 to 9th July 2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.
